War is the source of both fortune and calamity for the realm. When one fights in accordance with the principles of Heaven, it becomes the foundation for bringing peace and virtue to all lands. But when one wages war for personal gain and ambition, it marks the beginning of dark arts and the ruin of the nation. One must be deeply mindful of this. In general, warfare has three stages: beginning, middle, and end. Harmony among people comes first; the advantage of terrain comes second; and the timeliness of Heaven comes last. Truly, governing a great nation is like cooking a small fish — it must be done with great care and without unnecessary disturbance. How much more so in the matter of battle.

…Even if such a person happens to hold a position of standing, if they bend the proper way, disregard the hardship and inconvenience caused to the people at large, make the pursuit of personal gain and greed their guiding principle, and cause the commander to be despised by the people — such a person should be made to commit ritual suicide (seppuku). Make the people prosperous and at ease; extend compassion even to the widowed, the widowers, the orphaned, and the elderly without children; ensure that there is not a single wretched soul suffering from hunger and cold; in every way, make it so that the people of the realm think to themselves, "There is no lord but this one" — and even from within enemy territory, bring about a situation where people defect and pledge their loyalty to your side. If one then goes to war after all this has been accomplished, defeat shall never come.

Article Seven: There are those who fight by relying on the Opportune Moment of Heaven (ten no toki). The Opportune Moment of Heaven refers to the selection of auspicious days, the choosing of propitious hours, the taking of favorable directions, and the prioritization of the four seasons and the principles of yin and yang…

【Left Page】

…They surround the enemy castle and attack it, yet cannot bring it down; days are wasted in vain, and the stalemate drags on for a long time. However, the Opportune Moment of Heaven cannot match the Advantages of Terrain (chi no ri). The various elements of the Advantages of Terrain are as follows: favorable topography; a castle that is steep and difficult to approach; stone walls that are high; walls and watchtowers that are well-constructed and sturdy; moats that are deep and layered in multiple rings; an abundance of bows, firearms, and all manner of weapons and horse equipment; provisions that are fully stocked; and a water supply that never runs dry. And yet, even when fighting with all these Advantages of Terrain on one's side, if the hearts of the soldiers are fragmented and divided, in the end some will abandon the castle, flee, and surrender.

A Guide to Sericulture

1. Silkworm eggs should be hung in high places during the summer months.
   Alternatively, they may be wrapped in paper and placed in boxes
   in locations that do not receive moisture.

1. Forty days before Hangeshō is the proper time for hatching. Seven or eight
   days before this, take them out and hang them in a place where they can
   naturally receive warmth without being exposed to direct sunlight.

Regarding the Sericulture House
1. Around the 88th night from the beginning of the third month, sweep away soot, thoroughly remove spider webs, keep everything clean, and seal all gaps including the fitting of doors and shoji screens so as to keep the place warm. However, if there are high windows, they are very good for letting out steam.
1. Smoking tobacco in the silkworm house is absolutely forbidden.
1. After the silkworms emerge, handling anything with bad odors in the house

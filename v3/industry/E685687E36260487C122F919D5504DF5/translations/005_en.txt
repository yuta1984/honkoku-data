Preface to Kaimotsu Shinsho
Heaven was formed and earth became level, and all nations flourish in abundance. However, the speed of their civilization and enlightenment exists only within the people themselves, and should not be judged by whether they are new or old. Places like our Ezo land still remain in chaos even now, unchanged from a thousand years ago. The fishing industry has flourished year after year...

　One　Shedding variety　《Marginal note: Young buds shed easily, hence this name》
Generally, when discussing the grades among the various types of early and late mulberry, four-eye, cross shape, and green stem are considered first class. This is because their trunk nodes are short, their leaves are thick and luxuriant, and the substances they contain are suitable for silkworm feed. However, since their sprouting is delayed compared to the hatching period of silkworm eggs, several varieties of early mulberry are also considered indispensable.

　　　Chapter Two
When early mulberry sprouts, not only is it frequently affected by spring cold, but since it coincides with the silkworm egg hatching period, there is no time for luxuriant growth, and since silkworms are fed with tender leaves, the nutrients are concentrated

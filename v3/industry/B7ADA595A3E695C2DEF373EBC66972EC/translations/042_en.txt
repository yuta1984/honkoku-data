　　　Chapter 18
When placing mature silkworms in "mabushi" and setting them on shelves, the silkworm room should be kept clean and proper air circulation should be maintained. Stuffiness and humidity in the room causes the greatest harm.

　Seed Production and Cocoon Harvesting
　　　Chapter 1
Mature silkworms that have already begun forming cocoons complete their work in three days and nights, and transform into pupae in about four days. Their color is water-like in luster, and after six or seven days it changes to tea-brown. At this point, those who wish to collect egg seeds arrange about one to (measure) each in baskets, placing them side by side on shelves

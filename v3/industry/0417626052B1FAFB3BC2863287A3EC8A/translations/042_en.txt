1. Dye the base fabric in light pale yellow, boil kariyasu grass and soak, wring out moderately, add a little alum to water, soak the dyed item in it, immediately wring out and hang to dry.

　　　Dark Brown Dyeing
1. Dye the base fabric dark with turmeric, boil kariyasu grass, add a little alum and green vitriol, add a little iron solution and stir while dyeing.

　　　Silver Soot Bamboo Dyeing
1. Add a little sappanwood to kariyasu, boil down with water, add a little alum and green vitriol, stir and dry in sunlight.

　　　Yellow-Brown Tea Dyeing
1. Dye the base fabric dark with turmeric, boil kariyasu grass and sappanwood with water, add a little alum, stir and dye. If the color is light, make a second decoction, add alum and dye twice.

【Left Page】
　　　Chitose Tea Dyeing
1. Dye the base fabric sky blue with indigo, boil astringent wood with water, apply three times over the above. Add about 7 go of water to about 3 go of iron solution, apply once over, rinse well with water and dry.

　　　Indigo Sea Pine Tea Dyeing
1. Dye the base fabric sky blue, boil astringent wood and dye once, then dry and dye once more. Put the dye bath in a basin, add a little iron solution and stir, dye evenly. Add a little alum to the dye bath, dye, rinse with water and dry.

　　　Willow Soot Bamboo Tea
1. Dye the base fabric pale yellow, then apply kariyasu to both sides. Grind ink into soybean juice, and when it becomes gray color, apply with a brush.

If used in cold regions, cut out all of the paper tent's ceiling completely, use it only from dawn until around the fifth hour (8 AM), and after that, remove it entirely day and night. Also, in low-ceilinged areas under second floors, cut open a three-shaku square (approximately 90cm square) section of the ceiling. Generally, even after nightfall, do not completely close the doors of one room. The weather can change during a single night.

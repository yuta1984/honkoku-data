○The 210th day is when the earth's position changes, and as the heat subsides and cold emerges, great winds arise. Since this is the time when rice flowers and forms grain, farmers consider it inauspicious.

○The onset of cold (kan no iri) extends from minor cold (shokan) to the beginning of spring (risshun). As the cold can sometimes be severe, yin-yang practitioners offer various theories, but one must not be misled by them.

Even the depth of the ocean's abyss can be known without entering it. How much more so with the principles of heaven! The movements of the sun and moon, the seasonal changes of spring and autumn, and besides these, diviners, doctors, and all craftsmen seek good and bad fortune through mathematics and understand the proper flow of vital energy. How could one know the mysteries and comprehend principles without relying on this way? However, to know its depths and understand its details is not easily achieved. Therefore, I thought to record its essential points to serve as one gateway into the realm of learning, but due to my own foolishness, there may be deficiencies or redundancies, and it is not perfect. If someone with clear wisdom would correct the errors and omissions, it would truly be of great help.

Even the depths of the ocean's abyss can eventually have their bottom known. How much more so for the principles of heaven! The movements of the sun and moon, the seasonal changes of spring and autumn, and all other matters including shamans, doctors, and craftsmen—all seek good and ill fortune through arithmetic and understand the proper flow and disruption of vital energy. How could one know the mysteries and comprehend the principles without relying on this way? However, to know the profound depths and understand the details is difficult to attain unless one reaches sufficient mastery. Therefore, I wish to record these essential points as an aid for entering the way of the sages. But since I am of limited intelligence and not sufficiently diligent, there may be deficiencies or redundancies that cause problems. If a truly discerning person would correct the errors and omissions, it would truly be of great assistance.

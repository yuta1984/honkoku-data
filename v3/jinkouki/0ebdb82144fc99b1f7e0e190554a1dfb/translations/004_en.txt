Even the depths of ocean abysses can be known without entering them to reach their bottom. How much more so the principles of heaven! The movements of the sun and moon, the circulation of the seasons of spring and autumn, and beyond that, shamans, doctors, and all kinds of craftsmen—all seek good and bad fortune through mathematics and know the proper flow and disruption of vital energy. Without relying on this way, how could one know mysteries and understand principles? However, to know its depths and understand its details is not easily achieved. Therefore, I thought to record its essential points as an aid to entering the gate of the sages' learning, but because I am foolish and negligent, there may be deficiencies or redundancies—how could it be clear? If an intelligent person would correct the errors and omissions, it would truly be a great help.

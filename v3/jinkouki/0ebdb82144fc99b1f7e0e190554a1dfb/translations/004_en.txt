Even the depths of ocean abysses can be measured to know their bottom. How much more so can the principles of heaven be understood! The movements of the sun and moon, the seasonal cycles of spring and autumn, and all other matters involving diviners, doctors, and craftsmen - all seek fortune and misfortune through mathematics and understand the proper and reverse flow of vital energy. How could one know the mysteries and comprehend the principles without following this way? However, to know its depths and understand its details is extremely difficult. Therefore, I thought to record its essential points as an aid to entering the sacred gate of learning. But being of humble intelligence, there may be insufficiencies or redundancies. If a truly enlightened person would correct the errors and omissions, it would truly be of great assistance.

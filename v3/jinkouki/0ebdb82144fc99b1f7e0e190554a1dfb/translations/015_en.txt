**Chapter 8: Division Methods (Ken-ichi no warigoe) with Addition Calculations**

Ken-ichi Method 91:
Return-one times-one, one advance of ten
This is used when dividing by numbers from 11 to 19
Also applies when dividing numbers above 100 by numbers above 1000

Ken-ichi Method 92:
Return-one times-two, second level of eight calculations
This is used when dividing by numbers from 21 to 29
Also applies to numbers above 200, 2000, or 20000

Ken-san Method 93:
Return-one times-three, third level of eight calculations
This is used when dividing by numbers from 31 to 39, also for numbers above 300 or 3000
All follow the same principle

Ken-shi Method 94:
Return-one times-four, fourth level of eight calculations
This is used when dividing by numbers from 41 to 49
Numbers above 400 or 4000 follow the same principle

Ken-go Method 95:
Return-one times-five, fifth level of eight calculations
This is used when dividing by numbers from 51 to 59
Numbers above 500, 5000, or 50000 follow the same principle

Ken-roku Method 96:
Return-one times-six, sixth level of eight calculations
This is used when dividing by numbers above 60, 600, or 6000
All follow the same principle

Ken-shichi Method 97:
Return-one times-seven, seventh level of eight calculations
This is used when dividing by numbers from 71 to 79
Numbers above 700 or 7000 follow the same principle

Ken-hachi Method 98:
Return-one times-eight, eighth level of eight calculations
This is used when dividing by numbers from 81 to 89
Numbers above 800 or 8000 follow the same principle

Ken-ku Method 99:
Return-one times-nine, ninth level of eight calculations
This is used when dividing by numbers from 91 to 99
Numbers above 900 or 9000 follow the same principle

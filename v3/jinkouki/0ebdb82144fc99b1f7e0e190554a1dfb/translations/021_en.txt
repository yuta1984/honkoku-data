Chapter 10: Rice Trading

○When there are 810 koku of rice, with an exchange rate of 4 to 3 shō 2 gō of rice per 10 monme of silver, when asking how much silver the rice is worth, the answer is "18 kan 150 me of silver." Place 810 koku of rice on the right and the rate of 4 to 3 shō 2 gō on the left, then divide the rice on the right by 4 to 3 shō 2 gō to get 18 kan 750 me. When rice is divided by the rice exchange rate, it becomes silver; when silver is multiplied, it becomes rice.

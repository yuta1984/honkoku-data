[Right equilateral triangle]
[Right side] 15 ken [Left side] Same [Bottom side] Same
　By the calculation method, place 15 ken on both sides. Multiplying gives 225 tsubo.
　When multiplying this by the triangle method of 433,

　you should know it as 3 se 7 bu 4 bu 2 ri 5 mo.

[Isosceles triangle]
[Left side] Length 39 ken [Left and right] 14 ken
　By the calculation method, dividing the width of 14 ken by two gives 7 ken.
　Multiplying this by the length of 39 ken gives 273 tsubo,

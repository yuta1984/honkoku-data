　By the calculation method, when the diameter of a circle is unknown, measuring
　the circumference gives 47 ken 2 shaku 6 sun. Dividing the 2 shaku 6 sun
　by 65 gives 47 ken 4.

※○1 tan 3 se 14 bu 4 bu 8 ri
[Semicircle]
[Right side] Length 32 ken [Left] 16 ken
　By the calculation method, multiplying length 32 ken by width 16 ken
　gives 512 tsubo. Multiplying this again by the circular method
　of 79 gives 404 tsubo 4 bu 8 ri. When
　dividing this by the field method of 3, you should know it as 1 tan 3 se 14 bu
　4 bu 8 ri.

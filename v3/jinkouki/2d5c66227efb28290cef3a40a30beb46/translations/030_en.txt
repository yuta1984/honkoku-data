[Left page]
　※One to measuring box　width　1 shaku 7 bu 7 rin 2 mo
　　　　　　　　　　　　　depth　5 sun 3 bu 8 rin 6 mo

　Method for making measuring boxes. When making a one go measuring box,
　one sho has 62,500 tsubo in one bu square units. One go has
　6,250 tsubo. When this is divided by five,
　it becomes 12,500. When this is calculated using the cube root method,
　the width of a one go measuring box is known to be 2 sun 3 bu 2 rin.
　Also, to know the depth, multiply 2 sun 3 bu 2 rin by five,
　and the depth is known to be 1 sun 1 bu 6 rin.

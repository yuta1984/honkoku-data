　※〇With new measures: holds 3 koku 8 sho 5 go 1 shaku
　　〇With old measures: holds 3 koku 2 to
[Figure at bottom right]
[Top edge horizontally] length 5 shaku　[Vertically] depth 2 shaku
[Depth at 3 locations] 2 shaku
　Calculation method: multiply length 5 shaku by width 2 shaku
　to get 10 tsubo. Multiply this by height 2 shaku to get 20 tsubo.
　Then divide the above 20 tsubo by the new measure coefficient 64827
　to know the result as 3 koku 8 sho 5 go 1 shaku.
　Also, multiply 20 tsubo by 16 to know the result as 3 koku 2 to.
　Similarly, dividing 20 tsubo by 625 gives the same result.

　※Tsurube (well bucket)
　　〇With new measures: holds 2 sho 1 go 3 shaku
　　〇With old measures: holds 2 sho 2 go 1 shaku
[Figure at bottom left]
[Top surface right] 6 sun　[Top surface left] 6 sun　[Left side] depth 6 sun
[Bottom surface right] inner bottom diameter 3 sun 6 bu　[Bottom surface left] 3 sun 6 bu
　Calculation method: combine the top opening 6 sun and bottom 3 sun 6 bu to get 9 sun 6 bu.
　When this is divided by two, it becomes 4 sun 8 bu.
　Square this to get 230.4 tsubo in sun units.
　Multiply this by depth 6 sun to get 1382.4 tsubo.
　Divide this tsubo number by the new measure coefficient 64827
　to know the result as 2 sho 1 go 3 shaku.

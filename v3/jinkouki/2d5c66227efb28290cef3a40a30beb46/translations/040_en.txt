Calculation method: Place 9 shaku 8 sun 8 bu for both dimensions and multiply,
which gives 976,144. Multiply this by the square method coefficient 433,
to get 422,670,352. Multiply this by the height of 2 ken
to get 453,407,04. Divide this by the flat tsubo method
coefficient 4225 to get 2 tsubo.

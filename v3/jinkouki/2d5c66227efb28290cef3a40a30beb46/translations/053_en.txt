Problem 34: Doubling One Unit Each Day

　※Starting with a single poppy seed and doubling it every day,
　　※after 30 days: (assuming 4,000,000 seeds fit in 1 shō)
　　　Total becomes 1 koku, 3 to, 4 shō, 2 gō, 1 shaku, 7 sai, 7 satsu, 2 kei, 8 soku
　　　　　The above number is:
　　　　　　530,687,090,912 seeds
　※Starting with 1 mon in coins and doubling every day,
　　※after 30 days: (calculating at the rate of 96 mon per 100 mon, i.e., accounting for exchange fees)
　　　Total: 559,240 kan, 532 mon, including exchange fees (mokusen)
　　　　　The exchange fee (mokusen) amounts to 22,369 kan, 620 mon

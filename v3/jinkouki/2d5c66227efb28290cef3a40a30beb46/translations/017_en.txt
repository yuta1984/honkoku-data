※1 tan 12 bu
[Right triangle]
[Vertically] Length 39 ken [Bottom side] 16 ken
　By the calculation method, when dividing the length of 16 ken by two, it becomes 8 ken. When
　multiplying this by the length of 39 ken, it becomes 312 tsubo. When dividing by the field method of 3,
　it should be known as 1 tan 12 bu.

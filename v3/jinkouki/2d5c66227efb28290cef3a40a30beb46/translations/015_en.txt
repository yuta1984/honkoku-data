[Right track-shaped figure]
[Vertical direction] Length 30 ken [Horizontal direction] 14 ken
　By the calculation method, when subtracting the width of 14 ken from the length of 30 ken,
　16 ken remains. Multiplying this by 14 ken gives 224 tsubo.
　Setting this aside on the right, and also placing 14 ken on the left and right sides and
　multiplying gives 196. Multiplying this by the circular method of 79 gives
　154 tsubo 8 bu 4 ri. When adding this to the right side calculation,
　it becomes 378 tsubo 8 bu 4 ri. Dividing this by the field method
　of 3 gives 1 tan 2 se 18 bu 8 bu 4 ri.

[Right figure]

[Horizontally] Width 18 ken
　By the calculation method, add 24 ken to the length of 33 ken to make 57 ken.
　When divided by two, it becomes 28 ken and a half. When multiplying this by
　18 ken, it becomes 513 tsubo. When
　dividing this by the field method of 3, it should be known as 1 tan 7 se 3 bu.
　　The right land survey calculations copied here are essential for comparison.
　　Although the basic approach is the same for all, this is roughly
　　for the purpose of teaching beginners.

※[Left trapezoid]
[Top side] 13 ken [Bottom side] 5 ken [Right side] Length 57 ken [Center] 1 tan 7 se 3 bu


[Left page]
　By the calculation method, when adding 5 ken to the width of 13 ken,
　it becomes 18 ken. When dividing this by two, it becomes 9 ken. Multiplying this by
　the length of 57 ken gives 513 tsubo. Dividing this by
　the field method of 3 gives 1 tan 7 se 3 bu, as you should know.

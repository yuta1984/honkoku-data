Illustrated Actors Sangaikyo
　　　　Preface by the Author

What is the meaning of the patriarch's coming from the west? In the madness of love where willows are green and flowers are red.
Being obsessed with Buddhist chanting becomes nonsensical, and breaking the five precepts leads to uproar on the second floor.
The Daruma-like group of geishas. Not according to one's wishes, they strike back.
The direct pointing finger-guessing drinking games soften the human heart - this too is a separate transmission of kyōgen.
In the innocent letters that establish no reliance on written words, what are nine years? The anguished face of wall-gazing meditation.
Some awaken to "form is emptiness," while others are deluded by "emptiness is form."
The emotions of the bedroom in seeing one's nature and attaining Buddhahood - even the eye of great enlightenment...

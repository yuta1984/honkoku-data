【Left Vertical Text】
Afteekening Han Het Slachten Han Koebeesten 
En Harkens
　　　　　Illustration of Raising Pigs and Slaughtering Cattle

【Right Horizontal Text】
Writing is a vessel that penetrates the Way, and painting is a tool that conveys form.
However, if writing is crude, how can it penetrate the Way? If a painting is
clumsy, it naturally cannot convey form. Drawing a tiger that
resembles a dog is an example of this. When Hokusai
depicts all things in nature, he conveys their true forms, so that
even a three-year-old child can look at them and recognize what the objects are...

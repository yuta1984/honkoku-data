Then when the next day arrived, since there would be an unprecedented strange event, all the civil and military officials crowded together in the government hall, waiting eagerly. The Copper Plate, surrounded by guard soldiers, entered carrying a single volume of a book in his hands. He removed its case and respectfully presented it to the king, then requested a basin, opened the case and placed it on top, and again offered it to the king. Then he said, "After you behead this humble servant, if you place the head on this basin, the bleeding will immediately stop. When the bleeding stops and Your Majesty personally opens that book and reads the third line on the back of the sixth leaf, please ask any question you wish. My head will answer each one.

Eighth Generation: Ai, reigned 13 years
Ninth Generation: Satto, reigned 46 years
　During this period, the lord of Chūzan first received investiture from the Ming Dynasty. This occurred in the 28th year of Hongwu under Emperor Taizu of Ming, corresponding to the 2nd year of Ōei under Emperor Go-Komatsu of Japan.
Tenth Generation: Bunei, reigned 10 years
Eleventh Generation: Shō Shishō, reigned 16 years
Twelfth Generation: Shō Hashi, reigned 18 years
　During this period, Nanzan and Hokuzan were unified with Chūzan to form the unified Ryukyu Kingdom.
Thirteenth Generation: Shō Chū, reigned 5 years
Fourteenth Generation: Shō Shitatsu, reigned 5 years

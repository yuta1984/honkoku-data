North of Ezo country there is another country. It is separated by only six or seven ri of ocean from the northwestern border of Ezo. This place is called Karafuto Island {{its original name is Taraikai, also called Tarakai}}. It is said to be an island with twenty-one settlements and a circumference of three hundred ri, but no one has seen its details. However, since geographical studies have recently become more precise, it seems that the general situation of this place can now be understood. This place is not a completely isolated island, but is connected to the land of Eastern Tartary (Eastern Tatars), located in the Shiwei region, and is said to be a peninsula of the southeastern sea.

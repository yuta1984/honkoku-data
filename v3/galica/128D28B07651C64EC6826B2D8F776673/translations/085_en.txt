Attushi: All clothing is called "Attushi" or "Jittoku"
　　　　　　　　　　　The sleeve edges are made of cotton　　The length is made to tsuitate (matching length)
This clothing is worn by male Ainu on formal
occasions. They are all made from
Japanese used clothing,　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　worn left front
using donsu (satin) and shuchin (figured satin)　　　　　　 　　　　　　　　wrapped
and other unlined garments. The
patterns are made by cutting
navy cotton in various
shapes and sewing them on. Women's Ainu clothing is prohibited from having patterns.

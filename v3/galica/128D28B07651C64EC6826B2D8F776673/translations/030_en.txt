Fifteenth Generation: Shō Kinpuku, reigned 4 years
　During this period, Ryukyu people came and presented tribute to Shogun Yoshimasa. From this time onward, people from that country came annually to Hyōgo Bay in Settsu Province to conduct trade. The coming of Ryukyu envoys to our court should be considered to have begun at this time. This occurred in the 3rd year of Hōtoku under Emperor Go-Hanazono.
Sixteenth Generation: Shō Nei'i, reigned 60 months
Seventeenth Generation: Shō Shin, reigned 50 years
Eighteenth Generation: Shō Sei, reigned 29 years
Nineteenth Generation: Shō Gen, reigned 29 years
Twentieth Generation: Shō Nei, reigned 32 years
　During this period, the king was captured alive by Satsuma forces and remained in our court for approximately four years.

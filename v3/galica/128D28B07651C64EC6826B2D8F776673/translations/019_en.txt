Pyeongan Province (Pyeongando): Forty-two administrative units - two mok, ten bu, seventeen gun, eight ryeong, five gam, two yeok, eighteen bo, one military commander, one advance scout, one judge, eleven inspectors, seven myriarchs, twenty-nine temporary officials.

Hamgyeong Province (Hamgyeongdo): Thirty-two administrative units - two mok, fifteen bu, four gun, two gam, three yeok, three bo, one northern military commander, one southern military commander, two advance scouts, twelve inspectors, eighteen myriarchs, twenty-one temporary officials.

　　　　Total of eight provinces

From Uiju at the western border of this country to Liaodong is fifty Korean ri, and to Beijing is two hundred and fifty ri.

Until the time of Taiko's conquest, this country had weak customs and military preparations were not as substantial as they are today. Therefore, all eight provinces were conquered in merely three months. Afterward, they seem to have greatly reflected and repented, and successive generations have studied military arts, so that now their naval and land defenses are well organized.

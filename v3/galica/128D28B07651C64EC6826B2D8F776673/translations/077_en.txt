A lower-class Ainu person wearing animal hides. The headgear they wear includes Japanese snow caps, and they also make their own in their country.

The short swords worn by the middle and lower class people are called "tashiro" or "makiri." All of these are imported from Japan. They are similar to kitchen knives and are all made in Sakata style.

The middle and lower class sword mountings have white scabbards wrapped with cherry bark. Their bodies smell very fishy.

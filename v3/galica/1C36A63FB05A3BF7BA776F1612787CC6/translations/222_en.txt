【Upper Section】
　Prohibition Decree
The disorderly conduct and
violent acts by military personnel of all ranks
are hereby strictly prohibited. If there are any
who disobey this order, they shall be quickly arrested
and brought before the headquarters for reporting.
Severe punishment shall be imposed.
Therefore, as stated herein.

　Notice Board

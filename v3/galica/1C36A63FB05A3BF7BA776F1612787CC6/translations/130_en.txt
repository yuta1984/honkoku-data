The Shukei-shiki records regular tribute as: silk 200 rolls, dark blue cloth 60 tan, light blue cloth 40 tan, yellow cloth 30 tan, with the remainder paid in cloth.
Labor tax was paid in cloth. Products made by chūnan (middle-aged men) were: hemp 400 kin, paper, processed hemp, safflower ○ The Shuzei-shiki records regular tax and public funds of 400,000 bundles each, provincial temple funds 50,000 bundles, Yakushi-ji temple funds 35,000 bundles, Monju-e ceremony funds 2,000 bundles, medicine funds 10,000 bundles, pond and ditch repair funds 40,000 bundles, emergency relief funds 70,000 bundles, and prisoner funds 20,000 bundles.

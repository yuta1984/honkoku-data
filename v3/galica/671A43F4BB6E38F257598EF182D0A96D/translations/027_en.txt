Yin-Yang Stones  Thirty-four

In Suruga Province, upstream of the Oi River, there is a river called Warashina River. In this area, stones called yin-yang stones are produced. The yin stones have the shape of female genitalia, and the yang stones have the shape of male genitalia. These stones come in various sizes, and when collected, they are all black in color. However, the yin stones are red inside and have a core in the center, making them a type of horseshoe stone. They truly resemble a woman's vulva. They are rarely found in the Fuji River and Oi River, and likely flow out from this location.

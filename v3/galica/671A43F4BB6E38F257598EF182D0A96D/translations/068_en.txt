Fox Chisel, Eleven

The thing called "fox chisel" is found in the same place as the fox plane of Noto Province, and I obtained two items at the same time in the same location. The stone nature differs greatly from that of the plane - it is extremely hard and jade-like, with stone veins having wood grain patterns resembling wood. It is thin, long, somewhat flat and round, with the tip slightly widened and double-edged. It must be an object from the age of gods. In both places, the local people call them fox chisels. Among the mineral collecting community, no one yet possesses this object, nor have I heard of them existing in other provinces.

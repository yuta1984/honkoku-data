Stone Knives - Nine

The shape is like a small sword. Length is 2 sun 5 bu, width is 7 bu, or sometimes 1 sun. It has a single edge. This is also a sacred object from ancient times. The two items in Fugen-in Temple's collection are said to have been excavated at Miwa in Yamato Province. One item that I keep was found at Mount Akiha in Shinano Province. They are generally the same objects but differ in shape. All three items are not of superior quality. The shape is as shown in the diagram.

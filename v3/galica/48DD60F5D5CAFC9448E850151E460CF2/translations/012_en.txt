There is also something called the "Eastern Tsubo Monument" (higashi no tsubo no ishibumi). This is located about seventy to eighty ri northeast of this Taga Castle, in the vicinity of Nohechi in Nanbu domain, where there is a place called Tsubo Village. In that village there is a mountain called Tsubo Mountain, and on this mountain there is a stone monument.

■《Eastern Direction》Trailokyavijaya Wisdom King (Gōzanze Myōō)
【Lower section】
Original ground: Bhaiṣajyaguru (Medicine Buddha). The so-called "three worlds" refers to greed, anger, and ignorance. Because he subdues these
three poisons, he is named Trailokyavijaya. His left foot trampling Maheśvara represents the severing of afflictive
obstructions.
　　【Illustration】
His right
foot trampling Umā represents the severing of cognitive obstructions. Since afflictive
obstructions are coarse, they are symbolized by the male and trampled strongly; since cognitive obstructions are subtle, they are symbolized by the female and trampled gently.

【Lower Right】
【Upper section】
■《Western Direction》Yamāntaka Wisdom King (Great威徳 Wisdom King)
【Lower section】
Original ground: Amitābha. According to the Blue Dragon Commentary, he subdues all evil poisonous dragons.
The Light Commentary states: Yamāntaka means that his virtue surpasses all others and he
intimidates all dragons.

【Upper section】
■Buddha Mother Great Peacock Wisdom King
【Lower section】
The Peacock Sutra states: Those who recite the divine mantra of the Peacock Wisdom King will have their life
span extended to a hundred years, their longevity lasting a hundred autumns.

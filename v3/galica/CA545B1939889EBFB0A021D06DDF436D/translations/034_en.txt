【Upper Section】
■Yamagoshi Nyorai (Tathagata of Mountain Crossing)
【Lower Section】
It is told that when the venerable form of Amida appeared on the peak of Yokawa on Mount Hiei, the monk Genshin (Sōzu) worshipped it and immediately drew and preserved the image on raw silk sleeves. In detail, at Yokawa Eshin-in there is the painted image he copied (along with its origin story).

【Lower Right】
【Upper Section】
■Tahō Nyorai (Many Treasures Tathagata)
【Lower Section】
According to the Lotus Sutra, in the past in the eastern direction, in immeasurable thousands of millions of billions of asamkhya worlds, there was a country named Hōjō (Treasure Pure). In it there was a Buddha called Tahō Nyorai (Many Treasures Tathagata).

【Upper Left】
【Upper Section】
■Dainichi of the Diamond Realm (Kongōkai no Dainichi)
【Lower Section】
The Diamond Realm arises from the wisdom of Vairocana Buddha's fruition, and vajra (diamond) is the nature of the bodhi realm. According to the Dainichi-kyō Fruit Treasure Commentary, Vairocana Buddha is another name for the sun, meaning to remove darkness and illuminate everywhere.
【Above the figure】
Wisdom Fist Mudra

【Lower Left】
【Upper Section】
■Dainichi of the Womb Realm (Taizōkai no Dainichi)
【Lower Section】
In Sanskrit it is called Vairocana, which in this country is translated as "Supreme Manifest Wide-Eyed Treasury Tathagata." The Womb Realm originally arises from the afflictions and desires of sentient beings, using the wisdom seal of the Tathagata.

Dharma Realm Concentration Mudra

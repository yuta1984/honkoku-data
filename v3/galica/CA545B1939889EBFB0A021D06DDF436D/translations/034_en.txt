**Amida Coming Over the Mountain**
It is transmitted that when the venerable form of Amida appeared on the peak of Yokawa on Mount Hiei, the monk Genshin reverently observed this and immediately drew and preserved it on silk sleeves. For details, at Yokawa Eshin-in there is the image he copied (together with its origin story).

**Taho Tathagata (Many Treasures Buddha)**
The Lotus Sutra states: "In the distant past, in the eastern direction, beyond immeasurable thousands of millions of billions of asamkhya worlds, there was a land called Treasure Pure, and in it there was a Buddha named Many Treasures."

**Dainichi of the Diamond Realm**
The Diamond Realm arises from the fruition wisdom of Vairocana Buddha. Diamond is the nature of bodhi (enlightenment).
Wisdom Fist Mudra.

Commentary on the Dainichi Sutra: Vairocana Buddha is another name for the sun, meaning the removal of darkness and universal illumination.

**Dainichi of the Womb Realm**
In Sanskrit, Vairocana Buddha; here it is translated as Supreme Manifest Wide-Eyed Treasury Tathagata.
Dharma Realm Concentration Mudra.

The Womb Realm fundamentally arises from the place of sentient beings' afflictions and desires, governing through the wisdom mudra of the Tathagata.

【Upper Right】
【Title】
Four Honored Ones of the South - Vajraratna (Diamond Jewel)
【Description】
Homage to the buddhas Vajraratna and others of the merit-accumulation of all Tathāgatas. The Treatise on Generation states: "Through the principle of great adornment of all Tathāgatas, Vajraratna is generated."

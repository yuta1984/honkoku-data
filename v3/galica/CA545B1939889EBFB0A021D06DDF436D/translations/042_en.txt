【Upper Right】
【Title】
The Four Honored Ones of the Southern Direction - Vajraratna (Diamond Jewel)
【Description】
"We pay reverence to the Buddhas of Vajraratna and others of the merit-accumulation of all Tathāgatas who bestow namo." The Meaning of Generation states: "Through the principle of great adornment of all Tathāgatas, Vajraratna is generated."

【Lower Right】
【Title】
Vajratejas (Diamond Light)
【Description】
"We pay reverence to the Buddhas of Vajratejas and others of the merit-accumulation of all Tathāgatas who bestow namo." The Meaning of Generation states: "Through the principle of great威耀 (awesome radiance) of all Tathāgatas, Vajra-sun is generated."

【Title】
Vajraketu (Diamond Banner)
【Description】
"We pay reverence to the Buddhas of Vajraketu and others of the merit-accumulation of all Tathāgatas." The Meaning of Generation states: "Through the principle of great wish-fulfillment of all Tathāgatas, Vajraketu is generated."

【Lower Left】
【Title】
Vajrahāsa (Diamond Laughter)
【Description】
"We pay reverence to the Buddhas of Vajrahāsa and others of the merit-accumulation of all Tathāgatas." The Meaning of Generation states: "Through the principle of great joy of all Tathāgatas, Vajrahāsa is generated."

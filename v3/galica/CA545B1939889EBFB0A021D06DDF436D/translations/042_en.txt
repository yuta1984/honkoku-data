【Upper Right】
【Title】
The Four Honored Ones of the Southern Direction - Vajraratna (Diamond Jewel)
【Description】
Homage to Vajraratna and other buddhas of the merit accumulation of all Tathāgatas.
The Treatise on Generation states: "Through the principle of the great adornment of all Tathāgatas, the diamond jewel is generated."

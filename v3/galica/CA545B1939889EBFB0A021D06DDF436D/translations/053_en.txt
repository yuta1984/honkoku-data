【Upper Right】
Cintāmaṇicakra Avalokiteśvara (Wish-Fulfilling Wheel Kannon)

The text of the fundamental vow states: "In my vowed great compassion, if there is even one person who cannot fulfill the wish of the two worlds, then I shall remain in false and sinful transgressions, not returning to original enlightenment, and abandon great compassion." Cintāmaṇicakra is the transformation lord of the heaven realm. This wish-fulfilling jewel is the supreme treasure of heaven. ○Great Brahma Profound Avalokiteśvara

【Lower Right】
Cundī Avalokiteśvara

The Śamatha-vipaśyanā states: "Entering into samādhi such as the Kannon Illusion-like Samādhi, all beings in the human realm break through the three obstacles, subdue pride and arrogance, and are shown their Buddha-nature." ○Celestial Being Great Person Kannon

【Upper Left】
Amoghapāśa (Unfailing Lasso)

The Thousand-Armed Sutra states: "The Buddha said, 'This bodhisattva is named Avalokiteśvara Īśvara, and is also named Pāśa (Lasso).'" ○The meaning is to scatter the bait of the wonderful Dharma lotus in the great ocean of birth-and-death, and using the lasso of unfailing mindfulness, fish up the fish of sentient beings and deliver them to the other shore of bodhi.

【Lower Left】
Willow Kannon

Kannon responds with compassionate heart, and in every way corresponds to the desires and inclinations of beings' spiritual capacity, just as a willow bends in the spring breeze. Therefore, this extends across all six Kannon in general.

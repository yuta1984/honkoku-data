【Upper frame - horizontal text】
Six Jizōs

【Upper Right】
■Yotengaka Jizō
Holding a wish-fulfilling jewel in the left hand
The right hand forms the dharma-teaching mudrā
　　【Illustration】
Saves all celestial beings

【Lower Right】
■Hōkōō Jizō  
Holding a staff (khakkhara) in the left hand and wish-granting mudrā in the right hand
　　【Illustration】
Brings rain and ensures the fulfillment of the five grains

【Center Upper】
■Kongōgan Jizō
In the left hand
Yama
banner
The right forms
the debate mudrā
　　【Illustration】
Saves from the suffering of entering hell

【Center Lower】
■Kongōhō Jizō
Holding a precious jewel in the left hand, the right forms the nectar mudrā
　　【Illustration】
Saves the hungry ghost realm

【Upper Left】
■Kongōtō Jizō
Holding a vajra banner in the left hand
The right forms the fearlessness-granting
mudrā
　　【Illustration】
Saves the asura realm

【Lower Left】
■Kongōhi Jizō
Holding a staff (khakkhara) in the left hand
　　【Illustration】
The right forms the guidance mudrā　Saves the animal realm

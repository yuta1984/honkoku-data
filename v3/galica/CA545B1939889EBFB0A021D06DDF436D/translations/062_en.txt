【The Six Jizō (Roku Jizō)】

**Yotenga Jizō (Jizō of Heavenly Blessings)**
Left hand holds the wish-fulfilling jewel (cintāmaṇi); right hand forms the Dharma-preaching mudrā.
Saves beings in the heavenly realm (devas).

**Hōkōō Jizō (Jizō of Radiant Light King)**
Left hand holds the monk's staff (khakkhara); right hand forms the wish-granting mudrā.
Causes rain to fall and brings about the successful harvest of the five grains.

**Kongōgan Jizō (Vajra-Vow Jizō)**
Left hand holds the Yama banner (enmadō); right hand forms the accomplishment mudrā.
Saves beings from the suffering of entering hell.

**Kongōhō Jizō (Vajra-Treasure Jizō)**
Left hand holds the precious jewel; right hand forms the ambrosia mudrā.
Saves beings in the hungry ghost realm (preta realm).

**Kongōtō Jizō (Vajra-Banner Jizō)**
Left hand holds the vajra banner; right hand forms the fearlessness-bestowing mudrā (abhaya).
Saves beings in the asura realm.

**Kongōhi Jizō (Vajra-Compassion Jizō)**
Left hand holds the monk's staff; right hand forms the welcoming mudrā.
Saves beings in the animal realm.

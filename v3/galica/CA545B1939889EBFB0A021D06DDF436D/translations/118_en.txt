King Udayana (Uten-ō)
Properly called King Udayana. In Tang China, it is called "Chushou." The merit of creating Buddhist images begins with King Udayana's long treasury.

Five Realms Underworld Official (Godō Meikan)
Hell, hungry ghosts, animals, humans, and heavenly beings—these are called the Five Realms. The underworld official is the deity who judges good and evil.

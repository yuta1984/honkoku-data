【Upper Right】
【Title】
Mañjuśrī Bodhisattva
【Description】
This is the great teacher from the distant past, the mother of wisdom when all Buddhas of the three times appear in the world. This is the bodhisattva when the Tathāgatas of the ten directions first give rise to bodhicitta.

【Lower Right】
【Title】
Child Mañjuśrī
【Description】
The Nirvana Sutra states that in the ten directions of the world, whether in Buddha-lands or in lands without Buddhas, wherever the Mahāyāna is propagated and expounded, it is all due to the power of Mañjuśrī.

【Upper Left】
【Title】
Samantabhadra Bodhisattva
【Description】
The pervasive spreading of virtuous benefit is called "universal" (fu), and benevolent compassion and wise awakening is called "worthy" (gen). The Gaṇḍavyūha Samantabhadra's Vows states: "I vow that when I am about to reach the end of my life, I will completely remove all obstacles and directly see Buddha Amitābha, immediately attaining rebirth in the Land of Peace and Bliss."

【Lower Left】
【Title】
Child Samantabhadra
【Description】
The Commentary on the Adhyardhaśatikā Prajñāpāramitā states: "Following conditions and reaching everywhere is called 'universal' (fu), and leading into the Buddha-path is called 'worthy' (gen)."

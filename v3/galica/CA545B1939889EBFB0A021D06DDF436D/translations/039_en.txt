【Upper Right】
【Title】
Mañjuśrī Bodhisattva
【Description】
This is the great teacher of the distant past, the mother of wisdom when buddhas of the three periods appear in the world. This is the bodhisattva of the awakening mind of the Tathāgatas of the ten directions.

【Lower Right】
【Title】
Child Mañjuśrī
【Description】
The Nirvana Sutra states: "In the ten directions of worlds, whether in buddha-lands with buddhas or without buddhas, wherever the Mahāyāna is propagated and expounded, this is all through the power of Mañjuśrī."

【Upper Left】
【Title】
Samantabhadra Bodhisattva
【Description】
Virtue and benefit pervading everywhere is called "universal" (fu), and compassionate wisdom-awakening is called "worthy" (ken). The Avatamsaka Sutra's Chapter on Samantabhadra's Practices and Vows states: "I vow that when I approach the time of death, I will completely remove all obstacles and hindrances, directly behold Buddha Amitābha, and immediately attain rebirth in the Land of Peace and Bliss."

【Lower Left】
【Title】
Child Samantabhadra
【Description】
The Commentary on the Adhyardhaśatikā Prajñāpāramitā states: "Following conditions to reach everywhere is called 'universal,' and leading beings into the Buddha-path is called 'worthy.'"

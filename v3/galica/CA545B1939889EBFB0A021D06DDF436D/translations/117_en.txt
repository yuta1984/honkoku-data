Six Great Black Bhikṣu Daikoku (Roku Daikoku Biku Daikoku)
The Buddha's Discourse on Daikoku states: "This bodhisattva attained perfect enlightenment in ancient times and was called Mahāmaṇi-ratna Tathāgata. For this reason, he now comes to the sahā world and manifests as the Great Black Deva (Daikokuten)."

He has 84,000 retainers. If there are sentient beings who make offerings in order to obtain great merit and virtue, he will transform their poverty and make them wealthy persons of great fortune.

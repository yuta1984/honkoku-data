The Twenty-Five Bodhisattvas (continued)

**Muhen-shin Bodhisattva (Limitless Body Bodhisattva) - 25th**
"Limitless Body" is another name for Ksitigarbha Bodhisattva. The Hongan Sutra states: "In sleep and dreams, one sees the limitless body manifested by Ksitigarbha Bodhisattva, and at this person's place, consecration water is bestowed," etc. Truly, being a bodhisattva of profound great compassion, he manifests various emanation bodies to transform beings in the six realms.

**Mujin-i Bodhisattva (Inexhaustible Intent Bodhisattva)**
The voice of this honored one's gatha (verses) awakens ordinary beings from the sleep of delusion. The Mahaprajnaparamita Sutra states that "emptiness is immeasurable," etc.

**Myōon Bodhisattva (Wonderful Sound Bodhisattva)**
Jōchin said: "Using wonderful sound to pervade the ten directions of the world and widely proclaiming the wonderful Dharma, therefore he is named and called 'Wonderful Sound.'"

**Mangetsu Bodhisattva (Full Moon Bodhisattva)**
He holds flower garlands.

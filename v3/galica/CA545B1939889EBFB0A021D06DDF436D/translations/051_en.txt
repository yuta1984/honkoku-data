【Upper Right】
《Twenty-five》 Infinite Body Bodhisattva

Infinite Body is an alternative name for Ksitigarbha Bodhisattva. The Original Vow Sutra states: "In dreams during sleep, one clearly sees Ksitigarbha Bodhisattva manifesting an infinite body. At this person's place, he bestows the water of abhisheka (consecration)." Truly, being a bodhisattva of profound great compassion, he manifests various emanation bodies to teach and transform the six realms.

【Lower Right】
Inexhaustible Intent Bodhisattva

The sound of this venerable one's verses awakens ordinary beings from the sleep of delusion. The Great Perfection of Wisdom Sutra states: "Emptiness is immeasurable."

【Upper Left】
Wonderful Sound Bodhisattva

Master Teichin said: "Using wonderful sound to pervade the ten directions of the world, propagating and proclaiming the wonderful Dharma, therefore he is named and called Wonderful Sound."

【Lower Left】
Full Moon Bodhisattva

He holds a flower garland.

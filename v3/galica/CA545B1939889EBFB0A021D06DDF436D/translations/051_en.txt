【Upper Right】
《Twenty-five》 Limitless Body Bodhisattva

Limitless Body is another name for Ksitigarbha Bodhisattva. The Original Vow Sutra states: "In sleep and dreams, one clearly sees Ksitigarbha Bodhisattva manifesting his limitless body. At that person's place, he bestows the water of consecration," and so forth. Truly, being a bodhisattva of profound great compassion, he manifests various emanation bodies to teach and transform beings in the six realms.

【Lower Right】
Inexhaustible Intent Bodhisattva

The voice of this venerable one's gatha (verse) awakens ordinary beings from the sleep of delusion. The Mahaprajnaparamita Sutra states "emptiness is limitless," and so forth.

【Upper Left】
Wonderful Sound Bodhisattva

Master Teichin said: "Because he uses wonderful sound to universally propagate the wonderful Dharma throughout the ten directions, he is named Wonderful Sound."

【Lower Left】
Full Moon Bodhisattva

He holds a flower garland.

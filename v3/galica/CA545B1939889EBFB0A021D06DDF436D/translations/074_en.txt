■Akṣobhya Buddha 《Fourth day》
【Lower section】
The Vimalakīrti Sūtra states: "There is a land called Abhirati, and the Buddha is called Akṣobhya (Immovable)." According to the commentary, the word "a" means "not" and the word "kṣobhya" means "moving."

■Maitreya Buddha 《Fifth day》
【Lower section】
Also called Ajita. According to the first volume of the Perfect Enlightenment Sūtra Commentary: "Here it is called Compassionate Clan (Jishi). 'Compassionate' is his family name. His personal name is Ajita (Invincible). Because his virtue surpasses others, he is now called by his family name, Maitreya."

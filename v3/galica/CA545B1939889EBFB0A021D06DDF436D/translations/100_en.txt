Emptiness Constellation (Xu)
Eleventh
According to the Tong Kao, the two stars of Xu represent the minister of the household steward. They govern matters of northern settlements, ancestral halls, religious ceremonies, and prayers.

Danger Constellation (Wei)
Eleventh
According to the Tong Kao, the three stars of Wei govern the Heavenly Treasury.

Encampment Constellation (Shi)
Twelfth
According to the Tong Kao, the two stars of Shi represent the officials of the Son of Heaven and govern meritorious deeds.

Wall Constellation (Bi)
Fourteenth
According to the Tong Kao, the two stars of Bi represent the officials of the Son of Heaven and serve as the secret treasury.

Legs Constellation (Kui)
Fifteenth
According to the Tong Kao, the sixteen stars of Kui represent the heavenly armory and govern the use of military force to suppress violence.

Bond Constellation (Lou)
Sixteenth
According to the Tong Kao, the three stars of Lou govern imperial parks and livestock, sacrificial offerings, and suburban sacrificial ceremonies.

【Upper Right】
Roku-ji Avalokiteśvara (Six-Period Kannon)
Manifests in the form of a Layman (Gṛhapati)

【Lower Right】
Fuhi Avalokiteśvara (Universal Compassion Kannon)
Manifests in the form of Maheśvara (the Great Sovereign Heavenly Deity)

【Upper Left】
Marōfu Avalokiteśvara (Horse-Herder's Wife Kannon)
Appeared during the Yuanhe era of Emperor Xianzong of Tang (recorded in the *Guanyin Ganying zhuan*)
Manifests in the form of a Woman

【Lower Left】
Gasshō Avalokiteśvara (Kannon with Hands in Prayer)
Manifests in the form of a Brahmin (Brāhmaṇa)

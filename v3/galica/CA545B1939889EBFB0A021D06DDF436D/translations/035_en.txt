**Seven Buddhas Medicine Buddha Tathagata**
The Seven Buddhas Merit Sutra states: "In the eastern direction, beyond this, there are worlds possessing four Ganges sands of Buddha lands, called Invincible. The Buddha is called Good Name称 Auspicious King Tathagata, who has made eight great vows."

**Treasure Moon Wisdom Majestic Sound Self-Sovereign King Tathagata**
"In the eastern direction, beyond five Ganges sands of Buddha lands, there is a world called Wonderful Treasure Country. Called Treasure Moon Wisdom Majestic Sound Self-Sovereign King Tathagata, who has made eight great vows."

**Golden-Colored Treasure Light Wonderful Practice Achievement King Tathagata**
"In the eastern direction, beyond six Ganges sands of Buddha lands, there is a world called Perfect Fragrant Accumulation Country. The Buddha is called Golden-Colored Treasure Light Wonderful Practice Achievement King Tathagata, who has made four great vows."

**Worry-Free Supreme Auspicious King Tathagata**
"In the eastern direction, beyond seven Ganges sands of Buddha lands, there is a world called Worry-Free Country. The Buddha is named Worry-Free Supreme Auspicious King Tathagata, who has made four great vows."

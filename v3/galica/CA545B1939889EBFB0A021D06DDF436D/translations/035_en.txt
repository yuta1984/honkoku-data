【Upper Frame, Horizontal Text】
Seven Buddha Healers

【Upper Right】
【Upper Section】
1■Zenmyōshō-Kichijōō Nyorai (Good Name Praise Auspicious King Tathagata)
【Lower Section】
The Seven Buddhas Merit Sutra states: In the eastern direction, beyond this world, passing four Ganges rivers' worth of Buddha lands, there is a world called Munōshō (Invincible).
　　【Figure】
That Buddha is called Good Name Praise Auspicious King Tathagata and has made eight great vows.

【Lower Right】
【Upper Section】
2■Hōgetsu-Chigon-Onjizaiō (Treasure Moon Wisdom Stern Sound Sovereign King)
【Lower Section】
In the eastern direction, beyond five Ganges sands' worth of Buddha lands, there is a world called Wonderful Treasure Country. It is called Treasure Moon Wisdom Stern Sound Sovereign King Tathagata and has made eight great vows.
　　【Figure】

【Upper Left】
【Upper Section】
3■Konjiki-Hōkō-Myōgyō-Jōjuō (Golden Treasure Light Wonderful Practice Achievement King)
【Lower Section】
In the eastern direction, beyond six Ganges sands' worth of Buddha lands, there is a world called Perfect Fragrant Accumulation Country. That Buddha is called Golden Treasure Light Wonderful Practice Achievement King Tathagata and has made four great vows.
　　【Figure】

【Lower Left】
【Upper Section】
4■Muu-Saishō-Kichijōō (No Worry Most Excellent Auspicious King)
【Lower Section】
In the eastern direction, beyond seven Ganges sands' worth of Buddha lands, there is a world called No Worry Country. That Buddha is named No Worry Most Excellent Auspicious King Tathagata and has made four great vows.
　　【Figure】

【Upper Horizontal Text】
Seven Buddha Healers (Yakushi)

【Upper Right】
【Upper Section】
■First: Zenmyōshō-Kichijōō Nyorai (Tathagata of Good Reputation and Auspicious King)
【Lower Section】
According to the Seven Buddhas Merit Sutra, in the eastern direction, passing through buddha-lands equal to four Ganges Rivers from this world, there is a world called Munōshō (Invincible). The Buddha is called Zenmyōshō-Kichijōō Nyorai (Good Reputation称Auspicious King Tathagata) and has made eight great vows.

【Lower Right】
【Upper Section】
■Second: Hōgatsu-Chigon'on-Jizaiō (Treasure Moon Dignified Sound Sovereign King)
【Lower Section】
In the eastern direction, passing through buddha-lands equal to five Ganges River sands, there is a world called Myōhō-koku (Wonderful Treasure Country). It is called Hōgatsu-Chigon'on-Jizaiō Nyorai (Treasure Moon Dignified Sound Sovereign King Tathagata) and has made eight great vows.

【Upper Left】
【Upper Section】
■Third: Konjiki-Hōkō-Myōgyō-Jōjuō (Golden Treasure Light Wonderful Practice Accomplishment King)
【Lower Section】
In the eastern direction, passing through buddha-lands equal to six Ganges River sands, there is a world called Enman-Kōshaku-koku (Perfect Fragrant Accumulation Country). The Buddha is called Konjiki-Hōkō-Myōgyō-Jōjuō Nyorai (Golden Treasure Light Wonderful Practice Accomplishment King Tathagata) and has made four great vows.

【Lower Left】
【Upper Section】
■Fourth: Muyu-Saishō-Kichijōō (Worry-Free Supreme Auspicious King)
【Lower Section】
In the eastern direction, passing through buddha-lands equal to seven Ganges River sands, there is a world called Muyu-koku (Worry-Free Country). The Buddha is named Muyu-Saishō-Kichijōō Nyorai (Worry-Free Supreme Auspicious King Tathagata) and has made four great vows.

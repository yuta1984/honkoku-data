Seven Buddha Medicine Masters

【Upper Right】
【Title】
1. Shanming Cheng Jixiang Wang Tathagata (Good Name称 Auspicious King Tathagata)
【Description】
According to the "Seven Buddhas Merit Sutra," in the eastern direction, beyond four Ganges River sands worth of Buddha lands from here, there is a world called "Invincible" (Wu Neng Sheng). The Buddha there is called Good Name Auspicious King Tathagata, who has made eight great vows.

【Lower Right】
【Title】
2. Baoyue Zhiyan Yin Zizai Wang (Precious Moon Wisdom Majestic Sound Sovereign King)
【Description】
In the eastern direction, beyond five Ganges River sands worth of Buddha lands, there is a world called "Wondrous Treasure Country." The Buddha there is called Precious Moon Wisdom Majestic Sound Sovereign King Tathagata, who has made eight great vows.

【Upper Left】
【Title】
3. Jinse Baoguang Miaoxing Chengjiu Wang (Golden Color Precious Light Wonderful Practice Achievement King)
【Description】
In the eastern direction, beyond six Ganges River sands worth of Buddha lands, there is a world called "Perfect Fragrant Accumulated Country." The Buddha there is called Golden Color Precious Light Wonderful Practice Achievement King Tathagata, who has made four great vows.

【Lower Left】
【Title】
4. Wuyou Zuisheng Jixiang Wang (Worry-Free Supreme Auspicious King)
【Description】
In the eastern direction, beyond seven Ganges River sands worth of Buddha lands, there is a world called "Worry-Free Country." The Buddha there is called Worry-Free Supreme Auspicious King Tathagata, who has made four great vows.

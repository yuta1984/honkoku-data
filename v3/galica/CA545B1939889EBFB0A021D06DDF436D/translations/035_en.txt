**Seven Medicine Buddhas**

**1. Good Name-Fame Auspicious King Tathagata**
The Seven Buddhas' Merit Sutra states: "In the eastern direction, passing beyond four Ganges-sand buddha-lands from here, there is a world called Invincible. The Buddha is called Good Name-Fame Auspicious King Tathagata, who made eight great vows."

**2. Treasure Moon Wisdom-Majestic Sound Self-Sovereign King Tathagata**
In the eastern direction, passing beyond five Ganges-sand buddha-lands, there is a world called Wondrous Treasure Country. The Buddha is called Treasure Moon Wisdom-Majestic Sound Self-Sovereign King Tathagata, who made eight great vows.

**3. Golden Treasure Light Wondrous Practice Accomplishment King Tathagata**
In the eastern direction, passing beyond six Ganges-sand buddha-lands, there is a world called Perfect Fragrant Accumulation Country. The Buddha is called Golden Treasure Light Wondrous Practice Accomplishment King Tathagata, who made four great vows.

**4. Sorrowless Supreme Auspicious King Tathagata**
In the eastern direction, passing beyond seven Ganges-sand buddha-lands, there is a world called Sorrowless Country. The Buddha is named Sorrowless Supreme Auspicious King Tathagata, who made four great vows.

1. Good Name-Fame Auspicious King Tathagata (zen-myōshō-kichijō-ō-nyorai)
2. Treasure Moon Wisdom-Majestic Sound Self-Sovereign King (hōgetsu-chigon-on-jizai-ō)
3. Golden Treasure Light Wondrous Practice Accomplishment King (konjiki-hōkō-myōgyō-jōju-ō)
4. Sorrowless Supreme Auspicious King (mu-u-saishō-kichijō-ō)

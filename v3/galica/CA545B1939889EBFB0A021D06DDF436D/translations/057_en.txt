Thirty-Three Forms of Kannon

**Seizu Kannon (Blue-Head Avalokiteśvara)**
Immediately manifesting the body of a Buddha

**Itoku Kannon (Awesome-Virtue Avalokiteśvara)**
The body of a Heavenly Great General

**Enmei Kannon (Life-Prolonging Avalokiteśvara)**
Curses and all manner of poisons
　　※ As seen in the *Xuanyan ji* (*Sengenki*)

**Shūhō Kannon (Many-Treasures Avalokiteśvara)**
The body of an elder (wealthy householder)

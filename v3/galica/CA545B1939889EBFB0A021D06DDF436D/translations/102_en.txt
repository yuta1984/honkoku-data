Ghost Constellation (Gui)
Twenty-third
According to the Tong Kao, the five stars of Gui are the eyes of heaven and govern clear vision and the examination of hidden schemes.

Willow Constellation (Liu)
Twenty-fourth
According to the Tong Kao, the eight stars of Liu are heaven's kitchen steward.

Star Constellation (Xing)
Twenty-fifth
According to the Tong Kao, the seven stars of Xing govern clothing and embroidered patterns.

Extended Net Constellation (Zhang)
Twenty-sixth
According to the Tong Kao, the six stars of Zhang govern precious treasures and also govern heaven's kitchen.

Wings Constellation (Yi)
Twenty-seventh
According to the Tong Kao, the twenty stars of Yi are heaven's music bureau and govern distant guests.

Chariot Constellation (Zhen)
Twenty-eighth
According to the Tong Kao, the four stars of Zhen also govern assistant ministers.

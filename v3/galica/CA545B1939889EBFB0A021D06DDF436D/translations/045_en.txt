【Upper Frame - Horizontal Text】
Twenty-Five Bodhisattvas

【Upper Right】
【Title】
Avalokiteśvara Bodhisattva (Bodhisattva Who Perceives the Sounds of the World)
【Description】
In Sanskrit "Avalokitā Īśvara," translated in Chinese as "Guanzizai" (One Who Perceives Freely). In the Lotus Sutra Commentary it appears as "Bārukiteśa," and in the Collection of Essential Points for Rebirth it is recorded as Amitābha Buddha.
After the True Dharma perishes, Avalokiteśvara will achieve perfect enlightenment and be called "Universal Light Merit Mountain King Tathāgata."

【Lower Right】
【Title】
Bhaiṣajyarāja Bodhisattva (Medicine King Bodhisattva)
【Description】
In Sanskrit called "Agada." This bodhisattva, through the arts of great compassion, freely removes the suffering and illness caused by the various karma of all sentient beings and grants them happiness. Therefore, he is called "Medicine King."
He also burned his body as an offering to the Buddha, and though it burned for 1,200 years, it never was extinguished.

【Upper Left】
【Title】
Mahāsthāmaprāpta Bodhisattva (Great Power Bodhisattva)
【Description】
In Sanskrit "Mahāsthāmaprāpta," translated as "Great Power Attained." In the Śūraṅgama Sūtra: "Through my original causal ground, I entered the tolerance of non-birth by means of Buddha-mindfulness, and now in this realm I gather those who practice Buddha-mindfulness and cause them to return to the Pure Land."
In the Vimalakīrti Sūtra, he is called "Attainer of Great Power."

【Lower Left】
【Title】
Bhaiṣajyasamudgata Bodhisattva (Superior Medicine Bodhisattva)
【Description】
In the past time of Vaiḍūryaprabharāja Buddha, there was a monk called "Sūryagarbha." In order to listen to the Dharma teaching of an elder called "Nakṣatraprabha," he offered various medicines including myrobalan. Therefore he had a younger brother called Medicine King.
Called "Lightning Brightness," because he offered the supreme medicine of ghee, he was named "Superior Medicine."

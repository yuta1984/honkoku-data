【Lower Right】
【Upper Section】
■Niman-Tōmyō-butsu (Twenty Thousand Lamp Light Buddha)
【Lower Section】
According to Hokke Teichin, what twenty thousand represents is ten thousand practices and ten thousand virtues. Also, if one places the thousand suchnesses in ten virtues, it becomes ten thousand. Dividing this into expedient and true teachings makes twenty thousand.

【Upper Right】
【Title】
Sun-Moon Lamp Light Buddha
【Description】
The first volume of the Lotus Sutra states: "In the immeasurable, limitless, inconceivable, countless eons of the past, at that time there was a Buddha called Sun-Moon Lamp Light Buddha, the World-Honored One."

【Lower Right】
【Title】
Twenty Thousand Lamp Light Buddhas
【Description】
According to the "Hokke Teichin," the twenty thousand represents ten thousand practices and ten thousand virtues. Also, if one places the thousand suchnesses in the ten virtues, it becomes ten thousand. When this is divided between expedient means (provisional teachings) and reality (true teachings), it becomes twenty thousand.

【Upper Left】
【Title】
Thirty Thousand Lamp Light Buddhas
【Description】
These represent the three truths of emptiness, provisional existence, and the middle way. In the Lotus Sutra, it mentions Twenty Thousand Lamp Light Buddhas.

【Lower Left】
【Title】
Amoghasiddhi Buddha (Infallible Success Buddha)
【Description】
According to the "Treatise on Bodhicitta," "The Amoghasiddhi Buddha of the northern direction is called Karma Wisdom because he accomplishes the All-Accomplishing Wisdom."

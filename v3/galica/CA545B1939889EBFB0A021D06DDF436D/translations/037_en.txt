**Sun-Moon Lamp-Light Buddha**
The first volume of the Lotus Sutra states: "In the past, immeasurable, boundless, inconceivable, infinite kalpas ago, at that time there was a Buddha called Sun-Moon Lamp-Light Buddha, who was called World-Honored One."

**Twenty Thousand Lamp-Light Buddha**
According to Hokke Teichin's interpretation, what twenty thousand represents is the ten thousand practices. Also, in the Lotus Sutra it is goodness. If one places the thousand tathagatas in the ten good deeds, it becomes ten thousand. When this is applied to provisional and true teachings, it becomes twenty thousand.

**Thirty Thousand Lamp-Light Buddha**
These are the three truths of emptiness, provisional existence, and the middle way. In the Lotus Sutra, there is the Ten Thousand Lamp-Light Buddha.
[Lamp-Light Seal]

**Amoghasiddhi Buddha (Infallible Success Buddha)**
The Treatise on Bodhicitta states: "The Northern Amoghasiddhi Buddha derives its name from the accomplishing wisdom, which is called karma-wisdom."

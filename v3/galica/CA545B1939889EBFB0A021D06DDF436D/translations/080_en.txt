Semui Bodhisattva (Abhayaṃdada)
22nd Day

The inner realization is Kanzeon Bodhisattva (Avalokiteśvara).

Inari Daimyōjin
22nd Day

Enshrined in Kii County, Yamashiro Province. When Kōbō Daishi founded Tōji Temple during the Daidō era, this deity manifested at the temple gate.

Daiseishi Bodhisattva (Mahāsthāmaprāpta)
23rd Day

The third prince of King Mujōnen. The Vimalakīrti Sutra states: "Daiseishi has great power, flies to all countries in the ten directions, causes six kinds of trembling, and brings rest to the evil realms."

Sumiyoshi Daimyōjin
23rd Day

Enshrined in Sumiyoshi County, Settsu Province. The original ground is Shō Kannon (Sacred Avalokiteśvara).

Jizō Bodhisattva (Kṣitigarbha)
24th Day

The commentary on the Daśacakra Sutra says: "Ji (earth) means beginning, zō (storehouse) means end." The Jūō Sutra says: "Named and called Gakuka-jōjizai Buddha."

Gion Daimyōjin
24th Day

This is Susanoo-no-mikoto, called Gozu Tennō (Ox-Head Heavenly King). Located in Yasaka village, Yamashiro Province. One shrine is Gozu Tennō, one shrine is Inadahime (called Shōshō), and one shrine is Hachiōji.

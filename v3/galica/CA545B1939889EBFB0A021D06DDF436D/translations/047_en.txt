【Upper Right】
Ākāśagarbha Bodhisattva

Being the dharma-body of the Five Dharmas and Five Buddhas, there is nothing in the realm of empty space and dharma-realm where enlightenment cannot be attained. Just as empty space is called a "treasury," that void realm has no boundaries or locations. This venerable one likewise has infinite wisdom of great compassion for benefiting beings, and is named by comparing this limitless quality to the treasury of empty space.

【Lower Right】
Virtue Treasury Bodhisattva

This bodhisattva is a great being possessing the merit of vast great compassion. Following the myriad conditions of each and every sentient being, this bodhisattva opens the millions of storehouses of merit and great compassion to guide all sentient beings. Therefore, this bodhisattva is called Virtue Treasury Bodhisattva.

【Upper Left】
Jewel Treasury Bodhisattva

Jewels are various precious treasures. Treasury means storehouse. For example, the blessed precious treasures of the world are stored in earthen storehouses to provide for all matters. This great being likewise opens the great treasure house of merit and great compassion according to the wishes and aspirations of all sentient beings, thereby saving them.

【Lower Left】
Gold Treasury Bodhisattva

Gold has an indestructible essential nature - it does not drown when entering water, nor does it change color when entering fire. This bodhisattva, responding to the merit of reciting names in the causal stage, becomes part of the sacred assembly of the Western Pure Land and obtains the virtue of indestructible gold. Therefore called Gold Treasury.

【Upper section】
■Aizen Myoo (Love-Tinted Wisdom King)
【Lower section】
The Diamond Dharma Sutra states: His body color is like the radiance of the sun, dwelling in the blazing wheel, with three
eyes glaring wrathfully, wearing a lion crown on his topknot, in the form of bristling fury, and also
holding a five-pronged vajra and
　　【Illustration】
a hook placed on
the lion's crown, with five-colored flower garlands hanging down and celestial sashes covering him.

【Lower Left】
【Upper section】
■Two-Headed Aizen Myoo
【Lower section】
At the ears, the left hand holds a golden bell, and the right hand grasps a five-peaked vajra. Next,
the left holds a diamond bow and the right a diamond arrow. Like the light of many stars shooting forth,
he achieves and is capable of great
love-tinted
dharma.
　　【Illustration】
The right lotus is like a striking force, swiftly destroying all evil-hearted sentient beings,
and below the lotus there is a treasure vase with precious things flowing out from both sides.

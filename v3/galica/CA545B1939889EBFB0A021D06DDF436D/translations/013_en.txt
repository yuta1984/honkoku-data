Master Zhao said: "The dharmakaya is without form, yet various forms all manifest in response. If this is so, then from the buddhas above down to the eight classes of heavenly dragons, none are not the formless dharmakaya." The compilation of this book perhaps lies in this intention. Previously, [images] entrusted to wood have circulated in the world for a long time. Therefore, the sacred forms have become worn and damaged, the hand gestures are difficult to distinguish, smiles and anger cannot be discerned, and those who view them suffer from this.

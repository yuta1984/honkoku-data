【Upper Right】
【Upper Section】
Destroyer of Evil Realms
【Lower Section】
The Ten Kings Sutra states "destroys evil realms" and also says "removes all evil realms." This is the honored one who dwells in the great compassion samadhi and destroys the three evils such as hell.
　　【Illustration】

【Lower Right】
【Upper Section】
Superior Practice of Limitless Action
【Lower Section】
The Hōshō-ki states: "Superior practice means practicing the supreme great dharma. Limitless means that merit is boundless."
　　【Illustration】

【Center Upper】
【Upper Section】
Dharani Bodhisattva
【Lower Section】
The Yūyo Mandala Commentary calls this the "inner realization Avalokiteśvara." This bodhisattva particularly has compassion for hell-dwelling beings and guides all sentient beings with karmic connections.
　　【Illustration】

【Center Lower】
【Upper Section】
Pure Practice and Established Practice
【Lower Section】
Pure practice is so named because of the purity of true suchness. Established practice is so named because the three thousand [realms] are perfectly manifest. These are the four bodhisattvas of original transformation in the Lotus Sutra commentary.

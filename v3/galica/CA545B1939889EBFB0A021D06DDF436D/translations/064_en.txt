【Upper Right】
【Upper section】
Destroyer of Evil Destinies
【Lower section】
The Ten Kings Sutra says "destroys evil destinies" and also says "removes all evil destinies." Dwelling in the Great Compassion Samadhi,
　　【Illustration】
this is an honored one who destroys the three evils such as hell

【Lower Right】
【Upper section】
Superior Practice of Limitless Practice
【Lower section】
In the Hōshō-ki it states: "Superior Practice means practicing the supreme great Dharma.
　　【Illustration】
Limitless means that merit is limitless."

【Center Upper】
【Upper section】
Dhāraṇī Bodhisattva
【Lower section】
In the Yūyo Mandala Commentary it says "inner realization Avalokiteśvara," and also this
　　【Illustration】
bodhisattva especially has compassion for hell beings,
and furthermore guides sentient beings with karmic connections

【Center Lower】
【Upper section】
Pure Practice and Peaceful Establishment Practice
【Lower section】
Pure Practice is so named because True Suchness is pure
　　【Illustration】
Peaceful Establishment is so named because the three thousand [worlds] are manifest.
These are the four bodhisattvas of original transformation in the Lotus Commentary

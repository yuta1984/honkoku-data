【Upper Right】
【Title】
Four Honored Ones of the West - Vajradharma (Diamond Dharma)
【Description】
Namo Sarva-Tathāgata-Prajñā-Dvāra Vajradharma and all Buddhas.
The Treatise on Generation states: "Through the wisdom of freedom and purity of all Tathāgatas, Vajradharma is generated."

【Lower Right】
【Title】
Vajratīkṣṇa (Diamond Sharpness)
【Description】
Namo Sarva-Tathāgata-Prajñā-Dvāra Vajratīkṣṇa and all Buddhas.
The Treatise on Generation states: "Through the wisdom by which all Tathāgatas eternally cut off karmic impressions, Vajratīkṣṇa is generated."

【Upper Left】
【Title】
Vajrahetu (Diamond Cause)
【Description】
Namo Sarva-Tathāgata-Prajñā-Dvāra Vajrahetu and all Buddhas.
The Treatise on Generation states: "Through the wisdom of turning the great Dharma wheel of all Tathāgatas, Vajrahetu is generated."

【Lower Left】
【Title】
Vajrabhāṣa (Diamond Speech)
【Description】
Namo Sarva-Tathāgata-Prajñā-Dvāra Vajrabhāṣa and all Buddhas.
The Treatise on Generation states: "Through the wisdom that transcends verbal expression and conceptual elaboration of all Tathāgatas, Vajra Secret Speech is also generated."

Sixteen Great Bodhisattvas

Four Honored Ones of the West:
Vajradharma (kongōhō)
Vajratīkṣṇa (kongōri)
Vajrahetu (kongōin)
Vajrabhāṣa (kongōgo)

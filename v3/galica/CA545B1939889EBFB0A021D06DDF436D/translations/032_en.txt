【Upper Right】
■Middle Grade, Middle Birth
Those of the middle grade, middle birth are those who uphold various precepts. Amida and monks appear in the sky before the practitioner, holding lotus flowers, and a voice says: "Because you follow the teachings of the buddhas, I have come to welcome you and enable your birth in that land."

【Lower Right】
■Middle Grade, Inferior Birth
Those of the middle grade, inferior birth are those who, at the time of death, encounter a good spiritual friend who praises and explains the blissful aspects of Amida Buddha's land. They also explain the forty-eight vows of Dharmākara Bodhisattva, and upon hearing this, in the time it takes to bend and extend an arm, they are immediately born in the Land of Ultimate Bliss.

【Upper Left】
■Lower Grade, Superior Birth
Those of the lower grade, superior birth are those who have committed various evil karma and, when their life is about to end, encounter a good spiritual friend who praises the Mahayana, and a wise person who teaches them to recite "Namo Amida Butsu." The practitioner immediately sees transformation buddhas and is born in the jeweled pond.

【Lower Left】
■Lower Grade, Middle Birth
Those of the lower grade, middle birth are monks who have broken precepts and adorned themselves. At the time of death, they encounter a good spiritual friend, and through mindfulness of Buddha, fire transforms into wind, blowing celestial flowers, and transformation buddhas and bodhisattvas come to welcome them. This person immediately attains rebirth.

**Middle Grade, Middle Birth**
Those of the middle grade, middle birth are those who uphold the various monks. Holding lotus flowers, they appear before the practitioner in the sky. There is a voice that says, "Following the teachings of the various Buddhas, therefore I come to welcome you to be reborn in that land."

**Middle Grade, Lowest Birth**
Those of the middle grade, lowest birth are those who encounter a good spiritual friend at the time of death, who praises and explains the joyful matters of Amida Buddha's land. They also expound the forty-eight vows of Dharmakara Bhikkhu. After hearing this, in the time it takes to bend and extend an arm, they are immediately reborn in the Land of Bliss.

**Lowest Grade, Highest Birth**
Those of the lowest grade, highest birth are those who have committed evil karma, and when their life is about to end, they encounter a good spiritual friend who praises the wisdom of the Mahayana for them. They are also taught to recite "Namo Amida Buddha." The practitioner immediately sees transformation Buddhas and is reborn in the jeweled pond.

**Lowest Grade, Middle Birth**
Those of the lowest grade, middle birth are those who have despised monks and defiled themselves. At the time of death, a good spiritual friend appears and encourages them to recite the Buddha's name. The fires of hell transform into cool wind, scattering the flowers of the heavens, and transformation Buddhas and bodhisattvas come to welcome them, and this person is immediately reborn.

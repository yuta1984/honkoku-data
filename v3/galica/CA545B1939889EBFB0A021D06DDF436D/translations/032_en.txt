**Middle Grade, Middle Birth**
Those who uphold the precepts of monks. [Buddhas] appear before the practitioner in the sky, holding lotus flowers.

A voice speaks accordingly: "Because of the Buddha's teachings, I have come to welcome you. Be born in that land."

**Middle Grade, Inferior Birth**
Those who encounter a good spiritual friend at the time of death, who praises and explains the joyful aspects of Amida Buddha's land.

[The friend] also explains the forty-eight vows of Dharmakara Bhikṣu. Upon hearing this, in the time it takes to bend and extend one's arm, one is immediately born in the Land of Ultimate Bliss.

**Inferior Grade, Superior Birth**
Those who have committed evil karma and, when their life is about to end, encounter a good spiritual friend who praises the wisdom of the Mahayana and further teaches them to recite the name.

Practitioners who chant "Namo Amida Butsu" immediately see transformation Buddhas and are born in the jeweled pond.

**Inferior Grade, Middle Birth**
Those who have despised monks and defiled the Buddhist teachings. At the time of death, a good spiritual friend appears and teaches them to recite the Buddha's name. The fires of hell transform into a cool breeze.

[The breeze] scatters the flowers of various heavens, and transformation Buddhas and bodhisattvas welcome and receive this person. They are immediately reborn.

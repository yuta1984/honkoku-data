【Upper Section】
■Middle Grade, Middle Birth (Chūbon-chūshō)
【Lower Section】
Those of middle grade, middle birth are those who uphold various precepts. Amida appears with monks in the sky before the practitioner, holding lotuses, and a voice proclaims: "Because you have followed the teachings of all Buddhas, I have come to welcome you and cause you to be born in that land."

【Lower Right】
【Upper Section】
■Middle Grade, Lower Birth (Chūbon-geshō)
【Lower Section】
Those of middle grade, lower birth encounter a good spiritual friend at their deathbed, who praises and explains the joyful aspects of Amida Buddha's land for them. They also explain the forty-eight vows of Dharmakara Bhikkhu. Upon hearing this, in the time it takes to bend and extend one's arm, they are immediately born in the Land of Bliss.

【Upper Left】
【Upper Section】
■Lower Grade, Superior Birth (Gebon-jōshō)
【Lower Section】
Those of lower grade, superior birth have committed many evil deeds, but when their life is about to end, they encounter a good spiritual friend who praises the wisdom of the Mahayana for them and teaches them to recite "Namo Amida Butsu." The practitioner immediately sees a transformation Buddha and is born in the jeweled pond.

【Lower Left】
【Upper Section】
■Lower Grade, Middle Birth (Gebon-chūshō)
【Lower Section】
Those of lower grade, middle birth are monks who have broken precepts and who adorn themselves. At the time of death, hellish fires appear, but when they encounter a good spiritual friend and recite the Buddha's name, the fire transforms into wind that blows celestial flowers, and transformation Buddhas and bodhisattvas come to welcome this person, who immediately attains birth in the Pure Land.

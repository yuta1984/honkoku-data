Thirty-Three Forms of Kannon

**Gyoran Kannon (Fish-Basket Avalokiteśvara)**
Or encountering evil rākṣasas

**Tokuo Kannon (Virtue-King Avalokiteśvara)**
The body of Brahmā

**Suigetsu Kannon (Water-Moon Avalokiteśvara)**
The body of a pratyekabuddha

**Ichiyo Kannon (Single-Leaf Avalokiteśvara)**
The body of a government official

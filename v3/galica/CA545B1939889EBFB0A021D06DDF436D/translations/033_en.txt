**Inferior Grade, Inferior Birth**
Those who have committed the five grave offenses. At the time of death, a good spiritual friend teaches them to recite the Buddha's name.念 by 念, the sins of life and death are removed, and immediately a golden lotus flower appears.

Shining like the sun disk, it remains before that person, and they immediately attain rebirth in the Land of Ultimate Bliss.

**Amida of Wonderful Discerning Wisdom**
The "Compendium of Essential Sutras" states: The father is the Great Wheel-Turning Sacred King Getsujo, and the mother is Shusho Myogan (Supremely Excellent Beautiful Face).
Also, together with the second Harishikemi-da, the name is called Kaushika. Renouncing the position of royalty and entering monastic life, he is named Dharmakara Bhikṣu.

**Mandala Amida**
The mudra of the three bodies: reveal the right palm and conceal the left palm. There is a swastika on the chest. Join the thumb with the ring finger and middle finger.

This is the mudra of the two bodies - the Dharma body and the reward body. Joining the thumb and index finger of the right hand is the mudra of the response body.

**Amida of Five Kalpas of Contemplation**
The three characters "Immeasurable Life" represent the three truths of emptiness, provisional existence, and the middle way of all Buddhas of the three periods.

The name resulting from five kalpas of contemplation is the fundamental source of the eighty thousand dharma treasures.

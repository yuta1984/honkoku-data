【Upper Section】
■Lower Grade, Lower Birth
【Lower Section】
Lower grade, lower birth refers to those who have committed the five grave sins. At the time of death, when a good spiritual friend recites the Buddha's name, the sins of birth and death are removed moment by moment, and immediately a golden lotus flower appears before that person like the sun disc, enabling immediate rebirth in the Pure Land.

【Lower Right】
【Upper Section】
■Amida of the Wisdom of Wonderful Observation
【Lower Section】
According to the "Collection of Essential Scriptures," his father was King Moon-Supreme Wheel-Turning Sage, his mother was Supremely Wonderful Countenance, also called Red Lotus Color Amida. His name was called Kauśika, and abandoning his royal position, he became a monk called Dharma Treasury Bhikṣu.

【Upper Left】
【Upper Section】
■Mandala Amida
【Lower Section】
The mudrā of the three bodies shows the right palm and conceals the left palm. There is a swastika character on the chest, joining the thumb with the ring finger and middle finger. This is the mudrā of the Dharma Body and Reward Body. Joining the thumb and index finger of the right hand is the mudrā of the Response Body.

【Lower Left】
【Upper Section】
■Amida of Five Kalpa Contemplation
【Lower Section】
The three characters "Immeasurable Life" represent the three truths of emptiness, provisional existence, and the middle way of all Buddhas of the three times. The sacred name of five kalpa contemplation is the root source of the eighty-four thousand dharma treasury.

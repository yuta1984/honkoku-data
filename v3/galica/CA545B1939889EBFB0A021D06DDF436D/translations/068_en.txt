【Lower Left】
【Upper section】
■Kurikara Fudo (Immovable One)
【Lower section】
According to the teaching of the Kurikara Sutra, it is seen that he manifests the Kurikara
form in order to subdue the ninety-five types of great heretics.
　　【Illustration】
Some say that the sword in the mouth is the sword of the main deity's right side, and the dragon is the
rope of the left side. Thus, the sword and rope represent the two dharmas of male-female and concentration-wisdom.

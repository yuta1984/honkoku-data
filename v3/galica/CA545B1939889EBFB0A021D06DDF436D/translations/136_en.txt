The Thirteen Buddhas

1. Fudō Myōō (Acalanātha) - First Seven Days Memorial Buddha

2. Shaka Nyorai (Śākyamuni Buddha) - Second Seven Days

3. Monju Bosatsu (Mañjuśrī Bodhisattva) - Third Seven Days

4. Fugen Bosatsu (Samantabhadra Bodhisattva) - Fourth Seven Days

5. Jizō Bosatsu (Kṣitigarbha Bodhisattva) - Fifth Seven Days

6. Miroku Bosatsu (Maitreya Bodhisattva) - Sixth Seven Days

Flower Light Bodhisattva
【Lower section】
Flower Light means that other teachers saw the raining flowers and emitting light in the Introductory Chapter and achieved enlightenment, hence called Flower Light. [This bodhisattva] dwells in the World Free from Defilement.
　　【Illustration】
It is also said that this country is named so because flowers and plants are abundant.
This appears in the Lotus Scientific Commentary.

**Sixteen Great Bodhisattvas**

**Vajrasattva (Diamond Being)**
Homage to all Tathāgatas' bodhi-mind Vajrasattva and others, who exhaust empty space and pervade the dharma realm with the same unified essence, all the buddhas whose bodies were born in the Vajra realm [Four Honored Ones of the East]

**Vajrarāja (Diamond King)**
Homage to all Tathāgatas' bodhi-mind Vajrarāja and others, who exhaust empty space and pervade the dharma realm with the same unified essence, all the buddhas born in the Vajra realm

**Vajrarāga (Diamond Love)**
Homage to all Tathāgatas' bodhi-mind Vajrarāga and other buddhas

The Treatise on Arising states: "Diamond Love that arises in the unstained pure essence of all Tathāgatas' bodhi"

**Vajrasādhu (Diamond Joy)**
Homage to all Tathāgatas' bodhi-mind Vajrasādhu and other buddhas

The Treatise on Arising states: "Diamond Excellence (sādhu) that arises wherever all Tathāgatas are praised and recited"

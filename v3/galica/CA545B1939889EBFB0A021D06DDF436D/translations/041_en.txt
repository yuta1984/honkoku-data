【Upper Frame - Horizontal Text】
Sixteen Great Bodhisattvas

【Upper Right】
【Title】
Vajrasattva
【Description】
Taking refuge in Namo Sarva-Tathāgata-Bodhicitta Vajrasattva and others, who throughout limitless space and pervading the dharma realm share the same essence-nature. All the Buddhas of the body born in the Vajra realm.
【Lower Left of Figure】
Four Honored Ones of the East

【Lower Right】
【Title】
Vajrarāja (Diamond King)
【Description】
Namo Sarva-Tathāgata-Bodhicitta Vajrarāja and others, all Buddhas born in the Vajra realm that exhausts empty space, pervades the dharma realm, and shares the same essence-nature.

【Upper Left】
【Title】
Vajrarāga (Diamond Love)
【Description】
Namo Sarva-Tathāgata-Bodhicitta Vajrarāga and other Buddhas. The Treatise on Generation states: "In the undefiled pure essence of the bodhicitta of all Tathāgatas, Diamond Love is generated."

【Lower Left】
【Title】
Vajrasādhu (Diamond Joy)
【Description】
Namo Sarva-Tathāgata-Bodhicitta Vajrasādhu and other Buddhas. The Treatise on Generation states: "In the essence of all Tathāgatas' praise wherever it may be, Diamond善哉 (excellent/well done) is born."

Sixteen Great Bodhisattvas

【Upper Right】
【Title】
Vajrasattva
【Description】
Homage to Vajrasattva and others of the bodhicitta of all Tathāgatas, who, sharing the same essence throughout infinite space and the entire dharma realm, are the bodies of all buddhas born in the Vajra Realm.

【Lower Right】
【Title】
Vajrarāja (Diamond King)
【Description】
Homage to Vajrarāja and others of the bodhicitta of all Tathāgatas, who share the same essence throughout infinite space and the entire dharma realm, and are all buddhas born in the Vajra Realm.

【Upper Left】
【Title】
Vajrarāga (Diamond Love)
【Description】
Homage to the buddhas Vajrarāga and others of the bodhicitta of all Tathāgatas. The Treatise on Generation states: "From the unstained and pure essence of the bodhicitta of all Tathāgatas, Vajrarāga is generated."

【Lower Left】
【Title】
Vajrasādhu (Diamond Joy)
【Description】
Homage to the buddhas Vajrasādhu and others of the bodhicitta of all Tathāgatas. The Treatise on Generation states: "From the essence of the praise-worthy nature of all Tathāgatas, Vajra-sādhu (excellent/well done) is generated."

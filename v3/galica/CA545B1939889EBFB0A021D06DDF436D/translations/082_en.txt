■Dainichi Nyorai (Mahāvairocana)《28th day》
【Lower Section】
Regarding the teaching lord of the Land of Tranquil Light (Jakukō-do), there are three divisions of Dainichi. This refers to the Dainichi of Konkai-so-shitsu (Gold-Matrix-Su-Siddham).

【Lower Right】
【Upper Section】
■Hyōsu Daimyōjin《28th day》
【Lower Section】
Located in Hyōsu village, Yasu District, Ōmi Province. The honji is Fudō (Acala), and also Dainichi.

【Upper Center】
【Upper Section】
■Yakuō (Medicine King) Bodhisattva《29th day》
【Lower Section】
In the commentary of Sannō-in: "Yakuō is none other than Fudō, which is the manifestation of Daitsū-chishō (Mahābhijñā-jñānābhibhū). The Fudō Fire-Light Samādhi is precisely the burning of the body."

【Lower Center】
【Upper Section】
■Nauka Daimyōjin《29th day》
【Lower Section】
Ōmi Province. The honji is Amida (Amitābha).

【Upper Left】
【Upper Section】
■Shakyamuni Buddha《30th day》
【Lower Section】
In the commentary on the Ninnō-kyō (Benevolent King Sutra): "Shaka" is translated as "Nōnin" (Capable of Benevolence). Nōnin means compassion. Because he is able to give compassion to others, he is called "jin" (benevolent).

【Lower Left】
【Upper Section】
■Kibi Daimyōjin《30th day》
【Lower Section】
Kaya District, Bitchū Province. This is Prince Kibitsuhiko, son of Emperor Kōrei. The honji is Kokūzō (Ākāśagarbha).

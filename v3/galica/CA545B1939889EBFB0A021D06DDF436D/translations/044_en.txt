【Upper Right】
【Title】
The Four Honored Ones of the Northern Direction - Vajrakarma (Diamond Action)
【Description】
Homage to Vajrakarma and all buddhas of the great vigor of all Tathāgatas.
The Treatise on Generation states: "From the gate of skillful craftsmanship of all Tathāgatas, the diamond action is generated."

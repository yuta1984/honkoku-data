【Upper Right】
【Title】
Four Honored Ones of the North - Vajrakarma (Diamond Action)
【Description】
Namo Sarva-Tathāgata-Mahāvīrya Vajrakarma and all Buddhas.
The Treatise on Generation states: "From the gate of skillful arts and crafts of all Tathāgatas, Vajrakarma is generated."

【Lower Right】
【Title】
Vajrarakṣa (Diamond Protection)
【Description】
Namo Sarva-Tathāgata-Mahāvīrya Vajrarakṣa and all Buddhas.
The Treatise on Generation states: "From the gate of great compassionate armor of all Tathāgatas, Vajrarakṣa is generated."

【Upper Left】
【Title】
Vajradaṃṣṭra (Diamond Fang)
【Description】
Namo Sarva-Tathāgata-Mahāvīrya Vajradaṃṣṭra and all Buddhas.
The Treatise on Generation states: "From the gate of fearless subjugation of all Tathāgatas, Vajradaṃṣṭra is generated."

【Lower Left】
【Title】
Vajramuṣṭi (Diamond Fist)
【Description】
Namo Sarva-Tathāgata-Mahāvīrya Vajramuṣṭi and all Buddhas.
The Treatise on Generation states: "From the gate of sustaining accomplishment of all Tathāgatas, Vajramuṣṭi is also generated."

Sixteen Great Bodhisattvas

Four Honored Ones of the North:
Vajrakarma (kongōgō)
Vajrarakṣa (kongōgo)
Vajradaṃṣṭra (kongōge)
Vajramuṣṭi (kongōken)

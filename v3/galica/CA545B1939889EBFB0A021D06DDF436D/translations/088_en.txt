■Kōshin Shōmen Kongō (Blue-faced Vajra of the Kōshin Belief)
【Lower Section】
According to the Laozi Sanshi-kyō (Three Corpses Sutra), from the time a person is born, there are beings called Three Corpses (sanshi) that never leave the body and seek to harm one's life. On kōshin nights, they report sins to heaven. However, if one stays awake and calls out the names of the Three Corpses, disasters will be removed and fortune will come. After midnight, facing south, bow twice and recite: Upper Corpse, also called Peng Su (blue color), Middle Corpse (white color), Lower Corpse, also called Peng Jiao (red color), Peng Hou-zi, Peng Chang-zi, Ming Er-zi - this should be chanted three times.

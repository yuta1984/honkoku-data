The Twelve Divine Generals of Yakushi (Medicine Buddha)

Vikarāla Daiśō (Great General) - Zodiac sign: Rat - Holds a three-pronged vajra - Original ground: Śākyamuni

Caturāla Daiśō (Great General) - Zodiac sign: Ox - Holds a sword - Original ground: Vajrapāṇi

Śintarāla Daiśō (Great General) - Zodiac sign: Tiger - Holds a jeweled orb and staff - Original ground: Samantabhadra

Makorāla Daiśō (Great General) - Zodiac sign: Rabbit - Holds an axe - Original ground: Yakushi (Medicine Buddha)

Pairāla Daiśō (Great General) - Zodiac sign: Dragon - Holds a bow and arrow - Original ground: Mañjuśrī

Intarāla Daiśō (Great General) - Zodiac sign: Snake - Holds a spear - Original ground: Kṣitigarbha

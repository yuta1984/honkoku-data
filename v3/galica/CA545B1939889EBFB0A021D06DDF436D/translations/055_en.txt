Thirty-Three Forms of Kannon

**Byakue Kannon (White-Robed Avalokiteśvara)**
The body of a bhikṣu or bhikṣuṇī

**Renga Kannon (Lotus-Reclining Avalokiteśvara)**
The body of right response

**Takimi Kannon (Waterfall-Viewing Avalokiteśvara)**
The pit of fire transforms into a pond

**Seraku Kannon (Joy-Bestowing Avalokiteśvara)**
Dwelling in the void like the sun

The Great Enlightened World-Honored One, with his great compassionate heart, skillfully guides through expedient means, causing those ordinary people to pursue gradual cultivation. One day, a visitor came who had illustrated the sacred images of various Buddhas and bodhisattvas, as well as the heavenly deities and gods, and requested that I write a preface to be published. I arranged my sleeves, sat upright, and respectfully viewed them from the beginning.

【Upper Section】
■Buddha Shakyamuni at Birth
【Lower Section】
Having King Śuddhodana of Kapilavastu in Central India as father and Lady Maya as mother. Born in the 30th year of King Śuddhodana's reign, on the 8th day of the 4th month, at the hour of the Tiger. He took seven steps in each of the four directions, pointed above and below with his hands, and declared: "In the four directions, above and below, I alone am the World-Honored One." He was called Sarvārthasiddha. Details can be found in the Fayuan Zhulin, Keiko Ryaku, and other texts.

【Lower Right】
【Upper Section】
■Buddha Shakyamuni Leaving the Mountain
【Lower Section】
At nineteen years old, on the 8th day of the 2nd month, the prince left the royal palace and went to Mount Dandoku, where he practiced severe austerities for twelve years. At thirty years old, at the Vajra Seat in the Bodhimanda in Magadha, in the 33rd year of King Zhao (year of Wuyin), on the 8th day of the 12th month, when the morning star appeared, he suddenly achieved great enlightenment and attained perfect awakening.

【Upper Left】
【Upper Section】
■Buddha Shakyamuni in Perfect Enlightenment
【Lower Section】
The Avatamsaka teaching for thirty-seven days, the Agama teaching for twelve years, the Vaipulya and Prajnaparamita teachings for thirty years, the Lotus Sutra teaching for eight years, and the Nirvana Sutra teaching for one day and night. The Blue Cliff Record states: Shakyamuni lived in the world for forty-nine years, holding 360 assemblies and expounding both sudden teachings and expedient truths. This is called the "teachings of one lifetime."

【Lower Left】
【Upper Section】
■Buddha Shakyamuni in Nirvana
【Lower Section】
At eighty years old, by the Hiranyavati River near Kushinagara, between the twin sala trees, he entered nirvana at midnight on the 15th day of the 2nd month. This corresponds to the 36th year of King Mu of Zhou, the year of Renshin.

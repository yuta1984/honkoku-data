**Dharma Ocean Thunder Sound Tathagata**
In the eastern direction, beyond eight Ganges-sand buddha lands, there is a world called the Dharma Banner Country. It is called Dharma Ocean Thunder Sound Tathagata, who made four great vows.

**Six: Dharma Ocean Superior Wisdom Playful Supernatural Powers**
In the eastern direction, beyond nine Ganges-sand buddha lands, there is a world called the Good Dwelling Dharma Ocean Country. The buddha is named Dharma Ocean Superior Wisdom Playful Supernatural Powers Tathagata, who made four great vows.

**Seven: Medicine Master Lapis Lazuli Light Tathagata**
In the eastern direction, beyond ten Ganges-sand buddha lands from this world, there is a world called the Pure Lapis Lazuli Country. The buddha is named Medicine Master Lapis Lazuli Light Tathagata, who made twelve great vows.

The above is according to the teaching of the Seven Buddhas Merit Sutra.

**Ratnasambhava Tathagata (Jewel-Born Tathagata)**
The Private Record on the Two-Realm Mandala states: Ratnasambhava is the honored one who can generate myriad treasures, the Buddha of Merit Adornment.

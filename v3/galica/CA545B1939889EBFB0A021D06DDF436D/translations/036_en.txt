■Fifth: Hōkai-Raion Nyorai (Dharma Realm Thunder Sound Tathagata)
【Lower Section】
In the eastern direction, passing through buddha-lands equal to eight Ganges River sands, there is a world called Hōdō-koku (Dharma Banner Country). It is called Hōkai-Raion Nyorai (Dharma Ocean Thunder Sound Tathagata) and has made four great vows.

【Lower Right】
【Upper Section】
■Sixth: Hōkai-Shōe-Yuge-Jintsū (Dharma Realm Superior Wisdom Playful Supernatural Powers)
【Lower Section】
In the eastern direction, passing through buddha-lands equal to nine Ganges River sands, there is a world called Zenjū-Hōkai-koku (Well-Dwelling Dharma Ocean Country). The Buddha is named Hōkai-Shōe-Yuge-Jintsū Nyorai (Dharma Ocean Superior Wisdom Playful Supernatural Powers Tathagata) and has made four great vows.

【Upper Left】
【Upper Section】
■Seventh: Yakushi-Rurikō Nyorai (Medicine Master Lapis Lazuli Light Tathagata)
【Lower Section】
In the eastern direction, departing from this world and passing through buddha-lands equal to ten Ganges River sands, there is a world called Jō-Ruri-koku (Pure Lapis Lazuli Country). The Buddha is named Yakushi-Rurikō Nyorai (Medicine Master Lapis Lazuli Light Tathagata) and has made twelve great vows. 《The above is explained in the Seven Buddhas Merit Sutra》

【Lower Left】
【Upper Section】
■Hōshō Nyorai (Jewel-Born Tathagata)
【Lower Section】
According to the Private Records of the Two-Part Mandala, Hōshō (Jewel-Born) refers to the honored one of Manbō-Nōshō (capable of generating myriad treasures), a Buddha of merit and virtue adornment.

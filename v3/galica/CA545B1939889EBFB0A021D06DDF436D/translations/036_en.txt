5■Hōkai-Raion Nyorai (Dharma Realm Thunder Sound Tathagata)
【Lower Section】
In the eastern direction, beyond eight Ganges sands' worth of Buddha lands, there is a world called Dharma Banner Country.
　　【Figure】
It is called Dharma Ocean Thunder Sound Tathagata and has made four great vows.

6■Hōkai-Shōe-Yuge-Jintsū (Dharma Realm Superior Wisdom Playful Supernatural Powers)
【Lower Section】
In the eastern direction, beyond nine Ganges sands' worth of Buddha lands, there is a world called Good Dwelling Dharma Ocean Country. That Buddha is named Dharma Ocean Superior Wisdom Playful Supernatural Powers Tathagata and has made four great vows.

7■Yakushi-Ruri-kō Nyorai (Medicine Master Lapis Lazuli Light Tathagata)
【Lower Section】
In the eastern direction, departing from this world and passing ten Ganges sands' worth of Buddha lands, there is a world called Pure Lapis Lazuli Country.
　　【Figure】
That Buddha is named Medicine Master Lapis Lazuli Light Tathagata and has made twelve great vows. 《The above is according to the Seven Buddhas Merit Sutra》

■Hōshō Nyorai (Jewel-Born Tathagata)
【Lower Section】
The Private Records of the Two-Realm Mandala states: Hōshō (Jewel-Born) is the honored one who can generate all treasures, a Buddha of merit and virtue adornment.

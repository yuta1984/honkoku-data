【Upper Right】
【Title】
5. Dharma Ocean Thunder Sound Tathagata
【Description】
In the eastern direction, beyond eight Ganges River sands worth of Buddha lands, there is a world called "Dharma Banner Country." The Buddha there is called Dharma Ocean Thunder Sound Tathagata, who has made four great vows.

【Lower Right】
【Title】
6. Dharma Ocean Superior Wisdom Playful Supernatural Powers
【Description】
In the eastern direction, beyond nine Ganges River sands worth of Buddha lands, there is a world called "Well-Dwelling Dharma Ocean Country." The Buddha there is called Dharma Ocean Superior Wisdom Playful Supernatural Powers Tathagata, who has made four great vows.

【Upper Left】
【Title】
7. Medicine Master Lapis Lazuli Light Tathagata
【Description】
In the eastern direction, beyond ten Ganges River sands worth of Buddha lands from here, there is a world called "Pure Lapis Lazuli Country." The Buddha there is called Medicine Master Lapis Lazuli Light Tathagata, who has made twelve great vows. (The above is according to the teachings of the "Seven Buddhas Merit Sutra")

【Lower Left】
【Title】
Ratnasambhava Tathagata (Jewel-Born Tathagata)
【Description】
According to the "Private Notes on the Two-Realm Mandala," Ratnasambhava means the honored one who can generate all treasures, the Buddha of merit and virtue adornment.

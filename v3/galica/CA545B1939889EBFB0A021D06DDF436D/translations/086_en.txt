Nachi Hiryū Gongen
According to the Aināishō, this is a minister who assists Amaterasu Ōmikami. This is the place where [the deity] arose and descended from the beginning of the kalpa. It is a manifestation of Nanda.

Tōtō Tenjin (Tenjin Who Crossed to Tang)
This is a diagram of the place where the spirit of Kan no Kami went to Jingshan Temple in Song China, practiced Zen under Master Wuzhun, and was granted the dharma robe.

Kōyasan Four Shrine Myōjin・Niū Daimyōjin
This is the younger sister of Amaterasu Ōmikami. The reason for manifesting in female form is to bestow the blessings of growth, nourishment, and nurturing as the mother of all things in the world.

Kehi Myōjin
This is the daughter of Niū Gongen and younger sister of Kōya Myōjin. This is the venerable Kehi Taruhime. This is called Kehe-no-miya.

Kōya Daimyōjin
The first child of Niū Gongen. The original ground is Kongōkai Mahāvairocana Tathāgata. This is the teaching master who constantly expounds the dharma in the Maṇi Hall.

Itsukushima
The daughter of Shimiya Gongen and Niū Gongen. The original ground is the great Benzaiten.

【Lower Right】
【Title】
Buddha-Eye Buddha-Mother
【Description】
The "Meaning of Bodhicitta" states: "According to the Giketu, this is Vairocana. Among the five translations, it is also called Buddha-Mother." Therefore, we can understand that Prajñā Buddha-Mother is also another name for Vairocana.

【Upper Left】
【Title】
Prajñā Bodhisattva
【Description】
This is the chief of the separate divisions of the Mahāprajñāpāramitā. Prajñā means great wisdom pervading everywhere.

【Lower Left】
【Title】
Never-Resting Bodhisattva
【Description】
The "Collection of Name Meanings" states: "Gandhahastin, in our language this is called 'Never-Resting.' Moment by moment flowing into the ocean of all suffering, from the beginning having no rest."

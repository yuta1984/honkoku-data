【Lower Right】
【Title】
Buddha-Eye Buddha Mother
【Description】
According to the "Meaning of Bodhicitta," the "Giketu" states that this is Vairocana, and among the five translations, it is also called Buddha Mother. Therefore, we know that Prajñā Buddha Mother is also another name for Vairocana.

【Upper Left】
【Title】
Prajñā Bodhisattva
【Description】
This is the main deity of the separate section of the Mahāprajñāpāramitā. Prajñā means great wisdom that flows freely.

【Lower Left】
【Title】
Never-Resting Bodhisattva
【Description】
According to the "Collection of Name Meanings," Gandhahasti is called "Never-Resting" here. Moment by moment, flowing into the ocean of all suffering, from the beginning there is no rest.

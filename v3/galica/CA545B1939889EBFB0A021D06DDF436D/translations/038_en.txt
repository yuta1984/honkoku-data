**One-Character Golden Wheel**
Used in the practice of imperial ritual ceremonies.

Chiken Seal

**Buddha-Eye Buddha-Mother**
The Treatise on Bodhicitta states: "Definitively, this is Vairocana. Among the five translations, it is also called Buddha-Mother."

Therefore, wisdom, and also Prajña Buddha-Mother, are alternative names for Vairocana.

**Prajña Bodhisattva**
The principal deity of the separate section of the Great Prajñāpāramitā Sutra.

Prajña means great wisdom and perfect penetration.

**Never-Resting Bodhisattva**
The Collection of Name Meanings states: "Ganyamahauti, translated as 'never-resting,' meaning flowing in thought after thought."

Keman Seal

In all (sarva) seas of suffering, there is no rest from the very beginning.

【Upper Frame - Horizontal Text】
The Nine Grades of Amida

【Upper Right】
■Upper Grade, Superior Birth
Those of the upper grade, superior birth are: first, those who have compassionate hearts and do not kill, possessing all precepts and conduct; second, those who recite Mahayana Vaipulya sutras; third, those who practice the six mindfulnesses, dedicate merit, make vows, and wish to be born in that land.

【Lower Right】
■Upper Grade, Middle Birth
Those of the upper grade, middle birth do not necessarily need to uphold and recite Vaipulya sutras, but they well understand their meaning and principles. Amida Buddha, surrounded by a great assembly, carries a purple-gold dais and comes before the practitioner.

【Upper Left】
■Upper Grade, Inferior Birth
Those of the upper grade, inferior birth also believe in cause and effect, but simply generate the aspiration for the Way and are born in the Land of Ultimate Bliss. At the time of death, Amida Buddha, Avalokiteśvara, Mahāsthāmaprāpta, and five hundred transformation buddhas praise them, saying: "Dharma-son, you have now achieved birth."

【Lower Left】
■Middle Grade, Superior Birth
Those of the middle grade, superior birth uphold the five precepts, eight precepts, and various precepts, do not commit the five grave offenses, and wish to be born in the Western [Pure Land]. Amida Buddha, together with various monks, performs obeisance to the Buddha, and they are immediately born in the Land of Ultimate Bliss.

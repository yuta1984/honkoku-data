Nikkō Bodhisattva (Sūryaprabha)
19th Day

This is the great bodhisattva at the left side of Yakushi Buddha. Also called Nitten-shi (Sun Deva).

Shōshinshi Gongen
19th Day

Manifests in Shiga County, Ōmi Province. Sometimes called Hachiman Daibosatsu. The original ground is Amida Buddha.

Gakkō Bodhisattva (Candraprabha)
20th Day

Similarly, this is the great bodhisattva at the right side. Also called Gettō Daishi (Moon Lamp Great Being) and Gatten-shi (Moon Deva).

Kyakujin Gongen (Guest Deity)
20th Day

Enshrined in Shiga County, Ōmi Province. This is Izanami-no-mikoto. The original ground is Jūichimen Kannon (Eleven-faced Avalokiteśvara).

Mujin'i Bodhisattva (Akṣayamati)
21st Day

The Daihon Hannya Sutra clarifies that emptiness is inexhaustible. The Daishū Sutra clarifies the eighty gates of inexhaustibility.

Hachiōji Gongen
21st Day

One of the shrines within the seven Sannō shrines in Ōmi Province. The original ground is Senju Kannon (Thousand-armed Avalokiteśvara).

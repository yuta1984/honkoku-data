【Upper Right】
【Upper Section】
■Nikkō (Sunlight) Bodhisattva《19th day》
【Lower Section】
This is the great bodhisattva who serves as the left attendant of Yakushi Buddha. When in heaven, he is called Nitten-shi (Sun Deva).

【Lower Right】
【Upper Section】
■Shōshinshi Gongen《19th day》
【Lower Section】
Manifests in Shiga District, Ōmi Province. Sometimes identified as Hachiman Daibosatsu. The honji is Amida Buddha.

【Upper Center】
【Upper Section】
■Gakkō (Moonlight) Bodhisattva《20th day》
【Lower Section】
Similarly, this is the great bodhisattva who serves as the right attendant. Also called Gettō Daishi (Great Master Moon Lamp) and is the Gatten-shi (Moon Deva).

【Lower Center】
【Upper Section】
■Kyakujin Gongen (Guest Deity Manifestation)《20th day》
【Lower Section】
Enshrined in Shiga District, Ōmi Province. This is Izanami-no-mikoto. The honji is Jūichimen Kannon (Eleven-faced Avalokiteśvara).

【Upper Left】
【Upper Section】
■Akṣayamati (Inexhaustible Intent) Bodhisattva《21st day》
【Lower Section】
The "Mahāprajñāpāramitā Sūtra" clarifies that "emptiness is inexhaustible." The "Mahāvaipulya Sūtra" elucidates the eighty gates of inexhaustibility.

【Lower Left】
【Upper Section】
■Hachiōji Gongen (Eight Prince Manifestation)《21st day》
【Lower Section】
One shrine within the seven Sannō shrines of Ōmi Province. The honji is Senju Kannon (Thousand-armed Avalokiteśvara).

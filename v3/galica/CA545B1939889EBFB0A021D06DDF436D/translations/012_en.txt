Chanting their sacred names and engaging in true cultivation and practice, if one attains the principle of the non-duality of kami and Buddha, the benefits would be vast indeed. Therefore, without concern for my limited perspective like viewing through a tube or measuring with a shell, I have finally completed this work.

Written in:
The third year of Genroku, the year of Kanoe-Uma, in the month of Ishoku, on an auspicious day

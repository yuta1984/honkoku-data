Hokusai Manga First, Second, and Third Volumes 《Small print: One volume each》 《Small print: It is delightful that we can see in succession all the volumes containing the various marvelous drawings that the master created as his heart moved him, riding on inspiration and responding to each occasion》

Same　　Fourth Volume　　Complete in one volume 《Small print: Adding grass-style brushwork, aiming to make it like impromptu sketches done in a sitting room》

Same　　Fifth Volume　　Complete in one volume 《Small print: Depicting in detail flower gardens, temple halls and pagodas, Buddhist temples, noble courtiers, pavilions and residential buildings, with nothing left out that cannot be fully expressed in each volume》

Same　　Sixth Volume　　Complete in one volume 《Small print: Depicting the forms of practice in swordsmanship, spearmanship, archery and horsemanship, gunnery, etc., in great detail. This can particularly be called a book that expresses the nobility of martial virtue》

Same　　Seventh Volume　　Complete in one volume 《Small print: Depicting famous scenic spots throughout the provinces and the scenery of wind, rain, frost, and snow》

Same　　Eighth Volume　　Complete in one volume 《Small print: Interesting and amusing drawings that were omitted from previous volumes, and also depicting the crafts of brocade weaving and sericulture》

Same　　Ninth Volume　　Complete in one volume 《Small print: Recording famous warriors and brave men of Japan and China, both ancient and modern, as well as chaste wives and virtuous women》

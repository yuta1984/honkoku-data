[Illustration] Made of bamboo, when one holds it in the mouth and pulls the string while breathing out, the string and bamboo combine to produce a faint sound.

(Name: Motsukunirette)

These three musical instruments exist from Souya to Karafuto Island, but are not seen in the eastern regions.

All the items illustrated above are made by the Ainu. In addition to these, they make for themselves various items such as ladles (kashiyuku), chopsticks (chekube, shyatsuka), rope (harika), cord (tsushi), rush mats (shyuukina), and sedge mats (yarishyou).

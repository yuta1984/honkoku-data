Numbers and Language

One (shineppu, hitotsu) shinenan, Two (tuppu, futatsu) tuppunan, Three (reppu, mittsu) reinan, Four (ineppu, yottsu) yūbi, Five (ashiki, itsutsu) nekuru

Six (ihan, muttsu) karaton, Seven (aruhan, nanatsu) raaton, Eight (tobeshi, yattsu) yukuna, Nine (shinebeshi, kokonotsu) katsupo, Ten (wanan, tō) anchiki

Twenty (hotsu, nijū), One hundred (ashinehotsu, hyaku), Two hundred (shinewano, nihyaku), One thousand (ashikineshinewaano, sen), Two thousand (wanshinewa, nisen)

Ten thousand (ashikinewaanshinewaano, man), Twenty thousand (shinewaanoshinewaano, niman), One bundle (shineteshi, ichiren), One handful (shinemui, ichiha)

*Counting begins with small numbers and ends with large numbers

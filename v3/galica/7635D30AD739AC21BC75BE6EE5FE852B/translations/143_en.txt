Shiyōka is 1 sun 5 bu, seserete is 6 sun, arshin is 2 shaku 4 sun, sassen is 7 shaku 2 sun, and elsta equals 360 jō, which is 10 chō. When 105 of these are accumulated to make one do, one do equals 29 ri 6 chō in our country's measure.

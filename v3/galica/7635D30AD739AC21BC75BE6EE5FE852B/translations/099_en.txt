We called him and asked about various matters, but neither writing nor spoken language was mutually intelligible. Our people and the Akaito (red people) can only barely understand the Ezo language. Therefore, even when we attempt dialogue through the Ezo as interpreters, there are many things we cannot understand, and little that we can communicate clearly. Moreover, the Akaito are guarded and unwilling to speak in detail, so our single servant alone (cannot obtain sufficient information).

　　　〇Part II, Second Half
By the year 1840, although France maintained the laws of the House of Orleans and showed no signs of change, Louis-Napoleon, together with his small group of supporters in London, could no longer bear to sit idly and wait for the right moment. Therefore, he finally conceived wild ambitions and, hoping for a stroke of good fortune, devised a new strategy...

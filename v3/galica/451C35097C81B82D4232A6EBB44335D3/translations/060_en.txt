In 1851, he received the important mission of conducting secret negotiations with Prussian envoys in Frankfurt. Having received this command, he worked diligently for Prussia, and until Austria was separated from the confederation, he assisted Prussian envoys in Germany who had been unable to fully carry out their missions, accomplishing critical affairs.

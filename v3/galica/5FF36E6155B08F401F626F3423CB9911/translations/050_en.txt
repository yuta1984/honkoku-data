Ki no Yoshito defeated Sumitomo and subdued the rebels throughout the province by winning them over with benevolence, so the entire region was pacified. Meanwhile, in the capital, when news of Sumitomo's defeat was heard, people of all ranks felt relieved. However, from around the fourth month of the same seventh year, violent earthquakes occurred and comets appeared night after night, so court astronomers were summoned to the imperial palace to divine their meaning. They declared these to be omens foretelling that treacherous retainers would rise in rebellion.

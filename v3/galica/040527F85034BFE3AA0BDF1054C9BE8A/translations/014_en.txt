In Nanyang's Lei County there is Sweet Valley, where the water is sweet and delicious. Upstream there are many chrysanthemums, which fall into the water and flow downstream. The
households in the valley drink this water, and those with superior longevity live to 120-130 years, those with middle longevity live over 100 years, and 70-80 years is considered an early death.

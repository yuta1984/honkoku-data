Sen-kōka (Cut Red Flower)
Sen-shūra (Cut Autumn Creeper)
【Outside the box】The flowers have many color varieties. Purple Matsumoto has light purple flower color. Kara-nishiki 
Matsumoto has crimson red with white speckled patterns. Nishikigawa Matsumoto has fewer speckles than Kara-nishiki.
White double-flowered Matsumoto produces six to seven petals, sometimes eight to nine petals.
Hanagata Matsumoto has layered petals when blooming, with crimson red color. It has flowers in May.

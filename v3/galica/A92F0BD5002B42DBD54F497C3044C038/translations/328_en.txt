In the second year, seventh month, Major Counselor Tadazane was appointed Right Minister. Minamoto no Masazane was appointed Inner Minister. Masazane was the son of Akifusa. In the same year, Minamoto no Yoshiie's eldest son, Tsushima no kami Yoshichika, was exiled to Izumo Province for disobeying an imperial edict.

In the third year, former Kampaku Fujiwara no Morozane died at the age of sixty.

In the fourth year, first month, there was an imperial visit to Kasuga. In the third month, the Cloistered Emperor's fiftieth birthday was celebrated. In the sixth month, Ōe no Masafusa returned to the capital from Kyushu, and Middle Counselor Fujiwara no Suenaka became Dazai no sochi and departed for his post. This person was called "Black Commander" (Kuro no sochi) due to his dark complexion.

In the fifth year, first month, there was an imperial visit to Toba Detached Palace, where audience was granted to the Cloistered Emperor.

In the first year of Chōji, third month, there was an imperial visit to Sonshō-ji. In the eighth month, there was a Lotus Sutra lecture in the imperial calligraphy within the imperial palace.

In the seventh month of Kanji 2 (1088), Major Counselor Tadazane was appointed Right Minister. Minamoto no Masazane was appointed Inner Minister. Masazane was the son of Akifusa. In the same year, Yoshichika, the eldest son of Minamoto no Yoshiie and Governor of Tsushima, was exiled to Izumo Province for defying an imperial edict.

In the second month of the third year, former Regent Fujiwara no Morozane died at the age of sixty-four.

In the first month of the fourth year, there was an imperial visit to Kasuga. In the third month, the fiftieth birthday of the Cloistered Emperor was celebrated.

In the sixth month, Ōe no Masafusa returned to the capital from Kyushu. Middle Counselor Fujiwara no Suenaka became Dazai no sochi and departed for his post. Because this person was dark-skinned, he was called Kokusosu (Black Commander).

In the first month of the fifth year, there was an imperial visit to Toba Detached Palace, where audience was granted to the Cloistered Emperor.

In the third month of Chōji 1 (1104), there was an imperial visit to Sonshōji Temple. In the eighth month, there was a Lotus Sutra lecture in the emperor's own calligraphy within the imperial palace.

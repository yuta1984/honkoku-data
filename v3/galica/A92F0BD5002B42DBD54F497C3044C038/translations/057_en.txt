Twenty-fourth Generation
Emperor Kenzō was the grandson of Emperor Richū and son of Prince Ichinobe. Prince Ichinobe was killed by Emperor Yūryaku. At that time Kenzō was still young, and together with his elder brother Ninken, he disguised himself, pretending to be of lowly status, and fled to Harima Province. There he served Oshiumi-be no Hosome in Akashi District, tending cattle and horses without revealing his true identity. On one occasion, Yamabe no Otate, the provincial governor of Harima, arrived in Akashi District. Kenzō thought this was an opportune moment, and danced and sang before Otate.

Apart from these four, no one could appear before Yoriie. Even though these four men committed acts of violence throughout Kamakura, they were not subject to punishment. In the same month, Mongaku of Takao plotted a rebellion, attempting to enthrone the second prince of Retired Emperor Go-Toba. When the plot was discovered, he was exiled to Oki Province. As a result of this, Rokudai Zenji, the son of Taira no Koremori, was also executed. In the fifth month, the monk Shunzeu departed for Song China. In the sixth month, Kazanin Left Minister Kanemasa submitted his resignation. Right Minister Fujiwara no Yorizane was appointed as Chancellor (Daijō-daijin). Interior Minister Fujiwara no Yoshitsune became Left Minister. Great Counselor Fujiwara no Iezane became Right Minister. Great Counselor Minamoto no Michichika became Interior Minister. In the eighth month, Yoriie seized the concubine of Adachi Kagemori. Because of this, when Kagetoki slandered Kagemori by saying he harbored resentment, Kagemori was about to be immediately attacked. He was spared through the intervention of Taira no Masako. In the tenth month, Kagetoki [reported] that Yuki Tomomitsu longed for Yoritomo and resented Yoriie.

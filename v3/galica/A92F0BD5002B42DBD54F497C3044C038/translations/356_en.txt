Retired Emperor Go-Shirakawa consulted with Shinzei about how to handle his request. Shinzei remonstrated, saying, "Generals have been selected based on merit since ancient times. There are many examples of those who were appointed as ministers but could hardly become generals. This is not something that someone like Nobuyori should aspire to." When Nobuyori heard this, he became greatly enraged and constantly practiced martial arts, plotting to destroy Shinzei. Shinzei was close to Taira no Kiyomori. Therefore, Nobuyori enlisted Minamoto no Yoshitomo. Yoshitomo also agreed, riding the momentum and thinking to strike down Kiyomori. In the twelfth month, Kiyomori went on a pilgrimage to Kumano. Thinking this a good opportunity, Nobuyori and Yoshitomo led out their troops, burned down the Retired Emperor's palace at Sanjō, and killed many people. They searched for Shinzei but could not find him. Shinzei had already fled the palace beforehand and headed toward Nara, burying himself alive in the earth.

...and issued an edict to Prince Naka no Ōe, saying "What is this about?" Prince Naka no Ōe prostrated himself and reported, saying "Iruka has destroyed the various princes and seeks to overturn the imperial throne. How could the imperial position be given to Iruka?" The Emperor immediately stood up and went inside. Komaro and Tsunada finally killed Iruka by cutting him down. On this day, rain fell and the accumulated water filled the courtyard. They covered Iruka's corpse with mats and sliding screens. From that point, Prince Naka no Ōe built a fortress at Hōkō-ji temple. All the princes followed him. There were also some who went to Emishi's side. Prince Naka no Ōe had someone give Iruka's corpse to Emishi. He also made Kose no Tokuta the general to attack Emishi, and proclaimed to his followers: "Throughout all ages, who does not know the way of lord and vassal? Why do you follow a treasonous minister?" When he said this, all of Emishi's followers fled.

Twentieth Generation
Emperor Ingyō: He was the younger brother of Hanzei. He was sickly from birth and did not meet the expectations of his father and elder brothers. However, due to his benevolent and filial disposition, after Hanzei's death, the court ministers consulted together and requested that he ascend to the throne. He declined several times and would not comply. Empress Oshisaka Ōnakahime earnestly urged him, saying how could he ignore the wishes of the court ministers. After more than a year had passed, he finally ascended to the throne. An excellent physician came from Silla and treated him, so his illness was cured.

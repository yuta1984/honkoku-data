Though Morosuke petitioned for this, Saneyori did not agree, so the matter was not pursued. Therefore Tadafumi became angry and, resenting Saneyori, said that the position should be given to Morosuke, then died by fasting. It was said that due to his vengeful spirit, Saneyori's descendants would decline while Morosuke's descendants would prosper, but since Masakado had been defeated several years earlier and Tadafumi died at over seventy years of age, what the common people say is dubious. From the eighth month onward, smallpox spread throughout the realm, and offerings were made to various shrines while sutra readings and prayers were performed. In the ninth month, a shrine to Minister Sugawara was built at Kitano. In the eleventh month, there was an imperial excursion to Uji for hunting.

In the second year, there was a great drought in summer and heavy rains in autumn. On the twenty-fourth day of the eighth month, the sun and moon were visible simultaneously.

In the third year, in the first month, Grand Minister Tadahira retired due to illness. Saneyori and Morosuke jointly conducted government affairs. On the fourteenth day of the eighth month, Tadahira passed away at age seventy.

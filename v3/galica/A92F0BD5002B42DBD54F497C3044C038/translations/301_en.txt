Of the four current Dainagon (Major Counselors) at this time, Yoshinobu and Nagaie were Yorimichi's younger brothers. Minamoto no Morofusa was his brother-in-law (husband of his sister). Nobuie was Yorimichi's nephew and Norimichi's son.

In the fourth year (1061), eleventh month, they celebrated Yorimichi's seventieth birthday. In the twelfth month, Yorimichi was appointed Daijō-daijin (Chancellor of the Realm). In the seventh month, there was an imperial visit to Iwashimizu and Kamo shrines.

In the fifth year (1062), spring, when Minamoto no Yoriyoshi's term as Mutsu no kuni no kami (Governor of Mutsu Province) ended, Takashina no Tsuneshige was appointed as the new governor and went down to the province. However, fearing Sadatō's power, and moreover because all the soldiers in the province followed Yoriyoshi, Tsuneshige returned to the capital. In the seventh month of the same year, Kiyohara no Takenori, a resident of Senboku in Dewa Province, gathered ten thousand soldiers and came to Yoriyoshi's aid.

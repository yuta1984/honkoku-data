Since Kiyomori was about to attack the Cloistered Emperor's residence at Hōjūji Temple, Shigemori remonstrated with him in various ways and stopped him. Naritsune's son Naritsune, Taira no Yasuyori, and the monk Shunkan were exiled to Kikaigashima Island. All other associates were sentenced to exile. Later, Naritsune was finally executed at his place of exile. In the same month, Shigemori resigned from the position of Major Captain of the Left. In the twelfth month, Major Counselor Tokudaiji Sanesada concurrently assumed the position of Major Captain of the Left. Since the Taira clan revered Itsukushima in Aki Province, Sanesada visited Itsukushima and prayed to be appointed as Major Captain. When Kiyomori heard of this, he was moved and fulfilled that wish.

In the fourth month of the second year, there was an imperial visit to Kasuga. Taira no Munemori was appointed Major Counselor. On the twelfth day of the eleventh month, Empress Taira no Tokuko gave birth to a prince. Kiyomori rejoiced greatly. The Cloistered Emperor also made an imperial visit to Rokuhara, and the Chancellor and others went to offer congratulations. In the twelfth month, the prince was established as Crown Prince. Shigemori became the Crown Prince's tutor, and Yorimori became his assistant.

Dazai no sochi Middle Counselor Fujiwara no Suenaka was exiled to Hitachi Province. In the twelfth month, Right Minister Tadazane became Kampaku.

In the first year of Kashō, third month, Ōe no Masafusa became Dazai no sochi.

In the second year, the exile in Izumo Province, Minamoto no Yoshichika, continued his evil acts without cease, and there were rumors of rebellion, so Taira no Masamori was dispatched to subjugate Yoshichika. On the nineteenth day of the seventh month, the Emperor passed away at the age of twenty-nine. The era names were Kanji seven years, Kahō two years, Eichō one year, Jōtoku two years, Kōwa five years, Chōji two years, and Kashō two years, with a total reign of twenty-one years.

The Seventy-fourth Emperor
Emperor Toba: The first son of Emperor Horikawa, his personal name was Munehito. His mother was Fujiwara no Shirakiko, daughter of Kan'in Major Counselor Sanesue. He was born in the first month of Kōwa 5, and was made Crown Prince in the eighth month of the same year. In the seventh month of Kashō 2, Emperor Horikawa passed away, and the Crown Prince was five years old

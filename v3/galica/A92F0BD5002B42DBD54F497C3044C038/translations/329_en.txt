Dazai no sochi Middle Counselor Fujiwara no Suenaka was exiled to Hitachi Province. In the twelfth month, Right Minister Tadazane became regent.

In the third month of Kajō 1 (1106), Ōe no Masafusa again became Dazai no sochi.

In the second year, the exiled Minamoto no Yoshichika in Izumo Province continued his evil acts, and rumors of rebellion arose, so Taira no Masamori was dispatched to subjugate Yoshichika. On the nineteenth day of the seventh month, the Emperor passed away at the age of twenty-nine. The era names were Kanji 7 years, Kahō 2 years, Eichō 1 year, Jōtoku 2 years, Kōwa 5 years, Chōji 2 years, and Kajō 2 years. His reign lasted a total of twenty-one years.

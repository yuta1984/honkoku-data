He should be called Hachiman Tarō." From this time, Yoshiie was called Hachiman Tarō. According to one theory, he received this title because he came of age at Iwashimizu Hachiman Shrine.

In the twelfth month, Yoriyoshi sent word to various provinces including Dewa Province Governor Minamoto no Tokiyori and others to recruit military forces, but because provisions were scarce, no one came. Therefore Sadatō increasingly wielded rebellious authority and seized government property. According to one theory, Tokiyori was a falconer who accompanied Yoriyoshi to Mutsu Province.

In the first year of Kōhei, eighth month, the Daigokuden (main palace hall) burned down.

Yoriyoshi was promoted to Junior Fourth Rank Lower Grade and appointed Governor of Iyo Province. Yoshiie was promoted to Junior Fifth Rank Lower Grade and appointed Governor of Dewa Province. Yoshitsuna was made Left Gate Guard Lieutenant. Takenori was promoted to Junior Fifth Rank Lower Grade and appointed Chinju-fu General. The messengers Fujiwara no Suetoshi and Mononobe no Nagayori were also rewarded.

In the tenth month of the seventh year, there was an imperial visit to Tōhoku-in, where the Emperor paid his respects to his grandmother, Jōtōmon-in.

In the second month of Jiryaku 1 (1065), Horikawa Right Minister Fujiwara no Yorimune died at the age of seventy-three. In the sixth month, Fujiwara no Morozane became Right Minister, and Minamoto no Morofusa became Interior Minister. In the ninth month, the Hokke Hakkō (Eight Lectures on the Lotus Sutra) was performed with golden characters written by the Emperor's own hand. In the tenth month, there was reconstruction of Hōjō-ji temple, and an imperial visit on the day of the dedication ceremony.

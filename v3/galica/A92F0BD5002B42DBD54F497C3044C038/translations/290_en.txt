In the second year, fifth month, Chancellor Yorimichi invited ministers and below to his Shirakawa villa for horse racing and court music performances. After Michinaga's death, Yorimichi succeeded him and conducted politics as he pleased. In the tenth month, Grand Minister Fujiwara no Kinsue died at the age of seventy-two. He was granted the title of Duke of Kai and posthumously named Duke Jingi. After this, there were no more deliberations on posthumous titles, as both rulers and ministers had院号 (imperial designations). Kinsue was called the Kan'in Minister. His descendants formed three noble lineages of the Seiga rank: Sanjō, Saionji, and Tokudaiji. In the twelfth month, police commissioner Nakahara no Narimichi was recalled, as he had achieved no merit in suppressing Tadatsune.

When they returned to court, a person named Gao Biaoren came from Great Tang accompanying the imperial envoys. Welcome ships were sent as far as Naniwa. When returning to their country, they were escorted as far as Tsushima. This corresponded to the time of Emperor Taizong of Tang. In this reign, all three Korean kingdoms submitted, and the world was at peace, but comets appeared repeatedly. Besides these, unusual stars were seen, and there were also great winds and prolonged rains. A monk called Eon was summoned to the palace to expound the Sukhavativyuha Sutra. The practice of holding vegetarian feasts in the imperial palace and lecturing on sutras began from this time. During the emperor's reign, there was an imperial visit to the hot springs of Arima in Settsu Province. There were also imperial visits to the hot springs of Iyo. Besides these, he went hunting in various places. He reigned for thirteen years before passing away.

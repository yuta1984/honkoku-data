Munemori was thirty-nine years old, and Kiyomune was seventeen. Shigehira was executed in Nara. This was because he had burned the Southern Capital (Nara). In the eighth month, Yoshitsune was appointed Governor of Iyo and made to guard the capital. In the tenth month, Yoritomo sent Tosa-bō Shōshun to go up to the capital and attack Yoshitsune. The plan failed and Shōshun was killed. Yoritomo himself went up to the capital to attack Yoshitsune. In the eleventh month, Yoshitsune, together with his uncle Yukiie, forcibly requested an imperial decree for the pursuit of Yoritomo, and left the capital intending to go to the western provinces. At Daimotsu Beach they encountered a storm and were scattered. Yoshitsune hid in various places including Yoshino and Tōnomine. Yoritomo petitioned and received an imperial decree to search for him, but he was never found.

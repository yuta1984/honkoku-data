Awa no Minbu Shigenō welcomed the Taira and built an imperial palace at Yashima in Sanuki Province. Thus the Taira lived peacefully for a while and dominated the Nankaidō region. In the tenth month, Yoshinaka sent Minamoto no Yoshikiyo as general with troops to attack the Taira. At Mizushima in Bitchū Province, they fought against Tomomori and Noto no Kami Noritune and others, but the Minamoto were defeated and Yoshikiyo was killed. Noritune was the second son of Norimori. Yoshikiyo was from the Ashikaga clan and was an ancestor of the Nitta and Hosokawa families. Afterwards, Yoshinaka himself advanced and killed the Taira retainer Senoo Kaneyasu. Yoshinaka should have continued attacking the Taira, but hearing that Yukiie was wielding power in the capital and slandering him, he hurried to Kyoto. Yukiie did not remain in the capital but went to Harima Province, where he fought the Taira at Muroyama, but was defeated and fled to Kawachi. In the eleventh month, Yoshinaka was in the capital, and his evil deeds exceeded even those of the Taira. The Cloistered Emperor became angry and [summoned] the warrior monks of Mount Hiei and Mii-dera

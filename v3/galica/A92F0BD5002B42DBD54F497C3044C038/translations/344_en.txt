Fujiwara no Tokushi was the daughter of Middle Counselor Fujiwara no Nagasane. She gave birth in the fifth year of Hōen, and [the child] was immediately made Crown Prince, ascending the throne in the twelfth month of the first year of Hōji. At that time he was three years old. Regent Tadamichi served as regent. At this time, Retired Emperor Toba was called "First Retired Emperor" and Retired Emperor Sutoku was called "New Retired Emperor." All government affairs were conducted through the machinations of the [First] Retired Emperor, and the New Retired Emperor could not interfere in anything, spending his days with grievances about all matters.

In the first month of the second year of Kōji, there was an imperial visit for audience with the Empress Dowager. In the fifth month, the Retired Emperor received ordination at Tōdai-ji and also received ordination at Mount Hiei.

In the seventh month of the second year of Ten'yō, because a great comet appeared, the era name was changed to Kyūan. In the eighth month, Taikenmon-in, the mother of the New Retired Emperor, passed away. In the same year, there were imperial visits to Iwashimizu and Kamo.

In the third month of the third year, Ichijō Tsunetsune resigned from the position of regent (kanpaku). Kujō Fusazane became regent. In the fifth month, Minamoto no Michio resigned from the position of Chancellor (Daijō-daijin). Former regent Takatsukasa Fuyuhira was reappointed as minister.

In the first year of Shōchū era, former Minister of the Center Sanjō Kineshige passed away at the age of forty-one. In the third month, there was an imperial visit to Iwashimizu Hachiman Shrine. In the fourth month, there was an imperial visit to Kamo Shrine. In the same month, Minister of the Center Takatsukasa Fuyunori became Minister of the Left. Major Counselor Konoe Tsunetada became Minister of the Right. Major Counselor Saionji Sanehira was appointed Minister of the Center. In the fifth month, former regent Konoe Iehira passed away. In the sixth month, Retired Emperor Go-Uda died at the age of fifty-eight. In the eighth month, Hōjō Koresada returned from Rokuhara to Kamakura.

In the ninth month, when the plot of Toki Yoritei, Tajimi Kunitada and others to destroy Kamakura upon receiving secret imperial edicts was discovered, Norisada of Rokuhara dispatched military forces and killed Yoritei.

In the third month of Kajō 1 (1235), Regent Norizane died at the age of twenty-six. The current Kujō house descends from this line. Michiie again became regent. In the fourth month, the Zen monk Enni entered Song China and met Wuzhun at Mount Jing, receiving the dharma transmission. In the tenth month, Konoe Right Minister Kanetsune was transferred to Left Minister, Saionji Inner Minister Sanesuke was appointed Right Minister, and Major Counselor Fujiwara no Yoshizane was appointed Inner Minister. In the eleventh month, Yoritsune was promoted to Junior Second Rank. In the twelfth month, shrine attendants of Iwashimizu injured shrine attendants of Kasuga, causing the great assembly of Nara to rise up in anger and proceed to the capital carrying portable shrines. Hōjō Shigetoki dispatched soldiers to prevent this and sent envoys to pacify them, whereupon the portable shrines returned from the Kizu River. During this period, beginning with Michiie, people of the Fujiwara clan did not attend court.

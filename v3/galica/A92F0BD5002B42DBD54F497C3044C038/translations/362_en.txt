79th Emperor
Emperor Rokujō: Son of Emperor Nijō. His personal name was Norihito. His mother was the daughter of Ōkura-no-taifu Ki no Kanemori.

He ascended the throne in the sixth month of the first year of Eiman at the age of two. Chancellor Motozane served as regent. His grandfather, Retired Emperor Go-Shirakawa, conducted government affairs from the imperial court. In the eighth month of the same year, Taira no Kiyomori was appointed Major Counselor.

In the seventh month of the first year of Jin'an, Taira no Shigemori was appointed Middle Counselor. In the same month, Regent Motozane died at the age of twenty-four. He was posthumously granted the title of Chancellor. His younger brother, Minister of the Left Motofusa, became regent in his place. In the tenth month, through the arrangement of the Retired Emperor, Prince Norihito was appointed Crown Prince. Norihito was the son of the Retired Emperor and uncle to the current Emperor. The current Emperor was three years old.

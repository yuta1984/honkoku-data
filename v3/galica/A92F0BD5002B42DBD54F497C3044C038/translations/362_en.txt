[Emperor Go-Shirakawa] died at the age of twenty-three. The era names were Heiji 1 year, Nin'an 2 years, Ōhō 2 years, Chōkan 2 years, and Nin'an 1 year. His reign lasted a total of seven years.

The 79th Emperor
Emperor Rokujō - Son of Emperor Nijō, his personal name was Naohito, and his mother was the daughter of Kii no Kanemori, Assistant Director of the Imperial Treasury.

He ascended the throne in the sixth month of the first year of Nin'an (1166) at the age of two. Chancellor Motazane served as regent. His grandfather, Retired Emperor Go-Shirakawa, handled governmental affairs from the imperial court. In the eighth month of the same year, Taira no Kiyomori was appointed Grand Counselor.

In the seventh month of the first year of Nin'an, Taira no Shigemori was appointed Middle Counselor. In the same month, Regent Motazane died at the age of twenty-four. He was posthumously granted the title of Chancellor. His younger brother, Minister of the Left Motofusa, became regent in his place. In the tenth month, through the arrangement of the Retired Emperor, Prince Norihito was established as Crown Prince. Norihito was the son of the Retired Emperor and uncle to the current emperor. The current emperor was three years old.

In the eleventh month, Left Gate Guards Commander Fujiwara no Mototoshi took the tonsure at the age of eighty-four. He was accomplished in poetry and Chinese verse. Fujiwara no Shunzei was Mototoshi's disciple. Therefore, poets of that school regard Mototoshi as their founding master.

In the fifth year, in the fifth month, Prince Toshihito was born to Bifukumon-in, who was beloved by the retired emperor. This was Emperor Konoe. This empress had already given birth to two princesses. Wondering whether this time too it might be a princess, the retired emperor was anxious and troubled, and various prayers were offered, but since it was a boy, he was extremely delighted. The regent and all below offered their congratulations. Due to the retired emperor's excessive affection, he made the child the adopted son of the current emperor (Emperor Sutoku), with Empress Kōkamon-in as adoptive mother, and in the eighth month, he was finally installed as crown prince. In the twelfth month, Left Minister Minamoto no Arito resigned as Left General. Interior Minister Yorinaga concurrently held the position of Left General, and Major Counselor Fujiwara no Sanenō concurrently held the position of Right General.

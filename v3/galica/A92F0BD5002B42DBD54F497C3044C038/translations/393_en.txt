They built fortifications in the valley and gathered one hundred thousand men. Due to this, the former Emperor (Antoku) made an imperial progress from Yashima to Fukuhara. Among the nearby warriors who showed loyalty to the Genji, Noto no Kami Noritsune killed them all without exception. In the second month, Noriyori and Yoshitsune divided into two forces to attack the Taira clan. Yoshitsune first destroyed the encampment that Taira no Sukemori and others had established at Mikusayama, then headed toward the rear attack route at Ichi-no-tani. Noriyori headed toward Ikuta Forest at the main attack route. At the main attack, the Kawara brothers died in battle at the vanguard, while Kajiwara Kagetoki and his son Kagesue distinguished themselves with military achievements in two cavalry charges. At the rear attack, Kumagai Naosane and Hirayama Sueshige led the vanguard, followed by Doi Sanehira. However, when the fortress proved too strong to break, Yoshitsune circled around the mountain behind and attacked through Hiyodorigoe, setting fire to the Taira encampments. The Taira suffered a great defeat, and Echizen no Sanmi Tsunemori, Satsuma no Kami Tadanori, Bitchū no Kami Morimori, Musashi no Kami Tomoakira, Owari no Kami Kiyosada, Awaji no Kami Kiyofusa, and Kōgōgū no Suke Tsunemasa

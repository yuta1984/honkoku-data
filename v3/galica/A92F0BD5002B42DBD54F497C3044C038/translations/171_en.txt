In the third month of the twenty-fifth year, the Emperor passed away at the age of seventy. The era name was Enryaku. He reigned for twenty-five years.

The Fifty-first Sovereign
Emperor Heizei - He was the crown prince of Emperor Kammu. His personal name was Yasudono. His mother was Empress Fujiwara no Otomuro, daughter of Minister of the Interior Yoshitsugu. This emperor loved learning and composed poetry well. He ascended the throne after Emperor Kammu's death and made his younger brother Prince Kamino the crown prince.

In the fifth month of Daidō 1 (806), Great Counselor Fujiwara no Uchimaro was made Right Minister. Provincial inspection commissioners (kansatsu-shi) were established for the first time in various regions. All were made to concurrently hold the office of consultant (sangi).

In the sixth month, his maternal grandfather, Minister of the Interior Yoshitsugu, was posthumously granted the rank of Senior First Rank and the position of Grand Minister.

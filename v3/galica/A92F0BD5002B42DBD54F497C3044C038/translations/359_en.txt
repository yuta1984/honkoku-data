In the seventh month, Fujiwara no Munesuke resigned from the position of Chancellor (Daijō-daijin). In the same month, Minister of the Left Fujiwara no Kinnori died. The former Chancellor Fujiwara no Saneyuki also died at the age of eighty-four. In the eighth month, Minister of the Left Koremichi was appointed Chancellor. The Regent and Minister of the Right Motomichi was appointed Minister of the Left, Major Counselor Kimiyoshi was appointed Minister of the Right, and Major Counselor Motofusa, who was Motomichi's younger brother, was appointed Minister of the Interior. Taira no Kiyomori was appointed State Counselor (Sangi) and promoted to Senior Third Rank. With Yoshitomo already executed and the Minamoto clan weakened, Kiyomori finally seized control of the realm. In this year, Fujiwara no Tashi was summoned and made Empress. She had been the consort of Emperor Konoe. After Emperor Konoe's death, she had lived in obscurity, but when the Emperor heard of her beauty, he commanded her father, Minister of the Right Kimiyoshi, to summon her. The Retired Emperor thought this matter improper, and the court ministers also remonstrated against it, but the Emperor would not consent to their objections. The Empress also declined in various ways.

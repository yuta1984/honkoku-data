In the first year of Ryakuō (1338), in the first month, Akiie, Yoshioki, and others marched toward the capital. On the way, at Aono Plain in Mino Province, they fought against Takauji's general Momonoi Naotane and others. In the same month, Yoshisada and Yoshisuke emerged from Somayana and captured the castle of Echizen-fu. Ashikaga Takatsune fled and entered Kuromaru Castle. Yoshisada gained momentum and demonstrated his power in the northern provinces, with the intention of attacking Kyoto. In the second month, as Akiie was heading toward Yoshino, he fought several battles with Kō no Moronao, Momonoi Naotane, and others along the way at Hachiman and Nanto.

In the fifth month, Akiie was defeated in battle and died at Abeno in Izumi Province. He was twenty-one years old. Akiie's younger brother Akinobu, along with Nitta Yoshioki, took refuge in Hachiman.

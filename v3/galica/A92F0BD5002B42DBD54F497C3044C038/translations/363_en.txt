The Crown Prince was six years old.
In the eleventh month, Motofusa resigned from the position of Minister of the Left. Minister of the Right Tsunemune was transferred to Minister of the Left, Inner Minister Kanezane became Minister of the Right, and Taira no Kiyomori was appointed Inner Minister.
On the eleventh day of the second month of the second year, Kiyomori was promoted directly from Inner Minister to Chancellor. He was granted the court rank of Senior First Rank, given personal guards and weapons, and permitted to enter and exit the imperial palace in a palanquin. At this time, Kiyomori was fifty years old. Grand Counselor Fujiwara no Tadamasa was appointed Inner Minister. Taira no Shigemori was appointed Provisional Grand Counselor and permitted to wear a sword. In the fifth month, Kiyomori resigned from the position of Chancellor. In the eighth month, Kiyomori was granted an official document and awarded the districts and villages of Harima, Hizen, and Higo provinces as merit fields. Also, his third son Munemori was appointed to the council of state and granted the Third Court Rank. The Taira clan gradually prospered until no one could rival them.
In the second month of the third year, through the arrangement of the Retired Emperor, His Majesty abdicated and [became] Retired Emperor

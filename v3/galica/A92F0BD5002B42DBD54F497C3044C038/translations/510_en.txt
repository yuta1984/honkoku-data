Morotada, the Minister of the Left of the Nijō clan, became Regent. In the tenth month, a declaration was issued conferring imperial prince status upon Koreyasu, and he was promoted to the Second Rank (Nihon). In the same month, His Majesty (Emperor Gouda) abdicated in favor of Crown Prince Hirohito. As His Majesty was only eleven years old that year, the Retired Emperor Kameyama (Shin'in) felt deep reluctance to part with the throne, and His Majesty himself had no desire to abdicate either; however, since the Kamakura shogunate submitted a memorial saying "the Senior Retired Emperor of Fukakusa (Gofukakusa) must surely be impatiently waiting," the abdication took place against His Majesty's wishes. The reign spanned from the first year of Kenji through the tenth year of Kōan, totaling thirteen years on the throne.

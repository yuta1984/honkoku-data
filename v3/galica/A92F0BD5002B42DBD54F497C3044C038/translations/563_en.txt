In the second month of the second year [of Kenmu], Left Minister Nijō Michihira passed away at the age of forty-nine. Right Minister Takatsukasa Fuyunori was appointed as Left Minister. Former Inner Minister Tōin Kinkane was appointed as Right Minister. Yoshida Sadafusa resigned from Inner Minister. Great Counselor Ichijō Tsunemichi was appointed as Inner Minister. A dragon horse was presented from Izumo Province for imperial viewing. In the third month, Middle Counselor Fujifusa renounced the world. Great Counselor Saionji Kimimune conspired in rebellion with Eshō, younger brother of Hōjō Takatoki. Eshō returned to secular life and called himself Tokioki. Takatoki's son Tokiyuki rose up in the Kantō region, and his clansman Nagoe Tokikane rose up in the northern provinces. When the plot was discovered, Kimimune was executed. In the seventh month, Tokiyuki rose up from Shinano Province and attacked Kamakura. Tadayoshi took Prince Narinaga with him and departed from Kamakura. At this time he sent an envoy to assassinate Prince Morinaga. Takauji received imperial orders and [moved against] Tokiyuki...

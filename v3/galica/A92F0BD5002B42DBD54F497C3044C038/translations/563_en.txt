[Yoshida Sadafusa] was appointed to the position of Inner Minister. Takauji was appointed as Councillor (sangi). In the tenth month, former Left Minister Takatsukasa Fuyunori was reassigned as Right Minister.

In the second month of the second year, Left Minister Nijō Michihira died at the age of forty-nine. Right Minister Takatsukasa Fuyunori was appointed as Left Minister. Former Inner Minister Tōin Kinkane was appointed as Right Minister. Yoshida Sadafusa resigned from the position of Inner Minister. Great Counselor Ichijō Tsunemichi was appointed as Inner Minister. A dragon horse was presented from Izumo Province.

In the third month, Middle Counselor Fujifusa renounced the world. Great Counselor Saionji Kimmune conspired in rebellion with Eshō, the younger brother of Hōjō Takatoki. Eshō returned to lay life and called himself Tokioki. Takatoki's son Tokiyuki rose up in the Kantō region, and his clan member Nagoe Tokikane rose up in the northern provinces. When the plot was discovered, Kimmune was executed.

In the seventh month, Tokiyuki rose up from Shinano Province and attacked Kamakura. Tadayoshi escorted Prince Narinaga out of Kamakura. At this time, he sent an envoy to assassinate Prince Morinaga. Takauji, receiving imperial orders, [moved against] Tokiyuki...

Sukemasa and his younger brother Gorō Tokimune secretly entered Kudō Tsunetsune's house at night, killed him, and avenged their father. From there they burst into Yoritomo's lodging and wounded about ten people with their swords. Since this was a sudden incident in the middle of the night, it caused great commotion. They encountered Nitta Tadatsune, and Sukemasa was killed. Since Tokimune continued to advance, Yoritomo himself was about to come out. While Ōtomo Yoshinao held him back, a man of great strength called Gorōmaru captured Tokimune. Yoritomo directly questioned him about the details and was about to pardon him, but due to a petition from Tsunetsune's son, Tokimune was sentenced to death by beheading. Now, Sukemasa's father was called Kawazu Yasuyasu. Yasuyasu's father was called Itō Sukechika. When Tsunetsune's father died, since he was young, due to the friendship with the Sukechika clan, he had been entrusted with managing that territory. However, after Tsunetsune came of age, since they had mutual grievances, Tsunetsune directed his anger toward Yasuyasu, secretly killed Yasuyasu, and pretended not to know about it. But as Sukemasa and Tokimune gradually grew up, [it became clear] that he was their father's enemy.

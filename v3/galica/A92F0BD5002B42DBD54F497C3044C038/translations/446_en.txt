Sanetomo had been appointed shogun from the third year of Kennin era, and ruled for seventeen years until this year. He was twenty-eight years old when he died. Yoritomo, Yoriie, and Sanetomo are called the three-generation shoguns. The total period was forty years. Kugyō had lost his father Yoriie when he was four years old, and was nineteen this year.

In the second month, Hōjō Masako and Yoshitoki consulted together and sent Shinano-no-kami Yukimitsu to the capital, hoping to make one of the two imperial princes - Prince Rokujō Masanari or Prince Reizei Yorihito, sons of Retired Emperor Go-Toba - the lord of Kamakura. The Retired Emperor did not consent.

In the third month, Interior Minister Fujiwara no Iemichi was appointed Right Minister. Kuga Dainagon Minamoto no Michimitsu was appointed Interior Minister.

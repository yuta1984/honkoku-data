The Eighty-Sixth Emperor
Emperor Shijō: His personal name was Hidehito. He was the crown prince of Emperor Go-Horikawa. His mother was Sōheki Mon-in Fujiwara no Takeko, daughter of Regent and Minister of the Left Michiie. He was born in the second month of Kanki 3 (1231). In the tenth month of Jōei 1 (1232), he received the abdication and ascended the throne at the age of two. Michiie's eldest son, Chancellor Norisane, became regent. Though Emperor Go-Horikawa attempted to conduct cloistered government from his palace, Michiie wielded great influence as the maternal grandfather. Both Regent Norisane and Kamakura Shogun Yoritsune were sons of Michiie. Moreover, Norisane's younger brothers Yoshizane and Sanetsune also advanced in succession.

In the first year of Kanki (1229), in the fourth month, Empress Fujiwara Chōshi was removed and given the title Takatsukasa-in. Sonshi, daughter of Chancellor Michiie, entered the palace and became Empress. Her younger sister Masako became Naishi-no-kami (Head of Palace Attendants).

In the second year (1230), Hōjō Shigoki, Governor of Suruga, went up to the capital and resided at Rokuhara. In the fourth month, Hōjō Tokiuji, Assistant Master of Palace Repairs, went down to Kamakura. In the sixth month, Hōjō Tokiuji died of illness at the age of twenty-eight. In the twelfth month, the daughter of the late Yoriie, known as Take-no-gosho, became Yoritsune's wife. She was fifteen years older than Yoritsune.

In the same month, Matsudono, former Chancellor Motofusa, passed away at the age of eighty-six.

In the third year (1231), in the second month, Yoritsune was promoted to Junior Fourth Rank. In the third month, he was appointed Right Middle Captain.

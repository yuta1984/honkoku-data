He led his troops to attack the Hōjūji Palace, set it on fire, and confined the Cloistered Emperor to the Gojō Palace. Tendai Head Priest Myōun, Mii-dera Chief Administrator Prince Enkyō, and others - those killed numbered in the hundreds. All the court officials fled barefoot in confusion, and many suffered humiliation. Yoshinaka himself took control of Tanba Province, became the son-in-law of former Regent Matsudono Motofusa, and stripped forty-nine people of their court ranks, including Regent Mototō and Minister of the Interior Sanesada. He made Motofusa's son, Middle Counselor Moroie, who was only twelve years old, into Minister of the Interior and regent. In the twelfth month, Yoritomo killed Kazusa no Suke Hirotsune. Later, realizing his innocence, he was said to have regretted it.

In the first year of Antei (1227), in the first month, the Left Minister of Tokudaiji, Fujiwara Kinitsugu, passed away at the age of fifty-three. In the second month, Chōshi, daughter of Chancellor Ieane, entered the imperial palace and became Empress. The former Empress Ariko was removed and given the title Ankamon-in. Although Ariko was older than His Majesty, she was very intimate with him. Since Chōshi is now only nine years old, even if His Majesty wishes to express his feelings to Ariko, he cannot do as his heart desires. In the fourth month, Fujiwara Morotsune resigned from the position of Right Minister. Inner Minister Yoshihira was appointed Left Minister, Grand Counselor Fujiwara Norisane was appointed Right Minister, and Grand Counselor Fujiwara Kanetsune was appointed Inner Minister. Norisane was the eldest son of former Regent Michiie. Kanetsune was the son of Ieane. In the sixth month, Yasutoki's second son Tokizane was killed by a certain Takahashi at the age of sixteen. Takahashi was also captured alive and executed.

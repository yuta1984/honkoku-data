In the first month of the fourteenth year, Minor Secretary Sugawara no Michizane and others were ordered to greet the envoys from Balhae. Michizane was the Minister Sugawara. In the second month, Right Minister Fujiwara no Ujimune passed away. In the third month, Yoshifusa fell ill and was granted 500,000 coins for prayer expenses. He was also granted eighty ordinands. High Priest Shinga was appointed to the Office of Monastic Affairs. In the fifth month, the Balhae envoys entered the capital and were housed at the Kōrokan, where they were granted a banquet. During their stay in the capital, Ariwara no Narihira and others went as imperial messengers to entertain them. In addition, literati went to meet with them. Miyako no Yoshika also greeted them. Yoshika was a person of great learning. Afterwards, the envoys received an imperial edict and returned to their country from the Kōrokan without having an imperial audience. The Kōrokan is the Office for Barbarian Affairs. It is a place where foreigners are housed. It is located near the Rajō Gate of Tōji Temple. In the eighth month, Minamoto no Tōru became Left Minister. Fujiwara no Mototsune became Right Minister.

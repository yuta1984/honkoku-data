In the same month, Yoritsune petitioned the court and transferred the position of Sei-i Taishōgun to Yoritsugu, who was promoted to Junior Fifth Rank Upper Grade and appointed as Minor Captain of the Right. Yoritsune had gone down to Kamakura at age two, was appointed shogun at age nine, and after eighteen years in office, transferred the position to Yoritsugu. Although it is said that he abdicated due to caution regarding the celestial omens, in reality it was likely so that the Hōjō could exercise their authority and do as they pleased - revering the shogun while he was young and weak, then removing him when he matured, and replacing him with a young lord. In the sixth month, Regent Yoshizane resigned from the position of Minister of the Left. His younger brother, Minister of the Right Sanetsune, transferred to the left, and Minister of the Center Fujiwara no Kanehira became Minister of the Right. Major Counselor Tadaie was appointed as Minister of the Center. In the eighth month, Saionji lay priest and Grand Minister Kintsune passed away at age seventy-one.

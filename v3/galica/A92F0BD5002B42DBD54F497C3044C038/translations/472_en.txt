Tsunetoki oversaw this [ceremony]. In the same month, Yoritsune petitioned [the court] and transferred the position of Seii Taishogun to Yoritsugu, who was granted the junior fifth rank upper grade and appointed as Minor Captain of the Right. Yoritsune had gone down to Kamakura at age two, was appointed shogun at age nine, served for eighteen years, and then abdicated in favor of Yoritsugu. Although it is said he abdicated due to caution regarding heavenly portents, in reality this was likely an internal scheme by the Hōjō clan to exercise power as they pleased - venerating [a ruler] when young, removing him upon reaching maturity, and replacing him with another young lord. In the sixth month, Regent Yoshizane resigned from the position of Minister of the Left. His younger brother, Minister of the Right Sanetsune, was transferred to the left, and Interior Minister Fujiwara no Kanehira became Minister of the Right. Major Counselor Tadaie was appointed Interior Minister. In the eighth month, Saionji Nyūdō-Saikoku Kintsune passed away at age seventy-one.

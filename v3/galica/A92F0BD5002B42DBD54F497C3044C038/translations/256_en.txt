Minamoto no Kiyokage and others were sent as imperial messengers to the burial site. [This was during Tadahira's] twenty years as regent.【Note】
It says eight years as chancellor. In the ninth month, Retired Emperor Yōzei passed away at age eighty-one.
In the twelfth month, the scholars Ōe no Tomotsuna, Tachibana no Naomoto, Sugawara no Fumitoki, Ōe no Koretsugu, and others were commanded to select poems, and Ono no Michikaze was made to write those poems on folding screen paintings. The paintings were by the brush of Kose no Kimitada.
In the fourth year, seventh month, the second imperial prince Norihira was made crown prince.
In the fifth year, Fujiwara no Koretada was made director of the Yamato Poetry Bureau, and five men—Minamoto no Shitagau, Ōnakatomi no Yoshinobu, Kiyohara no Motosuke, Ki no Tokifumi, and Sakanoue no Mochiki—were commanded to compile the Gosen Wakashū (Later Selection of Yamato Poetry) in the Nashitsubo. Shitagau was a person of great learning, excellent in both Chinese poetry and prose as well as Yamato poetry.
In the sixth year, eighth month, Retired Emperor Suzaku passed away at age thirty.
In the seventh year, third month, Dainagon and Minister of Popular Affairs Fujiwara no Motomasa died at age sixty.

【Note: Another text says "twelve years"】

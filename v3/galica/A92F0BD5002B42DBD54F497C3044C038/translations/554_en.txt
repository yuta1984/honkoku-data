In the eighth month, Akamatsu Enshin built Kokenawa Castle in Banshū Province and became a supporter of the former emperor. Masashige built Chihaya Castle and fortified himself there. In the ninth month, Takatoki made his relatives Daibutsu Sadanao and Aso Tokiharu, along with Nikaidō Dōun and others, generals and mobilized a great army to dispatch them. In the tenth month, Dainagon Minamoto no Michiakirawas appointed to Naidaijin (Minister of the Center). In the eleventh month, Imadegawa Udaijin Kanesue was appointed to Daijō-daijin (Chancellor of the Realm).

In the first month of the second year, the great forces from the east divided and attacked Yoshino Castle where Prince Morinaga was holed up, Chihaya Castle where Masashige was fortified, and Akasaka Castle where Masashige's retainers were defending.

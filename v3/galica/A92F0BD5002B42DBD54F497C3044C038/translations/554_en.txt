In the eighth month, Akamatsu Enshin built Kokenawa Castle in Banshū Province and sided with the former emperor. Masashige built Chihaya Castle and fortified himself there. In the ninth month, Takatoki made his kinsmen Daibutsu Sadanao and Aso Tokiharu, along with Nikaidō Dōun and others, generals, and mobilized a great army to march to the capital. In the tenth month, Dainagon Minamoto no Michichika was appointed Naidaijin (Minister of the Center). In the eleventh month, Imadegawa Udaijin Kanesue was appointed Daijō-daijin (Chancellor).
In the second year, first month, the great forces from Kantō divided and attacked Yoshino Castle where Prince Morinaga was holed up, Chihaya Castle where Masashige was fortified, and Akasaka Castle where Masashige's retainers were stationed.

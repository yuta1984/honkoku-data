In the sixth month, Silla pirates came to Hakata and attacked tribute ships from Bunzen Province. The Dazaifu dispatched troops to capture them, but the pirate ships quickly fled back home.

In the first month of the twelfth year, Fujiwara no Ujimune became Right Minister. Minamoto no Tōru and Fujiwara no Mototsune became Major Counselors. Tōru was a son of Emperor Saga.

In the second month of the thirteenth year, the Emperor proceeded to the Shishin-den and for the first time personally attended to government affairs. Before Emperor Ninmyō, His Majesty would proceed to the Shishin-den every day. From the reign of Montoku onward, this ceremony had not been performed. Now it returned to the old protocol. Everyone rejoiced. In the fourth month, Yoshifusa was granted additional stipends and awarded personal guards with weapons, and was granted status equivalent to the three imperial princes. The rank of Jun-sankyū (quasi-three princes) began from this time.

In the eighth month, Right Minister Ujimune respectfully compiled and presented the Jōgan Procedures.

In the ninth month, Empress of Gojō, Fujiwara no Junshi, died. She was the empress of Ninmyō and mother of Montoku. In the twelfth month, envoys from the state of Bohai, Yang Chenggui and others, [arrived at] Kaga

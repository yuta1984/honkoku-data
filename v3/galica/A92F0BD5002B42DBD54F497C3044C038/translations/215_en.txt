In the sixth month, Silla pirates came to Hakata and attacked tribute ships from Buzen Province. The Dazaifu dispatched troops to capture them, but the pirate ships quickly fled back.

In the twelfth year, first month, Fujiwara no Ujimune became Right Minister. Minamoto no Tōru and Fujiwara no Mototsune became Major Counselors. Tōru was a son of Emperor Saga.

In the thirteenth year, second month, the Emperor appeared in the Shishin-den and for the first time personally conducted government affairs. Before Emperor Ninmyō, His Majesty would appear in the Shishin-den every day. Since Emperor Montoku's reign, this ceremony had not been performed. Now it returned to the old protocol. Everyone rejoiced. In the fourth month, food stipends were added for Yoshifusa, he was granted armed attendants, and he was accorded treatment equivalent to the three imperial princes. The rank of jun-san-gū (quasi-three-princes) began from this time.

In the eighth month, Right Minister Ujimune respectfully compiled and presented the Jōgan-shiki (Jōgan Ceremonial Code).

In the ninth month, Empress Gojō, Fujiwara no Junshi, passed away. She was the empress of Emperor Ninmyō and mother of Emperor Montoku. In the twelfth month, envoys from the Balhae Kingdom, Yang Chenggui and others, (arrived at) Kaga.

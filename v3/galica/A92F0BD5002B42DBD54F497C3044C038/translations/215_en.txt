In the sixth month, Silla pirates came to Hakata and plundered tribute ships from Bunzen Province. The Dazaifu dispatched troops to capture them, but the pirate ships quickly fled and escaped.

In the first month of the twelfth year, Fujiwara no Ujimune became Right Minister. Minamoto no Tōru and Fujiwara no Mototsune became Major Counselors. Tōru was a son of Emperor Saga.

In the second month of the thirteenth year, the Emperor appeared in the Shishin-den and for the first time listened to government affairs himself. Before Emperor Ninmyō, the sovereign appeared in the Shishin-den every day. From the reign of Emperor Montoku onward, this ceremony was not observed. Now it returned to the old protocol, and all the people rejoiced. In the fourth month, Yoshifusa was granted additional stipends and imperial bodyguards with weapons, and given treatment equivalent to the Three Princely Houses. The rank of Jun-Sankyū (Equivalent to Three Princely Houses) began from this time.

In the eighth month, Right Minister Ujimune respectfully compiled and presented the Jōgan Regulations.

In the ninth month, Empress Gojō, Fujiwara no Junshi, died. She was the empress of Emperor Ninmyō and mother of Emperor Montoku. In the twelfth month, envoys from the kingdom of Balhae, including Yang Chenggui, (arrived) in Kaga.

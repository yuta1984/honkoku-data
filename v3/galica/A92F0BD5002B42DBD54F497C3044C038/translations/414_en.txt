In the same month, Fujiwara no Kanefusa resigned as Daijō-daijin (Chancellor).

In the fourth month of Kenkyū 8 (1197), the Emperor made an imperial visit to Shichijō-in and had an audience with his mother Shokushi. In the twelfth month, Minamoto no Yoriie was appointed to Junior Fifth Rank and made Uchū-shō (Middle Captain of the Right).

In the first month of Kenkyū 9 (1198), the Emperor abdicated the throne to his son Tamehito. He was honored with the title of Daijō Tennō (Retired Emperor). The era names were Genryaku 1 year, Bunji 5 years, and Kenkyū 9 years, with a total reign of fifteen years.

In the fifth year, first month, Prince Nagaya was made Right Minister. Rewards were given to doctors of classical learning (myōkyō hakase) and those with talents and skills. In the twelfth month, the Retired Emperor (Empress Genmei) died at the age of sixty-one.

In the eighth year, first month, the Emperor abdicated the throne to Emperor Shōmu and was styled Retired Emperor. The reign lasted for Reiki 2 years and Yōrō 7 years, totaling nine years.

45th Emperor
Emperor Shōmu - He was the crown prince of Emperor Monmu. His mother was called Fujiwara Lady Miyako. She was the daughter of the posthumously appointed Grand Minister Fujiwara no Fuhito. When Emperor Monmu died, Shōmu did not immediately ascend the throne because he was still young. During Empress Genmei's reign, he became crown prince at age fourteen, and during Empress Genshō's reign, he took charge of and participated in government affairs. At this point, he received the abdication and ascended to the throne.

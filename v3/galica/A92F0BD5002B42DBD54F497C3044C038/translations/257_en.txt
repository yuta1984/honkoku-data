This person's daughter became the Emperor's consort and gave birth to First Prince Hirohira.【Note】However, Second Prince Norihira, being Morosuke's grandson, was made crown prince over the First Prince. Therefore Motomasa harbored resentment, sank into melancholy, and died. Soon after, both the consort and First Prince also passed away. Crown Prince Norihira was afflicted by a disease of evil spirits. It was said to be the vengeful spirit of Motomasa.

In the ninth year, first month, there was a Lotus Sutra lecture at the imperial palace. For the first time, court nobles were made to contribute offerings. In the third month, by oracle of Kitano Tenjin, it is said that a thousand pine trees grew overnight at the Right Palace Guards' riding ground.

In the first year of Tentoku, fourth month, Morosuke's fiftieth birthday was celebrated, a banquet was held at Fujitsubo, and the Emperor bestowed a cup upon Morosuke.

In the second year, third month, Saneyori was granted permission to use a palanquin carriage. In the eleventh month, Minamoto no Tsunemoto died.

Sixty-ninth Emperor
Emperor Go-Suzaku: Son of Emperor Ichijō, personal name Atsunaga. His mother was Retired Empress Jōtōmon'in, making him a full brother of Emperor Go-Ichijō. In Kannin 1, he became Crown Prince. In Chōgen 9, seventh month, he ascended the throne at age twenty-eight. His maternal uncle, Minister of the Left Yorimichi, remained unchanged as regent and continued to conduct government.

In Chōreki 1, first month, Yorimichi's daughter Genshi became imperial consort. When the emperor was still crown prince, Michinaga's daughter Kishi had served him and bore Prince Chikahito, but Kishi died. Afterward, Princess Teishi, daughter of Emperor Sanjō, became his concubine and bore Prince Takahito. Genshi was the daughter of the emperor's elder brother Prince Atsuyasu, whom Yorimichi adopted and had enter the court. In the third month, she was elevated to empress.

Legal judgments and the pursuit and capture of criminals by the Left and Right Gate Guards were all combined under the supervision of the Kebiishi. Under this office, a position called Kado no Osa was established with sixty-six personnel, and one person each was dispatched to the various provinces.

On New Year's Day of Jowa 1, the Emperor received court ceremonies in the Daigokuden Hall. On the second day, he paid a court visit to Retired Emperor Junna. On the third day, Retired Emperor Junna made an imperial visit to Retired Emperor Saga, and they mutually celebrated the New Year. On the seventh day, the Hakuba Sechie ceremony was held for the first time in Burakuden Hall. Sangi Fujiwara no Tsunetsugu was appointed as envoy to Tang China, and Ono no Takamura was appointed as vice-envoy, with other subordinate officials such as judges and recorders also being appointed.

In the second month, there was an imperial visit to the archery grounds, where wagers were set up to test superiority and inferiority in archery skills. The Emperor himself shot arrows, followed by ministers and others below.

In the third month, Retired Emperor Saga made an imperial visit to Right Minister Natsuno's mountain villa at Narabi no Oka.

If your response is unfavorable, you will be subjected to severe punishment," and he glared menacingly and threatened him with his hand on his sword. Kiyomaro made a pilgrimage to Usa and prayed: "This is a matter of great national importance. Even if there should be an oracle, it would be difficult to believe it lightly. I beseech you, please show me one miracle." Thereupon, the great deity suddenly manifested in a form about three jō tall and appeared in divine presence. The light was like that of the full moon. Kiyomaro prostrated himself in worship and could not look up at the deity. The divine oracle said: "The heavenly succession of our country has been, since the age of the gods, generation after generation of imperial lineage only, and no subject should presume to aspire to it. How much less so an immoral person. You should return and report exactly as things are. Do not fear Dōkyō." Since Kiyomaro was originally a man of loyalty, he took the divine oracle to heart, returned to the capital, and entered the palace. Dōkyō attended before the Emperor, leaning on a chair, and called Kiyomaro to hear the oracle.

They also dispatched troops to attack Akasaka Castle. Masashige resisted for a while but then secretly left the castle and hid in Mount Kongō. Prince Son'un hid in the vicinity of Totsukawa. The close retainers Fusafusa, Suefusa and others were all imprisoned. Prince Takayoshi and the other imperial princes were all taken alive. The era names of this reign were Gennin (2 years), Genkō (3 years), Shōchū (2 years), Karyaku (3 years), Gentoku (2 years), and Genkō (1 year), totaling thirteen years on the throne.


The Ninety-sixth Emperor

Emperor Kōgon. His personal name was Kazuhito. He was the first son of Retired Emperor Gofushimi. His mother was Kōgimmon'in, daughter of Minister of the Left Saionji Kinhira. When Go-Daigo ascended the throne, he was made Crown Prince through the arrangements of Hōjō Takatoki.

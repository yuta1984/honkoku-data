The close retainers Fusafusa, Suefusa and others were all captured. The First Prince Takayoshi and the other imperial princes below him were all taken alive as prisoners. The era names of this reign were Gennō two years, Genkō three years, Shōchū two years, Karyaku three years, Gentoku two years, and Genkō one year.
In total, [his] reign lasted thirteen years.

Ninety-sixth Emperor
Emperor Kōgon: His personal name was Kazuhito. He was the first son of Retired Emperor Gofushimi. His mother was Kōgimon'in, daughter of Minister of the Left Saionji Kinhira. When Go-Daigo ascended the throne, by Takatoki's arrangement, [Kazuhito] was made Crown Prince.
In the tenth month of the first year of Genkō, when Go-Daigo entered Rokuhara from Kasagi.

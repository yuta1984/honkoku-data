In the eighth month, there was a poetry gathering at the Middle Palace. In the tenth month, Sanjō Inner Palace Minister Fujiwara no Kimifusa was appointed Grand Minister of State. Sanetomo was appointed Inner Palace Minister. He remained General of the Left as before. Taira no Masako was granted the court rank of Junior Second Rank. She took the title of Second Rank Zen Nun. In the eleventh month, Minister of the Left Fujiwara no Ryōsuke died. He was the younger brother of Go-Kyōgoku Yoshitsune. In the twelfth month, Minister of the Right Fujiwara no Michiie was appointed Minister of the Left. Sanetomo was appointed Minister of the Right. Major Counselor Fujiwara no Iemichi was appointed Inner Palace Minister. By order of Retired Emperor Go-Toba, various ceremonial items for the minister's congratulatory ceremony were bestowed upon Sanetomo. Bōmon Major Counselor Fujiwara no Tadakiyo, his son Nobukiyo, Saionji Middle Counselor Fujiwara no Sanetoshi (son of Kintsune), Councillor Fujiwara no Kunitō, and several other court nobles went to Kamakura to serve as escorts.

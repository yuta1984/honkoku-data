In the third month of the fourth year, Ariwara no Narihira was promoted to Junior Fifth Rank Upper Grade. He was a grandson of Emperor Heizei and son of Prince Abo.

In the first month of the fifth year, Major Counselor and General of the Right Minamoto no Sadamu died. He was a beloved son of Retired Emperor Saga. In the same month, his elder brother Major Counselor Minamoto no Hiroshi also died. This person loved learning, excelled in calligraphy, and was fond of music. In the fifth month, at Shinsen'en, they held rituals for the vengeful spirits of Emperor Sudo, Prince Iyo, Fujiwara Lady Yoshiko, Tachibana no Hayanari, and Bunroomiya Tamaro. This was called the Goryōe (Imperial Spirit Festival). In recent years, epidemics had continued to spread, and from the spring of this year they became particularly severe, causing many deaths. Because some people said this might be the work of these evil spirits, this festival was held. In the tenth month, Yoshifusa was summoned and given a banquet to celebrate his sixtieth birthday. In this year, Yoshifusa respectfully commissioned Haruzumi Yoshinawa to compile the Shoku Nihon Kōki (Later Chronicle of Japan Continued).

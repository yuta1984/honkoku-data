In the fourth year, third month, Ariwara no Narihira was appointed to the rank of Junior Fifth Rank Upper Grade. He was the grandson of Emperor Heizei and son of Prince Abo.

In the fifth year, first month, Major Counselor and General of the Right Minamoto no Sadamu died. He was the beloved son of Retired Emperor Saga. In the same month, his elder brother Major Counselor Minamoto no Hiroshi died. This person loved learning, was accomplished in calligraphy, and was fond of music. In the fifth month, at Shinsen'en garden, they held rituals for the vengeful spirits of Emperor Sudo, Prince Iyo, Fujiwara Lady Yoshiko, Tachibana no Hayanari, Bunshitsu no Miyatamaro, and others. This is called the Goryōe ceremony. In recent years, epidemic diseases had continued to spread, and from the spring of this year they became particularly severe, causing many people to die. Because some people said this might be the work of these evil spirits, this ritual was performed. In the tenth month, Yoshifusa was summoned and granted a banquet to celebrate his sixtieth birthday. In this year, Yoshifusa respectfully commissioned Haruzumi no Yoshinawa to compile the Shoku Nihon Kōki.

In the third month of the fourth year, Ariwara no Narihira was granted the rank of Junior Fifth Rank Upper Grade. He was the grandson of Emperor Heizei and the son of Prince Abo.

In the first month of the fifth year, Minamoto no Sadamu, Major Counselor and Right General, passed away. He was a beloved son of Retired Emperor Saga. In the same month, his elder brother Minamoto no Hiromi, Major Counselor, also passed away. Hiromi was fond of learning, accomplished in calligraphy, and enjoyed music.

In the fifth month, at the Shinsen'en Garden, the vengeful spirits of Emperor Sudō, Prince Iyo, Lady Fujiwara Yoshiko, Tachibana no Hayanari, and Funya no Miyatamaro were placated in a ceremony. This was called the Goryōe. As epidemic disease had continued to spread in recent years, and especially severe since the spring of this year with many people dying, some suggested it was the work of these malevolent spirits, and thus this ceremony was performed.

In the tenth month, Yoshifusa was summoned and given a banquet in celebration of his sixtieth birthday. In this year, Yoshifusa accepted the commission and had Haruzumi no Yoshinawa compile the Shoku Nihon Kōki.

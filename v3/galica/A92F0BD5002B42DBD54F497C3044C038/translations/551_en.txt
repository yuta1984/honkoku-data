In the first year of Genkō, second month (1331), Fujiwara no Motoshige became Minister of the Left. Kuga Nagamichi became Minister of the Right. Saionji Suehira became Minister of the Center.

In the third month, there was an imperial excursion to Kitayama with flower-viewing entertainment. In the eighth month, two envoys from the Kantō came to the capital. This was to exile the Emperor and Prince Son'un. The Emperor, in fear, secretly made an imperial excursion to Mount Kasagi. Middle Counselor Banri-kōji Fusafusa and his younger brother Suefusa accompanied him. Major Counselor Kazanin Morokata pretended to impersonate the Emperor and climbed Mount Hiei to gather troops. [Forces] from Rokuhara dispatched soldiers to attack Mount Hiei. During this time, the Emperor entered Kasagi. Morokata also went to Kasagi. The Emperor summoned the warrior Kusunoki Masashige from Kawachi Province and entrusted him with military affairs.

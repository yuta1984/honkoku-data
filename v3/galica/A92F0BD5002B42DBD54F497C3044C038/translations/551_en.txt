志あり (there was intent/determination)

In the first year of Genkō, second month, Right Minister Fujiwara no Motetsugu became Left Minister. Kuga no Nagamichi became Right Minister. Saionji no Suehira became Naidaijin (Interior Minister). In the third month, there was an imperial visit to Kitayama with flower-viewing festivities. In the eighth month, two envoys from the east (Kamakura) came up to the capital. This was to exile the Emperor and Prince Son'un. The Emperor, in fear, secretly made an imperial progress to Mount Kasagi. Middle Counselor Manjūkōji Fusafusa and his younger brother Suefusa accompanied him. Kazanoin Great Counselor Morokata pretended to impersonate the Emperor and climbed Mount Hiei to gather troops. Forces were sent from Rokuhara to attack Mount Hiei. During this time, the Emperor entered Kasagi. Morokata also went to Kasagi. The Emperor summoned the warrior of Kawachi Province, Kusunoki Masashige, and entrusted military affairs to him. Masashige returned to Kawachi, raised a righteous army, and fortified himself at Mount Akasaka. In the ninth month, a great army from the east attacked and broke through Kasagi. The Emperor left the mountain...

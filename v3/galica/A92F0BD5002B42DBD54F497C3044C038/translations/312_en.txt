However, his authority and power were inferior to those of his father and elder brother. In the 11th month, there was an imperial visit to Hirano and Kitano shrines. In the 12th month, Enshū-ji temple was built, and there was an imperial visit on the day of its consecration ceremony.

In the 1st month of the 3rd year, there were imperial visits to Inari and Gion shrines. In the 8th month, the Emperor moved to the newly constructed imperial palace, and the congratulatory ceremonies were conducted with great formality. In the 11th month, there was the first imperial visit to Hiyoshi Shrine.

In this year, barbarian rebels in Ōshū rose in revolt. Mutsu no Kami Minamoto no Yoritoshi suppressed and pacified them.

In the 10th month of the 4th year, there was an imperial visit to Enshū-ji temple. There was a doctrinal debate between monks from Mii-dera and Kōfuku-ji temples. In the 12th month, the Emperor abdicated the throne to Crown Prince Sadahito. Reign: 4 years. Era name: Enkyū.

Seventy-second Emperor

The 77th Emperor
Emperor Go-Shirakawa: The fourth son of Emperor Toba. His personal name was Masahito. His mother was Taikenmon-in. He was born from the same womb as Emperor Sutoku.

In the seventh month of the second year of Kyūju, Emperor Konoe passed away. Regarding who should succeed to the imperial throne next, Retired Emperor Toba and Bifukumon-in consulted together and discussed making Princess Shōshi, who was born from Bifukumon-in's womb, into an empress regnant. However, since there had been no empress regnant since Empress Shōtoku, they decided upon Masahito as the sovereign of succession and had him ascend the throne. He was twenty-nine years old at the time.

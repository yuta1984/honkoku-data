In the second year, fourth month, Right Minister Fujiwara no Akitada passed away at the age of sixty-eight. In the twelfth month, there was a celebration for the Emperor's fortieth birthday.

In the third year, first month, Minamoto no Takaakira was appointed Right Minister. He was a prince of Emperor Daigo (the Engi Emperor). In the eighth month, Ritshi Genshin became the Chief Abbot of Tendai. This is Jie Sōjō (the compassionate monk-priest).

On the twenty-fifth day of the fifth month of the fourth year, the Emperor passed away at the age of forty-two. The era names were Tenryaku (10 years), Tentoku (4 years), Ōwa (3 years), and Kōhō (4 years), with a total reign of twenty-one years.

Sixty-third Emperor
Emperor Reizei: The second prince of Emperor Murakami, his personal name was Norihira. His mother was Empress Anshi, daughter of Right Minister Morosuke. He was born in the fifth month of Tenryaku 4 and became Crown Prince in the seventh month. From the second month of Kōhō 4, he suffered from an illness caused by evil spirits, and his mental state was not normal. When Emperor Murakami died in the fifth month, the Crown Prince ascended to the throne at Gyōka-sha. He was ten years old.

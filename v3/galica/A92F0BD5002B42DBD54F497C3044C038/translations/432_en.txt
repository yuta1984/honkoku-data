In the ninth month, Fujiwara no Teika presented the Shin Kokin-shū to Sanetomo, because Sanetomo loved waka poetry. In the eleventh month, Dainagon Fujiwara no Sanemune was appointed Naidaijin (Minister of the Interior). In the twelfth month, through Masako's arrangement, Ieie's son Zensai became a disciple of Sonkyo, the head priest of Tsurugaoka Hachiman-gū. Later he became Sanetomo's adopted son. Zensai took Buddhist vows and took the name Kugyō.

In the first year of Jōei (1206), in the second month, Sanetomo was promoted to Junior Fourth Rank Lower Grade. In the third month, the Retired Emperor was about to visit the residence of Regent Yoshitsune. On the night before, someone entered Yoshitsune's bedchamber and stabbed him to death with a spear from the ceiling. He was thirty-eight years old. It is unknown who committed this act.

In the first month of the third year, Oki no Kami Tokikiyo and Yamashiro no Kami Yukisada came up to the capital from Kamakura as envoys and had His Majesty abdicate the throne in favor of the Crown Prince. His Majesty was fourteen years old when he received the title of Retired Emperor. The era name was Shōan and he reigned for three years.

Ninety-third Emperor
Emperor Go-Nijō: His personal name was Kuniharu. He was the first son of Retired Emperor Go-Uda. His mother was Minamoto no Motoko, daughter of Kuga Dainagon Tomimori. He was made Crown Prince at thirteen years old. In the first month of Shōan 3, through the arrangements of the military government, he ascended the throne at seventeen years old. Nijō Kanenobu served as Kampaku. Retired Emperor Kameyama and Retired Emperor Go-Uda conducted government affairs from their cloistered courts.

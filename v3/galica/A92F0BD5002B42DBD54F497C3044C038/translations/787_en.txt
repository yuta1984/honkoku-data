The *Nihon Ōdai Ichiran* (A Survey of the Reigns of Japanese Sovereigns) spans from Emperor Jinmu to the Ōgimachi-in, comprising seven volumes in total. In response to the request of the Lord of Wakasa Province, Junior Fourth Rank, Left Minor Captain Minamoto no Tadakatsu Ason, the national histories and miscellaneous records were examined, their redundancies removed and their essentials extracted, and the work was newly compiled and hereby respectfully presented.

　　An auspicious day in the fifth month of the fifth year of Keian (1652), the year of the Water Dragon.

　　　　　Hōgen Shunsai, Hayashi Jo (Shinobu)

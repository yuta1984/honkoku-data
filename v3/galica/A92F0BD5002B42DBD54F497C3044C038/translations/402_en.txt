Fujiwara no Tsunemune died at the age of seventy-one. In the third month, Yoritomo petitioned the court, requesting to attack Yasuhira. Imperial permission was not granted. In the fourth month, Major Counselor Fujiwara no Tomosuke, Third Rank Fujiwara no Yoritsune, Treasury Minister Takashina no Yasetsune, and other court officials who had been close to Yoshitsune were either exiled or dismissed from office. A person called Yoshida Middle Counselor Fujiwara no Tsunefusa, who shared aspirations with Yoritomo and was on intimate terms with him, was used for all petitions to the court. In the intercalary fourth month, Yasuhira, going against his father's dying words, followed the imperial command and fearing Yoritomo's power, attacked the Koromo River mansion where Yoshitsune resided. Yoshitsune fought for a while, killed his wife and children, and then took his own life. He was thirty-one years old. Yasuhira sent his head to Kamakura. Since Yasuhira's younger brother Tadahira had been allied with Yoshitsune, Yasuhira killed him. However, Yoritomo's anger did not subside, and he gathered military forces to attack Yasuhira. Although the court ordered him to cease and desist, Yoritomo did not comply. In the seventh month, Tokudaiji Right Minister Sanesada (died).

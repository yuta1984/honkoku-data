The 83rd Emperor
Tsuchimikado-in was the crown prince of Go-Toba-in. His personal name was Tamehito. His mother was Minamoto no Ariko, who was titled Shōmyō-mon-in. She was the daughter of the Hōin (Buddhist priest) Nōen, but was adopted by the Naidaijin (Minister of the Center) Minamoto no Michichika and presented to Go-Toba-in. In the 12th month of Kenkyū 6, Tamehito was born. In the 1st month of Kenkyū 9, he received the abdication and ascended to the throne in the 3rd month. At that time he was four years old. The Konoe Kanpaku Fujiwara no Motomichi became regent. The title of Daijō Tennō was bestowed upon Go-Toba-in. Government affairs were conducted from the retired emperor's court. However, in all matters, everything was arranged by Minamoto no Yoritomo from Kantō.

In the 10th month, the Kyoto protector, Chūnagon Fujiwara no Yoshiyasu, died at the age of fifty-two.

While his son Yoshitaka defended and died in battle, Morinaga escaped and hid in the deep mountains. After that, military forces from all directions surrounded and attacked Chihaya Castle. They numbered in the hundreds of thousands. Masashige employed various clever strategies to defend, so many of the attacking forces were killed. Nitta Yoshisada had also joined these attacking forces, but he secretly communicated with Morinaga and planned to return to Kōzuke Province to raise a righteous army. In the same month, Akamatsu Enshin advanced to Maya Castle in Settsu Province, and in Iyo Province, Doi, Tokuno and others raised righteous armies. In the third month, Akamatsu Enshin attacked up to Kyoto. Due to this, the new emperor made an imperial visit to Rokuhara, and both Rokuhara forces repeatedly fought with Akamatsu. Around this time in Tsukushi, Kikuchi Jaku, Shōni Myōe, and Ōtomo Gukan conspired to attack Tandai Hōjō Hidetoki, but since two of them instead rescued Hidetoki, Kikuchi was killed. In the same month, former Emperor Go-Daigo secretly escaped from Oki Province, went to Hōki Province, relied on Nawa Nagatoshi, and entered Senjo-san (Mount Senjō).

While his son Yoshitaka fought defensively and died in battle, Morinaga escaped and hid in the deep mountains. After that, military forces from various regions all surrounded and attacked Chihaya Castle. They numbered in the hundreds of thousands. Because Masashige employed various ingenious strategies to defend, many of the besieging forces were killed. Nitta Yoshisada was also among these besieging forces, but he secretly communicated with Morinaga and returned to Kōzuke Province to raise a righteous army. In the same month, Akamatsu Enshin advanced to Maya Castle in Settsu Province, and in Iyo Province, Doi Tokuno and others raised a righteous army. In the third month, Akamatsu Enshin attacked up to the capital. Due to this, the new emperor made an imperial visit to Rokuhara. Both Rokuhara forces frequently fought battles with Akamatsu. Around this time, in Tsukushi, Kikuchi Jakua, Shōni Myōe, and Ōtomo Gukan conspired to attack Tandai Hōjō Hideki, but when the two instead saved Hideki, Kikuchi was killed.

In the same month, the former Emperor Go-Daigo secretly escaped from Oki Province, went to Hōki Province, relied on Nawa Nagatoshi, and entered Mount Senjo.

The 71st Emperor
Emperor Go-Sanjō: The second son of Emperor Go-Suzaku. His personal name was Takahito. He was the half-brother of Emperor Go-Reizei. His mother was called Yōmeimon-in Teishi, daughter of Emperor Sanjō. Through both his paternal and maternal lineages, he inherited the throne by combining the two imperial lines of Reizei and En'yū. On the 16th day of the 1st month of Kantoku 2 (1045), Emperor Go-Suzaku, due to illness, abdicated the throne to Emperor Go-Reizei. At that time, Major Counselor Fujiwara no Yoshinobu came before the emperor and asked, "Shall the Second Prince Takahito be made a monk?" Emperor Go-Suzaku heard this and declared, "He should be made the next Crown Prince." Yoshinobu said, "If that is so, there should be an early decision." Emperor Go-Suzaku said, "The appointment of the Crown Prince is not a matter to be delayed. If I speak to Regent Yorimichi, there will be further discussion of the matter."

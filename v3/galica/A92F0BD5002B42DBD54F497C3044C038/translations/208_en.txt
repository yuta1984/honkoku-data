Furthermore, divine ranks were granted to many shrines throughout the provinces. Right Minister Fujiwara no Yoshisuke built Sōshin-in to house members of the Fujiwara clan who had no residence, and built Enmei-in to house sick members of the Fujiwara clan. In the third month, Wake no Tsunenori and others were sent as imperial envoys to Usa Hachiman Shrine to report the enthronement. Once in each emperor's reign, imperial envoys are dispatched to Usa. Each time, members of the Wake clan are appointed. This likely follows the precedent set by Kiyomaro. In the fourth month, hunting grounds in Settsu Province were granted to Left Minister Minamoto no Makoto. In the same month, the Left and Right Palace Guards, Left and Right Gate Guards, and Left and Right Military Guards were ordered to provide security for the Kamo Festival. In the fifth month, the ship of the Balhae envoy U Kōshin arrived in Kaga Province. Abe no Kiyoyuki was dispatched to Kaga Province to receive the diplomatic correspondence and present it to the capital. He was given an imperial edict and ordered to return directly without entering the capital.

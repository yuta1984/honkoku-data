Divine ranks were bestowed upon many shrines in various provinces. Right Minister Fujiwara no Yoshisuke built Sōshin-in to house members of the Fujiwara clan who had no residences, and built Enmei-in to house sick members of the Fujiwara clan. In the third month, Wake no Tsunenori and others were sent as imperial envoys to Usa Hachiman to report the enthronement. Once in each emperor's reign, imperial envoys are dispatched to Usa. Each time, the Wake clan is commissioned. This would be following the precedent of Kiyomaro. In the fourth month, hunting grounds in Settsu Province were granted to Left Minister Minamoto no Makoto. In the same month, for the Kamo Festival, the Left and Right Imperial Guard Headquarters, Left and Right Gate Guard Headquarters, and Left and Right Military Guard Headquarters were ordered to provide security.

In the fifth month, the ship of Bokkai envoy U Kōshin arrived in Kaga Province. Abe no Kiyoyuki was sent to Kaga Province to receive the diplomatic documents and present them to the capital. He was given an imperial edict and made to return directly without entering the capital.

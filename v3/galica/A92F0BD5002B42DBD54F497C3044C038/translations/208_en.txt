Furthermore, divine court ranks were granted to many shrines throughout the various provinces. The Minister of the Right, Fujiwara no Yoshisuke, built Sōshin-in to house members of the Fujiwara clan who had no home, and built Enmei-in to house members of the Fujiwara clan who were ill. In the third month, Wake no Tsunenori and others were dispatched as imperial envoys to Usa Hachiman Shrine to report the enthronement. Once per reign, imperial envoys are sent to Usa. The fact that the Wake clan is entrusted with this duty each time is likely derived from the precedent set by Wake no Kiyomaro. In the fourth month, land for hunting in Settsu Province was granted to the Minister of the Left, Minamoto no Makoto. In the same month, the Left and Right Divisions of the Inner Palace Guards, the Left and Right Gate Guards, and the Left and Right Outer Palace Guards were ordered to provide security for the Kamo Festival. In the fifth month, a ship carrying Ū Kōshin, an envoy from Balhae, arrived in Kaga Province. Abe no Yukiyuki was dispatched to Kaga Province to receive the envoy's correspondence and present it to the capital; the envoy was granted an imperial letter but was made to return home directly without proceeding to the capital.

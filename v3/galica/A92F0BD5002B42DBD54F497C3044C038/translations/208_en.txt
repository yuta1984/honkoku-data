In addition, divine ranks were granted to many shrines throughout the provinces. Right Minister Fujiwara no Yoshisuke built Sōshin-in to house members of the Fujiwara clan who had no residence, and built Enmei-in to house sick members of the Fujiwara clan. In the third month, Wake no Tsunenori and others were sent as imperial envoys to Usa Hachiman to report the emperor's accession. Each emperor sends imperial envoys to Usa once per reign. The Wake clan is always appointed for this duty. This likely follows the precedent set by Kiyomaro. In the fourth month, hunting grounds in Settsu Province were granted to Left Minister Minamoto no Makoto. In the same month, for the Kamo Festival, the Left and Right Palace Guards Headquarters, Left and Right Gate Guards Headquarters, and Left and Right Military Guards Headquarters were ordered to provide security.
In the fifth month, the ship of the Balhae envoy U Kōshin arrived in Kaga Province. Abe no Kiyoyuki was sent to Kaga Province, received their diplomatic correspondence and presented it to the capital, was granted imperial documents in return, and made them return to their country directly without entering the capital.

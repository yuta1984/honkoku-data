Furthermore, divine court ranks were granted to many shrines throughout the provinces. Right Minister Fujiwara no Yoshisuke built Sōshin-in to house members of the Fujiwara clan who had no residence, and built Enmei-in to house sick members of the Fujiwara clan. In the third month, Wake no Tsunenori and others were sent as imperial envoys to Usa Hachiman to report the enthronement. Imperial envoys are sent to Usa once in each emperor's reign. Each time, members of the Wake clan are appointed. This probably follows the precedent of Kiyomaro. In the fourth month, hunting grounds in Settsu Province were granted to Left Minister Minamoto no Makoto. In the same month, during the Kamo Festival, the Left and Right Palace Guards Bureaus, Left and Right Gate Guards Bureaus, and Left and Right Military Guards Bureaus were ordered to provide security.

In the fifth month, the ship of Balhae envoy U Kōshin arrived in Kaga Province. Abe no Kiyoyuki was dispatched to Kaga Province to receive their documents and present them to the capital. He was given an imperial edict and made them return directly to their country without entering the capital.

In the tenth month, Right Minister Minamoto no Tōru passed away at the age of fifty-nine. He was a son of Emperor Ninmyō. In the eleventh month, the Daijōsai (Great Harvest Festival) was held.

In the first year of Kanpyō (889), on New Year's Day of the first month, the Shihōhai (worship of the four directions) was performed. From this time forward, it was performed every year in this manner. Senior Counselor Fujiwara no Yoshiyo became Left General. Middle Counselor Minamoto no Yoshiari became Right General. Yoshiyo was the younger brother of Yoshifusa. Yoshiari was a son of Emperor Montoku. In the fifth month, Prince Takamochi was granted the Taira surname. He was the great-grandson of Emperor Kanmu, grandson of Prince Kazurahara, and son of Prince Takami. He was the ancestor of Kiyomori and also of the Hōjō clan. In the tenth month, retired Emperor Yōzei developed a mental illness, bound women with koto strings and submerged them in water.

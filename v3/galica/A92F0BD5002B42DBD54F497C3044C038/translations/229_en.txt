In the 9th month, he ordered the court painter Kose no Kanaoka to paint pictures on the east-west sliding doors of the southern eaves of the imperial palace. In the 10th month, Right Minister Minamoto no Tōru passed away at the age of fifty-nine. He was a son of Emperor Ninmyō. In the 11th month, the Great Thanksgiving Festival (Daijōsai) was held.

In Kanpyō 1, on New Year's Day of the 1st month, the Four Directions Worship was performed. From this time forward, this ceremony was performed every year. Major Counselor Fujiwara no Yoshiyo became Left General. Middle Counselor Minamoto no Yoshiari became Right General. Yoshiyo was the younger brother of Fujiwara no Yoshifusa. Yoshiari was a son of Emperor Montoku. In the 5th month, Prince Takamochi was granted the surname Taira. He was the great-grandson of Emperor Kanmu, grandson of Prince Kazurahara, and son of Prince Takami. He was the ancestor of Taira no Kiyomori and also of the Hōjō clan. In the 10th month, Retired Emperor Yōzei developed a mental illness, and he bound women with koto strings and submerged them in water, and at other times he would mount a horse and ride out chasing people.

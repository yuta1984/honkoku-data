He was taken alive captive in Silla, and was told to surrender. He refused to comply. The Silla people drew their swords and threatened him, made Ikina turn his buttocks toward Japan, and demanded he say "Japanese general, eat my buttocks." Ikina raised his voice and called out "King of Silla, eat my buttocks." The enemy became angry and killed him. After that, Silla also submitted to Japan. In the final years of the Emperor's reign, because there was a divine oracle for the first time, Hachiman Great God was worshipped and enshrined in Usa District of Buzen Province. It is said that Kamo Myōjin of Yamashiro Province was also first worshipped in this reign. The Emperor reigned for thirty-two years and then passed away.

The Thirty-First Emperor
Emperor Bidatsu: He was the crown prince of Emperor Kinmei. His mother was called Ishihime, daughter of Emperor Senka. At the beginning of the Emperor's reign, he made Mononobe no Moriya the Great Muraji, and Soga no Umako the Great Minister. Moriya was the son of Okoshi. Umako was the son of Iname.

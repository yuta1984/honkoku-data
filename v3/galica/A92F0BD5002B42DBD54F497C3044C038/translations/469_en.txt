Yoshikage hurried to enter the capital and went to the palace of Shōmyōmon-in to convey Yasutoki's message. Both Shūmyōmon-in (mother of Emperor Juntoku) and Dōke were greatly surprised, but since Yoshikage was conveying Yasutoki's orders, they could not raise any objections. On the 20th of the same month, Prince Kunihito (aged 22 or 23) ascended the throne, with Left Minister Fujiwara no Yoshizane performing the crowning ceremony and Left Middle Controller Sadatsugu performing the hair-cutting ritual. The political ceremonies began in the second month, he was enthroned as Emperor Go-Saga in the third month, and Yoshizane became regent. Yoshizane was Dōke's second son, and the Nijō family line began from him. In the sixth month, Kitsuko, daughter of Saionji Right Minister Saneuji, entered the court and became consort (aged 18). On the 15th of the same month, Hōjō Musashi no Kami Yasutoki died (aged 60). From the first year of Gen'nin until this year, during his 19 years as regent, he conducted politics without personal bias and judged matters righteously, so both the court nobles and warrior families relied on him, and the realm was peaceful.

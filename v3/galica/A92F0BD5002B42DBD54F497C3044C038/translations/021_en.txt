Fifth Generation
Emperor Kōshō - He was the crown prince of Itoku. His mother was called Ama-toyotsu-hime. She was the granddaughter of Annei and the daughter of Ikishi-mimi-no-Mikoto. At this time, the capital was moved to Wakinokami in Yamato, and he resided at Ikegokoro Palace. Ideishi-kokoro-ōomi-no-Mikoto and Okitsu-yoso-no-Mikoto administered the government. He reigned for eighty-three years and passed away at the age of one hundred and fourteen.

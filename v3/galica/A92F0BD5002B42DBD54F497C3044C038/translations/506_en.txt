In the second month of the sixth year of Kōan (1283), Tokimune had Hōjō Naritoki assist with the duties of shikken (regent). Naritoki was the fifth son of Shigetoki.

In the first month of the seventh year of Kōan (1284), Kuga Dainagon Minamoto no Motomoto was promoted to the first rank below junior first rank and resigned from his official position. In the second month, he was permitted to attend court with the same status as a minister, was given the title Gidō Sanshi, and was ranked below ministers but above major counselors. On the fourth day of the fourth month, Hōjō Sagami-no-kami Tokimune took the tonsure due to illness and took the religious name Dōka. He died on the same day at the age of thirty-four. [His temple] was called Hōkō-ji. He had served as shikken for twenty-one years from the first year of Bun'ei until this year.

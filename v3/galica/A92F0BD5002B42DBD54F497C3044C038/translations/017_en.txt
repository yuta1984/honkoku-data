Chronicle of Japan's Royal Dynasties, Volume One
　　First Human Emperor
Emperor Jinmu: Fifth generation descendant from Amaterasu Omikami. He was the fourth son of Ugayafukiaezu-no-Mikoto. His mother was called Tamayori-hime, daughter of the Dragon God. Emperor Jinmu became crown prince at age fifteen. At age forty-five, he launched a naval expedition from Hyuga Province, pacified Tsukushi, and proceeded to Aki Province. From there he arrived in Kibi Province, where he organized his warships, gathered provisions, and remained for three years. (Kibi Province corresponds to present-day Bizen, Bitchu, and Bingo provinces.) From there, he passed through Naniwa and Kawachi, and at a place called Kusae-no-saka in Yamato Province, he fought against a great enemy named Nagasune-hiko. He also fought repeatedly in Nagusa and Kumano in Kii Province.

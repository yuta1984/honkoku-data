(Courtier Toshitsune and physician Takama were exiled to Sanuki Province. Sadamune, Assistant of the Bureau of Divination (Onmyō-no-suke), was arrested and imprisoned. This was because rumors had spread that these individuals had used foxes to curse Yoshimochi.) In the tenth month, Yoshimochi recovered from his illness.

In the same month, Hirohashi Grand Counselor Kananobu, Uramatsu Counselor Yoshisuke, Hino Counselor Arimitsu, and Kajūji Left Middle Controller Tsuneoki and others presented themselves at the Muromachi Palace, but were all, without apparent reason, confined to their residences under house arrest (chikkyo). After the passage of some years, they were pardoned. In the twelfth month, Saionji Sanénaga resigned from the position of Minister of the Right. Sanjō Kimimitsu was transferred to become Minister of the Right, and Ōinomikado Grand Counselor Muneuji was appointed Minister of the Center (Naidaijin).

In the twenty-eighth year, on the first day of the first month, Yoshimochi attended the Imperial Court and paid respects at the Retired Emperor's residence (In-san). In the same month, Kido, the Governor of Suruga, arrived in the capital as an envoy of Kamakura's Superintendent of Left Imperial Guards Mochiuji, to express gratitude for Mochiuji's promotion to the Third Rank and to congratulate Yoshimochi on his recovery from illness. In the fourth month, Ōinomikado Naidaijin Muneuji passed away. In the seventh month, Ichijō Grand Counselor Kaneyoshi was appointed Minister of the Center.

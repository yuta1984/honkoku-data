Fujiwara no Tokihira was appointed to the position of Major Counselor and concurrently served as Left General. Minamoto no Hikaru and Minister Sugawara were made Provisional Major Counselors. Minister Sugawara served concurrently as Right General and, like Tokihira, administered government affairs. The ministerial positions were left vacant and not yet filled. On the second day of the seventh month, the Emperor abdicated his throne to Crown Prince Atsuhito and retired to Suzaku-in at the age of thirty. He was also called Teishi-in. Later, because he shaved his head, he was also called Kampyō Hōō (Dharma Emperor Kampyō). Initially, he did not change the era name in the year following his accession, continuing to use Ninna 4 from Emperor Kōkō's reign. Subsequently, he reigned for nine years under the Kampyō era name, for a total reign of ten years.

60th Sovereign
Emperor Daigo: He was the first son of Emperor Uda. His personal name was Atsuhito. His mother was called Fujiwara no Taneko, daughter of Middle Counselor Takafuji. He became Crown Prince in Kampyō 5. He came of age on the third day of the seventh month of the ninth year at the age of thirteen. On the same day, he received the abdication and ascended the throne.

[Emperor Reizei, aged] eighteen. In the sixth month, Fujiwara no Saneyori was appointed Regent (kanpaku). In the twelfth month, Saneyori was appointed Grand Minister of State (daijō-daijin). Minamoto no Takaakira was transferred to the position of Minister of the Left (sadaijin), and Fujiwara no Morotada was appointed Minister of the Right (udaijin). Morotada was the younger brother of Saneyori. The Emperor's younger brother was called Tamehira, and his [next] younger brother was called Morihira. Tamehira was a beloved son of Emperor Murakami, and was the son-in-law of the Minister of the Left, Takaakira. Even after the Emperor's accession, since his illness showed no signs of recovery, people generally expected that Tamehira would be installed as Crown Prince. However, perhaps due to the discord between Saneyori and Takaakira, it was declared to be the dying wish (yuichoku) of Emperor Murakami, and Morihira was installed as Crown Prince (tōgū).

In the first year of An'wa [968], since the Emperor's afflictions arose frequently, most of the affairs of the court were conducted by Saneyori, Takaakira, and Morotada.

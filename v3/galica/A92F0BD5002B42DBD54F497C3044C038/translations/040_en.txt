it is said. The empress returned to Tsukushi and gave birth to a prince. This was Emperor Ōjin. That place was named Umi. Here, the empress returned to Toyoura, conducted the funeral rites for Emperor Chūai, and proceeded to Yamato. At this time, two sons of Emperor Chūai by his concubine, Prince Kagosaka and Prince Oshikuma, raised troops and blocked the empress in Harima Province, saying: "We are the elder brothers. What the empress has given birth to is the younger brother. Why should we submit to him?" At that time, Prince Kagosaka went out hunting and was killed by a red boar. Prince Oshikuma retreated and set up camp near Uji in Yamashiro Province. The empress made Takeuchi no Sukune the general and attacked Prince Oshikuma. Takeuchi deceived him, saying: "Prince Oshikuma should ascend to the imperial throne. The empress and her son should submit and serve you." When Oshikuma rejoiced and let his guard down, Takeuchi suddenly attacked, so Oshikuma was defeated, fled, and drowned himself in Seta. As a result, the empress took charge of governing the realm and resided at the Iware Palace in Yamato.

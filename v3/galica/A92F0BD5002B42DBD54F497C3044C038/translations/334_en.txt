In the eleventh month, Minister of the Left Minamoto no Toshifusa died at the age of eighty-seven.

In the twelfth month of the third year, Minister of the Right Minamoto no Masazane was appointed as Chancellor (Daijō-daijin). Kampaku Tadamichi was appointed Minister of the Left, and Major Counselor Fujiwara no Ietada, who ranked next after Masazane, was appointed Minister of the Right. Major Counselor Minamoto no Arihito was appointed Minister of the Center. At this time, Ietada and Arihito concurrently held the positions of Left and Right General.

On the twenty-eighth day of the first month of the fourth year, the Emperor abdicated the throne to his son Prince Akihito and took the title of Retired Emperor. He was only twenty-one years old at the time. The era names were Tennin (2 years), Ten'ei (3 years), Eikyū (5 years), Gen'ei (2 years), and Hōan (4 years), for a total reign of sixteen years.

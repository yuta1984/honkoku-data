The 80th Emperor
Emperor Takakura: Third son of Go-Shirakawa, his personal name was Norihito. His mother was Kenshun'mon-in Taira no Shigeko, daughter of the posthumously appointed Minister of the Left Toki-nobu. Because he was the beloved child of Retired Emperor Go-Shirakawa, he was made Crown Prince to Emperor Rokujō, and ascended the throne in the third month of Nin'an 3 (1168) at the age of eight. Motofusa served as regent. Since all governmental affairs were decided by Retired Emperor Go-Shirakawa, those who served at the retired emperor's court wielded great authority. Kiyomori's wife Taira no Tokiko was the elder sister of Kenshun'mon-in, so the Taira family gained even more power. Kenshun'mon-in's elder brother, Major Counselor Taira no Tokitada, had close relationships with the imperial palace, the retired emperor's court, and the Taira family, and thus wielded political power. People of the time called him the "Taira Regent." In the eighth month, Kazanin Inner Minister Fujiwara no Tadamasa was appointed Chancellor.

The Eightieth [Emperor]
Emperor Takakura: The third son of Emperor Go-Shirakawa, his personal name was Norihito, his mother was Kenshunmon'in Taira no Shigeko, daughter of the posthumously appointed Minister of the Left Tokinobu. Being the beloved child of Retired Emperor Go-Shirakawa, he was made Crown Prince to Emperor Rokujō, and ascended the throne in the third month of Nin'an 3 (1168). He was eight years old at the time. Motofusa served as regent. All governmental affairs were under the judgment of Retired Emperor Go-Shirakawa, so those who attended at the retired emperor's court wielded authority. Kiyomori's wife Taira no Tokiko was the elder sister of Kenshunmon'in. Therefore, the Taira clan gained even more power. Kenshunmon'in's elder brother, Grand Counselor Taira no Tokitada, wielded authority due to his close relationships with the imperial court, the retired emperor's court, and the Taira family. People of the time called him the "Taira Regent."

About ten thousand morgen consist of barren reef rock. Although the farmland is fertile, rats are extremely numerous and prevent the sowing of grain seeds, so the local people sustain themselves solely through livestock farming and vegetable cultivation. Wild cattle meat is considered most delicious, and there are also many wild beasts and birds, but trees are scarce. There are hot springs in several places, and the climate is good and clean.

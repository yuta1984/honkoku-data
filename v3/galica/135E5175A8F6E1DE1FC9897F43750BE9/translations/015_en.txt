To some extent, they are heard to sever the seeds of Buddha and become messengers of hell. How difficult it is for women with such deep evil karma to be saved! Yet graciously, the Teaching Master Śākyamuni expounded a wonderful sutra (or chapter) for the sake of women, showing the path to Buddhahood for women. Thus, in the Devadatta chapter of the fifth volume, it is taught: 'First, they cannot become Brahmā kings; second, Indra;

【Left page】
third, demon kings; fourth, wheel-turning sage kings; fifth, Buddha-body. How can the female form quickly attain Buddhahood?' This is the clear text of the Buddha. Therefore, without negligence, do not forget this sutra but deeply embrace it in your heart, and with your mouth chant the sacred name of Amida. Then you shall reach the Land of Bliss and join the Buddha's throne without doubt.

Dania (Denmark) is located within Europe, to the east of Polonia (Poland), and is a very cold country. It is an extremely large nation, long from north to south, with its southern part near the Mediterranean Sea and its northern part near the polar regions. In summer, the nights are extremely short and the days extremely long. In winter, the nights are very long and the days very short. Marine fish are abundant, and the wild animals of the mountains and forests surpass those of other countries. Grains and precious goods are plentiful, and it is reported that this country excels above all others in astronomical and calendrical measuring instruments.

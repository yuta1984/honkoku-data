Takasago (Taiwan) is an island nation in the sea southeast of China. In the past, when the Dutch people lived there, it was called Taiwan, and after Koxinga took residence there, it was renamed Dongning. It is a warm country. The customs of the local people are quite base, and they make their living by constantly hunting deer. The farmers make their living by cultivating sugar cane and watermelons. Rice and wheat are harvested twice a year. It is located 22-23 degrees south of the North Pole.

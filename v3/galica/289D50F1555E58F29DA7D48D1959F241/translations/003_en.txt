【Inscription】Emile Javal Sale. November 9, 1933

154. Miscellaneous. Shiju Nikoku Jimbutsu Jisetsu. Images of personages from forty-two nations.
    Preface signed by Ryūsensō and dated to the horse year of Shōtoku (1714).
    Author: Nishikawa of Nagasaki. The postface states that the work was created based on drawings by painters of Nagasaki. Colophon dated Kyōhō 5 (1720). Published by Ebisuten in Edo.
                            1 volume containing 54 pages of black ink engravings.

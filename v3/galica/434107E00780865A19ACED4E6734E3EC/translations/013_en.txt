Why should such a sorrowful person be so dear to me?

It was written that she should not resent that person's heart, but that only the sorrow of her own wretched circumstances was sad. Therefore, reasonably, she did nothing but press the letter to her face and weep in her grief. However, though it would be shameful to go somewhere, she had said "This is how it must be" and was there when the Minister sent me as a messenger, saying, "The Middle Captain is now a person who should not come here. If you are feeling lonely, please go to the Middle Captain's wet nurse at Kasuga." After she replied "I understand," the Princess wept and

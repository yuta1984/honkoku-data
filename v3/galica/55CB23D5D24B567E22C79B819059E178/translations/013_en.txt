A terrible roaring sound could be heard. Thunder rumbled and lightning flashed incessantly, creating indescribable terror. In the light of the lightning, one could see eight heads, each with two horns, and between them pine and nutmeg trees grew thickly. Its sixteen eyes shone no differently than the sun and moon, and the scales beneath its throat were like the waves of a great ocean illuminated by the evening sun. Seeing the princess upon the platform, it tried to devour her, but because fires had been lit all around,

"The payment has not yet been received. I present this saying it should be given to reward unworthy retainers," said the bamboo cutter. As the old man wondered what matter these craftsmen were speaking of and tilted his head in puzzlement, the prince was beside himself and utterly dismayed.

Hearing this, Princess Kaguya said "Bring me that document they are presenting," and when she looked at it, the document stated: "The prince spent a thousand days hidden away in the same place with lowly craftsmen, having them create an ingenious jeweled branch, and promised to grant them official positions as well. Considering this matter recently, we understood that what you should desire above all else as an emissary would be something that Princess Kaguya would desperately want, so from this palace we were to receive..."

There are two reasons for this error when counting troops.
One is that when recording the numbers of military provisions, barracks, and such in government ledgers, they record figures that exceed reality. This is because all French people prefer to make their numbers appear larger in order to maintain good appearances.
The other derives from spreading temporary false information not based on government records, but to intimidate people when defending other territories.

The reason the Prussian government records military numbers as smaller than reality also has two causes. Since the nature of Prussian people emphasizes prudence, they create true deficiencies in their troops

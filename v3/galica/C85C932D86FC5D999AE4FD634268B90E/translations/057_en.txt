There were also 18 ironclad vessels, with engine output ranging from 800 to 950 horsepower. They were equipped with 12 to 32 cannons, and their crews numbered from 570 to 600 men. However, many among them had not yet undergone actual combat experience. Only 9 ironclad ships, whose designs conformed to various superior methods, were consistently equipped with 12 cannons and 310 soldiers. When put to practical use, these were the most powerful and capable of achieving great results.

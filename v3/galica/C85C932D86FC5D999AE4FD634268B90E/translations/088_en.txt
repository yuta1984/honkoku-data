Conversely, unless the breech is fully closed, the firing spring cannot be drawn back. ○ Therefore, whether in the act of opening or closing the breech, it has been designed so as to naturally prevent any risk of accidental danger. Consequently, whether a cartridge has been loaded or not, the mechanism prevents unexpected discharge just as if it had not been loaded, ensuring complete safety. For these reasons, this rifle may be said to be the most excellent and perfectly designed of its kind.

Baden forces: 6 infantry regiments (each consisting of 3 battalions), 3 cavalry regiments, and 3 field artillery detachments. These consist of 16,656 field troops, 3,955 reserve troops, and 9,640 garrison troops. The total is 30,291 soldiers and 64 cannons.

○ Regarding the Chassepot Rifle:
Point Five: This rifle is extremely light, and its construction is not sufficiently robust to be used effectively in bayonet charges. It is evident that it lacks effectiveness in close combat.

Beyond the defects and disadvantages noted above, the Chassepot rifle also causes further serious problems. Specifically, a crust composed of rubber residue and the remnants of combustion and fouling from gunpowder accumulates on the tip of the firing needle, causing significant obstruction.

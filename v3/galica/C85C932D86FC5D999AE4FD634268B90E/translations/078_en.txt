This is so that when the loading mechanism handle is fully withdrawn to the rear end, it will stop at the point of this angular projection when pulled toward oneself, unless it is first rotated one turn downward. This loading mechanism handle is connected to a steel cylinder that travels back and forth within the breech chamber, thereby forming part of the bolt-action closing mechanism. In order to secure the outward-bending knee joint (toggle joint) located at the rear end of the barrel, an inward-inclined knee joint is provided at its front end, thus achieving a sufficient seal.

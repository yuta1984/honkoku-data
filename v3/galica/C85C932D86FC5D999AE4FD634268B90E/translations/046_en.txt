The state of "Prussia" has formed mutual assistance pacts with the Kingdom of "Saxony," the "Thuringian" states, "Hesse," "Mecklenburg-Schwerin," "Strelitz," and "Oldenburg," among other states. Therefore, the military forces of these aforementioned states are united and closely integrated with the military forces of "Prussia."

The military personnel of the North German Confederation are as follows:
Infantry: Four infantry regiments, four elite regiments, one rifle regiment - all of the above are "Prussian" nationals. Fifteen elite regiments, seventy-seven infantry regiments, thirteen rifle regiments.
"Hessian" personnel: four regiments (each composed of two battalions), one hunter battalion

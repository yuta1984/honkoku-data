In addition, 8 ironclad screw steamships to be equipped with 68 cannons, and 23 wooden screw steamships to be equipped with 144 cannons, are currently under construction.

Had all these numerous warships fully served their purpose and had the newly constructed vessels been completed promptly, the naval power would indeed have been greatly augmented. However, all of them failed to be ready in time. The number of vessels currently available for naval service is exceedingly small, and it is quite easy to count them.

At the parliamentary assembly, when calculating in advance the expenses of the various states for the following year of 1870, the annual amount comes to 56 million yuan. Of this, Prussia bears 39 million yuan, and the remainder constitutes the expenses of all the other states. The total national expenses for 1869 amounted to 72,734,601 thalers, which is approximately 54,550,950 yuan.

〇 Needle Gun (Dreyse Rifle)

Section Two: The needle guns used in Prussia are all breech-loading with external sealing mechanisms. As such, they prevent the many malfunctions arising from the smooth operation of the sealing mechanism, thereby guarding against all manner of dangers in advance.

〇 Chassepot Rifle

Section Two: Because this rifle employs an internal sealing mechanism, it gives rise to many malfunctions.

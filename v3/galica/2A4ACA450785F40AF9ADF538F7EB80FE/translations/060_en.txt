Swallow (Tsubame) - Arrives on the spring Shanichi and 
　　　　departs on the autumn Shanichi. It holds mud in its mouth
　　　　and builds its nest
　　　　under the eaves. It departs
　　　　　　　　　　on Tsuchinoe-Mi days.
The back feathers are
black
The belly is white
The beak is red

Kashitori (a type of bird) - It is charcoal-colored
　　　The head is blue
　　　The wings have spots/patterns

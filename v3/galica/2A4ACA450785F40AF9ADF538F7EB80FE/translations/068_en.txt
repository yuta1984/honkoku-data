Owl (Fukurou)
Has a face
like a cat
Flies at night
Rests during the day
Its call sounds
like an old person's
voice

Horned Owl (Mimizuku) - Has the same nature as the owl
　　　Has ears and its call
　　　sounds like laughter

Wren (Misosazai)
　About the size of a sparrow, with back feathers
　that are speckled gray
　Builds its nest
　　　　on legs/branches

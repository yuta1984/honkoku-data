Quiver (utsubo)
[Left page]
[Figure of a quiver maker with his attendant]
The Sancai Tuhui states:
Quivers are made from leather,
and are used according to the size
and length of bows, crossbows, and arrows.

　　From the Man'yōshū:
The warrior
　　taking up his gear
　　　and departing—
　his wife must have wept,
　　grieving over
　　　　　　their parting

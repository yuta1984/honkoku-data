Red-hair vegetable (Dutch vegetable)
Used as a vegetable for food. The taste is delicious. The shape of its leaves is like this - large and long, with two branches. When broken, it produces white sap like milk. There are also varieties where a single leaf divides several times.

Hydrangea
Azisai flower
Ajisai
The hydrangea has stems that grow in clusters from one spot. There can be about ten stems. The height does not exceed two to three shaku.

Record of the Rise and Fall of the Nanban Temples

At that time, during the reign of the 107th Human Emperor, Emperor Ōgimachi, Oda Nobunaga raised forces from Owari Province, defeated the Saitō clan and seized Mino Province, expelled the Sasaki clan and took Ōmi Province, protected Shōgun Ashikaga Yoshiaki and had him return to Kyoto. In the south, he formed an alliance with the Tokugawa house, destroyed the Asakura of Hokuriku and the Asai of northern Ōmi, sought to swallow the Chūgoku region with his military might, and his fame thundered throughout the western provinces. At this time, a single Nanban ship landed at the port of Nagasaki in the territory of Ryūzōji Takashige, lord of Hizen Province in Kyushu. On this ship came one person of strange appearance. This person was over nine shaku tall, had a small head relative to his body, a red face, round eyes and a high nose, and when looking sideways would turn his shoulder, with a wide mouth.

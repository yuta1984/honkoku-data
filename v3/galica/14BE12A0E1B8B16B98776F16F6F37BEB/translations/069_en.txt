[The grains] began to move like ants, gradually growing, blooming flowers and bearing fruit. Also, when they grasped a chicken egg in their palm and opened their hand, the shell would crack and it would become a chick, and while watching, it would become a chicken and cry out with its voice. When there was a request to "make Mount Fuji appear in the garden from behind the bamboo blinds," they briefly drew the sliding doors shut and went outside, then suddenly opened the doors, whereupon Mount Fuji appeared in the garden. Those above and below were filled with wonder and gasped "Ah!" in amazement. Again they drew the sliding doors shut, and after a while when they opened them, the eight scenic views of Lake ÅŒmi appeared. At times they projected Sakai Bay, Suma, Akashi, and other places in their entirety. Those in the hall, both high and low ranking, wondered what manner of immortal sages these two men might be. Lord Hideyoshi pondered matters that would be difficult to match their magical arts

However, on the second day of the sixth month of Tenshō 10 [June 21, 1582], Lord Nobunaga and his son met their tragic death in Kyoto at the hands of Akechi Mitsuhide. Hashiba Hideyoshi promptly came up to the capital, defeated Mitsuhide, destroyed Shibata, and seized control of the realm. Thus the Nanban temple and new sect flourished for a total of eighteen years from Eiroku 11 through Tenshō 13 [1568-1585]. However, this year in Tenshō 13 [1585], Hideyoshi suddenly destroyed this sect.

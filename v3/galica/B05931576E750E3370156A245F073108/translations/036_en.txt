The whole of "France" is praying to the heavens for your victory, and all are raising fervent and impassioned prayers, their eyes fixed upon your every action. Therefore, do not dare to be negligent. Should we succeed, we shall increase the blessings of the nation's unfettered prosperity and civilization. ○ Strive on, soldiers — each of you fulfill your duty. Be ever vigilant, soldiers — the god of war has come and now stands over your heads.

On the 28th day of the 7th month of the year 1870, at "Metz"……

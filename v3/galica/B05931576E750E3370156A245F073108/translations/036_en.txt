"…you shall be able to see the traces [of our ancestors]. And it is certain that, through the virtue they have left behind, victory shall be obtained.

All of France prays to the heavens for victory on your behalf, and with fervent and impassioned words of supplication, turns its eyes to your endeavors. Therefore, do not dare to be idle. Should we achieve success, the happiness of our nation's freedom and prosperity shall be greatly increased. ○ Strive forth, soldiers! Each of you fulfill your duty. Be steadfast, soldiers! The god of war has come and now stands above your heads.

The twenty-eighth day of the seventh month of the year eighteen hundred and seventy, at 'Metz.'"

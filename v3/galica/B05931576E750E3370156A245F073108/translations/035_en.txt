"In commencing this war, it will certainly require a great expenditure of time and days. This is because every battleground is equipped with the obstacles of densely constructed fortresses. Nevertheless, compared to the hardships and sufferings endured by the troops in Africa, Italy, and Mexico, the ease of the present undertaking is incomparably greater. ○ You must strive with renewed diligence so that the soldiers of the French army once again demonstrate perfect discipline, faithfully follow their orders, strictly observe the laws, and display their loyal hearts dedicated to their country. ○ Now, as I cross beyond our borders, whatever road I may take, all of these lands were once conquered by our ancestors, and therefore, wherever our feet may tread…"

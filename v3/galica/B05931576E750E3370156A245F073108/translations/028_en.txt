...[the Emperor] issued a command to the people of France, saying:

　　Hear this, ye people of France. When a person lives in this world and, when facing a great moment, rises up with fierce resolve and takes the lead before all others, they shall prevail over their adversaries and, by fully embracing the blessings that fortune bestows, attain a state of peace and security. The moment to rise up and take action has now arrived for this nation of France. ○ As for that nation Prussia — during the hostilities of the year 1856 and in all the years since, we have devoted ourselves wholeheartedly to friendship and have engaged with them in the most cordial of terms; yet they have shown not the slightest sign of welcoming our goodwill.

Those who have obtained the not-yet-revised articles must be well acquainted with the following matters. Prussia would be content with taking possession of Belgium, and France should feel free to seek a portion of Belgium within the provinces of Prussia at any time it wished — so it was arranged. As for France, the government made great efforts to relax the momentum of its disadvantageous situation. Furthermore, it was spread about among the neutral nations that these articles had been communicated by Prussia. However, since it was perfectly clear that this was an outright falsehood, there was not a single person who believed it.

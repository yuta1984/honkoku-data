That you soldiers are all well capable of bearing your respective duties, I already know. For wherever our footsteps have reached across the wide world, there has never been an occasion where we did not raise a song of victory. In this campaign, I shall bring my child with me. Young and tender though he may be, he well understands the duties of a sovereign. Moreover, how could I sit idly by while the people of this nation cast aside their lives and march into the face of danger? I too must devote my loyalty to the nation, so it was declared. May God grant favor upon our cause. As it was said of old: over the people who keep to the way of Heaven, even the devil cannot prevail.

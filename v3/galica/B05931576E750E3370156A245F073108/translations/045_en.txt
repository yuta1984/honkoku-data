Nevertheless, it was determined that no mediation from other nations would be accepted to resolve this discord. The three nations of Great Britain, Russia, and Austria made great efforts on behalf of both parties to encourage a reconciliation, but all came to nothing. The Emperor of France was in no position to give his consent to a peaceful settlement, and Prussia, having already made its decision, would likewise be unable to reach a reconciliation at this stage.

The Emperor of France, having already issued a declaration of war, was nevertheless hesitant to depart from the city of Paris. Many found this delay suspicious. ○ When it came time to mobilize the troops, the Emperor now discovered that the military preparations were entirely inadequate and that supplies and equipment were sorely lacking — a revelation that shocked him greatly. This was entirely a consequence of years spent indulging in extravagance and luxury, of rampant bribery and corruption, and of placing deceit and fraud above all else, while neglecting to devote any effort to military readiness. ○ Meanwhile, Prussia had fully equipped its forces and had already advanced to the border, where it was deploying its encampments.

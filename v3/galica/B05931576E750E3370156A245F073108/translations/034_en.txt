It was not until July 28th that he finally arrived at Metz. On this day, as the Emperor himself took command of the military forces, he conveyed the following proclamation to the troops. ○ At this point, the Emperor must have observed the condition of his army and recognized for the first time its weakness. He must also have come to understand that the military power of the enemy nation he was to engage was formidable and fully prepared for war.

　"Now, having become the commander of you soldiers, I shall protect our land and people. You soldiers shall also engage in battle against a nation known as one of the great powers of Europe. So long as no other nations come to their aid, you need have no fear. ○ Now, in this war…"

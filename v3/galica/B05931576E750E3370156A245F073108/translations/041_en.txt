Sub-article 2 (continued): Prussia shall freely select and annex states from among the German territories. However, excluding Austria, the total population thereof shall not exceed eight million. Therefore, these annexed territories shall be detached from the authority of the other powers and shall become part of the Kingdom of Prussia.

Sub-article 3: France shall be free to divide and acquire the territories along the Rhine River as it sees fit.

Article 7: The defense against external threats through military preparedness, and the securing of safety upon the seas for the people, shall be undertaken jointly between France and Prussia. Should Italy desire to do so, it may be admitted into their alliance.

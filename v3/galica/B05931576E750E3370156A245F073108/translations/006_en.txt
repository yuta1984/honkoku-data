Since Prince Leopold belonged to a cadet branch of the House of Hohenzollern, he had no aspiration whatsoever to succeed to the Prussian throne, nor was he plotting against the authority belonging to the King of Prussia; therefore, there was no reason for this matter to concern Prussian affairs or policy. This prince was originally a Catholic, aged thirty-five this year, highly accomplished in learning, and quite wealthy. He also held the rank of Lieutenant General (an honorary rank) in the Prussian military.

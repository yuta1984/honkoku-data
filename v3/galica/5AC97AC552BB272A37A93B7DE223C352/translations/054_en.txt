The Crown Prince recorded this battle as follows——

　　On the fifth of August, at the encampment of the Third Army, a dispatch was received. It reported that "Marshal MacMahon," while assembling his troops on the hills to the west of "Woerth," had been greatly reinforced by the continuous arrival of large forces by rail, even as skirmishing went on without interruption——

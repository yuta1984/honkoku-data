This fierce battle unfolded during the advance from "Saarbrücken" to "Forbach." Furthermore, the First Army of "German" forces distinguished itself greatly in battle against a multitude of enemy troops. On that same day, a remarkably intense engagement took place between the Third Army of "German" forces (led by Crown Prince "Friedrich Wilhelm" of "Prussia") and the left wing of the "French" army commanded by the most valiant and brilliant "Maréchal MacMahon."

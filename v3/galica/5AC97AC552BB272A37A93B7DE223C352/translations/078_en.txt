——[continued from previous page] Marshal MacMahon has been defeated. General Frossard, stationed at the Saar, was compelled to withdraw. Nevertheless, his retreating army maintained its formation without the slightest disorder. ○ The entire army mutually supported one another and fought well.

The following day came news of the French national army's defeat. ○ The great city of Hagenau in Alsace was seized by the cavalry of Baden; the French soldiers were routed, some taken prisoner and some driven away, while the German troops occupied the city. ○ The same cavalry, pressing their advantage, trampled over the greater part of Alsace, capturing many prisoners, and finally laid siege to Phalsbourg, Bitche, and Lunéville, pushing westward — [continued on next page]

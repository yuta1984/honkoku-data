However, upon hearing the thunderous cannon fire that shook the very earth, the Prussian main camp dispatched several detachments to the rescue. ○ The detachment led by General von Walckenaer initially found itself in an unfavorable position, but two artillery batteries swiftly advanced to support their comrades.

It was precisely four o'clock at this moment. ○ I [the reporting officer] had no choice but to order a temporary retreat; the First and Second Battalions, being valiant and resolute, were capable of advancing to cover the withdrawal of the other troops and of effectively holding back the enemy, and so they were designated as the rearguard. ○ The objective of this retreat was to proceed from Niederbronn to Saverne.

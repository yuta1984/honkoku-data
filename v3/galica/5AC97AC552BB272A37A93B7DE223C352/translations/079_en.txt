"I have long been without news from MacMahon. Only yesterday did I receive a brief report. ○ General Regnault informed me, saying that MacMahon had fought against a great enemy force, was defeated, yet maintained his formation and promptly withdrew his troops. ○ The battle began at around one o'clock.

49 How did you become acquainted with him?

50 He is an old friend of my relatives, and on account of that he is also friendly to me.

51 Do you go there today?

52 Yes.

53 When do you come back?

54 I will be back with you the day after tomorrow.

55 For certain?

56 You may confide in me, how can I speak with two mouths?

57 You are right, but there are people who never keep their word.

58 It is so, but my word is firm as a rock.

59 Then I will take leave, good bye till the day after tomorrow.

60 I wish you good bye too, fare well.

　　Third Dialogue
　between two friends

1 Is Mr.--- at home?

2 Who is there?

3 I have come to see you.

4 I thank you, walk into the parlour.

5 How do you do.

6 I am well, at your service.

7 It is extraordinary that you are at home today.

8 I would have gone out but unexpectedly some pressing business has occurred, it is on account of that I have stayed at home.

9 I am sorry for it.

10 Have you gone out today to amuse yourself?

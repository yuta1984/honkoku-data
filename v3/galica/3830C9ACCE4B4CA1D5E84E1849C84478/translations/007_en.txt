11 Have you been to Nagasaki often?
12 No, I am coming here for the first time this year.
13 Are you traveling alone?
14 Yes, I have no one with me.
15 Do you have children?
16 Yes, I have two, one of whom is a daughter.
17 Why then do you not bring your children with you?
18 Because they are still too young.
19 How old are they?
20 The son is thirteen, the daughter is eleven years old.
21 Have you seen everything?
22 Yes, I have seen all the places and temples, especially the Dutch and Chinese quarters. I am quite pleased, since now I am in a position to give a detailed account of my experiences.
23 Nagasaki has nothing particularly curious except that this year there are no Dutch ships nor Chinese junks.
24 In what season do Dutch ships depart from Batavia for Japan?
25 They leave there in the month of June and arrive here at the end of July or the beginning of August.
26 In what month do they depart from here?
27 In October.
28 What articles do the ships carry to Batavia?
29 They load principally copper and camphor, and several other articles in return.
30 Where do the copper and camphor come from?
31 The copper comes from Akita Province, and the camphor from Satsuma Province.

Because of this lifestyle, the education of the first and second sons who accompanied her (the eldest son died in 1807) was not adequately provided. Both sons studied German language and classics, but due to many obstacles, they were unable to make remarkable progress. Both sons joined the "Carbonari" party for political reform in "Italy" and entered into the Italian nationalist alliance. After the political reforms of July 1830 when "Louis Philippe" ascended to the throne, the second son believed he should return to "France" for the sake of himself and his relatives.

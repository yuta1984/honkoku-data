The third son "Louis Napoleon" crossed over both "Italy" and "France" and fled to "England," where he stayed temporarily before finally returning to "Arenenberg" castle where his mother was residing. Not long after "Louis Napoleon" arrived at this castle, the former heir of Napoleon I, the "Duke" of "Reichstadt," passed away. At this point, "Louis Napoleon" first became the heir for his family and became one who aspired to ascend to the imperial throne of "France." From this time forth, "Louis Napoleon" sought to overthrow the court of "Louis Philippe"

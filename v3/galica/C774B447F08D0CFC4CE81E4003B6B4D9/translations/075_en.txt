Louis Napoleon always had a fondness for theatrical performances, so he planned to employ the tactics of surprise attack that he had once devised, similar to when the first Napoleon returned from Elba. The party that participated in this affair consisted of two men: Colonel Baudrey of the Fourth Artillery Regiment currently stationed in Strasbourg, and Doctor Fialin, who later became famous under the name Duke de Persigny.

The New York publications "Tribune," "Herald," "World," "Times," and detailed war diaries were taken from the "Army and Navy Journal"; descriptions of battlefields and encampments were drawn from the "Courrier des États-Unis" and the "Cincinnati Commercial." For discussions of politics, I relied on the political and religious records of 1870, or "Bloc's" "Manuel de la Géographie," and newspapers from Leipzig and Frankfurt, etc. The biographical sections are based on "Bé Péréo's" "Dictionary des Contemporains" and "Ansorgieto."

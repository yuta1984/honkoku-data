○Regarding the official letter to Lord Matsudaira Oki-no-kami　　This is due to his current illness
　　The punishment for Asano Takumi's retainers who have been entrusted to your care has been ordered. Inspector Sugita Gozaemon and Guards Officer Komakiné Chōzaburō will be dispatched shortly. You need not appear at the scene yourself. The above.
　　　　　　Second month, 4th day　　　Council of Elders
　　　　　　Lord Matsudaira Oki-no-kami

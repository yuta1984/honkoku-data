Also, as I wish to bid farewell to everyone, I am writing numerous letters and cannot write each one in detail. My apologies. I humbly state this.

Twelfth month　　　　　　Yokogawa Kanpei Munetoshi (seal)

To Miharaya Chōzaemon-sama
To the same, Shichizaemon-sama
　　　　　　Respectfully submitted

This letter was brought to the Miharaya residence by Morizō, a retainer of Sugino Jūheiji.

Although it was regrettable toward Takumi that he pointlessly suffered disgrace and was made to withdraw, he should have served without negligence, yet he was left in that position, which was an act of unpreparedness. Consequently, even when Takumi's retainers pressed forward this time, it appeared he acted in a lingering, unseemly manner, and as a son cannot escape his father's disgrace, he is to be placed under the custody of Suwa Aki-no-kami.

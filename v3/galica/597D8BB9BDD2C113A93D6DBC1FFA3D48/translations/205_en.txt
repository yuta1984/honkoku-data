○1. Regarding the officials for the handover of the arrow storehouse, Kuranosuke had appointed two construction magistrates (sakuji-bugyō), but no officials came from the castle guard unit (gozaiban-kata). When [we] inquired about the keys to the inspectors (metsuke), it was communicated to the castle guard unit, and officials came out to the outer Ōte Gate and received the keys.

○One: Everyone returned to their lodgings, and Kuranosuke was summoned by the inspectors. At the castle today, regarding the petition and also the thorough cleaning of the castle grounds and the flawless manner in which all affairs were conducted, they expressed their admiration. "These matters will be reported by express messenger tonight, so bear this in mind," they were told. Regarding the future residences of the household members after their dispersion, certificates would be provided by the inspectors according to their wishes. For those going to Edo, even travel permits for women would be provided. For those wishing to remain in this locality, they should also submit petitions, it was announced.

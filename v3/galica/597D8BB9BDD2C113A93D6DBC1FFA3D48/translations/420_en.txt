Yoshihide《Kira Kōzuke-no-suke, Fourth Rank Shōshō》　　Yoshichika《Kira Sahyōe》

1. Uesugi Danjō-no-daihitsu Tsunanori is actually the legitimate son of Kira Yoshihide. His wife is the daughter of Kii Dainagon Kōkō-kō《She is the elder sister of Chūnagon Tsunanori-kō》

1. Uesugi Harima-no-kami adopted Danjō-dono as his son. Danjō-dono has two sons: the eldest is Uesugi Minbu-no-taifu, and the second son is Kira Sahyōe《18 years old, actually the grandson of Kōzuke-no-suke-dono》

○1. The wife of Kira Kōzuke-no-suke-dono is the daughter of Uesugi Harima-no-kami-dono《Tsunakatsu》

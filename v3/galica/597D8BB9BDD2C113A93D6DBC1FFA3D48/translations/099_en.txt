〇1. On the 5th day of the 4th month, at Akō Castle, Ōishi Kuranosuke distributed the severance money (warifu-kin), which everyone received
　　As follows:
　　　1. Mounted guard group (umamawari-gumi): 18 ryō of gold per 100 koku of stipend
　　　　　However, the amount decreases according to the actual stipend amount
　　　1. Middle pages group (chū-koshō-gumi): 14 ryō each
　　　1. Servants and menials: 11 ryō each
　　　1. Foot soldier group (hokō-gumi): 10 ryō each
　　　1. Regular officials of the same rank: 7 ryō each
　　　1. Minor officials: 5 ryō each

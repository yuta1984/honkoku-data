One district magistrate for both Katō and Kasai districts, rural magistrates - the above-mentioned personnel shall examine the records of the duties they have been performing, and prepare to respond without delay when questioned by officials, receiving instructions accordingly. Most importantly, upon receiving orders from above, they shall consult among their colleagues as much as possible and remain with reduced numbers.

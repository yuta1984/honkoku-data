○1. Since there were 1,214 koku and 4 to of remaining rice in the second bailey (ninomaru), on the 19th day of the 4th month, it was handed over with ledger documentation to the retainers of Kinoshita Higo-no-Kami-dono in the presence of the storehouse magistrate. Regarding the rice not being fresh, instructions came from Edo from Takumi-sama, and after giving it to foot soldiers and below as relocation allowances for their wives and children, word came from Edo to also leave rice attached to the castle, so from that point it was sealed and stored.

Record of remaining rice:
Rice: 1,214 koku and 4 to
Number of rice bags: 3,036 bags　　Note: 4-to bags
The above number of bags was received according to the ledger as had been examined and stored during the previous inspection, and there are no discrepancies. The above.

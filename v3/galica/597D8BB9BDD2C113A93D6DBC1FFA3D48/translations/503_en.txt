　Copy of a letter that Yokogawa Kanpei carried in his pocket
I humbly write to you. Since then, correspondence has been cut off and I have not heard of your circumstances, thinking of you fondly morning and evening. Though it is extremely cold for this time of year, I hope that you and your family are in the best of health. I would like to hear from you. As for myself, I came to this place at the end of the seventh month and until now have been without incident

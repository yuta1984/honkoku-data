Drum Flea Shi Qian

After treating Yang Xiong and Shi Xiu to duck hotpot and having them eat, he pawned himself as payment and suffered in the Zhu family prison. When he runs along the beams, the maidservant of Xu Ning's house mishears and thinks it's a rat; when he heads toward the tower, the gatekeeper of Beijing castle mistakes him for a dog. Stealing helmets and setting fires are acts of running for righteousness, not careless intentions like those of a chicken.

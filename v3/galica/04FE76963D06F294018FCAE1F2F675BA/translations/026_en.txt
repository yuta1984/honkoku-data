In the third year of Kakitsu (1443), Wakasa-no-kami Minamoto no Nobuhiro crossed the sea and entered into Ezo territory, finally taking the southern border and establishing the northern lands. Since then, his descendants have occupied and defended that territory generation after generation. {{Interlinear note: This matter is unclear and is recorded in the miscellaneous accounts section}} A person named Kakizaki Yoshihiro first entered the service of Toyotomi.

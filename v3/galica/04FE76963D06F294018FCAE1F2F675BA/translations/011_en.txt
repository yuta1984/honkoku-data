From Sotogahama in Tsugaru District of Mutsu Province, approximately seven to eight ri to the north, there are islands called the Thousand Islands of Ezo. "Thousand Islands" probably means that there are many (islands). The origin of the name "Ezo" is ancient and frequently appears in national histories.

【Original Text】
From Sotogahama in Tsugaru District of Mutsu Province, seven to eight ri by sea to the north, there are islands. Also, because there are many islands belonging to them, people of the world call these the Thousand Islands of Ezo.

【Revised Text】

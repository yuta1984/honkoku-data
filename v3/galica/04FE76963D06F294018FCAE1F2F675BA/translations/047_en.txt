The above [villages], lying to the southeast of Matsumae, from Setara to Matsumae by land route is over 30 ri in total: 77 villages, over 6,800 households, over 26,000 people.

Sekinai: There is one merchant house that purchases and collects various local products. By declaring payment of "unjo" (trade levy) to the Lord of Matsumae, Shimano-no-kami, and remitting a portion of their profits [they hold the right to operate the trading post].

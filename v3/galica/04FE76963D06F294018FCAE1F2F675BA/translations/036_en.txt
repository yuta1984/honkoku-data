Five to six ri. The back extends due north and ends at the border of Sōya, while the tail flows northeast and its terminus is called Shiretoko. The total circumference is over seven hundred ri. Matsumae is sheltered by Shirakami Cape, curves northward and faces south, situated between mountains and sea. When observing the North Star from Matsumae, it appears at an elevation of approximately forty-one and a half degrees above the horizon; at Sōya it is over forty-five degrees.

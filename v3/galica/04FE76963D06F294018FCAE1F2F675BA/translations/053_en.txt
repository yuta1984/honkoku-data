(Note continued: To the left and right of the river stretch vast open plains, lush with vegetation, their breadth impossible to measure. Furthermore, traveling upstream and reaching the Shikotsu River, one crosses only a short overland stretch to transfer to the Yuubetsu River, and heading southward, in less than forty ri one arrives at the shore of the eastern sea. Altogether, it is a place of great convenience in all four directions, and after development, it should become a fitting seat of provincial government.)

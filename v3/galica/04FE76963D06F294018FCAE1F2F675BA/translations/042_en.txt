Ōshima: Located 5 ri offshore from Eruchō Village. Circumference over 2 ri. No residential houses. Constantly emits smoke.

Okushiri Island: Located approximately over 10 ri offshore to the northwest of Kumaishi Village. Circumference less than 10 ri. No residential houses.

The above are located to the northwest of Matsumae. From Kumaishi Village to 
Katsura-mae, the overland route is over 26 ri.

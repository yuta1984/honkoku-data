【16a】
Yesterday and today
the clouds dance and swirl,
hiding away—
the flower groves
must seem sorrowful【other texts read "seemed sorrowful"】

【16b】
Wild geese cry
and chrysanthemums bloom—
though autumn is here,
in spring【other texts read "of spring"】
by the seashore
at Sumiyoshi【other texts read "Sumiyoshi"】
beach

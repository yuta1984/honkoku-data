【Right Page, Upper Section】
○The First Patriarch Daruma met with Emperor Wu of Liang, crossed the river, and entered Shaolin Temple in Wei. He is known to the world as "Daruma of the Reed Leaf" or "Daruma of One Reed."

○Fudo Myo-o holds a sharp sword in his right hand and a binding rope in his left hand, which represents his form of admonishing the evil in sentient beings. The flames behind him maintain an unmovable form, and also express and demonstrate the appearance of anger in ordinary people.

【Left Page, Upper Section】
○Bodhisattva Nagarjuna was born in South India, eight hundred years after Shakyamuni. He is the first patriarch of the Shingon sect. He propagated the Mahavairocana Sutra, Vajrasekhara Sutra, and Susiddhi Sutra.

○Master Shandao appeared from a waterfall in Chang'an, China. For over thirty years he never slept at all, and passed away on March 14th in the second year of Yonglong of Tang.

○Master Tiantai was the national teacher of both the Chen and Sui dynasties, and the founding patriarch of the Tiantai sect in China. He entered nirvana on November 24th at the age of sixty. He is also called Master Zhizhe.

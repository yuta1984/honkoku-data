【Right page, upper section】
This is what is called kaiba (sea horse). It is said that if a woman in labor holds it in her hand, she will have a safe delivery.

○Buri (yellowtail) benefits the liver and supplements the blood. Those with excess conditions in the spleen and stomach should not eat it.

○Eso (lizardfish) supplements deficiency fatigue. Taking its oil and applying it to burns is effective.

○Haraka fish is also written as "belly red." There was an occasion when this haraka fish was presented to the emperor.

【Left page, upper section】
○Yagaraio (cornetfish) resembles hamo but is red in color. It treats throat obstruction caused by diaphragmatic symptoms.

○Sagoshi fish causes no harm in various diseases. It is a small horse mackerel (sawara).

○Nishin (herring) - its medicinal properties and toxicity are not clear. It cures diseases in cats.

○Gomame is a small sardine fish. Its medicinal properties and toxicity are not yet clear.

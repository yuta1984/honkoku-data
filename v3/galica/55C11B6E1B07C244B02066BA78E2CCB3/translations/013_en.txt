【Running title】Tōsho Zōho Kinmō Zui 12, 4

【Right page, upper section】
○The rhinoceros is like a
hairy pig, with
three plates on its hooves.
Its head is like a horse's
with three horns on its nose,
forehead, and
head.

○The bear has black fur,
and its shape resembles a pig. It has
white fat on its chest. It is commonly
called "moon ring." Those that
live in caves are called cave bears,
and those that live in trees are called
tree bears. Yūhan refers to a bear's
palm, and yūtan refers to a bear's
gallbladder.

【Right page, lower section - Illustrations】
Bear (reading: kuma)
Rhinoceros

【Left page, upper section】
○The wolf resembles a dog but is large.
Its head is sharp with white cheeks,
front legs are high and rear legs low.
Its mouth is pointed and large.
It is strong and catches
and eats various beasts.
It often looks back
behind itself.

○The jackal is a type
of wolf. Its color is yellow
with white cheeks and a long tail.
It is slightly
smaller than a wolf, but strong
and eats various beasts.
It is an evil beast.

【Left page, lower section - Illustrations】
Jackal (reading: yama-inu)
Wolf (reading: ōkami)

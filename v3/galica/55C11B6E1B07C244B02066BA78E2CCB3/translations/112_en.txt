【Right Page, Upper Section】
○Lychnis (senshūra) has flowers like carnations, vermillion in color and beautiful. The flowers bloom in June. Fushi-guro is also of this type.

○Ganpi (senshunra) has flower colors lighter than senshūra with a yellowish tinge.

○Job's tears (yokuи) has seeds in white and black varieties. Called yokuishi, it treats the five obstructions.

【Left Page, Upper Section】
○Lily (yuri) has many varieties. These flowers bloom from the end of March. There are red and pale red varieties.

○Tiger lily (oniyuri) blooms in June and July. It is large and yellow-red in color. It grows five to six feet tall and produces many flowers. Black seeds form between the leaves. Also called bansantan.

○Coral lily (himeyuri) blooms in early April. It is small with red and white varieties. The red is extremely vermillion and beautiful. Okudan is the same. There are many other varieties besides these.

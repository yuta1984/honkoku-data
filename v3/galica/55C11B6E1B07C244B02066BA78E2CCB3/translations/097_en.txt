【Right Page, Upper Section】
○Weigela (yamauzuki) blooms in April. It resembles deutzia but both flowers and leaves are larger. The flowers are white when they first open, then turn red later.

○Deutzia has fine leaves and small flowers. The wood is good for dyeing yellow. The fruit forms pods. Same as kūsho (hollow/sparse).

○Bramble (ibara) is abundant in mountains and fields, has many thorns and grows in clusters. White flowers bloom in May. Same as kyokushi and kyokushin (thorns/spines).

【Right Page, Lower Section - Illustrations】

【Left Page, Upper Section】
○Catalpa (azusa) is called kawarahisagi. The fruit is long and slender like cowpea and forms clusters. In winter the leaves fall and horn-like pods remain.

○Soapberry tree (tsubunoki) blooms with white flowers in May-June. The fruit is initially blue and turns yellow when ripe.

【Right Page, Upper Section】
○Dandelion (tanpopo) has large white flowers and small yellow ones. The flowers bloom in February and March. The leaves are harvested for food.

○Violet (sumire) is also called arrow-head grass (sentōsō). It is also called sumo wrestler's grass. The flowers are purple, white, or light purple. The leaves are round and small.

○Japanese knotweed (itadori) promotes menstruation and breaks up stagnant blood. It stops thirst, promotes urination, and treats abdominal distension and fullness.

【Left Page, Upper Section】
○Day lily (wasuregusa) has yellow-red flowers like tiger lilies and blooms in early summer. Young leaves are harvested in spring for food. It treats fluid retention and painful swelling of the breasts. It aids digestion, and those who enjoy eating it will have joy and no worries. Double-flowered varieties are poisonous and must not be eaten by mistake.

○Wood sorrel (katabami) is also called sour grass (sansō). Commonly known as "lazy person's soup herb."

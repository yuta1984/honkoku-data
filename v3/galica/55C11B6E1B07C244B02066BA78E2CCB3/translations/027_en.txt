【Right page, upper section】
○The eagle is a large type of hawk. The extremely large ones reach seven to eight shaku (approximately 2-2.4 meters). Their color is yellow with black spots on the belly. The beak is yellow. They live in deep mountains and soar through the sky, skillfully grasping and eating beasts.

【Right page, lower section - Illustration】
Eagle (shiu, washi)

【Left page, upper section】
○The mountain hawk-eagle (kumataka) is a large type of hawk. Its wings are strong, and it flies high in the sky, catching and eating not only various birds but also beasts. Its body length is three to four shaku (approximately 90-120 centimeters). In China, what they call "large hawk" refers to eagles and mountain hawk-eagles. In Japan, what we call "large hawk" refers to falcons and such.

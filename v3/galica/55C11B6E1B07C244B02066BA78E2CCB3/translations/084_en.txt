【Right Page Upper Section】
(Continuation of thistle) opens the stomach, aids digestion, stops vomiting blood and nosebleeds, and reduces fever.

○Goosefoot (akaza) kills insects and treats insect bite wounds. People with spleen-stomach deficiency-cold should not use it.

○Purslane (suberihiyu) treats gonorrhea, disperses blood, reduces swelling, and benefits the intestines. Pregnant women should not eat it.

○Ginger (hajikami) opens the stomach, breaks up blood, and dispels wind-cold. It neutralizes mushroom poison and communicates with the divine.

○Pokeweed (yamagobō) opens the five organs and disperses water qi. Colored varieties harm people and should not be eaten.

【Left Page Upper Section】
○Konjac reduces thirst, moves blood downward, reduces swelling, treats abscesses, and treats fatigue. It is contraindicated for children who have not had smallpox.

○Chickweed (hakobe) is effective for chronic malignant sores and hemorrhoids that won't heal, breaks up blood, and promotes lactation. Postpartum women should not eat it.

○Dandelion (tanpopo) should be juiced and consumed for breast abscesses and edema. It eliminates food poisoning and disperses stagnant qi.

○Bracken (warabi) removes heat, promotes urination, and supplements deficiencies of the five organs.

○Dog spine fern (kuseki) is royal fern. Commonly called "dog bracken." For hernia...

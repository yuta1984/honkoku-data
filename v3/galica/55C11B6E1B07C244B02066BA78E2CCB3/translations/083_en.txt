【Right Page Upper Section】
○Bottle gourd (yūgao) treats mouth sores and pain, promotes urination, removes heart heat, and moistens the heart and lungs.

○Melons (uri) all promote urination, stop thirst, remove heat, and loosen the large intestine. Snake gourd (asauri).

○Winter melon (kamouri) promotes urination, stops thirst, increases qi, removes chest congestion, and dispels heat.

○Mushrooms (take) increase qi, treat wind conditions, and break up blood stasis. Those growing on the ground are called "kin," and those growing on trees are called "tan."

○Cucumber (kyūri) cools heat, resolves thirst, and promotes urination. It is contraindicated for small children.

【Left Page Upper Section】
○Pickling melon (aoUri) promotes urination, supplements the middle, and reduces swelling.

○Loofah (hechima) - its dried skin is good for wiping tatami mats and removing dirt from heels. It removes heat and benefits the intestines.

○Wasabi treats cold stomach pain, promotes appetite, benefits the chest, and opens phlegm.

○Eggplant (nasubi) disperses blood, stops pain, reduces swelling, loosens the intestines, and expels phlegm. Silver eggplant (ginka) is white eggplant.

○Wild aster (yomenāhagi) treats toxic swellings and is good for forgetful urination, benefiting people.

○Thistle (azami) breaks up static blood and [benefits] the stomach...

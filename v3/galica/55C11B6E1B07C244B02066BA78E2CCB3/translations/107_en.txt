○Nodeless bamboo (musetsuchiku) is bamboo used for the rau (middle section) of tobacco pipes (kiseru).

○Fuku refers to bamboo seeds called sasameguri. Also known as chikubei (bamboo rice). Called "jinenkoto" in common parlance, it is said to be a bamboo disease. Inside the seeds are rice-like substances.

○Taku is bamboo bark. Also called chikuhi (bamboo skin) or junpi (bamboo shoot skin).

【Left Page, Upper Section】

○Tō refers to bamboo tubes. Also written as yō. Chikusetsu refers to bamboo nodes.

○Betsu is the green bark of bamboo. Commonly called "kadzura" (vine). Same as hetsu and kin.

○Kan is the core of wood. Commonly called "miki" (trunk).

○Kon is the root of wood. Also written as tei. Also called hon.

○Shi refers to tree branches.

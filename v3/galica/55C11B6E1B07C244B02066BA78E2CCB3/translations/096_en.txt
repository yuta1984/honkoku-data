【Right Page, Upper Section】
○Snowball bush (temari) has round leaves and white flowers that resemble hand balls. It blooms in April. It is also called gyokushūka (jade embroidered flower) or shūkyūka (embroidered ball flower). The viburnum tree also has flowers similar to temari. There are two varieties: large temari and small temari.

○Hydrangea blooms in May and resembles temari. The color is lapis lazuli blue, and there are also light red and white varieties. The leaves are similar to temari but the leaf tips are pointed. The tree height is 3-4 shaku (about 0.9-1.2 meters).

【Left Page, Upper Section】
○Creeping fig (masaki no kazura) is also called mokumanjū (wooden steamed bun) or kimanjū (demon steamed bun). At the end of autumn, it bears blue fruits that are red inside.

○Gardenia has white flowers that bloom in May. The fruit is yellow and used for dyeing. It reduces heat from the upper burner and treats phlegm. The flower is called senpuku.

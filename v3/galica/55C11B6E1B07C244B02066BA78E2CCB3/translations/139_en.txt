【Right Page, Upper Section】
○The Sixth Patriarch Master was the sixth patriarch from Daruma in China, his personal name being Huineng. From this point, Zen Buddhism divided into five schools. Dajian Chanshi is his posthumous title.

○Master Dengyo is also called Saicho. He is the founder of Japanese Tendai Buddhism. He entered Tang China in the 21st year of Enryaku and entered nirvana on June 4th at the age of fifty-six.

○En no Gyoja is also called En no Ozunu. He was a person from Yamato Province who entered Mount Katsuragi and practiced the dharma of Kujaku Myo-o (Peacock Wisdom King), and later put his mother in a bowl and entered Tang China.

【Left Page, Upper Section】
○Hanshan was a person of early Tang. He hid in Mount Tiantai and was constantly a dharma friend with Shide. Later, no one knew where he went, and it is said he was an incarnation of Manjusri.

○Shide is so called because Chan Master Fenggan found and picked him up by the roadside. He constantly associated with Hanshan, and no one knows of his end.

○The Giant Spirit is an immortal who obtained great power and supernatural abilities. He has the power to split mountains and constantly loves the white tiger.

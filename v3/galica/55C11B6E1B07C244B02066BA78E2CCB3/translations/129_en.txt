【Right Page, Upper Section】
○Fennel (uikyō) removes hernia qi, stops lower back and abdominal pain, and warms the stomach. Kuaikō is the same.

○Sigesbeckia (menamomi) has yellow-white flowers. For all poisonous insect bites, rub this leaf and apply the juice.

○Black nightshade (konasubi) is also called ryūki. The leaves resemble eggplant but are small. At the end of May, it opens small white flowers and produces small blue fruits.

【Left Page, Upper Section】
○Artemisia (kao) is called seikō or hōkō. Same as reishō and hagi. It dispels evil qi.

○Motherwort (jūi) is called yakumosō. It grows in wetlands.

○Virgate wormwood (inchin) has white leaf undersides and blooms with fine yellow flowers in September.

○Schisandra (genkyū) - its fruit is called gomishi. When branches are cut and soaked in water, they produce stickiness. Used on hair as a substitute for oil.

○Kochia (jifu) - the young leaves are eaten. Same as rakushū and tokushū. This plant is used to make brooms.

【Left Page, Upper Section】
○Peony (botan) is also called fukamigusa or hatsukagusa. It is regarded as the king of flowers. Purple flowers are common. There are also red and white varieties, with red considered the finest grade. It is considered the most magnificent of flowers, praised by ancient people as well. Also called kaō (flower king) or mokushakuyaku (tree peony). Botan bark is used medicinally.

【Right page, upper section】
○The crow (karasu) has a large beak and likes to be greedy. When made into charcoal powder, it treats wasting disease, cough, and consumption.

○The raven (karasu) has a thin beak and is smaller than the crow. After birth, the mother feeds it for sixty days; after leaving the nest, it feeds its mother for sixty days. Therefore it is called the "filial raven."

○The kite (tobi) resembles a hawk. It is the same as the kite. When made into charcoal powder, it treats headaches.

【Left page, upper section】
○The nightjar (yotaka) is of the owl family, comes out at night and hides during the day. Its form resembles a hawk but is smaller. It is an inauspicious bird.

○The horned owl (mimizuku) has the form of an owl but is smaller. Its head and eyes are like a cat's, with tufted "horns" on both ears. It lies low during the day and comes out at night. Its voice is like an old person calling for something.

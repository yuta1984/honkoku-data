【Right Page, Upper Section】
○Hinoki cypress grows in deep mountains and becomes large trees. For white wood utensils and bentwood items, this wood is used and considered the finest quality. It is also made into oars.

○Chinese juniper (ibuki) has leaves resembling kaya (Japanese torreya) and fruit resembling pine. The leaves are pointed and hard. However, the leaves are similar to hinoki with a black color and rough bark.檼栢 is the same.

○Muro is tamarisk (teiryū). It is also called "rain master." The bark is red.

【Left Page, Upper Section】
○Cedar (sugi) grows in deep mountains and becomes large trees. The tree stands straight with dense branches and leaves. When decocted, it is used to wash poisonous sores, and when soaked in water, it treats beriberi and swelling.

○Buddhist pine (inumaki) has leaves resembling maki. The shape of the fruit is like hands pressed together in prayer. It is also called arhat pine.

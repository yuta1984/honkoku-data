【Right page, upper section】
○The starling (mukutori) is called "small muku." Its size is smaller than a bulbul, with a white head and black and white feathers on its back. In mid-autumn, many migrate in flocks. Its taste is delicious.

○The white-cheeked starling (mukudori) is called "large muku." It is larger than the small muku, and its feather colors are also different. It comes during summer and autumn but does not form flocks.

○The goldcrest (kikuitadaki) is an extremely small bird. Its entire body is light blue, with yellow feathers on the crown. When the weather is fine and warm, if it spreads the feathers on its crown, red feathers appear from within. It is a bird that comes in winter.

【Left page, upper section】
○The Java sparrow (bunchou) is about the size of a sparrow. Its feather color is black, with round white feathers on its cheeks. Its belly is white.

○The great tit (shijuukara) is smaller than a sparrow, with a black head and round white cheeks. Its back is light blue, and its belly is white with black feathers. It comes in autumn and winter.

Armenian People
Armenia is located to the west of Western India and is a country with four seasons. However, it is a cold country. There are many superior countries in this vicinity. In ancient times it belonged to Western India.

【24】
Bandanese People
Banda is an island country near Sumatra. It is a hot country whose customs resemble those of the Dutch but are also distinct. It is said they favor bravery and fierceness.

Persian People

Persia is a great nation located on the western border of India, within Asia. It has many animal species and local products, has four seasons, and is said to be a prosperous country. The characters "百児" can also be written as "百爾."

Turkish People

Turkey is a country located to the northwest of India, and has four seasons. Its people are brave and strong, and it is a nation that favors military affairs. Many neighboring countries are often annexed by it.

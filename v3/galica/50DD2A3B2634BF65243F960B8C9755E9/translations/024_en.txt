Takasago (Taiwan) People
Takasago is an island country in the sea to the southeast of Tang China (mainland China). When the Dutch lived there in the past, it was called Taiwan,
and after Koxinga took residence there, it was renamed Dongning. It is a warm country. The customs of the local people are very base, and they constantly make hunting deer and other animals
their livelihood. The farmers make cultivating sugar cane and watermelons their livelihood. Rice and wheat are harvested twice a year.
The degrees from the North Pole are twenty-two or twenty-three degrees.

【34】
Orankay People
Orankay is a cold country to the northeast of Korea. Using the character "艮" for "良" may be an error. This country is said to be very close to Korea.
Some say it is a dependency of the Jurchen state. The degrees from the North Pole are approximately forty-two degrees.

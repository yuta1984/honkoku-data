Muscovite (Russian) People

Muscovia is a great nation located to the east of the Netherlands within Europe, and is an extremely cold country. It is a land where many unusual types of beasts and livestock are found. Firearms originate from this country, so it is said to possess many of them. All the various countries in this region are located between fifty or sixty degrees north latitude.

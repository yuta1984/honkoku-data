【Left Page】
Sakura Tenmangu Shrine

　　　Climbing the Bell Tower of Reigakuin on a Spring Day
　　　　　　　　Matsunaga Kokka

From "Bakuro Shishu" (Wheat Wave Poetry Collection):
　Spring fills the hundred thousand households south of the castle, climbing the tower
　Straining my eyes to take in all the mist and clouds. Behold how the lotus-white
　Snow of a thousand ri mingles with the bell's sound to become falling blossoms.

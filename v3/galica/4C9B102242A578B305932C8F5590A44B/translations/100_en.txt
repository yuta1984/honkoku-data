Jizō Hall　Sub-temple Shōrinin 《Built in the first year of Keichō》

Kōkokuzan Daikōin 《Located on the west side of the same town, Sōtō Zen sect, branch temple of Seizenji in Oshi, Musashi Province》 At the beginning of Keichō, when Junior Third Rank Middle Captain Lord Tadayoshi was residing at Kiyosu Castle, he built this temple on the site of an abandoned temple called Unmonji, used the old name Unmon as the temple name, and invited Priest Meirei to become the founding abbot. The priest was from Musashi Province and was the head priest of Seizenji Temple

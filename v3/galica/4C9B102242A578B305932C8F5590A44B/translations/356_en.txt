【Left page】
The ground is pure and the temple is also ancient
Strange rocks embrace the mountain
The stone arhats on the rock summit
Sitting and standing in various irregular positions
Holding flowers, harmonizing with the singing birds
Come to expound the dhāraṇī

　Seiichi

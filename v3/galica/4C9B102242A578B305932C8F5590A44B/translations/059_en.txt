Head Priest of the Yoshimi clan: Originally from Kyoto, he was called Okazaki Sakon Sugawara Naokatsu, but by imperial decree became a shrine official of Tōshō-gū, changed his name to Yoshimi Kunai-tayū Yukikatsu, was granted the court rank of junior fifth rank lower grade, and his descendants have served as head priests for generations.

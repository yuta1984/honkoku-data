Entsu-ji Temple
Hayasume Akiba Shrine

The founding abbot of this temple, Zen Master Gihon, was a grand-disciple of Zen Master Kangan Giin (elder brother of Dōgen Zen Master's teacher and second imperial prince of Emperor Go-Toba), who founded Fusai-ji Temple in Hamamatsu, Enshu Province. This temple is said to be the beginning of Zen temples in Owari Province.

General Rules
1. For military caps: Staff Corps, Accounting Department, and garrison troops use dark blue woolen cloth; Imperial Guard uses scarlet woolen cloth.

1. For stars on the top of military caps: Second Lieutenant and above use gold thread, Accounting Department uses silver thread. Generals wear six stars, Lieutenant Generals wear five stars, Major Generals wear four stars, field officers wear three stars, and company officers wear two stars.
   However, regarding the number of stars on top of caps for the Accounting Department: Supervisory Chief corresponds to Major General rank, and others follow accordingly.

1. For the sun emblem on military caps: Second Lieutenant and above use gold, Sergeant and above use brass, Corporals and enlisted men use copper.

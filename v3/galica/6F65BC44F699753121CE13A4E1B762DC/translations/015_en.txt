Both Prince Friedrich Karl and the Crown Prince became Moltke's students, receiving instruction in military science, particularly in strategy. Moltke especially cherished the Crown Prince. In the 1870 war between Prussia and France, there is no doubt that Moltke had foreseen it several years earlier.

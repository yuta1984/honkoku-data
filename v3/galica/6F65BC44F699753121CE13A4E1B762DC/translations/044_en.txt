Due to newly earning honors there, he wished to immediately collaborate with "Lord Raglan," the commander of the "British" forces, in discussing the attack on "Sevastopol." However, "Lord Raglan" believed that the two generals could not work together harmoniously, and actually deeply disliked cooperating. Therefore, "Canrobert" resigned in favor of "General Pélissier" and departed from "Crimea" two months later.

In 1853, in "Crimea," with the rank of "Colonel," he became deputy to "Marshal Saint-Arnaud." Then in 1854 he was promoted to "Brigadier General." In 1859 he became "Major General" and went to the battlefield in "Italy," where he was praised for his bravery and skillful military tactics.

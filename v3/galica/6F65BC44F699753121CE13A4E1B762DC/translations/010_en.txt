Therefore, biographers mistakenly consider "Moltke" to be a native of "Holstein." "Moltke" and his brothers entered the military academy in "Copenhagen." The school regulations were upright and strict, which suited his inclinations, and he studied diligently, cherishing every moment. Here "Moltke" laid the foundation for his remarkable talents.

He then immediately joined military forces for the expedition to "Mascara" and fought fiercely in several battles against the hostile peoples of North Africa. He also assisted in the assault on "Constantine" in 1837. In 1839, he returned to France with the purpose of suppressing the "dragoons" in the foreign legion of the scattered Carlist forces.

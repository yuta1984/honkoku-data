He immediately joined the military forces for the expedition to "Mascara" and fought several fierce battles against the hostile peoples in North Africa. He also assisted in the attack on "Constantine" in 1837. In 1839, he returned to France with the purpose of commanding dragoons in the foreign legion of the scattered Carlist forces.

He advanced to the position of "Captain" and was immediately appointed to the "Staff." In 1850, due to disputes arising between the two countries of "Austria and Prussia" concerning the territory of "Hesse," "von Steinmetz" received orders in "Kassel." Thereafter he was commanded to govern that place. In 1864, during the Second War of "Schleswig-Holstein," although he had hoped in his heart to be assigned to that campaign, he instead received orders elsewhere, and since this was not according to his wishes, it is said he was reluctant to carry out his duties enthusiastically. During the war with "Austria," "von Steinmetz" commanded the Fifth Army "Corps"

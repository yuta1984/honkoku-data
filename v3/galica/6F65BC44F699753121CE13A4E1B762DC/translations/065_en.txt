It was said that even if we suppose that such military arrogance is sufficient, and even though we arbitrarily claim to be superior to all armies throughout Europe, our valor would probably be of no benefit. This is because there are many defects in the machinery (weapons and equipment) of the Prussian forces, and the resulting disasters would inevitably follow one after another. Therefore, it is advised that we should decide to make reforms at once. This theory was adopted as having keen insight and being the opinion of the highest military scholars.

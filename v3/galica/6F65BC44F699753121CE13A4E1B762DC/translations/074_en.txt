And the Third Army belonged to the "Crown Prince" of "Prussia" and was composed of the 5th, 6th, and 11th Corps, along with two "Bavarian" "Corps." Here there was also a fourth army, composed of the 4th and 12th "Corps" and the "Saxon" and "Prussian" "Guards." This army belonged to "Friedrich August Albert," the "Crown Prince" of "Saxony," who was born in 1828, received excellent education, and was a skilled officer. He was the person who had commanded the "Saxon" division of "Austria" in the war of 1866.

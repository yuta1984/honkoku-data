The "Crown Prince" was born on October 18, 1831. He received education in the academic subjects and military science that all Prussian princes were expected to master. He was also a diligent scholar who particularly favored the natural sciences. Recently he became a student of "von Moltke" and received instruction in the arts of military training and strategy, ultimately mastering their deepest secrets and being able to apply them in practice.

The fifth army, organized from the "Württemberg" and "Baden" contingents under the command of "General Werder," was divided because it had also fought in the siege of "Strasbourg." Among these, the "Landwehr" or "reserves" consisted of the sixth and seventh corps. The sixth belonged to "Friedrich Franz," the valiant and accomplished "Grand Duke" of "Mecklenburg-Schwerin." The seventh belonged to "General von Kamstein" in "Berlin" and to "General Ravenstein" in "Silesia." ○ "Corps Commanders"

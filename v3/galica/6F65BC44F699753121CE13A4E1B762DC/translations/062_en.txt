He became the commander of the first division and quickly deployed his forces to the border. This happened on the twenty-third day of the sixth month. At Liebenayu, Turnow, Podolí, Münchengrätz, and Jičín, they continued to achieve great victories and drove the enemy into the interior of Bohemia.

Here, at the end of the successful conflict in "Algeria," "General Achard" wished to send orders to "Colonel Larichiere" at "Blida," which was two or three ri (leagues) away, to change the order of his deployment. MacMahon volunteered for this mission and led a cavalry squadron as escort. However, he neglected his main guard and remained behind on horseback alone. His journey necessarily took him through entirely dangerous and lawless enemy territory.

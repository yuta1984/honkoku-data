The victory at "Skaritz" was achieved even though the enemy forces exceeded "von Steinmetz's" army by twice the number, for which reason he was called "the Lion of Skaritz." "von Steinmetz" on all battlefields, even when he himself decided to fight with numerically inferior forces on many occasions, never once encountered even the slightest misfortune. Moreover, this general, unlike other generals, when he thought a battle could be won, advanced without giving the slightest consideration to the life and death of his soldiers.

The veteran general was "Karl Friedrich von Steinmetz." This general was one of the old veterans of the Napoleonic Wars (from 1812 to 1815). Although he belonged to a very old tradition within the Prussian national army, he was extremely talented and an outstanding soldier. "Von Steinmetz" was born on December 27, 1796, and entered the military academy at "Kaem" at the age of ten. There he immediately displayed his genuine fondness for military affairs.

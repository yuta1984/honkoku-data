It is said that he once made all enemy forces surrender within sixteen hours.

"General Trochu" was initially not favored by the emperor and was kept at a distance, but finally, during the time of national crisis, he was ordered to govern and guard "Paris." His character was virtuous, brave, and talented. Moreover, both in public and private matters, he had not a single flaw. Born on March 12, 1815, in the "Ardennes" region, he received his education at "Saint-Cyr" and entered that school. In 1840 he became a "Lieutenant," and in 1843 he became a "Captain." In "Algeria" he served in "Marshal Bugeaud's" forces

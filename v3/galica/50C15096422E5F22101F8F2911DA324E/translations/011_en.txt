The Middle Captain lay prostrate, overwhelmed with tears. Meanwhile, the Princess returned to the demon country and, adorned beautifully like a flower, appeared before her father the Great King. Since this was to be her final moment, one could imagine her heart's anguish—how pitiful it was! The Great King looked upon her and said, "So you sent that man back? You are too contemplative and weak. Everyone, come and cast her down!" Thereupon they mercilessly dragged the Princess by her hair, she who had always been treated with reverence.

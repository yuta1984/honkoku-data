*(Continued from previous page)* ...separated by approximately ten *ri* of sea, there is a country called Santan [#1]. Its indigenous inhabitants are not Ezo people. From there, the land is continuous with Manchuria [#2], and consequently it is also connected by land to Beijing in China [#3].

Kunashiri Island [#4] and Etorofu Island [#5] resemble the shape of the character "人" (*hito*, "person") written in regular script (*kaisho*). The stroke "丿" corresponds to Etorofu Island, and the stroke "乀" resembles Kunashiri Island. From here northward to Kamchatka [#7], where the *Akabito* (Russians) [#6] reside, the islands continue in a chain through the sea, arranged like horseshoes.

To the north of Kamchatka, there is a foreign land called Chōkichi [#8], which is subject to the *Akabito*. From Kamchatka westward to the land of the *Akabito*'s royal capital, it is all continuous territory. Although it is land I have not yet visited myself, this information comes from detailed inquiries I made when I met in person with a Russian [#9] man named Simeon Trофей Zuizuyo [#10].

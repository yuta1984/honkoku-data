【Ainu Vocabulary List: Insects and Amphibians】

　　Silver-light lizard (銀光) → Ainu: Sirokane-haramu
Bee/wasp → Ainu: Syō-ya
Hornet (lit. "turtle-bee," i.e., large wasp) → Ainu: Si-syō-ya
Ground wasp/digger wasp (土蜂) → Ainu: Ya-syoya ▲ alternate: Ka-syoya
Bee/wasp with white spots → Ainu: Moyuk-syoya
Horsefly (虻, abu) → Ainu: Sirao ※ Note: the character 虻 is written in the original as an alternate form "亡＋虫"
Biting midge / black fly (buto, buyo) → Ainu: Ibirore
Biting midge, small variety → Ainu: Irairai
　　　Small variety → Ainu: Icyotcyare
Something resembling a biting midge → Ainu: Kamurusiyu
Mosquito → Ainu: Iṭ-tanne
Frog　Toad (hiki-gaeru) → Ainu: Terekei-be
　　　Tree frog / common frog (kawazu) → Ainu: Touron-kamuy
　　　One that lives underground → Ainu: Woatsu

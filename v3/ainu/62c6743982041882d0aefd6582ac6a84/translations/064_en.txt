Uteke an bano uhibaiyan

[They] have a debate/argument about what to do [with it].
　　　　　　　Nepu karukuse yai bata shiare

[They] say "Un-un" (yes, yes) and give thanks/express gratitude.
　　　　　　　Tokono ibawa unke shike

Do not speak while dozing off.
　　　　　　　Mokoro ikotte tsuuha tashi

[It] is unexpectedly wide.
　　　　　　　Ramukan bare chiri ushi

[It] is frightening because [they] do not even speak up in negotiation.
　　　　　　　Webake koshi ne watsuriki ramu chiri

I shall not say unnecessary things.
　　　　　　　Iteke yaiba rōushi

Tie [it] well with rope.
　　　　　　　Iran ma kakano harika a yubū

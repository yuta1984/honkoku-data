【Comparative Vocabulary List of Japanese and Ainu Names for Fish and Marine Creatures】

Kasago (scorpionfish / Sebastiscus marmoratus) — Fūresetsupu
Kachika (sculpin / Cottus pollux) — Nanuuwensetsupu ▲ Ibi
Salmon — Shibe
Cod (tara) — Herekushi ▲ (Note: A different fish species from Notoro Lagoon is called Kafutokara fūni fuha ka kai)
Dolphin (iruka) — Towayuku ▲ Omamu ▲ Terekechironop
Sea bream (tai) — Fūresetsupu ▲ Ninowusetsupu
Sea bass (suzuki) — Airo ▲ Shiyaha
Crucian carp (funa) — Tanbara
(Giant crab) Sharigani — Tekunbekorube ▲ Kupishidunbekorube ▲ Bēkakoruhe
Gokko (lumpfish / Aptocyclus ventricosus) — Akamukorube
Red stingray (aka-ei) — Aichikorube
Crocodile/Shark (wani) — Chikobakui ▲ Onneshame
Fish roe (fish eggs) — Chiboro ▲ Homa
Fish milt (shirako) — U-fu (unclear reading)
Fin (hire) — Mokiratsupu

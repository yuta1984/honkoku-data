【Ainu Vocabulary List: Birds and Reptiles】

(Continued bird names from previous page)
　　　Fure-syam-ciri (red-eyebrowed bird)
　　　Horu-ciri (horu bird)
　　　Wke-ura
　　　Rera-oki
　　　Home-ciri

A bird resembling a hawk with beautiful horns/crests → Ainu: Eṭ-hiruka
A bird resembling a swallow with long drooping eyebrows/markings → Ainu: Fure-ṭ-syam-ciri
Bird (large bird, 大鳥) → Ainu: Cikatpu
Fowl/bird (small bird, 小鳥) → Ainu: Ciri

【Reptiles and Snakes】
Nose-viper / pit viper (mamushi) → Ainu: Totko-kamuy
Hawk-snake (a type of snake) → Ainu: Haskuro-kamuy ※ Note: the character for "snake" (蛇) in the original is written as an alternate form "虫＋也"
White snake → Ainu: Tanne-kamuy
Smooth snake (nameragawa, a smooth-scaled snake) → Ainu: Fugo-oko
Lizard (蚖, tokage) → Ainu: Haramu
　　Golden-light lizard (金光) → Ainu: Kon-kane-haramu

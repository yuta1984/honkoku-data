[Modern English rendering:]
"Though there was agreement between us, due to unfavorable circumstances, it became difficult even to share food and drink, and the bond (between us) had been severed. Nevertheless, with the desire to forge a new agreement — to draw together the rope of kinship and family ties — his younger brother (the intermediary) has arrived. Beside the household goods and grandmother of the esteemed person present here, there are (worn/old) treasures…"

(Modern translation)
Although they reached such an agreement, because the season/timing was not good, they could not adequately obtain food and drink. There are traces of such difficult circumstances continuing. However, according to new rules, because they desire to strengthen bonds with relatives and connections, his younger brother came. There are household goods and wealth beside the grandmother.

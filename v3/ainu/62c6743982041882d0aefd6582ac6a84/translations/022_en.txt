【Ainu Vocabulary List: Insects and Small Creatures (continued)】

Frog (continued)
　　Blue/green variety → Ainu: Kokekketsu
　　　　　　　　　　 → Ainu: Wopon-baki
　　　　　　　　　　 → Ainu: Terekei-mo
Flea (nomi) → Ainu: Taike
Louse/lice (shirami) → Ainu: Kī ▲ alternate: Uruki
Tick (dani) → Ainu: Baraki
Cricket/katydid (kirigirisu) → Ainu: Sibebe
Dragonfly (tonbō) → Ainu: Tanko-kaciuy ▲ alternate: □ngu-kaciuy
Centipede (mukade) → Ainu: Itemeki-riri
Earthworm (mimizu) → Ainu: Tonin
Butterfly (chō) → Ainu: Marewww ▲ alternate: Moseci-katpu
Mole cricket (okera) → Ainu: Wō-kecyaro
Spider (kumo) → Ainu: Yawoshike ▲ alternate: Yatem
Hanetofushi (click beetle or similar winged insect) → Ainu: Imo
Firefly (hotaru) → Ainu: Nishikep ▲ alternate: Ninin-ketsupō

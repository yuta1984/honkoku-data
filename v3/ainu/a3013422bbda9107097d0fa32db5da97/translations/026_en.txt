**Figure of Wonetsbu (Kaikunshin / Fur Seal Kidney/Penis)**

Fur seal kidney (海狗腎, *kaikunshin*): Salty in flavor, greatly heating in nature, non-toxic.

Lei Xiào states: Soak in alcohol for one day, wrap in paper and roast until fragrant, then finely chop and pound; alternatively, place in a silver vessel, heat thoroughly with wine, and combine with other medicines.

Li Shizhen states: If stored together with Sichuan pepper (漢椒) and camphor (樟脳), it will not spoil.

[Indications:] Treats men's chronic ailments, accumulation of qi and masses, cold accumulation, fatigue of qi, decline and damage of kidney essence, as well as consumption and wasting of vital essence (精気) caused by excessive sexual indulgence.

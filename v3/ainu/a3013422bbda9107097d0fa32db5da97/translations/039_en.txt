**Figure of the Kunne-chiro-no-tsup**

This is a fox native to Shikotan Island.
Its fur is a pale black color.

As recorded in the *Shoku Nihongi*: In the seventh month of the fifth year of Wadō (712 CE), the provincial governor of Iga Province, Atai no Kei and others, presented a black fox as tribute. This was deemed a *jōzui* (an auspicious omen of the highest order), and the provincial governor and those below him were retroactively granted increases in court rank.

Preface to Moshiogusa (A Collection of Dialect Words)
The transmission of commands from superiors to subordinates is the foundation of governing a nation. Distinguishing between matters and understanding human emotions are the essential points of executing government. Now, when I examine this book of Ainu dialect, this too relates to the crucial means of governing the people - how can it be said to be merely a translator's pastime? Moreover, this is a time for spreading virtuous transformation and opening great enterprises.
This is the reason for publishing this work.

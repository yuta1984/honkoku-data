【Ainu Language Equivalents for Clothing and Tools】

Headband (hachimaki): Haunbe ▲ Baminapu
Jittoku [a type of men's outer garment]: Chiyoshikeimi
Seam: Ikiri
Hemp/Linen: Asakara
Indigo (dye): Kahai
Ohyō (Ulmus laciniata) bark [used for attush cloth]: Atsu
Shinanoki [Japanese linden/basswood (Tilia japonica)]: Nibeshi ▲ Kuberikep
Willow bark: Nikaunai ▲ Meromai (dialect of the Sōya area)
Hat/headgear: Konji
　This shape [of hat, type 1]: Ikari Konji
　This shape [of hat, type 2]: Iyane Konji
　This shape [of hat, type 3]: Watanbushi
Arrow case [possibly a quiver]: Ikayup ▲ Bushi
Kanjiki [snowshoes]: Teshima
Mountain knife/hatchet (nata): Tashiro

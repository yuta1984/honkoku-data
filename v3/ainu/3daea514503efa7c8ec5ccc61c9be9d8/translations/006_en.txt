Tools and Goods Section
Records items that constitute implements and wealth, such as household furniture, boats, machinery, and other utensils.

Birds and Beasts Section
Records not only birds and beasts, but generally all living creatures that are active and move about.

Plants and Trees Section
Records grasses and trees, including edible grains, fruits, and vegetables that sprout and grow in mountains and fields.

Categories and Items
Records things that are categorized and enumerated, such as large and small, good and evil, rise and fall, prosperity and decline.

Auxiliary Particles
Records dialect forms belonging to auxiliary particles.

Compound Words
Records compound expressions in colloquial language used in daily life.

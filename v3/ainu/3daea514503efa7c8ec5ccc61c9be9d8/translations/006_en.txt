Utensils and Goods Section

Records all things that serve as implements or constitute property, including household goods, tools, boats, and equipment.

Birds and Beasts Section

Records not only birds and beasts, but all living creatures that swim and run about.

Plants and Trees Section

Records things similar to plants and trees, including edible grains, fruits, vegetables, and things that sprout and grow in mountains and fields.

Categories Section

Records items classified and enumerated by their qualities, such as large/small, good/bad, rising/falling, and flourishing/declining.

Auxiliary Words Section

Records Ainu dialect words belonging to the category of auxiliary words (particles and grammatical function words).

Compound Expressions Section

Records commonly used compound expressions and set phrases that have become established in everyday usage.

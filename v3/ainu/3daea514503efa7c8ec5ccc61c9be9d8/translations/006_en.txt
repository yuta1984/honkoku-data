Implements and Goods Section
Records household implements, boats, tools, and all things that serve as utensils or constitute property.

Birds and Beasts Section
Records not only birds and beasts, but generally all living things that move about.

Plants and Trees Section
Records plants and trees, edible grains, fruits, vegetables, and things that sprout in mountains and fields.

Categories
Records things that are counted by raising items and establishing categories such as large and small, good and evil, rise and fall, prosperity and decline.

Auxiliary Particles
Records dialectal words that belong to auxiliary particles.

Compound Words
Records compound words that have been formed and established in colloquial language used in daily life.

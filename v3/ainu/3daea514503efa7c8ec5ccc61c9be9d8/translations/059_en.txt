Vocabulary glossary: Japanese terms with their Ainu language equivalents (current page)

To carry/transport → Iku
To mix/mingle → Ugū

To walk/proceed on foot → Apkashi
Slowly/gradually → Moiretara / Yayahauken
Not fast/slow → Uenkashiba
A gift/bestowal (something granted by a superior) → Ikirap
To reach/attain/accomplish → Kashikamuinu
Outrageous/unthinkable/unexpected (lit. "beyond reckoning; not imagined") → Emontabire
To be arranged/put in order/prepared → Itomotsu
To shrink/contract → Yomomuke / Yon-yon
Intense/fierce/strong → Yupke / Rui
To become quiet/calm → Shiaure / Hauke
Seam/joint/point of joining → Utsuru

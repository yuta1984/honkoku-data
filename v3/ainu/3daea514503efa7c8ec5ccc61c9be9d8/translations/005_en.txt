Ainu Dialect Sections

Heaven and Earth Section
Records notable matters concerning mountains as creations between heaven and earth, names of seas, and climate.

Human Relations Section

Records terms that distinguish between self and others, such as old and young, father and son, and other appellations that differ in their designations.

Body Parts Section

Records matters concerning internal and external body parts, longevity and premature death, etc.

Worldly Affairs Section

Records matters related to worldly affairs, including where thoughts work, where hands and feet labor, and everything that pertains to secular matters.

Mouth, Nose, Ears, Eyes, Heart

Among the Worldly Affairs section, items related to these five [senses/organs] are listed separately.

【Left Page】
1　In the 8th month of An'ei 6 (1777), by his father's petition,
　　he was granted his first audience with the lord
1　In the 10th month of Tenmei 4 (1784), by his father's petition, he was
　　appointed as a guard substitute and served castle guard duty
　　in Harada Goroemon's unit
1　In the 9th month of Kansei 1 (1789), after unit commander Harada Goroemon
　　was dismissed from his position, he was transferred to
　　Yokoyama Takiemon's unit
1　In the 3rd month of the same 4th year (1792), he participated in bird hunting
　　　Thereafter, he participated nine times and received game as rewards four times

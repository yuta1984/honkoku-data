1. In the 12th month of the same year, due to his poor eyesight,
   he was exempted from duties including guard service (gobankata), 
   accompanying his lord, and bird hunting, etc.

1. In the same month, his request to change his name to Gensen 
   was granted.

1. In the 9th month of the 6th year (Bunsei 6), due to his poor eyesight 
   preventing him from performing proper service for a long period, 
   and feeling deeply apologetic about this situation, he requested 
   permission to treat serious wounds with his own resources without 
   receiving medicine fees in addition to his regular stipend, and 
   this was granted.

【Right Page】
- In the same month, a person named Chōkichi from Hatazato in Sukawanosono Village, Inawashiro, was bitten by a wild dog, so [the doctor] was ordered to provide treatment. He went there, treated the patient, and achieved a complete recovery.

- In December of the same year, due to his deep dedication to medical arts and his efforts in treating sword wounds and dog bite poisoning—traveling even to distant regions to learn techniques, applying himself wholeheartedly, and achieving many remarkable cures—he was granted twenty bags of rice annually as support stipend and was granted an audience as a support physician.

【Left Page】
- In the third year (Year of the Tiger), eighth month, he was ordered to prepare emergency medicines and distilled liquor, and since he also understood good methods for treating sword wounds, he was ordered to attend not only during emergencies but also during falcon hunting expeditions.

- In the fourth year (Year of the Rabbit), seventh month, he was ordered to prepare a medicine according to the Katō Shikibu family's traditional method for treating deep cuts that reach muscles and bones.

- In the ninth month of the same year, he attended a falcon hunting expedition.

- In the eleventh month of the same year, due to his skillful techniques and the many excellent treatments that were reported, he was granted a stipend for five people, plus additional compensation

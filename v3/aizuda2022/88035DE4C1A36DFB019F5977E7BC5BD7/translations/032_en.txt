【Left Page】
One　In the intercalary 7th month of Kansei 9 (Year of the Snake): Was appointed as instructor of Ichishi-ryū spear techniques at the military academy and granted one ryō of gold as funding assistance

One　In the 11th month of Kyōwa 2 (Year of the Dog): Was appointed to the temporary position of trainee deputy magistrate

One　In the 12th month of the same year: Was formally appointed as trainee deputy magistrate

One　In the 9th month of Bunka 2 (Year of the Ox): Six koku was reduced from his father's stipend allowance, and he was granted ten koku and provisions for three persons, and summoned to the shinban group

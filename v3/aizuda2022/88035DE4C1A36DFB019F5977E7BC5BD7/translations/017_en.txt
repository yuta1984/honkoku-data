- Tenmei 3 (Year of the Rabbit), 3rd month: Following his late father's dismissal from official duties, his stipend was reduced by 2 koku and 1 person's rations, and he was granted 18 koku and 3 persons' rations and summoned to serve as a samurai at Inawashiro

- Tenmei 4 (Year of the Dragon), 8th month: As requested, he was summoned back to Wakamatsu, appointed to the Tozama-gumi unit, joined Takahashi Koemon's group, and assigned to guard duty at the castle

- Same month: Due to the reassignment of group leader Takahashi Koemon, he was transferred to Kayano Gondayū's group as a replacement

　　the matter
- In December of the same year, he was summoned as a physician for permanent duty in Sagami Province, granted ten-person stipend, of which three-person stipend was given as official duty allowance.
- In March of the 8th year, regarding treatment of serious illnesses as well as dog poison and rat poison treatments, having obtained transmission [of knowledge] from previous years and conducting extensive treatments, while the aforementioned pharmaceutical methods were traditionally passed from father to son only, since he was specially promoted to permanent duty in Sagami Province this time, wishing to preserve this transmission in the home domain as well, he transmitted the pharmaceutical methods and

【Left page】
　　the procedures of treatment methods completely to Momozaki Yōken. Regarding this matter, since he instructed Yōken so that military medical supplies could be prepared without expense to the domain, this being a meritorious act, as a reward he was privately granted three bundles of cotton.
- In December of the 10th year, regarding the Sagami military camp, although the climate was different, since he devoted himself diligently to medical treatment, as a reward he was granted two bundles of cotton.
- In October of the 11th year, he was reassigned to Misaki duty.

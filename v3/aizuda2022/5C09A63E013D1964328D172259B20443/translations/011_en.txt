Item 1: 4th month of Shōtoku 5, year of the sheep　Went up to Edo for guard duty service, returned in the 10th month of the same year

Item 1: 3rd month of Kyōhō 14, year of the rooster　Was appointed to the御次番 (alternate guard duty) position

Item 1: 4th month of the same 15th year, year of the dog　Went up to Edo for guard duty service, was appointed to manage lodging arrangements along the official travel route, returned in the 10th month of the same year
　　As a reward for managing the lodging arrangements along the official travel route, he was granted 3 pieces of silver

Item 1: 2nd month of the same 16th year, year of the boar　Was appointed as inspector (metsuke)

Item 1: 4th month of the same year　Was granted 50 koku as official duty stipend

Item 1: 9th month of the same year　Went up to Edo for guard duty service, returned in the 3rd month of the following year of the rat

Item 1: 7th month of the same 17th year, year of the rat　According to his petition, he was relieved of his official duties, appointed to the gaisama-gumi-tsuki position, joined Kasuga Jinzaemon's unit, and served castle guard duty

Item 1: 12th month of the same 18th year, year of the ox　His petition to adopt Bansuke, the younger brother of Suzuki Shōzaemon, as son-in-law and heir was approved

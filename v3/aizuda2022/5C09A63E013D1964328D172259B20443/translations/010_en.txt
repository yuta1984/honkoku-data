Itō Nanemon Sukeshō (originally Hasegi, originally Bannosuke, born on the 22nd day of the 3rd month of Enpō 3, year of the hare)
　Mother: Same as above
　Wife: Daughter of Onoda Sukenoyuki Naohō (died of illness at age 61 on the 28th day of the 11th month of Tenna 2, year of the dog, posthumous Buddhist name Tōrin-in Kaun Ryōten, buried at Banshōzan Tenneiji Temple)

Item 1: 8th month of Genroku 6, year of the rooster　When retirement was ordered according to his father's request, he was granted a divided inheritance of 100 koku, appointed to the gaisama-gumi-tsuki position, joined Hyūga Saburōemon's unit, and served castle guard duty

Item 1: 11th month of the same 8th year, year of the boar　Was appointed to the attendant guard (otomo-ban) position

Item 1: 4th month of the same 9th year, year of the rat　Went up to Edo as part of the official attendance procession return journey

Item 1: 9th month of the same year　When he went up to Edo for guard duty service, due to illness he petitioned and returned in the 2nd month of the following year of the ox

Item 1: 12th month of the same 13th year, year of the dragon　Was granted a residence

Item 1: 5th month of the same 15th year, year of the horse　Went up to Edo for guard duty service as an attendant, returned in the 10th month of the same year

Item 1: 4th month of the same 16th year, year of the sheep　According to his petition, he was relieved of his official duties, appointed to the gaisama-gumi-tsuki position, and assigned to Jinbo Rokuzaemon's unit

Item 1: 1st month of Tenna 2, year of the earth-dog　Had been entrusted with Kuruma Ichirōzaemon, but when he was banished in the 6th month of the same year, escorted him to Sukawa Noguchi and returned

Item 1: 9th month of Tenna 3, year of the boar　Went up to Edo for guard duty, returned in the 4th month of the following rat year

Item 1: 8th month of Jōkyō 1, year of the rat　Served as guard for Lord Hijikata Iga no Kami, traveled from the local town to Akataniguchi

Item 1: Jōkyō 2, year of the ox (month unknown)　Petitioned to be relieved of official duties, but was told that while he would be dismissed in due time, he should continue his service for now

Item 1: 2nd month of Genroku 1, year of the dragon　Upon his second petition, was relieved of official duties and appointed to the non-official group

Item 1: 6th month of the same year　During the lord's entry into the domain, went up to Edo and returned accompanying the pages

Item 1: 9th month of the same year　As requested, his son Saburōzaemon was appointed as his replacement in guard duty

Item 1: 5th month of Genroku 6, year of the rooster　As requested, was granted retirement

Item 1: 26th day of the 3rd month of Genroku 12, year of the rabbit　Died of illness at age 76, posthumous Buddhist name Dōsenindono Hakuō Korezō, buried at the same location

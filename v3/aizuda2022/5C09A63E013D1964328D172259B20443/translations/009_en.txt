Item 1: 5th month of Shōtoku 1, year of the hare　When rumors of adultery involving Kikkawa Kyūrōbei's wife were heard, if there was no stain on his own person, he should have promptly filed a complaint, but he did not do so. This time, through investigation, it became clear that he had negotiated with Kyūrōbei's wife's side and sent a written apology. This being the height of adultery and immorality, a detailed investigation was conducted, and although severe punishment should have been ordered, considering that his family had served since the time of the ancestors, by special mercy he was ordered into exile, and it was proclaimed that he was banned from both Edo and the Aizu road

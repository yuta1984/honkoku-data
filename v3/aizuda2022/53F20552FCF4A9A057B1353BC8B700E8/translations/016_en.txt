It is said by tradition that the father of the first generation Tōkichi was summoned to serve as御坊主 (gobōzu, a Buddhist attendant) in Takato, Shinano Province, but there is no evidence for this. He died of illness on the 27th day of the 9th month of Shōtoku 1, Year of the Rabbit. His posthumous Buddhist name was Hongen'in Shōgaku Dōnen.

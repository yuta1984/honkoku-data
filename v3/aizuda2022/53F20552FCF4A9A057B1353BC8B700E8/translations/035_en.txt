1. Kansei 1, Year of the Rooster, 1st month: Due to his diligent service, his official stipend was increased beyond the current two koku as a direct grant.

1. Kansei 2, Year of the Dog, 1st month: When the examination office was discontinued, he was appointed as a minor official of the accounting office with examination duties, maintaining his previous position.

1. Kansei 4, Year of the Rat, 12th month: After taking on examination duties, the administrative work between Edo and Aizu became extremely busy year-round. He made suggestions and, despite staff reductions, handled various examinations meticulously, making the higher-level administrative work even more demanding. The minor officials, temporary employees, and apprentices all considered the circumstances of their positions, coordinated together, and worked diligently to meet the demands. This was reported through proper channels, and he was told to continue his efforts with even greater dedication.

1. Kansei 5, Year of the Ox, 1st month: Having served for many years with meritorious achievements, though it was a weighty matter, he was granted the status of independent audience (dokurai).

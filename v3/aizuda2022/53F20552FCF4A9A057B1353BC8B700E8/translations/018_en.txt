1. Enkyō 3, Year of the Tiger, 4th month: Because he served well in practice during his late father Tōshichi's lifetime, out of consideration he was granted a stipend for two persons.
1. Hōreki 4, Year of the Dog, 7th month: He was granted the standard ceremonial gold of 3 ryō and a stipend for two persons, and was summoned to serve as a regular attendant priest (tsunedzume hira obōzu).

He was appointed to mansion duty and assigned attendants, so he was ordered to commute and perform his duties.
1. 12th month of the same year: He was ordered to return to secular life,
　and was appointed to concurrent duties as secretary and various meeting attendant to Lady Rinkō-in,
　with annual stipend granted.
1. 12th month of Meiwa 7, Year of the Tiger: Because he performed both his primary and concurrent duties with diligence, he was granted an Edo stipend for one person.
1. 6th month of An'ei 1, Year of the Dragon: He was appointed as secretary to the magistrate.

1. 11th month of An'ei 3, Year of the Horse: He was appointed as envoy to Ise Province for the following spring and performed the duty.

1. 12th month of An'ei 6, Year of the Rooster: Because he performed his duties diligently, he was granted an additional reward of one ryō in voucher money.
1. 11th month of An'ei 9, Year of the Rat: He was appointed as envoy to Ise Province for the following spring and performed the duty.

1. 3rd month of Tenmei 1, Year of the Ox: Upon his request, when the lord traveled down to the domain, he was appointed to accompany in his primary duty capacity.
1. 10th month of Tenmei 4, Year of the Dragon: Because he was not skilled at his current duties, he was dismissed from his position,
　appointed to no position, and ordered to be sent down to Aizu.

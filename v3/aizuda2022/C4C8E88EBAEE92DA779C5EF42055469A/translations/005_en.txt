## Kanbei Naoshige (born in the Year of the Tiger, Tenshō 6 [1578])

- **Mother**: Daughter of the Kambayashi clan
- **Wife**: Daughter of Sakai Shinuemon Chikatsugu (died of illness on the 4th day of the 3rd month of Shōho 1 [1644], the Year of the Monkey. Posthumous Buddhist name: Kōhinin-dono Kakei Rishun-shi. Place of burial: Shōuzan Goryūji Temple)

Item: Date unknown. Served at Takato in Shinano Province (stipend and title unknown). It is recorded that he served from the time of Lord Tōkō [a reference to an earlier lord].

Item: In the 6th year of Kan'ei [1629], Year of the Snake (month unknown), a separate land grant of 200 koku was bestowed upon his son Jingorōemon.

Item: In the 13th year of the same era [1636], Year of the Rat, in the 8th month, his stipend at Mogami in Dewa Province was increased to 1,300 koku, and he was appointed to the position of karō (senior retainer/chief councilor).

Item: In the 15th year of the same era [1638], Year of the Tiger, in the 4th month, he was granted an audience with Lord Genyūin (Tokugawa Iemitsu).

Item: On the 18th day of the 8th month of the 17th year of the same era [1640], Year of the Dragon, he died of illness at the age of 63. Posthumous Buddhist name: Hōonji Ippō Zeshin Koji. Place of burial: Mogami Chōgenji Temple.

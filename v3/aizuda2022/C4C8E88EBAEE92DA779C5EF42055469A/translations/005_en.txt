Kanbei Naoshige
　Mother: Daughter of the Kambayashi clan
　Wife: Daughter of Sakai Shinuemon Chikatsugu (died of illness on the 4th day of the 3rd month of Shōhō 1st year, Year of the Metal Monkey; Buddhist name: Kōhininden Kakeiri-shunshi; burial site: Shōunzan Goryūji Temple)

1. Date unknown: In Takato, Shinshū Province (fief and position details unknown)
   Served from the time of Lord Tōru

1. Kan'ei 6th year, Year of the Earth Snake, month unknown: As divided land for his son Jingozaemon
   Granted 200 koku

1. Same 13th year, Year of the Rat, 8th month: With fief in Mogami, Ushū Province
   Granted a stipend of 1,300 koku and appointed to the position of chief retainer

1. Same 15th year, Year of the Tiger, 4th month:
   Granted audience with Lord Gen'yūin

1. Same 17th year, Year of the Dragon, 8th month, 18th day: Died of illness at age 63
   Buddhist name: Hōonji Ippō Zeshin Koji; burial site:
   Chōgenji Temple in Mogami

Daughter: Wife of Ibuka Mozaemon Shigeyoshi
　Mother: Same as above

Daughter of Shinoda Hanzaemon Takakon

In Kansei 2 (Inu year), 3rd month: As per his father's request, was granted his first audience with the lord

In Kansei 6 (Tora year), 4th month: When traveling to Edo together with his father, was appointed as a gohirōma gaisama-shi (outer retainer of the great hall), and frequently served as messenger and escort for the lord's wife

In Kyōwa 2 (Tori year): Was appointed to assist in tea ceremony instruction

In the 8th month of the same year: Was appointed as tea ceremony assistant instructor

In the 9th month of the same year: Was appointed as administrator of the Sekishū school of tea ceremony

In Kyōwa 3 (I year), 2nd month: Due to Eguchi Den'yo's guard duty, was appointed as assistant instructor in his place

In Bunka 1 (Ne year), 9th month: Was granted the hereditary stipend of 100 koku without change, was appointed to the outer retainer group, entered Takahashi Yogorō's unit, and served guard duty at the castle

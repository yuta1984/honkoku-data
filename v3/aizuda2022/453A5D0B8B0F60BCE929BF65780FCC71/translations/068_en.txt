1　Same 4th year, Year of the Rabbit, 4th month, since Ichima, second son of Hara Hikobē,
　had fully recovered from his illness, his request for formal adoption
　was granted as petitioned
1　Same 6th year, Year of the Snake, 4th month, since adopted son Ichima's illness
　had relapsed, his request to return him to his original family was granted
　as petitioned
1　Same 8th year, Year of the Sheep, 12th month, his request to adopt
　Tōjirō, second son of Akabane Hachizaemon, was granted as petitioned
1　Same 10th year, Year of the Rooster, 3rd month, it was proclaimed that he had
　served diligently for ten years until last Year of the Monkey
1　Same 11th year, Year of the Dog, 11th month, due to adopted son Tōjirō's illness,
　his request to return him to his original family was granted as petitioned
1　Same 13th year, Year of the Rat, 12th month, for achieving superior rank in
　formation drills at the Third Bailey for three years and showing diligent effort,
　sake was bestowed upon the entire unit
1　Same 14th year, Year of the Ox, 2nd month, due to unit commander Jimbo Naizō's
　reassignment, he transferred to Kasuya Sakon's unit for subsequent service
1　Same year, 10th month, for his dedication to military drills, as a reward
　he was bestowed one new spear

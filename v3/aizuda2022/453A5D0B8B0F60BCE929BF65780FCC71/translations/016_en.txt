1. In the 12th month of the same year, by the inner intention of Lord Kyōtei, a marriage was arranged between his son Kazue and the daughter of Sasahara Genkichi
1. In the intercalary 6th month of the same 7th year (year of the tiger), went up to Edo for emergency guard duty and returned in the 3rd month of the following year of the rabbit
　　However, was appointed as assistant to the lord's retinue during campaign, and also received items from the lord's storehouse
1. In the 6th month of the same 8th year (year of the rabbit), was commended for diligently serving as assistant to the lord's retinue in Edo without negligence
1. In Anei 1 (year of the dragon), went up to Edo for guard duty

　　However, performed regular duties as well as guard duty at the upper residence's Shiba tea house quarters, and returned home in the 11th month

1. In the 7th month of the same 10th year (1813), year of the Rooster, he was appointed as temporary supervisor of Ichishi-ryū spear techniques.
　　Note: He was appointed to this position repeatedly thereafter.

1. In the 4th month of the same 13th year (1816), year of the Rat, three koku were reduced from his father's stipend allocation, and he was granted twenty koku and four-person stipend, summoned to the external retainer group, entered Jinbo Kuranosuke's unit, and served in castle guard duty.

1. In the 12th month of the same year, his request to change his name to Jūbei was granted.

1. In the same month, regarding the training at the Sannomaru training ground, his formations and marching were well-coordinated, he reached the highest level over three years, and his rank advanced. Due to his diligent efforts, he was granted sake, and this was announced to all members of his unit.

1. In the 2nd month of the same 14th year (1817), year of the Ox, when his superior Jinbo Kuranosuke was appointed as wakadoshiyori (junior councilor), he was transferred to serve under Kasuya Sakon's unit.

1. In the 8th month of the same year, he went out to Ōnohara for the bird hunting expedition.

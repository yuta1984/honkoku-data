Wife: daughter of Saka Tsunebei Naonori (born in Genroku 16, year of the sheep; died on the 27th day of the 12th month of An'ei 5, year of the monkey, at age 74 from illness; posthumous Buddhist name: Yōshun'in Kōsetsu Myōzen Daishi; burial site: same as above)

1. In the 3rd month of Kyōhō 15 (year of the dog), succeeded to family headship and received a fief of 100 koku without change, serving castle guard duty in Ichinose Denbei's unit
1. In the 4th month of the same 16th year (year of the boar), was appointed to personal attendant guard duty
1. In the 3rd month of the same 19th year (year of the tiger), went up to Edo for guard duty service, completed one tour of duty and returned
1. In the 5th month of the same year, was commended for having served as attendant during the two banquets held previously for the domain inspectors
1. In the 3rd month of Genbun 1 (year of the dragon), went up to Edo for guard duty service and returned in the 9th month of the same year
1. In the 12th month of the same 2nd year (year of the snake), was relieved of duty as requested and was appointed to the external retainer group, joining Ichinose Kenmotsu's unit to serve castle guard duty
1. In the 7th month of Kanpō 1 (year of the rooster), was appointed as district administrator (daikan)
1. In the 8th month of the same 2nd year (year of the dog), petitioned to be relieved of duty due to illness, but was ordered to continue serving while recuperating

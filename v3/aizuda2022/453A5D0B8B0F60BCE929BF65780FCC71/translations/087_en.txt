1. In the 6th month of Bunka 1 (1804), year of the Rat, he was appointed as provisional supervisor of Ichishi-ryū spear techniques.

1. In the 3rd month of the same 2nd year (1805), year of the Ox, he was appointed as supervisor of spear techniques.

1. In the 6th month of the same year, he was appointed as supervisor of Inzai-school archery.

1. In the 8th month of the same year, he was appointed to serve as a retainer for external samurai.
　　Note: He was appointed to this position repeatedly thereafter.

1. In the intercalary 8th month of the same year, at the time of Lord Sadaaki's succession, he was appointed to serve as a page attendant.

1. In the 12th month of the same year, he was appointed as a regular retainer for the escort guard.

1. In the 11th month of the same 6th year (1809), year of the Snake, he was appointed as assistant instructor at the domain school.

1. In the 4th month of the same 8th year (1811), year of the Sheep, he was appointed as instructor at the domain school.

1. In the 3rd month of the same 9th year (1812), year of the Monkey, as requested, he was relieved of his position as domain school instructor.

He went up as an escort for the return journey.
• Same year, 5th month: He was appointed as ashigaru inspector with the same duties as the regular position and served until the 6th month.
　　　However, he was frequently appointed to this position thereafter.
• Meiwa 1st year, Saru cycle, 3rd month: For the lord's journey to the capital, he was appointed to lodging arrangement duties.
　　　However, for performing these duties, he was granted two bu in gold as a reward.
• Same 2nd year, Tori cycle, 4th month: For the pilgrimage to Nikko, he was appointed as an escort, and as a return escort he went up to Edo and served in attendance.
• Same 3rd year, Inu cycle, 1st month: He received an increase of one person's rations and three koku in role stipend, and was appointed as ashigaru inspector.
• Same year, 4th month: When he went up to Edo for guard duty, he served in military camp receptions and wedding ceremony duties, was granted clothing allowance, and was dismissed in the 10th month of the same year.
　　　However, after this he served one term of guard duty and one return escort duty.
• Same 7th year, Tora cycle, 1st month: Carpentry workshop supervision for half a year

• Same 3rd year, Boar cycle (1803), 8th month: As he was naturally to accompany [his lord] during the autumn duty rotation, he was ordered to inquire about rice and money transactions in Edo, and regarding the receipt and payment of circulating rice as well as stipend warehouse transactions, all of which would henceforth be managed from Aizu. He was to hold thorough discussions with the head provisioning officer of the Edo rice circulation department and investigate matters before returning. Accordingly, he was appointed as rice circulation officer during the autumn duty period. Since the management of regular funds in Edo involved complex transactions with various inclusion personnel and others, he was ordered to investigate and report how the regular funds could be coordinated with local financial management. He departed in the same year's 10th month, stopping at Shirakawa, Akutsu, and Kubota to examine rice circulation accounts, and returned in the 3rd month of the following Rat year.

• Same year, 10th month: He was ordered to serve jointly in loan administration duties.

• Bunka 2nd year, Ox cycle (1805), 8th month: As there was official business requiring the investigation of regulatory matters in Edo, he was ordered to go up to Edo for examination. He departed and went up in the same month.

• Same year, 12th month: For his diligent service over many years, including his recent trip to Edo to investigate regulatory matters and his skillful management of rice circulation surplus grain accounting, he was granted a special promotion in seating rank above the meal administration reform.

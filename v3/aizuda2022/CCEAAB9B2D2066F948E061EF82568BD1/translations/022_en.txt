• Same year, 12th month: While he had been appointed to Ezo affairs, regarding the regular treasury position, Endo Sosuke was also appointed as temporary head accountant. However, separating both men at this time when there were substantial financial inflows and matters had not yet been settled according to established procedures would result in inadequate supervision. Therefore, he was relieved of his temporary head accountant position.

• Same 6th year, Snake cycle (1809), 8th month: He was appointed as head accountant with an official stipend of 2 koku and provisions for one person as before. He was also appointed as chief administrator and ordered to concurrently serve as accounting officer.

• Same month: As personnel were being dispatched to Ezo, despite the heavy official duties, he was commended for his diligent service.

• Same year, 9th month: He was appointed to additional duties overseeing both Aizu treasury departments concurrently.

• Same 7th year, Horse cycle (1810), 9th month: He was ordered to negotiate financing for Sukegawa, traveling there and borrowing 10,000 ryo from Naito Yatōzaemon and 1,000 ryo from Yūzaemon before returning.

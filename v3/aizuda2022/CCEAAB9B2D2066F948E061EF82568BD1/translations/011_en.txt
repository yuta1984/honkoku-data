Hikonai Tadafuru
　
　　Mother: Daughter of the Sanpei family

• Genbun 1st year, Tatsu cycle, 11th month: From his late father's stipend assessment, two koku were reduced, and he was granted eight koku and provisions for two people, and was summoned to serve as nakanokuchi-ban (middle gate guard).

• Same 4th year, Hitsuji cycle, 6th month: He was appointed to the foot soldiers (o-kachi) and granted an additional four koku increase.

• Kanpō 1st year, Tori cycle, 4th month: As part of his rotation duty, he went up to Edo and served one term of duty before returning.
　　　However, he served nine months in the garrison
　　　　He was appointed to accompany Lord Dotsū on his journey to Kyoto and served in that capacity.

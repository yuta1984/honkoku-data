He was granted permission to reside in the countryside as petitioned, and was allocated provisions for nine retainers.

1. Same month: During the peasant uprising, he was stationed in the castle town, and for this he was granted commendatory words along with all others.

1. Hōreki 4, year of the Dog (1754), 10th month: As petitioned, he was ordered to resume active duty, joined Suwa Isuke's unit, and served castle guard duty.

1. Same 6th year, year of the Rat (1756), 9th month: He went up to Edo for guard duty and served one tour, returning in the 4th month of the following year of the Ox.

1. Same year, 10th month: He petitioned to make Kozen, the biological son of Gentabyōe, his heir.

1. Meiwa 1, year of the Monkey (1764), 1st month: Since unit commander Suwa Isuke was relieved of his duties, the successor unit became Numasawa Gonbyōe's group.

1. Same 3rd year, year of the Dog (1766), 9th month: He went to Yamagata for guard duty and returned the following year of the Boar, 3rd month, when his replacement arrived.

1. Same 5th year, year of the Rat (1768), 3rd month: As petitioned, his son Kozen was appointed as guard substitute.

1. Same 6th year, year of the Ox (1769), 5th month: Since his son Kozen, who was serving as guard substitute, absconded, he should have returned to active duty himself, but due to illness, he petitioned and submitted the minor construction fee while remaining at home.

- In the same month, he petitioned to change his name to Kurōzaemon.

- In the 6th month of the same year, his son Gengo was appointed as a guard substitute as requested.

- In the 12th month of the 6th year (Year of the Horse), regarding the matter of borrowed goods and guaranty involving Tomekichi, the fourth son of Sone Ukyō, after investigation his stipend of 200 koku was reduced by half, he was removed from group assignment and placed on small construction duty status.

- In the 3rd month of the 8th year (Year of the Monkey), he was granted an audience.

- In the 9th month of the same year, he was assigned to an external group and entered Hyūga Saburōemon's unit to serve castle guard duty.

- In the 9th month of Kansei 1 (Year of the Rooster), since his adopted son Gengo became ill, he petitioned to return him to his original family under Saitō Genchi.

- In the 6th month of Kansei 2 (Year of the Dog), he petitioned to change his name to Tetsuzaemon.

- In the 1st month of Kansei 5 (Year of the Ox), due to private financial difficulties, he was granted permission to reside in the countryside as requested, and was given allowance for 8 retainers.

1. In the 10th year of the same era (1725, Year of the Snake), 10th month: When his father retired, he was granted a partial stipend of 50 koku from the 150 koku fief, and was summoned as a martial arts instructor of Shinten-ryū swordsmanship.

1. On the 14th day of the 6th month of the 14th year (1729, Year of the Rooster): He died of illness at the age of 33. His posthumous Buddhist name was Shingi-in Dōchō Shinji, and he was buried at Chōshōzan Kyūfuku-ji temple.

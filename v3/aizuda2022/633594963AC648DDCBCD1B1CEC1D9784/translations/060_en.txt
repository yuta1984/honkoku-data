Wife: daughter of adoptive father Gentabei Tadayoshi, died of illness on the 4th day of the 11th month of Kansei 1 (Year of the Rooster). Buddhist name was Tan'un Chisetsu Shinnyo, buried in the same place as above.
　　　　　　　　　　　　Daughter of Takamine Kinzaemon Yoshiaki

- In the 3rd month of Meiwa 7 (Year of the Tiger), he was granted his first audience as requested by his father.

- In the 6th month of the same year, he was appointed as a guard substitute as requested by his father, and served castle guard duty in Ichinose Naiki's unit.

- In the 6th month of An'ei 4 (Year of the Sheep), he petitioned to change his name to Magoichirō.

- In the 2nd month of the 8th year (Year of the Boar), he was granted the hereditary stipend of 200 koku without any reduction.

- In the 2nd month of the same year, due to group leader Ichinose Naiki's reassignment, he transferred to Saigō Genzō's unit as successor.

- In the 7th month of Tenmei 2 (Year of the Tiger), he petitioned to surrender his stipend due to illness and be placed on half-pay status.

- In the 9th month of the 3rd year (Year of the Rabbit), due to the death of group leader Saigō Genzō, he transferred to Takekawa Gojūrō's unit as successor.

- In the 4th month of the 4th year (Year of the Dragon), he petitioned to adopt Gengo, the eldest son of Saitō Genchi.

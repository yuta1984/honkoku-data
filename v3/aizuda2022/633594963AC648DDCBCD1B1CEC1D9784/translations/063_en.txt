Magotarō　　Died of illness on the 5th day of the 1st month of Kansei 2 (Year of the Dog), Buddhist name Zen'un Chichin Dōji, burial site same as above
　　　　　Mother was the daughter of grandfather Gentabee Tadayoshi

Ichinose Sukezaemon Tadayoshi　Initially Magojirō, later Kansuke, later Magoichirō, later Gentabee
　Mother same as above
　Wife was an adopted daughter of Misaka Shichitayū Takagen
　　Actually a daughter of Hara Rokuma (certain person)

- In the 11th month of Kansei 11 (Year of the Sheep), as his father petitioned, he was granted his first formal audience with the lord.
- In the 3rd month of Kansei 12 (Year of the Monkey), he inherited the family headship and was granted the family stipend of 100 koku without change. Since he was not assigned to regular duties for the purpose of studying the arts, he was ordered to submit fees for maintenance as a reserve retainer.
　　However, he was placed in Miyake Hannosuke's unit.
- In the 9th month of Kyōwa 1 (Year of the Rooster), he participated in bird hunting, and thereafter participated five times total, receiving prey as a gift on one occasion.

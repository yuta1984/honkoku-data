- In the 7th month of the 9th year (Year of the Snake), as requested, he was ordered to resume active duty and was assigned to an external group, entering Saigō Bunzō's unit to serve castle guard duty.

- In the 11th month of the same year, since his adopted son Gengo had become ill and had been returned to his original family as previously petitioned, his actual son Magoichirō had now grown up, so he petitioned to make him his heir.

- In the 3rd month of the 11th year (Year of the Sheep), he petitioned to change his name to Kansuke.

- In the 3rd month of the 12th year (Year of the Monkey), he was granted retirement as requested.

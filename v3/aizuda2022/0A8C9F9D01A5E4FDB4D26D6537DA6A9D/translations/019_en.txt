When Lord Kyōtei made his journey down to the domain, he was appointed to serve as escort guard duty during the journey and accompanied him down.
1. In the same year, 6th month: It was announced that he had served without incident as an escort during the journey.
1. In the same 5th year, Monkey year, 3rd month: Due to group leader Tanaka Kahyōe's role change, he was transferred to the successor Kasuga Ninzaemon's group.
1. In the same year, 4th month: For the pilgrimage to Nikko Shrine, he was appointed as temporary fire brigade duty at the upper mansion and served after relocating.
1. In the same month: It was announced that he had served the fire brigade patrol duty without incident during the shrine pilgrimage.

1. In the same year, 3rd month:
For the funeral procession of Lady Kōkyō-in, he was appointed to road security duty and served in that capacity.
1. In the same 9th year, Rat year, 9th month: He was appointed to regular escort guard duty.
1. In Tenmei 1st year, Ox year, 4th month: It was announced that since last autumn he had served diligently with perfect attendance.
　Note: From this time forward, he was praised each time for his perfect attendance.
1. In the same year, 5th month: He was appointed to serve in the broad hall guard unit and served in that capacity.
　Note: From this time forward, he was frequently appointed to and served in this role.

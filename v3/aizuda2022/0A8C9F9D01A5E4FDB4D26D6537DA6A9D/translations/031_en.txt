1. In the same year, 9th month: He brought his retainers and went down to Aizu.

1. In the same month: Regarding serving as Lord Sadaaki's page, he petitioned to serve guard duty at the temporary shrine as an expression of gratitude, whereupon he was appointed to serve as a hired page until the first anniversary and performed these duties.

1. In the same year, 12th month: The hired pages at the temporary shrine had been appointed to guard duty until the first anniversary, but since there would be a ceremonial transfer to the Mineyama Toyooka Shrine the following spring, he petitioned to continue serving as before until that time. He was ordered to continue serving as before until the shrine transfer and served until the 3rd month of the following rabbit year.

1. In the same 4th rabbit year, from the 4th month: He served guard duty at the castle.

1. In the same year, 6th month: After Lord Sadaaki's departure from Edo, he diligently served continuous guard duty at the temporary shrine, and it was announced that he had served the temporary shrine guard duty with great dedication not only here but elsewhere as well.

1. In the same month: It was announced that he would be appointed as a page.

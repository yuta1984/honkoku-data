1. In the same 18th year, Ox year (1729), 4th month, when his younger brother Nuinodono died of illness, managing the residence alone became too much for him, so he had petitioned for an exchange to a suitable residence. He was granted a transfer to Sakamoto Goemon's upper residence

1. In the same year, 10th month, when group leader Yamazaki Sasuke died of illness, he was assigned to Numasawa Gonbee's group as his successor role

1. In the same 20th year, Rabbit year (1731), 4th month, he went up to Edo for guard duty service and returned in the same year, 10th month

1. In Genbun 4th year, Sheep year (1739), 10th month, as petitioned, his son Sanai was appointed as his substitute for guard duty

1. On the 16th day of the 1st month of Enkyō 1st year, Rat year (1744), he died of illness at age 49. His posthumous Buddhist name was Zenso-in Jitsuyo Jōton Koji, and he was buried at Ganjo-ju Temple on Mt. Kano

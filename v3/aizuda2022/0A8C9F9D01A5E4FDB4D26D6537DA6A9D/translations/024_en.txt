1. In the same year, 12th month: Because he worked diligently as assistant inspector, Lord Sadaaki granted him one bundle of cotton.

1. In the same 10th year, Horse year, 6th month: Due to Lord Ryōtoku-in's excursions, he was appointed to serve as both front and rear escort, and was also ordered to concurrently serve as head escort. For a total of three years continuously, he served as escort on every occasion of His Lordship's outings.

1. In Kyōwa 2nd year, Dog year, 10th month: From the previous Sheep year until the current 9th month, he served completely for three years in both the temporary inspector role and assistant duties during the lord's residence in the domain, and for this perfect attendance, he was granted two silver coins as a reward.

1. In the same month: He was appointed to the temporary inspector role.

1. In the same 3rd year, Boar year, 11th month: During the period of administrative reduction, his service as announcer was suspended, and he was appointed as assistant for reception duties in the great hall. He was ordered to serve when Tatsuno Yoemon stepped down, and specifically to serve as escort about twice per month.

1. In Bunka 1st year, Rat year, 12th month: Because he worked diligently in his ceremonial garment duties, Lord Sadaaki granted him two bundles of cotton.

1. In the same month: Because he worked diligently as assistant for reception duties in the great hall, he was granted two silver coins as a reward.

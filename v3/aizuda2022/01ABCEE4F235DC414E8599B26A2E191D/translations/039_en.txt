Rokurōbei Mitsukuni 《Marginal note: First name Kumekichi, also Kume, also Matsunosuke
Born on the 8th day of the 11th month of Kansei 3, I year》
　Biological father: Eldest son of Ibuka Sadakura Shigewa
　　　　Second adopted son of Ibuka Shichinouemon Shigemoto
　Wife: Daughter of adoptive father Moku Mitsuyoshi 《Marginal note: Born on the 2nd day of the 2nd month of Kansei 3, I year》

1. Bunka 6, Mi year (1809), 6th month: As requested by his father, he was granted his first audience [with the lord].
1. Same [era] 14th year, Ushi year (1817), 8th month: When he accompanied his father as an attendant during the bird hunt, there was a shortage in the fixed number of twenty-five mounted warriors, so he was added from among the attendants and was appointed to participate in both foot and mounted hunting.
　　However, he received game as a reward.
1. Same year, 11th month: When he accompanied as an attendant during the current bird hunt, he was ranked among the mounted warriors and participated in both foot and mounted hunting, performing his duties diligently and without delay, for which he received praise and commendation.

1. Bunpō 1, Tora year (1818), 7th month: He changed his name to Rokurōbei.

One: In the 9th month of Kan'en 1, Dragon year, he was granted the residence site of Seizaemon of the same surname.

One: In the 9th month of Kan'en 3, Horse year, he was scheduled to go up for guard duty, but since Hara Gen'hachi was appointed as permanent construction magistrate, he was ordered to continue serving until (Hara Gen'hachi) went up, and he returned in the 5th month of the same year.
Note: For his diligent service during the military campaign, he was granted one bolt of bleached cloth in place of formal court dress.

One: In the 12th month of the same year, his request to adopt Budayū, the third son of Seizaemon of the same surname, was granted.

One: In the 1st month of Hōreki 2, Monkey year, for his diligent service in duties for the domain inspectors, he was granted one bolt of bleached cloth in place of formal court dress.

One: In the 10th month of Hōreki 3, Rooster year, regarding the roof replacement of the shrine at Mount Mine, he repeatedly went to that place and gave excellent direction, and it was proclaimed that he accomplished the construction project courageously while reducing expenses.

One: In the 5th month of Hōreki 4, Dog year, he was appointed to serve in charge of construction work for the mausoleum at Innai.

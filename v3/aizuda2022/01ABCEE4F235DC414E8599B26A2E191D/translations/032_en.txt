1. Same 3rd year, Tora (1806), 8th month: He requested and was granted permission to adopt Shichirōzaemon, son of Nakasho who was the adopted son of Kimoto Samon, as his son-in-law and heir.

1. Same year, 9th month: Regarding the matter of his petition to transfer bamboo wholesale business shares, although his conduct showed lack of proper understanding and there were grounds for strict punishment, considering his advanced age, he was pardoned and relieved of both his main duties and concurrent posts.

He was appointed to the outer retainer group and entered Jimbo Kuranosukeʼs unit, but did not report for duty due to illness.

1. Same year, 12th month: As requested, he was granted permission to retire.

1. Same 6th year, Mi (1809), 9th month, 16th day: He died of illness at the age of eighty-three.

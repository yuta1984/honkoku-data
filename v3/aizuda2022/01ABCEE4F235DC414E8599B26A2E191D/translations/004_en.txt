Genealogy of Ibuka Rokurōbei 【Surname: Minamoto, Home Province: Shinano】【Family crest: Circular idzutsu (well curb), Alternative crests: Idzutsu with two horizontal lines, the character "i" (well), Ishuku, Idzutsu-kuzushi】

Second son of Ibuka Mozaemon Shigeyoshi
　Ibuka Shirōbei Mitsumasa　Born on the 26th day of the 7th month of Keichō 11 (1606), the Year of the Horse
　　Mother: daughter of Sokura Heisuke [family name unknown]
　　Wife: daughter of Ueshima Monbei Tsugitoshi, named Kuni, born on the 29th day of the 4th month of Keichō 17 (1612), the Year of the Rat, died of illness on the 17th day of the 3rd month of Shōhō 1 (1644), the Year of the Dog, at age 33, posthumous Buddhist name: Genshin-in-den Kaseimyōkō-daishi, burial place: Shōun-zan Zenryūji Temple (originally at Zenryūji in Hanabatake, later reburied at the current location)
　　
　　Second wife: daughter of Gyōda Matazaemon Mitsunao 【named Ichi, after taking Buddhist vows called Myōshin】, died of illness on the 16th day of the 7th month of Genroku 16 (1703), the Year of the Sheep, posthumous Buddhist name: Meiju-in-den Ichisō-myōshin-daishi, burial place: same as above

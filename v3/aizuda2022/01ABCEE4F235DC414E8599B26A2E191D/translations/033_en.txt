Wife 《Marginal note: Initially her given name was Naka》 Adopted daughter of Onoda Sukeemon Naomasa 《Marginal note: Born on the 3rd day of the 10th month of Hōreki 13, Hitsuji year》
Actually: Daughter of Suzuki Sakuemon Shigewa

1. Meiwa 1, Saru year (1764), 3rd month: As requested by his father, he was granted his first audience with the lord.

1. Same 3, Inu year (1766), 2nd month: As requested by his father, he was granted permission to change his name to Nuinosuke.

1. Same 4, I year (1767), 9th month: His father's conduct in service was deemed improper and inappropriate, and he was banned from Aizu castle town.

1. Same 8, U year (1771), 9th month: The ban from the castle town was lifted and he was pardoned.

1. Anei 2, Mi year (1773), 12th month: Out of compassion, he was granted a stipend of five-person support.

1. Same 8, I year (1779), 1st month: He was granted permission to change his name to Samon.

1. Tenmei 5, Mi year (1785), 1st month: He was appointed as a samurai outside the regular units (kumi-gai no shi).

1. Same year, 2nd month: As requested, he was granted an audience with the lord.

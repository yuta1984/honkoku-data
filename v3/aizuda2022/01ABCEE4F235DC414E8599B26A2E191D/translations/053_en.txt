• Same year, 12th month: During the bird hunting expedition, the formation was well-aligned with proper spacing, unit coordination was executed, operations proceeded smoothly, and the entire group received praise from the lord.
　　Note: Subsequently received such praise on every occasion.

• Same year, 12th month of the Monkey year: Requested and was granted permission to change his name to Shurinosuke.

• Bunka 5, 1st month (1808): Due to official duties for the defense of Ezo (Hokkaido), he and his companions were granted audience, received sake cups at the small study, and were served sake at the large study.

• Same month: Departed with companions for official duties related to Ezo defense, arrived at Soya in Ezo in the 5th month, completed the duties in the 11th month, and upon returning were granted audience while still in travel clothes.
　　Note: He served as supervisor of pack animals during travel, and served as acting inspector during the return journey.

• Same year, 12th month: Having departed for official duties related to Ezo defense, he worked diligently for several days in that land, beginning with sea and land transport, completed all duties without incident, and upon the complete return of all personnel, the entire group was served a meal at the castle.

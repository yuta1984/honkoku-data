1. Same month: Although the rough survey work had been decided upon, since he was ordered to remain on duty until around the 11th month, he was instructed to conduct examinations without relaxing his vigilance, and furthermore, to report any ideas regarding cost-saving measures.

1. Same year, 10th month: He was ordered to take the lead in managing the construction work at the Shiba residence.

1. From the same month until the 10th month: He was ordered to remain on duty continuously. Without involvement in borrowing matters, he was entrusted with coordinating current expenses and Aizu operations, functioning like a chief accountant. He was ordered to engage in thorough discussions on all matters and work jointly with others under joint signatures.

1. Same year, intercalary 11th month: Regarding this survey matter, he had planned and managed it from the beginning, and there were considerable reductions achieved in various areas. Since examination of conducting future operations based on these standards was of primary importance, he was ordered to take sole responsibility for the survey department.
   However, while he had been informed that he would be entrusted with investigating construction materials for the Shiba residence, it was announced that such appointment was unnecessary.

1. Same year, 12th month: He diligently served in both duties, and regarding his concurrent official duties, he went up to Edo during last winter for survey work only...

1. In the same 12th year (Genroku 12, 1699), Year of the Rabbit, 3rd month: Went up to Edo for guard duty and returned in the 9th month of the same year

1. In the same 13th year (Genroku 13, 1700), Year of the Dragon, 9th month: Went up to Edo for guard duty and returned in the 3rd month of the following Year of the Snake

1. In the same 15th year (Genroku 15, 1702), Year of the Horse, 10th month: Went up to Edo for guard duty and returned in the 6th month of the following Year of the Sheep as part of a lord's retinue

1. In the same 16th year (Genroku 16, 1703), Year of the Sheep, 9th month: Was appointed to the position of personal archery commander (omochiyumigashira)

1. In Hōei 1 (1704), Year of the Monkey, 9th month: Went up to Edo for guard duty and returned in the 3rd month of the following Year of the Rooster

1. In the same 2nd year (Hōei 2, 1705), Year of the Rooster, 10th month: Petitioned to adopt Gonnosuke, the second son of Kimoto Kurando, as an adopted son-in-law, and the request was granted

1. In the same 4th year (Hōei 4, 1707), Year of the Boar, 9th month: Went up to Edo for guard duty and returned in the 3rd month of the following Year of the Rat

1. In the same 6th year (Hōei 6, 1709), Year of the Ox, 4th month: Since his colleague Moriyama Naozaemon died of illness in Edo, went up for emergency guard duty and returned in the 9th month of the same year

1. In the same 7th year (Hōei 7, 1710), Year of the Tiger, 4th month: Due to an invitation from the Senior Councilors, went up for that official business and returned in the 5th month of the same year

1. In Shōtoku 2 (1712), Year of the Dragon, 2nd month: Was directly granted an increase of 100 koku in official stipend

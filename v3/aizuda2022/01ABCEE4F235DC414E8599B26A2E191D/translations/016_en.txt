1. In Hōreki 2 (1752), Year of the Rooster, 12th month: Was granted his first audience with the lord as requested by his father, and was permitted to change his name to Shichirōzaemon as requested

1. In Kyōhō 8 (1723), Year of the Rabbit, 8th month: Was permitted to change his name to Ko-Hayato as requested by his father

1. In the same 11th year (Kyōhō 11, 1726), Year of the Horse, 5th month: Inherited the family headship and was granted a domain stipend of 400 koku without change, was appointed to the tozama group and entered Sugita Gorōbei's unit, serving castle guard duty

1. In the same year, 12th month: Was granted the vacant residence of Sakai Saibei in Yonedai 2-no-chō

1. In the same 13th year (Kyōhō 13, 1728), Year of the Monkey, 1st month: Was ordered to proceed to Edo urgently in connection with the lord's shrine visit

1. In the same month: Was appointed as temple and shrine town magistrate
　　However, moved to the official residence in the 2nd month

1. In the same 16th year (Kyōhō 16, 1731), Year of the Boar, 6th month: Due to diligent service, was granted the rank of jō-kamishimo (formal court dress privilege)

1. In the same 18th year (Kyōhō 18, 1733), Year of the Ox, 1st month: Was ordered to proceed to Edo to assist with the moat dredging construction project under orders from above, with specific duties to be assigned later upon arrival in Edo

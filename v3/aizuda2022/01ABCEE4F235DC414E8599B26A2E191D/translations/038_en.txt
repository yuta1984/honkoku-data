Chūsho Mitsukazu 《Marginal note: First name Toramatsu
》Divorced
　Biological father: Second son of Kurokawachi Jūdayū Ki
　　　　Second adopted son of Kimoto Kurōzaemon Nariyoshi
　Wife: Daughter of adoptive father Moku Mitsuyoshi

1. Bunka 4, U year (1807), 2nd month: He was appointed to handle the simplification of the literary and martial arts school, and due to staff reduction, he was relieved of his sword technique supervision duties. As a reward for his diligent service thus far, he was given one bundle of cotton.
1. Same month: As requested by his father, he had his first audience [with the lord].

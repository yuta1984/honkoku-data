Daughter of Nomura Zentayū Akitoshi《marginal note: died of illness on the 25th day of the 2nd month of Bunka 7, horse year, posthumous Buddhist name Chihō Jōkō Daishi, burial site same as above》
Daughter of Nagai Tamiya Michihiro

First, in the 4th month of Meiwa 4, boar year (April 1767): Due to his young age, his father's fief of 150 koku was confiscated, he was granted a stipend for seven retainers, and was appointed as a masterless samurai.

First, in the 11th month of An'ei 9, rat year (November 1780): He was granted the former residence of Mizushima Heijirō.

First, in Kansei 3, boar year《marginal note: month unclear》(1791): He was appointed as supervisor of Dōsetsu school archery.

First, in the 3rd month of the same 6th year, tiger year (March 1794): When Lord Sadaaki practiced archery, he was appointed as sparring partner and served together with Higuchi Nakaemon.

First, in the 12th month of the same year (December 1794): He was appointed to the Shinban guard unit.
　However, because he served as supervisor of archery, he was exempted from guard duty.

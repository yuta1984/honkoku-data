1. In the 12th month of Jōkyō 2 (1685), year of the ox, due to his young age,
   his deceased father's stipend of 100 koku was recalled and he was granted
   a stipend of 10 nin-fuchi instead

1. In the 8th month of the same 4th year (1687), year of the rabbit, he was
   appointed as gokoshō (page)

1. In the 2nd month of Genroku 1 (1688), year of the dragon, he went up to
   Edo for guard duty

1. In the 7th month of the same year, at the time of the lord's entry into the domain,
   he returned home as rear guard

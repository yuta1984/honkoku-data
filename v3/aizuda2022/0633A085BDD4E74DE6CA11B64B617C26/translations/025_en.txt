Naoji
　Naoji, nephew of Hirayama Kinbei, in the 16th year of Kyōhō (year of the boar), 6th month, petitioned to become an adopted son of the Machida Hikodayū family and was granted permission. This is recorded in the official records, but although there is no tradition from the main family line, it is recorded here for reference.

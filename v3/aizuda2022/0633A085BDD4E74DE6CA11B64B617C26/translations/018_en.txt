He accompanied [the lord] during the entry into Aizu domain, and afterwards was granted a stipend of 10 koku 8 to as a regular retainer, though the exact date is unknown.

However, on February 6th, 5th year of Enpō, carpenter Hirayama Kinbei was granted 2 bu in gold as a reward for his hard work on the Sannomaru construction project the previous year, as recorded in the official records, though there is no family tradition [recording this].

Item: On September 8th, 8th year of Genroku, year of the Boar, he died of illness at age 71. Buddhist name: Monhōin Shaku Jikō Zen'ei Koji; buried at: Hiryūzan Honkōji Temple

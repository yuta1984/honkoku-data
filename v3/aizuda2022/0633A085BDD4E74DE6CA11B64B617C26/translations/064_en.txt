Chōbei (certain person)
　　According to official records, on the 6th day of the 2nd month of Manji 3 (1660), 
　　Chōbei, son of Hirade Sōemon, was summoned to serve as regular duty ashigaru (foot soldier).
　　However, there is no person named Chōbei in the family records, so this may have been 
　　the initial name of Kyūbei Yoshinori, though details are unclear.

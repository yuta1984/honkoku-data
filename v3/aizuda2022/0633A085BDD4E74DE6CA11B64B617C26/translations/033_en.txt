　Actually the daughter of his younger brother Shinzō Morinao
First, twelfth month of Bunka 2 (1805), year of the Ox: From his grandfather's provisional assignment,
　three koku were reduced and he was granted eight koku with support for three people, summoned to the new
　guard unit. For the cultivation of martial arts, while not being
　assigned various duties, he was ordered to
　submit fees for minor construction work.
First, fifth month of Bunka 3 (1806), year of the Tiger: His request to change his name to
　Kakuji was granted.
First, eighth month of Bunka 8 (1811), year of the Sheep: He participated in a hunting expedition and
　received game as a reward.
　　However, after this he participated once more and achieved first place in hunting,
　　for which he received five mon in silver coins at the hunting ground.

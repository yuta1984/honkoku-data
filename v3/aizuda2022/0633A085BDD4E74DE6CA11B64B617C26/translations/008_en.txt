Regarding being ordered to learn [his duties]

1. In the 2nd month of Tenmei 1 (1781), year of the Ox, one koku was reduced from his deceased father's stipend, and he was granted 4 koku 5 to and provisions for 2 people, and was employed as a temporary worker in the Construction Office.
   However, on the same day, he was employed temporarily at the Legal Affairs Office document section and was ordered to learn current duties as well.

1. In the 12th month of Tenmei 3 (1783), year of the Rabbit, he was appointed as a regular employed scribe at the Legal Affairs Office.

1. In the 2nd month of Tenmei 5 (1785), year of the Snake, he was appointed as a scribe at the Legal Affairs Office and granted 4 koku 5 to as official salary.

1. In the 3rd month of Tenmei 6 (1786), year of the Horse, there were over 60 cases of investigation carried over from two years prior, and he completed the records for over 40 cases during the previous year with diligence, so he was granted 1 bu of gold as a reward.

1. In the 12th month of Tenmei 7 (1787), year of the Sheep, he completed over 90 investigations since the previous spring, and for his diligence and hard work, he was granted 1 ryō (1 bu) of gold as a reward.

1. In the 3rd month of Kansei 2 (1790), year of the Dog, he completed over 80 investigations from two years prior, and for his diligence and hard work, he was granted 5 ryō of silver as a reward.

1. In the 8th month of the same 12th year (1699), year of the rabbit, at Tsugawa
   when the lord made an inspection tour, he was appointed by personal designation
   to serve as escort and fulfilled this duty

1. In the 5th month of the same 13th year (1700), year of the dragon,
   at the time of sankin he served as escort on the return journey

1. In the 9th month of the same 14th year (1701), year of the snake, he was
   appointed to Edo guard duty

1. In the 2nd month of Hōei 1st year (1704), year of the monkey, due to private financial
   difficulties, he petitioned to be given some appointment,
   whereupon he was granted leave from Edo service and
   appointed to serve as Aizu escort guard

1. In the 7th month of the same 2nd year (1705), year of the rooster, his father Hachiemon, being
   a person of long-standing merit, had his provisional fief
   converted to a direct grant of 100 koku and was appointed to permanent Edo residence

1. In the 10th month of the same year, he moved and went up to reside
   at the Shiba mansion

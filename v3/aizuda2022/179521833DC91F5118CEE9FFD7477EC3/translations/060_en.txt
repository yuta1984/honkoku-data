One　10th month of the same year: It was proclaimed that he had served in military camp without negligence

One　4th month of the 13th year, Rat year: When His Lordship went up to Kyoto, he was ordered to serve as escort and accompanied him on both the outbound and return journeys

One　10th month of the same year: He went up to Edo for guard duty service, and in the 5th month of the following Ox year when His Lordship returned from the capital, he served as escort and came down with him

One　5th month of the 14th year, Ox year: It was proclaimed that he had served in military camp without negligence

One　6th month of the same year: During the current return journey from the capital, although the number of attendants and horses for the journey had been reduced, it was proclaimed that he had served with particular diligence without any absences

One　10th month of the same year: As he had earnestly applied himself to military drill with excellence, he was granted a new spear as a reward

One　6th month of the 1st year of Bunsei era, Tiger year: He was appointed as school inspector

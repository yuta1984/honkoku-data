Daughter of Ōsawa Jinbei Naonori　Divorced

　　　Daughter of Miyake Denzaemon Naomoto　Born in Tenmei 4th year (1784)

　One　12th month of Kansei 1st year (1789): Served diligently as assistant head of the academy (shogaku-ryō shichō), handled group affairs well, and was deemed remarkable for his young age, as proclaimed by his lord

　One　3rd month of Kansei 2nd year (1790): Was granted his first formal audience as requested by his father

　One　12th month of Kansei 3rd year (1791): Since the re-establishment of the school, he pursued literary and martial arts with deep commitment and without negligence, so he was awarded two silver pieces as a reward

　One　7th month of Kansei 5th year (1793): Was granted the regular stipend of five ryō of gold and provisions for three people, and was summoned to serve as a regular attendant page to Lord Sadaaki

　One　11th month of the same year: His request to change his name to Gen'nosuke was granted

　One　3rd month of Kansei 6th year (1794): Served diligently during a time of staff shortage

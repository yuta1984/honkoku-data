1. Same 18th year, year of the Ox, 1st month: The head Kayano Gonbee
　　　was relieved of his duties upon his request, and as his successor
　　　he joined Ohara Goroemon's unit

1. Same year, 4th month: Five koku from his late father's stipend
　　　were reduced, and he was granted twenty koku and provisions for four people
　　　and summoned to service

1. Genbun 1, year of the Dragon, 3rd month: The head Ohara Goro-
　　　emon was granted retirement upon his request, and as his successor
　　　he joined Hyuga Saburoemon's unit

1. Kanpō 3, year of the Boar, 3rd month: He was granted
　　　an increase of five koku and appointed to escort duty

1. Enkyō 3, year of the Tiger, 4th month: He went up to Edo

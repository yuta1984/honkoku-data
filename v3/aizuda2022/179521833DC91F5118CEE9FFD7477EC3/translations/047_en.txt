Adopted son
　Tamon Hideyasu　　《Marginal note: Originally named Tatsuzō｜Born on August 15th, Enkyō 1st year (1744)》

　　Actually the third son of Sōma Shirōzaemon Shigetsune

　　Wife: Adopted daughter of his adoptive father Gen'nosuke Tsuneyuki　《Marginal note: Born on January｜9th, Hōreki 7th year (1757)》

1. Meiwai 9th year (1772), 2nd month: As requested by his father, he was granted his first audience with his lord

1. Same year, 5th month: He received a hereditary stipend of 100 koku without error, was appointed to the gaisama group, joined Tanaka Kahei's unit, and served castle guard duty

1. An'ei 5th year (1776), 3rd month: When the head Tanaka Kahei was appointed to a magistrate position, he was reassigned to Kasuga Wa《crossed out: □｜Izumi》group as his successor role

1. Tenmei 2nd year (1782), 10th month: He was commended for his diligent service in castle guard duty, maintaining perfect attendance throughout his unit rotation, which was deemed particularly meritorious

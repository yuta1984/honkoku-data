- In the 2nd month of the same year:
　　　While the lord was staying at the castle, it was announced that pages should serve in rotation

- In the same month:
　　　He served during Lord Kinbun's bathing

- In the 3rd month of the same year: For this occasion of visiting Edo, by the lord's gracious will, four pages were left behind and ordered to guard the temporary palace, as announced
　　　　However, regarding this matter, during the visit to Edo in the 4th month of the same year,
　　　　he was left here on duty to guard the temporary palace,

　　　　serving until the 10th month of the same year when a replacement came down from Edo,
　　　　whereupon he went up to Edo for duty rotation

- In the same month:
　　　When Lord Kinbun moved to the temporary palace at Sannomaru, he served as attendant and immediately began guard duty

- In the same month:
　　　When Lord Kinbun was placed in the main coffin, he served in that capacity

- In the same month: During the funeral procession, being on duty,
　　　he served in the lord's retinue

- In the 11th month of the same year: Since all the pages served diligently during busy times and worked exceptionally hard, it was announced through a retainer that three ryō and two bu of gold each would be granted

- In the 1st month of Bunsei 5, Year of the Horse (1822): For his devoted attendance and exceptional service during the lord's illness, by the lord's gracious will, he was granted one tan of hakama fabric and a folding fan in the lord's bedchamber

- In the same month: For the same reason as above, by the lord's gracious will, he was granted three pieces of silver and two bundles of cotton from what was available in the lord's private treasury, receiving them in the lord's bedchamber

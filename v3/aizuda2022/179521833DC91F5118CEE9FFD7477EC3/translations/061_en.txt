However, he was ordered to serve concurrently as school administrator while also handling the teaching hall and martial arts instruction duties

One　8th month of the 2nd year, Rabbit year: During the hunting expedition, he was ordered to serve as inspector and went out

One　10th month of the same year: As the arrangements were changed this time, he was relieved of his current duties and given a sinecure position without specific role, and was appointed to serve as equipment officer for His Lordship. It was proclaimed that he had served with diligence up to this point

One　7th month of the 3rd year, Dragon year: He was appointed to school administrative duties while maintaining his sinecure position

One　5th month of the 6th year, Sheep year: He was appointed as assistant school magistrate while maintaining his sinecure position

One　12th month of Kansei 4, Rat year: It was proclaimed that he had attended school continuously for five years without absence and showed diligence

One　12th month of Kansei 7, Rabbit year: Having attended school and shown diligence for eight years, he was granted one piece of silver

One　12th month of Kansei 11, Sheep year: Having attended school and sword practice without negligence for thirteen years without absence and showing diligence, he was granted one piece of silver as a reward

One　12th month of Kyōwa 2, Dog year: Having attended school and the martial arts academy without negligence and without absence, showing diligence, he was granted one piece of silver and one bundle of cotton

One　2nd month of Kyōwa 3, Boar year: He was appointed to archery supervisor

One　12th month of Bunka 6, Snake year: As requested, he was relieved of his archery supervisor position

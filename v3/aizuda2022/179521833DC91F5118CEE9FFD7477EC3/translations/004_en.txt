Nakazawa Shuzen Genealogy　Surname: Minamoto clan, Takeda lineage, home province: Kai　Family crest: sword plum blossom within circle, alternate crest: five stars within sword-split diamond, ceremonial crest: sword plum blossom

Fifth son of Sakai Shin'emon Chikatsugu
Nakazawa Jin'zaemon Sukemitsu　Initially called Sakai Hachiya Chikafusa, also Kazuma, also Yososuke'emon. Born in Keicho 17, year of the rat, at Takato in Shinano Province

　Mother: daughter of the Suganuma clan

　Wife: daughter of Hirokawa Kiemon Toshinao　Died of illness on April 10, Genroku 14, year of the snake. Posthumous Buddhist name: Kakusho Mushin-nyo. Burial place: Taihozan Kenfuku-ji Temple

　In the 11th month of Kanei 15, year of the tiger, at Mogami in Dewa Province, granted a stipend of 40 koku of rice and provisions for two retainers, appointed to minor guard duty

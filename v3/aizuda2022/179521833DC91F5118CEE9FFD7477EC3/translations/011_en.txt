1. In the 9th month of Kyōhō 12th year, year of the sheep, he was granted his first audience with the lord as requested by his father.

1. In the 9th month of Enkyō 4th year, year of the rabbit, he was appointed as guard captain (goban-dai) as requested by his father, and served castle guard duty in Miyake Magobei's unit.

1. In the 7th month of Kan'en 1st year, year of the dragon, he was granted the hereditary stipend of 200 koku without change.

1. In the 12th month of Kan'en 2nd year, year of the snake, during the peasants' petition protest, he served guard duty at Nanoka-machi gate and other fortress gates for seven days.

1. In the 10th month of Kan'en 3rd year, year of the horse, he went up for guard duty and returned in the 4th month of the following year of the sheep.
　　　　However, during the above campaign, when the succession ceremony was conducted, he served as temporary envoy and was commended for performing his duties with great dedication.

1. In the 8th month of Hōreki 1st year, year of the sheep, his superior Miyake Magobei was appointed as wakadoshiyori (junior councilor), and he was transferred to Yanase Kyūma's unit as his successor.

1. In the 2nd month of Hōreki 12th year, year of the horse, his superior Yanase Kyūma was appointed as wakadoshiyori, and he was transferred to Yamazaki Sasuke's unit as his successor.

1. In the 1st month of Hōreki 14th year, year of the monkey, his request to change his name to Genjūrō was granted.

Wife: Daughter of adoptive father Jinuemon's deputy　《Born in Manji 2nd year, year of the snake, died of illness in Kyōhō 12th year, year of the sheep｜12th month, 2nd day at age 69, posthumous Buddhist name｜Shingyoku Chien Shinnyo, buried at same location》

　一　Enpō 5th year, year of the snake, 3rd month, inherited family stipend of 200 koku "without change"
　　　and was assigned to the outer retainer group, joined Shinoda Shichirōbei's unit
　　　and served castle guard duty
　一　Same 8th year, year of the monkey, 3rd month
　　　When Lord Tokuō made his first formal visit to Edo, served as
　　　escort guard and went up in the same 10th month, returned
　一 　Genroku 6th year, year of the rooster, 5th month, appointed
　　　to concurrent duty as district administrator

　一　Same 13th year, year of the dragon, 8th month, due to illness, as requested
　　　was relieved of concurrent duties
　一　Same 14th year, year of the snake, 10th month, as requested, his son Takurokuró was appointed
　　　as guard substitute
　一　Same 16th year, 3rd month, 27th day, died of illness at age 46,
　　　posthumous Buddhist name Tokushitsu Genryū Koji, buried at same location

　Daughter　　　Wife of adopted son Sōtasuke Tsunenori

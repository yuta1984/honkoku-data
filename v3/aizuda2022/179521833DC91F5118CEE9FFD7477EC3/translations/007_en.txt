Wife: Daughter of adoptive father Jinuemon Fukumitsu 《Born in Manji 2nd year, year of the snake, died of illness on the 2nd day of 12th month, Kyōhō 12th year, year of the sheep, at age 69, posthumous Buddhist name Shintama Chien Shinjo, buried at the same place》

　一　Enpō 5th year, year of the snake, 3rd month, inherited fief of 200 koku 【verified correct】 and
　　　was appointed to the outer vassals group, entered
　　　Shinoda Shirōbei's unit, and served castle guard duty
　一　Same 8th year, year of the monkey, 3rd month
　　　When Lord Tokuō made his first official visit to Edo, served as
　　　escort and went up for guard duty, returned in the same 10th month
　一 　Genroku 6th year, year of the rooster, 5th month, appointed to concurrent duty as
　　　district deputy

　一　Same 13th year, year of the dragon, 8th month, due to illness, as requested,
　　　relieved of concurrent duties
　一　Same 14th year, year of the snake, 10th month, as requested, his son Takerokurō was

　一　Same 16th year, 3rd month, 27th day, died of illness at age 46,

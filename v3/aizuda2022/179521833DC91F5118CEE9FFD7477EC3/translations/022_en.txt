- In the 2nd year of Kyōwa (1802, Year of the Dog), 8th month: As he did not neglect his studies and worked diligently, and his skills were recognized as excellent for his age, he was granted a copy of the Saiden (Zuo Zhuan) as a reward.

- In the 1st year of Bunka (1804, Year of the Rat), 2nd month: As requested by his father, he was granted his first formal audience with the lord.

- In the 5th year of Bunka (1808, Year of the Dragon), 7th month: He was appointed as a recitation teacher at Inawashiro Academy.

- In the same year, 8th month: He inherited the family stipend of 200 koku without reduction, was appointed to the gaiyo-gumi (external group), joined Hyūga Saburōemon's unit, and served castle guard duty.

- In the same year, 10th month: He was appointed as a page (okoshō).

- In the same month: Although he had been appointed as a recitation teacher at Inawashiro Academy, since he moved here, it was announced that he need not continue that duty.

- In the same year, 12th month: When the lord resided at the castle workshop quarters, as he diligently served the lord's needs, the lord granted him one bundle of cotton and two bundles of tissue paper through a retainer.
  Note: He continued to receive the same rewards for the same service thereafter.

- In the 6th year of Bunka (1809, Year of the Snake), 5th month: As there was an accumulation of used clothing items, by the lord's gracious consideration, he received one set of used hemp formal wear with stripes...

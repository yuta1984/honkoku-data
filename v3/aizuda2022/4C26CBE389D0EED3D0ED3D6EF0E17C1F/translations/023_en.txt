　　However, thereafter he served many times and received praise each time

One: Same 8th year, year of the boar, second month, appointed as assistant envoy to Kyoto and traveled to the capital

One: Tenmei 2nd year, year of the tiger, tenth month, appointed to serve as apprentice in the ceremonial costume department

One: Same year, twelfth month, from the New Year ceremonies of the following year until the fourth month, appointed as temporary officer of the ceremonial costume department during ceremonial occasions

One: Same 5th year, year of the snake, first month, having served diligently as duty officer during every absence [of the lord] for nine years, was granted one silver piece as a reward

One: Same 6th year, year of the horse, second month, ordered to serve as temporary commander at the Shiba estate, consulting with the regular fire brigade, patrolling inside and outside the estate frequently day and night, and in case of fires near the Ueno and Mita estates, to immediately gather troops and rush to the scene

One: Same 8th year, year of the monkey, among the tozama retainers with 250 koku or more, was reassigned from regular tozama retainer to tozama retainer attached to the senior councilor, [assigned to] the second unit

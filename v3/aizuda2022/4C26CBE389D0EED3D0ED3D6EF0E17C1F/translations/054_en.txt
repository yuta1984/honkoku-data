一Same 12th year (1727), 5th month:
　Due to the death of Shōkaku-in-sama, he was appointed to
　the tozama-gumi (outer retainer unit) and served castle
　guard duty in Hori Hanemon's unit
一Genbun 4 (1739), 8th month: He was appointed to the otomo-ban
　(lord's attendant guard) and granted 25 koku and a four-person stipend

一Kanpō 1 (1741), month unknown: For emergency guard duty
　he went up and accompanied the lord on his return journey down

一Enkyō 1 (1744), 3rd month: During the lord's visit to the capital
　he went up for the return journey and came down in the same 4th month
一Same 2nd year (1745), 7th month: He was appointed to
　the otsugi-ban (second guard unit)
一Same 5th year (1748), 4th month: During the lord's attendance at court
　he went up for attendant guard duty and returned in the same 10th month

- In the first month of the same 9th year, year of the dragon (Meiwa 9, 1772), he was granted the hereditary stipend of 150 koku without change

- Around the second month of An'ei 7, year of the dog (1778), due to Kayano Gonbei's retirement, he was transferred to county duty under Harada Ihei's unit

- Around the fourth month of An'ei 10, year of the ox (1781), due to Harada Ihei's death from illness, he was transferred to county duty under Takahashi Koemon's unit

- Around the ninth month of Tenmei 4, year of the dragon (1784), due to Takahashi Koemon's role change, he was transferred to various duties under Kayano Gonbei's unit

- Around the tenth month of Kansei 1, year of the rooster (1789), due to Kayano Gonbei's dismissal from his position, he was transferred to county duty under Ichinose Dengorō's unit

- Around the eleventh month of Kansei 2, year of the dog (1790), due to Ichinose Dengorō's dismissal from his position, he was transferred to successor duty under Sugita Gorobei's unit

- In the ninth month of Kansei 5, year of the ox (1793), since his son had recently died of illness, he petitioned to adopt Jōsuke, the younger brother of Sugita Gorobei, as his son-in-law and heir

- In the twelfth month of the same year, as petitioned, his son Jōsuke was appointed as guard substitute

- On the 15th day of the ninth month of Kansei 6, year of the tiger (1794), he died of illness at age sixty-one, posthumous Buddhist name Kōgan Ryōhin Koji, burial site same as above

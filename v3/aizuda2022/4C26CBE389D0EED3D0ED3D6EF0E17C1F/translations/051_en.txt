一Same 3rd year (1686), 10th month: He went up to Edo
　for guard duty, completed one term of service, and returned
一Genroku 1 (1688), 3rd month: He went up to Edo
　for guard duty, completed one term of service, and returned
一Same 2nd year (1689), 2nd month: He went up
　as an escort for the lord's return from sankin-kōtai
　　Note: He was appointed to arrange lodgings along the route as part of the lord's retinue
　　
一Same 3rd year (1690), 3rd month: He went up to Edo
　for guard duty, completed one term of service, and returned

一Same 5th year (1692), 4th month: He went up
　for escort guard duty
一Same year, 6th month: He was appointed
　as purchasing agent for Edo and Kyoto
　　Note: He served concurrently in his former position until the 9th month
　　
一Same 7th year (1694), 9th month, 25th day: He died of illness at age 37,
　posthumous Buddhist name: Hōjō Ren'in Hon'yo Kyūgen
　Koji, burial site: Gangōjōji Temple on Kanōyama

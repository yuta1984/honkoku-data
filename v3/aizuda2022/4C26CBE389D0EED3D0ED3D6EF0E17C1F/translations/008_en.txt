One: Same 5th year, year of the sheep, third month, as petitioned, served guard duty at the middle ceremonial platform (naka goshikidai) and other duties

One: Kyōhō 2, year of the rooster, seventh month, 5th day, died of illness at age 51, posthumous name Dōin Sekishitsu Ryōzan Koji, buried at Monryōin within Sengakuji Temple in Takanawa, Edo

　　　　　Initially Orinosuke, later Kināi, after retirement Jofū
Shingohyōe Yasusada
　　　　　Born in Genroku 16, year of the sheep

Mother: Daughter of the Furukawa family

　　Retainer of Lord Hori Shikibu-shōyū
Wife: Daughter of Kawashima Hikoemon

One: Shōtoku 4, year of the horse, seventh month, as his father petitioned, was summoned to serve as page (koshō) to Lord Shōsenin

One: Same year, eleventh month, petitioned to change his name to Kināi and was granted permission

One: Kyōhō 2, year of the rooster, ninth month, was granted a formal stipend of 100 koku without change, and was ordered to continue serving as page as before

One: Same 5th year, year of the rat, second month, as petitioned, was permitted to undergo formal coming-of-age ceremony (hon-genpuku)

Enshū [Marginal note: originally named Kyūsuke] - 11th abbot of Seiganjii Temple
Mother: same as above

Uheē Shigenobu [Marginal note: originally named Jibu]
Real father: fourth son of Yamakawa Jidayū Masutaka
Wife: daughter of Furuta Sakuemon Shigefusa [Marginal note: died of illness on the 2nd day of the 10th month of Bunka 5 (1808) at age 73, posthumous Buddhist name Shinshōin Seiyo Myōgon Daishi, burial place same as above]

One: In the 9th month of Hōreki 2 (1752), three koku were deducted from his late father's stipend allocation, he was granted 12 koku and provisions for 3 retainers, and was summoned to serve as Aizu Katte Koban (financial guard)

One: In the 12th month of the same year, his request to wear the crested garments bestowed upon his late father was granted

One: In the 2nd month of Hōreki 3 (1753), he was granted an increase of 3 koku and appointed to regular duty

One: In the 9th month of the same year, he was appointed as personal attendant to Lord Kansōin
Note: During this service, the crested haori with bracken-fern crest should be returned

【Written in red ink】Diary
One: In the 12th month of Meiwa 6 (1769), during recent times of pressing financial needs, he worked diligently on procuring rice and money from commoners, helping to overcome urgent financial difficulties, and for this he was awarded two bundles of cotton as a reward.

One: In the 11th month of An'ei 5 (1776), due to private financial difficulties, he worked diligently on emergency duties related to urgent procurement by the head office, and for this he was announced to receive two bundles of cotton as a reward.
One: In the 12th month of the same year, for his years of diligent service, he was appointed to the external retainer group and ordered to continue serving concurrently as deputy magistrate as before.
Note: He was placed in Ueda Hachirozaemon's group.

One: In the 2nd month of the same 8th year (1779), while working diligently on urgent fund collection, he labored exceptionally hard, and for this he was awarded two bundles of cotton as a reward.
One: In the 7th month of Tenmei 6 (1786), due to delaying the distribution of relief goods intended for impoverished commoners, he was stripped of his deputy magistrate position and dismissed from his group assignment.
One: In the 11th month of the same 7th year (1787), his court rank was set as next to Inawashiro retainers and above non-group retainers.
One: In the 3rd month of the same 8th year (1788), his court rank remained as before, but his role title became First Group Elder's Attendant Council Group.

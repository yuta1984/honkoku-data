Jinji Tsuneka (born on the 12th day of the 5th month of Kyōhō 5 (1720))
　Mother: Daughter of the Iwagami clan
　Wife: Adopted daughter of Misaka Gorozaemon Takamitsu - divorced
　Actual father: Daughter of Urano Hanbei Naotaka

　　　Daughter of Iida Hyōzaemon Shigeyō (born on the 15th day of the 2nd month of Kyōhō 16 (1731), died of illness on the 28th day of the 3rd month of Bunka 4 (1807) at the age of seventy-seven, Buddhist name Kōshōin Myōe Nichishō, burial site same as above)

- In the 5th month of Kyōhō 15 (1730)
　By the gracious will of Lord Tokuō,
　he was appointed as companion to Lord Doi Ōi-no-kami

- In the 1st month of Kyōhō 17 (1732), as requested by his father,
　he was granted his first formal audience

- In the 7th month of the same year, for appearing as companion,
　he was granted two ryō in gold

- In the 8th month of Kyōhō 18 (1733), due to his father's illness, he submitted a petition

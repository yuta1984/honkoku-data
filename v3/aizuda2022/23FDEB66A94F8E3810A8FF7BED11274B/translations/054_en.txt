Nahei Shigeyuki <<Born in Edo in the 1st year of Shōtoku (1711)>>
　Mother: Daughter of the Takahashi family
　Wife: Daughter of Satō Rihachi Tomokatu <<Born in Kyōhō 7 (1722), died of illness at age 65 on the 8th day of the 11th month of Tenmei 6 (1786), Buddhist name Enraku-in Myōi Nichijō, buried at the same place>>

1. In the 4th month of Kyōhō 9 (1724), he was employed as a regular foot soldier (ashigaru) and served as a rear gate guard.

1. In the 2nd month of Enkyō 2 (1745), he was appointed as assistant head of the rear gate at the Mita residence.

1. In the 6th month of Hōreki 4 (1754), he was appointed as a minor official attendant to Jusshin-in-sama.

1. In the 12th month of the same 7th year (1757), he was appointed as patrol guard for the crossroads at the Fukagawa residence.

1. On the 3rd day of the 10th month of the same 8th year (1758), he died of illness at age 48. Buddhist name Kaijō-in Zen'e Nichizen, buried at the same place.

Daughter <<Sayo>> Wife of Itabashi Shichiemon Masatane

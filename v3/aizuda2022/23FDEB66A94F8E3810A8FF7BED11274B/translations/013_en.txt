- In the 11th month of Tenmei 1 (1781), his son Jinkurō became ill, so he petitioned to have him removed from succession.

- In the 1st month of the 2nd year (1782), as requested, he was ordered to resume service.

- In the 2nd month of the 3rd year (1783), he petitioned to have his grandson Tsunezō join the inherited unit.

- In the 9th month of the same year, as requested, his grandson Tsunezō was appointed as substitute guard.

- On the 18th day of the 6th month of the 4th year (1784), he died of illness at age 65.

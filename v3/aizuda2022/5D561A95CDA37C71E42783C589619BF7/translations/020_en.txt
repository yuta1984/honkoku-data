【Right Page】
1　Same 5th year, year of the Ox, 5th month, having devoted utmost effort since the 4th year,
　　local administrative handling was also thorough, and regarding governmental reforms,
　　previously worked hard on various difficult inquiries to the authorities,
　　therefore was granted five pieces of silver as a reward

1　Same year, 9th month, due to personnel reduction, was relieved of current duties,
　　was appointed to the external retainer group,
　　entered Kasuga Jinzaemon's unit and served castle guard duty
　　　However, having served diligently until now, was granted
　　　three bundles of cotton as a reward

【Left Page】
1　Same 7th year, year of the Rabbit, 2nd month, as requested, his son Hatsugoro
　　was appointed as guard substitute

1　Same year, 4th month, regarding a dispute between the three villages of Ogawa-sho and
　　Kodo village in Kurokawa domain, during previous duties,
　　handled matters internally, arranged coordination with authorities and
　　various preparations well, performing labor beyond regular duties,
　　therefore was granted three bundles of cotton as a reward

1　Same 7th year, year of the Rabbit, 9th month, due to estrangement from adopted son Hatsugoro,
　　wished to return him to the Ito Mataemon family of his original surname

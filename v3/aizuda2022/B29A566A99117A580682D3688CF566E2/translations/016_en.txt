　　Actually the second son of Satō Hachibe Nobumine

　　Wife: Daughter of Nishimura Gorobei Katsutomo

One　In the 10th month of An'ei 2, snake year (1773), as petitioned by his adoptive mother, he was granted a stipend transfer for three persons.

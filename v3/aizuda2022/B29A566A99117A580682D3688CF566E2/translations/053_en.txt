一　In the 9th month of the same year, for his diligent service
　　in the military camp, as a reward he was given one bolt of
　　bleached cloth in place of ceremonial dress, and the items
　　were later bestowed in the lord's presence
一　During the same month, the two interval guards returned
　　without being needed
一　In the 12th month of the same 9th year, Year of the Rabbit (1759), for an extraordinary
　　sankin pilgrimage, he was assigned escort duties for the journey and went up

　　as guard duty escort
一　In the same month, he was assigned to serve
　　in escort duties for the journey to Kyoto
一　In the 2nd month of the same 10th year, Year of the Dragon (1760), it was announced
　　that he had served diligently in escort duties and during the journey
　　for the sankin pilgrimage in the previous 12th month
一　In the 3rd month of the same year, he served as escort and went up
　　for the Kyoto journey, and in the 4th month served as escort and returned
一　In the 4th month of the same year, for his diligent service
　　in the military camp, he was given one set of used hemp ceremonial dress from the lord's wardrobe

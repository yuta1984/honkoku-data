- In the 2nd month of the 6th year of Kyōhō (1721), Year of the Ox, before Tokuō-sama, for working diligently and performing well in the duties related to Shōsen'in-sama's ceremonial entry, by his lordship's gracious intention he was granted a kosode.

- In the 11th month of the same year, before Shōsen'in-sama, for working with dedication, by her gracious intention he was granted a naga-kamishimo.

- In the 4th month of the 7th year of Kyōhō (1722), Year of the Tiger, for working diligently and well despite being very busy with official duties, his current stipend was increased by fifty koku, granted directly by his lordship.

- In the 7th month of the same year, he was appointed to serve concurrently as construction magistrate (fusin-bugyō), ordered to serve in both roles together with the inspector (metsuke), and was granted one person's allowance for his assistants and five ryō in gold as his official stipend.

- In the 11th month of the same year, he was appointed as construction magistrate, and it was announced that he should continue to serve concurrently as inspector as before.

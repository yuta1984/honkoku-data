In the 10th month of the same year: Regarding the current bird hunting expedition, after the hunting grounds were changed, though it was the beginning [of the exercise], the formation and marching were exceptionally well-ordered, the units were properly connected, and operations proceeded without delay. Since he had been particularly diligent in joint unit training exercises as well, his entire unit was praised.

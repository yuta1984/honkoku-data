Adopted son
Tsurunosuke Tōka 《marginal note: Born in Kansei 1, year of the rooster》
　Biological father: Second son of Shinzaemon Tōtsune

1. In the 12th month of Kansei 10, year of the horse: He devoted himself to filial piety, performed meritorious acts, studied diligently, and showed skillful abilities beyond his years. As a reward, he was granted three pieces of silver.

1. In the 6th month of Kyōwa 3, year of the boar: He was granted the hereditary stipend of 100 koku without change, and was appointed as regular attendant page (gokoshō) to Lord Sadaaki.

1. In the same month: As his illness became severe and he had no male heir, his request to adopt Tomonosuke, the sixth son of Suwa Jūrozaemon, as his heir was granted.

1. On the 25th day of the same month: He died of illness at age 15. Buddhist name: Seigan Ryōshin. Burial place: Shibuya Hasedera Temple in Edo.

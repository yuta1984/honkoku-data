1. In the ninth month of Kyōwa 1, year of the rooster: He participated in the hunting expedition (tsuitori-gari)

1. Same month:
　He was appointed as a regular attendant page (tsunezume gokoshō) to Lord Sadaaki

1. In the tenth month of the same year: During the hunting expedition for preliminary duties, his formation order, marching pace, advances and retreats were well-coordinated, and the coordination of the four units was appropriate. This was recognized as the result of his diligent efforts, and he received praise from his lord

1. In the first month of Kyōwa 2, year of the dog: His request to change his name to Sennoshin was granted

1. In the second month of the same year: He was appointed to service before the lord (gozen-zutome)

1. In the fifth month of the same year: As there was an accumulation of old ceremonial items, they were bestowed upon him by the lord's gracious consideration

1. In the third month of Kyōwa 3, year of the boar: Although he was scheduled to accompany the lord on his journey to the domain, due to illness he was excused from this duty and ordered to rest leisurely and recover, then to depart when fully recovered

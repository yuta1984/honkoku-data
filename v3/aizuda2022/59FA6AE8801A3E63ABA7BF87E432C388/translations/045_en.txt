1. 7th year of Kyōhō, year of the tiger, 8th month: Was appointed to serve concurrently as palace guard at Mita residence and was ordered to serve in rotation for the personal attendant guard escort duty

1. 18th year of Kyōhō, year of the ox, 2nd month: Was appointed as sole firefighter for Mita residence

1. Same year, 11th month: Was ordered to serve as regular firefighter at Shiba residence

1. 19th year of Kyōhō, year of the tiger, 7th month: Was appointed to serve Eikōin-sama

　　Note: He served as regular firefighter for a total of fourteen years, during which he led groups of men and performed his duties well, receiving praise on numerous occasions

1. Same year, 12th month: Was appointed as ceremonial New Year representative and received gifts
　　Note: The specific items received are not detailed

1. 20th year of Kyōhō, year of the rabbit, 4th month: Due to his excellent service over many years, his second son Somenoshin was called to serve as page to Gan'ei-in-sama

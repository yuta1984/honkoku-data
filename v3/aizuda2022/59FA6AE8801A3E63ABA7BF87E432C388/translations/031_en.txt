Wife: Daughter of Ikegami Shinzaemon Takayasu 《marginal note: Born on the 1st day of the 2nd month of Kansei 1, year of the rooster》

1. In the 1st month of Kansei 7, year of the rabbit: He was appointed as a temporary retainer for escort duty
　　However, after this, he was frequently appointed to positions such as escort guard, outer retainer, regular temporary retainer, etc.

1. In the same month: He was appointed as instructor of Ittō-ryū spear techniques

1. In the 7th month of Kansei 12, year of the monkey: For diligently serving as spear technique instructor, he was granted two pieces of silver as a reward

1. In the 6th month of Kyōwa 3, year of the boar: He was officially appointed as adopted son according to his adoptive father's wish

1. In the 8th month of the same year: He was granted the hereditary stipend of 100 koku without change, appointed to the regular outer retainer group, and entered Miyake Magobei's unit

1. In the 12th month of the same year: He was appointed as regular escort guard

1. In the 4th month of Bunka 1, year of the rat: It was announced that he had served diligently without absence since the previous 12th month

1. In the 10th month of the same year: It was announced that he had served diligently without absence since the 4th month of that year

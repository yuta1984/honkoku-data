One　Same year, 11th month: For performing current duties diligently and well, by special consideration was granted regular upper and lower rank, as was announced

One　Same 10th year (Year of the Dog), 5th month: Due to an unexpected incident in the Echigo region, was ordered to deploy the second contingent of troops, was ordered to go on assignment to Akataniguchi to handle all matters without hindrance, went there and returned in the 6th month of the same year

　　However, regarding this official duty, took subordinates along for service and managed affairs so that all matters proceeded without hindrance

One　Same 12th year (Year of the Boar), 10th month: Regarding Inawashiro, it had long been a difficult administrative unit, and with consecutive poor harvests for two years, though considerable financial assistance was provided, there were continuing reports of poor control over popular sentiment.

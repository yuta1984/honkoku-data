【Right page】
- In the 11th month of the same 3rd year (Year of the Horse), appointed to employment at the investigation office (ginmisho), granted two ryō of gold annually as compensation for his efforts

- In the 2nd month of the same 4th year (Year of the Sheep), appointed to employment at the administrative office (goyōsho), compensation continued as before

- In the 11th month of the same year, on the occasion of the lord's entry into the domain, when meals were provided to all retainers as celebration, he served diligently without delay in this duty and was granted soup and sake

【Left page】
- In the 12th month of Kanpō 1 (Year of the Rooster), for his diligent service, he was granted a standard stipend of 10 koku and provisions for 2 people, and was appointed as a prison guard official

- In the 9th month of Kanpō 2 (Year of the Dog), he went up to Edo for guard duty (kinban)

- In the same month, he was commended for his diligent and flawless service in both escort duties and travel arrangements, and was particularly commended for the hardships endured when the lord's travel route was suddenly changed to pass through Sano due to water conditions

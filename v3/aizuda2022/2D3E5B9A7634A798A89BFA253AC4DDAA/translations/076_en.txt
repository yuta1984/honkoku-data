【Right Page】
- In the 10th year of Tenmei (1790, year of the dragon), 4th month: Went up to Edo for guard duty service, where he served in both the invitation ceremony and wedding ceremony duties, staying until the 12th month before returning
　　Note: For performing these duties, he was granted clothing allowances on both occasions

- In the 11th year (1791, year of the snake), 1st month: Due to various important domain affairs since last autumn, with additional appointments and staff shortages, he worked diligently and was rewarded with 100 hiki of gold

- Same year, 8th month: His chronic intestinal ailment frequently flared up, and old injuries from a fall from his horse during a journey also repeatedly caused problems, making it difficult to perform his duties. He petitioned to be relieved of his position, but was ordered to continue serving while taking care of his health

【Left Page】
- In the 13th year (1793, year of the sheep), 3rd month: As requested, he was relieved of his position and appointed to Naka-no-kuchi guard duty

- In Meiwa 2 (1765, year of the rooster), 5th month: Was appointed to assist with airing out books in the domain library to prevent insect damage

- Same year, 8th month: Was appointed to one-year service at the domain lending office

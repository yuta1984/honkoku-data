【Right page】
　　Having attended without absence and applying himself diligently to his studies, he was granted three bu of gold as a reward.
1. In January of Kyōwa 2 (Year of the Dog): Due to the school construction project, he dedicated himself with determination and worked personally. Although the extensive terrain would normally require a considerable number of workers, the project was completed in a short time. The construction was carried out with exceptional vigor, the training hall was successfully completed, and all members encouraged each other. This came to His Lordship's attention, and he was greatly pleased. As a result, because he personally participated for several days, sake was bestowed upon him at the school.

【Left page】
1. In September of the same year: Two koku and one person's stipend were reduced from his late father's allocated rice, and he was granted eight koku and two persons' stipend plus three koku as official duty allowance, and was appointed as a foot soldier (o-kachi).
2. In February of Bunka 1 (Year of the Rat): He had an audience when [the lord] passed by the shin-ban guard post once.
3. In April of the same year: Due to Lord Sadaaki's trip to Edo, he served as an attendant guard, went up to [Edo], and returned in October of the same year.

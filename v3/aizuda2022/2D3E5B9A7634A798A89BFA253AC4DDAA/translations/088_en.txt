【Right page】
　　There were official duties, and he made repeated trips to and from Tsugawa, carrying out various official tasks before returning in the 5th month of the same year.
　　　Note: Since the troops proceeded to Nakajo, he went to that location and performed various duties. He also went to Niigata to ensure official business was properly handled. The details were reported through the duty records of that time.

- In the 6th month of the same year, due to an unexpected incident in the Echigo region, troops were dispatched. Those who had gone to Nakajo for this official duty showed good dedication and promptly departed.

【Left page】
　　Despite the summer heat, they worked particularly hard, so sake was bestowed upon them at the domain school (daigaku-kō).

- In the 8th month of the same year, he went to Akutsu for guard duty.

- In the 11th month of the same 12th year (Year of the Boar), he was appointed as a rice transport official (kaimai yakunin) and was granted a stipend of 5 koku and provisions for one person as before.

- In the 1st month of the same 13th year (Year of the Rat), he was ordered to go and oversee the resolute execution of rice transport in the eastern districts, and returned in the 2nd month of the same year.

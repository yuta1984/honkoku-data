　　　　Daughter of Noya Sōdayū Mitsushige
• Kanei 4 (Year of the Boar, 1627), 12th month: Appointed to serve at the New Loan Office (shin kashikata yakusho) and granted 2 ryō of gold as compensation for his efforts.
• Shōtoku 1 (Year of the Rabbit, 1711), 4th month: Granted a three-person stipend and appointed to handle rice and money transactions at the New Loan Office.
【Red ink notation】Official audience granted
【Red ink notation】• Same 3rd year (Year of the Dragon), 11th month: Granted 2 ryō of gold annually as official salary.
• Kyōhō 1 (Year of the Monkey, 1716), 6th month: Granted a pseudo-fief (gisaku) of 10 koku and two-person stipend.

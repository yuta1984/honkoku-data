【Right page】
　　　Adopted daughter of Takebayashi Etsuzaemon Fusukage
　　　Actually the daughter of Takebayashi Kōhachi Fusaaki
1. In the 1st year of An'ei (Year of the Dragon, 1772), April: One koku and one person's stipend was deducted from his deceased father's provisional appointment, and he was granted nine koku and two persons' stipends, and was summoned to serve as middle gate guard.
2. In the 2nd year of the same era (Year of the Snake, 1773), June: He was ordered to assist with airing books to prevent insect damage in the lord's library.
3. In the 4th year of the same era (Year of the Sheep, 1775), November: He was appointed to temporary employment for secretarial duties for the senior retainer.
【Left page】
4. In the 6th year of the same era (Year of the Rooster, 1777), June: Due to his diligent service, he was appointed to secretarial duties for the senior retainer.
5. In the same year, December: For his diligent and devoted service during the lord's stay in the domain, he was privately granted three bu in gold.
6. In the 7th year of the same era (Year of the Dog, 1778), March: He returned and reported to the castle as part of the lord's entourage.
　　　　However, one bu for campaign dress allowance and two bu in gold for service were granted as per usual custom.

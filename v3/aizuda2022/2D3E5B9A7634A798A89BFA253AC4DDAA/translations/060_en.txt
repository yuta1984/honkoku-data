　　He was appointed to treat Lord Tokuō's illness, and moreover
　　was appointed to conduct regular pulse examinations, and thereafter was repeatedly
　　appointed to provide medical treatment.
1. In the 6th month of Kan'en 4 (1751, year of the sheep), when the domain inspectors
　　came over, he was ordered through official designation to
　　serve during their stay. He went up to Edo to
　　meet them, served during their stay, and in the following year of the rat accompanied them back down.
1. In the 2nd month of the same 5th year (1752, year of the monkey), for
[Left page]
　　serving the domain inspectors' duties,
　　he was awarded two pieces of silver as a reward.
1. In the 5th month of Hōreki 9 (1759, year of the rabbit), he was told that
　　henceforth he could be appointed when there was official business, and moreover
　　that he could also be appointed to conduct regular pulse examinations.
1. In the 6th month of the same 13th year (1763, year of the sheep), for
　　serving diligently for several years, he was granted an increase of five-person stipend.
1. In the 6th month of Meiwa 7 (1770, year of the tiger), [he received] the rank of personal physician

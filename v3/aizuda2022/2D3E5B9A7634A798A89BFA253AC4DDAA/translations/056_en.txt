【Right Page】
　　Having become a disciple of Nishi Genpo-rō, a physician to the Shogunate,
　　he studied surgical medicine and practiced widely in this locality.
　　For this, he was granted an audience at the Koshoin
一　Same 11th (Horse) year, 9th month
　　He was appointed to provide surgical medical services
　　to Lord Tokuō, and repeatedly
　　provided treatment
　　　However, although he received various gifts for the above services,
　　　the specific items are unknown
【Left Page】
一　Same 15th (Dog) year, 2nd month
　　He was summoned by Lord Tokuō and directly
　　received sake from him
　　　Furthermore,
　　　He was also appointed to provide surgical treatment
　　　for Lord Baien'in and Lord Ganshōin
一　Same 16th (Boar) year, 5th month, in the lord's presence
　　He received one each of the crested court robes
　　of Lord Baien'in and Lord Ganshōin

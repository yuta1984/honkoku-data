【Right page】
1. In the 2nd year of Tenmei (Year of the Tiger, 1782), November: When he petitioned to be relieved of his current duties due to illness, he was ordered to continue his service while taking care of his health.
2. In the 3rd year of the same era (Year of the Rabbit, 1783), January: As petitioned, he was relieved of his current position and appointed to middle gate guard duty.
3. In the same year, December: He was appointed as an assistant with a supervisor attached to the middle gate guard.
4. In the 1st year of Kansei (Year of the Rooster, 1789), March: He was commended for performing his middle gate administrative duties diligently and without delay.
5. In the same year, December: He was granted four koku and one person's stipend as official salary and appointed as foot inspector (ashigaru metsuke).
【Left page】
1. In the 3rd year of the same era (Year of the Boar, 1791), March: His petition to change his name to Kizaemon was granted.
2. In the same year, November: He was appointed to one-year supervision duty at the gold mine office's copper mine section.
3. In the 4th year of the same era (Year of the Rat, 1792), intercalary 2nd month: He was ordered to go to Kanose Copper Mine for inspection purposes.
4. In the same year, October: He was appointed to supervision duty for mine personnel at the gold mine office.
5. In the 5th year of the same era (Year of the Ox, 1793), April: He conducted inspections of various gold mines.

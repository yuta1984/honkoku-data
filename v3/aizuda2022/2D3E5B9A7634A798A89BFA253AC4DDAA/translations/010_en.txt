Niizuma Yanosuke of Tsue-tsuki was accused of being a Christian. Two years ago, he was ordered to be imprisoned and interrogated, after which he gave his explanation. Last year, when Saitō Seiemon went up to report, he gave a detailed account to Lord Inoue Chikugo-no-kami, who listened and granted him a pardon. However, he was ordered to record the details of the re-investigation in a ledger, verify whether it matched the ledger contents, and report again. After creating the ledger multiple times and reporting in the third month of this year, that official ledger was sent last month, but nothing was sent regarding Yanosuke's case.

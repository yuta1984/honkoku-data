Item: In the twelfth month of the same 2nd year, Rooster year, he was appointed as head of the gun bearers (mochizutsu-gashira).
Item: In the fourth month of the same 3rd year, Dog year, he went up to Edo for guard duty service as part of the lord's procession attendance and returned in the tenth month of the same year.
Item: In the ninth month of the same 5th year, Rat year, he went up for guard duty service and returned in the third month of the following Ox year.
　　However, upon his return, he was summoned before the lord and received the lord's commendation for his diligent service over many years, and as a bestowed item for the pacification, he was given goods

　　in exchange, and granted a kosode with the lord's crest.
Item: In the fourth month of the same 9th year, Dragon year, when there was a fire at Shiba Sukenosuke's tenement, both father and son quickly rushed to the scene and handled the situation well, for which they received the lord's commendation.
Item: In the second month of the same 10th year, Snake year, he was appointed as head of the lord's attendants (otomo-bangashira) in the lord's presence.
Item: In the first month of the same 12th year, Sheep year, due to his diligent service over many years, he was granted a stipend of 100 koku as role compensation.

When Sone Genzaemon-sama and Shibata Shichisaemon-sama returned from this locality to Edo, he was ordered to escort them as far as Seishi-dō.

Item: In the eleventh month of Jōkyō 2, Ox year, as requested, his son Tosho was appointed as substitute guard.

Item: In the ninth month of Genroku 1, Dragon year, when the head Hori Hanemon retired, he was transferred to Jinbo Mozaemon's unit as successor to the role.

Item: In the twelfth month of the fourth year, Sheep, he was granted retirement as requested.

Item: On the ninth day of the eighth month of Hōei 2, Rooster year, he died of illness at age eighty-five. His posthumous name was Jōsen-in Shin'an Gesshin Koji, burial place same as above.

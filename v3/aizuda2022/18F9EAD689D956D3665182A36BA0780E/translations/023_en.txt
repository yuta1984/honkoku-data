《Marginal note: Adopted son｜》
　Tosho Tamenaga　《Marginal note: Initially called Tetsuroku, also Mataemon｜Born on the 11th day of the 2nd month of Kyōhō 4th year, Boar year》
　　Actually the fifth son of Tosho Tamekage
　　Wife: daughter of Maruyama Katsuzaemon Tanetsugi《Marginal note: Named Taga｜》《Marginal note: Born on the 24th day of the 12th month of Kyōhō 14th year, Cock year｜Died of illness on the 17th day of the 4th month of Kansei 1st year, Cock year, Buddhist name Ryōun-in｜Temple name Natsu-hō Shin-pū Daishi, burial place same as above》

Item: In the intercalary 4th month of Kan'en 3rd year, Boar year, granted first audience with the lord as requested by his father

Item: In the 4th month of Hōreki 7th year, Ox year, appointed to guard duty as requested by his father, joined Takahashi Koemon's unit and served castle guard duty

Item: In the 1st month of the same 8th year, Tiger year, his request to change his name to Mataemon was granted

Item: In the 10th month of the same 9th year, Rabbit year, went up to Edo for guard duty service. In the 3rd month of the following Dragon year, during his guard duty term, he was appointed to accompany the lord's journey to Kyoto, served as mounted escort during the journey, and arrived at the府 in the 4th month of the same year. At that time, he was ordered to continue guard duty until his wife's service obligations were completed, which he fulfilled, and returned home in the same month

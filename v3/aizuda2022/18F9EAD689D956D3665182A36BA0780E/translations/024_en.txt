Item: In the 7th month of the same 11th year, Snake year, upon family succession, he was granted a fief of 400 koku without any reduction

Item: In the same month, his request was granted to adopt a daughter of Hanzaemon of the same family name as his adopted daughter, and to take梶原 Kajiwara Kyusuke's grandson Matajiro as an adopted son-in-law

Item: In the 6th month of the same 13th year, Sheep year, he was appointed as Stable Superintendent (Umaya-bettō), with his seat ranking placed after the Mountain Magistrate and above the Inspector (Metsuke)

Item: In the 8th month of the same year, for the Mineyama shrine festival, the lord made a shrine visit, and on that occasion also inspected both checkpoints at Tsuboshita of Sukawa, for which he served as escort

Item: In the 2nd month of Meiwa 1st year, Monkey year, his request was granted to take Tomohachiro, the second son of Aruga Magodayu, as an adopted son-in-law

Item: In the same month, for his diligent service during the lord's residence in the domain, he was awarded two pieces of silver as a reward

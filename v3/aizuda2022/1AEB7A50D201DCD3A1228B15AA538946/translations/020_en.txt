Kōzō Mitsumasa 《Marginal note: Originally named Kichijirō
Born on the 15th day of the 4th month of Anei 2, year of the Snake》
    Biological father: Second son of Yanase Daisaku Masanao
    Wife: Daughter of adoptive father Hachirō Mitsunaga

1. Kansei 2, year of the Dog, 3rd month: Granted his first audience [with the lord] as requested by his parent

1. Same 8th year, year of the Dragon, 2nd month: Appointed as guard substitute as requested by his parent, and served castle guard duty in Kajiwara Heima's unit

1. Same year, 10th month: It was proclaimed that he had worked diligently at spear technique, and sake was bestowed upon him

1. Same 11th year, year of the Sheep, 9th month: Participated in the hunting expedition
    Note: After this he participated twice more, once during a training session

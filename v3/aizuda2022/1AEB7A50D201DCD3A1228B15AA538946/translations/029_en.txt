In the 1st year of Jōkyō, year of the rat (1684), 4th month, he was appointed to concurrently serve as district magistrate.

In the same 3rd year, year of the tiger (1686) (month unknown), when unit commander Tanaka Kahyōe died of illness, he was transferred to Kasuga Nizaemon's unit as his successor.

In the 4th year of Genroku, year of the sheep (1691), 4th month, due to unit commander Kasuga Nizaemon's reassignment, he was transferred to Hinata Saburōemon's unit as his successor.

In the intercalary 8th month of the same year, he was relieved of his concurrent magistrate duties as requested, and from the 11th month of the same year served castle guard duty.

In the same 8th year, year of the boar (1695), 4th month, when unit commander Hinata Saburōemon was relieved of his post as requested, he was transferred to Tanaka Kahyōe's unit as his successor.

In the same 9th year, year of the rat (1696), 3rd month, his son Sukemon was appointed as his guard duty substitute as requested.

On the 23rd day of the 8th month of the same year, he died of illness at the age of fifty-seven. His posthumous Buddhist name was Nen'yo Rishū Shinji, and he was buried at Gokurakuji Temple on Mount Bodai.

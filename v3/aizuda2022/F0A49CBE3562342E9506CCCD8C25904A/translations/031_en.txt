One: In Jōkyō 2nd year, year of the ox (1685), 4th month
　　He was granted permission to adopt Heitayū, the second son of Ikegami Zenbei, as son-in-law and heir.

One: In the same year, 8th month
　　The head Harada Shuma was relieved of his duties, and he was transferred to Yamazaki Sasuke's unit as successor.

One: In Genroku 4th year, year of the sheep (1691), intercalary 8th month
　　As requested, he was relieved from his concurrent duty as magistrate.

One: In the same 6th year, year of the rooster (1693), 9th month
　　As requested, his son Heitayū was appointed to guard duty replacement.

One: In the same 8th year, year of the boar (1695), 2nd month, 3rd day
　　He died of illness at age seventy. His posthumous Buddhist name was Kōō Senchū Koji, burial place same as above.

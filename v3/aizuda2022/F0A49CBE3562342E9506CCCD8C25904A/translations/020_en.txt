He was appointed and served in Nagasaka Heitayū's unit.

- In the 10th year (Year of the Rooster), 12th month: As a new defense post was established in Sagami Province, and he had served diligently since being employed as an external retainer, he was granted two rolls of brocade as a reward.

- In the 11th year (Year of the Dog), 5th month: He was appointed to concurrently serve as a messenger official (goshiyaku).

- In the 12th year (Year of the Boar), 2nd month: He was appointed as a permanent inspector (gometsuke) in Sagami Province and was ordered to take charge of the Kannonzaki post.

- Same month: He moved to Kannonzaki.

[Left page]
- Same year, 12th month: When stationed at Misaki, he was appointed as an archery instructor. As he paid careful attention to the training of various students from the beginning and raised them well through diligent effort, he was granted one piece of silver as a reward.

- In the 14th year (Year of the Ox), 1st month: It was announced that he had served diligently during emergency situations when foreign ships were sighted the previous spring.

- Same year, 9th month: When there were reports of foreign ships sighted off the coast of Bōshū, since it was his year of duty at Hiraneyama, he was stationed there from the 1st to the 12th of the 10th month.

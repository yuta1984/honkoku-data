　　The matter of being granted his first formal audience
1. Eleventh month of Tenmei 4 (Dragon year): As inheritance, he was granted a stipend of 100 koku without change, appointed to the outer retainer group, entered Kasuya Gozaemon's unit, and performed castle guard duties
1. Third month of Tenmei 7 (Sheep year): He was appointed as an assistant to the senior councilor
1. Sixth month of Kansei 1 (Rooster year): He was transferred to Numasawa Gonbee's unit
1. Tenth month of the same year: Numasawa Gonbee's position was revoked, and the successor position became part of Jinbo Kuranosuke's unit

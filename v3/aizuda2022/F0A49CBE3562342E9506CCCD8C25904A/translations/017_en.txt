- In the 9th month of Kansei 1 (Year of the Rooster, 1789), when Numazawa Kurōbei's official position was revoked, he was assigned to Jinbo Kuranosuke's group as his successor.

- In the 9th month of Kansei 5 (Year of the Ox, 1793), he participated in the falcon hunting expedition. This group participated four times in total, including this occasion and subsequent training sessions.

- In the 4th month of Kansei 6 (Year of the Tiger, 1794), during the falcon hunting expedition of the previous autumn, the formation and ranks were well-coordinated, and considering his diligent training and practice, the entire group received commendation by the lord's gracious recognition.
　Note: He continued to receive such commendations thereafter.

- In the 8th month of Bunka 8 (Year of the Sheep, 1811), he was appointed as a retainer of the Sagami Province group and was assigned to Harada Matasuke's group.

- In the 10th month of the same year, he was dispatched to the Sagami Misaki garrison for guard duty.

- In the 11th month of the same year, he was appointed to permanent duty in Sagami Province.

- In the 12th month of the same year, he was appointed as temporary ashigaru commander and served until the 2nd month of the following year (Year of the Monkey).

- In the same month, when Harada Matasuke was appointed to Kannonzaki duty, he was assigned to Nagasaka Heidayū's group as his successor.

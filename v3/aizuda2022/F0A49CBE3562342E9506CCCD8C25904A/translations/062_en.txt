【Right Page】
　　Wife: Daughter of Nakazawa Tsunezaemon Takashige　　　Divorced
　　　　Adopted daughter of Nagaoka Katsudayū Hisatomo　　　Also divorced
　　　　Actually daughter of Nagaoka Banzaemon Hisatomi
　　　　Daughter of Nakamura Magohei Sanenaga
一　Meiwa 8th year (Year of the Hare), 7th month: As requested by his father, he was granted his first audience with the lord
一　Tenmei 2nd year (Year of the Tiger), 9th month: As requested by his father, his wish to change his name to Gihachirō was granted
一　Same 7th year (Year of the Sheep), 11th month: As requested by his father, he was appointed as a guard substitute

【Left Page】
　　He joined Ueda Hachirōuemon's unit and served castle guard duty
一　Kansei 1st year (Year of the Rooster), 4th month: As requested by his superior Ueda Hachirōuemon, he was relieved of his duties, and as his successor, he was transferred to Yanase Motouma's unit
一　Same year, intercalary 6th month: He was transferred to the third unit attached to the senior councilor
一　Same 5th year (Year of the Ox), 9th month: He participated in the bird hunting
　　　　However, of the two times he participated afterward, he made a catch once and received a reward
一　Same 6th year (Year of the Tiger), 4th month: During the autumn bird hunting last year, keeping pace in formation

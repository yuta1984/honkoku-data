【Right Page】
Seibei Katsushige
　　Mother's lineage unknown
　　Wife: daughter of Mukōyama Jizaemon Tsunenobu
One　In the 1st year of Keian《Year of the Rat》, November
　　Was summoned as a permanent Edo residence secretary for the audience chamber of Lord Iwahiko
　　《details of appointment unclear》
【Left Page】
One　In the 2nd year of Jōō《Year of the Snake》, May, regarding the reconstruction of the Three Buddha Hall at Nikkō,
　　went to that place and served as chief supervisor, for which
　　he was granted silver as a reward
One　In the same year, September
　　For Lord Hozutsu's journey to Kyoto, was appointed as lodging placard officer
　　and served in that capacity, for which he was granted a haori jacket, seasonal clothing, and gold
One　In the 1st year of Meireki《Year of the Sheep》, June
　　For Lord Iwahiko's return to the domain, accompanied him on the journey down,
　　and also accompanied him when he went back up to Edo

【Right page】
Ikiemon Katsutake　　　Initial name: Kajinosuke
　　Mother: Same as above
　　Wife: Daughter of Sasahara Gunpei Naoharu
1. Kyōhō 4 (Year of the Boar), 4th month: Upon succession, granted a hereditary stipend of 100 koku without change, appointed to the gaikaku (outer retainer) group, and assigned to serve castle guard duty under Numazawa Gonbei's unit.

【Left page】
1. Same 15th year (Year of the Dog), 7th month: Appointed to kobuhin (light construction/maintenance duty) as requested.

1. Same 17th year (Year of the Rat), 12th month: Unit leader Numazawa Gonbei was relieved of his duties as requested, and [Ikiemon] was transferred to Obara Goroemon's unit.

1. Same 20th year (Year of the Rabbit), intercalary 3rd month: His son Seitarō was appointed as guard substitute as requested.

1. Kanpō 2 (Year of the Dog), 12th month, 18th day: Died of illness at age 50. Buddhist posthumous name: Zen'an Dōkei Koji. Burial place: same as above.

　　Wife: daughter of adoptive father Sukesaburō Shigeken (died of illness at age 72 on the 12th day of the 5th month of Kansei 11, year of the sheep; Buddhist name: Gokan Myōshō Daishi; burial place same as above)
- Enkyō 4 (year of the rabbit), 5th month: As requested by his father, he was granted his first audience with the lord.
- Hōreki 8 (year of the tiger), 2nd month: As requested by his father, he was appointed to goban-dai (guard substitute) and served castle guard duty in Kajiwara Heima's unit.
- Meiwa 1 (year of the monkey), 10th month: He was appointed to concurrent duty as magistrate (daikan).
- Same 3 (year of the dog), 3rd month: Unit commander Kajiwara Heima was transferred to another position,
[Left page]
　　and he was reassigned to Hyūga Saburō-emon's unit as successor.
- Same 6 (year of the ox), 8th month: Due to family succession, he was granted a 150-koku stipend without reduction, and was ordered to continue his concurrent magistrate duties as before.
- Same year, 12th month: He was awarded two bundles of cotton as a reward for his diligent efforts in procuring rice and money from commoners during recent urgent financial needs and successfully managing emergency duties.
- Same 9 (year of the dragon), 3rd month: He was ordered to transfer to Kasuya Gozaemon's unit.

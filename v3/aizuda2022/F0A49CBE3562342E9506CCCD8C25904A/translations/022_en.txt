- Same year, 6th month: When Ishizawa Tajiemon was dispatched for the construction work of the official quarters within Misaki magistrate's office, it interfered with the investigation duties (tadashi-goy≈ç). Therefore, he was told to serve only in investigation duties. However, since investigation duties were his primary responsibility and not performing them would cause consistent problems, it was determined that he need not serve in investigation duties, and he was ordered to serve in the Misaki construction project as acting district magistrate.

- Same year, 8th month: While he had been appointed as acting district magistrate, since that duty was completed, he was told to return to his original duties.

[Left page]
- 3rd year (Year of the Dragon), 4th month: Regarding the construction of official quarters at Misaki, it was announced that he had worked diligently as acting district magistrate.

- Same year, 12th month: He was ordered to restore the old system in Sagami Province, was relieved of guard post duties, and was ordered to withdraw. Regarding this, in the following 5th month (Year of the Snake), he returned to Aizu bringing his retainers with him.

- 4th year (Year of the Snake), 8th month: He was appointed to the non-official group (mushaku-gumi). Since he had served continuously from the beginning at the Sagami guard post for many years and worked diligently, he was granted one set of old hemp formal wear (kamishimo) as a reward.

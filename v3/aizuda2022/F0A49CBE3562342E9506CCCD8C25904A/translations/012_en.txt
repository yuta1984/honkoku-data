- Same year, sixth month: He was appointed to the position of gun yakusho sadame tachiai (district office coordinator).
- Second year of the same era (year of the snake), eighth month: He was appointed as temporary district magistrate (gun bugyō kari-yaku).
- Third year of the same era (year of the horse), second month: Due to disturbances caused by peasants the previous winter, his official position was revoked.
- Same year, sixth month: When he served as temporary district magistrate two years prior, there were issues with his handling of surplus land surveys and rice loans. He had delegated matters to Saigō Niemon and Kawadome Sakusaku without proper consultation, and despite being in a temporary position, his conduct was rash and negligent in various matters.

[Left page]
- Therefore, although there were prospects for his appointment, those duties were cancelled, he was assigned to kobushin (construction/maintenance corps), and was ordered into house confinement.
- Hōreki 3 (year of the rooster), third month: He was ordered to have an audience with the lord, immediately went to Edo, and had his audience.
- Same year, ninth month: He was appointed to the tozama-gumi (outer retainer group) and joined Kohara Gorōemon's unit, serving guard duty at the castle.
- Hōreki 8 (year of the tiger), first month: At the request of his superior Kohara Gorōemon, he was relieved of his duties, and as his successor, [he was assigned] to Kajiwara Heima's unit.

One　Same 13th year (year of the monkey), 3rd month: He went up to Edo as an attendant for the daimyo's mandatory attendance in Edo
　　　　However, he was praised for serving without absence during the journey

One　Same year, 10th month: He went up to Edo for duty service, and in the 4th month of the following year of the rooster, when the lord returned to the domain, he served as a temporary guard during the journey and returned home
　　　　However, he was praised for serving diligently without absence both in camp and during the journey

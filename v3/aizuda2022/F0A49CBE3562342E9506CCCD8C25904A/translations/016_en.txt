Goroshichi Yoshinori　　　First name: Shigenori　Born on the 21st day of the 2nd month of Hōreki 5th year (Boar year)
　Mother: Same as above
　Wife: Daughter of Akaba Bunji Tomonaka　Born on the 22nd day of the 8th month of Hōreki 9th year (Rabbit year)
- Meiwa 6th year (Ox year), 11th month: Was granted his first audience with the lord as requested by his father.

- Tenmei 1st year (Ox year), 4th month: Was granted hereditary stipend of 150 koku without discrepancy, was appointed to the tozama group, and assigned to serve castle guard duty under Kasuya Gozaemon's unit.
【Left page】
- Same year (Tenmei) 6th year (Horse year), 3rd month: Group leader Kasuya Gozaemon was granted retirement as he had requested, and [Yoshinori] was transferred to Numazawa Kurōbei's unit as successor to the role.

- Same year, 8th month: Regarding the matter of poor harvest assessment for the current year's crops, was ordered to enter into land survey operations and, like Satō Takebei and Ōishi Kanpei, tour the villages to investigate and calculate crop failure assessments until completion. Due to this assignment, he was exempted from castle guard duty and residential duty attendance.

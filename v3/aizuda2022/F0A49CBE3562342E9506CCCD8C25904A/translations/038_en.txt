【Right Page】
1. In the 7th month of Meiwa 4 (year of the boar), Sugita Gorobei's request was granted and he was relieved of his duties. The successor position was assigned to Kasuya Gozaemon's unit.

1. In the 4th month of the same 6th year (year of the ox), a request to change the name to Kobei was submitted and granted.

1. In the 10th month of An'ei 4 (year of the sheep), a request to become an adopted son-in-law of Gonkichi, younger brother of Higuchi Kakuzaemon, was submitted and granted.

1. In the 3rd month of the same 6th year (year of the rooster), it was announced that he had faithfully served castle guard duty for twenty years without negligence.

1. On the 10th day of the 9th month of Tenmei 4 (year of the dragon), he died of illness at the age of 64. His posthumous Buddhist name was Kanchū Genpo Koji. His burial place is the same as above.

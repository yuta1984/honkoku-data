　　Daughter of Shibuya Buhei Mitsutō

Item: In the 7th month of Bunka 9, the Year of the Monkey, [his stipend] was reduced by 1 ryō from his father's provisional allocation (*gisaku*), and he was granted 6 ryō 2 bu and 3 persons' worth of stipend in rice (*ninbuchi*). He was called into service attached to the *Sotomane-gumi* [an outer/reserve unit]. As he was assigned to pursue training in the arts, he was not assigned regular duties, and was ordered to submit the *kofushin-ryō* [a levy paid by those without active assignment].
　　Note: He was entered into the unit of Takahashi Yogorō.

Item: In the 9th month of the same year [Bunka 10], the Year of the Rooster, he turned out for the *oitorigari* [a large-scale hunting exercise/military drill].
　　Note: After this, he turned out twice more; on one occasion he received game as a gift from the lord.

Item: In the 11th month of the same year, during the said *oitorigari*, [his unit's] formation, marching order, and similar matters were especially well-organized; the seamless coordination and operation of the combined units was noted, and it was deemed that thorough effort had been made in joint formation drills beforehand. In recognition of this, the entire unit received commendation.

Item: In the same month, he was assigned as an *tozama-shi* [outer/non-hereditary retainer designation] of the *Ichi-no-kofushin-gumi* [First Reserve Group] and placed under the supervision of the *Gakkō Bugyō* [School Magistrate].

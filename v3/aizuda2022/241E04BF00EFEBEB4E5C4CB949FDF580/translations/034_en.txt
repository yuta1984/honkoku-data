**Genealogy of Hinata Den'emon**　　Clan origin: home province is Shinano. Family crest: hawk feathers crossed within a circle (*maru-uchi chigai takaha*). Alternate crest: flowering bracken (*hana warabi*).

Fourth son of Hinata Shinbei Tsugitane

**Hinata Shinji Tsuguyoshi**　　Original name: Tomedō. Born on the 25th day of the 5th month of Hōreki 5, the Year of the Dog.
　　Mother: daughter of his paternal grandfather Shinbei Tsuginori
　　Wife: daughter of Murakami Shōzaemon Shigehusa. Died of illness on the 27th day of the 9th month of Kansei 3, the Year of the Boar. Posthumous Buddhist name: Kenjuin Myōen Shinnyo. Place of burial: Jōmyō-in within the precincts of Chōōji Temple at Isarago, Edo.

1. In the 12th month of Meiwa 4, the Year of the Boar, he was granted a stipend (*gigi-saku*) of 8 ryō and a ration allowance for 4 persons at the standard (*nami*) rank, and was summoned to serve as an *okoshō* (page/attendant).

(Continued from previous page) Because the staff of the administered lands (o-azukari-kata) was also to be reduced, he was relieved of his current duties and appointed to the Gorōjō-tsuki Nikai Yoriai-gumi [the second-rank assembly group attached to the senior councillors]. Because he had devoted himself diligently to his duties up to that point, he was awarded one bundle of cotton (wata hitotaba) as a reward, and was enrolled in the First Company (ichibangumi).

1. In the same 7th year, Year of the Rabbit (1795), ninth month: He participated in the falconry hunt (ōtorikari).
　　Note: After this occasion, he participated once more.

2. In the same 12th year, Year of the Monkey (1800), twelfth month: As requested, his honorary stipend allocation (go-gisaku) was confiscated/revoked.

3. In Bunka 1st year, Year of the Rat (1804), tenth month, fifth day: He died of illness at the age of seventy. Posthumous Buddhist name (hōmyō): Hōrin'in Seiyo Dōkaku Koji. Place of burial: same as previously noted.

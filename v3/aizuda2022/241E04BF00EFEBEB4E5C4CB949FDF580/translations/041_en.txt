- In the sixth month of the tenth year (Bunka 10, Year of the Rooster, 1813), following Machida Hachirozaemon's appointment to permanent duty in Sōshū (Sagami Province), he was appointed as instructor of gunnery (*jūjutsu shihan*) in Machida's place.

- In the twelfth month of the same year (Bunka 10), he was appointed district magistrate (*o-daikan*) while retaining his existing seat (*mochi-seki*).

- In the same month, it was declared that he had applied himself with great diligence to the extraction and handling of various timbers for the construction works at Mount Mine (*Mine-yama go-fushin*).

- In the third month of Bunka 14 (Year of the Ox, 1817), having fulfilled two concurrent posts and managed the domain warehouse (*machi-gura*) and its residential duties, and having applied himself with particular diligence to various extraordinary duties throughout the previous year, he was granted one *tan* of bleached cloth (*sarashi*) in lieu of a formal hemp ceremonial vest and hakama (*asa o-kamishimo*) as a reward.

- In the eighth month of Bunsei 1 (Year of the Tiger, 1818), when a demonstration of firearms (*kajutsu go-ichiran*) before the domain lord was held together with his disciples, it was declared that both he himself and his disciples had performed their gunnery with excellence.
　Note: After this, he appeared before the lord on two further occasions, and each time he received commendation (*go-shōbi*).

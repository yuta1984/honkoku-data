(Continued from the previous page)… Having attained the highest level of martial arts proficiency, [Naomichi] was exempted from paying the kozampkin-ryō (small fief fee) and was ordered to report for active duty, serving as a guard at the castle.

Item: In the 9th month of the same year, he appeared (turned out) for the toritoi-gari (a hunting exercise).
　　Note: After this, he turned out four times for this event, including for horse training.

Item: In the 11th month of the same year, during that hunting exercise, the coordination of marching in formation, the unity of action, and the management of maneuvers were all in excellent order. It was recognized as being the result of his prior diligent efforts, and accordingly the entire unit received commendation.
　　Note: After this, they received commendation on every subsequent occasion.

Item: In the 5th month of Kyōwa 2, the Year of the Dog, as the unit commander (kumi-gashira) Kasuga Ninzaemon retired, the successor unit became that of Takahashi Yogorō.

Item: In the 2nd month of Bunka 4, the Year of the Rabbit, [Naomichi] was ordered to carry out duties related to the management of the closure of the domain's restricted hunting grounds (go-kachū go-men-yama).

Item: In the 12th month of the same [Bunka] 5, the Year of the Dragon, he was ordered to manage the closure of the domain's restricted hunting grounds; he performed inspections at various locations, and it was reported that the closures were handled in an exceptionally thorough manner. For his diligent service in these ad hoc duties, he was granted sake (rice wine) as a reward.

One entry: In the seventh month of the same 9th year [Year of the Rabbit, 1759], as requested, he surrendered his stipend equivalent (o-gisaku).

One entry: On the twenty-fourth day of the fourth month of the 1st year of An'ei [Year of the Dragon, 1772], he died of illness at the age of sixty-five. His posthumous Buddhist name (hōmyō) was Tetsugen Sōju Koji. His place of burial is the same as above.

Adopted daughter:　Wife of Higuchi Kiyoemon Mitsujin.
　　Biological [father]: Yamadera Sosuke Yasusada's daughter.

Sakuzaemon Mitsuyoshi　《Interlinear note: formerly Daishinō / Born in the 15th year of Kyōhō [Year of the Dog, 1730]》
　　Biological [father]: Eldest son of Misaka Tomōnosuke Takatsugu.
　　Wife: Daughter of adoptive father Sakuzaemon Mitsuchika.《Interlinear note: Died of illness on the last day of the first month of the 9th year of An'ei [Year of the Rat, 1780] / Posthumous Buddhist name: Soshinin Shunzō Myōi Daishi / Place of burial: same as above》

One entry: In the seventh month of the 9th year of Hōreki [Year of the Rabbit, 1759], five koku were deducted from his father's stipend equivalent (o-gisaku), and he was granted twenty koku and four-man rations (yo-nin-fuchi); he was summoned and appointed to the tozama-gumi (outer guard unit), and served as castle guard in the unit of Suwa Isuke.

One entry: In the twelfth month of the same 13th year [Year of the Sheep, 1763], the unit commander (kashira) Suwa Isuke retired from his post, and he consequently came to be placed in the unit of Numazawa Kurōbei as the successor unit assignment.

One entry: In the ninth month of the 5th year of Meiwa [Year of the Rat, 1768], the unit commander Numazawa Gonbei retired from his post, and he consequently came to be placed in the unit of Ichinose Naiki as the successor unit assignment.

One entry: In the intercalary twelfth month of the 4th year of An'ei [Year of the Sheep, 1775], his request to change his given name to Sakuzaemon was granted.

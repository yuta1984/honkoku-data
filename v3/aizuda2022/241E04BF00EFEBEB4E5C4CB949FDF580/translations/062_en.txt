As for Yūsuke, he was deemed a person too dangerous to be left unconfined; therefore, it was ordered that he be locked within the sitting room, with a secure fastening attached so that he could by no means pass beyond the inner veranda.

　Note: As Yūsuke is a person too dangerous to be left unconfined, every precaution is to be taken to fasten the room securely so that he absolutely cannot pass beyond the inner veranda of the sitting room. However, as he is hereby released from formal custody (o-azuke), all parties are to continue to remain watchful and attentive — this was declared. This instruction was to be communicated to the clansmen and all relatives, and this order was conveyed to the unit commander Hinata Saburōemon.

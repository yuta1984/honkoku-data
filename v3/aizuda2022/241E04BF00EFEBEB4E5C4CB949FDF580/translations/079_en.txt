In the 2nd year of Bunsei, Year of the Rabbit (1819), tenth month: Due to a change in established precedent and there being no further need [for his services in the current role], he was relieved of his current duties and placed without office (murō) at a special seat ranking (tokuseki). He was appointed to serve as a Garrison Armored Retainer of the Caretaker Guard (o-rusuigoto-bi kōshi), and his official stipend allowance (o-yakunryō) of two koku five to continued as before. Furthermore, commendation was formally expressed for his many years of diligent service up to this point.

2. In the same 4th year, Year of the Snake (1821), sixth month: He was appointed to the First-Tier Assembly Group attached to the House Elder (o-karō-tsuki ichi-no yoriai-gumi), and his official stipend allowance (o-yakunryō) of two koku five to continued as before.
　　Note: He was assigned to the Second Squad (ni-ban-gumi) and served concurrently in the School Lodging Watch (gakkō shuku-ban).

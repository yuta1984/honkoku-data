1. In the same 6th year, Year of the Snake (1809), tenth month: From the summer of the year before last, various official duties had continued in great number; in recognition of his diligent service throughout this period, he was awarded three hundred hiki in gold as a mark of commendation.

[Written in red ink] 1. In the same 7th year, Year of the Horse (1810), fourth month: He returned to Edo in attendance [upon the lord], and descended [to the domain] again in the fifth month of the same year.

2. In the same 7th year, Year of the Horse (1810), fifth month: He was appointed and ordered to serve as an official in charge of the lord's (殿様) affairs.

3. In the same year, eleventh month: Due to the pressing nature of official business, he was appointed to the temporary post of Confidential Affairs Officer (mitsujigakari karitome).

4. In the same 8th year, Year of the Sheep (1811), third month: He was appointed as a Page Guard (o-kinshū-ban) to the lord, and his seat ranking was placed above that of the Provisions Chief (o-makanai-gashira). His official stipend allowance (o-yakunryō) of two koku five to continued as before.

5. In the same 10th year, Year of the Rooster (1813), twelfth month: He was appointed to the temporary post of Examiner (gimiyaku karitome) at the Domain School Office (gakkō yakusho).

6. In the same 11th year, Year of the Dog (1814), third month: He was appointed as Examiner (gimiyaku) at the Domain School Office (gakkō yakusho). Furthermore, in recognition of his many years of diligent service, his seat ranking was appointed to the second tier of the Page Guard assembly (o-kinshū ni-no yoriai). His official stipend allowance (o-yakunryō) of two koku five to continued as before.

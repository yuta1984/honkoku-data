Seiemon Mitsuyoshi
　Mother: unknown

One entry: In the eighth month of Tenshō 12 [Year of the Monkey, 1584], when his father was killed in battle, he was ordered to succeed to the family estate (stipend and official post both unknown).

One entry: In the fifth month of Keichō 20 [Year of the Rabbit, 1615], during the Siege of Osaka, he sortied in attendance upon Lord Dōgi, and together with six comrades having mutually agreed [to fight to the death], he died in battle on the seventh day of that month. His posthumous Buddhist name is Jūnyūin-dono Tansō Kentei Koji. Burial place unknown.

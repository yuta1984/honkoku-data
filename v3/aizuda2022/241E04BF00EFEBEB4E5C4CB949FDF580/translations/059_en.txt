One entry: In the twelfth month of the same 7th year [Year of the Dog, 1778], the unit commander (kashira) Ichinose Naiki was reassigned to a different post, and he consequently came to be placed in the unit of Saigō Genzō as the successor unit assignment.

One entry: On the third day of the eighth month of the 1st year of Tenmei [Year of the Ox, 1781], he died of illness at the age of fifty-two. His posthumous Buddhist name (hōmyō) was Gikanin Shinsō Taichu Koji. His place of burial is the same as above.

Daughter:　Wife of adopted son Sakuzaemon Mitsuyoshi.
　　Mother: Daughter of Ariga clan.

Daughter:　Wife of Ariga Enji Mitsunobu.

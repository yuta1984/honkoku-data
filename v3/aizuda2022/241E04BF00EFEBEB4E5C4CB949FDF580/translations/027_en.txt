One: In the sixth month of the third year of Meiwa (Meiwa 3, 1766), the year of the Dog, during a banquet hosted by the lord, he was commended for his diligent and exemplary service in attending the guests.

One: In the tenth month of the fourth year of Meiwa (Meiwa 4, 1767), the year of the Boar, he was relieved of his current duties due to his unsuitability for the position, and was assigned to a role without specific duties (murole/無役).

One: In the second month of the seventh year of Meiwa (Meiwa 7, 1770), the year of the Tiger, he was granted an audience with the lord (omemie).

One: In the sixth month of the eighth year of Meiwa (Meiwa 8, 1771), the year of the Hare, he was assigned to the Tozama-gumi (outer retainer unit) and entered Tanaka Kahei's unit, where he served as a castle guard (o-shiro o-ban).

One: In the second month of the fifth year of An'ei (An'ei 5, 1776), the year of the Monkey, the unit commander (kumigashira) Tanaka Kahei was reassigned, and consequently, he was transferred to Kasuga Jinzaemon's unit as the successor assignment.

One: In the sixth month of the second year of Tenmei (Tenmei 2, 1782), the year of the Tiger, he was granted the use of Enomoto Tetsurōemon's upper residence (uwayashiki).

One: In the tenth month of the same year, he was praised for his diligent service in the guard unit, having achieved perfect attendance in the first unit for two consecutive years.

One: In the fourth month of the eighth year of Kansei (Kansei 8, 1796), the year of the Dragon, his stipend land grant (gigigusa) was revoked as he had requested.

One: On the nineteenth day of the second month of the fifth year of Bunsei (Bunsei 5, 1822), the year of the Horse, he died of illness at the age of seventy-nine.
Posthumous Buddhist name: Seikōin Shin'an Dōsei Koji　Place of burial: Same as above (as previously noted).

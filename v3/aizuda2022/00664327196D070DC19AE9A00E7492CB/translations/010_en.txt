- In the third year of the same era (1753), year of the rooster, fourth month, was appointed as deputy head of the foot soldier unit and served until the tenth month of the same year
- In the fourth year (1754), year of the dog, tenth month, was appointed to oversee the stipend storehouse for one year and served until the ninth month of the following year of the boar
　　　For this service, he was granted three bu of gold
- In the fifth year (1755), year of the boar, tenth month, went up to Edo as a guard and returned in the fourth month of the following year of the rat
　　　As he was appointed as temporary camp inspector, he was granted one ryō of gold as compensation for his efforts

- In the seventh year (1757), year of the ox, second month, was appointed as temporary inspector for village rice loans and served until the middle of the fifth month of the same year
　　　For this service, he was granted one bu of gold as compensation for his efforts
- In the same year, eighth month, was granted an increase of two koku in official stipend and provisions for one person, and was appointed as foot soldier inspector
- In the same year, tenth month, went up to Edo as a guard and returned in the fourth month of the following year of the tiger
- In the tenth year (1760), year of the dragon, first month, was assigned to oversee the carpenter's workshop for half a year

One: In the eleventh month of Hōreki 2, Year of the Monkey, as requested by his father, he was granted his first
　　audience at Edo
One: In the tenth month of the same 13th year, Year of the Sheep, as requested by his father, he was appointed as deputy to the Tozama group,
　　joined Bumon Sukeemon's unit, and served
　　castle guard duty
One: In the twelfth month of the same year, when unit commander Bumon Sukeemon was
　　appointed wakadoshiyori (junior elder), he transferred to Ikoma Gohee's unit as successor
One: In the fifth month of Meiwa 2, Year of the Rooster, he was granted without change
　　a hereditary stipend of 500 koku
One: In the same month, he went up to Edo for guard duty service and returned in the tenth month

　　　However, although he was scheduled to go up for guard duty in the fourth month, because his father Yusuke died of
　　　illness, after receiving the hereditary succession he went up and was appointed
　　　temporary commander in the field and served in that capacity
One: In the first month of the same 3rd year, Year of the Dog, it was announced that he had served his previous role as temporary field commander
　　without incident
One: In the ninth month of the same year, when unit commander Ikoma Gohee died of illness, he transferred to Harada
　　Matasuke's unit as successor
One: In the third month of the same 4th year, Year of the Boar, he went to Yamagata for guard duty service and returned
　　in the ninth month of the same year
One: In the tenth month of the same 5th year, Year of the Rat, he went up to Edo for guard duty service, and in the fifth month of the following Year of the Ox

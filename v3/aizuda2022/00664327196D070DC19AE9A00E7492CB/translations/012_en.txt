- In the 1st month of the 6th year of the same era (Year of the Ox): His seating order was established as following Iizasa Shichirōemon and above the Fukagawa Residence Guard.

- In the 6th month of the 8th year of the same era (Year of the Rabbit): Due to his diligent service over many years, his son Tomikichi was granted 8 koku and 2-person stipend plus an additional 4 koku in official salary, and was recruited as a foot soldier (o-kachi).

- In the 3rd month of An'ei 1st year (Year of the Dragon): He went to Edo for guard duty and returned in the 10th month of the same year.

- In the 1st month of An'ei 6th year (Year of the Rooster): Due to his diligent service over many years, he was directly granted an increase of 5 koku and 1-person stipend in official salary.

- In the 5th month of An'ei 7th year (Year of the Dog): As requested, he was relieved of his ceremonial duties (gisaku).

- On the 5th day of the 8th month of An'ei 9th year (Year of the Rat): He died of illness at the age of 73. His posthumous Buddhist name was Taigen Ryōtetsu Koji. Burial site: same as above.

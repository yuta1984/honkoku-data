The senior retainers conveyed the lord's message through Yokoyama Ichidayu, but there was something puzzling about it, so he did not accept the appointment.

1. In the same 2nd year (Kanbun 2), year of the Tiger (month unknown), he was again summoned through Yokoyama Ichidayu, and when he accepted, he was granted a fief of 300 koku and summoned to service.

1. In March of the same year, he was granted an audience with the lord.

1. In April of the same year, he was ordered to wear the kataginu (formal court dress).
   However, when the lord went out, he was instructed to go to the lord's destination and inquire after his health, and he regularly attended at the lord's destinations for audiences.

1. In September of the same year, with the lord's intention to employ him as a personal attendant (go-kinshū), he was appointed to various messenger duties and stationed at the mansion in Shibata-chō 1st district.

1. In March of the 3rd year, year of the Rabbit, he was appointed as messenger captain (go-tsukaiban).

1. In the intercalary 9th month of the 9th year, year of the Rooster, he moved down to Aizu.

1. In March of the 10th year, year of the Dog, he went up to Edo as a messenger.
   For this service, he was granted five pieces of silver.

1. In June of the same year, he was appointed as presenter (go-sōshayaku) and made captain of foot soldiers (go-kachi-gashira).

Third Generation
Gun【written in red ink】
Gun-no-suke Tanesuke　　Initial name: Yogorō, also: Katō Osamu, also: Ryūsuke
　　　Mother: Daughter of the Endō family
　　　Wife: Daughter of Harada Iyo Tanetoshi《Died of illness on the 16th day of the intercalary 4th month of Kan'ei 2, year of the Rooster. Buddhist name: Jissōin Myōri Daishi. Buried at Ryūzan Hokke-ji temple. Died at age 92 on the 28th day of the 10th month of An'ei 6, year of the Rooster》
　　　　　　Daughter of Ōhara Uemon Kazusada《Died of illness. Buddhist name: Shingetsu-in Jakusō Chikō Daishi. Buried at Banshōzan Tenrei-ji temple》

1. Genroku 12, year of the Rabbit, 7th month: As requested by his father, he petitioned to change his name to Katō Osamu

1. 《Year and month unknown》: As requested by his father, he was granted his first audience with the lord

1. Kyōhō 7, year of the Tiger, 3rd month: Succeeded to the family headship and was granted a fief of 500 koku without change, appointed to the tozama group, joined Ikoma Gohei's unit, and served castle guard duty

1. Same year, 10th month: Went up for guard duty in Edo, returned home in the 4th month of the following year of the Rabbit as part of the lord's entourage

1. Same 9th year of the Dragon, 3rd month: Appointed as messenger official (shiyaku)

1. Same year, 9th month: Went up for guard duty in Edo, returned home in the 5th month of the following year of the Snake as part of the lord's entourage
　　　Note: During this time, he served temporarily as intelligence officer (kikiban) in the field

1. Same 10th year of the Snake, 6th month: Appointed as squad leader (monotō)

1. Same 11th year of the Horse, 1st month: Petitioned to change his name to Ryūsuke

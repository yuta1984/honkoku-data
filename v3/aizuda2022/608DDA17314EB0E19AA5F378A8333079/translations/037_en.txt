　　　Wife: Daughter of adoptive father Jūemon Takakazu《Died of illness at age six on January 13th, Meiwa 8th year (Year of the Rabbit). Buddhist name: Tairyōin Myōgen Shinyo. Burial place: same as above》
　　　　Adopted daughter of Yamamoto Heizaemon
　One: In the 6th month of Meiwa 4th year (Year of the Boar), from his late father's stipend provisions, three people's provisions were reduced and he was granted twelve people's provisions, summoned to the external retainer group, entered Harada Matasuke's unit, and served castle guard duty.

　One: In the 10th month of Meiwa 5th year (Year of the Rat), for his diligent devotion to martial arts training, it was particularly noted with approval and announced.

　One: Around the 2nd month of An'ei 2nd year (Year of the Snake), due to Harada Matasuke's death from illness, he succeeded to the role in Ikoma Kuranosuke's unit.

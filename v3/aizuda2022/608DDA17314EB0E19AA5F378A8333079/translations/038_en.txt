There were circumstances, and furthermore, due to signs of mental derangement, he was ordered to be strictly confined in a cage. However, regarding this recent incident of such nature, though it was extremely thoughtless, he wished to continue searching for as long as possible in the future. He submitted a request for permanent leave, surrendering his stipend provisions. It was announced that if she were to be captured, this should be reported according to his wishes.
　　　　　However, though he traveled throughout various provinces searching, her whereabouts could not be determined, so he returned to his home domain. Moreover, when she first fled and returned, the year and month when she was ordered to be placed in strict confinement are not clear.

　One: In the 12th month of Tenmei 8th year (Year of the Monkey), he was granted three people's provisions as a compassionate stipend.

　One: In the 1st month of Kansei 1st year (Year of the Rooster), his request to change his name to Kōzō was granted.

　One: In the 12th month of Kansei 3rd year (Year of the Boar), he was appointed as a retainer outside the regular units.

　One: In the 1st month of Kansei 4th year (Year of the Rat), he was granted an audience with the lord.

　One: In the 3rd month of the same year, he participated in the bird hunting expedition.
　　　　However, afterwards he participated four times in regular duties, and once received game as a reward.

　One: In the same month, at the beginning of the current bird hunting expedition, all units assembled well together.

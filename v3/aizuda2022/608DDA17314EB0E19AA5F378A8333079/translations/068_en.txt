One: Kansei 2nd year (Year of the Dog), 1st month - Due to his diligent service for several decades, two of his three-person stipend portions in Edo were directly converted to regular stipend.

One: Same 5th year (Year of the Ox), 9th month - Due to his years of diligent service, he was appointed to the regular duty external retainer unit and commanded to serve concurrently as head of stables for Lord Sadaaki-in, as was announced.
Note: He was placed in Kajiwara Heima's unit.

One: Same 6th year (Year of the Tiger), 11th month - Due to his decades of service and his son Inosuke's diligent service as well, by special consideration and as requested by his colleagues, it was announced that his son Inosuke would be granted an audience with the lord at an appropriate occasion.

One: Same 7th year (Year of the Rabbit), 1st month - Due to his son Inosuke's flight/desertion, his request to make his second son Tatsukichi his heir was granted.

One: Same 8th year (Year of the Dragon), 11th month, 8th day - He died of illness at age 78. His Buddhist name was Jōen-in Kanjitsu Nikkō Shinji. His burial place was Zuirin-ji temple in Yanaka, Edo.

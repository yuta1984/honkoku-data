First entry: Same 13th year, Year of the Rat [1816], 12th month - Regarding training at the Sannomaru training ground, his formation and ranks were well-ordered, he advanced to a superior rank after three years, and moreover progressed in grade due to his diligent service, whereupon sake was granted to all members of his unit

First entry: Same 14th year, Year of the Ox [1817], 3rd month - Due to his commander Jinbo Kuranosuke's role reassignment, he was transferred to the succeeding commander Kasuya Sakon's unit

First entry: Same year, 4th month - Because his heir Kumajūrō was of weak constitution, he petitioned to adopt Tomekichi, the younger brother of Iwata Gizaemon, as his son

First entry: Same month, 19th day - He died of illness at age fifty, posthumous Buddhist name Bunkō-in Chigen Nisshō Koji, burial place

Daughter: Wife of Nagasaka Shikanosuke Tadayori, retainer of Lord Inaba Tamba-no-kami

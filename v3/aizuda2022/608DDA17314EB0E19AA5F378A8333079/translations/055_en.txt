Was appointed to the position of onando-yaku (chamberlain) to Lord Senkei'in

One: Same year, 11th month - Due to the celebratory banquet for the shogun's appointment, was appointed as a temporary worker and served in the role of gozen[...]-yaku (banquet preparation officer)

One: Same 2nd year (Year of the Rooster), 9th month - As requested, his son Shichizaemon was appointed as his substitute guard

One: Same 3rd year (Year of the Dog), 7th month, 22nd day - Died of illness at age 52. Posthumous Buddhist name: Seigetsuin Ritei Shinji. Burial site: Shōgyōin within Zuirinji Temple in Yanaka, Edo

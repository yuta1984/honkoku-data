Mineemon (adopted son) Shigesada《Died of illness on the 26th day of the 10th month of Hōei 2, year of the rooster (1705). Buddhist name: Ichmine Fuhaku Koji. Burial place: Banshōzan Tenneiji Temple》
　Actually the third son of Yanase Zenbei Toshitsune
　Wife: daughter of adoptive father Shirōzaemon Shigenaga, separated by death

Goroku Shigenō《Initially called Kajinosuke, born on the 22nd day of the 8th month of Genroku 7, year of the dog (1694)》
　Actually the second son of Kamiyama Kantadayū Morisada
　Wife: daughter of adoptive father Shirōzaemon Shigenaga《Born in Genroku 7, year of the dog (1694), died of illness on the 10th day of the 7th month of Kyōhō 15, year of the dog (1730) at age 40. Buddhist name: Teiya Eishō Daishi. Burial place: same as above》

Shirōzaemon Shigetsugu (first name Magotarō, born on the 2nd day of the 11th month of Shōtoku 5th year, year of the sheep)
　Mother: daughter of grandfather Shirōzaemon Shigenaga
　Wife: daughter of Sōma Isoji Shigetada (born on the 25th day of the 11th month of Kyōhō 9th year, year of the dragon; died of illness on the 4th day of the 11th month of Bunka 10th year, year of the rooster, at age 90; posthumous Buddhist name Juzan Myōryō Daishi; burial site: same as above)

1. In the 6th month of Kyōhō 11th year, year of the horse (1726), as petitioned by his father, he was granted his first formal audience with his lord.

1. In the 1st month of Enkyō 2nd year, year of the ox (1745), as petitioned by his father, he changed his name to Shirōzaemon.

1. In the 7th month of the same 3rd year, year of the tiger (1746), as petitioned by his father, he was appointed as guard duty substitute in the outer retainer group and served castle guard duty in Numasawa Gonbee's group.

1. In the 10th month of the same year, he went up to Edo for duty service and came down in the 4th month of the following year of the rabbit.

1. In the 3rd month of Kan'en 4th year, year of the sheep (1751), as petitioned by his superior Numasawa Gonbee, he was appointed as guard duty substitute and was transferred to Ueda Hachirōemon's group as successor to the position.

1. In the 6th month of the same year, he went up to Edo for extraordinary duty service.

- In the 5th year (Genroku 5, 1692), 2nd month, he was appointed to escort duty (gotomoban).
  However, during this service, he performed Edo duty twice and escort duty for welcoming and seeing off his lord once, but the exact dates are unknown.

- In the 9th year (Genroku 9, 1696), 10th month, he was appointed as inspector (metsuke).
  However, during this service, he performed Edo duty twice and escort duty for departure and return once, but the exact dates are unknown.

- In the 14th year (Genroku 14, 1701), 3rd month, he was appointed as magistrate of public affairs (kuji bugyō).

- In the 15th year (Genroku 15, 1702), 10th month, regarding the matter of peasants from the domain's direct holdings abandoning horses in the current territory, he went up to Edo on official investigation business and returned in the 12th month of the same year.

- In Hōei 3 (1706), 2nd month, he was appointed as district magistrate (gun bugyō).

- In Hōei 5 (1708), 5th month, he was appointed as town magistrate (machi bugyō).

- In Hōei 6 (1709), 2nd month, he was appointed to permanent service in the inner circle (jōzume okutsuki), and in the 3rd month of the same year, he went up to Edo.

- In Shōtoku 3 (1713), 11th month, he was relieved of his duties as requested.

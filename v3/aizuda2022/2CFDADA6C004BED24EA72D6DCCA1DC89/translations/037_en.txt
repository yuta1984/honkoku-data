- In the 12th year (Kyōhō 12, 1727), 9th month, as requested, he was granted his first audience with the lord.

- In the 14th year (Kyōhō 14, 1729), 7th month, he was appointed to the Aizu Koban (palace guard unit).

- In the 17th year (Kyōhō 17, 1732), 8th month, he served as an attendant to Lord Baien'in and Lord Ganshō'in, traveling to and serving in Edo.

- In Genbun 5 (1740), 2nd month, as requested, he was appointed to kinban (guard duty), and in the 4th month of the same year, he went up to Edo.

- On the 23rd day of the intercalary 7th month of the same year, he died of illness in Edo at age 28. His Buddhist name was Tokuhōin Ryōtatsu, and he was buried at Ansenji Temple in Azabu, Edo, Musashi Province.

Buhachirou (adopted son) Chikayoshi 《marginal note: first called Matsutarō, later Jōsuke》

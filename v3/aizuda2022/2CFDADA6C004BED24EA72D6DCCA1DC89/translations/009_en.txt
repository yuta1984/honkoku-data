Without discrepancy, he was granted [the stipend] and appointed to the outer retainer group (gaisama-gumi-tsuki), entered Ueda Hachirōemon's group, and served guard duty at the castle.

1. In the 3rd month of the same 3rd year, year of the dog (1718), he was appointed to lord's escort duty (go-tomban).

1. In the 4th month of the same 5th year, year of the rat (1720), he went up [to Edo] as an escort for the lord's attendance at court (sankin), serving as duty officer, and came down in the 10th month of the same year.

1. In the 4th month of the same 6th year, year of the ox (1721), he went up to meet the lord on his return journey (go-gekō), and in the same month served as escort and came down.

1. In the 10th month of the same 7th year, year of the tiger (1722), he went up to Edo for duty service, and in the 4th month of the following year of the rabbit, he served as escort for the lord's return and came down.

1. In the 1st month of the same 11th year, year of the horse (1726), due to illness, he petitioned to be relieved of escort duty, but since he was still young, he was ordered to recover gradually while continuing his service.

1. In the 10th month of the same year, when he went up to Edo for duty service, from the 11th month he was appointed as temporary gate guard (go-toban kariyaku), served until the 12th month, and in the 4th month of the following year of the sheep, he was appointed temporary gate guard for the lord's return journey and served as escort coming down.

【Left Page】
　　...and he was granted one tan of bleached cloth for formal dress.

One: In the twelfth month of the same year, due to illness, he petitioned to be relieved of both his temporary district magistrate and head clerk positions. Since he had exerted himself in both roles and served appropriately until then, but illness was unavoidable, his petition was granted and it was declared that he would be placed in a position without official duties for the time being.

One: In the first month of the 18th year of Bunsei (6th year of Tenpō), he was appointed to the outer retainer group, joined Sugita Gorobei's unit, and served guard duty at the castle.

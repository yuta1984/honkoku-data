- An'ei 3rd year (1774), 3rd month: As requested by his father, he was granted his first formal audience (with the lord).
- Kansei 4th year (1792), 6th month: He was appointed as administrator of the domain school at the Shiba residence.
　　　However, regarding this appointment, he was granted one ryō of gold annually without interest from the lecture hall funds as expense supplement.

[Left page]
- Kansei 5th year (1793), 1st month: He inherited the family headship with a stipend of 150 koku without change, and was appointed as an outside retainer attached to the Aizu financial magistrate.
　　　However, since there was no one available to serve as school administrator at the Edo office, causing difficulties, he was stationed in Edo until a suitable person could be found, ordered to serve concurrently as school administrator and reading instructor along with his main duty of guard service, and entered the third unit to perform guard duties.
- Kansei 6th year (1794), 10th month: He was appointed to permanent duty.
- Kansei 8th year (1796), 4th month: [He was appointed to] the regular fire brigade at the Shiba residence

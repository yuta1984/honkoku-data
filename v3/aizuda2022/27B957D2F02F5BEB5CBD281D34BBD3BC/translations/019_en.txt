【Left Page】
　　Natural father: second son of Tōyama Iemon Kagetake
　　Wife: daughter of adoptive father Tarōemon Takayoshi (died of illness on the 23rd day of the 7th month of Enkyō 2, Year of the Ox, posthumous name Keiganteiju Daishi, burial site same as above)
　　　Hara: daughter of Bunemon Naotomi (born on the 19th day of the 4th month of Kyōhō 13, Year of the Monkey, died of illness on the 25th day of the 9th month of An'ei 6, Year of the Rooster, posthumous name Suigan Myōsen Daishi, buried at Annenji Temple in Inawashiro)
- In the 9th month of Genbun 2 (Year of the Snake), as he was reportedly diligent in his studies without interruption, he attended the lecture hall, continued his training, and provided lectures, literary discussions, and reading instruction to the children of the domain retainers

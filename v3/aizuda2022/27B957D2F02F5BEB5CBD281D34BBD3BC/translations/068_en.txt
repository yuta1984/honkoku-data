【Right Page】
He was privately granted one set of hemp kamishimo (formal court dress)

One: In the same month, he was granted a lined silk crepe under-kimono with family crests from the lord's wardrobe disposal

One: In the 2nd year of Meiwa (Year of the Rooster), 2nd month, he inherited the family headship and was granted a fief of 150 koku without change, and was appointed to continue serving as御祐筆 (secretary/scribe) as before

One: In the 10th month of the same year, he went up to Edo for guard duty, served one tour, and returned

One: In the 5th year of the same era (Year of the Rat), 9th month, he went up to Edo for guard duty

【Left Page】
One: In the 6th year of the same era (Year of the Ox), 4th month, although he was scheduled to be relieved, he was kept until the lord's return and appointed to handle lodging arrangements during the journey

One: In the 7th month of the same year, it was announced that he had diligently performed his assistant role in accompanying the lord in the field multiple times

One: In the same month, he returned accompanying the lord on his return journey

One: In the same month, for his diligent service in handling lodging arrangements during the lord's return journey, he was granted two pieces of silver

一　In the 1st month of An'ei 8 (Year of the Boar, 1779), he was permitted as requested to adopt Ichitarō, son of Ichijūrō who was the younger brother of Abei Matauemon, as his son-in-law and heir.
一　In the 6th month of the same year, his son Ichitarō was appointed as guard substitute as requested.
一　In the 10th month of Tenmei 1 (Year of the Ox, 1781), when unit head Sugita Gorōbei died of illness, he was transferred to Yamasaki Sasuke's unit as successor.
一　In the 7th month of Tenmei 4 (Year of the Dragon, 1784), his adopted son Yūuemon

一　In the same month, due to illness, he was permitted as requested to surrender his koshin stipend and take medical leave.
一　In the same month, he was permitted as requested to adopt Hanzaburō, second son of Takanashi Saburōsuke, as his son-in-law and heir.
一　In the 9th month of the same year, he was granted retirement as requested.
一　On the 21st day of the 8th month of Tenmei 5 (Year of the Snake, 1785), he died of illness at the age of seventy. His posthumous Buddhist name was Tanshōin Zuisen Ryōmyō Koji.

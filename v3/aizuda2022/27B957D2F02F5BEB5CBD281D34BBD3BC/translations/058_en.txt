【Right Page】
　　During the construction of the Shiba residence, it would not be proper to remain constantly stationed in Edo, so when going up to Edo at appropriate times, he should observe the progress of the construction and, if he had any opinions, should consult with the construction magistrate and might also give instructions to the construction officials, as was conveyed to him.

One: In the same month, two people were appointed as rotating head clerks (moto-shimeyaku), and it was ordered that they should carry out all duties as before, and should go up to serve regarding various official matters in Kyoto, Osaka, and Edo.

【Left Page】
One: In the ninth month of the same year, he went up to Edo on official business.

One: In the twelfth month of the same year, because he diligently carried out his official duties and handled all official matters without delay, Lord Tokuō (Matsudaira Katayoshi) was pleased to commend him, and therefore fifty koku was added to his official stipend as a direct reward from his lordship.

One: On the same day, regarding the matter of one volume of the social granary, permission was granted as requested for his position, and he was appointed to the jurisdiction of the district magistrate as per previous regulations. Due to his management up to this point, the actual stores of the social granary also increased...

One: In the 8th month of the same 13th year (Year of the Monkey), due to diligent performance as apprentice, granted seven koku and two-person stipend, and summoned to serve as administrative document clerk.
One: In the 2nd month of the same 17th year (Year of the Rat), as petitioned, relieved of official duties and appointed to inactive status.

• Same 12th year, 1st month: Granted an increase of stipend for 1 retainer
• Same 15th year, 12th month: Granted an increase of 2 koku
• Kyōhō 2, 10th month: Ordered to have an audience during the lord's passage

【Left page】

• Same year, 8th month: Also appointed to independent audience status, and in connection with this, ordered to attend during the new rice stockpiling period
• Same 3rd year, 9th month: For having served well, granted 1 ryō in gold
• Kyōhō 5, 3rd month: Appointed to the position of district affairs inspector
• Same 6th year, intercalary 7th month: Granted the former residence of Ishizuka Kikuemon

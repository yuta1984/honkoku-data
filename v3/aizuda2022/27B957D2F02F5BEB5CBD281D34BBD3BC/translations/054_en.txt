• In the 2nd month of the 14th year (Year of the Rooster): He went up to Edo for official business

• In the 3rd month of the same year: He worked day and night with utmost dedication managing the lord's private financial arrangements, and as a reward was given twenty ryō in gold

• In the 6th month of the same year: He went up to the Kansai region on official business

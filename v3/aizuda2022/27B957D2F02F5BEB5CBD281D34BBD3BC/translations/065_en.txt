- Hōreki 12th year (1762), intercalary 4th month:
　　Ordered to make a clean copy of the Historical Records of Lord Hōshō
　　During this recording work, he was appointed as temporary employee at the government office, so he was instructed to consult with the regular government office officials while recording, and completed the record by the 9th month of the same year
- Same year, 9th month: Ordered to make clean copies of the Historical Records. The clean copies recorded so far were sent to Edo for examination, and returned in the 4th month of the following Sheep year. From that time, he began work and together with the portions kept at hand, completed all three clean copies by the 8th month of the following Monkey year

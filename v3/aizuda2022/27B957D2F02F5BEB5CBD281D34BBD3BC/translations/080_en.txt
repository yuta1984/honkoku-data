《Sixth generation》
Sakaetaka Nobu　《Original name: Katsushirō. Born on July 27, Kansei 3 (1791)》
　　Biological father: Third son of Iida Kujūrō Fukun
　　Wife: Daughter of adoptive father Saichibē Takanori　《Died of illness on September 20, Bunka 9 (1812). Buddhist posthumous name: Shūun-in Hōshō Kangetsu Daishi. Burial place: Zuiun-zan Kōtoku-ji Temple》
　　　　Daughter of Aritake Gorōzaemon Takamasa《Born on June 25, Kansei 10 (1798)》
One　In the third month of Bunka 7 (1810), as requested by his father, he was granted
　　his first formal audience [with the lord]
[Left page]
One　In the tenth month of the same 8th year (1811), he was appointed
　　to assistant instructor at the domain school
One　In the third month of the same 10th year (1813), since his father Saichibē was appointed
　　as construction magistrate for Inawashiro and moved to Inawashiro,
　　he petitioned to be relieved of his assistant instructor duties,
　　and his request was granted
One　In the seventh month of the same year, he was appointed
　　assistant instructor at Inawashiro Academy
One　In the intercalary eleventh month of the same year, due to changes
　　in the school system, he was relieved of his assistant instructor duties at Inawashiro Academy

【Right Page】
1. In the 5th month of the 8th year of the Boar, he was appointed to concurrently serve as magistrate (daikan)
1. In the 9th year of the Rat (month unclear), he petitioned to be relieved of his concurrent magistrate duties, but was ordered to continue serving for a while longer
1. In the 3rd month of the 11th year of the Tiger, as requested, he was relieved of his concurrent magistrate duties
1. In the 10th month of the 14th year of the Snake, he went up to Edo for guard duty service and completed one tour of duty before returning
　　　Note: He served one additional tour after this
【Left Page】
1. In the 12th month of the 13th year of the Dragon, his request to change his name to Iemon was granted
1. In the 11th month of Hōei 4th year of the Boar, his request to adopt Miki, the second son of Endō Koemon, a retainer of Lord Kuze Yamato-no-kami, was granted
1. On the 17th day of the 6th month of Shōtoku 2nd year of the Dragon, he died of illness at age 53, posthumous Buddhist name Shinshū Ryōtetsu Shinji, officiant, burial place same as above

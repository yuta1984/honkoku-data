　　Appointed as weaponry official
- In the 11th year, 12th month: His petition to adopt Sakujiro, the second son of Tochigi Kanroku, as son-in-law was granted
- In the 12th year, 3rd month: His seating rank was placed next to the Ginmi-sho reform officials
- Same month: Permission granted upon petition to wear ceremonial garments with the warabi-te (bracken frond) crest
- Meiwa 1st year (1764), 5th month: His stipend was confiscated due to negligence in the theft of lead, salt, and saltpeter from the storehouse
- Same 5th year, 3rd month: By lord's grace, granted a two-person stipend

[Left page]
　　during Lord Ryōtokuin's meeting days, there were scheduling conflicts with Lord Sadaaki's duties, and moreover, it came to the lord's attention that he had not yet recovered his ability to walk properly after his illness, making regular service difficult. Therefore, he was ordered to serve Lord Ryōtokuin's instruction duties on a temporary/substitute basis.
- Kansei 5th year (1793), 1st month: He was granted retirement as requested.
- Same year, 7th month, 6th day: He died of illness at age 60. His posthumous Buddhist name was Shūgenin Fui Kōgetsu Koji. He was buried in Musashi Province.

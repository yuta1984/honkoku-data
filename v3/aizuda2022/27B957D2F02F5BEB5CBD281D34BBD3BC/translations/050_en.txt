Saichibei Keichū　《Originally named Gonzaburō, born in Genroku 13 (Year of the Dragon)》
　　Mother: Daughter of the Yamakawa clan
[Left page]
　　Wife: Daughter of Saku Gyōbu-shōyū Hōtō《Died of illness on the 5th day of 10th month of Meiwa 8, Buddhist name: Senshima-in Hōun Myōhō Daishi, burial site same as above》
• In Kan'ei 2 (Year of the Rooster), 3rd month: Appointed as apprentice clerk-accountant at the district office
• Same year, 5th month: Granted 7 koku 2 to 2 ninbuchi stipend and summoned as clerk under the district magistrate
• In Shōtoku 5 (Year of the Sheep), 6th month: Given one ryō of gold as reward for good service
• In Kyōhō 3, Year of the Dog, 1st month: Granted an increase of 1 koku 5 to for diligent service

One: In the same 7th year (year of the Tiger), 3rd month, an increase of three koku was granted.

One: In the same 9th year (year of the Dragon), 3rd month, having served diligently for several years with great effort, although matters of landholding grants are of great importance, his son's provisional assignment was combined with his own provisional assignment, and a direct grant of 100 koku landholding was bestowed. Direct grants of landholding are not easily bestowed, but since Saichihei also serves well, it was announced that this was granted for both reasons.

One: In the same 10th year (year of the Snake), 6th month, having served well in district administration training, although matters of rank are of great importance, by special favor he was appointed to the position next to the head surveyor

[Left page]
among the land survey officials.

One: In the same 12th year (year of the Sheep), 2nd month, due to illness, upon his request he was relieved of his official duties, and he privately instructed his son Saichihei in official matters so that he could perform the duties. Since he had not yet reached seventy years of age, retirement was not formally granted, and his son Saichihei was appointed to serve in a deputy capacity.

One: In the same 13th year (year of the Monkey), 8th month, the former residence of Shibuya Ryōtetsu was granted to him.

One: In the same 14th year (year of the Rooster), 5th month, as district magistrate, retirement by internal petition.

Fourth son of Hinata Saburōemon Tsugimasa
Hinata Eji Masatada
　Mother: Daughter of Kitahara Uneme Mitsuyoshi
　Wife: Daughter of a certain Nakano who served as rusui (castle keeper)

• In the 2nd year of Hōreki, year of the monkey (1752), at the end of the 9th month, he was granted 8 ryō in stipend money and provisions for 4 people, and was summoned to serve as a page (okoshō)
• In the same month, he went up to Edo to serve his turn of duty (kinban)

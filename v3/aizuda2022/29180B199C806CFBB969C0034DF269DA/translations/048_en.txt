1. In the 12th month of Bunka 11, Year of the Dog, he excelled in his studies and was noted for his skills being superior to his age, so as a reward he was granted the books "Kōkyō Taigi" (Great Meanings of the Classic of Filial Piety), "Shisho Shūchū" (Collected Commentaries on the Four Books), and "Shōgaku" (Elementary Learning).

1. In the 3rd month of the same 12th year, Year of the Boar, there was an enormous amount of debris and refuse in the castle, and he requested to be entrusted with cleaning it up before the lord's arrival, willing to undertake even menial tasks.

1. In the 8th month of the same year, he was appointed as a companion to Lord Kinbun, and for this service he was granted an annual stipend of 5 ryō of gold without interest as expense support.
   Note: During this service, he received various gifts directly from the lord's hand, accompanied the lord on outings, and was frequently granted sake.

1. In the 1st month of the same 13th year, Year of the Rat, regarding the previous year's preparations for the lord's arrival, when cleaning the castle interior, he came out during breaks from his various arts training to perform even menial work, which was considered an admirable act and was officially commended.

1. In the 6th month of the same 14th year, Year of the Ox, as requested by his father, he was granted his first formal audience with the lord.

1. In the 2nd month of the same 15th year, Year of the Tiger, he excelled in calligraphy studies beyond his age...

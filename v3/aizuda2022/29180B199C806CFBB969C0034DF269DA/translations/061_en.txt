1. Kansei 1, year of the rooster, seventh month: Two persons' stipend was reduced from his late father's support allowance, eleven persons' stipend was granted and he was summoned. His seat was placed above the entertainers, and he was ordered to continue serving as head lecturer as before.
　　However, the two persons' stipend for practice fees that had been granted until then was withdrawn.

1. Same year, eighth month: As he was ordered to teach academics to the young pages, he was appointed to the role of instructor.

1. Same year, eleventh month: He was ordered to serve as headmaster of both schools, and for this reason it was decreed that three ryō of gold each would be granted from the lecture hall funds.

1. Same year, twelfth month: As the entertainers were assigned to the former junior guard seats, he was placed above the seat entertainers with his previous rank.

1. Same 3rd year, year of the boar, seventh month: The seat for which he was appointed as Confucian scholar was above Kagaki Muragen Genji of Lord Sadaaki's retinue, and next above the unemployed Sakurai Yahei.

1. Same 4th year, year of the rat, second month: He petitioned that he wished to go up to Edo to pursue academic training and requested a three-year leave of absence.

Shōzaemon Fusauji, originally named Satarō, then Hikotarō, then Tōdayū
　Mother: daughter of the Harada family
　Wife: daughter of Suzuki Unsetsu Inshin

1. Hōreki 9 (1759), Year of the Rabbit, 9th month: As his father requested, he was granted his first audience with the lord
1. An'ei 4 (1775), Year of the Sheep, 4th month: As his father requested, he was appointed as a guard substitute and served castle guard duty in Tanaka Kahei's unit
1. Tenmei 2 (1782), Year of the Tiger, 10th month: He worked diligently in guard duties and achieved perfect attendance for his unit for two consecutive years, which was deemed admirable and announced by his lord
1. Same 8th year (1788), Year of the Monkey, 2nd month: Family succession - he was granted a fief of 200 koku without change
1. Kansei 1 (1789), Year of the Rooster, intercalary 6th month: He was transferred to the first unit attached to the senior retainer
1. Same 4th year (1792), Year of the Rat, 3rd month: He participated in a hunting expedition
　Note: He participated twice more after this
1. Same month: This being the beginning of the hunting expedition, all units performed well

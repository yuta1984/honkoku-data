• Same 2nd year (1805), Year of the Ox, 12th month: For his diligent effort in spear technique with good skill, paying attention to his duties as instructor, and handling many students well, he was granted two silver coins as a reward

• Same 4th year (1807), Year of the Rabbit, 5th month: Was appointed to concurrent supervision of ashigaru foot soldiers

【• Same year, 6th month: Was released from spear technique instructor duties as requested】

• Same year, 9th month: Attended the horse parade at Hongōhara and received game as a gift
However, after this, he attended bird hunting once and received game as a gift

• Same 6th year (1809), Year of the Snake, 4th month: Was appointed as personal attendant

• Same 7th year (1810), Year of the Horse, 4th month: Returned from service as personal attendant
However, in the same month, although the number of people for the lord's journey was reduced, it was declared that he served diligently without absence

• Same year, 10th month: Went up to Edo for garrison duty and returned in the 4th month of the following Year of the Sheep
However, it was declared that his continued diligent service was considered exemplary

• Same 10th year (1817), Year of the Rooster, 3rd month: His request to make Sengō, the actual son of his elder brother Kuranoshin, his heir was granted

• Same 11th year (1818), Year of the Dog, 4th month: Went up to Edo for garrison duty

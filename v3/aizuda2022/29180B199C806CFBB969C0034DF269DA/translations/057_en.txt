[Second wife:] Daughter of Hashizume Gendō Yukinori

1. In the 11th month of Hōreki 4 (Year of the Dog, 1754), because he diligently applied himself to learning, he was granted a stipend of three-person rations as study allowance from the lecture hall funds.

1. In the 2nd month of Meiwa 2 (Year of the Rooster, 1765), two-person rations were reduced from his late father's stipend allocation, and he was granted thirteen-person rations. He was summoned and assigned a seat above the entertainers, and was ordered to provide reading instruction at the lecture hall.
   However, the three-person rations study allowance that had been granted until now was withdrawn.

1. In the 1st month of Meiwa 4 (Year of the Boar, 1767), he was ordered to read aloud the family precepts at the meeting hall.
   He was subsequently ordered to read them aloud repeatedly.

1. In the 10th month of Meiwa 7 (Year of the Dog, 1770), he was ordered to go to Inawashiro and give lectures at the castle deputy's residence.
   He subsequently went there repeatedly to give lectures.

1. In the 4th month of An'ei 5 (Year of the Horse, 1776), his wish to adopt Denzō, the third son of Yokochi Sukegorō, as son-in-law and heir was granted.

1. In the 2nd month of An'ei 9 (Year of the Rat, 1780), his wish to change his name to Iemon was granted.

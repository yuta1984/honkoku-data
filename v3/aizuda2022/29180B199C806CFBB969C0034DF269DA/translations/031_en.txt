• In the first month of the same twelfth (Monkey) year, after Hara Kakunosuke was appointed to official duties in the Kamigata region and departed, he was ordered to supplement and serve as supervisor of school construction.
Note: Thereafter, he continued to serve in the same manner as ordered.

• In the eleventh month of the same year, regarding the recent case of thieves breaking into the storehouse of the rice circulation offices under the chief administrator, he was appointed as additional district magistrate for domain granaries to investigate this matter, and was ordered to serve specifically for this investigative duty.

• In the twelfth month of the same year, since his main duties and responsibilities for domestic product promotion were reportedly numerous, he was informed that it was not necessary for him to continue serving concurrently as mountain magistrate at that time.

• In the first month of the first year of Kyōwa (Rooster year), since two people were now appointed as mountain magistrates, he was relieved of his concurrent duties as mountain magistrate.

• In the tenth month of the same year, the sake produced by the brewing department turned out excellently and was consistently served during the lord's residence in the domain and was also suitable for carrying during his travels. Since he had provided good guidance from the beginning, he was privately granted sake.

1. Same year, 12th month: Was granted permission to change his name to Shōzaemon as requested
1. Same 4th year (1747), Year of the Rabbit, 6th month: Departed for Edo for emergency guard duty, returned in the 10th month of the same year
　　Note: After this, he served one tour of emergency guard duty
1. Hōreki 1 (1751), Year of the Sheep, 6th month: When his superior Miyake Magobei was transferred, he was assigned to Yanase Kyūma's unit as his successor
1. Same 12th year (1762), Year of the Horse, 1st month: When his superior Yanase Kyūma was transferred, he was assigned to Yamazaki Sasuke's unit as his successor
1. Meiwa 1 (1764), Year of the Monkey, 1st month: Was granted permission to change his name to Tōzaemon as requested
1. Same 2nd year (1765), Year of the Rooster, 3rd month: Went to Yamagata for guard duty, returned in the 9th month of the same year
1. Same 8th year (1771), Year of the Rabbit, 2nd month: When his superior Yamazaki Sasuke became a guard captain, he was assigned to Tanaka Kahei's unit as his successor
1. An'ei 4 (1775), Year of the Sheep, 4th month: As requested, his son Tōdayū was appointed as guard captain
1. Tenmei 8 (1788), Year of the Monkey, 2nd month: Was granted retirement as requested
1. Kansei 11 (1799), Year of the Sheep, 4th month, 10th day: At the age of 77

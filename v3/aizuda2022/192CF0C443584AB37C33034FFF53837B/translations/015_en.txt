Tsuneemon Tsunetsune　《original name Dengo, born Tenmei 1 (1781), year of the Ox, 8th month, 12th day》
　Mother　same as above
　Wife　daughter of Nagase Shoemon Gamy̌o

1　Kansei 12 (1800), year of the Monkey, 9th month: from his late father's stipend allocation,
　　two koku were reduced and eleven koku plus three-person support allowance were granted, he was
　　summoned as an attached member of the outer retainer group, and since various
　　duties were not assigned due to his training in the arts, he was ordered
　　to pay the koshin (construction reserve) fee
　　　Note: he was stationed in Miyake Hannosuke's unit
1　Same year, 10th month: his request to change his name
　　to Tsuneemon was granted
1　Same year, 12th month: having heard that he had devoted himself to martial arts and
　　mastered superior techniques, his koshin fee was exempted and he was
　　ordered to report for duty, serving as castle guard

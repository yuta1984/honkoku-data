　　　Genealogy of Fujisawa Daihachirō《Family name: Fujiwara｜Home province: Shinano》　《Primary crest: Single turtle shell｜Alternative crest: Wisteria tomoe》
Fujisawa Sakuzaemon (second son of Hoshina Hachirōzaemon)
　Mother: Daughter of Sakai Seizaemon
　Wife: Daughter of Kambayashi Hikoemon (retainer of Takeda Shingen)《Died of illness on the 21st day of the 3rd month, 3rd year of Kan'ei, year of the Tiger (1626). Buddhist name: Juryō Myōshun Shinnyo. Burial place: Outside the temple grounds at Takato in Shinano Province》
　　　The ancestors held the village of Fujisawa in Shinano Province for generations,
　　　and took the surname Fujisawa from the place name where they resided

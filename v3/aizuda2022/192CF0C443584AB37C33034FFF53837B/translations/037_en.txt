- In the 4th year (1687), 3rd month, he went up to Edo as a guard duty officer, and in the same year's intercalary 8th month, he returned accompanying his lord.

- In the 8th year (1691), 6th month, he went up to Edo to return as a welcoming party.

- In the 10th year (1693), 9th month, he was appointed as acting district magistrate and served in that capacity.

- In the 11th year (1694), 5th month, he went up to Edo to return as an escort.

- In the 12th year (1695), 8th month, he was appointed as acting district magistrate and served in that capacity.

- In the 13th year (1696), 2nd month, as requested, his son Nuinosuke was appointed as his substitute guard.

- In Hōei 1st year (1704), 6th month, 23rd day, he died of illness at age 70. His Buddhist name was Tetsugaku Sōsen, buried in the same location as mentioned above.

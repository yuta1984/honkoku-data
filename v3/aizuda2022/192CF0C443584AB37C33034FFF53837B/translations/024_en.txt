1　Same 5th year, year of the Ox (1793), 2nd month: for many years of dedicated and diligent service, he was appointed to concurrently serve as domain storehouse intermediary official and accounting official, his seat was placed above the ladies-in-waiting attendants, and his official stipend of 1 koku 2 to 8 shō was continued as before

1　Same 6th year, year of the Tiger (1794), 1st month: regarding the difficult matter of arrears in tenant rice payments for agricultural lands under Ojiya administration, where there were troublesome inquiries, for serving diligently and faithfully from beginning to end, he was granted 3 ryō of gold as a reward

1　Same year, 5th month: for diligent service, and particularly for his dedicated work regarding the recent internal consultation matter concerning the reassignment of administrative territories within the domain's administered lands, he was granted a stipend of one person's food allowance

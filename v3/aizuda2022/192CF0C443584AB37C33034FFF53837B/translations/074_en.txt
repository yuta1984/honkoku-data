One　Same 2nd year, Year of the Rabbit (1819), 8th month　Due to extraordinary financial difficulties, he was appointed to emergency
　　procurement duties, went to the Ojiya area, and arranged 2,200 ryō
　　of gold with 25 ryō interest, arranging for funds to be sent up from that location

One　Same year, during 8th month　Was appointed to inspect villages in Echigo domain territories
　　and departed, returning in the 9th month of the same year

One　Same year, 11th month　Regarding accounting and revenue inquiry duties,
　　he was appointed as temporary accounting official inspector,
　　served at the capital, and returned
　　in the 1st month of the following year

One　Same 3rd year, Year of the Dragon (1820), 1st month　Due to reassignment considerations, his current duties were transferred
　　and he was relieved, placed in the third council group attached to the chief retainer, and it was
　　proclaimed that he had served diligently
　　up to this point

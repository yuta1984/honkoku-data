One　In the same month, submitted for official use: diagrams of military preparations, diagrams of troop movements, calculations for emergency forces in Edo, and military equipment records - five volumes in total, plus one volume of personal observations.

One　In the same 5th year (1776), 4th month, during the Nikko shrine pilgrimage, went up to Edo on official business and returned in the 5th month.
　　However, for performing the aforementioned duties in Edo, was granted one set of hemp formal wear.

One　In the same year, 7th month, was appointed as magistrate of legal affairs (kuji-bugyō), while continuing to serve concurrently in military affairs as before.

One　In the same month, petitioned regarding the matter of boar insignia, and the petition was granted due to special circumstances.

One　In the same 6th year (1777), 12th month, when there were over forty cases requiring investigation, worked diligently day and night with full dedication to examine and investigate them thoroughly, and was granted three bundles of cotton as a reward.

One　In the same 7th year (1778), 3rd month, there was an official investigation, and [the subject] committed suicide while in custody, resulting in the confiscation of his stipend. His posthumous Buddhist name was Daisai Zensō, and his burial place was the same as above.

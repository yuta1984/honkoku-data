1. Kansei 1, Year of the Rooster, 4th month (April 1789): His request to change his name to Tōshichi was granted.

1. Kansei 3, Year of the Boar, 1st month (January 1791): He was appointed to provide instruction in horsemanship.

1. Same year, 3rd month (March 1791): His request to return his adopted son Umanosuke to his original family due to misconduct was granted.

1. Kansei 4, Year of the Rat, 4th month (April 1792): His request to take Eizō, younger brother of Miyashita Sadaemon, as an adopted son-in-law was granted.

1. Bunka 3, Year of the Tiger, 4th month (April 1806): As requested, he was elevated to the position of literary assistant (gisaku).

1. Bunpō 1, Year of the Tiger, 12th month, 16th day (December 16, 1818): He died of illness at the age of seventy-six. His posthumous Buddhist name was Banshō-gaku Sōju Koji, buried at Tokorokami.

Gintarō　　　　Born on the 20th day of the 10th month of Kyōhō 1 (1716), year of the monkey. Died of illness on the 22nd day of the 7th month of Kyōhō 3 (1718), year of the dog, at age 3. Buddhist posthumous name: Shakujōen. Burial place: Shōryūji Temple on Mount Shōun.

　Mother: Daughter of the Saiin family

Daughter　　　Born on the last day of the 9th month of Kyōhō 3 (1718), year of the dog. Died of illness on the 2nd day of the 6th month of Kyōhō 6 (1721), year of the ox, at age 4. Buddhist posthumous name: Myōyo Dōjo. Burial place: Daihōji Temple on Mount Myōryō.

　Mother: same as above

Zenji　　　　　Born on the 24th day of the 2nd month of Kyōhō 7 (1722), year of the tiger. Died of illness on the 12th day of the 8th month of Kyōhō 10 (1725), year of the snake, at age 4. Buddhist posthumous name: Kanryō Dōji. Burial place: unknown.

　Mother: same as above

Daughter　Born on the 27th day of the 6th month of Kyōhō 11 (1726), year of the horse. Became wife of Hasegawa Shinbei Shigeuji but divorced. Later became wife of Harada Gonji Tanetaka.

　Mother: same as above

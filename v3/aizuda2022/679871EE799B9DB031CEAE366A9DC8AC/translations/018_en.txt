Daughter of Kamiyama Saibē (name unknown)　Died of illness on the 26th day of the 11th month of Genroku 13 (1700), year of the dragon
　　　　　　　　　　　Buddhist name: Nun Juei, burial place: Hōfuku-ji temple at Nakashimayama

1. In Kanbun 5 (1665), year of the snake, he was granted the hereditary stipend of 200 koku without change, and served castle guard duty in Ibuka Shirōbē's unit.

1. In the same 17th year (1677), year of the sheep, 6th month, when the head Ibuka Shirōbē died of illness, he was transferred to Ikoma Gobē's unit as his successor in position.

1. In the same 10th year (1670), year of the dog, 8th month, he went up for attendant guard duty and returned in the 2nd month of the following year of the boar.

1. In Enpō 6 (1678), year of the horse, 10th month, he went up to Edo for guard duty and returned in the 4th month of the following year of the sheep.

1. In Tenna 2 (1682), year of the dog, 12th month, when the head Ikoma Gobē died of illness, he was transferred to Ichinose Denbē's unit as his successor in position.

1. In the same 3rd year (1683), year of the boar, 12th month, he petitioned to adopt his nephew Nezu Yahē's third son Isoemon as son-in-law and heir, and when this was granted, he went up to Edo to express gratitude and had an audience with his lord.

1. In Jōkyō 3 (1686), year of the tiger, 8th month, 9th day, he died of illness at age 56. Buddhist name: Koyo Den'ei, burial place: same as above.

　　Chūbē (name unknown)　Died of illness on the 15th day of the 6th month of Tenna 1 (1681), year of the rooster, Buddhist name: Gyokuhō Teigin, burial place: Jōan-ji temple at Kodateyama

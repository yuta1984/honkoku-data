1. In the 6th month of the same 7th year (1810), year of the horse, because he served guard duty at the Minamimachi-guchi Arsenal without incident, he was privately granted sake.

1. In the 7th month of the same year, when the lord moved to his new residence, it was ordered that in case of fire emergencies, six comrades at a time should be stationed, and should natural evacuation become necessary, they should serve as his escort.

1. In the 12th month of the same 8th year (1811), year of the sheep, he was appointed to the New Guard Unit (Shinban-gumi).

1. In the 4th month of the same 9th year (1812), year of the monkey, he was appointed as temporary officer in charge of the Kamiishi-gata artillery unit.
   Note: In connection with this appointment, he was exempted from shrine fire-watch duty when the lord was in the domain.

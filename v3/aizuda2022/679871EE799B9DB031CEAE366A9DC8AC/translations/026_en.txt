1. In the 12th month of Tenmei 1 (1781), year of the ox, one koku was added directly to his current service allowance of 2 koku as an increase.

1. In the 3rd month of the same 6th year (1786), year of the horse, he went up to Edo for garrison duty and returned in the 4th month of the following year of the sheep.

1. In the 7th month of the same year, regarding the invitation for the current 9th month, he was ordered to arrange without delay for the retainer attendants, monk attendants, food service staff, and various other matters.

1. In the 12th month of the same 8th year (1788), year of the monkey, he was granted 200 pieces of gold as a reward.

1. In the 3rd month of Kansei 2 (1790), year of the dog, he was granted 300 pieces of gold as a reward.

1. In the 5th month of the same 5th year (1793), year of the ox, he was granted a service allowance of rations for one person.

1. In the 3rd month of the same 8th year (1796), year of the dragon, his rank was appointed above that of御膳頭 (Gozenno-kami).

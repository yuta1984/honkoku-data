One: Same year, 11th month - For skillfully instructing in gunnery, he was granted sake.

One: Same 9th (Rat) year, 7th month - Regarding Kamiya Ruizaemon's residence, as per his petition, the residence was granted to him, with half being bestowed.

One: Tenmei 6th (Horse) year, 9th month - Since Kōno Gonhachi was going up to Edo for guard duty, he was entrusted with the firearms and military equipment that had been in Gonhachi's care, and was ordered to provide instruction as well.
　　However, the following (Sheep) year, 7th month - When Gonhachi returned, all the aforementioned military equipment was handed over.

One: Same 8th (Monkey) year, 12th month - For many years training many disciples well and dedicating himself to martial arts, mastering the Kyū-ryū school of gunnery, this was considered remarkable and worthy of recognition. As a reward, he was granted two pieces of silver.

One: Kansei 1st (Rooster) year, 7th month, 16th day - Died of illness at age 60. Buddhist name: Genri-in Ryūshō Koji. Burial place: same as above.

Adopted son:
　Shichizō Yoshitatsu - Returned to his birth family for consultation

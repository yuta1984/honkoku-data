Request granted to adopt second son Seiji as heir
1. Same 10th year (Kyōhō 10, 1725), first month, 18th day: Died of illness at age 62. Buddhist name: Shungyōin Yūkan Koji. Burial place: Myōryōzan

Seiji Mitsushige

Actual father: Kamiya Ruizaemon Katsutaka, his second son

1. Kyōhō 10 (1725), 4th month: Granted standard stipend and summoned to serve as a firearms expert, succeeding his father
1. Same 16th year (Kyōhō 16, 1731), 3rd month: During a fire that spread to nearby buildings, it was announced that the firearms entrusted to his care did not burn
1. Enkyō 3 (1746), 1st month: When ordered to receive armor equipment, appointed as assistant guard
   However, he was appointed to the same position again in Kan'en 3 (1750)
   Hōreki 2 (1752), 3rd month: His actual elder brother Kamiya Shirō

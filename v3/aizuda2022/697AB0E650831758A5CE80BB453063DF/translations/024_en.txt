一　In Genroku 4, Year of the Sheep (month unknown), he was ordered to shave his head and changed his name to Matsui.
一　In the same year (month unknown), he was appointed as御側医 (personal physician to the lord).
一　In the 2nd month of Genroku 7, Year of the Dog, as requested, he went up to the Kyoto-Osaka region for medical treatment. His official travel pass was confiscated, but his stipend allowance continued to be granted as before.
　　However, the year and month of his return are unknown.
一　In the 3rd month of Genroku 9, Year of the Rat, he was granted his original stipend of 25 koku and allowances for five attendants.
一　In the same month, he petitioned that his father Ikeiin's headship be transferred to his younger brother Gisaemon, and this was granted.
一　In the 2nd month of Genroku 11, Year of the Tiger, he was appointed to permanent duty in Edo.
一　In the 5th month of Genroku 12, Year of the Rabbit, it came to the lord's attention that he had leaked matters concerning the lord's personal affairs and the women's quarters to others. This was considered extremely improper, and although he should have been severely punished, by the lord's mercy he was sent back to Aizu and ordered to return immediately and remain in house confinement.

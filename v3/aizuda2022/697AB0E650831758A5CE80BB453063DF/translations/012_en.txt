一　In the 14th year, year of the Ox 《marginal note: month unknown》, when head Kayano Gonbee was dismissed from his post,
　　he was transferred to Ueda Hachirōemon's unit as successor.

一　In Hōei 1, year of the Monkey, 4th month, he went up to Edo
　　for guard duty rotation and returned in the 10th month of the same year.
一　In the 3rd year, year of the Dog, 1st month, it was proclaimed that he had
　　served guard duty diligently for several years without negligence.
一　In the 4th year, year of the Boar, 12th month, because he did not receive
　　the general relief aid, he was granted one silver piece
　　as a reward.


一　In Shōtoku 4, year of the Horse, 3rd month, his request to adopt
　　Heisuke, son of Ikegami Magoemon, was approved.
一　In Kyōhō 7, year of the Tiger, 8th month, when head Ueda Hachirōemon
　　retired, he was transferred to Ichise Denbee's unit as successor.
一　In the 9th year, year of the Dragon, 2nd month, as requested, his son Heisuke was
　　appointed as substitute guard.
一　In the 18th year, year of the Ox, 8th month, 10th day, he died of illness
　　at the age of 79. Buddhist name: Emyōin Chidō, burial place same as above.

Shin'nosuke Satotoshi 《marginal note: original name Jirōsaburō｜born in Meireki 1, year of the Sheep》
　　　Mother: daughter of the Kasuga clan
　　　Wife: daughter of Hara Gendayū Tanetoshi - divorced
　　　　　daughter of Ōhō Hayato Naoaki 《marginal note: died of illness on the 6th day of 2nd month, Shōtoku 4, year of the Horse, Buddhist name｜Sokujōin Myōshin Shinnyo, burial place same as above》
　　　Concubine: family name unknown

　一　In the intercalary 1st month of Genroku 2, year of the Snake, as his father requested, he was
　　　appointed as substitute guard and served in castle
　　　guard duty under Hyūga Saburōemon's unit.
　一　In the 3rd year, year of the Horse 《marginal note: month unknown》, when the head Hyūga Saburōemon was dismissed from his post,


　　　he was transferred to Hori Chūzaemon's unit as successor.
　一　In the 6th year, year of the Rooster, 6th month, when head Hori Chūzaemon was banished,
　　　he was transferred to Numasawa Gonbee's unit as successor.
　一　In the 7th year, year of the Dog 《marginal note: month unknown》, when Numasawa Gonbee was dismissed from his post,
　　　he was transferred to Ueda Hachirōemon's unit as successor.
　一　In the 9th year, year of the Rat, 3rd month, he inherited the family headship and was granted
　　　a fief of 100 koku without any changes.
　一　In the 12th year, year of the Rabbit, 10th month, he was summoned back to Wakamatsu, appointed to
　　　an outside unit assignment, joined Kayano Gonbee's unit, and
　　　served in castle guard duty.

One　9th month of the 2nd year (1705), Year of the Rooster　Since father Denbei had served faithfully for many years without incident, by his lord's benevolence toward the Denbei family, he was granted a stipend certificate for 25 koku and provisions for 4 men
　One　4th month of the 3rd year (1706), Year of the Dog　Went up to Edo for guard duty service, returned in the 10th month of the same year
　One　4th month of the 4th year (1707), Year of the Boar　Went up to return for the lord's reception, came down in his service
　One　6th month of Shotoku 1 (1711), Year of the Rabbit　Was granted the former residence of Machida Katsunosuke
　One　12th month of the 3rd year (1713), Year of the Snake　Was appointed to gate guard duty


　One　4th month of the 4th year (1714), Year of the Horse　Went up for guard duty service, returned in the 10th month of the same year
　One　6th month of the same year　Was granted one set of hemp formal wear
　One　4th month of the 5th year (1715), Year of the Sheep　Went up to return for the lord's reception, came down in his service
　One　6th month of Kyoho 1 (1716), Year of the Monkey　Went up to Edo for guard duty service, returned in the 10th month of the same year
　　　　However, since he performed his lodging arrangements during the campaign without delay, he was granted one piece of silver
　One　3rd month of the 2nd year (1717), Year of the Rooster　Was appointed to the next guard shift

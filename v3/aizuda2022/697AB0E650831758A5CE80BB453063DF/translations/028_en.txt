一　In the 9th month of Enpō 2, Year of the Ox, his son Hanbē died of illness, so he petitioned to make his legitimate grandson Ōkura his heir, which was granted.

一　In the intercalary 12th month of the same year, as requested, his son Ōkura was appointed as guard substitute.

一　On the 20th day of the 8th month of Hōreki 12, Year of the Horse, he died of illness at the age of 74. His posthumous Buddhist name was Rekikyaku-in Dōken Koji, burial place same as above.

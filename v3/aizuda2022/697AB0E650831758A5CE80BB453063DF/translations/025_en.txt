一　In the 9th month of the 14th year, Year of the Snake, he petitioned to submit a treatise on medical practice, which was granted.
一　In the 3rd month of Hōei 5, Year of the Rat, he was granted a fief of 100 koku and was reappointed as personal physician as before.
一　In the 12th month of Shōtoku 1, Year of the Rabbit, as requested, he was granted permission to retire. His fief of 100 koku was transferred without change to his son Denemon, and he (Matsui) was ordered to continue serving as before.

一　In the 2nd month of the 3rd year, Year of the Snake, as requested, he was exempted from both castle guard duty and monthly rotation duty.
一　On the 1st day of the 6th month of Kyōhō 14, Year of the Rooster, he died of illness at the age of 78. His posthumous Buddhist name was Kijuin Ryōzan Chisoku Koji, and he was buried at Banshōzan Tenneiji Temple.

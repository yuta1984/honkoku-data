- Same year, twelfth month: For diligent work in his family profession and having audience with the government authorities with successful medical treatments, ten pieces of silver were granted.

- Same 8, Kanoto-hitsuji year (1811), second month: When Lord Kyōtei was indisposed, he was ordered to provide medical treatment and presented medicine of his own judgment, whereupon His Lordship made a complete recovery.

- Same 9, Mizunoe-saru year (1812), third month, eighteenth day: Died of illness. Dharma name: Chisen'in Hōi Nichigon. Burial place: Honpōji Temple, Asakusa.

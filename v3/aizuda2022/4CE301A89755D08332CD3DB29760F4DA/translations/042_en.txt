- Same year, ninth month: Having diligently served for many years and performing the duties for the domain inspectors without delay, he was granted an increase of two koku and one-person rice allowance

- Meiwa 4, Kinoto-i year (1767), fourth month: Appointed to accompany his lord on the journey down from Edo

- Same year 8, Kinoto-u year (1771), sixth month: Ordered to move to the upper residence and serve there during his lord's absence, serving until the ninth month of the same year

- An'ei 1, Tsuchinoe-tatsu year (1772), eleventh month, twenty-eighth day: Died of illness at the age of seventy-one. Buddhist name: Saishin-in Jikan Nichitai. Burial place:

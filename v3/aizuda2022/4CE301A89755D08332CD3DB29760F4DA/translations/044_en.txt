- Same year 9, Kanoe-ne year (1780), first month: Appointed as regular attending physician,

- Tenmei 1, Kanoto-ushi year (1781), tenth month: In recognition of his diligent service in medical duties,
　　he was granted five ryō annually for medicinal expenses
　　
- Same year 2, Mizunoe-tora year (1782), first month: Though it had been only a short time since his appointment as attending physician,

　　he showed particular dedication to the healthcare of his superiors and also widely provided
　　medical treatment to others, therefore
　　by special consideration he was granted a crested haori jacket

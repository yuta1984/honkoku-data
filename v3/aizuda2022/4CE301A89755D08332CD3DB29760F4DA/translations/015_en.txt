Genealogy of Ishii Gonuemon　Surname: Fujiwara, Family crest: Three interlocked rings in a circle, Home province: Aizu, Alternative crest: Nine luminaries star

Son of a certain Sōgen
Ishii Mozaemon Yoshimasa　Original name: Sanzaburō, born during the Kan'ei era (1624-1644)
　Mother　Family name unknown　　Died of illness on the 26th day of the 4th month of Tenna 3, Year of the Boar (1683), Buddhist name: Shinteiin Shakuni Jōun[?] Haku Daishi, buried at Manpukuji Temple on Mount Nakajima
　Wife　Family name unknown　　Died of illness on the 13th day of the 1st month of Kyōhō 10, Year of the Snake (1725), Buddhist name: Kōkyōin Shakuni Ichiyō Myōhō Daishi, burial place same as above
　　His father Sōgen accompanied Lord Katō Samanosuke Yoshiaki when he entered Aizu domain from Matsuyama in Iyo Province

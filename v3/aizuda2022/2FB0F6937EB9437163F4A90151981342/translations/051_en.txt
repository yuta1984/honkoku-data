One entry: Died of illness on the 26th day of the 9th month of Genbun 2 (1737). Posthumous Buddhist name: Seishin-in Shōyo Dōsen Shinshi. Place of burial: Kōdai-in temple, Mita Jumokutani, Edo.

Daughter: Kayo — Wife of Hirose Kanshi Katsumi, a retainer of Lord Ōkubo Hikozaemon.
　　　　　　Her mother was a daughter of the Kondō clan.

Daughter: Tsuyo — Wife of Tominaga Jūuemon Naotora, a retainer of Lord Matsudaira Tonomo-no-kami.
　　　　　　Her mother was the same as above.

Zenjūrō Shigetaka — Born in Shōtoku 1, the year of the Rabbit (1711).
　　The second son of Hirayama Kinbei Shigenori, in reality.
　　A retainer of Lord Naitō Geki.
　　Wife: A daughter of Fukaya Danuemon (personal name unknown).
　　　　　Died of illness on the 16th day of the 9th month of Kōwa 3, the year of the Boar (1803). Posthumous Buddhist name: Teiju-in Eiyō Chihō Shinnyō. Place of burial: Kōdai-in temple, Mita.

One entry: In the 12th month of Kyōhō 19, the year of the Boar (1734), was appointed to a paid position at the Office of Accounts (御勘定所).

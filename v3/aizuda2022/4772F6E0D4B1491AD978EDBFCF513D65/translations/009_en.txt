Due to inappropriate conduct unbefitting his position, his fief was confiscated.

In the 14th year of Kyōhō (1729), 12th month: He was granted a stipend of ten persons' worth and summoned as an outsider retainer, placed in Yamazaki Sasuke's unit and served guard duty at the castle.

In the 17th year of Kyōhō (1732), 4th month: He was granted Kodaira Kihei's vacant residence.

In the 18th year of Kyōhō (1733), 9th month: Due to the death by illness of his superior Yamazaki Sasuke, he was transferred to Numasawa Gonbei's unit to fill the vacant position.

In the 3rd year of Genbun (1738), 4th month: As requested by his superior, his son Iemon was appointed as guard substitute.

In the 1st year of Kan'en (1748), intercalary 10th month, 2nd day: He died of illness at age 72. His posthumous Buddhist name was Shingaku Dōnyo Koji. His burial place was the same as above.

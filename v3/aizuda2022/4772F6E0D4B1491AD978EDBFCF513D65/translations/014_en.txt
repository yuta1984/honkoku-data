In the 5th month of Hōreki 14 (1764): He was appointed to serve as hired attendant during invitations from the Senior Councilors (Rōjū).

In the 10th month of the same year: He was recalled in rotation.
Note: He was commended for his diligent service while stationed there.

In the 5th month of Meiwa 2 (1765): He went to Edo to meet the lord returning from Edo, and accompanied him back to the domain.
Note: He was commended for his diligent service accompanying the lord during the journey.

In the 2nd month of Meiwa 4 (1767): He went to Edo for emergency guard duty and returned in the 4th month of the same year.
Note: He was commended for his diligent service while stationed there.

In the 3rd month of Meiwa 5 (1768): He went to Edo for attendant escort duty.
Note: He was commended for his diligent service accompanying the lord during the journey.

In the 5th month of Meiwa 8 (1771): He went to Edo to meet the lord returning from Edo, and accompanied him back to the domain.
Note: He was commended for his diligent service during the journey.

In the 3rd month of An'ei 3 (1774): He went to Edo for attendant guard duty and returned in the 9th month of the same year.
Note: He was commended for his diligent service both accompanying the lord during the journey and while stationed there.

In the 9th month of An'ei 6 (1777): He was appointed to the next guard shift.

In the 8th month of An'ei 7 (1778): He was privately granted one ceremonial garment.

In the 8th month of Tenmei 2 (1782): He was privately granted one set of used hemp formal wear (jacket and hakama).

In the 12th month of Tenmei 5 (1785): For his diligent service over several decades without absence, he was granted one set of fine hemp formal wear as a reward.

In the 12th month of Tenmei 8 (1788): For his diligent service over several decades, he was granted a stipend of 5 koku.

In the 12th month of Kansei 3 (1791): For his diligent service over several decades without negligence, his temporary fief was converted to a direct grant of 100 koku.

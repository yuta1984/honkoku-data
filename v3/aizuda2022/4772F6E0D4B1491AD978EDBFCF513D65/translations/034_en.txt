Kyōsuke Chikashiki　　《marginal note: initially Sakon, Osamu｜》
　　Mother: daughter of the Shinoda family
　　Wife: daughter of Takamine Kinemon Yoshiaki《marginal note: died of illness on 2nd month, 5th day of Hōreki 14th year｜Buddhist name: Matsuju Senshun Kōmyō-tei Daishi｜burial site: same as above》【＊】
　　　daughter of Watanabe Tamon Nariyuki　　divorced
• Kan'en 3rd year, 3rd month (1750): Was granted his first audience with the lord
　as requested by his father
• Hōreki 2nd year, 9th month (1752): Inherited the family stipend of 100 koku
　without reduction, joined Kayano Gonbei's unit and
　served guard duty at the castle
• 6th year, 12th month (1756): As requested by unit head Kayano Gonbei,
　was relieved of duty and transferred to
　Kasuga Nizaemon's unit as his successor
• 9th year, 8th month (1759): As requested by unit head Kasuga Nizaemon,
　was relieved of duty and transferred to Ujikawa Sukemon's unit
　as his successor
• 13th year, 12th month (1763): Unit head Ujikawa Sukemon was appointed
　wakadoshiyori (junior councilor), succeeded by Ikoma Gobei

【From 'Shoshi Keifu' (Volume 141) p.34/71.】

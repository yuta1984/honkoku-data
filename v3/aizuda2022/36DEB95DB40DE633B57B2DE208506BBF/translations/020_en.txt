**[Daughter]**
Adopted daughter of Jibu Tanekata
(Birth mother: a daughter of the Kurokōchi clan)

**[Hyōe Tanemi]** (childhood name: Hyōkichi, also Kihachirō; born on the 28th day of the 1st month of Meiwa 1, the year of the Monkey [1764])
Birth mother: same as above (a daughter of the Kurokōchi clan)
Wife: adopted daughter of Andō Kuzaemon Shigemitsu (biological daughter of Jibu Tanekata)

1. In the 3rd month of An'ei 3, the year of the Horse [1774], in accordance with his father's petition, he was granted his first formal audience (*o-memie*) with the domain lord.

2. In the 7th month of the same 6th year, the year of the Rooster [1777], in accordance with his father's petition, he was appointed as a replacement guard (*bандай*), and served castle guard duty (*o-shiro o-ban*) in the unit of Kasuya Gozaemon.

3. In the 11th month of Tenmei 6, the year of the Horse [1786], following the retirement of unit commander Kasuya Gozaemon, he was transferred to the unit of Numazawa Kurōbei as the successor arrangement for that post.

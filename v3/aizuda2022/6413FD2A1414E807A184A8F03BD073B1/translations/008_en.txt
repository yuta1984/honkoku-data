    Mother: Shirakami, wife of Hori Yojibei Nadasada

Dannosuke Yukinari    Born in Enpō 8 (1680)
    Mother: same as above

1. Eleventh month of Genroku 8 (1695), as his father petitioned, he was summoned to regular duty as an attendant guard and granted a standard stipend of 25 koku and provisions for 4 retainers.

1. Second month of the same 15th year (1702), as his father petitioned, he was appointed as substitute guard and ordered to continue serving as attendant guard as before.
    However, the stipend that had been granted until then was withdrawn.

1. Tenth month of Hōei 3 (1706), he absconded from his attendant duties.
    Later he served the Sakurai family and changed his name to Teranishi Nizaemon.

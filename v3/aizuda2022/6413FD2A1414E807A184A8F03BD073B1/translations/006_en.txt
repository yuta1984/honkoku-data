[He] took his retainers and others with him down to Aizu.

1. Fifth month of the same 3rd year (1675), he joined Jinbo Mozaemon's group and served guard duty at the castle.

1. Fourth month of the same 5th year (1677), he went up to Edo to serve as caretaker guard, and came back down in the tenth month of the same year.

1. Intercalary third month of Jōkyō 3 (1686), he was appointed to the position of ōnando-yaku (head of wardrobe) with permanent residence in Edo. In the fifth month of the same year, he went up to Edo with his retainers.

1. Third month of the same 5th year (1688), he was appointed as provisional inspector (kari-metsuke) and served at the Shiba residence.

1. Intercalary eighth month of Genroku 4 (1691), he was appointed to the position of intelligence officer (kikiban-yaku) and was granted a role stipend of fifty koku.

1. Seventh month of the same 7th year (1694), when he petitioned to be relieved of his intelligence officer duties, he was told that his dismissal would not be granted.

1. Eleventh month of the same 8th year (1695), when he petitioned for his son Kuninosuke to be appointed as an attendant (tomban), [the son] was granted the position of regular page (nami no koshō) and summoned.

1. Fourth month of the same 12th year (1699), due to his advanced age, his petition was granted and he was relieved of his duties, moving back down to Aizu.

1. Seventh month of the same year, he was assigned to the group without official duties (muyaku-gumi).

He served as attendant for the inspection tour of both checkpoint stations at Kuchitsubo-shita

In the tenth month of the same year: It was announced that he had worked diligently at military drill and encouraged others well

In the second month of Bunsei 1 (Year of the Tiger): He was appointed to the temporary position of okuban guard duty
　Additionally, he was appointed to this position repeatedly thereafter

In the eighth month of the same year: He was privately granted sweetfish from the lord's weir
　Additionally, he was granted this repeatedly thereafter

In the same month: As his colleagues showed good character and discretion, establishing their resolve without any slackening and exerting themselves utmost, serving well during the long period of the lord's residence in the domain, he was granted two pieces of silver

In the third month of the second year (Year of the Rabbit): When there were accumulated old garments in storage, by unexpected consideration he was granted one shouldered ceremonial garment with family crest, one dyed kosode robe, and one hakama

In the tenth month of the same year: As he worked diligently on regular days and maintained perfect attendance for thirty months since the previous Year of the Ox, he was granted one set of old hemp kamishimo court dress

In the eleventh month of the same year:
During Lord Kinbun's illness, he endured various hardships, and when [the lord] recovered completely and rose from his sickbed, he was granted one piece of silver as congratulations

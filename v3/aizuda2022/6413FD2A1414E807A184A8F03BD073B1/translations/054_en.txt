One: In the 9th month of Kanbun 3 (1663), Kurōdo, son of Morino Jūrōzaemon, was granted his first audience [with the lord]
One: In the 6th month of Enpō 3 (1675), Morino Kurōdo was granted his coming-of-age ceremony, and henceforth shall serve in the lord's escort guard duty
　Although these two items exist, it is unclear whether Shinnosuke was initially called Kurōdo

One: According to the daily record of the 28th day of the 5th month of Jōkyō 2 (1685), Morino Jūrōzaemon's adopted son Shinnosuke was recently granted leave upon his request.
　Therefore, there was a completed petition to make his biological son Danshirō the heir,
　but there are no family records, and the years and months of his actual birth and adoption are unknown.

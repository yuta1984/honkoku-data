First, in the 4th year of Jōkyō, 9th month (1687): He went up to Edo for duty rotation, served one tour of duty, and returned.

First, in the 2nd year of Genroku, 4th month (1689): When he went up to Edo for extraordinary duty rotation, he became ill and requested to return home. After the death and completion of mourning period, he went up again, served one tour of duty, and returned.

First, in the 4th year of Genroku, 3rd month (1691): He went up to Edo for duty rotation, served one tour of duty, and returned.

First, in the 6th year, 2nd month (1693): He was appointed to the position of koban-gashira (captain of the small guard).

First, in the same year, 6th month: When he served as escort for the lord's return journey to the domain, he performed the temporary role of chief retainer (goyōnin) during the journey.

First, in the 8th year of Genroku, 3rd month (1695): He went up to Edo for duty rotation. In the 6th month of the same year, due to the lord's return journey, he was appointed as temporary chief retainer for the journey as before. During the journey, his diligent service was recognized, and he was privately granted one set of clothing.

First, in the same year, 8th month: In the formal audience chamber, he was directly informed of his appointment as chief retainer (goyōnin).

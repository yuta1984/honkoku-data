1. Same month - For his devotion to martial arts including archery, horsemanship, spear, and sword, as well as other advanced skills, which His Lordship thought were held with exceptional dedication, he was granted two pieces of silver as a reward

1. Same 9th year (1812), year of the Monkey, 12th month - As requested, he was relieved of his duties and appointed as an outside retainer attached to the chief councilor, entered the first unit, and served guard duty at the castle

1. Same 11th year (1814), year of the Dog, 4th month - He was appointed as unit commander (monogashira)

1. Same year, 9th month - He went up to Edo for guard duty service and returned in the 5th month of the following year of the Boar, serving as an attendant

1. Same 12th year (1815), year of the Boar, 9th month - He participated in the hawk hunt and received game as a gift

1. Same year, 11th month - During that year's hawk hunt, the units coordinated well with smooth operations, and it was proclaimed that His Lordship thought this was due to his diligent training in unit coordination and excellent command

1. Same 13th year (1816), year of the Rat, 9th month - Because his son Sagoro had become ill, his request to have him step down as heir was granted as requested

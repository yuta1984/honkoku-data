　dismissed from his position, [Hiroshige] joined Hori Hanemon's unit

1. In the same month, went up to Edo for duty rotation, served one term and

1. In the same 10th year, year of the Dog, 9th month, as requested, his son Yaemon was
　appointed as his substitute
1. In the same 11th year, year of the Boar, 6th month, because his son Yaemon died of illness,
　he requested and was granted permission to return to active duty
1. In the same 12th year, year of the Rat, 6th month, because his son Yaemon had died of illness,
　he requested and was granted permission to make his second son Yadayū
　his heir
1. In the same month, as requested, his son Yadayū was
　appointed as his substitute

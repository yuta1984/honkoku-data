1. In the 7th year of the same era (An'ei 7, 1778), year of the Dog, 12th month - when the head Ichinose Naiki was transferred to another post, he was reassigned to Saigo Genzo's group as successor

2. In the 9th year of the same era (An'ei 9, 1780), year of the Rat, 4th month - his request to change his name to "Noboru" was granted

3. In the 3rd year of Tenmei (1783), year of the Rabbit, 7th month - when the head Saigo Genzo died of illness, he was reassigned to Takekawa Gojuro's group as successor

4. In the 4th year of the same era (Tenmei 4, 1784), year of the Dragon, 9th month - he was granted family headship with a fief of 350 koku without any changes

5. In the 5th year of the same era (Tenmei 5, 1785), year of the Snake, 8th month - when he went to Edo to serve as attendant guard duty, he was appointed temporary ashigaru commander and returned the following year of the Horse (Tenmei 6, 1786) in the 4th month

6. In the 7th year of the same era (Tenmei 7, 1787), year of the Sheep, 3rd month - he was appointed as ashigaru commander (monogashira)

7. In the same year, 4th month - he received custody of prisoners from Toenomeguchi Uemon and Ariga Bunzaemon and transferred them to the judicial office

8. In the same year, 5th month - his request to change his name to Seizaemon was granted

9. In the 8th year of the same era (Tenmei 8, 1788), year of the Monkey, 3rd month - he went to Edo to serve as attendant guard duty and returned in the 10th month of the same year

10. In the 1st year of Kansei (1789), year of the Rooster, 5th month - he was appointed to sword guard duty

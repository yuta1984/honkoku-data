1. In the 14th year of the same era, tori year (1729), fifth month, as his father requested, he was appointed to guard duty replacement, and served castle guard duty in Sugita Gorōbei's unit

1. In the tenth month of the same year, he was granted his hereditary stipend of 400 koku without any issues

1. In the 15th year, inu year (1730), seventh month, he was transferred to Yamazaki Sasuke's unit

1. In the tenth month of the same year, he went up to Edo for guard duty, and in the fourth month of the following i year, when [the lord] came down [from Edo], he served as temporary escort guard and came down

1. In the 18th year, ushi year (1733), tenth month, due to the death by illness of unit head Yamazaki Sasuke, he was transferred to the unit of Numazu Gonbei who took over the position

1. In the 20th year, u year (1735), fourth month, he went up to Edo for guard duty and came down in the tenth month of the same year

1. In Genbun 3, uma year (1738), tenth month, he went up to Edo for guard duty, and in the fourth month of the following hitsuji year, during [the lord's] entry into the domain, he served as mounted escort and came down

1. In Kanpō 3, i year (1743), eighth month, his request to change his name to Yadayū was granted

1. In Enkyō 3, tora year (1746), third month, due to illness he was unable to perform his duties

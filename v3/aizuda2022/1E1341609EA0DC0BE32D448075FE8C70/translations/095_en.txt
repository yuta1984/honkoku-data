Due to delays in payment collection, his official duties were revoked and he was ordered to house confinement

1. Same 3rd year (1820), year of the Dragon, 3rd month - Because his disinherited son Sagoro was ordered to be beheaded within the prison compound, thirty koku from his stipend were confiscated

1. Same 4th year (1821), year of the Snake, 2nd month - Due to a general amnesty, his punishment was pardoned and his rank was appointed above the Inawashiro retainers

1. Same year, 8th month - His request to adopt Ichiyanagi Matashichiro's third son Magonosuke as son-in-law was granted as wished

1. Same 5th year (1822), year of the Horse, intercalary 1st month - He was appointed as an outside retainer attached to the chief councilor, entered the second unit, and served guard duty at the castle

1. Same year, 8th month 25th day - Died of illness at age fifty-six

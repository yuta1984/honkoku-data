Daughter of Kumagai Asaemon (retainer of the Shirakawa domain)
　　Daughter of Honda Ichirōzaemon (rōnin of Honda Tōnosuke-sama)

First, in the 1st year of Tenna, 9th month (1681): As requested, he was appointed as a substitute guard and assigned to the tozama-gumi (outer retainer group), joining Ibuka Shirōbei's unit and serving guard duty at the castle.

First, in the 2nd year of Tenna, 11th month (1682): When the unit leader Ibuka Shirōbei was ordered into exile, his subsequent role was transferred to Kayano Gonbei's unit.

First, in the 2nd year of Jōkyō, 9th month (1685): When his father Sōgorō retired, of the 800-koku stipend, Seizaemon was granted 600 koku and his younger brother Jūrōdayū was granted 200 koku as a division.

First, in the same year, 12th month: He was appointed to the position of messenger (tsukaiban).

First, in the 3rd year of Jōkyō, 4th month (1686): He went up to Edo for duty rotation, served one tour of duty, and returned.

First, in the same year, 12th month: He was appointed to the position of master of ceremonies (sōjaban) and was entrusted with twenty-one gunners.

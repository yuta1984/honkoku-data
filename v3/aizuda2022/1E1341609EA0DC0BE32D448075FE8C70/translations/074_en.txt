In the twelfth month of the same year, due to the transfer of his unit head Takekawa Gensuke to another position, he was assigned to the successor unit under Miyake Magobei.

In Genbun 2, year of the Snake (1737), fifth month, as his ailment had recovered, he resumed active duty and served castle guard duty.

In Genbun 3, year of the Horse (1738), second month, he was ordered to transfer to Tanaka Kahei's unit.

In the fourth month of the same year, he went up to Edo for regular duty service and returned in the tenth month.

In Kanpō 1, year of the Rooster (1741), eighth month, due to the lord's journey to Kyoto, he went up to Edo for emergency duty service, traveled to Odawara in Sagami Province as a messenger, and returned in the eleventh month.

In Kanpō 2, year of the Dog (1742), seventh month, he was appointed to the position of messenger-administrator (otsukai-ban).

In Kanpō 3, year of the Boar (1743), tenth month, he went up to Edo for regular duty. In the following year of the Rat, fourth month, due to Lady Baien-in's marriage procession, he was ordered to remain on extended duty and served additionally as an intermediary during the wedding ceremony, returning in the fifth month.

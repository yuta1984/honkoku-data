First, in the 1st year of Jōō, 3rd month (1652): He was assigned official duties related to the Nikkō construction project and went there to serve.

First, in the 2nd year of Jōō, 5th month (1653): He was ordered to go up to Edo for entertainment by the rōjū (senior councilors) and was appointed to the position of go-toritsugi-yaku (liaison officer).

First, in the 3rd year of Jōō, 5th month (1654): He went up to Edo for guard duty.

First, in the 1st year of Manji, 4th month (1658): He went up to Edo for guard duty.

First, in the 2nd year of Kanbun, 2nd month (1662): He went up to Edo for guard duty.

First, in the 9th year of Kanbun, 9th month (1669): He went up to Edo for guard duty.

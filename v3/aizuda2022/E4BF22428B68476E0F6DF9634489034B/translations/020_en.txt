Daughter of Ibuka Saburōzaemon Shigenaga <<Born on February 4th of Enkyō 3 (1746). Died of illness on December 16th of Bunsei 3 (1820). Buddhist name: Genmoku Shin'in Chigan Myōkai Daishi. Burial place: Same as above>>

　One　In June of Genbun 4 (1739), was granted his first audience with the lord as requested by his father
　One　In the leap month of June of Kan'en 3 (1750), was appointed as guard substitute as requested by his father, and served castle guard duty in Miyake Magobei's unit
　One　In June of Hōreki 1 (1751), when unit commander Miyake Magobei was appointed as wakadoshiyori (junior councilor), he was transferred to Yanase Kyūma's unit as his successor in the role

　One　In June of the 2nd year (1752), due to prolonged illness, requested that his father Magoishiemon serve in his place, and retired from guard substitute duty
　One　In September of the 10th year (1760), having fully recovered from illness, was reappointed as guard substitute as requested by his father, and served castle guard duty in Yanase Kyūma's unit
　One　In November of the same year, was granted the hereditary stipend of 400 koku without change
　One　In February of the 12th year (1762), when unit commander Yanase Kyūma was appointed as wakadoshiyori, he was transferred to Yamazaki Sasuke's unit as his successor

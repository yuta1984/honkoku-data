　Regarding Busai's actual background: He was the great-grandson of a retainer of the Mogami Torii family named Katō Shinbei [surname unknown] (said to have held a 200-koku stipend). His grandfather Taemon [surname unknown] was summoned to serve the domain and worked as an ashigaru metsuke (foot soldier inspector). He had two sons: the eldest was named Taemon [surname unknown], and the second son was named Jun'an [surname unknown], who studied medicine. Later, he became the adopted son-in-law of the domain physician Shibuya Kōshō and was called Shibuya Chōan. At that time, one child was born who later became known as Shibamiya Saemon Yukimitsu.

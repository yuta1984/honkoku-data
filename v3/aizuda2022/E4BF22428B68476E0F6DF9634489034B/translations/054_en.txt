1. In August of the same year

1. In October of the same year, upon returning to the domain
   As before, he was ordered to have audiences with Lords Rinkōin and Kōshōin
   and to provide medical examinations
   From Lord Rinkōin he received an obi (sash) fabric and a tobacco pouch

   and returned home in the same month

   granted gifts and rewards

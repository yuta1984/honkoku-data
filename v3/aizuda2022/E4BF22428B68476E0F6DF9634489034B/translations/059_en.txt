Since he was also providing medical treatment, he was appointed to conduct midday examinations and medical consultations

1. In February of Tenmei 5, year of the Snake, as requested by his father, he was
   granted retirement and was granted a hereditary stipend of 100 koku
   without change for family succession, and was appointed as a domain physician.
   The practice allowance of five-person support that had been granted until now
   was withdrawn

1. In July of the same year, because he was providing medical treatment widely, he was
   appointed to the rank of personal physician and was ordered to
   work closely with other personal physicians in conducting medical examinations
   and discussing matters related to health maintenance

1. In August of the 6th year of the Horse, he was
   appointed as personal physician

1. In October of the same year, he went to Edo
   for guard duty service

1. In the intercalary 10th month of the same year,
   he was granted one bundle of cotton as a celebratory gift
   for the recovery ceremony of Shuntōin-sama

- Same year, 6th month: It was announced that during the previous winter, when there were many duties related to Lord Kinbun's official appointment, he served diligently.

- Bunka 12, Year of the Boar, 10th month: Since there were confidential matters to be investigated in Edo, he was urgently ordered to go up. He was commanded to quickly complete the investigative duties assigned to Aizu domain and depart, and he departed and went up on the 3rd day of the 11th month.

- Same year, 12th month: For the imperial visit to Kyoto planned for the coming summer, he was appointed to accompany as part of the entourage. Due to insufficient mounted horses for the journey, he was added to provide horses, and was ordered to join the messenger officials in Kyoto to perform guard duties.

- Bunka 13, Year of the Rat, 1st month: Regarding the compilation of the "Record of Family History" and legal documents, although his regular duties were numerous, he took primary responsibility from the beginning, devoted his full energy, managed affairs well, and successfully completed both major works. As a reward, he was granted one roll of silk, cotton batting, and five pieces of silver as substitutes for a formal kimono.

1. He was ordered to provide medicine for Lord Kyōtei's health maintenance

1. In May of the same year
   He was ordered to have an audience with Lord Renjuin and
   was ordered to provide medical examinations

1. In the intercalary seventh month of the same year, he was privately granted
   one ceremonial kosode (short-sleeved kimono)

1. In September of the same year, because Sawai Shōgen was ill and
   excused from service duty, he was ordered to serve
   the extended duty rotation

1. In October of the same year, he received five ryō of gold as
   medicine expenses from his previous service duty. With the rank of御側医格 (gosoku-i-kaku, personal physician rank),
   he went up for his second campaign and performed
   duties equivalent to a personal physician. Though this was his first campaign as a personal physician,
   his medicine allowance was increased by five ryō, and it was
   announced that he would receive ten ryō per year
   during his service duty

1. In October of the same year, since April he had
   taken on duties beyond his regular responsibilities and worked diligently,
   as a reward he was privately granted two bundles of cotton

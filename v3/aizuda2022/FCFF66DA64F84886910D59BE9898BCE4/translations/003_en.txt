## Genealogy of Higuchi Shōemon
**Clan name (姓):** Nakahara　**Home province:** Shinano
**Standard family crest:** Right-facing triple tomoe　**Alternate crest:** Tomoe with cursive character variant　**Camp curtain crest:** Same as above

Second son of Higuchi Kanzaburō (personal name unknown)

**Higuchi Shōemon Mitsutoyo** (born in the Year of the Horse, Keichō 11 \[1606\])

- Mother: Unknown
- Wife: Daughter of a certain Iijima　(Died of illness on the 7th day of the 2nd month, Genroku 4, Year of the Ram \[1691\]. Posthumous Buddhist name: Shōrin Sōmu Daishi. Place of burial: Unzan Zenryūji Temple \[details unclear\])

1\. In the 9th month of Kan'ei 14, Year of the Ox \[1637\], he traveled from Takatō to Mogami, and upon petitioning regarding his personal circumstances, was granted a stipend of 9 koku and a ration allowance for 2 persons.

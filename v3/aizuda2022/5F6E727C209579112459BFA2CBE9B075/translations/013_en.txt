- In the 4th month of the same year, because he had served diligently for several years and moreover had carried out gate guard duty for three shifts without delay, it was declared that he would be rewarded with one set of hemp formal wear.
　However, in the same month he went down [to the domain].

- In the 5th month of Hōreki 11th year (1761), it was declared that he should attend martial arts training every other day and would also be appointed to regular duty shifts. He attended continuously while the lord was in the domain.

- In the 6th month of the same year, he was appointed to make a wooden practice sword with sheath, and when he submitted the finished product in the 8th month, it was declared that it was well made and met with approval.

- In the 11th month of the same year, when the Ryūgi school martial arts were observed by the lord, it was declared that both he and his disciples performed the martial arts well.

- In the 3rd month of Hōreki 12th year (1762), when Lord Kyōtei practiced martial arts, because he had applied himself diligently for several years, it was declared that he would be rewarded with one set of hemp formal wear.

- In the 6th month of the same year, although it was not his scheduled guard duty, because he served as a martial arts partner, it was declared that he should go up [to Edo], and in the 10th month he went up as guard duty.

- In the 4th month of Hōreki 13th year (1763), he submitted [a certificate of] permission in martial arts and military techniques.

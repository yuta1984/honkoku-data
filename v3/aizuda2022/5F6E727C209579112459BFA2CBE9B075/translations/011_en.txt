Lord Tosa passed away, and when his coffin was brought down [from Edo], he served as an escort and went down [to the domain].

- In the 12th month of the same year, it was declared that he served diligently with perfect attendance both in the military camp and as escort for the coffin.

- In the 12th month of Hōreki 2nd year (1752), he was appointed to Edo guard duty.

- In the 1st month of Hōreki 3rd year (1753), during the stay of the domain inspectors, it was declared that he served diligently in temple and shrine water service duties at the mansion.

- In the 10th month of the same year, he went up to Edo for guard duty.

- In the 2nd month of Hōreki 4th year (1754), he was appointed to temporary next guard duty and served both closing guard duties.

- In the 4th month of the same year, he went down [to the domain].

- In the 10th month of Hōreki 6th year (1756), he went up to Edo for guard duty.

- In the 11th month of the same year, when Lord Kyōtei practiced martial arts training, it was declared that he should attend and serve as his practice partner.

- In the 12th month of the same year, he was appointed to serve in the guest reception room.

- In the 4th month of Hōreki 7th year (1757), [regarding] regular service, official appointment, and New Year duties - this being his first service under the current lord - he applied himself diligently with care.

• In the twelfth month of the same year, for his diligent service, he was granted an official stipend of one koku with support for one person.

• In the tenth month of the same seventh sheep year, he was assigned duties under the senior retainers and ordered to serve as an administrator for military preparations and construction projects.

• In the twelfth month of the same year, for his busy service throughout the year, he was granted two ryō of gold annually.

• In the twelfth month of the same eighth monkey year, for his diligent service and particularly laborious work on assigned duties, his previous official stipend of one koku with support for one person was directly increased by the lord.

• In the fourth month of Kansei 2 (dog year), he went up to serve guard duty as an attendant for the pilgrimage to Edo and was scheduled to return in the tenth month of the same year, but due to illness, he petitioned and returned in the ninth month of the same year.
　　　However, his diligent and uninterrupted service during the official journey as an attendant was formally commended.

• In the eleventh month of the same year, for his diligent service during his current guard duty assignment, he was formally notified that he would be granted two pieces of silver as a reward.

• In the sixth month of the same third rooster year, he was appointed to assist with the rotating rice warehouse affairs, serving until the eighth month of the same year.
　However, he was granted gold as compensation for his efforts.

　　　　【In red ink】To Edo

• In the fourth month of the same fourth dog year, he went up to Edo for guard duty, returning in the tenth month of the same year.
　However, during this period, he was appointed as construction supervisor for the repairs of the main gate of the upper mansion, serving until the ninth month.

• In the first month of the same fifth boar year, as a reward for diligently serving as construction supervisor during the previous year's repairs of the main gate of the upper mansion, he was granted 200 hiki of gold.

• In the sixth month of the same year, he was appointed to supervise the carpenter's workshop for half a year and served accordingly.

• In the fourth month of the same eighth tiger year, he went up to Edo for guard duty and was appointed as supervisor of the Shiba meeting place, serving until the tenth month of the same year.

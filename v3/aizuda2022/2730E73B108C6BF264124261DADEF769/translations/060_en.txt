Since then, despite having many official duties under his charge, he attended to details carefully and worked with particular diligence, and for exceptional reasons was granted a seat in the second rank of close attendants.

• In the third month of the same year, he was stationed at the hunting lodge for bird hunting duties.

• In the same month, as administrator for bird hunting duties, despite having many various official tasks related to arrangements and preparations, for his diligent service he was granted one bundle of cotton as a reward.

• In the seventh month of the same fifth snake year, he was assigned to serve in duties related to the senior councilor, and was ordered to continue serving in both regular peaceful duties and special assigned duties as before.

• In the ninth month of the same year, he was stationed at the hunting grounds for bird hunting duties and was assigned mounted hunting.
　　　However, after this, among the six times he went out for horse training, he received birds caught on two occasions as gifts.

• In the twelfth month of the same year, his petition to change his name to Shichirōemon was granted.

• In the same month, his petition to take Zōshu, the third son of Andō Bunsuke, as an adopted son-in-law was granted.

When serving in official duties, he received gifts bearing the lord's crest, and regarding regular gifts for each stint of duty service, as well as gifts given during various occasions of service such as Boys' Day and year-end, these are treated similarly and therefore not recorded individually.

• In the 9th month of Genroku 5 (1692), he went to Edo for duty service and returned in the 3rd month of the following year.

• In the 9th month of Genroku 7 (1694), he went to Edo for duty service and returned in the 3rd month of the following year.

• In the 7th month of Genroku 8 (1695), he was appointed to the position of go-tomo bangashira (head of lord's escort) in the presence of the lord.

• In the 4th month of Genroku 9 (1696), he went to Edo as part of the lord's escort for duty service and returned in the 9th month of the same year.
　Note: This time, the lord's journey to Edo marked the first passage through the new Matsukawa-dōri road.

• 5th month of the same year
　In celebration of Lady Shinshō-in's first shrine visit, he was granted two御帷子 (formal silk garments).

• 12th month of the same year
　In celebration of Lady Shinshō-in's birth, he was granted a meal at Lady Eiju-in's residence.

• 1st month of Genroku 10 (1697), in celebration of receiving an honorary title, he was granted a meal at Lady Eiju-in's residence.

Important matters were to be communicated by the senior retainers, while routine official business was to be communicated by the magistrates. When reporting directly to the magistrates, he should also inform the senior retainers. Regarding local matters that the lord would inquire about directly, when there were matters to report, he should attend and present them, as was announced.

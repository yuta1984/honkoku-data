Regarding obligations to the government, there was much back-and-forth deliberation that Uneme's side should remain. However, if Uneme's side were to remain, as Lord Denjūrō stated, there would certainly be no one who would abandon their lord and withdraw first, and since large numbers would cause various obstacles, it would be completely impossible for them to remain. Although I am of humble station, given my position as senior political counselor (mujo seiji karō), it would not be particularly inappropriate for me to face the government authorities. Moreover, regarding my official role, there are matters initially established at Matsumae, and with my unit of over eighty men meeting the numbers communicated internally for equipment, with proper organization of groups and leaders, there are no obstacles in dividing responsibilities. I earnestly desire that my unit be allowed to remain. Although with small numbers and my inadequacy in all matters, you may find this unreliable, I will engage the enemy with utmost dedication, and should the worst come to pass, I will die honorably in battle and will not commit any disgraceful acts that would soil our family name. Please be assured of this and arrange for me to remain. I strongly stated this request, and it was agreed upon, with formal notification given today. Accordingly, our unit will remain, with the addition of firearms specialist Sakai Dennai's son Tomikichi, physician Muramoto Genseki, and Hyakusaki Yōken's son Yōgen, making a total of eighty-seven men, both high and low ranking, who will remain.

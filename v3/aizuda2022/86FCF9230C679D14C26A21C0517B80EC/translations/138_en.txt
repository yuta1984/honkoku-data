1. In the same month
Since His Lordship was to move to the official residence this time, and they had diligently served in official matters up to this point,
His Lordship bestowed one bundle of cotton and three bundles of paper tissues.
However, even after the move and until their death from illness, not only during celebrations but also at various other times when summoned, including recreational outings to mountains and fields, they continued to be taken along unchanged, and various items including winnings from games, gifts received, calligraphy, paintings, and other items were frequently bestowed.

1. In the same month, since His Lordship was to travel to Edo this time, cloth and eagle feathers were bestowed by his gracious will.

1. In the seventh month of the ninth year (tsuchinoe-saru), since they excelled in learning and their skills were superior for their age, advancing to the first rank, the "Sanrei Kochū" (classical commentary on the Three Rituals) was bestowed.

1. In the fourth month of the twelfth year (tsuchinoto-i), while they had been appointed as companions for recreational activities, since there were also competitive elements involved, it was announced that they would be excused from this duty from the time of the current return to the domain.

1. In the eighth month of the same year, since they had diligently served as companions as previously appointed, three pieces of silver and two bundles of cotton were bestowed.

1. In the tenth month of the same year, since they excelled in learning and their skills were superior for their age, advancing to the study of the Great Learning, the "Gokyō Shinchū" (New Commentary on the Five Classics) was bestowed.

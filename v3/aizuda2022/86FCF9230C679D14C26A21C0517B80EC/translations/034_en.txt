It was proclaimed. In the anteroom, the war banner and horse insignia were permitted, and stipend equivalent to that of magistrates was bestowed.
Regarding the concurrent service as Lady Juhoin's attendant, he could continue serving as before. It was proclaimed that riding in the lord's palanquin procession was now permitted.

1. On the same day:
Regarding his appointment as assistant attendant to Lord Sadaaki, while serious matters of rewards and punishments were exceptional, for other official business, all attendants would appear before His Lordship together to report. When appearing at the main hall, he would join among the magistrates and appear in order of seniority. Most importantly, all attendants were to be stationed in Aizu for one year until next spring, as was proclaimed.

1. In April of the same year:
As he accompanied Lord Sadaaki on his journey down to the domain,
His Lordship bestowed upon him a crested lined haori jacket.
He also carried official correspondence during the journey.

1. In the same month:
Having been appointed as assistant attendant to Lord Sadaaki with the same rank as magistrate, when magistrates made official inquiries, he was to be seated with them, and when appearing at the main entrance, he was to appear among the magistrates in the night duty room for audience, as was proclaimed.
When seated in attendance, written documents were to be circulated from among the magistrates...

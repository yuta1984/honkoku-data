[Written in red] Year 1 of Kan'ei, Year of the Monkey: He served as an attendant for the pilgrimage to Nikkō Shrine

Year 7 of the same era, Year of the Tiger (month and day unclear): As requested, he was relieved of his duties and appointed to the rank next to kakushiki monogashira (ceremonial military officer)
Year 1 of Shōtoku, Year of the Rabbit (month and day unclear): As requested, his son Kitōji was appointed as a guard substitute
Year 1 of Kyōhō, Year of the Monkey, intercalary 2nd month, 23rd day: He died of illness at the age of 73. Posthumous Buddhist name: Hōzan Gan'in Reiyō Tetsudō Koji, buried at Ganshōju Temple on Mount Kanō

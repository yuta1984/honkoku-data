matters

1. Hōreki 13 (year of the sheep), from 4th month until the following year of the monkey 4th month: For serving as castle minor ferry duty official, granted three bu in gold

1. Meiwa 1 (year of the monkey) (month unclear): Appointed as temporary minor official of the treasury, served until the following year of the rooster 2nd month

1. Meiwa 2 (year of the rooster), 2nd month: Appointed to Yamagata garrison duty

1. Meiwa 3 (year of the dog), 1st month: Granted three koku from his late father's stipend allocation

1. Same year, 7th month: For diligently serving without delay during Yamagata garrison duty, received official commendation

1. Meiwa 4 (year of the boar), 11th month: Granted independent audience privilege (dokurei), with seating rank above accounting office minor official Koizumi Naoshirō and above treasury official for account settlement, money receipt/payment, and miscellaneous duties Takahashi Isoemon

1. Meiwa 5 (year of the rat), 2nd month: Appointed as temporary accounting inspector

1. Meiwa 7 (year of the tiger), 12th month: Appointed as accounting inspector

1. An'ei 6 (year of the rooster), 12th month: For diligent and excellent service, granted official salary of three koku

1. An'ei 8 (year of the boar), 8th month: Appointed as temporary magistrate of Takada district

1. Same month: Appointed as town magistrate, with official salary of three koku continuing as before, and seating rank above Yamamoto Han'uchi and above administrative investigator Nagase Shōemon of the chamberlain's office

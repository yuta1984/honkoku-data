One: Same 5th year (year of the ox), 6th month - He was also appointed to serve in the fixed harmonious duties of the official business room.

One: Same 8th year (year of the dragon), 8th month - As a temporary employee in the historical records section of the official business room, he worked diligently and completed clean copying tasks, for which he was granted one ryō of gold as compensation for his efforts.

One: Same 11th year (year of the sheep), 1st month - He petitioned to change his name to Kozaemon and was granted permission.

One: Same year, 4th month - He was appointed to serve as a regular temporary official in the official business room, and was assigned duties at the Domain Treasury Office, Legal Affairs Office, Gold Mine Office, and Shrine Offerings Office.

One: Kyōwa 3rd year (year of the boar), 6th month - As petitioned by his father, his retirement was accepted, one koku was reduced from his father's retirement stipend, and he was granted eight koku and provisions for two retainers. He was summoned and appointed as an employee of the official business room, and granted a standard audience [with the lord].

One: Same year, 7th month - His position was set as official business room employee with cavalry rank, above the minor officials of the Accounting Office.

One: Same year, 9th month - He was appointed as an employee of the Administrative Office.
　　However, he was ordered to take turns joining the regular officials and serve in both public and private ceremonies and legal administrative duties.

One: Bunka 1st year (year of the rat), 8th month - He was entrusted with and served in the great amnesty duties of this time, handling several hundred amnesty petitions, including some with complex investigations that required examination...

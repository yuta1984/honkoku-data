　　However, for this appointment, one ryō in gold was granted for hakama expenses

1. Genbun 3 (year of the horse), 12th month: Appointed as regular employee at the accounting office, and granted two ryō in gold each time as expense allowance

1. Genbun 5 (year of the monkey), 11th month: Granted eight koku in regular stipend and support for two people, and summoned as minor official at the accounting office

1. Enkyō 4 (year of the rabbit), 12th month: Granted two koku as official salary

1. Hōreki 6 (year of the rat), 8th month:
　For properly handling Lord Yōshō-in's rice tax affairs, granted the regular reward of two bu in gold

1. Hōreki 7 (year of the ox), 1st month:
　For properly managing Lord Yōshō-in's rice tax, granted three bu in gold as commendation

1. Same year, 8th month:
　For properly handling Lord Yōshō-in's rice tax affairs, granted the regular reward of two bu in gold

1. Hōreki 12 (year of the horse), 4th month: His current official salary of two koku was increased and adjusted

1. Same year, 11th month: Appointed as temporary military equipment official

1. Same year, 12th month: Due to illness, as requested, relieved of the temporary position

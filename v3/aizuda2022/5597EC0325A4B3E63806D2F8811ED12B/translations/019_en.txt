One: Kansei 2nd year (year of the dog), 2nd month - He petitioned to change his name to Den'nai and was granted permission.

One: Same year, 3rd month - Regarding financial affairs, he contributed to procurement arrangements by obtaining various items as requested, and applied himself meticulously to his work with exceptional accomplishment. Therefore, he was directly granted an additional increase of one koku to his current official salary.

One: Same 5th year (year of the ox), 5th month - He served in various assigned roles with complex calculations involved, and due to his diligent service, he was granted two bu of gold as a reward.

One: Same year, 12th month - Having served for several decades while observing the proper timing, and having distinguished himself through daily dedication and exceptional diligence, despite it being a weighty matter, he was granted the rank of dokurei (independent audience).

One: Same 6th year (year of the tiger), 5th month - Due to some misconduct, he was relieved of his official duties as announced.

One: Same 7th year (year of the rabbit), 4th month - He was appointed to the fourth group assembly attached to the chief retainer.

One: Same year, 9th month - He participated in the bird hunting expedition.
　　Note: He participated once more after this.

One: Same year, 11th month - During the current bird hunting expedition, he coordinated pace and timing, arranged formations and maneuvers properly, and worked diligently. For this reason, he received commendation as a sign of [the lord's] favorable consideration.
　　Note: He received commendation once more after this.

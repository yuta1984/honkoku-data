1. In the 3rd month of Kyōhō 16 (Year of the Boar), he was appointed to daiban duty as requested by his parent.

1. In the 6th month of the same 17th year (Year of the Rat), he was granted a hereditary stipend of 150 koku and summoned to serve as a yoriki.

1. In the 4th month of the same 18th year (Year of the Ox), during a fire in Yamato-machi, he was commended for working well.

1. In the 2nd month of Genbun 1 (Year of the Dragon), during a fire in Hanbei-machi Yokochō, he was commended for rushing to the scene promptly and working effectively.

1. In the 12th month of Kan'en 2 (Year of the Snake), during a peasant uprising, he was commended for his diligent service.

1. On the 12th day of the 7th month of the same 4th year (Year of the Sheep), he died of illness at age 46. His posthumous Buddhist name was Kan'yo Saigan Koji, buried in the same place [as above].

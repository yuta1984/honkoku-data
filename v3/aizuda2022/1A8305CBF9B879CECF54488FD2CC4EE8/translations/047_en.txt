First, same 2nd year (Year of the Dog), 1st month: He was appointed to attend to the rice transport and loan affairs of Kyoto and Osaka, departed in the 3rd month of the same year, and returned in the 1st month of the following Year of the Boar
　　As a reward, he was granted 3 ryō in gold

First, same 3rd year (Year of the Boar), 3rd month: He was appointed to serve one year as attendant at the investigation office

First, same year, 8th month: He was appointed as shrine offerings official, and it was announced that his stipend would continue as before

First, Enkyō 2nd year (Year of the Ox), 9th month: Due to illness, he requested to resign from his position, but was ordered to continue serving while recuperating

First, same 3rd year (Year of the Tiger), 1st month: He was appointed as castle keep armory official with retained status, and it was announced that his stipend would continue as before

First, Kan'en 3rd year (Year of the Horse), 1st month: It was announced that he served diligently during the peasant uprising

First, Hōreki 1st year (Year of the Sheep), 12th month: For his diligent and excellent service, it was announced that he would receive a direct increase of 3 koku added to his current stipend

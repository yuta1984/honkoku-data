Wife: Daughter of Inoue Hyōkichi Naofusa - Divorced

　　Daughter of Sugiura Tōjirō Naruyasu

First　Bunka 9 (Year of the Monkey), 12th month: Due to his excellence in studies and skill beyond his years, he was awarded the Kōkyō Taigi (Great Principles of the Classic of Filial Piety), Shisho Shūchū (Collected Commentaries on the Four Books), and Shōgaku as rewards.

First　Same 12th year (Year of the Boar), 2nd month: At his father's request, he was granted his first formal audience with the lord.

First　Same 14th year (Year of the Ox), 12th month: He was granted a stipend of 500 koku without change, assigned to the first small construction unit for martial arts training, appointed to Aizu duties, and in the following first month of the Year of the Tiger, his retainers relocated and went down to the domain.

First　Bunpō 1 (Year of the Tiger), 5th month: Regarding the previous October when foreign ships were sighted off the coasts of Sagami and Bōshū provinces and troops were dispatched, he remained behind and performed temporary duties with dedication, for which he was granted leave.

First　Same year, 12th month: When organizational changes were announced, while remaining in the small construction unit, he was appointed as an external retainer attached to the senior councilor and entered the first unit.

First　Same 3rd year (Year of the Dragon), 8th month: He was relieved of small construction duties and began regular service.

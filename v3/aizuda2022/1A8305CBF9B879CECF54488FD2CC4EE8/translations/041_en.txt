First, Shōtoku 2nd year (Year of the Dragon), 8th month: As per his father's request, his provisional stipend was revoked, a standard provisional stipend was granted, and he was summoned as a foot soldier
First, same 3rd year (Year of the Snake), 5th month: He returned to welcome the lord's descent, went up to serve as an attendant, and came down
First, same 4th year (Year of the Horse), 4th month: He went up for attendance duty accompanying the lord's alternate attendance (sankin-kōtai) and guard duty, returning in the 10th month of the same year
First, Kyōhō 1st year (Year of the Monkey), 10th month: He was appointed to treasury supervision duty and granted 2 bu in gold as compensation for his efforts

First, same 2nd year (Year of the Rooster), 1st month: He was appointed to one year of firewood office supervision duty and granted 1 ryō in gold as compensation for his efforts
First, same 3rd year (Year of the Dog), 4th month: He served as an attendant for the lord's alternate attendance and went up for guard duty, returning in the 10th month of the same year

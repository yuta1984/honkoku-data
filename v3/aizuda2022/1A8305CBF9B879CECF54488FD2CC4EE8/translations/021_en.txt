1. In the 4th month of Kansei 6 (Year of the Tiger), regarding the autumn bird hunting, it was proclaimed that His Lordship thought highly of how well he had arranged the formation and marching order of the armored warriors and had given excellent command.

1. In the 6th month of Kansei 7 (Year of the Rabbit), as requested, his son Hidegoro was appointed as guard substitute.

1. In the 3rd month of Bunka 4 (Year of the Rabbit), as requested, he was granted retirement.

1. On the 7th day of the 11th month of Bunka 11 (Year of the Dog), he died of illness at the age of 73.

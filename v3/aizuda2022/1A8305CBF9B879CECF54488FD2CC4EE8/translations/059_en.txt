First, Kyōwa 1st year (Year of the Rooster), 4th month: He was appointed as head monitor (daijūchō) of the school.

First, same 2nd year (Year of the Dog), 5th month: He was appointed to serve as an apprentice magistrate on temporary employment.

First, Bunka 3rd year (Year of the Tiger), 7th month: He was appointed to serve as magistrate on temporary employment.

First, same 4th year (Year of the Rabbit), 7th month: He was granted the hereditary stipend of 100 koku without change, continued to be appointed as magistrate as before, and was granted the status of first-class attendant assembly seat.

First, same year, 9th month: He participated in the fukushi horse parade and received game as reward.

First, same 6th year (Year of the Snake), 8th month: He participated in the bird hunting and received game as reward.

First, same 7th year (Year of the Horse), 5th month: He carefully attended to local administration matters, particularly worked diligently on educational facility management and tax collection procedures, which contributed to strengthening control, and also worked hard on emergency provisions and food supply management, taking good care of these matters. As a reward, he was granted one roll of bleached cloth in place of formal court dress.

First, same year, 11th month: He was appointed as magistrate of Sagami Province for two years...

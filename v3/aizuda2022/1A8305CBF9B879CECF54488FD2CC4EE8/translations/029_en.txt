A notification was issued, and after instructing everyone to be vigilant, on the night of the 3rd, foreign ships were reported drifting off the coast near Izu Peninsula. Since Misaki was an important forward position for coastal defense and fortification, at dawn on the 4th, he led troops to the Jōgasaki battery. However, when the lookout returned and reported that no foreign ships were visible, they withdrew and returned to base on the 11th.

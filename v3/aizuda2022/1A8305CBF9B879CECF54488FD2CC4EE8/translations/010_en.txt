1. In the 10th month of the same year, he went up to Edo for regular service duty, completed one term of service, and returned in the 4th month of the following year (Year of the Dog).
   However, since the unit commander Saka Seizaemon died of illness during the service period, he took on various supervisory duties.

1. In the 12th month of the same year, since the commander Saka Seizaemon had died of illness, he was transferred to Ohara Gorōemon's unit for various official duties.

1. In the 8th month of the 3rd year (Year of the Dog), he inherited the family headship and was granted the fief of 400 koku without any changes.

1. In the 9th month of the 6th year (Year of the Ox), he was appointed as messenger (tsukai-ban).

1. In the 4th month of the 7th year (Year of the Tiger), he went up as part of the daimyo's retinue for the mandatory attendance in Edo (sankin-kōtai), and returned in the 10th month of the same year.

1. In the 6th month of the 8th year (Year of the Rabbit), he was appointed as a messenger to inquire about the shogun's health at the beginning of the summer season, went up to Edo, fulfilled his messenger duties, and returned in the 7th month of the same year.

1. In the 9th month of the 9th year (Year of the Dragon), he went up to Edo for regular service duty and returned in the following year (Year of the Snake).

1. In the 9th month of the 10th year (Year of the Snake), he was appointed as presenter (sōsha-ban).

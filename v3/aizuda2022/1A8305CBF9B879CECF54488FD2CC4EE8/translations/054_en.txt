First, same 9th year (Year of the Rat), 10th month: He went up to Edo for guard duty service and returned in the 4th month of the following Year of the Ox as part of the lord's retinue

First, same month: He was granted the transfer of Kayano Kogenta's upper residence

First, same year, 11th month: It was announced that he had maintained the residence well

First, Tenmei 1st year (Year of the Ox), 4th month: For his diligent service without delay in the military camp, he was granted two pieces of silver

First, same year, 8th month: For his diligent and excellent service over several decades, he was appointed to the tozama-gumi (outer retainer group) and exempted from regular duty obligations
　　　However, he was to join Kasuya Gozaemon's group and serve guard duty at the castle

First, same 2nd year (Year of the Tiger), 2nd month: As requested, his son Tasuke was appointed as his substitute for guard duty

First, same 4th year (Year of the Dragon), 5th month, 22nd day: He died of illness at the age of sixty-four

He was granted one hundred koku without change, appointed to the tozama group, entered Hyūga Gen'emon's unit, and served as castle guard.

1. In the 10th month of the same year, he went up to Edo for guard duty service, and was dismissed in the 4th month of the following year of the Boar.

1. In the 5th month of the 7th year (Year of the Ox), he was appointed as a squad leader (monotō).

1. In the 4th month of the 8th year (Year of the Tiger), he went up for Edo guard duty service and came down in the 10th month of the same year.

1. In the 8th month of the 9th year (Year of the Rabbit), he received custody of prisoner Kajiwara Den'jūrō from Ikoma Asaemon's residence and transferred him to Andō Riemon's residence in the 9th month of the same year.

1. In the 3rd month of the 12th year (Year of the Horse), he went up for daimyo procession escort and guard duty service, and came down in the 10th month of the same year.

1. In the 2nd month of Meiwa 1 (Year of the Monkey), Shindō Saburō[ji] was placed in his custody, and he transferred him to Mochizuki Shinbei's residence in the 3rd month of the same year.

1. In the 3rd month of the 3rd year (Year of the Dog), he went to Yamagata for guard duty service and returned in the 9th month of the same year.

First, same 5th year (Year of the Rat), 10th month: He went up to Edo for guard duty service and returned in the 5th month of the following Year of the Ox

First, same 6th year (Year of the Ox), 4th month: For his diligent service without delay in the military camp, he was granted two pieces of silver as a reward

First, An'ei 2nd year (Year of the Snake), 1st month: For his diligent and excellent service over several years, he was granted an increase of five koku

First, same 5th year (Year of the Monkey), 10th month: He went up to Edo for guard duty service and returned in the 5th month of the following Year of the Rooster as part of the lord's retinue

First, same 6th year (Year of the Rooster): He went up to Edo for guard duty service, and for his diligent service without delay, he was granted three pieces of silver

First, same 7th year (Year of the Dog), 12th month: For his diligent and excellent service over the years, his previous provisional stipend was converted to a direct fief of one hundred koku

First, same 8th year (Year of the Boar), 8th month: His ancestors had served loyally generation after generation since Shinshū, and when Lord Hōshōin was born, his great-grandmother served as wet nurse.

　Kurō-uemon Tadaki　《Marginal note: Initially named Yasoji-uemon, also Uhē, after retirement called Goshū》
　　　Mother: same as above

　　　　　daughter of Sasahara Den-uemon Tadamichi

　One　In the 9th year of Kyōhō (year of the snake), 2nd month, granted first audience with the lord as requested by his father
　One　In the 20th year of the same era (year of the rabbit), 2nd month, appointed as guard substitute as requested by his father, served castle guard duty in Ueda Hachirō-uemon's unit
　One　In the 7th month of the same year, when the head Ueda Hachirō-uemon was relieved of his duties, transferred to Ichinose Kenmotsu's unit as his successor
　One　In the 1st year of Kanbō (year of the rooster), 4th month, went up to Edo for guard duty
　One　In the 9th month of the same year, when the lord was ordered to go to Kyoto, was appointed to accompany him during his guard duty term, and when the lord returned to Edo in the 10th month, he came down in the 11th month

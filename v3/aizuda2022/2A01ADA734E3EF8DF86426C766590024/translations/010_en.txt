1　In the 9th month of Meiwa 1, year of the Monkey
　　　As Lord Kyōtei's marriage arrangement was announced as requested,
　　　appointed to temporary employment at the catering office　
　　　　However, after this, when there were major ceremonies, temporary employment was
　　　　appointed and clothing allowances were granted each time
1　In the same month, ordered to learn the catering office work through his own efforts
1　In the 4th month of Meiwa 3, year of the Dog, appointed to temporary position as
　　　receiving and disbursing officer for writing brushes, ink, paper, candles and various items
　　　at the catering office, served until the 10th month of the same year
1　In the 9th month of the same year, appointed to temporary position as sake officer
　　　during the lord's residence in Edo, served until the 4th month of the following year of the Boar
1　In the 4th month of Meiwa 4, year of the Boar, there were frequent guests, and moreover
　　　various tea ceremony matters were ordered, and sudden duties occasionally
　　　arose; for serving diligently in these circumstances, was granted sake
1　In the 5th month of Meiwa 5, year of the Rat, as in the time of residence in Edo
　　　two years prior, appointed to temporary position as sake officer, served until the 5th month
　　　of the following year of the Ox
1　In the 1st month of Meiwa 6, year of the Ox, apprentice at the catering office and other temporary duties

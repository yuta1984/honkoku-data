1　In the 2nd month of Meiwa 8, year of the Rabbit, two parts were reduced from the deceased father's allotted stipend assignment,
　　and was granted 3 ryō of gold and stipend for two people; the previously granted
　　2 ryō of gold and stipend for two people was recalled

1　In the 7th month of the same year, due to the kitchen utensil inspection, the catering
　　officer served exclusively in that role, and [the subject] was ordered to serve as temporary catering officer
　　while maintaining his regular duties, served until the 9th month of the same year

1　In the 12th month of the same year, as Washino Shigehachi served exclusively as
　　personal attendant, [the subject] was ordered to serve as temporary catering officer in his place
　　while maintaining regular duties, served until the 1st month of the following year of the Dragon

1　In the 8th month of Meiwa 9, year of the Dragon, was appointed to temporary secretary
　　position for the magistrate

1　In the 9th month of An'ei 2, year of the Snake, regarding candles needed for the magistrate's office,
　　had been receiving them at a rate of one-fourth delivery standard, but through careful management produced
　　100 candles in six months; this arrangement that excelled among all departments was
　　deemed meritorious, and was granted 1 bu of silver as reward

1　In the 10th month of the same year, was appointed as regular secretary
　　for the magistrate, and granted 2 bu of gold as position salary

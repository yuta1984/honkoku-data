Had 1 ryō and 1 person's stipend reduced, was granted 5 ryō and 9 persons' stipend, was assigned to the tozama group and summoned.

- In the 4th year of the same era (1747), 10th month, as requested by head Jinbo Kuranosuke, he was relieved of his duties and transferred to various duties under Ohara Gorōemon's group.

- In the 2nd year of Kan'en (1749), 5th month, he was appointed as guard attendant for Lord Kyōtei's alternating attendance (sankin-kōtai).

- In the 3rd year (1750), 3rd month, he went up to Edo for guard duty and returned in the 9th month of the following year.

However, he was granted one pair of crested sandals and one summer kimono from the campaign treasury disbursement.

- In the 11th month of the same year, it was announced that his role title should be called "御進習番" (Go-shinshū-ban).

- In the same month, he was granted 2 pieces of silver as congratulatory gifts for Lord Kyōtei-in's succession to family headship.

- In the 1st year of Hōreki (1751), 9th month, he was appointed under the command of the attendant guard captain.

- In the 2nd year (1752), 9th month, [he went] to Edo for guard duty...

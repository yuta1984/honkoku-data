One　In the 2nd year of the same era (year of the dog), 2nd month, was granted a hereditary stipend of 250 koku without any reduction upon succession as head of household
　One　In the 6th month of the same year, when the head Ichinose Kenmotsu was relieved of his duties, transferred to Kanno Gonbē's unit as his successor
　One　In the 4th year of Genbun (year of the sheep), 2nd month, petitioned to change his name to Uhē
　One　In the 2nd year of Enkyō (year of the ox), 8th month, was commended along with others who had mastered martial arts for their diligent efforts without neglect
　One　In the 3rd year of the same era (year of the tiger), 10th month, petitioned to change his name to Kurō-uemon

　One　In the 4th year of the same era (year of the rabbit), 2nd month, petitioned to exchange residences with Tateishi Uhē for convenience of location
　One　In the 10th month of the same year, though guard duty was expected, was excused from guard duty as requested due to illness
　One　In the 1st year of Hōreki (year of the sheep), 10th month, went up to Edo for guard duty and came down in the 4th month of the following year of the rooster
　One　In the 6th year of the same era (year of the rat), 12th month, when the head Chino Gonbē was relieved of his duties, transferred to Kasuga Nizaemon's unit as his successor
　One　In the 7th year of the same era (year of the ox), 2nd month, petitioned to adopt his younger brother Kingo as his heir

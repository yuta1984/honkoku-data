- In the 1st year of Genbun (1736), the 4th month, a petition was submitted requesting to make Heizō, the second adopted son of Mochizuki Shinbei, a son-in-law and adopted heir.

- In the 4th year of the same era (1739), the 6th month, as requested by the head, his son Heizō was granted his first audience with the lord.

- In the 2nd year of Kanpō (1742), the 12th month, it was proclaimed that he had served castle guard duty for twenty years without negligence.

- In the 1st year of Enkyō (1744), the 6th month, as requested, his son Heizō was appointed as his replacement guard.

- In the 3rd year of the same era (1746), the 8th month, 9th day, he died of illness at age 65. His posthumous Buddhist name was Seigaku Yūjō Shinji, buried in the same place as mentioned above.

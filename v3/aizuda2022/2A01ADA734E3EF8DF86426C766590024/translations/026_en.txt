Having performed his duties diligently and served well, and with his achievements being outstanding and for special reasons, he was promoted from御近習一之 (First-rank Personal Attendant) to 寄合席 (Assembly Seat status).

One entry: In the first month of Bunka 3, Year of the Tiger (1806), he was assigned to長屋割御用 (Tenement Allocation Affairs) and ordered to serve as an official in charge of construction affairs.

One entry: In the fourth month of the same year, for the current administrative duties, he was ordered to return to Aizu domain. Together with Yajima Denzō and others for internal affairs investigation, he departed from Edo with Tanaka Saburōbei and others, went down to Aizu domain, completed his duties, and arrived back on the fifteenth day of the fifth month.

One entry: In the tenth month of the same year, since he served diligently and performed emergency duties when he went down for unexpected official business in the fourth month of that year, he was granted two pieces of silver as a reward.

One entry: In the eleventh month of the same year, from last autumn until this spring, he was pressed with official duties and worked busily even at night, but since he served diligently and well, he was granted two pieces of silver as a reward.

One entry: In the twelfth month of the same year, for internal affairs, he also went out as a go messenger and incurred labor expenses, so he was granted 2 bu in gold as expense compensation.

He was appointed to positions overseeing the domain's financial affairs, lecture halls, and various training activities.

1. In the 6th month of the 1st year of Kansei, year of the Rooster, when he was ill and absent, his lord made kind inquiries about his health condition.

1. In the 3rd month of the 2nd year of Kansei, year of the Dog, having diligently served for several years in his assigned duties which were of great importance, and having handled matters particularly well over the past four years since taking up his position, with all his conduct being thorough from the beginning and his consultations being well-executed, his achievements were deemed exceptional. Therefore, he was granted an increase of 300 koku in stipend. However, regarding the rice allowance (butsujo), it would be withheld for the time being, and when he submitted a request that it be paid when the domain's private finances permitted, his request was deemed unavoidable given his position, and it was announced that the rice allowance would be withheld for now but would be paid at a later time.

1. In the 4th month of the 3rd year of Kansei, year of the Boar, it was announced that the rice allowance for the 300 koku increase granted in the previous March would be paid starting from this year.

1. In the intercalary 2nd month of the 4th year of Kansei, year of the Rat, Lord Kyotei honored him with a visit, sending in advance 1,000 hiki as sake money and one type of delicacy.

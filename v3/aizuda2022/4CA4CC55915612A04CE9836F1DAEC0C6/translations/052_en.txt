The remaining matters were satisfactorily arranged, bringing no small joy to his lordship, and therefore as congratulations he was bestowed five pieces of standard gold, one load of sake barrels, and one box of side dishes, as was announced.
　However, these were bestowed through a messenger from the attendant guards.

1. In the same month
　On the occasion of Lord Kinbun's private celebration, for presenting formal court dress, he was given one load of sake barrels and one type of side dish.

1. In the same year of the Wood Dragon (1784), in the third month, his sixty-first birthday was celebrated, and he was given one load of sake barrels and one type of side dish.

1. In the fifth month of the same year, for private domain business he departed for Edo, and upon being granted leave in his lordship's presence, he was personally bestowed one bolt of crepe silk in place of a haori jacket, two bolts of thin silk in place of a summer garment, and two bolts of white bleached cloth. When he withdrew to the night quarters, he was given one box of confections through the inner guard, and sake barrels and side dishes were bestowed.

1. In the same month, when the aforementioned business was completed and he returned from Edo, a retainer was sent to the village headman's residence in Takizawa village, and soup and sake were bestowed.

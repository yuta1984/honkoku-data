Saburōbei Genken    Originally named Kazuma, also called Kahei    Born on the 24th day of the 12th month of Genroku 5, year of the Monkey

    Mother: same as above

    Wife: Daughter of Kayano Gonbei Nagaoki, divorced

    Retainer of Lord Honda Shimotsuke-no-kami
    Adopted daughter of Yamamoto Shinuemon    Died of illness on the 29th day of the 10th month of Kyōhō 18, year of the Ox    Buddhist name: Jōshūin Myōshō Nichikaku    Burial site: Myōkokuji Temple at Hōtōzan

    Actual father: daughter of Ashizawa Jūbei

    Retainer of Lord Matsudaira Shuzen
    Daughter of Katsura Jōemon    Died of illness on the 8th day of the 7th month of Kansei 7, year of the Rabbit    Buddhist name: Gyokuchōin-den Junpō Jikō Daishi    Burial site: Tenneiji Temple at Banshōzan

    Concubine: Daughter of Watanabe    Died of illness on the 28th day of the 11th month of Enkyō 3, year of the Rabbit    Buddhist name: Eishōin-den Sokuō Myōsei Daishi    Burial site: Hōsekiji Temple at Izan

1. In the 1st month of Kyōhō 3, year of the Dog, he was granted a new stipend of 300 koku and appointed to the position of go-sōja-ban.
1. In the 7th month of the same 6th year, year of the Ox, he was appointed to oversee the ashigaru foot soldiers.
1. In the 3rd month of the same 9th year, year of the Dragon, he was appointed as head of the bow-bearing retainers.

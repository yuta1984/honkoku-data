In the 11th month of the same year, when Lord Sadaaki wore his court dress (naoshi) for his coming-of-age ceremony, he presented as "osaku-shi" (ceremonial assistant) a set of long and short swords, one box of sea bream, and one load of sake barrels. For serving in the role of osaku-shi for the coming-of-age ceremony, he was given five bundles of cotton, and in the 12th month of the same year, as celebration for performing this role, Lord Sadaaki bestowed upon him in his presence one layer of kosode (short-sleeved kimono) and one set of hemp formal wear.

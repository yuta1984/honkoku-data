Saburōbei Gensai - Initial name: Kosaburō, also Kahei. Initial real name: Genken. Born on the 8th day of the 10th month of Kan'en 1, year of the Dragon.

　　Mother: same as above　　
　
　　Wife: daughter of Ichinose Kanbei Naohiro

1. In the 5th month of Kyōwa 1, year of the Rooster, for presenting a ceremonial sash during Lady Juhōin's pregnancy, he was granted silk fabric (saya) and one box of dried squid, plus 500 hiki of gold by Lord Sadaaki and Lady Juhōin.

1. In the 12th month of Hōreki 10, year of the Dragon, he was granted the hereditary stipend of 1,000 koku without change, appointed to non-official duty status, and his court rank was assigned to the upper level of the non-official group.

1. In the 6th month of Meiwa 2, year of the Rooster, he was appointed to the position of messenger (goshiban) in addition to his regular duties.

1. In the same month, he was appointed to serve as presentation officer (goshōja-ban) for training purposes. When he went up for duty service in the autumn, he was also ordered to serve as messenger during breaks in official duties and to learn through observation.

1. In the 8th month of Kansei 7 (1795), he was appointed as head page and supervisor of the horse division.

1. In the 3rd month of Kansei 8 (1796), because he handled his supervisory duties well, managed affairs properly, and worked diligently in all preparations, he was granted five silver coins as a reward.

1. On the 11th day of the 12th month of Kyōwa 1 (1801), he died of illness at age 30. Buddhist name: Shishun-in-den Gennari Kakudō Koji. Burial place: Banshōzan Tennei-ji Temple.

Same sixth month: He served as an attendant for the lord's return journey and went down (to Aizu domain).

Same year, seventh month: He was appointed to the position of御弓頭 (archery commander).

Same seventh year, year of the Dog, third month: He went up to Edo for duty rotation and returned in the ninth month of the same year.
　　Note: During this campaign
　　When Senkei-in-sama entered Hongo, he was appointed to serve as an attendant, and Kaga-no-Saisho-sama granted him three bolts of Kaga silk through his chamberlain.

Same eleventh year, year of the Tiger, fifth month: He went up to Edo as an attendant for the lord's return journey.

Same thirteenth year, year of the Dragon, tenth month: He went up to Edo for duty rotation and returned in the fifth month of the following year of the Snake, serving as an attendant.

Hōei 1, year of the Monkey, fourth month: He was appointed to the positions of御奏者役 (liaison officer) and御徒支配 (foot soldier commander), and his stipend was granted as before.

Same year, tenth month: He went up to Edo for duty rotation and returned in the fourth month of the following year of the Rooster, serving as an attendant.

Same third year, year of the Dog, second month: His son Muraemon served as a substitute guard

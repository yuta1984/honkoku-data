Saemon Takanobu (7th generation)　　　　Born on the 23rd day of the 11th month of Tenmei 6th year, year of the Horse
　Mother: same as above
　Wife: daughter of Miyake Magobei Tadayoshi　　Divorced
　　daughter of Wada Gunbei Shigechika

One: Kansei 12th year, year of the Horse (1800), 3rd month, as petitioned by his father, he was granted his first audience with the lord.

One: Kyōwa 2nd year, year of the Dog (1802), 1st month, as petitioned by his father, he was appointed as substitute retainer attached to the chief retainer for external vassals. As he was to focus on training in various arts, he was not assigned regular duties and was ordered to submit maintenance fees.
　　However, he was placed in the third unit.

One: Same 3rd year, year of the Boar (1803), 8th month, he participated in the hunting expedition.
　　However, of the five times he participated thereafter, once he attended the horse parade and stayed overnight at Hongōhara.

One: Same year, 9th month, during the current hunting expedition, in both hunts the coordination between units appeared excellent, and the formation, pace, advances and retreats were particularly well-coordinated. In short, recognizing his diligent efforts, the entire unit was granted commendation.

One: 1st year of Genroku (1688), year of the horse, fifth month, due to Hinata Saburōemon being relieved of his position as castellan of Inawashiro Castle, appointed to garrison duty and served.

One: Same 5th year (1692), year of the monkey, fourth month, went up to Edo for guard duty, returned in the same year ninth month.

One: Same 7th year (1694), year of the dog, third month, having reached advanced age and suffering from various illnesses, humbly requested to be relieved of official duties, but after deliberation, by [the lord's] decision, was not granted permission to retire.

One: Same 8th year (1695), year of the boar, second month, as requested, was relieved of official duties.

One: Same 11th year (1698), year of the tiger, eleventh month, as requested, was granted permission to retire.

One: 4th year of Hōei (1707), year of the boar, tenth month, third day, died of illness at age 84. Buddhist name: Jikōin-den Tenshō Juraku Koji. Burial place: same as above.

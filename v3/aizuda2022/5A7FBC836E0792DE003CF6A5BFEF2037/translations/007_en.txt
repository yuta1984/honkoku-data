One: 1st year of Jōō (1652), year of the dragon, seventh month, appointed to messenger service (tsukaiban).

One: Same 2nd year (1653), year of the snake, tenth month, went up to Edo for guard duty (kinban) and served one tour in both temporary liaison duty (kari-otoritsugiyaku) and castle keeper duty (oryūsuiban).

One: 1st year of Meireki (1655), year of the sheep, sixth month, went up to Edo for guard duty.

One: 2nd year of Manji (1659), year of the boar, second month, while on guard duty in Edo, when Lord Matsudaira Kaga-no-kami went to take hot spring baths at Atami, he was appointed as messenger and served by bringing various gifts from the lord's family. Kaga-no-kami bestowed upon him two kosode robes and a haori jacket.

One: Same 3rd year (1660), year of the rat, fourth month, went up to Edo for guard duty.

One: Same year (month unknown), "when he completed one tour of guard duty and returned," due to kind consideration, he was appointed to the role of master of ceremonies (sōjaban).

One: 3rd year of Kanbun (1663), year of the rabbit (month unknown), appointed to oversee foot soldiers (okachi-shihai).

One: Same 4th year (1664), year of the dragon, intercalary fifth month, his petition to adopt his real younger brother Saheiji was granted as requested.

One: Same year, fourth month, at Nikkō, when the memorial service for Lord Daiyūin was completed, as a celebration, he was appointed as messenger from Hōshōin-sama to the council of elders and others...

Gonnosuke Yasuhiro (originally named Jōnosuke, also Kyūgorō, also Jōzaemon, also Shinemon)
　Mother: same as above
　Wife: daughter of Narita Genbei Yasuchika　divorced
　　daughter of Akabane Genbei Tsugatoshi　separated by death

1. In Kyōhō 19th year, year of the Tiger, 9th month, he was granted his first audience with the lord as requested by his father

1. In Kanpō 2nd year, year of the Dog, 8th month, due to his father's merit, he was appointed as a sword practice partner for Lord Dojō, and was then ordered to serve as a practice partner along with others
　　Note: Because he attended as the aforementioned practice partner, he was frequently granted sake

1. In Enkyō 3rd year, year of the Tiger, 8th month, he was appointed as a guard substitute as requested by his father

1. On the 18th day of the 10th month of the same year, he died of illness at the age of twenty-three, posthumous Buddhist name Tengai Chihō Shinji, burial place same as above

In the 6th year, year of the Rooster, 3rd month, while maintaining his current position he was appointed town magistrate, and due to difficulties with the lord's private financial matters, he was assigned to work closely with the chief administrator's office. He was ordered to attend the chief administrator's office, handle all accounting matters and their management procedures, conduct the lord's household financial affairs both large and small, and when he had particular opinions to report them to the magistrate's office. Since there would also be additional temporary duties, in addition to his current role stipend he was given an additional 50 koku

1. In the same month, regarding the reserve funds of towns and rural villages, which served specifically as assistance for the lord's private financial arrangements, there had been flexible handling of payment schedules, but since he was now assigned to work closely with the chief administrator, henceforth the management of reserve funds and the matter of rural village reserve funds should be handled through consultation with Shiba Kanzaemon

In the 8th year, year of the Boar, 3rd month, regarding the previous year-end, though it was a year of financial difficulty, with celebratory events approaching he managed to arrange the necessary funds and meet all essential requirements on time through his exceptional diligence, and as a reward was given one set of formal hemp garments

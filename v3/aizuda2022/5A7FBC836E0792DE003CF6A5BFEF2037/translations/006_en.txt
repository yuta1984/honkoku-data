One: 17th year of Kanei (1640), year of the dragon (month unknown), appointed as substitute (bandai) as his father had petitioned.

One: Same 20th year (1643), year of the sheep, seventh month, regarding the domain transfer to Aizu, when word came from Edo that those with 300 koku or more should be brought up by the cavalry captain (sanjūki bantō) Shinoda Naizen, he reported to Horyō Minbu his desire to go up as a substitute, received permission, and immediately departed. In the eighth month of the same year, he served in the lord's retinue during the domain entry.

One: Same year, eleventh month, during the domain entry, his father was ill and was not supposed to receive another stipend increase, but because he petitioned to go up as a substitute and also served in the lord's retinue during the domain entry, unlike others and due to exceptional circumstances, he was granted an additional increase of 50 koku.

One: 1st year of Shōhō (1644), year of the dragon, tenth month, he inherited the fief of 350 koku without change and was appointed to the external vassal group (gaiyo-gumi), becoming part of Imamura Denjūrō's unit.

One: Same 2nd year (1645), year of the snake, tenth month, he went up to Edo for guard duty (kinban) and served one tour.

One: 1st year of Keian (1648), year of the rat, fourth month, during the lord's pilgrimage to Nikkō Shrine, he served in roadblock duty (tsujigame).

1. In the 1st month of Tenmei 7th year (Year of the Sheep), regarding the matter of instructorship in Inzai-style archery, he received transmission from Moriyama Yogoro and began giving instruction.

1. In the 7th month of the same year, when his disciples' archery skills were viewed by the lord, it was declared that both he and his students performed excellently in archery.

1. In the 1st month of Tenmei 8th year (Year of the Monkey), for dedicating himself to archery and instructing many disciples, and for performing well in the recent large-scale target practice, he was specially favored and awarded five silver coins as a reward.

1. In the 11th month of the same year, upon completion of the construction of the martial arts academy,

[Left page]
　　he was ordered to provide archery instruction to second sons and younger children.

1. In the 10th month of Kansei 1st year (Year of the Rooster), he transferred his archery instructorship to Ogawa Tokunosuke.

1. In the 2nd month of Kansei 4th year (Year of the Rat), he inherited the family headship with a fief of 600 koku granted without change, was appointed to the tozama-gumi unit, entered the third squad, and served as castle guard.

1. In the 9th month of the same year, he was appointed as a squad leader (monotō).

1. In the 12th month of the same year, his request to change his name to Sakon'emon was granted.

1. In the 9th month of Kansei 5th year (Year of the Ox), he participated in the bird hunting expedition.

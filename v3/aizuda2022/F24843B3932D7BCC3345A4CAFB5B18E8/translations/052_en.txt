- In the 4th year of the same era (Kanbun 4, 1664, Year of the Rabbit), 6th month: Went to Edo for guard duty service, completed one tour of duty, and returned.

- In the 1st year of Meireki (1655, Year of the Sheep), 5th month: Went to Edo for guard duty service, completed one tour of duty, and returned.

- In the 2nd year of Manji (1659, Year of the Boar), 9th month: Due to the death by illness of group commander Hori Hanemon, was transferred to Numazawa Kurōbei's group as his successor.

- In the 5th year of Kanbun (1665, Year of the Snake), 2nd month: Was appointed to concurrently serve as district administrator (daikan).

- In the 9th year of the same era (Kanbun 9, 1669, Year of the Rooster), 3rd month: Petitioned to be relieved of the concurrent duty as district administrator, and

【Left Page】
　　the petition was granted as requested.

- In the 10th month of the same year: As funds were being sent down from Edo, was ordered to provide security escort, went to Edo, performed escort duty for the money transport, and returned.

- In the 2nd year of Enpō (1674, Year of the Tiger), 3rd month: Was appointed as commander of long spears (naginata), with a stipend of 100 koku for this role.

- In the 3rd year of the same era (Enpō 3, 1675, Year of the Rabbit), 8th month: For the shrine relocation ceremony at Inawashiro, led his group members to the site and performed the required duties.

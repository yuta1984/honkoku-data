1. In the 4th year of the same era (Keian 4, year of the rabbit, 1651), in the 6th month, he went up to Edo for guard duty and completed one tour of service before returning.

1. In the 1st year of Meireki (year of the sheep, 1655), in the 5th month, he went up to Edo for guard duty and completed one tour of service before returning.

1. In the 2nd year of Manji (year of the boar, 1659), in the 9th month, when group leader Hori Hanemon died of illness, he was assigned to Numazawa Kurōbei's group as his successor.

1. In the 5th year of Kanbun (year of the snake, 1665), in the 2nd month, he was appointed to serve concurrently as a district magistrate (daikan).

1. In the 9th year of the same era (year of the rooster, 1669), in the 3rd month, he petitioned to be

[Left page]
　　relieved of his concurrent duty as district magistrate, and his request was granted.

1. In the 10th month of the same year, when funds were to be sent down from Edo, he was ordered to provide security, went up to escort the money transport, and returned after completing the guard duty.

1. In the 2nd year of Enpō (year of the tiger, 1674), in the 3rd month, he was appointed as chief of long spears (naginata-gashira) and granted a stipend of 100 koku for this role.

1. In the 3rd year of the same era (year of the rabbit, 1675), in the 8th month, for the relocation ceremony of Inawashiro Shrine, he led his group members and went to perform the duties.

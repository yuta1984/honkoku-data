1. Same 2nd year (Year of the Tiger), 11th month: Due to diligent service in regular attendance without negligence, granted an increase of 50 koku

1. Same 11th year (Year of the Boar), 12th month: Granted two used kosode robes

1. Enpō 1st year (Year of the Ox), 7th month, 16th day: Died of illness. Buddhist name: Zenjuin Seizan Kyūshō Koji. Burial place: Sengaku-ji Temple, Shiba, Edo

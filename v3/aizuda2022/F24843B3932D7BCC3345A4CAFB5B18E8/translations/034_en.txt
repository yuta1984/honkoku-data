1. In Kansei 1st year (Year of the Rooster), 10th month: Since his eldest son Choshichiro had died of illness some time before, his request to make his third son Rokudayu the heir was granted as wished.

1. In the 4th year (Year of the Rat), 2nd month: He was granted retirement as requested.

1. In Bunka 3rd year (Year of the Tiger), 7th month, 8th day: He died of illness at age seventy-five. His posthumous name was Kyuho-hiko Shinrei. Burial place: same as above.

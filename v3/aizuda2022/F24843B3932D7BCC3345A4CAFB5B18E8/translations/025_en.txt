Sakon'emon Hisataka (first name Hyōshirō, later Sukesaemon, born in the 3rd year of Kanbun, year of the Hare)
　　Mother: Same as above
　　Wife: Daughter of Kurokawachi Tarōzaemon Asateru
　　　　　Daughter of Sōma Shirōzaemon Shigenaga  
　　　　　Daughter of Baba Zusho Shigekazu

[Left page]
1. In the 1st month of Shōtoku 4 (year of the Horse), as requested by his father, he was appointed to the outer guard unit (goyaku-gumi) and entered Jimbo Mozaemon's unit to serve castle guard duty.

2. In the 8th month of the same 5th year (year of the Sheep), he was transferred to Ueda Hachirōemon's unit.

3. In the 10th month of Kyōhō 1 (year of the Monkey), he went up to Edo for guard duty, and in the 4th month of the following year (year of the Rooster), when the lord returned to the domain, he was appointed as temporary escort guard and accompanied the lord's return.

4. In the 12th month of the same 2nd year (year of the Rooster), he inherited the family headship and was granted a fief of 600 koku without change.

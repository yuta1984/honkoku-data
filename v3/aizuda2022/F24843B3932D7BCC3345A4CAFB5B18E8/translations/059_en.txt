1. In the 16th year (boar year), 10th month, went up to Edo

1. In the 17th year (rat year), 2nd month, was appointed as a

   home in exchange
1. In the same year, 12th month, at the request of head 
   Kayano Gonbei, was relieved of his official position and 
   transferred to serve under Kohara Gorōemon's group as successor

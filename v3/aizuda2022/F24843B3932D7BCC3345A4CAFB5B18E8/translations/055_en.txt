【Left Page】
1. Enpō 5, year of the snake, 8th month: When his elder brother Rokuzaemon died of illness, he was appointed as heir according to his parent's petition.

1. Same 6th year, year of the horse, 1st month: He was granted his first audience with the lord according to his parent's petition.

1. Jōkyō 4, year of the rabbit, 9th month: He was appointed to the guard duty external group according to his parent's petition, joined Yamazaki Sasuke's unit, and served castle guard duty.

1. Genroku 3, year of the horse, 3rd month: He went up to Edo for guard duty service, completed one term of duty, and returned.

1. In the 3rd month of Kansei 8th year (Year of the Dragon), for his excellent handling of his squad members, good command, encouragement of military preparedness, and diligent efforts, he was awarded three silver coins as a reward.

1. In the 12th month of the same year, for his diligent care of the assigned foot soldiers (ashigaru) in archery and firearms training and military formations for the past and current years, sparing no effort or expense in various matters, he was awarded one bundle of cotton as a reward.

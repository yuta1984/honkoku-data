【Right Page】
1. Same 3rd year, year of the rooster, 9th month: He was granted a hereditary stipend of 200 koku without change as family head.

1. Same 11th year, year of the tiger, 5th month: He went up to Edo for guard duty service, completed one term of duty, and returned.

1. Same 14th year, year of the snake, 11th month: When the unit leader Yamazaki Sasuke died of illness, he was transferred to Hori Hanemon's unit as his successor.

1. Hōei 5, year of the rat, 3rd month: Despite being of modest means, he was specifically commended by his lord's thoughts for continuously maintaining horses.

【Left Page】
1. Same 6th year, year of the ox, 11th month: According to unit leader Hori Hanemon's petition, he was relieved of his duty and transferred to Numasawa Gonbei's unit as successor.

1. Same 7th year, year of the tiger, 3rd month: He was commended for serving castle guard duty without negligence.

1. Shōtoku 2, year of the dragon, 4th month: He went up to Edo for guard duty service, completed one term of duty, and returned.

1. Kyōhō 2, year of the rooster, 9th month: He petitioned to adopt his elder brother Shichizaemon's son Kyūemon as son-in-law and heir, and was granted permission.

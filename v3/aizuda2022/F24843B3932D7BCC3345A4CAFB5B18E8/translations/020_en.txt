【Right page】
一　In the eighth month of the same year, he humbly petitioned to be relieved of his official duties, but was told that he should not be relieved of them.
一　In the third month of the second year of Dragon (辰), his son Banzō had been ill for seven years and still had not recovered, making his future service uncertain. Therefore, he was permitted to petition to remove his eldest son from succession and make his youngest son Sukesaemon the heir.
一　In the tenth month of the same year, he went up to Edo for guard duty, and in the fifth month of the following Snake year, he returned as part of the lord's entourage.

　　　　However, he was appointed due to circumstances related to his late father Kyūemon, and thereafter served in this capacity three times.

- Same 5th year, 8th month: Since his son Rokuzaemon died of illness, his request to make his third son Sannosuke his heir was granted.

- Jōkyō 3rd year, 1st month: His request to be relieved of his position as御長柄頭 (Onagayarigashira - spear captain) due to illness was granted.

- Same year, 3rd month: He was appointed to the non-official group (無役組).

[Left page]
- Same 4th year, 9th month: As requested, his son Sannosuke was appointed as his deputy (御番代).

- Genroku 6th year, 9th month: His request for retirement was granted as wished.

- Same 10th year, 5th month, 2nd day: Died of illness at age 79. Buddhist name: Jushōin Kokuyo Dōkyū Koji. Burial place: Jōkokuji Temple on Mount Kokon.

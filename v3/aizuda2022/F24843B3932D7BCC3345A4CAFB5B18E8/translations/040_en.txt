　　At that time, since there was no one equivalent to an instructor in the Insai school of archery, though it was a burdensome matter, he was ordered to provide instruction for a while.

1. In the 5th month of the same year, he was relieved of his concurrent duties as chief supervisor of tree planting operations.

1. In the 12th month of the same year, for properly investigating the protocols for preliminary visits to the castle, advance visits, and shrine visits, he was granted sake.

1. In the 10th month of the 8th year (Year of the Sheep), he went up to Edo for guard duty,

[Left page]
　　and returned in the 4th month of the following year (Year of the Monkey).

1. In the 12th month of the same year, despite his main duties being numerous, he diligently performed his archery instruction duties and successfully trained many disciples, for which he was granted one tan of bleached cloth as a substitute for hemp formal garments as a reward.

1. In the 3rd month of the 9th year (Year of the Monkey), to maintain discipline among the retainers at the Wadakura, Shiba, and Mita residences, he personally made nighttime rounds within the residences. Since everyone showed dedication and worked diligently in their rounds, this was considered especially commendable, and he was granted sake.

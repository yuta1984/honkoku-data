1. In the 3rd year (Year of the Horse), 3rd month: Due to the lord's visit to Edo, he served as escort guard and went up to Edo, returning in the 10th month.
   Note: In the 6th month during the campaign, he was appointed as chamberlain attendant.

1. In the 4th year (Year of the Sheep), 2nd month: He received custody from Yokoyama Genzaemon and Komatsu Denzo, and transferred it to Ariga Kyuzaemon in the 3rd month.

1. In Tenmei 4th year (Year of the Dragon), intercalary 1st month: Due to poor harvests in neighboring provinces, thinking that criminals and thieves might have infiltrated, for security purposes, he was ordered to patrol outside the castle walls day and night with two men, and if suspicious persons were found,

[Left page]
   they should be captured and handed over to the local officials.

1. In the 6th year (Year of the Horse), 9th month: For diligently serving three years in security patrol duty, he was granted two bundles of cotton.

1. In the 7th year (Year of the Sheep), 1st month: He was entrusted with the custody of Nakamura Chuujiro's retainer Inokuchi Hyoji, kept him for four days, then transferred him to the court of justice.
   Note: From where he received custody is unclear.

1. In the 8th year (Year of the Monkey), 3rd month: He was appointed as head of the senior councilor groups in the lord's presence.

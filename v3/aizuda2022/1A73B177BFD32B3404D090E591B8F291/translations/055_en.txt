One: Genroku 4 (Year of the Sheep, 1691), intercalary 8th month - As his son Yagōrō died of illness, he petitioned and was granted permission to make his second son Hachibei his heir.

One: Genroku 10 (Year of the Ox, 1697), 10th month - Nakamura Jinbei was placed under his custody. In the 11th month of the same year, Jinbei was granted leave.

One: Kan'ei 4 (Year of the Boar, 1627), 8th month - Appointed to the position of captain of ashigaru foot soldiers (o-sute-gashira-yaku).

One: Kan'ei 5 (Year of the Rat, 1628), 5th month - As his son Hachibei also died of illness, he petitioned and was granted permission to adopt Ikuemon, the fourth son of Kamio Denzaemon, as his heir.

One: Kan'ei 6 (Year of the Ox, 1629), 4th month - He petitioned and was granted permission to surrender his son Ikuemon's stipend and take him back under his own care.

One: Same year, 10th month - As petitioned, he was exempted from official duties, and due to his many years of faithful service, he was granted direct retirement.

One: Shōtoku 4 (Year of the Horse, 1714), 10th month, 23rd day - Died of illness at the age of 75. Buddhist name: Buishōin Dōyū Nikkan. Burial place: Hōshōzan Jōkōji Temple.

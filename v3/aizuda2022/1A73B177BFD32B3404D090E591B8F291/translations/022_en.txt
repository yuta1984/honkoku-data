1　In the 3rd month of the 2nd year (year of the Tiger), when he went up for the third rotation of regular duty, since he had been
　　appointed to guest reception the previous winter and served with diligence,
　　considering this among other things, his customary gifts were changed and he was given one of the lord's used small

　　　However, the item was to be bestowed later in the lord's presence, but
　　　due to pressing official business, it was bestowed at the retainer's office

1　In the 4th month of the same year, he completed his service and returned

1　In the 5th month of the 3rd year (year of the Rabbit), he was appointed to the position of bow bearer captain

One: Jōkyō 2 (Year of the Ox, 1685), 12th month - As petitioned, he was exempted from official duties and appointed to non-duty status (mu-yaku). He was ordered to coordinate and serve together with Endō Ichirōbei.

One: Jōkyō 3 (Year of the Tiger, 1686), 3rd month - Appointed to the non-duty group (mu-yaku-gumi).

and assigned to Sugita Gorōbei's unit, where he served castle guard duty.

One: Jōkyō 5 (Year of the Dragon, 1688), 9th month - Traveled to Edo for duty rotation, served one tour of duty, and returned.
Note: During his duty rotation, he was temporarily appointed to messenger duties (kari-tsukai-yaku) and temporary master of ceremonies (kari-sōja-yaku), which he performed.

One: Genroku 2 (Year of the Snake, 1689), 4th month - Appointed to the position of captain (mono-gashira).
Note: As captain, he served three tours of Edo duty rotation and once served as an attendant for a lord's return journey.

One: Same year, 5th month - Requested and was permitted to change his name to Iemon.

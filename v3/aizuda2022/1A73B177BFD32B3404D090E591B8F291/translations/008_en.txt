1　In Genroku 1, year of the Dragon, 11th month, due to old age and illness making it difficult to perform official duties, he apologized and requested relief, whereupon he was told to continue serving until a successor could be appointed

1　In the same 2nd year, year of the Snake, 3rd month, having received orders to assist with the Bakufu's construction work at the Third Bailey, he went up to Edo and served until completion in the 11th month

1　In the same year, 11th month, as there was official business at Edo, he was summoned by official letter to appear at the castle at the hour of the Horse (10 AM). When he appeared at the castle, Toda Yamashiro-no-kami and Akimoto Tajima-

[Left page]
　　no-kami were present in formal session, and regarding the Third Bailey construction work, as everyone had worked diligently, Yamashiro-no-kami announced that as a reward, two court robes, one haori jacket, and ten pieces of silver would be granted, which he received

1　In the same month when he was granted leave, he was awarded one used court kosode garment and five pieces of silver

1　In the same 3rd year, year of the Horse, 6th month, as his son Yagozaemon had been ordered to commit seppuku in the 2nd month of that year, his request that his second son Nuinosuke-zaemon be established as heir was granted

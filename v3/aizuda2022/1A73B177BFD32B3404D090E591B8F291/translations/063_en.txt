【Right page】
一　In the 3rd month of Kyōhō 19 (Year of the Tiger), went to
　　Edo for guard duty and returned in the 9th month of the same year
　　　However, as a bestowment for teisho, one set of formal court dress was
　　　granted
一　In the 9th month of Kyōhō 20 (Year of the Rabbit), went to
　　Edo for guard duty and returned in the 3rd month of the following Year of the Dragon
　　　However, as a bestowment for teisho, one set of formal court dress was
　　　granted
一　In the same month, was appointed as imperial messenger to Kyoto
　　for the imperial enthronement, to depart from his guard duty post
【Left page】
　　Made court visits to both the Imperial Palace and Sentō Palace,
　　presented the tribute items through heavenly petition and deposited them at Naishi-dokoro, and on the same day
　　received an imperial response, was summoned by
　　Kyoto Deputy Toki Tango-no-kami and granted leave
一　In the 3rd month of Genbun 3 (Year of the Horse), went to
　　Edo for guard duty and returned in the 9th month of the same year
　　　However, upon his return, having served diligently through four terms of guard duty without incident,
　　　the teisho bestowment of formal court dress was changed to
　　　lined garments and granted

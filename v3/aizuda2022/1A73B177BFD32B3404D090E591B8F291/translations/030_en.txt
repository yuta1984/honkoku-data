One: In the 6th month of Bunka 6, year of the Snake, the martial arts training ground within the school was discontinued. As a reward for his dedication as an instructor up to that point, he was granted two bundles of cotton
One: In the same month, as Ogasawara Jōnosuke was to go up to Edo, he was instructed to serve as the current martial arts instructor in his place. However, since martial arts training was generally conducted at private residences and not within the school, and since there was no private training ground either, he had been placed within the school facility. Now that training within the school was being discontinued, as he was the current instructor, he was to conduct instruction at his residence and report to the military academy

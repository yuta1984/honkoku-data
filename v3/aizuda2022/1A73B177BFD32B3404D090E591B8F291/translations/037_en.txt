- Same day: At the time of the imperial return
　　Lord Dotsū accompanied [the shogun] on his return journey, so
　　he finished the remaining official duties, immediately served as an envoy for congratulations on Lord Sakai Sanuki-no-kami's entry into Wakasa province, and returned to Takato in the 9th month

- Same 12th year (Year of the Boar), month unknown:
　　Due to Lady Jōkōin's illness,
　　when Lord Dotsū returned to Takato castle,
　　he served as Edo caretaker together with Kitahara Uneme

- Same year, 11th month: [The lord] performed sankin attendance, and as gratitude for the various hospitalities received from lords along the route during last year's journey to Kyoto, he was appointed as an envoy to Lord Mizuno Kenmostu, Lord Toda Saemon, Lord Matsudaira Mimasaku, Lord Hōjō Dewa-no-kami, Lord Matsudaira Etchū-no-kami, Higashi Gomonzeki, Lord Aoyama Ōkura, Nijō Goban, Lord Hoshina Danjōchū, and Lord Abe Settsu-no-kami, and returned to Takato on the last day of the 12th month

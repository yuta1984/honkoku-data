【Right page】
　　I would be employed as a close attendant, so I was to relinquish my position as ashigaru (foot soldier), and I traveled by palanquin during the journey. After completing the official business in Kyoto, I went to Kameyama as a messenger to Lord Matsudaira Iga-no-kami, and returned to Edo on the thirteenth day of the twelfth month

一　Same third year (Year of the Horse): In the third month, I was granted the former residence site of Adachi Hyōzaemon

一　Same year, tenth month: I petitioned to adopt Chōgorō, the second son of Kamio Shirōzaemon, and was granted permission

一　First year of Meireki (Year of the Sheep): When the weapons storehouse burned down

【Left page】
　　I was ordered to inspect and clean the various items that survived the fire, including guns and other burned metal fittings

一　Same month: When Korean envoys came to Japan and arrived in Edo, and messengers from Korea also came to our residence accompanied by a senior retainer of Lord Sō Tsushima-no-kami, I was appointed to receive documents and serve as host

一　Same third year (Year of the Rooster), first month: During the great fire in Edo, while I was temporarily staying in Aizu, I took leave from the senior retainers, departed on the second day of the second month, arrived in Edo on the fifth day of the same month, and when I had an audience, I was instructed to go to Tōkai-ji Temple

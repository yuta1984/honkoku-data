1. In the 3rd month of Kansei 1 (year of the rooster), he was appointed to assist with dining etiquette training and supervision, and was granted 2 bu of gold annually without interest as expense allowance.

1. In the 12th month of the same year, he was granted his hereditary stipend of 350 koku without change, was appointed to the tozama group, entered Kasuga Jin'zaemon's unit, and served guard duty at the castle.

1. In the 2nd month of Kansei 2 (year of the dog), as requested, he was relieved of his duties assisting with dining etiquette instruction and supervision.

1. In the 8th month of Kansei 3 (year of the boar), he was appointed as messenger guard (tsukaiban).

1. In the 3rd month of Kansei 4 (year of the rat), he participated in the bird hunting event and received the birds he caught as a gift.
　　Note: After this, he participated twice more and received the birds he caught each time.

1. In the 4th month of Kansei 5 (year of the ox), he went up to Edo for guard duty and returned in the 10th month of the same year.
　　Note: When he returned, he was commended for his diligent service in the camp's formal reception hall.

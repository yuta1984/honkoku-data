One: In the 3rd month of Kansei 1 (Year of the Rooster, 1789), he was appointed to serve as assistant instructor for meal service training and record-keeping, and was granted 2 bu of gold annually without interest as expense assistance.

One: In the 12th month of the same year, he was granted his hereditary stipend of 350 koku without change, was appointed to the gaisama group, entered Kasuga Jinzaemon's unit, and served castle guard duty.

One: In the 2nd month of Kansei 2 (Year of the Dog, 1790), as requested, he was relieved from his duties as assistant meal service instructor and record-keeper.

One: In the 8th month of Kansei 3 (Year of the Boar, 1791), he was appointed as messenger official (go-tsukaiban).

　　Both Daemon and Jinbei, after their father was ordered to commit seppuku, served together under Lord Kuroda Yamato-no-kami, according to what has been passed down in the Tōnoki family. However, in Hōei 3, year of the Dog, 3rd month, when it was announced that Kōzaemon, son of the deceased elder brother Yagozaemon and nephew of Tōnoki Nuinosuke, would be taken into service by Lord Akita Awaji-no-kami, they petitioned for permanent leave, which was granted as requested, according to the official records

1. In the 9th month of the same year, he was appointed to messenger service
1. In the 1st month of the same 8th year of the Tiger, his request to change his name to Iemon was granted
1. In the 4th month of the same year, he went up to Edo for garrison duty and returned in the 10th month
　　　However, during garrison duty, he was appointed to regular additional presenter service
1. In the 12th month of the same year, when Iinuma Kanzaemon fell ill during that autumn and returned home upon request, he was appointed to the presenter service position

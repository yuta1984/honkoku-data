One: In the tenth month of the same year, he was ordered to take charge of the buying and selling of rice.

One: In the twelfth month of the same year, having served diligently in the role of "Goryakushi-Ukagai-Yaku" (the position overseeing frugality measures and inquiries) and having incurred expenses, he was granted two bu from the funds of the Gimmi-sho (Inquiry Office) as a supplementary allowance for expenses.

One: In the fifth month of the eighth year (Year of the Rabbit), as requested, he was relieved of the role of Goryakushi-Ukagai-Yaku.

One: In the seventh month of the same year, he was appointed as a hired official of the Goyosho (Administrative Office).

One: In the eighth month of the same year, in sequence after Hirosawa Katsuemon, he was appointed as a minor official (ko-yakunin) of the Gimmi-sho (Inquiry Office).

One: In the second month of An'ei 1 (Year of the Dragon), having served diligently as a hired official, he was appointed as a full official of the Goyosho (Administrative Office).

One: In the third month of the same year, he submitted a request to change his name from Chuemon.

One: In the fifth month of the same year, he traveled up to Edo for his term of duty (kinban), and returned in the tenth month of the same year.
　　Note: It was declared that even during his time in service there (in Edo), he performed his duties diligently.

One entry: In the tenth month of the same year, he was ordered to oversee the buying and selling of rice.

One entry: In the twelfth month of the same year, having served diligently in his assigned role under the austerity measures (o-shōryaku nin'yaku) and having incurred expenses in doing so, he was granted two bu from the funds of the Ginmisho (Investigation Office) as reimbursement for those expenses.

One entry: In the fifth month of the same 8th year (Meiwa 8, Year of the Rabbit), in accordance with his petition, he was relieved of his duties under the austerity measures assignment.

One entry: In the seventh month of the same year, he was appointed as a hired official (o-yatoi) of the Goyōsho (Administrative Office).

One entry: In the eighth month of the same year, in terms of seniority, he was appointed above the junior officials (ko-yakunin) of the Ginmisho, ranked immediately below Hirosawa Katsuemon.

One entry: In the second month of Anei 1 (Year of the Dragon), having served diligently as a hired official, he was appointed as a full official (yakunin) of the Goyōsho.

One entry: In the third month of the same year, his petition to change his name to Chūemon was granted.

One entry: In the fifth month of the same year, he traveled up to Edo for garrison duty (kinban), and returned in the tenth month of the same year.
　　Note: Although this was his first active service deployment (shojin), it was declared that he had served with great diligence.

1. In the third month of the same third year (Year of the Boar), regarding Lord Sadaaki's journey down (from Edo), he returned to serve as an escort, went up to serve as an attendant, and then returned.

1. In the fifth month of the same year, although the number of people for the lord's travels was reduced, he was commended for serving with particular diligence without any absences.

1. In the same month, regarding this journey down, since the number of people along the travel route was reduced, it was a hardship for all the attendants, but everyone showed determination and served with diligence, allowing the lord's travel to proceed without delay. Therefore, sake was bestowed upon all the attendants.

1. In the fourth month of Bunka 1 (Year of the Rat), he went up to serve guard duty accompanying the lord for the sankin (alternate attendance), and returned in the tenth month of the same year.

1. In the tenth month of the same year, although the number of people for the lord's travels was reduced, he was commended for serving with particular diligence without any absences.

1. In the same month, since the number of people along the travel route was reduced, it was a hardship for all the guard attendants, but everyone showed determination and served with diligence, allowing [the journey to proceed] without delay

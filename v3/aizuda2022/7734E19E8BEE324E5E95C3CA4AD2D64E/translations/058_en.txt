The formations were well-aligned, the units were properly connected, operations proceeded without delay, and it was recognized that he had been diligent in combined training exercises over time. His peers all praised him for this.

First: In the same month - The military exercises were changed, and he was relieved from the personal guard unit. Due to his diligent service up to this point, he was granted two bundles of cotton as a reward. His son's audience was maintained, and it was announced that he could attend the literary and military academy at his own discretion.

First: In the 3rd year of Tatsumi (1832), 5th month - He was appointed to serve as substitute for Hara Chubei's official duties and served until the 10th month.
　　However, from this time forward, he was appointed to serve as substitute for official duties annually.

First: In the 4th year of Tsuchinoe-Mi (1833), 9th month - He was ordered to concentrate on training in Taishi-ryu swordsmanship, and especially to supervise the diligent training of various students.

First: In the 5th year of Tsuchinoe-Uma (1834), 11th month - Not only did he pursue his own training in martial arts, but he also supervised the practice of various students, attending daily without even having time to take over his father and elder brothers' menial labor. In recognition of this exceptional dedication, he was granted two bu in gold.

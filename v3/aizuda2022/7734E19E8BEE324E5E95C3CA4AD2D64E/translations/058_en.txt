The formations were well-aligned, the units were properly connected, operations proceeded without delay, and gradually through combined training exercises, his diligence was recognized. His Lordship expressed satisfaction and bestowed praise upon all the companions.

First: In the same month - The military exercises were changed, and he was relieved from the personal guard unit. Due to his diligent service up to this point, he was rewarded with two bundles of cotton, his son was granted an audience and allowed to remain, and it was announced that he could attend the literary and military academy at his discretion.

First: In the 3rd year of Tatsumi (1833), 5th month - He was appointed as substitute for Hara Chūbei's official duties and served until the 10th month.
　　However, from this point forward, he was appointed to substitute duties annually.

First: In the 4th year of Tsuchinotomi (1834), 9th month - He was ordered to undertake specialized training in Taishi-ryū swordsmanship, and furthermore to diligently oversee the training of various students.

First: In the 5th year of Kinoeum (1835), 11th month - Not only did he practice martial arts for his own training, but he also supervised the training of various students, going out daily without even time to spare for the domestic labor of gathering firewood and water for his father and elder brothers. Due to this exceptional dedication, he was granted two bu in gold.

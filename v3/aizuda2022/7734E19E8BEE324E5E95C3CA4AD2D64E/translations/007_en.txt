一　In the 2nd year of Kanbun, year of the tiger (1662)
　　Due to a change in the shogunate's accounting magistrates, he went up to Edo,
　　had an audience with the said officials, completed the accounting, and
　　returned.

一　At the end of the same year, when the people of Yamaguchi village in Ikita
　　submitted a petition to the shogunate,
　　he was ordered to settle the matter, and in the 4th year, year of the dragon (1664), 3rd month, he went up to Edo to express gratitude,
　　and while there completed the accounting before returning.

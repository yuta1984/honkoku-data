Shichiemon Takanori (originally named Shichinoshin, also Yūzaemon) - adopted son of Ogawa Gōzaemon Takaharu

　　Mother: Same as above

Bunzaemon Masanobu (originally named Bunpachi; born in Kyōhō 1st year)

　　Mother: Adopted daughter of the Tōyama family

　　Wife: Daughter of Tōyama Toneri Tamekō

• Kyōhō 13th year (1728), 1st month: As requested by his father, he was granted his first audience with the lord.

• Hōreki 4th year (1754), 4th month: As requested by his father, he was appointed as guard substitute and served castle guard duty in Ohara Gorōemon's unit.

• Same 6th year (1756), 3rd month: His request to change his name to Bunzaemon was granted.

• Same 8th year (1758), 2nd month: As requested by unit leader Ohara Gorōemon, he was relieved of his duties, with Kajiwara Heima as his successor.

Nakamura Giemon Nariaki (originally named Seki, originally Morinosuke, also Hachirobee, also Nikaidō Jūzaemon, born in Enpō 2, year of the Tiger)

Mother

Wife: adopted daughter of Hayashi Yatayū Shigenari

Actually the daughter of Machida Naotayū Takatō

1. Kyōhō 3, year of the Dog, 3rd month
   Summoned to serve as a lady-in-waiting to Lady Eikōin

1. Genbun 2, year of the Snake, 4th month
   Upon the passing of Lady Eikōin, was granted leave,
   and for having served well for several years, was granted
   a stipend for two persons

1. Enkyō 2, year of the Ox, 8th month, 7th day, died of illness at age 63
   Buddhist posthumous name

1. Genroku 9, year of the Rat, 11th month, as requested by his father,
   was appointed as guard substitute and served castle guard duty
   in Kamio Rokuzaemon's unit

1. Same 10th year, year of the Ox, 3rd month, went up to Edo for guard duty, same

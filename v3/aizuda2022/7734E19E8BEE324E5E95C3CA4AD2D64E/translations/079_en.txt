1. In the third month of Kansei 4 (Year of the Rat), he was granted his first formal audience (omemie) as requested by his parent.

1. In the eighth month of Kansei 6 (Year of the Tiger), he was granted a hereditary stipend of 150 koku without change and was assigned to the Tozama group. Since he was not assigned regular duties for the purpose of artistic training, he was ordered to submit small construction fees and was assigned to Kasuga Jinzaemon's group.

1. In the ninth month of the same year, because he showed exceptional diligence in spear techniques, he was granted sake at the Shogakuryo (academy).

1. In the second month of Kansei 9 (Year of the Snake), because he showed diligence in martial arts and had skills, his small construction fees were exempted, he was assigned regular duties, and began serving castle guard duty.

1. In the eighth month of the same year, he participated in the bird hunting expedition (oidori-gari).

1. In the seventh month of Kansei 10 (Year of the Horse), for his continuous diligence in spear training from the previous year through last year, achieving skills of nine-tenths or above, this was deemed remarkable and he was granted one bundle of cotton as a reward.

　一　In Kanbun 3, year of the rat (1663), 1st month, he was granted an increase of two ryō of gold

　一　In Enpō 3, year of the rabbit (1675), 5th month, he was ordered to undergo the coming-of-age ceremony, granted eight ryō in ceremonial money and eight ryō two bu for seasonal gifts, totaling sixteen ryō two bu and stipend for four people, and appointed to the lord's escort guard

　一　In the 4th year, year of the dragon (1676), 4th month, he went up to Edo as an escort for guard duty, and came down in the 10th month of the same year

　一　In the 6th year, year of the horse (1678), 10th month, he went up to Edo for guard duty, and in the 4th month of the following year of the sheep, he came down serving as escort when the lord returned to the domain

　一　In the 8th year, year of the monkey (1680), 10th month, he went up to Edo for guard duty and came down in the 4th month of the following year of the rooster

　一　In Tennā 2, year of the dog (1682), 4th month, he went up to Edo for guard duty, was appointed as a camp gate guard, and came down in the 10th month of the same year

　一　In the 3rd year, year of the boar (1683), 10th month, he went up to Edo for guard duty and came down in the 10th month of the following year of the rat

　一　In Jōkyō 1, year of the rat (1684), 10th month, because there was a sick person among his colleagues, he was sent back for guard duty to Edo and came down after completing one tour of duty

　一　In the 3rd year, year of the tiger (1686), 3rd month, he was appointed to the external retainer unit

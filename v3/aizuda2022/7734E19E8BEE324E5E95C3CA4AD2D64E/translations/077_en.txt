1. In the 8th month of Meiwa 4, year of the Boar (1767), he was granted his first audience as requested by his parent.

1. In the 8th month of the same 7th year, year of the Tiger (1770), he was granted the hereditary stipend of 150 koku without change, appointed to the tozama-gumi unit, and served castle guard duty in Yamazaki Sasuke's group.

1. In the 2nd month of the same 8th year, year of the Rabbit (1771), as requested by group leader Yamazaki Sasuke, he was relieved of his duties, and his successor was assigned to Tanaka Kahē's group.

1. In the 9th month of An'ei 3, year of the Horse (1774), he went up to Edo for guard duty service, and in the 5th month of the following year of the Sheep (1775), during the lord's return journey, he was appointed as temporary attendant guard and accompanied the lord down.

1. In the 6th month of the same year, during the lord's return journey, it was proclaimed that he had served diligently as temporary attendant guard without absence.

1. In the 2nd month of the same 5th year, year of the Monkey (1776), group leader Tanaka Kahē was reassigned to another position, and the successor was assigned to Hi Jinzaemon's group.

1. In the 2nd month of the same 6th year, year of the Rooster (1777), his request to change his name to Kyūzō was granted.

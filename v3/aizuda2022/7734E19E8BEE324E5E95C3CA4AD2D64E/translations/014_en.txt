June: Accompanied the lord on his return journey from Edo

1. Same 13th year, year of the Dragon, 9th month: Went up to Edo for guard duty,
   returned in the 3rd month of the following year of the Snake

1. Same 14th year, year of the Snake, 8th month: Was granted the hereditary
   stipend of 300 koku without change

1. Hōei 2, year of the Rooster, 1st month: As requested by unit commander
   Kamio Rokuzaemon, his stipend was confiscated and he was transferred
   to Yanase Sanzaemon's unit as replacement

1. Same year, 8th month: When unit commander Yanase Sanzaemon was
   appointed as wakadoshiyori, he was transferred to Hyūga Saburōemon's unit as replacement

1. Same 4th year, year of the Boar, 8th month: Six years earlier, at the request of
   Murakami Shirōbee's daughter, he forcibly delivered a love letter through
   Saka Sōemon to Sōzaemon. For this conduct unbecoming of a samurai, his
   stipend was confiscated and he was dismissed

1. Kyōhō 6, year of the Ox, 1st month: Was permitted to return to service,
   granted a stipend for five persons, and summoned to regular duty as a minor guard
1. Same year: During the lord's residence in the domain, served as palace guard
   among the tozama retainers

1. Same 9th year, year of the Dragon, 7th month: Was granted guard rank with
   a ceremonial stipend of 15 koku and support for three persons, plus one person's support for Edo duty

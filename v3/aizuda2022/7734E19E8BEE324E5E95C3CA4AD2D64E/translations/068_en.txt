He was appointed as a guard captain (goban-dai) and entered Ichinose Jingozaemon's unit, serving as castle guard.

First: Same 3rd year of Inu (1718), 2nd month - He inherited the family headship and was granted the fief of 100 koku without change.

First: Same 4th year of I (1719), 3rd month - He was appointed to concurrently serve as deputy magistrate (daikan).

First: Same 5th year of Ne (1720), 3rd month - Because he devoted himself to his duties and performed them well, he was granted three pieces of silver as a reward.

First: Same 6th year of Ushi (1721), 7th month - When he petitioned to resign from his position, he was told he should continue serving and his written petition was returned.

First: Same 7th year of Tora (1722), 7th month - When he petitioned to resign from his position, he was ordered to take care of his health and continue serving.

First: Same 9th year of Tatsu (1724), 7th month - It was announced that he had devoted himself to his duties and served well.

First: Same 10th year of Mi (1725), 7th month - Because he devoted himself to his duties and served well, he was granted an increase of 50 koku.

First: Same 11th year of Uma (1726), 1st month - He was appointed to supervise both the Ogawa and Shōnozawa units.

First: Same 12th year of Hitsuji (1727), 7th month - It was announced that he had devoted himself to his duties and served well.

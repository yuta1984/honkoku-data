Wife: adopted daughter of adoptive father Giemon Nariaki, divorced

1. Ganmon 3, year of the Dragon, 3rd month: was granted ordinary stipend of 8 ryō in gold and rice allowance for 4 people, and was summoned to serve as page (okoshō)

1. Enkyō 2, year of the Ox, 10th month: as requested by his father, was released from page duties and his stipend was withdrawn

1. Same year 3, year of the Tiger, 6th month: due to incompatibility, returned to his original family name

Daughter: adopted daughter of Giemon Nariyasu

　　Mother: adopted daughter of grandfather Giemon Nariaki

Giemon Nariyasu (adopted son)　　original name Shinjūrō

　Biological father: second son of Takagi Nuido-emon Shigetsura, born in Hōei 1, year of the Rat

　Wife: adopted daughter of adoptive father Giemon Nariaki, died of illness on the 23rd day of the 1st month of Kansei 12, year of the Monkey, at age 71; posthumous Buddhist name Gyōkaku-in Shaku-ni Shunryō, buried at Muryōzan Chōmei-ji Temple

Biological father: Daughter of Machida Naotayū Mitutoki

　　Giemon Narumitsu　　　　《Initially named Kōta, later Kurando》

　　　Biological father: Fourth son of Koike Shirōbee Hanari　《Born on the 14th day of the 4th month of Genbun 3, year of the Horse》

　　　Wife: Adopted daughter of adoptive father Giemon Nari□　《Born on the 14th day of the 2nd month of Kanpō 1, year of the Rooster; died of illness on the 29th day of the 9th month of Bunka 14, year of the Ox, at age 77; posthumous Buddhist name Gyōshōin Shakuni Jōtei; burial place same as above》

1. In the 12th month of Hōreki 7, year of the Ox, he was granted his first formal audience with the lord as requested by his parent

1. In the 5th month of Tenmei 4, year of the Dragon, he inherited the family headship with a stipend of 100 koku without change, was appointed to the gaisama group, joined Ikoma Kuranoske's unit, and served guard duty at the castle

1. In the 12th month of the same 6th year, year of the Horse, Giemon petitioned to change his name and was granted permission

1. In the 6th month of Kansei 1, year of the Rooster, by the lord's will regarding head Ikoma Kuranoske, he was relieved of his duties and transferred to Ibuka Mono group as a successor role

1. In the 9th month of the same 5th year, year of the Ox, he participated in the hunting expedition

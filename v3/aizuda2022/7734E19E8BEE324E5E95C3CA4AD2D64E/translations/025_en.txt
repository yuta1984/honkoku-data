1. Enkyō 3rd year of the Tiger, 5th month: As requested by his father, was granted his first formal audience with the lord

1. Same year, 8th month: Was granted the hereditary stipend of 100 koku without change, was appointed to the Aizu Katte Tozama group, entered Kayano Gonbei's unit, and served castle guard duty

1. Hōreki 6th year of the Rat, 7th month: Requested and was granted permission to adopt Kuranosuke, the fourth son of Koike Shirōbei, as son-in-law and heir

1. Same 7th year of the Ox, 3rd month: At the request of group leader Kayano Gonbei, was relieved of duty and transferred to Kasuga Nizaemon's unit as successor

1. Same 9th year of the Rabbit, 9th month: At the request of group leader Kasuga Nizaemon, was relieved of duty and transferred to Takekawa Sukeemon's unit

1. Same 13th year of the Sheep, 1st month: Group leader Takekawa Sukeemon was ordered to change positions, and he was transferred to Ikoma Asaemon's unit as successor

1. Same year, 2nd month: Was ordered to concurrently serve as horse inspector (koma-aratame-yaku)

1. Meiwa 2nd year of the Rooster, 9th month: Due to the death by illness of group leader Ikoma Gohei, was transferred to Harada Matasuke's unit as successor

1. Same 3rd year of the Dog, 11th month: Was granted the former residence of Miyake Magoshichi

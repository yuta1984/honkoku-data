1. In the 1st month of the same 3rd year, year of the Tiger
　　　　When Lord Sadaaki's remains were brought down to the domain, he served as attendant and went down, and
　　　　served guard duty at the temporary palace

　1. In the 2nd month of the same year, he was relieved of his current duties, and the succession to his eldest son was
　　　appointed, and as a reward for his diligent service up to that point, he was given
　　　2 pieces of silver and 1 bundle of cotton

　1. In the same month
　　　He was appointed to serve as a hired attendant (gokoshō) until Lord Sadaaki's first anniversary, and
　　　served guard duty at the Sannomaru temporary palace

　1. In the 4th month of the same year
　　　From Lord Sadaaki's personal funds, those who served closely and
　　　worked diligently were to be rewarded, as had been ordered while he was still alive,
　　　and he was given 1 ryō of gold

　1. In the 8th month of the same year
　　　As mementos of Lord Sadaaki, various garments were bestowed upon him

　1. In the 10th month of the same year, it was announced that his
　　　diligent service during the journey when the coffin was brought down in the spring
　　　had come to His Lordship's attention

　1. In the 5th month of the same 4th year, year of the Rabbit, he inherited the family headship and was granted a fief of 100 koku without fail,
　　　and was appointed to the outer retainer group (tozama-gumi), under Miyake

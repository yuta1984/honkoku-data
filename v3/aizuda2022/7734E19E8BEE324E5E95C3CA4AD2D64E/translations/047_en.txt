First: In the eleventh month of the same year, for working diligently on cost-reduction matters, he was granted 2 bu of gold as a reward

First: In the twelfth month of the same year, regarding financial administration matters, this being a new arrangement, he worked diligently and incurred labor costs, for which he was granted 3 bu of gold

First: In the twelfth month of the same 6th year, year of the ox (1769), due to few colleagues being available, he worked diligently on numerous duties, and as a reward, all colleagues were privately granted 2 bu of gold each
　　Note: After this, gold was granted privately on two more occasions

First: In the eighth month of the same year, for working diligently on stepping into official duties, although it was a weighty matter, he was appointed to the rank of dokurai (independent audience), as announced, and was appointed above Sakai Saibei of the intermediary staff

First: In the twelfth month of the same year, with few colleagues available but many duties to perform, he worked diligently and was also entrusted with financial administration

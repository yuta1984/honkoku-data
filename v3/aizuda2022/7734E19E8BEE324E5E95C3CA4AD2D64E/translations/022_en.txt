1. Same year, 10th month, as requested was relieved of the goyō-yaku position and appointed to the regular duty gaisama-gumi, joining Tanaka Kahē's unit

1. Same month, upon being relieved of official duties as requested, was granted three pieces of silver and two tan of Kaga-dyed cloth from Kaga-no-kami-sama, and two pieces of silver and one hiki of Kaga silk from Bai'en'in-sama

1. Same year, 11th month
   Was granted mementos from Bai'en'in-sama, but the specific items are unclear

1. Same 3rd year of the Tiger, 6th month, since the adopted son Nuido became estranged, requested and was granted permission to return him to his original family name

1. Same month, requested and was granted permission to take Takagi Nuido-emon's younger brother Shinjūrō as an adopted son-in-law

1. Same year, 7th day of the 7th month, died of illness at age 73. Buddhist name was Sokuō Genshin Koji, buried at Monryō-in, a sub-temple of Zōjōji Temple in Shiba, Edo

Daughter: Wife of Akamatsu Noriemon Naoharu

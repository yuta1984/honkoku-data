Biological father: Third son of Karasawa Heiemon Mitsukage

Wife: Daughter of Tsugawa Bunzaemon Katsunao

First: Enpō 4th year of Tatsu (1676), 12th month - As per his parent's petition, he was appointed as guard substitute (ban-dai) and entered Harada Shume's unit, serving as castle guard.

First: Same 5th year of Mi (1677), 4th month - He went up to Edo for guard duty, but when his adoptive parent Shichiemon died of illness, he returned home in the 7th month of the same year.

First: Same year, 8th month - He was granted the hereditary stipend of 100 koku without any changes.

First: Same 9th year of Tori (1681), 8th month - He was appointed to additionally serve as magistrate (daikan).

First: Jōkyō 2nd year of Ushi (1685), 9th month - As per the petition of his superior Harada Shume, he was relieved of his duties and transferred to Yamazaki Sasuke's unit for his next position.

First: Same 3rd year of Tora (1686), 6th month - He was relieved of his magistrate duties and from the 7th month of the same year served as castle guard.

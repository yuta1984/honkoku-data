He entered the castle and performed guard duty.

One: In the 8th month of Bunsei 1 (1818), he was appointed as a unit commander (monotō).

One: On the 1st day of the 9th month of the same year, he received the custody of Saka Sagorō from the Sugita Ukon residence and transferred him to the Suzuki Sakuemon residence on the last day of the same month.

One: In the 10th month of Bunsei 3 (1820), he went up to Edo for regular duty service, and in the 5th month of the following year, he accompanied the lord's return journey.

One: In the 2nd month of Bunsei 4 (1821), he was assigned to guard the front main gate for the bridal procession.

One: In the same month, he was appointed as a messenger from Tokugawa Uemon-sama to Tayasu Chūnagon-sama.

One: In the same month, while on regular duty, he was temporarily appointed to concurrently serve as master of ceremonies (sōjaban).

One: In the 2nd month of Bunsei 5 (1822), due to the death of Kinbun-sama, he was ordered to patrol inside and outside the castle grounds day and night, made frequent rounds, and also performed guard duty at the temporary palace in the Third Bailey.

One: In the 5th month of Bunsei 6 (1823), during the lord's return journey, he was appointed to be added to the master of ceremonies.

One: In the 8th month of the same year, he participated in the Ōnohara joint military exercise and received game as a reward.

One: In the 10th month of the same year, regarding the current joint military training, since the bird hunting exercises had been suspended since the year of the Rabbit, light drills were ordered in that format.

1. Same year, 4th month
1. When Baien'in-sama entered into marriage with Matsudaira Sado-no-kami-sama, he was appointed as mounted escort.

1. Same year, 10th month: Since this year was a half-celebration year, he was specially assigned as messenger guard, and it was announced that he had served diligently and frequently as escort to Genshō'in-sama as well.

1. Kan'en 2 (1749), year of the snake, 12th month: During the peasant uprising, he was assigned under his superior Hinata Saburōuemon and was stationed at Nanokamachi.

1. Same 3rd year (1750), year of the horse, 1st month: He was praised for his diligent service during the peasant uprising of the previous winter.

1. Hōreki 1 (1751), year of the sheep, 8th month: When the domain inspectors came, he served fire watch rounds.

1. Same 2nd year (1752), year of the monkey, 4th month: He went up to Edo for guard duty and returned in the 10th month of the same year.

1. Same year, 7th month: His request to change his name to Izaemon was granted.

1. Same 3rd year (1753), year of the rooster: He was appointed as ashigaru commander (monogashira).

1. Same 4th year (1754), year of the dog《month unknown》: He went up to Edo for guard duty and returned in the 10th month of the same year.

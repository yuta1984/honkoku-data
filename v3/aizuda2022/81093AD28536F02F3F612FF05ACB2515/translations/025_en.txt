- In the eighth month of the same year, he was appointed as ÅŒmetsuke (chief inspector) in the lord's presence.

- In the same month, his request to change his name to Izaemon was granted.

- In the seventh month of the thirteenth year (Year of the Rat), his court appointment was announced, and he successfully completed the formal gratitude ceremony. As congratulations, he was given three bundles of cotton.

- In the tenth month of the same year, he was summoned to Edo, went up to the capital, completed his duties, and returned in the same tenth month.

- In the third month of the fourteenth year (Year of the Ox), when Lord Kinbun recovered from smallpox and partook of celebratory sake, he was given three bundles of cotton as congratulations.

- In the same month, when Lord Kinbun completed his recovery celebration, he was given soup and sake as congratulations.

- In the fifth month of the same year, when the engagement of Lady Teikanin was announced, he was given three bundles of cotton as congratulations.

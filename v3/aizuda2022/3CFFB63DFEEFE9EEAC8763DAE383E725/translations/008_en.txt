When the patrol officers went together to Echigo province, there was a masterless samurai calling himself Matsuo Issui who was dressed as a warrior and wearing swords. [The subject] properly commanded the situation and captured him, and furthermore, regarding the recovery of stolen goods that had been sold off in other domains, when negotiating with the other parties, there were difficulties since it involved other domains, but without backing down, he earnestly negotiated and recovered everything without payment. For handling both matters appropriately, he was awarded 2 ryō and 2 bu of gold as a reward.

One　Same year, 12th month, appointed as regular temporary official for money receiving and disbursing duties in the lending department, granted as a one-generation annual stipend

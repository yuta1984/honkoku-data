Concubine: family name unknown　　《marginal note: said to be born in Edo Kanasugi》　《marginal note: died of illness on the 6th day of the 6th month of Kyōhō 11, Year of the Horse, posthumous spiritual title Goshin-hime Shinrei, burial place same as above》

一　In the 11th month of Kanbun 12, Year of the Rat (1672), he was granted his first formal audience (gokeitaku no omemie), was summoned to the lord's side, had his hand taken by the lord, was personally given dried abalone (go-noshi) by the lord's own hand, and received the lord's words "grow up to be a strong man." Additionally, he received the lord's instruction that "while your father Ankyū has received transmission of the Two Matters, you shall receive transmission of the Three Matters and serve at the shrine."

一　In the 12th month of Kanbun 12, Year of the Rat (1672), he was granted three pieces of gold.

一　In the 2nd month of Enpō 1, Year of the Ox (1673), he returned from Edo and was appointed as a shrine official (shanin). He served in the funeral rites for Lord Hoshina Masayuki (Dotsū-sama), and afterward, when the lord's symbolic representation (go-katashiro) was enshrined in the distant worship hall (go-yōhaiden), he served in the Shinto rituals following his father Ankyū.

一　From the end of the 12th month of the same year, he was granted three pieces of gold each time as financial support (go-gōriki).
　　　However, this was later increased to twenty-two ryō and two bu in gold.

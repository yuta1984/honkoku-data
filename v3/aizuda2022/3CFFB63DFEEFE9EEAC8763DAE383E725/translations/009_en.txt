One　Same 8th year (1796), dragon year, 10th month, granted 2 ryō of gold annually as official stipend

One　Same year, 12th month, appointed as money receiving and disbursing official for the lending department, and granted formal audience upon appointment

One　Same 10th year (1798), horse year, 9th month, appointed to concurrently serve as money receiving and disbursing official for the salt department, and also performed office duties

One　Same 11th year (1799), sheep year, 5th month, from the beginning of handling lending affairs, devoted himself meticulously, came to serve in one office role, worked diligently, and due to exceptional merit, granted an official stipend of 2 koku

One　Kyōwa 1st year (1801), rooster year, 6th month, when transferred from the salt department, received praise for having properly consulted and served regarding financial arrangements up to that point

One　Same 2nd year (1802), dog year, 8th month, regarding lending management, worked diligently on various complex official matters, and for this received 1 ryō and 3 bu of gold as reward

One　Bunka 1st year (1804), rat year, 6th month, ordered to write off the principal and interest of loans, and for having worked diligently and properly up to this point, received 2 bu of gold as reward, with new role designation while maintaining position

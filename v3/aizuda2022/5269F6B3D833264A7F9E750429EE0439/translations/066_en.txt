One: In the 8th month of Hōreki 10, year of the dragon [1760], as requested by his father, he was granted his first formal audience

One: In the 11th month of An'ei 9, year of the rat [1780], as requested by his father, he was appointed as deputy to the outer retainer group, joined Ikoma Kuranosuke's group, and served castle guard duty

One: In the 4th month of Tenmei 3, year of the rabbit [1783], he inherited the family headship and was granted a fief of 300 koku without change

One: In the 3rd month of the same 8th year, year of the monkey [1788], he was appointed as an outer retainer attached to the chief councilor and joined the first group

One: In the same month, he went up to Edo for garrison duty, served as temporary messenger during the journey, and returned in the 10th month of the same year
　　Furthermore, he was praised for performing his duties without incident during the campaign

One: In the 6th month of Kansei 1, year of the rooster [1789], he was reassigned to the third group

One: In the 7th month of the same year, he was appointed as a unit commander (mono-gashira)

One: In the 8th month of the same year, his request to change his name to Hachirōbei was granted

One: In the 3rd month of the same 4th year, year of the rat [1792], he participated in a hunting expedition

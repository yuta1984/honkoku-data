One: Same 14th year, year of the Monkey (1764), 3rd month - He went up to Edo for guard duty and returned in the 10th month of the same year.

One: Meiwa 2, year of the Rooster (1765), 7th month - In the lord's presence, he was appointed to the position of wakadoshiyori (junior councilor). He was to affix his seal to official documents in the lord's quarters. His matoi-uma insignia and permission to use a palanquin within Aizu domain were continued as before, and he was granted a dōshin stipend, as announced.

One: Same year, 9th month - He was appointed as commander-in-chief of the garrison at Yamagata Castle in Dewa Province, went there, and returned in the 3rd month of the following year of the Dog.

One: Same 3rd year, year of the Dog (1766), 4th month - Upon completing his guard duty at Yamagata and returning, he was granted one kosode robe.

One: Same 4th year, year of the Boar (1767), 3rd month - He went up to Edo for guard duty and returned in the 10th month of the same year.

One: Same year, 11th month - In the lord's presence, he was appointed to the position of karō (chief retainer). He was granted permission to carry a identifying banner in the lord's quarters, and it was announced that he would receive stipends equal to his colleagues and dōshin allowances.

One: Same 5th year, year of the Rat (1768), 5th month - He was granted permission to use a palanquin in Edo

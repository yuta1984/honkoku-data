One: In the 4th month of Meiwa 1, year of the monkey [1764], he went up to Edo as an attendant for the lord's sankin-kōtai attendance duty and returned in the 10th month of the same year

One: In the same month, it was announced that he had served faithfully as temporary escort guard for the journey without any absences

One: In the 7th month of the same 2nd year, year of the rooster [1765], when his superior Takahashi Koemon was reassigned, he was transferred to Kayano Gonbei's unit as his successor

One: In the 2nd month of the same 3rd year, year of the dog [1766], he requested and was granted permission to change his name to Hachirōbei

One: In the 3rd month of the same year, he went to Yamagata for garrison duty and returned in the 9th month of the same year

One: In the 10th month of the same 4th year, year of the boar [1767], he went up to Edo for garrison duty and returned in the 4th month of the following year of the rat

One: In the 4th month of An'ei 1, year of the dragon [1772], he went up to Edo for garrison duty and returned in the 10th month of the same year

One: In the 12th month of the same 4th year, year of the sheep [1775], he was appointed as a company commander (monotō)

One: In the 6th month of the same 5th year, year of the monkey [1776], he was assigned custody of Hara Tetsutarō and Yamazaki

Assigned to Hyuga Genemon's group and served castle guard duty

In the 12th month of the same 12th year, year of the horse, went up to Edo for guard duty service, and in the 4th month of the following year of the sheep, came down accompanying his lord

In the 1st month of Meiwa 1 [1764], year of the rooster, was appointed to the messenger guard position

In the 4th month of the same year, went up for guard duty service as an attendant, and came down in the 10th month of the same year

In the 4th month of Meiwa 3 [1766], year of the dog, went up to Edo for guard duty service, and came down in the 10th month of the same year

In the 11th month of Meiwa 4 [1767], year of the boar, was appointed as messenger for the lord's attendance inquiry at the shogun's court, went up to Edo, performed the duty and came down
　Note: After this, was appointed to perform this duty once more

In the 2nd month of Meiwa 6 [1769], year of the ox, due to Nagano Juroemon's illness, went up as emergency guard duty replacement, and came down in the 9th month of the same year

In the same year, was granted Onuma Matahachiro's upper residence

In the 5th month of Meiwa 8 [1771], year of the rabbit, went up to Edo for guard duty service, and came down in the 9th month of the same year

In the 7th month of An'ei 3 [1774], year of the horse, was appointed to the position of presenter to the shogun with command over foot soldiers

In the 9th month of the same year, went up to Edo for guard duty service, and came down in the 10th month
　Note: As standard compensation, was granted one roll of bleached cotton cloth in place of the hemp formal dress

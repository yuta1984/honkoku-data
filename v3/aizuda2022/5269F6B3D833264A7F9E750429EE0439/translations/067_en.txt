One: In the 12th month of the same year, it was announced that his group's foot soldiers performed excellently in military formations, long-range shooting, and target practice, and he was granted two bundles of cotton

One: In the 9th month of the same 7th year, year of the rabbit [1795], he participated in a hunting expedition
　　Furthermore, of the four times he participated thereafter, he received gifts for game caught on two occasions

One: In the 12th month of the same year, during the hunting expedition, he was praised for maintaining good coordination with his group's foot soldiers without incident, and for his continued diligence in giving instructions

　　Furthermore, he continued to receive praise on every occasion thereafter

One: In the 3rd month of the same 8th year, year of the dragon [1796], for his good management of his group members, proper leadership, encouragement of military preparedness, and diligence, he was rewarded with three pieces of silver

One: In the same month, he went up to Edo for garrison duty and returned in the 10th month of the same year
　　Furthermore, he was appointed as assistant to the court messenger during the campaign and was praised for performing his duties without incident

One: In the 5th month of Taika 1, year of the rat [1801], as requested, his son Kyūnai was appointed as deputy

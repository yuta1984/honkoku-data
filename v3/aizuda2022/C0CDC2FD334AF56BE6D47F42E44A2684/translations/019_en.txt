- In the 10th month of the same 3rd year (Year of the Horse), he traveled up to Edo for duty rotation (勤番), and returned home in the 4th month of the following Year of the Ram.

- In the 4th month of the same 4th year (Year of the Ram), as his unit commander Kasuga Jinzaemon was reassigned, he was transferred to the unit of Hyūga Saburōemon as the successor unit assignment.

- In the 1st month of the same 5th year (Year of the Monkey), he was appointed as temporary escort guard (仮御供番), and served in that capacity until the 2nd month of the same year.

- In the 4th month of the same 8th year (Year of the Tiger), as his unit commander Hyūga Saburōemon died of illness, he was transferred to the unit of Tanaka Kahei as the successor unit assignment.

- In the 9th month of the same 11th year (Year of the Tiger), he traveled up to Edo for duty rotation, and returned home in the 3rd month of the following Year of the Rabbit.

**[Right folio]**

*(Continued from previous page)* ...he was praised on every occasion.

- In the third month of Kansei 4 (Year of the Rat), he traveled up to Edo to accompany the lord on the sankin (alternate attendance journey), serving as an escort for the return trip.

- In the ninth month of Kansei 5 (Year of the Ox), during the falcon hunt (o-toritogari), he went out to the o-tachiba marsh (the lord's designated observation/hunting post).

- In the fourth month of Kansei 7 (Year of the Rabbit), he traveled up to Edo to accompany the lord on the return journey from the domain.

- In the eighth month of the same year, he went out to participate in the falcon hunt and was granted a portion of the catch as a reward.
　　Note: He participated on two further occasions after this.

- In the twelfth month of the same year, at the commencement of that year's falcon hunt,

**[Left folio]**

the formation order, marching in step, and advance and retreat movements were all carried out without hindrance, and it was recognized by the lord that he had also devoted himself diligently to interior training (internal practice drills). Accordingly, the entire unit received commendation from the lord.
　　Note: He continued to receive commendations on every subsequent occasion.

- In the tenth month of Kansei 9 (Year of the Snake), on the occasion of an unscheduled sankin (emergency alternate attendance), he traveled up to Edo as an escort and for garrison duty, returning in the fourth month of the following year (Year of the Horse).

- In the ninth month of Kansei 10 (Year of the Horse), his wish to change his name to "Genzaemon" was granted.

- In the same month, as he had shown particularly notable improvement in archery (kyūjutsu), he was rewarded with one bundle of cotton (wata hitotaba) as a prize.

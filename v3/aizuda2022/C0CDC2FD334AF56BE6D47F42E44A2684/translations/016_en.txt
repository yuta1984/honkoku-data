　　…placed in service, he was called out as an *okoshō* (page/personal attendant) on permanent assignment in Edo.

- In the twelfth month of the second year of the same era (Keian 2, Year of the Ox, 1649), he was granted an additional stipend of two *ryō* in gold.

- In the twelfth month of Meireki 1 (Year of the Ram, 1655), he was granted an additional stipend of two *ryō* in gold.

- In the tenth month of Manji 2 (Year of the Boar, 1659), upon his father Han'emon's retirement, he was granted two hundred *koku* as a divided inheritance (*bunchi*).

- In the sixth month of Tenna 1 (Year of the Rooster, 1681), he was appointed *otsukaiban* (messenger/envoy official), and was granted one hundred *koku* as an official stipend (*yakuryō*).

- In the eleventh month of Jōkyō 2 (Year of the Ox, 1685), regarding a matter of his duties as a messenger, it was

**[Left folio]**
　　deemed that he had conducted himself in an improper and unsatisfactory manner, and he was ordered to remain confined to his residence (*chikkyo*), after which it was declared that he was to be sent down to Aizu.

- In the second month of the third year of the same era (Jōkyō 3, Year of the Tiger, 1686), his appointment as *otsukaiban* was revoked, he was assigned to the *tozama-gumi* (outer retainer unit), and specifically assigned to the unit of Hori Han'emon.

- In the third month of the same year, he was reassigned and transferred to the unit of Sugita Gorōbei.

- In the second month of Genroku 2 (Year of the Snake, 1689), he was ordered to be banished (*tsuihō*) on account of disorderly conduct while intoxicated (*shuran*); however, due to illness, he and his relatives were placed under the custody of his family (*ichirui azukari*) until he recovered. In the fourth month of the same year, having recovered, he withdrew beyond the barrier (*kanai*).

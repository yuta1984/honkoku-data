Han'emon Nagamoto (originally named Gen'go)
- Mother: A concubine; clan name unknown
- Concubine: Daughter of a certain Satō

**[Left folio]**
attached to the Tozama-gumi (外様組), and took up his castle guard duties under Miyake Magobei.

- In the 2nd month of the same 19th year (Year of the Tiger), he was reassigned to the unit of Jimbo Kurannosuke.

- In the 4th month of the same year, he traveled to Edo for guard service (勤番), and returned in the 10th month of the same year.
  - Note: During the aforementioned period of service, he was additionally appointed as a courier officer (御使番定加).

**[Right folio]**

- In the fourth month of Kansei 12 (Year of the Monkey), on the occasion of Lady Teishō's attendance at the shogunal capital (gosanpu), he traveled up to Edo to serve as her escort for the return journey.

- In the sixth month of the same year, having applied himself diligently to spear techniques (sōjutsu), he was rewarded with one bundle of cotton (wata hitotaba) as a prize.

- In the tenth month of the same year, he traveled up to Edo for garrison duty (kinban), and returned in the fifth month of the following year (Year of the Rooster) as part of the lord's escort. During the journey, he was provisionally appointed to the role of Gate Guard (otobanyaku).

- In the third month of Bunka 1 (Year of the Rat), in accordance with his petition, his son Kamon was appointed in his place as a guard (gobandai).

**[Left folio]**

- In the seventh month of Bunsei 2 (Year of the Rabbit), in accordance with his petition, he was granted permission to retire (inkyo).

- On the twenty-second day of the fifth month of Bunsei 6 (Year of the Goat), he died of illness at the age of sixty-five. Posthumous Buddhist name (hōmyō): Ryōton. Place of burial: Kitatakezawa Koyama.

**[Right folio]**
　　Adopted daughter of Hyūga Heizaemon Chikashige;
　　biological daughter of Harada Gorōemon Taneaki.

— In the seventh month of Hōreki 11 (Year of the Snake, 1761), in accordance with his father's petition, he was granted his first formal audience (o-memie) with the lord.

— In the first month of Hōreki 12 (Year of the Horse, 1762), in accordance with his father's petition, he was appointed to castle guard duty (banshiro) and assigned to the unit of Hyūga Genemon, where he performed service as a castle guard (o-shiro o-ban).

— In the ninth month of the same year, he traveled up to Edo for rotational duty (kinban), and upon returning in the fifth month of the following year (Year of the Sheep, 1763) in the lord's escort party, he was appointed to the provisional role of unit commander (mono-gashira kari-yaku) from Ashino onward along the journey.

**[Left folio]**

— In the ninth month of Meiwa 1 (Year of the Monkey, 1764), he traveled to Yamagata Castle to participate in its formal transfer/reception, and during his tour of garrison duty there, he served in the role of mono-gashira tomo-yaku (attendant to a unit commander). He returned in the third month of the following year (Year of the Rooster, 1765).

— In the second month of Meiwa 6 (Year of the Ox, 1769), he inherited the family headship (katoku) and was granted the family stipend of one thousand koku without change, and was assigned to the unattached unit (muyaku-gumi).

— In the seventh month of the same year, he was appointed mono-gashira (unit commander).

— In the third month of Meiwa 7 (Year of the Tiger, 1770), he traveled up to Edo as part of the lord's escort for rotational duty, and returned in the tenth month of the same year.

— In the third month of Meiwa 8 (Year of the Rabbit, 1771), he was appointed o-sōja-ban (Master of Ceremonies / Announcer of Audiences).

— In the fifth month of the same year, he was appointed o-kachi-shihai (Superintendent of Foot Soldiers).

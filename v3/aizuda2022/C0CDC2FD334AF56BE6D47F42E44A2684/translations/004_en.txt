Eldest son of Ogawa Kanbei Nagamasa:
　Hori Han'uemon Nagasue
　　Mother: Unknown
　　Wife: Daughter of Hori Tango-no-Kami Naoyori 《Marginal note: Died of illness on the 6th day of the 8th month of Meireki 3 (Year of the Rooster). Posthumous Buddhist name: Shinmyōgetsu Zenni. Burial place: Muryōzan Chōmyōji Temple》

　　Han'uemon Nagasue was the eldest son of Ogawa Kanbei Nagamasa. He served under Hori Tango-no-Kami Naoyori, the lord of Murakami Castle in Echigo Province, and subsequently under Naosada. In the 19th year of Kan'ei (Year of the Horse), upon the death of Naosada and the crisis facing the Hori clan, [he had expected] that the hereditary estate of Lord Tango-no-Kami would be maintained as before, [receiving] ten (koku)…

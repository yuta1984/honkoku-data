　　成御免　御城御番相勤候事

Tenmei 3rd year (Year of the Rabbit), 6th month: As requested, he was relieved of concurrent duties and assigned to serve as castle guard.

一　同年九月願之通隠居被
　　仰付候事

Same year, 9th month: As requested, he was granted permission to retire.

一　同五《割書：巳》年五月十八日七拾六才ニ而病
　　死法名涼清院愍誉誠斉居士
　　導師鷲鷹山長楽寺葬所大窪山

Same 5th year (Year of the Snake), 5th month, 18th day: He died of illness at age 76. His posthumous Buddhist name was Ryōshin-in Minyō Seisai Koji. The officiating priest was from Chōraku-ji Temple on Mount Jūō. His burial place was Mount Ōkubo.

女　　　　橋本近右衛門辛照妻

Daughter: Wife of Hashimoto Kinemon Shinshō

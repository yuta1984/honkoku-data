One entry: In the 12th month of the 4th year of An'ei (Year of the Sheep), his petition to change his name to "Kiuemon" was granted.

One entry: In the 2nd month of the 5th year of the same era (Year of the Monkey), as his unit commander (*kashira*) Tanaka Kahei was reassigned to a different post (*oyakugae*), he consequently came under the command of Kasuga Jinzaemon's unit.

One entry: In the 8th month of the 9th year of the same era (Year of the Rat), out of his father's provisionally assessed stipend (*ogisaku*), [a stipend of] twenty *koku* and four persons' rations (*ninin-fuchi*) — reduced by five *koku* — was granted to him, and he was summoned into service (*meshidashi*).

One entry: In the 10th month of the 2nd year of Tenmei (Year of the Tiger), it was officially proclaimed that his conduct of performing continuous, uninterrupted castle guard duty (*oshiro goban*) for two full years with his entire unit was deemed especially admirable and praiseworthy.

One entry: On the 19th day of the 5th month of the 8th year of the same era (Year of the Monkey), he died of illness at the age of fifty-two. Buddhist posthumous name (*hōmyō*):

**[Left folio]**

　　Jakugen Jōsetsu Koji. Place of burial: same as above.

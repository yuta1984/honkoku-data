After completing his duties there, he proceeded to Kyoto, and returned to Edo in the fifth month of the same year.

　*Note*: While in Kyoto, he was granted one set of formal hemp ceremonial dress (*omeshi asa kamishimo*).

One: In the second month of Hōreki 13 (Year of the Sheep), because of his diligent service, his existing official stipend (*oyakuryō*) of five *koku* was increased and converted accordingly, and granted to him.

One: In the third month of Meiwa 1 (Year of the Monkey), on the occasion of the invitation of the Senior Councilors (*rōjū*), he was appointed to a special duty post (*nin'yaku*) related to the matter.

[Left folio]
One: In the seventh month of the same year, because of his diligent service in connection with the Senior Councilors' reception duties, he was granted two pieces of silver (*ginsu*) as a reward.

One: In the eleventh month of Meiwa 4 (Year of the Boar), because of his many years of diligent service in his current post, his existing provisional stipend (*gisaku go-chigyō*) was converted and upgraded to one hundred *koku*, as decreed.

One: In the seventh month of Meiwa 5 (Year of the Rat), having been found unsuited to his current duties, he was relieved of his post and it was decreed that he would be sent down to Aizu without assignment (*muyaku*).

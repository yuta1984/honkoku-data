Actually the second son of Yamamuro Izaemon Hidetake
Wife: daughter of his adoptive father Yōsuke Naofusa (died of illness on the 24th day of the intercalary 4th month, Kansei 12th year, Year of the Monkey, at age 48. Her posthumous Buddhist name was Shinkō-in Kan'yo Myōryō Daishi. Burial place: Mount Ōkubo)

Anei 6th year (Year of the Rooster), 6th month: As requested by his parent, he was granted his first formal audience with his lord.

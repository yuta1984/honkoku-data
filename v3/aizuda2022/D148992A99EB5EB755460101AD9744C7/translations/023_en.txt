Genealogy of the Niwa Yamagara family (Surname: Sakanoue, Main crest: Two dragons within a circle, Home province: Owari, Alternative crest: Character "良" within a circle)

Niwa Yamagara Yoshimasu (Initially named Heihachirō, later Sukenoshin, born in Jōkyō 4, year of the Rabbit)
　　Fifth son of Niwa Kanuemon Munenō
　　Mother: Name unknown
　　Wife: Daughter of Nakayama Jizaemon Kiyoharu

1. In Genroku 13, year of the Dragon, first month: Received recommendation for reappointment and was summoned as page (okoshō)
2. In Genroku 16, year of the Sheep, first month: Was appointed as trainee meal attendant (gozenban minarai)
3. In the same year, sixth month: Was appointed as temporary meal attendant (gozenban kariyaku)
4. In Hōei 2, year of the Rooster, third month: Was appointed as meal attendant (gozenban)

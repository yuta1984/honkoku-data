　　　Genealogy of Nishikawa Gunzō (Clan name: Tachibana; Standard family crest: Sword and flower diamond within a circle / Home province: Shimotsuke; Alternate crest: Ivy within a circle)

Nishikawa Kōuemon Chikanobu (sixth son of Nishikawa Kōzaemon [personal name unknown]): Born in the first year of Hōei, Year of the Monkey (1704).

　　Mother: Daughter of Katagiri Mokuuemon Sakyō. Born in the first year of Enpō, Year of the Ox (1673). Died of illness on the thirteenth day of the third month of the eleventh year of Hōreki, Year of the Snake (1761), at the age of sixty-nine. Posthumous Buddhist name: Seien Teirin Daishi. Place of burial: Kongōzan Eiron-ji Temple.

One: In the ninth year of Kyōhō, Year of the Dragon (1724), he was appointed as an apprentice inspector of gold in the Two御納戸 (treasury offices).

One: In the tenth year of the same era, Year of the Snake (1725), he was granted one ryō of gold.

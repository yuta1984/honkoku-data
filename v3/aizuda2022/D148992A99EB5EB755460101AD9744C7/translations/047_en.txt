**[Right folio]**

One entry: In the 12th month of the 5th year of the same era (Year of the Dragon), as he had been dispatched to the various locations assigned for the *gomen-yama shimari-kata* duties on behalf of the domain, he contributed to particularly strict enforcement of the restrictions, and also diligently performed duties during his off-hours; in recognition of this, he was bestowed with sake (*o-sake*) [as a reward].

One entry: In the 3rd month of the 11th year of the same era (Year of the Dog), as his son Tatsunosuke had previously died of illness, his petition to take Wakamori Genzaemon's second adopted son, Sazen, as his son-in-law and adopted heir (*muko-yōshi*) was granted.

One entry: On the 24th day of the 6th month of the 12th year of the same era (Year of the Boar), he died of illness at the age of fifty-two.
　　Posthumous Buddhist name (*hōmyō*): Shinkō Inkan Jōryōki Koji. Place of burial: same as above.

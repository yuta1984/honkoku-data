**[Left folio]**

One entry: In the 9th month of the 10th year of Kyōhō (Year of the Snake), as his parent had requested, he was granted his first formal audience (*o-memie*) \[with the domain lord\].

One entry: In the 1st month of the 13th year of the same era (Year of the Monkey), as his parent had requested, he was appointed as guard duty substitute (*goban-dai*) and performed castle guard duty (*oshiro goban*) in the unit of Obara Gorōemon.

One entry: In the 4th month of the 18th year of the same era (Year of the Ox), as his unit commander (*kashira*) Obara Gorōemon was reassigned to fill the vacancy left by Kayano Gonbei's dismissal from his post, he was transferred to the unit of Tanaka Kahei.

**[Right folio]**

One entry: \[In the 1st year of Jōkyō,\] he was granted a stipend of twenty-five *koku* of *gigi-saku* (*nami* rank) and a ration for four persons, and was appointed to the *oともban* (escort guard duty).

One entry: In the 2nd year of the same era (Year of the Ox), in the 4th month, he traveled up to Edo for *kinban* (rotational duty in Edo), and returned in the 10th month of the same year.

One entry: In the 3rd year of the same era (Year of the Tiger), in the 3rd month, he was relieved of his post as requested, was assigned to the *tozama-gumi* (outer retainer unit), entered the unit of Shinoda Hanzaemon, and performed castle guard duty (*oshiro goban*).

One entry: In the 7th month of the 1st year of Hōei (Year of the Monkey), as his unit commander (*kashira*) Shinoda Hanzaemon was dismissed from his post, he was transferred to the unit of Ōhara Sakon'emon as the successor unit assignment.

**[Left folio]**

One entry: In the 8th month of the 2nd year of the same era (Year of the Rooster), as his unit commander Ōhara Sakon'emon was relieved of his post as requested, he was transferred to the unit of Saka Seizaemon as the successor unit assignment.

One entry: In the 12th month of the 2nd year of Kyōhō (Year of the Rooster), as Saka Seizaemon died of illness, he was transferred to the unit of Obara Gorōemon as the successor unit assignment.

One entry: In the 1st month of the 13th year of the same era (Year of the Monkey), as he had requested, his son Kakuma was appointed as his guard duty substitute (*goban-dai*).

One entry: In the 2nd month of the 20th year of the same era (Year of the Rabbit), as his son and guard substitute Kakuma had died of illness, he was granted permission as requested to adopt his nephew — the younger brother of Miyake Kohei — Magozaemon as his adopted son.

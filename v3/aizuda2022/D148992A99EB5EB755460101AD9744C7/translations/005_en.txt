Served as kokashira (minor leader)
　However, although it is said and passed down that he served from the time in Shinshū province, both his official titles and stipends are unclear.

1. Keinan 2 (1650), year of the Ox (month unclear): Appointed as clerk at the district office, granted 11 koku and provisions for 2 retainers.

1. Kanbun 8 (1668), year of the Monkey (month unclear): His stipend was withdrawn as requested.

1. Enpō 7 (1678), year of the Sheep, 4th month, 2nd day: Died of illness. Buddhist name: Shin'yo Ichimu Shinshi. Burial place: Chōraku Temple on Mount Washitaka.

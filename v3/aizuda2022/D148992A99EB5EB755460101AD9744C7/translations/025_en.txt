　　accordingly, his previous gold stipend certificate was changed to a rice stipend certificate of 22 koku
1. In Enkyō 3, year of the Tiger, ninth month: Having served for ten years since becoming a page and performing current duties diligently, his previous provisional fief was changed to a direct grant of 100 koku
2. In Kan'en 1, year of the Dragon, third month: As requested, was relieved of current duties, and his son Sukenoshin was appointed as guard substitute
3. In Kan'en 3, year of the Horse, eighth month, eighteenth day: Died of illness at age 64
　　Buddhist name: Tsūgen Tsūtetsu Koji, Burial place: Hōun

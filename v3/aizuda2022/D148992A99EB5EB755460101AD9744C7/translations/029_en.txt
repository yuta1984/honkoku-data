Sanaka Yoshifusa《First name: Naka｜Born on the 9th day of the 12th month of Meiwa 8, year of the Hare》
　　Third son of his actual father Jōemon Yoshinori
　　Wife: Adopted daughter of Hara Hikodayū Toshiuji
　　Actual father: Daughter of Enjōji Hikohachi [surname unknown]
1. In Kansei 5, year of the Ox, eleventh month: Inherited 100 koku of fief without change as succession, and served castle guard duty in Jinbo Kuranosuke's unit
1. In Kansei 8, year of the Dragon, ninth month: Appointed as instructor of Ankō-ryū swordsmanship, and exempted from residential guard duty attendance at the castle

【Left page】
1. In the same year, eleventh month: The petition to make Sukenoshin, the actual son of his adoptive father, the heir was granted
1. In Kansei 9, year of the Snake, eighth month: Participated in the bird hunting event
　　　Note: Participated once more after this《crossed out: matter｜》
1. In the same year, ninth month: During the current bird hunting event, his advancing and retreating movements were well coordinated, and this was thought to be due to his continued diligent efforts, so his entire unit was praised together
　　　Note: Received praise after this as well
1. In Kansei 10, year of the Horse, seventh month: For his diligent practice in spear techniques, received one bundle of cotton as a reward

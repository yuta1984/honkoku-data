**[Right folio]**

- In the eleventh month of the same year, it was officially announced that he had served diligently as a temporary assistant secretary (*goyūhitsu kariryaku*) during the busy period of the previous autumn.

- In the first month of the fourth year (Year of the Rabbit), he was appointed as a hired document clerk (*goyōsho kakimono oyatoi*) at the administrative office.
  - Note: He served until the fifth month of the following Year of the Dragon.

- In the intercalary sixth month of the fifth year (Year of the Dragon), he was appointed to the temporary position of reading instructor (*kudokushi*) at the domain academy (*gakkan*).

- In the third month of the sixth year (Year of the Snake), he was ordered to additionally take on the role of supervisor of the calligraphy study (*shogaku shimari-kata*) at the academy.

**[Left folio]**

- In the fourth month of the same year, he was appointed to permanent Edo duty (*Edo jōzume*) and was additionally appointed as reading instructor and ■-method instructor (*■kata shihan*) at the academy.

- In the fifth month of the same year, he was additionally appointed as mathematics instructor (*sūgaku shinan*).

- In the eleventh month of the seventh year (Year of the Horse), he was appointed as supervisor (*shimari-kata*) of the domain academy.

- In the third month of the eighth year (Year of the Goat), he was appointed to the temporary position of academic supervisor (*shigyō*).

- In the ninth month of the ninth year (Year of the Monkey), he was appointed as permanent-duty assistant secretary (*jōzume goyūhitsu*). [Entry continues on next page.]

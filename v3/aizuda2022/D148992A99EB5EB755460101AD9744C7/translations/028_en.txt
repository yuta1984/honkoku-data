1. In Tenmei 7, year of the Sheep, second month: When unit leader Kasuya Gozaemon retired, transferred to Numasawa Kurōbee's unit for the successor position
1. In Kansei 1, year of the Rooster, tenth month: When unit leader Numasawa Kurōbee's position was revoked, transferred to Jinbo Kuranosuke's unit for the successor position
1. In Kansei 5, year of the Ox, eighth month: As his illness became severe, he petitioned to adopt his younger brother Sanaka, but died of illness

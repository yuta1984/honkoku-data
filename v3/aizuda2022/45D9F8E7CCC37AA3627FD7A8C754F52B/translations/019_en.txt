One　Same 12th year, year of the boar, 9th month, appointed to permanent duty in Edo

One　Same year, 10th month, regarding the crested formal wear (kosode, katabira) and court dress that his grandfather Sachū had received and kept in storage, he requested permission to wear them as an act of gratitude, and this was granted

One　Same year, 11th month, for serving guests, he was privately given one bolt of silk and one bundle of cotton
　　　Note: he was given such rewards frequently thereafter

One　Same 13th year, year of the rat, 4th month, when the lord traveled to Kyoto, he accompanied both the lord and his retinue

【Written in red ink　One　Same 14th year, year of the ox, 5th month, for serving guests he was given one bolt of bleached cloth】

One　Same 14th year, year of the ox, 6th month, while continuing his regular duties, he was appointed as temporary secretary

　　　Note: thereafter he frequently served as hired secretary, temporary official, etc.

One　Bunsei 4th year, year of the snake, 2nd month
　　During Lady Teikanin's wedding procession, he was appointed to serve and to arrange the celebratory rice cakes, and was given clothing allowance

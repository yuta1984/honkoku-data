In the 5th year of Bunka, year of the dragon, 7th month, having devoted himself to learning and showing good progress for his age, he was awarded the "Historical Outline Supplement" as a reward

One　Same 8th year, year of the sheep, 11th month, having devoted himself to learning and showing good progress for his age by advancing to first rank, he was awarded "Three Rites of Ancient Times" as a reward

One　Same 9th year, year of the monkey, 9th month, he was ordered to undertake training in court music (gagaku)
　　【Note in red ink: However, as financial assistance, one ryō of gold was provided annually as an interest-free loan】

One　Same 12th year, year of the boar, 10th month, having devoted himself to learning and showing excellent progress for his age by advancing to the university level, he was awarded the "New Commentary on the Five Classics" as a reward

One　Same 13th year, year of the rat, 4th month, from his late father's stipend allocation, one person's stipend was reduced, and he was granted eight koku and two persons' stipend; he was summoned to service with a position in the second rank of close attendants, placed under the supervision of the school magistrate without specific duties, exempted from construction duties fees, and ordered to concentrate exclusively on training in court music

One　Same 14th year, year of the ox, 11th month, having advanced to first rank in court music, he was granted one and a half persons' stipend

One　Same 9th year, year of the snake, 8th month, he traveled to Nikko for gagaku (court music) training
　　However, after this he was ordered to travel to Nikko twice more for training

One　Kyōwa 2nd year, year of the dog, 3rd month, he was ordered to go up to Kyoto for gagaku training, received instruction in Daikyoku-hantoku, and returned
　　However, at this time he was privately given ten ryō as financial assistance and silver as a token of gratitude

One　Same year, 8th month, because he applied himself wholeheartedly to gagaku, traveled to other domains for extensive training with good results, and also worked diligently at instruction and trained various students well, while serving as said instructor he was granted a stipend for three people from the academic rice allowance

One　Bunka 1st year, year of the rat, 10th month, one person's stipend was reduced from his late father's provisional appointment, he was granted eight koku and a stipend for three people, and was summoned to the shinban unit
　　However, the stipend for three people that had been granted until then was revoked

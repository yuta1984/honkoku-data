One　Kansei 1st year, year of the rooster, 3rd month, due to being appointed to the regulatory division position, was ordered to report directly to the magistrates

One　Same 2nd year, year of the dog, 10th month, for working diligently and conducting detailed investigations into the prices of various procurement items, thereby managing matters for the domain's benefit, was granted three bu of gold as a reward

One　Same month, was appointed to the temporary position of chief accountant and head clerk concurrently, with a seat above the shrine fund officials and next to Endo Sosuke of the temporary cost-saving investigation position at the accounting office, placed under direct supervision of the magistrates, and ordered to also handle personnel allocation duties in addition to investigation work

One　Same 3rd year, year of the boar, 8th month, was formally appointed to chief accountant and head clerk concurrent position, granted the same official stipend of 3 koku 5 to as before, and ordered to also take charge of personnel allocation at the Shiba estate

One　Same 4th year, year of the rat, 11th month, was appointed to Aizu financial affairs and ordered to serve in duties related to the head clerk position

He was ordered to serve as fire watch guard at the shrine when the lord was in residence in the domain

One　Same year, 7th month, he was ordered to serve as armored retainer for castle defense during the lord's absence

One　Same 2nd year, year of the rabbit, 8th month, he was ordered to provide instruction in court music to the musicians at Mount Mine, and proceeded there

One　Same year, 10th month, he was ordered to serve temporarily as instructor for the hichiriki (double-reed wind instrument) succeeding Yanase Tetsu-shirō

One　Same year, 12th month, regarding Omiya Toyooka's duty of fire watch guard at the shrine - when the lord is in residence in the domain, retainers attached to the vice-general are appointed, so it was announced that he need not serve in this capacity

One　Same 3rd year, year of the dragon, 7th month, his service as armored retainer for castle defense during the lord's absence was discontinued and he was relieved of this duty

One　Same year, 8th month, due to changes in arrangements at this time, his training in court music was discontinued and he was appointed to the New Guard Unit

One　Same 4th year, year of the snake, 3rd month, he was temporarily appointed to serve in his lordship's close attendant guard

One　Tenmei 1, year of the ox, 8th month, his request to
　　change his name to Sanaka was granted

One　Same year, 9th month, upon petition, he went up to Edo

One　Same 2nd year, year of the tiger, 7th month, appointed to koban goyō (minor guard employment)
　　　However, after this, he was repeatedly appointed to this same employment

One　Same 4th year, year of the dragon, 5th month, having devoted himself to learning for several years,
　　his special dedication was particularly recognized by his lord

One　Same 7th year, year of the sheep, 12th month, having served diligently as koban goyō for several years
　　and performing his duties well, he was promoted to jōzume koban (regular minor guard),
　　granted a stipend of two persons' rice allowance as official salary,
　　making a total of five persons' rice allowance

One　Same 8th year, year of the monkey, 8th month,
　　appointed as temporary aide to Lord Ryōtoku-in

One　Same year, 9th month, appointed concurrent supervisor
　　of archery arts under Satō Tanzō

One　Same year, 12th month, appointed concurrent supervisor of the academy
　　at the upper residence, responsible for managing students' attendance and seating,
　　supervising academic excellence and diligence, and instructed to
　　substitute when reading instructors were absent,
　　granted one ryō of gold annually

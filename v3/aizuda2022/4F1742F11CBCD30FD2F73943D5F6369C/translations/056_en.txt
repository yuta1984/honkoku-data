Since Inspector of Foot Soldiers (御徒目付) Kobayashi Genzo fell ill and withdrew earlier, he was ordered to serve as temporary Inspector of Foot Soldiers in his place.
However, since Kobayashi Genzo petitioned and withdrew together with the Military Magistrate (御軍事奉行), he was told that it was no longer necessary to serve in this temporary role.

First, same month: At the same location, regarding the military equipment section's replacement gear and other matters for the Military Magistrate's forces, he was ordered to take charge and handle affairs in the same manner as Inspector of Foot Soldiers Kobayashi Genzo, and to serve in supervision with the assistance of Yamadera Mitsugi.

First, same month: At the same location, he was ordered to perform rotating duties in the capacity of Inspector of Foot Soldiers, and although his regular duty shifts would likely not be lengthy, he was nevertheless ordered to serve the daytime guard duties.

First, same month: At the same location, regarding the Military Magistrate's forces which were scheduled to arrive by ship at Tsugaru domain, he was appointed to lodging allocation duties, and since he was also appointed as messenger to the various domain lords, he was ordered to prepare in advance, beginning with cargo loading and other preparations, so that rapid departure could be achieved after landing.

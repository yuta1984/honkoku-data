Entry: Same year, 5th month - For diligent handling of various arrangements, awarded 2 bu in gold as expense allowance

Entry: Kyōwa 1st year, year of the rooster (1801), 3rd month - Traveled to Yonezawa on official business for underground annual installment loans and horse purchases

Entry: Same 2nd year, year of the dog (1802), 8th month - Beginning with barren land development, devoted meticulous care to matters of permanent benefit, properly handled official duties both large and small, and achieved distinguished results, therefore as a special recognition awarded 1 koku 5 to 6 shō in official stipend

Entry: Same 3rd year, year of the boar (1803), 12th month - Appointed to temporary positions as accountant, irrigation and new field supervisor, and granary allocation supervisor, and served at Tonoguchi district office

Entry: Bunka 1st year, year of the rat (1804), 5th month - Appointed as accountant, with official stipend of 1 koku 5 to 6 shō continued as before

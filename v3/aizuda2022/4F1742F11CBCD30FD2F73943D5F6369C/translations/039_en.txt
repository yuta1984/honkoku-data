First, same 3rd year, year of the Boar (Kyōwa 3, 1803), 4th month
　Accompanied Lord Sadaaki on his journey down to the domain, and in the following year of the Rat (Bunka 1, 1804),
　4th month: Accompanied him on his return journey to Edo

First, Bunka 2, year of the Ox (1805), 8th month
　Appointed as tea attendant (o-cha o-ban) to Lord Kinbun, and granted
　3 ryō in ceremonial money

First, same month: Appointed as temporary group leader (kumigashira karigaku)
　　However, from this time until the year of the Sheep (Bunka 4, 1807), whenever in residence in the domain,
　　he was appointed to temporary roles

First, same 7th year, year of the Horse (1810), 3rd month: Appointed as
　temporary timekeeper (tokei karigaku)

First, same 9th year, year of the Monkey (1812), 2nd month: Appointed as
　temporary head of the priest group (bōzu kumigashira karigaku)

First, same year: Due to his diligent service, in place of Edo stipend,
　1 ryō in gold was granted annually

First, same 12th year, year of the Boar (1815): Appointed to
　a cost-saving position in personal service (o-temoto go-shōryaku nin'yaku)

First, same 13th year, year of the Rat (1816), 4th month: Appointed to
　accompany the journey to Kyoto

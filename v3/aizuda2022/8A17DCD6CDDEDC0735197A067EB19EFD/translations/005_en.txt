One entry: In the 7th month of Kanbun 10 (1670, the year of the Dog), [he] was granted a stipend of 20 koku and 3 persons' rations.
　　　[He] was summoned to serve as a personal secretary (yūhitsu) to Lady Hōshōin.
　　　　Note: Until the 10th month of the same year,
　　　　as Lord Hōtsusama and Lady Hōshōin were residing in the domain,
　　　　[he] served in attendance between the Main Keep (Honmaru) and the Third Enclosure (Sannomaru).

　One entry: In the 9th month of the same year, the request to change [his] family name to "Nakoya" was granted as petitioned.

　One entry: In the 10th month of the same year,

　　　As Lord Hōtsusama departed for Edo (sankō), [he] accompanied [him] and traveled up to the capital, serving in the first rotation (ichijin) until the spring of the following year (the year of the Boar). As Hayashi Shichidayū fell ill and was given leave, [he] was ordered to go directly to the Shiba residence as a replacement and serve there, and descended [to the domain] in the 10th month of the following year of the Boar.

　One entry: In the 2nd month of Kanbun 12 (1672, the year of the Rat),

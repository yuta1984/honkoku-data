Item: In the 10th month of the same year (Hōei 5 [1708]), in accordance with his parent's petition, he was granted leave and returned (descended) to Aizu.

Item: In the 2nd month of the 6th year of Hōei, the year of the Ox (1709), in accordance with his parent's petition, he was relieved of his duties as o-kosho (page/attendant).

Item: In the 8th month of the 3rd year of Shōtoku, the year of the Snake (1713), in accordance with his parent's petition, he was appointed to the castle guard rotation (御番代), entered the unit of Hyūga Saburōemon, and served in the castle guard.

Item: In the 9th month of the 4th year of Shōtoku, the year of the Horse (1714), he was granted an inherited stipend (跡式御知行) of 100 koku without discrepancy.

Item: In the 9th month of the 1st year of Kyōhō, the year of the Monkey (1716), his petition to change his name to Sōzaemon was approved as requested.

Item: In the 10th month of the 3rd year of Kyōhō, the year of the Dog (1718), he traveled up to Edo for duty rotation (勤番), and returned in the 4th month of the following year of the Boar (1719).

Item: In the 2nd month of the 15th year of Kyōhō, the year of the Dog (1730), his unit commander (頭) Hyūga Saburōemon was granted permission to retire in accordance with his petition, and [Sōzaemon] was transferred to the unit of Bukawa Gen'nosuke as the successor assignment.

Item: In the 12th month of the 16th year of Kyōhō, the year of the Boar (1731), his unit commander Bukawa Gen'nosuke was appointed as wakadoshiyori (若年寄, junior elder), and [Sōzaemon] was transferred to the unit of Miyake Magobei as the successor assignment.

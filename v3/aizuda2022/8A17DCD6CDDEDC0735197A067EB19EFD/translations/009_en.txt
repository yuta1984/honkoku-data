**Shirai Anken Shigechika** (first name: Iemon, later Kisōemon; born on the 21st day of the 3rd month of Tenna 2 [1682])

Mother: Same as above (as noted previously)

Item: In the 8th month of Genroku 9 [1696], he became involved in a quarrel with Negishi Kichinosuke, for which he was sentenced to banishment (oharai). He was thereafter permitted to return to his place of residence and changed his name to "Anken," under which he continued to serve.

Item: Died of illness. The date of death and posthumous Buddhist name (謐号) are unknown. Place of burial: Ōkuboyama.

**Second Generation: Sōzaemon Shigemochi** (first name: Chōnosuke, later Umon; born on the 1st day of the 10th month of Genroku 9 [1696])

Mother: Same as above

Wife: Daughter of Hayashi Shirōzaemon Shigeyuki (born on the 15th day of the 3rd month of Hōei 1 [1704]; died of illness on the 23rd day of the 11th month of An'ei 7 [1778] at the age of 75; posthumous name: Kakushuku-junin; place of burial: Ōkuboyama)

Item: In the 1st month of Hōei 5 [1708], he was granted a standard stipend grant (nami no ogisaku) and was appointed as a page (okoshō).

Item: In the 3rd month of the same year, he accompanied the lord on his journey to Edo (gosanpu), traveling up to Edo.

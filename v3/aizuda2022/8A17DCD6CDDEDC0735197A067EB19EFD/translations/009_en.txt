**Shirai Anken Shigechika** (first name: Iemon, later Kishūemon; born on the 21st day of the 3rd month of Tenna 2 [1682])

　Mother: Same as above (daughter of the Koizumi clan)

Item: In the 8th month of Genroku 9 [1696], he got into a fight with Negishi Kichinosuke and was therefore ordered into banishment (exile). He was subsequently permitted to return to his residence, changed his name to Anken, and lived accordingly.

Item: Died of illness. The date and posthumous name (諡) are unknown. Place of burial: Ōkuboyama.

**Second Generation**

**Sōzaemon Shigeyuki** (first name: Chōnosuke, later Umon; born on the 1st day of the 10th month of Genroku 9 [1696])

　Mother: Same as above

　Wife: Daughter of Hayashi Shirōzaemon Shigeyuki (born on the 15th day of the 3rd month of Hōei 1 [1704]; died of illness on the 23rd day of the 11th month of An'ei 7 [1778] at the age of 75; posthumous Buddhist name: Chōkaku Shukujūnin; place of burial: Ōkuboyama)

Item: In the 1st month of Hōei 5 [1708], he was granted the standard stipend allotment (並之御擬作) and was appointed as an o-kosho (page/attendant).

Item: In the 3rd month of the same year, he served as an escort for the lord's official visit to Edo (参府) and traveled up to Edo.

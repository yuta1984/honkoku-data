Sōbei Shigeyuki (初名/first name: Sōkichi | Born on the 16th day of the 11th month of the 17th year of Kyōhō, the year of the Rat [1732])

　　Mother: Same as above (a woman of the Hayashi clan)

　　Wife: A daughter of Sōma Kurobei Shigeatsu (Born on the 24th day of the 4th month of the 5th year of Genbun, the year of the Monkey [1740] | Died of illness on the 23rd day of the 12th month of the 5th year of Bunsei, the year of the Horse [1822], at the age of 83. Posthumous name [謐名]: Genshuku Jojin. Place of burial: Same as above.)

Item: In the 8th month of the 2nd year of Hōreki, the year of the Monkey (1752), he was granted, without question, the inherited stipend (跡式御知行) of 100 koku, and served in castle guard duty (御城御番) under the unit of Yanase Kyūma.

Item: In the 2nd month of the 12th year of Hōreki, the year of the Monkey (1762), due to the reassignment (組替え) of his unit commander Yanase Kyūma, he was transferred to the unit of Yamazaki Sasuke as the successor assignment.

Item: In the 4th month of the 14th year of Hōreki (1764), his petition to revert to his original family name of Nagoshi (名越) was approved as requested.

Item: In the 3rd month of the 8th year of Meiwa, the year of the Rabbit (1771), as his unit commander Yamazaki Sasuke was relieved of his post in accordance with his own petition, he was transferred to the unit of Tanaka Kahei as the successor assignment.

Item: In the 3rd month of the 5th year of An'ei, the year of the Monkey (1776), due to the reassignment of his unit commander Tanaka Kahei, he was transferred to the unit of Kasuga Jinzaemon as the successor assignment.

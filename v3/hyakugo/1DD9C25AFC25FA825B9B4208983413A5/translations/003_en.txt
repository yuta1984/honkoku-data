I humbly request: Regarding the various administrative duties of Ueno-no-sho in Yamashiro Province, a domain of Tō-ji Temple
1. Annual tribute and other obligations shall be carried out diligently according to the land registry compiled this time
2. Regarding wasteland due to river flooding and water damage, reports shall be made without concealment according to the actual conditions of restoration work. Details shall be reported to the temple administration for increasing annual tribute and other payments. One must never harbor thoughts of disloyalty or personal gain by following the words of deputy officials, village headmen, or peasants
3. Regarding matters when an inspector is sent concerning suspicious matters about wasteland, one must not speak following the words of deputy officials
4. Should illness occur, one must especially employ secret measures both internally and externally, making every effort to handle affairs to the utmost
5. Should misconduct by deputy officials be exposed and personnel changes be ordered, one must follow the temple's commands without objection and not collaborate with deputy officials
6. When judicial matters arise, following temple precedent, two-thirds shall be paid to the temple treasury and the remaining one-third shall be personal compensation. Unreasonable judicial decisions must not be made, and details must be reported to the temple administration. In all matters, caring for the people shall be fundamental, and the law shall take priority
7. The 6 koku 5 to of allocated rice from the current 5 tan of irrigated fields is the compensation for administrative duties. Nothing else may be used for private purposes
8. 200 mon for hot water cutting feet (half portion determined) shall be handled annually
The above administrative position has been allocated as temple benevolence. Even in a single matter, these articles must never be violated. Should anyone make false statements, they shall suffer the divine punishment of Hachiman Daibosatsu, the guardian deity of this temple, and the Great Master. Therefore, this written pledge is as stated above.
Kōan 1st year, 8th month, 5th day - Gon-risshi Yūkan (Seal)

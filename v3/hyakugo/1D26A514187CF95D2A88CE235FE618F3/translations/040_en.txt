Lecture Meeting

First volume of distributed commentaries

Lecturer: Sōshō

Question: Regarding the skillful and wonderful meaning of the sutra text being good in the beginning, middle, and end

Can it be said that in the realm of self-realization there exists the aspect of verbal teachings?

Tenshō 3rd year (1575)
Assembly Lecture, October 4th
First Distributed Commentary

Lecturer: Shōshō (chanting)
Questioners: Shūshū
           Kōshō (flower scattering), Keiyū
           Shōei section

Question: Regarding "Tathāgata Arhat" in the lecture-praise scripture, can the name "Tathāgata" be said to apply to both self-realization and the salvation of others?

Question: Concerning the clarification of principle-essence-characteristics in the commentary, can it be said that there are degrees of depth and shallowness in the essential principles taught in exoteric and esoteric Buddhism?

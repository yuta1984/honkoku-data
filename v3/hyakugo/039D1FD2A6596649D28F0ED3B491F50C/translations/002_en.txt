Prohibition Order
　　　　　To Tōji Temple
The right [hereby orders that] our military forces and various persons
are strictly prohibited from committing acts of violence and lawlessness.
If there are any who violate [this order],
they shall be severely punished. Therefore, we issue this directive as stated.
　　Eishō 8th year, 8th month, 27th day　Kii no Kami (monogram)
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Saemon no Jō (monogram)
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Nakatsukasa Shōsuke (monogram)

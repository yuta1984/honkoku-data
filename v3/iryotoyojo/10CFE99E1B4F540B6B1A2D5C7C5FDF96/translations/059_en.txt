　　Types of vegetables that use the root

Burdock　　The "Kyoka Hitsuyō" states: "In fertile, good soil, plow thoroughly three to five times in the first month, dig deeply to soften the earth, spread manure and level the surface, then sow seeds densely at the end of the second month. If sown thinly, the core of the root becomes hollow. After the seedlings emerge, weeds should be removed. During dry periods, water should be applied." It also states: "Those that bear fruit in the current year are not suitable for sowing. Seeds that have ripened on the roots should be planted." The "Getsuryō Kōgi" states: "If sowing is done during rain, germination occurs immediately. If there is no rain, water should be used." Also, cutting and eating the shoots should be done in the same manner as cutting chives. ◦〇 During autumn, winter, and spring, it is of the highest quality, same as daikon radish.

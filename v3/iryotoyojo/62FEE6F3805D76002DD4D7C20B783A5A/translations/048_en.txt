【Right page】
　　Relying on Medicine　Depending on medicine
If one relies only on taking medicine without being cautious in all matters, there will be no benefit

　　Late Nourishment　Suddenly practicing health cultivation in old age
Having lived carelessly and then being cautious in old age is like hurrying to find a guide at sunset

　　Cause of Disaster　The seed of calamity
Disaster comes along with the treasures one covets, and the soul deteriorates through sexual indulgence

　　Light Powder　Mercury powder
Using light powder as medicine for sores may heal for the moment but becomes a later disaster

【Left page】
　　Embracing Stone　Stone medicine that stimulates yang energy
Taking medicine for impotence without proper understanding is like adding fuel to a burning flame

　　Blaming Others　Resenting other people
One blames the doctor and demands quick results, but since I created the illness myself, I should not resent others

　　Salty and Sour　Salt and acid
If one consumes cold vinegar after eating salty hot food, one will lose their voice

　　Fear of Treatment　Being confused about treatment
Generally, people's thoughts are disturbed, but one should not think of dead stones in Go as if they could be made alive

【Right page】
　　Moderation　Not exceeding proper limits
Even wealth, when excessive, becomes one's own disaster, and if one eats to satiety, life becomes short

　　Selection　Choosing things
Though fleeting, worrying about fortune and misfortune exhausts the body in all matters

　　Oral Indulgence　Refers to mouth (eating/drinking) and sexual desire
If one restrains the mouth's intake and sexual desire, the two evils of disaster and disease will not approach the body

　　Sweating and Purging　Inducing sweating and using laxatives
Though one dislikes sweating and diarrhea, they are like the internal and external masters of one's own body

【Left page】
　　Military Medicine　Storing medicines
Understand effective medicines. When nothing is wrong, how should one take them?

　　Joy and Anger　Rejoicing and becoming angry
Even joy and laughter, when excessive, diminish yang energy. Anger especially causes yin deficiency

　　Strenuous Work　Physical labor
Bad horses, strong bows, wrestling - when such strenuous work accumulates, it becomes muscular apoplexy

　　Sleeping After Eating　Lying down when full
If one lies down immediately after eating to satiety, the lower back aches, and abdominal masses will surely form

Chapter 5: Guidelines for Using Medicine

The ancients said, "If the method is not complete, there is no way to cure." This means that if medicine is not used according to proper methods, illness will not be cured.

【Left page】
For this reason, the methods by which people of old used medicine are not easily accomplished by patients of today, but I will briefly raise only the essential points. First, the decoction of medicine must be done carefully and should not be treated carelessly. For water, one should use "well-flower water," which is freshly drawn water. Although there are various differences in the water used for decoction depending on the illness, in most cases well-flower water is used.

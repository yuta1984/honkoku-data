○Charm for hiccups
Without the person knowing, take one sheet of half-paper and fold it gradually toward the left side (for men) from the beginning, place it under the left knee and have them step on it firmly while sitting. For women, fold it to the right side and place it under the right knee. Face the person when performing this.
To stop one's own hiccups, write the character "temple" (寺) three times in cold water and drink that water in three gulps. The healing is wondrous.

○Method to cure bedwetting
Place a folded half-paper under the sleeping mat where urination occurred, have the child sleep on it, then burn this to charcoal and mix with five parts licorice to drink. It will stop from that very night, which is wondrous.

○Charm to prevent blisters during travel
At Setsubun (seasonal division), take roasted beans and keep them. When departing on a journey, eat a number equal to your age. Blisters will not form and you will be safe.

○Medicine for health
When traveling, one must carry pinellia. For straw sandal chafing, thong rubbing, blisters and such that become wounds and cause pain, shave raw pinellia pellets and apply the powder - it heals wondrously.

○Medicine to blacken white hair
Simmer black soybeans in vinegar and use regularly as hair oil. Simmered pomegranate skin is also good.

○Medicine to prevent hair from falling out when it is falling
Torreya nuts, walnuts, oriental arborvitae leaves
Soak these three ingredients in water and use as hair oil.

○Medicine to apply to thin hair areas or baldness
Baby hair, calomel, pine resin, mouse droppings, umbilical cord

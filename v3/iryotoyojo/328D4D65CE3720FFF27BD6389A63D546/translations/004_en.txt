○When west winds persist for long periods, there will be fires. This is because they dry out things. Northwest winds especially bring concerns of fire disasters.
○If rain falls at the fifth watch (around 2 AM), the next day will surely be clear. The fifth watch refers to the seventh hour of night (around 2 AM). Evening rain is difficult to clear.
○After long rains, when rain stops in the evening and it clears brightly, it will surely rain again. When rain and snow mix, it is difficult to clear.
○When rainwater has bubbles, it does not clear easily.
○During long rains when it's cloudy and doesn't clear, if it stops a little before noon, there will be heavy rain after noon. If it stops a little at exactly noon, that's good.
○Long rain with darkness is good. When the sky suddenly becomes bright, it will surely rain again. When the sun shines during rain, the weather is not good.

　○Treatment for nightmare-induced near-death (sleep paralysis)
Quickly blow powdered pinellia and gleditsia into the nose to expel phlegm, then give Suko pills. However, those whose bodies are still with sunken eyes, or those with blue-black complexions cannot be cured.

Another method: powder gleditsia alone to about the size of a bean and blow it into the nose. When sneezing occurs, qi will flow. Also blowing powdered realgar into the nose with a tube is good.
　○Method to cure those who cannot speak due to shock
There are those who encounter warfare, fires, bandits, or fierce beasts, become greatly shocked, and cannot speak. Make litharge into fine powder and give about five parts at a time with good tea. Before long, they will surely speak.

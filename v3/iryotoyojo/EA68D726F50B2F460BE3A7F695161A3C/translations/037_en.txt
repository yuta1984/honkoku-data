If one obtains a superior doctor and takes medicine, it is good. If one obtains an inferior doctor and takes medicine, it will make things worse, so if one does not take medicine, it will neither improve nor worsen, hence the saying about obtaining a mediocre doctor. Medicine should not be taken carelessly. As explained before, one should be cautious from the beginning. If, on the other hand, the first doctor errs in treatment and the condition worsens, since the doctor may have made treatment errors, one thinks it became this severe because a serious illness had developed.

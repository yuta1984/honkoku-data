It is a disease of summer and autumn, common in young people. It combines external pathogens with internal injury. In summer, the yang qi of heaven and earth emerges above ground, while yang qi below ground becomes weak. Therefore, well water becomes cold. In winter, yang qi above ground is weak while yang qi fills the underground. Therefore, well water is warm. Similarly in humans, in summer the body's yang qi makes the skin surface hot while the abdomen becomes cold. Yet people eat cold foods, drink cold water, cool themselves outside the gate or by waterside at night, sleep without proper bedding, become affected by wind and dampness, generate heat, the heat combines with water qi, urination becomes difficult, and beriberi develops.

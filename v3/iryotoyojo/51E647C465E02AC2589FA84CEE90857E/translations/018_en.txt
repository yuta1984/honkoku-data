○After food poisoning, it is good to abstain from food for even one day. If one does eat, one should consume only soft foods in small amounts for recovery. This method is better than medical treatment. Detoxifying medicines damage the spleen and stomach and reduce qi. Furthermore, they are not suitable for elderly or weak people. Most elderly people who die from sudden illness die from food poisoning. One should be cautious. At such times, one should use ginger juice mixed with equal parts salt. This will bring recovery.

○According to Li Liweng's theory, foods one constantly favors serve as medicinal supplements. One should eat them little by little. There is benefit in this. If one eats too much, it again becomes harmful. One should not eat foods one dislikes, as they become harmful.

○People who are weak from illness or constitutionally frail should regularly eat grilled meat of fish and fowl. It is said to surpass the supplemental benefits of ginseng.

○When any food feels like it gets stuck, this is a symptom of spleen and stomach deficiency. Whatever it is should be cut thin before eating. Thick pieces get stuck and are not good for one's health.

○There is an old saying: "Grains should prevail over meat; meat should not be allowed to prevail over grains." Elderly and weak people should always keep this saying in mind.

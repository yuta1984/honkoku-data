○Regarding moxibustion treatment: If a child is born with poor complexion, lacking luster, with weak crying voice and appears sickly, moxibustion treatment should be performed. If born with good color and luster, loud crying voice and strength, this indicates no illness. Moxibustion treatment must not be performed. The reason is that children as a whole grow through vigorous yang qi. Moxa mugwort nourishes and assists those with insufficient yang qi. Therefore, moxibustion is not good for healthy children. Since elderly people have insufficient yang qi, moxibustion treatment is generally good for them. When moxibustion is performed on healthy children without cause, there occurs what is called "partial excess and partial deficiency," and this usually inevitably produces head sores.

The classics state, "All pain, itching, and sores belong to heart fire." One should consider based on this. Also, there is the saying "When there is cause, there is no toxicity." If we reverse this phrase, when there is no cause, acupuncture, moxibustion, and medicine are all toxic. The common saying "Medicine does not kill people; doctors kill people" - one should contemplate and understand such matters.

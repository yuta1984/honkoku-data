○The "Longevity and Elder Care" states there are seven forms of nourishment: Use few words to nourish internal qi. Restrain sexual desire to nourish vital essence. Keep flavors mild to nourish blood qi. Drink body fluids to nourish organ qi. Suppress anger to nourish liver qi. Moderate eating and drinking to nourish stomach qi. Reduce contemplation to nourish heart qi. This is the way to preserve longevity. In all things, practice moderation and cultivation for health.

○Master Sun Simiao said there are five beneficial practices in the way of cultivation: It is good to comb the hair frequently. It is good to constantly apply hands to the face. It is good to tap the teeth frequently. It is good to constantly swallow saliva. It is good to constantly practice qi. "Practice" means to do quietly. "It is good to apply hands to the face" means to constantly stroke and massage the face. There is a method for this stroking: Rub the palms together vigorously until the inside of the hands becomes hot, then first apply them to the eyes to warm them repeatedly, and immediately stroke and massage the face. When practicing this two or three times daily, even if one's vision is blurred due to aging eyes, it will become clear after some time passes - this is truly wondrous and marvelous.

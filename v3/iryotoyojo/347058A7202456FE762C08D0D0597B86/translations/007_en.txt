Hot Spring Treatise
Hot springs are abundant throughout the various provinces of Japan. Beneath the mountains where these hot springs exist, there is sulfur, which heats the water and turns it into hot spring water. Therefore, all hot spring water has a yellowish odor for this reason. Consequently, it effectively cures various skin ailments such as scabies and ringworm, and other diseases affecting the body's surface. However, today's common people say that hot springs are good for internal ailments, consumptive diseases, deficiency of qi and blood, and women's infertility. This is entirely due to their ignorance of the true nature of what makes hot springs what they are. Therefore, many people die while attempting to cure their illnesses.

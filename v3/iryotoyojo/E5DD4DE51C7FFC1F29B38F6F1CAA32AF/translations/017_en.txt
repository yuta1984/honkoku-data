However, when there is some obstruction that causes movement and distress, the place where the membrane attaches to the uterus becomes misaligned from its proper position inside and out, creating discord, and through these gaps, excess water from the bladder leaks out, or blood may leak out. Having children is not within human power to control; it is entirely heaven's doing. Poverty and wealth are also heaven's doing and should not be attributed to the presence or absence of children.

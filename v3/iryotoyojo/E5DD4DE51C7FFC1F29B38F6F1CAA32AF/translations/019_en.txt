　　　Explanation of Fetal Development

According to the Theory of Five Organs: In the first month, it is like pearl dew. In the second month, it is like a peach blossom. In the third month, male and female are differentiated. In the fourth month, the form takes shape. In the fifth month, muscles and bones develop. In the sixth month, hair grows. In the seventh month, the soul is set free to play, and the child can move its left hand well. In the eighth month, the spirit is set free to play, and the child can move its right hand well. In the ninth month, it turns its body three times. In the tenth month, it receives qi and becomes complete.

　　　Distinguishing Male and Female

【Left page】
Generally, when yin-blood arrives first and yang-essence strikes afterward, the blood opens and envelops the essence, with yang inside and yin outside. Embracing yin and yang, the fetus takes male form. If yang-essence enters first and yin-blood joins afterward, then the essence opens and envelops the blood, with yin inside and yang outside. Embracing yang and yin, the fetus takes female form, as the ancients said.

Forbidden Food Combination Songs (Verses about prohibited food pairings)

Do not combine carp with black pepper or red beans
Do not combine eel with watermelon, papaya, or plum vinegar
Avoid sugar with apples, and udon noodles with watermelon
Do not combine sweet potato with muskmelon
Bayberry with roasted beans is bad, as is plum with papaya
Also avoid sweet potato with kumquat
Avoid pepper with beet and purslane
Avoid celery with pokeweed, and torreya nuts with mung beans
Avoid watermelon with buckwheat noodles, and raccoon dog meat with yellow sturgeon
Walnuts and bayberry, even bathing should be avoided

If one does not know swimming alone, one's work aboard ships on seas and rivers will not be free, and if one recklessly throws away one's life, this should be understood. Now, since there are no teachers specifically for swimming, there are no regulations such as certificates of proficiency. From the very basics of practice, it involves matters of life and death, and through training based on actual experience, people simply talk among themselves, open their own wisdom, and achieve mastery through self-realization. Seeing skilled practitioners emerge this way, one should not consider simplicity to be bad. These matters are unrelated to medicine,

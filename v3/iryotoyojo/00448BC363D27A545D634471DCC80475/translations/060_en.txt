【Right page】
Many shave their heads and masquerade as doctors. This is precisely why
the world is filled with quack doctors who cause great harm to people.
Akimichi (Gendō) has served under his late father and elder brother since his youth,
observing treatments for a long time, and the cases he has
personally treated number in the tens of thousands.
During that time, treating this disease was most frequent.
Perhaps due to this reputation, people wonder if he might have special techniques,
and they come from distant places seeking diagnosis and treatment.

【Left page】
Among these, there have been occasional successes,
but most were lucky coincidences, and when I look back on myself inwardly,
I often break out in cold sweat. Even with the most basic cases, I was unable
to distinguish between the two conditions of aconite and gypsum symptoms,
and spent many months and years in this state. Now, having passed
the age of seventy,
these poor tales of the past are
embarrassing, but medical practice is a difficult matter, and not
an art that ordinary people should undertake. Therefore, quack doctors are always numerous.

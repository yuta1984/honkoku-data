○It is commonly said that the eldest child is difficult to raise. Though the father is the same father and the mother is the same mother, the eldest is born weak while the second and third children are born robust and sturdy - this defies reason. Generally, with fruits and the like, the first ones to develop are considered the original and are good for seeds. The truth is that the eldest child is precious and is pampered with stroking and fondling, the household members also fuss over them, eating loses moderation, and even without illness, medicine is given daily as preventive medicine.

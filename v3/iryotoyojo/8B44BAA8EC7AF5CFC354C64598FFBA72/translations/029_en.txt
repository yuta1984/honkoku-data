This is called either "protective ki" (eiki) or "luminous radiance" (kōki). In sword testing techniques, what emanates from visual power and from sword points, which some people call "expansive force" (gakki), is the same phenomenon. {This matter is described in detail in the sword technique section of Volume 3.

I heard this. The people of that time called him the Immortal Hakuyū. It is said he was the teacher of the former Tsuneyama family. Hakuyū said: "Generally speaking, in the art of nurturing life, the upper part should always be cool and refreshing, while the lower part should always be warm. If you make the fundamental qi fill the lower part, you should know this is the most important aspect of nurturing life. People often only hear about refining the divine elixir by combining the five elements, but they don't know that the five elements of water, fire, wood, and metal are actually the five sense organs of eye, ear, nose, and tongue. [Interlinear note: Speaking of five elements without mentioning earth, and speaking of five sense organs while omitting body. Hakuin's original manuscript is like this. This is a momentary error and probably has no deep meaning.] What does it mean to gather the five sense organs to refine the divine elixir?

【Left page】
In answer to this, [Interlinear note: The term "refining elixir" is common parlance among Taoists, referring to their practice of exhaling old breath and inhaling new breath, comparing this to refining elixir medicine. They call this "internal elixir," and call the area two and a half inches below the navel the "elixir field." Medical practitioners since the Jin dynasty have specialized in advocating this. Though it is quite a vulgar term, this place is the pivot of the human body where heavenly qi enters through the nose and spreads throughout the body. Since Hakuin also received this technique from the earthly immortal Hakuyū, he cannot avoid speaking of it. Therefore, the vulgarity of the name should not harm the meaning.] There is the method of the five non-leakages: the eyes do not see carelessly, the ears do not hear carelessly, the tongue does not speak carelessly, the body does not touch carelessly, and the mind does not think carelessly. This is the primordial unified qi in its natural state - what Mencius called the "vast qi." Draw this down and gather it in the area of the navel wheel, qi sea, and elixir field, and over months and years, guard it with single-minded focus, nurture it to become without-

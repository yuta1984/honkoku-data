Judging the matter of one achievement and finding the heart that fits what is good, "righteousness" is annotated as "appropriate." Though we speak of benevolence, righteousness, propriety, wisdom, and trustworthiness as the Five Constants, righteousness among them relates to benevolence, propriety, wisdom, and trustworthiness, broadly judging and making them conform to the Way. Therefore, in the way that a lord employs retainers and retainers serve their lord, this is particularly praised, saying "there is righteousness between lord and retainer." This is because the relationship between lord and retainer differs from those who share the same flesh and blood as father-child or elder-younger brother, or the intimacy of husband and wife who share the same bed and drink from the wedding cups together - it is based solely on righteousness.

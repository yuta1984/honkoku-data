When one reaches the state of wu-di (non-attachment/natural spontaneity), the elixir furnace is overturned, and inner, outer, and middle realms, the eight directions and four dimensions - all become one great returning elixir. The self realizes that it is the great divine immortal of long life and enduring vision that neither was born before heaven and earth nor dies after empty space. Here, the great ocean is stirred to become cream and butter, and thick earth is transformed into gold. Therefore it is said: one grain of the returning elixir can transform iron into gold by touching it. one should know that this elixir is indeed not an external substance. The earth has jade fields and grain fields. Jade fields are places that produce pearls and precious stones; grain fields are places where crops are cultivated. Humans have qi-sea and elixir-field. The qi-sea is the precious place for gathering and nurturing fundamental qi; the elixir-field is the citadel where divine elixir is refined and longevity is preserved. According to ancient tradition, the reason the blue sea is the chief of a hundred rivers is because it flows downward. The blue sea already occupies the lowest position among all waters, containing and embracing a hundred rivers without increase or decrease. The qi-sea already resides below the five internal organs, receiving true qi without satiation, finally achieving the divine elixir and entering the immortal capital.

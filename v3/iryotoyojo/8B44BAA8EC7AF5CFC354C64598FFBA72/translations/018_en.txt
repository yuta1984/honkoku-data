　　　Discussing the nature of qi
Qi is the great atmosphere that fills the space between heaven and earth, and is the fundamental source from which all things derive their
生成 (formation) and 化育 (development and nurturing).
　Zhang Hengqu said: "What fills heaven and earth is my body. What governs heaven and earth is my nature. The people are my siblings, and all things are
my companions." These words completely express the principles of heaven and earth within just six phrases. This "filling of heaven and earth" refers to what fills the space between heaven and earth

Sound and color share the same origin, and their function arises from the mathematical principles of nature, creating wave patterns of varying length, brevity, height, and depth in this atmospheric medium, thereby producing sympathetic response in the eardrum. To understand the principle of the natural mysterious function of musical temperament arising from these numerical degrees, one must first comprehend the principle by which the accumulation of atoms forms substance.

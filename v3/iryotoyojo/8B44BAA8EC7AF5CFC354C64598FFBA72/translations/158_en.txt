Reflecting on this again, human illness and suffering mostly arise from seeing with the eyes and hearing with the ears, desiring in the mind, and extending to thoughts of food, drink, and comfort, as well as hopes for wealth, status, and power - all of which are perceived through these windows located in the upper part of the body: the eyes, ears, nose, and mouth.

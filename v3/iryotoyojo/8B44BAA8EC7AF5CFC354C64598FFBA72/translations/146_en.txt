In explaining Mencius's "vast-flowing qi," I speak of it only from the perspective of moral principle. Now, discussing what has been tested through practical matters such as martial arts, music, and various skills is merely to indicate the path from the near to the far, ascending from the lowly to the lofty. In the latter section, I record how the vast-flowing qi can be cultivated through the art of breath regulation, and conclude the chapter. Even though this long discourse and useless words, this unnecessary verbose talk, ultimately seeks only the realization of the great Way of simplicity and impartiality.

Yōkisetsu (Treatise on Nurturing Qi), Volume Three

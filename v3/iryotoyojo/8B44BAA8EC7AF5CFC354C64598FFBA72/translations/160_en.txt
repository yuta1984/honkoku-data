With the yang hexagram Qian below and the yin-yang in communication, this is taken as the symbol of peace, called "Earth-Heaven Peace" (Ditai). Tai can be read as "to flow through" or "peaceful," and the symbol of this hexagram is when worthy people achieve their aspirations, the Way is practiced above, petty people retreat to the countryside and do not harm the gentlemen, the five grains are abundant, and the four seas are peaceful. When the human body is well in this manner, illness does not arise. However, contrary to this, even if the ruler above has a benevolent and compassionate heart, if the ministers who assist him are not loyal and faithful, and the officials below them also harbor private interests, bribery is practiced, influence peddling by women becomes rampant, and the collection of taxes and tributes becomes excessive, causing the common people to fall into poverty,

Its harm is called the constant calamity of worldly fortune—various disasters befall the nation, with not a moment's peace, and the righteous way is not practiced.

Guidelines for Child Rearing

Children who appear plump and free from skin ailments may paradoxically suffer sudden onset of convulsions, constipation, or skin eruptions.

Children who develop many types of skin conditions typically show fewer symptoms of parasitic worms.

Children with spleen-related parasitic conditions often begin to lose weight and weaken from the time of weaning.

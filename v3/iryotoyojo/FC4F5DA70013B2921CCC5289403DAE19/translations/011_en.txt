○ Theory of Spleen and Stomach in Various Pediatric Diseases

In all matters, it is essential to know the root of things. Without knowing the root, even hard work brings no benefit.
For example, fertilizing the branches and leaves of plants brings no benefit, but when fertilizing their roots, both branches and leaves
flourish together. How much more so in the treatment of people. The foundation of the human body consists of the five
organs and six bowels. The foundation of the five organs and six bowels is the spleen. Also, the foundation of all things is earth.
Earth nourishes all things, and the spleen receives and transforms food to nourish the five organs and six bowels, therefore
the spleen is called both the mother of the five organs and also earth. When earth becomes deficient once, all things are damaged.
When the spleen becomes deficient, the five organs and six bowels all decline. Among the distribution of the five flavors—sweet, pungent, bitter, sour, and salty—to the five organs,
the spleen governs sweetness. Although there are many diseases, none arise

　　　　Appendix
　　○Prescriptions to be used for epidemic diseases (Note: Typhoid fever, febrile diseases, and epidemic illnesses are all the same disease, known by several different names)
1. For epidemic diseases with fever, remove the soil from earthworms' bellies thoroughly, decoct with water and drink. This effectively reduces fever. It may also be used for other diseases with fever symptoms. Earthworms with white streaks near the head are considered even better.

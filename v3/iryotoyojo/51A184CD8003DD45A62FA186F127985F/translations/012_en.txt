○Prescriptions to be used for dysentery
1. For dysentery with strong fever and symptoms like epidemic disease, the method of mixing chicken eggs in hot water and drinking it to induce sweating, as described in the section on epidemic diseases, is good. If there is no feverish qi and the person has diarrhea, moxibustion should be applied daily at the Tianshu point (beside the navel) with fifteen to sixteen moxa cones each time.
　　　　　　　　　　　　The above appears in "Gedaihō" (Outer Platform Prescriptions)
1. For dysentery, combine equal parts of old ginger root and finest quality tea, decoct as usual, and use.
1. For dysentery, cook deer horn moss (which is seaweed) in miso soup and use.
　　　　　　　　　　　　The above appears in "Nijō Kyūmin Myōyakushū" (Nijō Collection of Wonderful Medicines for Saving the People)
1. For dysentery, use morning glory seeds that have been shade-dried and decocted. This prescription may be used from beginning to end.
　　　　　　　　　　　　The above from "Tō Jakusui Keiken" (Tō Jakusui's Experience)
○Prescriptions to be used when poisoned by food
1. When suffering from poisoning by any kind of food, pound fresh Chinese chives and drink the juice.

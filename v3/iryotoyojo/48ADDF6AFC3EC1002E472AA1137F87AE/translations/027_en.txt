▲Gakuya Pickles
In general, "gakuya" is the name of a hidden place where things are prepared in various ways. Even when pickles become old and their taste deteriorates, if they are prepared in various ways and soaked in sake or soy sauce before serving, they become even more delicious, so it probably became "gakuya."

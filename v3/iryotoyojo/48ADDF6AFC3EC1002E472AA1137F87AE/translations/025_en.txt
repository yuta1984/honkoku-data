▲Shippoku
During the Zhou dynasty, when worshipping demons and spirits, the vessels used were necessarily made of seven treasures (precious materials). Of course, everything offered to the gods was deep-fried in oil, and there were various other items such as vegetables and meat. After this ritual was completed, the offerings were taken down and everyone put them together in a pot and cooked and ate everything without leaving any behind. Therefore, because they were offered in vessels of seven treasures,

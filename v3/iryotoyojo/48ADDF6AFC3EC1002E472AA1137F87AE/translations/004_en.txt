【Left Page】
　　　　　Author's Preface
The craftsmanship I thought "I can do this!" about in the vigor of my youth—such is the sadness of an old warrior that I cannot match my son. Having retreated from the floating world to a three-tatami room in the back, I enjoy orthodox haikai poetry, but being fundamentally an illiterate craftsman is quite troublesome.

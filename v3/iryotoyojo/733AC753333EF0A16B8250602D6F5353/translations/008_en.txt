【Left Page】
Though we call it the current times,
when we speak of the new era, it refers to the masters
of handsome men, associating
only with beautiful things.
The unpopular types
who used to add hair ornaments
from their own initiative
now, even when they go out to play,
are not popular, and even their attendants
don't tie their sashes, and they don't even
notice this from the bottom of their hearts.
They cling to old ways,
and no matter how much you say
contemporary style is better, men should be
manly with some contemporary flair too—
without any bitterness,
it becomes tediously unfashionable.

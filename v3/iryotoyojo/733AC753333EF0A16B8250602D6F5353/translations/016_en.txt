【Right Page】
Akatsuchi and Kuryōken, all of them
conspiring together, due to Oyamada
gathered
the poor quality
items
from various provinces' inferior leaves,
calling them
together
and with brooms
swept
the sand
of the back fields,
whereupon
they all
became
superior tobacco.

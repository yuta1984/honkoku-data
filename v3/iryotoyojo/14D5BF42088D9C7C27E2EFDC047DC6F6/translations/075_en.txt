If one speaks of longing for a beloved person, there is also an ancient poem that reads "How sad is the wife who waits day after day, thinking 'he will surely come today' for her lord who lies fallen in the inlet." Taking this as referring to my own situation, my detailed lamentations are like the countless grains of sand on the beach - an ocean of writing that cannot be exhausted, causing those who hear it to wet their sleeves with tears.

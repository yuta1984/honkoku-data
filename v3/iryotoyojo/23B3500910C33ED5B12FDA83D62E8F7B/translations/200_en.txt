　　Eyeballs Protruding from Their Sockets
○In the "Zhengzhi Zhunsheng," it states that when the black pupils of the eyes suddenly protrude outside the eye sockets,
this symptom is caused by becoming severely angry while intoxicated with alcohol.
Therefore, when heavily intoxicated, one should refrain from becoming severely angry.

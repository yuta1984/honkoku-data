　　On Jaundice
○Jaundice is a disease in which the entire body, face, nails, sweat, and even urine all turn yellow. Sometimes there is alternating chills and fever with body pain, sometimes thirst and vomiting occur. There are classifications of five types called "five jaundice" and nine types called "nine jaundice."

According to Danxi's theory, all jaundice symptoms are due to damp-heat. The manifestation of yellow color is like fermenting koji (rice malt) to produce a yellow color. The spleen belongs to the central earth element and its color is yellow. Because damp-heat stagnates in the spleen and stomach, its original color

There are categories of upper wasting, middle wasting, and lower wasting. Although the causes of this condition's onset are also various, it is often due to alcohol. The "Inner Classic" states: "Those who engage in sexual activity when drunk and full, and develop heat stroke and wasting-thirst, are all wealthy and noble people." The "Thousand Gold Prescriptions" discusses this condition in detail. In general, if one accumulates long periods of drinking alcohol, one will inevitably develop the wasting-thirst disease. In the great cold when the sea freezes, alcohol does not freeze. Its intensely hot nature is clear.

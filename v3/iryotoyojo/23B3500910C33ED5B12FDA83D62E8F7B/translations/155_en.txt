When people with deficient blood crave alcohol and favor sexual pleasure, kidney water does not ascend and heart fire does not descend, resulting in consumptive disorder. This shows that wasting disease is caused by alcohol.
Generally, consumptive disorder symptoms occur mostly in young people, and do not occur in the elderly. When I secretly consider the reason for this, although the elderly originally have deficient yin qi, their yang qi is also not exuberant, so even when circumstances further deplete their yin qi, there is little harm from yang qi alone predominating. Although they develop symptoms of yin deficiency disease, they do not develop symptoms of bone steaming and wasting fever.

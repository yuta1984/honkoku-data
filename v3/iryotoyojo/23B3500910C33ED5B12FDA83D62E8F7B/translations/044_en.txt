○Sometimes people ask, "Should alcohol never be consumed regardless of quantity, or would there be no harm in using a small amount?" Generally speaking, alcohol causes harm only when consumed excessively. When used in small amounts according to proper measure, it often brings benefits. Li Shizhen's explanation in the Bencao Gangmu states that "when drunk in small amounts, it harmonizes the blood, circulates qi, strengthens the spirit,

He said this because silk cloth and similar materials easily rot when soaked in alcohol for long periods, and people are the same - alcohol is not good for them. That person replied, "Don't you see how meat preserved in sake lees endures for a long time?" This means that fish, birds, and meat do not rot when pickled in sake lees, and people are the same - alcohol is beneficial. One cannot know which of these benefits or harms is correct. However, there is one small technique: if you write letters or even pictures on white paper using alcohol, then expose it to air and let it dry, it appears as just white paper. When you hold this over a distant fire, the writing or pictures you made will appear in brown color. This is because the places soaked with alcohol scorch in the fire.

Even though contemporary medical arts are exhaustively applied in detail, treatment is difficult. It is precisely because treatment is impossible that I particularly discuss this. I do not intend to treat that disease with what I discuss now. I merely wish to use what I discuss here to prevent that disease from occurring in the first place. If people well understand how this condition arises in this manner and how difficult this condition is to treat in this way, and if even one person can be cautious about this and avoid developing this terrible condition, the merit would be greater than being able to treat the disease after it has developed.

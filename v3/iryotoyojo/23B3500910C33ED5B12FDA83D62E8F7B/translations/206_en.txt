Treatment Methods for Alcohol-Related Disorders
○The "Inner Classic" states: "Regarding the five flavors, when the mouth desires to eat them, one must exercise self-restraint and not overindulge. When excessive, it injures the vital essence. If one is cautious about the Way as prescribed by law, one will long maintain the mandate of Heaven." Therefore, greatly abstain from excessive drinking, carefully maintain good health practices, and wish to avoid becoming ill.

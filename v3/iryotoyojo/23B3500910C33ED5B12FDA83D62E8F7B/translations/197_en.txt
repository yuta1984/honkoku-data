　　Intestinal Reversal 【腸 is a colloquial character】
○According to Zhu Danxi's theory, there was a person named Ma Xisheng. He was over fifty years old and by nature loved
alcohol, constantly drinking heavily without getting drunk. He became ill and his feces came out from the front opening (urethra),
while his urine came out from the rear opening (anus). All six pulses were sunken and rough. He was given Four Substances Decoction with added
Lygodium spores, costus root, betel nut, akebia, and peach kernel. Taking this medicine cured him. This
person drank much alcohol, his qi became unrestrained, the alcohol ascended without descending, yang reached its extreme causing yin deficiency,
alcohol-dampness accumulated for a long time, generating heat that concentrated and dried the blood, causing yin to also
become greatly deficient. This was partial deficiency of yin and yang, all of which should be supplemented.

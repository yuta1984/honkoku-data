Medical Discourse Volume One
　　Discourse on the Good and Bad Qualities of Ginseng

The origin of doctors dates back to eight hundred fifteen thousand five hundred eighty-two years after heaven and earth opened, which corresponds to twenty-two thousand eight hundred seventy-seven years before the present time. In the Great Tang, located at the center of the three countries of Tenjiku (India), Shintan (China), and our court (Japan), the sage Taikou Fukki (Fu Xi) appeared and opened the eight trigrams, clearly teaching the way of yin-yang and the five elements. This was named the Book of Changes (I Ching) and became a work in two volumes that has been transmitted to the present day.

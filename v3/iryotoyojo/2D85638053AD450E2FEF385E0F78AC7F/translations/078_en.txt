For any illness, if one wishes to induce mild vomiting, dip a bird feather in oil and probe the throat - this will also cause vomiting. Those struck dead by violent lightning can also be saved using the principles of treatment for freezing and drowning described here. Those struck by lightning have been buried in earth, with some reviving after three hours and others showing signs of breath after one night.

There have also been cases of infants smothered by their mothers who revived after being buried in manure pits. Also, in the past, a patient who died suddenly from typhoid fever was buried in a graveyard but revived in the middle of the night, emerging from the grave and returning home

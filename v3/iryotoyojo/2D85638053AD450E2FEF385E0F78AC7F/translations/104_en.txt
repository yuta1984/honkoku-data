New Theory on Stable Horses ("厩" is the correct character for "廏" which is colloquial) 《This book discusses how current methods of training horses are inadequate for military use, and details how to raise horses with little expense, even including how to diagnose horse diseases. One volume already published》

Method for Refining Saltpeter 《This work argues that saltpeter produced in our country is superior to that of all nations, details methods for manufacturing saltpeter oneself in Edo, and even records the secrets of pharmaceutical compounding. One volume to be published》

Record of the Origins of Japan's Maritime Opening 《Illustrated hiragana reader, seven volumes, by Ichimudojin. This book uses ancient texts to detail how Japan's opening in ancient times was unparalleled in the world, describing how this should be a land that surpasses all nations, narrating up to the Mongol invasions and how divine winds overturned their ships - a work that stirs the Japanese spirit.》

Published with official permission in Ansei 3, year of the Fire Dragon (1856) ("凖" is colloquial for "準")

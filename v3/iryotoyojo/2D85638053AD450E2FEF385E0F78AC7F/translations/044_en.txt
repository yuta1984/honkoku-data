【Right page】
To treat shoulder bone dislocation,
　use the same method as
　　for treating cervical vertebrae described earlier.
　　Place the heel like this
　　against the patient's armpit,
　　　and pull the arm downward.
　　The heel pushing the armpit upward
　　　and both hands pulling the arm—
　　　　apply force simultaneously
　　　　　　　　and pull.

【Left page】
The shoulder bone mentioned earlier
　can also be treated
　　by bending it
　toward the chest
　　and pulling it
　in this manner
　　　　as well.
　There are various
　different
　　　methods,
　but the basic
　　principle
　　remains
　　　the same.

Gentle Purgative
　Chinese rhubarb 《100 monme, select those that are glossy, substantial, and not blackish in color》
　Decoct the above in 3 shō of water down to 1 shō, strain out the residue, add 50 monme of high-quality saltpeter and 100 monme of white honey, cook in a double boiler until it becomes like starch syrup, store in a container. In summer it becomes soft after some time. Using sugar instead of honey is also acceptable. Use 1-2 monme at a time.

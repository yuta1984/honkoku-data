For a wound received on the front of the shin, to bandage it oneself
　as emergency treatment using a hand towel or similar cloth:
　　wash away the blood with water, crumple paper
　　　and apply it, then take a hand towel torn like this
　　　　from back to front and bandage, then tie it from above.
　　　　　It is also good to tear the end of the hand towel slightly and apply it when bandaging.
　　　　　　　　　　　This shows the method
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　for emergency first aid.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Other cases should be understood
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　following this principle.

In general, to bandage wounds received on the abdomen, like cloth for binding the shin,
　use cotton with both ends of each strip torn, place them like laying them
　　on the back of the patient lying supine, take them from left and right,
　　　　and bandage alternately. If you use long cotton, each time you wrap it around the back,
　　　　　the patient's body moves which is not good, so
　　　　　　　one should well understand this principle.

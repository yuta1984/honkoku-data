【Right page】
For hip bone dislocation, place one palm
　　　against the lower back, grasp the shin and
　　　　after rotating,
　　　　　　pull backward.

【Left page】
When there is swelling,
　have one person
　　hold the top of the foot, place
　　　a hand on the kneecap and rotate,
　　　then together with the assistant,
　　　　pull
　　　　　　　backward—
　　　　　　　　　this is also good.

Healing Water (isōsui)
　Green vitriol　　Alum 《100 monme each》
　Grind the above two ingredients into fine powder, add 5 shō of water, place over fire, dissolve well,
　remove from fire, put into pottery or barrel, and carry to the battlefield.
　When using, soak cloth or loose cotton thread and apply to wounds. If
　the wound is shallow, after washing the blood clean with water,
　quickly apply this medicine soaked in folded cloth and bandage
　with cotton, then most injuries will heal quickly, so the following

Chicken Egg Oil (keitanyu)
　Chicken eggs 《50 pieces》　Sesame oil 《2 gō》
　Remove the shells from the eggs, grind well in a mortar, gradually
　add sesame oil and mix together for use. 《It is best to remove the egg white when using. Making it fresh when needed is particularly good.》

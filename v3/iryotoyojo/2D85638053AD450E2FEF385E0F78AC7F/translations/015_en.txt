Tools such as forceps should be prepared in advance, and these should be quickly extracted. 《For physicians of feudal lords who accompany armies, these instruments must be ordered to be prepared.》 The wound opening should be well washed with lye or clean water, 《washing with vinegar is also good.》 Healing water should be soaked into cotton and stuffed into the wound, fish glue plaster or litharge plaster should be applied, and then it should be wrapped with cotton cloth. If bleeding is difficult to stop, sponge soaked in hot water or water should be squeezed and used to wipe the blood well, covered with Hahasodake, and cotton soaked in vinegar should be applied and wrapped. For internal medicine, finely powdered charred pine needles taken with sake will miraculously treat arrowheads that have entered the flesh, as well as iron bullets that are difficult to remove, stuck needles, and bamboo or wood splinters.

　　　Treatment for burns from incendiary projectiles
When burned by fire projectiles (incendiary shells), if there are fragments of gunpowder, iron pieces, etc., extract them well with needles or bamboo spatulas. For blisters, puncture with needles to drain the fluid, then wipe away blood with sponge 《soaked in hot water and wrung out for use.》 《If bleeding does not stop, wash as described previously.》
Afterward, soak cotton in chicken egg oil to cover the wound, and wrap with cotton cloth over it. For burns on the head and face, as well as sword wounds, I have illustrated simple bandaging methods for each, so these should be learned in advance.

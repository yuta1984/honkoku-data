【Right page】
For those who have drowned and died in water, quickly remove their clothing and sash, strip them naked, take both legs and lift them upside down, place both legs on your shoulders, put the drowned person's abdomen against your back, bend your body forward, and press their abdomen with your back while walking to make them expel water. After they have expelled all the water, lower them from your back, dress them in clean clothing, loosely tie their sash, lay them in a futon, first open their mouth and thoroughly wash out any dirt or sand inside, clean the inside of their nose thoroughly to the depths using twisted paper, then reopen their mouth and blow a large amount of sneezing powder into their nose. 《Interlinear note: Sneezing powder uses finely ground pepper, or sneezewort from veratrum root, or soap pod,細辛, etc. These should be prepared in advance.》Then at the center of the shoulder, at the point called 肩井 (shoulder well)

【Left page】
grasp firmly with the thumb and the next two fingers (index and middle), and twist and turn the muscle in circles. 《Interlinear note: This is what is called "shoulder revival" in martial arts.》Then rotate and massage thoroughly the areas alongside the spine, hip bones, and both legs, massage well from below the heart to the middle of both flanks, again strongly twist the shoulder well point, and strike firmly with the fist around the fifth and seventh vertebrae of the spine. 《Interlinear note: This is what is called "spine revival."》Once again clean inside the nose with paper and blow in much sneezing powder to test. If sneezing occurs, they will immediately revive. If even after all this breathing remains difficult, dig the ground deep enough to contain the drowned person's body, soften the soil at the bottom by stirring it up, pile the excavated earth around the four sides of the hole, and place firewood in the hole

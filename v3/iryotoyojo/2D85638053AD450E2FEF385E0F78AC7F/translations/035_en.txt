Fish Glue Ointment (nibe-kō)
　Fish glue 《100 monme》　Litharge 《30 monme》
　First soak the fine powder of litharge in 5 gō of shōchū for about two days, strain out the residue, then add about 1 shō of water to the fish glue,

【Left page】
place over fire and dissolve well. As the water evaporates, add the litharge-infused shōchū until it becomes like starch syrup, then remove from fire and spread on cloth or silk for storage. What is commonly called "transparent glue" boiled in water and spread on paper, sold as "instant-effect paper," is made by this simplified method and can also be used.

Soothing Ointment (kanwa-kō)
　White wax 《60 monme》　Sesame oil 《2 gō, use 1 gō in summer》
　Shave the white wax thinly, heat the sesame oil over fire until warm. When you see the oil surface moving, add the wax, and when dissolved, remove from fire

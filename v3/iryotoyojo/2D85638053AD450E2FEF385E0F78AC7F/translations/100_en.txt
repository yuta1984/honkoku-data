　Warm poultice medicine is difficult to use. In that case, use aloe, myrrh, and borneol {{what pharmacies call "shirode" may also be used}}, three ingredients, 2 monme each, grind each separately into fine powder, soak in 2 go of sake and 5 shaku of strong vinegar, warm slightly and wash with this. It is also good to use this medicine to wash inside wounds. In that case, use it while cold.
　The rest has been recorded in the previous volume, so refer to it for use.

【Left Page】
Diagram of Eight Flower Moxibustion Points
Using straw stems or paper cords, measure the distance from corner to corner of the mouth along the border between the red and white of the lower lip, fold it in half, place the fold at the navel, mark both ends at two places left and right, also place the paper cord vertically with the fold at the navel, mark the upper and lower ends at two places, apply the cord to those upper and lower marks and mark four places left and right. Upper, lower, left and right combined make eight places, this is called Eight Flower moxibustion. Good for severe vomiting and diarrhea in dysentery and cholera, as well as hernia, abdominal masses, and fluid retention. For yin syndrome of typhoid fever suitable for moxibustion, applying moxibustion at these locations often produces good effects.

Continued Torite-gusa (Handbook of Military Medicine)
　　　　　　　　　　Compiled by Tahara Chōzan Koji

　　　　Preface
　Hara Nan'yōshi of Mito Domain previously authored a book called "Torite-gusa" 
　containing emergency treatment methods for military situations. However, regarding
　the treatment of gunshot wounds and gunpowder poisoning, it was not yet possible
　to make it complete without omissions. Therefore, I have
　forgotten my own shallow learning and selected simple and convenient methods from Chinese and Dutch medical texts

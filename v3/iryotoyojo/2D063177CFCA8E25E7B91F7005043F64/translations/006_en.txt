Continued Toride-gusa (Fortress Grass)
　　　　　　　　　Compiled by Tahara Chisan Koji

　　　Preface
Suifu Hara Nan'yōshi previously authored a book called "Toride-gusa" (Fortress Grass), which contained emergency treatment methods for military gates. However, regarding the treatment methods for gunshot wounds, smoke poisoning, and the like, it was not yet possible to avoid omissions. Therefore, forgetting my own obstinate ignorance, I have selected simple and convenient methods from Chinese and Dutch medical texts

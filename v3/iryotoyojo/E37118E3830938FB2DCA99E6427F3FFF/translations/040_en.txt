If eaten with candy/malt syrup, it will not digest and cause illness.

○Jujube　　　Greatly affects the spleen and stomach adversely. Should not be eaten. This is even more so for sick people.
○Pear　　　Both healthy people and sick people suffer damage to the spleen and stomach, which is harmful. It is a mistake to apply the principle that eating a little after heavy drinking makes the mouth feel cool to sick people as well. Not good for fever diseases either.
○Japanese Quince　　　Not good for both healthy people and sick people.
○Apple　　　Not good for various diseases. Sick people experience stomach coldness and loss of appetite. Avoid sugar.
○Persimmon　　　Should not be eaten in large quantities. Astringency-removed persimmons are somewhat acceptable for sick people.
○Quince　　　Ordinary people may use a little. Not good for sick people.
○Pomegranate　　　Same as the above (quince).
○Mandarin Orange　　　People with spleen-stomach deficiency-cold should not use. Should warm by fire and use a little.
○Bitter Orange, Yuzu, Kumquat　　　All three of these may be used a little by ordinary people. For sick people, discretion should be used.
○Buddha's Hand Citron　　　Should not eat much of sugar-preserved Buddha's hand citron or asparagus root. It damages the spleen and stomach and depletes yang qi.
○Loquat　　　Even ordinary people should not eat much.
○Bayberry　　　Not good even for healthy people. Sick people should especially avoid it.
○Walnut　　　Even for sick people it is not poisonous, but if eaten in large quantities, qi becomes blocked.
○Hazelnut　　　Neutral nature and good. Should not use much.
○Horse Chestnut　　　Made into rice cakes, it supplements the spleen and stomach. Good for sick people too.

○Grape　　　Not good for people with illness. Bad for those with weak spleen and stomach and loose stools.
○Sugar　　　There is no difference between brown sugar and rock sugar. Not good for sick people and children.
○Lotus Seeds　　　People prone to diarrhea should not use.
○Lotus Root　　　Damages stomach qi and is not good for sick people.
○Water Caltrop Seeds　　　Poisonous. Should not be eaten.
○Yellow Seeds (Mitsubuki)　　　Same as lotus seeds.
○Lychee　　　Removes pathogenic heat from the heart and clarifies the eyes.
○Longan Flesh　　　If eaten for a long time, supplements various deficiencies. Should not eat much.
○Japanese Torreya Seeds　　　Strengthens the eyes and treats hemorrhoids. Not good for sick people.
○Fig　　　Stops diarrhea. If eaten together with rice cakes, causes tapeworms.

○Japanese Raisin Tree　　　Removes stomach heat and detoxifies alcohol poison.
○Raspberry　　　Opens phlegm and removes thirst. Sick people should use discretion.
○Autumn Olive　　　Good for watery diarrhea. Bad for sick people with no benefit.
○Japanese Pepper　　　Has mild toxicity. Warms the five organs and is good for cold abdominal pain.
○Black Pepper　　　Warm in nature, removes phlegm, and warms cold qi. Avoid eating together with peach, plum, and bayberry.
○Chinquapin Seeds　　　Sick people and children should not eat.
○Tea　　　Both thick and thin powdered tea (matcha) and brewed tea are not good for sick people. Among these, thin sencha with mild properties may be used.
○Tobacco　　　Has toxicity, but people today enjoy it and won't stop even briefly. Though not good, soft varieties may be used.

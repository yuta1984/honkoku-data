○Incidentally, one should continuously apply moxa to the Zusanli (ST36) acupoint. It greatly promotes blood circulation and has the effect of extending life. Most remarkably, recently in Koizumi Village, Toi District, Mikawa Province, a man named Manpei has lived over 220 years and remains healthy to this day. In previous years, when summoned by the authorities and asked "Is there some reason for your exceptional longevity?", he replied: "I do not particularly strive for health maintenance, but following ancestral tradition, I apply moxibustion to the Zusanli point from the 1st to the 8th of each month. There is a specific method for applying it." The method is as follows:

1st day: Left 10, Right 9　2nd day: Left 11, Right 10　3rd day: Left 16, Right 16　4th day: Left 9, Right 10　5th day: Left 10, Right 9　6th day: Left 10, Right 9
7th day: Left 9, Right 10　8th day: Left 8, Right 8

He diligently applies moxa in this manner every month, with no other special practices.
However, since all family members apply this moxibustion, Manpei's wife lived over 200 years, his child over 180 years, and his grandchild over 140 years - all achieving longevity entirely due to this moxibustion method, it is said. This is truly a blessed matter, so I record it here.

○In the three months of autumn, the qi of heaven and earth also contracts, so one should contain one's qi without letting it disperse outward, take care not to use qi carelessly, rise early in the morning to encounter the cool qi, dispel summer's heat qi, and receive cool qi. Especially during what is commonly called "early autumn," one should pay particular attention to food and drink.

○In the three months of winter, yang qi is stored away and water freezes while the earth cracks from frost, so one should understand to avoid using qi, contain the spirit below the navel, and not move one's qi. Most illnesses that people suffer in spring and summer arise from exposure to winter's cold qi. If one does not receive cold qi in winter, falling ill in spring and summer is rare. However, receiving cold qi in winter is entirely due to poor health cultivation in summer. When trying to overcome summer heat by craving cold foods, drinking much water, and seeking cool qi, this clashes with the heat qi and instead causes great sweating. When sweating becomes excessive, the yang qi of the body surface naturally becomes deficient, and one receives cold qi in winter. Generally, during hot weather the interior of the abdomen becomes cold, so warming it is proper health cultivation. However, cooling the abdomen with cold foods causes illness. In winter too, frequently bathing because it is cold, or sitting too close to charcoal fires causes yang qi to leak out, greatly violating the principle of storage and concealment, which is harmful. One should carefully consider and understand all four seasons.

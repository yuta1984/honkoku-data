○Bear　　　Softens muscles and bones, treats numbness. Should be avoided when there is accumulated heat in the body.
○Cattle　　　Warms the lower back and regulates the stomach.
○Wild Boar　　　Regulates the body's interior, effective for epilepsy. Should be avoided when taking medicine.
○Rabbit　　　Harmonizes stomach qi and circulates qi and blood.
○Raccoon Dog　　　Effective for hemorrhoids.
○Otter　　　Good for swelling diseases, removes fatigue fever. In general, all livestock that die naturally facing north, or those that do not close their mouths, have red feet, whose meat does not become hot when boiled, whose meat color does not change when vinegar is applied, whose meat does not get dirty when it falls to the ground, whose meat floats when put in water, whose meat does not cook after one night, that have star-like spots in the meat, or that died from poisoned arrows - all have poison and should not be eaten.

Fruit Section
○Plum (Japanese Plum)　　　Poisonous even to healthy people. Should not be eaten. Avoid sugar; if eaten with honey, it damages the five organs. If eaten with water, it causes violent diarrhea and vomiting. If eaten with sparrow, the poison is severe.
○Ume (Japanese Apricot)　　　No benefit from eating. Pickled ume should be boiled for use by sick people. If eaten with pork, it harms the spleen and stomach.
○Peach fruit　　　Those with poison cause great harm. Those without poison become medicine and extend life.
○Chestnuts　　　If well-boiled and eaten, they somewhat supplement the kidneys. Raw chestnuts harm the spleen and stomach and are poisonous. Not good for sick people. Very poisonous for those with phlegm.

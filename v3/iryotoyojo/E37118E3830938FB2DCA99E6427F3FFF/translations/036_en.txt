○Sushi　　　Whatever kind of sushi it may be, even healthy people and especially weak people should not eat it.

○Raw fish salad (namasu)　　　Weak people should not eat this either. Patients should especially avoid it.

○Dried abalone (noshi)　　　Neutral in nature. Good for various diseases. Fresh abalone is considered even better.

　　△When poisoned by various fish, burn the bones of that fish to charcoal and drink it for relief.

　　　　　Bird Section

○Crane　　　Warm in nature. Supplements yang qi and regulates deficiency. Good for elderly people with deficiency-cold. Those with blood stasis and consumptive cough should avoid this.

○Wild goose　　　For supplementing deficiency, it has much greater benefit than duck.

○Duck　　　Neutral in nature. Those with spleen-stomach deficiency-cold should consider carefully. There are many varieties of duck. All are the same and good for malignant sores.

○Domestic duck　　　Toxic. Patients should avoid it. Eggs are the same.

○Heron　　　Light in nature and non-toxic. Blue heron is slightly superior.

○Swan　　　Toxic. Should not be eaten.

○Grebe　　Although said to be non-toxic, it has minor toxicity. Should not be eaten.

○Chicken　　　Many benefits are listed in herbal texts. Although it supplements deficiency, it is not good for deficiency-cold conditions.

○Chicken egg　　Neutral in nature, stops deficient diarrhea. Should be avoided during smallpox and measles. Duck eggs, soft-shell turtle, or plums eaten together cause harm.

○Pheasant　　Has minor toxicity. Causes various sore toxins to manifest.

○Goose　　　Although it removes organ heat, should not be eaten in large quantities.

○Snipe　　　Regulates the center and has many beneficial functions.

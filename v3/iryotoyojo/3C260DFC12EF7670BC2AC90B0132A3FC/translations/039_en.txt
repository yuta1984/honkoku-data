Preserving [kidney essence] is the first principle of health cultivation. The ancients said, "Taking medicine for a thousand mornings cannot compare to sleeping alone for one night."

1. Desire must not come too early
　According to ancient law, although males reach sexual maturity at sixteen, they must wait until thirty to take a wife; although females begin menstruation at fourteen, they must wait until twenty to marry. In recent times, people marry before even reaching the ages of sixteen and fourteen, so males suffer from depletion and exhaustion of kidney essence, leading to consumptive diseases, while females suffer damage to their blood pathways, leading to leucorrhea disorders.

1. Seminal emission has limits
　According to the ancients' method, a person of twenty years should emit once every four days, one of thirty years should emit once every eight days, one of forty years should emit once every sixteen days, one of fifty years should emit once every twenty days, and those sixty and above should seal their essence and not emit at all. This is the great law. Those who are naturally weak should emit infrequently even in their youth and prime. Those whose physical constitution is vigorous and who eat and drink well should not force themselves to abstain even after sixty.

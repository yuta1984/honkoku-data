If you pluck white hairs from your temples and hair on these days, they will not grow back for a long time.

○In general, you should cut your fingernails on tiger (tora) days and cut your toenails on horse (uma) days.

○Chapter on Food and Drink

1. Moderation in food and drink
Bian Que said: "The foundation of maintaining good health must rely on food. Those who do not understand proper eating are not sufficient to preserve life," and so forth.

○ If one engages in sexual intercourse while heavily intoxicated, the blood vessels become disordered; in men, kidney water is depleted and reduced, while in women, menstruation stagnates and malignant sores develop.

○ If one engages in sexual intercourse while angry and the qi has not yet calmed, boils and carbuncles will develop.

○ If one engages in sexual intercourse while fearful and trembling, spontaneous sweating and night sweats will occur, leading to consumptive disease.

○ If one engages in sexual intercourse after long travel and exhaustion, the body becomes emaciated and fatigued, leading to consumptive disease.

○ If one engages in sexual intercourse while holding back urination, gonorrhea will develop.

○ If one engages in sexual intercourse by lamplight, it damages one's life.

○ If one sweats during sexual intercourse and is exposed to wind, it becomes internal wind.

○ If one takes medicine containing dragon bone or musk and engages in sexual intercourse, the true qi becomes depleted and dispersed.

○ If one takes cinnabar and mineral remedies for penile impotence and engages in sexual intercourse, the kidney water becomes depleted, leading to wasting-thirst and gonorrhea.

○ If one eats large amounts of garlic and engages in sexual intercourse, it injures the liver qi and causes eye disorders.

○ If a person with eye disease engages in sexual intercourse, it becomes internal cataract.

○ If one engages in sexual intercourse while epidemic disease has not yet fully healed, the tongue protrudes and one dies.

○ If one engages in sexual intercourse while sword wounds have not yet fully healed, the blood becomes disordered, the wound opening ruptures and bleeding cannot be stopped, or tetanus develops, leading to opisthotonus (backward arching).

○ If one engages in sexual intercourse while a woman's menstruation has not yet ceased, both men and women develop yellow coloration throughout the body and become emaciated, or develop white patches on the skin.

1. There are times when desire should be avoided

○ Great cold, great heat, great wind, great rain, great fog, thunder and lightning, rainbow, earthquake, solar eclipse, lunar eclipse - during these times, sexual intercourse should not be performed.

○ Sexual intercourse should not be performed under the sun, moon, and stars, near shrines and temples of gods and Buddhas, beside stoves and latrines, or near tombs and graves. Those who violate this damage the human spirit and shorten their lifespan. If conception occurs, the child will be unbenevolent and unfilial with many illnesses.

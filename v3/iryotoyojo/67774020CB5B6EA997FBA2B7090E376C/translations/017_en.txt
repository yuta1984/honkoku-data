1. In a certain mountain temple in Yamashiro, there is a medicine called Chōmei-gan (Longevity Pills). If taken to other provinces and villages and consumed, it shows miraculous effects. However, it doesn't work on people from villages around that temple. The medicine becomes familiar with the stomachs of local people and doesn't work immediately.

1. With all kinds of first seasonal foods, one should eat a little at first, then gradually increase the amount to avoid harm. Whether pears or persimmons, because one eats them for the first time in the current year after they ended the previous year, they cause problems due to the stomach being unaccustomed to them. The effectiveness of poison, medicine, and moxibustion

【Left Page】
all work on the same principle - only with distance do poison and medicine show clear effects. It's the same with people - if kept apart for a long time, they maintain reserve toward each other. As the saying goes, "If you haven't seen someone for three days, don't treat them as you did before."

1. When facing various food flavors arranged at a meal, it's good to first eat grilled or boiled dishes - soft, warm foods - then proceed to sashimi, vinegared dishes, and such. Sour foods cause the intestines to contract and not expand, and where they don't expand, qi necessarily accumulates and creates pain. Eating warm, soft foods first to prepare the stomach to receive food, then eating sour foods afterward serves to settle the stomach, which is good. Sour foods have the principle of contracting - they have an astringent, contracting flavor. Excess is also bad.

Five【enclosed in square】About bōrō (hemorrhage) 【heavy bleeding】
Unexpected heavy bleeding is called bōrō. When blood descends beyond control, it is called bō (collapse), and when it seeps down continuously without stopping, it is called rō (leakage). These are not easy conditions at all. Considering the causes of onset, they are all due to poor recuperation. When not many days have passed after childbirth, getting up early and exhausting the body, damaging the spirit, or arranging the hair, taking hip baths and being exposed to wind, or without letting days pass, carelessly engaging in sexual intercourse and destroying the vital essence - finally this condition appears

Fourteen: On Avoiding Foods, and also
　　　　Things to be seen and heard should all have prohibitions
For pregnant women, foods they are accustomed to eating need not be particularly avoided, but out-of-season foods and unusual or strange-tasting foods should be avoided. Octopus, squid, mackerel, gizzard shad and similar fish, especially oily foods should be avoided. All spicy foods, water pepper, black pepper, mustard, and others, as well as scallions, garlic chives, and garlic should be avoided. Not only do these harm the mother's body, but they also leave various troubles for the child to be born.

○Taking excessive care in raising a newborn child, always nurturing only within curtained enclosures, or having people take turns holding the baby without ever putting it down, actually impedes the circulation of qi and blood, causing the skin to become thin and generating illness. Therefore, the "Qian Jin Fang" states that on warm days, the child should be carried outside to be exposed to wind and to see the sun.

Eleven【enclosed in square】On postpartum miscellaneous disorders
After childbirth, epidemic diseases and other skin conditions and swellings should never be thought of as the same
as in ordinary people. Even if the disease appears mild, one should pay attention
and provide care. The typical postpartum diseases were recorded in the previous section, but the omissions
are presented here. There are cases where the vulva opens and does not close. There are cases where the vulva protrudes. There are cases where the uterus
comes out and does not retract. All of these occur when during childbirth, the pushing effort is too strong

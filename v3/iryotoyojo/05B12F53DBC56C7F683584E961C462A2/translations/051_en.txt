　　　Guidelines for eating after childbirth
When recommending food during childbirth, one should avoid eating to satiety. Avoid rice cakes, sticky rice dumplings, and similar foods that are difficult to digest. Naturally, when labor pains are strong, forcing food will instead cause nausea and vomiting.

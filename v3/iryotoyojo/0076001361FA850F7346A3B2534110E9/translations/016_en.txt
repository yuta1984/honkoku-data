Boufuu (Siler root)
　Same as hamaboufuu (Glehnia littoralis).

Hodawara (type of seaweed)　　Horse-tail algae
　Sweet, salty, smooth, and cold in nature. Non-toxic. Eliminates alcohol poisoning.

Dried persimmon　　hoshigaki
　Sweet and cold in nature. Non-toxic. Eliminates stagnant blood and treats bleeding from below. ▲Incompatible with crab.

Gray mullet　　bora
　Sweet and neutral in nature. Non-toxic. Opens the stomach and benefits the five organs. However, the flavor is thick and the nature is heavy. Not good for patients.

【Left page】
Red gurnard　　houbou
　Sweet and neutral in nature. Non-toxic.

Sea pineapple　　hoya
　Salty and cold in nature. Non-toxic. Promotes urination and treats women's vaginal discharge.

Dried abalone　　hoshiawabi
　Same as kushikai (scallop).

Cuckoo　　hototogisu
　Sweet and cold in nature. Non-toxic. Removes heat toxin from smallpox and kills parasites.

Meadow bunting　　houjiro
　Sweet and neutral in nature. Non-toxic.

Kurumi (walnut)　　胡桃

Kuwanomi (mulberry fruit)　　桑椹
　Sweet, slightly sour, and neutral. Non-toxic. Benefits the joints, stops numbness and pain, neutralizes alcohol toxicity, and reduces edema

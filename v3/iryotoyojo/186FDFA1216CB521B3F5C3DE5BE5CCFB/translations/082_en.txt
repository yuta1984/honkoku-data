【Right Page】
Dogs and birds and such creatures that race across the eternal heavens have only the hearts for male and female to mate, and do not know the way of loving boys mutually. When I consider this, those who abandon the elegant emotions of today and criticize it as wrong or heretical have human faces but bestial hearts - what can be done about this? How could humans be inferior to birds?
In the twelfth month of Meiwa 5 (1768), on a night when feet reach toward the sky, welcoming spring at lodgings in Hama-chō, Ōkura Senbun records this.

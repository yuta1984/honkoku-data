*Shokukan Sengen* (Food Reference Annotations)

　　**Category: The Five Flavors (Beverages)**

**Rōsetsu-sui (Midwinter Snow Water)**　This is the snow of Daikan (the "Greater Cold," the last of the 24 solar terms). Spring snow spoils easily and cannot be stored. One should place the snow in a ceramic jar, bury it in a shaded spot in the back garden, and open it as needed when spring or summer of the following year arrives.
● Its nature and taste are cold, and it is non-toxic. ● It may be used to brew tea; when used to wash clothing, it removes all manner of stains and grime, leaving things as if new. When various items are soaked in it, insects will not infest them; when mochi (rice cakes), vegetables, mushrooms, fish, or fowl are soaked in it, they will not spoil; and when used for bathing, the body and hair (become clean and refreshed…)

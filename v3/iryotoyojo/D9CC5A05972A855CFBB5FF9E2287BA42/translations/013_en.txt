How can one say they are not involved in this? Also, saying that medicine is a lowly profession is comparing it to the work of kings and great men and calling it base, but even if a person is born into humble circumstances, sometimes they associate with kings and great men, yet they do not consider this an honor, and even when they associate with outcasts and beggars, they do not consider it shameful - this is the nature of doctors. For this reason, in the current era, doctors are placed outside the class system. It should be called a most noble path. Therefore in China they are called "officials who govern life." Now, assisting the work of divine creation should make the treatment and care of children to help them grow the primary principle. For this reason,

Sugawara no Minetsugu's Kinranpō also places pediatric treatments first.

1. Purging the filth from within the abdomen when a child is first born should be done for seven to ten days, or even twenty to thirty days, until the foul poison is completely eliminated. The medicinal methods should follow the strength or weakness of the child and the depth of the poison. It is best to delay giving breast milk as much as possible. Generally, after four or five days is appropriate. If breast milk is given too early, the meconium will not come out, the foul poison will remain and become a future harm. This number of days should also follow the strength or weakness of the child.

You must first sleep deeply and awaken once. Before you have yet fallen asleep and before closing your eyes, stretch both legs long, step firmly together, and fill the vital energy of your entire body into the area between the navel wheel, qi-sea, elixir field, lower back and legs, and the center of your feet, performing this contemplation from time to time. "This qi-sea, elixir field, lower back and legs, and center of my feet—all of this is my original face. What nostrils could there be? This qi-sea and elixir field of mine—all of this is my fundamental homeland. What news could there be? This qi-sea and elixir field of mine—all of this

Repeat this over and over, always engaging in such visualization. When the merit of this visualization accumulates, the vital energy of your entire body will eventually become sufficient in the area between your lower back, legs, and the center of your feet, and the area below your navel will swell like a gourd, resembling a ball that has never been struck with bamboo.

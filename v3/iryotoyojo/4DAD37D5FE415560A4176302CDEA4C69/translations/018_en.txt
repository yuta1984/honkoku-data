To places already familiar and memorized, you attach connections with the current titles (chapter names of the Tale of Genji),
creating mental associations and entrusting each to its respective location.
Therefore, naturally the sequence of one, two, three will not be disturbed, even if reversed
or if taken every other one, the memory operates
freely and at will.

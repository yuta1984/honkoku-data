【Main Text】
Among the five parts of the human body, the nose, mouth, ears, and eyes are most susceptible to illness. Among these, the eyes are particularly important. They work constantly from morning when we wake up until night when we sleep, without any rest. When overused, they contract wind-eye disease or external fire ailments. Therefore, we must occasionally rest our eyes and show them interesting things. As an example of not allowing this rest before New Year's, there's the saying "give the eyes a New Year's holiday," where only the eyes are covered with a hood. At year's end, people peek at bean shows from Daishi to Yamashita, women stop to watch the lion dances with Kumagai dolls, and apprentices and children each

Food Efficacy and Toxicity: Record of Freedom from Disease and Life Extension

Indeed, the beginning of all things, regardless of size or length, whatever they may be, arise from small places and develop according to their respective degrees. Already in Japan too, the Supreme Ultimate arose, making three with heaven and earth, yin and yang mingled to establish five, the natural way produced seven, later the benefit of the living mind originated nine, combining the first and second to make eleven, incorporating many and few to create thirteen, the noble and base contemplated fifteen, high and low provided seventeen, long and short revealed nineteen, empty and real divided twenty-one, movement and change observed twenty-three, strong and weak considered twenty-five, fortune and hardship prayed for twenty-seven, being and non-being perceived twenty-nine, taking the beginning after death as thirty-one.

That is to say: beginning-middle-end transformation, first day and last day of the month, thirtieth day and first day, earth-water-fire-wind, Shinto-Confucianism-Buddhism-Taoism, indeed benevolence-righteousness-propriety-wisdom-trust, wood-fire-earth-metal-water - how could these be separated from the Five Elements? Already at the beginning of the people of India: Bhagavat Vairocana Buddha; at the beginning of the people of China: Pangu and Fuxi; at the beginning of the people of Japan: Kunitokotachi-no-mikoto, seven generations of heavenly gods and five generations of earthly gods. Although the chronologies of each are long, toward the end the people were not at peace, governing confusion. Because of this, Amaterasu Ōmikami established the great law. Thereafter the human emperors began, and Emperor Jinmu established the law.

　　○Hiccups
Hiccups that occur after childbirth, trauma, or serious illness indicate the exhaustion of stomach qi. Those that occur regularly are due to cold in the stomach. Each case varies in severity. Light cases can be stopped by redirecting the qi.

【Left Page】
If you say something unreasonable to a person with hiccups to make them angry, the hiccups will stop. The "Lingshu" also contains similar magical methods. The current folk practice of applying straw chaff to the forehead with saliva when the soles of the feet become numb also works on this principle by redirecting qi upward.

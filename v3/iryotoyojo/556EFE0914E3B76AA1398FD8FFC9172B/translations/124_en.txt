　　○Talismans for Childbirth
Since talismans are favored in households with illness, though I do not use them myself, I record them here as they cause no harm.
[The script resembling talisman characters is omitted]

【Left Page】
　　　　For childbirth to be easy and peaceful,
　　　　write with vermillion and paste it on the north
　　　　wall of the birthing woman's room.
　　　　This too, when childbirth is difficult, write with
　　　　cinnabar and paste it above the pillow.
　　　　When the child is positioned sideways and cannot be born easily,
　　　　write with cinnabar and have her drink it down
　　　　with plain hot water.
[The script of the head and left-side talismans is omitted]

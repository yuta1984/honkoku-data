《Third Month Season》
3rd day: Clear and cold from morning. Light north wind blowing. 《46 degrees》

4th day: North wind blowing from before the 7th hour of dawn, clear weather, strong wind until evening. 《48 degrees》

5th day: Clear and cold from morning. North wind from after the 9th hour, stopped in the evening, cloudy at night.

6th day: Black clouds in the east in the morning. From after the 5th hour 《peach blossoms in full bloom, sardines》, lightly cloudy with south wind bringing warm air. From the 4th and half hour 《sardine lees about 14 kanme, for 1 gold》until evening, strong south wind. After the 7th hour, light rain 《ryō, about 8 bu per unit》. From the 5th hour at night until after the 4th hour, rain and wind. 《Iso, minato a little, fishing activities》《Minato about 13 kanme for 4 bu per bale, iso about 12 kanme for 5 bu per bale, some merchant trading》

7th day: Rain from evening, warm air. Rain stopped at the 5th hour, cloudy all day.

8th day: Rain at dawn. Clear from the 6th and half hour. From after the 9th and half hour, suddenly west wind with heavy wind and rain, evening showers also...

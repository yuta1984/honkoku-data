September

1st day: North wind from morning with rain, heavy rain until after the 4th hour. The former Chūnagon departed for Edo this evening. The wind continued until evening with intermittent rain. At night the wind stopped with occasional rain.

2nd day: Cloudy from morning, overcast all day with occasional rain

3rd day: Morning scattered clouds became clear skies. Hot.

4th day: Cloudy from morning, light rain at the 5th hour. Today around the 7th hour, arrival at the castle, with all retainers going out to the town in hemp kamishimo to greet as per precedent. Heavy rain from the 8th hour for about one hour, then light rain from the 7th hour, stopping at half past the 7th hour.

5th day: Clear from morning, good weather all day. Cloudy at night. Earthquake at 8 PM.

6th day: Cloudy from morning, lightly overcast all day and humid. Thin sunshine in the evening. 67 degrees.

Today people suddenly came from various places to buy coins, and though the reason was unclear, it appears the government has issued new 100-mon coins. One ryō equals 4 kan-mon in coins, while plain white-edged coins are 5 kan-mon. The current coin exchange rate is 6 kan 800 mon for purchase.

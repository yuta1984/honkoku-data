In any location where one encounters foreigners, if they request written documents or reporting procedures, or if they offer gifts or goods, one must firmly decline. If there is an unavoidable circumstance requiring acceptance, one must bring said items and report the detailed circumstances promptly to the proper authorities. If one conceals this and it is discovered later, one will be punished after investigation.

This directive shall be proclaimed without exception to imperial domains, private domains, and temple/shrine lands alike. Moreover, the posted signs at various harbors that were erected by imperial decree in the 13th year of Tenpō (Year of the Tiger) shall be removed.

14th day: Clear weather from morning, hot with no wind. At half past the eighth hour of morning, strong earthquake. Extremely hot all day, hot until midnight.

15th day: Clear weather from morning, extremely hot weather. At the eighth hour, south wind blew. Stopped after the seventh hour passed, then became slightly cloudy with some thunder sounds from the north. At the fifth hour of night, rain fell but stopped immediately, becoming clear.

16th day: Morning with scattered clouds, light rain at the fifth hour, stopped immediately. Clear and hot weather from the fourth hour. Light wind from around the eighth hour. Scattered rain at the seventh hour, stopped immediately. Scattered clouds and strong heat even at night.

In this area, there has been no rain for some time, and despite the extreme heat, the weather conditions are extremely good.

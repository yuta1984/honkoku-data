13th day: Frost fell, clear weather.
　　　(41 degrees)
14th day: Clear from morning. Strong wind during the hours of the Cock and Dog (around 6-9 PM) after nightfall, stopped at dawn.
15th day: Heavy frost, cold and clear.
16th day: Cloudy in the morning, rain from around 7 o'clock, strong rain and wind during the night, heavy wind and rain until dawn.
　　　(Between the 21st and 25th of next month)
　　　(Notice received that there will be a bird hunt)
17th day: Rain from evening, cold with northern wind pattern, rain all day. Rain stopped after nightfall, but wind remained strong.
19th day: Heavy rain, then clear.
20th day: Clear from morning. Strong wind during the night.

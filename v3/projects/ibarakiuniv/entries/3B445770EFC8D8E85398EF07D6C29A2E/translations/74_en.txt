[The boat warehouses] were destroyed, and most of the temporary buildings and brothels at the same location were also destroyed. Matsui-machi suffered the same fate. Near Honjo Warishita-mizu, water flooded out like a great river. Many bannermen's estates were destroyed. Around Kameido, Yanagishima, and Oshiage, many farmers' houses and temple buildings were destroyed. It is said they also suffered hardship from flooding.

In the north-northeast direction, Koami-machi and Sakai-machi areas suffered some damage. Ryōgoku Bridge was safe. All the show stalls were blown down and destroyed. Okura-mae was safe. Most of the temporary buildings in Namiki Chaya-machi had their roofs blown off and destroyed. The Sensōji temple grounds suffered some damage, with many large trees fallen. At Saruwaka-machi theaters, similarly all houses had their roofs completely blown off and destroyed. The temporary buildings at Hanakawado and Yamano-yadori suffered the same fate. Along the riverside at Mitani and Imado areas, many houses were destroyed. Water flooded Tamachi Dote-shita street. Around Shin-Yoshiwara and Kyō-machi, some areas caught fire. About half a chō in front of Daion-ji temple burned. Around Shitaya Kanasugi, Minowa, and Sakamoto, most destroyed houses were at Iriya. Near Kōtoku-ji temple and Shamisen-bori, there was some damage. Around Asakusa Kappa-gawa-chō, there was severe damage with many collapsed houses.

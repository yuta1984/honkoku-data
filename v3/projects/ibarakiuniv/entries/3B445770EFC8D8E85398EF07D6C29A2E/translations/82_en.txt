18th day: Cloudy and cold from morning, occasionally sunny, light rain falls intermittently from around 4 o'clock, same continues through the night
(For whatever reason, insects have attacked the rapeseed, so they are fixing the fields and sowing wheat and barley)

19th day: Light rain from evening, clear and cold from 9 o'clock, warm from evening

20th day: Cloudy from morning with showery weather, frequent rain all day from 5:30, heavy rain through the night with southerly conditions and warm air
(This year insects have completely attacked the rapeseed, so they are re-sowing with wheat)

21st day: Very cloudy and warm from morning, clear from after 4 o'clock, west wind from evening
(This year the miscanthus did not produce ears, said to be bad for next year's crops)

22nd day: Cloudy and cold in morning, overcast all day, very cloudy through the night

23rd day: Very cloudy from morning, west wind blows and clear weather from 5 o'clock at night

24th day: Clear from morning
(35 degrees in morning)

25th day: Scattered clouds descending in morning, cloudy from 5 o'clock, clear from after 4 o'clock
(35 degrees in morning)

26th day: Cloudy and warm from morning, rain from after 8 o'clock, heavy rain at night

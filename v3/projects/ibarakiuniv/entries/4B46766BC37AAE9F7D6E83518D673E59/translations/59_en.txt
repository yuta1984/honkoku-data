4th day　Morning cloudy and cool, occasional light rain from past 8 o'clock
《Morning 65 degrees｜Noon 68 degrees》　Thundery weather, clear at night 4 o'clock
　　　　　　　　　　　　　Copied from Ōba
　His Excellency the Former Chūnagon has been submitting petitions for exemption regarding coastal defense duties and such matters, and on the 22nd day he ascended to the castle,
　an audience was granted, and accordingly on the 23rd he ascended to the castle via Hirakawa gate,
　where in the audience chamber
　he was granted an audience,
　His private petition was deemed to have reasonable grounds, and regarding coastal defense and
　military system reforms,
　exemption was granted,
　with gracious imperial favor
　he personally received a sword and short sword as gifts, and in the waiting room
　the Rōjū's messenger also
　conveyed gracious imperial favor, and sake, soup, sweets, and tea were served
　This matter is acknowledged and should be communicated to all administrators

On the night of the 22nd of last month, all three theaters in Saruwaka-chō were completely destroyed by fire. In the vicinity near Umamichi, a great fire burned an area of 5-6 chō square. The sumo wrestling at Ekōin corner was supposed to begin yesterday, but it was difficult due to rainy weather.
April 29th

On the night of the 4th, [someone] went to Iwaman, wearing a sword and hood, requesting to borrow 2 tan of Kokura hakama and 3 tan from Yūki Island. When refused because such business would be problematic, he drew his sword, so everyone fled and he took the goods. Three people also went to Tanoya Jūkichi in Izumi-chō, with one wearing a hood standing outside, and they fled with goods worth 3-4 ryō. At Furuuchiya in Izumi-chō as well, three people came and fled with goods worth about 2 ryō.

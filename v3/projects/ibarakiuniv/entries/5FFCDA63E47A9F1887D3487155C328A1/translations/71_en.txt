　　Notice from Osaka
Regarding His Excellency the Shōgun, the day before yesterday on the 1st, on an auspicious day, he
relocated to Nijō Castle, and on the same day
was appointed to the position of Naidaijin, receiving
imperial appointment to the Sophora Gate
Imperial edict　Quasi-empress envoy
The formal audience procedures were completed successfully in all respects, his mood was excellent, truly
a most auspicious occasion beyond compare, therefore from the same day
he shall be called His Excellency the Shōgun

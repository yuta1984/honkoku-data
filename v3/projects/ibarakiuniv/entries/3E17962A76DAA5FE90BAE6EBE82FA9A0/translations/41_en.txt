Water pushed up as far as three rice fields ahead. Water flooded the entire area around Hachiman-shita, Yamashita, Nemoto, and Tsuneba riverbank. From the Nakakawachi area through Akutsu-watari, Iitomi, and Iwane areas there was great flooding. From Kanazawa slope down it was like an ocean with flood waters over three jō high. Many houses were washed away, and countless pieces of lumber flowed downstream. Water is said to have pushed up to the Akanuma Kanejaku-te area. Around 8th district and Akanuma area, people travel by boat. There were reportedly quite a few drowning victims.

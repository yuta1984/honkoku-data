The fire brigade extinguished it, leaving fourteen or fifteen houses at the entrance outside Kanda Bridge gate.
At Kondō-sama's mansion, only the practice fence burned; at Asano Ikkaku-sama's place, only the enclosure burned.
Kaneda-sama's and Wakabayashi-sama's [properties] were extinguished by Ogasa Sakyōdayū-sama's personnel.
Ishikawa-sama's, Suzuki-sama's, and Ta-sama's mansions were extinguished by Matsudaira Buzen-no-kami-sama's personnel. At Honda-sama's place, the enclosure burned a little, and at Okada-sama's the front tenement houses remained. Nekoya Shindō and Kamakura riverbank burned, while the shops along the moat edge remained. South of Mondo riverbank and the lumber wharf were Jikken-dana, Honchō street, Ōdenma-machi street, Hatagoya-machi; Daimaru remained together with the tailor shop across from it. Daimon street, Tadokoro-machi, and Hasegawa-machi remained. Tomizawa-machi, Norimono-machi, and Izumi-machi burned, stopped at the dyer's shop. On the other side, the western half of Sakai-machi burned, Fukiya-machi burned completely, Koami-machi 1st district burned, Kobune-machi, Ise-machi, and Honbune-machi burned, Nihonbashi bridge burned and fell. Tōri 1chōme's west side remained, Nishi-gashi-machi remained, half of Gofuku-machi burned, and [the fire] was extinguished at the corner lot Shinban, at a money exchange shop called Koike. From there, eastward, Aomono-machi street and Shinba - [shop name] remained, and about ten houses remained toward Iketaiya drug store. From here [the fire] burned into Shinemon-machi Sujichigai, and this fire stopped at Iwakura-machi. At this time a whirlwind blew and [the fire] spread westward, with the 1st and 2nd [fire brigades] working around upper and lower Maki-machi, south Misaya-machi, Oke-machi, Nushishi-machi, Suzuki-machi, Minami-Daiku-machi, Kachi-machi 1st and 2nd districts, around Gorōbē-machi, Tatami-machi, Daikon riverbank, Kita-Kōnya, Kyōbashi vicinity.

October

1st day (Chrysanthemum flowers in full bloom at this time): Clear from morning with cold air, good weather all day.
《Marginal note: 43 degrees》

2nd day: Cloudy from morning, cloudy sky all day with a shower at half past the seventh hour, stopped immediately. Earthquake in the evening.
《Marginal note: 48 degrees》

3rd day: Clear from morning, good weather all day, warm air.

4th day: Clear and warm from morning, warm air all day.
《Marginal note: 47 degrees》

5th day: Clear and warm from morning.
《Marginal note: New rice 3 to 2-3 sho》

6th day: Clear from morning, cloudy from evening, rain at dawn.
《Marginal note: 43 degrees》

7th day: Rain from morning, clear from the fifth hour (around 8 AM), good weather all day.

8th day: Clear from morning with frost.
《Marginal note: 39 degrees》

9th day (Genii): Morning mist rises, clear and entering warm weather. Gentle major earthquake past the eighth hour (past 2 AM).
《Marginal note: 43 degrees》

10th day: Cloudy from morning, occasionally sunny and warm, clear at midnight.

11th day: Clear morning, showery sky from past the fifth hour (past 8 AM), half past fifth hour.

Question: Regarding the sutra passage in the lecture "completely observing all body, speech, and mind,"
　　can it be said that the three mysteries (body, speech, mind) when complete lead to buddhahood?

　　if so, can it be said that there are shallow and deep levels in the elimination of delusions by the two vehicles?

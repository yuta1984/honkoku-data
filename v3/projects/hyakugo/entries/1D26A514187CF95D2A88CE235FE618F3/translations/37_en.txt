Eiroku 10 (1567)
Assembly Lecture, April 4th

Lecturer: Kōshin (chanting)
Questioners: Sōshō
　Sōshū (flower scattering), Esuke, Jōzō, Kōshō
　　　　　　　Shō, Gōju

Question: Regarding the clarification of the characteristics of the interpretive essence in the Kōsan Sutra, can we ask about the essence of the principles that both esoteric and exoteric [teachings] ultimately address?

Question: Can we say that those who copy the title "Mahāyāna" in this treatise do not enter the universal gate [dharma]?

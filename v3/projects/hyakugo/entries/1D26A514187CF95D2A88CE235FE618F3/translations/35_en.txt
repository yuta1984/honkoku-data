Question: Regarding the lecture and praise of the scriptural text "Prajñāpāramitā," to which of the two teachings—exoteric or esoteric Buddhism—should the Heart Sutra be said to belong?

Question: In the current interpretation, concerning the clarification of the aspects of afflictions, can it be said that one can achieve enlightenment in this very body without completely severing afflictions?

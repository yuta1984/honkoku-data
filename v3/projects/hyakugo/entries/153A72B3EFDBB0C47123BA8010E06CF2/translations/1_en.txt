Prohibition Order - Regarding Toji Temple and Its Precinct

1. Military forces committing violence and lawless acts against common people
2. Cutting and taking bamboo and trees, or harvesting fields
3. Forcibly collecting arrow money (military tax) and provisions

The above articles are strictly prohibited. If there are any violators, they shall be swiftly subjected to severe punishment, as ordered from above.

Tenmon 15th year, 9th month, 15th day (September 15, 1546)
Hyobu-no-jo Miyoshi (with personal seal)

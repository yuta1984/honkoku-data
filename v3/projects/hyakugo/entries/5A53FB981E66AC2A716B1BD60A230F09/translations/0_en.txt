Regarding the matter of the deputy administrator (godaikan) for the 120 kan of public duties for Niimi Manor, we have reached an agreement. We request that 10 kan mon be submitted annually from the collected amount. The remaining 110 kan should be administered without negligence. We humbly submit this request.

Entoku 3rd year (1491)
Eleventh month, first day
Zōshū (Increased Excellence)
Zōyū (Increased Protection)

To Lord Senō Tarō Saemon-no-jō

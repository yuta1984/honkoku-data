Regarding the annual rice tax (nengu) of Nishi-go Omura-gushi in Murakushi-sho, Totomi Province, under the jurisdiction of Saisho-ko-in Temple:

I hereby confirm receipt of the complete payment for the three-year period from the first year of Gentoku to the third year of the same era. However, there remains an outstanding amount of one koku, four to, nine sho, and two go, which shall be paid in full. Furthermore, any receipts issued prior to today, even if they exist, shall be considered void, and upon discovery, shall be returned.

This document is issued accordingly.

The 15th day of the 9th month, 1st year of Shokyo
Jito-dai (Deputy Land Steward) Goen (with seal)

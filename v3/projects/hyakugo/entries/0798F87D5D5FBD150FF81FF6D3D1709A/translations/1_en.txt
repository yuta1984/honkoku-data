Regarding the shrine service position at Toji Hachiman Shrine
Article 1: I declare that I shall not engage in disorderly conduct toward my superiors
Article 1: I shall not engage in fighting or disputes
I shall not keep any tea service equipment in the shrine service quarters from the tea house <in front of the South Gate> charging one mon per serving, nor shall I cause any trouble for the tea house
Article 1: Even when off duty, I shall respond to summons and devote myself to service at the shrine
When on duty, I shall not employ anyone other than my fellow workers as a substitute
Anyone who violates these articles and goes against the intent of this pledge shall be immediately dismissed from their position. Therefore, this pledge document is as stated above.
Tenbun 3rd year, 11th month, 12th day 〈Yoshiro〉 Yoshihisa (abbreviated seal)

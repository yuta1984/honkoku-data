Request Declaration
　　Regarding the guarantor for the deputy administrator position of the monks and scholar-monks for the Yano Estate regular holdings in Harima Province, territory of Tōji Temple

Concerning the aforementioned deputy administrator Senkobo Ensai, if there are any issues such as non-payment of the annual tribute of fifty kan-mon, I, as guarantor, shall settle all payments in accordance with the intent of this request document. Should payment become difficult, I shall accept strict demands for payment. Furthermore, if payment is still not made, seizure and confiscation shall be carried out, and I shall pay the tribute from ordinary myōden fields and various official positions. At that time, I shall raise no objections whatsoever. Therefore, this request document is as stated.

　　Bunmei 13th year (Kanotono-Ushi) 8th month, 4th day　Jōkei-in

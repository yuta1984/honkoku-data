(2) Hungry Lions (Comedy Film) 2 Reels
　　　　　　　　　　――Fox Films
　　―― Cast ――
Ham Bohn…………Lloyd Hamilton
Bella Kosher……Mildred Lee
A Nipple…………Jimmy Adams
Bella's Father………………Tom Wilson
Lions………………………Ethel and Gene

(Synopsis)…Ham Bohn, wanting to avoid military service, tries to marry a girl named Bella but fails. Through an unexpected turn of events, he ends up jumping into the draft examination hall himself and is about to be forcibly enlisted. However, whether fortunately or unfortunately, an automobile crashes into the physical examination hall, injuring him and leading to his being carried to a nearby hospital. A monkey that had been doused with cold water after setting off the hospital alarm releases lions from their cage in revenge, so the hungry lions rampage through the hospital causing great chaos, and Ham, who was disliked by women, finds himself liked by the lions, much to his distress.
　　Narrator……Hosoyama Mugan

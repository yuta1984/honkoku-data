Holland is located in the lands of the North Sea of Europe, neighboring Germany to the west and bordering France to the north. It is a very cold country, a small nation spanning only three degrees from north to south. It is positioned to the northwest of Japan and China, and is 12,000 to 13,000 ri distant from Japan across the sea. Its latitude from the North Pole is 54 or 55 degrees, or perhaps 56 degrees.

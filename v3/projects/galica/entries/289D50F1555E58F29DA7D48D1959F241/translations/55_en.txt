Amacao (Amakou), Goa (Go-a), Portugal (Porutogaru). All three of these countries are said to be territories of Christian nations, and their people and customs are similar to each other. Amacao is located in the South China Sea. Goa is situated in the southern part of Tenjiku (India) and is a warm country. Portugal is located far to the west within Europe and is a country with four seasons.

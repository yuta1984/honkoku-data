Korea is the land of the ancient Three Hans (Mahan, Jinhan, and Byeonhan). In medieval times, it was divided into Silla, Baekje, and Goryeo, and in later periods it was unified and came to be called Joseon. The country is divided into eight provinces and is a cold nation. Gyeonggi Province is located thirty-eight degrees south of the North Pole, and Busan Port is at thirty-six degrees.

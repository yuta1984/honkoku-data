The Copper Plate replied, "That book contains wondrous and miraculous powers too numerous to count. These are not difficult to understand if one reads it thoroughly. Let me give you just one example: if Your Majesty personally opens the book and reads the third line on the back of the sixth leaf, then when Your Majesty asks a question as a test, my head, separated from my body, will answer each one."

When she finished chanting, the water immediately began to boil. The sorceress empress immediately went to the young king's chamber and, while sprinkling the water on him, chanted: "If the Creator has punished you and made you into this form, then let this form not change. But if I have transformed you into this form through my sorcery, then receive again your heaven-given form and return to your original shape." No sooner had she finished speaking than the young king suddenly returned to his original form, stood up, and gave thanks to God for this grace. Then the sorceress empress said to the king: "Quickly leave this palace and go away, never to return again. If you come back again, I will never forgive you at that time. Make haste!"

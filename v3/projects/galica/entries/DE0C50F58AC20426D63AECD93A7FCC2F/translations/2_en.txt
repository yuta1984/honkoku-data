　　　The Tale of the Fisherman
Long ago, there was an old man who made his living by fishing. His nature
was fundamentally honest, and he was also a diligent worker, so he never
was idle for even a single day. However, the profit he gained was meager, and his family was
large - he could barely support one wife and three children. His house was very poor,
and he could only just manage to sustain his precarious life from day to day.
Though he lived in such poverty, he prayed to God

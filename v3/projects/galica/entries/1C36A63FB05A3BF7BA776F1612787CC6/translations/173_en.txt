According to an examination of the ritual manuals, the deity wears a hat on his head, holds a bag in his left hand, and makes a hammer mudra with his right hand. Since Makatsu holds a hammer and Kikatsu holds a bag, this combines these two demons. He is said to be placed on a lotus leaf.

○ Regarding Daikokuten, it is detailed in the "Nankai-ki-ki-den" and "Busso Tsūsai," and in the "Shin'yaku Nin'ō-kyō" it states "Mahākāla Daikokuten is enshrined in the kitchen," and in the "Seiryū-so" it appears in a certain text that "Daikokuten is a god of battle."

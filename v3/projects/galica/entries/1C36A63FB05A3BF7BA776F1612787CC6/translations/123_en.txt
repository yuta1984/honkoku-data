Someone says: "To say both 'Imperial Empress' and 'Great Lady' is redundant language that furthermore mixes noble and base ranks. To say both 'Great Dharma Master' and 'nun' confuses male and female, which is deplorable." However, this must have been the genuine practice of that time.

【Grave Marker】
　【Sanskrit character】Tenpyō Ōshin Ninshō Imperial Empress First Rank Great Lady Kōmyō Honored Spirit

This should be taken seriously. If it were a forgery, would one not notice such errors?

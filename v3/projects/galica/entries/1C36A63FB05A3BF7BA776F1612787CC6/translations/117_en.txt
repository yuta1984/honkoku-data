Ukisu Sengen Shrine is located in a place called Sengensaki in Higashi-Komatsukawa Village. The shrine territory is six koku (recorded as being in Musashi Province on October 24th, first year of Keian era). Previously, it was under the authority of the Ina clan. There is a Shinto ritual every year on the last day of the fifth month. Ancient mirrors, two in total, are kept as sacred treasures (mentioned below). The shrine priest is called Sugiyama Shuzen (this family sheltered a retainer of the Satomi clan during the Battle of Konodai and was destroyed by the Hojo clan, but was restored in the second year of Kan'ei).

It is a village by the Kokai River. Also, in the Hitachi Fudoki it says "convenience post station at Enoura Port." This is also land along a river).

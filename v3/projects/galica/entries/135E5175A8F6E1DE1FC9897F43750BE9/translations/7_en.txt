He went to the temple and met with the sōzu (high-ranking monk) whom he had long relied upon, told him about what had happened, had his head shaved, changed his name to Shinkai, and received the Five Precepts. After two years had passed, he requested leave from the sōzu and set out on a pilgrimage of ascetic practice throughout the provinces.

Three to four bu thick, one sun and five bu wide, with a pointed end like a sword. The entire surface shows breakage, and the color is blue-black. Though it might be thought to be a type of arrowhead stone, it is not. The stone quality is extremely hard, beyond what human craftsmanship could achieve. It was obtained at Mount Oitake in Omura, Hizen Province. In this area, such specimens are occasionally unearthed from the soil, and the local people call them "sword stones."

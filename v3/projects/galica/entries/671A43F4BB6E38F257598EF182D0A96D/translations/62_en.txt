Jindaiseki (Divine Age Stones) - Six

Tainenshi of Bukgenin Temple at Kamakōsan in Yamato Province is a friend who shares my same passion. He collects several hundred varieties of rare stones. In the eighth month of Anei 4 (1775), year of the Wood Sheep, I first saw this jindaiseki that was brought to a natural products exhibition in Osaka. No one knows what kind of divine object from ancient times this might be. It is said to have been excavated at Mount Miwa in Yamato Province. Its shape is as shown in the illustration, the color is pale white, and the thickness is four bu.

The Essence of the Evil Teaching - Written by Sessō Sōsai

Originally, at the end of the Tenmun era, there were merchants who came from Rome, the capital of the Western country of Italy, and anchored their ship in Bungo Province. When we examine their sea route, they traveled from the western seas toward the south, then from the south toward the north to reach Japan, so the Japanese people call them "Nanban-jin" (Southern Barbarians). Looking inside their ship, there were over two hundred people in total, including merchants and sailors. Among them, we see two people whose clothing differed from ordinary people.

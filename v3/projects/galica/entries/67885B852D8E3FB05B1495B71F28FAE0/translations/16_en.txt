The fragrance of this medicine spreads widely in all four directions, and when it ascends pointing toward the cloud paths, five-colored clouds drift across the sky and heavenly beings constantly descend. If one takes this medicine, one's appearance remains forever youthful, one never ages, and one's life becomes truly limitless. Therefore, the Chinese immortal called Magu was a woman who, in ancient times, fled her parents' home at the age of fifteen due to her stepmother's slander and secluded herself in the mountains. She naturally came to understand the immortal arts and reached Mount Hōrai.

There she took the medicine of immortality. More than three hundred years later, she met a person named Zhang Zhonghua in the mountains and told him of ancient times. Her face and form at that time were completely unchanged from the past, so it is said.

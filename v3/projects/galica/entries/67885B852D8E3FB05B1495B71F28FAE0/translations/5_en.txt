Now, the so-called Mount Hōrai is reached by traveling from here toward the southern sea across waves and billows for more than thirty thousand ri, and the great ocean beyond this is named the Ming Sea (Dark Sea). The color of its water is black and its depth is limitless. For this reason, it is also called the Black Sea. Though no wind blows, the waves are always high and bright, full and brimming, so the waves of clouds and waves of smoke reach a height of more than one hundred jō, never ceasing day or night. In ordinary worldly matters, neither ships nor rafts can pass through, so humans are kept far away. Only through the divine power of celestial immortals

《Interlinear note: Among the thirty-six members of the Heavenly Spirits Stars, the Heavenly Cunning Star》The Resourceful Star Wu Yong
This one person lived in Yuncheng County, teaching children to read. His courtesy name was Xuejiu, his nickname was Zhiduoxing (Resourceful Star), and his Daoist title was Master Jialiang. His mind encompassed ten thousand li, and he well stored the wisdom of Taigong, Sunzi, Kongming, and Li Jing. Using the 108 men《Interlinear note: He says this because he too serves under others》as his hands and feet, he accomplished things through deception and truth intermixed. Beginning with seizing 100,000 strings of cash from the Eastern Capital at Huangni Ridge, at Jizhou River he tormented the government bandit He Tao with water attacks, at the execution ground in Jiangzhou he dressed as a member of the four social classes to rescue the Timely Rain, when the army returned he plotted the destruction of the Zhu family father and son at Dulong Ridge, at Gaotang Prefecture he crushed Gao Lian's supernatural army, and even Ling Zhen's thunder cannons and Hu Yanchuo's fierce courage were made to surrender within ten days.

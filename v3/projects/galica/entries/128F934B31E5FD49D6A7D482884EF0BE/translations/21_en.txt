Flowery Monk Lu Zhishen

When he rescued a woman in the wine tower, it was not for lust. Though he tasted meat and wine in the temple grounds, he was ten times purer than the stinking incense of the precept-keeping monks' star anise. Looking at the red bodies of the Nio guardian statues, he laughed and mocked them together with others. When he pulled up willow trees in the vegetable garden, he handled them more lightly than radishes. Even in jest, he naturally made the world fear him. How good this person is!

Little Overlord Zhou Tong

If we were to speak in Chinese dialect, taking fire across the mountains, continuing with candles, saying this and that. When the bedroom curtains open, it's pitch black. In love, like a wild pheasant burning recklessly in the fields. A monk's sermon like a gun. On the wedding night, how heartless to maintain the precept against adultery...

《Small text: Tiansha Star among the thirty-six members of the Tiangang Stars》Black Whirlwind Li Kui

This person is from Baizhang Village in Yishui County. His face is like a black bear, and his body resembles an iron ox.
Therefore he is also called Li Tieniu (Iron Ox Li). He skillfully wields twin axes and often has the bad habit of drunken madness.
In his youth he came to Jiangzhou and served as a minor jailer under Dai Zong. His fighting and shouting at the execution ground to save Song Jiang and Dai Zong ranks first among such deeds. On the mountain path going to fetch his mother, he killed a fake Li Kui and demolished a tavern. When his mother was bitten by a tiger at Yi Ridge, he wept in fury at Shangutou and killed four large tigers in his rage. The heads he cut off facing enemies include: Zhu Long and Zhu Biao at Dulonggang, Duan Pengjü at the Ten-Sided Ambush, He Yun and Zhenlei at Qingshi Valley, and Gao Keliu and Zhang Jinren at Piling Commandery. The heads he severed in correcting evil include: the lustful woman of the Di family at Siliu Village, the fake Song Gong at Niutou Mountain, or Yan Qing...

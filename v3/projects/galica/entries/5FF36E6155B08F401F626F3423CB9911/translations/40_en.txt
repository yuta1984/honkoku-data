[People gathering at the banquet]

"The solitary bird dwelling in the forest by the pond recognizes (the arrival of spring)
In the spring marsh, in the hidden valley, cold light observes the flower viewing
Sunlight and the sound of springs draw near
The first thunder announces its echo in the mountains
The colors are bright and clear, as evening rain passes by
From this I come to know even more deeply the profound imperial favor
With what in this lifetime
Shall I answer Heaven?"

Thus she composed. At this time, she was seventeen years of age. Thereafter, following this precedent, it became an annual custom that continues to this very reign.

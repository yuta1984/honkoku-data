【Masakado and His Six Body Doubles】
In every battle, Masakado would dress six warriors identical to himself in the same armor and equipment, mount them on horses of the same color, and have them advance and retreat as one body, making it completely impossible to distinguish which was Masakado and which were his retainers. Even now it was the same - seven Masakados rode side by side with their bits aligned as they charged forward, but they suffered a painful [attack].

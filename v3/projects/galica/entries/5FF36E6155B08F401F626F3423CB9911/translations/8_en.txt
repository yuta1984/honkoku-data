Contemporary painters of recent times employ various techniques from both Japanese and Chinese traditions in their work.
They depict all phenomena in meticulous detail, exhausting novelty and competing in refinement to
demonstrate skillful artistry. However, their brushwork tends to be delicate and weak,
and not a few have lost the ancient methods of painting. Now here we have the illustrated book
"Zen Taiheiki" (Previous Taiheiki) created by Hōkyō Okada Gyokuzan of Jiangnan.

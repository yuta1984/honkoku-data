The small people are from a country called "Hachiya." It is a land in the northeastern corner of Europe, extending northward to the frozen sea. It is an extremely cold country. For half the year only daylight continues, and for half the year only night continues. It is transmitted that people's height is 2 or 3 sun. However, it is also said to be over 1 shaku. In China, they are also called "short people." This refers to them.

【39】
Tall People
The tall people are from a country called "Chika." It is located within South America. Adjacent to this country is also a country called "Patagonia," where people are also tall in stature. Generally, their height is said to be 1 jō and 2 shaku by our measure. All are located in the tatsumi (southeast) direction from Japan. They have four seasons, and their customs are extremely brave and strong, favoring bows and arrows. The length of their arrows is 6 or 7 shaku.

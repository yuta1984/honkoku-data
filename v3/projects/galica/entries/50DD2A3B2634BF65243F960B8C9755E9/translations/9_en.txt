Javanese People

Java is a distant country located to the southwest of China. It is an extremely hot country, and the seasonal order of cold and heat is completely opposite to that of China and Japan, being quite different. Jakarta, where the Dutch people who currently come to Japan reside, is also at the northern tip of this country. Therefore, no separate illustration of people is provided. The North Star is not visible, and it is located at six or seven degrees south latitude.

【7】
Makassar People

Makassar is an island country south of Luzon, and it is an extremely hot country. The people are of low status. The visibility of both northern and southern polar stars is the same as in Sumatra.

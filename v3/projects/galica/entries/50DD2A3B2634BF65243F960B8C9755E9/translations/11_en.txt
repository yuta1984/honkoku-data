It is an error to equate the Mughals with the Hui-Hui (Muslims). This too is within Southern India and is the foremost great nation. It has fourteen provinces and is said to be a country rich in treasures and wealth. Though it is a warm country, its climate is roughly equivalent to that of Guangdong in China. Its northern latitude is 22 degrees.

Dutch People

Holland is located in the North Sea region of Europe. It is west of the former Germania and borders France. It is an extremely cold country and a small nation with a north-south distance of 2 degrees. It lies to the northwest of Japan and China, and is 13,000 ri across the sea from Japan. Its northern latitude is 54-55 degrees, or perhaps 56 degrees.

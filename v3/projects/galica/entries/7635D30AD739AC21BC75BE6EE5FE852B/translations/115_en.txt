Shishya shichya eru erui eri yakiya eya yusu
Wo ya ki bise hita irifu (What is recorded here is in grass script, with regular and semi-cursive scripts existing separately. Two or three characters, or four or five characters are written horizontally in continuous strokes to form sounds. This is indeed a method of writing.)

One: Worin, Two: Tsua, Three: Derii, Four: Zeteri, Five: Bea, Six: Sese, Seven: Semu, Eight: Wosemu, Nine: Kehee, Ten: Gese
(For one hundred, two circles; for one thousand, three; for ten thousand, four; for one hundred million, five; for one trillion, six; for ten quadrillion, seven, and so on.)

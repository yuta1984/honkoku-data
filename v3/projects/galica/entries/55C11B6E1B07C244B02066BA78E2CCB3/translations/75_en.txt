【Right Page Upper Section】
○Sei refers to rice bundles. Byō means seedlings, also called nursery beds.

○Barnyard millet (hie) supplements the center, increases qi, thickens the intestines and stomach, and relieves hunger.

○Wheat (mugi) supplements deficiency, invigorates blood vessels, enriches the five organs, and improves complexion.

○Buckwheat (soba) enriches the intestines and stomach, moves qi downward, harmonizes accumulated stagnation, and eliminates heat-induced swelling and wind-induced pain.

○Mung beans (ryokutō) digest food, move qi downward, remove heat, detoxify poison, promote urination, and treat abdominal distension and diarrhea.

【Left Page Upper Section】
○Hemp (asa) treats women's menstrual irregularities, forgetfulness, external wounds, and internal hemorrhoids, and removes bad blood.

○Black-eyed peas (sasage) increase qi, supplement the kidneys, improve stomach function, harmonize the five organs, and stop frequent urination.

○Peas (endō) promote urination, treat swelling, cure diabetes, and treat vomiting. Broad beans and other legumes have the same effects.

○Legumes (mame) treat edema, disperse bad blood, strengthen the spleen and stomach, detoxify alcohol poisoning, and remove heat from the stomach.

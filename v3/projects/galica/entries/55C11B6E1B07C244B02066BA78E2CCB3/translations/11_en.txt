【Running title】Tōsho Zōho Kinmō Zui 12, 3

○The baku resembles
a bear. It has an elephant's
trunk, a rhinoceros's
eyes, a tail like an ox's,
and tiger's
feet. It eats copper, iron, and
bamboo.
It is an animal that
sleeps well.
【Supplement】It is said to eat
all bad
dreams. Therefore
people draw it on
pillows and
name them baku pillows.

【Right page, lower section - Illustration】

【Left page, upper section】
○The elephant is a
large beast from foreign lands.
Its trunk and tusks are long.
【Supplement】It is said to eat
food through its mouth
and drink
water through its trunk. Once every
three years
it gives milk. It lives in
great high mountains.
People take its
tusks to make all kinds
of vessels.
These are called
ivory.

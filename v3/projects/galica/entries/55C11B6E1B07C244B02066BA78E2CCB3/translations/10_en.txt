【Running title】Tōsho Zōho Kinmō Zui 12, 2

【Right page, upper section】
○The tiger has a form
like a cat but
is as large as an ox
Its color is yellow
with thick front legs, and all the strength
of its body is in its front legs. When it
walks at night, one eye emits
light while the other eye
sees objects. Its voice is like
thunder and it can well
raise winds【Supplement】When a tiger
roars once on a
mountain, all beasts are said to
cower in fear

【Right page, lower section - Illustration】
Tiger

【Left page, upper section】
○The Zouyu is a white tiger
Its
tail is longer than
its body. It is a
benevolent beast

○The leopard has a form
very similar to the tiger
but smaller. Its head is round
and face white. Its fur color is【Supplement】
pale yellow with white
spots
It is exceedingly beautiful, therefore
it is said to cherish
its own fur

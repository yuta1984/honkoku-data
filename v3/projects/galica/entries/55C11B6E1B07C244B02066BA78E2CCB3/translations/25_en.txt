【Right page, upper section】
(Feed mandarin duck to those with marital discord.)

○The heron has a thin, long neck, and both its beak and legs are long. They come in various sizes. The smaller ones have long feathers on their crown. It strengthens the spleen and supplements qi.

○The night heron is a water bird. It is about the size of a heron, grayish-white in color with a black back. This is called "seguro-goi" (black-backed night heron), and those with spots are called "hoshi-goi" (starred night heron). It neutralizes the toxins of various fish.

○The red crane (crested ibis) is also called "shuro" (vermillion heron). It is larger than a heron, white in color with a slight reddish tint. It is commonly called "taugarasu."

【Left page, upper section】
○The sandpiper is slightly smaller than a dove. Its beak and legs are long, and its feathers are brown with black spots. It lives in rice fields and marshes. They come in various sizes, with the larger ones called "boto-shigi." It supplements deficiency and warms people.

○The cormorant resembles a crow, with a long neck and a somewhat long beak. It enters water and skillfully catches fish. It nests in forest trees. Fishermen raise them to catch fish.

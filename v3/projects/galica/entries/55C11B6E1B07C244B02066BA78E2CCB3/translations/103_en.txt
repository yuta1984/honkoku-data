○Poplar (yō) has four varieties: yellow, white, blue, and red. White poplar has round leaves, and blue poplar has long leaves. Red poplar's leaves turn red when frost falls. Water poplar is the river willow that grows by waterside.

○Parasitic plant (yadorigi) grows on various trees, referring to plants that grow between branches or in the forks of trees. The name changes depending on the host tree. It is also called gūboku.

○Willow (blue willow) has small drooping branches

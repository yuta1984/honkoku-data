【Right Page, Upper Section】
○Aconite (torikabu̇to̥) has flowers the color of bellflowers. The shape resembles a crow's head and also looks like a warrior's helmet. It blooms in September.

○Garden balsam (hōsenkwa) has red and white flowers. It blooms in July. It is also called golden phoenix flower (kinpōkwa), and the flowers come in yellow, purple, and blue colors.

【Left Page, Upper Section】
○Chili pepper (tōgarashi) treats hernia and kills insects, but is poisonous to humans.

○Sunflower (tengaibana) is also called "sun-greeting flower" (keiyōkwa). Because it faces the sun, it's also called "sun wheel" (higuruma). The flower resembles a chrysanthemum but is large. The color is yellow, and there are also white ones.

○Wild ginger (tokō) has leaves resembling horse hooves. Purple flowers bloom. It's called horsehoof fragrant (bateikō) or native wild ginger (dosaishiii).

Tōsho Zōho Kinmō Zui, Volume 12
Domestic and Wild Animals (This section describes various beasts that live in mountains, fields, and places where humans dwell)

【Upper section】
○The Qilin is
a benevolent beast.
It has the body of a deer and the tail of an ox,
with a single horn. The male is
called Ki, and the female is
called Lin. It does not step on
living creatures,
nor does it trample living grass.
It is a beast that appears
in the age of a sage ruler.

【Right page, upper section】
○Kongiri is a small hamo (pike conger). Its medicinal functions are the same as hamo.

○Sayori (halfbeak) is sweet and balanced with no toxicity. Eating this prevents epidemic diseases. Same as shingyo (needlefish).

○Masu (trout) warms the stomach and harmonizes the center.

○Ebi (shrimp) treats abdominal masses, and is good to apply to smallpox. It strengthens yang qi and promotes milk flow. When children eat it, their legs become weak. Same as ka (shrimp).

【Left page, upper section】
○Umiebi (sea shrimp) when made into namasu (vinegared dish) and eaten, treats tooth decay and cures head sores. Same as kōka (red shrimp), ryōka (dragon shrimp), and kaika (sea shrimp).

○Kaka is river shrimp. Commonly called tenaga-ebi (freshwater prawn).

○Ami (mysid shrimp) are small shrimp. Also called byōka, senka, and deika.

○Shirouo (ice goby) relaxes the center, makes the stomach healthy, promotes water circulation, and stops coughing.

○Shakunage is a type of shrimp

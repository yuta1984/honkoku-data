○Centipedes (mukade) have black and green backs, red legs, and yellow bellies. Those who are stung should apply black-boned chicken droppings or garlic.

○Toads (hikigaeru) have white bellies with black patterns. They have growths on their backs, and the oil from these is called senso. It is used in medicine.

○Toads (gama) have black spots on their backs. They are agile and jump well. They transform into quail.

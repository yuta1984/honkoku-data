【Right Page, Upper Section】
○Chrysanthemum (kiku) has one hundred varieties. The flowers also come in many different types. It is said to cure headaches, clarify vision, and extend life. For medicine, there is one variety of yellow chrysanthemum.

○Pampas grass (obana) resembles cogon grass. The bark is used to make rope or footwear. Large ones are called "sekibō" (stone miscanthus), small ones are called "habō" (plantain miscanthus).

○Perilla (e) is also called white perilla. It grows abundantly in mountains and fields. It is rich in oil. Called perilla oil.

【Left Page, Upper Section】
○Morning glory (asagao) has leaves with three pointed tips. The flowers are purple, and white varieties have existed since ancient times. Recently new flowers have appeared, including red-striped and speckled varieties, with various flower forms.

○Bindweed (hirugao) has flowers shaped like the drums (kushi) blown in military camps. Hence it is called "kushi flower." It is also called "senfuku flower."

○Elder (sokuzu) has five leaves on each branch. The flowers are white and the berries are blue like mung beans. It is good to apply the leaves to painful areas and wash with a decoction. Also called "bone-setting grass."

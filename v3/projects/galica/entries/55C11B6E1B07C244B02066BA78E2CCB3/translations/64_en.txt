【Right page, upper section】
[continuing from previous page: The praying mantis eats wart insects and] appears in midsummer. When angry, it raises its forelegs.

○The rakusen (cricket) is also called kirigirisu. Its alternative name is katsukatsuji. What is called itodo is also of this kind.

○The keireki is also called keitō. It is a hatahata insect, commonly called shōryō insect.

○The sōba (camel cricket) is also called sōkei. Its shape is round with long legs. It lives near the hearth.

【Left page, upper section】
○The dragonfly has six legs and four wings, appears in summer, and flies to catch and eat insects. The large ones are called yanma.

○The red dragonfly is a dragonfly with red coloring. It is commonly called aka-yanma. When burned black, it treats throat swelling.

○The inago (locust) appears on rice plants. It is the same as shū.

○The hatahata is also called shūshi. It resembles the locust.

【Running title】Tōsho Zōho Kinmō Zui 12, 1

【Right page, upper section】
○The lion is the king of all beasts
It runs five hundred ri in a day
It catches and devours
tigers and leopards, therefore
【Supplement】Even tigers and leopards
greatly fear
the lion
It is a fierce beast of Tenjiku (India)
said to have obtained
supernatural powers

It is also called
Shungei

【Right page, lower section - Illustration】
Lion

【Left page, upper section】
○The Xiezhi is
a beast from foreign lands
Its form
resembles a lion
with a single horn. It is also called
Shin'yō (Divine Sheep)
It can distinguish between
right and wrong. When Gāo Yáo
administered justice,
those whose guilt was
doubtful were entrusted
to the Xiezhi
【Supplement】Those who are guilty are
devoured by it, while those
without guilt are not eaten

○The bantam chicken (chabo) is numerous in the Jiangnan region of China. Its body is small, with legs only about two sun (approximately 6cm) long.

○The bullfinch (uso) is smaller than a sparrow, with beautiful patterns in its feather colors. The underside of its belly is white, making it a beautiful bird.

○The swallow (tsubame) is about the size of a sparrow. It holds mud in its mouth to build nests under the eaves of houses. It is said to avoid the tsuchinoe-mi days.

○Dove (hato) is a general term with many varieties. What is illustrated here is commonly called juzukake, or also called Hachiman dove. The area around the neck is black, as if wearing a rosary. The feather color is ash-white and beautiful. People do not catch this dove.

○The green dove (yamabato) lives in the mountains and does not come out to the villages. Its feather color is green-brown. When eaten, it supplements weakness and invigorates the blood. This is the color of the emperor's robes.

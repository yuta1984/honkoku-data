○Narcissus (suisenka) blooms from early winter and has flowers until early spring. The shape is like a sake cup, with yellow parts. The petals are white. Therefore it is called "golden cup, silver stand." The leaves resemble those of red spider lily.

○Dwarf lilyturf (janohige) blooms with pale purple flowers in April. The fruit is green and round like pearls. It bears fruit around autumn. The roots are used for medicine.

【Left Page, Upper Section】
○Fringed pink (nadeshiko) has pale purple flowers that bloom in June. It is abundant along riverbanks. Recently there are new varieties with various colors.

○China pink (sekichiku) closely resembles nadeshiko, with flowers in red and white or striped varieties. The flowers bloom in May and June. There are single and double varieties.

○Hosta (gibōshi) has large leaves. It blooms in autumn with pale purple color. There are several varieties with different colors. Also called "white crane immortal."

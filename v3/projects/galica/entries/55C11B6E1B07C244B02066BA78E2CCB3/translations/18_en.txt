【Running title】Tōsho Zōho Kinmō Zui 12, 10

【Right page, upper section】
○The mastiff is a large dog.
Those with a height of four shaku
are called gō. Commonly, these are
called Tang dogs (Chinese dogs).

○Dogs have a salty taste and are warm in nature with
no toxicity. They stabilize the five
organs, increase qi, and are good
for the kidneys.

○The nōken has long fur.
It is the same as the mōkōshiken,
a shaggy dog.

○The hedgehog is like the mita-tanuki
with short legs and a long tail.
Its color is bluish-white, and its foot fur
stings people. It lives in mountain valleys
and fields. It is the same as the i (hedgehog).

○The civet lives in the mountains

○Rabbits have short front
legs and nine holes in their buttocks.
They are pungent and neutral in nature with no toxicity. They supplement

○Monkeys are a type of kō (macaque)

They are good at climbing tree branches.

○Kō (macaques) have a shape similar
to humans. They have no spleen in their belly
and digest food through

walk. Their nature is restless
and they harm things.

○Red beans (azuki) move water and qi downward, clear pus and blood, promote urination, and treat abdominal distension and wasting thirst.

○Kidney beans (ingen-mame) harmonize the center, move qi downward, stop vomiting, supplement the five organs, and detoxify cholera morbus and alcohol poisoning. Hyacinth beans, yard-long beans, and lima beans have the same effects.

○Sesame (goma) increases vitality, develops skin and flesh, strengthens muscles and bones, benefits the large and small intestines, and clarifies hearing and vision.

○Poppy (keshi) removes wind toxins, expels pathogenic heat, treats phlegm, cures gastric reflux, and moistens thirst.

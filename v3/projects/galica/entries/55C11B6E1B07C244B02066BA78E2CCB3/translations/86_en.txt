【Right Page Upper Section】
○Pear stops hot coughs, stops thirst, eliminates phlegm, reduces fever, and moistens the lungs.

○Chinese apple (karanashi) supplements various deficient qi in the middle burner, harmonizes the spleen, and treats qi stagnation.

○Jujube (natsume) nourishes the spleen and stomach, generates body fluids, removes pathogenic qi from the heart and abdomen, and moistens the heart and lungs.

○Chestnut increases qi, warms the intestines and stomach, supplements the kidneys, and treats weakness in the lower back and legs. Includes shibauri (wild chestnut) and sasaguri (small chestnut).

○Yuzu aids digestion, neutralizes alcohol toxicity, removes bad qi from the intestines and stomach, and treats pregnant women's loss of appetite and bland taste in the mouth.

【Left Page Upper Section】
○Citrus (kunenbo) eliminates heat toxins in the intestines and stomach, stops sudden thirst, and promotes urination.

○Trifoliate orange (karatachi) promotes bowel movements, removes chest congestion, and eliminates phlegm. Those with weak spleen and stomach should not use it.

○Mandarin orange (tachibana) stops wasting thirst, opens the stomach, and removes congestion in the chest.

○Japanese nutmeg yew (kaya) treats pinworms, aids digestion, clarifies vision, stops coughing and leucorrhea, and treats hemorrhoids.

○Persimmon promotes water metabolism, neutralizes alcohol toxicity, and removes heat from the stomach.

○Chinquapin (shii) warms the intestines and stomach, makes people fat and healthy. Eating this prevents hunger.

○Hazel (hashibami) increases energy and strengthens the intestines and stomach.

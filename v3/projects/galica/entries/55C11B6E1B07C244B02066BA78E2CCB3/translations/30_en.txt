【Right page, upper section】
○The skylark (hibari) is also called kōjaku. It is slightly larger than a sparrow, brown-colored with spots. From the beginning of March until around the summer solstice, it rises into the sky and sings. It stimulates yang energy and supplements the vital essence.

○The pheasant (kiji) - the male has beautiful feather coloring and a long tail. The female is brown with spots. It calls when spring warmth arrives. It should be eaten from September to November.

【Left page, upper section】
○The waxwing (renjaku) has two varieties: long-tailed and short-tailed. Its size is smaller than a brown-eared bulbul, blackish-brown in color, with white feathers on the tail that look like a braided sash.

○The bunting (shitodo) is about the size of a sparrow, pale blue with slight spotting. It comes in winter months. Commonly called aoji. When this bird is charred black and applied to swellings, it becomes an excellent medicine.

【Running title】Tōsho Zōho Kinmō Zui 12, 11

【Right page, upper section】
○Otters live in water and
have short limbs. Their color is
bluish-black, and they catch and
eat fish. They treat water retention and abdominal
distension. One should not eat too much of them.

○Martens are a type of rodent,
large and yellowish-black
in color. Their fur is thick and
warm. They are made into hats and
collars to ward off cold. Commonly
written as "栗鼠" (squirrel).

○Flying squirrels are like small foxes
with flesh wings resembling bats.
Their legs are short and their tail
is about three shaku long. Their voice is like
a person calling, and they eat fire and smoke.

【Right page, lower section - Illustrations】
Kō (macaque) (reading: mashira)
En (monkey) (reading: saru)
Datsu (otter) (reading: kawauso)

【Left page, upper section】
They move from high
places to low places, but cannot
climb from low places
to high places.

○Kon (martens) are a type of rodent.
Their fur can be made into
fur clothing. Another name is reisom (礼鼠).

○Sea dogs are fur seals
(ontotsusei). Their shape resembles foxes
with fish-like tails. They have
bluish-white fur on their bodies, and also
bluish-black spots.
Their navels treat exhaustion
of the spleen and kidneys.

○Sea otters resemble otters
but are as large as dogs.

【Left page, lower section - Illustrations】
Deū (marten) (reading: risu)
Kon (reading: ten)
Go (flying squirrel) (reading: musasabi)

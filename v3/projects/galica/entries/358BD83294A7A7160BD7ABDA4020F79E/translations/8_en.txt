【Right page】
　　　Nihonbashi

　　　　　Higashi Inaka Detchi
For sashimi too
let the waves crash—
from the fish market's
Nihonbashi
comes the first bonito

　　　　　Asakura-an Sanshō
With Mount Fuji
　as a hanging scroll,
　　　Nihonbashi—
　its shape a round table,
　　the center of Edo

【Left page】
　　　　　Nanshi Shunkoku

First bonito—
　though they don't state the price,
　　it's gold coins!
showing their warped shape,
　　the fish vendors

　　　Chōhōdō Tamesumi
When summer comes
　Fuji too turns blue, and
　　　the first bonito—
　together
　　　we gaze enviously
　　　　　at Nihonbashi

【Right page】
　　Ryōgoku

　Tamagawa-tei
　　Tomoyobu

The evening shower
has cleared,
　cool
　in the boat, again
hearing thunder god's voice—
　the traffic on the bridge

　Hōshimanto

Watching fireworks—
　two stars in the sky
　　descending,
not magpies, but
　Ryōgoku
　　　Bridge

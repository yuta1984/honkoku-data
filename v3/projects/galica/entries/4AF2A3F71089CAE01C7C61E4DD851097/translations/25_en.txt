In 1806 (Bunka 4 in our country, the year of Hinoto-U), on July 12th of that year, the Rheinbund (Rhine Confederation) was established. Napoleon became its protector and proclaimed this to the world. In August, the German Emperor abdicated the French-Roman Imperial position. At this point, the ancient German national system completely collapsed. Prussia, though having once made peace with France, could not endure the oppression and again raised arms to fight France at Jena and Auerstedt, but was defeated in all battles. Even their strong fortresses in several locations could not be defended and surrendered to France. Saxony had its communication routes with Prussia cut off and could not provide relief.

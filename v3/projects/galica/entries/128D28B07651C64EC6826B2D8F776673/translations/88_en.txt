　Since the real arrowheads are coated

　with thread
　For arrowheads, deer
　leg bones are used

[Description of bow]
　The bow is called "fagu" in Ainu language

　Made from Japanese elm wood. This is what
　is commonly called kyara wood. Circumference
　of two sun, six to seven bu. Length of three shaku,
　seven to eight sun. All wrapped with leather. The string
　is made from the bark of a grass called "ai"
　twisted into three strands

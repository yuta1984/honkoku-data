The Ainu, both men and women, have eyebrows that grow in a straight line across their faces. In addition, they have abundant body hair.

A middle-class Ainu woman. Generally, they appear like this.

Regardless of social status, both men and women go barefoot, treading on frost, snow, and rocks without showing any sign of pain. When Ainu men enter the mountains to hunt beasts, they all go barefoot as well.

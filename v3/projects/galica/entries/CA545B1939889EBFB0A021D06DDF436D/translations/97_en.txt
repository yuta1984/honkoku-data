Twenty-Eight Constellations

Horn Constellation (Jiao)
First
According to the Shu Jing Tong Kao, the two stars of Jiao form the Heavenly Gate, with the space between them being the Heavenly Gate, and the interior being the Heavenly Court. The ecliptic passes through it, and it governs military affairs.

Neck Constellation (Kang)
Second
According to the Tong Kao, the four stars of Kang represent the inner court of the Son of Heaven.

Root Constellation (Di)
Third
According to the Tong Kao, the four stars of Di represent the residential officials of the Son of Heaven.

Room Constellation (Fang)
Fourth
According to the Tong Kao, the four stars of Fang form the Bright Hall, the palace where the Son of Heaven promulgates his policies. (remainder omitted)

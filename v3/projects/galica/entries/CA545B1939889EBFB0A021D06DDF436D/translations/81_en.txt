Dainichi Nyorai (Mahāvairocana)
28th Day

Regarding the lord of the Land of Tranquil Light (Jakukōdo), there are three divisions of Dainichi. These are called the Dainichi of Gold Realm, Womb Realm, and Susiddhi.

Hyōsu Daimyōjin
28th Day

Located in Hyōsu village, Yasu County, Ōmi Province. The original ground is Fudō, also Dainichi.

Yakuō Bodhisattva (Bhaiṣajyarāja)
29th Day

The honorable Yakuō of Sannō-in is Fudō, which is the manifestation of Daitsūchishō (Mahābhijñājñānābhibhū). Fudō's fire-light samādhi is the burning of the body.

Nauka Daimyōjin
29th Day

Enshrined in Ōmi Province. The original ground is Amida.

Shaka Nyorai (Śākyamuni)
30th Day

The Ninnōkyō-shō states that Shaka is translated as "Nōnin" (Capable of Benevolence). Nōnin means compassion. It is called "nin" (benevolence) because it can give compassion to others.

Kibi Daimyōjin
30th Day

Enshrined in Kaya County, Bitchū Province. This is Prince Kibitsuhiko, son of Emperor Kōrei. The original ground is Kokūzō (Ākāśagarbha).

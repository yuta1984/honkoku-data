The Seven Shrine Manifestations of the Mountain King (Sannō Nanasha Gongen)

Ninomiya Kohiei
Five-colored flowers bloomed on the great cedar and descended from heaven. The honorable name is Kaedai Bodhisattva. The original ground is Yakushi (Bhaiṣajyaguru).

Sanmiya  
During Emperor Kanmu's Enryaku era, in the form of a noble lady riding purple clouds and holding the Lotus Sutra, descended to the vicinity of Ōdake. The original ground is Fugen (Samantabhadra).

Ōmiya Gongen
In the first year of Emperor Kinmei (庚申), the Miwa Myōjin of Yamato descended from heaven, and appeared at Ōhiei in the first year of Emperor Tenji. This is Ōnamuchi. The original ground is Shaka (Śākyamuni).

Hachiōji Temple
This is Kuninokutachi-no-mikoto. In the first year of the tenth emperor, Sujin, descended beside Ōdake in Shiga County. Misaki-yama Myōjin. The original ground is Senju (Sahasrabhuja).

Shōshinshi
In the first year of Emperor Monmu, appeared in Shiga County, Ōmi Province. Hachiman Daibosatsu. The original ground is Amida (Amitābha).

Kyakujin Gongen
During Emperor Kanmu's Enryaku era, appeared at the foot of Hachiōji Mountain. The great priest Keimyō built and relocated the shrine. This is Hakusan Myōri Gongen. The original ground is Jūichimen (Ekādaśamukha).

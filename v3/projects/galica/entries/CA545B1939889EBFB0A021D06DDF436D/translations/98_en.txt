Heart Constellation (Xin)
Fifth
According to the Tong Kao, the three stars of Xin represent the rightful position of the Heavenly King. (middle portion omitted) The front star represents the Crown Prince, and the rear star represents the Imperial Son.

Tail Constellation (Wei)
Sixth
According to the Tong Kao, the nine stars of Wei represent the quarters of the rear palace, and are also called the Nine Sons.

Winnowing Basket Constellation (Ji)
Seventh
According to the Tong Kao, the four stars of Ji also represent the palace of the empress and concubines, and govern the eight winds.

Dipper Constellation (Dou)
Eighth
According to the Tong Kao, the six stars of Dou represent the Heavenly Temple and also govern military affairs.

Ox Constellation (Niu)
Ninth
According to the Tong Kao, the six stars of Niu represent the heavenly bridge and govern matters of sacrificial offerings.

Girl Constellation (Nü)
Tenth
According to the Tong Kao, the four stars of Nü represent the Heavenly Minor Palace and govern textiles.

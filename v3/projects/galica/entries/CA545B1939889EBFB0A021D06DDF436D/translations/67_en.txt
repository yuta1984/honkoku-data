Southern Gundari Myōō (Military Tea-Benefit Wisdom King)
- The Blue Dragon Commentary states: "He subjugates all asuras and various evil demon deities." His original ground (honji) is Avalokiteśvara Bodhisattva.

Northern Kongō Yasha Myōō (Diamond Yaksha Wisdom King)
- The Blue Dragon Commentary states: "He subjugates all fearsome yakshas." His original ground is Śākyamuni Buddha.

Central Five Great Honored Myōō (Great Saint Fudō Myōō)
- The Blue Dragon Commentary states: "He subjugates all demons and spirits and those who create various obstacles and afflictions." His original ground is Mahāvairocana Buddha.

Kurikara Fudō Myōō (Kurikara Immovable Wisdom King)
- According to the explanation in the Kurikara Sutra: "To manifest the Kurikara body and bring good fortune, he observed the ninety-five types of great heretics." Alternatively, it is said: "The sword is simply the sword on the right side of the main deity, and the dragon is the rope on the left side. However, the sword and rope represent the two dharmas of male-female and concentration-wisdom."

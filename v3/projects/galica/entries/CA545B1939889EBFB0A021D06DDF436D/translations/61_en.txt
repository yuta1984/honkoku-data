The Six Jizo Bodhisattvas

Yoten Gaji-zo
Left hand holds a wish-fulfilling jewel, right hand forms the teaching mudra
Saves all heavenly beings and humans

Hoko Oji-zo  
Left hand holds a monk's staff, right hand forms the wish-granting mudra
Brings rain and ensures the fruition of the five grains

Kongo Ganji-zo
Left hand holds a Yama banner, right hand forms the fearlessness mudra
Relieves the suffering of those entering hell

Kongo Hoji-zo
Left hand holds a precious jewel, right hand forms the nectar mudra
Saves beings in the hungry ghost realm

Kongo Toji-zo
Left hand holds a vajra banner, right hand forms the fearlessness mudra
Saves beings in the asura realm

Kongo Hiji-zo
Left hand holds a monk's staff, right hand forms the welcoming mudra
Saves beings in the animal realm

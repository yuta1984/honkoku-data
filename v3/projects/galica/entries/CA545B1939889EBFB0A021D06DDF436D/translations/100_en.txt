Stomach Constellation (Wei)
Seventeenth
According to the Tong Kao, it governs storehouses and the treasury of five grains.

Pleiades Constellation (Mao)
Eighteenth
According to the Tong Kao, the seven stars of Mao are the ears of heaven and govern the western direction.

Net Constellation (Bi)
Nineteenth
According to the Tong Kao, the eight stars of Bi govern frontier troops and hunting expeditions. When the moon enters Bi, there is much rain.

Beak Constellation (Zi)
Twentieth
According to the Tong Kao, the three stars of Zishi serve as sentries for the army.

Orion Constellation (Shen)
Twenty-first
According to the Tong Kao, the ten stars of Shen form the body of a white beast, and the three stars in the center govern generals.

Well Constellation (Jing)
Twenty-second
According to the Tong Kao, the eight stars of Eastern Well are the southern gate of heaven, where the ecliptic passes, and they govern water management.

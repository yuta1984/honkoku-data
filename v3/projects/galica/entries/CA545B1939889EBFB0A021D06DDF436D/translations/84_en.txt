Iizuna Gongen
Original ground: Fudō (Acala)

Suzuka Gongen
One deity enshrined in Suzuka District, Ise Province

Atago Gongen
Enshrines Kagutsuchi no Kami. This deity died from being burned by fire, therefore made a vow to rescue from fire. Behind Tarōbō of the inner sanctuary, Shōgun Jizō serves as the main shrine.

Izu-Hakone Gongen
The main shrine is Hikohohodemi no Mikoto. There are Komagatake Gongen, Hakuwa Ryūō, Right Magpie Left Magpie Kings, and guest shrines.

Yoshino Zaō Gongen
Manifested the wrathful form of Mahāsthāmaprāpta and appeared at Mount Kinpu. In the right hand holds a three-pronged vajra, raises the left arm, presses the abdomen with the left hand, and both eyes clearly express anger. This is the aspect of subjugating demonic obstacles.

Dewa Yudono Gongen
Manifested as Yudono Gongen through harmonious adaptation at the headwaters of the Daibonjiriver in Dewa Province. The original ground is Dharmakāya Dainichi (Mahāvairocana).

【Right page】
Susanoo-no-Mikoto remained in Izumo Province and became a deity there, so he is called Taisha Kizuki Daimyōjin (Great Deity of Izumo Grand Shrine). October is the month when this deity conceals himself, so it is called Kannazuki (month without gods). Also, in Izumo Province there is a place called Kamiari-no-ura (Bay of Gods' Presence), and every year in October, the deities of Japan come to that bay. As evidence of this, countless small bamboo boats float upon the waves. For this reason, Izumo Province also calls October Kamiarizuki (month of gods' presence).

At that time, eight million deities followed the divine decree and descended from heaven together with the heavenly grandson.

The goddess was beautiful in countenance, and Kashikone-kashikone-no-mikoto had a most reverent heart. Together they reigned for two hundred billion million years. Although the above three generations had male and female forms, there was not yet the way of marital union between husband and wife. In the seventh generation, Izanagi-no-mikoto and Izanami-no-mikoto became husband and wife and gave birth to one country, which is present-day Awaji Island. Because this country was extremely small, they called it "Ahachi-no-kuni" (Land of Shame). The two deities descended to that land, and eventually went out into the fields

【Left Page】
and began eating water plants. This is what is now called "laughter." The practice of eating things found in the fields began from this time. Thus, when they observed the state of this land, seeing that it was overgrown with reeds and there was no space, they cleared away these reeds. The places where they placed the reeds became Yamato, and the traces where they cast them away became rivers. Even so, not knowing the way of yin-yang union, when they saw a wagtail bird beating its tail against the ground, the two deities first learned the way of conjugal union.

【Right page】
Those who are mentioned are Ame-no-Koyane-no-mikoto, Ame-no-Uzume-no-mikoto, Ishikoridome-no-mikoto, Tamano-oya-no-mikoto, and Ame-no-Futotama-no-mikoto. These are the five deities. Ame-no-Koyane-no-mikoto is Kasuga Daimyōjin. Ame-no-Futotama-no-mikoto is Katori Daimyōjin. These two deities especially received the divine decree and descended from heaven to assist the heavenly grandson. Therefore, they would be like both wings.

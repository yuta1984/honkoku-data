She was born in the village of Katakurabe, and first partook of御鳥草 (mitorigusa). This is what we now call seri (water dropwort). This was the beginning of eating food from the village. Also, after emerging from the Heavenly Rock Cave, when she made an imperial visit to Mount Kagu, she ate椎の実 (shiinomi, chinquapin nuts). This was said to be the beginning of partaking of mountain foods. Thus, after twenty-five hundred thousand years had passed, she ascended to heaven. Later, in the twenty-fifth year of Emperor Suinin, the eleventh emperor of the human imperial line, around the third month, Amaterasu Omikami taught Yamato-hime-no-Mikoto, saying: "The land of Ise of the divine winds is

indeed a land where the eternal waves surge in layers, a beautiful outlying land. I wish to dwell in this land." Because of this divine proclamation, Yamato-hime followed the divine decree, took the sacred mirror and sacred sword from the age of the gods, and enshrined them at Ujigami in Ise Province. That river is called both Mimosuso River and Isuzu River. This is the present Inner Shrine (Naiku). Later, in the twenty-second year of Emperor Yuryaku, the twenty-second emperor of the human imperial line, by divine oracle, Toyouke Daimyojin was invited from Yosa District in Tanba Province.

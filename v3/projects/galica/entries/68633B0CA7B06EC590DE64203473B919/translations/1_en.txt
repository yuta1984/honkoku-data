Dawn Birds at Takanawa 【enclosed in a square】
Snow on Mount Fuji Peak

As if a single crow
has dipped ink
on the underside
of the sleeves
of the misty robe
worn by
the Spring Goddess Saohime

Tsubosumirō
Narasuke

Kafunatei
Chizuna

With grated daikon radish
from Mount Fuji's wind,
creating sashimi
from rippling waves—
a great bowl of the sea

From this point forward, their intimate relationship became a marriage. Now, the great king who was the father of the demon country heard of this and said, "How detestable! In this invasion of the capital, we failed to capture even one person, and she has been reunited with the Middle Captain. How brazen! How detestable! Come now, let us cross over to Japan, and this time we shall capture both of them together for our revenge." With this shared resolve, they spread the word. When Tamonten of Kurama heard of this, there was a new divine oracle: "They have set out from the enemy country to attack Japan. If they invade this country even once, there will be no human race left in Japan. A righteous master should be employed to seal them. The gods and Buddhas of Japan have also heard this, and

if that is their intention, there should be an imperial seal." With this divine oracle, when this matter was reported to the Emperor, the Emperor heard it and, together with the imperial master Aishin, made a decree. According to the divination: "These demons will certainly cross to Japan on the night of Setsubun to capture people." Gather forty-nine thousand people from families under seven imperial masters, establish a ritual court at the monk's quarters in the depths of Kurama, confine them at the mouth of the cave and seal it, roast three koku and three to of beans, and strike saying "we strike the demons' eyes." The demons, having eighty-six eyes struck and crushed, returned, and those demons said "kakuban."

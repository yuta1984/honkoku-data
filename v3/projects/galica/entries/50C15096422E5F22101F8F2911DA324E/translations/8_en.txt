"All that follows is like dust, becoming like dreams and illusions." As she spoke eloquently in various ways, the Middle Captain said, "If that is so, then I shall follow your words in whatever way." And they wrung each other's sleeves. Then the Princess, weeping, accompanied the Middle Captain and hurried along the distant road through the night, emerging at Sōjō-ga-tani in the depths of Kurama. The Middle Captain too sobbed with tears, the path unclear. As this was to be their final parting, the Princess also returned in tears.

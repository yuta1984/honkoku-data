The Middle Captain, having heard this, said, "That young person has a close connection to Sadahira, I hear. She was found on the death anniversary of Konzume, from whom we parted empty-handed. Today is also the death anniversary of the Prince. You should perform sutra recitation for him right now. Tell stories about the Prince, always read sutras, and hold memorial services for the Prince." The princess, having heard this, said, "The Middle Captain is the master of my life, so I would like to come and meet him, but I feel embarrassed." The lady-in-waiting replied, "The Middle Captain is said to be a close acquaintance. Moreover, he is one who observes the Five Precepts, so what could be troublesome about it?"

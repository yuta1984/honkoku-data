Mitsumura and his clan harbored deep affection for Yoritsune, and in their arrogance, they conceived harmful intentions. In the twelfth month, Saneuji resigned from the position of Chancellor (Daijō-daijin). Inner Minister Minamoto no Michimitsu was appointed as Chancellor. Regent Sanetsune resigned from the position of Minister of the Left. Minister of the Right Fujiwara no Kanehira was appointed as Minister of the Left. Inner Minister Tadaie became Minister of the Right. Major Counselor Sanemoto became Inner Minister. Shogun Yoritsugu was elevated to Junior Fourth Rank Lower Grade. The Minor Captain remained in his former position.

In the first year of Kangen, in the first month, Ichijō Sanetsune stepped down as regent, and Konoe Kanetsune again became regent. In the second month, Yoritsugu and Tokiyori held a dog-chasing event (inuoumono). In the fourth month, the father of Akita-jō-no-suke Yoshikage, the lay monk Kagesada (known as Gakuchi), who had been at Mount Kōya, came to Kamakura and met with Tokiyori, secretly plotting to destroy Miura Yasumura.

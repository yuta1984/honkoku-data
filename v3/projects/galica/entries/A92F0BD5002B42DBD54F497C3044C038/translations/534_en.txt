In the fifth year, first month, former Naidaijin Minamoto no Tomamori passed away. In the seventh month, Hōjō Takatoki became Shikken to Shōgun Moriyoshi for the first time and appeared at the council seat. He was fourteen years old at this time. Hōjō Motoki resigned from the position of Shikken. In the tenth month, Konoe Udaijin Tsunehira transferred to the left [as Sadaijin], Dōin Naidaijin Sanetai was appointed to Udaijin, and Imadegawa Dainagon Kimiaki was appointed to Naidaijin.

In the first year of Bunpō [1317], third month, Takatoki was appointed Sagami-no-kami at the age of fifteen. Although it was said that he was not naturally suited to the capacity of Shikken, according to the precedent established since Yasutoki's time, Akita-jō-no-suke Tokiaki and Nagasaki Enki attempted to promote him. In the sixth month, Dōin Sanetai resigned from Udaijin. Imadegawa Naidaijin Kimiaki was appointed to Udaijin.

In the ninth month, Sadatoki built Saishōon-ji Temple and held a dedication ceremony, which Shōgun Prince Hisaaki attended. In the eleventh month, Tokudaiji Udaijin Kintaka was appointed Chancellor, Takatsukasa Naidaijin Tada no Tadafuyu was transferred to Udaijin, and Ichijō Dainagon Narizane was appointed Naidaijin.

In the tenth month of Kagen 1, Hōjō Mototoki returned from Kyoto to Kamakura. In the eleventh month, Hōjō Tokinori came up to the capital as his replacement. In this year, Sadatoki's son Takatoki was born. It is also said that he may have been born in Einin 1.

In the third month of the second year, Tokudaiji Kintaka resigned from the position of Chancellor. In the seventh month, Retired Emperor Go-Fukakusa passed away at the Tominokōji Palace at the age of sixty-two. Both Rokuhara Deputies, Sadaaki and Tokinori, came to the palace gates with their troops and sat on folding chairs in attendance. The funeral ceremony was held at Fushimi Palace. In the twelfth month, Ichijō Naidaijin Narizane died at the age of twenty.

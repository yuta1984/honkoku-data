In the second year, on the seventeenth day of the first month, Minamoto no Michimitsu resigned from the position of Chancellor (Daijō-daijin). He died the following day at the age of sixty-two. In the tenth month, Retired Emperor Go-Saga made an imperial visit to Uji to view the autumn leaves. At this time, since all major state affairs were being handled by the warrior government, the Retired Emperor made visits to various places for recreation and consolation. As the reigning Emperor was still young, he spent his days only in childish games.

In the first year of Kenchō, in the second month, the Kan'in Palace burned down. In the third month, more than half of the capital was destroyed by fire.

In the second year, in the third month, there was an imperial visit to Kumano. In the fourth month, Fujiwara no Sanemoto resigned as Minister of the Interior, and Kuga Dainagon Minamoto no Tomozane was appointed as Minister of the Interior. In the fifth month, Shogun Yoritsugu read the "Teihan" (Emperor's Model), with Kiyohara no Noritaka serving as lecturer. Tokiyori copied and presented the "Zhenguan Zhengyao" (Essentials of Government from the Zhenguan Era). In the twelfth month, Minamoto no Tomozane resigned as Minister of the Interior, and Dainagon Fujiwara no Michinaga was appointed as Minister of the Interior.

Nofuji-sō (Wild Wisteria Grass)

【Left Page】
Hana-myōga (Flowering Ginger)

【Dividing line】
【Heading, boxed section】Nofuji (Wild Wisteria) 【Wild Wisteria?】《Small text: April-May》
【Outside the box】The leaves are like wisteria, and the flowers also generally resemble them, blooming in clusters at each location. The flower axis does not extend, and the color is medium purple. Flowers appear in July.
【Dividing line】
【Heading, boxed section】Hana-myōga (Flowering Ginger)
Shukusha
【Outside the box】The flowers are white and bloom in a wheel pattern (arranged in a circle) with five petals. The leaves are like myōga-take (ginger shoots), and flowers appear in June. Also called yabu-myōga (bush ginger). The proper name is wa-no-shukusha (Japanese cardamom).

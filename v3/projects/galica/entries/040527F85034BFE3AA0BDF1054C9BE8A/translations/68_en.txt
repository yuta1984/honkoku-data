【Heading, Boxed Section】
Money mallow (Zeni-aoi)
Small mallow (Ko-aoi)
Rabbit mallow (To-aoi)
【Outside the box】The leaf shape resembles Chinese mallow (Kara-aoi) but is round. The flower size is like a coin, resembling cherry blossom flowers. Colors include white, light purple, and light crimson. Height is about three to four shaku.
However, flowers emerge from between the leaves, and the leaves are magnificent. Present in April and May.

【Heading, Boxed Section】
Beauty willow (Biyou-yanagi)
Golden thread peach (Kinshi-tou)
【Outside the box】Flowers are yellow like peach, with yellow stamens like thread. Leaves are long like willow. The wood is also thin like thread. Leaves spread horizontally on both sides, not extending vertically, creeping on the ground.
【Dividing line】
Golden thread peach (Kinshi-tou) flowers are like peach, with yellow whiskers in the center. Scattering petals outside the flower like golden thread. Also splits open from the root base to divide seeds. Flowers bloom in March and April.
【Dividing line】
【Heading, Boxed Section】Day lily (Kanzo) 【Outside the box】Flower color is crimson and turmeric, shaped like lily flowers. Leaves resemble sedge but are thick. Flowers in March.
【Dividing line】
【Heading, Boxed Section】Hydrangea (Ajisai)
【Outside the box】Branch stance resembles Japanese snowball, height five to six shaku. Leaves are paired oppositely and similar. Shape also resembles cherry with fine serrations. Flowers bloom in clusters, size of one peony bloom like Japanese snowball. Also has large and small varieties. Four petals, large ones are white in color, medium are light blue, small ones are purple and blue. Also has white, light crimson, and yellow. Flowers bloom in April and May.
【Dividing line】
【Heading, Boxed Section】
Mountain purple (Yama-murasaki)
【Outside the box】Resembles small beautyberry tree, with drooping branches and slightly yellow flowers. Five petals, blooms in April and May. Has fruit in autumn. Color is wisteria purple like clusters. All leaves are like wild chrysanthemum, attached oppositely.
【Dividing line】
【Heading, Boxed Section】Autumn mallow (Aki-aoi) 【Outside the box】Also called yellow hibiscus or mucilage grass. Flower shape same as May mallow.

【Left Page】
Leaf shape is elongated like monkshood or hemp leaves. Leaves come in large and small sizes. Large ones are over one shaku. Stems are long, leaf edges are pointed, and some have serrations while others don't.
However, the form is refreshing. Flowers are yellowish-white with deep purple brilliance at the bottom. The center core is the same as hibiscus. Flowers have a sac covering, with five platforms entangling outside. Though called autumn mallow, it blooms from summer's dog days through July and August. Height is about six to seven shaku. ○There are many types of mallow. Yellow-flowered ones are called Shu-ki. Small-flowered ones are called money mallow or Chinese mallow. Red-flowered ones are called crimson mallow. ○Leans toward the sun. "Shuowen": Yellow mallow constantly tilts its leaves toward the sun, not letting it shine on its roots. ○Does this refer to sunflower? ○Protects the base. "Zuo Zhuan": "Mallow still can protect its base."
【Dividing line】
Autumn mallow (Shu-ki), also called yellow hibiscus. "Shuowen": Yellow mallow constantly tilts leaves toward the sun, not letting it shine on its roots. Each has poetic composition. When flowers are about to fall, use chopsticks to take them, soak in vegetable oil to treat burns - very effective.
【Dividing line】
Collected Explanations
○Yu Xi says: Yellow hibiscus exists everywhere in nearby regions. Springs forth seedlings in spring, leaves quite resemble hibiscus but are pointed, small with many notches. Opens flowers at summer's end, light yellow color. Harvest in June-July and dry in shade. ○Zong Shi says: Yellow hibiscus and hibiscus are different species, not the yellow variety among hibiscus. Below the leaf center is purple sandalwood color. Pick and scatter to dry in sun.

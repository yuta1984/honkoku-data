Those who know my form shall attain Buddhahood in this very body, shall attain Buddhahood in this very body
So saying, [the monk] invoked the vajra binding of the Wisdom King and pressed his attack, intensely
praying her into submission. Now be chastened by this!
【Shite exits】Until now she had been a demon woman
manifesting such fierce anger, but suddenly
she became completely weakened, and wrapped her body in heaven and earth

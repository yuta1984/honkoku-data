1. Messengers from Lord Toda Uneme-no-kami - Toda Gengobyōe and Uemura Shichirōemon - arrived in Akō on the 28th day of the 3rd month. A letter from Lord Uneme-no-kami and a copy of written instructions conveyed from Lord Tsuchiya Sagami-no-kami.

"Regarding the recent matter concerning Asano Takumi-no-kami, as Lord Tsuchiya Sagami-no-kami has ordered that this be communicated to each of you, I am conveying this through these two messengers. Therefore, you are to come to the town residence and acknowledge receipt of this message.

18th day, 3rd month　Toda Uneme-no-kami [seal]

To: Asano Takumi-no-kami
Senior Retainers
Personal Attendants
Inspectors"

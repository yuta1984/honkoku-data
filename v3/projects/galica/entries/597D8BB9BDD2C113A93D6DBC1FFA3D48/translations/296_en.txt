Kuranosuke would not agree. He said "We must by all means wait for a good opportunity" and held off. Perhaps because of this their courage waned - among those who had resolved to die in Kamigata and came together to Edo, some became separated from parents, children, and brothers, developed cowardly hearts, and two or three fled back to Kamigata during the eleventh month.

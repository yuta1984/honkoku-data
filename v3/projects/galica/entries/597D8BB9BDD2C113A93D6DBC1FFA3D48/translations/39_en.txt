○ First, the wife was taken into the care of Shikibu-shōyū-sama during the night of the 14th, and her belongings were sent to be stored at a townsman's residence in Fukagawa that was borrowed for this purpose. Regarding Takumi-no-kami-sama's belongings, an inquiry was made, and they were to be disposed of according to official orders, so they were placed in the mansion's storehouse. Later, it was determined that the belongings were of no concern, and the matter was settled under the supervision of Lord Asano Mino-no-kami.

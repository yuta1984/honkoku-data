When asked whether this matter should definitely be reported to the metsuke (magistrates), [they replied that] if there were any reasonable intentions behind this matter, they would postpone reporting to the metsuke and return home. In any case, they conveyed that they would give their acceptance only after viewing Lord Uneme-no-kami's letter.

June 11th　Eizaiji Temple　Dōmei [with personal seal]
To Lord Ōishi Kuranosuke
To all the people

1. Copy of certificate for safekeeping of support funds for Enrinji Temple
Regarding the money deposited:
One item: Fifty ryō in gold　Exchange rate: fifty-five monme per ryō
Equivalent silver: two kan seven hundred fifty me
I have truly received the above silver in safekeeping. Whenever needed, I shall return it.
Even if there are any missing parties among the co-signers, there shall be no difference whatsoever in the matter of repayment. Therefore

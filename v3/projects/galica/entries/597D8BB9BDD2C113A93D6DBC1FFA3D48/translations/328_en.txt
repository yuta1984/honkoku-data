As described above, groups of three to four people each were organized, and it was agreed that the assault and retreat would be carried out together.

○ Item: Everyone made preparations and gathered at the rented houses of Honjō and Horiabe Yasubē【Left note: Azukiya Zenbē】and Sugino Jūheiji【Left note: Kiriuri Gohē】respectively. At both locations, they changed into their battle gear and arranged signals, then departed at the upper part of the hour of the Tiger (around 4 AM), and divided into two groups as planned near Lord Kōzuke-no-suke's mansion. Among the front gate attack force, Ōtaka Gengo and Hazama Jūjirō both rushed forward, seized two ladders that had been placed at the townhouses in front of the mansion for fire prevention, set them against the wall, and these two men achieved the first assault.
【The young men were caught off guard as they did not notice that these two men had removed the ladders. There were various issues regarding these ladders, but details are uncertain】

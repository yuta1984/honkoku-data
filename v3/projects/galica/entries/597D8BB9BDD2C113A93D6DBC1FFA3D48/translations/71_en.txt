17th day of the 3rd month　　　　Tsuchiya Sagami-no-kami
　　　　　　To Toda Uneme-no-shō
　　　　Furthermore, Awaji-no-kami will also serve as a stationed guard. The above

○ We all went to the lodgings of the aforementioned two envoys and examined the written documents. Accordingly, we acknowledge receipt as follows:
　　As written below
　　　　Regarding the matter of Takumi this time, through written documents from
　　　　Tsuchiya Sagami-no-kami-sama and Uneme-no-shō-sama, we have been given instructions.
　　　　We have examined the content of the aforementioned written documents, and through the two envoys
　　　　we have been ordered to humbly acknowledge and comply, which we respectfully accept. The above

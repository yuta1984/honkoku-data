Tagawa Kuzaemon, Sakayori Yuiemon, Kimura Magoemon, Tanaka Rokurōzaemon, Matsushita Shingozaemon, Hashimoto Jibei, Iguchi Hanzō, Tsuchida Saburōemon, Ōtsuka Tōbei, Ikise Jūzaemon, Miwa Kibei, Tanaka Daiemon, and Maeno Shinzō. These are the men determined to die this time.

Satomura Ban'emon, Tanaka Joemon, Kaji Hanzaemon, and Kondō Shingo. Among these, many went out to Kyoto, but regarding Tsuchida Saburōemon and Ikise Jūzaemon: they departed for Kyoto without delay, but upon hearing of the aforementioned matter, they made gestures [of alarm] and fled back. This I heard from Kuranosuke's account here at this place. Ha ha ha! These are matters concerning men of little consequence, so we need not be greatly surprised.

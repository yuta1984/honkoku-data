Isobe Jūrōzaemon and Tanaka Sadashirō also conveyed this, and everyone agreed, so this was reported to Kuranosuke Sōemon, but he would not consent. Therefore, Gengorōemon, Jūrōzaemon, and Genshirō severed relations. Later, when Sadashirō fell ill, Jūrōzaemon went to Gengorōemon again and made various apologies to Kuranosuke, joining the group.

Memorandum

One: About 2-3 squad leaders　　One: About 5 senior leaders
One: 1 steward　　　　　　　　One: 1 inspector
One: 1-2 estate inspection officers　One: Small contingent of hall guards

It was ordered that the above six articles should be announced, and that guards at various locations should also be reduced by half - where there are ten men, reduce to five; where there are five, reduce to two or three. Regarding the clause at the end concerning guards at various locations, where it mentions five assistant leaders for guidance, it was ordered that ten men should be assigned to this duty.

One: Officials from Katō should be summoned to the castle town, and regarding their withdrawal, it should be at their own discretion. First, this intention should be communicated by official order. The details will be given after arrival tomorrow.

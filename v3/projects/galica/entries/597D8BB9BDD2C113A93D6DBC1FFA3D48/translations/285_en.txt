○On the 22nd day of the 7th month, Lord Daigaku was placed under the custody of Lord Matsudaira Suruga-no-kami at the upper residence in Kobiki-chō 4th district.

○At the Mukai residence, the entrance guards, pages, and gate guards were increased, and foot soldiers were also added.

○As attendants to serve Lord Daigaku, Senior Inspector Matsumiya Gorōbei and Archery Captain Nishiyama Kan'emon were assigned.

○Regarding Lord Daigaku's travel route and residence arrangements, when Lord Aki-no-kami made inquiries, the government replied that there were no restrictions, and he could proceed as he wished.

○ Kayano Sanpei has an admirable character and excellent natural qualities. His father is called Kayano Shichirōemon, a rōnin who withdrew to a place called Kayano in the domain of Lord Ōshima Ise-no-kami in Settsu Province, where he works as a farmer. In the summer of the year of the Snake, he joined Kuranosuke's group at Akō, but after the castle was handed over and the retainer band was disbanded, he withdrew to his father Shichirōemon's place. Occasionally he also goes out to Yamashina and Osaka, waiting for the right moment. Now, since Shichirōemon is not an ordinary farmer, he is on intimate terms with the domain lord, and when Sanpei was previously summoned by Lord Takumi-no-kami, it was arranged through this Lord Ise-no-kami's recommendation.

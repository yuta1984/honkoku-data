One item: A memorandum from those in custody
　　Regarding Ōishi Chikara, since Lord Oki-no-kami had met him only once and had no recollection of his character, he requested through an intermediary that he would like to become acquainted with him. Chikara responded: "As I am a young man, my foolish father instructed me thus: 'Until we assault Lord Kōzuke-no-suke's mansion, we must naturally maintain a resolve to die. Depending on how things proceed, we may commit seppuku at the mansion itself. If not, should Lord Danjō provide reinforcement with his men, depending on circumstances, we may fight to the death regardless of location. If not, having achieved our purpose and withdrawing safely to Sengaku-ji temple, we may commit seppuku there. That too would be..."

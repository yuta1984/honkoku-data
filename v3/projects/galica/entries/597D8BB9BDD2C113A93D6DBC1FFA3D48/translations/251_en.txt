○ Article: On May 6th, Toda Uneme-no-shō's house confinement was pardoned. Lord Asano Mino-no-kami was relieved of his official duties. Lord Asano Sahyōe was to continue serving in his position as before.

○ Article: On May 24th, Lord Araki Jūzaemon and Lord Sakakibara Uneme returned to Edo from Akō.

Death poem upon committing suicide　　Dharma name: Sekishin-in Myōren Nisshō Shinja
　　Before I even realize this is reality, I awaken from a dream
　　　And shall ride upon the flower of the wondrous Dharma
　　Though urged to hurry because wife and children await
　　　Why should I leave my heart attached to this world?
One: In the 9th month of Genroku 16, year of the sheep, Lord Uesugi Danjō-daihitsu retired as he wished, and the family headship was duly granted to his heir, Lord Minbu-dayu

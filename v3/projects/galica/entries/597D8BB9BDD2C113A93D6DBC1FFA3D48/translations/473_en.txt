1. Hara Sōemon, from the beginning, had agreed with Kuranosuke of Akō to commit seppuku at Kagakuji Temple as one of the six men who had made this determination. While four of them changed their minds, this man remained unchanged and maintained his resolve throughout. Even when the debate over whether to defend the castle remained undecided, he alone stepped forward and spoke difficult words among the retainers, encouraging them all. His achievements were outstanding.

This work has been printed on luxury Japanese paper.

150 copies on Tori-no-ko paper (extra quality), Nos. 1 to 150.
200 copies on Hô-sho paper...... Nos. 151 to 350.
350 copies, numbered.

The text of these three hundred and fifty copies had to be printed on Hô-sho paper, as the Tori-no-ko paper was too thick to be folded. Japanese paper, due to its composition, can only be printed on one side.

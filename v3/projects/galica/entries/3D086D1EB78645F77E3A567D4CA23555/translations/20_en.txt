The Assistant Governor, weeping bitterly, tried to get up and throw himself into the water, but his eyes grew dim and his heart seemed to fade away, and though his legs could barely support him properly, he finally managed to come out and saw it was the Governor of Harima. "What has happened? Are you here alone?" he asked, and the reply came, "Lord Chūjō has also entered here." Upon hearing this, he could not speak at all and simply wept without cease. "Why, when we have joyfully met again, what is this matter?" he asked, and the response was only "Ah, how heartbreaking," and he seemed about to faint.

11 Have you been to Nagasaki often?
12 No, I came here for the first time this year.
13 Did you come alone?
14 Yes, I have nobody with me.
15 Do you have children?
16 Yes, I have two, one of them is a girl.
17 Then why didn't you bring your children with you?
18 Because they are still too young.
19 What are their ages?
20 The boy is thirteen, the girl eleven years old.
21 Have you seen everything?
22 Yes, I have been to all the quarters (of the town) and the temples, particularly the Dutch and Chinese ones. I am very pleased with it, because it enables me to give an account of my journey.
23 Nagasaki offers nothing extraordinary, except for the non-arrival this year of Dutch ships and Chinese junks.
24 At what time do the Dutch ships leave Batavia?
25 They sail from Batavia in the month of June, and arrive here about the end of July or in the beginning of August.
26 In what month do they leave from here?
27 In the month of October.
28 What goods do the ships take in for Batavia?
29 They take chiefly copper and camphor besides other articles in return.
30 From where do the copper and camphor come?
31 The copper comes from the Province of Akita, the camphor from that of Satsuma.

When the Emperor heard of this matter, he sent an imperial messenger to the home of the bamboo cutter. The bamboo cutter came out to meet the imperial messenger and wept without limit. From grieving over this matter, his beard had turned white, his back had bent, and his eyes had become swollen. The old man was about fifty years old this year, but due to his worry, he appeared to have aged in just a short time.

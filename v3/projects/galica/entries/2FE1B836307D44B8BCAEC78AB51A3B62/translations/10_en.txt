Ferdinand became king of his country himself, but Bonaparte seized this opportunity to attack and defeat him, taking Ferdinand captive and carrying him away to France where he was imprisoned. He then advanced and attacked Portugal. The king was unable to defend against this and fled to Brazil with his wife and children.
《Annotation: Brazil belongs to the South American continent and was a territory that the Portuguese had previously annexed and possessed》

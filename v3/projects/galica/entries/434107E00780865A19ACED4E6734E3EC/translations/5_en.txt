What a detestable thing! Everything happens according to fate, they said with joy. The still young younger brother thought deeply in his heart: Alas, he had such profound feelings for the princess, yet how sad it is that the Chūjō has forgotten her so completely. What a frightening heart he has, the brother thought, wetting his sleeves with tears.

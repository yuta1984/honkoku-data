In recent years, publications called "meisho zue" (illustrated guides to famous places) have become popular in the world. Not only those of the Gokinai (five provinces around the capital) but also those of other provinces have been published one after another and are celebrated by society. This is because there is pleasure in being able to learn in detail about the conditions and historical origins of places one has never visited, all while remaining at home.

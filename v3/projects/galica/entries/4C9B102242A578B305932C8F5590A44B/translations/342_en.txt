Hazu Shrine 《Small text: Located at the summit of Hazu Cape, enshrines Takeinadane-no-mikoto, commonly called Hazu Cape Hachiman》This is the shrine listed as Hazu Shrine in the Engishiki Jinmyōchō and as Senior First Rank Hazu Myōjin in the Honkokuchō. According to tradition, a man of the Owari clan called Shisuke lived in this area, so he built this shrine to worship his ancestral deity Takeinadane-no-mikoto.

Also, the Kojiki states "Ameoshitarashihiko-no-mikoto was the ancestor of the Chita-no-omi," and the Shinsen Shōjiroku records "Hatsuka-no-obito are descendants of Hikoubatsu-no-mikoto, son of Ametarashihiko-hikokunioshiiri-no-mikoto." The Honkokuchō Shūsetsu suggests this might be the deity being worshipped here.

Miroku Temple

The matter principally concerns nurturing one's parents
In the deep forest where joy is shared, who
Planted these rows of seeds? The fragrant orchids
Send forth their scent - one should wait many years
Do not depart, do not regard these as
Ordinary flowers and plants

　　Shōen

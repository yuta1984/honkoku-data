Nagoya Castle, magnificent and grand, surpasses the three capitals,
Its multi-story tower with golden shachihoko enters into paintings,
The land lies close, adjoining the mystical Penglai islands,
Where immortal gentlemen proceed gracefully along paths of five-colored clouds.

　　　　　Kinryū Dōjin (Golden Dragon Hermit)

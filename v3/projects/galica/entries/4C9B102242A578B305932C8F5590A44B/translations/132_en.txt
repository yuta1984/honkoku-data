The comings and goings of elegant and common pleasure-seekers never cease, and this autumn spectacle is truly the finest in the domain.

Zuiunzan Dōsen-ji Temple [located on the east side of Nakaitchiba street in Furuwatari, Sōtō sect, branch temple of Hōji-ji in Atsuta, originally called Gyokusen-ji but renamed in Kanbun 7 (1667)] Main image [Thousand-armed Kannon, work of Eshin Sōzu, a wooden statue revered for generations from Minamoto no Yorinobu to Right General Yoritomo.

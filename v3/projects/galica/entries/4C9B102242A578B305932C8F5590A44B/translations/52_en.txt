Toshogu Shrine 《Small print: enshrined in the third bailey within the castle grounds》　The national founder Source Kei-ko (referring to Tokugawa Ieyasu) invited the great monk Tenkai 《Small print: whose religious name was Nankobo》and founded this shrine on September 17, 5th year of Genna, year of Tsuchinoto-Hitsuji (1619).
In the center is enshrined the divine image of the late Grand Minister of State, Senior First Rank, Source Asomi Ieyasu-ko, with Sanno Gongen on the left and Nikko Gongen on the right as subsidiary deities.
○Main shrine 《Small print: facing south》 ritual hall, connecting hall, middle gate, sacred fence, offering hall, bell tower, portable shrine hall, treasure house, sacred well, torii gate, tower gate - all exhibit the finest beauty, some decorated with vermillion powder and mineral pigments, others inlaid with gold, silver, copper, and iron, creating such magnificent splendor that words cannot adequately describe it.
○Honji-do Hall 《Small print: houses statues of Yakushi Buddha and three attendant bodhisattvas, twelve divine generals, and four heavenly kings》 Goma Hall 《Small print: wooden statues of Fudo and the four heavenly kings, twelve devas》

When Yuanbin was staying at Kokushoji Temple in Azabu, Edo, there were three ronin named Fukuno Shichiro Uemon, Isogai Jiro Zaemon, and Miura Yoji Uemon who were also lodging at the same temple. Yuanbin told them, "In the Ming Dynasty (China), there are techniques for capturing people. Let me show you these skills." The three men eventually perfected their training and mastered these techniques. This became the Kitō-ryū jujutsu.

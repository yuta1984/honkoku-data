Kamezaki

This place is a harbor, and it is a fishing village of over one thousand households. Since it is particularly prosperous and wealthy, there are also many wealthy families. The views are especially expansive, with hundreds and thousands of scenic views of mountains and islands arrayed before one's eyes. Looking far to the south, one can see as far as Sakushima Island in Mikawa Province and Himakajima Island in this province, creating an unparalleled

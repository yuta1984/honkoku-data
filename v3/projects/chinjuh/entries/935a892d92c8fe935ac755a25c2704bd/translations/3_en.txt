Long ago, there lived an old man named Mizuemon who made his living by training animals to perform tricks. He kept dogs, cats, mice, monkeys, raccoon dogs - whatever animals he could find - and specialized in teaching them various skills. He was also fond of birds and insects, keeping many of them as well.

Now, behind this Mizuemon's house stood a large persimmon tree that bore abundant fruit. One of his pet monkeys would climb this persimmon tree and secretly steal and eat the persimmons.

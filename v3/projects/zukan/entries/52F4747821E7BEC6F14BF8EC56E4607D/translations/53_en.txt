Kiji (pheasant)
　Pheasant (chi)
　The taste is sweet and sour, of warm nature with no toxicity. It supplements weakness and increases vitality. People with cold-deficiency constitution will benefit from eating it (sick people should not eat it). The meat has excellent flavor and is served at fine dining tables. However, it should be eaten in autumn and winter, which is beneficial. From spring onward, it becomes fishy, lacks fat, and does not taste good. Moreover, it becomes toxic and should not be eaten.

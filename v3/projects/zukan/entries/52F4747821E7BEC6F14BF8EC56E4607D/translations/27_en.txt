Toki (Japanese Crested Ibis)
　朱鷺 (shuro) 《from Qinjing》　紅鶴 (koukaku) 《from Wang Ying's Shiwu Bencao》
　The taste is sweet and slightly warm in nature, with no toxicity. It treats women's blood disorders. However, the meat quality is poor and
　often causes small sores to develop.

Hakugan (White goose)

　Its nature and taste are the same as the wild goose. The taste is mild with little fat, making it excellent.
　In the eighth month of autumn, hakugan (white geese) arrive first, followed by karigane (wild geese), and magan (true geese)
　arrive last. At the end of the second month of spring, magan (true geese) return first, and by the third and fourth months,
　hakugan (white geese) return last.

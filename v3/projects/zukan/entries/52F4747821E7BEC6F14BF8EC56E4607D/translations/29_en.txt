Aosagi (Grey Heron)
　Sōro (Grey Heron) 《Side note: Cui Yuxizhi | Shijing (Dietary Classic)》
　The nature, taste, and efficacy are the same as the heron. However, the taste is most sweet and delicious. It has the effect of stopping perspiration and
　promoting urination.

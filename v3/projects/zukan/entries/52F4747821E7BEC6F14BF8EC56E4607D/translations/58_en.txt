Hibari (skylark)
　Tenryū (celestial lark)　Kōtenshi (one who announces to heaven) 《Reference note: Senkaku Ruisho | Jiangyin County Gazetteer》
　Its nature and taste are generally the same as quail. The taste is sweet and warming, with no toxicity.

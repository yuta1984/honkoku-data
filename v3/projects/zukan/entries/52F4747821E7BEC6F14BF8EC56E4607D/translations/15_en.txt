Ahiru (Duck) - Also called ahiro
　Boku (duck)
　The taste is sweet and has cooling properties with no toxicity. It supplements bodily weakness, removes heat, and treats
　convulsions and malnutrition in children. Duck eggs have the same efficacy. Sick people should avoid eating it.
　As food, white duck is considered the finest quality.

Hato (pigeon/dove)
　Pigeon (kyū), dove (gafu)
　The taste is salty and of neutral nature with no toxicity. There are distinctions between domestic pigeons and wild pigeons, but all have the same properties and can be eaten. They effectively treat malignant sores and skin diseases such as ringworm and eczema.

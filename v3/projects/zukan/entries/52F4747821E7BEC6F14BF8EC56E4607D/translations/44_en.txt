U (Cormorant)
　Luzi (Chinese reading for cormorant)
　The taste is sour and salty, cooling to the body, with slight toxicity. The meat treats abdominal distension, facilitates urination, and treats fish bones stuck in the throat.
　There are two types: river and sea varieties. For eating, the sea variety is considered superior. The river variety has poor taste and is inedible.

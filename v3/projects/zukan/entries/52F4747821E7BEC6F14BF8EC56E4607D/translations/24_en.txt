Kaitsuburi (Little grebe) - This is the name used in Kyoto. In the Kanto region, it is called "meguricho."
　鸊鷉 (hekitei)
　The taste is sweet and warm in nature, with no toxicity. It supplements the internal organs and benefits qi. When boiled and eaten,
　the taste has a fishy odor and is not delicious. When grilled and eaten, it is delicious. For people suffering from anal fistula or rectal prolapse,
　eating this has effects as if divine.

○ Sekiran (Stone Orchid)
Flower form is like an orchid
and turmeric-colored. The base rises
two to three sun above
　　　　the soil
taking the form of a rock
growing in clumps
　from the sides of the clump
new shoots emerge
　　annually
　　and later
form cave-like shapes
　therefore
　　　it is also called
　　　　　Ganseki-ran (Rock Orchid)
　From between
　　the clumped stems
　　　flowers emerge
　　　　　　and bloom
Blooms from late April to May
　　　Leaves remain even in winter
○ There are two varieties: one with turmeric color and another
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　with reddish beni-ukon color

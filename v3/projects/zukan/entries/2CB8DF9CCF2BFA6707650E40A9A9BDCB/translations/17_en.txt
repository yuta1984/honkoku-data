○ Ryūkyū Yuri-sō (Ryukyu Lily Grass)

The flowers are white
　facing sideways
　　　long and narrow
　　　　　　in bloom
The leaves
　　emerge in September
　　　and remain through winter
　　　　It could be called
　　"Winter Lily"
　　The flowers bloom in April and May

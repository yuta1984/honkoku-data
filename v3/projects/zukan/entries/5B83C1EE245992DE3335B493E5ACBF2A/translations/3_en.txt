1. The field of herbalism (honzō) is originally extremely vast in scope. When it comes to the properties and toxicity of various plants, it becomes increasingly difficult to investigate thoroughly. However, among the commonly found plants and trees, there are many that definitely contain poison yet remain unknown to people.

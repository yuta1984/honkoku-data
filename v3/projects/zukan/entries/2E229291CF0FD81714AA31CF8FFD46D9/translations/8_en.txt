【Right Page】
▲Kuchibeni (Lipstick)
　　　Light red colored flowers with
　　　red-stained edges,
　　　resembling wheel flowers.
　　　In summer the color turns blue
　　　and doesn't scatter

【Left Page】
▲Miyoshino
　　Also called Kōshin
　　　White base with crimson and
　　　light colors scattered throughout,
　　　blooming in variegated patterns,
　　　medium-sized flowers in various colors

▲Nijū-murasaki (Double Purple)
　　　Deep purple medium-sized flowers,
　　　double-flowered variety

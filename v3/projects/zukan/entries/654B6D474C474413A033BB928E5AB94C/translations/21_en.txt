The Enlarged Tashikihen, Vine Plants section states:
Monthly season flower (gekki)　Japanese name　Upon current examination: evergreen　Kokan's Saihoku-shū says "This flower blooms
　　　　constantly through the four seasons, hence it is named 'monthly flower,' and is commonly called 'evergreen' today"
　　Supplementary alternative names　Monthly flower ○ Victory spring ○ Thin guest ○ Snow-fighting red

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Honzō Ikka-gen states "Monthly succession flower is the same
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　as evergreen monthly plum flower"

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Copied again in the 5th month of Mizunoe-uma year

Thorny bramble flower　Rose　From the Chikkin-shō
　　　　Begonia rose

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　True copy made in the 5th month of Mizunotō-hitsuji year

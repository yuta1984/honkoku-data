[Golden Coin Flower (Kinsenka) 《marginal note: 《marginal note: also》 Golden Cup Flower (Kinsanka) | also called》]
The flower shape is like a chrysanthemum, with single, double, and thousand-petaled varieties. The flowers bloom in mid-season and open immediately without delay. The leaves resemble nine-star grass. Flowers bloom in all four seasons, with colors of red, yellow, vermillion, leaf-color, and yellow-blue that are light. Length is about one shaku. They bloom from around August-September, with peak flowering in spring March. These names Golden Cup Flower and Golden Coin Flower are also Chinese names. According to the Eight Collections Painting Manual's Plant Compendium, Golden Coin Flower is a colloquial name, while Golden Cup Flower is so called because it resembles such a cup. Golden Coin Flower refers to the noon-time flower. Golden Coin Flower is called "noon-time" because it blooms at the hour of the horse (noon). Red ones are called child-noon flowers, yellow ones are called golden coin flowers. Also, the original name of Golden Cup Flower is lettuce flower 《marginal note: also》 golden cup grass 《marginal note: also》 eternal spring chrysanthemum.

【vertical rule】
Eternal Spring Chrysanthemum: Common name is Huihui chrysanthemum. Has flowers in all four seasons. Even when planted between hedges and fences, it can naturally be admired.

【vertical rule】
[Cold Chrysanthemum] Flowers are yellow. The flower center is yellow and tubular stamens. Raised with white petals. Single and sparse. Leaves grow thick and turn red like crimson from October, lasting until December. Flower color is like gold. Also, narcissus chrysanthemum flowers are single with white petals that shine blue. Leaf color is gray on the back, blue-green on top. Present in September-October, called winter chrysanthemum.

[Mandala Flower]
Grows from seed in spring, shaped like eggplant seedlings, with branches emerging in stages, leaves also like eggplant. Therefore, its alternate name is mountain eggplant. Flowers bloom in autumn, white colored, large blooms. Flower shape is like morning glory but robust and strangely formed, so commonly called "foreign flute." Most aptly named for its shape. Planted in flower beds and called Korean morning glory. Opens in morning and closes in evening. ○ Shizhen says: Mandala flower grows seedlings in spring in human dwellings, grows tall in summer. Single stem rises straight, height four to five shaku. Leaves and stem like eggplant. White flowers open in August, lasting until September. Leaves are large like morning glory flowers. Opens in morning and closes in evening. Both wood and stem are black-red. Fruit is round with thorns.

【vertical rule】
[Large Yatsushiro Grass 《marginal note: August-September | blooms》] Flowers resemble Yatsushiro grass. Like bellflower with similar color. Flowers bloom at crossroads. Each leaf bears flowers. Resembles gentian. Later than Yatsushiro grass. Found in mountains.

[Autumn Peony 《marginal note: from August》
Kifune Chrysanthemum 《marginal note: September》
Kaga Chrysanthemum 《marginal note: until | flowers present》]
Leaf shape resembles chrysanthemum leaves but with shallow cuts and hard texture. Flowers also like chrysanthemums, purple-red colored. Double and single blooms, also white flowers exist. Both flowers and leaves are the same. Also called Kaga chrysanthemum. True chrysanthemums have veined petals, but this chrysanthemum has petals without veins. Like peonies, flowers open with stamens surrounding the fruit center. Like single poppies with poppy heads.

【Left page】
Rice (tō)　ine
　　　Glutinous rice (dabei)
　　　　mochi rice《Wamyōshō》

　The rice recorded in classical texts and historical works
　is a general term for two types: glutinous rice (da) and non-glutinous rice (kō).
　The rice recorded in medical and pharmacological texts
　refers to glutinous rice. Varieties with awns are called
　chōshūda《Jusshi Tsūkō》, and those without awns are called
　kashōda.

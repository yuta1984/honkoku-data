Buckwheat (kyōhaku)　sobamuki《marginal note: Wamyōshō》　kuromuki《marginal note: same as above》　soba
　　　　Fagopyrum《marginal note: Latin》　Boekweit《marginal note: Dutch》

　This is made into noodles for food consumption. Seeds are sown in the 6th month and harvested in the 9th month. The stem
　grows to about 2 shaku in height. The 4-5 sun above the root is deep red in color. The leaves are triangular and alternately arranged. In autumn, branches divide at the tips
　and small white flowers cluster densely. Later it bears fruit with three ridges. One variety called sando-
　soba ripens three times in one year. The fruit is large with high ridges, resembling caltrop seeds
　but without thorns.

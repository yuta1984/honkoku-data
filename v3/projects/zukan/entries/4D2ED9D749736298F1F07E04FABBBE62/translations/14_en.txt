Fujiougi (Fujiwaugi)
Found in Mount Fuji in Suruga Province. The stem is vine-like, and the roots are white, hard, and have a deep flavor. It produces pale yellow flowers and forms angular seed pods. There is one variety that has purple flowers. Both the seedlings and roots are small. Both types belong to the wood astragalus family and are of inferior quality.

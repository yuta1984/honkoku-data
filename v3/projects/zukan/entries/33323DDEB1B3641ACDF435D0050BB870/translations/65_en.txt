○Igensei says: It has thousands of petals and is white in color. They cluster and droop at the tips of branches. This is the Ōtemari variety with drooping branches.
○Donnei says: The flower form stands upright, and regarding color, when in bud it is dark, but when it opens it becomes remarkably pale. It has three to four layers of large petals. This is also called Ōchōchin (Large Lantern).

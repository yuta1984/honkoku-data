Having doubts about this, I visited various places to investigate, and found that Shibayama cherry and Washinoo cherry are completely the same plant with two different names. This tree is said to either originate from the Shibayama family or to grow abundantly in Shibayama. However, it is difficult to classify as either double or single petaled. Leaves emerge as the flowers begin to bloom, and when the leaves are fully developed, the flowers scatter.

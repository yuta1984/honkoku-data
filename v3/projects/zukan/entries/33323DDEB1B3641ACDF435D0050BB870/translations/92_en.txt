Kishi-zakura (Shore Cherry)　　　　　　　　　　by Shun'e
Even on days when the wind is harsh and I pass through the sunny straits, am I not blessed by the shore cherry?

Waka-zakura (Young Cherry)　　　　　　　　　　by Akisue
The young cherry I planted, digging beyond the roots to see the flowers, seems to have bloomed while the wind was still

The above

○Types that have cherry names but are not true cherries

○Shide-zakura　Also called Zaifuri
In the Book of Songs (Shijing), called Tōtei, also Fui

○Chōji-zakura (Clove Cherry)　Also called Shi-genji
In the Compendium of Materia Medica, called Genka

○Niwa-zakura (Garden Cherry)　In the Agricultural Compendium of Six Books, called Kibai

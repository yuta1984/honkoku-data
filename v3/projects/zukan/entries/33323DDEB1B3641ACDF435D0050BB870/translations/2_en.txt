Famous trees with master poets speaking
　　　　　　　　around them
　A clear resolution shows on the face—
　　　　wisdom at the tip of the nose
　　　　Shizen-ken Donzen　【Seal　Orchid】

Among the flowers of grasses and trees in our country, those worthy of love
are exceedingly numerous. However, when it comes to cherry blossoms, we need not even name them—
if we simply say "flower," the true flower of all under heaven is solely
the cherry blossom. That its fame is so established can be known without even borrowing the character for "cherry."
That it is so beloved and treasured is thus, and as a flower...

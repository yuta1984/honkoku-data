Kaba-zakura (Birch Cherry)
[Illustration]
○Igan-sai says: The flower form resembles Kiritani and is double-flowered. It is turmeric-colored with wrinkled petals. It is also called Ki-zakura (Yellow Cherry). It is birch-tea colored. Commonly and mistakenly confused with Asagi-zakura. It is not light yellow.

Kaba-zakura (Birch Cherry)
[Illustration]
○Igan-sai says: It is called the white birch tree. Its bark is dried and used as torches. The fire does not go out even in rain. Dishonest merchants use this smoke to fumigate new paintings to make them resemble old paintings. This is recorded in the Bencao Gangmu (Compendium of Materia Medica).

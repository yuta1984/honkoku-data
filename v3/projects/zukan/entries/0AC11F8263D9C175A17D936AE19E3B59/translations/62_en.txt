【Right Bottom】
Ki shimeji (Yellow shimeji)

An illustration from the Shin'yō Kinpu (Shin'yō Mushroom Compendium). Both the cap and stem are yellow in color, the taste is inferior, and it is considered to be of low quality.

【Left Page】
Aka shimeji (Red shimeji)

An illustration from the Kinshi (Mushroom History). According to the same book, it is pale red in color with short stems, and the taste is inferior.

【Right Page】
Matsukawatake
　　Karatake

This is a figure recorded in "Shin'yō Kinfu." The same book states that it is commonly called kawatake, and those growing in nearby mountains have a sweet and delicious taste and are considered high grade.

【Left Page】
Tsukagawatake

This is a figure recorded in "Shin'yō Kinfu." The same book states that those growing in deep mountains are black in color, with thin flesh that has a bitter taste, and are considered low grade.

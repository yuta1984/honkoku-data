[Spring Orchid (shunran)
Mountain Orchid (sanran)  
Secluded Orchid (yuuran)
National Fragrance (kokkō)]
The flower has one bloom per stem. The flower's fragrance is the same as the Kei orchid, and the color is slightly yellow. The leaves are also the same as the Kei orchid. The flower has distinct sheaths, and the sheath colors are white, pale yellow, and blue.
The stem is pale blue-purple. In the "Zuo Zhuan" it says: "National Fragrance - in a dream, an orchid was given to Yan Ji, a concubine of Duke Wen of Zheng." The orchid has national fragrance and captivates people's eyes. Flowers bloom in March.
【Vertical rule line】
[Spring Gentian (haru-rindō) 《Also called haru-gikyō》] The flower shape resembles double bellflower, with white on the back of the petals, light blue, and pale red spots. The leaves are shorter and rounder than regular gentian, with pointed tips. The flower's appearance is the same as gentian, blooming in March.
【Vertical rule line】
[Buddha's Child Grass (hotoke-no-kogusa)
《Flowers bloom in March》]
The flower has six petals and resembles dianthus. The flower color has deep crimson at the base with blackness from below, and the inner back is pale crimson with whiteness from the tip. The leaves resemble mandala flowers and are large. The flower's appearance resembles columbine grass.
【Vertical rule line】
[Nine-star Grass (kuyōsō) 《Flowers bloom in March》
Dog's Tongue Grass (kuzetsusō)]
The flower shape is like a chrysanthemum, the color is originally yellow, and they bloom in clusters around. The leaves resemble bitter mallow leaves and are small. There are single petals attached below, and also thousand-petaled varieties. The single type is also called sawa-mokkō.

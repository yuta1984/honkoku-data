Matsumoto-sen (Matsumoto Pruned)　　　　　　Ganpi 《Marginal note: Flower interior crimson-red-yellow, slightly flower petals thin lead-colored deep red》

【In illustration】
Crimson brocade

【Vertical ruled lines】
[Small iris]
The flower is an ordinary iris but small, with light purple color. The upright petals are white and
light purple, with some varieties being entirely white. The leaves are similarly small, and flowers bloom in March.

[Matsumoto-sen
Pruned safflower (senkōka)
Pruned autumn silk vine (senshūra)]
The flowers have many colors. Purple Matsumoto has light purple flower color. Tang brocade
Matsumoto is deep red with dense white kasuri patterns. Nishiki river Matsumoto has
fewer kasuri patterns than Tang brocade. White double Matsumoto has six to seven petals up to eight or nine
petals attached. Flower-form Matsumoto has layered blooming petals, with deep red color. Flowers appear in May.

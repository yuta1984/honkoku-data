Plover (Chidori)
　Iso-chidori, Kawara-hashiri (in Echigo Province)
　Iso-gandou (in Ise Province)
　Kochou (in Chinese literature, Min-sho)
　The character "鴴" is commonly used, but its origin is unclear
　Found along riverbanks and waterside areas, several species exist. The form resembles wagtails or snipes

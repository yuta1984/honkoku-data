The "Bencao Gangmu" (Compendium of Materia Medica) states:
Shokunan (Pieris japonica) - Wind medicine. The main text states that Shokunan grows between mountain rocks in sunny places, hence its name.

Japanese name: "tohiranoki"
Commonly called "sakunamusa"

What is now called "tobiranoki" is a different species. There are four or five types of rhododendron with the same name, with flowers in red, blue, white, yellow, and purple colors.
○There is one type of Shokunan called "Kōkōshō"

28th day of the 3rd month, Year of the Ox
Sketched from life

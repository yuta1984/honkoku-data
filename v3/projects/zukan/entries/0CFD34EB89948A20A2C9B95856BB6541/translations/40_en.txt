　○Fertilizer
Take up soil from ditches where
small streams flow, dry it well, sift it
finely, and in any area,
mix one-third with the local soil
and plant.
Also, bury tree leaves in the soil.
When they rot and become
like soil,
take them out and mix one-third
with the local soil and plant. This is the best method.

　　　　　　　　　○Ba grass (tsutsumigrass) ○Yellow flowers are called yellow flower ground nail
　　　　　　　　　　White Ba-nail (hatsuyutei) refers to white flowers, as recorded in the Kyuukō Yafu (Famine Relief Wild Plant Manual)
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　16th day of Koshi (March), year of Mizunoto-hitsuji
According to Kyuukō Honzō (Famine Relief Materia Medica)　　　　　　　　　Copied again faithfully
　Botsubotsu-chō vegetable, also named yellow flower sprout

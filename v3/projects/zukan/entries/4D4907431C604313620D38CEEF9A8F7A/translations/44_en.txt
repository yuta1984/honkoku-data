　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　My observation states:
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Spicy vegetable and wasabi are treated as one plant in various texts. The above books
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　divide them into two plants, but their forms differ greatly, and flowers and leaves also differ.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　It is clear they are not the same plant. However, the root of spicy vegetable is also
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　extremely pungent in taste. For this reason, they are confused with each other

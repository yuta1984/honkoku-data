According to Nanpō Sōmokujō, Tree section:
　　　Purple Wisteria
　　　　Purple wisteria has long narrow leaves and stems like bamboo. The roots are extremely firm and heavy.
　　　　It has bark, white flowers, and black fruit. When placed in wine, even after two or three thousand years
　　　　it does not rot or decay. When the stem is cut and placed in soot,
　　　　it has purple fragrance and can be used to invoke spirits.

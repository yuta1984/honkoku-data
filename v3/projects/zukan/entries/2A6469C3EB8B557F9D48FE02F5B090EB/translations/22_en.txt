　　　　〇 Seed Collection Method
　Among varieties that have difficulty setting seeds, if reserve seeds are
　sown at Slight Heat (Shosho), there will be no failure in seed setting.
　There are single-stem plants that are too thin and fail to set seeds, or
　plants that are too vigorous and fail to set seeds. For thin plants, use large containers

　and add fertilizer; for overly vigorous plants, spread white sand in large containers,
　remove the previous pot as is and place it there, then fill the outside with

　for plants that are too thin and fail to set seeds, cutting small branches is
　good. When adding fertilizer, it should always be placed at the edge of the pot.

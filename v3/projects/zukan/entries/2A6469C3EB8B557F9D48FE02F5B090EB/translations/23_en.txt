　　　　〇 Method for Large Flowers
In a pot holding five go of soil, add fertilizer soil well, sow four or five seeds
in the center, keep the good ones and
remove the rest. When buds appear, in a pot holding about one sho, mix fertilizer
soil with dried sardines etc. and spread on the bottom of the pot, then
【Left page】
transplant it there as is, and gently fill the outside with the aforementioned fertilizer soil
that is rather sandy. Then bury that pot in the
soil, or it should be buried in a pot holding about three sho.
Allow three or four flowers to bloom and
remove other buds as they appear. In the evening of the previous day, if
the bud measures two sun and six bu from base to tip,
you should know that a flower four sun in diameter will open the next day.
Small leaves with large flowers are considered best.

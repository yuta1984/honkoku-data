Gastrodia elata 《Bencao Gangmu: root name is tianma》 grows in plains, waterside areas, and bamboo groves. In summer, it sends up a single stem
reaching four to five shaku in height, with bracts at each node but no leaves. It opens flowers at the stem tip,
forming spikes. Its shape is like Orobanche. Both flower and stem have a pale yellow-red tinge.
Its root is a large bulb that grows horizontally without fibrous roots, shaped like a human foot.

The Bencao Gangmu anthology states: "Baosheng said: Yuanzhou, Qinwei, Weizhou, and Lingzhou all have this plant. In late spring it sends up shoots, harvested in mid-April. Length from five-six cun to one chi or more. The stem is round and white. After harvesting, it is pressed flat and sun-dried. Su Song said: The root of Cistanche tubulosa is extremely similar to Cistanche deserticola. Those who scrape off the flowers and press them flat to substitute for the fleshy type have distinctly inferior efficacy. This is namely Orobanche."

Six Gaku-dōji (Musical Pages)

These are mostly beautiful young men. From fourteen to sixteen years of age, they wear the most exquisite clothing befitting their position. All are young lords of Ryukyuan nobility. They perform seated music in the Peechin rank seating. They are particularly versed in musical scales and rhythm, all skilled in calligraphy, and additionally excel in poetry and songs.

【Left page】
Some are made to be worn by reversing both sides. Generally,
the textiles for caps and sashes are woven in lands called Tōdo and Min, and sold
to this country. In the Ryukyu Kingdom, only banana fiber cloth is made. Women of each
household all do hand-weaving. Those made in Shuri are considered
of superior quality.

　○Annual Events
On New Year's Day, the king changes his crown and robes, first worships the year deity, then
receives ceremonies from his retainers. The ceremony on the fifteenth is the same as New Year's Day. 《Marginal note: On the fifteenth of every month, retainers visit the castle》

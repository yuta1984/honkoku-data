Preface to the Brief Record of Ryukyu Tribute Missions【One square seal】
Beyond the seas, those who ride the winds and tides to pay tribute to our country include Ryukyu and Korea. As for Ryukyu, considering it to be descendants of the Ming dynasty, we regarded them as if they were one family. Later, diplomatic relations were sometimes interrupted, but since the Shimazu clan's warships sailed west, they completely submitted and became our vassal state permanently. Since then, they have come to court from time to time, maintaining proper ceremonial protocols without daring to neglect them for over two hundred years already. How magnificent this has been! I am already over seventy years old, and I have been fortunate to personally witness their tribute missions on multiple occasions.

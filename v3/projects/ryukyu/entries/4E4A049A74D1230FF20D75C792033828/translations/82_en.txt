【Right Page】
　Hōin and Hōgen ranked court physicians ascended to the castle, wearing noshi-me long ceremonial dress in the palace
　At the latter part of the hour of the Snake, [the Shogun] appeared in the Great Hall
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Matsudaira Bungo-no-kami
　Regarding the above audience upon coming to Edo, [the Shogun] graciously thought it was commendable that the Ryukyuan envoy was brought along this time
　There was an official proclamation and [he] withdrew
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Envoy of the King of Chūzan
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Prince Ōgimi
　The above came forward, the catalog of the presented ceremonial sword was brought out and announced by the senior page at the lower level
　After performing nine prostrations and completing three bows at the veranda, personal respects were paid and announced by the senior page

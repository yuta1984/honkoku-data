○Four young boys hold golden fans decorated with flowers, wear red headbands, dress in five-colored robes in an extremely splendid manner, hang rope rings adorned with five-colored flowers around their necks, ascend the stage, and dance with foot rhythms while holding the ropes in their hands, similar to the Hat Dance. This is called the "Flower Rope Dance."

○Three young boys decorate their heads with artificial flowers, wear brocade happi coats, hang small flower baskets on their shoulders, ascend the stage, and dance as before. This is called the "Basket Dance."

○Four young boys wear five-colored robes and ascend the stage. When they sit before the musicians, the musicians give each of them four pieces of small bamboo clappers. The boys take them, stand up, and dance while beating the rhythm. This is called the "Clapper Dance."

○Six warriors wear short robes with black and white striped patterns, with sleeves tailored in different sizes, tie golden headbands around their foreheads, ascend the stage carrying white staffs, and dance with the sounds of striking matching the musical phrases. This is called the "Warrior Dance."

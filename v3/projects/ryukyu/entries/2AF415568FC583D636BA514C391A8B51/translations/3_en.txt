Hakuiwa of Satsuma Province
The Ryukyu Kingdom lies to the south of Satsuma Province, approximately 840 ri away by sea route. 《Note: On the sea, six cho equals one ri. When using the land measurement of thirty-six cho to one ri, it would be 140 ri. The Wakan Sansai Zue's record of 380 ri over sea is an error》 The country extends 59 ri north to south, while east to west it is barely 10 ri. 《Note: Using the land measurement of thirty-six cho to one ri》

This land is located 26 degrees, 2 minutes, and 3 seconds north of the equator. Therefore, its warm climate surpasses that of other countries. 《Note: Peach blossoms bloom in the first month, even in winter there is no need to put cotton padding in clothes, mosquitoes do not disappear, and fans are never put away》

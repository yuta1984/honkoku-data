[Figure descriptions]
襪 (Tabi/Socks)
　Made of cloth or leather, the tabi are short, reaching above the ankle
　with an opening along the center line facing outward, crossed and tied. At the
　toe area, a separate hole is made for the big toe
　to fit into the grass sandals.

靸 (Grass Sandals)
　Sandals are woven from fine mat grass, with one
　rope in front separating the big toe. The heel drags when walking.
　Both men and women wear them.

Preface

The Kingdom of Ryukyu is located to the southwest of Japan, an island nation in the sea more than 300 ri from Satsuma. To the southwest of Ryukyu lies the Kingdom of Siam, while to the northeast it faces Japan. The travel distance is seven to eight days from east to west, and twelve to thirteen days from north to south. The four seasons are warm, and there are rice paddies.

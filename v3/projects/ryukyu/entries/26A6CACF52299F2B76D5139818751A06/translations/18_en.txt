Chatan 《Marginal note: Also called Hokuikei. Located forty ri north of Shuri. This district has many rice fields.》 Twelve affiliated village counties: Chatan 《Marginal note: Has Murō Valley. During King Gihon's
reign, corresponding to the Chunyou era of Song Dynasty, there was an evil dragon in the valley. It caused storms and brought calamity. Young women were recruited as sacrifices to worship it. Shintsuru, daughter of the Shō family from Ginowan, responded to the recruitment and sacrificed herself to support her mother. Her filial piety moved the heavenly
gods, who destroyed the dragon and eliminated the harm. The king was greatly pleased and had her married to a prince.》 Hamakawa, Sunabe, Noguni, Nozato, Tamayo-

There are seven islands to the south.

Taiheizan (also called Meko, now corrupted to Mako-yama)
○Located 200 ri south of Chuzan (60 cho equals one ri). By the local measurement system (36 cho equals one ri), it is 300 ri.

○Products: tatami mats (Ryukyu mats)
○There is a large mountain called Chikuzan

Ikema (located south of Taiheizan)
Irabu (located southwest of Taiheizan)
Torama (located due west of Taiheizan. There are many strange creatures. There are beings with dog-like bodies and human faces)
Mitsuna (located southwest of Taiheizan. There are large serpents that regularly visit people's homes)
Ukami (located northwest of Taiheizan. There are many large snakes that regularly visit people's homes)
Kurema (located west of Taiheizan)

○The local people collectively call these seven islands Taiheizan.

There are nine islands to the southwest.

Yaeyama (also called Ishigaki-yama. Located four ri southwest of Taiheizan)
○Distance from Chuzan: 240 ri by Ryukyu measurement (60 cho equals one ri). By the local measurement system (36 cho equals one ri), it is 400 ri.
○Products: sea moss, tortoiseshell, sea cucumber, conch shells, tatami mats, and many others.

【Description of Heirotsubaa】
Heirotsubaa
Its head is like a human, and its body is like a tiger.
It runs swiftly across the water surface.
It constantly catches and eats snakes and fish.
When it sees people, it laughs heartily but never causes harm.

【Note to the left of Kikaigashima in the illustration】
This creature is said to be abundant in the seas around Kikaigashima.

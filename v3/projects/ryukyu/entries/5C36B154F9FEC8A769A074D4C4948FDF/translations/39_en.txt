　　　　November 30th　Visit to Toeizan Shrine

1. Today the Ryukyuans' route for the shrine visit was, as on the previous occasion of their castle visit, via Yashirogawa riverside road, past the front of Inoue Kawachi-no-kami's residence and the rear rest area, from Ote to Hitotsubashi, then from the front gate of Gojiin through the front of the government office outside Kandabashi to exit through Sujikai Gate, passing in front of Honda Shinano-no-kami's residence, directly to the Nio Gate of Toeizan. On the return journey, they entered through Sujikai Gate as before, past the front of Matsudaira Izu-no-kami's residence, via the same route past the government office outside Kandabashi, along Kamakura riverside road, entering Honcho, from the second block to Tori-machi, through Shibaguchi Gate, then via Tori-machi and the front of Zojoji Temple to arrive at Satsuma-no-kami's Shiba residence.

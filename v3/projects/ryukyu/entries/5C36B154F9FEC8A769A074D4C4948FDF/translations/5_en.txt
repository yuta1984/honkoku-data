Tomb of Ryukyu, En-sei Nakanishi Chikutono
　　　　En-sei Nakanishi Chikutono was a retainer of
　　　　Prince Misato, an envoy of the King of Chūzan of Ryukyu Kingdom. While accompanying Prince Misato to
　　　　Edo, on November 2nd of Hōei 7th year, the year of the Metal Tiger,
　　　　he died of illness at Hamamatsu station in Enshū Province at the age of forty. His remains were
　　　　buried at Saiken-ji Temple. Written with tears by his countrymen.
　　　　　Stone pagoda height: 2 shaku 5 sun (Square platform base, foundation stone 5 sun)

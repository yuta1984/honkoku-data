Minister Baoguang notes: The silk goods bestowed during the previous Ming dynasty varied according to the times. I append and record this here for reference. The Chuzan Seikan records: In the 14th year of Zhengtong era, bestowed upon King Sho Shitasu:

Brocade 4 bolts [ornamental flower ball pattern red 1, ornamental flower continuous victory treasure pattern red 1, cross-pattern yellow 1, hundred flowers velvet brocade yellow 1], fine silk 6 bolts [gold-woven chest and back qilin red 1, lion blue 1, hidden eight treasures heavenly flower cloud red 1, cloud green 1, plain blue 1, green 1], gauze 8 bolts [gold-woven chest and back baize red 1, qilin blue 1, hidden flower indigo 1, hidden flower bone cloud eight treasures red 1, plain red 1, blue 1, green 1, indigo 1], thin silk 6 bolts [gold-woven chest and back qilin red 1, blue 1, plain red 1, blue 1, green 1, indigo 1].

Bestowed upon the Queen Consort: Brocade 2 bolts [ornamental flower cloud phoenix blue 1, hundred flowers velvet brocade yellow 1], fine silk 4 bolts [gold-woven chest and back baize red 1, hidden flower bone cloud blue 1, hidden heavenly flower eight treasures cloud green 1, plain blue 1], gauze 4 bolts [gold-woven chest and back baize red 1, hidden flower bone cloud green 1, plain red 1, blue 1], thin silk 4 bolts [gold-woven chest and back leopard pattern 1, plain blue 1, green 1, indigo 1].

Also bestowed upon the Royal Uncle: Brocade 1 bolt [continuous victory treasure pattern yellow 1], fine silk 4 bolts [gold-woven baize large red 1, hidden flower bone cloud blue 1, hidden eight treasures heavenly flower cloud green 1, plain red 1].

In the 2nd year of Jingtai era, bestowed upon King Sho Kinpuku and his consort: The number of brocades and fine silks was the same. It was considered unusual that nothing was bestowed upon the Royal Uncle.

In the 13th year of Jiajing era, Investiture Envoy Chen Kan recorded: Bestowed upon King Sho Sei: One gauze cap [with extended horns complete], one gold-rimmed rhinoceros horn belt, one regular robe of thin silk, one large red gold-woven chest and back qilin round collar, one blue protective vest, one green lining, one set of leather cap court dress, one seven-pendant black wrinkled gauze leather crown [with pendant pearls and gold fittings complete], one jade tablet [with pouch complete], one set of five-chapter silk gauze leather cap court dress, one large red plain leather cap court dress, one reddish-yellow plain front and back skirt, one reddish-yellow plain knee covering [with jade hook complete], one reddish-yellow ornamental flower brocade sash [with gold hook and jade pendants], two red and white plain large belts, one pair of large red plain fine silk shoes.

1. When Prince Yomitanzan arrives at the top of the steps of the main entrance, two ōmetsuke (chief inspectors) come forward to guide him and have him take his seat in the lower tier of the palace hall. His retainers are seated in rows in the adjacent room of the same location, while lower-ranking officials gather in the courtyard in front of the main entrance.

1. Matsudaira Satsuma-no-kami (lord of Satsuma domain) and Matsudaira Bungo-no-kami ascend to the castle and take their seats in the upper positions of the lower tier of the palace hall.

1. All those in attendance wear long hakama (formal divided skirts).

1. The letter from the King of Chūzan is received by two ōmetsuke.

And to fill positions as tribute envoys throughout the years, their courtesy names and ranks are listed together as reference material.

The Cai clan originally came from Jinjiang County, Quanzhou Prefecture, Fujian Province. 《Note: According to the Ming Veritable Records, in the fifth year of Chenghua, when Chief Clerk Cai Jing came to pay tribute, he stated that his ancestor was from Nan'an County》 They are descendants of Song Dynasty Scholar of the Duanming Hall Xiang, with twelve generations totaling eighty-one people.

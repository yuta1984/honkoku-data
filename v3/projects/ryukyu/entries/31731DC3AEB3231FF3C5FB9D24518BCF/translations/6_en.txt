Surveillance Attendant Officials (the natives read this as "Zashiki" - the three characters of Satsujiki sound like "zashiki," following this pattern below) - Junior 4th rank, no fixed number - Serve directly at the royal palace and manage various offices

Palace Gate Officials (the natives read this as "Toshoku") - 12 people - National Academy Palace Gate Officials, 3 people - All Senior 5th rank, serve directly in the royal palace - Additional Palace Gate Officials, Junior 5th rank, no fixed number

承直郎 Ceremonial Guard Commanders (local name: Sedo) - 9 people, Senior 6th rank - Manage the king's ceremonial weapons and escort attendants. There are also 叙徳郎 (local name: Ka-sedo), Junior 6th rank, a title granted to clerical officials

承務郎 - Junior 6th rank; 承事郎 (local name: Haikin) - Sato no Ko Oyakumo, Senior 7th rank. There is also 従職郎, Junior 7th rank, a title granted to Confucian scholars

叙功郎 (local name: Okite Haikin) - A title granted to clerical officials; Chikudun no Oyakumo - Junior 7th rank

Inner Attendant (local name: Saidonu-shi, translated as Sato no Ko) - Senior 8th rank, also called Sando Inner Attendant, serving in rotation for royal palace internal duties; Assistant Inner Attendant (local name: Sato no Ko-za) - Junior 8th rank

登仕郎 (local name: Chikudun) - Ceremonial Attendants, 9 people managing court ceremonies and processions, Senior 9th rank; Assistant 登仕郎 (local name: Chikudun-za) - Junior 9th rank

　　However, even if the offense remains attempted, depending on circumstances, it shall be treated the same as completed acts. When small amounts of sugar are sold:
　　1. Sugar master: 4 to 10 sen per kin in gold
　　1. Village official: 3 to 9 sen per kin in gold  
　　1. Supervisor: Same as above
　　1. Representative: Same as above
　　1. Cultivator: Same as above
　　1. Group leader: Same as above
　　1. Chief cultivator: Same as above
　　1. When one barrel or more is sold:
　　　　1. Sugar master: 4 to 10 yen per chō in gold
　　　　1. Village official: 1 to 5 yen per chō in gold
　　　　1. Supervisor: Same as above
　　　　1. Representative: 2 to 6 yen per chō in gold
　　　　1. Cultivator: Same as above
　　　　1. Group leader: Same as above
　　　　1. Chief cultivator: 50 sen to 2 yen 50 sen per chō in gold

【Figure Description】
Clothing
　Without belt
　Collar has
　small notch
　style
　All garments are loose and wide. There is no crossing collar at the back. The sleeves are two to three shaku in size. The length does not extend beyond the fingertips. The end of the right collar is made with a notched style. There is no clothing belt. Most are made from banana fiber cloth and banana kudzu fiber.
　They have beautiful patterns and colors. Men's and women's clothing are all the same. This is called "qin."

Belt
　The large belt is one jō and four to five shaku long. The width is six to seven sun. It is wound around the waist three to four times. Mixed flower brocade ground is considered most precious. Large flower brocade belts come next.
　Those with dragons coiled on yellow or red ground come next.
　The lower grade ones are all mixed-color flower belts.

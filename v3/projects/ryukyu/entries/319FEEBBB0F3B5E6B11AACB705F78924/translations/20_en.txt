1. When Gushikawa arrived at the top of the steps of the main entrance,
　the ōmetsuke (chief inspectors) Kōno Buzen-no-kami and Nose Inaba-no-kami
　came out to meet him and took their seats in the lower tier of the palace hall via the rear gate.
　The attendants sat in rows in the adjacent room of the same location, while those of lower rank
　gathered and sat in the courtyard in front of the main entrance.
1. Matsudaira Satsuma-no-kami ascended to the castle and
　took his seat in the upper position of the lower tier in the palace hall.
1. Those in attendance wore hitatare, kariginu, daimon, hōi,
　and suō formal court dress.
1. The box containing the letter from the King of Chūzan was received by
　the ōmetsuke Kōno Buzen-no-kami and Nose Inaba-no-kami.
　　Procedures for Gushikawa's formal audience

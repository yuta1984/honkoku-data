Item: The Ryukyuan music was heard, and after granting dismissal, [they] ascended to the castle

Item: When Prince Gushikawa reached the top of the entrance hall stairs, two ōmetsuke came out to guide him, and he was seated in the lower section of the palace hall; his attendants were arranged in rows in the adjacent room of the same place, while the lower-ranking officials gathered in the front courtyard of the entrance hall

Item: Matsudaira Satsuma-no-kami ascended to the castle and was seated in the upper part of the lower section of the palace hall

Item: The officials in attendance wore hitatare, kariginu, daimon, and hōi court dress

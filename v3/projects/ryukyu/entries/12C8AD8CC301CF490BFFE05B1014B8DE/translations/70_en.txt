1. The Ryukyu envoy from the King of Chūzan, Prince Gushikawa, after completing his farewell audience at the Main Castle, proceeded to the West Castle following the same route and protocol as during the formal courtesy visit.

1. When Prince Gushikawa reached the top of the stairs at the main entrance, two ōmetsuke (senior inspectors) came forward to guide him and seated him in the lower section of the palace reception hall. His attendants were arranged in rows in the adjacent room, while the lower-ranking officials gathered in the courtyard of the main entrance.

1. Matsudaira Satsuma-no-kami arrived at the castle and took his seat in the upper part of the lower section of the palace reception hall, wearing long hakama.

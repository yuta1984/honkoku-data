The Koharu has golden-yellow feathers, a long beak and short tail, and sings in April.
　　　　Koharu (Japanese name: Miyama-shōbin [Mountain Kingfisher])

The Maseki has green wing feathers with white eyebrows, and comes in September. Also, the Ishikyūshi resembles the Maseki.

　　　　　　Maseki (also called Maseki-kyūshi, Japanese name: Uguisu [Bush Warbler])
　　　　　　Ishikyūshi (Japanese name: Gojūkara [Nuthatch])

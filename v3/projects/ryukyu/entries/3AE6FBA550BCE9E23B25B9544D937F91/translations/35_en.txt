The lands are all used for cattle and horse ranching. If proper methods of cultivation were obtained, the profits generated would also be great.

The Yaeyama Island government office is located in Tonoshiro Village, Ōhama Magiri, Ishigaki Island. There is also a medical clinic established there. There are over 2,230 households with a population of over 11,980. It is about 157 ri from Naha.

Ishigaki Island is the largest among the islands, with a circumference of about 16 ri and 17 chō. Mount Omoto towers in the center. There is much good timber in the mountains. Its ridges extend northward and end at Hirakubo Cape.

Kabira Port is on the northwest coast. Large ships can anchor there. Although there are many other harbors and bays, they are all inconvenient for mooring ships.

Steamships anchor between Ōhama Magiri and Taketomi Island.

The islands of Taketomi, Kuroshima, Aragusuku, and Kohama are scattered to the southwest of Ishigaki Island. The large ones have a circumference of over three ri. The small ones are no more than one ri.

Iriomote Island is located about four ri and eighteen chō west of Ishigaki Island. Its circumference is about fifteen ri. Mount Komi towers in the center, and the trees grow densely. There is Komi Port on the east coast. There is Higekawa Village Port on the north coast. Small boats can be moored at both. To the south is Hateruma Island. Its circumference is over three ri.

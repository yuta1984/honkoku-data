A Brief Geography of Okinawa Prefecture
　　　Preface
Regarding education in Okinawa Prefecture: In the 13th year of Meiji (1880), school districts were established according to the Education System Order, with a normal school in Naha, a middle school in Shuri, and elementary schools in each magiri (administrative district) and outlying islands. Although only a few years have passed since then, general education has spread widely throughout the prefecture, and facilities for enlightening wisdom and dispelling ignorance can be said to be well established. However, regarding the geography of this prefecture, there are no good textbooks suitable for elementary school use and for children's reading practice.

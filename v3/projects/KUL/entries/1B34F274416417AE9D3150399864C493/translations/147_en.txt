On March 15th of An'ei 3, Kinoe-uma year (1774), the oral testimony was completed. Ten months after initially departing from Okinoerabu Island, they safely set foot on their homeland, returned to their family homes, and the joy of being able to tell their relatives and acquaintances about the hardships and pleasures during their drift was beyond words.

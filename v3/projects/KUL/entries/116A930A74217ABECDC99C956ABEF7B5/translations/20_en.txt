Copy of the official written orders from Edo regarding the repelling of Chinese ships

　　　　Memorandum

One When Chinese ships are sighted nearby, until now guard boats have been dispatched, but
　this practice should first be suspended, and we should carefully observe the Chinese ships hiding their vessels. Moreover, those who intend to engage in smuggling with Japanese people should be investigated. Guard boats should be dispatched so that they are not visible to the Chinese ships.

【Left Page】
One When Chinese ships remain anchored, waiting for favorable weather, whaling boats or
　small boats from the river should carry about two gunners each, dispatching four or five boats to fire with muskets.
One The above number of boats is for handling one or two Chinese ships. If there are more than ten ships gathered in one place, three domains should coordinate to dispatch boats for attack.
One The above large cannons should be fired with guns carrying twenty to thirty monme shot.
One It is not only about firing at the upper parts of the ships. They should fire at any part whatsoever.

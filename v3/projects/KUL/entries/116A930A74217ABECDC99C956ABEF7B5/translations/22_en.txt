　Regarding the matter of expelling Chinese ships when they remain in harbor, based on the written instructions on the separate sheet,
　consultations should be ordered among all parties. The details have been communicated to Ukon-shōgen,

　　Soon Watanabe Geki will be dispatched to Nagasaki on official business. Regarding Geki's duties,
　　expulsion should be postponed until he reaches lodgings within the three domains. The matter of
　　sending guard ships to Chinese vessels has been suspended indefinitely, and orders should be given to observe
　　Chinese ships remaining leisurely in harbor.

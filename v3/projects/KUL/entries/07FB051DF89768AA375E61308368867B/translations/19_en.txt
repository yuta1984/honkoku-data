【Right Page】
28th day: Though the weather appeared rainy, departed Kaminoseki around 6:30. As the weather worsened, arrived at Murozumi around 9 o'clock.
　　Lord Mōri Yamato-no-kami provided 6 rowing boats and service vessels, and 1 guard ship.

29th day: Though the weather was good, due to strong westerly winds, remained anchored at the same place.

1st day of the 4th month: Due to rain, remained anchored at the same place.

From Murozumi to Nakanoseki: 13 ri

2nd day: Good weather. Departed Murozumi around the 7th hour of dawn, arrived at Nakanoseki around 7:30.

From Nakanoseki to Chōshū Honzan: 12 ri

3rd day: Good weather. Departed Nakaseki around the 7th hour, arrived at Chōshū Honzan around 5:30 in the evening.
　　Lord Mōri Kai-no-kami provided 1 guard ship and 53 rowing boats and service vessels.

【Left Page】
4th day: Good weather. Departed Chōshū Honzan around 6:30, arrived at Shimonoseki around the 9th hour.
　　Lord Ogasawara Daizen-dayū provided 3 guard ships, 2 whale boats, and 60 rowing boats and service vessels.

5th day: Due to rain, remained anchored at the same place.

From Shimonoseki to Minamikazetomari: 3 ri

6th day: Though the weather appeared rainy, as the tide was favorable, departed Shimonoseki around 5 o'clock. Due to westerly winds, arrived at Minamikazetomari around 9 o'clock.
　　Lord Matsudaira Bizen-no-kami provided 1 guard ship, 1 small fast boat, and 64 rowing boats and service vessels.

7th day: Though the weather was good, due to northeast winds and high waves, remained anchored at the same place.

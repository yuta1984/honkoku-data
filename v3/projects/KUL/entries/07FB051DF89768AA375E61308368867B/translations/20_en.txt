【Right Page】
8th day: Great south wind with rain. Moreover, as the escort ship's rudder was damaged, summoned a carpenter and had repairs made at the same place, thus remained anchored.

9th day: Though the weather was good, due to great west wind and high waves, remained anchored at the same place.

From Minamikazetomari to Karatomari in Chikuzen: 26 ri

10th day: Good weather. Departed Chōshū Minamikazetomari around the 6th hour, with favorable tailwinds, arrived at Karatomari in Chikuzen around the 6th hour of evening.

From Karatomari to Yobuko in Hizen: 16 ri

11th day: Good weather. Departed Karatomari around 6 o'clock, arrived at Yobuko in Hizen around 7 o'clock.
　　Lord Ogasawara Tonomo-no-kami provided 2 large and medium guard ships, 1 whale boat, and 78 other rowing boats.

【Left Page】
12th day: Due to rain, remained anchored at the same place.

13th day: Due to south wind and rain, remained anchored at the same place.

14th day: Due to south wind blowing, remained anchored at the same place.

15th day: Due to great south wind blowing, remained anchored at the same place.

16th day: Same as above.

17th day: Due to heavy rain and wind, remained anchored at the same place.

From Yobuko in Hizen to Tasuke: 13 ri

18th day: Good weather. Departed Yobuko around 6:30. From around 8 o'clock, south wind blew and light rain fell, making navigation difficult, so arrived at Tasuke Bay around 6 o'clock.
　　Lord Matsuura Hizen-no-kami provided 2 large guard ships and 64 rowing boats and service vessels.

One - Bunsei 4th year, Snake year, 3rd month, 7th day, morning around 6 o'clock departed Wakayama, arrived Shiotsu around 9 o'clock

Same 8th day, good weather. As it was commanded to show [the foreigners around], we went around to Saigasaki Bay
9th day, good weather. At the same place, the elder officials conducted an inspection
10th day, good weather. At the same place, [the officials] observed [the foreigners]
11th day, northeast wind with rain, so returned to Shiotsu
12th day, good weather but strong west wind, so the ship remained at Shiotsu
13th day, good weather. As [the foreigners] made a pilgrimage to Kimii-dera temple, the boat stayed at the same place. From Shiotsu to Yura in Awaji Province is 10 ri
14th day, good weather. Departed Shiotsu around 6 o'clock, but south wind and clouds quickly from the south

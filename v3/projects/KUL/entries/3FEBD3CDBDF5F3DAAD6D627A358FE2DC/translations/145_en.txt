【Right Page】
This is the place. My daily records and travel writings are now complete. Ah, you who indulge in worldly pleasures! If you constantly array in your hearts these accounts of my myriad transformations through suffering and joy, it would perhaps be sufficient to admonish lazy and dissolute minds and rouse weak and base emotions. If you merely view this casually as a theatrical performance, even the writings of sages would be of no benefit whatsoever. The reader need only imagine the events of my past journey, reflect upon themselves, and strive to cultivate their conduct.

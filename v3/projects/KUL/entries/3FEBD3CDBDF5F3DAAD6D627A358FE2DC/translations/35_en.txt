On the 11th day, I covered the upper surface inside the cellar with boards, like a ceiling. After seven days I completed the work.

From the 17th to the 20th day, I stored various implements and also made large plates for preparing food.

On the 24th day, due to heavy rain day and night, I did not leave the hut.

On the 25th day, the rain became increasingly severe.

On the 26th day, it alternated between clear and rainy weather, washing the tree branches and making them even more vivid.

On the 27th day, I caught one goat. This one had previously been hit by a lead shot and injured its leg, so I bound it myself and dragged it back. The other goats have also become quite tame and approach the hut, so I give them green grass to eat. I thought that if I could domesticate the goats, I could capture them for food without expending gunpowder,

[Left page]
and I was greatly pleased.

From the 28th to the 30th day, there was no wind and the sultry heat was unbearable. Each evening I tried to enter the mountains to hunt beasts, but due to this heat I remained in the hut.

Year 1660, 1st month, 1st day - on this day the heat was particularly severe. After sunset I briefly entered the mountains to hunt. I saw a herd of goats in the mountains, but they were startled upon seeing people, so I set the dog on them to catch them, but the sheep were extremely swift and could not be caught.

Item　During the attack on the twenty-sixth day of the fifth month, the gunners were few in number, so it was decided to increase the defensive cannons. From this guard duty, two more gunners were added and three large guns were sent elsewhere. Therefore, during the attack on the twenty-sixth day, the Chinese ship could not be seen even from the Jishima watchtower, so it was pointless to remain stationed at Jishima for several days. It was decided that they should instead station themselves at islands or bays where the Chinese ship might drift, so on the fourth day of the sixth month of the year of the Dog, they departed Jishima and the aforementioned personnel sailed to Aishima by boat. Even from the watchtower at Jishima, the Chinese ship could not be seen, and assuming it was drifting in the lower sea, on the sixth day they departed Aishima and sailed to Karadomari.

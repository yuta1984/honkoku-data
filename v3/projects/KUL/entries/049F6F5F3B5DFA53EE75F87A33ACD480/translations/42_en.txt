Since the Chinese ship's sails and such became completely invisible from the 19th of last month, on the 12th day of the 1st month of the 4th year of Kyōhō, Year of the Boar, word came from Fukuoka to withdraw all personnel stationed in Ashiya. The cannon ammunition and other equipment were placed in the Yamakado storehouse with seals attached and entrusted to Ashiya Deputy Governor Keya Kanbei. On the 13th of the same month, they returned to Fukuoka.

　We have understood the above matter. As initially stated, we shall consult with the Magistrate's office and, until we receive instructions, we shall station guard ships and maintain careful watch.

Second response from Tokunaga Yajiemon
1. The express messenger from the 12th arrived yesterday morning on the 14th. I immediately went to Lord Tanba and conveyed the letters from everyone to Matsumoto Riheibe's place, as well as the matters you instructed me to convey. He said that the contents of the written conversation would be carefully deliberated and that he would give his response later. I also went to Lord Geki, submitted a copy of the written conversation, and presented the letter to his retainer in the same manner as to Lord Tanba, where I was told that a response would be given later. Today I was summoned by both lords, and what they told me is stated in the separate document. With utmost reverence and respect.

　　　I respectfully state:
　　　　　Son of Kyūgorō, farmer of Sansami Bay, Kumano, Kii Province
　　　　　Captain of the thousand-koku capacity ship
　　　　　Eijumaru, owned by Ihei of Nakamuraya, Hyōgo, Settsu Province
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Zensuke
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Age 29, Year of the Snake
　【Red square seal, round blue stamp of Kyushu Imperial University Library collection mark】

I entered service at Nakamuraya Ihei's establishment in Hyōgo, Settsu Province 
when I was about fifteen years old. Thereafter I became the captain of his 
thousand-koku capacity ship Eijumaru and sailed to various provinces. 
In the eighth month of the previous Year of the Ox, we loaded sake, sugar, incense,
cotton and other goods at Hyōgo port and set out for commercial trade in Nanbu, Ōshū, with crew members

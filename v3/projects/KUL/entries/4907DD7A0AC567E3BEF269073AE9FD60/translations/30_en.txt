Animals like raccoon dogs and deer are in the mountains. There are many beasts in the mountains that are rabbit-shaped with short ears, about 2.5 sun in size, with long hair, gray color, and very short tails.

First, there are dogs both large and small, shaped the same as in Japan. The smaller ones are about one shaku, and newborn puppies are about the size of mice. The barking sounds of both large and small dogs are the same as in Japan.

First, I accompanied the "Comandante" to hunt in the mountains and at sea, carrying guns and leading dogs. The "Comandante" appears to be skilled with guns, hitting birds and beasts well when shooting. The dogs we brought would retrieve the shot game in their mouths and release it at our feet. Even when birds were shot over the sea, they would swim out and retrieve them. The training of these dogs

First, cotton trees are about one to two shaku in circumference and about two ken in height, planted in various places. The cotton and leaves are the same as in Japan. When harvesting the cotton that bursts forth, they climb the trees to pick it. New buds grow afterward and burst forth again, so it can be harvested year-round.

First, watermelons and muskmelons are abundant year-round due to the warm climate.

First, there are sweet potatoes, lettuce, and turnip varieties, but I do not see eggplants or daikon radishes.

　　　The situation in San Francisco

1. Regarding people: Both men and women are of average height, with light brown complexions, though some have fair skin. Their clothing and other aspects are similar to New Bedford.

1. Regarding food: They eat something called "fray" - made from wheat flour, formed round and flat like rice cakes - as a substitute for rice three times a day. Other foods are similar to those in New Bedford.

1. Regarding house construction: Houses are built along the coast. The houses that have existed for years appear crude, while the gold mining sites were established by the local administrator called "Nuinkiran-sutei." In recent years, houses have been built one after another, with some still incomplete, and the newly built houses appear quite good.

1. No castles or temple-like places were observed.

1. Regarding the land: There are many flatlands, generally open with good visibility. There are no mountains nearby, but high mountains can be seen in the distance, including some bare mountains.

1. Vegetation, climate, etc. are generally similar to New Bedford.

1. Regarding birds and animals: Chickens, cattle, sheep, etc. were observed.

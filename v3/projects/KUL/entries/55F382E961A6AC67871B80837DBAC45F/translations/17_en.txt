　　Conditions in Russia

The place where we first drifted ashore was an island called "Onderetsu Kē-ōstoro,"
governed by Russia. No vegetation could be seen anywhere, only sandy ground.
In the interior, high mountains covered with pure white snow were visible. The people
resemble the Ezo and wear animal skins or bird feathers attached to hides,
with no headwear, walking barefoot. Their food consists

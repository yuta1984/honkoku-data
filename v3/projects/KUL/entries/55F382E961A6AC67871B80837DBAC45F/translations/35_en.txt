【Right Page】
Hospitals for the sick exist in various places, and the poor or foreigners are placed in these hospitals to receive treatment, with meals and everything provided by the government offices. There are physicians, but they use external treatments, mainly water medicines or single-ingredient medicines, and there is no proper medical practice.

There are many people like outcasts and beggars, but there are no people like eta (untouchables).

【Left Page】
The Russian ship departed from the port of "Kanashita" within the same country around mid-June of the year of the boar, sailing mainly westward, and arrived at a place called "Kōheigahani" around early July of the same year. This place is a distant port from "Kanashita," and the people are generally similar to Russians. They stayed at this place for twenty-seven or twenty-eight days, and though I don't remember the exact date, they departed from there sailing westward and arrived at a port within "Ankeriya" (place name forgotten) after twenty-four or twenty-five days, though I don't remember the exact date. They moored the ship at this place for about twenty days, during which time envoys also went ashore. This was a better port than "Kōheigahani," and the people were similar to Russians. Though I don't remember the exact date, they departed from there sailing toward the southwest for more than two months...

【Right Page】
...since I had long heard about this matter, when I asked the Dutch to help send me back to Japan, they said that if I withdrew the petition for return that had been submitted to the Russian Emperor and was released from Russian custody, they would deliver me. When I asked how long it would take by sea, they answered that it would take three years. I heard that it wouldn't take so many years from Russia, but if the petition should go awry, I was thinking of relying on the Dutch when I was informed about your country's matter. In Irkutsk, I saw Koreans. I also saw Chinese people. They were said to be from Beijing. During winter, they ride on sleds pulled by dogs over the ice. Each person uses four dogs. It is an extremely fast means of transportation. In Petersburg, there are wild boars as small as mice, rabbits, and bantam chickens as small as sparrows.

【Left Page】
I thought to bring back the wild boars when returning to Japan and kept three of them, but the local people told me that I probably wouldn't be able to keep them alive. However, thinking there might be a chance, I kept raising them, but they all died. Currently, there is an Empress whose name is Ekaterina Alekseevna. She is sixty-four years old. The Crown Prince's name is Pavel Petrovich, and he is thirty-nine years old. There are two grandsons: one is called Aleksandr Pavlovich and is sixteen years old, and the other is called Konstantin Pavlovich and is fourteen years old.

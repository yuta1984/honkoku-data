1. Tengoku [marginal note: Supreme Imperial Treasure] from the reign of Emperor Monmu, around the Taih≈ç era (701-704), Yamato Province

Concerning the form, appearance, and oral traditions of this tachi sword: The forging is itame-hada, extremely fine. The ground color is bluish and clear. The bottom blade has a tan-colored core, and the forging resembles fine sand left behind by receding waves after the surf has washed ashore. The ground and blade are completely without cloudiness. The blade has abundant nie (crystalline formations). The kissaki (point) is small and hardened at the tip. It has ko-midare hamon (small irregular temper line) with ashi (leg-like formations) tempered into it.

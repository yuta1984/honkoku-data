"Kyogen Happyaku Man Hachi Den" (The Tales of Eight Million and Eight Lies) (Written and illustrated by: Shikataya Hontarō)

【Uso Happyaku Man Hachi Den】
【Column title: Man Hachi】
【This was advertised as the 16th installment of the "Berabō" series in Kōshodō (Tsutaya)'s shop as a new publication in An'ei 9. While this book itself does not bear Tsutaya's shop mark, this title appears at the end of "Mitate Hōrai"】
【Author: Shikataya Hontarō (same person as Yomo Akara and Ōta Nanpo)】
【Illustrator: (There is information suggesting Torii Kiyotsune, but no signature is found within the book)】
【Kusazōshi (illustrated popular fiction)】
【Publisher: Tsutaya (Kōshodō)】
【An'ei 9, 1780】

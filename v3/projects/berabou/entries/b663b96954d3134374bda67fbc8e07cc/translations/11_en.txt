Horses are living creatures, and no two horses among a hundred have the same temperament or habits. Just because one horse can be ridden well doesn't mean other horses can definitely be ridden well. Courtesans are also like this - just because one can be handled doesn't mean another can be handled in the same way. Especially since they have ridden all the available horses, they come day and night to ride the countless courtesans of Shimabara, Maruyama, Ishigaki, Nawate and other districts, thinking that with such variety of temperaments, their horsemanship practice will improve, and they practice until dawn.

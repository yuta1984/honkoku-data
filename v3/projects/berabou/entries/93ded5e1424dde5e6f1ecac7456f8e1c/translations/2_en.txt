Preface

Taking up my brush and opening the Tsurezuregusa, I find that Priest KenkÅ wrote: "Women's nature is entirely biased, their ego-attachment runs deep, their greed is extreme, they do not understand the principles of things, and lacking in honesty, women are foolish creatures." Also, in China, as recorded in the Great Learning, "Gold and jade are not treasures; virtue is the treasure." Now in these five volumes, I shall describe and reveal women's virtue, and [counter] that prejudice...

How to eat in the seven-five-three style: Take the chopsticks with the right hand, pick up rice and eat two or three bites, then while holding the chopsticks, pick up the soup with the right hand and transfer it to the left, sip the soup and eat the solid ingredients with two or three bites, then transfer back to the right and place it down. Again eat rice with two chopsticks, then take soup as before and eat the ingredients. On the third time, sip the soup but do not eat the ingredients, and eat from the center serving of the main tray to the left corner, then to the right corner.

Good foods during pregnancy:
1. Barley, 1. millet, 1. proso millet, 1. black beans, 1. daikon radish
1. burdock root, 1. eleuthero, 1. strawberries, 1. goji berries, 1. thistle
1. water dropwort, 1. Japanese spikenard, 1. mountain yam, 1. carp, 1. persimmon
1. squid, 1. wild goose, 1. jellyfish, 1. wheat gluten, 1. carrot

Similarly, foods that are not good:
1. pear, 1. plum, 1. peach, 1. Japanese plum, 1. apricot
1. water chestnut, 1. camphor, 1. Chinese chives, 1. noodles, 1. beans

【Modern annotations】
This work "Ukiyo Dōchū Hizakurige" (Floating World Journey on Shank's Mare) is a book mentioned in the commentary of Berabō Episode 44, and is considered a precursor to the famous "Tōkaidō Dōchū Hizakurige" (Along the Tōkaidō on Foot). This collection appears to include Tōkaidō Dōchū Hizakurige as well in the latter part.

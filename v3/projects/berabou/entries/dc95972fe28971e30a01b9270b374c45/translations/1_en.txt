Forty-Eight Techniques
【Forty-Eight Techniques for Buying Courtesans】
【Reading: keiseikai shijūhatte / keiseigai shijūhatte】
【Running title: Forty-Eight Techniques】
【Description: The thirty-seventh Berabo story. This sharebon (witty book) was created as a guide for buying courtesans, conceived as a way to improve society without criticizing the shogunate. Kyōden commented that although he intended to write an instructional guide, he found himself enjoying writing about the true nature of courtesans and their clients.】
【Author and illustrator: Santō Kyōden】
【Genre: Sharebon】
【Publisher: Tsutaya】
【Kansei 2 (1790)】

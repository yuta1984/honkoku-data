Muttering thus, upon entering through the gate, red and white ceremonial cords were strung overhead, and that fart-performer sat with the musical accompanists on a slightly elevated place. His physique was of medium build with fair complexion, wearing the crescent-shaped topknot of a servant, dressed in a light blue single-layer kimono with a bright red crepe silk undergarment. His introductory speech was refreshing and without unpleasantness, and matching his talk:

First came the auspicious Sanbaso fart: "Totsu ha-hiyoro-hiyoro hiwa-hiwa-hiwa" with good rhythm. Next was the rooster's dawn crow rendered as "bu-bu-buu-bu," dispersed in sections, followed by the water wheel. While releasing "bu-bu-bu-bu" sounds, he spun his own body like a wheel. Leaning left, he pressed against the wheel's water force, showing the manner of drawing and pouring water. "Now, time to change!" He stood up with the exit drum beat, and when he later told his friends that he had seen the fart-man perform, the whole gathering discussed this together. Some spoke of medicine...

New Yoshiwara Directory Collection, Volume 1

[Alternative title: Seirō Bijin Awase Sugata Kagami (Mirror of Beautiful Courtesans in Various Poses)]
[Shin Yoshiwara Saiken Awase]
[Featured in Berabō Episode 10, etc. This is an illustrated collection depicting the everyday lives of courtesans, including Segawa Tayū shortly after her succession to the name (frame 4). Originally three volumes, but this collection contains only the upper volume. This is the thick book that Jūzaburō handed to Segawa when she was being ransomed.]
[Artists: Katsukawa Shunshō / Kitao Shigemasa]
[Publishers: Edo booksellers (Yamazaki Kinbē / Tsutaya Jūzaburō)]
[An'ei 5 (1776)]

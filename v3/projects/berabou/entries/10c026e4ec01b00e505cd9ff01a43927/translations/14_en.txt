【Main text】
Having thrown away every last piece of gold and silver, just as he was rejoicing that the clouds weighing on his heart had finally cleared, the discarded gold and silver formed into a single mass and flew up into the air. Drawn by this force, gold and silver from around the world gathered together and came flying toward Manman's treasure storehouse - a sight too terrible to behold. He had his retainers climb up onto the roof of the treasure house to fend off the golden orbs.

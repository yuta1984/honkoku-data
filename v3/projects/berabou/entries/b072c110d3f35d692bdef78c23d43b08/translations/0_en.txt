【Ten no Amijima (The Love Suicides at Amijima)】
【Berabō Episode 9: The courtesan Segawa is about to be ransomed by Toriyama Kengyō. Tsutajū plans to create a fake travel permit and escape together with Segawa. Tsutajū, who was a book lender, hides the permit in this book and gives it to Segawa】
【Author: Chikamatsu Monzaemon】
【Jōruri (puppet theater)】
【Publisher: Yamamoto Kuemon】
【First performance: Kyōhō 5, 1720】

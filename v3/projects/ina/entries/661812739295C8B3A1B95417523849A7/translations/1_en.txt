We have read your letter of the third day past. Though the cold is severe, His Lordship the Shogun is in increasingly good health and is eating well, so please be at ease. There is nothing unusual to report from here.

We are pleased to report that the Imperial Palace and Court continue to remain safe and secure. Regarding the matter of the court lady's appointment to quasi-empress status that you mentioned, we have understood the details. We will communicate further about this matter at a later time.

As for yourself, we are gratified that you continue to serve faithfully without incident. All here are also well. The above.

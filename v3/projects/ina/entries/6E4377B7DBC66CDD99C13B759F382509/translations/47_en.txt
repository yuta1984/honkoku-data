After this, the realm was unified and merits and faults were investigated, with rewards and punishments deliberated. Nagashige made his statement: "From the beginning, Nagashige had no thought of which side, east or west, to join. he had repeatedly received messengers with orders to "arrange matters in the Hokuriku region to be completely peaceful and hurry to join the campaign." Therefore, Toshinaga conveyed this to Nagashige, reconciled with him, and rode up together. Now, if he were to be punished, people would say "Toshinaga, fearing the previous battle, temporarily deceived him to pass through," which would be a disgrace for a warrior. "Please, just as I was initially ordered, may his negligence be pardoned," he pleaded. His plea was deemed quite reasonable, but since Kaga Province had already been granted to Toshinaga as a reward, Nagashige lost his domain and went to the Kantō region.

【Left page】
　　　Satake
Middle Captain of the Left concurrently Governor of Ukyō, Minamoto no Yoshinobu, is an eighteenth-generation descendant of Satake no Kanja Masayoshi, grandson of Shinra Saburō Shigemitsu, the third son of Chinjufu-shōgun Yoriyoshi. Masayoshi went down to Hitachi Province through his maternal connections and ruled the land of Satake in Kuji District, so he took the name Satake. Masayoshi's grandson, Satake Bettō Hideyoshi, should have been killed for the sake of the late General Yoritomo, but by rare fortune was spared. When Yoritomo reached Utsunomiya in Shimotsuke Province to attack Yasuhira of the north,

Hideyoshi came with his soldiers. Since all of Satake's banners were pure white, to distinguish them from His Lordship's banners, he was given a fan painted with a moon to attach to the top of the flags.

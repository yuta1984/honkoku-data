"Since this is a country we conquered, if we are granted the province of Echizen, we should defeat one front for the Inner Minister (Tokugawa Ieyasu) and come to serve," he said. Then Toshinaga replied again: "Granting the province of Echizen would be according to the Inner Minister's will, so it has been difficult for Toshinaga to petition for beforehand. If you achieve military merit for his house, how could your wish not be fulfilled? It goes without saying that Toshinaga will use his modest power to intercede for you." Nagashige was greatly pleased by this and said "Then I shall join your side," and for military council, retainers from both houses met at Mattō. First, there was a request to provide hostages to Toshinaga's side. Nagashige's retainers became greatly angered:

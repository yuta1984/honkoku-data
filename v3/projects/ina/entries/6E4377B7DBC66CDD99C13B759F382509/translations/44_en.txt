They clamored saying "he will send forces." Kaga no Kami Nagashige went to Tokugawa-dono and said, "If the rumors are true, the subjugation of Toshinaga will surely happen soon. In that case, since the territory I govern is near that border, the vanguard should not be entrusted to others," he requested. This man's father Nagahide, like Toshinaga, had married Nobunaga's daughter and they were related by marriage, yet despite this private bond, he requested the vanguard for the sake of public duty. Tokugawa-dono was extremely moved by this and granted him a sword (Yoshimitsu). Toshinaga's rebellion turned out to be completely groundless rumors, so there was no need to mobilize troops.

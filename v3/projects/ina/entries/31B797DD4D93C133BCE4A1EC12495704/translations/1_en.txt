Senior Councillors and Junior Councillors:
Regarding the ceremonial procession equipment, which has been
inconsistent up to now, henceforth regardless of 
the original rank's retinue, palanquins,
silk mats, portable shrine canopies,
bagged umbrellas, front boxes and
box cords, sliding doors, portable seats,
mats,
mat coverings, vehicles, and also
six-foot attendants, conspicuous personal items,
ceremonial spear bearers, box carriers, sandal
attendants, horse minders, haori jackets, etc.
shall not be used.

　Saddle covers should also avoid woolen cloth, applied crests
　　　　　and other conspicuous items

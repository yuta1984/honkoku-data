Memorandum

One item: Nine ryō of gold
Regarding this occasion, for the treasure house within the precinct of Hokoji Gongen Shrine, the subordinate shrine at the gate, and the sacred mound,
concerning the matter of labor costs and wages for completing the above-mentioned locations with earthen storehouse-style roofing,
we have consulted that the town administration shall provide the funds. Should there be any damage from cold weather,
we shall make replacements. However, regarding matters of iron fittings and metal reinforcements,
the town administration shall make arrangements. As a precautionary measure, this contract document is executed as stated.

【Right Page】
Respectfully submitted as a written petition

I am an incompetent person, but unexpectedly I was appointed to the position of town representative, which I have been gratefully fulfilling. However, my personal circumstances have become difficult, and I believe it is hard to continue serving in this role. Therefore, I petition to be pardoned from this official duty.

Regarding the above matter, if you would graciously grant my petition as requested, it would be most grateful. The above.

Nakamachi
Masuya
Tenpo 8, Year of the Rooster, 2nd month    Petitioner: Kichiemon
To the Magistrate's Office                  Town Representative: Manbei
《Approved on the 27th day}}

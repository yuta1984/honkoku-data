【Right Page】
Respectfully submitted as a written petition

Regarding my dependent Zenemon, I had sent him as an adopted son to his father Koemon's family in Nakamura, taking his mother with him. However, this time they have divorced, and I am taking back the mother, Zenemon, and dependents Miyo and Yasokichi. Therefore, I petition that my household members be added to the temple registry.

Regarding the above matter, if you would graciously grant my petition as requested, it would be most grateful. The above.

Honmachi
Iseya

                                           Town Representative: Hikobei
                                           Same: Chobei
To the Magistrate's Office

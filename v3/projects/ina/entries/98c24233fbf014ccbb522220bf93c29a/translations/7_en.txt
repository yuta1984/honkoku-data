　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Town representative: Heiemon
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Same: Eizō
　　　　To the Magistrate's Office (approved on the 25th day)

　　　　　I humbly petition with this oral statement
　　　I previously went as an adopted son to Yamaya Denshirō's family in Shimomachi and
　　　inherited his succession. Now, a person named Ushijirō, son of Minemon from
　　　Yatsude village in Tera district under the jurisdiction of Chimimura Heiemon-sama,
　　　is a blood relative of my adoptive family, so I wish to adopt him and have him succeed
　　　to my line. Furthermore, since my birth family, Urokoya Kōemon's house in Hokomotimura,
　　　has become extinct, I wish to send my son Denshirō to continue that family name

Due to these pressing circumstances as described above, I had earnestly petitioned regarding Sakuemon's payment. When this New Year arrived, a message came to the town officials requesting that I appear with relatives, so I immediately went accompanied by the group leader Omi-ya Heiemon. There I was informed that since the annual tax matter had been settled by the officials last winter, the said rice field would be returned to the original village and made into village communal farmland. This caused me great distress and I could not give an immediate response, so I returned home and immediately called back my son Kihei, who had been sent out for service, to discuss the matter. He was truly shocked and immediately went to the officials to humbly apologize for the delays. He earnestly requested that they forgive the delays up to this point, saying that even if he had to borrow from my master, he would pay the settlement amount by that very evening. However, this was not accepted, and it has now resulted in this formal petition. I am deeply ashamed of this situation. As I have stated above, regarding the officials'

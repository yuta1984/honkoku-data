Humbly submitting this written petition

Regarding my elder sister "Moto," as there is an informal engagement, I have held private discussions about sending her as wife to Hata Kintaro-dono, son of Hata Genhachi-dono of the castle magistrate group. I humbly petition regarding this matter.

Concerning the above matter, if you would graciously grant what I have petitioned through your benevolence, it would be most grateful and fortunate. The above.

Tenpō 8, Year of the Rooster, 2nd month　　Shimosato-machi
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Higashiya
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Petitioner: Kuwataro
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Town representative: Heibei  
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Same: Jinpei

To: Ōhira Genemon-sama
    Iwasaki Takuemon-sama

The above endorsement was made and submitted to Ōhira-sama on the 11th day of the 2nd month, and approval was granted on the 13th day.

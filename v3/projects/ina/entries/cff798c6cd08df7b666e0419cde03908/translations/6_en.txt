The above distribution methods shall be followed as the standard. Regarding stipends, the provision that they shall be paid from the day of announcement refers to cases of interruption, which should be understood accordingly.

One: Since ten-person stipends are equivalent to seventy koku, three-person stipends each shall be distributed from the stipend allocation at stationed posts. For Edo expenses, three-person stipends each shall be paid at Edo rates from the stipend allocation.

One: Those of retainer rank and above who have additional stipends and are unmarried shall henceforth have their stationed-duty stipends discontinued and manage with their stipend holdings, according to the official regulation.

Additional Regulations for Stipend Distribution Methods

Reference to Article 9 of the main register
Article 1: When promoting ashigaru foot soldiers (muashi) to stipend-receiving retainers (kyūnin), regardless of whether there is an increase in stipend allocation, they shall be treated as holders of new stipends (shinchi), and deductions shall be waived in the first year.

However, this regulation was established from the time of Itō Rokubē's promotion in the fifth month of the year of the Dog.

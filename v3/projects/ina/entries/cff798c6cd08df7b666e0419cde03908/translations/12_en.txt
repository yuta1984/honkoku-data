One: When going out for garrison duty but having no official business and returning before staying for a full month, deductions shall be waived for half a year. If the stay exceeds thirty days, payment shall be made without deductions for the entire year.
　However, even if one departs for garrison duty after mid-December and returns by mid-January of the following year, spanning two years, if the stay is within thirty days, half a year's deductions shall be waived only for the year of departure.

One: When rice stipends measured in bags (hyō) are converted to land grants (chihō), following the precedent of new grants, the land grant amount shall be paid without deductions for the entire year.

Document of Commitment

Regarding the post-horse assistance fund of seventy ryo that we have been permitted to borrow on installment payments over ten years from the coming winter of the Tsuchinoto-Mi year to the winter of the Mizunoe-Tora year, we are most grateful for this favorable arrangement that has been approved and granted to us.

In connection with this matter, you have provided your official seal on the loan certificate confirming that we will make payments without fail during the specified period. We pledge that this money will be lent only to persons of reliable character and will be used exclusively for post-horse assistance as per our sincere intention. Therefore, we will never default on our payments. Under these circumstances, we will cause no trouble whatsoever to any parties concerned. We submit this written pledge for future reference. Thus stated.

Keio 4, Tsuchinoto-Tatsu Year (1868), Seventh Month

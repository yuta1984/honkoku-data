Kōka 2nd year (1845)

Nakamachi
Yoshidaya
Son of Kanemon
2 kan mon of coins - Kanzō

This person consistently demonstrates good character and conduct. He handles not only his family business but all matters with sincerity and integrity. Furthermore, he shows true devotion to his adoptive parents, which is truly admirable. Therefore, as a reward, coins are hereby granted to him.

15th day of the 1st month

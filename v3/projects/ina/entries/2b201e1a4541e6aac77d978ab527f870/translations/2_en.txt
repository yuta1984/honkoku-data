1. For epidemic disease: crush the roots and leaves of Japanese ginger (myoga) to extract the juice, and drink it in large quantities.
　　The above appears in the Chugo Bikyu-ho [Emergency Remedies]

1. For epidemic disease: crush burdock root to squeeze out the juice, drink half a tea bowl twice, then roast about a handful of mulberry leaves over fire until they turn yellow, put four bowls of water in a tea bowl, decoct it down to two bowls, drink it once and sweat well. If mulberry leaves are not available, branches may be used.
　　The above appears in Son Shinjin Shokuki [Dietary Taboos by Master Sun]

1. For epidemic disease with extremely strong fever, when one suffers and rages like a madman: crush banana plant roots to squeeze out the juice and drink it.
　　The above appears in the Chugo Bikyu-ho [Emergency Remedies]

Use the following to escape death when poisoned by any food or suffering from eating various plants, mushrooms, fish, birds, beasts, etc.

1. When suffering from poisoning by any food: lick roasted salt, or stir it into warm water and drink it.
　　However, this is especially good for poisoning from eating plant leaves.
　　The above appears in the Nosei Zensho [Complete Book of Agriculture]

1. When poisoned by any food and suffering from chest tightness, abdominal distension and pain: decoct sophora root (kushen) well in water, drink it and vomit up the food.

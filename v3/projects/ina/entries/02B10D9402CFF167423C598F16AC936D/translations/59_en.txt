Memorandum

Item: Regarding the four locations of the protected forest (go-rin) in this village's Kurihaba Shinden, together with the upper Sakabuchi slope area - although it has been claimed as private holdings, no clear boundaries can be seen. Therefore, we have no way to respond to the various inquiries and suspicions raised. Until future roles are determined, since these areas have been designated as protected forest lands, even if they are private holdings, they shall be treated the same as protected forests, and no unauthorized or arbitrary use shall be permitted without official permission. We have received and understood these instructions. For future reference, therefore as stated above.

1. As we were instructed to somehow make our way to Iida, we requested leave and departed
   We stayed one night at Shioya Yahyōeji's residence

1. Regarding our inquiries to the village officials of Asuke, we have recorded the details in a separate document. Furthermore, according to what we heard:
   About six or seven desks need to be prepared. Inkstone boxes are not required. There are rumors that in Koromo, officials of the rank of gokūban (attendant) are being dispatched,
   but in Asuke, high-ranking goshiban (messenger) officials
   visit the honjin (official inn). Also, when departing,
   young retainers carry document boxes in succession,
   dressed in formal attire, presenting official documents and
   conducting formal greetings, which we witnessed ourselves

1. Generally, we understand that they stop at places where there are government offices (jin'ya)
1. Regarding their separate actions and rounds in Sanshū region, the details are as recorded in the separate document
1. Along the entire route of their passage, road maintenance has been carried out
   Even mountain paths have been covered with sand and kept clean

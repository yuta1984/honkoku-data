- On the 28th day of the 2nd month of An'ei 7 (1778), year of the Earth Dog, he was appointed as gatekeeper of the quarters where the lord's ladies-in-waiting resided.

- On the 15th day of the 5th month of Tenmei 4 (1784), year of the Wood Dragon, he was ordered to serve at the South Gate.

- On the 22nd day of the 3rd month of Tenmei 7 (1787), year of the Fire Sheep, he was satisfactorily relieved of his South Gate guard duties. It was noted that further assignments would be determined upon his recovery from illness.

- On the 12th day of the 2nd month of Kansei 2 (1790), year of the Metal Dog, he died of illness.

Kōno Banzaemon

During the reign of Lord Honryūin
- On the 15th day of the 4th month of An'ei 9 (1780), year of the Rat, he served as an employed monk and was granted provisions for one person as financial assistance.

- On the 28th day of the 11th month of Tenmei 7 (1787), year of the Sheep, he became a substitute for his father Uemon upon request, was granted a stipend of 5 koku and provisions for two people, and served as a monk.

- On the 21st day of the 3rd month of Kansei 2 (1790), year of the Dog, he was relieved of monk duties and placed under the supervision of the ōmetsuke.

During the reign of Lord Raiin
- On the 28th day of the 12th month of Kansei 5 (1793), year of the Ox, he was transferred from monk duties to become a subordinate in the treasury and armory departments, with a stipend of 5 koku 6 tō and provisions for two people.

- On the 15th day of the 1st month of Kansei 13 (1801), year of the Rooster, he was promoted to ashigaru foot soldier with a stipend of 18 hyō and provisions for two people. He was assigned to serve in the accounting office as a subordinate in the purchasing and construction departments, while temporarily maintaining his duties in the treasury and armory departments.

- On the 7th day of the 7th month of Bunka 7 (1810), year of the Horse, he was granted the rank privilege of escort duty with an increase of 2 hyō. His duties remained the same as before.

- On the 28th day of the 11th month of the same year, he was ordered to also handle baggage affairs.

Izawa Suemon

During the reign of Lord Shinnyo-in
- Kyōhō 5, kanoe-ne (1720), 3rd month, 21st day: Granted one ryō stipend money, appointed to ashigaru rank, and assigned as clerk to the chief retainers
- Same 9th year, kinoe-tatsu (1724), 12th month, 28th day: Received additional stipend of one bu, total granted four ryō one bu
- Same 13th year, tsuchi-no-e saru (1728), 5th month, 11th day: Granted attendant duty rotation, appointed as clerk to the chief retainers
- Same 14th year, tsuchi-no-to tori (1729), 6th month, 24th day: Having served in Edo for several years under special circumstances, ordered to relocate to the domain territory, with duty assignments to be announced at Takato

During the reign of Lord Genryū-in
- Genbun 1, mizuno-e tatsu (1736), 10th month, 20th day: Appointed to ōnando (treasury keeper)
- Same 2nd year, tsuchi-no-to mi (1737), 12th month, 23rd day: Appointed as assistant purchasing officer
- Kanpō 1, kanoto-tori (1741), 12th month, 1st day: Granted increase of three hyō, total twenty-two hyō granted
- Kan'en 2, tsuchi-no-to mi (1749), 7th month, 28th day: Granted the rank of chū-koshō, increase of two hyō and one person's stipend, appointed to bunichi-kata
- Hōreki 4, kinoe-inu (1754), 10th month, 1st day: Appointed as magistrate, two hyō

During the reign of Lord Yoriyasu
- Bunsei 8 (1825), 12th month, 25th day: Previously due to poor conduct, disciplinary
　　measures were taken against father Heizō, but subsequently hearing that he has been prudent in all matters and
　　diligent in literary and martial training, this time granted provisions for 2 persons as
　　go-gōryoku, appointed to bōzu employment service
- Same 9th year (1826), 5th month, 28th day: Appointed to o-tomogata o-kachi employment service,
　　under supervision of o-kachi-gashira
- Tenpō 7 (1836), 5th month, 28th day: Promoted to o-kachi by rank, granted allocated
　　stipend of 16 hyō and provisions for 2 persons, duties continue as before
- Same year, 12th month, 28th day: Inherited deceased father Heizō's position with stipend of 22 hyō and provisions for 2 persons,
　　appointed to o-tomogata o-kyūji-gata by rank
- Same 8th year (1837), 7th month, 29th day: Due to consistently poor conduct and questionable behavior
　　being reported, rank and 4 hyō of stipend confiscated, assigned to o-kachi messenger
　　duty with house confinement
- Same year, 9th month, 20th day: Released from house confinement, due to circumstances under supervision of o-kachi-gashira

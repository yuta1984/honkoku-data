Ito Okuemon

During the reign of Lord Shinnyoin
1. On the 11th day of the 1st month of Kyōhō 12, year of the Fire Sheep (1727), appointed to the ceremonial attendant guard position, granted a stipend increase of 2 bu with 4 ryō and provisions for 2 people, assigned concurrently as secretary to the chief retainers and accounting office

1. On the 28th day of the 12th month of the same 13th year, year of the Earth Monkey (1728), granted 100 hiki in gold <<as senior retainer secretary>>

1. On the 25th day of the 12th month of the same 19th year, year of the Wood Tiger (1734), granted a stipend increase of 2 bu with 4 ryō and 2 bu

During the reign of Lord Genryūin
1. On the 5th day of the 4th month of the same 21st year, year of the Fire Dragon (1736), appointed as apprentice secretary with 2 bu additional stipend, concurrent secretary duties

1. On the 15th day of the 1st month of Genbun 6, year of the Metal Rooster (1741), appointed as secretary, granted 2 bags increase for a total stipend of 24 bags, released from secretary duties

1. On the 1st day of the 11th month of Enkyō 2, year of the Wood Ox (1745), granted the ceremonial middle page position, assigned concurrently as head of servants and warehouse supervisor for cargo management, granted increase of 2 bags and provisions for 1 person, total stipend of 26 bags and provisions for 3 people, concurrent duty room assignment

1. On the 28th day of the 6th month of Kan'en 2, year of the Earth Snake (1749), due to numerous main duties, assigned as warehouse supervisor

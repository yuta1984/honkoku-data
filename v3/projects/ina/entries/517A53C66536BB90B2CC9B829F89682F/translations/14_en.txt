Reign of Lord Raiin

1. Kyōwa 1, Year of the Metal Rooster (1801), 8th month, 11th day: As requested in Ōwada Riemon's deathbed wish, appointed as adopted son, granted succession with eighteen hyō and two-person stipend, appointed as attendant foot soldier under foot soldier captain's command

2. Bunka 12, Year of the Wood Boar (1815), 9th month, 15th day: Appointed as foot soldier inspector

Reign of Lord Yoriyasu

1. Bunpō 4, Year of the Metal Snake (1821), 1st month, 15th day: Granted increase of two hyō. Duties to continue as before

2. Same 9th year, Year of the Dog (1826), 3rd month, 15th day: Granted formal attendant guard status, appointed to storehouse affairs. Henceforth under chief administrator's command

3. Tenpō 8, Year of the Rooster (1837), 1st month, 15th day: Increase of two hyō. Duties as before

4. Kōka 2, Year of the Wood Snake (1845), 1st month, 15th day: Increase of two hyō. Having served faithfully for several years, successfully released from storehouse affairs and appointed to guard duty. For faithful service, granted 200 hiki in gold

5. Kaei 4, Year of the Metal Boar (1851), 11th month, 8th day: Due to old age and illness, granted retirement as requested, family headship passed to son Inosaku

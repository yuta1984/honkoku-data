1. Kyōhō 21, Year of the Fire Dragon (1736), 4th month, 5th day: Appointed as attendant foot soldier, granted three ryō, two bu, and two-person stipend

2. Kanpō 1, Year of the Metal Rooster (1741), 7th month, 28th day: Appointed as secretary to the senior councilors and elders

3. Same 2nd year, Year of the Water Dog (1742), 1st month, 25th day: Marriage arrangement with Fujita Heijizaemon's sister completed

4. Same year, 5th month, 11th day: Appointed to formal attendant guard duty, granted two-hyō increase, receiving eighteen hyō and two-person stipend

5. Same 3rd year, Year of the Water Boar (1743), 5th month, 28th day: Upon father Jūzaemon's retirement, inherited the family headship, granted twenty-two hyō and two-person stipend, duties to continue as before

6. Kan'en 4, Year of the Metal Sheep (1751), 4th month, 21st day: Appointed as assistant to remittance affairs, granted two-hyō increase

7. Hōreki 3, Year of the Water Rooster (1753), 12th month, 23rd day: Promoted to formal page rank, granted two-hyō and one-person stipend increase, total of twenty-six hyō and three-person stipend

8. Same 5th year, Year of the Wood Boar (1755), 9th month, 18th day: Appointed as supervisor of rice purchases. Assistant remittance role to continue as before under chief supervisor's guidance, also to assist with Nakayama Yasōji's duties

9. Same 10th year, Year of the Metal Dragon (1760), 1st month, 28th day: For diligent service in chief supervisor affairs, rewarded with formal crested court dress

10. Same 13th year, Year of the Water Sheep (1763), 9th month, 10th day: Daughter's marriage to Hama Shirōemon approved

11. Meiwa 3, Year of the Fire Dog (1766), 5th month, 8th day: Granted four-hyō increase

12. Same 5th year, Year of the Earth Mouse (1768), 4th month, 7th day: Appointed to remittance affairs with page-inspector rank, granted three-hyō increase, total of thirty-three hyō and three-person stipend

13. Same 6th year, Year of the Earth Ox (1769), 11th month, 11th day: Additionally appointed to economy affairs

14. Same 9th year, Year of the Water Dragon (1772), 1st month, 14th day: For diligent service in official duties, rewarded with hemp court dress (as remittance officer)

15. Same year, 6th month, 9th day: Regarding son Gōzō, currently serving after appointment, though the heir, the request to send him as adopted son to the Suzuki Motoshichi family was approved as unavoidable circumstances warranting special consideration

16. Same year, 6th month: Upon sending son Gōzō as adopted son to Suzuki Motoshichi family, request to make second son Wazō the heir was approved

17. Same year, 9th month: Request for second daughter to become second wife of Tanaka Jirōdayū approved

18. An'ei 3, Year of the Wood Horse (1774), 4th month, 11th day: During official duties, granted two-hyō and one-person stipend increase, total of thirty-five hyō and four-person stipend. Permitted to carry spear when traveling on official business (as remittance officer)

19. Same 4th year, Year of the Wood Sheep (1775), 10th month, 5th day: Request to change name to Jūzaemon, his late father's name, approved

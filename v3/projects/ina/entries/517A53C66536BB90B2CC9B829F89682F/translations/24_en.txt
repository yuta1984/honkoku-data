During the reign of Lord Gen'ryūin
- Hōreki 5, year of the boar (1755), 9th month, 21st day: Served as a page (bōzu) with a stipend of 5 koku
- Meiwa 9, year of the dragon (1772), 5th month, 19th day: Promoted to ashigaru (foot soldier) rank with a stipend of 18 hyō and provisions for 2 persons. Was to be temporarily assigned to Hoshino Nuinodono's service in Edo, but as relocation to Edo was not possible, continued service as a resident guard
- An'ei 5, year of the monkey (1776): Recalled to home domain and assigned to alternate duty

During the reign of Lord Hon'ryūin
- Tenmei 1, year of the ox (1781), 12th month, 17th day: Appointed to purchasing department
- Same 6th year, year of the horse (1786), 3rd month, 15th day: Appointed as ashigaru inspector
- Same 7th year, year of the sheep (1787), 2nd month, 4th day: Received an increase of 2 hyō
- Kansei 1, year of the rooster (1789), 10th month, 9th day: Granted the rank of formal attendant, appointed concurrently as head of servants and baggage supervisor

7th day
One - For the inspection of the paulownia and mulberry fields at Rokudō-hara, we went out today as follows:
　　　　　　　　　Iwasaki Minato
　　　　　　　　　Matsuzawa Ritōta
　　　　　　　　　Suzuki Kinjū
　　　　　　　　　Iwatare Takito

The village headman Riemon of Suehiro village and others, along with group leader Orita, served as guides and we inspected 95,000 tsubo of paulownia and mulberry fields. We found that the paulownia trees were poorly grown with many dead trees, so they would certainly not meet the expected standards. The mulberry trees also had problems with poor quality wood continuing to grow. Therefore, we reported to the bureau chief upon our return that we would like this land to be handed down to the village.

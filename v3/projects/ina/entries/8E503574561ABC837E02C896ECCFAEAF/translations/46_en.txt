　　　　　Separate Document
　　Naval affairs are an urgent matter at present, and particularly
　　the construction of ships and vessels being of critical importance, officials from the Ministry of Military Affairs
　　are being dispatched to investigate good timber.
　　Therefore, when they make their official visits, officials from each fu, han, and ken shall
　　attend and ensure there are no obstacles to their work.
　　　Shinbi March　　　　　Council of State

　　For the purpose of warship construction, officials from the Ministry of Military Affairs
　　are being dispatched to investigate good timber.
　　As official proclamations have already been issued regarding this matter,
　　before the dispatch of said officials, within their jurisdiction, even on private estates,
　　all fine timber such as zelkova, chestnut, pine, beech, and shioji
　　must be strictly prohibited from cutting.
　　　　However, if there are unavoidable circumstances requiring cutting, applications must be
　　　　submitted to the Ministry of Military Affairs.

29th day
1. We inspected the government forests of Kamiyamada Village and Kitabayashi Village, then went to Sakurai Village to plant cedar and cypress in that village's government forest. I reported this matter to the bureau chief and submitted the following notification to the office supervisor:
　　　I am going on official duty to Nakazawa district today for government business. I hereby report this matter.

　　　　　To the Domain Office

1. After 4 o'clock, I took along assistant Suzuki Kinjū to Kamiyamada Village's government forest to inspect dead standing cypress trees, then proceeded from Kitabayashi Village to Sakurai Village and stayed at the headman Ichiemon's residence.

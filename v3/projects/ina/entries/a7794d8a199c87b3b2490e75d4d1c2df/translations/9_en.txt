Humbly submitted
I am an incompetent person, but unexpectedly I was
appointed to the position of town representative, which I consider a grateful honor. I have
served for several years, but have become ill and find it difficult to perform my duties,
so I request to be relieved of my position
Regarding the above matter　　　　　Fukuromachi
　Ansei 6, Year of the Boar, First Month　　Town Representative Ikusichi
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Former Official Tōzaemon
　To the Magistrate's Office
On the 23rd day, same as above, approval was granted at the end of the month

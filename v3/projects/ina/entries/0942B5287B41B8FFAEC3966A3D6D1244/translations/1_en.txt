　from Nishiharu-chika village. He requested that I
　conduct a survey regarding the current land dispute
　between Shinden farmland. However, due to my
　advanced age and chronic asthma condition,
　it would be difficult for me to traverse mountains and fields,
　so I declined the request. However, around
　11:50 PM, two men from Shinden farmland, Matsuzawa Shinemon
　and Ozawa Seirokuemon, visited and made the same
　surveying request. Although I declined as mentioned above,

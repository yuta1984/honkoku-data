Tōke Forest
One miscellaneous tree stumps, 5 se bu (15 ken × 10 ken)
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Forest keeper contractors: Sōgorō
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Shōbei
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Currently:　　　　Hidezō
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Sezaemon

The above is an official forest that was sold to Kichizaemon of Kitafukuike Village in the 11th year of Bunsei, year of the Rat (1828)
In the 6th year of Tenpō, year of the Sheep (1835), a petition to donate the mountain was granted as requested with official commendation
The above official forest keeper was appointed
In the 1st year of Bunsei, year of the Tiger (1818), officials went to inspect the boundaries and marked 36 boundary trees plus
3 burial mounds

　　　December, Year of the Horse
1. Lacquer shops shall pay an annual levy of 500 mon in copper coins per establishment
1. Tofu shops in rural towns shall pay a levy of 200 mon in copper coins per establishment throughout the year
The above items have been newly established as service fees and levies, so they shall be announced throughout the town. Officials shall conduct strict examinations and periodically investigate changes in personnel numbers, etc. The above.

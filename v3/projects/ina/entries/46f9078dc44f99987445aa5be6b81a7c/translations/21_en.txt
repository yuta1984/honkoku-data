　　　Memorandum
October 3rd
Regarding the memorial day of the lord's wife, for a period of 3 days,
as on days when it is difficult to make external requests each month, musical performances and construction work
shall be refrained from.
The above matter shall be announced to the countryside and towns. That is all.
　　Year of the Ox, October 25th

　　　Memorandum
1. Regarding carpenters, sawyers, blacksmiths, and plasterers - these four trades:
　From this spring, the construction office shall reform the master craftsmen system and
　it is thought this should be done accordingly. Regarding this, those of the four trades
　shall go to the master craftsmen and have their names entered in the registry ledger.
　For ledgers submitted to the office, names in that section shall henceforth
　be removed.
1. For those who have quit their family trade, or if there are relatives who wish to
　begin a family trade, they shall go to the master craftsmen to be registered.
　When craftsmen have illness or other problems, the same applies.
1. If there are improper matters related to trades, there will be others besides those from the construction
　office. Henceforth, the master craftsmen
　shall conduct examinations, and it is thought this
　should be done accordingly.
1. Among those of the four trades, if there are already those going to work sites,
　from the assistants to the master craftsmen,
　that decision shall be reformed and names recorded in the ledger.

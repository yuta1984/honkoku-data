Hōreki 11th year, Year of the Metal Snake
Last month, 12th day
Due to the passing of the Great Retired Shogun (Ōgosho-sama), construction work and musical performances are prohibited. Everyone should exercise strict restraint and pay careful attention to fire prevention and other matters.
The above should be announced throughout the town. The above.
6th month, 15th day

Memorandum
1. Firing威し鉄砲 (threatening firearms) in town and villages should be suspended
1. People from town and villages going out to other places should be suspended
   Those with unavoidable circumstances may do so only after reporting their reasons
1. In general, musical performances and entertainments should be strictly prohibited
1. Shops, stalls, bird vendors, and of course all commerce in town should be suspended
1. Town watch duties should be assigned throughout the town
The above points should be announced throughout the town. The above.
6th month, 15th day

Memorandum
1. Regarding opening shops in town, from tomorrow the 19th, only upper curtains may be opened, and commerce should be conducted discretely so as not to attract attention
   However, lower curtains should be kept to the side, and projecting displays should also be suspended
1. Regarding peddlers selling spring goods and various items, from tomorrow the 19th, they should conduct business quietly and not in conspicuous places
The above points should be announced throughout the town. The above.
6th month, 18th day

Opinion
　Copy of official notice from Edo
Regarding old silver, it was announced in the previous Year of the Rat that it should be used in circulation at the prescribed rate. However, there are those who neither put it into circulation nor submit it to the silver mint for exchange, keeping it stored away. Particularly in the five home provinces (Kinai), northern provinces, Chūgoku, Shikoku, and western provinces, it is heard that there is much old silver. In distant provinces, after the exchange period, there may still be stored Keichō silver, old silver, and cupelled silver. Therefore, silver mint officials shall travel to those provinces and purchase at a 50% premium rate. Keichō silver, old silver, and cupelled silver should not be stored but should be exchanged for receiving the standard substitute silver. Of course, submitting to the silver mint for exchange shall be at one's discretion. However, for exchange applications submitted to the silver mint by the coming 5th month of the Year of the Rat, exchange will be at the current premium rate.

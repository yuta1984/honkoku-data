Hōreki 4, Year of the Dog, 10th month

Since the calendar reform of Jōkyō, the Jōkyō calendar has been used until now, but as there were discrepancies, astronomical measurements were ordered. This time in Kyoto, a calendar reform imperial decree was issued, the matter of determining the calendar name was carried out, and the new calendar was designated as the Hōreki Kinoe-Inu Calendar. Accordingly, the new calendar shall be distributed from the coming year.

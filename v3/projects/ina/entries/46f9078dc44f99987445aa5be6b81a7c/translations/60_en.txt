　Hōreki 9th year
　　4th month, 1st day of the rabbit year

The young lord passed away on the 22nd day of the 3rd month.
It was ordered that congratulations be offered on the 6th day of the 4th month.

Regarding the name of the young lord:
Since it has been changed from Tango-no-kami-sama, henceforth the character "tan" (丹) shall be avoided by both men and women. Moreover, family names shall not become Tango or Tanba either.
The above matter shall be announced throughout the town.
　　Same year, 12th month

The weights used for gold and silver transactions were reformed during the Kanbun era.
It has been heard that these weights are causing confusion among money changers. Therefore, regarding the areas of Kyoto, Osaka, and Sakai, [it was ordered] not to conduct sales elsewhere until [reaching] the licensed dealers, but to have Gotō Shirōbē purchase them at the standard price, and not to secretly trade old weights of light measure. This has been announced repeatedly, but it is still heard that in the western provinces and along the Nagasaki route, old weights are still being widely used in trade. Hereafter, secretly conducting trade is naturally forbidden, and [such weights] should be deposited and delivered to Shirōbē's office. However, inspection officials from Shirōbē's office will make rounds and confiscate suspicious weights. This matter should be strictly observed.

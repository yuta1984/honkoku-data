Regarding the distant memorial service for Bunki-in-sama, Buddhist memorial services will be held in Edo from tomorrow the 10th through the 14th. Construction work, musical performances, festivals, and memorial services need not be suspended, however regarding musical performances, they should be restrained during the aforementioned memorial service period. Reception activities and miscellaneous work other than essential occupations should be stopped, and particular attention should be paid to fire prevention.

The above matter should be announced throughout the town. 
己 October 9th

1. On the coming 4th day
Due to the memorial service for Tokutei-in-sama from the 3rd to the 4th, memorial prayers will be held at Manko-ji temple. Throughout the town, fire prevention should be especially carefully enforced, and all other matters should be conducted with restraint.

1. The town's self-defense guard from the 3rd at midday until the samurai family's temple visits are completed should follow the same procedures as previous memorial services. Furthermore, the self-defense guard and patrol guard on the night of the 3rd should perform their duties with even greater diligence.

1. From the 3rd to the 4th, the roads and bridges from Sagari-bashi to Yokocho in front of Manko-ji temple gate should be cleaned.

The above should be understood accordingly.

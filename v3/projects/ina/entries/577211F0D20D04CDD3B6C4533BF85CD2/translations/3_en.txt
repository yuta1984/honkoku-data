Those with fifteen-person stipends shall be treated the same as those receiving one hundred koku. However, since the months of New Year and year-end are exempted, laborers shall be provided as stated above for the remaining ten months.

1. To prevent wandering about recklessly in the castle town, throughout the town, and in various rural areas, and to avoid improper and rough behavior, supervising authorities must, of course, strictly instruct everyone from retainers of the domain down to the lowest servants.

1. When there are official punishments being carried out, no one except officials shall be present to witness them.

1. Street sumo wrestling and setting off fireworks are strictly prohibited.

The above has been decreed and must be strictly observed.

Genroku 4th year, 12th month, 1st day    Naito Kazue [monogram]

We humbly submit this written petition
I have been working as a blacksmith, and in Bunsei 7, Year of Metal Monkey,
I was appointed as an overseer of fellow craftsmen and given half-duty status, and furthermore
this time I have been granted release from duty, which I consider a most grateful situation. Therefore, I petition for
the rewriting of my duty tablet. Above　　　　　Hokoji-machi　Sohachi

The above petition by Sohachi is accurate. Above

Humbly submitted oral petition

Item: Regarding my cousin Denzō of Satoya in Hokomotemachi, who wishes to serve as an apprentice clerk (urikobōkō) at Mannya Tōzaemon's establishment in Anayamamachi, Kōfu, to learn commerce, and to work there for a while. Therefore, I humbly request that during his service period, he be granted an unmarked seal certificate for the religious sect inspection.

I humbly request that you grant this petition with your compassion as stated above, for which I would be most grateful. Respectfully submitted.

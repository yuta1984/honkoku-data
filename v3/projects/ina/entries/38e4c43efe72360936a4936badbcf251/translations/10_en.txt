One: Regarding Sakabe Teibei-sama's group: on the 15th day they lodged at Matsushima inn, and on the 16th day they remained due to rainy weather.

One: On the 17th day they departed early in the morning, conducted surveying along the way, and lodged at Hirade village.
One: At Higuchi village they rested and were served tea and red rice.

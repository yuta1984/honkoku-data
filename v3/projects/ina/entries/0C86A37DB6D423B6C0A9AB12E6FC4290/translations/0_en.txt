Memorandum

Yesterday, the 28th day, at half a quarter before the hour of the Monkey, there was an imperial visit from Sento Imperial Palace to the Forbidden Palace. His Majesty returned in good health and spirits at the hour of the Dog. We inform you of this for your knowledge. The above.

4th month, 29th day
　　　　　Former Middle Counselor Chigusa
　　　　　Former Major Counselor Yanagiwara

To Lord Naito Yamato-no-kami

Adopted son
　　　　　　　　　　　　Ōhashi Seikurō
- Anei 1, year of the Water Dragon (1772), 23rd day of the 12th month: Summoned and appointed as foot soldier in the escort unit, granted a stipend of 16 hyō and provisions for 2 people, under the command of the foot soldier captain
- Anei 3, year of the Wood Horse (1774), 20th day of the 3rd month: Due to reports of misconduct, granted permanent leave. However, prohibited from loitering around both the Edo residences and the domain residence

　　　　　　　　　　　Adopted son
　　　　　　　　　　　　Ōhashi Sen'emon
During the reign of Lord Honryū-in
- Anei 7, year of the Earth Dog (1778), 25th day of the 5th month: Inherited his late father Tameuemon's position, granted a stipend of 18 hyō and provisions for 2 people, appointed as foot soldier in the escort unit
- Anei 12, year of the Ox (1783), 15th day of the 5th month: Appointed as proxy pilgrim to Myōgi on the coming 19th day at the hour of the Rabbit
- Tenmei 4, year of the Wood Dragon (1784), 7th day of the 1st month: Due to prolonged illness, unable to perform duties as gate guard and clothing manager, relieved of both positions as requested
- Same year, 1st day of the 3rd month: Request approved to adopt Senzō, second son of Kusano Gorōdayū, retainer of Lord Ōkubo Shimosa-no-kami
- Same year, 15th day of the 5th month: Due to illness, retired as requested and headship granted to Senzō {{marginal note: continuing from grandfather's generation}}
　　　　　Service record

　　　　　　　　　　　　Ōhashi Gondayū
　　　　　　　　　　　　　Original name Senzō
- Tenmei 4, year of the Wood Dragon (1784), 15th day of the 5th month: Upon his father Sen'emon's retirement as requested, inherited the headship, granted a stipend of 18 hyō and provisions for 2 people, appointed as foot soldier in the escort unit under the command of the foot soldier captain
- Tenmei 5, year of the Wood Snake (1785), 23rd day of the 1st month: Changed name to Gondayū (previously Senzō)
- Same year, 17th day of the 5th month: Appointed as proxy pilgrim to Myōgi on the coming 21st day at the hour of the Rabbit
- Tenmei 8, year of the Earth Monkey (1788), 29th day of the 1st month: Appointed to manage the domain residence kitchen
- Same year, 28th day of the 2nd month: Absconded {{marginal note: while in Edo}}

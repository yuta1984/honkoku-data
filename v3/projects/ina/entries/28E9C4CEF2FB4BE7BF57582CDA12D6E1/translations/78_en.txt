- On the 27th day of the 3rd month of Kyōhō 10 (1725), Year of the Snake, due to Okumura Uemon's retirement, he was ordered to handle small monetary transactions.
- On the 25th day of the 4th month of the same year, small transactions were deemed unnecessary.
- On the 15th day of the 12th month of the same year, with an additional 1 ryō, his total stipend became 6 ryō.
- On the 24th day of the 2nd month of Kyōhō 15 (1730), Year of the Dog, due to annual tribute duties, he handed over [responsibilities]. On the 26th day, he was given buckwheat noodles.
- On the 12th day of the 7th month of Kyōhō 18 (1733), Year of the Ox, he was appointed as secretary to the senior councilors.
- On the 28th day of the 8th month of Kyōhō 19 (1734), Year of the Tiger, he was appointed to provisions management.

Reign of Lord Genryūin
- On the 13th day of the 5th month of Genbun 3 (1738), Year of the Horse, he was appointed to the additional role of managing the custody of various tools distributed to various offices. Henceforth, he was to examine tools in custody at various offices that were not in the original inventory, prepare master ledgers, and also receive from the construction office various tools distributed to the garrison quarters of the retainers. According to established procedures, after distribution at the quarters was completed, tools were to be withdrawn, and if damage was reported, repairs were to be arranged.
- On the 28th day of the 7th month of the same year, he was appointed as supervisor of the complete kitchen cooking operations and rice distribution for cooking, to be held concurrently with provisions management and tool custody. He received an increase of 2 hyō, making his total stipend 28 hyō. His rank was that of attendant.
- On the 3rd day of the 4th month of Genbun 4 (1739), Year of the Sheep, his petition to take the sister of Okano Katsuhachi from the household of Lord Morikawa Oribenokama as wife was approved. He took her in marriage on the 20th day of the same month.
- On the last day of the 8th month of the same year, due to numerous duties, he was relieved of cooking operations.
- On the night of the 14th day of the 8th month of Genbun 5 (1740), Year of the Monkey, he committed suicide in a state of madness and died after the 15th day. His mother was granted sustenance provisions for one person.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　Original name: Yūseki
- On the 7th day of the 7th month of Kyōhō 19 (1734), Year of the Tiger, he was promoted to foot soldier and appointed as monk inspector, granted additional stipend of 4 ryō 1 bu and provisions for two people, and ordered to return to lay life.
- On the 7th day of the 8th month of Kyōhō 20 (1735), Year of the Rabbit, he was appointed to provisions duty. [His stipend was] 4 ryō and provisions for two people.
- On the 26th day of the 12th month of Kanpō 3 (1743), Year of the Boar, he received an increase of 2 hyō, making his total stipend 20 hyō.
- On the 12th day of the 4th month of Enkyō 3 (1746), Year of the Tiger, he was appointed to attendant rank.
- On the 2nd day of the 12th month of Kan'en 1 (1748), Year of the Dragon, he was appointed as New Year ceremonial attendant.

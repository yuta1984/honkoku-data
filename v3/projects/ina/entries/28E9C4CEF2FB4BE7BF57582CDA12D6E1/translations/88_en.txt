- 2nd year (Bunsei 2, 1819), 4th month, 28th day: He was relieved of his duties as assistant cook supervisor for the lord's wife, gate guard, and kitchen supervisor without incident, and was appointed as assistant attendant. Accordingly, he was assigned to the lower residence service under the supervision of the foot soldier captain.

- 8th year (Bunsei 8, 1825), 1st month, 15th day: He received an increase of two hyō of rice in the foot soldier ranking. His duties remained the same as before.

- 9th year (Bunsei 9, 1826), 3rd month, 26th day: He was appointed as accounting department trainee under the supervision of the chief retainer.

- Same year, 6th month, 24th day: He was appointed as secretary for the pressing matters department.

- 10th year (Bunsei 10, 1827), 4th month, 1st day: Since he also began serving at the castle, he was granted five to of rice as allowance.

- 12th year (Bunsei 12, 1829), 9th month, 28th day: He was appointed as messenger under the supervision of the foot soldier captain.

- Tenpō 2 (1831), 6th month, 28th day: He was appointed as South Gate guard.

- Same year, 7th month, 29th day: On the 27th, when the retired lord departed through the South Gate, there was an incident of carelessness and disrespect, and he was ordered into house arrest.

- End of 8th month: He was pardoned from house arrest.

- 7th year (Tenpō 7, 1836), 5th month, 27th day: Died of illness.

- Bunsei 12 (1829), 12th month, 25th day: He was appointed as temporary hired foot soldier attendant and granted assistance of two-person stipend.

- Tenpō 2 (1831), 9th month, 1st day: He was appointed to temporary service at Lord Naitō Iori's residence.

- 3rd year (Tenpō 3, 1832), 5th month, 13th day: Due to reorganization, he was relieved of service at Lord Naitō Iori's residence without incident.

- Same year, 12th month, 8th day: Died of illness.

- Tenpō 5 (1834), 6th month, 3rd day: He was appointed as hired foot soldier for temporary service at Lord Naitō Iori's temporary residence, granted assistance of two-person stipend, under the supervision of the chief retainer.

- 7th year (Tenpō 7, 1836), 7th month, 20th day: He inherited his father Kanzaemon's position with a stipend of seventeen hyō and two-person support, was granted the rank of foot soldier, and his duties remained the same as before.

　　　　Service record

　　　　　　　　　　　Son of Tomosaemon, initially Ichigoro
　　　　　　　　　　　　Nakamura Ichishiro

During the reign of Lord Honryu-in
- On the 13th day of the 5th month of An'ei 10 (1781), Year of the Ox, due to his young age, he was granted support provisions for 4 people, with the promise that upon reaching maturity he would be appointed to a position appropriate to his inherited status.

- On the 8th day of the 3rd month of Tenmei 4 (1784), Year of the Dragon, he was appointed as yobitsugu (page/junior attendant).

- On the 28th day of the 11th month of Tenmei 7 (1787), Year of the Sheep, having come of age, he was granted his deceased father's inherited position of 18 hyō and provisions for 2 people, with service duties as before, to be assigned ashigaru duties from time to time.

- On the 21st day of the 3rd month of Kansei 2 (1790), Year of the Dog, he was appointed to messenger duties.

During the reign of Lord Yoriyori
- On the 5th day of the 5th month of Kansei 3 (1791), Year of the Boar, he was assigned to service at the lord's upper residence.

- On the 12th day of the 3rd month of Kansei 4 (1792), Year of the Rat, he was also ordered to report for attendant duties.

- On the 1st day of the 2nd month of Kansei 5 (1793), Year of the Ox, he was henceforth placed under the supervision of the ashigaru commander.

- On the 24th day of the 5th month of Kansei 7 (1795), Year of the Rabbit, his sister's marriage to Masazo, younger brother of Suzuki Hachigoro of magistrate Wada Hyodayu's group, was approved upon petition.

- On the 1st day of the 6th month of Kansei 8 (1796), Year of the Dragon, he changed his name to Ichishiro, having previously been called Ichigoro.

- On the 25th day of the 8th month of the same year, he was ordered to serve as proxy pilgrim to Myogi on the Day of the Rabbit, the 8th day of the following month.

- On the 5th day of the 2nd month of Kansei 12 (1800), Year of the Monkey, he was appointed ashigaru inspector, with his assignment to the accounting office continuing as before.

- On the 15th day of the 5th month of Kyōwa 1 (1801), Year of the Rooster, he was granted the status of formal attendant, appointed to clothing management duties, with his accounting office assignment continuing as before, receiving an increase of 2 hyō and placed under magistrate supervision.

- On the 12th day of the 8th month of Kyōwa 2 (1802), Year of the Dog, he was ordered to move to the lord's lower residence.

- On the 14th day of the 9th month of the same year, due to improper conduct, by lordly decree he was to be granted permanent dismissal, but through extraordinary mercy he was retained in the household, stripped of his formal position, returned to ashigaru status with messenger duties, and granted 18 hyō and provisions for 2 people.

- On the 11th day of the 10th month of Bunka 1 (1804), Year of the Rat, his stipend was confiscated and he was granted permanent dismissal, resulting in the extinction of his family line.

- On the 4th day of the 5th month of Bunka 11 (1814), Year of the Dog, he was granted permission to enter the lord's residence.

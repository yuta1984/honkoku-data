1. An'ei 4th year, Year of the Sheep (1775), April 19th: As Tomioka Genshichi had petitioned during his lifetime, adoption approved, moved to adoptive family and to observe prescribed mourning period

1. Same year, May 19th: Appointed to lower mansion administration

Reign of Lord Honryūin
1. Same year, July 24th: As Genshichi had petitioned on his deathbed, adoption approved, inherited position with stipend of eighteen hyō and support for two retainers, appointed as foot soldier in the lord's escort, under command of foot soldier captain

1. Same 6th year, Year of the Rooster (1777), November 20th: Changed name to Genshichi after deceased father

1. Tenmei 6th year, Year of the Horse (1786), January 4th: Appointed as proxy pilgrim to Myōgi on the coming 10th day of the Rabbit

1. Kansei 2nd year, Year of the Dog (1790), February 20th: Appointed inspector of foot soldiers, granted two hyō salary increase

1. Same year, November 4th: Ordered to show restraint due to late return from outing yesterday, pardoned on the 11th in Osaka

Reign of Lord Raijikun
1. Kansei 5th year, Year of the Ox (1793), February 1st: Appointed foot soldier sub-captain concurrently serving as personal attendant captain, granted rank of escort guard with two hyō salary increase

1. Kyōwa 1st year, Year of the Rooster (1801), June 15th: Marriage to dependent of Maeno Yonezō, retainer of Lord Kose Hyūganomami, approved

1. Same year, October 26th: Divorced wife

1. Same year, October 30th: Appointed to catering for Lord Shōun'in, under steward supervision

1. Same 2nd year, Year of the Dog (1802), November 10th: Marriage to dependent of Inoue Kichizaemon of Lord Suzuki Kunisaburō's household approved

1. Same 3rd year, Year of the Boar (1803), March 22nd: Fled domain, house abolished

Reign of Lord Genryūin
1. Genbun 4th year, Year of the Sheep (1739), December 23rd: Newly appointed to tea ceremony duties in Osaka, granted rank equivalent to foot soldier with stipend of seventeen hyō and support for two retainers, duties as before, granted allowance for formal coat

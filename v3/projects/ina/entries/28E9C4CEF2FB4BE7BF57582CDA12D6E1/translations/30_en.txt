1. 6th year of Hōei, Year of the Ox (1709), June 8th: Submitted family register
　　　　Both home province and birthplace: Musashi Province, Edo, 27 years old, father: retainer Gonzaemon of Sakai Shuritayū
During the reign of Shinnyo-in-sama
1. 7th year of Kyōhō, Year of the Tiger (1722), July 11th: Appointed as gate guard at upper residence
1. Same 10th year, Year of the Snake (1725), August 12th: Wife gave birth yesterday evening
1. Same 14th year, Year of the Rooster (1729), March 5th: Birth
1. Same 18th year, Year of the Ox (1733), April 18th: Matsumoto rōnin named Kitō Kyūzaemon, now thirty years old in this Ox year, petition to adopt as son approved, taken in on the 21st
1. Same 20th year, Year of the Rabbit (1735), April 28th: As petitioned, adopted son Kyūzaemon granted permanent leave, no need for restraint inquiry regarding this matter
During the reign of Genryū-in-sama
1. Same year, December 23rd: Daughter died of illness
1. 2nd year of Genbun, Year of the Snake (1737), December 19th: Appointed to formal attendant position
1. Same 5th year, Year of the Horse (1740), August: Petition approved to take as second wife the sister of cousin Yajima Han'nai, a rōnin
1. 2nd year of Kanpō, Year of the Dog (1742), October 1st: Petition approved to adopt Kitahara Banshichi, brother of Kitahara Bungoemon who serves as middle page under Ōkubo Aki-no-kami-sama, 25 years old
1. 2nd year of Enkyō, Year of the Ox (1745), March 2nd: Son Kumeshichi did not meet expectations, returned to Kitahara Bungoemon's household, notification submitted
1. Same year, June 19th: Petition approved to adopt Hirano Shichizō, 26 years old, rōnin of Honda Nakatsukasa-taifu-sama
1. Same 3rd year, Year of the Tiger (1746), December 26th: Granted increase of two hyō
1. 2nd year of Hōreki, Year of the Monkey (1752), May: Died of illness
1. Same year, July 22nd: Succession to son Genshichi with twenty hyō, support for two retainers, appointed to attendant rank

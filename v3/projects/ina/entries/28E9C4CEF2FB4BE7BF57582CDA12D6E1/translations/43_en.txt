- Kanpō 2, year of the Water Dog (1742), 18th day of the 1st month: Received an increase of 2 hyō, totaling 20 hyō
- Kanpō 3, year of the Water Boar (1743), 26th day of the 12th month: Increase of 2 hyō
- Enkyō 2, year of the Wood Ox (1745), 2nd day of the 11th month: Temporarily appointed as assistant scribe for the administrative office
- Same year, 11th day of the intercalary 12th month: No longer needed as assistant
- Enkyō 3, year of the Fire Tiger (1746), 28th day of the 8th month: Appointed to concurrently serve as temporary purchasing officer, receiving the standard role stipend of one person's provisions
- Enkyō 5, year of the Earth Dragon (1748), 10th day of the 4th month: Request approved to take as second wife the daughter of Taguchi Buemon, a guard of the main castle's inner quarters under Tsuchiya Hyōbu-shōyū
- Hōreki 5, year of the Wood Boar (1755), 5th day of the 4th month: Regarding Nogami Keisuke's flight due to debt, though a colleague, was ill and provided his seal as guarantor. Received reprimand for giving his personal seal to outsiders, even though they were colleagues
- Hōreki 6, year of the Fire Rat (1756), 28th day of the 3rd month: Appointed as secretary (goyuhitsu), granted the rank of middle page (gochūkoshō), with an increase of 4 hyō and 1 person's provisions, totaling 26 hyō and 3 people's provisions
- Same year, 17th day of the 5th month: Request approved to take as second wife the younger sister of Kasahara Kingorō from the unit of Yamaguchi Koheiji, head of the main castle's front kitchen
- Hōreki 7, year of the Fire Ox (1757), 13th day of the 4th month: Divorced his wife
- Same year, 9th day of the 6th month: Request approved to take as second wife the daughter of Nishimura Heinai, retainer of Ōkubo Jinzaemon of the advance guard
- Hōreki 8, year of the Earth Tiger (1758), 1st day of the 3rd month: As requested, father Jōsuke retired and Tameuemon inherited the family headship, receiving the previous stipend of 26 hyō and 3 people's provisions
- Hōreki 10, year of the Metal Dragon (1760), 28th day of the 1st month: Due to advanced age and deteriorating eyesight causing difficulty with documents, requested resignation from duties. However, officials determined he had not yet reached old age, and if his eye condition wasn't too severe, he should continue serving after proper rest. For fine writing difficulties, he could consult with colleagues for assistance
- Hōreki 11, year of the Metal Snake (1761), 9th day of the 3rd month: Received increase of 1 person's provisions, totaling 26 hyō and 4 people's provisions
- Hōreki 14, year of the Wood Monkey (1764), 24th day of the 3rd month: Request for resignation rejected by officials
- Meiwa 5, year of the Earth Rat (1768), 1st day of the 2nd month: Appointed as inspector of middle pages, with an increase of 4 hyō, totaling 30 hyō and 4 people's provisions. Secretary duties continued as before
- Meiwa 7, year of the Metal Tiger (1770), 28th day of the 7th month: Requested resignation from both positions due to old age. However, since his calligraphy remained skilled and he could handle official duties, he was to continue estate and official duties as before for the time being. Due to difficulty walking, castle attendance was excused

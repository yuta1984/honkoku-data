- On the 5th day of the 4th month of the 11th year (1761), Year of the Snake, as the young lord was entering the domain but there were few personnel in the area, he was assigned to extended garrison duty until the coming Year of the Sheep, with Edo garrison duty taking precedence.

- On the 11th day of the 11th month of the same year, due to his diligent service day and night during the official seal transport, he was granted soup and sake. 《At Takatō》

- On the 21st day of the 10th month of Meiwa 2 (1765), Year of the Rooster, he was appointed as chief clerk.

- On the 20th day of the 11th month of Meiwa 7 (1770), Year of the Tiger, his marriage arrangement petition was approved.

- On the 12th day of the 4th month of An'ei 3 (1774), Year of the Horse, he was granted formal escort rank with an increase of 2 hyō.

- On the 5th day of the 10th month of An'ei 3, his petition to take the sister of farmer Genemon from Nagaoka village in Minowa domain as his second wife was approved. 《On the 12th day of the 12th month of An'ei 4 (1775), Year of the Sheep, he was appointed deputy magistrate with an increase of 4 hyō and provisions for one person, plus role allowance of provisions for two people》

Reign of Lord Honryūin

- On the 1st day of the 11th month of Tenmei 3 (1783), Year of the Rabbit, following his father Tōhachi's petition for retirement, he inherited the family headship and was granted a stipend of 24 hyō with provisions for three people.

- On the 5th day of the same month, he was assigned a change of administrative territory to supervise Kawashimo and Seba villages.

- On the 28th day of the 12th month of the same year, he was ordered to inspect due to poor rice harvests that autumn. Having worked diligently for several days and subsequently completing the accounting, he was granted 300 mon in gold.

- On the 3rd day of the intercalary 1st month of Tenmei 4 (1784), Year of the Dragon, by petition he changed his original surname to Kojima.

- On the 10th day of the 11th month of Tenmei 5 (1785), Year of the Snake, he was granted formal middle page status with an increase of 2 hyō.

- On the 21st day of the 9th month of Tenmei 7 (1787), Year of the Sheep, he was appointed to warehouse administration under chamberlain supervision.

Reign of Lord Yorimoto

- On the 3rd day of the 12th month of Kansei 4 (1792), Year of the Rat, he was relieved of his official duties and appointed to guard rotation.

- On the 28th day of the 12th month of Kansei 5 (1793), Year of the Ox, regarding the matter of Hyōdō Daemon, his wife and children were sentenced to light banishment. 《He was Daemon's older brother》 They were banned from within 10 ri of Edo, as well as from Kyoto, Osaka, Kōfu, the Tōkaidō route, and Shinano province.

- On the 8th day of the 6th month of Bunka 11 (1814), Year of the Dog, regarding Hachirozaemon's younger brother Daemon who had been sentenced to banishment years earlier for serious crimes, on this occasion of the birth celebration of Lord Takechiyō, amnesty was proclaimed by the shogunate, and by this gracious consideration he was to be permitted to return to service. However, as it was heard that Hachirozaemon's sons had all died, it was difficult to issue the order.

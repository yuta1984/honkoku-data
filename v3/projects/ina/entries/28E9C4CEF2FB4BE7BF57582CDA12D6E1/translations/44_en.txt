- Meiwa 9, year of the Water Dragon (1772), 23rd day of the 9th month: Having no male heir, request approved to adopt Kurokawa Seikurō (28 years old), nephew of Miki Santayū, retainer of Hisanaga Sagami-no-kami
- Same year, 19th day of the 11th month: Appointed to manage the lower residence kitchen, and appointed as secretary to the young lord. Inspector of middle pages continued as before
- Anei 3, year of the Wood Horse (1774), 20th day of the 3rd month: By his lordship's consideration, due to illness, relieved of both positions and ordered into house confinement
- Same year, 9th day of the 4th month: House confinement lifted

During the reign of Lord Zuiun-in
- Anei 5, year of the Fire Monkey (1776), 15th day of the 10th month: Request approved to adopt Gonjiūrō (22 years old), younger brother sharing the same family name as Takamoto Zenzaburō of Ōkatahira village, Hara district, Bōshū province, under the jurisdiction of Iizuka Ihē. Gonjiūrō was the maternal cousin of Nishio Gonnosuke, who was the nephew of his wife and served under Miyauchi-gō

During the reign of Lord Honryū-in
- Same year, 27th day of the 12th month: Adopted son Gonjiūrō was brought in and renamed Kimata
- Anei 6, year of the Fire Cock (1777), 24th day of the 1st month: Due to old age, by gracious mercy appointed to alternating duty at the lower residence
- Same year, 14th day of the 2nd month: Adopted son Kimata divorced due to misconduct not being properly supervised
- Anei 7, year of the Earth Dog (1778), 6th day of the 3rd month: Request approved to adopt Kōshichi (27 years old), second son of Shinozaki Genkichi, physician to Lord Aoyama Daizen-no-suke. Brought in on the 7th day of the same month and renamed Sen'emon
- Same year, 28th day of the 3rd month: Died of illness

- Hōreki 11, year of the Metal Snake (1761), 9th day of the 3rd month: Henceforth to serve by reporting to the duty office for roll call, receiving one person's provisions, though without formal rank
- Meiwa 2, year of the Wood Cock (1765), 15th day of the 6th month: Appointed as foot soldier in the escort unit, granted 16 hyō and 2 people's provisions, under the command of the foot soldier captain
- Same year, 24th day of the 12th month: Appointed as proxy pilgrim to Myōgi for the coming New Year
- Meiwa 7, year of the Metal Tiger (1770), 30th day of the 1st month: Absconded

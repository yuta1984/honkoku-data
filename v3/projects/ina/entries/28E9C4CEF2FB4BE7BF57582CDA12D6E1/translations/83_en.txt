　　　　　　　　　　　　Kitahara Ihē
During the reign of Lord Yoriyasu
- On the 28th day of the intercalary 1st month of Bunsei 5 (1822), Year of the Horse, he was appointed as adopted son according to Kitahara Gonnosuke's deathbed wishes, granted a hereditary stipend of 5 koku with provisions for two people, and appointed as head monk attendant.
- On the 6th day of the 8th month of Bunsei 6 (1823), Year of the Sheep, he absconded and his house was dissolved.
- On the 13th day of the 9th month of Bunsei 7 (1824), Year of the Monkey, Ihē's adoptive mother petitioned at Taishōji Temple, and given that her husband Gonnosuke had served for many years at Lord Honjuin's palace and this year marked the first anniversary memorial service, both were granted lifelong provisions for one person as special relief.
- On the 16th day of the 10th month of Tenpō 8 (1837), Year of the Rooster, she died of illness.

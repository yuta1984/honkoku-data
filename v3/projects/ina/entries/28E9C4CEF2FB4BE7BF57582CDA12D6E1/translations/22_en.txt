Reign of Lord Yorimoto
1. 3rd year of Kansei, Year of the Boar (1791), October 23rd: After Baba Seigoro's succession, he soon passed away leading to the extinction of the line. Since adoptive father Emon served diligently for many years, the Emon name line was reestablished, granted a stipend of five koku and support for two retainers, appointed to castle deputy group {{Note: Nakayama Seizaemon also received orders}}
1. 11th year of same era, Year of the Sheep (1799), November 25th: Appointed as assistant mountain magistrate.
1. 12th year of same era, Year of the Monkey (1800), June 12th: Granted additional rice allowance of three to.
1. 1st year of Kyōwa, Year of the Cock (1801), March 20th: Appointed as assistant firewood administrator, continues as assistant mountain magistrate as before.

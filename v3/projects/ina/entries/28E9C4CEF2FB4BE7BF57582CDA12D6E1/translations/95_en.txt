- 8th year (Bunsei 8, 1825), 9th month, 6th day: Appointed to remittance affairs, middle page inspector rank second seat with position rice of one koku granted
- 9th year (Bunsei 9, 1826), 4th month, 13th day: Appointed head of attendants, other duties to continue as before
- 10th year (Bunsei 10, 1827), 9th month, 8th day: By lord's intention, appointed assistant chief accountant, remittance affairs and head of attendants to continue as before
- Tenpō 4 (1833), 5th month, 28th day: Appointed middle page inspector, head of attendants position relieved without incident, other duties to continue as before
- 7th year (Tenpō 7, 1836), 4th month, 20th day: Assistant chief accountant and remittance affairs positions relieved without incident, middle page inspector to continue as before, assigned to lower residence

- 9th year (Tenpō 9, 1838), 4th month, 28th day: Appointed to temporarily assist as attendant to the lord's wife
- Same year, 12th month, 20th day: Appointed to concurrent remittance affairs return service, middle page inspector to continue as before, assigned to upper residence

- 12th year (Tenpō 12, 1841), 7th month, 10th day: Appointed to supervision duties for the young lords
- Same month, 12th day: Concurrent middle gate guard position relieved without incident

When we approached them intending to file a lawsuit, three men from the same village - Zenbei, Jinzaburo, and Ryusuke from Nakamura - mediated, and we reached an amicable settlement through thorough discussion. Furthermore, the stone quarry guild supervisors, Sabrobei-dono from Himochi Village and Jingoemon-dono from Katakura Village, prepared regulatory documents so that the six member households could conduct business smoothly in the future, and we reached reconciliation. However, due to unavoidable circumstances, Sabrobei violated these regulations, and under such conditions our livelihood cannot be sustained, causing us extreme hardship. Once again, we humbly submit this petition despite our trepidation.

For those who wish to engage in the production of tools and equipment, collection activities, whale wax, or any other productive activities, as well as those who want to establish rest stations or tea houses in areas near ports, people will be sent as desired. Of course, allowances will be provided according to the type of goods. Furthermore, for those who demonstrate particular kindness or integrity that benefits the nation, their circumstances will be investigated according to their rank, and such persons will receive promotions in status. For farmers, artisans, and merchants, land and houses will be provided, along with rewards and allowances. Those who understand this intent and are willing volunteers should submit their applications through the proper channels. Moreover,

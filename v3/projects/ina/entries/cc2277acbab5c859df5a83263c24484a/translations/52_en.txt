Fourth month

Regarding the monthly hired worker Kichizō who killed the wife "Eyo" of Kurō at Jirōemon's shop in Asakusa Moto-Torigoe-chō on the morning of the 9th day of the 5th month of last year of the Dog, and then fled.

Personal description
- Age twenty-nine years, appears his age
- Born in Edo Ushigome Onadogai-chō, son of Yosāburō who was formerly there but died of illness

[Left page]

- Medium height, medium build - Face thin with sunken cheeks, rather dark complexion
- Large, sharp eyes
- Rather thin hair - Straight, high nose
- Short, blackened teeth - Ears and lips normal
- Rather quiet manner of speaking
- Right leg is short, steps outward when walking
- At that time wore mouse-colored checkered striped single-layer kimono with a small indigo cotton three-shaku obi
If such a person is found, detain him at that location

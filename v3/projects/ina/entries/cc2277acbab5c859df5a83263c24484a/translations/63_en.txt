　　　1st month

Regarding the one-shu silver coins that have been ordered to be minted for circulation in society,
they shall be put into use from the coming 24th day. Naturally, they shall be used in transactions without discrimination, mixed together with five-ryo ban, koban, one-bu gold, two-shu gold, one-bu silver, and other currencies.
Therefore, their circulation must not be hindered.
Article 1: Regarding the exchange of one-shu silver coins, the matter of conversion shall be understood in the same manner as one-bu silver, and exchanges shall be conducted accordingly.

Shinto priest of Rokusho Shrine, Sōja Fuchū, Musashi Province
Saruwatari Ōmi
Shimōsa Province, Shimotsuke Province, Shinano Province

The main hall, zuijin gate, and various other shrine buildings of the aforementioned Rokusho Shrine have fallen into severe disrepair. To assist with repairs, fundraising permission has been granted for these three provinces. Shrine officials carrying fundraising letters bearing the joint seals of the Temples and Shrines Magistrate will travel from this year of the Dog (1850), tenth month, until the ninth month of the coming year of the Ox (1853), for a period of three years, visiting bakufu lands, private domains, temple/shrine lands, and country towns. Those with faith should make donations regardless of the amount.

1. On the 13th day of the 9th month of Tenpō 5, Kinoe-Uma (1834), he was promoted to go-shiban rank and appointed as Ryōnoshin-sama's personal attendant. Go-soku service and go-kin-bugyō remain as before.
1. On the 23rd day of the 5th month of Tenpō 6, Kinoto-Mi (1835), he was appointed go-konando go-meshimono-gata. Other duties remain as before.
1. On the 25th day of the 10th month of Tenpō 8, Hinoto-Tori (1837), due to go-kuriawase, he was satisfactorily relieved of Ryōnoshin-sama's personal attendant duties. Other duties remain as before.
1. On the 15th day of the 1st month of Tenpō 11, Kanoe-Ne (1840), he was appointed ōmetsuke-rank go-kinshū-metsuke. Go-konando go-meshimono-gata remains concurrent as before.
1. On the 7th day of the 7th month of the same year, he was appointed Ryōnoshin-sama's assistant personal attendant. He was relieved of go-kinshū-metsuke go-konando go-meshimono-gata duties and returned to his domain residence (marginal note: while stationed in Edo).
1. On the 13th day of the 6th month of Tenpō 14, Mizunoto-U (1843), from ōmetsuke-rank Ryōnoshin-sama's assistant personal attendant, he was appointed temporary ōmetsuke and temporary go-zenban.

1. On the 23rd day of the 7th month of Kōka 2, Kinoto-Mi (1845), he was appointed concurrent temporary go-konando go-meshimono-gata. Other duties remain as before.
1. On the 9th day of the 9th month of Kōka 4, Hinoto-Hitsuji (1847), he was appointed permanent go-zenban concurrent with go-kinshū-metsuke. Temporary go-konando go-meshimono-gata remains as before.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Ubugata Urokononosuke
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(marginal note: later Kurata)
1. On the 28th day of the 7th month of Bunsei 5, Mizunoe-Uma (1822), he was appointed Ryōnoshin-sama's go-soku from go-bangata.
1. On the 15th day of the 6th month of Bunsei 8, Kinoto-Tori (1825), he was appointed lord's go-soku service.
1. On the 5th day of the 9th month of Bunsei 10, Hinoto-I (1827), he was satisfactorily relieved of go-soku service and appointed to return to regular service.

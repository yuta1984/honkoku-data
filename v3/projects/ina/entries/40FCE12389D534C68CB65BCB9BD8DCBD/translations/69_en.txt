- Genroku 1, year of Hinoe-Tatsu (1736), 12th month, 19th day: Appointed to the position of御小納戸 (Go-konando, personal attendant managing the lord's private quarters).
- Kanpō 2, year of Mizunoe-Inu (1742), 1st month, 18th day: Appointed to the position of 御使番役 (Go-tsukaiban-yaku, messenger official).

　　　　　　　Second succession, first generation, actually fifth generation
　　　　　　　　Formerly Okano
　　　　　　　　Kaminami Tōzō
- Genbun 2, year of Hinoto-Mi (1737), 1st month, 21st day: Summoned and appointed to 御側勤 (Go-soba-tsutome, personal attendant to the lord).
　　　Note: In Genbun 5, year of Saru (1740), 9th month, 20th day, appointed to inherit the Kaminami Tosho family name. Until then, served in the Okano family.
- Enkyō 1, year of Kinoe-Ne (1744), 10th month, 2nd day: Appointed as 御近習目付 (Go-kinshū-metsuke, close attendant inspector).
- Enkyō 3, year of Hinoe-Tora (1746), 1st month, 15th day: Appointed to concurrently serve as 御小納戸 (Go-konando).
- Same year, 3rd month, 24th day: Appointed as 大目付格御膳番 (Ōmetsuke-kaku Go-zenban, inspector-ranked meal attendant). Other duties remained as before.
- Kan'en 4, year of Kanoto-Hitsuji (1751), 5th month, 6th day: Promoted to 物頭格 (Monotō-kaku, military officer rank) and appointed as 押合加役 (Oshiai-kayaku, mediation assistant). Relieved of 御近習目付 duties. Other positions remained as before.
- Hōreki 6, year of Hinoe-Ne (1756), 6th month, 18th day: Had been serving concurrently as 御小納戸, but since the 御小納戸 staff had become experienced, relieved of the concurrent 小納戸 duties.
- Hōreki 7, year of Hinoto-Ushi (1757), 8th month, 7th day: Ordered to relocate to Edo. Since he was to focus exclusively on mediation duties, relieved of 御膳番 position. 御側勤 continued as before.
- Hōreki 9, year of Tsuchinoto-U (1759), 3rd month, 2nd day: Since the young lord entered (came of age), appointed as 御側向頭取 (Go-sobamuki-tōdori, head of personal attendants) to handle various official matters. Naturally continued to serve concurrently as mediation officer as before.
- Hōreki 10, year of Kanoe-Tatsu (1760), 10th month, 29th day: Died of illness.

First Generation
Nogi Heiroku
In the 2nd year of Jōkyō, Year of the Wood Ox (1685), he was ordered to return to service and appointed to personal attendant duty.
During the Genroku era, he was appointed as head of the advance guard.
However, the exact year and month are unclear.

Second Generation
Kaminami Heishichi
Later Kameemon
In the spring of the 2nd year of Jōkyō, Year of the Wood Ox (1685), he was summoned and appointed to chamber duty.
In the winter of the same year, he was appointed to meal service duty.

Son of the first generation Kainnoshin
Yano Tomoemon
At an unknown date (around the beginning of the Jōkyō to Genroku period), he was summoned and appointed as a personal attendant, later serving in meal service duty.
He died of illness on the 25th day of the 4th month of the 10th year of Genroku, Year of the Fire Ox (1697).

First Generation
Nagaoka Kanbei
In the spring of the 4th year of Genroku, Year of the Metal Goat (1691), he was appointed to the position of liaison officer and exempted from escort duty supervision.
However, until this time he had been serving in personal attendant service.
This person was summoned as a page in the spring of the 8th year of Enpō, Year of the Metal Monkey (1680).
On the 27th day of the 9th month of the 2nd year of Jōkyō, Year of the Wood Ox (1685), he was granted a new stipend of 120 koku.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Fourth generation
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Kotani Gunzō
1. On the 29th day of the 3rd month of Kansei 9, year of the Fire Snake (1797), he was appointed to go-soku go-yatoikin.
1. On the 10th day of the 4th month of Kyōwa 3, year of the Water Boar (1803), he was appointed to go-konando and go-meshimono-kata.
1. On the 15th day of the 1st month of Bunka 3, year of the Fire Tiger (1806), he was appointed go-zenban with the rank of ō-metsuke-kaku, while continuing his go-konando duties as before.
1. On the 26th day of the 8th month of the 6th year, year of the Earth Snake (1809), he died of illness.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Fourth generation
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Miyoshi Tanomo
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Later Asai 《marginal note: later name》 Matashichirō
1. On the 1st day of the 9th month of Kansei 9, year of the Fire Rabbit (1797), he was appointed from go-bangata to go-soku service.
1. On the 23rd day of the 1st month of the 12th year, year of the Metal Monkey (1800), he was appointed temporary go-zenban.
1. On the 7th day of the 7th month of Kyōwa 1, year of the Metal Rooster (1801), he was elevated to ō-metsuke-kaku rank and appointed to full go-zenban position with concurrent go-kachi duties, with other responsibilities continuing as before.
1. On the last day of the 10th month of the 3rd year, year of the Water Boar (1803), he was appointed temporary go-konando.
1. On the 25th day of the 3rd month of Bunka 1, year of the Wood Rat (1804), he was appointed temporary go-kinjū metsuke.
1. On the 15th day of the 1st month of the 3rd year, year of the Fire Tiger (1806), he was appointed to concurrent positions of go-kinjū metsuke and go-konando.
1. On the 15th day of the 1st month of the 5th year, year of the Earth Dragon (1808), he was appointed go-mochi-nagae bugyō, with other duties continuing as before.
1. On the 1st day of the 10th month of the 6th year, year of the Earth Snake (1809), he was appointed go-sente mono-gashira, with all go-soku duties continuing as before.
1. On the 20th day of the 7th month of the 9th year, year of the Water Monkey (1812), he was appointed concurrent ō-metsuke, with his mono-gashira position continuing as before.

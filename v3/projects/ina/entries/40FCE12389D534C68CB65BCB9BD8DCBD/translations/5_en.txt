Later Arai
On the 26th day of the 9th month of the 3rd year of Genroku, Year of the Metal Horse (1690), he was summoned and appointed as a personal attendant.
In the 8th year of Genroku, Year of the Boar (1695), he was granted the succession of Arai Magoemon's position with 70 koku, which combined with the 50 koku previously granted to Kiroku, totaled 120 koku, and he inherited the Arai family name.
On the 28th day of the 10th month of the 11th year, Year of the Earth Tiger (1698), he was appointed to messenger duty.

Second Generation

Later Genbei
On the 25th day of the 6th month of the 10th year of Genroku, Year of the Fire Ox (1697), he was promoted from middle page to personal attendant.
On the 18th day of the 6th month of the 13th year, Year of the Metal Dragon (1700), he was appointed to meal service duty.
On the 7th day of the 5th month of the 1st year of Hōei, Year of the Wood Monkey (1704), as requested, he was released from personal attendant duty and appointed to guard duty.

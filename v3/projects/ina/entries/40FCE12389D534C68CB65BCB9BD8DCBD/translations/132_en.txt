　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　First generation
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Kōno Sōzaburō
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(marginal note: later Sōzaemon)

1. On the 5th day of the 5th month of Bunsei 11, Tsuchinoe-Ne (1828), he was appointed retired lord's go-soku service from go-bangata.

1. On the 13th day of the 8th month of Tenpō 5, Kinoe-Uma (1834), he was appointed go-konando and go-meshimono-kata.

1. On the 1st day of the 4th month of Tenpō 8, Hinoto-Tori (1837), he was granted go-tsukai-ban rank and appointed accordingly, continuing go-konando duties as before.

1. On the 15th day of the 8th month of Tenpō 13, Mizunoe-Tora (1842), as requested, he was exempted from go-soku service and all duties, and ordered to return to regular service.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　First generation
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Miyama Danzō

1. On the 5th day of the 6th month of Bunsei 11, Tsuchinoe-Ne (1828), he was appointed go-soku service concurrent with go-soezaban from go-tomogata go-chū-koshō hired service.

1. On the 1st day of the 4th month of Tenpō 8, Hinoto-Tori (1837), he was appointed go-konando and go-meshimono-kata.

1. On the 5th day of the 8th month of Tenpō 9, Tsuchinoe-Inu (1838), he was appointed temporary go-zenban, continuing go-konando and go-meshimono-kata duties as before.

1. On the 1st day of the 3rd month of Kōka 2, Kinoto-Mi (1845), he was granted ōmetsuke rank and appointed permanent go-zenban with concurrent go-kinshū-metsuke duties, continuing go-konando and go-meshimono-kata duties as before.

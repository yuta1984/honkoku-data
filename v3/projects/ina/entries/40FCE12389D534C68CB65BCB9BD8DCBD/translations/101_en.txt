1. On the 8th day of the 10th month of the 4th year, year of the Water Rat (1792), he was appointed to concurrently hold go-kinshū metsuke (close attendant inspector) and go-konando (personal treasury keeper).
1. On the 15th day of the 5th month of the 6th year, year of the Wood Tiger (1794), he was appointed to ōmetsuke-kaku go-zenban (senior inspector rank, dining service). Go-kinshū metsuke and go-konando to continue as before.
1. On the 1st day of the 10th month of the 7th year, year of the Wood Rabbit (1795), he was appointed to go-sentemono-gashira (leader of advance guard). The concurrent positions of go-zenban, go-kinshū metsuke, and go-konando to continue as before.
1. On the 15th day of the 2nd month of the 8th year, year of the Fire Dragon (1796), he was appointed to concurrently hold ōmetsuke (senior inspector). Due to good performance in go-soku service, he was dismissed and retained go-zenban until the lord's departure.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Fourth generation
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Miura Koichirō
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　《marginal note: later Tadaemon | also Dan'emon》
1. On the 15th day of the intercalary 6th month of Kansei 1, year of the Earth Rooster (1789), he was appointed to go-soku go-yatoikin.
1. On the 15th day of the 2nd month of the 8th year, year of the Fire Dragon (1796), he was appointed to go-konando.
1. On the 9th day of the 2nd month of the 9th year, year of the Fire Snake (1797), he was granted ōmetsuke-kaku rank and appointed to okusama go-tsuki (lady's attendant) in Edo, and ordered to relocate.
1. On the 28th day of the 4th month of the 11th year, year of the Earth Sheep (1799), he was transferred from ōmetsuke-kaku okusama go-tsuki to concurrent positions of go-zenban, go-kinshū metsuke, and go-konando.

Sixth Generation
Hoshino Tetsusaburō
On the 20th day of the 10th month of the 4th year of Kōka, Year of the Fire Sheep (1847), he was appointed from guard duty to personal attendant service.
On the 25th day of the 11th month of the 4th year of Kaei, Year of the Metal Boar (1851), he was appointed to messenger service, minor treasury duty, and wardrobe management concurrently.

Re-established family line, third generation after restoration, actually ninth generation
Kurahashi Sukesaburō
On the 23rd day of the 6th month of the 1st year of Kaei, Year of the Earth Monkey (1848), he was appointed to temporary personal attendant service for the retired lord.

Son of fifth generation Hachirōemon
Kōno Jūji
On the 8th day of the 7th month of the 1st year of Kaei, Year of the Earth Monkey (1848), he was appointed to temporary personal attendant service.
He died of illness on the 10th day of the 9th month of the same year.

Son of second-generation Junhachi
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　 Saeki Sadayoshi
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　{{Marginal note: Later Hansuke, Ichizō}}
1. On the 15th day of the 4th month of Bunsei 2, Tsuchinoto-U (1819), he was appointed go-soku service to the young lord from go-chū-koshō go-yatoi service.
1. On the 17th day of the 2nd month of Bunsei 3, Kanoe-Tatsu (1820), regarding arrangements, he was appointed go-soku service to the retired lord.
1. On the 1st day of the 9th month of Bunsei 6, Mizunoto-Hitsuji (1823), he was appointed go-omote go-soku service go-soeban as an additional duty.
1. On the 15th day of the 9th month of Bunsei 7, Kinoe-Saru (1824), he died of illness.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Sixth generation
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Misawa Seisaburō
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　{{Marginal note: Later Wadazō, Kiemon, Wadazō, Kiemon}}
1. On the 1st day of the 9th month of Bunsei 2, Tsuchinoto-U (1819), from go-bangata, in consideration of his grandfather Kiemon's years of faithful service in important official duties, he was appointed go-soku service.
1. On the 17th day of the 2nd month of Bunsei 3, Kanoe-Tatsu (1820), this time as the retirement request was approved as requested, he was appointed go-tsuki. Of course, his manner of service remained as before.
1. On the 15th day of the 1st month of Bunsei 10, Hinoto-I (1827), he was appointed go-dōsho-sama go-konando go-meshimono-kata.
1. On the 15th day of the 9th month of Bunsei 12, Tsuchinoto-Ushi (1829), he was granted the rank of ō-metsuke and appointed go-dōsho-sama go-zenban. Other duties remained as before.
1. On the 13th day of the 9th month of Tenpō 4, Mizunoto-Mi (1833), he was appointed go-dōsho-sama go-kinshū as an additional duty.
1. On the 28th day of the 7th month of Tenpō 14, Mizunoto-U (1843), as requested, he was relieved of all official duties and appointed to return to regular service.
1. On the 15th day of the 1st month of Tenpō 15, Kinoe-Tatsu (1844), from go-shiban-yaku go-fushin-bugyō additional duty, he was granted ō-metsuke rank and [appointed] go-inkyo-sama go-zenban go-konando go-meshimono-kata concurrently

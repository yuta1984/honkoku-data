　　　　　　　　　　　Fifth generation
　　　　　　　　　　　　　Kaburagi Gingo
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　《marginal note: later Tadazō》
1. On the 14th day of the 2nd month of Kansei 2, year of the Metal Dog (1790), he was appointed to go-soku go-yatoikin.

1. On the 13th day of the 1st month of the 6th year, year of the Wood Tiger (1794), he was dismissed from go-soku go-yatoikin and became unemployed.

　　　　　　　　　　　Fifth generation
　　　　　　　　　　　　　Okano Chōzō
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　《marginal note: later Kumeemon》
1. On the 5th day of the 3rd month of Kansei 2, year of the Metal Dog (1790), he was appointed to go-soku service from go-kin bugyō (treasury magistrate).
1. On the 15th day of the 5th month of the 6th year, year of the Wood Tiger (1794), he was appointed to go-konando.
1. On the 15th day of the 2nd month of the 8th year, year of the Fire Dragon (1796), he was granted ōmetsuke-kaku rank and appointed to concurrent positions of go-zenban, go-kinshū metsuke, and go-konando.
1. On the 25th day of the 11th month of the 11th year, year of the Earth Sheep (1799), he was appointed to go-sentemono-gashira. Other duties to continue as before.
1. On the 1st day of the 6th month of Kyōwa 1, year of the Metal Rooster (1801), due to excellent performance in go-soku service, he was dismissed and could devote himself exclusively to his position as head (gashira).

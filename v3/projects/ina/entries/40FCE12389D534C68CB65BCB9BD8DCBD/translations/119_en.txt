　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Sixth generation
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Sanpei Tansaburō
1. On the 5th day of the 7th month of Bunka 13, Hinoe-Ne (1816), he was appointed go-soku go-yatoi service to the young lord.
1. On the 28th day of the 7th month of Bunsei 3, Kanoe-Tatsu (1820), due to illness, as requested, he was dismissed from go-soku service and appointed go-chū-koshō go-kyūji-kata, under the supervision of go-yōnin.
1. On the 1st day of the 9th month of Bunsei 6, Mizunoto-Hitsuji (1823), he was appointed go-soku service from go-bangata.
1. On the 8th day of the 10th month of Bunsei 11, Tsuchinoe-Ne (1828), due to illness, as requested, he was dismissed from go-soku service and ordered to return to regular duty.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Naito Ichinosuke
1. On the 28th day of the 7th month of Bunsei 4, Kanoto-Mi (1821), he was appointed go-soku go-yatoi.
1. On the 25th day of the 11th month of Bunsei 6, Mizunoto-Hitsuji (1823), due to this go-kirikae he was relieved of go-yatoi service and granted one-person stipend.
1. On the 21st day of the 9th month of Bunsei 8, Kinoto-Tori (1825), he was appointed go-soku go-yatoi service during residence in the castle.
1. On the 3rd day of the 6th month of Bunsei 9, Hinoe-Inu (1826), he was appointed go-yatoi service from this time forward.
1. On the 17th day of the 11th month of Tenpō 2, Kanoto-U (1831), he was appointed go-shiban main position chief administrator concurrently.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Azami Denzaemon

1. On the 23rd day of the 11th month of Bunsei 7, Kinoe-Saru (1824), he was appointed go-kin-bugyō.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Fifth generation
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Kotani Gunzō
1. On the 1st day of the 5th month of Bunsei 5, Mizunoe-Uma (1822), he was appointed Ryōnoshin-sama go-soku from go-bangata.
1. On the 15th day of the 6th month of Bunsei 8, Kinoto-Tori (1825), he was appointed go-bugu-bugyō.
1. On the 1st day of the 9th month of Bunsei 10, Hinoto-I (1827), from go-bugu-bugyō go-kin-bugyō concurrent position, he was appointed go-soku service. Go-kin-bugyō remains concurrent as before.

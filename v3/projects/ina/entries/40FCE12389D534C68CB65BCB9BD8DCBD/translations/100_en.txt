　　　　　　　　　　　　Third generation successor, actually fifth generation
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Okumura Genzaburō
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Later Araki 《marginal note: later Jibu-suke | also Ara-suke》

1. On the 28th day of the 11th month of Tenmei 8, year of the Earth Monkey (1788), he was appointed to御側御雇勤 (goshoku go-yatoikin - temporary attendant service to the lord).
1. On the 14th day of the 2nd month of Kansei 2, year of the Metal Dog (1790), he was dismissed from goshoku service and appointed to go-tomogata (lord's escort service).
1. On the 15th day of the 1st month of Kyōwa 2, year of the Water Dog (1802), he was appointed to goshoku go-yatoikin.
1. On the 15th day of the 6th month of Bunka 5, year of the Earth Dragon (1808), he was appointed to the position of ōmetsuke-kaku go-zenban (senior inspector rank, dining service).
1. On the 7th day of the 7th month of the 10th year, year of the Water Rooster (1813), he was appointed to gun-dai jiseki goshoku kashira-dori (deputy district magistrate, head of lord's attendants). He was to handle the duties of goshoku go-yōnin (chamberlain), oversee go-kinshū metsuke (close attendant inspectors), and understand what to do when there were problems with the dining service.

　　On the 7th day of the 7th month of the 11th year, year of the Wood Dog (1814), he was appointed to concurrently hold the positions of goshoku go-yōyaku (private secretary) and omote go-yōyaku (public secretary).

　　　　　　　　　　　　　First generation
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Aoyama Tanzō
1. On the 7th day of the 2nd month of Kansei 1, year of the Earth Rooster (1789), he was appointed to goshoku go-yatoikin.
1. On the 21st day of the 9th month of the 2nd year, year of the Metal Dog (1790), he was appointed as temporary officer for go-konando go-meshimono-gata (wardrobe management) in Osaka.
1. On the 25th day of the 2nd month of the 4th year, year of the Water Rat (1792), as requested, he was dismissed from goshoku service and granted the rank of go-chūkoshō metsuke-kaku (middle page inspector rank).

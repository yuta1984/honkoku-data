Fifth generation
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Fukuhara Uhachi
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　 {{Marginal note: Later Jinbei}}
1. On the 3rd day of the 10th month of Bunka 13, Hinoe-Ne (1816), he was appointed go-soku service to Ryōnoshin-sama from go-bangata assistant go-tomogata.
1. On the 1st day of the 5th month of Bunsei 5, Mizunoe-Uma (1822), he was appointed go-omote go-soku service.
1. On the 25th day of the 11th month of Bunsei 6, Mizunoto-Hitsuji (1823), he was dismissed from go-yatoi service due to personnel changes and became without duties.
1. On the 21st day of the 9th month of Bunsei 8, Kinoto-Tori (1825), he was appointed go-soku go-yatoi service during residence in the castle.
1. On the 15th day of the 1st month of Bunsei 9, Hinoe-Inu (1826), he was appointed go-soku service.
1. On the 17th day of the 8th month of Bunsei 10, Hinoto-I (1827), he was appointed provisional go-konando go-meshimono-kata during travels and residence in the castle. Furthermore, he was to understand that during times of need, he should also assist with regular trial duties.
1. On the 15th day of the 1st month of Tenpō 3, Mizunoe-Tatsu (1832), he was appointed to the permanent position of go-konando go-meshimono-kata and concurrently go-kin-bugyō.
1. On the 19th day of the 9th month of Tenpō 5, Kinoe-Uma (1834), he died of illness.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Fifth generation
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Namiki Chōzō
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　 {{Marginal note: Later Shichirōzaemon}}
1. On the 18th day of the 9th month of Bunka 14, Hinoto-Ushi (1817), he was appointed go-soku service from go-bangata.
1. On the 13th day of the 9th month of Bunsei 2, Tsuchinoto-U (1819), he was appointed go-bugu-bugyō.
1. On the 20th day of the 10th month of Bunsei 6, Mizunoto-Hitsuji (1823), he was promoted from go-kin-bugyō to go-shiban rank and appointed go-soku service to the retired lord with concurrent go-shiyaku go-konando duties.
1. On the 10th day of the 6th month of Bunsei 9, Hinoe-Inu (1826), he was promoted to ōmetsuke rank.

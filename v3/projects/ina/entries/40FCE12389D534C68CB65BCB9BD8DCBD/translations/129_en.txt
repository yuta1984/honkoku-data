　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Fukaya Tetsuzō

1. On the 20th day of the 9th month of Bunsei 7, Kinoe-Saru (1824), he was appointed go-soku employed service.

1. On the 23rd day of the 10th month of Tenpō 2, Kanoto-U (1831), due to personnel adjustments, he was appointed go-chū-koshō retired lord's go-soku service.

1. On the 5th day of the 4th month of Tenpō 3, Mizunoe-Tatsu (1832), due to illness, as requested, he was relieved of retired lord's go-soku service and appointed to go-bangata senior position.

1. On the 26th day of the 3rd month of Bunsei 9, Hinoe-Inu (1826), he was appointed go-soku service from go-kura-bugyō and go-bugu-bugyō positions.

1. On the 1st day of the 4th month of Bunsei 10, Hinoto-I (1827), he died of illness.

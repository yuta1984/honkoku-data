Second Generation
Kaminami Zusho
During the Genroku era, he was appointed to personal attendant duty, and additionally served as intermediary, becoming senior retainer.
Later he was appointed to the position of chief retainer.

Third Generation
Saji Shinnosuke
Later Tomouemon
Tomonosuke
On the 4th day of the 3rd month of the 15th year of Genroku, Year of the Water Horse (1702), he was promoted from middle page to personal attendant.
He died of illness on the 10th day of the intercalary 4th month of the 2nd year of Hōei, Year of the Wood Rooster (1705).

Second Generation
Nagashima Shinnosuke
Later Kouemon
Shinuemon
On the 18th day of the 8th month of the 16th year of Genroku, Year of the Water Sheep (1703), he was promoted from middle page to personal attendant duty.
On the 22nd day of the 3rd month of the 4th year of Shōtoku, Year of the Wood Horse (1714), he was appointed to meal service duty.
He died of illness on the 6th day of the 12th month of the 10th year of Kyōhō, Year of the Wood Snake (1725).

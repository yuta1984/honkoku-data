Humbly submitting this petition in writing
I have been selling small oil buckets until now, but at this time I wish
to cease this business. Therefore, I humbly submit this petition.
Regarding the above matter, if you would graciously grant my petition as requested,
it would be most grateful. Above stated.

Tenpo 15, Year of the Wood Dragon, 2nd month　　Petitioner: Manbei
　　　　　　　　　　　　　Town Representative: Sobei
　　To the Magistrate's Office
The above petition was submitted on the 15th through Mr. Goto's intermediation.

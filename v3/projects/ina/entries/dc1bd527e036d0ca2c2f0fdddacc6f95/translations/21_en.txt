Humbly submitting this petition in writing
I have been engaged in a sundry goods business until now, but I wish to cease this business from this year. Therefore, I humbly petition to return my business license.
If you would graciously grant my petition as stated above, I would be most grateful and fortunate. Above stated.

Tenpō 15, Year of the Metal Dragon, 2nd month　Hokomotchi-machi
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Kanaya
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Petitioner: Kichibei
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Town representative: Chōbei
To the Magistrate's Office

The above petition was submitted on the 5th day of the 2nd month through Mr. Shimizu's intermediation, but regarding whether Mr. Honuemon has returned the license for this matter, there has been no response yet.

I wish to engage in peddling business from this year.

Tenpō 15, Year of the Metal Dragon, 2nd month　Hokomotchi-machi
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Kanaya
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Petitioner: Inosaburo
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Town representative: Chōbei
To the Magistrate's Office

The above petition was submitted on the 5th day of the 2nd month through Mr. Shimizu's intermediation. On the 27th, it was approved, and instructions were given to appear on the coming 29th to receive the license from Mr. Mikami Jinzabei. This was communicated to the town representative on the 28th.

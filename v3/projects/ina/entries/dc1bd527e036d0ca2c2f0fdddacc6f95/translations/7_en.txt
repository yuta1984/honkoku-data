　　　I humbly submit this oral petition
　Regarding Zensaku of Wagai Village, Shimo-Ina District, under the jurisdiction of Lord Ikeda Iwanosuke,
　who until now has been working as a sawyer while serving water duties under the guarantee of Kichizaemon of Seri-machi,
　since Kichizaemon has petitioned for pardon from his guarantee obligation, I now wish
　to provide guarantee for him to serve water duties and work as both a sawyer and a woodcutter. Therefore I submit this petition.

【Right Page】
Respectfully submitting this oral petition
Regarding my relative Kiinokuya Katsubei, who has been temporarily discontinued, since there is a connection with a person named Katshichi, the younger brother of Sayokichi from Yazawa Village, Kagura District, Joshu Province, under the jurisdiction of Lord Minokasa-no-suke, I wish to adopt him along with his wife and one child to continue the family name, and therefore respectfully submit this petition.
Regarding the above matter, if you would graciously grant this petition as requested,
it would be most grateful and fortunate. The above.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Sericho
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Masaya
　Tenpō 15, Year of the Wood Dragon, 2nd month　　Petitioner: Kozaemon
　　To the Magistrate's Office　　　　　Group Head: Hangoro
The above petition was submitted through Shimizu-shi's intermediation on the 7th day, whereupon it was approved the same day, and this was communicated in writing from Goto-shi and conveyed to the town representative.

【Left Page】
Respectfully submitting this oral petition
Regarding my relative Yamaguchiyu Matsutaro of Shinmachi, since he cannot continue the family name due to illness, I have discussed with a person named Jinzaemon, the younger brother of Kiemon from Shimotdera Village, Tdera District, under the jurisdiction of Lord Ikeda Iwa-no-jo, about adopting him to continue the family succession, and therefore respectfully submit this petition.
Regarding the above matter, if you would graciously grant this petition as requested,
it would be most grateful and fortunate. The above.　　　Hokojimochi-cho
　　　　　　　　　　　　　Enshuya
　Tenpō 15, Year of the Wood Dragon, 2nd month　　Petitioner: Shinemon
　　　　　　　　　　　　Shinmachi
　　　　　　　　　　　　Town Representative: Soshichi
　　To the Magistrate's Office
The above petition was submitted through Shimizu-shi's intermediation on the 20th day, whereupon on the 27th day the official decision was that regarding Matsutaro, since it is a family succession matter, the petition should be submitted under his own name, so town representative Soshichi was called and the petition was returned on the evening of the 27th day.

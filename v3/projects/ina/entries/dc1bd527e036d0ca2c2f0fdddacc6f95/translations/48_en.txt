　　　　Oral statement record
　The items necessary for making tofu - firewood, lees, and bittern - these three items have
　become increasingly expensive in recent years, costing about 54 mon per cake.
　We humbly request that you permit us to sell at 40 mon as petitioned.
　If you would grant this, we would be most grateful. End.
　　　10th month of Dragon year　　　　　Tofu sellers association
The above petition was submitted on the 7th through Mr. Shimizu's intermediation, and on the 20th received official decision through Mr. Kitano's intermediation.

Respectfully, I humbly submit this oral petition:
I wish to employ one Hatsutaro from Ichinose Village as my boarding apprentice for a five-year term beginning this Year of the Dragon, to have him serve water duties and learn blacksmithing.
Therefore, I submit this petition.

Regarding the above matter, I humbly petition that you mercifully command as I have requested,
and if you would graciously grant permission, it would be a most grateful fortune. The above.

Tenpo 15, Year of the Wood Dragon, 5th month　　Shimomachi
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Petitioner: Gonshiro
To the Magistrate's Office　　　　　　　　　　　District Representative: Tomouemon

The above petition was submitted on the 27th day through Takahashi's mediation for official response.

　　　I humbly petition with this oral statement:
　I wish to engage in peddling business from this year onward.
　I petition for this matter.
　Regarding the above matter, if you would graciously grant
　my petition as humbly requested, it would be most grateful and fortunate. The above
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Shimizu-machi
　　Tenpo 15, year of the wood dragon, second month　　　Wataya
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Petitioner: Rihei
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Town Representative: Gihei
　　To the Magistrate's Office
The above petition was submitted through Mr. Shimizu's intermediation on the 5th day of the second month, and approval was granted on the 27th.
An official notice stated that the license would be handed over to Mr. Mikami Jinjibei on the 29th and should be collected,
so this was conveyed to the town representative on the 28th.

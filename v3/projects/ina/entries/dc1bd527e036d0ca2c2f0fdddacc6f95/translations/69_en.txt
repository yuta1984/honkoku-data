【Right Page】
　　Humbly submitting this oral petition
I wish to engage in tofu business, and regarding the mold box,
I have discussed with Fukusaya Kaemon in our town about
transferring it to me. Therefore, I humbly submit this petition.
Regarding the above matter, if you would mercifully grant
my petition as requested, I would be most grateful. The above.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Seri-machi
　　Kōka 2nd year, 2nd month　　Petitioner: Yajibei
　　　To the Magistrate's Office　Town Representative: Koemon
The above petition was submitted on the 10th through Mr. Shimizu's intermediation. On the 22nd, approval was granted, and instructions were given that if the license is to be issued on the 27th, I should bring my seal and report to Mr. Yamashita Toragoro.

【Left Page】
　　　Humbly submitting this oral petition
My adopted son Hyōemon has proven inadequate, so I wish to divorce him
and send him back to Shinemon's place in Yatsude Village, Tera-gō,
under the jurisdiction of Mr. Chimura Heiemon.
Therefore, I humbly submit this petition.
Regarding the above matter, if you would mercifully grant
my petition as requested, I would be most grateful. The above. Seri-machi Fukusaya
　Kōka 2nd year, 2nd month　　　　Petitioner: Kaemon
　　To the Magistrate's Office　　　Town Representative: Koemon
The above petition was submitted in the same manner, and approval was granted on the same day, so I informed the town representative on the morning of the 11th.

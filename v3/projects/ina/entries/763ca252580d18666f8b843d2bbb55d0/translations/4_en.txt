At that time, sealed documents addressed to both Shoemon and Hachiemon were submitted. Regarding this matter, meetings were held annually in the 4th month to settle accounts and divide profits, with annual accounts settled from the Year of the Dog through the Year of the Tiger. On the 25th day of the 3rd month of the Year of the Rabbit, 20 ryō in gold was sent to Kyūzō's place, with Jinemon as messenger, accompanied by a letter. On the 20th day of the same 4th month, 55 ryō in gold was repaid as partial payment toward the principal, and in addition to the written record and verification against the original promissory note, 3 ryō in gold for store rental was sent and received, which was securely kept. Furthermore, on the 6th day of the 4th month of the Year of the Boar, 55 ryō in gold was repaid separately as principal and interest, and since Kyūzō was absent, it was handed over to Kyūhachi and his wife. At the same time, an additional 3 ryō in gold was sent as interest on 30 ryō. Receipt documents for these were kept.

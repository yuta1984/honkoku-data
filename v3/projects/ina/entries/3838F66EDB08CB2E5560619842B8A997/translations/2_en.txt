《Marginal note: Senior Court Noble, Karasumaru Middle Counselor》
Imperial Edict, 16th day of the 12th month of Kaei 3 (1850)

Fujiwara no Yoritaka, Junior Fifth Rank Lower Grade

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Respectfully submitted
　　　Chamberlain, Assistant Minor Controller of the Left concurrent Assistant Captain of the Right Gate Guards, Fujiwara no Taneyasu

Regarding the monk ranks and monk officials that have traditionally been granted through imperial edicts from various temples for a long time, all such positions shall be abolished from now on. Please understand this directive and ensure it is communicated without fail to all temples within the prefectural jurisdictions.
　2nd month of Jinshin (1872)　　　　　　　Council of State (Daijōkan)

The titles of village officials have varied inconsistently until now, but henceforth they shall be called Village Headman (nanushi-yaku), Group Leader (kumigashira-yaku), and Peasant Representative (hyakushō-dai). This directive is to be communicated to these officials.

　　4th month, Jinshin year　　　　　Council of State

　This time, as our country's products are to be exhibited at the Austrian exposition, there has been a directive from that office to collect and submit the items listed on the separate sheet. Each village should consult within their associations, conduct thorough searches with careful attention, and villages that have such items should bring samples and conduct detailed surveys of the production locations - which mountains, rivers, districts, etc. - and depending on the items, attach drawings and submit them without delay by the end of this month. Furthermore, besides the items listed on the separate sheet, if there are any newly invented

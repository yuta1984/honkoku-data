Article Four
One: The roadside trees along various roads and highways shall not be cut down.

Article Five  
One: In mountain forests that are water sources, neither quality timber nor miscellaneous trees shall be cut indiscriminately.
However, standing dead trees, trees broken by wind and snow, and rotten decayed trees [shall be handled] as per Article Two.

Jinshi (1872), 4th month, 17th day

As per the separate document, orders have been issued and notifications made. All villages without exception shall be notified, stored goods shall be investigated in detail, and by the 25th day deadline, districts shall again...

...collect all villages together and submit [reports] through representatives. Villages with no stored goods shall similarly report that fact.

This time, for the purpose of inspecting each region, Ministry of Finance officials are being dispatched. Accordingly, not only the official treasuries of each prefecture but also temples, shrines, and mountain people shall investigate and record inscriptions on treasures they have stored from the past. Each prefecture shall understand this matter and widely communicate it to temples and shrines within their jurisdiction.

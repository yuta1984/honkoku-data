This time, for the investigation of castle fortifications and weapons, officials from the Ministry of the Army are being dispatched to various regions. Not only official treasuries but also temples, shrines, and all four classes of people shall investigate in detail any items among their stored treasures that belong to the category of weapons. Each prefecture shall understand this matter and widely communicate it to temples and shrines within their jurisdiction.

Previously, regarding student education at private schools, the government did not give instructions, but originally in the way of people's education, there should be no distinction between public and private [education].

[Left page]

Therefore, even private school teachers cannot arbitrarily conduct education without obtaining official permission. Henceforth, those who wish to open private schools must first submit detailed information including their name, age, previous background, curriculum, school regulations, educational methods, location of the school, etc., to this ministry to obtain a license before opening their schools. Accordingly, in Tokyo, namely the capital prefecture, if private schools have been established up to now, they must quickly investigate the aforementioned school regulations and submit inquiries to this ministry through the prefectural office between the 17th and 20th days of the coming [month].

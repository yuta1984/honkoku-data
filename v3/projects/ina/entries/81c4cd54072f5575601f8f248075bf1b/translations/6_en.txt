【Right Page】

I had heard of Mount Minobu in Kai Province, but I am only now learning about succeeding to Mino's position.
The middle of the fourth month with its thousand cherry blossoms has passed, and the samurai have been dismissed from service.
The consequence of drowning in personal greed is that Mino, Higo, and Minobe will go to Kai Province on a pilgrimage.
Chikuzen will serve not in Dazaifu but in Kofu, though this is punishment for crimes.
Mizuno has vanished like foam, losing his position due to personal difficulties, receiving severe punishment and being expelled from the Chrysanthemum Chamber.
The mausoleum has been completed and the grove is to be cleared away.

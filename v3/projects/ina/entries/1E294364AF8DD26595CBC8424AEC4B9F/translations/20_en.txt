March (31 days)    Lord Takeda Shichirōemon
                   Kanashima Yagohei
At the meeting office    Otagiri Kinbei
Sake brewing office     Hata Yahachi

1st day
- Kurōemon from Furumi village came to visit, so we arranged for him to stay at Tsuruya inn.

Same day
- Hata Yahachi completed his business regarding receipt of goods at the construction site of Jingūgawa river crossing in Nakazawa Takami village and returned.

- Kurōemon departed early in the morning for the planting site at Rokudōhara.

- Regarding instructions for the paulownia seedling planting site at Rokudōhara, subordinate officials and office staff are working daily shifts.

- Tōbei was assigned to sake house duty starting today.

- Itō Sukehachi departed from the lord's upper residence in Ogawamachi, Edo on the 1st, with a journey expected to take about five days, but due to foot pain, he was delayed by half a day until before noon today.

- Kurōbei from Furumi village brought 10,000 paulownia seedlings and will go to Rokudōhara from the 2nd day to prepare the fields for planting. Regarding this, there was an order for subordinate officials and office staff to make daily inspections.

- We received word that kurogwa (construction workers) for planting work from Arasuma district are gradually arriving.

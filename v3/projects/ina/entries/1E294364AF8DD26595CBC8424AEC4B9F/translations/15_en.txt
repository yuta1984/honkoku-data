Kakeya (Broker)
Genzō

You were engaged in the business of weaving black hachijō silk in this locality, and had borrowed money for the time being. However, claiming difficulties with tax payments, you fled. Upon summoning you for investigation, your excuses are untenable and extremely improper. Although there are methods of severe punishment, by special mercy we impose banishment. Henceforth you shall not enter the domain territory. Furthermore, Iida, Matsumoto, and Suwa are designated as prohibited areas.

February 15th

The above ruling was delivered and completed.

Regarding this matter, at the time of escort from the jurisdiction, both Danroku and Tokuya from the office accompanied him, along with one laborer and additionally one eta as escort, sending him to Karamatsu-zawa. Upon their return and report, notification was made to the magistrates.

One matter: There was a petition from Kichisaburō of Omaise Village to cease cotton ginning.

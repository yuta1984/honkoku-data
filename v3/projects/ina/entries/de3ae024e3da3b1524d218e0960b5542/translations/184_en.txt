As stated above,
since an official notice has been issued from the government,
it should be announced to the townspeople. That is all.
　Ninth month

Regarding the above official notice being sent
from Mr. Izawa, I sent word to my fellow officials,
called the town representatives from the magistrate towns,
and ordered them to announce it to the ten towns.

One official document relay from Hokimochi village to lodging village arrived on the twenty-fourth day of the ninth month at around half past the fourth hour of the night, and was immediately received:

　　One official box
　　One official notice
　　One accompanying letter

　The above items were certainly received as stated.
　　　　　　　　　　　　That is all.

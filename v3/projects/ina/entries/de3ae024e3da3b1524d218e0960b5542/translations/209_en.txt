【Right Page】

Regarding the reminting of two-shu coins, concerning the circulation
of old two-shu coins, until the 2nd month of the coming year of the Ox,
new two-shu coins shall continue to circulate
uniformly as before. After that, the circulation of old
two-shu coins shall be discontinued.
Therefore, those possessing old two-shu coins should
exchange them promptly without delay. Furthermore,
in shogunal domains, the deputies shall consult, and lords and
local magistrates shall order that even in distant provinces,
all coins be exchanged without exception. If
in distant provinces or overseas territories the exchange is
inconvenient, the deputies, lords, and
local magistrates shall provide thorough assistance
and arrange for delivery to the nearest
exchange location.
　　7th month

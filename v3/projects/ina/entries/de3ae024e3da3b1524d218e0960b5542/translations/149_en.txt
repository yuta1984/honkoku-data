11th day of the 3rd month, Boar year
One　An official notice was as follows

During the official mourning period of the government,
[we] issued a notice that music and entertainment should be refrained from
until the 10th of next month

[However,] the mourning period for the Shogun has ended,
and the mourning period for the Lord of the Interior has also been
lifted, so it is no longer necessary to refrain from music and entertainment

The above matters should be announced throughout the town　The above
　10th day of the 3rd month
As ordered above,
[this] was conveyed to the magistrate. [He was instructed to] circulate [this] to the ten districts.

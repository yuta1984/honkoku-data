　　Year of the Ox (Ushi)
　　　2nd month

Regarding the matter of increased rates for packhorse fees and ferry crossing fees at post stations along the highways: whereas the time limit was from the 1st month of Bunsei 2, year of the Rabbit (U) until the 12th month of the current year of the Rat (Ne), we have heard that the post stations are in financial difficulty and hardship. Therefore, for the Tōkaidō from Shinagawa to Moriguchi, including the Saya route, from the coming 1st month of the year of the Ox until the 12th month of the coming year of the Dog (Inu), for a period of ten years, both packhorse fees and ferry crossing fees shall continue to have a 20% increase as before. For the Nakasendō from Itabashi to Moriyama

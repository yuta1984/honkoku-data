These six post stations serve as relay stations for men and horses on the secondary highways. However, they have been suffering from poverty for consecutive years, and particularly in recent years, with the increase in various travelers, there are concerns that the relay operations may be hindered. They submitted various complaints about their hardships and petitioned the Highway Magistrate's Office (Dōchū Bugyōsho) last year regarding the continuation of operations, but their petition was not accepted. However, after subsequent investigation, it was determined that their difficulties were unavoidable, so from the coming Year of the Dog to the coming Year of the Sheep,

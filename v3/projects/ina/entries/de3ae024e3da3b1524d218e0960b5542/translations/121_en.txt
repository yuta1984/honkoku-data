Since there was no delay in this matter, it should have been dealt with strictly rather than being neglected. Due to such negligence being extremely improper, the town officials are sentenced to confinement (oshikomi), and the neighborhood representatives (chōdai) are fined.

1. Uhyōe's landlords and the Shimizu-chō association members - if they had heard about his improper conduct up to this point, they should have taken appropriate measures, but since they neglected this matter, which is extremely improper, they are fined.

1. Members of both shop associations in Hon-chō are treated the same way and are strictly reprimanded.

1. Regarding Uhyōe's servants Kyūsaku and Tono - since there were improper matters concerning this person and respective punishments were applied, both individuals, having no involvement, may go wherever they wish at their own discretion.

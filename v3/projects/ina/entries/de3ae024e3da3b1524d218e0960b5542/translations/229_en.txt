2nd year of the Ox (Bunsei 8, 1825), 2nd month, 27th day
1. The following official notice was issued from the government office:
In Edo, Urushiyama Genzaemon has been appointed to the position of Senior Councilor (Chuuro-yaku). If there are any persons with the same name as Genzaemon, they must change their names. This matter shall be announced throughout the town.

The above official notice was delivered through the magistrate to the town representatives of Shimozato-machi and others, and announced throughout the town.

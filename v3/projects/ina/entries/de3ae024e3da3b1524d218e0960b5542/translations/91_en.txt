【Right Page】

There is a matter involving Koda Gohei's petition, and since thorough investigation and clarification are necessary to maintain proper standing, we show special consideration by not pursuing this matter further, and instead order house confinement (disciplinary confinement). Therefore, exercise strict caution in your conduct. The above instructions shall be conveyed. 

January 17th
To Shimizu Yazaemon
Among the scribes

1. From Kanbara Bridge along the Mitsumine River [Katsu?] to Ashizawa Joshimo
2. From Nakamura on the Fujisawa River to the ferry crossing
   Regarding the above locations, from this time forward
   These shall be designated as rivers where hunting/fishing is prohibited while His Lordship is in residence at the castle

【Left Page】

The designated areas where this prohibition has been announced shall be patrolled and monitored by lower-ranking officials and fine-officers (kōori)

Bunsei 10, Year of the Boar (1827)

Regarding His Lordship's remarriage, permission has been granted as requested.
The name of the bride is
Lady Omandai (Mayo). Therefore,
from now on, men and women of any status
must refrain from using the name "Mandai."
Please announce this matter throughout the town.
Above
　7th day of the 1st month

The above written notice was sent from
Lord Isaku Sadaemon, which we received and showed to our colleagues,
passed it to the magistrate's town, and ordered
it to be circulated through the ten towns.

1. From the government office, as follows:
　Regarding the use of old gold and silver coins, which was
　announced in the 2nd month of this year, in the coming Year of the Boar

From the 2nd month, their use will be completely prohibited.

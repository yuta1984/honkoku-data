　　　　　Hokimochi Village　Sakai Seibei
　　　　　　　　To the Officials

　The official notice is as follows:

One　One gun　　Barrel length: nine sun
　　　　　　　　Bullet caliber: approximately three monme five bu
　　　　　　　　Stock: oak wood
　　　　　　　　Metal fittings: brass

　The above gun was brought by two out of ten people who lodged at the inn of Zensaemon at Motoyama post station on the Nakasendo road in Matsudaira Tamba-no-kami's territory on the night of the twentieth day of the intercalary sixth month of this year. They left it behind and fled, and their whereabouts are unknown. If anyone has knowledge of this gun, they must immediately report to the office of Tamba-no-kami's territory. Should anyone conceal it and it be discovered by others, strict punishment will be applied to that person and the village officials.

Bunsei 12, Year of the Ox
　First month

Regarding sake brewing in all provinces: while it was proclaimed in Bunka 3, Year of the Tiger, that sake brewing could be conducted at will not only by sake brewers but also by holders of dormant licenses and others who had not previously engaged in such business, the sake brewing by holders of dormant licenses and those not engaged in business that began from the said Tiger year shall be strictly prohibited until further notice.

The above directive shall be promptly communicated without omission to shogunal domains, private domains, and temple and shrine lands alike.

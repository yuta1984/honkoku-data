【Left Page】

It has been reported that townspeople
have committed acts of disrespect
toward retainers of the domain. Regarding this matter, orders have been
issued according to precedent, and the village office has thoroughly
spread the word, with each person supposed to understand, yet improper
conduct still occurs.

The disrespectful behavior toward superiors is incomprehensible.
After all, through particularly lenient measures,
misconduct once or twice has sometimes been
overlooked, but instead some people
consider this natural, which is extremely
improper. Already during the village inspection
in the past Dog year, orders were immediately given
that toward retainers and indeed all sword-bearing
persons, no unlawful acts whatsoever
should be committed. Yet disrespectful
behavior has occurred, which shows a misunderstanding.

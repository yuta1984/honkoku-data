January 20th
Lord Takada Rokuzaemon
Among the scribes
Regarding the above matter being proclaimed,
I have communicated this to my fellow officials.

Concerning the prohibition of musical instruments and music until the 15th of next month, which was previously announced,
there has been a change in the lord's intention, and the prohibition of musical instruments is no longer necessary.

The above matter shall be announced throughout the town.
The above.

January 29th

The above official notice was delivered to Chuuzaemon, the town representative of Honmachi, and announced throughout the town.

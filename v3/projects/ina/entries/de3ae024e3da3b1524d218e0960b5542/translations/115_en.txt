Kozuke

The various halls and other structures in the above three provinces having suffered major damage, permission has been granted for soliciting donations for three years to assist with repairs. Bearing donation solicitation documents with the joint seals of the temple and shrine magistrates, from the ninth month of this year of the Dog until the eighth month of the coming year of the Ox, they shall make rounds through shogunal lands, private domains, temple and shrine lands, and rural towns. Therefore, those who comply with this directive shall make donations regardless of the amount, and this order shall be conveyed by the shogunal deputies for shogunal lands and by the domain lords and local lords for private domains.

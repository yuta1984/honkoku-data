Reign of Lord Yoriyasu
- Bunsei 4, Year of the Snake, 7th month, 28th day (July 28, 1821): As requested by Kanemon, permitted to retire, granted family inheritance of stipend 22 hyō and 2-person sustenance, rank of attendant guard granted, appointed to next shift
- Same year, 10th month, 28th day: Appointed to attendance service
- Tenpō 3, Year of the Dragon, 12th month, 18th day (December 18, 1832): Appointed to concurrently handle petitions from servants and baggage matters
- Same 7th year, Year of the Monkey, 1st month, 15th day (January 15, 1836): Appointed to remittance department, concurrently serving as supervisor of servant petitions and baggage
- Same 8th year, Year of the Rooster, 12th month, 5th day (December 5, 1837): Due to reorganization, relieved without incident of concurrent duties supervising servant petitions and baggage, remittance duties to continue as before
- Same 10th year, Year of the Boar, 2nd month, 28th day (February 28, 1838): Due to reorganization, appointed to head of servants baggage supervisor position, same day appointed temporary acting stable supervisor by directive
- Same 11th year, Year of the Rat, 1st month, 15th day (January 15, 1839): Increase of 2 hyō
- Same 14th year, Year of the Rabbit, 1st month, 28th day (January 28, 1841): Relieved of both head of servants supervisor and temporary stable supervisor duties, appointed to next shift
- Kōka 2, Year of the Wood Snake, 11th month, 25th day (November 25, 1845): Appointed temporary assistant service at Lord Yoshinosuke's palace
- Kaei 6, Year of the Water Ox, 4th month, 18th day (April 18, 1853): Granted rank of middle page, appointed to full service at Lord Yoshinosuke's palace, increase of 1-person sustenance, granted total of 24 hyō and 3-person sustenance

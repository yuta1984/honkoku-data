Advance Guard Unit, son of Yamashita Mineemon
Yamashita Tomoho
Later changed name to Sano Uemon

During the reign of Lord Genryūin
- Enpō 2, Year of the Ox (1745), 9th month, 15th day: Called to serve as a monk, granted 4 koku 2 to and stipend for one person
- Enpō 5, Year of the Dragon (1748), 3rd month, 29th day: Granted regular rank of 5 koku and stipend for two people, appointed to garrison duty
- Same year, 10th month, 28th day: Appointed as secretary to the administrative officer and ordered to return to secular life, recalled from garrison duty and made rankless
- Enpō 4, Year of the Sheep (1747), 1st month, 15th day: Promoted to foot soldier rank, granted 16 hyō and stipend for two people
- Hōreki 8, Year of the Boar (1758), 8th month, 11th day: Granted attendant rank, received increase of 2 hyō, appointed to 20 hyō land administration
- Hōreki 9, Year of the Rat (1758), 4th month, 7th day: Appointed to concurrent position as secretary to the chief retainer
- An'ei 2, Year of the Snake (1773), 1st month, 15th day: Received increase of 4 hyō
- An'ei 4, Year of the Sheep (1775), 8th month, 9th day: Released from secretary duties upon request
(Written in red ink) Yamashita Tomo

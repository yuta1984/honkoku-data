　　　　　　　　　　　　Nagasaka Yoshiemon

Reign of Lord Gentakuin
- Kyōhō 21, Year of the Fire Dragon, 4th month, 6th day (April 6, 1736): Kitchen department apprentice without rank, called by examination department supervision, granted 3 ryō and 1.5-person sustenance
- Genbun 2, Year of the Fire Snake, 12th month, 2nd day (December 2, 1737): Promoted to foot soldier, appointed to kitchen department under steward supervision, increase of 2.5 bu sustenance
- Same 5th year, Year of the Metal Monkey, 12th month, 25th day (December 25, 1740): Increase of 2 hyō, granted total of 18 hyō
- Enkyō 3, Year of the Fire Tiger, 4th month, 12th day (April 12, 1746): Increase of 1-person sustenance, granted 3-person sustenance
- Hōreki 2, Year of the Water Monkey, 1st month, 15th day (January 15, 1752): Attendant guard rank, increase of 2 hyō
- Same 5th year, Year of the Boar, 7th month, 12th day (July 12, 1755): Appointed to catering department, concurrently serving kitchen department
- Same 7th year, Year of the Fire Ox, 11th month, 4th day (November 4, 1757): Relieved of duties, appointed to next shift, concurrently serving kitchen department, ordered to appear as duties require

「(Red ink) Nagasaka」

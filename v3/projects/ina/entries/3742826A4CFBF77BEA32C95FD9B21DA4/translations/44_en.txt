Yoneyama Senji
《Name changed to: Kanemon》
- Meiwa 7, Year of the Metal Tiger, 4th month, 28th day (April 28, 1770): Called to serve as foot soldier in attendance service, stipend of 16 hyō and 2-person sustenance
- Same 9th year, Year of the Water Dragon, 6th month, 9th day (June 9, 1772): Inherited father's position as attendant guard rank, granted stipend of 22 hyō and 2-person sustenance, duties to continue as before in attendance service

Reign of Lord Honryūin
- Tenmei 7, Year of the Fire Goat, 2nd month, 4th day (February 4, 1787): Appointed to tax collection department
- Kansei 2, Year of the Earth Dog, 2nd month, 14th day (February 14, 1790): Appointed to corridor duty with increase of 2 hyō

Reign of Lord Yorimoto
- Same 4th year, Year of the Rat, 11th month, 2nd day (November 2, 1792): Appointed to chief clerk's office and remittance department
- Same 5th year, Year of the Ox, 12th month, 28th day (December 28, 1793): Ordered to self-restraint
- Same 6th year, Year of the Tiger, 12th month, 17th day (December 17, 1794): Rank of middle page with increase of 2 hyō and 1-person sustenance
- Same 8th year, Year of the Dragon, 1st month, 15th day (January 15, 1796): Increase of 2 hyō
- Bunka 1, Year of the Rat, 4th month, 20th day (April 20, 1804): Appointed to investigation department, remittance and chief clerk duties to continue as before
- Same 4th year, Year of the Rabbit, 2nd month, 13th day (February 13, 1807): Appointed inspector of middle pages with increase of 2 hyō, investigation duties to continue concurrently
- Same 8th year, Year of the Sheep, 10th month, 28th day (October 28, 1811): Appointed to provisions department concurrently, to oversee rest quarters business, other duties to continue as before
- Same 10th year, Year of the Rooster, 12th month, 10th day (December 10, 1813): Due to illness, as requested, relieved of three official duties without incident and appointed to next shift

Reign of Lord Yoriyasu
- Bunsei 4, Year of the Snake, 7th month, 28th day (July 28, 1821): Due to old age and illness, as requested, permitted to retire

As requested by the family clan, was appointed as adopted son and granted a stipend of 22 hyō and provisions for two retainers, with appointment to the positions of gokūban (lord's attendant) and gokyūjigata (service attendant). Furthermore, as it was heard that he had been devoted to Confucian studies, he was instructed to continue his efforts without negligence and to work diligently so as to be useful for official duties in the future.

1. From the 15th day of the 9th month of the same 3rd year, year of the metal dog (1851), was notified to also report to the gokūkata (lord's attendant department). On the same day, due to the young lord's outing, was appointed to intensive escort duty, and for this service was granted a monetary allowance of 2 ryō and 3 bu.

During the reign of Lord Yoriyuki

1. On the 5th day of the 5th month of Kyōwa 2, year of the dog (1802), was granted assistance provisions for three retainers.

1. On the 15th day of the 1st month of Bunka 7, year of the sheep (1810), was newly summoned and granted a stipend of 35 hyō and provisions for three retainers, and appointed to the rank of ochū-koshō (middle page).

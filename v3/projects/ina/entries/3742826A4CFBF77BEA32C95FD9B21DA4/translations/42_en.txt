Yoneyama Shinka
《Marginal note: Changed name to Senemon》
Reign of Lady Shin'nyoin
- Kyōhō 20, Year of the Wood Rabbit (1735), 8th month, 7th day: Appointed as monk supervisor

Reign of Lord Gen'ryūin
- Genbun 1, Year of the Fire Dragon (1736), 4th month, 6th day: Promoted to foot soldier, ordered to return to secular life, granted 16 hyō and 2-person stipend for residential service
- Kanpō 1, Year of the Metal Rooster (1741), 12th month, 1st day: Granted increase of 2 hyō
- Kanpō 4, Year of the Wood Rat (1744), 1st month, 15th day: Granted escort guard status with increase of 2 hyō, residential service
- Enkyō 5, Year of the Earth Dragon (1748), 1st month, 15th day: Granted increase of 1-person stipend
- Hōreki 4, Year of the Wood Dog (1754), 11th month, 1st day: Relieved of garrison duty and sent back to home domain
- Hōreki 5, Year of the Wood Boar (1755), 9th month, 18th day: Granted middle page status, appointed to investigation duties with increase of 6 hyō, totaling 26 hyō and 3-person stipend
- Meiwa 8, Year of the Metal Rabbit (1771), 6th month, 25th day: As requested, relieved of duties and appointed to alternate service
- Meiwa 9, Year of the Water Dragon (1772), 4th month, 16th day: Died of illness
"(Red ink) Yoneyama"

Eldest son of Katsuemon
Yamashita Shōsuke
(Name changed to: Sakuji)

During the reign of Lord Yorimoto
1. Bunka 9, year of the monkey, 2nd month, 8th day: Due to an internal petition,
   appointed to apprentice service, and due to current difficulties in the monk affairs department,
   temporarily appointed as assistant
1. Same 11th year, year of the dog, 1st month, 15th day: appointed as
   assistant clerk and granted one and a half person's stipend
1. Same 14th year, year of the ox, 1st month, 15th day: appointed to
   full clerk duties and granted an additional half stipend
1. Bunpō 2, year of the rabbit, intercalary 4th month, 18th day: granted a stipend of 16 hyō and two person's rations,
   promoted to foot soldier, continuing clerk duties as before
During the reign of Lord Yoriyasu
1. Same 3rd year, year of the dragon, 2nd month, 23rd day: granted additional two hyō, rank of attendant guard class,
   appointed to district administrator for territorial matters
1. Same 5th year, year of the horse, 9th month, 28th day: due to reorganization of rural affairs, successfully
   relieved of district administrator duties, appointed to deputy service under chief retainer supervision
"(Red ink) Yamashita Shō"

Humbly submitted petition in writing:
Regarding a person named Tomokichi, son of Denbei from Mizoguchi village, as we have
an informal marriage arrangement with him, I would like to take him in as a dependent. Therefore,
I humbly petition for this.
If Your Honor would graciously grant this petition as requested,
it would be most grateful and fortunate. The above.

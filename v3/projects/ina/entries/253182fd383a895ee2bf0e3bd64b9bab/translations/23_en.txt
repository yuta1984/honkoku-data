　The above petition was submitted on the 1st day of the 11th month at the government office through the mediation of Lord Inokuchi Sadamutsu. On the 6th day, we received a letter from Mr. Izawa stating that the price should be 405 mon per shō, and sales should begin from tomorrow, the 7th day. We called Man'ya Shōzō and conveyed these instructions to him.

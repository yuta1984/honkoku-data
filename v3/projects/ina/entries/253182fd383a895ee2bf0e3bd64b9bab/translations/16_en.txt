【Right Page】
　　Respectfully submitting this petition in writing
My younger brother Kanzo has an informal relationship with the widow 
of Kisoya Ihei in Shimomachi, so I have entered into negotiations
to send him as an adopted son. Therefore, 
I respectfully submit this petition.
Regarding the above matter, if you would graciously grant this petition
as requested, it would be most grateful and fortunate. The above.
　　　　　　　　　　　　Nakamachi
　　　　　　　　　　　　Yamagataya
Bunsei 9, Year of the Dog, 8th month　　Petitioner: Taroemon
【Left Page】
　　　　　　　　　　　　Agent: Yoshiemon
　　　　　　　　　　　　Also: Kanshiro
　　To the Magistrate's Office
The above petition was submitted with the customary endorsement and seal on the 15th
through the intermediary of Mr. Izawa Iwaemon at the government office,
and approval was immediately granted. Agent Kanshiro was summoned and informed.

Memorandum
- Rice, 2 bales　　　　　　Containing 3 tō 3 shō each

The above rice was given for performing the town lantern rounds for three nights from the 1st day of the 12th month of the current Year of the Dog until the last day of the 1st month of the coming Year of the Boar. As usual, it was given to the eta, delivered from Kawashimo village using the Dog year tribute rice. We have certainly received it and immediately distributed it to the eta. This serves as our record.

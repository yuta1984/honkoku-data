【Left Page】
Humbly submitting this written petition

I have been working as a plasterer (sakan) until now, but from this year forward, I would like to serve official duties for the Public Works Department three days per year and dedicate myself to my professional duties. Therefore, I humbly submit this petition.

Regarding the above matter, if you would graciously grant my petition as requested, I would consider it a most grateful blessing. The above.

                        Seri-machi Plasterer

Possession, receipt, transfer, and exchange shall all be conducted without delay, and tax payments shall be handled in the same manner.

1. Regarding exchange money, there is no distinction between koban and one-bu coins. Even if all koban or all one-bu coins are presented, the exchange will be conducted at a ratio of seven ryō in koban to three ryō in one-bu coins. Starting from the twentieth day of this month, bring them to the Gotō Sanemon office and other places listed separately for exchange.

1. Samurai and others may also arrange through townspeople to bring them to the aforementioned

[Left Page]
listed parties for exchange, which is also permitted at their discretion.

1. Regarding the koban and one-bu coins to be presented for exchange, since the quantities are known, exchange them without hoarding. If anyone is found to be hoarding without exchanging, they will be strictly punished after investigation.

The above matters shall be announced.

　　　March
Last December of the year of the Ox, when Nishimura Shinzaburo, former retainer of Honda Omi-no-kami, and Shimada Sonosuke, former foot soldier, were traveling with a woman from Nagasaki to Edo, a merchant-like person named Seizo of unknown residence, who was asked by Shinzaburo to accompany the woman and bypassed the Imagiri checkpoint by boat, physical description:
【Left Page】
1. About forty years old in the current year of the Rabbit　1. Birthplace said to be Nagato Province
1. Medium height and rather fat　1. Rather round face
1. Thin sideburns and thin ears　1. Thin eyebrows
1. Rather large eyes　1. Rather low nose
1. Thin mouth　1. Poor speaking ability
1. Clothing at that time: indigo-striped cotton padded jacket, cotton half-coat, cotton underwear, cotton kosode with obi, light-colored tie-dyed cotton three-shaku hand towel tied around, navy cotton hakama-style trousers, and cotton gaiters.
If anyone matching this description is found, they should be detained at that location...

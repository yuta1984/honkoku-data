This time, it has been ordered that one-shu front-stamped coins be minted using ryōme nanryō high-grade silver that is currently in circulation. Sixteen of these front-stamped coins shall be calculated as equivalent to one ryō of gold. Moreover, silver coins should be understood at the same exchange rate as the two-shu coins, and should be used in circulation mixed together with the one-shu coins used until now. Regarding these nanryō one-shu silver coins, since they have been ordered to circulate in the same manner as gold, they should be put into circulation without delay. This matter should also be announced to all provinces.
　　Sixth month

As stated above, an official document has been issued from the government, so this is being announced throughout the town. The above.

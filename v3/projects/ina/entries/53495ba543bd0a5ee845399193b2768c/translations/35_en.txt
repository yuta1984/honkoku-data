One: When traveling, if advance notices are sent out without having lodging arrangements, the person in charge of arranging horses and porters at post stations, as well as lodging arrangements, will be reduced and managed accordingly, causing great confusion. Therefore, advance notices without arrangements should not be sent out.

One: At post stations, even for cargo to be forwarded without directly hiring horses and porters through private negotiation, everything shall be uniformly inspected at the weight inspection stations.

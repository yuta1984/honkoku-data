Although the above directive was announced in the fourth month of Shōtoku 3 (1713), year of the Snake,

　　　Hemp cloth, one roll　　　length: 5 jō 4 shaku

　　　Same, one tan　　　　　　length: 2 jō 7 shaku
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　width: same

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　width: same

　　　Same, one tan　　　　　　length: 2 jō 7 shaku
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　width: 1 shaku
Both cotton and hemp cloth shorter than the above measurements
must not be bought or sold at all. Should there be anyone who misunderstands,
and someone weaves and sells cloth or cotton that falls short
of the above measurements, punishment will be imposed.
However, short-measured cloth and cotton that has been purchased and stored up until now
should be sold off by the sixth month of this year, and from the seventh month onward, dealings should be conducted
according to the measurements established above.

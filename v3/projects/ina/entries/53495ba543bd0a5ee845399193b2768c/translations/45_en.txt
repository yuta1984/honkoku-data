1. Government deliberation is unnecessary. Therefore, repairs to embankments and river maintenance works shall henceforth be assigned as local public works (jifushin). Regarding this matter, national duty money (kokuyaku-kin) need not be submitted. However, regarding the costs of public works completed up to now, the portion of supplementary fees (warizeni) shall be collected proportionally as planned. Furthermore, regarding domains of 10,000 koku or more and temple/shrine lands, they should understand the intent of previous proclamations: while cases of actual major damage that are beyond repair are exceptional, in other cases they should perform local public works as much as possible, and while requesting assistance when beyond their capacity is naturally acceptable, they must not frivolously request national public works on ordinary grounds.

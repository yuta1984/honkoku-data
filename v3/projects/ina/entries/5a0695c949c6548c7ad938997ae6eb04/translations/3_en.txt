Oral Statement Record

Regarding the dispute between Miyashita Heibei of Takasago-machi and Banyaku Rihei, we were ordered to provide mediation. Both parties have accepted arbitration, their respective claims have been heard, and an amicable settlement has been reached. As stated in the separate document of intent, both parties have agreed and a satisfactory resolution has been achieved. Therefore, there will be no further troublesome matters requiring your attention regarding this issue. We hereby submit this oral statement. The above.

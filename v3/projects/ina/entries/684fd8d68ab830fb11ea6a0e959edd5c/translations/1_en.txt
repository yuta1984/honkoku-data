Article One: Regarding the matter of stone cutters in our village, we have thoroughly investigated all residents of the village and found that there is not a single person engaged in this occupation in our village. Should our investigation prove insufficient and even one person be discovered, we will accept your notification of such discovery, and whatever punishment may be imposed upon us thereafter, we harbor no resentment whatsoever. As proof for future reference, we submit this jointly signed document.

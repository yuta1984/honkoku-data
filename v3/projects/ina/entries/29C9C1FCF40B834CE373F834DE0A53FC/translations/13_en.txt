Same Jōsuke (Second Generation)
1. On the same day of the same year, as per the succession petition, he was assigned to the castle deputy group with a stipend of 5 koku and provisions for 2 people, but the details of his employment and service prior to this are unknown.

2. On the 15th day of the 2nd month of Bunka 7th year, Kanoe-Uma year (1810), he was appointed to serve as a court attendant monk (hyō-bōzu), and served twice in garrison duty at Edo.

3. On the 15th day of the 2nd month of the same 13th year, Hinoe-Ne year (1816), he died of illness. His total years of service were fifteen years.

　　　　　　　His Son
1. On the 13th day of the 5th month of the same year, as per the succession petition, he was assigned to the advance guard unit under Kanbe Goroemon-sama with a stipend of 5 koku and provisions for 2 people.

2. On the 15th day of the 3rd month of Tenpō 2nd year, Kanoto-U year (1831), he was appointed to handle the unit's financial affairs, and served for seventeen years until Tenpō 3rd year, Mizunoe-Tatsu year.

　　　　　　　Ikeda Yamaemon
This document was submitted on the 10th day of the 5th month of Tenpō 3rd year, Mizunoe-Tatsu year (1832) in accordance with official instructions.

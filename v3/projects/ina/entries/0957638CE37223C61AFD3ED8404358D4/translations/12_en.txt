11th day
One: Lord Mizoguchi Kamejirō's inquiry was completed, and his coming-of-age ceremony (mage-tori) was performed on the 6th.

　　　12th day
One: Lord Mōri Ise-no-kami was scheduled to depart on the 9th for his leave of absence, but due to sudden indisposition, he postponed his departure and submitted a notification to that effect.

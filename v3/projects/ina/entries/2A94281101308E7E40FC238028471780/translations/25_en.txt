March 29th day - Okutani Genzaemon

On the 13th day past:
There being official business, [you] should ascend to the castle at four o'clock. The previous day, an official letter jointly signed by the Senior Councilors arrived. However, as His Lordship was feeling unwell, Naito Tonomo-no-kami served as his representative and ascended to the castle, whereupon the marriage proposal for Princess Tsune was approved as requested. Therefore, in celebration, all samurai from ashigaru foot soldiers and above should wear hemp formal dress and appear at the registry today.
The above.

Both officials of the same rank took their seats together, entered near the threshold of Yosozaemon's house parlor, the reader read the preamble aloud, the blood seal was completed, both of us reviewed it, submitted it to the middle pages and inspectors, the inspectors completed their review, and it was received by the official department.

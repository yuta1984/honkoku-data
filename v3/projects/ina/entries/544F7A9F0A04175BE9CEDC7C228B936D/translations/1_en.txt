Copy of Lord Yoshimune's Vermillion Seal Document
This vermillion seal document (crossed out) on one sheet of large high-quality danshi paper, with character arrangement as shown
Upper wrapper also on one sheet of the same paper
In the 10th month of Kyōhō 19, year of the Wood Tiger, as ordered from Edo, this copy was submitted by courier on the 13th day of the same month. Regarding this matter, on the 11th day of the same month, a copy was made exactly as written in the original vermillion seal document. Castle copy - although on smaller paper than the original document, the paper quality, characters, upper wrapper, folding method, etc. are identical without any differences.

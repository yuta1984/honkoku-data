Kanoe-Hitsuji 8th month (August 1871)

Attached: Draft Receipt

Since the postal station law reform in April of last year, regarding the wages for official correspondence and official goods dispatched from the Ekitei-ryo (Postal Administration) and various outposts, payments shall be made for the period up to July of this year, based on verified porter records.

The conditions are that details such as night relay services shall be categorized, investigated within each jurisdictional area according to the format of the attached template, and submitted by the coming October. For records from August onward, they shall be investigated within one month and submitted directly to the Ekitei-ryo through station-village relay by the 20th of the following month.

We acknowledge the directive instructing us to issue the above orders.

Kanoe-Hitsuji 8th month (August 1871)

The above directive shall be communicated to temples and shrines throughout the rural areas as well.

　　　　　　　Development Office
　　　　　　　　　To the Officials of the Government Office

　　　Humbly submitting this written petition
One matter: I have until now possessed four lime kilns and have been paying the transportation tax to the authorities,
　　but I wish to suspend operations of one kiln in the government forest mountain this year.
　　I humbly request that through your benevolence, you grant exemption for one kiln's worth
　　of both transportation tax and mountain usage fees.

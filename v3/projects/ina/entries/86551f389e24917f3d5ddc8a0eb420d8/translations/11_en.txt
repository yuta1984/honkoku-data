Regarding the money borrowed

Item: Eighteen ryō of gold　　　Mr. Hosoda
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Mediation money

The above amount has been borrowed as capital for purchasing cocoons.
We will certainly add interest at the rate of twelve bu and five rin per one ryō,
and will promptly repay the loan as soon as we sell the silk thread.
This certificate of borrowing money is executed for future reference, as stated herein.

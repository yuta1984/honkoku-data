1. Regarding the matter when both stipend recipients (kyūnin) and above, and those without rank (musoku) and below are granted leave
　　When leave is granted without delay upon petition, both rice yields (motonari) and the number of rice bales shall be
　　calculated monthly and deducted, with shortfalls to be distributed and excess amounts to be
　　returned. For both large stipends (ōbuchi-kata) and attached stipends, distribution shall continue until the
　　day leave is granted, with amounts from the following day to be returned. If
　　leave is granted due to misconduct, there shall be no adjustment for excess or shortage,
　　no deductions made, and rice yields and bale numbers previously received and held

1. For types such as abandonment stipends (o-sute-buchi), when stipends for the following month are distributed at month's end

1. When monks are summoned, their stipend distribution shall follow the same method as for ashigaru rank and below (無足以下)
　　　　However, small stipends (小扶持) shall be paid from the month of notification
1. For ashigaru captains and below, both stone-cut rice (石切米) and monetary stipends shall be paid according to monthly calculations from the month they were hired
　　　　However, small stipends shall be paid from the month of the pledge document (請状). If there is no pledge document, payment shall begin from the month they came under their captain's command
1. Regarding salary increases, including regional rice allocations, even if announced at the end of the twelfth month, the full amount of the increase shall be paid all at once

1. Salary increases for those in the large stipend category (大扶持方) shall be paid calculated from the month the increase was announced
1. When someone is transferred from a large stipend of two rice bales to regional allocation, even if announced in the twelfth month, the regional allocation amount for that full year shall be paid, with the previous large stipend rice allocation deducted

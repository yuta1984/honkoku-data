Koizumi Katsuroku
Later known as Wasuke

During the reign of Shinnyo-in-sama
- Kyōhō 13, Year of the Earth Monkey (1728), 5th month, 11th day: Due to his young age, he was granted four-person stipend as financial support and appointed to the rank of personal attendant guard
- Same 20th year, Year of the Wood Rabbit (1735), 5th month, 19th day: Appointed as caller/messenger

During the reign of Genryū-in-sama  
- Genbun 2, Year of the Fire Snake (1737), 1st month, 11th day: Inherited his deceased father Wasuke's position with a stipend of 18 hyō and support for two people, appointed to regular guard duty according to his rank
- Same 4th year, Year of the Earth Sheep (1739), 12th month, 12th day: Appointed as middle page in personal attendance, with an increase of 12 hyō and support for one person, bringing his total to 30 hyō and support for three people
- Kan'en 2, Year of the Earth Snake (1749), 3rd month, 23rd day: Appointed as inspector of middle pages, with an increase of 5 hyō bringing his total to 35 hyō
- Hōreki 8, Year of the Earth Tiger (1758), 1st month, 15th day: Appointed concurrently as chief steward of investigations
- Same 12th year, Year of the Water Horse (1762), intercalary 4th month, 29th day:
"(In red ink) Koizumi Katsu"

Sanpei Tanzaburō
Later called Moriemon
Retirement name: Ryūsui

During the reign of Lord Genryūin
1. On the 1st day of the 8th month of Kanpō 1, year of the Rooster (1741), he had his first audience with the lord.
1. On the 15th day of the 2nd month of Kanpō 4, year of the Wood Rat (1744), he was summoned as an attendant page (gochū-koshō) and granted a stipend of 26 hyō and 3 persons' portions.
1. On the 19th day of the 7th month of Enkyō 2, year of the Wood Ox (1745), he inherited his deceased father's position without change, receiving 40 hyō and 3 persons' portions, and was appointed to guard duty.
1. On the 5th day of the 8th month of the same year, he entered guard duty.
1. On the 15th day of the 1st month of Hōreki 6, year of the Fire Rat (1756), he was appointed to water management duties.
1. On the 23rd day of the 10th month of the same year, he was appointed to fire watch duties.
1. On the 9th day of the 9th month of Hōreki 10, year of the Metal Dragon (1760), he was ordered to go to Edo for official seal protection duties.
1. On the 9th day of the 4th month of Hōreki 12, year of the Water Horse (1762), he was appointed to Edo residence duty, serving as assistant guard during his tenure.
1. In the 11th month of Meiwa 1, year of the Wood Monkey (1764), he was ordered to move to Edo as concurrent construction magistrate and armaments magistrate.
1. On the 12th day of the 4th month of Meiwa 9, year of the Water Dragon (1772), he received an increase of 10 hyō, bringing his stipend to 50 hyō.
1. On the 15th day of the 5th month of An'ei 2, year of the Water Snake (1773), he was appointed to messenger and liaison duties, and was relieved of both previous positions.

During the reign of Lord Honryūin
1. On the 29th day of the 1st month of Tenmei 4, year of the Wood Dragon (1784), he was ordered to move to the domain residence.
1. On the 3rd day of the 2nd month of Tenmei 7, year of the Fire Goat (1787), he was relieved of his duties and ordered to return to regular guard service.
1. On the 15th day of the 1st month of Tenmei 8, year of the Earth Monkey (1788), he was appointed as senior inspector rank liaison officer.
1. On the 20th day of the 3rd month of Kansei 3, year of the Boar (1791), he was granted 60 koku and 3 persons' portions, and ordered to move to Edo.

During the reign of Lord Yorimoto
1. On the 15th day of the 1st month of Kansei 11, year of the Goat (1799), he was appointed as senior inspector.
1. On the 15th day of the 1st month of Kansei 13, year of the Rooster (1801), he was temporarily appointed as armaments magistrate.
1. On the 5th day of the 4th month of Kyōwa 2, year of the Dog (1802), he was relieved of his temporary armaments magistrate position.
1. On the 23rd day of the 12th month of Kyōwa 3, year of the Boar (1803), as requested, he was relieved of his duties and assigned to guard duty according to stipend rank.

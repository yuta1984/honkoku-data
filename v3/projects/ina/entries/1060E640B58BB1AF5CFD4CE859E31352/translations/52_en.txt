　　　　Service record

　　　　　　　　Emergency Adopted Son
　　　　　　　　　Nakamura Tsunegorō
During the reign of Lord Yoriyasu
- On the 23rd day of the 9th month of Tenpō 14, Year of the Rabbit (1843), as per the deathbed request of Nakamura Sōzō, he was appointed as adopted son, granted succession with a stipend of 35 hyō and provisions for 3 people, and assigned to guard duty according to stipend rank.

- On the 23rd day of the intercalary 9th month of the same year, he was appointed to guard duty in the grand chamber.

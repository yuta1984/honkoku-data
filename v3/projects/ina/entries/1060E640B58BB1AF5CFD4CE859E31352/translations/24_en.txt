Fujita Chūjirō
Later called Heijiunemon

During the reign of Lord Honryūin
- 11th day of the 7th month of Tenmei 3, Year of the Water Rabbit (1783): Inherited family headship without issue, stipend of 40 hyō of rice and provisions for 3 people, ranked according to guard unit stipend
- 12th day of the 8th month of Tenmei 7, Year of the Fire Sheep (1787): Appointed to 水之手 (water supply management)

- 15th day of the 1st month of Bunka 13, Year of the Rat (1816): Appointed to 御使番 (messenger)

During the reign of Lord Yoriyasu
- 18th day of the 10th month of Bunsei, Year of the Snake (1821): Appointed to 大目付格御取次 (senior inspector rank liaison)
- 15th day of the 1st month of Bunsei 9, Year of the Dog (1826): For excellence in arts instruction, awarded hemp formal wear with the wisteria family crest as a reward

- On the 15th day of the 6th month of the 9th year (1822), submitted a petition to resign from office due to illness, but was ordered to remain in position
- On the 15th day of the 8th month of the same year, due to illness, was relieved of personal attendant duties without incident and reassigned to regular guard rotation
- On the 5th day of the 3rd month of the 10th year (1823), appointed to personal attendant service
- On the 5th day of the 6th month of the 11th year (1824), appointed to the small treasury and clothing department
- On the 8th day of the same month of the same year, appointed to the medicine department
- On the 29th day of the 3rd month of the 12th year (1825), appointed to personal secretary and record-keeping duties
- On the 10th day of the 8th month of the same year, appointed to assist with meal service duties until Kamiki Hanzō returns to duty
- On the 20th day of the 9th month of Tenpō 4 (1833), appointed as training inspector and granted the rank of messenger, duties remain as before
- On the 13th day of the 5th month of Tenpō 5 (1834), submitted a petition to be relieved of official duties due to illness, but on the 15th day, the petition was returned by the officials
- On the 25th day of the 12th month of Tenpō 6 (1835), as requested due to illness, relieved of personal attendant duties and reassigned to regular rotation at the lower residence
- On the 20th day of the 4th month of Tenpō 7 (1836), appointed as construction magistrate

- On the 9th day of the 9th month of the same year, granted messenger rank and appointed as intermediary. Construction magistrate duties remain as before
- On the 28th day of the 11th month of Tenpō 9 (1838), granted senior inspector rank. Duties remain as before
- On the 27th day of the 10th month of Tenpō 11 (1840), appointed to assist the head of intermediaries. Construction magistrate duties remain as before
- On the 15th day of the 1st month of Tenpō 12 (1841), appointed to concurrently serve as senior inspector and head of intermediaries. Construction magistrate duties remain as before
- On the 12th day of the 7th month of the same year, appointed to concurrently serve as assistant caretaker. Senior inspector duties remain as before
- On the 27th day of the 8th month of Tenpō 13 (1842), appointed as accounting magistrate and mountain district supervisor. Senior inspector duties remain as before. To relocate to official residence below the turret at the lower residence
- On the 20th day of the 6th month of Tenpō 15 (1844), as requested due to illness, relieved of accounting magistrate, senior inspector, and mountain district supervisor duties and reassigned to regular rotation
- On the 13th day of the 1st month of Kōka 5 (1848), died of illness

Kawanishi Iwasaburō
During the reign of Lord Yoriyasu:
1. On the 23rd day of the 12th month of Tenpō 12, year of the Ox (1841), he was granted succession to Kawanishi Kotōta's family name and awarded a stipend of 26 hyō and provisions for three people. He was appointed to the rank of middle page with alternate duty.

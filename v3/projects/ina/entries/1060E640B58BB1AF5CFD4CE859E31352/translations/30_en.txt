Takada ryō, two-person stipend
Endō Wakahachi

During the reign of Lord Shinnyoin
- On the 21st day of the 12th month of Kyōhō 18, year of the Water Ox (1733), he was appointed as palanquin guard and assigned to the role of Dainagon household duties.

During the reign of Lord Genryūin
- On the 16th day of the 10th month of Genbun 1, year of the Fire Dragon (1736), he was appointed as middle chief and granted additional payment of 4 ryō 2 bu.
- On the 15th day of the 1st month of the 6th year, Kanoe-Tori (1741), he received an increase of 4 hyō 1-person stipend, totaling 24 hyō 3-person stipend, and was appointed as magistrate.
- On the 15th day of the 2nd month of Kanpō 4, year of the Wood Rat (1744), he was granted the rank of middle page and received an additional 2 hyō, totaling 26 hyō.
- On the 23rd day of the 12th month of Hōreki 3, year of the Water Rooster (1753), he was appointed as middle page inspector, also serving as examiner, with an additional 7 hyō increase, totaling 33 hyō.
- On the 22nd day of the 5th month of the 9th year, Tsuchinoto-U (1759), he died of illness.

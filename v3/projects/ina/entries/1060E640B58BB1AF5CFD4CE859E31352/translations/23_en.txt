Fujita Oriunemon
Later called Heijiunemon

During the reign of Lord Shinnyoin
- On the 1st day of the 8th month of Kyōhō 11, Year of the Fire Horse (1726): Appointed to the rank of御供番格 (personal attendant rank) succeeding his father Heijiunemon
- Intercalary 9th month of Kyōhō 14, Year of the Earth Rooster (1729): Appointed to 分一方 (accounting/taxation office)
- 15th day of the 1st month of Kyōhō 19, Year of the Wood Tiger (1734): Promoted to 御中小姓 (middle page), with a stipend of 35 hyō of rice and provisions for 3 people, duties remain as before
- 22nd day of the 4th month of the same year: Appointed to 御蔵方 (storehouse administration)

During the reign of Lord Genryūin
- 23rd day of the 12th month of Genbun 2, Year of the Fire Snake (1737): Relieved of duties and appointed to 御供方 (personal attendant service)
- 28th day of the 5th month of Kanpō 3, Year of the Water Boar (1743): Relieved of 御供方 duties as requested due to illness
- 28th day of the 7th month of Kan'en 2, Year of the Earth Snake (1749): Appointed to 会所預荷物方 (guild warehouse cargo management)
- 18th day of the 9th month of Hōreki 5, Year of the Wood Boar (1755): [Appointed to] 御中小姓目付 (middle page inspector) (red ink notation) "Fujita"

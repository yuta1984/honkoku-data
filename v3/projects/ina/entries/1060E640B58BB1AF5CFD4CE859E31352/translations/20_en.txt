Miyabe Entarō
During the reign of Lord Yoriyasu
- On the 5th day of the 3rd month of Kōka 5 (1848), the wishes left by his late father Zenbe'e on his deathbed were reported and acknowledged. Due to his young age, he was granted eight-person stipend as assistance, and the matter of inheritance would be appointed upon his coming of age. Service as companion to the young lord remains as before
- On the 5th day of the 9th month of Ka'ei 3 (1850), appointed as personal page due to staffing adjustments
- On the 18th day of the 7th month of Ka'ei 4 (1851), having come of age, he was granted his late father's inherited stipend of thirteen-person stipend without change, and appointed as personal attendant trainee and duty officer
- On the 18th day of the 4th month of Ka'ei 5 (1852), appointed to upper residence household management and granted the Yamashita Ino-san successor's residence
- On the 13th day of the 6th month of the same year, appointed to personal attendant service and granted the standard official stipend
- On the 8th day of the 8th month of Ansei 2 (1855), granted messenger rank and appointed to concurrently serve as temporary meal attendant to the retired lord, personal attendant inspector, small treasury clothing department, and medicine department
- On the 8th day of the 4th month of Ansei 3 (1856), appointed as duty officer due to staffing adjustments, with further duty assignments to be appointed later. Messenger duties remain as before
- On the 23rd day of the 7th month of the same year, due to negligence in duties and

immediate forgetfulness of the great benevolence of Kantoku-in, ordered into retirement. Though without precedent, through special consideration, arrangements were made for a suitable person to be selected and adopted by his sister. Until that time, due to the large household, through special consideration, assistance of three-person stipend was granted
- On the 10th day of the 9th month of the same year, family headship was transferred to adopted son Tetsutarō
- On the 14th day of the 11th month of the same year, absconded. Has one older sister and one younger sister

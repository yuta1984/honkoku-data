Former Lord Honda Hyōbu-shōyū-sama　Kawanishi Sajiemon

During the reign of Muchaku-in-sama:
1. In the 10th month of Jōkyō 2, year of the Metal Ox (1685), he was summoned to service and granted a stipend of fifteen person-portions.

1. In the 10th month of Jōkyō 3, year of the Fire Tiger (1686), he was granted an increase of five person-portions to his stipend.

1. In the 10th month of Genroku 2, year of the Earth Snake (1689), he was appointed to the position of messenger and his stipend was directly increased to 150 koku.

During the reign of Shingo-in-sama:
1. On the 28th day of the 7th month of Genroku 7, year of the Wood Dog (1694), he was appointed to the position of deputy (caretaker official) and relocated to Edo.

1. On the 28th day of the 12th month of the same year, he was granted a position allowance of thirty koku.

1. In the intercalary 2nd month of Genroku 10, year of the Fire Ox (1697), he was henceforth appointed to the position of assistant commander of the musket unit.
　　　However, from this time forward, his main role as deputy was to work alongside the commanders, and he was incorporated into the advance guard commanders with seating order determined by precedence.

1. On the 23rd day of the 6th month of Genroku 11, year of the Earth Tiger (1698), he was granted an increase of twenty koku to his stipend.

1. On the 10th day of the 8th month of Hōreki 11, year of the Snake (1761), he was appointed as messenger and served concurrently as financial department arbitration officer.
1. On the 19th day of the 11th month of the same year, he was appointed to personal attendance duty, and together with financial department arbitration, he was to focus primarily on arbitration duties as requested, becoming senior personal attendant inspector.
1. On the 5th day of the 7th month of Meiwa 2, year of the Rooster (1765), he was appointed as apprentice chief accounting magistrate, while his personal attendance, arbitration, and financial magistrate duties continued as before.
1. On the 26th day of the 3rd month of Meiwa 5, year of the Rat (1768), he was appointed to the rank of senior inspector, granted an increase of 20 koku making his total stipend 70 koku, with duties continuing as before.
1. On the 29th day of the 4th month of Meiwa 7, year of the Tiger (1770), he became chief accounting magistrate with concurrent arbitration duties, was relieved of personal attendance and financial magistrate duties, and granted an increase of 30 koku making his total stipend 100 koku.
1. On the 21st day of the 2nd month of An'ei 4, year of the Sheep (1775), he was appointed to the rank of chief retainer, with duties continuing as before.
1. In the same year, when his lord requested to be relieved of the role of court presenter, on the 1st day of the 11th month, he was relieved of arbitration duties.

During the reign of Zuiun'in-sama:
1. On the 1st day of the 3rd month of An'ei 5, year of the Monkey (1776), he was appointed to the full position of chief retainer and granted the standard supplementary stipend.

During the reign of Honryūin-sama:
1. On the 29th day of the 3rd month of An'ei 6, year of the Rooster (1777), he was appointed as personal chief retainer, served concurrently as public chief retainer, and was successfully relieved of chief accounting magistrate duties.
1. On the 23rd day of the 10th month of An'ei 8, year of the Boar (1779), by special consideration he was relieved of his duties, appointed as senior inspector rank intermediary, and relocated to the lower residence.
1. On the 18th day of the 9th month of An'ei 9, year of the Rat (1780), he died of illness.

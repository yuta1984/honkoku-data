　　　　　　　　Adopted Son
　　　　　　　　　Third son of Okamura Matauemon
　　　　　　　　　Nakamura Gunji
During the reign of Lord Genryūin
- On the 1st day of the intercalary 9th month of Meiwa 4, Year of the Fire Boar (1767), he was summoned as a foot soldier for escort duty and granted an allocation of 16 hyō with provisions for 2 people.

- On the 10th day of the 2nd month of the same 9th year, Year of the Water Dragon (1772), he was appointed as secretary to the chamberlain.

- On the 16th day of the 12th month of An'ei 3, Year of the Wood Horse (1774), due to illness, as per the petition of his adoptive and birth parents, he was granted permanent leave, separated from the Sōzō family, taken back by the Matauemon family, and later summoned anew.

　　　　　　　　Nakamura Sōzō
During the reign of Lord Yoriyasu
- On the 18th day of the 7th month of Bunsei 3, Year of the Dragon (1820), as per his late father's deathbed request, being still a minor, he was granted support provisions for 7 people; the matter of succession would be appropriately arranged upon his coming of age.

- On the 20th day of the 8th month of the same 10th year, Year of the Boar (1827), he had his first audience.

- On the 8th day of the 10th month of the same year, having come of age, he was appointed to succeed his late father Jinuemon, granted 35 hyō with provisions for 3 people, and assigned to guard duty according to stipend rank.

- On the 19th day of the 7th month of Tenpō 9, Year of the Dog (1838), he was appointed to serve at the side of the retired lord.

- On the 17th day of the 8th month of the same 11th year, Year of the Rat (1840), due to improper conduct, he was dismissed from service at the retired lord's side, ordered to return to regular rotation, and confined to his residence.

Nakamura Sozō
Younger brother of Nakamura Magoshichi

During the reign of Lord Shinnyoin
- On the 15th day of the 1st month of Kyōhō 17, Year of the Water Rat (1732), he was summoned to serve as an ashigaru foot soldier in the lord's escort unit and granted a stipend of 4 ryō and provisions for 2 people.

During the reign of Lord Genryūin
- On the 15th day of the 3rd month of the same 21st year, Year of the Fire Dragon (1736), he was appointed to the position of secretary to the chief retainer (goyōnin shoyaku).
- On the 26th day of the 6th month of Kanpō 1, Year of the Metal Cock (1741), he was appointed as secretary to the senior councilors (karō-domo shoyaku).
- On the 18th day of the 1st month of the same 2nd year, Year of the Water Dog (1742), he was granted the rank of guard in the lord's escort unit (kakushiki gotomoban).
- On the 26th day of the 12th month of the same 3rd year, Year of the Water Boar (1743), he received an increase of 2 hyō, raising his stipend to 20 hyō.
- On the 1st day of the 5th month of Kan'en 2, Year of the Earth Snake (1749), he concurrently served as temporary purchasing agent (okaikata kariyaku).
- On the 28th day of the 4th month of the same 4th year, Year of the Metal Sheep (1751), he was appointed as secretary (oyuuhitsu) and granted the rank of middle page (chūkoshō), with an increase of 6 hyō and provisions for 1 person, bringing his total to 26 hyō and provisions for 3 people.
- On the 9th day of the 3rd month of Hōreki 11, Year of the Metal Snake (1761), he received an increase of provisions for 1 person, bringing the total to provisions for 4 people.

(Written in red ink) Nakamura

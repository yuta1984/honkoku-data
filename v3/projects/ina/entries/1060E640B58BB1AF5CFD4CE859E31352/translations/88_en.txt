He was appointed to undertake various expenses and duties related to the young lord's kitchen quarters and rest areas.

- Meiwa 4, Year of the Fire Boar (1767), 12th month, 15th day: Promoted to the rank of stipend recipient (kyūjin) with an increase of 5 hyō, bringing his total to 40 hyō with support for three people. His duties remained the same as before, but he was relieved of his role as inspector of middle pages.

- An'ei 2, Year of the Water Snake (1773), 5th month, 15th day: Appointed as construction magistrate (fushин bugyō)

- Same 4th year, Year of the Wood Sheep (1775), 5th month, 5th day: Appointed concurrently as arms magistrate (bugu bugyō)

During the reign of Zuiun-in-sama
- Same 5th year, Year of the Fire Monkey (1776), 3rd month, 1st day: Received an increase of 10 hyō, bringing his total to 50 hyō

During the reign of Honryū-in-sama  
- Same 6th year, Year of the Fire Rooster (1777), 3rd month, 29th day: Appointed concurrently as storehouse magistrate (kura bugyō)

- Same year, 10th month: Died of illness
　　End of service

Koizumi Watōji
During the reign of Genryū-in-sama
- Meiwa 6, Year of the Earth Ox (1769), 6th month, 14th day: First audience with the lord

- Same 9th year, Year of the Water Dragon (1772), 7th month, 15th day: Summoned as middle page in personal attendance, granted an allocation of 26 hyō with support for three people

During the reign of Honryū-in-sama
- An'ei 6, Year of the Fire Rooster (1777), 11th month, 21st day: Inherited his deceased father's position without change, receiving 50 hyō with support for three people, appointed to guard duties

- Same 7th year, Year of the Earth Dog (1778), 9th month, 11th day: Appointed to personal attendant service

- Tenmei 5, Year of the Wood Snake (1785), 10th month, 23rd day: Appointed as gold magistrate and small implements officer

- Same 8th year, Year of the Earth Monkey (1788), 6th month, 24th day: Appointed as temporary meal service officer

- Kansei 1, Year of the Earth Rooster (1789), 2nd month, 15th day: Appointed concurrently as personal attendant inspector and small treasury officer; relieved of temporary meal service duties

- Same 2nd year, Year of the Dog (1790), 7th month, 23rd day: While Watōji was accompanying the lord to Osaka, he was wounded by a deranged person on the night of the 19th at Kanaya station and committed suicide. Until the official judgment, his entire household was placed under the care of Koizumi Uzaemon.

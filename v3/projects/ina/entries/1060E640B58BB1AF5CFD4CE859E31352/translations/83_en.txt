Tanaka Hasuke
　Original name: Yahōzaburō

During the reign of Lord Yorimoto
- On November 28th of Kyōwa 1, year of Tori (1801), he was appointed as apprentice assistant clerk
- On March 13th of Bunka 1, year of Ne (1804), he was appointed to permanent clerk position and received rations for 2 people
- On January 15th of Bunka 6, year of Mi (1809), since his father Jōemon was promoted to stipend-receiving retainer status, he was notified to attend the second rank of escort guard class
- On April 23rd of Bunka 8, year of Hitsuji (1811), he inherited his deceased father Jōemon's position without change, receiving 30 hyō and rations for 3 people, and was formally appointed to serve in the middle page escort department
- On July 20th of the same year, he was appointed to the treasury department
- On November 5th of Bunka 10, year of Tori (1813), he was appointed middle page inspector
- On January 15th of Bunka 15, year of Tora (1818), he was promoted to stipend-receiving retainer with an increase of 3 hyō, and temporarily oversaw middle page inspector duties
- On May 2nd of the same year, he died of illness

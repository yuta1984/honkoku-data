Sanpei Moriemon

During the reign of Lord Shinnyoin
1. On the 19th day of the 7th month of Kyōhō 11, year of the Fire Horse (1726), he was appointed to the treasury office (okura-kata), granted an increase of 1 ryō and 1 person's stipend, with a total stipend of 7 ryō and 3 persons' portions.
1. On the 1st day of the 9th month of Kyōhō 18, year of the Water Ox (1733), he received succession rights as requested by his father Kurōemon upon retirement.
1. On the 22nd day of the 4th month of Kyōhō 19, year of the Wood Tiger (1734), he was appointed as assistant page inspector (chū-koshō metsuke), with an additional 2 ryō, making his total stipend 9 ryō and 3 persons' portions.

During the reign of Lord Genryūin
1. On the 9th day of the 5th month of Genbun 1, year of the Fire Dragon (1736), he was appointed to concurrently serve in the investigation department (gimmi-kata).
1. On the 28th day of the 2nd month of Genbun 3, year of the Earth Horse (1738), he was granted 40 hyō of rice.
1. On the 15th day of the 1st month of Genbun 6, year of the Metal Rooster (1741), he was promoted to retainer status (kyūjin) and appointed as financial magistrate (kin-bugyō).
1. On the 10th day of the 2nd month of the same year, he entered guard duty.
1. 6th month of Enkyō 2, year of the Wood Ox (1745): Died of illness

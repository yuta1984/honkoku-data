During the reign of Lord Genryūin

1. In the 2nd month of An'ei 5, hinoe-saru (1776), granted an increase of 5 hyō for a total of 38 hyō

During the reign of Lord Honryūin
1. On the 23rd day of the 10th month of Tenmei 5, kinoto-mi (1785), promoted to kyūjin (stipend retainer) status, granted 40 hyō and provisions for 3 people

During the reign of Lord Yorimoto
1. On the 15th day of the 5th month of Kansei 8, tatsu (1796), granted retirement as requested, family headship transferred to his son Sūho without change

During the reign of Lord Yorimoto
1. On the 18th day of the 7th month of Kansei 4, ne (1792), appointed to hired service (oyatoi-tsutome)
1. On the 15th day of the 6th month of the same 6th year, tora (1794), granted financial assistance of provisions for 3 people
1. On the 15th day of the 5th month of the same 8th year, tatsu (1796), granted retirement as his father Sōkan had requested, inherited family headship without change, granted 40 hyō and provisions for 3 people

During the reign of Lord Yoriyasu
1. On the 8th day of the 10th month of Bunsei 10, i (1827), regarding the desire to inherit as a secular family, though this intention has been expressed for some time, it cannot be granted. However, considering the circumstances when grandfather Yōkei was appointed to medical service, and furthermore that you have faithfully performed guard duties and other services over the years without negligence, the matter of family succession shall be granted as a secular family. With this understanding, you should consider adopting an heir
1. On the 25th day of the 2nd month of Tenpō 7, saru (1836), due to old age and illness, granted retirement as requested, family headship transferred to son Kanji without change
End of service record

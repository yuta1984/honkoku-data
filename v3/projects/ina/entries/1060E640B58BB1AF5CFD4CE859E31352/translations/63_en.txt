Actual nephew, younger brother of Seda Magodayū
Adopted son
Kawanishi Kisaburō
Later called Sajiemon

During the reign of Genryū-in-sama:
1. On the 11th day of the 10th month of Enkyō 4, year of the Fire Rabbit (1747), he was granted adoption as requested.

1. On the 17th day of the 7th month of Enkyō 5, year of the Earth Dragon (1748), he had his first audience with the lord.

1. On the 15th day of the 1st month of Kan'en 2, year of the Earth Snake (1749), he was summoned as a page in the lord's retinue and granted a stipend of 26 hyō and provisions for three people.

1. On the 7th day of the 8th month of Hōreki 2, year of the Water Monkey (1752), he succeeded his late adoptive father Kobei's household without change, was granted a stipend of 170 koku, appointed to guard duty, and entered guard service on the 28th of the same month.

1. On the 23rd day of the 2nd month of Hōreki 5, year of the Wood Boar (1755), he was assigned as assistant to the head of foot soldiers and ordered to also serve as additional guard. On the 7th day of the 6th month of the following year of the Rat, he was appointed as additional guard.

1. On the 5th day of the 4th month of Hōreki 12, year of the Water Horse (1762), he was appointed as head of the young lord's retinue.

1. On the 27th day of the 6th month of the same year, he was ordered to serve alone with full responsibility.

1. On the 2nd day of the 5th month of Hōreki 14, year of the Wood Monkey (1764), he was appointed to concurrently serve as head of the young lord's personal retinue.

1. On the 5th day of the 3rd month of Meiwa 9, year of the Water Dragon (1772), due to Lord Suruga-no-kami's death the previous year, he was relieved of personal attendance duties.

1. On the 11th day of the 9th month of the same year, he was appointed as head of foot soldiers.

1. On the 21st day of the 8th month of An'ei 4, year of the Wood Sheep (1775), due to matters concerning his elder brother Seda Magodayū's flight, he was granted permanent leave and his family line was discontinued.

During the reign of Honryū-in-sama:
1. On the 11th day of the 3rd month of Kansei 1, year of the Rooster (1789), he was ordered to return to service, granted a stipend of provisions for 15 people, and appointed to guard duty according to his stipend rank.

1. On the 13th day of the 2nd month of Kansei 2, year of the Dog (1790), he was ordered to relocate to the lord's domain residence.

During the reign of Lord Yoriyuki:
1. In the 5th month of Kansei 8, year of the Dragon (1796), he died of illness. His son Tokunosuke was granted assistance provisions.

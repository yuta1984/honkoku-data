1. On the 25th day of the 9th month of Kōka 4, Sheep (1847), due to administrative reorganization, he was smoothly relieved of his secretarial duties and temporarily appointed to attend the young lords' palace, continuing his duties as inspector of middle pages as before.
1. On the 15th day of the 11th month of the same year, he was granted money for compiling the family genealogy.
1. On the 10th day of the 7th month of Kaei 5, Water-Rat (1852), due to administrative reorganization, he was smoothly relieved of his duties attending the young lords' residence, continuing as inspector of middle pages as before.
1. On the 8th day of the 6th month of Kaei 7, Wood-Tiger (1854), he was granted the rank equivalent to stipend holder, appointed as assistant financial magistrate, continuing as inspector of middle pages as before, with an increase of 4 hyō bringing his total to 30 hyō with support for three retainers.
1. On the 2nd day of the 3rd month of Ansei 3, Fire-Dragon (1856), the night before last while staying overnight in the great hall at the financial office, he lost a money box entrusted to him. Although he was not assigned to night watch duty, he had been specifically ordered to stay overnight, making this incident pointless and representing extremely poor supervision and negligent conduct. Therefore, he should have received severe punishment, but due to the timing during an important mourning period, he was mercifully sentenced to house confinement.
1. On the 24th day of the same month, he was released from house confinement.
1. On the 15th day of the 1st month of Ansei 4, Fire-Snake (1857), he was promoted to stipend holder rank with seating order according to guard unit stipend level, and appointed to concurrently serve as acting warehouse magistrate and financial magistrate.
1. On the 15th day of the 8th month of Ansei 5, Earth-Horse (1858), he died of illness.

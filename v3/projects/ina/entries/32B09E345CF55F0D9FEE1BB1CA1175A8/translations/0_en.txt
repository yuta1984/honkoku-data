Memorandum

Yesterday on the 25th, at the half of the hour of the Monkey (around 3 PM), His Majesty proceeded from Sento Palace to the Imperial Palace, and returned in excellent spirits at the beginning of the hour of the Dog (around 8 PM). We inform you of this for your knowledge. The above.

Chigusa, Former Middle Counselor
December 26th
Yanagiwara, Former Grand Counselor

To Lord Naito Yamato-no-kami

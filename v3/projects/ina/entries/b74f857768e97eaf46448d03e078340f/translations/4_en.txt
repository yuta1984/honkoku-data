A written statement humbly submitted
Regarding the Takeda grave monument within the territory of Iwakubo Village in Kai Province, it stands five shaku and six sun tall on a three-tiered stone base. [The subject] died of illness on April 12th of Tenshō 1 (1573), was temporarily interred, then on April 12th of Tenshō 3 (1575) was cremated at this location, and the divided bones were buried.

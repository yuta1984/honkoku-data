　　　　Article 10
When requesting that consolidated land certificates be issued as a single document, certificate stamp tax shall be paid at the rate specified in Article 8 based on the price for each individual plot.
　　　　Article 11
For all land held by the people, should official use be required in the future for any reason, purchase will be made at the price recorded on the land certificate, therefore appropriate land prices must be recorded.
　　However, when conducting land transactions, the price recorded on the land certificate

　　　　Article 12
Land additions and clearings to existing properties, trial cultivation on tax-exempt land, construction of buildings on abandoned or neglected land, development of forests and thickets, and other hidden fields - all such activities are permitted this time only, so they must be reported truthfully.

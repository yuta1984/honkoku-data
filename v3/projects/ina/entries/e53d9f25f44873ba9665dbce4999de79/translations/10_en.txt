Generally, when adjudicating lawsuits concerning movable and immovable property transactions, when one party among both plaintiff and defendant loses the case, a payment deadline shall be ordered, and if payment is still not completed thereafter, execution shall be carried out to the extent of the debtor's assets.
　　Jinshi (1872), September　　Ministry of Justice

Previously, regarding litigation expenses, both plaintiffs and defendants bore their own costs or the costs were borne by their villages or towns, with only witnesses

Regarding the issuance of land certificates: When submitting applications for land certificates, investigations should be conducted with sincerity and the prescribed format that has been communicated should be followed.
　　　Article 2
When wishing to divide and sell a single plot of land, the total area in bu should be re-examined on site, cross-referenced with the land survey records, and the division should be carried out equally by the village headman and assistant village headman to ensure there is no bias in the division method before submitting the application.

　　　Article 3
The aforementioned land certificates serve as proof of land ownership and should be kept with great care. In the event that land certificates are lost due to fire, theft, or other disasters, an application for replacement should be submitted with two or more witnesses and the joint seal of the village headman and assistant village headman.

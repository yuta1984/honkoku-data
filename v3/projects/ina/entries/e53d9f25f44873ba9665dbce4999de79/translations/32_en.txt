　9th month of Mizunoe-Saru (1872)
As perpetual buying and selling of land has been officially permitted, and land certificates will henceforth be issued for sales and transfers, when conducting mortgage transactions (shichii-kakiire), the following articles must be observed and improper handling must be avoided.
　Article 1

【Left Page】
1. It shall be permitted to mortgage land (shichii-kakiire) and borrow money for financial accommodation. In such cases, the land certificate shall be given to the creditor as security, and a receipt certificate shall be given to and kept by the landowner.
　Article 2
1. When mortgaging land, whether the land remains with the landowner or the land certificate is given to the creditor shall be left to mutual consultation between the parties.

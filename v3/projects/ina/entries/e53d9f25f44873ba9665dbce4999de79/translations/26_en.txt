　　　　　Article 8
When applying for land certificates, certificate stamp tax shall be paid as follows:
　　Certificate Stamp Tax
　　　Amount　Under 100 yen　《marginal note: 5 per thousand｜that is, 5 sen per 10 yen》
　　　《marginal note: However, even for property valued at 10 yen or less, 5 sen per certificate document}}
　　《marginal note: Same》Same　　《marginal note: 100 yen or more｜up to 200 yen》　　　50 sen
　　《marginal note: Same》Same　　《marginal note: 200 yen or more｜up to 500 yen》　　1 yen
　　《marginal note: Same》Same　　《marginal note: 500 yen or more｜up to 1,000 yen》　　1 yen 25 sen

　　《marginal note: Same》Same　　《marginal note: 1,000 yen or more｜up to 2,000 yen》　　　1 yen 50 sen
　　《marginal note: Same》Same　　《marginal note: 2,000 yen or more｜up to 5,000 yen》　　 2 yen 50 sen
　　《marginal note: Same》Same　　《marginal note: 5,000 yen or more》　　3 yen 75 sen
　　　　　Article 9
Henceforth, those who conduct secret sales without applying for land certificates shall have both the land and the payment confiscated.
　　However, village heads and deputy village heads who co-sign such transactions shall be fined one-third of the land price.

From now on, Buddhist monks shall adopt surnames, and those serving as head priests shall be called "So-and-so Temple Priest [Name]."
　　However, when adopting a surname, it must be reported to the supervising authority.
　Jinshin 9th year, 9th month, 14th day　　Daijōkan (Grand Council of State)

As decreed above, after adopting surnames and receiving the endorsement seal of the head and deputy (head), compile them by district and submit by the 20th day of the 10th month.

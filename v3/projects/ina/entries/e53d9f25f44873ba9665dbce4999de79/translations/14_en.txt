Whereas previously number plates were hung at each household,
these shall be removed and replaced with new ones written according to the template shown below,
to be hung in easily visible places such as gates and doorways.
Of course, when the head of household changes due to retirement or death,
they should be rewritten each time. This matter is hereby communicated.

【Left Page】
Regarding money and grain lending between commoners that occurred before
the last day of the twelfth month of Keiō 3 (1867), these generally
shall not be subject to judicial proceedings, but matters from
New Year's Day of Meiji 1 (1868) onward shall be subject to judicial proceedings.
　Mizunoe-saru year, 10th month, 22nd day　　Council of State
As this has been announced as above, all should take note of it.

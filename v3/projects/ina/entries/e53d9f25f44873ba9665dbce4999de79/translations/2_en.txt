【Left page】
Regarding the people's personal seals, these are important items that serve as evidence in all matters. However, since there are those who carelessly entrust them to others, fraudulent lawsuits occasionally arise, which is extremely problematic. Henceforth, entrusting personal seals to others is strictly prohibited. Therefore, should anyone violate this understanding, both the person who entrusts the seal and the person who receives it will face severe punishment.

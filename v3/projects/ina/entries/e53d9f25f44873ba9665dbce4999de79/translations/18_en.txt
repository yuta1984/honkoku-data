【Right page】
　All attached Buddhist ritual implements and temple treasures shall be
　recorded in ledgers, and after receiving the endorsement seals of lay representative parishioners and fellow priests,
　they must be kept on file. Should there be any falsification or irregularities,
　strict measures will be taken.
　Additionally, temple-affiliated rice fields and farmland are naturally to be understood as
　belonging to the temple properties.
Hossō sect, Kegon sect, Ritsu sect, Kengaku sect,
Yūzū Nenbutsu sect - these five sects and their various branches, as well as others

【Left page】
Among the various sects, those with independent branch head temples and temples without main temples
shall all be placed under the jurisdiction of their respective appropriate main temples within their sect.
Each prefecture should understand this directive,
communicate it to temples within their jurisdiction, compile petitions, and
submit inquiries regarding jurisdictional matters to the Ministry of Religious Education.
　　Jinshin 9th year, 9th month　　Daijōkan (Grand Council of State)

12th month of Mi-Hitsuji year (1787)
1 item: 25 ryō of gold

The above allowance money, in the 12th month of Hitsuji year,
at the government office 《marginal note: On the 10th day of the 1st month of Saru year, through the intermediary of Lord Izawa Gyōsuke | Yozaemon went up and received it, then returned》
was given and we received it. The receipt document
was recorded in the official ledger, and with Yozaemon's seal
it was submitted. Upon return,
it was given on the evening of the 10th day of the 1st month of Saru year, so on the following day,
the 11th day, it was distributed to the town representatives.

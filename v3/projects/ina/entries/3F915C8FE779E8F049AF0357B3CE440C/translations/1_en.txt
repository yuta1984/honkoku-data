General Duties of Village Headmen (Nanushi) and Village Elders (Toshiyori)

Article 1: Matters Concerning Religious Registry (Shūmon-chō)
　　　These are matters related to what is now the family register system, and the 
　　　household head possessed authority equivalent to that of present-day judges
　　　in these matters

Article 2: Matters Concerning Rice Yield Assessment (Takaire) and Land
　　　These are matters related to what is now land registration. The additions and 
　　　deletions must be made at the beginning of each year. All land transactions 
　　　require the endorsement of the village headman. Land surveys and 
　　　determination of village boundaries must be conducted with the village 
　　　headman in attendance

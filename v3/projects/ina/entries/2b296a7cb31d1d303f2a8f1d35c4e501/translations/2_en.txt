Regulations for the Association

Article 1: Regarding the current cotton thread machinery project based on Mr. Ito's ideas, association members shall discuss matters openly without reservation and proceed accordingly.

Article 2: Regarding the manufacturing and sales of machinery, even if there are direct requests to Mr. Ito's establishment, all members must decline and only proceed with manufacturing and sales after consultation among association members with their consent.

Article 3: When selling completed machinery, a profit margin of 1.5 percent (one and a half percent) shall be received.

Article 4: Even when selling to relatives or close acquaintances, prices shall not be reduced under any circumstances.

Article 5: Regarding increasing machinery within the association, this shall be done as convenient for members. However, lending to other households is strictly prohibited.

Article 6: Should circumstances naturally require cessation of operations, even if there are those who desire to take over and offer to contribute money, this shall never be permitted. The association members shall take responsibility internally.

Article 7: All expenses and various costs related to Mr. Ito's craftsmanship shall be shared proportionally among association members.

Having established these regulations through discussion among association members as stated above, they shall be strictly observed. Should any member violate these articles, they shall be expelled from the association. These regulations are established for future reference.

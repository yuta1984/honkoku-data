Yamashita Teishirō (fourth son of Yamashita Teizō)

During the reign of Lord Yoriyasu
- On the 24th day of the 6th month of Kaei 3 (1850), year of the Metal Dog, he was appointed to serve temporarily as an ashigaru (foot soldier) in employment. He was granted stipend assistance of one and a half person's rations and assigned to the clerical office. He was also temporarily appointed to messenger duties.

(Written in red ink: "Yamashita")

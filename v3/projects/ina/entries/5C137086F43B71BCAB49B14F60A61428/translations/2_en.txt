　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Younger brother of Makabe Yokuro
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Makabe Yoichiro
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Originally named Toyokichi

During the reign of Lord Yoriyasu
1. On the 3rd day of the 5th month of Tenpo 2 (Year of the Rabbit), when his elder brother Yokuro absconded,
   he continued diligently in his horseback riding training and achieved proficiency. Due to this,
   he was newly summoned with special benevolence, given the rank of ashigaru foot soldier,
   with a stipend of 18 hyō and provisions for 2 people, assigned to mounted service and placed under the supervision of the local financial administrator.
1. On the 8th day of the 5th month of Tenpo 5 (Year of the Horse), he performed duty during a fire in the town quarters,
   for which he was granted money.
1. On the 17th day of the 12th month of Tenpo 12 (Year of the Ox), suspicious
   persons were reported to have entered the domain during the summer. He was ordered to patrol the vicinity for investigation,
   for which he was granted soup and sake.

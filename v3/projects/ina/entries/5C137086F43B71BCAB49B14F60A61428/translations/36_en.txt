Ikegami Sanemon

During the reign of Lord Yorimoto
- Bunka 1 (1804), 9th month, 23rd day: While still young, he had requested Seishiro to serve as his substitute for document duty (kirugami bangashira), but when the term limit was reached, he was ordered to transfer the substitute position. He was granted a stipend of 5 koku and provisions for 2 people, and was appointed to the advance guard unit (osenkite-gumi).

- Same 12th year (1815), 8th month, 28th day: Transferred to the rural group (go-gumi)

During the reign of Lord Yoriyasu
- Bunpolitsu 8 (1825), 12th month, 28th day: Appointed to local administration [unclear character] role, granted 3 to of role rice

- Same 12th year (1829), 3rd month, 18th day: Appointed to manage firewood, mountains, forests, and bamboo groves; local administration role continued as before

- Tempo 2 (1831), 11th month, 8th day: 1 koku was confiscated from his stipend due to some issue, placed under house arrest, and appointed to the advance guard unit

- Same 4th year (1833), 7th month, 22nd day: Appointed as inspector (metsuke) of Sato Seitayu's group in the rural unit, with an additional rice allowance of 1 koku 6 to

- Same 5th year (1834), 6th month, 28th day: Temporarily appointed to concurrently serve as assistant in the products department

- Same year, 7th month, 13th day: Appointed as assistant group leader (kumi kogashira nami)

- Same year, 12th month, 24th day: 3 to of role rice for the products department assistant position

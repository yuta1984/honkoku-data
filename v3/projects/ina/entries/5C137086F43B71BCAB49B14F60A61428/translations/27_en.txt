Advance Guard Unit Leader (Osente-gumi Kogashira) 
Nagasaka Motoemon

Nagasaka Banemon
(Originally named Motoemon)
- Tenmei 2, Year of the Water Tiger (1782)
- Kansei 3, Year of the Metal Boar (1791), 10th month, 14th day: Appointed to Oku-go-jōguchi-ban (Inner Palace Gate Guard)
- Same 8th year, Year of the Fire Dragon (1796), 12th month, 5th day: Appointed as metsuke (inspector) of Tegami Tarōzaemon's unit
　Granted additional rice allowance for three years
- Same 12th year, Year of the Metal Monkey (1800), 5th month, 4th day: Promoted to unit leader (kogashira), granted additional rice allowance of 5 koku 9 to for six years
"(In red ink) Nagasaka"

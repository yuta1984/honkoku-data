Misawa Genemon

During the reign of Lord Yorimoto:
• Kansei 3 (Year of the Boar), 5th month, 10th day: Appointed as district group inspector (gōgumi metsuke)
• Kansei 8 (Year of the Dragon), 2nd month, 20th day: Appointed as district group head, granted additional rice stipend of 5 koku 9 to 3 to, and provisions for two retainers
• Bunka 2 (Year of the Ox), 1st month, 15th day: Exempted from scribe ranking duties with high stipend
• Bunka 5 (Year of the Dragon), 7th month, 8th day: Assigned to oversee firewood procurement duties
• Bunka 6 (Year of the Snake), 11th month, 10th day: Appointed as inspector of mountain forests and bamboo groves
• Bunka 7 (Year of the Horse), 4th month, 15th day: Promoted to formal foot soldier rank, granted stipend of 18 hyō and provisions for two retainers, serving in that capacity
• Bunka 11 (Year of the Dog), 9th month, 8th day: Appointed to the great storehouse clothing department
• Bunpō 2 (Year of the Rabbit), 6th month, 13th day: Successfully discharged from both great storehouse clothing department duties, appointed to guard rotation duty

During the reign of Lord Yoriyasu:
• Bunpō 8 (Year of the Rooster), 9th month, 9th day: Appointed to duty at the lord's rest quarters during castle residence
• Bunpō 13 (Year of the Tiger), 4th month, 16th day: Due to old age and illness, granted retirement as requested, family headship transferred to his son Yozō
"(In red ink) Misawa Gen"

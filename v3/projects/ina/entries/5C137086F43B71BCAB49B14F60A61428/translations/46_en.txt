Koike Tahei

During the reign of Lord Yorimoto
• Bunka 9, Year of the Monkey (1812), 15th day of the 1st month: Appointed as a fixed employee of the rural group (gōgumi), granted one person's stipend
• Same 10th year, Year of the Rooster (1813), 28th day of the 9th month: As a replacement for his father Jinzaemon, granted a stipend of 5 koku and two persons' allowance, continued as a member of the rural group
• Bunpō 3, Year of the Dragon (1820), 15th day of the 1st month: Appointed to the local administrative clerk position, granted three years of service allowance

During the reign of Lord Yoriyasu
• Same 4th year, Year of the Snake (1821), 3rd day of the 12th month: Appointed as chief clerk, granted three years of additional rice allowance
• Same 11th year, Year of the Rat (1828), 20th day of the 7th month: Granted three to of additional rice allowance
• Tenpō 4, Year of the Snake (1833), 15th day of the 1st month: Due to adjustment of the three to rice allowance, appointed to Edo service
• Same 7th year, Year of the Monkey (1836), 20th day of the 7th month: Promoted to the rank of ashigaru foot soldier, granted hyō number, received 17 hyō and two persons' stipend. Appointed as assistant in attendance duties, chief clerk position continued under the supervision of the chamberlain as before
• Same 8th year, Year of the Rooster (1837), 25th day of the 5th month: Became apprentice in the disbursement section and lower official in the construction department

(In red ink) Koike

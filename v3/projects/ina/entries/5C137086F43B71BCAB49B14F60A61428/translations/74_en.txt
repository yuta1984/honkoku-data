Ueda Bansuke
(Note: Brother of Kyūbei and possibly father of Kyūzaemon)

During the reign of Lord Genryūin
- Genbun 2 (1737), Year of the Snake, 2nd month, 28th day: Transferred from Okabe Jūrozaemon's group to河野 Kōno Asaemon's group as metsuke (inspector) (stationed in Edo)
- Hōreki 12 (1762), Year of the Horse, 4th month, 13th day: Died of illness

Ueda Kyūzaemon
During the reign of Lord Genryūin
- Meiwa 8 (1771), Year of the Rabbit, 6th month, 25th day: Appointed as shita-metsuke (lower inspector) from Takeda Shichirōemon's magistrate group; granted additional rice stipend of 6 to, total stipend of 5 koku 6 to
- Same year 9 (1772), Year of the Dragon, 2nd month, 23rd day: Ordered to change residences; granted temporary long house south of Kitamura Kotani Yūzaemon

Ueda Bansuke (Kyūzaemon's younger brother, Edo)
- Meiwa 5 (1768), Year of the Rat, 12th month, 20th day: Appointed with additional rice stipend of 6 to, total stipend of 5 koku and provisions for 2 people; under the supervision of the ōmetsuke (chief inspector) at the upper residence

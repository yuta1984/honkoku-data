Adopted Son
Actually the second son of Yoneyama Kanuemon
Kitahara Bungouemon
Original name: Tashichi

1. On the 15th day of the 12th month of Bunka 14, Hinoto-ushi (1817), as his adoptive father Gensuke was granted retirement as requested, he inherited the family headship with a stipend of 18 hyō and 2 persons' rations, and was appointed as attendant foot soldier (gokūkata go-ashigaru).

Reign of Lord Yorinobu

1. On the 2nd day of the 11th month of Bunsei 8, Kinotono-tori (1825), he was ordered into house confinement (hissoku) for holding a drinking and dining gathering.

1. On the 3rd day of the 12th month of the same year, he was released from house confinement.

1. On the 15th day of the 1st month of Tenpō 2, Kanotono-u (1831), he was granted the rank of gokūban-kaku, appointed as magistrate (go-daikan), received an increase of 2 hyō, and came under the supervision of the district administrator (gunkai).

1. On the 8th day of the 5th month of the same 5th year, Kinoe-uma (1834), due to misconduct during a town fire incident, he was ordered into house confinement.

1. On the 8th day of the 6th month of the same year, he was released from house confinement.

1. On the 13th day of the 10th month of the same year, he was relieved of his position as magistrate, henceforth appointed to alternate duty (go-jiban), and placed under the supervision of the chamberlains (go-yōnin).

1. On the 7th day of the 9th month of the same 6th year, Kinotono-hitsuji (1835), he died of illness.

Adopted Son
Actually the second son of Takahashi Sakunojō
Kitahara Masutarō

Reign of Lord Yorinobu

1. On the 15th day of the 12th month of Tenpō 6, Kinotono-hitsuji (1835), he was appointed as adopted son of Kitahara Bungouemon, inherited the position with a stipend of 18 hyō and 2 persons' rations, and was appointed as ranked foot soldier attendant (kakushiki go-ashigaru gokūkata). Henceforth under the supervision of the foot soldier commanders.

1. On the 28th day of the 8th month of the same 11th year, Kanoe-ne (1840), he was appointed as secretary among the senior councilors and chamberlains, under chamberlain supervision.

1. On the 20th day of the 11th month of Kōka 2, Tsuchinoto-mi (1845), he was additionally appointed to the clothing department of the great treasury, while continuing his secretarial duties as before.

1. On the 4th day of the 11th month of Kaei 2, Tsuchinoto-tori (1849), he was ordered to return to Edo, with official matters to be reported at Edo. On the 15th day of the same month, as two prisoners were being sent to the domain headquarters, he was appointed as their escort.

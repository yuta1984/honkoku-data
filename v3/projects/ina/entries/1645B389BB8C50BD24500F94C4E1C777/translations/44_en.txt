Ohira Saheta
Reign of Lord Raiin
- 3rd year of Kansei, year of the boar, 3rd month, 20th day: Promoted to ashigaru foot soldier, granted a stipend of 18 hyō and provisions for two people, appointed to the guard rotation
- 5th year of the same era, year of the ox: Died of illness, family line discontinued due to lack of heir

Ohira Magodayū
First generation
- 11th year of Bunka, year of the dog, 7th month, 20th day: When the Ohira Saheta line was discontinued, there were circumstances that required deliberation, but through special consideration and according to Yaemon's petition, the Saheta family name was restored. Although he was Yaemon's son, due to the main family matter, he was granted a stipend of 3 koku 5 to and provisions for two people, and appointed as a monk apprentice
- 14th year of the same era, year of the ox, 11th month, 28th day: Appointed to full monk duties
- 3rd year of Bunsei, year of the dragon, 3rd month, 18th day: Appointed as head clerk and placed under head administrator supervision

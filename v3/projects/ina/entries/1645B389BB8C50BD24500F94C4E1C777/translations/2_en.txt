Kitahara Rokurojji
During the reign of Lord Genryuin
- On the 17th day of the 8th month of Anei 8 (1779), year of the boar, granted the rank of ashigaru with seven-person stipend
  To serve in the county magistrate's administration headquarters as directed

During the reign of Lord Honryuin  
- On the 5th day of the 7th month of Tenmei 3 (1783), year of the rabbit, for diligent service in financial affairs
  This gradually reached His Lordship's attention, and accordingly was granted a summer robe, and given
  proper seating order among the ashigaru
- On the 23rd day of the 3rd month of Kansei 2 (1790), year of the dog, granted an additional three-person stipend increase in rank
  Appointed to the lord's escort guard

During the reign of Lord Yorimoto
- On the 15th day of the 1st month of the same 8th year (1796), year of the dragon, granted ceremonial court dress
- On the 8th day of the 12th month of the same 10th year (1798), year of the horse, as requested, retirement was
  granted, succession was granted to his son Kinzaburo, for handling financial
  affairs for many years and diligently providing funds
  as special consideration for assistance, granted three-person stipend
　　　　Service record
- On the same 10th day, took the tonsure, changed name to Taien

Honda Yagozaemon
During the reign of Lord Yorimoto
- 15th day, 1st month, Kansei 12 (1800): Appointed to assistant head (kogashira-nami)
- 15th day, 1st month, Bunka 3 (1806): Document clerk assistant (monogaki-nami) duties to continue as before
- 20th day, 5th month, Bunka 6 (1809): Promoted to foot soldier (o-kachi), granted a stipend of 18 hyō and provisions for 2 people, appointed to guard duty rotation (o-tsugiban)
- 1st day, intercalary 1st month, Bunpō 5 (1822): Died of illness

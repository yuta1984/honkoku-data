During the reign of Lord Yorimoto
- Kansei 11 (1799), 1st month, 15th day: Appointed to bugukata-teiyakoi (regular employee of weapons department) with 1.5-person support allowance
- Kyōwa 1 (1801), 11th month, 15th day: Increased by half-person support allowance
- Bunka 3 (1806), 1st month, 15th day: Granted ishikiri rice stipend of 2 koku and 2-person support allowance; duties remain the same as before
- Bunka 14 (1817), 1st month, 15th day: Additional 1 koku rice stipend

During the reign of Lord Yoriyasu
- Bunsei 8 (1825), 10th month, 15th day: Following his father Sango's petition for retirement, inherited family headship; appointed as kachi (foot soldier) with stipend of 18 hyō and 2-person support allowance; appointed to bugukata-geyaku (assistant in weapons department) under bugukata supervision
- Tenpō 13 (1842), 9th month, 11th day: Died of illness

Yasui Sanzaburō

During the reign of Lord Yoriyasu
- Tenpō 13 (1842), 11th month, 3rd day: Inherited position from deceased father Nabekura; granted stipend of 18 hyō and 2-person support allowance; appointed as formal kachi; due to having family duties, appointed under bugu-bugyō (weapons magistrate) supervision

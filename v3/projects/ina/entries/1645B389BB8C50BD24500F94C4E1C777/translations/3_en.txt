Adopted son
　　　　　　　　　　　Actual second son of Yoshida Tazaemon
　　　　　　　　　　　　Kitahara Kinzaburo
　　　　　　　　　　　　　Later called Rokurozhi
First, Kansei 10, year of the horse (1798), 12th month, 8th day: As requested by adoptive father Rokurozhi
　As requested, retirement was granted, and he received succession with seven-person stipend
　Appointed to regular ashigaru rank and guard duty rotation
First, Bunka 2, year of the ox (1805), 1st month, 15th day: Temporary role in the great storehouse clothing department
First, same year, 11th month, 8th day: Appointed as ashigaru inspector
First, same 3rd year, year of the tiger (1806), 9th month, 15th day: Promoted to formal guard duty rank, revenue department
　Also assigned concurrent duty in great storehouse clothing department
First, same 5th year, year of the dragon (1808), 4th month, 10th day: Released from great storehouse clothing duties without incident
First, same 11th year, year of the dragon (1814), 11th month, 20th day: Appointed to storehouse department
First, Bunsei 2, year of the rabbit (1819), 4th month, 18th day: Appointed to return from
　district magistrate local duties, high rank maintained
During the reign of Lord Yoriyasu
First, same 5th year, year of the horse (1822), 9th month, 28th day: Due to rural administration reorganization, district magistrate duties completed without incident
　Appointed to guard rotation under supervision of chamberlain
First, same 9th year, year of the dog (1826), 5th month, 12th day:
　Appointed as assistant attendant to Lord Yoshinoshin's residence
First, same 10th year, year of the boar (1827), 8th month, 23rd day:
　Appointed as attendant to Lord Yoshinoshin's residence
First, same day: As attendant to Lord Yoshinoshin's residence, showing consideration during
　his illness and other matters, by special favor granted stipend rice
　of one koku equivalent to middle-ranking retainers
First, same year, 9th month, 5th day: Appointed to handle
　detailed administrative matters for the inspector
First, Tempo 2, year of the rabbit (1831), 8th month, 23rd day: Due to staff reorganization,
　released without incident from handling detailed duties for Lord Yoshinoshin,
　residence attendance continues as before
First, same 6th year, year of the sheep (1835), 10th month, 18th day: Due to illness,
　released from Lord Yoshinoshin's residence attendance as requested, henceforth appointed to
　guard rotation duty
First, same 11th year, year of the rat (1840), 1st month, last day: Promoted to formal middle page rank, with duties

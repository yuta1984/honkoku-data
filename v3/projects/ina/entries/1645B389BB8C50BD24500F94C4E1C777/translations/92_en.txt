Yasui Sango
During the reign of Lord Yorimoto
- Kansei 6 (1794), 12th month, 25th day: Appointed to koshokunin-nami (junior clerk) position with 6 to of additional rice stipend
- Kansei 13 (1801), 1st month, 15th day: Appointed to bugukata-geyaku-kogashira-nami (assistant head of the weapons department)
- Bunka 4 (1807), 11th month, 8th day: Exempted from monogaki-nami-takashi position
- Bunka 7 (1810), 4th month, 20th day: Promoted to kachi-kaku (foot soldier status) with residential stipend of 17 hyō and 2-person support allowance
- Bunsei 3 (1820), 1st month, 15th day: Appointed to formal kachi ranking with 1 hyō increase; duties remain the same as before

During the reign of Lord Yoriyasu
- Bunsei 8 (1825), 8th month, 23rd day: Due to old age and illness, as petitioned, relieved of duties without issue and appointed to jiban (standby position)
- Same year, 10th month, 15th day: Due to old age and illness, as petitioned, granted retirement; family headship transferred to his son Nabekura

In service
Yasui Nabekura

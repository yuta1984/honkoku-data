Hata Buemon

During the reign of Lord Yorimoto:
- Kansei 7, Year of the Rabbit (1795), 12th month, 23rd day: Became substitute for Hata Goemon
- Same 11th year, Year of the Sheep (1799), 10th month, 18th day: Entered the district magistrate group
- Bunsei 3, Year of the Dragon (1820), 1st month, 15th day: Appointed to full clerical duties

During the reign of Lord Yoriyasu:
- Same 8th year, Year of the Rooster (1825), 12th month, 28th day: Promoted to the rank of ashigaru (foot soldier), granted a stipend of 16 hyō and provisions for two people, duties to continue as before
- Same 12th year, Year of the Ox (1829), 11th month, 28th day: Elevated to attendant rank, appointed as deputy magistrate with an increase of 2 hyō
- Tenpō 5, Year of the Horse (1834), 8th month, 8th day: His son Katsugorō went river fishing near Fujisawa on the 5th, violating prohibitions, and was ordered into house confinement. Pardoned on the 28th day of the 9th month
- Same year, 10th month, 13th day: Relieved of deputy magistrate duties, henceforth appointed to assistant duties under the supervision of the chief retainer
- Same 6th year, Year of the Sheep (1835), intercalary 7th month, 5th day: Died of illness

End of service record

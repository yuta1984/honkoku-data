Hata Buemon

During the reign of Lord Yoriyasu:
- Tenpō 6, Year of the Sheep (1835), 8th month, 28th day: Succeeded to his late father Buemon's position, granted a stipend of 16 hyō and provisions for two people, appointed to assistant duties in the lord's escort service with ashigaru rank, henceforth under the supervision of the ashigaru commander

- Same 11th year, Year of the Rat (1840), 10th month, 13th day: Request approved to give his sister in marriage to Nakayama Genroku, a subordinate official in the treasury

- Same 12th year, Year of the Ox (1841), intercalary 1st month, 18th day: Request approved to take the daughter of Misawa Ichirōbei as wife

- Same year, 6th month, 13th day: Request approved to give his sister in marriage to Karakishi Takahiro, son of Karaki Takuhaku

- Same 13th year, Year of the Tiger (1842), 6th month, 20th day: Birth of a son

- Same 15th year, Year of the Dragon (1844), 3rd month, 28th day: Request approved to take as second wife a dependent woman of peasant Shūzō from Shimotomida Village, Ina District, under the jurisdiction of Lord Chiku Seinosuke

- Kōka 5, Year of the Monkey (1848), 1st month, 15th day: Promoted to regular ashigaru rank, granted an increase of 2 hyō, appointed to full escort duties

- Kaei 3, Year of the Dog (1850), [month] [day]: Appointed to Edo residence duty

- Same year, 9th month, 15th day: Due to the young lord's outing, appointed to temporary combined duty as ashigaru inspector in the escort service, and for this received a stipend of 1 ryō 1 bu in gold

- Same 4th year, Year of the Boar (1851), 12th month, 1st day: Appointed as ashigaru inspector

- Ansei 2, Year of the Rabbit (1855), 7th month, [day]: Appointed to Edo residence duty

- Same year, same month, 23rd day: Elevated to attendant rank, granted an increase of 2 hyō, total stipend of 20 hyō and provisions for two people, duties to continue as before

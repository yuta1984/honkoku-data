Recently, we were summoned by the official in charge of timber matters for the Noro River, and when we went to meet him, we were ordered to create a map showing the boundaries of the official mountain lands, and we returned to the village. Since this matter involves expenses, yesterday on the 16th the entire village gathered, and I explained the purpose of the summons mentioned above. After discussing the expenses and explaining the matter of being ordered to create the official map, the village representatives Sōbei, Heikurō, and Hikoemon stated that even if they were to submit the official map this time, since the matter of the official mountain lands particularly involves neighboring villages as well, they believe it would be too presumptuous for the village officials to affix their seals. Therefore, they requested that they be excused from affixing their seals to this matter. Consequently, we officials will submit a formal response document...

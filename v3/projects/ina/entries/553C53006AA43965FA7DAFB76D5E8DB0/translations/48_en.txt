Five koku, two-person stipend
Osakte-gumi [illegible character]
Yoshihara Kiriemon
Original name: Yosohei

During the reign of Lord Genryuin:
- Hōreki 7th year, Hinoto-Ushi (1757), 11th month, 4th day: Appointed to supervision of kitchen apprentices and inspection duties
- Same 11th year, Kanoto-Mi (1761), 12th month, 28th day: Promoted to foot soldier rank, stipend of 18 hyō and two-person allowance, appointed to kitchen duties

During the reign of Lord Honryuin:
- An'ei 6th year, Hinoto-Tori (1777), 2nd month, 28th day: Appointed to provisions management, concurrently serving kitchen duties
- Same 7th year, Tsuchinoe-Inu (1778), 8th month, 25th day: Died of illness

Service record:
Yoshihara Yosoji
Later called Sadazaemon

- An'ei 7th year, Tsuchinoe-Inu (1778), 10th month, 21st day: Inherited his late father's position without change, foot soldier under magistrate supervision, rotating kitchen duties

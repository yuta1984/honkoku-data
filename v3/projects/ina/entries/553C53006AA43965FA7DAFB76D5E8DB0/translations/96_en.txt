Takematsu Ichizaemon

During the reign of Lord Honryuin
- Kansei 1 (1789), 6th month, 20th day: Promoted from assistant warehouse clerk to temple attendant duty
  Stipend: 5 koku 6 to of rice plus provisions for 2 people

During the reign of Lord Yorimoto
- Kansei 11 (1799), 11th month, 25th day: Appointed to chief clerk scribe and purchasing duties
- Kyowa 1 (1801), 11th month, 15th day: Appointed to chief clerk scribe main duty and purchasing duties as before
- Bunka 1 (1804), 11th month, 23rd day: Promoted to ranked foot soldier, stipend of 18 bales of rice plus provisions for 2 people, appointed to payment duties at the chief clerk's office
- Bunka 11 (1814), 1st month, 15th day: Appointed to ranked personal guard duty, continuing service
- Bunsei 1 (1818), 11th month, 15th day: Appointed as head of mountain affairs, forestry, and bamboo grove/tree management
- Same year, 12th month, 18th day: Died of illness

Service Record
　　Adopted son
　　Actual fifth son of Yoshida Tazaemon

During the reign of Lord Yorimoto
- Bunka 13 (1816), 6th month, 15th day: Appointed to temporary service in the personal guard, granted provisions for 2 people as assistance pay
- Bunsei 2 (1819), 2nd month, 20th day: Inherited his late father Ichizaemon's position with stipend of 18 bales and provisions for 2 people, continuing service as foot soldier in personal guard at his assigned post

During the reign of Lord Yoriyasu
- Bunsei 9 (1826), 2nd month, 25th day: Appointed to inspector of receipt and payment of various construction materials, under the supervision of senior officials
- Same year, 9th month, 13th day: Appointed to inspector role for receipt and payment of various construction office materials, granted 1 koku as role stipend
- Bunsei 12 (1829), 1st month, 15th day: Appointed as foot soldier inspector
- Tenpo 4 (1833), 8th month, 3rd day: Successfully completed foot soldier inspector duties and was relieved, appointed to alternate duty
- Tenpo 5 (1834), 8th month, 8th day: His son Tadanosuke went fishing near Fujisawa on the 5th, violating regulations, so [Toyohachi] was ordered into house confinement, but was pardoned on 9th month, 9th day
- Tenpo 6 (1835), intercalary 7th month, 1st day: Appointed to duty at the rest inspector's office during castle residence, handling quantities and other expense matters

- Tenpo 7 (1836), 3rd month, 25th day: Appointed to service at Lord Shokitsu's residence
- Tenpo 8 (1837), 7th month, 28th day: Reappointed to foot soldier inspector with 2-bale increase, continuing service at Lord Shokitsu's residence as before, henceforth under foot soldier commander supervision
- Tenpo 10 (1839), 11th month, 3rd day: Request approved to send adopted daughter as wife to Kubota Uemon of Ikeda-cho in Matsumoto domain
- Tenpo 12 (1841), 10th month, 13th day: Request approved to send [daughter] as wife to Yasuda Kodenji
- Tenpo 13 (1842), 2nd month, 28th day: Granted personal guard rank, appointed to concurrently serve as foot soldier sub-commander and personal attendant commander
- Koka 2 (1845), 7th month, 13th day: Due to administrative reorganization, successfully relieved of concurrent duties as foot soldier sub-commander and personal attendant commander, appointed to alternate duty, henceforth under senior official supervision

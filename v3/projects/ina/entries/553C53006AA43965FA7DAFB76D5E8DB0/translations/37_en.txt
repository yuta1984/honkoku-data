First Service Record
Ishikawa Jūzaemon

During the reign of Lord Yorimoto
- Kansei 7, year of the Rabbit (1795), last day of the 6th month: Entered the castle deputy group
- Same 11th year, year of the Sheep (1799), 10th month, 18th day: Appointed as chief clerk, granted 3 to increase, total of 5 koku 3 to awarded
- Kyōwa 3, year of the Boar (1803), 2nd month, 15th day: 3 to increase
- Bunka 3, year of the Tiger (1806), 6th month, 13th day: Rural affairs clerk
- Same 4th year, year of the Rabbit (1807), 11th month, 8th day: Foot soldier standing duty as before, granted stipend of 18 hyō and provisions for 2 people
- Same 7th year, year of the Horse (1810), 3rd month, 18th day: 2 hyō increase
- Same 10th year, year of the Rooster (1813), 10th month, 23rd day: Granted formal rank of lord's attendant, appointed as regional deputy
- Bunsei 2, year of the Rabbit (1819), 4th month, 18th day: Successfully completed deputy role and was relieved, appointed to next position, formal rank and stipend remain the same

During the reign of Lord Yoriyasu
- Same 3rd year, year of the Dragon (1820), 12th month, 28th day: Appointed to warehouse administration
- Same 6th year, year of the Sheep (1823), 12th month, 18th day: 2 hyō increase
- Same 9th year, year of the Dog (1826), 1st month, 15th day: Returned to deputy position

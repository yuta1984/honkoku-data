During the reign of Lord Shinnyo-in

During the reign of Lord Genryū-in
1. Genbun 4, year of the Earth Goat (1739), 2nd month, 11th day: Appointed to ōnando (head of treasury), granted stipend of 33 hyō and 3 nin-fuchi
1. Enkyō 1, year of the Wood Rat (1744), 5th month, 17th day: As requested, relieved of duties and appointed to go-jiban (next guard duty)
1. Same 2nd year, year of the Wood Ox (1745), 6th month, 18th day: As requested, permitted to retire

Ariga Yososazaemon

During the reign of Honryuin-sama (Lord Naito Yoritsune):
- Tenmei 7 (1787), 4th month, 9th day: Assigned to the castle deputy group as substitute for his father Yososazaemon
- Kansei 1 (1789), 12th month, 28th day: Appointed to official duty rotation

During the reign of Raii-kun (Lord Naito Yoritaka):
- Kansei 4 (1792), 6th month, 27th day: Transferred from castle deputy group to advance guard group
- Kansei 6 (1794), 12th month, 25th day: Assigned as subordinate officer in the accounting division from Arai Magoemon's group
- Kansei 9 (1797), 4th month, 3rd day: Received additional rice stipend of six to
- Kansei 11 (1799), 2nd month, 25th day: Appointed as subordinate officer in the storehouse administration
- Bunka 1 (1804), 11th month, 23rd day: Appointed to chief administrator's clerical and purchasing division
- Bunka 5 (1808), 4th month, 10th day: Received three-year additional rice stipend

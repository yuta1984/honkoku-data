Shirokura Jōnosuke
Later renamed: Tamehachi
Also: Shichiemon

- Same year, July 21st: As his deceased father had petitioned, being of young age, granted assistance in the form of stipend for 5 people and ashigaru rank

- Same 8th year, Year of the Metal Rabbit (1771), September 9th: Appointed to yobitsugu duty

During the reign of Lord Honryūin
- Anei 6th year, Year of the Fire Rooster (1777), February 25th: Having reached proper age, inherited his deceased father's position with 18 hyō and stipend for 2 people, ashigaru rank, guard duty rotation, under supervision of chamberlain

- Same 7th year, Year of the Earth Dog (1778), October 21st: Appointed as apprentice cook

- Same 8th year, Year of the Earth Boar (1779): Temporary catering officer

- Tenmei 7th year, Year of the Fire Sheep (1787), April 5th: Appointed as cook

- Same year, October 12th: Appointed as head catering officer

- Died of illness
End of service record

Shirokura Mokusaburō
Later: Yatōji
Also: Tomouemon

- Kansei 2nd year, Year of the Dog (1790): Granted assistance in the form of stipend for 3 people

During the reign of Lord Yorimoto
- Same 9th year, Year of the Snake (1797), February 3rd: Appointed to yobitsugu position

- Kyōwa 4th year, Year of the Rat (1804), January 15th: Granted ashigaru rank inheriting his deceased father's position, granted 18 hyō and stipend for 2 people. Current duties to continue as before

- Bunka 3rd year, Year of the Ox (1806), May 1st: Appointed to personal attendant service under supervision of ashigaru commander

- Same 5th year, Year of the Dragon (1808), June 8th: Appointed as apprentice cook, daily duties to continue as before

- Same 8th year, Year of the Sheep (1811), October 28th: Appointed as head cook under chamberlain supervision

- Same 14th year, Year of the Ox (1817), December 22nd: Appointed as catering officer, concurrent cooking duties to continue as before

During the reign of Lord Yoriyasu
- Bunsei 3rd year, Year of the Dragon (1820), March 18th: Granted personal attendant rank status, duties to continue as before

- Same 7th year, Year of the Monkey (1824), May 5th: Appointed as temporary attendant to Lord Ryōnoshin's palace

- Same 8th year, Year of the Rooster (1825), June 20th: Due to changes in Lord Ryōnoshin's residence management system, palace attendant title discontinued, appointed as permanent residence attendant

- Same 10th year, Year of the Boar (1827), August 23rd: Appointed to rest quarters duty

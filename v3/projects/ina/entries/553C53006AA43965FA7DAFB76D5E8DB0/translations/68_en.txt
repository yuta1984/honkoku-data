From the Assistant Head of the Castle Magistrate Group
Imamura Rihei

During the reign of Lord Genryuin
- On the 25th day of the 12th month of Meiwa 8, Kanoto-u (1771), he was promoted to the rank of ashigaru (foot soldier) and granted a stipend of 18 hyō and rations for 2 people, temporarily assigned as a retainer to Naito Kazue

During the reign of Lord Honryuin  
- 11th month of An'ei 7, Tsuchinoe-inu (1778): Died of illness. His son Ikuya was still young.
Moreover, since it was a one-generation appointment, he submitted a deathbed petition stating that he did not wish for his son to inherit his position.

Service Record

Imamura Ikuya
《Marginal note: Later took the name Rihei》

- On the 15th day of the 1st month of An'ei 8, Tsuchinoto-i (1779), due to his young age, he was granted relief support of rations for 3 people, but without formal rank. At the time of this appointment, he was treated as without rank.

- Tenmei 7, Hinoto-hitsuji (1787): Called to duty

- 20th day of the 6th month of Kansei 1, Tori (1789): Appointed to succeed his late father Rihei's guard duty

From the Osente-gumi (Advance Guard Unit)
Shirokura Uhyōe
Later renamed: Shichiemon

During the reign of Lord Genryūin
- Genroku 3rd year, Year of the Earth Horse (1738), end of February: Appointed as kitchen duty under the supervision of the examination office without rank, assigned as apprentice cook, granted 5 koku and stipend for 2 people

- Same year, May 28th: Assigned to bowl service and kitchen purchasing duties, granted an additional 6 to (rice measure), instructed to demonstrate dedication in cooking duties

- Kanpō 3rd year, Year of the Water Boar (1743), July 22nd: Promoted to ashigaru (foot soldier) rank, granted 17 hyō (rice stipend), assigned to cooking duties for the lord's local residence

- Hōreki 2nd year, Year of the Water Monkey (1752), January 15th: Granted additional stipend for 1 person, total stipend increased to 3 people

- Same 5th year, Year of the Wood Boar (1755), July 12th: Assigned dual duties in catering and cooking

- Meiwa 3rd year, Year of the Fire Dog (1766), May 8th: Appointed to otomo-ban (personal attendant) rank, granted additional 3 hyō, total increased to 20 hyō and stipend for 3 people

- Same 7th year, Year of the Metal Tiger (1770), May 11th: Granted additional 5 hyō

- Same year, June 29th: Died of illness
End of service record

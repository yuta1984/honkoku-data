From Ashigaru-rank Purchase Officer
Koike Motoemon

During the reign of Shinnyo-in-sama
- On the 22nd day of the 4th month of Kyōhō 19, year of the Wood Tiger (1734), he was appointed to the rank of escort guard (gosupban-kaku), and granted a stipend of 5 ryō 2 bu in additional gold and provisions for 2 people per side.

During the reign of Genryū-in-sama
- On the 9th day of the 9th month of Enkyō 3, year of the Fire Tiger (1746), he was promoted to middle page (go-chūkoshō) and received an increase of 2 hyō and provisions for 1 person.
- On the 21st day of the intercalary 10th month of Kan'en 1, year of the Earth Dragon (1748), he was appointed as district administrator (godaikan).
- In the 4th month of Hōreki 4, year of the Wood Dog (1754), he died of illness.
Service record

Koike Genzō
- On the 1st day of the 4th month of Enkyō 5, year of the Earth Dragon (1748), he was summoned from attendant status (yobitsugu) to escort ashigaru, and granted 16 hyō and provisions for 2 people.
- On the 29th day of the 9th month of the same year, he died of illness.

I humbly submit this written petition
Regarding the children's lion dance performance scheduled for one day only on July 3rd,
which was approved, due to circumstances we now wish to
hold the performance on July 2nd instead. I humbly petition for this change. The above
Meiji 5, Year of the Water Monkey (1872), 7th month　　Hokoji-machi
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Petitioner: Saitō Gorō
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Group Head: Itō Kanzō
Takato
　Administrative Office

Following the usual procedure, the official seal was applied, and Group Head Kanzō together with petitioner Gorō
were brought along when we appeared to make the request, whereupon approval was granted. Morning of July 2nd

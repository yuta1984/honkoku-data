　Following proper procedures for official investigations, various arrangements were made, and the ship captain and sailors were summoned for examination. Upon investigation, there were no suspicious circumstances whatsoever, and there are no objections regarding this shipwreck incident. Since the original vessel was swept far offshore by strong winds, it was requested that the rice cargo be written off as abandoned at sea and the coastal settlement procedures be completed. The coastal certificate was prepared, delivered, and received. This is recorded for future reference. The above.

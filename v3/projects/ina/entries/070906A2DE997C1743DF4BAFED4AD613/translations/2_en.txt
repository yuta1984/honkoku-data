His Excellency the Udaijin (Minister of the Right)
Okoshōgumi (Pages' Guard)
Group Leader under Takeda Izumi-no-kami
One thousand koku: Suwa Shōuemon
Of which 750 koku is supplementary stipend, age 72
Total years of service as group leader: 52 years

Regarding the aforementioned Shōuemon, since we served as attendants to His Excellency the Udaijin, he has been serving as group leader. His personal conduct and household management are excellent - this goes without saying - and he is a person of particularly exceptional dedication who constantly keeps matters of loyal service in his heart with extraordinary commitment, conducting all affairs with exceptional diligence. Particularly regarding the attendant guard unit, since this was a new system, there were various internal difficulties, but his handling of each matter was remarkably thorough, and the entire unit is pleased.

However, since the attendant guard group leadership position requires two people working together, guard duties are frequent, and in addition there are ceremonial accompaniments and such. Should a colleague fall ill or have scheduling conflicts, one person must serve both as ceremonial attendant and overnight guard duty for both groups combined.

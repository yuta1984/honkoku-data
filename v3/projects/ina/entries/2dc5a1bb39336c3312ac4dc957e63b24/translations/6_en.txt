Memorandum of Verbal Statement

My sister's husband, a person named Bansuke, resided in Hokoji-machi. This Bansuke died of illness on the 14th day of the 12th month of the previous year of the Boar (An'ei 8, 1779). However, the matter of his succession has still not been decided.

Although Bansuke's younger brother named Magozō resides in Tonoshima village in this area, he took care of all matters during the illness and before and after the death. The same clan member Bunemon was also relieved, and regarding succession matters, he sent his thoughts by letter to the aforementioned Magozō at the time of the earlier death. However, no reply letter was sent, and from rumors we heard gradually, it seems there are some circumstances regarding the succession matter, so Bunemon also remains quite uneasy.

Under these circumstances, news came that my sister was ill, so wishing to discuss such matters, I visited the other day.

Item: According to the aforementioned Magozō's thinking, regarding a person named Wakichi who is Bansuke's nephew, since there were things mentioned while he was alive, he wishes to have this person succeed. My sister acknowledged that there were indeed things Bansuke mentioned last spring, but afterward, she frequently stated that the aforementioned Wakichi was still immature and succession would be quite uncertain. With such circumstances having changed, my sister's wishes regarding the aforementioned Wakichi cannot be fulfilled, so she seeks to find a suitable person elsewhere, or alternatively...

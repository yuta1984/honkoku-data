1. Nishinomaru announcement official □
　　　First Noh chanting of the year
1. The congratulations for the first Noh chanting of the year are offered with everyone seated. When the first Noh chanting begins, members of the three branch families
　　gradually come out in pairs in order to receive the ceremonial sake cups from His Lordship. Once completed, everyone should judge the timing to send [gifts] to Kanze
　　Tayu, which should be prepared in advance. Afterward, the performers should be assembled.

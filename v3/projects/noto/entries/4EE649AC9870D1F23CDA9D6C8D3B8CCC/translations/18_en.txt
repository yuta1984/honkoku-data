I write this in my own hand. Since last year, Sasa Kuranosuke has been duplicitous and has committed rebellion, repeatedly sending troops to the Kaga border. However, due to your prudent mind, you defeated and repelled them during the rear attack at Suemori, then pressed into Etchu territory with repeated military actions, particularly burning down a place called Hasunuma-no-ma. These successive achievements have been fully acknowledged even in the capital. There are truly no words to express this adequately. Therefore, thinking that this should be heard throughout all of Japan, I dispatched my forces. At this point, Kuranosuke pleaded for mercy at the main castle with various entreaties, and furthermore, you also requested that he be pardoned in the same manner. Having no choice, I arranged to grant him pardon extending to Niikawa and Ichi districts in Etchu.

However, regarding the granting of Etchu to you, since you took it with your incomparable spear point, you may not feel fully satisfied. Therefore, as a reward this time, I grant you my family name and title. From today forth, you shall change from Maeda Matazaemon and be called Hashiba Chikuzen-no-kami. Furthermore, your son Magoshiro shall also take the Hashiba name. Your devotion is no less than others. I have heard of each of your dedicated efforts and achievements and am satisfied. Regarding the matter of those three Etchu districts, I shall issue a certificate in Magoshiro's name...

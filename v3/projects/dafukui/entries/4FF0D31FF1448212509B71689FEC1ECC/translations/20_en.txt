One: On the 26th day of the 10th month, regarding the ceremonial offering for the Chōyō (Double Ninth Festival) celebration, an official letter was delivered at the castle, and the retainers' granted items, etc., were as usual.

One: On the 27th day of the same month, the following joint official notice came from the senior councilors:
　　There is an official matter, so you should ascend to the castle at 5:30 AM tomorrow, the 28th.
　　Above
　　　10th month, 27th day　　Inaba Tango-no-kami
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Akimoto Tajima-no-kami
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Ogasawara Sado-no-kami
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Tsuchiya Sagami-no-kami
　　　To Matsudaira Hyōbu-taiyu
　　　　However, an official notice also arrived for Masataki-sama.

One: On the 28th day of the same month
　Accompanying Masataki-sama, [the lord] ascended to the castle, had an audience together at the veranda corridor of the Black Study, and received the shogun's words. Afterward, [he] withdrew to the antechamber, took his short sword, came out to be seated, and took his seat in the western part within the threshold of the lower level. At that time, Senior Councilor Tsuchiya Sagami-no-kami (Masanao) presented a folded paper (with the single character "Yoshi"), and [they] came forward one by one.

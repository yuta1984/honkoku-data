10 pieces of silver, 2 seasonal garments, haori jacket　　Caretakers
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Matsubara Zen'zaemon
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Hori Jūbei
One: On the 9th day of the 11th month, Naidaijō (Naotomo) passed away, and from today until the coming 11th day, mourning was observed as the adopted nephew.
One: On the same day, regarding the above matter, when an inquiry was made to the Senior Councilor about duties at the construction site, instructions were given that duties should be suspended during the mourning period.
One: On the 13th day of the same month, Dewa-no-kami (Yoshitōru) received one character [from the shogun's name] and was appointed to the court rank of Junior Fourth Rank Lower Grade, Chamberlain.
One: On the 15th day of the same month, [he] ascended to the castle (yesterday, the 14th, a joint written order came from Duty Councilor Akimoto Tajima-no-kami). Gratitude was expressed for receiving the one character, and the following were presented: one sword (Shinkuni, worth 10 pieces of gold), 200 bundles of cotton, and horse money (10 ryō of gold).
　To the Shogun's wife and the First Rank Lady, 10 rolls of silk gauze and one type of delicacy each were presented, and upon departure, courtesy visits were made to the Senior Councilors, personal chamberlains, and junior councilors as thanks.
One: On the 20th day of the same month, since all the assistance construction work was completed, [he] went to the construction site accompanied by Yoshikuni.
One: On the 21st day of the same month, regarding the above, it was handed over to the government officials.
　　However, concerning this, Senior Retainer Matsudaira Shōbei and others gradually returned to their domain.
One: On the 6th day of the 12th month, Kōfu Chūnagon (Tsunatoyo) was proclaimed as adopted heir, and since he moved to the Western Palace yesterday, the 5th, [we] ascended to the castle to offer congratulations (yesterday, the 5th, a joint written order from the Senior Councilors arrived).

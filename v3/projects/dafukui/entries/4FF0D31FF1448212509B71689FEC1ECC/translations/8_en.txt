Ascended to the castle to inquire after the Shogun's health, and performed duties with Senior Councillor Akimoto Tajima-no-kami Takatsugu to inquire after Her Highness the First Rank's health.
　However, on the following 13th day, similarly ascended to the castle and performed duties with Honjo Aki-no-kami to inquire after Her Highness the First Rank's health, and also ascended to the castle on the 15th.

One: On the same day, regarding the same matter, a messenger (the castellan deputy) was dispatched to the duty officer Ogasawara Sado-no-kami Nagashige and chamberlain Matsudaira Ukyo-no-daibu Terusada.
　However, messengers were dispatched daily until the 15th.

One: On the 15th day of the 4th month, regarding the same matter, one set of cypress boxes was presented via messenger to inquire after their health.

One: On the 18th day of the same month, performed duties with duty officer Ogasawara Sado-no-kami Nagashige and chamberlain Matsudaira Ukyo-no-daibu Terusada to inquire after the Shogun's health, and performed duties with Senior Councillor Akimoto Tajima-no-kami Takatsugu to inquire after Her Highness the First Rank's health.

One: On the 23rd day of the same month, regarding the visit to Edo, five-colored hōsho paper was presented as usual via messenger as a souvenir gift.

One: On the same day, following a communication from the construction magistrate, Kaga Tōzaemon Nariyuki and Takada Kin-dayū Masafusa went out and were given documents specifying the locations for assisting with construction work.

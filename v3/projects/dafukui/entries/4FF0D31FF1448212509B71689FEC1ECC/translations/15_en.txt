One: Those with a domain assessment (takadaka) of 20,000 koku shall submit tribute rice at a rate of 1.6% of their assessed yield.
　　　However, when on duty in Edo, they shall submit tribute at a rate of 0.7% of their assessed yield.

One: Those with domain assessments from 3,000 to 4,500 koku shall submit tribute rice at a rate of 1.5% of their assessed yield.
　　　However, when on duty in Edo, they shall submit tribute at a rate of 0.6%.

One: Those with domain assessments from 1,650 to 2,600 koku shall submit tribute rice at a rate of 1.4% of their assessed yield.
　　　However, when on duty in Edo, they shall submit tribute at a rate of 0.55% of their assessed yield.

One: Those with domain assessments from 1,000 to 1,500 koku shall submit tribute rice at a rate of 1.3% of their assessed yield.
　　　However, when on duty in Edo, they shall submit tribute at a rate of 0.5%.

One: Those with domain assessments from 600 to 900 koku shall submit tribute rice at a rate of 1.2% of their assessed yield.
　　　However, when on duty in Edo, the same applies.

One: Both those assigned to district administrators (daikan-tsuki) and those receiving rice from the lord's storehouse, with assessments of 550 koku or below, shall submit tribute rice at a rate of 1% of their assessed yield.
　　　However, when on duty in Edo, the same applies.

One: Those who have been granted assistance rice (gōryoku-mai) shall submit tribute rice calculated at a rate of 1% of their stipend assessment.

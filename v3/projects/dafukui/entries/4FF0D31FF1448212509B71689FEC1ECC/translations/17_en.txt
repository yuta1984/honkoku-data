One: Those receiving stipend rice from 8 to 5 hyō shall submit tribute rice at a rate of 4 to per person.
　　　　　Note: Portions related to sustenance allowances are excluded.
　　　However, when on duty in Edo, they shall submit 2 to per person.

One: Those receiving stipend rice of 4 hyō shall submit tribute rice at a rate of 3 to per person.
　　　　　Note: Portions related to sustenance allowances are excluded.
　　　However, when on duty in Edo, they shall submit 1 to and 5 shō per person.

One: Both the year before departing for Edo and the year of service in Edo, tribute shall be submitted according to the Edo duty regulations stated above. In the year of return, tribute shall be paid the same as those in the domain.

One: Those who travel to Edo or other provinces as official messengers, bringing along retainers and horses appropriate to their rank of uma-mochi or above, shall submit tribute equivalent to those on Edo duty.

One: When stipend rice recipients travel to Edo on official business or other domain duties and happen to stay for extended periods, while they receive loans for tribute payments, they will receive increased sustenance allowances throughout their stay but will not be exempted from tribute payments.

One: Among those in regular attendance (jō-gokū), those who remain in the domain shall pay tribute equivalent to domain standards.

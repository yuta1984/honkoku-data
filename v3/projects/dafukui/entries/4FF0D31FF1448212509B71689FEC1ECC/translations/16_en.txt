　　　　　Note: Portions related to sustenance allowances are excluded.

One: Those receiving stipend rice from 9 koku to 4.5 koku shall submit tribute rice calculated at a rate of 0.7% against their stipend assessment.
　　　　　Note: Portions related to sustenance allowances are excluded.

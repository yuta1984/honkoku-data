September 23rd
In Edo　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Domain Administrator
○Regarding the matter of ceremonial horses: From now on, even on ordinary days, three horses shall be led as part of the ceremonial procession. Keep this in mind and communicate to the relevant departments for appropriate arrangements.
October 22nd
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　To respective offices
○Concerning various domain debts, various expenditures, and procurement silver, etc.: These had been deferred from the Year of the Tiger until the current Year of the Sheep. Although the term limit has expired and settlements were to begin from next Year of the Monkey, due to increased expenses, another deferment has been ordered.
October 25th
In Edo　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Domain Administrator
○Regarding the memorial day of Lady Tenryo-in, which falls on the 12th: It has been ordered that the observance shall be conducted over a two-day period. Communicate this matter to all concerned parties.

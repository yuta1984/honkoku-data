First, Bunsei 9th year, Year of the Dog (1826)
                                    To all samurai retainers
○Regarding last month's 23rd day, [the lord] received the appointment to family headship without incident, and this gracious decision was announced to be conveyed. Shibuya Gonzaemon and Katayama Heishichi were dispatched [to convey this message]. Everyone should be filled with joy and gratitude. Furthermore, [you are] ordered to faithfully observe and serve according to the hereditary house rules.
                    February 3rd, at the main castle hall

                                    Matsudaira Gentaro
○Regarding Lady Teishoin, [she] has been accorded the treatment of an honored birth mother. Accordingly, in conformity with the treatment of other noble ladies, henceforth her name shall be written with honorific characters in formal correspondence.
                    February 24th

                                    Town Magistrate
○The kimono fabric and secondhand clothing merchants of the castle town have petitioned as follows: In previous years they petitioned regarding guild organization matters and received official permits, but now they again request that through [your] further benevolence, the guild membership be fixed at 220 licensed establishments and new membership certificates be issued. In return, they wish to contribute 10 pieces of silver annually in perpetuity as a tribute offering. Their petition was submitted, and for now it has been approved as requested for this year and next, while the tribute silver matter is deferred...

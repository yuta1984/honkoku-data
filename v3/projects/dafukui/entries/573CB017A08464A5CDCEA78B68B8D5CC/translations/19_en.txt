Regarding this, ceremonial protocols will also be reformed according to his lordship's wishes, so all those in positions beneficial to the domain should work with particular dedication and diligence. Therefore, the attached document is hereby provided. Act according to these instructions.
　However, regarding the contents of this announcement, it should be communicated to all those under the supervision of each respective department.
　　　　　December 17th
　　　　　　Attached Document
1. Regarding the official duties of His Lordship and the Young Lord: only duties to the Three Branch Houses and Three Lords will be performed. All other official duties are to be declined.
　　However, members of the main family branch will perform duties once during their Edo residence period.
1. Regarding visits to the Shogunal Mausoleums: visits at New Year, Obon, monthly memorial days, arrival in Edo, and departure.
1. Regarding personal visits: visits at New Year, Obon, arrival in Edo, departure, and monthly memorial days.
　　However, visits to Lord Kakuryōin's spirit shall be made monthly.
1. The Young Lord's visits shall follow the same pattern.
　　However, visits to successive lords shall be at New Year, Obon, general memorial services, and monthly memorial days, the same as for Lords Kakuryōin and Bodaishin'in.
1. Guard duties at the middle mansion are discontinued; only patrol rounds will be assigned.

1. At both the upper and middle mansions, physicians will only serve day shifts; overnight stays are exempted.
1. At the middle mansion's entrance guardhouse, only foot soldiers will serve; samurai guards are discontinued.
1. Reduce personal attendant pages by two persons.
1. Retinue arrangements are as follows:
　　Palanquin bearers: reduce by 2; foot soldier inspectors: reduce by 1; advance foot soldiers: same reduction
1. Whereas previously three horses accompanied the retinue, from now on the protocol officer shall lead one horse. Also, whereas previously three ceremonial spears were carried in the retinue, from now on the inspector shall provide one, the protocol officer one, and the retinue head one.
1. Overall, reduce the size of accompanying forces as much as possible.
1. The Young Lord's retinue shall be similarly reduced.
　　However, for abbreviated processions, whereas previously three ceremonial spears were carried by personal attendants, from now on this is discontinued and they shall be carried by inspectors and protocol officers.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Inaba Oribe
1. Foot soldiers positioned in advance for retainer horsemanship reviews are discontinued.
1. Personnel reductions are as follows:
　　During Edo residence: reduce foot soldier group leaders on duty, reduce foot soldiers on duty by 1, same for regular hired personnel
　　During lord's absence: reduce regular hired foot soldiers by 1, same for foot soldiers on duty

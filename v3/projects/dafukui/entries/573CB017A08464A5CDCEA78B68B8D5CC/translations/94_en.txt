Official Notice　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Monthly Officers
〇As announced last winter through direct written copy, the financial situation has become difficult to manage, and at present it has reached a critical state for the domain. Therefore, with special consideration, from this year of the Ox for seven years, all matters shall be reduced and only essential financial continuation policies shall be implemented. It is well understood that this will cause increasing hardship for the retainer band, but due to these critical circumstances, there is no choice but to request a 50% reduction in loan rice for seven years, so this matter is to be announced　　　April 5th

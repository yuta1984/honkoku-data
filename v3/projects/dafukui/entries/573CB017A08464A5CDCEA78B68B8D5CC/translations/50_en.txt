1. Kansei 4th year, Year of the Rat (1792)
To the Monthly Duty Officers

○ In the previous Year of the Boar, it was ordered that personnel be reduced as a relief measure and that duties continue to be performed. However, the previous Year of the Boar was a year of poor harvest, with tax exemption rates also declining, and it is recognized that everyone is in distress. Therefore, for this Year of the Rat as well, with special consideration as a relief measure, following the same manner as the previous Year of the Boar, everyone should understand all matters and serve with frugality as their primary focus and exercise discretion. The above.

However, those who receive official stipends shall also receive loans this year. The matter of proportions will be communicated later by the magistrates, so this point should be understood.

First Month, 29th Day
To the Chief Administrator of the Entrusted Territories and District Magistrates

○ Regarding the handling of various matters of the entrusted territories, these have been handled at the administrative office until now, but due to certain intentions, it has been ordered that they be handled within the accounting office from now on. Therefore, except on deliberation days, after enclosures and such are completed, they shall be moved to the accounting office for various [two lines illegible]

The above points are to be communicated by me
To the Magistrates

○ Regarding the handling of various matters by the Chief Administrator of Entrusted Territories, District Magistrates, and Deputy Officials, these have been handled at the administrative office until now, but due to certain intentions, it has been ordered that from now on various matters be handled within the accounting office in the same manner as before, and this has been communicated. This should be understood accordingly. Furthermore, the Mountain Magistrate and Irrigation Magistrate have until now been stationed at the aforementioned administrative office handling various matters, but due to certain intentions, from now on an office within the accounting office has been designated, and after construction and renovations as per internal deliberations are completed, various matters should be handled at that office in the same manner as before. This is to be communicated to both officials, that matters should be handled without hindrance and without incurring expenses. Furthermore, consultation should be made with the inspectors.
To the Inspectors

○ Regarding the handling by the Chief Administrator of Entrusted Territories, District Magistrates, and Deputy Officials, these have been handled at the administrative office until now, but due to certain intentions, it has been ordered that from now on, except on deliberation days, various matters be handled within the accounting office in the same manner as before, and this has been communicated. Furthermore, the Mountain Magistrate and Irrigation Magistrate have handled various matters at the aforementioned administrative office until now, but from now on an office within the accounting office has been designated, and after completion, various matters should be handled at that office in the same manner as before. It has been communicated to the magistrates that matters should be handled without hindrance and without incurring expenses. These points should be understood accordingly and proper consultation should be made with the magistrates.

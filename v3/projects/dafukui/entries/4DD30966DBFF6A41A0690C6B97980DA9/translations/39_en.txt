Tatsu [Year of the Dragon], 12th month

1. 12th month (day unspecified): Regarding Tenryuji Temple in Shinagawa, which was founded by Lord Kenshoin and has no other major patrons, since Lord Hosen'in has succeeded [as patron], henceforth Lord Hosen'in's memorial tablet will be enshrined and a mausoleum will be constructed. As Tenryuji Temple petitioned that without special donations for memorial services and such, the temple would be difficult to maintain, it was decreed that henceforth 200 bales of 3-to [rice] measures shall be granted annually.

1. Tang brocade with gold thread and similar materials shall not be used for covers of night clothes, bedding, shell buckets, or document boxes. Covers for long chests, folding screen boxes, etc. shall use silk cloth or leather.

1. For wedding processions, the retinue and palanquins shall not exceed ten units.

1. For celebratory banquets, recent precedents should be followed even more strictly, and the number of dishes should be reduced. For regular gatherings, even high-ranking individuals shall not exceed two soups and six dishes. Pickled vegetables are included in this count.

In general, clear soups and side dishes should be reduced in proportion to the number of main dishes.

1. The exchange of wedding gifts should be considered in accordance with the recent instructions regarding ceremonial gifts.

The above items should be adopted by those with ten thousand koku or more according to their appropriate status.

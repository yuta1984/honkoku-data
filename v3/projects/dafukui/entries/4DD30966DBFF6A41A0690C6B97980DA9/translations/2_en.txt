Kyōhō 9, Year of the Wood Dragon
On the 11th day of the 5th month, Lord Shimano-kami, serving as a representative, went to
Mizuno Izumi-no-kami, who was on duty that day. The rōjū (senior councilors) were present in assembly, and
an official announcement was made: "The succession of Nakatsukasa-no-taiyu to Senjirō shall proceed without issue, 
so this matter should be communicated accordingly." Lord Shimano-kami then came directly to this
mansion, whereupon he was welcomed as far as the wooden floor area of the entrance hall,
and they proceeded together
to the reception hall. The aforementioned announcement was conveyed, congratulations for longevity were offered,

Castle guards and extra personnel shall each maintain men and horses by half, alternating every other year.

1. Among those with 250 koku stipends, reduce the voluntary rice contribution by one bu. However, those serving guard duty in Edo shall reduce it by two bu.

1. Among those with 200 koku stipends, reduce the voluntary rice contribution by one bu five rin. However, those serving guard duty in Edo shall reduce it by two bu five rin.

1. Those with 150 koku and below shall reduce their voluntary rice contribution by two bu five rin. However, those serving guard duty in Edo shall reduce it by three bu.

1. Those with 500 to 900 koku shall conduct all affairs at a level inferior to 200 koku holders.

1. Those with 1000 koku and above shall conduct all affairs from personal matters to private ones [at reduced levels].

1. Among the regular attendants, those with 300 koku and above shall reduce their voluntary rice contribution by half. If they are left to remain in the domain and serve on a four-year rotation, during the two years they remain in the domain, they shall contribute as they have until now.

1. Among the regular attendants with 250 koku and below, their voluntary rice contribution shall be one bu less than those serving alternating years. If they are left to remain in the domain on a four-year rotation, during the two years in the domain, they shall contribute the same amount as those serving alternate guard duty.

- Same day: Attended the castle (a kirimi note came from Senior Councilor Kuze Yamato-no-kami yesterday on the 18th). Expressed gratitude for recovery from illness and presented one box of dried sea bream. Upon departure, made courtesy calls to the Senior Councilors and Sakai Kawachi-no-kami Tadaaki for thanks.

- April 10th: Through envoy Ozeki Kan'emon, received five hawks (bans) as usual.

- April 15th (past 6 o'clock): Through envoy Senior Councilor Inaba Mino-no-kami Masanori, was granted leave to return to the domain, and received white silver and a lined garment as usual.

- Same day (past 5 o'clock): Regarding the above matter, attended the castle to express gratitude for the leave. As in the previous generation's precedent, received two hawks and one horse. The retainer brought along, Inaba Uneme Harufusa, was granted an audience, and after retiring was granted four lined garments and one haori jacket. After this was completed and upon departure, made courtesy calls to the Senior Councilors, Junior Councilors, and Sakai Kawachi-no-kami Tadaaki for thanks.

- Same day: Through a messenger, presented the first mackerel of the domain's produce as usual.

- Same day: The granted horse was brought by a messenger from Senior Councilor Okubo Kaga-no-kami Tadatomo.
  Note: The hawks were delivered after being taken from their roosts.

- April 21st:
  Since Masachika-sama was granted leave to return to the domain, messenger Saito Minbu Sadayuki was dispatched to the Senior Councilors, Junior Councilors, and Sakai Kawachi-no-kami Tadaaki to express thanks.

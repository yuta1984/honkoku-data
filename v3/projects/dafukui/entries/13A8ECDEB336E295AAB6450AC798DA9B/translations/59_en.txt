　One: When those of unified rank fall seriously ill, if they request leave for themselves and petition that their sons be summoned and granted positions in some capacity, then after the parent's illness petition, leave shall be granted and the son shall be granted a stipend of 10 koku and support for 3 persons and summoned as an accountant, as shall be announced
　One: Those who are succession accountants receive the same treatment as above
　One: When specially appointed accountants fall seriously ill, petitions are handled in the same manner as above, but for those who have served their full term with years of service, they may be summoned directly as accountants upon petition from their fellow accountants
　　　However, this is extremely rare, with only about one such case
　One: Those without years of service or merit have their sons appointed to various clerk positions, and when they become assistant clerks, they are appointed as temporary deputies in financial roles
　　　　End

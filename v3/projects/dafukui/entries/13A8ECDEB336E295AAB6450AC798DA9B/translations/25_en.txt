○ Article 1: For those whose years of service to date fall short of the years stipulated this time, they shall be promoted in rank by making up for the shortfall in years.
　　However, regardless of generational rank, they shall be promoted to the lowest position of their original rank based on years of service.

○ Article 1: For those who have been demoted due to misconduct, those who have been demoted to within the御取立席 (promotion-eligible positions) shall be restored to the lowest position of their original rank after fifty years, and those demoted to 新番 (new guard) after two years, according to the stipulated number of years.
　　However, they shall be restored based on years of service regardless of generational rank.

○ Article 1: For those in 新番 who have completed the stipulated number of years, the first generation shall be promoted to the lowest position of 御取立現身 (first-generation promotion-eligible), the second generation to the lowest position of 御取立二代 (second-generation promotion-eligible), and the third generation to the lowest position of 御取立三代 (third-generation promotion-eligible).
　　However, from the third generation onward, regardless of how many generations, they shall be promoted to the lowest position of the third generation.

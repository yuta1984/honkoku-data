Shōtoku 4, Year of the Wood Horse (1714)
1. On New Year's Day, the New Year ceremony was held at the main castle
1. On the same day, for New Year's congratulations, through the messenger council member Hahaibe Hachizaemon Yoriyasu, a ceremonial sword and horse substitute were presented as customary
   Note: Hachizaemon departed from the domain on the 5th day of the 12th month of the previous year
1. On the same day, for the New Year's first Noh chanting ceremony congratulations, a ceremonial sake cup stand and sake substitute were presented through a messenger as customary
1. On the 5th day of the 1st month, regarding the fire damage to the residence of Nakatsukasa-no-taifu Shōhei, for condolences

When young men gathered at their house day and night, the uncle strongly objected and forced the girl up to the third floor. Though the woman was eighteen years old, he would not let her meet anyone. While things continued this way, one night a young man came and placed a ladder against the outside [of the house], enticed the woman out, and together they ran to the village headman's house, where they explained the entire situation in detail. The headman took pity on them and gave them a written document. With this they went to a temple, obtained official approval, and returned. After this, the uncle had no choice but to leave that house.

1. Medicine is not yet advanced. However, there are doctors who come from Holland. Something called "Japanese disease" is prevalent - it is a severe fever. The treatment for this involves putting the patient in a tub of water, or digging a hole in the ground and burying the body to cool it and relieve the fever. Few recover from it.

1. In the West, there are countless cases of smallpox vaccination. In America, when pox appears, they have a treatment of scraping and removing the pox with blades. Therefore, it does not seriously harm people.

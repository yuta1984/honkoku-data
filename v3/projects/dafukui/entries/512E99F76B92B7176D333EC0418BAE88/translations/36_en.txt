One: April 10th - Regarding the same matter, Senior Councilor Tsuchiya Sagami-no-kami came as an imperial envoy
　　On that occasion,
　　Monomimi-sama accompanied him and went out to the gate to greet and guide him, leading Sagami-no-kami
　　to the formal reception room. The imperial message was conveyed, and after Sagami-no-kami's
　　departure, courtesy visits were made to the senior councilors to express gratitude
One: Same month, 18th - An imperial letter regarding the year-end ceremonial offerings was
　　delivered at the castle, and the gifts bestowed upon retainers followed the usual protocol

One: Same month, 21st - He made a castle visit and
　　expressed gratitude for his sankin attendance, presenting the customary ceremonial sword, silver, and cotton. To the Shogun's wife, white silver and
　　silk crepe were presented. Upon departure, he made courtesy visits to the senior councilors, chamberlains, and junior councilors
One: Same day - For the Boys' Day celebration, through an envoy, he presented the customary summer robes and single-layer garments,
　　and offered white silver
　　　　However, the senior councilors instructed that he should withdraw due to memorial services starting from the 26th
One: Same month, 25th - Regarding his arrival in the capital, for the birth celebration, through an envoy he presented the customary colored ceremonial paper
　　and offered double-sided clouded bird-child paper to the Shogun's wife

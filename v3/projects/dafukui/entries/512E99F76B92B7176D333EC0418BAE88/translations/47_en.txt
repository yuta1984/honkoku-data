One: 22nd day of the 2nd month
　　Together with Kenmotsu-sama, they attended the castle, listened to the lecture reading, and withdrew
　　As thanks for this, together they made courtesy visits to the rōjū and goyōnin, and sent
　　a messenger to the osoba-shū (personal attendants)

One: 23rd day of the same month
　　Together with Kenmotsu-sama, they attended the castle and expressed gratitude for
　　listening to the lecture reading
One: On the same day, regarding the above matter, through a messenger they presented one box of kelp, one box of dried sea bream, and one thousand hiki as sake money

　　Kenmotsu-sama also presented one box of dried sea bream and one thousand hiki as sake money
One: 29th day of the same month, as thanks for listening to the lecture reading
　　Together with Kenmotsu-sama, they performed their duties to rōjū Toda Yamashiro-no-kami Tadamasa
One: 23rd day of the 3rd month
　　There was a celebration for Kenmotsu-sama's coming-of-age ceremony (sode-dome), he entered the formal area, and in the inner reception room
　　met with Masaaki-sama, presented long noshi (ceremonial gifts), and held celebratory sake ceremonies with ozōni (New Year soup)
　　After this concluded, in the Shisen room they dined together on a formal meal (three soups and eight dishes)
　　Kenmotsu-sama bestowed sake cups from the chief retainer down to Takaya Zenemon Shigeshide, and gave
　　long noshi to the sōja-ban (protocol officials) and other officials

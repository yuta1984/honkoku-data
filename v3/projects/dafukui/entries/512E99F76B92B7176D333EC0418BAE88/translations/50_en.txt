One: On the 11th day of the 5th month, he arrived at his castle.

One: On the 12th day of the same month, for the same occasion as thanks, he dispatched the messenger Takachi Saitō Minbu Sadatake to Edo, who presented formal paper, kelp, dried sea bream, and sake barrel money. Minbu departed from Fukui today.
　　　　Note: The details of Minbu's arrival in Edo and the delivery of the tribute are unknown.

One: On the 22nd day of the same month, karō Honda Sahyōe Naoshige was relieved of his duties due to illness for convalescence.

One: On the 27th day of the same month, when messenger Saitō Minbu Sadatake appeared at the castle, a reply letter was delivered, and at that time Minbu was granted ceremonial clothing.

One: On the 10th day of the 6th month, for the celebration of his return to the domain,
Kenmotsu-sama sent messenger Obata Uhyōe with two kinds in one load, separately dried sweets in one box, and pickled vegetables in one bucket. He was granted an audience in the enclosed room of the formal reception hall and was given abalone.
　　　　Note: The date of Uhyōe's departure from Edo is unknown.

One: On the 22nd day of the same month, due to the hot season, through an official letter he presented the customary domain products of sōmen noodles and dried sea bream.

One: On the 24th day of the same month, regarding the installation at Eiheiji Temple, he went to the castle, had an audience in the great hall, and presented ten scrolls and one item. This was announced by the ometsuke. Kōkenji Temple, Chintokuji Temple.

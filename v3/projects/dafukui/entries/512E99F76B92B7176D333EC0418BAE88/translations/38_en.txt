(Written instructions were given on the 26th of last month)
One: May 9th - As the memorial service had been completed, through envoy and resident official Kaga
　　Tozaemon (Nari), he presented post-abstinence delicacies (three sea bream)
One: Same month, 10th - Senior Councilor Tsuchiya Sagami-no-kami summoned him, and when resident official Hara
　　Heizaemon appeared, he received private advice that since other daimyo of equal rank were requesting
　　to attend scholarly lectures, it would be appropriate for him to make such a request as well
One: Same month, 11th - He visited the Senior Councilor's chamberlain and
　　made a request to attend scholarly lectures
One: Same month, 12th -

　　Together with Monomimi-sama, he visited the Senior Councilor's chamberlain, and
　　also made a request for Monomimi-sama to attend scholarly lectures
One: Same month, 21st - He visited chamberlain Yanagisawa Dewa-no-kami (Yasuaki) and
　　made a request to attend scholarly lectures
　　　　Also on the 27th of the same month,
　　　　　together with Monomimi-sama, he made the same request
One: Same month, 29th - At Tokoji Temple, a tea ceremony for the 70th anniversary memorial
　　of Obaiin-sama was conducted

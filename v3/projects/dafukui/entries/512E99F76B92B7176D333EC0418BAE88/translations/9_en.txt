One: On the 4th of the same month at half past six, Lord Chōkichi moved to the Reiganjima residence (accompanied by Lord Mukai Shōgen). Matsudaira Shōbei Masatsune was sent to welcome him. When he entered, the chief retainers and court announcers along with other officials came out to the shiratsu (white sand courtyard), the military commanders and guard units came out to the great hall, and the direct retainers came out to welcome him up to the formal entrance hall. After being seated in the front study, Lord Masaaki appeared and they had a face-to-face meeting. At that time, the presented ceremonial sword, gold horse substitute, and twenty sets of seasonal clothing in two loads were announced by court announcer Ōtani Sukeroku. After the lengthy ceremony concluded, Kuchibane Sukenoshin, a retainer of Lord Nagato-no-kami Yoshinari, brought a sword catalog and was granted an audience. After the lengthy ceremony was personally concluded by the lord, the matter was completed.

Lord Chōkichi was accompanied to the inner study, where after the lengthy ceremony, celebratory soup and clear soup were served, and a three-round celebration with sake cups took place. A sake cup was offered to Lord Chōkichi, and at that time, a pair of swords (a ceremonial sword by Bizen Kanemitsu worth twenty gold pieces, and a short sword by Bizen Kanemasa worth six gold pieces) were presented.

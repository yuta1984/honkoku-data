One: September 13th
　　Together with Kenmotsu, they visited Chamberlain Yanagisawa Dewa-no-kami (Yasuaki) and requested permission to attend lecture readings
One: Same month, 16th - To Roju Okubo Kaga-no-kami (Tadatomo) and Tsuchiya Sagami-no-kami (Masanao), through the messenger and caretaker Hara Heizaemon (Masatake), they reported that on the 9th, Takumi-no-kami (Masahira) arrived at Matsuoka, and the physician Okuyama Ryuan also arrived the same day and immediately conducted an examination. The patient took medicine and his condition remained the same, according to reports from the domain
One: October 5th - Nakatsukasa-daifu (Masakatsu) gradually recovered, and physician Miyama Ryuan

　　departed Matsuoka on the 23rd of last month and returned today. For gratitude regarding this matter, messengers were sent to duty officer Toda Yamashiro-no-kami (Tadamasa) and last month's duty officer Tsuchiya Sagami-no-kami (Masanao), with remaining duties to the Roju and chamberlains
One: Same month, 13th
　　Together with Kenmotsu, they visited Chamberlain Yanagisawa Dewa-no-kami (Yasuaki) and requested permission to attend lecture readings
　　　　　　However, on the 23rd of the same month, they made the same request again
One: November 14th - To Keishoin (Tsunayoshi's birth mother), they privately presented 100 bundles of cotton and five sea bream in a box, using messenger and caretaker Kaga Tozaemon (Narioki) through Rokkaku

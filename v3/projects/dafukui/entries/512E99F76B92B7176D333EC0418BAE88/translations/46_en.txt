One: 7th day of the 1st month - For the first eating ceremony of the New Year, through a messenger, they presented fish dishes as usual
One: 11th day of the same month - A celebration for the display of armor-shaped rice cakes was held
　　Kenmotsu-sama also took his seat, and the chief retainer was ordered to accompany them,
　　and rice cakes were bestowed upon the various samurai
One: 12th day of the same month
　　Together with Kenmotsu-sama, they visited the goyōnin (personal attendant) Yanagisawa Dewa-no-kami Yasuaki
　　and made a request to attend a lecture reading
　　　　However, on the 26th day of the same month, they made the same request again
One: 3rd day of the 2nd month
　　Since Tsunamasa-sama's support rice was to be received three times per year from this year,
　　regarding the endorsement of the certificate, they made a request through messenger and castellan Hara Heizaemon Masatake to the duty magistrate Toda Yamashiro-no-kami Tadamasa, and the endorsement was prepared and delivered
One: 19th day of the same month
　　When they attended the castle together with Kenmotsu-sama (a written order jointly signed by the rōjū had arrived on the 18th),
　　the rōjū conveyed that as requested, they would be permitted to attend the lecture reading on the coming 22nd day, and as thanks for this, they made courtesy visits to the rōjū, goyōnin, and wakadoshiyori

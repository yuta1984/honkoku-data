After receiving the long sake cups, from Takachi down to all retainers and officials received the ceremonial long sake cups at their respective seats.
　　However, those from the various magistrates (monogashira) and below received them through the intermediation of the master of ceremonies (osojaban).
　One: On the 2nd, during the children's ceremony at the main castle, they similarly received the ceremonial long sake cups.

One: On the same day, for New Year congratulations, through messenger Tsuneoka Yasuemon Toshiyoshi, ceremonial swords and horse money were presented as usual.

One: On January 3rd, for the congratulations of the first Noh performance of the year, through a messenger, ceremonial sake cup stands and sake money were presented as usual.

One: On the 7th of the same month, for the first ascent ceremony, ceremonial fish were presented through a messenger as usual.

One: On the 9th of the same month, regarding the lord's recovery from illness after taking medicine from Nagashima Dosen, messenger Koma Mokunosuke Sadaharu was dispatched to Edo for thanksgiving. He departed Fukui today.
　　Mokunosuke arrived in Edo on the 19th of the same month.
　　One: On January 23rd, a reply letter was delivered by Senior Councilor Abe Bungo-no-kami.

One: On the same day, for the same reason, as Nagashima Dosen was to return to Edo, and departed Fukui today, retainer Kumagai Kohyoe Masanaga was assigned to accompany him on the journey.

One: On the 11th of the same month, for congratulations on the completion of the three-day New Year ceremonies

One: On April 11th, for the same reason as above, Senior Councilor Tsuchiya Sagami-no-kami Masanao came as an envoy and conveyed the shogun's intentions (the envoy's reception followed precedent). After his departure, courtesy visits were made to the Senior Councilors, chamberlains, and junior councilors as thanks for this.

One: On the 22nd of the same month at 6:30, the lord went to the castle (yesterday, a written notice came from Toda Yamashiro-no-kami Tadamasa, who was on duty). He offered thanks for his attendance at the capital and, following precedent, presented a ceremonial sword and silver thread. To the Shogun's wife, white silver crepe was presented. Upon departure, courtesy visits were made to the Senior Councilors, chamberlains, and junior councilors.

One: On the same day, regarding the year-end congratulatory offerings, an official letter was presented at the castle, and retainers received gifts as usual.

One: On the 26th of the same month, through messengers, the first mackerel of the season from the domain was offered as customary.

One: On the 29th of the same month, a petition regarding adoption was brought to Toda Yamashiro-no-kami Tadamasa, who was on duty.

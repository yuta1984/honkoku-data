At Kōkenji Temple, a memorial service was conducted from the evening of yesterday, the 8th, until this morning.

One: On the 18th day of the 8th month, messenger Imadate Jidayū Masafusa was dispatched to Edo for the monthly inquiry about His Lordship's well-being, departing from Fukui this morning.
However, the date of Jidayū's arrival in Edo is unknown.

One: On the 7th day of the 9th month, for the Chōyō (Double Ninth) festival congratulations, a kosode (short-sleeved kimono) was presented through a messenger as customary, and white silver was offered.

One: On the 25th day of the same month, regarding the hereditary domain of the late father Nakatsukasa-no-taifu Masakatsu-sama being granted without alteration to Takumi-no-kami Masahira-sama (on the 21st of last month), messenger Tōdō Kageyu-zaemon was dispatched from Takumi-no-kami Masahira-sama to inform of this matter, arriving in Fukui today.

One: On the 27th day of the same month, Honjō magistrate Taga Matashirō-dono requested that a diagram showing the tsubo area of the Honjō residence be submitted due to the current revision of Edo maps, with the message arriving yesterday, the 26th, to the caretaker Hara Heizaemon Masatake. Accordingly, the residence diagram was submitted by Heizaemon today.

One: On the 4th day of the 10th month, for expressing gratitude that Takumi-no-kami Masahira-sama was granted family headship without alteration, messenger Sakakibara Sukezaemon Takahiro was dispatched to Edo, departing from Fukui today.

One: On the 14th day of the same month, the same messenger for gratitude, Sakakibara Sukezaemon Takahiro, arrived in Edo this morning and presented himself to the duty officer Toda Yamashiro-no-kami Tadamasa-dono (accompanied by caretaker Hara Heizaemon), submitting the official letter.
However, a reply letter was delivered on the 16th of the same month.

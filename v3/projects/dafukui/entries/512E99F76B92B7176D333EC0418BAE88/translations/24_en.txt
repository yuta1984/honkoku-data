One: March 10th - From private funds, five sets of three-piece items and five sets of two-piece items (commissioned from carver Watō and completed) were presented (upon inquiry). They were submitted to Senior Councilor Tsuchiya Sagami-no-kami Masanao through messenger and deputy castellan Murakami Santayū Masamine. A written notice came from Sagami-no-kami stating that these presentation items were discussed with Chamberlain Makino Bingo-no-kami and the presentation was completed.

One: April 14th - Through the imperial envoy Senior Councilor Tsuchiya Sagami-no-kami Masanao, permission to return to the domain was granted, and as customary, white silver and a lined robe were received as gifts.

When Sagami-no-kami came:
Chōkitsu-sama accompanied him and went out to welcome him to just outside the front gate entrance (aside from this, the treatment of the imperial envoy was as usual). After Sagami-no-kami's departure, courtesy visits were made to the Senior Councilors and Chamberlains as thanks.

One: 15th of the same month (6:30) - Made a castle visit, offered thanks for the leave permission, received a horse as customary gift, and upon departure, made courtesy visits to the Chamberlains and Junior Councilors as thanks.

One: 18th of the same month - The gifted horse was brought by messenger from Senior Councilor Ōkubo Kaga-no-kami Tada[?].

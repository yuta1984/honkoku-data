[The lord] performed his duties to the chamberlain (gosoba goyōnin).

One: December 18th - For year-end congratulations, through a messenger as usual, a small-sleeved kimono (kosode) was presented and white silver was offered.

One: Same month, 26th day - Regarding the announcement of Takumi-sama's court rank promotion, for congratulations on this matter, [the lord] performed his duties to the duty officer Ōkubo Kaga-no-kami Tadatomo-dono and chamberlain Makino Bingo-no-kami Sada-dono.

One: Same month, last day - Fresh cod for winter presentation, both first and second grade, arrived this evening after seven o'clock, but since presentation tomorrow would be difficult, after reporting this matter to duty officer Ōkubo Kaga-no-kami Tadatomo-dono, the presentation was not made.

One: Same day
Regarding the matter of Chōkitsu-sama's New Year courtesy call, when inquiry was made, instructions were given to visit the castle on January 3rd.

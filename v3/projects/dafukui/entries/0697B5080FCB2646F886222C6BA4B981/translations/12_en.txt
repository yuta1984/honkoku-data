　　　　　　　Memorandum

　　　　　20,000 bales of relief rice 《marginal note: each containing 3 to 5 shō》
　　　　　shall be granted from this year of the Tiger. Henceforth, for record-keeping purposes,
　　　　　endorsement certificates with joint seals shall be issued. The above.

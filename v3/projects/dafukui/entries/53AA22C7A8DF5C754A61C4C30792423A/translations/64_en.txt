and five hundred bundles of cotton. The senior retainer Nagami Shima Yoshihiro presented a sword, silver, horse money, and ten bundles of ceremonial paper,
Iida Shukome Yasunari presented a sword, silver, horse money, and two hundred candles, and was granted audience. After completion,
upon departure, as thanks for this, he made courtesy calls to the senior councilors, junior councilors, and Lord Sakai Kawachi-no-kami Tadataka

30th day of the 12th month, Enpō 2nd year
Presented by Daigeki concurrently Kamō-no-kami and Sake-no-kami, Nakahara Asomi Morosada

1. On the 24th day of the 3rd month, the first anniversary memorial service for Lord Daian-in was conducted at Jōkō-in from the 20th until today.

However, at Tentoku-ji in Nishikubo as well, memorial services were conducted from the evening of the 23rd until this morning, sponsored by Princess Fuyo.

1. On the 13th day of the 4th month, official letters came from Senior Councilors Lord Inaba Mino-no-kami Masanori and Lord Tsuchiya Tajima-no-kami Kazunao. Regarding the 17th day: as the 25th anniversary memorial service for Lord Daiyū-in is being conducted, after returning from Momijiyama, one should visit Momijiyama wearing eboshi and hitatare court dress. For the 20th day, they requested advance participation at the Buddha hall of Lord Daiyū-in at Tōei-zan.

1. On the 17th day of the same month, due to indisposition, the visit to Momijiyama did not take place.

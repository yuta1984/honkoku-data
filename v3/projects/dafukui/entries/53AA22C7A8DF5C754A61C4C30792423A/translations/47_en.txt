Inaba Mino-no-kami Masanori (seal)

Lord Matsudaira Hyōbu-taifu

1st, 7th month 19th day: Through a messenger, the first mackerel of the domain's produce was presented as customary.

1st, same day: Sumo wrestling was observed at the fountain estate (Osensui residence).

1st, 8th month 1st day: For congratulations, through messenger and military officer Ishikawa Denemon, a ceremonial sword and horse substitute (ten ryō of gold) were presented.

1st, same day: After receiving formal greetings at the main castle, all the townspeople were granted an audience. As customary, cotton and delicacies were presented.

1st, same month 3rd day: At the fountain estate, spear techniques by disciples of Nakamura Gondayū Naoyuki, Miyakita Chōemon Sadashige, Arakawa Hikotayū, and Keimasu Danemon (retainer of Sakai Genba) were observed.

1st, same month 13th day: Messenger Yamada Ichirōemon was dispatched to Edo to inquire about the Shogun's health and well-being.

1st, Regarding Tsunamasa-sama's court appointment, Yoriai Hishinuma Ichizaemon Ieji carried an official letter from the Senior Councilors to Kyoto Deputy Nagai Iga-no-kami Naoyasu. He departed from Fukui today.

1st, On the 23rd day of the 12th month, regarding the recent (25th day of last month)
fire at Hon'in Palace, messenger Ōida Kuemon was dispatched to Kyoto
as a gesture of sympathy.
Ten bundles of official letter paper were presented to Hon'in Palace. Kuemon departed from Fukui today.

1st, On the same day, Tokumaru-sama (eldest son of Echigo-no-kami Mitsunaga-sama, actually the second son) underwent his coming-of-age ceremony and was appointed to Junior Fourth Rank Lower Grade, Chamberlain, and was named
Mikawa-no-kami Tsunakuni (receiving one character from [the lord's] name)

1st, On the 26th day of the same month, Gonzō-sama received court rank and changed his name to Bitchū-no-kami Naokata

1st, On the same day, Dewa-no-kami Tsunachika-sama was appointed Chamberlain, Wakasa-no-kami Naoaki-sama was promoted to Junior Fourth Rank Lower Grade, and Daisuke Chikitoki-sama
(eldest son of Kōzuke-no-suke Chikanaga-sama) received court rank and was renamed Shikibu-shōyū

1st, On the 27th day of the same month, for year-end congratulations, five court robes were presented through a messenger as usual
Later, an inner letter was delivered at the castle. Gifts to retainers etc. were as usual

1st, On the same day, for the same purpose, ten pieces of silver were presented to Her Ladyship through a messenger

1st, On the 28th day of the same month, for the winter season, second-grade fresh codfish from the domain was presented through a messenger as usual

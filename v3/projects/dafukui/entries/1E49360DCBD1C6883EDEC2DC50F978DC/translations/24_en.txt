Genna 6, Year of the Metal Monkey (1620)
　- April 4th: Princess Hana departed from the Tatsunokuchi residence and arrived at Takada Castle in Echigo Province on the 14th of the same month
　- May 15th: [The lord] returned to Takada Castle in Echigo Province from Edo
　　　　Note: The departure date from Edo is unknown
　- June 1st: To celebrate the completion of the marriage arrangements, meals were bestowed upon the members of the household retainers, and Princess Hana was granted audiences with the chief retainers and other leading figures

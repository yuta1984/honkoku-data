1. 7th month, 12th day, Hiratsuka Seibei arrived in Fukui accompanied by Eiheiji's Soten,
　　whereupon through Takada Tōtōmi-no-kami (Kazuhide), Sano Shikibu (Kagetoshi), and Shimada Kura-nosuke (Shigekazu), the contents of the official letter
　　were conveyed to Soten, and he was ordered to submit a written pledge. He submitted the following:
　　　　　This time in Edo, this humble monk's case was adjudicated, and due to my misconduct,
　　　　　by higher order I am expelled from Eiheiji, and furthermore am prohibited from residing in Echizen Province,
　　　　　Kantō, Chūkyō, Osaka, Owari Province, Kii Province, and Suruga Province.

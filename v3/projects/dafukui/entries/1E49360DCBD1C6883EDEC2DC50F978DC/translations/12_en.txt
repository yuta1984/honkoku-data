　　一　1st month, 3rd day:
　　　　The Divine Lord departed from Kyoto to return to Suruga Castle (returned on the 14th day of the 2nd month)
　　一　Same month, 11th day: At the Okayama camp
　　　　In the presence of Lord Hidetada
　　　　Lord Toranosuke underwent his coming-of-age ceremony, was appointed to the court rank of Junior Fifth Rank Lower Grade, Chamberlain concurrent Governor of Iyo Province, received
　　　　an honorary name and came to be called Tadamasa (age 19)

【Right Page】
　　- July 27th
　　　　Lord Iemitsu received the shogunal proclamation
　　- August 6th
　　　　Lord Iemitsu made a court visit, and on this occasion
　　　　Lord Tadamasa accompanied him
　　- Same day　Lord Dewa-no-kami (Naomasa) was granted the court rank of Junior Fourth Rank Lower Grade
　　- 9th day of the same month　Lord Nagami Minbu passed away in Takada, Echigo Province, and was buried at
　　　　Tōkōji Temple on Mount Fuji in the same location. His posthumous Buddhist name was
　　　　Kōgenin-den Yūzan-sōei-daikoji

1. 10th month, 10th day
　　Regarding the betrothal of Tadanao-sama's daughter, Princess Kame, to Takamatsu-dono (Prince Yoshihito),
　　messenger and guard captain Rikimaru Tōzaemon (Harutoki) was dispatched to Edo. One hundred pieces of silver,

Genna 4th year, Year of the Earth Horse
　One　1st month, 11th day　An engagement was arranged with
　　　his younger sister Lady Hana-hime (initially called Nagamitsu-hime, actually the daughter of Sakyo-no-daibu Yukinaga, born in Keichō 12th year), who was the adopted daughter of Lord Asano Tajima-no-kami Nagaakira, lord of Hiroshima Castle in Aki Province (the matchmaker was Lord Inaba Takumi-no-kami Masanari)
　One　3rd month (day unclear)　Received Takada Castle in Kubiki District, Echigo Province (domain worth 250,000 koku) (at age 22)
　　　At this time, by imperial command, Inaba Takumi-no-kami Masanari was appointed as retainer (original family name Ochi, former domain 10,000 koku, at this time received an additional 10,000 koku at Itoigawa in Echigo)
　　　(total domain 20,000 koku)
　　　　　　However, the month and day of the first entry into the domain thereafter are unclear
　One　4th month, 1st day　Formal betrothal gifts were presented to Lady Hana-hime

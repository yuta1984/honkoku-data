　　1. On January 11th, there was a celebration for the first accounting ceremony, attended by senior councilors, along with Makino Tango-no-kami Yasusada,
　　　　Takada Tōtōmi-no-kami Kazuhide, Nomura Jūdayū Tomoaki, magistrates, inspectors, and others.
　　1. On the 15th of the same month, in the great hall, the deputies for rice stipend recipients brought 100 hiki each as sake barrel fees to
　　　　pay their respects. They were served flowing sake cups and long abalone

○By special intention, a service fee of one monme of silver per ten kanme of metal sold by metals wholesalers from ships arriving from other provinces has been ordered.
Understanding the above, payment shall be made without delay.
1. Monthly metal trading amounts shall be recorded in daily ledgers, and service fees shall be paid to the Mikuni Port Administrative Office by the 5th of the following month.
1. It is strictly forbidden to obstruct trade by claiming service fees, or to devise promissory notes for excessive profits, or to plan secret trading - all actions that would hinder the official intention.
1. Since metals wholesalers in the castle town have been discontinued, the regulations issued six years ago in the year of the Ox shall not be applied.
As above, this shall be communicated first to Mikuni Port wholesalers and all others who directly purchase and sell metals arriving from other provinces. Should anyone fail to comply with the official intention, they will be investigated and punished accordingly, so this matter shall be communicated. Note: Those who purchase from wholesalers for retail need not pay the service fee.

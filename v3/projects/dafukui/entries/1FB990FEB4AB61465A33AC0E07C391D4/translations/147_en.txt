Year 1: Bunka 12, Year of the Boar

○Regarding formal greetings by goyaku (attendant officials) when the lord departs from the castle
1. When a goyaku from the shinban-gumi (new guard unit) is appointed to join the goshoin-ban (chamberlain guard) and continues to serve as goyaku, if they participate in the lord's retinue for the first time during his departure, they shall offer formal greetings through the introduction by the ashigaru-gashira (foot soldier commander)
1. Even if the aforementioned individuals are appointed during the lord's absence, when they first join the retinue after the lord's return to the castle, they shall still offer formal greetings
1. When goyaku receive salary increases or other favorable appointments, and subsequently join the lord's retinue for the first time during his departure, they have been offering formal greetings through the ashigaru-gashira's introduction, but such greetings shall henceforth be discontinued
　　However, even when appointed as goyaku or to join the goshoin-ban from the daioban (great guard), goryusuban (castle keeper), unranked positions, or goryusuban-gumi, no formal greeting is required
1. Even when appointed as goyaku while remaining in the goshoin-ban-gumi, no formal greeting is required
1. When a goshoin-ban goyaku is granted retirement and his son is appointed as goyaku and enters the goshoin-ban-gumi, no formal greeting is required either

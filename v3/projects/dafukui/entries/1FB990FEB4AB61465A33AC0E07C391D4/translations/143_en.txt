1. Bunka 10, Year of the Rooster (1813)
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Shimada Kyūma
○For the Great Guard units (ōgoban-gumi) that are short-staffed: During the biennial guard duty assignments, after examination, personnel shall be assigned from the unemployed reserve guard units (gorususuban-gumi). This matter has been announced for your knowledge and understanding.
　　　　　　　April 1st

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Chief Administrator
○It has come to our attention that temples have recently been improperly using the lord's family crest, which is inappropriate conduct. Particularly regarding vestments bearing the lord's crest: it has been newly decreed that they shall not be used henceforth.
In addition to the above, this shall be communicated to all temples under jurisdiction except for the family temple.
　　　　　　　May 16th

In Edo
○Regarding the young men on permanent residence duty who have been devoted to martial arts since previous years: It is the lord's wish that they should consult about tightening the practice halls and make even more diligent efforts without fail. This matter shall be communicated to the relevant parties.
　　　　　　　August 1st

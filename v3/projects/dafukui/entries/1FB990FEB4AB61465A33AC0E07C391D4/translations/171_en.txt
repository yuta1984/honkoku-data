1. Two night watchmen from among the staff under the women's quarters administrator are ordered to serve.

　　　For Reiganjima:
1. The two third-ward fire brigades are temporarily exempted from regular duty and ordered to patrol the mansion twice during the day and twice at night.
   However, the accompanying personnel shall be four people from among the cleaning staff and new servants, working in shifts day and night assigned to the third-ward duties.

1. For the shortage of guard personnel due to the above, unpaid employment is ordered.

1. Two ashigaru inspectors each are assigned to night watch duty and ordered to patrol day and night.

1. For the shortage of ashigaru inspectors due to the above, assistance from the ashigaru is ordered.

1. Since the mansion is currently under repair with daily comings and goings, two night watchmen are assigned to the construction office to patrol day and night.

1. Personnel from the inspector units and advance musket units are ordered to increase their day and night patrols.
   However, if there are difficulties due to personnel shortage, two floating personnel shall be deployed to serve.

　　　　　　　　　　　　To the chief administrator

○Last summer, fire watch duty at Zojoji Temple was ordered, making it unavoidable to impose silver payments from July, which was considered a great burden. However, due to the Korean embassy visit, the shogunate has ordered special levies and provincial duties. Therefore, from next Hitsuji year, these provincial duties will be imposed, and the silver payments from this July are hereby exempted. Details of the provincial duty allocation will be communicated later by the magistrates.
　　　　　　June 11th　　　　　　Also to Fuchu

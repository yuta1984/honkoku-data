　　　20th day of 1st month
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Administrative heads in attendance

○As informed to the administrative heads, assistance duties have been ordered this time, but the amount of tribute money is excessive, causing concern about financing methods. Regarding towns and rural areas, procurement funds have been ordered in recent years through various financial measures, and their diligent service has been satisfactory. Although their current state of hardship has reached His Lordship's attention and causes him great concern, due to the excessive tribute money, half-reduced borrowed rice and other measures have again been ordered for retainers, and with no other alternatives, service money must be levied. Since official duties are of utmost importance as national service, everyone should work diligently.

○This time, a fire broke out from the inner palace kitchen and all buildings were completely burned down. The various lords' successive kind orders regarding this matter are gratefully received. Especially, the assistance duty previously ordered has now been exempted. After such duties had been ordered for the first time in a long while, due to this unfortunate incident, the exemption has been granted. Though this may seem regrettable, with major expenditures looming and not anticipating such heavy official duties, the prompt exemption and peace of mind are gratefully received.

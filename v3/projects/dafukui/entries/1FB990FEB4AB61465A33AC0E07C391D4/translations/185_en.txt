To be announced to each respective party　　　Chief Retainer
In Edo　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Magistrate
○Due to the domain's finances reaching a state of extreme difficulty and obstruction, comprehensive austerity measures are being ordered. Administrative offices should investigate with sincerity and take care to significantly reduce expenses. Furthermore, if there are ideas for profit-increasing measures, these should be reported. According to the austerity measures ordered this time, detailed instructions should be given down to the lowest levels, and above all, fiscal management should be conducted well and with utmost integrity. If it is heard that various departments are being negligent in their handling of matters due to adherence to past customs or formalities, this will certainly result in official censure. Therefore, one should prioritize the domain's benefit and work diligently.
　The above points should be thoroughly communicated to each respective party.

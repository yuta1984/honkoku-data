Careful consideration shall be given to promotions and they shall be arranged accordingly
1. Intermediate officials who go to both residences for external inspections and official business at both residences have been provided lunch, but this shall be discontinued henceforth. However, for temple inspections and other distant duties, as before, five go of lunch rice shall be provided
1. Those serving construction shifts at the upper residence compound have been provided lunch until now, but this shall be discontinued henceforth
　　　However, those serving full-day shifts for construction work at Reiganjima shall continue to receive lunch as before. For extraordinary construction projects, those appointed to special duties who remain on duty all day have received two lunches until now, but henceforth shall receive one lunch each
1. Evening shift extra lunches for construction workers shall be discontinued entirely
　　　However, when serving into the night, extra lunch shall be provided
1. Boat handlers: fifteen persons
　　　Ten regular personnel including: one squad leader, one quarters guard,
　　　　　　　　　one advance guard, three messengers, four gate guards
　　　　However, three of the messengers shall each receive ten extra lunches per month
　　　One tatami maker
　　　Since this tatami maker receives stipend for two persons, regular lunch is discontinued; lunch shall be provided only as work fee when performing tatami-making duties
　　Four daily duty workers

　　These have received continuation allowances until now, but this is discontinued; no matter how many times per day they are employed, each person shall receive ten extra lunches per month
1. Fire prevention personnel: twenty-five persons
　　Three regular personnel including: one squad leader, one quarters guard, one palace guard
　　Two roof specialists
　　Since these roof specialists receive stipend for two persons, regular duty lunch is discontinued; lunch shall be provided as work fee only when performing roof tiling duties
　　Three banner bearer assistants
　　These have received regular duty lunch until now, but henceforth shall receive fifteen lunches per person per month
　　　　However, these three persons shall serve regular daily duties. When serving daily duties, they shall receive one lunch at that time in addition to the fifteen
　　One plasterer
　　　Since this person serves as substitute for craftsmen with daily duties, five go lunch shall be provided as before
　　Nineteen daily duty workers, including three who also serve as banner bearers
　　　These daily duty workers, no matter how many times employed for duties within the upper residence, shall no longer receive continuation allowances; each shall receive ten extra lunches per month

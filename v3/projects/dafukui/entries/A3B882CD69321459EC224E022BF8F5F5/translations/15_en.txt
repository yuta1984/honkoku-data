1. When offering congratulations for succession, even if congratulations are offered through a representative, would it be possible for retainers to be granted an audience? We humbly inquire about your inner intention regarding this matter. Above
　　　　　　　　　Intercalary 4th month, 23rd day　　Matsudaira Senjirō

When the above was submitted, regarding the matter of retainers' audience, the following guidance was given by means of an attached note:

　　　　　　Regarding retainers when succession congratulations are offered through a representative, it is not necessary to present them. When you next make a request for an audience, you should inquire at that time.

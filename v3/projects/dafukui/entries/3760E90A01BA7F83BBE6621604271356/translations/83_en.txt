1. Kaei 4 (Year of the Boar), 4th month: After consulting with the senior councilors and receiving instructions, the following was sent to Edo on the 6th of the same month:
　1. When group members are disciplined and require substitution, both the main head (honzu) and acting head (karigatashira) must submit inquiries from both Edo and the domain
　1. When various unit commanders in the domain temporarily take custody of colleagues' group members who are ordered confined, inquiries must be submitted as with one's own group
　　However, main heads in Edo need not submit inquiries. But if confinement is ordered in the domain for misconduct before the main head's departure, only the main head in Edo must submit an inquiry.
　　　If confinement is ordered in Edo for misconduct before a group member's departure, the main head remaining in the domain must submit an inquiry
　1. In Edo, when colleagues' groups other than one's direct group and their subordinates are ordered substituted, no inquiry need be submitted
　　However, if severe punishments such as expulsion from the gate or higher are ordered, an inquiry must be submitted. When ninja supervisory heads, escort heads, and other supervisory personnel are ordered substituted,
　　　inquiries must be submitted only when those persons are under their direct command at the time

　　　　　　　　The matter of the mid-summer greetings inquiry is also understood, so it is recorded here.
1. 7th month, 6th day: For the celebration of the birth anniversary, gold was offered through a messenger as usual.
1. 8th month, 5th day: Regarding next year's
　　pilgrimage by the Shogun to Nikko-san, an inquiry was made through Samue Jinzaemon to the duty magistrate Inaba Mino-no-kami regarding the desire to offer sword cases and document boxes, but
　　instructions were given that such offerings from daimyo are not customary and therefore unnecessary.
1. 8th month {{marginal note: day unclear}}: Regarding the matter of currency gold and silver, a request was made to use silver notes within the domain, and
　　as the request was granted as petitioned, it was ordered that silver notes be used henceforth.

【Right Page】
　　　　1. All clothing, kosode robes, and katabira summer robes with Kyoto dyeing, gold/silver leaf, and tie-dye patterns, including all obi sashes are
　　　　　prohibited. However, items already brought are acceptable
　　　　1. Night robes and futon bedding should all use silk and pongee from this domain
　　　　1. Female servants and young maids: 6 people in total
　　　　　　Additionally, female servants wearing silk obi received from their masters is prohibited

1. Same day: Upon returning to the domain,
　　messenger Makino Kurōzaemon was sent to Kyoto to inquire after the health of
　　the Nyōin Imperial Palace, and as customary, 10 bundles of ceremonial paper and 30 skewered mackerel were presented

1. Same month, 8th: Departed for falconry to Mikuni in Sakai District (returned to castle on the 13th of the same month)

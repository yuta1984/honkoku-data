Near the New Mansion area, an idle samurai beckoned him over and called him inside the gate. "Do you buy any kind of goods?" he asked. When the merchant replied "I buy anything," the samurai showed him a large water jar (Oda bottle) that was finely cracked and asked "How much will you pay for this?"

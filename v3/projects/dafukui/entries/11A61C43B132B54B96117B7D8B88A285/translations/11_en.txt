To Utsunomiya Yamato-no-kami

Regarding the suppression of rebels in the northern provinces,
you should hasten to participate without delay and
render military service. This document is as stated.
　　　　　　　　　　[monogram]
　Jōwa 2nd year
　　　　5th month, 2nd day
　　　To Utsunomiya Yamato-no-kami

Hereby granted to Utsunomiya Sama-no-gon-no-kami Naonao
　The position of shugo (military governor) of
　　Shimotsuke, Echigo, Buzen, and
　　Toyoda District of Shimōsa Province
The above should be administered in accordance with the deed of transfer from his father Dewa-no-kami Morotsuna. This document is as stated.
　　Kōan 2nd year, 1st month, 7th day

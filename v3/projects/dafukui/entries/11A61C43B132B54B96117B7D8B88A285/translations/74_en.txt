"Seeing the front cart overturn serves as a warning for the following cart" - this was said by the ancients. However, in recent times, with manufacturing machinery in general, the convenience of sea and land transportation, the technology of steam engines and railways, horse-drawn carriages and rickshaws becoming freely available, everything has become a world of vehicles. Therefore, one should devote even more attention to family business, strengthen the axle's core metal, firmly drive in the wedge of the heart, improve household management, work diligently in an honest livelihood, understand the principles of samsara, and never ride the demon cart of the next world.

　　　　　Matters of Aspirations Befitting One's Station

It is said that there is no person who does not have hopes appropriate to their respective station in life. Among such cases, there is one interesting story. A townsman from the same era mentioned earlier said, "In my lifetime, I want to have the metal body of the breathing staff, which is the most important tool for making a living, made of pure silver." Everyone admired this as an aspiration befitting one's station.

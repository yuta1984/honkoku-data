First, Ansei 5th year, Uma year (1858)
　　From May it began to fall, in June rice certificates were expensive at about 89.5 monme per hyō
　　In July it reached 96-97 monme, and from July 12th the weather became increasingly hot

First, same year, December
　　Official rice payment: 5 gō of white rice per 1 monme of silver　One hyō: 80 monme of silver, accordingly
　　Rice certificates declined slightly　Gold market rate same as before: 50 mon per monme

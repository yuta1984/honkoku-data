Origins and History of Ancient Books Related to Commercial Law
Records written in succession based on what was seen and heard

1. Imperial Edict from Emperor Go-Hanazono　　With the crest of tachibana (orange blossom) and golden treasure

It is hereby declared and granted that medicine merchants may establish trading posts throughout the provinces.
The above, following previous regulations, has been established in the current reign as well.
Beginning with Tsubota Market in Chikushi and extending to markets, towns, and villages throughout the provinces,
there shall be no objection to conducting trade. If anyone
causes violations or disturbances regarding this matter, regardless of what kind of merchant they may be, they shall be subject to criminal punishment.

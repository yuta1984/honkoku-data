　　　　　　Memorandum of oral statement
　　　　Regarding visits to Ueno Zōjōji Temple and other places, when returning from such visits, may we
　　　　send a messenger to inquire after [His Lordship's] health in the same manner as heya-zumi (junior retainers), please provide
　　　　your guidance on this matter. The above
　　　　　1st month, 5th day　　　　　Matsudaira Ōi-no-kami

1. 1st month, 11th day - There was a summons from Rōjū Akimoto Tajima-no-kami Takaasa-dono, and when rusuigokari Murakami Sandayū Masamine
　　went there, he was informed that regarding the monthly castle attendance that had been requested last December, it would be granted as requested,
　　and the following written notice was handed over
[Left page]
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Matsudaira Ōi-no-kami
　　　　Shall attend castle for monthly courtesy calls
　　　　　Regarding courtesy calls to each [official], shall visit after having been granted audience

1. Same month, 13th day - Regarding the matter of receiving the kudashi (imperial oral decree), messenger yoriai Matsubara Hachizaemon Yoshio was ordered to carry
　　the official letter from the Rōjū to the Kyoto Shoshidai and was dispatched to Kyoto. He departed from Fukui today

1. Same month, 15th day - First monthly castle attendance, paid respects

1. Same month, 18th day - Messenger Matsubara Hachizaemon Yoshio arrived in Kyoto on the 16th, and today went to Shoshidai
　　Matsudaira Kii-no-kami Nobuyasu-dono and submitted the letter along with the official correspondence from the Rōjū

One　12th month, 28th day
[Left page]
　　Together with Lord Yoshishina, went to the castle {{wearing long hakama}}, offered thanks for the appointment
　　Presented ceremonial sword and horse substitute money {{ten pieces of gold}} and five court robes
　　To the Middle Counselor, presented ceremonial sword and horse substitute money {{ten pieces of silver}}, to the Honorable Wife, the First Rank Lady,
　　and the Honorable Ladies of the Inner Palace, presented five pieces of silver each. Upon departure, made courtesy visits to the senior councilors and chamberlain
　　attendants, and junior councilors
　　　However, at this time there were gifts to the senior ladies-in-waiting of the castle, but these are not recorded

One　Same day, the messenger for receiving the imperial verbal decree, yoriai Takada Kozaemon {{Kazuuji}}, who had arrived in Kyoto yesterday on the 27th,
　　was dispatched from Edo. He went to the Kyoto Deputy Lord Matsudaira Kii-no-kami {{Nobuyasu}}-dono, and submitted letters and
　　official letters from the senior councilors {{accompanied by Honda [?]bei}}. From there to the court liaison Yanagiwara Dainagon {{Sukekado}}-dono

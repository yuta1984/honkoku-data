1. On February 2nd, for the celebration of Princess Yae's safe delivery, all retainers were summoned {{as notified by the senior inspectors}}, went to the castle, then went to the Western Castle and had an audience with the senior councilors

1. On March 7th, for the celebration of the completion of the proclamation of crown prince investiture and empress enthronement, went to the castle {{as notified by the senior inspectors}}, had an audience with the senior councilors, then went to the Western Castle

1. On the 12th of the same month, regarding the 8th when
　　the Imperial Palace caught fire, for inquiring after His Majesty's well-being, all retainers were summoned {{as notified by the senior inspectors}}, went to both castles and had an audience with the senior councilors

1. On the 28th of the same month, during the monthly castle visit, with the senior councilors in attendance, it was announced that Princess Matsu, younger sister of Lord Owari Chunagon,
[Left page]
　　had been declared an adopted daughter of the Shogun, and to celebrate this, also went to the Western Castle

1. On April 10th, the engagement between Princess Matsu and Lord Matsudaira Wakasa-no-kami {{Yoshinori}} {{eldest son of Lord Kaga Saishō Akinori}} was announced, and for celebration all retainers were summoned, went to the castle to offer congratulations, then went to the Western Castle, and after returning to the residence, again as Lord Yoshishina's representative, attended to duties with duty officer Lord Tsuchiya Sagami-no-kami {{Masanao}}, Western Castle duty officer Lord Ogasawara Sado-no-kami {{Nagashige}}, and the chamberlain attendants

1. On May 5th, Princess Ume arrived in Fukui {{with senior retainer Matsudaira Shōbei as escort}}
　　　　However, she departed from Kyoto on the 29th of last month and traveled via the Eastern Ōmi route

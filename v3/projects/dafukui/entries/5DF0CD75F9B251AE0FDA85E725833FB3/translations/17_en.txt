1. 1st month, 26th day - Regarding the summons to the residence of the Densō-shū, messenger Matsubara Hachizaemon Yoshio went there
　　and found that Former Dainagon Takano Yasuharu-dono and Former Dainagon Yanagiwara Sukekane-dono were in attendance, and the kudashi (imperial oral decree) was
　　presented. The official gifts presented on that occasion were as follows, and were handed over to the zassho:
　　　　　For promotion to Junior Fifth Rank Lower, Shōdayū
　　　To the Imperial Palace: one gold piece
　　　To Sentō Imperial Palace: three silver pieces
　　　To Nyōin Imperial Palace: three pieces (same)
　　　To Jungo's quarters: three pieces (same)
　　　To Nyōgo's quarters: three pieces (same)

　　　To Naishi-dokoro: forty monme (same)

　　　　　For promotion to Junior Fourth Rank
　　　To the Imperial Palace: one gold piece
　　　To Sentō Imperial Palace: three silver pieces
　　　To Nyōin Imperial Palace: three pieces (same)
　　　To Jungo's quarters: three pieces (same)
　　　To Nyōgo's quarters: three pieces (same)
　　　To Naishi-dokoro: forty monme (same)

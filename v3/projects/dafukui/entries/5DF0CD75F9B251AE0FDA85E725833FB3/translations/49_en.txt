1. On the 25th day of the ninth month, he went out to Yoshie for falconry {{returned on the last day of the same month}}.

1. On the 27th day of the same month, he observed archery by his retainers at the main citadel.

1. On the 12th day of the tenth month, he went out to Aizu for sightseeing {{returned on the 16th day of the same month}}.

1. On the 6th day of the same month
   Due to the Shōgun's illness, a letter was sent to Edo to inquire about his health.
　　　　However, a reply was received on the 14th day of the same month.

1. On the 10th day of the same month, regarding the timing of the sankin-kōtai attendance for the coming year of the Boar,
   when Lord Yoshishina made an inquiry, instructions were given that he should go to the capital during the third month.

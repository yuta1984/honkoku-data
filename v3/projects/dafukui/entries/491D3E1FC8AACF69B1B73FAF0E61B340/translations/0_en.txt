Regarding the Borrowing of Silver

One item: Silver amount of 200 me (principal silver)
I hereby truly and certainly borrow the aforementioned silver. As collateral for this loan, I pledge all of my share of one plot of sloped field in Yuritani within Ōhira, without exception. The repayment of this silver shall be settled without fail by the coming eleventh month. Should the silver repayment be delayed even slightly, I will transfer the collateral field to the guarantors and ensure prompt resolution. This certificate is executed for future reference.

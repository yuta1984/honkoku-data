　Intercalary 10th month

1. On the 3rd day of the 11th month, to Zenjirō-sama (marginal note: Naoyoshi), the inherited domain (marginal note: Echigo Itoigawa, 10,000 koku) of Ōmi-no-kami-sama (marginal note: Naoyuki) was granted without alteration as proclaimed. For this, as gratitude, a deputy was sent as messenger to the rōjū (senior councillors).

　Regarding Hinaga Peak within the three villages of Kami, Naka, and Shimo Hirabuki in Nanjō District, Echizen Province - in which direction within these three villages is it located? One map is being sent, so please mark east and west on this map, draw the shape of Hinaga Peak, and submit it.

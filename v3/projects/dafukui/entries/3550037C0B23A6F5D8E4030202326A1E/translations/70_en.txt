One, On the 12th day of the 6th month, at Unshō-ji temple, from the evening of yesterday the 11th until this morning, the seventh anniversary memorial service for Lord Seishōin was conducted in advance.
　Note: Though the proper date would be the 14th day of the 7th month, since it would fall after the departure, it was conducted in advance.

One, On the 14th day of the 6th month, at Reigan-ji temple in Fukagawa, the seventh anniversary memorial service for Lord Seishōin was conducted in advance.
　Note: Since it would fall during the Obon period, it was conducted in advance.

One, On the 18th day of the 6th month, for summer greetings to inquire about [his lord's] health, the messenger Samie Kyūzaemon was sent to Edo, and one type of fish delicacy was presented. Kyūzaemon today [returned to] Fukui

1. On the 12th day of the 4th month, as the inspection commissioners (junkenshi) departed from Fukui, the senior retainers Sakai Genba (Tomofusa), Honda Shuri (Tsunehisa), and Inaba Uneme (Masakata) went out to the Kaga-guchi gate to see them off.

1. On the 14th day of the 4th month, the inspection commissioners, who had stayed overnight at Natsume-go, were delayed due to rainy weather.

1. On the 16th day of the 4th month (at 4 o'clock at night), a fire broke out from the servant quarters of a person called Aburaya Kiemon in Shimoteramachi. Due to strong south winds, the fire spread to Nishiyama-cho, and 110 houses were destroyed.

1. On the 19th day of the 4th month, [the lord] performed his duty of audience with Senior Councilor Kuze Yamato-no-kami (Shigeyuki).

1. On the 20th day of the 4th month, as the inspection commissioners proceeded from their stay in Kanazu to Kaga Province, the officials who had been assigned to provide hospitality went out to see them off, and the physicians saw them off to the provincial border before returning.

1. On the 21st day of the 4th month, to Goyōban Inoue Kawachi-no-kami (Masamine), a written document was submitted through messenger and resident agent Ozeki Shingozaemon (Naomi) regarding the presentation of Honda Magotaro (Nagakazu)'s posthumous effects and Ōkura (Naganori)'s succession礼, as follows:

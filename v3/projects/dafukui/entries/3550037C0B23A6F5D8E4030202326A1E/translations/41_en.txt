10th month of the Year of the Rooster
One: 4th day of 11th month - Regarding the upcoming sankin-kōtai duty in the Year of the Dog, messenger and guard officer
　Yamamoto Uemonhachi《Shigenori》 was dispatched to Edo for inquiry. He departed Fukui today
One: 11th day of 11th month - The domain registry catalog arrived via foot messenger《Kawachi Nakaemon》, and
　a letter of gratitude《dated the 13th》 was sent to Edo for receiving it
One: 15th day of 11th month - The messenger for sankin-kōtai inquiry, Yamamoto Uemonhachi《Shigenori》, arrived in Edo
　and visited the duty officer Lord Kuze Yamato-no-kami《Shigeyuki》《accompanied by caretaker Ōzeki Shingorōzaemon》 to present the letter
One: 19th day of 11th month - Lord Kuze Yamato-no-kami《Shigeyuki》, the duty officer, summoned messenger Yamamoto
　Uemonhachi《Shigenori》, who appeared and received an official letter stating that he should come to Edo

　during the 8th month of the coming Year of the Dog
One: 21st day of 11th month - Regarding the directive issued in the 6th month of this year, documents for the upper mansion
　were previously submitted, but because government officials had increased in number and there were many documents,
　it was postponed. Today, documents for the upper mansion and the Sunamura Shinden subsidiary estate were
　submitted as follows. Caretaker Ōzeki Shingorōzaemon《Naomi》 carried them and presented them to Lord Shimada Sado-no-kami《Masanobu》
　　　　　Memorandum
　　　Granted　　　　　Within Tokiwabashi

　　　　　Tori 8th month (August)
One: September 3rd - Tsuda Denshichi (Munenori) and Tsuda Sanroku demonstrated rifle shooting with 100 shots. Upon viewing,
　Sanroku hit all 100 shots, so as a reward he was given a lined kimono and kamishimo; Denshichi hit 98 out of 100 shots, so he was given one lined kimono
One: Same day - For the Chrysanthemum Festival celebration, a kosode was presented as usual through a messenger
One: September 6th - Sumo wrestlers from other domains were viewed at Kawabata tea house, and viewing was ordered for everyone from senior councilors down to magistrates and attendants
One: September 12th - For receiving the official document, Nakatsukasamaru Daibu (Masahira)-sama went as proxy to

　the castle, where at the Black Study
　the Shogun appeared, and the proxies of Matsudaira Kaga-no-kami (Tsunanori), Matsudaira Satsuma-no-kami (Yoshisada), Matsudaira Higo-no-kami (Masayoshi),
　and Matsudaira Shimosa-no-kami (Tadamasa) were seated in order. Through the announcement of Master of Ceremonies Miura
　Iki-no-kami (Akiyoshi), they offered collective obeisance from the first tatami mat inside the threshold,
　and received the Shogun's words. Afterward, Senior Councilor Abe Bungo-no-kami (Masataka) handed over the official
　documents in order, and after receiving them and withdrawing, courtesy visits were made to the senior councilors and
　junior councilors. Then returning to this mansion, the official document was viewed, and after ordering the senior councilor and other officials to view it, it was handed over to Senior Councilor Sakai Genba (Tomofusa)

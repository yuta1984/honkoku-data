　Matsudaira Tsushima-no-kami's residence within Shōhei Bridge, 
　Sakai Saemon-suke's residence within Kanda Bridge

Fire prevention positions from Hongō direction to Hamachō:
　In front of vacant land outside Sujikai Bridge, Honda Karanouke's residence
　Yanagihara embankment road, Koyanagi-chō, Shirogane-chō embankment road, Kodenma-chō

Fire prevention positions in case of fires from the Koishikawa direction:
　Matsudaira Sanuki-no-kami's residence within Koishikawa Gate, Ogawa-chō
　Vacant land beside Mizuno Iki-no-kami's residence

　Wide roads in front of residences, samurai residences

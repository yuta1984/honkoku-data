1. On March 13th at 4 o'clock at night, a fire broke out from the house of a man named Nakajirōemon in Katsumi Village, and was extinguished at dawn on the 14th. One hundred and eleven houses were burned down.

1. On March 28th, regarding the nine-year period of guard duty inspections from Hōei 6th year to Kyōhō 2nd year, and the retinue inspections from Hōei 7th year to Kyōhō 3rd year, rewards were bestowed today at the main castle upon those who served faithfully during these years.
   However, in Edo as well, rewards were bestowed on April 13th to those on duty.

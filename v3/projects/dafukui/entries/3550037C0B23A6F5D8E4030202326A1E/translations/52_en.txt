On December 28th, a written directive was received from Mizuno Izumi-no-kami Tadayuki, 
the duty officer at the castle, and circulated as follows by the caretaker of Matsudaira 
Mutsu-no-kami Munemura:

　It has been repeatedly ordered that frugality should be practiced in all matters. 
　Particularly due to frequent fires in recent years, house construction has been made lighter, 
　but those who possess multiple residences should have the mindset of reduction from the outset.
　Residences that are larger than appropriate to one's status may be ordered to be surrendered 
　when needed for official use, and of course no replacement land would be granted. The above.

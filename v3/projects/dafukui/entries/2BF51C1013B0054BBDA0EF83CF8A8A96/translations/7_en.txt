1. March 4th, Bunka 3 (Year of the Tiger) - A fire broke out from Shibata-chō and the Tokiwa Bridge residence was destroyed by fire, as recorded in documents that arrived on March 12th.
   However, at this time, official firefighters were dispatched. But as the residence gradually became endangered, the caretaker consulted with the messenger officials and withdrew the personnel.

1. October 21st, Kyōhō 3 (Year of the Dog) - Lord Mizuno Izumi-no-kami issued the following orders to those with stipends of 100 koku or more: Memorandum
As previously ordered, in case of fire, personnel should be dispatched within two to three chō around the residence to extinguish small fires. Not limited to main residences, but also for middle and lower residences where retainers are stationed - those who have retainers should dispatch them. Of course, those with small numbers of personnel need not worry, but they should be strictly ordered beforehand to rush as quickly as possible. When dispatching personnel, report to the inspectors or messenger officials at the location. Should there be delays in dispatching personnel for nearby fires, the details will be investigated and reported by the inspectors. The above.
   Dog, October
Regarding the above, details about the deployment arrangements for personnel are recorded under the intercalary 10th month, 5th day.

1. May 2nd, same 17th year (Year of the Rat) - Since Honda Kura-no-suke's Edo Honjo residence was destroyed by fire on March 28th of this year, 300 ryō in gold was sent to Kura-no-suke as assistance.

Group/Town Fires

1. Kyōhō period - Hori Bunzaemon went to Inspector Hori Jūbē and reported: "There was a story that a toilet on the opposite side of my group's squad leader Saemon caught fire slightly and was immediately extinguished." Therefore, he quickly sent group members to investigate the matter. This was naturally reported to the senior retainers, and Bunzaemon was also to report it.

1. July 16th, Hōreki 2 (Year of the Monkey) - There was a fire incident at the house of Iizuka Sagashichi from Senior Commander Iwamura Mon'emon's group, which was quickly extinguished. Although it was not serious enough to be called a real fire, since the careless handling of the fire source was negligent, Sagashichi was placed under house arrest, as reported. This matter was conveyed to the senior retainers. Regarding this, Mon'emon also requested abstention through his colleague and monthly duty officer Takada Kintayū, but was told it was unnecessary. The record is kept in the general records.

1. November 27th, the same 3rd year (Year of the Rooster) - A man named Imura Yasubē from Senior Commander Hori Kanzaemon's group had left a fire burning in his brazier with a futon cover on it, and after the entire household had gone elsewhere, the fire spread to the futon and flared up. A neighbor discovered it and extinguished it. The damage was limited to the futon with no harm to anything outside, but since it was a case of fire negligence, Yasubē was placed under house arrest. We were told to take note of this, so it was reported to the senior retainers at an appropriate time. Kanzaemon reported that he was released on the last day of the same month.

1. Nakane Shinzaemon's group - In the ten years between Kyōwa 1 (Year of the Rooster) and Bunka 6 (Year of the Snake), they suffered fire damage twice, and since the second fire occurred shortly after the first, they were granted loan silver both times. However, due to extreme financial difficulty, from the end of Bunka 8 (Year of the Sheep), upon request, they were granted a twenty-year repayment plan.

1. February 26th, Kyōwa 1 (Year of the Rooster) - Twenty houses in Sugita Gonnosuke's group suffered fire damage. Until temporary shelters could be built, it was requested that auxiliary duties be assigned to the Senior Firearms Unit as per previous regulations.

Tenpō 2 (1831), 4th month, 25th day: Regarding the repair of damaged fencing within the moat behind the residence of Deputy Keeper Makino Tonomo, and the repair of the bridge in front of the water gate - hereafter these matters shall be ordered by the Construction Office. The previous circumstances regarding this matter are unclear, but this time, after consultation among all parties, the above was conveyed to Arakawa Saburōdayū.

Various Matters Concerning Construction and Repairs

1. Issued in Hōei 5 (1708): When there are damaged areas within the castle compound, inquiry should be made to the shogunate authorities. For matters that can reasonably be handled, hereafter the Castle Deputy, Deputy Keeper, and Construction Magistrates shall consult together, conduct inspections, and report their findings to the Senior Councillors.
   However, when the Castle Deputy is ill, a deputy yoriki shall be dispatched instead.
   [Circle with X mark inside] A seal should be added later.

1. During years when the lord is absent, when construction work occurs at the lord's quarters, foot soldier inspectors shall be dispatched.
   However, for construction work at locations separate from the lord's residence, foot soldier inspectors need not be dispatched.

1. Before the lord's return to the domain, when applying final plaster to the walls of the three gates - Geba Gate, Taiko Gate, and Kitte Gate - even if the guard posts request that foot soldier inspectors be sent to remove posted papers, seals, and other items from the guard stations, we shall not interfere. Since foot soldier inspectors were initially sent to post these items, foot soldier inspectors should generally be dispatched to ensure nothing is lost, and this should be communicated. However, foot soldier inspectors need not be present for the removal.
   However, foot soldier inspectors shall be dispatched to attend to matters at the lord's quarters.

1. During the lord's absence, even if the Construction Magistrates and Works Magistrates request to enter through Taiko Gate and pass through the stable's small gate to inspect the vicinity of former construction work, this is not permissible. They should be told to enter through Kitte Gate to conduct their inspection.

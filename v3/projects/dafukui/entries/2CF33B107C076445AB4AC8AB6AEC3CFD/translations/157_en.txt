1. Court physician of the attendants' office: ordered to serve in Edo next spring (Year of the Sheep). Besides pages, formal audience granted, and all tea ceremony attendants also ordered for Edo service today.

1. November 15th - After all formal greetings concluded, the annual tea ceremony opening ritual was held as customary, with seating in the formal chamber. Tea masters attended and presented tea before his lordship, then immediately withdrew. Subsequently, Honda Kuranosuke and other senior retainers, chamberlains, and deputy lords took their seats. His lordship expressed desire to inspect the hawks and horses bestowed this year. After withdrawing for inspection and returning to seats upon completion, they offered gratitude for the viewing and departed. High-ranking retainers followed in order with the same procedure.

1. After the second meal, tea tasting continued with both teas and confections being enjoyed.

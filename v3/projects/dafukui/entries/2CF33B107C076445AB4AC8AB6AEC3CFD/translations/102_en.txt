1. Today, after completing the practice conclusion ceremony including the horse officials' audience, certificates of the Nakamura Ichiemon school were subsequently presented. His lordship took his seat at the usual place in the practice hall. At this time, the area was cleared except for chamberlain Akita Un'nosuke and chief side attendant Koheiei only. Ichiemon presented the certificate scrolls before his lordship, Kohei opened them for viewing, and Ichiemon made his presentation.

After viewing all three scrolls, Ichiemon demonstrated the techniques for viewing, and Ido Komakichi performed one complete sword technique demonstration before his lordship. Komakichi then performed another technique for viewing, and after completion, the transmission was formally granted. Gratitude was expressed in thanks, and the chamberlain facilitated the proceedings. However, when the scrolls were first brought forward and presented, words were given (expressing great joy at the presentation of the school certificates). After this was completed, gifts were received in the secretary's quarters, and soup and sake were served to both men in the long brazier room.

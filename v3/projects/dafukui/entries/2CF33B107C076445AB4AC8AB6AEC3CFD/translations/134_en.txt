Item: After entering for the meeting with Lord Kegorō, for two days following the customary practice, same as usual
Item: May 17th　Before half past seven, he entered the former audience chamber from Kita-fumyō, observed Lord Kegorō's archery practice, then entered the corner tower and made a circuit of the second bailey garden.
Item: May 18th　At half past five, the retinue was assembled for the first shrine visit and general worship. From the evening before, ritual purification was conducted.
　　From Lady Teishōin:
　　　　Morning glories, 1 pot
　　　　Fireflies, 1 basket
Item: May 19th　At four o'clock assembly, he listened to caretaker duties.
　　From Princess: Sweets, 1 box
　　From Princess Koto: A letter
Item: May 20th　At half past five, the retinue was assembled for general shrine worship. From the evening before, ritual purification was conducted. However, only those in charge of meals, attendants, and advance guard participated.
　　Falconry grounds map　　　Presented by falconry chief Kuze Hachizaemon
　　
　　Fish offering money, 100 hiki　　Presented as customary each year by Shiroi Jōsei
　　Music tea bowls, 5 boxes　　Presented by Higashi Honganji Temple
Item: At seven o'clock assembly, Lord Kegorō played polo at the main bailey riding grounds, so his lordship entered to observe.
Item: May 21st　Due to the great heat today, he moved to the audience chamber, and after taking his second meal, this became the regular practice thereafter.
　Lord Kegorō presented eggplant from the former audience hall's vegetable garden, 1 basket

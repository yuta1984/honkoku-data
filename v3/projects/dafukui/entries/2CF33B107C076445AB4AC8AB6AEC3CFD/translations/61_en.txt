　On the 29th of the 10th month of the same year, at the council chamber, through a written notice from the official on duty Koma Mokugata, instructions were conveyed and Shinemon appeared
Due to financial difficulties in domestic affairs, in the year of the dog reforms were made and frugality guidelines were issued to all government offices. Particularly since last year, economy measures were ordered beginning with personal expenses and extending to all matters, and with these exceptional reforms, after further deliberation, various instructions were issued according to different departments. Although there should be no misunderstanding of these matters, it was ordered to maintain simplicity and serve with integrity. Moreover, the following was now ordered:
1. During the period of half reduction, for all regular funds including routine silver and rice disbursements that had already been reduced in some cases, this time each department should carefully consider all matters regardless of precedent and reduce everything by half from the original amount through sincere deliberation.
1. Stipend substitute money, silver and gold, as well as silver, gold and rice granted outside of stipends, shall all be reduced by half from the original amount.
1. All goods distributions shall be reduced by 20 percent.
1. Money and rice grants to retainers and others were all stopped in the year of the dog, and for unavoidable cases that were newly granted by appropriate channels, henceforth half the original amount shall be granted.

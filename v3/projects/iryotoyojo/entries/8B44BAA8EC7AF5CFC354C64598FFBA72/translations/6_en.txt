However, he showed that generally for people to become precise in analyzing principles and achieve complete penetration without anything remaining unclear, the period around forty years of age serves as that time. Therefore, from the time of zhi xue (aspiring to learning) and er li (establishing oneself) in youth and prime years, if one concentrates the mind here and practices diligently without negligence until reaching this period of achievement, one should naturally be able to clearly perceive everything from the traces of order and disorder, rise and fall throughout history, to the good and evil, orthodox and heterodox, wise and foolish, gain and loss of people existing in the world, and even the profound essence of all arts and skills. This is the great Way of supreme sincerity and consistency.

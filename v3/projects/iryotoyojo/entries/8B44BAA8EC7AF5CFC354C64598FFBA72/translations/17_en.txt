　Discourse on Cultivating Qi, Volume One

　　　　Discussing the nature of qi
Qi is the great atmosphere that fills the space between heaven and earth, and is the fundamental source from which all things are born, grow, transform, and are nurtured.
　Zhang Hengqu said: "What fills heaven and earth is my body. What governs heaven and earth is my nature. The people are my brothers and sisters. All things are
my companions." These words, in merely six phrases, exhaust the principles of heaven and earth. This "filling of heaven and earth" refers to what fills the space between heaven and earth

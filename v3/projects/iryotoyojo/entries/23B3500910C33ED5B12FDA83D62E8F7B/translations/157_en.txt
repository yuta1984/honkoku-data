　　On Epilepsy and Madness
○Epilepsy and madness is what people commonly call derangement of mind or insanity. The occurrence of this condition has various causes. Sometimes those born with dull wits who are manic gradually increase their foolish nature and develop this condition. All other cases are due to illness. Since ancient times, many have described the causes of this disease.

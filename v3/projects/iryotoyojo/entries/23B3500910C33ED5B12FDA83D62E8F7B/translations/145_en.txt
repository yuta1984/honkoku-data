　　Theory of Distension and Fullness
○Distension and fullness is a disease of abdominal swelling and distention. Its symptoms have names such as drum distension, grain distension, simple abdominal distension, spider disease, etc. When the exterior is hard and the interior empty like a drum, it is called drum distension. When the hands and feet are thin and emaciated while the abdomen is large, resembling the shape of a spider, it gets this name. There are also distension diseases of the five organs and six bowels. When the whole body, face, hands and feet all have edema, it is called water swelling. When the abdomen is large but the face and limbs are not swollen, it is called distension and fullness.

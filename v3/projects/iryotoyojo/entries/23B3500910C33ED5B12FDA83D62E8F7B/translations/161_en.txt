These are examples from ancient times of alcohol-induced madness. In today's world as well, there are many who develop madness in this way. Such people should know this themselves and exercise restraint, but if they cannot, people around them should take good care not to give them alcohol carelessly, and should regulate this so as not to cause this terrible condition. There is also the condition of evil possession. This is when evil qi creates a curse. According to ancient theories, when people sometimes mourn the dead and enter temples, or go to empty houses, uninhabited places, or dark and shadowy places in mountains and valleys, they become possessed by spirits, causing their symptoms to become like madness.

○Treatment for alcohol intoxication and poisoning, fearing corruption of the five organs
○Put hot water in a tub and immerse the person in it. When it cools, change the water again. Use hot water in summer as well. The tub is like a vessel for holding water. An ordinary bucket-like container may be used. "Immerse" means to soak the person in the hot water.

○Treatment for headache from drinking alcohol
○Bamboo shavings: 50 monme. Boil with 8 sho of water to make 5 sho, remove the dregs and cool. Break 5 chicken eggs and put them in, stir and mix well. Boil again until it bubbles twice, and if you drink 2 sho, it is said to heal completely.

○Treatment for gums bleeding profusely due to alcohol intoxication
○Medicine to hold in mouth:
　Angelica root《2 monme》　　Alum《1 monme 5 bu》　　Cinnamon heart
　Asarum　　Licorice《10 monme each》　　Chop these five ingredients, boil with 5 sho of water to make 3 sho, hold this in the mouth. Five to six times during the day, three times at night
○Another method: Boil wolfberry and extract the juice, hold this in the mouth

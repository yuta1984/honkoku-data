○Drinking alcohol and lying down on wet ground, or being exposed to wind and sitting or lying under trees or on wet grass will cause evil diseases, as seen in the "Treatise on Disease Origins." ○Generally, when intoxicated with alcohol, one cannot lie peacefully without moving. One must have someone shake and rouse them, and should not remain still. Especially avoid being exposed to wind, lying on the ground, washing with water, or drinking water. ○Avoid sexual intercourse when drunk. The above appears in the "Prescriptions Worth a Thousand Gold." ○Wine that shows no reflection when a person looks into it should not be drunk. ○Sacrificial wine that has naturally diminished should not be drunk. ○Drinking wine stored in new tin vessels for a long time is said to kill people effectively. Swimming in water when drunk will inevitably cause muscle cramps, making it impossible to move hands and feet. Even those who are usually well-accustomed to swimming will immediately sink to the bottom of the water, swallow water, and die instantly. This is because when the body is feverish due to alcohol vapor and suddenly encounters cold water, hot and cold contend with each other, causing muscle cramps.

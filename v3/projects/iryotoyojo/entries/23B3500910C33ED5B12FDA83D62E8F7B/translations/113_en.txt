　　　On Heart Pain
○Heart pain is a disease of chest pain. There are various types of symptoms. The "Inner Canon" states: "True heart pain causes the hands and feet to become cold extending to the joints, and the heart pain is severe. If it occurs in the morning, death comes by evening; if it occurs in the evening, death comes by morning." Also, stomach cavity pain is pain when the stomach cavity affects the heart.

There are nine types of heart pain in total, as seen in the "Treatise on Disease Origins." The disease symptoms and causes also have various explanations. Among those who develop this condition now, cases of true heart pain as mentioned in the classics are rare, and most are all stomach cavity pain. As stated in works like "Renzhai's Direct Guide," these are cases where the disease is in the heart's connecting vessels. The symptoms involve pain in the center of the chest and diaphragm area corresponding to the heart.

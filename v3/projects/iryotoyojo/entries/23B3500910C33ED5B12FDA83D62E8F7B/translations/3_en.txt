This volume is intended for those who desire to live long lives free from illness. It discusses where diseases arise and what should be observed in health maintenance. It is written in kana script with the wish that it be easily understood. If one tries placing this single volume at their side and cherishing it, it will truly serve as an aid in ascending to the realm of longevity. Collected in early summer of Bunka Hinoe-Tora year.

Preface to Discourse on Sake and Health Preservation Theory - Complete Seven Volumes
It is unknown what kind of eccentric person created this volume. The words of its preface state: "Indeed, humans receive life from Heaven and should all be able to maintain good health and longevity, abundantly preserving a lifespan of one hundred years. Those who fall ill and die young are mostly all [affected by] human desires..."

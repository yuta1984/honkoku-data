Thus, while he well knew that alcohol could resolve sorrow, he remonstrated to Emperor Wu that it was impossible to achieve longevity and immortality by drinking alcohol.

People of today cannot easily attain the state of tranquil simplicity and emptiness of ancient times, nor can they practice the cultivation and ingestion of elixirs of the immortals. Therefore, at the very least, restraining excess in alcohol should be the first principle of health preservation. However, perhaps even this cannot be easily practiced in these latter days.

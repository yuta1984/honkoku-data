Now, although humans are equally precious, there are also distinctions of high and low status: those who are kings, lords, and great men with noble ranks are called noble people, while commoners, slaves, and those without official positions or stipends are called base people. Even these base people cannot neglect nurturing their lives. How much more so for noble people! Therefore, generally speaking, noble people and great men spare no effort in achieving excellence in everything from clothing, food and drink, palaces, carriages and horses, to favored concubines and servants. All of this is for the purpose of nurturing life. And not only that,

Therefore, in the Inner Classic, the Yellow Emperor says: "Kings, princes, great men, and lords who enjoy rich food are arrogant and self-indulgent, following their desires, despising others, and unable to prohibit this well. If one prohibits this, it goes against their will; if one complies with this, it adds to their illness. How can this be made convenient? What should be prioritized in treating this?" Qibo said: "It is human nature to hate death and wish for life. If you inform them of their failures, speak to them of goodness, guide them through what is convenient for them, and enlighten them about what troubles them, even if there were immoral people, how could there be any who would not listen?"

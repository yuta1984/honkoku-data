【Right page】
One should select a skilled physician. This becomes
an aid in treating illness. Since human life and death
follow the physician to such a degree, one must be cautious.
This is the meaning. ○Since physicians hold human life in their care,
selecting a physician is the foremost concern for a sick household.
Common people with shallow wisdom judge the good and evil of physicians
and trust the eloquent words of quack doctors, losing lives
that should not have been lost - this happens more than half the time.

○Soba noodles (buckwheat noodles)
They cause harm because people eat too much of them. There is also a theory that they should not be used for people with spleen-stomach deficiency-cold constitution, but when made soft

they are not troublesome even for sick people. Since it is said that they knead the dregs of the intestines and stomach, they are things that should be eaten occasionally. Like watermelon, it has been passed down that they are highly toxic if eaten, so there is no loss in not eating them either.

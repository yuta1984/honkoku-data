Herring roe (Kazunoko)　　Fish roe
　Sweet, neutral, and astringent. Non-toxic. Should not be consumed in large quantities.

Fish cake (Kamaboko)　　Fish meat cake
　Sweet and mildly warm. Non-toxic. Quality depends on the nature of the fish used. Should be carefully selected for sick people.

　Sweet and salty. Non-toxic. Treats postpartum abdominal pain. ○There are three types of karasumi. Mullet roe is called "nomo" and comes from Nagasaki. Its flavor is the finest. The others are made from sea bass and Spanish mackerel roe. Their taste is greatly inferior.

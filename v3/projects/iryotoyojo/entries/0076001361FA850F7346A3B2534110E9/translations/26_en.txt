Kanten (agar)　　Same properties as tokoroten (gelatin noodles)

Kashiita (quince confection)
　Sweet and neutral. Non-toxic. Effectively treats chronic cough and alcohol-induced thirst. A rice cake confection made by adding sugar to quince.

Kaya nuts　　Torreya nuts
　Sweet, neutral, and astringent. Non-toxic. Kills tapeworms and treats various parasitic abdominal conditions. Good for frequent urination in elderly people.

Dried chestnuts　　Dried chestnuts
　Sweet, salty, and warm. Non-toxic.

Karamatsu nuts　　Same as Korean pine nuts

Persimmon　　Persimmon
　Sweet, cold, and astringent. Non-toxic. Fresh persimmons should not be eaten in large quantities. Dried persimmons, hanging persimmons, and skewered persimmons strengthen the spleen, firm the intestines, stop coughs, and settle stomach disorders. Also neutralizes alcohol toxins. Although there are many varieties of persimmons, their properties are all the same.

Karakawa　　Pepper tree bark
　Pungent and warm. Non-toxic. Same as Japanese pepper (sanshō).

River chub　　Stone gudgeon fish
　Sweet and neutral. Slightly toxic.

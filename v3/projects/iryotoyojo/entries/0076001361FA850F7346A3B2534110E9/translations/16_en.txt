hechima (luffa)　　Sponge gourd
　Sweet and neutral. Non-toxic. Reduces swelling, transforms phlegm, and kills parasites.
benibana (safflower)　　Safflower sprout
　Sweet and warm. Non-toxic. Activates blood circulation and stops pain.
benitake (red mushroom)　　Rouge mushroom
　Those growing in spring are non-toxic. Those growing in autumn are toxic.
beniringo (red apple)
　Same as apple.

【Left page】
touhoshi (Chinese rice)　　Great Tang rice
　Sweet and neutral. Non-toxic. People suffering from leprosy benefit from eating this regularly.
　No benefit for people with weak spleen and stomach, elderly people, and patients.
toumorokoshi (corn)　《Also written as》nanbankimi　　Corn
　Sweet and neutral. Non-toxic. Warms the interior and astringes the intestines and stomach. Stops
　cholera. Difficult to digest if eaten in large quantities. Children and patients should avoid it.
toufu (tofu)　　Bean curd
　Sweet, salty, and cold. Non-toxic. Relaxes the interior, benefits qi, harmonizes the spleen and stomach,
　and neutralizes alcohol toxicity. ○For tofu poisoning, use squeezed
　daikon radish juice.

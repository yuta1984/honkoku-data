Mi [Water accidents] If someone falls into water, quickly throw any available board, box, pole, or anything that can float on water and serve as something to grab onto into the water, and have them grasp it lightly. If they use too much force, they will sink together with the object. If done this way, the body will necessarily float up on the water and will not drown to death. Since the human body originally has air sacs called lungs within the chest and ribs, in truth even without something to hold onto, if one paddles the water with hands, one will naturally float and everything above the nipples should be above water. However, people drown because they panic. It is the same principle that allows dogs and cats to swim across water naturally when they enter it. Whether one dies or survives after falling into water depends simply on whether the mind remains calm or becomes agitated - this should be well understood.

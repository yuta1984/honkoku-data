They should be cared for so as to avoid worry. Also, they should not be allowed to become angry. If anger is frequent, life becomes short. If an elderly person's heart is stirred to anger, they should grasp their prayer beads and wholeheartedly chant the texts of their religious sect. This is a good method for suppressing anger and is the foremost practice in elderly care and health maintenance. Also, as one ages, desires become extremely deep. Whatever pleases their heart should be prepared according to their station in life. Elderly people should restrain and endure their desires. When the heart labors with desire, life becomes short. In all things, one should consider insufficiency as sufficiency and nourish oneself morning and evening. The sage also always speaks of knowing contentment. Now, although there are many other ways of caring for the elderly, I will abbreviate them here.

　　　Childbirth and Child Rearing

○Regarding the birth of children, one should take great care from when the child is in the mother's womb, and even more care should be taken at the time of delivery. From the month of binding the obi (pregnancy sash), an experienced midwife should be engaged and the matter left to her hands. If the woman is weak, a doctor should also be engaged, and preparations for childbirth should be made before the due month arrives. When the due month arrives, "easy birth medicine" should be used.

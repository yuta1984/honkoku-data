○Regarding moxibustion therapy: People whose yang qi is consistently weak should receive moxibustion every season or every other month. When there is illness, one should consult a physician and inquire carefully even about the number of moxibustion treatments before applying them. For daily health maintenance, it is said that people who apply moxibustion daily to the Sanli point on the foot, five to seven moxa cones or even fifteen cones at a time, will surely achieve longevity. People over sixty years old should occasionally apply moxibustion to the Qihai point. This is good for health preservation.

○Regarding sexual intercourse: The "Qianjin Fang" states: "Those who are twenty should do it once every four days, those who are thirty once every eight days, those who are forty once every sixteen days, those who are fifty once every twenty days, and those who are sixty should close their essence and not release it. If one has vigorous vitality, one should release once a month." All matters should follow this standard.

○The "Dasheng Lu" states: "Men who have not yet reached twenty have insufficient essence and qi, and their desires are easily stirred. They should certainly abstain from sexual intercourse. Not being careful at this time damages the qi of development and harms the foundation of one's entire life." It also says, "If people under forty completely abstain from sexual relations, they will suffer from an illness called 'solitary yang.' This can also become swellings or venereal disease. In any case, moderation with less frequency is best."

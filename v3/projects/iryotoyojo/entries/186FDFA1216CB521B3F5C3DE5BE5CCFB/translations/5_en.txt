Nenashigusa (Rootless Grass) Sequel, Volume One
If this were a world without falsehood, how delightful human words would be! However, just as eggs are not square and courtesans have no sincerity, if Buddhism has expedient means then military law has stratagems, if the floating world has flattery and frivolity then gatherings have temporizing and pretense. Where there are lies there are theatrical affectations, where there is falsehood there are tricks. We speak of deceiving, of cajolery, of embellishment, of manipulation. Though the nuances may differ, in the end it's all a world wrapped up and rounded with lies. The only thing that is not false is that those who are born will die - whether it's the thousand-year [life span] of the north or the evening of the mayfly, long or short

Discussions on Doctors, Volume 5
　　　Discussion on the Smallpox Deity

A student named Jisetsuan came from Toki village in Mino Province - a shallow and reckless person - asked a question, saying: "According to someone's story, there was a man who had a thirteen-year-old son whom he loved extraordinarily. The child contracted smallpox. From the initial fever, the father was greatly worried about this and decorated a household shrine to worship the smallpox deity. With great reverence, he prayed day and night, saying 'Please let this child's smallpox pass safely,' but instead it became a severe case of smallpox...

A Discussion on Whether Doctors Become Popular or Not

Setsu'an asked again, saying, "Unlearned doctors tend to become popular, but why do learned doctors not become popular?" The answer was: "This matter has already been discussed by Tansuishi. Tansuishi said that although unlearned doctors normally kill ten people, if they have the good fortune of a lucky hit and cure one person of high rank and fame, their reputation spreads from that point and they become popular. Learned doctors normally cure ten people, but if they are unfortunate and fail to cure a famous person, their reputation falls and they do not become popular." This is a one-sided view. Whether a doctor becomes popular or not is determined by Heaven. Among those who practice medicine, which one does not desire popularity? None. Doctors who are ostentatious, fawning, foolishly obsequious, and chatter as much as Odawara medicine peddlers are generally disliked by people. Some who are taciturn, honest and proper, and speak little lack charm and become distant in company.

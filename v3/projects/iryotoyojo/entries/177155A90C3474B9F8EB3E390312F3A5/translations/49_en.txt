The pain finally became severe, and her cries day and night were so loud they startled the neighboring houses. Many doctors were summoned to treat her, but there was no effect whatsoever. Eventually it became purulent and festered, rotting into ulcers, and the pain became even more intense. As death approached, she gathered her children and had abortion prescriptions brought to her, saying "Burn these in front of me. You must never pass these medicines on to others." When the children said, "We have made this prescription our family business and many people have lived prosperously until today - what is the reason for this?" the mother replied: "Since I fell ill, hundreds of small children come to me in my dreams day and night, constantly gnawing at my brain. That is why I continue to scream. This is the retribution for making abortion my constant occupation throughout my life." Having said this, she died.

Confucius also said, "Those who make burial figurines will have no descendants." In ancient times, when people died, attendants were made to follow them in death, burying people alive. Later, straw dolls were made and buried in place of people. These straw dolls are called "yong." There were those who made and sold these, but Confucius lamented that such people would have their lineages cut off. How much more so when one kills through abortion that which has received life from Heaven through the harmony of yin and yang and formed into a human body - the retribution for this goes without saying. Even if doctors who administer and practice with these medicines do not see their retribution manifestly now...

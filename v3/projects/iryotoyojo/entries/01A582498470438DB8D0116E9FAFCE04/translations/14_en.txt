Balloon flower seedlings　Harvest the leaves, boil them, change the water and soak to remove the bitter taste, wash thoroughly, and season with
　miso or soy sauce to eat. One theory says the taste is pungent and bitter, with a slightly warm nature and mild toxicity. Another theory says the taste is bitter with a neutral nature and no toxicity.
　The presence or absence of toxicity is unknown, so one should consult a good physician.
Self-heal　The seedlings of this grass grow at the winter solstice. It is said to wither when other grasses flourish in summer. Take young leaves
　and boil them, change the water and soak to wash away the bitter taste thoroughly. Also, boil well in lye water and soak in water for about two nights
　before eating. Even when it becomes withered leaves in autumn, it is good to use as food according to
　the tradition of mountain-dwelling poor people. It should be tried and used. Generally, for grass roots, tree leaves, and tree fruits
　even those with mild toxicity, if boiled in lye water and soaked for two or three nights with water changes, then washed well before eating, there is no harm.

　For lye water, ash from burning hardwood among miscellaneous trees is good. Ash from burning pine or cedar is said to be ineffective.
Goji seedlings　They remove vexation and benefit vital energy. Boil and use as food.
Akebia tender shoots　Boil and use as food.
Soap pod tree tender shoots　Boil, change water and soak, wash and cleanse thoroughly, season with salt or miso and eat.
Honeysuckle leaves　Harvest young leaves and flowers, boil, change water and soak to remove evil qi, wash thoroughly and prepare as mentioned above.
Silvervine leaves　They have mild toxicity. Boil and use as food. Salt neutralizes the poison. Must not be eaten without salt.
Wisteria tender shoots　Boil in lye water, change water, soak for two or three nights and wash thoroughly before eating. Since they
　break blood, they are prohibited for postpartum women and must not be eaten. For ordinary people, it is also good to cook with barley and rice
　and eat as rice. Salt neutralizes the poison well. Must not be eaten without miso or salt.

　　　Treating sprains and dislocations
If one treats bone joint dislocations from sprains immediately, there is no need to apply medicine or apply heat, and they quickly return to normal with free movement. Therefore, this is something that should be thoroughly understood. When one understands and knows the principle of restoring bone joints to their original position, one can also know how to dislocate them, so for capturing enemies that are too strong to handle, keeping this knowledge is greatly

When one knows in what state bone joints are connected, even if one teaches someone nearby to do it, it can be accomplished immediately. All bone joint mechanisms are entirely connected with socket joints, and though there are differences in size and shape, the nature of the mechanism is the same. In sprains, when the joint comes out and exits the socket, the muscles naturally pull it tight and do not relax, so with it protruding outward, the bone joints are misaligned.

《The medicinal recipes and bandaging cotton are described later》In ancient times in our country, there was a divine method of crushing small sea snails called kisago, extracting their raw juice, mixing it with raw clam juice, and applying it to burns for treatment. Even now, people living by the sea say that raw clam juice effectively treats burns. One theory states that the kisa shells mentioned in the Kojiki refer to what we call ark shells or red clams. The text reads kisa shells as "kisage," which seems to mean crushing them, but if we consider the character "shū" (集) as a mistake for "shō" (焦), then it could mean burning red clams to powder.

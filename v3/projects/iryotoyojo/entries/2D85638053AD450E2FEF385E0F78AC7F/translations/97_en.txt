　　What military personnel should always prepare for adjusting their armor is this medicine for removing arrowheads. When this is applied, no matter how deeply shot in an arrowhead may be, there is no case where it will not come out. Especially for arrows whose shafts have broken, they are difficult to remove without this medicine. The Morinaga Diary and other ancient books also speak of the effectiveness of this medicine. It is said that the Divine Ruler [Tokugawa Ieyasu] also kept it as a special secret treasure. As a test, if you drive a nail into a pillar and apply this medicine, by the next day the head of the nail will protrude slightly. Its power to attract iron should be said to surpass that of a magnet.

Another Prescription for Removing Arrowheads
　　Praying mantis {{1 insect}} Croton seed {{half piece}}

【Left Page】
　　Grind the above two ingredients together equally and apply to the wound site. Soon a slight itching will emerge - endure this, and when it becomes extremely itchy and unbearable, move and extract [the arrowhead]. Afterwards, decoct coptis and cyrtomium in hot water and wash [the wound], then wipe it clean, apply very fine lime powder, and bind with cloth.
　　This prescription appears in Chinese medical texts. When the aforementioned cattle lice are difficult to obtain, this prescription should also be tried.

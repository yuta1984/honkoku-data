　　　Treatment for those affected by poisonous smoke
When poisoned by toxic smoke, if consciousness becomes confused and the body becomes paralyzed, first deeply stimulate the nostrils with twisted paper to induce sneezing. 《This is because smoke necessarily enters first through the nose and invades the brain. Therefore, if cotton or paper is stuffed into the nostrils from the beginning to block them, poisoning can be avoided.》
For sneezing medicine, pepper should be finely powdered and stored. In Mount Hakone in Sagami Province, there grows what the locals call "sneeze grass." This is a type of sternutatory medicine. When this plant is taken internally as a test, it either induces vomiting or causes bodily paralysis. It seems like it would be effective for some diseases, but this is not yet clear. However, it has superior ability to induce sneezing compared to other substances. After inducing sneezing, strong vinegar should be poured into the mouth and nose

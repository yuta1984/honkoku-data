Sweet flag can also skillfully remove oil mixed with water, so bathhouse operators keep these leaves in stock, and when lamp oil is accidentally dropped into the bath water, they use this plant to collect and remove the oil floating on the surface. Moreover, all stagnant water, though invisible to the human eye, generates an enormous number of insects, so water that has been drawn and left for days must never be drunk, as it causes great harm to people. I once had water drawn from an artesian well during summer months and placed it in a sunny location as an experiment.

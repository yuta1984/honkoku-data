【Right page】
For the aforementioned elbow dislocation,
　in this manner
placing one palm against the chest
　　　　　and
　　pulling will also
　　　　　　effect a cure.

【Left page】
For wrist bone dislocation,
　interlock with the patient's hand,
　　apply force and
　rotate toward the opposite side two or three times,
　　then when pulling toward oneself,
　　　it will be healed. This method
　　will also work
　　　if you pull
　　　　without rotating
　　　　　　as well.

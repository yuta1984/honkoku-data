　　　Method to rescue those who died instantly from contusions
When the head, forehead, chest, ribs, spine, lumbar vertebrae, etc. are struck, depending on the location, instant death may occur, but one must definitely try applying the katai method at the shoulder well point (center of the shoulder), the katai method on the spine, and the katai method below the navel strongly. 《marginal note: For the time being, it is best to entrust this to those among jujutsu practitioners who transmit the methods of life and death》 Sometimes there are those who revive. Among these, the katai method at the shoulder well is especially effective because it penetrates close to the brain (inside the head), so its effect is swift. Even if breathing has stopped, one must not abandon the person as is. In any case, one should ordinarily be mindful to learn from martial artists who transmit the arts of life and death and keep this knowledge. 　　　Treatment for broken bones
Those that break easily are the bones of the elbow, upper arm, thigh, and shin. When you have the patient lift the painful area, it will certainly bend and make a faint sound, so the broken location can be definitely identified. If there is swelling at that location, after thoroughly applying hot vinegar compresses, 《marginal note: it is good to add much camphor to the vinegar》 wrap with cloth spread with密陀膏 (a type of plaster), cover the top with vinegar-soaked cotton cloth, apply the bamboo blind shown in the diagram 《marginal note: thin wooden boards of narrow width can also be substituted》, and bind tightly from above with torn cotton cloth.

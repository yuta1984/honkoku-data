　　　　Insect/Mollusk Section
Spiral shell (nishi) has cold properties and is good medicine for long-lasting eye pain
Nishi is particularly good medicine for chest disorders. It should be cooked with vegetables and eaten
Small nishi should be well ground with the stomach and eaten in large quantities. Good for tapeworms
River snail (nina) has cooling properties, cools alcohol toxicity, and is good medicine for blood circulation. It treats tinnitus
Nina particularly moves upper qi downward, clarifies the eyes, and strengthens the kidneys

Horo miso has warm properties. It is good for parasites, opens stagnant qi, and removes phlegm
Horo miso is medicine for the spleen and stomach, benefits vital energy, and treats hoarseness
Dried rice (hoshiii) may cause chest congestion and qi blockage. Regular rice is good, but glutinous rice should be avoided
If you eat too much dried rice, you will have diarrhea. It causes malaria and stomach damage

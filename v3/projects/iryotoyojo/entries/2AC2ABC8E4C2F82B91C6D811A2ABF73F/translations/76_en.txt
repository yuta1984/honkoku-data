Corbicula clams are cold in nature and effectively cool fever. They clarify the eyes and eliminate alcohol toxicity.

　　　　Plant Section
Scallion bulb (white part of green onion) is pungent and neutral in nature. It is good for headaches from cold damage and promotes sweating.
Scallion bulb reduces facial swelling from insect bites and also reduces swelling from bee stings.
Scallion bulb increases eye essence. It supplements the center, draws out qi, and eliminates fish toxicity.

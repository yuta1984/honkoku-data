Hangover

First, the usual morning bath, then before noon, it is good to drink a little hot sake diluted with hot water. Drinking hot broth is also good. Also, making a decoction of dried persimmons and using it is good. Eating soft-shelled turtle is also good. Applying moxa to the Sanri acupuncture point is also good.

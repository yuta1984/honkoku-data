Instructions for Health Maintenance in Common Households
Among the countless multitudes of people, there are exceedingly many who suffer from illness. Among them, those who are afflicted with the disease called syphilis—this condition originates from immoral sexual relations, and because it is deeply concealed not only from fathers and elder brothers, but also from other members of the same household, the afflicted become outcasts for life, and it is not uncommon for them to ultimately lose their lives.

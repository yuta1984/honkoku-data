Health Instructions for Common Households, Volume 2
　　　　　　　　　　　　Written by Ogawa Kendō

The four seas are calm with tranquil waves, and we have enjoyed peace since the Genna era. This is the most wonderful reign since Japan's founding, and the abundance in all things is more than worthy of reverence. People living in such an unprecedented and blessed age strike their bellies in contentment, yet still find it insufficient. Both high and low in station give in to frivolous desires, indulging in the two vices of food and drink, and women...

○Do not expose your head to wind and cold. This will cause cough, phlegm, and head wind ailments. Also, do not wrap your head with thick cotton. Heat will accumulate in the brain, causing headaches and dizziness.

○If you get drunk, sweat, remove your socks, and expose yourself to wind, you will develop beriberi or apoplexy.

Chapter: On Bathing

○Do not wash your hair frequently. Your body will become thin and heavy.

○Do not bathe frequently. Blood will congeal and qi will scatter.

○Do not wash your hair when full from eating. Do not bathe when hungry.

○After bathing, you should consume a small amount of food and drink. If you lie down without doing so, your heart will become deficient, you will have many dreams, and you will sweat.

○Do not sleep while your hair is still wet after bathing.

○Do not wash your hair after the hour of the Horse (noon).

○People with eye diseases should not wash their hair. Constantly washing hair frequently will damage the eyes.

○Women should not wash their hair during menstruation.

○Do not bathe in cold water when sweating.

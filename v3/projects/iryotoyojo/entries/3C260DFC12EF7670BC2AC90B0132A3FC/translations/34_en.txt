Monthly Dietary Prohibitions

○First Month: Tiger, badger, raw smartweed, raw onions, pears

○Second Month: Rabbit, fox, chicken eggs, smartweed seeds, pears, garlic
　On the 9th day, fish should not be eaten
　On kanoe-tora days, fish should not be eaten (some say this applies to the third month)

○Third Month: Chicken eggs, hu-smartweed, internal organs of birds and beasts, all wild plants (some say that on the 3rd day of the third month, internal organs of birds and beasts, as well as all fruits, vegetables, five pungent plants, water celery, etc. should not be eaten)

○Fourth Month: Chicken, pheasant, eel, snake, five pungent plants
　On the 8th day, wild plants should not be eaten

○Fifth Month: Deer, Chinese chives, rich/fatty foods, boiled rice cakes
　On Dragon Boat Festival day, all vegetables and carp should not be eaten

○Sixth Month: Sheep, wild goose, duck, marsh water

○Seventh Month: Wild goose, raw honey, water shield, [unclear] fruit

○Eighth Month: Chicken, pheasant, crab, raw honey, fresh ginger, hu-plant, water celery, raw fruits

○Ninth Month: Dog, crab, fresh ginger, sweet melon

○Tenth Month: Bear, wild boar, scallion, Japanese pepper

○Eleventh Month: Turtle, shrimp, [unclear character], all shellfish, dried meat, mandarin duck, raw vegetables, scallion

Preface

Childbirth is originally a natural process governed by the laws of nature, and when it becomes difficult and dangerous, this is due to carelessness on the part of women. In the early years of the Dazhong era (847-860 CE) of the Tang Dynasty, when Bai Minzhong was governing Chengdu, deaths occurred in his household due to childbirth and nursing complications. When he sought out a renowned physician, he was told of a doctor named Zan Yin. Minzhong summoned him, and Yin authored and presented a book on obstetrics. From this time onward, specialized books on safe childbirth numbered in the dozens.

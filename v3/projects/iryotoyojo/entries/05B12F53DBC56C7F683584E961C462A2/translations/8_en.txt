Wanting to earnestly convey both the protection and nurturing of infants and the prudence and care required of new mothers, I have referenced the essence of ancient texts from afar and recorded matters I have constantly tried and tested in recent times. Though I do not know the merits or demerits of Naniwa Bay, I believe this may serve as one form of assistance for men who might not know, women, or young country folk.

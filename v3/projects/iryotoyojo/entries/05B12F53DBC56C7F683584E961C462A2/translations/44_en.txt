○Things for the birthing mother to understand during childbirth
Even if you think labor has begun, when there is only slight abdominal pain and nothing else unusual, there are cases where birth occurs after two to three days or even four to five days. You must not rush in your heart, and think that when the time for birth comes, the baby will be born. Do not hurriedly prepare for delivery, but keep your heart at ease, eat food that suits your taste in moderation, close your eyes, and calm your mind

If you exhaust your spirit without restraint and strain yourself to hasten delivery before real contractions even come, your strength will be depleted and your energy weakened, eventually leading to difficult labor and inviting various troubles. This is something to be well understood. When the pain descends to the lower abdomen, pierces the genital area, urination becomes frequent, the lower back feels heavy and painful, pressure is felt in the anus as if bowel movement might occur, and after great pain continues two or three times, when the fetal water (toake) 【note】 gushes out, the birth occurs as if awakening from a dream. In any case, the path of childbirth follows the natural way of heaven's principle

【Note: This refers to "door-opening water." The fluid that first emerges from the uterus during delivery. The rupture of membranes is commonly called "toake no mizu."]

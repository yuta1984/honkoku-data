Five: Things to understand after childbirth

After giving birth, it is most important for the birthing mother to close her eyes and calm her spirit. After a while, when the dizziness in her head stops and the palpitations in her chest subside, she should quietly move to a clean, separate bed. Even if it was an easy birth and her strength is as usual, standing must definitely be prohibited. Without this understanding, dizziness from blood loss will often occur.

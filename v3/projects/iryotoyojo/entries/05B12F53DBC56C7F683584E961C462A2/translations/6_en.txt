【Right page】
The teaching of preventing illness has become buried and forgotten, with very few people to pass it down. A person named Kojima from Harima Province deeply lamented this situation and authored a book in kana script called "Boshigusa" (Mother and Child Grass) to prevent the ailments of women in childbirth beforehand. Since childbirth requires even more careful attention than other illnesses, it would be best to begin with this book. Indeed, women tend to be shallow in thought and easily confused, so they are prone to neglecting their usual precautions and only become alarmed and frightened when childbirth approaches.

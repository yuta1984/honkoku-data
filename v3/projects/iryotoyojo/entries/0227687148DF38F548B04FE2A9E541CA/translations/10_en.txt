　　Fifth: The wholesaler of contemplation and ingenuity
When you hear bad news and anger burns like fire, transfer that fire to tobacco, blow it, and watch the smoke.

　　　　Mistakes
　　First: Hitting people's heads with a tobacco pipe
No matter how angry you become, you must not strike people's heads with a tobacco pipe.

　　Second: Using a tobacco pipe as a substitute for fire tongs
Even when fire tongs cannot be found quickly, always be cautious about using a tobacco pipe to grab fire.

　　Third: Walking over food while holding a tobacco pipe in one's mouth
No matter how much you want to smoke, restrain yourself beforehand when around food.

　　Fourth: Emptying ash without extinguishing it
Tobacco lovers who empty ash without extinguishing it - using paper to [extinguish] is unnecessary.

　　Fifth: Burning tatami or clothing with smoldering tobacco
Tobacco lovers who light up without paying attention, covered in tobacco and ash, keep puffing.

　　Sixth: Spitting into the ashes of braziers, kotatsu, or stoves
Where there are no ash receptacles, be mindful - those who spit should stop after just a little.

　　Seventh: Spitting between the joints of tatami mats
At teahouses and inns, no matter how casual the place, one does not spit between tatami mats.

　　Eighth: Striking the tobacco pipe loudly
When the sound of the tobacco pipe is loud late at night, sleeping people who hear it will find it annoying.

　　Ninth: Neglecting to clean ash receptacles until throwing them away
Lazy people who let bugs breed in ash receptacles - how well they continue smoking tobacco!

　　Tenth: Using ash receptacles as a substitute for nose paper
There's no such thing - after wiping a runny nose with paper, if you dry it, know that it can serve as toilet paper.

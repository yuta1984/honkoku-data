▲Thunder-dried
Shallow melons (round pickled melons) are salted and dried for a full day under the blazing sun during the dog days of summer. They're rolled up in spirals resembling the shape of lightning bolts. When you eat them, they make a loud crunching sound that resonates in your teeth, hence they're probably called "thunder-dried."

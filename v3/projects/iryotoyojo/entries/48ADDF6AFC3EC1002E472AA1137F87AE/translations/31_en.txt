at all?" The old man replied, "Having truly received your great kindness for many years, what wishes could I possibly have? I only wish to spend my remaining life in comfort," he would say morning and evening. Through his lord's compassion, there was a temple called Dōmyōji in Kawachi Province. Since it had recently become vacant, he decided to go there and quietly spend his old age. Thus he moved to live at this temple. For some reason, this temple had long handled dried cooked rice, a specialty product of Sendai.

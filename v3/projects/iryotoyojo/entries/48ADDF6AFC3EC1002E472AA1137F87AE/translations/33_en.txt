▲Senbei (rice crackers)

【Left Page】
Around the Tenshō period (1573-1593), there was a man called Naya no Yoshirō in Sakai. He gained fame in the tea ceremony and was summoned even by persons of high rank, conducting tea ceremonies daily. Through this art, he took the name Sen no Rikyū and had many disciples. Among them was a man named Kōbei, who was also skilled in the way of tea and had particular talent in various matters, skillfully making confections and such.

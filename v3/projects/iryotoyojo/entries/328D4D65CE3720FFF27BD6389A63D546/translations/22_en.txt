○Method to cure excessive sleepiness no matter how much one sleeps
Take one mouse eye, burn it, and make pills with fish oil, then place it in the corner of that person's eye. Also put two in a red bag and carry it on the body.

○Charm to stop children's bedwetting
Cut four horse shapes from red paper

and place them under the child's sleeping floor. Most importantly, continue this for seven nights and it will be cured miraculously.

○Charm to cure bad breath
On the first day of every month, before sunrise, hold water in the mouth, walk seven steps from the east, then turn around and face the eastern wall, and spit out the held water seven times while standing. The bad smell of the mouth will disappear miraculously.

○Charm to cure night blindness
Take a person with night blindness to where sparrows roost in the evening, show them the sparrows, then strike the sparrows with a bamboo pole to startle them, and when the sparrows fly, recite this incantation.

When bamboo or wood splinters are embedded, chew licorice and apply it, and they will come out. For deeply embedded ones, apply repeatedly. Also, catch a praying mantis, dry it in the shade, mix it into barley rice porridge and apply. The splinters will come out miraculously.

○Method to cure repetitive strain injury
For repetitive strain injury (when hands hurt for no particular reason and throb greatly when picking up things, commonly called repetitive strain injury; also refers to when the forearm droops), burn old cotton and fumigate with its smoke, and it will stop immediately. It's also good for dislocations and fractures.

○One method for stopping bleeding
On May 5th, extract juice from Chinese chives, knead it with lime powder to make it like mochi, and hang it in a windy place. Make it into fine powder and apply to any cuts.

It stops bleeding and heals quickly, which is truly miraculous. The older it gets over the years, the more effective it becomes, so it should be stored.

○Medicine for fish bones stuck in the throat
When fish bones are stuck in the throat and difficult to remove, place garden balsam seeds on the tip of the tongue and they will come out immediately, which is miraculous. Alternatively, powder them and swallow. Drinking powdered Chinese olive seeds is also good. Also, take a cormorant's throat, dry it, and drink water through it. It will come out quickly. (What is called a cormorant's throat, which looks like something tightly coiled, is not actually the throat. Inquire carefully and store it.)

○Emergency treatment for cholera
Being struck by heat during hot weather, causing severe abdominal pain, vomiting and diarrhea, and writhing in agony is called cholera. Without quick treatment, it leads to death. Among all medicines, the most convenient ones are recorded here.

On the day of Boshin, one should enshrine the hearth with a boar's head. What you seek will be as your heart desires.

○Method to prevent foxes from returning to their den
Place rhinoceros horn in the fox's den. It will never return to that den twice, nor will it cause harm.

○Charm to prevent wild boars from ravaging fields
Every night, place women's weaving tools where the boars come.

○Method to identify what possesses people - foxes, raccoon dogs, cat demons, or unknown entities
Pound deer antler shavings into powder and have the person swallow one spoonful with water. The possessing entity will surely reveal its true nature.

○Method to avoid hunger without eating the five grains
Take white cogon grass root, wash it, and chew it. Or dry it on stones, pound it into powder, and drink one spoonful at a time. You will not go hungry.

○Medicine for jaundice
Char one chicken egg with its shell, warm it with one gō of vinegar and take it. Worms will emerge from the nose and it will heal. Also,
Take 49 melon stems in the sixth month, combine with 49 cloves, burn in a pot until no more smoke rises, make into powder, and blow into the nostrils repeatedly - one spoonful for adults, half a spoonful for children. It will heal immediately.

○Medicine for swellings (nebuto)
When aucuba leaves are held to fire, they make a sound and pop. Take them at that moment, split into two layers, and apply to the swelling - it will absorb pus well and heal quickly.

○Miraculous medicine for all swellings
For any swelling that has festered with severe pain, mix rhubarb powder with chaulmoogra oil and apply to the swollen area - within one night it will open and discharge much pus and blood, healing immediately.

○Medicine for phlegm
Decoct bamboo leaves (large portion), black beans (medium portion), and licorice (small portion) in water. It has immediate effect. Another method:
Decoct candy in tofu water and drink frequently.
Also, mix lotus root juice and pear juice in equal parts and use.

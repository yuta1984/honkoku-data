【Right page】
　　Bath Wind　This refers to catching cold after bathing
Indiscriminate elderly who take long baths and sweat, when wind enters, their face and mouth become contorted

　　Frequent Bathing　Using baths repeatedly
Even with water for washing body dirt, if used repeatedly, it invites sores on the skin

　　Beneficial Springs　This refers to hot spring therapy
Hot springs heal wounds, scabies, and beriberi, and are good for warming muscles and bones

　　Prohibited Springs　Prohibiting hot spring therapy
For upper respiratory conditions, cough, bloating, edema, abdominal masses, deficiency heat, and red eyes, hot springs are prohibited

【Left page】
　　Foot Washing　Washing feet
Wash your feet warmly before sleeping. Simply cooling with water causes beriberi

　　Wind Eye　This refers to diseased eyes
When eyes are particularly diseased more than usual, abstain from hot baths, charcoal fire, and excessive drinking

　　Discerning Eyes　Distinguishing eye diseases
Water and fire, pain and itching, deficiency and excess, dry and moist - when eyes are afflicted, it is good to distinguish these

　　Late Eating　Late meals
Eating when slightly hungry tastes good, suits the stomach, and becomes like medicine

　　Governing the Body　Being careful with one's body
Countries where the people are at peace prosper, and those who value their qi naturally preserve their bodies

　　Food and Medicine　This refers to food and medicine
Everyone knows that eating and drinking stops hunger, but they neglect the medicine that cures illness

　　Supporting the Elderly　Helping old age
Generally, the young and vigorous in the world have strong bodies, but when old and weak, medicine becomes salvation

　　Appropriate Clothing　Wearing clothes in proper measure
Change clothes wet with water and damp with sweat, and in summer and winter, dress appropriately

【Left page】
　　Discerning Physicians　Choosing a doctor
For physicians, one should discern their skills beforehand, become familiar with them, and have one's pulse examined

　　Lycium Decoction　《Note: On the 2nd day of the 2nd month, 8th day of the 4th month, 14th day of the 10th month, and 11th day of the 11th month, boil lycium berries for use in bath water》
If you do not forget the designated days for lycium berry baths, your life thread will extend without illness

　　Bathing　Using hot water/baths
Do not wash your head when full, and do not enter hot baths when hungry, as this goes against the principles of fullness and emptiness

　　Warm Lying　Sleeping after bathing
Those who sleep with wet hair after bathing may develop head wind and dizziness

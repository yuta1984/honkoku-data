【Right page】
　　Consumptive Disposition　The foundation for developing consumptive cough
Desires affect the heart, intermittent fever affects the spleen and liver, cough affects the lungs. These all occur in people with deficiency constitution, later becoming consumptive cough

　　Distinguishing Deficiency　Discriminating the origins of deficiency patterns
Intoxication damages the lungs, overeating damages the stomach, weakening the qi's function. Sexual indulgence especially withers the root

　　Nosebleed Causes　The causes of nosebleeds
When alcohol accumulates, behavior becomes rough, and jumping about causes facial flushing, eventually leading to nosebleeds

　　Alcohol Damage　Damage caused by alcohol
Cups stacked through the night break the spleen, and distilled spirits also harm the stomach organ

【Left page】
　　Disturbing the Spirit　Confusing the mind
Stepping on frogs and being suspicious of snakes in cups, tormenting the mind - this is due to one's own doubt

　　Restraining Anger　Being cautious of anger and resentment
If anger does not come to the mouth, there is no harm; if not in the chest, there is no suffering

　　Grain Essence　The qi of food and drink
In diarrhea, grain qi is damaged; when stomach fluid dries up from phlegm and vomiting, even Buddha nature is exhausted

　　Body and Mind　Body and mind
The blood and qi within our physical form are the temporary lodging of the spiritual Buddha. Therefore, do not let them be disturbed

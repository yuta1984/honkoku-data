Ginseng is a substance of concentrated qi ("condensation"), so one should by all means value those that are substantial.
If more than twelve years have passed, it loses its efficacy. The great effects recorded
in pharmaceutical texts refer to varieties that are extremely old. They do not refer to today's ginseng.
This variety becomes substantial without fertilizer and
grows vigorously in twisted, contorted shapes. There are some that have lasted twenty or even thirty years,
and only this one variety conforms to the explanations in pharmaceutical texts.
This variety is extremely effective, and even today's Korean ginseng cannot

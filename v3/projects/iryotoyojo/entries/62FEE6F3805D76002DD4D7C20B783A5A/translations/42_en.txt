【Left page】
Civil and Military　Speaking of the ordinary and the extraordinary
Taking care of one's body beforehand is the way of letters; curing illness is the work of warriors

　　Well Flower　Water drawn from a well at dawn
First draw the water from the well bucket divided at dawn - the water of the well curb becomes medicine

　　Swallowing Saliva　This refers to saliva
The cord of life that does not spit upstream saliva continues, and the light of old age never ends

　　Face Rubbing　This refers to stroking the face
Every morning, rub and warm with hands; when eyes and face become accustomed, the ripples of old age recede

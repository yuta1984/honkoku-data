【Fire Prevention Guidelines】

Normally, important people should patrol and ensure there are no fire hazards
Normally, observe carefully - it's too late after a guest has caused a great fire
Normally, in summer watch mosquito nets and mosquito-repelling incense while sleeping, in winter be careful with kotatsu for those sensitive to cold
Normally, during the day check vacant houses, but especially at night inspect the second floor, storehouses, and under the verandas thoroughly
Normally, be careful of places where carpenters and plasterers have scattered and burned wood shavings
Normally, watch pipes, lanterns, flint boxes, kindling, and candles used in dark places
Normally, be careful of bath house ashes, fire extinguishing pots, and places connected to fire

For this purpose, keep ladders, water baskets, water brooms, fire beaters, and fire hooks ready
For this purpose, occasionally open and move storehouse doors and roof hatches
For this purpose, always prepare straw, hand towels, sashes, belts, and things used as medicine
For this purpose, decide on a change of clothes for when you're told to hurry
For this purpose, keep hand buckets, water jars, and similar containers filled with water at night
For this purpose, don't let fires die out at night, but extinguish flames that could flare up, even bath water
For this purpose, patrol constantly, don't leave rat holes as they are, and check storehouses

During a fire, evacuate elderly people, children, and sick people without injury
During a fire, take out household Buddhist altars, death registers, various account books, and documents
During a fire, even if not putting things in storehouses, carry out and escape with precious items
During a fire, don't panic immediately, use poles from chests and tansu as tools
During a fire, there are many thieves - look at people's faces before handing over belongings
During a fire, don't hastily put fire-containing braziers or fire containers into storehouses
During a fire, don't lose your precious life by being distracted by gold and silver

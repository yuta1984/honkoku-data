Around the third year of Kan'ei (1626), year of the dog, on the morning of February 5th at the fourth hour, a fire broke out from around Kojimachi 5-chome. The north wind was fierce at the time, and at 1-chome of the same area, about thirty ken of the north side frontage remained, with over ten ken remaining on the south side. From there it went south. In this area, the 2nd and 8th fire brigades, along with the Honjo-Fukagawa brigade, fought and extinguished it. To the west, Yamamoto-cho and three districts of Hirakawa burned. In Yushima Kamari-cho substitute land, a little remained, and the adjacent Kobiki-cho substitute land burned. Hirakawa Tenjin shrine was unharmed, but all the townhouses in front of the gate burned completely. The adjacent Yushima three-district town granted estates and many other places in this area burned. On the adjacent slope, the district magistrate Satomi Genzaemon and his retainers were safe. This area was extinguished by the 5th brigade. From there to the east: Mieda Sō, Nagai Tetsuya-sama, Shimazu Iyo-no-kami-sama, Takeuchi-sama, Naitō Shirō-sama, Ishimaru Gonrokurō-sama, Tani Tatewaki-sama, Sugaya Hyōgo-sama. From there, the commonly called Sanken-ya, Kojimachi Tani-machi, from Shibata Hyūga-no-kami-sama south: Ōmura Tango-no-kami-sama, Takagi Kura-no-suke-sama, Abe Matashirō-sama, Takagi Seizaburō-sama, Toda Ukon-sama. From there at Komai-koji, Otagiri Izumo-no-kami-sama's front gate row house remained. This area was defended and extinguished by the 2nd and 5th brigades and Bishū-sama's forces. Going east from there, at Torii Tamba-no-kami-sama's middle residence front gate, it was extinguished by Matsudaira Dewa-no-kami-sama's forces. Matsudaira Buzen-no-kami-sama remained. Matsuoka Bunzō-sama. From there, going out to Nagata-machi: Okano Daigaku-sama, Hosokawa Buzen-no-kami-sama, Natsume Sakon-sama, Matsudaira Daizen-no-suke-sama, up to Ōoka Satarō-sama burned, while Abe Tora-no-suke-sama remained, along with Katsuda Sakyō-sama and Gotō Hyōbu-sama. From there, all the townhouses in front of Sannō gate burned, but the shrine was unharmed. At the same location, Niwa Sakyō-dayū-sama, Kyōgoku Bitchū-no-kami-sama, and Naitō Kii-no-kami-sama had their front gates burn, but everything beyond the main entrance was unharmed. In the adjacent area, Naitō Kazunosuke-sama, and to the north Matsudaira Akino-kami-sama's middle residence, Honda Bungo-no-kami-sama, Naitō Kii-no-kami-sama's middle residence, Matsudaira Genba-sama's middle residence, Sano Hyūga-no-kami-sama, Torii Gonnoshin-sama, Hoshina Eijirō-sama, Matsudaira Tetsunosuke-sama, Hayashi Shikibu-shōyū-sama and others were consumed by fire. Another area from Kojimachi 1-chome to Hayabusa - in this area many hatamoto physicians and others were consumed by fire. From there, along the moat, Kyōgoku-sama, fire chief Jinbo Sanzenjirō-sama, and Matsudaira Hyōbu-dayū-sama's moat-side row houses remained. West of here, many hatamoto residences burned. From there, going toward Sakurada, Miyake Tosa-no-kami-sama and Ii Kamon-no-kami-sama burned, with a little remaining on the east side. Across from there, Matsudaira Aki-no-kami-sama burned a little with nothing particularly significant, and reaching Kasumigaseki, Matsudaira Mino-no-kami-sama's front gate remained. At this location, the Fukagawa brigade worked hard and carefully defended it. The north row houses were extinguished with the help of the 6th brigade and fire services assistance from Matsudaira Ōsumi-no-kami-sama, Tōdō Izumi-no-kami-sama, Kikkawa Kenbutsu-sama and others. To the west of the same area: Kuki Nagato-no-kami-sama, Tokunaga Iyo-no-kami-sama, Niwa Sakyō-dayū-sama's middle residence, Kuki Nagato-no-kami-sama's middle residence, Sugiura Hakurō-sama, Nakagawa-sama, Kamio Iori-sama, Iwamoto Ōsumi-no-kami-sama. From there, going out to Sasabiki, all the hatamoto burned, and on the back Kasumi street: Koide Toyojirō-sama, Takagi Mondo-sama, Matsudaira Hōki-no-kami-sama, Murase Heishirō-sama, up to Naitō Noto-no-kami-sama burned. Up to this point burned to the moat's limit, then the wind changed, and across from the gate interior, Miura-sama, Nishio-sama and others remained. Flying sparks to outside the gate caused the accounting office and Kyōgoku Nagato-no-kami-sama to be consumed by fire, while the opposite government office remained. From there, along Shinbashi street: Kinoshita Kazue-no-kami-sama, Inaba Tomotarō-sama, Nika-ho Rikinosuke, Hitotsuyanagi Tosa-no-kami-sama burned, while Hayashi Hyakusuke-sama remained. Hijikata Bitchū-no-kami-sama, Mōri Awa-no-kami-sama, Bizen-machi, Kaji-machi, Hotta Buzen-no-kami-sama, Izumi-machi, Fushimi-machi remained. Hitotsuyanagi Hyōbu-sama, Matsudaira Hyōbu-sama remained. Abe-sama's middle residence and Kobori Daizen-sama had only the front side remain. Another area from Konpira-sama: Tani Kazue-sama, Sagara Shima-no-kami-sama burned, while Nose Sōemon-sama remained. In this area many hatamoto burned. Aoyama-sama's middle residence and Takenaka Zusho-sama both remained, while all the hatamoto on the east side burned. Katō Bitchū-no-kami-sama, Matsudaira Tamba-no-kami-sama's middle residence, Magabuchi Kai-no-kami-sama, Tamura-koji Tamura-sama's middle residence, Matsudaira Saburōzaemon-sama, Matsudaira Kojūrō-sama, Koide Suke-shirō, Hosokawa Buzen-no-kami-sama's middle residence, Toki Shinano-no-kami-sama, Inoue Shinzaemon-sama, Ōshima Higo-sama Sakyō-sama, Matsudaira Heijibē, Ōsawa-sama, Inaba-sama, Arima Yumeichi-sama, Nika-ho-sama, Hotta-sama's middle residence, Kawakatsu-sama, Tamura Sakyō-dayū-sama, Akita Awa-no-kami-sama, Yonezu Etchū-no-kami-sama, up to Inoue Tōtōmi-no-kami-sama burned, and it was extinguished at Ōkubo Tetsunosuke-sama. Sendai middle residence, Uemura-sama's middle residence, Hirano Gonpei-sama, Niki-sama, Inoue-sama, Yagyū Tajima-no-kami-sama, Arima Hyūga-no-kami-sama. From there, Shibai-machi, Shinmei-machi, Udagawa-machi, Shinzenza and Matsudaira Higo-no-kami-sama remained. Another area: Kuchiki Suō-no-kami-sama - in this area many residences burned. Shitaya-machi and Kurumazaka-machi remained. Tentoku-ji, Atago-yama - temples in this area burned. Makino-sama's middle residence, Katagiri Sukesaku-sama, Matsudaira Oki-no-kami-sama, Tamura Iyo-no-kami-sama, Hasegawa Tamesaburō-sama, Ikeda-sama, Honda-sama. From there, Kōzu-sama burned while Seki Tajima-sama remained. Ōkubo Kaga-no-kami-sama, Toda-sama's middle residence, from Fujikake Dewa-no-kami-sama both back and front gates remained, while temples on the east side burned south. From there, Katamon-mae, Nakamon-mae six districts, Matsu-machi, Niwa-sama burned a little, while Toda Uneme-sama remained. Up to Kanasugi waterfront, temples burned together. Kanasugi 4-chome remained a little. Matsudaira Inaba-no-kami-sama burned, and the fire stopped at Manabe Shimōsa-no-kami's middle residence.

1. On the morning of the 3rd day of the 7th month, it is said that two sun discs were seen in Dewa and Ōshū provinces
1. On the mornings of the 4th day of the 7th month and 5th day of the 8th month, the color of the sun was like crimson
1. In the 8th month, hail fell in Ōshū province, reaching sizes of 9 shaku, or 2 shaku, or 1 shaku 7 sun
1. In the 9th month, persimmon flowers bloomed in Usui District
1. In the 10th month, mulberry fruits ripened in Hanazawa District of Musashi Province
1. In Agatsuma District, wheat formed ears
1. In the 11th month, azalea flowers bloomed in Mikura, Murata, and Nakamura areas of Gunma Prefecture, and in Watase Village of Kodama District in Musashi Province

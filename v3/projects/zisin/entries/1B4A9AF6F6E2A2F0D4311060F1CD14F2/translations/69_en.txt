Report No. 19
　　Regarding tremors and other phenomena related to Mount Shirane in Yashū Province
On the 12th day of the current 3rd month at around 3 PM, in the direction of Mount Shirane, which is connected to Mount Kurokami in Nikkō, sand and smoke suddenly rose up, the blue sky turned black, intense vibrations occurred, and though the sound was like light thunder, it reverberated through heaven and earth. Ash with the smell of sulfur rained down for six hours, causing people to be shocked and prostrate themselves in fear. At that time, the appearance of Mount Shirane looked like fireworks. This report comes from Rengaseki Village in Tsuga District, Yashū Province, which is under our jurisdiction. Regarding the details, we will report after conducting an on-site investigation, but we urgently submit this preliminary report.

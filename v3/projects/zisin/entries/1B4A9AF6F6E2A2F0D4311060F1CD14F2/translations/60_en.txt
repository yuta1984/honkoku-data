　　Copy of report from former Gunma Prefecture to Treasury Ministry officials
Regarding the matter of sulfurous gases burning on the western slope of Mount Arayama within the territory of Higashi-Ogawa Village, Tone District, Kōzuke Province under our jurisdiction, a report was submitted with attached maps and stones from the burned area as shown in the separate document. We immediately dispatched officials for inspection, and finding no discrepancy with the aforementioned report, we hereby submit this notification.

The islanders of Niijima Village and Okada Village are those with native family names, and these two villages have many occupations and do not lack in production. Nomashi Village, Sakigichi Village, and Senzu Village do not compare to the former two villages and have many poor people. The two villages of Sakigichi and Senzu do not shave their hair. There are no professional doctors on the entire island. The island has many kites, crows, and sparrows, with snipes seen in various places. There are over a thousand wild horses, over two hundred wild cattle, and over a thousand sheep.

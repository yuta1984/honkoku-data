○Same year, 12th month: A close retainer of Lord Sakyōdayū named Ōmura Sukejūrō had an argument on the road with retainers of Lord Minobe Gen'emon, a hatamoto serving as chest magistrate, in front of Lord Mizuno Tosa-no-kami's residence in Yotsuya. The situation was that Lord Gen'emon's retainer came back toward Sukejūrō's attendants, so Sukejūrō's side called out to him, but he would not withdraw. When they called out again, he came back and spoke abusively, but Sukejūrō paid no attention and continued on. Then Gen'emon's retainer turned around, drew his sword, and struck at Sukejūrō who was on horseback. Therefore Sukejūrō dismounted and immediately cut down that person.

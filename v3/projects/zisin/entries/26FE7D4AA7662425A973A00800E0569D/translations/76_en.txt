One: 18th day of the 2nd month of Kanbun 8, year of the monkey - Proclamation from the government to temples:
　One: The beam span shall be limited to 3 ken in Kyoto measurement, however the purlin span may be as desired
　One: Buddhist altar corner buildings shall be limited to 3 ken square in Kyoto measurement  
　One: Small ridge construction shall be made　One: Eaves on all four sides shall be limited to 1.5 ken in Kyoto measurement
　One: Decorative structural elements above the wooden wall construction shall not be used
　The above applies to temple halls, guest halls, abbots' quarters, temple kitchens, and anything else - the beam span shall not be built wider than these regulations. If there are reasons to build wider, one must inquire at the Temple and Shrine Magistrate's office and follow their instructions.

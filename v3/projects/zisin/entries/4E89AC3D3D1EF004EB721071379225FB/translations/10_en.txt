　　April 2nd　　　　　　　　　　　Honda Bungo-no-kami

At my magistrate office's temporarily entrusted territory of Murakami in Kubiki District, Echigo Province, from the fourth hour of the night on the 24th of last month, there were strong earthquakes with frequent aftershocks. At Kawaura

Village, the pillars of the magistrate's office, honjin, and row houses buckled, with extensive damage from fallen walls and such. In the same village and neighboring villages, many houses collapsed, and there were immediate deaths and injuries. Reports have not yet come from distant villages, but similar situations are heard of, so I will report the details later, but first I report this matter.
　　April 2nd, Sheep year　　　　　Ogasawara Nobusuke

At my domain of Takada in Echigo Province, from the hour of the Boar on the 24th of last month, there was a great earthquake. In the retainer residences, castle town, and villages of the domain, there were extensive collapsed houses and damaged areas, injuries to people and horses, and collapses and cave-ins at various points along the Hokuriku highway, according to reports from my domain.

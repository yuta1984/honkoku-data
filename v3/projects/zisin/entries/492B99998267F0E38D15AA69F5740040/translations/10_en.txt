Fresh flowers, artificial flowers, incense, and candles are offered, and the arrangement of decorations is both excellent and beautiful. On the following tenth day, the Deputy Abbot and Provisional High Priest conducts a ceremonial parade in a middle palanquin. This was bestowed by the Lord Abbot of Nikko, with gold and silver fittings gleaming so magnificently that calling it "beautiful" would still be inadequate.

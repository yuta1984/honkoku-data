Also, since this concerns the special temple opening ceremony, I made small banners as children's playthings, pricing them very cheaply so they could easily be carried even to distant provinces. Though there are schemes for increasing profits, since both the location and appearance are desirable, this too is not easy. Therefore, when I completed my pilgrimage to my family temple Kubota-sha, since we are blood relatives and particularly close, we coordinated our efforts and made an internal petition to the head temple. However, the sacred titles of the four gates are copies of imperial inscriptions and must be treated with utmost reverence, making this no easy matter.

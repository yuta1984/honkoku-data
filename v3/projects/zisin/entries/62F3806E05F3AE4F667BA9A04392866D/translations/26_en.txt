○The Ogawa-chō regular firefighting unit's residence had no head officer at the time and was an idle unit. Thirty-eight people from this unit including dōshin (patrol officers), scaffolding workers, and laborers died instantly, over a hundred were injured, and moreover the entire residence was destroyed by fire.

○Ōkubo Chikuzen-no-kami, supervisor of minor construction with 1,900 koku stipend on Koishikawa-chō Hitotsubashi-dōri, had Hayakawa Seigo visit today regarding a marriage proposal. After the discussion was completed and he was seeing him out to the next room, his sons Shume and Einosuke were also seeing the guest out and were about to enter the inner quarters when everything suddenly shook and collapsed. The father and two sons died instantly, and nine male and female retainers also died instantly. The aforementioned Hayakawa had walked more than one chō when fires broke out in various places in Ogawa-chō, blocking his return route, so he returned to Ushigome via the moat embankment.

○Ōkubo Awaji-no-kami, a middle inner page with 300 hyō stipend in the same area, had a retainer named Shimada Kinai who

was a filial person. Due to his adoptive father's old age and illness, he gave him three ryō in gold and granted him leave to go to his adoptive father's place to tend to his illness. Later, when the master was entering his own sitting room, the earthquake caused the entire residence to collapse. The master died instantly, and eleven male and female retainers died instantly. At that time, Kinai rushed back from the road and tried to check on his master's safety. In the midst of the chaos, he finally managed to dig out where his master was, but he was already dead. However, because fire was approaching, he carried his master's corpse and retreated to Ichigaya, then returned again to the residence and extinguished fires in various places. His hardship should be acknowledged.

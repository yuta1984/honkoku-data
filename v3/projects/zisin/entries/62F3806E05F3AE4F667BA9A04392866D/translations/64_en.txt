【Right Page】
I record here one or two earthquakes that have appeared in recent records:
　Meiwa 8, year of the Metal Rabbit (1771), 5th month 2nd day, 6th month 2nd day: Great earthquake
　Tenmei 2, year of the Water Tiger (1782), 7th month 14th day at 9 PM, 15th day morning: Great earthquake, with frequent occurrences during this period
　《Note: All people went outdoors. Ōyama and Odawara shook most severely》
　Same 3rd year, year of the Water Rabbit (1783), 2nd month 2nd day: Great earthquake
　Kansei 2, year of the Metal Dog (1790), 11th month 27th day: Great earthquake
　Same 6th year, year of the Wood Tiger (1794), 11th month 3rd day, hour of the Rat: Great earthquake
　Kyōwa 3, year of the Water Boar (1803), 3rd month 4th day, after 6 PM: Great earthquake
　Bunka 9, year of the Water Monkey (1812), 11th month 4th day, 8 AM: Similarly, many collapsed houses and injured people in Shinagawa and Kanagawa

【Left Page】
　Tenpō 8, year of the Fire Rooster (1837), 12th month 9th day, after 8 PM
　Same 9th year, year of the Earth Dog (1838), 8th month 25th day: Great windstorm, rain, and earthquake
　Same 14th year, year of the Water Rabbit (1843), 2nd month 9th day, latter part of the Snake hour: Cold enough that water in irrigation buckets froze
　Kōka 4, year of the Fire Sheep (1847), 3rd month 24th day, night at 4 AM: Edo shook only slightly, but there was a great earthquake in Shinshū
　Kaei 6, year of the Water Ox (1853), 2nd month 2nd day, latter part of the Snake hour: Three earthquakes, great earthquake in Odawara
　Ansei 1, year of the Wood Tiger (1854), 11th month 4th day, middle of the Dragon hour, continuing with several occurrences until late night of the 5th: Great earthquakes in various provinces

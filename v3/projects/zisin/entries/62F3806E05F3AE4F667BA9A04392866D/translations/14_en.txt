Wondering if they were under this house and already dead, or if perhaps they might still be alive, he continued calling out at the top of his voice for several hours until the fire approached, but received no answer. As the fierce fire gradually drew near and he could no longer remain, he reluctantly left the place at others' urging. Thinking they might have evacuated to their family temple Daion-ji, he went there to inquire, but learned that this temple had already collapsed and the head priest had also died, crushed underneath. When evacuating the quarter, he heard voices of men and women from under the houses crying "Help us! We'll burn to death! It's hot! Unbearable!" - hearing this made his heart feel as though it would break. Though his heart was unwilling while not knowing his wife and children's safety, and since he alone had no power to save the many people in distress, he had no choice but to ignore their cries and flee. After the fire subsided, he searched out the bodies of his wife and child, and his grief and tears knew no end.

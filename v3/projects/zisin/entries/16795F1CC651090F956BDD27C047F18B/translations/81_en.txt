12th day, 10th month, Year of the Rabbit    Jodo sect
Charitable donations due to earthquake        Shogaku-ji Temple
One: 7 to of white rice                     Said temple grounds townhouses
One: 2 ryo 3 bu in gold                     14 front street shops
One: 7 kan-mon in coins                     No back street shops
    However, per front street shop: 5 sho of white rice    Additionally, servants and workers
                                   500 mon in coins each           7 houses
    Of 7 houses of servants and workers, 4 houses received 2 bu in gold each
    3 houses received 1 bu in gold each

The charitable donations from said temple being a commendable act, it was reported and on the 14th of last month, commendation was received at the office of Ota Settsu-no-kami, Commissioner of Temples and Shrines.

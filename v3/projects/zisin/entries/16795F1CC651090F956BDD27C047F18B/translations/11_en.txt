Honjo
　　　　　　　　　　　　Ekoin Temple
　　　　　　　　　Sekiguchi
　　　　　　　　　　　　Chokoji Temple

　　The above are those who performed particularly meritorious deeds regarding this recent earthquake.
　　Upon conducting a confidential investigation, the aforementioned Reigan-ji Temple and
　　nine other temples sent rice, money, and other aid to
　　people living in front of their gates or parishioners with close relationships.
　　Among the above, Ekoin Temple is originally a
　　temple without parishioners or connections, but regarding this earthquake, there were
　　very many people who died, and the temple conducted
　　funeral services without requesting memorial fees. Chokoji Temple is a poor temple, but in ordinary times
　　their conduct has been good, and regarding this earthquake
　　they provided aid of fifty hiki of money each to impoverished parishioners, and furthermore
　　conducted funeral services for the deceased without requesting any memorial fees whatsoever.
　　This being particularly meritorious conduct, to Ekoin Temple

10th month, Year of the Rabbit
1. Shibaguchi 2-chōme monthly administrator Kahē, Shibaguchi 3-chōme officials Hyōzō and Gensuke-chō official Kamejirō report that due to the earthquake on the night of the 2nd of this month, houses, storehouses and other structures suffered extensive damage. Therefore, from Lord Matsudaira Mutsu-no-kami's mansion, the following was bestowed upon those living in front of the mansion in our town districts as well as those who have business dealings with the estate:
1. 48 hyō of unpolished rice    Shibaguchi 2-chōme, 40 households

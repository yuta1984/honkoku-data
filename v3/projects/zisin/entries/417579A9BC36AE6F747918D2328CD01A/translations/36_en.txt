【Right page】
Regarding the content of that divine oracle, (the god) spoke as follows: "How well you have done, petitioner, to give me hot water just now! I am pleased to be saved from the three burning torments. The great earthquake shook terrifyingly, and though you pray to me, I too worry. Even now with this earthquake that shook, I too was so frightened that I leaped up into the pine tree. It is only natural that the parishioners are afraid. However, understand my heart well. Though I think to protect the parishioners with all my might, each time a great earthquake shakes, my heart pounds and I cannot continue protecting steadily. You must each take very good care." Having said this, the god ascended.

【Left page】
The Omi road was particularly shaken by the great earthquake, so here and there, even at small shrines and sanctuaries, hot water was offered from various localities while prayers were made. Though the shrines differ and the localities vary, there is only one shrine maiden - the shrine maiden of the Fourth Shrine. She is hired to go here and there to deliver divine oracles, and the content is generally the same. When hot water was offered to Moroha Daimyojin around the Otsu highway and Yamashina area, the shrine maiden of the Fourth Shrine was hired to deliver the divine oracle. Having already finished bathing in the hot water, the oracle soon came: "Listen well, parishioners! During these recent months and days, you have not thought whether I even exist.

　　　　　　　Record of the Great Shinshū Earthquake　【seal】【seal】
　Report to Toda Yamashiro-no-kami, duty officer, regarding the great earthquake in Shinano Province on the 24th day of the 3rd month of Kōka 4, year of the sheep
Item: In my domain territory within Takai District of Shinano Province, from around the hour of the boar on the 24th day before yesterday,
　the earthquake was severe, with damage to several locations in the magistrate's office, retainers' residences and row houses,
　peasant houses collapsed in the villages, and in addition, fields cracked open in
　several hundred places, sand and mud gushed forth, affecting all areas of the domain without exception, and tremors continue to this day.
　Fortunately, there were no injuries to people or horses in the domain territory. However, among those who had gone
　on pilgrimage to Zenkōji Temple or for seasonal work, it is heard that there may be some deaths,
　but investigations have not been completed. Details will be reported later. First,
　I respectfully submit this report. The above.

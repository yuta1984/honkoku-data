Recited the Vajra Prajna Sutra to apologize for the disasters of earthquakes, wind, and floods, and to suppress the invasion of neighboring troops seeking opportunities to attack.

29th day, Mizunoe-Ne: Dispatched an envoy to Iwashimizu Shrine to present sacred offerings and submit a written prayer stating various matters.

Upon careful examination: Regarding the matter of Silla pirate ships, the matter of large birds appearing as omens, the matter of earthquake and flood disasters in Higo Province, the matter of earthquake disasters in Mutsu Province, and the matter of other provinces having quite similar disasters - prayers were offered for these matters, but since the text is entirely the same as the written prayer presented to Ise Grand Shrine on the 14th day, it is abbreviated here.

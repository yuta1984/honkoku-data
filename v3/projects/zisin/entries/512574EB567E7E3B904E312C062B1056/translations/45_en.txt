The 23rd day, mizunoe-tora: Today there were again frequent earth movements, though not as severe as yesterday and the day before yesterday.

The 24th day, mizunoto-u: At dawn during the Hour of the Tiger (or perhaps the Rabbit?), there was again a great earthquake that made one's soul disappear. With great earthquakes continuing like this for consecutive days, in what year has there been such precedent? Has there been such an occurrence since Shōwa? When asking Chikayoshi Ason whether daily movements warrant secret reports to the court, he replied: "On the 3rd day of the 1st month of Shōwa 6, there was a great earthquake, and besides that, frequent small movements. At that time too, it was the movement of dragon and water deities. That year, the Retired Emperor Fushimi passed away. Besides that, there is no precedent for such continuous large and small movements." This dawn was also the movement of water deities, surpassing Shōwa. Since the 21st and 22nd were the same matter, divination texts are not particularly necessary. In Shōwa, there were small movements on days like the 4th and 6th, with great movements only twice. This time there have been five great movements and countless small ones. Also, during continuous periods, secret reports are not necessary (abbreviated below).

The 25th day, kinoe-tatsu: Today there were again frequent earthquakes.

The 26th day, kinoto-mi: Today there was again an earthquake. The 27th day, hinoe-uma: Today there was again an earthquake.

The 4th day of the 7th month, mizunoto-ushi: At the Hour of the Monkey, the earth shook greatly again. Such great movements occurred on the evening of the 21st of last month, the 24th, and again today - spanning several days, should this be called a rare occurrence?

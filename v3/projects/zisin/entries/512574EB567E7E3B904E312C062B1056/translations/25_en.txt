Kōwa 5
○Hyakurenshō [Chronicle of a Hundred Refinements]　Emperor Horikawa
　In Kōwa 5, on the 22nd day of the 4th month there was a great earthquake, on the 24th day another tremor, and on the 1st day of the 5th month there was another tremor. People's fear was extreme.
Ten'ei 2
○Chūyūki [Middle Right Record]　Ten'ei 2, on the 27th day of the 3rd month, there was a great earthquake at midnight tonight. On the 28th day at the hour of the Monkey there was a small earthquake.
Chōshō 4　Changed to Hōen 1
○Chūyūki [Middle Right Record]　Chōshō 4《marginal note: Hōen 1｜》, 3rd month, 18th day《marginal note: xinmao｜summer season》
　《marginal note: division｜》At dawn there was a great earthquake. On the 22nd day, Geki-no-taifu Moronaga came to inform [us]. [Regarding] a scholar of astronomy
【red inscription】"Discussion"
　[Someone] asked about the earthquake of one day. [He] replied that earth is yin, and this is a caution regarding the empress's great crossing.

【"关" is a variant character for "gui." However, the correct sexagenary cycle designation for the 24th day of the 1st month of Jōtoku 3 is dingmao.】

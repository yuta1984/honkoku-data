○ Hyakuren-shō, Emperor Go-Toba's reign
Bunji 1st year, July 9th, Kanoeigeru day, hour of the Horse: Great earthquake. Its sound was like thunder. During the shaking, time had already passed, and afterward it continued incessantly without stop. The tiled walls of the imperial palace and the common houses throughout the capital - some were damaged, some collapsed, with not a single place remaining intact.

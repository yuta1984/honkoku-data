Near the end of the Hour of the Snake, there was a great earthquake that shook three times. After a while, it shook once lightly, then once more lightly. Throughout the entire day there was rumbling, and after the Hour of the Sheep it rained. Deep into the night, at the beginning of the Hour of the Monkey, Minister of the Left and Middle Captain Takana came and reported: "From the Retired Emperor's Palace, through a lady-in-waiting's formal letter, it was commanded that from the coming 9th day for three nights, the extraordinary sacred music ceremony at the Naishi-dokoro should be performed, and arrangements should be made." [omitted section]

At the Palace, Third Rank Minister Arimori of the Tsuchimikado family discussed: "Today's earthquake was caused by the movement of the water deity. The Emperor should be cautious, but military conflicts will not occur within the year." He also said: "Stone walls and earthworks in various places have collapsed. I have never seen anything like this. There has been no such great movement in recent times."

Eikyō 5 (1433)

○ From the Nanchō-kiden: Eikyō 5, 5th month, 11th day, at the Hour of the Horse, there was a great earthquake. In autumn, 9th month, 16th day, at the Hour of the Rat, there was again a great earthquake, occurring more than thirty times during the night. Thereafter, earthquakes continued without stopping for more than twenty days.

Kakitsu 2 (1442)

○ From the Nanchō-kiden: Kakitsu 2, spring, 1st month, 21st day, there was a great earthquake.

Same year, 3rd year (Kakitsu 3, 1443)

○ From the Kōtomi-ki: Kakitsu 3, 6th month, 27th day, this dawn there was an anomaly in the moon's movement. Mars, Venus, and Saturn formed a triple conjunction near the moon's orbital path, visible in the eastern direction. Should this not be subject to formal divination and discussion?

Meiō 3rd year
○ Wachō-kyō-ki: Meiō 3rd year, 5th month, 7th day, around the hour of the horse, there was a great earthquake. Upon examination {{Kōhi Dairon}}, today is the Tei constellation day. According to that theory, on days of the twenty-six constellations Zhang, Di, Shi, Bi, namely Lou and Zhou, if there is an earthquake, mountains collapse, and this is the fire god moving. The great rivers will not dry up, the year will not be favorable, and the emperor and ministers will suffer calamity, it is said. Even into the night, there were still tremors from time to time, which is most fearful. The divination document of yin-yang should be sought and recorded.

From the twenty-fifth day onward, earthquake occurrences gradually subsided, but the tremors remained irregular. On the twenty-ninth day, around cockcrow, Mount Hakuo collapsed with a thunderous rumbling sound. On the same day at the same time, Mount Kazuo in Sakaki village also collapsed, and the severe damage to houses and the collapse of residences continued for over a month without stopping. On April 22nd at the seventh hour (around 4 PM), there was a major earthquake, causing public anxiety, and lanterns were offered to Hachiman Daijin praying for safety. That same night, there were tremors at Yamahirabayashi in Minochi County, blocking the Sai River. On the 28th of the same month, the sun blazed brilliantly all day, accompanied by cloud formations, with the sun's color as red as crimson. On May 6th at the fourth hour (around 10 AM) there was a major earthquake, on the 7th at dawn there was a major earthquake, and until the night of the 21st, there were major earthquakes three or four times each night. On the night of the 26th, there were major earthquakes during both the Hour of the Ox and the Hour of the Tiger, continuing for several more months without stopping. On the night of August 22nd, there were two major earthquakes, and people went out to the fields to spend the night.

On October 22nd there was a major earthquake, on November 15th, lasting until the night of the winter solstice, there was a major earthquake equal to the initial outbreak in late spring. On the night of December 3rd there was a major earthquake equal to that of the winter solstice night, and the major earthquakes did not stop throughout the year.

○Hyakurenshō [Chronicle of a Hundred Refinements]　Emperor Horikawa
　On the 24th day of the 11th month of Eichō 1, there was an earthquake incomparable in all of history. All the people cried out in distress. His Majesty boarded the imperial boat.

Eichō 2　Changed to Jōtoku 1
　○Chūyūki [Middle Right Record]　On the 6th day of the 7th month of Eichō 2, around the hour of the Monkey, there was a great earthquake.
　　Around midnight last night there was also an earthquake.
　On the 6th day of the 8th month, around midnight, there was an earthquake.
　On the 8th, today around the hour of the Rabbit, there was a small earthquake. This past year, celestial omens and earthquakes have appeared frequently, and these abnormal phenomena are most fearsome.
　On the 21st day of the 11th month, there was an era change. [The new era name is] Jōtoku.
　○Genhihetsu-roku [Secret Record of Origins]
　On the 21st day of the 11th month of Eichō 2, the era was changed to Jōtoku.
　This was due to disasters such as celestial omens, earthquakes, floods, and great winds.

Jōtoku 3　Changed to Kōwa 1
　○Shikan-ki [Record of the Court Historian]　On the 24th day of the 1st month of Jōtoku 3《marginal note: Kōwa｜1》, at the hour of the Rabbit, there was a great earthquake. The former Grand Minister of State and many others came to court《marginal note: Shōyōin｜》Today an imperial envoy to Ise Shrine was appointed《marginal note: Provisional Grand Counselor｜Toshinaga》Therefore there was an imperial visit to the Eight Ministries. The matter of this morning's earthquake was recorded in the imperial edict. Farewell address.

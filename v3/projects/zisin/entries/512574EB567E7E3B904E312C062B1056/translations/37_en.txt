【Red inscription】"Offerings to the Nine Shrines"
From the 9th of last month, daily earthquakes have continued without cease until now. 13th:

【Red inscription】"Change of era name"
Envoys for offerings to the nine shrines were appointed. This was for imperial prayers due to the earthquake. 14th: There was a change of era name. This was due to the earthquake.

Bunji 3rd year
○ Gyokuyō, Bunji 3rd year, October 12th: Today at the hour of the Snake there was a great earthquake. Though it did not match the震動 of July last year, among others it was the greatest tremor. Heavenly portents appear frequently, and on top of this there is this震動 - frightening and still more frightening. The court astronomers Hiroki, Yasushige and others came to report, each with fearful concern.

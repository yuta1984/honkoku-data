Eiwa 2 (1376)

○ From the Kōtomi-ki, Bunnan 6, 4th month, 13th day: The Geki investigated precedent cases of great earthquakes.

Eiwa 2, 4th month, 25th day: Great earthquake. 6th month, 21st day: From today, deputy officials began daily attendance. The main officials performed prayers concerning celestial changes and earth movements.

Meitoku 2 (1391)

○ From the Meitoku-ki: On the 15th day of the 10th month, at the Hour of the Horse, the great earth shook so violently that people traveling on the roads could not walk, and even those sitting peacefully at home were frightened out of their wits. At this time, Ariyo, the Third Rank Minister and Head of the Bureau of Divination from the Tsuchimikado family, rushed to the Imperial Palace and reported something frightening: "Today's earthquake was caused by the movement of the Golden-winged Bird and is extraordinary. According to astronomical divination, rebellious vassals will appear in the world seeking to control state affairs, and within seventy-five days there will be a great military rebellion. However, it will be settled within a single day. Although there may be temporary difficulties for Your Majesty, in the end it will be auspicious," he reported.

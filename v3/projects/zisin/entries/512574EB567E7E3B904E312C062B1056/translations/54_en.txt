○ Kōnendai-ki, opening passage: Kanbun 3rd year, 12th month, 6th day, at the hour of the dog, there was an earthquake, and earthen walls and such in various places toppled over.

Hōei 4th year
○ Hōei 4th year record: 10th month, 4th day, at the hour of the sheep, there was a great earthquake, after which it shook slightly. Due to the earthquake, Osaka suffered great damage, and over thirty thousand people died at Ōshio. This was unprecedented. There were earthquakes in various provinces, and Kishū, Tosa, and other places suffered great destruction with many deaths.

Kan'en 4th year - changed to Hōreki 1st year
○ Kōnendai-ki opening passage: Kan'en 4th year, 2nd month, 29th day, around the hour of the sheep, there was a great earthquake, and various places in Kyoto were damaged.
　　　　　　　　Hōreki {{1st year, Kanoto-hitsuji, 10th month, 27th day, era name changed due to earthquake}}

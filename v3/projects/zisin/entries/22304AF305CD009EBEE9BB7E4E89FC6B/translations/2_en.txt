Record of the Great Earthquake

This occurred on the second day of the seventh month of Bunsei 13 (the year of the Metal Tiger), at the seventh hour of the day. A great earthquake struck Kyoto, beginning with a rumbling motion, then continuing as a major earthquake. For a while, houses were on the verge of collapse, and unexpectedly everyone prostrated themselves on the ground, lay flat on tatami mats, embraced pillars, and used fences as walking sticks, all praying only for their own safety.

Submitted to both magistrate offices in the tenth month of Kaei 6, Year of the Ox [this line in red ink]

Under the supervision of Lord Tsushima-no-kami
Document reporting on the matter of additional allowances
for increased night watch duties within the auxiliary areas 
of Eitai Bridge and two other bridges
　　　　　Official notification　　　　　Honjo patrol

Since strict orders were issued last Ne month regarding
fire prevention measures, concerning the auxiliary areas 
of Eitai Bridge and two other bridges,
bridge guards and merchants have agreed to take turns 
stationed at the bridge guard posts for night watch duties

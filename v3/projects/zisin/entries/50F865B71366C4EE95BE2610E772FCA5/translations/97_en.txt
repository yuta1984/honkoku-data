1. During strong winds, the headman (nanushi) shall also patrol within his jurisdiction and order that fire sources not be neglected.

1. On ordinary days, water storage buckets shall be prepared, and water shall be continuously drawn and stored so that it does not dry up.

1. Among the headmen, groups of two or three persons shall coordinate with each other, constantly paying attention to fire sources and such within their jurisdictions. Where fire places are built near eaves or other locations where fire sources appear to be handled carelessly, the headmen shall patrol and have them corrected.

One: Last year (Ne year) in the first month, strict orders were issued to all town districts in the city regarding fire prevention measures. Concerning the assisted land areas of the two bridges besides Eitai Bridge, every year from the tenth month to the third month, bridge guards and merchants who come to the same location arrange for two people at a time to be stationed at the bridge guard posts to perform night watch duties. However, after the strict orders were issued, patrols must be conducted every small half-hour (one hour), and since half-night shifts are insufficient to maintain proper coverage, when personnel naturally become short due to illness, scheduling conflicts, and such, night watch duties must be continued without interruption.

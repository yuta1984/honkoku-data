Specifications for ground preparation and road construction at the east and west abutments of Ōkawa Bridge
East and West
One item: Bridge abutments - length 3 ken, width 4 ken - 12 tsubo each, 2 locations
For the above ground preparation, excavate the entire area, place 3 tsubo of foundation soil as fill, cover with the excavated earth, compact thoroughly, spread 5 gō of gravel, and level flat.

【Right page, upper】
Kibe's wife, Katori-hime, was beautiful in appearance, so she was pleasing to her husband's heart and they lived harmoniously. However, one time when she was applying makeup and looked at her face reflected in the mirror, she was startled. But since the kurando (her husband) was broad-minded, he let it pass without comment.

【Right page, lower】
"The fact that I have a serpent form is probably unknown to my wife. I shall adorn myself well with Matsumotoya's Iwato-ko incense."

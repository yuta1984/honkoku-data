One　Report on disruptions caused by the great earthquake at Murei post station and three other villages on the Hokurikudō highway
　　　　　　　Memorandum　　　　　　　　　My magistrate's office
　　One　Village yield: 1,367 koku, 9 to, 3 shō, 8 gō　Shinshū Minochi District
　　　　Number of houses: 189　　　　　　　　　　　Murei post station
　　　　　Of which: 189 houses collapsed (all completely destroyed)
　　　　　　Of which: 10 houses burned down
　　　　　　89 men and women died, plus 5 horses and 2 cattle died
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Same province, same district
　　One　Village yield: 398 koku, 6 to, 5 shō, 2 gō, 4 shaku　　Ōkoma village
　　　　Number of houses: 109
　　　　　Of which: 16 men and women died instantly, plus 13 horses died
　　One　Village yield: 1,365 koku, 8 to　　　　　Same province, same district
　　　　Number of houses: 274　　　　　　　　　　Kashiwabara post station
　　　　　Of which: 154 houses collapsed, 37 men and women died
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　plus 10 horses died
　　One　Village yield: 948 koku, 2 to, 3 shō, 8 gō　　Same province, same district
　　　　Number of houses: 233　　　　　　　　　　Nojiri post station
　　　　　Of which: 143 houses collapsed, 17 men and women died, plus 14 horses died
　　The above concerns my magistrate's office in Shinano Province, Minochi District, at Murei post station on the Hokurikudō highway
　　and three other post stations. From around the middle of the hour of the Dog (around 9 PM) on the night of the 24th of this month until around the first part of the hour of the Boar (around 10 PM) on the following 25th day, there was an unprecedented great earthquake, and as written above, all houses were completely destroyed

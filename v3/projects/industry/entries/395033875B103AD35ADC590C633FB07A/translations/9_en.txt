On the Method of Changing Silkworm Bedding

If you intend to change the silkworm bedding tomorrow, the night before feeding mulberry, sprinkle sugar evenly over the silkworms so that the silkworms are still visible, then immediately feed them cut mulberry. When you do this, they will dislike the sugar and crawl up onto the mulberry. Feed them mulberry in the morning of that day, wait a little while, then use chopsticks to gradually roll up and remove the bedding from the edge of the container. However, this method is too early to use before the second molting.

There is also a method of picking up active silkworms using a net, or changing the silkworms. First spread a net over the silkworms, then feed mulberry on top of it two or three times. While the mulberry is still there, lift the front and back of the net and transfer everything including the net to another container. This net is used continuously from when the silkworms are born until they become thin. The mesh size of the net should be changed according to the time.

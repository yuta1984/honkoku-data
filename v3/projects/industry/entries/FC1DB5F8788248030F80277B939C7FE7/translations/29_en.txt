Chapter 13: Knowing the Success, Failure, and Reliable Development of Silkworms

For initial raising, set up shelves in wide spaces such as tea rooms, placing them on shelves about two feet higher than the tatami mats. Never stack basket upon basket. Particularly, placing baskets directly on top of tatami mats is very bad. Generally, from the day of initial raising, nothing should be placed over the silkworms.

The beginning of sericulture dates back to when the Empress of Emperor Yūryaku, the 22nd human emperor, personally engaged in silkworm raising, as recorded in the Nihon Shoki (Chronicles of Japan).

【Left Page】
Since then, this knowledge has been passed down from one wise person to another, and the operation of silk looms has now become a national treasure (major industry) of our country.

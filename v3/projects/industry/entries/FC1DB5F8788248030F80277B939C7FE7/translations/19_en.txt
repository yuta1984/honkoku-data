Chapter Five《Marginal note: Methods for transporting silkworm eggs over long distances and how to distinguish between superior, medium, and inferior egg quality》
When obtaining silkworm eggs from distant places, do not place them in one's 
bosom or wrap them in furoshiki cloth for transport. Simply place them inside boxes or other containers like baskets, wrap the top
in persimmon-tannin paper and transport them thus. Especially when the sun is strong, one must pay even more attention.
Additionally, do not spread the eggs near fire. In any case, when handling them, wash your hands and treat them with utmost care. However, eggs have
three grades: superior, medium, and inferior. First, superior eggs have firm adhesion, good clarity, and are black in color,

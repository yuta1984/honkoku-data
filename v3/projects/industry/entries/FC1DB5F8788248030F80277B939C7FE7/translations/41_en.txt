When mulberry leaves are applied, they may wither as they are. At such times, water should be sprinkled strongly on the mulberry leaves before giving them. In this way, within five to ten years there will be years of great adversity for silkworms from beginning to end, when the extreme heat becomes unbearable both indoors and outdoors, and the silkworms become agitated. At such times, mulberry should be given any number of times without limitation. In general, having silkworms eat mulberry stems is very bad. If mulberry cannot be prepared in time, sake should be sprayed inside the house and the heat should be cooled with large fans. Also, there are times when one wants to store mulberry for a long time during yard-rearing. At such times, water should be sprinkled on each bundle of mulberry

and placed in the mulberry storehouse, standing them straight without any tilting. Water should be sprinkled once daily, and fresh straw mats should be placed on top. One should think of discarding five or three horse-loads and store beyond the calculations - this is most important.

Fourteen days before, remove the bags and hang the seeds high near the south window. Avoid exposure to warm air and smoke. There are various techniques to make seeds turn blue-green, but none surpass natural blue-green development.

△The silkworm house should face east and south. Avoid the western sun. Seven to eight days before the silkworm hatching, clean thoroughly, sweep away soot, re-paper the windows and sliding doors, wash and clean everything from shelves and beams to bamboo mats, boards, and pillars, set up shelves, open the hearth, light fires to warm the interior of the house, circulate warm air, and prepare all equipment and tools

early. So that when the time comes, there will be no inconvenience. Generally, when raising silkworms, one should measure according to human capacity. Even if one masters the methods of rearing and feeding, if mulberry leaves are insufficient and the silkworms go hungry, if there are too many silkworms with insufficient space in the house, and if there are insufficient people so that hands cannot manage everything, the silkworms will develop diseases and little silk will be obtained. Be cautious not to greedily attempt too much.

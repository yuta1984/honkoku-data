Along with a handbook on cultivation methods - these two volumes in total were distributed. I was commissioned to craft the printing blocks, and after completion, they were kept stored away. However, for the thirteenth memorial service of the late Dōkei, these printing blocks were given to me free of charge as a meritorious act. I gratefully received them and now use them for commercial purposes, hence this explanatory statement. The above.

Kansei 2nd year, 4th month, 2nd day, Sendai Kokubun-chō, Kashiwaya Shōroku (Block carver)

Instructions for Girls in Silkworm Cultivation - Selected by Master Shinshi Shirou

△All things are born from seeds. However, if the seeds are bad, there will surely be harm later. Particularly with silkworms, though they are influenced by the seasonal climate, it is mostly due to poor seeds that very few cocoons are obtained. Even if one seeks good seeds, without knowing the proper methods of raising them, diseases will arise at each molting, causing them to die entirely, and the cocoons will be thin. Since this is women's work, it should be learned and mastered.

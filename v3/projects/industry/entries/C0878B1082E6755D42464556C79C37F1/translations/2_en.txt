Preface to Sericulture Essentials
Our family has for generations made a living by selling silk thread. Therefore, from the way of sericulture to the methods of planting mulberry trees, we have never failed to study their proper methods and research their essence. One day, seeing a work called "Sericulture Essentials" written by the venerable Ishiguro, I struck my knee and sighed, saying: "Ah, how wonderfully this way has become widely practiced! This is not merely a great fortune for us alone, but..."

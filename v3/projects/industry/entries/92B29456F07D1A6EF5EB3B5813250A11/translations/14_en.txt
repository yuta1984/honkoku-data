　　　　How to Plant Mulberry Trees
一 In spring during the summer solstice period, in autumn at the end of August, dig holes about three feet deep, remove
　the hardpan soil, put fertile topsoil inside, spread the mulberry roots well,
　and plant so that the roots do not become entangled.

【Left Page】
一 From the following year onward, every year in spring during the summer solstice period and in autumn in September,
　dig around the mulberry roots a little, apply liquid manure, dry for four to five days,
　and then cover with soil.
一 In October, gather the branches and bundle them with rope. When spring comes, remove the
　rope and spread out the branches.

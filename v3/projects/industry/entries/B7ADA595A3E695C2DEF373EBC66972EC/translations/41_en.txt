　Seed Production and Cocoon Harvesting
　　　Chapter 1
Mature silkworms that have already begun forming cocoons complete their work in three days and nights, and transform into pupae in about four days. Their color and luster are like water, and after six or seven days they change to tea-brown color. At this point, those who intend to collect egg seeds arrange about one to (volume unit) each in baskets, lining them up on shelves

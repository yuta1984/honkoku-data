Brief Introduction to the Sericulture Compendium

   it was first recorded in the Age of Gods chronicles, and thereafter appears 
   scattered throughout the official histories of successive dynasties. We can 
   see that this industry already existed in ancient times, sufficient evidence 
   to know that it was certainly not something transmitted from overseas. However, 
   since this industry was largely in the hands of rural people, there are no 
   precise records, making it impossible to clearly understand its origins. 
   Nevertheless, if we trace back to antiquity, seek similar cases, and explore 
   the roots, investigating these source causes would not be extremely difficult.

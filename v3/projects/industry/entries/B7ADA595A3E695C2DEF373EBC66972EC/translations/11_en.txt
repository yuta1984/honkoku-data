Moreover, facts are often intermixed and indistinguishable. Upon reflection, silkworms originally arose from the Creator's particularly profound and benevolent intention toward humanity. The process from hatching to cocoon formation, transformation into moths, and leaving egg seeds for the following year—if this covenant is not broken, how could this be mere coincidence? Therefore, those who fail to pay attention to nurturing them with care and thus ruin the industry are sinners against the Creator.

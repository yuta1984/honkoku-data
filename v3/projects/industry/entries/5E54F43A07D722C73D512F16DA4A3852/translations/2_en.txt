Silkworm Seed Directory by Elder Kenzan

Preface
Our Ōshū region's Date and Shinobu counties are well-suited lands for sericulture, and have been renowned since ancient times as the home of silkworm seeds—a fact widely known throughout the world. Therefore, people seeking these seeds gather like clouds from all provinces. There are also many from both counties who go out to sell them. However, in recent times, human nature has become increasingly shallow and corrupt, and people try to profit by borrowing the name of good seeds to sell inferior ones...

　Preface to Secret Records of Sericulture
Planting mulberry trees on a five-tan residential plot was a system established by ancient sage kings to provide sustenance and care for the people. The benefits that mulberry and silkworms bring to the people are indeed great in their merit. Therefore, from ancient times to the present, those who cultivate them have been most numerous. However, without detailed knowledge of the methods for cultivation, one cannot make proper use of them.

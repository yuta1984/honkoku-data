Inaba Jirōemon's Record of Military Achievements
　Jirōemon was a person from Owari Province. He joined the forces of Matsudaira Tango-no-kami and went to Arima, where he distinguished himself in military service. This is a record of Jirōemon's military achievements written by one Tsutsumi Iori.

Nishimura Gohyōe Memorandum
　Gohyōe was a retainer of Matsuura Hizen-no-kami. He arrived in Arima following Matsuura Takumi, who was an envoy of Hizen-no-kami.

Bettō Mokuza'emon Memorandum
　A memorandum by Kumabe Mokuza'emon, the bettō (administrator) of Arima-machi in Shimabara.

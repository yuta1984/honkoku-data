Uchi Kyūzaemon and others fought and attacked without difficulty. Negishi Shōdayū and Akabane Genbei achieved first breakthrough with this unit. Kamio Tarōbei, Katsuura Jitayū, Maeshima Tazaemon, Hirai Shingobei, Ogawa Matazaemon, and Ōtsuka Kakubei - some were wounded in the hand, others suffered injuries. Suzuki Sakuzaemon, Kobayashi Kyūbei, Ishikawa Ichizaemon, Asai Taibei, Suzuki Sukeemon and others suffered wounds. Mochizuki Jiemon, Kawase Jizaemon, Ikeda Shinbei, Watanabe Izaemon, Hirai Jūdayū, Nakano Yadayū, Noguchi Yoemon, Ikeda Hyōdayū, Kimura Jinbei, Narita Kizaemon, Murasame Hachirōzaemon, Ingai Sadayū, Kunieda Moza'emon, Ashisuke Hachibei, Murasame Yahei,

Aida Shichirōemon, Niwa Jizaemon, Asai Rokubei, Kurosawa Jūemon, Mizukami Jūrōemon, Akimoto Yaemon, Iwata Tarōemon and others distinguished themselves in battle. The messengers of his old acquaintance Koide Yamato-no-kami Yoshihide - Mutō Shōdayū and Ban Sanzaemon - as well as the messenger of his brother-in-law Ogasawara Saemon-no-suke Masanobu - Tokiwa San'emon - each distinguished themselves in battle. Kasuga Sakuzaemon, messenger of Ogasawara Iki-no-kami, also distinguished himself.

It also records that tonight Mizuno Hyūga-no-kami went to Itakura Mondo's camp position and said: "Though you are young in years, your repeated distinguished service and today's memorial battle for your late father are admirable in every respect," and praised him. "You have achieved fame seven times, and with this you have proven much," he said, and brought out the sword of Uta Kunifusa.

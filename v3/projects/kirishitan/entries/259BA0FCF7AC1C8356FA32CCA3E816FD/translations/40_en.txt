"The area at the base of the stone wall where we are attacking will surely become our house's point of assault, but if you say the climbing is difficult, then we should withdraw from there and advance into the main castle from our vanguard position." Based on this, Nabenosuke, Ikuta, Takamura, Kajimura and others gathered before Naiki's formation. Also, Yoda Sōemon, the banner magistrate of the aforementioned vanguard, had something on his mind and had removed his armor, wearing only his helmet, when he was wounded by an arrow at the second bailey. However, since it was a shallow wound, he forced himself to advance toward the main castle. When Itō Kanzaemon and others were attacking up the waist enclosure, Yoda also followed, rushing up and raising his banner within the waist bailey. Yamamura Hikoshirō, Toda Den'emon, Ikeda Moemon, Satomura Jirōzaemon, Okabe Gorōbei, Kawamura Ichizaemon, Taki Uemon Saburō, the rōnin Ikoma Han'emon, and about thirty others in total gathered around the banner that Yoda had erected.

Someone said, "Even if the enemy should thrust out at us, this place has narrow terrain and poor conditions for battle. Please quickly lower the banner to the lower tier and withdraw from this position immediately." Yoda said, "Each of you desires to thrust with spears and make your names known, but one must choose the terrain wisely. We have the duty to establish and defend the banner.

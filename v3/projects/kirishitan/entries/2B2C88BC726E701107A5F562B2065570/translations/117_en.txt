Later, Lord Terasawa Hyōganokami completed his administration of Amakusa and crossed the sea to Arima. Between the positions held by Lords Kuroda and Nabeshima, siege works were constructed in rotation, with each adding watchtowers and bamboo bundles as they saw fit. The Kitaoka entrance and third bailey were situated on high ground, with each position connected by slopes. Lord Tachibana Sakon

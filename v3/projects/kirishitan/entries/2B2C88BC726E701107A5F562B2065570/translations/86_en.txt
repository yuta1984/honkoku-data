Chronicle of the Christian Suppression, Volume 20

First month, second day, hinoe-tora (Fire Tiger day)

According to the memorandum of Betto Mokuzaemon: From the second day of the first month, we sent rifle fire over bamboo bundles.

According to the Chronicle of Brave Deeds: The military forces of our house that were dispatched to Nagasaki in the previous winter arrived at the Arima camp on the evening of the second day.

According to the Hosokawa House Chronicle of the Shimabara Campaign: On the second day of the first month, Lord Mitsutoshi led his troops from Kawashiri by ship and crossed over to Tsugikawa in Shimabara. That night they made camp in the field at Tsugikawa and lit bonfires in many places.

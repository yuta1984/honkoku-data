Chronicle of the Suppression of Christians, Volume 18

15th year, Year of the Dog and Tiger, First month (large)
According to the military record of Inaba Jiroemon, Lord Matsudaira Tango-no-kami
ordered that Lord Matsudaira Uemon-no-suke should take up position in late first month.
He immediately departed from Kunimoto, and the aforementioned Jiroemon also accompanied him.

New Year's Day, Day of Wood and Ox
According to the Amakusa Military Chronicle, on New Year's Day, Arima Hyobu-taiyu Tadayori
hastened the vanguard, and during the night advanced a great army to the outer bailey's
Hirahata area outside the castle.

He possessed explosive devices and requested through Kataoka and Ishikawa whether he might use them and whether assistance could be provided. Since this was transmitted by a veteran meritorious retainer from the Takeda house and was Shigemon's proposal, permission was granted according to his wishes, and many hands were assigned to help manufacture them. However, when thrown into the castle, the fire spread poorly, and before the flames could take hold, the enemy threw them back at our forces, rendering them completely useless.

Knowing this, they first used treasures to make your mother submit to their sect, then gradually spreading from there, made you a follower, and plotted to approach and become intimate with me through you - this is certain. Now, while Buddhist preaching and religious instruction may be said to be the same regardless of who speaks, people believe and the Way is practiced according to that person's moral virtue. Like the Nanban sect, spending treasures to make people submit through their material desires is something I have never heard of before. This plot to approach and become intimate with your mother is truly something to be feared. What harm they might cause afterward is incalculable. Especially since I hear that among the daimyo of our time, there are some who have faith in this sect. If we treat this matter carelessly and many distinguished persons of high rank come to submit to this sect, even if we wanted to prohibit this sect later, it would not be easily accomplished. Nothing is better than cutting off the roots while they are still tender shoots. The Nanban temple should be destroyed immediately." So saying, he commanded Masuda Uemon-no-jō and Natsuka Ōkura-taifu: "Do not kill a single person who came from Nanban - capture them alive. Arrest every single person who became a follower at the Nanban temple and belongs to the sect's domain." He dispatched three thousand cavalry toward the Nanban temple.

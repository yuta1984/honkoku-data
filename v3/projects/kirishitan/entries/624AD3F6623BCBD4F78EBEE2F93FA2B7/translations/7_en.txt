...is the Great Compassion Kanzeon (Avalokiteśvara), when he softens his light and mingles with the dust, he appears as Kan Shōjō (Sugawara no Michizane) and manifests his traces at Kitano, being venerated as the deity who protects a hundred generations of emperors. Which great shrine or ancestral temple lacks this principle? Moreover, what we reverently call Kunitokotachi-no-mikoto is a deity who existed from before the opening of heaven and earth, when there was not a single human being—would you call even this a human? There is nothing to say.

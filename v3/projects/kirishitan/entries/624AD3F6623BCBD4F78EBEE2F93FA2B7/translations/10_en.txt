Having Shuddhodana as father and Queen Maya as mother, he manifested the appearance of birth, proclaimed his final nirvana at the crane grove, and Hachiman Daibosatsu was born with Emperor Chuai as father and Empress Jingu as mother—based on this principle of embodiment, they appear to think [these deities] are human. If so, then when Christians speak of their principal deity Jesus Christ being born with Joseph as father and Santa Maria as mother, this

is indeed right in the midst of humanity. On this side [Buddhism], we do not make humans the lord of heaven and earth.
The Jesus Christ that Christians speak of, in his causal state, was originally human, and this does not differ from the provisional manifestations of gods or the causal states of buddhas. Therefore, let us set aside this matter for now between us. Since the original ground of gods is also Buddha, there is no need to argue about it.

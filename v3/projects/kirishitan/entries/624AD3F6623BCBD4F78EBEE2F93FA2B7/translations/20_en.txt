What do you call practicing such immorality? Even regarding human rulers, this should be criticized. When Yu of the Xia dynasty ascended to the throne, after seeing criminals, he descended from his carriage and wept, saying: "The people of Yao and Shun made the heart of Yao and Shun their heart. I have become ruler, and each of the common people makes their own heart their heart. I grieve over this." Thus he blamed himself.

【Left page】
When King Tang of the Shang dynasty faced a great drought lasting seven years and the people suffered, the grand historian divined and reported that "you should pray using human sacrifice." Tang thought, "This is all a heavenly disaster caused by my sins, not the fault of the people." He decided to become the sacrifice himself, riding in a plain carriage with white horses, wrapping his body in white cogongrass to serve as the sacrificial offering, and praying in the field of the mulberry grove. With six matters he blamed himself, saying: "Is my governance without moderation? Have the people lost their occupations? Are the palace buildings too high? Are women's petitions too prevalent? Am I giving bribes? Are slanderous words..."

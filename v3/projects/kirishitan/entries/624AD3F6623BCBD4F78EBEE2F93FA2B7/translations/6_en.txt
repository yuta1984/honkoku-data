Refuting this, [the author] says: "To see buddhas and gods as merely human beings is the mistaken view of unlearned people, truly a perspective befitting the Christians. All buddhas have three bodies: the Dharma Body (hosshin), Reward Body (hōshin), and Response Body (ōshin). Although the responsive Tathāgata manifests the eight phases for the sake of saving sentient beings, providing benefits, and employing skillful means, the Dharma Body Tathāgata is the eternally abiding Buddha who has existed since beginningless time, therefore being beyond...

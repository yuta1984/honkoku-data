Preface to Hadaius
What Hadaius (Christians) make their doctrine consists of teachings about the nomenclature of scriptures, principal objects of worship, causal stages, and other such matters. However, unless one is a Buddhist, one cannot hear these teachings and cannot refute their doctrine. Unless one is a Shinto priest, one cannot know these matters and cannot argue against their followers. Therefore, heretical teachings arise daily, and immorality flourishes monthly - this has continued for many long years.

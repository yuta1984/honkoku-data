When the Lord's advent comes and the time arrives for Him to manifest His divine glory, this was predetermined and planned by Him in advance, so it absolutely does not apply as a path for all people. Therefore, to place improper hope that He will make such arrangements for any person whatsoever—is this not the folly of those who have sunk completely into delusion?

When we look at the governance of the world's nations, although we may cite examples of righteous government, there are times when pardons are granted outside the law. This does not apply to all people, nor should it be said that this means going against the normal constitution and deliberately implementing extraordinary laws. The governance of the "Ecclesia" that God has arranged is also like this. The ordinary laws are, as the "Apostle" declares, that the end of evil people follows their conduct—good conduct accompanies a good death, and an evil life brings about an evil death. Moreover, good works lead to the path of eternal life, while those who commit evil shall sink into endless suffering. This is the ordinary principle.

These principles are fully revealed in scripture, sung in the "Psalms," taught in the scripture called "Proverbs," shown by the "Apostle," and recorded in the scriptures of the "Evangelio." Since these are God's incomparable divine power and mercy, there shall be recompense according to each person's conduct. In the end, this is what the complete doctrine of Christian learning means. Based on this principle, understand that both good and evil people shall receive recompense according to their ordinary conduct.

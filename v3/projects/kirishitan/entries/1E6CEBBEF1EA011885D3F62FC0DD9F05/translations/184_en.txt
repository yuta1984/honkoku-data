○ Chapter 12: Other Essential Articles for Christians

Disciple: "Are there other matters pertaining to doctrine?"

Master: "Indeed there are many. The works of Misericordia (mercy), the virtues of Te Deum Laudamus, the Cardinal Virtues, the gifts of Spiritus Sanctus (Holy Spirit), Pena Venturanza (beatitudes), the Confisan oratio (prayer of confession), prayers when eating - these are such matters."

○ Works of Mercy

There are fourteen works of mercy: the first seven pertain to the body, and the latter seven pertain to the spirit.

Disciple: What is the fifth commandment?

Master: It is the duty to offer donations to the bishop according to local customs.

　　　○Chapter 10: On the Seven Mortal Sins

Disciple: You have already taught us the commandments of Deus (God) and the commandments of Santa Ecclesia (Holy Church). Now, how many mortal sins are there?

Master: Although there are many types of sins, there are seven sins that form the root of all sins. ○First is pride, second is greed, third is lust, fourth is envy, fifth is gluttony, sixth is envy, seventh is sloth. These are all called mortal sins.

Disciple: What does the eighth Article mean: "I believe in the Spiritus Sanctus (Holy Spirit) and also in the holy Ecclesia Catholica (Catholic Church)"?

Master: This Article shows us two things. First, the Spiritus Sanctus is a distinct Persona (Person) who shares the same divine nature, wisdom, power, and goodness as Deus Patre (God the Father) and Filius (God the Son), and we must believe in Him.

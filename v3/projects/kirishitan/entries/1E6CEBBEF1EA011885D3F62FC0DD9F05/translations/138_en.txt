...without [merit], understanding that one makes [confession] directly before God, with deep reverence, humbling oneself and becoming like a neighbor, one should confess one's sins.

Disciple: What does it mean to [confess] truthfully and honestly?

Master: One should not reveal sins one has not committed, and regardless of shame or for whatever detailed reason, one should not hide one's sins but confess them clearly, understanding that one reveals them directly to God, who knows completely even things we have not yet thought or will think.

Disciple: What does it mean to leave no sins unreported?

Master: One should carefully examine one's conscience and confess all the sins that one can remember...

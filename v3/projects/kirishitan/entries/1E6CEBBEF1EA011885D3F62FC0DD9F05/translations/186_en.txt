Seventh: praying to God for those who are dying and those who bear enmity against us

○ There are four cardinal virtues:
First: Prudence, Second: Justice, Third: Fortitude, Fourth: Temperance

○ There are three theological virtues:
First: Faith, Second: Hope, Third: Charity

○ There are seven gifts of the Holy Spirit:
First: Wisdom, Second: Understanding, Third: Counsel, Fourth: Fortitude, Fifth: Knowledge, Sixth: Piety, Seventh: Fear of God

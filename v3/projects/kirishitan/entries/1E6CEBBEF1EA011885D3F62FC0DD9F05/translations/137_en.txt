...even though [priests] are present, since there are many Christians, when it is impossible for each one to have their confession heard individually, if one does not make confession once during the year, this does not constitute contempt of this mandamento (commandment). Nevertheless, when it is possible, one should make confession.

Disciple: What are the essential matters for making confession properly?

Master: There are three essential matters: first, to be humble; second, to reveal [sins] truthfully and honestly; third, to leave no sins [unconfessed].

Disciple: In what manner should one be humble?

Master: The person making confession should think in their heart that they are a sinner, and [should approach] the cleric who can grant forgiveness of sins...

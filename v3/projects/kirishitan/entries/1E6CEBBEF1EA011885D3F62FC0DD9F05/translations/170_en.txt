Master: Merit and suffering of the heart are what arrange [atonement] through the suffering of sins bestowed from above.

Disciple: What is the fifth sacrament?

Master: It is the sacrament called Extremaunção (Extreme Unction), received through the precious holy oil blessed by the bishop. This sacrament is given only to the sick when facing death. Through this sacrament, our Lord Jesus Christ bestows His grace upon the body, purifies the soul of remaining sin's defilement, and adds His strength so that one may endure the difficulties of death in a good manner.

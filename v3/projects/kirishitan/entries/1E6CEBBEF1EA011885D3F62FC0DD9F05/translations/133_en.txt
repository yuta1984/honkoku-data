Master: Among the many aids available, the most important is not speaking during Mass and avoiding anything that would cause distraction of the mind.

Disciple: Are there prayers when the Most Holy Father has the people venerate the sacrament?

Master: Certainly there are. Since Our Lord Jesus Christ supports the world upon the holy cross, we kneel in worship and pray, asking Him to forgive our sins. This is that prayer.

Disciple: What prayers are said when the chalice is presented for veneration?

Master: The precious [blood] of Our Lord Jesus Christ, which He shed upon the cross to save all people...

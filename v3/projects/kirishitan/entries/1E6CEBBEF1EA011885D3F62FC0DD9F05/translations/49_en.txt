Disciple: What is the significance of making the sign of the cross on these three places - the forehead, mouth, and chest?

Master: Making it on the forehead is so that God may remove evil thoughts. Making it on the mouth is so that evil words and falsehoods may be driven away from the mouth. And making it on the chest is so that evil deeds arising from the heart may be driven away. Demons fear nothing more than the cross. This is because, being spirits, swords and weapons are of no use against them. However, since our Lord Jesus Christ died upon the cross, He has defeated them and set mankind free, so apart from those who would approach them...

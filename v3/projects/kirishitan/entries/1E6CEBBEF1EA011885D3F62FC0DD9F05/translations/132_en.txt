Disciple: What is the Mass?

Master: It is the sacrifice offered to God the Father together with the sacred body and blood of Our Lord Jesus Christ as the sacrifice of the cross, for both the living and the dead. This is ordained to help us remember the entire life's works and passion of Our Lord Jesus Christ. Therefore, when Christians attend Mass, they should contemplate Our Lord's passion and worship with devotion. These matters will be clarified later when we discuss the sacrament of the Eucharist.

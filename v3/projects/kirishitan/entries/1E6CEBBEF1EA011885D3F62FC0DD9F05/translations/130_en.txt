These are commandments that the Christians of that place must observe without fail. Among the numerous commandments that apply to the entire Church, five articles are particularly distinguished:

First: One must attend and worship at Mass on Domingo (Sunday) and feast days.

Second: One must make confession at least once a year.

Third: One must receive the sacrament of the Eucharist at Easter.

Fourth: When ordained by Santa Egreja (Holy Church), one must observe fasting, and must not eat meat on Friday and Saturday.

Fifth: One must offer tithes and Mass stipends.

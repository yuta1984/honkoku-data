Disciple: What are the three powers of the soul?

Master: First is the power to recall past events - memory; second is the power called understanding that knows and distinguishes things; third is the power called will that inclines toward love and hatred.

Disciple: Why are these called the powers of the soul?

Master: Because they are three natural powers inherent in the soul. These accompany the soul even after departing from this body. Through these, they become the instruments for receiving joy or suffering in the afterlife.

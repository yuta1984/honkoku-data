Disciple: Are there any other meanings besides this?

Master: It is to represent that He saves us through the Holy Cross.

Disciple: When should one recite this sign?

Master: When beginning all things and when encountering difficulties, especially when going to sleep and waking up, when leaving one's dwelling or entering a church, and also when taking food and drink.

Disciple: What is the purpose of reciting this sign frequently?

Master: Since it is so that Deus may deliver us from the hands of the enemy, one should recite it at any time and in any place.

Disciple: What is the significance of reciting it when beginning any action?

Master: So that our actions may not be hindered by our enemy...

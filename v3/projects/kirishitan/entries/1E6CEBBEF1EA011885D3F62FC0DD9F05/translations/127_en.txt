Disciple: What should one do in order not to neglect even upon waking, and to fulfill one's duties beyond that?

Master: First, upon waking, one should examine that day's thoughts, words, and deeds, and through contrição (contrition) beseech forgiveness for the sins committed. Likewise, with grace, one should resolve to reform one's conduct and offer appropriate oratio (prayers).

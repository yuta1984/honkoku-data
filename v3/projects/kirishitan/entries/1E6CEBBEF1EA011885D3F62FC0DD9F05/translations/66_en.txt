Disciple: What do we petition for in the second article?

Master: "Thy kingdom come." The meaning of this is that we may escape from evil deeds and sins, and that Deus and His Son Jesus Christ may guide us with grace in this world and with glory in the afterlife.

Disciple: What is the third thing we petition for?

Master: "Thy will be done on earth as it is in heaven." The meaning of this is that just as the angels in heaven obey Deus and carry out His will, so too may all human beings on earth obey Deus and serve according to His precious will.

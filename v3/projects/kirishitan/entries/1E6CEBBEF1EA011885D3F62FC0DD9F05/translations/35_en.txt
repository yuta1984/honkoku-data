○ Things to be striven for through conduct correspond to the virtue of ☩ Caritas (Charity/Love). Without understanding these things, one would frequently transgress against the commandments of Deus. Therefore, matters pertaining to these three virtues are of paramount importance for Christians.

Hence, good men who have gained the name of scholars have written many scriptures about these matters. From among these, the essential parts should be selected and arranged in print to serve as a mirror that illuminates confusion.

Thus, in order to teach Christians what is most important for salvation in the afterlife, the superior of the ☩ Companhia (Society of Jesus) has prepared this small scripture

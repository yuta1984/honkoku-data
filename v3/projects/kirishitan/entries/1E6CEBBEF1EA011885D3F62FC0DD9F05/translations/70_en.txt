This means that when we are assailed by temptations in this world, we ask with the heart that relies upon God's grace so that we may not succumb to them.

Disciple: What should we ask for in the seventh article?

Master: "Deliver us from evil." This means asking to be delivered from sins that harm the soul and from calamities that affect the physical body.

Disciple: Is there any prayer superior to the Pater Noster?

Master: There is no other prayer superior to this. This is the supreme prayer.

Disciple: What is the reason for this?

Master: Our Lord Jesus included in this prayer the essential articles that should be requested of God...

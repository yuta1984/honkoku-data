Master: One should constantly be careful not to make sworn statements.

Disciple: Then, in order to affirm the truth of things, how should one speak?

Master: One should say "truly" or "in truth," or use expressions like "without doubt" or "certainly."

Disciple: How should one observe the third commandment?

Master: There are two things in observing this. First, on Sundays and feast days proclaimed by the Church, one should refrain from work. However, when there are unavoidable circumstances, working may not constitute a sin. Second, on such days one should attend Mass in its entirety from the beginning...

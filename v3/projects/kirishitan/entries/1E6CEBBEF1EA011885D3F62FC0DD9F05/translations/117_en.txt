Disciple: What does it mean to truly make an oath?

Master: To make a sworn statement knowing it to be false, or to make an oath concerning matters that are doubtful whether true or false, is to call upon God as witness to falsehood, and therefore becomes a grave sin even if it concerns a trivial matter.

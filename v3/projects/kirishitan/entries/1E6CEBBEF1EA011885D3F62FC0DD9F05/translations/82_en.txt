Disciple: Having taught us well how to properly rely upon and pray to [God], now please show us the way to believe with certainty.

Master: This is about knowing the Creed and the articles of faith contained within it. Now I shall teach this. The Creed is: We truly believe through the cross that Deus (God), who fulfills all things and created heaven and earth, is the Father, and also [we believe in] His only Son, our Lord Jesus Christ.

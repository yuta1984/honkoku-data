This sacrament serves as the foundation for becoming a Christian and also for receiving other sacraments.

Disciple: What is baptism?

Master: Baptism is the sacrament by which one becomes a Christian. Through this, one receives faith and grace, and it is the sacrament that forgives original sin and all sins committed up to that time. This applies when it is received according to the proper method.

However, since this sacrament is an indispensable path for salvation in the afterlife, Lord Jesus Christ has granted permission for anyone, whether man or woman, to administer this sacrament in places where there is no priest. Therefore, anyone can administer it. This is also in accordance with what Lord Jesus Christ taught, in order to receive this sacrament properly while maintaining the essential ceremony mentioned above. Since this administration is often needed in places where there are no priests, it is essential that all Christians learn the way to administer baptism.

Master: It is not such a ritual. To explain how this is: In both the Host and the Chalice, the body of our Lord Jesus Christ does not separate from His blood, but dwells there as He exists in heaven. However, when He shed His blood on the cross, that blood was separated from His body. Therefore, in order to perform this immeasurable mystery of His Passion, the Lord taught us to recite the Catholic formula over the Host and Chalice. Through the holy power of those words, the substance of bread is transformed into the being of our Lord Jesus Christ, and the substance of wine is also transformed into the blood of our Lord.

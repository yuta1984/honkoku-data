Master: The sixty-three prayers are offered in correspondence to the number of years of the Holy Mother Birzen. Also, the one hundred fifty prayers correspond to the fifteen mysteries: five articles are of joy, five articles are of sorrow, and the present five articles are offered in correspondence to the mysteries of glory. The titles of these fifteen articles are found in a book opened in sequential order.

Disciple: Who is the female figure that is enshrined on the altar?

Master: It is an image for remembering the Holy Mother Birzen Maria who resides in heaven, therefore it is something that should be venerated.

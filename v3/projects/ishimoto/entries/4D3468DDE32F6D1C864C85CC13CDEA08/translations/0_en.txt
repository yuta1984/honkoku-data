Record of Places Affected by the Great Earthquake and Great Tsunami

One: From Osaka to the Shikoku road, Chugoku region, and Kyushu area, earthquakes continued daily without cease for five days from the 4th and 5th to the 8th day. In Owari Province's Miya-juku, there was great damage with 23 buildings collapsed along the coast, the government office collapsed, great tsunamis at Kamezaki and Handa, and countless minor damages around Tsushima, Nagoya, and Kiyosu. In Ise, both Kuwana Castle and its castle town suffered great damage. Around Nagashima, 43 houses collapsed in four markets, the coastal area was hit by great tsunamis, and around Shono and Ishiyakushi there were many collapsed houses due to ground fissures. Kameyama Castle was damaged and many townhouses collapsed severely. There was some damage around Kambe and Shiroko, great damage to Tsu Castle and its towns with great tsunamis along the coast. In Yamada town, houses and storehouses suffered great damage, but reverently, both Grand Shrines had no particular damage. Around Tamaru castle town, the Yaki-yama pass collapsed greatly, blocking traffic. Around Kii Province's Tanabe castle town, around Shingu castle town, around Kumano, at Sōmyō, Tsukumo-ura, Kuroe, Hikata, and Fujishiro, great tsunamis caused tides to rise about three feet above floor level. The same Hiro-ura suffered about 70% destruction and loss. Also, Kawahara, Minoshima, and Yura port were washed away, and there was great damage around Ōsaki, Arida, Hino, Kada, and Hidaka. In Izumi Province, Kishiwada suffered great damage, and Sakai town had great damage similar to Osaka. Around Settsu Province's Takatsuki castle town, around Yawata and Yamazaki, Yodo castle town, Fushimi towns, Kyoto's inner and outer areas, Yamashiro, Yamato, and Kawachi provinces had countless minor collapses.

One: Echizen Province's Fukui castle town suffered great damage, as did around Tsuruga, and around Tanba Kameyama. Throughout the Shikoku road, Awa Province's Tokushima castle town suffered great damage, and additionally there were fires with great damage around the Five Hundred Rakan. Tosa Province had great tsunamis, Awaji Island had great tsunamis, around Marugame castle town, and Takamatsu castle town suffered great damage. Iyo Province had great earthquakes, around Settsu Province's Sanda castle town, Ikeda and Itami, Harima Province's Akashi castle town suffered great damage, around Himeji and Akō. Around Bizen Province's Tanokuchi and Shimomura, Bitchu Province's Kurashiki and Tamashima, Bingo Province's Onomichi, Tomo, around Fukuyama castle town, around Mihara castle town, Aki Province's Hiroshima and Miyajima. Suō and Nagato provinces, around Buzen Province's Kokura castle town, around Tsurusaki had many collapsed houses. In Bungo Province, about 400 houses collapsed around Funai castle town, 234 houses collapsed at Beppu magistrate's office, and at Tsurusaki. In Kumamoto domain territory there were great earthquakes and great tsunamis, the Hyūga Sea had abnormal tidal movements and the ocean was greatly turbulent. In Hizen and Higo provinces, Nagasaki had 400 collapsed houses, and the Chinese quarter and Dutch quarter suffered great damage. This is recorded here as it is an unprecedented occurrence since ancient times.

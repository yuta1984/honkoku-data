Detailed Map of the Great Edo Fire on the 15th Day of the 11th Month, Year of the Horse, Ansei 5

On the 15th day of the 11th month at the hour of the Tiger (around 4 AM), a fire broke out near Sakuma-machi. Due to strong north winds, the fire spread to Uchi-Kanda and Toshima-machi. To the west, it reached Kanda Mikawa-machi, Kamakura riverside, the moat edge, and Kaji Bridge. To the east, from Kanda, Bakuro-machi, Yokoyama-machi, Odenma-machi, Kodenma-machi, extending from Imagawa Bridge to Nangoku Bridge - all were completely destroyed. The fire reached Nihonbashi Honcho area, Nihonbashi, Minami Gofuku-cho, Aomono-cho, Yokkaichi, and Hon Zaimoku-cho.

Tori Ichome, both east and west sides up to Kyobashi. Tadokoro-cho, Setomono-cho, Ise-cho, Horidome, Horie-cho, Kobuna-cho, Ningyo-cho, Tomizawa-cho, and the area around Koami-cho - the entire Hatchobori district was completely burned down.

1. Imo tofu (Sweet potato tofu)　　Sometimes made by putting it in bamboo shoots and steaming
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　[There are also other methods]

1. Tansaku tofu (Search tofu)　　　[Special] cutting method

1. Kinukoshi tofu (Silk-strained tofu)　[No method provided]

1. Arare tofu (Hailstone tofu)　　　Cut well-drained tofu into small pieces,
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　pass through a coarse sieve to remove corners

1. Haze tofu (Goby fish tofu)　　　Grind softly drained tofu and
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　chop with a knife, mix with hot water

1. Shibori tofu (Squeezed tofu)　　Roughly squeeze tsukami tofu, wrap in blue [cloth],
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　spread on cloth, steam and cut into pieces

1. Aomame tofu (Green bean tofu)　[No method provided]

1. Ō-chikuwa tofu (Large tube tofu)　[No method provided]

1. Ichara tofu - Same as above

1. Pine-fiber tofu - Spread on a board, sprinkle with poppy seeds, grill and cut

1. Purple tofu - Grind well and mix, grind and mix with perilla
   Add a little red coloring, spread and steam for use

1. Drawstring-bag tofu - Hollow out the inside of an eggplant, add seasoned
   ingredients of various kinds to ground tofu, close the opening
   Season and use

1. Deer-roll tofu - Wrap tube-shaped tofu with ice and roll back
   Season well and use

1. Fawn-spot tofu - Grind well, then while hot
   Mix before it can be cut and steam for use

1. [Unnamed] tofu - Grill dengaku tofu, soak glutinous rice
   in honey, grind well, apply and grill
   Use with sauce

1. Daikon radish rice - Whether shaved thin or julienned, 
                       can be grated and mixed into regular rice

1. Nori seaweed rice - Any method, sprinkle on top

1. Rapeseed rice - Crumble tofu and mix for coloring
                   This is steamed rice

1. Fawn-spot rice - Sprinkle red azuki beans over
                    parboiled rice and serve

1. Reed rice varieties - Julienned, whether cut small
                        or finely cut, sprinkle on top

1. Dried rice - Wash dried rice well, when steaming rice
               sprinkle with salt and just lay underneath

1. Taro and greens rice - Cook with red ingredients underneath, adjust salt

1. Sea bream center rice - Soak very well in water, steaming is best

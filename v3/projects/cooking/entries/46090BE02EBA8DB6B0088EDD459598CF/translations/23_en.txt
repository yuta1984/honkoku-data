37. Grass Sesame Tofu　　Grind sesame with water mill, strain and put in water, remove the clear water on top, mix with ground tofu and steam
○True sesame tofu is shown in the appendix

38. Wrapped Tofu　　Cut one block in two, hollow out the center and fill with walnut miso, cover again with tofu, wrap in Mino paper and boil. The seasoning on top is according to preference
○Walnut miso is made by grinding white sesame and walnuts in equal parts, then grinding together with miso

39. Millet Tofu　　Make kudzu sauce base with grated wasabi, take well-cooked oboro tofu, squeeze out the liquid and arrange, then thickly sprinkle only the yolks of hard-boiled eggs on top

Seventy-six: Eel-style Tofu　　Spread Asakusa nori seaweed on a board, then spread ground tofu mixed with a little wheat flour to about three bu thickness on top, cut into strips about 2.5-2.6 sun wide, quickly fry in sesame oil, skewer, and grill with sansho pepper and soy sauce glaze.

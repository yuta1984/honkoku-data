Forty-five: Tofu Sushi　　Cut the pressed tofu from item fifteen of the previous volume into large pieces, put into boiled raw soy sauce and quickly simmer, then set aside to cool. ○Spread yuba (tofu skin) seasoned with sake, salt, and soy sauce on a board, lay out white rice, sprinkle a little vinegar, evenly scatter chopped wood ear mushrooms, arrowhead, pickled ginger in plum vinegar, and Japanese pepper, arrange the simmered tofu on top, roll tightly, wrap in bamboo sheath, apply weight, leave for about two hours, remove and cut into small rounds.

Forty-six: Pine Wood Tofu　　Add soy sauce from the beginning to bonito dashi, dissolve fukusa miso, bring to a boil over high heat, cut tofu into appropriate sizes and add, then simmer over medium heat for about half a day. Add ground Japanese pepper. Raku-yaki square dishes are good for serving.

Forty-seven: Azuma Dengaku　　Grill like regular dengaku, mix mustard with water, spread over one surface, grill until dry, then immediately apply kudzu starch to make dengaku. ○Instead of mustard, mix Kanshinjitemple's winter-bleached powder with water, spread and grill until it puffs up moderately, serve with Japanese pepper miso - this is also one type of preparation.
△Soak glutinous rice in water, grind well and apply to dengaku, grill, and serve with fresh simmered soy sauce and grated wasabi - this is called "paradise beans"

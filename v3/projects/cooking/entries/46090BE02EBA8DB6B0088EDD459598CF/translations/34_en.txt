Seventy: Pine Mountain　　Cut grilled tofu into flat circles with a diameter of one sun four to five bu (about 4.2-4.5 cm) and grill. A flat circle refers to a flattened【diagram】circle, not a sphere shape. Most importantly, pour green seaweed tororo soup generously over it while freshly grilled. Use plenty of green seaweed. ○This is an alternative cooking method of the potato-topped tofu from recipe forty-seven in the previous volume, made with miso.

Seventy-one: Koromo-de　　Take the quick-frozen tofu that appeared in recipe twelve of the previous volume on frozen tofu, and simmer it in bonito stock and soy sauce. Then pour fluffy beaten egg generously over it and sprinkle with pepper powder.

Seventy-two: A Type of Shakin Tofu　　Squeeze the moisture from tofu and grind it well, mix in one-tenth portion of burdock root, and grind together thoroughly. Lay Mino paper on a board, spread the ground tofu mixture thinly and evenly, place a mixture of old ginger and miso with added sugar in the center, wrap it together with the paper and tie it, boil it, then remove the paper before use. ○This is an alternative preparation of the Shakin tofu from recipe thirty-five in the previous volume.

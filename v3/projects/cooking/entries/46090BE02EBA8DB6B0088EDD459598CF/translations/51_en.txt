Seventeen: Ochiba-kara (Fallen Leaves Dregs)　　Mix equal parts tofu dregs and finely ground bonito flakes, add a little ground sanshō pepper and sesame oil, and stir-fry with the usual amount of soy sauce seasoning. Separately season chopped shiitake mushrooms, split ginkgo nuts, roasted chestnuts, and deep-fried wheat gluten, then mix in. Pack into a rice mold and press out. This makes an excellent drinking appetizer.

Eighteen: Kinchaku Tofu (Purse Tofu)　　Hollow out the inside of an eggplant, fill with ground tofu mixed with two or three items selected appropriately from the ingredients for Hiryōzu in number 19 of the previous volume, seasoned well. Close the opening, deep-fry in oil, then simmer in light soy sauce with a little sake and salt.

Nineteen: Dojō-jiru (Loach Soup)　　Garnish with split scallions or julienned burdock root, and add finely cut deep-fried tofu. The flavor is delicious.

Twenty: Mumei Isshu no Nimono (Nameless Stewed Dish)　　Sea bream slices, grilled tofu, matsutake mushrooms

Eighty-seven: Soybean Milk Porridge (Tofu Porridge)　　Cut tofu into small dice as shown in the diagram, and cook in kudzu starch soup. Season with roasted salt, and just before serving, sprinkle finely chopped boiled greens and add squeezed ginger juice. ○When cut into large dice, this is called "tide simmering."

Eighty-eight: Tengu Tofu　　Arrange four pots in a row: the first pot contains sesame oil, the second pot white hot water, the third pot sake, and the fourth pot soy sauce. Make strong fires under all of them and keep them bubbling vigorously. One person per pot, four people in total, each carrying a mesh ladle. Cut the tofu to an appropriate size and put it in the first pot, swirl it around two or three times, immediately scoop it up and put it in the second pot. Again swirl it around two or three times and immediately transfer to the third pot. Do the same and transfer to the fourth pot. Meanwhile, another person warms tea bowls and prepares wasabi miso and serving miso, waiting ready. The tofu is passed from the fourth pot to the serving miso.

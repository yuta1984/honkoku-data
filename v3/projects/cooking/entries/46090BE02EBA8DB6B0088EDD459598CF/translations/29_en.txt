Rare Items

Fifty-five: Ono Dengaku　　【Illustration】Cut into rectangular strips, brush with thin soy sauce and grill, then pour black seasoning vinegar over it. ○Preparation of black seasoning vinegar: Mix seven parts black sesame with three parts charred kelp, grind well with vinegar and add Japanese pepper powder.

Fifty-six: Nitori Dengaku　　Mix nitori with white miso in equal parts, grind well together, and prepare like regular dengaku. ○Nitori refers to the liquid from making katsuobushi (dried bonito flakes) that has been boiled down - essentially it is the oil of bonito. It comes from Tosa and various other places.

Fifty-seven: Okamoto Tofu　　Take soft tofu whole, drip a little water on it, and repeatedly shave off the corners with a thin blade to make it completely round like a ball. Oil a confectionery pan and very slowly roll and grill it. This is a preparation method to enjoy grilled tofu that is exceptionally soft. Of course, handle it carefully so it doesn't crumble.

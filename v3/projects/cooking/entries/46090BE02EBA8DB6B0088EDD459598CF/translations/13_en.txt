　Ordinary Grade

1. Imperial Pheasant Tofu　　Cut one block into about four pieces, flat and square,
　　sprinkle with salt and grill, then pour warm sake over it while hot
　　and serve
　　○ This is a New Year's delicacy in the Imperial Court. From Nagahashi-no-tsubone to the hundred court officials

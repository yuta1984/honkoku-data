If you intend to cook for several people, first prepare about twenty pieces and grill them. This is because some may break during grilling or boiling, so extras should be prepared. Using a suitable pot or earthenware pot, arrange twenty pieces and put them in one by one to cook all at once. ○Cook well in dashi broth with a light soy sauce seasoning, but put in the tofu to cook. Serve one piece each in large Nara tea bowls and place grated daikon radish on top. ○This is a cooking method by a person named Okamoto Sanzaemon.

Fifty-eight: Narazuke Tofu　　Briefly boil the tofu and squeeze it well, then grind together roasted salt and kudzu powder and set aside. ○Insert a stick-like object of any shape (square or round) into the narazuke lees and pull it out, then wrap a piece of cloth around the tip of that stick and insert it again into the original hole, leaving the cloth behind when you pull it out. Push the aforementioned tofu into this space, twist and seal the opening, and cover with the lees. When you want to remove it, pull it out together with the cloth and cut to an appropriate size.

Fifty-nine: A Type of Egg Tofu　　Grab and crumble the tofu, strain it through a fine water bag, then cook with one cup of sake and two cups of water. Especially if you cook it over charcoal fire for more than three hours, it will blend with one piece of the aforementioned strained tofu. Season with dashi and soy sauce...

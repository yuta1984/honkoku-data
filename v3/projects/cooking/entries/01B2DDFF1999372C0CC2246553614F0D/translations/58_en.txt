【Tongue (saya)】
This refers to the tongue.

【Throat Princess (nodohime)】- This refers to the epiglottis
【Throat Ring (nodowa)】- This refers to the pharyngeal tube
【Princess (hime)】- This refers to the throat tube that leads to the stomach. It is the esophagus.

【Blowing Intestine (fukiwata)】
Blowing intestine refers to the lungs.

【Round】
Round refers to the heart. Also called "usu."

【Clove】
Clove refers to the stomach.

【Red Intestine】
Red intestine refers to the duodenum.

【Shell Liver (karakimo)】
Shell liver refers to the liver.

【Organ Meat】
Organ meat refers to a type of thick meat different from that found in human bodies.

For udon noodles, scoop the mixed paste with a copper ladle
and cook it. For grilled sea bream, prepare it hot,
crush the roe, season with thin soy sauce
to taste, add both the sea bream and roe
to the broth-like mixture, soften and serve. If left
too long, it becomes bad. For soroiko (small fish), chop dried small fish
into tiny pieces, boil well until soft,
and serve with soy sauce. For anglerfish
soup, peel and cut the skin,
put both skin and flesh in hot water, when the flesh turns white
remove and cool in water, then pour sake
over and let sit. When making miso soup,
add the fish
and serve.

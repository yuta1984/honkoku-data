Pour the plum vinegar over the flowers up to the top properly
Just pour it in. Then cover with a lid to prevent wind from entering
and place in a location where people do not pass, bring out in summer.
When using, rinse thoroughly and when the flowers
　　are added, the flowers will bloom and be no different

Method for Storing Water Shield and Seaweed　Briefly pass through hot water
　　　　　　　then drain
One sho measure, mix with one go of salt and set aside
Color will not change and will keep well


Method for Storing Mozuku Seaweed　One sho of mozuku, remove
　　　　　water and add three go of salt
Seal well and store. Will not spoil for a long time
When using, soak in water briefly to remove salt


Method for Tree Buds　On the last day of each month, place Japanese pepper
buds on a stone platform
If you spread them out, they will sprout. Take these
and use as garnish. Every morning sprinkle with water
and dry in the sun. This will make
buds emerge quickly

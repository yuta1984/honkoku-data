Koto no Ne (Sound of Koto)
Add gardenia to ground ingredients, add water and strain thinly through a strainer,
steam it, sprinkle with mustard seed, cut beautifully like pine cones,
and use for side dishes or appetizers.

Thousand-Sheet Pickle
Layer many shiso leaves, making them into salt pickle.
Apply heavy weights until the thickness becomes about 2 bu (approximately 6mm),
trim the four sides and use as side dishes or
pickled vegetables.

Kaya Sake (Torreya Nut Sake)
Roast high-quality torreya nuts, remove the astringent skin, roast again until colored,
grind well in a mortar, strain with sake,
and use warmed. The ratio is 200 torreya nuts to one sho of sake.

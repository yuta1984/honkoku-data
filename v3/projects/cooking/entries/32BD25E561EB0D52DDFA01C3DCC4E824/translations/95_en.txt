Watermelon Sugar
Take one large watermelon, use only the red parts, remove seeds and cut up. Add 100 monme of white sugar. Remove impurities. If the watermelon is medium-sized, 90 monme of sugar is also acceptable. Remove the skin from ginger, boil in hot water, and cut into pieces. The amount is according to taste. Cook well in a pot without metal contamination until the juice becomes sticky, then store in a jar. The flavor will not change no matter how long it is kept.

Grilled Wheat Gluten
Thread skewers through wheat gluten and grill. Turn over together with the skewers. They will not fall off. After grilling both sides well, remove the skewers.

Long-lasting White Fish
To keep white fish for a long time, coat them with rice bran and they will keep for nine to ten days.

Steam millet and mix in salt to pickle it. The outside is scattered rice. Also, it is good to pickle mackerel for sushi, fillet it into three pieces, remove the skin and fold it for use.

Sardine Sushi
Lightly salt fresh sardines and pickle in vinegar, then pickle in sake lees. Use together with the lees.

Pomegranate (Sweet)
Boil dainagon azuki beans well so they don't get crushed, quickly steam dried rice and let it cool, add sugar and mix with the azuki beans. Knead glutinous rice flour with water, take wheat flour and make it into powder, flatten it, wrap the aforementioned sweet filling, tie the mouth of each one and steam. Alternatively, they may be deep-fried.

Asaji (Shallow Grass)
Knead wheat flour with water and soy sauce, dust with bracken fern buds and deep-fry in oil. Use corn flour as coating.

Cook over charcoal fire. Adjust the proportions as needed according to the quantities mentioned above.

Mikasa Miso
Roast one shō of rice bran until it barely changes color. While still warm, mix in one shō of koji and five gō of finest soy sauce. Store in a jar. It will be ready in twenty days. Once opened, it keeps for about fifty days.

One-Night Sake
Soak white koji in water, knead and crush well, add white sugar to taste, strain and use.

Shrunk Sea Eel (Chijimi-hamo)
Fillet a sea eel, remove small bones, add sake, place a sheet of paper on the cutting board, spread the ground fish paste thinly on top, and pour boiling water over it. It will shrink. Cut and use in clear soup and similar dishes.

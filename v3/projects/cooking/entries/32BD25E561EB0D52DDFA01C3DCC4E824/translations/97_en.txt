Perilla Seed Sake
Add one gō of perilla seeds to one shō of sake, put in a cloth bag, soak in the sake, and remove after three days for use.

Salted Fish Entrails Soup
For any kind of salted fish entrails, wash them and strain the cooking liquid when it becomes cloudy before use. Adjust the flavor with bonito stock and add a little barley miso, which is good. However, since this is meant to appreciate the aroma of the salted entrails, ingredients with strong fragrances like shiitake mushrooms or mitsuba should not be added as they would be detrimental.

Kasuga Miso
Six kan of sake lees, six shō of soybeans, six shō of koji, six shō of salt. Mix four shō of this salt with the lees. However, cut the lees finely, mix with four shō of salt, let stand for about two days, then (add) the soybeans.

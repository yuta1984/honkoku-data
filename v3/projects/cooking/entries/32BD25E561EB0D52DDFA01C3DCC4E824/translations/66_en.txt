Kaya Miso (Japanese Torreya Nut Miso)
100 monme of grilled miso, 40 monme of Japanese torreya nuts roasted and hulled then ground into powder, 1 go of roasted black sesame seeds, 20 monme of refined white sugar, and 5 bu of red pepper - grind each together and store in a jar.

Sugar Plums
Take 100 Bungo plums, salt-press them, add a handful of ash and let sit overnight. Wash and drain, remove moisture, dry them, add high-quality sake to cover, add 1 kin of sugar, and add 30 fresh plums (this is to bring out the fragrance). Put in a jar, seal the mouth well, and after 30 days the flavor will be complete.

Shiso Liqueur
20 monme of shiso, 5 monme of safflower - put these two ingredients in separate bags, 400 monme of sugar, 1 sho of shochu in a jar

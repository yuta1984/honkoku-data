Tofu Pickled Vegetables
Briefly boil tofu and squeeze it, mix with salt and kudzu,
insert into Nara pickle lees whether square or round shaped,
make a hole, wrap the inserted item
with cloth strips and insert again, leaving the cloth
strips in the hole, insert the tofu there,
twist the opening and cover with lees, and when serving,
remove together with the cloth strips, cut and use.

　　　Butterbur Mix
Boil butterbur leaves in hot water, squeeze well, mix with sesame
miso, and dress with anything.

　　　Bag Eggplant
Cut off the stem of a large eggplant, hollow out the eggplant
from the cut opening, and fill with miso. The miso can be
any type according to individual preference, and apply kudzu powder to the opening

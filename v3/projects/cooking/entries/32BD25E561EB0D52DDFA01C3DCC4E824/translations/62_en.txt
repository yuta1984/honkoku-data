Bean Powder Mix
Mix sesame and bean powder into miso, then mix with whole white cowpeas, udo (Japanese spikenard), wood ear mushrooms, and other ingredients according to season and preference.

Pale Green
Make cuts in sea bream, remove the red flesh, and cover with white kudzu paste. The white kudzu paste is prepared with roasted salt.

Quick Kudzu Noodles
Keep water boiling, dissolve kudzu in a tray, coat thin white agar well with the kudzu mixture, put it in the hot water, and remove immediately. This is like real kudzu noodles.

Orchid Flower
Soak broad beans in water to soften them, then cut the head into six pieces

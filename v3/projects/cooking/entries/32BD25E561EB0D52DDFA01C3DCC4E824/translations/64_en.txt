Sake Lees Clams
Soak shucked clam meat directly in sake lees. It's good to finely chop mandarin orange peel and add a little ginger. Thin the sake lees with sake, but don't make it too sweet. It's good to add a little salt. It will be ready in three days.

Shrimp Salt-Cured Paste
Take two and a half cups of small shrimp, scrape off the whiskers, put them in a jar with one cup of salt and a little koji, pour hot water over them, and close the lid. Stir daily and it will be ready quickly.

Yuzu Paste
For one hundred yuzu citrus, add three sho of sake and half a kin of sugar, and knead over charcoal fire for about three days.

Blanched Ginger
During the cold season, peel ginger, slice thinly, soak in water for about seven days, then press with salt for one night and dry in good weather. For one shō of pure ginger, add seven gō of salt.

Persimmon Pickles
Pickle whole persimmons in rice bran. Astringent persimmons are best.

Meguro Snow White
Remove bones from sea bream, cut along the back, boil in hot water, pour vinegar over it, and top with grated daikon radish.

Wrapped Miso
Remove scales and entrails from fresh small sea bream, wash well, stuff the belly with aralia shoot miso, and steam.

Sōmen Tofu
Add kudzu starch to tofu, grind together well, and spread paper on a cutting board

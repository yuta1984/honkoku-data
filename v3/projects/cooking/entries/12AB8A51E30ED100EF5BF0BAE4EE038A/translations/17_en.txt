The packing method is generally like this. However, according to each person's preferences, the packing method should be done in any way. Just be sure not to pack in the same regular pattern. The color combination is most important. Similar colors should not gather in one place. Also, warm colors should be placed toward the center. 【Left page】

Generally, for tiered box dishes of nine, seven, five, or three varieties, it is good that the flavoring and cutting shapes are not all the same. However, that doesn't mean cutting into countless various shapes without reason. One should simply have the awareness to vary large and small, long and short, round and diagonal, etc. Also, looking at the dimensions of the box, one must plan from the beginning and cut in a way that makes packing convenient, otherwise when packed up there will be irregularities in various places. One should plan the division of the packing method and make the cutting method proper.

First, using hijiki seaweed, royal fern, or kelp as underlining is extremely vulgar and bad. It's better to have no underlining at all. However, packing can be done without underlining too. That packing method is: first, place items that don't sit well in several layers where you intend to place them in the box, then pack items that stack well beside them, letting them lean against these, and it can be done in any way. Also, crumbly items should all be put into gaps after finishing packing other items. However, thinking of this as leftover space from the packing division, one should begin packing with this in mind from the start.

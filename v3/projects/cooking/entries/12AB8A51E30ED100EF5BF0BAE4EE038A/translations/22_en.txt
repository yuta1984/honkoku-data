Same - Miscellaneous Section

Fish Rice: Fish (sea bream, flounder, bonito - all prepared the same way) - fillet the fish, grind the flesh and parboil, knead well, and strain through a metal sieve cloth. Cook rice as usual, steam well, then mix in the fish.

Hand Salt: Mikushi pickles, pickled ginger

Small Cup: Garland chrysanthemum, blanched dish, Japanese spikenard with walnut dressing

Medium Plate: Grilled salted fish, passed through hot water, shredded, topped with bonito flakes and sake salt, with pickled shiso rolls

Flat Plate: Whole peeled daikon, hen, pepper, long pepper, crucian carp miso (preparation method is in the previous volume)

Tea Bowl: Tofu with thick sauce, wasabi

Additional Ingredients: Colorful grated mixture, julienned scallions, crumbled nori

Soup Serving: Fresh vinegar clear soup, dashi with yuzu

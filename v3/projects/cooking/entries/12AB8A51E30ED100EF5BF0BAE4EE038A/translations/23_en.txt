However, miscellaneous named rice dishes use various seasonal menu items. One should not say that they are called "zōni" and used only in miscellaneous menus.

Pheasant Rice: Roast pheasant meat well over a distant flame, shred it finely and mix it in. Although there is also a method of steaming it completely, grilling gives better flavor. Sometimes both meat and bones are cooked well together and mixed.

Hand Salt: Nara pickles, rikyū, hanamaru, miso pickles

Small Cup: Young greens, black sesame, blanched dish

Medium Plate: Turnip, flounder, vinegar miso dressing, kneaded nori, red pepper

Flat Plate: Carrot, burdock root, Chinese yam, abalone, wood ear mushrooms, noppei, wasabi

Tea Bowl: Grey mullet cut in rounds, white miso, pepper, radish sprouts, with skin

Seasonings: Scallions, long pepper, yuzu

Soup Serving: Clear soup, as usual

Replacement Bowl

In addition to the above, sardine rice, cod roe rice, gnomefish roe rice, etc., are all prepared in the same manner. However, the vegetables for fish rice should be combined according to the type of fish.

Corbicula Rice: Make yellow rice and mix in corbicula clams. Proper yellow rice uses gardenia.

Hand Salt: Tōza pickles, turnip, thin roots, daikon

Small Cup: Lily bulb, green dressing

Medium Plate: Steamed flounder, colored grilling, Chinese wolfberry cut dressing

Flat Plate: Yellowtail on small skewers grilled, taro, shiitake mushrooms, white miso, pepper

Tea Bowl: Fish cake, picked greens, wasabi, yuzu

Additional Ingredients: Prepared mustard, kneaded nori

Soup Serving: Clear soup as usual, with mozuku seaweed added

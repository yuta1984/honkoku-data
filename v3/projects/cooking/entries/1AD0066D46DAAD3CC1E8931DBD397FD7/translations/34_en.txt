○ Kenchin (Buddhist vegetable soup)
《Note: Also called kaminari tofu (thunder tofu)》
　　　The method is to put plenty of oil in a pot, add tofu as is,
　　　fry it well in oil while cooking, then add sake, salt,
　　　and dashi broth, season with soy sauce, and serve
　　　with dried sansho pepper and the like. The seasonings are: shiitake mushrooms, tree mushrooms, lily bulbs or
　　　roasted chestnuts or ginkgo nuts, wheat gluten, dropwort or mitsuba,
　　　pickled matsutake mushrooms, shimeji mushrooms, arrowhead, Chinese yam, konjac
　　　About five varieties from the above should be added. This is good at any time.

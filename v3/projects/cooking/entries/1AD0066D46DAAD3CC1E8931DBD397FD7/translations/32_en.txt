○Ise-style Mixed Vegetables　　This method: season all ingredients, squeeze well, mix with miso and white sesame,
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　season with white sugar, combine about five different seasonings for mixing

○Kuronushi Dengaku　　　　　This method: use fried tofu, boil in hot water and grill, Japanese pepper
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　miso for glazing, serving on wooden boards is especially good

○Yuzu Manjū　　　　　　　　This method: thinly peel the yuzu skin, thoroughly clean out the flesh, mountain yam
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　boiled in hot water, peeled, grated and ground well, mixed with white sugar,
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　stuffed into the yuzu, seal the opening well, tie with paper cord and steam before serving
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　○Also remove yuzu flesh well, stuff with manjū dough and steam, then slice into rounds《It's good to press in through the manjū opening》

○Chawanmushi (Savory Egg Custard)　This method: mix soft tofu with a little kudzu, then with kombu or any
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　dashi stock, add soy sauce to taste and mix with the tofu, also
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　seasonings such as fried tofu, roasted chestnuts, wood ear mushrooms, shiitake, lily bulbs are good
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Put this mixture of seasonings in tea bowls, pour the mixture over and steam before serving

○Yudōfu (Hot Water Tofu)　　　Method: either silken tofu or regular tofu is fine, boil water very well
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　dissolve kudzu powder thinly and add to the water, then cut tofu as usual
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　and serve after one boil, because a little kudzu is added, no matter how long you cook it
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　the tofu will not become spongy, the flavor is exceptionally good

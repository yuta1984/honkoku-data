25. How to make dengaku daikon (grilled daikon with miso)

Peel the daikon, cut into pieces about 2 sun long, prepare like dengaku,
and simmer in thin soy sauce broth. However, add it after the broth
starts boiling and cook. Then lift onto a
bamboo strainer, skewer with bamboo sticks, heat slightly over fire, and for miso,
any kind of miso will do, according to the timing. Then
spread miso on it and heat over fire again before serving. This dengaku is
a dish that was started by tea masters.

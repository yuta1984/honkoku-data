　　　Saponin Pod (Saikachi) Rice
Salt-boil it, chop it, then mix it in afterwards.
　　　Bulbil (Nukago) Rice　《Note: Commonly called mukago｜》
Rub the bulbils well in a mortar to remove the skin,
mix with rice, add a little salt and cook.
　　　Twice-Cooked Rice
Cook it slightly firm, sprinkle a little sake over it and
steam it. Use premium white rice for this.

　　　Shrimp Rice
Whether using kuruma shrimp or Kamakura shrimp, boil in salt water.
When cooked, remove the shells and shred very finely, then
serve the rice in bowls with the shrimp scattered on top.
The soup should be clear broth with various seasonings.
Also, rice colored with gardenia seeds makes an interesting variation.

Sword Bean Rice
Soak the beans in water, remove the skins, and cook.

　　　Shepherd's Purse Rice
This refers to what is called mother-and-child grass (cudweed). Pick the leaves, boil them, soak in water for a while, then chop finely and cook.

　　　Acorn Rice
Cut the acorn shells, add salt and cook. Same as regular rice.

　　　Tofu Rice
Cut tofu finely into small cubes, boil in plenty of hot water, scoop up with a mesh ladle and dry. Cook the rice softly, and just before serving, place in a bowl, put the prepared tofu on top, and season with clear broth with various condiments.

　　　Tree Bud Rice
Around the second month (February), when buds are just beginning to sprout, pick them, pour boiling water over them, dry in the sun, and chop finely

　　　Green Cowpea Rice
Cut green cowpeas finely crosswise and cook with salt added.

　　　Cowpea Rice
Boil the cowpeas carefully so they don't get crushed, and mix with tea rice to cook. If cooked in the cooking liquid, the lye becomes too strong and is not good.

　　　Red Bean Rice
Use the azuki beans called "dainagon" for cooking. The method like cowpea rice is also good. Discarding the cooking liquid and mixing to cook it white is also good.

　　　Black Bean Rice
This follows the same principle as cowpea rice and red bean rice, so there is no need to describe it.

　　　Mung Bean Rice
This follows the same principle.

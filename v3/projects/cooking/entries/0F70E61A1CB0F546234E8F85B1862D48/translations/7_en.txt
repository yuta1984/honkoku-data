　　　Taro Rice
Chop only small taro roots in any way you like, add salt and boil them, then mix with rice and cook. Since the salt from the taro will season the rice, one should be mindful of the proper balance.

　　　Millet Rice
Soak millet well in water and mix thoroughly with rice to cook.

　　　Barnyard Grass Rice
This is the same as millet rice, but it is also good to squeeze vegetable greens and add the liquid.

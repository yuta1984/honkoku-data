Bonito Rice
Fillet the bonito and use only the meat from the back side.
Cut it and blanch it well, let it cool and tear it all
into fine pieces. Wrap in cloth, squeeze and knead
to loosen it, making it even finer, then sprinkle it
on top of the rice. The soup should be clear broth with various
seasonings.

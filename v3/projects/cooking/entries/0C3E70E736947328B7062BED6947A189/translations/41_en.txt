　(85) Millet Mochi　Dry glutinous millet well in the sun,
pound it like pounding rice, remove the outer skin, then
when making it into mochi, soak it in water for about two hours,
steam it and then pound it into mochi
　(86) Yam Mochi　Pound kan-zarashi glutinous rice and
sift it in a steamer, grate yams and grind well
in a mortar, then use a ratio of one yam to ten parts of the above glutinous rice flour

The height should be five shaku on the outside and about three shaku on the inside. For the sides, like a plastered wall, put in stones or broken tiles and plaster them. coats of top plaster to the outside. However, the thickness of the side earth should be plastered to about seven to eight sun. Make the oven opening about one shaku five sun square. The upper sides on three sides of the opening should be made of stone. The bottom rests on foundation tiles. With firewood of about three shaku circumference, burn about two bundles, then rake out the embers, dip a straw broom in water, sweep out the debris inside well, then arrange the bread.

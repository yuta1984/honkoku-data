Takeri　This is the penis. In sei-bi and chōsu whales it is pale black, while in right whales and young whales
　　　　it is white. Young whales are best. Fresh or salt-
　　　　cured ones are not used.
○Deep-fried. Or boiled in water and served with raw soy sauce or iri-zake.
　　　　During the cold season, takeri is dried and stored. When suffering from stomach pain, shave it and
　　　　boil it in miso soup for miraculous effects. It is also
　　　　effective for symptoms of lower back coldness. Hina dried in the cold
　　　　has the same efficacy. Takeri should be used for women, and
　　　　hina should be used for men.

Kintsū　This is the scrotum. There are left and right ones. White on the outside, reddish-black on the inside.
　　　　　Fresh or salt-cured ones are not used.
○Deep-fried and served with raw soy sauce or iri-zake.

First: Concerning Ushio Suimono (Clear Salt Soup)

1. This ushio suimono is made by adding roasted salt to plain water, seasoning it appropriately, then adding sea bream fillets or other fine ingredients that have been marinated, along with green vegetables, and serving it.

　　　Note: While ushio suimono traditionally uses roasted salt in pure water, it is said that adding dashi stock makes it even more delicious.

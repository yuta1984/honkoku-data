　　　　Secret Transmission Section


　　　　　　Fish Cracker Tradition

One　For whiting crackers, shrimp crackers, or any other fish crackers: fillet the fish
　　remove the skin, powder fine kudzu and sift through silk strainer,
　　wrap with that kudzu powder, pound well with large bamboo spatula until thin,
　　shape into rounds with suitable sake cups and prepare in salt containers

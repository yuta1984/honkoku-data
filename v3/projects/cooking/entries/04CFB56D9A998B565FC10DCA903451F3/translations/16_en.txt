【Autumn】
Pears, finely chopped and salt-rubbed eggplant with shiitake
Gosho persimmons, fried wheat gluten, pickled mitsuba
Wood ear mushrooms, vinegar soy sauce
Daikon radish with green vinegar

Grate daikon radish, squeeze out moisture and mix with vinegar, plum vinegar hajigami, wood ear mushrooms, mitsuba, bundled wheat gluten, etc. Place in a box and press to form sushi shapes.

【Winter】
Turnips, myoga buds
Shiitake mushrooms, pressed tofu
Water dropwort and chestnuts, wood ear mushrooms
Walnuts, mustard vinegar

Steamed wheat gluten, julienned carrots
Dried gourd strips, dried persimmons
Wood ear mushrooms, water dropwort and chestnuts
Vinegar kudzu, dried abalone
Tamari soy sauce, grilled tofu

Thinly sliced matsutake mushrooms, chopped sakurame seaweed
Chopped kelp, chestnuts with skin
Water dropwort, finely cut millet wheat gluten

Finely pound bundled wheat gluten and season, with thread wood ear mushrooms, chestnut meat, daikon leaves, dried sardines, julienned ginger, water dropwort, vinegar soy sauce.

【Sashimi Section】
Use irizake (seasoned sake) and wasabi. When wasabi is not available, pound ginger and use as substitute.

【Spring】
Squid tentacles, burdock with sugar
Fragrant mushrooms, needle-cut chestnuts
Green nori seaweed, steamed wheat gluten
Irizake, irizake vinegar

Citrus wedges, large citrus with slight sprouting
Fragrant mushrooms, dried inside the citrus
Narcissus, thinly sliced into rounds
Combinations should be visually balanced.

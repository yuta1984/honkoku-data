Kudzu Clear Soup

Season with water and roasted salt, knead kudzu firmly,
tear into pieces and add. When it floats up, pound well
with ground ginger and serve.


Wheat Gluten (Fu)

Knead winter-bleached rice cake flour firmly with hot water, form into balls and
boil in hot water. Scoop up what floats, and round
in clear water. Sprinkling with sugar is also good.


New-Style Kashiwa

Remove the astringent skin from oak leaves, grind rice grains well, add salt
and sprinkle, coat with green seaweed as clothing for this new-style

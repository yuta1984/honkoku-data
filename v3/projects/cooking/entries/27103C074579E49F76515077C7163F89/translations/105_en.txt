Mikasa Miso

Toast one shō of rice bran lightly without letting it color, and while still warm, add one shō of koji and five gō of finest soy sauce, mix well and store in a jar. It will be ready in about fifty days.

One-Night Sake

Soak white koji in water, knead and crush it well, mix with white sugar to taste, strain and use.

Shrunken Sea Eel

Fillet the sea eel and remove small bones, add sake, place a sheet of paper on the cutting board, spread the ground fish paste thinly on top, and pour hot water over it - it will shrink. Cut and use in clear soups and such.

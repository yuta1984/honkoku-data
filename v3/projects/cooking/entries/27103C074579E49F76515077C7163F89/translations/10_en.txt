　　Butterbur Soufflé
Thin fish paste with bonito broth, grind in plenty
of potatoes, season with salt, and prepare in a pot. When this
becomes fluffy and rises to the top, bring it out in the pot
and eat. Adding egg to soy sauce

　　Sardine Soup
Prepare clear soup with bonito broth, and it's good to
add large pieces of daikon radish. Wash red sardines well and add them.
However, when adding cut sardines, it's better to add them late. If added
early, they become too salty and unpalatable.

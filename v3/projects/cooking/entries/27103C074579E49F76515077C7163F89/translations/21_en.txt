Gardenia Sound
Put small gardenias in soup bowls, spread thinly through a color strainer
and steam, sprinkle with mustard, cut like pine cones,
and use for side dishes, appetizers, and relishes.


Thousand-Layer Pickle
Layer many perilla leaves and make into salt pickle.
Apply heavy pressing weight until it becomes about two bu thick,
trim the four sides, and use as appetizer or pickled vegetables.


Torreya Sake
Roast high-quality torreya nuts and remove the astringent skin, then roast again until colored,
grind well in a mortar, strain with sake
and drink warm. The ratio is 200 torreya nuts to one sho of sake.

Quick Rice Bran Miso

Wash glutinous rice bran well, mix in equal parts with bean powder,
add a good amount of salt, put in a little sugar, and
knead with sake as needed for use.


Sprout Vegetables

Soak rapeseed in water, sift the soil dust well,
dry fertilizer in the sun, crush rice husks, mix with duck eggs and
bone ash and dry in the sun. Prepare the
ground with soil, sow the rapeseed, evenly place
the above ash, and pour hot water over it.

Even when boiled twice a day, the seasoning will not be spoiled.

Yutoji (Hot Soup with Soba)
Boil soba noodles, make miso-yaki broth and remove all the daikon radish, put the soba noodles into the miso soup and warm it, place the julienned daikon on top in the serving bowl and serve. In Shinano, after soba-kiri, yutoji is served instead of clear soup.

Amazake-zuke (Sweet Rice Wine Pickles)
Boil amazake (sweet rice wine) and let it cool, add salt and pickle eggplant. There should be other pickled vegetables as well.

Hamanaka (Mackerel Koji Pickle)
Finely chop one serving of sliced mackerel, also finely chop five go of koji, add sake until just covered, put in a jar, seal the mouth well, and use.

Sea Bream Roe Basket (Tai-ko-kago)
Remove the gills, entrails, and scales from sea bream, stuff well with crucian carp roe without opening the belly, cut into pieces and simmer in salt water.

Seaweed Yuzu Dry (Koke-yuzu-boshi)
Mix flour well with miso, spread thinly on nori seaweed, roll up and cut into rounds.

Millet Roll (Awa-maki)
Soak glutinous millet in water, sprinkle a little flour on coarse seaweed, spread the millet evenly, roll it up, press down with a bowl, steam and cut into rounds.

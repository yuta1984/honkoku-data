Snow-Viewing Bird
Whether carp or sea eel, soak in salt water until the flesh becomes tender, then break up the flesh and use in soup bowls. Extend with the second brewing of clear broth. The flesh is exceptionally white. Season this flesh accordingly.

Bleached Shrimp
Remove the shells from raw shrimp and place the red meat in cold water. When the flesh has swelled, boil in hot water and use with black sesame and roasted salt.

Oribe Miso
Black sesame 15 monme, mustard seed 10 monme, torreya nuts 15 monme, raw kudzu 7 monme, cracked Japanese pepper 1 monme, ground Japanese pepper 7 monme, sugar 15 monme, miso 30 monme. Extend the above with sake.

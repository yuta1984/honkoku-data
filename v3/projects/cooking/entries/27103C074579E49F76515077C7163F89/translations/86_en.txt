Take one to (about 18 liters) of barley, hull it, and soak it in water until it begins to rot,
changing the water frequently. Take one to of wheat flour and one shō (about 1.8 liters) of black soybeans,
roast them and remove the black skins. Mix these three ingredients well together,
steam them and let them ferment like koji mold, then cook this well.
Add two shō of salt to one to of water and boil it down to eight shō, then let it cool well.
Sprinkle the aforementioned koji over this and press it down well. Roast five gō (about 900ml) of black soybeans
and grind into powder. Roast five gō of glutinous rice and grind into powder. Sprinkle these two colored
powders over the mixture. Prepare this in the sixth month and press well for about fifteen days.
It is also good to add Japanese pepper seeds.


Mackerel Eye Pounded Vinegar

Remove the gills from mackerel, finely
chop the bones and flesh together,
and mix with salt, sesame, soy sauce, vinegar, grated daikon, and mustard
all together.

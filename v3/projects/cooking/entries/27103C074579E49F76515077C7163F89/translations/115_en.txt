To one sho (1.8 liters) of sake, add twenty chrysanthemum leaves and half a kin (300g) of grated ingredients, put them together in a jar with a lid, place in a location away from wind for five days. On the sixth day, stir the contents well, cover again as before, and repeat this process every five days until the thirtieth day.

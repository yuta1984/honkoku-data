Quick Kudzu Starch
Boil sweet potato, remove the skin, pass it through a sieve, grind it, strain it through water, let it settle, and then use. The method of use is the same as regular kudzu starch. Regular kudzu starch sometimes forms lumps, but this kudzu starch does not form lumps.

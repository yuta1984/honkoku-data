Kaya Miso (Japanese Torreya Nut Miso)
Take 100 monme of grilled miso, 4-5 kaya nuts roasted with rough skin removed and ground to powder, 1 gō of roasted black sesame, 20 monme of refined white sugar, and 5 monme of mustard. Grind each separately and mix together, then store in a jar.

Sugar Plums (Satō-ume)
Take 100 Bungo plums, salt-press them, add a handful of ash and let sit overnight. Wash and drain, removing moisture, then dry. Soak in peach wine with 1 kin of sugar added, and add 30 fresh plums - this is to provide fragrance. Put in a jar, seal the mouth well, and after 30 days the flavor will develop.

Shiso Wine (Shiso-shu)
20 monme of shiso, 5 monme of safflower - put these two colors in separate bags, with 400 monme of sugar and 1 shō of shōchū in a jar.

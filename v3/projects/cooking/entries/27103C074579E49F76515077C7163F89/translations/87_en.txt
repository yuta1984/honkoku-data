Combine three shō of vinegar, one shō of sake, and seven gō of salt, boil this mixture twice,
let it cool well, and use it to pickle any vegetables.
This is prepared for regular use.


Tea Pouch Tofu

Skillfully deep-fry tofu into round shapes, cut one side,
scoop out the inside, and stuff it with fish, fowl, wood ear mushrooms, and egg.
Tie the opening with kelp, simmer in sake, then serve with grated
wasabi on top. Fill about seven-tenths full with egg.


Quick Udo (Japanese Spikenard)

Grind young udo shoots and add green miso,
grind again, then mix with thinly sliced daikon radish that has been
well simmered in sake. Use this when the udo's flavor is weak.


Reconstituted Fresh Fish

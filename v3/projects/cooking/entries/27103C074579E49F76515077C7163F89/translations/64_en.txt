Bean Powder Mix
Mix sesame and bean powder into miso, then gradually mix in tsubugai shellfish, udo (Japanese spikenard), and kikurage mushrooms.

Usu-midori (Pale Green)
Make cuts in the sea bream, remove the red flesh, steam and cover with white kudzu starch paste. The white kudzu paste is prepared with roasted salt.

Quick Kudzu Noodles
Boil water and set aside, dissolve kudzu in a tray, coat fine white kanten (agar) well with the kudzu, put into the hot water and immediately remove. This is like real kudzu noodles.

Eiка (Glory Flower)
Soak fava beans in water to soften, then split the heads into six pieces.

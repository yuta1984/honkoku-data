Tsutsuhei

With fish, birds, or shellfish of any kind, chop finely and pound well,
stir-fry in sesame oil, then pour in beaten egg. Good for flat dishes.


Uruka (Fish Entrail Paste)

Combine ayu sweetfish entrails and milt together, mix well
with the entrail base, and for about one shō volume,
mix in four gō of salt, put in a jar and press down. Make sure there is
no moisture at all.


Salt Proportions for Shiokara

In summer, use five gō of salt per one shō of fish; in winter, three gō is
good. Around New Year, make the salt strong, and if you put in anything,
it will become good by around March-April.

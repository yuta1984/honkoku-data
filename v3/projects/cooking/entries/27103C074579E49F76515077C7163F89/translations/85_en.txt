Three-Item Pickle

Boil vinegar and sake in equal parts and let cool. Pickle river trout, daikon radish,防風 (bōfū herb), 
and red pepper and use.


Bonito Sashimi

Cut bonito into appropriate pieces and apply fire treatment
《Side note: Method of fire treatment | detailed in the namasu section |》, sprinkle salt
evenly all over, and pour on citron vinegar.


Amber Sugar

Put water in a bucket, add one egg and salt—
the egg will float up. Put in as many eggs as desired,
boil and remove them, then crack the eggs.
Discard the egg whites; use the yolks that have turned red and hardened with roasted salt.

Dig into the ground, build a fire to heat sand, sprinkle with salt water,
wash the sea bream and place it in the sand, put sand on top, then
sprinkle with salt water again, and build a fire with this. Steam and
roast it well, then dig it out and use with soy sauce. Slight
adjustments are according to one's discretion.


Stem Namasu

Used in winter in Kyoto. Pickle daikon radish stems, remove the leaves,
chop the roots and use for namasu. The body makes good ink stone. For
accompaniments, ark shell and such are good. For vegetarian dishes,
Chinese bellflower and similar are good.


Mi-goromo (Battered Coating)

Pound only the duck meat well, form into rounds, coat with wheat flour
batter, and deep-fry in oil. Good as a flat dish.

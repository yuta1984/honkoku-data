【Right Page】
　　Tofu Pickles
Briefly boil tofu and squeeze it, mix with salt and kudzu starch,
then press into Nara pickle lees, whether square-shaped or round.
Make a hole and push the mixture into that hole,
wrap with cloth strips and press in again, leaving the cloth
strips in the hole. Push in the aforementioned tofu,
twist the opening and cover with the lees. When serving,
remove along with the cloth strips and use.

【Left Page】
　　Butterbur Leaf Dressing
Boil butterbur leaves and squeeze them well, mix with sesame
miso and grind. This can be used to dress anything.
　　Stuffed Eggplant Pouches
Cut off the stem of large eggplants, hollow out the eggplant
flesh from the cut opening, and stuff with miso. The miso can be
of any type according to individual preference. Apply kudzu powder to the opening

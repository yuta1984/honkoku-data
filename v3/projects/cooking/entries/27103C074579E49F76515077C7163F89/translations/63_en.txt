Tofu Dengaku
Grill tofu in the usual dengaku style, mix with egg and soy sauce, shake well, and when grilled it will puff up. Use irizake (seasoned sake).

Koki-dojo (Loach Dish)
This is loach soup. Boil water and put them in, when well cooked, poke with chopsticks, and the loach will separate into three pieces. Remove the head and bones, using only the flesh. Make the miso soup using the boiling water from cooking.

Awa-kamono (Millet Pickles)
Steam glutinous millet well, add salt and kudzu. The rest is the same as tofu pickles.

Kiri-zansho (Cut Sansho)
Finely powder the finest rice flour, five go, add a little powdered sansho pepper and a little sugar. Mix these three ingredients with soy sauce and steam in a steamer.

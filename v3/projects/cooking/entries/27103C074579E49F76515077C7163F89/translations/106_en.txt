During the cold season, peel and slice thinly, soak in water, then salt-press overnight and dry in good weather. For one shō of ginger, add seven gō of salt.

Persimmon Pickles

Pickle whole persimmons in rice bran. Astringent persimmons are best.

Meguro Seaweed

Remove bones from meguro, cut along the back, boil in hot water, pour vinegar over it, and top with grated daikon radish.

Wrapped Miso

Remove scales and entrails from fresh small sea bream, wash well, place in a bowl, add udo miso and steam.

Sōmen Tofu

Add wheat to tofu, grind together well, spread [cloth] on a cutting board

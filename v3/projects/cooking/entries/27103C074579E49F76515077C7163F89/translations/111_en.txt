Steam chestnuts, add salt and pickle them. The water is 
sprinkled rice. Also, it is good to pickle mackerel for sushi, fillet it into three pieces,
remove the skin, and fold it for use.

Sardine Sushi

Add a little salt to fresh sardines and pickle in vinegar, then afterward pickle in
sake lees. Use together with the lees.

Pomegranate

Cook dainagon (azuki beans) well so they don't break apart, add a little dried rice
and steam, let cool and add sugar, then cook with the azuki beans.
Knead glutinous rice flour with water, take wheat flour,
make it into powder and flatten it, wrap the aforementioned filling, tie
the opening of each one and steam. Or it can be fried mochi.

Cogon Grass

Knead wheat flour with water and soy sauce, coat with
bracken shoots, and fry in oil. Use corn flour as batter

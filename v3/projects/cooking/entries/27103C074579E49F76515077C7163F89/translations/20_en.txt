Add a little ground yam, prepare the fish as usual
making it a little soft when grilled, and season with salt.
Make it into a white-colored paste. Put it in earthenware, brush with oil,
and bake using a cedar bentwood container as a lid.


Sugar Fu (Wheat Gluten)

Add a little sugar and shinjo tofu to wheat flour for udon, 
knead with sweet sake, wrap it flat, cut finely and wrap,
make it round and deep-fry in oil.


Vinegar Pickle

Cut small fish into small pieces, boil vinegar, 
add salt, and pickle immediately. It is no good
unless the fish is alive.


Millet Wrap

Pound millet for rice cakes and set aside, wrap with wheat gluten, 
tie the opening with straw leaf
containers, and season with soy sauce.

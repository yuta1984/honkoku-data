Boil Japanese yam and cut into maple leaf shapes, soak in
plum vinegar, then cut into rounds for use. To enhance the color,

White Tofu

Cut regular tofu into thin pieces. Apply vinegar to the thin blade
and you can cut freely. Season according to taste.

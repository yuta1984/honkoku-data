Quick Amazake (Sweet Rice Drink)

One egg, one portion of sake, one cup of sugar, one cup of dried rice,
boil with an appropriate amount of water and use.


ÅŒbaku Stir-fry

Add plenty of frying oil, cut tofu into appropriate pieces,
put in an oil strainer and fry, keeping the oil hot.
Stir-fry the tofu in oil two or three times,
then put in soy sauce. Add a little sake to the soy sauce, adjust the seasoning,
and keep it boiled separately.


Tosa Powder

Take the finest glutinous rice, soak in water and leave overnight.
On the seventh or eighth day, add water drop by drop, put in a mortar
and pound from time to time. Then put it through a sieve
and sift it.

　　　Transmission of Kamaboko (Fish Cake)
First, fillet sea bream into half-bodies, scrape and take the upper flesh, grind very well in a mortar, add broth, mirin, and sake that have been boiled and cooled to egg white, and season with salt. For any fish the method is the same, though small sea bream is best for sea bream.

　　　Transmission of Hollow Tofu
First, cut a small half-block of fresh tofu and place in a deep stone bowl or similar vessel, float in water, place fire on a metal grate, and while rotating the tofu in the water, grill all four sides. Wrap that tofu in confectionery kelp, bind with thin rope, and simmer well in a large pot for one day until it becomes rock-like in shape, then season with mirin and soy sauce.

　　　Transmission of Mock Tofu
First, thoroughly boil fresh tofu, crumble it and place on a bamboo strainer to drain, season with mirin and soy sauce. Also, oil a square pot with sesame oil, add the above tofu and simmer very well, cover with a lid and press with a stone weight, leave overnight then cut.

　　　Transmission of Udon Tofu・Somen Tofu
First, wrap tofu in cloth and drain well, strain through fine cloth, and add kudzu powder

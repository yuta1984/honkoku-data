【Cooking Example (Lightly Salted Cod)】
Rice Bowl Dish
　Cut cod into dice, parboil well with sake, then simmer with sake and soy sauce, drop in a little sesame oil, and sprinkle with dried sansho pepper powder.

【Cooking Example】
Sashimi
With Irizake (seasoned sake)
　Shred finely like hair and arrange. Also parboil cod roe, knead it, and arrange together.
　　Accompaniments: rock mushrooms, raw chestnut julienne, agar, wasabi, and other suitable items

【Ingredients】
Cod Roe
　Pass through boiling water, lift out, and knead by hand to break apart before using.

【Cooking Example】
Light Clear Soup
　After drawing out salt, without passing through hot water, cut whole into thin rounds from the end and put in bowl, pour clear broth over.
　　Accompaniments: pine greens, hondawara seaweed, horsetail shoots, grated kelp, etc. should be matched appropriately

【Cooking Example】
Hand Salt Plate
　Cut from the end while still salted, top with bonito flakes, and pour sake-salt over.
　　Accompaniments: tateho, shiso seeds, green sansho, spicy skin varieties

【Ingredients】
Dried Cod (Bōdara)
　Soak in water, cut small and finely chop, mix with kōyadōfu cut into squares and simmer, generously sprinkle with plum fragrance. This is called cod floss (tara denbu).

【Ingredients】
Salted Mackerel
　Lightly salted and refined. If you quickly draw out the salt before using, it is not inferior to fresh fish, and depending on the cooking method, can be even better.

【Cooking Example】
Light Clear Soup
　Remove the flesh and top with bonito flakes.

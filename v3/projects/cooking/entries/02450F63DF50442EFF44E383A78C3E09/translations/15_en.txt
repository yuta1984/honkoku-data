【Cooking Example】
Tai-kaga (Sea Bream Powder)
　Remove the skin from the above sea bream, place only the flesh over charcoal fire
　to dry well, then pound in a stone mortar until fine.
　Season this like plum branches with sake and soy sauce.

【Ingredient】
Dried Cod
　Soak in water, remove and use within
　two or three days. Depending on the method, some
　wash and cook immediately without soaking in water.
　The following preparation methods are described in detail.

【Cooking Example】
Mixed Namasu
　Drain the cod that was soaked in water, grill and finely
　shred, cut lettuce leaves finely and mix together, pour vinegar and
　soy sauce over, crush raw chestnuts and add.

【Cooking Example】
Small Bowl Dish
Denbu (Seasoned Fish Flakes)
　Take only the large bones of dried cod, cut at the bone joints to make
　small wheels. Add plenty of miso beans and bonito flakes,
　season with mirin sake and soy sauce and simmer《However, if left until the beans are cooked,｜the bones become soft｜as well》

【Cooking Example】
Same as Above
　Peel the skin of dried cod soaked in water, finely cut both
　skin and flesh, simmer in sake only and squeeze out moisture,
　dress with pepper miso.

【Cooking Example】
Hand-Salt Preparation
　Wash dried cod well from the beginning, cut into small strips
　about the size of tanzaku, put sake in a jar, soak in this, seal well
　and use after four to five days or six to seven days.

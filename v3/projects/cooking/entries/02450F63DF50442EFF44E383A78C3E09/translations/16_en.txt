【Ingredient】
Dried Pike Conger (Hoshihamo)
　Soak this also in water for 5-7 days, changing the water frequently,
　remove when sufficiently softened, then expose to sun
　for about half a day before use.

【Cooking Example】
Long Plate
　Remove bones, grill with sansho pepper soy sauce marinade
　　Accompaniments　Braised burdock root, or simmered plums
　　　　　　　　　　Also types like braised Chinese yam

【Cooking Example】
Medium Plate
　Cut lengthwise, make into simmered dish. Add plenty of
　green kelp strips and bonito flakes.

【Cooking Example】
Mixed Namasu
　Finely chop with knife,
　mix with finely chopped daikon radish and walnuts, dress with
　strong vinegar.

【Cooking Example】
Inkstone Lid
or　Jar Combination Dish
　Chinese yam, wild yam, or new burdock root types
　Peel the skin of the above pike conger, wrap with this skin, make into
　soy sauce marinade grilled dish and cut from the end.

【Ingredient】
Monkfish Skin (Anpi)
　Soak dried skin in hot water before use. Some have
　flesh attached, but that is not good. It's best to have
　only the strained skin.

【Cooking Example】
Red Miso
Soup
　Prepare like regular monkfish soup.
　　　Accompaniments　◦Aralia sprouts　　◦Aralia buds
　　　Garnish　　　　　Dried sansho pepper

【Cooking Example】
Bowl Dish
　Cut aralia or strip-cut daikon radish to appropriate size,
　add and dress with mustard miso nuta sauce
　　　　Red pepper miso is also good.

【Cooking Example】
Clear Soup (Suimono)
　Cut red jellyfish into strips or diamond shapes
　　　Accompaniments　◦Sea pine clusters　◦Kamoji seaweed　◦Mozuku seaweed
　　　　　　Combine with the above types. However, wasabi may be
　　　　　　added to the clear soup. Karashi (mustard) is also good.

【Ingredient】
Sea Urchin (Uni)
　Add to clear soup as garnish. Wasabi for the finishing touch.
　Also mix with miso for tofu dengaku as a single dish.
　Also good on its own. All domestic products are the same.

【Ingredient】
Karasumi, Salted Shrimp, Sea Cucumber Intestines (Konowata), Fukudami, Salted Ami Shrimp
And Others
　All domestic salted preserved foods are useful items of one kind.

【Ingredient】
Salted Birds
　Crane, wild goose, duck, and any other birds - if the salt is well removed,
　there is no difference from ordinary bird preparation methods.
　　　　However, salted sparrow is excellent in clear soup with squeezed daikon juice added.

　　　Section on Cooking Dried Fish

【Ingredient】
Dried Sea Bream
　Soak in fresh water for four to five days. Of course, change the water daily.
　When well swollen, remove and drain well of moisture,
　then expose to sun for about half a day, after which various
　preparations can be made.

【Cooking Example】
Red Miso Main Soup
　Take sea bream prepared as above, cut with skin attached into pieces about
　eight-tenths square, grill both sides until well browned, prepare
　dried greens by simmering well in advance, serve in red miso main soup
　with the sea bream as oka-ire (mountain-style garnish).

【Cooking Example】
Bowl Fish Dish (Hachi-zakana)
　Cut the same sea bream to appropriate size, simmer only with
　mirin sake and grilled salt. When the cooking liquid becomes
　like syrup, arrange in vessel and cover abundantly with
　plum branch denbu, burying it before serving.

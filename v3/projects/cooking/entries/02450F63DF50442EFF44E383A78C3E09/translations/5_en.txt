Salted Items - Fish Cooking Section

【Ingredients and Preparation】
Salt-cured Sea Bream
Take good salt-cured sea bream from the new rice season, soak it in clear water for two to three days, and when the salt has been sufficiently drawn out, remove it and wash thoroughly with water, then parboil and set aside for use.

【Cooking Example】
Light Miso Soup
Cut into appropriate-sized cubes and sear the surface
Accompaniments: ○Thinly sliced Chinese artichoke ○Grated Japanese yam ○Squeezed fresh shiso leaves, rubbed ○Other suitable items as desired

【Cooking Example】
Light Clear Soup
For this, only draw out the salt without parboiling, thoroughly wipe away moisture with a cloth, and shred finely like hair for use.
Accompaniments: Large pickled plum (one with salt boiled out), enoki mushrooms
When these two items are cooked and served in bowls, place the aforementioned sea bream on top.

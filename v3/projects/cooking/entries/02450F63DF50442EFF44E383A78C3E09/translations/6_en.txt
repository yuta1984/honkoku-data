【Cooking Example (Salt-cured Sea Bream)】
Bowl Side Dish
　Take the parboiled fish and cut into large pieces, cook thoroughly, simmer well with only mirin and sake, and sprinkle generously with bonito flakes.
　　However, if the saltiness is insufficient after cooking, add roasted salt.

【Cooking Example】
Also
　For this, only draw out the salt without parboiling, wash well and remove moisture, prepare as sashimi and pour ginger vinegar over it.
　　However, depending on the accompaniments, it can also be made into a fresh namasu salad.

【Ingredients】
Hanging Sea Bream
　Though the same type of fish, since it is small sea bream, the preparation method differs. Salt removal is the same as above, but since it is generally saltier than regular salt-cured sea bream, one should be aware of this.

【Cooking Example】
Soup
　When the salt has been drawn out, grill well until charred, rub off the skin, finely pound both flesh and bones together, and add to clear tofu soup.
　　　　　　　　　　　　Garnish: roasted red pepper

【Ingredients】
Grilled Sea Bream
　This is a specialty that comes from Echigo around the third month of spring. It is said to be grilled directly with seawater at the beach and served - this is true beach-grilled fish. It is extremely high-quality. It is good to use as is.

【Ingredients】
Lightly Salted Cod
　Soak in water to draw out the salt. However, if too much salt is drawn out, the flavor becomes poor. It is best when a little saltiness remains.

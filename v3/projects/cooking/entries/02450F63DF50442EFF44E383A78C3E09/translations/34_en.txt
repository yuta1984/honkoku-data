　　　Besides this, there are already numerous menu arrangements corresponding to the four seasons of spring, summer, autumn and winter in the previous two volumes, so one should combine and arrange them with creativity. What is written above is merely recording the general order of dishes. The sequence is not limited to this, but it is essential to understand not to serve dishes with the same flavors and same vessels repeatedly. The ingenuity should be left to the intention of the person doing the cooking.

　　　　　　　Preparation Methods Section
○Yam cake: Peel thick natural yam, grate and grind well, add domyoji rice
　and high-quality wheat flour, steam in a steamer, let cool and cut large,
　deep-fry in sesame oil and season

○Quince (or Buddha's hand citron): Shave the skin and flesh into good-sized pieces, wrap in kudzu
　powder and deep-fry in sesame oil. Sprinkle with grilled salt

【Ingredient (Whale Azuki Meat)】
Same
Azuki meat
　This is commonly available but of inferior quality.
　Add white parts of green onions, or root parsley, or mizuna greens,
　Good for clear soups as a substitute for duck, etc.

【Ingredient】
Salted Trout
　Same as salt-cured salmon but with limited uses.
　Make into sushi, or grill and blanch in hot water with salt,
　With grated daikon and ginger, only these types.

【Ingredient】
Salted Mullet
　This also has limited uses. Even when salt is removed and used fresh-like
　in clear soups, the flavor is not
　good.

【Ingredient】
Salted Mackerel
　Lightly salted ones are extremely delicious
　　　　　　Also　Fresh mackerel

【Cooking Example】
Overnight Sushi
　Remove some salt and wipe off moisture well, fillet the fish
　Remove bones and marinate in vinegar, squeeze out at the right timing
　Remove moisture with cloth, season rice appropriately with roasted salt and press
　flat in sushi box, cut into proper squares. Making it with okara is also
　good.　　Accompaniments　Water pepper　Fresh ginger

【Cooking Example】
Hand-salt Plate
　Wash well and remove bones, slice thin from the end
　Pour on water pepper vinegar. This is the method for fresh mackerel but the salt balance is
　good, looks like fresh fish and has a light, pleasant flavor.

【Cooking Example】
Tea Bowl (Steamed)
　Wash well, remove flesh and cut into medium pieces, parboil
　in hot water, change the water and adjust salt seasoning
　Add a little vinegar when removing from heat.
　　　　However, use plenty of broth and keep it from becoming cloudy.

【Cooking Example】
Medium Plate
　Wash well and fillet into three pieces, remove top and bottom, only the middle
　flesh is grilled. When white with salt rising and grilled,
　place on medium plate each time, strain daikon juice with something acidic
　and pour over the top before serving.

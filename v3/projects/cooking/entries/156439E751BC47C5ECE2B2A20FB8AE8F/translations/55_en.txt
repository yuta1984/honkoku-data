　　　Large煎貫 (Seasoned Tube) Egg Method
First, prepare a bamboo tube setup: the outer bamboo tube should have joints attached, while the inner bamboo tube should be bottomless. Separate the egg yolks and whites, put them in two different containers, and beat them well. First, put a little kudzu powder in the bottom of the outer bamboo tube and press it down firmly. Insert the inner bamboo tube, fill the space between the outer and inner bamboo tubes with egg white, then fill the inner bamboo tube with egg yolk - this should be put somewhat toward the inside. Put a little more kudzu powder on top, then slowly pull out the inner bamboo tube, so that the egg white will enclose the egg yolk. Then sprinkle kudzu powder generously on top, cover tightly with thick paper two or three times

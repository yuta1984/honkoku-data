　　　Method for Tied Octopus
First, as described above, cut into two or three sun pieces, peel thinly, flatten the flesh. For appetizer dishes, cut the width to about five bu, tie like noshi ribbons, and simmer with equal parts soy sauce and sake. For darker soy sauce, tie with slightly wider strips. Usage depends on preference.

　　　Method for Tender-Cooked Octopus
First, wash the octopus very well, and on a cutting board, gently pound with a pestle. However, sprinkle powdered tea and then pound. Cut the legs in half, cook with sake over charcoal fire for a long time, then add soy sauce on top and simmer slowly. The cutting method is as you wish. It is good with wasabi miso or ginger vinegar. Other methods as desired.

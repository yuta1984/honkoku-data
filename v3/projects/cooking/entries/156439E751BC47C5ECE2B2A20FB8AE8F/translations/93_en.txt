Method for Somen-style Squid

First, wash large squid well, slice very thinly with a sashimi knife, roll it up and cut into small rounds, briefly dip in warm water, then put in cold water to cool, dye in various colors such as red and yellow - most importantly, soak in the dye while hot and let cool. Methods of use: first for sashimi, second for side dishes, third for clear soup, fourth for tea bowl dishes and clear broth, fifth for dressed dishes, sixth for various second soups - consider each appropriately.

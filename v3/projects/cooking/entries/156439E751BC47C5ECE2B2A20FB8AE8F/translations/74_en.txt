　　　Method for Egg White Porridge
First, although this method is known to people of the world, I record it here. Wash about five gō of finest new rice well with water, then
for the water to be added to this, soak finest kelp - but not the kind pickled in vinegar, mountain kelp - for about half a day or one night in water, rub it out very well with water
and wash it, then add two shō of water to this, when the extraction water becomes about one shō two gō, remove the aforementioned kelp,
then put the aforementioned washed rice into this, add one small cup of aged sake, and cook well. When it becomes white porridge, remove from heat,
into this, crack about fifteen eggs, mix very well and pour in. Immediately cover the pot with a lid, after a while,
serve it out. However, if these eggs are from silky fowl, it is extremely good. It is a great medicine for vital energy.

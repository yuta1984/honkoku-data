　　　Method for Potato-topped Eggs
First, peel mountain yam and grate it very well. If this quantity is about five go, prepare about three go of dashi soy sauce. The proper proportion should be about the same as udon broth. Then gradually add this into the yam and mix together. For about ten eggs, add about three go of dashi. However, this should be water-based dashi. Mix well with the eggs, put in a bowl and steam. Serve this in a dish with the yam sauce poured over it. However, the dashi mixed with the yam should be quite hot.

　　　Method for Barley Rice Eggs
First, take about five go of barley, select it well, wash with water and cook it as rice, adding about two go of rice as well. After the rice is cooked, put the barley rice in a bamboo strainer, wash well in water, pour water over it and let it drip. Then when putting it back in the pot, crack about twenty-five eggs into it, mix well, and add one and a half small cups of aged sake.

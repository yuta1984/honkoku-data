　　　Method for Shiso-pickled Eggs
One: Take the shiso (perilla leaves) that was used with pickled plums, spread it out as is, make small thin omelets, layer about three shiso leaves, sprinkle white sugar, layer the thin omelet on top of this, sprinkle white sugar on top of this again, gradually layering them up. Then press it down in a bowl or pot, sprinkle sugar on top of this as well, press down firmly and let it pickle. After about fourteen or fifteen days, it should be ready to serve. How to use: 1) for sashimi, cut and layer; 2) namasu (vinegared salad); 3) tori-zakana (appetizer); 4) teiryū-mono (boxed dishes); 5) first, second, third course soups; 6) suimono (clear soup); 7) small dish items; and other suitable accompaniments.

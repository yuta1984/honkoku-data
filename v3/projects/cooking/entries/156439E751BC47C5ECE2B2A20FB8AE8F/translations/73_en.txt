　　　Method for Egg Kamaboko
First, whether using conger eel or sea bream, grind the flesh slightly, then add more than half egg whites, grind thoroughly. However, it's better to use very little ground fish. Mix in a little kansarashi powder, 
also dissolve this with egg white, then mix together and attach to a board to steam. However, first apply a little heat to the top, then put in a steaming basket to steam. For usage, it's especially excellent for sashimi.

　　　Method for Egg Yamabuki Kamaboko
First, mix the yolk of hard-boiled eggs into the aforementioned ground fish,
then also mix in half the yolk of raw eggs and grind together. This is also the same as before. Apply a light browning without burning, then steam.

　　　Method for Spiced Clove-grilled Eggs
First, powder about one ryō of cinnamon, crack about five eggs into it, mix these two ingredients very well together, season according to taste.

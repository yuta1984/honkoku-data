　　　Method for Konpeitō Eggs
First, strain the egg whites through cloth, heat an egg pan well, and drop the egg whites little by little with chopstick tips to cook them. Those that are too large are not good. Cook many of these and set aside, combining them with white sugar. Then, dip each of these small cooked pieces in egg liquid and cook them - they will become round. Adding ginger juice when cooking is especially good. Usage: good for one portable lunch box dish, one appetizer dish, one hand-held appetizer dish, and small plate dishes.

　　　Method for Round Snow Eggs
First, put egg whites in a bowl and beat vigorously with five thin bamboo skewers continuously until they foam up, then continue beating even more until they become firm.

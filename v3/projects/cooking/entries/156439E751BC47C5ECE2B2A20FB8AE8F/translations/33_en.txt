Apply raw egg white to the cut opening, lightly cook over distant fire, then briefly immerse in hot water and remove. Wrap in paper, shape into a round with your hands, then press flat. This can be used for any kind of finger food. ○For Ogura eggs, follow the same method, but fill with cooked azuki beans mixed with a little roasted salt and white sugar, seal the opening well, and proceed as before.

　　Method for Azuki Bean Mochi Eggs
For this, eggs from small bantam chickens are good. Hard-boil very small eggs and remove the shells. Put the eggs in sugar syrup and coat them all around. When cooked azuki beans are attached, they look like mochi. Usage is the same as before for: serving platters, finger foods, tiered box dishes, appetizers, large covered dishes, and others.

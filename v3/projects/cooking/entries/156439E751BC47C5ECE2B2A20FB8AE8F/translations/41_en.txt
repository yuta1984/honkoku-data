　　　Method for Steamed Bamboo Shoot Eggs
Use bamboo shoots about one shaku long, but madake bamboo is not good. Go to a bamboo grove and dig up considerably thick bamboo shoots deeply from their roots, and use them as they are. Cut the aforementioned bamboo shoot horizontally about five sun from the vertical, cutting only one side vertically, and scrape out the inside of the bamboo shoot cleanly with a small knife. However, be careful not to remove the outer skin on top. Crack eggs according to visual estimate, beat them very well, add a little salt, turn the bamboo shoot cut side up and open it slightly, then put the beaten eggs inside. Then sprinkle a little high-quality kudzu powder at the opening, close the outer skin of the cut opening well, tie tightly with thin rope, put in a pot standing upright, stuff with rice bran around it, add water at the bottom, cover well and steam.

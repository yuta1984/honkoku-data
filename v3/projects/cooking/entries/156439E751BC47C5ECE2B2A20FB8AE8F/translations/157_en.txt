　　　Method for Golden Tofu
Take two bowls of rough tofu and drain the water. Then put only the yolks of fifteen hard-boiled eggs in a mortar and grind well. Then crack five raw eggs into it and grind them together well. Also add one cup of mirin sake and half a cup of wheat flour for steamed buns, mix everything well by grinding, then gradually add the aforementioned rough tofu and mix well by grinding. Lay a cloth in a steamer, put the mixture on top, level it well and steam - this steams easily. It can be used for anything.

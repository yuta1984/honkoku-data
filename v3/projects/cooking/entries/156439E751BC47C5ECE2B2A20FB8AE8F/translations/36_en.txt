　　　Method for Rikyū Eggs
For this, take one gō of white sesame seeds, remove the oil, grind well, then add about five shaku of aged sake, grind well. Break ten eggs into this, mix very well together, put this also in a box or bowl and steam. Usage is the same as before.

　　　Method for Walnut Eggs
For this method, take about thirty walnut meats, remove the shells, grind and spread in a mortar. Add one small cup of sake to this, add a little soy sauce, then break in about eight eggs, grind well together, and steam. Usage is the same as before for any purpose. However, for sashimi, cool and layer by cutting. Also especially good for daibyiki service.

　　　Method for Genji Eggs
For this, break eggs in an egg pan, cook to about one bu thickness. Then peel thick lotus root, boil it well and remove moisture. Spread egg white on the aforementioned egg, sprinkle a little wheat flour, put it inside the lotus root, tie well, and roll...

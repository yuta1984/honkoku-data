Soft-Boiled Ark Shell Preparation
1. Remove the ark shell from its shell, immediately place it on a cutting board, and gently pound it with daikon radish. This will make the shellfish somewhat flattened. First simmer this gently in mirin wine, then braise it with soy sauce and sake, and it will become very tender.

　　　Ark Shell Kamaboko Preparation  
1. Similarly, after gently pounding with daikon radish, put it in a mortar and grind it very well. Add salt. Also, whether mutsu fish or sea bream, add a little and mix, grind together very well, attach to a board and grill over fire, then steam the result - the flavor and aroma will be exceptional. Usage depends on the cook's preference: flat plate dishes, tea bowl dishes, or whatever else.

1. For hanpei, add a little egg white on top of this, thin it out with dashi broth, and make it soft. Usage: for flat plate dishes, tea bowl dishes, and shiki miso types, use wasabi or Japanese pepper, or shiki-an sauce is also good.

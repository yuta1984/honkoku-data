Put the aforementioned sake over fire, add sugar, and also dissolve one small cup of finest kuzu starch in separate sake, add to the mixture, and stir very well ○ When making this into medicinal sake, take one ryō of aconite, one ryō of cinnamon, two ryō of lotus seeds - grind each of these three ingredients finely into powder and mix into the sake. In all cases, bring to a good boil, remove the pot from heat, cool well, and store in jars. However, if prepared during the cold season, it will keep until the following year's dog days. This medicinal sake method is a secret transmission of the Nagasaki Gotō school. Mix one small dish of this sake with one dish of regular sake, warm it, and drink morning and evening - this is the foremost medicine for vital energy. Also, for cooking, steam sea bream that has been salted overnight, pour sweet dew eggs over it, and serve.

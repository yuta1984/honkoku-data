　　　Ogura Ark Shell Preparation
1. Split open the ark shell, insert a knife, and remove the intestines. Gently pound the opened meat portion with daikon radish. What to put inside the right shell are roasted soybeans, green soybeans, ginkgo nuts, or roasted azuki beans. For the opening of the shell that has been opened as described, mix steamed bun flour with kudzu and apply it, wrap the top with semi-transparent paper, and steam. Usage varies according to the dish: raised platform dishes, tea bowl dishes, flat plate dishes, confection bowl dishes, Nagasaki shippoku cuisine, and other dishes as appropriate.

　　　Ark Shell Medicine Rice Crackers Preparation
1. Fillet the ark shell into two pieces, sprinkle with kudzu, and on a cutting board, gently pound with a pestle until it becomes thin. When this happens, sprinkle white sugar little by little, also sprinkle kudzu powder, put in a steamer and steam. Then take it out, cut it round, gradually fire-dry it until it dries, press it with a board, and after that, put it in a kotatsu and dry it thoroughly. What is called Nagasaki ground rice crackers refers to this.

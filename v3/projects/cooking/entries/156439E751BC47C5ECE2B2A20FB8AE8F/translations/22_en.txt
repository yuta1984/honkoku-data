Method for Salt-Cured Bird
Take only the meat of wild goose or duck, salt it, wrap in kombu, and store in a jar - it will remain fresh like raw bird indefinitely without drying out. The bones should be removed first. Now, when cooking salt-cured bird, put lukewarm water in a bowl, soak the bird as is, and when the skin loosens, remove it and pluck the feathers as you would with fresh bird. Hot water is not good. The way to prepare this bird is to cut it thick, put a little salt in the lukewarm water, and the salt will come out of the bird quickly, which is good. If you stir it too much, the bird will fall apart, which is bad.

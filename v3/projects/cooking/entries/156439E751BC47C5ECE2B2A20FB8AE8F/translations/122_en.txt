　　　White Ball Squid Preparation
1. Prepare a large squid, peel the skin and wash thoroughly, then grind in a mortar. Mix egg white with kanzarashi flour dissolved in water, add little by little and grind well together. Shape into white balls on a cutting board, put in water and gently roll with hands to create a lustrous and exceptionally beautiful result. Usage is the same as before. This is appreciated for its beauty.

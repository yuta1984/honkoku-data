　　　Method for Nori-wrapped Eggs
First, cook eggs in a pan, making them round without browning. Then soak Suizenji nori in water for a while, remove and wipe off the water well, then spread on a board.
Mix kansarashi powder with half the amount of kudzu powder, sprinkle thinly on the nori, apply a little egg white, place the cooked egg on top, roll tightly, then wrap the top in cloth, put in a steaming basket, steam and cool, then cut as desired. Usage is the same as before. However, cut thinly, it's also good for sashimi.

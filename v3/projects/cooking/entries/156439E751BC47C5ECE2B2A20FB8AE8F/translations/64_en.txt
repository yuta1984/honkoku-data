　　　Method for Nagasaki Egg Hiryōzu (Fried Tofu Balls)
First, crack about ten eggs and beat them roughly, then add two small cups of wheat flour for steamed buns into this mixture. For flavoring, add julienned wood ear mushrooms, diced roasted chestnuts, whole peppercorns, black sesame seeds, and finely chopped wild goose, duck, or dove, then put this in a box or bowl and steam it. Meanwhile, heat sesame oil, scoop up the steamed egg mixture with a golden ladle and put it in a pot to deep-fry briefly. To serve, it's particularly good to immediately pour wasabi soy sauce over it and serve - all the same as before.

First, cut off the octopus suckers while still raw, put only the suckers in hot water and stir around. Also mix vinegar with water, put the suckers in, immediately remove, and when rubbed by hand, the outer skin comes off and they become like popped glutinous rice. For usage: main soup (miso), second soup (clear broth), clear soup, and for ōhira-mono dishes, sprinkle on top. For aemono (dressed dishes), choko cups are good.

　　　Method for Thread Octopus
First, discard the tips of the octopus legs from which the suckers have been removed as described above, put them one by one in hot water, stir around, immediately remove, cool in water, cut into pieces about two sun long, and peel very thinly. Then roll as before, cut finely from the edge, put in water and stretch out, and they become long like threads. For red thread, soak in hot water and immediately remove, dye with sappanwood. There are various ways to use it. First of all, it is good for sashimi.

　　　Method for Flower Octopus
First, take the thick parts of octopus legs from which suckers have been removed, cut into pieces about two sun long, peel the outer skin, and soak in lukewarm water, which makes it very easy to peel.

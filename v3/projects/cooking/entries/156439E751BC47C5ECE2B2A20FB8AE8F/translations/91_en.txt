　　　Method for Otsu Ishiba Funazushi (Fermented Crucian Carp Sushi)

First, during the cold season, remove the gills and entrails mentioned above and crush the head. Prepare plenty of salt, press the crucian carp onto the salt from both sides, and salt the carp thoroughly. Then cook black rice as hard-cooked rice, let it cool well, add salt to taste, and pickle with plenty of this rice. Also, apply pressure at the beginning, after about twenty days lighten the pressure, and after about seventy days it should be well fermented. If done in this manner, it will keep until the following year, and especially during summer and autumn, the flavor will be even better and the bones will be soft - exceptionally good. It serves well as hand-picked appetizers and also makes good gifts.

　　　Method for Quick Sushi (Hayazushi)

First, add three go (540ml) of salt to about one sho (1.8 liters) of sake and cook, add one go (180ml) of vinegar to this, let the alcoholic aroma cool, cook rice with this mixture, cool it well, and season it slightly saltier than normal taste from the above mixture

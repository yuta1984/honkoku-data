　　　Method for Sake Lees Pickled Eggs
First, this is for hard-boiled eggs. Mix rice bran miso with hot water in about half proportion, bring the water to a boil, add eggs from cold water into this and boil them through. When they are about half-cooked, remove them and put them in water, peel them, and pickle them in Nara pickle lees. Take them out after about five days. They should keep for about fourteen to fifteen days. Do not add salt. Usage: for one appetizer dish, one tea bowl dish, one small plate dish, for formal presentation dishes, lightly brown them and serve with plum kernel miso.

　　　Method for Hard-boiled Eggs with Kuzu Starch Sauce
First, peel ordinary hard-boiled eggs, cut them into about three pieces, cover with thin kuzu starch sauce, and pour ginger juice over them. Also serve with grated wasabi on the side.

　　How to Make Ogura Pike Conger
First, grind the pike conger very well until it becomes like soft princess nori seaweed,
scoop a little into a moderately-sized round tea bowl, and into it put
about twenty black beans, add a little roasted salt. Also put the
ground fish meat on top so the beans cannot be seen. However, fill the tea bowl to about eight-tenths,
steam it in a rice steamer, cool in water, and
make it into a side dish. How to use it: on flat plates, confection bowls,
with thick sauce on top, place grated wasabi or ginger,
or for tea bowl dishes, bed it with miso.

　　How to Make Manjū Pike Conger
First, also make this into ground fish meat, wrap
strained sweet bean paste mixed with miso inside,
steam it, then cool in water, then dry it, then in an egg-frying
pan, lightly brown it and steam in a bamboo steamer,
then make it into a side dish.

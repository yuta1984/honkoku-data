　　　Method for Bird Simmered Eggs
Take a small duck, snipe, or pigeon, remove the bones by the bag method, remove the bird's feathers, wash well with water, remove the backbone by breaking and pulling out - if the bones are difficult to remove, cut from the belly, open it up, and take out the bones. Then remove the shell from hard-boiled eggs, insert them lengthwise into the bird's body, sprinkle a little of wheat flour mixed with equal parts of cold-bleached flour, apply a little egg white, wrap the bird's body tightly, sew up both ends or the seams with hemp thread, wrap in thin cloth, steam, cool well, and cut into rounds. At that time, untie the thread. For serving platters, flat dishes, confection bowls, tea bowls, and large inkstone lid dishes, all should be cut into rounds for use.

　　　Method for Egg Sōmen

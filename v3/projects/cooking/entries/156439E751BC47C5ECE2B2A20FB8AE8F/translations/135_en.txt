Method for Cooking Turban Shells (Sazae)
1. Remove turban shells from their shells, wash the meat well, cut and layer them, arrange in rounds, and serve in a vessel. For miso pickling or sake lees pickling method: sprinkle the turban shell meat with a little white sugar, wait a while, then pickle in miso. For sake lees pickling, add salt. This method uses five gō of sake lees mixed with two gō of salt, pickle it, and for four to five days, apply light pressure on top. When using, wash well. For sashimi, peel thinly, roll, and cut into small rounds. You can also arrange them into thread-like strips. For main soup use miso, for second soup use clear broth, for thick soy sauce slice thinly, and for drinking accompaniment, quickly stir-fry with sake and soy sauce.

　　　Method for Matsukaze Eggs
First, crack eggs as they are, beat well, add a little ginger juice, also add a little steamed bun flour, sugar, and also a little sake. Strain this through cloth, spread in an egg pan to a thickness of about [blank] bu, and cook. Sprinkle poppy seeds on top, cook without burning, cut to size, serve on chopstick plates, and use.

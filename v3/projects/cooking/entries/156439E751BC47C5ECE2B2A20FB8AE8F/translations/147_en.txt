　　　How to Make Miso-Pickled Salmon

First, this is also done the same way as previously described. However, this one is good without sugar. For trout, sugar is added out of fear that the aroma might be lost. Salmon pickled in this miso also has exceptional color and good flavor.

　　　How to Make Sake Lees Pickled Fish

First, this is also done as described above. Mix about three gō of salt with approximately one shō of sake lees, and the pickling method is the same for all.

　　　How to Make Miso-Pickled Semi-Dried Round Herring

First, remove the tail and head of the round herring, and also remove the bones. When you want to use this quickly, pickle it in white miso for one day and night, take it out the next day, wipe it well with paper, and cook. However, washing with water is very bad.

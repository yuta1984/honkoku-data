　　　Method for Cracked Barley with Egg Binding
One: Pound barley well to hull it, then grind it with a stone mill, sift through a medium-fine sieve, remove the fallen powder, take what looks like small rice grains and prepare like rice bran, cook like rice, after a while rinse roughly with water, then prepare kelp dashi, let it cool slightly, in a soup bowl with one bowl of dashi per egg, mix very well, add about half of the aforementioned cracked barley to this, put in a pot and steam. For the broth, use clear soup, or if using soy sauce broth with grated yam, it will be exceptionally good. Seasonings are red pepper, grated daikon radish, green nori seaweed or dried citrus peel, and black pepper.

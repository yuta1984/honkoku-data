　　Method for Raft Eggs
This also involves cracking eggs, mixing them well, and cooking them thin in an egg-frying pan. Cut to appropriate size, then wrap around a brush handle or straw. Apply the aforementioned egg white to the seam, cook slightly, then remove the inner bamboo (shaft) or straw. Next, apply egg white vertically to this, attach the rolled portions in various lengths in stages, and cook slightly in the egg-frying pan - this will resemble a raft. Uses include platform dishes, assorted appetizers, clear soups, sashimi, and other appropriate applications.

　　Method for Bamboo-Style Grilled Eggs
Strain the egg white through half-paper and cook by double-boiling in a suisen pot. This should also be cooked quite thin, then hold by the corners and work it, place in the auxiliary brazier, and use elegantly. For platform dishes and large inkstone lid dishes.

　　Method for Bamboo-Mat Grilled Eggs
This involves cracking eggs as they are and cooking in an egg pot. Cook this thin, then cut to appropriate size, following the previous raft egg method...

　　Egg Section
　　　Method for Bainin Eggs and Gyūhi Eggs

1. First, take about one gō of bainin (apricot kernels), remove the thin skin and soak in warm water for a while, then grind well in a mortar. Into this, add 50 monme of white sugar and 5 gō of aged sake, mix well, then crack in about 12 fresh eggs one by one, mix this very well and put into a box or bowl to steam. The way to use it is with wasabi

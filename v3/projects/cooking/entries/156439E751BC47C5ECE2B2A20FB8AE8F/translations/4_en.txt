The Secret Box of Ten Thousand Treasured Recipes (Manpō Ryōri Himitsu-bako) ≪First Part | Five Volumes≫
　　　Also known as "One Hundred Delicacies of Eggs"
This book is a collection of one hundred varieties of chicken eggs, birds, fish, as well as Buddhist vegetarian cuisine, tea ceremony cuisine, and moreover, rare preparation methods that have not appeared in cookbooks since ancient times.
It details secret culinary techniques unknown to people of the world, as well as the preparation methods of dishes from our school, written clearly in hiragana script, making it a book that can be used for cooking anywhere.

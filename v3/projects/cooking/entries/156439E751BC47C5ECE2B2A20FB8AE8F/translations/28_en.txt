　　Method for Silver Thread Eggs
This also uses egg white, mixed with silver leaf powder, following the same method as the gold thread eggs above.

　　Method for Kamoji Eggs
This also uses egg white strained through paper. Finely grind pot soot and strain through fine silk, then mix into the egg white and blend well. Strain again through silk cloth to achieve a good black color. Cook this also using double-boiling method with a narcissus pan. Usage is the same as before. Also for tea cuisine, same as before.

　　Method for White Hair Eggs
This also uses egg white, well-strained as in the previous method, and cooked by double-boiling in the pan. Usage same as before.

　　Method for Thread-Braided Eggs
This also uses well-strained egg white. Keep the white as is, for red color add fresh safflower rouge, for blue finely chop green vegetables

　　　Method for Castella Eggs
Take eighteen finest large eggs, 160 monme of white sugar, and eight gō of wheat flour. Mix these three ingredients well together, put into a cloth strainer or coarse-meshed cloth, squeeze once, then generously oil the bottom of a Yamato pot or Edo pot, put in the aforementioned egg mixture, make a weak fire below, place an andon fire dish on top, spread a layer of ash in this, put fire on top of that, and bake. This method is the same as for finest confections.

　　　Method for Foam Snow Eggs
Put egg whites into paper, squeeze and strain well, put into a deep bowl, and whisk busily with six or seven thin bamboo skewers until the egg whites increase greatly in volume and foam up. Place this on a thin board about three bu thick, put in a steamer, steam, and remove immediately. To tie them, put into water, cut finely and long, tie in the water. Usage is for clear soup...

How to Pickle Fresh Sea Cucumber

First, use very good quality rice bran miso paste. Bury the sea cucumber in it, press more rice bran miso on top, then cover completely with arame seaweed or coarse kelp, leaving no gaps. Cover the container tightly so no air can enter, and it will keep unchanged until June of the following year. Also, for winter preservation, mix crushed pottery shards with vinegar and pickle the sea cucumber while still alive - this will preserve the flavor unchanged for about twenty days.

How to Prepare Kinnamako (Golden Sea Cucumber) Hanpei

First, soak the dried sea cucumber in water for four to five days, changing the water frequently until the water runs clear. Wash out the flesh and innards, then soak in water for another day. However, add straw and sugar to the soaking water, shake well, and leave overnight. When cooking, simmer briefly with a little of the sugar and straw, then remove the straw. Add a little water and a little mirin sake, simmer a while longer, then remove from heat. Let it drain and dry, then place in a grinding bowl and grind slowly until it becomes like paste.

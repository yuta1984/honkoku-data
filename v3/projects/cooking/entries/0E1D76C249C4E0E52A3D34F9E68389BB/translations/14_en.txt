　　Pickled Water Dropwort
Pickle root water dropwort as stem pickles. The pickling method is the same as for vegetables. Use with soy sauce and vinegar.

　　Strawberry-colored Soup
Remove the shells from fresh kuruma prawns, pound only the flesh and grind it, shape into small round pieces and add to soup. The color becomes red.

　　Cloud-covered Tofu
Cut tofu into appropriate sizes, dust with rice flour and steam. Top with wasabi miso.

　　Simmered and Dressed Vegetables
Cut daikon radish into rectangular strips and boil. Grind sesame seeds, ginger, and miso into a hard paste, discard the boiling water from the daikon, add the miso to the pot and mix together.

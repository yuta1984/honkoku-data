　　Furofuki (Bath-warmed dish)
Make conger eel into a paste as usual, add a little binding agent,
put it in a bamboo tube and push it out, boil it well in
hot water, then simmer in soy sauce. Thicken with kudzu starch and
serve with grated ginger.

　　Yoshino River
Cut pumpkin thinly into the shape of cherry blossoms, make thin
rice bran miso soup and pour over it. Use cherry seeds as garnish.

　　Smartweed Pickle
Pick eggplants and pickle them whole with smartweed. Use
strong salt and apply heavy weights. It resembles sushi. Until the following year
the color does not change, and it is like pickled vegetables.

Remove the stems from eggplants, wash them, and cut them into wedge shapes about 2 bu thick with the skin on. Simmer them in raw soy sauce, arrange on bamboo drying racks, and dry in shade. Cannot be stored for long periods.

　　Egg Yuba (Tofu Skin)
Make scrambled eggs without burning them, spread them out in strips about 3 sun wide and long, then fold them to about 2.5 sun and shape like yuba (tofu skin). Simmer with kudzu starch using bonito dashi and soy sauce.

　　Sea Urchin Eggs
Add sea urchin to scrambled eggs, roll them up, slice and use.

　　【Red mark】Dashi Kuzushi
This is an abbreviation of shippoku (Chinese-style banquet cuisine). Make shippoku cuisine with ordinary cooking methods. Or serve nimono (simmered dishes), grilled items, hikioto-shi (clear soup), boiled dishes, miso soup, clear soups, etc. The richness of the cuisine should be left to one's discretion. From beginning to end, it should have the character of shippoku cuisine.

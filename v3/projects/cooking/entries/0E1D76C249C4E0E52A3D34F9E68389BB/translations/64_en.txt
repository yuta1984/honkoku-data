Soak hosho paper in water for about three days, pound it well until it becomes soft, mix with kudzu starch and knead with miso soup, cut into appropriate pieces and boil in miso soup. Those who eat this mochi will be protected from illness throughout the year. Use during the summer doyo period.

　　Ise Tofu
Whether using sea bream or pike conger, finely mince only the flesh and grind it, thin it with dashi stock, make it smooth and soft, put it in a bowl and steam. Scoop it up, top with kudzu sauce, and serve with mustard.

　　Sea Bream Rice
Remove the skin from sea bream and cut only the flesh finely. Cook rice with broth made from sea bream bones. Of course, add the fish to the rice. Top with eggs, julienned grated daikon radish and other pickled vegetables, and serve with soup.

　　Deep-fried Burdock
Grate burdock root into rope-like strips, take small portions and deep-fry in oil.

　　Nagasaki Pounded Sea Bream (Nagasaki Uchi-dai)
Fillet a fresh sea bream into three pieces, remove the skin, and slice thinly. Alternatively, remove the red flesh.
Use kudzu starch as dusting powder and pound the fish meat to flatten it. Stretch it freely until it becomes flat,
then cut it like udon noodles. A thin sōmen-like finish is preferable.
If overcooked, the fish will fall apart, so one must be very careful about the cooking time.

Teika Rice
Remove the caps from shiitake mushrooms and mix them with rice to cook. Season with salt as usual.

Seaweed Tofu
Roast green seaweed (aonori) over a distant fire to make powder, and sift it finely. Drain the water from tofu, add wheat flour, gradually mix in the seaweed powder while grinding, boil in hot water, scoop it out, and add to clear soup.

Conger Eel Hanpei
Wash lightly salted conger eel and fillet it like sea eel, then grind it. Add three eggs to one conger eel, grind well to the right consistency, put it in boiling water where it turns white, then quickly fry in oil.

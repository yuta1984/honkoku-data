Red miso 50 monme, cinnamon powder ½ monme, clove powder ½ monme - pound and mix well. Pickle eggplant, ginger and similar vegetables. This depends on preference. When pickling, add a little white sugar, about 5-6 monme, and use on the seventh day.

　　Whale-Style Preparation
Remove scales from sea bass and slice thinly with skin on. Use as a substitute for whale. Good accompaniments include daikon radish, burdock root, taro root, etc.

　　Koromode (Clothing Style)
Simmer frozen tofu in bonito broth seasoned with soy sauce. Top with fluffy beaten egg and sprinkle with pepper.

　　Mock Matsutake (Pine Dew Substitute)
Boil mukago (bulbils) and remove skins, use in clear soup.

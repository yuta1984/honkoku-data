Shell walnuts and soak them in hot water for a while, remove the astringent skin, and grind them well. Drain the water from tofu and grind it together with the walnuts. Press the mixture onto a cedar board to shape it, steam it, then lightly grill and cut. Use as a garnish for sashimi.

Matsutake Quick Sushi (Matsutake Hayazushi)
Season soy sauce to taste and bring to a boil, then add cut matsutake mushrooms and quickly cook through. Add spices and make into quick sushi with rice. Adjust the saltiness with the cooking liquid.

Udon-style Abalone (Udon Awabi)
Remove the ears from abalone and cut very finely like udon noodles. Put in water and cook, then dip in hot water and remove immediately. Do not overcook. Use with udon broth.

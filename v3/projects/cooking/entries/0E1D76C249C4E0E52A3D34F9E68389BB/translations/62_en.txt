Make yudofu (hot tofu in broth) with seasoned hot water as the base flavor, and top with grated yam. For the grated yam, use fine soy sauce with bonito broth, seasoning it sweet and salty before adding. Good toppings include pepper, green nori, or sweet nori.

　　Day-Night Potato
Cut Chinese yam to a thickness of 4-5 bu, with size according to preference. Cut into rectangular pieces, steam, then cut about one-third horizontally and simmer in raw soy sauce. [Picture] Do it like this. Good for sashimi.

　　Green Seaweed Porridge
Add salt to white porridge, cook so it's not too salty, place in bowl and sprinkle green nori powder. It's also good to omit the salt and add soy sauce instead.

　　Three-Cup Pickle
Combine three parts vinegar, two parts sake, and one part soy sauce. Cut only sea bass meat finely, coat with salt and pickle. Sea bass has an oily quality

Mountain Stream Somen
Cut kamaboko into long strips of 2-bu square. Deep-fry in sesame oil. Cut green onions into 5-bu pieces and sauté in the remaining oil, then remove the oil. Add soy sauce to bonito stock and boil somen noodles separately. Add the kamaboko and green onions and simmer together.

　　Spring Colt Tofu
Remove the cloth marks from one block of tofu and cut into four pieces. Simmer in fresh soy sauce, let cool, then deep-fry in oil. Serve with irizake and wasabi.

　　Sugar Burdock Root
Wash Horikawa burdock root and cut into pieces about 2 sun long. Boil thoroughly in hot water. Mix 1 gō of kudzu starch and half a gō of sugar, dissolve with soy sauce, warm, and pour over.

　　Nanking Clams
Simmer shelled clam meat in fresh soy sauce. Put sansho pepper powder in tofu shells, sauté with soy sauce, and add the above-mentioned clams.

　　Black Bean Soup
Soak black beans in water and if planted, they sprout quickly. Take the sprouts
and use them for soup.

　　Shiraae (White Dressed Salad)
Cut fish into large pieces, boil in salted water and remove
moisture. Add miso and tofu to make shiraae, and add sesame seeds.

　　Oil Removal
Deep-fry tofu and immediately put it from the pot into water to remove
the oil. Also boil in water and pour miso over it.

　　Shrunken Abalone
Remove the ear of the abalone, slice it thinly, boil it,
and pour hot water over it.

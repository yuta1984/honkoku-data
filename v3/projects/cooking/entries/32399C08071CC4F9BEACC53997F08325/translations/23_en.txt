○Method for Young Greens Mochi (Wakana Mochi)
Corn flour　　　1 shō
Bracken starch　　2 gō
White sugar　　　10 monme

Knead these three ingredients well together, steam thoroughly, tear into appropriate pieces, and roast green seaweed over fire until fine, sift it, and sprinkle well over the mochi before using.

○Method for Pomegranate Mochi (Zakuro Mochi)
For this, boil dainagon azuki beans well so they don't get crushed and set aside. Also, quickly steam Sendai dried rice, cool it, add 20 monme of sugar to 1 gō of the flour, mix with the azuki beans. Also, knead cold-processed glutinous rice flour firmly with water, use udon flour as dusting flour and flatten, then wrap the above filling. Then wrap each one in cloth, tie the opening, steam and remove the cloth. If you want to add color to this, before steaming, color with crimson or gardenia.

○Method for Japanese Pepper Mochi (Sanshō Mochi)
Glutinous rice flour (cold-processed)　1 gō
Grilled miso　　　　　　　　　　　about the size of a walnut, well grilled
White sugar　　　　　　　　　　　　20 monme
Japanese pepper powder　　　　　　　2 bu

Part Three　　　　　　　　　　　　Fifty-five

Platform Service　Kelp
Burdock clusters
White wisteria

△Kelp burdock clusters are available at kelp shops
△White wisteria is available at confectionery shops

Heavy Service　Bamboo shoots (moso bamboo)
Yellow-white simmering

△For bamboo shoots, remove skin and cut off roots, boil and cut to good size, then deep-fry in sesame oil
△Thin red miso with kelp stock to soften, add the above bamboo shoots and simmer well

Marinated Dish　White seaweed
Mustard soy sauce

△Wash white seaweed in water and cut to good size
△Pour mixture of raw soy sauce with dissolved mustard over it

Clear Soup　Kinshi (gold thread)
Matsutake (pine mushroom)
Arrowhead new bulb grains
With wasabi

△For matsutake, trim both ends slightly, peel thin in rounds, roll back to original shape, chop very finely from the end, simmer just before serving
△Knead arrowhead new bulb powder with water, quickly form into grain-sized balls with grated wasabi inside, boil and simmer

Side Dish　Takuan burdock
Suita arrowhead
Yuzu citrus paste

△Scrape fresh burdock well, blanch quickly, peel in rounds, season only the peel and flatten. Apply wheat flour mixed with water in skin color, then sandwich burdock peel. For commercial purposes, apply wheat flour to multiple layers and sandwich peels, stacking five or six layers. Place on bamboo skin in steamer and steam until done, then slice from the end in layers
△Arrange and wash Suita arrowhead, boil, simmer in sake and soy sauce, serve in sake cups with small spoons for combining. May also be served without cups or spoons
△Slice yuzu citrus paste thinly from the end in layers. Using hanabishi (flower diamond shapes) is also good

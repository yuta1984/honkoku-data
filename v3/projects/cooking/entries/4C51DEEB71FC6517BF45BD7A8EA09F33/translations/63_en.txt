○Poppy Flower Instructions

Poppy Seeds
Make with arrowhead tubers as shown. It is best to grind the green skin of arrowhead tubers with rice powder.

Petal Diagram
Make with chestnuts or arrowhead tubers. Cut extremely thin. Use four petals.

Peel with a rounded back curve.

Attach as shown. Make a long stem with bamboo.
For leaves, if poppy flowers are unavailable, use daikon radish leaves.

For buds, use small arrowhead tubers. Make angular shapes like this, and push small petal-like pieces into the split sections. Also wrap the bamboo stem with zigzag cotton thread.

○Maple Leaf Instructions

Make with daikon radish etc. as shown, cut very thinly, color with red or gardenia juice, pierce these spots with thin wire, and wrap wire onto maple branches with wide zelkova thread. Make branches well. The size and number of leaves depend on the appearance of the branches.

○Bellflower Instructions

Make five petals with chestnuts and soak in water. For the base, use arrowhead tubers, peel the skin from the top as shown in the diagram, leave the skin on the bottom, and make cuts in five places as shown in the diagram.

Insert petals into the right cut sections and secure with bamboo pegs. Also make bud bases with arrowhead tubers, and make buds with chestnuts or arrowhead tubers as shown in the diagram. For leaves, use asters or anything similar. Use bamboo for stems, and zigzag cotton thread for wire.

Diagram of Finished Bellflower

For the flower's fragrance, insert bamboo pegs slightly into the center.

Color bellflowers with red and blue flower dye.

Vinegared Dish　Yellow-white carrot
　　　　　　　　Rock mushroom
　　　　　　　　Lotus taro
Sake cup
Mustard vinegar miso

△For yellow-white carrot, julienne the carrot finely like white hair and deep-fry raw in sesame oil
△For rock mushroom, boil and wash well, season lightly, spread each piece and hold two loosened mushrooms by hand, putting one inside and rolling it up
△For lotus taro, peel and blanch quickly, then chop from the end
○Serve mustard vinegar miso in sake cups

Soup　Red miso soup
　　　Fresh nori seaweed
　　　Julienned ginger

△Make soup with red miso and kelp stock, strain through a miso strainer
△Wash fresh nori in water, cut into tuft-like pieces of good size, soak in warm water and place in bowls
△For julienned ginger, peel ginger and slice thinly, then stack and cut vertically into very fine strips from the end, use as garnish

Soup　Enclosed loquat
　　　Water chestnuts
　　　Pumpkin
Decorative flowers　Plum branches
　　　　　　　　　Camellia
The decorative flowers mentioned above are found at the end of this book

△For enclosed loquat, wash and cut the flesh in continuous pieces of two or three. Use loquat leaves as individual place mats
△For water chestnuts, peel the outer skin and inner skin, cut into flower shapes. Detailed flower cutting methods are given throughout this book, and any ingredient can be crafted this way. Use one whole chestnut per round serving for combination
△For pumpkin, remove the skin, separate one inner chamber, cut the core section vertically, then turn inside out through that cut opening along with the inner flesh

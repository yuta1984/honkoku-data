Nara tea: First roast tea leaves lightly and put in a bag,
　　　brew only the adzuki beans and tea. Then add beans and
　　　rice, half of each portion is good. For beans, split them
　　　and discard the skins. Also good to add sword beans, arrowhead, roasted chestnuts,
　　　etc. There is proper amounts for sansho pepper powder and salt. The cooking time for everything
　　　is crucial.
Goji tea: Pick young goji berries, steam them and place on a hoiro
　　　drying rack. Boiling is also good. When serving, it's good to add
　　　one-third portion of regular tea.
Acanthopanax tea: This is also prepared like goji. Mulberry tea
　　　should also be made in the same way as goji.

　　　△Chapter Twenty: Various Written Records
One-night sushi preparation: Wash sweetfish, make the rice saltier
　　　than usual salt seasoning, stuff into the fish, wrap in grass bundles,
　　　build a fire in the yard and roast together, then wrap the top with straw mat
　　　two or three times, place on top of the fire site, and apply
　　　strong weights. Also good to tie tightly to a pillar
　　　and bind firmly. It becomes ready in one night. Remove the briny fish.
Whale ocean pickling: Boil one sho of salt with one sho of water, let it
　　　cool well, pickle for three days, then remove and wrap in bundles,
　　　can be kept indefinitely. There is also a method to keep it pickled in the barrel
　　　as is. Also pickle in sake lees, add salt and put in a jar.

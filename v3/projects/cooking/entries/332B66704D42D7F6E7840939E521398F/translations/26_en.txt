Grilled bone namasu　Grill and steam thin flesh and bones of sea bream
　　　remove them, add tazukuri (grilled), river shrimp, wood ear mushroom,
　　　chestnuts, grated ginger, etc., season with vinegar and salt
　　　and mix
Wasabi-ae　Take wild goose, duck, or thigh meat, prepare with vinegar,
　　　sprinkle a little salt and stir-fry, discard that vinegar, add flounder,
　　　abalone, sea bream, etc., and mix with wasabi vinegar
　　　Also serve with vinegar added
Kazera-ae　Whether quail or small birds, coat with soy sauce
　　　and grill well, cut finely and mix with
　　　mustard vinegar. Also called aogachi-ae
Mizu-ae　Add vinegar to toasted sake, chopped leeks,
　　　tazukuri, dried squid, roasted powder, small birds (grilled and added), salted salmon, green
　　　nori, myoga buds, wood ear mushroom, burdock root - combine from among these
　　　and mix. Good with chopped sansho leaves added
Mikawa-ae　Chop cucumber with skin, sprinkle a little salt,
　　　knead and quickly rinse and squeeze, add hanakatsuba,
　　　thin mustard miso with toasted sake and vinegar and mix. When it becomes hard,
　　　it can also be made by removing the skin
Ao-ae　Boil roasted powder well, simmer well with dashi and soy sauce,
　　　grind green beans, season with salt and mix

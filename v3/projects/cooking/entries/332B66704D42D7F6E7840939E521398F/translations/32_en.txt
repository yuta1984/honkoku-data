Nobusuma　Pound small birds and grind like senba
Then finely mince sea bream and pour boiling water over it
Set aside; thinly slice large abalone
When this is also blanched, it becomes like a pouch; at this
time season with dashi-soy sauce and when it comes to a boil, add the three ingredients
Mix together and they will be wrapped in the pouch; egg
soboro placed on top is good; various garnishes

Nibitashi　Grill crucian carp plain and simmer
in dashi with soy sauce

Ise Tofu　Grate mountain yam, mince sea bream and grind
Add one-third of the yam to tofu with egg whites
added and grind; grind everything together well, line a cedar
box with cloth, wrap and boil under weight, then cut
Pour kudzu sauce over it and serve; also bird miso, wasabi
miso and such poured over is even better; also with tofu alone
ground well and prepared as above and served

Kudzu Sea Bream　Cut sea bream as for grilled dishes, skewer
boil and pour kudzu sauce over and serve; kudzu sea bream
with kelp is also prepared in the same manner

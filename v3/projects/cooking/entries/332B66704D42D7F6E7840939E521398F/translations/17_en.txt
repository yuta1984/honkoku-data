Tai fugu-style　Make a thick base with medium miso,
　　　add tai and adjust salt, then serve as soup
　　　If it becomes too thick, dilute it several times　However,
　　　it's good to add dried fugu skin　It's also good to grill dried fugu and peel it
Sea bass soup　Make clear soup with kombu dashi　For the top,
　　　use oki-kombu (deep-sea kelp)　It's good to add seaweed or sea squirt innards　
　　　It can also be prepared with light miso
Carp gall soup　First remove the gall, thoroughly
　　　mash the gall and intestines, put in pot and fry until fox-colored, remove dregs,
　　　rinse the pot with sake or dashi and discard, then
　　　add dashi and cook　Fillet the carp into three pieces

　　　cut with scales attached　In summer, adding scales is not good
　　　There are oral traditions　Salt seasoning is crucial　Also, grind the gall
　　　thin with sake, set aside, adjust the bitterness and serve
　　　There is also this method《Gosaden tradition》　For carp miso soup,
　　　prepare it like crucian carp
Crucian carp soup　Use miso from medium to thick consistency, add dashi
　　　Good　Wrap crucian carp with wakame or kajime seaweed
　　　and cook　When sweetness is lacking, add ground bonito
　　　In any case, it's good to make the miso with dashi　Cook well
　　　then add sake and salt, garnish with powdered sansho pepper

Called [tamazusa], stir-fry in soy sauce until done. Additionally, sweet bean paste,
carrot, peach kernels, black beans, chili pepper, ginger, etc.
may also be added. Evening glory seeds are also used.
Butterbur sprouts: Boil and skewer, coat with sansho pepper miso and
grill. Can also be used without boiling.
Cut sansho pepper: Remove the white part from inside sansho pepper,
grind well and add bonito flake miso. The spiciness should
be to a good degree of heat. Minced flounder
cut finely (one bu square), dry well and further
roast in an earthen pan. Add sinkers and soy sauce.
Abalone nama-bi: Slice abalone thinly crosswise, dry in vinegar
and serve with irizake poured over.

Chilled items: Daikon radish, melon, eggplant, lotus root, (black) arrowhead,
apple, peach, plum, apricot, chestnut, pear.
Various other seasonal delicacies are good.
For accompaniments: Sea bream roe and its innards, mackerel back-gut,
pufferfish [fukudame], sardine, sea urchin, sweetfish, its roe, naruko,
duck innards, salmon innards, roe, shizuki, bonito
tataki, skylark, quail. Various others exist.
For vinegar pickles: Myoga ginger, ginger, plum, (mountain yam), bamboo shoots,
saposhnikovia, udo, lotus root, carrot, scallop, perilla,
sansho pepper, silver vine. Various others. However, add three
go of salt to one sho of vinegar.
Abalone innards mix: Boil the innards, grind with grilled miso and ginger.

▲Spotted snipe (beach snipe)
The bird's size is about that of small butterfly snipe, but since there aren't
many, it is rarely used
in cooking. The flavor is not
bad. Fat content is the same as above.

▲Kashiragi snipe (beach snipe)
Serves three people as soup. Also when well-fattened,
even as stir-fried bird it serves
two to three people. It has good flavor.
Also called muneguro (black breast) or daikashira (big head).

▲Majiu snipe (same)
This is generally the same as kashiragi.

▲Akajiu snipe (same)
This serves two to three people when used for soup, and for stir-fried bird
when well-fattened also serves two to three people. Also
for grilled bird it serves up to four people.
However, when well-fattened it is
best for cooking rather than grilled bird.

▲Oguro medium snipe (same)
This bird's size is also the same as akajiu.
It's particularly good and can substitute for hodo snipe.
It's found in both fields and beaches. Primarily it's among field snipe.
Fat is absent in spring, develops well in autumn.

▲Mute duck
This is a domesticated bird. Extremely rarely used for cooking. However, it may occasionally be used when duck is unavailable.
The taste is about the same as duck. The fat content is also similar to flavored duck.

▲Pheasant
The season for use is from September through the New Year. As roasted bird, male birds serve eleven to twelve people. Female birds serve up to ten people.
For soup, male birds serve twelve to thirteen people. When used as stir-fried bird, male birds serve ten people, female birds serve eight to nine people.

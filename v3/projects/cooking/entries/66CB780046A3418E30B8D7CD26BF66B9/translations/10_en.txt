▲Regarding sugi-mori (cedar arrangement)
The three angular arrangements represent sixteen great countries, five hundred middle countries, ten thousand small countries, and countless grain-scattered countries. The namasu should be daikon radish namasu. This represents male; vinegared turnip represents female, with female birds, and should be understood as paired with the birds of the main banquet tray. Winged fish - when a wise king ascends the throne, heavenly beings descend, earthly deities move, and fish in the water rejoice, so we learn. However, the taro represents the nine luminous stars. The food consists of princess rice arranged in cedar style. This is paired with the aforementioned hard rice. Both are

▲Regarding standing pine
Only the main banquet tray and cedar arrangement should have standing pine. The pine of the main banquet tray is male pine. The pine of the cedar arrangement is female pine. This understanding should be maintained. Pine should also be decorated by tying branch threads. The knot is the akemaki knot. This is judged by Hachiman Great Deity. Also, since spring's color is blue, this should be declared as judged by Kasuga Great Bright Deity. Therefore, Hachiman and Kasuga Bright Deity are increasingly present in the colors. Pine represents a thousand-year celebration.

○Same Fish - Tade-su (Water Pepper Vinegar)
The preparation is the same as before. Grill it plain,
grind water pepper and mix with vinegar,
add a little soy sauce and pour
over the fish before serving.

○Same Fish - Glazed Grilling

○Same Fish - Tempura
Remove the head and thoroughly remove
the black innards, wipe away moisture
with a cloth, place in a mortar and
grind well together with the bones until smooth,
then deep-fry in sesame oil.
　Sliced green onions, water dropwort, mitsuba parsley
　Shaved burdock root, mizuna greens
Use any of these as suitable accompaniments.
Serve with ginger or Japanese pepper.

○Same Fish - Ushio-ni (Clear Broth)
As before, thoroughly remove the black innards,
rub with salt and let sit for a while,
then wash with water and pour boiling water
over from above to remove surface oil. Then
prepare like regular ushio-ni and serve.
This makes an excellent clear soup.
If you put in too many pieces, the bowl
looks unsightly, so put in about three pieces.
Choose the garnish appropriately.

○Same Fish - Senba-ni (Senba-style Simmering)
As before, remove the head and thoroughly
remove the black innards, fillet into three pieces, and the tail

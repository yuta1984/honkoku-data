○Ise Lobster Dengaku
Blanch quickly as usual, remove the shell,
cut in half and skewer horizontally
with two skewers, grill well
on both sides, brush with sesame oil
once, then place over
fire again
and char slightly. Tree bud miso,
Japanese pepper miso, chili miso,
butterbur miso—use according to preference.
　To remove the shell from the boiled lobster,
　remove the head, insert a knife
　from both sides of the belly, remove the inner membrane,
　and extract the meat.

○Same,
Wax-grilled Shrimp
First, while raw, prepare as before
to extract the meat, place on a cutting board
and press flat, skewer horizontally
with about two skewers 《sprinkle the meat lightly with salt》, grill well
on both front and back sides, then beat
eggs, add a little wheat flour
and a little soy sauce, whisk well,
brush this mixture on the meat
five or six times while grilling.
Grill over a distant fire.
Use according to preference.

○Oranda Namasu (Dutch-style vinegared salad)

Regardless of fish size, fillet into three
pieces, cut finely
as desired,
skewer four or five pieces each on
skewers and grill in oil like tsuke-yaki,
then split negi onions and quickly
blanch them, thoroughly drain the
moisture and serve in a bowl
as one dish. ■■ should be
served arranged on a small salt dish. Either
way, pool sanpai-su (three-cup vinegar) and place
finely chopped togarashi (chili pepper) on top.
It pairs extremely well with sake
and is a delicious appetizer.

　　○Ankake (Thick sauce dish)

This too, like before, regardless of fish size,
fillet into three pieces,
scrape away the belly bones, whether
cut lengthwise or diagonally,
prepare as desired
　　Matsutake mushrooms
　　Turnips
　　Daikon radish
Take one ingredient from the above and combine,
steam as one dish
　　《Bowl steaming is especially good》
Pour kuzu starch sauce over
the top before serving
　　Ginger　Wasabi
　　Pepper
Place these types of condiments on top

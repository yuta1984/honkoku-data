○Same, Salt-cured Oysters
Wash well and place in a bowl, etc.
Sprinkle plenty of salt on top,
mix together, place a light weight
on top and let sit for a while, then remove.
Wash well and wipe away
moisture. Serve with sake-salt sauce
topped generously with bonito flakes.

○Same, with Miso Topping
Wash well, place in a bowl, etc., and
steam. May also be boiled in hot water
and served.
　Ginger miso　Wasabi miso
　Butterbur miso　Red pepper miso
Any of these misos may be used according
to preference. May be spread underneath or poured
on top. Serve in covered dishes.

○Same, Vinegar-pressed
Like vinegar-cured sardines, first
apply a little salt, then as is,
squeeze out moisture, place in a vessel, soak in vinegar
and place a weight on top. Do this
four or five times, changing the vinegar.
When serving, change the vinegar,
squeeze out excess, top generously with bonito flakes
and serve.
　Daikon radish　Tree buds (kinome)
etc. may be pressed together with the oysters.

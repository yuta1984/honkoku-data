○Same / Moromi (fermented soy sauce paste) topping
Chop thinly as before, blanch in hot water, transfer to a bowl, grind moromi and pour over. Wood ear mushrooms and daikon radish varieties should be combined.

○Same / Nanba vinegar
This too, after blanching as before, combine with wood ear mushrooms, daikon radish, udo, rock fungus, mitsuba (trefoil), etc. Nanba vinegar should be prepared by storing sanbaizu (three-flavor vinegar) and serving. It is an extremely elegant dish.

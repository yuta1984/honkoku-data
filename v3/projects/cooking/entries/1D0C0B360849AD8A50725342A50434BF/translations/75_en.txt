○Same / Dengaku (grilled tofu/fish with miso)
Cut into small cubes, skewer on small skewers, brush once with sesame oil, grill well from both front and back sides, and make into dengaku with sansho pepper miso and chili pepper miso.

○Squid Dengaku
Wash well with water as usual, cut lengthwise in half, make cuts on both front and back sides, skewer horizontally, grill well, brush once with soy sauce, then make into dengaku with sansho pepper miso, chili pepper miso, or tree bud miso. Alternatively, cutting into rectangular sticks and grilling on skewers is also good.

○Same / Tsuke-yaki (marinated grilling)
Same preparation as before. Sansho pepper soy sauce, chili pepper, black pepper, or tree buds - any of these will do.

○Same / Maki-ika (rolled squid)
Wash well as before, make lengthwise cuts, roll from the edge and tie with straw, mix sake and soy sauce in equal parts, when it's well boiled, add the squid, simmer until colored, remove the straw, and serve cut into rounds.

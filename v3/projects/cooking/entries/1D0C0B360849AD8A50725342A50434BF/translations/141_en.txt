○Miscellaneous Fish Preparation

Sprinkle a little salt and let it sit, then prepare
mustard and chili pepper
vinegar-miso sauce with:
　　Shredded daikon radish
　　Young myoga ginger buds
　　Cucumber
　　White melon
Using any one of the above
ingredients as garnish, make it into nuta
(miso-vinegar dressed dish) and serve. Note that
for nuta dressing, the miso should be
made slightly thinner than for teppō dressing.
　　○Same fish - Teppō-ae (Spicy miso dressing)
As before, regardless of fish size,
fillet into three pieces,
remove belly bones,
cut both fillets diagonally against the grain,
sprinkle with a little salt and set aside, then
prepare chili pepper miso with:
　　Wood ear mushrooms
　　Daikon radish
　　○Shred the daikon like
　　　burdock root, salt it and knead,
　　　wash with water to remove
　　　salt well and drain the water
Mix with the above ingredients and serve.
Daikon alone is also good.

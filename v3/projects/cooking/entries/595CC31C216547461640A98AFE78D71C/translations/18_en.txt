Bonito　　　This should also be prepared like the above sake dish, and
　　　　　　when serving, it's good to add squeezed daikon radish juice.

Horse Mackerel　Take the backbone out of fresh evening horse mackerel,
　　　　　　　　pull out the intestines through the mouth, wash well,
　　　　　　　　sprinkle with salt and set aside. Add a little soy sauce
　　　　　　　　and grilled salt to the dashi, adjust seasoning, bring to
　　　　　　　　a boil, add the fish and cook before serving.

Clams　　　　This is the method for celebratory occasions. Fill a pot
　　　　　　　seven parts with water, add about ten medium clams,
　　　　　　　first boil them, then scoop up both shells and meat.

Separately, steam clams of uniform size with sake, remove the meat,
wash the shells well, put one piece of meat in each shell,
arrange in bowls, pour the aforementioned broth over them
and serve. Use about two clams per bowl.

Vegetarian Salt Broth　Simmer good kelp with salt in water as is,
　　　　　　　　　　　add a little soy sauce and clear sake for seasoning.
　　　　　　　　　　　Flying fish roe, cockscomb seaweed, sea pine tufts,
　　　　　　　　　　　agar grass, baked sweet potato, torn matsutake
　　　　　　　　　　　mushrooms are good. Wasabi juice with eggplant
　　　　　　　　　　　is also suitable.

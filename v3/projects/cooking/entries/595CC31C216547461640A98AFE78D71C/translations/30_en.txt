About Dobu　　　Make doburoku (unrefined sake) with finest sake lees and water, strain through a grinding cloth,
　　　　　　　and filter before use. Cloudy sake is not good.
　　　　　　　　However, when dobu is not available, using mirin sake is also good.

　　　Miscellaneous Items Section

Fluffy Eggs　　　Beat any number of eggs and mix,
　　Seasoning　　　add soy sauce from triple-colored stock in proportion to the volume of eggs.

Rolled Squid　　Wash dried squid, sprinkle a little kudzu powder and roll,
　　　　　　　tie with straw and boil. Adding bracken shoots is also good.

Nabiki　　　　　For bonito, Spanish mackerel and similar fish, when in good condition, remove the flesh,
　　　　　　　sandwich between cedar chopsticks, hang and boil, then grill and cut,
　　　　　　　salt and serve.
　　　　　　　For abalone, thinly slice, simmer,
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　dry and grill.

Shaken Sea Cucumber　Remove the gut and sinews from sea cucumber, cut into pieces about 3 sun long,
　　　　　　　place in a bamboo basket, sprinkle heavily with salt and rub in, cover with a tray
　　　　　　　and shake vigorously until the slime comes off and the sea cucumber
　　　　　　　firms up, becomes round and turns pale green,
　　　　　　　then boil and rinse well, serve fox-colored with vinegar mixture and grated
　　　　　　　ginger. This is excellent. Adding shellfish is recommended.

Fukume Style　　Grill dried sea bream, pound on a board and shred, then grind in a
　　　　　　　mortar. The same applies to barracuda, shark, salt-cured fish, or
　　　　　　　whiting, shrimp, etc.

Nanban Cuisine　　Pluck the feathers from chicken, remove head and feet, discard innards and wash well,
　　　　　　　put in pot with daikon cut into thick rounds, add just enough water to cover,
　　　　　　　boil until the daikon becomes very soft,
　　　　　　　remove and pound well with bones, form into balls and add to the cooking
　　　　　　　broth with kage dropped in, also boil daikon and season,
　　　　　　　add sake and salt, then add the chicken.

○Question: How can one prevent blood from pooling on the plate when making bonito sashimi?

△Answer: First, remove the fish's head, also cut away the belly and wash well,
tie a string to the tail and hang it for a while, then make it into sashimi when serving.
　　However, while you should not use water on the knife for this as well,
　　bonito is extremely difficult to prepare, so everyone uses water
　　while making it, which causes blood to pool. For beginners,
　　it cannot be made without moisture. In such cases:

Spread white sugar on the plate and arrange the preparation on top.

○Question: Castella cakes and such do not rise properly - how can one make them rise?

△Answer: Not just castella, but whenever you want any baked goods to
rise, add lye to the batter and the rising will be quite good.
The same applies to steamed dishes.
　　However, regarding lye, it has already been recorded
　　in the steamed dishes section of Hanafune-shū, so refer to that.

Rokujo Tofu　　Cut tofu into good-sized pieces, put in water with salty mustard
　　　　　　　　until it becomes quite sour, salt the skewers and pierce, then
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　dry in the sun

Frozen Tofu　　　Cut tofu into good-sized pieces, put in salted water
　　　　　　　　　leave for one or two nights indoors until it freezes
　　　　　　　　　　However, pouring hot water over it raw is also good

Frozen Konjac　　Wash konjac well, preparation is same as tofu
　　　　　　　　　　but takes more days than tofu

Yuzu Mochi　　　5 go of rice flour, remove peel and seeds from large yuzu and pound well
　　　　　　　grind well, add appropriate amount of miso and white sugar, mix well by grinding
　　　　　　　strain through a hair sieve, add 5 cups of aged sake and rice flour
　　　　　　　knead well together, put in steamer and steam, then dry in sun
　　　　　　　However, using domyoji rice flour instead of regular rice flour is also good
　　　　　　　Also good to remove the white part of yuzu peel, slice thin, stuff and steam
　　　　　　　Also good to wrap in mandarin peel, or to make without adding miso
　　　　　　　　　　Also may add powdered chili pepper
　　　　　　　or ground walnuts

Yuzu Paste　　　Use only yuzu peel, scrape off the white part and chop very finely
　　　　　　　put in mortar and grind well, add white sugar and a little mirin
　　　　　　　sake, take whole yuzu fruit and carefully remove white pith
　　　　　　　make light knife cuts from outside and remove seeds
　　　　　　　put whole into mirin sake with kage and simmer well, then grind whole
　　　　　　　and mix for use, or tear into individual segments
　　　　　　　and prepare as above, or discard peel, membrane and seeds
　　　　　　　and knead only the flesh

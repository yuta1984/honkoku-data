Udon　　　　　　For finest udon flour, no matter how much you make, adjust the salt content
　　　　　　　　In summer, add three shō of water to one shō of salt, in winter use less, and with that
　　　　　　　　salt water, knead appropriately
　　　　　　　　Wrap in fresh straw mats and knead well until it becomes
　　　　　　　　beautifully glossy, form into balls, place damp cloth in a chest,
　　　　　　　　lay it over and take out when ready to roll
　　　　　　　　If it dries out, cracks will appear when forming balls. Prevent it from drying

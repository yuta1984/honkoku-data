　　　Preparation of An (Thickened Sauces)

Superior An　　　Soak kudzu called "Kyusuke" in plenty of water and set aside to cool,
　　　　　　　add soy sauce in proper proportion and bring to boil, pour off
　　　　　　　the clear liquid from the kudzu and discard, dissolve it, strain through silk,
　　　　　　　add and stir gradually until a proper silver color appears, then immerse
　　　　　　　the pot in boiling water to thicken. However, use equal parts
　　　　　　　bonito flakes and soy sauce. Clay pots work best

Secondary An　　Dissolve regular kudzu well, use only sake and soy sauce
　　　　　　　with more soy sauce and simmer while stirring

White An　　　　Boil Chinese yam, peel the skin, strain through the back of a fine sieve
　　　　　　　add white sugar, dissolve kudzu well with a little soy sauce
　　　　　　　and plenty of bonito flakes, while stirring the kudzu
　　　　　　　mix in the strained Chinese yam

Warabi An　　　For both colors, use the same proportions as secondary an
Tokoro An　　　Stirring method is also the same

Katakuri An　　Use the same stirring method as superior an but with
　　　　　　　less soy sauce and add white sugar

《Side note: Quick Guide | Volume 4》《Title: Collection of Culinary Discussions》
　　　General Principles
1. Generally speaking, regarding menu composition, preparation methods, and cooking techniques, many have been recorded from the first volume through the third volume, so there should be nothing lacking. What is recorded in this volume begins with the proper proportions of dashi (soup stock), and teaches in detail about miso soup (superior and medium grades), light and rich flavors of soy sauce, and other simmered dishes and seasoned foods, classifying how these vary according to their types.

1. The proper proportions for vinegared dishes, irizake (seasoned cooking wine), and dressed dishes are the same as above.

1. The proper proportions for grilled dishes and molded dishes are the same as above.

1. For dishes that have been called by traditional names since ancient times, their varieties and preparation methods are recorded in detail.

1. Illustrations of cooking utensils and vessels necessary for cooking are provided and recorded on the left.

Place about one sun (3cm) of ground rice bran in a pot, place the sea cucumber on top, and spread about two sun (6cm) more of bran on top of that. Do not add water and simmer well. Then remove and extract the intestines, and use for any kind of cooking.

○Semi-dried Simmered Sea Cucumber
One: Remove the sea cucumber's intestines well, boil in plain hot water until it bubbles twice, add plenty of sake, and boil again until it bubbles twice. Then skewer and dry in the sun.

○Semi-dried Fish
One: Whether sea bream, horse mackerel, or any other fish, fillet into three pieces, remove scales, mix salt in water saltier than regular salt water, wash once with this, then dry in the sun.

○Method for Removing Salt from All Fish

【Left Page】
One: Whether sea bream, salmon, or any other fish, fillet into three pieces, wrap in paper, moisten the top with water, and bury overnight in damp soil in the shade. It will become about lightly salted.

○Method for Softening All Dried Fish
One: Whether dried sea bream, dried cod, or other dried fish, soak in water, frequently changing the water, leave for about three days then remove - good for grilling. If too much salt has been removed, grill after adding salt to sake or applying soy sauce. This makes them tender. Also, the softened dried fish can be removed from water, moisture thoroughly removed, and soaked in sake lees.

△Types of Rice Cakes
　　　○Method for Cold-Soaked Rice Cakes
1. Soak the finest white glutinous rice in water for thirty days, changing the water once every day and night. After thirty days have passed, remove and dry well in the sun, then store in paper bags. When making rice cakes, moisten the glutinous rice with water, pound in a mortar, sift well through silk, and knead with very hot water. At first it is too hot to put your hands in, so stir with chopsticks, and after it cools a little, knead well with your hands until it becomes like pounded rice cake. Tear into appropriate pieces, boil water in a pot, put them in and cook until they float to the surface. Then remove and coat with soybean powder or similar...

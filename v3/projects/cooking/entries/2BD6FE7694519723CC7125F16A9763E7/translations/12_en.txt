　The method for making this is like ordinary morohaku, adding lycium bark
　1 kan 500 monme and aralia bark 1 kan monme to the above.
　The preparation method for these medicinal ingredients is to soak overnight in hot water,
　wash them quite well, and rinse with ordinary water.
　Afterwards, chop them like medicinal decoctions, dry them well, and roast
　without burning. When making wine with these medicinal ingredients,
　chop them and put in hot water, squeeze as is, and mix into the sake meal.
　The water temperature should be pleasant to drink normally.
　Hot water from rice is bad.

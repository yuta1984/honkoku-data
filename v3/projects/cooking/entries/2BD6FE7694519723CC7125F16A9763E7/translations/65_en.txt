Standing the bamboo shoots upright to fill the kettle's opening is even better. When well steamed, take them out and press them flat on a board, then prop them upright to let the moisture drip off well. They will not spoil for over twenty days. 　　　○Method for Simmered Sansho Pepper
One: Sansho peppercorns, one sho; One: Miso, five go (ground)
One: Soy sauce, one sho

Simmer these three ingredients in a pot, then roast them in a parching pan and store in a sake bottle.

　　　○Preparation of Irori (Seasoning Paste)
One: Horon miso, one sho; One: Kelp, two sheets
One: Miso, one go; One: Skewered persimmons, ten pieces
One: Soy sauce (a little for flavoring)

Simmer these five ingredients, strain them, and discard the dregs.

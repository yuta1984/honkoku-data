○Method for Yam Fritters (Joyosen)
One: Peel mountain yams, grate them finely with a wasabi grater, mix with one-third white sugar, break into appropriate pieces with a tea spoon, and fry in oil. Sprinkling roasted salt on top is good.

○Shishi-rato (Lion Sweets)
One: White sesame - 1 shō (soak in water overnight, rub to remove outer skin)
One: White sugar - 3 gō, One: White honey - 2 gō 5 shaku
Mix the above two ingredients together, knead until it reaches the hardness of large candy

【Left Page】
Knead thoroughly to this consistency. Then place on a board and press flat with a pot lid from above. Cut into pieces as desired.

○Hiryōsu (Deep-fried Tofu Balls)
One: Wheat flour or rice flour will do. Put water in a pot, add wheat gluten flour and let it swell, then mix with hot water from below, add egg yolks, grind in a mortar, and fry in oil.
However, you can also use 7 gō of non-glutinous rice flour and 3 gō of mochi flour, finely pounded. Add about 7 eggs to 1 shō of the above flour, soak in boiled sugar syrup, then serve. The method for preparing the above sugar syrup is to add 1 shō of water to 1 kin of rock sugar and boil down to seven-tenths.
When frying in oil, scoop with a large spoon.

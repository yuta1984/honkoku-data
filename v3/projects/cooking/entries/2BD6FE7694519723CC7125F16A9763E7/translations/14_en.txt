　　　○Quick Irizake Method
1. Aged sake - 4 cups　1. Soy sauce - 1 cup　1. Vinegar - half cup
　Combine the above three ingredients and bring to a boil once over charcoal fire,
　then remove from heat and stir with chopsticks until it cools
　to body temperature, then heat again to boiling.
　Cool in this manner. By boiling three times like this,
　it becomes irizake.

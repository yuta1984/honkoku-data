　　　　○Mountain Yam Powder
1. Boil wild yam (mountain yam) well, dry it, and strain it through the sediment of rice-washing water.
　　　　○White Azuki Bean Powder
1. Split azuki beans roughly, soak in water overnight, peel off the red skin, then remove the skin well with chopsticks, steam in a bamboo steamer, dry thoroughly, and grind in a mortar.
　　　　○Soybean Powder
1. Quickly roast green soybeans until about one-third of the skins split, taste to check, and when the raw smell is gone, make into powder - this is good.

　　　○Method for Medicinal Kōsen
1. Black soybeans (1 monme, roasted)　1. Glutinous rice 1 monme, roasted
1. Black sesame 3 bu　1. Sansho 6 grains
1. Roasted salt 1 bu
　Make all of the above into powder, sift finely through silk, mix into hot water and drink. This regulates the spleen and stomach, removes phlegm, and treats cough.

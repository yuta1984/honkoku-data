　　　○Sea Bream Sushi
One: Combine two shō of salt and one shō of water, making three shō total, boil down to two shō, and cool well.
One: Fillet the sea bream into three pieces, put it in the salt water mentioned above, and place
　a press on it. After two days have passed, on the third day cook one shō of black rice into rice,
　then mix with one shō of sake lees, and pickle the sea bream mentioned above so that the pieces don't
　rub against each other. However, for salt-cured salmon as well, salt it well, press it, and pickle
　as described above.

○How to Keep Summer Rice from Spoiling
One: During extremely hot summer weather, rice doesn't last even one day. Either it develops an odor or becomes sticky, causing problems during excursions, sightseeing, or travel. In such cases:

One: Cook premium white rice slightly harder than regular rice. When it's well-cooked, remove from heat and while still warm and not yet cooled, transfer to containers, pack well and press down, then cover with a lid so the steam doesn't escape. No matter how hot the weather, it will keep for two days. The secret is to transfer to containers immediately after cooking and removing from heat, then press down and store.

○Method for Castella Bōro (Sponge Cake Cookies)
One: White sugar - 160 monme, One: Eggs - 16
One: Wheat flour - 1 shō
Mix the above well in a bowl. Line a confectionery pot with oil paper cut round to fit the pot, and put the above mixture on top. Keep the fire below quite low, and make the fire above strong to bake. It's fine not to line with paper, but we do so because it won't come out easily otherwise.

○Method for Syrup Candy
One: Barley - 1 shō. Wash the above and place in a bamboo strainer, leave for about two days, then wash again with water and set aside. Do the same many times until white roots appear and it becomes mushy. Wash and loosen it once daily, and when sprouts about 2 sun long appear, wash well, remove all moisture, and pound roughly in a mortar.

One: Fine white glutinous rice - 3 shō
Soak the above in water and steam like ordinary hard-cooked rice. Then while still hot, add the above malt, add hot water until just covered, put in a cotton bag and squeeze, then strain through a water cloth and put in a pot to boil.

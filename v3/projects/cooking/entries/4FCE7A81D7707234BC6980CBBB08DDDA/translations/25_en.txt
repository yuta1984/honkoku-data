　　　　　About Tori Miso (Chicken Miso Paste)
One: Make chicken into a paste, cook to about the saltiness
　of pickled food and let cool, add a little miso and cod,
　grind together and simmer with dashi stock, and if the ground mixture
　is too thick, thin it out with dashi stock
　　　　　About Furofuki Daikon (Simmered Daikon with Miso)

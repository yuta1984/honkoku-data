　　　　　Yuzu Miso
One: red miso 690 me　One: dried rice 200 me
One: refined white sugar 200 me　One: black pepper (sesame) 1 cup
　　　However, walnuts, kaya nuts, ginkgo nuts and similar items
　　　may be added according to preference

　　　　　Rice Cake Confection
One: knead chicken eggs with rice cake flour 1 sho, rice flour 2 cups in boiling water. For the filling, use chopped walnuts with white miso

【Left Page】
　Add a little red miso. Additionally, adding sweet red bean paste, cinnamon, and cloves is according to preference. Coloring is oral tradition.

　　　　　Chinese Kinchin
One: rice cake flour 1 sho, corn flour 5 cups. Mix the above together, knead with boiling water and wrap sweet red bean paste inside. Then boil again in boiling water, and use sweet potato crumbles and chestnut crumbles.
　Oral tradition

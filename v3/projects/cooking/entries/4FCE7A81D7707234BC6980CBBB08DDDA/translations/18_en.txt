　　　　　Steamed Flatfish
One: Soak in water beforehand, when ready to eat add salt, flatfish should be
　well browned, remove backbone and intestines, marinate for six hours, when oil
　emerges from around the fins, arrange in box, stuff into
　the body cavity and steam

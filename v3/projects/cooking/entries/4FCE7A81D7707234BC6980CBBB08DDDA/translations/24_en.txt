　　　　　About Niyose Imo (Jellied Potato Dish)
One: Season the sauce skillfully, add potato
　crumbles, add a little agar, or kuzu starch

　　　　　About Jōyo-men (Yam Noodles)

【Left Page】
One: Grate yam, knead with wheat flour and roll out,
　make like sōmen noodles, use in chawanmushi,
　clear soups, etc.
　　　　　About Tarako Yose (Cod Roe Terrine)
One: Grind cod roe, add eggs - about six eggs
　per cup of cod roe, grind well and put in paper bag,
　quickly tie both ends and steam
　Use for flat dishes, jar dishes, etc.

　　　　　Regarding mock cod (tara-modoki)
1. For mock cod, cut into large pieces, lay Matsumae kelp in the pot, arrange the fish on top, and place kelp over it again. Add about two sho (3.6 liters) of second-grade dashi broth, three to four go (540-720ml) of miso, and season with salt and soy sauce. Put the dashi ingredients in a bag to extract the broth. Sometimes add dried cod heads and such to make the broth.

　　　　Uiro roll

【Left Page】
　Add about half a ladle and approximately seven ladles of soy sauce using a ten-shaku ladle. If prepared with dashi water, tamari soy sauce becomes exceptionally flavorful. Plain water dashi is also acceptable.

　　　　　Regarding shell-grilled eggs
1. For shell-grilled eggs, add about one ladleful of water to ten chicken eggs. It should be prepared slightly firmer than fluffy.

(31) Daikon Winter-Spring Sauce - Preparation Method
《This is a specialty of Sagami Province, prepared on the night of December 30th as a sacred ritual dish for the seaweed cutting ceremony. The origin of this tradition is recorded in a later volume》

First, thoroughly re-boil dried daikon from Owari Province and squeeze out the water. Also, briefly blanch cut arame seaweed, wash with water, season lightly with soy sauce, and cool well. Mix thoroughly with ground sansho miso and serve.

(21) One Type of Daikon Hand-Picked Clear Soup
Cut daikon into 2-3 sun pieces, peel quite thinly, roll tightly and cut thinly crosswise. Put in water, stretch out about thirty strands, tie them together and cut the ends. Boil until well cooked, then put in water to cool. Now make red miso or jinko miso into a thin broth and bring to a boil again. Put dried daikon leaves over distant fire, sieve them, and add this powder little by little as a garnish. However, if it is yuzu season, squeeze in yuzu vinegar before serving. Of course, include one tied daikon bundle. Thin ones are better for this.

(22) Tango Specialty: Millet-Steamed Daikon Method
《This dish is served during Shinto rituals in a place called Yosa in Tango Province》
When cooking millet rice, cut daikon into rings of 5-6 bu thickness and place on top. Since this place is by the seaside, there are various kinds of fish. Cook any fish in salt water without adding soy sauce. Pour the fish cooking liquid over it and eat. The flavor is exceptional. During ritual occasions

【Left page】
　　　　　With edamame
　　　　　　How to make soy sauce
1　Soybeans　　　　　1 to, boiled to the degree of miso beans
1　White barley　　　1 to, this is also roasted before use
　　Combine the above two beans, lay out about three rice straw mats
　　Spread leaves on top - whether they be vine leaves, hackberry leaves,
　　or sedge grass - and let ferment for about three days.
　　After three days pass, turn it upside down, then
　　let it ferment for another three to four days. Afterwards,
　　take it out and dry well in the sun for two to three days.

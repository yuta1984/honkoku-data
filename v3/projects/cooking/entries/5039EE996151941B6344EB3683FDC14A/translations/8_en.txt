　　　　　　Pickled vegetables and fragrant pickles
1　Wash round melons well with water, split in two,
　　remove the contents well, wash with water and let drip,
　　apply salt generously to the melon, pile
　　sake lees on top, place sake lees at the bottom of the tub,
　　arrange the melons face up so they don't rub against each other and
　　pickle them
　　　　　　How to make yume-isen
1　Dissolve kudzu in water, put it in a red copper pot and
　　cook, then heat water in a cooking pot, warm the red copper

　　　　　　How to make sweet bean paste mochi　　
1　Japonica rice 7 gō　　　1　Glutinous rice 3 gō
　　Refine both to white rice, grind finely, sift and
　　knead. Boil very well, drain the liquid,
　　pound well in a mortar, then form into mochi and add sweet bean paste

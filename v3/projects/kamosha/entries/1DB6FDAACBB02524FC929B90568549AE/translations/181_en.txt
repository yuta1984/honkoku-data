Summer of Bunsei 4 (1821), Year of the Metal Snake
Senior Fourth Rank, Acting Governor of Hizen Province, Tsunekazu

Bunsei 6 (1823)
[Regarding the postponement of the horse racing ceremony due to ritual defilement] 【Written in red ink】
On the 3rd day of the 4th month, the new Empress Kamon'in passed away (daughter of Takatsukasa Quasi-Empress Masahiro)
Due to this, from the 11th of the same month, the horse racing ceremony was in a state of ritual defilement, and the ceremony date was postponed to be held on the 21st and 25th days of the 6th month. Regarding the irregular conditions, there were communications from the shrine community to the young officials, recorded separately.

6th month, 21st day - Ashiarai (horse selection)
Number 10: Funaki-no-shō　　　Shimojimo Iwami-no-suke Yasumichi
During the preliminary run, he fell from his horse in front of the main grandstand. When entering the direct shift, his representative was Shika-no-suke Dayū Hakuho

Number 4: Shōshuku　　　Middle rank: Kii-no-suke Hōken

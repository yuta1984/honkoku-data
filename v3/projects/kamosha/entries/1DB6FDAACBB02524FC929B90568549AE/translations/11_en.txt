           Right - Jibu-shōyū Kiesada

One source says: There was a dispute between Kaneken and Kiesada regarding horses, and all twenty horses were returned. However, horses were borrowed from the spectators to make arrangements.

Same 3rd year
Shrine administrator Matsushita Chikahisa (Nori-nari), Acting Shoshi-dayū Ōmi-no-kami Hyōbu-shōyū, Takahira's deputy Acting Iga-no-kami Bizen-no-kami. Both the 1st and 5th days
《Marginal note: On the 5th day, Shoshi-dai Takahira attended the morning meal at the head priest's residence wearing hakama without proper shoulder dress》【Red ink】
The weather was good. On the 5th day, at the shrine administrator's morning meal, the shoshi attended wearing hakama without proper shoulder dress as a dining companion. This was questionable. For seating: Shoshi Ōmi-no-kami, first and second Bettō-dayū, third deputy Bettō-dayū were seated together. This was questionable.

Kan'ei 9th year (1632)
　　《Marginal note: My opinion》
　　Horse racing proceeded as in previous years

《Marginal note: The matter of Taitokuin-den's passing on the 24th day of the 1st month》【Red ink】
　　According to the Shigemura Record, on the 24th day of the 1st month of Kan'ei 9th year, the Great Retired Shogun of Edo passed away. This was Taitokuin-den (Lord Hidetada). For this condolence, Sekime Hayato-no-sho Suehisa went down to Edo as representative.

　　《Marginal note: My opinion》Suehisa was the administrative officer (zassho) at that time

《Marginal note: The first match left side fell from horse slightly below the temporary shrine, the right side dismounted from horse, but there was no re-entry》【Red ink】
　　First match left side, Higo-no-kami Hisanao, fell from his horse slightly below the temporary shrine. The right side dismounted from his horse. He mounted his horse as in every year. However, there is also the opinion whether there should be another competition, or when mounting for competition, whether it would be better for the right side's horse not to gallop but to withdraw immediately.

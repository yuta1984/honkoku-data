First day
　　Nine　Funaki-shō《Marginal note: Wakamiya celebrant Tsunesue》Junior Shinano-no-suke Noriaki
　　　　　　　　　　　Substitute Saemon-dayū Yoshiyuki

1. When Yoshiyuki, substitute for Funaki-shō rider Noriaki, was in the ninth turn, he fell from his horse in front of the general viewing stand
《Marginal note: Substitute was requested due to injury during the turn》【Red ink】
　　and sustained injuries, making it difficult to continue riding. Therefore, he came to the administrative building via the Kyōsho road to request a substitute. Assistant head priest and rider Kiyonaga served as substitute, rode the horse, dismounted within the barrier area, re-entered directly, and completed the turn. This matter was reported to the exit supervisor through someone who had been requested beforehand.

《Marginal note: Head priest (Torii Ōji) Ōhira taking seat in the office building》【Red ink notation】
First, head priest Ōhira took his seat in the office building, and the sacred food banquet was held together with the lower courtiers in one place.
　　《Marginal note: Personal note》Head priest and the three lower courtiers all with ceremonial stands

　In Tenna 2nd year (Mizunoe-Inu), 5th month, 5th day horse racing, there was a settled dispute regarding the competition between head priest Yasuyoshi and the lower courtiers. From this year onwards, the head priest no longer took seat in the office building and withdrew directly. This year, Ōhira took seat in the office.

《Marginal note: Matter of whether lower courtiers should wear swords during the sacred food banquet in the office building》【Red ink notation】
First, when Arinao, Kiyotatsu and others were at the sacred food banquet in the office building, Yasukaku said to Kiyotatsu "At this time, one should not wear a sword," so Kiyotatsu likewise removed his sword. Kiyoshige said "To change by personal opinion what has been practiced since ancient times - wearing swords - is extremely discourteous. It is a position that should bear swords. Even if it is a sacred food banquet, should one remove the sword?

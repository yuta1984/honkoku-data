The year representatives were both Aka-dayū and Sakon-shōgen.

On both the 1st and 5th days, His Lordship visited. Details omitted. There was one special horse race, with no clear winner or loser.

《Marginal note: Regarding sleeve money》【Red ink】
From this year, the sleeve money was fixed at 1 kan 500 mon. Bitchū-no-kami (sixth race right, Suetsugu) forgot his sword and put it on at the stable. First ceremonial race.

《Marginal note: Forgetting the sword and putting it on at the stable》【Red ink】
The mounted horse was changed and officials rode out. For mounting, Jibu-dayū (Tomohisa) rode on the left of the first race, and Settsu-no-kami (Yoshihiro) rode in the second race. The first rider's opponent struck directly. The match result was favorable.
　　　Left　Settsu-no-kami Yoshihiro
　Second race
　　　Right　First, Chichiyo-dayū Mochihisa

Eiroku 5th year
Lord Sasaki Shirō and Lord Jirō came to observe. Sake was offered from the entire group. Two horses were substituted. In the first race, neither right nor left riders mounted. They were the main messengers. Sleeve money was 1 kan 500 mon. All ten races rode as main messengers on the 1st day.

《Marginal note: Ninth race left, Kaneken leading and riding the tenth race horse》【Red ink】
The horse that was to stand for the tenth messenger was led up and ridden by Wakasa-no-kami Kaneken in the ninth race. The right Tsushima-no-kami's horse was made to stand. There was something to report about this, but nevertheless there were no problems.
　　　Left　Wakasa-no-kami Kaneken (Jō also)
　Ninth race
　　　Right　Tsushima-no-kami Suetsugu lost

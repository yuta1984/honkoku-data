Completed courtesy visits.

7th Day, Hinoto-Hitsuji, Rain: Was invited by the Third Headman Shiken, sent twenty eggs in advance.
○ The Young Lord's White Horse Festival, External Controller's ceremonial participation, one round of sake and early departure.

12th Day, Mizunoe-Ne, Clear: The beginning of memorial services as in previous years. Ancestral founder received the court rank of Senior Fourth Rank Upper cloud cloud, at forty-six years of age.

14th Day, Kinoe-Tora, Clear: This year's ceremonial conclusion and withdrawal procedures as last year. Therefore abbreviated here.

15th Day, Kinoto-U, Cloudy: Around half past one o'clock, hail fell. ○ Received sake cups from the lord's family as customary. ○ Yamaguchi Nakatsukasa-no-jō and Murata Shikibu-no-jō were newly appointed to chamberlain positions this year, and will henceforth receive seven koku of rice each, as announced this morning. The chief retainer Shimohashi Saemon received five to of rice as head retainer's stipend.
○ Though invited by Third Headman Shiken, could not attend due to full attendance required at the lord's residence. ○ The new headmen invited me to tomorrow's sixteenth day gathering, so sent gifts today as follows:
To New First Headman Yasufumi: Initial offering rice 2 shō, 15 eggs, 30 small candles
To New Second Headman Kiyotsugu: Same rice 2 shō, fish money 1 monme silver, 2 fans
To New Third Headman Hisaaki: Same rice 2 shō, sake 2 shō, 30 small candles

16th Day, Hinoe-Tatsu, Clear: Tsunenobu and Tsunesato visited New Headman Yasufumi's residence, my father visited New Second Headman Kiyotsugu's residence.
Yasuko, Chiko, Tsunenori, Tsunehiro and others visited New Third Headman Hisaaki's residence.

18th Day, Tsuchinoe-Uma, Clear: Attended the beginning of the Western District gathering, announcements as in previous years. The ceremony heads were Takayasu and Kiyoyoshi.

20th Day, Kanoe-Saru, Clear: The Fifth Group's soup gathering was held at Hiraki's residence, Fun'kei attended but I did not. However, Senzō's petition to retire from the repair magistrate position was approved, and Ujihiko will take over the leadership, etc. Traditional attendant Fun'kei appointed Narishige, Hiraki appointed First Day Horse, Kiyoyoshi appointed Fifth Day Horse, Kiyoya Yasumori appointed, etc. The rest as in previous years.

22nd Day, Mizunoe-Inu, Clear: Invited to First Headman Yasufumi's soup gathering, Senchi and Tsunehiro attended, sent 2 shō of sake.

24th Day, Kinoe-Ne, Clear: A mouse entered the inner sanctuary of Kibune端 Shrine, so a petition was made to the palace for an emergency door opening. Instructions were issued to stop musical performances and prevent both officials from leaving.

25th Day, Kinoto-Ushi, Clear: Regarding the door opening at Kifune 端 Shrine, with palace approval completed, from New Year's Day

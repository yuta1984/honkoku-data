9th day, Mizunoe-Ne, clear - The celebratory ceremonies for the auspicious festival of Chōyō (Double Ninth) were conducted as in previous years. In early morning, we each departed from Upper Kuga village. However, the accompanying laborers came from Upper Kuga village. We returned to the shogun's residence at half past the hour of the Horse and reported this matter. As usual, the three of us offered thirty ladder-gifts, and after presenting congratulatory words on that day, I returned home before the hour of the Monkey. ○ Today around the hour of the Sheep, Tsuchiyama Suruga-no-kami sent word that his father Awaji-no-kami had passed away last night. Furthermore, the funeral will be held on the coming 13th at the hour of the Rooster, and since the temple gate is in Upper Kuga village, the account office will be established at Honkoku-ji Temple.

11th day, Kinoe-Tora, clear - After lunch, there was an accounting for the 8th meeting of the Sansha lecture at the lecture hall. Both Keieki and Keisen attended. Ujihiko was absent due to being in light mourning.

13th day, Hinoe-Tatsu, clear - At the hour of the Rooster, for the funeral of the late Tsuchiyama Awaji-no-kami, I sent Kashio Ryūzō from the palace middle shift as a representative of my father and Keisen, and sent one cask of sake weighing two kin. I arranged for the send-off to be conducted at Honkoku-ji Temple, which serves as the account office for the temple gate.

15th day, Tsuchinoe-Uma, clear - As usual annually, for the Kei family gathering, after breakfast, my father and Keisen assembled at the lodging place of Kei-ki's residence. All matters proceeded as usual, with Keisen serving as the key-keeper for this year.

21st day, Kinoe-Ne, clear - Today there was an imperial visit to Shugakuin Imperial Villa.

28th day, Kanoto-Hitsuji, clear - Since Hisaaki discontinued all the calligraphy students after the Bon festival, I made a new request to Kentoku. I sent my second son Keikan from the 26th of the 8th month. Tsuguki was delayed due to a swelling, but finally started going today.

10th Month (small month)

1st day, Mizunoto-Tori, clear - This morning at the council office, there was the customary drawing of lots for retainers. Ikuaki was appointed as the deputy administrator of the shelf office, and the appointment slip was handed over. Keisen went as my father's representative. ○ Today is my father's duty shift to bring evening meals. All matters proceed as usual, and I had previously requested Teisei to serve as substitute.

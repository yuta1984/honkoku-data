Article 1: After the hour of the Rooster, shrine officials of various ranks and functionaries assembled at the retreat quarters. After preparations were complete, they emerged from the south entrance of the retreat building. The procession order was: leading shrine servants carrying torches, followed by tōne bearing sacred streamers, followed by head priest Hirohisa (wearing court robes, accompanied by one in cloth court dress, one in plain court jacket, shoe bearer, umbrella bearer, etc.), with the three offices in attendance (deputy head Kaneyasu, deputy administrator Yasuhiro, representative deputy Kaneryū), followed by Kataoka negi Yoshihisa, assistant priest Kazuhira (substitute Kiyohiro due to illness), Wakamiya negi Chikuaki, assistant priest Motoyasu (each wearing court dress, with one hakuchō attendant), rotating priests fourth group Yaaki, sixth group Fumiyuki (wearing purified robes and hakama, with one hakuchō attendant), followed by shrine officials attending at will: senior negi Naruhisa, assistant priest Shigeueru, deputy negi Yasuhisa, assistant priest Yasaurei (each in court dress), Kifu negi Ōhisa, Shingū priest Yasumune, Ōta priest Yasuto, Nara priest Kenaki, Sawada priest Kanetame, clan deity priest Kaneyasu (each in purified robes) proceeded. Entering through the two torii gates from Nara stone bridge, head priest and both Kataoka officials took seats before the deity, while other shrine officials and clan members attending at will sat in the northern garden east of the bridge hall, facing north. Other attendees included deputy Kanezuke, four tōne, six shrine servants, and two pine ceremony officials (wearing formal upper and lower garments).

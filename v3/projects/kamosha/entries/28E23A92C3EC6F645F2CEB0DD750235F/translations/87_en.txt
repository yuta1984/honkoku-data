　　March 18th　　　　From Kamo Shrine Complex

One: The supervisors Yasunori, Yasutake, and Kanemune served as chief administrators, with Hisayasu and Migitoshi dispatched (accompanied by six tonne and twenty-one shrine servants)
　for official service

One: After the imperial messenger's departure, at the administrative building, the chief priest and other shrine officials, along with those who participated in worship at their discretion,
　took their seats and viewed the imperial edict. It was circulated from the chief priest serving as administrative officer down to all voluntary participants.
　The record is as follows:

　From the Left Controller's Office to Kamo Wakeikazuchi Shrine
　　Regarding appointing the date and time for performing the pillar-raising ceremony for the temporary shrine during the current shrine reconstruction
　　　The 19th of this month (Day of Wood-Rooster)　　　at the appointed hour
　The Right Acting Middle Counselor Fujiwara no Ason Sanemoto proclaims by imperial command

　By imperial edict, it is appropriate to appoint the date and time for the ceremony. The shrine shall duly acknowledge this
　and carry it out according to the proclamation
　　Anei 6, March 18th　Right Senior Historian Miyoshi no Ason seal (respectfully submitted)

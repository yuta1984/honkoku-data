Copy of a Regulation Document from the Tensho Era

Article 1: Various regulations for the community of shrine parishioners [Kamo Wakeikazuchi Shrine Historical Materials - Regulation Document 189]
　- When receiving the sacred purification head position on the 16th day of the first month, the term shall be three days
　- When absent or unavailable, the term shall be twenty days  
　- During times of absence, duties shall be performed according to the shrine law's yearly order, and within the same year, it shall follow the order of seniority

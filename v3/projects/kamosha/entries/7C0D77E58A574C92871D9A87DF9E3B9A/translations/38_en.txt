Ujigami-sha-hafuri　　　　　Junior Fourth Rank Lower Kamo Jutsubō

- Around the hour of the Rooster, representative Ikyū Kanemochi returned home and explained that less than half an hour had passed when a summons came by letter from the magistrate Ben-dono through the office staff to the intermediate lodging, and when he immediately appeared, he was later told in audience that "regarding the matter of Yamato-no-kami Tsunekiyo's resignation, it has been deliberated upon and accepted." He expressed gratitude for this acceptance and withdrew. In the antechamber, he submitted small folded documents for transfers and new appointments, along with recommendation letters, accompanying documents, instruction letters, etc., requesting favorable consideration, which were received. He then reported this matter to Imperial Messenger Kushinoki-dono and withdrew.

21st day, clear weather

　- On the 19th, Kawaguchi and Kuranonjō visited and reported that all the sacred paintings were completed by today, and they also submitted assistant scribe documents.

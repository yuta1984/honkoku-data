8th Day, Clear Weather
One: Early morning, regarding the security officials of the Imperial Palace wishing to inspect the security positions for the Kibune formal reconstruction ceremony, around the Hour of Rabbit, Suei alone went up the mountain as a guide.
One: Hiraoka Kaimon-no-suke《same as above》clerk Tanaka Uhyōe-shi came and requested guidance to inspect the area in front of the shrine, so Kanemochi went out as a guide.

9th Day, Clear Weather  
One: Today for the rope barrier installation, after morning meal, chief administrators Chin Kenmori and Yasuai Ujun appeared.
One: After lunch, Suei alone appeared as representative to the Imperial Messenger, [presenting] documents of shrine officials' seating arrangements during the Enpō and Shōtoku main shrine formal reconstruction ceremonies, as well as documents listing the names of rest house proprietors for Senior Nobles and below.

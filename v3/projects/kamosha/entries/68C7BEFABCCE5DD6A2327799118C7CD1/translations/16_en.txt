In general, among the many varieties of sword construction, there are divine swords, dedicatory swords, imperial swords, yin swords, yang swords, noble-style swords, attendant swords, unofficial swords, and Noh drama swords - each type differs in its construction. The crafting follows specific traditions with gold construction, silver construction, special construction, rough construction, leopard and tiger fur scabbards, bear fur upper scabbards, and others. The four-piece cutting elements represent the four seasons' weather. Inside the pommel's scabbard form, yang numbers are displayed with carved sun disc roundness, and seventy-two shitodome fittings are attached. At the end of the golden chain are two hanging jeweled ornaments, representing the seventy-two seasonal periods with day and night elements in the scabbard fittings. In the scabbard form, as yin essence, moon disc roundness is used as nail covers and hammered in place. For the prosperity of the realm, each is based on the fulfillment of the five grains.

Respectfully copied by Naoju　【Red seal: Seal of Naoju】

This painting was presented by Lord Tsunenari at the time of Emperor Kameyama's御絵合 (imperial painting competition). The painting was done by Lord Tamenobu, and the accompanying text was written by Sadanari Ason. It was copied around the middle of the intercalary sixth month of Gentoku 2. The painting section was overseen by Takakane Ason, and the text was copied by the lay priest and deputy head of the Imperial Treasury, Suekuni Ason.

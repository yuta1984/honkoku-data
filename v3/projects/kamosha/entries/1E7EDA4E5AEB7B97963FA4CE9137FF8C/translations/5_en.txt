The female messengers, including the myōbu court ladies and kurōdo chamberlains, were omitted from the record. When the Saiin (High Priestess) was present, there were purification rituals, with the ben (controller) crossing Ichijō Avenue, the tenji (court lady) visiting the shrine, and entertainment on the return from Murasaki-no. Because of such activities, the festival ceremonies were splendid, but after the Genkyū era, these traditions were discontinued for a long time.

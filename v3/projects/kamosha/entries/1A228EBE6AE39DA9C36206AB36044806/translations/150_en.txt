[Red ink notation: Three Groups] Articles established by group consensus during the three-group assembly at the lecture hall on the 28th day of the 1st month of the same year
Article 1: Regarding those who are borrowing residences and do not attend the three-group sessions until they are assigned to correspondence rice fields, when they do attend upon being assigned to fields - whether such persons were originally from the Eastern Group or originally from the West-Central Group, when assigned to fields they shall attend the borrowing residence group.
Furthermore, regarding those from the Eastern Group who have already attended but seek residences in the West-Central area and wish to attend West-Central Group sessions, or those from the West-Central Group who have already attended but seek residences in the Eastern area and wish to attend Eastern Group sessions - in either case, if barrel fees have already been paid to one side, separate barrel fees need not be paid to the group for re-attendance.

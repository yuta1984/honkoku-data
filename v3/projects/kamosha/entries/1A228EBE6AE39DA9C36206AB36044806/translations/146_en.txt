【Red ink notation: West-Central Group】Articles established by the West-Central Group in the same 13th year, year of the Metal Dragon (Hōreki 13, 1763)
Article 1: In recent years, due to the lack of annual officials, there has been no inspection of equipment during the New Year assembly meetings, and various matters regarding ancient practices have become neglected. Therefore, this time for the sake of the group, all equipment shall be examined and repairs added, annual events shall be reformed and handed over to the current year's officials. Hereafter, according to ancient practice, on the morning of participation in the New Year assembly meeting, it shall be promptly conducted after breakfast. At that time, the previous year's officials shall inspect all equipment, and if there are any losses, compensation shall be made according to regulations before transfer to the current year's officials. 《Marginal note: At that time, servants shall be summoned and stationed》 Furthermore, the current year's officials shall examine all matters and properly conduct the handover. Should there be any negligence, the current year's officials shall make compensation.
Privately, the remaining two articles regarding prayer lecture matters are omitted.

Article 1: Regarding role assignments for chanting during prayer lectures, "dono" (lord) shall be attached to senior members, while those without stipend shall have their names written alongside.

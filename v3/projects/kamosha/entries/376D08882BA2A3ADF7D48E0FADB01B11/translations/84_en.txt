Tenpō 8, Hinoto-tori year
First month, major

New Year's Day, Tsuchinoto-u, clear weather, the four seas are peaceful, seasonal divine rituals were conducted as in previous years. ○ Kyōkan for the first time
served in attendance for New Year's court ceremonies and seasonal banquets, which was most auspicious. ○ In the palace, the Western Palace's
joyous celebrations were postponed due to certain circumstances. Private worship at the protective shrine and ceremonial offerings were suspended,
New Year's court ceremonies were postponed, congratulatory events were cancelled, household ceremonies were delayed, attire was restricted to mourning hemp
kamishimo, decorative arrangements were properly arranged, and purification will take place on the coming 7th day.

5th day, Mizunoto-hitsuji, light snow. This dawn, Matsumoto
Koyo, mother of Tachikawa Tonomo (maternal relative of the Great Lord), passed away, so formal mourning attire was adopted according to regulations. Consequently, congratulatory events and household ceremonies
were further postponed until the coming 15th day, and naturally all decorations were also delayed.

6th day, Kinoe-saru, clear. Regarding the end of the 7-day mourning period for Naijitsu-suke of the Young Lord's household, tomorrow morning Kyōsen's
purification ritual was ordered through Senior Retainer Namba Iyo-no-kami, and acknowledgment was humbly submitted.

Sixth month (short month)

Second day, Tsuchinoe-Saru, Clear - Around the hour of the Rat (11 PM-1 AM), a letter arrived from the palace.
Regarding the Retired Shogun's illness being quite severe, both men immediately went to the palace and inquired before his presence. From after the hour of the Monkey (3-5 PM) today, he became withdrawn, and after agitation, fell into unconsciousness.

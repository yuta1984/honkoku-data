24th day, Hinoto-ushi, clear. As the 27th will be the 13th anniversary memorial service for Lady Teigen'in (daughter of Lord Hosokawa), today audiences were granted to all the inner retainers.

25th day, Tsuchinoe-tora, clear. I requested the Confucian scholar Kyōkei Kanjōsuke, and today had him enter as a disciple under my guidance. He wore hemp formal dress (kamishimo), and I sent three monme of silver as a fan fee. This was the first reading of the Zuo Commentary (Sashiden). Buds have emerged - most auspicious! ○ The rice distribution from the Aoi Festival was handed over and received as usual. ○ The accounting for the gold-backed lecture was conducted as usual, and from this time's deposited funds, two bu and two shu each were returned.

26th day, Tsuchinoto-u, clear. For Lady Teigen'in's vigil night, I visited Tōfuku-ji temple. The memorial service will be conducted at the same temple today and tomorrow.

27th day, Kanoe-tatsu, clear. For Lady Teigen'in's memorial day, the Young Palace made a formal visit to Tōfuku-ji temple, with Tsunenori participating in the entourage.

28th day, Kanoto-mi, rain, clearing in the afternoon. Following the reconstruction, Jingū-ji, Shōjin-ji and other temples began their 30-day exhibitions today as per previous regulations.

Last day, Mizunoto-hitsuji, cloudy. Distribution of rice allowance money to the fifth group. 3,776 mon per person was distributed. ○ For the monthly rotation of senior and junior duty assignments, I requested Takekiyo to serve as substitute. ○ Regarding Hiiragi Kyūemon's portion, while 28 monme of silver from last winter had been deposited, it was handed over due to Hiiragi Shōjirō's service today. However, he should investigate and submit whatever more he can for the tenth-day lecture group.

Third Month (short month)
3rd day, Hinoe-inu, after rain. Celebratory sake for the third celebration was bestowed as in previous years. ○ The sacred ritual proceeded as usual.

5th day, Tsuchinoe-ne, clear. As it was the opening day of the Tōfuku-ji exhibition, Ijichi and I went privately for inspection, toured under the guidance of the guest quarters, and were served food and sake.

In all matters as usual, among the former officials, Yasutora, Kinchou, Yasumori and others assembled. Concluded at half past the hour of the cock, returned home after the hour of the dog.

9th day, Kinoe-tora, rain. For the shrine duty rotation, Keikei was ordered to serve as substitute. ○Due to Lord Daishoji's pagoda dedication, Taifu Yasuda, steward Murata and others paid their respects. Three or four people from the inner quarters, from senior ladies-in-waiting downward, also paid their respects. ○Regarding the vacancy in the head priest position, when the matter of conducting Shinto rituals was inquired about, as was decreed during the Kyoho era, during the vacancy Kataoka Negi should serve as acting head priest, and Kataoka Negi should serve as deputy official, which was announced by the court liaison, and this matter was communicated from the shrine to the deputies.

13th day, Tsuchinoe-uma, rain. For the 10th meeting of the Kagura lecture, as usual went to service at Okudaya in Sanbongi. The officials in charge were four people: Akiyoshi-akira, myself, Hissaken, etc. Returned home before the hour of the dog.
○Lord Safu departed today for his journey down to the Kanto region.

14th day, Tsuchinoto-hitsuji, wind and rain. Lord Naifu departed today for his journey down to the Kanto region.
○At the morning hour, flooding occurred, bridges at various village entrances were swept away, at half past the noon hour emergency drums were beaten, and due to the alarm bell tower, [I] hurried from midway to serve at Kawaramachi, where at the back of Sakaiya Kichibei's residence, water formed two retention pools, and as the tree-lined areas were gradually being swept away, driftwood caught in various places, but in about half an hour the water decreased somewhat, flood prevention measures were arranged, and the situation calmed down.

15th day, Kanoe-saru, clear. The liberation ceremony [hojoe] was as in previous years.

16th day, Shintou-tori, overcast with light rain. Tsuneki had been in confinement since the 5th day of the 6th month due to a mild epidemic, and afterward became weak, but has gradually recovered completely and was ordered to report for duty today. Most fortunate indeed. ○Due to this shogun appointment, Lord Sakai Saemon-no-jo of Choshu Shoda will soon come up to Kyoto as an envoy of Lord Kanto Dainagon.

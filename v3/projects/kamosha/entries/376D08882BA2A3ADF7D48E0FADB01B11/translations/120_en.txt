Memorandum
1. Due to the accumulation of debts in the shrine kitchen from previous times, for the repayment thereof, strict economies shall be instituted for ten years beginning from this sixth month. In general, if there are reductions in service taxes and distributions, this will naturally affect everyone and cause hardship, so everyone should take special care that there be no wasteful expenditures whatsoever.

1. As repeatedly instructed from the Imperial Palace, there shall be no conduct inappropriate to one's station.

1. Not only elsewhere but also at this place, quarrels and disputes shall not be engaged in. Even if there are disrespectful persons, one should respond prudently. Loud arguments, physical violence, and such shall absolutely not be committed.

1. Since gambling gatherings become disorderly without distinction between high and low ranks, and commoners may become disrespectful toward their superiors without restraint, shrine family members shall absolutely not frequent gambling establishments.

1. Recently, water officials were summoned by the Eastern Government Office and instructed to manage matters so that the official water supply would not be interrupted, and written acknowledgments were submitted before returning. This matter has been reported to the shrine community, so there must be no violations regarding the river. Especially during this period of continued drought with daily water shortages, if there are stone dams, grass blockages, and such, water flow will be poor and may interfere with irrigation of rice fields, so everyone should take care not to misunderstand this matter.

1. Regarding Bon dancing, this is suspended during the aforementioned period of economy.

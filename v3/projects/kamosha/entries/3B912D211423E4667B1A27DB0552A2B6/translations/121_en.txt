Next the shrine officials who have returned proceed to the corridor《dividing left and right and taking seats》. Next the head priest and chief priest rise and enter the inner sanctuary《as initially》. Next the head negi priest and below rise and wait on the upper and lower steps《those waiting at the side of the steps each form a single line on the negi priest side》. Next they roll up the sacred curtains. Next the offering bearers rise and present round trays. Next they remove the divine offerings《from the inner sanctuary》. First《divine offerings from the priest side》salted sweetfish, next wrapped imperial provisions, next stacked imperial provisions, next sacred raw items《five throats/pieces》《removing these five times as initially》, next sacred birds, next sacred carp, next oak leaf rice cakes, next rice cakes, next the same, next sacred rice, next《negi priest portion》salted sweetfish, next sedge rice dumplings, next cypress bark rice dumplings, next wrapped imperial provisions, next stacked imperial provisions, next sacred raw items《five throats/pieces》, next sacred birds, next sacred carp, next oak leaf rice cakes, next rice cakes, next the same, next sacred rice, next sacred sake, next sacred chopsticks, next outer sanctuary Korean-style imperial provisions《priest side》, next the same《negi priest side》, next they present sacred wiping cloths. Next they lower the sacred curtains. Next the offering bearers return to their seats. Next starting from the Wakamiya priest they proceed to the lower steps in order, and after one bow wait on the priest side. Next the head priest exits the inner sanctuary and waits on the priest side《at the base of the eastern worship pillar》. Next the priest exits the inner sanctuary and closes the sacred doors《everyone prostrates》. Next the deputy master presents the sacred lock wrapping, and the chief priest takes it《at this point the sacred key is handed over to the deputy master》. After wrapping the sacred lock and bowing once, each withdraws from the priest side. From Wakamiya Shrine to Nara Shrine, they make pilgrimage visits to each shrine and descend《The five officials and Kataoka negi priests, after the divine offerings are removed and they return to their lodgings, send subordinates to the offering hall to collect items, and at that time what is handed over from the deputy to the head priest is brought by shrine attendants. Also, the head priest and below shrine officials descend from the side of the Jinguji temple pond, and for the head priest, the administrators, deputy governors, and deputies accompany him to the village pavilion》. Next they offer the outer sanctuary evening provisions《The duty shrine officials prepare this, and the offering bearers on shrine duty perform this task. However, there is no evening provision communion ceremony, and in the evening it is said that the head priest and below and those of lower rank on the steps hold communion》

When finished, each withdraws.

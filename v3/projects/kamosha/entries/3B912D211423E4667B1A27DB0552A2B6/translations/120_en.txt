Next the deputy master grants the sacred key to the priests of Kataoka and below. Next the shrine officials of the seven shrines rise and proceed to their respective shrines to prepare the inner and outer divine offerings.

The ceremony is as follows: First the two officials and offering bearers take seats in the worship hall. Next the priest opens the sacred doors《At this time everyone prostrates》. Next the two officials enter the inner sanctuary. Next they roll up the sacred curtains. Next the offering bearers rise and present the divine offerings. The items are: iris《Two stalks divided left and right, later stored in boxes of the inner sanctuary》, sacred chopsticks《One set, white wood》, sacred rice《Divided left and right into two bowls, served in flat earthenware》, salted sea bream《Four pieces》, ray fish《Four pieces, of which two are dried》, rice cakes《Four pieces》, oak leaf rice cakes《Four pieces》, cypress bark rice dumplings《Two bundles》, sedge rice dumplings《Two bundles, about four shaku long》, sacred sake《Served in triple earthenware vessels, platform of two cut half-papers》, salted sweetfish《Two or four fish, sometimes not prepared depending on the time, all seen in the New Year's Day section below》etc.《Placed in sacred containers》are prepared by the two officials《At the exact center of the sacred platform》. Next they lower the sacred curtains, the negi priest exits and returns to his seat. Next the priest exits and prepares the outer sanctuary divine offerings《The items being the same as New Year's Day, omitted for brevity》

When finished, the offering bearers and priests return to their seats, next the two officials bow twice and recite prayers in two stages, after clapping hands the priests and offering bearers rise and remove the outer sanctuary divine offerings. Next the negi priest rises, stands in line and enters the inner sanctuary《After bowing up and down》. Next they roll up the sacred curtains and remove the divine offerings《Especially regarding the iris, they store it in left and right boxes》. Next they lower the sacred curtains. Next the negi priest returns to his seat and the priest closes the sacred doors《Everyone prostrates》. Next they present the sacred lock wrapping《The offering bearers present this and after finishing return to their seats》. The priest wraps the sacred lock and returns to the worship hall. Next is the divine offering communion meal《This is the inner sanctuary divine offering. The sword-bearer distributes it, especially dividing it in two for the two officials as usual》

When finished they withdraw《However, since there is no communion meal seating, each withdraws as convenient. When finished the offering bearers return to their seats, and the assistant two officials take their places at the prayer seats《Each from outside the viewing space》. Next the deputy master《Hearing that the Kataoka earth ritual is to be performed》proceeds to the transparent corridor《As usual》

At this time the five officials rise and bow once, next the deputy master announces that the earth ritual is completed《At this time descending to the outer veranda》. Next the five officials bow once and perform prayers and clapping.

[Left page]
　　　Third day of third month: Jōshi Sacred Ritual
On the day, at the Hour of the Rabbit, shrine personnel announce the honorable visit three times
Next, the shrine administrator puts on court dress and goes to the retreat house
Next, the director comes to the retreat house asking "Shall we set up the sacred offerings?" and the chief priest responds
Next, the shrine administrator moves and goes to the earth house
Next, the sacred offerings depart《each prostrating》
Next, the shrine administrator advances before the deity《purification by onmyō permitted at the jeweled bridge》and takes his seat in the corridor
Next, the Deputy Administrator passes the sacred key to the Chief Hafuri
Next, the Chief Hafuri opens the sacred doors《each moves from their seat and prostrates》
Next, the Chief Priest and Chief Hafuri advance to the inner sanctuary, with attendants from the Chief Prayer Master to the Clan Deity Hafuri waiting above and below the stairs, left and right

May 5th

Early morning imperial visit guidance《The shrine attendants announce this as usual》Up to three times《Around the hour of the Rabbit (5-7 AM)》Each exits the gate《Wearing court dress with iris and mugwort as waist sashes》

《One attendant is appointed, but the two Kibune officials do not participate in the main shrine's door-opening divine service, and from before dawn they don court robes from their residences and proceed directly to Kibune. Especially the one attendant and costume bearers etc. are as usual》They arrive at the earthen building《Without waiting rooms they assemble and enter directly from the third torii to the second torii. Especially passing through the shrines along the route, the processional bows and shoe-removal bows etc. are as usual》The offering bearers《five people》, yin-yang purification leaders etc. assemble at the shrine grounds. After the shrine officials gather, the chief administrator inquires about the divine offering dispatch ceremony《The head priest responds to this, the manner being as usual》Next, divine offerings are dispatched from the offering hall. First the advance guard, next sake vessels, next sixteen containers of rice offerings《However, within these, first seven containers for the seven shrines, next nine containers for the main shrine's negi priests》Next the advance guard《priests' portion》next sake vessels, next seven rice containers《However, the final one container is carried by shrine attendants. This is shiiba-mochi (oak leaf rice cakes)》When these pass through the earthen building, the shrine officials prostrate themselves. Next, the yin-yang master purifies the divine offerings at the customary place. The divine offerings are placed inverted on the east and west offering shelves.

Next the shrine officials rise from their seats, and the yin-yang master purifies and cleanses their bodies《Like with the divine offerings》Next the shrine officials divide left and right and take seats at the transparent corridor seats before the deities《Especially the worship bows are as usual》Next the offering bearers take their seats, next the deputy master presents the sacred key to the head priest, and the priests open the sacred doors《At this time each moves from their seats and prostrates》At this time the musicians make sounds, next the head priest and priests enter the inner sanctuary《Advancing, retreating, and worship bows are as usual》Next, starting from the head negi priest, they rise in order and after one bow, the left and right sides wait on the upper and lower steps. Next they roll up the sacred curtains, next the offering bearers rise《They wait in the passage for divine offering procession, but the two junior attendants》《After opening the sacred doors, they wait with the priests as usual》Next they announce the advance guard《From the negi priests》Next iris《Two stalks placed on small round seats, loaded on round trays, then stored in the inner sanctuary》are processed forward《The procedure is as usual》Next they announce the advance guard《From the priests》

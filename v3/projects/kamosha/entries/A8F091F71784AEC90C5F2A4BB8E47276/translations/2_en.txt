Kōka 2, Year of the Wood Snake
First Month (29 days)

New Year's Day, Day of the Water Boar, clear weather. The four seas are peaceful and tranquil. The most important sacred rituals performed as in previous years. ○Regarding the celebratory arrangements for the imperial family, due to the period of economizing for five years starting this year, the celebratory diamond-shaped rice cakes, oak leaf rice cakes, stacked boxes, and remnants of the tooth-hardening ceremony are omitted. On the day of New Year's congratulations, Keiki and Keikei attend as usual. ○At the evening banquet, two people attend the outer minister's visit, same as usual. After the formation, there is an early departure.

2nd day, Day of the Wood Rat, clear weather. The customary New Year's first visit to the clan shrine is completed as usual.

3rd day, Day of the Wood Ox, rain. Irie Tosa-no-kami is appointed as the New Year's envoy to the Kantō region.

6th day, Day of the Earth Dragon, clear weather. Keikan visits the shrine for New Year's greetings and to express gratitude for successfully performing his duties as an attendant at next winter's special festival without incident. ○The day before yesterday, the 4th, Yamada Ichizaemon, the Satsuma domain's representative, rested at the Seo residence in Muromachi, so Chiyozō went there and provided rice soup, sake, and meals. I also went to accompany him. This was my first meeting with the domain representative, and we concluded our conversation.

8th day, Day of the Metal Horse, clear weather. Keikei makes his New Year's visit to the shrine.

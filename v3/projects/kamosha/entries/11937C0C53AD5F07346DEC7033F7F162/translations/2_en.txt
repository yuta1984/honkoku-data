　　　　　　　　　　　　Direct First Examination and Correction

This original record of shrine priest appointments is from the collection of the Okamoto family (Tsunetate). His ancestor, Kiyoshige Agata-nushi, unexpectedly obtained this document during the dismissal of five families in Kyōhō 6 (1721). I respectfully borrowed it, made a new copy, and upon examination found that there appeared to be considerable disorder in the sequence of positions. Therefore, I consulted various books to investigate the dates of appointments and corrected the sequence of positions. For cases where no appointment dates could be found, I used the appointment dates of fathers, grandfathers, sons, and brothers as evidence for verification. Although I have recorded these tentatively, there are likely many errors. I hope that future generations will critique and examine this work.

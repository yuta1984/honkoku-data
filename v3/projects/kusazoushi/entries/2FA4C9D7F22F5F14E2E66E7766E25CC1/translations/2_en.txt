【Left Page】
Long, long ago, in the deep mountains of Echigo Province, there lived a monster called the Three-Eyed Great Monk, a tall monster who was said to be the leader of all monsters. Here also in Shinano Province lived another monster boss called Meshi-bitsu Furusō, and the Three-Eyed one fell in love with his daughter O-Roku and sent betrothal gifts.

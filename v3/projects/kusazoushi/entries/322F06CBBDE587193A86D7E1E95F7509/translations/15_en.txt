Jarin felt as if he had been brought back to life and was greatly rejoiced, but wondered "Who might this be that has saved me?" Looking around, he saw a terrifying-looking monster emerge from the cave, who said: "I am called Satori from Gōshū. I have been traveling through various provinces training as a monster, and am now resting in this roadside shrine. Hearing the situation, it seems to be a matter of revenge, so I take pity on you. I shall assist you and help you devour that Master Judge."

Hearing this, Jarin was greatly pleased and said "First, let us deal with one half of our enemies." Together with Satori, he devoured and killed those monsters from before, and ignoring any injuries, he bravely prepared to hurry immediately to Twin Rocks.

Thus, Jarin successfully devoured Shuhan, his parent's enemy, and returned to his homeland. In celebration of this joy, he took Nikari, the daughter of Bato, as his bride, inherited his parent's position, and was revered as the master of monsters, commanding many subordinates as he welcomed the spring in great fortune.

"There is no greater happiness than this! Momommon, gwaa!"

"How auspicious, how auspicious!"

Now Ten-ten awoke from his dream and returned home, pondering deeply over the divine revelation he had received from the gods in his dream, and he became greatly disheartened. This was because if the pleasure-seekers throughout the provinces, especially the frivolous types in Kamakura, were to reform their hearts and correct their conduct, education, and manners, then the business of flatterers and toadies would be ruined. Despite being a great deity himself, he was troubled about what to do.

"This is quite a problem," he thought, along with the head priest of Dōjōji Temple and Kita...

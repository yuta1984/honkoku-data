The profession of being a monster means frightening people on the road and taking money as business, but unlike humans, they don't do truly wicked things - they frighten those with crooked hearts, but for honest and upright people, they would rather flee themselves. This is the proper way of monsters. Having made some money, Mikoshi-nyudo thought about opening a shop somewhere. At the foot of this mountain along the highway, there was an ancient large willow tree where a ghost with a visible head appeared night after night. Mikoshi bought this spot and planned to open a shop there. However, thinking the location had bad feng shui, he ordered his disciples to work on it, had them buy and arrange moss-covered stone lanterns and such items around the base of the willow tree, making it quite eerie. "Oh, well done! Let me just come out and see how it looks," he said, slipping behind the embankment. "Hyou-toro-toro-toro, momongwaa!" he suddenly appeared, and "Good, good, everyone is frightened!" he rejoiced greatly.

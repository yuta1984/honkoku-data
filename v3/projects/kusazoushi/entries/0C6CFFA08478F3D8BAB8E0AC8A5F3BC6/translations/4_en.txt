【Character】Minamoto no Yorimitsu
【Actor】Sōjūrō

【Left page, upper section, main text】
Yorimitsu is ill, and the Four Heavenly Kings attend to him, comforting him. They offer him earthenware called "uneme" made from soil of Mount Katsuragi and famous sake called Asakayama. When a spider descends, suspecting poison in the earthenware, they attempt to smash it. At that moment, Sakata Kintoki and Watanabe no Tsuna appear prominently, Tsumagiku speaks persuasively, and Sadamitsu and Suetake try to take charge, mediating the sake banquet.
Yorimitsu gives a gold tablet to Tsuna, ordering him to find the lost Hiru-no-omashi sword, and also

【Right page, lower section, continuation】
Orders Kintoki to find the previous emperor's testament and investigate Hakamadare, the ringleader of the bandits. When both men are dismissed and a woman is brought near, they are startled when the Hizamaru sword unsheathes itself.

Tsumagiku is a bold woman who has set her heart on Yorimitsu. Deceiving everyone, she attempts to accompany Yorimitsu to his sleeping quarters, but becomes frightened by the Hizamaru sword.

【Character】Tsumagiku, a dancer from Rokujō in the capital, actually the spirit of the spider of Mount Katsuragi
【Actor】Segawa Kikunojō

【This scene depicts the "Tsuchigumo" (Earth Spider) story found in the Heike Monogatari and Noh theater. When Minamoto no Yorimitsu falls ill with malaria and is bedridden, a mysterious monk appears and tries to abduct him, so the Four Heavenly Kings attack with the Hizamaru sword. The monk was actually the spirit of a giant spider from Mount Katsuragi.】

He too is human. Even an apprentice doing menial work (picking up barrels) wants to have fun and dance - this is something everyone possesses. As a lifelong memory of his service, he brought twelve mon.

Though he hadn't decided on a specific place, while peering here and there at the cheap brothels, something white as snow caught his eye, and unable to resist his longing, he went up. But the young shop owner came calling, so he had no choice but to say "I'll come soon, please wait" and return inside. After finishing his business, he ran out again, and as the lights of the third district were extinguished at the secret house across the way, a dog barked and a fox fled with a rustling sound.

He got a bargain on Gojūzō for half price at twenty-four mon.

"Hey, big brother, come over here!"

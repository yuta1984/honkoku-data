【Right Upper Section】
After the initial drinking ceremony was completely finished and they came to the sleeping quarters, there was no greeting and the other person seemed to be lost in thought with a bad expression. So Shaka thought to himself, "This fellow must be some kind of good host, but I must have come at the wrong time." From then on, he made some effort and finally managed to find out what was going on.

【Left Upper Section】
He put his heart into this first encounter and somehow called that man to meet with them.

He arranged for a courtesan to meet them, whose name was "Byōbu" (Screen), and while drinking by herself outside, she said, "What I did - spending extra money to pursue love affairs - how foolish of me."

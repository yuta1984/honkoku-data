【Right Page Main Text】
The imperial messenger after Saimyōji (Tokiyori) is revealed to be Echigo no Kami Mitsutoki. The imperial envoy's message is that the Kanpaku's daughter who was adopted is suspected of having seditious intentions after growing up and marrying the Prince, and therefore Princess Ayako, who was adopted, should be beheaded by imperial command. This being a pressing difficult matter, [Tokiyori] asks Tsuneyo what should be done, and they divine by drawing lots using the flourishing of plum, cherry, and pine trees.

Saimyōji Tokiyori Nyūdō - Yamashina Shirōjūrō (Character/Actor)
Gentōta - Ichikawa Tomouemon (Character/Actor)
Tsuneyo, perplexed about whom to kill, thinks to cut down a cherry tree representing the princess and provide a substitute.

Tsukihashi (Character: Tsuneyo's stepmother) - Junemon (Actor)

【Left Page Main Text】
Tsuneyo (Matsumoto Kōshirō) intended to make his own daughter Tamazusa serve as substitute for Princess Ayako. He deliberately treats his accompanying wife coldly, and knowing that she is the sister of the rebel Asahara, he gives her a divorce document. Later, seeing that the divorce is near, he reveals the truth and laments.

Shirotae regrets her young life and commits suicide. Hearing about the substitution plan, she rejoices in her husband's loyalty and passes away peacefully.

Shirotae - Nakamura Hanjūrō (Character/Actor)
Actually Asahara's sister

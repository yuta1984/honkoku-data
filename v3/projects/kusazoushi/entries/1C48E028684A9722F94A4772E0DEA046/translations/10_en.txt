Shinano-no-suke (character, actor: Monnosuke)
Receives orders from Tokiyori and comes as an envoy to entrust Magosaburō with the governance of the realm, waiting for his return

The rōnin of Aoto village, Aoto Magosaburō, goes out for snow viewing and hears the voice of a warbler announcing an appointment. He returns and after a while meets with Shinano-no-suke to hear the details. Referencing the Chinese story of Xu You, he speaks of taking "future perspective"

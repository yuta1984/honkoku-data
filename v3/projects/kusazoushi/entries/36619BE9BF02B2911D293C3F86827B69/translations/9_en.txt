The Bird of Greed

The bird of greed desires ill-gotten wealth, constantly dancing and playing above the floating clouds. It has two heads and thus a duplicitous heart. It is full of falsehoods and deceives people.

This bird exists in this world of suffering, having no compassion or善根 (virtuous deeds) in its heart whatsoever. When there is something profitable for itself, it does not consider the hardships it causes others. It greedily hoards gold and silver obtained through injustice and inhumanity, spending its entire life suffering in the burning house of a wealthy ghost, not knowing satisfaction in its avarice.

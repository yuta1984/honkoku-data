Soon they attacked the nyūdō's castle, with enemies and allies fighting in chaos, making this place their battlefield. Just when it seemed the fox faction might be heading toward a slight defeat, the snow woman courageously advanced with her great naginata held horizontally for cutting. Seeing this, the Mikoshi faction appeared greatly dismayed. Unlike human warfare, when cut they disappear, when stabbed they vanish entirely, so neither enemy nor ally has anyone tenaciously pursuing others. Somehow, at this rate, there seems to be no end in sight.

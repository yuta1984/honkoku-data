Preface【Seal】
One day, treading upon the fallen blossoms of Mount Tōei,
Following the uncertain path by the wisteria of Negishi,
Along the clear stream where yamabuki flowers bloom,
On my way home at dusk, I visited
The studio of Sekien. The master was away,
And it seemed his return would be some time yet, so the
Attendant boy offered me tea

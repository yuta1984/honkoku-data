The above-mentioned things should be avoided and shunned. If carelessly violated, one may develop itching, or become restless and unable to sleep, or somehow feel unwell, and unexpected changes may occur.

Foods that should not be eaten:
- Bracken (warabi)　　- Royal fern (zenmai)　　- Eggplant
- Oriental melon　　- Watermelon　　- Cucumber  
- Peach　　- Pear (good when the fruit has heat)　　- Persimmon
- Chestnut　　- Winter melon (avoid for 10 days)　　- Pumpkin
- Cowpea (avoid for 10 days)　　- Water chestnut (avoid for 12 days)　　- Walnut (same avoidance)
- Kumquat　　- Citrus reticulata　　- Lettuce (avoid for 15 days)
- Garland chrysanthemum (avoid for 10 days)　　- Spinach

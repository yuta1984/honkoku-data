Cholera is an extremely frightening acute disease, and when urgent treatment is not administered, even patients who could be cured will quickly die - this is truly lamentable. Moreover, when this disease breaks out in epidemic form, doctors are often unable to arrive quickly enough. Therefore, while waiting for a doctor, laypeople should prepare themselves in advance with knowledge so that when the time comes, they will not panic and can provide thorough care. This book records in detail the methods for doing so, with the desire that it may serve as an aid in saving the world.

《Title: A Layperson's Guide to Treating Violent Diarrhea (Cholera) Disease》

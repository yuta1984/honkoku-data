Or the abdominal rumbling can be clearly heard from outside, nausea and vomiting occur, initially vomiting food or bile-like substances or other matter, later vomiting watery fluid mixed with spotted material. The gastric obstruction and heaviness increases, urine becomes scanty, and later complete urinary retention occurs. Unable to endure severe restlessness and distress, the muscles of the upper arm, forearm, especially the calves cramp, and it is not rare for even the scrotum to convulse. When such two or three symptoms, or even more various symptoms develop, it is beyond doubt that this is already the violent diarrhea (korori) disease. 《Note: In addition to this, there are symptoms such as hoarseness of voice》

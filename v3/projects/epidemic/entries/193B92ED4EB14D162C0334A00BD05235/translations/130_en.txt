He struck Juncho's back and pushed him down from above the clouds. The four figures disappeared, hidden in the clouds. Juncho, having been dropped headlong from mid-heaven, felt his heart spinning in confusion, lost himself, and unconsciously cried out "Ah!"—that voice resonating in his own ears. He awoke with a start, only to find it was merely a Nanka dream, and his body was in his original study, leaning against a desk under a dying lamp that was about to go out. Even after awakening, his heart remained in a trance, and for a while he could not distinguish between dream and waking.

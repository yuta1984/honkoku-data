Statement of Intent
My purpose in writing this work is certainly not to gain fame or reputation. When I carefully observe the state of the present world, those who suffer from syphilis are frivolous and weak-willed, lacking the true intention to cure their disease. Therefore, they are deceived by quack doctors and ignorant physicians, eventually developing incurable symptoms and ruining their entire lives - such people are countless. Wishing to resolve the delusions of these people and clarify the wickedness and ignorance of dishonest doctors, so that patients will not fall into their poisonous hands, I have compiled this book without regard for my own shallow learning, and spread it throughout society. Generally, those who suffer from syphilis have within their bodies an innate poison that readily harmonizes with evil qi. Therefore, when they consort with those who have syphilis, that evil qi harmonizes with their innate poison and flows into their body. Initially, it manifests as chancre, bubo, gonorrhea, and such.

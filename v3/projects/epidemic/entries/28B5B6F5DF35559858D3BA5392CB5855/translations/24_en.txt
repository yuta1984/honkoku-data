○ Supplementary Food Therapy Items
Kochia (ground cypress) - Good when fever is strong and urination is difficult; Watermelon - Good when fever is strong and thirsty
Mung beans - Good when heat toxin is strong and smallpox boils develop; Barley - Good when spleen and stomach are weak and food digestion is difficult
Radish - Good for phlegm and cough; Lily bulb - Good when spleen and stomach are weak and coughing occurs
Polygonum multiflorum - Good when pus is thin and residual toxin develops; Job's tears - Good for edema or when ulceration prevents scab formation
Kelp - Good for edema after scabbing; Sugar - Good when smallpox scabs are slow to fall off

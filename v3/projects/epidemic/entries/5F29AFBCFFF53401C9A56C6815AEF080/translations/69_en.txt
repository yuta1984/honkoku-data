1. Today Tachibana-ya Hanzaemon departs, and since he is unfamiliar with the journey, he requested that Kitajima Heijizaemon be assigned as a guide. This was granted. Furthermore, on this occasion, ten kanme of silver was sent via the same person as remittance to Osaka, and official correspondence to Edo and Osaka was also sent.
1. From tonight at the audience hall
   A festival for the Sacred Spirits was arranged, and accordingly a monk from Sochiji Temple
   came to participate, so one hundred hiki of gold was bestowed as religious offering.
1. The Bon duties at the temples and the head servants at Sakuraoka follow the usual yearly practice.
   July 14th

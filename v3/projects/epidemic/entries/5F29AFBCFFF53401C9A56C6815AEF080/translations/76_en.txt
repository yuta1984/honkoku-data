August 2nd
One - When Lord Kuranosuke went to the Second Castle yesterday, he was given the following written document from the elders:
　　From Yaeno Yurano to me
　　It has been conveyed [unclear characters]
One - Regarding the above matter, various officials attended and conducted an investigation.
　　Same day, 3rd
One - Served as intermediary for ceremonial incense offering to Muryoji Temple.
　　Same day, 4th
　　Same day, 5th
One - The appointment of Takagi Chuemon as side inspector was conveyed by Lord Nuinosuke. Since the current official is ill, and other chief retainers are also ill, causing impediments to duties, this person conveyed the message.
One - Due to insect damage in the rural areas causing withered rice ears and extraordinary circumstances, prayers were ordered at Ganzoji Temple, and two pieces of ceremonial silver were provided.
　　Same day, 6th

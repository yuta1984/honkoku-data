It was handed over to Yoshitomi Saburōbei, so Yoshitomi Saburōbei promptly sent a report to this office.

1. While Toneri-dono, Sukezaemon-dono, and others were on duty at the receiving office, they were granted deer from the lord's personal hunt.
   February 10th

1. Served as intermediary for the ceremonial incense offering to Mount Matsuo.

1. Since Hideishima Uemon was sent to Osaka, he was granted fifteen pieces of silver.
   Same day, 11th

1. Regarding the borrowed silver matter, Zusho-dono conveyed that Sukezaemon-dono would be sent to Osaka. He was granted twenty pieces of silver for expenses related to the completion of affairs.

1. Regarding Miyaji Nihei, it was conveyed by the current officials that he was appointed to the position of chief clerk in the lord's presence and was also ordered to concurrently serve as advisor.

1. Regarding Ureshino Zenemon, he was ordered in the lord's presence to serve concurrently as chief clerk.

One - For the lord's retinue, porters were arranged up to Maru, and for lodging, eight houses from Shimomachi to Horikoji were requisitioned and provided.

August 9th
One - Last night at the hour of Ku (around midnight), Lord Izumi-no-kami arrived at our domain with his full retinue assembled. Accordingly, one patrol ashigaru was stationed at the Saigo lodging and one at the Tsunomachi exit.

One - Today at half past the hour of Ku (around 1:30 PM), he arrived at Hannyaji Temple and then entered Sakuraoka. At that time there was communication from our side, and around the hour of Yatsu (around 2-3 PM) he entered Sakuraoka. For dismounting, according to proper protocol, he dismounted within the gate.

One - In the lower reception room of the main entrance, Lord Tosho and Lord Tokujiro were seated together. In front of them by the north screen were Lord Zensaemon and Lord Kuranosuke (others were absent due to illness at the time). Next were the chief inspectors, administrators, counselors, inner attendants, Nishioka headmen, and general inspectors - all had audience. From the senior retainers down, Kashima was at his side.

　　　　　　September 19th
1. Miyaji Shingoemon, who had been appointed as御用人 (attendant official) and was scheduled to accompany [his lord] to Kyoto, was delayed due to illness, but preparations were finally completed, so he departed today. On this occasion, Matsukuma Isen accompanied him, and since Kitajima Yohaku was also going to Kyoto for studies, they departed together.
1. For this month's prayers, Iwakura Temple went out, and at Komatsu-ma, the customary two nights and three days of ritual execution began today.
　　　　　September 20th

　　　　　Same month, 21st day
1. The squad leaders, chief retainers, and all samurai were summoned today, and matters regarding this autumn's administrative methods and the unified salary distribution were communicated by Lord Zensaemon.

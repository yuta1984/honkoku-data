　　　　March 3rd

　　Messengers from Ō-dono-sama, Mother Dowager-sama, Kazu-hime-sama, and Hisakane-sama were all served by Nishimaru.
1. Regarding the formal ceremony on this day, due to illness, only those who offered congratulations in the inner quarters - namely relatives, senior retainers, and those who made New Year's greetings - were granted audience in the outer sitting room.

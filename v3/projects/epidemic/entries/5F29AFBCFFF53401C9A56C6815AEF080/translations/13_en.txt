　　　　　February 29th
1. Regarding the wedding ceremony: As congratulatory ceremonies for the wedding gifts that were recently presented to both castles, messengers were dispatched today. One set of ceremonial gifts has records kept with the wedding ceremony office.
　　　　　　　　　　　　Hishū-sama's messenger: Takagi Itayū
　　　　　　　　　　　　Tanshū-sama's messenger: Koga Jūbei
　　　　From others: attached messengers
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Two spear-bearing attendants with free hands
The above messengers received direct audience at the audience hall, after which appropriate food and drink were served in Komatsu-no-ma. Companionship was provided by the intermediary officials. The two attendants received only sake in the great hall. The supervisors and foot soldiers received no special treatment.
　　　　Last day of February
1. For Shōkōzan temple: incense offering on behalf of the lord, performed by the intermediary official.
　　　　March 1st
1. Regarding the departure: It was scheduled for the 5th of this month and notification was duly submitted. However, since the funds were not ready, the departure was postponed under the pretext of illness. Today, notification was also sent to the main family.

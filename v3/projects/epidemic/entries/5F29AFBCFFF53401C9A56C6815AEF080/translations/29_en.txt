April 16th
1. Due to drought, rain-praying rituals have been conducted at Ganzōji Temple in recent days, but since there has been no rainfall at all, prayer rituals were ordered again at Ganzōji Temple. From tomorrow, five pieces of silver were provided as payment for five nights and five days of ceremonies. Furthermore, regarding village matters, orders were given to the village headman of Sengoku, instructing that prayers be offered at the shrines under his jurisdiction according to each one's wishes. Naturally, as assistance from above, 1 kan-mon of copper coins each was granted to each district and each village headman.

1. An epidemic has been spreading in Eriyama Village since some time ago, and it was reported that dozens of people fell ill and died. Therefore, three mountain ascetics (yamabushi) were ordered to perform purification rituals in the same village. Accordingly, the people of Eriyama Village were also ordered to make vigil visits to Gion Shrine as had been previously requested and arranged.

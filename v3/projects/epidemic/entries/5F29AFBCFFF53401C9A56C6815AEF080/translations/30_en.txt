April 20th
1. Dutch people traveling downward stayed overnight in Ushizu tonight, so the officials went to the same place as usual.
1. Performed the intermediary service for incense offering on behalf (of the lord) at both temples.

April 21st
1. The rain-praying rituals at Ganzōji Temple concluded today, so three officials of the current administration along with the inspector, treasury officials, district officials, and village officials made a barefoot pilgrimage to Tenzan Shrine. The three chief retainers contributed one ryō of silver each, while others contributed one ryō of silver by boat. One thousand people from within the domain were ordered to make a barefoot pilgrimage. Heavy rain began in the afternoon.

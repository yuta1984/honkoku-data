October 16th

Same, 17th
1. Today there was a summons from the senior retainer at the Second Compound, so Lord Zenzaemon went out and was handed a written document.

Regarding Lord Kaga-no-kami, word has come from Edo that there is an official notice he will be appointed to a position next spring. However, due to the sudden expenses involved, it would be possible to make internal appeals to the officials in Edo to have him excluded from this appointment if possible.
However, matters of the public administration are difficult to predict, and if he is forcibly appointed, it will be a duty that must absolutely be fulfilled, so preparations should be made internally from now. If arrangements cannot be made at that time,
appropriate assistance from family assets could be provided, but the treasury has been extremely strained for years, and on top of that, the Osaka waterfront operations unexpectedly collapsed with several hundred kan of superior silver involved, requiring stabilization measures...

   For the above personnel, packed lunch boxes were provided from Nishimaru from noon on the 8th until today.
1. For the proxy incense offering, Lord Sonoda Zensaemon; for the incense donation, twenty bundles of white hemp were offered to the temple.
1. For His Lordship's proxy incense offering, the head retainer served; the incense donation was likewise twenty bundles.
1. Visits to the hall by relatives and senior retainers are at their own discretion.
1. Today, a prohibition on killing living beings was ordered throughout the domain.
1. From Lord Hishū and Lord Tanshū, there were proxy incense offerings but no incense donations.
1. Since Shinshō Temple arranged the memorial service on their own, one silver piece and six to of rice were offered to the temple as tea ceremony fees.

October 10th
1. Hashimoto Jisaemon, Tateishi Yoichi, and Kurihara Gisaemon returned from Osaka yesterday evening.
1. For Lord Kōgakuin's proper memorial day, a proxy incense offering messenger was sent to Mount Matsuo.

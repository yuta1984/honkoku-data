One - From the beginning of this month, after continuous rain, there was heavy rainfall last night, resulting in extensive flood damage in various places throughout the domain territory. Reports of this have come from the villages.

August 7th
One - For inspection of flood-damaged rice fields, Lord Sukezaemon along with counselors, chief inspectors, and rural officials went out.

One - Prayers were ordered at Hannyaji Temple and two pieces of ceremonial silver were provided. The reason for this is unknown.

August 8th
One - Lord Izumi-no-kami is expected to arrive today or tomorrow. We had previously informed his advance messenger, and word came that he will arrive tomorrow.

One - Regarding his arrival tomorrow, one person from the middle pages has been sent to Ote-machi from today to arrange for lodging accommodations and prepare for his stay. Sagara Juronosuke met with him to discuss the arrangements.

One - The official lodging will be at Hannyaji Temple.

　　　　　　March 12th
1. Yoshitomi Saburōbei was relieved of his official duties.
1. Tonight again, officials of all ranks attended and there was deliberation on simplification measures.
1. Served as intermediary for incense offering on behalf of [the lord] to Shōkōzan temple.
　　　　　　The 13th
1. The regular assembly attendance proceeded as usual.
　　　　　　The 14th
　　　　　　The 15th
1. On the occasion of his lordship's official birthday, served as intermediary for proxy worship at Tenzan shrine, and made an offering of three monme of silver to the shrine.
1. Sukesaemon-sama conveyed that Tokushima Hachiemon was appointed to the current summer duty at Takashima.
1. A peasant named Jōhachi from Tateiwa village in Yamashiro district has an elderly mother, and it was heard that Jōhachi and his wife, children, and younger brothers all practice filial piety, so as a reward for the household, two kan-mon of copper coins were bestowed.

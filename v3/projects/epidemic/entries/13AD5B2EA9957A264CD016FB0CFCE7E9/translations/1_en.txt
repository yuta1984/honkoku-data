　　　Method for Making Odor Eliminators
Mix 25 monme of green vitriol (available at drug stores) with 1 kan-me of sawdust, and
mix them well together. Sprinkle this mixture in all places with bad odors - latrines (should be sprinkled
every time after defecation), garbage dumps, etc., and it will undoubtedly
eliminate bad odors immediately.
Also, it is good to mix 300 monme of green vitriol with 1 to of water, mix them well together, and
sprinkle this in places with bad smells.

　　　〇Precautions for Cholera Prevention
Cholera is a contagious disease, so one must always pay careful attention to food and drink, and if one
suffers from digestive disorders or diarrhea, one must quickly call a physician and take medicine. However, if through poor health management
one eats bad food and damages the intestines and stomach, this becomes a medium for cholera,
so if one pays careful attention to the following regarding food and drink, not to mention all other matters, and always
takes care to keep the intestines and stomach strong, there will be no worry of contracting this disease.

〇All food should be easily digestible, so for fish and the like, it is best to consume
light, fresh items.

〇Duck, goose, pork, small fish, clams, octopus, sardines, gizzard shad, dotted gizzard shad, salted foods, and dried foods are bad. Especially
tuna, bonito, crab, and shrimp are extremely bad.

〇Beef, veal, mutton, chicken, and eggs are said to be good, but even these
should not be eaten in large quantities. Also, one must not eat anything even slightly old.

　　　Theory of Vital Functions
Two Bodies
　Although the varieties of all things in the universe are extremely numerous, when these are unified, they can be divided into two bodies. One of these is called the "organic body" (bewerkt organismus). Animals and plants constitute this category. They are equipped with mechanisms, harbor vital force, and are subject to life and death. They obtain nourishment from different types of substances, undergo internal transformation, and thereby form their physical substance.

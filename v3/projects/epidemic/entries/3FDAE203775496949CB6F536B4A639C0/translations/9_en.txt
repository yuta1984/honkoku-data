The fever varies in severity from mild to severe. In severe cases, patients suffer from delirium and incoherent speech. The face becomes red, the eyes swell with tears flowing, there is headache, throat pain, coughing, sneezing, vomiting with phlegm, vexation and thirst, and whole-body pain. For these symptoms, regardless of whether the constitution is deficient or excessive, the universally used medicine is Sanhuang Xiexin Tang (Three Yellows Heart-Draining Decoction). If initially there is moisture on the body with sweating and diarrheal bowel movements, this is very good—it is a favorable symptom. There is no need to give Three Yellows Decoction. Now, for symptoms with diarrheal bowel movements, or when using Three Yellows causes two to three episodes of diarrhea, Paidoku Tang (Toxin-Expelling Decoction) should be given.

Sanhuang Xiexin Tang (Three Yellows Heart-Draining Decoction)
　Rhubarb 1 monme　　Scutellaria 1 monme　　Coptis 5 bu
　For the above three ingredients, add 1.5 gō of water, boil well, add all medicines and reduce to 1 gō. Take twice daily. Reduce by half for children.

Paidoku Tang (Toxin-Expelling Decoction)
　Burdock seed 7 bu　Schizonepeta 3 bu　　Notopterygium　　Ligusticum
　Scutellaria　　Saposhnikovia　　Pueraria　5 bu each　Bupleurum 3 bu
　Licorice 1 bu
For the above eight ingredients, add 1.5 gō of water and reduce to 1 gō. Take all at once. Reduce by half for children.

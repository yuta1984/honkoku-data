　　　　Theory of Causation

Measles is a disease that people contract once in their lifetime, just like smallpox. Smallpox is caused by congenital fetal toxin in the blood portion, which manifests when exposed to epidemic pestilence. Measles is caused by congenital fetal toxin in the qi portion, and this too develops according to atmospheric influences around the age of twelve

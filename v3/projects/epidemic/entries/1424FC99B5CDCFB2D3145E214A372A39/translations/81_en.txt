May 26th
　　　Same [month] 27th
One, for the third memorial service of Lord Tōten'in
　held at Lord Sakon's residence,
　incense offerings were presented by those serving as common samurai
　Ceremonial offering: one bundle of incense sticks
　　　Same [month] 28th

One, for the Mizukami-yama Fudō-e (Fudō ceremony)
　Proxy worship　performed by those serving as intermediaries
　Proxy worship from Her Ladyship (Gozen-sama) and Seimei'in-sama
　performed by those serving as common samurai
　The monetary offerings for worship come from each individual's own funds

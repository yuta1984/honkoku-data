October 9th
　　Same day, 10th
One. The 150th anniversary memorial service for Lord Kōgakuin
　　The Buddhist memorial service from the day before yesterday, the 8th,
　　until today was conducted at Mount Matsuo.
　　Accordingly, the following:
　One. Memorial service provisions:

　　　　　　　　　5 bales of rice
　　　　　　　　　7 pieces of silver
　　　　　　　　　30 loads of firewood
　　　　　　　　　30 laborers (fumaru)
　　　　　　　　　10 laborers (onaji)
One. In addition to the memorial service provisions, 300 monme of silver
　　was provided as construction funds.

Five koku each were donated, and accordingly
　both temples were summoned to Sakuraoka, where the matter was conveyed
　between Zenzaemon-dono and Komatsu. The written notification
　from the temple and shrine office is as follows:
　　This time, five koku of rice will be
　　donated in perpetuity to your temple. Therefore,
　　this is recorded as such for future years. The above.

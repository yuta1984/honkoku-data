Eighteenth day of the sixth month
One, regarding the memorial service for Lord Honryo-in on his death anniversary,
　to Shoko-zan temple
　Incense offering on behalf (of the lord)　performed by the chief retainer
　Memorial offering　one bundle of incense
　　　Same, nineteenth day
One, regarding the foot soldier Ohara Yashichi, who was ordered to procure urgent silver for official use

　He accomplished the procurement swiftly,
　and as a reward for his efforts,
　he was promoted to ashigaru (foot soldier) status and granted
　five koku of rice stipend,
　as communicated by the current official in charge.
　The official notification document reads as follows:
　　　　　　Foot soldier Ohara Yashichi
　　All members of the retainer band have fallen into great hardship

September 8th
Same 9th
One. As there was a notification from Saga as follows, a notification is issued:

Senior Councilor Matsudaira Izumi-no-kami-sama
suffered from illness and despite medical treatment
passed away on the 19th of last month, as has been reported

Therefore, immediately from tomorrow the 10th until
for a period of three days, throughout the domain peaceful conduct
is ordered, with the condition that noh chanting, dancing,
musical instruments, etc. be suspended. However, construction work
is permissible under these conditions, and the current official stated that this matter should be
communicated thoroughly and carefully to all departments. Above
Ox year, September 8th　　Ninomaru

1. From Hishū-sama:
　There is urgent business for Gozen-sama,
　so he requested that [the lord] come
　as soon as possible. Therefore, this evening at dusk,
　with attendants assembled, [the lord] will depart
　from dawn.
1. Kagiyama Kanbei went out to

　Sakuraoka today to pay his respects,
　so food and drink were served in the Komatsu room, and
　there was a greeting from the head clerk Nagahashi
　Taisuke, who accompanied [the meal].
　From the treasury official, he also received
　200 hiki in money. His offerings were as follows:
　1. Folding fans　one box
　1. Sake and delicacies

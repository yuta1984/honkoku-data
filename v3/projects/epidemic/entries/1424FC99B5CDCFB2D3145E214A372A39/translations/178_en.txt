1. From Kyoto by express town messenger this morning
　A document box arrived
　Regarding Lady Chika-hime, as has been
　reported repeatedly, she was ill, and around the middle
　of last month she had recovered somewhat,
　but from around the 20th of the same month her condition
　deteriorated severely, and despite various medical treatments

　and every effort being made, ultimately the treatment
　was unsuccessful, and on the 23rd of the same month at the
　hour of the Monkey (3-5 PM) she passed away, as reported
1. Due to the aforementioned passing, for the peaceful order of the domain

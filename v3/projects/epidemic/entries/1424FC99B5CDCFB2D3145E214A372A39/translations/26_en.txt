2nd month, 3rd day
　　　Same, 4th day
One: Regarding the incident in Shimabara domain last year
　An envoy was dispatched from our domain
　As thanks for this, envoy Katayama Daini
　With an entourage of 24 people above and below came to our locality
　Upon receiving advance notice of this, lodging arrangements
　
　Ushizu Shinmachi, Kanegae Seizaburō's
　Residence was arranged as accommodation, so to this residence
　For inspection, ashigaru [foot soldiers] were assigned, with food and drink
　As stated above, for lodging arrangements, junior inspector
　Noguchi Takeemon was dispatched, and of course food and drink
　Were provided as a courtesy from our domain, and then
　The next day, the 5th, the envoy performed his duties, and of course the envoy quarters

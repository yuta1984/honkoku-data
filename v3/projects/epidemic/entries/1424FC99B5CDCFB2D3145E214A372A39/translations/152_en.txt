September 28th
One. As Lord Hiraga Shikibu-shōyū, the returning Nagasaki magistrate, rested in Ushizu in his capacity as construction accounting officer, a messenger was dispatched to pay respects, and kudzu powder was presented as a gift, one box each. In connection with this, district administrators and other officials were dispatched.

One. Regarding the festivals of Honjo Shrine and Yoka Shrine, no proxy worship was sent as [the domain] is currently refraining from dispatching such representatives.

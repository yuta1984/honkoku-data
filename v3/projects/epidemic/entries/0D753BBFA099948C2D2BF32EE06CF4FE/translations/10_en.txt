Article 19: Every morning, one should rise early and observe the color of one's urine. Upon waking, everyone is still drowsy and may not notice, but this is the most important point to pay attention to. If the urine color has changed even slightly to yellow or red, one must absolutely not eat breakfast. If there is any change in the color of urine, no matter what else needs to be done, one should immediately see a doctor and

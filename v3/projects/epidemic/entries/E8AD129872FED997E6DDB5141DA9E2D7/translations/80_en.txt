- Dandelion (tanpopo)　Non-toxic. It effectively resolves food poisoning. It disperses stagnant qi and removes heat toxins.
　　　　　It may be used during and after smallpox without problem. It should be well boiled
　　　　　and mixed with salted miso before giving. It also effectively disperses breast swelling in women.

Therefore, when the smallpox period arrives, although there are good and bad prohibitions according to the symptoms, it is difficult to write about everything exhaustively. However, we roughly select the dietary prohibitions during and after smallpox as an aid in smallpox treatment. Indeed, during the hundred days of smallpox, one must be cautious and abstain, and must not carelessly give [forbidden foods]. From past experience, children from wealthy families rarely become disabled even if they develop residual toxicity after smallpox. Children from poor families often become disabled due to the transformation of residual toxicity after smallpox. This is all due to the fault of not carefully observing the prohibitions. Do not stubbornly insist on feeding them [these foods]. Parents and attendants should be very careful and abstain.

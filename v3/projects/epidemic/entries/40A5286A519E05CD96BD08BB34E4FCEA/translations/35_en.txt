Notes on the Treatise on Warm Epidemics, Volume 2

　　On Various Qi　《Classified Collection Volume 1《5 Back》》
Mountain mists, miasmatic qi, southern ridge toxic fog《1 Front》　According to the Biography of Ma Yuan in the Book of the Later Han, Ma Yuan led
　over 2,000 large and small tower ships and more than 20,000 warriors to attack the remnant
　party of the Nine Commanderies rebel Zheng Ce, including Du Yang and others. From Wugong to Jufeng, they beheaded and captured over 5,000 people, completely pacifying
　the southern ridges.《Note: Qiao refers to the mountain ridges》In the twentieth year of autumn, they returned triumphantly to the capital, but the military officials suffered from miasmatic epidemics, with
　deaths reaching four out of ten.
Wild kudzu《1 Back》　According to the Divine Farmer's Classic of Materia Medica, Gouwu, also called wild kudzu, has a pungent and warm taste, grows in mountain
　valleys, treats metal wounds, convulsions during nursing, malevolent possession, wind cough, rebellious ascending qi, and edema, and kills demonic parasitic poisons.

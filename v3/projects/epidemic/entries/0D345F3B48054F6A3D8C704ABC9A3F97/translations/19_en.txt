Incense offering: one bundle of incense sticks

Nineteenth day of the first month
Item: For New Year's congratulations, Lord Yamato visited today.
He proceeded to Hannyaji Sakuraoka at the middle lodging, then in the reception room
sake was served along with one soup dish, two side dishes, and one appetizer. Lord Ōkura served as companion,
Kitashima gave greetings, three-tiered serving dishes were used, and hemp ceremonial dress was worn.
He proceeded to the council members' quarters, and after the formal audience, he privately
departed and visited the new residence of Ana■ Azumi.
For the accompanying samurai, sake was served with two types of side dishes
in the Komatsu room, with the palace guards serving as companions.

Item: This month's prayers at Iwakuraji temple in the Komatsu room
will be conducted for two nights and three days starting today, as is customary.

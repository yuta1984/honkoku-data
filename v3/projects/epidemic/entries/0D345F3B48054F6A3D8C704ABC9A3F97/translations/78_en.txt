5th month, 2nd day
One　[His Lordship] attended the regular assembly as usual

Same month, 3rd day
One　The representative to Muryō-ji temple was served by a messenger attendant
One　An official letter from Osaka arrived by town courier

Same month, 4th day
One　[His Lordship] went out to various places on official business
One　To inquire about the Young Lord's eye condition, Matsukuma Hoan
　　came to the inner palace again today. On this occasion, consultation meals
　　and other refreshments were served
One　The chief priest (hōin) of Hannya-ji temple, who has been ill since some time ago,
　　has become even more seriously ill recently, so for convalescence
　　he withdrew to Inasa from last night

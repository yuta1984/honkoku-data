4th month, 14th day
Same, 15th day
One　The Chief Priest (Hōin) of Hannya-ji temple has been ill for a long time, and since there are no signs of recovery at all, he was granted seven bu of ginseng from the stock available in the treasury

One　On this day, various parties attended for the congratulatory ceremony

　　　　4th month, 16th day
One　The official correspondence to Edo and Osaka was completed today, and the messenger to deliver it to Kokura is the foot soldier Mutō Shinroku

One　Both princesses made a pilgrimage to the Goma Hall on foot this evening after dusk

4th month, 25th day
One　As Lord Chikuzen-no-kami was traveling to Nagasaki and staying tonight in Ushizu,
　　the fire brigade chief Seki Ichinoshin served in attendance.
　　Additionally, various officials from the district administration were dispatched as usual.
　　Of course, there were no official messengers.

Same, 26th day
One　The representative to Shōkōzan was served by a messenger attendant
One　Regarding the Dharma Seal of Iwakura-ji temple, he had previously been appointed
　　from Saga to serve at the Goma Hall in Edo, and today he departed from here

Same, 27th day
One　Regarding Lord Manhachi, we received word that he has changed his name
　　and has been renamed Lord Chikara

Same, 28th day

Following the completion of a thorough investigation, the following message came from Saga:
　　　　　　　Noguchi Shinnosuke, retainer of Lord Kii-no-kami, and
　　　　　　　　　　　Shimomura Uemon
The above persons shall be questioned in the presence of the senior retainers,
therefore on the coming 26th day at the hour of six at dawn, accompanied by colleagues and relatives at the appointed time
they shall appear at the council chamber, and even if currently ill,
in this matter there shall be no delay and they must forcibly appear, this message to be conveyed earnestly.
　　　　　　　　　　　　Inoue Bunnai
As the above message came from Saga, accompanied by colleagues from the same group, [they] appeared today.

1. This evening from around dusk, a Gion festival was established at the seaside tea house, and at this time relatives, senior retainers, and others including those stationed at Sakuraoka, attendant samurai, as well as district officials, hunting officials, mountain officials, and companions were ordered to participate in worship.

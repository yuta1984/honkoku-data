　　　June 27th
1. From Lord Tanshu, as congratulations for his arrival, messengers Tasaburo and 
   Kuroemon were sent. At this time, flying fish, beer, and other items were served to the messengers at Komatsu-ma, and accompaniment was provided by the attendant.
1. Regarding Shimomura Uemon, he was summoned yesterday by the Saga council chamber and 
   appeared, but there was no business for him,
   so he returned as he was.

　　　　Same day, 28th
1. Nozaki Yazaemon, the Hamasaki storehouse keeper, came today to pay his respects 
   and inquire after [his lord's] health. Accordingly, he was granted a meal as customary and
   one kan-mon of bronze coins. At this time, he made the following offerings:
　　　　　One box of three folding fans
　　　　　One serving of ten abalones

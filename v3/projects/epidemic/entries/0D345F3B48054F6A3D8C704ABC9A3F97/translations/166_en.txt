October 6th
One　At the morning hour of seven, departed for mountain hunting with full retinue
　　Same day, 7th
One　Regarding Yokoo Heizaemon's matter, due to official business for the loan silver office
　he was dispatched to Nagasaki from today
　　Same day, 8th
One　The kyōgen performance that was arranged in Ogi town this spring was again
　summoned to Sakuraoka, and the most honored lords viewed it
　On this occasion, five shō of sake
　and one type of delicacy were bestowed upon the kyōgen actors, naturally provided from the kitchen
　　October 9th
One　Past the morning hour of seven, departed for bird hunting with full retinue

April 8th
One　Mizumachi Umanosuke, whose private financial circumstances have become extremely difficult in recent years, petitioned for property settlement as he could no longer maintain his hereditary succession. When this matter was reported to Edo, word came that his request was granted as petitioned. Accordingly, he was summoned and this was communicated by the council advisors

One　Regarding the Young Lord's illness, prayers were again performed today at the viewing hall by five yamabushi practitioners

　　　　Same day, 9th
One　To examine the Young Lord's eye condition, Matsukuma Hoan again attended today. On this occasion as well, a meal of one soup and three dishes with sake was served, as has been the custom recently

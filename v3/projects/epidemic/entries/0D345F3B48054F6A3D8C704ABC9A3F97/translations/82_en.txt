5th month, 15th day
One　Hoshino Niuemon, who had been dispatched to Osaka on business for the domain loan office,
　　developed a wind illness and suddenly required medical treatment, so he requested leave,
　　which was granted as requested, and he returned to the domain, arriving today
　　　　Same month, 16th day
One　This morning an express messenger arrived from Edo,
　　reporting that his lordship departed Edo on the 2nd day

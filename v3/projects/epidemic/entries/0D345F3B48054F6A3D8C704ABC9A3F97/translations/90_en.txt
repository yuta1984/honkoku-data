One　For Okume-sama's yakuiri (unlucky age), a prayer petition was submitted from his lordship as follows:
　　　One　Fudo-son (Acala)　　Three goma fire rituals
　　　One　Konpira Shrine　　One hundred sacred lanterns
　　　One　Inari Shrine　　Release of two kinds of fish and birds
　　　Accompanied by salt-cured sea bream, one fish.
　　　June 2nd
One　The ceremonial gathering proceeded as usual with attendance.
　　　Same day, 3rd
One　A messenger from Osato arrived at dawn.
　　His lordship arrived at Osato on the 1st of last month, and departed from that place on the 2nd.
　　This news was reported, and accordingly preparations for welcome at Todoroki-gi

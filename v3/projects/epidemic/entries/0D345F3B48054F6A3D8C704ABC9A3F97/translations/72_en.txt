4th month, 12th day
One　Toda Saburōbei went as proxy representative to Engaku-in-sama

One　Regarding the matter of Iwakura-ji temple, the monk was recently appointed from Saga to serve at the Goma Hall in Edo, and will depart soon. Therefore, His Lordship bestowed 1,000 hiki in gold as a farewell gift. The messenger duty was performed by a foot soldier

　　　　4th month, 13th day
One　The regular meeting was attended as usual

One　Regarding the desire to employ Murata Juntetsu in the household retinue, a request was made to Saga some time ago, and it has been approved as requested. The detailed matters are recorded in the Saga correspondence ledger

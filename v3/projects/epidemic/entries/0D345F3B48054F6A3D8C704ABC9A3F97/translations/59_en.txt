One: To inquire about the young lord's eye condition, Matsukuma Hoan
　　came to the inner quarters again today. On this occasion,
　　refreshments and sake were served for consultation
　　　　March 19th
One: Regarding Lord Yamato's wedding being successfully completed,
　　we had previously sent a messenger from our side to offer congratulations.
　　Today a messenger bearing return courtesies was sent
　　from their side. The celebratory gifts that arrived were as follows:
　　　　One　Dried squid　Seven strings　　One box
　　　　One　Money in lieu of sake barrel　　Two hundred hiki
　　　The above
　　　　To our Lord　　From Lord Yamato

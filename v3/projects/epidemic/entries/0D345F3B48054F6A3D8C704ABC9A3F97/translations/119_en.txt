At Sakuraoka, performed service for the sacred spirits as customary
Details are recorded in the Bon duty allocation pouch

1. From Sōchiji Temple, as customary, one monk from the evening of the 13th
   until tomorrow the 15th will remain stationed at Sakuraoka, so as payment for one tan of bleached cloth,
   100 hiki of gold coins were bestowed

1. To His Lordship and Her Ladyship, from the three young lords,
   by boat for the celebration of living souls, two shō of sake vessels

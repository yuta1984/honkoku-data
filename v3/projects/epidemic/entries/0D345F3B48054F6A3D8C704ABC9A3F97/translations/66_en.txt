March 28th
One　Today, Lord Bizen-no-kami departed from his domain residence
　　for sankin-kotai service, and as he passed through Ushizu, messenger
　　Seki Ichinoshin, representing the retainer household Deputy Rusu Hachirōzaemon,
　　Onoda Tōzō, Shō Tōbei, and various other district officials
　　were dispatched.
One　Her Ladyship also concluded matters with a single messenger.
　　　Same, 29th
One　To Shōkōzan, a messenger served as the lord's representative.
One　For visiting the ailing Lord Kisaburō,
　　Lord Tanshū today sent messenger Noda Kanbei.
　　On this occasion, the messenger was served
　　a meal of one soup and three dishes with sake in the Komatsu room.

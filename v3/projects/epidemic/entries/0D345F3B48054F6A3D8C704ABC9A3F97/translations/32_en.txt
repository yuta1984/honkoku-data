February 9th
　　　　　Same, 10th
1. A messenger on duty served as representative to Mount Matsuo
1. As Kizaburo-sama's illness shows no improvement at all,
   prayer ceremonies were ordered to be conducted at Hannya-ji temple
   for his peaceful recovery. For this purpose, ten monme of silver
   was allocated as payment
1. Various parties attended to inquire about his well-being
　　　　　February 11th
1. A messenger on duty served as representative to Mount Shoko
1. Official correspondence from Edo (with Osaka noted to the right) arrived
   via Hakomachi express courier
1. Various parties attended to inquire about his well-being

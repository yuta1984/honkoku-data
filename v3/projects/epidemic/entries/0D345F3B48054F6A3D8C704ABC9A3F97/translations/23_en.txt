Twenty-seventh day of the first month
Item: Document boxes for official business from Edo and Osaka arrived
　via the return journey of the rice transport supervisor

Item: Tanshū-sama crossed through the Nishime route this morning for hunting
　in the mountains. As customary, various officials from the district office
　were dispatched to Ushizu

Same day, twenty-eighth
Same day, twenty-ninth

Item: Gozen-sama visited Shōkōzan Sairei Temple for temple worship
　at half past the fifth hour with full attendant retinue

Same day, thirtieth

Item: At Shōkōzan, Kashiro Mehei offered one bundle of incense sticks
　as incense offering on behalf [of the lord] for Kenshōin-sama

Second day of the twelfth month
One　[His lordship] attended the regular assembly as usual.
One　At the hour of Yatsu (around 2 PM), with his retinue assembled, he went out bird hunting.
　　　　Third day of the same month
One　A messenger on duty served as official representative to Muryōji Temple.
One　Zenbei of Hiranoya, a silver merchant from Osaka, returned home today.
　　　　Fourth day of the same month
One　At the hour of Nanatsu (around 4 PM), with his retinue assembled, he went out for a mountain hunt.
One　Heitadayū Kido was appointed to the hereditary position
　　at the Minaki guard post.
　　　　Fifth day of the same month
One　At the hour of Mutsu (around 6 PM), with his retinue assembled, he went out bird hunting.

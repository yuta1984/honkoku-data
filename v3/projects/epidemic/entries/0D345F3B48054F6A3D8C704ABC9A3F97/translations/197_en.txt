Sixth day of the twelfth month
One　Lord Yamato came to this estate today for mountain hunting.
　　During this visit, he stopped at Hannyaji Temple on both his way there and back,
　　but did not enter Sakuraoka. The complete preparations for the hunt are
　　recorded in a separate ledger. The complete meal arrangements are
　　recorded in the kitchen records. On this occasion, one person named Nakano
【bound pages cannot be transcribed】from the main family's retainers was brought along.
　　　Seventh day of the same month
One　After the hour of Mutsu at dawn (around 6 AM), with his retinue assembled, he went out bird hunting.
One　The house of a peasant named Kataemon in Nishiyamada Village caught fire
　　the night before last and burned down completely.
　　　Eighth day of the same month
One　New Year prayers will be conducted for two nights and three days starting today
　　at the Komatsu room in Iwakuraji Temple.

November 9th
One　Regarding Donkei, today he was admitted to Eisho-an temple
One　Since Lord Kisaburo had made a vow to Dazaifu Tenmangu when he fell ill this spring, and the votive picture tablet (ema) marking the fulfillment of that vow has been sent, Mitsuno Kinzaemon was ordered to serve as proxy pilgrim
One　At the hour of seven, [the lord] went out for evening devotions with his retinue assembled
November 10th
One　A messenger official served as proxy representative to Mount Matsuo
One　Various parties attended for official duties
One　Her Ladyship tonight at the hour of seven with her retinue assembled to Hannyaji temple

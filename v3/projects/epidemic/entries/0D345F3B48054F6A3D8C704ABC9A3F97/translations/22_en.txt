Twenty-fifth day of the first month
Item: In order to settle the borrowed silver from Osaka in advance this time,
　prayers were conducted at Hannyaji Temple. For this purpose, five hundred me
　of twenty-mon coins were offered as prayer fees

Same day, twenty-sixth
Item: At Shōkōzan, Toda Saburōbei offered one bundle of incense sticks
　as incense offering on behalf [of the lord] for Kōtokuin-sama
Item: At the same temple, the same person offered the same
　as incense offering on behalf [of the lord] for Chōjuin-sama

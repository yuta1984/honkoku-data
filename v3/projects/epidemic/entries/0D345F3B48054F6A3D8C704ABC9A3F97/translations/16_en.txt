Item: Official correspondence from Edo arrived via the upper residence's messenger service

Thirteenth day of the first month
Item: For the customary religious observance, his lordship made his appearance as usual

Item: Regarding the female attendant Riu from Kyoto who was ordered to return last winter, foot soldier Oka Yagobei and manservant Shinzaemon were assigned as escorts to accompany her on the journey up to the capital, and they returned today

Fourteenth day of the first month
Item: Peasants from the Isahaya Kobunagoshi camp came to offer congratulations, so they were granted two kan-mon of copper coins according to precedent

Same day, the fifteenth
Item: On this day, various parties came to serve in celebration

Pure white ox hair《5 monme, roasted in a silver dish until it becomes ash, then powdered》　Cinnabar《2 monme, ground into powder》　Loofah stem base《3 monme, 3 sun from near the stem base, roasted and ground into powder》

Mix the above three ingredients and take with hot water in the early morning when smallpox is about to emerge. In severe cases, it is also good to use with honey water. The smallpox eruption becomes extremely mild.

Light Spot Powder (Keihan-san)《Also called Shōon-tan》
Loofah《3 sun from near the stem base, remove skin and seeds, char black and grind into fine powder》　Cinnabar《ground into powder: 3 bu》

Mix the above two ingredients, knead with sugar or honey, and take when smallpox has not yet emerged, then it becomes extremely mild.

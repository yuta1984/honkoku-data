　Finely powder these twelve ingredients on the winter solstice day, decoct cimicifuga, mix equal parts of bamboo sap, powder divine leaven and dissolve into a paste, knead with the previous powdered medicine to form pills, finely grind equal parts of cinnabar and realgar powder to coat the pills.
　When smallpox breaks out, take 2-3 pills with a decoction of bamboo leaves. This will surely prevent smallpox, and even if contracted, symptoms will be very mild.

Preparation of Human Waste Yellow: Scrape away the skin of large licorice root, cut green bamboo leaving one node, put the licorice inside the bamboo, tightly seal the bamboo opening to prevent water entry, and soak in feces for forty-nine days. Remove, wash and dry, take out the licorice from inside, expose to sunlight to dry, and powder finely.

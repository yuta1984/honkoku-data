Rainy Night Tales (Amayo-banashi)
Divine Efficacy Detoxifying Infant-Protecting Pills (Shinkō Shōdoku Hōei-tan)
　Taking one pill each on the spring equinox and autumn equinox every year will eliminate smallpox poison. If taken as described for three years, the poison will be completely eliminated and one will never contract smallpox in their lifetime.
　Hovenia dulcis vine (shade-dried, 15 monme), Rehmannia root, Burdock seeds (roasted), Hawthorn fruit (each)
　(10 monme) Angelica sinensis (washed with sake), Coptis (roasted with sake sprinkled on), Platycodon grandiflorus, Saposhnikovia divaricata, Schizonepeta

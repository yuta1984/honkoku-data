Preface to Questions and Answers for Beginners in Smallpox Medicine
Taking paulownia and catalpa trees in hand to nurture them. If people desire to make them live, they all know the methods to nourish them. How much more so would a loving father not love and raise his children? Nevertheless, longevity and early death are fates that are not the same. Even when planting paulownia and catalpa, differences arise depending on whether the soil is fertile or barren. Alas, how lamentable!

The four seas are at peace, and people bathe in the beneficent waves of virtuous transformation, cherishing their stable livelihood. Yet there are times when heavenly epidemics spread. People naturally worry about this. Among these, smallpox is particularly severe. Though it may be the circulation of heavenly mandate, it can be avoided through human intention, leading to a proper lifespan. The gentleman accepts it in due order.

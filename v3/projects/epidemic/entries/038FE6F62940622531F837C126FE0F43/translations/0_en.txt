【Title: Popular Measles Guide - Playfully Written by Shuntei, Kyōsuzume】
Well then, this epidemic illness. With measles,
examine each spot one by one. Children have
mild cases. Adults have severe ones. The crisis
comes on the eighth day, then toxins leave and dietary restrictions begin.
With Inari-san's divine blessing
naturally. Let's recover smoothly
as is common, Yoiyasa!

◯Foods that should not be eaten
- Cold foods - Raw foods - Five pungent plants (green onions, Chinese chives, smelly things, various spicy things)
- Fruits (peaches, pears, loquat, grapes are bad before recovery) - Vinegar, sake
- Fried tofu - Noodles - Roasted foods - Mushrooms - Glutinous rice
- Perilla - Water dropwort - Cucumber - Eggplant - Pumpkin - Fish and fowl
- Shellfish - Eggs - Salty foods - Green/grassy-smelling foods - Konjac

◯Foods that are good to eat
- Black beans - Red beans - Baked wheat gluten - Water chestnut - Lotus root - Kidney beans
- Chinese yam - Wild yam - Daikon radish - Dried gourd strips - Lily bulbs - White gourd - Kelp
- Wakame seaweed - Kudzu - Royal fern - Mizuame syrup - Burdock root - Miso

1. When the filter bed area is one square meter (3 shaku 6 sun in diameter), approximately 6 to (about 108 liters) can be filtered per hour
2. The fine sand layer is the most important part. Its thickness must not be less than 3 shaku (about 90cm)
3. It is advisable to remove 2-3 sun (6-9cm) of the surface layer of fine sand once a month and wash it well with clean water
4. Washing and replacing the entire fine sand layer once or twice a year is sufficient
5. In places where the cold is severe, it is necessary to keep the container always filled with water and wrap the exterior of the container with materials like straw matting to prevent it from freezing

Regarding all matters of variolation, laypeople often make mistakes by relying on their own solitary judgment or by following the instructions of physicians who do not practice variolation. Therefore, I present here the essential knowledge from beginning to end to teach the way to ensure complete safety. Those who wish to have variolation performed on their children must read this book and not let their minds be swayed by other matters.

　　　Symptoms

Initially, there are one or two episodes of diarrheal bowel movements, vomiting does not stop, legs and back experience convulsions, and the lower abdomen experiences mild pain or begins to hurt severely. The condition of the bowel movements is like pouring water—severe diarrhea that, after two or three episodes, suddenly weakens one's energy, makes the pulse feeble, causes the whole body to become cold, causes the eye sockets to become sunken, and turns the face pale.

【Left side】
The skin becomes like cold water, the tongue becomes as cold as ice and dry, the voice becomes low and hoarse, suddenly the mind becomes delirious, uttering slight delirium before death.

In severe cases, after one or two episodes of diarrheal discharge, the patient falls into the same kind of weakness as the previous symptoms and dies. The color of the stool sometimes involves discharging brownish-black material like corrupted blood. The stool color of patients in Meiji 10 (1877) was fundamentally like rice water, but we also observed many cases of semi-transparent, sticky, glue-like substances resembling boiled wheat gluten paste.

Volatile Powder (same as above formula) - Main indication: for rapid pulse and diarrhea, mental stupor, or picking at clothes and fumbling with bedding.

Musk (high grade), Camphor (high grade) - 4 mō each, Opium - 2 mō, White sugar - 2 sen

Grind the above four ingredients into fine powder, mix evenly, and take twice every thirty minutes.

For weak pulse, apply cardiac stimulant to the chest area. Steam preparations may also be used (formula appears in Chapter 5 of the lower volume).

For mental decline and weakness, rub camphor oil over the entire body (Note by Seki: Second volatile liniment may also be used. Formula appears in Chapter 2 of the lower volume).

For difficult-to-stop diarrhea, anti-diarrheal enema may be administered (formula appears in Chapter 6 of the lower volume).

For lower limb paralysis or stuporous state with difficulty awakening, apply white mustard paste to the calves (formula appears in Chapter 3 of the lower volume).

For persistent vomiting, volatile steam treatment should be applied to the gastric region.

Second Volatile Decoction (author's own formula) - Main indication: for weak rapid pulse, unresolved continuous fever, muscle spasms and tremors, delirium and fumbling movements, dry tongue, diarrhea and vomiting.

Patrinia - 2 sen, Snakeroot - 5 bu, Aconite - 2 bu, Calamus - 6 bu

Boil the above three ingredients with 2 gō of spring water to obtain 1 gō 5 shaku, add patrinia and boil again for 3-4 bubbling. Remove from heat, let settle for a short time, remove dregs, and take 6 shaku every hour, 12-13 times daily.

○For throat discomfort with preference for hot drinks, add 3-4 bu of fritillaria bulb.

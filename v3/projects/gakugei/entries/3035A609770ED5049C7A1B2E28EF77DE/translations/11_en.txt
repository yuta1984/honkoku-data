【Right Page, Upper Section】
Tan (驒) [Rensen - Linked Coin Pattern]: Types include rensen-ashige (linked coin dapple gray), hoshi-ashige (star dapple gray), ki-ashige (yellow dapple gray), and tora-ashige (tiger dapple gray)
In (駰): Black dapple gray
Botan-ashige: Has larger patterns than ordinary linked coin patterns

Horse Equipment:
Tail cover
Horse blanket
Large belt

【Right Page, Lower Section】
Sou (驄) [Ashige - Dapple Gray]: Dapple gray is a mixture of blue and white colors
Sai (䮨): White dapple gray, white with black hooves and mouth
Gou-sou: Sesame dapple gray, white with black mouth and leg tips
Goma-sou (Sesame dapple): Black mouth and hooves, hair is black at the root, white in the middle, and red at the tips

Saruki (Monkey Pole):
Belly rope
Used to control rearing, tied up to the monkey pole above the stable

【Left Page, Upper Section】
Iyo (騟) [Black Chestnut]: Types include chestnut, black chestnut, chichimi, jo, hime, koji, and gonda
Shiba-kurige (brushwood chestnut), kaba horse has plum-colored chestnut. Gen refers to chestnut horses with white bellies
Coat color is darker than deer coat; true red chestnut has only black legs and hooves

Such long hair is called nogami (wild hair) or eye-cover
The mane is the same color as the body
Hooves are black

Coloring: Burnt yellow ochre paint, shading with vermillion and ink, eyes and hooves are black
Shiba horses have raw crimson shading

【Left Page, Lower Section】
Ryu (騮) [Deer Coat]: Deer coat has a reddish body with black mane, light yellow color
U (crow): Black deer coat; Hyo: White deer coat
Crow horse: Black legs, chestnut-colored body, slightly red mouth
Deer coat and chestnut are related; chestnut is black, deer coat is pale yellow-red

Coloring: Yellow ochre, shading with vermillion and ink
Mouth is slightly red, shaded with ink, hair similarly drawn
Hooves are black

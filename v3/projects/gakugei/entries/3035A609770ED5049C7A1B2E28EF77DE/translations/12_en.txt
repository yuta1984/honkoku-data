【Right Page】
【Upper Section】
Ken (騝) [Hibari-ke - Skylark Coat]: Skylark coat, water skylark coat. Back hair is yellow.
Coloring: Using yellow ochre, shading with vermillion and ink, tail and mane shaded with light ink, drawn in with dark ink.

Backbone (spine)
Mirror stand bone, mirror joint. Hooves and mouth are black.

【Lower Section】
Haku (駁) [Buchi - Spotted]: Spotted horse (mottled), escaping cloud horse, white-headed, white-rumped horse.
Kou horse has a white belly. Hoof horse (four whites) has white hooves.
Chouba horse is white above the knees. Four-hagi is white below the knees.

Eyes, mouth, and hooves are black.

Coat colors include black and white, chestnut and white.

Mane (tategami), torigami, tatsunagami
The space between these is called Komatsuhara.

【Left Page】
【Upper Section】
Raku (雒) [Kami-shiro - Head White]: Black body with white mane.
Generally, for black parts of horses, ink is added to white pigment for painting.
Finishing with ink shading. Horse coat colors are not uniform and vary greatly, but there is no time to record all variations.

【Lower Section】
Setsu (䮕): White forehead, kaku, kaku, teki, hou
Taisei (moon forehead), Tekirojun (moon forehead)
Ryusou (flowing forehead). Coat color is black or chestnut.

Eyes colored with indigo
Mouth and hooves are black

The number of braids for the standing mane is twenty-eight, or sometimes said to be thirty-six.

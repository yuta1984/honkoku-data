Various academic subjects such as military science, astronomy, geography, and navigation, as well as foreign languages of various nations - if one studies and researches these during one's free time, they will greatly assist in the way of commerce. There is no doubt that one will achieve tangible results and attain social advancement and promotion. Indeed, the profession of merchants involves being fair and flexible, sharing wealth between oneself and others, and providing methods to exchange what is available with what is needed.

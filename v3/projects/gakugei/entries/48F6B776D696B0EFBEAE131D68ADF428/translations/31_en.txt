【Main Text】
Toad (hikigaeru). Centipede (mukade). Caterpillar (kemushi). Spider (kumo). Green caterpillar (aomushi). Flea (nomi). Louse (shirami), etc. All of these are born from eggs through moisture. They represent a fraction of a thousand forms. Various instruments and machines include: astronomical mirror (tenmon-kyou). Star observation mirror (kansei-kyou). Telescope (senri-kyou). Burning glass (hi-kyou).
Kaleidoscope (tahou-kyou). Microscope (kenbi-kyou).
Camera (shashin-kyou). Hot air balloon (keiki-kyuu).
Air pump (chuuki-ki). Globe (chikyuu-ki).
Armillary sphere (konten-gi). Weather vane (fuuu-shin).

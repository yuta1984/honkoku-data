Mirror of Daily Conduct for Ladies-in-Waiting

Now, rouge and eyebrow paint are tools for enhancing feminine beauty,
beloved in China and Japan. Beauties of every generation have never
gone without cosmetic powder, composing songs and poems about this. There-
fore, when women adorn themselves with white powder and rouge,
it is not merely to enhance their complexion.
This is feminine etiquette, so with that spirit
one should apply powder lightly, rouge and such
with proper understanding.
○Eyebrows, regardless of social rank, are now drawn with ink.
This follows the custom of courtesans of old.
The way of drawing eyebrows should be subtle, likened to distant mountain
mist, and also compared to the setting
crescent moon. Simply elegant and not osten-

　　Fine Varieties

●[37] Najimi Tofu　Grind the finest white miso well and thin it moderately with sake,
　　　cut tofu into appropriate sizes, soak for over an hour, then

【Left page】
　　　simmer over bunbu-bi (medium heat - neither too strong nor too weak). ○Serve with roughly chopped scallion whites,
　　　green chili peppers, and grated daikon radish. ○Karatsu miso plates or similar vessels are good.

●[38] Tsuto (Bundle) Tofu　Squeeze water well from tofu, mix with ground amazake,
　　　shape into rod form, wrap in bamboo mat, steam and cut into rounds.

●[39] Imadegawa Tofu　Lay kombu as base, simmer thoroughly with bonito dashi,
　　　sake and salt. Add soy sauce from the middle stage to adjust seasoning,
　　　thicken with kudzu starch, serve in bowls and sprinkle with crushed walnuts.

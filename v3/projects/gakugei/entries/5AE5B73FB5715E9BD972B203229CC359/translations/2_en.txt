Preface to One Hundred Tofu Delicacies
Fundamentally, curd is the transformation of grain particles, the metamorphosis of matter. The Creator presides over this. Humans, unlike other things, are capable of self-transformation. However, even though they can transform themselves, this too must be brought about by the Creator. And transformation is without limit. Just as dragons appear and disappear, and flowers and plants flourish and wither, the state of the world waxes and wanes accordingly, and human affairs rise and fall in response. Thus things gather and scatter, and there is nothing that does not change, no spirit that does not transform. Indeed, the arts of Huainan are from distant antiquity.

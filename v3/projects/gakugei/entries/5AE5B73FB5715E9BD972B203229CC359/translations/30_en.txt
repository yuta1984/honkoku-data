This method of grilling again is called dengaku (grilled tofu).

●[80] Chicken Egg Dengaku: Crack eggs and add a little soy sauce, sake, and salt, 
add a very small amount of vinegar, mix well, and spread on dengaku for grilling.
The degree is when it puffs up. ○ Serve with mustard seeds and grated wasabi.

[81] True Eight-Cup Tofu: Use silken scooped tofu with six cups water
and one cup sake, boil well, then add one cup soy sauce and boil again well,
then add tofu. The cooking method is like [97] Hot Water Yakko. Serve with grated daikon radish.

【Left page】
●[82] Tea Tofu: For ten blocks of tofu, use the amount of finest tea equivalent to one kin, 
boil the tea, and when it comes to a boil, remove the cloth texture from the tofu and add it, cook well
until it is dyed tea color. Separately boil tea and add it at the moment of brewing,
this should be done. Then squeeze out the tea. ○ Serve with light soy sauce broth, bonito flakes,
and julienned wasabi. Wasabi miso is also good.
▲ The preparation of wasabi miso: mix miso with white sesame and walnuts, grind well together
and set aside. When using, add grated wasabi.
○ Pepper miso is also good.

[83] Stone-Grilled Tofu: Originally grilled on stone, but simplified to use a pan.

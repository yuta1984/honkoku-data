●[45] Thread Noodle Tofu　Strain well through a sieve and add egg white as binding,
　　　spread Mino paper on a board, spread the tofu thinly with a knife,
　　　evenly without lumps, and pour boiling water with steam bubbles to cook through.
　　　Then soak in water, remove and cut very finely.
　　　△The method above, when rolled and grilled in a flat pan, is called Shibe Tofu.

●[47] Yam-topped Tofu　Grate mountain yam and grind well,
　　　make bonito dashi with a little soy sauce, saltier than usual, and bring to a gentle
　　　boil, scoop the grated yam with a large metal ladle and add it, serving when it
　　　puffs up softly. ○Cook [No. 100] True Udon Tofu in kudzu
　　　broth to proper consistency, drain the liquid and serve in warmed small Nara
　　　tea bowls, topping with the yam preparation above. The timing of cooking both pots
　　　is crucial, and it's best to have two people working. ○Sprinkle with ground
　　　pepper. ○Sprinkling finely ground aonori seaweed is also excellent.

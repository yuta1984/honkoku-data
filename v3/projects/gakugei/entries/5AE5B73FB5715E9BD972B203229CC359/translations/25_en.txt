●[62] Six-Sided Grilled Tofu　Cut one block into pieces about the size of quarters,
　　　cut into cubes, and grill all four sides plus top and bottom in a grilling pan. Of course, remove moisture,
　　　and put a little oil in the pan. Season according to taste

●[63] Tea Ceremony Tofu　Line the bottom of a large flat pot densely with bamboo grass leaves,
　　　arrange pieces of tofu (one block cut into about five pieces) densely on top,
　　　spread fukusa miso thickly over that, then lay more bamboo grass leaves,
　　　then tofu, then miso, repeating this process two or three


　　　times and simmer for more than half a day○ Serve in flat tea bowls,
　　　sprinkle with ground sanshō pepper. It's also good to serve with the bamboo grass leaves still in place
　　　▲ Alternatively, simmer all day in fukusa miso, then remove the miso and
　　　season as desired - this is called "草 (grass) tea ceremony tofu"

●[64] Sake Lees Sautéed Tofu　Mash tofu well and soften with aged sake,
　　　add seasoned aromatics and simmer○ For sliced ingredients use salted sea bream,
　　　salt-cured cod, or white whale meat; for poultry use wild goose or duck;
　　　add as accompaniments roasted chestnuts, wood ear mushrooms, fried matsutake mushrooms, etc.

　Enkōji Temple
　　Commonly known as
　　Fuji-dera (Wisteria Temple)

　In the garden, trellises are
　built around, and wisteria
　is trained to climb on them.
　The branches reach
　three to four shaku in length,
　and the flower colors are most
　beautiful and lustrous.

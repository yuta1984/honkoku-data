　　　【Text in box】West bank of Sumida River

　　Maichi is quiet, and the treetops
　　board the Imado Bridge.
　　Sharing an umbrella on the embankment,
　　an evening shower of changing fortunes.
　　When I think of you, the fine cloth
　　of old times when we did not meet
　　　　　　Right: A playful composition by Hanabusa Itchō

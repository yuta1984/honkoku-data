【Lower section】
When Yan Pingzhong went out one day,
the wife of the man who drove his chariot
peeked through the gap in the gate and
observed that her husband, the charioteer, was using a large parasol
and whipping the four-horse team,
looking spirited and self-
satisfied. When he
returned home, his wife
requested a divorce. When the charioteer asked for the
reason, his wife said, "Your master
Yanzi is less than five feet tall,
yet he serves as
prime minister of the state of Qi and his name is
known among the feudal lords. Today
when I look at my husband, though he is eight

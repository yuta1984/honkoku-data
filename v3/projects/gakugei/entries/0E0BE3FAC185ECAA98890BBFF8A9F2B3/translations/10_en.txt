[Right page, upper section]
○Small conger (kongiri) is a small version of sea eel (hamo). Its efficacy is the same as hamo.

○Halfbeak (sayori) is sweet in taste, neutral in nature, and non-toxic. Eating this prevents epidemic diseases. Needlefish (shingyo) is the same.

○Trout (masu) warms the stomach and harmonizes the body's center.

○Shrimp (ebi) treats abdominal masses, and is good when applied to smallpox. It invigorates yang qi and promotes milk flow. When children eat it, their legs become weak. Prawn (ka) is the same.

[Left page, upper section]
○Sea shrimp (umiebi) when made into vinegared salad (namasu) and eaten treats tooth decay and head sores. Red shrimp (kōka), dragon shrimp (ryūka), and sea shrimp (kaika) are the same.

○River shrimp (kaka) is freshwater shrimp. Commonly called tenaga-ebi (long-armed shrimp).

○Sauce shrimp (ami) is a type of small shrimp. Also called seedling shrimp (byōka), thread shrimp (senka), and mud shrimp (deika).

○Ice goby (shirouo) relaxes the body's interior, makes the stomach healthy, promotes water metabolism, and stops coughing.

○Mantis shrimp (shakunage) is a type of shrimp.

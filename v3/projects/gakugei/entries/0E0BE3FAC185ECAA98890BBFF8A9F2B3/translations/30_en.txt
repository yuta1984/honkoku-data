【Upper Section】
○Adzuki beans descend water qi, dispel pus and blood, promote urination, and treat abdominal distension and wasting thirst.

○Kidney beans (ajimame) harmonize the center, descend qi, stop vomiting, supplement the five organs, and resolve cholera and alcohol poisoning. Hyacinth beans, lentils, and lima beans have the same effects.

○Sesame increases vitality, grows muscle and flesh, strengthens sinews and bones, benefits the large and small intestines, and clarifies hearing and vision.

○Poppy removes wind toxins, expels pathogenic heat, treats phlegm, treats gastroptosis, and moistens thirst.

○Broad beans make the stomach comfortable and harmonize the internal organs. Also called horse beans.

○Corn (nanban-kibi) increases qi, harmonizes the center, stops diarrhea, stops cholera-related diarrhea and abdominal pain, and promotes urination.

○Sorghum (tō-kibi) warms the center, constricts the intestines and stomach, and treats cholera. Reed millet and bush clover millet have the same effects.

○Sword beans warm the center, descend qi, benefit the intestines and stomach, stop hiccups, strengthen the kidneys, and supplement vitality.

○Lima beans (hasshō-mame) regulate the center, strengthen the stomach, and promote urination. Also called raccoon beans.

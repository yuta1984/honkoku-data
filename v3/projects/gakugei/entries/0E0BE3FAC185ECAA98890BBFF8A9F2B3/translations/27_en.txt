○Oil insects are also called cockroaches. They are numerous around kitchen stoves. They have wings and can fly. Their color is black.

○Shells is the general name for shells of the clam and conch family. Clam shells are called xuanming powder and treat phlegm. Abalone opercula are called shell incense, also written as yan, and are put in incense. Abalone shells are called shi jue ming and treat eye pain and stop nosebleeds. Also, oyster shells are called oyster shells and effectively stop night sweats.

○Molted skins - snake molts are snake skins. They are also called snake skin or snake molt.

[Left page, upper section]
When made into charcoal and used with sake, they are good for difficult births. Cicada molts are also called cicada molt or dried cicadas. When powdered and mixed with oil, if applied to ear discharge, it heals.

○Shells refer to turtle shells. In ancient times, people would burn turtles and examine the patterns on the shells to divine good and bad fortune. This is called turtle divination. Also, when used as medicine, it supplements the kidneys and eliminates blood stasis. Soft-shelled turtle shells disperse blood stasis and reduce swelling.

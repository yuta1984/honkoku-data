[Right page, upper section]
Butterflies are formed from the transformation of silkworms. Also, wheat transforms to become butterflies. Phoenix butterfly is swallowtail, and the same applies to butterfly, fritillary, and field moth.

○Flies make a rope-twisting motion with their front legs. Therefore, the character is written with the insect radical and the character for "rope." They emerge from decayed ash.

○The golden beetle (jewel beetle) is about the size of a sword bean. In summer, it lives among creeping plants.

○The lamp moth (solitary insect) brushes against lamplight

[Left page, upper section]
and is also called flying moth or candle moth. It's called hitorimushi (solitary insect).

○The horse bee (bumblebee) is a large type of horsefly. Its color is black.

○The click beetle (bowing insect) is also called weaving insect or (supplement) rice-pounding insect.

○The pine cricket (supplement) appears from the end of July. Its voice is like the sound of wind through pine trees. It lives in open fields.

○The golden bell (bell cricket) is also called golden mirror child or moon bell child.

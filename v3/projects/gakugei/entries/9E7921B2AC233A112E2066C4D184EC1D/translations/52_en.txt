Kameido Tenjin Shrine Festival (Kameido Tenmangu Sairei)
Illustration of the Divine Palanquin (Mikoshi) Procession
　Every year on August 24th, there is a divine procession (shinko) to
　the temporary shrine (otabisho) in Kita-Matsushiro-cho.
　The procession arrangements are elaborate and solemn.
　The parishioner towns also put out parade floats (nerimono),
　and nobles and commoners from both the capital and countryside gather in crowds,
　making this the greatest festive event of this area.

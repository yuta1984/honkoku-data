　　　Lu Ji (Riku Seki)
When Lu Ji was six years old, he went to visit a man named Yuan Shu. Yuan Shu served oranges as sweets. Lu Ji took three of them and put them in his sleeve, but when he was taking leave of Yuan Shu on his way home, they fell from his sleeve. Seeing this, Yuan Shu said, "This is not appropriate behavior for a young child." Lu Ji replied, "These oranges were so splendid that I thought I would give them to my mother rather than eat them myself."

【Left Page, Upper】
Hearing this, Yuan Shu was deeply moved and said, "Though only six years old, I have never known anyone with such filial devotion. This is a rare filial child in all ages." From this time on, his reputation for filial piety became known throughout the world, and all people were moved by it.
Filial piety and brotherly respect are all natural endowments. A six-year-old child kept green oranges in his sleeve to leave for his mother, repaying her for the kindness of giving him sweets to eat.
You who are children of this world, follow this example of Lu Ji

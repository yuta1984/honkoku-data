I once heard that Master Ina Jakusui authored the "Independent Judgments on Medicinal Plant Gathering" (Saiyaku Dokudan), demonstrating what he had deeply devoted his mind to throughout his life. However, did it ultimately become a forbidden secret of the imperial court? Or perhaps it was stored away in some famous mountain's remote region? In the end, it was not transmitted to the human world. This too is regrettable. I could not help but admire this, and secretly emulating his intention, I authored several volumes of books, which I titled

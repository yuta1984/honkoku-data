When women write on tanzaku, they write their name on the back of the tanzaku.
The dimensions of shikishi (colored paper) are not fixed. They vary from house to house.
Generally, large shikishi are written with dimensions of six sun four bu vertically and five sun five bu horizontally.

【Left page】
Small shikishi are six sun vertically and five sun three bu horizontally.
The way of writing on shikishi has various patterns, which can be divided into fifty styles or even a hundred styles. Now I will illustrate some of these methods here.

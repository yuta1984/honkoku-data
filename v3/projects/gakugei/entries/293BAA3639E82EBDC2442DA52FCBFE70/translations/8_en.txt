Reading Materials
When teaching girls, since Chinese texts are difficult to read, one should teach books such as "Wa Rongo" (Japanese Analects), "Onna Shisho" (Four Books for Women), "Yamato Shōgaku" (Japanese Elementary Learning), "Onna Shōgaku" (Women's Elementary Learning), "Onna Daigaku" (Greater Learning for Women), "Yamato Zokukun" (Japanese Popular Instructions), "Gojō-kun" (Instructions on the Five Constants), "Dōji-kun" (Instructions for Children), "Kadō-kun" (Instructions on Family Way), "Yamato Jokun" (Japanese Instructions for Women), "Jokun Misago-gusa" (Women's Instructions: Osprey Grass), "Jokun Okina-gusa" (Women's Instructions: Old Man Grass), "Onna Jitsugo-kyō" (True Word Teaching for Women), "Onna Dōji-kyō" (Child's Teaching for Women), "Onna Imagawa" (Imagawa for Women), and other such books. A certain writing says that the reason people in the world fall into debauchery and lose their way back might be because of "The Tale of Genji" and "The Tales of Ise." "Genji" records the debauchery of men and women, and "Ise" writes only about amorous affairs, so they should not be taught even temporarily to young girls. This seems reasonable, but there are those who speak well and should exist in the world, yet from mouths accustomed to telling lies, "Genji" was created as a warning for men and women.

Lotus Leaf consists of: First, aloeswood seven ryō two bu. Second, shell incense two ryō two bu. Third, cloves two ryō two bu. Fourth, sandalwood two shu. Fifth, sweet pine one bu. Sixth, patchouli four shu. Seventh, turmeric two ryō. Add musk.

Chamberlain consists of: First, aloeswood large four ryō two bu. Second, shell incense large one ryō two bu. Third, cloves large two ryō two bu. Fourth, sweet pine small one ryō. Fifth, turmeric small one ryō. Add musk.

Black Method consists of: First, aloeswood four ryō. Second, shell incense one ryō. Third, frankincense large one bu. Fourth, sandalwood one bu. Fifth, cloves two ryō. Sixth, musk two bu.

Chrysanthemum Flower consists of: First, aloeswood four ryō. Second, cloves two ryō. Third, shell incense one ryō two bu. Fourth, frankincense one bu. Fifth, sandalwood one bu. Sixth, musk two bu.

Fallen Leaves consists of: First, aloeswood nine ryō. Second, cloves four ryō. Third, shell incense one ryō two bu. Fourth, musk two bu. Fifth, cyperus three bu. Sixth, sandalwood one bu three shu. Seventh, frankincense one bu. Eighth, storax one ryō.

Hachijō General's Method consists of: First, aloeswood small four ryō two bu. Second, frankincense small one bu. Third, sandalwood three bu. Fourth, cloves small two ryō. Fifth, shell incense small one ryō. Sixth, musk small one bu four shu.

Emperor Suzaku's Imperial Method consists of: First, aloeswood four ryō. Second, cloves two ryō. Third, shell incense one ryō. Fourth, frankincense two bu. Fifth, turmeric two ryō.

Hanging Incense Methods
The blending methods for hanging incense are also generally not far removed from the methods for burning incense. Among the many varieties, I record five types here.

Sweet Flag, Mugwort Growth, New Pillow, Pine Wind, Plum Blossom. These are all beautiful blending methods.

Sweet Flag consists of: First, aloeswood one ryō. Second, cloves one ryō. Third, sandalwood one ryō two bu. Fourth, sweet pine one ryō.

The Large Bronze Lantern in Front of Shingu Shrine - Total height approximately six shaku, with an inscription on the round pillar:
　　　　Respectfully cast and forged
　　　　One sacred lantern before the treasure hall of Shingu
　　　　This intention is for the perfect accomplishment of complete attainment in this and future lives
　　　　　　May benefits universally extend to all living beings
　　　　　　　　Fifth year of Shōō (1292), Year of the Water Dragon, First day of the Third Month
　　　　　　Patron: Kanuma Gonzaburō Nyūdō Kyōa
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Woman of the Kiyohara clan, respectfully inscribed
　　　　　　　　　　Craftsman: Mimura Rokurō Morisue of Hitachi Province

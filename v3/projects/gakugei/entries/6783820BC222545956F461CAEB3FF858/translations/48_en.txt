【Right page】
From this point on, they are respectfully called the Prince of Rinnai-ji Temple
　Prince Shujō of Hon-shō-in, First Imperial Prince rank (initially respectfully called Prince Sonkei)
　　　　Second prince of Retired Emperor Go-Mizunoo, Imperial Dharma Emperor Enjō; his revered mother was the adopted child of Tōfukumon-in, his actual
　　　　mother was Lady Kyōgoku; he received the position in the 3rd year of Jōō, passed away on the 16th day of the 5th month of the 8th year of Enpō, year of Kōshin
　Prince Tenshin of Gedatsu-in, First Imperial Prince rank
　　　　Prince of Retired Emperor Go-Sai; his revered mother was Lady Shin-Dainagon; he received the position on the 14th day of the 3rd month of the 8th year of Enpō,
　　　　passed away on the 1st day of the 3rd month of the 3rd year of Genroku, year of Kanoe-Uma
　Prince Kōben of Daimyō-in, Quasi-Empress rank, First Imperial Prince rank
　　　　Prince of Retired Emperor Go-Sai; his revered mother was Lady Rokujō; he received the position on the 5th day of the 3rd month of the 3rd year of Genroku, in Shōtoku

　Prince Kōkan of Suhō-in, Quasi-Empress rank, First Imperial Prince rank

　Prince Kōkei of Saijōjō-in, Quasi-Empress rank, First Imperial Prince rank

　○17th year of the same era - 25th anniversary memorial service
　　Lord Daiyū departed Edo on the 13th day of the 4th month, made pilgrimage to the shrine on the 17th,
　　and returned to Edo on the 21st.

　○19th year of the same era - For the memorial service of the reconstruction of the inner shrine pagoda
　　Lord Daiyū departed Edo on the 13th day of the 4th month, made pilgrimage to the shrine on the 17th,
　　and returned to Edo on the 21st.

　○Keian 1 (1648) - 33rd anniversary memorial service
　　Lord Daiyū departed Edo on the 13th day of the 4th month to conduct a great Buddhist service. The
　　Buddhist service began on the 13th, concluded on the 22nd-23rd, Lotus Mandala offerings were held on both days of the 24th-25th, sacred Noh performances were held
　　within the shrine on the 26th, and he departed on the 27th. He stayed at Nik-

　　Lord Yūtoku departed Edo on the 13th day of the 4th month, arrived at Mount Nikkō on the 16th,

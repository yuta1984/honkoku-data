The proprietor said, "We have already made dozens of impressions. If you will not permit this, we will ask someone else to write the preface." I am poor and destitute.

The ancients said, "To avoid trouble, plan early; to avoid poverty, prepare early." It is my fault for not consigning this to the flames of Qin early on. However, if you don't want people to know, it's best not to do it; if you don't want people to hear, it's best not to say it. Alas, what is this!

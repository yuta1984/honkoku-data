　There is an inscription carved on the back of the Buddha body, which reads as follows:
　　Respectfully announced: Forged gilt bronze shadow image dharma body Amida seated light three shaku six sun
　Offered for the Emperor, Japan's sovereign, this province's lord, jitō and village headman
　May wishes be fulfilled, peace and tranquility, faithful dharma master, descendants' peace
　Complete accomplishment, teacher elders and parents, both parents' souls, assistance and cooperation
　Together achieving rebirth, extending to the dharma realm, equal benefit, Kenchō second year
　Great year kanoe-inu, first month of summer, seventh day mizunoe-ne, Jambudvīpa
　Japan Bushū, Tanishi-gun Tomi, Shinjihiji Temple, patron Minamoto clan
　Wish-maker Buddha-child Keiyu respectfully announces

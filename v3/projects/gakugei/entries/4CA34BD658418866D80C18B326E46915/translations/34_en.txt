[Upper section]
○The Mukutori (small mukudori) is called "ko-muku." Its size is smaller than a bulbul, with a white head and black and white feathers on its back. In mid-autumn, many come in flocks. Its taste is delicious.
○The Mukudori (white-cheeked starling) is called "ō-muku" (large muku). It is larger than the small muku, and its feather colors are also different. It comes in summer and autumn but does not form flocks.
○The Kikuitadaki (goldcrest) is an extremely small bird. Its entire body is light blue, with yellow feathers on the crown. When the weather is fine and warm, if it opens the feathers on its crown, red feathers emerge from within. It is a bird that comes in winter.
○The Bunchō (Java sparrow) is about the size of a sparrow. Its feather color is black, with round white patches on the cheeks. Its belly is white.
○The Shijūkara (great tit) is smaller than a sparrow, with a black head and round white cheeks. Its back is light blue, and its belly is white with black feathers. It comes in autumn and winter.

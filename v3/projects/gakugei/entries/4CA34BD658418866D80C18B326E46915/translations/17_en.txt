○The goose comes in two colors,
blue and white, with green eyes, yellow
beak, and red legs. It fights well.
When consumed, it relieves heat
in the five organs.

○The duck resembles
a mallard in shape. It cannot fly,
and its feather color includes white varieties, while those with black heads
resemble the coloring of wild ducks.
It has no major cold toxicity and treats wind deficiency,
cold-heat conditions, and edema.

○The grebe is about the size
of a pigeon and cannot
walk on land, but enters water
to catch fish.

○Ducks have many varieties, both large
and small, with feather colors that vary
greatly. What is illustrated here is what is commonly
called the mallard. It supplements the interior,
increases qi, and regulates the stomach.

○The seagull is like a white
pigeon. Its beak is long, and they fly
in flocks, shining in the sun. They live
by the seashore and lay eggs in the third month.

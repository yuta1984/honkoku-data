[Upper section]
There is skin under its legs, and its fur does not
get wet even when touched by water. This is called a seal.

○The water buffalo is blue in color with a large belly and a
pointed head, resembling a wild boar in shape.
When eaten, it stops excessive thirst,
nourishes the spleen and stomach, supplements
deficiency, and treats edema.

○The orangutan is a beast that lives in the sea.
Its fur is yellow like a monkey.
Its ears are white, and its face and feet are like humans.
It likes alcohol. Its blood is taken for dyeing.

○The baboon is said to become a baboon
when a monkey ages. Its form is like a human
but large. It has long lips and wears
reverse-heel hair, runs swiftly and eats people. When it
sees people, it laughs greatly.

○The rat has four teeth but no fangs. It has four
claws in front and five claws behind. It treats
convulsions and epilepsy in children.

○The kei is a field mouse. It is a small
type of rat. Even when it bites people, it doesn't
hurt but causes sores.

○The mole is said to be a transformation
of a shrike. It resembles a rat but has a head like
a wild boar and no tail. Its fur color is
yellow-black. It digs in ponds and
eats earthworms. It fears the light of sun and moon.

○The weasel is larger than a rat with a long body.
Its four legs are short and its tail is large. Its color is
yellow and red. It catches rats well.

○"Kaku" (horn) can be read as "to fight." This is because beasts
fight using their horns.

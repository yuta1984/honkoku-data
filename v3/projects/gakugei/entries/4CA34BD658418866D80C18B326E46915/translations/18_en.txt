○The heron has a thin, long neck,
with both beak and legs being long. They come in large and small sizes,
and the small ones have long feathers
on their crown. It strengthens the spleen and supplements qi.
○The night heron is a water bird.
It is about the size of a heron, with gray-white
color and a black back, called seguro-goi,
and those with spots are called
hoshi-goi. It neutralizes the toxins of various fish.
○The crested ibis is also called shuro (vermillion heron)
and is larger than a heron.
Its color is white with a slight red tinge.
It is commonly called taugarasu.
○The sandpiper is slightly smaller
than a pigeon in size. Its beak and legs are long,
and its feathers are brown with black
spots. It lives in rice fields and marshes. They come in large and small sizes,
and the large one is called
botoshigi. It supplements deficiency and warms people.
○The cormorant resembles a crow
but has a long neck and a slightly long
beak. It enters water and skillfully catches
fish. It nests in forest
trees. Fishermen raise them to
catch fish.

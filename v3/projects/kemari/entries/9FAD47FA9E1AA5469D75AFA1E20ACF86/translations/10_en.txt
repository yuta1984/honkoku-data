appear frightening, one's facial expression and head position should not be
frivolous, one's hand position should not be forced or unseemly, but should be graceful,
not clenching one's fists rigidly, with natural body movement that is not
stiff, not thrusting out the chest, not bending the waist too low, without
misaligning the feet, and when kicking the ball, not swaying inward or outward, one should
step firmly with the feet. In general, the body should be positioned slightly upright
with the head forward and feet positioned toward the back

1. Second month of the fourth year of the Kyōhō era (1719)
Regarding the matter of presenting New Year's greetings for Ganshō-in-sama's year-end remaining celebration, and also regarding the preliminary arrangements for the autumn invitation by the Senior Councilor, having worked diligently on various matters, a total of five pieces of silver were bestowed.

1. Third month of the fifth year of the Kyōhō era (1720)
As requested, he was relieved of his current duties and appointed to the Aizu Financial Independent Group, and was ordered to continue serving until a successor was appointed.
However, he served until the 15th day of the 4th month of the same year, and in the 5th month, he went down to Echigo with his retainers.

1. Fifth month of the same year
It was announced that during the recent absence, he had served diligently and without delay until a successor was appointed for the current service in Edo.

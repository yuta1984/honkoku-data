1. In March of the 4th year (Year of the Rat), he served as a posting guard during the hunting expedition.
However, he also served once more after this.

1. In September of the same year, he went to Edo for guard duty and returned in March of the following year (Year of the Ox).
However, as a ceremonial gift for regular service, he was granted one old kosode, and upon his return, he was given one old kosode, and moreover:
From Lord Teiaki: one bolt of silk
From Shojuin-sama: one tan of dyed silk and gift parcels were granted.

1. In January of the 6th year (Year of the Tiger),
because he diligently performed his duties for Lord Teiaki's visit to the domain, he was granted one set of hemp kamishimo made of the lord's silk as a reward.

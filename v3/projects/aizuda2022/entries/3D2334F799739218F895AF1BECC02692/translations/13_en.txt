Teishichi Takatsugu: Originally named Manjirō. Born in the third year of Jōkyō (1686), died of illness on the fourteenth day of the first month of the sixth year of Hōei (1709). Buddhist name: Ryōzan Ryōun Shinji, buried in Edo. Became adopted son of Tsuzawa Hachirōbei but later separated.
Mother: Same as above

Daughter: Became wife of Oda Seiemon, a retainer of Matsudaira Mino-no-kami, but divorced. Later became wife of Kitahara Uneme Mitsuyoshi.
Mother: Same as above

Daughter: Born in the fifth year of Genroku (1692), died of illness on the seventh day of the sixth month of the seventh year of Hōei (1710). Buddhist name: Kagaku Myōrin Shinnyo, buried at Monryōin.

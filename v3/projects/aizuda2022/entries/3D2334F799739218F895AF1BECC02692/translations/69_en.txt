Katsusuke Tanetsune 《Marginal note: Initially Kenjirō | Born on the 23rd day of March, Hōreki 14 (Year of the Monkey)》
　Mother: Daughter of the Nakabayashi clan
　Wife: Daughter of Miyake Yahichi Chikatada

1. In August of the 6th year of An'ei (Year of the Rooster), his first audience with the lord was granted according to his father's petition.

1. In July of the 1st year of Tenmei (Year of the Ox), due to misconduct regarding male homosexual relations, he was ordered to be disinherited.

1. In December of the 4th year of Tenmei (Year of the Dragon), he was ordered to be restored as heir.

1. In May of the 8th year of Tenmei (Year of the Monkey), he was appointed to serve in meal service training and supervision.
　　However, as expense補助, two bu of gold were granted annually.

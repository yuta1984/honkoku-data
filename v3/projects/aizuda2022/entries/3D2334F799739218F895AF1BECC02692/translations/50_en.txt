Twelfth year, year of the Rat: As requested, he was relieved of his official post and was appointed to the tozama group under Miyake Magobei's unit, where he served castle guard duty.

Enpō 8, year of the Monkey, ninth month: He went up to Edo for duty rotation and returned in the third month of the following year of the Rooster.

Tenna 2, year of the Dog, twelfth month: When his superior Miyake Magobei was relieved of his official post, he was transferred to Sugita Gorōbei's unit as successor to the role.

Same third year, year of the Boar, third month: For recuperation from illness, he petitioned to go to Arima in Settsu Province for hot spring therapy, departed, and returned in the sixth month of the same year.

Jōkyō 3, year of the Tiger, seventh month: He was appointed to the position of ometsuke-ban and granted a stipend of 100 koku for his official duties.

He failed to meet expectations, and therefore was dismissed from his position

1. In the 4th year, year of the Dragon, 4th month, regarding his concurrent service as chief administrator, he was supposed to replace old certificates and later recover the substitute money, but this resulted in financial loss to the domain. Therefore, 100 koku was confiscated from his stipend and he was ordered into house confinement.
   However, in the 5th month of the same year, the house confinement was lifted

1. In the 5th year, year of the Snake, 8th month, he was granted an audience with the lord

1. In the 12th month of the same year, he was assigned to the outer retainer group, joined Kasuga Jinzaemon's unit, and served guard duty at the castle

1. On the 19th day of the 7th month of the 8th year, year of the Monkey, he died of illness at age 57

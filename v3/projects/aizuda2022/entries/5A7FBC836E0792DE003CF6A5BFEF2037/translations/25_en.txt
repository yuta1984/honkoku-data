One: Same 2nd year, year of the Dog (1790), 7th month, submitted a petition to resign from office due to illness. However, it was deemed that this went against the intent of the written directive personally given by the lord the previous autumn, and that he had committed improper conduct toward his superior without restraint. Therefore, it was announced that he would be stripped of his official position and assigned to minor construction duties.

However, thereafter he was granted commendation every time.

One: Bunka 4th year, year of the Rabbit (1807), 3rd month, he was granted the hereditary stipend of 800 koku without any changes.

One: Same 8th year, year of the Sheep (1811), 1st month, having devoted himself to martial arts and achieved mastery, and having devoted himself to military studies completing three volumes of tactical formations this time, although his scholarship had not reached the level previously commanded, considering that he had reached the age of twenty or more, he was specially exempted from maintenance fees and ordered to begin active duty. He was further commanded to devote himself even more to scholarly pursuits and training.

One: Same 10th year, year of the Rooster (1813), 9th month, he was appointed as unit commander (monotō).

One: Same year, 11th month, for his diligent efforts in encouraging and instructing his unit members in military drills, he was granted one whip.

One: Bunpō 1st year, year of the Tiger (1818), 10th month, he served as escort for the lord's attendance in Edo, went up to serve in the first formation, and returned in the 4th month of the following year of the Rabbit.
　　However, during his service term, he also served as a regular assistant ceremonial attendant.

One: Same 3rd year, year of the Dragon (1820), 7th month, he took custody of Shōnosuke, son of the confined person Tsutsui Kannosuke, from the judicial office and was ordered to execute judgment upon him at the tribunal.

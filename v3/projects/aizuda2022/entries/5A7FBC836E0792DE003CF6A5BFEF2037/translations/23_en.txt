One: Same year, 11th month, for his diligent service, was appointed to the rank equivalent to second-class magistrate.

One: Same year, 12th month, at Aizu, permission to use a palanquin was granted and announced.

One: Same 5th year of the Monkey (1776), 9th month, went up to Edo for guard duty service, returned in the 3rd month of the following year of the Rooster (1777).
　　However, as customary grant items, go-kosode was bestowed twice, and furthermore, for his continued diligent service from the 1st month of the current year of the Rooster, a crested padded haori was bestowed in the lord's rest chamber.

One: Same 6th year of the Rooster (1777), 3rd month, was appointed to the position of magistrate and was announced to be appointed for joint signature authority.

One: Same 7th year of the Dog (1778), 7th month, as items for the lord's retainer group, was granted rinzu silk shoulder garment (unlined), hakama trousers, and lined rinzu silk shoulder garment.

One: Same year, 10th month, went up to Edo for guard duty service, returned in the 4th month of the following year of the Boar (1779).
　　However, as customary grant items, go-kosode was bestowed twice.

One: Same 9th year of the Rat (1780), 3rd month, for his hardships in establishing the foundation for ceremonial funds and accumulating them, was granted one haori.

One: 10th year of Genroku (1697), 11th month, granted the hereditary stipend of 700 koku without change, and appointed to the gaisama-gumi (outer retainer group).

One: Same 14th year (1701), 11th month, appointed as messenger officer (oshitsukai-ban).

One: 4th year of Hōei (1707), 2nd month, appointed as sword keeper (okatana-ban).

One: 1st year of Shōtoku (1711), 6th month, appointed as announcer officer (osōsha-ban).

One: 2nd year of Kyōhō (1717), 4th month, appointed as gun unit commander (omochizutsu-gashira).

One: Same 6th year (1721), 9th month, when troops were dispatched to Nanzan, appointed as general commander through personnel assignment, went to Tajima village, served there until the 7th month of the following tiger year, and returned after completing duties.

One: Same year, 12th month, appointed as attendant officer commander (otomo-bangashira).

One: Same 7th year (1722), 7th month, regarding the Tajima duties, from the previous year he paid careful attention to details, consulted well with fellow officials, diligently served in long-term matters, endured various hardships, and completed duties without delay; for this he was granted twenty silver pieces.

One: Same year, 8th month, appointed as commander (bangashira).

One: Same 9th year (1724), 10th month, went up to Edo for guard duty service, served one tour of duty, and returned.

One: Same 15th year (1730), 7th month, as requested, was relieved of official duties.

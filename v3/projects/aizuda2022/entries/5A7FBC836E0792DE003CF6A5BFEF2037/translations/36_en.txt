1. In Kyōhō 2nd year, year of the Rooster, 2nd month, as requested, he was relieved of his official duties
　and was appointed to a seat above the御納戸 (treasury/wardrobe office)
1. In the 7th month of the same year, as requested, his son Banemon was
　appointed as his substitute guard
1. In the 10th month of the same year
　He was granted an audience with Gyokurin-in-sama, and was told through the chief retainer
　that he should come to pay his respects from time to time in the future
1. In the 10th year of the same era, year of the Snake, 1st day of the 7th month, he died of illness at age 69
　Posthumous Buddhist name: Musan Yuishin Koji, burial place: same as above
Daughter　　　　　　　Wife of Mukōyama Tōbee Tsugumitsu
　Mother: daughter of the Yamauchi clan

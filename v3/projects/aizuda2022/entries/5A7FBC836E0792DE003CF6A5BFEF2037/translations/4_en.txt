One: Same year (month unknown), he petitioned to adopt Denbei, second son of Shinoda Naizen, and was granted permission.

One: Same year (month unknown), as requested, his son Denbei was appointed as a substitute (bandai).

One: Same 20th year of Kanei (1643), year of the sheep, during the domain transfer to Aizu, he was ill and was not supposed to receive another increase in stipend, but his son Denbei petitioned and went up to Edo as a substitute, also serving in the lord's retinue during the domain entry. Due to these exceptional circumstances, he was granted an additional increase of 50 koku.

One: First year of Shōhō (1644), year of the monkey, ninth month, died of illness. Buddhist name: Ichige Sōgei Koji. Burial place: Shōunzan Zenryūji Temple.

First: Kyōwa 2nd year, year of the dog [1802], 8th month - regarding the complex dispute case involving the successor abbot of Genshoji Temple in Atashio Village, he diligently resolved the matter and worked hard on establishing the five occupations and matters concerning reserved rice, so he was granted 5 ryō of silver as reward

First: Bunka 1st year, year of the rat [1804], 12th month - his request to adopt Yūzaburō, the third son of Sase Yūhachi, as his heir was granted

First: Same 2nd year, year of the ox [1805], 12th month - for dedicating himself to various administrative matters in the town district and working diligently on procurement funds and other duties including temporary services, he was granted 1 ryō of gold as reward

First: Same 3rd year, year of the tiger [1806], 2nd month - since the trade department officials occasionally had absences that caused disruptions, he was appointed as assistant to these officials and ordered to serve with the understanding of being actively involved and present

First: Same 3rd year, year of the tiger [1806], 8th month - appointed as investigation officer at the town office and to concurrent positions as financial clerk and subordinate in temples and shrines department; although these were weighty responsibilities, he was granted the privilege of individual audience with the lord

First: Same 4th year, year of the rabbit [1807], 12th month - due to illness, his official duties were dismissed as requested, and he was appointed to the advisory group attached to the senior councilor

First: Same 5th year, year of the dragon [1808], 1st month - appointed to serve in the cost reduction department

First: Same 6th year, year of the snake [1809], 9th month - appointed as investigator for both domain granaries and additional service in financial inspection

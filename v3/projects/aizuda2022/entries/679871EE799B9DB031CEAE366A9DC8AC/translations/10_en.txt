Actually the fourth son of Miyashita Yasazaemon Dōken
　　Wife: daughter of adoptive father Tōshichi Mitsutomo　Born on the 3rd day of the 8th month of An'ei 7, Year of the Dog

1. Kansei 11, Year of the Sheep, 3rd month (March 1799): He was appointed as assistant instructor at Inawashiro Academy.

1. Kyōwa 1, Year of the Rooster, 7th month (July 1801): He was appointed to serve as a hired samurai of Inawashiro.

1. Kyōwa 3, Year of the Boar, 5th month (May 1803): Since Inawashiro was short-staffed, he served as a hired samurai cultivating wasteland and was elevated to the rank of armored warrior (kōshi). Due to these exceptional circumstances, it was announced that he would be granted an audience at an appropriate occasion.

1. Bunka 4, Year of the Rabbit, 2nd month (February 1807): Due to personnel reductions at the literary and martial academy, his position as assistant instructor at Inawashiro Academy was terminated. In recognition of his diligent service, he was rewarded with one bundle of cotton.

1. Bunka 8, Year of the Sheep, intercalary 2nd month (March 1811): It was announced that he had served castle guard duty without negligence.

1. Bunka 12, Year of the Boar, 4th month (April 1815): Following his father's precedent as literary assistant, he was granted a stipend of two persons' worth from an eight-person stipend as salary for his official duties.

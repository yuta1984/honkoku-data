1. Same month: Went on official business to Tsuno for procuring horses, completed the duties and returned in the 6th month of the same year.

1. Same year, 6th month (June 1767): For his diligent practice of horsemanship and training horses, serving as duty officer at the castle stables, and performing duties as stable magistrate and attendant on regular days, he was granted a stipend of five persons' provisions. Furthermore, for his diligent practice of horsemanship, he was ordered to understand his role as supervisor of the horse department.

1. Meiwa 5, Year of the Rat, 2nd month (February 1768): He was granted a stipend of ten persons' provisions, summoned, and assigned a position above the minor guard (koban).

1. Same year, 3rd month (March 1768): He went up to Edo as an attendant for guard duty service and returned in the 10th month of the same year.
　　However, for serving without incident during his time on duty, he was granted seven monme of silver.

1. Meiwa 6, Year of the Ox, 6th month (June 1769): When he fell ill, he was excused from guard duty until he recovered, and was privately told to rest slowly and serve for a long time.

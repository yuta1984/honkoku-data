1. In Shōhō 4 (1647), year of the boar, 8th month, he went up to Edo as a guard resident, and returned in the 1st month of the following year of the rat.

1. In Keian 1 (1648), year of the rat, he went out for land surveys.

1. In Manji 2 (1659), year of the boar, he was appointed to the position of deputy magistrate.

1. In Kanbun 5 (1665), year of the snake, 10th month, 7th day, he died of illness. Buddhist name: Zen'yo Tōetsu. Burial place: Jōan-ji temple at Kodateyama.

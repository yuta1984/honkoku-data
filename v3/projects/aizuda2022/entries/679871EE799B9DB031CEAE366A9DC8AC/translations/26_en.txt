Shichirōzaemon Morihisa 《Marginal note: Originally named Shichirō | Born in Hōreki 10 (1760), year of the dragon》

Mother: daughter of the Higuchi clan

Wife: daughter of Mitsuhashi Bunzaemon Motake - divorced

[Second wife]: daughter of Sase Jirōuemon Yoshitaka - born on the 10th day of the 7th month of An'ei 1 (1772), year of the dragon

1. In the 3rd month of Tenmei 6 (1786), year of the horse, when his father went up [to Edo] for garrison duty, he accompanied him, and during the invitation ceremony, he was appointed to serve in the same manner as minor officials of the Accounting Office and was granted clothing allowance.

1. In the 2nd month of the same 7th year (1787), year of the sheep, he was appointed as a temporary employee serving as a minor official of the Accounting Office and served until the same 4th month.

1. In the 5th month of Kyōwa 2 (1802), year of the dog, from his deceased father's stipend allocation, 3 koku were reduced and he was granted 5 koku and rations for 3 people for his household.

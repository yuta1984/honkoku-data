1. Same year of the rabbit, 9th month: Was appointed to rice transport supervision and served until the following 1st month

1. Same 5th year (1785), year of the snake, 8th month: During an extraordinary visit to the shogun, went up as part of the entourage, was appointed as deputy group leader for the journey, and was appointed as temporary inspector in the field. On the 12th month 5th day, was appointed as regular temporary inspector for Lord Gansho-in, serving concurrently as foot soldier group leader while also being ordered to serve in women's quarters duties. On the 16th of the same month, when official duties were completed, served again as extraordinary temporary inspector

1. Same 6th year (1788), year of the monkey, 1st month: Was appointed as regular temporary inspector of the inner palace

1. Kansei 2 (1790), year of the dog, 8th month: Submitted a retirement request as wished

1. Same 5th year (1793), year of the ox, 12th month 8th day: Died of illness at age 73, Buddhist name Shaku Ryodo, burial place same as above

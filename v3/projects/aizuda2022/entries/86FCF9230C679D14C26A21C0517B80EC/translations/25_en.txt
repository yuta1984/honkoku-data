One: In the first year of Hōreki (1751), eighth month, when the head Miyake Magohei was transferred to another position, he was assigned to Yanase Kyūma's unit as successor.

One: In the fourth year of the same era (1754), tenth month, his illness having recovered, he requested to return to active duty and was granted permission.

One: In the fifth year of the same era (1755), fourth month, he went up to Edo for guard duty service and returned in the tenth month of the same year.

One: In the eighth year of the same era (1758), tenth month, he went up to Edo for guard duty service, and in the fourth month of the following year of the Rabbit, he served as an escort guard during the lord's travel and returned.
　　However, he was commended for serving diligently as temporary escort guard during the lord's travel without any absences.

One: In the twelfth year of the same era (1762), second month, when the head Yanase Kyūma was appointed as wakadoshiyori (junior councilor), he was assigned to Yamazaki Sasuke's unit as successor.

One: In the first year of Meiwa (1764), third month, he requested and was granted permission to change his name to Kageyu.

One: In the second year of the same era (1765), third month, he went to Yamagata for guard duty service and returned in the ninth month of the same year.

One: In the third year of the same era (1766), tenth month, he went up to Edo for guard duty service, and in the fifth month of the following year of the Boar, he served as a mounted escort and returned.

One　In Shōhō 1, year of the monkey (1644)　He was appointed to castle duty (goshiroyaku)
One　In the 4th month of the same year, for Miharu official business　when the lord made his descent, he was
　　ordered to accompany and served in official duties
One　In the 8th month of Keian 2, year of the ox (1649), he was appointed to the role of announcer (gosōshayaku), was entrusted with
　　the yoriyuki group, and granted an additional 100 koku
One　In the 5th month of the same 4th year, year of the rabbit (1651)　For the new burial at Mount Nikkō
　　as assistance for the mausoleum construction work, together with Sakai Sanuki-no-kami Tadakatsu-sama
　　they received orders, and as personnel including Tanaka Saburōbei and Kayano Gonbei were
　　dispatched, he was ordered to negotiate with the shogunate officials
　　and served official duties while stationed in Edo
One　In the 9th month of Jōō 3, year of the horse (1654)　When the lord went up to Kyoto
　　he accompanied on horseback for both going and returning
One　In the 8th month of Meireki 1, year of the sheep (1655), while he was down in Aizu, from
　　Nihonmatsu castle lord Niwa Sakyō-dayū Mitsushige-sama
　　an envoy was sent as congratulations for Iwahiko-sama's arrival, so he was
　　ordered as a return courtesy envoy and when he went, with very polite treatment
　　crane cuisine was ordered and he was granted a ceremonial cup and five court garments

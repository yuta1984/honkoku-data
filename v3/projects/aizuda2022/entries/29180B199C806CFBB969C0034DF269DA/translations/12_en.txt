　　However, since Iyoda Matazaemon was ordered to go investigate two-year-old horses in Miharu as official business, the regular October rotation would not be in time, so he went up in the ninth month.

• In the tenth month of the same year, when a fire broke out from Nakamura Taitō's quarters in the northern official residences, it was announced that he worked diligently [in firefighting].

• In the twelfth month of the same year, regarding the unexpected presentation of horses [to the shogun], for his various efforts and concerns, and also for presenting his horsemanship skills in the spring, he was granted one formal kosode with the [lord's] crest, and on the same day was summoned before [the lord] and received his gracious words.

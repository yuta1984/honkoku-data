It was announced that he was retired without incident.

Item: 2nd month of the 3rd year, Year of the Tiger: His seat rank was appointed to be next to Ōtake Kisaburō.

Item: 11th month of the same year:
He was granted one bolt of raw cotton as a bequest from Lord Sadaaki.

Item: Same month: He was ordered to investigate old records at the Public Affairs Office. Following the precedent of those previously appointed, he was to report to the Public Affairs Office, conduct investigations together with others, engage in thorough discussions with the Public Affairs Magistrate, and take decisive action.

Item: 4th month of the 6th year, Year of the Snake: He was appointed to the temporary position of Deputy Administrator trainee.

Item: 3rd month of the 7th year, Year of the Horse: He was appointed as an assistant in horse affairs.

Item: 3rd month of the 8th year, Year of the Sheep: He was appointed to be in charge of pasture affairs.

Item: 5th month of the same year: He was appointed as a regular instructor at the domain school. His seat rank was placed above the inspector, and it was announced that he would receive a stipend of three persons' support as his official salary.

Item: 8th month of the same year: It was announced that on the following days, he would reverently read the family precepts here.

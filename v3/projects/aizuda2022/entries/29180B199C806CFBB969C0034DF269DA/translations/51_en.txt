1. In the same year (month unknown), he was entrusted with the Mita library.

1. In the same year (month unknown), he was appointed to the temporary position of palace guard duty.

1. In the 6th month of the same year, it was commanded that he be granted five ryō annually for study expenses, making a total of ten ryō granted annually.

1. In the 4th month of the same 4th year, Year of the Sheep, he returned to meet the lord upon his entry into the domain, then accompanied him back down, serving as guard at the main lodging stations during the journey.

1. In the 6th month of the same year, he was granted items from the palace storehouse clearance.
　　However, regarding the items received from subsequent palace storehouse clearances, the details are not known.

1. In the 8th month of the same year, he was summoned to Aizu and went down, being told to plan to remain there from the 9th month until the following spring.

1. In the 10th month of the same year, he was granted paintings by the lord's brush as well as paintings by Ensa's brush.

1. In the 1st month of the same 5th year, Year of the Monkey, he was granted one mallard duck for the New Year reading ceremony.

1. In the 2nd month of the same year, he was granted poetry written by the lord's brush.

1. In the 3rd month of the same year, he was granted two pieces of silver as a reward.

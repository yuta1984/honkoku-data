Regarding the collection of monetary payments and the management of circulation funds for confidential domain affairs, having devoted himself wholeheartedly and worked diligently for many years, he was granted an increase of thirty koku to his current stipend.

• In the ninth month of the same ninth (Snake) year, regarding the promotion of domestic products: Since the foundation lies in the excellence of craftsmen in the production methods of local specialties, he was ordered to wholeheartedly summon skilled artisans from other domains to focus on training. In connection with this, he was to undertake missions to other domains as circumstances required, discuss trade matters extensively at the three administrative offices, and manage the promotion of domestic products.

• In the same month, having been appointed to oversee the promotion of domestic products, he was additionally appointed as concurrent mountain magistrate and ordered to attend the mountain office to jointly manage these administrative matters.

• In the fifth month of the same eleventh (Sheep) year, having excellently supervised the townspeople's affairs, achieved results in administrative control, and diligently served in special official duties, he was granted three pieces of silver and three bundles of cotton as a reward.

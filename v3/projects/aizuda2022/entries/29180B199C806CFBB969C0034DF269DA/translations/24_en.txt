• In the tenth month of the same year, because he served diligently while the lord was in the domain, he was given one piece of silver as a reward.

• In the fourth month of the same second (Tiger) year, he went up to Edo for guard duty and returned in the tenth month of the same year.
　　　During this time, he served in matters related to the horses to be presented to the shogun in the fifth year, and when the lord called for his horse during the military exercises, because he served diligently, he was given one piece of silver as a reward.

• In the sixth month of the same third (Rabbit) year, he was appointed to the position of御次番 (gojiban - reserve duty).

• On the same day, it was ordered that when on duty, he should appear in formal dress, and even when off duty, if horses for riding practice appeared, he should also attend to horse matters.

• In the same month, he was appointed as supervisor of the Ninochō stables, and was ordered to attend whenever [the lord] entered the stables.

• In the same year (month unknown), he was appointed as supervisor of court music.

• In the first month of the same fourth (Dragon) year, he was ordered to attend the first wooden horse ceremony of the year as he had done previously.

• In the same month, he was appointed as supervisor of the Furukawa tea house.

　　Mother: Adopted daughter of the Teshiroki family
　　Wife: Daughter of Iyoda Zusho Tameaki. Died of illness on 24th day of 9th month, Hōei 4 (1707), Year of the Boar. Buddhist name: Zesō-in Myōhō Nichitō. Burial place: same as above
　　　Adopted daughter of Shirai Gorotayū Tanechika. Died of illness on 20th day of 3rd month, Kyōhō 15 (1730), Year of the Dog. Buddhist name: Chisen-in Myōshō Nichijō Daishi. Burial place: same as above
　　　Actually adopted daughter of Hasegawa Gozaemon
　　　Daughter of Unno Gunzaemon Yoshihisa. Died of illness at age 60 on 3rd day of 5th month, Hōreki 12 (1762), Year of the Horse. Buddhist name: Hōtō-in Myōgo Nichii Daishi. Burial place: same as above

1. Genroku 13 (1700), Year of the Dragon, 12th month: He inherited the family stipend of 200 koku without reduction, was appointed to the outer retainer unit (tozama-gumi), joined Harada Goroemon's unit, and served castle guard duty.

1. Hōei 4 (1707), Year of the Boar, 8th month: He was appointed to escort duty (otomo-ban).

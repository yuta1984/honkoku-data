Kuranosuké Teishun (originally named Kyūma, also Tōzō)
Mother: daughter of the Suzuki family
Wife: daughter of Baba Seibē Shigutane, died (separated by death)

• Tenmei 8 (1788), Year of the Monkey, 3rd month: Was granted his first formal audience (with the lord) as requested by his parent
• Kansei 4 (1792), Year of the Rat, 12th month: Was commended for his admirable dedication in attending school without absence for five years
• Same 6th year (1794), Year of the Tiger, 4th month: Was granted permission to change his name to "Kuranosin" as requested by his parent
• Same 7th year (1795), Year of the Rabbit, 1st month: Was appointed as senior squad leader (nenchō jūchō) at the school
• Same year, 12th month: While serving as senior squad leader, although participation in additional arts was optional, he continued to attend both the regular school and the military academy without absence for eight years, and was rewarded with one piece of silver and one bundle of cotton
• Same 9th year (1797), Year of the Snake, 4th month: As requested by his parent, was appointed as guard substitute (goban-dai)...

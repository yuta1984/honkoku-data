Enpō 7 (1679), Year of the Sheep, 11th month, 14th day: After raising the daughter of Shōzaemon's wife for several years, a petition was submitted and granted for her to be arranged in marriage to the Toe Yagozaemon family. Whether this woman first married into the Toe clan and later was connected to the Ōkuma clan is uncertain. Since there is no record in either family's traditions, the details as they appear in the official records are noted here.

• In the twelfth month of the same year, regarding the dredging of the town's moats, he went to the construction office and arranged for laborers, giving instructions for various matters and executing them vigorously with good results, for which he was awarded a meal.

• In the twelfth month of the same fourth (Rabbit) year, his request to change his name to Jōemon was granted.

• In the fourth month of the same fifth (Dragon) year, his current duties were exempted as requested, and he was immediately ordered to retire as per his private petition.

• In the intercalary sixth month of the same year, during his service he handled town affairs well and managed them effectively, procured funds for school construction, and diligently arranged for the recruitment of additional scaffolding workers with voluntary contributions, properly explaining this to the townspeople, for which he was awarded three silver coins and two bundles of cotton as a reward.

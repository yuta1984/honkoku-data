• Same 12th year (1819), Year of the Boar, 8th month: Was appointed as temporary inspector (metsuke) during the bird hunting season

• Same year, 11th month: Was appointed as inspector (metsuke)

• Same year, 12th month: His request to change his name to Tōzaemon was granted

【• Bunsei 1st year (1818), Year of the Tiger, 4th month: Went up to Sagami Province for garrison duty and returned in the 10th month of the same year. However, it was declared that he served diligently without delay in the military camp】

• Bunsei 2nd year (1819), Year of the Rabbit, 2nd month: During his garrison duty in Sagami Province last summer, when foreign ships arrived, he quickly set out by boat and promptly boarded, beginning guard duty against foreign ships and performing these official duties with dedication. For this, he was granted two bundles of cotton as a reward

• Same 6th year (1823), Year of the Sheep, 10th month: While serving the annual rotation for ceremonial affairs, he worked diligently on ceremonial duties for Lord Kinbun's formal audience and festival ceremonies. For this, he was granted two silver coins as a reward

• Same 7th year (1824), Year of the Monkey, 2nd month: For not responding to his duties at the office, his official position was revoked and he was assigned to minor construction work (kobushin)

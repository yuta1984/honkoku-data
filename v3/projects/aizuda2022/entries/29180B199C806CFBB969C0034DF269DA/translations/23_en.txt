• In the same month, he went up to Edo as part of the lord's retinue for the mandatory attendance (sankin-kōtai), serving as guard duty, and performed guard duty at the lord's headquarters during the journey, returning in the tenth month of the same year.
　　　During the military exercises when the lord called for his horse, because he served diligently, he was given one piece of silver as a reward.

• In the seventh month of Tenmei 1 (Ox) year, he presented the horsemanship manual and certification documents for the secret techniques of the school that his father was supposed to present, along with all the necessary equipment, and additionally presented a light horse bit of the school's secret techniques measuring one ken, as well as scroll paintings of the seven-stage fast horse techniques.

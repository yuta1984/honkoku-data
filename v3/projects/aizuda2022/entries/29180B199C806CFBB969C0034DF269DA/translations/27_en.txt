• In the same sixth (Tiger) year, regarding the matter of a mountain ascetic (shugenja) who had come from Yonezawa, there was an official meeting, and because he handled the investigation and arrangements for this matter, he was given three bundles of cotton by Lord Uesugi Danjō-daihitsu.

• In the same year (month unknown according to marginal note), he was ordered to take charge of and serve in various administrative matters pertaining to the office.

• In the twelfth month of the same year, because Satō Heizō was put in charge of lending affairs, he was ordered to join and serve under the said Heizō.

• In the fifth month of the same seventh (Rabbit) year, although he diligently served and handled official duties that were all major matters, he paid careful attention to details and managed them well. Moreover, since the year before last's end, he had been serving alone and worked with particular diligence. For this exceptional service, he was granted a special rank advancement and was awarded formal court dress of higher grade.

• In the sixth month of the same year, when Lord Naitō Bunzen-no-kami passed through this town, he attended at the lodging residence, and His Lordship granted him 200 mon in gold.

1. In the same month, because he served diligently in horse-related duties,

1. In the 4th month of the same year, he was ordered to copy
　the calligraphy style called "Gen-shō" used in court music
1. In the 12th month of the same year, the polo practice at Sakura Horse Ground was
　a new undertaking, but because he managed it well with great effort,
　he was granted sake
1. In the 9th month of Kansei 9, Year of the Snake, because he was diligent
　in horse-related duties, he was granted three ryō of silver
　as a reward
1. In the 5th month of Kansei 10, Year of the Horse, when the kyōin-shurei

　and take charge of the court music arrangements

• In the intercalary sixth month of the same year, he was assigned to serve at the Naka-Arai district office.

• In the eighth month of the same year, due to illness, his current duties were excused as requested, he was appointed to the tozama-gumi (external group), entered the group of Numasawa Gen'kurō Hyōe, and served castle guard duty.

• In the third month of the same second (Dog) year, he was appointed as assistant town magistrate (machi-bugyō soeyaku).

• In the first month of the same third (Boar) year, regarding the succession to Saka Jūrōzaemon's position, since he was not appointed at this time, he was ordered to serve by consulting thoroughly on all eastern and western administrative matters.

• In the third month of the same year, because he served in busy duties and incurred labor expenses, he was given two ryō and two bu of gold as expense compensation without borrowing.
　　　However, from this time forward, the same amount was given annually.

• In the fifth month of the same fifth (Ox) year, he was appointed town magistrate and was granted a stipend of 150 koku as official salary.

• In the ninth month of the same year, regarding the current Yukawa construction project, although it was an extraordinary construction project different from previous years, it proceeded well...

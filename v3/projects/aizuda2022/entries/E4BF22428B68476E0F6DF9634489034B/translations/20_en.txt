- In the 12th month of Meiwa 4 (1767), granted the former residence site of Hara Oriuemon
- In the 5th month of An'ei 5 (1776), appointed as monogashira (military officer)
- In the 11th month of the same year, ordered to take custody of Hara Tetsutaro for thirty days, then transferred him to the household of Higuchi Yauemon
　　However, it is unclear from where he was originally taken into custody
- In the 9th month of Tenmei 6 (1786), assigned to patrol duties for inspections, and after three years of diligent service, rewarded with two bundles of cotton

- In the 3rd month of Kansei 2 (1790), granted permission to adopt Gosaburō, the younger brother of Yanase Kyūma, as son-in-law
- In the 8th month of Kansei 3, his son Gosaburō was appointed as guard substitute as requested
- In the intercalary 11th month of Kansei 6, when his son Gosaburō fled, he petitioned to return his stipend and focus on medical treatment due to illness making work difficult, which was granted
- In the 1st month of Kansei 7, granted permission to adopt Sukejirou, the second son of Akamatsu Chūsuke, as son-in-law
- In the 7th month of the same year, his son Sukejirou was appointed as guard substitute as requested

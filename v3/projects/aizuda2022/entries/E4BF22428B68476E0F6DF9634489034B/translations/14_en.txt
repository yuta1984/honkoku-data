Magouemon Chikanori (first name Kameichiro, later changed to Chikai, born on September 9th of the 7th year of Genroku, year of the Dog)

　Mother: Same as above (daughter of the Toyama family)

　Wife: Daughter of Ueda Hachirozaemon Ujiyoshi - divorced

　　　Daughter of Sasahara Denuemon Tadamichi - also divorced



　　1. In the 2nd month of the 4th year of Enkyō, year of the Rabbit,
　　　　a marriage arrangement between Ōtake Magouemon's wife and
　　　　Igari Shinzaburo's niece was approved as recorded in the official
　　　　records, but since no records remain with either party, this is noted here
　　2. In the 6th month of the 4th year of Hōei, year of the Boar,
　　　　he was granted the coming-of-age ceremony as requested by his father
　　3. In the 9th month of the 7th year of the same era, year of the Tiger,
　　　　his father's request to change his name to Magouemon was approved

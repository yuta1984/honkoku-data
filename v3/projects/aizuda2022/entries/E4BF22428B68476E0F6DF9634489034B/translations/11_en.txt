Jin'uemon Kanechika　　　(originally Heihachirō; born in Kanbun 2nd year, tiger year)

　　　Mother: concubine, family name unknown

　　　Wife: daughter of Tōyama Jirōza-emon Naoka (died of illness on Kanpō 3rd year, boar year, 2nd month, 12th day; Buddhist name: Seigan-in Jōyo Chishun Shinjo; burial site: same as above)

• Enpō 3rd year, rabbit year, 9th month: as requested by his father,
　　　was granted his first formal audience with the lord.

• Jōkyō 3rd year, tiger year, 6th month: was granted the hereditary stipend of 400 koku
　　　without change, was appointed to the outer retainer group,
　　　entered Shinoda Hanzaemon's unit, and served castle guard duty.

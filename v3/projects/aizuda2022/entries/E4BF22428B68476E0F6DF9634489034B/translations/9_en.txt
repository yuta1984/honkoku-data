Wife: Family name unknown. Date of death by illness, posthumous Buddhist name, and burial site unknown.

1. In the 12th month of Manji 1 (1658), through succession (atoshiki), he was granted 400 koku out of a 500-koku stipend, appointed to the gaiyo-gumi (external retainer group), and 100 koku was granted as divided land (bunchi) to his younger brother Gakubei.
   Note: He joined Wakisaka Uemon's group and served castle guard duty.

1. In the 3rd month of Kanbun 1 (1661), he went to Edo for guard duty (kinban), served one tour, and returned.

1. In the 1st month of the same 5th year (1665), he went to Edo for guard duty, served one tour, and returned.

1. In the 3rd month of Enpō 2 (1674), he was appointed as a squad leader (monotō).

1. In the same 4th year (1676) (month unknown), he went to Edo for guard duty, served one tour, and returned.

1. In the 8th month of the same 6th year (1678), he accompanied his lord on the official journey to Edo (sankin-kōtai).

1. From the 2nd month to the 6th month of Tenna 2 (1682), he was placed under the custody of Hyūga Gunbei, and in the same month was banished.

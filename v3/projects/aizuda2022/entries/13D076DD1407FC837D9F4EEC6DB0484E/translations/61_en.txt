1. In the seventh month of Bunka 8 (Year of the Sheep), upon being appointed as head of the guard unit leaders, he was granted permission to carry the commander's baton (saihai).

1. In the ninth month of Bunka 10 (Year of the Rooster), he participated in the hunting expedition.

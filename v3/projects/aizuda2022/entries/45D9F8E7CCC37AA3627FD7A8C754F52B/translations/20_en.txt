In the death register of Teidokoro Itokusan Jōkeiji Temple, it states "Kanbun 10th year, 
　　　year of the dog, 5th month, Kusaba Dōshin Shinji, father of Hosokawa Ichi-
　　　bee," but again the burial place 
　　　etc. are unknown
One　Kanbun 10th year, year of the dog, 11th month, in addition to his father's hereditary stipend of 13 koku and sustenance for 2 people, he was granted sustenance for 1 person as checkpoint fees, and served at both Ishimaguchi guardhouse and ship duties accounting for 26 years
One　Genroku 7th year, year of the dog, 5th month, my father lived in a place called Sekita within Mogami territory, and from there had been using the local name, but petitioned to change back to the ancestral name Hosokawa
One　Genroku 8th year, year of the boar, 5th month, his petition was granted and he was relieved of his official duties
One　Same year, 11th month, he was appointed as annual tribute wax official
One　Same 14th year, year of the snake, 2nd month, 23rd day, died of illness, posthumous Buddhist name Kitaka Ōshun Shinji, presiding priest at Itokusan Jōkeiji Temple, burial place Ōkuboyama

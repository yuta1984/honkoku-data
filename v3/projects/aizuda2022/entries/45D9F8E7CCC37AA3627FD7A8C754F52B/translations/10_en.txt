One　Same year, 8th month, granted a second salary increase for meal service attendant
One　Same year, 10th month, due to illness returned to Aizu
　　requested permission to recuperate, which was granted, and returned that same month
One　Same year, 12th month, as requested was relieved of official duties,
　　placed in non-official status, and ordered to
　　pay the small construction fee
One　Same 2nd year, year of the monkey, 11th month, having recovered from illness
　　and having been paying the small construction fee, humbly requested
　　to be appointed to some duty,
　　whereupon in the same 12th month was appointed
　　to the outer retainer group, joined Kayano Gonbei's unit,
　　and served castle guard duty
One　Same 13th year, year of the sheep, 11th month, requested
　　to adopt Sataro, younger brother of Kurokawachi Taro-
　　zaemon, which was granted
One　Meiwa 9, year of the dragon, 3rd month, 22nd day, died
　　of illness at age forty-seven, posthumous Buddhist name Seiryūin Shunzan
　　Hyūga Koji, burial place same as above

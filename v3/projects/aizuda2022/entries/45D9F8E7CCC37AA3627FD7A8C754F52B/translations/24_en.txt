Jūtasuke Chikanori　　Originally named Oriemon
　　Mother: daughter of Ikegami family
　　Wife: daughter of Sekiwaki Den'nai Shigeken
One　Hōreki 4th year, year of the dog, 11th month, one person from his father's provisional appointment, his stipend was reduced and he was granted 13 koku and stipend for 2 men, summoned as a guard in the annual rotation middle division

One　Same 8th year, year of the tiger, 2nd month, was appointed as an ashigaru foot soldier

One　Same year, 4th month, went up to Edo for rotation duty, returned in the 10th month of the same year

One　Same 9th year, year of the rabbit, 8th month, 25th day, died of illness, Buddhist name Shun-gaku Ryōi Koji, officiating priest, burial place same as above

　Daughter　　　　　　Wife of Nagasaka Hachibe Kanehiro
　　Mother: same as above

Banhichi Chikayoshi　《Marginal note: First name Gunnosuke, later Jōtayū》
　　Mother: Daughter of the Katōno clan
　　Wife: Daughter of Ikegami Yaemon Tomosaka
One　Genroku 14th year, year of the snake, 6th month, was granted a stipend of 10 koku sustenance for 2 people, and was summoned as a minor official of the accounting office
One　Hōei 2nd year, year of the rooster, 5th month, was appointed as checkpoint guard
One　Shōtoku 1st year, year of the rabbit, was appointed as new lending division official
One　Same 5th year, year of the sheep, 10th month, was granted an increase of sustenance for 1 person, and his seat rank was appointed above the office attendants
One　Kyōhō 4th year, year of the boar, 7th month, was granted an increase of 3 koku
One　Same 6th year, year of the ox, 3rd month, because the new lending office was abolished, he was maintained without official duties
One　Same 7th year, year of the tiger, 3rd month, [appointed as] accounting office minor official

Daughter of Arai Shinbei Shigeki　《Side note: Born in Kyōwa 2nd year, year of the dog, Bunsei 7th year, year of the monkey｜Died of illness on the 12th day of 4th month at age 23, Shinto｜funeral rites, posthumous title Toyohime Reisha burial｜Shrine burial site at Honjo Oshiage, within the estate of Lord Yoshikawa Genjūrō》
　Daughter of Takahashi Genjun Kōkō

One　Bunsei 8th year, year of the sheep, 1st month, from his late father's provisional stipend
　　one person's stipend was reduced and granted six persons' stipend, summoned to the new
　　guard unit, and during the period when he was not assigned various duties for training in arts,
　　ordered to submit small construction fees

One　Same 10th year, year of the rooster, 5th month, his request to change
　　his name to Tachū was granted

One　Same year, 9th month, attended the bird hunting expedition

One　Same year, intercalary 11th month, during this bird hunting expedition, his formation
　　and marching alignment were exceptionally well coordinated, the units were connected
　　and operations proceeded without delay, and it was recognized that he had been particularly
　　diligent in joint unit training exercises, and the entire group under his command was commended

One　Same year, 11th month, appointed to the small construction unit
　　and transferred to the supervision of the school magistrate

One　Same 11th year, year of the dog, 11th month, devoted himself to martial arts

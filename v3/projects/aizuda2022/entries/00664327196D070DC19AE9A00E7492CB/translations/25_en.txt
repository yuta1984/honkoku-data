1. Same 13th year of Tatsu (辰), 3rd month: Was appointed to the position of koban-gashira (chief of small guard unit)
1. Same year, 10th month: Went up for guard duty in Edo, returned home in the 5th month of the following Mi year as attendant for His Lordship's return journey
　　Note: Was granted one kosode robe for military service, one set of formal court dress, and ten pieces of reward silver
1. Same 16th year of Hitsuji (未), 6th month: Was appointed to the position of okosho-gashira (chief page)
1. Same year, 8th month: Petitioned to exchange residences with Ohara Rokudayu's mansion for convenience of location
1. Hoei 1st year of Saru (申), 3rd month: Went up for guard duty in Edo, returned home in the 9th month of the same year
　　Note: Served in military duties related to horse presentation and献上, and served once more thereafter
1. Same 2nd year of Tori (酉), 5th month: Presented two copper coins, one bearing moon and star designs, one bearing the character "kano" (叶)
1. Same year, 6th month: Petitioned to change name to Chiba Wasuke
　　Note: When he stated that his original family name was Chiba, he was told that while there was no Chiba family among the retainers nor elsewhere, since it was an early name adoption, it would be acceptable, and he was kindly advised to submit this petition, which he accordingly did

- In the same month, it was declared that he had served diligently in the quarters without absence, and in the same month, he was appointed to lodging arrangements duties for the lord's return to the domain and went down after completing this service.

- In the 5th month of the same year, because he carried out the lodging arrangements duties without delay, he was given one piece of silver as a reward.

- In the 3rd month of Meiwa 1st year (1764), he submitted a book on Ryūgi school martial arts.

- In the same month, he went up [to Edo] as part of the entourage for the lord's sankin-kōtai journey.
　However, it was declared that he served diligently without absence as part of the entourage during the journey.

- In the 5th month of Meiwa 2nd year (1765), after the lord's return to the castle, both father and son attended every martial arts training session.

- In the 10th month of the same year, he went up [to Edo] as part of the entourage for an unscheduled journey to the capital.
　However, in the same month, it was declared that he served diligently without absence as part of the entourage during the journey.

- In the 10th month of Meiwa 3rd year (1766), he went up to Edo for guard duty service.

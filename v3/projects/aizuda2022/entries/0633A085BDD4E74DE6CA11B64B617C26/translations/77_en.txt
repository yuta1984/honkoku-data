In the twelfth month of the same year, at the newly established fortifications in Sagami Province, he diligently served in both construction department and accounting office duties, and for this he was rewarded with two silver pieces.

In the twelfth month of the tenth year of the Tori cycle, since he was to serve as duty officer for the yoriai-gumi group, he was granted one bu of gold annually as expense support.

In the fifth month of the eleventh year of the Inu cycle, during the repair construction of the permanent quarters at Hiraneyama guard station, he handled matters well and was granted sake as a reward.

In the third month of the twelfth year of the I cycle, due to personnel reassignments, he was relieved of his concurrent duties, and it was announced that he had served diligently up to that point.

In the sixth month of the same year, he served as an instructor of Mugaku-ryu iaijutsu.

In the eighth month of the same year, he was ordered to prepare maps and drawings of the camp farmlands.

In the first month of the thirteenth year of the Ne cycle, because foreign ships were sighted off Shimoda in Izu Province, since he was on annual duty at Hiraneyama, he was stationed there, and immediately went to Nobi Cape as part of the strike force.

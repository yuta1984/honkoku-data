1. In the 4th month of Bunsei 2 (1819), year of the Rabbit, regarding the completion of 139 investigations since the previous spring, for his diligent hard work during this particularly busy period, he was granted 2 bundles of cotton as a reward.

1. In the 10th month of the same year, as circumstances changed and there was no need for expenses, his current duties were exempted, he was placed without official duties while maintaining his position, was appointed to serve as a reserve armored warrior during the lord's absence, his official salary of 2 koku 5 to continued as before, and his diligent service up to that point was officially recognized.

1. In the 1st month of Bunsei 3 (1820), year of the Dragon, regarding a boundary dispute case between Hōfuku-ji temple in Uragō village, Miura district, Sagami Province, and the village headman Jirōemon of the same village, he was ordered to go up to the capital for investigation duties. Upon completing preparations, he was to proceed urgently, and was appointed to serve under the supervision of Yokoyama Kazuma and Higuchi Genta. On the 28th day of the same month, he departed and was also appointed to investigate the murder case of the retired priest Onshūai at Jōraku-ji temple in Ashina village at the same location. The investigation duties were completed in the 9th month of the same year, and he returned.

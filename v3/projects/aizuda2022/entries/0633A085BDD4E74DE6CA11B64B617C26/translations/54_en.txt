First, in the 4th month of Hōreki 5, boar year (April 1755): He went up to Edo for guard duty service and returned in the 10th month of the same year.

First, in the 8th month of the same 9th year, rabbit year (August 1759): He inherited the family headship and was granted a fief of 150 koku without change.

First, in the 9th month of the same year: His request to adopt Tetsuma, the second son of Saka Jūrōzaemon, as his heir was granted.

First, in the 1st month of the same 12th year, horse year (January 1762): His commander Yanase Gumma was appointed to wakadoshiyori (junior councilor), and as his successor he was transferred to Yamazaki Tasuke's unit.

First, on the 26th day of the 8th month of the same year: He died of illness at the age of 55. His posthumous Buddhist name was Jōgaku Chisei Koji. Burial place: same as above.

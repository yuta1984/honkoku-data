Actually the third son of Kawahara Kanji Masayasu
　　Wife: daughter of Hosoya Wasaburō Eisei
- Tenmei 5 (Year of the Snake), 2nd month: Due to diligent practice in martial arts, was granted sake at the castle
- Kansei 4 (Year of the Rat), 12th month: Due to diligent practice in archery and completing the required number of arrows day and night without delay, was granted three sets of target arrows as a reward
- Same 6th year (Year of the Tiger), 8th month: Appointed as temporary employee for investigating old records in the accounting office examination section
- Same 7th year (Year of the Rabbit), 1st month: Due to contributing lumber transport as a voluntary contribution for the fire damage at the Shiba residence, was granted provisions at the school
- Same year, 6th month: Had three koku reduced from his father's stipend allocation, was granted nine koku and provisions for two people, and was summoned as yoriki
　　However, was ordered to continue serving as temporary employee in the accounting office examination section as before
- Same year, 9th month: Participated in the bird hunting drive
- Same year, 12th month: Appointed to regular administrative duties at the accounting office
- Same 8th year (Year of the Dragon), 10th month: Assistant to reform officer in the territorial division section

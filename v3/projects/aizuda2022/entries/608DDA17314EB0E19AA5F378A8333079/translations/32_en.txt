One: In Genroku 1st year (Year of the Dragon) (month unclear), he was appointed to the outer retainer group and entered Kamio Rokuzaemon's unit, serving castle guard duty.

One: In Hōei 3rd year (Year of the Dog) (month unclear), he was appointed as district magistrate (daikan).
One: In the 8th month of the same 6th year (Year of the Ox), due to poor performance in his duties, his official position was revoked and he was assigned to minor construction works (kobushin).
One: In Shōtoku 4th year (Year of the Horse) (month unclear), he was appointed as Inawashiro retainer.

• Same 3rd year (Year of the Dog), 2nd month: Was appointed to the position of Mountain Magistrate (yama-bugyō).

• Same 4th year (Year of the Boar), 2nd month: Was relieved of his official duties as requested and appointed to the non-official retainer group (muyaku-gumi).

• Same year, 4th month, 3rd day: Died of illness at the age of 56. Posthumous Buddhist name [not recorded].

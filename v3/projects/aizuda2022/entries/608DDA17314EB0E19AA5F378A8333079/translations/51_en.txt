Shōuemon Takatsura (original name: Yamasaburō)
　Mother same as above
　Wife: daughter of Tatsuno Sōuemon Munetsugi

　　According to records from Genroku 5th year (Year of the Monkey), 11th month, 27th day, a marriage arrangement between Ikegami Shōuemon and the daughter of Hara Mohē was requested and approved, but since both families lack genealogical records, they may have divorced, though details are unclear.

One: Enpō 8th year (Year of the Monkey), 3rd month - At age 14, was summoned to serve as page to Lord Hōshōin, granted standard court dress allowance, and served as a young attendant

One: Same year, 6th month - Went up to Edo for guard duty service, but due to illness returned home in the 10th month of the same year

One: Tenna 1st year (Year of the Rooster), 2nd month - Went up for the celebration of the Shogun's appointment, was ordered to undergo coming-of-age ceremony, and was granted a Kunisada sword with fittings and three ryō in gold

One: Same 3rd year (Year of the Boar), 10th month - Returned home

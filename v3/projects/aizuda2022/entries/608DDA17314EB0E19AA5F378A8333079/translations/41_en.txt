Hachiuemon Kōhan　　　　《Original name: Chiyokichi | Born on the 28th day of the 8th month of Kansei 12th year (Year of the Rabbit)》
　Actually the eldest son of Yamamoto Einosuke (a certain person)
　Wife: Daughter of Ozawa Tōshirō Shige　《Born on the 16th day of the 6th month of Bunka 1st year (Year of the Rat)》

One: In the 9th month of Bunka 12th year (Year of the Boar), during the bird hunting expedition, he went out as a proxy for his parent.
　　However, after this, he went out once more as a proxy, and on both occasions he received game as a gift.

One: In the 11th month of the same year, during the current bird hunting expedition, the formation and ranks were well-aligned, the squads maintained proper coordination, operations proceeded without delay, and as he had consistently devoted himself to joint squad training, this was recognized with approval, and his entire unit was commended together.
　　However, after this, he went out once as a proxy and was commended.

One: In the 12th month of the same year, regarding his perfect attendance at school for six years, this was considered exemplary and an official announcement was made.

One: In the 2nd month of Bunka 13th year (Year of the Rat), due to his diligent perfect attendance at school for eight years, he was given one silver coin as a reward.

It was reported that his performance was observed favorably, and all members of his unit were praised together.
　　　　However, from this time forward, he was consistently praised.

One: In the 3rd month of Kansei 5th year (Year of the Ox), since he was already experienced in sericulture, he was ordered to manage this matter together with Kawahara Katsutarō.
　　　　However, since this involved dealing with other domains, village duties, and various expenses, he was given 2 ryō and 2 bu annually as expense compensation.

One: In the 6th month of Kansei 7th year (Year of the Rabbit), he was ordered to travel to the Kawamata domain territory in Date County for discussions regarding silkworm seed investigation duties, and he fulfilled this service.
　　　　However, from this time forward, he frequently served in duties related to this work.

One: In the 3rd month of Kansei 8th year (Year of the Dragon), through compassionate consideration, he was granted a five-person stipend, making his total stipend eight people's worth, and he was appointed to the new guard unit.

One: In the 5th month of Kansei 11th year (Year of the Sheep), due to his diligent work in sericulture and planting mulberry seedlings, he was given one bundle of cotton as a reward.

One: In the 10th month of Kyōwa 1st year (Year of the Rooster), he served concurrently as an apprentice deputy official.

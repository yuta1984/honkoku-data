Wife: daughter of Ikegami Zenbei Takasato

• Enpō 6th year (Year of the Horse), 1st month: As requested by his father, was granted his first audience with the lord.

• Tenna 1st year (Year of the Rooster), 3rd month: As requested by his father, his wish to change his name to Tōdayū was approved.

• Genroku 6th year (Year of the Rooster), 8th month: As requested by his father, was appointed as a guard replacement attached to the tozama group, entered Hinata Saburōzaemon's unit, and served castle guard duty.

• Same 7th year (Year of the Dog), 7th month: Was granted 230 koku as inherited domain stipend without alteration.

• Same 8th year (Year of the Boar), 3rd month: Went up to Edo for guard duty service and returned in the 9th month of the same year.

• Hōei 1st year (Year of the Monkey), 10th month: Went up to Edo for guard duty service and returned in the 4th month of the following year (Year of the Rooster).
　　Note: During this tour of duty, he was appointed as temporary escort guard for the Nikkō proxy service and served in the lord's escort.

• Same 2nd year (Year of the Rooster), intercalary 4th month: Was appointed to the position of magistrate, but due to previous accounting irregularities, immediately submitted an apology.

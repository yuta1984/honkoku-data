　　　　Daughter of Yamada Genzaemon Tadayoshi
One: In the 11th month of Kan'en 1st year (Year of the Dragon), from his father's stipend provisions, two people's provisions were reduced and he was granted fifteen people's provisions, summoned to serve as an Inawashiro retainer.

One: In Hōreki 9th year (Year of the Rabbit), as requested, he was recalled to Wakamatsu and appointed to minor construction duties.

One: In Hōreki 12th year (Year of the Horse) 《month and day unclear》, he was appointed to the external retainer group, entered Takekawa Sukemon's unit, and served castle guard duty.

One: In the 3rd month of Meiwa 4th year (Year of the Boar), his request to adopt Tanaka Hisakata's third son Daikichi as son-in-law and heir was approved.

One: In the 4th month of the same year, as the adopted son Daikichi died of illness, his request to adopt Kurimura Yahisa's younger brother Torasaburō as son-in-law and heir was approved.

One: On May 6th of the same year, he died of illness at age fifty. Buddhist name: Kaitai-in Kenri Shinji. Burial place: same as above.

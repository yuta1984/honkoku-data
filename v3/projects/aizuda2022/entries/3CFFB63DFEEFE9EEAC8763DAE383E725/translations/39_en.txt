1. Kyōwa 2 (Year of the Dog, 1802) - He was appointed to regular guard duty (teiban kinmu).

1. Same year - He was appointed to assist with the paper accounting reform (ichishi go-kanjō aratame).

1. Same year 3 (Year of the Boar, 1803) - He was appointed to assist with the additional funds department (zōkakingata).

1. Bunka 2 (Year of the Ox, 1805), 9th month, 28th day - He died of illness at age 28. His posthumous Buddhist name was Kakuō Ryōdō Koji, burial place same as above.

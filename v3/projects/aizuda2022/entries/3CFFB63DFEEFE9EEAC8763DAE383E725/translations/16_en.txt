Wife: Adopted daughter of Suzuki Yaemon Shigetsugu, born on the 13th day of 6th month, Hōreki 3 (Rooster year)
　　　Actual father: daughter of Suzuki Jūbei Shigeun
1. In Hōreki 8 (Tiger year, month unknown), appointed as temporary employment for the lower official position in timber and small wooden board procurement for the Edo residence
　　　However, during this service, he traveled to various places including Hinoemata Village and Yokogawa Village of Nanzan imperial domains, Mibu, Kanuma, Nikkō shrine lands, etc., conducted inspections (saihan), and delivered materials to the Edo residence for storage
1. In the same 13th year (Sheep year), 9th month, employed as an imperial domain dōshin (low-ranking samurai)
　　　However, during this service, he was dispatched multiple times to Edo, Echigo, Fukushima, and Nanzan
1. In the same month, his request to change his family name to Satō was granted
1. In Meiwa 3 (Dog year), 6th month, appointed as temporary employment for the Echigo domain administration establishment role
1. In the same year, 12th month, due to his diligent service, [granted] formal dress privileges

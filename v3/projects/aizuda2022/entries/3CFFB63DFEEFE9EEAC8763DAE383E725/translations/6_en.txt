One　Same 8th year (1788), monkey year, 6th month, all middle-rank retainers were appointed as banner assistants and ordered to continue serving together at the West Middle Gate as before
One　Same month, appointed to concurrent duties in the patrol section
One　Same year, 12th month, while serving concurrent patrol duties, was appointed to investigate old records at the town office, serving until the 12th month of the rooster year
One　Kansei 1st year (1789), rooster year, 12th month, received commendation for perfect attendance in banner duties and diligent service
One　Same 3rd year (1791), boar year, 7th month, there was information that the shogunate prisoner Yokochi Ukon was in the Echigo province area, and when ordered to capture him through the patrol section, was appointed as chief through official appointment, led seven men including patrol officers and rural patrol personnel to Echigo province, and captured him at Gosen in Echigo Shirakawa domain. However, it was not Ukon but a masterless samurai wearing swords, and Ukon's group had stolen and sold off all three sets of long and short swords taken from three locations in the domain. Therefore, took the criminals around to Mizuhara domain and Shibata area, negotiated with the authorities there, recovered all the stolen goods without payment, and returned to the domain in the 8th month

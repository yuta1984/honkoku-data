1. In the 8th month of Anei 3 (1774), Uma year, he was appointed to the main salary position.

1. In the intercalary 12th month of Anei 4 (1775), Hitsuji year, for official duties related to the miscellaneous storehouse currency custody, he worked through the night continuously, for which he was granted one ryo of silver as expense compensation.

1. In the 5th month of Anei 7 (1778), Inu year, he was temporarily appointed as a subordinate officer at the lending office.

1. In the 1st month of Anei 8 (1779), I year, regarding the account investigation during the handover at the miscellaneous storehouse, he was appointed to this investigation duty while remaining in his temporary subordinate role. During his service, he worked diligently from early morning until very late at night without rest days, for which he received official commendation.

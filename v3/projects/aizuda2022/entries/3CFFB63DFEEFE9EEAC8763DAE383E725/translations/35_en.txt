The matter of being granted permission to do as requested
One: In the intercalary sixth month of the same year, having diligently served for several years in financial administration matters, regarding the shortage of principal funds, he arranged for additional capital based on his proposals. Concerning castle duties, although personnel had recently been reduced, he coordinated well with various departments to ensure no disruption to official duties, managed to reduce expenditures, and regarding the distribution of stipend provisions to retainers, this being an initial undertaking, due to his diligent management, twelve ryō in gold was converted to four koku, and his official salary of one ryō and three bu was converted to three koku and five to and granted to him.

One: In the twelfth month of the same year, when meals were provided to those returning from garrison duty in Ezo (Hokkaido), he temporarily managed the receipt and payment of necessary charcoal, firewood, and other supplies for this purpose, and as a result was granted sake at the castle.

One: In the third month of the sixth year of Mi (Snake), regarding the incident of missing money from both treasury offices, the account books and certificates from the minor treasury officials' transactions were submitted to the judicial office in the form of accounting records, and he was ordered to investigate using these books and certificates during the inquiry period, and promptly conducted the investigation and underwent inspection.

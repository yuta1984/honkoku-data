Five times, and once to Niigata port for duty assignments

1. In the 8th month, Kyōwa 2 (Dog year), when Bakufu officials had previously come to investigate rice market prices, he served diligently from beginning to end and was granted 1 koku as official duty allowance

1. In the 4th month, Bunka 2 (Ox year), appointed concurrently as official investigator and junior official for the Southern Imperial Domain survey
   During this service: three trips to Edo, twice stationed at Ojiya camp, four times stationed at Okashima camp, twice assigned to Niigata

1. In the 12th month of the same year, efficiently resolved various disputes during his posting at Ojiya camp, and when Bakufu officials came for inspection, he responded well and served diligently, for which he was granted 5 ryō of silver

1. In the intercalary 6th month, 5th year of the same era (Dragon year), he diligently handled emergency financial procurement matters for the head office, and was rewarded with 5 ryō of silver

1. In the 5th month, 8th year of the same era (Sheep year), regarding the Hakodate rice transport, though this was a new matter, he handled it well and during emergency duties showed great diligence

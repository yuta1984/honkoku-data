Item: In the tenth month of the same year, he went to Edo to serve his rotation duty.
　　However, while in military service,
　　he was granted his first audience with Lord Dotsune,
　　and was consequently appointed as temporary messenger,
　　and received acknowledgment that he
　　performed his duties without incident.
Item: In the seventh month of Kanpō 2nd year, Dog year, he was
　appointed as squad leader (monogashira).
Item: In the fourth month of Hōreki 7th year, Ox year, as requested, he was
　relieved of his official duties, and his son Tetsuroku was
　appointed as his replacement guard.
Item: In the seventh month of the same 11th year, Snake year, as requested, he was
　granted retirement.
Item: On the tenth day of the third month of An'ei 7th year, Dog year, at age seventy-five,
　he died of illness, posthumous name Anshūin-den Shōō Chisan
　Koji, burial place same as above

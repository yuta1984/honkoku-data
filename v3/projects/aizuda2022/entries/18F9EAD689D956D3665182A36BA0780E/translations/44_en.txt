Daughter (Chise) - Born on February 14, 1799 (Kansei 11). Wife of Ohara Sozaemon Toshikatsu (divorced).
　Mother: daughter of the Takahashi family

Sataró Tamesan - Born on April 19, 1804 (Bunka 1)
　Mother: same as above (daughter of the Takahashi family)
　Wife: daughter of Saigó Gengo Chikató (Misa), born on October 1, 1809 (Bunka 6)

1. In November 1815 (Bunka 12), as requested by his father,
　he was granted his first audience with the lord

1. In December 1821 (Bunsei 4), he inherited the family stipend
　of 400 koku without reduction and was appointed as an outer retainer
　attached to the senior councilor. For the purpose of studying the arts,
　he was not assigned regular duties and was ordered to
　pay kobushin-ryó (maintenance fee for unassigned samurai).
　　However, he was placed in the first unit.

1. In July of the same 6th year (Bunsei 6, 1823), having devoted himself to literary and martial arts
　and having reached the prescribed standard, his kobushin-ryó was
　exempted, he was ordered to report for duty, and
　began serving guard duty at the castle.

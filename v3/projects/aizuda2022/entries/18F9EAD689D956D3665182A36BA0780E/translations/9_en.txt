Item: In the eleventh month of Meireki 1, Sheep year, he accompanied Lord Hōsakuin and went up [to Edo], served one tour of guard duty, and returned.

Item: In the ninth month of Manji 1, Dog year, he went up to Edo for guard duty service and served one tour before returning.

Item: In the eighth month of Kanbun 2, Tiger year, when the head Ikoma Gohei was relieved of his duties, he became part of Hori Hanemon's unit, which succeeded to the position.

Item: In the eighth month of the third year, Rabbit, he went up to Edo for guard duty service and served one tour before returning.

Item: In the eighth month of the fifth year, Snake, as requested, his son Rokubei was appointed as his substitute for guard duties.

Item: On the twenty-first day of the seventh month of Enpō 7, Sheep year, he died of illness at the age of eighty-five. His posthumous Buddhist name was Kōgetsu-in Issō Dōshū Koji. His burial place is Shōun-zan Zenryū-ji temple in Kita-Aoki village.

He joined the first group and served castle guard duty

In the 3rd month of Bunsei 1st Tiger year, during the recent hunting expedition when the hunting grounds were changed for the first attempt, the formation and marching of the warriors were exceptionally well-ordered, and the coordination of the units operated smoothly without delay. Having always devoted special effort to joint training exercises and managing them well, he received praise for these accomplishments

In the 6th month of Bunsei 4th Snake year, due to illness he submitted his kobushin stipend and was granted permission to convalesce in the future

1. On the 21st day of the 10th month of the same year, he died of illness at age 53. His posthumous Buddhist name was Chūtei-in-den Setsu-gaku Jun-kō Koji, buried in the same place as above

Item: In the eleventh month of the same 4th year, Boar year, he went up to Edo as a messenger to inquire about the lord's attendance procession.

Item: In the sixth month of Shōtoku 1, Rabbit year, he was appointed as御奏者番 (gososha-ban, ceremonial attendant).

Item: In the ninth month of the same year, he went up to Edo for guard duty service and returned in the third month of the following Dragon year.
　　However, as a bestowed item for the pacification, he was granted one set of formal court dress (kamishimo).
Item: In the ninth month of the same 3rd year, Snake year, when he went up to Edo for guard duty service, he was appointed as supervisor of foot soldiers and returned in the third month of the following Dragon year.
Item: In the third month of Kyōhō 1, Monkey year, he went up to Edo for guard duty service and returned in the ninth month of the same year.

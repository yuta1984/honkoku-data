Tosho Tameyoshi　　《marginal note: initially Jirōsaburō, also Takeemon｜initially Tamesumi, born in Kanbun 7, Sheep year》
　Actually the second son of Nomura Kyūbei Toshinage
　Wife: daughter of adoptive father Matasaemon Tamehira《marginal note: born in Kanbun 9, Rooster year, died of illness on New Year's Day of Genroku 14｜Snake year, January｜posthumous name Jakukō Ryōen Daishi, burial｜place same as above》
　　Wife of Ryūzōji Katsunosuke Mitsuhiro《marginal note:｜named Toshi》
　　　　　　　　　　　《marginal note:｜born on the twenty-third day of the seventh month of Tenna 2, Dog year｜died of illness on the fourteenth day of the sixth month of Hōreki 8, Tiger year｜posthumous name Kahōin-den Fūrai Eryō｜Daishi, burial place same as above》

Item: In the twelfth month of Jōkyō 2, Ox year, as requested by his father, he was appointed to guard duty and served castle guard duty in Hori Hanemon's unit.

Item: In the fourth month of the same 3rd year, Tiger year, he went up to Edo for guard duty service, completed one tour of duty, and returned.

Item: In the ninth month of Genroku 2, Snake year, he went up to Edo for guard duty service and returned in the third month of the following Horse year.

Item: In the twelfth month of the same 4th year, Sheep year, he inherited the family headship and was granted a stipend of 300 koku without change.

Item: In the third month of the same 6th year, Rooster year, due to the passing of En'yōin-sama, for paying respects...

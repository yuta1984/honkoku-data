Matasaemon Tamehira《marginal note: Initially Rokubei, then Rokusaemon, after retirement Gesshin | Born in Genna 7, Rooster year》
　Mother: Daughter of the Baba clan
　Wife: Daughter of Katō Sukesaemon Yoshinobu《marginal note: Born in Kan'ei 17, Dog year [possibly Dog is an error for Dragon year], died of illness on the first day of the seventh month of Shōtoku 5, Sheep year | Posthumous name Shōju-in Shō■ei Choku Daishi, burial place same as above》

Item: In the eighth month of Kanbun 5, Snake year, as his father had requested, he was appointed as substitute for guard duties and served castle guard duty in Hori Hanemon's unit.

Item: In the tenth month of Enpō 2, Tiger year, he went up to Edo for guard duty service and served one tour before returning.

Item: In the ninth month of the seventh year, Sheep, he was granted the hereditary stipend of 300 koku without change.

Item: In the eighth year, Monkey《marginal note: month unknown》, his request to adopt Tosho, the second son of Nomura Kyūbei, as son-in-law was granted.

Item: In the tenth month of Tenna 1, Rooster year, he went up to Edo for guard duty service and served one tour before returning.

Item: In the first month of the third year, Boar, Shibata Shichisaemon-sama

Hachibei Shigeyuki　<<Originally named Hachinosuke, born on the 28th day of the 5th month of Enkyō 1 (1744)>>

　　This Hachibei absconded and was later employed by Inagaki Shinano-no-kami. While serving in Osaka, he died of illness at that place on the 8th day of the 1st month of An'ei 4 (1775) at age 32. Having no heir, the line was discontinued. Buddhist name Hōgetsu Jōen, buried at Kohasedani Zenkōji temple in Settsu Province, Osaka.

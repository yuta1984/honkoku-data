1. In the 3rd month of the 9th year of Bunsei (1826), using funds from regulatory surplus money to increase procurement and management of the lord's clothing, there had been outstanding payments for several years' worth of bestowed items, but when these accounts were settled, it was announced that 2 bu of gold would be granted as a reward.

1. In the 5th month of the same year, he was appointed to manage cost-saving measures for the lord's personal expenses during his residence in the domain.

1. In the 10th month of the same year, regarding the management of cost-saving measures for the lord's personal finances, he was ordered to continue serving in the same manner as during the lord's residence in the domain from then on.

Wife　Daughter of Kashiwabara Shigeharu Shigeaki　<<Born on the 21st day of the 2nd month of Kan'en 1 (1748), died of illness on the 18th day of the 8th month of Bunsei 5 (1822) at age 75, Buddhist name Honshō-in Myōshin Nisshō, burial place same as above>>

1. In the 12th month of Hōreki 8 (1758), due to his late father's diligent service, he was granted a stipend for two people and appointed under the supervision of the Finance Commissioner.

1. In the 9th month of the same 10th year (1760), in addition to his previous stipend for two people, he was granted 3 ryō in promissory notes, appointed as a ceremonial monk (kōshiki bōzu), and changed his name to Chōeki.

1. In the 2nd month of the same 14th year (1764), he was appointed to front court duty.

1. In the 9th month of the same year, due to his diligent service during the summer when there was a shortage of personnel, he was granted 100 hiki of gold as a reward.

1. In Meiwa 7 (1770), he was appointed to the provisional position of personal attendant to the lord's private quarters.

1. In the 12th month of An'ei 1 (1772), he was appointed as personal attendant to the lord's private quarters.

1. In the 1st month of the same 5th year (1776), due to his diligent service, he was appointed to receive an annual stipend allocation.

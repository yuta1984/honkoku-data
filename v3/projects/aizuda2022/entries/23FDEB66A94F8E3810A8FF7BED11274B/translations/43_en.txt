One: In the intercalary 11th month of the same 10th year (1813), he was appointed to concurrently serve as instructor at the academy (shogakuryō), and was ordered to perform his duties under the supervision of the academic magistrate (gakujū bugyō).
One: In the 11th month of the same 11th year (1814), for working diligently and also working hard on investigating old records at the secretariat and on copying work, he was granted five pieces of silver as a reward.
One: In the 6th month of the same 12th year (1815), since there were accumulated unbestowed items, by special consideration one white small-sleeved kimono was granted.

One: In the 4th month of the same 13th year (1816), he accompanied the lord's journey to Kyoto, departing and going up, then returning in the 6th month of the same year.
One: In the 8th month of the same year, he was appointed to the position overseeing secretarial work and administrative streamlining at government offices.

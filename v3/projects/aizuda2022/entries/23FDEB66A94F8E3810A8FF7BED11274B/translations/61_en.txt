In the 12th month of Kyōwa 2, year of the Dog (1802), due to his diligent service, careful attention to various cost-saving measures with excellent management, and achieving considerable reduction in expenditures, he was exceptionally granted directly a stipend certificate of 4 ryō 2 bu of gold converted to a 9-koku certificate, plus an additional 2-koku role salary.

1. In the 11th month of Kyōwa 3, year of the Boar (1803), he was appointed to the cost-saving management position at the senior retainer's office.

1. In the 1st month of Bunka 3, year of the Tiger (1806), while continuing his current duties, he was appointed to the general cost-saving management position, and due to his diligent service thus far, he was granted the status of second-rank assembly seat among the close attendants, and was ordered to continue his storehouse attendant duties as before.

1. In the same month, regarding his appointment to the general cost-saving management position, he was ordered to attend various administrative offices as needed for his duties and to consult and properly manage matters related to expenditure reduction.

　　Wife: Daughter of Shimada Tomouemon Munemasa - divorced
　　　　Daughter of Soma Chūzaemon Shigetaka - born on the 19th day of the 8th month of Kansei 2 (1790)

One: In the 3rd month of Kansei 6 (1794), he was granted his first audience as requested by his father.

One: In the 1st month of Bunka 5 (1808), he accompanied his father as an attendant to Soya in Ezo (Hokkaido).
　　　However, at the time of departure, he was granted an audience at the Ōshoin, given leave, served sake at the Koshoin, and then served sake at the Ōshoin.

One: In the 11th month of the same year, when he returned with his father, he was granted an audience at the Ōshoin while still in traveling clothes.
　　　However, sake and delicacies were provided at the lodging before arriving in Wakamatsu.

One: In the 8th month of the 6th year (Bunka 6, 1809), he went as an attendant for Ezo guard duty, joined the armored warriors, entered the guard unit, and performed various duties, for which he was granted three bundles of cotton.

One: In the 12th month of the 10th year (Bunka 10, 1813), his request to change his name to Chūuemon was granted.

One: In the 9th month of the 14th year (Bunka 14, 1817), he inherited the family headship with a fief of 300 koku without change, was appointed as an external retainer attached to the chief retainer, and assigned to the 1st unit.

Izaemon Shigetsugu　《formerly Hannosuke, born in Shōhō 2 (1645), year of the rooster》
　Mother: daughter of the Suzuki family
　Concubine: family name unknown
1. In Kanbun 7 (1667), year of the sheep, tenth month: As succession inheritance, he was granted 300 koku out of 350 koku of the hereditary stipend, with 50 koku allocated to his younger brother Hikozaemon. He served castle guard duty under Numazawa Kurōbee's unit.

1. In the same 14th year (1674), year of the tiger, fourth month: He went up to Edo for guard duty service, returning in the tenth month of the same year.

1. In Enpō 4 (1676), year of the dragon, second month: Due to the death from illness of unit commander Numazawa Kurōbee, he was transferred to Kamio Rokuzaemon's unit as successor to the position.

1. In Tenna 1 (1681), year of the rooster, fourth month: He went up to Edo for guard duty service, returning in the tenth month of the same year.

1. In Jōkyō 1 (1684), year of the rat, eleventh month: Due to the lord receiving a grant of an upper residence and an increase in retainer numbers, he went up for guard duty service, returning in the third month of the following year of the ox.

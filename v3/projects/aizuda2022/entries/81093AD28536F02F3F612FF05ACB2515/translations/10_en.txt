　Izaemon (4th generation adoptee　　　　) Kitchō　　《Initially called Teijirō, also Shigehachi｜Born in Hōei 2 (1705), year of the rooster》
　　Biological father: Second son of Higuchi Tomouemon Mitsutoshi  
　　Wife: Daughter of adoptive father Izaemon Shigetada《Born in Kyōhō 1 (1716), year of the monkey; died of illness on the 13th day of the 11th month of Kyōhō 19 (1734), year of the tiger; posthumous name Jikōin Kansō Myōkō Daishi; buried in the same place》　　
　　Daughter of Numasawa Gonbei Norimichi《Born in Kyōhō 3 (1718), year of the dog; died of illness on the 20th day of the 6th month of Bunka 4 (1807), year of the rabbit; posthumous name Gyokuhōin Hōzan Chikyō Daishi; buried in the same place》
1. In Genbun 5 (1740), year of the monkey, intercalary 7th month: He inherited the fief of 300 koku without change, was appointed to the gaiyo group, entered Hinata Saburōuemon's unit, and served guard duty at the castle.

1. In the same 3rd year (1742), year of the boar, 9th month: He was commanded through Higuchi Tanouemon to demonstrate his archery skills before the lord, and performed the demonstration in front of the target.

1. In Enkyō 1 (1744), year of the rat, 3rd month: He went up to Edo as escort duty during the lord's visit to the shogun and returned in the 10th month of the same year.
　　　However, when he was appointed as temporary escort guard for the journey, in the same 4th month, he served diligently as temporary escort guard during the journey without any absences.

1. In Genroku 7 (1694), year of the dog, third month: He was granted his first audience with his lord as requested by his father.

1. In the same 10th year (1697), year of the ox: He was granted the hereditary stipend of 300 koku without change, appointed to the outer retainer group (gaisama-gumi), and served castle guard duty in Kamio Rokuzaemon's group.

1. In the same 13th year (1700), year of the dragon, ninth month: He went up to Edo for duty service and returned in the third month of the following year of the snake.

1. In Hōei 2 (1705), year of the rooster, fourth month: He went up to Edo for duty service and returned in the tenth month of the same year.

1. In the same 7th year (1710), year of the tiger, tenth month: He went up to Edo for duty service and returned in the fourth month of the following year of the rabbit.
　　However, he was appointed as temporary escort guard for the lord's journey down from Edo.

1. In Shōtoku 5 (1715), year of the sheep, twelfth month: He was appointed as group leader (kumigashira).

1. In Kyōhō 3 (1718), year of the dog, tenth month: He went up to Edo for duty service and returned as an escort when the lord came down in the fourth month of the following year of the boar.

1. In the same 5th year (1720), year of the rat, second month: His position as group leader was terminated, so he was appointed to the outer retainer group and entered Hinata Saburōemon's group to serve castle guard duty.

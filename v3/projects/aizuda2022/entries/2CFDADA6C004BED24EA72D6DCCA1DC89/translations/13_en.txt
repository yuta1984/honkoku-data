He was appointed to the Tozama-gumi unit and served castle guard duty in Sugita Gorobee's group.

1. In the 9th month of Tenmei 1st year, year of the ox (1781), due to the death by illness of group leader Sugita Gorobee, the successor position was transferred to Yamazaki Sasuke's group.

1. In the 2nd month of the same 3rd year, year of the rabbit (1783), he was granted the family succession stipend of 150 koku without change.

1. In the 4th month of the same year, his petition to adopt Tomejirou, the second son of Takahashi Touhachi, as son-in-law and heir was approved.

1. In the 2nd month of the same 6th year, year of the horse (1786), it was proclaimed that he had served ten years of castle guard duty without negligence.

1. In the 3rd month of the same 8th year, year of the monkey (1788), he was appointed as assistant to the wakadoshiyori (junior councilor).

1. In the intercalary 6th month of Kansei 1st year, year of the rooster (1789), it was proclaimed that his position as assistant to the wakadoshiyori was changed to assistant to the third-group kar≈ç (chief retainer).

1. In the 9th month of the same 5th year, year of the ox (1793), he participated in a hunting expedition (oitori-gari).

In the 4th month of the same 6th year, year of the tiger (1794), regarding the hunting expedition of the previous autumn, it was proclaimed that the marching formation and ranks were well-coordinated, which was attributed to his diligent practice beforehand, and the entire group received commendation.

However, since the rotation duty was reduced, he came down in the 9th month of the same year.

1. In the 3rd month of Hōreki 4th year, year of the dog (1754), as petitioned by his superior Ueda Hachirōemon, retirement was granted, and he was transferred to Hyūga Genemon's group as successor to the position.

1. In the 5th month of the same 6th year, year of the rat (1756), he was granted his hereditary stipend of 150 koku without any changes.

1. In the 10th month of the same 14th year, year of the monkey (1764), as petitioned by his superior Hyūga Genemon, retirement was granted, and he was transferred to Miyake Magobee's group as successor to the position.

1. In the 9th month of Meiwa 1st year, year of the monkey (1764), he went to Yamagata for duty service and returned in the 3rd month of the following year of the rooster.

1. In the 12th month of An'ei 4th year, year of the sheep (1775), his superior Miyake Magobee was appointed as wakadoshiyori (junior councilor), and he was transferred to Sugita Gorobee's group as successor to the position.

1. In the 1st month of the same 5th year, year of the monkey (1776), as petitioned, his son Chūzaemon was appointed as guard duty substitute.

1. In the 2nd month of Tenmei 3rd year, year of the rabbit (1783), as petitioned, retirement was granted.

1. On the 29th day of the 2nd month of Kansei 5th year, year of the ox (1793), he died of illness at age 79. His posthumous Buddhist name was Kengai Jushō Koji, burial site: same as above.

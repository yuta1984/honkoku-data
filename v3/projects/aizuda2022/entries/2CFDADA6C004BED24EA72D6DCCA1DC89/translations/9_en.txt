1. In the 5th month of the same 12th year, year of the sheep (1727), due to illness, upon petition by his superior, he was relieved of escort duty and appointed to the outer retainer group, entered Yamazaki Sasuke's group, and served guard duty at the castle.

1. In the 9th month of the same 18th year, year of the ox (1733), due to the death by illness of his superior Yamazaki Sasuke, he was transferred to Numasawa Gonbee's group as successor to the position.

1. In the 4th month of the same 20th year, year of the rabbit (1735), he went up to Edo for duty service and came down in the 10th month of the same year.

1. In the 7th month of Enkyō 3rd year, year of the tiger (1746), as petitioned, his son Shirōzaemon was appointed as his guard duty substitute.

1. On the 29th day of the 3rd month of Hōreki 6th year, year of the rat (1756), he died of illness at the age of 63. His posthumous Buddhist name was Daiō Ryōtetsu Koji. Burial site: same as above.

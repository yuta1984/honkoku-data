[Continuing from] what was not recorded, while Takemon was in service, his biological father Tazaemon's past meritorious service was also recognized, and of course being a person with good reason, the origins of Tazaemon are recorded here.

1. In Kan'ei 16, year of the rabbit, 2nd month, in Edo he was granted the regular stipend and summoned to serve as a foot soldier (ashigaru).

1. In the same [Kan'ei] 20, year of the sheep, 8th month, he served as attendant during the lord's entry into the domain and went down [to Aizu], but since he had been stationed in Edo for a long time, he remained at rest until Shōhō 1, year of the monkey.

1. In Shōhō 1, year of the monkey, 8th month, being a hereditary retainer and moreover from a lineage of consistently capable men, he was sent to serve Iwahiko-sama. [He was told] to make preparations privately as convenient and proceed to Edo, and that eventually he would be promoted, according to his lordship's intention.

1. In the same year, 10th month, he proceeded to Edo.

1. In Keian 1, year of the rat, 4th month, he was appointed as adopted son according to his adoptive father Zenbei's petition.

1. In the same 4th year, year of the rabbit, 4th month, he was appointed as Iwahiko-sama's personal attendant (懐守).

First, in the same 5th year, year of the rat, 3rd month, his previous voucher money was converted to ten koku and two-person stipend, and he was appointed to the position of treasury money receiving and disbursement officer.

First, in the same 6th year, year of the ox, 3rd month, because he performed well in the Mita construction project, he was granted three pieces of silver as a reward.

First, in the same 9th year, year of the dragon, 3rd month, he went up to Edo and skillfully negotiated the reduction of interest on domain loans of over 70,000 ryō, and also arranged for some to become interest-free, for which he was granted two pieces of silver as a reward.

First, in the same 11th year, year of the horse (month unknown), as he requested, he was relieved of his official duties and placed in a non-official position, with his seat ranking placed above Mori Shinkurō, following the junior clerks.

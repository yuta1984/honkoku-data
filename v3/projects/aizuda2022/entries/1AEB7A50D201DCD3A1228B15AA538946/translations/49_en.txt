He was granted eight koku and a two-person stipend, summoned, and appointed as temporary head of rice transport porters.

First, in the same 5th year, year of the rat, 3rd month, he was appointed as head of rice transport porters.

First, in Shōtoku 3, year of the snake, 3rd month, he was appointed to both positions of Kyoto loan office and Osaka rice payment office through official personnel designation, and because he served both roles alone, he was granted three ryō of gold.

First, in the same 4th year, year of the horse (month unknown), because he skillfully managed and executed the financial affairs of the Tone River construction project, he was granted three ryō of gold as a reward.

First, in the same 5th year, year of the sheep, 11th month, he was granted an additional stipend increase of two koku.

First, in Kyōhō 1, year of the monkey (month unknown), he went up to Edo and skillfully managed rice buyback operations, for which he was granted three ryō of gold as a reward.

First, in the same year, 11th month, when Shinoda Yūsen absconded after incurring a deficit of 10,000 bags of transported rice, he was reprimanded for failing to properly oversee the rice delivery process and for his mismanagement, and his stipend was revoked.

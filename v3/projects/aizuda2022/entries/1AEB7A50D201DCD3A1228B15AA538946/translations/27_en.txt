Regarding the matter of succession, although there may have been some consideration about how this should be handled, because he had served most faithfully since the Mogami period, he was granted 100 koku out of the 150 koku stipend and appointed to the tozama group, joining Hinata Hannosuke's unit.

In the same 2nd year, year of the snake (month unknown), when Hinata Hannosuke was appointed as castellan of Inawashiro Castle, he was transferred to Wakisaka Uemon's unit as his successor.

From the same 3rd year, year of the horse (1654), 12th month, he served castle guard duty.

In the 1st year of Kanbun, year of the ox (1661), 3rd month, he went up to Edo for guard duty and returned in the intercalary 8th month of the same year.
　Note: After this, he served one tour of guard duty.

In the same 6th year, year of the horse (1666) (month unknown), when unit commander Wakisaka Uemon was relieved of his post as requested, he was transferred to Sugita Gorohyōe's unit as his successor.

In the 4th year of Enpō, year of the dragon (1676), 3rd month, when unit commander Sugita Gorohyōe was relieved of his post as requested, he was transferred to Akahane Niemon's unit as his successor.

In the same 7th year, year of the sheep (1679) (month unknown), when unit commander Akahane Niemon was granted permission to retire to the countryside as requested, he was transferred to Tanaka Kahyōe's unit as his successor.

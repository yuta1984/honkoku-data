Sōyo Dōnan, buried at Shōgakuzan Amida Temple

Zenjibyōe Mitsufusa (originally named Zenbyōe, born in Kanbun 1, year of the ox)
　Mother: Daughter of the Miyashita family
　Wife: Daughter of Hideyama (某)

First, in the 8th month of Tenna 1, year of the rooster, he was granted a stipend of 8 koku and provisions for 2 attendants, and was employed as an attendant at the rice transport office.

First, in the 9th month of Genroku 12, year of the rabbit, he was appointed as head of rice transport porters.

First, in the 2nd month of Hōei 2, year of the rooster, he was granted an audience during a lord's procession.

First, (year and month unknown) his request to adopt Hachiemon, the second son of Akahani Kibyōe, as his heir was approved.

First, on the 7th day of the 11th month of the same 4th year, year of the boar, he died of illness at age 53. His Buddhist posthumous name was Ganyo Jōsei, buried at the same location as above.

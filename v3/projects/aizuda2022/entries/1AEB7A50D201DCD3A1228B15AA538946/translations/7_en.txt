He was ordered to continue serving in the same manner as before while becoming accustomed to his successor role.
1. In the first month of the second year of Genroku (1689, year of the snake), for the domain retainers' relief fund business, Akabane Kichizaemon was appointed, so he was ordered through official designation to serve in this capacity.
1. In the tenth month of the third year (1690, year of the horse), he was appointed as accounting inspector, and for serving concurrent duties beyond his main role, he was granted rewards of one bu in gold and one piece of silver twice.
1. In the fifth month of the eighth year (1695, year of the boar), he was appointed as head of provisions.
　　However, during this service, each time he went up for the five guard duties rotation, he was granted one piece of silver each time.
1. In the first month of the thirteenth year (1700, year of the dragon), he was granted an additional five koku.
1. In the seventh month of the sixteenth year (1703, year of the sheep), as requested, he was relieved of his current duties and appointed to a position without specific duties under the magistrate's supervision, ranked next to the head of the kitchen.
1. In the ninth month of the first year of Hōei (1704, year of the monkey), when he petitioned to be appointed to an appropriate official position, he was told that an official position would be appointed later, and was ordered to assist when the accounting inspectors were busy with official business.
1. In the fifth month of the second year (1705, year of the rooster), he was appointed to the regular treasury role.

　　However, after this he was commended many times.

1. In the same 7th year, year of the horse, as requested by unit commander Hyuga Saburōemon, he was relieved of his duties and was reassigned to Ohara Taitō's unit as a replacement.

1. In the 3rd month of the same 12th year, year of the boar, it was announced that he had served castle guard duty diligently for ten years without negligence.

1. In the 12th month of the same year, regarding training at the Sannomaru training ground, his formation and marching were well coordinated and he achieved top rank for three years, so his entire unit was given sake.

1. In the 2nd month of the same 14th year, year of the ox, as requested by unit commander Ohara Taitō, he was granted relief from duty and was reassigned to Ueda Hachirōemon's unit as a replacement.

1. In the 10th month of the same year, for his diligence in military drill, he was given one new spear as a reward.

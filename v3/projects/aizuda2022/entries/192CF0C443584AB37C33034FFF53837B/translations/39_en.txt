Wife　Sister of Hattori Dōsaku, details of death or separation unknown
　　　Daughter of Akahane Gizaemon (Died of illness on Kyōhō 5th year (1720), 12th month, 2nd day. Buddhist name: Shinkyō Ekō Shinnyo, buried in the same location as above)
　　　Daughter of Hyūga Genemon Ujifusa (Died of illness on Hōreki 3rd year (1753), 9th month, 7th day. Buddhist name: Gen'yō Sōkiku Shinnyo, buried in the same location as above)

One　In Genroku 13th year (1700), 2nd month, as requested by his father, he was appointed to the guard duty external group and entered Yamazaki Sasuke's unit, serving castle guard duty
One　In the same 14th year (1701), 12th month, due to the death of unit head Yamazaki Sasuke, he was transferred to the successor unit under Hori Hanemon
One　In the same 16th year (1703), 4th month, he went up to Edo for guard duty during the lord's absence, returning in the 10th month of the same year
One　In Hōei 1st year (1704), 9th month, he was granted an inherited stipend of 200 koku without change
One　In the same 6th year (1709), 11th month, as unit head Hori Hanemon was relieved of his duties, he was transferred to the successor unit under Numazawa Gonbei
One　In Shōtoku 1st year (1711), 8th month, he was appointed as magistrate (daikan)

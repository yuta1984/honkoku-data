One　In Kyōhō 9th year (1724), 5th month, as requested, he was relieved of his official duties and continued to serve castle guard duty
One　In the same 11th year (1726), 2nd month, due to the death of unit head Numazawa Gonbei, he was transferred to the successor unit under Kayano Gonbei
One　In the same 13th year (1728), 3rd month, he went up to Edo for guard duty, returning in the 10th month of the same year
One　In the same 18th year (1733), 1st month, as unit head Kayano Gonbei was relieved of his duties as requested, he was transferred to the successor unit under Obara Gorōemon
One　In the same year, 3rd month, as requested, his son Kurōemon was appointed to guard duty
One　In Genbun 5th year (1740), 2nd month, 1st day, he died of illness at age 75. Buddhist name: Ansō Kyūshin, buried in the same location as above

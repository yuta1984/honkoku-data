Kurōuemon Tadao　　《Marginal note: Initial name Sakon | Born in Hōreki 8, Year of the Tiger (1758)》
　　Mother: Daughter of Suwa family

One　In Anei 5, Year of the Monkey (1776), 4th month, during the Nikko shrine pilgrimage, was appointed to service in the Edo palace reception hall.

One　In the same 6th year, Year of the Rooster (1777), 12th month, 12th day, died of illness at age 20.
　　Posthumous Buddhist name: Ittō Shiken Koji, burial place same as above.

　　Mother: Same as above

1　Same [era] 2 (1717), year of the Rooster, 10th month: was appointed to the position of inspector (kaikaku) in the accounting office
1　Same 5 (1720), year of the Rat, 3rd month: was granted an increase of 3 koku
1　Same 6 (1721), year of the Ox: his wish to adopt Mokuza'emon, the second son of Tabata Shichibei, as son-in-law and heir was granted
1　Same 10 (1725), year of the Snake: prepared and submitted a general single-sheet catalog covering 7 years
　　　However, because he handled the rice tax calculations properly, it was proclaimed that this contributed to the financial management of the general accounting and rice distribution departments
1　Same 10 (1725), year of the Snake, 11th month: was appointed to the position of inspector general (ginmi-gashira)
1　Same 11 (1726), year of the Horse: when his predecessor left the inspector position, it was proclaimed that he had prepared and submitted a general single-sheet catalog covering 7 years
1　Same 13 (1728), year of the Monkey, 2nd month: as requested, was relieved of his official duties and placed in a position without specific duties
1　Same year, 3rd month: was appointed to the minor role of treasury official (o-nando-yaku)

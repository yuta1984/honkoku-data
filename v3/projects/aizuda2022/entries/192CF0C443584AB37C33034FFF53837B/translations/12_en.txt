1　Anei 2 (1773), year of the Snake, 2nd month: head Harada Matasuke died of illness
　　and so he was transferred to Ikoma Kuranosuke's unit
　　as the successor role
1　Same year, 3rd month: it was proclaimed that he
　　served castle guard duty without negligence
1　Tenmei 3 (1783), year of the Rabbit, 2nd month: it was proclaimed that he served
　　castle guard duty for twenty years without negligence
1　Kansei 1 (1789), year of the Rooster, 6th month: head Ikoma Kuranosuke
　　was relieved of his role, and so he was transferred to Ibuka Kenbutsu's unit
　　as the successor role
1　Same 4th year (1792), year of the Rat, 1st month: it was proclaimed that he served
　　castle guard duty for thirty years without negligence
1　Same 5th year (1793), year of the Ox, 9th month: he participated in the bird hunting event
　　　Note: he participated two more times after this
1　Same 6th year (1794), year of the Tiger, 4th month: during last autumn's bird hunting event,
　　his marching formation was well-ordered, and this was thought to be due to
　　his diligent private training and other efforts,
　　and his entire unit was commended together

  　　　　Daughter of Yamakawa Jitayū Shigenin　　Widowed

　One　In the 3rd month of Hōreki 5, boar year (1755), as petitioned by his grandfather Rokuzaemon, he was appointed as heir to succeed his ancestor.
　One　In the 7th month of the same 9th year, rabbit year (1759), as petitioned by his grandfather, he was granted his first audience with the lord.
　One　In the 12th month of Meiwa 1, monkey year (1764), he was granted the hereditary stipend of 200 koku without change, appointed to the tozama-gumi unit, joined Sugita Gorobei's group, and served castle guard duty.
　One　In the 9th month of the same 2nd year, rooster year (1765), he went to Yamagata in Ushū Province for guard duty, served one tour, and returned.

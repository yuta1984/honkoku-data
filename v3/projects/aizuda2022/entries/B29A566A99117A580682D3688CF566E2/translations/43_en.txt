- In the 5th month of the same year, as relics of Shōsen'in-sama, he was granted one ceremonial court dress (noshi-me), one undyed formal garment, one summer kimono, and one set of long formal attire.

- In the 11th month of the same year, because the Shiba construction project was successfully completed and he had served well, he was granted one ceremonial small-sleeved kimono.

- In the 4th month of the 13th year of Kyōhō (1728), Year of the Monkey, during his service while the lord was away for the shrine visit to Nikkō, there were many various duties, but it was announced that he had worked diligently and served well.

- In the 2nd month of the 14th year of Kyōhō (1729), Year of the Rooster, as requested, he was relieved of his official duties and assigned to the group without official roles, and was ordered to return to Aizu.

- In the 12th month of the same year, Zenjirou, the second son of Nagano Yasudayū, was appointed as his adopted son as requested.

- In the 11th month of the 15th year of Kyōhō (1730), Year of the Dog, as requested, his son Zenjirou was appointed as his substitute in guard duty.

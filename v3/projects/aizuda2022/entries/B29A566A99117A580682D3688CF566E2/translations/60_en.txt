He was ordered to undertake construction work, and his administrative responsibilities were transferred. Accordingly,
the military academy was also placed under the supervision 
of the school magistrate, and he was relieved of his main role.
As a reward for his diligent service thus far,
he was granted a complete set of ceremonial hemp garments.

一　On the same day, as there were matters that would interfere
with his spear instruction duties, he was relieved of them, but during breaks from official duties, he was to
go to the spear training ground within the military academy to provide instruction
and assistance, ensuring that training would be carried out resolutely,
as was announced.

一　In the 12th month of Bunka 2, Year of the Ox (1805), for various official duties
performed for several years without absence, as a reward
he was granted a complete set of old hemp ceremonial garments.

一　In the 2nd month of Bunka 4, Year of the Rabbit (1807), he was ordered
to attend the Ōuchi-ryū training ground, but as the training ground
arrangements were completed, he was relieved of these duties.
For his diligent service thus far, as a reward
he was granted two bundles of cotton.

一　In the 11th month of the same year, as requested, he was relieved of his current duties

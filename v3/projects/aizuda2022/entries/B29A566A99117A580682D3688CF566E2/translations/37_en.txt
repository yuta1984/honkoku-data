1. In the first year of Jōkyō (1684), year of the Rat, twelfth month, he was appointed to oversee gold and baggage transport and went down to Aizu for his return.

1. In the second year of the same era (1685), year of the Ox (month unknown), he joined Yamazaki Sasuke's unit.

1. In the fourth year of the same era (1687), year of the Rabbit (month unknown), he was appointed as temporary inspector at the Mita residence.

1. In the fifth year of Genroku (1692), year of the Monkey, tenth month, as requested, he was relieved of his official duties and went down to Aizu.

1. In the same year, twelfth month, as requested, his son Tetsuzaemon was appointed as his replacement guard.

1. In the fourteenth year of the same era (1701), year of the Snake, twelfth month, as requested, he was granted retirement.

1. In the fifteenth year of the same era (1702), year of the Horse, eleventh month, twenty-fifth day, he died of illness. His posthumous Buddhist name was Fukōin Hōjōshō Koji, and he was buried at Shōrinzan Jōkōji Temple.

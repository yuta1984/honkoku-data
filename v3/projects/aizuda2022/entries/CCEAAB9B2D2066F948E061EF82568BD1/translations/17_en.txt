• Same 6th year, Tora cycle (1794), 9th month: He was appointed to the financial payment duties of the chief administrator's office, and also appointed to serve concurrently in the regular duties department and the gold and silver coordination for senior officials.
　　Note: Regarding the additional inspector duties, he was instructed to understand them as previously communicated.
• Same 7th year, U cycle (1795), 3th month: For his diligent work in the regulatory coordination duties during the autumn two years prior, he was granted three bu of gold as a reward.
• Same year, 7th month: He was appointed as accounting inspector and instructed to serve in regular duties while also concurrently handling financial payments.
• Same year, 9th month: He was sent to Edo together with Shiba Kizaemon to handle regulatory affairs and other incoming revenue coordination and various official duties. He departed in the same year's 10th month and returned in the 1st month of the following Tatsu year.
• Same 8th year, Tatsu cycle (1796), 3rd month: For his exceptional diligence over the years and for traveling to Edo to perform official duties without delay, he was granted one hundred hiki of gold as a reward.
• Same 11th year, Hitsuji cycle (1799), 5th month: For his diligent service over several years and for skillfully managing the domain's internal financial arrangements, he received an official salary

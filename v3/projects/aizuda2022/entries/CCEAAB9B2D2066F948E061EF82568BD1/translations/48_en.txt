However, regarding the position of goyōtashi-gashira (chief procurement officer), concerning his son Tsunegorō, the 110 nin-buchi that had been granted to Heishichirō up to this point, plus 10 portions of clerk allowances, totaling 120 nin-buchi were granted, he was appointed to motoshime-gata goyōtashi-gashira (chief procurement officer of the accounting department), granted audience with the lord alongside Tsukahara Chūzō in the same seating arrangement, and it was announced that both sword-bearing and high footwear privileges would be granted.

One: In the 1st month of the same 12th year (year of the Boar), he was ordered to also concurrently serve as a minor official in the motoshime-gakari gokanjōsho (accounting office under the supervision of the accounting department).

One: In the 5th month of the same year, his seating position was placed next to the ō-makanai-yaku (chief catering officer) and above the go-daidokoro-metsuke (kitchen inspector).

One: In the 4th month of Bunpolitics 2 (year of the Rabbit), he diligently performed his duties in the chōtatsu-gata (procurement department), and regarding the funding preparations for the recent kaisho (meeting hall) repair expenses, he also presented his opinions, and for these exceptional reasons, although it was a matter of great importance, he was granted the privilege of dokudan (independent decision-making authority).

One: In the 12th month of the same year, due to personnel reshuffling, he was transferred and appointed to the go-karō-tsuki yon-no-yoriai-gumi (fourth council group attached to the senior councilors).

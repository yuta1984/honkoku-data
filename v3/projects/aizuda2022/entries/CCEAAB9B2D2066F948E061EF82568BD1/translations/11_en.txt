• Same 2nd year, Inu cycle, 9th month: He went up to Edo as an attendant for the lord's return journey from sankin-kōtai (alternate attendance).
• Same year, 11th month: He was appointed to oversee tax collection and for his service in this role, he was granted two bu in gold.
• Same 3rd year, I cycle, 11th month: He was appointed as provisional inspector of foot soldiers (kari-metsuke) with the same duties as his regular position.
• (Year and month unknown) His wish to adopt Jinemon, the second son of Kashiwaya Kinuemon, was granted.
• Enkyō 2nd year, Ushi cycle, 4th month: He died of illness. His posthumous Buddhist name was Genshinin Sokusen Koji. Burial site: same as above.

- Hōreki 9, Year of the Rabbit (1759), 3rd month: From his late father's stipend allocation, reduced by four koku, granted eight koku and stipend for two attendants, summoned to middle gate guard duty
- Same 11th year, Year of the Dragon (1761), 6th month: Granted standard stipend allocation, appointed to foot soldier position
- An'ei 8, Year of the Boar (1779), 10th month: Granted three koku and stipend for one attendant as official duty allowance, appointed as foot soldier inspector
- Kansei 4, Year of the Rat (1792), 12th month: From his official duty allowance, granted an increase adjustment of one koku and stipend for one attendant

- Same 5th year, Year of the Ox (1793), 8th month, 8th day: died of illness at age 51, posthumous Buddhist name Seigan Hōdō Koji, burial site same as above

Daughter　　　　　　Wife of adopted son Chūbei Tanetada

　　Mother: daughter of the Sukarigara family

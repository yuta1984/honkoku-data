• Same 9th year, Monkey cycle (1812), 2nd month: Regarding rice transportation management, he was ordered to restore the old system, and since there was also increased shipment volume this year, he was ordered to serve on duty along the transportation routes, carry out shipping efficiently, and thoroughly investigate all management matters. He departed in the same 3rd month and returned in the same 4th month.

• Same year, 5th month: The rice shipments to Niigata were greatly increased, and there were also rice payments at that location, so he was urgently ordered to serve on duty and manage matters well. He departed in the same 5th month, traveled to Niigata, and returned in the same 7th month.

• Same 10th year, Rooster cycle (1813), 3rd month:
Since His Lordship will enter the domain this autumn, there will be considerable financial requirements for this occasion. Therefore, funds must be procured from Naito Yatōzaemon, and as soon as preparations are ready, he should quickly go to Edo to discuss these procurement matters properly. Moreover, while the official duties in Edo are substantial, from this time forward the financial inflows will gradually become regulated and are of great importance, so he should remain there directly until the end of this year.

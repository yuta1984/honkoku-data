During this period, for his diligent service, he was granted 300 hiki of gold as a reward.

One: Same 8th year (year of the sheep), 9th month - He was appointed to a temporary position for confidential matters.

One: Same year, 12th month - For having devoted himself to the essence of his duties for several years and serving with dedication, as a special consideration, one person's stipend was added to his current official salary of one koku as an increase, granted directly.

One: Same 9th year (year of the monkey), 3rd month - Due to Sagami Province official duties and personnel evaluations and investigations, which were particularly numerous, he served with dedication from the beginning, and for this was granted two bundles of cotton as a reward.

One: Same 10th year (year of the rooster), 8th month - He went to that place in advance for the Mineyama Shrine festival duties.

One: Same year, 11th month - Although all the investigators were thoroughly examined and removed at the end of last year, he handled numerous investigation records and also served diligently in the general amnesty duties, and for this was granted two pieces of silver as a reward.

One: Same 12th year (year of the boar), 3rd month - He was appointed to confidential official duties.

One: Same 13th year (year of the rat), 1st month - For being entrusted with and diligently serving the lord's domain entry duties during the previous year, he was granted one piece of silver as a reward.

One: Same year, intercalary 8th month - With few colleagues available and being busy, and with defendant examinations being particularly numerous, and although all the investigators were thoroughly examined and removed at the end of last year, he handled numerous investigation records and served well with dedication, and for this was granted two pieces of silver as a reward.

One: Same 4th year (year of the sheep), 11th month - One koku of official salary was directly granted as an increase.

One: Kanpō 1st year (year of the rooster), 1st month - He was appointed as an official of the stipend storehouse.

One: Same year, 12th month - Two koku of official salary was granted.

One: Enkyō 1st year (year of the rooster), 11th month - Two koku of official salary was directly granted as an additional increase.

One: Meiwa 5th year (year of the rat), 8th month - As petitioned, he was released from current duties and appointed as a guard at Nakanokuchi.

One: An'ei 3rd year (year of the horse), 5th month - As petitioned, his retirement from service was accepted.

One: Same 7th year (year of the dog), 6th month, 15th day - He died of illness at the age of 85. Buddhist name: Kazan Dōki Shinji; officiating priest from Daihōzan Chōzen Temple; burial place: Daikusasan.

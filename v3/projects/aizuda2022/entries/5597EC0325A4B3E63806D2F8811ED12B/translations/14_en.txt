One: Genroku 14th year (year of the snake), month unknown
　He was ordered by the shogunate's Christian investigation officials to send one scribe, and he went up to Edo.
　　However, for this matter, he was granted two ryō of gold.

One: Same 15th year (year of the horse), 4th month - He was granted an increase of two koku in stipend.

One: Same year, month unknown - Regarding abandoned horses in the domain's direct holdings, he went up to Edo and returned the following year (year of the sheep) in the 1st month.

One: Hōei 2nd year (year of the rooster), month unknown - He was granted an audience during the lord's passage.

One: Shōtoku 2nd year (year of the dragon), month unknown - He was granted an annual allocation.

One: Same 3rd year (year of the snake), 8th month - Having become elderly, he petitioned regarding his official duties, and was appointed as middle gate guard while maintaining his regular duties.

One: Kyōhō 2nd year (year of the rooster), 4th month - He was appointed as lacquerer official.

One: Same 12th year (year of the sheep), 9th month - As requested, his retirement was accepted.

One: Same year, 10th month, 28th day - Died of illness. Buddhist name: Daiyo Dōkō Koji
　Officiating priest from Daihōzan Chōzen Temple. Burial place: Daikusasan

Gozaemon Shigeyuki - Separate lineage
　Mother: daughter of the Aikawa family

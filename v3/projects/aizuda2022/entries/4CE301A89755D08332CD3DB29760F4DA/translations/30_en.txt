One: In the 12th month of the same year, regarding the new construction work at the Shiba tea house and horse viewing area, for his diligent and proper management of various inspection matters, he was awarded 200 hiki of gold as a reward.

One: In the 12th month of Bunka 7, Year of the Horse (1810), he was appointed as inspector of the military equipment department with regular duties, while continuing his concurrent position as construction department inspector and records disbursement officer. His official stipend continued as before. His seat order was placed above the provisions manager, and he was ordered to maintain his understanding of investigation procedures as before.

One: In the 5th month of Bunka 8, Year of the Sheep (1811), when Ishihara Saori was appointed to construction duties in Sagami Province, he was ordered to serve concurrently as his replacement in the upper residence construction department inspection role and records disbursement duties during his assignment.

One: In the 7th month of the same year, when the lord's procession was to pass through the Hamanaka gate, he urgently repaired the damaged sections of Sakuragawa Bridge with great effort, completing it in time, for which he was granted sake as a reward.

One: In the 3rd month of Bunka 9, Year of the Monkey (1812), he became supervisor of construction works in Sagami Province.

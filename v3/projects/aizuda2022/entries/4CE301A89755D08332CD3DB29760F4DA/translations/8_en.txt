一　In the 11th month of Bunka 8, Year of the Sheep (November 1811), he was granted
　　three residential plots that had belonged to Shimizu Sakuemon.
一　In the 4th month of Bunka 9, Year of the Monkey (April 1812), he was appointed as head of domain land accounting
　　and concurrently as investigation officer.
一　In the 11th month of Bunka 10, Year of the Rooster (November 1813), having served diligently, and regarding the matter of
　　investigating the new domain territories, the administrative arrangements were completed according to established practice,
　　and for appropriately handling the numerous official duties and laboring diligently, he was rewarded with
　　three pieces of silver and two bundles of cotton.
一　In the 12th month of Bunka 13, Year of the Rat (December 1816), regarding the compilation of the "Genealogical Records of the Domain Houses,"

　　he was assigned to handle the investigation materials for this project
　　and it was officially announced that he had diligently served in this special duty.
一　In the 3rd month of Bunka 14, Year of the Ox (March 1817), having served diligently and faithfully
　　for several decades, his current stipend of 2 koku
　　was increased by the direct addition of one person's food allowance.
　　In the 12th month of Bunsei 1, Year of the Tiger (December 1818), regarding the domain administration office's
　　new construction and subsequent repairs, the regular funding method was

《Side note: Retainer of Lord Matsudaira Sagami-no-kami》
　　Wife　　　Younger sister of Mihara Sōgen [certain person]　　Divorced

　　　　　　《Side note: Retainer of Lord Nanbu Kuranosuke》
　　　　　　Younger sister of Kawasaki Hanbei [certain person]

- Hōreki 4, Tsuchinoe-inu year (1754), fifth month: Went up to Edo to broadly study medical practice as requested, and was granted a study allowance of five-person rice stipend

- Same year 10, Kinoe-tatsu year (1760), ninth month:
　　Ordered to examine Lord Genkage-in, he diagnosed an undeveloped illness and presented medicine

　　As a result of the lord's complete recovery, he was granted a crested haori jacket
　　and 500 pieces of gold
　　from his lordship

- An'ei 2, Tsuchinoto-mi year (1773), first month: In recognition of his father's decades of service, three koku and one-person stipend were reduced from his late father's provisional allotment, and he was granted seven koku and two-person stipend and appointed as Aizu domain physician-in-ordinary
　　　However, the previous study allowance of five-person stipend
　　　was withdrawn

- Same month: As before, continued medical practice in Edo

He was employed as an ashigaru (foot soldier) under the supervision of the Public Works Magistrate.

1. In the 3rd year of Tenna, Year of the Boar (month unknown), he was granted eight koku and stipend for two retainers, selected for night patrol duty, and served for eighteen years. During this period, he went up to Edo for garrison duty several times, performed various official duties in villages and districts, was frequently appointed by direct designation for secret missions to other domains, and received commendation for compiling and submitting calculation records.

1. In the 14th year of Genroku, Year of the Snake (month unknown), he was appointed as Treasury Guard.
   Note: During this service, he was frequently assigned duties related to押金 (押金).

1. In the 5th year of Hōei, Year of the Rat (month unknown), he was selected as a subordinate official in the New Lending Office.
   Note: During this service, he made several trips to other domains for financial advance duties.

1. In the 5th year of Shōtoku, Year of the Sheep, 10th month, he was appointed as an attending physician, with a rank above that of Hoshino Un'i.
   Note: At this time, he changed his name to Ikeuchi Gentan.

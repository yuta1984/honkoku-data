- Same year, ninth month of the Kanoe-tatsu year (1760), fourth month: Traveled up to Edo to accompany his lord on the return journey

- Same year (month unclear): Appointed to gairyo (external physician) first class, but then ordered to serve in both hondo (internal medicine) and gairyo roles
- Same year, fifteenth year Tsuchinoe-inu (1718), third month: As requested, his gisaku (medical apprentice) position was terminated

- Kanpo 1, Kinoe-ne year (1741), third month: Died of illness. Posthumous Buddhist name: Jikyo-in Ho-nichi-ken. Buried at: Hoju-san Honko-ji Temple

Wife: Daughter (Sei) of Ōhori Hikoemon Moto■【扌＋救】, born on 19th day of 2nd month of Kansei 6, Year of the Tiger

1. Intercalary 6th month of Kansei 1, Year of the Rooster: He was appointed as assistant instructor for lower-grade students at the domain school.
1. 5th month of Kansei 2, Year of the Dog: He was appointed as assistant instructor for discipline and conduct.
　　However, in 4th month of Kansei 11, Year of the Sheep: He was dismissed as requested.
1. 9th month of the same year: He was appointed as temporary employment of minor clerk at the accounting office.
1. 1st month of Kansei 4, Year of the Rat: He was appointed as assistant for accounting inspection.
1. 6th month of the same year: He was appointed to serve concurrently as administrator of the domain school for lower-grade students and assistant reading instructor.
　　However, in 4th month of Kansei 11, Year of the Sheep: He was dismissed as requested.
1. 12th month of the same year: Since the domain school's revival, he studied diligently without negligence and served as assistant reading instructor for lower-grade students. After Hosokawa Chūzō was dismissed from his instructor position, while serving as temporary employee at the accounting office, he conducted instruction alone like Chūzō did. For these various reasons, he was exceptionally granted three bu of gold.
1. 4th month of Kansei 5, Year of the Ox: He was appointed as administrator of the sword practice hall.
1. 7th month of the same year: He was appointed as regular employee at the accounting office.
　　However, due to illness, he was dismissed in the 10th month upon request.
1. 11th month of the same year: He was appointed as temporary foot soldier.

1. Kansei 8, Year of the Dragon, 3rd month: Having worked with exceptional dedication for years and carefully handled various matters including profitable tax collection, he was awarded 200 hiki of gold as a reward.

1. Same year, 12th month: When sickles were forged at the blacksmith workshop for official disposal, he submitted suggestions for profit, and was ordered to proceed as he had proposed.

1. Kansei 11, Year of the Sheep, 5th month: Having served faithfully for several years and carefully implementing cost-saving measures, he was granted an official stipend of 2 koku.

1. Same year, 12th month: He was appointed to the Fourth Council Group attached to the Senior Councilor.

1. Kyōwa 1, Year of the Rooster, 8th month: He participated in the bird hunting expedition.
   Note: He participated once more after this.

1. Kyōwa 3, Year of the Boar, 12th month: He was appointed to the temporary position attached to the women's quarters.

1. Bunka 2, Year of the Ox, 12th month: For his busy year-round service to Lord Kinbun, he was privately awarded 5 ryō of silver.

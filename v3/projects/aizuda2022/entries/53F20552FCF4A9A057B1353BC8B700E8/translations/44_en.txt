Same year, 5th month: He was ordered to go to the capital on official business for Osaka rice sales and domain debt matters, departed in the 5th month and returned in the 12th month.

Same 2nd Rabbit year [1819], 1st month: For his diligent service and proper handling of Kyoto region duties, he was specially granted an increase of 5 to (measures) to his current stipend, plus an additional 1 koku of stipend outside of direct grants.

Same year, 7th month: His request to change his name to Riseki-emon was approved.

Same 3rd Dragon year [1820], 10th month: Due to personnel reshuffling, he was relieved of his current duties and appointed to the senior retainer's fourth joint group, with his 1 koku stipend continuing as before, and his diligent service to date was formally commended.

Same year, 11th month: He was ordered to investigate and compile records from his previous position in the accounting office, and since the relevant department requested that this be granted, he was ordered to go to his former office to conduct the investigation until this official business was completed.

1. Kyōwa 2, Year of the Dog, 7th month: He was appointed to serve at the level of minor officials (koyakunin-nami).

1. Same year, 8th month: He was assigned from temporary employment at the Finance Office to regular service, worked diligently and attentively, and also handled cost-saving measures, for which he was awarded two ryō of silver as a reward.

1. Kyōwa 3, Year of the Boar, 1st month: He was appointed to temporary service in charge of investigating magistrates' accounts.

1. Same year, 10th month: He was relieved of the aforementioned temporary service in charge of investigating magistrates' accounts.

1. Bunka 2, Year of the Ox, 5th month: Regarding the folding of temporary certificates for both treasury payment departments, he submitted his suggestions for handling the cleanup, and was ordered to proceed according to his proposal.

1. Bunka 4, Year of the Rabbit, 6th month: He was appointed to service for the defense of Ezo-chi (Hokkaido), but due to his father's illness, he was appointed as a substitute as requested.

1. Same year, 9th month: He attended the inspection ceremony.

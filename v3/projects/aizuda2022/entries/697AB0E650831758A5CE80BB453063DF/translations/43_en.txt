　One　10th month of the 20th year (1735), Year of the Rabbit　Went up for guard duty service, returned in the 4th month of the following Dragon year
　One　3rd month of the 1st year of Genbun era (1736), Year of the Dragon　In Edo, for diligent service in his second campaign in his current position, was granted hemp ceremonial dress (kamishimo)
　One　4th month of the 3rd year of Genbun (1738), Year of the Horse　Went up for guard duty service, returned in the 10th month of the same year
　One　10th month of the same year　For excellent and diligent service in his third campaign in his current position, was granted a small-sleeved kimono
　One　11th month of the 5th year of Genbun (1740), Year of the Monkey　Was appointed as mountain magistrate (yama-bugyō), with official stipend of 50 koku continued as before


　One　7th month of the 2nd year of Kanpō (1742), Year of the Dog　Due to misconduct regarding mountain timber matters, his official position was revoked and he was appointed to tozama-gumi-tsuki (attached to non-hereditary retainer group), entered Tanaka Kahei's unit and served castle guard duty
　One　29th day, 9th month of the 3rd year of Enkyō (1746), Year of the Tiger　Died of illness at age 62. Buddhist name: Kenshō-in Tokuganzenkikoji, buried at Shōryūzan Zenryū-ji Temple

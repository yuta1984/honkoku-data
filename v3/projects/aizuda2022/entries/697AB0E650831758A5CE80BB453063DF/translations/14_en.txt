Zensuke Yoshisato　　《Marginal note: Born in Kyōhō 1, Year of the Monkey》
　　Mother　Daughter of the Ikegami family

一　In the 1st month of Enkyō 3, Year of the Tiger, he was granted his first audience with the lord as requested by his father.
一　In the 8th month of Hōreki 6, Year of the Rat, he was granted the hereditary stipend of 100 koku without change, appointed to an outsider unit, entered Hinata Genemon's unit, and served castle guard duty.
一　In the 7th month of the 7th year, Year of the Ox, due to illness, he was assigned to minor construction work as requested.

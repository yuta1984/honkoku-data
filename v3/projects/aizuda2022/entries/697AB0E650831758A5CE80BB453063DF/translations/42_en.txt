One　4th month of the 3rd year (1718), Year of the Dog　Went up to Edo for guard duty service, returned in the 10th month of the same year
　One　6th month of the same year　Petitioned to change his family name to Ichinose and was granted permission
　One　10th month of the 5th year (1720), Year of the Rat　Went up for guard duty service, returned in the 4th month of the following Ox year
　One　10th month of the 8th year (1723), Year of the Rabbit　Since he had served faithfully for many years, his previous provisional fief was directly granted as 100 koku
　One　10th month of the 11th year (1726), Year of the Horse　Went up to Edo for guard duty service
　One　1st month of the 12th year (1727), Year of the Sheep　While on guard duty, was appointed to the position of御納戸役 (onando-yaku, steward of the lord's private quarters)


One　12th month of the same year　As requested, was relieved of the onando-yaku position and was appointed to the外様組付 (tozama-gumi-tsuki, attached to non-hereditary retainer group), entered Kayano Gonbei's unit and served castle guard duty
　One　7th month of the 18th year (1733), Year of the Ox　Was appointed to the position of ometsuke (inspector)
　One　9th month of the same year　Was granted 50 koku as official stipend
　One　10th month of the same year　Went up for guard duty service, returned in the 4th month of the following Tiger year
　One　3rd month of the 19th year (1734), Year of the Tiger　Although this was his first campaign in Edo, it was proclaimed that he served diligently without delay
　One　10th month of the same year　Was granted the former residence of Kitahara Tanji through exchange

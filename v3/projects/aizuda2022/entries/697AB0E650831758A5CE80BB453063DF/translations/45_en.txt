　One　4th month of the 8th year of Hōreki (1758), Year of the Tiger　As requested by his father, was granted his first audience with the lord

　One　11th month of the 13th year of Hōreki (1763), Year of the Sheep　As requested by his father, was appointed as guard substitute, served castle guard duty in Ueda Hachirozaemon's unit
　One　5th month of the 3rd year of Meiwa (1766), Year of the Dog　Due to the death of his superior Ueda Hachirozaemon, was reassigned to the unit of his successor Ohara Gorozaemon
　One　10th month of the 1st year of An'ei (1772), Year of the Dragon　Due to the transfer of his superior Ohara Gorozaemon, was reassigned to the unit of his successor Ueda Hachirozaemon
　One　2nd month of the 2nd year of An'ei (1773), Year of the Snake　Was commended for serving castle guard duty without negligence
　One　10th month of the 5th year of An'ei (1776), Year of the Monkey　Inherited domain of 100 koku without issue

Second adopted son
　Wasaku Nagatsu (original name: Tsunosuke)　Adopted son of Miyahara Sanouemon Nagataka
　　Actual father: Hirano Jinemon Shigeyoshi

　Hanbē Shigeyoshi (original name: Denji)
　　Mother: Daughter of the Hirano family
　　Wife: Daughter of Yanase Saburoji Masahisa

一　In the 2nd month of Kyōhō 9, Year of the Dragon, he was granted his first audience with the lord as requested by his parent.

一　In the 2nd month of Kanpō 2, Year of the Dog, he was appointed as guard substitute as requested by his parent, and served castle guard duty in Numasawa Gonbē's unit.

一　In the 9th month of Enpō 2, Year of the Ox, he died of illness.

　Adopted daughter　　Wife of Hirota Kannouemon Toshitsune (divorced)
　　　　　　　　　　Wife of Kikkawa Ichibē Taneetsu (divorced)

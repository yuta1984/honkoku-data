Biological child: Uchida Tokuemon Chikafoshi
Third generation
First name Komugita, after retirement Yukijiru
Shōbei Kimishige
Mother: daughter of the Matsumoto family
Born on the twenty-first day of the fourth month of Shōtoku 4, year of the horse (1714), died of illness on the fifth day of the second month of Meiwa 7, year of the tiger (1770), posthumous Buddhist name Shunkō-in Baishin Teikō Daishi, buried at Shōryūji temple at Shōunzan
Wife: daughter of Suzuki Unsetsu Shigeyoshi, at the fifth hour of the second month, Ishiai-hyō sakumotsu-shin

First: In the ninth month of Kyōhō 7, year of the tiger (1722), as requested by relatives, was appointed as guard substitute and served as guard at Inawashiro Castle

First: In the sixth month of the same 8th year of the rabbit (1723), was granted the hereditary stipend of 100 koku without change

First: In the first month of the same 14th year of the rooster (1729), was appointed to the lord's escort guard

First: In the second month of the same 15th year of the dog (1730), was granted at the residence

First: In the fourth month of the same year, went up to serve as first guard in the lord's escort. However, was commended for serving without absence during the lord's travels

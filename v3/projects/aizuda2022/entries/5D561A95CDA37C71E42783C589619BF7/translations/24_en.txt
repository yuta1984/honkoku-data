【Right Page】
　　He was appointed to manage the Chu-gomen mountain area,
　　and when he performed his duties with exceptional management,
　　he was granted sake as a reward
1　Same 6th year, year of the Snake, 10th month, having no male heir
　　and his illness having become severe at this time, he requested
　　to adopt Koichiro, son of Niki Bansuke, as son-in-law and heir.
　　However, he died of illness before the official permission was granted,
　　whereupon his relatives were informed that the request
　　would be granted posthumously
1　Same month, he died of illness at age 50, Buddhist name: Shin

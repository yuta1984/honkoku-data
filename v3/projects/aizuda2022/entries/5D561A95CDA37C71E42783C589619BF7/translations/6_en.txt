【Right page】
1. In the 4th month of Kanbun 1, Year of the Ox, he was granted
　　the upper residence of Ono Genseki
1. In the 12th month of the same year, when his superior Jinpō Kuranosuke
　　was relieved of his duties, he was transferred
　　to Harada Shuma's unit as a successor
1. In the 7th month of Kanbun 2, Year of the Tiger, he was appointed
　　to concurrently serve as daikan (regional administrator)
1. In the 8th month of Kanbun 10, Year of the Dog, as requested, he was relieved
　　of his daikan duties and served castle guard duty
1. On the 20th day of the 3rd month of Enpō 3, Year of the Rabbit, at age sixty-seven

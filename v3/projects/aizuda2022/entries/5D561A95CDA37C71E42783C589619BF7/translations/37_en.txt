【Right Page】
1　Kansei 5, Year of the Ox, 2nd month: As his parent petitioned, appointed to guard duty,
　　serving castle guard duty in Jinbo Kuranosuke's unit

1　Same year, 9th month: Participated in the autumn hunting event

1　Same year, 12th month: Granted hereditary stipend of 100 koku without change

1　Same 6th year, Year of the Tiger, 4th month: At last autumn's hunting event,
　　his formation marching was well-coordinated, and having diligently
　　practiced privately, His Lordship thought favorably of this,
　　and praised the entire unit

　　granted his request to surrender his minor construction duties
　　in order to recuperate

1　Kyōwa 2, Year of the Dog, 1st month, 18th day: Died of illness at age 35,
　　posthumous Buddhist name Shaku Shu Ryōyō Dōshi, burial site same as above

Oriemon Ritoji
　Birth father: third son of Nakata Rokudayū Katsuyori
　Wife: daughter of adoptive father Buemon Ritchū《marginal note: died of illness on the 18th day of the 7th month of Meiwa 4, Year of the Boar, posthumous Buddhist name Kukeiei Daishi》
　　　　　　　　　　　　《marginal note: burial site same as above｜》
1. In the 2nd month of Shōtoku 4, Year of the Horse, as per his father's petition, he was
　　appointed as guard substitute and served
　　castle guard duty in Numazawa Gonbei's unit
【Left page】
1. In the 7th month of Shōtoku 5, Year of the Sheep, he was appointed
　　to escort guard duty
1. In the 6th month of Kyōhō 1, Year of the Monkey, he went up to Edo
　　to serve escort duty and returned in the 10th month of the same year
1. In the 4th month of Kyōhō 2, Year of the Rooster, due to the lord's return journey, he went up
　　to serve as advance escort
1. In the 3rd month of Kyōhō 4, Year of the Boar, he was granted
　　an inheritance stipend of 150 koku without change
1. In the 4th month of Kyōhō 5, Year of the Rat, he went up
　　for escort duty, served temporary Edo guard duty during the journey, and in the same 10th [month]

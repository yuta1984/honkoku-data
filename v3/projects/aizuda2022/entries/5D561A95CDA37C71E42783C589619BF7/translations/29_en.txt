1　Same year, 6th month: Each time during the lord's visits to Edo, he rotated military drills with his colleagues, served as an attendant, and worked diligently in temporary duties, for which he was granted two silver coins as a reward
1　Same year, 12th month: His private financial difficulties were heard, and he was privately granted 2 ryō 2 bu in gold

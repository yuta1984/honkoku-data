1. Kanpō 2, year of the Dog (1742), twelfth month: From his father's stipend,
　one ryō in gold was deducted and he was granted eight ryō and provisions for four people,
　was appointed to the outer retainer unit and served castle guard duty in Ikoma Gohei's unit
1. Hōreki 8, year of the Tiger (1758), fifth month: His request to change his name
　to Jūrōemon was granted
1. Meiwa 5, year of the Rat (1768), twelfth month: Was appointed to permanent Edo duty
　and moved there in the first month of the following year of the Ox
1. Same 6th year of the Ox (1769), third month: Due to increased clerical work,
　was appointed as assistant secretary
1. Same 7th year of the Tiger (1770), intercalary sixth month: Was appointed
　as acting secretary
1. Same year, seventh month: Was granted one lined summer robe
　with the lord's crest from the wardrobe department
1. Same 8th year of the Rabbit (1771), eleventh month: His previous stipend was converted
　to sixteen koku and provisions for four people, and he was appointed as permanent secretary
1. An'ei 2, year of the Snake (1773), first month: Was entrusted with
　confidential documents

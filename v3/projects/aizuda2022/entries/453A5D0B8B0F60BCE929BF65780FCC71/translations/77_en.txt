1. Same 6th year of the Horse (1786), first month: As requested, he was relieved
   of his official duties and appointed to the Aizu finance external retainer group.
   In the same fourth month, he moved and went down to join Harada Matasuke's
   group and served in castle guard duty

1. 1st year of Kansei, year of the Cock (1789), tenth month: He was
   appointed as magistrate and given the rank
   of senior attendant first assembly seat

1. Same 2nd year of the Dog (1790), second month: Having served in various
   official positions for several decades, his son Kyūma was granted
   his first audience [with the lord], as announced

1. Same 3rd year of the Boar (1791), first month: He was
   appointed to next-in-line duty

1. Same year, eighth month: As requested, his son Kyūma was
   appointed as substitute guard

1. 4th year of Bunka, year of the Rabbit (1807), ninth month, thirteenth day: He died
   of illness at age seventy-eight. Posthumous Buddhist name: Jōkanin Sai-
   gan Seigetsu. Burial site: same as above

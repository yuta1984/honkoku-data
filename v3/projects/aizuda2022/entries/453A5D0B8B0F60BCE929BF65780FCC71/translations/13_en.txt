1. In the 12th month of the same year, petitioned again for resignation, was granted relief from duty as requested, and was assigned to castle guard duty
1. In the 10th month of Enkyō 1 (year of the rat), went up to Edo for guard duty service, and in the 4th month of the following year (year of the ox), served as main camp guard in personal attendance and returned
1. At the end of the same 2nd year (year of the ox), during the great ceremony at Edo, messenger duties and other tasks were particularly numerous, but was commended for serving diligently with great effort
1. In the intercalary 6th month of Hōreki 1 (year of the sheep), his son Saburōsuke was appointed as guard substitute as requested
1. In the 9th month of Meiwa 4 (year of the boar), was granted retirement as requested
1. On the 5th day of the 12th month of Tenmei 6 (year of the horse), died of illness at age 90; posthumous Buddhist name: Rōjuin-den Saun Zangetsu Koji; burial site: same as above

一In the fourth year of Ne (1792), intercalary second month: He was appointed to okuban (inner guard)
一Same year, fifth month: He was appointed as temporary gonando (storehouse keeper)
　　Note: Thereafter, he was repeatedly appointed to this position
一Same year, tenth month: It was announced that ten ryō in gold for the four seasonal charity distributions would be granted directly
一In the fifth year of Ushi (1793), fourth month: He was appointed to the position of cost-reduction officer for the lord's personal staff. Accordingly, he was ordered that matters he noticed should be discussed with the goyōnin (administrators), and depending on circumstances, also reported to the senior retainers, with the general aim of reducing personnel expenses
一Same year, eighth month: For diligent service, by the lord's gracious consideration, he was granted one haori jacket bearing the family crest
一Same year, twelfth month: Though there was a long period of residence duty in Edo, for diligent service and particularly careful attention to managing the lord's quarters, he was granted one tan of raw silk as a reward
一In the sixth year of Tora (1794), first month: For four years of diligent service and perfect attendance, he was granted one set of old hemp formal wear (kamishimo)

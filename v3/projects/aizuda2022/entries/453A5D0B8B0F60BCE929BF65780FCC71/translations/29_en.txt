【Marginal note in red ink】
Bunka 2 (Ox year)
Requested to pay daily respects at Lord Kyōtei's temporary palace, and since there were proper precedents, the request was granted and he was also appointed to serve in the funeral procession

was also appointed to serve in duties

In Bunka 4 (Rabbit year), 2nd month: Was appointed as administrator of the domain's exempt mountain areas

In Bunka 8 (Sheep year), 8th month: Participated in the bird hunting expedition
Note: Participated three more times after this

In the 12th month of the same year: During this bird hunting expedition, the formation and ranks were well-ordered and operations proceeded smoothly, and recognizing his diligent practice in group training, the entire unit was praised together
Note: Received such praise on every occasion thereafter

In Bunka 10 (Rooster year), intercalary 12th month [possibly error for 11th month]: Due to changes in assignments, was relieved of tea ceremony administration duties, and for his diligent service over many years, was rewarded with one bundle of cotton

In the 12th month of the same year: For the training at Sannomaru training ground and group exercises at Hongōhara, recognizing that formations and ranks were well-ordered and conducted diligently, the entire unit was praised together

In Bunka 12 (Boar year), 12th month: For training at Sannomaru training ground, with formations and ranks well-ordered and achieving superior rank for three years through diligent effort, was rewarded with sake

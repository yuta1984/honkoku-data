1　Bunsei 2nd year, Year of the Rabbit, 5th month, since Tōjirō, second son of Akabane Hachizaemon,
　had fully recovered from his illness, his request for formal adoption
　was granted as petitioned
1　Same month, since adoption attempts had failed several times and he again petitioned
　for adoption, he was dismissed from unit assignment and appointed to minor construction duties
1　Same year, 10th month, he was granted formal audience
1　Same 4th year, Year of the Snake, 1st month, his seat rank was appointed
　to follow after the Inawashiro samurai
1　Same 5th year, Year of the Horse, 1st month, he was appointed to external unit assignment,
　joined Kayano Gonbē's unit, and served
　castle guard duty
1　Same 6th year, Year of the Sheep, 9th month, as petitioned, his mock performance
　was discontinued

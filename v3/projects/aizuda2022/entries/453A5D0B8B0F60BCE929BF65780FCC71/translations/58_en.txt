Granted fifteen koku and rice stipend for four retainers, and summoned
　to serve in the lord's personal guard
One: Genroku 4 (Year of the Sheep, 1691), 7th month, granted the
　residence plot of Ueda Yaemon Aki
One: Same 14th year (Year of the Snake, 1701), 3rd month, permitted as requested
　to exchange his residence with that of Kōno Kizaemon
One: Same 15th year (Year of the Horse, 1702), 3rd month, due to illness, as requested,
　was relieved of service in the lord's personal guard, assigned to the outer retainer group,
　entered Jimbo Mozaemon's unit, and served
　in castle guard duty
One: Kyōhō 6 (Year of the Ox, 1721), 11th month, as requested, his rice
　stipend was revoked
One: Same 9th year (Year of the Dragon, 1724), intercalary 4th month, 15th day, died of illness,
　Buddhist name: Jikyōin Dōki Nisshuku Koji, officiant:
　Hōshōzan Jōkōji temple, burial site: Bodaiin mountain

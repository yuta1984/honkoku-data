Daughter of Shiga Zenbyōe Shigesuke
One: Kyōhō 6 (Year of the Ox, 1721), 11th month, five koku was deducted from his father's rice stipend,
　granted twenty koku and rice stipend for four retainers,
　summoned and assigned to the outer retainer group, entered
　Ohara Gorōemon's unit, and served in castle guard duty
One: Same 7th year (Year of the Tiger, 1722), 6th month,
　appointed to serve in Lord Shōsenin's escort for the sankin-kōtai journey
One: Same 8th year (Year of the Rabbit, 1723), 3rd month, as rotating guard duty, went up
　to Edo, served one tour of duty and returned
One: Same 11th year (Year of the Horse, 1726), 3rd month, as rotating guard duty, went up
　to Edo, served one tour of duty and returned
One: Same 12th year (Year of the Sheep, 1727), 3rd month,
　due to Lord Shōsenin's passing, there was no need for his services,
　assigned to the outer retainer group, entered
　Yamazaki Sasuke's unit, and served in castle guard duty
One: Same 14th year (Year of the Rooster, 1729), intercalary 9th month,
　appointed as personal attendant for Lord Dotsune's sankin-kōtai journey
One: Same 15th year (Year of the Dog, 1730), 3rd month, as rotating guard duty, went up
　to Edo, served one tour of duty and returned

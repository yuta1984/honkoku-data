One: Same 17th year (Year of the Rat, 1732), 1st month,
　granted two pieces of silver as congratulations
　for Lord Dotsune's succession to family headship
One: Same year, 12th month, his role title was changed
　to personal attendant guard (go-kinshūban)
One: Same 18th year (Year of the Ox, 1733), 3rd month, as rotating guard duty, went up
　to Edo, served one tour of duty and returned
One: Same 19th year (Year of the Tiger, 1734), 3rd month, by rotation, his personal attendant guard duty was
　terminated along with others
One: Same year, 8th month, assigned to no official position, and due to his diligent
　service thus far, granted two bolts of silk, and exempted from
　minor construction fees, as was announced
One: Same 20th year (Year of the Rabbit, 1735), 6th month, granted an increase
　of five koku and appointed to escort guard duty
One: Same year, 9th month, went up to Edo as rotating guard duty
　and served one tour of duty
One: Genbun 2 (Year of the Snake, 1737), 2nd month, requested to adopt
　Tetsusuke, second son of Ogawa Shichiemon, as son-in-law and
　was granted permission
One: Same year, 2nd month, 4th day, died of illness at age fifty, Buddhist name

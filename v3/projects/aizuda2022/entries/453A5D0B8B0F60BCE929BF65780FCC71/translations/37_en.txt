1. In the 11th month of the 7th year, Tiger year, his petition to change his name
　to Tōemon was granted
1. In the 1st month of the 6th year of An'ei, Rooster year, his seat rank was appointed
　to be next after the Inawashiro retainers and above the non-group retainers
1. In the 10th month of the 1st year of Tenmei, Ox year, he was appointed
　as an Inawashiro retainer
1. In the 3rd month of the 5th year of the same era, Snake year, he was summoned back to Wakamatsu and appointed
　to the outer retainer group, joining Kasuga Ninzaemon's unit
　and serving guard duty at the castle
1. In the 6th month of the 6th year, Horse year, his petition to adopt
　Tōkurō, younger brother of Kurokawachi Gonnosuke, as his son
　was granted
1. On the 24th day of the same month, he died of illness at age fifty-nine. His Buddhist name was
　Jōjitsu-in Shaku Shu Ryōshun. His burial place was at
　Muryōzan Chōmei-ji Temple
Tōemon Nariatsushi 《marginal note: originally called Tōkurō | wife born in the 7th year, Ox year》
　Actually the second son of Kurokawachi Anzaemon Sadakami

Yaemon Mitsudai - Born in Kyōhō 10, Snake year
Mother: daughter of the Murakami family
Wife: daughter of Higuchi Sayō Mitsunaka, divorced
1. In the 1st month of Enkyō 1, Rat year, he was appointed
   as an apprentice secretary (goyūhitsu minarai)
1. In the 2nd month of Kan'en 1, Dragon year, he was granted
   15 koku and stipend for 3 retainers, and was summoned to serve as secretary (goyūhitsu)
1. In the 2nd month of the same 3rd year, Horse year, he was appointed
   to confidential duties (goneiyō)
1. In the 6th month of Hōreki 1, Sheep year, due to illness, as requested,
   he was relieved of his official duties and his provisional appointment was revoked
1. In the 12th month of the same year, due to poor health, as requested by his father,
   he was ordered to be disinherited

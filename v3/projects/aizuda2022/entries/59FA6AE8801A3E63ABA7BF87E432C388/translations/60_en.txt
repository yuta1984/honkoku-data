Bunshirō (original names: Tsunenoshin, also Shichirō) - Divorced/separated
　Biological father: Second son of Sakai Kichibei Tsuguhiro
　Wife: Adopted daughter of adoptive father Kaemon Mitsukun

1. Ninth month of Kyōwa 3 (1803): Granted first audience [with the lord] as requested by his parent.
1. Twelfth month of Bunka 2 (1805): Request to change name to Bunshirō was granted as requested by his parent.

　Biological father: Second son of Yokoyama Denzō Tomohiro
　Wife: Adopted daughter of adoptive father Kaemon Bikun
　　Biological father: Daughter of Hiraide Tadazō Tadahide

Served guard duty at the castle

1. Fifth month of Meiwa 3 (1766): When head retainer Kajiwara Heima was appointed to wakadoshiyori (junior councilor), he joined Hyūga Saburōemon's unit as his successor

1. Ninth month of Meiwa 8 (1771): Appointed to otomo-ban (lord's escort guard)

1. Third month of Meiwa 9 (1772): Went up to Edo for duty service as part of the lord's escort, returned in the tenth month
　　Moreover, maintained perfect attendance during the journey and while stationed

　　This achievement was officially recognized, and thereafter he repeatedly received commendations

1. Fourth month of Tenmei 2 (1782): Went up to Edo for duty service, returned in the tenth month

1. Fourth month of Tenmei 3 (1783): Inherited the family headship with a stipend of 100 koku without change, ordered to continue serving as otomo-ban as before

1. Fourth month of the same year: Went up again to serve as advance escort, returned with the lord's party in the fifth month

1. Tenth month of Tenmei 6 (1786): To Edo for duty service

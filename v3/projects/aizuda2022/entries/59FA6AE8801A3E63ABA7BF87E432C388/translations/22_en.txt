1. In the ninth year, year of the hare, fourth month: He served as an attendant during the lord's entry into his domain and went down [to the domain]

1. In the fifth month of the same year, he was commended for his diligent service during the lord's first journey

1. In the first month of the tenth year, year of the dragon: He was appointed as an attendant for the lord's journey to Kyoto and went up [to the capital]

1. In the twelfth month of the eleventh year, year of the snake: For his daily diligent service and enduring hardship during busy times with personal duties for the lord, he was granted two bolts of raw silk

1. In the eighth month of the twelfth year, year of the horse: He was appointed as assistant guard duty in Edo and went down [to the domain] in the same month

1. In the tenth month of the same year: He went up to Edo for guard duty service, and in the fifth month of the following year of the sheep, he went down accompanying the lord's return to the domain

1. In Meiwa 6, year of the ox (month unknown): Due to a shortage of inner guards, he was appointed to temporary duty

1. In the twelfth month of the same year: He was appointed as close attendant, granted a seat above the grand wardrobe keeper, and ordered to continue his assigned duties as before

1. In the seventh year, year of the tiger (month unknown): He was appointed as ceremonial supervisor for meal service for the pages

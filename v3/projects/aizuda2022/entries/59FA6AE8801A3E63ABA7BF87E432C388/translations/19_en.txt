Genealogy of Ikegami Yūzaemon (Surname: Minamoto, Home Province: Shinano) (Main crest: The character "ichi" with five-three paulownia, Alternative crest: Bamboo grass and gentian, Curtain crest: The character "ichi" within a square)

Fourth son of Ikegami Zenzaemon Takatomi
　Ikegami Shinzaemon Tōtsune (First name: Shigezaburō, also: Kansuke, born on the fifteenth day of the twelfth month of Kyōhō 14, year of the rooster)
　　Mother: Daughter of grandfather Shinzaemon Takashine
　　Wife: Daughter of Higuchi Kanji Mitsusine (died of illness at age 80 on the last day of the tenth month of Bunka 12, year of the boar; posthumous Buddhist name Tenrin Sōshū Daishi; buried at Daihōzan Kenfuku-ji Temple)

In the intercalary eighth month of Bunka 1, year of the rat, because his moral character had always been upright and his guidance of children was exceptional, he was granted two bolts of silk as a reward.

One: In the 6th month of Shōtoku 5, year of the sheep [1715], as his parent had requested, he was granted his first formal audience

One: In the 5th month of Kyōhō 8, year of the rabbit [1723], as his parent had requested, he was appointed as a guard deputy and assigned to the outer retainer group, entering Kajiwara Heima's unit to serve in castle guard duty

One: In the 4th month of the 11th year, year of the tiger [1726], for the lord's attendance in Edo, he went up as a mounted attendant for guard duty and returned in the 10th month of the same year

One: In the 7th month of the 15th year, year of the dog [1730], he was granted the hereditary stipend of 300 koku without any reduction

One: In the 1st month of the 19th year, year of the tiger [1734], his superior Kajiwara Heima was relieved of his duties as requested, and he was transferred to serve under Jimbo Kuranosuke as the successor

One: In the 4th month of the same year, he went up to Edo for guard duty and returned in the 10th month

One: In the 8th month of Genbun 2, year of the snake [1737], his request to change his name to Hachirōbei was granted

One: In the 10th month of the same year, he went up to Edo for guard duty and returned in the 4th month of the following year of the horse

One: In the 5th month of Enkyō 2, year of the ox [1745], he was appointed as a squad leader (monotō)

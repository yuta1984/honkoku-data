One: In the first month of Genroku 12, year of the Rabbit (1699) - He was granted a stipend of eight ryō in imitation money and provisions for four people of regular rank, and was summoned to serve as a page (koshō).

One: In the first month of Hōei 1, year of the Monkey (1704) - He was appointed to the dining service (gozenban).
　　However, at this time, regarding his father Koemon,
　　as he was one who served in establishing Lord Dotsū's palace, and moreover at present
　　he is distinguished by his connection to Her Ladyship's lineage, and particularly his natural disposition
　　appears to be of genuine character, for all these reasons he shall be treated
　　with special kindness beyond the usual middle-ranking attendants, as this is His Lordship's wish, and we are grateful for this consideration.

　　He should conduct himself with prudence in all matters, and furthermore, except for close relatives and
　　family connections, he should absolutely refrain from making courtesy visits
　　outside the middle quarters, as instructed in the written order given to him.
One: Same year - He was granted an additional allowance of one ryō for his dining service duties.
One: Same year, 12th month - He accompanied His Lordship on a pilgrimage to Nikkō.

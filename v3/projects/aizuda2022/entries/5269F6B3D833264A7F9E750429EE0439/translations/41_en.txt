One: In the 9th month of the same year, for not neglecting marching drills year after year and having the entire group train earnestly, and moreover since the previous year of the snake, during every bird hunting expedition, the unit members showed resolve and did not borrow the lord's horses, which was considered admirable. His Lordship praised the entire unit for this

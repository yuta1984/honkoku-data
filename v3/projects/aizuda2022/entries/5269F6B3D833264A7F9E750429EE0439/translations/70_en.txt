One: In the 5th month of the same year, as requested, he was relieved from his concurrent duty as assistant instructor of horizontal flute

One: In the 8th month of the same year, he went out for bird hunting
Note: After this he went out once more

One: In the 10th month of the same year, during the current bird hunting season, the hunting ground was moved, and at the beginning, the vice-general's unit formations were well-ordered, the squads maintained proper connection, operations proceeded without delay, and he had been particularly diligent in combined unit training and performed well in command duties, which was thought commendable and was announced
Note: After this he received praise on every occasion

One: In the 3rd month of Bunka 15, year of the tiger [1818], when it was announced that the samurai attached to the vice-general and the district magistrate's assistants had been diligent in their military drill training and performed well in their duties, an official from the administrative office carelessly investigated and reported negligent conduct, for which he received a reprimand. As a result, he was granted one command baton

One: In the 7th month of the same year, his official title was appointed as "Vice-General Attached Unit Head"

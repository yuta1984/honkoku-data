One: Kan'en 2, year of the Snake (1749), 8th month - In the lord's presence, [it was noted that] he had served diligently for several years, but since he had been in poor health for some time, his guard duty (kinban) would be excused, as was announced.

One: Hōreki 1, year of the Sheep (1751), 1st month - As requested, he was released from his current position, and as privately requested, he was granted permission to retire.
　　However, during his service he was granted calligraphy works, hanging scrolls, clothing, inkstone boxes, folding screens, flower vases, hanging scrolls, incense ceremony implements, and various other items.

One: An'ei 1, year of the Dragon (1772), 1st month, 14th day - Died of illness at age 86.
　Shinto funeral rites. Spirit name: Shigeyuan Reisha. Burial place: Ōkubo-yama

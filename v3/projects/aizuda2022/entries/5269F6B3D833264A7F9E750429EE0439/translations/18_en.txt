One: Same year (Tenmei 1, 1781), 8th month - When he submitted a petition to resign from his position, although the content of his petition was reasonable, since he had served for many years and was not yet so advanced in age, his current position would not be dismissed, but he should take care of his health and continue to serve for a long time. Accordingly, it was announced that he was exempted from guard duty.

One: Same 2nd year, year of the Tiger (1782), 2nd month - When he submitted a petition to resign from his position, since he had served for many years, there was a desire to retain him, but his request was granted. Although he had not reached an advanced age, he was directly ordered to retire. For his diligent and faithful service over many years, he was granted one haori jacket and one kosode robe, as announced.

Note: During his service period, in addition to the above, he was granted court dress, garments, hanging scrolls, and various other items.

One: Same 3rd year, year of the Rabbit (1783), 2nd month, 25th day - He died of illness at the age of 65.
Legal name: Harin-in-den Genshitsu Dōkei Nichiō Koji
Officiating priest: Myōryōzan Daihōji Temple, Burial site: Ōkuboyama

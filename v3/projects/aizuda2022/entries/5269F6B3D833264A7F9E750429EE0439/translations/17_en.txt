One: An'ei 1, year of the Dragon (1772), 8th month - Due to his diligent service, by his lord's grace, his son Ichinojin was granted a fief of 300 koku and appointed to the ranks of gosoba (personal attendants).

One: Same year, 9th month - His request to change his name to Geki was granted.

One: Same year, 10th month - He went up to Edo for guard duty, and in the 5th month of the following year of the Snake, he accompanied the lord on his return journey and came back.

One: Same 5th year, year of the Monkey (1776), 3rd month - He went up as guard duty accompanying the lord's sankin journey and returned in the 10th month of the same year.

One: Same 9th year, year of the Rat (1780), 3rd month - He went up as guard duty accompanying the lord's sankin journey and returned in the 10th month of the same year.

One: Same month - During his guard duty last summer, when Lord Sadaaki went out and there were many preparations and items needed for the occasion, he labored hard, and for this he was granted a kosode robe.

One: Tenmei 1, year of the Ox (1781), 4th month - He served diligently in his current position.

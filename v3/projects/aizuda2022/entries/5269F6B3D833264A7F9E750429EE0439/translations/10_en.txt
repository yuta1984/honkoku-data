One: Same 14th year, year of the Rooster (Kyōhō 14, 1729), 9th month - He went up to Edo for guard duty, completed one term of service, and returned.

One: Same 16th year, year of the Boar (Kyōhō 16, 1731), 3rd month - He went up to Edo for guard duty, completed one term of service, and returned.

One: Same 17th year, year of the Rat (Kyōhō 17, 1732), 5th month - For having served diligently and well since the previous lord's time, [the lord] consulted with the advisors in charge of personnel matters and appointed him to the position of magistrate (bugyō), ordered him to co-sign official documents, granted him permission for unit banners, and announced that retainer stipends would be granted to him.

One: Same year, 9th month - He went up to Edo for guard duty, completed one term of service, and returned.

One: Same year, 11th month - He was granted permission to use a palanquin in Aizu.

One: Same 19th year, year of the Tiger (Kyōhō 19, 1734), 11th month - As requested, he was relieved of his current position. Since he is a person of special standing, he was assigned to a seat next to Makihara Tadaemon without official duties. Moreover, since he is still young, it was announced that if he recovers his health through rest and recuperation, he may be appointed to appropriate duties according to [the lord's] wishes, and therefore no substitute would be appointed for now.

One: In the 4th month of the same year, for successfully presenting formal thanks for being granted succession to the family headship, he was granted two rolls of silk crepe as celebratory gifts

One: In the 9th month of the same year, as there had been much correspondence work since spring and he had diligently performed his duties without delay, he was granted an old court kimono as a reward

One: In the 11th month of the same year, for the first presentation of new grain since assuming family headship, he was granted a ceremonial meal

One: In the same month, his servant and young retainer Genzo (son of Morimatsu Gen'nosuke, who was under the stipend of Masukawa Hayato, a retainer of the main house Tanaka Tosa), who had served for about eleven years since the year of the monkey, had always performed his duties faithfully, and showed particularly meritorious conduct during the withdrawal from Sagami Province the year before last, demonstrating loyal service, was granted two bu in gold as a reward

One: In the same month, as mementos of Lord Kinbun, he was granted one dyed lined kimono and one white under-kimono

One: In the 12th month of the same year, as celebration for receiving official appointment, he was granted five bundles of cotton

One: In the 1st month of the 6th year of the sheep [1823], he first performed the duty of advance guard

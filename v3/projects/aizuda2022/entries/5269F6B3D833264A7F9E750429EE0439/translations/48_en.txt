One: In the 5th month of the 10th year of the rooster [Bunka 10, 1813], his first audience with the lord was successfully completed, and as congratulations, he was granted one bolt of silk

One: In the intercalary 1st month of the same year, regarding difficulties with the lord's private financial arrangements, when everyone's opinions were sought and he submitted his thoughts in a sealed letter, he detailed his considerations without delay and with great care. This was recognized as being due to his constant dedication to the lord's interests, and he was commended as being reliable, as was officially announced

One: In the 12th month of the same year, his appointment to office was announced, and his formal thanks were successfully presented, for which he was granted three bundles of cotton as congratulations

One: In the 11th month of the 11th year of the dog [Bunka 11, 1814], when the lord had his court cap adjusted and sleeves altered, he was granted two bundles of cotton as congratulations

One: In the 3rd month of the same year, regarding his official duties, his position was incorporated with the school magistrate and accounting magistrate, and he was appointed according to seniority. Since then, he was granted a regular seat, permitted to use military banners, and as financial support, was granted twenty pieces of silver annually, as was officially announced

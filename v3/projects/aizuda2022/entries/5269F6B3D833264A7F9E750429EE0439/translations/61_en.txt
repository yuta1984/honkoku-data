One: In the 3rd month of the 3rd year, year of the tiger [1746], he went up as an attendant for guard duty and returned in the 10th month of the same year
Note: He was appointed to guard the official documents and returned

One: In the 12th month of the same year, due to his excellent service in official document duties, he received commendation

One: In the 12th month of Hōreki 1, year of the sheep [1751], when there was a fire at the residences of the provincial inspectors Lord Akizuki Tonomo and Lord Kyōgoku Hyōbu, it was announced that he had properly handled the fire prevention

One: In the 2nd month of the 2nd year, year of the monkey [1752], when the provincial inspectors Lord Akizuki Tonomo and Lord Kyōgoku Hyōbu returned to Edo, he served as an escort to Karasawa

One: In the 6th month of the same year, he received the prisoner Tsuchiya Orie from Wada Hanbei and handed him over to the court office in the 7th month

One: In the 4th month of the 6th year, year of the rat [1756], he went up to Edo for guard duty and returned in the 10th month

One: In the 5th month of the 10th year, year of the dragon [1760], he received the prisoners Kajiwara Denkurō and Minami Hirosuke Tononosuke and handed them over to Kawate Gōzaemon in the 6th month

One: In the 8th month of the same year, as requested, his son Seibei [was appointed to] guard duty

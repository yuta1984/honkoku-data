One: Hōreki 1, year of the Sheep (1751), 1st month - He inherited the family headship and was granted a fief of 1,000 koku without any changes.
　　However, his previous fief was recalled.

One: Same 2nd year, year of the Monkey (1752), 3rd month - He was appointed to the position of kosaban-gashira (junior guard captain).

One: Same year, 9th month - He went up to Edo for guard duty and returned in the 3rd month of the following year of the Rooster.

One: Same 4th year, year of the Dog (1754), 5th month - He was appointed to the position of otomo-bangashira (attendant guard captain).

One: Same 5th year, year of the Boar (1755), 6th month - He was appointed to the position of bangashira (guard captain) and was granted permission for the matoi-uma insignia.

One: Same year, 7th month - His request to change his name to Koemon was approved.

One: Same year, 8th month - He was granted permission to use a palanquin within Aizu domain.

One: Same 6th year, year of the Rat (1756), 4th month - He went up to Edo for guard duty and returned in the 10th month of the same year.

One: Same 9th year, year of the Rabbit (1759), 10th month - He went up to Edo for guard duty, and in the 3rd month of the following year of the Dragon, he was ordered to accompany [the lord] on the imperial visit to Kyoto.

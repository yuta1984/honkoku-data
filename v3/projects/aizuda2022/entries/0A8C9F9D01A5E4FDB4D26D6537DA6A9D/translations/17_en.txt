《Small text:
Third generation》
Katsuemon Toshimitsu　《Small text: First name Yakichi, also Takuemon
Born in Hōreki 4th year, Dog year》
　Mother: Daughter of the Naganami family
　Wife: Daughter of Nakamura Keidayū Yoshihiro　《Small text: Born in Hōreki 5th year, Boar year, died of illness in Bunka 6th year, Snake year, 10th month, 6th day at age 55, Buddhist name Chishin-in Myōei Nijō Daishi, buried at the same location as above》

1. In Meiwa 2nd year, Rooster year, 4th month: As requested by his father, he was granted his first formal audience with the lord.

1. In the same 3rd year, Dog year, 5th month:
　To celebrate Lord Kyōtei's completion of the hair-cutting ceremony,
　when the senior councilors extended invitations, he was appointed as an attendant guard
　and hired server, and served in this capacity.
1. In the same 5th year, Rat year, 12th month: Since he was repeatedly appointed to serve guests when there were visitors, he was given one gold ryō.
1. In Anei 3rd year, Horse year, 4th month: As requested by his father, he was appointed to regular guard duty as a substitute guard in the external retainer group and joined Tanaka Kahyōe's unit.
1. In the same 4th year, Sheep year, 4th month: He petitioned to visit his ancestors' graves in Aizu territory.

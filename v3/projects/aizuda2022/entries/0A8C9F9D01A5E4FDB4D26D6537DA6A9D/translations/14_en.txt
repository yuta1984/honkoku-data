1. In the same year, 11th month:
For Lord Dotsune's wedding ceremony and father-in-law's entry ceremony, he was appointed 
to the Great Hall banquet service and performed these duties.

1. In the 3rd year, Tiger year, 9th month:
For the celebration of the Shogun's appointment, when the senior councilors 
extended invitations, he was appointed to the Small Hall banquet service 
and performed these duties.

1. In Kan'en 2nd year, Snake year, 3rd month, 2nd day: He died of illness at age 68. His posthumous Buddhist name was 
Chishōin Sōken Nichitai Koji. He was buried at Shōgyōin, a sub-temple of Zuirinji Temple in Yanaka, Edo.

In the 7th month of Bunka 8, Year of the Sheep (1811), he inherited the family position and was granted a rice stipend of 150 koku without change, was appointed to the Aizu katte tozama group, and since he was not assigned regular duties for the purpose of studying the arts, he was ordered to submit a kobushin allowance.
   However, he was placed in Jinbo Kuranosuke's group.

1. In the 9th month of the same year, he was granted his first audience with the lord.

1. In the 11th month of the 10th year, Year of the Rooster (1813), having devoted himself to martial arts and achieved mastery in various disciplines that came to the lord's attention, he was released from kobushin status and ordered to regular service.

1. In the same month, for his devotion to archery, he was granted two sets of target arrows as a reward.

1. In the 6th month of the 11th year, Year of the Dog (1814), he was appointed to permanent duty in Edo.

1. In the 8th month of Bunsei 4, Year of the Snake (1821), due to the death of Lord Teikanin, he was appointed as an express messenger to Aizu and served in that capacity.

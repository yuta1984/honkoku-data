Second Generation
　Hikouemon Gentaka　　First name: Daishichi　　Also called: Hachiya

　　Mother: Unknown
　　　　　　　　　　Genbun 5 (1740), 4th month, 22nd day: died of illness, posthumous Buddhist name
　　Wife: Daughter of Akabane Kichizaemon
　　　　　　　　　　Fūyo Teiun Daishi, buried at Gannōjōji Temple on Mt. Kanō
一Kanbun 7 (1667), 3rd month: Being still young,

　he was granted 10 ryō and rations for 5 people
　and summoned to serve as a page to Ishihiko-sama
一Same 11th year (1671), 7th month:
　Ishihiko-sama passed away
　He accompanied the remains and went down [to the domain], then in the 8th month
　returned to Edo
一Same 12th year (1672), 8th month:
　He was appointed as a page to Dotsu-sama
一Same year, 12th month:
　Dotsu-sama passed away

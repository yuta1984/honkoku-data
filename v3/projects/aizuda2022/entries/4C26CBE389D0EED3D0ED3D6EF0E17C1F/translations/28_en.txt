Was ordered to serve as sword guard commander (katana-ban monotō) on a monthly rotation basis, and when serving as sword guard, was to concurrently serve as formal presenter (sōja-ban) with regular additional duties, with administrative matters to be understood as before

One: Same year, intercalary eighth month, was relieved of current duties and appointed to the inactive group (muyaku-gumi), and sent down to Aizu. For having served diligently up to this point, was granted two pieces of silver as a reward. Moreover, was ordered to continue serving as before until a replacement was sent up, and departed in the tenth month of the same year

One: Same year, eleventh month
From Lord Kyōsada, received striped unlined garment, one dyed lined garment, with cotton as an additional gift

One: Same 3rd year, year of the tiger, eighth month, those who wished to work in the costume department were ordered to practice at the school

One: Same year, eleventh month, regarding the previous duty period when serving as duty officer, it was declared that service was performed with diligence

One: Same 4th year, year of the rabbit, fourth month, was appointed as banner magistrate (hata-bugyō) attached to the senior councilor

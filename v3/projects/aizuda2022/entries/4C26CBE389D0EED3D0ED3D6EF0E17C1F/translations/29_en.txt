One: Same 5th year, year of the dragon, first month, was appointed to official duties in Ezo (Hokkaido) territory, and accordingly was granted an audience and received the lord's beneficence (sake was bestowed, and departed in the same month to proceed there)

One: (Same year, ninth month, having completed the official duties and returned to base, was granted an audience while still in travel attire)

Same year, twelfth month, the high and low ranking officials as well as attendants who were dispatched for official duties in Ezo territory endured hardships including sea and land travel, and served for many days in that place. Since the current official duties were completed without delay and all returned to base, a meal was bestowed at the castle

One: Same 6th year, year of the snake, eighth month, for the labor of serving in Ezo territory guard duty, one summer garment (katabira) was bestowed

One: Same 8th year, year of the sheep, eighth month, was granted Nomura Chūjirō's upper residence

One: Same 12th year, year of the boar, twelfth month, regarding training at the Sannomaru training ground, the banner corps members assembled for tactical exercises and such, and advanced to superior level this year. It was declared that this was considered to be due to diligent efforts in instruction

One: Same 13th year, year of the rat, fourth month, eighth day, died of illness at age fifty-seven

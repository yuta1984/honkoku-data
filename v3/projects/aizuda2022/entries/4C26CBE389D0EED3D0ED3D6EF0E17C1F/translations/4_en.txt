One: Enpō 8, year of the monkey, third month
　Was summoned to serve as page (kosho) to Lord Tokuō, granted 5 ryō of gold and stipend for 3 men

One: Same month, performed escort duty and attended at the castle

One: Tenna 1, year of the rooster, second month　Due to succession of the household
　when [the lord] moved to the main residence, immediately appointed as page of the main residence,
　granted an increase of 3 ryō of gold and stipend for 1 man

One: Same 3rd year, year of the boar, fourth month, having served long duty in Edo, was granted
　leave for rest, returned home in the tenth month of the same year

One: Jōkyō 2, year of the ox, eighth month, upon request performed coming-of-age ceremony, was
　appointed to escort guard duty (otomoba), granted the regular stipend allocation of 25 koku and stipend for 4 men

One: Same year, ninth month, his request to change his name to
　Shingobei was granted

One: Same 4th year, year of the hare, ninth month, due to improper conduct by
　his father Yajibei in the past twelfth month, both his official duties and stipend lands were
　confiscated and he was ordered into house confinement, therefore he was ordered to show restraint,
　but when pardoned, he was appointed without official duties

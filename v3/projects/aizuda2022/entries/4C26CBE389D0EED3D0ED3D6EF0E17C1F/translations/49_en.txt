一Enpō 2 (1674), 8th month: He moved northeast and
　went down to Aizu
一Same 3rd year (1675), 5th month: He was ordered to perform the coming-of-age ceremony
　and was appointed as an attendant to Hōshōin-sama. The seasonal gift money vouchers he had received up to this point were suspended,
　and he was granted a total of 18 ryō 2 bu and rations for 5 people

一Same 4th year (1676), 4th month: He went up to Edo
　
　for guard duty, completed one term of service, and returned

　he went up [to Edo] as an escort

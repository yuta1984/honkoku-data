Birth father: Second son of Sugita Gorobee Tadaomi

　Wife: Daughter of adoptive father Monshichi Yasumura, born in Meiwa 3 (1766)

Item: In the 11th month of Kansei 5 (1793), was granted first audience
　with the lord as requested by his parent
Item: In the 12th month of the same year, was appointed to the guard unit for outside vassals
　as requested by his parent, and served in castle guard duty
　　However, being the younger brother of Sugita Gorobee, he was transferred
　　to Kajiwara Tenzen's unit
Item: In the 11th month of Kansei 6 (1794), was granted hereditary stipend of 150 koku
　without any reduction
Item: In the 9th month of Kansei 7 (1795), participated in the bird hunting expedition
　　He participated six more times after this, receiving game as a gift on one occasion
Item: In the 12th month of the same year, during the bird hunting expedition, his unit's
　formation was well-ordered without delay, and his diligence was recognized,
　resulting in commendation for the entire unit
　　He continued to receive such commendations thereafter
Item: In the 12th month of Bunka 1 (1804), was appointed to concurrently supervise
　lower-ranking retainers (yoriki)
Item: In the 11th month of Bunka 4 (1807), was dispatched for Ezo defense duties

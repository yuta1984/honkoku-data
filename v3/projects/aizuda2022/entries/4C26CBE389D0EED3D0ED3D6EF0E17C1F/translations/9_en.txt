One: Same year, ninth month, for working diligently and well for several years, was granted five pieces of silver

One: Same year, eleventh month, was appointed to inner guard duty (okuban)

One: Same year, twelfth month, was summoned to the lord's bedchamber and for diligent and excellent service, was privately granted three pieces of silver

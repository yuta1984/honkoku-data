Daughter　　　Wife of Kashiwazaki Uzen Tadakiyo　Divorced

　Mother: same as above

One: Anei 5th year, year of the monkey, twelfth month, regarding the matter of elder sister Sato
　While she was serving Lord Rinkōin, upon his death
　she was granted extended leave, and until she could settle somewhere (remarry or find new employment)
　she was granted one person's worth of meal stipend, as decreed

One: Year and month unknown　She served as rōjo (senior lady-in-waiting) to Lord Matsudaira Masanosuke

One: Kyōwa 3rd year, year of the dog, ninth month, fourteenth day, died of illness at age sixty-one
　Posthumous Buddhist name: Shōtai-in Myōyō Nichijū Daishi, burial place: Honkōji Temple, Asō, Edo
　　However, from Lord Kuroda, incense money and funeral expenses
　　totaling twenty ryō in gold were granted

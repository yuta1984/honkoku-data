Entry: Same 5th year, year of the dragon (1808), intercalary 2nd month, he worked diligently on land surveying with measuring rods in several villages, and his work was excellent and particularly effective, so he was granted a special salary of one person's stipend

Entry: Same 8th year, year of the sheep (1811), regarding disputes over the location of the new irrigation channel at Kitada Dōhata in Oikawa group and Katsusei in the same group, he was appointed for a three-year term to oversee the water supply difficulties at Kitada Dōhata, performing regular duties from before rice planting, managing village canal construction methods and water distribution, serving through official appointment

Entry: Same 9th year, year of the monkey (1812), 3rd month, he was ordered to conduct inspections of replacement land and shrine sites

Entry: Same year, for the land survey entry at Ezoe branch of Shiokawa group, he was appointed to the temporary position of rod leader

Entry: Same 11th year, year of the dog (1814), 2nd month, he was appointed to the temporary position of regular rod leader for land survey officials, and although this was a weighty matter, he was granted independent audience privileges

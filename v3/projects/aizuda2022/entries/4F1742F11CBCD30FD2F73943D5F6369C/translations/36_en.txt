Regarding the aforementioned Gen'emon, he was the seventh-generation descendant of Kuwata Jūrōbee, a rural samurai from Kuwana in Ise Province, who went down to Musashi Province and resided in Shimoochiai.

First (year and month unknown): Granted 3 ryō in gold and stipend for two retainers
　Appointed as a squad leader (kogashira) in the personal retinue of Lord Ganshōin

First, Anei 6, year of the Rooster (1777), 12th month, 7th day: Died of illness at age 66
　Buddhist name: Daian Ryōgi Zenjōmon　Burial site: Kōrin-in at Takanawa

Shunsaku Nobutaka (originally Sanosuke, born on the 4th day of the 8th month of Meiwa 4, year of the Boar [1767])
　Mother: Adopted daughter of the Kuwana family
　Wife: Daughter of Yamada Chūzaemon Yasutaka

First, Anei 7, year of the Dog (1778), 2nd month: From his late father's allocated stipend, granted stipend for two retainers and summoned to service
　Appointed as apprentice lay priest (hira-bōzu minarai) in service to Lord Ganshōin

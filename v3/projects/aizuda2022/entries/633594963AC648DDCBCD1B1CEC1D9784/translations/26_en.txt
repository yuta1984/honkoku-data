Actually the daughter of Murata Yotadayū Shigeyoshi

1. Kanbun 10 (1670), 11th month, at age 17
　Was summoned to serve as page to Ishihiko-sama, granted a stipend certificate of 4 ryō and provisions for 4 people
1. Kanbun 11 (1671), 5th month
　Accompanied Ishihiko-sama on his journey to Edo for sankin-kōtai attendance
1. Same year, 7th month
　Accompanied Ishihiko-sama's remains on the return journey
　Served until the funeral was completed, and until the first anniversary memorial service
　Was stationed at Jōkōji temple to perform various services
1. Kanbun 12 (1672), 8th month
　Was appointed as page to Dotsū-sama, granted a stipend certificate and quarterly salary
1. Same year, 9th month　Accompanied the journey to Edo for sankin-kōtai attendance
1. Same year, 10th month　Was granted 8 ryō 2 bu for quarterly sewing expenses
1. Same year, 12th month
　Dotsū-sama passed away, and the attendants all proceeded ahead

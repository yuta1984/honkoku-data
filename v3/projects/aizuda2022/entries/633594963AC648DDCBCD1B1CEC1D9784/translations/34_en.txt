Biological son of Shibamiya Genemon Yukihisa (second son)

Wife: adopted daughter of adoptive father Uzaemon Katsui
　
　Biological daughter of Watabe Ruiemon Kiyonobu

　Daughter of Toeda Sakuemon Tameshige

　Daughter of Yokota Kyūbei Kiyofusa

1. In the 4th year of Genbun, year of the Sheep (1739), 6th month: As requested by his parent, he was granted his first audience with the lord.

2. In the 1st year of Kanpō, year of the Rooster (1741), 9th month: Due to his late father Uzaemon's service as deputy administrator (daikan), by the lord's special consideration, 20 koku was deducted from his stipend and he was granted 80 koku. He was appointed to the external group (gaiyō-gumi) and served castle guard duty under Ichinose Kenmotsu's unit.

3. In the same 2nd year, year of the Dog (1742), 6th month: As requested by his superior Ichinose Kenmotsu, he was relieved of his duties, and his successor position was transferred to Kayano Gonbei's unit.

1. In the same 3rd year, year of the Boar (1743), 12th month: He petitioned to change his name to Zahyōe.

2. In the 7th year of Hōreki, year of the Ox (1757), 1st month: Due to the retirement of his superior Kayano Gonbei, he was transferred to Kasuga Nizaemon's unit as a successor position.

3. In the same 9th year, year of the Rabbit (1759), 9th month: As requested by Kasuga Nizaemon, he was relieved of his duties and transferred to Takegawa Sukeemon's unit as a successor position.

4. In the 1st year of Meiwa, year of the Monkey (1764), 1st month: Due to his superior Takegawa Sukeemon's position change, he was transferred to Ikoma Gohei's unit as a successor position.

5. In the same 2nd year, year of the Rooster (1765), 9th month: Due to the death by illness of his superior Ikoma Gohei, he was transferred to Harada Matasuke's unit as a successor position.

6. In the same 6th year, year of the Ox (1769), 1st month: He petitioned to change his name to Shinbei.

7. In the 2nd year of An'ei, year of the Snake (1773), 2nd month: Due to the death by illness of his superior Harada Matasuke, he was transferred to Ikoma Kuranosuke's unit as a successor position.

8. In the same 3rd year, year of the Horse (1774), 2nd month: It was announced that he had diligently performed his castle guard duties without negligence.

9. In the same year, 9th month: He petitioned to make Shinjirō, the younger brother of Mikoshiba Saijirō, his son-in-law and adopted heir.

10. In the same 6th year, year of the Rooster (1777), 3rd month: [He had served] castle guard duty for thirty-six years

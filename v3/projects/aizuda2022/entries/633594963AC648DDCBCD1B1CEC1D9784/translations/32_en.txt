1. Kyōhō 1 (1716), 3rd month - As his father requested, he was appointed as replacement guard and served castle guard duty in Jimbo Mozaemon's unit

1. Kyōhō 4 (1719), 11th month - He was granted the hereditary stipend of 100 koku without any changes

1. Same year, 7th month - Due to Jimbo Mozaemon's request for retirement, he was transferred to Sugita Gohei's unit as his successor

1. Kyōhō 6 (1721), 5th month - He was appointed as attendant guard (gosuppoban)

1. Same month - He petitioned to change his name to Uzaemon

1. Kyōhō 7 (1722), 4th month - He went up to Edo for guard duty service and returned in the 10th month of the same year

1. Kyōhō 9 (1724), 10th month - He went up to Edo for guard duty and returned in the 5th month of the following Snake year as part of the lord's retinue
   Note: He served as temporary gate guard during the journey back

1. Kyōhō 14 (1729), 5th month - He was appointed as gate guard (gotoba-ban)

1. Kyōhō 15 (1730), 4th month - He went up for guard duty as part of the lord's retinue and returned in the 10th month
   Note: Upon his return, he was granted used formal court dress (kamishimo)

At the time of the lord's inspection, it was declared that his and his disciples' swordsmanship served well.
　Note: On one subsequent occasion when the lord observed, the same declaration was made.

In the seventh month of Bunsei 5 (1822), having devoted himself for several years as a swordsmanship instructor and having trained many disciples well, he was granted three pieces of silver as a reward.

In the 5th month of Bunpō 1 (1818), according to his parent's petition, he was granted his first audience with the lord.

In the 2nd month of the same 3rd year (1820), he inherited the family headship and was granted a stipend of 100 koku without change, appointed to the external retainer unit. Since he was studying the arts and could not be assigned to various duties during this time, he was ordered to pay the kobushin fee.

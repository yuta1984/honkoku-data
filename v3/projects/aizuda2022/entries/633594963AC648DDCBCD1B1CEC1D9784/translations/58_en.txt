- In the 3rd month of Hōreki 7 (Year of the Ox), he was granted his first audience (with the lord) in Edo as requested by his father.

- In the 3rd month of Meiwa 5 (Year of the Rat), he was appointed as a guard substitute as requested by his father, and served castle guard duty in Numasawa Gonbei's unit.

- In the 11th month of the same year, he transferred to Ichinose Naiki's unit as successor to the position of guard substitute under group leader Numasawa Gonbei.

- In the 5th month of the 6th year (Year of the Ox), he absconded.

- In An'ei 1 (Year of the Dragon), month unknown, he was pardoned and allowed to return to his home domain.

- He died of illness on the 23rd day of the 12th month of Kyōwa 2 (Year of the Dog). His Buddhist name was Ikadayama Sōtaku Shinji, buried in the same place as above.

Katsutarō: Died of illness on the 7th day of the 8th month of Meiwa 7 (Year of the Tiger). Buddhist name was Shōgen Dōji, buried in the same place as above.

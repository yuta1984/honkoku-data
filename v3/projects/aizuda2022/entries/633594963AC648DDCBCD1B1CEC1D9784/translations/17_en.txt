- Same 11th year (1814), 9th month: As requested, he was relieved of his current duties and appointed to the external group (tozama-gumi-tsuki), entering Ibuka Shizuma's group.

- Same 12th year (1815), 4th month: Due to illness, he petitioned to surrender his construction allowance (kobushin-ryō). He was appointed as a first-class construction group external retainer (ichi-no-kobushin-gumi tozama-shi) under the supervision of the chief inspector (ōmetsuke).

- Same 13th year (1816), 2nd month: As requested, Ume Kakugorō was appointed as his guard substitute (ban-dai).

- Same year, 5th month: The guard substitute Ume Kakugorō died of illness, so although he should have resumed duty, as his illness was still unclear, he petitioned to surrender his construction allowance. He was appointed as a first-class construction group external retainer under the supervision of the chief inspector.

- Bunsei 1st year (1818), 6th month: He petitioned to make his second son Katsusaburō his heir.

- Same 2nd year (1819), 12th month: As the construction group members were restored to their former status as before, he returned to Ibuka Shizuma's group.

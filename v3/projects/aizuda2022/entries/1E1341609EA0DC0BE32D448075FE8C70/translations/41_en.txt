1. In the 10th month of Kan'en 1, he went up to Edo, and in the 4th month of the following year (Kan'en 2, 1749), he came down as an escort.

1. In the 12th month of the same 3rd year (Kan'en 3, 1750), because he served diligently with dedication during his duty assignment, he was granted one roll of silk with cotton added as compensation for a court robe.

1. In the 8th month of the same 4th year (Kan'en 4, 1751), he went on patrol as a guard for the domain inspectors' visits to nearby villages and returned in the 9th month of the same year.

1. In the 11th month of Hōreki 2 (1752), he received custody of the detained person Maruyama Sanpei from Ban Iori's residence and transferred him to the court office in the 12th month of the same year.

1. In the 10th month of the same 3rd year (Hōreki 3, 1753), he went up to Edo for guard duty and came down in the 4th month of the following year (Hōreki 4).

1. In the 4th month of the same 9th year (Hōreki 9, 1759), he went up to Edo for guard duty and came down in the 10th month of the same year.

1. In the 8th month of the same 11th year (Hōreki 11, 1761), he received custody of the detained person Suwa Sakon at the meeting office and transferred him to Ichiyanagi Sanemon's residence in the 9th month of the same year.

1. In the 4th month of the same 14th year (Hōreki 14, 1764), he went up for guard duty as an escort and came down in the 10th month of the same year.

1. In the 3rd month of Meiwa 3 (1766), he received custody of the detained person Honda Monjirō from Ban Iori's residence and in the 4th month transferred him to Suzuki Sakuzaemon [to be continued].

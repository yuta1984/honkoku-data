Gorōzaemon Tsunefumi　Originally named Samon
　Mother: daughter of the Takamine family

　Wife: daughter of Hasegawa Goemon Tsunemine (died of illness on the 25th day, 11th month of Tenmei 1 (1781), posthumous Buddhist name Shinchin'in Myōgo Nichihō Daishi, burial site: same as above)
　　Daughter of Miyahara Gakuzaemon Nagatora (died of illness on the 20th day, 9th month of Bunka 12 (1815), posthumous Buddhist name Jissōin Myōri Nisshō Daishi, burial site: same as above)

1. In the 3rd month of Enkyō 4 (1747), as requested by his father, he was granted his first audience with the lord.

1. In the 2nd month of Meiwa 4 (1767), as requested by his father, he was appointed as a guard replacement in the tozama group and entered Sugita Gorōbē's unit, serving castle guard duty.

1. In the 10th month of the same year, due to his superior Sugita Gorōbē's request for relief from duty being granted, he transferred to Kasuya Gozaemon's unit as a replacement.

1. In the 3rd month of the same 5th year (Meiwa 5, 1768), he went up to Edo for guard duty service and returned in the 10th month of the same year.

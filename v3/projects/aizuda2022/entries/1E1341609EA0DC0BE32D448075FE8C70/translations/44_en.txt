1. In the 3rd month of An'ei 5 (1776), he went up to Edo for guard duty service, served as temporary attendant during the lord's journey, and returned in the 10th month of the same year.

1. In the 4th month of the same year, during the lord's pilgrimage to Nikko shrine, he joined as a squad leader and was commended for his diligent service.

1. In the 2nd month of the same 9th year (An'ei 9, 1780), he inherited the family headship with a fief of 350 koku granted without change.

1. In the 1st month of Tenmei 4 (1784), he was commended for serving castle guard duty without negligence.

1. In the 5th month of the same year, he petitioned to change his name to Gorōzaemon.

1. In the 7th month of the same year, he was appointed as squad leader (monotō).

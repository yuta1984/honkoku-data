Wife: Daughter of Ohara Sōemon Shigeyuki (died of illness on the 1st day of the 9th month of Genroku 3 (1690), Buddhist name Chikō'in Myōnichi Daishi, buried at the same location (Myōryōzan Daihōji Temple))
Daughter of Sakai Takehei Gen'ga (died of illness on the 8th day of the 2nd month of Enkyō 3 (1746), Buddhist name Butsugen'in Myōshō Nichihō Daishi, buried at the same location (Myōryōzan Daihōji Temple))

- Kanbun 10 (1670), 5th month: As requested by his father, was appointed to the guard duty outside group (ban-dai tozama-gumi) and joined Seki Saburōemon's unit to serve castle guard duty

- Same year, 7th month: When group leader Seki Saburōemon was relieved of his duties, transferred to Jinbo Mozaemon's group as his successor

- Enpō 3 (1675), 10th month: Went up to Edo for guard duty service, completed one tour of duty and returned

- Enpō 7 (1679), 5th month: Received hereditary fief of 350 koku without any changes

[Written in vermillion] Same year: Requested and was granted permission to change his name to Gorōzaemon

- Same year, 12th month: When group leader Jinbo Mozaemon was relieved of his duties as requested, transferred to Ibuka Shirōbei's group as his successor

- Tenna 2 (1682), 11th month: When group leader Ibuka Shirōbei was relieved of his duties, transferred to Kayano Gonbei's group as his successor

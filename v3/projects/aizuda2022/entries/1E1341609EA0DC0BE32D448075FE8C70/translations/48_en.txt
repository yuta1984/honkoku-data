1. In the same month, during the current bird hunting expedition, regarding the coordination and rhythm of his unit members, their unified cooperation, and the good arrangement of their advances and retreats, and his long-standing diligence and instruction in these matters, he received praise as his lord's intention.
　　However, he continued to receive praise on every occasion thereafter.
1. In the 10th month of the same 12th year (Kansei 12, 1800), he received [someone] from the custody of Takagi Kichimase Shigemon and transferred [them] to Sugita Dainosuke in the 11th month of the same year.
1. In the 8th month of Kyōwa 1 (1801), his request to change his name to Gorozaemon was granted.
1. In the 10th month of the same year, he went up for guard duty as an emergency attendant, and returned in the 4th month of the following dog year (Kyōwa 2, 1802).

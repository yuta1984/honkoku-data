1. In the 1st month of the same 2nd year (Kyōwa 2, 1802), due to a fire in the Azabu area, he went with troops under Hoshina Echizen-no-kami-sama, where he received sake and provisions, and was later granted 200 mon in gold according to the official record.
1. In the 4th month of the same year, for serving in the reception duties at Sadaaki-sama's audience chamber, he was granted one bundle of cotton as a reward.
1. In the 1st month of the same 3rd year (Kyōwa 3, 1803), due to disturbances in Shinmachi in Kasama domain, troops were dispatched to the border, so he went leading his unit members, but as that area became peaceful, he returned in the same month.
1. In the same month, regarding this unexpected incident where troops were dispatched to the border, his usual preparedness was excellent, he quickly organized preparations and departed, and from before departure through his stay, he devoted himself wholeheartedly to all matters and handled them well. Due to his particular hardship during the cold season, it was announced that he would be granted special provisions.

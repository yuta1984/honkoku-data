1. In the 1st month of the 11th year, Inu year, he was appointed as a company commander (monogashira).

1. In the 4th month of the same year, he went up to Edo for guard duty service, served one term, and came back down.
　　However, during the campaign he served as assistant to the court liaison officer.

1. In the 6th month of the 12th year, Inoshishi year, due to the box money incident, his official duties were taken away and it was announced that his heir was to be dismissed.

1. On the 6th day of the 8th month of the same year, he died of illness at the age of twenty-three. His posthumous Buddhist name was Gongiin Jun'sei Nichigyō Koji, buried in the same place as above.

Kozen　　　　Born on the 26th day of the 9th month of the 7th year, Usagi year of Sadamasa; died of illness on the 21st day of the 3rd month of the 9th year, Mi year. Posthumous Buddhist name: Yōkō Magoko. Buried in the same place as above.

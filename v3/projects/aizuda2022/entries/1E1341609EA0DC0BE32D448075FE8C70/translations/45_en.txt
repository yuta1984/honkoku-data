1. In the intercalary 10th month of the same year, he received custody from Toe Uemon and Ogawa Jūemon, and transferred it to Ohara Gen'gorō in the 11th month of the same year.

1. In the 7th month of Tenmei 7 (1787), for diligently serving three consecutive years of rotation duty, he was granted two bundles of cotton as a reward.

1. In the 12th month of the same 8th year (Tenmei 8, 1788), he was appointed as assistant head guard, and was granted permission to exercise command authority in his official capacity.

1. In the 2nd month of Kansei 4 (1792), as requested, he was relieved of his official duties, and his son Chūta was appointed as guard substitute.

1. On the 1st day of the 9th month of the same 11th year (Kansei 11, 1799), he died of illness at age 65. His Buddhist name was Hōrin'in Enshō Nissoku, buried in the same place as above.

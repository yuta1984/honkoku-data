He was granted an audience with the lord.

1. In the 1st month of the 5th year, Tatsu year, when his father went on official duty for the defense of Ezo-chi (Hokkaido), he accompanied him as an attendant, and for this reason was granted an audience, received sake from the lord's own cup, and was bestowed sake.

1. In the 10th month of the same year, upon returning from the campaign, he was given sake at the lodging before arrival, and on the day of arrival, still in traveling clothes, was granted an audience with the lord.

1. In the 12th month of the same year, when he went as an attendant for the defense of Ezo-chi, traveling by sea and land routes and serving diligently for several days in that place, since he returned from the campaign without exception at this time, he was served a meal at the castle.

1. In the 8th month of the 6th year, Mi year, because he went as an attendant for Ezo-chi business, he was bestowed one bundle of cotton.

1. In the 11th month of the 10th year, Tori year, as requested by his father, he was appointed as a substitute guard, retainer to the senior councilor, and outer vassal, and entered the third unit.
　　However, since he was appointed to military lecture duty, he was exempted from castle guard resident duty attendance, as announced.

In Hōei 6, year of the Ox (1709), sixth month, he was granted his first audience with the lord as requested by his father.

In Kyōhō 3, year of the Dog (1718), second month, he inherited a stipend of 700 koku without reduction and was assigned to the tozama-gumi (outer retainer group), serving castle guard duty in Ichinose Jingozaemon's unit.

In Kyōhō 5, year of the Rat (1720), tenth month, he went up to Edo for guard duty service. In the first month of the following year of the Ox, he served as an additional messenger during the wedding ceremony of Shōsentei-in-sama.

In Kyōhō 6, year of the Ox (1721), second month, he was appointed to the messenger corps, and it was announced that he had diligently and properly performed his duties as temporary messenger. In the fourth month of the same year, he returned home as part of the lord's retinue.

In the same month, his request to change his name to Seizaemon was granted.

In the same year, ninth month, he went up to Edo for guard duty service and returned home in the third month of the following year of the Tiger.

In Kyōhō 8, year of the Rabbit (1723), eleventh month, he was appointed as messenger for the lord's formal visit inquiry and went up to Edo.

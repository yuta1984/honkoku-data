• Same 6th year, Snake year, 3rd month (Bunka 6, 1809): As requested by his father Budayu, he was granted permission to retire, and the family headship and stipend of 200 koku were granted without change.

• Same year, 8th month: For his diligent service in official duties related to Ezo defense, he was granted one ceremonial garment, and it was proclaimed that he had diligently performed the supervision of pack animals during travel and had worked earnestly as acting inspector.

• Same year: Requested and was granted permission to change his name to Naiki.

• Same 9th year, Monkey year, 12th month (Bunka 9, 1812): During combined unit training at the Sannomaru training ground and Hongo Plain, his formation and spacing were excellent and he showed great dedication, which was recognized as meritorious, and his entire unit received praise.

• Same 10th year, Rooster year, 10th month (Bunka 10, 1813): For going to Hongo Yamabe with his companions and engaging in drinking and eating, he was dismissed from his unit assignment, had 30 koku deducted from his stipend, and was assigned to minor construction duties.

• Same 11th year, Dog year, 12th month (Bunka 11, 1814): Was reassigned to a unit and entered Kayano Kuranosuke's group, serving castle guard duty.

• Same 13th year, Rat year, 12th month (Bunka 13, 1816): In training ground exercises, his formation and spacing were well-coordinated, achieving superior rank for three years...

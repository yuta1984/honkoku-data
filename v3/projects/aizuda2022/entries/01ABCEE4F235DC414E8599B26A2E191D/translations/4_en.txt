1. In Genna 4 (1618), the Year of the Horse 【month unknown】, from his father Mozaemon's retirement stipend, he was granted 100 koku 【official position unknown】 and was summoned to service, receiving the honor of using the character "Mitsu" (光) in his name

1. In the 7th year of the same era (1621), the Year of the Rooster 【month unknown】, when official timber procurement from Mount Ōkawara in Shinshū was ordered, he went as deputy for his elder brother Kenmotsu and performed the duty

1. In Kan'ei 11 (1634), the Year of the Dog, 7th month: He was assigned lodging arrangements for the lord's journey to Kyoto and accompanied the procession to the capital

1. In the 12th year of the same era (1635), the Year of the Boar 【month unknown】, he served as guard at Sakurada Gate in Edo

1. In the 13th year of the same era (1636), the Year of the Rat, 8th month: Upon the domain transfer to Mogami in Ushū, he received a second stipend increase of 100 koku

1. In the same year, 12th month: He accompanied his lord's procession to Edo for the mandatory attendance (sankin-kōtai)

1. In the 15th year of the same era (1638), the Year of the Tiger 【month unknown】, he was appointed to the lord's escort guard
　　During this service, he performed guard duty for two campaigns

1. In the 18th year of the same era (1641), the Year of the Snake 【month unknown】, he was appointed as messenger and received a stipend increase of 50 koku
　　During this service, he performed guard duty for one campaign

1. In the 20th year of the same era (1643), the Year of the Sheep, 8th month: He accompanied his lord during the domain transfer to Aizu

• In the 9th year of the same era, 6th month (1800): As requested, his son Shurinosuke was appointed as his guard substitute.

• Bunka 6, 3rd month (1809): As requested, he was granted permission to retire.

• Same month, 29th day: Died of illness at age 76. Buddhist name: Rinkōin Banzan Ketsuryu Koji. Presiding priest from Shōunzan Zenryūji temple. Burial site: Ōkubo Mountain.


Iwatarō Sukehodo
　Mother: daughter of the Tanaka family

• Anei 6, 7th month (1777): As requested by his parent, granted his first formal audience with the lord.

• Tenmei 7, 12th month (1787): Due to illness, at his parent's request, was dismissed as heir.

• Kansei 6, intercalary 11th month, 8th day (1794): Died of illness. Buddhist name: Chinjuin Sesō Myōhaku Koji. Presiding priest and burial site: same as above.

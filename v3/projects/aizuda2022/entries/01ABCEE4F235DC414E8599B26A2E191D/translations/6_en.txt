He was appointed to a combined unit

1. In Manji 2 (1659), the Year of the Boar, 4th month: He was appointed as head of the gun corps (teppō-gashira)

1. In the same year, 10th month: Regarding his father Mozaemon
   Because he had rendered great service to Lord Dōgi, he was appointed as group leader (kumi-gashira)

1. In Kanbun 1 (1661), the Year of the Ox, 4th month: He was granted permission to use a palanquin in Aizu

1. In the same year, intercalary 8th month: He went to Edo for guard duty and returned in the 2nd month of the following Tiger year

1. In the 5th year of the same era (1665), the Year of the Snake, 8th month: He went to Edo for guard duty and returned in the 3rd month of the following Horse year

1. In the 7th year of the same era (1667), the Year of the Sheep, 6th month: He died of illness at age 62. His Buddhist name was Hōshōin-den Shin'ō Dōtetsu Koji. His grave was at Kongozan Erin-ji temple. In later years, because this name conflicted with the title Hōshōin-sama, it was changed to Eishōin
   However, during his illness and after his death, he received inquiries from his lord, and as a memento, one suit of armor was presented

Daughter: Named Ritsu, born on the 10th day of the 8th month of Kan'ei 13 (1636), the Year of the Rooster. Wife of Kimoto Gonnosuke Nariyoshi

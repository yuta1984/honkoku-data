1. Same month: His petition to adopt Matsunosuke, the second adopted son of Ibuka Nanauemon, as his son-in-law and heir was approved.

1. Same 7, Uma year (1810), 3rd month: His petition to change his name to Moku was approved.

1. Same 8, Hitsuji year (1811), 8th month: He attended the bird hunting expedition.
   Note: He attended twice more after this occasion.

1. Same year, 12th month: He was commended for his diligent performance in horse unit logistics.
   Note: He continued to receive praise on many occasions thereafter.

1. Same 14, Ushi year (1817), 1st month: Due to eruptions on his head, his petition to shave his head completely was approved.

1. Same year, 10th month: For his diligent efforts and good supervision in training the retainer assistants (yoriki), he was rewarded with one new spear.

1. Bunsei 1, Tora year (1818), 8th month: Regarding his duties as supervisor of retainer assistants, he was appointed to a position ranked after the 250-koku stipend level.

1. Same 2, U year (1819), 3rd month: As requested, he was granted permission to retire.

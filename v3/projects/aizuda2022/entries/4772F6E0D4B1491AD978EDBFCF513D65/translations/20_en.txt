One: In the 2nd month of the 9th year of the same era (Year of the Monkey, 1817), he was appointed to serve as a hired retainer for Sagami Province external affairs and was granted a stipend of three persons' worth of rice.

One: In the 9th month of the 10th year (Year of the Rooster, 1818), he was granted the hereditary fief of 100 koku without change, appointed to the Aizu Katsutoshi external group, went down and entered Jinbo Kuranoske's unit, and served guard duty at the castle.

One: In the 1st month of the 12th year (Year of the Boar, 1820), it was announced that he had served diligently during his time as a hired external retainer for Sagami Province.

One: In the 1st month of the 13th year (Year of the Rat, 1821), he was appointed as an assistant instructor of artillery at Kodaira San'gorō's place.
　However, in connection with this appointment, he was exempted from castle guard duty and attendance obligations.

One: In the 2nd month of the 14th year (Year of the Ox, 1822), due to the reassignment of his superior Jinbo Kuranoske, he was transferred to Kasuya Sakon's unit as the successor role.

One: In the 8th month of the same year, he participated in a hunting expedition and was granted the birds he caught.
　However, after this, during the two times he participated in combined military drills, he was granted the birds he caught each time.

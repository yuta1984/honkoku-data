• In the 4th year, 4th month (1739):
  When Lord Dotsune first entered his domain, returned home to welcome him,
  then went back up to serve as his attendant and returned home
• In the 10th month of the same year: Was appointed to御次番 (gojiban, next in rotation guard duty)
• In the 5th year, 3rd month (1740): Went up to Edo for guard duty,
  returned in the 10th month of the same year
• In Enkyō 1st year, 3rd month (1744): Went up to Edo for guard duty,
  returned in the 10th month of the same year
• In the 3rd year, 10th month (1746): Made an emergency trip to Edo for guard duty,
  returned in the 4th month of the following Mō year
• In the 4th year, 8th month (1747): For his many years of faithful service,
  his previous provisional stipend was converted to a direct grant of 100 koku
• In Kan'en 3rd year, 3rd month (1750):
  Was granted Lord Dotsune's clothing as a memento
• Hōreki 2nd year, 9th month, 6th day (1752): Died of illness. Buddhist name: Mutetsu
  Sōkan, buried in the same location [as mentioned previously]

• In the 5th year, 3rd month (1720):
  Was appointed as attendant to Lord Shōsentei-in
• In the 6th year, 3rd month (1721): Went up to Edo for guard duty,
  returned in the 9th month of the same year
• In the 7th year, 2nd month (1722): Was granted the former residence
  of Kasuga Kitōji
• In the 9th year, 9th month (1724): Went up to Edo for guard duty,
  returned in the 3rd month of the following Mi year
  Note: Served twice as temporary gate guard during military campaigns
• In the 12th year, 3rd month (1727):
  Due to the passing of Lord Shōsentei-in, was reassigned to kobanshu (small guard unit)
  and appointed as head of kobanshu for having served as attendant
• In the 12th month of the same year:
  Was appointed as personal guard to Lord Dotsune
• In the 13th year, 3rd month (1728):
  When Lord Dotsune went up to Edo, served as returning attendant
  and accompanied him
• In the 15th year, 3rd month (1730): Went up to Edo for guard duty,
  returned in the 9th month of the same year

- In Kanbun 8th year, Monkey year (1668), 8th month: As requested by his father, was appointed as a deputy guard in the external vassals group, joined Miyake Magobē's unit, and served castle guard duty

- In the 12th year, Rat year (1672), 2nd month: Was granted the hereditary stipend of 600 koku without change

- In Enpō 4th year, Dragon year (1675), 11th month: Was appointed to Edo fire watch duty and went up; returned in the 5th month of the following Snake year as part of the lord's entourage on his return journey
　Note: For this service, was granted one set of seasonal clothing

- In the 7th year, Sheep year (1678), 6th month: When a marriage alliance with Lord Kōfu Chūjō was arranged, was ordered to send an envoy to Edo for congratulations and was granted three pieces of silver. However, Inoue Kin'emon was ordered to carry out this duty, and the silver that had been granted was given as a reserve fund for future envoy appointments

- In the same year, 6th month: When Lord Matsudaira Hyūga-no-kami received an increase in territory and was transferred to Yamato Kōriyama, was appointed as envoy to deliver congratulatory sake barrels and was granted three pieces of silver
　Note: On this occasion, Lord Hyūga-no-kami bestowed five sets of seasonal clothing

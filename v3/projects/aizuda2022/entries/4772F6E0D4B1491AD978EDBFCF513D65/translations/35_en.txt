Hazaemon Chikamura　　《marginal note: original name Tomoya｜born on the 28th day, 2nd month of Hōreki 1st year》
　　Actual second son of Yamada Jintadayū Koreshige
　　Wife: daughter of adoptive father Kyōsuke Chikashiki　《marginal note: born on the 28th day, 9th month of Hōreki 12th year》
• Meiwa 4th year, 6th month (1767): Was granted an audience with the lord
　as petitioned by his father
• Same year, 9th month: Was appointed to oban-dai (deputy guard duty)
　as petitioned by his father, and served castle guard duty in Harada Matasuke's unit
• Anei 1st year, 10th month (1772): Due to the death by illness of unit head Harada Matasuke,
　was transferred to Ikoma Kuranosuke's unit as successor
• Tenmei 1st year, 3rd month (1781): Was commended for serving
　castle guard duty diligently for ten years without negligence
• 5th year of the same era, 4th month (1785): Was granted a hereditary stipend
　of 100 koku without change as succession
• Kansei 1st year, 4th month (1789): Due to unit head Ikoma Kuranosuke
　being relieved of his duties, was transferred to Ibuka Kenmotsu's unit
　as successor

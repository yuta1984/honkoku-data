　　changed his name from Kiyose to Tsuneyasu, and resided in
　　Takatō in Shinano Province. From there,
　　Lord Tōru【Red ink note: Hoshina Danjō-chū Masanao-kō】sent a messenger requesting he serve as a yoriki (retainer), but
　　he declined. Also, during the wars with Nagao Kenshin and Suwa Yorinobu,
　　he was requested to join the military ranks
　　three times and participated in military campaigns, but when summoned after the completion of affairs, he firmly declined
　　and would not accept. However, his military achievements were praised, and as Sayō,
　　he was granted annual rice stipends. Later,
　　Lord Dōgi【Red ink note: Hoshina Danjō-chū Masamitsu-kō】inquired whether he had any sons, so
　　he reported that his son Genzaemon was in Edo,
　　whereupon he received orders to summon him.

Actually the third son of Onoda Sukezaemon Naotane
Wife: daughter of Sasahara Yogohei Tadatoshi (born in Hōreki 5 [1755], died of illness on the 29th day of the 1st month of An'ei 2 [1773] at the age of 19. Posthumous name: Shunkyō-in Kunshitsu Teishō Daishi. Burial place: same as above)
[Second wife:] daughter of Miyake Kohyōe Naotane

In Hōreki 10, 10th month (1760): Two-person stipend was reduced from his late father's allocated portion, granted an eight-person stipend, and summoned as an Inawashiro samurai.

In the 11th year of the same era, 3rd month (1761): Returned to Wakamatsu and appointed as a samurai outside the regular groups.

In Meiwa 6, 2nd month (1769): Appointed to the外様組付 (tozama group attachment), entered Kajiya Gozaemon's group, and served as a castle guard.

1. In the 9th month of Jōō 2 (1653), year of the snake, he was appointed as an attendant for the lord's journey to Kyoto.

1. In Kanbun 2 (1662), year of the tiger, he was appointed to the messenger corps (otsukai-ban).

1. In the 4th month of Kanbun 9 (1669), year of the rooster, when Lord Hoshina Masayuki retired and moved to the Mita residence, he was appointed to serve the retired lord and moved to the Mita residence, where he served as an intermediary in his capacity as messenger corps member.

1. In the 1st month of Kanbun 11 (1671), year of the boar, when the Kii Dainagon (Lord Tokugawa Mitsusada) passed away, he was appointed as messenger to Kii Province. He was summoned before his lord, who expressed kind words to him and granted him 10 pieces of silver.

1. In the 12th month of Kanbun 12 (1672), year of the rat, when Lord Hoshina Masayuki passed away, he went to Kuribashi as a messenger to maintain communications there, and remained in that location until the 2nd month of the following year of the ox.

1. In the 7th month of Enpō 2 (1674), year of the tiger, due to the death of Lord Matsudaira Awaji-no-kami, he was appointed as messenger to Etchū Province.

1. In the 3rd month of Enpō 5 (1677), year of the snake, he was appointed to the intelligence corps (okiki-ban).

1. In Enpō 7 (1679), year of the sheep, he was appointed to distribute relief funds within the domain.

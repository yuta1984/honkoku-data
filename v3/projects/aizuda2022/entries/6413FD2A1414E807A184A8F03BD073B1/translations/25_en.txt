First year of Kyōwa, year of the Rooster, tenth month: Since his son Susumu died of illness, he petitioned to adopt Eijirō, the younger brother of Yanase Genjirō, as a son-in-law and adopted son, and this was granted.

Twelfth month of the same year: It was announced that he would be appointed as御年男 (ceremonial New Year attendant) the following spring, and he was granted a formal robe (noshime) bearing the lord's crest.

Same month: Since he performed extraordinary labor duties in connection with school construction work, he was granted two pieces of silver as a reward.

Second year, year of the Dog, third month: Since he had diligently served for years in the temporary position of inner guard (okuban), he was granted three pieces of silver as a reward.

Third year, year of the Boar, fourth month: When Lord Sadaaki traveled down from Edo, he was appointed to the temporary position of travel escort guard and accompanied him down.
Note: It was announced that he served without incident during the journey.

Sixth month of the same year: Due to the dissolution of relations with his adopted son Eijirō, he petitioned to return him to his original family, the Yanase Genjirō household, and this was granted, allowing him to return to his birth family.

Ninth month of the same year: He petitioned to adopt Benjirō, the second son of Morokado Jōji, as a son-in-law and adopted son, and this was granted.

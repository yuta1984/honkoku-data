Same month: From serving as escort at Lord Kyōtei's funeral procession until the first anniversary memorial in the eighth month of the following year of the Tiger, he served as guard at the temporary palace.

Ninth month of the same year: He was relieved of his current duties and appointed above the rank of gaisama-shi (outside retainers) in the unemployed group, and it was announced that he would be exempted from the kofushin-ryō (maintenance stipend).

Same month: Since he had served diligently in close attendance to Lord Kyōtei, it was announced through a retainer that five ryō from the lord's personal funds would be granted to him, as had been announced during the lord's lifetime. Additionally, since he had also served diligently in the cost-reduction duty position under the lord's personal administration, it was announced that an additional five ryō in gold would be granted.

Eleventh month of the same year: As Lord Kyōtei's bequeathed items, he was granted one noshime formal robe with cotton lining, one white kosode undergarment, one plain haori jacket without family crest, one plain hakama, one dyed plain garment, and one絞り-patterned kosode.

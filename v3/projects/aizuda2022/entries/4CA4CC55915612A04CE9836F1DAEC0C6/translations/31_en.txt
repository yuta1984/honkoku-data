He served as an envoy and received thirty pieces of silver and five rolls of silk crepe from Lord Matsudaira Kaga-no-kami.

1. In the 11th month of the same year, he was appointed to the position of chief retainer (karo). Although he was supposed to be summoned to Edo, since he had only recently returned and moreover there was no official residence available for him, he was not summoned. He was granted permission for ceremonial equipment and given an allowance equivalent to intermediate rank retainers.

1. In the 12th month of the same year, having been appointed as general superintendent of the new construction of the Upper Residence, he worked diligently alone through extended duty until the 10th month of that year, successfully completing the major construction project. In recognition of this, he was granted one haori jacket and one kosode robe.

1. In the 10th month of the 2nd year, year of the Tiger, due to illness, his lord made kind inquiries about his health and granted him one type of delicacy.
Note: Both before and after this time, his lord frequently made caring inquiries about his health condition.

1. In the 12th month of the same year, when he submitted a request for resignation due to illness, although the reason for his request was understandable, he was told that since he was still young, his resignation would not be accepted, and he was ordered to focus on recovery while continuing his duties.

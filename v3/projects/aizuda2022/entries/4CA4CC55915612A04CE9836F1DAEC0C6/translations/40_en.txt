Regarding the audience duties for Ryōtoku-in-sama, due to handling various busy matters well and providing proper guidance, one haori jacket and one kosode garment were bestowed.

1. In the first month of the eleventh year (Year of the Sheep), regarding the guard duty from the previous year, due to working hard on various matters concerning the new construction of the Ōkawabata mansion and achieving successful completion, one set of formal court dress (tsugi kamishimo) was bestowed.

1. In the third month of the same year, due to continuously serving monthly duty from the previous September through February and working particularly hard, one set of formal court dress was bestowed.

1. In the sixth month of the same year, Sadaaki-sama honored [us] with his visit, and one load of sake barrels (white wooden barrels containing Edo sake "Yōrōkai") and delicacies (fifty grilled sea bream from the beach) were bestowed.

1. In the ninth month of the same year, Sadaaki-sama stopped by for a visit.

1. In the fourth month of the twelfth year (Year of the Monkey), there was a memorial service for the 150th anniversary of Daiyū-in-sama at Nikkō-san, and being ordered to serve fire watch duty, the said duties were assigned. On the fourth day of the same month, [he] departed from Edo, traveled to that place to serve, and returned to Aizu on the twenty-fifth day of the same month.

1. In the 5th month of the 4th year, year of the Dragon, his lord made kind inquiries about his health condition.
Note: After this time as well, his lord continued to make inquiries about his health condition.

1. In the 8th month of the same year, due to illness making it difficult to perform his duties and having unavoidable circumstances, he submitted a second request for resignation. His request was deemed reasonable and accepted, his current position was granted exemption, and he was appointed to a rank next to the chief retainers and above the junior elders (wakatoshiyori). Since he was still young, he was told that his lord wished him to focus well on his recovery.

1. In the 10th month of the 5th year, year of the Snake, when Suwa Chuujiro went down to the domain, Lady Senmyoin made kind inquiries about his health condition.

1. In the 12th month of the same year, he was ordered to return to the position of chief retainer (karo), and was told that his original standing would be restored.

1. In the 4th month of the 7th year, year of the Sheep, he submitted a request to change his name to Saburoubei.

1. In the 5th month of the same year, when he went up to the capital to welcome the lord's return to the domain, since he performed his duties there without delay, he was granted one haori jacket.

1. In the 10th month of the same year, he was appointed to special duties for the townspeople's affairs (record incomplete).

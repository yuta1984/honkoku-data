The distant ancestors are unknown, but the grandfather was Tanaka Jibuzaemon
Gengi. His birth province was Seshū (Ise Province), and he later came to Kōshū where he
served Takeda Shingen. On the 9th day of the 5th month of Tenshō year of the Boar, following Shirō Katsuyori,

he died in battle at the Battle of Nagakusa in Sanshū Province. Both his Buddhist posthumous name and
burial site are entirely unknown. Gengi had two children, male and female.
The daughter married Kōsaka Danjō

In later years he served the Toyotomi family and managed

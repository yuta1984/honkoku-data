1. In the same month, when he departed for Edo, he was granted leave in the lord's presence. The lord personally bestowed one roll of crepe silk in place of a haori jacket, two rolls of habutae silk in place of a kosode, both with cotton added. When he withdrew to the night quarters, he was given one box of confections through the inner guard, then after partaking of a meal, when he went to the inner chambers, his lordship bestowed one package of confections.

1. In the twelfth month of the same year, when he returned from Edo, an attendant messenger came to Miyomura with one cask of sake and two kinds of side dishes as gifts.

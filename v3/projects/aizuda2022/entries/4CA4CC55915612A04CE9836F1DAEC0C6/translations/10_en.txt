However, when Lord Doshin made his visit, [the retainer's condition] had not yet cleared and he could not meet him, being unlike his usual state, and it was heard that he also had some ritual abstinences. Since there was no need to undertake major official duties, he was to focus primarily on maintaining his health at all times. Soon moat construction work would also be assigned. Until the previous year, he had remained at construction sites all day and exhausted his strength, which was thought to be the cause of his illness. Regarding magistrate duties, since construction magistrates and other group leaders, unit commanders, chief inspectors, Edo rotation inspectors, district magistrates and others came and went occasionally, there was sufficient coverage, so he was commanded to focus primarily on careful health maintenance.

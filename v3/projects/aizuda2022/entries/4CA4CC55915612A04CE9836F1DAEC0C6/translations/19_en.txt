1. In the 6th month of the same year, he was appointed to the messenger guard (oshitsukeban).

1. In the 2nd month of Genroku 3, year of the Horse, he was appointed to the court presentation guard (osōshaban).

1. In the 4th month of Genroku 5, year of the Monkey, he was appointed as head of the escort guard (otomobantō).

1. In the 4th month of Genroku 8, year of the Boar, he was appointed as captain (bantō).

1. In the 6th month of Genroku 14, year of the Snake, he was appointed to the magistrate position in the lord's presence, but due to incompetent conduct, he apologized and, as requested, was relieved of his duties and reassigned to his former position as captain.

1. In the 5th month of Shōtoku 1, year of the Rabbit, he was appointed as junior councilor (wakadoshiyori) in the lord's presence.

1. In the same month, he was appointed to affix his seal (kaihan) to official documents.

1. In the 4th month of Shōtoku 4, year of the Horse, he was appointed to the position of chief retainer (karō). He was to be summoned to Edo and assigned to Edo service, but since he would ascend to serve guard duty in the coming 10th month, with little time before that, and considering it appropriate for him to also serve monthly duty in Aizu, he was not summoned. Additionally, he was exempted from carrying the identifying banner (mochisashimono) and was granted allowances for his colleagues and subordinates.

1. In the 7th month of the 3rd year (Meiwa 3), year of the Dog, he was appointed to the position of goshōshaban (presenter to the lord).

1. In the 11th month of the 4th year (Meiwa 4), year of the Boar, he was appointed as kobangashira (captain of the small guard) in the lord's presence.

1. In the 6th month of the 6th year (Meiwa 6), year of the Ox, he was appointed as gotomobangashira (captain of the attendant guard) in the lord's presence.

1. In the 3rd month of the 8th year (Meiwa 8), year of the Rabbit, he was appointed as bangashira (guard captain) and granted permission to use the matoi-ba insignia.

1. In the 5th month of the same year, he was granted permission to use a palanquin in Aizu.

1. In the 2nd month of An'ei 5, year of the Monkey, he was appointed to the position of bugyō (magistrate) in the lord's presence, with his court rank assigned above other magistrates. He was appointed to co-sign official documents for domain business, and was granted continued permission to use the matoi-ba insignia and palanquin in Aizu as before.

1. In the 5th month of An'ei 8, year of the Boar, he was appointed as a messenger for the lord's arrival in Edo, traveled to Edo, was granted an audience with the Shogun, and was awarded two rolls of silk crepe.

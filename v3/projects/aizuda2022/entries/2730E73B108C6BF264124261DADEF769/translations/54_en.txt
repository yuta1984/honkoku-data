• In the fourth month of the same eleventh snake year, he was appointed to serve at the tailoring office, serving until the fourth month of the following horse year.

• In the eighth month of the same twelfth horse year, he was appointed as supervisor of the investigation office for one year, serving until the eighth month of the following sheep year.

• In the twelfth month of the same year, he petitioned to change his name to Shichiroemon.

• In the ninth month of the same thirteenth sheep year, he was appointed as supervisor for procuring fresh fish from Niigata in Echigo Province, returning in the tenth month of the same year.
　However, as a reward, he was granted 200 hiki of gold.

• In the eleventh month of the same year, he was appointed as supervisor for advance tax collection from various places from the Shirakawa route to Sendo, departing and returning in the twelfth month of the same year.

• In the first month of Meiwa 1 (monkey year), due to his diligent service, his current official salary was increased by 2 koku as a direct grant.

• In the second month of the same year, he was appointed as ashigaru group leader (o-kachi gumigashira), granted an official salary of 5 koku and one person's stipend.

• In the tenth month of the same year, he went up to Edo for guard duty, and in the third month of the following rooster year

He was ordered to travel south.

In the 4th month of Bunka 12 (Year of the Boar), he was appointed to the temporary position of transverse flute instructor.

In the 5th month of Bunka 13 (Year of the Rat), after his gagaku examination, his skills were reported to be excellent, so he was promoted to first rank and granted one and a half portions of stipend rice. He was further ordered to continue his diligent training without neglect.

In the 12th month of the same year, at the Sannomaru training ground and Hongohara combined unit practice, the formations were well-ordered and he advanced to the upper rank that year. This was considered to be due to his diligence, and his entire unit was praised and commended.

In the 6th month of Bunka 14 (Year of the Ox), as a private gagaku performance was to be arranged, he was appointed as assistant transverse flute instructor.

In the 7th month of the same year, he was granted permission to wear the crested hemp formal dress (kamishimo) that his great-great-grandfather Ikusaemon had received, as a mark of divine favor.

In the 10th month of the same year, due to Shiba Tetsunoshin being in mourning, he was appointed to assist with transverse flute instruction.

In the same month, having diligently encouraged drill practice, he was granted a new spear as a reward.

In the 2nd month of Bunsei 1 (Year of the Tiger), he was appointed to assist with gagaku instruction.

In the 12th month of the same year, regarding the Sannomaru training ground practice, the formations and marching were well-coordinated, achieving upper rank status for three years and advancing in rank due to his diligence, so he was granted sake.

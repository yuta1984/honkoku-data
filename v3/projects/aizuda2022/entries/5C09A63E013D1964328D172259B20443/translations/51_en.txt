He was ordered to consult with Machino Shōzaburō regarding the appointment and to serve accordingly.

Item 1: Same month - Through the transfer of labor wages, he was granted one gold piece per year at Edo.

Item 1: Same year, 10th month - This time he was appointed to serve as rear horse attendant in the lord's retinue, and as various expenses were incurred, he was granted five pieces of silver annually as special compensation for mounted service.

Item 1: Same year, intercalary 11th month - Having served diligently in daily horse-related duties, and furthermore having completed without delay the emergency castle attendance and horse deployment during the recent nearby fire, as a reward he was privately granted one bolt of silk.

Item 1: Same month - Each time there was a military departure, one person at a time was appointed as rear horse attendant on a rotating basis. However, since the regular duties alone did not include constant retainer service, henceforth during retainer duties he was appointed to additional sword guard service, and was to serve by consulting with the sword guards on all matters. Moreover, from now on, during castle attendance he should be accompanied to the castle entrance. However, since there are also regulations regarding accompanying retainers within the castle, when costume attendants are specially brought along, he should consult with the sword guards so as not to cause any interference.

Item 1: Same year, 3rd month - Upon his departure, Lord Kinbun personally granted him one box of folding fans, and Lady Juhoin granted him wrapped gifts.

Item 1: Same year, 10th month - He went up to Edo, and in the 5th month of the same 12th year of the Boar, he accompanied the lord's entourage when returning to the domain.

Item 1: Same year, 12th month - For performing duties related to horse affairs, he was privately granted one bundle of cotton
By his lordship's order, this was granted repeatedly thereafter.

Item 1: Same 9th year, Year of the Monkey, 5th month
During the time when the young lord was still a child, everyone devoted themselves wholeheartedly to his upbringing and education, serving diligently, and despite the small number of attendants, also performed night watch duties, working hard in various ways, so as a reward, one bolt of raw silk was granted.

Item 1: Same year, 6th month - Having previously been appointed to the position of managing economies in the lord's personal affairs, since comprehensive economies were now newly ordered for all departments, regarding matters of the lord's personal affairs, he was to pay even more attention to the lord's clothing and furnishings for his quarters, charcoal, candles, and other garden-related expenses - to reduce all household expenses significantly. He was ordered to consult frankly with the treasury and other departments, report all matters to the senior retainers as appropriate, and arrange everything for the benefit of the lord.

Wife: Daughter of Hasegawa Goemon Tsunekiyo (Born 10th month, 8th day of Hōreki 11, year of the snake)

Item 1: 12th month of An'ei 4, year of the sheep - According to his parent's petition, he was granted his first formal audience with his lord

Item 1: Intercalary 12th month of the same year - According to his parent's petition, he was appointed to guard duty rotation and served castle guard duty in Tanaka Kahē's unit

Item 1: 3rd month of An'ei 5, year of the monkey - When commander Tanaka Kahē was transferred to different duties, he was reassigned to his successor Kasuga Izumi's unit

Item 1: 10th month of Tenmei 2, year of the tiger - For his diligent service in guard duties, maintaining perfect attendance for his entire unit for two consecutive years, he was commended as exemplary and this was officially proclaimed

Item 1: 6th month of Tenmei 5, year of the snake - He was granted 100 koku of hereditary stipend land without any changes

Item 1: 2nd month of Tenmei 7, year of the sheep - For his diligent service in guard duties, maintaining perfect attendance for ten years, he was commended as exemplary and this was officially proclaimed

Item 1: 9th month of Tenmei 8, year of the monkey - He was appointed to concurrently serve as Town Storehouse Magistrate

Item 1: 7th month of Genbun 1, year of the dragon　According to his father's petition, he was appointed to guard duty rotation and entered Kasuga Jinzaemon's unit, serving castle guard duty

Item 1: 2nd month of the same 3rd year, year of the horse　He was granted the hereditary stipend of 100 koku without any changes

Item 1: 10th month of the same 4th year, year of the sheep　He was appointed to the lord's escort guard

Item 1: Same month　His petition to change his name to Naemon was granted as requested

Item 1: 4th month of Kanpō 1, year of the rooster　He went up to Edo on rotation duty, and in the same 9th month was immediately appointed to escort the lord to Kyoto, returning with the escort in the same 11th month

Item 1: 12th month of the same year　It was announced that he had served diligently and without incident during the journey

Item 1: 4th month of the same 3rd year, year of the boar　He went up to Edo again to meet the returning lord, and returned with the escort in the intercalary 4th month of the same year

Item 1: 8th month of Enkyō 1, year of the rat　According to his petition, he was relieved of his duties and appointed to the outer retainer unit, entering Miyake Magobei's unit to serve castle guard duty

Item 1: 8th month of Kan'en 4, year of the sheep　When his commander Miyake Magobei was transferred to other duties, he was reassigned to the unit of his successor Yanase Kyūma

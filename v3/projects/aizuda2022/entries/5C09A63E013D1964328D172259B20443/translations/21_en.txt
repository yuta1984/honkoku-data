Without discrepancy, he was granted the position and appointed to the tozama (outer) unit attachment, entered Takahashi Yogorō's unit, and served castle guard duty.

Item 1: 8th month of the same year - He went out for torioi-gari (bird hunting)
　　Note: He went out once thereafter

Item 1: 10th month of the same year - When the hunting grounds were changed during the current torioi-gari, at the beginning, his footwork and coordination were well-aligned, operations proceeded without delay, and his diligent practice in joint military drills was recognized. His Lordship's favorable impression was expressed, and the entire unit received commendation.
　　Note: He continued to receive commendation thereafter

Item 1: Same month - It was announced that he had diligently and well performed his military drill duties

Item 1: 11th month of the same year - He was ordered to transfer units and joined Ibuka Shizuma's unit

Item 1: 12th month of Bunsei 1, year of the tiger - At the Sannomaru training ground, after three years of superior-level practice and advancing to tan-rank, his diligence was rewarded with sake

Item 1: 7th month of Bunsei 2, year of the rabbit - His request to change his name to Bansuke was granted

Item 1: 8th month of the same year - His request to adopt Nanikura, the second son of Nagai Magodayū, as son-in-law and heir was granted

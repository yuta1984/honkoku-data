• In the sixth month of Genroku 14 (1701), as requested by his father, he was appointed as a guard substitute and served castle guard duty in Sugano Gonbei's unit.
• In the eighth month of the same year, at the request of unit head Sugano Gonbei, he was relieved of his duty and transferred to Ueda Hachiroemon's unit as a replacement.
• In the fourth month of Hoei 1 (1704), he went up to Edo for guard duty service and returned in the tenth month of the same year.
• In the tenth month of Hoei 5 (1708), he went up to Edo for guard duty service, and in the fifth month of the following year of the Ox (Hoei 6), he returned accompanying the lord's descent from Edo.
　　Note: During the journey, he was appointed as temporary escort guard and served in that capacity.
• In the tenth month of Shotoku 1 (1711), he was appointed as escort guard.

• In the fourth month of Shotoku 2 (1712), due to the lord's visit to the shogun, he went up to Edo for guard duty service and returned in the tenth month of the same year.
• In the fifth month of Shotoku 3 (1713), he went up to Edo to meet the lord.
• In the tenth month of Shotoku 4 (1714), he went up to Edo for guard duty service, and in the fifth month of the following year of the Sheep (Kyoho 1), he returned accompanying the lord's descent from Edo.
• In the third month of Kyoho 3 (1718), due to a shortage of mounted warriors in the unit-attached guard duty, it was announced that escort guards of 250-koku stipend would be returned to outer retainer unit assignments. He entered Hinata Saburoemon's unit and served castle guard duty.
• In the tenth month of the same year, he went up to Edo for guard duty service.

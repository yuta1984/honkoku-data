[The lord] departed and arrived in Kyoto on the tenth day of the same month. Having successfully completed his official mission duties, he departed Kyoto on the twenty-fourth day of the same month and returned to Edo on the ninth day of the sixth month. For faithfully performing his escort duties without incident and returning, he was immediately awarded a meal.
Note: During the journey back and forth, twice in Kyoto, when the first audience with the Emperor was completed, he was awarded sake.

1. In the sixth month of the same year, regarding the Kyoto mission affairs
Although there were precedents for such matters, there were also extraordinary arrangements to be made, and as this would establish precedents for the future, he was ordered to investigate these matters apart from his regular duties. Together with Fukada Shinuemon and Kusakari Tsudayu, he conducted the investigation and began regular duties from the ninth day of the tenth month of the same year.

1. In the eighth month of the same year
While maintaining his current position, he was reassigned to confidential administrative duties. His manner of service was to remain as before, working under the supervision of the administrative group leader, and he was instructed that any matters concerning himself should be reported through the said group leader.

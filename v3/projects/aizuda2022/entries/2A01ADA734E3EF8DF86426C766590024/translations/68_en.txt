One　In the 3rd month of the same year, his son Kingo was appointed as his substitute guard as petitioned

One　In the 3rd month of the 12th year of the same era (year of the horse), his son Kingo had been suffering from illness since last year and currently appeared unable to attend duty, but since his own illness was improving, he petitioned to resume duty

One　In the 10th month of the same year, was appointed as gold mine magistrate

One　In the 1st month of the 13th year of the same era (year of the sheep), petitioned to disinherit his son Kingo due to illness

One　In the 4th month of the same year, petitioned to adopt Kanehei, the son of Kurasawa Denuemon, as son-in-law and heir

One　In the 5th month of the 1st year of Meiwa (year of the monkey), was appointed to concurrently serve as wax and lacquer wood supervisor

One　In the 10th month of the same year, was appointed as magistrate of directly controlled districts and granted an official stipend of 50 koku
　　　However, until a successor was appointed, he was ordered to continue serving both as gold mine magistrate and wax and lacquer wood supervisor as before

One　In the 12th month of the same year, his seat was appointed to rank after town magistrate and above district magistrate

One　In the 10th month of the 2nd year of the same era (year of the rooster), departed for Edo on official accounting business

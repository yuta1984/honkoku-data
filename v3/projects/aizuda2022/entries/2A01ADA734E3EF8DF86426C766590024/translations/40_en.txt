Sagōemon Tamekiyo 《marginal note: initially Yoichirō, also Hikōemon, after retirement Ban'an》
　　　Mother: daughter of the Momiyama clan
　　　Wife: adopted daughter of Maruyama Katsuemon Tanenaga 《marginal note: died of illness on the 29th day of the 8th month of Enkyō 1 (1744), year of the Rooster, Buddhist name Buon Myōkō Shinnyo, burial site same as above》

　　　　Actually Takamizawa Shiemon Uneme

One: In the 8th year of Kanbun (1668), year of the Monkey, 8th month, being young, granted 10 nin-buchi (provisions for 10 people) as onasa and appointed as kumi-gai-no-shi (unattached retainer)

One: In the 10th year of the same era (1670), year of the Dog, 9th month, appointed as page to Ishihiko-sama, granted 6 ryō and provisions for 10 people

One: In the 12th year of the same era (1672), year of the Rat, 8th month, appointed as page to Dotsū-sama, granted an increase of 2 ryō 2 bu in gold, totaling 8 ryō 2 bu and provisions for 10 people

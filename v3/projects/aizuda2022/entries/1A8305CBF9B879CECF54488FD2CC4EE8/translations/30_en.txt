First　Same 4th year (Year of the Snake), 11th month: When an envoy was sent from Akita Yamashiro-no-kami, he served guard duty at the guest residence.

First　Same 5th year (Year of the Horse), 3rd month: During the funeral procession of Lord Kinbun, he served as a street guard.

First　Same 6th year (Year of the Sheep), 8th month: He participated in combined unit training at Ōnohara.

First　Same year, 10th month: Regarding the current combined unit training, bird hunting had been suspended since the year of the Rabbit, and light training exercises were ordered in that manner. His formation alignment, marching, and maneuvering were all executed without delay, which was recognized as evidence of his consistent dedication to training, and he and his fellow unit members were all commended.

First　Same year, 9th month: He was appointed as a unit commander (monotō).

1. In the 2nd month of Kan'en 1 (Year of the Dragon, 1748), he was summoned before the lord and, due to his diligent service over several years, received gracious words of appreciation and was granted one formal haori with the family crest and ten pieces of silver.

1. In the 9th month of the same year, he went up to Edo for regular service duty and returned in the 3rd month of the following year (Year of the Snake).

1. In the 9th month of the 3rd year of the same era (Year of the Horse), he went up to Edo for regular service duty and returned in the 3rd month of the following year (Year of the Sheep).

1. In the 1st month of Hōreki 1 (Year of the Sheep, 1751), due to his diligent service over several years, he was granted an increase of one hundred koku of rice stipend in the lord's presence.

1. In the 9th month of the 2nd year of the same era (Year of the Monkey), he went up to Edo for regular service duty.

1. In the 2nd month of the 3rd year of the same era (Year of the Rooster), he was appointed as chamberlain (goyōnin) in the lord's presence and served in correspondence matters until replacement, then was immediately appointed for extended service duty and returned in the 9th month of the same year.

1. In the 10th month of the same year, Lady Kanso-in and Lady Rinko-in were suffering from smallpox.

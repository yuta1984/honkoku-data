1. In the 1st year of Genbun (Year of the Dragon), 3rd month, he went up to Edo for regular service duty and returned in the 10th month of the same year.

1. In the 2nd year of the same era (Year of the Snake), 9th month, he went up to Edo for regular service duty and returned in the 3rd month of the following year (Year of the Horse).

1. In the 4th month of the 4th year of the same era (Year of the Sheep), he was appointed as escort guard commander (go-tomaban-gashira).

1. In the 9th month of the same year, he went up to Edo for regular service duty and returned in the 3rd month of the following year (Year of the Monkey).

1. In the 3rd month of the 1st year of Kanpō (Year of the Rooster), he went up to Edo for regular service duty and returned in the 11th month of the same year accompanying the lord's return journey.
   However, during the campaign, he was appointed to accompany the lord on his journey to Kyoto and served in this capacity.

1. In the 11th month of the same year, he was appointed in the lord's presence as head page and horse division commander (go-koshō-gashira go-uma-kata-shikai).

1. In the 3rd month of the 2nd year of the same era (Year of the Dog), he went up to Edo for regular service duty and returned in the 9th month of the same year.

1. In the 3rd month of the 1st year of Enkyō (Year of the Rat), he went up to Edo for regular service duty and returned in the 9th month of the same year.

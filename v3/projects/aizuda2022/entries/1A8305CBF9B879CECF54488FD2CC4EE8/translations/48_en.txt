First, same 3rd year (Year of the Horse), 9th month: He was appointed as an accounting trainee and granted one ryō of gold annually for hakama expenses

First, Kanpō 1st year (Year of the Rooster), 11th month: He was appointed as an employee at the same office (accounting office) and granted two ryō of gold annually as compensation for his efforts

First, same 2nd year (Year of the Dog), 8th month: Due to his diligent and excellent service, he was granted a stipend of 18 koku and provisions for two people, and was summoned to serve as a minor official at the accounting office

First, Enkyō 1st year (Year of the Rat), 7th month: For the clean copying of the Ruijū Kyūki (Classified Old Records), he was appointed as a temporary employee at the administrative office

First, same year, 10th month: He was appointed as an employee at the administrative office, with his rank placed above minor officials of the accounting office but below minor officials of the investigation office

First, same 2nd year (Year of the Ox), 9th month: He was ordered to copy official documents, which he wrote at his home
　　Note: Due to his diligent service in this official duty, he was granted soup and sake

First, Kan'en 3rd year (Year of the Horse), 12th month: Due to his diligent service over several years, he was granted an official stipend of two koku

First, same 9th year (Year of the Monkey), 8th month: He was appointed to assist at the Sagami Province permanent district office and concurrently serve as head of accounts, with his seat ranking above the former gold mine magistrate.

First, same 10th year (Year of the Rooster), 1st month: He was appointed to concurrently serve as ship magistrate.

First, same year, 12th month: He devoted careful attention to the administration of the new domain territory, handled well the newly complex matters that arose, and worked diligently in both preliminary and main duties as well as concurrent positions. As a reward, he was granted one roll of silk in place of a formal robe, with added cotton, and five pieces of silver.

First, same 12th year (Year of the Boar), 2nd month: He was appointed as permanent district magistrate.

First, same year, 3rd month: He was granted a service allowance of fifty koku.

First, same month: He was appointed to concurrently serve as arms magistrate.

First, same 13th year (Year of the Rat), 12th month: Having worked diligently for several years, the simplified administrative system for district affairs that was discussed and decided upon last spring was implemented.

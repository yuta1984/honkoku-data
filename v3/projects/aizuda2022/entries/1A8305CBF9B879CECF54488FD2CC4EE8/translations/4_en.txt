1. In the 7th month of Enpō 3 (Year of the Rabbit), he was granted a stipend of 200 koku, summoned to service, and temporarily appointed to the yoriai-gumi (senior retainer group).

1. In the 3rd month of Enpō 5 (Year of the Snake), it was heard that the former residence of Tōyama Chōgorō had become the residence of Ibuka Shigozaemon due to aging, so it was granted to Shigozaemon's household as a reward for his devotion.

1. In the 12th month of the same 7th year (Year of the Sheep), he was appointed as messenger (tsukaiban) and granted a role stipend of 100 koku.

1. In the 4th month of the same 8th year (Year of the Monkey), he went up to Edo for regular service duty, completed one term of service, and returned.
　However, he was ordered to be accompanied by pages both going up and coming down.

1. In the 2nd month of Tenna 1 (Year of the Rooster), he was urgently summoned to Edo and served as a guide to the lord's seat during the celebratory banquet for the shogunal appointment, returning in the 4th month of the same year.

1. In the 7th month of the same year, he went up to Edo for emergency service duty, completed one term of service, and returned.

1. In the 4th month of Tenna 3 (Year of the Boar), he went up to Edo for regular service duty and completed one term of service before returning.
　However, during his service he acted as proxy representative to Nikkō, and also served as messenger to Kawasaki for the noon rest when Lord Inaba Tango-no-kami went up to the capital.

First, same 8th year (Year of the Dragon), 9th month: He diligently practiced spear techniques without neglect, improving his skills, and because this was commendable behavior, he was given sake as a reward, and it was announced that His Lordship's intention was for him to continue working even harder without laziness in training and technique.

First, same 9th year (Year of the Snake): The duties of assisting at the training ground while serving concurrently became too busy to manage, so upon his request, he was relieved of his training ground assistant duties.

First, same 12th year (Year of the Monkey), 3rd month: While Kuchiki Gakuunoshin was serving as temporary magistrate of the town storehouse, he was ordered to assist with the various training grounds.

First, Bunka 2nd year (Year of the Ox), 12th month: Since he diligently served as martial arts instructor and assisted with training ground supervision, he was given three pieces of silver as a reward.

First, same 4th year (Year of the Rabbit), 2nd month: As requested, he was relieved of his duties as martial arts instructor.

First, same year, 5th month, 8th day: He died of illness at age 61. His posthumous Buddhist name was Shintanin Setsubu Gikei Koji. Burial site: same as above.

Daughter: Died of illness on the 15th day of the 1st month of Hōreki 4th year (Year of the Dog). Posthumous Buddhist name was Myōsen Dōjo. Burial site: same as above.

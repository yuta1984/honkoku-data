Since it was a temporary residence, it was proclaimed that when an appropriate high-ranking residence became available, he would be granted a reassignment.

1. In the 5th month of the same year, it was proclaimed that he had properly maintained and improved the upper residence.

1. In the 9th month of the same year, he was placed under the custody of Akaba Tarōzaemon, and in the 10th month was transferred to Saigō Fusanosuke.

1. In the 11th month of Kansei 1 (Year of the Rooster), he was appointed as head of the guard captain group, and it was proclaimed that he was granted the right to carry a war fan due to his official position.

   However, he was instructed to understand that he was the head of the 9th guard group.

1. In the 1st month of Kansei 2 (Year of the Dog), regarding the recent military reform of the ashigaru foot soldiers under his supervision, when firearms and archery training was to begin, he gave good instructions and ordered the training, so as a reward he was granted two rolls of brocade.

1. In the 2nd month of Kansei 3 (Year of the Boar), he was granted the reassignment of Hayashiro Kichi's various residences.

1. In the 9th month of Kansei 5 (Year of the Ox), he participated in the bird hunting at Hongōhara.

Article 5: The personal guarantor mentioned in the previous article must be a resident of this city, maintaining an independent livelihood, and be a reliable adult of legal age.
Article 6: The treatment period for inpatient patients shall be three months per term. However, this may be extended or shortened depending on medical conditions and other circumstances.
Article 7: Inpatient patients may request visits from external physicians at their own expense with the approval of the hospital director.
Article 8: When inpatient patients do not comply with the various regulations concerning this hospital, discharge orders may be issued at any time.
　　　　　　　Chapter 2　　　Staff Organization
Article 9: The following staff shall be placed at this hospital:
　Hospital Director　　　　1 person
　Medical Staff　　　　　Several persons
　Pharmacist　　　　　　Several persons
　Clerk　　　　　　　　Several persons
　Nurse　　　　　　　　Several persons
Article 10: The hospital director shall oversee hospital affairs under the mayor's orders and supervise and direct the staff.
　When the hospital director is unavailable, the senior medical staff member shall act as deputy.
　Medical staff shall examine patients, prevent disease transmission, and handle disinfection duties under superior orders.
　Pharmacists shall engage in dispensing and drug evaluation under superior direction.
　Clerks shall engage in general affairs, accounting-related duties, and supervision under superior orders.
　In addition to what is specified in the preceding paragraphs, the hospital director may assign staff members to other duties as necessary.
Article 11: The hospital director may exclusively handle the following matters.
　However, this does not apply to particularly important matters or those of an exceptional nature.
　1. Dispatching staff for judicial appraisals and police cases
　2. Receiving notifications regarding staff members' parental memorial days, mourning leave, sick leave, military conscription examinations, army and navy roll calls and summons, etc.
　3. Ordering staff to remove mourning attire and report for duty
　4. Matters concerning acceptance of staff absences within two days
　5. Matters concerning purchase and repair of goods under 30 yen per item
　6. Matters concerning employment of laborers within budgetary limits
　7. Matters concerning delivery of corpses or performing autopsies through proper procedures
　　　　　　　Chapter 3　　　Departments
Article 12: The following three departments shall be established at this hospital:
　Medical Department
　Pharmacy Department
　Administrative Department
Article 13: The division of responsibilities for each department is as follows:
　Medical Department
　1. Matters concerning examination and treatment
　2. Matters concerning medical statistics and reporting materials
　3. Matters concerning organization of patient rooms and patient placement
　4. Matters concerning disinfection
　5. Matters concerning receipt, payment, and storage of items belonging to the medical department
　6. Other matters concerning medical affairs
　Pharmacy Department
　1. Matters concerning dispensing
　2. Matters concerning storage of prescription records and prescriptions
　3. Matters concerning organization and supervision of the pharmacy
　4. Matters concerning receipt, payment, and storage of medicines and other items belonging to the pharmacy

　　　　　Functions of the Chamber of Commerce and Industry
　1. To provide notifications regarding commercial and industrial matters
　2. To provide intermediation or mediation regarding commercial and industrial matters
　3. To provide arbitration or conciliation regarding commercial and industrial matters
　4. To provide certification or appraisal regarding commercial and industrial matters
　5. To conduct surveys and compile statistics regarding commercial and industrial matters
　6. To establish and manage facilities regarding commercial and industrial matters
　7. To implement other necessary facilities to improve commercial and industrial activities
　8. The Chamber of Commerce shall make recommendations to administrative authorities on matters concerning commerce and industry
　9. The Chamber of Commerce shall respond to consultations from administrative authorities
　10. Administrative authorities may order the Chamber of Commerce to conduct investigations on matters concerning commerce and industry
　11. The Chamber of Commerce may request the submission of necessary materials for conducting statistics and other surveys regarding commercial and industrial operators

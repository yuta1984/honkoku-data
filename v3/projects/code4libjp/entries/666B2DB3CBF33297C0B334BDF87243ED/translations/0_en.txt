Futagawa, Along the Tōkaidō
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Painted by Ichyō Kunichika

Grass along the road
　Violets
　When asked about
　　Graves
　　　Do not take
　　　　Poem by Ryōko
　　　　　　　　　Local specialty: kashiwa-mochi (oak leaf rice cakes)

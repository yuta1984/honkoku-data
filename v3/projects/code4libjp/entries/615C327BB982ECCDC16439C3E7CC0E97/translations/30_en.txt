《Quick Donning》
1. How to put on armor quickly: Hang the gauntlets on the armor's breastplate clasps as if wearing them, then don the armor as is, insert the gauntlets, and with one hand lift the gauntlet tops and insert the hands. It is also good to tie the upper belt, insert the sword, and then put on the gauntlets.

《Identification Signs》
1. To prevent friendly fire: Cut paper to two or three sun width and wrap it around sword sheaths, whether in two or three coils. Also good to attach cords to both shoulders or temples. One method is passwords, one is spear markings - anything conspicuous - one is insignia, one is helmet crests, one is two or three coils on guns and bows, same for spears. Most importantly, shooting guns from behind should be prohibited. Friendly fire is the work of those who arrive late in haste.

《Strengthening Formation》
1. What is meant by strengthening formation: each unit forms up properly in circles, avoiding disorder, with each type of equipment grouped together. Dismount from horses and position near the banners, placing horses far to the side and together in one location, avoiding scattered deployment - this is called good formation.

Having sought your intentions through correspondence,
since our audience was completed, we officials from various districts have been meeting and consulting on the duties assigned to us until the day before yesterday, the 7th.
Regarding the matters proclaimed concerning pawnshop licenses, lamp oil licenses, and cattle and horse hides for increasing profits,
we have been commanded regarding pawnshop licenses, so we prepare this copy to report to you.

Document to be delivered to the North and South group head village headmen
In rural areas, those who appear to respond to temporary financial needs have been conducting pawn business, and when stolen goods become evident during investigations of captured thieves and such activities are terminated, there are cases of confusion occurring. Therefore,
after investigation, as a trial measure, both urban and rural areas are now ordered to establish pawnshop licenses as follows,
so each should understand the method of notification:

1. Regarding urban areas: approximately sixty-eight establishments that have been conducting pawn business up to now shall be assigned pawnshop licenses.

2. Regarding rural areas: those who have been conducting pawnshop business from before and are listed in the district registers,
   as well as those who have newly applied to conduct pawnshop business, shall each be assigned pawnshop licenses, so this matter should be announced. Accordingly, signboards will be distributed to each establishment,
   so signboards should be prepared uniformly and submitted.

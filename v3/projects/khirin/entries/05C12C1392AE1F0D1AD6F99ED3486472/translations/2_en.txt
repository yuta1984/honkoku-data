When signboards are withdrawn and new applications to conduct pawnshop business are submitted, after investigation and acknowledgment of the application, signboards shall be distributed each time.

1. The sale or transfer of said pawnshop licenses shall not be permitted. As a trial measure, myōga silver of twenty me per year shall be collected annually from each pawnshop.

2. In each district, judges shall be appointed from among the pawnshops to ensure that no illegal pawning is conducted outside of those with proper signboards. Suitable persons shall be selected and their recommendations submitted.

3. Regarding the areas within the city limits of Sako, Shōzōmoto, Shimada, Yazo, Jōge Suketo, Tomida, Kitahama, Minamihama, and Shimohachiman village vicinity, no new applications shall be accepted beyond the pawnshops that have operated there traditionally.

As stated above, each should understand the method of implementation. Furthermore, as has been established repeatedly since previous years for investigation purposes, to avoid any misunderstanding, this shall be announced on a single sheet beginning with the pawnshops as stated above.

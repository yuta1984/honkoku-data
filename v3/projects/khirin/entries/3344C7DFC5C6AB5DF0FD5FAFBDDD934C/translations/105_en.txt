It is heard that there has been little rainfall since late last month, causing hardship. Regarding the matter of ordering rain-prayer rituals, as communicated on the 22nd day, prayers for rain are now to be offered to Hayagumo Sado at the Ryūō Shrine within the castle grounds for three days.

Accordingly, since such benevolent grace being bestowed is extremely fortunate and worthy of gratitude, this should be urgently communicated without omission to all officials and peasants of your district villages and bays. The above.

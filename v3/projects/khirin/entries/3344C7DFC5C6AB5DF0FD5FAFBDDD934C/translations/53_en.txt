Regarding the dancing from the coming 14th to 16th for three nights, this shall be announced as usual. However, concerning daytime activities, what is called "daytime improvisation" where common people participate, as well as disguising oneself as children while going about the streets, these have been customary practices. But in recent years, many people have taken to changing their appearance and carrying musical instruments while going about in broad daylight. Particularly, women go about in broad daylight either naked or wearing women's undergarments in strange attire. Even people of respectable social standing have been heard to engage in such behavior, which is especially harmful to public morals and quite problematic. Therefore, henceforth, regarding daytime activities, except for the aforementioned daytime improvisation and children's activities, it is strictly forbidden for both men and women to change their appearance and go about carrying musical instruments.

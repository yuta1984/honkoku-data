○【Red ink】Fifty-one
Regarding the external kanno (agricultural promotion) public works within the southern and northern counties, having the villages undertake and establish the public works as contracted construction has proven difficult to arrange. Upon investigation, it was found that some cases involve establishing public works through contracts based on consultations among village associations. It is heard that there are cases where the work performed by such contractors is unsatisfactory. This being a problematic matter, if such situations occur in the future, punishment will certainly be imposed, so this should be understood accordingly. Furthermore, regarding petitions for kanno public works and the recording of construction methods, the following is hereby ordered:

1. When there is damage to internal and external public works sites, even if it is a minor matter, if repairs cannot be managed without requesting public works assistance from the nearest localities, one should report to the responsible group head (yoto) and village headman (shoya) for inspection. When making a formal petition, the petition document should bear the endorsement of the group head and village headman, and village officials along with one or two head farmers should accompany and submit it.

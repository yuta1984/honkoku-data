○【In red ink】Copy of the directive handed down on the 17th day of the 11th month of the Year of the Tiger, when Matsue Shimasaburō and Gotō Shōsuke were summoned

【57】
The members of the domain retainer class have been facing continued high prices of various goods in recent years, and due to last year's crop failure, the common people have naturally fallen into hardship. Even in the market towns and rural villages, economic conditions have worsened year by year, causing them to fall into ever deeper poverty, as we have heard. Under such circumstances, the recent great earthquake and tsunami caused damage starting with residences, resulting in many people whose homes were swept away or collapsed. Moreover, fires broke out in various places, and areas such as Uchimachi and Komatsushima were almost entirely burned down, creating an unprecedented situation in history. Given these circumstances, economic conditions will worsen further, and the hardships of both the domain retainers and the common people will increase even more. Therefore, through special deliberation, it has been ordered that all matters related to various borrowed silver and agricultural promotion be granted a reprieve from payment for this winter season as follows. Furthermore, since debts have accumulated in all cases, it appears that unless some appropriate measures are taken, it will be difficult to continue.

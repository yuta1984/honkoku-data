1. Regarding merchants in market towns and their tin, copper, iron, old pots and other
old metals as well as similar utensils being shipped out to other domains,
this was prohibited as announced in a recent edict. Concerning this, tin utensils
of any kind should not be dispersed by those who possess them,
but should be used carefully and not sold, which should be thoroughly investigated and arranged.
Should there be anyone who cannot avoid selling them, they should
report that matter. Written acknowledgments have been compiled and submitted as stated above, but
since there are no follow-up records, this is recorded here.

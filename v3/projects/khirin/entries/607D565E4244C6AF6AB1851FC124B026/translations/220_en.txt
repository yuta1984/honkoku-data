　　Memorandum of Report with Accompanying Letter
Regarding the exchange of old gold and silver coins with genuine characters, two-bu denomination, old two-shu silver coins, as well as
Bunsei-era gold and silver coins with cursive characters, two-bu silver and one-shu silver coins,
concerning this matter
there was an official notice from the Shogunate, so with this understanding, regardless of group villages or coastal areas,
we were ordered to notify everyone, collect group acknowledgment letters and submit them.
We humbly received this order and immediately issued notifications. As shown in the separate document, acknowledgment
letters stating humble acceptance were submitted, so we have collected them and respectfully present them. The above
　12th month, Year of the Rat　　　　　　　Village Head and Headman of Hayabuchi Village

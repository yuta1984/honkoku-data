Memorandum
This time, regarding the matter of exchanging old gold and silver coins, two-shu silver coins, one-shu gold coins, etc. from the government, 
as stated in the separate document, we have been given written instructions. Accordingly, we have been ordered to announce this throughout the domain.
Please understand this intent and announce it throughout the district. The above.
　　　Eleventh month, eleventh day　　　　Kashima Izumo
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Hachisuka Shinano
　　　　　To the District Governors

　　　To the Chief Inspector
Regarding the matter of exchange offices for old gold and silver coins, two-shu silver coins, one-shu gold coins, etc., which were to remain open until the tenth month of the current year of the Sheep,
we announced in the previous year of the Horse, but as there still remain coins to be exchanged,
the exchange offices shall continue to be maintained as before until the tenth month of the coming year of the Monkey.
From remote provinces to the farthest regions, in government lands through magistrates and in private domains through lords and local governors,
thorough investigation shall be conducted, and even the smallest amounts should be brought to the nearest exchange office to be completely exchanged by the tenth month of the coming year of the Monkey.
If there are still those who hoard and do not exchange
their coins, after investigation, strict orders shall be given.
The above intent should be announced.
　　　　Ninth month

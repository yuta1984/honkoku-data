Accordingly, we have been ordered to ensure that this information is thoroughly communicated using respective official documents without omission. We humbly and promptly circulated this information, and [the villagers] have expressed their gratitude and reverence.
Therefore, we hereby submit this receipt acknowledgment from us. The above.

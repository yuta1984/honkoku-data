**Illustrated Record of the Earthquake Disaster: Sensoji Temple**

Illustrated by Soshu (seal)

Although surrounded on all sides by raging flames, miraculously, the Kannon (the deity of Sensoji Temple) was left unharmed. Deeply revering this divine blessing, the temple has become even more bustling since the disaster, with hundreds of thousands of worshippers visiting every day, and their numbers continuing to grow with each passing day.

【Text within the illustration】
- We provide care and assistance for the injured and sick
- We will write letters on your behalf to send to your home regions
- Please do not hesitate to come forward and ask

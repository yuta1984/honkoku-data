Guide to the Great Earthquake and Fire Damage Sites in Edo on October 2nd, 2nd Year of Ansei

During this great earthquake, large fires broke out in various locations. ○In Yoshiwara, Fushimi-chō, Edo-chō, Ageya-chō, Minami-chō, and Kyō-chō - all houses and storehouses within the pleasure quarter burned completely without exception. However, the government notice board outside Ōmon remained, while from the opposite side, Nihon-zutsumi, Amigasa Chaya Tamachi, San'ya, and up to Hori Teppō were burned. ○Similarly, one block north of Dote-shita and Imado Bridge burned. Temples, town districts, and eta quarters up to Masaki Inari were all severely damaged. ○Both sides of Umamichi-dōri burned. Fire started from the east of Henshōin temple, burning Saru temple, Tori temple and other temples' back areas, Iroha tenements, up to Zuishin gate - no storehouses remained. △Asakusa Main Hall was safe, but various shrines were severely damaged, with several temple areas collapsed. ○In Saruwaka-chō, all three theaters - Kawarasaki, Ichimura, and Nakamura - along with their backstage areas and Shindō street burned completely. North of Morita Kanya's residence remained. The Yabu-no-uchi area, east side, Iōin temple, and shops south of this area burned. South Umamichi also burned. ▲The Eastern mountain lodgings remained, Hanakawado-chō burned, but the riverbank remained. △Higashi Honganji was safe, but the east gate collapsed and nine buildings within the temple grounds were destroyed. ▲Seigan temple, Nichirin temple, Kōryū temple and other gate-front districts suffered major collapse. Keiion temple's main hall collapsed in this area, with severe damage in all four directions. ○Similarly, Kikuya Bridge and half of Shinhoribata burned.

○Both sides of Komagata-chō burned - the west side stopped at the river's edge, while the east side burned up to the Ōkawa River. Suwa Myōjin in Suwa-chō burned. Kurofune-chō also burned, but Kaya temple remained and the fire stopped there. ▲From Miyoshi-chō to Omaya-gashi burned, Kuramae remained but was severely damaged. ▲Kaya-chō in the same area was severely damaged. ▲From Ryōgoku Yokoyama-chō street to Bakurō-chō street suffered major damage but no fires. ○Ikenohata Kaya-chō first and second districts burned, Izumo-sama suffered minor fire damage, Bingo-sama was severely damaged. ○Nezu Shichiken-chō burned, with many small estates and townhouses in the four directions collapsing. The Shitaya section is recorded below. ○At Mukōjima Bokuboji temple, south of Umewaka burial mound, the ground split open and spewed mud. Shirahige Myōjin, Chōmeiji, Ushigozen, and Mimmawari Inari in this area were severely damaged. ○Koume Kawara-chō and Ogura-an burned, with major collapse in all four directions. ○At Honjo Itsutsumedōshi ferry landing, half of Gofune-chō burned, with many collapsed houses in the four directions. ○Ishihara-chō burned, with small estates and townhouses in this area severely damaged.

○Nakanogō Omotechō also burned. ○South of there, from Midori-chō to Hana-chō burned. ▲Estates and townhouses near the north-south drainage canal suffered major collapse. ▲Aioi-chō street was severely damaged, northern estates were severely damaged, and to the east, estates and village houses around Kameido Tenjin, Yanagishima, and Gohyaku Rakan suffered many collapses. ▲Ekōin's corridors collapsed, and towns and estates in the northeast direction were severely damaged. Overall, it's impossible to record all the collapsed houses in the Honjo area. ▲Hitotsumeashi bridge's stone wall collapsed, blocking traffic. Benten, Saikōji, Hatsune Inari, and Hachiman pilgrimage site in this vicinity had many collapsed houses. ○Ofunakura-mae-chō burned, but the naval estate was safe. ○Fukagawa Rokken-bori burned, from next to Aie River to Yamyōgawa-chō, rice storehouses, and Sarukō Bridge. ○Morishita-chō and Ogasawara suffered minor fires, from Tokiwa-chō 2nd district, Inoue lower estate, Ōta lower estate, to Takahashi burned. ▲Kaigan Daiku-chō, Reiun-in main hall and front gate collapsed, Honshōji temple grounds severely damaged, Reigan-ji front collapsed, Jōshinji front gate collapsed, turtle-eye stones broke, three temples in the grounds collapsed, others severely damaged. Unkōin and Hōzenji temples in this area, along with Tachibana, were severely damaged. Both families' lower estates and lumber yards were severely damaged. Shōgakuji Bridge, temple district street, Mannen-chō, and Sankaku area suffered major collapse. ▲Saga-chō area severely damaged.

○At Aikawa-chō checkpoint, three houses remained, from there the fire spread south. Kumai-chō, Ōshima-chō, Nakashima-chō, Kitajima-chō, Hamaguri-chō, Kurofune-chō, and Kuroe-chō all burned completely. Both sides of Nakamachi street burned. The stone yard estates to the south remained. ○Ishihara-chō substitute land 2nd district burned, stopping at Hachiman front gate in this area. The stone torii there collapsed, shrine grounds severely damaged, townhouses and estates in all four directions suffered major collapse. Sanjūsangen-dō split at the ridge, Susaki Benten severely damaged, major damage extending east to former Hachiman area. ▲West ○Reigan Island, Ōkawabata-chō, Shio-chō, to Minami-shinkawa-chō burned, other areas including the district in front of Matsudaira Echizen-sama suffered major collapse. Minato-chō, Kawaguchi-chō and others severely damaged. To the north, Hakozaki estates and townhouses collapsed. ○Teppōzu Jikken-chō and Akashi-chō burned, Matsudaira Awaji-sama burned, other districts severely damaged. ▲South Hatchōbori, Ashū-sama estate severely damaged. ▲Tsukuda Island fishermen's district burned, same for the south. ▲Tsukiji Honganji was safe, but over fifty temples within the grounds suffered major collapse with many injured. Estates in the four directions, Iida-chō, Odawara-chō and others all severely damaged. Shiodome Bridge, Wakisaka-sama, Sendai-sama severely damaged, many other nearby collapsed houses. ○Southwest of there, districts along Shibaguchi street severely damaged, Shibai-chō front and back burned.

Similarly, Udagawa-chō, Mishima-chō, Shinmei-mae area severely damaged beyond description. ▲Shinmei main shrine was safe, gate-front district severely damaged. ▲Zōjōji was safe, temple interior severely damaged. Shōgen Bridge, Kanasugi Bridge area severely damaged. Satsuma-sama, Arima-sama, Naitō-sama, Oda-sama area severely damaged. Akabane, Suitengū were safe. Mita, Kasuga area severely damaged. Fuda-no-tsuji had many major collapses in three directions. ▲Azabu, Meguro area severely damaged. ○Kane-bō-chō burned, major collapse in four directions. ○Kyōbashi area suffered major collapse in four directions. North of there, Minami-kaji-chō, Suzuki-chō, Inari-chō, Kanō estate, Sumi-chō, Gusoku-chō, Yanagi-chō, Kita-kon'ya-chō, Shirauoyanshiki, Sumi-chō, and east to Zaimoku-chō 8th district burned. ○North of there, Nihonbashi street to Sujikai gate, front side suffered minor damage, back side severely damaged. ▲Muro-chō, Jikken-dana, to Imagawa Bridge severely damaged. ▲Kanda severely damaged, detailed earlier.

○In Shitaya direction, both east and west of Ueno Hirokōji severely damaged, but the mountain temple main quarters, inner sacred halls, Taihei and others suffered damage. Gate-front Roku Amida south, Ueno-chō 2nd district, Tokudaiji, Ichijōin burned. North of there, Kitadaimon-chō, Kuromon-chō, Shitaya Dōbō-machi, Ueno retainer estates, Inoue Chikugo-sama collapsed. Ishikawa-sama and Kuroda-sama's front tenements burned and stopped there. ○Similarly, Chōja-chō 1st and 2nd districts, Shitaya-chō 2nd district, substitute land Chōja-chō 2nd district remaining land burned. ○From Shitaya Kurumazaka-chō, Okachi-chō burned, stopping at Takano family. Similarly, Mukō-yokochō, imperial physician Tsuji-moto and other small estates half-district burned, southwest of there.

○Outside Hitotsubashi gate, Matsudaira Buzen-sama, Hongō Tango burned, severe damage in four directions. ▲Iida-chō, Ban-chō several locations, countless estate severe damage. Same direction Suruga-dai generally moderate damage without fire. ▲From Mikawa-chō to below Suruga-dai severely damaged, Suruga-dai slightly damaged. Outside Shōhei Bridge, Kanda Myōjin, Tsuma-koi Inari, Yushima Tenjin area severely damaged. Hongō street Kaga-sama long houses damaged. From Komagome Oiwake, Asuka mountain road, Kirigaya mountain base, Somei area severely damaged. ▲West of there, Goji-in, waterworks severely damaged. Denzū-in, nearby areas severely damaged. ▲Zōshigaya, Nedzu mountain area not particularly damaged. ○Northeast Negishi, Kanasugi, Minowa area severely damaged. ○North Nakamura-chō, Kozukahara-chō, Senjū burned, many collapsed houses in nearby villages. ○Within imperial castle, opposite Tatsu-no-kuchi, Sakai-sama, Ōoka-sama severely damaged. Within Wada-kura gate, Higo-sama, Matsudaira Shimōsa-sama, Matsudaira Iga-sama, Matsudaira Genba-sama severely damaged. Same location Tatsu-no-kuchi Abe-sama east side collapsed. From Yashiro-gashi to Baba-saki gate collapsed. ▲Daimyō-kōji and other various locations severely damaged, with related fires, briefly recorded.

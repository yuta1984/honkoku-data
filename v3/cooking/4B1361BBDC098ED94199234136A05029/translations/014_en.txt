　　　　　　　Listed below　　　　　　　　
Items to prepare in May　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　


Method for storing ginger　　Dig deep into the earth

in front of the stove and spread coarse rice bran


on the bottom, place ginger on top

and cover with earth. This stores well for a long time


Method for storing fresh bamboo shoots　　Put fresh bamboo shoots in a tub with a lid

and bury in a fast-flowing part of a river


Place stones as weights on top. They will

keep fresh indefinitely. The method for storing eggplant


is the same as above

Method for salt-pickling ginger without mold　　Chop ginger to an appropriate


size and remove moisture well

then pickle in plum vinegar. When pickling, wrap mustard seeds


in a silk cloth and place at the bottom.

Done this way, it will keep for a year

Now, when the hot cooking liquid returns to temperature, add to the daikon either finely cut carrot, finely chopped yuzu citrus, wood ear mushrooms, dropwort cut into one-inch pieces, persimmon julienne, high-quality flat wheat gluten, seasoned with soy sauce, along with julienned or small rectangular cuts, julienned ginger, and diced chestnuts - add these various ingredients and simmer. Add soy sauce, vinegar, and roasted salt to taste, cover the pot with a lid, and once it comes to a boil, remove the lid, take the pot off the heat, serve in bowls and add plenty of vinegar broth before serving. For fish, whether yellowtail, yellowtail roe, skipjack tuna, or eel - any will do - cut the raw fish into small pieces, add when the daikon is finished cooking, simmer once and serve.

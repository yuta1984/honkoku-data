(7) Method for Daikon Oyashiro-ni (Great Shrine Simmering) - Specialty of Unshu Province
First, this is an October dish. Use daikon leaves and stems, or whole daikon still long, taking only those that look like they're covered with soil, and cook with the leaves still attached. Then lay about two sheets of flat kelp on the bottom of a large pot, and you may add as much daikon as seems appropriate. Then add a handful of white soybeans, a little sake, soy sauce and water, making plenty of broth to simmer. Then serve on flat dishes.

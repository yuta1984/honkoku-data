(38) Method for Quick-Pickled Daikon Aromatic Vegetables

First, cut daikon to a length of three to four sun, cut lengthwise into three pieces, put in a tub,
pour hot water over from above, take out while hot, put in another tub,
sprinkle plenty of salt, add a little sake, and place pressing stones lightly
on top. In about half an hour it becomes excellent aromatic pickles. Also putting it into
rice bran miso is good. When pickling daikon with leaves attached, put the leaves upward
so the hot water doesn't reach them. The pickling method is the same.

(39) Method for Quick Miso-Pickled Daikon

First, quarter lightly-pickled daikon and cut to about two sun in length, soak briefly in
warmed sake, take out when cooled, and pickle in miso.
However, if you apply pressing stones, it will be pickled in about one hour. Also, if you
pickle it this way for one day and one night, it has exceptional flavor. However, taking it out and
washing it is not good. It's better to wipe with paper before cutting.

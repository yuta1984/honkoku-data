For chestnuts, select quite large ones and peel off the outer skin. If you roast them quickly over fire, the inner skin will peel off easily. Then either split them in half or chop into dice, and when the rice is half-cooked, place them on top and continue cooking. If overcooked, the chestnuts will crumble completely and look unsightly.

　　　Turnip Rice
The method is the same as radish rice, but since turnips are quite sweet, putting in pieces that are too large is not good. One should devise accordingly.

　　　Eggplant Rice
Take fresh good eggplants and roast them thoroughly over charcoal fire, then put in water to remove the skin, tear finely and serve in a bowl with rice piled on top. For the broth, use light soy sauce, and for seasonings use ground pepper and other ingredients according to taste.

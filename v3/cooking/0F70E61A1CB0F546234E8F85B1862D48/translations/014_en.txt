　　　Red Shrimp (Akaebi) Rice
Boil shiba shrimp in light salt water, completely remove the shells,
remove the heads, and when the rice is mostly cooked, add them in.
Let it cook thoroughly into the rice, then remove from heat and set aside.
Mix well and serve in bowls. Use various seasonings. The soup
should be clear broth.
　　　Sardine Rice
Before removing from heat, remove the sardine heads and

wash them well, then insert them upside down into the rice. After that, remove
from heat. When it has steamed well, pull up the tails of the
inserted sardines, and all the bones will
come out attached. Mix the rice well from top to bottom and
serve. Prepare clear soup with various seasonings.

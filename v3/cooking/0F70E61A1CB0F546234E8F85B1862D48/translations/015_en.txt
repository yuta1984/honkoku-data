　　　Hishiko Rice
White hishiko is considered best. Remove the bones with a
bamboo spatula, wash well, and prepare in the same manner as
sardine rice. The soup is the same.
　　　Pheasant Rice
Simmer fine Tosa katsuobushi (bonito flakes) to make broth and
use it to cook the rice. Use only the pheasant meat,

blanch it and place it on top of the rice when serving. The soup
should be clear broth with various seasonings.
　　　Chicken Rice
Use only kashiwa (yellow chicken). The preparation method is the
same as pheasant rice.
　　　Duck Rice
Use only the duck meat, cut it finely and blanch it,
then place it on top of the rice. The soup should be clear broth with
various seasonings.

Genji Porridge
　　　　Cook white porridge as usual
　　　　Add the same seasonings as soba base
　　　　and pour over the top
　　　　　The steaming of the porridge is crucial

Grilled Rice
　　　Form into small rice balls and grill
　　　until golden brown, put about two in a tea bowl,
　　　add one ball of grilled miso, top with
　　　good nanohana (rapeseed flowers), cover the bowl and serve

Hail Soup
　　　Take hail rice cakes or clear water rice,
　　　roast well and cook in hot water
　　　Season with roasted salt and serve

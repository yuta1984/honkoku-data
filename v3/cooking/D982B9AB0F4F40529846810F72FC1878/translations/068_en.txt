Bowl dish
　　Take the parboiled fish and cut into large pieces,
　　cook thoroughly, simmer well in mirin sake only
　　and sprinkle generously with bonito flakes
　　　　However, if lacking salt after cooking, add roasted salt

　Also
　　For this, only extract salt without parboiling,
　　wash well, remove moisture, make into sashimi and pour
　　ginger vinegar over it
　　　　However, depending on accompaniments, it's also good as fresh namasu

Small sea bream
　　Same item but since it's small sea bream, the method changes.
　　Salt extraction is the same as above. However, compared to regular salt sea bream,
　　this is generally saltier, so one should be aware of this.

　Soup
　　When salt has been extracted, grill well until charred,
　　rub off the skin, and together with bones, finely
　　mince and add to tofu shell soup

Grilled sea bream
　　This is a specialty from spring March in the Etsu□□□□
　　region. It is grilled directly with seawater at the beach and served,
　　this is truly called "beach grilling." It has extremely refined taste.
　　It's excellent used as is.

Lightly salted cod
　　Soak in water to extract salt. However, if salt is extracted too much,
　　the flavor becomes poor; it's best when a little
　　saltiness remains.

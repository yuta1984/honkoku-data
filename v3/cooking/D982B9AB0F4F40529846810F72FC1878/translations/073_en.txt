Salted mackerel
　Those with mild salt are extremely delicious
　　　　Also called sashi-saba

　Overnight sushi
　　Remove some salt, wipe off moisture well and fillet the fish
　　Remove bones, soak in vinegar, squeeze appropriately
　　Remove moisture with cloth, season rice properly with grilled salt, press flat in sushi box
　　Cut into appropriate squares. It's also good to put in without cutting
　　Accompaniments: water pepper, fresh ginger

　Hand-salt plate
　　Wash well, remove bones, slice thin from the end
　　Pour water pepper vinegar over. This is the method for sashi-saba but the salt balance is
　　good, appears like fresh fish with light flavor

　Tea bowl
　　Wash well, remove flesh, cut into medium pieces, parboil
　　Change the water to light salt seasoning
　　Add a little vinegar when removing
　　　　　However, be sure to watch that the broth doesn't become cloudy

　Medium plate
　　Wash well, fillet into three pieces, remove top and bottom, grill only the middle
　　flesh. When grilled white with salt rising to surface
　　Place on medium plate, strain daikon juice with
　　soup stock and pour over the top before serving

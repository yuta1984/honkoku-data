Same with fried shell
　　This is a single dish that is common in Kyoto but
　　rarely found in Edo and surrounding provinces, with a light
　　flavor. It's good to simmer with leeks, daikon and such

Same with pressed
　　This is also a single dish, pressed like domestic Mishima seaweed.
　　When hot water is poured and rubbed, it becomes hair-like.
　　Good as accompaniment to sashimi, or as a dish by itself with seasoned vinegar

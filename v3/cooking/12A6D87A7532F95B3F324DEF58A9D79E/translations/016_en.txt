22. Finishing daikon furofuki (simmered daikon)
Take winter daikon straight from the field as freshly dug, sprinkle a little
sake on it, immediately wrap it in paper and push it into a straw fire, and it will cook quickly. Then take it out, wash off the earthiness with water, pour soy sauce over it and serve. This method is
a specialty of a place called Iinohara in Harima Province.

23. How to make daikon vinegar dish
Peel the outer skin of daikon, grate it with the coarse
side of a wasabi grater, squeeze out the water very well, and whether for fish or

vegetarian dishes, chop finely, both coarse and
fine, season it, and especially shiitake mushrooms and wood ear mushrooms
should be similarly well-seasoned, for raw ingredients add vinegar well, mix with the aforementioned grated daikon, wrap in clean cloth, adding a little salt, sake, and vinegar, press with a stone, and when serving, cut with a thin-bladed knife. Alternatively, it is good to add fried
wheat gluten for flavoring. For raw fish, use vinegar.

【Recipe 47】Roasted and Steamed Pickled Daikon Method
At any time, take overnight pickled daikon, wash it well with water, wrap it in paper, and bury it in the ashes of a burning fire to roast it.
This paper should be moistened with water before placing it in. When the paper burns, take it out, wash with warm water, and serve with red pepper and soy sauce. For drinking accompaniment, it is also good to serve with red pepper miso.

Contemporary lightly pickled daikon prepared in the above manner is generally used in tea cuisine. This is also excellent.

【Recipe 48】Daikon Intimate Dish Method
This method involves taking winter-pickled salty pickles, removing the outer skin, soaking in sake overnight, taking it out the next day, washing once with water, cutting to about 1.5 sun in length, then slicing thinly crosswise and cutting into julienne strips like white hair.

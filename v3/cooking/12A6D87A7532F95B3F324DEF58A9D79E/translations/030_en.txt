【Recipe 50】Thin Dried Daikon Dressed Salad Method
Cut dried daikon as is into thin rounds, put in a nested box or bowl, pour boiling water over it, wait a while, then squeeze well. If it's too hard, briefly blanch in hot water. Then mix with sansho pepper miso and sesame. However, if using fish ingredients, add kazunoko (herring roe) and black sesame for a black-dressed version, which is also excellent. Black dressing uses pot soot.

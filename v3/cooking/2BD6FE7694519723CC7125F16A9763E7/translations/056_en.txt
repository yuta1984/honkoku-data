　　○Sweet Eggplant Pickle
One: For one hundred medium eggplants　　One: Black rice - six sho
One: Koji - six sho
Cook the above rice like regular sweet rice wine, combine with koji, press into a tub and leave overnight. Do not add any water. At noon the next day, add six sho of salt and about five monme of copper coin scraps, mix with the salt, and pickle so the eggplants don't rub against each other.

　　○Method for Pickling Young Melons
One: Remove the seeds from young melons and wash well so there is no sweetness. For one hundred melons, apply four to five sho of salt inside and outside the melons, dry in the sun, and when water comes out of the melons, bring them inside and arrange them. Cool well, mix salt with good lees to a palatable saltiness level, pickle in a tub so the melons don't rub against each other, cover well, coat the top with earth, and seal so wind cannot enter. The flavor will develop in twenty-four to twenty-five days.

　　○Method for Pickling Whole Round Melons
One: Apply salt to whole melons and pickle as described above.

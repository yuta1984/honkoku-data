One: Cut grilled whale into short pieces and soak in water for about three days, washing well and cutting, then changing the water. After three to four days it becomes tender. At that time, slice thinly and simmer in a pot with rice bran miso. When well cooked, it can be used for sashimi or soup. Normally, prepare using the above method, simmering in rice bran miso. If you wash the whale with water and dry it briefly in the sun, it can be stored for a long time without problems. When needed, soften in lukewarm water. It's useful even for urgent needs.
　　　　○Fluffy Eggs
One: Beat eggs and add water soy sauce or dashi soy sauce, preparing as usual. Adding sake is not good as it makes the eggs stick strongly to the pot. When it's about well done, make holes here and there in the eggs in the pot, pour in seasoned sake diluted with water, and simmer briefly.

【Left Page】
　　　　○Sliced Eggs
One: Beat eggs well and mix. For five eggs, add about one teaspoon of kudzu powder and cook like fu-yaki (gluten cake). Keep the fire very low, add a little egg at a time, and cook as thinly as possible. Then cut finely and mix with seasoned sake for sashimi, simmered dishes, or namasu.
　　　　○Rolled Eggs
One: Make fu-yaki with eggs as before, spread minced fish in the center, roll up tightly, tie with dried gourd strips, and simmer in soy sauce dashi. Afterward, slice as desired.

　　　○Method for Preserving Grapes
One: Pick grapes when they are not too ripe, arranging them bunch by bunch.
Then dig in dry ground, place bamboo screens about 2 sun (6cm)
from the bottom at mid-level, arrange them on top so they don't rub against each other,
place boards on top, and put about
5 sun (15cm) of soil on top. Of course, arrange the boards so they don't rub
against the grapes either.

　　　○Method for Preserving Pears
One: Dig up well-flourishing mondo grass with its roots
and wrap the pears in this grass, then dig in dry

　　　○Same Secret Method
One: When picking pears from the tree, those that have been knocked
to the ground are no good. If you place them among fresh red azuki bean grains
so they don't rub against each other, they will keep indefinitely.
About seven or eight out of ten will remain as if
freshly picked.

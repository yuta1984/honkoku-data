　　　○Method for Early-bearing Eggplants
One: Do not pull up the eggplant plants that bore fruit in the current autumn, but leave them
as they are, wrap them well with straw mats, and also make a straw
roof on top, covering well against snow and frost.
In spring they will sprout and grow leaves,
producing fruit a full month earlier than
regular eggplant season, and with excellent flavor.

　　　○Method for Obtaining White Water
One: Nowadays people use first-run white water to obtain white water, which is
a great mistake. The first run has strong oil content and
is quite toxic. Use second or third-run water instead,

　△Miscellaneous Items
　　　○Method for Yuzu Mochi
One: Dōmyōji dried rice, ground as finely as possible
One: Miso {{marginal note: ground | two colors in equal parts}} One: A little white sugar added
Mix these three ingredients together with fresh soy sauce to the right consistency.
The amount of sugar to add depends on preference.
The preparation of yuzu is the same as making yuzu miso: cut off the top,
remove the flesh, wash the inside well with lukewarm water and wipe clean.
Fill the yuzu seven-tenths full with the mixture, replace the lid,
and steam. Steam until the heat penetrates well to the center.

　　○Method for Pickled Polygonum Spikes
One: In August, during the good season, coat a cutting tub with a little salt, arrange polygonum spikes in order, sprinkle salt little by little and place weights. After about one hour has passed, since we need rice, mix about five go of salt per sho until it becomes just covered with liquid.

　　○Method for Pickled Black-eyed Peas (One Method)
One: Take black-eyed peas during the good season, place them in a tub in rows, sprinkle salt on top until they are not visible, and place weights. When using, if you boil them in a copper pot, the color becomes blue-green.

　　○Method for Pickled Udo (Japanese Spikenard)
One: Remove the leaves from udo, trim the hard parts evenly, remove the skin, soak in water for about three days, changing the water three times daily. Wash and knead well, drain the water, layer alternately with udo and salt, and place weights.

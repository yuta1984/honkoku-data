　　　　Preparation Method for Mountain Yam
One: Wrap the mountain yam in a cloth towel to peel it, and it will not slip, allowing you to peel it in any way. After removing the skin, soak the remainder in salted sake, and no matter how much you cook it, it will not fall apart. This is a secret method.

　　　　○Honsho-ji Temple Fu (Wheat Gluten) Method
One: Boil old sake well, add fresh fu (wheat gluten) and simmer it well again. The fu will dissolve. Wrap it in cloth and put it in boiling water again. When boiled, it will solidify and become fu again. When used in cooking, it is beautiful and has excellent flavor. It is also good as a side dish with vinegar miso.

　　　○Method for Sobering Up from Sake
1. Peel large water pears, grate them with a wasabi grater into a ceramic bowl, squeeze out the water and collect it. Dry the pulp well. When dried, soak it again in the above pear water. Take it out and dry it. Do this daily until the pear water is exhausted. Then dry it well in the sun and make it into powder. For someone severely intoxicated with sake, add about one tea serving of this powder to freshly drawn water. If used, the intoxication will immediately clear and they will return to normal. This is a marvelous medicine.
　　　○Mountain River Sake Method

【Left Page】
1. Top quality white rice - 1 to　Steam well like rice for sake brewing
1. White rice koji - 8 shō　1. Water - 8 shō
　Mix the above three ingredients well and put in a half-barrel, stirring twice daily. When fermenting well, put in a jar or barrel, cover and set aside. Large bubbles will appear, then fine bubbles appear when fermentation peaks. When fermentation subsides, steam 1 to of top white rice as above, and add 8 shō of koji and 8 shō of water again. After this secondary addition, in summer it will be ready in about three days. In summer it develops a sour taste, so add a little lye from that time.

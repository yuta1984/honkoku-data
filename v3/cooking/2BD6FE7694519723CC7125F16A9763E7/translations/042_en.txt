○Method for Mung Beans
One: Soak yaenari lightly in water during the cold season, then grind with a stone mortar. Make a bag with very finely woven cloth or cotton, put it inside, and knead it out in water. Grind and knead out about twice in this manner. Then remove the hulls, let the white water settle, discard the supernatant, and dry in the sun - it becomes like kudzu. For cooking

○Method for Preparing Job's Tears (Coix seed)
One: Grind and crush with a Chinese mortar, removing the hulls. Then afterwards, pound gradually with the Chinese mortar. Change the water frequently, working it like washing rice, then dry in the sun.

△Types of Confections
○Method for Yōkan (Sweet Bean Jelly)
One: Azuki beans - 1 shō. Boil these well, grind with an ikaki, and strain through water. Grind the residue again in a mortar, strain with ikaki, then strain through sennatō and let settle. Squeeze through a cotton bag and let it air dry.

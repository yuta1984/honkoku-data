　　　　○Long-keeping Salmon Sushi
One: Fillet the salmon into three pieces, split the head into three parts, salt heavily,
　and leave for about four days. After that, cook black rice quite hard,
　add salt to a salty degree, pickle the fish mentioned above,
　and place a press. This is the method for long-term storage. The
　sushi mentioned above is good as is, or when rearranging and storing it,
　the salmon sushi method described later is good.

Put one cup of the kneaded kudzu and let it flow down - it falls in continuous strands like sōmen noodles. Make three round holes about 5 bu in diameter in the bottom of a ladle, put the kneaded kudzu into it. Boil water well in a pot and drop it in. Keep the fire under the pot burning steadily without neglect. If the water becomes lukewarm, the sōmen will break. When the sōmen accumulates in the pot, gather it up and put it directly into water.

○Method for Egg Sōmen
One portion: Chicken eggs - 5, Duck eggs - 5
For both types, remove the whites and use only the yolks. Strain through cotton cloth and mix well.
One portion: Finest large white sugar - 4 kin, Water - 2 shō

【Left Page】
Dissolve the above sugar in water, add only the whites of 15 eggs and simmer. Strain through sennatō, simmer again until cooked, and when "crab foam" appears, make holes of appropriate size in eggshells and drop it through. Then lift with an egg-scooping ladle, squeeze out the sugar liquid well to make it look beautiful. When lifting the above sōmen, put about two cups of water in the ladle before lifting.

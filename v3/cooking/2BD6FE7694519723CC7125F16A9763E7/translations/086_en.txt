　　　　○Simmered Squid Roe
One: Open the squid's belly and you will find something white and hardened. Cut this into two or three pieces for simmered dishes - it makes an exceptionally good ingredient.
　　　　○Octopus Nanban Style
One: Wash the octopus well, place it on a board and beat it thoroughly with a pestle to soften it, cut to desired size, and simmer in sake alone for a long time until it becomes very tender. Add soy sauce on top and simmer again, then remove and cut to desired size, serving without broth. Wasabi or ginger with vinegar is also good.

【Left Page】
　　　　○Oyster Dressed Salad
One: Skewer and grill the oysters, then simmer in light soy sauce and drain well. Dress with these three colors: black sesame, mustard miso, and wasabi.
　　　　○Oyster Clear Soup
One: Roast salt in a pot, remove that salt, put two or three oysters in the pot and crush them. Then add just enough water to cover the oysters, bring to a boil once, and add the aforementioned roasted salt. Sometimes add a little soy sauce, and of course add sake.
　　　　○Clam Clear Soup
One: Squeeze a little juice from the clam meat in your hand, then add equal parts sake and water, making it smell of sake...

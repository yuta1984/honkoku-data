　　　○Method for Hamana Nattō
1. White soybeans - 1 to　Choose truly excellent ones, wash well, and boil thoroughly like miso beans. Leave overnight in the pot as is, then the next day spread on four straw mats, sprinkle 6 shō of wheat flour over them and mix well, then dry in the sun. The drying should be to the firmness of cooked rice. Spread on two straw mats, place sumac leaves thinly on top and let ferment. The fermentation should be done well like soy sauce, and

　　　　○Cooked Bird
One: Cook wild goose and duck well in thin sauce,
　then pour soy sauce over it. Lay katsuobushi flakes underneath.
　　　　○Stir-fried Wild Goose and Duck
One: Prepare wild goose and duck in the usual way, mix aged sake and soy sauce in
　equal parts, put the bird in a pot, add the above broth until it just covers
　the ingredients. If the bird lacks fat, add a little more,
　and stir-fry over charcoal fire with sake.
　　　　○Small Bird Stir-fry
One: Mix aged sake, soy sauce, and water in equal parts, add a little
　katsuobushi flakes, put in a pot with the bird until just covered,
　and cook.
【Left Page】
　　　　○Dove Habushi Stir-fry
One: Whether dove or any other bird, crush plenty of bones
　and add them, add katsuobushi flakes, use sake and soy sauce in equal parts
　with less broth, stir-fry until somewhat satisfying.
　Then warm wasabi miso in this pot and
　let it dry, put it inside, and eat either as is or poured over other dishes.
　　　　○Small Bird Dumplings
One: Chop any small bird as
　finely as possible, mix bird meat and rice flour in equal parts, grind together
　in a mortar, boil in hot water and add to
　stewed dishes. The size of the dumplings is up to personal preference.

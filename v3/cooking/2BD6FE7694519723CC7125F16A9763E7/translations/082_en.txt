　　　　○Pheasant Soup with Mountain Topping
One: Cut pheasant in any manner, add plenty of sake, keep the fire low and simmer until it doesn't smell strongly of alcohol. Add water on top and bring to a boil, then add salt. If there's too much liquid, add a little soy sauce. Don't add any dashi at all. Also, it's good to fry the innards to make dashi and finish with yuzu vinegar.
　　　　○Ground Pheasant Soup
One: Cut pheasant finely, discard sinews and bones, put in mortar and grind as finely as possible, add sake and grind smooth to the consistency of dengaku miso, then set aside. Make the soup like natto. You can also add green onions

　　　　○Grilled Bird (Yakitori)
One: Skewer the bird and sprinkle salt lightly like a thin frost, then grill. When well grilled, add a little sake to soy sauce, dip the grilled bird in it, dip once more, and serve on a platter before the soy sauce dries. Only for pheasant, apply the sauce from the beginning and grill.

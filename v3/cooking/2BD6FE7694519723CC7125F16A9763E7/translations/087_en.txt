　　　　○Sea Cucumber Clear Soup
One: Split the sea cucumber in two, wash, and cut into large pieces. Stir-fry in a copper pot and soak in lukewarm water. When serving, prepare the broth with good seasoning, place yuzu citrus rings in the bowl, put the aforementioned sea cucumber on top, pour the broth over it, and serve as is. Placing parsley or similar greens on top is also good.

　　　　○Summer Aspic (Nikogori)
One: Whether using carp, crucian carp, or vegetarian ingredients, simmer in sauce. When cooking, add about five pieces of agar cut into squares. After cooking well, place in a bowl and chill with water.
　　　○Pounded Abalone
One: Soak abalone in kudzu water, then rub the kudzu well into the abalone and pound it. Pound it very well until properly prepared. It is good for steamed shellfish, simmered shellfish, vinegared shellfish, or puffed preparations.
　　　○Another Pounded Abalone Method
One: Female shells are best. Remove the shell and take out the intestines, then pound with a pestle on a board. It works well whether grilled like dengaku or used in simmered dishes.

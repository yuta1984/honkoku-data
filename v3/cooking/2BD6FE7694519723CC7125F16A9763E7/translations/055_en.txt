　　○Daikon Quick-Pickle (Pickled Vegetables)
One: Dry daikon in the sun for about three days, and pickle when slightly wilted
One: Rice bran - one to (however, five sho as is, five sho roasted until blackened)
Add salt to the above rice bran in an amount somewhat less than what would be suitable for eating, and pickle so the daikon pieces don't rub against each other. Do not apply weights.

　　○Eggplant Sweet Rice Wine Pickle
One: Black rice - one to (however, new rice); Koji - eight sho
Whether using a tub or jar, make sweet rice wine without adding any water, and when the rice has fermented well, add four sho of salt and pickle one hundred eggplants. Use small eggplants picked at the end of August, pickle them as they are, cover well, and store so that wind cannot enter. When using, take out the required amount the day before, quickly boil out the salt in a copper pot, then soak in water before use, and the color will turn blue.

○Miraculous method to make characters or pictures appear naturally on shells, ivory, and horn materials
Write anything you desire with grinding lacquer, dry it well, soak in raw vinegar for about twenty days, remove the vinegar and polish off the lacquer - the lacquer traces will naturally appear as raised characters, clear and vivid, looking like natural lettering.

○Method for glossy cloth
Cut light yellow cotton cloth to one shaku in length, soak in water to remove lime thoroughly and dry, paste backing paper in a cross pattern, and sprinkle iodine compound thinly and evenly on top.

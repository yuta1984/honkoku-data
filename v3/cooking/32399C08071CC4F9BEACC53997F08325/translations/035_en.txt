○Method to Prevent Mold in Vinegar or Plum Vinegar Pickles in Summer
In summer, pickling bamboo shoots, myōga ginger, fresh ginger, etc. in plum vinegar or rice vinegar is very convenient, but within four or five days mold appears and the taste changes. The method to prevent this is to make a bag with silk or cloth containing finely ground white mustard seed and submerge it in the vinegar - mold will absolutely never appear. This is a mysteriously wonderful method. Also, when mold appears on soy sauce in summer, this method will wonderfully stop it.

○Method to Make Poor Wine-Keeping Bottles Better Preserve Sake
For bottles that poorly preserve sake and spoil the wine, during the summer doyō period, put water in them for fourteen or fifteen days, then discard the water before putting in sake. Thereafter, no matter how many days you keep sake in them, the sake flavor will not be damaged.

○Method to Powder Pepper
Put pepper in a tea bowl and grind it with the bottom of a gourd. It will become fine powder in a short time - this is wondrous. The mysterious principle behind this wonder cannot be fathomed.

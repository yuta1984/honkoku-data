○Method for hardening lotus roots to make carvings
Boil lotus roots and burdock roots together in clear water. When they are thoroughly cooked, take them out and dry them in the sun. As they dry, the lotus roots gradually become hard, which is marvelous. Use these to carve freely as desired.

○Method to prevent ink from spreading on silk cloth without applying sizing
When ginger juice or glutinous rice powder is rubbed into ink, the ink will not spread. Shop curtains and such should be written with this method.

○Method to remove grime and dirt from copper utensils
Polish with plum vinegar and rub with polishing powder, and they will become like new vessels.

○Method for mending broken utensils
Press wheat gluten flat on a board until it becomes amber-colored and hardens, then powder it and store. When utensils break, knead this with water for mending. It will not separate again and works the same as lacquer repair - truly marvelous.

○Method to prevent foot pain when walking long distances
When traveling long distances, apply sesame oil to the soles and tops of feet to prevent pain. Also, after washing feet, dissolve a little salt with saliva, apply to the soles and tops of feet, and warm by the fire. Doing this will prevent foot pain.

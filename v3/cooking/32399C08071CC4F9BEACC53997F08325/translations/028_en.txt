○Method for Making Roasted Rice
Using superior white rice, if it is new rice, soak it in water for one day and one night, then remove and place in a bamboo strainer, drain the water well, and roast over charcoal fire. While the roasted rice is still hot, add a little brown sugar and soy sauce, mix well to coat thoroughly, cover with a lid and seal tightly so no steam escapes. In a short time it will dry with a light, crispy texture and achieve truly exquisite flavor.
Black beans should be roasted separately and then mixed in together. These should also be added while hot.

○Method for Lotus Egg
Sharpen the tip of a bamboo chopstick and carefully remove the threads from inside the lotus seed pod. Mix chicken egg with wheat flour and a little soy sauce, blend well, and pour this mixture into the lotus pod. Then bring water to a boil in a pot, boil this until it is cooked and solidified, then slice into rounds or diagonally for use.

○Method for Crucian Carp Yodogawa Style
For crucian carp measuring two to three sun up to five to six sun, soak in sake for approximately two and a half hours on long days, or about three hours on short days. The scales should be left on, but the intestines should have all mud and sand carefully removed. After soaking in sake as described above, use equal parts sake and water and simmer over charcoal fire for about half a day, then add soy sauce

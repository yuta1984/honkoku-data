White sugar　　150 monme
Green powder　　to taste

First, boil the syrup well in a copper pot until it dissolves completely, then add the bean flour and mix well, add the sugar on top and mix well, then add green powder to taste and mix very thoroughly, knead together sufficiently until it forms a long stick-like shape, then use three bamboo sticks to pinch it into the shape of suhama (a traditional confection shaped like a coastline).

○Gyūhi Cake
Glutinous rice flour (winter-bleached)　1 gō (knead with water and steam)

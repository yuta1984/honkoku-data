○Methods for preserving vegetables in sugar
First, the general method for preserving vegetables in sugar is to first cut the vegetables you wish to preserve as desired,
chopping them, or cutting them thinly, or into rounds, etc., cutting and chopping them accordingly for each type of vegetable,
and then boiling them very thoroughly in hot water.
　(There are slight variations in the degree of boiling for this hot water cooking, but for most things it is best to overcook them rather than undercook them)
Take the vegetables boiled as described above and lift them onto a bamboo strainer, squeeze out most of the water,
put them in a pot, add white sugar equal to the volume of the vegetables, add water

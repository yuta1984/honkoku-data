○Gyūhi Candy
Rice flour　　1 shō
Water　　　　1 shō

Mix the above well together and put in a pot, simmer over charcoal fire for about half a day, then add 1 shō 2 gō of refined sugar syrup and knead well. When it becomes soft like pounded mochi mixed with water, transfer to a separate vessel with flour spread on the bottom, pour the mixture in smoothly, cool well, and cut into various shapes for use.

○Method for rice flour: Wash the finest white glutinous rice until the water runs clear, dry thoroughly, grind in a mortar, and sift through silk gauze before use.

○Method for sugar syrup: Add 1 shō of water to 1 shō of finest white sugar, steam until foam appears and discard it, also remove the white foam that appears, strain through silk, put back in the pot and reduce by simmering from 1 shō down to 7 gō.

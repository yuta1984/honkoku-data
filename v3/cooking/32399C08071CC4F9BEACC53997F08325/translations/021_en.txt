Fox nut (qian shi)　　　　15 monme
Lotus seed meat　　　　　15 monme
Job's tears (coix seed)　　20 monme
Chinese yam　　　　　　　25 monme

Grind these four ingredients into very fine powder, sprinkle and mix in, then knead together. Like gyūhi cake, transfer to a separate vessel, spread flour on the bottom, pour the mixture smoothly on top and let it cool well. After five or six days have passed, wipe off the flour with cloth, sprinkle sugar on top, cut into appropriate pieces, and store in a box away from drafts - this way it will keep indefinitely.

○Rakkan Cake
Flour　　　100 monme
Sugar　　　80 monme

Add 20 monme of roasted sugar to the above, mix well together, knead thoroughly, and shape into various forms. The color may be dyed according to preference with any color desired.

○The method for preparing the flour: select the finest glutinous rice grain by grain, steam it into firm rice, dry it thoroughly until the grains separate individually, roast them white in a pot, grind in a mortar, and sift through silk gauze before use.

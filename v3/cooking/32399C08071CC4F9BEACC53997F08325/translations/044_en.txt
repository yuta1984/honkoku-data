Cloves: 5 bu
Nardostachys: 1 monme
Sandalwood: 1 monme
Turmeric: 3 bu
Frankincense: 1 bu
Cedar charcoal: 3 monme
Roasted salt: 5 bu
Grind the above eight ingredients into extremely fine powder and mix with kneaded honey.

○Method for preparing kneaded honey for incense
Add one small tea bowl of water to 40 monme of honey, simmer gently in a pot for about one day and night, let cool, put in a jar, seal the mouth well with paper and store. The longer it ages over months and years, the better it becomes mature. All incense should be prepared using this. It enhances the fragrance.

○How to cut bamboo flower vases
When cutting bamboo for flower vases, it tends to warp easily. The method to cut without warping is to fold one thick piece of paper in half and mark it with ink lines, then apply glue evenly and thoroughly to attach it to the bamboo, and saw according to the ink lines on this paper without deviation. It will not warp.

○Alternative Method
Boil red adzuki beans well, grind thoroughly in a mortar until sufficiently fine, strain through a sieve and let settle. Put small amounts at a time into raw cotton cloth or fabric and squeeze tightly.

This squeezed powder - 100 monme
White sugar - 100 monme
Water - 50 monme

No matter the quantity, use this ratio.

The method for removing impurities from sugar: peel good quality wild yam and grate a little into the sugar, mix well, add water and when it comes to a boil, let it rest briefly. The sugar impurities will solidify completely and float to the surface. At that time, scoop them away with a mesh ladle and it will become pure quickly.
Strain through cloth, simmer this sugar until concentrated, add salt and mix in the above squeezed powder, knead until it reaches a good firmness, put in a bowl and store. This is the supreme method for making bean paste.

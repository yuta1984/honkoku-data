○Method to separate water and tea in a teacup with brewed tea
Add a little fine powder of copper sulfate to the green tea and stir - the water and tea will separate mysteriously.

○Method to put fireflies in a bamboo tube so their light can be seen from outside
Leave joints at the top and bottom of fresh bamboo, drill holes with an awl, and insert fireflies through the holes. No matter how thick the bamboo, the light will mysteriously appear outside. It cannot be seen with dried bamboo.

○Method to draw pictures on tofu that won't come off
Whether using rouge or ink, dissolve in vinegar and add a little aged alum, then draw anything on the tofu. Even when boiled, it will not fade.

○Divine remedy to cure chafing between the thighs when walking
Wash with ginger juice. This divine remedy will cure it immediately. Alternatively, putting ginger in one's sleeve also works.

○Method to extract rouge powder from flowers and plants
Any red flower contains rouge. Cockscomb leaves work well. Grind these until liquid, strain through silk, add pine green to the juice, and boil in an earthen pot - the rouge powder will remain at the bottom. Add ume plum juice as a balancer and extract as you would ordinary rouge powder. If you lack proper equipment, lay silk crepe in a metal basin, pour the juice in, add water dropwise to extract the rouge powder. When using, dissolve in ordinary vinegar and add a little aged alum.

　　　　　Carp Togawa-zukuri Style
1. Fillet carp into three pieces, or as many pieces as skill allows. Wrap the roe inside and out, roll it up tightly, and slice thinly from the end. It will look like thin rolls. The cut surface should be arranged so the roe appears to bulge out.

【Right Page】
1. For fresh burdock root, place it in rice bran and add just enough water to cover in a large pot, then boil well. It becomes quite hard in the middle of cooking, but if boiled well again, it will become soft at that point. Once boiled, cut off both ends and wash the inside thoroughly to remove all sand. Thread a split skewer through the belly and clean it like cleaning a gun barrel.

　　　　　Regarding egg tempura cubes
1. Add ground fish paste to miso and apply it to the cut surface of halved eggs.

【Left Page】
1. For uiro rolls, spread the ground fish paste thinly on a board, pick it up with thin chopsticks from on top of the uiro and put it in water. It would also be good to pick it up with the tip of a thin spatula-like tool. In any case, shape it to become round. For filling, use chopped greens, minced Japanese parsley, and similar seasonal available ingredients.

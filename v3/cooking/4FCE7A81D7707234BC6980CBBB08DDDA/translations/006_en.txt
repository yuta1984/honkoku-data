　　　　　Regarding spiced salmon dishes
1. For spiced salmon dishes, cut regular salt-cured salmon into fine julienne strips and let the salt draw out for about two hours. Lightly tie the julienned pieces with straw rope and pull them up. At that time, briefly soak them in broth, being careful not to boil, and also add torn pieces.

　　　　　Regarding cedar plank cooking
1. Cedar plank cooking can be done with sea bream or Spanish mackerel. Like yellowtail grilling, cut with a little skin attached, grill with sake and soy sauce like yellowtail, then place on a cedar board to enjoy the cedar fragrance. Serve on the plate together with the board.

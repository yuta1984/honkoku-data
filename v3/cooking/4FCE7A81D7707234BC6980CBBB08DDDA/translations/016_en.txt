　　　　　Choyo Manjū
One: sweet potato crumbles about 1 cup　One: dried rice powder 5 shaku
　Mix the above together and steam, then add filling and steam again

　　　　　Fuji Mochi (Wisteria Rice Cake)
One: rice flour 2 cups, rice cake flour 1 cup 5 shaku, mix together
　Knead with boiling water and attach to wisteria leaves　Oral tradition

　　　　　Kudzu Paste
One: kudzu 1 cup, rice cake flour 5 shaku, knead with boiling water

【Left Page】
　Boil in hot water and add to sweet red beans. This creates a floating texture.

　　　　　Yōkan (Sweet Bean Jelly)
One: boil 1 sho of red beans and strain the paste. To this, add 300 me of brown sugar and about 2 cups of water, simmer and dissolve, then let cool. Mix this paste with 10 monme of unton-shi and 20 me of kudzu, knead together, place in a box and steam for about one hour.

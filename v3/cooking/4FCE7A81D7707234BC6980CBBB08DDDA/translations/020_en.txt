【Right Page】
　　　　　Cod Roe Karasumi (Salted Fish Roe)
One: Loosen the roe well, remove the membrane, add salt and
　knead with egg white. The shape depends on
　preference. Also prepare in various ways and dry in the sun
　　　　　Large Karasumi
One: Sprinkle salt on whole roe, place under weight for about ten days
　to pickle, then remove and quickly dry in the sun until dried, cut into cubes
　and use

【Left Page】
　　　　　Regular Yose-dōfu (Pressed Tofu)
One: To one shō of raw soybean flour, add about one cup of rice flour,
　grind raw soybeans into powder, sift through a strainer, mix with water
　and when it comes to a boil, add about one ladle of nigari (coagulant)
　and remove from heat, then it will sink to the bottom
　Scoop off the clear liquid on top and scoop up the tofu at the bottom
　to use

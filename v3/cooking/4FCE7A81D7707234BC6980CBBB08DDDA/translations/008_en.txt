　　　　　Regarding Tatsuta-maki
1. For Tatsuta-maki, add grated taro to ground fish paste, and place paper under Suisenji nori seaweed. Roll with citrus, kyunenpo, and similar ingredients.

　　　　　Square shinjo (fish dumpling)
1. For square shinjo, the current combination uses chopped shiso, or minced ingredients, nakomaka, parsley, miyashiga, meuto, and similar types should be used together.

　　　　　Tamatebako (Treasure box)
1. For Tamatebako, pack "snow" (grated daikon) into a square shape, put various items inside such as pickled plum flesh, chopped pickled plum, wood ear mushrooms, sen, and other varieties. Apply ground fish paste on top of the snow and parboil. Pierce with split skewers and attach the ground paste to the bottom.

　　　　　About Kanro-ni (Sweet Simmered Dish)

【Left Page】
　　　　　About Kinome Imo (Tree Bud Potatoes)
One: Boil yam until tender, remove skin, on a tray
　gently pound with a pestle, sprinkle kuzu powder
　over it, cut, wrap and shape into rounds, steam
　for use
　　　　　About Kinome Gobō (Tree Bud Burdock Root)
One: Boil burdock root until tender, remove skin, pound with pestle,
　sprinkle kuzu powder, roll into rounds, with straw

　　　　　Noshi Sanshō Mochi (Flattened Pepper Rice Cake)
One: Steam and pound one piece of rice flour repeatedly, strain black sugar with
　water and remove, add powdered sanshō pepper. For the bean paste, use the yō-
　kan method, put in box and cut the same way as senbei《Note: add a little tamari soy sauce》

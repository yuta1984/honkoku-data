1. For Matsuyoi tofu (Evening-wait tofu), mix ground walnuts into crumbled tofu and simmer. Since it tastes better than regular crumbled tofu, it is called "matsuyoi" (evening-wait).

　　　　　Imadegawa Tofu
1. Imadegawa tofu is simmered in sake. For kombu-wrapped tofu, simmer thoroughly in sake and water. Then season with soy sauce.

【Left Page】
　　　　　Regarding Zōni Tofu (New Year's Soup Tofu)
1. Cut tofu into large pieces, coat with rice cake powder, and grill. Add various ingredients like those in zōni (New Year's soup) to make it the same as rice cake zōni.

　　　　　Sangoya Tofu (Fifteenth Night Tofu)
1. Drain the water from tofu and crumble it, then simmer in sake, sauce, dashi, and water. Transfer to a draining cloth to remove excess liquid, then place in a copper warming pot and cook until browned.

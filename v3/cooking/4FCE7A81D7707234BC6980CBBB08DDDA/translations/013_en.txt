　　　　　Parasitic Wheat Gluten

【Left Page】
1. Mix one sho of fresh wheat gluten with ground sea bream, five eggs, a little grated mountain yam, add a little sake and salt, and grind together well. Shape into balls or flatten as desired depending on the gluten, briefly boil and good for soup.

　　　　　Kudzu-coated Sea Bream
1. Make ground sea bream paste, wrap with kudzu powder, briefly blanch in hot water, soak in cold water and prepare as sashimi.

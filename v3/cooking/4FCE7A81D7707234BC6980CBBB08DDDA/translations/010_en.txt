【Right Page】
　Arrange with the grilled side facing up.

　　　　　Keshō Tofu (Makeup Tofu)
1. Cut tofu like dengaku, coat with rice cake powder and grill, then serve with sauce poured over it.

　　　　　Tama Tofu (Round Tofu)
1. Shape tofu into rounds and insert walnuts in the center.

　　　　　Kuzu Dengaku (Kudzu Dengaku)

【Left Page】
1. For kuzu dengaku, apply kudzu paste over regular dengaku, top with chopped walnuts, and grill until charred. The kudzu should be made thicker than usual.

　　　　　Matsuba Dengaku (Pine Needle Dengaku)
1. Prepare tofu like regular dengaku and grill with iwanori (rock seaweed) attached.

　　　　　Hitate Dengaku (Steamed Dengaku)
1. For hitate dengaku, thinly coat and wrap tofu with black seaweed.

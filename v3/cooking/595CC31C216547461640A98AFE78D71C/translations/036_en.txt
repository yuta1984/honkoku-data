How to Extract Bird Fat　Put the fatty parts and fatty skin of birds in a pot, hollow out
　　　　　　　　　　　an eggplant or pumpkin, wash everything well, place it
　　　　　　　　　　　mouth-down in the pot, and boil with water - the bird fat will
　　　　　　　　　　　rise to the surface. Boil well and let cool, then
　　　　　　　　　　　naturally only the fat will solidify and collect
　　　　　　　　　　　inside that eggplant

How to Make Sprouts　　Soybeans, azuki beans, black-eyed peas, yae-nari beans, buckwheat, peas
　　　　　　　　　　　Soak the above varieties in water, spread sand on a tray,
　　　　　　　　　　　sprinkle water appropriately and put in a kotatsu,
　　　　　　　　　　　then sprouts will soon emerge. Ready to use in several days
　　　　　　　　　　　　　Also, pour hot water over them and wrap in straw - sprouts
　　　　　　　　　　　　　will emerge overnight

How to Preserve Bamboo Shoots　Boil well and pickle in amazake with salt
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　made slightly salty for preservation

How to Preserve Melons　　　　Hang good, unblemished melons or winter melons
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　over a fire and they will keep

Green Pickling of Eggplant　　Mix salt with the sand from the bottom of an indigo vat
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　and the color will not change. Also good to squeeze
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　bean leaves, add wheat gluten and salt to the juice for pickling

How to Remove Scum from Sugar　Add appropriate water to sugar and boil, divide into
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　jars or buckets, crack eggs into it and foam will rise
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　to the surface along with the eggs floating up. At that time
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　skim off the scum
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　However, for strict vegetarian cooking, grate mountain yam
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　instead of eggs

About Molded Confections　　　To boil and mold walnuts, black beans, kaya nuts, etc.,

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　then it will soon solidify. Short-cut candy is good

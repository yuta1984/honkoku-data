Smooth Soup
　　Add ground tofu to regular miso, add pepper and bring to boil,
　　strain and add grated daikon radish

Loach Soup
　　This too should be made thick with regular miso dashi, brought to boil,
　　and pepper added
　　　However, there are various ways to prepare loach. Usually it is
　　　sautéed with salt, or sautéed with sake. Here is one secret method:
　　　△First put loach in a tub, season well with water, crack eggs and
　　　add them. When you see the eggs and the loach weakens, if you cook in soup
　　　　there will be eggs in the loach's belly making the flavor exceptionally delicious
　　　　Also, grating wasabi and sautéing the loach is good. It has aroma and is excellent

Wakame Soup
　All prepared separately
　　Mix equal parts red miso and regular miso by grinding, with dashi
　　prepare like regular hon-jiru, bring to boil and strain
　　△For all of these separately, prepare regular miso soup with water,
　　pre-cook well with this, serve in bowls and
　　pour hon-jiru from above, sprinkle plenty of bonito flakes and serve

Grated Yam Soup
　　Clear soup style is also good, but miso has better flavor. Six parts white miso,
　　four parts regular miso mixed by grinding, adjust with dashi like hon-jiru,
　　bring to boil, strain, cool well, then thin with grated
　　yam. In winter, reheat and serve

Eighty-nine: Anhei Tofu　　Put silky tofu in a small tea bowl, wrap one or two matsutake mushrooms inside, and steam. It is like the method for making chawan-mushi (savory egg custard). ○Serve with kudzu starch sauce and grated wasabi.

Ninety: Salt Tofu　　This appears in the section on recipe ninety-six, "Three Pure Tofu."

Ninety-one: Dawn Dengaku　　Mix white miso with sesame oil and spread it on the tofu from the beginning, then grill. The fire penetrates well to the back before the miso burns.
△Dengaku grilled with a light soy sauce glaze and topped with warm white kudzu starch sauce and grated yuzu is called "eji dengaku."

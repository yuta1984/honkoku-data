Chop finely, and for about one sho (1.8 liters) of seven kinds combined, use seven to eight shaku (about 140-160ml) of oil. Heat the oil well to boiling, first stir-fry the ginkgo nuts, burdock root skin, and green vegetables, then add shiitake mushrooms, wheat gluten, arrowhead, and fried tofu and stir-fry. Season with soy sauce and let cool. ○Knead wheat flour well and add a little sake and salt. Roll very thin - adding sake and salt makes it stretch thin. Cut into circles of two sun three to four bu (about 7-8cm) in diameter, fold into the shape of a woven hat, wrap the filling, moisten fingers with water to smooth and seal the edges, and steam. ○Use Kenchien vinegar as shown in number 77 of the previous volume.

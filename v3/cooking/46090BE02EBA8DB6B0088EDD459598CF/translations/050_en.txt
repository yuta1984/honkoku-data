Ten: Nori-wrapped Okara Sushi　　This refers to nori-wrapped okara sushi.
Apply a little vinegar to Asakusa nori and prepare it like regular nori-wrapped sushi, using okara instead of rice. Add egg as a binder, season with sesame oil, sake, salt, and soy sauce, and include flaked sea bream, wood ear mushrooms, thin slices of chestnuts, and powdered Japanese pepper.

Eleven: Yuba White Salad　　Cut yuba finely and mix with poppy seed miso blended with tofu to make a white salad.

Twelve: Narcissus Salad　　Boil white scallions well, make a white salad with poppy seed sesame miso mixed with tofu, and sprinkle with powdered Japanese pepper.

Thirteen: White Salad　　Cut any fish meat into large dice, boil in salted water and drain the liquid. Grind white sesame seeds, mix well with six parts miso and four parts tofu, and dress the ingredients.

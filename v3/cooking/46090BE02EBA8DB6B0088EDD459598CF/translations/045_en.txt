Ninety-seven: Kumoi Tofu　　Keep water boiling vigorously in a large pot. Arrange sea bream fillets about one inch in size on a board, set up a bamboo pipe from the pot and pour boiling water over them to remove the fish oil. Then cut tofu to the same size, quickly sear it, arrange in Nara tea bowls, making sure to serve it quite hot, with fresh simmered soy sauce and grated daikon...

Ninety-eight: Kaku-oboro　　This is a dish from His Imperial Majesty's table. It is a secret tradition of professional cooks and is not transmitted to the world. For the sake of completeness, only the name is mentioned here to include it in the count of one hundred delicacies.

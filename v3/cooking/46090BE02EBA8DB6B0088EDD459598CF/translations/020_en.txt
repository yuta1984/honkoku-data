23. Sanemori Tofu　　Cut thinly to a good thickness, cook in light
soy sauce with dashi broth, serve with little liquid, top with squeezed
ginger juice, and place ground black sesame on top so thickly
that the tofu cannot be seen

24. Unohana Tofu　　Quickly deep-fry the tofu in oil, after about
one hour, boil it with one cup soy sauce, one cup water, and three cups sake.
However, do not let liquid accumulate in the vessel, and sprinkle potato starch on top.
○The preparation of potato starch can be found below
"Nadeshiko Tofu" in recipe fifty-four of the previous volume
△When sprinkling boiled egg yolk instead of potato starch,
it is called Yamabuki Tofu

Eighty-five: Thick Grilled Tofu　　Oil a flat pan, put in the tofu, add a little wheat flour without moisture, season from the beginning with sake, salt, and fresh soy sauce. ○Add chopped ginkgo nuts, wood ear mushrooms, pine mushrooms, and other ingredients as in recipe nineteen (hiryōzu) from the previous volume, and simmer over medium heat. During cooking, repeatedly scoop out the liquid with a metal ladle. Cook well until the bottom is slightly scorched, then flip the pan over to remove it, scrape off the burnt parts, cut as desired, and serve hot. ○Also called Abe Tofu.

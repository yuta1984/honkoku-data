Seventy-four: Maple Leaf Tofu　　Squeeze out water well and mix in wheat flour thoroughly. ○Remove the core and seeds from red chili peppers, simmer in sake for about half a day, chop finely like needles, and also finely chop a little old ginger like needles. Mix these two ingredients appropriately into the above tofu, form into squares the size of one block, deep-fry whole, cut into rounds, steam, and serve with fukusa miso diluted with water and warmed as a base sauce.
　　The cooking liquid from the above chili peppers should be mixed with miso to make a type of chili miso. The spiciness is not too intense and is exquisite. Though not included here now, I note this for reference.
　　○Mix ground tofu with wheat flour and a little white sugar, combine with well-cooked Dainagon azuki beans left whole, and prepare using the above method - this is named Sakura Tofu.

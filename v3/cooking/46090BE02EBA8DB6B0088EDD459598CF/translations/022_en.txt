30. Yokogumo (Horizontal Cloud) Tofu　　Cut one block flat into about four pieces, sprinkle thick layer of finely powdered green seaweed, place the same tofu on top and make pressed tofu　○Refer to pressed tofu in section 15 of the previous volume

31. Icicle Tofu　　Take the finely cut tofu described in the sōmen section 45 of the previous volume, coat with kudzu powder and boil　Beyond that, cooking should follow personal preference

32. Buckwheat Tofu　　Mix buckwheat flower powder into the sōmen tofu from section 45 of the previous volume, and season like regular buckwheat noodles

33. Whale Tofu　　Squeeze out the water from tofu well, pack it full into a long cedar box, place another layer of tofu mixed with pot soot on top, and apply pressure for a while　○Bring water to a rolling boil, enough to reach the bottom of the box, steam well, break the box to remove, cut into rounds, and fry lightly in sesame oil so as not to color it　○It's also good to use charred kelp instead of pot soot　○One method is to grind white sesame seeds well and mix with tofu

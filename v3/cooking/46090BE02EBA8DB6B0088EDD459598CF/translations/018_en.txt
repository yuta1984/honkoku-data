12. Yūgao Tofu　　Cut one block flat into two pieces, and make
a bottomless square box from cedar wood that fits exactly half a block.
Put in the half block and pour boiling water with rising steam through it two or three times.
Then, in a shallow bowl with a flat bottom, pool raw soy sauce shallowly
to about one bu depth, gently place the boxed tofu on top,
set it outside in extremely cold weather and expose it for three nights. Remove the box
and slice it into rounds as is, using it as a drinking accompaniment.

13. Kwanze Soup　　Cook thinly sliced tofu in medium-strength miso
(neither too thin nor too thick) and cover with sesame paste. ○Almost the same as rich soy sauce

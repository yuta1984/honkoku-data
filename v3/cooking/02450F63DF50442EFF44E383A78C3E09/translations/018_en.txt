Similarly
　Cut to appropriate size, stir-fry in sesame oil,

【Ingredient】
Dried Pufferfish (Hifugu)
　There are two types: terikawa and fuguhito, both have
　their uses. Both should be soaked in water before use.

【Cooking Example】
Boiled and drained
Soup
Or Scallop Miso
　Add only terikawa, or combine with round-peeled daikon radish.
　However, round-peeled daikon should be well pre-boiled before use.
　Miso should be thin. Of course, bonito flake dashi is good.
　　　　Aromatics: dried citrus peel, chives

【Cooking Example】
Regular Miso
Same
Called May Soup
　Using dried pufferfish, cut both skin and flesh to appropriate size, combine with grilled tofu,
　dried daikon, and new burdock root, all cut small.
　No aromatics needed.

【Cooking Example】
Small Bowl
　Cut terikawa finely, simmer with mirin, sake,
　and soy sauce, then mix in
　plum-like manner.

【Cooking Example】
Roasting Tray Dish
　Cut terikawa into strips or diamond shapes, simmer with sake and
　soy sauce and set aside, sprinkle with tea whisk,
　grill over distant fire, or place on roasting tray.

【Ingredient】
Dried Sweetfish
　Soak in water and wash well before use. There is also grilled sweetfish,
　prepared the same way. There are domestically produced salted sweetfish,
　but the preparation method is poor, so it is omitted.

【Cooking Example】
Medium Bowl

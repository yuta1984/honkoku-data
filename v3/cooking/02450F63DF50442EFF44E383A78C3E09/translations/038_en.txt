"Shūi Ryōri-shū" (Supplementary Cooking Collection) - Complete volume, currently being published
This collection contains formal meal arrangements, banquet service, shichi-go-san ceremonies, gold and silver service settings, and procedures for weddings and ritual ceremonies. Combined with the previous and following three volumes, it is intended to serve as a comprehensive culinary manual.

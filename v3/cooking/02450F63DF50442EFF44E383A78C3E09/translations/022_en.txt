【Ingredient】
Wakasagi (Pond Smelt)
　Comes from Hitachi Province. There are few ways to use it.
　There is no method other than sprinkling with soy sauce and grilling.

【Ingredient】
Kushiko (Skewered Sea Cucumber), Kinko (Golden Sea Cucumber)
　When soaked in water and used, same as boiled sea cucumber,
　stir-fried sea cucumber, etc. Many methods of use
　already appear in the previous volume.

【Ingredient】
Dried Octopus
　Soak thoroughly in water, then use for vinegared octopus,
　or sakura-ni (cherry blossom simmering) and such.

【Ingredient】
Skewered Abalone
　Soak good quality fresh abalone in water for four to five days,
　remove when properly softened, blanch in hot water,
　wash well and use.

【Cooking Example】
Small Bowl Dish
　Slice thinly, add plenty of bonito flakes and simmer well with mirin and soy sauce.
　Also good to simmer and thicken well with kudzu starch.

【Cooking Example】
Large Sake Cup
　Slice thinly, dress with pepper miso
　　　or dress with sea urchin

【Ingredient】
Suboshi-aji (Sandbar-dried Horse Mackerel)
　This makes a complete dish. When grilled and squeezed by hand, the bones and flesh separate. Good for long plates as
　mukōzuke and such. Side dishes, accompaniments.

【Ingredient】
Hime-iwashi (Princess Sardines)
　This also makes a complete dish like the above.
　A product of Kaga Province. Good for long plates in kaiseki
　grilled dishes and such. Side dishes, accompaniments.

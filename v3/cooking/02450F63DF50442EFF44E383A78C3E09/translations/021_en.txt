【Cooking Example (Herring Roe)】
Ohira (Large Flat Bowl)
　Wash the pickled roe well, simmer briefly in
　hot water, pour kudzu starch sauce over it, with wasabi
　or pepper miso paste

【Ingredient】
Gomame (Dried Sardines)
　Remove heads and bellies, roast well in a horoku pan,
　rub by hand and sift the powder well before using

【Cooking Example】
Inkstone Lid
Arranged Dish
Called glossy gomame
　Boil down mirin and soy sauce until syrup-like,
　cool well, then add the above gomame and mix
　《Note: If still warm, gomame will soften and wrinkle》

【Cooking Example】
Jotan (Ceremonial Dish)
　Used for wedding ceremony dishes and such
　Place copper pot over fire, dissolve high-quality kudzu in water,
　pour over the above gomame while placing on warm charcoal

【Cooking Example】
Namasu Plate
　Grill and pour hot water over, tear and trim edges well,
　add finely julienned udo, sake, raw chestnut slivers,
　fresh arrangement

【Ingredient】
Dried Squid
　Also kaki-surume (scraped dried squid)
　　However, kaki-surume need not be soaked in water
　　Surume should be soaked in water, though depending on quality, can be used directly

【Cooking Example】
Ceremonial Side Dish
　Wash surume, remove legs and head, roll tightly,
　tie with straw, place in warm ash briefly then remove,
　wipe ash clean and slice crosswise
　Also, when washing surume, twist into fine strands to proper degree

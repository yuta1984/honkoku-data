Chakutai (The belly band worn in the fifth month is called chakutai)

When a woman becomes pregnant, an auspicious day in the fifth month should be chosen to perform the belt ceremony. At that time, an elderly woman should be appointed to handle the hikime (whistling arrows) and yanagui (willow arrows). Also, the belt should be entrusted to a woman who has been blessed with many descendants, designating her as the "belt parent" who will tie the belt.

Transmission of Rolled Fish Types
First, for rolled sea bream, rolled pike conger, whitebait, or any other fish, fillet into half-bodies and carefully remove all bones, removing the upper flesh. Soak the skin in salt water and leave for a while, then lift out and drain well. Apply shinjo-seasoned flesh and wrap in cloth, then steam in a bamboo steamer. Fire control is oral transmission.

　　　Transmission of Shinjo (Fish Paste Dumpling)
First, for duck, sea bream, whiting, tilefish, flounder and similar types, take the upper flesh of the fish with a knife as if shaving sea bream, grind very well in a mortar, and add Chinese yam and egg white. Steep bonito flakes in water and add, soak well, extend the flesh with that broth, for sweetness boil mirin and sake then cool, season with salt to proper taste, shape using a tea bowl lid, and finish by adding plenty of hot water to a large pot.
First, for duck, pound the flesh very well on a board for preparation, same as above.

　　　Transmission of Fish Tofu
First, for any fish, the preparation is the same as above, prepare a frame and lay cloth, moisten very well with water, add the above fish paste and steam. Fire control is oral transmission.

　　　Transmission of Tender-Simmered Octopus
First, pound octopus very well with daikon radish, add white beans, and one cup each of mirin and sake

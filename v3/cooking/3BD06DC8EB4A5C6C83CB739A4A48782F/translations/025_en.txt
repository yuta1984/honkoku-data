　　　Transmission of Grilled Salt Long Yam
First, pound the long yam extremely fine, grate grilled salt, separate the long yam one piece at a time, wrap in salt, and finish by placing in a roasting oven.

　　　Transmission of Fire-dried Mandarin Orange
First, peel the mandarin orange thinly from the small end, arrange on a board and dry for one day, then place in a roasting oven.

　　　Transmission of Leaf-shaped Flounder Roasting
First, apply one salting to flounder, wash well with water, arrange on ramie stands and dry for one day. Then grill over distant fire using a wire mesh, and finish by placing in a roasting oven.

　　　Transmission of Shiso Thousand-layer Pickle Roasting
First, peel off the shiso from thousand-layer pickles one sheet at a time, wash very well with water, spread out again one sheet at a time, and finish in a roasting oven.

　　　Transmission of Tender Simmering
First, pound raw shellfish, ark shells, turban shells, abalone and similar types very well with daikon radish, cut that daikon into small rounds, add a little rice bran into it, put in a pot, and simmer very well over a cooking fire for one day and one night.

Julienned mitsuba as garnish for soup

　　Wrinkled Potato
Cut Chinese yam into rectangular strips about 1 sun 3 bu in size, cutting as
thinly as possible. Arrange bamboo skewers on a grilling net, place the yam pieces
on top, grill over distant flame, sprinkle with salt, grill without burning
to remove moisture, then place on a roasting pan.

　　Icicle Soup
In salt water heated over charcoal fire, break off suitably thick
icicles to appropriate lengths and serve. They do not melt for a while.

　　Grated Abalone
Grate abalone with a grater and add to
miso soup. It is also good to cut and add trout or other fish. Tama-miso is also good.

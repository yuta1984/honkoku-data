In the time of the Yellow Emperor, cooking pots and steamers were first made, and since the way of cooking with fire was established until now, cooks have devoted their skills to presenting delicious food. Through their techniques, they make grains resemble meat and transform meat to be like grains. If this is not due to the art of cooking, then what is it?

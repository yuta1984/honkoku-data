　　Shigaraki Aemono (Dressed Dish)
Shave burdock root into thin strips, boil in hot water, drain the water, and dress with Japanese pepper miso.

　　White Plum Wine
During winter, soak about a hundred white plum blossoms in water overnight to remove the inner scent. Soak only the petals in light sake. For one shō (1.8 liters) of sake, use one hundred flowers and add a little snow water. The sake prepared in mid-November is intended to be used from New Year's Day. When serving, strain it before use.

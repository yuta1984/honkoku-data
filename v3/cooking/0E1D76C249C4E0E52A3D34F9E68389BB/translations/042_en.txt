Ujimaru
Open the hamo (pike conger eel), remove the head and bones, and salt-cure it. It's fine to cure for four to five months. When making sushi, soak in water for one day and night to remove the appropriate amount of salt. Cut into five-bu pieces (about 1.5cm) and make sushi with vinegared rice. The sushi preparation method is the usual way with nothing special.

Ni-dengaku (Simmered Dengaku)
Cut eggplant for dengaku, skewer it, brush with oil, apply white miso paste and grill. Boil salted water and put the dengaku with skewers into it, then immediately remove and use.

Kaori-zu (Fragrant Vinegar)
Combine various citrus juices from yuzu, mikan (mandarin orange),kunenbo, and similar fruits to use.

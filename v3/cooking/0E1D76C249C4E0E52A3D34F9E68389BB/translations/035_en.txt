Boil water and gently scoop it in, place on a flat dish with thin kudzu starch sauce and mustard. The kudzu sauce should use bonito stock and soy sauce, and should be served quite warm.

Dried Daikon Salad
Finely chop dried daikon, soak in hot water and knead slightly, squeeze firmly, and dress with mustard sesame miso.

Omi Koro-ni (Omi-style Simmered Fish)
Whether using sweetfish or catfish, remove the scales, cut into rounds, and simmer in soy sauce.

Tea House Tofu
For ten blocks of tofu, use the equivalent of one kin of tea - use good quality sencha with fine aroma. Remove the cloth marks from the tofu and cut into dice. When the tea comes to a boil, add the tofu and simmer well until it becomes tea-colored.

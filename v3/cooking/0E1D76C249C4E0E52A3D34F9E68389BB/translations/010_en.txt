　　Bellflower Eggs
Boil eggs and remove the shells, then boil again in hot water.
While still warm, hold with chopsticks and shape like bellflowers, then boil again in hot water. The yolk inside will form a flower shape together with the white.
Making persimmon shapes or twisting them follows the same method.

　　【Red seal mark】These Flowers
Take fallen plum blossoms (after the flowers have scattered, when they become about the size of beans or small red beans), soak in water for about seven days.
Remove the sourness, coat with salt, and pickle for about one month.
When the salt brine rises, discard the brine and coat with white sugar.

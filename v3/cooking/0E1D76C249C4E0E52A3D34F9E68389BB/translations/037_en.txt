Remove the skin from small taro, boil well and pound, take an appropriate amount and coat with azuki beans. It is not good to wrap sweet filling inside.

Sesame Dressing (Goma-ae)
Cut eggplant into strips, soak in water to remove the bitterness, drain the water and dress with sesame miso. The eggplant is used raw.

Autumn Leaf Clams (Momiji Hamaguri)
Take small clam meat and simmer in soy sauce, then drain the liquid. Grind bonito flakes into powder, mix with hemp seeds and coat the clams. More bonito flakes is better.

Combined Hot Tofu (Awase Yudofu)
Boil mochi separately, boil tofu separately in hot water, place the mochi on top and pour kudzu starch sauce over it.

　　Method for Preserving Sea Cucumber for Long Periods
If sea cucumber is pickled in yuzu vinegar, it will keep for about twenty days.

　　Sake Rice
Fill a tea bowl seven-tenths with sake, seven-tenths with soy sauce, cook as usual with the intention of using one sho of rice. It colors like tea rice. Salt is unnecessary as it's contained in the soy sauce. Adding sake makes the flavor refreshing and light.

　　Soy Sauce Rice
This is the aforementioned sake rice without the sake. This is often used instead of cooking tea rice.

　　Nagasaki-maki Chien

Scrape the burdock root until white, put it in water, cut to appropriate size
and place in a pot, cook with plain hot water. However, place a weight on the lid
and boil well, then simmer in sake. Allow approximately one cup of sake per person,
for eight people add one cup of soy sauce and simmer well, then
thicken with kudzu starch and serve with grated ginger.

　　Mizoshiri-mushi (Steamed Sardine)
Fillet fresh sardines into three pieces and steam, pour kudzu sauce over them,
and serve with grated ginger.

　　Yakidashi (Grilled and Sauced)
Cut tofu in the shape of dengaku, slice horizontally into three pieces, skewer
three pieces on a stick to make dengaku, and grill until
fox-colored, then immediately place on a plate and pour vinegar miso over it.

　　Hanagasumi (Flower Mist)
Boil lotus root, remove the outer skin, shape like flowers,
grate Japanese yam, add kudzu powder as binding agent, and place in a box

『Season for using salt-preserved birds is
　　from end of February to June』
　　　Method for salt-preserving geese and ducks
First split the bird's back and stuff with salt,
preserving with feathers and juices intact.
Alternatively, pluck the bird's feathers, split the back, remove innards, and salt,
stuffing the inside with seaweed and salt as described above.
Put feathers in a straw bag and wrap the bird with feathers

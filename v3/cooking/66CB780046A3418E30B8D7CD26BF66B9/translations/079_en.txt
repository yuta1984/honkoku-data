【Lower Right】
●"Sen" refers to fine cutting. This is used for
raw bird sashimi, pheasant sashimi, etc. ●"Skin-bone reversal" means splitting the bird's back open, pushing
the organs wide and pushing back the bones at the belly edge to remove them. This is
used for small birds like snipe. For large birds it's called "eagle belly muscle," for small birds it's called "skin-bone"
●About crane tendons: Extract the tendons from the separate legs,
align and bundle them, cut to about 3 sun in length for use. The method of tendon extraction
is to cut the front and back of the thigh swelling and extract the tendons from the cut opening. This
is used as a crane's mark. Used for both raw birds and salted birds
●Lark's split claws: The foot digits are called split claws,
with long claws. Leave these and cut all the front digits from the root along with
sinews, attach to the thigh together with the shins for use. This is the lark's mark
●Shrike's separate foot: Enclose the foot joints in the middle, cut to about 1 sun in length for
use. This is the mark ●Pheasant's eight-way split: This is for grilled birds.
First peel off the skin, of course clean the inside well, then make slight cuts with the knife at both
shoulder joints, hold the wings, and split into two on both sides

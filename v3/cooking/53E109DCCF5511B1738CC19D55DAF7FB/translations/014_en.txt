May Food and Cooking Menu

Preferred foods: Eat warm foods. This month, cooling the stomach is not good.

Prohibited foods: Avoid cold foods, raw melons, and honey. Do not eat loquat and grilled fish at the same time. Do not drink stagnant water from valley streams. Fish and turtle saliva is in the water, and drinking it causes convulsions.

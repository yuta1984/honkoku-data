Walnut Vinegar
For walnut vinegar, grind walnuts well, dissolve in vinegar, and strain through a water bag before use.

Deep Mountain Mushrooms
Steam large shimeji mushrooms, place barley miso inside the caps, and top with strained egg. Good as an accompaniment to flat dishes or grilled items.

Pale Pink Plum
Take only the flesh of boiled plums, add plenty of sugar and a little wheat flour, and knead in a metal-free pot over charcoal fire. Add split apricot-plums.

Teika Kamaboko
Make the ground fish soft, boil clear water in a pot, scoop in the ground fish and cook, then (add) kudzu starch sauce.

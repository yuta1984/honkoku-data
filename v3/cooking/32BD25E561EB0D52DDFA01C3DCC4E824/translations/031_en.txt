　　　Oden Higaku (Oden-style Dish)
Roll out mochi (rice cake) thin like flower petals and lightly
toast it. The mochi should be quite thin. Apply miso to tofu dengaku
and wrap it in the aforementioned mochi.

　　　Quick Udon

and pound it. Afterwards, fold it back as usual, and for dusting flour use

　　　Oil Not-Fried
Deep-fry one block of tofu whole, then remove the skin
and cut the inside to a good size for use in clear soup.
The broth has no oil taste, but the tofu retains its oil flavor.

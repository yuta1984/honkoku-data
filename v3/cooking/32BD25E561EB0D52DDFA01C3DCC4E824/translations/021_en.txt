Ebi-kane (Lobster Cakes)
Pound and grind Ise lobster meat, add various
seasonings inside and form together, deep-fry in oil, add
scallions and simmer.

　　　Shimofuri (Frost-covered)
Whether using chrysanthemum leaves or vegetable leaves, attach
ground fish paste and put in hot water to float, use in clear soup.

　　　Echizen Iri-ae (Echizen Stir-fried Mix)
Add a little soy sauce to vinegar, cut daikon radish into large pieces and
simmer. Grill fish well and chop finely,
add to the above mixture. Remove from heat for a delicious warm
namasu (vinegared dish).

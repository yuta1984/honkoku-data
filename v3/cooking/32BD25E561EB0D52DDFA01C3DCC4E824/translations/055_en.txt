Early Sansho Sprouts (Cultivation Method)
Sow Japanese pepper seeds in tiers, and they will sprout in about twenty days. If they do not sprout, it is good to put them in a sprouting chamber (moyashi kamuro). Sprouts can be grown year-round.

Suspended Sea Bream (Cooking Method)
Suspend fresh sea bream with tail and fins by thread, sprinkle well with salt, then remove the thread.

Whole Grilled Abalone (Cooking Method)
Wash abalone thoroughly with shell, place on a gotoku (cooking stand) and grill. Pierce in several places with skewers, add soy sauce and simmer. Remove from shell, slice and use.

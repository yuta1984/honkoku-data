Octopus Ice Preparation
Briefly blanch octopus and place it outside as is. During the cold season, it will freeze in one night. Cut into rounds and simmer with soy sauce for use.

Egg Drop in Hot Oil
Heat frying oil well, crack eggs into it, and scoop them up. Use with roasted sake and wasabi.

Miso-Simmered Crucian Carp
Prepare miso broth and add raw cut crucian carp from the beginning to simmer. Do not remove the lid after the first boil. Pour sake over the carp and simmer again. Check the condition after the second boil. The fish will become tender and well-cooked.

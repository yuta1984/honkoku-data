Salmon Quick Sushi
Cook the roe with sake to prepare the flesh, and place the roe on top. Use kelp as divider and pickle again. Wash the rice with vinegar, adjust salt seasoning, and place weights on top. Start with light weight, then make it heavier later.

Ukiyo Udon (Worldly Udon)
Add two or three eggs to one sho of udon flour. Add salt and knead firmly with sake, step on it well and roll it out. Kudzu is best for dusting flour. If not kudzu, then cut it. What the world calls "egg udon" is this.

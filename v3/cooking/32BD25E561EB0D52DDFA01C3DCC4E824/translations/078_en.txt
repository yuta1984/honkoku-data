Hokukōrai (Northern Goryeo)
These are the eyes of dried salmon. Soak them in water until properly softened, then use in miso soup or clear soup. They come from Echizen province.

　　　Fu Hanpen (Wheat Gluten Fishcake)
Grind fresh wheat gluten well and cook like regular hanpen fishcake.

　　　Tatsuta River
Boil Japanese yam and cut into maple leaf shapes, then pickle in plum vinegar. Cut into rounds when using. When in a hurry, it's fine to dye with safflower.

　　　White Flower Tofu
Cut regular tofu into thin strips. If you coat a thin-bladed knife with vinegar when cutting, it cuts freely. Seasoning should be according to preference.

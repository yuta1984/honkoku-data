　　　Haya Amazake (Quick Sweet Rice Drink)
One egg, one cup of sake, one cup of sugar, one cup of dried cooked rice, warm with an appropriate amount of water and use.

　　　Ōbaku Iridashi (Ōbaku-style Fried Tofu)
Use a generous amount of frying oil, cut tofu into appropriate amounts, place in a copper mesh basket. Keep the frying oil heated, swirl the tofu in the oil two or three times, then immediately put into soy sauce. Add a little sake to the soy sauce and adjust the seasoning, prepare separately.

　　　Tosa Powder
Put finest quality glutinous rice in water during the cold season, leave it outside. On the seventh or eighth day, place in a bamboo strainer, drizzle water over it, put in a mortar and pound gradually. Then sift it through a sieve.

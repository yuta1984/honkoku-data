White Fish Kamaboko
Rinse whitebait to remove any dirt, add 1 sho of water to 1 sho of fish, soak from morning until evening when the water becomes cloudy. Boil down this cloudy water in a pot until it thickens to form kamaboko. Scoop it out, add kuzu starch sauce, and serve with grated wasabi according to the number of people. Though it uses a lot of fish, the yield is small.

Grilled Yuzu Citron Cakes
Remove the flesh from yuzu citrus, mix with 1 go each of wheat flour, koji powder, and rice cake flour, a little black sesame, 30 monme of miso, julienned ginger, and julienned chestnuts. Stuff the mixture back in, steam, let cool, slice into rounds, and grill.

Ten monme, one and a half go of miso well ground, one and a half go of soy sauce.
Mix these five ingredients well by grinding, press flat and dry slightly
in the sun, then bake well on both sides
in a pot and cut.

　　　Kudzu Clear Soup
Season water with roasted salt, knead kudzu starch firmly,
tear into pieces and put in. When they float up, add well-pounded
grated ginger and serve.

　　　Floating Fu (Wheat Gluten)
Knead winter-bleached rice cake flour firmly with hot water, shape into balls and
boil in hot water. Scoop up what floats, and coat with
clear sweet bean paste. Sprinkling with sugar is also good.

　　　New-Style Kaya (Japanese Nutmeg)
Remove the astringent skin of kaya nuts, grind cooked rice grains well, add salt
and coat, then cover with green seaweed as coating to make it new-style.

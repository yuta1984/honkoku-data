Cooking Recipes from Mountain and Sea Villages, Volume 3

Nambu Shellfish (Cooking Method)
Place large shavings of bonito flakes in a pot, spread kelp over them. Place abalone shell-side up on top, and simmer with sake and soy sauce over charcoal fire.

Hachiman Stems (Cooking Method)  
Remove the roots and leaves of burdock, and peel the skin from the leaf stalks.

Tare Miso (Dripping Miso)
Boil red miso with water, simmer until the saltiness is almost gone,
strain through cloth and use.

Yose-gamo (Molded Duck)
Pound and grind only the duck meat well, steam it one scoop at a time
and pour kudzu starch sauce over it.

Chichibu Dengaku
Grill tofu as regular dengaku, mix mustard with water
and apply thinly once, grill until dry, then
pour kudzu tamari over it.

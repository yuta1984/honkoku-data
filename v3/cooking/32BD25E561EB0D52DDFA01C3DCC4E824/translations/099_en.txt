Deep-Fried Wheat Gluten
Mix bean flour into raw wheat gluten, knead well, cut into appropriate pieces, and deep-fry in oil. It will not fall apart.

Wheat Gluten-Grilled Buckwheat Noodles
Grill wheat gluten with egg, cut finely and place wasabi on top. Serve with dipping sauce and roasted sake separately.

Tsuyu-ko (Steamed Mountain Yam Cake)
Grate mountain yam, add wheat flour and a little salt, add sugar, color deeply with gardenia, and steam in a steamer. Make it into stick-shaped portions. It's also good served with miso.

Sake-Soaked Grilled Fish
Cut blackfin flounder into one-bu thick pieces crosswise, soak in sake, and grill.

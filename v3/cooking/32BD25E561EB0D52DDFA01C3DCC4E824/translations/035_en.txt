　　　Ōmi Samegai Rice Cakes

Take about three shō of the finest glutinous rice per mortar and pound it. Then carefully select each grain to ensure there are no foreign substances mixed in. Steam it as usual, then pound it into rice cakes without adding water, dry them, weave them with cut straw, and hang them in a place where wind cannot reach them.

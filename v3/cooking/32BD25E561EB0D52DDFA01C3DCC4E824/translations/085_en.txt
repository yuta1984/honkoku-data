Quick-Dried Yuzu (Haya-yuu-boshi)
Remove the inside of yuzu citrus well, boil and mash tsukune-imo (a type of yam), soak kudzu, black sesame, red pepper, and glutinous millet in hot water, mix all of these together with the aforementioned yam and stuff well into the yuzu, cover with a lid and steam. Cut and use while warm. If it cools, it quickly dries out and the flavor becomes thin.

　　　Basket-Dried Fish (Kago-su-boshi)
Cut fresh bonito into pieces about the size for grilling, sprinkle with salt, sandwich between bamboo basket weave from both sides, tie in places with wire, hang on a pole with the flesh protruding as is and dry in the sun. Afterward, grill together with the basket, remove the basket and use. The basket pattern marks are considered the specialty of this preparation.

　　　Longan Wine (Ryugan-shu)
Two sho of shochu, half kin of rock sugar, one kin of longan meat (with skin and pit removed, meat only), put all of these together in a jar

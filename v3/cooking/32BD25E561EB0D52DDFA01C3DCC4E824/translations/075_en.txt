　　　Vegetarian Mock Whale Skin
Drain water from tofu, place in a box and flatten it. Layer tofu mixed with pot soot on top, flatten again, place weights on it, break the box to remove it, boil in hot water, then cut crosswise and deep-fry lightly so as not to color it too much. Sesame oil works well for frying.

　　　Sea Bream Milt
Boil sea bream milt until soft like grilled rice cake, making it foamy like bubbles.

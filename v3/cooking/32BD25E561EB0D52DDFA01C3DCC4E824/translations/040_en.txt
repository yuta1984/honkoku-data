　　　Boneless Duck
Small duck (similar to takabe horse mackerel), remove feathers and head, completely debone, stuff the cavity with ground fish paste and steam. Cut into rounds for serving.

　　　Tango Salt-Cured Fish
During winter, fillet yellowtail into three pieces, remove the pin bones from the flesh, and hang upside down in a place where the north wind hits. Occasionally sprinkle with sake, and by March-April the flesh develops a gloss. It's also good to move it to a place with light smoke.

　　　Udon Noodles
Knead udon flour with little water to make a firm dough, wrap in straw matting and knead by foot. Knead well until it becomes elastic, then boil in water. After the sixth boil, reduce the heat

　　　Snow Picking (Yuki Midori)
Whether using sea bream or pike conger, soak in salt water and when the flesh becomes white, flake the flesh and use for ground preparations. Thin with second-grade white dashi stock. The flesh becomes exceptionally white. Season according to taste preference.

　　　Blanched Shrimp
Remove shells from fresh shrimp, remove the red portions and place in water. When the flesh becomes plump, boil briefly and use with black sesame and roasted salt.

　　　Oribe Miso
Black sesame 15 monme, poppy seeds 10 monme, Japanese nutmeg seeds 15 monme, ginger 7 monme chopped in two colors, ground red pepper 1 monme, Japanese pepper 7 monme, sugar 15 monme, miso 30 monme. Thin the above with sake.

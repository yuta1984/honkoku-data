　　　　　　Kamaboko (Fish Cake) Tradition

One　Fillet sea bream to one side, scrape and take the upper flesh, grind very well
　　in a mortar, add a little egg white with mirin and sake boiled and cooled, season with salt.
　　Same procedure for any fish. Sea bream should preferably be small sea bream



　　　　　　Hollow Tofu Tradition

One　Cut one block of fresh tofu and put in a deep stone bowl, return to water, place fire
　　on wire mesh, use while rotating the tofu in water, roast on all four sides, wrap tofu in confection
　　kelp, tie with thin rope, boil well in large pot for one day until the shape
　　becomes as desired, then season with mirin and soy sauce



　　　　　　Mock Tofu Tradition

One　Boil fresh tofu thoroughly and crumble, place on bamboo strainer, season with mirin and soy
　　sauce, then oil a square pot with sesame oil, add the tofu and boil well,
　　cover with lid and place weight on top, leave overnight then cut



　　　　　　Udon Tofu・Somen Tofu Tradition

One　Wrap tofu in cloth and drain water well, strain through fine sieve, add kudzu powder

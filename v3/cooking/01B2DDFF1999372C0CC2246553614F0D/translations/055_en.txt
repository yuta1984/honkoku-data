【Kakeme】
When dismantling a whale, the back skin is cut into five-sun squares and offered to the fishing deity. This is called "kakeme."

【Kai】
This is a diagram of the male whale's kai. The penis is usually hidden inside the opening and not visible. The area around the opening is called "kai." The length of the opening is about one shaku five sun. Above is the navel, below is the anus.

【Kai】
This is a diagram of the female whale's kai. This refers to the genital opening. There are nipples on the left and right. The navel and anus are the same as in males.

【Kai Red Meat】
The shape of the genital opening is not different from humans. The actual part is called "hina," and deep inside there is a bone called "ring bone."

【Cutting Ritual】
When dismantling a whale, two one-shaku squares of back skin are cut and sent to the harpooners. This is called "cutting ritual."

【Mountain Office Affairs】
When dismantling a whale, one piece each of one-shaku square, two-shaku square, and three-shaku square are cut from the "shiki" skin and sent to the small storehouse. "Shiki skin" refers to the underside of the belly. This is called "mountain office affairs."

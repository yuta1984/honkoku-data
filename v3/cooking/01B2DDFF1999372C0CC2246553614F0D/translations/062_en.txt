**Sōgai Boats (Net Boats)** - 100-koku capacity. 6 boats per group. Length: 4 jō, width: 1 jō 2 feet. 8 oars. Crew of 10 people.

**Net** - 19 tan of net per boat (however, 1 tan measures 18 fathoms square).

**Mast** - Length: 2 jō 5 feet.

**Rudder** - Length: 1 jō 4 feet.

**Anchor** - 3 anchors per boat (however, each anchor weighs 17 kanme).

**Thorn Rope** - 3 bundles per boat.

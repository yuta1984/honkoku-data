Agikone (Jaw bone)　Found on both sides of the beak of zatō-nagasu whales. This is the meat inside
　　　the une skin. White and hard. Similar to mawashi. Not used
　　　raw.
○Cut the salt-cured meat like ito-zukuri, rinse in water,
　good for mustard mixing.
○Prepare as above, good for vinegar miso mixing.

Nodowami (Throat ring meat)　This is the pharynx. The bone is white and harder than kabura bone. The meat
　　　　is red and also hard. In rural areas, it serves as
　　　　food for servants. It is low-grade.
○Cut and use with soy sauce.

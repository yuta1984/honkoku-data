Head (kabachi) bone　　　This is the skull bone. There is one in number.
Rengi bone　　There is one next to the skull bone.
Suribachi bone　There is one section next to the rengi bone.
Lantern (chōchin) bone　　　There are five sections next to the suribachi bone.
Ate bone　　　There are fourteen sections next to the lantern bone.
Large bone　　　　There are eleven sections next to the ate bone.
Round-cut bone　　　There are thirteen sections next to the large bone.
Grip-tube (nigiri-tsutsuro) bone　　There are thirteen sections next to the round-cut bone.
Beak (hashi) bone　　　　This is the lower jaw bone. There are two in number.
Chest (muna) bone　　　　Located next to the mouth of the throat ring. About three shaku long. About one
　　　　　　shaku in circumference. Attached horizontally. There is one in number.
Turtle bone　　　　There is one in the upper part of the chest.
Ear bone　　　　There are two at the bottom of the ears, left and right. Also called ear stones.
Abdominal bone　　　These are the abdominal bones. There are twenty-eight on the left and right sides.

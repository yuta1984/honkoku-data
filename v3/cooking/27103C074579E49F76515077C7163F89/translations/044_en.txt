Cook matsutake mushrooms in miso soup. For about ten cups of broth,
grate half a portion of ginger, add it together with sake lees to the miso soup
and boil. One boil is sufficient.


Suhama Eggs

Make bag-shaped eggs, clamp between three bamboo sticks, and boil in hot water.
Making them in wrapped form follows the same method.


Seaweed Scraps Grilling

Fillet fresh bonito into three pieces, apply fire using seaweed scraps
as fuel, fold against wood, sprinkle with salt, press with
a knife to remove blood, add sake, and use.


Millet Yuzu Dried

Add a little flour to miso and soy sauce, soak glutinous millet
and steam it in this manner.

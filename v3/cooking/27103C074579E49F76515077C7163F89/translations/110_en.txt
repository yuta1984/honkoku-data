Perilla Seed Sake

Add one go of perilla seeds to one sho of sake, put in a cloth
bag, place in the sake, and remove after three days for use.

Salted Fish Entrails Broth

Wash any kind of salted fish entrails and boil to extract liquid,
strain the cloudiness and let cool before use. A substitute for bonito broth.

Ukiyo Udon

Add two or three eggs to one sho of wheat flour, add salt,
knead firmly with sake, knead well by treading and roll out.
Kudzu is good. If not kudzu, make cut noodles. What people call egg
udon is this.

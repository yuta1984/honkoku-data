Walnut Vinegar

For walnuts, grind the walnuts well, dissolve with vinegar,
and strain through a water bag before use.

Deep Mountain Mushrooms

Boil large shimeji mushrooms, place green miso inside the caps,
and pour strained egg over them. Good as an accompaniment to flat dishes or
grilled dishes.

Pale Pink Plum

Take only the flesh of boiled plums, add plenty of sugar,
add a little wheat flour, and knead over distant heat in a thin-bottomed pot,
then add cracked plum pits.

Teika Kamaboko

Make fish paste soft, bring white water to a boil in a pot,
scoop in the fish paste as desired and boil, then make kudzu sauce

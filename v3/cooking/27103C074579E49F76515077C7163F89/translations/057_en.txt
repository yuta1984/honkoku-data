Add salt strongly to the sake lees and pickle as is. Wash the flattened sake lees, press them flat, and cut into pieces about 5 bu (approximately 1.5cm) for use.

Early Sansho Buds
Boil the sansho pepper seeds to the extent that they could be sown. If the result is poor, it is good to put sprouts in a cultivation room. This produces buds year-round.

First Tai (Sea Bream)
Hang fresh sea bream with its tail fin using string, sprinkle salt well all over, then remove the string.

Semimaru-yaki (Cicada Maru Grilling)
Wash abalone thoroughly with the shell, pierce in various places with a skewer suitable for hanging, add soy sauce, remove the cooked shellfish from the shell, cut and use.

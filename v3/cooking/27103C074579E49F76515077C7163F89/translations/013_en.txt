【Right Page】
　　Shrimp Kane
Pound Ise lobster meat and make it into a paste, add various
seasonings inside and form it together, fry in oil, add
green onions and simmer.
　　Shimofuri (Frost-covered)
Whether using chrysanthemum leaves or vegetable leaves, attach ground fish paste
to them, put in hot water to float, and use in clear soup.

【Left Page】
　　Echizen Stir-mixed Salad
Add a little soy sauce to vinegar, cut daikon radish into large pieces and
simmer. Grill fish well and chop finely,
then add to the above mixture. Remove from heat - consider this a delicious warm
namasu salad.

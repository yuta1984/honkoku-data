Quick-Dried Yuzu

Remove the flesh of yuzu well, grind yam-like tubers in hot water,
soak kudzu, black sesame, red pepper, mochi, and chestnuts in hot water,
mix all of these together with the aforementioned tubers and stuff well
into the yuzu, cover and steam. While warm,
slice and use. When cooled, it is best to grill over fire.


　　　Basket-Dried Fish

Cut raw fish into pieces like grilled yuzu, sprinkle with salt, sandwich
between basket mesh from top and bottom, tie in places with wire, and with the flesh
protruding as is, hang on poles and dry in the sun.
Later, grill together with the basket, remove the basket and use.
The basket pattern marks are considered desirable.


　　　Longan Wine

Two sho of shochu, half kin of grated daikon, one to (one kin) of longan with skin
removed. Put all of these together into a jar

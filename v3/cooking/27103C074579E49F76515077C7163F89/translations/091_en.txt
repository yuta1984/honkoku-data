　　　Kitsuki Natto

One sho of black soybeans, boil to the consistency of miso. Roast one sho of barley and
grind finely. However, wheat is also good. Steam the aforementioned
soybeans and let rest for seven days, then dry for about half a day
to cool. Boil three go of salt with six go of water and let cool well,
knead this together and pound firmly into a jar. After seven days,
take out and pound three times. When storing, add mountain pepper, fresh
shiso leaves, etc.


　　　Assorted Mixed Dishes

Pear with mustard dressing, carrot with black dressing, mushroom with white dressing.
Like these dressed dishes, serve arranged in three colors separately.

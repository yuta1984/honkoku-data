Botamochi (Sweet Rice Cake)

One cup of wheat flour, half cup of kudzu starch, a little rice flour, grated yam
to make it as firm as rice, one cup full, knead softly with appropriate amount of sugar,
sprinkle black sesame seeds on a cloth, pour the mixture
on top and steam. Soak in water for half a day, which is good. Let it
cool. Cut into shapes according to preference.


Duck Turnip

Cut turnip flatly, hollow out the inside and stuff with duck
meat and fatty skin, cover with turnip and steam,
then pour kudzu sauce over it.

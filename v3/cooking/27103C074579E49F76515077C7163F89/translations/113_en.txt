Eggplant Miso
Chop eggplants and pickle them in first-grade soy sauce with
plenty of koji added. Pickle in the seventh or eighth month and use around the frost month (November).

Pickled Plums
Add five go of salt to one sho of plums, apply weight and frequently
turn them upside down. When the plum vinegar rises to the top,
sun-dry the plums for one and a half days, then put them in a jar
and sprinkle salt on top. Using the aforementioned plum vinegar, remove the plum skins
and add them, sprinkle over two go of roasted salt, and place shiso leaves on top
after washing them well and sun-drying until all moisture is gone.
Place this over the aforementioned plums like a lid, sprinkle two go of salt
on top, and gently press down with chopsticks.
[Illegible section] may be added, but remove moisture with anything
so there is no water content, and pickle under the shiso leaves.

Pick plenty of flowers and put them in, and when they are about the volume of the flowers,
press them down to about 3-4 bu. Cut what has been pressed to 3-4 bu
into good-sized pieces, wrap each piece in finest Mino paper,
then pickle in red miso from above, place light weights on top,
and take out one package at a time as needed for use. However, after picking the flowers
and salting them, press firmly with heavy weights,
and discard the salt water that comes out
several times


Mitsu-yama-zuke (Three Mountain Pickle)

Prepare vinegared mackerel like sashimi, cut the flesh into pieces about 4-5 bu thick,
cut the head thinly, put them together with mackerel,
salt, koji, and three colors, portioning them separately in equal amounts,
then mix them together, add aged sake and
pickle

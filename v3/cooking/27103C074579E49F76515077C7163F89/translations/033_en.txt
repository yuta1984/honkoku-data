Four Seasons Mushrooms
Dig up dried matsutake mushrooms, burn straw and let it die out, then arrange the mushrooms there, cover with a mat, and leave for a while before using. They will become like fresh mushrooms. The same applies to shiitake mushrooms. They will be like freshly picked throughout the four seasons.

Chimaki (Rice Dumplings)
Shape hayazushi (quick sushi) into chimaki form and wrap in bamboo grass leaves as is.

Nanzen-ji Temple Sansho Pepper
Remove the seeds from dried sansho pepper and soak in water to remove the bitterness. Grind white miso well and mix it thick, coat the sansho thoroughly, dry it well, and place it on a roasting pan.

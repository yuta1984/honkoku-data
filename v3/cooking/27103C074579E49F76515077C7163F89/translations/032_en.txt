Potato Pickles
Whether long yam or mountain yam, peel and boil them, wrap in paper and pickle in pickled vegetables. After three days they will be ready.

Yuzu Dengaku
Dip yuzu peel in soy sauce, grill it, and sprinkle with poppy seeds.

Tsumuki Soup
Cook tsumuki finely and mix it into miso to make soup. Green seaweed should be boiled as appropriate.

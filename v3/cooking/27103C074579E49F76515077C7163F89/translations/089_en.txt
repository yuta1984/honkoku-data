　　　Grilled Whitebait

Arrange whitebait on a net and grill them, turning them over together.
The grilled fish will not separate. When grilled appropriately,
put them together in water and the fish will separate.


　　　Flower Matsutake

Remove the caps of matsutake mushrooms, cut only the stems to about six sun in length,
dry them, and in spring, simmer them in the shape of flowers,
then cut into small rounds and use.


　　　Brocade Sea Bream

Take a live medium sea bream with scales intact, remove the gills and intestines, peel the skin
from the belly side in one piece without tearing it,
keep it attached to the flesh on the belly side, do the same for both front and back,
then boil salted water hot and cook the aforementioned
sea bream in it.

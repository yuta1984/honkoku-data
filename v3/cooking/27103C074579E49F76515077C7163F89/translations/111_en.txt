Insu Soup

Add miso, daikon radish cut into rounds, and trout to make a soup.
This trout is not the red-fleshed trout, but a type of algae fish,
about one to two sun in size per person. It is caught in pine seaweed.
There are three varieties of this trout: "hata" and "white trout" which
are the finest quality, followed by black trout and yellow trout.
Details are provided in "Zoku Sankai-go" (Continued Mountains and Seas).

Quick Water Shield

Take the curled leaves of mountain yam and such, dissolve kudzu and
coat them well, put in boiling water, and immediately remove. It is like
water shield (junsai).

1. First grade: Stock - color is black
2. Second grade: Stock - color is the same
3. Third grade: Stock - color is white
As noted above, no matter how delicious and nutritious the dishes may be, they all use these three types of stock as their foundation. Therefore, they are divided into three grades as categories.

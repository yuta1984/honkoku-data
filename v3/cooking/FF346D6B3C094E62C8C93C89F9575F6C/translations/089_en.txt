**○Item No. 105　Apple Pie (Appuru Pai) [Name of confection] — Ingredients**

6 large apples; pastry dough prepared according to Item No. 103: 1 kin; sugar; cloves (chōji); finely sliced yuzu peel

**Method for the Above**

Peel the apples and remove the seeds and any hard parts (the core). Cut each apple into quarters and place them in a pastry dish. Scatter sugar, yuzu peel, and cloves over the top, then lay the pastry dough from Item No. 103 over everything, as if forming a lid, and bake. Baking time: three-quarters of one hour (approximately 45 minutes).

**○Item No. 106　Pudding [Dried Persimmon] — Ingredients**

Dried persimmon (hoshigaki): 2 kin; Preparation from Item No. 104: 2 kin; Eggs: 4

**Method for the Above**

Chop the persimmons finely. Break the eggs into a bowl and mix well, then add the chopped persimmons.

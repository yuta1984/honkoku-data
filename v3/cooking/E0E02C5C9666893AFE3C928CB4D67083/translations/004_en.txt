Method for grinding and using mugwort
Grind mugwort roots and add glutinous rice into it. Roll this into balls and deep-fry in oil.
It can be used for anything.

About preparing bamboo shoots (imitation)
For this, tofu is fine, yam is fine,
kudzu potato is fine, lily bulb is fine.
Grind anything well and add kudzu starch, add wasanbon sugar and grind well, spread on paper,
boil it, then roll it diagonally. It will look like bamboo shoots. Cut this in the bamboo shoot cutting style.

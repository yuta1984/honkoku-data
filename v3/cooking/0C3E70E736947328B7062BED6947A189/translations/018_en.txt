　(18) Senbei　Place two round metal tools in a crossed
arrangement and attach handles like scissors, sandwich small amounts of mochi

【Left Page】
　(19) Sugar Senbei　Add two gō of white
sugar to one shō of wheat flour, knead smoothly with water, sandwich
bit by bit with the above metal tools and bake,
and it will become the size of the metal tools.
　(20) Samegawa-mochi　Select Chinese glutinous rice grain
by grain, make into mochi and roll out, dry in shade for one day and one night

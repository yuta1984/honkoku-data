　(47) Kinton Mochi　Take sugar-preserved glutinous rice made into jōhaku
grade, select grain by grain, grind into powder and strain through silk sieve,
knead with hot water, cut into small pieces, flatten, wrap white sugar
in the center, form into rounds, bring water to boil and simmer, when they float
to the surface, 【place on a round tray】 dust with kinako or

　(48) Yaki-mochi　Take jōhaku grade non-glutinous rice, select grain
by grain, grind into powder, sift through silk sieve, knead with hot water, cook azuki beans
well with a little salt added, mash them, take the above kneaded mixture,
press into small flat pieces, wrap the azuki beans in the center, and bake
in a flat pan.
　(49) Chestnut Flour Mochi　Roast chestnuts, remove the outer skin

Ancient and Modern Famous Confections Secret Recipe Collection
　(1) Aruhei-tō (Hard candy)　Superior grade / Use one kin of rock sugar,
wash repeatedly and discard [the wash water].
Add two shō of water to one shō of sugar, boil until the sugar dissolves
to the proper degree, strain through silk, then boil down and with a ladle
scoop a little, cool in water, spread thinly,
when it becomes firm and breaks crisply, apply walnut
oil to a flat copper pot, transfer [the mixture] into it, and cool the pot's sides with water

　(97) Method for kaya mochi (Japanese torreya seed rice cake): Roast one shō of kaya seeds well and pound them,
sift through an Arima sieve so that no dregs remain,
combine five gō of glutinous rice flour and five gō of white sugar with the above kaya powder
together, spread thinly in a steamer and steam.
When well steamed, pound in a mortar, sprinkle rice cake flour on top
of a cutting board, roll out with a rolling pin to about five bu thick,
let cool slightly, then cut.

【Left Page】
　(98) Kuzu-yaki (grilled kudzu): One shō of kudzu, one kin of white sugar, water
one shō three gō - mix the above and knead. When the timing is good, remove and use fine
kudzu powder as dusting flour, roll out like making udon,
cut to desired thickness. Can also be made into squares.
Apply sansho miso and grill.

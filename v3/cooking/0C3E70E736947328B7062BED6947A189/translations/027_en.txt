　(43) Sugar Porridge　Like the above utsura-yaki, make sugar-
preserved rice into mochi, cut into small pieces, wrap bean paste in the center,
shape like utsura-yaki and【without baking】sprinkle kinako (roasted soybean flour)
on top.
　(44) Powder-dusted Mochi　As above, make sugar-preserved rice into
mochi, cut into small pieces, form into rounds, and wrap bean paste
on top.

【Left Page】
　(45) Mitsukara　Take high-quality kelp, let it sit for three and a half hours,
cut into small squares, wrap Asakura sansho pepper inside,
cut thin kelp into strips and tie with them,
then dry in the sun until it becomes white and beautiful.
　(46) Corn Mochi　Select corn grain
by grain, remove the outer skin, grind the inner kernel into powder,
sift through silk sieve, one sho of powder to one sho of mochi powder

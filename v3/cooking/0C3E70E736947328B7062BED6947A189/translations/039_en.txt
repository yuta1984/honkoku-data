　(77) Muki-mochi (Peeled Rice Cake)　Seven gō of barley flour, three
gō of glutinous rice flour, fifty monme of white sugar, add two gō of water and simmer slightly then cool,
knead the above flour with the simmered sugar to make mochi, then
boil and pound together.
　(78) Owari Mochi　Take the finest quality glutinous rice and pound it very
white, wash well with water, dry, beat in a mortar,
sift through a silk sieve, then with water make rice

　(31) Mame-ame　Select white soybeans one grain at a time,
roast in a pot, remove the skins and pound in a wooden mortar,
sift through silk, knead with liquid candy,
and finish like suhama confection.

【Left Page】
　(32) Ken-hin　Add three gō five shaku of white sugar
to one shō of wheat flour, chop peeled walnuts to about five shaku,
roast black sesame seeds and add five shaku, mix soy sauce and water in
equal parts and knead until moist, grease a flat copper pot with
walnut oil, bake while flipping, cool slightly,
then cut into various shapes.
　(33) Fu-no-yaki　Knead wheat with water until smooth

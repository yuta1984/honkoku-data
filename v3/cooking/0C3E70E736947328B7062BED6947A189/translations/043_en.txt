　(87) Kesaina Mochi - How to prepare the bean paste filling
For one pumpkin, one
kin of sugar and six gō of water. Remove both the skin and
seeds from the pumpkin and boil it. Boil it until it can be
easily cut with chopsticks.
Then remove it and squeeze it with cloth,
mash it very thoroughly and press out the moisture.
As described above, add sugar and water and knead in a pot.

　(80) Method for Sansho Mochi　One and a half kin of white sugar, two gō of red
miso ground well, one shō of glutinous rice flour
made fine, pound these three ingredients together, place thinly
in a steamer, steam well, then pound together well, and add
sansho pepper powder. The proper consistency is tested while still warm—
when it feels slightly firm, that is the right consistency.

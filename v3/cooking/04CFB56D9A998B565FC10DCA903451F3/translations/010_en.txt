Fish Island Culinary Guide

〇Method for Making Seasoned Sake (Irizake)
Old sake 3 sho, soy sauce 5 go
Bonito flakes (shaved and quickly rinsed with water) 1 sho
Simmer these three ingredients over charcoal fire
Reduce to 7 go (approximately 2 sho 3-4 go), then when removing from heat, add a little vinegar

〇Method for Quick Seasoned Sake
Old sake 3 sho, soy sauce 1 sho
Vinegar 5 go - simmer these three ingredients over charcoal fire
When it comes to a boil, remove from heat
Cool, then put back on fire and simmer
Use in three separate batches

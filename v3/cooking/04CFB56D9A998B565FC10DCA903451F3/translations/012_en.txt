○Tempura
Take grey mullet or egg fish paste, deep-fry it, and serve with dashi soy sauce, green onions, and grated daikon - this is what we call tempura. Now we call any fish coated in batter and deep-fried tempura. Coating means dusting with wheat flour and deep-frying.

○Chicken egg cooking
Take whole bamboo shoots, remove the inner nodes, crack and mix eggs, pour into the bamboo, seal the opening and steam, remove skin and simmer, cut into rounds

○Pour eggs into the holes of boiled lotus root, seal openings with dumplings, simmer, and cut for use

○Pine mushrooms
Turn opened caps upward, put eggs inside and place over fire. When eggs are cooked, apply soy sauce to the top of the mushroom and grill, cut to proper size and use as appetizer

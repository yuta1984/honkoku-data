▲How to make dried sea cucumber. This would be done when sea cucumber is cheap. Cut the sea cucumber lengthwise, remove the innards without washing, boil in plain water for about two bubbling periods, then add enough sake to thoroughly penetrate the sea cucumber and boil for another two bubbling periods. Remove, skewer, and dry in the sun - this will become like golden sea cucumber (kinko).

　　　How to Use Uchi-gai (Shellfish)

First, take fifty or one hundred noshi shells, wash them thoroughly with water all at once, cut them to a length of six or seven sun, and soak them in water again. However, if you mix in a little shochu and let them soak for about half an hour, the noshi shells will turn amber-colored and become tender.

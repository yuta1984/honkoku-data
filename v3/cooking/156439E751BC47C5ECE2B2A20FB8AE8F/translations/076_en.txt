First, prepare charcoal fire in a brazier, place a metal grill over it, and on top of that
prepare a straight-grain board of finest red cedar, then take hot
hard-boiled eggs, remove the shells, mix sake and soy sauce
half and half, soak the aforementioned hard-boiled eggs, then cut vertically into three pieces, and separately crack eggs
into a bowl and mix well, put the cut hard-boiled egg
pieces into this, pick them up with a thin skewer with the egg liquid attached,

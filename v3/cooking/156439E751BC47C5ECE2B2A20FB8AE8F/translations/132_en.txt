　　　Green Abalone Preparation
1. Take the kudzu-pounded abalone described previously, immediately cut it thinly crosswise, coat it with kudzu powder, lay straw in a steamer, arrange the thinly cut abalone, and steam it. After taking it out, handle it with bamboo skewers. However, lightly cook it through, place it in the upper tier to dry, and occasionally rearrange it so it doesn't get overcooked, training it to become straight. This preparation method is good for any use.

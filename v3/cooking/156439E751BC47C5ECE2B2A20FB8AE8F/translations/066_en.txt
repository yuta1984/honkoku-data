　　　Method for Hot Water Eggs
First, crack about two duck eggs and about ten fresh chicken eggs, beat them together well, heat oil in a pot, dissolve about one and a half small cups of high-quality kuzu starch powder in water and add it to this, also add a little aged sake, beat the eggs and scoop up the foam with a ladle, adding one ladle at a time.

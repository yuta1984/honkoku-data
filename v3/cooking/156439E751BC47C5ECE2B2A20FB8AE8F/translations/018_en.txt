Method for Simmered Bird
One: Simmer wild goose or duck well in light miso, then add soy sauce on top and include base katsuobushi (bonito flakes) before serving.

Method for Dove Habushi-ni (Bone-in Simmering)
One: Whether dove or any other bird, crush the bones thoroughly and add them, put in hana-katsuobushi (shaved bonito flakes), mix sake and soy sauce in equal parts with little broth, simmer until it bubbles slightly. In another pot, warm wasabi miso and put the meat in it. It tastes good either as is or poured over rice.

Method for Bird Kokusho (Concentrated Miso Preparation)
One: Grind miso well, thin with water, mix in one-third sake, finely chop the bird and mix well. Use equal parts bird and miso, but make the miso thick enough, put in a pot and simmer over charcoal fire. Adding sansho pepper is also good. In any case, it should be according to the cook's judgment. However, stir with chopsticks while it's boiling before serving.

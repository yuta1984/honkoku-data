　Egg Section
　　Method for Making Golden Thread Eggs
Take the white of an egg and strain it through half-paper. Add a little gold leaf powder. Mix gently with a bamboo skewer. Then boil water in a flat pan and cook using double-boiling method with a narcissus pan. For this, grease the bottom of the pan with walnut oil.

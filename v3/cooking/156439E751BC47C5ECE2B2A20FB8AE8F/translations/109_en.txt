　　　Method for Yamabuki (Mountain Rose) Kamaboko
First, similarly, one rice bowl full of ground fish paste. Twenty hard-boiled egg yolks. Add these one by one as well and grind thoroughly. Then add three raw eggs, also adding these one by one and grinding together. As before, apply a little heat and steam. The usage is also the same as before. Good for gifts as well. [Illustration] Also, chikuwa and yokogumo are excellent items. Their usage is also the same as before.

　　　Method for Five-Color Kamaboko
First, prepare this also in the same manner as before, coloring in blue, yellow, red, white, and black. However, for blue, use powdered green seaweed mixed with beaten greens, briefly pour hot water over them, and grind the green seaweed mixture appropriately. For black, it is good to grind and mix charcoal from a pot that has no ash residue.

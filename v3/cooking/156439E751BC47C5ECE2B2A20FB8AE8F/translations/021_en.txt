Method for Pheasant Mountain Shadow Soup
Cut the pheasant in any manner, add plenty of sake, use low heat, and cook until the sake smell disappears. Then add water, bring to a boil, add a little salt, and when the broth becomes light, add a little soy sauce - no additional stock is needed. You may also fry the innards separately and finish with yuzu vinegar.

Method for Salt-Cured Bird and Braised Bird
Remove salt thoroughly from the salt-cured bird, cut into large pieces, and cook. Put a little bird fat in the pot, add plenty of sake at the beginning of cooking, add a little soy sauce and simmer, then add the salt-cured bird and cook. Add red clams and grilled wheat gluten to this, along with crushed sansho pepper and finely chopped kaya nuts - this makes an excellent dish.

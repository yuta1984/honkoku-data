　　　Okara Scrambled Egg Method
First, crack however many eggs and beat them roughly together. Line the bottom of a pot with about two pieces of grilled tofu and cook. Gently pour the beaten eggs over this, then cook slowly over low heat. Take fresh wakame seaweed, dry it over fire, immediately chop it finely, and sift it through a fine sieve. Then put the eggs in a serving dish, sprinkle this wakame powder over it, add light soy sauce, and top with okara before serving. This is a secret recipe from a certain tea master. Okara neutralizes the toxicity of eggs. However, do not use the tofu at the bottom of the pot.

　　　Method for Egg and Karasumi (Dried Mullet Roe) Mix
One: Use sawara (Spanish mackerel) roe, which refers to karasumi (dried mullet roe). Dry the thin skin of this into dried goods, then grate it finely with wasabi grater in small pieces, and it will become fine. Put this in a bowl. Next, make hard-boiled eggs from sufficiently small eggs, remove the shells while hot, and it's good to add a little white sugar and sake to mizuame (starch syrup). Add these eggs and stir, then mix into the karasumi above, coat well, and serve in small dishes. This is exceptionally good.

　　　Method for Egg Soboro (Crumbled Egg) Mix
One: For this, take out the flesh of kunenbō (a type of citrus) or pumpkin, remove the seeds and thin skin, and put in a bowl.

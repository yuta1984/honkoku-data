　　　Method for Miso-Pickled Dace

First, river-flowing dace is good, and if in summer, it is exceptionally good. Remove the scales and wash well, lightly grill it, at this time pour sake over it about twice, grill it without burning and let it cool, remove the skewer, wrap each dace individually in half-paper, put it into the above miso, press it down firmly. If in summer, about two days; if in winter, about five days is good. For use, take it out, grill it again with fire, and sprinkle grated ice on top.

　　　Method for Arare Squid

First, wash the squid well and cut it into squares of about one bu (3mm), place it on kudzu powder, and gently tap it with a pestle, coating it with kudzu. Then add half water and half sake with a little salt and boil. When this mixture comes to a boil, add the above squid, after a short while remove it and put it in water to cool. Methods of use: first for soup, second for sashimi, third for second clear soup, fourth for suimono (clear soup), fifth for dressed dishes and plum kernel miso dressing - exceptionally good.

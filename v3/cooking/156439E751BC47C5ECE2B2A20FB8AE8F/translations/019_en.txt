Method for Takano Sashigiri-dori (Finely Chopped Bird for Hawking)
One: Finely chop both meat and bones of wild goose or duck, pour the collected blood as is over the bone and meat, mix well, add intestines and feathers whole, add 2.5 go of salt to 5 go of bird, knead well by hand, put in a jar, after about ten days take out and add koji (rice malt). Since blood is added, put in 7 go of koji, rub off the koji bloom well, roast the koji dregs to a burnt color, grind in a mortar, combine with the bloom, mix well into the bird, add aged sake, pound again until it reaches the consistency of soy sauce broth, put back in jar, seal the mouth so no air enters, and it will be ready after about eight days.

　　　Method for Wrapped Steamed Eggs
First, take daikon stems or vegetable stems, and finely
chop them as they are, with their pickling liquid as is, add roasted black sesame seeds, grate fresh
ginger and add a little, then put in a pot and roast over charcoal fire.
When the aforementioned stems are well cooked, crack fresh eggs one by one
into it, wrap the eggs with the aforementioned stems, fan the charcoal fire
but not too vigorously. Roast for about half an hour. Then
put a little of the stems in a medium dish or tea bowl, place the aforementioned eggs
on top, and again place stems on top of that before serving. Also excellent
for tea cuisine.

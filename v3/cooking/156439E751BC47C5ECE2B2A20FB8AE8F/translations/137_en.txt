Method for Making Genji Lotus
1. During bamboo shoot season, take a large madake bamboo, remove the inner nodes, boil well, cool in water and let it cool thoroughly. Cut the bamboo shoot vertically, flatten it out, and scrape away the inner flesh with a thin blade. Apply egg white to that area, sprinkle with wheat flour, and also sprinkle with kanazarashi flour. Then take a sufficiently large lotus root, boil it in hot water, let it cool, peel the outer skin up to the edges of the holes, cut it to about the length of the bamboo shoot. Also sprinkle this lotus root generously with egg white and wheat flour, wrap it inside the aforementioned bamboo shoot, mix wheat flour and kanazarashi flour with egg white to make a thick paste for the seam, join them together well, tie the top with straw, simmer in light soy sauce with a little sake added, then cut into rounds.

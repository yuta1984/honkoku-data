One: Wash kazunoko (herring roe) well with water and cut into pieces about 3 to 5 bu (9-15mm) long. Cut these thinly. Mix salt with a little sake and soy sauce, rub the kazunoko well with this mixture once, then squeeze out the liquid thoroughly. Next, grind high-quality white miso well and mix in the kazunoko. Crumble hard-boiled egg yolks finely and add more than half of this to the miso mixture, mixing well. However, adding a little red pepper powder makes it much better.

　　　Method for Egg Rice Porridge (Zōsui)
One: Wash about 5 gō (900ml) of white rice well, then prepare high-quality white miso soup slightly thin, adjusting by observation as you simmer. For the eggs to add to this, when the rice porridge is cooked about 80%, do not beat the eggs but crack fresh, high-quality eggs directly in, being careful not to break the yolks. For seasoning, add roasted chestnuts cut in half, or thinly sliced burdock root cut into small rounds - either works well. Add whole eggs one per person according to the number of diners, remove from heat, let it steam well, then serve.

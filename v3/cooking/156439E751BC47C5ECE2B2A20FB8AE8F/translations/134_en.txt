Cut away the edges, and using a thin blade, carefully peel only the thick center portion very thinly. Large male abalone are quite easy to peel thinly. After finishing the peeling, roll it back up as before, and when cut crosswise into small rounds, it becomes like white thread. However, this depends on the cook's skill. For serving, use as sashimi, or red thread shellfish is most beautiful. The dyeing method is to first soak in warm water, then immerse in red liquid. But drain the liquid completely and expose to air briefly. If serving as sashimi, the front and back should be trimmed evenly. For flat plates, tea bowls, and sweet bowls. The above types remain white and look beautiful like braided thread.

　　　Sendai Roasted Shellfish Cooking Method
1. Whether using overnight shellfish or roasted shellfish, soak for five to six days, changing the water frequently, wash well, add a little sake and miso, and simmer until tender. When the color becomes red and white, remove thoroughly, wash well, shell and soak in hot water, then serve with wasabi miso or Japanese pepper miso. Also, finely chop green vegetables and cut the above shellfish into strips for a miso mixture - the flavor will be exceptionally good.

　　　Method for Nagasaki Oil Rice Cake Eggs
In Nagasaki, this is made with duck eggs, but regular eggs are also fine. Large eggs are particularly good. First, hard-boil the eggs in sake. During this time, heat sesame oil well in an oil pot. Make a small crack in the aforementioned eggs, and while continuing to boil them, when the egg contents burst out from the crack, immediately remove them and peel off the shells. Mix wheat flour with a little black sesame, coat the hard-boiled eggs with this mixture, then deep-fry them in oil. Usage methods include: serving tray dishes, flat plate dishes, confection bowl dishes, and for tea bowl dishes, serve with garlic miso, wasabi miso, or Japanese pepper miso, or use as an accompaniment for shippoku cuisine or other dishes as appropriate. Overcooking these is also bad.

The above bird combinations should be made into tea-towel bundles according to seasonal ingredients, which is good.

Method for Bird Dumplings
One: Mix aged rice flour with the above bird meat, add a little kudzu, and knead with egg white. The usage is to make them small and put them in primary dashi broth. For garnish, add either finely chopped burdock root, julienned daikon radish, or julienned shiitake mushrooms. For flavoring, add crushed pepper. Alternatively, goto miso soup is also good. Jinta miso is also acceptable.

Method for Nagasaki Bird Dengaku
One: Whether chicken, wild goose, or duck - marinate in miso for about half an hour, then remove as is and wipe off the miso well. Remove the skin and skewer the meat on bamboo sticks, brush with a little sake, and grill. Serve with either sansho pepper miso or wasabi miso, changing to green skewers before serving. Use as either a raised dish, finger food, or drinking appetizer. Gather two or three small pieces of meat together on a skewer, grill, and apply miso for good appearance. Sprinkling black sesame seeds is also good.

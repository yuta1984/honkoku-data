Arrange the abalone and steam them. Then afterward, arrange bamboo skewers on a copper grill, lightly cook them over fire, sandwich them between boards for a while, and apply pressure - this will straighten out any unevenness in the flattened flesh. If you store these in a chopstick box, it will be good. However, before steaming, skillfully scrape away the unevenness with a thin blade. Use for large inkstone lids, hand-held appetizers, pulled dishes, and other suitable presentations.

　　　Tea Towel Abalone Preparation
1. Following the same method as the rice cracker abalone above, after pounding with kudzu on a board, leave it as is. For seasonings, add whole roasted chestnuts, five or six green soybeans, and wood ear mushrooms cut into strips, placing them inside. Other ingredients as suitable. Then pinch the edges and draw them together, tie the center with gauze, place in a steamer and steam. This is also good with broth poured over it. Good for flat plate dishes, tea bowl dishes, sweets bowl dishes, clear soups, and for pulled dishes, chilled preparations are excellent. This is primarily an excellent item for tea cuisine.

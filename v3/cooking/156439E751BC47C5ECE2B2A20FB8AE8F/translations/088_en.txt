　　　Method for Cutting Eggs with Shell Intact
One: Take eggs of superior quality, soak in vinegar for about half an hour (approximately 1 hour), then boil thoroughly in the same vinegar, but cook with the pot lid removed, take out the boiled eggs, briefly dip in sake, then remove and cut with a thin blade. This will yield about fifteen slices.

　　River Fish Section

　　　Method for Cold-Season Carp
One: At the end of winter or during the cold season, select carp that are about three years old, with scales intact, prepare thin miso soup, over charcoal fire put the whole carp in and boil, when the miso soup becomes thick, add more miso soup, this time place kelp under the carp, cook as described above, when the miso soup becomes thick again, carefully lift out the carp, put it in water, after a short while, wash it to remove the miso, place on a bamboo mat and let the wind blow over it - however, when the carp is boiled and then cooled, it becomes like dried salmon

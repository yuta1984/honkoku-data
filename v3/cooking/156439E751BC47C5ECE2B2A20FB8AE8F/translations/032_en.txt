　　Method for締活卵 (Shimeji-Style Eggs)
【Illustration】This uses what is called an egg dimple pot, first adding a little egg white and cooking it. Then add a little yolk, cut kamaboko to the thickness of straw and about one sun (3cm) in length, insert it in the center and cook - this will look like shimeji mushroom stems. Remove it, attach two or three to the stem with egg white and cook - this creates something resembling shimeji mushrooms with multiple pieces attached for use. Usage is the same as before.

　　Method for Red Hard-Boiled Eggs
Hard-boil eggs as usual, remove shells, soak in hot water, and when the eggs are warmed through, soak in either fresh rouge juice or red coloring juice, cool well, then slice. When making eggs into flower shapes, while the eggs are still hot, wrap in paper and shape as desired.

　　Method for Yamabuki (Golden) Eggs
This also uses the aforementioned hard-boiled eggs, coating them with egg yolk and cooking over distant heat. When the fire is too strong, the eggs will burn and become poor quality. Skewer with thin bamboo skewers and cook. Usage methods...

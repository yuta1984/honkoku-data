　　　Method for Green Eggs
First, cut them long as in the previous method, put them in a side dish container, but for this one you should frequently adjust the shape. Also, coating them with grated ice before putting them in the side dish container is especially good.

　　　One Type of Hand-held Quail-grilled Eggs
First, remove the skin from hard-boiled eggs, cut them vertically in half, then with the cut surface down, heat a fire poker and make a brand mark on the top, then serve with grated ice on top. Usage: one appetizer dish, one portable lunch box dish, one ceremonial dish, and other suitable combinations.

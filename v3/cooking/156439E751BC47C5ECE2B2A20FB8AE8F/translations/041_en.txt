　　　Method for Yuzu Citron Egg
Take winter yuzu citron, cut about three bu from the top where the stem is attached, like regular yuzu mochi, scrape out the seeds inside with a clam shell, soak in water for a while to remove the yuzu's bitterness, then remove and dry briefly. Take fresh eggs, put a little white sugar into the aforementioned yuzu, then crack eggs on top - crack them in without breaking the yolks. However, fill the yuzu to about 80% capacity, then add black sesame seeds on top, add six or seven pickled sansho peppercorns, sprinkle a little rice flour lightly on top, put in a steamer, and steam well. Usage: as appetizer, main dish, clear soup, flat plate dishes, sashimi, confection bowls, tea bowls, heaped dishes. For sashimi, cool and cut into small rounds and stack.

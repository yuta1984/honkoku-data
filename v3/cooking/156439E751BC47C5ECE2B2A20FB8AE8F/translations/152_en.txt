How to Make Salted Ayu Fresh Again

First, no matter how heavily salted the ayu (sweetfish) is, wash it once or twice with water, then put it in coarse sand with plenty of water added, bury the ayu in plenty of sand like making sushi, and leave it for one day and night. Even the most heavily salted ayu will become like fresh fish. The usage should be at the cook's discretion according to the occasion.

How to Remove Salt from Salted Trout

First, dig a hole in moist soil, wash the salted trout well with a little water, then put it back in the original earth hole. Mix water, salt, and astringent liquid in three parts and pour plenty of this mixture over the trout. Then cover with soil on top and leave for one night, and it will become like fresh trout. Usage is the same as for fresh trout.

How to Make Three-Cup Pickle and Lees Pickle for Dried Fish

First, take any kind of dried fish, soak in water for about three days, changing the water frequently, then soak in lukewarm water for about half an hour, wipe well, and pickle in equal parts of soy sauce, sake, and vinegar mixed together. It's also good to pickle in sake lees.

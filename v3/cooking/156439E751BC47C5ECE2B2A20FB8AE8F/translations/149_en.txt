　　　How to Make Miso-Pickled Hairtail Fish

First, take a hairtail fish with sufficient width, salt it, and pickle it in miso. After about two days, remove it from the miso and immediately wipe it with paper before use. This can also be used for dai-hiki, yose-yaki dishes, appetizers, and clear soup in various ways. Sashimi is the premier tea cuisine preparation.

　　　How to Make Miso-Pickled Ark Shell

First, remove the shell, quickly take out the innards so the blood doesn't drain away, and pickle in miso. The pickling method is like regular sushi, pressing down layer by layer. After about three days have passed, remove and wash off the miso well before use. Used for sashimi, vinegared salad, fresh arrangement, small dish items, clear soup, clear broth, Gotō miso, thin sauce, kuzu dishes, rich soy sauce dishes, and various other applications as appropriate.

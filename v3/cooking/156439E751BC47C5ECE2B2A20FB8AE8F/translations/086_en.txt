Dilute the strained flour with the dashi broth, simmer this, crack eggs in carefully so the yolks don't break, and cook. For seasonings, add diced dried turnip, julienned suizenji nori seaweed, roasted chestnuts, and ginkgo nuts, add a little soy sauce, and steam with the sauce poured over. Adding kudzu starch sauce is also good. In spring, adding peeled udo (Japanese spikenard) rounds and things like horsetail shoots makes it exceptionally good.

　　　Method for Potato-wrapped Eggs
One: Boil long yam well and let it cool, remove the outer skin, crush the white part on a cutting board, and mince it well with a knife like ground fish paste. Then make a thin omelet, spread a little egg mixture on top of this, sprinkle a little kudzu flour, then spread the long yam on top of this, press down, and roll it up tightly. Also brush the edges with a little egg mixture, sprinkle kudzu flour as well, seal it well, wrap the top with cloth, roll it tight, and tie it. This steams quickly. How to use is the same as before. There are many ways to use any of these.

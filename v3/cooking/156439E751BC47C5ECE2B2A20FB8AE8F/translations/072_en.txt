　　　Method for Bamboo Shoot Simmered Eggs
First, boil bamboo shoots well, then using a thin knife from the base of the bamboo shoot, hollow out the interior of the bamboo shoot, removing it thoroughly.
Crack eggs and beat them well, then pour into the hollowed bamboo shoot. Then sprinkle kudzu powder at the opening, wrap in thick paper, and tie securely. Steam well and remove. The usage is the same as before. Especially good as an insertion for flat plate dishes.

　　　Method for Burdock Root Simmered Dish  
First, cut large burdock roots into pieces about 3-4 sun long, wash the outer skin well with water, boil in water with a little vinegar added, and remove.
Then insert a round skewer between the skin and flesh, inserting it slowly and carefully. Then insert a thin knife and remove the inner flesh. Layer about two sheets of thick paper, tie one end securely, put a little kudzu powder at the bottom, crack eggs,
beat them well, and pour in like the bamboo shoot simmered dish. Also sprinkle kudzu powder at the top opening, and cover well with paper.

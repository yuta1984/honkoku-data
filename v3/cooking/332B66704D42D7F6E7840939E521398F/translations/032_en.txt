Spring Rolls　Boil bamboo shoots well and cut into various shapes
　　　abalone, small birds, fish cake, fan shell, eggs《marginal note: grilled wheat gluten》
　　　bracken, sagara seaweed; put these ingredients in and simmer
　　　in dashi with soy sauce, which is good; also remove bamboo shoot nodes, put fish cake
　　　in the center, simmer and cut to add
Nozuheidō　Prepare duck like seared chicken, simmer in dashi
　　　with soy sauce; when it comes to a boil, add seasoned vinegar
　　　mixed together; dissolve wheat flour in dashi until it becomes sticky
　　　and add; serve when it comes to a boil; snipe and woodcock, as well as quail work too
Raw Skin　Whether wild goose or duck, peel the skin and prepare
　　　pour vinegar over it twice and set aside; also prepare the meat
　　　pour vinegar once for preliminary seasoning, season with dashi-soy sauce
　　　when it comes to a boil, add the vinegar-treated bird and serve immediately
　　　　top with parsley or other garnishes as desired
　　　　sea bream soboro with white sesame is also good
Thousand Wings　Whether small birds or large birds, add a little
　　　shadow to the dashi, which is good
Bone Removal　Cut the duck's rump, from there remove
　　　the bones up to the legs, stuff with eggs and fish cake, sew up
　　　the opening, simmer like boiled chicken, slice into rounds
　　　and serve; leave the red ankle bones
Boiled Chicken　Simmer bones and all in dashi with soy sauce for a long time
Seared Chicken　Prepare duck, first sear the skin, then the meat

Eel　White-grilled and served with blue vinegar is good
Pheasant　Whole, plucked, with sansho pepper miso vinegar is good
Duck and goose　Like pheasant, or deboned and blade-cut
　　　served with wasabi vinegar, ginger miso vinegar is good
Chicken　This is also prepared like pheasant
Small duck　With pheasant sashimi, boiled sea bream flakes served
　　　together with wasabi miso vinegar is good for presentation, with garnish
　　　dried nori, kumquat, all birds plucked
Steamed bamboo shoots　Cut the roots, place vertically with skin
　　　steam well in a steamer, cut in various ways, with white
　　　vinegar for sashimi is good　Sea grapes, abalone, cooked shellfish
　　　Also shiitake mushrooms, wood ear mushrooms and such served together is good

Driftwood　Blanched　ginger vinegar is good
Turban shell　Night crying shell　Sea grapes　Bird clam　Fan mussel
　　　intestines and such, prepared and boiled with wasabi miso vinegar
　　　is good
River lettuce　Wild parsley　Scallions　or chrysanthemum flowers, dendrobium
　　　medicinal types are all good with vinegar miso
Sand lance　Boiled　with white vinegar is good
Turtle　Well boiled and plucked, with ginger miso vinegar is good
Sake accompaniments　Sea bream　Abalone　Cod　Salmon　Salted sweetfish
　　　Mullet roe　Turnip bones　Crane and goose　Duck
　　　From the above, select those with good salt flavor and combine to prepare
　　　and serve　Garnish kunenbō　Others as prepared

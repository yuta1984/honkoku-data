　　　△ Chapter 12　Cooked Dishes Section
Seared Sea Bream　Cut slightly thicker than for sashimi
　　　Whether sea bream or carp, take half the roe and pick
　　　cut, half crush and add to iri-sake with vinegar dropped in
　　　simmer briefly like dashi, add both the fish and roe
　　　serve immediately; if overcooked it becomes bad
Seared Carp　Prepare and serve as above
Sea Bream Suruga-style　White-grill the sea bream, in dashi with egg
　　　add a little vinegar and prepare very well
　　　serve; also grill and fry in pork fat, then
　　　it becomes even better; this is also called Nanban cuisine
Cedar Grilling　Cut sea bream thick and set aside, dissolve miso
　　　in dashi, put in pot, when it boils use chopsticks, first
　　　put in bones and head to cook, then add the flesh and immediately it's good, insert
　　　earthenware bottle, which is good; oysters, clams, tofu, leeks
　　　add others as prepared
Pot Cooking　With miso soup in a pot, serve as is
　　　Sea bream, mullet, flathead, combine with whatever
Conger Eel Roe Seared　Drop salt or soy sauce into dashi, also
　　　add a little vinegar to prepare; cut conger eel thicker
　　　than for namasu; adding roe and intestines is also good
Octopus Suruga-style　Wash octopus well, as is in dashi

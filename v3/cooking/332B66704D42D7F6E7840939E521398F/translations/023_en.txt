Bakuchi soup　Tofu　Cut into dice　Same for the broth
Mixed vegetables　Cut turnips together with other vegetables into one bundle
　　　Add dashi to medium miso
Uemon Goro　Cut vegetables long or short and flat
　　　Also add bonito and fermented rice bran miso
Yanagi nimaru　Add taro to picked vegetables
Dried vegetable soup　Add dashi to medium miso　Black beans,
　　　clams, small birds etc. pound and add, taro is also good to add
Carrot soup　Cut daikon large, add lightly salted sea bream
　　　to miso soup with dashi added and simmer very well
Grated soup　Grate daikon, add oysters, clams
　　　etc., prepare with medium miso and dashi added

Tororo soup　Simmered ingredients are good　Mountain yam, green nori, grate and
　　　grind very finely　Add nori to a good color
　　　Good to add, but bad if overheated　Garnish with
　　　powdered pepper
Natto soup　Make miso thick and add dashi　Stems,
　　　tofu cut very finely is good　Small birds
　　　pounded and added are good　Wash stems well and add like dashi　For
　　　natto, grind and spread well with dashi　Garnish with mustard,
　　　yuzu, garlic
Mugwort soup　Add dashi to miso　Cut mugwort roughly,
　　　add a little salt, rub and wash before adding　Also good to blanch
　　　Tofu etc. cut finely and add　Good in the 1st, 2nd, and 3rd months

Kneaded sake　Add white sugar to eggs and mix with cold sake,
knead together very well, warm and serve
Ginger sake　Grate ginger into miso and mix,
stir-fry and add sake, then warm. Can also use
ginger alone. Miso sake uses only miso
Quick sweet sake　Wash one sho of domyoji rice with hot water,
set aside to drain. Add one sho of koji to one sho and five go of water,
grind well in a mortar, strain through a cloth bag. Put these three ingredients
in a pot and simmer until thick - it will be ready quickly.
Adding white sugar is good

Zurin sake　Roast one sho of black beans and let cool, add one sho
and five go of good sake and let soak. Drink when the beans
have softened and swollen

△Chapter 16: Side Dishes Section
Fluffy eggs　Crack eggs and add dashi soy sauce and sake
equal to one-third the volume of eggs, cook until well puffed
and serve. If it becomes hard, it is no good. When chicken
thigh meat etc. is added, it is also called "enshu"
Rolled kamaboko　Attach kamaboko to grilled egg fu,
arrange arame seaweed on top, roll up tightly,
tie the top, add a little salt and boil, then slice to serve

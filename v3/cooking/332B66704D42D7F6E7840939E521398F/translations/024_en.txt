Chickweed soup　Cut chickweed, rub and wash　In the 3rd month　Add daikon etc.
　　　This is also prepared with miso
Karage soup　Cut eggplant in half and hollow out the center slightly　Green
　　　Japanese pepper, mustard etc. ground, also add walnuts and mix　Wrap in shiso leaves
　　　and tie well with kelp strings, add to miso soup
　　　with dashi added and simmer well　When serving, add liquid thickener
　　　dissolve kuzu starch and beat in, then serve
Jinpu soup　Cut eggplant in half or diagonally slice it finely
　　　This refers to the sliced preparation　Also make miso thick
　　　and add dashi　For garnish, grind mustard and green Japanese pepper and set aside
Wanzetsu soup　Cut tofu thinly and prepare with medium miso
　　　This is also good served with thick sauce poured over

Leek soup　Make miso thick and add dashi　Add lightly salted
　　　sea bream, good　Also prepare as clear soup
Carp wanzetsu soup　Scale carp and cut small　Toast and cut tofu
　　　and add　Good with medium miso　Serve with mustard and Japanese pepper
　　　thick sauce poured over, good

　　　△ Chapter 10　Raw Fish Salad Section
Cooking namasu　Sea bream, turban shell, whiting, flounder, small shrimp
　　　etc. add various kinds　Add grated daikon etc. and prepare　For all namasu,
　　　mix when about to serve the meal　Salt seasoning is
　　　crucial　Salt should be added once with proper judgment
　　　for good results　Adding it two or three times makes it progressively worse

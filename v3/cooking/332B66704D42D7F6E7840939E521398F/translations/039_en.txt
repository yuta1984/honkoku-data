Rolled surume squid　Wash surume squid and sprinkle a little
kudzu powder, tie with straw and press down into boiling water
to cook
Pounded surume squid　Grill the outside and then
pound and tear well on a board
Kelp-wrapped　Wrap mountain yam in arame kelp and boil,
then cut in various ways. Also sprinkle a little kudzu powder
Nama-bi　Cut fresh bonito at the right time, not too much,
clamp between chopsticks and twist while boiling, then grill, cut and
serve with soy sauce. Can also be made with small sardines
Salmon nama-bi　Cut fresh salmon lengthwise like
samegai mochi, soak in salt water for a short time,
then dry and grill before serving
What is called "hawk's feather"　Attach kamaboko to cedar board with
arame kelp in between, separate and cut diagonally so it
looks like hawk feathers when arranged
Shaved items for sake　Dried cod, surume squid, split dried salmon,
dried squid, smelt roe, puffer fish eyes, roasted sardines, skewered abalone,
bonito flakes, wakame seaweed, green laver, rokujō. Mix and match from
the above. Rokujō is used when there is no moon
Kneaded fu　Knead fu well with sake and simmer
in dashi soy sauce. Also add dried plums and dried bonito, simmer
in aged sake
Tamazusa　Seeds of snake gourd, also called kinbunshi

It becomes so　It is an unclean thing　The献 (side dishes) should be placed according to various seasonal preparations at that time
Bird namasu　Prepare everything　Stir-fry only the bird with vinegar
　　　Then add sea bream and other fish, mix and serve well　Adding wasabi
　　　is good
Ganzō namasu　Whiting, needlefish, flounder, ray, squid
　　　etc. prepare and mix various kinds　For this, adjust vinegar and salt
　　　seasoning and mix, only place as side dish
Oki namasu　Cut horse mackerel, young yellowtail etc. in rounds and
　　　roughly chop water pepper and add　This is what it means　Mackerel is also
　　　good　Sea bream and other fish can also be used　Salt seasoning is
　　　extremely important

Carp roe namasu　Scale carp into three pieces, thinly
　　　slice the flesh, remove skin and cut finely　Roast and toast
　　　roe and attach it, good　If rushed it won't stick properly
　　　Then add vinegar to toasted sake and pour half of the white-seasoned namasu
　　　and mix half with cold vinegar with wasabi added
　　　Mix both together and serve　However, there is also a tradition of pouring
　　　the white-seasoned mixture over the carp flesh
Crucian carp namasu　Scale crucian carp into three pieces, coat bones and head
　　　with soy sauce, grill well and chop finely
　　　Slice the flesh as thinly as possible　Mix with toasted roe
　　　and dress with mustard vinegar　Water pepper vinegar is also
　　　good　Serve the grilled head to each person individually

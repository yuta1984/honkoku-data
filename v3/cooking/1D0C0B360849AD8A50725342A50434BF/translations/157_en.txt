　　○Soy sauce glazed grilling
This too, as before, regardless of fish size,
wash well with water
and skewer two or three pieces at a time.
Grill with soy sauce glaze using
Japanese pepper soy sauce
or tree bud soy sauce.

[Left page]
　　○Deep-frying with batter coating

As before, wash well with water,
grill plain and let cool
for a while. Then dust with
winter-bleached flour and
deep-fry well in sesame oil.
　　Grated daikon radish
　　Finely chopped green onions
　　Fresh soy sauce
　　　　　　Red pepper

Serve with these condiments.

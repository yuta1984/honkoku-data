○Dried cod resembling soft-shell turtle
Take fresh cod with good salt-curing,
using only the thin flesh and scraps, quickly simmer with sake, salt,
and soy sauce, then deep-fry
in sesame oil.
Of course, adjust the bonito stock and soy sauce well,
　Shaved burdock root　Split scallions
Use either as accompaniment for hidden flavor,
and serve with ginger.

○Same, pounded
For this too, take quite good cod, removing
the bony parts, cut small,
quickly boil, tear apart,
pound well with the spine of a knife,
put in a bowl and steam. Pour kudzu
starch sauce over the top,
　Wasabi　Ginger
and serve with these. Miso
varieties are also good.

○Same - Hidden Vinegar Octopus
Take a considerably large, good octopus and boil it, then cut it into thin strips or slice it very thinly from the edge. Soak it in excellent vinegar mixed with a little salt. Change the vinegar four or five times, and each time, knead it by hand. When serving, squeeze it well and place finely shredded ginger on top. There is no need to reserve the vinegar.

○Same - Light Kudzu 《Clear soup}}
Wash the octopus as usual and briefly boil it. Cut the thick parts of the tentacles very thinly from the edge, and prepare with well-balanced bonito stock as light kudzu clear soup. Red miso is also acceptable. Peppercorns, green sansho pepper, tree buds, shiso leaves, udo shoots, butterbur shoots - use any of these as appropriate.

○The same / Shigure-ni (autumn rain cooking)
This method works regardless of size. Take the meat raw, add sake, soy sauce, and water in equal parts, and simmer down over charcoal fire. Above all, do not let it burn.

○Cook the shellfish in equal parts sake and water over low heat, and when it comes to a boil, remove the meat and reduce the cooking liquid over low heat. Then add sake, soy sauce, and water in equal parts with dried bonito flakes, and reduce without burning. This is most delicious.

○The same / Vinegar dressing
Take the meat that has been boiled as before, and dress it together with bracken, royal fern, lettuce, Japanese spikenard and similar vegetables with thick vinegar-miso and serve.

○The same / Black sesame dressing
Take the shucked meat as before, season quickly with soy sauce, drain well, heat sesame oil in a pot, put the meat in it and stir well, then let cool. Then grind black sesame, pickled plum meat, and white miso, and mix with sake-salt with a little soy sauce added.

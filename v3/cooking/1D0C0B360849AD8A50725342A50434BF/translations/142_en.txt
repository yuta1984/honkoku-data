○Miscellaneous Fish Preparation

Sprinkle a little salt and
let sit, then dress with vinegar-miso sauce
mixed with mustard and red pepper
　　Shredded daikon radish
　　Young myoga ginger buds
　　Cucumber
　　White melon
Using any one item
from the above as garnish, prepare as nuta
(miso-vinegar dressed dish) and serve. Note that
nuta dressing should use
slightly thinner miso than teppō-ae
If too thick, it won't be proper nuta
　　○Same fish　Teppō-ae (Spicy miso dressing)
As before, regardless of fish size,
fillet into three pieces,
remove belly bones,
cut both fillets diagonally against the grain,
sprinkle with a little salt and let sit, then
dress with red pepper miso along with:
　　Wood ear mushrooms
　　Daikon radish
　　○Shred the daikon like
　　　burdock root, rub with salt,
　　　wash with water to remove
　　　salt, and drain well
Dress with the above ingredients and serve
Daikon alone is also acceptable
   　　○Same fish　Dutch-style dressing
   As before, wash well with water,
fillet into three pieces, cut diagonally
against the grain, then marinate briefly
in vinegar mixed with a little
soy sauce,
then squeeze well

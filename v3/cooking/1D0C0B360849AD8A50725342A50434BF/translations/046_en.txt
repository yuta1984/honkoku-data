○Quick Simmered Sea Bream Noodles (Can be used for ōhira, confection bowls, soup bowls, or hot pot dishes)

Preparation method: Fillet the sea bream in the usual manner into three pieces, cut into cubes, sprinkle lightly with salt, let sit for a while, then thoroughly remove moisture. Skewer on small skewers and grill plain, then brush with oil and cook over a distant fire two or three times. Each time, brush with oil before grilling.

Ingredients (kayaku):
・Thin sōmen noodles　・Wood ear mushrooms
・Thin omelet　　　　　・Shiitake mushrooms  
・Chopped green onions　・Mitsuba (Japanese parsley)
・Daikon radish sprouts　・Water dropwort
・Spinach

Combine the above ingredients as appropriate, and choose garnishes according to the season.

○Sea Bream Fresh Sushi (Namazushi)
Fillet into three pieces as usual, rub salt well on both front and back sides, place under weight for a while, wash once with water, remove moisture with a cloth, and cut into flat slices, rough cuts, square cuts, or julienne strips—any method will do. Serve with Japanese pepper, water pepper, tree buds, and fermented rice paste.

○Same as Asakusa Roll
Take the fresh sushi preparation from above, cut into large flat pieces, lightly toast Asakusa nori seaweed over fire, wrap the sea bream meat and roll like sushi, then slice crosswise to serve. Accompany with ginger, water pepper, tree buds, pickled Japanese pepper, and shiso.

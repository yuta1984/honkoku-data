○Kokera Sushi (Shaved Fish Sushi)
○How to Cook Rice
For sushi, the proper preparation of rice is most important.
　Kanaya　Choshu　Higo
　Nakatsu　Takatsuki
Take fine white rice from places like these, wash it well and set aside. Then quickly blanch the backbone and other scraps of sea bream and remove them, using that broth to prepare and cook the rice. The rice turns out extremely well and the taste is exquisite.

○Tanago (Japanese bitterling)

　　○Miso wrapping

This fish is rarely
larger than six or seven inches.
First, regardless of size, split from the back
and remove the central bone
　　Ginger miso
　　Japanese pepper miso
　　Red pepper miso
Stuff the belly cavity with one of these types,
and to prevent the miso from leaking,
wrap the body section with thin paper

　　○Dengaku (grilled with miso)

Regardless of the fish's size,
if large use one, if small use
two on skewers, grill well on both sides
and apply red pepper miso
or similar to make dengaku

　　○Dozō-yaki (storehouse-style grilling)

For this, thoroughly wash small fish
with water, split from the back
and remove the central bone, stuff with
miso and wrap the body with paper

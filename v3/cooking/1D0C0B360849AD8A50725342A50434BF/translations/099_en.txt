○Same, White Sesame Mix
For this too, prepare abalone as before, grind white
sesame, mix a little miso with grilled tofu,
grind together into one,
and mix.

○Same, Intestine Mix
Cut the shellfish blanched as before
into cubes, fine cuts, or flat cuts—
whichever way you prefer, then
simmer the intestines well with
sake, salt, and soy sauce,
grind in a mortar, discard
the sac, and mix.

○Same, Plum Flesh Mix (mixed with plum flesh)
○Same, Miso Mix (mixed with any miso as preferred)
○Same, Seaweed Mix (mixed with well-soaked Asakusa nori)
○Same, Sea Urchin Mix (mixed with sea urchin)

Among the above, for seaweed mix and sea urchin mix,
cut raw into small cubes or thin strips
like paper twists and mix for use.

○Same, Miso-Simmered
Take very vigorous live shellfish,
remove from shells and wash well,
then add plenty of sake to miso
and simmer over charcoal fire for more than half a day
(light seasoning is best).
Take care not to let it burn, simmer well
until done, and serve cut up
with the miso as is.

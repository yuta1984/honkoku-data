○Same, deep mountain style (miyama-ni)
Take very tender sea cucumber,
cut into rounds or thin strips,
mix sake, soy sauce, and water in good
proportion, simmer well with direct bonito,
transfer to a bowl, sprinkle with black
pepper powder, then on top place mountain yam
that has been boiled and peeled, then ground
in a gold suribachi and spread
over - extremely delicious.

○Same, triple comma steamed (tomoe-mushi)
Take a very large, tender
sea cucumber, boil it well,
remove moisture, then with either conger eel
surimi or sea bream surimi,
prepare it slightly firmer than anpei,
then wrap around the sea cucumber from the belly
outward, embrace together, wrap in mi-paper
or bamboo skin so it doesn't leak
out, and steam.
After steaming, unwrap and
cut into rounds,
serve with　thin kudzu sauce　clear broth　miso topping
in confectionery bowl-style vessels -

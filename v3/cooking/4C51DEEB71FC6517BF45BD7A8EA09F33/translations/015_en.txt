　　　　Garnish

　　　　△Wash lily bulbs well, peel and steam the yellow parts well, mash them, mix lily bulb ten parts with refined sugar about three cups in good proportion, add water about 2 shaku 8 sun, cook well, pour onto a strainer, wait and cut to proper size, steam in a steamer, place in bowls - cutting is important △Boil shellfish mushrooms well, wash, simmer in sesame oil with sound, season with soy sauce △For cinnamon miso, powder ground cinnamon bark, mix into cooked miso and spread on the bottom of sweet bowls △For red potato garnish, peel mountain potato, wash, steam and mash well, in grinding bowl... before grinding, mix well in bowl... strain, when fine long potato emerges, place about 6 sun worth as bowl ingredients

　　Thousand needle arrangement
　　　Fuji-so (plant)
　　　　Burdock root
Flat plate　Autumn mushrooms
　　　　Shiitake mushrooms
　　Thousand slices
　　　　Daikon radish
Soup accent: split pepper

　　　　△Burdock root: boil well, cut to proper size
　　　　season and set aside. Separately grate kudzu
　　　　coat the burdock with it
　　　　deep-fry in sesame oil, steam in steamer
　　　　set aside for side dishes △Autumn mushrooms and shiitake:
　　　　soak in water, remove stems and simmer △Thousand-slice
　　　　daikon: cut leaves short, cut daikon like taro heads
　　　　about two inches, make square, leaving blade edge
　　　　slightly, slice thin vertically from small end
　　　　boil and add to side dishes. This is essentially
　　　　the butterbur daikon from the general method made thinner

　　　Sesame

Tea bowl　Shimeji mushrooms
　　　Yoshino-style
　　　　Udo (spikenard)
　Grated
　　　wasabi

　　　　△White sesame: soak in water for about a day, grind well in mortar
　　　　mix well with above kudzu, thin with water
　　　　strain through fine sieve into pot, cook gently
　　　　when properly thickened, put in mold, place in water
　　　　immerse bottom of mold to cool
　　　　when firm, submerge mold completely in water
　　　　separate edges with thin blade all around
　　　　lift out and cut to proper size, then
　　　　boil water in another pot, add contents, remove from fire
　　　　serve in small bowls △Shimeji: wash
　　　　arrange evenly, simmer stems △Yoshino udo:
　　　　cut about 1.5 inches, peel and round
　　　　cook in sake for side dishes ○Base: light kudzu thickening

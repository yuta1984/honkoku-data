Section on Flavored Rice Dishes

1. Buckwheat rice - Soak buckwheat in water and steam on top

1. Split grain rice - Similarly soak in water and place when steaming rice

1. Superior tea rice - Take tea porridge, cook it, then steam

1. Tree leaf rice - Made with sake and nori seaweed

1. Tofu rice - Whether cut small or julienned,
              mix with washed rice
              However, parboil and lay down, then steam

1. Abalone rice - Place on top of rice soaked in water and cook
                 Use less water (reduce the amount)

1. Tea rice - Add soy sauce, sake, and a little salt
             Add ginkgo nuts

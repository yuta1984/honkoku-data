1. Hagani tofu - Add two sho of sake to one block, boil well to extract
   Use in any manner

1. Tea ceremony tofu - Boil well in miso from the previous day to extract
   Remove that miso and use in any manner

1. Sea urchin tofu - Grind well and apply to a board, this is kamaboko
   Place kuchihana-nashi seasoning on top

1. Stir-fried tofu - Squeeze well and crumble, stir-fry with
   sake and soy sauce, spice with Japanese pepper

1. Board-grilled tofu - Cut small and arrange on a board, broil
   Apply heat and broil from above

1. Mate tofu - Grind well and put inside wheat gluten
   Steam and use

1. Abalone tofu - Cut one block into squares and place in shells
   Remove water and apply green seaweed well

1. Light yellow tofu - Grind well and put in a mold, mix by grinding
   Steam, season, and use

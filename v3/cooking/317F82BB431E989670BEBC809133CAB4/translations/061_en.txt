1. Rolled tofu - Spread thin and sprinkle fish meat over it
   Roll and steam for use

1. Pear tofu - Squeeze well and make into powder
   Grind and mix, spread and steam for use

1. Turmeric tofu - Squeeze well and grind with gardenia
   Steam for use

1. Ink-dyed tofu - Grind well with kelp in hot water
   Make into powder and grind together

1. Rice bran pickled tofu - Prepare as above, wrap in paper and pickle
   Remove paper, add water and use

1. Peeled tofu - Cut into dice, remove corners and strain with miso
   Sprinkle over, roast the meat small and cool
   Ginger and wasabi - Remove skin and use

1. Scattered tofu - Boil to extract, season, then top with cut mustard

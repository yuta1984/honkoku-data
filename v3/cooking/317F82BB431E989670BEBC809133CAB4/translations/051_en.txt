1. Ground tofu　　　　Squeeze out water and grind well in a mortar,
　　　　　　　　　　add a little kuzu starch and grind together,
　　　　　　　　　　then firm up and remove for use

1. Udon tofu　　　　　Squeeze out water and grind well in a mortar,
　　　　　　　　　　spread on a bamboo mat, hollow out the center,
　　　　　　　　　　boil well, cool in water, and cut into pieces

1. Soba tofu　　　　　Grind well in the same way, add a little buckwheat flour,
　　　　　　　　　　spread on a bamboo mat as above, follow same procedure,
　　　　　　　　　　and cut into squares

1. Brocade tofu　　　　Grind well, steam the white paste first,
　　　　　　　　　　then layer with colored varieties and steam

1. Whale tofu　　　　　Steam white paste first, then mix with
　　　　　　　　　　ground black sesame and steam for use

1. Sesame tofu　　　　Grate sesame with water, strain, add to water,
　　　　　　　　　　take the clear water on top and gradually mix into tofu,
　　　　　　　　　　steam and cut into pieces

1. Tsuri tofu　　　　Same method as tenkaku

1. Musubi tofu　　　　Tie tofu that has been spread on a bamboo mat

1. Kōri tofu　　　　　Simmer with kanten (agar), squeeze out the cooking liquid well
　　　　　　　　　　and use

　　　　　　　　　　and adjust seasoning

1. Ninuki tofu　　　　Cook thoroughly and simmer down

1. Kamo-iri tofu　　　Cut finely and drain water,

1. Shibe tofu　　　　Grind well like udon tofu,
　　　　　　　　　　then grill like tamagoyaki (rolled omelet)

1. Sen tofu　　　　　Spread the mixture thinly with water
　　　　　　　　　　on a bamboo mat and cut into pieces

1. Orihe tofu　　　　Layer chikuwa kelp in the center, simmer well
　　　　　　　　　　in dashi and sake, then adjust and add thin kuzu starch

1. Imadegawa tofu　　Finish with the same proportions, wrap the paste,
　　　　　　　　　　shake and serve

1. Tea tofu　　　　　Cook with same proportions, place in steamer and steam,
　　　　　　　　　　add tea leaves to the steaming water and steam well,
　　　　　　　　　　serve with pepper miso

　　　　　　　　　　in a cross pattern from the center

　　　　　Takenaka Genbei-sama's Method
　　　　　　　How to Make Rice Bran Miso
1. Fine white rice bran - 2 sho
1. Koji - 1 sho《Note: But pound it well to make it fine》　
1. Salt - 2 go
　　　The mixing method uses regular water to knead to
　　　the hardness of ordinary miso.
　　　Morohaku koji is even better. About seven days after
　　　preparation, it will be ready.
　　
　　　　　Transmitted by Konchi-in-sama

　　　　　　How to Make Vinegar
1. New rice and black rice - 1 to, steam into firm rice
1. Koji - about 3 sho, finely crushed and crumbled

【Left Page】
1. Water - 2 to 5 sho
1. Ginger leaves - 10 bundles, cut into 2-sun pieces, discarding the leaf portions
　　First add the above water and koji while warm,
　　mix well, add the ginger leaves and mix,
　　cover tightly so it doesn't cool. On the third day, check by hand
　　and the top of the lid should be warm, which is good. If it has
　　cooled, warm it sufficiently. On the tenth day,
　　remove the lid and skim off the white film that has formed on top,
　　stir it, cover as before,
　　and after another fifty days, remove the film again

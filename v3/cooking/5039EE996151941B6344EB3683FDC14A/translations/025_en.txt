1. Kudzu powder　　　　7 monme
1. Bracken starch　　　7 monme  
1. Rice flour　　　　　15 monme
1. Wheat flour　　　　 50 monme
1. Water　　　　　　　 2 sho
　　Of the above 2 sho of water, use 1 sho to dissolve the sugar, strain it through a strainer to remove impurities from the sugar. With the remaining 1 sho of water, mix together the kudzu powder, bracken starch, rice flour, and wheat flour, then combine with the sugar water

【Left Page】
　　Mix together and stir constantly in a copper pot without letting your guard down, and knead. However, the consistency when kneading should be like thin rice gruel when fully kneaded. When removing from the pot, spread the remaining wheat flour underneath and sprinkle a little on top, then roll it out. Cut when it has cooled.

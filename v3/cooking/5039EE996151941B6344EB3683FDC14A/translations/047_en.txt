　　　　Lord Minbu's recipe
　　　　　Quick braised sea cucumber preparation
1. Spread rice bran about one inch thick on the bottom of the pot, arrange large fresh sea cucumbers whole on top, then sprinkle enough bran over them to cover and steam. Much water will come out of the fresh sea cucumbers. If there is too much or too little cooking liquid, add the appropriate amount of

【Left page】
　　water and continue cooking. Small pieces of sea cucumber will be undercooked. When properly cooked, they become plump. Afterward, remove and wash well with water, also removing the intestines, then boil again. It's fine not to boil twice, but the bran smell may not come out completely. The degree of tenderness in cooking should be adjusted to taste. Afterward, use for cooking.

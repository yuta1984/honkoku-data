　　　　How to make kaki-mochi (rice crackers)
1. Pound year-old glutinous rice well and make
　　into mochi as usual. However, add
　　ash so there is no moisture and pound it. Then flatten it
　　and let it sit for about two days. On the second day, cut to about 2 sun in length,

【Left Page】
　　and about 1.5 to 2 bu thick.
　　Put the cut mochi in a box, keeping it away
　　from wind, and within a day or two, put it in
　　lukewarm water. When it becomes slightly soft, remove it
　　and arrange it on a bamboo screen, leaving it out overnight. After
　　that, dry it in the sun for two to three days. At the beginning, middle,
　　and end, keep it away from wind.

1　Koji flowers 5 gō, if cooking this as well, reduce accordingly.
　　Put these into shōchū, pack into a jar,
　　cover and bury in earth, plaster up to the lid,
　　seal so no air escapes, and after about 21 days
　　stir it, seal the opening as before,
　　let sit for another 21 days, then open.

　　　　　How to make kudzu sōmen
1　Make kudzu as fine as possible, sift with a fine sieve,
　　knead, dissolve today's seaweed in hot water,
　　let cool well and knead.
1　Make holes in the bottom of a ladle, bring water to a full

　　　　　How to make Masamune hoshii (dried rice)
【Left page】
1　Use equal parts glutinous rice and japonica rice, steam, then wash with water,

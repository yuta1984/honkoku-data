　　　　About Karazume Uzaemon's Turnip Pickling Method
1. Large turnips - 100 pieces, pickle each one individually
1. Koji - 3 sho
1. Salt - 4 go
　　　Mix the above koji and salt together, arrange the turnips one by one
　　　neatly, sprinkle the koji-salt mixture while
　　　pickling, and apply strong weights.

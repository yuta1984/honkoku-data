　　　　　How to Make Castella (Sponge Cake)
1. Add 10 monme of sugar to 1 shaku of eggs, knead and mix well until smooth, drop it down, and work it until it becomes somewhat flattened in the middle. For the flour, make it about the consistency of udon noodles without adding water. Whether 5 shaku or 10, crack the eggs and use only eggs, then add flour and knead. Then, brush sesame oil on Mino paper at the edge of the pot, lay that paper down, transfer the kneaded mixture on top, and also place oiled paper like the above on top.

【Left Page】
　　Put charcoal fire in a fire iron and apply it, also baking from above until it's well-baked. When the bottom is baked, turn the top to the bottom and bake well. Then cut it, and without putting anything inside, bake well from all four sides in the same pot.

　　　　About Making Sweet Sake
1. Steam 1 sho of glutinous rice that has been ground into coarse powder, steam it well and let it cool. Take great care with the koji, using 2 sho of good-colored koji, mix with just enough water to cover, knead and squeeze.

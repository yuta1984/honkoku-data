　　　Strain through a cloth for broth, and it is good to prepare the split beans with that liquid. The koji mixed in should also be made quite thick, which is good. Making the mixture rather firm is also good. It can withstand being left overnight.

　　　　　Kenbin-yaki (A type of pancake)
1. Add about 5 monme of sugar to 3 go of wheat flour, knead smoothly with water, drop with a ladle and knead to the extent that it becomes soft and pliable.

【Left Page】
1. Take dried persimmons that have been hanging for a long time, cut them, remove the seeds, tear into small strips, and cut small.
1. Quarter the walnuts.
　　Then warm the pot with walnut oil, put in a little of the kneaded mixture, then sprinkle the persimmons and walnuts evenly, add more of the wheat flour mixture, cover the pot with a lid and cook slowly. The heat should be gentle like when making fluffy eggs. If the fire is too strong, it will burn and stick. Add sugar as desired. There are oral instructions.

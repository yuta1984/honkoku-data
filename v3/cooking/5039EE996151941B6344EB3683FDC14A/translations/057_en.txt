　　　　　Recipe for Sumi-gata (Charcoal-shaped / Square-shaped Confection)
1.　White glutinous rice flour　　3 gō
1.　White non-glutinous rice flour　　7 gō
　　Sift the above two ingredients through a □ sieve as finely as possible.

　　　　　　　Method for Pickling Fragrant Melon
1.　Split the melon and remove the interior as thoroughly as possible. Place about 6 gō of salt
　　into a tub (fune), then squeeze sake lees (kasu) on top and place them inside the tub.
　　Place the melons face down, ensuring the melons do not rub against each other, and
　　pickle them in the lees as one would sushi. Cover the top tightly so that no air enters.

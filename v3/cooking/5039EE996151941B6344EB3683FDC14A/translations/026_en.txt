　　　　　　How to Make Japanese Pepper Mochi
【Left Page】
1. Make 1 sho of glutinous rice into flour, add a little red miso, mix the flour together and knead until it becomes crumbly. The way to prepare the miso is to grind it in a mortar, and if it becomes too hard, thin it with a little water, grind it from the foam clumps and strain. Then, lay cloth in a steamer, sift it in with a rice paddle and steam - this should work well. Add a good amount of Japanese pepper powder, pound together in a mortar, take it with white powder and roll it out. Let it cool and cut into various shapes.

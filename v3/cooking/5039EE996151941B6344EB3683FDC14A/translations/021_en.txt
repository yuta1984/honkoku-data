Method for Making Regular Glutinous Rice into Mochi Like Winter-Bleached Rice
1. Make glutinous rice into superior white rice, soak it after breakfast,
   after about two hours have passed, drain the water,
   grind into powder and make into mochi like winter-bleached rice,
   and it will turn out well.

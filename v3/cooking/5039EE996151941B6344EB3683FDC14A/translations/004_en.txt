　　　　　Recipe for Aokachi soup
1　Chop pheasant finely and pound the bones,
　　first fry the bones in a pot
1　Fry the meat second
1　Remove the innards and pound them together with the liver,
　　fry third
1　Cut daikon radish, burdock root, and tofu into pieces the size of shellfish spawn
　　and add them

　　　　To Lord Takenaka Uneme
　　　　　Method for preparing taro stems
1　Take only the good parts from the base of striped taro stems, about one shaku
　　in length, cut them up, peel the skin and split them finely, dry them on a net
　　for one day in good weather, then hang them to dry in the shade,
　　arranging the taro stems so they touch each other slightly

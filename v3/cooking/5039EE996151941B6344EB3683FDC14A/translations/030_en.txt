　　　　Medicine to be added to sake for use
1. Turmeric　9 go, lightly roasted
1. Black beans　3 go, lightly roasted  
1. Black sesame　2 go《Steam about 30 times, then make into powder - even 9 times is acceptable》
　　Grind the above three ingredients with a medicine grinder for use.

　　　　How to cook black beans
1. Beans　　5 go
1. Sugar　　8 cups
1. Sake　　1 cup《However, dissolve the sugar in the above sake and add》
　　Wash the above beans well and dry them, then put in a pot, add the sugar dissolved in sake, add plenty of seasoning miso and boil. When cooked, if there is much liquid, add seasoning, then dry it out.

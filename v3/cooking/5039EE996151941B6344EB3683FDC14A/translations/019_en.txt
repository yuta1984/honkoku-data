Method for Making Grated Cucumber Pickles
1. Cut off both ends of small cucumbers, pierce through the center with a stick, put in boiling water, then soak in salt overnight and pickle in sake lees. Salt it thoroughly.

About Corn Syrup
《Marginal note: Using tiger bamboo, dry in the sun》
1. Wash glutinous rice well with water and set aside. Expose to sunlight, change the water, soak for seven days, drain the water, grind with a stone mortar, put in a cloth bag and strain. Place in a bucket, and as the water clears, drain the water, lay cloth underneath, open the strainer and let it dry.

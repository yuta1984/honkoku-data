　　　　　Method for Kudzu Mochi
1. Good kudzu, one cup; sugar, likewise one cup; water, likewise one cup - mix these three ingredients in equal parts

【Left Page】
　　Grind well together in a mortar, then put into bamboo tubes and boil for about 30 minutes. When boiled, cook until there are no white lumps remaining inside. At that time, remove from the pot and cool in water together with the tubes.

　　　　　Method for Yuzu Mochi
1. Cut off the top of the yuzu and remove the flesh.
1. Roast black glutinous rice to a fox color and make it into powder.
　　Add a little white sesame, knead with lukewarm water to the right consistency. For one shaku of yuzu, about two shaku of skin

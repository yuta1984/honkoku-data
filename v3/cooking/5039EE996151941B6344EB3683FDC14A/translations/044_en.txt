　　　　　　　How to Make Citrus Miso　《Note: This amount is good for about 50 citrus fruits》　
1. Sugar - 140 monme
1. Dried rice - 180 monme
1. Miso - 160 monme
　　　　Grind the miso well, mix together the sugar and dried rice,
　　　add 5 bowls of soy sauce to the above, and in a pot
　　　knead until the dried rice becomes soft, add citrus peel to about 80% full
　　　and steam.

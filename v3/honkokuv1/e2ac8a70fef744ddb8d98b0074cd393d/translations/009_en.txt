Lord Kii no Kami's residence burned down. In this area around Sakurada Gate and Babasaki Gate, most buildings collapsed. Outside Babasaki Gate, Lord Endo Tajima no Kami (12,000 koku) and Lord Matsudaira Sagami no Kami (325,000 koku) were half-burned. The fire brigade quarters also burned down.

Inside Sukiyabashi Gate, there were many collapses, and among the residences that caught fire were those of Lord Nagai Totomi no Kami (36,000 koku) and Lord Honda Nakasei Taiyu (50,000 koku), which burned down. The guardhouse at Hibiya Gate burned down. The residences of Lord Matsudaira Tosa no Kami and Lord Tsuchiya Okiukami no Kami suffered major damage. Outside the same gate, Lord Matsudaira Hizen no Kami (over 357,000 koku) burned down. The front gate area of Lord Choshu (over 369,000 koku) burned slightly. Lord Arima Bingo no Kami (10,000 koku) and Lord Nanbu Mino no Kami (200,000 koku) burned down, and on the opposite side, the front longhouses of Lord Satsuma's (770,800 koku) costume residence burned slightly.

Inside Saiwaibashi Gate, one building of the middle longhouses belonging to Lord Yanagisawa Tokinosuke (150,1280 koku), Lord Ito Shuri Taiyu (over 50,1080 koku), and Lord Kamei Oki no Kami (43,000 koku) burned down, and the fire stopped there. At Tatsunokuchi corner, Lord Morikawa Dewa no Kami (10,000 koku) burned down. At Otemae, Lord Sakai Utanokami (150,000 koku) burned down but the front gate remained. The residence across from it also burned down. In this area, the residences of Lord Echizen, Lord Hitotsubashi, and Lord Ogasawara suffered extensive collapses. From inside Gofukubashi to inside Sukiyabashi, there are numerous collapsed areas everywhere.

In Kasumigaseki, Lord Geishu suffered no particular damage, and for Lord Kuroda, there were no collapses in the longhouses from the corner turret watchtower to the front gate. Other various houses suffered many collapses. Around the top of Edomizaka and Nagatacho, small collapses can be seen here and there. Sanno Shrine was unharmed, but inside Akasaka Gate, all the residences have numerous collapsed areas.

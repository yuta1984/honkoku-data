《Box: 22》The north and south Hatchobori area had few collapses, while Teppoza had many collapses. The area around Funamatsu-machi was particularly severely affected, and the residence of 【family crest】Matsudaira Awaji-no-kami (15,000 koku) burned down. Almost all the residences in this area suffered significant damage, while the Inari shrine was unaffected. In Reiganjima, Minato-machi, Nagasaki-machi, Tomishima-cho, Kawaguchi-machi, the Minato-bashi street, North and South Shimbori, and Hakozaki-cho all suffered many collapses. From half of Minami-Shimbori and one side of Shio-machi, fires burned through to Okawabata-cho. The residence of Matsudaira Echizen-no-kami survived. From there, along Koami-cho street, around Inari-bori and the residence of Sakai Uta-no-kami, from Jinzaemon-cho through the Ginza residences, around Kakigaracho, and around Matsushima-cho, there were many collapses. Hama-cho and the Manbe riverside street suffered damage for the most part up to the vicinity of Ohashi. The residence of Mizuno Dewa-no-kami burned down.

《Box: 23》In Honjo, temples collapsed entirely, and the residences of daimyo lords and hatamoto retainers largely collapsed, with those that escaped unharmed being rare. Among them, the lower residence of Matsudaira Suo-no-kami, east beyond the bridge, burned down. From there, many buildings collapsed around Matsushima-cho, and Koume and Kawaramachi suffered minor fires. In the Nakanogo area, many temples were damaged. Arai-cho was completely destroyed, and small residences around Ishiwara, the Ushino-Gozen temporary shrine, and around Benten-koji burned. Also, at Hitokuchi, two locations — in front of Tenman-gu Shrine and in Kameido-machi — burned. In the Yanagishima and Oshiage areas, there were many collapses in various places. Also, some townhouses near Hoon-ji Bridge burned. Yoshida-cho, Yoshioka-cho, and the small residences around Minami-Warishisugi suffered great damage, and collapses were frequent up to the vicinity of the Tsugaru domain residence. The sheds along Otakekura-mae street collapsed greatly.

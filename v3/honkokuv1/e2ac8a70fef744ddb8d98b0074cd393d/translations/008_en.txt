Also, there is slight collapse at Chojagamaru and Kogai-bashi areas, and at Higakubo and Ryudo areas, with collapsed spots visible in various places. In Azabu, there is generally little collapse, and in Hiroo, Shirogane, and Meguro areas, there are also spots with slight collapse here and there.

[Section 15] In the Hongo and Yushima areas, places with some collapse can be seen in various locations, but nothing particularly serious. However, the koji-muro (fermentation chambers) collapsed, causing tremendous losses. In Koishikawa-sakai, Kikuzaka-shita, and Ta-machi, there is much collapse.

[Section 16] In the Komagome and Sendagi areas, various places collapsed slightly. From Hakusan-shita to Sashigatani-cho-shita and Tomizaka, there is much collapse, while in Sugamo and Somei areas there is little collapse. There is gradual collapse up to the Oji and Itabashi areas.

[Section 17] Outside Koishikawa Gate, the Mito domain residence, hundred-unit row houses, and the residence across from them all collapsed, with five or six houses burned. From Ushi-tenjin-shita and Suwa-cho to the Ando-zaka area, there is much collapse throughout. Ryukei-bashi and the residence of Matsudaira Sanuki-no-kami burned down. From there, Dentsuin-dori has little collapse, but Uramon-dori has much collapse. In Kohinata Mizuya-cho and Sekiguchi On'nawa, collapse can be seen in various places. Mejiro Shiina-cho has no damage. Around Ushigome, various places collapsed, and Kaitai-cho suffered major collapse. In the Ichigaya and Takada areas, only low-lying places have many collapsed spots.

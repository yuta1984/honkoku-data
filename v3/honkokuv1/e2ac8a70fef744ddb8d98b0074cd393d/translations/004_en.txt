《Five》 Sakamoto 2-chome and 3-chome were burned. Go-tansu-cho was slightly burned, but from 1-chome to the Kanasugi area, Miwa area, and Daion-ji temple front area had many collapses. Negishi had nothing particularly noteworthy. The Yanaka area had damage in various places, and the nine-ring finial of Tennoji Temple's five-story pagoda fell. The Misaki and Dango-zaka areas collapsed slightly.

《Six》 Toeizan (Kaneiji Temple) had no particular problems, but only the lodging quarters suffered some minor damage. Ikenohata Kaya-cho's 2-chome and 1-chome collapsed and then burned completely. The mountain side remained, though there were many collapses. The area below Kiridoshi suffered major collapse, and the towns on both sides of Naka-cho were reduced. Kata-kawa-cho also had many divided collapses. Go-sukiya-cho collapsed completely. The mansions along Tenjin-shita street mostly collapsed.

《Seven》 The front and back organized mansions along Shitaya Kotoku-ji temple street had no particularly severe collapses in various places.

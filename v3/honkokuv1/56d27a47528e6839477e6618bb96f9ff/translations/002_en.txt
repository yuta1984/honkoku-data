The atmosphere extends thirteen ri around the earth. The yin energy that receives the yang energy of the sun and is contained within it emanates from the earth. When combined, it becomes wind, rain, and clouds. When yin energy rises excessively, it becomes thunder. When yang energy descends excessively and enters the earth, it causes trembling. This is an earthquake.
Although everyone knows such things, they cannot predict when the trembling will occur.
Therefore, fires break out and many people die from sudden disasters. It is known that earthquakes since ancient times follow the same pattern. Here I intend to record in detail the earthquakes from ancient times for your examination.

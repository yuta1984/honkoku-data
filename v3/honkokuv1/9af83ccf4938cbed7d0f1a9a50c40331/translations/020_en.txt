Sand falls, unknown even to the gods
　　　　　　rice market prices
For buying food to eat
　　　　　　the water runs out

At this time in Edo, the water pipes burst and water ran out. One load cost about 132 mon, and rice was about 8 gō for 100 mon. The people in the back-alley tenements could not possibly continue to make ends meet.

　　　　Composed in the style of noh chanting
Takasago ya, opening the door of this back-alley tenement, pounding rice
Going out to buy, those tears of pity
One piece of island cloth, leaving possessions behind, cheap
transformation, love is exhausted, taking and taking

The above matter concerns Edo, where rice prices were especially high, while Asama (volcanic ash) was cheap. It was impossible to continue making a living, and the people in the back-alley tenements could not sustain their livelihoods. Therefore, this matter is being reported.

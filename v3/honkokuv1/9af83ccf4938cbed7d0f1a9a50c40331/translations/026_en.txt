These official reports and various petitions from neighboring provinces regarding the Shinshū Mount Asama eruption and the resulting Jōshū landslide tsunami in Tenmei 3 are borrowed from the collection of Mr. Kobori Toyokichi of Akuto Shinden, Koga-machi, Sashima-gun, Ibaraki Prefecture. They have been transcribed by an assistant clerk for permanent preservation and to serve as reference for future years.

January 17th, Meiji 33 (1900)
At the Tone River Flood Control Association
Clerk: Negishi Yūkichi (seal)

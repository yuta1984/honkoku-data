Record of the Great Urban Earthquake of Genroku 16, Year of the Boar
Including the fire from Lord Mito's residence in the following year, Hōei 1, Year of the Monkey

On the night of November 22nd, after 9 o'clock, an earthquake caused major damage and collapse throughout the castle - some turret gates collapsed, walls fell, moat perimeters crumbled, and various residences in nearby provinces were damaged. From that same night, frequent earthquakes continued daily for some time, causing many people to leave their homes and endure the cold outside in makeshift shelters of reed screens and board enclosures. During this period, the long houses of Lord Kōfu's Sakurada residence collapsed and caught fire.

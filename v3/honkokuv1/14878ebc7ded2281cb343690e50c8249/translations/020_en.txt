　　　　　　18th day of the 10th month

　　[Marginal note] Accordingly, the advance palanquin boxes (sakibako) of Etchū (Echizen) and Awa domains are to be discontinued; the same applies to the Shiba Hōjō residence. No foot soldiers (kachi); minimal retinue only. In all cases, for hereditary vassals (fudai) with domains between 10,000 and 100,000 koku, approximately four foot soldiers and more mounted attendants are to be used. The Ōmetsuke are to use a four-man retinue; the head of the guard (ban'gashira) and others are to discontinue the narrow palanquin boxes (hasami-bako), with double-carry becoming more common.

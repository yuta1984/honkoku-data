What a pity that the minor flaw in a white jade does not damage the quality of the jade itself, yet in the end it cannot but leave the reader with some lingering regret. Therefore, I shall now truly expound upon this theory, and taking what is useful and discarding what is not, present my humble views through a balanced synthesis, as follows:

First: When the Supreme Ultimate (Taiji) moves, it generates Yang; when it is still, it generates Yin. Yin qi produces cold, and cold qi generates water. Yang qi produces heat, and heat qi generates fire. Now, fire fundamentally has no fixed form or substance. When dispersed, it is simply the qi of Yang; when gathered, it takes on the substance of fire. The ancients said: "Fire is one of the Five Elements; it has qi but no substance — its qi circulates in Heaven, is stored in the Earth, and is put to use by humanity."

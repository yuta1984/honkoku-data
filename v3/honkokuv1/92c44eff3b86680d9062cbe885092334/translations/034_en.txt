How could there be any principle that prevents yang energy from gathering and becoming fire within the great earth? People merely fail to know of its existence because it is invisible to the eye. That which manifests in heaven represents the trigram Li, while that which lies hidden in the earth represents the trigram Kan. I believe that yang energy existing within the earth—that which lies dormant, hidden, stagnant, gathered, and condensed—this is what we call underground fire. When this underground fire emerges and erupts according to location, it is called a "fire well" (fire spring). Zhang Hua's Bowuzhi (Record of Various Matters) from Tang China also mentions the existence of fire wells in the world, and there are countless other records in various books stating that fire wells naturally emit fire constantly. Indeed, examples include the fire and smoke of Mount Fuji-Asama, the fire that burns up from underground in Myōhōji Village in Echigo Province, the fire and smoke in the valleys of Mount Aso in Higo, the fire of Sulfur Mountain in Bungo, and in various other provinces, the smoldering from underground in mountains, valleys, fields and plains

Preface to the Record of Atami
The topography, backed by mountains and embracing the sea, is magnificent. A person of exceptional talent
was exiled and lived here for a long time. He swept away the luxurious habits of the mundane world,
and separately established infinite merit in service to the hegemon. The military government
has now come to an end. This land produces sacred springs and eternally preserves its name. The healing
effects are miraculous and surpass all others. Year after year, saving and reviving people

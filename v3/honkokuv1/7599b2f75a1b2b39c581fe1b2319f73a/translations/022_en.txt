Now then, up to this point we have shown you the good and bad compatibility in the three types of physiognomy (face reading, palm reading, and house divination). All of these are such that depending on a person's state of mind, they may form either bad connections or good connections. These are all things done by the gods in the land of Izumo, and are beyond human capability.

Once a connection is formed, it is under the jurisdiction of the Kojin deity who determines fortune and misfortune and protects the household. This is not done by the Kojin deity alone, but the Fukujin (god of fortune) and the god of poverty come and go - into households with good-hearted compatibility come Daikoku and Ebisu, who from time to time make their rounds to assist in the Kojin deity's duties.

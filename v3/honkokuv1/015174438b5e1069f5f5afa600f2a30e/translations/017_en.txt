**Eyewitness Accounts of Injuries and Deaths from the Great Earthquake on the Night of the 2nd Day of the 10th Month of Ansei 2 (1855), at the Hour of the Boar**

○ Lord Sakai Uta-no-kami:
The number of deaths is immeasurable. On the night of the 2nd day of the 10th month, as people fled from the upper residence (kami-yashiki) to the lower residence (shimo-yashiki), some thirty to forty persons perished.

○ Lord Mashiyama Kawachi-no-kami:
Many deaths reported.
The aunt of Taniguchi Genjun, a woman named Seizan who served as a senior lady-in-waiting (rōjo), died instantly. Genjun himself was at his outside residence and also perished. The location on duty was Sonomura Matsumachi. Similarly, the house collapsed, and both the elderly mother and a female member of the household died.

○ Lord Abe Ise-no-kami:
The number of deaths is particularly high.
It is said that the only one able to escape unharmed was the lord (daishu) himself. Even the lady of the house sustained injuries so severe they were nearly fatal.

○ Lord Naitō Kii-no-kami:
The house collapsed, and many deaths occurred.

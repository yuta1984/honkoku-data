6th day of the 10th month

Item: Due to this earthquake, damage has occurred at several locations within the Castle grounds. However, taking into consideration the general shortage of timber and other materials throughout the city, it has been ordered that, except for areas requiring essential repairs, all other damage is to be left as is. Each residence should act accordingly and carry out only the most necessary repairs, done in an especially simple and economical manner.
As the above directive has been issued to the samurai households, the towns need not concern themselves with unsightly damage — it is permissible to leave things as they are.

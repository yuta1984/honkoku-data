## Inspector of the Finance Magistrate's Office (*Go-Kanjō Ginmigata Aratameyaku*)

**Aoyama Yasōemon** — *Note: This person is not a retainer of the Aoyama clan, but rather a lady-in-waiting serving the wife of Lord Hori.*
　　[Classical Chinese annotation: "That the wife of a feudal lord should be buried in such a forlorn manner — was this not brought about by the causes of a previous life, as Buddhism teaches? And is this then the karmic fruit thereof?"]

　　His mother was gravely ill and bedridden when the house collapsed. A maidservant threw herself over the sick woman and endured the falling roof beams to protect her. In the meantime, the master rushed to the scene and pulled the mother to safety. The maidservant also fortunately escaped with her life.

## Commander of the Great Guard (*Ō-Oban Kumigashira*)

**Ishiguro Sakuemon** — Daughter killed instantly. Age: sixteen.
　[Classical Chinese annotation: "Alas! The deed of this loyal and brave daughter is no less than that of a grown man. Surely Heaven's grace will not go unrewarded in days to come."]

**Naitō Kannojō** — Killed instantly.

**Matsudaira Sanuki-no-kami** — Many injured among his household retainers.

**Koide Shinano-no-kami**
　　The lord himself was uninjured and evacuated to his subsidiary residence at Nezumiyama. The physician Kimura (given name unknown) was killed instantly. A person named Kake(?) was unharmed.

---

## Keeper of the Inner Wardrobe (*O-Konando*)

**Moriyama Yasojirō** — Daughter and maidservant killed.

---

## Palace Guards (*Go-Shoinban*)

**Tsuji Jūbee** — Killed instantly.

**Kuze Masayoshi**
　　The master was severely injured and evacuated to the residence of Tsutsui Kii-no-kami. His forehead wound required eleven stitches. His wife and others were killed instantly.

---

## Reserve Corps (*Kobushin*)

**Tanuma Yasaburō** — The retired master, known as Seiō, along with Yasaburō's wife and concubine, all killed instantly.

**Tōdō Sado-no-kami** — Two daughters killed instantly. Others, both upper and lower servants, also among the dead.

**Tachibana Shikibu**
　　Killed instantly. According to one account, he was a dependent (*yakkai*) living in the household of a branch family of the greater Tachibana clan, serving at the time as a guardian (*kōken*), and his residence was in Honjo.

---

## Uraga Magistrate (*Uraga Bugyō*)

**Toki Buzen-no-kami** — Nineteen retainers killed instantly.

**Morikawa Dewa-no-kami** — Including all levels of household members down to servants, a total of over forty people died.

---

## Ogawamachi

**Hyūga (given name unknown)** — Daughter and others, six or seven people killed instantly.

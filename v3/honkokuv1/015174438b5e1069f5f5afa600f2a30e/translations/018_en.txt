[He] borrowed a formal hemp kamishimo (ceremonial overvest and hakama) with an official insignia over a fukusa kosode (under-robe), and appeared in the palace (shogunal castle). While some said this was too casual for a person of the rank of kokurou (senior domain elder), others argued that, on the contrary, it was the right thing to do.

One: Hayashi Daigaku-no-kami — The entire residence collapsed; fifteen deaths among upper and lower retainers combined.
The Daigaku-no-kami himself was also crushed under the building in the sleeping quarters of the Katsura room, but he crawled out uninjured. His wife died. Retainers, manservants, and maidservants perished. At the residence of one Nanigashi Hachinosuke, the head of the academy, a maidservant also died.

One: Ōkubo Chikuzen-no-kami — All family members died instantly; numerous retainers also perished.
According to public rumor, his son (name unknown) survived unharmed, but since both the young lord and his wife died, there was talk that depending on circumstances, the family line might face extinction.

One: Matsudaira Buzen-no-kami — Numerous deaths.
The lord himself was also pinned under the collapsed building, but escaped unharmed and reported to the castle the following day. He subsequently fell ill.

One: Takai Yasojirō
His wife died instantly. Many manservants and maidservants perished. Yasojirō himself suffered only injuries.

One: Matsudaira Higo-no-kami — Numerous deaths.
There were reports of people seen loading the dead onto carts to remove them. It was said that approximately six hundred or more people perished at the inner residence at Wadakura and at the Shinzenza residence.

One: Mori Denemon
The master initially fled to safety, but returned to the residence to retrieve money, and was ultimately crushed to death.

One: Seki Harima-no-kami — Numerous deaths.

One: The fire brigade residence at Ogawamachi
Of thirty doshin (low-ranking officers) in the unit, it was said that twelve household heads died. Yoriki (mounted police officer) Amano Jōuemon died. Including family members, the total came to over fifty deaths.

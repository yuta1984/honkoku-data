　　　13th day of the 4th month

---

In my domain territory in Shiozaki Village, Imai Village, and Hyūzetsu Village, within Sarashina District, Shinano Province, a great earthquake occurred from around the Hour of the Boar (亥の刻, approximately 9–11 p.m.) on the night of the 24th of last month, causing houses to collapse from the shaking and resulting in deaths among people and horses. I am informed that tremors continued intermittently even as of the 26th of the same month. Furthermore, I have received word from a retainer stationed in the domain that the government public works site (国役御普請所) at Shiozaki Village has also collapsed. While full details should be reported after further investigation, I humbly submit this preliminary notice for now.

　　　4th month　　　　　　　　　　Matsudaira Hida-no-Kami

---

In my domain territory of Matsumoto in Shinano Province, a great earthquake occurred from around the Hour of the Four (四つ時, approximately 10 p.m.) on the night of the 24th of last month. From the 25th onward, there has been no major obstruction, and the tremors have become more distant, yet they have not entirely ceased even now. The castle itself sustained no particular damage, but there are damaged sites at the samurai residences and various locations in the surrounding town. Regarding outlying areas, details are not yet clear, but I humbly submit this preliminary notice for now. Further details will be reported subsequently.

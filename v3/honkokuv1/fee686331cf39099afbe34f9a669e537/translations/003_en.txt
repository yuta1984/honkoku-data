Add [a rice bowl] and carry it with you, pouring water to prevent [the fire from spreading]. This is good. To extinguish flying sparks,
if you splash water with a rice bowl, the water will reach where you intend
and the fire will be extinguished. First, preventing and
extinguishing the source of the fire is of primary importance. You must not
delay by focusing on household goods. If the fire source is extinguished,
household goods will naturally remain safe. When you understand this principle well
and take great care to prevent fires effectively, people will be moved to
help each other, and above all, the gods will surely provide assistance.

changes as it blows. Moreover, fires are not things that cannot occur, so
when the wind is strong, without negligence, fill buckets, pots, kettles,
and fixed bathtubs with water, and additionally, you should take care to organize and put away
household goods.

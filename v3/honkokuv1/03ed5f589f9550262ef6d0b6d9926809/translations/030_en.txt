On Luminous Phenomena

Someone asked: "In summer nights, there are things called luminous objects that can often be seen from time to time. Also, what people commonly call human soul fires fly through the sky, and it is said by many that where they fall, charcoal-like substances are formed. What kind of phenomena are these?"

I answered: Luminous objects certainly exist. I too have seen them from time to time. These are caused when the great yang fire energy that exists underground bursts forth and splits open, with the fire energy ascending from below to the heavens above. When it ascends and there is no moisture above but dry heat instead, it takes form and becomes what people nowadays call night meteors. These night meteors are not stars but yang fire that thrusts upward from the earth. Their height is not at the same level as the sun and moon. They are much lower than the sun and moon, so they cannot be seen from different locations on earth. They are visible only where that yang energy ascends. It is like the halo around the moon.

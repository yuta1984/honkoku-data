(Continued from previous page) Many storehouses (dozō) were damaged and collapsed, causing numerous buildings to fall and many people to be injured as a result.

**[Section 10]** In Kanda, the areas of Hashimoto-chō and Dōjima-chō suffered minor damage, while the Koyanagi-chō area and Hiranaga suffered extensive collapse. Along the road from Sujikai Gomon to Imagawa Bridge, there was widespread damage in many places. West Kanda suffered minor damage throughout the entire area. The area around Wagura Bridge suffered only minor collapse.

**[Section 11]** In Ogawa-chō, along the stretch from Sujikai Gomon to Suidō Bridge, the residences of Lord Inaba Nagato-no-kami and Lord Tsuchiya Uneme-no-shō suffered damage. The residence of Lord Toda Ōi-no-kami (11,000 koku) [family crest] was destroyed by fire, though the front tenement row (omote nagaya) survived. The residence of Lord Naitō Suruga-no-kami (32,000 koku) [family crest] was burned down, with the front tenement row surviving. On the opposite side, the residence of Lord Tanaka Yuiichi survived. Furthermore, the residence of Lord Hotta Bitchū-no-kami (110,000 koku) [family crest] was largely destroyed by fire. On the opposite side, one building belonging to Lord Nakarai Izumo-no-kami was burned down. The residences of Lord Mizoguchi Hachijūgorō, Lord Satō Kinbei, Lord Fushiya, Lord Ōkubo, and Lord Tsugeki were all destroyed by fire. One residence of Lord Yoda survived. The residences of Lord Kaminori-be, Lord Arakawa, Lord Soga, Lord Kondō, Lord Aoki, Lord Honda, and Lord Niimi were all burned down. The residences of Lord Kawachi, Lord Kobayashi, and Lord Satō were destroyed by fire. Further along, in the back lane of Jinbō-kōji, the residences from Lord Mashimo, Lord Nagasaka, Lord Morishima, and Lord Kurai were burned down. Along Hitotsubashi-dōri, the residences of Lord Meiraku, Lord Amemori, and Lord Ōoka survived. On the front side of Jinbō-kōji, the residence of the Jōhikeshi (standing fire brigade) was destroyed by fire. The residence of Lord Sakakibara Shikibu-no-tayū (150,000 koku) [family crest] was half-burned. On the opposite side from the service gate, the residence of Lord Honda Buzen-no-kami (40,000 koku) [family crest] was half-burned. Beyond that, the residence of Lord Toda Kaga-no-kami was burned, with only a small portion of the southwest corner surviving. The residence of Lord Washizu Awaji-no-kami was destroyed by fire. Two residences belonging to Lord Hasegawa and Lord Arai survived. On the front side only, Lord Yamamoto's residence suffered minor burning before the fire stopped. In another fire origin, in the Kijibashi-dōri and Ogawa-chō area, the residence of Lord Hongō Tango-no-kami was burned. In Sanbannohara, the residence of Lord Matsudaira Buzen-no-kami (50,000 koku) [family crest] was destroyed by fire, as was the residence of Lord Hanawa Sōetsu. In yet another fire origin, the residence of Lord Watanabe Sanosuke was burned. In still another fire origin, the residence of Lord Isshiki Tango-no-kami was destroyed by fire, and the inner quarters of Lord Isshiki Kuninousuke were partially burned. It should be noted that throughout the entire area, earthquake-caused collapses were extremely severe and widespread.

1. Fixed Crossing Degree (交定度, Corrected Node Degree)
　February: 181 degrees, 31 minutes, 84 seconds
　August: 1 degree, 23 minutes, 10 seconds

○Method: List the "extinction days" (没日) of the eclipse month; multiply by the lunar motion multiplier (月行乗方) of 2,139; divide by 160. If the quotient yields a "fullness difference" (盈差), add it; if it yields a "contraction difference" (縮差), subtract it, to obtain the Fixed Crossing Degree.

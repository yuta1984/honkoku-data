Chen states: In the case of a solar eclipse, at the intersection of the true node and middle node, if the distance before or after the node reaches 6 degrees on the yang side (陽暦) or 8 degrees on the yin side (陰暦), a total eclipse will occur in all cases. If it exceeds 6 or 8 degrees, no eclipse will occur. If it does not reach 6 or 8 degrees, a partial eclipse of some fraction will occur.

○Method for calculating the "Reduction Difference" (減差, *gensa*) for the Eighth Month, which states:
　Arrange the Contraction Difference (縮差, *shukusa*) and the Swift Difference (疾差, *shissa*) side by side on the left and right — they are of the same sign (同名). Add the left and right together, then divide by the Entry-into-Cycle Day's Fixed Daily Degree of Twelve-point-six-nine-four (12.694); the resulting quotient becomes the Reduction Difference.

　　Chen states: In general, on the day of a solar eclipse, the Entry-into-Cycle Day (入転日, *nyūtenbi*) of the new moon varies according to the number of days, and the Fixed Daily Degree of the Cycle (転定度, *tenteido*) differs accordingly.

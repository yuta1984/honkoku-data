**Diagram of the Vernal Equinox and Ascending Node (Zhèngjiāo)**

(Diagram)

[Annotations within the diagram:]
- Ascending Node (正交, *zhèngjiāo*)
- The White Road (Moon's path) is 74 degrees from the North Pole
- Equator · Mid-node · North Pole · White Road · Ecliptic
- The Autumnal Equinox becomes the Descending Node (中交, *zhōngjiāo*)
- The distance is zero
- When the Vernal Equinox is at the Ascending Node, the Autumnal Equinox becomes the Descending Node

[Main text:]
When the Ascending Node (正交) is at the lodge of the Vernal Equinox,
the Mid-node point (半交) extends six degrees
beyond the ecliptic,
and lies eighteen degrees
within (i.e., south of) the celestial equator.

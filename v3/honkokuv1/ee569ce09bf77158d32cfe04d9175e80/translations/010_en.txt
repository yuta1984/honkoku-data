Once every month, on the day of the Dragon (tatsu) during the hour of the Dragon, one should draw well water from the direction of the Dragon and have a person born in the year of the Dragon sprinkle it on the roof. This is for fire prevention. However, if there is no Dragon well or person born in the year of the Dragon, this may be omitted, but one must not forget the timing.

1. There is something called "east ridge with west eaves." If one extends a ridge due east from the center of a house and lowers eaves toward the true west, that house will surely experience calamity. Since this has been tested, one should be deeply cautious about this.

1. There are many inauspicious days for beginning house construction. Choosing the right day and performing water purification when laying stones and foundations also prevents fires. Additionally, one should pray and perform virtuous deeds.

Article: On Knowing the Scale of Fire Disasters
This is a method well known by people, but I record it here for reference.

Divination using the left hand:
Thumb = Fire, Index finger = Wind, Middle finger = Water

(Thumb: 1st, 4th, 7th, 10th months = months of Tiger, Snake, Monkey, Boar)
(Index finger: 2nd, 5th, 8th, 11th months = months of Rabbit, Horse, Rooster, Rat)
(Middle finger: 3rd, 6th, 9th, 12th months = months of Dragon, Sheep, Dog, Ox)

The method of this divination is to designate the thumb as the first month and count through the three fingers in order through the twelve months. If it is within the first month, start counting from the index finger as the first day, second day, and so on. Where the day falls - if it is fire, it will be a great fire; if wind, a medium fire; if water, a small fire. The middle finger represents water, but since it corresponds to three, it is a fire number. Therefore, there are examples like Meireki 3, Enkyō 3, and Bunka 3.

Also, great fires occur in the third month. The first month is the month of Tiger (Wood), so there are many winds. The third month is the month of Dragon (Earth), which favors fire by principle. In Bunsei 12, Year of the Ox, on the 21st day of the 3rd month, though it corresponded to water, there was a great fire, but it was a fire year and the blaze started at the hour of Snake (around 10 AM) when fire is vigorous. Two is the number of metal, and fire overcomes metal. The second month is the month of Rabbit (Wood), and wood generates fire. Therefore, accidental fires are frequent.

- Meiwa 9 (1772), Year of the Water Dragon, 2nd month (from the evening of the 29th day at the hour of the Snake until the morning of the 30th/1st day): Demon constellation (Water), flowing water
  From Meguro to Senju, the Eastern Gate ruins burned down (Meiwa 9 refers to the previous era name; the era was changed to An'ei in the 11th month)

- An'ei 7 (1778), Year of the Earth Dog, 2nd month, 12th day: Great fire in Edo, Horn constellation (Wood), trees on flat ground

- Tenmei 4 (1784), Year of the Wood Dragon, 12th month, 26th day: Winnowing Basket constellation (Metal), lamp fire
  From Yashiro-gashi wharf to Tsukiji Western Gate ruins burned down

- Same 6th year (1786), Year of the Fire Horse, 1st month: Ox constellation (Earth), pond water
  Great fires in various places throughout Edo

- Kansei 4 (1792), Year of the Water Rat, 7th month, 21st day, daytime: Legs constellation (Wood), mulberry tree
  From Azabu Kōgai Bridge to Banchō Kōji-machi area, Hikone Senjō-dana burned down

- Same 5th year (1793), Year of the Water Ox, 10th month, 25th day: Bond constellation (Fire), same tree
  Great fire in Edo, both theaters' name holders changed (Sakai-chō Tsuden'uchi, Fukiya-chō Kirinaga-kiri)

- Same 6th year (1794), Year of the Wood Tiger, 1st month, 10th day, middle of the hour of the Sheep: Stomach constellation (Water), mountain stream water
  From Kōji-machi 5-chōme to Shiba Sendai lord's residence

- Bunka 3 (1806), Year of the Fire Tiger, 3rd month, 4th day, hour of the Snake: Horn constellation (Wood), hearth fire

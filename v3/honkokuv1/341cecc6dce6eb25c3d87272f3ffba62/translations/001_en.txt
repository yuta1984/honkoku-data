This hanging scroll displayed here is
a single scroll that Kashima Yōnosuke painted
with his brush during an earthquake. Heaven and earth shake, earthquakes have
occurred repeatedly since ancient times, and recently in the
21st day of the 10th month of Genroku 16, it began to sway gently.
"Please, have mercy!" Yōnosuke tried to hold it down with a large stone, but
hearing tales of recent earthquakes in Shinshū, Kyoto, and Ōsaka【gourd illustration】that seemed all too real,
he was greatly shocked by this earthquake.
Houses lean and slide, fires break out - to save people from these disasters,
Yōnosuke painted this sacred image to calm earthquakes.
Those who worship it once will have their houses strengthened, and drinking sake,
this is the sacred form of Kashima protecting homes.
This sacred treasure brings infinite merit.

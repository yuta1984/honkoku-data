In the first district, Shirahata Inari Shrine burned down. In the second district, Mondo Bridge remained intact, and at that time market days were being held. In the third and fourth districts, as well as Hon-ishi-cho's first, second, third, and fourth districts, a stone shop called Higuchi in the Jukken-dana (Ten Shops) area burned down.

In Teppo-cho, Ko-denma-cho's first, second, and third districts, temples, shrines, and the new road, as well as the prison compound and Ishide-sama's residence in O-denma-shita-cho burned down. The Inari shrine in Ko-denma-kami-cho burned down, and Hakugin-cho's first district and second district were consumed by fire. From the kindling wood shops through that area, Hatago-cho (Inn District) largely remained intact, while the Inari shrine on the same new road burned down.

In Tadokoro-cho, some areas including Shosuke's residence and Chogorou's residence were affected. In Horitome first and second districts, the shop warehouses of Chojiya and Naraya collapsed. In Shin-zaimoku-cho, Suginomori Inari Shrine burned down, and Minami-zaimoku-cho, Shin-norimono-cho, Iwashiro-cho, Fukiya-cho, Fushimi-cho, Moto-Osaka-cho, Jinzaemon-cho, and the moat area near Oyaji Bridge were damaged.

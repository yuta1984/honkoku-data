Meanwhile, at Tatsu-no-kuchi, the west row houses of Lord Abe Ise-no-kami (110,000 koku) and Lord Matsudaira Kuranokashira (315,200 koku) suffered various degrees of collapse. On Daimyō-kōji, the front gate side of Lord Kuse Yamato-no-kami's residence (88,000 koku) collapsed greatly. Lord Ogasawara Uemon-no-suke's residence (22,700 koku) suffered enormous damage, and Lord Matsudaira Awa-no-kami's (257,500 koku) rear gate collapsed. Inside Wadakura Gate, the upper and middle residences of Lord Matsudaira Higo-no-kami (230,000 koku) burned down, and Lord Matsudaira Shimōsa-no-kami (100,000 koku) and Lord Naitō Kii-no-kami (59,000 koku) also burned. At Yayosu-gashi, Lord Matsudaira Sagami-no-kami's residence (325,000 koku), Lord Endō Tajima-no-kami's (12,000 koku) fire brigade residence were consumed by fire, Lord Matsudaira Ukyō (82,000 koku) was damaged, and Lord Nagai Tōtōmi-no-kami (36,000 koku) and Lord Honda Nakatsukasa (50,000 koku) were also damaged.

Outside Hibiya, Lord Matsudaira Hizen-no-kami's residence (357,000 koku) burned down, Lord Matsudaira Danzō-no-daisuke's residence (369,500 koku) partially burned, and Lord Abe Harima-no-kami (100,000 koku) suffered minor damage. The Satsuma domain's costume/ceremonial residence was consumed by fire. Lord Tanba Nagato-no-kami suffered minor damage, and Lord Arima Bingo-no-kami's residence (10,000 koku) burned down. Lord Nanbu Mino-no-kami (200,000 koku), Lord Matsudaira Tokinosuke (151,282 koku), Lord Itō Shuri (51,080 koku), and Lord Oguri Mino-no-kami (10,000 koku) all suffered from spreading fires. Lord Kamei Oki-no-kami's (46,000 koku) rear tenement buildings partially burned. From there, outside Toranomon Gate, the areas of Lord Kyōgoku Sado-no-kami (51,500 koku), Lord Inaba Iyo-no-kami (56,000 koku), and Lord Kinoshita Hida-no-kami (25,000 koku), and the area along Atago-shita street suffered only minor damage. From there, inside Sujikai Gate, from Suda-machi through Imagawa-bashi street, Jikken-dana, from Takaramachi to the Nihonbashi waterfront, this area suffered no particular damage. Similarly, along the back streets from the west side toward Gofuku-chō, and along Higashi-naka-dōri in the Yokkaichi area, there was minor damage. Ningyō-chō street and Koami-chō area also suffered some damage, while Matsushima-chō was approximately nine-tenths destroyed. In Kakigarachō, Lord Hayashi Higo-no-kami's residence suffered severe damage, and the residences in this area were greatly damaged overall. In Hama-chō, Lord Inoue Kawachi-no-kami (60,000 koku) suffered minor damage.

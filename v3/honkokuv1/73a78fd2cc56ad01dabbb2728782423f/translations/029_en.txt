During the day, when the sun's light fills the entire sky, such phenomena cannot be seen. During the quiet nighttime, such phenomena appear. This follows the same principle as comets and thunder and lightning. Sometimes during the day one may see soldiers and horses with spears and halberds moving across the sky, or palatial towers and buildings standing majestically. This occurs when sunlight is blocked by humid clouds - the humid clouds receive sunlight above and absorb the earth's shadows below, thus creating such phenomena. If reflected inversely on a water surface, this is similar to mirages. However, during winter when qi contracts and fire weakens, such phenomena do not appear. Moreover, stars that are dozens of times larger than the earth appear to people as merely single points. So how could people see buildings and figures at such distances?

　　Earthquakes
　Question: The earth is positioned in the center of heaven, with primordial qi rotating day and night, encompassed by the various heavens, yet it remains eternally still and unmoving. What causes earthquakes?

Answer: The earth is originally formed from the dregs and sediment of qi gathering into physical matter, bound within the rotation of primordial qi. Therefore it floats motionlessly in space without falling. Being extremely heavy, it penetrates through the center point for stabilization. ⟨Marginal note: Master Huangshi Zhai said: The earth moves while heaven is still. The earth rotates while heaven revolves. It is like a boat moving while the shore appears to move. The shore does not actually move - it is truly the boat that moves. I once questioned this, saying: If the earth rotates and heaven revolves, then the paths of the sun and moon would not deviate north and south. The degrees of the five planets would have no variation in speed. The earth would have no tremors. The seasons would have no cold and heat. However, heaven is light and clear, thus it rotates quickly. Earth is heavy and turbid, thus it remains stable. But when a boat travels several hundred li, people cannot sleep or eat peacefully. The earth's circumference is ninety thousand li - how could people be at peace in their sleeping and eating? It must be that heaven moves and revolves around the outside, while earth remains still and penetrates through the center. The teacher agreed with this.⟩ Around the earth's circumference there are interconnected apertures, some like honeycomb, some like mushroom gills. The qi of water and fire lies dormant within these. The qi surges and seeks to expand but cannot do so freely. Like when a person's muscles contract and pulse, this also follows the same principle as thunder. In places like the Arctic where it is extremely cold and cannot generate heat, there are fewer earthquakes.

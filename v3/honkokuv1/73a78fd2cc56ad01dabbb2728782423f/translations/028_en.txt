When smoke rises straight up in a stately manner, it is a sign of clear weather. Evening clouds with beautiful colors are a sign of clear weather. Many red clouds in the afternoon are a sign of clear weather. Clouds that appear like firelight are a sign of drought. When winter is very cold but suddenly becomes warm for several days, it is a sign of snow. When winter is cold and flocks of birds fly, it is a sign of snow. When disaster qi steams oppressively on summer days, it is a sign of hail. When clouds appear like motionless lines in the sky, it is a sign of hail. When well water suddenly becomes turbid, it is a sign of hail. When cloud fragments chase each other, with pale white cloud colors that gather and scatter irregularly, surrounding below the sun, when the sun and moon are dim with halos, when the sun is red with rings around it, when the moon passes through the constellations Fang, Ji, Bi, and Zhen, when stars at night flicker and leap, when the constellation Shen sways, when Venus appears in the morning - these are all signs of wind. Sometimes rain follows. In human affairs and natural phenomena, there are also ways to predict wind and rain. For example: itchy head, hot ears, red face, damp hair, dry body, aching limbs, birds flying about and making noise while circling in the sky, fish jumping out of water, swarms of ants emerging from holes, earthworms crossing roads, snakes basking in the sun, stone veins becoming moist, trees sweating, unclear qin sounds, dim drum sounds, lamp flames flickering and flashing, flames suddenly making sounds. These are all early signs of wind and rain.

On "Heaven Opening"
Question: Sometimes at night, there are large red and white lights in the sky, with flames that are extraordinary and unusual. Sometimes one sees military horses and tower pavilions, seemingly with form and sound. Common people call this "Heaven Opening its Gates," while the Hongfan Commentary calls it "Heaven Splitting." Which is correct?

Answer: This is fire qi that accidentally absorbs the qi of the earth below, accumulating and producing flames. It is like the state of gold and silver melting and bubbling in a furnace. This should occur below the lunar sphere, and since the great void is limitless, how could there be splitting and cracking? Moreover, the heavenly body is extremely solid. If it were to split, it would have been destroyed long ago. If there were gates that could open, then opening would be constant. Only during Emperor Hui's Yuankang second year in the second month it split once, in Tai'an second year in the eighth month it split once, in Emperor Mu's Shengping fifth year in the eighth month it split once, with a width of three or four...

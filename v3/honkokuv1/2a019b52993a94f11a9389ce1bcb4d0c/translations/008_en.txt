Chronic Rheumatism
　This disease occurs when the body is exposed to wind, cold, rain, and dampness regardless of the seasonal heat or cold, and is characterized by pain in muscles throughout the body. In the West, this is called the disease of the poor. Also, what our country's folk medicine calls senki, sunpaku, or shijude and gojukata are mostly attributed to this rheumatism.

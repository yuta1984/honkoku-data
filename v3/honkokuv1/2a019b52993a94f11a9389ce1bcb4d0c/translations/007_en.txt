　　　Bathing Method
Bathing is generally done twice or three times a day as the normal practice. However, extremely hot water is harmful. Lukewarm water is considered best.
After bathing, wipe the skin with dry cloth until there is no moisture whatsoever, immediately put on clothes, and never expose the body to the wind.
Those who bathe in this hot spring should especially moderate their food and drink and abstain from sexual relations

## Description of Surveying Instruments

**Chōgyoku (Plumb Bob)**
　　Weight (錘)
　　Thread
　　Needle (針)

**Suishin (Plumb Needle)**
　　Needle aperture

**Sumisashi-fude (Ink Marking Brush)** [character with bamboo radical over 心]

**Ken-nawa (Measuring Rope)**
　　Diagram showing the rope wound on a reel (waku)

**Ken-sao (Measuring Rod)**
　　Length: 9 shaku
　　Width: 2 sun
　　Metal ferrules: 4 locations　　　　From this point, the far half of the tip is shaved down

**Shirushi-kui (Marking Stakes)**
　　Used for measuring nearby distances (near-cho)

　　Used for measuring distant distances (far-cho)

　　Used with the large surveying board (daiban)

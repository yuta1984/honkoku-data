Answer: It forms a right angle with the Earth's axis, and all points on it are equidistant from both poles.

Question: What is the meridian of a given location?

Answer: It refers to the great circle that passes through that location and both poles.

Question: How does a meridian divide the Earth?

Answer: It divides it into two parts: the Eastern Hemisphere and the Western Hemisphere.

Question: What is the latitude of a given location?

Answer: It refers to the angular distance north or south from the equator.

Question: How is this measured?

Answer: It is measured using the meridian.

Question: How far can latitude measure?

Answer: Up to ninety degrees.

Question: What location corresponds to ninety degrees of latitude?

Answer: The poles.

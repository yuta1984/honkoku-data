Preface to Illustrated Astronomy
1. The original book of this work is titled "Illustrated Astronomy"
   and was written by an American scholar named Smith for elementary school education
   and published in Keiō 3 (1867)
1. The main purpose of this book is specifically to inform beginners
   of the general principles of astronomy. Therefore, all detailed annotations in the original have been omitted
1. Although there is a star chart at the end of the original volume, it is too detailed and difficult to reprint,
   so we have temporarily set it aside and will seek a simpler one to supplement it later
1. The measurements recorded in the volume are in the English system, so they are roughly equivalent to our country's measurements

　　　　　　Various Omens Concerning Fog

○If the fog is deep, it is a sign that rain will surely fall within three days.
○If there is no morning fog, it is a sign of clear weather.
○Morning fog foretells rain; evening fog foretells clear skies.
○If fog disperses before dawn and then gathers again, it is an omen of rain.
○If clouds and fog cover the sky after the sun rises, it is an omen of rain.

　　　　　　Various Omens Concerning Dew

○If dew does not fall, it means the heavenly energy does not descend to bestow its gifts upon the earth, and therefore both great and small droughts will occur. There may also be fires on the ground. Note that evening dew is born from yin, while morning dew is born from yang.

　　　　　　Various Omens Concerning Haze

○If the color of the haze is not a clear blue, it portends flooding that year. Also, if the haze is brief and lacks color, heavy rain will fall within three days. Furthermore, if it takes the shape of a reclining ox, a great wind will certainly blow the following day at the Hour of the Dragon (around 8 a.m.). If it takes the shape of a snake, it is a sign of famine.

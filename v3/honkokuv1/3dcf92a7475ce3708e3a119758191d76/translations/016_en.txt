○ If rain has fallen for a long time and a rainbow appears in the eastern sky at dusk, it is a sign that the weather will clear. Conversely, if after a long dry spell a rainbow appears in the western sky, rain will fall.

○ If a rainbow rises from below, the rain will stop by the following morning. Also, if it rises toward the west in the morning, rain will fall the next morning.

　　　　　Method for Creating the Appearance of a Rainbow

○ To experimentally create the form of a rainbow: on a calm, clear day with no wind, hold water in your mouth, face toward the setting sun, and blow the water out like mist. Within that spray of water vapor, you will invariably see the colors red and blue appear.

　　　　　Miscellaneous Divinations Concerning Solar and Lunar Eclipses

○ A lunar eclipse in the second month means that the price of millet will fall, and calamity will befall people.
○ A lunar eclipse in the third month means that the price of rice will rise, and people will go hungry.
○ A lunar eclipse in the fourth month means that the grain harvest will fail, and people will go hungry.
○ A lunar eclipse in the seventh month means that by the following year, the price of cattle and horses will rise.
○ A lunar eclipse in the ninth month means that the year will be difficult and the harvest will be poor.
○ A lunar eclipse in the tenth month means that by autumn, the price of the five grains will rise.
○ A solar eclipse in the eleventh month means that there will be famine, and people will suffer from epidemic disease.

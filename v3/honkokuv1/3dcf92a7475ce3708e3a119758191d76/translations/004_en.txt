○On the day of Rittō (the beginning of winter, 10th month node), if the east wind blows, thunder will sound that winter bringing misfortune. If the south wind blows, many people will suffer from epidemic diseases in the 5th month of the following year. If the west wind blows, it brings misfortune. If the north wind blows, that winter will have snow and cold that kills animals.
○On the day of Tōji (winter solstice, 11th month center), if the east wind blows, it governs disasters for people. If the south wind blows, the price of five grains will be high.

○From Risshun (beginning of spring, 1st month node) until around Shunbun (spring equinox), winds from the northeast promote the growth of all things. Winds from the southwest harm people.

○From Shunbun (spring equinox, 2nd month center) until around Rikka (beginning of summer), winds from due east help the growth of all things. Winds from due west harm all things.

○From Rikka (beginning of summer, 4th month node) until around Geshi (summer solstice), winds from the southeast harm people and things.

○From Geshi (summer solstice, 5th month center) until around Risshū (beginning of autumn), winds from due south help the growth of all things. Winds from due north harm people and things.

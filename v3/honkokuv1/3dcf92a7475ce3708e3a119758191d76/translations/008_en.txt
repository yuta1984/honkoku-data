Miscellaneous Divinations of Wind and Rain on New Moon and Last Day

○If there is a great wind on New Year's Day that breaks houses and blows up sand, know that the price of silk threads and similar goods will become expensive, and silkworms will fall ill, causing the five grains not to ripen.

○If there is wind and rain on the first day of the second month, rice will grow poorly and the price of rice will be high. Also, if there is heavy rain on the last day of the month, it will cause illness in people.

○If there is wind and rain on the first day of the third month, it will cause illness in people. Also, harmful insects will emerge and bring disaster to the plants in fields and gardens. If it rains on the last day, wheat will ripen.

○If there is heavy rain on the first day of the fourth month, there will be a disaster of locusts. Also, if it rains on the last day, wheat will grow poorly and the price of rice will be high.

○If there is wind and rain on the first day of the fifth month, the five grains will not ripen that year and people will suffer from famine.

○If there is wind and rain on the first day or last day of the sixth month, the price of rice will be high.

○If there is wind and rain on the first day of the seventh month, the price of rice will be high.

○If there is wind and rain on the first day of the eighth month, the price of hemp cloth and hemp seeds will be high.

○If there is wind and rain on the first day of the ninth month, drought will occur in the following spring, and great floods will occur in summer.

Kogai Pond: A small pond located at the northern foot of Shirane. There are small floating islands in the pond - the large ones are about twenty shaku, the small ones about ten shaku, and their location is not fixed as they float with the wind. There is a theory that the reference to "Daimyōjin" (Great Deity) refers to the worship of this pond.

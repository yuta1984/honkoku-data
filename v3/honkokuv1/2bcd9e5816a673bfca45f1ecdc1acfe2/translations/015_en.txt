The two forces of yin and yang are the origin of all things in heaven and earth. When yin and yang clash in the heavens, they create thunder; when they are in the earth, they create earthquakes. Thus, the great earthquake of Genroku 16, and more recently the great earthquakes at Sanjo in Echigo Province and at Zenkoji in Shinano Province, as well as the earthquakes and tsunamis in Kyoto and Osaka, have been extremely rare occurrences since ancient times. Here, on the second day of the tenth month of Ansei 2 (year of the wood rabbit), during the early hours of the boar, there was a great earthquake throughout the Kanto provinces that destroyed houses and toppled storehouses, causing tens of thousands to die from being crushed or burned in fires. There has never been such an example in previous eras - it was truly an unprecedented calamity.

Within the four ri square area of Great Edo, the numbers of damaged houses and storehouses, and the dead, are as follows:

Number of districts: 4,057 districts damaged, of which over 68,080 houses collapsed
Storehouses: Over 107,000 damaged
Daimyo residences: Over 300 damaged
Number of fires: 38 locations
Dead: Over 229,000 people

The sight of people losing parents, children, and siblings and grieving in sorrow was truly unbearable to witness. Those fortunate enough to escape near-certain death are told that if they write the following songs and place them in their hair, they will not be injured - these are earthquake charms:

○ Saved by the water god's warning, how joyful to enter within the six parts

Revelation of Kumano Gongen - a charm to avoid earthquake disaster:
○ Eight ridgepoles, nine gates, one door - my body enters within Izanagi

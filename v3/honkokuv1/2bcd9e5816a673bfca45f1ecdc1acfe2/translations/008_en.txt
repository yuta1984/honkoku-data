【Upper Section】
○Near Ōhashi, in the area around Inari-bori (Inari moat), the residences of various daimyo including Tayasu-sama, Kuze-sama, Sakai-sama, Hayashi-sama, Andō-sama, Kii-no-kami-sama, and Owari-no-kami's middle residence were all severely damaged.

○Ginza was severely damaged, and in Koami-chō, all the riverside warehouses suffered major damage.

○The Yakushi temple in Kayaba-chō remained undamaged.

○Makino-sama's residence was severely damaged.

○The stone walls of Ikkoku-bashi bridge collapsed, blocking traffic at this location.

【Lower Section】
○From Kyōbashi north entrance through Sumi-chō, Yanagi-chō, Gusoku-chō, Inaba-chō, Suzuki-chō, Hon-zaimoku-chō 8th district, Gorōbei-chō, Tatami-chō, Daikon-gashi, Take-chō, Kon'ya-chō to Bikuni-bashi, all burned down. In Minami-kaji-chō 1st and 2nd districts, Kanō Shinto area, about four or five storehouses remained. From Minami-tenma-chō 3rd district, 2nd district, Mizuguchi to the south, everything burned without exception. On the second day at the seventh hour (around 4 AM), the fire finally subsided. There were many casualties.

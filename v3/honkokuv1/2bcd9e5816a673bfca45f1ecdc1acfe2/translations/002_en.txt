○Earthquakes occur because yang energy lies beneath yin energy and is pressed by the yin, making it unable to rise, thus causing the earth to move. Also, there are holes in the earth like a honeycomb, where water flows underground and yang energy constantly enters and exits. When the yin and yang harmonize, all is normal, but if yang energy stagnates and cannot emerge, and after accumulating over months and years, the yang energy overflows beneath the yin and finally erupts, this causes the earth to shake. Therefore, when it first trembles, it is violent, but subsequent tremors are all gradually gentler.

So, since it was such a difficult time, five or six eloquent men went to the widow's house, saying they should try asking her for some assistance. "Regarding the matter for which we have come, as you well know, the whole country is suffering from crop failure and hardship. Our area in particular has reached a dead end, and everyone is thinking of abandoning their household goods and moving to some other province. However, each of us has wives, children, and elderly parents to support, so we are stuck here in distress with no options, which is why we have come to make this request of you.

If you have any savings at this time, we would very much like to borrow some to sustain our lives. We are sorry to trouble you," they said, pressing their hands together in supplication. Upon hearing this, the widow thought that surely such a situation would arise and had been waiting for this, but since there had been no word until now, she had been worrying about how everyone was faring.

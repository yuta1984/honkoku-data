Bamboo weirs were set across Yada Plain, but who would know of the mother trees in the deer-inhabited fields? Though people at Iwashita scattered east and west, they all gathered at one valley where the shrine entrance, Sanmyō, and companions entered the new town together. At Katakura and Kasuga, moonlight that emerged over Mount Mikasa in Nara shone clearly. Passing through Kamikido and the various small streets, where do our feet head? Whether to Hanjitsu, Yuzawa, or Nitta, our fates are bound together. When people of Yatōishi gather, what might there be at Beppu, at the lower shrine? Here is the boundary slope between life and death, where we come head-to-head in deadlock. Here we rely on the vow of Tenman Tenjin's sacred poem. Those who make people's efforts vain seek to gain reputation by claiming to pursue virtue - this understanding was reached at Katakura. Speaking of Takoro here, Sanada's provisions immediately brought Ōyachi and Kodaira together with child Mochizuki and Mitsui to stop at Kure. At Motai, bustling about under divine guidance at Kannon-ji temple, here too are the deep seal grounds and narrow paths along the ridges, racing around to the very end of the precious upper rice fields. From here lies the lower castle ahead, leading to the Hattan-da area. Though I thought this was Kirekubo, before I knew it, Ōhinata came into view.

They hurled insults saying "If Hōmi is to become a Buddha, let him make miso from excrement," and shouting "tokko takase" as they trampled in, crushing the three-pronged vajra to pieces. They said the bells and priest's staff were unnecessary and moved about violently like a rough disturbance, producing flames and striking about.

They performed Noh and court music in an extremely rough manner, struck the bottom of thousand-ryō money boxes, kicked them about chanting "untara untara to kanmatsume abiraunke," thoroughly smashing everything. Shouting "renge baya" with great energy, they put Bonten deity first in their penitential chanting, saying "The sun has already set behind the western peaks - we just haven't blown the conch shell yet." They immediately rushed into the Hotai-ya Buzaemon residence, smashed the portable stages to pieces, recklessly and randomly slashing even gold, silver, treasures, and clothing.

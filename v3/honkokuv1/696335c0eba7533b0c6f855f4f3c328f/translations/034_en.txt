right now." As the night grew late, they poured sake of all the finest varieties, drinking without pause from cup after cup like buckets, never changing. Tilting their hats, stumbling over the seating, their legs growing unsteady and weak, they collapsed beneath the pine trees in the cold — before they knew it, their companions had stopped coming, one after another. The riot had already pushed toward Mitsuzuka, intending to destroy the house of Kanzaburō. There, too, preparations had been made to provide cooked food, and since an offer was made, everyone came and received food. But within a short time, tempers flared and could no longer be reconciled, and taking that as a trigger, they rose up in violence — the doors and sliding screens too...

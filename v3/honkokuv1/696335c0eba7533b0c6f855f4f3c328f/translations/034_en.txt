As night deepened, the renowned sake connoisseurs never stopped pouring drinks. Drinking from bucket-like cups and tilting their hats, they stumbled over thresholds, their legs gave way and they collapsed under pine trees. Feeling cold, they found that their companions had somehow disappeared. Thus the disturbance quickly reached Mitsuzuka. They tried to destroy Kanzaburo's house. Since he too had prepared food distribution and made arrangements, everyone gathered to receive food. After a brief consultation, they used this as an excuse for anger and became violent, breaking down doors and sliding screens.

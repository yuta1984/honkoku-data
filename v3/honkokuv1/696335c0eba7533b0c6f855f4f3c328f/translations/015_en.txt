Hiding in the shadows and heaving great sighs, when they observed the state of this floating world, they saw that the merchants of Kōzuke Province (present-day Gunma) and Shinano Province (present-day Nagano) were extremely greedy and merciless people who suddenly hoarded grain, causing both provinces to run out of wheat, rice, and miscellaneous grains in a short time. Taking advantage of this opportunity, they thought to blow evil spirits into the situation and strike back at Mount Asama, but having learned from their previous painful experience, this would be difficult. So they decided to agitate the peasants to threaten the evil spirits of Mount Asama and relieve their frustration even a little. They erected bulletin boards at crossroads everywhere, writing: "The grain merchants of Shinshū and Jōshū have hoarded and hidden away the grain of both provinces, so on the 23rd day of the 9th month, we shall completely destroy all the grain shops of both provinces and others who have stockpiled grain." They posted these notices and, moreover, circulated ominous written messages of unknown origin. When rumors of these arrangements spread, village headmen and officials mediated with grain dealers in Matsuida, Shimonita, and other places, persuading them to sell at current market prices.

About three chō of Morishita-machi and from Tokiwa-machi 1st chōme to 3rd chōme were burned. Lord Ōta Settsu-no-kami's middle residence was half-burned, and the fire was stopped at Takabashi. Then a fire broke out from Aikawa-machi at Eitai Bridge, burning Kumai-machi, Nakajima-machi, Ōshima-machi, Hamaguri-machi, Tomiyoshi-machi, and Kitagawa-machi, but Iijima Kizaemon's (property) remained. Kuroi-machi and Naka-machi were stopped from burning at Hachiman-mae. Hachiman Shrine suffered no particular damage. Sanjūsangen-dō was severely damaged and collapsed. Then in Kiba, Kashima Seizaemon suffered damage, and Seikichi also suffered minor damage. Kashima Seijirō's rental shops were severely damaged, but Yorozuya Mataemon was unharmed. Ōtaya Kagikurō suffered minor damage. Then in Shinkawa, Kashima Seibei was damaged but did not collapse. A fire broke out from the third or fourth house in Higashie, burning about two to three chō.

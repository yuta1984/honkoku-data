From Kōzu to Odawara is just over one *ri* (approximately 4 km), and from Odawara to Yumoto is one and a half *ri*. For the total distance of just over three *ri*, one can travel to Yumoto by horse-drawn carriage or rickshaw (a single-passenger rickshaw on this section costs around 20 sen). Therefore, by taking the first train from Tokyo, one can arrive at Yumoto or Tōnozawa, take a bath, and return to Tokyo the same day. The speed and convenience of the train speaks for itself. Thanks to this development, the prosperity of the Seven Hot Springs of Hakone has grown to a hundredfold compared to former times.

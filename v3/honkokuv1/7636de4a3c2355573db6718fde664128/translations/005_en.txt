Even an explanation by an ordinary person leads one to believe in things unseen through its reasoning alone. How much more so should one not doubt the world of Mount Sumeru (Sumeru-kai), which was seen with penetrating clarity by the Great Sage (the Buddha), simply because ordinary people cannot see it? Furthermore, when one thoroughly investigates the principles of heaven and earth today, the theory of the Sumeru world fits the direct observations of our present day as perfectly as two halves of a tally stick. Let us now present the items worthy of debate regarding the Sumeru world, and resolve them one by one to show the way to those who have lost their path in this world.

　　　On Ships Appearing in the Sky

A person called "Scoresby," when he and his father each boarded whaling ships and went to the Arctic Ocean, became separated from his father's ship by a distance of more than ten ri, so they could not see each other. Suddenly, the shadow of a ship appeared in the sky, hanging upside down. Looking at it, it was precisely his father's ship. By seeing that shadow, he understood where the real ship was located.

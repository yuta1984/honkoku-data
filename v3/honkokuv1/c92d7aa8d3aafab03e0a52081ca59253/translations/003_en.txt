In regard to the recent earthquake and the fires that broke out, we have conducted an investigation into the deaths and injuries, and the findings are approximately as stated in the accompanying documents. However, it should be noted that in the Honjo and Fukagawa districts and elsewhere, the clearing of collapsed buildings and debris has not yet been completed, and there remain many towns where the investigation has not been fully carried out. It is possible that the number of deaths will increase further. Likewise, regarding the injured, there are those who have left to stay with relatives, family members, or people in nearby areas, and as there are still towns where the investigation is incomplete, we believe that upon completion of those investigations, the numbers will also increase considerably. Therefore, we respectfully submit this report along with the attached register.

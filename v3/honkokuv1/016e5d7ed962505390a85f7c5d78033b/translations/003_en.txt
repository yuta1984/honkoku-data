　A Poem on the Earthquake and Tsunami
The earthquake and the raging tides (tsunami) — what devastation since the Hōei era.
Facing peril, some fortunately escaped the danger; others, trying to avoid calamity, instead met with calamity.
Houses collapsed and buried the roads; bridges fell, and only the ships' masts rose towering above.
If you wish to understand the full extent of this recent calamity, you must seek out this very publication.
　　　　　　　　Sankyo Rōjin, composed in jest

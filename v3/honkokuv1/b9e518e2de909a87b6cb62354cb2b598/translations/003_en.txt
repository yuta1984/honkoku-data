Post-Earthquake Teachings

Post-Earthquake Teachings (Chishin-go-kyō) by Elder Kyūō-sai of Nankoku

A house is not noble because it is tall　　　Consider a single-story house as noble
Because one's bottom is heavy, it is difficult to be saved　　　Consider escaping as salvation
Fire is a lifelong disaster　　　If fire is not extinguished, all burn together
Earthquakes are ten-thousand-year celebrations　　　They shake continuously until life ends
When seeking shops, there are decidedly none　　　When there are no shops, set up outdoor stalls

Crossing the valley stream with round stones placed across it, going back and forth countless times, we finally emerge near a village. Before we know it, we arrive at Yugawara. It's called Miyakami Village. We rest at an inn that looks like a stable and take hot spring baths. Having suffered from the treacherous mountain path, we cannot walk on the return journey. From here we go to a place called Mongawa, and return to our lodgings in Atami by boat.

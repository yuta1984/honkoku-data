People in hardship and suffering
19 Now gather here in countless numbers
　　How pitiful and heartbreaking to hear

　　Suddenly bestowed relief money
20 Looking up to the gods and thinking of themselves
　　Sadness and joy come at once

Meiji 29 [1896]　埼玉Prefecture, Minami-Saitama County, Wado Village, No. 15
《Side note: July 9th | Same 12th》Printer, publisher and editor: Commoner Kawashima Okujirō

In the area around Yui and Okitsu, when we observed the coastal conditions the morning after that great windstorm, we saw large and small boats damaged and drifting in the waves that had been blown ashore - a sight that was truly shocking to behold. However, being a coastal area with views extending as far as the eye could see, the distant bays showed no signs of having experienced a storm, appearing as beautiful as always with forests lush and green, retaining their moisture, and villages with rising smoke that looked very prosperous. This account is merely illustrated here.

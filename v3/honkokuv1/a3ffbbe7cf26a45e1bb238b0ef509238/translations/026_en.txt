With all means of livelihood exhausted, at the beginning of autumn, he tearfully parted with his wife and daughter, remaining at home alone and gathering nuts and grass leaves for sustenance, but even this was insufficient. He wandered out at the beginning of the frost month (October) to beg for food elsewhere, but perhaps due to an existing illness, he suffered and never returned home.

The young child found it difficult to care for his grandmother, and their food supply was completely exhausted. The wife had only one brother back in her hometown, but he too was poor. Although people urged her to remarry, she refused to seek a second husband, and lived day by day working for others. During this time, she heard that her mother-in-law from her former household was suffering from hunger, so she hurried to visit and saw her condition with tears, then quickly returned home. With the small wages in rice that she had earned that day from working for others, she tenderly cared for her mother-in-law. During the day, she would spend all day going out to fields and into mountains, digging up roots of plants and trees, and in the evening would return home to serve her blind mother-in-law. Her devotion as a wife could not even be matched by Dong Yong's wife.

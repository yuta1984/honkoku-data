Regarding the Council Meeting within Komoro Castle - Volume 2

If you guide people through politics and make them equal through punishment, the people will only try to avoid punishment without feeling shame. However, if you guide them through virtue and make them equal through ritual propriety, they will have shame and moreover reach the correct path. When news reached Komoro Castle that the disturbance had already entered the domain, they sent an urgent report to Edo by express messenger. The officials from the administrative offices and middle chambers were gathered at the meeting place, deliberations were held, and it was decided to dispatch forces immediately.

At Hōfukuji Misayama Pass, peasants were stationed with orders to raise signal fires if bandits came. Smoke signals during the day, fire beacons at night were designated. All of this was nothing but lies and nonsense that caused such an uproar. There was one greedy and corrupt person who caused chaos throughout the entire province.

After the disturbance subsided, two magistrate officials came from Edo again - Magaribuchi Kai-no-kami and Makino Ōsumi-no-kami - with two to three hundred men to conduct various investigations, and many people were arrested. Magaribuchi came first, conducted a general investigation, and returned, but Makino arrived later and stayed for a long time, thoroughly investigating even those with old crimes in various villages and towns, arresting many people. Someone is said to have composed these verses:

"Though his name is 'Crooked Pond' (Magaribuchi), his heart is straight, so his coming to Kai Province was worthwhile and immediately effective."
"I thought Makino alone would achieve merit, but after Ōsumi, he doesn't search every corner."

On top of years of poor harvest and famine, there was such turmoil that gold and silver were exhausted...

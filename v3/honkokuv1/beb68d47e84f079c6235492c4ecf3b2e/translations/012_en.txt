Many houses collapsed in the remaining estates and districts that escaped fire. The front gate of Mirokuji Temple collapsed, and Yōtsū Temple and Chōraku Temple suffered major damage. The eastern area of Iyobashi Street was similarly affected.

18. Tokuyemon-chō burned from the second to third blocks. All areas suffered major collapse and damage.

19. At Ichinohashi on the right side of Ryōgoku Bridge, the stone walls collapsed. At Ekōin Temple in Motochō, the bell tower collapsed. The residence of Honda Naikura no Suke suffered major damage. Aioi-chō from the first to fifth blocks suffered major collapse, Midori-chō first and second blocks burned, and the fire stopped at the corner of the third block. Similarly, areas from the fourth and fifth blocks to Hana-chō and near Shuki Bridge burned. The Tsugaru residence suffered major damage. From Yanagihara fifth block, Kayaba-chō fourth block, Kameido Tenjin pilgrimage lodgings, Honjo Kawara-chō, Matsushiro-chō fourth block, Nakanogō, to Gohashi-chō - all completely collapsed with no storehouses remaining.

20. Near the Itsutsu-me ferry crossing in Honjo, about ten houses burned and were extinguished. Kameido Tenjin, Fumon-in, Kōmyōji, Hagi Temple, Kōzōji, Chōjuji, Kawabe, Chōtokuji, Kameido-chō, Yanagishima, Hōshōji, Myōken Shrine, Jōshōji, Oshiage Village, Saikyōji, Daiun Temple, Tokushōji, Eisenji, Zenshōji, Shunkei Temple, Hōnin Temple, Hashidōri, Reizan Temple, Honpōji, Daihōji, Shinsēji, Minami-Honjo Demura-chō, Kita-warishimomizu - many small estates in these areas collapsed, and several storehouses crumbled. On Ōkawa Street, the middle estate of Tōdō Izumi, the warehouse estate of Tsugaru suffered major damage, while the bamboo storehouse of Matsumae Izu no Kami remained intact. From Komadomeshi Street, Sugiura Iki no Kami's residence, similar daimyo lower estates, Mogami's residence, Ishihara-chō, Sekiun Temple, the ship inspection office, stables, to the riverside street - all suffered major damage with no remaining storehouses.

21. In Honjo Ishihara-chō, half the district burned. The residences of Mukai Shōgen and Tokuyama, Ushigomae pilgrimage lodgings, Ishihara-chō administrative district, Arai-chō, and Matsukura-chō all suffered major collapse.

22. Similarly, in Nakanogō Omotechō, half the district burned. Kita-Honjo Baba, Minami-Honjo Baba, Kyōniwa-chō, Tada Yakushi, and Myōtoiseki - all these areas completely collapsed with nothing remaining. The eastern Narihira area was similarly affected.

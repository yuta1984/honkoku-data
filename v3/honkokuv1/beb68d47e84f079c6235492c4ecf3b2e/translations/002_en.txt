On the night of October 2nd, Ansei 2 (1855), at the hour of four, an earthquake suddenly struck within a four-ri radius of Edo, causing countless destruction. Fires broke out from collapsed houses, and there were extremely many dead and injured. Those who remained safe among them were truly blessed by the divine protection of gods and buddhas. It is difficult to obtain detailed information to notify relatives in distant provinces and other countries. Therefore, to make various locations easily visible at a glance, the fire locations are arranged in a ranking format and detailed information is provided as follows.

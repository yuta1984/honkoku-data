▲Those traveling from Kyoto may follow the route described above.
Those coming from Fushimi need not enter Kyoto; there is a direct road leading to the Myōjin shrine beyond Shimotoba and then on to Kutsukake. 《Three ri from Fushimi》　Those traveling up from Osaka should ascend from Yodo and exit via Mizutare toward Ao-no-Kōmyōji Temple. However, from the Myōjin shrine to Kutsukake, there are many narrow paths through the small pine grove that can be confusing, so night travel should be avoided.

(Located slightly west of Hiromine.) Enshrines Lord Kibi. Shosha-zan Enkyō-ji Temple (the 27th temple of the Saigoku Thirty-Three Kannon Pilgrimage; temple lands of 833 koku; Tendai sect; principal deity Nyoirin Kannon; founded by the monk Shōkū; located 2.5 ri from Himeji).

There is a road that visits the above four sites (Masuisan Zuigan-ji, Hiromine Shrine, Hakuhei-yama, and Shosha-zan Enkyō-ji) and then leads out to Shinmachi. Though it is a detour of some 5 to 6 ri, these are places one must absolutely visit.

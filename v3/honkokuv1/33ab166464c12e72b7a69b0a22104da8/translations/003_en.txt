○ General Description of the Geography

The place where the hot springs are located is called "Yushima," and it lies within Kinosaki District of Tajima Province. There are three routes to reach it:

　Via Harima　　　(From Osaka to Yushima: approximately 51.5 ri and 8 chō)
　Via Sanda　　　(From Osaka to Yushima: approximately 39 ri and 6 chō)
　Via Fukuchiyama　(From Kyoto to Yushima: approximately 35.5 ri and 14 chō)

All of the above routes lead to a place called Toyooka, from which one travels three ri downstream by river boat.

Sasa-yu (Bamboo grass hot spring): Effectively dispels dampness from cold and heat
　　　One need only soak in this
　　　This is the fundamental hot spring. Long ago, it
　　　sprang forth from bamboo grass fields, hence the name.
　　　In ancient times, when Akagi Myōjin and the avatar deity of this mountain
　　　waged divine war, suffering greatly from illness,
　　　they bathed and washed in it, and immediately there was
　　　beneficial effect. This is because it is miraculous for
　　　sword wounds. Since the gods bathed in it, even now
　　　impurity is avoided and people are not permitted to enter.
Gosho-yu (Imperial hot spring): First and foremost miraculous for cuts. However,
　　　　it is best to first soak in the middle hot spring, then
　　　　enter this one afterward

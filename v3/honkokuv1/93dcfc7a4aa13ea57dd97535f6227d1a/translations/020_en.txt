He took out dice and said, "Come on, one!" and threw them, and one came up. Then he said "Five!" and threw them, and five came up. Just as he commanded, those numbers appeared. Watching from up in the tree, this was truly a mysterious thing. Thinking he must somehow obtain this, he immediately climbed down from the tree and said, "You possess a truly interesting item. Could you somehow wager this with us?" He pleaded earnestly. However, the man answered, "This is a tool of my trade. I could never part with it even for a moment. If I let it go, I would not be able to make a living. Please forgive me," showing no sign at all of wanting to give it up.

The more he refused, the more desperately this side wanted it, so he proposed, "Then let me trade it for my prized invisibility hat and invisibility straw raincoat." The man was clever and immediately agreed to the exchange. The man then asked again, "What do you dislike?" "I dislike thorny plants like brambles and trifoliate orange," he answered. When the man asked again "What do you hate?", he said "Soybean flour rice cakes are strictly forbidden to me." My ancestor was foolish, so thinking this was the truth, he took that dice...

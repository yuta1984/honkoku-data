The deity of Shinkai-yochi and others lost so badly at gambling that even the river water of their territory was taken as collateral, causing them to dry up the river. To this day, people must hold their breath when passing through the Sarukubo area. Also, the deity of Hirai conspired with Saimyoji to covet the Doriku deity, lost at gambling and had his estate taken. When that wasn't enough and he was about to be stripped naked, the Doriku deity left his estate and fled into the infinite void. He was later discovered and pursued, and even now there remains a Doriku deity mound. Since then, the Doriku deity of Hirai has been wandering without shelter and continues to suffer hardship to this day. When various tales were told about how these matters were extremely despicable, the deities of Shinano spoke in unison: "The Furui deity falsely claims to be at the border of both provinces, but while residing closer to Shinano, supporting Kozuke shows poor judgment. We want nothing more to do with Shinano." The deities of Kozuke responded: "In that case, we should bring Lord Furui to our side and have him keep watch over Asama and such, for our benefit..."

The traveling monk was invited over, and after listening to various stories, [the host deity] spoke: "We have gathered tonight for some deliberations. You must be tired from your travels and find this tedious, so please rest as you wish."

"Not at all, it is truly miraculous that I have been granted lodging tonight and am able to witness such a gathering of distinguished personages. I find it not burdensome at all. I would like to remain here and listen to your deliberations," he replied. "Very well then," [the host] said, and immediately had him seated nearby.

Regarding the assembly of deities from Kozuke and Shinano provinces at Suwa Shrine

Thus seated in order were: first, in the upper seat on the left, Myogi Daigongen of Mount Hakuun in Kozuke Province; second, Haruna Kaikyo Daigongen; third, Ichinomiya Nukisaki Daimyojin; fourth, Ninomiya Akagi Daimyojin; fifth, Nitta Daimyojin; sixth, Hachiman-gu of Mount Hachiman; and other deities including those of Sosha, Maebashi, and Isesaki, as well as shrines from the villages of Uma and Tone in Agatsuma District to the north, and to the east, beginning with Kumano Gongen of Usui Pass

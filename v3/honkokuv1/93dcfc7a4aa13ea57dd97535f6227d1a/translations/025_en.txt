The tension was terrifying as, already on the morning of the twenty-fourth day of the fifth month, they prepared for battle. Black Cloud and Horizontal Cloud led the vanguard, lighting signal fires and beating the war drums with thunderous booms, advancing as far as Kumoba-ga-hara. The deity of the mountain pass, seeing this, instructed Torinakanushi to send swift messengers to inform Mount Hakuun. At Mount Hakuun, they were thrown into panic and uproar; they gathered several tengu together and it appeared they moved out to Usui Pass to mount a defense. However, judging that their small force would be unable to withstand the enemy, they devised military strategies and cunning plans. After some days had passed, on the twenty-seventh day of the sixth month, a council decided they should advance once more. Since there had been intelligence sent from Usui to Mount Hakuun, urgent messages were sent to the gods of Haruna, Akagi, and the other deities of Kōzuke and Agatsuma, requesting reinforcements. Each gathered their wind forces, loosened the openings of their wind bags, and with a force of hundreds of thousands, they lined up from Ōzasa to Mount Nakao and the summit of Usui Pass, like the teeth of a comb, hanging curtains of white and black clouds and taking up their positions.

[The deity] continued to speak: "Since Amaterasu Omikami began to rule over the realm until this first year of Tenmei, approximately 3,188,847 years have passed, and the divine virtue has greatly declined. Those who conduct business borrowing the power of Buddhism prosper with wealth and success, while the gods of Shinto have greatly weakened and lost their divine authority. This is because many deities have neglected their protective duties over their assigned domains and failed to maintain their responsibilities, thereby harming their parishioners. Each deity should reform their improper and disrespectful conduct and purify the various sins, curses, heavenly crimes, earthly crimes, and countless other transgressions." When this was proclaimed, the deity of Togakushi responded: "As the chief deity commands, we have all failed to protect our assigned domains and often acted contrary to our duties. However, we of Mount Tateshina and Mount Kinpu unite our hearts with the chief deity to protect the treasures of the realm and guard agriculture. Following this example, all the local tutelary deities, clan gods, and shrines in the villages should unite their hearts as one..."

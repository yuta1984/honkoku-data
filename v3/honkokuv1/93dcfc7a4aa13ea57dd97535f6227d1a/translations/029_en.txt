When word of this reached Hakuun-zan (White Cloud Mountain), secret communications were sent in all directions and preparations for battle were urgently made. First and foremost, the Great Deity of Akagi, accompanied by the gods of Agatsuma, Gunma, Tone, Nitta and other districts, came forward to render assistance. The Gongen of Haruna fortified its own strongholds and shut itself within its castle walls. In Narukami district, forces known as Manriki Taro and Hayakaze and others were reinforced with large numbers of troops. In organizing the army's command, Mountain Wind Kojin (荒神), Hiyauchi Shumaru and others were ordered to guard the strongholds. The castle was firmly defended with vigilance against night attacks, and strict orders were issued declaring: "We must never sally forth from our boundaries; it is essential only to guard our own domain and prevent the enemy from approaching." The deities of Arafune, being on the border between Shinano and Kozuke provinces, found it difficult to join either side, and thus kept watch over their own single mountain.

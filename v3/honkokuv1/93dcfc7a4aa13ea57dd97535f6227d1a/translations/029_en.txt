When this matter reached Hakuunzan (Mount Shirane), communications were sent in all directions to prepare for war. First, Akagi Daimyojin, along with the deities of Agatsuma, Gunma, Tone, Nitta and other regions, joined forces to provide reinforcement. Haruna Gongen fortified the strategic points in front and took shelter within the castle. For Narukami district, they assigned large forces to Manriki Taro and Hayakaze among others as generals, and commanded Yamakaze Kojin, Hiyauchi, Shumaru and others to guard the strategic points and defend the castle firmly. They prepared for night attacks and gave strict orders that they must not sally forth from their position, but should focus only on defending their own territory and preventing enemy approaches, as this was most essential. The deities of Arafune, being on the border between Shinano and Kozuke provinces, found it difficult to join either side, so they focused on guarding their own mountain with vigilance.

This is outrageous and extremely wicked behavior. Moreover, in recent years, Suwa and others have used the waters of the Tenryu River solely for commercial profit, refusing to make it rain. Mount Asama and others have had the fires they should tend themselves tended by local wives and daughters-in-law instead, and because they treat fire carelessly, the flames rise high and become unmanageable. Generally speaking, the gods of Shinano have become too selfish and arrogant. If we present these matters in detail and appeal to the Divine Authority, imposing every possible sin and fault upon them, our hearts would be cleared, they thought, and in various ways

They deliberated and devised all manner of schemes to appeal to the Divine Authority. The Divine Authority then sent Hinomotonabiko-no-mikoto to prohibit fire, hung the eightfold clouds of heaven over Mount Asama, and ordered three years of confinement. All the gods of Shinano were made to bear unforgivable great sins and curses. At this, the gods of Kozuke (upper Musashi) all raised their voices together saying "How delightful!" and offered prayers of celebration.

(Continuing from the previous page) ...gnashing with wild hunger, wearing the armor of ignorance and earthly passions, bearing the helmet of the three poisons and five desires, outfitted with gauntlets and belly-armor of violent robbery, wearing greaves of wicked and lawless ways, carrying at their sides bows and arrows of arrogance and perverse pride, girded with swords of wanton cruelty, each rattling the iron staffs passed down from their forebears, they came rushing through the sky leading great numbers of their followers. Black Cloud Boy and Horizontal Cloud Boy greeted the assembled demons and declared: "This mountain of ours is the finest and most unrivaled mountain in all of Japan, and has been a sacred place forbidden to entry since ancient times. Yet through the machinations of Mount Hakuun, we have been subjected to such humiliation—how great indeed is the disgrace to Mount Asama and to us all! It is to exact revenge for this grievance that we have summoned you all here. Let each of you speak your intentions without reservation."

Upon hearing this, the descendants of the Kumbhanda demons, the progeny of the Pishaca demons, and the wicked Rakshasas opened their great maws of vile intent, clashed their fangs together, and with great roaring cries declared, "We shall smash those utterly impudent fools to pieces, every last one of them!"—glaring with eyes like mirrors ablaze with fury, gnashing their fangs like swords...

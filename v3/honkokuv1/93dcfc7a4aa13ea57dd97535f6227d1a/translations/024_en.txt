Wearing armor of ignorance and worldly desires, donning helmets of the three poisons and five desires, with gauntlets and breastplates of brigands and robbers, shin guards of evil and wickedness, carrying bows and arrows of arrogance and wrong pride, girding swords of indulgence and cruelty, each striking their ancestral iron clubs, leading great numbers of their retainers, they came racing through the sky. The black cloud demon child, horizontal cloud demon child, and other demons welcomed them, saying: "This mountain of ours is Japan's peerless famous mountain, a sacred place that has been protected and forbidden to outsiders since ancient times. That we should suffer such humiliation through the actions of Mount Hakuun is a disgrace to Mount Asama and to all of us. How greatly we must avenge this resentment is why we have summoned you all. Speak your thoughts without concealment." When they spoke these words in unison, the descendants of the Kumbhanda demons, the offspring of the Pishacha demons, and the evil Rakshasa demons opened their mouths wide with malicious intent blazing, gnashed their fangs, and raised their voices loudly, saying "We shall reduce those utterly insolent fools to dust, every last one!" Their eyes blazed like mirrors in anger, and they gnashed their sword-like fangs.

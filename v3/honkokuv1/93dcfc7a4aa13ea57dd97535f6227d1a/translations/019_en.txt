They established residences: Chira-tengu was sent to Shinano Province, Tame-ashi-tengu to Suruga Province, Tatsu-tengu to Kaga Province, Chiketsu-tengu to Kii Province, and Meimyō-tengu to Yamato Province. Besides these, there are over 120,000 greater tengu and over 80,000 lesser tengu retainers. Hitomi-tengu resides in Hōki Province, Asuka-tengu in Shimotsuke Province, Dōsoku-tengu in Dewa Province, and countless konoha-tengu (leaf tengu) dwelling in various mountains - their numbers are unknowable. Particularly those called himō-daikaku (hair-bearing, horn-wearing ones) have hair covering their entire bodies, horns on their foreheads, bird wings, bird beaks, and long noses. Though they suffer from the five declines and three heats, they can fly freely through heaven and earth in an instant. This ability is not inferior to Idaten, who can traverse the three thousand realms in the snap of a finger. That we specialize in gambling is absolutely certain. Long ago, when our ancestor was traveling for pleasure to a place called Mount Hōrai in Shinano Province, there was a great tree over ten jō tall.

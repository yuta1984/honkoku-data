Haruna Gongen said, "Since wind and thunder are under my jurisdiction, I do not exercise them carelessly. Although it is indeed difficult for each of us to follow the ancient rules as they were originally prescribed, if the chief deity sends rain once every ten days, then I too shall blow wind once every five days," speaking gently.

Hakuunzan said, "In recent years, even rain has a price attached to it, being bought and sold for one ryō gold and one bu, they say. People's faults are easy to see, but one's own faults are hard to recognize. Above all, Asama-yama, who should control the source of fire - are you perhaps putting it up for sale? Making light of fire that rarely

goes out, in recent years you set fires even on April 8th without concern, so no matter how we try to prevent it, since you produce it recklessly from the start, like money flowing down abundantly from money changers, we simply cannot prevent it all. In the future, it would be proper to set specific times from the ninth hour until the evening sixth hour, and establish a rule to erupt only about twice a month," he said.

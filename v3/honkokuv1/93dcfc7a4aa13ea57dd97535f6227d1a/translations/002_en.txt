The Tale of Mount Asama's Eruption, Volume 1　　　　　Kaisen-tei [Library seal mark]
　　　On Supernatural Disasters
According to Yang Xiong's "Jie Chao" (Resolving Ridicule), those who blaze with intense vigor will perish, and things that flourish with great momentum will come to an end. To see thunder and fire is because they are overflowing. Heaven restrains its voice, and earth conceals its heat. Those who are full will decline.

"This fire under my jurisdiction is neither the fire of Shiki Daibonten of India nor the fire of Ogara Star, nor is it the fire of Suijin Entei or Shukuyu of China. Even in our Japan, it is not the fire of Tenazuchi Ashinazuchi. Though it is a fire unparalleled in the three countries, I have performed my duties without negligence from morning till morning. This is extremely presumptuous behavior, and I absolutely need no interference!" he declared forcefully.

"You speak presumptuously with such a long tongue! Say one more word and see what happens!" The deity rolled up the sleeves of his divine garments, and divine conflict ensued.

The deity of drawing and sheathing at the First Shrine approached and said, "Both parties, please calm down. Your mutual arguments are pointless. Simply avoid all provocations and stop unreasonable behavior."

The distant gods, sitting respectfully in the lower seats, said, "As expected of the First Shrine of Kozuke Province - how dignified and divine your words are. Though we speak of the ways of the times, and while they say 'learn from above and below,' in reality those who learn from below become superior.

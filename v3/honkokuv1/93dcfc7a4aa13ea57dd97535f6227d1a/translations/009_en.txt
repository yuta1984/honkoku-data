The deities of Nakanotake, Arafune, Myogi, Hakone Shrine, as well as Usui, Kanra, Tago, and Midorino were seated in great numbers. Furthermore, on the right side, with Togakushi-yama Daigongen in the place of honor, were seated Asama-yama Daigongen, Tateshina, Yashio-ga-mine Daigongen, Kawakami Kinpu-san Daigongen, Kawanakajima Hachiman-gu, Tanokuchi Shinkai Daimyojin, Chikatsu Daimyojin, Yahata-juku Hachiman-gu, Matsubara and other deities with shrine territories, as well as Unokuchi Shiratori, Komoro Gion, Ueda Omiya, Nezumi Myojin, Nishi-no-miya, Shioda Shrine, and beginning with Sanada Hakusan, Mochizuki Otomo, Kasugane deities - in total, all the tutelary deities of the villages of Saku and Chiisagata districts, properly attired in court dress and seated in rows.

The chief deity proclaimed: "Tonight I have gathered all the deities here because there was a directive from the deity of Izumo to hold divine deliberations, and so I have invited you all here. First, let me speak generally, so prick up the eight ears of the sacred deer of Suwa and listen carefully." When he said this, all the deities respectfully bowed their heads in unison.

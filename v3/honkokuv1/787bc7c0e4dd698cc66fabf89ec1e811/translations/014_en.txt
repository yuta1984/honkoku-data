○In the 2nd month (February), the 7th, 17th, 21st, and 29th days are to be avoided for sea travel, as sudden storms are to be feared on these days.
○In the 3rd month (March), the 7th, 15th, 23rd, and 28th days are days when violent storms occur at sea, and therefore one should avoid boarding a boat.
○In the 4th month (April), the 1st, 8th, 23rd, and 25th days are days when one should be cautious at sea.
○In the 5th month (May), the 5th, 13th, and 21st days are days when one should avoid boarding a boat.
○In the 6th month (June), on the 12th and 24th days, those who ride boats should strictly avoid going out to sea.
○In the 7th month (July), on the 8th day, those who ride boats should exercise caution at sea.
○In the 8th month (August), on the 14th and 24th days, one should be careful when boarding a boat.

○In the 9th month (September), the 9th and 27th days are days when wind and waves arise at sea. Those who ride boats should take care.
○In the 10th month (October), on the 5th and 20th days, those who ride boats should be careful.
○In the 11th month (November), on the 14th and 29th days, those traveling by sea should pay close attention.
○In the 12th month (December), the 24th day is a day when wind and waves arise at sea. One should board with caution.

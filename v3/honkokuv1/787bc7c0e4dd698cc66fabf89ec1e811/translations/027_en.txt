**[Star Chart] Jiao (角, "Horn" — 1st Eastern Lunar Mansion)**
The two stars of Jiao are the first mansion of the Eastern Palace, aligned straight from south to north, and govern the creation and transformation of all things. If Mars (熒惑, Huoxi) transgresses Jiao, there will be drought. When these stars shine brightly, it is an omen of great peace throughout the realm.
People born under this star will suffer great misfortune in their youth due to matters concerning their spouse and children. In old age, however, all affairs will proceed according to their wishes. That said, it is inauspicious to handle or judge matters ahead of others.

**[Star Chart] Kang (亢, "Neck" — 2nd Eastern Lunar Mansion)**
When the four stars of Kang shine brightly, the four borders will submit to moral influence, lawsuits will be heard, and criminal cases (訴訟) will be properly adjudicated. These stars also govern epidemics. If the stars shift (in position), disease will be widespread; if they do not shine brightly, drought will occur.
People born under this star will find fortune and prosperity difficult to attain. In old age there will be misfortune, but if one abandons arrogance and humbles oneself, one will maintain safety and flourish.

**[Star Chart] Di (氐, "Root" — 3rd Eastern Lunar Mansion)**
When the four stars are visible, water sources dry up.
People born under this star will enjoy abundant fortune and prosperity, their wishes will be granted to their heart's content, and great good fortune will follow.

**[Star Chart] Fang (房, "Room" — 4th Eastern Lunar Mansion)**
The four stars govern the imperial carriage (the Emperor's procession), drought and mourning, and opening and closing (of gates or seasons).
People born under this star will enjoy good fortune while young, but in old age misfortune will arise, and one should be careful and prudent.

**[Star Chart] Xin (心, "Heart" — 5th Eastern Lunar Mansion)**
If Jupiter (歳星) watches over the three stars of Xin, the year's grain harvest will be abundant. When these stars shine brightly, civilization flourishes and the Way prospers.
People born under this star will repeatedly encounter dangers from fire and theft. Nevertheless, fortune and prosperity will be granted according to their wishes.

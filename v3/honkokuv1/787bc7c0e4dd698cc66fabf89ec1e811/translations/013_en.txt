　　　A Method to Demonstrate the Form of an Earthquake

〇A certain person, as an experiment, constructed a model of an earthquake to demonstrate its principles. For example, he filled a barrel that could hold two or three to (a unit of volume) with coarse sand, poured water into it, and made an opening (a spout) at the side of the bottom through which water could flow out, like a vessel that drips water. Then several tens of people took turns blowing breath through the spout, and when the hole of the spout was quickly blocked, the yang of the breath was pressed upon by the yin, and as the qi tried to escape, the barrel shook and moved on its own. When the yang qi was exhausted, the shaking subsided. Though it may seem like the play of a small child, its principle is not far from the truth. (Source: Wakan Sansai Zue)

　　　Days Inauspicious for Boarding a Boat

〇If there is no rain on the 25th and 26th of each month, it is inauspicious to launch a boat on the 3rd and 4th of the following month.
〇The 9th and 29th of the first month (January) are considered days of wind-related disasters at sea, and are days that ship captains avoid.

　　　　On the Theory of "Ichiyō Raifuku" (The Return of the Single Yang Force)

○ Five days and nights constitute one "kō" (a meteorological period); three kō constitute one "ki" (a solar term); six ki constitute one "toki" (a season). Four seasons make one year, and thus Heaven and Earth are formed. The distance between Heaven and Earth is 84,000 ri, and within that space exists the "chūwa no ki" (the harmonizing qi of balanced yin and yang). Dividing those 84,000 ri in two, the upper 42,000 ri is designated as the "yō-i" (yang position), and the lower 42,000 ri as the "in" (yin [position])……

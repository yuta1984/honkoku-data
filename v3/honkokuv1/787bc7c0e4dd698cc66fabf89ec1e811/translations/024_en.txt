**[Star Chart] Wall (壁, Bì) Mansion**
The two stars of the Wall mansion govern matters of literature/writing and land affairs. If Jupiter guards these stars, the five grains will be damaged by flooding. When the stars cluster together, events relating to fields and residences will occur.
People born under this star are predisposed to frequent illness and a short life, but if they always maintain an upright heart and mind, they shall be able to attain longevity.

**[Star Chart] Legs (奎, Kuí) Mansion**
The sixteen stars of the Legs mansion constitute the first mansion of the West and serve as Heaven's armory. They govern military affairs and suppress violence. When the central star of the Legs mansion is bright, great floods will occur. When the Legs mansion is bright, the realm will be at peace.
People born under this star are long-lived, but their fortune worsens in old age. In youth, their fortune is auspicious.

**[Star Chart] Bond (婁, Lóu) Mansion**
The three stars of the Bond mansion govern matters relating to parks, enclosures, and sacrificial animals. When bright, the realm will be at peace.
People born under this star will face misfortune in their youth, but in their later years they shall attain joy and pleasure, preserve their good fortune and prosperity, and flourish.

**[Star Chart] Stomach (胃, Wèi) Mansion**
The three stars of the Stomach mansion resemble the three legs of a ceremonial tripod (鼎). When bright, the four seasons will be harmonious and peaceful. When the stars cluster together, the price of the five grains will rise steeply.
People born under this star have a sickly constitution and many things will not go as they wish, but in old age, all matters will become auspicious.

**[Star Chart] Pleiades (昴, Mǎo) Mansion**
The seven stars of the Pleiades mansion are the ears and eyes of Heaven. They govern matters of imprisonment, presiding over mourning, and disputes and quarrels.
People born under this star will, in their youth, frequently suffer great mental anguish over many matters, but their fortune will improve in old age.

**[Star Chart] Net (畢, Bì) Mansion**
The eight stars of the Net mansion govern hunting with nets and arrows, streets and alleyways, and rainy weather. They are Heaven's Master of Rain. When the stars are bright and stable, the realm will be at peace.

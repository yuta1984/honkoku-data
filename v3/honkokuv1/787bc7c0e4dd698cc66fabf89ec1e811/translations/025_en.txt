【Bi (畢) Lunar Mansion — continued】
People born under this star will find it difficult to maintain good fortune and prosperity, and their plans and ambitions will likewise be difficult to sustain — this is greatly inauspicious. Nevertheless, if one is prudent in conduct and keeps an honest and upright heart, one may preserve one's blessings and escape misfortune.

【Zi (觜) Lunar Mansion】
When the three stars of Zi are bright and large, the realm is at peace and the five grains ripen abundantly. However, when they move and shine brightly, bands of thieves and robbers will roam freely.
People born under this star will find it difficult to settle in one place throughout their lives, and old age will bring misfortune. Nevertheless, if one shows compassion to others and does not oppose or defy others, things will go as one wishes.

【Shen (参) Lunar Mansion】
The ten stars of Shen represent loyal ministers and filial children. When they are bright and large, ministers show loyalty and children show filial piety, and all is peaceful and auspicious. The constellation also governs matters of cutting, reaping, and armed conflict.
People born under this star will maintain good fortune and prosperity throughout their lives, and moreover will live long, with their aspirations and desires resolving themselves just as they wish.

【Jing (井) Lunar Mansion】
The eight stars of Jing form the first lunar mansion of the southern quarter, arrayed horizontally in the midst of the Milky Way. They govern springs and water. If Mars (the "Sparkling Deluder," 蛍惑) violates this constellation, severe drought will follow.
People born under this star are born with weak ties to a spouse and children. Nevertheless, they will find that all matters become auspicious in their old age.

【Gui (鬼) Lunar Mansion】
The five stars of Gui are the eyes of heaven. They govern sight and perceive stratagems with clarity. When they are bright, the five grains ripen abundantly. They also govern death and illness.
People born under this star will have much to trouble their hearts in their youth. Nevertheless, they will attain great good fortune when they reach old age.

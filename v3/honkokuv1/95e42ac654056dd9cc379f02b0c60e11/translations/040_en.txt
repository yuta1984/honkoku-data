**Advance Notice**
**Record of Tsunami Disaster — Volume Three (Lower Volume)**

The tsunami of this occasion was truly an unprecedented disaster in our nation's history. Raging, furious waves crashed in reverse upon the entire Sanriku coastline, sweeping all before them across a distance of many tens of leagues. Without exception, every inlet and every village in their path was mercilessly struck — houses were swept away, farmlands destroyed, and thirty thousand souls were transformed in a single night into spirits left without proper memorial rites.

Upon receiving this news, our company immediately dispatched investigators to the affected areas, gathering firsthand accounts and illustrations to faithfully document the actual conditions on the ground. Hoping that these records might also serve as historical materials for future generations, we published a special supplementary edition of *Fūzoku Gahō* under the title *Record of Tsunami Disaster*. To our surprise, it was received with extraordinary acclaim, and the upper volume went through multiple print runs. That we are now honored to publish the middle volume as well is due entirely to our company's sincere and earnest efforts, and to our readers' prompt and heartfelt sympathy for the victims. The lower volume is scheduled for publication on August 5th, and we humbly request that you continue to read it with even greater enthusiasm than you have shown for the upper and middle volumes.

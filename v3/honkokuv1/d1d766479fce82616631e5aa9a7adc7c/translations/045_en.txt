**Ōkoma Village**
Of the 130 houses in the village, 8 remained standing while all the rest were completely destroyed. More than 30 people died, and over 20 horses and cattle perished.

**Kodama Village**
Of the 80 houses in the village, 5 remained standing while all the rest were completely destroyed. Approximately 14 to 15 people died, 10 horses and cattle perished, and there were also injured persons.

**Muroi**
All 130 houses in the area were completely destroyed. A fire broke out from the bathhouse, burning down more than 20 houses. 160 people died in total — this was reported to the lord of the domain. 24 horses and cattle perished, and the number of injured persons is unknown.

**Hiraide**
In this area, [some houses] remained and [some] were destroyed; 13 people died, more than 10 horses and cattle perished, and the number of injured persons is unknown.

**Aida-no-shuku, Yoshimura**
This village of more than 150 houses was struck by violent shaking at the river crossing, and a mountain landslide/debris flow buried the entire area underground, turning it into sunken ground. A total of 350 to 360 people — young and old alike — along with 40 to 50 horses and cattle were all buried alive.

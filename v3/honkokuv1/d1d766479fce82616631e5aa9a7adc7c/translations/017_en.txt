At my residence in Iiyama, Shinshū Province, a great earthquake occurred around 10 PM on the 24th of last month, as I had previously reported to you. Since then, the tremors have continued incessantly day and night. While details from distant villages remain unclear, the damage to the castle grounds and castle town is as follows:

Main Bailey (Honmaru):
- One watchtower (watari-yagura) collapsed
- One crowned gate (kabuki-mon) collapsed  
- Stone walls collapsed in two locations
- All surrounding walls collapsed
- One storehouse collapsed
- One storehouse half-collapsed
- One two-story tower damaged
- One storage building collapsed

Second Bailey (Ninomaru):
- One gate half-collapsed
- All surrounding walls collapsed
- Residential quarters half-collapsed
- Three storehouses collapsed
- One resting pavilion collapsed

Belt Bailey (Obi-kuruwa):
- One armory damaged
- One armory half-collapsed
- One guardhouse collapsed
- Surrounding walls (western and eastern sections) collapsed
- One storage building damaged

Third Bailey (San-no-maru):
- One gate collapsed
- One tower collapsed
- One storehouse similarly collapsed
- All surrounding walls collapsed

Western Bailey (Nishi-kuruwa):
- Left and right sides of one gate damaged
- Residential quarters half-collapsed

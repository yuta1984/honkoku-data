Complete Illustrated Guide to the Seven Hot Springs of Hakone
Guide to the Seven Hot Springs
Preface

Generally speaking, great Confucian scholars and renowned physicians explain the principles of hot springs and enumerate their therapeutic effects. While these principles are the same in both Japan and China, their respective effects differ from one another. As for the principles, if they provide no benefit for illness, it may not be necessary to study them thoroughly. However, regarding their effects, those who practice medicine must understand them. I have decided to focus my attention on this matter, and in the eighth year of Bunka (1811), together with my friend Rōka, brushing our sleeves white as the snow of deutzia flowers, we took our walking sticks and headed southwest.

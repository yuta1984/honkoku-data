Ikaho Hot Springs　《Ikaho Village, Gunma District, Kozuke Province》

The hot springs of this place have been known since the reign of Emperor Suinin, when many people bathed here and discovered their beneficial effects on various ailments. Year after year, their therapeutic properties became widely known throughout society. In recent years, following chemical analysis of the spring water quality, it has become even more renowned for its particular efficacy for women's health. Men and women from both urban and rural areas now endure treacherous journeys to recuperate here, with most returning home in good health, making this a truly famous hot spring.

Tonosawa Hot Springs, Same District

Hot Spring Efficacy
The spring quality of this hot spring
contains cinnabar and salt,
and is effective for various ailments.

Efficacy
Dizziness, gonorrhea
Excessive bleeding, leucorrhea
Headaches, bruises
Rectal bleeding, apoplexy
Lung pain, muscle ailments
Cold constitution, cuts
Hemorrhoids, hernia
Phlegm, genital discharge
Intestinal bloating

Mount Kintoki

Located in the direction of
the Hakone Highway,
it is said that in this mountain
the mountain witch (yamauba)
raised Kintoki.
In joruri puppet theater texts it is called
Ashigara Mountain, but the original name
is said to be Inohana Mountain (Boar's Nose Mountain).

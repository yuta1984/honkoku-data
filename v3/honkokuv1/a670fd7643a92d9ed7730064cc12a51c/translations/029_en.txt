Ashi-no-yu Hot Spring (Ashigara-shimo County, Ashino-yu Village)

The hot springs in this area have no individual owners (yu-nushi) and are all communal baths (sō-yu) that are extremely clean.
Additionally, there are "maku-yu" (curtained baths), which are private bathing rooms that can be rented exclusively upon request by guests.

Seneki-yu Bath
　Spring quality: Contains four elements - sulfur, alum, salt, and iron
　Efficacy:
　Ringworm, scalp diseases
　Body odor, rheumatism
　Bruises, muscle strains
　Beriberi, athlete's foot
　Lower back pain, poor circulation
　Numbness, headaches
　Gonorrhea, roundworms
　Skin diseases, diarrhea
　Anal fistula, syphilis

Daruma-yu Bath
　Spring quality: Same as above
　Effective for all eye diseases

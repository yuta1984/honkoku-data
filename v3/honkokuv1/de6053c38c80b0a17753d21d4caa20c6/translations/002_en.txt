**The Great Earthquake of the Night of the 14th Day of the 6th Month of Kaei 7 (1854), Year of the Tiger — Rumors from All Directions**
(Note: Details of fires that broke out; the earth shook seventy-three times from the night of the 14th until the evening of the 16th.)

○ Nanto (Nara), Yamato Province: Beginning at the 8th hour of the Ox (approximately 2:00 a.m.) on the morning of the 15th, with a great tremor at the 5th hour (approximately 8:00 a.m.). Seven to eight tenths of all houses were destroyed. Over 300 people were killed instantly; the number of injured was beyond counting.

○ Furuichi and Kizu areas, same province: At the same time, the shaking was particularly violent. The ground split open and muddy water gushed forth; many people were killed or injured.

　Kōriyama, same province: Shook at the same time, with a great tremor at the 8th hour of the Ox. Three tenths of houses in the town collapsed. Approximately 120 to 130 dead; many injured. All surrounding areas suffered similarly.

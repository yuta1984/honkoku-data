On the Minute Motion of the North Pole
First, if one does not know that the North Star has minute motion, it becomes impossible to measure the altitude above the horizon in various countries. For example, when measuring in a country where the North Pole's altitude is thirty-five degrees, depending on the time, it may appear to be more than thirty-six degrees.

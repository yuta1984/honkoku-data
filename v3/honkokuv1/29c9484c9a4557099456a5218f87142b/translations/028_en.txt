**A Discussion on the Subtle Movement of the North Pole Star (*Hokkyoku Bidō no Ben*)**

When one is unaware that the North Pole Star undergoes subtle movement, it becomes impossible to accurately survey and measure the altitude of the celestial pole (*shutsuchi*, i.e., geographic latitude) in various regions.

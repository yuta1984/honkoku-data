(Continued from the previous page)...though Mercury is a large star like the parent star of Vega (Orihime), it is covered by the residual light of the Sun and thus appears very small and reddish. In the morning of the twenty-sixth day of the intercalary sixth month of the Year of the Dragon in the Bunka era, it was observed at approximately the tenth degree of the Well Mansion (Jingxiu/Iinoshuku), and continued to be observed until the tenth day of the seventh month. This observation should be used as a benchmark for future measurements.

In the ninth month of Bunka 6 (1809, Year of the Snake), Mercury was visible. In the upper ten days of the third month of Bunka 8 (1811, Year of the Sheep), it was visible in the evening, at approximately the tenth degree of the Net Mansion (Bìxiu/Hitsunoshuku).

In the upper ten days of the eighth month of the same year (Bunka 8), it was visible at dawn at just over ten degrees of the Wing Mansion (Yìxiu/Yokunoshuku). In the upper ten days of the third month of Bunka 9 (1812, Year of the Monkey), it was visible in the evening, at the second to third degree of the Stomach Mansion (Wèixiu/Inoshuku). On the fifteenth day of the twelfth month of the same year (Bunka 9), it was visible at dawn at the first degree of the Winnowing Basket Mansion (Jīxiu/Kinoshuku). On the twenty-seventh day of the eighth month of Bunka 10 (1813, Year of the Rooster), it was visible at dawn at the thirteenth degree of the Extended Net Mansion (Zhānxiu/Chounoshuku). In the middle ten days of the fifth month of Bunka 11 (1814, Year of the Dog), it was visible in the evening.

The "lower half of the year" refers to the period from the day of entry into the fourth qi until the end of the final qi. What must be feared is this "guest qi" (kakki). The analysis of changes and variations is described in detail in the following sections on the six qi and in each month's entry.

〇The Year of Bing (丙): This is a year of excess in the Water Phase. It is called a "year of disharmony" (fuka no toshi).
〇The Year of Yin (寅): Governing Heaven (Siten) is Lesser Yang Ministerial Fire; Governing Earth (Zaisen) is Terminal Yin Wind-Wood.

"Bing" is the elder brother of Fire (Yang Fire)—it is yang, strong, and represents the husband. "Yin" is Wood. The Elemental Phase is Water. Since Water overcomes Fire (the controlling cycle), this is called a "year of disharmony," also referred to as a "record of epidemic [tendencies]" (ryūkō no ki).

〇On Water Phase: In the first month of the year Bingin (丙寅), the star Yaoguang (揺光, the seventh star of the Big Dipper) stands at the position of Gengyin (庚寅). Geng is Metal, and Metal generates Water (metal-generates-water cycle), hence it is designated as the Water Phase. This corresponds to the "First month, major establishment, Gengyin (庚寅)" noted in the almanac of the current year. The Taishi Tianyuan Ce (太始天元冊, "Primordial Celestial Origin Register") states: "In the years of Bing and Xin, the qi of the Dark Heavens (Xuantian) passes over the lunar lodges of Zhang (張), Yi (翼), Lou (婁), and Wei (胃), reaches the position of Bing-Xin, and is established as the Water Phase."

〇As described above, the Elemental Phase (Un), Governing Heaven (Siten), and Governing Earth (Zaisen) are arranged in three levels—upper, middle, and lower—in order to analyze the weather conditions and auspicious/inauspicious signs throughout the year. First, the "Lesser Yang Ministerial Fire" of Governing Heaven, being the workings of Heaven, occupies the upper position and corresponds to the upper half of the year. "Lesser Yang" means that yang is just beginning to arise and its qi is still small, hence "lesser yang." Ministerial Fire, with its exceedingly strict authority, carries out the transformations of summer qi. "Ministerial" (shō/sō) is like a prime minister (in common parlance, a senior councilor/karō). The Water Phase governs the entire year: in the upper half of the year, it operates in conjunction with the Lesser Yang Ministerial Fire of Governing Heaven; in the lower half of the year, it operates in conjunction with Terminal Yin Wind-Wood, carrying out the seasonal authority of spring qi. Since the qi transformation and elemental movement precede Heaven, the heavenly qi is orderly while the earthly qi is disturbed. Therefore, violent winds arise, trees are toppled, sand is sent flying, flames surge and spread, yin moves while yang transforms, and sudden rains respond accordingly. The planet Mars (Yinghuo-xing, 熒惑星) governs fire. With heat transformation, it shares the virtues of both fire and wood, and there may even be a great drought.

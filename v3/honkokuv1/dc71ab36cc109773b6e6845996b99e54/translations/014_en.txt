**[Section 1]**

Regarding the method of bathing: during the first one or two days, limit yourself to one, two, or at most three sessions per day. As the waters begin to agree more with your body, up to four or five sessions per day may be appropriate. However, simply because it feels pleasant, bathing excessively without regard for the difference between day and night is highly inadvisable. Elderly persons and those of weak constitution should take particular care in this regard. Those suffering from longstanding or chronic ailments should continue the bathing treatment over many days.

#### ○ Medical Applications

Based on the above analytical results, as this spring belongs to the category of Glauber's salt springs (芒硝泉, sodium sulfate springs), it may generally be applied to the following conditions:

- ○ Adiposis (Obesity)
- ○ Chronic constipation (i.e., habitual constipation), particularly constipation in sedentary workers
- ○ General plethora (excess of blood), and conditions of blood rushing to the head
- ○ Hepatomegaly (enlarged liver), hepatic congestion, portal vein obstruction, and hemorrhoids
- ○ Chronic catarrh of the intestines, nervous atony of the intestines, and congestion of the lower abdomen
- ○ Constipation associated with hypochondria and hysteria

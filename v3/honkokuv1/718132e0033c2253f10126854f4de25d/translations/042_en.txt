◎ **The Four-Story Beef Shop Collapses**　The well-known beef restaurant "Futaba" at Shin-Kyōbashi had recently completed the splendid construction of a new four-story tower. Perhaps because the structure was too lightly built and too tall, when the wind began to blow, the building swayed back and forth and creaked and groaned. All the occupants, overcome with fear, crawled without exception into the bamboo thicket behind the building and waited desperately for the wind to subside—whereupon the fourth floor suddenly came crashing down…

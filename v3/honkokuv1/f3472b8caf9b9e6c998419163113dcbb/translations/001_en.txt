Bunkyū 3, Year of the Boar, 11th Month, 21st Day　　　　　　Osaka　Main Investigation　　　　　　Great Fire

On the night of the 21st day of the 11th month of Bunkyū 3 (Year of the Boar), at the first quarter of the hour of the dog (around 8 PM), a fire broke out from the area north of the east end of Shinmachi Bridge at Nishi-yokobori. The fire was extinguished at the hour of the dragon (around 8 AM) on the 23rd day of the same month.

The fire extended: to the east as far as Tamatsukuri Niken-chaya, to the north in the Uemachi area as far as Tanimachi-dōri and Ōte-suji, to the south as far as Kunnosuke Bridge, and in the Senba area to the north from ＿＿-machi beach to Nagahori.

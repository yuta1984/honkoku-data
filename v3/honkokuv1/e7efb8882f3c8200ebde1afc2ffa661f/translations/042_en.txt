### Deserts of North America

Along the high ground on the eastern side of the Rocky Mountains, there are vast wastelands. Furthermore, the land between those mountains and the Sierra Nevada is largely barren and infertile.

### Deserts of South America

In this continent, there are the Campos Parecis and the Atacama Desert. The Campos Parecis desert is located near the headwaters of Paraguay. The Atacama desert lies to the north of Chile.

### Deserts of Australia

It is surmised that the interior of this great continent consists largely of barren sandy plains, marshlands, and grasslands.

Because the direction of valleys differs depending on the relative positions of the two mountains flanking them, valleys are divided into two types.

　　Chapter 1
　　　General Introduction

Geography is divided into three branches. The first is called Physical Geography (天然地理学), the second is called Political Geography (邦制地理学), and the third is called Mathematical Geography (算数地理学). What is explained here is Physical Geography, which discusses the composition of the Earth, beginning with seawater, land, atmosphere, wind and rain, humans and livestock, birds and fish, plants and trees, minerals and rocks, and so forth.

The third horizontal row, Horizontal Intensity of Magnetism, refers to the degree of magnetic response in the direction parallel to the horizontal plane of the ground. The figures listed in this table follow the unit (Unite) defined by Gauss.

The fourth and subsequent horizontal rows contain the names of the provinces, counties, cities, and towns where observations were conducted. As these were registered in the order in which operations proceeded along all major routes throughout the country, there are some places where entries from different provinces and counties overlap and intermingle.

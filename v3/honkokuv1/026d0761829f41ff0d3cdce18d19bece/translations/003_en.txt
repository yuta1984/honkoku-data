○Magnetic Force Observation Report (Ministry of Agriculture and Commerce)

The Geological Survey of the Ministry of Agriculture and Commerce conducted magnetic force observations along the roads throughout the country during Meiji 15 (1882) and Meiji 16 (1883), at intervals of approximately 5 to 6 ri. The objectives were: to determine the true position of meridians necessary for adjusting topographical maps, based on the declination of compass needles; and to investigate the relationship between magnetic force strength and the composition of rocks forming the earth's crust. In particular, this relationship is considered most significant in areas where iron ore and iron-bearing rocks are found.

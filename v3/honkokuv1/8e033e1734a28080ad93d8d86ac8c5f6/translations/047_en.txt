When discussing the purpose of maintaining one's health, it is always a good time throughout the year, and there are certainly no such seasonal limitations. For this purpose, it is acceptable to use mineral water that has been transported in bottles from the spring source, but since many people do not pay strict attention when bottling mineral water, I shall now describe one or two points about this.

When water contains a large amount of gas, the bottle mouth should always be tightly sealed. There are only two or three types of mineral springs suitable for transport in wooden containers. With those stored in wooden containers, much of the gas escapes, and many undergo decomposition due to contact between their chemical components and the container material. Even if the mineral spring contains no gas and its chemical components do not decompose upon contact with wood, the water often takes on the flavor and aroma of the wood, so one cannot say that such harm is completely avoided.

When bathers arrive at hot spring resorts, if they are healthy and come only for rest and recreation, they should first try to become familiar with local society as quickly as possible and share in its pleasures and amusements. However, it is important that all things be done in moderation, and one must not forget to follow the principle of the golden mean.

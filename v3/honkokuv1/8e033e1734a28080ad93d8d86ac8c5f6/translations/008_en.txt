Temperature: 212 degrees [Fahrenheit]. However, there is considerable variation depending on the time of the spring's output. This mineral spring is a saline mineral water containing large quantities of "chloro-alkali" and "chloride" earth salts, along with small quantities of sulfates. It is considered the most efficacious among the mineral springs of Izu. Furthermore, the fact that within this saline mineral water there exists yet another simple hot spring of a non-saline character is a matter to which scholars ought to pay due attention. ○ This location lies in a coastal bay, gradually rising in elevation and to be ascended slowly.

## Kōzuke Province (上野国)

**Gunma District (Surveyed) Ikaho (伊香保)**　Six *ri* from Takasaki. Temperature ranging from 104 to 113 degrees. Elevation above sea level approximately 30 *shaku*. The principal components of this spring are sodium sulfate ("soda") and common salt, which possess the ability to dissolve congealed matter and promote excretion (*kasei*). It is effective for the following conditions:

- Stomach weakness and leukorrhea (vaginal discharge)　→　Those suffering from the above conditions should take it internally. The dose per occasion should be from one to three *san* (small cups).
- Chronic and malignant rheumatism, rheumatic joint pain, lower back pain, neuralgia, numbness caused by mineral poisoning, and skin diseases such as stubborn tinea (*ganzen*) arising from measles and smallpox　→　Those suffering from the above conditions should apply it externally for benefit.
- Anemia, and uterine dysfunction including irregular menstruation and related conditions　→　Those suffering from the above conditions should benefit from both internal and external use. Dosage same as before.

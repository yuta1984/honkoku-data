## Kōzuke Province (上野国)

**Gunma District (Surveyed) Ikaho (伊香保)**　Six *ri* from Takasaki. Temperature ranging from 104 to 113 degrees. Elevation approximately 30 *shaku* above sea level. The principal constituents of this spring are sodium sulfate (*sōda*) and common salt, and by virtue of their dispersing and purgative properties, the spring is effective for the following conditions:

　· Stomach weakness; leucorrhea (*koshike*)
　　Patients with the above conditions should take this water internally for effect. Dosage should be from one to three cups per session.

　· Chronic severe rheumatism; rheumatic joint pain; lower back pain; neuralgia; paralysis (numbness) arising from mineral poisoning; skin diseases, namely stubborn eczema arising from measles or smallpox
　　Patients with the above conditions should use this water externally for effect.

　· Anemia; uterine dysfunction, namely menstrual irregularity, and related conditions
　　Patients with the above conditions should use this water both internally and externally. Dosage same as above.

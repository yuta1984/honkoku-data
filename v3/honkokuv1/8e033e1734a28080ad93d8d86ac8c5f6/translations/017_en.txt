○ Ishi-yu: Contains alum gas (all the following likewise). Recommended for colic and related conditions.
○ Kuga-yu: Recommended for lower back pain and coughing.
○ Daishi-yu: Recommended for consumptive exhaustion (*kyorō*) and moist skin sores.
○ Ō-yu: Recommended for headaches and infant toxic conditions (*taidoku*).
○ Gensai-yu: Recommended for stroke and scabies.

**(Surveyed) Tazawa (田沢)**　Located in Tazawa Village. Contains slightly less sulfurous gas than Bessho springs. Found in two locations.
○ (1) Temperature 102 degrees; contains sulfur and gas. Probably also contains carbonic acid gas. The water rises from ancient volcanic rock; flow rate approximately 5 *to* per minute.
○ (2) Same, 104 degrees; approximately 3 *to* 7 *shō*. All others same as above.

**Same (Local) Kutsukake (沓掛)**　Located in Kutsukake Village; also known as Ogura-yu. Spring quality uncertain. Recommended for sore toxins.

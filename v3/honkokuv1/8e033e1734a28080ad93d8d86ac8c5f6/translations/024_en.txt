The rocks of this area are greenish-gray, somewhat decomposed metamorphic rocks (Kamoikotan Group). There are hot springs at twelve locations, and their temperatures and approximate amounts of water flowing out per minute are listed below.

○Omiya-no-yu: Temperature 140°, outflow 2.5 koku ○Arima-yu: 138.2°, 1.5 koku ○Yonekura-yu: 138.2°, large amount ○Mukai-taki: 134.6° at the pipe mouth ○Inn's hot spring: same as above, 8 to 7 sho ○Ana-yu: same as above, 2 to 5 sho ○So-yu: same as above ○Fujiya-no-yu: 132.8°, 3 to 7 sho ○Chojiya-yu: 131°, small amount ○Kitsune-yu: 129.2°, small amount ○Old waterfall and new waterfall: 116.6° ○Mujina-yu: 104°, about 3 to

All these hot springs are of similar quality, have no odor or taste, contain no sediment, sulfur, or gas, and only have slightly white substances containing silica and calcium carbonate.

Yuhara (locality): Located in Yuhara village, effective for scabies, hernia, and phlegmatic asthma. There are two springs by the riverside, one with temperature 113° and one with 143.5°. Each flows about 5 to per minute; the spring quality contains no sulfur gas but minimal gas and has a small amount of white limestone-like sediment.

Ashinomaki: Springs emerge from rock crevices in Tsurunuma River, Ashinomaki village. There are three springs: spring A at 106°, spring B at 109.5°, spring C the same. The spring water is tasteless, contains no sulfur gas, and has minimal white salt sediment. Effective for eye diseases, beriberi, etc. Across from this hot spring is Oya-yu with similar temperature.

Yumata: Located in Yumata village, temperature 160°, effective for paralysis, beriberi, wounds, bruises, swellings, syphilis, etc. ○Ishi-yu: temperature 120°

Tokusa: Located in Tokusa village, temperature 130°, spring quality contains sulfur gas, effective for scabies.

Yunokami village: ○Tate-yu: temperature 110° ○Kiri-yu: same ○Saru-yu: temperature 105°

Onuma County, Hayato: Located in Hayato village, spring quality contains salt and has an astringent, salty taste. Effective for stagnant qi, wounds, and arm pain. Temperature 95°, bathhouse not yet built.

Gojojiki village: Spring quality contains salt, effective for muscle and bone pain, weakness from cold, hernia pain, etc. ○Ara-yu: temperature 120° ○Oisawa-yu: 130° ○Shimo-no-yu: 116°

Oya: Located in Oya village, temperature 90°, taste has acidic quality, effective for eye diseases, syphilis, etc.

Tamanashi: Springs from rock crevices in Nojiri River, Tamanashi village. Temperature 90°, has salty taste, effective for various sores.

Hachimachi: Springs from the banks of Nojiri River, Hachimachi village. Temperature 75°, has salty taste.

Yukura: Springs from rock crevices on the banks of Tadami River, Honmyo village. Temperature 95°, has salty taste, effective for hemorrhoids.

Oshio: Located in Oshio village, temperature 70°, has salty taste, effective for syphilis.

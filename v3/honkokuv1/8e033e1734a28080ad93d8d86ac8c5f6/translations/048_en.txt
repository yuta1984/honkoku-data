Therefore, this naturally helps one's health and leads to acquiring the good habit of retiring to bed early at night. Although mineral water is most effective, one must not mistakenly believe it can digest excessive eating and drinking, eliminate the harmful effects of intoxication from distilled spirits, or compensate for the fatigue of dancing and revelry all night long.

The quantity for taking mineral water varies among different types of mineral waters, so it is impossible to indicate a general rule; however, generally one may take two to three bowls each morning and use an additional two or three bowls during the day. The cups should be of ordinary medium capacity. The method of use is to allow fifteen to twenty minutes between each cup, during which time it is good to stroll in nearby forests. If one encounters inclement weather, one should walk under the eaves near the mineral springs.

After taking mineral water, if one wishes to eat, one must wait at least thirty minutes to one hour. Allowing water to overflow in the stomach is most contraindicated and sometimes causes severe symptoms. There was once a case where a chronic disease patient developed acute kidney inflammation due to this error.

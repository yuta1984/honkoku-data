Regarding bathing methods, there are three prohibitions. 《Section Eight》
During hot spring treatment, after four to five days or even a week has passed, the pain from bruises, puncture wounds, fractures, or conditions like beriberi, rheumatism, and hemorrhoids may unexpectedly increase significantly. However, one must never be alarmed by this. This is actually a sign that the hot spring is taking proper effect.

《Section Nine》
The places where it is extremely harmful to be hit by hot spring waterfalls are the three locations: head, chest, and abdomen. These must never be exposed to the waterfall. Generally, people mistakenly expose their heads to waterfalls thinking it will alleviate dizziness, but this is a grave error. This is because blood circulation concentrates where the waterfall strikes, so later on, headaches, dizziness, hot flashes, and blurred vision will come as calamities twice as severe as before. Also, if the chest and abdomen are struck, it will cause phlegm, constipation and urinary retention, poor abdominal condition, and will bring forth illnesses other than chronic diseases, causing various ailments to emerge.

　　　Spring water quality

Contains abundant salt content with an excellent taste
Sulfuric acid, hydrochloric acid (in large quantities), lime, copper
Alkali (in large quantities), alum earth (small amount present)

When the hot spring water is evaporated, crystalline deposits remain. When these
crystals are melted in intense fire and cooled, then dissolved in water
and tested with litmus paper, it turns blue

　　　Medical indications
Upon observing the condition of patients, [the springs] invigorate skin
function, promote perspiration, dilute thick blood,
suppress gastric acid, and [improve] appetite

Dryness/Moisture of the Land
Extremely humid

Cleanliness/Uncleanliness of the Land
Both the bathing rooms and the land are unclean

Source Spring
Springs forth from between the stone walls below Yakushi-do Hall at the foot of Yakushi Mountain. Water is guided by wooden conduits for 3-4 ken (approximately 5.4-7.2 meters) to reach the bathing pools. There are three spring sources.

Clarity/Turbidity
Clear spring

Before the day when the Five Chō laborers carved [the path], I had always prided myself on my courage and strength in climbing, but reaching this point, even my spirit became weak and my legs trembled.
As the dangers gradually ended, the grand view opened up, and as my initial shock settled, heroic excitement arose. On this day, smoke and clouds were distant and dim,
The mountains changed their appearance, appearing and disappearing at times, just like a group of dragons in the height of battle, their rising and falling, hiding and appearing, how sudden!
Several waves of whirling wind swept the long sky, blowing forth lotus-like peaks, green and prominent. I heard that on clear days the scenery is even grander,
Extending to Suruga, Tōtōmi, Izu, Sagami, Shinano, and Echigo provinces - what one can see spans more than ten thousand ri. The southern seas and northern seas opened to my field of vision.
I am a wanderer of rivers and lakes, having traveled through many scenic mountains and rivers. Though I praise their beauty with my mouth, my heart is never satisfied.
I always dislike when false reputation exceeds reality. Ah, the wonders of Mount Kinpō are truly wondrous - who will follow my journey
and verify my account?

On hearing of the Shinshū earthquake in the third month of Teii (1667)
In the snap of a finger, the human world sent calamity's ashes flying; I was shocked to hear urgent reports of this strange disaster. The earth's foundation suddenly broke and a thousand mountains shook,
The flames had barely subsided when ten thousand bones piled up. Though the year was in the fire signs of hinoe-uma, even if by chance, Heaven's dropping of warnings is surely not in vain.
Shinano was a place I had once traveled to; weeping once, dear friends came to me in dreams.
Disasters east and west have not yet been dispelled - what is the reason for this change in yin and yang? I already heard that great valleys were filled with sand and stone,
And further fear that the fields have turned to marsh. Starving mouths and wandering souls lose their way out, while bitter winds and sorrowful rains enter into long sighs.
The Goddess Nüwa has no place to apply her giant turtle legs; I can only think of King Wen of Zhou beginning to cultivate virtue.

Birthday verses for the eightieth birthday of Elder Gozān Kikuchi - two poems
Leader of the poetry world for fifty years, both great reputation and pure fortune completely fulfilled. Why is it that only enlightened scholars deeply observe the world,
And also recognize worthy people, receiving Heaven's generous favor? The Southern Pole star spirit in paintings, Eastern Mountain courtesans beside wine cups.
The fine affairs of this house pile up in poetry talks - good to supplement several new volumes.
Honored wine in high towers opens birthday banquets, the white-haired sage is truly a divine immortal. With his eyes he watches the vicissitudes of the morning market.

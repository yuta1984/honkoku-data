This hot spring has been gushing forth for many years already. No one has ever dug to examine this source. Now, fortunately, this is land under my jurisdiction, so I shall dig and examine it, he said. The residents said, "We have heard that this hot spring was long ago transferred by Buddhist deities from the ocean floor to this place through prayer. Please, we beg you to refrain from digging." The provincial governor did not believe them and immediately dug into the hot spring source. A vermillion bird emerged and flew away. The place seemed to penetrate deep inside, with talc stone laid out and mine shafts running in all directions.

The many holes resembled looking at lotus seed pods. Because of this, the provincial governor thought, "This is truly the work of Buddhist power and divine providence," and in fear and awe, he buried it back as it was before, it is said. May the hot spring vapors last forever - this is the highest blessing. Respectfully submitted.

Methods of Hot Spring Bathing

1. On the first day, one should pour hot water over oneself once. On the second and third days, one should bathe twice per day.
1. During the seven days, one should bathe three to four times each day.
1. During the second seven days (14 days), one should bathe five to six times each day.
1. During the third seven days (21 days), one should bathe six to seven times each day.
   However, the frequency of bathing should be increased or decreased according to the severity of the person's illness.

1. When the water is lukewarm, the stomach becomes distended. Although very hot water is best, excessively hot water causes fatigue. However, one should adjust to a good temperature according to one's stamina.

1. Hot spring water should not be drunk. If one drinks it, there will be signs of diarrhea. However, this depends on the strength or weakness of the person's stomach. Strong people may not experience such problems. One should not argue with biased opinions.

When the above bathing methods are appropriate, one's throat will become thirsty and one will desire much food and drink.

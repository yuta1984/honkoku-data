The Origin Story of Atami Hot Springs in Izu Province

In the fourth year of Emperor Jinken's reign, there was a man named Kashima no Hobe no Kimi who committed a crime and died in prison. However, the emperor's anger was still not appeased, and by imperial decree ordering that he be sunk to the bottom of the deep abyss in Kamo District of Tozu Province, government officials poled their boats and sank him in the lake water where swirling tides flowed backward.

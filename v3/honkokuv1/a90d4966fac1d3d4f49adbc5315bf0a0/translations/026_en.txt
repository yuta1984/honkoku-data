Question: What is the ratio between spring tides and neap tides?

Question: When do spring tides occur?
Answer: Twice in a lunar month, namely at new moon and full moon.
Question: When do neap tides occur?
Answer: Twice in a lunar month, namely at first quarter and last quarter.

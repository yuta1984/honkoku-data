Just as they were making a commotion, threatening to break in, an elderly retainer rushed out and commanded, "Who are you? Stand back! Scatter these rogues!" The hot-blooded young men surrounded the large group of enemies and easily captured their leader, dragging him to one side. The remaining villains all fled and returned home.

Thus, when they inquired about the princess, the lord looked upon her and said, "What a beautiful woman she is. What circumstances could have brought her here?" He summoned his wife, saying, "Look upon her." His wife observed her and said, "Indeed, she appears to be traveling, but looking closely, she seems to be a person of noble birth. Her age and bearing are quite extraordinary. From what country and of what station might she be? First, please tell us your story. If you speak truthfully, as you are a woman, we shall surely be able to help you."

The princess replied, "I am grateful that you listen to me with such kind words. How could I possibly conceal anything now? I am the only daughter of a wealthy lord worth fifty thousand koku in Shimotsuke Province. Due to these circumstances, because someone wished to court me, I incurred my parent's anger and was cast out. At Kawagoe Plain in Musashi Province..."

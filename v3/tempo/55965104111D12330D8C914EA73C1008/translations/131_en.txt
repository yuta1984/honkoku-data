The Kadokawa Dictionary of Place Names entry for Kitashiroishi Shinden (early modern period) in Yoshikawa Town, Niigata Prefecture states: "Both the 'Genroku Village Register' and 'Tenpō Village Register' record over 217 koku. Within the area, there is Zenchōji Temple of the Jōdo Shinshū Honganji sect (formerly known as Shingon sect Sankōji Temple) that existed before the shinden development."]

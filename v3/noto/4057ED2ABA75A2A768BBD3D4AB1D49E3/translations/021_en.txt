A Copy of the Report Submitted by the Village Headmen of Tonami District, Etchu Province, in Kyōhō 10 (1725)

1. Saigyō's Burial Mound
Located in Mitani Village of Genroku Group, Nakata Village. In former years, when Lord Hara Gorozaemon, the magistrate of Tonami District, resided in Ōshimizu Village of Magoroku Group, Uchijima Village, he had it relocated, and it remains in Ōshimizu Village to this day.

1. Nawa Pond
In the mountains owned by Minodani Village of Yozaemon Group, Mikiyo Village, there is a pond approximately 100 ken by 150 ken located at a height of about 8 chō. Water drains down 14 to 15 chō below. In years of drought, when rain ceremonies are performed at this pond, they are often efficacious.

1. Izumidani Water
In the mountains owned by Hayashimichi Village of the same group, poisonous water springs forth. It has an extremely strong smell of alcohol. Birds and beasts die if they drink it.

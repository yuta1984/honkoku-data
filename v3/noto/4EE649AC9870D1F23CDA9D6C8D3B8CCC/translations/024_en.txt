To the east, he ordered fires to be set from Tateyama, Unazuki, Tsurugi, and the foothills of Noto province, completely destroying and confiscating Kifune Moriyama, Maiyama, and all areas below. Additionally, he made Kuranosuke (Sassa Narimasa) surrender, and on the condition that he relied on Oda Nobuo, handed over his castle at Toyama, and came running in dressed as a monk, his life was pardoned.

First, regarding the matter of the Anengakou forces of Hida province, since they conspired secretly with the aforementioned Kuranosuke to plot rebellion, troops were dispatched to punish them. The matter of men and horses on the route to that province was indescribably difficult, with treacherous and perilous paths, but they crossed over the difficult mountain passes of Tsurugi and penetrated deep, completely beheading all those rebels and pacifying the entire province, bringing both provinces under control with complete freedom over swords and blades.

First, advancing horses to Kuranosuke's castle at Toyama, inspecting the entire province, posting provisional officers at various castles, and establishing provincial regulations. Then military forces were moved into the castle town of Oyama in Kaga province, where men and horses rested for three days. Recalling ordinary matters, though this was not unusual, highly valuing loyalty and pardoning while admonishing crimes - this is all part of governing the realm. Therefore, regarding the loyal service of Maeda Matazaemon Toshiie during the battle against Shibata Shuri Katsuie at Yanagase in Asai District of Omi province in previous years, now obtaining the opportunity to show gratitude for that service, two or three provinces are granted to Maeda Matazaemon Toshiie. Presented to Lord Mori Umanosuke.

According to analysis, based on the phrase "the 7th of this month, etc.," this is a letter from the intercalary 8th month.

First, according to the Zoku Honcho Tsugan: In the 8th month of Tensho 13 (1585), first day Kinotoi (4th day Mizunoe-Tora), Hideyoshi conducted a northern campaign against Sassa Narimasa of Etchu. On this day the vanguard had already departed. (6th day Kinoe-Tatsu) Hideyoshi departed from Osaka.

　　　Later 8th month, 11th day　　　Mataza Toshiie (seal)
　　　　　Kokutai-ji Temple
　　　　　　To the temple administrator
Regarding the abbot's quarters of your temple being taken to Moriyama for official use. In that case,
the remaining small temples shall be transferred without incident. As for the abbot's quarters, even temporary huts
may be constructed. Since you have sent a seal stating that there shall be no problems with the temple grounds,
even if such matters are repeatedly mentioned, we shall not accept them. Furthermore, if such matters are mentioned, you shall identify the names and
report them. We have also informed the messenger monk of this. Respectfully and humbly submitted.

## Map of the Ōda-naka Shinnō-zuka and Kame-zuka, Kashima District, Noto Province

According to the oral tradition of the Miyata clan, Shinto priests of Notobe-shimo village, this is the imperial mausoleum of the deity Ōirikino-mikoto.

**Shinnō-zuka (Prince's Mound)**
Precinct dimensions: approximately 150 ken east-to-west; height approximately 10 ken; summit approximately 10 ken × 11 ken

**Kame-zuka (Turtle Mound)**
Also known as the "御車塚" (Honorable Carriage Mound). Approximately 101 ken in length; height approximately 4 ken; approximately 6 ken × 8 ken.
Length of the "neck" (narrow section): approximately 14 ken

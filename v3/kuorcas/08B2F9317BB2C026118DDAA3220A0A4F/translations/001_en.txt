...Kamata・Shimokamo Talkie Productions...

"Song of Memories"
Starring Mizuhara Yuriko and Yamauchi Hikaru
Japan's first all-mountain talkie film, the third work of this year by director Shimazu Yasujiro. Location shooting traversing the Japan Alps.

"The Shining Throne"
Starring Oka Joji and Tanaka Kinuyo
A grand sports talkie mobilizing Kamata's all-sports young stars in a unique college story celebrating youth.

"Happy Times"
Starring Kawasaki Hiroko and Egawa Ureo
A newlywed humor all-talkie announced as a sister production to the previous works "The Bride's Sleep Talk" and "Before Marriage."

"Song of Japanese Women"
Starring Kurishima Sumiko and Takeuchi Ryoichi
An all-talkie that Kamata has been secretly preparing since its announcement. A great alarm bell for Japanese women in times of emergency.

"The Drunken Buddha"
Starring Hayashi Chojiro and Chihaya Akiko
A devoted ambitious work by director Kinugasa Teinosuke based on the masterful screenplay by the genius Yamagami Itaro. An epoch-making period drama talkie.

"Bat no Yasu-san"
Starring Bando Kotaro and Iizuka Toshiko
Based on Kimura Kinka's original work, directed by Akiyama Kosaku. A talkie featuring the highly regarded combination of Kotaro and Iizuka.

"Koimyo no Ginpei"
Starring Hayashi Chojiro and Inoue Hisae
Hasegawa Shin's masterpiece, his specialty chivalrous story. The fourth work by renowned talkie director Kinugasa Teinosuke.

"Toribeyama Love Suicide"
Starring Bando Kotaro and Iizuka Toshiko
A story as beautiful as a brocade print, filled with emotion. Kotaro's well-regarded erotic scenes, Shimokamo's specialty emotional drama.

Coming soon to theaters - Please look forward to it!

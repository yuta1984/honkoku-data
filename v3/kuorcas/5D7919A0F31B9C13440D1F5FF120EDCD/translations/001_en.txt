[Right Side]
"Cradle Song"
Shinko Tokyo's first film adaptation of an original work by Hamamoto Hiroshi

Originally published in "Fujin Club" (Ladies' Club magazine)
Original Story: Hamamoto Hiroshi
Script: Kisaragi Satoshi  
Director: Aoyama Saburo
Cinematography: Yukiyama Koichi
Opening: 24th

Starring: Mayama Kumiko
Co-starring: Nitta Minoru, Egawa Naomi, Sugiyama Yoshiko

Even her pure devotion, giving everything to first love, was cruelly trampled upon, and her beloved child has vanished without a trace! How pitiful! The beautiful young mother searches for her beloved child, singing the memorable "Cradle Song" with an aching heart. Let maidens weep! Let mothers weep! Shed tears for the sentiment of youth and pure love!

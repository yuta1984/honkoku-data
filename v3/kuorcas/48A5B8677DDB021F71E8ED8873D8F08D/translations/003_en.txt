◇Grand Public Opening Soon of Luxurious Shochiku Period Films◇

Now here, the three great leaders of Shochiku Cinema period films have completed their devoted works…………The three great films of mystery, interest, and chivalry that will cast ripples in the film world at the year's end are coming - behold the refreshing spirit and dashing lineup, with tremendous expectations……………

Nezumi Kozo Jirokichi [Part Two]　Kyoto Shimogamo Special Production Film
・Original work by Osaragi Jiro・Serialized in Kodan Club magazine・
・Hayashi Chojiro starring in dual roles・Special appearance by Onoe Eigoro・
Appearing and disappearing like a ghost!! What happens next in the story of the phantom thief Nezumi Kozo who continues his superhuman leaps within Edo's surveillance network……The strange tale of Jirokichi and Kotani Shinkuro, cornered in the dark tenement house with crisis approaching on the rooftop where Shinkuro rages, Nezumi Kozo appearing and disappearing freely on the ground, and the great leap of ronin Tachimi Yuichiro

Chivalrous Yakuza Way　Shochiku Cinema Kyoto Shimogamo Period Film
・Suimon Okichi・Original story and adaptation・Directed by Hirose Goro・
・Starring Bando Kotaro・Iizuka Toshiko………Co-starring・
In the world of duty and human emotion, traveling footwear, Kitaro of Nanao burns with chivalrous tears and the blood of gallant spirit in this thrilling sword film. Original work by Suimon Okichi's specialty yakuza story, directed by Hirose Goro, starring new star Bando Kotaro in collaboration with Iizuka Toshiko in this period drama

Muto Taheita　Ichikawa Utaemon Production Super Special Period Drama
・Original work by Sasaki Mitsuzou・Featured in Sunday Mainichi Special Issue・
・Ichikawa Utaemon・Oe Michiko…………………Co-starring・
Combined with the bored man who appeared in Sendai, this is the masterpiece of Utaemon films directed by Shirai Sentaro based on Sasaki Mitsuzou's original work………Serialized and written in Sunday Mainichi magazine, this grand period film takes its material from the tumultuous light and dark of the Meiji Restoration and the end of the shogunate

Memorandum of completion receipt document
To the Chief Inspector

Refined silver and other scrap silver shall be sold to the official silver mint and authorized buyers,
and those who need raw silver for silver implements shall purchase from the official silver mint and must not
conduct trade elsewhere. Although this was previously proclaimed, we have heard that there are those
who trade indiscriminately, which is improper. As previously proclaimed, this must be strictly observed, and trade
must not be conducted anywhere other than the official silver mint and authorized buyers.

1. Regarding silver leaf, official permits and raw materials for leaf production are distributed from the silver mint,
and craftsmen in Kyoto produce and sell these to the public. However, we have heard that there are those
in other provinces who produce silver leaf using questionable raw materials.
Since it is difficult for anyone other than the official silver leaf craftsmen of Kyoto to produce silver leaf
elsewhere, this must be completely prohibited.

As proclaimed in previous years, since various trade guilds were subsequently suspended,
there has been a misunderstanding that any trade may be conducted at will,
and in recent years it has become even more indiscriminate, with what appears to be clandestine production of silver leaf,
which is improper. Henceforth, this must be strictly observed: refined silver and other scrap silver
must not be traded anywhere other than the official silver mint, and regarding silver leaf,

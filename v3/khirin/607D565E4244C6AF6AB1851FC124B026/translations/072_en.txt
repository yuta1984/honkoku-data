　　　To the Chief Inspector
Regarding the five-ryō coins whose new minting has been ordered this time, they shall begin
circulation from the first day of the eleventh month. Small gold coins (koban) and one-bu coins will be gradually exchanged from the fifteenth day of the same month.
Furthermore, regarding small gold coins and one-bu coins, until further notice is given, they should be
mixed with the new gold coins, and receiving, disbursing, and exchanging should all proceed smoothly, and tax payments should
remain the same as before.

Memorandum of Submitted Written Acknowledgment
　　　To the Ōmetsuke (Senior Inspector)

Regarding the exchange method for old script silver and old nishu silver coins as well as the exchange locations, although this has been proclaimed previously, there remains a considerable amount that has not yet been exchanged. Since the exchange process involves various miscellaneous expenses, for those who have submitted old script silver and old nishu silver for exchange where the distance to the exchange location is considerable, various expenses have been provided according to the number of ri (distance). However, going forward, regardless of the distance near or far, 100 me of silver per kan of old script silver and 10 ryō of gold per 100 ryō of old nishu silver will be provided as allowances, so exchanges must be completed by the limit of the coming Inu (Dog Year) 10th month.

1. Regarding the proclamation of remelting circulating silver and minting new ichibu silver, and the previous proclamation that nishu silver circulation will be discontinued soon: those possessing either circulating silver or nishu silver should promptly submit them for exchange. For the owners who submit for exchange, 10 monme of silver per kan for circulating silver and 1 ryō of gold per 100 ryō for nishu silver will also be provided as allowances, so exchanges should be diligently carried out.

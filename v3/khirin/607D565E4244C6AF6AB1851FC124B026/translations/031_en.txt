Record of Completed Receipt Acknowledgments

This time, regarding the matter of exchanging old gold and silver nishu coins from the Shogunate, a written directive has been issued as stated in the separate document. Accordingly, it has been announced that this should be made known throughout the domain, so understanding this intent, please announce it throughout the districts. The above.
　　　11th month, 25th day

　　　To the Chief Inspector:
Regarding the matter of exchange locations for old gold and silver nibu coins and shinji nibu coins, it was announced in the 6th year of Tenpō that these would be held in reserve until the 10th month of the current 8th year of Tenpō. However, there are still remaining unexchanged coins, with particularly large remaining amounts of shinji nibu coins, so regarding the matter of exchange locations, please continue the current arrangement until the 10th month of next year (the year of the snake).

Additionally, we have heard that there are particularly large amounts of unexchanged old gold and silver coins remaining in the western and central provinces. In territories of various domains with extensive lands, there are naturally locations where the exchange process has been delayed, so the domain lords should conduct thorough investigations and ensure that these coins are brought to the nearest exchange locations for conversion.

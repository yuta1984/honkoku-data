Record of completed acknowledgment letter
One　　　　　Masterless samurai attached to Awa Province
　　　　　　Identity　Onodera Saburoemon
　　　　　　His adopted son
　　　　　　　　　Mizuno Tetsutaro
　　　　　　　　　　Age twenty-six years
The aforementioned Mizuno Tetsutaro, who is said to call himself either Mizuno Tetsutaro or Saito Tetsutaro - we were ordered to investigate and report details about what kind of family this Tetsutaro's birth family is, and also who served as go-between when he was adopted as a son, so that the circumstances may be clearly understood. Having humbly accepted this order, since we report that no such person exists in our group villages, we submit this report in writing regarding this matter.
This case is submitted jointly with Sato Kumesaburo.
　　Monkey year, 2nd month, submitted to the Office

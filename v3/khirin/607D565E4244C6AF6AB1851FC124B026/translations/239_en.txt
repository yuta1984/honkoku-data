　　　Memorandum of Additional Report
This time, regarding the cessation of five-ryo silver coins circulation from the shogunate, with this understanding, we were instructed to notify all villages and coastal areas in our group without omission and to compile and submit acknowledgment documents by group. We humbly acknowledged this and made the notifications, whereupon acknowledgment documents expressing humble compliance were submitted, and we have compiled these and respectfully submit them herewith.

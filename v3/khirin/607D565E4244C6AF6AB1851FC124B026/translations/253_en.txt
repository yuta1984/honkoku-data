Memorandum of Submission
Recently, an official notice has been issued from the
Shogunate regarding the Ezo land matter. In accordance with this notice,
we were ordered to take note of this matter and notify all villages in our group without omission,
and to compile and submit acknowledgment documents by administrative groups.
We reverently complied, and as the acknowledgment documents were submitted as shown in the separate document,
we have compiled them and submit herewith. The above.
　　　　　　　　　　Village Headman of Hayabuchi Village
　　19th day, 2nd month, year of the Dragon　　　　　　Goto Shosuke

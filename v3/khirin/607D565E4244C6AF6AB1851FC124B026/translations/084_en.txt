Those who possess old gold and silver coins and other such currency must urgently exchange them by the 10th month of the coming year of the Boar.
Item: Regarding grass-script two-bu coins and Bunsei-era recast two-shu silver coins, as there was also an announcement in the previous year of the Rooster that their circulation would eventually be discontinued, those who possess them must promptly present them to Gotō Sanemon's Silver Guild office, as well as to those currently handling exchange duties in Edo, Kyoto, Osaka, and other regions throughout the country for exchange.
So that this matter is thoroughly understood even in distant provinces, it should be carefully ordered by magistrates in shogunal territories and by domain lords and local lords in private domains.

Record of completion supplementary letter
This time, regarding the matter of recoining and exchanging circulating silver as ordered by the government, we have been given an official notice, and accordingly we were ordered to inform all villages in the district without exception, collect acknowledgment letters by group divisions, and submit everything.
Humbly accepting this order, we immediately communicated it to the group villages, whereupon acknowledgment letters were submitted as shown in the attached documents. Having collected these, we respectfully submit this report with a supplementary letter regarding the above matter.

　　　　Memorandum of Report
Regarding townspeople and villagers traveling by palanquin, although regulations were established during the Tenmei era, since this practice has become rampant in recent years, new regulations have been ordered once again. Accordingly, we were instructed to notify the associated villages without omission, to humbly acknowledge the official intent, and to compile and submit written acknowledgments. Having humbly notified the associated villages, written acknowledgments were submitted as shown in the separate document, which we hereby compile and present. The above.

Regarding townspeople and villagers, both men and women, traveling while keeping to the left side is a matter of course, however in recent years many people with poor understanding of proper conduct have been observed, which is quite problematic. First and foremost, when recognizing samurai, one should understand to keep to the side when passing by during travel.
   However, those leading horses in the countryside as well as porters carrying loads in towns and villages should understand to keep even further to the side.

1. Townspeople during hot weather place benches at shopfronts and along roadside edges for sitting, which results in improper behavior, and even when observing the manner of samurai passing by, they remain in misconduct, which is quite problematic. Henceforth, such improper behavior should be avoided and proper conduct should be understood.

1. Regarding countryside people, while it is difficult to manage having outriders and leading horses with long ropes, there appear to be occasional cases of those who misunderstand proper conduct. Henceforth, such matters should absolutely not occur, and first and foremost, at the foot of the mountain, one should understand to lead horses by holding their bridles during travel.

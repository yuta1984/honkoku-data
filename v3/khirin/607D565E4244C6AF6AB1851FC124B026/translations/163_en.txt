Record of completed acknowledgment document

On the 29th day of the 12th month of the previous Snake year, regarding
the peasant Kazaemon's widow who was murdered in Katori District, Shimōsa Province,
and the perpetrator who fled - a wanted notice for Ishimatsu, the son of said person, who is without fixed residence:

1. Age twenty-six years, appearing older than his age
1. Native of Katori District, Shimōsa Province  
1. Medium height and build with a sturdy bone structure
1. Round face with much facial hair, some smallpox scars
1. Reddish-dark complexion
1. Eyes with much black and sunken
1. Thick and dark eyebrows
1. Aquiline nose
1. Large mouth, quick speech, hoarse voice
1. Even teeth
1. Normal physique

Copy of the written directive issued by Lord Ōkubo Kaga-no-kami
from the 24th day of the 5th month of Tenpō 5, Year of the Horse

　　　　To the Grand Inspector:
In the provinces where Jinzen Shirō's scales are being used, officials from Jinzen Shirō's establishment shall
be sent around, and during scale inspections, those who possess many scales must not conceal any
but bring out all of them for inspection and approval. Naturally, any suspicious scales shall be
confiscated. This directive must be strictly observed.
The above applies to the Five Home Provinces, San'yōdō, Nankaidō, Saikaidō, and within San'indō:
Inaba, Hōki, Izumo, Iwami, Oki, and also Iki

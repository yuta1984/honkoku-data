Therefore, as written below, relief funds will be distributed according to the degree of poverty and the number of family members, so please take note of this. The above.

Kaei 3rd year, 11th month of the Dog year
　　　　　General Rules for Relief Fund Distribution

Upper category
1. Among those in extreme poverty:
　　　Those with six or more family members　　　60 monme per household
　　　Those with three to five family members　　　50 monme each
　　　Those with two or fewer family members　　　40 monme each

Middle category
1. Among those in severe poverty:
　　　Those with six or more family members　　　50 monme per household
　　　Those with three to five family members　　　40 monme each
　　　Those with two or fewer family members　　　30 monme each

Lower category
1. Among those in poverty:
　　　Those with six or more family members　　　40 monme per household
　　　Those with three to five family members　　　30 monme each

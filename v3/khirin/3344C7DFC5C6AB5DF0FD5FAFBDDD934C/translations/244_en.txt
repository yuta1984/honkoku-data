○
1. Regarding the gōshi (rural samurai): The gōshi duty officer shall summon one reliable person from each district,
   thoroughly discuss the matter of the official directive, have it communicated to their associates, and then
   one or two representatives wearing hemp kamishimo shall present themselves to express gratitude for receiving the order.
     However, regarding Ama district, this shall be communicated at Hiwasa.
○
1. Regarding the genshi (low-ranking samurai): The chief inspector shall summon all members of the same shift together, communicate the same as above,
   have it passed on to their associates, and then have them present themselves to express gratitude in the same manner.
○
1. Regarding Dairyūji, Kakurinji, Shōgon'in, and Kōseiji temples: Each temple shall be
   summoned and given the same communication, and shall present themselves to express gratitude.
1. Regarding minor fief holders and guard personnel at various locations: Two or three people from each district shall be summoned for the same discussion,
   have it communicated to their associates, and then one or two representatives shall present themselves to express gratitude.
○
1. Regarding rural gunners: The squad leaders shall be summoned and given the same communication to pass on to the rural gunners
   under their command, and then have them present themselves to express gratitude.

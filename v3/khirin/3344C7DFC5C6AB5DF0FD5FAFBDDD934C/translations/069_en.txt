　　　Kaei 2, Rooster year, 11th month, 17th day - Copy delivered to Matsue Shimasaburo

○【Red ink】Twenty-eight
This autumn, depending on regional conditions, poor harvests due to wind, rain, and floods have been reported. Consequently, rice exports from Osaka and other regions have been suspended due to high rice prices, further increasing grain prices. In our domain, while standing crops in northern villages have been damaged, rice cultivation in both north and south has experienced good harvests in recent years, and rice and wheat prices are gradually declining. Under current circumstances, competing with prices from other domains would not necessarily result in high prices. However, since this is a year when prices are high in various provinces, if people become complacent thinking of it as a good harvest and relax their vigilance, future hardships are unpredictable. Therefore, it is essential to gratefully observe the official instructions, pay attention to food matters, live frugally and economically, and extend food supplies. No matter how high grain prices become in other regions, it is crucial that people in our domain thoroughly understand the importance of continuing their livelihoods.

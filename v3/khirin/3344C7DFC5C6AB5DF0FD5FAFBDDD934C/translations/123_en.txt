Since there are no such benefits, regarding the borrowed money secured by domain administration documents and internal loans, for this winter season, half the principal and interest shall be paid for six months in the same manner. However, regarding the principal and interest deferred this season, they should be paid to the creditors when the respective loan terms expire. The reason for this directive is based on the generous consideration to help alleviate the hardships of the common people, so this should not be taken lightly in the slightest, but should be well understood and continued based on proper principles without any impropriety. This directive should be communicated to the domain retainers as well as residents of Ōsato, Ikeda, Sanmyōshi, Mugi-ura, and Kigi-ura, and also to temples and shrines. For those of lower status at the bottom of society, this should be communicated through their respective supervisors. Also, this should be communicated to the town magistrates, construction magistrates, district administrators, granary magistrates, new granary magistrates, and northern granary magistrates for their understanding, and this should be conveyed within the main administrative group.

However, regarding the administrative loan payment silver, although it was planned to grant the same deferment as other loans, due to the circumstances of the loans being granted through special funding arrangements, half the remaining principal and interest for this season shall be deferred. Particularly for rural payments, summer and winter payments may differ...

Notice to Village Headmen
Memorandum

In the southern and northern villages and coastal areas, many people are suffering hardships due to last year's earthquake and high tide. Regarding their livelihood, among the tsunami and various fires and collapsed houses in different places, those who are particularly impoverished and cannot even secure daily provisions are gradually receiving government assistance, and in some locations, temporary shelters have been built and rice gruel distributed to help them survive. From these places, not only within their own villages but also with other villages, people are mutually helping each other...

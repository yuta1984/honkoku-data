○【Written in vermillion】Twenty-two: Directive, ninth month of the first year of Kaei, year of the Monkey
Regarding the enforcement of regulations in the northern and southern reserved hunting ground villages, discipline has gradually become lax, and recently there have been frequent cases of thoughtless and misconduct. After deliberation on this matter for thorough investigation, and considering that there are existing directives from the past, it is generally ordered to follow the previous established decisions. However, since birds eating crops in the fields causes real hardship and difficulty for the farmers, in the Bunka era, through special deliberation, a relaxation was granted allowing the driving away of wild geese during approximately fifteen days of the grain-filling season from the end of the first month until the equinox. This practice has spread in various ways, and over the past year or two there have been many who have misunderstood the regulations. Therefore, this time again, strict reform is ordered according to the ancient established decisions. However, due to the aforementioned circumstances, various birds damage the fields

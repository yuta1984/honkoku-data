Memorandum

It is reported that all members of the retainer households, down to the lowest-ranking unranked individuals, are gradually falling into financial hardship. Moreover, in recent years there have been arrivals of foreign ships, and due to orders to guard against them, many retainers beginning with various samurai have been summoned to Edo, and preparations against foreign ships in the southern regions have also been ordered to be increasingly strict. Therefore, it is naturally essential to be even more thorough in military preparations, but under current circumstances, if the retainer households, down to the unranked members, fall further into poverty, this will affect military readiness and lead to even greater difficulties. Although this is a time when enormous expenses must be covered, due to special circumstances and with special consideration, for this winter season, tax remissions of up to 15% increase on the walk tax are ordered, and regarding loans from the financial office, from this winter season all advanced payments and interest...

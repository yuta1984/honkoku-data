　　　Instructions to Group Headmen and Village Chiefs regarding investigations at Myōdō Tezaki in Kaei 6, Year of the Ox

Regarding suspicious persons from other domains and unlicensed Shikoku pilgrims and similar types, although strict investigations have been conducted in recent years, they still appear to be infiltrating remote areas, which is a serious matter. Therefore, investigations are once again ordered. With thorough understanding of the previous instructions, starting from the 26th of this month, investigations shall be conducted simultaneously in all villages and coastal areas, with officials in attendance, and after expulsion at the nearest domain borders, their domain of origin and names shall be recorded in registers.

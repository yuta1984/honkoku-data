1. Oil produced in rural areas should be sold to urban markets as much as possible.

1. Where oil produced in market towns and rural areas is insufficient to meet domestic demand, Kamigata oil shall be procured concurrently, and as per the shogunate's official notification, orders shall be placed with oil wholesalers in Osaka's Uchihonmachi-hashizume district for purchase and distribution to urban markets. However, oil purchased for Tomioka Shikken'ya-machi shall have wholesale sales suspended.

1. Regarding cotton seeds and rapeseed, these shall be handled in urban markets through direct negotiation at the discretion of the parties involved, with prices and other matters to be conducted through legitimate trade as ordered.
　However, concerning cotton seeds, small collection points shall be established in rural areas, and regarding sales methods, this shall be according to the convenience of the common people, and those who establish market prices in market towns shall be appointed to ensure legitimate handling as ordered.

1. The export of cotton seeds, rapeseed, and lamp oil to other domains is prohibited. While crushed cotton seeds may be used directly as fertilizer for rice fields and dry fields, cotton seeds should be sold to produce oil cake for use as fertilizer.
　However, as an investigation measure for oil and oil cake exports, inspectors shall be dispatched from Tomioka Shikken'ya in urban markets, and should any smuggling occur, the cargo shall be confiscated and both cargo owners and ship captains shall be punished.

1. When there is surplus oil and oil cake beyond what is needed domestically, it shall be shipped and sold from Tomioka Shikken'ya in urban markets to designated locations such as Hyogo, Nada area, Osaka, etc.

One: On May 18th - Ten volumes of kinire (gold-decorated items) were presented in secret. (After seeking approval from Lord Tsuchiya Sagami-no-kami on the 13th) They were submitted to Lord Tsuchiya Sagami-no-kami Masanao through messenger and keeper Murakami Santayū Masamine. A notification slip came from Sagami-no-kami confirming that the presentation had been completed.
　　　　　　　　The kinire that were presented have been successfully
　　　　　　　　disclosed in private. I write to inform you of this matter.
　　　　　　　　　May 15th　　　　　Tsuchiya Sagami-no-kami
　　　　　　　　　　To Lord Matsudaira Hyōbu-taiyu

One: On the 16th of the same month - Visited the chamberlain of Lord Abe Bungo-no-kami Masatake, who was on duty, to request permission to attend kōdan (storytelling) lectures.
　　　　　　Note: Also visited on the 18th.
One: On June 6th - Due to the summer heat, presented local products of sōmen noodles and dried sea bream through a messenger as customary.
One: On July 6th - For the celebration of the birth examination ceremony, presented gold through a messenger as customary.
One: On July 9th - As the construction of the accounting office was completed, there was a celebration for the first accounting ceremony.
　　　　　　Note: The previous accounting office was located behind the Iron Gate (currently the construction office),
　　　　　　but as it became the residence of Lady Seishōin, it was
　　　　　　relocated to Sugita Gotayū's residence within Kawaguchi Gate.

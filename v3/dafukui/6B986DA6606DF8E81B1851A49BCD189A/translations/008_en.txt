- May 15th: He presented ten volumes of gold-decorated items from his private funds (after making inquiry to Tsuchiya Sagami-no-kami on the 13th). Through messenger and caretaker Murakami Santayū (Masamine), they were presented to Senior Councilor Tsuchiya Sagami-no-kami (Masanao). A written confirmation came from Sagami-no-kami regarding the completion of this presentation:
　　　　　　　　　"The gold-decorated items you presented have been
　　　　　　　　　successfully reported in private. I write this to inform
　　　　　　　　　you of this matter. The above.
　　　　　　　　　　May 15th　　　　　Tsuchiya Sagami-no-kami

　　　　　　　　　　　To Matsudaira Hyōbu-no-taiyu-sama"

- May 16th: He visited the chamberlain (goyōnin) of duty magistrate Abe Bungo-no-kami (Masatake) to request permission to attend a lecture.
　　　　　　He also visited on the 18th.

- June 6th: Due to the summer heat, he presented the customary local products of sōmen noodles and dried sea bream through a messenger.

- July 6th: As congratulations for the birth of a prince, he presented gold through a messenger as was customary.

- July 9th: Upon completion of construction of the accounting office, there was a ceremony for the first accounting session.
　　　　　　Previously, the accounting office had been located behind the Iron Gate (at what was then the construction office),
　　　　　　　but as it became the residence of Seishōin-sama, it was
　　　　　　　relocated to the Sugita Gotayū residence within Kawaguchi Gate.

One　On the 7th day of the first month, ritual fish offerings were presented through an envoy as usual for the first eating ceremony of the year (go-hajime)
One　On the 11th day of the same month, as congratulations for the completion of the three-day New Year ceremonies, envoy Asami Tokuemon (Shigeku) was dispatched to Edo and presented one type of ritual fish offering. Tokuemon departed from Fukui today
　　　　　　　However, a reply letter was received on the 26th day of the same month
One　On the 25th day of the same month, due to the restoration of the shrine at Mount Manpuku, tonight (at the lower hour of the Rooster) there was a temporary enshrinement
One　On the 9th day of the second month, for the monthly inquiry into His Lordship's well-being, envoy Ōfuji Sukezō (Shigemasa) was dispatched to Edo and presented one type of ritual fish offering. Sukezō departed from Fukui today

　　　　　　However, a reply letter was received on the 22nd day of the same month
One　On the 11th day of the same month, at Jōkōin temple, from dawn of yesterday the 10th until this morning, the first anniversary memorial service for Lord Seijōin was conducted. The same service was also conducted at Reigan-ji temple in Fukagawa
One　On the 17th day of the same month, as the restoration of the shrine at Mount Manpuku was completed, this dawn there was a formal enshrinement from the temporary shrine
One　On the 24th day of the same month, at Jōkōin temple, from dawn of the 22nd until this morning, the 27th anniversary memorial service for Lord Daian-in was conducted. At Daian-ji temple as well, from dawn of yesterday the 23rd until this morning, the memorial service was conducted

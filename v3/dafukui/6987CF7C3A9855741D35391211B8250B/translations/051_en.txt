One　August 18th: When he attended court, with the senior councilors assembled, there was an announcement that he had been appointed to the position of Sakon-e-gon-no-shōshō (Minor Captain of the Left Palace Guards), and upon departure, he made courtesy visits to the senior councilors, junior councilors, and Sakai Kawachi-no-kami Tadataka to express gratitude for this appointment
　　　However, from today forward, when attending court, all gate guards and guard posts shall perform complete prostration as per previous protocol

One　Regarding the above matter, letters were sent to the chief retainers and castle governors, beginning with Honda Magotarō Nagazane

One　Same month, 19th day
　　The Retired Emperor (personal name: Masahito) passed away (Emperor Go-Mizunoo)

One　Same month, 21st day: A sealed official letter came from Senior Councilor Ōkubo Kaga-no-kami Tadatomo stating that attendance at court should be made the day after tomorrow, the 23rd, for the shogunal investiture ceremony

　　　Address on sealed official letter
　　　　To Matsudaira Echizen-no-kami　　　　　　　　　　　　Tadatomo
　　　　　　　　　　　　　Endorsement
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Ōkubo Kaga-no-kami
　　　The day after tomorrow, the 23rd, there will be an audience with the imperial messenger and court messenger for the shogunal investiture, so please attend court in formal court dress (sokutai)
　　　I respectfully and fearfully state this
　　　　　August 21st　　　　　　　Tadatomo (with seal)

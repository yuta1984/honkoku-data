Item: Fifth month, sixth day
Due to the Shogun's illness, Kofu Saisho (Tsunatoyo) sama, Tatebayashi Saisho (Tsunayoshi) sama, and members of the Three Houses were to ascend to the castle to inquire about his condition. When [our lord] ascended to the castle, the entry of various daimyo was suspended at the great dismounting area, so he stopped by the Imaji-ro Dozo Elder's residence to assess the situation and then returned to his mansion.

Item: Same month, seventh day: When he ascended to the castle (notice came from the Senior Councilors yesterday on the sixth), the Senior Councilors informed him that Tatebayashi Saisho sama had been designated as the Shogun's adopted heir, and it was also announced that he had been appointed to the rank of Junior Second Rank, Major Counselor, and would move to the Ninomaru. After this was completed, he ascended to Ninomaru castle and had an audience.

Item: Same month, eighth day
The Shogun [Ietsuna] passed away (posthumous Buddhist name: Genyuin sama)

Item: Same day: Regarding the above matter, Kaga Tozaemon (Narioki) and Yoshida Gonnosuke were dispatched as express messengers to the home domain.

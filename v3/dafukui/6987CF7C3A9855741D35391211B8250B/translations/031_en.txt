The retainers of the domain are successively falling into financial difficulties, so there have been various consultations on how to somehow make ends meet. However, as everyone knows the financial situation of the domain, His Lordship is troubled. Nevertheless, thinking that the retainers' finances should continue even a little, he issues this proclamation. Therefore, you should increasingly practice frugality to maintain your households and refrain from submitting requests for financial assistance hereafter. If anyone neglects their finances and falls into ruin, His Lordship would not think well of it, so you should understand this intention and strictly observe it. The above.

Articles:
1. Apart from military equipment, do not acquire any unnecessary tools, and keep all matters simple.
1. For clothing, do not wear anything finer than silk or pongee. Those of lower rank should primarily use cotton, paper clothing, etc. However, it is acceptable to use old clothing brought from home as undergarments. As for haori jackets, you may wear old habutae silk that you brought with you. Even if old, woolen haori and rain capes are completely prohibited. Even for habutae silk, new haori should follow instructions.

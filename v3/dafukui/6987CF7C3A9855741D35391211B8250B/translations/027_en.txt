Memorandum of inquiry regarding the custody of Nakanae Chōzaemon, retainer of Matsudaira Echigo-no-kami, under Matsudaira Echizen-no-kami

1. Regarding the palanquin for transporting Nakanae Chōzaemon to his home domain during the journey, should we use an ordinary palanquin?
　　Official response:
　　　Use whatever seems appropriate.

1. How should we handle Chōzaemon's swords and short swords, as well as those of his retainers?
　　Official response:
　　　Chōzaemon's swords and short swords should be returned to him in his home domain. His retainers should be relieved of theirs from the journey onward. That is all.

1. How many samurai and foot soldiers should be assigned to accompany him during the journey?
　　Official response:
　　　It would be appropriate to assign one captain (monogashira), two samurai, and ten foot soldiers as escorts.

1. Should official documents be sent to the Hakone and Imakiri checkpoints?
　　Official response:
　　　No documents are necessary.

1. How many guards should be stationed for surveillance in his home domain?
　　Official response:
　　　Guard posts should be established wherever appropriate with good security. One guard post with only foot soldiers stationed would be appropriate.

1. Should he be allowed freedom within the assigned residence, and may his retainers be permitted to go outside the gate when they have business?

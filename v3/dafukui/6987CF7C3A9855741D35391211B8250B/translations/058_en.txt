After deliberation, if there are those who have received deposits, punishment shall certainly be ordered.

1. Those who have hidden and deposited anything in other domains, even if it be a small amount, shall be investigated and certainly punished.

1. Regarding temples and shrines, and even extending to their gate-front areas, there are reports of receiving deposits of gold, silver, rice, money, miscellaneous grains, etc. from farmers. Those who have received such deposits must report immediately. Regardless of who they are, if they have received anything whatsoever as deposits, they must consult with group leaders and village headmen. This directive must be strictly conveyed to temples and shrines.
November

1. On November 20th, Senior Councilor Okubo Kaga-no-kami Tadatomo summoned the residence keeper, who attended.
He was ordered to assign fire-fighting duties in case of fires near the residence, and was instructed to dispatch retainers for this purpose.

1. On the 22nd of the same month, at the residence of Senior Retainer Inaba Uneme Harufusa, Uneme informed all the monthly duty officers that the rice loans to the domain retainers for this year had been forgiven.

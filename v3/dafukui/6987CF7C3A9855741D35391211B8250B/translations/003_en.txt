Enpō 6th year, Year of the Earth Horse (1678)
1. On New Year's Day, the New Year ceremonial protocol was held at the Main Castle. In the Goza-no-ma (Lord's sitting room), Honda Magotarō (Nagazane) for the first time as chief retainer presented a sword catalog and offered his respects, was appointed to participate in the three-献 (san-kon) celebratory feast, received the ceremonial cup, and was bestowed御長熨斗 (long ceremonial gift). Thereafter, in the Inner Study, Honda Minbu (Shigekata) offered his respects, received the ceremonial cup, and was bestowed the long ceremonial gift. In the Outer Study, the members of high-ranking positions starting from Kōchi presented sword catalogs, offered their respects, received ceremonial cups according to protocol, and were bestowed long ceremonial gifts. After this was completed, members of the council and above wearing long hakama presented sword catalogs, offered their respects, received ceremonial cups according to protocol, and were bestowed long ceremonial gifts.

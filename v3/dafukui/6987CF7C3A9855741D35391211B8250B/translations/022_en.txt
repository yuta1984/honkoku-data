1. August 18th, this time for going around with guns to the hawk hunting grounds in Kazusa Province,
   regarding the endorsement of travel permits through Nakagawa checkpoint, requests were made to
   Lord Matsudaira Inaba-no-kami and Lord Ishikawa Mimasaku-no-kami, and as arrangements were completed,
   letters of greeting were sent regarding this matter.

1. The 25th of the same month: It was announced that a stone pagoda for Lady Kosho-in
   would be erected at Zuigen Temple.

1. The 29th of the same month: Lord Wakasa-no-kami《Note: Naoaki》 arrived at the Fukui residence from Ono, and from there
   proceeded to the main castle. On that occasion, the chief retainers and senior retainers came out to the main entrance,
   high-ranking members waited in the Crane Room, and they went out to welcome him as far as the Gun Room, then
   escorted him to the inner sleeping quarters where a formal meeting took place. Zoni soup and cuisine《Note: three soups, ten dishes》were served,
   Lord Masachika and Lord Tsunamasa were seated with gifts,
   and they dined together with Lord Tsunamasa, with chief retainer Honda Minbu《Note: Michishige》 accompanying them.

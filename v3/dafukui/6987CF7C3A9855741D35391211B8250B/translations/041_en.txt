Lord Tsunamasa □
Lord Tsunayoshi's reign (Shogun proclamation on August 23rd)
Enpō 8, Year of the Metal Monkey

1. New Year's Day: The New Year's ceremony was held in the main keep's living quarters.
   However, due to illness, the ceremonies from the guard commanders and below were not received today.

1. Same day: For New Year's congratulations, through messenger Suganuma Ichizaemon Ietsugu, a ceremonial sword and horse substitute (ten ryō of gold) were presented.
   However, Ichizaemon departed from the domain on the 7th of last month.

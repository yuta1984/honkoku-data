Item: On the seventh day of the fourth month, [the lord] met with the Junior Councillor (wakadoshiyori), Lord Hotta Kaga-no-kami (Masanobu/Masaoki), and fulfilled his official duties.

Item: On the same day, concerning the upcoming visit to Edo of Lord Naniwa Chūnagon (Sō■), the matter of sending a gift of courtesy (o-todoke), and furthermore, concerning the visit of Lord Hamuro, the former Middle Counselor (Yoritane), the matter of [the lord's retainer] Honda Kura-no-suke (Tomitsugu) having an audience — [the lord] had his residential representative (o-rusui), Tsuda Kuemon (Morihiro), deliver the following two written oral communications to the Duty Councillor, Lord Sakai Uta-no-kami (Tadayuki):

　　　　　　　　　　Memorandum of Oral Communication

　　　　　　On this occasion, I have heard that Lord Naniwa Chūnagon is traveling down to Edo. He is a close relation (sūki) of mine. He has sent word that he wishes to meet with me on this occasion; however, as the connection [between us] is distant, I have conveyed my regrets [and declined].
　　　　　　Nevertheless, as the exchange of messengers and gifts (oto-mono) [with him] has been the established custom from previous times, I wish to send a messenger with gifts to his lodgings on this occasion as well. I humbly request your guidance on this matter.
　　　　　　Respectfully submitted.
　　　　　　　　　　Seventh day of the fourth month　　　　　　Matsudaira Hyōbu-no-taifu

　　　　　　Regarding Lord Hamuro, the former Middle Counselor: he is the maternal uncle of my retainer Honda Kura-no-suke. At this time, Kura-no-suke happens to be in attendance here in Edo; therefore, during Lord Hamuro's stay following his arrival, [we] wish to have Kura-no-suke call upon him and meet with him. Furthermore, Lord Hamuro himself has also expressed his desire to meet on this occasion.
　　　　　　I humbly request your guidance regarding the above matters. Respectfully submitted.

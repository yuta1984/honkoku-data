1. On September 2nd, for the Chōyō festival celebrations, formal court robes were presented as usual through an official messenger.

1. On the 8th of the same month, regarding assistance with the Nikkō construction project, as there was a shortage of over 180 ashigaru (foot soldiers) to be dispatched to that location, word came from Edo that personnel should be borrowed from Honda Kuranosuke and others from Kōchi domain. Accordingly, the following written notice was delivered to these individuals through the monthly duty senior retainer at the residence of Matsudaira Umano-shō Akinao:

　　　　　　Memorandum
　　　　　　　　　　　　To the people of Kōchi domain
　　Due to this official duty, there is a shortage of 185 ashigaru, and although new personnel could be ordered to be hired, His Lordship considers that hiring new ashigaru due to shortage might affect public reputation unfavorably. Therefore, ashigaru and others are to be borrowed from Honda Kuranosuke and others from Kōchi domain. Each of you should arrange for five or three people from your associates, or even more if available, to be sent to Edo as soon as possible.

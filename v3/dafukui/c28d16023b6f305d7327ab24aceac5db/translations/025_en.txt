September

1. On September 12th, a report came that a ground-breaking ceremony (jichinsai) was held at Nikkō Palace on the 8th, so notification was given to Senior Councilor Honda Nakatsukasa-taifu Tadayoshi, who was in charge of this matter.

1. On the same day, at Zuigen-ji Temple, from the evening of the 11th until this morning, the 33rd memorial service for Lord Saigen-in was conducted. At Nanzen-ji Temple in Yanaka, a tea ceremony was also conducted.

1. On the 15th of the same month, due to illness, attendance at the castle was declined.

1. On the same day, for the same reason as above, regarding the matter of dispatching to the fire at the Yanagihara construction hut area, the following oral statement was submitted to Duty Councilor Honda Nakatsukasa-taifu Tadayoshi:

Memorandum of Oral Statement
When a fire broke out in the direction of the Yanagihara construction huts, I was prepared to go to that location depending on the situation of the fire. However, due to my recent illness, I did not go to the location when the fire occurred, but instead sent my retainers. Therefore, I am reporting this through a messenger.

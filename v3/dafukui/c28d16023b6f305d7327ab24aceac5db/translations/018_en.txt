1. On the 6th day of the 7th month, for the birthday celebration, gold was presented as usual through a messenger.

1. On the 8th day of the same month, at Tentokuji Temple, regarding the completion of Shōkōin-sama's stone pagoda and memorial tablet, a memorial service was held this morning.

1. On the 12th day of the same month, through envoy and messenger official Kaneda Tonomo, regarding the consideration of larks for falconry, the envoy's handling was as usual, and for thanks regarding this matter, went to both citadels, then made rounds to the senior councilors.

1. On the 21st day of the same month, local specialty first cod was presented as usual through a messenger.

1. On the 23rd day of the same month, performed duty of audience with Senior Councilor Honda Nakatsukasa-dayū Tadayoshi.

1. On the 1st day of the 8th month, for celebration went to the castle, and presented sword and horse substitute as usual.

1. On the 8th day of the same month, at Tentokuji Temple, from the evening of the 7th until this morning, Shōkōin-sama's hundredth-day advance memorial service was conducted.

1.

1. On September 10th, regarding Senshō-in and Unshō-ji temples, a rice loan was announced through the written notice as described above, and the senior retainer gave instructions to Iwagami Kajitayū Chōyo, the magistrate of temples and shrines, to convey this message.

Last year, due to financial difficulties, rice loans were ordered from temples and shrines as well. However, regarding Senshō-in temple, because it houses the shrine and official memorial tablets of the shogunate, the rice loan was specially exempted. In this case, assistance has been ordered for the repair of Nikkō shrine, and due to this official government business, rice loans have been ordered from everyone according to their proportional share. Therefore, this should be understood and the rice should be delivered accordingly. The above.

1. On the 19th day of the 8th month, at Unshōji Temple, the 100th-day memorial service for Shōkōin-sama and the completion blessing of the stone pagoda were held.

1. On the 21st day of the same month, performed duty of audience with Senior Councilor Matsudaira Sakon-shōgen Shūyū.

1. On the same day, performed duty of audience with Nishinomaru Senior Councilor Matsudaira Noto-no-kami Norimasa.

1. On the same day, regarding the Nikkō construction project supervisor, when the officials in charge of this construction assistance project went to Senior Councilor Honda Nakatsukasa-dayū Tadayoshi, there was an audience, and this matter was conveyed through the estate keeper on the 20th.

1. On the same day, went out to the Yanagihara construction site for private inspection.

1. On the 23rd day of the same month, the initial ceremony for the Nikkō construction project was held at the Yanagihara construction site, and [the lord] went to the construction site. On that occasion, Senior Councilor Honda Nakatsukasa-dayū Tadayoshi, who was in charge of the project, and other officials in charge also attended.
However, all the officials in charge were present without exception.

1. On the same day, regarding the safe delivery of Tokugawa Gyōbu-kyō Mune[?]-sama's wife, messengers and estate keepers were dispatched to the duty officer and Nishinomaru senior councilors for congratulations.

1. On the same day, since assistance for the Nikkō construction project was ordered, the following was announced in [the lord's] own handwriting to the retainers regarding additional rice loans:

"Being ordered to assist with the Nikkō restoration is an extremely honorable and grateful matter. No matter what [duties] are ordered, serving in official matters is grateful and should be performed without [question]..."

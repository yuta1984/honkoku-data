[Regarding] the repair of the shrine, mausoleum, and main temple quarters, assistance was ordered.
However, regarding official matters, consultation should be made with the Nakatsukasa-dayū.

After expressing gratitude for the above, and then going to Nishinomaru castle to express thanks, being appointed as court announcer and upon departure, made courtesy visits to the senior councilors and junior councilors.

1. On the same day, there was a summons from duty officer Matsudaira Izu-no-kami Nobuaki, and when the estate keeper Asai Genzaemon Masayuki went out, he was informed through the following written notice that the fire watch duty at Tōeizan had been exempted.

Since assistance for Nikkō has been ordered, the fire watch duty at Tōeizan is exempted.

1. On the 12th day of the 8th month, performed duty of audience with duty officer Matsudaira Izu-no-kami Nobuaki.

1. On the same day, at Unshōji Temple, from the evening of the 11th until this morning, the thirty-third anniversary advance memorial service for Tangenin-sama was conducted.

1. On the 13th day of the same month, performed duty of audience with Senior Councilor Honda Nakatsukasa-dayū Tadayoshi.

1. On the 14th day of the same month, regarding the order for assistance with the Nikkō construction project, concerning the transfer of the construction site huts at Yanagihara for this construction project, officials were dispatched to receive them.

1. On the 16th day of the same month, performed duty of audience with Senior Councilor Toki Tango-no-kami Yoritoshi.

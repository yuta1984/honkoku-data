12th month, 22nd day

　Attachment
1. Regarding rice distributed to community granaries this year: 100 hyō each to the town and four districts, totaling 500 hyō were distributed. This was reported by the Magistrate on the 29th day of the intercalary 11th month, and was further communicated to the Senior Councilors, then transmitted by Sasakawa Tōnai, who oversees community granaries, to the town and four district magistrates respectively.

　　　　　7th month, 5th day

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Magistrate
○ A three-year fixed tax exemption period has been announced from this Rabbit year to the Snake year. The exemption shall follow the Bunka 3 Tiger year exemption, so please handle accordingly.
　　　　　　Separate Document
○ Regarding the three-year fixed tax exemption announced from this year, since inspections and regular official visits are also suspended, additional exemptions should normally be granted, but through special consideration, fixed exemptions are being implemented.
1. Within villages, if there are unavoidable circumstances and some villages cannot naturally receive the periodic exemption, assessment-based exemptions shall be granted as before, and expenses for official duties shall be provided by the county villages.
1. During the fixed exemption period, relief measures for crop failures and emergency rice distributions are completely suspended. However, if there are exceptional circumstances requiring special requests, and villages request field surveys for crop failures, if the magistrate's office conducts thorough investigations and reports urgently, appropriate relief measures may be granted after examination at that time.
1. During the exemption period, various petitions need not be strictly collected.

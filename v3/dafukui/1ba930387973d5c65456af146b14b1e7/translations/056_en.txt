　Summoned to service during Lord Tadamasa's reign (year unknown)
　Adopted son
　　　Minagawa Tazaemon Katsushige (birth province unknown, original name Ochiai Sakyō, Buddhist name Genbo, biological father Ochiai Mimasaka)
　　　　His adoptive father Heiuemon Katsuteru had no heir, so Katsushige was made an adopted son-in-law. His biological father Ochiai
　　　　Mimasaka incurred Lord Tadanao's displeasure and was confined at Sakai in Izumi Province, but was later summoned by
　　　　Lord Yorinobu of Kii and served him. At that time he was called Ochiai Shūzen. His son
　　　　Katsushige was living with his father when he was summoned during Lord Tadamasa's reign (year unknown), took the name Ochiai Sakyō,
　　　　and served. Later, he was appointed as adopted son-in-law to his adoptive father Heiuemon Katsuteru's
　　　　family and succeeded to the family headship. Subsequently, he was appointed as attendant to Lord Yoshishina and
　　　　went to serve in the domain of Yoshie.

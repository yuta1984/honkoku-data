　　　　　　　△Sengoku

Summoned in Settsu Province, Osaka during Lord Hideyasu's reign《year unknown》
　　● Sengoku Man'emon (true name unknown)《home province unknown, birth country Settsu, family name unknown》
　　　　Summoned by Lord Hideyasu, assigned to Lord Tadamasa, and served in the Siege of Osaka.
　　　　Man'emon's wife was the elder sister of Ariga Koemon Masanari, and was
　　　　summoned as a nursemaid during Lord Mitsumichi's childhood, coming to be called Sengoku. When Lord Mitsumichi
　　　　had his first formal audience in Edo, she also accompanied him to the castle. Later she became a nun,
　　　　taking the name Eijuin.

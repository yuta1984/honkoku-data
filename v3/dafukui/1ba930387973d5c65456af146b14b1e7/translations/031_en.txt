【Left Page】
　　　　　　△　Yamakawa

　During Lord Hideyasu's reign, accompanied [the lord] from Yuki
　　● Yamakawa Sanuki-no-kami Tomosada (both home province and birth province: Shimousa, family name: Fujiwara)
　　　　His ancestor was a descendant of the sixteenth generation from Yamakawa Kōzuke Hangan Shigemitsu, third son of Lord Yuki Tomomitsu
　　　　(genealogical records exist). For generations [the family] ruled Yamakawa-no-shō in Shimousa Province. Tomosada at age eleven accompanied [the lord] to Echizen
　　　　and resided in Shibidaniguchi. In Genna 1, during the Osaka campaign, he served in Lord Tadanao's van-
　　　　guard. After Tomosada's eldest son Sanuki Tomoshige succeeded to the family headship, during Lord Tadamasa's reign
　　　　he requested leave due to illness (year unknown) and returned to the Kantō region where he died

　Summoned during Lord Tadamasa's reign
　　　Yamakawa Naizen Tomanari (birth province: Echizen, summoned after his elder brother Tomoshige's death)

　　　　35,000 koku, serving the Ashikaga shogunal house. His son Iino Sanshirō, and his son Yano Den-

　　　　he was at Sowugae Castle when Oda Gorōzaemon, nephew of Lord Oda Nobunaga,
　　　　was garrisoned at Imo in Mino province. Due to Kurando's private grudge,

　　　　a retainer named Hayashi Yogorō committed treason, causing Sowugae to fall.

　During Lord Tadanao's reign, Keichō 19th year, year of the Wood Tiger (1614)
　Granted inheritance of position
　　　Kiuchi Jinbei Morimasa (birth province Echizen)
　　　　Regarding Morimasa's eldest son Kiuchi Hansuke, he was summoned during Lord Mitsumichi's reign but died in Enpō 5th year (1677).
　　　　His son Denshichi was granted a provisional position to succeed him, but later due to illness, he requested
　　　　Koshirō, the second son of the later Kiuchi Jinbei Sadamori, as a deathbed adoption for succession. However, when
　　　　Kojima Gorōsaku Yoshikata died while still young, no adoption request was submitted, resulting in the extinction of the family name.
　　　　Therefore, Koshirō Morisuke was appointed to inherit the Kojima family name and position, while
　　　　the succession line of Kiuchi Denshichi was terminated.

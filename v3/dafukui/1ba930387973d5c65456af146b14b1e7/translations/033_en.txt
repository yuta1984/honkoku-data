　　　　　　　△　Ogino

　Summoned to service during Lord Hideyasu's reign (year unknown)
　　● Ogino Kawachi-no-kami Nagamichi (home province: Tanba, birth province: unknown, family name: Taira)
　　　　Nagamichi's father was called Ogino Iga-no-kami. Regarding his ancestors, since they are the main line
　　　　of Ogino Shirōemon Nagatada of the same clan, a written record is submitted herewith. Nagamichi was summoned to service during Lord Hideyasu's reign,
　　　　granted a stipend of one thousand men, served in the winter campaign at Osaka, and served as a unit commander
　　　　in Lord Nagami Minbu's formation. During the summer campaign, he remained behind to guard the castle and did not serve in the campaign

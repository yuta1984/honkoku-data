　　　　　　　　△　Hasegawa

　Summoned at Yūki during Lord Hideyasu's reign
　　● Hasegawa Jirōzaemon, personal name unknown (home province Suruga, birth province Musashi, family name unknown)
　　　　Jirōzaemon's grandfather was Hasegawa Awaji-no-kami, and his son was Hasegawa Chikugo-no-kami, both of whom
　　　　served as hatamoto retainers in Edo. When Lady Takada entered into marriage, Chikugo-no-kami (Jirō-
　　　　zaemon's father) accompanied her to Echizen as an attendant. As for Jirōzaemon, he served
　　　　the house from the very beginning, and during Lord Tadanao's reign, he accompanied both
　　　　campaigns of the Siege of Osaka. When Lord Tadamasa entered the domain from Echigo,
　　　　he received prior instructions and remained in the domain to serve.

　During Lord Tadamasa's reign, Kan'ei 11th year, year of the Wood Dog (1634)
　Granted inheritance of position
　　Adopted son
　　　Hasegawa Nihei Masayoshi (birth province Echizen, actually the third son of Oguri Jiuemon Shigemasa)

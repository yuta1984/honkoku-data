【Left Page】
　　　　　　　△　Hagino　　Yamagata

Summoned during Lord Hideyasu's reign
　　● Hagino Kawachi-no-kami Nagamichi <<home country Tamba, birth country unknown, surname Taira>>
　　　　Nagamichi's father, called Hagino Iga-no-kami, lived in Tamba Province but died as a masterless samurai.
　　　　Nagamichi was invited by Lord Aoki Kii-no-kami, received a stipend of 7,000 koku and served as castle deputy.
　　　　After Lord Kii-no-kami's death from illness, he returned to Tamba, whereupon Lord Hideyasu summoned him and
　　　　granted him support for 1,000 men. He served in the winter siege of Osaka as a unit commander. In the following year's summer
　　　　siege, he was appointed castle deputy and did not serve in the campaign.

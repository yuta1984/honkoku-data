【Left Page】
　　　　　　△　Matsudaira

　During Lord Tadamasa's era, at Matsumoto Castle in Shinano Province
　Genna 3rd year, Year of the Fire Snake, summoned to service
　　● Matsudaira Bizen Masayo (Home and birth provinces both Mikawa, surname: Minamoto, original name: Shōbei)
　　　　Ancestor: From Matsudaira Bitchū-no-kami Chikatada, seventh son of Lord Nobumitsu of our lord's legitimate lineage,
　　　　to Masayo spans eight generations (genealogical records exist). Masayo's father, Matsudaira Katsuemon Chikakiyo, held a fief of 500 kan at Omuma
　　　　village in Sanshū. His eldest son, Dewa-no-kami Kiyonao, was assigned by Gongen-sama to serve Lord Kozuke-no-suke Tadateru,
　　　　and his second son Masayo also served together. After Lord Kozuke-no-suke's disinheritance,
　　　　both Doi Ōi-no-kami Toshikatsu and Sakai Sanuki-no-kami Tadakatsu made requests to Lord Tadamasa,
　　　　and thus he was summoned to service

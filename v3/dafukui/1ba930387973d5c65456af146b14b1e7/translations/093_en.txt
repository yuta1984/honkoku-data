　　　　　　　　　△　Yukifuki

　During Lord Hideyasu's reign, Bunroku 5th year, year of the Fire Monkey (1596)
　Summoned to service at Yūki
　　● Yukifuki Kizaemon Shigeyoshi (both home province and birth province Chikugo, surname Minamoto, original name Kuzō)
　　　　It is handed down that he was one of the seven defenders of Takatengjin Castle. Summoned by Lord Hideyasu,
　　　　he served as attendant from Yūki to Echizen. During the Osaka campaigns, he served as attendant to Lord Tadanao
　　　　and participated in the campaign. When Lord Tadamasa entered Echizen domain, he was summoned as one of
　　　　105 selected retainers from the advance guard.

【Left Page】
　　　　　　　　　△　Nagata

　Served from Yūki during Lord Hideyasu's reign (year unknown)
　　● Nagata Shirōbei Mochishige (home province unknown, birth province unknown, family name unknown)
　　　　　Mochishige served under Hōjō Ujiyasu, but was later summoned by Lord Harutomo and achieved
　　　　　military merits in various places, but the documents from that time were lost in fire and are no longer possessed.
　　　　　During Lord Harutomo's reign, Mochishige carried a white five-ring battle standard, while at that time one
　　　　　Tateno Gemba carried a black five-ring battle standard, and they competed with each other over battlefield positions.
　　　　　During some campaign, Gemba was killed in battle, and afterwards it was decreed that no one in the retainer band
　　　　　should carry black five-ring battle standards, leaving Mochishige alone to carry the white five-ring standard, as has been
　　　　　passed down. During the Osaka campaigns, the second-generation Shirōbei served in the campaign
　　　　　with the white five-ring battle standard

【Right Page】
After being summoned and serving during Lord Tadamasa's reign, he was again
granted his father Nagamichi's allocated domain
　　　Hagino Naizen, personal name unknown <<birth country Echizen, later took the name Genemon>>

Granted family headship during Lord Mitsumichi's reign
　　　Hagino Magoemon Ieji <<birth country Echizen, original name Genemon, records of descendants are held by the current Magoemon Masafū>>

Summoned during Lord Mitsumichi's reign, in the 2nd year of Jōō, year of the water snake
　　　Hagino Riemon Masaji <<birth country Echizen, original name Watanabe Kansuke, later also Hagino Jihē>>
　　　　Initially he went as an adopted son to one called Watanabe Yajiemon, and at that time
　　　　he performed archery in Kyoto, so in the temple records he is listed as Watanabe Kansuke. Later, when his adoptive father Yaji-
　　　　emon had a biological son, he respectfully declined and returned to his original family.

【Left Page】
Summoned during Lord Yoshishina's reign, in the 6th year of Hōei, year of the earth ox
　　Current Yamagata Tajiemon Itsuji <<birth country Echizen>>
　　　　Although he was the legitimate son, because his father Masaji's transmission of archery arts did not suit his abilities, the family tradition was to be passed to the legitimate grandson, and
　　　　consequently Itsuji did not receive the family stipend.

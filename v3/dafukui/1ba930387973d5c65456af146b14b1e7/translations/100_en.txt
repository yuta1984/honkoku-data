　　　Sengoku Gon'emon Takanari《birth country Izumo, biological father Nara Sakon'emon Takoki》
　　　　Regarding Takanari: While serving after being summoned during Lord Tadamasa's reign, Tanaka Hachihei
　　　　died of illness in Edo, and having no heir, his family line was discontinued. According to the petition of Sengoku Eijuin
　　　　to take Hachihei's daughter as wife and continue the family name through marriage adoption,
　　　　both Hachihei's inherited stipend and Takanari's own stipend were granted together

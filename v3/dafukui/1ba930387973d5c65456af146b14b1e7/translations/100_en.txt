After being summoned and serving during Lord Tadamasa's reign
During Lord Mitsumichi's reign, in the 3rd year of Meireki, year of the fire rooster, adopted as son
　　　Sengoku Gon'emon Takanari <<birth country Izumo, biological father Nara Sakon'emon Takasoki>>
　　　　Regarding Takanari: he had been summoned and served during Lord Tadamasa's reign, when Tanaka Hachihei
　　　　died of illness in Edo, leaving no heir and causing the family line to be discontinued. Therefore, wishing to have
　　　　Hachihei's daughter marry and continue the family name through adoption, at the request of Sengoku Eijuin,
　　　　Hachihei's inherited stipend and Takanari's own granted stipend were combined and bestowed.

　During Lord Mitsumichi's reign, Kanbun 11, year of Kanoto-I
　Granted family headship
　　　Suganuma Ichizaemon Ietsugu (birth province Echizen)
　　　　From his father Yoichirō's hereditary stipend, an allocation of 400 koku was granted to his younger brother Yamamoto Chūzaemon,
　　　　but he died young and the lineage was discontinued. Since many of the brothers died young, 
　　　　Lord Mitsumichi ordered that the family name be changed, and it became Suganuma after the maternal line.
　　　　Ietsugu's mother was the daughter of Suganuma Oribe, lord of Zeze Castle in Enshū.

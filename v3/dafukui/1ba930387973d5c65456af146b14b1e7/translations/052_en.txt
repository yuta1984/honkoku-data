【Left Page】
　　　　　　　△　Iwagami

　During Lord Hideyasu's reign, continuing service from Yuki,
　accompanied the lord to Echizen
　　● Iwagami Etchū Tomoyoshi (home province: Shimotsuke, birth province: unknown, family name: Minamoto, original name: Sakyō)
　　　　Tomoyoshi's father, Miura Oki-no-kami Yoshikata, during Lord Harutomo's reign, was ordered to change his
　　　　original name from Miura and take the name Iwagami, a branch family of Oyama, and was granted
　　　　one character from the lord's name as well as the tomoe family crest, thereafter being called Iwagami Oki-no-kami Yoshikata

　During Lord Tadamasa's reign, granted family headship (year unknown)
　　Adopted son, actually the younger brother of Sasaji Daizen-no-shō Toki
　　　Iwagami Sakyō Yoshitsugu (birth province: Owari, served together with his adoptive father during both years of the Ōsaka campaigns. Later, Yoshitsugu's successor main family line became extinct)

　During Lord Tadamasa's reign, granted a fief (year unknown)
　　　Iwagami Shichino-jō Tomochika (birth province: Echizen, original name: Baiyō)

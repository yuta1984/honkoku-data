　　　　　　△　Mito

　During Lord Hideyasu's reign, from Yuki
　Accompanied [the lord] to Echizen
　　● Mito Sanshichi Sanemichi (home province: Musashi, birth province: Hitachi, family name: Taira, original name: Edo Tajima-no-kami)
　　　　Sanemichi's father Edo Tajima-no-kami Shigemichi's ancestor was a descendant thirty
　　　　generations after Prince Takamochi, fourth generation from Emperor Kanmu (genealogical records exist). For several generations [the family] served as lords of Mito Castle in Hitachi Province, but in Sanemichi's generation, due to circumstances,
　　　　he served under Lord Yuki Harutomo and resided in Yuki (Lord Harutomo was Sanemichi's maternal grandfather). At this time he changed his name to Mito Sanshichi, and after
　　　　coming to Echizen, due to illness, during Lord Tadanao's reign in Genna 9 (year of the water boar), he was
　　　　granted relief rice

　During Lord Tadamasa's reign, in Kan'ei 16 (year of the earth rabbit)
　Relief rice was granted
　　　Mito Takumi Tomoyoshi (birth province: Echizen. Since he was born after his father Sanemichi was granted relief rice, the relief rice that had been given to Sanemichi's mother Zenshōin was transferred to Tomoyoshi)

　　　　　　　△　Honda

Summoned during Lord Hideyasu's reign
　　● Honda Gorōemon Masatsugu <<home country Mikawa, birth country Mutsu, surname Fujiwara>>
　　　　Masatsugu's father was called Honda Han'emon, a key retainer of Honda Bungo-no-kami.
　　　　Han'emon's wife served　Gongen-sama (Tokugawa Ieyasu), so both Han'emon and
　　　　Gorōemon were known to him, and both father and son had audiences with him. Later
　　　　he was summoned by Lord Hideyasu.　Gongen-sama, Taitokuin-sama, and Daiyūin-sama all
　　　　knew of his lineage, and during Lord Tadamasa's reign there were even inquiries by superior order,
　　　　as has been passed down. During the Osaka campaigns, he was attached to Dewa-no-kami Lord Naomasa and served
　　　　as his attendant. He served through the reigns of　Lord Tadanao, Lord Tadamasa, and Lord Mitsumichi.

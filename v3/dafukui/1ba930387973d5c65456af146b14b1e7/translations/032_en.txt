【Left Page】
　　　　　　△　Urakami

　During Lord Yoshishina's time at Yoshie, in Kanbun 5 (year of the wood snake)
　Summoned to service
　　● Urakami Jūzaemon Shigeaki (home province: Harima, birth province: Yamashiro, family name: Minamoto, original name: Akamatsu)
　　　　Shigeaki's grandfather Urakami Kuranosuke Shigenori was the grandson of Akamatsu Sakyō-no-daibu Norifusa and lord of Kan-
　　　　jōzan Castle in Harima Province, it is said (period unknown). Shigeaki's father was Urakami Naiki Shigemasa (later took the name Sōshun),
　　　　who lived in Kyoto as a masterless samurai. Shigeaki was also residing in Kyoto when
　　　　he was summoned to service due to connections with Lady Kōshōin (Lord Yoshishina's mother)

　In the same reign, in Hōei 2 (year of the wood rooster), he was granted succession rights
　　Current [head] Urakami Jūzaemon Akimine (birth province: Echizen)

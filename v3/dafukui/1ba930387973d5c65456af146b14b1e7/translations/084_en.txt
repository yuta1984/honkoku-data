【Right Page】
　During Lord Yoshishina's reign, Enpō 4th year, year of the Fire Dragon (1676)
　Summoned to service
　　Current generation: Tsuda Shōzaemon Terunari (birth province Echizen, original name Ichibei)
　　　　His father Dōi's eldest son, called Dōsetsu, was in poor health and requested leave of absence. Due to this, during the time when the family profession was not 
　　　　being continued, a request for leave was submitted. However, it was declared that since this was a family that had served the house for many generations, 
　　　　he should continue in service as before. Though this was received with gratitude, the following year another petition was submitted, stating that due to old age 
　　　　he could no longer perform his duties, and ultimately this concerned the stipend granted for the family profession. 
　　　　Since the eldest son was ill and could not inherit, receiving the stipend was considered troublesome. 
　　　　As petitioned, the stipend was revoked and the second son Ichibei Terunari was summoned to serve in whatever light capacity possible.

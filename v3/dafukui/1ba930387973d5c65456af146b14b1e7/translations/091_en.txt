　　　　　　　△　Mitera　　Nagano

　During Lord Hideyasu's reign, Keichō 5th year, year of the Metal Rat (1600), summoned to service
　　● Mitera Kyūzaemon Masanobu (home province Kōzuke, birth province unknown, family name Ariwara, original name Kyūsuke)
　　　　Masanobu's grandfather Nagano Shinano-no-kami Yeshimasa was a descendant of Prince Aho, son of Emperor Heizei,
　　　　and of Narihira Asomi. For generations, [his] ancestors were retainers of the Uesugi family and lords of Minowa
　　　　Castle in Jōshū. His son Nagano Kyūsuke Masanobu was Masanobu's father. He served Takeda Shingen,
　　　　held domain of fifty-three kan, and the vermillion seal from Eiroku 10th year (1567) still exists today. He lived in the
　　　　village of Mitera in Kōzuke Province, hence his descendants use this as their family name.

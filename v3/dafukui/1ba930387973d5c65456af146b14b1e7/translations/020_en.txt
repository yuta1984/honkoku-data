　　　　　　△　Inaba　Hayashi

　From Taitoku-in-sama　Lord Tadamasa's relocation to
　Takada in Echigo, attached retainer (at that time, 4th year of Genna, year of the Earth Horse)
　　● Inaba Sado-no-kami Masanari (Home province: Mino　Birth province: same　Clan: Ochi)
　　　　His ancestor was Kōno Shichirō Michitame, a 49th-generation descendant of Prince Iyo, the third son of Emperor Kōrei
　　　　(changed his title to Inaba, genealogical records exist), and he was the sixth-generation grandson. He was the lord of Shimizu Castle in Ōno District, Mino Province, but when he was
　　　　assigned to Lord Tadamasa, he arrived at Itoigawa in Echigo Province and established his residence there. When moving to Echizen
　　　　Province, Masanari, being elderly and in poor health, requested to be excused from residing in the snowy country, and
　　　　as his representative, he had his son Hachiuemon serve as an attendant to Echizen, while Masanari himself went to Edo and
　　　　resided with Inaba Ise-no-kami.

　During Lord Tadamasa's era, at Takada in Echigo
　He was granted a stipend and accompanied to Echizen
　　　Inaba Hachiuemon Masafusa (Birth province: Echigo　Original names: Uneme, Izumo)

【Left Page】
　　　　　　△　Mabuchi

　From Gongen-sama (Tokugawa Ieyasu) to Lord Hideyasu as an attendant
　　● Mabuchi Saburōbei Katsuyoshi (home province and birth province both Tōtōmi, surname Minamoto)
　　　　Initially served Gongen-sama, then was assigned to Chōshōin-sama (Oai no Kata), and later
　　　　was assigned at the time of Lord Hideyasu's birth. Later became aged and infirm, and moreover lost his adult son,
　　　　so took the tonsure and retired to Kamiishida village in Enshū. Even now his descendants
　　　　remain in Kamiishida village and possess the vermillion seal document granted by Gongen-sama.

　Summoned when Lord Hideyasu entered Echizen province
　　Adopted son (actual lineage unknown)
　　　Mabuchi Sukezaemon Katsunao (birth province: Tōtōmi)
　　　　When Lord Hideyasu entered Echizen province, his adoptive father Katsuyoshi brought him for an audience
　　　　Katsunao was summoned and assigned to Lord Tadamasa, accompanied him to the Osaka campaigns, and thereafter gradually
　　　　continued his loyal service

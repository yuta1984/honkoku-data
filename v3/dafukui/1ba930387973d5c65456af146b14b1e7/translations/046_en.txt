【Left Page】
　　　　　　△　Mabuchi

　From Gongen-sama (Tokugawa Ieyasu) to Lord Hideyasu as an attendant
　　● Mabuchi Saburōbei Katsuyoshi (home province and birth province both Tōtōmi, surname Minamoto)
　　　　Initially served Gongen-sama, but after being assigned to Chōshōin-sama (Lady Oai)
　　　　was assigned at the time of Lord Hideyasu's birth, later became aged and infirm, and furthermore lost his adult son
　　　　so he took the tonsure and secluded himself in Kami-Ishida village in Enshū. Even now his descendants
　　　　remain in Kami-Ishida village and possess the vermillion seal document granted by Gongen-sama

　Summoned when Lord Hideyasu entered Echizen province
　　Adopted son, actual lineage unknown
　　　Mabuchi Sukezaemon Katsunao (birth province: Tōtōmi)
　　　　When Lord Hideyasu entered Echizen province, brought his adoptive father Katsuyoshi and presented himself for audience
　　　　Katsunao was summoned and assigned to Lord Tadamasa, served in the Osaka campaigns, and thereafter gradually
　　　　performed loyal service

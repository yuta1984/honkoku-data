1. March 28th, Tōeizan
　　Due to the fire that spread to the Great Daiyūin's Buddhist hall, the keeper of the Edo residence, Miyakita Jūrozaemon Sadayoshi, went out to inquire about duties from Rōjū Toda Yamashiro-no-kami Tadamasa, and was instructed to submit a messenger's tablet.

1. On the same day at Tentokuji Temple, from the evening of the 27th until this morning, the 50th memorial service for Seichiinsan was conducted, and tea ceremony was also conducted at Unshōji Temple.

1. April 4th, directly from the rest at Shinagawa post station, to the Rōjū as notification of arrival in Edo

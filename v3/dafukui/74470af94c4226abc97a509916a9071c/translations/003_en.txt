Genbun 2nd year, Year of the Fire Snake (1737)

1. On New Year's Day (1st day of the 1st month), the New Year ceremony was held at the Main Castle
1. On the same day, as New Year's congratulations, a ceremonial sword and horse substitute were presented as usual through the messenger and guard captain Nagami Souemon 《marginal note: Yasu｜taka》
1. On the 3rd day of the same month, as congratulations for the first Noh chanting of the year, sake cup stand and sake substitute were presented as usual through a messenger
1. On the 7th day of the same month, as congratulations for the young greens ceremony, dried squid was presented as usual through a messenger

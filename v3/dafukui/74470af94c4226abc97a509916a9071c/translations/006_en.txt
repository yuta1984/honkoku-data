Matsudaira Hyobu-no-taiyu's younger sister
Mourning period: From March 14th to the 23rd of the same month
Mourning dress: From March 14th to April 28th

The above person: Sakakibara Shikibu-no-taiyu's wife died of illness last night on the 14th. Since Hyobu-no-taiyu previously went as an adopted son to the Nakatsukasa-no-taiyu family, he observes a reduced mourning period. We hereby submit this notification. Above.

October 10th: A message came from Great Inspector Andō Chikugo-no-kami Nobumine, and deputy Matsubara Zenzaemon Norinari went to him and received the following written instructions regarding fire precautions:
　　　　　　　　　　　Memorandum
　　　　　　　　Fire prevention measures should be strictly enforced. In case of fire, without waiting for fire brigades, the person in charge as well as neighbors should gather people and work to extinguish the fire. When fire brigades arrive in succession, neighbors should withdraw and attend to firefighting at their respective residences, as has been ordered. Keep this directive in mind. The above.
　　　　　　　　　　October

October 15th: A messenger was sent via the deputy to Senior Councilor Abe Bungo-no-kami Masatake to report that the handover of Honda Magotarō Naganori's residence to the Construction Magistrate was completed today.

November 12th: [He] visited the Chamberlain Attendants and made a request for reading and listening privileges.

November 21st (around 5:30 AM): Due to a fire in the Kaminosuji area, [he] rode out, but as it was extinguished, [he] returned.
　　　　　　　However, since it was quickly extinguished, no formal report was made.

Same day (before 8 AM): Due to a fire at Shōrinji temple in the Ueno hills, [he] rode out, but as it was extinguished, [he] returned. Regarding this matter, deputy Murakami Santayū Masamine was sent as messenger to report to Senior Councilor Tsuchiya Sagami-no-kami Shigenao.

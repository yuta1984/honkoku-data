Same day: During the aforementioned fire incident,
Lords Tsunamasa, Kenmotsu, Princess Kiyohime, and Princess Toyohime took refuge in the rear fire-prevention storehouse.
Lord Masaaki sent messenger Kaga Tōzaemon Narioki with instructions:
Lords Tsunamasa, Princess Kiyohime, and Princess Toyohime were to proceed to the Asakusa residence,
Lord Kenmotsu was to go to Matsudaira Daizen-dayū Tsunahiro's lower residence (Aoyama). Sakai Yosanzaemon Motonobu was ordered to remain at the residence to oversee all affairs.

Same day: Through the deputy, a messenger was sent to Duty Councilor Ogasawara Sado-no-kami Nagashige reporting that [he] had ridden out to Mt. Tōei but there were no particular issues and the fire was extinguished, so [he] withdrew. Also reported was that due to the fire damage to the residence, [he] had moved to and was residing at Lord Takumi-no-kami Masahira's residence.

December 11th: Due to Princess Chiyohime's (consort of Owari Dainagon Mitsutomo) illness, [he] went to the castle to inquire after the Shogun's health. However, (having received notification from the Great Inspectors yesterday, the 10th), the Senior Councilors announced Princess Chiyohime's death, and accordingly [he] began to withdraw...

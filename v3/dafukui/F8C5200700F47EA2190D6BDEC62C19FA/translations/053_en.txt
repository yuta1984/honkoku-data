1. May 26th - Regarding the same matter, Lord Fujikake Uneme Eiji and Lord Nishio Shichibei came and conveyed the response from Lord Matsudaira Daizen-no-taifu Tsunahiro:
　　　　　　　　　　　Record of Verbal Message

　　　　　　　　Yesterday, through both lords, I was informed in detail that Lord Kenmotsu's illness was not improving, and I understood this. Recently, as he seems to be gradually recovering, I was surprised and
　　　　　　　　did not give an immediate reply. In this case, I have received and understood your thoughts as stated.
　　　　　　　　At present, there are various matters to be discussed, but the delay
　　　　　　　　is regrettable. Please convey this message as stated. The above.

1. In Edo, four of the designated sake shops that have been notified of official duties will conduct inspections and
   collect transportation taxes. In rural areas, the deputy magistrate will send around clerks to conduct inspections
   　and collect transportation taxes
   　However, in places where there are many sake shops, it is difficult for the deputy magistrate's clerks alone to conduct detailed inspections,
   　so one or two people, or about three or four people from among the sake shops in that area,
   　will be assigned according to the number of sake shops to conduct inspections and collect transportation taxes
   　and collect them accordingly
1. The sake shops assigned the duty of collecting transportation taxes shall employ clerks and such,

   and since expenses will be incurred for such matters, they will be allowed to collect fees so as to have some profit
   without suffering losses, so private domains should also be aware of this
1. The sake shops that perform the duties of inspection and transportation tax collection must also
   pay transportation taxes on sake they brew themselves, same as others

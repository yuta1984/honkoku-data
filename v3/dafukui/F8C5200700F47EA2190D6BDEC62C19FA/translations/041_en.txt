December 5th: Previously (on the 16th of last month), the residence of Ichimura Kan'emon within the Third Bailey was destroyed by fire. Since it was within the castle enclosure, [we] inquired through deputy Murakami Santayū Masamine to Senior Councilor Tsuchiya Sagami-no-kami Masanao whether a report should be made. Although it was a minor matter, since it was within the castle enclosure, [we] were instructed to make a report. Accordingly, the following report was submitted to Duty Councilor Ogasawara Sado-no-kami Nagashige:
　　　　　　　　　　Verbal Memorandum
　　　　　　　One samurai residence within the Third Bailey of my domain's castle was destroyed by fire on the 26th of last month. There was no spread of fire to other buildings, and it was quickly extinguished. I am reporting this matter through a messenger to obtain your acknowledgment. The above.

　　　　　　　　December 5th　　　　　　Matsudaira Hyōbu-dayū

December 9th: [He] visited the Chamberlain Attendants and made a request for listening to lectures.

December 10th (after 9 AM): A fire broke out from Ishimachi 2-chōme, and with strong northwest winds it became a great conflagration, so [he] rode out to Mt. Tōei.

Same day: Due to the same great fire, the Reiganjima residence was destroyed by the spreading flames (before 7 AM). When this was reported to the designated location, since Lord Takumi-no-kami Masahira was absent, [he] expressed his desire to proceed to the Asakusa residence. Therefore, [the matter was conveyed] to Lord Takumi-no-kami's chief retainer Shibuya Tanezaemon Mototada...

　　　　　　Iyo-no-kami
　　　　　　　Lord Tadamasa

　Keichō 2, Year of the Fire Rooster (1597)
　　一　On the 14th day of the 12th month, born in Osaka, Settsu Province (his fundamental trigram is Dui, his nature is Fire), respectfully called Lord Toramatsu (later renamed Toranosuke)
　　　　Second son of Lord Hideyasu, his mother being from the Nakagawa clan (called Okayama-dono, same mother as Lord Tadanao)
　　一　On a certain day of a certain month, there was a shrine visit to Ikutama Myōjin

1. 7th month, 14th day, the notification document of Soten's departure from the domain and the written pledge
　　were dispatched to Edo today
1. Same month, 17th day
　　The Shogun had been ill, but news came of his recovery, so messenger Hara Heizaemon (Masa-■)
　　was dispatched to Edo
　　　　　However, Heizaemon's arrival date in Edo is unknown
1. 9th month (day unknown), the assistance work for the construction of Tayasu Gate was completed, so Honda Izumo-no-kami (Tomimasa)
　　was granted leave at the castle and ordered to receive a horse and court robes
1. Same month, 12th day, regarding the completion of assistance work for Tayasu Gate construction, an official letter was

【Right Page】
　　一　8th month, 1st day: Utsunomiya Jibu-zaemon (Suefusa)
　　　　presented to the Divine Lord (Tokugawa Ieyasu) one Taihei bow (called Tenichi bow, made by Ryōkai), two arrowheads (also made by Ryōkai), and one spear (with Hidaritomoe inscription).
　　　　On that occasion, he was commanded to transmit the Gaikyu archery method to
　　　　Lord Toranosuke three days later.
　一　Same month, 20th day: By imperial command, Utsunomiya Jibu-zaemon (Suefusa) was ordered to perform at Sunpu
　　　　archery rituals for seven days for the pacification of the realm and the eternal military fortune of His Lordship. Upon completion,
　　　　he was granted two horses by the Divine Lord.
　　　　　　　However, in the summer of Genna 1st year,
　　　　　　　　although he accompanied Lord Tadamasa to the Osaka campaign, due to illness he did not

　　　　　　　　participate in battle and later died of his illness.
　一　9th month (day not specified):
　　　　Lord Toranosuke sent his envoy Sakakibara Shinbei (Shigenari) from his Edo Asakusa residence
　　　　to Kitanoshō to inquire after the health of Lady Chōshōin.
　　　　　　　However, the exact date when the aforementioned residence was granted is unknown.
　一　10th month, 1st day: As Toyotomi Hideyori gathered various rōnin and turmoil arose in Osaka,
　　　　the campaign of the Divine Lord and Lord Hidetada was announced.
　一　(Date unspecified): For this campaign to Osaka, there was a rumor that
　　　　those with maegami (young men's hairstyle) would not be allowed to participate in the campaign.

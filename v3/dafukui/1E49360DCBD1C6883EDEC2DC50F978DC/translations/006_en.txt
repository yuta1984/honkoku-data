During the reign of Lord Hidetada
　Keichō 12, Year of the Fire Sheep (1607)
　　一　Intercalary 4th month, 8th day
　　　　Lord Hideyasu passed away at Kitanosho Castle
　　一　11th month (day not specified)
　　　　Lord Toramatsu was summoned to Suruga Province
　　　　He had an audience with the Divine Lord (Tokugawa Ieyasu), and by his decree at that time, he was given in marriage to Katsukata (a concubine of the Divine Lord and adopted daughter of Ōta Shinroku Yasutsuna, actually the daughter of Kageyama Nagato-no-kami Ujitora) and became her husband, then proceeded to Edo (Honda Sado-no-kami Masanobu was residing in Suruga Province at this time, so the matter of accompanying [Toramatsu] was entrusted to Lord Sado-no-kami)

　Keichō 19, Year of the Wood Tiger (1614)
　　一　2nd month, 7th day: Utsunomiya Yasaburō (Suefusa) was assigned to
　　　　Lord Toranosuke by the Divine Lord (Tokugawa Ieyasu) based on a recommendation from Lord Konoe Nobutomo.
　　　　At this time, he changed his name to Shiroi Yazaemon.
　一　7th month, 14th day: Shiroi Yazaemon (Suefusa)
　　　　was granted an audience with the Divine Lord, and on that occasion was commanded to select an auspicious day
　　　　to transmit his family's traditional archery methods. He was bestowed a sword (Rai Kunitoshi) and three hundred ryō of gold.
　　　　By imperial command, he changed his name to Utsunomiya Jibu-zaemon.

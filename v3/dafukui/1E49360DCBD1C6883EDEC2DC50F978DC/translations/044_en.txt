1. Besides the prohibitions regarding bribes in writing, one must not greedily take and consume peasants' belongings.

1. Although the prohibitions are written before your eyes in official documents, one must absolutely not receive even the smallest items from peasants, nor accept gifts or be asked for favors.
　　Similarly, one must not pretend not to see or hear such violations.

1. Straw sandals, foot-half sandals, and leggings should be purchased and worn. Having peasants make and take them shall be considered a criminal offense.

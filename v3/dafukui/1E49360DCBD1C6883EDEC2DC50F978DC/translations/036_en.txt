　　　　　　　　　　Articles　　　　　　　　Fukui Town Notice
　　　　　　- One must not disobey the prohibitions issued by the Shogunate

【Left Page】
　　　　　　- One must not cut down bamboo, trees, or roots in the mountains and forests
　　　　　　- One must not engage in fights or quarrels
　　　　　　- One must not engage in gambling or betting at all
　　　　　　- Shooting various birds with bows and guns, or catching them with nets and traps,
　　　　　　　　is strictly forbidden in places without official permission
　　　　　　- Townspeople and peasants must not be disrespectful to retainers
　　　　　　- When there are violent people or wounded persons, such persons should be detained
　　　　　　　　and reported to the magistrate's office for judgment
　　　　　　- Regarding those who fled from the domain during the previous lord's time, whatever the circumstances

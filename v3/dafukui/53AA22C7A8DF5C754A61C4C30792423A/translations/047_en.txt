1st, same month 24th day: The lord went to Sabae for river hunting. The advance riders were Ichimura Kanemon (leading his unit with ten firearms) and Hasegawa Nihei Masayoshi (leading his unit with ten cedar-shaped spears). They carried the advance equipment (one set of bow stands, one streamer with crossbow gourds, camp stakes, umbrellas, standing hats, large bird feathers, spears, armor chests, backpack spear rests, two spears, two sword cases) (returned to the castle on the 1st day of the 7th month).

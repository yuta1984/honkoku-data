1. On the 11th day of the 2nd month, construction of a lord's quarters in the Third Bailey within the Gesha Gate was ordered. Regarding this construction work, the monthly duty senior retainer Nagami Shima Yoshihiro gave instructions to the construction magistrate Ogawa Jihē.

1. On the 18th day of the same month, regarding the presentation of year-end celebration gifts, an inner letter was to be delivered. The castle keeper went to the castle, and the inner letter was delivered in the Yanagi-no-ma (Willow Room).

"Five kosode robes have arrived as year-end celebration gifts, which are received with joy. Details will be explained by Sakai Gakutō.
27th day of the 12th month [Imperial black seal]"

To Lord Echizen Jijū

As thanks for the above, the castle keeper was sent as a messenger to the senior councilors and to Lord Sakai Kawachi-no-kami Tadataka.

1. On the 1st day of the 3rd month, an oral imperial decree was received.
Draft of the oral decree:
Presiding official: Sanjō Dainagon
30th day of the 12th month, Enpō 2nd year Imperial decree
Minamoto Masachika Asomi
Should be appointed as Jijū (Chamberlain)

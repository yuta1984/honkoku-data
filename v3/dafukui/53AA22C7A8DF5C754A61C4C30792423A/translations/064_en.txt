Hiroyuki (with official seal)
Inaba Mino-no-kami
Masanori (with official seal)

To Lord Matsudaira Hyobu-no-taiyu

1st, March 2nd
Regarding the occasion of the third anniversary memorial service for Lord Daian-in, Ozeki Sukezaemon Naomasa was dispatched to Mount Koya as a proxy worshipper

1st, On the 10th of the same month, regarding the year-end private letter delivery when retainers were ordered to receive gifts,
a messenger was sent for thanks, and today the reply letter was delivered

1st, On the 14th of the same month, pilgrimage to Heisen-ji temple in Katsuyama, returned to the castle on the 15th

1st, On the 24th of the same month, at Joko-in temple from the 20th until today
the third anniversary memorial service for Lord Daian-in was conducted. A tea ceremony was also
conducted at Daian-ji temple

1st, On the last day of the same month, departed Fukui for mandatory attendance [in Edo]

1st, April 8th, at Joko-in temple from last evening of the 7th until this morning
the seventieth anniversary memorial service for Lord Joko-in was conducted

1st, On the 12th of the same month, arrived in Edo. The notification of this was sent to the senior councilors, junior councilors, and Sakai

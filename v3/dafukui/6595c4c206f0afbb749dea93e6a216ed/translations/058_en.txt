One: Same day, regarding fire incidents in the castle town, at the residence of monthly duty senior retainer Honda Minbu Masayoshi,
　　the following was conveyed to the Takachi officials:
　　　　　　　When there is a fire, drums shall be beaten. Upon discovering a fire, drums should be beaten immediately.
　　　　　　　Although this has been the practice until now, henceforth colleagues should
　　　　　　　consult together and beat the drums as quickly as possible. By doing so, all quarters can be
　　　　　　　notified promptly, which is appropriate. Regarding deployment, in such cases, preparations should be made
　　　　　　　and personnel should proceed immediately once ready. Those who are prepared may
　　　　　　　be dispatched ahead by the monthly duty official. Such matters are not
　　　　　　　burdensome. In any case, notifying all quarters quickly is of primary importance. This matter
　　　　　　　was conveyed with instructions that each should discuss among themselves.

One: October 25th, local products of salted sweetfish baskets were presented as usual through official letter.

One: Same day, the hawk bestowed this summer was delivered through Wakadoshiyori Itakura Sado-no-kami Kiyoshi.

One: Same month, 4th day, regarding the official seal for Fukui Shinmei shrine territory delivered at Senior Councilor Hotta Sagami-no-kami Masasuke's residence on the 22nd of last month, an official letter of gratitude was submitted to Edo.

One: Same month, 6th day, regarding the same official seal, Ōban guard Ōtani Gen'noshin Yoshiyuki served as travel escort,
　　arriving on the 5th, so today it was delivered to Jufuku-in at the residence of monthly duty senior retainer Honda Minbu Michiyoshi.

One: Same day, Ashida Zusho Masashi was appointed to the position of senior retainer.

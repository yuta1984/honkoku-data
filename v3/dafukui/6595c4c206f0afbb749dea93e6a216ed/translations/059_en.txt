One: Intercalary 10th month, 7th day, since the delivery of the official seal to Jufuku-in was completed, an official letter was submitted to Edo as notification.

One: Same month, 16th day, the bestowed hawk arrived in Fukui and was received at the lord's quarters.

One: Same month, 22nd day, the shogunate's komono-metsuke (minor inspector) Kawamura Kakichi came over, and regarding matters such as deploying domain personnel to the mansion's crossroad guard posts during official visits, metsuke (inspector) Tsuchiya Chōsaburō made inquiries. The message was conveyed by rusui (caretaker) Tsuda Kuemon Morimasa, so the following written report was circulated to him:

　　　　　　　During official visits to Ueno, foot soldiers are deployed in front of the mansion's crossroad guard posts, traffic is blocked from the first ceremonial clearing, and passersby are detained. The following officials proceed to the guard posts to give directions:

　　　　　　　　Ōmetsuke (senior inspector)　　Rusui (caretaker)　　　　Yashiki-bugyō (mansion magistrate)
　　　　　　　　Kachi-metsuke (foot inspector)　Rusui assistant　　　　Traffic-blocking foot soldiers

　　　　　　As described above, foot soldiers are deployed and passersby are detained, though there have been no recent orders from any quarter regarding this matter. However, this has been the practice since long ago, with road duty foot soldiers consulting together and blocking traffic from the first ceremonial clearing. The above.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Retainer of Matsudaira Hyōbu-no-taifu
　　　　　　　　Intercalary 10th month　　　　Tsuda Kuemon

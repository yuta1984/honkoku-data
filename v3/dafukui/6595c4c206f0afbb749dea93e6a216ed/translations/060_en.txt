An inquiry was made to Chōemon Moritoshi, so the following written report was circulated to him:

　　　　　　　　From Ōte to the front of our mansion, then avoidance of Tokiwa Bridge street.
　　　　　　　　During official visits, officials, guard foot soldiers, etc. proceed to the crossroad guard post
　　　　　　　　in front of our mansion as during visits to Tōeizan. When the foot soldiers of the first
　　　　　　　　ceremonial clearing arrive, the aforementioned personnel and guard post staff
　　　　　　　　withdraw into the mansion grounds.

　　　　　　One: At such times, since the Dōsanbashi joint guard post is under our jurisdiction,
　　　　　　　　the deputy caretaker samurai responsible for the guard post and guard foot soldiers proceed,
　　　　　　　　and when the foot soldiers of the first ceremonial clearing arrive, they lead the guard post
　　　　　　　　staff and other domain foot soldiers to withdraw behind Lord Toki Ise-no-kami's mansion
　　　　　　　　and carry out traffic blocking duties there.

　　　　　　　This is how it is done. The above.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Retainer of Matsudaira Hyōbu-no-taifu
　　　　　　　　　　Intercalary 10th month　　　　Tsuda Kuemon

　　　　　　In addition to the above, the following was submitted as a written report:
　　　　　　　　Personnel who proceed to the guard post in front of Hyōbu-no-taifu's mansion
　　　　　　　　during official visits through Monzen street:
　　　　　　　　　　　Senior inspector, Caretaker, Mansion magistrate
　　　　　　　　　　　Foot inspector, Caretaker assistant, Traffic-blocking guard foot soldiers

　　　　　　　　When the foot soldiers of the first ceremonial clearing arrive, the aforementioned
　　　　　　　　personnel together with the guard post staff withdraw into the mansion grounds.

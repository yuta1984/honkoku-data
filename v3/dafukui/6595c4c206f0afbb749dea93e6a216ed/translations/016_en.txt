Item: March (day unspecified)
During Dainagon-sama's mourning period, vegetarian offerings were presented to inquire after his well-being.
From Yoshimaru-sama as well, one set of cypress boxes was presented.
　　However, regarding the offerings from Yoshimaru-sama, consultation was made with Elder Councillor Hotta Sagami-no-kami (Masasuke).

Item: April (day unspecified)

【Left page】
After Yoshimaru-sama's relocation, when serving in close attendance, short swords were to be worn. Since the same would apply when Muneyoshi-sama returns to his domain, a message was sent to the domain stating that family elders and others should wear short swords when carrying out official duties.

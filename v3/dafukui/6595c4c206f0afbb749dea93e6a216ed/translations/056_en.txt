　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　To the District Magistrate
　　　　　　　　　Memorandum
　　　　　One: The various portions of assessed silver taxes that group leaders communicate to village headmen in each village shall
　　　　　　　be recorded in monthly revision ledgers.
　　　　　One: Village headmen shall record in monthly revision ledgers all assessed silver taxes communicated from group leaders, including those from: the deputy administrator's office,
　　　　　　　mountain affairs, public works office, construction office, flood control office, irrigation office, road office, falconry
　　　　　　　office, river office, accounting office, permit office, and all other assessed silver taxes.
　　　　　　　These ledgers shall be submitted twice yearly in May and October, and based on these submitted ledgers, at the government office

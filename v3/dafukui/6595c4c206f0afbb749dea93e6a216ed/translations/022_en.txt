1. On the same day, the residence keeper Otani Ichiemon Yoshitsugu was summoned by the duty officer Honda Hoki-no-kami Masayoshi, and when he appeared, he was informed of the following through a written notice:
"Tomorrow, the 15th, Lord Ogimaru should ascend to the Great Interior. You should also offer your respects tomorrow. You should wear noshi-me lined robes and long hakama."
April 14th

1. April 15th　For the castle ascent, as usual, he waited in the bamboo corridor, whereupon
he was guided by Lord Kamio Ichizaemon through the Black Audience Hall veranda to near the entrance gate,
at which time Junior Councilor Mizuno Iki-no-kami Tadasada provided guidance to the audience chamber corridor.

When he proceeded to the audience chamber, Senior Councilor Hotta Sagami-no-kami Masasuke was present, and arrangements were made regarding the ceremonial seating, after which Iki-no-kami guided him to the audience chamber for the presentation. Junior Councilor Itakura Sado-no-kami Katsukiyo presented the ceremonial sword catalog, respects were offered, and he withdrew.
Subsequently, the presented twenty pieces of silver and twenty bundles of cotton were received, and he again took his seat. When waiting outside the threshold, there was a shogunal command to be seated within the threshold.
Lord Ogimaru offered respects for today's ascent to the Great Interior, had an audience with Senior Councilor Honda Hoki-no-kami Masayoshi, withdrew to the antechamber, and when he received his short sword, there was a shogunal command in the garden, where he personally received the gift of long abalone and withdrew to wait in the bamboo corridor, then proceeded as usual to the steps of the Black Audience Hall.

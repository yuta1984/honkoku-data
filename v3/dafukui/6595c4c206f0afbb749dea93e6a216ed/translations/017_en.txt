Offerings for when Lord Ogimaru ascends to the Great Interior

To the Shogun:
　A ceremonial sword
　Twenty pieces of silver　　　　Matsudaira Hyōbu-dayu
　Twenty bundles of cotton

On the day of Lord Ogimaru's castle ascension, Hyōbu-dayu shall ascend to the castle and present the usual offerings, and there should be formal thanks.

To the Retired Shogun:
　A ceremonial sword　　　　　　　　The same person
　Twenty pieces of silver

To His Excellency the Great Counselor:
　The same　　　　　　　　　　　　The same person

The above should be presented as specified.

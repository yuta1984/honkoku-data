Kan'en 1, Year of the Earth Dragon (This year was originally Enkyō 5, but the era name was changed in the seventh month)

1. On New Year's Day (1st day of the 1st month), for the New Year's celebration, [Lord Munefumi] went to the castle as usual, presented a ceremonial sword and horse fee, and received silk garments as a gift.

1. On the 21st day of the same month, at the Council of State (hyōjōsho), regarding matters of frugality, the monthly duty senior retainer Honda Minbu (Dōkō) issued instructions to all monthly duty officials through a written notice.
　　Note: The content of this written notice was the same as what was proclaimed at Edo last winter on the 22nd day, so it is not recorded here.

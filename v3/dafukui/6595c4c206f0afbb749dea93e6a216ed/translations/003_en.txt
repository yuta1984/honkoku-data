First Year of Kan'en Era, Year of the Earth Dragon 《Marginal note: This year was the fifth year of Enkyō; the era name was changed in the seventh month》

1. On New Year's Day, [Lord Munetsugu] went to the castle for New Year's congratulations, presented a sword and horse money as usual, and received silk garments as a gift.

1. On the 21st of the same month, at the Hyōjōsho (Council of State), regarding the matter of frugality, the monthly duty elder Honda Minbu 《Marginal note: Dōkō》 issued instructions to all monthly duty officials through a written directive.
　　　　However, the content of the aforementioned written directive was the same as what was proclaimed at Edo on the 22nd of last winter, so it is not recorded here.

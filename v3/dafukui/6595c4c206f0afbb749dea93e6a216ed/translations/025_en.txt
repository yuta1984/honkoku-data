Regarding Giyoshi-maru's formal visit to the Ōoku (inner palace) of the main castle, the caretaker Katō Chōemon Masatoshi brought and submitted the following inquiry document to Senior Councilor Hotta Sagami-no-kami Masasuke concerning gifts to be presented to officials as congratulatory offerings:

On the 15th of this month
Regarding Giyoshi-maru's visit to the Ōoku of the main castle, as congratulatory gifts to be presented to the Senior Councilors, Junior Councilors, Chamberlains, and Caretakers toward their respective quarters, we wish to inquire about presenting gifts from Hyōbu-tayū Giyoshi-maru, as has been ordered. Therefore, we humbly inquire as written in the attached document. The above.

April 18th　　　Retainer of Matsudaira Hyōbu-tayū
　　　　　　　　Katō Chōemon

On the 15th of this month
Congratulatory gifts for Giyoshi-maru's visit to the Ōoku:
　　Sacred sword and gold horse substitute　　To the Senior Councilors
　　Two kinds, 500 hiki
　　Sacred sword and gold horse substitute　　To the Junior Councilors  
　　Same as above　　　　　　　　　　　　To the Chamberlains
　　One folding box of sea bream　　　　　　To the Caretakers
　　The above from Hyōbu-tayū
　　Two rolls of silk gauze　　　　　　　　To the Caretakers

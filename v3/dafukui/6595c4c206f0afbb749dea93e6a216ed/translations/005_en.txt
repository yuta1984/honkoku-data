1. In the 1st month (date unclear), regarding the provision of escort horses along the route during the upcoming Korean embassy visit, the following circular came from the senior inspectors (ōmetsuke):
　　　　　Memorandum
1. Regarding the provision of saddled horses with full equipment along the route for the Korean embassy visit: since the roads became extremely congested in previous years, ensure that overcrowding does not occur.

1. At lodging and rest stops, horses that dismount before reaching the three envoys' inn should be led ahead of the three envoys' lodging, held back as requested, and stationed as planned. After the three envoys' palanquin enters the inn, at the discretion of Lord Sō Tsushima-no-kami's officials, lead them to their respective lodgings. When stationing horses, position them so as not to interfere with the Korean delegation's entry into their lodgings. There must be no overtaking of horses that were led ahead by those following behind; all procedures must be conducted with proper decorum. Officials of the Lord of Tsushima will be present in front of the envoys' lodgings to give instructions, so follow their directions.

1. On the 18th day of the 2nd month, when a government levy was ordered, both townspeople and rural residents complained of hardship, so the chief retainer issued the following directive to the town magistrate and district magistrate:

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　To Iwagami Kajidayū
　　　　　　　Due to extraordinary expenses this time, a government levy has unavoidably been ordered
　　　　　　　However, what are they thinking? Large numbers are creating disturbances, and moreover they are
　　　　　　　wandering about the castle town indiscriminately, showing no respect for the authorities' measures. In times of hardship,
　　　　　　　it is natural that there will be suffering, and everyone knows that there may be those who die of starvation

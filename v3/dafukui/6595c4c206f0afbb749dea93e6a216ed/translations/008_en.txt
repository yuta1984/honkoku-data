1. On the 18th day of the 2nd month, when official levies were announced, both town and rural areas appealed their state of poverty, so the senior retainer issued the following instructions to the town magistrate and district magistrate:

To Iwagami Kajitayū
Recently, due to excessive expenses, official levies have been imposed without suspension.
However, what kind of understanding do they have? Large crowds are causing disturbances, and moreover they do not hesitate to wander about the castle town improperly, which is behavior toward the authorities. If it is a time of poverty, it will cause hardship, and there will likely be those who die of starvation. Since these are all known matters, there are also thoughts regarding handling methods, and this is under consideration. Although things have been calmed through your own judgment, this should be communicated for future reference.

2nd month, 18th day
　　　　　　　　　　　　　To the District Magistrate

Recently, due to excessive expenses, official levies have been imposed without suspension.
However, what kind of understanding do they have? Large crowds are causing disturbances, and moreover the matter of wandering about the castle town improperly is no light matter toward the authorities. Furthermore, last year's crop damage was extensive, causing hardship, and there will likely be those who die of starvation.

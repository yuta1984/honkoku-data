He offered his respects, received the gift of hawks and horses, and his accompanying retainer Honda Yazumi Masaoki was granted an audience and bestowed gifts, after which they proceeded to ascend to the Western Citadel.
In the Teikan-ma chamber, he had an audience with the master of ceremonies Kobori Izumi-no-kami Masamine, offered respects for taking leave and for Lord Ogimaru's castle ascent, then withdrew and made courtesy visits to the Senior Councilors and Junior Councilors.
　　　　However, while in ordinary years the farewell respects are offered wearing half-hakama, today because the respects for Lord Ogimaru's castle ascent were offered in long hakama in the audience chamber, the farewell respects were also offered in the same attire.

1. On the same day at seven o'clock
From His Lordship the Shogun and His Lordship the Major Counselor
He appeared again to offer thanks for the sake and delicacies received on behalf of Lord Ogimaru, and made courtesy visits to the Senior Councilors.
Thanks for Lord Ogimaru's audience and bestowed gifts were also conveyed.

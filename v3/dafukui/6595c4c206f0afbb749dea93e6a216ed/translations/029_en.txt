Regarding the presentation of incense money from Lord Yoshimaru, an inquiry document was previously submitted to Lord Honda Hōki-no-kami Masazane, who was on duty. As no instructions had been received by today, caretaker Katō Chōuemon Masatoshi went to the same office to inquire. He was told that even if tomorrow's presentation were postponed and made at a later date, it would be acceptable, and that further instructions would be given.

Item: On May 1st, at Zōjōji Temple, regarding the same matter, messenger and guard captain Amemori Denzaemon Sadahide presented five pieces of silver as incense money. This was received at the temple's main hall.

Item: On the same day, regarding the announcement that Lord Yoshimaru would soon visit the Nishi-maru Ōoku (Western Palace women's quarters), concerning the presentations and gifts for that occasion, caretaker Ōtani Ichiuemon Yoshitsugu submitted the following written document to Senior Councilor Lord Hotta Sagami-no-kami Masasuke:

　　　　　　On the day of Yoshimaru's visit to the Nishi-maru Ōoku
　　　To His Retired Excellency:
　　　　　One type, one load
　　　　　　Plus one additional item
　　　We humbly request permission to present the above from our private stores. Above.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Retainer of Matsudaira Hyōbu-dayū　
　　　　　　　May　　　　　　　　Ōtani Ichiuemon
　　For His Retired Excellency's attendants:
　　　　Three pieces of silver each　　　　　　　For the senior ladies-in-waiting

Retainer of Matsudaira Hyobu-no-taiyu
　　　12th month　　　　　　　　　Tsuda Kuemon

One: On the 25th day of the 12th month
　　To celebrate His Imperial Majesty's recovery from smallpox and his taking of medicinal sake bath, messenger Moro Taemon Katsune was dispatched to Edo. He departed from Fukui today.

One: On the same day, salted cod for imperial presentation having arrived, it was presented.

One: On the 27th day of the same month, when Lord Kihachiro, Lord Dewa-no-kami Muneen, and Lord Saemon-no-suke Naozumi visited duty officer Lord Hotta Sagami-no-kami Masasuke together, it was announced that the hereditary domain of the late Lord Yamato-no-kami Akihiro would be granted to Lord Kihachiro, and due to his young age, a domain transfer would be ordered until next spring.

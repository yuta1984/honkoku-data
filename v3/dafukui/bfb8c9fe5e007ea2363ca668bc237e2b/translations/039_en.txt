The above tribute offerings, bestowals, and audiences for retainers, etc. 
were conducted according to the precedent of the previous lord.
Annual celebratory tribute items as well as local products offered as tribute, and bestowals,
etc. were conducted according to the precedent of the previous lord.
In addition:
From Kyōhō 12 (1727), when horses raised in the domain were first offered as tribute, thereafter
they were offered once every four to five years.
- Regarding banners, horse insignia, flags and below,
they were used according to the precedent of the previous lord.

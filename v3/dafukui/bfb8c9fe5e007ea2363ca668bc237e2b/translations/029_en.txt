Echizen Province Fukui Unseiji Temple's Zentatsu has requested retirement this time, so we are also informing Zōjōji Temple of this matter. Regarding the successor, as he has previously had audience, we are making this notification. The above.

　　　　　　　　　Retainer of Matsudaira Hyōbu-dayū
October 4th　　　　　Katō Chōzaemon

1. On October 10th, regarding the matter of Unseiji Temple's successor, notification came from the chief priest of Zōjōji Temple that a person named Ichimonji Sanbyōseki Hakuzen had been appointed.

1. On October 11th, audience and service with Senior Councilor Sakai Saemon-no-jō Tadayori.

　　However, since this was the first time for an audience after Saemon-no-jō's appointment to office, as a courtesy gift, a short sword and horse substitute were sent ahead through a messenger.

1. On October 15th, when going to the castle for monthly courtesy visits and proceeding as far as the dismounting point, he suddenly felt unwell and immediately returned to his residence. To report this, from the attendant group, caretaker Katō Chōzaemon Masatoshi went to the main castle and informed Inspector Kamio Ichizaemon. Caretaker Tsuda Kyūzaemon Moriyoshi also went to Duty Officer Matsudaira Ukon-shōgen Takemoto.

1. On the same day, for the same reason, through Chamberlain Daidōji Magokurō Shigegō, court physicians Mochizuki San'ei-rō and Takeda Chōshun-in were contacted. Chōshun-in came directly from the main castle and administered medicine.

Mochizuki Sanei administered medicine to her.

1. On the sixteenth day of the first month, the situation was the same as mentioned above, but due to the same illness, the physician was changed to Takeda Chōshun-in.

1. On the seventeenth day of the same month, the situation remained the same, but as her condition had changed and become critical, Dewako-no-kami-sama paid a visit, and through a request to physician Murata Chōan-rō, the medicine was changed.

1. On the same day, under the same circumstances, she passed away at the late hour of the Boar (around 10 PM). Her posthumous Buddhist name was:
　　Chikōin Shingetsu Myōshō Nichizen Daishi

1. On the eighteenth day of the same month, regarding the above matter, a notification was submitted to the duty officer Matsudaira Ukon-shōgen Takemoto-dono, with the resident officer Tsuda Kuemon Morohiro presenting the following written document:

　　　The birth mother of the former Iyo-no-kami, who resided in the middle residence, died last night on the seventeenth. Although this does not place Hyōbu-taifu under mourning restrictions, we respectfully submit this notification. The above.
　　　　　　　　　　　Retainer of Matsudaira Hyōbu-taifu
　　The eighteenth day of the first month　　　　Tsuda Kuemon

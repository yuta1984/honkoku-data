To the shiratsu (white sand courtyard), the senior retainers, castle deputy, officials, caretakers, personal attendants, and other officials and close attendants came out. From the Teppō-no-ma (gun room) to the Otsugi-no-ma (antechamber), from Honda Kuranosuke (Tomitsugu) to Kōchi Mitotanaka (Chōrin), Urakami Naizen, head of the great guard, from the rotating guard extras to the equipment heads, messenger guards and other regular members were stationed. From the long hearth room to the front great hall, messenger guard quarters, and great guard station, from the academy guard units to the great guard, reserve guard, and new guard of the same rank were stationed.
　　　After the coffin's arrival, from Kuranosuke and the senior retainers to officials and close attendants from Kōchi, all were ordered to pay their respects.
- November 24th: Funeral procession to Unshōji temple
- December 3rd: At Unshōji temple, immediate funeral services were conducted from the 28th of last month until this morning.
- The 10th of the same month: At Unshōji temple, the 49-day funeral services were conducted from yesterday evening the 9th until this morning. The same services were also conducted at Tentokuji temple.

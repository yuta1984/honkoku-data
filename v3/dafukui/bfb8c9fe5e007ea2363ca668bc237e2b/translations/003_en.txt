Second Year of Kan'ei, Year of the Earth Snake (1629)

1. On New Year's Day (January 1st), the New Year ceremony was held at the main castle keep.

1. On the same day, for New Year's congratulations, a short sword and horse substitute payment were presented through the messenger, Guard Captain Nishimori Denzaemon (Sadaei), following the usual custom.

1. On the 3rd of the same month, for the congratulations of the first Noh chanting ceremony of the year, a sake cup stand and sake substitute payment were presented through a messenger, following the usual custom.

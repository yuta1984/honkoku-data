Second Year of Kan'ei, Year of the Earth Snake (1629)

1. On New Year's Day, the annual New Year ceremony was held at the main castle keep.

1. On the same day, as New Year's congratulations, a ceremonial sword and horse substitute were presented through messenger and guard captain Amemori Denzaemon (Sadaei), following established custom.

1. On the third day of the same month, as congratulations for the first Noh chanting of the year, a ceremonial sake cup stand and sake substitute were presented through a messenger, following established custom.

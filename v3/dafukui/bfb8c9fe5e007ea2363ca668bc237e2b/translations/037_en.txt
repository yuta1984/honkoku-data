- November 5th: Regarding the departure of the coffin from the Edo residence tomorrow, the 6th, caretaker Yokota Sakudayū (Naomichi) went to report to Lord Honda Hōki-no-kami (Masachin), who was on duty.
- On the same day, the 6th (after the sixth hour of dawn), the coffin departed from Edo and traveled via the Tōkaidō route.
- On the 11th of the same month, at Tentokuji temple, from dawn on the 9th until this morning (two nights and three days), immediate funeral services were conducted.
- On the 22nd of the same month, the coffin arrived in Fukui. At that time, as was done during his lifetime, [it was placed] at the entrance to his quarters.

Regarding the above matters, we request guidance on duties while Ōi-no-kami of the same surname remains in his domain. That is all.
   August 14th      Matsudaira Hyōbu-taifu

1. On August 17th, there was a summons from Senior Councilor Tsuchiya Sagami-no-kami Masanao, and when the residential representative appeared, regarding the inquiry made on the 14th about
   Yoshikuni-sama's duties while residing in his domain, after consultation with Matsudaira Ōsumi-no-kami Tsugutoyo and Matsudaira Bingo-no-kami Yoshinaga, instructions were given through the chief retainer that duties should be performed as stated above.

1. On the 23rd of the same month
   Yoshikuni-sama departed from Edo and traveled via the Tōkaidō route.

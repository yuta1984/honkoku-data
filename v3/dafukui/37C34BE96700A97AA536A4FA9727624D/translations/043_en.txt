An increase in borrowed rice was ordered.

Memorandum
1. Those who have been granted fiefs of 600 koku or more in rural areas shall make uniform payments
1. Those with fiefs granted through deputy officials and from Akesato granary, with 550 koku or less, shall pay at a rate of 7 parts per 10
1. Those who receive cooperative silver and rice shall pay rice at a rate of 7 parts per 10 calculated based on their fief value
   However, stipend portions are excluded
1. Those receiving cut rice allocations of 21 koku or more shall pay at a rate of 7 parts per 10 calculated based on fief value
   However, stipend portions are excluded
1. Those receiving cut rice allocations from 20 koku down to just over 10 koku shall pay at a rate of 5 parts per 10 calculated based on fief value
   However, stipend portions are excluded. Those with cut rice allocations of 10 koku or less, down to the lowest ranks, are exempted from payment
1. Those receiving stipend allocations of 15 or more persons' worth shall pay at a rate of 7 parts per 10 calculated based on fief value
1. Those receiving stipend allocations from 14 down to 10 persons' worth shall pay at a rate of 5 parts per 10 calculated based on fief value
   However, those with 9 persons' worth or less are exempted from payment

Kan'ei 5, Year of the Earth Rat, 8th month, 28th day    Inaba Tamon
                                                        Sakai Tenzen
                                                        Honda Magotarō
To Yamamoto Jin'bei, Lord of Fukushima Checkpoint in Shinshū

1. On the 7th day of the 9th month, for the Chrysanthemum Festival celebrations, messengers were dispatched and kosode robes were presented as customary.
Ten silver pieces were presented to His Excellency the Dainagon, and white silver was offered to Her Ladyship and the Ladies of the Inner Palace.

1. On the 13th day of the same month
The preparations for Lord Yoshikuni's wedding ceremony were completed, and he entered the formal quarters.
There was a meeting with Lord Yoshipin, and as celebration gifts, a ceremonial sword and horse代 (ten ryō of gold) in two varieties and one load were presented, completing the ceremony.
Lord Yoshipin also entered the inner palace quarters, and at the formal meeting in the residential chambers, a ceremonial sword, horse代, and seed offerings were presented.

1. On the 28th day of the same month: Attendance at the castle (yesterday, the 27th, a formal letter signed by all the senior councilors arrived)
Gratitude was expressed for the successful completion of Lord Yoshikuni's wedding ceremony.

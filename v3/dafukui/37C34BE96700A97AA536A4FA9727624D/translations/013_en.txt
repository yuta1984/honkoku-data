To Yoshikuni-sama, a sword, gold horse substitute, and court dress (one unlined garment, one dyed summer robe, one white summer robe) - two types, one load were presented.
From Yoshikuni-sama as well, through envoy Ogino Magouemon (Masafusa), a sword, gold horse substitute, and received court dress with two types, one load were presented.

1. On the same day, a circular notice came from the chief inspectors requesting a written report on whether there were any persons under custody, so the following notification was submitted:
   "Regarding whether there are or are not persons under custody, we have been instructed to submit a written report down to our retainers. At present, there are no persons under our custody. That is all."
   August 13th   Matsudaira Hyōbu-taifu

   Record
   1. The matter of whether monthly inquiries about his well-being should be conducted via express letter
   1. The matter of whether duties should be performed via express letter when the Shogun and Great Counselor make visits to various places
   1. The matter of whether duties should be performed via messenger letter to inquire about well-being during cold weather

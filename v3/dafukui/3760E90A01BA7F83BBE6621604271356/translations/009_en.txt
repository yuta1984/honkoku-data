1. Empo 7 (Year of the Sheep), March 16: Suetaka Yotayu quarreled with Mizuno Heibei, and Heibei died. Yotayu was placed in custody of gun commander Sakakibara Jurozaemon and committed seppuku. Witnesses: inspector Hatta Kinuemon, messenger Ishihara Goemon.

1. Empo 8 (Year of the Monkey), February 15: Yamada Ichirozaemon committed embezzlement as finance magistrate and was placed in custody of gun commander Imaizumi Okuemon, then committed seppuku. Witnesses: inspector Niwa Hachizaemon, messenger Iinuma Kambei.

1. Empo 5 (Year of the Snake), May 21: Numano Shiroemon of the castle-keeping group committed irregularities while serving as lending finance magistrate and was placed in custody of gun commander Ichimura Kanuemon, then committed seppuku as punishment. Witnesses: inspector Niwa Hachizaemon, messenger Akita Rokurozaemon. Regarding Shiroemon's son Genhachi, he was placed in custody of gun commander Debuchi Shichibei, but being innocent, by mercy his life was spared and he was ordered into exile. It was ordered that inspectors would be present for the announcement, and that walking inspectors and foot soldiers would accompany him to the domain border, as conveyed to the inspectors.

1. Jokyo 4 (Year of the Rabbit), February 27: Senior councilor Taki Kazue was placed in custody of gun commander Debuchi Heibei, and his son Heizo was placed in custody of gun commander Suzuki Hikotayu. They committed seppuku. Witnesses: Murakami Santayu and Kaifuku Kansuke. Heibei served as Kazue's second, and Hikotayu served as Heizo's second.

1. Genroku 6 (Year of the Rooster), July 8: Castle-keeping commander Mon[unclear] was placed in custody of gun commander Yagi Heiroku and committed seppuku on the 10th. Witnesses: Takada Kintayu, with Imamura Denbei serving as second.

1. Same 8th year (Year of the Boar), May 21: Honda Gorozaemon, actual father of court page Kato Juzo, was placed in custody and committed seppuku. Second: Imamura Denbei.

1. Same 9th year (Year of the Rat), June 9: Chamber attendant Obata Niemon was placed in custody of gun commander Matsubara Shirohei and likewise committed seppuku. Witnesses: Iinuma Kambei and Hasebe Sobei. From this record, it was ordered that one foot inspector be dispatched, and Yagishita Sehei went out. Shirohei's son Genzaemon served as second by request.

1. Same 16th year (Year of the Sheep), February 18: Court page Hara Sukejiemon was placed in custody of Iida Jizaemon and committed seppuku at the guardhouse. Witnesses: Inoue Hantayu and Kawase Jitayu, with Jitayu also ordered to serve as second.

1. Hoei 6 (Year of the Ox), April 7: In Edo, Oshio Shinpei and Yasumi Juhaku quarreled. Shinpei was placed in custody of gun commander Ishikawa Jinzaemon, and Juhaku in custody of gun commander Ono Sanzaemon. They committed seppuku. Shinpei's second was Jinzaemon, witnesses were Inoue Hantayu, Kuzumaki Tojibe, and one foot inspector. Juhaku's second was Sanzaemon, witnesses were Tsutsumi Kichizaemon, Hiki Sazaemon, and one foot inspector.

1. Same 7th year (Year of the Tiger), March 14: Yasuda Toyotayu and Ochiai Shichibei quarreled. Toyotayu was placed in custody of advance guard Sakakibara Sukuzaemon, and Shichibei in custody of advance guard Kunieda Koichiro. They likewise committed seppuku. Shichibei's second was Koichiro, witnesses were Kawazu Zentayu, Negoro Seitayu, and foot inspector Tada Moemon. Toyotayu's second was Sukuzaemon, witnesses were Yano Gonpei, Yanada Tarotayu, and foot inspector Nagata Gitayu.

Seppuku
Punishment

1. When samurai of guard group rank and above are ordered to commit seppuku or be executed, one inspector, one messenger, and one foot inspector shall attend as witnesses. However, the number of foot inspectors is not limited to one.

1. When those below guard group rank are ordered punished, one foot inspector and members of the inspector group shall be sent to attend.
   Note: The same applies to quarrel-suicide cases.

1. When there is execution at the site, inspectors do not attend; one foot inspector and group members are sent as witnesses.

1. When there is a sword ceremony at the site, one inspector attends, along with one foot inspector and group officials.
   Note: When inspector Masugi Shozaemon attended such a site, he returned without reporting to his lord or notifying anyone else.
   1. Attire: field hakama, mounted during travel, both swords, summer and winter Buddhist-style haori, two attendants, sandal bearer, document box, spear.

1. When there are those for fire punishment, one foot inspector and group members are sent to the site.
(Red ink) Kyoho 2 (Year of the Rooster), June: Of eight for fire punishment, four were at Kamiguchi Yamasaki and four at Shimoguchi Funabashi, both locations.
   Note: Sending foot inspectors and group members for fire punishment is not for witnessing the punishment but because large crowds gather, and there may be various types of people among them, so they are sent to observe this situation. This is noted in the record of Kyoho 2 (Year of the Rooster), June 21.

1. Foot inspectors and group members shall also be sent to crucifixion sites.
   Note: Same as above.

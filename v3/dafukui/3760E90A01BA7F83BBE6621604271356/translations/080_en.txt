Punishment of Group Members and Below

1. Kyōhō 11 (Year of the Horse), between the 8th, 9th, and 10th months: When Yasugi Kakudayū of Nakamura Tarōzaemon's group was practicing cormorant fishing, he struck Arako ※crossed out: side｜shoulder※ and was ordered confined ※crossed out: restraint｜confinement※.
   Later, as Arako's wound healed completely, the confinement was lifted. 1. Same 18th year (Year of the Ox): A retainer of Sakai Genba named Takahashi Danzō committed counterfeiting, and since this was a serious crime,
   he was handed over to the authorities. Regarding this matter, Ishikawa Shōdayū of Nishio Gentazaemon's group, who was Danzō's brother-in-law,
   was serving duty in Edo at that time, so he was ordered to refrain from duties. When inquired whether a guard should also be assigned, it was decreed that
   he should refrain from duties but assigning a guard was unnecessary.

1. Genbun 2 (Year of the Snake), 6th month: A written directive was issued from Edo as follows:
   Recently, misconduct has occurred among various groups, and after investigation, appropriate punishments have been imposed on those involved. Regarding this, some group supervisors' service has been
   driven by personal gain, not considering the benefit of their superiors, and furthermore, not caring about the difficulties of their leaders, their service performance has been poor.
   Although this matter has been under investigation for some time, henceforth ※crossed out: people｜faces※, pay more attention, and if there are unsuitable persons among group supervisors,
   dismiss them and replace them before receiving guidance from above. Since each person should use their own judgment,
   if orders come from above for each case, it would cause particular hardship for the leaders. This message should be conveyed to subordinates as ordered,
   so please take note accordingly.

1. Same 5th year (Year of the Monkey): A member of Oguri Hachibe's advance guard group was escorting a prisoner in chains through the northern gate of the third bailey while crossing through the district office area.
   For this, his stipend was revoked, and Sodekawa Yozaemon of gate guard Hayashi Matazaemon's advance guard group was ordered to be confined, and this has been recorded.

House Confinement

1. When someone is stripped of their position and sentenced to house confinement, four men from their group are summoned to the council chamber. When the confined person returns home, two men are sent to follow and watch from a distance, while the remaining two are stationed at the council chamber to handle various matters. This is done in rotation with the council chamber guards.

1. At the same time, if the foot inspector under the jurisdiction of the council chamber is on duty that day, he is excused from duty, and the matter is reported to the duty foot inspector.

1. For house confinement, chains are lowered from the inside and the house is kept tightly closed.

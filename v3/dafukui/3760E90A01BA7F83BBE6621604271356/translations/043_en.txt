1. 12th day of the 7th month of Genroku 5, Year of the Monkey (1692): Kishi Dennosuke went to Hikime village estate on the 13th of last month, where recently the children of that village's peasants had repeatedly entered his privately owned mountain and cut firewood. When he inquired about this situation from village headman Juemon, there was misconduct and furthermore disrespectful behavior, so he killed Juemon. This was reported through written documents and oral statements via monthly duty residence keeper Makino Tonomo as intermediary. Dennosuke had been in discretionary confinement since that time. When this was reported to Edo, Dennosuke's behavior was inappropriate for his age and he was reprimanded through the residence keeper's office, and he was told that discretionary confinement would be pardoned. Monthly duty residence keeper Tagaya Gondayu conveyed these instructions this month.

1. 27th day of the 5th month of Kyoho 6, Year of the Ox (1721): Shimokawa Yajiemon's son was playing with a sparrow the evening of the 26th at the residence when a small hawk came and attacked the sparrow, which he killed, and there were also foot marks. Upon investigation, it was learned to be a training hawk of the falconers' children. 1. 8th day of the 4th month of Kyoho 16, Year of the Boar (1731): Senior guard Kaifuku Kansuke, during his recent journey at Kiyosu station, his retainer's sword scabbard slipped from horseback and injured a packhorse driver. His handling of the situation was poor, and moreover, the injured driver's parent followed Kansuke to Okaki station. Even after arriving in Edo, he failed to report immediately to the inspectors, which was considered negligent, and he was sternly told this must be reported. 1. 23rd day of the 10th month of Enkyo 3, Year of the Tiger (1746): Monthly duty inspector Nara Gonzaemon made an error in the Christian pledge document office, and regarding this negligent matter, discretionary confinement was requested. If it were a matter not brought before the lord, there would be ways to handle it, but since the document was presented to the lord and received his seal, strict measures were not taken. However, since inquiry was made, discretionary confinement was ordered. Monthly duty court attendant Namikawa Ichikuro was told that the error in the document was all due to negligence, and henceforth everyone should be careful and attentive. The scribes and others should be even more careful in their duties from now on, and intermediary Sasaji Gonemon was instructed to convey this.

○As per the separate document, to be ordered to the villages:
　An inspection of paper mulberry plants has been ordered to determine how many paper mulberry plants are planted in each village within the domain territory, so members of each group shall make rounds for examination and submit written records of plant quantities.
　　　　　However, the quantities of said paper mulberry plants shall not be reduced henceforth.

1. Due to the lord's current policy, increased planting of paper mulberry for official use has been ordered, so in villages, on wasteland or mountain fields, roadsides, and other places where other crops are difficult to cultivate, about ten locations per village shall plant cuttings or divided roots.
　　　　　However, villages with many planting locations shall be ordered to cultivate as many increased plants as possible.

1. When the time comes to harvest these official paper mulberry plants, the yield amounts shall be reported respectively.　　However, matters concerning storage locations and compensation will be ordered later.

1. Regarding paper mulberry middlemen and steaming houses within the domain territory, these have been prohibited in recent years, but henceforth if there are those who wish to engage in such trade, they may report after examination.

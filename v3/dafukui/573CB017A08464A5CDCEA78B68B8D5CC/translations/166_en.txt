1. Kansei 11, Year of the Sheep [1799]

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Administrative Head
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Magistrate

○Regarding the matter of reporting rapeseed quantities in koku to the Osaka Town Magistrate's Office following the official notice from the shogunate in the fourth month of the previous Year of the Snake, when inquiry was made to the authorities, it was announced that following the precedent from before April of last year, no notification was required for now, and accordingly the respective responsible officials were relieved of this duty.
　　　　　First Month, 16th Day

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　To the Monthly Duty Officers

○The years of borrowed rice among the retainer households have accumulated, and their increasing destitution is causing great concern. Therefore, following the precedent of the previous Year of the Horse, relief will be provided based on the rate per hyō [rice bale], so please understand accordingly.

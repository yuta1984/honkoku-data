In Edo　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Sano Naizen

○As previously announced regarding the handling of memorial services, those honorable persons interred in the home domain should have memorial services conducted only in the home domain, while those honorable persons interred in Edo should have memorial services conducted only in Edo. 　　　　　Tenth Month, 6th Day

In Edo　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Sano Naizen

○Regarding the status of minor officials as a group, marriage arrangements without proper lineage are not permitted, as announced last summer. However, for daughters of this status who serve in the inner palace and have been granted audience and bestowed with family crests, marriage proposals to samurai are permissible even without proper lineage. Of course, this applies only to this location [Edo], and regarding the home domain, the previous announcement should be understood.

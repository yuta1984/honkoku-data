Regarding the matter of capturing and pacifying all remaining rebels in Kyushu,
I recognize this supreme display of benevolence and courage, and my gratitude is extraordinary.
Hereby I add the position of Shugo (Military Governor) of Bunzen Province, and furthermore
this is an official decision. All should maintain complete tranquility. Therefore
this document is as stated.
　　　Kenkyū 6th year, 5th month, 16th day　　Yoritomo [monogram]

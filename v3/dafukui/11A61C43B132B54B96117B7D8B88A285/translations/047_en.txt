1. Same 8th year, 5th month: Regarding trade restrictions (tsudome) in our domain, a written report in response to an inquiry from the shogunate

Memorandum
1. Rice and miscellaneous grains: We export to other domains. When there is shortage within our domain depending on the year's conditions, we also import from other domains.
1. Wax, lacquer, oil, nuts: We export to other domains. When there is shortage within our domain depending on the year's conditions, we restrict exports.
1. Salt: We do not export to other domains. When there is shortage within our domain, we import from other domains.
1. Soybeans: Due to annual shortages within our domain, we do not export to other domains. We do import from other domains.
1. Salmon, trout, cod: When supplies are depleted within our domain, we do not export to other domains. We do import from other domains.

The above describes the situation regarding trade restrictions and imports/exports of rice, miscellaneous grains, and various commodities within the domain of Matsudaira Echizen-no-kami. For silk thread, cotton, hemp, and other minor commodities, there are no particular restrictions.

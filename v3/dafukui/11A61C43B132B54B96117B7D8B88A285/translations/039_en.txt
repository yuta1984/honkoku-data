First, Meiji 14th year (1881)
　　January rice per hyō: from 4 yen 25 sen to May 4 yen 40 sen
　　July-August: 3 yen 70-80 sen　November: 4 yen 10 sen
　　December new rice: 3 yen 90 sen
　Various commodity prices from October onwards: Charcoal 56 sen 2 rin　Lime 15 sen
　　Salt per hyō: 30 sen　Soy sauce: 20 sen　Seed oil: 55 sen
　　Paulownia oil: 50 sen

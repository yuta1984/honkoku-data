Chamberlain Minamoto no Ason Muneki
Senior Second Rank Acting Major Counselor concurrently Right Palace Guards General Fujiwara no Ason Tsunemasa

By imperial command it is decreed that the said person shall be appointed Assistant Captain of the Left Palace Guards

Kyōhō 18th year, 12th month, 18th day - Senior Secretary concurrently Head of Palace Maintenance and Sake Production Nakahara no Ason Moromori - Respectfully transmitted

However, as the 3rd and 4th were inauspicious days, the ceremony was held today

1. On the eighth day of the second month, items to be placed beneath Yōjuin-sama's stone pagoda were brought to Fukui by palace attendant Kumano Kakuzaemon and deposited at Unshōji Temple according to the senior retainer's instructions

1. On the same day, the 100th day memorial service for Yōjuin-sama was held at Tentokuji Temple

1. On the twelfth day of the same month, due to the above mourning period, a memorial tea ceremony was held this morning at Unshōji Temple

1. On the twenty-first day of the same month, when Edo representative Arakawa Sōzaemon Nao[aki] appeared at the castle, he received from the senior councilors an inner letter and official letter regarding the year-end congratulatory offerings.

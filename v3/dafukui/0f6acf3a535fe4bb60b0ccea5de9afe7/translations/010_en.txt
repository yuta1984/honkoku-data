[The lord] went to the Western Citadel to express gratitude, was appointed as master of ceremonies, and then made courtesy visits to the Senior Councilors and Junior Councilors.

1. On the same day, as a substitute for the fire watch duty at Tōeizan temple, the keeper of the residence was sent as an envoy to Matsudaira Sanuki no Kami Tadane.

1. On the 19th day of the 4th month, [the lord] had audiences with Senior Councilor Sakai Sanuki no Kami Tadane and Western Citadel Senior Councilor Kuroda Buzen no Kami Naokuni.

1. On the same day, the bestowed horse (a gray eight-year-old) was brought by an envoy from Senior Councilor Sakai Sanuki no Kami Tadane.

1. On the same day, regarding the transfer of the bestowed hawks through Junior Councilor Mizuno Iki no Kami Tadasada, Toda Gosuke was present and the hawks were handed over with accompanying documentation:

1. June 26th: There was a fire at the residence of Nishinomaru Rōjū Lord Kuroda Buzen-no-kami Naokuni on the 6th. Since this occurred within the castle grounds, as an inquiry about his well-being, messenger and caretaker Arakawa Sōemon Naoaki was dispatched to the duty rōjū Matsudaira Sakon-shōgen Norimura.

1. June 29th: At the Hyōjōsho (Council of State), eight retainers from among the domain's samurai were dismissed due to poor conduct.

1. July 2nd: For summer heat season well-being inquiries, messenger Tateiwa Kyūdayū Naomasa went to the duty rōjū in Edo and presented an official letter.

1. July 5th: To celebrate the announcement of the engagement of Lord Tokugawa Uemon-no-kami Munetake, an official letter was sent to Edo.

1. July 6th: For the birthday celebration, gold was presented through a messenger as usual.

1. July 11th: The following notice was issued to all domain retainers through a written directive:
　　　　　　All domain retainers should be mindful of their conduct on journeys and
　　　　　　should refrain from any improper behavior. Should anyone violate this,
　　　　　　there will be consequences as announced. All should take note of this. The above.
　　　　　　　　July 11th

1. July 12th: The 23rd memorial service tea ceremony for Lord Sei-in was held at Unshōji Temple.

1. July 18th: A petition for rebuilding the stone wall sections of the outer bailey of the castle's third citadel was submitted by caretaker Sakata Shichiemon Nagatsune to duty rōjū Lord Sakai Sanuki-no-kami Tadaoto.

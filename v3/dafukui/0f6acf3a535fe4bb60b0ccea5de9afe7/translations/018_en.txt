　Addendum
　　During residence in Edo, all visits should be even more modest, and even when receiving
　　various guests from construction sites and such, hospitality should be light in accordance with the regulations.
1. Regarding gifts
　　Except for礼物 (ceremonial gifts) sent in dealings with outsiders, doctors, and teachers, even for other
　　relatives and in-laws, sending gifts for
　　celebrations or condolences, farewell gifts, souvenirs, etc. shall be unnecessary.
　　However, sending light items between

　　parents, children, and siblings is acceptable.
1. Regarding various implements
　　As for weapons, one should maintain them appropriately according to one's station. For all else, make do with what the household
　　currently possesses, and acquiring new items shall be entirely unnecessary.
1. Regarding weddings
　　Though there were previous regulations, henceforth all matters shall be handled simply, with various
　　ceremonial gifts handled only between father-in-law and son-in-law, completed by
　　the day of the ceremony, and post-ceremony entertainment shall be unnecessary.

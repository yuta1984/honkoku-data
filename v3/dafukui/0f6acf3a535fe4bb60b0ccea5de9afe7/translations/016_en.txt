1. July 22nd: Regarding matters of simplification, a written directive issued in the lord's own hand along with accompanying documents was transmitted to all monthly duty officers through the chief retainer.

　　　　　　　　Regulations
　　　　1. Regarding retinue accompaniment for domain members
　　　　　　　All members, regardless of rank, should keep their retinues light according to
　　　　　　　their station. Even those of high rank, when in the castle town, should use
　　　　　　　only one horse for mounted transport. However, young men should primarily
　　　　　　　travel on foot, which is most appropriate.

　　　　　　　Addendum
　　　　　　　　When retinue members or messengers travel to Edo,
　　　　　　　　both high and low ranking officials should take fewer attendants than their
　　　　　　　　status would normally permit. When traveling as messengers to other domains,
　　　　　　　　the retinue should be appropriate to one's status.

1. Regarding building construction
　　　New construction work, even minor projects, is prohibited
　　　However, unavoidable circumstances are exceptional. If there is damage, repairs
　　　should be made, and if it does not look unsightly, it is acceptable.

One　On September 17th, the following circular notice came from the ōmetsuke regarding abandoned children:
　　　　　　Taking in abandoned children or giving them to others is strictly prohibited. However, when there are
　　　　　　unavoidable circumstances and they must be given to others, within ten years of age, first
　　　　　　report to the magistrate's office that took them in, or to the residence that took them in, and then
　　　　　　give them away according to instructions.

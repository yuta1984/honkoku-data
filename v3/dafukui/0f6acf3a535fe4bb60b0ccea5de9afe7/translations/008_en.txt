A kosode robe has arrived as a year-end congratulatory gift, which brings great joy. Details will be explained by Matsudaira Izu no Kami.

December 27th, Black Seal
　　　　　　To Lord Fukui Shōshō

Note: The seasonal inner letters mentioned above will have the same format after the appointment to Shōshō rank, so they will not be recorded hereafter.

1. On the 23rd day of the 2nd month, due to the official appointment, Suzuki Jūzaemon Junjun of the imperial table attendants was dispatched as a proxy visitor to both Ise Shrines and Iwashimizu Hachiman Shrine, departing from the Edo residence today.

1. On the 25th day of the same month, dried flounder from the domain's local products was offered through an official messenger as usual.

1. On the 29th day of the same month, due to financial restraint measures, it was decreed that in addition to 2,000 ryō as the annual allowance for Princess Hatsu, paper, candles, cotton, and cloth should also be provided.

1. On the 1st day of the 3rd month, due to financial difficulties in domain management, the senior retainer issued the following written instructions to all departments:
　　　Due to financial difficulties in domain management, there have been particular financial strains since last year. Therefore, gifts and stipends should generally be discontinued, however, for fire brigade duties, the three-district fire brigades and other public duties...

　　　　　　July 22nd

　　　　　　Summary
　　　　　　The domain finances have been in extreme difficulty since the end of last year, causing impediments and hardships.
　　　　　　Therefore, rice loans to retainers may need to be increased,
　　　　　　but as this would cause difficulties for all, His Lordship is also practicing frugality,
　　　　　　so for now there is no such measure. However, should unavoidable financial difficulties arise,

　　　　　　there may be emergency orders issued. Furthermore, hereafter
　　　　　　for official government duties or suitable expenses within His Lordship's means,
　　　　　　there may be occasions when excessive borrowing is made within the knowledge of the retainers.
　　　　　　Therefore, keep this in mind beforehand, and when orders are issued through written notices,
　　　　　　follow them accordingly and observe your proper station strictly. This is His Lordship's will. The above
　　　　　　　July 22nd

On the 18th day of the 1st month, the envoy for receiving the oral imperial decree, Wakabayashi Mizu-no-kami Go, and Kyoto caretaker Kawamura Gen'goemon went to the residence of Court Noble Sanjonishi Former Grand Counselor Ninkofuku-den. There, following instructions from the Kyoto Deputy's summons on the 16th, in the presence of Former Grand Counselor Nakayama Kanechika-den, the oral imperial decree was formally received. Immediately afterward, the official gifts were delivered to the steward as follows, and then by instruction, accompanied by the steward, they went to both Imperial Palaces to present the subsequent ceremonial gifts. This completed, they went to Kyoto Deputy Makino Kawachi-no-kami Hidenari and received a return letter for the Senior Councilor.
　　　　　　　　To the Forbidden Imperial Palace: sword and horse substitute, 30 pieces of silver
　　　　　　　　To the Crown Prince's Palace: sword and horse substitute, 20 pieces of silver

　　　　　　　　To the Palace of Sacred Mirror: sword and horse substitute, 2 pieces of silver
　　　However, there were also gifts to the senior court ladies and their quarters, but these are not recorded
　　　　　　　　Subsequent ceremonial gifts:
　　　　　　To the Forbidden Imperial Palace: sword and horse substitute, 1 piece of gold
　　　　　　To the Crown Prince's Palace: same as above
On the 20th day of the same month, the oral imperial decree and official letter were handed to Deura Ukon'emon and Hayashi Mizu-no-kami Go to be sent up to Edo. Ukon'emon departed from Kyoto today.
　　　However, Mizu-no-kami departed Kyoto on the 22nd and returned to Fukui on the 25th

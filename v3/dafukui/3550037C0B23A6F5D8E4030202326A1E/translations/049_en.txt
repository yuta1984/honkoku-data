One: On January 11th, as congratulations for the completion of the three-day New Year ceremonial rites, a letter was sent to Edo and one type of fish offering (dried squid) was presented

One: On the same day, a circular notice came from the senior inspectors stating that mounted men accompanying the directional fire brigades and additional fire brigades to fire scenes were causing congestion at the sites, so henceforth they should dismount from their horses about one to two chō away from fire scenes

One: On January 29th, a message came from Lord Shimada Sado-no-kami (Masanobu), and when resident keeper Ōzeki Shingozaemon (Naomi) appeared, he was informed through the following written notice regarding the matter of the Sunamura Shinden estate compound:

　　　Memorandum (on a paper pasted on the reverse fold: "Lord Matsudaira Iyo-no-kami's Sunamura Shinden estate compound")
　One: Regarding the enclosed buildings of the estate compound that were ordered to be demolished by this February as announced last year - they must be demolished without delay, promptly reported to the estate inspection officials, and the details must also be communicated here
　　　However, tenant farmers' houses of common grade should be left as they are
　One: The surrounding groves should be thinned so that the interior is visible. Other bamboo and trees should be cut and cleared according to instructions
　　　　January

One: On February 11th, the construction office, which had been located within the Kitte Gate until now, was to be returned to its former location behind the Iron Gate, so it should be relocated, as was ordered

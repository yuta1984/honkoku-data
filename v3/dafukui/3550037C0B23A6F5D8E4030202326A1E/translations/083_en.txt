One, on the 18th day of the 8th month, regarding the visit to Edo, ten bundles of colored ceremonial paper were presented as local gifts, as usual.

One, on the 21st day of the 8th month, for the Sekisai ceremony (at 6:30), he went out to the Sacred Hall (wearing formal long hakama), and presented ceremonial swords, gold, and horse substitutes as offerings.
　　However, for ceremonial texts, instead of sending a messenger, he went out himself due to an arrangement with Hayashi Daigaku-no-kami.

One, on the 25th day of the 8th month, there was a summons from duty magistrate Toda Yamashiro-no-kami Tadamasa, and when the caretaker went out, he was given a written document stating that regarding the celebratory ceremony for the shogun's appointment that had been mentioned beforehand, concerning the senior retainers' invitation, everyone should come on the 13th of next month.

One, on the same day, as thanks for the above matter, he served duty magistrate Toda Yamashiro-no-kami Tadamasa, sent senior retainer Ashida Uemon Kenshin as messenger to the remaining senior retainers, and sent protocol officer Hayashi Hachiuemon Masayoshi as messenger to the junior retainers.

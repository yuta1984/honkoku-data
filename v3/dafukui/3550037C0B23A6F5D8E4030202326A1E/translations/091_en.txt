1. Regarding items for which prices have been determined through contracts since the 9th year of Genroku (year of the Rat): for items that will continue to use those amounts in the future, fixed prices shall be declared based on current price calculations

For annual tax rice, small taxes, various transportation fees, and all commodities: for items whose prices were determined based on prevailing price calculations from the 9th year of Genroku (year of the Rat) until the intercalary 10th month of this year of the Dog, regarding unpaid balances or remaining payments for commodity purchases, where old gold was 100 ryō, 50 ryō of new gold shall be given; where circulating silver was 10 kan-me, 2 kan and 500 me of new silver shall be given

1. Regarding tribute offerings and bestowed gold and silver: since there are ancient precedents, there shall be no discrimination even with new gold and silver. The same shall apply to worldly ceremonial exchanges or gift-giving

1. Regarding borrowed gold and silver: for remaining repayments of loans borrowed before the 9th year of Genroku (year of the Rat), the same amounts shall be repaid even with new gold and silver. For loans from the year of the Rat onward: where 100 ryō of gold was borrowed, 50 ryō of new gold shall be repaid; where 10 kan-me of silver was borrowed, 2 kan and 500 me of new silver shall be repaid

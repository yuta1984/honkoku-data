Regarding the matter of attendants accompanying [lords] within and outside the castle, although instructions were issued in previous years, as conduct has become disorderly in recent years, the directives issued in the 12th year of Genroku, Year of the Rabbit, should be increasingly observed and enforced. The above.
　　4th month

On the same day, regarding this morning's official notification from Senior Inspector Naitō Hyūga no kami Masamine, when the house steward Miyakita Jūrōzaemon Sadayoshi went out to the residence, the following was communicated orally:

In recent years, during castle attendance, the sword bearers of the Echizen house and other daimyo families have been disorderly climbing up onto the ceremonial platform. Henceforth, except for those listed in the following document, climbing up is forbidden. Sitting on the cut stones is also absolutely not permitted, as was conveyed.

　Those whose sword bearers may ascend to the entrance ceremonial platform:

　　Matsudaira Sanuki no kami　　Matsudaira Wakasa no kami
　　Matsudaira Echigo no kami　　Matsudaira Izumo no kami

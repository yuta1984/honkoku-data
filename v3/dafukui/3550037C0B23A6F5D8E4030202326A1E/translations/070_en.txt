One, On the 11th day of the 6th month, regarding the matter of viewing mountains in neighboring provinces from our domain as previously ordered, the caretaker Miyakita Jūrōzaemon Sadayoshi brought and submitted the following written document to the accounting magistrate Ōkubo Shimōsa-no-kami Tadakura:

　　　　　Memorandum of mountain views from Echizen Province Fukui domain to Kaga Province and Wakasa Province

One, From the plateau at a place called Hamasaka-ura within Sakai District of Echizen Province Fukui domain, looking toward the northeast (ushi direction), a flat mountain with a pine forest extending into the sea can be seen at a place called Koshiozaki within Enuma District of Kaga Province Daishōji domain. The sea view extends about one and a half ri.
　Note: Hamasaka-ura is located north-northeast (between ne and ushi directions) from Mikuni Port in Echizen Province, at a distance of four ri.

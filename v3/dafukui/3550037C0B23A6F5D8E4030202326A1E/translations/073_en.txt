One, 22nd day of the 6th month: Regarding the official documents and domain registers that were customarily brought to Edo during the annual sankin attendance, it was decreed that from this year forward only copies should be brought.

One, 24th day of the 6th month: A message came from Accounting Magistrate Ōkubo Shimotsuke-no-kami (Tadakura), and when the caretaker Miyakita Jūrozaemon (Sadayoshi) appeared, he was instructed to submit another written document as follows, in addition to the previously submitted document regarding nearby mountains for observation:
　　　　From within Sakai District of Fukui domain in Echizen Province
　　　　From the plateau of Hamasaka-ura
　One, Koshiosaki in Enuma District of Kaga Province
　　　
　　　　From Shimoumi-ura in Nyū District of the same province and domain
　One, Tomarigadake in Onyu District of Wakasa Province
　One, Aoba-yama in Ōi District of the same province
　The above mountains for observation from Fukui domain toward Kaga and Wakasa are as written above.
　　Note: Although submitted as per the draft paper above, details are unclear.

One, 25th day of the 6th month: Regarding the presentation of Boys' Day congratulatory gifts, an inner letter was delivered at the castle, and retainer rewards followed custom.

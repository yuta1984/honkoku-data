1. When samurai of ban-gai rank and above go out, they shall wear shoulder garments (kataginu). Also, samurai who go out without attendants shall not wear crested formal attire.

1. Regarding requests by samurai who have ailments and wish to visit town medicinal baths repeatedly, there are precedents for this. They must submit a written petition. The reason for the physician's instructions must also be acknowledged. When it involves daily visits, consultation with this office is required initially, and afterward, when the bathing treatment is completed for the day, permission is returned with notification of the circumstances.

1. Regarding the entry and exit of ladies-in-waiting through the inner quarters gate, from 6 o'clock in the evening, consultation with the duty inspector from the inner quarters and acknowledgment thereof is required before passage.

Departure and return journey lodging arrangements: direct return to the domain as per the outbound journey, passing through Edo from Senju.
　　However, from Satte, Kurihashi to Ōta village being the territory of Lord Minbu-kyō, the district magistrate's subordinates and local inspectors came out to provide hospitality, with Retinue Inspector Assistant Ogino Koemon serving as guide and announcer for the journey.

1. In the 9th month of Hōreki 13 (Year of the Sheep), Lord Dainagon visited Sannō Shrine, then proceeded to worship at Momijiyama Shrine. At that time, preliminary worship was performed, so dismounting occurred outside Sakashita Gate. From the dismounting point, the retinue circled inside to wait at the advance guard station. The inspectors sent a minor inspector as guide, then preliminary worship at Momijiyama was performed. After completion, return to the palace was made as before, with the retinue waiting at the rest area outside Sakurada Gate and then assembling.
1. For preliminary visits during Ueno memorial services, waiting occurred at the inspection office from Meiseiinn lodging temple, then proceeding to the mausoleum as guided. The retinue members went around to the retinue area, with the general retinue going around to the front of Daijiin Gate under the direction of minor inspectors.
1. At Zōjōji, the same procedure: stopping at Ten'yōin, then waiting in the main hall. As in the previous article, the retinue waited outside the side gate of the mausoleum, with the general retinue going around outside the main gate. 《Marginal note: However, at both locations, among the retinue, court dress, plain robes, and white court garments as for New Year's, and falconers wear eight-virtue robes》
1. On the 1st day of the 3rd month of Hōreki 14 (Year of the Monkey), visit to Fukiage viewing area for Korean equestrian performance. After 5 o'clock, ascent to the main castle, then from the main entrance through the middle gate to the right via Terasawa Gate, Hasuike Gate, and Momijiyama Lower Gate, entering Fukiage. The retinue waited below Momijiyama, with the general retinue at the Ōte rest area. Return was made via the original route through Ōte.
1. On the 12th day of the 6th month of the 11th year (Year of the Snake), due to the death of the Great Retired Shogun, the funeral procession to Zōjōji on the 10th day of the 7th month at 8 o'clock. Dismounting outside Hitotsubashi Gate, the retinue waiting at that location, audience granted outside Hirakawaguchi Gate. At that time, Senior Councilor Lord Akashi Tadayoshi alone served in the retinue.

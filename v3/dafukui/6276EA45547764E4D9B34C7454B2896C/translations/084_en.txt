Circle mark

1. When the lord's consort and princesses summon theater performers for viewing, this takes place in the front formal reception hall. On such occasions, one inspector each is stationed on duty. Most importantly, all matters concerning the backstage area are secured, fire sources are inspected, and foot inspectors are also deployed.
   However, according to recent precedent (Hōreki 10/Meiwa 1), in the 4th year of the Monkey, inspectors Matsuura Kōemon, Kasuya Hikoemon, and Imamura Danemon and others served in this capacity.

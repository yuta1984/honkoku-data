1. Even those who come from the home domain, if they are not on official business and there is no notification from the official administrative offices, they must not be allowed to lodge within the residence.

1. Women entering improperly is not acceptable. If there is legitimate business it is acceptable, but nighttime entry is strictly forbidden.

1. Women from pleasure quarters and geisha-like persons entering is strictly forbidden.
   [Note in red ink: This passage was included but may have been removed this time. Also, practice within the long houses is acceptable, but holding improper entertainment and amusements]

1. Noh chanting within the residence is acceptable, but shamisen kouta (short songs) and similar music is strictly forbidden.
   The above regulations must be observed as written.

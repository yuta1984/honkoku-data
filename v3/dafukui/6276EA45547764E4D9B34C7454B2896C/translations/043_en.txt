During formal ceremonies and falconry outings by His Lordship, if fires break out along the route of the procession or return journey, even when firefighters, townspeople, and officials heading to the fire scene, or those evacuating from the fire scene need to pass through and would otherwise obstruct the passage, when clearing foot soldiers arrive, they may pass without hindrance.                     Articles
1. As previously established, regarding troublemakers, those engaging in fights or disputes, as well as the wounded, and all suspicious persons, they shall be immediately detained at intersection guard posts and reported to the authorities. Without instructions from the inspectors, they shall not be handed over to anyone.
   Addendum: When there are wounded persons, they shall receive medical treatment, and calling for a physician requires no special instruction.

1. When there are sick persons, they shall be brought into the guard post for treatment. Subsequently, sending them to their lodgings shall be at discretion. However, depending on circumstances, if the matter must be reported to the inspectors, it shall be according to their instructions.
   The above must be strictly observed.

                    Memorandum
1. The buying and selling of old metal is absolutely prohibited at road bridges. If anyone engages in such trade, the local intersection guards shall arrest them and send them to the magistrate's office. Those who overlook this shall be equally guilty.

1. All new metal goods, as well as copper roof tiles, lead roof tiles, and copper gutters, are absolutely prohibited from being bought and sold from now on. However, if someone unavoidably must engage in such trade, they must report to the magistrate's office.

1. Regarding the buying and selling of old items and other used goods, guarantors must be obtained for such transactions.

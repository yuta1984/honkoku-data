Gate Area

1. When there is commotion at the gate area, first send out foot inspector unit attendants to observe, and only if it is absolutely necessary to go out after that assessment, consult with the deputy and then proceed. In general, matters concerning the gate area are handled by the street guard personnel

1. Regarding matters handled by street guards, when there are instructions from the deputies, dispatch foot inspector unit attendants and others to be present

1. When daimyo wedding processions or funeral processions pass in front of the mansion, samurai and foot soldiers shall come out for security duty. This is directly ordered by the stewards. For ashigaru (light foot soldiers), inspectors handle the arrangements and assign numbers to unit attendants. Decorative water buckets are to be displayed

1. When holes and such open in the road in front of the gate, if it is along the water supply route, the deputy's office contacts the water supply magistrates. Otherwise, repairs are made by the construction office at our own expense

1. When there is cleaning of the water supply canal and the water supply needs to be stopped, notice comes from the deputy. In such cases, immediately notify the construction office, and the construction office issues notices about the water stoppage

1. When notice comes from the deputy about weeding the earthwork and stone walls behind the mansion, order the unit attendants and notify the construction office. Open the side gates and order unit members to patrol

1. When neighboring mansions give notice about sending people up to high viewing spots, notice comes from the deputy. At such times, order the foot inspectors and notify the inner storehouse officials

【Left page】
1. Those going out on personal business should report to the secretary's office as before, within the time limit until 4 o'clock in the morning. If there are unavoidable matters requiring permission to go out after this time limit, the circumstances should be reported and instructions requested.
1. Regarding the direction each person goes when going out on personal business, it has been ordered that this should henceforth be reported to the secretary's office, so the direction should be written on the personal outing form when submitted.
1. When returning from personal outings, those stationed at the upper mansion should go to the secretary's office and report their return to the protocol officials. Those stationed at the Reiganjima mansion should report to the temporary inspector Kanie Zen'emon. Since these matters have been ordered, you should understand their intent. The above.
　　　Kyōhō 1st year, Year of the Rooster, 4th month, 19th day

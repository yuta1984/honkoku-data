Kansei 5th Year, Year of the Water Ox (1793)

Emergency Fire Brigade Regulations

Article 1: When emergency fire prevention activities are ordered during a fire, personnel must be dispatched immediately. Officials should understand this in advance and refrain from going out on windy days.
　Addendum: Retainers should likewise not be sent outside the gate during windy conditions. This should be understood.

Article 1: Regarding fire prevention locations, follow the instructions of the caretaker officials, proceeding to the scene in order starting from the fire brigade banner (identifying marker).

Article 1: At the scene, when encountering shogunate officials or other daimyo, do not behave in an unseemly manner by stepping aside obsequiously. This point must be strictly communicated even to the lowest-ranking personnel.
　When the Three Branch Houses, Three Lords, Senior Councilors, or related family members pass by, there is no need to dismount from horses or bow - simply proceed as normal.

Article 1: Taking anything at a fire scene is prohibited by shogunate law, so this must be strictly observed by all, down to the lowest ranks.

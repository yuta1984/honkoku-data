[Chapter Four on Compound Words] (written in red ink at the top)
If one works diligently at the family business, one can achieve wealth.
○ Earth, sun and moon, day and night, this year,
last year, spring and summer, autumn and winter, east and west,
north and south, wind and rain, frost and snow, cold and heat,
thunder, forest, grass, flowers blooming, insects singing
The earth revolves around the sun, and the moon circles
following the earth. ○ The time when the sun is present
is called day, and the time after the sun has hidden
is called night. ○ The direction of the morning sun
is called east, and the direction of the evening sun is called west.
[○ marks are written in red ink]

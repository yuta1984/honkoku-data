　　　　　△　Nakarai
　During Lord Tadamasa's reign, 8th year of Genna, mizunoe-inu year (1622), in Echigo
　Summoned
　 ● Okamoto Taketake Hōgen Jukyō《Home province and birth province both Yamashiro｜Family name unknown》
　　　It is handed down that his ancestors were retainers of Kamo. Taketake's father was called Okamoto
　　　Sōju. Both father and son were disciples of Nakarai Kean, when Lord Tadamasa heard of this
　　　and sent a written order to Kean stating they should be taken into service, which is still
　　　possessed today. Because of this, in 8th year of Genna, mizunoe-inu year, he was summoned
　　　at Takada in Echigo Province and granted a stipend of 1,000 koku.

【Left page】
　　　　　　△　Iijima

　During Lord Tadamasa's reign, in Genna 1, year of Kinoto-U (1615),
　he was summoned.
　　● Iijima Shinzaemon Yoshihisa《home province and birth province both Kazusa, family name unknown, original name: Shirōuemon》
　　　　He served as a ronin retainer under Tagaya Sakon. During Lord Tadanao's reign, at the time of
　　　　the execution of Kuze Tajima, he followed Sakon and achieved merit with his bow. He received
　　　　certification from his teacher Hayashi Rokubei, and furthermore received a commendatory postscript. Later, during the Osaka campaigns, he
　　　　belonged to Sakon's forces in both years. During the winter campaign, at the time of the peace negotiations, a castle soldier named Takeda Denbei
　　　　brought twenty-six arrows inscribed with "Iijima Shinzaemon Yoshihisa" and sought out Shinzaemon,
　　　　saying: "There were arrows from various forces shot into the castle,
　　　　but none were shot as numerously and skillfully as these arrows. Being impressed by your exceptional archery,
　　　　I wish to make a master-disciple contract." He sent these arrows with this request.
　　　　Therefore, six of them were given to him, and the desired master-disciple agreement was made. At that time,
　　　　a secret manual was also transmitted from Hayashi Rokubei. This also had
　　　　a commendatory postscript.

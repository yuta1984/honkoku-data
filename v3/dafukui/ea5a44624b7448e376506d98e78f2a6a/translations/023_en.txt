　　　　　　　△　Sakai　　Terasawa

　When Lord Hideyasu entered Echizen Province
　Summoned to service in Echizen
　　● Terasawa Kanzaemon (personal name unknown, home province Kaga, birth province Mino, family name unknown)
　　　　During the Tenshō era, he served Niwa Nagahide and accompanied him during his domain transfer. During the Keichō era, he
　　　　served Niwa Gorōzaemon Nagashige, and the official documents still exist. During the Battle of Azai Nawate,
　　　　his reconnaissance work was excellent, and as an immediate reward he was granted a fine spear befitting a high-ranking warrior. Also on that occasion, he
　　　　speared an enemy and received a letter of commendation. There were many other records of military achievements, but after
　　　　coming to this land, having certain thoughts, he burned all those documents in a fire.
　　　　When Lord Tadamasa entered Echizen Province, among those who had served Lord Tadanao, Kaniza-
　　　　emon was summoned through an official document. During the journey to Kyoto in Kan'ei 11, year of Kinoe-inu (1634),
　　　　he served as an advance rider.

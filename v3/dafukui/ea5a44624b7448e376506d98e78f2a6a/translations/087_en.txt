　　　　　　　△ Ōtani

　During Lord Tadamasa's reign, Genna 5, Year of the Earth Goat (1619), at Takada in
　Echigo Province, summoned to service
　　● Ōtani Gizaemon Tomonao 《Note: Home province is Tōtōmi, birth province is Sagami, family name unknown, original name Seizaburō》
　　　　Tomonao's father was called Ōtani Seibei Shigeyuki. Shigeyuki's adoptive father was Ōtani Hachirōbei Shige-
　　　　tsugu, who lived in Enshū as a rōnin. His biological father was Ajioka Ichihei Masayuki,
　　　　who served Gongen-sama (Tokugawa Ieyasu), worked as ō-goban under Ishikawa Hyūga-no-kami's command,
　　　　and was granted a stipend of 700 koku. Once he was summoned before his lord and told, "Since you have been
　　　　training with firearms daily, when Takeda Katsuyori makes his expedition to the Enshū region and returns,
　　　　ambush him at the Niizaka mountain pass and shoot Katsuyori with your firearm."
　　　　He accepted the order and secretly took two retainers to that place, hiding in the mountain forests.
　　　　However, this plan apparently leaked to the enemy, and he was immediately discovered and ultimately
　　　　killed by the enemy. At that time, Ichihei's heir Seibei Shigeyuki was still young, so perhaps for this reason

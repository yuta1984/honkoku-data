[Right page]
　　　　There was a dispute between Kamiko-no-ue Tenzen and Tsuji Tarōsuke over who struck first, so Lord Umanosuke
　　　　sent a messenger to inquire of Seiuemon. Standing as witness,
　　　　he determined and testified that Kamiko-no-ue had struck first. Later, while he was a rōnin in Shinshū,
　　　　Lord Hideyasu heard of his aforementioned deeds and summoned him from Echizen,
　　　　but having his own thoughts, he did not accept at first. However, when summoned again through
　　　　Daikuhara Kuuemon, who was Seiuemon's friend, he accepted and
　　　　came forth, and later also served in the campaigns at Osaka.

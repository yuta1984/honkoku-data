【Left page】
　　　　　　　△　Ishihara

　Summoned in Echigo during the reign of Lord Tadamasa (year unknown)
　　● Ishihara Kurōzaemon (real name unknown)《Both home province and birthplace in Ōmi, surname Gen [Minamoto], original name Sasaki》
　　　　Served under Lord Katō Samanosuke, went to the Korean campaign, and later also
　　　　showed merit in the Battle of Sekigahara. He had a commendation letter from Lord Samanosuke, but it was lost in a fire and is no longer in his possession.
　　　　His original name was Sasaki. When his ancestors resided in a place called Ishihara in Gōshū, they took
　　　　the place name as their family name.

　　　　　　　△　Yamamoto

　During Lord Tadamasa's reign, summoned to service in Takada, Echigo Province during the Genna era
　　● Yamamoto Shirōbei Toshishige 《Note: Home province is Kaga, birth province unknown｜Family name unknown, original name Sakano Hachiemon》
　　　　Toshishige's father Sakano Hayato served Lord Maeda Toshiie. During the attack on Hachiōji Castle in Musashi Province in Tenshō 18 (1590), he took an enemy head in close combat. During the Battle of Sekigahara, when Maeda Hizen-no-kami Toshinaga attacked Daishōji Castle in Kaga Province, he died in battle at the assault point called Kanegamaru.
　　　　Toshishige participated in the Winter Siege of Osaka under the vanguard of Okajima Bitchū in the forces of Matsudaira Hizen-no-kami Toshitsune and fought courageously. Though wounded, he disputed the order of withdrawal from the battlefield and withdrew last, for which Bitchū praised him. During the Summer Siege of Osaka, Okajima Bitchū did not go to battle as he was serving as castle keeper of Takaoka, and Toshishige, being part of his unit, also did not participate in the campaign. After that, he was summoned to serve the domain.

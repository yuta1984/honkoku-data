【Left Page】
　　　　　　　△　Tateiwa

　During Lord Tadamasa's reign, the third year of Genna, hinoto-mi year,
　summoned to service at Matsumoto Castle in Shinano Province
　　● Tateiwa Giemon Masanaga《Note: Home province Kai, birth province Shinano, family name Minamoto》
　　　　It is said that Masanaga was the grandson of Katsurayama Rokurō Sadanori,
　　　　the youngest son of Takeda Shingen of Kōshū, who resided in Katsurayama in Shinshū. When serving Lord Kazusa-no-suke in Shinshū, his original name Katsurayama was
　　　　considered to be from an enemy lineage, so there were restrictions in Edo, and he was ordered
　　　　to change his name, thus adopting the name Tateiwa. He also accompanied his lord to the Siege of Osaka, and later was
　　　　summoned into service by the domain, after which he became an attendant to Yoshie.

　During Lord Tadamasa's reign, summoned to service
　in Takada, Echigo Province (year unknown)
　　　Tateiwa Kichidayū Yukimasa《Note: Birth province Shinano》
　　　　Previously served Lord Kazusa-no-suke together with his elder brother Masanaga, and also accompanied
　　　　him to the Siege of Osaka. After his elder brother Masanaga was summoned into domain service, Yukimasa was also summoned, and later became an
　　　　attendant to Yoshie.

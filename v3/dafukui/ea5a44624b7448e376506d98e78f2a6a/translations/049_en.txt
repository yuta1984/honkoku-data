【Left page】
　　　　　　　△　Inoue

　During Lord Tadamasa's reign, in Keicho 13, year of Tsuchinoe-Saru (1608),
　summoned at Anezaki in Kazusa Province
　　● Inoue Magozaemon Fusanobu《home province unknown, birth province: Mino, surname: Sugawara, original name: Hori》
　　　　Regarding his ancestors, it is passed down that he was from the clan of Lord Hori Kyutaro. He adopted the
　　　　name Inoue based on his maternal lineage. Fusanobu served as an attendant during the Osaka campaigns.

　During Lord Mitsumichi's reign, in Keian 4, year of Kanoto-U (1651),
　granted inherited stipend
　　　Inoue Shinsuke, personal name unknown《birth province: Echigo》

　During Lord Mitsumichi's reign, in Manji 1, year of Tsuchinoe-Inu (1658), granted inherited stipend
　　Adopted son
　　　Inoue Magozaemon Toshihide《birth province: Echizen, actually Shinsuke's younger brother》
　　　　From the adoptive father's inherited stipend, his younger brother named Inoue Ushidayu was granted
　　　　an allocated stipend of 100 koku, but in Jokyo 3, year of Hinoe-Tora (1686), they were all granted dismissal and the line was discontinued.

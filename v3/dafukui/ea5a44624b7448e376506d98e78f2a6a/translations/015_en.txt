　Summoned during Lord Yoshishina's reign in Jōkyō 3 (1686):
　　　Kunieda Rokudayū Sadamasa (born in Echizen Province)
　　　　Sadamasa had previously gone as an adopted son to Okayama in Bizen Province, but when the adoptive family had a biological child, the arrangement was dissolved and he returned to this province. At that time, his father Shichinosuke was ordered to retire, and his eldest son Ushinosuke inherited the family headship but died shortly thereafter. Since that child was still young, it was announced that Sadamasa would be specially summoned to care for the elderly Shichinosuke.

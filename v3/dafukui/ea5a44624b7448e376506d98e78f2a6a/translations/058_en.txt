【Left Page】
　　　　　　　△　The Akita Family

During Lord Tadamasa's reign, in the third year of Genna, hinoto-mi year, in Shinshū
Appointed
　　● Akita Kuranosuke Mitsukatsu《Note: Both home and birth provinces unknown, family name Gen, original name Hachihei》
　　　　Mitsukatsu's biological father was called Tsukada Yaemon, and he went as an adopted son to
　　　　one called Akita Shinemon. When Lord Tadamasa was in charge of construction work at
　　　　Edo's Taan Gate during the rotation period, he was appointed as construction overseer,
　　　　and at that time was ordered to carry matching ceremonial items during the journey.
　　　　He was granted and received two cedar-shaped spears. Moreover, during the construction
　　　　when Taitoku-in-sama made a formal visit, by imperial command
　　　　Lord Tadamasa bestowed upon Kuranosuke a personal letter, which he still possesses today.

During Lord Tadamasa's reign, the sixth year of Kan'ei, tsuchinoto-mi year
Granted succession rights
　　　Akita Ichihei Katsushige《Note: Birth province Echigo》
　　　　From his father Mitsukatsu's inheritance, an allocation was granted to his second son Hachirōbei,
　　　　and later he was assigned to Lord Masakatsu. His descendants still serve in Matsuoka today.

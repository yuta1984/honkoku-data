　　　　he served various roles at Yoshie, and after Lord Yoshishina entered Fukui he received gradual increases in stipend

　　　　fiefs were granted to second son Hikoshirō and third son Gonshirō, fourth son Kajikawa Tō-
　　　　bei was summoned, but eldest son Sanai soon died, so the succession went to second son Hikoshirō

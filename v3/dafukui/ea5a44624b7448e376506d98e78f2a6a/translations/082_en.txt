　　　　Because Seki Nagato-no-kami Kazumasa was ill, Sano Naizen Kagetoshi was to serve as military deputy and deploy
　　　　by imperial command, and upon receiving orders from Honda Kōzuke-no-suke, he led troops and
　　　　had an audience with Gongen-sama (Tokugawa Ieyasu) in Kyoto. After the Seki Nagato-no-kami house was dismantled,
　　　　he was summoned by Lord Tadamasa.

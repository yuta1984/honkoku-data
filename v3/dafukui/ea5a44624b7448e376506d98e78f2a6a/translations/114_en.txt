　　　　　　　　△　Enami

　5th year of Genna, tsuchinoe-hitsuji year (1619) during Lord Tadamasa's reign
　Summoned in Echizen
　　● Enami Kanbei, personal name unknown《Home province unknown, born in Mino Province｜Family name unknown》
　　　　Kanbei's father was called Enami Sakon-shōgen, and served
　　　　under Toki Sakyō-dayū Yoriyoshi. When Saitō Yamashiro-no-kami rebelled and Lord Sakyō-dayū was destroyed, it is unknown what
　　　　happened to him afterward, and whether he lived or died is not known. His son Kanbei served
　　　　under Lord Sassa Kurando-no-suke and also participated in the Korean campaigns. After the Sassa family was discontinued, he became a rōnin and withdrew to Mino Province,
　　　　where he was summoned during Lord Tadamasa's reign

【Left Page】
　　　　　　　　△　Ogawa

　During Lord Tadamasa's reign, Genna 8, year of the water dog (1622), in Echigo
　Summoned to service
　　● Ogawa Yoemon Atsukiyo《Note: Both home and birth provinces are Echigo｜Family name unknown》
　　　　His eldest son Ogawa Jiheiji Hideatsu was granted a separate stipend and summoned to serve as attendant to
　　　　Yoshie. Therefore, he adopted a person called Hachiya, the second son of Suzaki Saburōemon Masafusa,
　　　　to continue the family succession. However, because Hachiya suffered from chronic illness, he adopted another person
　　　　called Heitayū, the younger brother of Arakawa Sōhyōe Masamutake. This adoptee died some time ago, and shortly
　　　　thereafter Hachiya also died, causing the main family line to become extinct.

　　　　　　　　△　Hiratsuka

　During Lord Tadamasa's reign, at Matsushiro Castle in Shinano Province
　Summoned to service (year unknown)
　　● Hiratsuka Seizaemon Takamasa《Note: Original province Sagami, birth province Mino, family name Taira, Miura clan》
　　　　According to Takamasa, his ancestors were descendants of Prince Kazurahara in the 11th generation, lineage of Hiratsuka Gonzaemon Tametaka
　　　　Grandfather Hiratsuka Saburobei Takatada served Lord Hideyoshi and was granted a domain of 10,000 koku
　　　　His son Hiratsuka Inaba-no-kami Tamehiro died in battle at Sekigahara fighting for Ishida Mitsunari's side
　　　　His younger brother Hiratsuka Zenbo had been in Buddhist robes since childhood and served as Lord Hideyoshi's companion
　　　　Although his elder brother Tamehiro had opposed [Tokugawa] at Sekigahara,
　　　　Lord Ieyasu granted pardoned him and while he was a ronin, Zenbo's eldest son Takamasa was
　　　　summoned by Lord Tadamasa

　During Lord Tadamasa's reign, first year of Shōhō, kinoe-saru year
　Granted land allocation
　　　Hiratsuka Seiemon Takaitsu《Note: Birth province Echizen. His elder brother Saburozaemon Shikitaka was dismissed during Lord Mitsumichi's reign and the main family line was discontinued》

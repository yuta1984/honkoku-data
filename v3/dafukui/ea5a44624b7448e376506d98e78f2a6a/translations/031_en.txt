　　　　　　　△　Eguchi　Watanabe

During Lord Tadamasa's reign, Kan'ei 2nd year (1625), year of the wood ox, in Echizen
Summoned, and thereafter became an attendant to Lord Yoshishina
　　● Eguchi Hantayū Yukimura《home province: Hitachi, birth province: Echizen, family name unknown》
　　　　His ancestor Edosaki Gorōemon《personal name unknown》 was lord of Edosaki Castle in Hitachi Province, but in Tenshō
　　　　18th year (1590) the castle fell. One of his clansmen called Edosaki Iwami was summoned
　　　　during Lord Hideyasu's reign and by his lordship's will changed his title to Eguchi. During Lord Tadanao's reign he was placed under house arrest and
　　　　resided at the foot of Mount Echizen in this province. Hantayū Yukimura is said to be
　　　　the son of the aforementioned Iwami.

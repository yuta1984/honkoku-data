　　　Tochiya Hanemon Takatoshi《Note: Born in Echizen Province, original name Jinnosuke｜Biological father Arisawa Tazaemon》
　　　　His biological father Arisawa Tazaemon was the younger brother of his adoptive father Hanemon. Tazaemon
　　　　served as a masterless samurai under Naoe Yamashiro-no-kami, a retainer of Uesugi Kagekatsu, but later
　　　　was summoned to service together with Hanemon during Lord Tadamasa's reign. After his death, his son Hanshirō
　　　　fought with a man named Ueno Gonhei, and the Arisawa house was discontinued.

 During Lord Tadamasa's reign, at Takada in Echigo Province, 4th year of Genna,
 tsuchinoe-uma year (1618), summoned
　 ● Hattori Saburōzaemon Yoshinari《Home province and birth province both Iga｜Clan name Taira》
　　　　Ancestors unknown. Yoshinari's father, called Hattori Kyūzaemon, had
　　　　domain holdings in Iga Province, but fell into ruin during the collapse of Iga.

　During Lord Mitsumichi's reign, 4th year of Kanbun, kinoe-tatsu year (1664), granted inherited stipend
　　Adopted son
　　　Hattori Saburōbei Kaneyasu《Born in Echizen Province｜Biological father Fujii Kakuuemon》
　　　　Kaneyasu was the grandson through the daughter's line of his adoptive father Yoshinari.
　　　　Kaneyasu's biological father Kakuuemon, during Lord Tadamasa's reign, at the time of the
　　　　Shimabara Rebellion in Hizen Province, went to that place as an envoy and performed
　　　　meritorious service. He still possesses a testimonial letter from Matsui Shume,
　　　　a retainer of Lord Matsudaira Izu-no-kami. Kakuuemon's line became extinct.

【Left Page】
　　　　　　　　△　Tochiya

　Summoned to service at Takada in Echigo Province during Lord Tadamasa's reign
　Year unknown《Note: However｜》there exists a vermillion seal document from Genna 5
　　● Tochiya Hanemon, personal name unknown《Note: Home province unknown, birth province Etchū｜Family name unknown》
　　　　The father of the aforementioned Hanemon, a man named Tochiya Tondono'emon, served for generations under the banner of the Uesugi house, in service to Dohi Mimasaku-no-kami Masashige, lord of Yuminoshō Castle in Niikawa District, Etchū Province. During the Tenshō period, when Masashige came into conflict with Sassa Kuranosuke Narimasa and frequent skirmishes occurred, Hanemon also distinguished himself in battle and killed a man named Katō Daikura. Thereafter, he crossed spears with a man named Tashiro Yogorō from Sassa Shinemon's unit, and also took one head at a castle on the Etchū border. At that time, Uesugi Kagekatsu summoned him before all the assembled forces and bestowed upon him a cup. The letter of commendation from Kagekatsu from that time is still in his possession today. After Masashige's death, his son Hanemon also died, the hereditary retainers of the Dohi house were scattered, and Hanemon was summoned into [our lord's] service.

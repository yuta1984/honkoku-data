　During Lord Yoshikuni's reign, 3rd year of Kyōhō, tsuchinoe-inu year (1718)
　Granted succession
　　Adopted son
　Current head: Taki Kanzaemon Naotomo《Note: Birth province Echizen, biological father Akizuki Kichizaemon》
　　　His father Kanbei Naosen had become elderly and sickly, so in the 7th year of Hōei, kanoe-tora year (1710), the family headship was granted to his heir Kanbei, and Naosen retired, taking the Buddhist name Ryōgi. However, in the 2nd year of Kyōhō, hinoto-tori year (1717), Kanbei had certain circumstances and was granted leave from Edo, causing the family line to be severed. Nevertheless, since this was the family line of one who had committed ritual suicide, a stipend was granted to the retired Ryōgi, and based on this, Ryōgi petitioned for adoption. The petition was approved as requested, and after Ryōgi subsequently passed away, succession was granted.

【Left Page】
　　　　　　　　△ Iida

　During Lord Tadamasa's reign, at Takada in Echigo Province
　Summoned to service (year unknown)
　　● Iida Sakuzaemon Yasushige 《Note: Home province unknown, birth province is Tōtōmi, family name unknown》
　　　　Yasushige's father, a man called Iida Ichibei, was living in seclusion in a place called Magaya in Tōtōmi Province,
　　　　but he was known to Lord Tadamasa, and whenever the lord passed through that area, he was regularly
　　　　granted audiences. Regarding ancient ancestors, genealogies were lost in fires and details are unknown.
　　　　After Yasushige was summoned, he was appointed to the position of chief retainer, and his eldest son Shume inherited the family headship
　　　　and was also appointed chief retainer and served in that capacity, but during the reign before Lord Yoshishina he was granted retirement
　　　　and the line became extinct

　During Lord Mitsumichi's reign, Manji 2, Year of the Earth Boar (1659), granted his father Yasushige's
　allocated domain
　　　Iida Jizaemon Shigehisa 《Note: Birth province is Echizen, original name was Saemon or Chūshirō, religious name was Sanseki》

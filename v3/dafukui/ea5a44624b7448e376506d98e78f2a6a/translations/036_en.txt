　　　Kojima Gorōsaku Yoshikata《birth province: Echizen; Yoshikata died young and did not request adoption due to his youth》

　During Lord Yoshishina's reign, Genroku 14th year, kanoto-mi (1701)
　Summoned and served in a special capacity from childhood
　　Current　Ōki Yogorōemon Noritsuna《birth province: Echizen》
　　　　As many brothers had died and the Kojima surname had become unlucky,
　　　　he was ordered to change his title and adopted his maternal family name

【Left page】
　During Lord Yoshishina's reign, Hōei 2nd year, kinoto-tori (1705)
　　Ordered to succeed the Kojima house
　　Kojima Tonpachi Morisuke《birth province: Echizen, original name Kiuchi Koshirō, actually second son of Kiuchi Jinbei Sadamori》
　　　Originally he became heir to Kiuchi Denshichi and received a stipend to serve, but when Gorōsaku
　　　Yoshikata died without an heir and the Kojima house faced extinction, Morisuke was
　　　appointed as adopted heir to continue the line

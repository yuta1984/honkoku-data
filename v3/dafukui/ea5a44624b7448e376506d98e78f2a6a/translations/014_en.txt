　During Lord Mitsumichi's reign, family headship granted in Jōō 2 (1653):
　　　Kunieda Shichinosuke Ianobu (born in Echizen Province, Buddhist name Kōun)
　　　　Ianobu was ordered to retire in his old age, and family headship was granted to his eldest son Ushinosuke Sadakiyo, but he died of illness after serving only two months.

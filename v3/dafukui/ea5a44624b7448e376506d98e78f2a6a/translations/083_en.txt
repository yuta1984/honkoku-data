【Left Page】
　　　　　　　　△ Watanabe

　During Lord Tadamasa's reign, Genna 5, Year of the Earth Sheep (1619)
　Summoned at Takada in Echigo Province
　　● Watanabe Matahei Masayoshi 《Note: Home province is Shinano, birth province is Mikawa, family name Fujiwara, original name was Kana'uchi》
　　　　His ancestor served Lord Oda Nobunaga and distinguished himself in battle at Azukizaka in Sanshu, according to family tradition
　　　　After Lord Nobunaga's death, he served under Ishikawa Genba-no-kami, but after the Osaka campaigns became a
　　　　rōnin and lived in retirement in Shinshū. When Lord Tadamasa was residing in Takada, Echigo,
　　　　he was invited through Nagami Shima by private letter and summoned to service

　During the same reign, Kan'ei 6, Year of the Earth Snake (1629)
　Inherited family headship
　　　Watanabe Matahei Masatoshi 《Note: Birth province is Shinano, original name was Matajūrō》

Appointed during Lord Tadamasa's reign in the second year of Genna, hinoe-tatsu year
　　● Shimada Kuranosuke, personal name unknown《Note: Both home province and birth province are Mikawa》
　　　　During Lord Tadamasa's reign, Shimada Jiubei, father of Kuranosuke, was ordered
　　　　to recommend one person from among those of the same surname for service.
　　　　He accepted the order to recommend Kuranosuke from among his sons, and 
　　　　[Kuranosuke] was subsequently appointed.

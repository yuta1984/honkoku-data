【Left Page】
　　　　　　　　△　The Honda Family

During Lord Tadamasa's reign, the third year of Genna, hinoto-mi year
Appointed at Matsushiro Castle in Shinano Province
　　● Honda Gotayū Naoshige《Note: Both home and birth provinces Mikawa, surname Minamoto》
　　　　He has the same name as Honda Sado-no-kami Masanobu《Note: However, there are circumstances}}. Initially he served Kazusa-no-suke-sama and accompanied him to the Osaka
　　　　campaigns. Later he was appointed to the household through the mediation of Watanabe Tosho-dono.

During the same reign, the fourth year of Genna, tsuchinoe-uma year, in Shinano Province
Separately appointed to service
　　　Honda Genemon Naofusa《Note: Birth province Mikawa, original name Naiki》
　　　　Naofusa's younger brother, called Honda Jirozaemon Masaaki, was also separately appointed and served. His
　　　　adopted son Honda Gotayū was granted leave during Lord Tsunamasa's reign, and that lineage was discontinued.

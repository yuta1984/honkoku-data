[Left page]
　　　　　　　　△　Ichimura

During Lord Tadamasa's reign, Kan'ei 17th year (1640), year of the metal tiger
Summoned
　　● Ichimura Kanzaemon Masayoshi《home province: Mikawa, birth province unknown, family name: Tachibana, original name: Sugiura Sukenoshin》
　　　　His grandfather, called Ichimura Nuinosuke, became an attendant to Lady Chōshōin, and his
　　　　wife served as wet nurse to Lord Tadamasa. The couple served loyally, but when Nuinosuke died
　　　　without an heir, the family line was discontinued. However, during Lord Mitsumichi's reign, due to these
　　　　meritorious circumstances, he was ordered to inherit the Ichimura family name, and thus changed
　　　　from the Sugiura surname to become Ichimura. His biological father was called Sugiura Kurōemon Masachika,
　　　　who also served in the Osaka campaigns.

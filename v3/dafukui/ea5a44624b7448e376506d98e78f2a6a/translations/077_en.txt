【Left Page】
　During Lord Mitsumichi's reign, in the 10th year of Kanbun, year of the Metal Dog (1670)
　Summoned with special status
　　　Matsubara Shichidayū Katshide 《Note: Birth province is Echizen》
　　　　The succession of his father Katsiie was inherited by Katshide's elder brother called Magodayū, but later due to illness
　　　　he was granted leave. Katshide was summoned with special status.

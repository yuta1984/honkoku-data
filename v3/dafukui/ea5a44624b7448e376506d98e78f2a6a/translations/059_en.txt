During Lord Mitsumichi's reign, after father Katsushige passed away
Appointed with new stipend
　　Current Akita Sangozaemon Katsuaki《Note: Birth province Echizen》
　　　　After father Ichihei Katsushige died due to certain circumstances, the eldest son Rokuroemon was granted 500 koku,
　　　　and the second son Sangozaemon Katsuaki was granted 200 koku as a new stipend. As for Rokuroemon,
　　　　he was granted leave in the third year of Jōkyō, hinoe-tora year, and the main family line was discontinued.

【Left Page】
　　　　　　　△　The Ōzeki Family

During Lord Tadamasa's reign, in the fourth year of Genna, tsuchinoe-uma year
Appointed at Matsushiro Castle in Shinano Province
　　● Yasuda Uhēji Takayoshi《Note: Both home and birth provinces unknown, family name unknown》
　　　　During the Keichō years, he served Kazusa-no-suke-sama and still possesses the vermillion seal document. During Lord Mitsumichi's
　　　　reign, in the Jōō years, by his lordship's intention, the eldest son of Ōzeki Sukezaemon from among
　　　　the relatives was appointed as adopted son and was called Yasuda Sukenosuke.

During Lord Mitsumichi's reign, the second year of Jōō, mizunoto-mi year
Granted family headship
　　Adopted son
　　　Ōzeki Goroemon Masatsugu《Note: Birth province Echizen, original name Yasuda Sukenosuke, also called Ōzeki Yagoro》
　　　　During Lord Mitsumichi's reign, he was separately appointed while his father Uhēji was still serving, and later
　　　　in the second year of Jōō was granted family headship. By his lordship's intention, he changed from the Yasuda surname
　　　　to his biological father's surname Ōzeki, and was called Yagoro, but later changed again to Goroemon.

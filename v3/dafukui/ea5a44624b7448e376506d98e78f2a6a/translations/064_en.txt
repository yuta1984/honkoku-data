　　　　　　　　△　Kuno

During Lord Tadamasa's reign, summoned to service 
at Matsumoto Castle in Shinano Province. Year unknown
　　● Kuno Yohei Noriaki《Note: Home province Shinano, birth province unknown, family name Minamoto》
　　　　Noriaki's father was one called Kuno Rokurōzaemon, who served Asakura Yoshikage and distinguished himself in battle,
　　　　receiving a commendation letter which is still in possession today. Details of his military achievements are recorded in the Asakura Chronicles.

After being summoned to service during Lord Tadamasa's reign,

　　　Kuno Ihei Norimasa《Note: Birth province unknown, original name Kiuchi》

　　　Kuno Hikosaburō Norinori《Note: Birth province Echizen》

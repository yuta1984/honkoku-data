　　　　　　　　△　Abe

　During Lord Tadamasa's reign, summoned to service
　at Matsumoto Castle in Shinano Province (year unknown)
　　● Abe Kihei Muneshige《Note: Home province Shimotsuke, birth province unknown, family name Fujiwara》
　　　　Regarding his ancestors, during the Tenshō era, a man named Abe Tanba Munetake served as
　　　　lord of Sano Castle in Shimotsuke Province. Munetake's son, Abe Tōzaemon Munaharu, inherited his father's position and
　　　　served in that capacity. However, during the conflict between Lord Nobunaga and Hōjō Ujinao, he was stationed at Odawara.
　　　　After the fall of Odawara Castle and the dispersal of various forces, he served Uesugi Kagekatsu, and later
　　　　served under Honda Izumo-no-kami Tadatomo, accompanying him in the Osaka campaigns. His son, Abe Kihei Muneshige, first served Sano
　　　　Shimotsuke-no-kami Munetsuna, then wandered from place to place with his father, and was later
　　　　summoned by Lord Tadamasa.

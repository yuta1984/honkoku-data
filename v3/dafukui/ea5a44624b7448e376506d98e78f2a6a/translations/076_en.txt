【Left Page】
　　　　　　△　Matsubara family

　During Lord Tadamasa's reign, summoned at Takada in Echigo Province during the Genna era
　　● Matsubara Jinzō (true name unknown) 《Note: Both home province and birth province are Ōmi, family name Taira, later called Uemon》
　　　　His ancestors served the Oda family, and Jinzō's father was called Kitagawa Zenzaemon. After Lord Nobunaga's death, he withdrew to Matsubara village in Gōshū, and at this time changed his title from Kitagawa to Matsubara. Jinzō first served Tanaka Hyōbu-shōyū Yoshimasa, lord of Okazaki Castle in Sanshū. During the Battle of Sekigahara, he fought in various places, and moreover at Gōdo and Watarigawara, he grappled and killed Sugie Kanbei, a military commander under Ishida Jibu-shōyū. On that occasion he received a letter of commendation, which he still possesses today. When Lord Tanaka Hyōbu, later called Chikugo-no-kami, received Chikugo Province, Jinzō was granted a fief of 4,150 koku. After that house fell, he was summoned in Echigo and granted the same fief as before. Additionally, his second son Zenzaemon and third son Kakuzaemon were summoned with special status, and all three father and sons expressed their gratitude simultaneously.

　　　　　　　　△　Takahashi

　Summoned in Echigo during Lord Tadamasa's reign
　Year unknown
　　● Hashima Mohei (personal name unknown)《Home province unknown, born in Dewa Province｜Family name Fujiwara, original name Takahashi》
　　　　Mohei's father, called Takahashi Tajima, served under Lord Mogami Gyōbu, but when the Mogami house was dissolved,
　　　　he went to Edo and rebuilt his fortune. Mohei, in his youth,
　　　　fell victim to his stepmother's slander and jealousy, killed two members of her faction with his own hands, and fled his father's house.
　　　　During the Kan'ei era under Lord Tadamasa's reign, he relied on Hiraoka Ukon to petition and was
　　　　summoned. The year is unknown, but the vermillion seal document is dated Kan'ei 6, which he still
　　　　possesses. He changed his family name to Hashima, perhaps because
　　　　he had fled his father's house. The detailed lineage is unknown.

　9th year of Kan'ei, mizunoe-saru year (1632) during Lord Tadamasa's reign
　Granted succession

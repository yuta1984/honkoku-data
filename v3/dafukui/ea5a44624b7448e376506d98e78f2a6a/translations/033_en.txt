　　　　　　　　△　Yamamoto

　Summoned during Lord Hideyasu's reign, then later given leave
　During Lord Tadamasa's reign, Genna 6th year (1620), year of the metal monkey, recalled to service
　　● Yamamoto Shinano no Kami Ietsugu《home province: Suruga, birth province: Izu, family name: Gen (Minamoto), original name: Tarōzaemon》
　　　　He resided in Tago in Suruga Province and served the Hōjō family for generations, performing the role of naval commander. He still possesses and has handed down several letters of commendation from the Hōjō family, ancient documents, and written records with Tora's vermillion seal. After the fall of the Hōjō family, he was summoned by Lord Hideyasu. Since he had served as naval commander under the Hōjō family, he was ordered to guard and control Mikuni Port in this province, which was an important location. However, there were mistakes in his acceptance of duties, and as he did not comply with the lord's wishes, he was given leave. Thereafter, he went to Musashi Province and resided there temporarily. During Lord Tadamasa's reign, he was summoned in Echigo, and both Ietsugu and his heir Tarōzaemon Shigetsugu were granted stipends and accompanied the lord to Echizen. The lineage of Tarōzaemon Shigetsugu was later discontinued for certain reasons.

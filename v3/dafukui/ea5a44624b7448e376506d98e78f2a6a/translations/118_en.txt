　　　　　　　△　Hiraoka

　In the 6th year of Genna, kanoe-saru year (1620) during Lord Tadamasa's reign
　Summoned at Takada in Echigo
　　● Hiraoka Uemon, personal name unknown《Home province Iyo, birth province Aki｜Family name Ochi, Kōno house, original name Ukon》
　　　　Ancestry unknown. Initially served Fukushima Saemon-dayū Masanori, but having certain thoughts, he
　　　　left that house and was summoned to our domain, receiving gradual increases in stipend. During Lord Mitsumichi's reign,
　　　　his son Rokurōbei was also granted separate knowledge. After that, both father and son were
　　　　sent as attendants to Lord Masakatsu, and carried out the layout planning for Matsuoka Palace's grounds and the samurai residential districts.
　　　　After being appointed to the position of chief retainer and serving in that capacity, he was summoned back to Fukui.

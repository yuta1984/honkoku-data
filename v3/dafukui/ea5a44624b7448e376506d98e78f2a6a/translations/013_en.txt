　　　　　　　△　Kunieda

During Lord Hideyasu's reign, summoned in Keichō 8 (1603):
　　● Kunieda Tanomo (true name unknown, home province Mino, born in same province, family name unknown)
　　　　Tanomo had connections with Inaba Ukyō-no-shin and had served him from a young age. Later he served under Lord Terasawa Shima-no-kami, but for certain reasons he departed and was staying in Usuki in Bungo Province when, by Lord Hideyasu's wishes, he was summoned through Takaya Echigo. He participated in both Osaka campaigns and was appointed as an attendant to Yamakawa Sanuki due to the latter's young age.

During Lord Tadamasa's reign, family headship granted in Kan'ei 8 (1631):
　　　Kunieda Matahei (true name unknown, born in Mino Province)

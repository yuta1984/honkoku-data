There was an archery demonstration.

March 12th: The provincial inspectors (kuni-metsuke) Lord Koriki Heihachiro and Lord Mizoguchi Magozaemon came to this residence, so there was a meeting at the audience hall.

March 21st: As the provincial inspectors were traveling to the domain, for their official business, the residences of Ariga Honjiro (Masaai) and Takada Kozaemon (Kazuaki?) were borrowed, with the Sensui residence being lent to Honjiro and the Ikadamachi residence being lent to Kozaemon, as was announced.

March 27th: At Unsho-ji temple, from yesterday morning the 26th until this morning, the first anniversary memorial service for Lord Hosen-in was conducted. At Tenryu-ji temple in Shinagawa, a tea ceremony was also held.

【Left Page】
April 4th:
As the young lord went on his first hawking expedition, after his return, a caretaker was sent as a messenger to Senior Councilor Lord Ando Tsushima-no-kami (Nobuyoshi) to inquire about his well-being.

April 6th: Provincial inspectors Lord Koriki Heihachiro and Lord Mizoguchi Magozaemon were invited, and musical entertainment was ordered. On that occasion, by request, Lord Suwa Ise-no-kami (Tadabayashi) served as representative for various matters.

April 8th:
Due to the young lord's coming-of-age ceremony and appointment to Junior Second Rank and Major Counselor, as celebration of this occasion, all retainers were to attend court tomorrow the 9th wearing formal court dress (naoshi) and hunting robes (kariginu) etc. (notification was sent to the senior inspectors on the 5th).

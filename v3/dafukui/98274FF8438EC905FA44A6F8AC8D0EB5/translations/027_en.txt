When [the retainer] went there, the ōmetsuke Maeda Aki-no-kami Naokatsu, the machi-bugyō Kawaguchi Settsu-no-kami Shigekira, Nose Izumo-no-kami Yorisuke, and others including Andō Yojūrō Nobumune and Matsuno Hachirōbei Sukeyoshi were seated in attendance. Regarding the inquiry, Aki-no-kami declared that "the person called Kimura Sagotayū has still not appeared. Regarding this matter, since your retainer Okabe Hanbei Motonari is his brother-in-law, Sagotayū might well turn to him for help. You should order Hanbei to search him out."

On the same day, by written order, the first cod of the domain's products was presented as usual.

On the same day, from private quarters, the first cod of the domain's products was presented to Keishōin-sama as usual.

On the 23rd day of the 7th month, townspeople petitioned to sell nine guns from among their commercial firearms. This was granted as requested, and accordingly, one certificate each was submitted through a messenger retainer to the firearms officials Katō Hyōsuke and Nakabō Chōbei.

On the 1st day of the 8th month, for celebratory purposes, a ceremonial sword and horse substitute were presented through a messenger as usual.

On the 6th day of the same month, pears from the trees within the mansion grounds were presented to Keishōin-sama from private quarters, submitted through the messenger retainer Matsubara Zenzaemon Norinari to Honjō Inaba-no-kami Munesuke.

On the 9th day of the same month, the transfer of Maruoka Castle to Arima Saemon-no-suke Kiyozumi was completed as a grant. When the resident administrator Wakebe Hayato-no-shō Nobumasa, inspector Naruse Takiemon, and Watanabe Yanosuke departed from that place and passed through the castle town, the chief retainer Koma Mokunosuke Sadazumi and messenger Hori Mataemon Motochika were sent to the Ōhashi checkpoint.

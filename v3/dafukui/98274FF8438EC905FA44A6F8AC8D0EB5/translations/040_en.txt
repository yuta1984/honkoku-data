One: The same day - A message came from Chief Inspector Sengoku Hoki-no-kami Hisanao, and when the Resident Caretaker went to him, he was given a written directive stating that henceforth, regarding offerings to Odai-sama, Himegimi-sama, and Keishoin-sama, regardless of what they are, consultation should be made with the Senior Councilors and Personal Attendants before making the offerings.

One: July 22nd - Due to the great earthquake (from the 19th), [the lord] performed his duty to inquire about [the shogun's] well-being to Duty Officer Okubo Kaga-no-kami Tadatomo.

One: The 28th of the same month - Through an envoy, the first cod of the season from the domain was offered as usual.

One: August 1st - For congratulations
Together with Kenmotsu-sama, [the lord] went to the castle and offered a ceremonial sword and horse fee as usual.

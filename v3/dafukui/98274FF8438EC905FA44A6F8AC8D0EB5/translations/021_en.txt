1. March 26th: Regarding the recent order (from the 22nd of last month) that the domain of Honda Hida-no-kami Shigemitsu, lord of Maruoka Castle in Sakai District, be confiscated and placed under the custody of Matsudaira Iki-no-kami Nakazumi, and his eldest son Kazu be placed under the custody of Kyōgoku Kinai Takamichi, as the Maruoka disturbance was unpredictable, the monthly chief retainer instructed the magistrates to dispatch foot inspectors and their subordinates inconspicuously to that vicinity.

1. March 27th: Regarding the above matter, the monthly chief retainer instructed the advanced guard commander Kumagai Kohyōe Masanaga to lead his men to Imajō and strictly control the Itatori Futatsuya checkpoint.

1. April 2nd: When Yamato-no-kami Naonori departed for his domain after requesting permission to go to Edo for medical treatment due to illness, his condition became critical during the journey (at Suzukakesawa in Shimotsuke Province). His eldest son Izumi-no-kami Mototomo requested to go attend to him and asked for a physician to be dispatched. When this request was granted as asked, an envoy was sent to the duty councilor to express gratitude.

1. April 10th: Kenbutsu-sama accompanied [his lord] to visit the chamberlain Yanagisawa Dewa-no-kami Yasuaki to request an audience for listening to lectures.

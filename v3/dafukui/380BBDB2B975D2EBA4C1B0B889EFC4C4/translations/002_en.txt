　　Preface
1. This compilation has been prepared for elementary school students. Depending on the convenience of lessons, there is no problem with teaching one lesson in two or three sessions. This should be entirely at the teacher's discretion.

1. The characters in the text, whether elegant or colloquial, are based on those familiar to daily life, with easier ones placed first and more difficult ones later. This is done in consideration of the varying levels of students' academic ability.

1. From Volume 1 to Volume 3, the vocabulary words listed at the beginning of each lesson are intended for students to first recite and memorize

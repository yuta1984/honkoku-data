　△　Kojima family
Summoned in the third year of Enpō, year of the Wood Rabbit (1675) during Lord Yoshishina's reign
　●　Kojima Ryōho Sadaro 《margin note: original province Ōmi, birth province Yamashiro｜surname unknown》
　　　　Great-grandfather Kojima Sōin Yasusada served Lord Mori Chūjō-sama and Lord Dōnai-ki-sama
　　　　His son Kojima Ryōtaku Sadakatsu became a disciple of Hōin Gensaku of Enjuin and practiced
　　　　medicine, but died in his prime. His son Kojima Ryōho Sadaro was still young at that time
　　　　and did not inherit the family profession. Raised by his grandfather Sōin, he grew up and studied
　　　　the Furuta Oribe school of tea ceremony, then became a disciple of Sen-shi Sōtan and was in Kyoto when Lord Yoshishina
　　　　heard of him through connections with the Mori family and summoned him
Summoned in the second year of Genroku, year of the Earth Snake (1689) during Lord Yoshishina's reign
Granted succession in the eleventh year of Genroku, year of the Earth Tiger (1698) of the same reign
　Current　Kojima Sōtsu Hōro 《margin note: birth province Yamashiro》

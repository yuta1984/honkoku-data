　△　Kojima family
Summoned in the third year of Enpō, year of the Wood Rabbit (1675) during Lord Yoshishina's reign
　●　Kojima Ryōho Sadaro 《margin note: original province Ōmi, birth province Yamashiro｜surname unknown》
　　　　His great-grandfather Kojima Sōin Yasusada served Lord Mori Chūjō and Lord Naiki
　　　　His son Kojima Ryōyo Sadakatsu became a disciple of Enjuin Gensaku Hōin and
　　　　practiced medicine, but died in his prime. His son Kojima Ryōho Sadaro was
　　　　still young at that time and could not inherit the family profession. He was raised
　　　　by his grandfather Sōin, studied tea ceremony of the Furuta Oribe school,
　　　　became a disciple of Sen-shi Sōtan, and was in Kyoto when Lord Yoshishina
　　　　heard of him through connections with the Mori family and summoned him
Summoned in the second year of Genroku, year of the Earth Snake (1689) during Lord Yoshishina's reign
Granted succession in the eleventh year of Genroku, year of the Earth Tiger (1698) during the same reign
　Current　Kojima Sōtsu Hōro 《margin note: birth province Yamashiro》

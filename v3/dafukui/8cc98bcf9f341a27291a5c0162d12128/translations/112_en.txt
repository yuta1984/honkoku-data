△ Ugai
Summoned in the era of Lord Tsunamasa, in the 6th year of Enpō, year of the Earth Horse (1678)
Current: Ugai Hachirōuemon Shigeyoshi <<Original province: Unknown, Birth province: Echizen | Surname: Unknown>>
His father, Ugai Hachihyōe Shigemasa, was summoned during Lord Tadamasa's reign and served until the 5th year of Enpō, year of the Fire Snake (1677) during Lord Tsunamasa's reign. When the succession was given to his legitimate son Dennojō, Hachirōuemon was also summoned into service.

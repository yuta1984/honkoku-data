△Ugai
Summoned to service during Lord Tsunamasa's reign, in the 6th year of Enpō, year of the Earth Horse (1678)
Current head: Ugai Hachirōemon Shigeyoshi 《Original province unknown, birthplace: Echizen | Surname unknown》
His father, Ugai Hachihyōe Shigemasa, was summoned to service during Lord Tadamasa's reign and served until the 5th year of Enpō, year of the Fire Snake (1677) during Lord Tsunamasa's reign. When the succession was passed to his heir, his eldest son Dennosuke, Hachirōemon was summoned to service.

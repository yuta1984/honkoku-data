　　　　△　Imadate
Summoned to service in the reign of Lord Mitsumichi, in the third year of Keian, Kanoe-Tora year (1650).
　●Imadate Rokuuemon Masatane《Note: Home province Echizen, birth province Iga｜Family name Fujiwara》
　　Regarding his ancestors, he was the younger brother of one called Yasoshima Kagauemon. Details are found
　　in the biography of Imadate Sukezaemon Toyohide.
In the reign of Lord Yoshishina, in the third year of Genroku, Kanoe-Uma year (1690), he was granted succession.
　Yasoshima Sanzaemon Masafusa《Note: Birth province Echizen｜Originally called Imadate, later became Yasoshima》
In the reign of Lord Yoshikuni, in the seventh year of Hōei, Kanoe-Tora year (1710), he was granted inherited lands.

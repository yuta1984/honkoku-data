　　　　△　Arakawa
Summoned to service in the reign of Lord Mitsumichi, in the first year of Kanbun, Kanoto-Ushi year (1661).
　●Arakawa Hikodayū Takayoshi《Note: Home province Shimotsuke, birth province Echizen｜Family name unknown, Buddhist name Bokujun》
　　Takayoshi's father, a man called Arakawa Shirōdayū Shigemasa, served during the reign of Lord Tadanao, but due to certain circumstances, he requested leave and was living in retirement in Tsuruga in this province. However, when the Siege of Osaka occurred, he borrowed military equipment from Honda Izunokami Tomimasa, went to battle, and died in combat. Due to this connection, Takayoshi had previously received support from Izunokami and was residing in Fuchū, and was subsequently summoned to service.

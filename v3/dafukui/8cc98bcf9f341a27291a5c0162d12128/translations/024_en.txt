　　　　△　Arakawa
Summoned to service in the reign of Lord Mitsumichi, in the first year of Kanbun, Kanoto-Ushi year (1661).
　●Arakawa Hikodayū Takayoshi《Note: Home province Shimotsuke, birth province Echizen｜Family name unknown, religious name Bokujun》
　　Takayoshi's father, called Arakawa Shirōdayū Shigemasa, served during the reign of Lord Tadanao, but due to certain circumstances he requested leave and was living in retirement in Tsuruga in this province. When the Siege of Osaka occurred, he borrowed military equipment from Honda Izunokami Tomimasa, went to battle, and died in combat. Due to this lineage, Takayoshi had previously received support from Izunokami and lived in Fuchū, and was later summoned to service.

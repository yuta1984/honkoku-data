　●Hayashi Jūbei Yoshikata《Note: Home province Bitchū, Birth province Bingo, Original name Genemon｜Family name unknown, Original surname Tai》
　　　　Yoshikata's grandfather Tai Nagato-no-kami Harushige had been the lord of Tai Castle in Kawakami District, Bitchū Province since ancient times, but due to certain circumstances he abandoned the castle and served as a guest retainer under the invitation of Lord Mizuno Sōhyōe-no-jō. His son Tai Sukenosuke Shigeie served as flag magistrate under Lord Mizuno Hyūga-no-kami, and Yoshikata also served in that household, but after leaving that house, he came to serve our domain (Fukui Domain).

In the reign of Lord Tsunamasa, in the sixth year of Enpō, Tsuchinoe-Uma year (1678), he was separately summoned to service.
In the reign of Lord Yoshikuni, in the second year of Kyōhō, Hinoto-Tori year (1717), he was granted succession.
　The current Hayashi Tōbei Motoyoshi《Note: Birth province Yamashiro》

　　　　△　Hayashi
In the reign of Lord Mitsumichi, summoned to service in the 1st year of Manji, Tsuchinoe-Inu year (1658).
　●Hayashi Jūbei Yoshikata《Note: Home province Bitchū　Birth province Bingo　Original name Genemon｜Family name unknown　Original surname Tai》
　　Yoshikata's grandfather Tai Nagato-no-kami Harushige had been the lord of Tai Castle in Kawakami District, Bitchū Province since ancient times, but due to certain circumstances he abandoned the castle and served as a guest retainer under the invitation of Lord Mizuno Sōhyōe-no-jō. His son Tai Sukenosuke Shigeie served as acting flag magistrate under Lord Mizuno Hyūga-no-kami. Yoshikata also served there, but after leaving that household, he entered service with our domain (Fukui Domain).

Separately summoned to service in the reign of Lord Tsunamasa, in the 6th year of Enpō, Tsuchinoe-Uma year (1678)
Granted succession in the reign of Lord Yoshikuni, in the 2nd year of Kyōhō, Hinoto-Tori year (1717)
　Current generation　Hayashi Tōbei Motoyoshi《Note: Birth province Yamashiro》

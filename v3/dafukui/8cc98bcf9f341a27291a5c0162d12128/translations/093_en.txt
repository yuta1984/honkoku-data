　　　△　Tsukaya
When Lord Yoshishina was at Yoshie, summoned in the 6th year of Kanbun, year of the Fire Horse (1666)
　Tō　Tsukaya Genzaemon Masatsugu (home province: Kaga, birth province: Wakasa, clan unknown)
　　　His grandfather, one called Tsukaya Genzaemon Nobukatsu, served Lord Kyōgoku Wakasa-no-kami, but afterward
　　　was summoned during Lord Tadamasa's reign and served in the hawking department. His son, Tsukaya Yasuemon Nobumasa,
　　　was brought along to Yoshie and served there. His son is this Tsukaya Genzaemon Masatsugu.

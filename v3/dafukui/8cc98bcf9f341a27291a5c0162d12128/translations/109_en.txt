△Takeuchi
Summoned to service during Lord Tsunamasa's reign, in the 8th year of Enpō, year of the Metal Monkey (1680)
Current head: Takeuchi Yogotayū Nobusada 《Original province: Ōmi, Born in: Echizen | Surname unknown》
His grandfather, called Takeuchi Sasanojō, served Lord Kyōgoku Wakasa-no-kami, but when that house's stipend was reduced, he became a rōnin and later died. His son, Takeuchi Sandayū Shigenji, relied on his wife's elder brother, called Kimura Jūbee, who was serving in the domain, and came to this province. Through Jūbee's petition, he was summoned to service during Lord Tadamasa's reign, and later served as an attendant to Lord Nagamatsu.

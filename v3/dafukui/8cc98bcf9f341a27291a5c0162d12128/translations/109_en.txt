△ Takeuchi
Summoned in the era of Lord Tsunamasa, in the 8th year of Enpō, year of the Metal Monkey (1680)
Current: Takeuchi Yogotayū Nobusada <<Original province: Ōmi, Born in: Echizen | Surname: Unknown>>
His grandfather, called Takeuchi Sasanojō, served Lord Kyōgoku Wakasa-no-kami, but when that house's stipend was reduced, he became a masterless samurai (rōnin) and later died. His son, Takeuchi Sandayū Shigenobu, relied on his wife's elder brother, called Kimura Jūbei, who was serving in the lord's house, and came to this province. Through Jūbei's petition, he was summoned in the era of Lord Tadamasa and subsequently served as an attendant to Lord Nagamatsu.

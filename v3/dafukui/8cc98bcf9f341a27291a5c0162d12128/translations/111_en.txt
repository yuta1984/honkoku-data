△ Fukuda
Summoned in the era of Lord Tsunamasa, in the 3rd year of Tenna, year of the Water Boar (1683)
Current: Fukuda Monzaemon Nobuhisa <<Original province and birth province both Sagami | Surname: Unknown>>
His grandfather, a man called Katsuta Yozo, served as a page to Hōjō Ujinao. During the fall of the Hōjō family in the 18th year of Tenshō (1590), he briefly served Lord Kazusanosuke (Matsudaira Tadamasa) and received a sword as a gift, which has been passed down to this day. Later, in the 12th year of Keichō (1607), during Lord Tadamasa's reign, he was summoned at Anezaki in Kazusa Province. His son was Fukuda Monzaemon Nobushige, and his son is the current Monzaemon Nobuhisa.

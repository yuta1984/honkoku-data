△Fukuda
Summoned to service during Lord Tsunamasa's reign, in the 3rd year of Tenna, year of the Water Boar (1683)
Current head: Fukuda Monzaemon Nobuhisa 《Original province and birthplace: both Sagami | Surname unknown》
His grandfather, called Katsuda Yozō, served as a page to Hōjō Ujinao. When the Hōjō family was destroyed in the 18th year of Tenshō (1590), he briefly served Kazusa-no-suke-sama (Yūki Hideyasu) and received a ceremonial sword, which has been passed down to this day. Later, in the 12th year of Keichō (1607), during Lord Tadamasa's reign, he was summoned to service at Anesaki in Kazusa Province. His son was Fukuda Monzaemon Nobushige, and his son is the current Monzaemon Nobuhisa.

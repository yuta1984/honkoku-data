　●Hikosaka Matabei Shigetsune《Note: Home province Mikawa, birth province same｜Family name Minamoto　
　　The ancestor Hikosaka Shuzen Shigemasa served for generations as a hereditary retainer of Gongen-sama (Tokugawa Ieyasu). Shuzen was assigned as a page to Lord Owari Satsuma-no-kami (Matsudaira Tadayoshi) and at the age of fifteen accompanied him to the Battle of Sekigahara, where he also performed military service. After the death of Satsuma-no-kami, he went to serve under Matsudaira Shimotsuke-sama (Matsudaira Tadateru), but after the fall from grace at Aizu, he was summoned by Lord Owari Dainagon (Tokugawa Yoshinao). The descendants of his son Hikosaka Mataemon Shigezumi continue to serve in Owari Province to this day. Matabei Shigetsune was the second son of the aforementioned Mataemon Shigezumi. Through the good offices of Lord Dewa-no-kami Tsunataka, he was summoned to service during the reign of Lord Mitsumichi. Shigetsune's domain succession was granted to his eldest son Asaemon, but due to certain circumstances, the line was discontinued.

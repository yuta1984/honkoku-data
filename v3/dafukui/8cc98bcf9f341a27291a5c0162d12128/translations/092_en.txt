　　　△　Katsumura　Matsunaga
When Lord Yoshishina was at Yoshie, summoned in the 3rd year of Manji, year of the Metal Rat (1660)
　●　Katsumura Gihei Yoshinari (home province: Shimōsa, birth province: Echizen, clan unknown)
　　　His father, Katsumura Mozaemon Yoshishige, came from Yūki following Yamamoto Yoichirō to Echizen,
　　　and thereafter served as a yoriki in Yoichirō's unit
During Lord Yoshikuni's reign, inherited the position in the 2nd year of Shōtoku, year of the Water Dragon (1712)
　Hontō　Katsumura Santazaemon Yoshitoshi (birth province: Echizen)
During Lord Yoshishina's reign, summoned separately in the 2nd year of Jōkyō, year of the Wood Ox (1685)
　Tō　Matsunaga Gōzaemon Yoshinobu (birth province: Echizen, title from maternal side)

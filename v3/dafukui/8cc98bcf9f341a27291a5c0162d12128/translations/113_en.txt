△ Tashiro
Summoned in the era of Lord Yoshishina, in the 7th year of Enpō, year of the Earth Goat (1679)
Current: Tashiro Yōgen Dōson <<Original province: Shimousa, Birth province: Echizen | Surname: Unknown>>
An ancestor who had lived in Utsunomiya in Shimotsuke Province for generations, named Tashiro Ukon, served the Koga Kubō. He still possesses two ink seals from Lord Hareuji. During the Tenshō era (1573-1592), when Utsunomiya Shimotsuke-no-kami fell from power, Ukon went to Edo, shaved his head, became a physician, and took the name Tashiro Yōgen Tsunamichi. At that time, Lord Ōkubo Sagami-no-kami and Lord Doi Ōi-no-kami recommended him before Gongen-sama (Tokugawa Ieyasu), reporting that he was skilled in medicine and had also performed military exploits in the Kantō region. He was immediately summoned as a physician and granted a stipend of 300 koku. Later, during the Osaka campaigns, he distinguished himself before Taitokuin-sama (Tokugawa Hidetada). Subsequently, he became an attendant to Suruga Dainagon-sama (Tokugawa Tadanaga), was ordered to take the tonsure, and was called Tashiro Daizen. Later he received an additional stipend of 200 koku, concurrently served as o-saki-mono-gashira (advance guard leader), and during imperial visits to Kyoto served as an advance rider, steadily advancing in his duties.

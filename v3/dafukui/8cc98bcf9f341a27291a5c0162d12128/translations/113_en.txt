△Tashiro
Summoned to service during Lord Yoshishina's reign, in the 7th year of Enpō, year of the Earth Goat (1679)
Current head: Tashiro Yōgen Dōzon 《Original province: Shimōsa, birthplace: Echizen | Surname unknown》
An ancestor named Tashiro Ukon, who had resided in Utsunomiya, Shimotsuke Province for generations, served the Koga Kubō. He still possesses two ink seals from Lord Hareuji. During the Tenshō period (1573-1592), when Utsunomiya Shimotsuke-no-kami fell from power, Ukon went to Edo, took the tonsure, became a physician, and called himself Tashiro Yōgen Tsunamichi. At that time, Lord Ōkubo Sagami-no-kami and Lord Doi Ōi-no-kami recommended him before Gongen-sama (Tokugawa Ieyasu), saying "He is skilled in medicine and moreover has military achievements in the Kantō region." He was immediately summoned as a court physician and granted a stipend of 300 koku. Later, during the Osaka campaigns, he distinguished himself before Taitorku-in-sama (Tokugawa Hidetada). Subsequently, he served as an attendant to Suruga Dainagon-sama (Tokugawa Tadanaga), was ordered to take the tonsure, and was called Tashiro Daizen. Later he received an additional stipend of 200 koku, served as captain of the advance guard, and performed various duties including serving as an advance rider during imperial visits to Kyoto.

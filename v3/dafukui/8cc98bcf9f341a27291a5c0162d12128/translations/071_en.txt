　　　Aoki Yoemon Yoshiharu (birth province: Echizen, biological father: Kinamera Kinzaemon)
During Lord Tsunamasa's reign, he was summoned separately, but in the 3rd year of Jōkyō (1686) during the new regulations, his provisional position was revoked and he was
treated as equal to the children
In Lord Yoshishina's later reign, in the 3rd year of Genroku, year of the Metal Horse (1690), he was again summoned with special status

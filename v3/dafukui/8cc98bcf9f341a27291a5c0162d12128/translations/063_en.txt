Summoned by Lord Yoshishina at Yoshie in the third year of Keian, year of the Metal Tiger (1650)
　　●Asami Tokuemon Shigetoshi《Original province: Ōmi　Birth province: Echizen｜Surname unknown》　　
　　　　Shigetoshi's great-grandfather Asami Gozaemon Shigetsuna served
　　　　Kyōgoku Takakiyo, the lay priest Kanzan-ji-dono, and later served the Asai family.
　　　　Shigetsuna's eldest son Asami Tōgorō Shigekane, after the fall of the Asai family,
　　　　served the houses of Tanaka Hyōbu-dayū-dono《Yoshimasa》and the same Minbu-dayū-dono《Nagaaki》.
　　　　After those houses were abolished, he moved to Fuchū in Echizen and died as a masterless samurai.
　　　　His son Tsukaya Shirōbee Shigeyoshi changed his title to Tsukaya for certain reasons.
　　　　He was Shigetoshi's father and served from the time of Honda Izu-no-kami Tomimasa
　　　　through the time of the same Kuranosuke Masanaga.
In Lord Yoshishina's reign, in the fourth year of Genroku, year of the Metal Sheep (1691), he was granted hereditary stipend

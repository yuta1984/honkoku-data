　　　　　△　Asami　Matsuyama
　Summoned by Lord Yoshishina in Yoshie in the 3rd year of Keian, year of the Metal Tiger (1650)
　　　●　Asami Tokuemon Shigutoshi (home province: Ōmi, birth province: Echizen, family name unknown)　　
　　　　Shigutoshi's great-grandfather Asami Gozaemon Shigetsuna served
　　　　Kyōgoku Takakiyo, the monk Kanzan-ji-dono,
　　　　and later served the Asai family. Shigetsuna's heir Asami Tōgorō Shigekane, after
　　　　the fall of the Asai family, served the houses of Tanaka Hyōbu-no-taifu-dono (Yoshimasa) and the same Minbu-no-taifu-dono (Nagaaki).
　　　　After those houses were extinguished, he moved to Fuchū in Echizen and died as a masterless samurai. His son
　　　　Tsukaya Shirōbei Shigeyoshi changed his title due to circumstances and came to be called Tsukaya. He was Shigutoshi's father
　　　　and served from the time of Honda Izu-no-kami Tomimasa through the time of the same Kuranosuke Masanaga.
　In Lord Yoshishina's reign, in the 4th year of Genroku, year of the Metal Sheep (1691), he was granted hereditary stipend
　Actual: Asami Tokuemon Shigekyū (birth province: Echizen)

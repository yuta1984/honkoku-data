　　　　　△　Kawamura
In Lord Yoshishina's reign, summoned at Yoshie in the 6th year of Kanbun, year of the Fire Horse (1666)
　●　Kawamura Gozaemon Ujikiyo (home province: Mino, birth province: Echizen, family name: Ariwara)
　　　His ancestor named Kobayashi Tajima resided in
　　　Hondo village, Fukutsuwa-no-uchi, Yasuhachi district, Mino Province. He died in battle during a conflict with Marumo Awa-no-kami, lord of Imao in the same province
　　　His son Kawamura Jizaemon was young at that time, so he went into hiding in the Ōgaki area
　　　Later, in Lord Hideyasu's reign, he was summoned as a yoriko (dependent) of Takaya Chikugo-no-kami. He still
　　　preserves the documents of his land grants and correspondence from that time. Afterwards he went to Echigo and served there. His son
　　　Kawamura Gozaemon was granted succession and served, but due to certain circumstances he left Echigo and withdrew, then
　　　was summoned at Yoshie
In Lord Tsunamasa's reign, granted inherited domain in the 5th year of Enpō, year of the Fire Snake (1677)
　　　Kawamura Gōemon Ujisada (birth province: Echigo)

　　　△　Kawamura
In Lord Yoshishina's reign, summoned at Yoshie in the 6th year of Kanbun, year of the Fire Horse (1666)
　●Kawamura Gozaemon Ujikiyo《Original province: Mino　Birth province: Echizen｜Surname: Ariwara》
　　　The ancestor named Kobayashi Tajima resided in Hondo village within Fukutsuwa-no-uchi, Yahachi district, Mino province
　　　He died in battle during a conflict with Marumo Awa-no-kami, the lord of Imao in the same province
　　　His son Kawamura Jizaemon was young at that time, so he hid in the vicinity of Ōgaki
　　　Later, in Lord Hideyasu's reign, he was summoned as a yoriko (retainer) of Takaya Chikugo-no-kami. Documents regarding his stipend
　　　and letters are still preserved and passed down. Afterward, he went to Echigo and served there. His son
　　　Kawamura Gozaemon was granted succession and served, but due to certain circumstances, he left Echigo and
　　　was summoned at Yoshie
In Lord Tsunamasa's reign, granted succession of territory in the 5th year of Enpō, year of the Fire Snake (1677)
　　　Kawamura Gōemon Ujisada《Birth province: Echigo》

　　　　△　Aoki　　Kinamera
In Lord Yoshishina's time, summoned at Yoshie, but the era name is unknown
　●　Aoki Matazaemon (personal name unknown, home province unknown, birth province: Mutsu, family name unknown, original name: Harishige)
　　　Matazaemon's father, Harishige Daigaku, served under Nagao Kagekatsu, held a domain of 20,000 koku, and as a commander of a hundred cavalry,
　　　was entrusted with an outpost castle. He had numerous military achievements and wore an ornament of Fukurokuju on his helmet
　　　in battle formation, which the enemy camp praised and reportedly said they would permit shooting at with long arrows.
　　　When encamping in the field at a place called Kikyōhara, he had a prophetic dream for certain reasons
　　　and changed his title to Kinamera. His descendants still serve the Uesugi family.
　　　Matazaemon's elder brother, Kinamera Kinzaemon, killed someone and left the Uesugi family,
　　　going to serve Ōta Awa in Echizen. In Lord Tadamasa's reign, he was summoned and appointed
　　　as a personal guard for Lord Nagamasu, but due to his early death, he was later assigned to Lord Yoshishina
　　　and served generation after generation. However, Kinzaemon's grandson Rizaemon was granted leave,
　　　and the main family line was discontinued. Matazaemon left Yonezawa in Ōshū and went to his elder brother Kinzaemon,
　　　from where he was summoned and appointed as chief retainer to Lady Kōshōin (Lord Yoshishina's mother).

　　　　△　Aoki　　Kinameri
In Lord Yoshishina's reign, summoned at Yoshie (year unknown)
　　●　Aoki Matazaemon (personal name unknown)《Original province: unknown　Birth province: Mutsu｜Surname unknown　Original name: Harishige》　　
　　　　Matazaemon's father, Harishige Daigaku, served Nagao Kagekatsu with a domain of 20,000 koku and as a commander of one hundred horsemen,
　　　　entrusted with an outpost castle. He had numerous military achievements and wore Fukurokuju as a helmet
　　　　ornament in his battle dress. It is said that the enemy camp admired this and requested
　　　　permission to shoot at him with long-range arrows. When he made camp at a place called Kikyōhara,
　　　　he had a divine dream for certain reasons and changed his title to Kinameri. His descendants still serve
　　　　the Uesugi family today. Matazaemon's elder brother, Kinameri Kinzaemon, killed a man and left the Uesugi family,
　　　　coming to Ōta Awa in Echizen Province. He was summoned during Lord Tadamasa's reign and appointed as
　　　　guard to Lord Nagamatsu, but due to the lord's early death, he was later assigned to Lord Yoshishina
　　　　and served for generations. However, Kinzaemon's grandson Risaemon was granted leave,
　　　　and the main family line was discontinued. Matazaemon left Yonezawa in Ōshū Province and came to his elder brother Kinza-
　　　　emon, whereupon he was summoned and appointed as chief retainer to Lady Kōshōin《Lord Yoshishina's mother》.

　　　　△　Harada
During Lord Mitsumichi's reign, summoned to service in Kanbun 4, year of Mizunoe-tatsu《Note: possibly mistaken for kinoe》(1664)
　●Harada Shirōemon Sadanaga《Note: Original domain unknown, born in Echizen｜Family name unknown》
　　Sadanaga's grandfather, a man called Harada Shirōbē Tamesada, served
　　Lord Ogasawara Shinano-no-kami. When Shinano-no-kami died in battle at the Siege of Osaka, he distinguished himself in service. Thereafter he moved to this domain (Echizen)
　　and petitioned to serve the house. During Lord Tadamasa's reign, he was summoned and
　　appointed as yoriki (assistant) to Sano Shikibu. His son Harada Asaemon was to
　　be summoned to succeed his father Shirōbē after his death, but Asaemon
　　expressed his desire to petition for direct service (chokusanban) to the lord, no matter how humble the position. Subsequently,
　　during Lord Tadanao's reign, he was summoned as an ashigaru (foot soldier). Thereafter he
　　received promotions.

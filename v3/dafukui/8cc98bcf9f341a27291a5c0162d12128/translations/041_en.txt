　　　　△　Harada
　During the reign of Lord Mitsumichi, in the 4th year of Kanbun, year of the Water Dragon (1664) 《Note: Perhaps mistaken for Wood》, he was summoned to service.
　　●Harada Shirōemon Sadanaga《Note: Home province unknown, Birth province Echizen｜Family name unknown》
　　　　Sadanaga's grandfather, a man called Harada Shirōbē Tamesada, served
　　　　Lord Ogasawara Shinano-no-kami. When Shinano-no-kami died in battle during the Siege of Osaka, he performed meritorious service. Thereafter he came to this province
　　　　and requested to serve the domain. During the reign of Lord Tadamasa, he was summoned and
　　　　appointed as an assistant (yoriki) to Sano Shikibu. His son, Harada Asaemon, was supposed to be summoned to continue
　　　　after his father Shirōbē died, but Asaemon expressed his desire to request direct service (chokusanban) even if it meant
　　　　only light duties. Later, during the reign of Lord Tadanao, he was summoned as an ashigaru foot soldier and subsequently
　　　　received promotions.

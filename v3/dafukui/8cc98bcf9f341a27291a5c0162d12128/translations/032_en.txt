　　　　　△　Nagano
Summoned to service during the reign of Lord Mitsumichi, in the 9th year of Kanbun, year of the Earth Rooster (1669).
　Current generation Nagano Yozaemon Tomomei《Note: Home province Kōzuke｜Family name Ariwara》
　　Ancestor Nagano Shinano-no-kami Yerimasa, his son Nagano Daizen, his son Nagano Yogobei, his son Nagano Yozaemon. From the aforementioned Daizen to Yozaemon, three generations wandered as masterless samurai and resided temporarily in the Kōzuke Province area. During the Siege of Osaka, he joined the forces of Lord Honda Noto-no-kami and died in battle. Therefore, his son called Nagano Jūemon Masatoshi was Tomomei's father. He was summoned by Lord Honda Noto-no-kami, granted a stipend of 350 koku, and served commanding ashigaru footsoldiers. Later he took leave from Lord Noto-no-kami's service and died as a masterless samurai.

In Lord Yoshikuni's reign, in the 1st year of Shōtoku, year of the Metal Ox (1711), granted inherited domain
　Adopted son
　Current　Wanibuchi Saburōbei Yukiaki (birth province: Echizen, biological father: Uchida Iemon, a retainer of Sakai Tange)
　　　The ancestors of his biological father's Uchida family served the Ogasawara family as Uchida Bizen-no-kami, and were entrusted with
　　　a small castle in Iho village in Shinshū. Later they served the Takeda family, and also served the Sanada family.
　　　In Lord Hideyasu's reign, they were among the number of Sanada masterless samurai who were selected and summoned. Iemon
　　　went as an adopted son to the Uchida family, but was actually the younger brother of Yukiyori.

　　　△　Kobori
During the reign of Lord Mitsumichi, in the 2nd year of Jōō, year of the Water Snake (1653), he was summoned to service
　●Kobori Shichiu'emon Hidesada《Note: Home province unknown, Birth province Musashi｜Family name unknown》
　　His ancestor, a man called Kobori Shimotsuke, served as chief retainer (karō) to Lord Yura Shinano-no-kami
　　His son, a man called Kobori Hikosaburō, was the father of Shichiu'emon, and served under Lord Andō Ukyō-no-suke
　　receiving a stipend of 200 koku, but later became a masterless samurai (rōnin) and died

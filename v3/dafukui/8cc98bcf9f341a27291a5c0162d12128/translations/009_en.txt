　　　　△　Iinuma

Summoned when Lord Mitsumichi was heir apparent (year unknown)
　●Iinuma Kanbei Masayoshi (original province unknown, born in Hida | surname unknown)
　　　Masayoshi's grandfather, Iinuma Jūrōemon Masayasu, served the Kanamori family in Hida Province,
　　　but was summoned during Lord Hideyasu's reign. His son Genzaemon and his son Jūrōemon 
　　　served faithfully. This third-generation Jūrōemon was granted retirement, and the main family line 
　　　was discontinued. Masayoshi was among the children of Jūrōemon Masayasu who was sent as an 
　　　adopted child to one called Utsunomiya Hyōbu in Hida Province, and was the third son of one called 
　　　Utsunomiya Shirōzaemon. Though he was Masayasu's grandson, Masayasu had taken him in and 
　　　raised him from childhood, and he came to serve Lord Mitsumichi from when he was heir apparent.

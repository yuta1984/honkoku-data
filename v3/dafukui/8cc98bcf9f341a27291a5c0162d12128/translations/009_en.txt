　　△　Iinuma

Summoned during Lord Mitsumichi's time as heir apparent《year unknown》
　●Iinuma Kanbē Masayoshi《original province unknown, born in Hida｜family name unknown》
　　　Masayoshi's grandfather, Iinuma Jūrōemon Masayasu, served the Kanamori family in Hida Province,
　　　but was summoned during Lord Hideyasu's reign. His son Genzaemon and his son Jūrōemon 
　　　served faithfully. This third-generation Jūrōemon was granted leave, and the main family line 
　　　was extinguished. Masayoshi was one of Jūrōemon Masayasu's children who was sent as an adopted 
　　　son to one called Utsunomiya Hyōbu in Hishū, becoming the third son of one called 
　　　Utsunomiya Shirōzaemon. Therefore, while he was Masayasu's grandson, he had been taken in and 
　　　raised by Masayasu from childhood, and was taken into service from the time when Lord Mitsumichi 
　　　was heir apparent.

In the 4th year of Enpō (1676) during Lord Tsunamasa's reign,
he was granted an inherited stipend

Those summoned during Lord Tsunamasa's reign

△ Seno
Summoned during Lord Tsunamasa's reign in Enpō 9, year of the Metal Rooster (1681)
Current Seno Yozaemon Yoshitsura (original domain: Shimōsa, birth domain: Echizen, family name: Ōe, father: Kawasaki Jirōzaemon Yoshitsugu)

His ancestor Seno Kōzuke-no-suke served the Hōjō family for generations, and to this day [the family] possesses documentary proof of this. Later, due to connections with Lord Yūki Harutomo, when Lord Hideyasu entered Echizen, [Seno Kōzuke-no-suke] accompanied him. Two letters bestowed by Lord Harutomo are still in possession today. Kōzuke-no-suke's eldest son Umanosuke, after Lord Tadanao was deposed, accompanied Princess Takada when she moved to Echigo, and there had two daughters. One daughter married into the household of one Iwata Chōemon of the Owari domain retainers, and the other became the wife of Kawasaki Jirōzaemon Yoshitsugu, father of the current Yozaemon Yoshitsura. As a result, the Seno family name faced extinction, but there was a constant petition to have one of the children continue the family name. Yoshitsura had been in the position of an adopted child of his maternal grandfather since childhood.

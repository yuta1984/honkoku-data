△ Shioya
Summoned in the era of Lord Tsunamasa, in the 6th year of Enpō, year of the Earth Horse (1678):
● Shioya Sadaemon Sadatake <<Original province: Shimotsuke, Born in: Echizen | Surname: Fujiwara, Original name: Utsunomiya>>

Regarding his ancestors, there was a lord of Yamada Castle in Shimotsuke Province called Yamada Shimōsa-no-kami, whose original clan was Utsunomiya, but being a younger son, he adopted another clan name according to family law, as has been handed down. His son Yamada Ukon later changed his name to Gotō Hyōe. During Lord Hideyasu's era, when he was invited and came to Echizen, he went to Honda Izu-no-kami and was ordered to serve in the vanguard. Together with Akami, Tsukuda, Abo, Ichimura, Anzai and others, he went to Fuchū and served as captain of the first unit. In the 17th year of Keichō, year of the Water Rat (1612), during the punishment of Kuse Tajima, he wore the traditional all-black banners and heron-feathered battle surcoat that had been used for generations and served his duty, receiving a gunshot wound. His son Yamada Yagozaemon, later called Shioya Tōbei, served as acting captain because his father Tōbei had been wounded. Later, he served in both years of the Osaka campaigns and took one head near Chausuyama, presenting it [to his lord].

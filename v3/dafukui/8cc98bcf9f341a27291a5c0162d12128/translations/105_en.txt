△ Shioya
Summoned in the era of Lord Tsunamasa, in the 6th year of Enpō, year of the Earth Horse (1678):
● Shioya Sadaemon Sadatake <<Original province: Shimotsuke, Born in: Echizen | Surname: Fujiwara, Original name: Utsunomiya>>

Regarding his ancestors, there was a castle lord of Yamada in Shimotsuke Province called Yamada Shimōsa-no-kami, whose original clan was Utsunomiya, but being a secondary son, he adopted another surname according to family law, as has been passed down. His son Yamada Ukon later changed his name to Gotō Hyōe. During Lord Hideyasu's era, when invited to serve, he came to Echizen Province. He went to Honda Izu-no-kami and was ordered to serve in the advance guard. Together with Akami, Tsukuda, Ambo, Ichimura, Anzai and others, he went to Fuchū and served as captain (banchō) of the first unit. In the 17th year of Keichō, year of the Water Rat (1612), during the punishment of Kuze Tajima, he wore the hereditary all-black insignia banners and heron-feathered battle coats and served in official duties, receiving a gunshot wound. His son, Yamada Yagozaemon, later called Shioya Tōbei, took over the captain's role because his father Tōbei had been wounded. Afterwards, he served in both years of the Osaka campaigns and took one head near Chausuyama, which he presented.

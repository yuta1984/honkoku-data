△　Kurisaki
Summoned during Lord Mitsumichi's reign in Kanbun 6, year of hinoe-uma (1666).
●　Kurisaki Dōki Masakatsu《Note: Home province Higo, birth province Hizen｜Family name known》
His father, called Kurisaki Dōki Masamoto, traveled to the foreign country Tenkawa at age nine, remained there for over thirty years, and after returning to Japan, practiced surgery in Nagasaki in Hizen Province.

Summoned during Lord Yoshishina's reign in Jōkyō 3, year of hinoe-tora (1686).
Adopted son
Current generation: Kurisaki Dōsatsu Keigyū《Note: Birth province Hizen｜Real father Terada Shichirōbei, retainer of Lord Matsuura Hizen-no-kami》
Father Dōki Masakatsu had no natural son, so he petitioned in Enpō 3, year of kinoto-u (1675) to adopt him. However, father Dōki was granted leave during the personnel reshuffling in Jōkyō 3, year of hinoe-tora (1686), and subsequently Dōsatsu was summoned.

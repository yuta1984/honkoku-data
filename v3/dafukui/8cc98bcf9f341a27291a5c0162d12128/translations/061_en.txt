　　　　　△　Kurizaki
During Lord Mitsumichi's reign, summoned in Kanbun 6, year of hinoe-uma (1666).
　●Kurizaki Dōki Masakatsu《Note: Original province Higo, birth province Hizen｜Surname known》
　　　　His father was called Kurizaki Dōki Masamoto, who went to the southern barbarian country of Tengawa at age nine, stayed there for over thirty
　　　　years, and after returning to Japan, lived in Nagasaki in Hizen province and practiced surgery.
During Lord Yoshishina's reign, summoned in Jōkyō 3, year of hinoe-tora (1686).
　Adopted son
　Current　Kurizaki Dōsatsu Yoshiushi《Note: Birth province Hizen｜Biological father Terada Shichirōbei, retainer of Lord Matsuura Hizen-no-kami》
　　　　Father Dōki Masakatsu had no biological son, so in Enpō 3, year of kinoto-u (1675), he petitioned to adopt him as his son.
　　　　However, father Dōki was granted leave during the new appointments in Jōkyō 3, year of hinoe-tora (1686), and subsequently Dō-
　　　　satsu was summoned.

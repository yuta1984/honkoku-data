　　　　△　Takae

　During the reign of Lord Mitsumichi, in the 3rd year of Jōō, year of the Wood Horse (1654), he was summoned to service.
　　●Takae Iemon Nobunaga《Note: Home province unknown, Birth province Echizen｜Family name unknown》
　　　　Nobunaga's father was also called Takae Iemon. His formal name is not known.
　　　　During the reign of Lord Tadamasa, he was summoned to service and served at Anesaki in Sōshū. The matters of
　　　　ancestors before that time have not been transmitted down.

　　　　　△　Katsuyama
Summoned to service in the reign of Lord Mitsumichi, in the first year of Kanbun, Kanoto-Ushi year (1661).
　●　Kimura Shichiuemon Yukitsugu《Note: Both home and birth provinces Harima｜Family name unknown, original names Katsuyama Hayanonosuke and Tōgorō》
　　Yukitsugu's father was named Kimura Zennosuke Yukimasa and served Lord Ikeda Jizaemon.
　　He was indeed a member of Lord Jizaemon's clan. When Yukitsugu was young, he had an uncle named Murai Jinzaemon
　　in the service of Lord Kyōgoku Wakasa-no-kami, so he requested to be raised by him and
　　later came to serve the domain. He had connections with a physician named Katsuyama Tsuian,
　　and through this relationship came to this province, where he was summoned as a page.

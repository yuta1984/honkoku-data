　　　　△　Uchida
When Lord Yoshishina was at Yoshie, summoned in the 1st year of Jōō, year of the Water Dragon (1652)
　●　Uchida Kozaemon Masatoshi (home province and birth province both Echizen, clan unknown)
　　　　His grandfather, a man called Matsudaira Munedayū, served the Asakura house and died in battle. His son,
　　　　a man called Uchida Bunzaemon Masanori, was the father of Kozaemon Masatoshi. After the Asakura house's
　　　　destruction, he died as a rōnin
In the reign of Lord Tsunamasa, summoned in the 2nd year of Tenna, year of the Water Dog (1682)
In the reign of Lord Yoshishina, in the 7th year of Genroku, year of the Wood Dog (1694), he was granted succession
　Current　Uchida Yasuemon Masafumi (birth province: Echizen)

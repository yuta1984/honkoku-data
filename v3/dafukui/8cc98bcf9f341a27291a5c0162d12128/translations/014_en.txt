　　　　△　Kawazu
During Lord Mitsumichi's reign, he was summoned in the 1st year of Meireki (1655), year of the Wood Sheep
　●Kawazu Jūrōemon Sukechika (original province: Izu, birth province: Musashi | surname Fujiwara)
　　　His ancestor possesses the genealogical chart of Kawazu Jirō Sukechika (grandfather of Soga Jūrō and Gorō), a descendant of Ōshikonokami Kamatari
　　　(however, the last two or three generations are missing). Sukechika's father was called Kawazu Kyūsai Sukeyoshi, who was summoned while
　　　serving in a role at Momijiyama in Edo.
During Lord Mitsumichi's reign, he was summoned in the 2nd year of Kanbun (1662), year of the Water Tiger
　　Kawazu Zentayū Takasuke (birth province: Shimotsuke)
　　　In the 1st year of Kanbun (1661), year of the Metal Ox, after his father Sukechika died due to certain circumstances, he was summoned the following year of the Water Tiger and
　　　served. Thereafter, he served at Torigoe after Lord Tsunamasa's illness, and after His Lordship's death,
　　　he was summoned during the later reign of Lord Yoshishina.

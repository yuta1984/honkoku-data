　　　　△　Kawazu
Summoned in the 1st year of Meireki (1655, Year of the Wood Sheep) during Lord Mitsumichi's reign
　●Kawazu Jūrōemon Suketomo《original province: Izu, birthplace: Musashi｜family name: Fujiwara》
　　　His ancestor was a descendant of Ōshikoukan Kamatari, Kawazu Jirō Sukeoya《grandfather of Soga Jūrō and Gorō》, and he possesses
　　　the genealogical records of this lineage《however, the last two or three generations are missing》Suketomo's father was called Kawazu Kyūsai Sukeyoshi, and he
　　　served in an official position at Momijiyama in Edo. Suketomo served under Lord Okudaira Mimasaka-no-kami but due to certain circumstances had to withdraw and
　　　was summoned while residing in Edo.
Summoned in the 2nd year of Kanbun (1662, Year of the Water Tiger) during Lord Mitsumichi's reign
　　Kawazu Zentayū Takasuke《birthplace: Shimotsuke》
　　　In the 1st year of Kanbun (1661, Year of the Metal Ox), after his father Suketomo died due to certain circumstances, he was summoned in the following year (Mizunoe-Tora) and
　　　served. Later, after Lord Tsunamasa's illness, he served at Torigoe, and after His Lordship's death,
　　　he was summoned during the reign following Lord Yoshishina.

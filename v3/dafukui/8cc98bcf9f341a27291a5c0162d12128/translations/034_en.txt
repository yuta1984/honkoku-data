Summoned to service during the reign of Lord Mitsumichi, in the 2nd year of Kanbun, year of the Water Tiger (1662).
　●Saitō Jizaemon Muneharu《Note: Home province unknown, Birth province Echizen｜Family name unknown, Ancestral matters unknown》

During the reign of Lord Yoshishina, in the 1st year of Hōei, year of the Wood Monkey (1704), he was granted succession.
　Adopted son
　　Current generation Saitō Riemon Muneken《Note: Birth province Echizen}}
　　　　His biological father, called Saitō Kihē, was the nephew of his adoptive father Muneharu, whom he had previously adopted as his son. However, when Kihē became sickly, he then adopted Kihē's son, the current Riemon Muneken, as his heir.

　　　　　△　Saitō
Summoned to service during the reign of Lord Mitsumichi, in the 2nd year of Kanbun, year of the Water Tiger (1662).
　●Saitō Jizaemon Muneharu《Note: Home province unknown, Birth province Echizen｜Family name unknown, ancestors unknown》
During the reign of Lord Yoshishina, in the 1st year of Hōei, year of the Wood Monkey (1704), he was granted succession.
Adopted son
　　Current generation Saitō Riemon Muneken《Note: Birth province Echizen》
　　His biological father, called Saitō Kihyōe, was the nephew of his adoptive father Muneharu. 
　　He had initially been made an adopted son, but when he became sickly, his son, the current 
　　Riemon Muneken, was made an adopted son in turn.

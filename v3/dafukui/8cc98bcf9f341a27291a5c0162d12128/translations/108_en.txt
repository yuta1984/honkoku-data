△ Kashio
Summoned in the era of Lord Tsunamasa, in the 5th year of Enpō, year of the Fire Snake (1677)
Current: Kashio Matazaemon Takasada <<Original province: Unknown, Born in: Echizen | Surname: Unknown>>
His father, called Kashio Matazaemon Takatomo, came from Kaga Province to this province when he was young. Later, he was summoned in the era of Lord Mitsumichi. Subsequently, he was appointed as an attendant during the marriage arrangements of Lady Jakkōin and served at the residence of Lord Matsudaira Shinano-no-kami for about twenty years. When he grew old, he petitioned to return to his home province, was granted a retirement stipend, took the tonsure, adopted the Buddhist name Ekyū, and lived thus until his death. Since he came from another province as a child, his ancestry is unknown.

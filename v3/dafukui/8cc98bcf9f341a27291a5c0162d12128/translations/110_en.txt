△Kokubun
Summoned to service during Lord Tsunamasa's reign, in the 4th year of Enpō, year of the Fire Dragon (1676)
Current head: Kokubun San'emon Tadahide 《Original province: Mutsu, Born in: same | Surname unknown》
He was serving under Lord Niwa Sakyō-no-suke, lord of Nihonmatsu Castle, when he was transferred and summoned through Iikawa Hikobee. Regarding his ancestors: his grandfather was called Kokubun Umanojō, and his father was called Kokubun Yogosaemon Tadamoto.

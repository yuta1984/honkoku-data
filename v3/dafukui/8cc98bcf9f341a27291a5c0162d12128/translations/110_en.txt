△ Kokubu
Summoned in the era of Lord Tsunamasa, in the 4th year of Enpō, year of the Fire Dragon (1676)
Current: Kokubu Sanemon Tadahide <<Original province: Mutsu, Born in: same | Surname: Unknown>>
While serving under Lord Niwa Sakyōnosuke, lord of Nihonmatsu Castle, he was granted and summoned through Iikawa Hikobei. Regarding his ancestors, his grandfather was called Kokubu Umanosuke, and his father was called Kokubu Yogozaemon Tadamoto.

　　　　△　Enjō
Summoned to service during the reign of Lord Mitsumichi, but the year is unknown.
　●Enjō Hikozaemon Masanori《Note: Home province Izumi　Birth province unknown｜Family name unknown　Original name Takamatsu Gonnosuke》
　　Masanori's father, called Takamatsu Munemon, served Lord Matsudaira Suō-no-kami, received a stipend of 150 koku, and worked as a treasurer. His eldest son, called Gondayū, inherited his father's headship. Since Masanori was the second son, he desired separate employment and went to Wakayama in Kii Province, where he served as an ashigaru footsoldier under Lord Kii Dainagon. However, having certain thoughts, he took leave, and afterward served Lord Kyōgoku Bitchū-no-kami, but again took leave. Later he came to serve our domain. He was summoned as an ashigaru and served in escort duty and sumo wrestling duties. During this time, an ashigaru named Naoi Seiemon was ordered to carry out execution duties during a punishment, which he performed. Afterward, he was granted a new stipend.

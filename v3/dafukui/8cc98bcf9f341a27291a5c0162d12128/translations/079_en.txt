　　　△　Seo　Akaza
In Lord Yoshishina's time, at Yoshie in the 2nd year of Meireki, year of the Fire Monkey (1656), summoned
　●Seo San'emon Naotsugu (home province unknown, birth province: Echizen, clan name: Fujiwara)
　　　His ancestor is said to be a descendant of Taishokkan Kamatari. During the Bunmei era (1469-1487), a person called Akaza Bingo-no-kami
　　　served Asakura Takakage, and regarding the stewardship of Imajō in Nanjō District of this province, written orders and official documents
　　　still exist today. His son Akaza Bingo-no-kami succeeded him, and official documents of written orders for the stewardship of the same place
　　　granted by Asakura Sadakage still exist today. His son Akaza Bingo-no-kami Yoshitaka succeeded
　　　and resided in the same place, but having no legitimate heir, he adopted Akaza Kyūbei-no-jō Naoyasu from among his
　　　clan. Kyūbei later took the title Akazawa Tosa-no-kami, succeeded to family headship and resided in the same place.
　　　In the 5th year of Keichō (1600), during the Battle of Sekigahara, he went to battle simultaneously with Kuchiki, Ogawa and others, serving our lord's house.
　　　Afterwards he went to Kaga Province and served Maeda Toshitsune, changing his name to Naganohara Tosa-no-kami.
　　　His descendants continue the succession to this day. His son Uozumi Sake-no-jō Naozumi, and his son Uozumi Mago-
　　　zaemon Naotaka were born in Kaga Province. After coming of age, he returned to this province and briefly served under Honda Hi-
　　　zen-no-kami, but due to illness he took leave and took temporary residence in Hirose Village, Nanjō District

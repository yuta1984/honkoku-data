　　　△　Seo　Akaza
In Lord Yoshishina's reign, at Yoshie in the 2nd year of Meireki, year of the Fire Monkey (1656), summoned
　●Seo San'emon Naotsugu《Original province unknown, birth province Echizen｜Family name Fujiwara》
　　　 His ancestors are said to be descendants of Ōshikonokami Kamatari. During the Bunmei era (1469-1487), one called Akaza Bingo no Kami
　　　 served Asakura Takakage, and regarding the position of jitō (estate steward) of Imajō in Nanjō District of this province, the decree documents and sealed orders
　　　 still exist today. His son Akaza Bingo no Kami succeeded him, and the decree document for the jitō position at the same place, along with the sealed order granted by Asakura
　　　 Sadakage, still exists today. His son Akaza Bingo no Kami Yoshitaka succeeded and
　　　 resided at the same place, but having no legitimate heir, he adopted Akaza Kyūbei no jō Naoyasu from among his
　　　 clan. Kyūbei later took the title Akazawa Tosa no Kami, succeeded to family headship, and resided at the same place. In Keichō
　　　 5th year (1600), at the Battle of Sekigahara, he went to war alongside Kuchiki, Ogawa and others, and served our lord's house.
　　　 Later he went to Kaga Province and served Maeda Toshitsune, changing his name to Nagahara Tosa no Kami,
　　　 and it is said his descendants continue the line to this day. His son Uozumi Sakunojō Naozumi, and his son Uozumi Mago
　　　 zaemon Naotaka were born in Kaga Province, and after coming of age returned to this province, serving Honda Hi-
　　　 zennno Kami for a time, but due to ill health took leave and took up residence in Hirose Village, Nanjō District

　　　　　△　Katō
During Lord Mitsumichi's reign, Kanbun 11, year of kanoto-i (1671)
Summoned to service
　●Katō Shinbē Masaharu《Note: Original domain Shinano, born in Echizen｜Biological father Nagasaki Heizaemon Yoshishige》
　　Grandfather Katō Shinbē was summoned to service during Lord Tadamasa's reign at Matsushiro in Shinshū in Genna 4 (1618)
　　and was granted a stipend of 100 koku, but he died. Subsequently, his heir Katō Kan'nosuke
　　also died shortly thereafter, leading to the extinction of the line. Therefore, the second son Katō Kyūzaemon Yoshi
　　tsugu served in a temporary falcon-hunting position under Kagatsume Kai-no-kami, and was summoned during Lord Mitsumichi's
　　reign in Meireki 1, year of kinoto-hitsuji (1655). He was Masaharu's adoptive father.

　●Katō Shinbē Masaharu《Note: Home province Shinano, Birth province Echizen｜Biological father Nagasaki Heizaemon Yoshishige》
　　　Grandfather Katō Shinbē was summoned to service during the reign of Lord Tadamasa at Matsushiro in Shinano Province in the 4th year of Genna (1618)
　　　and was granted a stipend of 100 koku, but later died. After that, his eldest son Katō Kannosuke
　　　also died shortly thereafter, resulting in the line's extinction. Therefore, the second son Katō Kyūzaemon Yoshi-
　　　tsugu, who had been serving as a falconer under Kagatsume Kai-no-kami, was summoned during Lord Mitsumichi's
　　　reign in the 1st year of Meireki, year of the Wood Sheep (1655). He was Masaharu's adoptive father.

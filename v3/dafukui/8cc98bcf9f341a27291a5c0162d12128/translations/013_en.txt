　●Horie Genzaemon Motomasa (original province: Mino, birth province: Echigo | surname Fujiwara, original name Hori, Buddhist name Jōtei)
　　　Motomasa's father was called Hori Yahyōbee. He was a member of Hori Kyūdayū Hidemasa's clan and was
　　　in that household when it was disbanded during the Keichō era, whereupon he became a rōnin and went to Kashū, served Hizen-
　　　no-Kami Toshitada, and died in battle beneath Sanadamaru during the Winter Siege of Osaka.
　　　Regarding the title "Horie," Motomasa's mother was the daughter of one called Horie Saemon Saburō Toshishige from the Asakura family,
　　　so he inherited his maternal grandfather's title.

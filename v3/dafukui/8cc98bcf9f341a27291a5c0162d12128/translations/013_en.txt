　●Horie Genzaemon Sukemasa《original province: Mino, birthplace: Echigo｜family name: Fujiwara, original name: Hori, Buddhist name: Jōtei Gyōmoku》
　　　Hisamasa's father was called Hori Yahei. He was from the Hori Kyūtarō Hidemasa clan and
　　　served in that house, but when that house was abolished during the Keichō period, he became a rōnin and went to Kaga Province,
　　　serving Hizen-no-kami Toshinage. He died in battle below Sanadamaru during the Winter Siege of Osaka.
　　　Regarding the title Horie, Hisamasa's mother was the daughter of Horie Saemon Saburō Toshishige
　　　from the Asakura house, so he inherited his maternal grandfather's title.

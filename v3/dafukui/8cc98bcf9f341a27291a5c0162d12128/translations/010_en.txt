　　came to serve Lord Kiyoyasu in the 1st year of Kyōroku (1528), year of the Rat, and achieved military distinction

　　performed distinguished service before Lord Hirotada, but due to circumstances retreated to Owari Province and served Lord Nobunaga,

　　a letter of appointment from Lord Sakai Saemonnosuke, and the following evening was summoned to the castle

Summoned during Lord Mitsumichi's reign, but the year is unknown
　●Fukuda Hachirōzaemon Yoshihiro《Note: Original province Shimōsa, birth province Musashi｜surname unknown》
　　　It is handed down that an ancestor named Fukuda Jinbei served the Yuki house. His son
　　　was Fukuda Hachirōzaemon, and his younger brother was called Fukuda Shichirōzaemon. This Shichirō-
　　　zaemon was the son-in-law of a man named Kawara Kiemon. This Kiemon served Lord Nabeshima
　　　Tango-no-kami, but during the reign of Lord Taiyūin he was summoned to serve as a direct retainer and falconer.
　　　Since he had no biological son, he petitioned to adopt his son-in-law Fukuda Shichirōzaemon (who at that time
　　　was serving Lord Matsudaira Yamato-no-kami) and he took the name Kawara
　　　Kihei. Hachirōzaemon Yoshihiro was the elder brother of this Kihei, and
　　　was summoned during Lord Mitsumichi's reign.

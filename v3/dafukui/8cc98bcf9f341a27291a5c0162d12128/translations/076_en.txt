　●Suganuma Heibei (personal name unknown)《Original province: Ōmi, birth province: Tango｜Family name: Minamoto》
　　　　Around the Tenshō era (1573-1592), an ancestor named Oka Kozaemon Munemitsu, who in his later years
　　　　called himself Dōmi Nyūdō. In his prime, he served Kampaku Toyotomi Hidetsugu, and afterwards
　　　　performed military duties in various places, with various meritorious deeds, and has preserved records
　　　　that are maintained to this day. His son, called Oka Gonemon,
　　　　served Lord Kyōgoku Tangonokami during the Kan'ei years (1624-1644), and upon inquiry by Tangonokami,
　　　　submitted records of his brief service around the Keichō era (1596-1615), copies of which exist to this day. His son,
　　　　called Oka Jirōzaemon, later took the name Suganuma Heibei and was summoned

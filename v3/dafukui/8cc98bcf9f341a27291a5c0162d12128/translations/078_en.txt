　　　△　Watanabe
In Lord Yoshishina's time, at Yoshie in the 2nd year of Kanbun, year of the Water Tiger (1662),
summoned
　●Watanabe Rokudayū Sadayoshi (home province unknown, birth province: Echizen, clan name unknown)
　　　His father, named Watanabe Goroemon, served Lord Tajima-no-kami, but later became a masterless samurai and left for other
　　　provinces. At that time, Rokudayū was left in the care of relatives during his childhood.
　　　Later, Ko Asami Tokuemon and Tomita Sukeemon both acted as intermediaries, and at age sixteen
　　　he was summoned to Yoshie as a page. As described above, since he was separated from his father in childhood,
　　　the lineage of his ancestors was not passed down
In Lord Yoshikuni's reign, in the 4th year of Shōtoku, year of the Wood Horse (1714), granted family headship
　Current　Watanabe Rokudayū Sadatsugu (birth province: Echizen)

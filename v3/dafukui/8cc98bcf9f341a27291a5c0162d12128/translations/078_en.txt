　　　　△　Watanabe
In Lord Yoshishina's reign, at Yoshie in the 2nd year of Kanbun, year of the Water Tiger (1662),
summoned
　●Watanabe Rokudayū Sadayoshi《Original province unknown, birth province Echizen｜Family name unknown》
　　　 His father, called Watanabe Gorōemon, served Lord Tajima no Kami, but later became a masterless samurai and
　　　 departed for another province. At that time, Rokudayū was very young and was left in the care
　　　 of relatives. There, Ko-Asami Tokuemon and Tomita Sukeemon took care of him, and at age sixteen
　　　 he was summoned to Yoshie as a page. As described above, since he was separated from his father in childhood,
　　　 he did not inherit knowledge about his ancestors
In Lord Yoshikuni's reign, in the 4th year of Shōtoku, year of the Wood Horse (1714), granted family headship
　Current Watanabe Rokudayū Sadatsugu《Birth province: Echizen》

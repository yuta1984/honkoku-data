△ Uesaka
Summoned in the era of Lord Tsunamasa, in the 5th year of Enpō, year of the Fire Snake (1677)
Current: Uesaka Saiemon Yoshichin <<Original province: Unknown, Born in: Echizen | Surname: Unknown>>
His adoptive father, called Uesaka Tahei Katsumoto, was summoned in the era of Lord Mitsumichi. Regarding his ancestors, Uesaka Yozaemon and Genemon served the Asakura family for generations, but after that house fell, they became rōnin and withdrew to live in seclusion in the vicinity of this domain. Saiemon's actual father was called Kojima Heizaemon, a rōnin from the house of Lord Horio Yamashiro-no-kami. Being a cousin of Nara Sakon'emon Takoki, he relied on him for support and came to this province, where he ended his life as a rōnin.

△ Uesaka
Summoned in the era of Lord Tsunamasa, in the 5th year of Enpō, year of the Fire Snake (1677):
Current Uesaka Saiemon Yoshichin <<Original province: Unknown, Born in: Echizen | Surname: Unknown>>
His adoptive father, called Uesaka Tahei Katsumoto, was summoned in the era of Lord Mitsumichi. Regarding his ancestors, they were of the same origin as Uesaka Yozaemon, with Saemon serving the Asakura family for generations. After that house fell, he became a rōnin and withdrew to live in the vicinity of this domain. Saiemon's actual father was called Kojima Heizaemon, who was a rōnin from the house of Lord Horio Yamashiro-no-kami. Being a cousin of Nara Sakon'emon Takamitsu, he relied on him for support in his circumstances, came to this province, and ended his life as a rōnin.

　　　△　Yasumi
During Lord Mitsumichi's reign, summoned in Keian 4, year of kanoto-u (1651)
　●Yasumi Juki Yūken《Note: Original domain Kii, born in Echizen｜Family name unknown》
　　Yūken's father, one Andō [surname unknown], was serving in the Kishū domain household, but due to certain circumstances left Kishū in Kan'ei 5 (1628) and came to this domain, changing his name to Yasumi. His son Yūken was summoned during Lord Mitsumichi's reign and was appointed as a disciple of one Jusen, who was a pupil of an acupuncturist named Jutsu from Suruga. He served in Kyoto for several years, studied acupuncture techniques, returned, and devoted himself to service
During Lord Yoshishina's reign, granted succession in Hōei 5, year of tsuchinoe-ne (1708)
　Current Yasumi Juki Yoshisumi《Note: Born in Echizen｜Biological father is Yasumi Juhaku》
　　His biological father Juhaku was specially summoned as Yūken's adopted son and served faithfully, but due to certain circumstances he passed away, so his son Yoshisumi was made the adopted son of his grandfather Yūken

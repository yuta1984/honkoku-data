Later he became a disciple of a physician called Tashiro Keian Hoin in the Owari lord's retinue, subsequently changed his name to Tashiro Mantetsu, studied medicine, came to this province, and worked as a town physician under the name Tashiro Mansada. His son is Tashiro Mansada Nagasuke.

In the 3rd year of Shotoku, year of the Snake, during Lord Yoshikuni's reign, he was granted hereditary stipend.
Current generation: Tashiro Mansada Kyurei (birthplace: Echizen)

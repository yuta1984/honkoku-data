Later, he became a disciple of a physician called Tashiro Keian Hoin in the Owari lord's service. He later changed his name to Tashiro Mantetsu, studied medicine, came to this province, and practiced as a town physician under the name Tashiro Masada. His son was Tashiro Masada Nagasuke.

In Lord Yoshikuni's reign, in the 3rd year of Shotoku, year of the Snake, he was granted inherited stipend.

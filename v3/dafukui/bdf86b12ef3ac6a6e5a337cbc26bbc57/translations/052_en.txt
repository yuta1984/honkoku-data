△ Ueda

Summoned to service in the fifth year of Kyōhō, kanoe-ne year (1720), during the reign of Lord Yoshikuni.

● Ueda Manjun Harukata (home province unknown, born in Echizen | original surname unknown)
Appointed as attendant to Princess Toyo, he went to Kyoto and served at the residence of Lord Karasuma Saishō, where he died.

In the same reign of Lord Yoshikuni, in the sixth year of Kyōhō, kanoto-ushi year (1721), succession was granted.

Adopted son
Current head: Ueda Manryū Haritsune (born in Echizen | biological father: Sugiyama Kihyōe, retainer of Honda Minbu Shigetake)

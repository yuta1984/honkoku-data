△ Hirai

In Lord Yoshishina's reign, in the 7th year of Genroku, year of the Dog, he was summoned to service.
●Hirai Gensen Hoju (home province unknown, birthplace Echizen, family name unknown)
He was appointed as an attendant to Lady Seijoin and served at the Torigoe residence in Edo. After her death, he served Lady Seishoin and Princess Toyo at this location.

In Lord Yoshikuni's reign, in the 3rd year of Kyoho, year of the Dog, he was granted succession.

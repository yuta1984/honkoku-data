△ Hirai

Summoned in the 7th year of Genroku, year of the Dog, during Lord Yoshishina's reign.
●Hirai Gensen Hoju (home province unknown, birthplace Echizen, family name unknown)
He was appointed as an attendant to Lady Seijoin and served at the Torigoe residence in Edo, but after her death, he served Lady Seisho-in and Princess Toyo in the local domain.

In the 3rd year of Kyoho, year of the Dog, during Lord Yoshikuni's reign, he was granted succession.
Adopted son
Current generation: Hirai Dosen Sanju (birthplace Echizen, actual father: Kitajima Kichiemon)

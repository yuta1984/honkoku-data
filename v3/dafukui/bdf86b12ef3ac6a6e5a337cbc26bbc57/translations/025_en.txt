△ Tashiro

Summoned to service during the reign of Lord Yoshishina in the 6th year of Genroku, year of the Water Rooster
● Tashiro Mansada Chōkyū 《Interlinear note: Both home and birth provinces Echizen | Family name Fujiwara, original name Aoki》
His ancestor, called Aoki Shirōzaemon, was from the clan of Lord Aoki Kii-no-kami and became his retainer, residing in this province. It is handed down that the grounds of Kongōin Temple in Fuchū were Shirōzaemon's residence site. Later, while serving Lord Maeda Toshiie, he got into a fight in Kyoto, killed his opponent, and successfully escaped. At that time, Kimura Nagato-no-kami Shigenari was Aoki Shirōzaemon's nephew, so he went into seclusion at Shigenari's place. When the Osaka campaigns began, he was entrusted with twenty mounted soldiers by Lord Hideyori and distinguished himself in battle. A copy of the commendation letter from that time is still preserved today. After the peace settlement following the Winter Campaign, the Maeda family invited him to return with his former stipend of 1,000 koku, but since the situation at Osaka was still unsettled, he waited to see how things developed. On May 6th of the following year, he finally died in battle.

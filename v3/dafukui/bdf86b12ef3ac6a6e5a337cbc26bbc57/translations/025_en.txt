△ Tashiro

Summoned in the 6th year of Genroku, year of the Rooster, during Lord Yoshishina's reign.
●Tashiro Mansada Nagasuke (home province and birthplace both Echizen, family name Fujiwara, original name Aoki)
His ancestor, called Aoki Shirosaemon, was a member of Lord Aoki Kii-no-kami's clan and became his retainer, residing in this province. It is handed down that the grounds of Kongorin Temple in Fuchu were Shirosaemon's estate. Later, while serving Lord Maeda Toshiie, he got into a fight in Kyoto, killed his opponent, and successfully escaped. At that time, Kimura Nagato-no-kami Shigenari was Aoki Shirosaemon's nephew, so he went into retirement at Shigenari's place. When the Osaka conflict erupted, he received twenty cavalry soldiers from Lord Hideyori and achieved military merit. A copy of the commendation letter from that time is still preserved today. After the winter campaign's peace settlement, the Maeda family invited him to return to service with his former stipend of 1,000 koku, but since the situation at Osaka had not yet settled, he waited. The following year, on May 6th, he finally died in battle.

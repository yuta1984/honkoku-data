● Tsumaki Sōun Masafusa《Interlinear note: Home province unknown, birth province Harima | Family name unknown, common name Tsumaki Den'emon》
　　Sōun's grandfather, Takeda Shōkei Hōin, went to China to study medical arts and received the secret formulas of Gokōen. What is now called Takeda's Gokōen derives from this. After returning to Japan, he was summoned to serve as physician to Gongen-sama. Shōkei's wife was the daughter of Lord Hirotada and the child of Lord Sakurai Yojirō's wife, thus making her Lord Hirotada's granddaughter. Shōkei's eldest son, called Tsumaki Jinzaemon, disliked the medical profession. Since Lord Matsumoto Yamashiro-no-kami was a relative on his mother's side, he went to serve that house and performed the duties of chief retainer. Sōun Masafusa was the second son of the aforementioned Jinzaemon.

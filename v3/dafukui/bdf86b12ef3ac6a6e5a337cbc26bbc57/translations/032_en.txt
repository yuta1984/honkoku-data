△ Tsumaki

During the reign of Lord Yoshishina, in the 4th year of Jōkyō, year of the Fire Rabbit, he was summoned to service
● Tsumaki Sōun Masafusa 《Interlinear note: Home province unknown, birth province Harima | Family name unknown, common name Tsumaki Denemon》
　Sōun's grandfather, Takeda Shōkei Hōin, traveled to China to study medical arts and received transmission of the secret formulas of Gokōkoku. What is now called Takeda's Gokōkoku refers to this. After returning to Japan, he was summoned to serve as physician to Lord Gongen. Shōkei's wife was a child of the wife of Lord Sakurai Yojirō, who was a daughter of Lord Hirotada, making her a grandchild of Hirotada. Shōkei's eldest son, called Tsumaki Jinzaemon, disliked the medical profession and, since Lord Matsumoto Yamashiro-no-kami was a relative on his mother's side, he entered that household and served as chief retainer. Sōun Masafusa was the second son of the aforementioned Jinzaemon.

△ Tsumaki

During the reign of Lord Yoshishina, in the 4th year of Jōkyō, year of the Fire Rabbit, he was summoned to service
● Tsumaki Sōun Masafusa《Interlinear note: Home province unknown, birth province Harima | Family name unknown, secular name Tsumaki Den'emon》
　　Sōun's grandfather Takeda Shōkei Hōin traveled to Tang China to study medical arts and received the secret prescriptions of the Gokōkoku school.
　　This is now called Takeda's Gokōkoku school. After returning to Japan,
　　he was summoned as personal physician to Lord Gongen. Shōkei's wife was the daughter of Lord Hirotada and
　　the child of Lord Sakurai Yojirō's wife, thus she was Lord Hirotada's granddaughter. Shōkei's
　　eldest son, called Tsumaki Jinzaemon, disliked the medical profession, and since Lord Matsumoto Yamashiro-no-kami was
　　a relative on his mother's side, he went to serve that house as a chief retainer. Sōun Masa-
　　fusa was the second son of the aforementioned Jinzaemon.

△ Enami

During the reign of Lord Yoshikuni, in the 3rd year of Kyōhō, year of the Earth Dog (1718)
Summoned to service

The current Enami Geisui 《Original domain unknown, born in Echizen | family name unknown》
His grandfather, called Enami Gensawa, was a masterless samurai (rōnin) who served as a town doctor in this locality. His son,
Enami Jūboku, was blind and served as an acupuncturist, being a disciple of Ami Ko-Jusui.

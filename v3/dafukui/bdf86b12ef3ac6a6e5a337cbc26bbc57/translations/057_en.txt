△ Enami

Summoned to service in the third year of Kyōhō, year of the earth dog (1718), during the reign of Lord Yoshikuni.

The current Enami Geiki 《Home province: unknown, birth province: Echizen, family name: unknown》
His grandfather, called Enami Gensaku, was a rōnin (masterless samurai) who practiced as a town doctor in this locality. His son,
Enami Jumaki, was a blind person who practiced acupuncture medicine and was a disciple of Yasumi Ko Juteki.

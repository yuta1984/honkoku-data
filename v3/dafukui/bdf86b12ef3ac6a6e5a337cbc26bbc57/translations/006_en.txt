△ Inaba [family]

<<Summoned to service in the 16th year of Genroku, year of the Metal Sheep (1703), during Lord Yoshishina's reign>>
● Current head: Inaba Kaito Masaaki <<home province Mino, birth province Sagami, clan name Ochi>>
Regarding ancestors, [information] appears in the biography of grandfather Inaba Sado-no-kami Masanari. Father was Inaba Furuuneme Masanobu.
Younger brother Inaba Iori Masayo <<Buddhist name Motsuji>> initially served as chief retainer to Inaba Mino-no-kami Masanori, but later during the era of Tamba-no-kami <<Masamichi>> became a rōnin and came to Echizen Province, whereupon his son Masaaki was summoned to service.

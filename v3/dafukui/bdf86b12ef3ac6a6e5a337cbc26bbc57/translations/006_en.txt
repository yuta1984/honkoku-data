　　△　Inaba

Summoned during Lord Yoshikuni's reign in Genroku 16, year of the water sheep (1703)

●Present Inaba Kaijin Masaaki (home province: Mino, birth province: Sagami, family name: Ochi)
　　Regarding his ancestors, they appear in the biography of his grandfather Inaba Sado-no-kami Masanari. His father was Inaba Ko-uneme Masanobu.
　　His younger brother Inaba Iori Masayo initially served as chief retainer to Inaba Mino-no-kami Masanori, but later
　　became a masterless samurai during Tamba-no-kami Masamichi's time and came to Echizen, whereupon his son Masaaki was summoned to service.
Summoned at the same time as his elder brother Masaaki
Present　Inaba Genemon Masasuke (birth province: Sagami)

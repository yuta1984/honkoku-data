　　　　△　Inaba

Summoned during the reign of Lord Yoshishina, in Genroku 16, year of the water sheep (1703)

●Current Inaba Yōjin Masaaki 《home province Mino, birth province Sagami, clan name Ochi》
　　His ancestor appears in the biography of his grandfather Inaba Sado no kami Masanari. His father was Inaba Ko-uneme Masanobu.
　　His younger brother Inaba Iori Masayo 《religious name Motsuni》 initially served as chief retainer to Inaba Mino no kami Masanori, and later
　　during the time of Tamba no kami 《Masamichi》 became a masterless samurai and came to Echizen, whereupon his son Masaaki was summoned into service.
Summoned at the same time as his elder brother Masaaki
　Current Inaba Genemon Masasuke 《birth province Sagami》

　　△　Sanada

Summoned in the 4th year of Shōtoku, Year of the Wood Horse (1714) during Lord Yoshikuni's reign
　●Sanada Gentazaemon Yukiaki (home province: Shinano, birth province: Echizen, family name: Shigeno, Buddhist name: Nyūtoku-sai)
　　　Regarding ancient generations, as recorded in the genealogy, his great-great-grandfather Sanada Danjō-chū Yukitaka, who took the religious name Ittoku-sai, served under Takeda Shingen's banner and held approximately 300,000 koku of territory. His second son, Sanada Hyōbu-shōyū Masateru, served under Takeda Katsuyori's banner with approximately 100,000 koku of territory, but was killed in battle at age 26 during the Battle of Nagakute. His son, Sanada Chikara-no-suke Nobumasa, served Kazusa-no-suke-sama, but after that house fell, when Lord Tadamasa was residing in Echigo, he was summoned in the 6th year of Genna, Year of the Metal Monkey (1620), granted 600 koku, and appointed as advance guard when entering Echizen Province. His son, Sanada Gorōbei Yukihiro, inherited his father Chikara-no-suke's position in the 1st year of Jōō, Year of the Water Dragon (1652) during Lord Mitsumichi's reign and was granted 500 koku. However, when Sanada Iga-no-kami's side petitioned Lord Mitsumichi requesting his service, he was sent there, received a stipend of 2,000 koku, and served as chief retainer.

　　　　△ Kurusu

Summoned during Lord Yoshishina's reign in Genroku 4, year of the metal sheep (1691)
　〇Kurusu Hannosuke Sadaaki (home province: unknown, birth province: Echizen, family name: unknown)
    　　His father Kurusu Hannosuke Zen'ei served under Suzuki Tamiya. On the first day of the eighth
　　　month of Shōhō 2 (1645), when Lord Tadamasa passed away, Tamiya committed junshi on the nineteenth of the same month.
　　　At that time, Hannosuke Zen'ei also wished to commit junshi for Tamiya,
　　　but as Tamiya was dying, he petitioned for his younger brother Kogenta's succession. Since he was still young,
　　　Zen'ei abandoned his intention to commit junshi, and Tamiya earnestly requested that he serve as guardian until Kogenta's maturation,
　　　so he did not fulfill his intention. However, in Keian 1, year of the earth rat (1648),
　　　when Kogenta died of illness, he carried out his previous intention and committed tsuibara on August 19th, Tamiya's death anniversary.
　　　It is said that because these matters came to the lord's attention, Sadaaki was summoned.

(Left page)
　　　　△Taketa
Summoned to serve from the time of Lord Yoshikuni's 御部屋住 period
　Current Taketa Uzaemon Masanori (home province: Kai, birthplace: Echizen, surname: Minamoto, original name: Takeda)
　　　In the 4th year of Jōkyō, Hinoto-U year (1687), he was summoned by Lord Masakatsu and became an attendant. Masanori's ancestry is unknown.
　　　His grandfather was Sekido Jidayū (original name: Takeda Gonbei) who served the house of Kaga Dainagon (Maeda Toshiie). His father was called Amano Jinpei (original name: Kawazumi Gonya), and being related to Kawazumi Kakuhei of the current retainer group, he came to Echizen and served under Komako Ise, after which he received leave.

△ Itō

Summoned to service during the reign of Lord Yoshishina in the 8th year of Genroku, year of the Wood Boar
Current: Itō Sakuemon Shigemasa 《Interlinear note: Home province unknown, birth province Echizen | Family name unknown》
It has been passed down that an ancestor named Itō Shichirōbei served the Asakura house as a probationary squad leader. After the fall of the Asakura house, he became a masterless samurai, and generations of the family lived in the rural areas of this province. His father, called Sakuemon Shigenori, served as a minor clerk in the accounting office during the reign of Lord Mitsumichi.

△ Ito

Summoned in the 8th year of Genroku, year of the Boar, during Lord Yoshishina's reign.
Current generation: Ito Sakuemon Shigemasa (home province unknown, birthplace Echizen, family name unknown)
It has been passed down that his ancestor, a man called Ito Shichiroبei, served the Asakura family as a guard captain. After the fall of the Asakura family, he became a masterless samurai, and for generations they resided in the countryside of this province. His father, called Sakuemon Shigetsugu, served as a minor clerk in the accounting office during Lord Mitsumichi's reign.

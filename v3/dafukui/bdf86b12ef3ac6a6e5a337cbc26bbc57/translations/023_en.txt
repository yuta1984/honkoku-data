△ Ito

Summoned in the 8th year of Genroku, year of the Boar, during Lord Yoshishina's reign.
Current generation: Ito Sakuemon Shigemasa (home province unknown, birthplace Echizen, family name unknown)
It has been passed down that his ancestor, called Ito Shichiroubei, served the Asakura family in the role of guard captain. After the fall of the Asakura family, he became a masterless samurai, and generations have resided in the vicinity of this province. His father, called Sakuemon Shigenext, served in a minor role in the accounting office during Lord Mitsumichi's reign.

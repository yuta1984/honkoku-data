　　　△　Ochiai

Summoned in the 6th year of Kyōhō, Kanoto-Ushi year (1721) during Lord Yoshikuni's reign
　Current　Ochiai Rihei Koretaka 《Native province: Owari　Birth province: Echizen｜Family name unknown》
　　　The ancestors of his father Ochiai Shichiemon Yoshishige were from Usaka Honkōji Temple, and it is recorded
　　　in that temple's genealogy that they were descendants of the Chiba clan. Regarding the surname Ochiai, during Lord Hideyasu's reign, the daughter of one
　　　called Ochiai Shōgen was the wife of the late Honkōji, and therefore his father Shichiemon
　　　uses his maternal grandfather's family name.

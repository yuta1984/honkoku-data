△ Motoyama

Summoned to service in the fourth year of Shōtoku, year of the wood horse (1714), during the reign of Lord Yoshikuni.

●Motoyama Isen Teiwa《Home province: unknown, birth province: Echizen, family name: unknown》
　　His father, called Jōeki, was a blind man who practiced acupuncture medicine and had lived in the castle town for many years.
　　Even when Lord Yoshishina fell ill, he was ordered to provide acupuncture treatment and served in this capacity.

In the fifth year of Shōtoku, year of the wood sheep (1715), during the same reign, he was granted succession.
　　Adopted son
　　The current Motoyama Isen Tatsuyoshi《Birth province: Echizen, biological father: Uryu Juteki》

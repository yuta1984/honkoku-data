△ Hatanaka

Summoned in the 15th year of Genroku, year of the Horse, during Lord Yoshishina's reign.
Current generation: Hatanaka Gihei Kagebaku (home province unknown, birthplace Echizen, family name unknown)

His father was a man called Tanaka Kyubei Nagatomi, who was summoned in the 6th year of Enpō during Lord Tsunamasa's reign and served until the 7th year of Genroku, year of the Dog.

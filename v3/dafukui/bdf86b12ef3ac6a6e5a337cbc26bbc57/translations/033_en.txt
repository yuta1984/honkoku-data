△ Tsurusawa

During the reign of Lord Yoshishina, in the 5th year of Hōei, year of the Earth Rat, he was summoned to service
　Current　Tsurusawa Yōtaku Tsunekage《Interlinear note: Home province unknown, birth province Musashi | Family name Fujiwara》
　　His grandfather, called Hasegawa Tahei Masanori, served Lord Matsudaira Izu-no-kami with a stipend of 150 koku plus temporary allowances, serving as caretaker, and later became a rōnin. His son Hasegawa Jinzaemon Masakatsu, after his father Tahei passed away, returned to serve Lord Matsudaira Izu-no-kami and died in service. After his death, the succession should have been given to Yōtaku, but due to illness he declined and became a rōnin. He stayed with his maternal uncle Tsurusawa Tanzan and pursued training in calligraphy and painting. During Lord Yoshishina's reign, he was summoned to service, and thereafter was ordered to become a disciple of Kanō Yōboku.

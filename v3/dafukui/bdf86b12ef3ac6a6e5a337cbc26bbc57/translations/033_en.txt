　　　　　△　Tsurusawa

During the reign of Lord Yoshishina, in the 5th year of Hōei, year of the Earth Rat, he was summoned to service
　Current　Tsurusawa Yōtaku Tsunekage《Interlinear note: Home province unknown, birth province Musashi | Family name Fujiwara》
　　　　His grandfather, called Hasegawa Tahei Masanori, served Lord Matsudaira Izu-no-kami with a stipend
　　　　of 150 koku plus additional role allowances, and after serving as caretaker official, became a rōnin. His son, Hasegawa
　　　　Jinzaemon Masakatsu, after his father Tahei died, returned to serve Lord Matsudaira Izu-no-kami
　　　　and continued in service until his death. After that, it was decided that the succession should be given to Yōtaku, but due to poor
　　　　health he declined and became a rōnin. He stayed with his maternal uncle Tsurusawa Tanzan and pursued studies in calligraphy and painting.
　　　　While living there, he was summoned during Lord Yoshishina's reign, and afterward was ordered
　　　　to become a disciple of Kanō Yōboku.

　　　　　△　Anzai

Summoned in Jōkyō 3, year of the fire tiger (1686) during Lord Yoshishina's reign
　Current Anzai Umeboshi-no-suke Katsu■ (both home province and birth province are Echizen, family name unknown)
　　　His adoptive father, a man called Anzai Mozaemon, was summoned at Yoshie in Kanbun 4, year of the wood dragon (1664)
　　　His biological father was called Okada Jirōemon and was a retainer of Honda Magotarō

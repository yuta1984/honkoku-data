△ Anzai

During the reign of Lord Yoshishina, summoned to service in the 3rd year of Jōkyō, year of the Fire Tiger (1686)
The current Anzai Umeboshi-no-suke Katsu■ 《Marginal note: Both home province and birth province are Echizen | Family name unknown》
His adoptive father, called Anzai Mozaemon, was summoned to service at Yoshie in the 4th year of Kanbun, year of the Wood Dragon (1664)
His biological father was called Okada Jirōemon and was a retainer of Honda Magotarō

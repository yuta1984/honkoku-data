△ Katō

During the reign of Lord Yoshishina, in the 11th year of Genroku, year of the Earth Tiger, he was summoned to service
● Katō Genzō Muneoki 《Interlinear note: Home province Mutsu, birth province Echizen | Family name Fujiwara》
His great-great-grandfather, called Katō Uemonnosuke, moved from Ōshū to this province and resided here. His grandson, called Katō Jinemon, served during the reign of Lord Mitsumichi, but due to illness requested leave, and thereafter went to Kyoto where he became a physician and took the name Katō Seian. Genzō was the son of this Seian.

During the reign of Lord Yoshikuni, in the 5th year of Shōtoku, year of the Wood Sheep, he was granted succession
Current head: Katō Genjun Jutsusen 《Interlinear note: Birth province Echizen | Biological father: Sugita Sakuzaemon Nobuyasu, yoriki to town magistrate Ōmiya Hikouemon》

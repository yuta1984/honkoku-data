△ Kato

Summoned in the 11th year of Genroku, year of the Tiger, during Lord Yoshishina's reign.
●Kato Genzo Muneoki (home province Mutsu, birthplace Echizen, family name Fujiwara)
His great-great-grandfather, called Kato Emon-no-jo, moved from Oshu to this province and resided here. His grandson, called Kato Jinemon, served during Lord Mitsumichi's reign, but due to illness requested leave, then went to Kyoto and became a physician called Kato Seian. Genzo is the son of this Seian.

In the 5th year of Shotoku, year of the Sheep, during Lord Yoshikuni's reign, he was granted succession.
Current generation: Kato Genjun Jutsen (birthplace Echizen, actual father: Sugita Sakuzaemon Nobuyasu, yoriki to town magistrate Omiya Hikoemon)

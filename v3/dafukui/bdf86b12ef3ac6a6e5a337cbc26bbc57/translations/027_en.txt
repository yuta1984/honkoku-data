△ Kato

In Lord Yoshishina's reign, in the 11th year of Genroku, year of the Tiger, he was summoned to service.
●Kato Genzo Muneki (home province Mutsu, birthplace Echizen, family name Fujiwara)
His great-great-grandfather, called Kato Emonjo, moved from Oshu to this province and resided here. His grandson, called Kato Jinemon, served in Lord Mitsumichi's reign, but due to illness he requested leave, and later went to Kyoto to become a physician, calling himself Kato Seian. Genzo was the son of this Seian.

In Lord Yoshikuni's reign, in the 5th year of Shotoku, year of the Sheep, he was granted succession.
Current: Kato Genjun Jutsusen (birthplace Echizen, actual father: Sugita Sakuzaemon Nobuyasu, yoriki under town magistrate Omiya Hikouemon)

　　　　△　Maki

Summoned in the 8th year of Genroku, Kinoto-I year (1695) during Lord Yoshishina's reign
　Current Maki Yojibei Tomohide (home province unknown, birthplace: Ōmi, family name unknown)
　　　Tomohide's father, called Maki Hyōuemon Tomoki, was actually the third son of Kiuchi Jinbei Morimasa, called Kiuchi Heidayū. He was adopted into the family of a certain Maki Hyōuemon in the retainer group of Lord Wakebe Iga-no-kami of Ōmizo in Gōshū and inherited the family headship, but later became ill, took leave, withdrew from Ōmizo, went to Kiuchi Jinbei Sadamori's place, and died as a masterless samurai. Therefore, his son, the current Yojibei Tomohide, was cared for by Kiuchi Jinbei Sadamori from childhood, and was later summoned through Sadamori's petition.

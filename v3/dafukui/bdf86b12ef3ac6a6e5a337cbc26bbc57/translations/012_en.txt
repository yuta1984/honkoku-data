　　　　△　Maki

Summoned in Genroku 8, year of the wood boar (1695) during Lord Yoshishina's reign
　Current Maki Yojibei Tomohide (home province unknown, birth province Ōmi, family name unknown)　
　　　Tomohide's father, called Maki Hyōemon Tomoki, was actually the third son of Kiuchi Jinbei Morimasa,
　　　called Kiuchi Heidayū. He was adopted into the family of one Maki Hyōemon of the household of Lord Wakebe Iga-no-kami
　　　of Ōmizo in Gōshū and inherited the family headship, but later became ill, took leave,
　　　withdrew from Ōmizo, went to Kiuchi Jinbei Sadamori, and died as a rōnin.
　　　Therefore, his son, the current Yojibei Tomohide, was cared for by Kiuchi Jinbei Sadamori from childhood,
　　　and was later summoned at Sadamori's request.

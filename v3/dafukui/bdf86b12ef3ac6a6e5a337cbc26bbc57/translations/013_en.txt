　　　　△　Kondō

Summoned in the 8th year of Genroku, Kinoto-I year (1695) during Lord Yoshishina's reign
　Current Kondō Tasuke Masayoshi (home province unknown, birthplace: Echizen, family name: Fujiwara)
　　　The ancestors are unknown. His father, Kondō Shozaemon Masatsugu, was summoned at Yoshie in the 1st year of Kanbun, Kanoto-Ushi year (1661) during Lord Yoshishina's reign. Masayoshi initially served Lord Abe Bungo-no-kami, but when his father Shozaemon died, he was summoned in Edo.

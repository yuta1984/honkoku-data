　　　　△　Kondō

Summoned in Genroku 8, year of the wood boar (1695) during Lord Yoshishina's reign
　Current Kondō Tasuke Masayoshi (home province unknown, born in Echizen, family name Fujiwara)
　　　The ancestry is unknown. His father, Kondō Shozaemon Masatsugu, was summoned at Yoshie during Lord Yoshishina's reign in Kanbun 1, year of the metal ox (1661). Masayoshi initially served Lord Abe Bungo-no-kami, but when his father Shozaemon died, he was summoned in Edo.

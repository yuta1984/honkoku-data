△ Katsusawa

During the reign of Lord Yoshishina, in the 9th year of Genroku, year of the Fire Rat, he was summoned to service
● Katsusawa Ichimasu Chiun 《Interlinear note: Home province unknown, birth province Echizen | Family name unknown》
As a disciple of Yoshida Koichi Sadamon, he practiced acupuncture and had been residing in the castle town for many years when he was summoned to service. He was appointed as an attendant to Lady Jakko-in and stationed at the residence of Lord Matsudaira Shinano-no-kami. After Lady Jakko-in's passing, he returned to this domain.

During the reign of Lord Yoshikuni, in the 5th year of Shōtoku, year of the Wood Sheep, he was granted succession
Current head: Katsusawa Ichijun Shūjū 《Interlinear note: Birth province Echizen》

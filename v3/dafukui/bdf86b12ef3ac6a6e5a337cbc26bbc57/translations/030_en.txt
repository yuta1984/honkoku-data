△ Katsuzawa

In Lord Yoshishina's reign, in the 9th year of Genroku, year of the Rat, he was summoned to service.
●Katsuzawa Ichimasu Chiun (home province unknown, birthplace Echizen, family name unknown)
As a disciple of Yoshida Koichi Sadamon, he had practiced acupuncture for many years and was residing in the castle town when he was summoned to service and appointed as an attendant to Lady Jakko-in. He served at Lord Matsudaira Shinano-no-kami's residence. After Lady Jakko-in passed away, he returned to this domain.

In Lord Yoshikuni's reign, in the 5th year of Shotoku, year of the Sheep, he was granted succession.
Current: Katsuzawa Ichijun Shugen (birthplace Echizen)

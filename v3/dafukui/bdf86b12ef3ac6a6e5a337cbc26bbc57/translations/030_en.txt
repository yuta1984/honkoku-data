△ Katsuzawa

Summoned in the 9th year of Genroku, year of the Rat, during Lord Yoshishina's reign.
●Katsuzawa Ichieki Chiun (home province unknown, birthplace Echizen, family name unknown)
As a disciple of Yoshida Koichi Sadamon, he practiced acupuncture and had been residing in the castle town for many years when he was summoned and appointed as an attendant to Lady Jakko-in. He was stationed at Lord Matsudaira Shinano-no-kami's residence. After Lady Jakko-in's death, he returned to this locality.

In the 5th year of Shotoku, year of the Sheep, during Lord Yoshikuni's reign, he was granted succession.
Current generation: Katsuzawa Ichijun Shu'in (birthplace Echizen)

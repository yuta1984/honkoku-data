【Right page】
At Katsuura Castle in Kazusa Province, Uemura Tosa-no-kami Yasutada was stationed, and each was ordered to defend their respective castles and repel Kagekatsu's forces.

One item, date unspecified: During this recent disturbance in the Kamigata region, Toramatsu-sama and Oman-sama were staying in the hostage quarters within Osaka Castle along with Okayama-dono. Therefore, Hideyasu-sama sent Toda Rokubei as a messenger from his camp at Oyama to Osaka, dispatching a secret letter to Sakakibara Rokuemon, who was in attendance there. Subsequently, when Toramatsu-sama developed a fever and might contract smallpox, out of consideration for Hideyori-kyō, Oman-sama conveyed through Masuda Uemon-no-jō Nagamori the desire to move to a residence outside the castle.

【Left page】
The aforementioned persons then left the castle. Rokuemon reported this matter to the camp at Oyama.

One item, July 28th: Tagaya Shuridayū Shigetsune, father of Sakon Mitsune, changed his allegiance at his domain in Shimotsuma, Hitachi Province. He conspired with Satake Ukyōdayū Yoshinobu, seeking reinforcements to plan a night attack on the main camp at Oyama. However, when Yoshinobu's reinforcements were delayed, Shuridayū sent one Hanazawa to Mito (Yoshinobu's castle seat) to urge haste. Since this person's father, Gentazaemon, was serving under Sakon at Oyama, he stopped by for a meeting. When Sakon heard of this and questioned Gentazaemon about the details, he replied that he had gone to Mito as a messenger and was on his return journey.

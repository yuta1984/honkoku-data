One: 7th month (date unclear)
The birth of His Lordship's eighth son, Senchiyosama (his mother was of the Shimizu clan)

One: (Date unclear) In recent years, military expenses have been numerous and caused great impediment, so Honda Genshirō Tomimasa was dispatched to Yuki to investigate this matter.
However, Genshirō went to Yuki, consulted with officials, sold bamboo and timber from the mountain forests, and obtained ten pieces of gold. Furthermore, after receiving instructions regarding provincial governance, he hurried back to Fushimi and presented the gold, whereupon His Lordship was greatly pleased and bestowed one piece upon Genshirō.

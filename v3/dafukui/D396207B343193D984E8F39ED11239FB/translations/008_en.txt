Tenshō 10, year of the water-horse (1582)
1. On the 16th day of the 4th month, when Lord Nobunaga returned triumphantly after defeating and destroying Takeda Shirō Katsuyori, he stopped by the castle at Hamamatsu in Tōtōmi Province. On that occasion, he also had an audience with Lord Ogimaru (Yūki Hideyasu), and praised him, saying that his intelligence and ability were exceptional.

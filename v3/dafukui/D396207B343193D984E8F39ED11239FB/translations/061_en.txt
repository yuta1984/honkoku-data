When Lord Hideyasu stood up from his seat and looked sternly around the garden where the people were gathered, everyone prostrated themselves and became quiet.

After the Sacred Lord (Ieyasu) returned, he asked his close attendants, "Today's sumo was an unusual spectacle to behold. Did you all see it?" When everyone offered their evaluations of the sumo match between Oite and Junrei, the Sacred Lord said, "That is not what I meant. I was impressed that all the people in the garden became quiet due to Lord Hideyasu's dignified presence."

• On the 5th day of the intercalary first month, Nakamura Masaemon was summoned to Edo for spear practice training, and Masaemon departed from Fukui today.

• On the 11th day of the same month, regarding the new record book that the Finance Magistrate Kakehi Harima-no-kami had inquired about previously, concerning the drift ashore of Takeuchi Tōzaemon from Houra, the document was sent from our domain, so the Edo representative Ōtani Ichiemon brought and submitted it to Harima-no-kami.

• On the 5th day of the second month, a request was made through Rokugō Uma-no-kami to the duty magistrate Mizuno Izumi-no-kami expressing the desire to invite the senior councilors for celebratory festivities regarding the succession to family headship.

• On the 7th day of the same month, there was a summons from the duty magistrate Mizuno Izumi-no-kami, and when the Edo representative Miyakita Jūrōzaemon attended, he was informed through a written notice that everyone should come on the 18th day of next month. In gratitude for this, messengers were sent to Izumi-no-kami and to the remaining senior councilors.

• On the 18th day of the third month, senior councilors were invited for celebratory festivities regarding the succession to family headship, and a banquet was held.

• On the 22nd day of the same month, similarly, a banquet was held for the members of the main family and the retainer groups.
　　Note: The same occurred on the 26th and 29th days.

• On the 28th day of the same month, 36,500 tsubo of land for official use at Mizuno Hayato-no-shō's upper residence near the Sugamo lower mansion was granted as domain land, and through the transfer by Suzuki Ise-no-kami, the Edo representative Ōtani Ichiemon attended and received it.

• On the 1st day of the fourth month, Unshōji temple made a castle visit to express gratitude for the temple appointment.

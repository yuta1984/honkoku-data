The above acknowledgment letter was submitted through a messenger from the messenger officials.

Item: On August 11th, due to a summons from the duty officer Lord Matsudaira Sakon-shōgen Norimura, the resident retainer went to the castle, where the following written directive regarding horses from the domain was presented:

　　　If horses are produced from within the domain for riding purposes and can serve
　　　as mounts for the household retainers, there may be occasions when they should
　　　be offered as tribute. Therefore, if horses are produced, approximately how many
　　　are produced per year should be reported. Regarding which ones should be offered
　　　as tribute, instructions will be given later.
　　　However, if no horses are produced in the domain, that fact should also be reported.

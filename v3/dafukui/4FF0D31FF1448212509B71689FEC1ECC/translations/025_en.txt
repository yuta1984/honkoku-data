One: On the 7th day of the 12th month, regarding the above matter, courtesy visits were made to the Senior Councilors, personal chamberlains, and junior councilors as expressions of joy.

One: On the 9th day of the same month, the Shogun bestowed the personal name "Ienobu" upon the Middle Counselor.

One: On the 11th day of the same month, upon summons, when Yoshikuni ascended to the castle, in the White Study Hall with the Senior Councilors in attendance, Duty Councilor Inaba Tango-no-kami (Masatsune) announced his appointment as Chamberlain.

One: On the 12th day of the same month, regarding the above matter, accompanied by Yoshikuni, [he] had an audience and performed duties with Duty Councilor Inaba Tango-no-kami (Masatsune).
　　However, audiences and duties with the remaining Senior Councilors were performed subsequently.

One: On the 18th day of the same month, due to the cold season, duties were performed to inquire about the well-being of the Senior Councilors.

One: On the 21st day of the same month, [he] ascended to the castle (wearing court dress) to offer congratulations for the proclamation of the adopted heir.
The Shogun and Middle Counselor appeared, and [he] brought sword documents (one sword and one horse worth 10 ryō of gold) and was granted an audience.
　　However, regarding the sword and horse money presented to the Middle Counselor, the messenger delivered them to the Second Palace.

One: On the 22nd day of the same month, due to the cold season, fresh cod from the domain (first grade) was presented as usual through a messenger.

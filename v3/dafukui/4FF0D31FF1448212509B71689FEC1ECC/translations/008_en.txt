Tomorrow on the 11th at the fourth hour, you shall ascend to the castle and express gratitude for the sankin attendance.
　　The above
　　　4th month, 10th day　　　Inaba Tango-no-kami
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Akimoto Tajima-no-kami
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Ogasawara Sado-no-kami
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Tsuchiya Sagami-no-kami
　　　　　To Matsudaira Hyōbu-dayu

One: On the 11th day of the 4th month, ascended to the castle and expressed gratitude for sankin attendance, presenting one ceremonial sword, 300 pieces of silver, and 200 bundles of cotton. Then Honda Magotarō Nagazane was granted an audience.
After presenting 20 bundles of hōsho paper, a ceremonial sword, silver, and horse money, upon departing made courtesy calls to the senior councillors, chamberlains, and junior councillors.
　However, after the resumption of service, at the time of the aforementioned ceremony, 200 pieces of silver and 100 bundles of cotton were presented, but since the bestowed gifts were increased at the time of last year's leave, after inquiry it was decided that henceforth the presentations would be as stated above. Also regarding retainer audiences, from this time forward it was ordered to follow previous precedent.

One: On the same day, regarding the above matter, white silver and silk crepe were presented as usual to Her Ladyship and Her Highness the First Rank.

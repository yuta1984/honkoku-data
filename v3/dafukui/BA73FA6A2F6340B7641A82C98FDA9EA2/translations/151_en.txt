## Local Famous Products

- **Fukui Candles** (Daikokuya)
- **Tinder/Fire Starter** (Nakamura)
- **Goō-en** (a medicinal pill made from bezoar) (Misaki)
- **Gyokuunken** (a shop or brand name)
- **Hōsho Paper** (Gokka, Ōmushi, Higashimata)
- **Sea Urchin**: Saū-ura, Sugō-ura, Ikura-ura
- **Crab**: Wame-ura
- **Young Wakame Seaweed**: Yasushima-ura
- **Nori (Seaweed)**: Sugō-ura
- **Hamachi (Yellowtail)**: Karakijō-ura
- **Sea Bream**: Tazume-ura
- **Flounder**: Kōno-ura (all in the Upper Coastal area)
- **Salt**: Kuriya-ura

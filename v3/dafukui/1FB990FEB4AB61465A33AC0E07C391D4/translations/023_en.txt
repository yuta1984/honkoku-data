　　　　　　July 14th

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　To Castle Deputy and
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　All Department Officials
○As announced last spring, financial difficulties have been of concern, and while ongoing deliberations continue, this spring's fire at the Edo upper residence has caused excessive additional expenses and distress. Recently announced economizations beginning with escort reductions are as everyone knows. In accordance with this, all departments shall implement cost reductions and economization measures. As this may require reducing even essential services, escort operations and similar matters shall be reduced regardless of previous customs, and further economization proposals shall be submitted. These instructions are to be communicated

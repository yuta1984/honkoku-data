　　　　Matter to be communicated to various departments　　　To the Inspector
○When the wife of a deceased samurai retainer is young, it has been customary to report at the time of death that divorce was declared during illness. However, henceforth there is no need to report divorce, and later, when relatives consult and the natal family wishes to arrange remarriage, this is acceptable. Even if she has once cut her hair and years have passed, this is acceptable.

2nd Year of Bunka, Year of the Ox (1805)

In Edo　　　　　　　　　　　　Chief Retainer
○Regarding the title of御聞番 (go-kikiban): when there are position changes, they shall be called御聞番 as before, but in ordinary circumstances they shall be called御留守居 (go-rusuii). This matter should be communicated to Daidōji Shichiemon and Kanie Zenemon. Each person should communicate this matter to appropriate parties as necessary.
　　　　　　　　January 4th

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Construction Magistrate
○At the construction office, regarding various recent expenses, after investigation this time, the annual budget amount from this year of the Ox has been determined as shown in the attached document, and a lump-sum allocation has been ordered to the construction office. Using this allocated budget, they are ordered to undertake repairs without delay. However, if there are unexpected major damages or new projects, the magistrates should consult with each other at each occasion. This matter should also be thoroughly communicated to subordinate officials, and they should be instructed to handle matters so as to avoid any unprofitable outcomes. Additionally, further instructions have been given as shown in the attached document, so they should conduct themselves accordingly with this understanding.
　　　　　　　　Attached Document
1. Gold: 416 ryō, 2 bu, 8 monme, 5 fun, 5 rin

April 24th

To Monthly Duty Officers
○Regarding the financial difficulties of all retainers: a small amount of silver loans shall be granted. Further details will be communicated from the accounting office, and this matter should be announced accordingly.

June 5th
However, regarding the loan allocation: following the precedent of the Bunka 9 (1812) loans, silver shall be lent at a rate of 100 momme per 100 koku of rice stipend.

To the Magistrate
○Regarding horses used by salt sellers from various regions to transport salt to Ōno territory: while there have been established practices since ancient times, recently there appear to be changes from old customs in Ōno domain. Until this matter is clarified, salt shall not be loaded on return horses from Ōno territory, but shall be transported through by coastal pack horses. Moreover, since certified horses have been designated, salt shall not be distributed to those without horse certificates.

These instructions shall be communicated to salt shops in the castle town.

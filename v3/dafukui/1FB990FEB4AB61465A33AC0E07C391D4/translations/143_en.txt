To be entered in October　　　　　　Addendum
○Regarding temple officials and local households providing meal preparation services when the lord goes out for falconry, river hunting, and all field inspections: After special deliberation, from now on, one bale of rice shall be given to the meal preparation location and two bales to the village, totaling three bales. Furthermore, at said meal preparation locations, only soup and tea shall be served as before. However, when vegetables, radishes, etc. are available, prepared dishes including tofu must not be used.

1. Regarding alcoholic beverages with appetizers, simmered dishes, etc.: From now on, presenting such items is strictly forbidden. If anyone presents them due to misunderstanding, even slightly, they will be reprimanded by their supervisors.
　Note: The same applies to alcoholic beverages, appetizers, and simmered dishes during preliminary inspections before the lord's visits.

1. Since the intention is to lighten the burden on those serving at meal preparation locations, all customary practices should be simplified as much as possible.

　　　　　　　October 8th
As the above was announced this time, Utsunomiya Kageyu conveyed that the foot inspectors should be informed to keep this in mind, and accordingly, instructions were given to the foot inspectors and group leaders so that their subordinates would also keep this in mind.

To the Administrative Head
○Previously, Nishi Honganji Temple made contact through Mori Jiuemon, and the officials also gave their consent. After uniformly receiving the seal documents from both clergy and lay followers, they plan to report to Edo. Honganji Temple has requested that everyone here submit their seal documents, and this has been communicated through Jiuemon. Therefore, each temple and one or two lay follower representatives should submit their seal documents at their respective offices. The supervising temple officials must be present on that occasion. Since we have been causing you trouble since before, you should instruct them to submit their seal documents promptly without any misunderstanding.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　To the Administrative Head
○Due to the communication from Nishi Honganji Temple previously, at that time clergy and lay followers under the temples were summoned to each office and given instructions. They submitted written pledges with seals to Honganji Temple stating they would not disobey in the slightest when attending temple duties, continuing judicial documents, and when teaching monks and rotating officials are dispatched. Based on this, when Honganji Temple requested withdrawal of the investigation, it was granted by the magistrate's office. This is due to the special efforts made since before, and we must never forget the domain's benevolence.

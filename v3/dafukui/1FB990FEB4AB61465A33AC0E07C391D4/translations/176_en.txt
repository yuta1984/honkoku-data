　Memorandum of instructions from the middle elder　　　　　　To the teaching staff
○Recently, the conduct at the training halls has been poor, and it has been reported that during training completion ceremonies and such occasions, disciples have engaged in improper behavior while intoxicated. Since such misconduct has been heard of, it is ordered that proper etiquette be maintained and unlawful acts avoided, and the instructors should also be mindful of this and strictly correct their attitudes.
　However, all matters should be conducted frugally without expense, and even when disciples gather, there should be no extravagant behavior.
　The matter of promoting and working diligently at the training halls has been announced repeatedly. This time, with special consideration, stipends will be provided. As detailed in the separate document, these are granted from the lord's personal funds, so you should be especially mindful of this consideration.
1. 100 monme of silver each to teaching staff; 60 monme each to academic arts, medical school, Ihara, Akashi
　30 monme each to horse attendants　However, individual names are omitted

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Miya Jiemon
○The unemployed residence guards shall henceforth be organized into two groups. This should be understood and handled accordingly. Also, the supervising heads should be informed as detailed in the separate document.
1. Although unemployed residence guard duty shifts have now been established, all handling of petitions and other matters shall continue to be processed by the monthly duty officers as before.
1. All petitions and communications from members of the unemployed residence guard groups shall be submitted to the monthly duty supervisors through their respective shift caretakers.
　However, when a shift caretaker is unavailable, the caretaker from the alternate shift shall handle the matter.
1. For New Year and other ceremonial occasions, the first group and residence guard groups one and two shall attend in sequential order.

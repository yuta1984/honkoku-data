  Foot Soldier Supervisor
○When foot soldiers request leave due to illness and petition for their sons to be summoned as replacements, such requests have been immediately granted as petitioned, but henceforth such matters will be decided after investigation. If the petitioner dies of illness after submitting the request, funeral arrangements may be handled at their discretion.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Town Magistrate
○Since Kanaya Yasuke is in financial difficulty, he petitioned to quarry and sell stones from the stone mine within his granted mountain, in the same manner as retainer household mines. Due to special compassion, this was permitted for ten years from the previous Year of the Boar until this year. Since his financial situation continues to be extremely difficult, he petitioned for this to be permitted for one more year from next year in the same manner as before, but this matter was difficult to approve, so it was permitted for another ten years from next year in the same manner as before
　　　However, while previously ten assistant workers and ten child stone craftsmen were permitted to be employed at this mine and exemption certificates were granted, he now petitions for permission to freely employ child stone craftsmen in the same manner as Ishizaka town mine holders. This matter is permitted at his discretion. However, if there are any problems, operations may be suspended

○Since the above has been ordered, the document regarding sake brewing control passed from the Magistrate to the supervisory heads is as follows:
One　Since administrative policies regarding rural sake brewing have now been established, sake prices should henceforth be handled at the lowest possible prices in comparison with each year's rice prices, and this should be understood
One　Regarding retail guild commerce, buying and selling may be conducted at discretion
One　Sales agents shall not be established for groups outside one's own district association
One　Since sake brewing stock licenses have now been issued, henceforth no sake brewing applications will be accepted
One　When main stockholders cease sake brewing, while they could previously transfer stocks anywhere in the domain at their discretion, henceforth such transfers must be arranged without hindrance after consultation with the district association supervisors and general controllers
　　However, when new stockholders cease operations, the same consultation process applies
One　When both main stocks and new stocks are discontinued, until it is determined whether a successor stockholder will be established or whether stocks will be held in trust by the association, tribute payments must continue from the original stock
One　As tribute for sake brewing stock licenses, two bu of gold shall be paid annually
One　Annual magistrates shall be established within each district association and serve in yearly rotation
One　Those who have applied for sake brewing but have not yet begun brewing, as well as those with dormant or discontinued stocks, shall henceforth be suspended

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Magistrate　Also to Fuchu
○Regarding indigo balls used in towns and villages, due to recent high procurement costs, all dyers are experiencing difficulties and dyeing prices have become expensive. Therefore, by this policy, indigo balls arriving at port will be purchased directly from Awa Province wholesalers and distributed in Fukui as ordered, appointing purchasing supervisors and town/village sales annual administrators
一、The indigo balls ordered for distribution shall bear special brand marks, be of uniform quality regardless of grade (high, medium, low), and be sold at low prices, so they must be handled properly
　　However, direct transactions with other provinces to receive indigo balls are strictly forbidden. If anyone handles goods without brand marks, annual administrators must investigate and report
一、Port fees at Mikuni Port should be submitted as tribute according to regulations
　　However, for indigo ball shipments bound for other territories, upon arrival they must be examined, silver amounts investigated, and three monme per hundred monme collected as service silver
一、Payment silver for indigo balls should be submitted twice yearly, in July and December
　　However, the three annual administrators responsible for silver collection must ensure proper submission
一、Dyers should investigate their current indigo ball inventory and report in writing how much will be needed when ships arrive next spring
　　However, monthly indigo ball usage in towns and villages should be investigated in detail
　Since distribution within the domain is ordered by this policy, direct shipment goods from other provinces shall not be handled at all

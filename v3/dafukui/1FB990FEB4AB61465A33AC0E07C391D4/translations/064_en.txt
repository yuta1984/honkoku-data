   　　　Regarding punishment for those who return
1. For those banished from the castle town to a seven-ri radius who are again banished from within the domain, if there are no issues when the matter is submitted, it may be decided immediately with only a single consultation session as submitted.
1. For those banished from a ten-ri radius, when submitting a request for thirteen-ri banishment within the domain, since they have been banished once before, if there are no issues, it may be decided immediately with only a single consultation session as submitted.
1. When submitting a request for tattooing and banishment of those already banished from the domain, if there are no issues, same as the previous article.
1. If those who received tattooing and banishment return, they shall be subject to the death penalty.
1. For those who repeatedly returned for minor offenses and finally received domain banishment, if they are extremely foolish or returned due to illness and became beggars with no other wrongdoing, after special consultation, tattooing may be waived and domain banishment may be imposed again.

1. When those with light banishment become seriously ill, if relatives and village or town officials request exemption, and the district head submits this after investigation, exemption shall be handled immediately. If they recover, they shall be banished again; if they die of illness, this shall be reported then. The inspectors should be informed of this matter.
   However, if they do not recover after even a month, group members shall be sent to investigate, and thereafter every ten days the local officials shall visit and report on the patient's condition.
   Addendum: When requesting to meet parents due to illness, entry and stay shall be permitted for a limit of seven days.

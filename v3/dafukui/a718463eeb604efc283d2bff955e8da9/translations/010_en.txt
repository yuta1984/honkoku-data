Regarding those who hold horses for official duty from a basic stipend of 375 koku or more, due to the additional rice levy, horse-keeping is exempted for the current year within the five-year period. However, in years when one does not serve guard duty, mounted service shall be excluded, so one may serve with assistance from colleagues. Furthermore, regarding years of horse exemption, the inspectors (metsuke) will provide instructions. However, when the months of one year of horse exemption for both basic stipend and duty allowance horses are completed, horses should be kept immediately. At that time, one must consult with the monthly duty inspectors. Moreover, regarding detailed matters, the inspectors will communicate them.

To Lady Teisho-in: Military register, one copy (as promised directly yesterday when visiting her residence, sent with accompanying letter to Tsuda Shirōzaemon)

1. December 1st - After 6:30, retinue assembled, proceeding from the formal chamber's veranda through the mountain village path to Unshōji Temple. Paid respects at Lady Itokuin's memorial altar and mausoleum, returning to the palace just before 4 o'clock. Received formal greetings from all on this day. Lord Kai-gorō also assembled his retinue at 5:30 for pilgrimage to Unshōji Temple, and upon returning to his residence, had the customary audience.

1. December 2nd - Hawking expedition held, and upon returning to the palace, entered the formal chamber to view the presented ducks.
　　To Princess Koto: Letter
　　　　　　　True ducks, two - purchased
　The above to be sent tonight together with the presented birds, with the letter carried by the supervisor.

1. December 5th - Courier arrived
　　From Princess: Confections in presentation box
　　From Lord Matsudaira Iyo-no-kami: Jellyfish, one barrel - return gift from earlier
　　　　　　　　　　　Reply letter

1. This morning, the monthly duty officer at the Council Chamber was summoned. When Kobei came off night duty, he was handed an official document stating that although the seven-year installment plan's term doesn't expire until next year of the Sheep, exemption will be granted from next year, and furthermore, year-end allowances will be provided to all retainers down to the lowest ranks. This was announced by Matsudaira Shume's office. For the formal thanks, only Kobei alone attended from the scribes' office and duty officers.

The needle physicians and members of the close attendant guard took their seats in the Peacock Room and offered their standing formal respects.

1. Around 7 o'clock, due to thunder and heavy rain, through close attendant guard Yamada Tōbei, he inquired about Lady Teishōin's well-being.

1. On the 2nd day of the 6th month, there was a lecture in the audience chamber, with Takano Sōzaemon Daigaku presenting. Since this was the opening lecture today, by his gracious will he donned formal dress. Accordingly, the accompanying members, steward and side attendants completed donning formal dress, and during Ihara Genbei's lecture he changed to formal hakama, so the side attendants wore plain clothes. However, both lecturers presented from within the same room inside the threshold.

1. Today during the castle town rounds, during a brief rest, 100 pieces of gold were privately given to Tsubota Izumi, chief priest of Matsumoto Tennō Shrine.

11th day
1. Duck from falconry given to Koma Takumi's group and sent to the secretarial office

1. 12th day of 12th month: Akashi Bungoro gave his first lecture on military studies from "Yuken Taisho San no Shuhei." Since this was Bungoro's first time, only he wore hemp court dress.

1. 13th day of 12th month: Went falconing at Suehiro-suji area, departing the palace at exactly 5 o'clock, returning at 5 o'clock and 5 steps before. However, since tomorrow the 14th is Junan-in-sama's memorial day, vegetarian meals begin from the third meal. Due to the year-end cleaning celebration, only ceremonial celebration was observed at the third formal meal.

1. Today for year-end cleaning, the "year man" Yoshida Denemon had audience in the third room of the small sitting room as usual. When awakening and entering the small sitting room, the sliding doors of the second room were opened to left and right.

1. 14th day of 12th month: The senior councilor had business, and today his lordship suffered slight cold and headache, so he rested for recuperation, receiving the audience while still in bed.
[Red ink] One duck each from falconry to Nagami Tonomo and Amagata Magohachi

1. 15th day of 12th month: Before receiving the day's ceremonies, he took his seat in the audience chamber. Messengers bearing congratulations for the announcement of the time for attending court were received from various lords, with chamberlains and Tsuda Shirozaemon handling the proceedings.

1. The tutor, senior councilors, chamberlains, and chief attendants (duty rotation only), pages (duty rotation only) offered congratulations for the announcement of the court attendance time. The chamberlain handled this, and it was received while his lordship was resting in bed wearing plain clothes.

1. Kengoro-sama entered and offered congratulations for the day's ceremonies and the announcement of court attendance time, with the usual face-to-face meeting.

1. Items presented by Princess-sama for celebrating the moving day of November 29th - sake, soup, and delicacies - were celebrated during today's second meal. Similar items from Teisho-in-sama for celebrating the move were celebrated in the evening. Both celebrations were conducted while his lordship remained in bed due to slight indisposition.

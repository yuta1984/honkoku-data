Item: July 21st, after 8 o'clock, with escort assembled for a tour of the castle town, rested briefly at Sakai Geki's nagaya lower residence. Entered through the main entrance, and after a while entered the pavilion in the garden, then entered the original tea house, viewing the entire hanging garden.
　The escort and advance guard all served in the garden. Viewing the flourishing flowers and trees, poetry composition was ordered, having arrived ahead.
　The arrangements for today were communicated by the administrator yesterday.
　　Mixed delicacies, one tray　　　To Sakai Geki
　The above was given for today's visit. Meal attendant Moro Kyūzaemon served as messenger from among the escort.
　　3 shō of sake, 2 strings of dried squid　　　To his retainers
　The above was given similarly.
　　Quail eggs, one box of 200　　　　　　　Same person
　The above was presented similarly through a messenger. The administrator announced it.
　　Red rice, simmered dishes　　　　　　　From same person
　The above was a feast for the escort and advance guard.

Item: July 22nd, yesterday the chamberlain's head was to finish his duty shift, but since Kohyōe came out for escort duty, today's duty is Kohyōe's.
　By special dispensation, ordered to withdraw after 9 o'clock.

Item: July 25th, castle town tour (escort assembled after 7 o'clock), brief rest at Sakura Baba horse viewing stand.

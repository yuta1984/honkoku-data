1. 8th day of 10th month　Until now, fresh confections were ordered for distant pilgrimages, field excursions, castle town rounds, moxa treatment, etc., but henceforth preparation is not necessary. For moxa treatment, roasted confections including first offerings will continue as before. However, for Daian-ji and Tenryu-ji temples, orders will continue as before. For the two moon viewings, year-end parties, departures, celebrations, etc., preparation is also not necessary as ordered, and this was communicated to meal attendant Iwaki Shozaburo

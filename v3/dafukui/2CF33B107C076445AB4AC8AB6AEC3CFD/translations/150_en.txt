Item: September 10th - A courier arrived and the following was presented:
　　From Himegimi-sama: One inkstone box - received when attending Nishimaru castle, it is said

Item: September 13th - Regarding the bestowal matter, in coordination, at five o'clock tonight a courier departed
　　To Himegimi-sama: A letter
　　　　　　　Garden chestnuts: One box of several hundred - presented via the inner palace

Item: Tonight as usual for moon viewing, those not attending the mid-autumn [festival] were summoned. Finished before five-thirty. Takano Sōzaemon was ordered to compose poetry, Tsumaki Keisai to compose waka. However, from the attendant chamberlain to the tea master, the facing seats were for the page chief and pages.

Item: September 14th
　　To Kairōgorō-sama: Ten matsutake mushrooms
　　To Teishōin-sama: Eleven of the same - were presented and sent around to Shinroku Shiemon's household

Item: September 15th - After thanks for reporting for duty, audience in the sitting room: Honda Kuranosuke
　　　　　　　One basket of matsutake: Seventeen pieces - To the same person
　The above was from what Daianji temple presented, given by his gracious will, and presented to the secretary's quarters

Item: September 16th - After eight o'clock, assembled for kemari ball game, Honda Kuranosuke's household and Okabe Sazen's household (when previously viewed, due to illness withdrawal) were ordered to observe, and sweets and tea were bestowed. Everything as in the year before last. However, Sazen's household received the same treatment. For sweets, Kuranosuke's household received fifteen, Sazen's household received eleven, with details recorded by the meal attendant. Both attendant chamberlains, the monthly duty chamberlain, and duty chamberlain attended.
　　Sweet money: One hundred mon - Honda Kuranosuke's household
　The above was presented and announced by the chamberlain

Item: Matsutake mushrooms successively presented from both Tateyama mountains, as every year, to:
　　Elder councilors, attendant chamberlains, chamberlains
　　Attendant chiefs, ceremony chamberlains, attendant heads
　　Physicians, retired physicians, pages
　　Close attendant guards, tea masters, inner palace
　In addition to the above, to those presenting mukago bulbils and such, also bestowed and sent around for inner palace dried food duties, etc. Recorded by the meal attendant.
　　Only the attendant chiefs attached to Young Lord and Kairōgorō-sama
　These portions were also bestowed. Not bestowed to officials and Confucian scholars.

Item: September 18th - At five o'clock the attendants assembled, made pilgrimage to Daianji temple, entered the study from the entrance, ordered worship, and noshi [document ends mid-sentence]

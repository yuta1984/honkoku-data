Item: October 7th - One protective amulet and one basket of palace persimmons offered by Ōmori shrine priest
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Hirobe Kunaida-no-suke
　　　　　　First fruit offering of 100 hiki in gold presented privately

Item: October 8th - At five o'clock, assembled escorts and made pilgrimage to Awaga Kasuga Shrine, stopping at Yoshida Toichirō's residence. Ordered escort assembly, changed into noshime-han formal wear, rode in palanquin, dismounted below the torii gate, performed twelve bows at Kasuga Shrine's main hall, Taki Hall and all other distant worship sites, reboarded the palanquin at the original location, stopped again at Toichirō's residence and took a meal. After completion, ordered escort assembly, then entered Shōun-in temple within the wooden gate. Wearing the same attire as initially, completed worship, ordered escort assembly at the appropriate time and returned to palace at 6:30 and 2.5 bu. Wore hakama trousers throughout the journey there and back. For the route both ways to Kasuga, Kobei served as advance guard.

Item: Tonight at six o'clock assembled for the Boar Day celebration. After returning to the palace, changed into fukusa formal wear and immediately entered the inner palace. After completing the inner palace celebration, partook of the triple meal service. During the meal, ordered the outer assembly, and immediately after the meal moved to the Tartar Room. The meal attendants presented noshi and rice cakes, and rice cakes were bestowed as usual to the chamberlain and all assembled retainers.

Item: Kaigorō-sama was also to enter, but due to heavy rain, was advised not to enter.

Item: Since it was the day after being on duty as side attendant, made inquiry, and Shin'emon attended from off-duty status.

Item: October 10th - Fish money offering of 100 hiki　　　Nakamura Hachidayū
　This was offered privately due to his recent honorable appointment.
　　New buckwheat　One box　　　　Jōhōji Village, Sonotsugi
　This person had requested permission to develop new rice fields this year, and when planted in that location, it grew well, so he requested to offer first fruits. The magistrate reported this and it was brought to the kitchen for inspection. This person was formerly employed in the meal preparation service and was a pardoned individual due to this connection.

Item: October 12th - At 5:30, assembled escorts and made pilgrimage to Matsuoka Tenryū-ji temple. Travel was via the front entrance. At Tenryū-ji, first entered the rest area from the veranda side, remained briefly, then ordered worship and changed into noshime-han formal wear. Worshipped the image of Lord Kenshō-in, then immediately performed one bow to the memorial tablet at that incense burner. There were hand purifications before and after. Then entered the mausoleum area and worshipped Lord Kenshō-in's mausoleum, then worshipped Lord Jissō-in's mausoleum, then returned via the original path to the rest area and took seat.

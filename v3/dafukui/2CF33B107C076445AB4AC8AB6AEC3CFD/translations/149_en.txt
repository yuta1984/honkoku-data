To Kairōgorō-sama: One mallard duck - directly presented during this morning's meeting

Item: September 6th - Before five o'clock the attendants assembled, entered Urushifuchi, boarded ship at Ofunamachi, after ship provisions, went up the Hinogawa River by boat, landed at Asamiya, returned to the palace at six-thirty, two sun five bu. On the ship, gifts were bestowed before his presence starting with the attendant chamberlain, and by his gracious will, clothing was ordered to be worn at discretion, so informal dress with field haori was worn. Hanaki Ei was brought along and went together with the advance guard; after landing at Asamiya, returned at will from behind his lordship. The catch was as follows:

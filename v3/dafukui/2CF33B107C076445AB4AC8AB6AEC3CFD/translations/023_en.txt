One　On August 19th at 5 o'clock, with attendants assembled, his lordship went to observe the Bando Island weirs under the pretext of river hunting. The attendant was Kobei. Rest stops were at Fujishima Chokatsuki Temple, the second meal location was at Matsukoi Kodama Ichemon's, rest stops at Komyoji Village Heizaemon's and Sanno Village Mozaemon's. From there he entered the Bando Island weirs for observation, and after sunset took lodging at Shimizu Village Kichibei's residence.

One　On August 20th this morning at 6:30, with attendants assembled, he again entered the weirs for observation until about 9:30. He remained at the weirs, then observed Wakaba Pool and Kamaga Pool, after which he entered the highway and followed the regular route with rest stops at Komyoji Village Heizaemon's, second meal location at Matsukoi Nonakaya Chobei's, rest stop at Fujishima Rear Chokatsuki Temple. He returned to the castle before 5 PM.

(Vermillion insertion: During the weir observation, Lord Ogasawara Sagami no Kami provided red rice and simmered dishes to the party, with hospitality staff present.)

One　The ceremony for entering the Hasegawa Buemon school was as follows:
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Hasegawa Buemon
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Same [family] Kichitaro
The above first had an audience in the reception chamber, with the domain official announcing their names, receiving auspicious words from his lordship.
After the domain official's arrangements they withdrew, then entered the practice hall. At this time Buemon directly reported that he would demonstrate the etiquette of firearms families, then performed two forms of knee platform techniques for observation. After completion, his lordship expressed satisfaction that it was conducted without delay, and it concluded. In the long hearth room, both father and son were ordered to receive soup and sake. His lordship wore hemp formal dress. The domain official and one practice supervisor, plus Buemon and his son wore formal dress. However, no practice was conducted before his lordship today.

One　Before 7 o'clock, with attendants assembled for fireworks viewing, he boarded a boat at the boat district and descended to Ishitani riverbank. He dined his third meal on the boat, and after the fireworks concluded, took the ascending boat back to the boat district, returning to the castle after 5:30. However, today it suddenly became rainy weather, affecting the decorative items.

Honda Kuranosuke, chief retainers, castle deputy, and elders were invited to partake in soup and sake. Ceremonial cups were bestowed. The cups were presented before his lordship, and a spoon and one physician were supposed to attend the banquet, but as time had passed and it would cause delays, the ceremonial cups were respectfully declined.

- After the above ceremony was completed, Kohei and Shingobei withdrew. Also, during the ten-day rest period, Kohei and Shingobei served the fourth shift, while Hayato served the return shift.

- The celebratory meal was consumed together with the three regular meals.

- 5th month, 16th day: Honda Roya first became a trusted retired retainer, and along with the elder's son and other trusted retired retainers, was granted audience in the sitting room while his lordship wore formal hakama.

- 5th month, 18th day: At 5:30 with full retinue, visited the shrine, mausoleum, Unsho-ji, Koken-ji, Zuigen-ji, Kezo-ji, and Toko-ji temples in formal attire. First worshipped at the shrine and mausoleum, then visited Unsho-ji temple, worshipped at the shogunate mausoleum, then entered the study hall, changed into long hakama, worshipped at the memorial tablet hall, then worshipped at all remaining mausolea.

- 19th day: Played kemari (kickball) at the main castle's horse grounds, and ordered the next round of kemari as well.

- 5th month, 24th day: At 4:00 assembly, heard reports on caretaker duties from chief retainers through district magistrates.

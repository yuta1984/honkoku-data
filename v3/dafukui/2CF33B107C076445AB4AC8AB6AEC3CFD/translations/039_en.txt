December 24th:
- One direct letter from the lord
- Three ducks caught by hawks (Note: hawks' birds prepared and purchased)

The above was sent as a reply letter to Lord Matsudaira Hyuga-no-kami, arranged by Utsunomiya Kageyu and dispatched to Kawai Gentayu in Osaka.

December 27th: Assembled at evening time for the year-end celebration ceremony. The senior councilors, one chamberlain on duty, Maeda Hikojiro, Honda Kutayu, Yamashina Hachijuro (Note: Hiko withdrew due to defilement), and others proceeded as usual for moon-viewing, with no changes in various matters. It concluded after 7:30 AM the next dawn, and His Lordship retired at 6 AM.

December 28th: After returning from year-end courtesy calls, at the audience chamber, the senior retainers, senior councilors, chamberlains, sons of senior councilors, followed by attendant chiefs, page chiefs, and pages assembled to offer year-end congratulations.

[The following line in red ink]
December 29th, 5:30 AM: With full retinue, visited the spirit altar and mausoleum of Unsho-ji Itoku-in-sama.

December 29th, after 7:30 AM: Worship was conducted at the four garden shrines. As follows, leading the way, inner guard duty:
- Main shrine (the principal shrine), Benten, two Inari shrines (combined shrine)

For the above worship, all garden officials managed arrangements. Garden official Kato Gonjuro appeared at the four shrines beforehand. Kasuya Seijiro attended duty. The matter of this worship was reported by the attendant chief.

Today from 7 o'clock until the end of night duty, all attendants wore formal dress, lanterns were lit, all attendants assembled along with court physicians and acupuncturists for year-end congratulations, appearing in a line at the entrance side of the audience chamber and small parlor. However, when they appeared at evening time to make inquiry, they were ordered to appear after the meal. When His Lordship entered the audience chamber after the meal, from the head of the north entrance side - attendant chiefs, court physicians, acupuncture physicians, page chiefs, pages, close attendant group chiefs, and close attendants - all appeared in a single line at the east entrance side. Once assembled, the attendant chief announced that they would offer year-end congratulations.

This month being a short month, various gifts to attendants were given on the 27th:
- 1000 hiki of gold - Kiyota Tanzo
The above ceremonial items were arranged and given by the senior councilors.

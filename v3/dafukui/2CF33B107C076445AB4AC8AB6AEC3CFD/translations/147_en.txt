Item: August 21st, as Shinemon was to serve as attendant for tomorrow's visit to Yunohana River, tonight's lodging duty was excused, and he withdrew at eight o'clock.

【Written in red ink】
Item: Before nine o'clock, archery practice was performed with rolled straw targets and regular targets. As Iijima Shirōemon was indisposed, only his son Shinzaemon appeared.【End of red ink】

Item: August 22nd, before five o'clock the attendants assembled, and His Lordship went to Yunohana River inn for river hunting. After the second meal at Nikawa, at Ichikawa he ordered the boatmen to cast nets throughout the river, and observed from aboard his boat.

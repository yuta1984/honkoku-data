One item: On the 2nd day of 12th month, instruction in archery techniques was received. Iijima Shirōemon and his son appeared, wearing formal silk and court dress to the practice hall. His lordship was seated and spoke (father and son's names). At this time, father and son appeared and explained the origins of archery, famous places for bows and arrows, etc. Completed, Gentarō performed demonstration shooting for viewing, completed. The instruction was completed without delay, and Shirōemon expressed gratitude to the chief retainer, with formal response. There was acknowledgment (praise), and formal response (rest), completed. However, at this time people were dismissed except chief retainer, senior attendant chief, one practice supervisor, and naturally those pages who had already completed instruction remained. Also, only practice supervisors wore formal dress, others in plain clothes.

Gold 300 hiki - Iijima Shirōemon
Gold 100 hiki - Same, Gentarō

The above bestowed for today's technical instruction, soup and sake bestowed, handled by chief retainer.

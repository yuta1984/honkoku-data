• November 27th: By imperial command, Gorohachi-sama inherited the Yuki family lineage and came to be called Yuki Gorohachi (later called Matsudaira). He grew up under the care of Harutomo-sama (inheriting a stipend of 5,000 koku).

• December 28th: For the same reason as above, Gorohachi-sama sent a representative to Edo to express his gratitude. There were献上物 (tribute gifts) to both御所様 (the Shogun and retired Shogun).

• This year: The Yamanote residence was granted, and Honda Izunokami (Tomimasa) was also granted the Asakusa residence.

Date unknown: Yoshimatsu-sama became the adopted son of Honda Izunokami and moved to Fuchu Castle.

Keicho 13, Year of the Earth Monkey (1608)
• This year (date unknown): Returned to the domain

• June 16th: Kisa-hime-sama was declared the adopted daughter of Hidetatsu-sama, and today departed from Kitanosho for Edo.

• July 17th: The marriage between Kisa-hime-sama and Mori Toshichiro (Hidenari), lord of Suo and Nagato provinces (later called Matsudaira Nagatonokami), was announced. Today she entered his residence from the castle (Keicho 15, ■■). Her escort included Senior Councilors Sakai Utatonokami (Tadayo) and Doi Oinokami (Toshikatsu).

• 18th of the same month: The construction of Sunpu Castle was completed.

1. On the 13th day of the 7th month: Lord Yamato-no-kami Naonori was ordered to transfer to Yamagata in Dewa Province with an increase of 30,000 koku
　　(totaling 100,000 koku), and Lord Kozuke-no-suke Chikahide was granted an increase of 5,000 koku (totaling 20,000 koku)
1. On the 23rd day of the same month: Regarding the matter of fuda-gin (domain currency), after various deliberations, consultation was also made with Lord Hikosaka Iki-no-kami Ichi■, and furthermore through a written document from Senior Retainer Koma Moku-no-jo Teizumi to Lord Iki-no-kami,
　　after inquiry and consultation it was decided that two-thirds of the fuda-gin value would be discarded and one-third would be
　　exchanged for new currency as ordered
1. On the 25th day of the same month: Regarding the above matter, the accounting office issued the following notice to all concerned parties:
　　　　　Although all fuda-gin should be discarded due to the new territorial arrangements recently ordered,
　　　　　considering the hardship this would cause everyone,
　　　　　out of compassion, after consultation it was decided that one-third of the value would be
　　　　　converted to new currency for distribution, and all should understand this arrangement

1. 7th month (day unclear): The lord's name was changed to Masaaki-sama
1. 1st day of 8th month: For celebratory purposes, went to the castle and presented a ceremonial sword and horse substitute (10 ryo of gold)
1. 5th day of the same month: When a retainer was summoned due to being called by Senior Councilor Toda Yamashiro-no-kami Tadamasa,
　　a vermillion seal document for Fukui Shinmei Shrine territory was handed over. As thanks for this,
　　Senior Retainer Matsudaira Shobei Masatsune was sent as an envoy to Lord Yamashiro-no-kami

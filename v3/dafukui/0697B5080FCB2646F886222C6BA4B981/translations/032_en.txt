1. May 2nd: For the Boys' Day celebrations, through a messenger as customary, presented御帷子 (summer court robe) and 御単 (single-layer garment). To Her Ladyship, presented five pieces of white silver.

1. Same month, 4th day: As congratulations for the completion of the imperial enthronement, made a formal visit to the castle.

1. Same month, 6th day: For the same matter, through messenger and caretaker, presented two varieties in one load to the Shogun.

1. Same month, 25th day: Through imperial messenger Senior Councilor Abe Bungo-no-kami (Masatake), was granted permission to return to the domain, and received 300 pieces of silver, lined summer robes, and thirty items.

1. Same day: Made formal castle visit to express gratitude for the leave permission, and received a horse as a gift.

1. June 9th: Due to the summer heat, through a messenger as customary, presented locally produced somen noodles and dried sea bream.

1. Same month, 10th day: Departed from Edo, traveled via the Tōkaidō route.

1. Same month, 13th day: At the residence of monthly duty senior retainer Honda Sahyōe (Naoshige), regarding the recent order to destroy paper money, various retainers led by Takachi and others unanimously submitted a written petition expressing their desire to contribute rice from their stipends. When this reached His Lordship's attention, the following was ordered and Sahyōe conveyed it to all monthly duty officers:
　　　Regarding the recent order to destroy paper money, they should contribute rice as a token of their sincerity.

1. On June 11th, at the residence of senior retainer Inaba Uneme Masanobu, from Honda Magotarō Nagataka first to Sakai Magoichirō Motonobu, all were seated in attendance, and Uneme conveyed the lord's intentions as follows to those summoned:
　　　　Since new domain lands have been granted this time, all of you will be newly employed. However, your stipends will be reduced by half from what you have been receiving until now, so you should understand this situation. His Lordship feels it regrettable that this will cause hardship for everyone, but
　　　　
　　　　with the current assessed yield of the domain, it is difficult to provide what His Lordship would wish, so
　　　　you should serve lightly in all matters and carry out your duties accordingly. The above.
　　　　　　Memorandum
　　1. All stipends (chigyo) are reduced by half
　　1. Those receiving 150 koku become 100 koku
　　1. Those receiving 100 koku have no reduction
　　1. Those receiving 50 koku are treated the same [as 100 koku holders]
　　　　　However, the names of those summoned above are not recorded.

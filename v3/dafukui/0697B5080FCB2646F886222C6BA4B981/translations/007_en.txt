　Additionally, matters of unpaid taxes (mishin) should be handled without delay

The above points should be observed

1. Intercalary 3rd month, 11th day: Those who went to serve in various posts in Edo were ordered to
　postpone their departure until further instructions, and this was conveyed by the chief retainer to
　Ōtani Sukuroku Shigēteru of the imperial audience group, Menō Samon Yorihisa, and Suzuki Tamiya

1. Same day: Regarding the rice in Akesato storehouse, as there may be official need for it at this time,
　the release of rice should be suspended for now, and this was conveyed by the chief retainer to
　magistrates Kanie Gyōbu-emon Tadahira and Ōtani Gizaemon Yoshiyuki

1. Same day: Regarding public works, except for those specifically requested for river maintenance and irrigation,
　others should be postponed and delayed, and this was conveyed by the chief retainer to
　public works magistrate Kōzuki Hachirōzaemon Yoshitomi

Jōkyō 4, Year of the Fire Rabbit (1687)
1. January 1st - For New Year's ceremonial greetings, [the lord] attended the castle and presented a ceremonial sword and horse substitute
   (ten ryō of gold), and received silk garments as a gift
1. Same month, 4th day - This year
   As the Shōgun was in his unlucky year, last December messenger-official Ōida Shinkurō (Naka)
   was dispatched to Ise, and prayers were requested.
   Prayers (Daikagura) were performed at both shrines, and the said prayers were completed today.
   However, when Shinkurō returned carrying the purification items from the said prayers, during the journey
   they were handed over to messenger Misawa Jinzaemon and sent to Edo.

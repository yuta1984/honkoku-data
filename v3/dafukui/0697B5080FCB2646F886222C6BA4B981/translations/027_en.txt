Jōkyō 4th year, Hinoto-u (1687)
1. January 1st - For New Year ceremonial greetings, [the lord] attended the castle and
   presented ten ryō of gold as a sword and horse substitute, and received silk garments as a reward
1. Same month, 4th day - This year
   Since the Shōgun was in his unlucky year, in the previous December, envoy Ōida Shinkirō Naka was
   dispatched to Ise to request prayers, whereupon
   prayers (Daikagura) were performed at both shrines, and the aforementioned prayers were completed today
   　However, when Shinkirō returned carrying the purification items from the prayers, on the road
   　　they were handed over to messenger Misawa Jinzaemon and sent up to Edo

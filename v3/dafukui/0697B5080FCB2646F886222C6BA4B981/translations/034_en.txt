This immediately reached His Lordship's ears, and he was particularly pleased. Given the circumstances of this time, it would cause everyone hardship, so he would not accept (their offerings). However, regarding the order to destroy the paper money, this cannot be managed by domain finances alone, so since there are remaining rice from last year and paper money stored in the domain treasury, of that amount, those with stipends of 1,000 koku or more at a rate of 2 bu, and those with 900 koku or less at 1 bu 2 rin - keeping this amount aside first, the paper money destruction will be ordered. That portion will be distributed promptly.
　　　　　　　However, no borrowing will be made from those receiving rice stipends and support allowances.

　　　　　1. To Honda Magotarō, this was conveyed by calling out his retainer Honda Shōzaemon

1. June 13th: Regarding the same matter, if all paper money is ordered to be destroyed, the townspeople had previously petitioned that they would like to contribute small amounts according to their means. Today it was ordered that they should contribute as they had requested.
　　　　However, since this was ordered for the townspeople as they had requested, it was also ordered that rural inhabitants should contribute paper money according to their means.

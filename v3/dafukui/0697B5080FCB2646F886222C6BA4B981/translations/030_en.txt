1. April 4th: Due to a summons (yesterday on the 3rd, a notice came from the Senior Councilors), Caretaker Guard Kaga Tōzaemon (Narioki) went to the castle. In the Yanagi-no-ma (Willow Room), he received an official letter from Duty Officer Toda Yamashiro-no-kami (Tadamasa) regarding the year-end congratulatory tribute. At that time, Tōzaemon was granted a lined kimono, and after returning, he presented the official letter. It was received in the audience chamber (wearing formal court dress). Tōzaemon was directly given dried abalone. Following this, he performed the courtesy visit to Yamashiro-no-kami as thanks.
　　Note: Hereafter, the courtesy visits for Boys' Day, Chrysanthemum Festival, and year-end will not be recorded

1. Same month, 6th day: Performed audience visits to Senior Councilors Ōkubo Kaga-no-kami (Tadatomo) and Toda Yamashiro-no-kami (Tadamasa)

1. Same month, 13th day: Through a messenger, the first mackerel of the domain's produce was presented

1. Same month, 17th day: There was a summons from Duty Officer Toda Yamashiro-no-kami (Tadamasa), and Retainer Koma Mokunosuke (Sadazumi, accompanied by Caretaker Guard Arakawa Zenemon) went out. Regarding the request to repair damaged sections of the castle's surrounding stone walls and the remaining unfinished moat construction that had been previously approved, an official document was handed over by Yamashiro-no-kami.

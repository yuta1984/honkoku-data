1. April 4th: Due to a summons [a written notice came from the Senior Councilor yesterday on the 3rd], Kaga Tōzaemon [Narioki], the caretaker, went to the castle. At Yanagi-no-ma (Willow Room), he received an official letter from Toda Yamashiro-no-kami [Tadamasa], the duty councilor, regarding the year-end congratulatory tribute. On this occasion, Tōzaemon was granted the honor of receiving a lined silk garment. Upon his return, he presented the official letter and received it with honor in the formal audience chamber [wearing formal court dress]. He was personally given dried abalone by his lord, and thereafter performed courtesy visits to Yamashiro-no-kami as thanks for this honor.
　　Note: Hereafter, such courtesy duties for Boys' Day, Chrysanthemum Festival, and year-end will not be recorded

1. Same month, 6th day: Performed formal audience duties with Senior Councilors Ōkubo Kaga-no-kami [Tadatomo] and Toda Yamashiro-no-kami [Tadamasa]

1. Same month, 13th day: Through a messenger, the first bonito of the season from the domain was presented

1. Same month, 17th day: There was a summons from duty councilor Toda Yamashiro-no-kami [Tadamasa], and retainer Koma Moku-no-jō [Sadazumi, accompanied by caretaker Arakawa Zen'emon] went to the castle. Regarding the petition to conduct construction work on damaged stone walls around the castle and remaining moat construction that had been previously approved, an official letter was handed over by Yamashiro-no-kami.

Regarding the collapse of two sections of stone walls at the third bailey of Fukui Castle in Echizen Province, the request to rebuild them has been acknowledged according to the marked drawings, and it shall be restored as before.

1. April 4th: Due to a summons (notification came yesterday the 3rd via official note from the rōjū), caretaker guard Kaga Tōzaemon (Narioki) went to the castle, where in the Yanagi-no-ma room he received an official letter from duty officer Toda Yamashiro-no-kami (Tadamasa) regarding the presentation of year-end congratulations. On that occasion, Tōzaemon was granted a lined kimono, and upon returning, he presented the official letter and received it respectfully in the formal audience chamber (wearing formal court dress). Tōzaemon was personally given long hakama trousers, and thereafter performed the courtesy visit to Yamashiro-no-kami as thanks.
　　　　Note: Regarding courtesy duties for Boys' Day, Chrysanthemum Festival, and year-end, these will not be recorded hereafter

1. Same month, 6th day: Performed formal audience duties with rōjū Ōkubo Kaga-no-kami (Tadatomo) and Toda Yamashiro-no-kami (Tadamasa)

1. Same month, 13th day: Through a messenger, the first mackerel of the domain's products was presented

1. Same month, 17th day: Following a summons from duty officer Toda Yamashiro-no-kami (Tadamasa), chief retainer Koma Mokunosuke (Masumi, accompanied by caretaker guard Arakawa Zenemon) went [to the castle], where regarding the request to conduct construction work on damaged stone wall sections around the castle and remaining portions of moat crossing work previously approved, an official letter was handed over by Yamashiro-no-kami.

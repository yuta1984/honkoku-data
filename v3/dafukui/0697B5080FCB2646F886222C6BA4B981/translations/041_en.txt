1. August 21st: The Kawara gate guards Makino Shuden (Yukiyuki), Ōta Sanya, Suganuma Ichizaemon (Ietsugu),
　　Okabe Shinkurō (Sadanori), Matsubara Uemon (Yoshihaku), and Nishio Chikara (Muneshiki), who had been
　　serving as Kawara gate guards since the lord's arrival at the castle, were henceforth relieved of guard duty, and the title
　　formerly called "Kawara gate guards" was changed, and henceforth they shall be called "yoriai" (senior councilors)

1. Same day: Regarding the same matter above, for the Kawara gate guard duties, the retainers of Koma Mokunosuke (Sadazumi), Inaba Uneme (Masanobu),
　　and Sakai Genba (Mototomo) were assigned to guard duty, with three men serving day and night shifts,
　　and one retainer each from Jōdai (castle keeper) Ariga Saemon (Masakata) was to join only for night watch duty

1. Same month, 23rd day: By invitation, [the lord] went out to Matsuoka

1. September 4th: Regarding the current revision of regulations, in the formal audience chamber, as follows:
　　one copy each of the regulatory document was distributed to all monthly duty officers by the monthly duty senior retainer Honda
　　Sahyōe (Naoshige), and in the next room, with the inspectors in attendance, one copy each of a memorandum was
　　distributed

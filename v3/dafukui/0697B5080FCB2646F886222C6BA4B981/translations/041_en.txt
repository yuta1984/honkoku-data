1. August 21st: The Kawara gate guards Makino Tonomo (Yukiyuki), Ota Sanshiya, Suganuma Ichizaemon (Ietsugu),
　　Okabe Shinkuro (Sadanori), Matsubara Uemon (Yoshihaku), and Nishio Chikara (Munesato), who had been
　　serving guard duty at the Kawara gate since the lord's arrival at the castle, were henceforth relieved of guard duty, and the
　　designation they had held until now as "Kawara gate guards" was changed, and hereafter they would be
　　called "yoriai" (senior retainers)
1. Same day: Regarding the same matter above, concerning the Kawara gate guard duties, the retainers of
　　Koma Mokunosuke (Sadazumi), Inaba Uneme (Masanobu), and
　　Sakai Genba (Mototomo) were assigned guard duty, with three men serving day and night shifts,
　　and one retainer each from Castellan Ariga Saemon's (Masakata) men would join only for overnight duty,
　　as was ordered
1. Same month, 23rd day: By invitation, [the lord] went out to Matsuoka
1. September 4th: Due to the current revision of regulations, in the formal audience chamber, as follows:
　　one copy each of the regulatory document was distributed to all monthly duty officers by the monthly senior retainer Honda
　　Sahyoe (Naoshige), and in the next room, with the inspectors in attendance, one copy each of a
　　memorandum was distributed
　　　　　　　　Regulations
　　　　　1. The prohibitions issued by the Shogunate must not be disobeyed
　　　　　　The way of loyalty, filial piety, benevolence, and righteousness must be especially cultivated
　　　　　1. Retainers must prepare weapons and horse equipment without negligence, and useless

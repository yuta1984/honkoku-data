Regarding the matter of the endorsement for Lord Tsunamasa's relief rice certificate, having previously communicated to Senior Councilor Ōkubo Kaga-no-kami Tadatomo-dono, the following written notice was delivered today:

　　　　　　　　Memorandum
　　　　　Regarding Matsudaira Echizen, whose domain has been confiscated, 20,000 bales
　　　　　(3.5 to per bale) of relief rice shall be granted from this year of the Tiger, and henceforth
　　　　　endorsed certificates with joint seals shall be issued for record-keeping purposes. The above.

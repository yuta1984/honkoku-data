1. February 4th: Purification rituals for prayers at Ise and Nishinomiya were requested and entrusted to Kanri-in temple
　　Note: Misawa Jinemon carried [the request], but arrival in Edo is unclear

1. Same month, 7th day: This time there was a guard duty assignment for Edo, and hereafter there will be two groups of palace guards (goshoin-ban), four groups of great guards (ōban), and two groups of caretaker guards (rusui). Regarding this matter, the guard commanders were informed today

1. Same day: When retainers apply for deathbed adoption, from now on the commanders shall examine the documents of such applications, receive and confirm the details, and report accordingly. Should the commanders fall ill or have conflicts of duty, corrections shall be made through secretaries - this was announced

1. Same month, 12th day: At Zuigen-ji temple, from dawn of the 10th until this morning, the first anniversary memorial service for Lady Shōkei-in was conducted. At Nanzen-ji temple in Yanaka as well, memorial services were conducted from the evening of the 11th until today

1. Same month, 27th day: Inspector Murakami Santayū (Masamine) was dispatched from Edo, and Taki Kazue (Akinori)'s son Heizō was ordered to commit seppuku

1. March 28th: At Jōkō-in temple, from the evening of the 27th until this morning, the 27th anniversary memorial service for Lady Seichiū-in was conducted. At Tentoku-ji temple as well, from the 26th until today, memorial services were conducted for Lady Fuyo-hime [daughter of Mitsumichi]

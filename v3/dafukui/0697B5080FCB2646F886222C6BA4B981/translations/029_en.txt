1. February 4th: Purification rituals for prayers at Ise and Nishinomiya were requested and entrusted to Kanri-in temple
　　　　Note: Misawa Jinemon carried [the request], but arrival in Edo is unknown

1. Same month, 7th day: This time there was a reorganization of guard duties in Edo, with henceforth two groups of palace guards (goshoin-ban), four groups of grand guards (ō-goban), and two groups of caretaker guards (goryusui). This matter was announced to the guard commanders today

1. Same day: When retainers submit requests for deathbed adoptions, from now on the guard commanders shall examine the draft documents of such requests, review the details, and report accordingly. Should the guard commander be ill or otherwise unavailable, the matter shall be handled through the secretary, as was decreed

1. Same month, 12th day: At Zuigen-ji temple, from dawn on the 10th until this morning, the first anniversary memorial service for Lady Shōkei-in was conducted. At Nansen-ji temple in Yanaka as well, memorial services were conducted from the evening of the 11th until today

1. Same month, 27th day: Inspector Murakami Santayū (Masamine) was dispatched from Edo, and Heizō, son of Taki Kazue (Akinori), was ordered to commit seppuku

1. March 28th: At Jōkō-in temple, from the evening of the 27th until this morning, the 27th anniversary memorial service for Lord Seichihi-in was conducted. At Tentoku-ji temple as well, from the 26th until today, memorial services for Lady Fukō-hime were conducted

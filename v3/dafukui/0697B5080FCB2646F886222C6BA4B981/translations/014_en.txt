1. 5th month《marginal note: day unclear》: Regarding the matter of the family crest on the curtains, there was a private consultation through Lord Hikosaka Iki-no-kami Shigetsugu, and following his guidance that the previous practice should continue, the matter of luggage tags during travel shall also continue to use the hollyhock crest as before.

1. 6th month, 4th day: Due to the summer heat, sōmen noodles and dried sea bream, products of the domain, were presented via messenger. As acknowledgment of the successful presentation, a formal letter came from Senior Councilor Abe Bungo-no-kami Masatake:
　　　　　　　Address:
　　　　　　　　To Lord Matsudaira Hyōbu-no-taiyu　　Masatake
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Endorsement:
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Abe Bungo-no-kami
　　　　One box of sōmen noodles and one type of delicacy were presented,
　　　　and the presentation was successfully completed. With humble respect.
　　　　　　6th month, 4th day　　　　Masatake (written seal)
　　　　Note: Regarding formal letters of acknowledgment for presenting sōmen noodles,
　　　　this has become routine practice hereafter, so it will not be recorded.

1. On the 11th day of the 6th month, regarding those who had yoriki (assistant officers) assigned to them, all except役yoriki (active yoriki) were to be discontinued, so
　　Inaba Uneme Masanobu conveyed the following. And a written notice stating that this should be communicated to the yoriki
　　was delivered by the ometsuke to their respective heads
　　　　　Regarding the matter of everyone's yoriki, His Lordship feels it unfortunate, but they are to be
　　　　　discontinued this time, so you should understand this
　　　　　　　The above
　　　　　　　　Written notice to be communicated to the yoriki
　　　1. Residential quarters should be cleared up leisurely and may be surrendered at your discretion
　　　1. If the residence has a perimeter fence, whatever it may be, leave it as is, and the main house shall

　　　　　be granted
　　　1. Trees within the residence grounds, as well as house servants and stepping stones, may be
　　　　　taken at your discretion

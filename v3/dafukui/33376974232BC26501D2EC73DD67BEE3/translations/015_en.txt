One　5th Month, 28th Day: As thanks for returning to his domain, through messenger Nagami Jibuemon Yoshimasa,
　　he presented two types of gifts in one load. Jibuemon was granted an audience.
One　6th Month, 1st Day: When messenger Nagami Jibuemon Yoshimasa went to the castle,
　　a formal letter was handed over, and on that occasion Jibuemon was ordered to receive three summer robes as gifts.
One　Same month, 23rd Day: As thanks for messenger Nagami Jibuemon Yoshimasa's audience and the gifts he was
　　ordered to receive, a messenger was dispatched, whereupon a reply letter was handed over today.
One　Same month, 26th Day: Due to the summer heat, a letter inquiring after [the shogun's] well-being was submitted, whereupon
　　a reply letter was handed over today by Senior Councillor Ōkubo Kaga-no-kami Tadatomo.
One　10th Month, 29th Day: Due to a summons from the Senior Councillors, the caretaker went and received hawks and cranes

　　as gifts, along with a formal letter signed by the Senior Councillors, one crane, and relay station certificates
　　which were handed over. For this purpose, foot soldier Takeda Shichiemon was sent along to the domain.
One　11th Month, 4th Day (hour of the rat): The gifted hawks and cranes arrived in Fukui with relay station formal letters,
　　the formal reception was completed at the residence, and a letter of acknowledgment was submitted to Edo through foot soldier Kondō Koemon by express messenger.
One　Same month, 5th Day: For the same matter as above, as thanks, messenger Sasa Jihyōgo Yoshitsugu was
　　dispatched to Edo. Carrying the formal letter and relay station certificates, he departed Fukui today.

Item: On the 7th day of the 12th month, when a second inquiry was made regarding the matter of visiting Edo, a reply was given that the inquiry should be made until next spring, as per the initial instructions.

　　　　　I have respectfully read your letter.
　　　　　It is most joyful and appropriate that
　　　　　the Shogun is in increasingly good spirits. Also, regarding the matter of visiting Edo,
　　　　　I have understood from the content of your letter that an inquiry was made concerning
　　　　　the change of reign. As previously
　　　　　communicated, the inquiry shall be made again in the coming spring. The careful
　　　　　consideration shown in this matter has

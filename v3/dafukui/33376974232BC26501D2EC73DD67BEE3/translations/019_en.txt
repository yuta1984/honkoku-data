【Right Page】
One　6th Month, 22nd Day: For congratulations on the succession, messenger Honda Shuri Tominobu was dispatched to Edo. He departed Fukui today.
One　7th Month, 10th Day: To inquire after well-being during the summer heat, through messenger Inoue Magodayū, fish delicacies were offered.
　　　　However, the above messenger was dispatched from the home domain for this purpose, and the offering was made through Magodayū who was stationed in Edo.
One　Same month, 11th Day: For congratulations on the relocation, through messenger Nakagawa Rokuzaemon Shigeyoshi, one type, one load was offered.
　　　　However, the catalog was presented at Honmaru, and the actual items were delivered to Ninomaru.

【Left Page】
One　Same month, 16th Day: Regarding the above matter, a joint letter from the Council of Elders was handed over:
　　　　　"This time, regarding congratulations for the relocation to Honmaru, through a messenger
　　　　　one type, one load was offered as per the catalog. This matter
　　　　　has been successfully reported. With reverence and respect."

Tsuchiya Tajima-no-kami
　　　　　　　　September 19th　　　　　　　Kazunao (signature and seal)
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Kuze Yamato-no-kami
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Hiroyuki (signature and seal)
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Inaba Mino-no-kami
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Masanori (signature and seal)
　　　　　　　　Lord Matsudaira Hyōbu-dayū

One　11th Month, 19th Day: Regarding the recent order (on the 3rd) that 10,000 hyō of relief rice be granted to Lord Bitchū-no-kami Naotaka,
　　a messenger was sent to Edo to express gratitude for this matter.
　　The reply letter was delivered today by Senior Councillor Ōkubo Kaga-no-kami Tadatomo.
One　12th Month, 22nd Day: The Senior Councillors summoned [us], and when the caretaker official went to attend, regarding the bestowal of a crane from the Shōgun's falconry,
　　an official letter jointly signed by the Senior Councillors, along with one crane and relay station certification documents
　　were handed over.
One　Same month, 26th Day: The crane bestowed from the Shōgun's falconry arrived in Fukui via relay station official letter
　　and was received at the lord's residence.
　　　　However, although a messenger of gratitude should have been sent, the records are unclear.

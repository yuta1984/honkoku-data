Jōkyō 2nd Year, Year of the Wood Ox (1685)
Item: On New Year's Day, as New Year's felicitations, envoy Iida Chūshirō was sent to present the customary ceremonial sword and horse substitute
　　　　However, Chūshirō departed from the domain on the 7th day of the previous month
Item: On the same day, at the lord's residence, the New Year's courtesy was received in the customary manner
Item: On the 18th day of the same month,
　　upon learning that the Shogun, who had been slightly indisposed, had recovered well, a letter of joy was submitted

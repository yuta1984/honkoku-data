【Right Page】
　　Saemon, Saitō Minbu Sadayuki, Tagaya Gondayū Daihiro, and Inagaki Yasuemon Shigenao(?) were given
　　御長蚫 (ceremonial gifts). The tile gate guards and below returned after being recorded in the register without formal audience.
One　1st Month, 11th Day: As congratulations for the completion of the three-day New Year ceremonies, a letter was submitted to
　　Doi Noto-no-kami Toshifusa, who was on duty.
　　　　　However, the reply letter was handed over on the 25th of the same month.
One　5th Month, 11th Day:
　　Due to the Shogun's illness, messenger Ajima Sadanonjō was dispatched by express courier
　　to Edo to inquire after his condition. He departed Fukui today.
One　Same month (day unknown): On the 7th, it was announced that Lord Tsunayoshi, Minister of the Left of Tatebayashi, had been designated as heir,

【Left Page】
　　and as congratulations for his promotion in court rank, messenger Kōzai Hachirōbei Masasuke was
　　dispatched to the Senior Councillors.
　　　　　However, this messenger was to be sent from the home domain in principle, but Hachirōbei,
　　　　　　who was stationed in Edo, carried out this duty.
One　Same month, 13th Day:
　　Due to the Shogun's death on the 8th (posthumous Buddhist name: Genyūin-sama),
　　messenger Ariga Saikū was dispatched by express courier to Edo
　　to inquire after Lord Dainagon's well-being. He departed Fukui today.
One　Same month, 17th Day: Messenger Ariga Saikū arrived in Edo and [reported] to the Senior Councillors

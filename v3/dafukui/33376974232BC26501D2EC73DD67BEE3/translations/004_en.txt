【Right Page】
　　As such, all administrative matters in the domain shall be entrusted to you, and there was an imperial command that you should carry out orders without hesitation. He respectfully accepted this gracious instruction and withdrew.
One　9th Month, 13th Day: Upon being summoned, he ascended to the castle where he had an audience in the Black Shoin, was granted leave for pilgrimage to Nikko and return to his domain, received hawks (2) and a horse (1) as gifts, and after withdrawing, made courtesy calls to the Senior Councilors, Junior Councilors, and Lord Sakai Kawachi-no-kami Tadataka for thanks.
One　Same month, 14th Day: The gifted horse was brought by messenger from Senior Councilor Lord Inaba Mino-no-kami Masanori. He appeared at the front reception hall, Inaba Uneme Harufusa received the horse and presented the reins, the ceremonial reception was completed. Upon entering, he met with the messenger in the transit room, was given dried abalone as a gift, gave his response, and sent messengers to Lord Mino-no-kami and Lord Doi Noto-no-kami Toshifusa as thanks for this.

【Left Page】
　　The caretaker officials were dispatched.
One　Same month, 16th Day: Departed Edo, made pilgrimage to Nikko, returned to the capital on the 23rd of the same month.
One　Same month, 28th Day: Departed from the Reigan Island residence, traveled via the Tokaido route.
One　10th Month, 6th Day: To receive the gifted hawks, Hikosaka Matahei Shigeyasu and the hawk masters Isobe Kamozaemon and Usui Shinuemon went to Junior Councilor Lord Hotta Bitchu-no-kami Masatoshi (word came from Lord Abe Shirogoro yesterday on the 5th), received the two hawks and returned.
One　Same month, 11th Day: The gifted hawks (2) were sent to the home domain with Okutsu Sukesaburo and the hawk masters Isobe Kamozaemon and Usui Shinuemon accompanying them.
One　Same month, 12th Day: From lodging at Imajo Station, stopped at Yoshie, at the residence of Takaya Iori.

　　　　　　"The matter of the improper individuals consulting together has reached high ears, and it is a matter of great consequence. I will immediately instruct the mansion magistrate, so please understand this intention. With great respect and humility"
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Ōkubo Kaga-no-kami
　　　　　　　　February 24th　　　　　　Tadatomo (with seal)
　　　　　　　　To Matsudaira Hyōbu-taiyu-dono
Item: On the 2nd day of the 3rd month, regarding the successful completion of the proclamation of Crown Prince Asahito-shinnō as Middle Palace, a letter of congratulations was submitted to Edo

【Left Page】
　　　　However, on the 13th day of the same month, the aforementioned reply was received
Item: On the 15th day of the same month, regarding the petition to offer up the Hamachō upper residence, when it was granted as requested, a letter of gratitude was submitted, and today the reply was received from Rōjū Abe Bungo-no-kami Masatake-dono
Item: On the 18th day of the 4th month, having learned that Princess Tsuru-hime had contracted smallpox but was in mild condition, [the lord] submitted a letter further inquiring about her condition
　　　　However, on the 25th day of the same month, the aforementioned reply was received
Item: On the 20th day of the same month, regarding the 33rd anniversary memorial service for Taiyū-in-sama at Tōeizan, a messenger [was sent]

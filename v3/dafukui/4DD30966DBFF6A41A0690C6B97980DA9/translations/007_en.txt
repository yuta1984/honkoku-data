One: On the twenty-second day of the fifth month, the succession to family headship was announced, and accordingly the messenger from Edo, Goshoin-bantō Honda Tonomo, arrived in Fukui on the twentieth. This morning at the main castle, the monthly chief retainer Inaba Uneme conveyed this to all members of the domain retainers.

One: On the twenty-fifth of the same month, regarding the presentation of the first mackerel of the domain's production, the caretaker governor Miyakita Jūrōzaemon (Sadayoshi) went to inquire with the duty officer Mizuno Izumi-no-kami. He was instructed to make the inquiry after the succession celebration ceremony.

One: On the same day, regarding Lord Shima-no-kami serving as proxy for the succession celebration ceremony, this matter was conveyed to the duty officer Mizuno Izumi-no-kami that Lord Shima-no-kami would perform this duty.

One: On the twenty-sixth of the same month, Honda Minbu and Sakai Yosanzaemon were appointed to the position of chief retainer.

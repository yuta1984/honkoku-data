Regarding the memorial service for Lord Bunshōin, envoy and court announcer Isono Tamiya (marginal note: accompanied by castle keeper Miyakita Jūrōzaemon) presented five pieces of silver as incense money (marginal note: notification issued on the 19th of last month).

1. The same day, regarding Princess Hatsu's recovery to good health, as celebration, meals were bestowed upon the senior retainers and officials in her chambers.

1. October 21st, Regarding the water rights dispute between Sadatomo Village in the domain territory and Nooka Village in Lord Manabe Shimōsa-no-Kami's territory, Finance Commissioner Komakiné Higo-no-Kami had previously ordered that things should remain as they were originally, so the district magistrate conveyed this decision to the peasants.

1. The same day, Regarding the matter of permission for senior retainer Honda Nuinodono to use a palanquin and for attendant Okabe Sazen to use a sedan chair, the following petition was submitted to duty officer Andō Tsushima-no-Kami (marginal note: Shigeyuki):

　　　　　　　　　　　　Senior Retainer
　　　　　　　　　　　　　Honda Nuinodono
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Currently 57 years old in the year of the Dragon

　This person is my retainer who has been assigned duties in this locality, but he has ailments and finds it difficult to serve solely on horseback, so I humbly request that he be granted permission to use a palanquin. The above.

　　　October 21st　　　Matsudaira Senjirō (marginal note: seal applied due to young age)
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Official seal
　　　　　To Lord Andō Tsushima-no-Kami

1. On the 25th day of the 6th month, regarding the matter of simplification within the retainer households, Monthly Duty Senior Retainer Sakai Tange conveyed the following written directive to all monthly duty officers at the Council Chamber:

Regulations

1. Regarding retainers' attendants

Regardless of individual rank, each person should keep their retinue appropriately light according to their means. Even those of high stipend should use either a palanquin or horseback riding, but not both, within the castle town. However, for young men, horseback riding is especially appropriate.

Addition
When accompanying to Edo or when dispatched as messengers, both high-ranking and low-ranking retainers should keep their retinue lighter than their stipend would normally allow. When serving as messengers to other domains, they should keep retinues appropriate to their status.

1. On the 19th day of the 9th month, at the Reiganjima residence, through the senior guard commander Takagi Mizu-no-kami as an imperial messenger, an incense offering (300 pieces of silver) was received. On that occasion, Nakatsukasa-no-taifu (Masahira) and Yamato-no-kami (Naotomo) visited. As Yoshikuni's representative, Nakatsukasa-no-taifu received the offering, and afterward, he went to express gratitude to Senior Councilor Ii Kamon-no-kami (Naomichi) and Chamberlain Manabe Echizen-no-kami (Akifusa).

1. On the 22nd day of the same month, at Zuigenji Temple, memorial services were conducted from the 20th until today.

1. On the 27th day of the same month, at Unsho-ji Temple, memorial services were conducted from the 25th until today.

1. On the 3rd day of the 10th month, at Zuigenji Temple, the third seven-day memorial service was conducted by Princess Ume.

1. On the 10th day of the same month, at Zuigenji Temple, the fourth seven-day memorial service was conducted by Seishoin.

1. On the 17th day of the same month, it was ordered that a memorial tablet be enshrined at Tentokuji Temple and memorial services be conducted for the soul's repose.

1. On the same day, at Zuigenji Temple, the thirty-five day memorial service was conducted.

1. On the 1st day of the 11th month, at Zuigenji Temple, the forty-nine day memorial service was conducted from last evening until this morning.

1. On the 12th day of the same month, at Zuigenji Temple, the one-hundred day memorial service was conducted in advance from the evening of the 11th until today.

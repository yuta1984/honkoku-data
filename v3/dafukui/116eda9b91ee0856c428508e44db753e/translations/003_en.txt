Enkyō 4th Year, Year of the Metal Rabbit

1. On New Year's Day of the first month, the New Year ceremony was held at the main castle keep
1. On the same day, for New Year's congratulations, a small sword and horse代 were presented through messenger and guard captain Nagami Tamon (Nariaki) according to custom
1. On the 3rd of the same month, for the congratulations of the first Noh chanting of the year, a sake cup stand and sake money were presented through a messenger according to custom
1. On the 7th of the same month, for the congratulations of young herbs (wakana), ceremonial fish (sea bream) was presented through a messenger according to custom

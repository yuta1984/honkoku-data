First year of Genroku 15, Year of the Horse
　○Recently, a stone sarcophagus was discovered at a stone quarry in the mountains owned by peasants of Koyamaya village. Items that were inside this sarcophagus fell out and scattered, and some people picked them up. Furthermore, if anyone picks up such items in the future, they must promptly report to the inspectors. The above.
　　　　　　Intercalary 8th month, 13th day　To all　Additionally to groups〜　　　　　　From the Inspectors
　　　However, this sarcophagus apparently emerged due to heavy rains washing it out from that location in late 7th month, and peasants reported it on the 29th of the same month. District magistrate Tanaka Jōzaemon investigated and reported to the senior retainers. When the contents were examined, various items were found, which Jōzaemon's subordinates brought back. Some bones were also found and were entrusted to the village headman and senior peasants of Koyamaya village. The items from inside the stone sarcophagus were kept in Jōzaemon's custody, and the senior retainers ordered that the sarcophagus should be buried in a suitable location where stones would not be in the way. It was promptly buried near Koyamaya village on 8th month, 9th day. However, on the 10th day, Seishirō (son of Hajima Maenosuke of Tagaya Gondayū's guard unit) and Kihēji (son of Yamana Jirōemon of Himi Jirōbē Emon's guard unit) came to view the sarcophagus site and found two agates lying nearby, each picking up one. They reported asking what should be done about this, and when this was communicated to the senior retainers, following instructions from Matsudaira Shōbē's office, they were told to keep the items they had picked up in their personal custody for now. Details are recorded separately.
　○On the night of the 25th of this month, around the hour of the lower fourth (around 10 PM), a woman of about twenty years was murdered at Moto Sanmai on Mount Hiraoka above Imura village.

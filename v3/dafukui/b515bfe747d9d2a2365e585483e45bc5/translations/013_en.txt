First, Genroku 10, Year of the Ox (1697)
　○Regarding the capturing of pigeons in the castle town, this has long been prohibited by law. Furthermore, the domesticated pigeons from the inner quarters of the castle - those with short wings and white tails - have recently been flying and scattering to various places. Each person must strictly order their subordinates not to capture them, even within their own estates. This matter should be communicated among all colleagues. The above.
　　　　May 25th　To all　Also to the groups～　　　　　From the Inspectors

　○As previously announced, the domesticated pigeons from the inner quarters of the castle with short wings and white tails are flying around to various places. If anyone spots them on their estate, they must promptly report this to Yano Gonpei. Keep this in mind. The above.
　　　　June 2nd　　　　Also to the groups～　　　　　From the Inspectors

1. When dōshin-sha (lay Buddhist practitioners) and Buddhist nuns enter and leave, they must obtain seals from temple officials of their religious sect and submit them to the magistrates. If dōshin-sha do not submit certificates, their entry is strictly prohibited. This should be communicated to all, down to the lowest ranks. However, dōshin-sha passing through from other places and staying temporarily are exceptions. The above.

Proclamation issued around the 10th day of the 3rd month【Red ink】"Since this became a regular proclamation from Hōreki 4th year of the Dog, it has been sealed and kept in the official box, with yearly deadlines for continued use"

○ This is the season for fire prevention, so fire safety matters should be thoroughly communicated to all, down to the lowest ranks. When wind blows, retainers should patrol around each estate at night, and if suspicious persons are found, they should be arrested and sent to the magistrates. Those remaining in Edo may have few people, so relatives or neighboring families should consult to maintain vigilance. Moreover【Red ink insertion】"carrying lanterns from the 5th hour for travel"【Red ink】"has been established, so this should be strictly observed, and lantern lighting should be firmly ordered for all ranks down to the lowest. The above" It is heard that among the samurai class at gates or street corners, some lower-ranking individuals are receiving tobacco. Such behavior should be strictly prohibited among subordinates. As per regulations, lanterns should be lit from the 5th hour for travel, so firm orders should be given for all ranks down to the lowest to carry lit lanterns when traveling. The above.

Proclamation issued around the 10th day of the 4th month
Oral instruction

○ Regarding fire safety matters, although they have been announced repeatedly, even more careful and strict attention should be paid. As previously announced, lanterns should be lit from the 5th hour for travel. Furthermore, this should be firmly communicated to all retainers down to the lowest ranks. If suspicious persons are observed around estates, they should be arrested and reported to the magistrates. Irregular behavior is inauspicious. When going out to Kawano area, not only near residential areas but fire safety should not be neglected anywhere. Moreover【Enclosed in red】"this time especially"【Red ink marginal note】"this spring" there are instructions for special vigilance in the castle town, and strict investigations will be conducted, so care should be taken to avoid any careless behavior. The above【Red ink】"As written in red ink above, this was revised after strong deliberation from Kaei 2nd year of the Rooster"

Proclamation issued around the 10th day of the third month【Red ink】"From Hōreki 4 (1754), year of the dog, this became a regular proclamation, so it was sealed and kept in the official box, used annually by cutting and continuing the dates"

○ This is the season for fire prevention, so matters concerning fire sources should be thoroughly ordered down to the lowest ranks. On windy days, retainers should patrol around each estate at night, and if suspicious persons are found, they should be arrested and sent to the magistrates. Those remaining in Edo may be short-staffed, so their relatives or neighboring families should discuss being vigilant without negligence. Proclamation issued around the 10th day of the fourth month

Oral instruction

○ Although fire prevention matters have been announced repeatedly, even more careful and strict attention should be paid. As communicated previously, lanterns should be lit from the fifth hour for travel. Furthermore, this should be strictly ordered down to retainers and the lowest ranks. If suspicious persons are observed around estates, they should be arrested and reported to the magistrates. Wrong actions are inauspicious. When going out to Kawano area, not only near residential areas but fire sources should not be neglected.

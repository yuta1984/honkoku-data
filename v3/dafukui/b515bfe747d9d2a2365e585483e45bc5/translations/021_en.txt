First year of Hōei 6, Year of the Ox
　○Due to a conflict with the posthumous Buddhist name of His Lordship Gōbō, Jōkōin temple will henceforth be called Renshōji temple. This matter is
　　being announced to inform the samurai households as well. The above.
　　　　　　　2nd month, 5th day　　Additionally～　　　　　　　　From the Inspectors
　　　However, Atsumi Shinemon was dispatched to convey the above matter to the head priest of Jōkōin.
　○Communicated in writing. Regarding the various rumors currently circulating in Edo and anonymous writings coming from Edo and Kyoto,
　　it is most appropriate to instruct even the lowest ranks not to handle them carelessly. While this matter does not warrant issuing a formal directive, the senior retainers have instructed that it should be
　　communicated as a matter of understanding in gatherings, so we inform you thus. This should also be communicated among colleagues. The above.
　　　　　　　3rd month, 18th day　　　　　　　　　　　Kawazu Zentayū
　　　■■■■■This spring, regarding the successive deaths of Lord Tsunayoshi and the Midaidokoro
【Left page】
　○On the government's abstinence days of the 8th, 17th, 20th, and 24th, all killing of living beings is prohibited as before, and henceforth the 10th day should also be
　　observed in the same manner, as has been decreed. Everyone should understand this. The above.
　　　　　　　3rd month, 21st day　　　　　　　　　　　　From the Inspectors
　○Within the outer citadel and gates of Edo castle, those accompanying as attendants should not wear
　hats on fine weather days. The above　　6th month, 28th day　　　To all　　　　　　　From the Inspectors
　　Additionally, those with group supervision should give the same instructions. Furthermore, regarding townspeople and farmers, those accompanying as attendants should not wear helper hats or woven
　　hats either. Of course, this should also be announced to towns and villages. The above.
　○The 14th of every month is the memorial day of Seiyōin-sama and is therefore a government abstinence day. From now on, all killing of living beings is strictly prohibited, as has been
　　decreed, so everyone should understand this. The above.
　　　　　　　7th month, 12th day　　　To all 　Additionally to groups～　　From the Inspectors
　One regarding hiring female servants for samurai households: men have been serving as intermediaries, but henceforth men should be strictly prohibited from serving as intermediaries. In the future,
　　female intermediary lodgings will be designated in the towns, so when hiring female servants, applications should be made through these intermediary lodgings.

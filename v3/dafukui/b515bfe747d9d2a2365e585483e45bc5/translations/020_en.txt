【Left page】
Fifth year of Hōei, Year of the Rat
　○On the night of the 2nd of this month, a farmer named Kyuzaemon from Matsumoto Shimo[■]machi went out to patrol his fields, when someone appeared to be stealing melons and
　　eggplants that Kyuzaemon had cultivated in his field. When Kyuzaemon tried to catch this person, Kyuzaemon was
　　killed by cutting. Kyuzaemon had wounds in several places on his hands, none of which appeared to be from swords or short swords. The opponent also appears to have
　　sustained wounds, and blood was spilled on roads other than where the death occurred. Regarding this, there will be an investigation,
　　so each person should strictly examine their retainers and subordinates, and if there are any suspicious persons, they should immediately report
　　them. Those who provide lodging to suspicious persons or house them in tenements should submit written reports. If among those
　　persons there are any who went elsewhere after the 2nd of this month, their names should be written below and submitted.
　　The above　　8th month, 8th day　　To all　Additionally to groups～　　　　From the Inspectors
　　　Regarding the above, Atsumi Shinemon's office conducted various investigations but could not determine [the perpetrator]. Therefore, he requested to investigate samurai households, post stations, etc.
　　　Since Shinemon made this request, the matter was quickly discussed with colleagues, consulted with Shinemon, and communicated to Sakai Tenzen and Kawa[■]
　　　Saburonosuke. Immediately everyone discussed it, the directive was reviewed by the senior retainers, and today it was announced to the samurai households.

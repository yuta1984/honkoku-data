　　　　　　　12th month, 12th day　　To all　　　　　　　　From the Inspectors
First year of Hōei 7, Year of the Tiger
　○The inspection commissioners are scheduled to visit soon. Therefore, fire precautions should be even more strictly enforced during their passage through the domain. During their
　　stay in the castle town, firearms must not be fired. Also, when sending subordinate servants to rural areas, prior confirmation should be made to ensure they do not go in the direction of the commissioners' route. During their stay in the castle town, subordinates should not be sent to town carelessly. If they must be sent on business, all matters should be handled without rudeness or negligence, and if they encounter the inspection commissioners' retainers in town, they should be instructed to step aside and show deference. The area before and behind the lodgings is completely off-limits, with guards posted.

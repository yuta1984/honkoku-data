Proclamation issued before lord's arrival at castle
Oral instruction

○ When the lord arrives at the castle, if there are those from the samurai household down to the lowest ranks who go out to the town streets, they must be careful to avoid any improper behavior. Since the household staff will likely be reduced down to the lowest ranks, first priority should be given to careful attention to fire prevention before going out. Those of lower status should coordinate with neighbors to maintain vigilant watch over their homes and take care that there be no negligence with fire sources. The above matters should be strictly ordered to retainers down to the lowest ranks, so this intent should be understood. The above.

Fifth month　　　　　　　　　　　　　Magistrates

Additionally, those with group supervision should communicate the above matters.

Proclamation issued around the 23rd day of the sixth month

○ As the weather has continued and it is dry, each person should pay careful attention to fire prevention matters, so this intent should be understood. As this is the season for fumigating mosquitoes, strict orders should be given down to the lowest ranks. The above.

Proclamation issued around the 9th day of the seventh month【Red ink】"Like this since the Genroku era"

○ As in previous years, during the Bon festival, gate ceremonies, street dancing, street wrestling, lower ranks wandering around various places and towns without purpose, and generally people gathering in the castle town squares are forbidden. However, children thirteen years and under may join in dancing within their respective estates without mixing with others, joining groups of servants' children and such. For townspeople, within their own district, children thirteen years and under may dance a little without mixing with other districts. All else is prohibited, so this intent should be understood. The above.

Seventh month　　　　　　　　　　　　Magistrates
To all【Red ink】"It appears this was stopped from Kyōhō 1 (1716), year of the rooster"

Additionally, lower ranks should be ordered not to go to Mikuni and Kanazu【Red ink insertion】"In the Genroku era, to Shihi boundary" without permission. Generally, magistrates are stationed there, so this should be understood. Those with group supervision should communicate the above matters.

Proclamation issued before the lord's arrival at the castle
　　　　　　　Oral instruction
○ When the lord arrives at the castle, if there are those from the samurai households down to the lowest ranks who go out to the town streets, they must be careful to avoid any improper conduct. Since the household staff will likely be reduced down to the lowest ranks, first and foremost, careful attention should be paid to fire prevention when going out. Those of lower status should consult with neighbors to maintain vigilance in their households and be mindful that there be no negligence regarding fire sources. The above matters should be strictly ordered to retainers down to the lowest ranks, so this intention should be understood. The above.
　　　　Fifth month　　　　　　　　　　　　　Magistrates
　　Additionally, those with group supervision should communicate the above matters.

Proclamation issued around the 23rd day of the sixth month
○ As the weather has continued and conditions are very dry, each person should pay careful attention to fire prevention matters, so this intention should be understood. As this is the season for fumigating mosquitoes, strict orders should be given down to the lowest ranks. The above.

Proclamation issued around the 9th day of the seventh month【Red ink】"Like this since the Genroku era"
○ As every year, during the Bon period: gate standing, street dancing, street sumo wrestling, lower ranks wandering around various places and streets without purpose, and generally people gathering in the castle town squares are forbidden. However, children thirteen years old and younger dancing within their respective estates together with servants' children and such, without involving outsiders, is acceptable. For townspeople, children thirteen years old and younger dancing a little within their own town districts without involving other towns is acceptable. Everything else is prohibited, so this intention should be understood. The above.
　　　　Seventh month　　　　　　　　　　　　Magistrates
　　　　　　　To all【Red ink】"It appears this was stopped from Kyōhō 1 (1716), year of the rooster"
　　Additionally, those of lower ranks should be ordered not to go to Mikuni and Kanazu【Red ink insertion】"Around Genroku it was Shihi border" without permission and on their own. Generally, magistrates have been dispatched, so this should be kept in mind. Those with group supervision should communicate the above matters.

　○Lord Matsudaira Kaga-no-kami will be passing through this area in the near future. Accordingly, no one is to go out to watch the procession. Each party should understand this intent and strictly instruct those below them as well. The above.　4th month, 17th day　To all　　Additionally to groups～　Inspectors

　○It has been heard that night watchmen of the retinue households have been negligent, failing to make regular announcements of the time, and furthermore that there are those who carry no lanterns, yet there is no firm effort to detain such persons or send them back through the town—a most disorderly state of affairs. Henceforth, watchmen must strictly adhere to their duties, going out at the sixth hour of the evening and returning at the sixth hour of the morning. This is to be strictly enforced. The night patrol officers are also hereby instructed to conduct their inspections with renewed rigor. Each party should understand this intent and strictly instruct the night watchmen accordingly. The above.　5th month, 27th day　　To all　　　　Inspectors
　　　Additionally: It is heard that among the night watchmen, there are those of uncertain residence who have been employed as such. Henceforth, only those who are householders of reliable standing are to be employed as watchmen. The above.

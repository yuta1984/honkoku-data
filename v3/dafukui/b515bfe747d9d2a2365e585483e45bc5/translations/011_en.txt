Circular Proclamation (Yokoori)

　Following last year's firearm inspection, as this year's inspection is also being conducted, in cases where ronin placed in your employ possess firearms, you are to submit a certificate (tegata) with the attached draft document as per the separate enclosure. Please note that even in cases where there are no firearms in possession, a certificate must still be submitted. Please be duly informed of this requirement. The above.

　　　　　Sixth month, fifth day　　　　　　　　　　　《Split writing: Kenmochi Kyūemon｜Nara Sakon'emon》

　Additionally, certificates are to be submitted by the twenty-fifth of this month. Furthermore, this notice is to be communicated to all persons, down to the lowest members, under your respective unit commands. The above.

　　　　The above is a record from the fifth year of Shōtoku [1715], year of the Sheep.

The circular from around the Kōka era [1844–1848] is as follows. Note that the two draft certificate texts are identical to those recorded above.

　As the firearm inspection has been ordered as in previous years, in cases where ronin for whom you hold certificates possess firearms, you are to prepare and submit a certificate as per the attached draft document. Please note that even in cases where there are no firearms in possession, a certificate must still be submitted. The above.

　　　　　Third month, twenty-fifth day　　　　　　　　　《Split writing: Takae Tomoemon｜Ikoma Gozaemon》

　Additionally, certificates are to be prepared and submitted by the thirteenth of the fourth month. Furthermore, this notice is to be communicated to all persons down to the lowest members of your command.

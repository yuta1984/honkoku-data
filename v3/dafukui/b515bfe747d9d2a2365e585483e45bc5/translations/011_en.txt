Circular notice
　Last year firearms were inspected, and this year they will be inspected again. If any rōnin (masterless samurai) employed by each of you possesses firearms, submit certificates following the format of the attached draft. Note that certificates must be submitted even if no firearms are possessed. Please understand this directive. The above.
　　　　　June 5th　　　　　　　　　　　Tsurugi-mochi Kyūemon・Nara Sakon'emon
　Additionally, certificates must be submitted by the 25th of this month. Also, this must be communicated to all members under group jurisdiction. The above.

　　　　The above is a record from the 5th year of Shōtoku, year of the sheep (1715)

The circular notice from around the Kōka era (1844-1848) is as follows. Note that the two certificate draft texts are identical to those mentioned above
　As commanded, firearms inspection will be conducted as before. If any rōnin employed by each of you possesses firearms, prepare and submit certificates according to the attached draft text. Note that certificates must be submitted even if no firearms are possessed. The above.
　　　　　March 25th　　　　　　　　Takae Tomouemon・Ikoma Gozaemon
　Additionally, certificates must be prepared and submitted by April 13th. This must be communicated down to all subordinates under jurisdiction.

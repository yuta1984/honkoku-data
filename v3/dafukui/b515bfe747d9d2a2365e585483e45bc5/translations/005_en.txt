【Red ink】"From Genroku like this"

○ If there are those who go to serve in other household retainer groups, it is forbidden for intermediaries to serve as guarantors. Except for parents, children, and siblings, no matter what kind of relatives they may be, when they are placed elsewhere, not only should they not serve as guarantors, but they should not even serve as intermediaries. If there are unavoidable circumstances and they serve as guarantors for parents, children, or siblings, masters or those with authority should report to their (authorities) and act according to their instructions.

The above principles should be strictly communicated to retainers. Those with authority should communicate this even more thoroughly, so this intention should be understood. The above.

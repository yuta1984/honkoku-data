One: Kyōhō 4, Year of the Boar (1719)
　○Regarding the replacement of lower-ranking personnel this spring, as initially announced【marginal note】"This is a regular official notice," it has been heard recently that there are those who selfishly demand high wages regarding the new gold and silver currency,
　　which is extremely inappropriate. Regarding wages, whether in new gold or dry gold, they should be determined according to the wage standards initially announced,
　　with the master's consultation. If there are any who employ at high wages, the master will be held responsible for misconduct. Of course, if there are those who make
　　various complaints from below, the details should be reported to the inspectors. Those matters should be investigated and promptly addressed. The above.

　　　　　　　Memorandum
　One: Regarding employing household servants at high wages, this has been forbidden since long ago, but it is heard that there is a trend of demanding high wages year after year,
　　which is extremely inappropriate. Until now, due to the confusion in gold and silver currency, it was thus, but from next spring new gold will be in circulation, so like during the time of Keichō
　　gold circulation, servant wages should be set accordingly. However, since servants have been scarce in recent years, it has been left
　　as is. At next spring's replacement time, employ at the same wage rate as employed this spring of the boar year, calculated in dry gold equivalent. If employed at high wages contrary to the
　　prohibition, the master will be held responsible for misconduct, so understand this intent.
　One: Regarding the replacement of lower-ranking personnel, when they claim unavoidable business or withdraw into seclusion, currently hiding and causing servant shortages,
　　there are those who demand high wages. From this year's end, it has been ordered to the town magistrates and district magistrates that from next New Year, those who make claims to become merchants
　　or withdraw for rest should not be retained for more than about fifty days, even if they are parents, children, or siblings, as has been
　　announced.

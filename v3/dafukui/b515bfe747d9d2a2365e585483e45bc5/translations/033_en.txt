　　　　　　4th day of the 8th month

One: Kyōhō 7, Year of the Tiger (1722)

　◯Gambling has long been strictly prohibited. Furthermore, all those in charge of groups, as well as their retainers, are to be instructed that no activities even resembling such conduct are to take place. Group members are to make rounds, and in cases of concern, the master himself is to conduct an investigation. The intent of the above is to be strictly conveyed in advance. The above.　16th day of the 5th month　To all　Additionally to groups～　Inspectors

　◯When sending retainers and others to Kyoto, they are to be instructed under no circumstances to stay more than one night. Until a further notice is issued, the above rule is to be strictly observed. The above.　28th day of the 8th month　To all　Additionally to groups～　Inspectors
　　　[Note:] This is because a notice was issued throughout the city of Kyoto strictly enforcing the prohibition against armed persons staying more than one night within the capital, and Yoshizawa Kanzaemon's office informed the magistrates accordingly.

One: Kyōhō 8, Year of the Rabbit (1723)

　◯Since gambling has long been strictly prohibited, all activities resembling gambling that involve wagering—even of small amounts—including those done as amusement, are to be entirely refrained from. Should any such conduct come to light, an investigation will be conducted and appropriate penalties will certainly be imposed. Each party is to understand this and to strictly instruct their retainers to observe it as well. The above.　9th day of the 2nd month　To all　Additionally to groups～　Inspectors

【Left page】
　　　　　　Verbal Statement

　◯Today, the ladies-in-waiting are to be moved to the garden pond area under their care. From this time forward, when raising persons onto rooftops or other elevated areas, prior notice must be given to the officials in charge of attending the ladies-in-waiting, and their instructions are to be followed. It is to be strictly enforced that lower-ranking persons do not go up to elevated places without authorization. The above.

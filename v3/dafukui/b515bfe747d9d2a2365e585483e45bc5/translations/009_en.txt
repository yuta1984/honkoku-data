○ Regarding gambling and betting competitions, recently the shogunate has issued particularly strict orders, and proclamations have been issued repeatedly on this matter. It should be observed even more strictly, and if there are those who misunderstand, it will certainly be brought to official attention, so each person should order their retainers down to the lowest ranks without negligence. This intention should be understood. The above.

○ Regarding gambling and betting competitions, as there have been strict orders from the shogunate, the prohibition matters have been communicated repeatedly through proclamations and should be known by all, but somehow it does not stop, and particularly it seems there are those who think that small wagers in early spring and such are acceptable. Ultimately, this is most shameful for those who are samurai, so all gambling competitions should be strictly avoided. If anyone is seen or heard violating this, it should be reported immediately to the proper authorities. If one overlooks it and it becomes known later, one will be subject to the same official punishment as the perpetrator, so even small gambling matters should be strictly avoided, as has been【surrounded in red ink】"newly"【marginal note in red ink】"once again" ordered.

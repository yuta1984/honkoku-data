One: Kyōhō 9, Year of the Dragon (1724)

　[Horizontal fold note]

◯In recent years, servants have become scarce, and moreover they have begun to demand higher wages. Although repeated notices have been issued regarding this matter, the towns, villages, and administered territories have observed the instructions, and for the past year or two servants have been available at appropriate wages. Going forward, they are to be hired without offering high wages. Should any difficulties arise, this is to be reported to the monthly duty inspectors. If upon investigation a servant's misconduct is confirmed, official punishment will be administered. Each party is to understand this intent and promptly and firmly instruct their retainers accordingly. The above.
　　　　　　5th month, 28th day　　To all　Additionally to groups～　　Inspectors

【In red ink】"On the 11th day of the 1st month of the following year of the Snake, regarding the above notice, it was inquired of the senior councillors whether the intent of the notice should be communicated to those under the jurisdiction of the four district town magistrates with particular care, and it is recorded that this was duly reported."

　In addition to this, one further notice is issued today. Please also refer to the red-ink annotations in the standard notices.

◯Night watchmen are to make their time-calls without fail and without negligence, and those not carrying a lit lantern are not to be allowed to pass. It has been heard that in recent times there are those who go about without lanterns. In accordance with long-established rules, all retainers down to the lowest ranks are to be firmly instructed to carry lit lanterns and conduct their patrols from the fifth hour [approximately 8 p.m.]. Furthermore, as the night patrol members are expected to conduct inspections, each party is to be aware of this. The above.
　　　　　　5th month, 28th day　　To all　Additionally to groups～　　Inspectors

◯I write to inform you by letter. As a notice has been issued to the domain's retainer community today, it goes without saying that if there are any suspicious persons or those not carrying lit lanterns, I am to notify you that they are to be investigated with all due diligence. I write to inform you of this. The above.

　　　Furthermore, please also convey this to the night patrol companions. The above.

　　　　　　To: Matsudaira Gentayū　　　　　　　Ōtani Gizaemon

◯I write to inform you by letter. In your area, there are a considerable number of residences relocated from Matsuoka, so I am to inform you that all parties should confer together and post night watchmen. Please convey this to all concerned, and in accordance with the above, inform the residents of the new residences and ensure that night watchmen are posted. I write to inform you of this. The above.
　　　　　　5th month, 28th day

　　　　　　To: Murakami Kinai
　　　　　　To: Kuze Shōkichi　　　　　　　　　　Ōtani Gizaemon

　　　This was done, having consulted with the senior councillors, because there are a considerable number of households in the new residences at Sakura no Baba.

Kyōhō 10, Year of the Snake (1725)

◯This time, the shogunate is dispatching official inspectors [metsuke], Kōriki Heihachi and Mizoguchi Magozaemon, who are expected to visit the domain in the near future. In light of this, any areas around each retainer's residence that are damaged or unsightly are to be modestly repaired. There is no need to carry out full-scale restorations urgently, but places where walls, fences, or boards are crumbling or have fallen away are to be attended to as appropriately as possible. The above.

On the 15th of the same month, regarding the above matter, ascended to the castle and offered thanks for arrival in the capital, presented ceremonial sword, gold horse substitute, and one hundred bundles of cotton, then ascended to the Western Castle and presented ceremonial sword and gold horse substitute, and upon departure made courtesy visits to the senior councillors, chamberlains, and junior councillors

1. On the same day, regarding the above matter, sent an official letter to Kyoto Deputy Lord Matsudaira Kii-no-kami {{Nobuyasu}}

1. On the 18th of the same month, had an audience with Senior Councillor Lord Tsuchiya Sagami-no-kami {{Masanao}} and performed duties

1. On June 23rd, as there was a third memorial service for Lady Keishōin at Zōjōji Temple, messenger Inagaki Yasuemon {{Shigenobu}} was sent to present funeral offerings of two silver coins

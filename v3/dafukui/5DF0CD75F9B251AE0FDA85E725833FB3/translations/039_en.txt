One　　12th month, 21st day: For the celebration of being designated as adopted heir (goyōkun),
　　going in company with Lord Yoshishina, he went to the castle {{wearing court dress (hitatare)}}
　　The Shogun and Middle Counselor appeared, and he presented horse substitute money {{10 ryō of gold}} and offered thanks,
　　then withdrew
　　　　　However, regarding the sword and horse substitute money presented to the Middle Counselor, the messenger went to the Ni-no-maru and
　　　　　delivered them

One　　Same day: Elder Lord Tsuchiya Sagami-no-kami {{Masanao}} summoned the castle keeper Hori Jūbē {{Hisaritsu}}, who appeared.
　　Regarding the matter of presentation gifts when offering thanks for official appointment, concerning the inquiry
　　made by Lord Yoshishina on the 12th day, the following written document was handed over through a retainer:
[Left page]
　　　　　　　On the occasion of thanks for court rank
　　　　　　　　　　　　　From Matsudaira Ōi-no-kami
　　　To His Lordship (Ue-sama):
　　　　Ceremonial sword
　　　　Gold horse substitute money
　　　　Five court robes

　　　To the First Rank Lady (Ichii-sama):
　　　　Five pieces of white silver
　　　To Her Ladyship (Odai-sama):
　　　　Five pieces of white silver
　　　To the Middle Counselor:
　　　　Horse substitute money: ten pieces of white silver

[Right page]
1. On July 3rd, due to the summer heat, made courtesy visits to inquire about health to the duty officer Lord Inaba Tango-no-kami {{Masayuki}} and Lord Ogasawara Sado-no-kami {{Nagashige}}

1. On the 11th of the same month
　　For the celebration of the young lord's birth, ascended to the castle and had an audience with the senior councillors, then
　　ascended to the Western Castle. At that time, regarding the name,
　　there was an announcement from the senior councillors that he would be called Lord Ichiyo

1. On the 14th of the same month
　　To inquire about Lord Ichiyo's health, sent messenger Ōseki Shingozaemon {{Naoto}}, the castle keeper, to the Western Castle duty officer Lord Ogasawara Sado-no-kami {{Nagashige}}

[Left page]
1. On the 15th of the same month
　　To inquire about Lord Ichiyo's health, ascended to the castle and had an audience with the senior councillors

1. On the 18th of the same month
　　For the celebration of Lord Ichiyo's seventh night ceremony, due to general attendance, ascended to the castle and had an audience with the senior councillors, then ascended to the Western Castle

1. On the same day, regarding the above matter, through messenger Nagano Hachidayū
　　presented to the Shōgun one box of dried sea bream and one thousand mon as sake money {{written instruction given on the 13th, same below}}. At the Great Hall of the castle,
　　delivered the gifts together with the catalog to the retainer of Lord Miura Iki-no-kami {{Akitaka}}, the master of ceremonies
　　To the Great Counselor, through messenger Hayakawa Sanzaemon, one thousand mon for one item

　　Audience　From service duties to Tsuchiya Sagami-no-kami Masanao-dono　Service duties (accompanied by Tokuyama Gohei-dono)

1. 12th month, 22nd day - A message came from Tokuyama Gohei Shigenari-dono that Ogino Magoemon Masafū appeared, regarding
　　The matter of monthly castle attendance:
　　Should a petition be made by Masaaki-sama?
　　Or should a petition be made by Masanao-sama? When Gohei-dono inquired about this to Rōjū Tsuchiya Sagami-no-kami Masanao-dono,
　　[the response was that] if submitted from the domain, it would cause delays, so
　　it would be appropriate for Masanao-sama to make the petition. Gohei-dono conveyed this instruction.
　　　　However, regarding the above, when inquiring to the castle caretaker through Gohei-dono that the petition should be made after the ceremony of gratitude for court rank appointment was completed, Sagami-no-kami-dono later sent word that
[Left page]
　　　　　Murakami Sandayū appeared and [reported that] regarding matters concerning those of Fourth Court Rank and above,
　　　　　since these are brought to the Shogun's attention, it would be appropriate to make the petition promptly. This was conveyed through yōnin Kaneko Okaemon.

1. Same month, 27th day - Through Tokuyama Gohei Shigenari-dono, the following petition was submitted to goyōban Akimoto Tajima-no-kami Takatomo-dono:
　　　　I am deeply grateful that court rank has been bestowed upon me. Hereafter, I
　　　　humbly request to be allowed to pay monthly respects. The above.
　　　　　12th month, 27th day　　　Matsudaira Ōi-no-kami

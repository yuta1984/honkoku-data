1. On October 16th, Princess Ume had been suffering from measles for some time, but upon her recovery there was a celebration with sake and hot water baths

1. On November 15th, declined going to the castle due to illness

1. On the 19th of the same month, Princess Matsu's marriage ceremony to Lord Matsudaira Wakasa-no-kami {{Yoshinori}} was completed on the 18th yesterday, and although all retainers were to attend for celebration, declined going to the castle due to measles, and therefore sent messenger officials to duty officer Lord Akimoto Tajima-no-kami {{Takatomo}} and Western Castle duty officer Lord Honda Hōki-no-kami {{Masanaga}} for the celebration

1. On the same day, for the same reason as above, sent messenger official Kuzumaki Jibu-uemon {{Kanenori}} to present one box of dried sea bream, one box of kelp, and 500 hiki as sake money to the Shogun
[Left page]
　　At the main castle, the gifts and their inventory were handed over to the retainer of Lord Torii Harima-no-kami {{Tadamasa}}, the presentation officer. To the Shogun's wife, sent messenger official Hiki Sazaemon {{Eisaku}} to present one box of dried sea bream and 500 hiki as sake money, and at the Ninomaru gate guard station, the gifts and inventory were handed over to Lord Harima-no-kami's retainer

1. On the 22nd of the same month, there was a celebration with sake and hot water baths due to recovery from measles

1. On the 28th of the same month, declined going to the castle as recovery from measles was not yet complete
　　　　　　However, resumed going to the castle from December 1st

1. On December 5th, for inquiring after well-being during the cold season
　　accompanied Lord Yoshishina to attend to duties with duty officer Lord Tsuchiya Sagami-no-kami {{Masanao}}

1. On October 16th, Princess Ume had been suffering from measles for some time, but upon her recovery, there was a celebration with sake and hot water

1. On November 15th, due to indisposition, declined attendance at the castle

1. On the 19th of the same month, Princess Matsu's wedding procession to Lord Matsudaira Wakasa-no-kami {{Yoshinori}} was completed yesterday on the 18th. Though all retainers were to attend for celebration, due to measles, castle attendance was declined. Therefore, envoys from the messenger corps were sent to duty officer Lord Akimoto Tajima-no-kami {{Takatomo}} and Western Castle duty officer Lord Honda Hōki-no-kami {{Masanaga}} for congratulations

1. On the same day, for the same reason, through envoy messenger Kuzumaki Jibu-uemon {{Tsunenori}},
　　presented to the Shogun one box of dried sea bream, one box of kelp, and 500 mon as sake money

[Left page]
　　At the main castle, the gifts and their catalog were handed over to a retainer of Lord Torii Harima-no-kami {{Tadamasa}}, the chamberlain. To the Shogun's wife, through envoy messenger Hiki Sazaemon {{Eisetsu}}, presented one box of dried sea bream and 500 mon as sake money, and at the Ninomaru gate guardhouse, the gifts and catalog were handed over to a retainer of Lord Harima-no-kami

1. On the 22nd of the same month, due to recovery from measles, there was a celebration with sake and hot water

1. On the 28th of the same month, still not fully recovered after measles, declined castle attendance
　　　　　　However, castle attendance will resume from December 1st

1. On December 5th, for inquiring after well-being during the cold season,
　　went together with Lord Yoshishina to attend duties with duty officer Lord Tsuchiya Sagami-no-kami {{Masanao}}

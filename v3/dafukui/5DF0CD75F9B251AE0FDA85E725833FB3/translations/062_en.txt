[Right page]
1. On June 6th, for inquiring after His Majesty's well-being during the hot season,
　　went together with Lord Yoshishina to attend duties with duty officer Lord Inoue Kawachi-no-kami {{Masamine}}

1. On the 7th of the same month, for the same reason,
　　for inquiring after the Grand Councilor's well-being,
　　went together with Lord Yoshishina to attend duties with Western Castle duty officer Lord Ogasawara Sado-no-kami {{Nagashige}}

1. On the 16th of the same month, for the celebration of Katei, went to the castle and received confections as usual

1. On the 26th of the same month, the engagement between Princess Take and Matsudaira Kuchiyo {{Masakuni}} {{eldest son of Lord Higo-no-kami Masayuki}} was announced, and for celebration all retainers were summoned, went to the castle and had an audience with the senior councilors

1. On August 1st, for celebration went to the castle and presented a sword and horse substitute offering as usual

[Left page]
1. On the 9th of the same month,
　　went together with Lord Yoshishina to meet with duty officer Lord Tsuchiya Sagami-no-kami {{Masanao}} and attended duties

1. On the 28th of the same month, Princess Ume departed from Fukui via the Nakasendo route to Edo {{accompanied by senior retainer Matsudaira Shōbei}}

1. On September 13th, Princess Ume arrived at the Reiganjima residence and preparations for the wedding were completed
{{The waiting court lady was the daughter of Lord Mukai Shōgen}}

1. On the 14th of the same month, as the wedding ceremony was completed,
　　went together with Lord Yoshishina to attend duties with duty officers Lord Ōkubo Kaga-no-kami {{Tadamasu}} and Lord Tsuchiya Sagami-no-kami {{Masanao}}

1. On the 28th of the same month, went to the castle to offer congratulations for the marriage
　　presented ten kosode garments to the Shogun
　　presented twenty pieces of silver to the Grand Councilor

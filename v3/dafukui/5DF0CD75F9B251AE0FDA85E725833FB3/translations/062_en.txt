1. On June 6th, for inquiring after well-being during the summer heat
　　accompanied Lord Yoshishina to attend to duties with duty officer Lord Inoue Kawachi-no-kami {{Masamine}}

1. On the 7th of the same month, for the same reason as above
　　for inquiring after the Great Counselor's well-being
　　accompanied Lord Yoshishina to attend to duties with Western Castle duty officer Lord Ogasawara Sado-no-kami {{Nagashige}}

1. On the 16th of the same month, for the celebration of Katei, went to the castle and received sweets as usual

1. On the 26th of the same month, the engagement between Princess Take and Lord Matsudaira Kuchiyo {{Masakuní}} {{eldest son of Lord Higo-no-kami Masayuki}} was announced, and for celebration all retainers were summoned, went to the castle and had an audience with the senior councilors

1. On August 1st, for celebration went to the castle and presented ceremonial swords and horse substitutes as usual
[Left page]
1. On the 9th of the same month
　　accompanied Lord Yoshishina to meet with duty officer Lord Tsuchiya Sagami-no-kami {{Masanao}} and attended to duties

1. On the 28th of the same month, Princess Ume departed from Fukui and proceeded to Edo via the Nakasendo route {{accompanied by senior retainer Matsudaira Shōbei}}

1. On September 13th, Princess Ume arrived at the Reiganjima residence and preparations for the marriage were completed
{{The waiting lady-in-waiting was the daughter of Lord Mukai Mochitomi}}

1. On the 14th of the same month, after the wedding ceremony was completed
　　accompanied Lord Yoshishina to attend to duties with duty officer Lord Ōkubo Kaga-no-kami {{Tadamasu}} and Lord Tsuchiya Sagami-no-kami {{Masanao}}

1. On the 28th of the same month, went to the castle to offer thanks for the marriage
　　presented ten small-sleeved garments to the Shogun
　　presented twenty pieces of silver to the Great Counselor

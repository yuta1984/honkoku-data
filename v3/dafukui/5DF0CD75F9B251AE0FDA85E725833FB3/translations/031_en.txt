One　　Eighth month, 4th day: As Lady Kiku, wife of Lord Suwa Aki-no-kami {{Tadatora}}, passed away and she was a younger sister, on the 10th day informed through a written document via the caretaker retainer to duty officer Lord Tsuchiya Sagami-no-kami {{Masanao}} that forty-five days of mourning would be observed

One　　Tenth month, 12th day: Had an audience with duty officer Lord Inaba Tango-no-kami {{Masatoshi}} and performed duties

One　　Same month, 20th day: Due to His Excellency Ichii being unwell, went to Elders Akimoto Tajima-no-kami {{Takatomo}} and Honjō Aki-no-kami {{Munesuke}} to inquire after his well-being

One　　Same month, 23rd day: Due to a great earthquake, went to duty officer Lord Ogasawara Sado-no-kami {{Nagashige}} to inquire after well-being. Went to Elder Akimoto Tajima-no-kami {{Takatomo}} to inquire after the well-being of His Excellency Ichii

For this celebration, to Daijō Dainagon Nishinishi {{Kunitoyo}}: one ceremonial sword, one horse {{substitute: ten gold ryō}}, three layers of court robes, one box of kelp, one box of dried sea bream, one box of dried squid, two loads of sake barrels
   To Lord Sakumaru: ceremonial sword and horse substitute {{ten gold ryō}}, five rolls of saya silk, one box of kelp, one box of dried sea bream, one load of sake barrels

【Right Page】
　　　　　　Regulations for Wedding Ceremonies
　　　　1. 5,000 koku and above:
　　　　　1. Ceremonial tools: black lacquered with maki-e decoration
　　　　　1. Attendants' palanquins: no more than 5 required
　　　　　1. Long storage chests: no more than 10 required; if oil cloth covers are used, they should be cotton
　　　　　1. Wicker baskets and insertion boxes should correspond to the above ceremonial tools
　　　　　1. Celebration rice cakes: 3 loads in ceremonial containers
　　　　1. From 4,900 to 3,000 koku:
　　　　　1. Ceremonial tools: same as above

【Left Page】
　　　　　1. Attendants' palanquins: no more than 3 required
　　　　　1. Long storage chests: no more than 8 required; oil cloth covers same as above
　　　　　1. Wicker baskets and insertion boxes: same as above
　　　　　1. Celebration rice cakes: 2 loads in ceremonial containers
　　　　1. From 2,900 to 1,000 koku:
　　　　　1. Ceremonial tools: same as above
　　　　　1. Attendants' palanquins: no more than 2 required
　　　　　1. Long storage chests: no more than 6 required; no oil cloth covers
　　　　　1. Wicker baskets and insertion boxes: same as above

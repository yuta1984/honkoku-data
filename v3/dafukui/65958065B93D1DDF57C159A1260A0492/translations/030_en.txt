　　　　　　To Matsudaira Echizen-no-kami
1. November 21st:
　　Since the Shogun's cough has been cured, a letter was submitted through a messenger
　　from the falconry grounds, and today the reply letter was delivered.
　　　　　　I have respectfully read your letter.
　　　　　　The Shogun's cough has quickly been cured, and he is in increasingly good spirits
　　　　　　and good health, this was communicated, and I understand this auspicious news.

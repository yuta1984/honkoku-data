【Right Page】
　　　　　1. Among all guard units, the number of assistant guards shall not be used to fulfill regular guard duties.
　　　　　　Manji 3rd year, 2nd month, [day not specified]

1. 8th day of the 3rd month: Previously,
　　it was ordered that a stone pagoda for Lord Ryūhōin be constructed at Daian-ji Temple.
　　Today a memorial service was held upon its completion.

1. Same day: To Daian-ji Temple, from among Tanya Village, South Narahara Village, North Narahara Village, Shijūtani Village,
　　Fukatani Village, Ōtoshi Village, and Kakitani Village in Sakai District, temple lands of 300 koku were donated.

1. Same month: At the site where Nitta Yoshisada died, on the ground at Fukuman Village in Yoshida District (marginal note: Azana Mukōda),
　　a stone monument was erected.

【Left Page】
　　　　　Front of stone monument:
　　　　　　　　Ryakuō 1st year, intercalary 7th month, 2nd day
　　　　　　Nitta Yoshisada died in battle at this place
　　　　　Back side:
　　　　　　Built in the 3rd year of Manji, year of the Metal Rat, spring, 3rd month
　　　　　　From the time of death until this year: 325 years

　　　　　However, from Ryakuō 1st year to Manji 3rd year is 323 years. The inscription of 325 years
　　　　　　here may be an error in writing.

1. Same month (marginal note: day unknown): Departed from Fukui for sankin service.

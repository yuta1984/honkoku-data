1. Regarding absence from guard duty: For the main castle keep, at Kawara Gate, Taiko Gate, Geba Gate, and Kitte Gate - all those encountered outside Hoate Gate shall be considered absent. Those inside Hoate should not be considered absent.
　　　Additionally, when the chief officers have urgent business requiring consultation, they may proceed from Geba Gate to Taiko. Those who exit from inner gates to outer gates shall be considered absent. Inspections should also be conducted progressively from the main castle keep.

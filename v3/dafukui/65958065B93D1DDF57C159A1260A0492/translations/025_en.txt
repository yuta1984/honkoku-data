【Right Page】
1. April 11th: Arrived in Edo from lodging at Kanagawa station
1. Same month, 12th: Regarding the arrival in Edo, the envoy Senior Councilor Inaba Mino-no-kami Masanori came
   The shogun's intentions were conveyed. As thanks for this, [the lord] paid respects to Mino-no-kami, and to the remaining senior councilors
   sent the retainer Aruga Koemon Masanari as messenger
1. Same month, 14th: A twisted-seal official letter came from the duty councilor Inaba Mino-no-kami Masanori
　　　　　　　Address on the twisted-seal official letter
　　　　　　　　　To Matsudaira Echizen-no-kami　　　　　　　　Masanori
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Endorsement
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Inaba Mino-no-kami
　　　　　　　　Tomorrow the 15th, there will be formal thanks for sankin
　　　　　　　　so castle attendance around 4 o'clock would be most appropriate

【Left Page】
　　　　　　　　Humbly and respectfully submitted
　　　　　　　　　　　April 14th　　　　　　Masanori (signature seal)
1. Same month, 15th (5:30): Attended the castle to offer formal thanks for sankin attendance, presented a sword, gold horse-money,
   and seasonal clothing as offerings, and after the presentation was completed and withdrawal made, the accompanying retainers
   were granted audience, and upon departure, made courtesy visits to the senior councilors
1. Same month, 28th: Through envoy Nose Jizaemon Yorimune, received as gifts hawks' rails (2 large rails,
   3 small rails). As thanks for this, attended the castle and sent messengers
   (castle keepers and liaison officers) to the senior councilors and junior councilors
1. May 1st: Great earthquake throughout the provinces

　　　　Note: Regarding the armored rice cake celebration
　　　　　During Lord Tadamasa's reign, celebrations were held on January 20th, starting with the shogunate,
　　　　　but because it coincided with Lord Taiyūin's death anniversary, from Jōō 1 (1652) the shogunate's celebration was moved to the 11th,
　　　　　so from that time our domain also changed the date, though details up to the current year are unclear

Genroku 12th year (1699)
　　One: First month - Having been unwell since the twelfth month of the previous year, the physicians prepared medicine which he took, but
　　　　on the night of the 23rd he suffered abdominal pain and severe agony, so surgery was quickly performed, however
　　　　his overall condition remained poor and he became exhausted, so the physician
　　　　Elder Nagashima Dōsen was consulted and medicine was administered.
　　One: On the 7th day of the second month, his illness gradually worsened.
　　One: On the 11th day of the same month at the hour of the Dragon (around 8 AM), he passed away at the Edo Torigoe residence (at the age of 39).

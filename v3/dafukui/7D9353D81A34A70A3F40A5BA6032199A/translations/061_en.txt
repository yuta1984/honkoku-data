1. Second month: The castle town map was completed
　　　However, the order for this had been issued in the autumn of last year
1. Third month, 12th day: At Zuigenji temple, from the evening of the 10th until this morning (two nights and three days),
　　　the initial memorial service for Shōkei-in [Yoshishina's wife, Princess Man] was conducted
1. Same month, 24th day: At Jōkō-in, from the 20th until today (five days and nights),
　　　the 13th anniversary memorial service for Daian-in was conducted. At Daianji temple as well,
　　　a simplified service was conducted

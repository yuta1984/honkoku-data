A written directive was issued, and copies were later posted at public notice boards throughout the domain.

Articles:
1. Regarding the sale of poison and counterfeit medicines, these are strictly prohibited.
Anyone engaging in such commerce shall be punished. Even if they are accomplices, those who report such activities will certainly receive rewards.

1. The buying and selling of counterfeit gold and silver shall be completely prohibited. If such items are brought forward, money changers shall destroy them and return [the materials] to their owners. Additionally, debased gold and silver, and counterfeit gold and silver shall be sent to the gold and silver mints for inspection.
Note: Counterfeit items must not be manufactured at all.

1. Regarding Kan'ei new coins, one ryō of gold equals 4,000 mon, and naturally one bu equals 1,000 mon. For both shogunal and private domains, these shall be the established rates for tax collection and other purposes.

1. Regarding new coins, they shall not be minted anywhere without official permission. Those who violate this shall be punished.
Note: Bad coins, imitation coins, old coins, and others must not be sorted [for preferential use].

1. Unreliable newly-made goods shall not be sold.

1. In the commerce of various goods, one must not buy and hoard in one location for speculation, nor conspire to raise prices.

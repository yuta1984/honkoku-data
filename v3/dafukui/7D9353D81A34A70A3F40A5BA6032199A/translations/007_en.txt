      　　　　　　One　Regarding messengers sent with official seals for purchases and such, when those claiming to be retainers come
　　　　　　　　bearing seal impressions, after comparing with the official seals and confirming there are no discrepancies, their business may be conducted. Since there may be imposters, do not conduct business for those without seal impressions.
　　　　　　　　Now, should the official seals be lost, punishment shall not be
　　　　　　　　imposed on that person without prior notice
　　　　　　　　After notification, there shall be consultation. The aforementioned seal impressions shall
　　　　　　　　be retrieved in Edo after the official inspection
　　　　　　One　In lodging houses and rest stops during the day, no women, even elderly ones, shall be placed inside

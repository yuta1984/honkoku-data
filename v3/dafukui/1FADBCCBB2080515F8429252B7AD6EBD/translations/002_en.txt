1. Regarding fields and farmland, even if there should be any discrepancy in the land measurements, discrepancies of ten steps or less shall not be handled. For discrepancies of ten steps or more, anything above ten steps shall be handled, and this is hereby decided.

1. Regarding the lottery grouping of hemp fields and farmland without residents, all villagers shall gather and until the lottery grouping is completed and lots are drawn, no one shall report any minor good or bad matters to the village authorities, even if there are any. Even if someone reports such matters due to a misunderstanding, they shall not be handled, and this is hereby decided.

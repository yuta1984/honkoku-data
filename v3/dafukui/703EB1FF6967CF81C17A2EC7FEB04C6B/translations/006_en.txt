1. On the 11th day of the 3rd month, a fire broke out in the kitchen quarters of Eiheiji temple (on the 9th), and the entire temple compound burned down completely, so as condolences, fifty pieces of silver and one roll of kelp from Shakai were sent through messenger and guard officer Kuzumaki Jibuemon Houkaku

1. On the 13th day of the 3rd month, there was a banquet at the main castle in response to an invitation from Nakatsukasa-no-taifu Shōhei-sama

1. On the same day, since the traveling monk Yugyō Shōnin had been staying at Jōkyūji temple in Aburamachi since the 10th, two hundred candles and one roll of kelp from Shakai were sent through messenger and guard officer Ichimura Kanemon Kentsū

1. On the 16th day of the 3rd month, [the lord] went out to Matsuoka in response to an invitation from Nakatsukasa-no-taifu Shōhei-sama

1. On the 17th day of the 3rd month, since Yugyō Shōnin would depart soon, ten pieces of silver were sent through messenger and guard officer Yamamoto Emonhachi

1. On the 21st day of the 3rd month, [the lord] departed from Fukui and traveled via the Tōkaidō road

1. On the same day, Chikōin-sama, who had been residing at the Tokiwabashi mansion, moved to the Reiganjima mansion

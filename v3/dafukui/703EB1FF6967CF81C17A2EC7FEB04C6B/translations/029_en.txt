5th Month, 21st Day    Matsudaira Ōi-no-kami

Oral Statement

Regarding the daughter of the late Echizen-no-kami mentioned in the main document: In previous years, her late father Hyōbu-dayū petitioned to send her as an adopted daughter to the Asuka-i Chūnagon household for a marriage arrangement with Lord Kazanin Dainagon. However, before the wedding ceremony, Lord Dainagon fell ill, so the Asuka-i Chūnagon household declined the marriage arrangement. Thereafter, regarding whether Hyōbu-dayū should petition each time this daughter's marriage was arranged, we consulted Lord Tsuchiya Sagami-no-kami through Tokuyama Matahē and Ōoka Jiuemon. We were informed that since the adoption petition to the Asuka-i Chūnagon household had already been completed once, there was no need to petition again, and we should simply report when a marriage arrangement was decided.

Regarding the exchange of various gold and silver coins from the Genroku era onward with the new gold and new silver decreed this time: Since this will take years to complete, exchange offices have been established in three locations—Edo, Kyoto, and Osaka—to facilitate gradual exchange according to the convenience of various provinces and localities. These offices will conduct direct transactions with those seeking to exchange, following the established rates without procedural complications.

　　Addendum: If there are those who, due to their circumstances, wish to exchange the new gold and silver for the previously circulating gold and silver, this too shall be permitted according to their wishes, following the established exchange rates.

1. Regarding the gold and silver decreed this time: Since their quality is the same as the old gold and old silver that circulated from the Keichō period until Genroku 7, there is no need to exchange the old gold and silver from before Genroku 7, and they should continue to circulate permanently in combination with the new gold and silver.

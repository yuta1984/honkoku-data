If one captures them as described in the aforementioned manner, the initial crime of conspiracy shall be pardoned, and the reward shall be double the agreed-upon payment for borrowing the ship.

Addendum:
Regarding the ship matter, even if the ship owner and captain lend it through mutual agreement, if a sailor through his efforts captures those who purchase smuggled goods and the conspiring captains, the reward given to that sailor shall also be double the payment agreed upon with the ship owner and captain when borrowing the ship.

Article: If there are those in coastal areas of various provinces who report the existence of people purchasing smuggled goods, the local people must immediately gather and capture them. If they are negligent and allow them to escape, they shall be strictly punished.

The above articles must be strictly observed.

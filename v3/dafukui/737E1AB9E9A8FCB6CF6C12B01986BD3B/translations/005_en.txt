Inspections shall be conducted at the lord's quarters gate. However, suspicious matters shall be examined thoroughly before allowing passage. Generally, lower-ranking persons wearing hoods or red cotton caps shall be prohibited from passing through.
　　Addition: Fugitives, mentally disturbed persons, and those intoxicated shall not be admitted. Stray horses entering or leaving shall be detained.

1. Needless to say, people from other domains, as well as those from public lands and other territories, regardless of whether they are monks, laypeople, men, or women, shall not be allowed to pass. Even those from our domain who have no business shall not be admitted.
　　Addition: If messengers or couriers to the lord's quarters arrive at the Geba gate without proper notification, they shall be detained and the matter reported to the duty chamberlains for their instructions.
　However, messengers from Matsuoka, couriers on official business from there, and retainers coming to inquire about his lordship's health may pass. Those going to the official residence or castle deputy's residence, whether from public lands, other territories, or even from Matsuoka, shall be detained, the matter reported to those residences, and passage allowed only upon approval from within.

1. When lower-ranking persons and even the lowest-ranking men and women of the household leave the castle by palanquin or vehicle due to illness or injury, their supervisor must receive permission from their master and also inform the inspectors. If it is confirmed that they are being transported by palanquin or vehicle, this shall be acknowledged and passage allowed after observing the situation.

1. When artisans, day laborers, and people from the town and countryside come calling, and such cases arise, they shall be inspected according to the same principles as above and allowed to pass.

1. For palanquins and vehicles leaving from within, regardless of whether the occupants are men or women of high or low rank, permission must be received from the appropriate authority before passage is allowed. However, if anything appears suspicious, the interior of the vehicle shall be inspected before allowing passage.

1. Due to the possibility of unexpected incidents such as fights or arguments within the castle, not only lower-ranking persons but even retainers entering or leaving shall not be allowed to pass carelessly, and the instructions of the inspectors must be followed.

1. After the gates are closed at night, for entry and exit to the lord's quarters, whether for official or private business, from retainers to the lowest-ranking persons, all must be cautioned and passage allowed only with direct permission from the gate guards or written authorization. Even if they claim to have been summoned or to be going on official business, if there is no prior authorization from the gate guards, the matter must be reported to the gate guards and passage allowed only after receiving permission. Furthermore, when there is urgent business from the main bailey, lord's quarters, or the Nanatsu warehouse guards' quarters requiring communication with the guard post, messengers from those quarters shall be detained at the gate, the matter communicated to the guard post, and passage allowed only after the guard post's examination and authorization.

1. Generally, after the gates are closed, even those who entered with permission must present written authorization from the gate guards when leaving.
　　Addition: For those with authorization from the inspectors, entry and exit after gate closure does not require authorization from the gate guards.

1. After gate closure, when the following officials pass through on official business: senior councilors, administrators, caretakers, chamberlains, pages' supervisors, Suzuki Chūemon, magistrates, and inspectors - both entry and exit do not require authorization from the gate guards.

　　Addition: When the above-mentioned officials send people to the main bailey or lord's quarters on official business during the night, this shall be acknowledged and escorts from the various gate units shall be assigned to accompany them.

1. After gate closure, for entry and exit from the official residence and castle deputy's residence, authorization from the gate guards is not required. For the official residence, permission from their administrator suffices; for the castle deputy's residence, permission from the master suffices.

1. When attendants for falconry, river hunting, and other activities depart before dawn, the gates may be opened one hour before the official announcement.

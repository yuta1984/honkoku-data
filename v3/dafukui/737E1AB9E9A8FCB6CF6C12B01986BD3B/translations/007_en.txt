1. Hoei 2, Year of the Rooster, 9th month, 19th day: Geba drum permits for three gate locations, posted notices, various additions and revisions made to drafts for review. Three copies of these regulations were completed and submitted through the foot inspectors on the 3rd day of the 10th month. The old regulation documents were placed in the official document box.

●
△1. During night duty hours, after night duty ends, those exiting from inside who wish to pass through the gate must obtain a permit from the gate inspectors. In cases of unavoidable urgent business, they may pass with authorization from the gate inspectors. When there is urgent business from guard personnel quarters requiring communication with the main bailey lord's quarters, the person concerned, their relatives, or neighbors may add their seals to a written statement of the business and submit it. This matter should also be communicated to the main bailey lord's quarters, and passage should be allowed after examination by the gate inspectors.
　　Addition: Generally, when the gates are closed, even those entering from outside with authorization must obtain permits from the gate inspectors when departing.

●
△1. When the lord goes hawking at night, his retinue may pass through one hour before the official announcement. However, when departing at the sixth hour of dawn, they may pass from the seventh hour.

●1. When senior councilors, administrators, external caretakers, ceremonial officials, and page supervisors visit the lord's quarters at night, upon passing through the Geba Gate, group members shall be dispatched to the lord's quarters gate in advance to inform the guard personnel. When visiting the main bailey at night, all gates shall be notified in advance. When visiting, authorization from gate inspectors is not required. When senior councilors, castle deputies, administrators, both caretakers, ceremonial officials, senior guard commanders, and page supervisors send personnel to the main bailey lord's quarters on business, the nature of the business shall be acknowledged and confirmed, group members shall accompany them, and they shall be allowed to pass through all gates.

●
△1. Messengers and couriers from other places at night shall be kept outside the gates, reported inside, and handled according to the instructions of the ceremonial officials.

●　Ihara Genbei may pass through at any time. Generally, regarding gate entry and exit matters, they shall be acknowledged and allowed to pass as in previous times, case by case.

△1. Regardless of the time, those with authorization from the inspectors may pass through. The inspectors may enter and exit the gates at any time without requiring authorization from the gate inspectors. Generally, regarding gate entry and exit matters, they shall be acknowledged and allowed to pass according to their status.

The following three articles were added in Kyoho 4, Year of the Boar, 6th month

1. Should people from other domains or territories arrive at the Geba Gate without prior arrangement, they shall be detained. For messengers and couriers to the lord's quarters, this shall be reported to the duty ceremonial officials and their instructions followed. For those visiting official residences or the castle deputy's residence, this shall be reported to those residences, and they may pass according to internal authorization procedures. Generally, people from public domains or other territories, whether servants, townspeople, or farmers, shall absolutely not be allowed to pass. If there are unavoidable circumstances, this shall be reported to the relevant residence, authorization obtained, and then passage allowed. Even for people from the domain's own territory, if they have no business, they shall not be allowed to pass.
　　Addition: For messengers from Matsuoka, official couriers, and those coming to inquire about the lord's health among the inquiry attendants, they may pass as before.
　　　For those visiting official residences or the castle deputy's residence, they shall be detained, the matter reported to those residences, and passage allowed upon receiving authorization.

1. When lower-ranking personnel within the castle and lower-ranking retainers use palanquins or vehicles for gate entry and exit due to illness, their supervisors or masters must provide authorization, and their condition must be observed before allowing passage. When townspeople or farmers arrive and claim they cannot travel on foot due to sudden illness and must use palanquins or vehicles, this shall be reported to the inspectors to confirm if this follows their instructions, and passage shall be allowed after observing and verifying their condition.

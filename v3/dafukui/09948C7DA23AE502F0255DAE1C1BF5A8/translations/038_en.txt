- December 8th: Regarding the aforementioned matter, since Nagashima Dōsen was to depart from Edo, Ōgoban Sakata Yohē (Nagaharu) and two ashigaru were assigned as escorts.

- Same month, 10th: For the cold season, the first-grade fresh cod from the domain's products was presented as customary.
  Note: In years when the lord was residing in the domain, it was customary to present these with a written letter, but due to his illness this time, it was difficult to affix his seal, so no letter was included.

- Same month, 11th: As his condition remained unchanged with no particular improvement, the medicine from Arima Ryōkyū was changed to that of town physician Yamazaki Dōsen.
  Note: Due to this change, Ryōkyū departed from Fukui on the following 12th.

- Same month, 12th: For the cold season, one type of fish was presented through messenger Hiki Sazaemon (Ei-[character unclear]) to inquire after his health.
  Note: Sazaemon had departed from the domain on the first of last month.
  Due to his illness this time, it was difficult to affix his seal, so no written letter was submitted.

- Same month, 14th: For the cold season, the second-grade fresh cod from domain products was presented as customary.
  Note: As with the first-grade fresh cod, no written letter was included.

- Same month, 15th: For the presentation of the Chōyō inner letter and as thanks for the retainers' bestowed gifts, messenger Fujita Sōdayū (Shigeken) was dispatched to Edo, departing from Fukui today.
  Note: Although a written letter should have been submitted, due to his indisposition making it difficult to affix his seal, a messenger was dispatched instead.

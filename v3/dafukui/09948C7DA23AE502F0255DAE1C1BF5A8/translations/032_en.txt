To Abe Bungo-no-kami-sama
　　　　　　　　　　To Toda Yamashiro-no-kami-sama
　　　　　　　　　　To Tsuchiya Sagami-no-kami-sama

　　　　　　1. As I have no natural son, I wish to request adoption
　　　　　　1. I am grateful for the repeated generous favors bestowed upon me. Though it is presumptuous for me to request adoption on my own initiative, I would like to ask that a suitable adoptee be appointed. However, this may cause trouble for those above
　　　　　　1. I currently have adopted a daughter from before my illness in Echizen, who is now nine years old. Should I request a son-in-law adoption?

　　　　　　Please discuss the above matters in confidence
　　　　　　　　　　　The above

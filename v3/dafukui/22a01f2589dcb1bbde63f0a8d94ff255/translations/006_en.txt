　　　　　△　Ikuro

Summoned during Lord Tadamasa's reign, year unknown
●Tanaka Genbei Shigetsugu 《Home province: Omi, Birth province: Echigo | Original name: Ikuro Genbei》
His ancestor, Ikuro Genzaemon Shigenobu, lived for generations in a place called Ikuro in Omi Province. When Shigenobu was young, the Ikuro residence was destroyed in warfare, and he fled with the care of his retainers. Later he came to serve Murakami Suo-no-kami Yorikatsu in Echigo Province. There were official documents from that time, but they were lost in a fire in Edo in previous years.
His son, Tanaka Genbei Shigetsugu, also served the Murakami house. During the Siege of Osaka, he went to battle at age seventeen as his father Shigenobu's representative. Since Lord Tadamasa thought the surname Ikuro was inauspicious, he ordered it to be changed, so it was changed to Tanaka. Later, he became an attendant to Lord Yoshishina and served in Yoshie, then changed back to his original name Ikuro.

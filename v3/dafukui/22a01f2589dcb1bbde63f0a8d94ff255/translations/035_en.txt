　　　　　　　△　Ihara

　In Kan'ei 20, Mizunoto-hitsuji (1643) during Lord Tadamasa's reign,
　summoned in Edo, Musashi Province
　　● Ihara Banemon Yorifumi《Home province Mino, Birth province Inaba｜Surname Minamoto, Original name Toki or Shimoishi》
　　　　His ancestor was Toki Jibu-dayū Yoriyasu, and his son Shimoishi Kambu-no-kami Yoriyoshi served Oda Nobunaga
　　　　and achieved military merit many times. Later, having business matters, he was staying at Lord Nakagawa Sehyōe's
　　　　residence when Lord Nobunaga was killed. After that, he served Lord Chikuzen Chūnagon as a guest retainer,
　　　　receiving 1,200 koku of support rice and commanding yoriki and ashigaru troops,
　　　　but died of illness. His son Shimoishi Chōzaemon Yoriyasu became a rōnin when his father Kambu Yoriyoshi died
　　　　because he was still young. Later he served Lord Ishikawa Bizen-no-kami and at the Battle of Sekigahara
　　　　achieved military merit many times and received tokens of appreciation, but when Lord Bizen-no-kami entered religious life,
　　　　he became a rōnin again. Later he was summoned by Lord Ikeda Bitchū-no-kami to Tottori in Inaba Province,
　　　　and served military duty during the Osaka campaigns, but afterward having some dissatisfaction, he left
　　　　the Ikeda house and became a rōnin. Lord Tōdō Daigaku-no-kami had long known that Chōzaemon's
　　　　grandfather's generation had achieved military merit in the Oda house

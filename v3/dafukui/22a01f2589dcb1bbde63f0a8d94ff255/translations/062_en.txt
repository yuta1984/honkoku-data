　　　　　　△　Senbon

During Lord Tadamasa's reign, 6th year of Genna, year of Metal Monkey (1620)
Summoned in Echigo Province
　　● Senbon Chōzaemon Hisatō《Home province: Hitachi, Birth province unknown｜Family name unknown》
　　　　Initially served Lord Kazusa-no-suke. During the confiscation in the 2nd year of Genna, year of Fire Dragon (1616), he was placed under the custody of Kuki Nagato-no-kami in Ise Province, and accompanied him to serve in Ise Province for three years. Later, when custody was transferred to Kanamori Izumo-no-kami in Hida Province, he was granted leave, and was subsequently summoned to the house.

During the same reign, 3rd year, year of Fire Rooster (1657), granted inheritance rights
　　Adopted son
　　　Senbon Chōzaemon Hisanaga《Birth province: Echizen｜Biological father: Kiuchi Jinbei Morimasa》
　　　　The mother of his biological father Kiuchi Jinbei was the elder sister of Sakai Yozaemon Shigenari. She married into the family of Kiuchi Sandayū, Jinbei Morimasa's father, and after Sandayū's death, remarried into the family of Senbon Chōzaemon Hisatō. Due to this connection, Hisanaga was adopted as son. During Lord Yoshishina's reign, in the 3rd year of Jōkyō, year of Fire Tiger (1686), during the reorganization, he was granted leave, and was summoned back the following year of Fire Rabbit.

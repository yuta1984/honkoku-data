　　　　　△　Kōzuki

　　● Kōzuki Hachirōzaemon Kagaharu《Home province: Harima, Birth province: Owari｜Surname: Minamoto》
　　　　A ninth-generation descendant of Prince Tomohira, the seventh son of Emperor Murakami, and of the Seisai Shogun and Harima Provincial Governor
　　　　Suefusa《founder of the Akamatsu clan》. From the fourth generation, Kōzuki Jirō Kagemori, they were hereditary castle lords of Kōzuki Sayō.
　　　　His descendant Kōzuki Bungo-no-kami Shigekage held a domain of 70,000 koku, but when
　　　　their home province fell, they retreated to Mihara in Bingo. His son, called Kōzuki Kyūzaburō Kageyoshi, served
　　　　Fukushima Saemon-dayū Masanori. Kagaharu was the second son of the aforementioned Kyūzaburō, and was granted an allocation
　　　　of 1,000 koku while serving. After Saemon-dayū's house collapsed, through the kind offices of Tōdō Daigaku-
　　　　no-kami, he was placed under the care of Ikoma Kobōshi and Ikoma Iki-no-kami.
　　　　Then, through the mediation of Hiraoka Ukon, he was summoned by Lord Tadamasa. Kagaharu's brothers
　　　　entered service under Hosokawa Etchū-no-kami, and their descendants still serve the Hosokawa house today.

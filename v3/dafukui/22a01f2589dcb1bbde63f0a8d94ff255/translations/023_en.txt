【Left page】
　　　　　　△　Ochiai

　Summoned in the 19th year of Kan'ei, Mizunoe-uma (1642) during Lord Tadamasa's reign
　Later appointed as personal attendant to Lord Yoshishina
　　● Ochiai Zen'bei Yoshiyasu《Home province: unknown, Birth province: Izumo｜Surname: unknown》
　　　　The ancestors are unknown. Yoshiyasu's father, Ochiai Samanosuke Yoshiyori, served during Lord Hideyasu's reign and was
　　　　the younger brother of a man called Ochiai Shuzen. As for Shuzen, after Lord Tadanao's domain abolition,
　　　　he requested leave and departed. Samanosuke served Lord Horio Yamashiro-no-kami, but when
　　　　that house fell, he became a masterless samurai.

　Summoned at Yoshie in the 3rd year of Kanbun, Mizunoto-u (1663) during Lord Yoshishina's reign
　　Current Ochiai Zen'bei Yoshitada《Birth province: Echizen｜》
　　　　His father Yoshiyasu's succession was granted to Yoshitada's younger brother, Ochiai Hikozaemon Yoshitoshi, but later
　　　　Yoshitoshi was granted leave and the line was discontinued. As for Yoshitada, he was specially
　　　　summoned from the age of eight and served by accompanying from Yoshie to Fukui.

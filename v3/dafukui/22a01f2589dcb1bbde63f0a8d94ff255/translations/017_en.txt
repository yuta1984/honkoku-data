　Specially summoned during Lord Tadamasa's reign, and later
　In Kan'ei 13, Hinoe-ne year (1636), granted inheritance rights
　　　Yamada Iori Masayoshi《Birth province: Kaga》
　　　　Since his father Fusayoshi was born while living temporarily in Kaga Province during his rōnin period,
　　　　he was left in the care of relatives in that province. He petitioned to serve the domain and was
　　　　summoned to this province and established as heir.

　　　　Initially served under Tagaya Sakon and came from Yūki, participated in
　　　　the Siege of Osaka as well. After Sakon's house collapsed, he became a masterless samurai and was summoned by Lord Matsudaira Shimousa-no-kami,
　　　　receiving a stipend of 150 koku. Later he took leave and returned to this province (Echizen), whereupon
　　　　he was summoned during Lord Tadamasa's reign.

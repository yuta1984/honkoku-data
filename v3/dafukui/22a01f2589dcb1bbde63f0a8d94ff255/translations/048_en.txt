【Left page】
　　　　　　　△　Imaeda

　In Kan'ei 15, year of the Earth Tiger (1638) during Lord Tadamasa's reign
　Summoned into service
　　● Hayashi Gensuke Masatoshi 《Home province unknown, Birth province: Kaga, Surname unknown》
　　　　His ancestor was reportedly a relative of a retainer of the Maeda family named Imaeda Minbu. Due to certain circumstances, he left Kashū
　　　　and entered service with our domain during Lord Tadamasa's reign. Later he was appointed as an attendant
　　　　to Lord Yoshishina, served when assigned to Yoshie, and also accompanied the lord to Fukui.

　During Lord Yoshishina's reign, in Genroku 6, year of the Water Rooster (1693), he was granted succession rights
　　Adopted son
　　Current: Imaeda Jingobei Eiji 《Birth province: Echizen, Actual father: Sakakibara Buzaemon Sadanori》
　　　　The origins of the surname Hayashi were unclear, and since it was his original name, he changed it to Imaeda.

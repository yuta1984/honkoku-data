　　　　　　　△　Tanabe

　Summoned during Lord Tadamasa's reign in Kan'ei 4, year of the Earth Dragon (1628)
　　● Tanabe Koan Tomiharu 《Home province Kii, birth province Echizen; Tachibana clan》
　　　　His ancestors were said to be descendants of Lord Tachibana no Moroe. A man named Tanabe Ryūsuke was
　　　　the lord of a place called Tanabe in Kii Province, but became a fugitive and came to this province, where he became a physician and established the Tachibana
　　　　pharmacy, making the selling of medicine his family business. He received an imperial edict during the reign of Emperor Go-Hanazono in
　　　　Kakitsu 1 (1441), which is still preserved today. Since then, generations have continued the family business,
　　　　and [the family] possesses vermillion seal documents granted by successive lords of former generations in this province, all generations of the Asakura family without exception,
　　　　as well as vermillion seal documents from Lord Nobunaga and Lord Hideyoshi.

　Summoned during Lord Mitsumichi's reign in Kanbun 4, year of the Wood Dragon (1664)
　　Current head Tanabe Sōmin Kenpaku 《Birth province: Echizen》

【Left page】
　　　　　△　Minobe

　Summoned during Lord Tadamasa's reign
　 ● Minobe Hanshichi Shigenari《Original province: Ōmi　Birth province: Yamashiro｜Surname: Sugawara　Original name: Kintadayū》
　　　　When Lord Mitsumichi was young, Hanshichi's mother《Later called Chōkōin｜》was summoned as wet nurse, and at that time Han-
　　　　shichi Shigenari was also summoned from his youth. Upon Lord Mitsumichi's death,
　　　　he relinquished his stipend and became a monk, taking the Buddhist name Kyūzan. His ancestry is unknown, but he was
　　　　said to be from the same family as Minobe Gonnosuke, who was among the Edo hatamoto.

　During Lord Yoshishina's reign, Enpō 3, Kinoto-u year (1675)
　Summoned
　　　Minobe Hanshichi Shigekiyo《Birth province: Echizen｜Original name: Gonpachi》
　　　　Shigekiyo's younger brother, Minobe Sukesaku Shigefusa, received
　　　　the inheritance after their grandmother Chōkōin passed away, was summoned and served, but died of illness, and his line became extinct.

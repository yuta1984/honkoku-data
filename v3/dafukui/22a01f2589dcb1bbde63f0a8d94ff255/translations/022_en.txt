　　　　　　△　Arakawa

　In the 2nd year of Kan'ei, Kinoto-ushi (1625) during Lord Tadamasa's reign
　Summoned in Echizen
　　● Matsudaira Oribe Shigekatsu《Home province: Mikawa, Birth province: unknown｜Surname: Minamoto》
　　　　　He is the seventh-generation grandson of Matsudaira Bitchū-no-kami Chikanori. During the Keichō era, by the command of Gongen-sama (Tokugawa Ieyasu),
　　　　　he served Kazusa-no-suke-sama (Matsudaira Tadateru) and served as castle deputy of Matsushiro Castle in Shinshū. The
　　　　　written seal, sword, and curtain that he received at that time are still preserved today. After Kazusa-no-suke-sama's domain abolition,
　　　　　he was living in confinement in Echigo Province when word came from Honda Izu-no-kami and Matsudaira Bizen, and he moved to this province and was summoned.

　In the 3rd year of Kan'ei, Hinoe-tora (1626) of the same reign
　Granted inherited territory at the age of two
　　　Matsudaira Zen'emon Katsuyoshi《Birth province: Echizen｜Original name: Matsudaira Sagenji》
　　　　　In the middle period, he changed his title to Arakawa, but by Lord Mitsumichi's intention, he restored the name Matsudaira, and at that time
　　　　　he was granted the paulownia crest.

　　　　　　△　Sakata

　Summoned during the Kan'ei years (1624-1644) in Lord Tadamasa's reign
　　● Sakata Yohei Yoshinobu《Home province and birth province both Bingo｜Surname unknown》
　　　　　Ancestral matters unknown, but generations served Lord Mizuno Hyūga-no-kami, then after being
　　　　　summoned to the (Matsudaira) household, was assigned to Lord Yoshishina and served at Yoshie

　Summoned at Yoshie during Lord Yoshishina's reign
　Granted succession stipend in Enpō 8, Kanoe-saru (1680) during Lord Tsunamasa's reign
　　Adopted son
　　　 Sakata Ukon'emon Nagaharu《Birth province Echigo｜》
　　　　　His actual father was from the lineage of one called Koshi Riemon who was in the household
　　　　　of Lord Kazusa-no-suke, and after becoming a masterless samurai retreated to Echigo Province,
　　　　　whereupon Yoshinobu adopted his son Nagaharu

　　　　　　　　△　Shibuya

　In Kan'ei 2, year of the Fire Rabbit (1627) during Lord Tadamasa's reign, summoned into service
　　● Shibuya Saburōbei Masaki 《Home province: Musashi, Birth province: unknown, Surname: unknown》
　　　　Masaki's father, Shibuya Kyūzaemon Masayoshi, served Lord Katō Shikibu-shōyū, and when
　　　　Shikibu-sama was exiled to Ishū, he accompanied him there and died in Ishū. His son Saburō-
　　　　bei Masaki served Lord Katō Higo-no-kami, and later was summoned into service by Lord Tadamasa.

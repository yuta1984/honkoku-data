　　　　　△　Suzuki

　Summoned in Echizen during Lord Tadamasa's reign《Year unknown》
　　　● Suzuki Hikotayū Shigenobu《Home province: Kii, Birth province: Echigo｜Surname: Hozumi》
　　　　　An ancestor named Ikeda Saemon-no-dayū resided in
　　　　　a place called Ōhara in the village of Mitsuke in Tōtōmi Province. His son Suzuki Munemon, and his son Gyōbu-emon were the
　　　　　maternal grandfather of Hikotayū Shigenobu. He was a nephew of 
　　　　　Suzuki Ichiemon, father of Suzuki Kotamiya who serves the domain. Though his original family name was Niwa, he changed to his maternal
　　　　　surname, so the lineage is as follows.

　Specially summoned during Lord Mitsumichi's reign, and later granted inherited territory
　　　　Suzuki Hikodayū Shigetada《Birth province: Echizen》

　After his father Shigenobu was granted stipend allocation during Lord Mitsumichi's reign
　In Jōkyō 3, Hinoe-tora year (1686), granted leave together with others
　　　　Suzuki Jingotayū Shigenori《Birth province: Echizen》

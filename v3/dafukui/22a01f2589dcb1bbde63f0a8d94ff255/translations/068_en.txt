　　　　　　△　Matsue

　　● Tsuga Chōbee Fusaaki《Home province and birth province both Echizen｜Family name unknown》
　　　　His father was a retainer of Asakura Yoshikage, Tsuga Saburōuemon Yoshinao, who resided in
　　　　the domain of Tsugano Hiki in Echizen Province. He had numerous military achievements and eventually died in battle. This is recorded in various documents and Asakura memoranda,
　　　　and is also found in genealogical records

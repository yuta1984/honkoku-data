【Left page】
　　　　　　　△　Ariga

　Summoned during Lord Tadamasa's reign in Kan'ei 11, year of the Wood Dog (1634)
　　● Ariga Gentetsu Yoshiharu 《Home province Echizen, birth province the same; Minamoto clan》
　　　　His ancestors were descendants of the Rokkaku family of Ōmi Genji and served the Asakura family. A man named
　　　　Toda Ichiemon Hisamitsu, district magistrate of Tokumitsu in this province, had military achievements, and [the family]
　　　　still possesses Yoshikage's commendation letter. He later received war wounds and died of illness. His son, called
　　　　Ariga Dōjun Tatemasa, became an orphan. In the provincial capital of this province lived a physician named Kō Saishū. Both his grandfather and father were
　　　　Chinese, and their son was also called Saishū and practiced medicine. Dōjun
　　　　became the adopted son of the aforementioned Saishū and inherited the family business. Later, for certain reasons, he became a retainer
　　　　of Honda Izu-no-kami and resided in Fukui. Dōjun's second son, Ariga Gentetsu Yoshiharu, was
　　　　summoned during Lord Tadamasa's reign. The surname Ariga comes from his mother's family name.

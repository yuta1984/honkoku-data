【Left page】
　　　　　　　△　Ariga

　Summoned during Lord Tadamasa's reign in Kan'ei 11, year of the Wood Dog (1634)
　　● Ariga Gentetsu Motoharu 《Home province Echizen, birth province the same; Family name Minamoto》
　　　　His ancestors were said to be descendants of the Rokkaku family of Ōmi Genji. He served the Asakura family, and
　　　　one named Toda Ichiuemon Suemitsu, district magistrate of Tokumitsu in this province, had military achievements
　　　　and possessed a commendation letter from Yoshikage, which is still held today. Later he received war wounds and
　　　　died of illness. His son, called Ariga Dōjun Tatsumasa, became an orphan, and in the capital of this province
　　　　there was a physician called Takasaishū, whose grandfather and father were both Chinese, and whose son was also
　　　　called Saishū and practiced medicine. Dōjun became the adopted son of the aforementioned Saishū and inherited
　　　　the family profession. Later, for certain reasons, he became a retainer of Honda Izu-no-kami and resided in Fukui.
　　　　Dōjun's second son, Ariga Gentetsu Motoharu, was summoned during Lord Tadamasa's reign. The surname Ariga
　　　　comes from his mother's family name.

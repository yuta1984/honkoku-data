　　　　　　　△　Danno

　Summoned into service in Echizen during Lord Tadamasa's reign
　　● Danno Man'emon Sadayoshi 《Home province and birth province both Mino, Family name Fujiwara, Original name Ichihashi Gunbei》
　　　　Sadayoshi's father, one called Ichihashi Kenmotsu Teisha, held dominion over the village of Ichihashi in Mino Province.
　　　　His son Danno Man'emon Sadayoshi, while traveling the provinces to train in jujutsu, was passing through
　　　　Toba-nawate in Yamashiro Province during the night when many bandits appeared and blocked the road, so he had no choice but to
　　　　cut down every last one of them, captured one as evidence, and reported to Lord Itakura Iga-no-kami, the Kyoto Deputy
　　　　(shoshidai). He was praised and received a certificate of proof, which he still possesses. Furthermore,
　　　　news of his deeds and his mastery of jujutsu reached the Imperial ear, and in Kan'ei 1, year of the Wood Rat (1624) during the reign of Retired Emperor Go-Mizunoo,
　　　　he was commanded to appear at court, granted the title of "Peerless in the Realm," and
　　　　moreover received an Imperial seal, which he still possesses. Afterwards he went to Kii Province and
　　　　stayed there for a while. When Lord Tadamasa heard of this, through Arakawa Uemon-ta he was told that
　　　　a stipend of 500 koku would be granted, so he presented himself and received 300 koku.

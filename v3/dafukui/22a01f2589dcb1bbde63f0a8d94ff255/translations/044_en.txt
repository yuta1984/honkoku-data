　　　　　　　△　Takamura

　Summoned during the Kan'ei era (1624-1644) of Lord Tadamasa's reign
　　● Takamura Tarōbei Naritsugu《Original province unknown, Birth province: Higo, Family name unknown, Original name: Terashima》
　　　　Naritsugu had previously served Lord Katō Higo-no-kami, but when that house was dismantled, all the
　　　　retainers became masterless samurai. Since Naritsugu had relatives in Owari Province, he was traveling there to consult about his circumstances
　　　　when he passed through this province. As Hiraoka Ukon, who served our domain, was
　　　　a relative, he stopped by to visit. This reached Lord Tadamasa's attention, and an order was
　　　　issued that he should be summoned. When he reported his service record under his former lord Higo-no-kami, he was
　　　　appointed to the position of advance guard commander and summoned into service

　Specially summoned during Lord Tadamasa's reign (year unknown)
　　　Takamura Shirōzaemon Narifusa《Birth province: Higo》
　　　　Naritsugu's eldest son Seidayū and his son Rizaemon successively inherited the family headship, but in Jōkyō 3 (1686)
　　　　during the administrative reform, Rizaemon was granted leave from service

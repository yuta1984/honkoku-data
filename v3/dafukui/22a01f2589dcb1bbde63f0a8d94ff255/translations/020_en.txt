　　　　　　　△　Inoue

　　● Asahi Taheita (given name unknown)《Home province and birth province both Yamato｜Surname unknown》
　　　　　Initially he was a retainer of Kaishige Kai-no-kami, the castle lord of Kaizu in Yamato Province, and was a colleague
　　　　　of Gonnosuke, the grandfather of Sugita Kogorōbei, and was indeed the brother-in-law of Gorōbei. After the fall
　　　　　of Kaishige's house, he lived as a masterless samurai. When Gorōbei gradually received promotion and was summoned to serve
　　　　　in the domain, after the lord's entry into Echizen, Gorō-
　　　　　bei invited him, so he came to this province and was summoned through Gorōbei's mediation.

　　　Karasawa Anzaemon Sadayue《Birth province: Echizen》
　　　　After Sadayue's elder brother Watari Jūbee was granted leave, Sadayue was a masterless samurai (rōnin) staying with his uncle
　　　　Karasawa Ichibee when he was summoned as Ichibee's younger brother. Therefore,
　　　　he took Karasawa as his family name. Ichibee's father, a man called Karasawa Namiemon,
　　　　was summoned at Takada in Echigo Province during Lord Tadamasa's reign. His son Ichibee
　　　　was granted leave during the previous Lord Yoshishina's reign.

　　　　　　　△　Ajioka

　However, there exists a written seal from the 2nd year of Genna, year of Fire Dragon (1616)
　　● Ajioka Hikodayū Hisakatsu《Home province: Tanba, birth province unknown｜Family name unknown》
　　　　Ancestor Ajioka Shichirō Hisatsugu fought many battles with various samurai of neighboring provinces during the Ashikaga warring period,
　　　　and was killed in battle around Miyazu in Tanba. His son Ajioka Tango-no-jō
　　　　served under Tsutsui Junkei during Lord Nobunaga's time and achieved military merit. His son Ajioka Jinzaemon Hisahide
　　　　served under Priest Tokunaga during Lord Hideyoshi's time. His son Ajioka Hikodayū Hisa-
　　　　katsu was summoned by Lord Tadamasa.

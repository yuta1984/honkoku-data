【Left page】
　　　　　　　△　Katō

　During Lord Tadamasa's reign, summoned in the 6th year of Genna, year of Metal Monkey (1620)
　　● Katō Hanzaemon, personal name unknown《Home province: Kaga, birth province unknown｜Family name: Fujiwara》
　　　　His father was a man called Toyota Ichiuemon, who served Lord Hideyoshi and died in battle
　　　　at Minakuchi in Ōmi Province. His son Hanzaemon served under Seki Nagato-no-kami around the Tenshō era, and distinguished himself with his spear during the siege of Kameyama
　　　　Castle in Ise Province. Later he served under Maeno Tajima and twice distinguished himself during the Korean campaigns. Subsequently, while serving under Mori Ukon, he performed meritoriously at the Sanada front during the Kawanakajima campaign in Shinano Province. During the Summer Siege of Osaka, at Senba-guchi, when enemies took refuge in a storehouse, he shot and killed many enemies as a gunner. Regarding this matter, when Sueki Ukyō inquired about it, he corresponded with him, and immediately received word from Ukyō conveying
　　　　the purport of the lord's intentions. Thereafter, Hanzaemon secluded himself in Tsuchiyama in Ōmi Province, from where
　　　　he was summoned during Lord Tadamasa's reign

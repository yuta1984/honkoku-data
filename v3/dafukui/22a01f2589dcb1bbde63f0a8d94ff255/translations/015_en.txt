【Left page】
　　　　　　△　Mizuno

　Summoned during Lord Tadamasa's reign, Kan'ei 16, Tsuchinoto-u year (1639)
　　● Mizuno Gozaemon Masahide《Original province and birth province both Bingo｜Surname unknown》
　　　　Ancestry unknown. Masahide's father was called Mizuno Zenzaemon Tomomasa. Masahide initially
　　　　served under Lord Mizuno Hyūga no kami, participated in both Osaka campaigns with military achievements, but later due to circumstances
　　　　took leave and served under Lord Akiyama Shuzen in Yamato Province. When Lord Shuzen died young
　　　　without an heir, all the retainers became rōnin and came to Echizen. There, through the mediation of Koma Ise,
　　　　Gozaemon and his second son Kogyōbu were summoned together as father and son, and Kogyōbu was
　　　　graciously granted a stipend of 1,000 koku.

　Summoned during the same reign, year unknown
　　　 Mizuno Kanbei Katsuhide《Birth province: Echizen｜》
　　　　 While serving separately, when his younger brother Kogyōbu died in service, during Lord Mitsumichi's reign in Shōhō 2 (1645),
　　　　 Kanbei inherited Kogyōbu's stipend and served as Kawara Gate guard.

【Left page】
　　　　　　△　Mizuno

　Summoned during Lord Tadamasa's reign, Kan'ei 16, Tsuchinoto-u year (1639)
　　● Mizuno Gozaemon Masahide《Both original and birth provinces: Bingo｜Surname unknown》
　　　　Ancestry unknown. Masahide's father was called Mizuno Zenzaemon Tomomasa. Masahide initially
　　　　served Mizuno Hyūga-no-kami-dono, accompanied him to both Osaka campaigns, and distinguished himself in service. Later, for certain reasons,
　　　　he took leave and served Akiyama Shuzen-dono in Yamato Province, but Shuzen-dono died young
　　　　without an heir, and all the retainers became rōnin. When he came to Echizen, through the mediation of Koma Ise,
　　　　Gozaemon and his second son Kogyōbu were summoned together as father and son, and Kogyōbu
　　　　received a special grant of 1,000 koku stipend.

　Summoned during the same reign (year unknown)
　　　 Mizuno Kanbei Katsuhide《Birth province: Echizen｜》
　　　　 While serving separately, when his younger brother Kogyōbu died in loyal service, during Lord Mitsumichi's reign in Shōhō 2 (1645),
　　　　 Kogyōbu's stipend was granted to Kanbei, and he served as Kawara gate guard.

　　　　　　　　△　Nakamura

　Summoned during Lord Tadamasa's reign, but the year is unknown
　　● Nakamura Ichiemon Naomasa《Original province: Kawachi, Birth province: Yamato, Surname: Fujiwara》
　　　　It is said that his ancestor, a man called Nakamura Kinsetsu, was the lord of Takai Castle in Kawachi Province. Naomasa initially
　　　　served under Matsunaga Danjo. During the reign of Taitokuin-sama (Tokugawa Hidetada), when seeking out
　　　　martial arts masters from various provinces, he demonstrated his school's spear technique
　　　　before the Shogun in Edo

　Granted family headship in Jōō 1, Mizunoe-tatsu year (1652) during Lord Mitsumichi's reign
　　Adopted son
　　　Nakamura Hachidayū Shigeyuki《Birth province: Echizen, Biological father: Nakazawa Kinemon》
　　　　Before becoming Naomasa's adopted son, he had served as Lord Tadanao's page, and later became an adopted
　　　　son. During the reign of Daiyūin-sama (Tokugawa Iemitsu), when he went to demonstrate spear techniques before the Shogun,
　　　　the viewing was cancelled due to the Shogun's illness. He served as Lord Mitsumichi's spear instructor and was granted a written certificate

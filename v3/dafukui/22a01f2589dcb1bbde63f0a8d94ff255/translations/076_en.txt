　　　　　　　△　Kanō

　During Lord Tadamasa's reign, 15th year of Kan'ei, year of Earth Tiger (1638)
　Summoned
　　● Kanō Ryōshi《Both home province and birth province: Yamashiro｜Family name: Fujiwara, common name: Kyūbē》
　　　　His ancestors were said to be rōnin from Dewa Province. Ryōshi's father was called Ryōkei. Both father and son were
　　　　disciples of Kanō Kokyō and made painting their family profession.

　During Lord Mitsumichi's reign, 3rd year of Manji, year of Metal Rat (1660), granted succession
　　　Kanō Kurōjirō Motoaki《Birth province: Yamashiro》

　During Lord Yoshishina's reign, 7th year of Genroku, year of Wood Dog (1694), summoned
　　Current: Kanō Okuseki Motofusa《Birth province: Echizen》
　　　　Father Kurōjirō Motoaki passed away, and succession was granted to legitimate son Chikuun, but he died young, and the main
　　　　family line was discontinued. Motofusa was summoned in Edo.

　　　　△　Saitō

　Summoned during Lord Tadamasa's reign, Kan'ei 8, Kanoto-hitsuji year (1631)
　 　● Saitō Minbu Sadanao《Both original and birth provinces unknown｜Surname: Fujiwara》
　　　　 Served faithfully from a young age. On one occasion, he was granted the hilt of a ceremonial sword, which was
　　　　 of such fine craftsmanship that a general-class warrior should wield it《The pommel had an elephant's trunk》. Later, with the lord's intention that he should become
　　　 　a commander of troops, he received celebratory gifts, and it is
　　　 　transmitted that he received these with gratitude. He still possesses the aforementioned hilt. In Shōhō 2, Kinoto-tori year (1645),
　　　 　he committed junshi (ritual suicide following his lord) upon Lord Tadamasa's death.

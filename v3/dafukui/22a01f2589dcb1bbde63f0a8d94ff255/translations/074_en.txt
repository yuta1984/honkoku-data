【Left page】
　　　　　　　△　Nakayama

　Summoned during Lord Tadamasa's reign in the 2nd year of Genna, year of Fire Dragon (1616)
　　● Nakayama Uma-no-suke, personal name unknown《Home province unknown, birth province Hitachi｜Family name unknown, original name Shirōzaemon》
　　　　When Uma-no-suke was seventeen years old, during a battle at Tsuchiura in Shimōsa Province, he used his spear to pull in a boat carrying over a hundred men and distinguished himself in battle. At that time, he received a commendation from Lord Satake Shuridayū, and he still possesses the armor, spear, and other equipment he wore that day. The family crest was originally bellflower, but because of his outstanding achievement that day, he began using the crest of a circle with a horizontal line from that time, as has been passed down. Initially he served under Tagaya Sakon, but after Sakon's house was abolished, Uma-no-suke's reputation came to be known and he was summoned, later becoming a banner magistrate. When his son Chūsuke was granted leave due to certain circumstances, Uma-no-suke's succession rights were granted to his legitimate grandson Tōzaemon, but during his grandson Bunshichirō's generation, in the 3rd year of Jōkyō, year of Fire Tiger (1686), they were all granted leave together and the main family line was abolished.

One: September 13th
　　Together with Kenmotsu, they went to chamberlain Yanagisawa Dewa-no-kami (Yasuaki) and requested to attend a lecture session
One: Same month, 16th - To Roju Okubo Kaga-no-kami (Tadatomo) and Tsuchiya Sagami-no-kami (Masanao), through messenger and caretaker Hara Heizaemon (Masatake), they reported that on the 9th, Takumi-no-kami (Masahira) arrived at Matsuoka, physician Okuyama Ryuan-ro also arrived the same day and immediately conducted an examination, medicine was administered, and regarding the patient's condition being similar, news had arrived from the domain
One: October 5th - Nakatsukasa-daifu (Masakatsu) was gradually recovering, and physician Miyama Ryuan

　　departed Matsuoka on the 23rd of last month and returned today, so for gratitude regarding this matter, to duty officer Toda Yamashiro-no-kami (Tadamasa) and former duty officer Tsuchiya Sagami-no-kami (Masanao), remaining official duties were performed, and messengers were sent to the Roju and chamberlains
One: Same month, 13th
　　Together with Kenmotsu, they went to chamberlain Yanagisawa Dewa-no-kami (Yasuaki) and requested to attend a lecture session
　　　　　　However, on the 23rd of the same month, the same request was made again
One: November 14th - To Keishoin (mother of Tsunayoshi), privately offered one hundred bundles of cotton and five sea bream in a box, through messenger and caretaker Kaga Tozaemon (Narioki) at Rokkaku

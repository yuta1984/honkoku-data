One: September 13th
　　Together with Kenmotsu-sama, they went to Goyō-gakari Yanagisawa Dewa-no-kami (Yasuaki) and requested to attend a lecture

One: Same month, 16th - To Rōjū Okubo Kaga-no-kami (Tadatomo) and Tsuchiya Sagami-no-kami (Masanao), through the messenger and caretaker Hara Heizaemon (Masatake), they reported that on the 9th, Takumi-no-kami (Masahira) arrived at Matsuoka, and the physician Okuyama Ryūan-rō also arrived the same day and immediately conducted an examination. Medicine was administered, and the patient's condition remained the same, as reported from the domain

One: October 5th - Nakatsukasa-daifu (Masakatsu) gradually recovered, and the physician Miyama Ryūan

　　left Matsuoka on the 23rd of last month and returned today. For this reason, as gratitude, messengers were sent to the duty officer Toda Yamashiro-no-kami (Tadamasa), the previous month's duty officer Tsuchiya Sagami-no-kami (Masanao), and the Rōjū chamberlains

One: Same month, 13th
　　Together with Kenmotsu-sama, they went to chamberlain Yanagisawa Dewa-no-kami (Yasuaki) and requested to attend a lecture
　　　　　　However, on the 23rd of the same month, they made the same request again

One: November 14th - To Keishōin-sama (mother of Tsunayoshi-sama), they privately presented 100 bundles of cotton and five tai fish in one box. Through the messenger and caretaker Kaga Tōzaemon (Narioki) at Rokkaku

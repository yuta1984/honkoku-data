One: June 5th - An order was issued to construct townhouses in the vacant land area from the end of Nagahama-cho to Katsumi Gate
　　　　However, although this land was under the jurisdiction of the district magistrate as granted territory within the general compound of Fuku Gate,
　　　　it was ordered on July 21st that the town magistrate should have jurisdiction over these townhouses
One: Same month, 9th - Due to the summer heat, he made courtesy rounds to the Senior Councilor's
　　chamberlain to inquire after his health
One: Same month, 11th - Due to the summer heat, through an envoy he presented the customary local products of somen noodles
　　and dried sea bream

One: Same month, 14th
　　Accompanied by Monomimi-sama, he went to the chamberlain and Hayashi Daigaku-no-kami (Nobuatsu) and
　　submitted a request to attend the lectures
One: Same month, 16th - For the Katei celebration
　　accompanied by Monomimi-sama, he went to the castle
　　Since this was Monomimi-sama's first castle visit for Katei, upon departure he made courtesy rounds to the Senior Councilor's
　　chamberlain
One: Same month, 29th
　　Accompanied by Monomimi-sama, he went to chamberlain Yanagisawa Dewa-no-kami (Yasuaki [Yanagisawa Yoshiyasu])

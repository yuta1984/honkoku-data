At Kokenji Temple, a memorial service was conducted from the evening of yesterday, the 8th, until this morning.

One: On the 18th day of the 8th month, for the monthly inquiry into his well-being, messenger Imadate Jihidayu Masafusa was
　　dispatched to Edo. Departed from Fukui this morning.
　　　　　　However, Jihidayu's arrival date in Edo is unknown.

One: On the 7th day of the 9th month, for Choyo (Double Ninth Festival) congratulations, through a messenger, kosode (small sleeves) were presented as customary,
　　and white silver was offered.

One: On the 25th day of the same month, regarding Takumi-no-kami Masahira-sama inheriting his father Nakatsukasa-taifu Masataka-sama's domain without change
　　(announced on the 21st), Takumi-no-kami Masahira-sama sent messenger Todo Kageyuzaemon for notification,
　　who arrived in Fukui today.

One: On the 27th day of the same month, Honjo magistrate Taga Matashiro-den requested that regarding the current revision of Edo maps,
　　a map showing the area measurements of the Honjo residence be submitted. This request came yesterday, the 26th, to
　　caretaker Hara Heizaemon Masatake, so the map of the said residence was submitted by Heizaemon today.

One: On the 4th day of the 10th month, for gratitude regarding Takumi-no-kami Masahira-sama being granted succession without change,
　　messenger Sakakibara Sukezaemon Takahiro was dispatched to Edo. Departed from Fukui today.

One: On the 14th day of the same month, the same messenger for gratitude, Sakakibara Sukezaemon Takahiro, arrived in Edo this morning and
　　went to the duty magistrate Toda Yamashiro-no-kami Tadamasa-den (accompanied by caretaker Hara Heizaemon) to submit a letter.
　　　　　　However, a reply letter was delivered on the 16th day of the same month.

(Last month the 26th - written notice was delivered)
One: May 9th - Regarding the completion of the memorial service, through envoy and resident representative Kaga
　　Tozaemon (Narioki), he presented post-abstinence celebratory delicacies (three sea bream)
One: Same month, 10th - Senior Councilor Tsuchiya Sagami-no-kami (Masanao) summoned him, and when resident representative Hara
　　Heizaemon (Masatake) appeared, he was given private counsel that since other daimyo of equivalent rank were requesting to attend scholarly lectures,
　　it would be appropriate for him to make such a request as well
One: Same month, 11th - He appeared before the Senior Councilors and chamberlains to
　　request permission to attend scholarly discussions
One: Same month, 12th -

　　Accompanied by Monomimi-sama, he appeared before the Senior Councilors and chamberlains
　　He also requested that Monomimi-sama be allowed to attend the scholarly discussions
One: Same month, 21st - He appeared before chamberlain Yanagisawa Dewa-no-kami (Yasuaki) to
　　request permission to attend scholarly discussions
　　　　However, on the 27th of the same month as well,
　　　　　accompanied by Monomimi-sama, he made the same request
One: Same month, 29th - At Tokoji Temple, a tea ceremony for the 70th anniversary memorial
　　of Obaiin-sama was conducted

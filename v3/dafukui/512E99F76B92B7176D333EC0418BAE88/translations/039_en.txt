《Written notice was delivered on the 26th of last month》
One: May 9th - As the memorial service was completed, through envoy and resident official Kaga
　　Tōemon《Narioki》, ceremonial fish for post-abstinence celebration《three sea bream》were offered
One: Same month, 10th - Senior Councilor Tsuchiya Sagami-no-kami《Masanao》summoned resident official Hara
　　Heizaemon《Masatake》, who appeared and was given private guidance that it would be appropriate to request
　　to attend the lecture, as other daimyo of equal rank were making such requests
One: Same month, 11th - He went to the Senior Councilor's chamberlain and
　　requested to attend the lecture
One: Same month, 12th -

　　He went to the Senior Councilor's chamberlain accompanied by Monomimi-sama
　　A request was made that Monomimi-sama also wished to attend the lecture
One: Same month, 21st - He went to chamberlain Yanagisawa Dewa-no-kami《Yasuaki》and
　　requested to attend the lecture
　　　　However, on the 27th of the same month as well
　　　　　The same request was made while accompanied by Monomimi-sama
One: Same month, 29th - At Tōkōji Temple, a tea ceremony for the seventieth anniversary memorial
　　of Ōbaiin-sama was conducted

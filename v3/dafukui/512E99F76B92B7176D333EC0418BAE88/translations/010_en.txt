One: On the 4th of the same month at 6:30, Chōkichi-sama moved to the Reiganjima residence (accompanied by Mukai Shōgen). Matsudaira Shōbei Masatsune was sent to welcome him, and when he entered, senior retainers and ceremonial attendants and other officials went out to the shirasu (white sand courtyard), while military commanders and guard unit members went out to the large hall. The direct retainers who handled entries and exits went out to welcome him up to the formal entrance hall, and after he was seated in the front study, Masaaki-sama came out and they met face to face. At that time, the ceremonial attendant Ōtani Sukeroku Shigeatsu presented the sword he brought, gold horse substitute, and twenty formal garments of two types in two loads. After the formal farewell meal was served, Kuchiba Sukenoshin, a retainer of Nagato-no-kami Yoshinari, brought a sword catalog, was granted an audience, and the matter of directly bestowing the formal farewell meal was completed.

With Chōkichi-sama accompanying, they entered the inner study, the formal farewell meal was served, New Year soup and clear soup were served, and there was a celebration with three rounds of sake. A cup was offered to Chōkichi-sama, and at that time a pair of swords (the long sword was Bizen Kanemitsu worth twenty gold pieces, the short sword was Bizen Kanemasa worth six gold pieces) was presented.

One: April 21st - Matsudaira Nagato-no-kami invited [us] for congratulations on the adoption of an heir.
When seated in Nagato-no-kami's study, the sword brought as a gift was
displayed.
Chōkitsu-sama wore formal long hakama and a meal was served (three soups and nine dishes).
Masaaki-sama and Chōkitsu-sama presented gifts, and Nagato-no-kami's
retainers - Toda Bizen, Awaya Taitō, Shiwa Sukenoshin, and Shidō Hyōgo - were
appointed as companions and given sake cups. Nagato-no-kami also gave sake cups to Sakai
Yosazaemon Moto[?], Takaya Zenemon [?], and Habe Koemon [?].
After this concluded, they proceeded to the inner study where sweets and clear soup were served.

One: 27th of the same month - Regarding the presentation for year-end congratulations, an inner letter was
handed over at the castle, and gifts for retainers etc. were as usual.

One: May 2nd - A message came from Senior Inspector Takagi Ise-no-kami Mori[?], and when the deputy castellan
went out, regarding the prohibition of the Hitada sect, the following was
handed over by the study attendant:

　　　　　Regarding the Fuju-Fuse sect within Nichiren Buddhism, it has been under official

　　　　　Kan'ō-ji, calling themselves the Hitada sect, [practice] the heretical doctrine of Fuju-Fuse...

One: April 21st - Matsudaira Nagato-no-kami Yoshinari invited [us] for congratulations on the adoption of an heir.
　When Nagato-no-kami was seated in the study, the sword he brought with him was
　displayed.
　Chōkitsu-sama took the seat of honor and a meal was served (three soups and nine dishes).
　After Masaaki-sama and Chōkitsu-sama exchanged gifts, Nagato-no-kami's
　retainers Shishido Bizen, Awaya Taitō, Shiba Sukenoshin, and Shidō Hyōgo were
　invited to dine together and given sake cups. Nagato-no-kami also gave sake cups to Sakai
　Yosazaemon Motonobu, Takaya Zenemon Nagahide, and Hahakabe Koemon Yorihiro.
　After this was completed, they moved to the inner study where sweets and soup were served.

One: 27th of the same month - Regarding the presentation of year-end congratulatory gifts, an official letter was
　handed over at the castle, and gifts to retainers, etc. were as usual.
One: May 2nd - A message came from Inspector General Takagi Ise-no-kami, and when the deputy castellan
　went out, regarding the prohibition of the Hiden sect, the following was
　handed over by a study attendant:
　　　　　　Memorandum of Verbal Instructions
　　　　Within the Nichiren sect, the matter of Fujufuse has long been under official
　　　　prohibition. However, Komonatsu Tanjōji, Himonya Hokkeji, and Yanaka
　　　　Kan'ōji call themselves the Hiden sect and [practice] the heretical doctrine of Fujufuse

One: On the same day, regarding Takumi-no-kami Masahira-sama being appointed to oku-go-tsume (inner palace duty) this time (on the 5th of last month), messenger Suzuki Uzaemon Yoriyoshi was dispatched to Edo for expressing gratitude, departing from Fukui today.
However, the date of Uzaemon's arrival in Edo is unknown; a reply letter was delivered on the 18th of the 8th month.

One: On the 21st day of the 7th month, the first cod of the provincial products was presented as customary through an official letter.

One: On the same day, he went out to Yoshie for falconry (on the 23rd day of the same month □□□).

One: On the 27th day of the 7th month, Nakatsukasa-no-taifu Masakatsu-sama passed away in Edo. His posthumous Buddhist name was
Kenshō-in-den Zen-chūsho-daigō Tekkan-ryōmu-dai-koji (Taishō 15, 7th month, Shinagawa Kaiunji Temple).

One: On the 1st day of the 8th month, a ceremonial sword and horse substitution fee were presented through a messenger as customary for congratulations.

One: On the same day, at Jōkōin Temple, from the 26th of last month until today,
the 50th memorial service for Ryūhōin-sama was conducted in advance.
However, although it should properly be held in the coming year of the dog, it was conducted in advance due to the sankin year.

One: On the 2nd day of the same month, regarding the death of Nakatsukasa-no-taifu Masakatsu-sama,
Kenmotsu-sama dispatched messenger Tsuda Ichibei Terunari by express horse, who arrived in Fukui today.
However, regarding this matter, mourning was observed until the 17th.

One: On the same day, for the same matter, messenger Hayashi Hachiemon Masayoshi was dispatched to Edo for condolences,
and proxy incense burning was ordered, with ten pieces of silver as incense money offered to the spirit hall. Hachiemon
departed from Fukui today.

One: On the same day, regarding Takumi-no-kami Masahira-sama being appointed to Oku-gozume (inner palace duty) on this occasion (on the 5th), for congratulations
　　messenger Suzuki Uzaemon Yoriyoshi was dispatched to Edo. Departed from Fukui today.
　　　　　　However, Uzaemon's arrival date in Edo is unknown; a reply letter was delivered on the 18th day of the 8th month.

One: On the 21st day of the 7th month, through a letter, the first cod of the domain's products was presented as customary.

One: On the same day, he went out to Yoshie for falconry (returned on the 23rd of the same month).

One: On the 27th day of the 7th month, Nakatsukasa-taifu Masataka-sama passed away in Edo. His posthumous Buddhist name was
　　Kenshoin-den Zen-chusho-daigo Tekkan-ryomu-daijoji (Taisho 15, 7th month, Kaianji Temple, Shinagawa).

One: On the 1st day of the 8th month, for congratulations, through a messenger, a ceremonial sword and horse substitute were presented as customary.

One: On the same day, at Jokoin Temple, from the 26th of last month until today,
　　the 50th memorial service for Ryuhoin-sama was conducted in advance.
　　　　　　However, while it should correspond to next year of the Dog, it was conducted in advance due to the sankin year.

One: On the 2nd day of the same month, regarding the death of Nakatsukasa-taifu Masataka-sama,
　　from Kenmotsu-sama, messenger Tsuda Ichihee Terunari was dispatched by express horse and arrived in Fukui today.
　　　　　　However, regarding this matter, mourning was observed until the 17th.

One: On the same day, for the same matter, for condolences, messenger Hayashi Hachiemon Masayoshi was dispatched to Edo,
　　and memorial incense burning was ordered on his behalf, offering ten pieces of silver for incense money to the spirit hall. Hachiemon
　　departed from Fukui today.

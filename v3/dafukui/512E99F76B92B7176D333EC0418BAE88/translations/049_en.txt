From Masaaki-sama
　　To Kenmotsu-sama's attendants Iida Chūshirō, Inaba Kyūsuke, Obata Uhyōe, and Nakayama Kanzaemon,
　　abalone was bestowed
One: On the same day, as celebratory gifts for the above
　　From Kenmotsu-sama, through messenger Hababe Koemon Yorihiro,
　　three varieties in one load were presented to Masaaki-sama
　　From Masaaki-sama, through messenger Sengoku Gonemon Takamei,
　　two varieties in one load were presented to Kenmotsu-sama
One: On the 28th day of the 3rd month, at Jōkōin temple, from the evening of yesterday the 27th until this morning, the 23rd anniversary memorial service for Seichiin-sama was conducted. At Tentokuji temple as well, the same memorial service was conducted by Fuyohime-sama
One: On the 14th day of the 4th month, regarding the year-end celebratory presentations, an official letter was handed over at the castle, and retainer gifts and such were as usual
One: On the same day, through the imperial envoy Senior Councilor Tsuchiya Sagami-no-kami Masanao, leave to return to the home domain was announced, and as usual white silver and lined garments were received as gifts (the imperial envoy's handling was as usual). As thanks for this, courtesy visits were made to the senior councilors and chief pages
One: On the 15th day of the same month, they attended the castle and expressed gratitude for the leave, and as usual a horse

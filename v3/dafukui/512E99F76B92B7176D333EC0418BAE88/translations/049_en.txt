From Masaaki-sama
　　To Kenmotsu-sama's personal attendants: Iida Chūshirō, Inaba Kyūsuke, Obata Uhyōe, and Nakayama Kanzaemon,
　　long noshi were bestowed
One: On the same day, for the above celebration
　　From Kenmotsu-sama, through messenger Hahakaibe Koemon Yorihiro,
　　three varieties in one load were presented to Masaaki-sama
　　From Masaaki-sama, through messenger Sengoku Gonemon Takayoshi,
　　two varieties in one load were presented to Kenmotsu-sama
One: On the 28th day of the 3rd month, at Jōkō-in temple, from the evening of the 27th until this morning, the 23rd memorial
　　Buddhist service for Seiike-in-sama was conducted, and at Tentoku-ji temple as well, the same type of
　　service was conducted by Fuyo-hime-sama
One: On the 14th day of the 4th month, regarding the year-end celebratory presentation, an official letter was
　　delivered at the castle, and bestowals to retainers were as usual
One: On the same day, through the imperial envoy rōjū Tsuchiya Sagami-no-kami Masanao, permission to return to his domain was
　　announced, and as usual he received silver and a lined kimono. As thanks for this, he made courtesy visits to the rōjū and
　　goyōnin
One: On the 15th day of the same month, he attended the castle and expressed gratitude for the leave permission, and as usual a horse [was given/received]

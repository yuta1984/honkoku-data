After entering, they came out in two lines and received the ceremonial shadow sake cups. When this was completed, in celebration of the lord's complete recovery from illness, red rice, sake, and soup were bestowed upon the senior retainers, close attendants, and those on duty in the personal service rotation.

One: On February 24th, at Jōkō-in temple, from the 22nd until today (two nights and three days), the 17th anniversary memorial service for Lord Daian-in was conducted. The memorial service was also conducted at Daian-ji temple.
　　Although March would have been the proper time, it was moved up because it would have fallen after the lord's departure.

One: On March 3rd, in the formal audience chamber of the main castle, Honda Magotarō Nagataka and other senior retainers offered their respects and were given ceremonial long sake cups and diamond-shaped rice cakes. Afterwards, in the great hall, Takachi and other samurai came out in two lines to offer their respects and personally received diamond-shaped rice cakes.

One: On the 19th of the same month, rewards were bestowed upon those who had perfect attendance at their guard duties.

One: On the 26th of the same month, the lord departed Fukui and traveled along the Tōkaidō route.

One: On April 9th, proceeding directly from lodging at Shinagawa station for attendance at the capital, as a courtesy visit to Senior Councilors Toda Yamashiro-no-kami Tadamasa and Tsuchiya Sagami-no-kami Masanao, the lord arrived at the Reigan-jima residence.
　　However, courtesy visits were made to the remaining Senior Councilors, chamberlains, and junior councilors.

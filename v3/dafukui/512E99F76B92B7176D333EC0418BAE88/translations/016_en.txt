One: August 12th
Accompanied by Lord Chōkichi, he went to Edo Castle and viewed the Noh performances.

One: On the same day, regarding the Noh performance, through the envoy and castle keeper Kaga Tōzaemon, one box of cypress wood containers and one package of fish (three barracuda) were presented (a written document was delivered on the 7th).

One: August 13th
Accompanied by Lord Chōkichi, he went to Edo Castle and expressed gratitude for viewing yesterday's Noh performance. Upon departure, he made courtesy visits to the senior councilors and close attendants.

One: August 14th
Accompanied by Lord Chōkichi, he had an audience with the duty officer Lord Ōkubo Kaga-no-kami Tadatomo and performed his duties.

One: August 15th, as thanks for viewing the Noh performance, through the envoy and castle keeper Hara Heizaemon Masa[?], fish (sea bream) was presented.

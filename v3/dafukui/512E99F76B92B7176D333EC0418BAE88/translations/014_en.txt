One: On June 27th, the Nikko construction was successfully completed, and the formal enshrinement was accomplished without incident. As congratulations for this, he went to Edo Castle.

One: July 3rd
　　Accompanied by Lord Chōkichi, he went to Edo Castle (yesterday on the 2nd, a written order bearing the joint signatures of the senior councilors came from Lord Toda Yamashiro-no-kami, who shares the same duty rotation)
　　He expressed gratitude for the adoption.

　　From Lord Shōmei
　　To the Shogun: he presented a sword, gold in place of a horse, and one hundred bundles of cotton. To the Shogun's wife,
　　he offered ten rolls of silk crepe.
　　From Lord Chōkichi
　　To the Shogun: he presented a sword, gold in place of a horse, summer robes, and unlined garments.
　　To the Shogun's wife: he offered ten pieces of silver and rolls of silk gauze. After completion, he withdrew.
　　Upon departure, as thanks, they made courtesy visits together to the senior councilors, close attendants,
　　and junior councilors.

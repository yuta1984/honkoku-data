One: 22nd day of the 2nd month
　　Together with Kenmotsu-sama, they attended the castle, listened to the lectures, and upon withdrawal,
　　as thanks, they made courtesy visits together to the senior councilors and chamberlains, and sent
　　messengers to the attendants

One: 23rd day of the same month
　　Together with Kenmotsu-sama, they attended the castle and expressed gratitude for
　　listening to the lectures
One: On the same day, regarding the above matter, through messengers one box of kelp, one box of dried sea bream, and one thousand mon as sake barrel money were
　　presented

　　Kenmotsu-sama also presented one box of dried sea bream and one thousand mon as sake barrel money
One: 29th day of the same month, as thanks for listening to the lectures
　　Together with Kenmotsu-sama, they performed their duties to senior councilor Toda Yamashiro-no-kami Tadamasa
One: 23rd day of the 3rd month
　　Congratulatory ceremonies were held for Kenmotsu-sama's sleeve-fastening ceremony, and after entering the front area, in the inner reception room
　　they had an audience with Masaaki-sama, long noshi were presented, and celebratory sake cups were exchanged with ozoni (New Year soup)
　　After this was completed, in the Shisen-no-ma room, they dined together on a meal (three soups and eight dishes), and afterwards
　　Kenmotsu-sama bestowed sake cups from the chief retainer down to Takaya Zenemon Shigefusa. To the ceremony announcers
　　and other officials, long noshi were bestowed

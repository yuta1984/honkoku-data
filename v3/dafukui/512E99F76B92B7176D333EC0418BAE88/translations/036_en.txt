The reply letter from Ogasawara Sado-no-kami Nagashige to the Senior Councilors was brought by envoy and guard captain
　　Tominaga Shinzaemon Nagasuke, who arrived in Fukui today
　　　　Note: He did not bring the oral decree or court rank documents
One: February 9th - Regarding the same matter, Kasuya Kazaemon Mototake was ordered to carry the oral decree and imperial edict
　　and was dispatched to Edo; Kazaemon departed from Fukui today

One: Same month, 12th - At Zuigenji Temple, from the 10th until today (two nights, three days), the seventh memorial
　　service for Shōkei-in-sama was conducted, and a memorial service was also
　　conducted at Nansenji Temple in Yanaka
One: March 3rd

　　Monomimi-sama made his first court visit for the seasonal festival and expressed his gratitude
One: Same month, 6th - An envoy was dispatched to Edo for the monthly inquiry about his lordship's well-being,
　　offering one type of fish delicacy. This envoy departed from Fukui today
One: Same month, 23rd - Rewards were bestowed upon those who maintained perfect attendance at guard duty
One: Same month, 26th - Departed from Fukui, traveling via the Tōkaidō route
One: April 9th - After the midday rest at Shinagawa station, proceeded directly to the capital; for guidance,
　　visited Senior Councilors Toda Yamashiro-no-kami Tadamasa and Tsuchiya Sagami-no-kami Masanao, and Chamberlain
　　Yanagisawa Dewa-no-kami Yasuaki, arriving at the Reiganjima residence for official duties

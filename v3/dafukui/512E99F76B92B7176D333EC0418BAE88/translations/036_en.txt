One: February 9th - Regarding the same matter, Kasuya Kazaemon Mototake was ordered to carry
　　the imperial proclamation and imperial decree to Edo, and departed from Fukui today

One: Same month, 12th - At Zuigenji Temple, from the 10th until today (two nights, three days), the seventh memorial
　　service for Matsukei-in-sama was conducted, and a memorial service was also
　　conducted at Nansenji Temple in Yanaka

One: March 3rd
　　Monomimi-sama made his first court visit for the seasonal festival and expressed his gratitude

One: Same month, 6th - An envoy was dispatched to Edo for the monthly inquiry about His Lordship's well-being,
　　presenting one type of fish offering. This envoy departed from Fukui today

One: Same month, 23rd - Rewards were granted to those who had perfect attendance for guard duty

One: Same month, 26th - Departed from Fukui and traveled via the Tōkaidō route

Note: Due to the court appointment in the previous December, this year it became a New Year's ceremony
One: January 3rd - For the celebration of the first Noh chanting of the year, through an envoy, the customary sake cups and
　　sake money were presented
One: Same month, 7th - For the first calligraphy ceremony, through an envoy, the customary delicacies were presented
One: Same day
　　Monomimi-sama made a court visit, expressed gratitude for being appointed to the Fourth Court Rank, and presented ceremonial sword,
　　horse money (ten ryō of gold), and court garments (five sets), after which he withdrew. At that time, regarding the matter of the imperial proclamation,
　　a letter from the senior councilors to Kyoto Deputy Governor Ogasawara Sado-no-kami Nagashige
　　was handed over by Abe Bungo-no-kami Masatake, which was received directly

　　　Note: The aforementioned letter was sent to the domain through foot messengers
One: Same month, 8th - Fishing boats in the bays within the domain territory encountered severe storms, and thirty boats
　　were wrecked
　　　Note: Of the 169 fishermen from these thirty boats, nine drowned and eighty-one went missing
One: Same month, 9th - Regarding the previous December (26th day)
　　appointment of Monomimi-sama to the Fourth Court Rank, envoy Nishio Nizaemon Mune■ was dispatched to Edo
　　for thanksgiving, departing from Fukui today
One: Same month, 11th - As thanks for the completion of the three-day New Year ceremonies, envoy
　　Azuma Shingorōemon Sukeshige was dispatched to Edo, presenting one type of delicacy. Shingorōemon

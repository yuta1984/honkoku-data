One: March 10th - From private quarters, five copies of three-piece items and five copies of two-piece items (completed by the engraver Watō as ordered) were presented (after consultation). These were submitted to Senior Councilor Tsuchiya Sagami-no-kami Masanao through messenger and deputy castellan Murakami Santayū Masamine. Regarding these presented items, a written notice came from Sagami-no-kami stating that they had been discussed with Chamberlain Makino Bingo-no-kami Narisada and the presentation had been completed.

One: April 14th - Through the imperial envoy Senior Councilor Tsuchiya Sagami-no-kami Masanao, leave to return to the domain was granted, and as customary, silver and lined silk garments were received.

When Sagami-no-kami came:
Chōkitsu-sama accompanied him and went out to welcome him to just outside the front gate's entrance
(Other than this, the treatment of the imperial envoy was as usual). After Sagami-no-kami's departure, courtesy visits were made to the Senior Councilors and Chamberlains as thanks.

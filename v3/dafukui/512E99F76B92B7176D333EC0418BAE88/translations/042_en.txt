One: September 1st - Nakatsukasa-daifu (Masakatsu) became seriously ill at his residence, and when this news arrived (by express messenger on the 25th of last month),御膳番 Katsuyama Togoro (Yukiteru) was dispatched by express horse for a visit of sympathy
　　　　　　However, Togoro arrived at Matsuoka on September 4th
One: Same month, 3rd - For the Chrysanthemum Festival congratulatory ceremony, a small-sleeved kimono was presented through an envoy as customary, and white silver was offered
One: Same day - Regarding Nakatsukasa-daifu (Masakatsu)'s illness
　　Masaaki and Takumi-no-kami (Masahira) went together to Roju Okubo Kaga-no-kami (Tadatomo)

　　After private consultation, they requested that a physician be dispatched for the illness, and also that Takumi-no-kami be granted leave to go to the residence for nursing care. Both of these petition documents were submitted to the duty officer Tsuchiya Sagami-no-kami (Masanao)
One: Same day evening - Regarding the same matter, the duty officer Tsuchiya Sagami-no-kami (Masanao) summoned the caretaker Hara Heizaemon (Masatake), who appeared and was informed that the request regarding Takumi-no-kami (Masahira) had been approved as petitioned. Consequently, he made courtesy visits to the Roju and chamberlains to express gratitude
One: September 5th - 御奏者番 Mizoguchi Ichizaemon (Takashige) was ordered by the monthly duty senior retainer to go to Matsuoka daily to inquire about the patient's condition and report the situation to Edo

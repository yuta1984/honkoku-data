One: 7th day of the 1st month - For the first ascent to the castle of the year, through a messenger, fish delicacies were presented as usual
One: 11th day of the same month - Congratulatory ceremonies were held for the display of armor and mochi (rice cakes)
　　Kenmotsu-sama also took his seat together, the chief retainer was appointed to attend, and afterwards
　　mochi were bestowed upon the various samurai
One: 12th day of the same month
　　Together with Kenmotsu-sama, they visited Yanagisawa Dewa-no-kami Yasuaki, the chamberlain, and
　　submitted a request to attend lecture sessions
　　　　However, on the 26th of the same month, the same request was submitted again
One: 3rd day of the 2nd month
　　Regarding the arrangement that Tsunamasa-sama's relief rice would be received in three installments from this year,
　　concerning the matter of endorsing the certificate, through messenger and
　　castellan Hara Heizaemon Masatake, a request was made to the duty officer Toda Yamashiro-no-kami Tadamasa, and the endorsement was prepared and delivered
One: 19th day of the same month
　　When they attended the castle together with Kenmotsu-sama (yesterday on the 18th, an official notice signed by the senior councilors arrived),
　　as requested, it was communicated by the senior councilors that permission would be granted to attend the lectures on the coming 22nd day, and in gratitude for this,
　　courtesy visits were made to the senior councilors, chamberlains, and junior councilors

Lord Chōkichi received congratulatory礼 in the inner study. The chief retainer and others including Sakai Gonshichi and Takaya Zen'emon Shige[?] offered their congratulations and were given lengthy gifts. Then in the middle study, the close attendants offered their congratulations and were given lengthy gifts. After that, in the front study, from the various commanders to the members of the guard units, all offered congratulations and were given lengthy gifts through intermediaries. In the middle study, the physicians offered their congratulations and were given lengthy gifts. Subsequently, the inner and outer pages offered their congratulations and were given lengthy gifts through intermediaries. Various officials, foot soldiers, stable hands, and record keepers were granted audiences as they passed through.

One: On June 7th, the messenger from Edo, Ihara Genbee Yorinori, having arrived in Fukui yesterday on the 6th, the monthly duty chief retainer announced to all monthly duty officers at the main castle that, as petitioned, Lord Chōkichi had been officially proclaimed as an adopted son.

One: On the 9th of the same month, the reconstruction of the Kokuryū Daimyōjin shrine at Mount Atago in Asuwa District, which had been ordered, was completed, and the ceremonial transfer of the deity took place today.

One: On the 18th of the same month, due to the summer heat, local products of wheat noodles and dried sea bream were presented as usual through a messenger.

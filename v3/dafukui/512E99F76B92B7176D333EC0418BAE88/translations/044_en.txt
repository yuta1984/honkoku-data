One: November 25th - Through imperial messenger Yanagibara Iori, they received cranes from the shogun's falconry as usual
One: December 4th - Regarding the presentation of congratulatory gifts for the Double Ninth Festival, an official letter was delivered at the castle, and retainer gifts followed precedent
One: Same month, 9th - Due to the cold season, they made rounds to the Roju and chamberlains to inquire about the shogun's well-being
One: Same month, 10th - At Kokenji Temple, from dawn of the 9th until this morning, the 50th memorial service for Reigan Kosetsu was conducted

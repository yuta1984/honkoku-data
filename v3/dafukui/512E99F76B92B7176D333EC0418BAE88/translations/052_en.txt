An audience was granted, and afterwards in the Tartar Room, cuisine (three soups and nine dishes) was served, and gifts were directly presented by his lordship. Kōkenji Temple and Chintokuji Temple were commanded to accompany, and in the great hall, three head priests together were granted an audience, and after his lordship's entrance, cuisine (two soups and seven dishes) was bestowed.

One: On the 25th day of the 6th month, he went out to tour the coastal areas (today to Yoshizaki Bay, lodging at Shimbo Bay on the 5th day of the 7th month, then returning to the palace).

One: On the 27th day of the same month, for the customary summer inquiry about his lordship's health, messenger Masugi Shozaemon Nobuho arrived in Edo yesterday, and today appeared before the duty officer Toda Yamashiro-no-kami Tadamasa (accompanied by residence keeper Matsubara Zenzaemon) and presented an official letter.

One: On the same day, for the same purpose, messenger Masugi Shozaemon Nobuho appeared at the castle (accompanied by residence keeper Hara Heizaemon) and delivered one box of tribute fish (dried sea bream).

One: On the 29th day of the same month, there was a summons from duty officer Toda Yamashiro-no-kami Tadamasa, and messenger Masugi Shozaemon Nobuho appeared (accompanied by residence keeper Matsubara Zenzaemon), whereupon a reply letter was delivered.

One: On the 6th day of the 7th month, for the celebration of the birth viewing ceremony, gold was presented through a messenger as customary.

One: On the 9th day of the same month, a certificate for firearm inspection was submitted to Edo today.

One: On the 18th day of the same month, regarding Nakatsukasa-no-taifu Masakatsu-sama's illness, word came from Edo that his condition was critical, so messenger Kawamura Tohachi Ujichika was dispatched from Edo for a visit of sympathy, departing from Fukui today.

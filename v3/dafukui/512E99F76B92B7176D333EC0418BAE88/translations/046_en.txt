Genroku 6, Year of the Water Rooster (1693)

One: New Year's Day, 1st month - For New Year's congratulations, they attended the castle and presented a ceremonial sword and horse substitute as usual, and received gofuku (silk garments) as a reward
　　Kenmotsu-sama also attended the castle, presented a ceremonial sword and horse substitute as usual, and received gofuku as a reward
One: Same day - New Year's ceremonial rites were held at the residence
One: Same month, 3rd day - For the New Year's first Noh chanting celebration, through a messenger, they presented sake cup stands and sake money as usual

One: April 26th - From his private residence, he presented two pairs of folding screens (one pair depicting monkeys in willow robes by Kano Doun, one pair depicting red and white plum blossoms by Kano Yobo) 
　　They were presented to Senior Councilor Tsuchiya Sagami-no-kami (Masanao) through the envoy and resident official
　　Hara Heizaemon (Masatake). A formal notification of the successful presentation came
　　from Sagami-no-kami:
　　　　　　Regarding the folding screens you presented, I consulted with Makino Bingo-no-kami and
　　　　　　successfully presented them privately with good results, so please be
　　　　　　at ease. I write to inform you of this matter. Above
　　　　　　　　April 26th　　　　Tsuchiya Sagami-no-kami
　　　　　　　　To Matsudaira Hyobu-no-taiyu

One: May 3rd - At Toeisan Temple,
　　for the 13th anniversary memorial service of Genyuin, to inquire after his well-being,
　　he sent envoy and resident official Kaga Tozaemon to present one set of cypress boxes
One: Same month, 8th - For the same matter,
　　as the Shogun was visiting Toeisan Temple, he performed advance preparatory duties
One: Same day - For the same matter, a tea ceremony was conducted at Manpukuzan
One: Same month, 9th - For the same matter, through envoy Nagami Jiroemon (Yoshimasa, wearing deep court dress and long hakama),
　　he presented ten pieces of silver as incense money to the sutra hall at Toeisan Temple
　　Monomimi-sama also sent envoy Idebuchi Heibei (Morikyo) to present three pieces of silver as incense money

One: On the 20th day of the 10th month, messenger Hori Kinshichi Narishige for the monthly inquiry about His Lordship's well-being arrived in Edo yesterday, the 19th, and today presented himself to the duty officer Toda Yamashiro-no-kami Tadamasa-dono (accompanied by caretaker Matsubara Zenzaemon), submitting the official letter.
However, Kinshichi departed from the domain on the 9th of last month.

One: On the 21st day of the same month, the same messenger Hori Kinshichi Narishige presented himself at the castle (accompanied by caretaker Matsubara Zenzaemon) and presented one box of offering fish (dried whiting).

One: On the 22nd day of the same month, messenger Hori Kinshichi Narishige was summoned by the duty officer Toda Yamashiro-no-kami Tadamasa-dono and when he presented himself (accompanied by caretaker Hara Heizaemon), the reply letter was delivered through a retainer.
However, although Yamashiro-no-kami-dono was supposed to deliver it personally, the retainer explained that due to his many duties, it was delivered through an intermediary.

One: On the 25th day of the same month, His Lordship departed for Mikuni for falconry.
However, the date of return to the residence is unknown.

One: On the 18th day of the 11th month, His Lordship departed for Yoshie for falconry (returned to the residence on the 19th).

One: On the 25th day of the same month, an official letter regarding the Chōyō festival congratulatory offerings was delivered at the castle, with gifts for retainers distributed as customary.

One: On the 18th day of the 12th month, for year-end congratulations, a kosode was presented through a messenger as customary, and white silver was offered.

One: On the same day, Gennnosuke Nobutomi-sama, the second son of Yamato-no-kami Naonori-sama, became the adopted son of Echigo-no-kami Mitsunaga-sama.

Bunsei 1st year (1818)

▲Regarding haifuki silver and other silver trading at the silver mint
1. Concerning silver leaf - Same purpose as Tenmei 6 (1786), Kansei 10 (1798), and Bunka 2 (1805)
　　　　February

●This time, for the sake of circulation in society, new two-bu ban gold coins have been ordered to be minted. Two pieces of these two-bu ban shall be calculated as one ryō of gold.
　Furthermore, in exchanging with silver coins and copper coins, understand this at the same rate as koban (one-ryō coins) and ichi-bu ban (one-bu coins), and ensure smooth circulation by using them together without delay.
　This directive shall also be communicated to all provinces.　April

●Regarding the commencement of two-bu ban circulation from the 10th of next month
1. Not only cut gold pieces of five bu or more from koban, but also damaged gold coins below that amount shall be exchanged without distinction and free of charge for koban, two-bu ban, one-bu ban, and two-shu ban. Bring them to the persons listed on the separate paper.
1. The cutting fee for two-bu ban shall be the same as for one-bu ban.
　　　　May

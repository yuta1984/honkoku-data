　　　　Copy of Proclamation from Senior Inspectors

▲○In rural areas throughout the provinces, many people have recently been recklessly setting wildfires, causing hardship to the people. Henceforth, setting wildfires is strictly prohibited. If there are any violators, they shall be immediately apprehended and, after investigation, severely punished. In government domains, magistrates and deputies, and in private domains, lords and local administrators shall conduct strict investigations. When wildfire setters are spotted, regardless of whether in other domains or under other jurisdictions, they shall be pursued and captured without distinction, and handed over to the nearest magistrate's office or appropriate authority. This directive shall be proclaimed without exception to all government domains, private domains, and temple/shrine lands.
　　　　　Intercalary 3rd Month
　　　　Same

▲○In this Year of the Tiger, many provinces have suffered crop failures and rice shortages. Therefore, sake brewers shall reduce their usual production by one-third, producing only two-thirds of their normal output. If anyone engages in secret brewing, not only that person but also the local officials shall be investigated and severely punished. To avoid any misunderstanding, in government domains, magistrates, deputies, and administrators of entrusted territories, and in private domains, lords and local administrators shall promptly issue proclamations. This shall be proclaimed accordingly.

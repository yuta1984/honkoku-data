1. Kanen 1, year of the Dragon - Copy of circular from the Great Inspectors

○When Morisui Hikotarō's assistants travel to various provinces for weight and measure inspections, arrogant behavior and inappropriate conduct have been reported at various locations. If such behavior occurs in the future, Hikotarō will be severely punished, so he should be instructed to exercise caution and careful supervision.

The above was communicated to the Head of Artisans, so it should be transmitted for general knowledge. Furthermore, if arrogant behavior or inappropriate conduct occurs at various locations, the matter should be reported by the domain lords, and this should be communicated to all concerned parties. 5th month - Communicated only to the Town Magistrates - 29th day of 5th month

Copy of documents distributed to officials at the Government Accounting Office

▲○In government territory villages, when peasants and townspeople adopt surnames and wear swords, if there are exceptional circumstances, this should be done after inquiry and according to government instructions through the magistrate's office. The magistrate's office cannot easily authorize such matters. However, if there are those who adopt surnames, after investigation appropriate punishment should be administered. Investigation should be conducted to determine if there are such individuals elsewhere, and reforms should be made to prevent improper behavior.

Dragon year, 11th month, 3rd day

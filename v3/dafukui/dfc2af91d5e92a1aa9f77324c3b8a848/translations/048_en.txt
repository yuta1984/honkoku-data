Copy of an official directive distributed by the Bakufu's Finance Office

○In recent years, rice prices have continued to be high, causing hardship for the common people. Although this year's harvest was good, people have become inclined to hoard rice in preparation for potential crop failures, making it particularly difficult for rice prices to fall, as we have heard. While it is natural to prepare for bad years, if people focus only on hoarding at this time, the circulation of rice shipments and sales to Osaka may naturally deteriorate. Should circulation become poor, the common people will suffer hardship, which would be pitiful. Therefore, it is essential at this time to ship rice not only to Osaka but also to Edo and other places under respective jurisdictions, and to increase rice sales as much as possible. If this is achieved, it will benefit the common people and naturally resolve the pitiful circumstances. Therefore, please give this careful consideration, issue instructions to your respective subordinates, and also give instructions within your domains. Please take the above to heart. The above intent should be proclaimed. 12th month of the Year of the Sheep

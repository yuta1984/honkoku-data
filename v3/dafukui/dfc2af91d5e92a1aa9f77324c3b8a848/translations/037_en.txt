One, Anei 5, Year of the Monkey　12th month
One, Regarding all daimyo houses: concerning documents and notifications issued by the senior inspectors (ōmetsuke), matters concerning your house have until now been communicated through circular notices distributed uniformly to seats in the great hall. However, from now on, when such circular notices are issued, it is desired that your house be separately notified through special separate documents. When this was communicated to the current monthly duty officer, Tanuma Tonomo-no-kami, on the 6th of this month Tonomo-no-kami summoned the resident representatives, and Kobayashi Mataemon attended. It was then proclaimed through an official notice that henceforth the senior inspectors would issue separate circular communications.

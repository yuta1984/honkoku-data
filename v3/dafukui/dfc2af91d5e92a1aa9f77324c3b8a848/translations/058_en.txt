【Part hidden by the pasted paper on the right page of frame 57】

○Regarding the matter of rice prices announced in the 7th month of Tenmei 8 (1788), concerning stipend rice for retainers of various daimyo houses in Edo: Understanding the directive that each domain should endeavor to send rice from their own territories as much as possible, until further notice, stipend rice for Edo should be sent from the domains as much as possible.
This directive should be communicated without fail to all those with holdings of 10,000 koku or more.　10th month

○The hundred-day education program for students that Taki Kōjuin conducted annually at the Medical Academy is temporarily suspended. From now on, daily lectures and readings will be held, so retainers of retainers (hatamoto vassals) and town doctors may freely attend and listen at their convenience. For details, inquiries should be made to the Medical Academy. Also, those who have been making annual donations to the Medical Academy need no longer submit them. This should be announced accordingly.
　　　　10th month

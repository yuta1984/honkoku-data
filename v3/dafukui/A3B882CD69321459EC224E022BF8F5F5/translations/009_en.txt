• On May 6th, as the construction of the Asakusa residence was completed, [the lord] moved there today.
• On August 5th, Honda Awaji-no-kami died. As he was an uncle on the mother's side, [the lord] observed half the usual period of mourning.
• On September 1st, as Kinnosuke-sama made his first appearance at the castle and was granted an audience, congratulations were offered through the messenger Shimada Uemon with a ceremonial sword and silver horse substitute, two kinds in one package.
• On September 2nd,
  As Munemasa-sama was coming to Edo, [the lord] went to the Tokiwabashi residence to receive him.
• On September 4th, for the same reason as above, as an official messenger was coming, [the lord] went to the Tokiwabashi residence. The messenger met with Senior Councilor Ando Tsushima-no-kami. {{Marginal note: Also met with Rokugo Shuma}}
• On September 7th, word came that Iori-sama was critically ill in his domain, so a foot messenger {{Marginal note: Samue Yogotayu}} was dispatched on a seven-day journey to visit him.
  However, when Yogotayu reached Odawara, word came of [Iori's] death, so he returned.
• On September 21st, regarding the current economizing measures, it was ordered that of the ten attendants who had been accompanying the palanquin, two should be reduced, the horses should also be temporarily reduced, and tea and lunch boxes should be discontinued when going to the upper residence.

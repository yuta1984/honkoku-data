   impoverished, living with elderly relatives including his grandmother, mother, 
   uncle, and household members. His wife 
   has entrusted their two children to her family's care,

   Upon returning from this arrangement, he has cared for the aforementioned three elderly persons.

   He has accepted this responsibility, and moreover, his reputation as a 
   filial person has long been known. Therefore, as a reward, 
   Tadauemon has been granted 
   six to of rice.

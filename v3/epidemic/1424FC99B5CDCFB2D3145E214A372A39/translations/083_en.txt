May 29th
One, for the memorial day of Lord Kenshō'in,
　the chief retainer performed the proxy incense offering
　　Ceremonial offering: one bundle of incense sticks
One, Yonekura Gonbei came
　to pay his respects, so an audience was granted in the Komatsu room,
　where rice cakes and confections were served

June 1st
One, there was a celebration on this day
One, Lord Bizen-no-kami made his descent today and
　stopped by here. However,
　due to matters concerning His Lordship's illness,
　visits from outsiders were declined,
　and he departed directly to Nishioka through the back gate

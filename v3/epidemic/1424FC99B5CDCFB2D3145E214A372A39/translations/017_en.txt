1. Regarding the daughter of the deceased Ihei from Yamauchi Ōgushi Village, who has reached three years of age:
   Last year due to epidemic disease,
   both parents and siblings all perished, leaving her orphaned.
   Therefore, upon reporting this matter to the proper authorities, five hyō of rice
   was provided as support, and she was ordered to become
   the adopted daughter of a farmer named Zenemon from the same village.
   The written pledge that the aforementioned Zenemon
   submitted to the magistrate's office
   reads as follows:
   　　　Memorandum
   　Ine, daughter of the deceased Ihei from Ōgushi Village,
   　who has reached three years of age. Her parents and siblings
   　all fell ill with the epidemic disease last winter
   　and perished, leaving only the three-year-old daughter
   　remaining, who is being raised by the village

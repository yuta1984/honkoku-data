One. Regarding Chikuzen-no-kami's visit to inspect the Nagasaki guard post, he will stay overnight tonight at Ushizu on his route. For this occasion, the district magistrates, chief administrators, and other officials were to dispatch and serve as messengers for the inspection visit, but this was declined, so there will be none.

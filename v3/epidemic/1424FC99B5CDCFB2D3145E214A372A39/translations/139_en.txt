　　　September 3rd
One. Bizen-no-kami-sama was supposed to visit our domain today, however, regarding his lordship's restraint, yesterday's notification was acknowledged, and furthermore at Saga

It was heard that our domain's relatives were also exercising restraint. Therefore, Bizen-no-kami-sama was also [advised] regarding restraint. Today a messenger was sent to the second compound to convey this, so the visit to our domain was cancelled. This was communicated from Karashima.

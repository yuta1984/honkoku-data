One. Aihara Sajibyōe returned last night from Osaka. Tanaka Kobyōe also arrived at the same time.

September 14th
September 15th

One. Regarding the matter of the lord's retirement, orders were previously given to the second bailey to refrain from activities. Due to this period of restraint, celebratory ceremonies for that day will not be held.

One. For the same reason as above, festival rituals and Shinto ceremonies have been postponed, and naturally the sacred ropes (shimenawa) have also been discontinued.

One. At Tenzan Shrine, proxy worship and intermediary duties are being performed for the lord's birthday.

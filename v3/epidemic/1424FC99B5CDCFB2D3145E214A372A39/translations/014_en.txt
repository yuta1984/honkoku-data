1. Regarding the retirement seating arrangements for captains (bantō) and military commanders (monotō):
   They have been changed to the next seat after the unassigned group (mugumi).
   In connection with this, regarding the seating for various congratulatory ceremonies,
   congratulatory gifts are to be prepared in the outer first tatami area.

1. When promoted from inspector (metsuke) or messenger (tsukaiban) to the main group (hongumi):
   Until now, during group reassignments, they were excluded from various congratulatory ceremonies,
   but from now on, they shall receive congratulations as military commanders (monotō).

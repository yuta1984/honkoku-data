November 11th
　　Same　　12th
　　Same　　13th
1. Due to the death of Teiseiin-sama, various matters
　including mourning restrictions that had been
　ordered from the 3rd are to be lifted as follows:
　1. The prohibition on shaving the forehead (monthly shaving)
　　for retainers is lifted from tomorrow, the 14th
　1. The mourning restrictions in the domain territory
　　are lifted from tomorrow, the 14th. However,
　　all are ordered to remain in a state of
　　restraint until the 49th day
　　The above orders were issued and also
　　sent to Yamashiro district

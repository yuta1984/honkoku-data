One, the Gion Festival celebration is to be postponed
to the fifteenth day of the eighth month due to this year's circumstances.
The notification to Ninomaru and other matters, the details of which
were recorded on the twenty-fifth day of last month.
　　Sixteenth day of the sixth month
　　Same, seventeenth day

One, regarding the seventeenth night and eighteenth hall at Entsu-ji temple

To be conducted as in previous years, however, regarding the matter of security,
since the Gion Festival and others have also been postponed,
there should be no pilgrims coming from other places,
therefore security forces need not be dispatched.
Understanding this, the temple requested
that Entsu-ji be excused from this requirement,
and accordingly (security) was not dispatched.

1. For the first memorial day of Lord Kōtoku-in and Lord Chōju-in,
　the head of the incense deputies performed the duties, with no monetary offering.
1. The peasants from the Omura encampment at Isahaya Obunakoshi
　came as is customary, so they were given sake and
　one kan-mon of copper coins. This was at the discretion of the kitchen magistrate.
　　　1st month, 27th day

1. This morning at exactly the sixth hour (6 AM), a Noh performance
　was held. It was a Noh performance with three variations of "Okina."
　The musicians for "Okina" wore
　plain robes, court caps, chorus sashes,
　and long court dress. The performance roster was as follows:
　　　Okina

July 9th　Death of the Shogunate's Young Lord
One, Shogunate
　Regarding the death of the Young Lord, from the coming Ninomaru
　the following notification has been issued:
　　Regarding the Young Lord's illness,
　　medical treatment was unsuccessful and on the
　　24th of last month he passed away
　
　　A report has come from Edo, therefore from now
　　until the coming 17th, for ten days
　　throughout the domain, restraint shall be ordered
　　noh chanting, wild dancing, musical instruments, etc. shall be suspended
　　this shall be earnestly communicated through
　　all channels, the officials have stated. The above.
　　Ox year, July 8th　　Ninomaru Administrative Office

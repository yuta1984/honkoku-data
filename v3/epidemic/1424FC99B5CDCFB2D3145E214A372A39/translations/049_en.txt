As you have reached an advanced age,
　your repeated request for retirement
　has been granted as petitioned.
　However, regarding official duties, you shall
　continue to be consulted as before,
　as has been decreed. Furthermore, the stipend rice

　of thirty koku that has been provided to you
　shall continue as retirement
　allowance,
　as has been ordered. [You are instructed] to convey
　all these matters with proper courtesy,
　as has been decreed.
　The above.

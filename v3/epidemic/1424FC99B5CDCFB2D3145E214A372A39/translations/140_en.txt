September 5th
　　　Same 6th
　　　Same 7th
One. Regarding Miyachi Nihei, there was urgent business and
　he was dispatched to Edo, which was
　communicated by the current official
One. Regarding his lordship's retirement, the succession
　being granted to Rintaro-sama as requested
　was decided, and this
　was communicated to the
　retainers at the seven o'clock hour this evening as official business
　in the Komatsu room by means of an official document with the lord's seal. Thereupon
　there were joint signatures on the acknowledgment document. Details are
　recorded in a separate ledger

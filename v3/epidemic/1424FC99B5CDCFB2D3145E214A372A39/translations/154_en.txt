September 29th
One. Jinpachi, a servant of Jindai Taichirō who was residing in a makeshift hut within his master's estate grounds, was found dead this morning, having hanged himself. As he was a solitary person and the circumstances of his death were unknown, the assistant inspector conducted an investigation, obtained a certificate from Taichirō's servants, and handed over the corpse to the said servants.

February 28th
One item: At half past the eighth hour this morning, with his retinue assembled,
　[the lord] went out for falconry/hunting
　On this occasion, both Ogi and Oka towns were ordered
　to provide hospitality and beaters [for the hunt]
One item: Regarding Zushō-dono, who had previously
　submitted a request for retirement,
　his petition was granted as requested, and
　the family headship was definitely
　conferred upon Uneme-dono, as was
　officially announced. To convey this decision,
　the envoys Tanomo-dono and Jūdayū-dono
　went to his residence, and the verbal message was as follows:
　　　To Zushō-dono

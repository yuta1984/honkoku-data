Regarding the execution of the thirty-third anniversary memorial
service for Lord Junshin-in
at Shōjō-ji temple:

One, on the first day of the twelfth month, a prohibition on the taking of life
within the domain has been ordered

One, light offenders who had been sentenced by his lordship
within the retainer households are to be pardoned

The above notification having been received, it is to be
thoroughly communicated down to the lowest levels. The above.

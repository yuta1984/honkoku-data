1. Regarding Kawanami Sukē-emon's retired son Magohei

　The year before last, he broke into
　the ageya (entertainment house) on our side and
　took refuge at Saga Council Office, so
　gradually his relatives and others were investigated,
　and on the 26th of last month, with the chief retainer
　present, the matter was settled, so Magohei was
　entrusted to the private domain office, as reported.

March 16th
One, Jinnai Bunzaemon had misconduct, so
　he was recently ordered to retire and become a ronin,
　whereupon his younger brother Tokujirou was
　ordered to continue the family line. The same person was summoned and
　informed by the current officials
One, Recently continuous rainfall in the district during the wheat crop's
　ripening season, so prayers for stopping the rain were
　ordered to Fukuchi-in temple, from the 10th of last month until
　today the rituals were performed. Payment in silver:
　2 pieces
One, Regarding various petitions from this February, official notifications
　were conveyed by Zensaemon-dono at Komatsu-ma through written documents

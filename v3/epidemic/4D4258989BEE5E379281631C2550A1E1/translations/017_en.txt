One Method
　Decoct raw rehmannia alone thickly, and use two or three mouthfuls while the child has not yet cried even once after birth. That child will be free from disease and avoid smallpox.

One Method
　Put one white-necked earthworm into a chicken egg, cook it thoroughly on the day of Beginning of Spring (Risshun), and if you make the child eat it, smallpox will be mild.

○Soak white poplar wood-boring insects in soy sauce, roast them and make the child eat them. If used when pox erupts, it cures itching and eliminates toxin. They should always be finely chopped and eaten.

○If you soak rat meat in soy sauce, roast it and eat it finely chopped, smallpox will be mild. I have also tried both methods and experienced their effectiveness.

○Using charred cuckoo makes smallpox mild. My father recorded its effectiveness.

○Also, boiling wild yam and making them drink the juice, as well as bathing in it, is good. I have heard that this is exclusively used in Nanto, Yamato Province.

○Also, making them eat hovenia fruit is good. Decocting branches of pear trees and making them drink it is also said to make smallpox mild.

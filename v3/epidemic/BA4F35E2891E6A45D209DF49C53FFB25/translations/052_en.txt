　One　When there are rumors of a smallpox epidemic, one should first carefully teach the household members that smallpox is a disease contracted only through contact infection, and therefore is easier to escape than other diseases. For children three years and older who can understand language, one should teach them daily, multiple times, that smallpox is a terrible disease, and that approaching it will cause contact infection leading to death, pockmarked face, blindness, or disability, and one should instruct them to fear it. Because honest children's hearts will be frightened and keep their distance, this becomes a great help in prevention methods.

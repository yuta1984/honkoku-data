One　When there are rumors of a smallpox epidemic, first teach the household members carefully that smallpox is contracted only through contact infection, and therefore is easier to avoid than other diseases. For children three years and older who can understand language, you should teach them daily, multiple times, that smallpox is a terrifying disease, and if they get close they will become infected and may die, be left with pockmarks, become blind, or become disabled, instructing them to fear it. Because they will fear it and stay away with their honest child-like hearts, this becomes a great help in prevention.

One　When smallpox is epidemic, you should tell all relatives and acquaintances in advance that you are avoiding smallpox, and strictly refuse to receive any gifts, food, or anything else until after the smallpox has completely recovered. If there are any gifts sent, you should immediately dispose of them in water. Even smallpox households sending gifts is extremely wrong.

One　The greatest misunderstanding in society is thinking that mild smallpox transmits mildly, and therefore becoming familiar with and approaching mild smallpox cases. This is extremely bad. The poison of all diseases is like fire, and the human body is like kindling. It is no different from a small fire becoming a great conflagration. Even with mild smallpox, if the infected person has a constitution with deep smallpox poison, it will immediately consume their body. When you see three or four people lying side by side sick in one house, with various degrees of mild and severe cases, you should understand that contracting it from mild cases doesn't make it mild, and contracting it from severe cases doesn't make it severe.

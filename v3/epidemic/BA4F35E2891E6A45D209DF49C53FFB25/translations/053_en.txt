Especially foolish people believe that there are smallpox deities - some that cause severe illness and others that cause mild illness - and they take their children to houses with mild smallpox cases to let them touch the patients. However, this is more dangerous than approaching a raging fire. It is something that must never be done.

One　When there is a smallpox epidemic during travel, you must not stay at inns where there are smallpox cases. This awareness is essential even when just resting. You must bring your own lunch and not carelessly buy and eat food along the way. Noble persons should have all clothing of smallpox patients, along with everything that has been in contact with the patients, moved to other houses before staying, and then there will be no infection. Since smallpox is a disease that spreads by infection and gradually circulates, it does not spread over distances of dozens of ri. Since you pass through epidemic areas in just about one or two days, prevention methods during travel are actually much easier than when staying at home.

One　When encountering smallpox patients on the road, you should avoid smelling their odor. You do not become infected just by seeing them with your eyes. At such times, you should moisten your nostrils with water. The cleanliness of water has a wonderful effect in avoiding the defilement of smallpox poison. When there is no water, you should moisten your nostrils with saliva. As long as you feel coolness in your nose, it prevents the heat of smallpox poison. Since saliva is the pure liquid of the human body, it has no taste or cloudiness and is no different from the rain and dew of heaven and earth. Try moistening your nostrils with saliva when facing bad odors. You will immediately stop sensing that bad odor. This is proof that it also avoids the defiled air of smallpox poison.

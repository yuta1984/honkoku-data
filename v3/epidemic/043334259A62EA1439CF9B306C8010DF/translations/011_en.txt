Drinking water is of utmost importance, and the difference between clean and unclean water is as great as the difference between the moon and a turtle - this is no different from the benefits and harms of clean versus polluted air. Therefore, whether it be river water or well water, it must either be filtered through sand once or boiled before it can be drunk. However, those who use well water must pay particular attention when there are unclean things nearby such as ditches, sewers, toilets, or garbage dumps, as the putrid liquid from these seeps into the soil and enters the well.

　　On Smallpox in Pregnant Women
Smallpox during pregnancy is extremely severe. The key is simply to prevent the fetus from being aborted.
If there is no disease within the abdomen, one should avoid massaging and rubbing the belly.
Those who miscarry during the four to five days (eruption period) can be cured through treatment. When the fetus is aborted during the suppuration
period, this is extremely adverse. If the fetus falls after ten days, there are also treatments available. All
cases of miscarriage are not due to blood heat but are necessarily symptoms of qi deficiency, therefore one should
make qi-supplementing medicines the first priority. Great ginseng should be used appropriately. One must diligently observe
the prohibitions.

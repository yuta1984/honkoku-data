When it has not yet emerged, decoct 1 ryō of madder root (akane) thickly in water, mix it in equal parts with the previous loofah juice, and give it to the child to drink - the smallpox will become extremely mild.

Sesame Oil Rubbing Method (Ma-yu-ra-hō)
When smallpox is about to manifest, apply sesame oil to three fingers of the hand, then apply it to the child's head, forehead, crown and back, lower back, both wrists, and both ankles, then let them sleep. The smallpox becomes extremely mild.

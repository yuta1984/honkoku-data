Four Sheddings Pills (Shidatsu-tan)
　Cicada molt　Snake skin　Phoenix platform (chicken eggshells)　Immortal shedding (parents' fingernails)
　Roast these four ingredients in equal portions, powder them, and form into pills the size of mung beans with honey. Take 1 monme each year on the night of Setsubun to prevent smallpox.

Black Rabbit Pills (Gento-tan)
　Scrophularia (5 ryō) Cuscuta seeds (10 ryō)
　Avoid iron when powdering these two ingredients, knead with brown sugar into pills the size of soapberry seeds, and take three pills daily with sugar water.

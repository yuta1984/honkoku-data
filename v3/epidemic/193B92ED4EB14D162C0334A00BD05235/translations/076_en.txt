They fired water cannons made with medicinal liquid like rain, so the medicinal vapor filled the air like fog, and the Syphilis Army could not open their eyes. From the front, Enjumaru increased his valor tenfold from the beginning, kicking down the confused syphilis soldiers with his horse's hooves, striking them down under his spear, and rampaging with complete disregard for others. Ketsunetsu and Kotsu-itami were also personally fighting to fend off the medicine soldiers, but when Enjumaru saw this from afar, he greatly rejoiced and said "There are the enemies I've been hoping for!" He galloped over in one dash, flashed his great spear and thrust with a sharp strike. Pitiably, Kotsu-itami-ugokazu had blood smoke rise from his chest, let out one long scream, and fell headfirst from his horse. Ketsunetsu's soul flew beyond the heavens, and he struck his horse and fled, so the remaining soldiers also collapsed entirely and fled in defeat toward Inkei-do. Thousands of medicine troops pursued relentlessly from behind, and the disease bandits ran without looking aside, trusting to their feet.

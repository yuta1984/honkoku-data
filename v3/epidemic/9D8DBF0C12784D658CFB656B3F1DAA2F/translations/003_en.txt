　　　A Discourse on Cowpox
The experiment of inoculating cowpox into the human body to prevent smallpox began in England, and subsequently Western nations tried this method. In China, from the Jiaqing era (1796-1820) to the present, everyone has praised it, and books titled "New Methods of Smallpox Inoculation" have already been written. In our country as well, for thirty years physicians have desired this, requesting it from Dutch people who come to Nagasaki by ship, obtaining the vaccine material, and have tried and inoculated it several times up to now.

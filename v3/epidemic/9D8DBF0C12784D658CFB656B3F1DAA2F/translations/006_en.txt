If left neglected, one will certainly contract natural smallpox. Therefore, it is essential to distinguish between true and false pox. Although we call this material cowpox, since it was first taken from cattle pox in ancient times and has since passed through tens of millions of human bodies, "cow" is merely a name, and it should be called a type of benign pox. If the vaccination method is performed correctly, even if applied to hundreds of millions of people, there will be no errors. Let the people of the world not doubt this.

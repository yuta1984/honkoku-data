　　　　　Preface to Methods for Nursing Smallpox Patients
　1. This book records practical matters that households with smallpox patients should keep in mind. I hope that by keeping this in remote mountain villages and isolated places far from doctors, it may serve as some help in saving lives during times of suffering and mortal danger. Since it is intended for illiterate common people and women to understand easily, I have deliberately avoided ornate language and clarified the meaning entirely in hiragana script.

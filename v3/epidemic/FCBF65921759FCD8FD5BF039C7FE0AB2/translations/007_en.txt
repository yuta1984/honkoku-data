Question: Where did the cowpox that is spreading this time come from? Since it is unusual, I find it somewhat suspicious.

Answer: It was brought by a Dutch person named Mohnike last year. While it is unusual in Japan, it has already been practiced in the West for over seventy years, and currently there are few countries worldwide where it has not spread. According to the "Complete Book of Variolation" (Yin Dou Quan Shu), it has been about forty years since it was transmitted to China. Moreover, in China, offices have been established in Beijing and other regions, selecting excellent people to implement this practice, and wealthy people are made to contribute funds,

Despite the vastness of all nations, the fact that this has spread so suddenly in less than a hundred years is certainly not due to human power alone, but entirely due to the divine will of the gods of heaven and earth. Cowpox was transmitted early to countries where smallpox spread early, and to countries like Japan where it was transmitted late, cowpox has only recently been transmitted - this seems to have its own reasons.

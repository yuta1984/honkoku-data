Prevention and Treatment of Diphtheria

1. It is essential to pay constant attention to prevent children from catching colds.

2. If a child shows one or two suspicious symptoms such as chills or shivering, fever, throat pain, pharyngeal redness, pseudomembrane formation, hoarseness, or characteristic cough, it is necessary to immediately seek medical examination.

3. If diphtheria can be diagnosed early in this way, it can be cured by diphtheria serum injection. Even if it is not this disease, no harm will come from the injection. Therefore, in suspicious cases, it is better to apply serum therapy early and immediately.

4. If medical examination is delayed and consequently the timing of serum injection is late, even the injection will be ineffective and will inevitably result in death.

(B) Prevention methods during infectious disease outbreaks

(1) The aforementioned peacetime prevention methods must be implemented even more strictly. Pay special attention to fly extermination, and it is advisable to eat and drink inside mosquito nets.

(2) The harmful practice of concealment must be corrected.

(3) It is essential to discover suspicious patients as early as possible and have them examined by physicians.

(4) Physicians residing in this prefecture who examine patients with typhoid, paratyphoid, dysentery, and suspected dysentery cases must report these cases.

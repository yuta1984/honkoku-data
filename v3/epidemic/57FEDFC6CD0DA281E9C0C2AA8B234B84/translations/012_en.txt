Many people die due to incorrect treatment methods and the carelessness of those nursing them. However, the families of patients do not know that their care was inadequate, and they all say it was fate. Nevertheless, for those who died without ever using medicines that are effective against that particular smallpox, and with careless nursing from the beginning, it would be difficult to say that this too was fate.

○The Oda Family Tradition
Have the child wear a new horse sandal - boys on the left foot, girls on the right foot. Take the excess cord and throw it into a river about five towns away from the house.

The sandal should be hung in a place where it will not be exposed to rain or dew, and left there until the smallpox is finished. After the smallpox is cured without incident, dig about five feet into the ground and bury the sandal. Regardless of what type of smallpox it is, it will undoubtedly finish safely without hindrance. This is a mysterious method. Do not carelessly teach it to people who lack faith.

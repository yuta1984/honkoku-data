Secret Collection of Smallpox Prohibition and Exorcism (Hōsō Kin'en Hidenshū)
　　　Preface

Most reverently and fearfully speaking, in this bountiful land of Toyoashihara no Mizuho no Kuni, the fact that the arts of medicine and prohibition-exorcism are established is a divine work that has existed since the age of the gods. Its origin comes from the compassionate virtue of Sukunahikona-no-Mikoto and Ōanamuchi-no-Mikoto, who joined their divine hearts to teach medical arts, rescue people from illness and calamity, and used exorcism to purify and drive away misfortune and inauspicious events.

○Rare smallpox method
　　《Marginal note: This method is very difficult and hard to use, but it is recorded because of its effectiveness}}
One part red adzuki beans　　One part black soybeans
One part mung beans　　One part licorice《Marginal note: each finely powdered, 1 ryo}}
　　Take the powdered medicine of the above four ingredients, and scrape the outer skin of a bamboo tube,

[Left page]
　　embed nodes at both ends, firmly plug the hole where the above medicine was inserted with cedar wood, ensuring there are no gaps whatsoever, seal the top with beeswax, tie with rope《Marginal note: however, hemp rope is better}}, and place it in the privy for thirty days during the cold season. After thirty days have passed, take it out, wash the tube clean, expose it to wind, leave it for one or two days, then break the bamboo and extract the medicine.

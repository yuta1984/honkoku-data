One - Official correspondence to Edo and Osaka was sent via express messenger, foot soldier Takasono Kinzaemon, as far as Kokura.
August 1st

One - On this day, ceremonial greetings were received in the inner quarters, following the Tanabata custom.
One - Congratulatory messengers to both castle compounds - serving as intermediaries
All messengers from the Great Lord and other high-ranking lords were handled by West Castle personnel, and two representatives from the retainer group also served from the West Castle.
One - After the ceremonial greetings, Princess Kazu had an audience with the related lords and chief retainers.
One - The elders from the Second Castle requested that one chief retainer come forth, so Lord Kuranosuke went over.
One - A document box from Osaka arrived via regular town mail service.

November 8th
1. For the Inari shrine festival, in accordance with past precedent, [officials] went to Hannya-ji temple to perform their duties

1. Toneri-dono and Kuranosuke-dono returned last night from Saga. This was for consultation regarding the imposition of jinbetsu-gin (head tax) throughout the domain

Same day, 9th
1. Tarobei-dono, Toneri-dono, Sajima-dono, and Kuranosuke-dono - these four gentlemen were ordered from the harbor to serve in rotating duty shifts, performing duties equivalent to the current office holders. This was the result of a request made by Zenzaemon-dono to his colleagues

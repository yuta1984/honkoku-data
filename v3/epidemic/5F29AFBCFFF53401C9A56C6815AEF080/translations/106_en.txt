September 22nd
1. Regarding Tanaka Shozaemon, he was appointed as substitute for Heitanji in the role of kitchen attendant at the seaside residence
1. The regular assembly proceeded as usual with officials attending from various departments
1. Messenger duty for incense offering to Shokosan Temple
　　　　　　Same month, 23rd day
1. At half past six, the retinue assembled and
　　His Lordship made a pilgrimage to Kiyomizu. On this occasion
　　Lord Hisakiku and Lord Kumakiku accompanied him. Offerings, as well as
　　vegetable donations to Hochiin Temple were sent. All were provided from the inner household.
　　Nothing from outside sources. The arrangements for Hochiin Temple were handled by the reception office.

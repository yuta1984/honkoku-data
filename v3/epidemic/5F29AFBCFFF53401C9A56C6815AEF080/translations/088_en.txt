August 20th
One - For Kanakuri-sama's memorial day, to Gyokugō Temple as proxy incense, Sukeza'emon-dono
　　Incense offering: one bundle of incense sticks
　　　　　Same day, 21st
One - Senshū-sama has been suffering from what appears to be a cold since the evening of the 16th,
　　and since the fever had not subsided until yesterday, a request came this morning
　　from their chamberlain to our chamberlain to dispatch Yamada Genju and
　　Murata Chūetsu today for consultation, therefore
　　Yamada Genju was immediately ordered to depart
One - Regarding the above, a messenger for condolence visit was dispatched through proper channels
One - Today, the following appointments to official positions were ordered:
　　　　Nishimaru kikiban izuki (West Palace Intelligence Officer, Resident)　　　Tomioka Kobei
　　　　Onryōgata izuki (Hunting Department, Resident)　　　Matsuzaki Jūbei
　　　　Onshihōgata tōnin (Administrative Methods Department Head)　　　Yoshitomi Saburōbei
　　Regarding the above, they were granted an additional rice stipend of ten koku and appointed to the rank of cavalry or higher during their service
　　　　　August 22nd

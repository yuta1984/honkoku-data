　　　　　　March 19th

1. Regarding the misfortune at Hasunóike, Tarōbē-dono went today as representative of the senior retainers to inquire about [the lord's] well-being.
　　　　　　Same, 20th

1. Yokoo Kuranosuke was sent to Osaka regarding loan silver matters. He departed this morning, with remittance silver of 35 kan-me for this occasion, accompanied by the retainer ashigaru Kitajima Heijibē.

1. For the anniversary of Jushō-in-sama, representative incense offering to Shōkōzan: Zensaemon-dono's incense offering was one bundle of incense sticks.

1. For Ōtono-sama's representative incense offering, a retainer performed the duty, with no monetary offering.

1. Having heard that a fire was seen around the small streets in the takuen [performance area] at Senkyōbō last year, prayers were ordered at the same temple for averting [disaster]. 100 hiki in gold was paid as fee.
　　　　　March 21st

1. Furukawa Mokunosuke was sent to Osaka from today.

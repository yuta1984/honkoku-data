　　　　　August 29th
1. Seki Hayato was appointed to serve at the seaside residence
1. Matsukuma Kyōan requested that his son Isen be allowed to accompany the lord's retinue to Edo for medical studies; he would cover travel expenses himself and requested that a single person's food allowance be provided during the stay in the capital; his request was granted as wished
　　　　　　Same month, last day
1. Incense offering on behalf of the lord to Shōkōzan temple; messenger duty officer served
1. [Regarding] Tameike: henceforth the Lady should be addressed as "Great Lady"; this instruction came from the Second Palace

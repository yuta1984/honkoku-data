1. This time, regarding the imposition of individual silver tax (jinbetsu-gin) from Saga domain territory, senior retainers from Renchi and Kashima were invited for consultation about declining this measure. Today Sakuraoka went out to meet them. From Renchi came Nabeshima Kazuma, and from Kashima came Kufu Heitadayū and Hara Gagaku-no-jō. A conference of senior retainers was held in the Komatsu room. Two kinds of clear soup, four side dishes, two appetizers with sake, plus a formal meal of one soup and three dishes were served.

1. For the Mishima Shrine festival, Hannya-ji temple went out and performed their duties as usual. Appropriate food and drink were served.

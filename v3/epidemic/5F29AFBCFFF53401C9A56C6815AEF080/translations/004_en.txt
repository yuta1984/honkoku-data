Second Month, Fifth Day

[Left section]
Same day, sixth
- The matter of borrowing silver from Edo and Osaka was successfully arranged, and since the lord's trip to the capital would proceed without delay, prayers were commissioned at Iwakura Temple and Hannya Temple, with 5 pieces of silver offered to each.

- Both lords went out hunting to Kitayama field. On this occasion, young samurai passed by, and beaters from Oka town provided hospitality.

Same day, seventh
- There was an official summons from the second citadel, so Lord Sukezaemon went there.

1. His Lordship the Great Lord made a temple visit to Shokosan.

1. Members of the retainer group went out to worship in small groups, one after another.
                September 13th

1. The regular assembly took place with officials attending from various departments as usual.
                September 14th

1. Ureshino Zenemon was ordered to be dispatched to Hita for official business related to the lending department. In connection with this, he was granted 500 hiki of gold.

1. Regarding Nozoe Masaemon, who has been working hard for the lending department, it was communicated by the current officials that he was granted 2 koku of rice as assistance.
                September 15th

May 18th
1. Conducted the intermediary service for incense offering on behalf [of the lord] to Shōkōzan temple.

1. Lord Sukezaemon went to the Western Circle (Nishinomaru) on official business.

1. A peasant named Jōhachi from Tateiwa village in Yamashiro district, who has been known for his filial piety toward his parents, was granted 5 kan-mon of bird-eye coins from Saga.

1. A peasant from the same village named Shinemon, who turned 88 years old this year and remarkably lives with three generations of couples together, was granted celebratory sake from Saga in honor of this occasion.

May 19th
1. Yamamoto Rizaemon, who was dispatched to Nagasaki regarding rice sales matters, was granted 200 hiki in gold as travel expenses.

1. This month's prayers will be conducted at Iwakuraji temple starting today, performed in the Komatsu quarters for the customary two nights and three days.

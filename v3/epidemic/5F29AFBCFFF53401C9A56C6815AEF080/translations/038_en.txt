This time, on the occasion of the 50th anniversary memorial service of Lord Hōshōin, the crimes were pardoned through the Buddhist memorial service.

As stated above, written orders were delivered from the Second Circle (Ninomaru), and family members were summoned to be informed by the advisor.

May 16th
1. Regarding the kitchen attendant position, it was initially assigned to Tokumoto Yūemon, but he declined due to illness, so it was instead assigned to Noguchi Yazaemon as his replacement.

1. From among Lord Kizaburō's attendants, Tsutsumi Gyōbu-zaemon and Terasaki Gunji were dismissed from their positions due to staff reductions.

1. Regarding Ureshino Zenemon's matter, it was communicated from the current officials that he was dispatched to Bungo and Hyūga provinces concerning borrowed silver affairs.

1. Lord Kizaburō moved to his new residence today.

1. Concerning Lord Toneri, there was a special consideration, and he was relieved of his duty to accompany [the lord] to Edo and was ordered into house confinement.

As stated above, tonight the chief inspector and general inspector were dispatched to his residence.

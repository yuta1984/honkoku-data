April 26th
1. Performed the intermediary service for incense offering on behalf (of the lord) at Shōkōzan.
1. A document box was sent to Edo from the inner quarters via the upper residence courier service. This concerns the matter that the lord's attendance at the capital in May cannot be fulfilled.

April 27th
1. Kodera Denhei was appointed to the position of hama-gotomoban (beachside attendant guard).
1. A messenger regarding the notification that attendance at the capital in May cannot be fulfilled due to illness will go to Kokura. He departs today. Furthermore, the same report was sent yesterday via the Ninomaru courier service.

April 29th

May 1st
1. On this day, there were no formal courtesies, only celebratory proceedings were received.
1. As every year, prayers for abundant five grains were commissioned to Iwakuraji and Hannaji temples, with six to of prayer rice and two pieces of silver each being offered.

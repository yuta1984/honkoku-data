October 29th
1. Regarding the recent misfortune of Nuinosuke-dono's death, His Lordship sent an official messenger to Sajima-dono to express condolences. Instructions were sent to guide the messenger from the rank of common samurai to the relevant officials

October 30th
1. Official documents from Edo arrived via town messenger service. His Lordship is in increasingly good spirits and arrived in Edo on the 2nd. However, he appears to be suffering from hemorrhoids, and on the 7th, through a messenger, the tributary gifts for the sankin-kotai were successfully presented. As a result, direct letters were sent to each of the related family members, and travel letters also arrived for the senior retainers

1. Notification letters were sent to Hizen-no-kami-sama, Izumi-no-kami-sama, and Nagasaki Magistrate Tsuge Nagato-no-kami-dono

1. To Tanba-no-kami-sama, notification was sent via messenger regarding service at the Western Citadel, and to other acquaintances in Saga domain, notifications were sent from the Western Citadel

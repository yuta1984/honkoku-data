1. Today completed the official correspondence to Osaka and dispatched ashigaru (foot soldier) Noda Yozaemon. The journey via Ōsato route takes one day and one night, and via the Chūgoku route eight days. The official document box for Edo was also sent along.
　　　　　　　Third day of the sixth month

1. For the festival of Nichimine Shrine, Toda Yōsuke served as proxy worshipper with an offering of 2 ryō in silver.
　　For Ōdono-sama's proxy worship, a retainer from the western citadel attended, with the offering silver paid from the provisions office.

1. For Nichimine-sama's memorial day, proxy incense offering at Sōchi-ji temple was conducted by a western citadel retainer, with 20 bundles of white hemp offered to the temple.

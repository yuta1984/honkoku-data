April 12th
1. For the Boys' Day tribute, 100 ryō of gold was sent to Edo. From this morning, foot soldier Oka Hachibe was dispatched via the Ōzato route for one day and night, the Sanyōdō for seven days, and the Tōkaidō for eleven days as the time limit.

1. Served as intermediary for the ceremonial incense offering to Shōkōzan.

April 13th
1. Today being the Day of the Rabbit, the festival of the Shōichi-i Shrine was conducted. The proxy worship was performed by those from the personal attendants' office, with 3 monme of silver for worship and the following donations:
　　　　　　3 monme of silver　　　　　　Ganzōji Temple
　　　　　　1 monme each　　　　　　　　Three accompanying monks
　　　　　　　　　　　For the kagura performance
　　　　　　1 monme 5 bu　　　　　　　　Miyajima Iwami
Besides this, the festival preparations were arranged by Ganzōji Temple, with no concern from above.

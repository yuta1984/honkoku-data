February 18th
1. Served as intermediary for the ceremonial incense offering to Mount Shōkō.
   Same day, 19th
1. In the village of Sato in Yamashiro district, a retainer of Lord Kenmotsu named Tajiri Kanzaemon was killed by another retainer named Tajiri Ugenta. Ugenta then went to the village cemetery and committed seppuku. Reports of this incident came from both Lord Kenmotsu's officials and the head village administrator of Yamashiro, so two assistant inspectors were dispatched to investigate the scene.
   Same day, 20th
1. Tachibana-ya Hanemon and Morozuya Riemon were granted an audience in the outer living quarters, and afterward were bestowed a meal in the Komatsu room. Furukawa Mokunosuke served as companion, and Toneri-dono, Sukezaemon-dono, the chief administrator, and the counseling officials also joined in drinking.
1. Served as intermediary for the ceremonial incense offerings to both temples.

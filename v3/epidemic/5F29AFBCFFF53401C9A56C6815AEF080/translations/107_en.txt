1. Lord Hishu passed through Ushizu today on his return journey from Nagasaki
　　　　　　September 25th
1. The Nagasaki Magistrate Kuze Tango-no-kami, returning to the capital, rested at Ushizu today.
　　Therefore, through a messenger, one box of kudzu powder was presented to him. Furthermore, since his subordinate
　　accounting officials and construction officials also rested there,
　　one portion each of pickled plums was sent to them as well. This messenger service was performed by the Ushizu teahouse attendant.
　　District officials, horse and transport officials, and other various officers proceeded to the same location as usual.
1. Regarding the ongoing matter of Aihara Sajibei and Fujiyama Soemon from some time ago, tonight
　　there was another investigation at the council chamber.

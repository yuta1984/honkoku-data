1. Nuinosuke-dono had been ill for some time, and last night his condition became particularly serious according to the report received, so His Lordship privately sent a foot soldier messenger to inquire about his condition

1. A report was received that Nuinosuke-dono died of illness in the early afternoon, and expressing condolences, an official messenger was dispatched. Since he was a "heya-zumi" (dependent family member), there were no formal funeral protocols

October 26th
1. Official documents for Edo and Osaka were sent via foot soldier Kitajima Heijibei. The journey is scheduled for three days on the Dairi route, eight days on the Chūgoku route, and seven days on the Tōkaidō route. At this time, 500 ryō in gold was also sent to Edo

1. Ureshino Zenemon was dispatched to Hita in Bungo today regarding loan silver matters. For this purpose, he was granted 500 hiki in funds for expenses

October 27th
1. Due to official business, Zensaemon-dono departed for Saga today

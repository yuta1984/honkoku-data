November 10th
1. For the Konpira shrine festival, the master and disciples of Hannya-ji temple went out to perform their duties. Sake was served at the oil storehouse

1. Regarding the matter of Aihara Sajibei and Fujiyama Sōemon that has been ongoing since autumn, an investigation was conducted tonight at the hyōjōsho (council chamber), but since the official judgment could not be decided, they were ordered to remain detained at the same location

Same day, 11th
1. For Getsudō-sama's memorial day, a karō (senior retainer) was to serve as representative for the incense offering at Sōchi-ji temple, but since all were unavailable, a bantō (captain) served instead. The funeral offering was 20 bundles of white hemp

1. Similarly, for the representative incense offering to Shōkōzan, a messenger-guard served. The funeral offering was one bundle of incense sticks

1. A bantō served for Ōtono-sama's representative incense offering

1. Today as well, the investigation continued at the hyōjōsho with the same number of people as yesterday

Same day, 12th
1. A messenger-guard served for the representative incense offering to Shōkōzan

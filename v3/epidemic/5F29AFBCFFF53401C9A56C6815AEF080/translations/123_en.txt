1. A document box from Edo arrived by town messenger. This was notification of safe arrival at the capital

1. Today as well, an investigation was held at the hyōjōsho with the same number of people as the day before yesterday

November 13th
1. The regular council meeting was held with officials attending from various departments

Same day, 14th
1. A Nichiren sect monk named Nikken has access to Senior Councilor Tanuma Tonomo-no-kami-sama. Regarding the upcoming spring appointment that has been announced, wishing to request his assistance with influence peddling, and requesting that he come to the capital soon, today Sakuraoka was summoned and there was a discussion with the senior retainers about making this request. Appropriate food and drink were served

November 15th
1. On this day there was a celebration with officials attending from various departments

1. For the Inari festival at Ushizu jōshiya, Hannya-ji temple and Miyajima Iwami went over to perform their duties. The ritual offerings were provided from the treasury

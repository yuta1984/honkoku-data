　　dismissed from his official position by Saga and ordered into house confinement. Regarding this, our domain also
　　reprimanded him. Details are recorded in the district administration record book.
1. There were reports that Kijima Mizukari Riheiji showed filial piety to his parents,
　　and when the inspector's office made inquiries, it was confirmed to be true, so
　　he was granted one kan of bronze coins as a reward.

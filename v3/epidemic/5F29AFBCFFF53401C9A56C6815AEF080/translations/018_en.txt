His lordship and the great lord are to refrain from shaving their forelocks (monthly head shaving), though this does not apply to retainers of retainers, as was reported.

1. Regarding the official mourning, the following circular notice arrived from Ninomaru:
"It was reported from Edo that Dainagon-sama passed away on the 24th of last month. Therefore, from today the 10th, confinement has been ordered throughout the domain. Accordingly, noh chanting, dancing, loud voices, musical instruments, construction work, etc. are to be stopped. Townspeople are to close their shops and not engage in street selling. Furthermore, prohibition of taking life has been ordered, so this must be thoroughly communicated to all areas. Details regarding the number of people will be communicated later. The above."

March 11th
1. At the beach, his lordship summoned relatives and senior retainers, and furthermore issued orders regarding frugality.
1. Regarding the same matter, officials of all ranks and office holders attended tonight, and there was deliberation on frugality measures.

1. Renchi Gyokusen-in-sama passed away yesterday after her illness worsened and recovery was not possible, as reported last night from Nishinomaru.

1. Regarding the same matter, concerning the great-aunt for Tanshū-sama's sake, served as intermediary for condolence messengers from Tono-sama, Gozen-sama, Ōtono-sama, Nishioka-sama, and Hisakiku-sama. However, since going as an intermediary would not be timely, it became a Nishinomaru duty.

1. For Hishū-sama and Tsunemaru-sama, condolences are to be sent to Edo due to their residence there.

1. Due to the official period of discretion, there were no celebratory ceremonies on this day.

1. Officials of all ranks attended for official discussions.

1. An official document box arrived from Osaka via town courier.

1. A directive arrived from Saga stating a five-day period of discretion starting from yesterday the 14th due to Gyokusen-in-sama's death.

1. Ureshino Zen'emon returned this evening from Nagasaki.
　　　　　March 16th

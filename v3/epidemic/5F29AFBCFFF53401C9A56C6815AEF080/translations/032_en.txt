April 23rd
1. Yasumoto Tomohei was appointed to the position of hama-goshoba (beachside attendant).

April 24th
1. As every year, a proxy pilgrimage was sent from Nishioka to Kongō Tenjin at Chōonji Temple in Kami-Saga Kusaba Village. This is customarily performed by those from the Saga-zai kotō-dōri district. One ryō of silver for the pilgrimage offering comes from external sources.
1. Due to the illness of Iwase Naga, a lady-in-waiting in Lady Saho's quarters, she was granted leave, and in connection with this, was granted a lifetime stipend of one person's portion. This matter was communicated to her relative Nagahashi Ichirōemon by Lord Zenzaemon.
1. Due to official business matters, Lord Toneri and Lord Sukezaemon traveled to Saga today.

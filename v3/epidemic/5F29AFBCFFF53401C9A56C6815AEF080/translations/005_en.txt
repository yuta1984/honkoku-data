Second Month, Eighth Day
Same day, ninth
- When Lord Sukezaemon went to the second citadel yesterday, he was given the following official notification:
　Regarding Lord Kaga no Kami's delayed delivery of rice for repaying borrowed silver in Osaka, there was a plaintiff on the 21st day of last month. Although there was a summons from officials at the local magistrate's office on the 24th of the same month, they declined citing illness or that they were currently visiting here, and Tateishi Yoichi went instead. However, since he did not understand the intent, it was decided that no investigation would be conducted for now.

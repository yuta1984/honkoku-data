April 9th
April 10th

1. As the administrative accountant Naito Jinemon-dono and construction magistrate Nishihara Kohachiro-dono were returning to the capital and resting at Ushizu today, kudzu powder was sent to them, one kyoku each, through a messenger. The messenger was served by the teahouse attendant.

1. Due to drought, rain-making prayers were ordered at Ganzoji Temple, to be conducted for two nights and three days starting today, and two pieces of silver were provided as payment.

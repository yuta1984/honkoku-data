April 4th
April 5th

1. There was a matter of official business, so today Toneri-dono Sukezaemon-dono went to Saga.

April 6th

1. As congratulations for the wedding, today Sagara Genbee appeared at the outer and inner quarters. Also, since there was official business to discuss, upon departure there was a meeting with Sajiuma-dono and Minamigaso-sama of the current administrative office, and appropriate food and drink were served, so 150 me of coins were disbursed for expenses.

1. From Hishu-sama, privately as wedding congratulations, a messenger was sent - Tatarano Kuroemon was dispatched from the outer pages. As gifts, two mullet fish in one fold each were presented to the married couple. The messenger was served cuisine and sake in the Komatsu room. 100 hiki of gold coins each were received from both parties.

1. For official discussions, today various parties came to serve.

April 7th
April 8th

1. Baba Seizaemon was ordered by the inner chief to concurrently serve at Nishioka as well.

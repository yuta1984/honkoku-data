1. For relatives and chief retainers up to their eldest sons: ceremonial meals of one soup and two side dishes, plus one clear soup, two fish dishes, and one appetizer fish dish. This took place at the reception office.
1. The group leaders received one clear soup and two fish dishes with sake in the upper room of Komatsu-no-ma.
1. All samurai, represented only by their direct deputies, received three varieties of fish dishes served on wooden trays with individual earthenware cups in the messenger's room, donation room, and great hall. With sake.
　　　　　February 27th
1. The various samurai of Sakuraoka and the officials in charge of the wedding ceremony received sake with one clear soup and two fish dishes.
1. Minor leaders and foot soldiers, represented only by their direct deputies, received sake in the great hall. Ashigaru (foot soldiers) and minor equipment handlers, represented only by their group deputies, received their portions below the spear rack at the middle entrance.
　　All of these were return celebrations from yesterday.
　　　　　Same day, 28th
1. Regarding Zenzaemon-dono: during Sukezaemon-dono's absence, he was appointed to reception duties, as ordered in the lord's presence.
1. Yokoo Kuranosuke was appointed to concurrent duty as chief supervisor, effective soon.

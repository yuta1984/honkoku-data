1. Regarding Lord Izumi-no-kami's arrival in the domain: he stayed overnight at Kanzaki last night, today
　　he performed his duties in Saga, and tonight he will stay at Ushizu. To the residence at Kawarakoji
　　from the western citadel, as an attendant messenger,
　　Their Lordships and all Their Ladyships sent regards combining both sympathy and congratulations.
　　Subsequently, as a messenger to pay respects to his lodging at Ushizu,
　　the gifts presented were as follows:
　　From His Lordship:
　　One box of eggs - 100 pieces
　　From Her Ladyship:
　　One box of salted small sea bream - 15 fish
　　　　The above via one messenger: Higashishima Mokuemon
　　From the Great Lord:
　　One box of salted small sea bream - 15 fish
　　From Lord Nishinooka:
　　One box of large watermelons - 2 pieces
　　　　The above via one messenger: Ezoe Hyōbu-zaemon
1. From the branch family lords, from Lady Saho: only words of greeting

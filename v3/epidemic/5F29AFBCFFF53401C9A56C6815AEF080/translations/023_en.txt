1. For inspection of the Ashikari waterway construction site, Sukesaemon-dono and other officials including the chief administrator, advisors, chief inspectors, assistant officials, storehouse officials, district officials, and village officials went out.
　　　　　March 22nd

1. The regular assembly proceeded as usual with attendance from all departments.
　　　　　Same, 23rd

1. Regarding the death of Dainagon-sama, house confinement had been ordered, but word came from Ninomaru that exemption would be granted from the 2nd of next month.

1. The financial reform measures that were recently ordered to be simplified have been completed, so they are to be reported to the lord. Accordingly, relatives, senior retainers, chief administrators, and advisors went out.
　　　　　Same, 24th

1. For the anniversary of Kyōsei-in-sama, Nishimaru performed the representative incense offering to Kōdenji Temple, with an incense offering of ten rolls of white hemp cloth.

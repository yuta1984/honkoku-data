March 4th
1. Official correspondence from Osaka arrived today via town messenger.
　　　March 5th
　　　March 6th
1. For wedding congratulations, Nabeshima Kizaemon and Nabeshima Sadayū visited today, so sake and other refreshments were served in the inner quarters, and afterwards outside as well, in Komatsu-ma, a meal of one soup and five dishes along with clear soup and four kinds of side dishes with sake were served. Sukesaemon-dono served as companion.
1. For official business regarding borrowed silver, Ureshino Zen'emon was dispatched to Nagasaki from today. For this, he was granted three pieces of silver.
　　　March 7th
1. Yoshitomi Saburōbei arrived this evening.
　　　March 8th
1. Yoshitomi Saburōbei reported on Osaka official business, so officials from various quarters attended and acknowledgment was given.
1. Kurihara Gizaemon and Morozuya Riemon (were dispatched) to Kurosaki Matsubara in Chikushū.

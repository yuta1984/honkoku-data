1. Through the mediation of Hasunōike Chief Retainer Matsueda Senbei, a financial backer was to be established for matters concerning succession in Edo, and to discuss this matter, Senbei visited here today. Upon arrival, tea and sweets were served at Lord Toneri's residence, after which he proceeded to Sakuraoka where the chief retainers, chief administrator-advisor, and treasury officials held discussions. Soup was served with sake, and he was also granted a hemp kamishimo outfit and 2,000 hiki in gold. After completing business at Sakuraoka, he went to Lord Toneri's residence where the chief retainers gathered, and soup with four kinds of delicacies, sake, and a meal of one soup and three dishes were served. Tonight he will stay overnight at Lord Toneri's residence, and from tomorrow morning he is scheduled to proceed toward Shioda. Therefore, palanquin, bearers, porters, etc. will be provided from our side

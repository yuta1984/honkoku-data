March 9th
1. Since the Osaka official business was heard and received above, Yoshitomi Saburōbei appeared before his lordship and reported directly.
1. For official business, Nishikawa Tamon came down from Edo and arrived today. Last winter, Nomura Sugasaemon had been dispatched as a proxy pilgrim to the three shrines in the Kamigata region, but he was detained because the Osaka residence was particularly busy with official duties and short-staffed. He has now also arrived together with the others.

March 10th
1. Since Nishikawa Tamon reported on Edo official business, officials from various quarters attended and acknowledgment was given.
1. It was reported that Hizen-sama's leave to return to his domain was announced on the 13th of last month, and gratitude for the leave was expressed on the 15th of the same month.

June 18th
1. For the seventh-year memorial service of Lord Honryoin, 
   600 me of 20-mon coins and 5 bales of rice were offered 
   to Shokosan temple as memorial service fees
1. At dawn, 6:30, visited the temple hall with full retinue escort,
   offering white hemp cloth, 10 bundles
1. Her Ladyship's offering: 3 bundles of incense sticks
1. In connection with the memorial service, killing of living beings
   was prohibited throughout the domain for this entire day
1. Donation exemption:

　　September 17th
One: Lord Chikuzen-no-kami traveled to Nagasaki ["from his return journey" crossed out] and rested at Ushizu today, so various officials were dispatched according to the usual protocol.

　　September 18th
One: [Someone] made a temple visit to Shōkōzan. On this occasion, a donation was made to the same temple.
One: Various parties attended to official duties.
One: Lady Kiyō and both princesses made a pilgrimage to Saireiji Temple on foot this evening.

June 1st
One　For Tanshu-sama's yakuiri (unlucky age), prayer petitions were submitted as follows:
　　　One　Sanno Shrine　　For ritual release of living beings
　　　One　Yoka Shrine　　For 300 purification ceremonies
　　　One　Hachiman Shrine　　For kagura performance fees
　　The above were accompanied by offerings from his lordship: five dried sea bream in one box.
　　The messenger was Nakahara Bunzaemon, assistant to Nishi-maru attendant.
　　　One　Sanno Shrine　　For ritual release of living beings
　　　One　Yoka Shrine　　For 200 purification ceremonies
　　　One　Hachiman Shrine　　For kagura performance fees
　　The above were accompanied by offerings from her ladyship: three dried sea bream in one box.

　　　September 25th
　　　Same, 26th
• As Tanshū-sama departed today for his attendance duty (sankin), Danjō-dono, Ureshino Yashichizaemon, and Fujishima Seizaemon went as representatives of the retainer band to see him off. Additionally, two others from the western citadel were assigned to this duty.
• A representative and intermediary were sent on duty to Shōkōzan.
• The kyōgen troupe arranged in Nishikawa was summoned, and tonight at Sakuraoka the noble lords and ladies watched the performance. On this occasion, lunch was to be served at the beach tea house, and the kyōgen actors had been present since midday.

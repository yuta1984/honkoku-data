September 5th
One: Lord Tanshu came to this estate today to enjoy Noh performances. He entered Sakuraoka before 5 AM and also passed through the inner residence. Then the Noh performance began. On this occasion, Lord Yamato and Lord Hyogo attended as companions.

One: During the intermission of the Noh performance, [they] went to the seaside tea house

From Lord Tanshu to His Lordship:
One: Money for sake - 500 hiki in gold
One: Dried cod - one box

To Her Ladyship:
One: Red and white silk damask - three rolls

To the Young Lord:
One: Silk damask - two rolls

One: Ten pieces of silver were bestowed upon the Noh actors

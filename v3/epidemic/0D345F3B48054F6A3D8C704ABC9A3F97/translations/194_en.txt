November 27th
One　Last night around 9:30 PM, with retainers assembled, in hunting attire
　　His Lordship departed for Shiraishi. On this occasion, from Usutsu
　　he boarded a boat and traveled by ship to Fukadori, then proceeded to Shiraishi.
　　Detailed records are kept with the steward's office.
　　　　　Same month, 28th day
One　From Shiraishi, tonight around 10 PM, His Lordship returned to the residence.
　　　　　November 29th
One　The 13th anniversary memorial service for Lord Shinnyo-in
　　was conducted ahead of schedule. As payment for the memorial service,
　　two silver pieces were donated to Shokozan temple.
One　His Lordship's representative incense offering, Eisuke Zaemon bearing the funeral gift

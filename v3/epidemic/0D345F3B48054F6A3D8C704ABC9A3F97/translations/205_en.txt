December 10th
One　To Matsuoyama　Representative messenger duty
One　Fujiyama Ichirobei is ordered to concurrently serve
　　as advisor for the time being
One　Murasaki Shichizaemon is ordered to be dispatched
　　to Osaka on official business
　　　　Same day, 11th
One　To Shokoyama　Representative intermediary duty
　　　　Same day, 12th
One　Visited the temple hall at Shokoyama
　　　　Same day, 13th
One　No formal meetings
One　At the ninth hour of midday, departed with full retinue for bird hunting

[The lord] granted an audience to Utsunomiya Fukyū

One　Regarding O-san-sama, this year her directional fortune is
particularly bad, and especially lately she has been frequently ill,
so at Hannya-ji temple, prayers for the elimination of malevolent stars
will be performed for two nights and three days.
Accordingly, 150 monme of silver will be provided as payment for this.

One　Regarding Kizaburō-sama's illness from some time ago, which has not
improved at all, the relatives and chief retainers
submitted a prayer petition as follows:
　　One　Tenzan Shrine - Seven copies of the Saishōō-kyō sutra
　　One　Konpira - Fifty votive lamps
　　One　Inari Shrine - Votive picture tablet
　These [offerings] are from four of the relatives

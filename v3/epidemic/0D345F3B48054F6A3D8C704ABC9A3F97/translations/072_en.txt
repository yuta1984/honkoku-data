4th month, 10th day
One　A messenger official served as proxy representative to Matsuo-yama

One　Following yearly custom, for the prayers for abundant five-grain harvest performed at Iwakura-ji and Hannya-ji temples, six to of rice as offering and three silver coins each were presented to both temples

One　Regarding the Young Lord's illness, a request was made for Nishioka Chōen to be sent from the second compound as a physician. Today he came to the inner quarters and examined the Young Lord's condition. On this occasion, hospitality meals and sake were served

One　A messenger official served as proxy representative to Shōkō-zan

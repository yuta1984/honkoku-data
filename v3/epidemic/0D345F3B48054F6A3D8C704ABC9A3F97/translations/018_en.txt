Sixteenth day of the first month
Item: Her ladyship made a pilgrimage to the temple at Shōkōzan today at the hour of the Horse (around noon), with her full retinue in attendance

Seventeenth day of the first month
Item: A retainer of Lord Tsushima named Ehara Sōzaemon shot a crane in our private domain at Ashikari the other day, and reports were made from the head village official to the district office and to the various hunting officials in Saga. While we have no objection to the hunting of birds itself, the discharge of firearms within our private domain cannot be overlooked, so we instructed Minoda Sakuzaemon to deal firmly with that retainer as follows:

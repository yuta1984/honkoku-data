September 30th
One item: A chamberlain served as representative to Shōkōzan.

October 1st
One item: The monthly courtesy visit was received.
One item: From around the hour of eight in the daytime (around 2 PM), [the lord] went out for bird hunting.
One item: Her ladyship went to the new residence.

October 2nd
One item: [The lord] attended the regular meeting as usual.
One item: Murakawa Denaemon arrived last night from Osaka.
One item: The foot soldier Tsutsumi Seizaemon was appointed as supervisor of the Hamasaki granary.

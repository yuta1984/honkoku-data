May 17th
One　Today, a practice Noh performance was held on the stage.
　　On this occasion, His Lordship and the princesses watched the performance.

　　Same month, 18th
One　Miyachi Gozaemon (was sent) as a representative to Honryoin-sama.

　　Same month, 19th
One　For the welcoming party to Oozato, personnel and horses were dispatched from today, and for coordination, Ikeda
　　Hyoubu-zaemon was sent, along with four foot soldiers and ten equipment bearers
　　who were dispatched. On this occasion, the official duties were simplified.
One　This month's prayers, as usual, will be conducted at Iwakura-ji temple in the Komatsu room
　　for two nights and three days (starting from today).
One　His Lordship today at half past the fourth hour, with his retinue assembled, (went to) the new residence

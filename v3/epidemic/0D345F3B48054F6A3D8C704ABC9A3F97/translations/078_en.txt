4th month, 29th day
Same month, last day
One　The representative to Shōkōzan was served by a messenger attendant
One　Due to the particularly severe drought this year, rain-prayer
　　rituals were conducted at Iwakura-ji temple, and on this occasion three pieces of
　　ritual silver were sent
One　Her Ladyship and the young ladies went out around dusk tonight to
　　the Gion riverside to view fireflies

5th month, 1st day
One　As Lord Chikuzen-no-kami was resting today in Ushizu on his return journey from Nagasaki,
　　various officials were dispatched as usual
One　Regarding the arrangements at Nagasaki that were ordered, Lord Toneri
　　went to the second citadel today

November 3rd
One　For the memorial day of Gyokushin-in-sama, at Muryoji Temple
　　served as intermediary for the memorial incense offering, and donated
　　one bundle of incense sticks as an offering to the temple
　　　　Same day, 4th
One　For official business, dispatched ashigaru (foot soldier) Nishi
　　Jubei as a messenger to Osaka from today
One　Tomorrow at 6 o'clock, departed for bird hunting with full retinue
One　Regarding Donkei, was appointed as
　　head priest of Eisho-an temple, this was conveyed today through summons from the temple and shrine office

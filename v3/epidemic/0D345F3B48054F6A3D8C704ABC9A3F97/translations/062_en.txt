One　For the aforementioned messenger Fukahori Sanai, in the Komatsu room, three types of soup,
　　three types of appetizers up to the main fish dish, and sake were bestowed, and moreover white hemp
　　twenty bundles were granted. For the assistant, under the spear rack,
　　one type of soup and three types of fish dishes with sake were given, and furthermore white hemp
　　ten bundles were bestowed.
One　Regarding the young lord's illness, from Saga, a town doctor named
　　Ikejiri Mototora was summoned to the inner quarters to examine
　　his condition. On this occasion, a meal of one soup and three dishes, and sake were served.

　　　Preface
The preciousness of life is well known among the general populace.
Who would desire premature death or a short life? Using all manner of methods
and approaches to hope for the preservation of life is
the common sentiment of each person. Generally, those who possess an innate nature must
surely all possess this heart without exception. However, merely
hoping for this alone, if one does not know the methods to preserve it and the
reasons for carrying them out, one will greatly err

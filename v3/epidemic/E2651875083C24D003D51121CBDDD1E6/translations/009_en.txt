Cowpox was discovered years ago in the German states of Europe, when cattle herders
accidentally contracted it on their palms while milking,
and first realized that it was a benign form of pox. Later, physicians in England
inoculated it into the human body, achieving mild and complete effectiveness. They verified
that those who contract it once absolutely never
re-contract natural smallpox. The technique gradually came to be widely practiced.
Now people throughout various states
universally believe in and trust it. This theory is discussed
and recorded in various books. In this year, the summer of the second year of Kaei (1849), Western ships brought the vaccine,
and vaccination in our country began in our Nagasaki. Thus it is now being transmitted through inoculation.

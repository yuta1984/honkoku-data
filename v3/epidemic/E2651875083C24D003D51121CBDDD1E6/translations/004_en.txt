Preface to A Small Study on Cowpox Vaccination
The physician Wasan Narabayashi, who serves the Hizen domain, comes from a family that has been renowned for generations and resides in Nagasaki. His ancestor, Master Eikyū, was the founder and advocate of Western medicine. At that time, scholars in our country did not know what Western learning was. Narabayashi inherited his ancestors' work and is broadly learned with extensive knowledge, his medical techniques being extremely precise. His reputation continues to grow.

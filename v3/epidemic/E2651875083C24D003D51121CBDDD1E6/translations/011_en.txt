　　　　Oral Instructions for Vaccination Method
First: The physician should first have the person to be vaccinated bare one arm, 
　hold the arm steady with the left hand, pull and press down on the lower edge 
　of the area to be vaccinated with the thumb to tighten the skin and flesh of that area, 
　take the needle in the right hand, apply pox fluid to the needle tip, and insert it 
　between the epidermis and dermis about one bu deep, pause the needle slightly 
　to allow the pox fluid to be well absorbed, then withdraw the needle, 
　immediately press the wound opening briefly with the back of the needle to stop 
　the blood that seeps out and prevent the pox fluid from leaking, and then

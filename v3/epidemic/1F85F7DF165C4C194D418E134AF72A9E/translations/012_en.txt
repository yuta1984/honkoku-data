It is twelve days from when the eruptions appear fully, which is the fifteenth day from the initial onset.

　　Regarding making the sleeves of undergarments long
Long sleeves means that when both hands are extended, the sleeves should be about three sun longer
than the fingertips, loose and comfortable. The sleeve openings should not be narrow. It is important that
hands can be easily put in and taken out. If the smallpox patient sleeps, the ends of those sleeves

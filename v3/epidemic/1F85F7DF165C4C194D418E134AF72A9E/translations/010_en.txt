　　Regarding the proper way of examining with paper candles (shisoku)
During the initial stage of illness, when determining whether it is another disease or smallpox,
one should twist paper candles and observe to examine the severity and extent of symptoms. If it is daytime,
one should enclose the area with folding screens to make it dark, and begin examining from the left cheek of the patient,
carefully observing the center of the forehead. In any case, symptoms cannot be seen
in daylight. Once they have already appeared on the skin surface, lamplight is
not good. At that time, without sunlight, it is difficult to distinguish the red and white coloration and the condition of deficiency or excess.
This is only when fever is suspected. Red paper should be used.

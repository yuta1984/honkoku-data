《Title: Hōsō Kokoroegusa (Smallpox Manual) (Complete in one volume)》
This book, from the first arrival of smallpox in Japan, through the understanding of days from initial fever, including knowledge of measles and chickenpox, to the care and nursing of smallpox patients and the understanding for caregivers, is written in detail in hiragana with the sole wish to spread this knowledge widely to the world.

Preface to Hōsō Kokoroegusa
It is said in the classics: "Parents worry only about their children's illnesses."
How heartbreaking is the parents' care and concern for their child's illness! Among the many various diseases in our Japan, there is probably nothing that frightens and torments parents' hearts more than smallpox, regardless of whether it is mild or severe. It is truly a great calamity during one's lifetime. However, smallpox spreads unknowingly year after year, but perhaps due to the season, this year this disease has spread widely throughout all provinces and has become inescapable.

A Study on Foods for Smallpox Treatment

The earliest records of the disease called smallpox appear to begin with works such as the Shoku Nihongi (Chronicles of Japan Continued). In China as well, it is said to have originated around the time of the Han and Wei dynasties. In ancient times it was extremely rare, but now it occurs every year without fail, and moreover, there is no one among the people who does not contract this disease at least once.

　　Laryngotracheal Croup Prevention Guidelines《Horse-hide Wind》
Horse-hide wind occurs when invaded by cold winds in spring and autumn, or when other children accidentally inhale droplets of mucus from affected children, and generally affects children from about two to five years of age

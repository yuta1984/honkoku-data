Medicine Formula
For children from four years old to six years old:
○ Fennel oil (Uikyō-yu) 3-4 drops
○ Sodium bicarbonate (by weight) 3 bu
○ Premium white sugar (by weight) 5 bu
These three ingredients should be dissolved in about one small cup (choko) of water, divided into four portions, and used four times a day.

For those from four years old down to two years old:
○ Fennel oil 3 drops

The god of poverty, as he had long planned, made all people become debauched and spend enormous amounts of gold and silver, making them quite poor. However, masterless samurai, ugly men, and those born naturally stingy couldn't be moved by either sensual or culinary pleasures, so he made them develop great greed and aroused their desire for making money.

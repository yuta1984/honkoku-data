○Second Act New Kyōgen Play - Curtain rises, naturally taking the form of a forest
Behind is the flow of hot water
The spirit of the ginkgo tree appears, coming onto the rocky stage, performing two or three acts
With elaborate movements flowing in all directions

Looking around, tonight too has passed the hour of the Ox (around 2-3 AM), and while all others sleep without moving their branches and leaves, I alone have deep desires at my roots, and have been suffering in my heart for many days - but whose fault is this? <<Margin note: Are! Are!>>

Seeing what appears to be a marital quarrel, with the sound of a mortar striking and a pestle breaking, going up and down, thinking that with this commotion the bean-throwing must be over, the demon shuffles in to see. But what is this? The wife has grown horns of jealousy, and in her frenzy the husband calmly handles a terrible snake. Though the demon finds this frightening, it cannot leave.

"How terrifying, how terrifying!"

At that moment, the sky suddenly darkened with clouds, and heavy rain and fierce winds brought down stones, while the house shook and trembled without cease.

Therefore, Kamata Matahachi, entreated by Kaemon, gallantly bound his headband and briskly tied his sash, sharpened his sword, and cut his way into the retirement quarters. What had appeared to be an old woman revealed its true form as a red cat and tried to escape, but he struck it down repeatedly and killed it.

"How insolent of you to try to flee before my blade, you wretched demon cat!"

Some quick-witted young men had come to capture the demon cat, but upon seeing this spectacle, they fainted dead away.

Tamashin unexpectedly returned to Naka-no-chō after about twenty days away. Reports reached the Umeya establishment one after another like the steady cutting of comb teeth, and Hatsune hastily put on her wooden clogs, preparing to go out to greet him as far as Naka-no-chō.

First, she sent ahead a kamuro named Hatsume as an advance guard.

Before the courtesan herself appeared, her entourage came together, and from outside the latticed entrance, they proclaimed in loud voices about "Tamashin's arrival" and "what he had accomplished," boasting of his noteworthy deeds as they clattered and bustled up to the second floor.

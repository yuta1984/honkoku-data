In great commotion with everything turned upside down, treating the cherry blossom viewing at Mount Eitō and the kana verse composition as matters for elsewhere, here at Hakone Gongen in Sagami Province, following the annual auspicious custom, Lord Minamoto no Yoritomo has completed his pilgrimage and his return journey as well. The rear guard, led by Arashirō of Shinkai, maintains order with a large force of foot soldiers lined up at Shōbu-ga-hara, and as the great lords descend the mountain one by one, among them the enemy Kudō Saemon-no-jō, who currently holds high office in Kamakura, emerges with his court cap worn majestically, flaunting his own authority. Seeing this, the elder brother and Kiō grind their teeth in frustration and resentment.

"That one with the mokko crest on his canopy-like insignia is Lord Suketsune. You two masters, observe him well. From now on, we shall request Lord Asahina's assistance and have him put in a good word with Lord Hachiman. Once we can move freely as we wish, our revenge will be within reach. Such matters are better handled swiftly."

"Just as we discussed in our plan earlier with all the details, let us go to Lord Kobayashi's residence on our way back."

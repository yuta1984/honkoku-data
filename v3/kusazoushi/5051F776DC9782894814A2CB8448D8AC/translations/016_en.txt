"Takao Senjimono" (The Tale of Takao's Ship and Letters) <<Note: Complete set of 5 volumes | By Kyōtei Bakin>>
This book takes the jōruri puppet play "Sendai Hagi" (Date clan succession dispute) and substitutes familiar parts from "Suikoden" (Water Margin), creating a novel approach for the entertainment of young masters.

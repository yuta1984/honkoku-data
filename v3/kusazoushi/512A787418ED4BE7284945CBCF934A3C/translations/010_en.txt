The next customer appears to be a theater enthusiast. While waiting, he discusses the theater reviews and programs. The spring kyōgen featuring the Soga brothers has been his delight since the kaomise performances. Speculating about "who will play what role" since December, he impatiently awaited opening day. When he finally went to see the performance, the box seat applications, general admission rush seating, and the rakandai were packed with people - an unprecedented full house. At the confrontation scene of the first act, Saemon Suketsune appears.

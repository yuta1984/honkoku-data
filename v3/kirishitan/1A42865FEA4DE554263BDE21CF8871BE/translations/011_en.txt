Therefore, could there be any madman greater than one who, as a mere human being, thinks of time—which belongs to the divine essence of the Lord God—as his own possession?
Behold, since many have fallen into hell through such delusion, why should we not take the overturning of the front cart as a warning for the rear cart? According to the teachings of Ecclesiasticus, strive diligently in this matter. Please, do not postpone your return to God from today to tomorrow.

Also, on the battlefield, though the body thinks of retreating, considering honor and thinking it better than fleeing and being pointed at with scorn, one forces one's reluctant body to die in battle. From this it is clear that the anima rationalis does not emerge from material existence but is created by Deus as the master of one's body. Thus, being an anima rationalis distinct from the material body, it does not perish together with the material body.

Even so, to say that Deus surpasses dharma-nature (hōshō) because he has wisdom and discrimination is unbearably laughable. You cannot understand the principle of void-spirit and non-obscuration (kyorei fumai).

Daiusu (the Christian) also says: "If there were no wisdom and virtue in the original source, from where would the contemplative wisdom found in humans and the virtue and righteousness found in all phenomena arise? When we see through this principle, wisdom and virtue must be present in the original source."

【Left page】
Refuting this, I say: "Willows are green, flowers are red - this is simply the natural principle. Crush the roots of a willow and look - there is no green. Break open a flowering tree and look - there is no red, yet this is the natural manifestation of heaven and earth. Year after year they bloom on Mount Yoshino - break open a cherry tree and see if there are flowers there. That which is not in the root source but exists in the branch tips is the normal principle.

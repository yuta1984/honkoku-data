Now, the school of Deus (God) has seven stages of doctrine for those who are newcomers. The essence of the first stage is to know the Creator through heaven, earth, and all phenomena, and to know the One who governs through the unfailing succession of the four seasons. For example, when one sees a palace hall, one knows there is a master craftsman; when one sees regulations posted in a household and the household governed according to their intent, one necessarily knows there is a master.

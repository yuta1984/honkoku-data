When [Lucifer] saw this and desired to usurp the position of the Lord of Heaven, gathering various anjos (angels) into his faction, the Lord of Heaven arose in righteous anger and banished Lucifer to the lower world, creating a great fire pit and placing him in these flames. This was named Inferno, which is translated as Hell. Thereupon Lucifer said, "I wish to share the same suffering with the couple Adam [and Eve]." He then transformed his own body into a tengu and went to Terrestrial (Paradise), saying to Eve: "Why do you not eat the fruit of Maçã (the tree of knowledge of good and evil)?" Eve said, "The Lord of Heaven has forbidden eating Maçã." The tengu said, "Those who eat Maçã will gain great sovereignty and become creators of heaven and earth, sharing the same virtuous position as our Lord of Heaven. Therefore the Lord of Heaven strictly forbids this. Please, eat this fruit." Eve took it and ate. Adam said, "What are you eating?" Eve said, "Following the tengu's teaching, I eat Maçã." Adam said, "I too shall eat," and took it and ate. Therefore the Lord of Heaven banished this couple to the lower world. Because the sentient beings of the final age are their descendants, they become bodies of birth, aging, sickness, and death. Sixty years after Deus created heaven and earth, Jesus Christ was born. He said of himself: "I am the Lord of Paradise, the honored one without beginning or end, the incarnation of Deus who created heaven and earth and established all things.

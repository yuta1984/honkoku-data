This present life is like being in a dream. One should quickly convert to this doctrine. When preaching this teaching, the listeners request to convert to the religion. At this point, the padre performs the ritual of baptism. On the forehead of the person becoming a believer for the first time, a white cloth is placed, a candle is held in their hand, salt is put in their mouth, and the padre takes water and pours it on that person's head. There are recitations. Also, they are brought into the padre's room to make confession. At this time, wheat bread is given. This is received and eaten. Wine is also given. This is received and drunk. Later, they meet with the bishop padre. At that time, the bishop writes a cross on their forehead. He takes oil and anoints that person's head. He strikes that person's right cheek with his hand. Thereafter, the daily practices and rituals involve making the sign of the cross when facing tea and meals, then eating and drinking. They also strike their back to draw blood, and through this meritorious power, they eliminate sinful obstacles. Morning and evening they hold rosary beads and chant orações. They wear relics around their neck, and there are various other special practices. There is also a teaching to make people fix their hearts completely on this doctrine. It is called "Fides."

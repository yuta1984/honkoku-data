Outline of the Heretical Teaching　　　　　　By Setsusō Sōsai

Originally, at the end of the Tenbun era, there were merchants who came from Rome, the capital of Italy in the western barbarian lands, and anchored their ship in Bungo Province. When we inquire about their sea route, they traveled from the western seas toward the south, then from the south toward the north to reach Japan. Therefore, the people of Japan called them "Nanban-jin" (Southern Barbarians). Looking inside their ship, there were merchants, sailors, and others, totaling more than two hundred people. Among them, we see two people whose appearance and clothing differed from the masses. One was called San Francisco Xavier, and the other was called Gaspar. These two people are called "Bateren."

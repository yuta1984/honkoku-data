According to the "Shimabara Subjugation Record," Matsudaira Uemon-suke Tadayuki achieved the first assault on the main citadel on February 28th.

According to the "Amakusa Arima-hara Memorandum," on February 28th in the early morning, when they attempted to climb the stone walls of the main citadel, the enemy thrust at them with spears and naginata, and threw torches and stones, making it appear difficult to climb. At that point, Toki Sanyanosuke of Lord Esaemon's advance guard and Takumi-no-kami's forces came to that location and waited at the base of the stone walls, watching for the right moment. During this time, Jidayū, Nomura Yoemon, and a third person climbed together through gaps in the stone wall. Taking firearms from the ashigaru, they climbed the stone wall while firing two or three shots, striking many enemies, and when they reached the top of the stone wall and climbed onto the fence, the rebels rushed at them but were struck down by their subordinates. Jidayū and Yoemon rushed up together, and Yoemon remained attached to Lord Machidaira Dewa-no-kami. Additionally, Taya no Gorozaemon from the retinue of the Great Minister of Kii, Iwanaga Gonuemon from the retinue of Lord Ōmura Inaba-no-kami, and others fought together. After a while, Lord Itakura Monnōkami and Lord Ishitani Jūzō were at the base of the stone wall...

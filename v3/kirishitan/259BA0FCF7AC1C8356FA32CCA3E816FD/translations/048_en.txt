Kirishitan Suppression Record, Volume 33

According to the memorandum of Sano Yahichizaemon, at the eighth hour (around 2 PM) on the 27th day of the 2nd month, Nabeshima-dono's forces launched an attack on the outer bailey (demaru). In response, those from Matsuura's forces who were on duty at the siege works immediately attacked the second bailey (ninomaru). A man of the same name, Koheiji, was also on duty at the siege works and was the first to charge through Matsuura's assault point, taking a head and being wounded. Since the off-duty members of Matsuura's forces were in their huts, the full force arrived late to the attack on the second bailey.

Okuyama Shigeta and Nagata Ichiroemon were killed in action, Sugiyama Gorobei was wounded by a spear and withdrew, and Murata Sakuemon and Miura Juemon were also wounded and withdrew. Additionally, one servant was killed and three wounded remained. Only Kuwano, Sano, and Kishino continued to fight fiercely for a long time, attempting to break through the enemy, but Koheiji and Kuroemon were also wounded and remained at this location. When Kuwano pursued the enemy's rear, three enemies turned back and engaged in fierce battle. Toriyama Gonzaburo came to assist and speared one enemy to death. Kuwano also speared another enemy to death. The remaining enemy struck Toriyama's face hard with a stone and

fled, running toward the main bailey. Toriyama was struck hard by the flying stone and died on the spot. Kuwano was also wounded by stones and spears but endured the pain and rested there for a while. Kimura Shinzaemon, Kobata Kannoshin, Tachibana Shichiroemon, Hyōchiji Gonnosuke, and Tarao Mokuemon arrived, and together they rested on the right side of the lotus pond below the main bailey, observing which direction they should attack. There they saw their lord Matsukura Ukon charge into the wooden gate entrance of the main bailey and rushed to join him.

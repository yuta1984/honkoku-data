Records of Christian Suppression, Volume 35

Record of the actions of Kurosawa Sadayū and Kishi Hanemon during the Shimabara Rebellion
On the 28th day of the second month at the hour of the Rabbit, fire arrows and incendiary balls were launched from Hosokawa-dono's forces, setting fires. When the Christians climbed onto the roofs, another arrow was shot, and this arrow burned down the houses in the main compound. When Jūkura-dono advanced to the front lines, Hosokawa-dono's military commander ordered him to stop, so Mondo-dono commanded that spears should be used and that he should accompany them. Orders were given to cease the gunfire that was occurring between allied forces across the arrow range, and notifications were sent to Kuroda-dono and Nabeshima-dono as well.

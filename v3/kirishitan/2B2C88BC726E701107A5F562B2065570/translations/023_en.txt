According to Namikawa's memorandum: From the last day of the twelfth month, joining the forces of Baba Saburōzaemon-dono, Okajima Hyōzaemon participated, with Seki Zenzaemon as ashigaru commander, and from the mounted attendants, both Taneda Heiemon and Yamada Magodayū joined.

According to the Christian Uprising Memorandum:
Our forces are to cross immediately to Shimabara front, as just now ordered by Itakura Naizen-dono and Ishitani Jūkura-dono. We intend to cross the sea forthwith. We write this to report this matter.
　　December 31st　　　　　　Hosokawa Higo
　　　　　　To Hineno Oribe-no-kami-sama
　　　　　　　　　　And all gentlemen

Recorded in the Shimabara Chronicle:
During this time, we secretly sent a letter to Dembei's place, so it should certainly have arrived. What a strange turn of events this time has been - all the local people have become enemies. Though we have been held as hostages since spring, we cannot even go to the flowing river. Not only that, though there was one small boat available, thinking we should flee on it, we cut paper and released it on the river, but now there is no way to do anything. Thus we have become like birds in a cage, and we ask that you understand our state of mind. This time, here alone, Gonnnosuke was taken as hostage last year, causing great distress.

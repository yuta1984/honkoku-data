Record of the Christian Suppression, Volume One

　　Kan'ei 14th year, Hinoto-Ushi (Year of the Fire Ox, 1637)
According to the Shimabara records, Matsukura Nagato-no-kami Shigetsugi, lord of Shimabara Castle, had a father, Bungo-no-kami Shigemasa, who was originally a retainer of the Tsutsui family and a relative of Shima Sakon, chief retainer of Ishida Jibu-shōyū (Mitsunari). However, during the Battle of Sekigahara in Keichō 5th year, Kanoe-Ne (Year of the Metal Rat, 1600), he severed his family ties and sided with Tōshō-gū-kun (Tokugawa Ieyasu).

Kumagai Family Memorandum
　A memorandum by Kumagai Sazaemon Ryōgen, retainer of Arima Saemon-no-suke Naozumi. Ryōgen was the second son of Jirōzaemon Ryōchoku. Ryōchoku later became a physician and took the name Dōyo. Ryōchoku's eldest son adopted into another family and was called Hiki Kiemon Kazunobu. Both served as retainers of the Arima house.

【Right page】
I deliberately send word to you. Heaven's messengers (angels) have descended, and through the padre who performs baptisms, they have received the baptism of fire. Therefore, whoever becomes a Christian should come here quickly. Village headmen and others should come quickly. This letter should be circulated throughout the island. Even those who have been baptized, if they become Christians, will be pardoned. I humbly and respectfully state this.
Ox year, tenth month, fifteenth day

As stated above, this should be quickly circulated to all villages. Since I am sending this through Heaven's messenger, please instruct all people in the village. Those who become Christians, in all sixty-six provinces of Japan, by Deus's

Another paper:
The person called Jujuan of Kazusa village is also accompanying the heavenly messenger, so I send this from Jujuan's place to various destinations. It is said that he is Masuda Shirō Ietsuna, the shogun of Japan. The above.

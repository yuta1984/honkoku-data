...you can see, but [the Church] has established [the rule of] at least once or twice a year. The reason is that just as we cleanse the body each time it becomes severely polluted, so too must the anima (soul), which is frequently defiled by evil, be frequently cleansed through confession. Moreover, confession should be made when facing the difficulty of death, and when one resolves to receive the precious Eucharistia (Eucharist). This should be done whenever one clearly recognizes having committed mortal sins, or when one has doubts about such matters, in accordance with Deus's (God's) ordinances.

Disciple: Are there means to repel these sins that are the root of evil?

Master: There are many means. There are seven virtues that oppose these seven sins. Besides these, there are aids that guard the three powers of the soul and the five senses of the body.

Disciple: What are those virtues that oppose the seven sins?

Master: First, humility against pride; second, liberality against greed; third, chastity against lust; fourth, patience against wrath; fifth, temperance against gluttony; sixth, charity against envy; seventh, against sloth...

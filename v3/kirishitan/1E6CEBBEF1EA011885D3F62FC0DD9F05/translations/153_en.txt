Master: These were established by our Lord Jesus Christ in order to give us the grace of His body and the merits of His Passion.

Disciple: How should one receive these sacraments?

Master: Those who receive the sacrament of the Eucharist must, if they have mortal sins, make confession and contrition above all. Those who receive other sacraments should at least receive them with contrition. However, making confession is even more excellent.

Disciple: Which is the first among these seven sacraments?

Master: First of all is the sacrament of baptism

Disciple: What is it that Christians chant?

Master: It is the precious name of Jesus.

Disciple: What is the reason for this?

Master: Jesus means "Savior" in its essence. Therefore, so that we may receive salvation in times of hardship and crisis, we chant the precious name of Jesus. For this reason, when we chant and hear the revered name of Jesus, we should deeply revere it.

Disciple: How should one make Confession?

Master: If it is one's first Confession, one should confess all sins from after baptism until that time. If it is a Confession after having confessed once before, it is essential to reflect upon and confess without omitting anything all sins committed from the previous Confession until that time. For this purpose, one should maintain what is to be expressed in the aforementioned nine articles.

Disciple: What is Satisfaction?

Master: It is to properly offer atonement for our sins to our Lord Jesus Christ. This is indeed our merit...

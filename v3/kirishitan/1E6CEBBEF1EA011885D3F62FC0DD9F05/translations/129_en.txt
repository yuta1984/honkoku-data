Master: According to the commandments of Deus (God), one should desire for one's neighbor the same good things that one desires for oneself.

Disciple: What does it mean to follow the commandments of Deus (God)?

Master: Here lies the detail: when one desires anything for one's neighbor while transgressing against the commandments of Deus (God), even if it seems desirable for oneself, this is not truly loving one's neighbor as oneself. Rather, it is hating one's neighbor just as one hates oneself.

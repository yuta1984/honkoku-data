Disciple: When should oaths be used?

Master: Even if one makes a sworn statement truthfully about minor matters, to make an oath when it is unnecessary - though it may not become a grave sin depending on the circumstances - one should not commit even venial sins.

Disciple: Are there sworn statements made by invoking things other than God?

Master: Indeed there are. For example, one may make oaths invoking the cross or the saints, or invoking noble things, or upon one's own life, or upon any other of God's creations.

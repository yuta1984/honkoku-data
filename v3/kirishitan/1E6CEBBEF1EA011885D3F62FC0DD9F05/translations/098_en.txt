Disciple: What is the meaning of the third artigo (article): "conceived by the Spirito Santo (Holy Spirit) and born of Virgen Maria (Virgin Mary)"?

Master: Deus Filio (God the Son), who is the true Son of Deus Atere (God the Father), received within the precious womb of Virgen Maria (Virgin Mary) a true physical body and true anima (soul) no different from our flesh, and thus became true man.

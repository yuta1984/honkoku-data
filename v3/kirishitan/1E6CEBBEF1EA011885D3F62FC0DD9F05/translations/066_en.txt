Disciple: With what manner of words should we offer our petitions to Deus?

Master: With the remaining words that follow.

Disciple: What do we request with these continuing words?

Master: Seven articles.

Disciple: What are these?

Master: The first is the article saying "Hallowed be Thy name." The meaning of this is that the name of Deus and His gloria (glory) may spread throughout the world, and that all human beings may come to know and revere our Lord Deus and His Son, our Lord Jesus Christ, and honor and esteem Them.

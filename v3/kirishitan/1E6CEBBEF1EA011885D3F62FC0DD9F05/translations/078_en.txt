○Fifth: Concerning the Salve Regina

Disciple: Is there a separate prayer for asking the intercession of the Mother Virgen Santa Maria?

Master: Among the many prayers that Santa Ecclesia (Holy Church) employs, the prayer called Salve Regina is foremost. Now I shall teach you one by one. ○We give thanks to you, Mother of mercy, who art our Queen. We give thanks to you who are our life, our sweetness, and our hope. The children of Eve in exile cry out to you. In this valley of tears, lamenting and weeping, we place our petition before you. Therefore, we ask for your intercession...

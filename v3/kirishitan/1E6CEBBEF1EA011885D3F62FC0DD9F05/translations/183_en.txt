Disciple: "Can these sacraments be received multiple times?"

Master: "Baptism, Confirmation, and Holy Orders - these three sacraments can only be received once. The others can be received multiple times. Among them, the sacraments of Penance and the Most Holy Eucharist are of primary importance for us, so receiving them frequently is especially recommended."

Disciple: Why are these sins called venial sins?

Master: "Venial" means "easily forgivable." These sins are called venial because God forgives them easily.

Disciple: What is the way to receive forgiveness for these sins?

Master: By receiving any sacrament, attending Mass, saying prayers of contrition, receiving penance from a bishop, partaking in Holy Communion, beating one's breast with pious acts, saying Ave Maria prayers with faith, and performing any other acts that serve as signs of contrition - when doing these, forgiveness...

Disciple: What is "persinaal"?

Master: With the right thumb, one traces the sign of the cross on the forehead, mouth, and chest.

Disciple: When making these three signs, what words are spoken?

Master: "Persi in nomine sancte crucis de inimicis nostris libera nos Deus noster." â—‹ The meaning of these words is: "Our God, by the sign of the holy cross, deliver us from our enemies." While reciting the phrase "Persi in nomine sancte crucis," one makes the sign of the cross on the forehead. With the phrase "de inimicis nostris," one makes the sign of the cross on the mouth. With the phrase "libera nos Deus noster," one makes the sign of the cross on the chest.

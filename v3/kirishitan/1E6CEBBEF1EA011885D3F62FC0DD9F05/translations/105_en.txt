Third, above Purgatory there is the Limbo of Children, which is the place for children who died without receiving baptism and while still lacking the discernment to fall into grave sins. Fourth, above this Limbo there is a place called Abraham's Bosom, where the good people of ancient times waited for the resurrection. Our Lord Jesus Christ descended to this place and summoned the souls of those saints from there.

Disciple: What does it mean that He rose again on the third day?

Master: On Sexta Feria (Friday) at the time of His death, His precious soul separated from His body, and on the following Domingo (Sunday) His soul was placed in the coffin

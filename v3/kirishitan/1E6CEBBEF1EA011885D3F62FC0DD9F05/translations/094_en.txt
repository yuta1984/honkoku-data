Those things He creates are not His essence itself. Moreover, since Deus (God) is infinite and capable of accomplishing all things, He creates all things without need for foundation, seeds, tools, or anything else, and therefore it is said that He creates from nothing. Furthermore, the things He creates are finite things. Therefore, the distinction between Deus's essence and created things is like the difference between heaven and earth, and even that comparison falls short.

Disciple: From the above, I have quickly understood the distinction between Deus and the things He creates. Now please clarify whether created things are one body or separate bodies in relation to each other.

Disciple: How many articles are there in the commandments of God's laws?

Master: There are ten articles. These are divided into two parts. The first three articles pertain to the glory of God, and the remaining seven articles are for the virtue between neighbors.

First: You shall worship and revere the one true God
Second: You shall not make vain oaths in His holy name
Third: You shall diligently observe the Sunday holy day
Fourth: You shall be filial to your father and mother
Fifth: You shall not kill people
Sixth: You shall not commit adultery
Seventh: You shall not steal

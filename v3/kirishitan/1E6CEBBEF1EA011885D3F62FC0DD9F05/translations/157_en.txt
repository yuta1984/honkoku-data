Disciple: Is there another path to salvation without receiving baptism?

Master: For salvation in the afterlife, it is generally an indispensable path. Through this, one should be completely saved when it is accomplished. However, even if there is a person who desires to receive baptism but cannot find the opportunity and dies in that state, if that person is truly without negligence, they can still be saved even without receiving water baptism. This is because their desire for baptism was deep. Nevertheless, as I mentioned before, repentance for sins committed is most essential.

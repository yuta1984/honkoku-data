Disciple: Who composed this Creed?

Master: The Apostles of our Lord Jesus Christ, gathering together in one place under the guidance of the Holy Spirit, composed it by connecting together what they had heard directly from the mouth of our Lord Jesus Christ.

Disciple: For what purpose did they compose it?

Master: In order to teach us the articles that should be received in faith.

Disciple: What is faith?

Master: Faith is when God earnestly [desires us to] firmly believe in the things that Deus (God) reveals and makes known to us, just as Santa Ecclesia (Holy Church) teaches,

Master: That is not so. In order to maintain and practice these, the grace of God is essential.

Disciple: What means are there for receiving that grace from God?

Master: The sacraments of our Holy Mother Church. It is essential to receive these sacraments with proper preparation and devotion.

Disciple: How many sacraments are there?

Master: There are seven. First is baptism, second is confirmation, third is the Eucharist, fourth is penance, fifth is extreme unction, sixth is ordination, and seventh is matrimony.

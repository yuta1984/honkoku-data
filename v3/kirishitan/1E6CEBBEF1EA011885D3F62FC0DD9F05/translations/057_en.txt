Disciple: When we call the world an enemy, what kind of thing is the world for us?

Master: We name the evil deeds, evil acts, and evil people in the world as "the world."

Disciple: How does the world promote temptations?

Master: It is that which makes one recall the evil deeds and evil acts mentioned above, as well as the wicked gossip and such of evil people.

Disciple: What is the way to defend against these things?

Master: The way is to remember the commandments of Deus and Lord Jesus Christ first and foremost, as well as the good conduct of virtuous people.

We have not witnessed Jesus Christ's birth, death, and resurrection, nor have we seen the other articles contained in the Creed, so how should we believe in them?

Master: Although we have not seen these things, we must believe them because God has revealed them to us. Therefore, the articles of faith are even more certain than things we see with our eyes.

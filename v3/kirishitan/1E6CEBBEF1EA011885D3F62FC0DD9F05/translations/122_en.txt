Disciple: How should one observe the fifth commandment?

Master: One should observe it by not doing harm to people, not injuring them, not wounding them, and by not wishing for or rejoicing in such evil deeds being done to others. This is because our bodies are all made in God's image.

Disciple: As a master, is it not permissible to punish one's retainers and subordinates?

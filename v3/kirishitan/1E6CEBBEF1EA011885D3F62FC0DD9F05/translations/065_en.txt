Disciple: Why do we say "Our Father" rather than "My Father"?

Master: So that we may think of all people as brothers and children of a good Father, and thus cherish one another.

Disciple: What does it mean that Deus resides in heaven?

Master: By remembering that our Father and our joy are both in heaven, we should cast aside our thoughts of this world.

Disciple: Earlier you taught that Deus resides everywhere, but now you say He resides in heaven. What does this mean?

Master: Although Deus resides everywhere, He directly reveals His being to the good people He has chosen...

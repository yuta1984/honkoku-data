How unfortunate are you who lack salvation! Rise up from your sleep. So that when the time of judgment comes, you will not be passed over in His selection, prepare yourself now while there is still time - this admonition to oneself is of utmost importance.

Furthermore, although there are many scriptural proofs beyond this, I shall omit them here. When our Lord, who is the precious heavenly Teacher, directly teaches us the righteous path of judgment to such an extent, what reason could there be to think of opposing it?

Section 4: Resolving doubts regarding the above principles

If someone were to say, "Was not the thief on the right saved by a single word at his final moment?" The answer to this is different. Listen to what Saint Augustine declares: That thief on the right began to believe in Christ at that moment, and at the same time received words equivalent to baptism, and died immediately, which is why he was instantly saved. Just as today, if someone who is about to receive baptism dies at that very moment, they would undoubtedly be saved - the thief's final moment was the same as the moment of baptism, he adds. The manifestation of God's Son is evidenced as proof that He is God, exactly as written in the ancient prophecies. At the climax of His Passion, the sun lost its light, the earth trembled, the dead came back to life, and among the various miracles He revealed, one was precisely the saving of the thief on the right. That thief received faith and revered Him as Emperor, and while all the people mocked Him, he alone praised Him - is this not a miracle of unprecedented wonder? Truly, such a divine miracle is...

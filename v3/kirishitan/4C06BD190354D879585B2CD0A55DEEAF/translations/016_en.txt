"Delusion" means: first, the mind that prefers suffering and takes it as pleasure, not knowing true pleasure. Second, the mind that does not understand the principle of impermanence, becomes attached to this world, and creates thoughts of permanence. Third, the mind that receives a body of ten evils and eight sufferings, and though bound by the fetters of afflictions, thinks itself to be free. Fourth, not knowing that this body is impure

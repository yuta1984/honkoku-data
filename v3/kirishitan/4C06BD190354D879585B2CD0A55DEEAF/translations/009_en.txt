When this mind is of the same substance as empty space, such a person is called a practitioner of the six unobstructed powers. They are also called a person of no-form and no-thought. The sutra says, "Rather than making offerings to all Buddhas of the three periods, it is better to make offerings to one practitioner of no-mind." Those who practice the Buddha Way study this path.

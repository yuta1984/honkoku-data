## Chigyo Mokuroku (Register of Stipend Grant) — Translation

**Register of Stipend Grant**

Domain of Koyanagawa Yukie

　　For the twenty ashigaru (foot soldiers) of Notezaki:

　　　　Within Notezaki Village, Esashi District:
One item: 10 kan, 200 mon

The above amount is hereby granted as stipulated. Issued accordingly.

　　　　　Kumagaya Sai

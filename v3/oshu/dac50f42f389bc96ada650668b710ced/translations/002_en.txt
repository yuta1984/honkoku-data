**Record of Curtain Crests (Makumon Oboe)**

Item: The *ichimon* (immediate clan members) shall use the crest of the *take-suzume* (bamboo and sparrow), along with the *nitan-gashira* (two-ended) and *ni-hikiryō* (double horizontal stripes) crests.

Item: The *ikka* (close family members) shall use the *nitan-gashira* and *ni-hikiryō* crests.

Item: The *ichizoku* (extended clan members) shall be assigned the *sandan-gashira* (three-ended) and *ni-hikiryō* crests.

Item: The crests of each household shall not be allowed to become extinct.

Item: Needless to say, those of the *ikka* and *ichizoku* rank and below shall not use the aforementioned crests.

The above concerns the matter of the said crests.

25th day, 12th month, Enpō 3 [1675]

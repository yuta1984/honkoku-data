You have devoted yourself wholeheartedly to the study of Taneda-ryū spear techniques, and have achieved admirable progress. On this occasion, I hereby confer upon you the "Meshiai Mokuroku" (intermediate certificate of proficiency). Beyond this, it is of utmost importance that you continue to apply yourself diligently until you reach the levels of full license (menkyo) and the innermost secrets (ōgi). That is all.

　　　Kuribayashi Tokugorō
An auspicious day in the twelfth month of Keiō 1 (1865)

　　To: Koyanaigawa Uneme

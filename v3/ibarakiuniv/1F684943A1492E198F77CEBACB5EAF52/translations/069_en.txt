10th day: Cloudy from morning, continuing all day. Warm weather.
《Marginal note: 65 degrees》

11th day: Rain from dawn, cold weather. Intermittent rain. Cold with north wind. Unsettled weather. Heavy rain from evening. Rain all night.
《Marginal note: From two or three days ago, in the area of Ueno Kanecho and Shinyashiki》
《Marginal note: There are those who have died from cholera》

12th day: Rain from evening, cold weather. Rain all day, occasionally stopping. Also intermittent at night.
《Marginal note: In various towns, there are prayers for protection against cholera》
《Marginal note: People from various towns flocking to Hachiman Shrine and Tennoji Temple, great commotion》

13th day: Rain until past the 6th hour of dawn, then clearing. Exceptionally clear at night too.
《Marginal note: 58 degrees》

Written on September 8th, Edo

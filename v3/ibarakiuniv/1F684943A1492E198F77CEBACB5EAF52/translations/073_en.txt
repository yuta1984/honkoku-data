However, when both parents became seriously ill with cholera, the villagers made them take [the family] out to that place and build a temporary hut for them. Even though they built it, it was the same as sleeping in the fields, and [the girl] said it was sad that they were letting her parents die without care. [The guard] said that such treatment was too harsh, and that he should immediately report this to the district office for proper handling. He gave her 2 shu in gold and sent her back. The district office immediately dispatched [officials], but by then the parents had already died.

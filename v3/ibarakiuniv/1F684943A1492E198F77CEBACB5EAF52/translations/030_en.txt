　　　Fifth Month

1st day　　　Clear from morning with light heat, cloudy from evening
(63 degrees)
2nd day　　　Rain from dawn, occasionally heavy. Rain all day, continuing into the night
(In many places there was no water, rice seedbeds were like dry land with cracked fields, and seedlings seemed likely to wither and die. However, after making rain prayers at shrines and temples in various places, today it rained for the first time in a long while, and people in the countryside are said to be rejoicing, saying "gold has fallen from the sky")
3rd day　　　Clear and hot from morning. Light heat. Rain from the 5th half-hour of night
(67 degrees. For the next two or three days again...)
(It appears that many people came out from the countryside, heading toward Koume and Kogane areas)
4th day　　　Clear from morning with light heat, scattered clouds appearing. Evening
(71 degrees)　　　Drizzle from past 7 o'clock, stopping immediately. Rain from 4 o'clock at night

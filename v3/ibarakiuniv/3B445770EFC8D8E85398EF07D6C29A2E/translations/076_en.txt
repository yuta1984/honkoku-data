Large trees fell, and teahouses and restaurants were destroyed throughout. Soto-Kanda suffered nothing particular. Yanagihara Dote-shita was damaged, with some willows broken. Around Kanda, new houses had their roofs damaged in various places. Toshima-machi and Hashimoto-machi suffered some damage. Bakuro-machi, Yokoyama-machi, and Abura-machi - all these areas had little damage. Daimon-dori, Odenma-machi, Hon-machi, Ishi-machi, Muro-machi, and Ryogae-machi also suffered nothing particular. The estate walls in Mikawa-machi and Ogawa-machi were extensively damaged. The estates in Marunouchi suffered nothing particular. In Yamanote, there were many collapsed houses at Suidobashi and Koishikawa. Denzu-in temple in Kohinata suffered some damage. Suido-machi suffered some damage. Around Otsuka there were collapsed houses. Otowa suffered severe damage. Small estates around Ushigome were damaged. Ichigaya had damage in various places, with large trees fallen in several locations. Yotsuya suffered some damage. Kojimachi-dori had nothing particular. Estate damage could be seen in various places around Ban-machi. There were destroyed houses near Akasaka Tameike.

Many farmers' houses in Omi province and nearby areas collapsed, but there were not many deaths or injuries.

What benevolent virtue this is - how grateful we should be!

I have merely recorded what I saw and heard, so while there are no omissions [in what I witnessed], please excuse the circumstances of places I did not know or hear about.

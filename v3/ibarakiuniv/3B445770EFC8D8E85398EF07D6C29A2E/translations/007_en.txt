17th Day: From morning clear and warm weather. From the 4th hour (around 10 AM) a strong west wind blew strongly, stopping at the 7th and half hour (around 5 PM).

18th Day: From morning clear but the lingering cold was strong. From past the 4th and half hour (around 11 AM) a strong west wind blew, stopping at the 7th hour (around 4 PM).

19th Day: Heavy frost and cold, but spring-like. All day the weather was clear.
(28 degrees)

20th Day: Cloudy from morning, overcast sky all day. From past the 9th hour (past noon) rain fell until dawn.
(34 degrees)

21st Day: In the morning light rain fell intermittently but soon stopped. Warm weather. Held up all day, cleared at night.

22nd Day: Cloudy from morning, snow falling lightly. From the 9th hour (noon) snow fell and it was cold. Then it turned to rain. Snow fell even at night, and the sea was exceptionally rough.
(42 degrees)

23rd Day: Snow fell until dawn. Stopped from the 4th hour (around 10 AM). From the 8th hour of night (around 2 AM) the snow melted.

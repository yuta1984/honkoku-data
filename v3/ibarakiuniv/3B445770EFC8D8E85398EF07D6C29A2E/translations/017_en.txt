(Snow fell on the mountains around Nikko-san and Ota area)

A copy of the oral statement regarding the incident in the first month of Ansei 3, year of the Dragon, when Kaneko Kenshiro of the Mito domain visited the residence of Fukuda Hanko in Motoya-no-kura, Edo, for New Year's greetings, and his retainer Shimizu Kakujiro killed Teisei.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　Kaneko Kenshiro

On the 17th, I had business and went to visit one Fukuda Hanko in Motoya-no-kura. It happened that one Ubukata Teisei, a resident of Muramatsu-cho, was also present. During our casual conversation, Teisei said to me that I was "like a child," so I also playfully called Teisei a "little boy." Teisei appeared to become angry at this, so I said that "child" and "little boy" should mean the same thing, but Teisei seemed unsatisfied with this reasoning. Then Hanko suggested that I should apologize, so I said that since there was no reason to be angry over the aforementioned discussion, we should settle it amicably. However, Hanko then insisted that I should leave immediately. Since I rejected such demands, and as I had no intention of fighting over this matter, I was saying we should let go of each other when my retainer Shimizu Kakujiro also rushed over to separate us. When everyone let go and we were being separated, Kakujiro noticed that my hair had become disheveled, and though he appeared very angry, both sides were separated...

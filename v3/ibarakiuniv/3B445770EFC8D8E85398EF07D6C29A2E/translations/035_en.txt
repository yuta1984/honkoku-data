The first daughter, Omatsu, is 6 shaku 2 sun tall, weighs 32 kan 800 monme, and is 16 years old. The second daughter, Oyuki, is 5 shaku 5 sun tall, weighs 25 kan 500 monme, and is 12 years old. The third daughter, Oume, is 4 shaku 8 sun tall, weighs 18 kan 800 monme, and is 8 years old.

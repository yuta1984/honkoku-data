Wax: the greatest decline in 30-40 years
From last winter to this spring, the market price was around 2,300-400 me per 2 sen, 
but now it has fallen to around 4,300 me per 4 sen - a rare decline in recent years, 
it is said.

Rice
Near Nihonbashi, within three chō, at places like Fukiage and Matsu, the market price for brown rice 
purchased was 5 to 9 shō per 6 to, and even premium rice was available. Sendai domain rice sales: 
no rice available at this time.

Morita Sadakichi
　　　　　　　　　　　　　Takahashi Kihei
　　　　　　　　　　Total: Fifteen persons

Message sent to Tsuchiya Uneme-no-shō on the 18th day of the 7th month of the Year of the Dragon via relay station courier:

Regarding the construction of new batteries at both river mouths in your territory, the installation of cannons, and the preparation of guard ships, etc., it has been ordered that these shall be manufactured in the Western style as detailed in the attached document. Understanding this intention, the local magistrates shall take charge of the aforementioned coastal defense construction, cannon casting, and ship construction, and shall investigate and manage the necessary expenses accordingly. Therefore, four drawings of the batteries are being sent. For detailed matters, Matsudaira Kawachi-no-kami, Kawachi Saemon-no-jō Tatsuta Iwatarō, Iwase Shurinosuke, Ōkubo Ukon-shōgen, and Muragaki Yosaburo shall be consulted, and this too shall be communicated. Furthermore, the personnel to be entrusted with the batteries will be appointed successively, so please understand this matter accordingly.

The above
　　　　　　　　　　　　　Joint signatures
　7th month, 18th day
　　　To Lord Tsuchiya Uneme-no-shō

11th day: Cloudy from morning. The sun came out strongly after 4 o'clock.
(Half-grown)

12th day: Clear weather from morning.
(70 degrees)

13th day: Clear weather from morning. Cloudy from 8 o'clock, humid and hot. Occasional light rain. Clear during the night.
(83 degrees after noon)

14th day: Clear weather from morning. Light rain at 8 o'clock, humid and hot. Stopped immediately. Exceptionally clear after nightfall.
(77 degrees, Minato Ouchi Seizaemon departed)
(At the end of the Dragon Year, he was appointed as Finance Magistrate, but today his main duties were removed and he was appointed to Minato Office Affairs with Junior Treasurer rank)

15th day: Clear weather from morning with scattered clouds. Cloudy from 8 o'clock, light rain stopped immediately. After 7:30, sudden heavy rain at dusk. Heavy rain all night, stopped at dawn.
(77 degrees)

16th day: Light rain from morning, cold air. Cloudy sky all day, unsettled weather.
(70 degrees)

17th day: Fog in the morning. Light rain from 9:30, occasionally clear. Heavy rain from 7:30, thunder at 6 o'clock in the evening. Heavy rain during the night as well.
(72 degrees, Minor Heat)
(June solar term)

18th day: Light rain from morning. Stopped immediately. Light rain from after 9:30, stopped in the evening. Cold air. Strong rain from after 5 o'clock during the night.

19th day: Cloudy from morning, cold air. Cloudy sky all day, unsettled weather. Northern weather pattern, cold air.
(62 degrees)

20th day: Cloudy from morning, cold air. Rain from 8 o'clock.

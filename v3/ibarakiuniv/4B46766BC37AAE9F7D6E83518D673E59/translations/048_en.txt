《Marginal note: Upper and lower honorable towns　　　　Shishido 6 to 3-4 sho, from yesterday selling 8 go per 100 mon, Kasama 6 to 3-4 sho》
　Weather has been unsettled, continuing cold and overcast from before the summer solstice period
　Under these circumstances, should there be any disaster such as storms, famine would surely follow
　The extent is incalculable, and though everyone should be mindful, as has been
　repeatedly proclaimed and previously communicated, even more frugality and economy
　should be practiced, especially eating coarse food, being very careful even with provisions
　so that in times of greatest need, relief food can be sustained. This message should be
　conveyed to everyone including tenant farmers without exception. Above

23rd day: West wind blew from after the 8th hour of night (around 2 AM), wind stopped at dawn with clear weather. West wind blew from after the 4th hour (around 10 AM) until evening.
(Temperature: 37 degrees)

On the 19th day at the first part of the dog hour (around 8 PM), a fire broke out in the area of the kitchen administration and inspection offices within the Edo residence, and the kitchen administration offices and others were burned down. A notice regarding this matter is expected. End of report.

24th day: Clear from morning. West wind blew from half past the 5th hour (around 9 AM), wind stopped from around the 8th hour (around 2 AM).
(Temperature: 30 degrees)

25th day: Heavy frost, strong cold. Clear all day.
(Temperature: 25 degrees)

26th day: Heavy frost, clear. Snow fell from evening, stopped after the 5th hour of night (after 9 AM) and became clear.
(Temperature: 30 degrees)

27th day: Cloudy from morning. Overcast all day, rain fell at night.
(Temperature: 30 degrees)

28th day: Clear from morning.
(Temperature: 37 degrees)

Letter dated 22nd day of first month:
The goods we sent up to you during last winter have now suddenly become very profitable items. If you have any remaining stock on hand, we believe you should confidently sell them off. Cotton goods have been at extremely high prices until now, and the demand is gradually becoming stronger.

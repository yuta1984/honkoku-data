20th day: Rain from morning, rain all day with strong south wind, strong rain and wind continued through the night

On the night of the 13th from the 8th hour, there was a great fire in Edo starting from Setomono-cho. The south wind was particularly strong at the time, and it burned from Setomono-cho 2-chome to Odawara-cho, Anjin-cho, Ukiyo-koji, Ise-cho, Honda-cho from the middle of 3-chome to 4-chome, Hon-ishi-cho from the middle of 4-chome to Teppo-cho, Ko-denma-cho 2-chome, the prison, Shirogane-cho 4-chome, O-denma Shio-cho, Kamei-cho up to the new bridge. The fire was extinguished past the 4th hour on the morning of the following 14th day. 360-70 prisoners were executed, so food vendors in that area are said to be in great distress.

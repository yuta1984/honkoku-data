19th day　　Rain from evening, cold air. Rain stopped after 8 o'clock, cold air all day
《Side note: 65 degrees |　》

Three French people came to Edo, became instructors at the Kyōbusho (military academy), were granted the Matsudaira family name, and appointed as Shimōsa-no-kami. It is said they became senior wakadoshiyori (junior councilors).

20th day　　Cloudy from morning with cold air, occasional sunshine. Rain from 5 o'clock after nightfall
《Side note: 65 degrees |　》

21st day　　Cloudy from morning with cold air, light sunshine all day, light rain during the night
《Side note: 64 degrees |　》

Masakiya from Iioka in Shinshū province visited. According to his story, he departed in mid-July when brown rice had fallen to about 2 to 2 shō per ryō, whereas until recently it was about 1 to 2 shō per ryō. During July, the rice had not yet headed, but due to good weather, prices continued to fall.

22nd day: Clear skies from morning, cold, cloudy from evening
(44 degrees)

From yesterday, respectable people of the upper town collectively contributed money, and at the Teppo-machi meeting place began selling rice for disaster relief at 2 gō per 100 mon. It is said that over 2,000 people came in one day. Each person receives 2 gō per day, with identification certificates issued by the village headman, and town officials working at the meeting place.

Koku-machi sells at 1 gō 3 shaku per 100 mon

23rd day: Light rain from before seven in the morning, rain stops
(62 degrees) around eight o'clock

24th day: Cloudy morning, patchy clouds from five o'clock, intermittent
sunshine all day, north wind blows at eight o'clock, heavy rain
for about half an hour from five o'clock at night, clear at four o'clock

25th day: Cold morning, clear skies, light north wind
(47 degrees)

26th day: Lightly cloudy morning, southern weather pattern all day with light
(48 degrees) sunshine, cloudy during the night

27th day: Lightly sunny morning, warm, clear from five-thirty, east wind
(55 degrees) from around four o'clock until around eight o'clock

(88th night)

28th day: Lightly cloudy morning, intermittent sunshine until five o'clock, warm,
(54 degrees) wind stops and becomes cloudy from seven o'clock

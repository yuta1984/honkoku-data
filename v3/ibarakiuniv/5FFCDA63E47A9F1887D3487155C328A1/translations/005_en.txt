New Year's Day: Clear skies from morning, extremely calm all day with no wind. From dawn until after the hour of the Horse, there were streaky clouds from the east to the southeast direction. (26 degrees)

2nd day: Clear skies from morning, streaky clouds from east to southeast direction. Scattered clouds all day, after the hour of the Monkey small hail fell intermittently. Cleared immediately. (25 degrees)

3rd day: Frost falls. Clear skies with cold air. North wind blows from the hour of the Horse. Earthquake at the hour of the Snake. North wind until evening, stops at the hour of the Rooster at dusk. (28 degrees)

4th day: Heavy frost, very strong cold air, extremely clear skies. (23 degrees)

5th day: Light overcast from morning, bright sunshine from after the hour of the Boar, clear skies. (32 degrees)

6th day: Light scattered clouds in morning, cold air eases. Cloudy from after the hour of the Horse, light rain from the hour of the Monkey. Rain begins from half past the hour of the Monkey. Rain continues all night. (35 degrees)

7th day: Rain from morning. Rain stops after half past the hour of the Boar. Clear with bright sunshine from after half past the hour of the Horse. West wind from after half past the hour of the Monkey, stops in the evening. (35 degrees)

Regarding His Imperial Majesty's illness, as he passed away on the 29th day of the 12th month of last year, singing, hunting, construction, and martial arts [were prohibited] from the 4th day...

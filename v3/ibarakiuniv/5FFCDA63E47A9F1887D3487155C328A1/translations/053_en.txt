From tomorrow, the first day of the month, an official notice was issued regarding the exchange rate for copper coins: 8 kan 500 mon per 1 ryō of gold shall be used. Accordingly, various goods will be marked up in price, or small items will be traded in silver currency. From morning, matters concerning goods cannot be settled through silver currency trading. Should there be anyone who violates this directive, they will be arrested and subjected to interrogation, so this must be communicated strictly without exception to all under jurisdiction.

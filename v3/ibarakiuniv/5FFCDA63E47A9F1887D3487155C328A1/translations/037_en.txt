12th day: Drizzle falling from morning, stopping at 5:30
(67 degrees)　Occasional weak sunshine from 7 o'clock, clear at night with
　　　　patchy clouds and cool air

Early this month, thirteen or fourteen night thieves broke into a kimono shop in Toride-juku,
taking over eighty ryō in saved money that was in the shop, and furthermore
entered the kitchen, took the master hostage and led him away. As they departed, they told
the shop workers that if they brought one hundred fifty ryō in gold, they would
return the master, and took him away, it is said.

Generally, their daily conduct was poor - they were the ringleaders who raised house rents throughout Ōta and also raised the annual tribute on rice fields, etc. Moreover, with grain prices high, although everyone else provided relief sales last year and this year, they did not do so and only indulged in greed. Therefore, they had been extremely hated until now, and there were many spectators during the house-breaking as well.

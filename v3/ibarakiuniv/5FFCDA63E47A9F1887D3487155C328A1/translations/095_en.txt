At the Ise Inuiya main house, when the night watchman was making his rounds, 
he found a white horse at the front entrance and was surprised. When he looked with a lantern and such, 
it was a wooden horse, so he thought it strange. It was said that one of the four 
sacred horses of Ise Grand Shrine had gone missing and they were searching for it. 
The Inuiya also sent a person to Ise to inquire with the oshi priests, 
and they said it was indeed a sacred horse and should be returned immediately. However, the Inuiya 
said that since the sacred horse had descended to their own residence, they could not 
return it and should make a new one instead, it is said.

At the house of Hakamaya Kihei in Owari Province, sacred amulets from seventy-two shrines 
descended, it is said. Hearing this, countless pilgrims came dancing and walking from various villages, 
it is said.

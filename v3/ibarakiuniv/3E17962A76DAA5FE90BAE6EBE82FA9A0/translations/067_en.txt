　　《Side note: 400 koku　》　　　　　　　Mikami　Kaian

　Today at the harbor, 1,400 bales of Nambu fish meal arrived, at 14 mon per bale, with
　light-weight bales at about 4 bu per bale, regular ones at 5-6 bu per bale. Saida salt
　arrived yesterday and was priced at 13 mon 3 bu. It is said that sardine fishing
　has been taking place around Kuji for the past 2-3 days. Around Iso harbor and Hiraiso,
　there has been a great catch of bonito for the past 2-3 days. Four boats from Suya
　caught 1,300 fish yesterday. Large mizuna sells for 158 mon per piece. At Hiraiso,
　some boats caught 1,400-1,500 fish per boat, loading them back onto the boats
　like stacking bales. New head meal at 14 mon per bale, about 6 bu per bale.

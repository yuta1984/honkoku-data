Seventh Month
1st day (Kinoe-Inu): Clear weather from morning, strong south wind from evening, four o'clock
《Marginal note: 74 degrees | 87 degrees》 half past until eight-thirty, south wind particularly strong
《Marginal note: From this spring, bonito | fishing completely absent》 Wind calmed at seven o'clock. Weather clear for the first time in a long while
《Marginal note: Coastal areas like famine | years with many empty shops》 Night also particularly clear and cool
2nd day: Extremely clear from morning, not a cloud in the sky
《Marginal note: 75 degrees | 87 degrees》 Strong heat, clear and cool until night
3rd day: Extremely clear from morning, excellent weather all day, strong heat
《Marginal note: 76 degrees | 88 degrees》 Scattered clouds at night
4th day: South wind and intermittent rain from before dawn, muggy
《Marginal note: 80 degrees | 90 degrees》 Sunny from four-thirty, strong south wind
Wind continued until after four o'clock at night, heavy rain from four-thirty at night
5th day: Rain from evening, stopped at five o'clock. Muggy, occasional rain
《Marginal note: 80 degrees | 74 degrees》 Heavy rain after four-thirty, then extremely heavy rain after eight-thirty, became like the Hour of the Ox and Tiger pattern, cold air. Particularly heavy rain all day. Rain stopped during the night
6th day: Clear from morning, scattered clouds occasionally, rain falls. Heavy rain at night

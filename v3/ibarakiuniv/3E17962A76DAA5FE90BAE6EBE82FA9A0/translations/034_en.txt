15th day: Heavy rain until dawn. Northeast wind, cold air. Clear from 5:30 (71 degrees), cloudy from evening.

16th day: Rain from before 7 AM, cold weather. Rain stopped from 8 o'clock (to Saida salt port, 18 units, about 14 monme 5 bu per ryo).

17th day: Clear from morning, scattered clouds from after 4:30.

18th day: Morning mist and clouds, from 5 o'clock northeast wind (68 degrees) became cold.

19th day: Cloudy from morning, overcast all day (safflowers: 750 mon).

20th day: Rain from morning, rain stopped from after 4 o'clock (water flowers: 800 mon).

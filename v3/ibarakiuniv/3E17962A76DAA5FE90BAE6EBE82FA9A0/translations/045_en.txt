《Side note: 73 degrees, cold weather》 Rain occasionally stopped, heavy rain continued all day until midnight.

On the 4th of this month, a fire broke out in Kyoto from the hour of the Horse until the 5th hour of night. From Gojō, the upper, middle, and lower Shushuya-machi districts and Higashi Hongan-ji temple burned, with the fire reaching as far as the Karatachi Palace area.

24th day: Rain from evening, stopped around the 4th hour, drizzling rain from the 9th hour.
《Side note: 77 degrees, 74 degrees at the 9th hour》 Cold weather with northern winds, light rain all day.
《Side note: Due to cold weather, wearing lined kimono》 Same conditions continued through the night.

Yesterday on the 18th, intelligence about captives reported by Yokoyama Jinzaemon attached to the castle is as follows:

1. 13th day: One black ship arrived at Shimoda
1. 15th day: One ship arrived at Shimoda  
1. 17th day: One ship arrived at Kanagawa

According to him, of the ships that came on the 13th and 14th, one ship is scheduled to go to Kanagawa today. Following them, fast ships from Russia, England, France, Turkey, etc., about 35-36 to 40 vessels in total, are expected to arrive successively. It is said they come riding on their victory after defeating Qing China.

This report reached the military headquarters on the evening of the 17th, the day before yesterday. Immediately, Chief Accounting Magistrate Nagai Genba-no-kami, Inspector Iwase Higo-no-kami, Uraga Magistrate Sakai Ukon, Shimoda Magistrate Inoue Shinano-no-kami, and others departed from headquarters.

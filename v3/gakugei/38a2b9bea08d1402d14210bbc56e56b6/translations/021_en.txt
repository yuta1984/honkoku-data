**On Charms to Prevent Lightning from Striking**

"*Akata*" 〔Affix to the eastern pillar〕　"*Setsuteiro*" 〔Affix to the southern pillar〕
"*Shūdakō*" 〔Affix to the western pillar〕　"*Sodamani*" 〔Affix to the northern pillar〕

Write these charms on paper and affix them to the four pillars (posts) of the house in each cardinal direction. Chant aloud: "*Akata, Setsuteiro, Shūdakō, Sodamani*" — seven times. Write "*Ryōhōsha*" —

---

**[Left Page — Lower Section]**

—and affix it to the very center of the ceiling. Then chant the following verse seven times:

"*Unrai ko seiden* 雲雷鼓掣電 — Thunder clouds rumble and lightning flashes;
*Kōbaku ju taiū* 降雹澍大雨 — Hail falls and great rains pour down;
*Nenpi Kannon riki* 念彼観音力 — By the power of Kannon whom we call to mind,
*Ōji toku shōsan* 応時得消散 — In that very moment, all disperses and is extinguished."

If one does all of this, lightning will never strike the house.

**On a Method for Revealing a Thief**

- Take the kelp (*kombu*) that was offered to the *Toshitoku-shin* (the deity of the year's auspicious direction), burn it to a black char (*kurodaki*), put it into sake, and have all persons under suspicion drink that sake. Whoever committed the theft will immediately develop a swollen cheek — a wondrous effect.

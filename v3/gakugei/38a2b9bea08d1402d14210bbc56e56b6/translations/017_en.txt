**A Method for Quickly Drying Lacquerware**

- If one wishes to dry lacquerware quickly for immediate use, add a small amount of powdered Japanese pepper (*sansho*, *Zanthoxylum piperitum*) to the lacquer before applying it. The lacquer will dry rapidly.

**A Method for Causing Pictures or Characters to Appear on an Abalone Shell as if Born Naturally into It**

…inside [the shell], use *seshime* lacquer to paint pictures or write characters…

---

**[Left Page — Lower Section]**

- Inside an abalone shell of sufficiently fine grain and free of blemishes, paint pictures or write characters using *seshime* lacquer. Seal the holes of the abalone shell tightly, pour in a full measure of vinegar, and leave it for about one month. After that, discard the vinegar and wash away the lacquer and examine the shell. What has been written will appear vividly and distinctly [etched into the shell's surface], and it will truly become a remarkable and curious vessel (*kibutsu no ki*) that will endure for generations to come.

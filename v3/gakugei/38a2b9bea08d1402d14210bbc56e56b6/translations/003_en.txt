**"Jinchino Daitoku Banpo no Sho" (A Book of Great Virtue and Ten Thousand Treasures of Human Wisdom) — Complete in 9 Volumes**

The first two volumes of this work reveal the way to use one's mind so that wisdom and all virtues become perfectly fulfilled, enabling one to escape suffering throughout one's life and freely attain wealth and prosperity at will. The remaining seven volumes, dealing with wondrous arts and natural philosophy, reveal the profound and mysterious benefits that lie beyond ordinary human knowledge.

---

**"Kinno Banka Setsuyoho" (Treasured Household Encyclopedia) — Complete in 1 Volume**

This book collects eighty important rules and practices for daily household use, followed by an iroha-ordered dictionary (setsuyōshū), and then an illustrated encyclopedia of all things (Kinmō Zui). This illustrated volume covers everything from the sun, moon, and stars above, down to the smallest birds, beasts, fish, and insects; the mountains, rivers, people, living creatures, and plants of various provinces; and indeed every thing found between heaven and earth. It concludes with a complete collection of annual events, pilgrimages, and all matters related to the calendar. Truly, it is a treasury of practical knowledge.

---

**"Sode no Naka Doji Orai" (Children's Correspondence Primer) — Small Format, Complete in 1 Volume**

This book collects, with kana readings, texts from works such as *Teikin Orai*, *Jitsugokyō*, and *Kojō Zoroe* in their entirety, with illustrations added, so that children may enjoy it as a plaything and naturally learn to read on their own, making it a valuable aid to their future studies.

---

**"Shōni Iryō Tebikigusa" (A Guide to Children's Medicine) — Complete in 3 Volumes**

This work collects highly secret and effective remedies for the five childhood ailments (gogan), convulsions (kyōfū), smallpox (tōshin), and various other diseases, recording in full the methods of reading the pulse and carrying out treatment. In particular, detailed instruction on health preservation and raising children to be robust is provided throughout. Written entirely in kana, it is meant to be kept in ordinary homes so that, by following its guidance in raising children and administering medicines, one will make not a single mistake in ten thousand cases.

---

**"Shinsen Kibu Buirui Biki" (Newly Engraved Seasonal Classified Dictionary) — Small Format, Complete in 1 Volume**

This is an iroha-ordered dictionary, greatly expanded with characters commonly used in daily life, supplemented by characters used in haikai poetry, with seasonal topics (keibusutsu) written out month by month from the first to the twelfth month, organized by category. Everything, including how to study and receive transmission in haikai, is arranged in iroha order. It serves both as a great practical reference for daily life and as an excellent guide to haikai poetry.

---

**"Tsukiyama Sansui Den" (Treatise on Garden Design and Landscapes) — Complete in 2 Volumes**

This work records how to design a garden, how to arrange stepping stones, how to plant trees, and more. Even within a small plot of land, building a garden by following this book will accord with auspicious principles, bringing prosperity to the household and freedom from disaster.

The Tang Poetry Selection Picture Book began with quatrains around the Kansei era, extracting from here and there, with various volumes gradually being carved and published. At that time, there was no intention of continued publication, but fortunately it became widely popular in the world. Subsequently, not omitting any poems from the selection, we now aim to complete all volumes. However, as stated in the preface to this volume, during the approximately three hundred years of Tang dynasty, poet-scholars surpassed those of any other era.

Reply to the above
Your sincere letter has reached me, and I have gratefully read it. I am pleased to hear that your household is in increasingly good health. Now, regarding your kind inquiry about the sick person, I am grateful for your concern. Recently, their condition was

[Left page]
very poor, but a friend recommended a physician with whom he is well acquainted, saying that this doctor is extremely skilled. So we consulted him, and since then the patient has gradually begun to take meals again, and lately has recovered considerably.

【Right Page】
Four attendants in white robes accompany them.

Sacred Purification Spears: Three spears, two guards, advancing to the front as before
These three spears are called the sacred spears of the Three Imperial Regalia. The first spear is respectfully called the Sacred Sword, the next is the Sun Disc, and the next is called the Moon Disc spear. The sacred banner streamers are made of dragon-gate silk with one imperial crest attached. Others have various crests such as tomoe, myōga, or nine-star patterns, with five attached to each streamer. Some have only five imperial crests attached. The dye colors are five-colored and various other dye colors with imperial crests dyed in white. The handles are black-lacquered with scattered maki-e decoration. They are mounted on stands of the same lacquer and carried alternately by four people. A crimson large rope is attached to the top of the spear handle, which one person holds by pulling. All wear white robes, five people each for three spears, totaling fifteen people. The height of the sacred spears is approximately one jō two shaku. All black-lacquered with gilt metal fittings.

Festival Sacred Spears: Eight spears, five white-robed attendants each, totaling forty people
At the base of these eight sacred spears, autumn maple leaves or flowering plants are crafted in gilt.

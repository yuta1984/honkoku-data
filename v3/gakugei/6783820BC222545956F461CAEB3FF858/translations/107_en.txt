On-rusui (Imperial Caretaker): This is a position held concurrently by one temple from among the shuto. Regardless of rank in Buddhist duties or age, those suited for the role are selected and promoted from among the shuto.
　The monzhu (head priest), retainers, shrine families, court musicians, and all shrine attendants are under the command of the On-rusui.

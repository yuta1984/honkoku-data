On the Use of the Abacus (Soroban)

The way to use the abacus is to place the divisor (which means the standard) on the left and the dividend on the right. For example, when dividing 100 items among 16 people, the 16 people are the divisor and the 100 items are the dividend. Therefore, place 16 on the left and 100 on the right. For addition and subtraction (meaning addition), there is no distinction between dividend and divisor. Therefore, numbers may be placed anywhere as desired. Also, the number of columns on an abacus is commonly 21 columns.

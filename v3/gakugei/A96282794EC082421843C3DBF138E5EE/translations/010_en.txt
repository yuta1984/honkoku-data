Addition (Gathering calculation)
5 rin, 7 rin, 9 rin, 1 sen 1 rin, 1 sen 3 rin - what is the total of these silver amounts?　　Answer: 4 sen 5 rin

The method says: Place 5 rin as shown below [abacus diagram]. To add 7 rin to this, say "seven plus three makes ten" and raise two beads from below the five, remove the upper five, and place one in the left column [abacus diagram]. To add 9 rin to this, say "nine plus one makes ten," remove one from these 2 rin, and place one in the left column [abacus diagram]. To add 1 sen 1 rin to this, place one in the left position of two, and place one in the next 1 rin column [abacus diagram].

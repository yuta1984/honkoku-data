　　　Addition Calculation
Five rin, seven rin, nine rin, one sen one rin, one sen three rin. When these silver coins are added together, what is the total?　　　Answer: Four sen five rin

The method says: Place five rin as shown below【abacus diagram】. To add seven rin to this, say "seven plus three makes ten" and raise two beads from below the five, remove the upper five, and place one in the left column【abacus diagram】. To add nine rin to this, say "nine plus one makes ten" and take one from these two rin, placing one in the left column【abacus diagram】. To add one sen one rin to this, place one in the left position of two and place one in the next one rin column【abacus diagram】. To add one sen three rin to this, place one sen in the left position and place three rin in the next position of two

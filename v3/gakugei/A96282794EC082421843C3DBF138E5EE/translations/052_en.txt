○Next, when the price of three persimmons is 2 *sen*, and one asks the price of one persimmon:
　Set up as "㈠ three items　㈡ 2 *sen*　㈢ one item — how much?": place three at the first position as ㈠; place its paired number, 2 *sen*, as ㈡; and in ㈢, place one — which is paired with the quantity sought and is of the same kind as ㈠. Multiply ㈡ (2 *sen*) by ㈢ (one) to get 2 *sen*. Divide by ㈠ (three), and the price of one persimmon is obtained as 6 *rin* 6 *mō* 666… with a remainder. ○Further, if there are 21 persimmons for 14 *sen*, and one asks the price of one persimmon:
　Set up as "㈠ 21　㈡ 14 *sen*　㈢ one item — how much?": place 21 at the first position as ㈠; place its paired number, 14 *sen*, as ㈡; and in ㈢, place one — paired with the quantity sought and of the same kind as ㈠. Multiply ㈡ (14 *sen*) by ㈢ (one) to get 14 *sen*. Divide by ㈠ (21), and the price of one persimmon is obtained as 6 *rin* 6 *mō* 666… with a remainder.

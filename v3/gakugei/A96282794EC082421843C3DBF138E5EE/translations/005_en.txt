　　　Basic Numbers
　One, Two, Three, Four, Five, Six, Seven, Eight, Nine, Ten
　One, three, five, seven, nine are positive numbers (yōsū). These are called odd numbers. They are also called heavenly numbers. Two,
　four, six, eight, ten are negative numbers (insū). These are called even numbers. They are also called earthly numbers. When heavenly numbers are
　combined they make twenty-five; when earthly numbers are combined they make thirty. Also, when these are
　arranged together they become fifty-five. This is called the number of heaven and earth. There is an explanation in the I Ching.

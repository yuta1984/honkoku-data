　　　Basic Numbers
　One, Two, Three, Four, Five, Six, Seven, Eight, Nine, Ten

　One, three, five, seven, nine are yang numbers. These are called odd numbers, also called heavenly numbers. Two,
　four, six, eight, ten are yin numbers. These are called even numbers, also called earthly numbers. The heavenly numbers
　sum to twenty-five, the earthly numbers sum to thirty. When these are combined, they make fifty-five.
　This is called the number of heaven and earth. There is an explanation of this in the I Ching (Book of Changes).

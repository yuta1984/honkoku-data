　　　Basic Numbers
　One, Two, Three, Four, Five, Six, Seven, Eight, Nine, Ten
　One, three, five, seven, nine are yang numbers, called odd numbers. They are also called heaven numbers. Two,
　four, six, eight, ten are yin numbers, called even numbers. They are also called earth numbers. The heaven numbers
　sum to twenty-five, the earth numbers sum to thirty. Also, arranging these gives fifty-five.
　This is called the number of heaven and earth. There is an explanation in the I Ching.

Preface
Following my hand's inclination, I painted two or three
scenes from Tang poetry, adding two or three brushstrokes
to paper over the course of several days, creating
my own cooling remedy for the summer heat.
Unexpectedly, it fell into the hands of a bookseller.
The proprietor of the bookshop Kozanbo compiled five volumes

Preface
Following my impulse, I painted scenes from Tang poetry on two or three
sheets of paper, adding another two or three brushstrokes, and thus
spent several days enjoying this as a refreshing
pastime to escape the summer heat. Unexpectedly, these fell into
the hands of a bookstore, and the proprietor of Kōzanbō bookstore compiled them into five volumes

【Right page, upper section】
After the celebration ceremony is completed, the senior lady-in-waiting should guide [the bride] to appear before the father-in-law. From the bride to the father-in-law, mother-in-law, and other family members including brothers-in-law, there should be appropriate celebratory gifts of kosode robes, sake barrels, and delicacies. For the sake cups, first bring out the preliminary cup, then bring out the three cups, and set up the exchange ceremony for the three people: father-in-law, mother-in-law, and bride. The father-in-law drinks three times and offers it to the bride. When the bride drinks twice, the father-in-law drinks three times and offers it to the bride. When the bride drinks twice, there should be a ceremonial gift from the father-in-law.

【Left page, upper section】
The bride adds one drink and returns it to the father-in-law. The father-in-law drinks three times and concludes the ceremony. At this point, the meal is brought out. For common people, this would be ozoni (New Year soup). When the father-in-law and mother-in-law drink the first cup, pour a little sake from the serving pitcher into the sake flask.

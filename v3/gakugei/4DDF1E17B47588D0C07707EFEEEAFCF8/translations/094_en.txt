excessive or insufficient,
they are not proper etiquette. Especially
for weddings, even lowly men and lowly
women must not
omit appropriate ceremonies. Since this is where
the good and ill fortune of descendants
from ancestors to children and grandchildren is determined,
one should inquire and deliberate very carefully.

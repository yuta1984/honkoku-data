○For wedding bed decorations, in upper-class weddings the sitting room should also be newly built, and the tokonoma alcove should be a three-ken-wide alcove. If it is a two-ken alcove, there should be an additional attached alcove outside. There should be rules for decorative items such as double hanging fixtures. In addition, the husband's clothing should be hung, and night wear and futons should be in the storage room.

The water shelf and black shelf are originally called "fukuro-dana" (bag shelf), but in weddings the word "fukuro" (bag) is considered inauspicious, so they are called black shelves. There are places to put decorative items on these. Shell buckets should be decorated in the tokonoma alcove.

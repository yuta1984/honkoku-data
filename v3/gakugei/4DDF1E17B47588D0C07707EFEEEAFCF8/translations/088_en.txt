After offering congratulations,
the people on this side receive
the palanquin. If there is a shell bucket,
after the palanquin, an elder should
also handle its receiving and delivering.
○What the woman brings to her husband includes:
one kosode, one complete set of upper and lower garments,
one upper obi, one lower obi, one fan,
five sets of paper folders, one sword—
these seven types of items are
presented on a broad lid tray.
○The attire of a high-ranking lady consists of a white
kosode underneath, a white kosode with auspicious diamond patterns
on top, and a white silk lined robe

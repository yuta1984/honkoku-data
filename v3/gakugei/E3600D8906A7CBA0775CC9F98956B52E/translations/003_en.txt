　　　　　Calculations of measured goods (masumono)
Problem 1: When one koku of rice costs 3 yen 80 sen, how much rice can be obtained for 5,700 yen?
　　　　　Answer: 1,500 koku
　　Solution: ㈠3 yen 80 sen　㈡1 koku　㈢5,700 yen　Therefore,
　　divide ㈢5,700 yen by ㈠3 yen 80 sen to get 1,500,
　　then multiply by ㈡1 koku to find 1,500 koku.
　　The reason we don't multiply by ㈡1 koku is that it represents
　　the unit "1" as shown previously. Follow this method for other problems.

○Scales (uroko) are the scales of fish-dragons. Among those with scales, the dragon is their chief. Carp, both large and small, have thirty-six scales on their backs.

○Gills (sai) are the bones inside a fish's cheeks. Commonly called "era." Also called "osa."

○Swim bladder (hyō) is found in a fish's belly. Called "fue." It is the fish bladder (gyohō). Made into glue, it is called "nibe."

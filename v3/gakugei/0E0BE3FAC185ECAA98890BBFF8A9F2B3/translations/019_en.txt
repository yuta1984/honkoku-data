[Upper section]
When the body is pained by exposure to rain and dampness, it should be boiled in broth and eaten. Also called yōsuisoku (positive water foot), and also known as kaiban (sea plate).

○Kōna (parasitic worm) improves complexion and makes the spirit lustrous.

○Sea urchin (uni) - its toxicity is not yet clear.

○Sugai shell - for women in difficult childbirth, if held in the hand, the baby will be born. When put in vinegar, it moves.

○Firefly (hotaru) - decayed grass or rotten bamboo roots transform into fireflies. They transform by obtaining the great fire energy of summer, so they have light.

○Cricket (kirigirisu) - also called shissotsu or seiretsu. Resembles summer locusts but is larger. Appears at the end of summer.

○Mole cricket (kera) - lives in mud in the soil. Does it eat earth? Also called doku (earth dog) or sekiso (stone mouse).

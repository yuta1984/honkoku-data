[Right page, upper section]
○Carp (koi) has scales of uniform size from head to tail, all thirty-six scales. When boiled and eaten, it treats hiccups, rising qi (flushing), and jaundice, and cures thirst and edema.

○Sculpin (tofu) is also called ishimochi, nusubito, or fuguri-kurai. It supplements the five organs and harmonizes the spleen and stomach. Also, tobun refers to ikariio (a type of fish). Tohō, tofu, and tofu are the same.

[Left page, upper section]
○Dace (hasu) supplements deficiency fatigue. Its oil can be extracted and applied to burns with excellent results.

○Lamprey (yatsume-unagi) supplements the interior, increases blood, supplements deficiency, and treats postpartum lochia.

○Eel (unagi) kills parasites, treats sores, cures beriberi and dampness impediment between the lower back and kidneys, and assists yang qi.

○Yellow catfish (kigi) should not be eaten in large quantities. It damages the spleen and stomach and causes diarrhea. It is also called ōsō.

Called fish

○Loach (dojō) warms the interior, increases qi, sobers from alcohol, stops thirst, and treats hemorrhoids.

○Goldfish (kingyo) is born among algae. It is sweet in taste, neutral in nature, and non-toxic. It treats chronic diarrhea. There are also silverfish (gingyo), red carp (higoi), and red crucian carp (hibuna).

○Sweetfish (ayu) when boiled and eaten stops worry, warms the stomach, and stops cold diarrhea.

○Dace (ugui) has red eyes and is called trout (masu). It is also called red-eye fish (sekigangyō).

[Left page, upper section]
○Cutlassfish (tachiuo) assists fire qi, moves phlegm, triggers diseases, and causes sores to break out. It should not be eaten in large quantities.

○Flathead (kochi) - its efficacy and toxicity are not yet clear. It is also written as 鯒 (kochi).

○Pufferfish (fugu) supplements deficiency, removes dampness, regulates the lower back and legs, removes hemorrhoids, and kills parasites. This fish has great toxicity and must not be eaten.

○Young yellowtail (hamachi) - its efficacy is not yet clear.

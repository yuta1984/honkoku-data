○Garden peas (endō) promote urination, treat abdominal distension, treat wasting thirst, and treat vomiting and hiccups. Broad beans and snap peas are the same.

○Beans treat edema, disperse bad blood, strengthen the spleen and stomach, resolve alcohol-related illness, and remove heat from the stomach.

【Left Page】
The old pine trees in front of this shrine stand majestically, sweeping away the clouds, displaying the dignity of several hundred years. Master Hakuseki also praised these pines, writing in the "Shin'an Shukan" that he had never seen so many magnificent tall pines even in the provinces of Ōu, Bōsō, Tōsō, along the Tōkaidō route, in Kinai, or in Shōbi. Also, the main road in front of the shrine is the ancient Kamakura Highway, which local people now call the Shōyō Highway. The fact that there is something called Kamakura Bridge in Kami-Takaido is also said to be an old name remaining from when it was an ancient highway.

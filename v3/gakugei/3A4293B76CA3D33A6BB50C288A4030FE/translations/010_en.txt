Okubo Tenmangu Shrine

Since the shrine building faces west, it is called "Nishi-muki" (West-facing), and is also called "Natsume no Tenjin" (Jujube Tenjin), but the origin of the name "jujube" is unknown. The shrine grounds are extremely tranquil and have a profound atmosphere.

Ōkubo Tenmangu Shrine

Because the shrine building faces west, it is called "Nishimuki" (West-facing), and is also called "Natsume no Tenjin" (Jujube Tenjin), but the origin of the name "jujube" is unknown. The shrine grounds are extremely tranquil and atmospheric.

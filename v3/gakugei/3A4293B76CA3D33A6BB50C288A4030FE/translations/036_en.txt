【Right Page】
Koganei Bridge gets its name from being built over the open canal of the Tamagawa Josui waterway that flows adjacent to the land of Koganei Village. The cherry blossoms flanking both banks line up thousands of treetops, with falling petals dancing beautifully in profusion. When viewing from atop this bridge during blooming season, the scattered petals mix with clouds like snow, creating a vista that spans a thousand ri in a single glance, with no end visible before or behind. Therefore, many literati from the capital do not mind the distance and come here for pleasure excursions and flower viewing.
At the foot of the bridge there are two or three shops that warm sake and brew tea, where some visitors rest while others lodge for the night.

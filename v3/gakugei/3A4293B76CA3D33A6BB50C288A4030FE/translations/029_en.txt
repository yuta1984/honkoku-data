Horinouchi
　　Myōhō-ji Temple
The spiritual responses of Nichiren Daishi enshrined at this temple are always remarkable, so regardless of wind, rain, cold, or heat, people of all social classes from both urban and rural areas come here daily to worship, performing hundred-fold pilgrimages and other devotions without a moment's pause. Especially during the Hokke Thousand-Volume Assembly in the seventh month and the Eshiki ceremony in the tenth month, crowds gather as densely as rice and hemp plants, creating a bustle that is beyond description.

【Right Page】
Elephant Bones　Kyōhō 13th year, Boshin　From Cochin China, a man named Tei Taii brought two large elephants, male and female, born in Guangnan and presented them to Japan《According to Hayashi Nobuyuki's "Jibutsu Kenyo," it states they came from Taniko country. The female elephant died in Nagasaki on September 11th of the same year》They arrived in Nagasaki on June 13th of the same year《Landed on the 19th. Two elephant keepers named Tansō and Tanmen, and two interpreters Li Shakumei and Chen Ahō from Guangnan accompanied them》The following 14th year, Kiyu, they left Nagasaki on March 13th, arrived in Osaka on April 16th, entered Kyoto from Fushimi on the 26th, and on the 28th attended the imperial court and received imperial viewing《Since there was no precedent for entering the forbidden palace without court rank, even though it was an animal, it was granted the rank of Junior Fourth Rank and called "Guangnan Junior Fourth Rank White Elephant"》

On May 25th of the same year, it was welcomed to Edo, and on the 27th there was an imperial viewing in the military camp.
Afterwards, an elephant stable was built in Nakano to keep it, but after more than twenty years, it died around the Kan'en period《What remains at this temple are the bones of that male elephant》

Male Elephant《7 years old》　Entire body gray, head length 2 shaku 7 sun《The head cannot bow down or turn around》Trunk length about 4 shaku《Some say 3 shaku 3 sun》circumference 1 shaku 5 sun《At the tip it's about 6 sun. Two nostrils deeply recessed at the end that open and close well. Inside there are small flesh claws that can pick up needles and pinch mustard seeds. It drinks water and sips sake with its trunk. When eating, it also rolls food in with its trunk. All the body's strength is entirely in the trunk. When rising to walk, it first supports the ground with its trunk then moves its feet. The mouth is hidden under the jaw, far from the ground and usually not visible》Tusk length about 1 shaku 2 sun《Some say 1 shaku 4 sun. Circumference at the base about 1 shaku 6 sun》

【Left Page】
Eye length 3 sun《Some say 1 sun 5 bu. Shape like bamboo grass leaves》Ear width over 8 sun《Some say 1 shaku 3 sun. Shape resembles bat wings or ginkgo leaves》Body length 7 shaku 4 sun, circumference 1 jō. Back height 5 shaku《Some say 5 shaku 7 sun》Leg length 2 shaku 2 sun, circumference 1 shaku 5 sun《Some say 3 shaku 5 sun circumference 2 shaku 5 sun. Leg shape like round pillars with no fingers. Five claws resembling chestnuts. Though the whole body is plump and fat, it climbs steep peaks and descends winding paths like lightning, crosses deep water swiftly. Its nature readily becomes familiar with people and understands their intentions. Therefore elephant keepers straddle its head and control it with iron hooks for advancing, retreating, turning left and right》Tail length 3 shaku 3 sun《Some say 2 shaku 7-8 sun. Shape resembles cow's tail》

Female Elephant《5 years old》Entire body gray, head length 2 shaku 5 sun, trunk length 2 shaku 8 sun
Body length about 5 shaku, circumference 8 shaku 6 sun, back height 4 shaku 7 sun《Some say 4 shaku》Tusk length about 5 sun, the rest equal to the male elephant《This female elephant died while in Nagasaki. Only the male elephant came to Edo》

Feed《Daily: 200 kin fresh vegetables, 150 kin bamboo leaves, 100 kin green grass, 2 banana plants excluding roots, 8 shō Chinese rice of which about 4 shō is cooked into porridge and cooled before feeding. Hot water《about 2 to per time》50 manjū, 50 bitter oranges, 30 kunenbo citrus. Sometimes boiled and cooled soybeans are fed. Among green grasses, it especially favors what common people call "sumo wrestler's grass." When there's no green grass, it's fed unhulled rice with stems and ears, or straw and daikon radish. It also enjoys drinking sake》

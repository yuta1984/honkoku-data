Yodobashi Bridge spans between Naruko and Nakano. There are both a large bridge and a small bridge, and because water wheels rotate on this side of the bridge, there was an imperial command to name it Yodobashi after the Yodo River in Yamashiro Province. The waterway flowing under the large bridge is the Kanda Josui canal.

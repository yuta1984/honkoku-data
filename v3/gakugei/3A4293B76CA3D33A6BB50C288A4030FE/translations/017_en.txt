Yodobashi Bridge spans between Naruko and Nakano. There are both a large bridge and a small bridge, and because water wheels turn on this side of the bridge, it was named "Yodobashi" following an imperial command that it should be named after the Yodo River in Yamashiro Province. What flows beneath the large bridge is the canal of the Kanda water supply system.

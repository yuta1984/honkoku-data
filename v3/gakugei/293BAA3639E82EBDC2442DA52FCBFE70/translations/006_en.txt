They read many frivolous books such as tales and illustrated books that have been created, and descending to even more vulgar works, they favor joruri puppet theater and popular songs that are produced year after year. They forget the length of spring days and are unaware of how deeply winter nights grow late, spending their time in such diversions. Many people keep in mind all the volumes published each year, discussing and enjoying the superior or inferior qualities of their content. Some even fall into madness and sing these works - truly a deplorable custom to witness. Generally speaking, since these works record tales of lewdness, disorder, and sexual indulgence, it is natural that viewing them would disturb one's aspirations and corrupt the heart. These are not suitable materials for young girls to handle and enjoy. However, since Chinese texts cannot be easily read, one should instead read Japanese works such as

　　The Way of Three Obediences
For women, there is the Way of Three Obediences: when young, obey one's parents; once married, obey one's husband; and in old age, obey one's children. Parents are comparable to heaven and earth themselves. How could one ever forget, even momentarily, their great loving-kindness?

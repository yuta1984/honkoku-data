【Right Page　Upper Section】
○ Fei Changfang was a person from the Later Han dynasty. He learned and mastered immortal arts, riding on white cranes and flying through the air for pleasure. He was an immortal.

○ Qin Gao is said to have learned the arts of divine immortals and accomplished his training, riding on large carp and flying over water while reading books for entertainment. He was an immortal.

【Left Page　Upper Section】
○ Taigongwang is also called Shangfu. He was a hermit who enjoyed fishing by the Wei River. Later, when he was over eighty years old, King Wen of Zhou recognized his wisdom and made him his teacher. He also taught military strategy to King Wu, and finally destroyed King Zhou.

○ Shangli Jian is said to have mastered the art of using a sword as a vehicle to fly over the waves of the great sea.

【Right Page　Upper Section】
○ The Sixth Patriarch was the sixth patriarch in the lineage from Bodhidharma in China. His personal name was Huineng, and from him onward, the Zen sect divided into five schools. "Great Mirror Zen Master" is his posthumous title.

○ Great Master Dengyo is also called Saicho. He was the founder of Japanese Tendai Buddhism. He entered Tang China in the 21st year of Enryaku and entered nirvana at age 56 on June 4th.

○ En no Gyoja is also called En no Ozunu. He was from Yamato Province, entered Mount Katsuragi to practice the dharma of Peacock Wisdom King, and later went to Tang China with his mother in a bowl.

【Left Page　Upper Section】
○ Hanshan was a person from the early Tang period who lived as a hermit on Mount Tiantai and was always a dharma friend with Shide. Later, no one knew where he went. It is said he was an incarnation of Manjusri Bodhisattva.

○ Shide is so called because Zen Master Fenggan found and picked him up by the roadside. He constantly associated with Hanshan, and no one knows of his end.

○ The Giant Spirit is an immortal who obtained great strength and supernatural powers. He has the power to split mountains and always loves white tigers.

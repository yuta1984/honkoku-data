【Right Page　Upper Section】
○ The First Patriarch Bodhidharma had an audience with Emperor Wu of Liang, crossed the river, and entered Shaolin Temple in Wei. He is commonly called "Bodhidharma of the Reed Leaf" or "Bodhidharma of One Reed."

○ Fudo Myo-o (Acala) holds a sharp sword in his right hand and a rope in his left hand, which represents his form of admonishing the evil of sentient beings. The flames behind him represent his immovable nature and also manifest the appearance of ordinary people's anger.

【Left Page　Upper Section】
○ Nagarjuna Bodhisattva was born in South India, eight hundred years after Shakyamuni Buddha. He is the first patriarch of the Shingon sect. He propagated the Mahavairocana Sutra, Vajrasekhara Sutra, and Susiddhikara Sutra.

○ Master Shandao appeared from a waterfall in Chang'an, China. For over thirty years, he never slept at all, and passed away on March 14th in the second year of Yonglong of the Tang dynasty.

○ The Great Master Tiantai served as national teacher during the Chen and Sui dynasties and was the founding patriarch of the Tiantai sect in China. He entered nirvana at age sixty on November 24th. He is also called Great Master Zhizhe.

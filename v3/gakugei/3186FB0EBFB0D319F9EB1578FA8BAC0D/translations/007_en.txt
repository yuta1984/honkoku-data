【Right Page　Upper Section】
○ Also called Vimalakirti Layman. He held a hossu (fly whisk) in his hand, decorated the gate of his ten-foot square room with lion seats in eight directions, and admitted three thousand followers to teach them the Buddhist dharma.

○ The Amida of Yamagoshi: Amida Buddha manifested his sacred form on the peak of Yokawa at Mount Hiei, which the priest Eshin Sozu respectfully observed and depicted.

○ Prince Shotoku was the prince of Emperor Yomei, the 32nd human emperor. He served as regent during the reign of Empress Suiko, the 34th emperor. He is the founder of Buddhism in Japan. He destroyed Moriya and established Shitenno-ji Temple in Settsu Province.

【Left Page　Upper Section】
○ Shakyamuni Leaving the Mountain: The Tathagata renounced the world at age seventeen, and at age thirty, on December 8th, when the morning star appeared, he achieved great enlightenment and attained perfect awakening.

○ The Birth Buddha: Shakyamuni Tathagata was born on the eighth day of the fourth month at the hour of the Tiger, took seven steps, and pointing up and down with his left and right hands, declared "Above heaven and below heaven, I alone am honored." His parinirvana (death) was on February 15th.

There are some that rotate. Among these, rotating earthquakes are not strong but tend to cause much harm instead. Now, a single earthquake generally lasts no longer than one minute, but because they sometimes occur repeatedly, there are occasionally long ones. Due to earthquakes, fields have been buried, or sometimes entire countries and villages have completely fallen underground along with people and livestock. ◯ On Temperature/Heat
Temperature is the most essential thing that cannot be lacking in the world, and all things grow and develop because of temperature. If there were no temperature, all things would immediately change their form. For example, water maintains its form and flows due to appropriate temperature, but if temperature decreases, it becomes ice, and when temperature increases, it becomes steam and rises into the air.

[9] Arare Tofu　Squeeze out water well, cut into small dice, shake in a bamboo strainer
　　　to round the edges, then quickly deep-fry in oil. Season to taste.
　　　○Slightly larger pieces are called shōro tofu.

[10] Thunder Tofu　Heat sesame oil, grab and crumble tofu by hand and add it,
　　　immediately add soy sauce and adjust seasoning. ○Add chopped scallion whites,
　　　grated daikon radish, and grated wasabi. ○Ground sansho pepper is also
　　　good. ○Also called Nanking tofu. ○Or squeeze out water well and

　　　prepare as above - this is also called Ōbaku tofu or Kenporo tofu.
　　　The preparation differs slightly from the Ōbaku tofu appearing in [40].
　　　This is one theory. Also called Ingen tofu.
　　　▲Squeeze water from tofu, crumble well by hand, finely chop
　　　green vegetables in equal amount to the tofu, heat oil well, first add tofu
　　　and stir well, then add green vegetables and stir well again,
　　　season with soy sauce. For ten blocks of tofu, use about two gō of oil.
　　　This is called kudaki tofu (crumbled tofu).

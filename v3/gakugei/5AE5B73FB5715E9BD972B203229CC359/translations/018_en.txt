●[20] Rich Soy Sauce　Cut one block into quarters, place one piece per bowl, bonito flakes should be
　　　added at the end. Adding them from the beginning and cooking is not good. Just before serving,
　　　sprinkle ground sansho pepper and generously top with bonito flakes.

●[21] Fluffy Tofu　Mix chicken eggs and tofu in equal parts, grind well together,
　　　and cook until fluffy. Sprinkle with pepper powder. ○The fluffy texture and flavor
　　　are no different from eggs alone. Those practicing frugality should use this especially.

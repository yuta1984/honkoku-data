●[57] Mock Corbicula Clams　Simmer whole tofu over low heat without water.
　　　Scoop out the water that emerges with a metal ladle, and when more water appears, scoop it out again, repeating
　　　many times until it firms up and becomes crumbly like corbicula clam meat, then quickly deep-fry in oil.
　　　Season like corbicula clams by simmering in light soy sauce and top with fresh
　　　green sanshō pepper

●[58] Crystal Clear Tofu　Boil kanten (agar) completely, then cook tofu in that broth,
　　　let it cool and serve. Season according to taste

●[59] Vegetarian Sea Urchin Dengaku　Mix koji, fermented soybean paste, sweet cooking sake, and soy sauce in equal parts,
　　　add finely ground red pepper and store it. When it has aged well, it becomes excellent.
　　　Use this and prepare like the sea urchin dengaku in [recipe 43]

●[60] Cocoon Dengaku　Roll out freshly pounded mochi very thin like flower petals,
　　　lightly grill it, then wrap it around tofu dengaku that has been grilled with
　　　sanshō pepper miso sauce

●[61] Straw Raincoat Dengaku　Mix spicy seasonings into miso, prepare like regular dengaku,
　　　and place well-arranged, clean bonito flakes on top of the miso

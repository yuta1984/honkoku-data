●[16] Golden Sand Tofu　Squeeze out water well and grind thoroughly, add egg white as
　　　binder, spread on a board, sprinkle boiled egg yolk
　　　on top like sand grains, press down well and steam. Cut into small square pieces.

●[17] Splashed Udon Tofu　[100] Cut thicker and
　　　flatter than true udon tofu, squeeze out hot water with the same cooking method as udon tofu and serve,
　　　pour fresh simmered soy sauce directly over it, top with
　　　bonito flakes, grated daikon radish, roughly chopped white parts of scallions, and powdered red pepper. This is grass udon tofu.
　　　▲Cut like true udon tofu, place in a Nara tea bowl for chawan-
　　　mushi (steamed egg custard), serve with kudzu sauce and grated wasabi - this is called chirimen tofu

　　　(crepe tofu).

●[18] Layered Miso Tofu　Warm the tea bowl well, place warm
　　　wasabi miso at the bottom, add bonito flakes, and scoop
　　　soft tofu with good texture using a mesh ladle. ○Wasabi miso is shown
　　　under [82] tea tofu.

[19] Hiryōzu (Flying Dragon's Head)　Squeeze water from tofu and grind well, add kudzu powder as
　　　binder, for ingredients add julienned peeled burdock root, ginkgo nuts, wood ear mushrooms, sesame seeds, and
　　　for diced ingredients, add one item such as roasted chestnuts or arrowhead tubers. ○For ingredients

【Upper section】
　　The Two Principles
At the time of the creation of heaven and earth, what was light and pure rose up to become heaven, and what was heavy and turbid descended to become earth. Heaven is called yang and earth is called yin. Yin and yang are called the Two Principles.
○The Seven Governors refers to the sun, moon, and five planets combined. They are also called the Seven Luminaries. The sun, moon, and five planets govern the affairs of heaven.
Jupiter is called the Year Star, Mars is called the Confusing Fire, and Saturn is called the Stabilizing Star. Venus is called the Great White, and Mercury is called the Morning Star.

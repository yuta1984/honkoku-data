Ko-zakura (Small Cherry)

【Lower section】
○Ikansai said: "Ko-zakura is a variety of Yama-zakura. Its blossoms are of a pale, delicate color, and like the Yaé-higan cherry, they bloom in dense clusters. In the ornamentation of armor, there is a style of lacing known as 'Ko-zakura-odoshi' (small cherry blossom lacing); this flower takes its name from resemblance to that color."

【Left page】
【Upper section: Illustration】
Shiro-zakura (White Cherry)

【Lower section】
○Don'ei said: "Shiro-zakura resembles Yama-zakura but is of a pure, immaculate white. It is single-petaled, with broad and rounded petals. Both the stems and the leaves are green. There is a variety called 'Yuki-yama-zakura' (Snow Mountain Cherry) for the snow-like color of its blossoms, but Shiro-zakura surpasses even that in its whiteness. This is surely what is referred to as the 'Yoshino Yama-zakura.'"

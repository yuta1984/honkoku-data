Usuzumi-zakura (Pale Ink Cherry)

【Lower section】
○Don'ei said: "Usuzumi-zakura resembles Shiro-zakura (White Cherry), but its blossoms are small, single-petaled, and the petals are slender. The color is white, and both the stems and leaves have a bluish tint, like diluted ink. Furthermore, I have heard from a person of that province that there is one tree in the garden of Saihō-ji temple in Idai village, Wake district, Iyo Province;

【Left page】
the blossoms are single-petaled, deeper in color than pale ink, the petals are large, and the tips of the petals come to a point." This must be a different variety that shares the same name.

Higan cherry (Higan-zakura)

○Master Igansan (Matsuoka Gentatsu) says: This blooms earliest of all, opening during the vernal equinox period in the second month. This is likely the Sōshi-kaidō (broom-shaped begonia) recorded in begonia catalogs. The flowers cluster and grow at the tips of branches, resembling the branches and leaves of the kochia plant. The flowers do not open very flat and are single-petaled.

— [someone] had earnestly desired [the secret document], and so [the owner] said, "I will exchange it for whatever you wish." The other replied, "Then let us agree to exchange it for one cherished cherry tree." When the day finally came to send it, the owner of the tree lamented parting from it, and recalled the story of Wang Zhaojun (Ō Shōkun) being sent away to the land of the Hu (Kokoku). And so, on the spot, he gave the tree that name before sending it off — or so the story goes.

Nioi-zakura (Fragrant Cherry) [illustration]

【Lower section】
○Igan-sai states: It is a multi-petaled (senyo) variety, the color is red (crimson), and the fragrance is exceedingly rich and full. It is small-flowered, and the blossoms are sparsely attached. The variety that lacks this fragrance is the Hi-zakura (Scarlet Cherry), and the two are very closely resembling one another.

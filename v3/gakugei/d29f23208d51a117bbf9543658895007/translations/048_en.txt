【Upper section: Illustration】
Yuki-yama-zakura (Snow Mountain Cherry)

【Lower section】
○Don'ei said: "Yuki-yama-zakura is of medium size, and its white color is like that of snow. However, the shape of its blossoms is exactly the same as that of Uba-zakura; if one is not careful, it can easily be mistaken for Uba-zakura."

【Left page】
【Upper section: Illustration】
Ichimonji (Single-Line Cherry)

【Lower section】
○Ikansai said: "The blossoms are white. It belongs to the Yama-zakura group and bears large flowers. It is single-petaled, and the petals neither curl outward nor fold inward. When the flower opens, the petals lie perfectly flat. It is for this reason that it is called 'Ichimonji' (meaning 'a single horizontal stroke,' as in the character 一)."

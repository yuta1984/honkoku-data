【Right page】
It was named after the story of Kumagae Naozane and Hirayama Mushyadokoro taking the lead in the battle of Ichinotani. Another theory states that a certain person said
there is a thousand-petaled variety that resembles the thousand-petaled garden cherry. This is
not the true Kumagae cherry. Rather, this is what the flower garden calls
Yōkihi (Yang Guifei). It resembles scarlet cherry but is smaller. Also, calling single-petaled
large-flowered varieties with reddish coloring that resemble the variety called Shibayama
"Kumagae" is also incorrect.

【Left page】
This is what is called reverse-hand cherry.
○Elder Kassho said: "It is the earliest of cherry blossoms. It opens flowers in the second month,
blooming before equinox cherry. The flowers are small and double-petaled,
with a slightly red color."

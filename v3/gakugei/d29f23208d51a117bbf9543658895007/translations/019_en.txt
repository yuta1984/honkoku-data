There are many records of flower viewing banquets throughout the generations. Kiyosuke states
in his Ōgi-shō that Ki no Tsurayuki composed the poem:

　　Since there is no flower superior to cherry blossoms,
　　　all other plants and trees are as nothing.

This sentiment is indeed reasonable. The appreciation by people of all social ranks, both refined and common,
is concentrated on this (cherry blossom). Here,

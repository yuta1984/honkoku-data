【Right Page】
Fill to that level. Pay attention to the top and bottom of the ink stick
so that it doesn't bend when grinding
it smoothly, checking whether it's thin or thick—
examine and determine the proper shade of the ink.

【Left Page】
Then dip the brush, lift your elbow
up, and hold it firmly. Face
the copybook, and for each character,
observe whether it's large, small, long, or short.

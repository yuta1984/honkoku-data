【Upper right page】
"If you welcome a new mother, not only I but my two younger brothers would also become stepchildren, and all three of us would suffer and be cold. However, if you bear with and forgive the current mother, only I alone would be cold and suffer hardship, while my two brothers would be warm. No matter what happens to me, I would not suffer, but if you drive away my current mother, it is my two young brothers who would be pitiful." He admonished his father while weeping. Moved by his sincerity and filial devotion, even the stepmother was greatly touched and shed tears.

【Upper left page】
She reformed her previous evil heart into a truthful one, apologizing to Min Ziqian and also apologizing to the father. Although the father had thought his stepmother hateful, he was moved to tears of emotion by Min Ziqian's sincerity and forgave her, allowing her to stay. From then on, the stepmother respected Min Ziqian's virtue, harmonized with goodness, and came to love Ziqian more truly than her own biological children. Thus Ziqian continued to honor his parents and devoted himself even more to filial piety.

【Lower right page】
When one devotes oneself exclusively to artistic pursuits, naturally one's family business becomes neglected, and eventually this leads to losing one's house, destroying oneself, dishonoring one's ancestors' name, and cutting off one's descendants.

【Lower left page】
Since this becomes the foundation for such ruin, one should first put learning and practicing the arts aside, and with a merchant's temperament, single-mindedly focus well on one's own family business and occupation.

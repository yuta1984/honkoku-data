The Great Deity of Family Occupation and Profession

People's passage through this world is entirely due to the blessings of each person's respective Great Deity of Family Occupation and Profession. Therefore, one should gratefully and devotedly worship this deity with complete focus and utmost care. However, many people in the world neglect and treat this Great Deity of Family Occupation carelessly, and consequently,

Through their wandering attention, misconduct, and negligence, various evil heresies such as drinking, sexual indulgence, amusements, extravagance, stinginess, pursuit of fame and profit, and all manner of selfish misdeeds infiltrate, ultimately destroying and losing both body and household. This should be avoided with caution and fear.

Fundamentally, regarding human family occupations: it is the family occupation of one who is a master to teach and guide retainers toward goodness, and to love and show compassion for them. It is the family profession of parents to show deep affection to their children and to teach and raise them well. It is the family occupation of an elder brother to love and show kindness to his younger brother.

[Left Page]
It is the family profession of a husband to maintain proper behavior and teach his wife well. It is the family occupation of retainers to cherish their master and serve with loyalty. It is the family profession of children to sacrifice themselves, respect their parents, and give them peace of mind. It is the family occupation of a wife to obey her husband meekly, without any selfish or prejudiced thoughts, and to maintain chastity. It is the family profession of a younger brother to respect his elder brother as a parent with sincere heart and obey him well. It is the family occupation of friends to associate with mutual trust, not turn their hearts toward evil, and help each other harmoniously.

It is the family profession of samurai to strive in the way of the warrior, and of farmers to engage in the agriculture befitting farmers. It is the family occupation of craftsmen to devote themselves to their craft, and of merchants to work diligently in their trade without negligence.

If all four classes—samurai, farmers, artisans, and merchants—each devotedly worship this Great Deity of Family Occupation and Profession with complete focus and without distraction, then household harmony will be achieved, descendants will prosper gloriously, and they will pass through the world with lasting prosperity. Therefore, all people should wholeheartedly

give grateful reverence to this Great Deity of Family Occupation and Profession with single-minded devotion and utmost care.

【Upper right page】
○ Those who are children of this world should, like Yang Xiang, naturally be willing to sacrifice their lives for their parents. Yet, setting aside the matter of life itself, they begrudge their hands and feet and spare their bones, treating their own bodies as precious. However, if we consider whether they truly treat their own bodies as precious, do they not spare neither hands nor feet for worthless wine, lust, fame, and profit, acting carelessly and throwing away their very lives? What kind of error is this? You should think about this very carefully. If you cannot understand this error, you will not be able to establish yourself properly. Learn the Way from good people and put your parents' hearts at ease.

【Lower right page】
If you make bad friends your companions, you will unknowingly and naturally drift toward an evil heart, so you should exclusively choose good friends as your companions.

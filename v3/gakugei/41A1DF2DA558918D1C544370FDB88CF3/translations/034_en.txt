When Mencius entered school and returned home during breaks from his studies wanting to see his mother, his mother asked him, "Has your learning progressed?" Mencius replied, "It is the same as before." At that time, his mother was weaving by herself, and she cut the silk on the loom with a knife, saying, "Your returning to your mother's house now without advancing in your studies

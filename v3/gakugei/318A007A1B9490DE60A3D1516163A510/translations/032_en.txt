【Illustration】
Sending off Secretary Liu
By Li Duan

How many people entered together to serve Xie of Xuancheng,
But have not yet been able to repay his kindness.
Separated by death and life, only the night apes
Know the traveler's resentment.
On the stream path of Yiyang,
The third tone.

《Commentary: Like Xie Tiao, while you too held official position, many people served under you and received your favor. But once you left office, people abandoned you, failing to repay the kindness they received, and no one comes to visit as if separated by death. There is no one to tell this resentment to. People abandon you and no one comes, but only the apes know of this person's grievance and cry out. If one were to hear this, how sorrowful it would be.》

　The beam plaque inscription states:
　　Nii-no-ama was the daughter of Hōjō Shirō Tokimasa and
　　the wife of the Right General. She was the loving mother of both Yoriie and Sanetomo,
　　and died twenty-six years after General Yoritomo's death,
　　on the 13th day of the 7th month of Karyaku 1, year of the wood rooster. Her Buddhist name was Nyojitsu, and people called her the Nun
　　General. Since Idogaya was the nun's divided territory, during her lifetime she established a memorial hall
　　and called it Jōren-ji. However, it was repeatedly destroyed by military conflicts, and now the monk Shūyo, through
　　■ other power, had it rebuilt.
　　Respectfully constructing one Kamakura Nii-no-ama Memorial Hall, praying for national peace and the fulfillment

Yatsumi Bridge (Eight-View Bridge)
This is an alternate name for Ichikoku Bridge. When one looks around from atop this bridge, one can see:
　Tokiwa Bridge
　Zenikame Bridge
　Dōsan Bridge
　Gofuku Bridge
　Nihon Bridge
　Edo Bridge
　Kaji Bridge
All of these are visible.
Including Ichikoku Bridge itself, eight bridges can be seen, which is why it is called Yatsumi Bridge (Eight-View Bridge).
The illustrations of Nihon Bridge and Edo Bridge will appear next, so they are omitted here.

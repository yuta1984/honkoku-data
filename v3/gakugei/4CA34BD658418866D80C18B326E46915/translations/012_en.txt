[Upper section]
○The mastiff is a large dog.
Those that reach a height of four shaku
are called gō. Commonly these are
called Tang dogs (Chinese dogs).

○The dog has a salty taste, warm nature, and no
toxicity. It stabilizes the five organs, increases
qi, and is good for the kidneys.

○The nōken has long fur.
Like the bō, hō, and lion dogs,
it is a fluffy dog.

○The hedgehog is like the Asian badger.
Its legs are short and tail long.
Its color is bluish-white. The fur on its feet
stings people. It lives in mountain valleys
and fields. It is the same as the i (hedgehog).

○The civet lives in the mountain
valleys of the South Sea. Its shape
is like a raccoon dog. Its genital area
is like musk.

○The rabbit has short front
legs and nine holes in its rump.
It is pungent, balanced, and non-toxic. It supplements
the body's interior and increases qi.

○The monkey is of the same family as the yū (ape)
and resembles the kō with long arms.
It is good at grasping tree branches.

○The macaque has a shape resembling
humans. It has no spleen in its
abdomen, so it digests food
while walking. It often stands and
walks. Its nature is restless
and it harms things.

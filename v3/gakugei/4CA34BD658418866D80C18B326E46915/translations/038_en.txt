[Upper section]
○The Nogodori (throat-red bird) is about the size of a sparrow, with red coloring from throat to chest, making it a beautiful bird. It is rarely found.
○The Miyama-hōjiro (chestnut-eared bunting) is a small bird with beautiful wing colors.
○The Ki-suzume (yellow sparrow) resembles a sparrow but is yellow. There is also the Beni-suzume (red sparrow) which has red feathers, and the Nyūnai-suzume (rustic bunting) also exists.
○The Ran (phoenix) is a divine bird. Its shape resembles a chicken with a long tail, and its voice corresponds to the five musical notes. It dances when it sees a mirror.
○The Ao-sagi (grey heron) is larger than regular herons and is blue with a white belly. On rainy nights, its feathers glow blue, causing people to find it mysterious and fearsome.
○The Yoshi-hara-suzume (reed sparrow) is larger than sparrows and makes noisy calls. It dwells among reeds and is abundant near riverbanks and marshes.

Chinese hawks, Korean, Southern Barbarian,
Ryukyu, and in Japan too: Eastern provinces,
Western provinces, Northern provinces, Shikoku, Chugoku,
and Tsukushi - each of these regions
has its differences, it is said. A hawk's
feathers number twenty-four per wing,
forty-eight feathers total for both wings,
and twelve tail feathers.
Each has a name.
An eagle's tail has fourteen
feathers.

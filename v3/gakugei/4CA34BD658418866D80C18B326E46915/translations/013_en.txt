[Upper section]
○The otter lives in water.
All four legs are short. Its color is
blue-black. It catches and
eats fish. It treats water retention and
abdominal distension. One should not eat too much of it.

○The marten is of the mouse family,
large, and yellow-black
in color. Its fur is thick and
warm. It is used for hats and
collars to ward off cold. Commonly
written as "chestnut mouse."

○The flying squirrel is like a small fox,
its flesh wings resemble those of a bat.
Its legs are short and its tail is
about three shaku long. Its voice is like
a person calling, and it eats
fire and smoke. It goes from high
places to low places. It cannot
climb from low places
to high places.

○The kon is of the mouse family.
Its hide can be made into
fur clothing. Its other name is礼鼠 (courtesy mouse).

○The sea dog is the northern fur seal.
Its shape resembles a fox and
its tail is like a fish. Its body has
blue-white fur and also
has blue-black spots.
Its navel treats exhaustion of
the spleen and kidneys.

○The sea otter resembles the otter
and is about the size of a dog.

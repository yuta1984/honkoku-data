He said that Kotaka should watch the situation from there. With no other recourse, Kotaka reluctantly replied, "Then I shall obey your command. Kinchō, now free from all the clouds of worry weighing on his heart, rejoiced greatly, united his forces as one, and charged straight into the encampment where Rokuemon was stationed.

Now then, at Tsuda-ura Ana Kannon, with General Shōichi-i Rokuemon at the head, along with Kawashima Kurōzaemon, the same Sakuemon, Sanshū Yashima Dannoura Hachibe, Yakuemon, and besides these, with badgers and small tanuki as leaders, numerous clan members arranged their seating order according to court rank, and the council regarding the subjugation of Kinchō was varied in opinion. At that time, General Rokuemon said: "Although Kinchō is exceedingly brave, he is still young, and moreover, this spring for the first time he has against me..."

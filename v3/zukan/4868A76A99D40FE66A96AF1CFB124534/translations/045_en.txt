One variety
　White Peony Lotus (shiro-shakyaku-ren)

　Illustration from Lord Shirakawa's Lotus Catalog
　The flower is abundant with petals
　that are round. When in full bloom, it resembles
　a peony (shakyaku).
　Having buds at the base of the flower
　is also like peonies and tree peonies.
　It is white in color with
　a bluish tinge on the back of the petals.

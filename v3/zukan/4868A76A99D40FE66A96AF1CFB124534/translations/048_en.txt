One variety
　Tenjiku White Lotus (Tenjiku Hakuren)

　A lotus from Lord Shirakawa's collection depicted in the illustrated catalog. The flower shape resembles that of a peony, with both inner and outer petals being pointed. White in color with a slight yellowish tinge.

One variety
　White Peony Lotus (Haku Shakuyaku Ren)

　Same source as above. The flower shape resembles the previous variety, but the inner slender petals are larger than those of the previous entry. White in color, with the outer parts having a bluish tinge and the inner parts having a yellowish tinge.

One Variety
　　Double-headed Lotus (Sōtōren)

　　　　According to Shimura's illustrated book, this variety
　　　　grows in a pond at a private residence in Kamo,
　　　　beyond Sōka in Musashi Province. Both flowers and leaves are
　　　　small, and while the flower shape resembles
　　　　ordinary varieties, two flowers bloom side by side
　　　　on one stem. The color is pale pink.

One variety
　　Yamato Nishiki (Yamato Brocade)

　From the lotus catalog of Lord Shirakawa. The flowers are small, belonging to the tea bowl lotus category, with rounded petals of pale red color. The seeds are also small, with three to four seeds in each pod.

　　One variety　　Kunshi Small Lotus (Gentleman's Small Lotus)

　　　　　From the same catalog above. Both the flower shape and color resemble the previous entry.

One variety　　Tea Bowl Lotus (Chawan Ren)

　From the same catalog above. The flowers have thick petals, few in number, with pointed tips and pale red color. The seeds form pods of three to four. There is a green calyx at the base of the flower.

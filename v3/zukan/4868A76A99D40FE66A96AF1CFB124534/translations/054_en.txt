One variety

Tea Bowl Lotus (Chawan Ren)

　From Mr. Shimura's catalog. Both flowers and leaves are small, with the underside of leaves being pale purple. The petals are round and pale red in color.


One variety
　　Tsūshi Red Lotus (Tsūshi Kōren)

　From Lord Shirakawa's lotus catalog. Both flowers and leaves are small, with thin petals of pale red color that show a slight blue tinge at the base. Resembling peonies, it has blue-purple calyxes.

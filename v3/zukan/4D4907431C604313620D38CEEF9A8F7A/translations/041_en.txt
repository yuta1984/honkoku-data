Chijin-shō　Weeping Willow (Shidari-yanagi)

Yamato Honzō
　　Willow flower　Yang-liu　　　Yang-liu is the common name. Specifically, it refers to the weeping willow
　　　　　　　　　　Shi-zhen said "Yang-liu is weak and droops and flows, hence it is called liu"
　　　　　　　　　　This is indeed Yang-liu (Shidari-yanagi)


　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　9th day of late Kyōshō of Kinoe-Saru year
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Faithfully copied at U-kan-tei

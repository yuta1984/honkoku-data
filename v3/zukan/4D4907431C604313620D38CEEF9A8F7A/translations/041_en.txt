Yamato Honzō
　　　Willow flower　Yang-liu　　　Yang-liu is the common name. Particularly it refers to the weeping willow
　　　　　　　　　　　Shi-zhen said "Yang-liu is weak and hangs down flowing, therefore it is called liu"
　　　　　　　　　　　This is indeed the Yang-liu (Shidari-yanagi, weeping willow)

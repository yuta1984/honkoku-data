Yamato Honzo
　　　Willow flower　Yang-ryu　　　Yang-ryu is a common name. Particularly referring to weeping willow
　　　　　　　　　　　Shizhen said: Yang-ryu is weak and hangs down flowing, hence it is called ryu
　　　　　　　　　　　This is indeed yang-ryu (weeping willow)

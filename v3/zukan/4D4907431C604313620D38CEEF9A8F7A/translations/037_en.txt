According to Nanpō Sōmokujō, Tree section:
　　　Purple Wisteria
　　　　Purple wisteria has thin, long leaves, stems like bamboo, roots extremely firm and heavy
　　　　It has bark, white flowers and black seeds. Even when placed in wine for two or three thousand years
　　　　it does not decay. When its stem is cut and placed in soot,
　　　　it has purple fragrance and can be used to invoke spirits

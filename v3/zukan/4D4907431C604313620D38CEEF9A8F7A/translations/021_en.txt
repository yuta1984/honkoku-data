　Boboding (Dandelion) vegetable　(Also called) Yellow flower seedling

　Dandelion grass　In "Materia Medica" called dandelion grass. Japanese name is "fuchina," alternatively "tana"
　　　　　Alternative name "disgrace grass"　Pronounced "ekou" above and "nuzu" below

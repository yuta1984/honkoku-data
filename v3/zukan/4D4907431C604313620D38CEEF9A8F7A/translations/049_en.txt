## 【*Mitsuki* (美豆木; *Mizuki*) — Possibly a variety of *Tosa-mizuki* (*Corylopsis* sp.)】

### According to the *Wakan Sansai Zue* (和漢三才図会), section on Tall Trees (*Kyōmoku-rui*):

　　　*Mitsuki* (美豆木; "Water Tree")
　　　The correct character is unknown. Some use the character 椹 (*chin/jin*),
　　　but that too is merely a popular or informal usage.

Flower of the *Mizu-no-ki* (水ノ木; "Water Tree").

Sketched from life on the sixteenth day of *Kosen* (姑洗; the third lunar month), in the year *Kinoe-Saru* (甲申) [Bunsei 7, 1824].

[Seal: Trees (*Kimoku-rui*)]

## 【*Mumazeri* (馬蘄) — Possibly Watercress (*Nasturtium officinale*) or a related plant】

### According to the *Wakan Sansai Zue* (和漢三才図会), section on Soft and Smooth Herbs (*Jūkatsu-rui*):

　　　*Mumazeri* (馬蘄; "Horse Parsley")　[Alternate names:] *Gyūki* (牛蘄; "Ox Parsley"), *Kōkin* (胡芹; "Barbarian Parsley"),
　　　　　　*No-uikyō* (野茴香; "Wild Fennel") — [Note: There is separately a plant called *No-uikyō* (野茴香), and that plant is a different species from this one.]

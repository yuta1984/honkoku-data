## Broad Bean (Fava Bean)

蚕豆 (Soramame / Broad bean)
　　*From "Shokukyō" (Food Competition/Records):*
　　　This may be used [as food]
　　*From "Yamato Honzō" (Yamato Materia Medica):*
　　　Named so because its pods face upward toward the sky (空, sora)

　　空豆 (Soramame) — In the western provinces it is called "Tō-mame" (Chinese bean)
　　　Copied on a spring day of Kanoe-Tatsu (庚辰) year
　　　　　　[Seal: Grain category]
　　　　　　[Seal: Medicinal herb]

*From "Zōho Tashiki" (Expanded Polyglot Encyclopedia), section on legumes:*

　　　　　　　Yukiwari-mame (Snow-splitting bean)

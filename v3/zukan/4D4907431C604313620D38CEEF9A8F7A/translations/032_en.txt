【Garden Pea】

Wandou (豌豆, garden pea)
　　According to Shokkyō:
　　　　Nomame (field bean)
　　　　Hiramame (flat bean)
　　　　Entōmame (pea) — there is also a variety with white flowers

Lower third of the third month (Koshi/姑洗) of the year Mizunoto-Hitsuji (癸未, 1823), copied and distributed in Katsushika
[Seal] Grain/Legume Category (穀類)

According to Wamyō Ruijushō:
　　Noramame (野豆)　— The Honzōso states: "豌豆, alternate name 野豆"; Japanese name: Noramame (乃良末女)

According to the Zōho Tashikiben, Legumes Section (菽豆類):
　　Endō (豌豆, garden pea)
　　　Japanese name: Noramame (乃羅末米)
　　　Alternate names: Kozu/Hudu (胡豆) [from Shiyi/拾遺]; Jūshuku (戎菽) [from Erya/爾雅]
　　　Additional names (from the Zōho edition):
　　　　Kaikotsu-tō (回鶻豆) [from Liaozhi; the Yinshan Zhengyao writes it as 回回豆]
　　　　Hitsutō (畢豆) [from Tang History; Cui Shi's Yueling writes it as 蹕豆]　Aokodzu (青小豆) [from Qianjin/千金]
　　　　Seihantō (青斑豆) [from Bielu/別録]　Marui (麻累)

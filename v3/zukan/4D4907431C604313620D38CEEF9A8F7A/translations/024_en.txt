Gold Coin Flower, Gold Cover Flower
　　　　Eternal Spring Chrysanthemum (as stated in Sensuji-fumoto)
　　　There are different varieties
　　Copied on a mid-spring day of Mizunoe-Uma year

According to Yamato Honzō:
　　　This plant is the gold coin flower. It is most
　　　abundant in spring and continues through the four seasons, therefore it is also
　　　　　　called the eternal spring flower

Jūun's opinion:
What the world calls begonia flowers are round
flowers with light red color, resembling
crab apples, these are called begonia.
Their stems are thick and flowers large, with deep and light
red colors. Those with red stems that hang down long
are called drooping-silk begonia, considered the finest
type of begonia. There is one variety called peony begonia
which is domestically called small crab apple.
Its flowers are light red-white and it bears fruit
very abundantly. There is also a variety called
Western Prefecture begonia. Modern begonias generally
do not bear much fruit.

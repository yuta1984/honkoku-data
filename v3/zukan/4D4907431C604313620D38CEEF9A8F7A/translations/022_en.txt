According to Toyoda Yokei's Shabenjo-roku:　Abotsusam　Bebegaya, Inukaya
　　　　　　　　According to Wang's Detailed Annotations on Classified Garden: Abotsusam grows in Furin country, over one zhang tall, bark blue-white, leaves thin growing opposite in pairs resembling turnips, yellow seeds resembling black pepper in red color. When its branches are ground, the juice is like oil. Applied to scabies and ringworm, none fail to heal. This oil is extremely precious, worth a thousand gold pieces. Also appears in Curved Records Handbook. This is the Bebegaya tree of Goshu. Bebegaya, also called Dog-kaya, grows everywhere in the mountains. Mountain people extract oil for use by surgeons.

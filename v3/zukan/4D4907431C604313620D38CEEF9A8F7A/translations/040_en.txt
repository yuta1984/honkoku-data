Yamato Honzō
　　Grass quince　(Shidomi)

　　　　　　　　　Same record
　　　　　　　　　　The grass quince grows one to two feet tall and is abundant in fields. Its flowers are red and the plant has thorns.
　　　　　　　　　　The fruit is small. In Musashino there is a grass quince called Shidomi, whose fruit
　　　　　　　　　　is about the size of Higo strawberries. The local people use its vinegar. The fruit of the 
　　　　　　　　　　Chinese quince is also said to be very small. This corresponds well to the plant in question.
　　　　　　　　　　Two other varieties are recorded but I have not yet seen them. These are included in the quince section of this book.



　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Written on the 7th day of early Kōsen of the same year

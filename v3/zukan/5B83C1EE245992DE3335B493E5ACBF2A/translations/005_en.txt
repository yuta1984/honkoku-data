【Right Page】
　　　I have investigated many cases through experience. Therefore, I have detailed the names and characteristics of these plants in specific records, compiled them into a single volume, and present this work to those who lack knowledge in this area.
1. Regarding the presence or absence of toxicity and its severity, there are many cases where various theories do not agree. Since it is impossible to debate and determine their validity, I generally rely on herbal medicine texts and the theories of predecessors to present these cases.
1. Generally speaking, among fruits and vegetables, even those items used for daily meals, many have biased properties that are not beneficial to humans. However, they should not be confused with poisonous plants and trees. Therefore, I have collected such items and made them a separate appendix.

【Left Page】
1. Items such as nux vomica (banmokubetsu) and croton seeds (hazu) that are not native to Japan are omitted. Rare items and exotic materials are also not included. Even among common plants, it is difficult to avoid omissions. I can only hope for knowledgeable people to supplement this work.
1. For each item, I record the source below the Chinese name. For those whose Chinese names are not yet clear, I use Japanese characters to identify them. Although I record Japanese names alongside Chinese names, there are so many regional dialects that there is no time to list them all. Readers may find some unfamiliar to their ears. For what cannot be fully covered in words, one should refer to the illustrations for understanding.

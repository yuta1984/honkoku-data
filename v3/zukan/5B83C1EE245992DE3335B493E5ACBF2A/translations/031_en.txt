Rōga (Wolf Fang) - Compendium of Materia Medica
It has toxicity. It grows in deep mountains. Height is one to two shaku. The leaves are three per stem, resembling snake strawberry leaves with hair growing on them. In summer it opens flowers. Five petals, yellow in color, resembling snake strawberry flowers but smaller.

Shiji (Cocklebur) [Onamomi] - Compendium of Materia Medica
It has minor toxicity. In spring it grows here and there in thickets, and the shoots wither after autumn. Height reaches four to five shaku. In autumn it opens flowers and produces fruit. Both ends are pointed with small thorns that stick to clothing. If mistakenly eaten together with rice washing water, it becomes greatly toxic.

Rōga (Wolf's Fang) 《Compendium of Materia Medica》
It has toxicity. It grows in deep mountains. Height is one to two shaku. The leaves are three per stem, resembling snake strawberry leaves but with hair. In summer it opens flowers. Five petals, yellow color, resembling snake strawberry flowers but smaller.

Shiji (Cocklebur) 《Onamomi》《Compendium of Materia Medica》
It has mild toxicity. In spring it grows here and there in thickets, and the shoots wither after autumn. Height reaches four to five shaku. In autumn it opens flowers and produces fruit. Both ends are pointed with small thorns that stick to clothing. If mistakenly eaten together with rice washing water, it becomes highly toxic.

Kiren (Adhesive Weed) 《Menamomi》《Compendium of Materia Medica》
It has mild toxicity. It grows spontaneously in various places from seeds. The branches and leaves are opposite with serrated edges and light green color. It has hair. In autumn it divides branches and opens small yellow flowers. The calyx is divided into five parts and is sticky and viscous, easily adhering to hands. There are small hooks on top of the calyx. It produces fruit, and when it ripens, both the shoots and roots wither together.

Sanhakusō (Three White Grass) 《Hangeshō》《Compendium of Materia Medica》
It has mild toxicity. It is an aquatic plant but also grows on land. In spring it extends its stem. The leaves are alternate and have an offensive odor. When the hangeshō season arrives, the three leaves at the tip of the stem turn white. At the top it opens small white flowers forming spikes.

Liyu (mountain thistle | Euphorbia species) "Bencao Gangmu"
　　　Has mild toxicity. Grows from perennial roots in winter. Purple-red in color, reaching over one shaku in length by spring. Leaves are alternate
　　　and both stem and leaves exude white sap. At the stem tip five leaves grow, dividing into five branches
　　　at whose ends two leaves grow opposite each other, opening four-petaled flowers and bearing fruit.
　　　Its roots exude yellow sap.

【Left Page】
Cao Liyu (marsh spurge | Euphorbia species) "Bencao Gangmu Liyu Jijie"
　　　Has toxicity. Grows beside rivers and lakeshores. Sprouts early in spring. Light red in color, growing
　　　to reach two to three shaku in height. Leaves resemble those of Liyu (mountain thistle), light green in color, turning yellow at the tips. Five leaves grow
　　　opposite each other, dividing into five branches, with three leaves opposing at the ends, opening five-petaled light yellow flowers. Its roots exude white sap.

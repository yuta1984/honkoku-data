Stinging Nettle (Jinma | Irakusa) "Bencao Gangmu"
　　Has great toxicity. Grows in shaded areas of mountains. Height is two to three shaku. Leaves resemble ramie, are deep green in color, and
　　grow opposite each other. Both stem and leaves have stinging hairs. Flowers grow between the leaves. Resembles ramie.
　　When its stings pierce people, they cause pain and swelling. If fish eat this plant juice,
　　they die immediately.

Stinging Nettle (Jinma | Irakusa) "Bencao Gangmu"
　　Has great toxicity. Grows in shaded areas of mountains. Height is two to three shaku. Leaves resemble ramie, are deep green in color, and
　　grow opposite each other. Both stem and leaves have stinging hairs. Flowers grow between the leaves. Resembles ramie.
　　When its stings pierce people, they cause pain and swelling. If fish eat this plant juice,
　　they die immediately.

Morning Glory Seeds (Kengiushi | Asagao no Mi) "Bencao Gangmu"
　　Has toxicity. Seeds are sown in spring. Leaves are three-pointed and light green. Flowers bloom from summer to autumn. Flower
　　petals are five-pointed and blue-green, tubes are white. Bears fruit after flowering. Has three vertical ridges with seeds inside. White ones
　　are called white morning glory, black ones are called black morning glory. Insects do not develop on these leaves.
□□□□□□ used as lamp oil, various insects will not approach. Also pressing cotton seeds
□□□□□□ is made. Cotton seeds are toxic.

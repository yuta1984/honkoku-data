Jiyi (Kyūi | Futari-shizuka) "Bencao Gangmu"
　　Has toxicity. Grows in shady places in mountains. In spring it produces seedlings about one shaku tall. The stem has nodes.
　　At the stem tip, two leaves grow opposite each other, with four leaves alternating. At the top it produces two stems and opens white flowers.
　　Sometimes there are white flowers like threads. This is called mayuhakesō. It is one variety of this plant.

【Left Page】
Ziwei (Shii | Nōzen-kazura) "Bencao Gangmu"
　　Has toxicity. It is a vine with aerial roots that grows by leaning on trees. In spring it produces leaves with deep serrations.
　　In summer it opens flowers. They form tubes with five-lobed petals that are round and pointed at the tips. They are reddish-yellow in color.
　　After flowering, it produces pods. If dew from this flower enters the eyes, one immediately becomes ill. Also, pregnant women should avoid its flower fragrance.

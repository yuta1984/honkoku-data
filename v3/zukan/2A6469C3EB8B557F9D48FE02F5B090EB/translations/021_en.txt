　　　Appendix: Soil Preparation Method
　Mix three parts sand with reddish, coarse soil suitable for potted plants,
　and store it in a covered area where rainwater cannot reach from Beginning of Winter (Rittō) 
　until the Spring Equinox (Shunbun). After it dries, turn it over by hand
　before use. Alternatively, compost and red clay may be used similarly.

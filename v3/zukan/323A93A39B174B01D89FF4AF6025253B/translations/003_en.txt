Preface to Baihin

Among the ancients, there were many who wrote about plum blossoms, but when it comes to the classification and differentiation of varieties, none surpassed the *Meipu* (Plum Register) of Master Fan. For this reason, later generations have all relied upon it as their authority. However, it contained only some ten-odd varieties. Our Master Joan's *Baihin* records more than sixty varieties, and one may truly say that it is comprehensive. Indeed——

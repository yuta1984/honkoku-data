**Earth Honey Bee (土蜜蜂 / Do-mitsubachi)** *(Source: Senchūfu)*

The *Senchūfu* states: "The earth bee (*dobachi*) is found in Fukuwadato Village, Kamisio-bara, Shioya District, Yashū (present-day Tochigi Prefecture), where there is a hot spring. It is a creature that inhabits that area."

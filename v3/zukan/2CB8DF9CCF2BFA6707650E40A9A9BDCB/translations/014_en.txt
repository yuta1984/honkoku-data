○ Sagisō (White Egret Flower)
The flower shape is
exactly like that bird
　in flight, and the color is also
　　　　　as shown on the left
　　The plant height is about six to seven sun
It is an aquatic plant
　　It blooms during the Dog Days of June
　　　One plant produces
　　　　　　one flower
　or sometimes
　　two to three flowers
　　　bloom, so it is
　　　also called
　　　　"companion
　　　　　　blooming"

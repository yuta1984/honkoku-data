Preface to the Complete Collection of Grass and Flower Illustrations
Heaven and earth are in harmony, causing plants and trees to sprout; the seasons obtain proper cold and warmth, causing flowers to bloom and bear fruit. Indeed, the forms of flowers are beautiful and elegant, the stance of grasses is pure and clean, and among them appear those with fragrant scents. As countless varieties of grasses and flowers, they are cultivated and admired in the world. I plant and grow them with surplus energy from farming, and let them flourish naturally during breaks from agricultural work. My father constantly observed the emergence of unusual varieties, contemplated alone, nodded to himself in understanding, and took up his brush to draft manuscripts.

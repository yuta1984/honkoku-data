○ Futaba Asagao (Two-leaf Morning Glory)

The flower shape is the same as ordinary morning glory
　　　The color is also lapis blue
　　After germinating, flowers bloom
　　　　　　　　from the two-leaf stage

Also called
Chabo Asagao
(Bantam Morning Glory)

　　　Blooms from
　　　　　March and April

【Left Page】
○ Shiro Hirugao (White Bindweed)
The flowers have vines
and are distinctly white
Flowers that open suddenly in the morning
　　remain all day long
　　　　then wither
The next buds
　　　open gradually
It is a plant with a long blooming period
　　Blooms from June
Light-colored flowers
　are common in fields
　　White ones have become
　　　　　　rare in recent years

○ Himawari (Sunflower) 《Alternative names: ○ One name is Himawari (Sun-turner) ｜○ One name is Nichirinso (Sun-wheel grass)》

Flowers are yellow and large
Plant height reaches about eight to nine shaku, or about ten shaku. No matter where
it is planted, the flower opens facing east　Blooms in August and September

【Left Page】
○ Setteika (Daylily)
Flower form is like
　　that of lily grass
　　and yellow in color
Flowers that open in the morning
wilt in the evening, then
　　　　the next flowers
gradually
　　open
Flowers bloom in all four seasons
○ Also called
　　　　Senteika (Dyed garden flower)

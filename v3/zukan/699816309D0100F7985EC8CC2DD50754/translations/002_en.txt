Haikai Mechiori (A Collection of Names for Haikai Poetry)

Renga poetry forms verses using elegant court language (gago), while haikai renga creates verses using colloquial language. The distinction between elegant and vulgar reflects the difference between literary refinement and plainness, which also corresponds to the difference between nouns and descriptive words. Although there have been many books throughout history that collect seasonal terms used in composing verses, [this work addresses] cities, countryside, mountains, and seas...

Regarding the explanations of the rare insects in the Bencao Gangmu (Compendium of Materia Medica), even the ancients could not speak with certainty about them, but now for the first time they have been explained in such detail - this is truly a grateful matter for those who favor this field of study. Now, since you command Hiroken to add a word, and since my learning from your teachings regarding the compilation of my "Survey of Ancient and Modern Essentials" is not shallow, I wish to repay your years of kindness. Previously, I wrote studies on pine crickets and bell crickets and presented them to you. This year again, I add these humble words to the margins of this volume. The fifth day of early fourth month of Bunsei 2nd year, written by the old man of sixty-two years, Minamoto Hiroken.

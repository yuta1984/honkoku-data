Sweet Chrysanthemum (kankiku)　Culinary Chrysanthemum

　A type of autumn chrysanthemum, 3-4 shaku tall (approximately 90-120 cm), flowering in September. The petals are tubular and yellow in color, with numerous petals and no visible center. Each individual petal has
　a tubular base and splits into five parts at the tip. The taste is sweet. Good for both medicinal and culinary use. When preserved in salt, the color and flavor
　do not change for a long time, hence this variety is called "longevity chrysanthemum" (jumei-kiku).

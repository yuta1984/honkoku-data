One variety

Nankinbō (Nanjing squash)
Tōnasu (Chinese eggplant) [Edo name] / Ban-gua [from Qunfang Pu]
The seedlings and leaves have the same form as the previous entry. The fruit is shaped like a pot, broad at the bottom and narrow at the top.
There are also round and flattened varieties called kikuza (chrysanthemum seat). It has ridges running vertically with indentations and protrusions.
The skin color is initially green, turning yellow when ripe. The flesh is thick with a sweet, rich, and delicious taste.
In this category there is also akodauri (bullet gourd). It has the same shape but is smaller.

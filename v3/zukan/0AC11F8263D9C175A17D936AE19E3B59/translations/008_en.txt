【Right page】
Blue Ganoderma (Seishi)

　A type related to purple ganoderma
　that lacks reddish tinge
　and has a blue color

【Left center】
Yellow Ganoderma (Ōshi)

　Those without reddish tinge and of pure yellow
　color. Rare, and those with
　reddish tinge are common

【Left page】
White Ganoderma (Hakushi)

　This variety is a rare species.
　In Edo, the Kawashiri family
　has specimens that are
　treasured in their collection.
　The form is the same as the
　previous entry, and its color
　is pure white.

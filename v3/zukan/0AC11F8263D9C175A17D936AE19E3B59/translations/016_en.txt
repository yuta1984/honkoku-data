Honey locust mushroom (sōkyōshin) - Saikachimotase 《Ōshū》
　　Saikachitake　Masutake 《Akita, Ushū》

　No illustration is provided for this item. The Kōwa Shinfu states: "Its shape resembles tōshin (hatsutake), but the color is white, and the gills are even more brilliantly white. Large specimens have a diameter of about three sun. Some say the taste is not good."

【Right Page】
Persimmon shimeji

An illustration from the Shin'yō Kinpu (Shin'yō Mushroom Catalog). According to the same book, the form is small, the cap is reddish-purple, and the stem is entirely white. There is a similar type with similar form and color but with a slimy, slippery texture that is poisonous - eating it causes diarrhea.

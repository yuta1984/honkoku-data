Written by Master Shōtei Kinsui
《Shōtei Manpitsu (Casual Writings of Shōtei) - Complete 2 Volumes》
This book distinguishes the historical records of Japan and China, ancient and modern, or clarifies the delusions of common customs. It also collects and records similar matters from people's conduct, whether preceding or following, gathering this and that to serve as a convenience for later learners. Beyond that, it adds casual praise and criticism to whatever comes within the scope of observation and hearing, expressing humble opinions. Therefore, it bears the name "casual writings" and serves as enlightening material for women and children.
【Vertical ruled lines】

《Shika Zetsumyō (Poetic Excellence) - Pocket Edition, Complete 2 Volumes, Appendix 1 Volume》
This book divides sections by the four seasons, attaching familiar phrases, rhyme characters, historical anecdotes, excellent verses, or occasionally compositional examples. Therefore, regardless of which type of poetry, anything belonging to the four seasons can be opened to any volume and viewed at a glance to immediately obtain excellent compositions. Since it is especially for portable convenience, not only is it made with thin paper binding, but even the half-sheet format is made similarly thin, making it an indescribably convenient pocket book.

【Left page】
《Bokumin Shinkan Kai (Commentary on Heart Mirror for Governing People) - Complete 2 Volumes, Compiled by Ming Dynasty's Zhu Fengjie, Commentary by Hiratsuka Hyōsai》
This book uses the teachings of sages as a mirror and records in detail the path of cultivating oneself and governing people. Therefore, it is employed by rulers, regional lords, and governors above, and village headmen and town leaders - all those who stand above others and guide and teach the masses - seek it out and always keep it at their right hand, reading it without fail morning and evening as an aid to increasingly clarify virtue. It is a precious book.
【Vertical ruled lines】

《Zoku Renshū Shikaku (Continued String of Pearls: Poetic Forms) - Small Format Complete 2 Volumes, However Thin Paper Print Cover Complete 1 Volume》
That poetry has forms is like ascending a hall having steps - this serves as the title of this book. The previous edition has already been widely distributed. However, since there were still omissions, Master Tōjō, with his strong memory and extensive reading, made efforts to search through the collected works of various great masters from ancient times and created this edition as well. Therefore, excellent words and wonderful verses are considered complete here. It is like stringing pearls and gems together to supplement the insufficiency of beautiful steps.

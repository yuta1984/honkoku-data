Preface to the Cherry Genealogy

In general, the most revered things are known without needing to be named. This applies not only to emperors, princes, and nobles, but also to trees and flowers. The peonies of Luoyang, the crabapples of Western Shu, and the cherry blossoms of Japan are such examples. Peonies and crabapples each have their genealogies.

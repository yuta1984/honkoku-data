Late Cherry (Oso-zakura)
[Illustration]
○ Igen-sai says: This is a double cherry from Hanakiri Valley that is somewhat small.
The color is slightly red, and regardless of
temperature differences, it blooms later than other flowers.
In poetry it is paired with
April's fresh greenery and expressed as
"late cherry hidden among green leaves" and such.

Nara Cherry (Nara-zakura)
[Illustration]
○ Igan-sai says: "This is a double cherry blossom.
The flowers are small in circumference and extremely graceful,
with a red color. The stems are long and slender.
People of refined taste gave it this name
based on the poem by Ise no Ohosuke."
○ Donnei says: "In Kiyosuke's
Fukuro-zōshi, when Jōtōmon-in was the Empress Consort,

Ise no Ohosuke first entered court service. When double
cherry blossoms were presented—someone offered those flowers before Ise
and placed fine paper and an inkstone before her.
At that moment, everyone watched intently, wondering 'How will she compose?'

① Ginkgo Shell (銀杏介)　　[Gunbun Hinkai]

○ The Gunbun Hinkai states: Among the clam family, its shape resembles the Chiyo-no-hana Shell (千代ノ花介). The surface is white with horizontal grain patterns. The shell is thin and has a lustrous sheen.

○ Sekiju states: Its shape resembles the Shobu Shell (菖蒲介; Iris Shell). The color is water-white (a pale, translucent white) or snow-white, with fine, closely-spaced horizontal striations. The outer surface is smooth and lustrous. The inner surface (where the flesh adheres) lacks luster. The remainder of the shell is lustrous and white in color. The size does not exceed seven or eight bu [approximately 2.1–2.4 cm], and the valves fit together in a interlocking bite (牙合).

※ "○" is written in red (vermilion). Each number is encircled with ○.

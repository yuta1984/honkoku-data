The plant recorded in the Wuyinmang has leaves like those of poppies, with small four-petaled flowers in several varieties of yellow, white, and red colors. Later it forms pods that are long, measuring four to five sun (12-15cm). They resemble the pods of rape blossoms (na no hana). Inside are fine seeds.

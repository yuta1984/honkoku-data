Grass Plover

　In size it resembles the plover but is somewhat smaller.
　The bill is glossy, somewhat long, and pale black. The feet are
　green. There is one stripe on the head and one stripe through the eye that are pale
　black. From above the eye to the back it is green with black
　spots. The tail is the same color. The throat and belly are white, and the
　central part of the wings is gray with white edges on the feathers.

Medahi-chidori (Large-eyed Plover)
　Medai-mushikui　Mebori

　A member of the plover family, with black
　eyes that are large, surrounded by white. The bill resembles a dove's and is black, the feet have
　chestnut-colored edges. Three toes. From head to back it is pale bluish-
　gray. The base of the bill and forehead are white, from eye to tail it is pale gray.
　The cheeks and throat are pale red, the belly is white, the wing tips are black, and the tail is
　also black.

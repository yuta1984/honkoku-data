Red Dragonfly　　　　　　　　　Shuraku Kankō
Though one should endure in silence rather than cry out, the red dragonfly
grows thin and gaunt from a man's yearning love

Grasshopper　　　　　　　　　　Asahata Sugimaru
When I pull back the grass sleeves wet only with dew, the grasshopper
says "no" (ina) and flies away - how melancholy!

　　　　　　　Kusa

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　15th day of Koshō, year of Hinoe-inu
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　True copy from garden

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　  　　　　　Grass category

There are many in the mountains of Kamo in Yamashiro Province. In Kamo shrine rituals, hollyhock is attached to katsura branches and hung on blinds and vessels. This is called the Aoi Festival (Hollyhock Festival). It is performed annually on the day of the Rooster in the fourth month, with hollyhock offered from Kitayama Nakamura.

Stone Self-heal (Iwa-utsubo)

Thousand-petaled Adonis (Double-flowered Adonis)

In the Chijin-shō,
the characters for "yaë" (double-flowered) are used,
but in the Honzō it is written as "thousand-petaled."
What is called "heavy leaves"
is indeed the same as double-flowered.

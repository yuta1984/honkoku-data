"Wakan Sōmoku Washū" (Collection of Japanese and Chinese Plants) states:
　Garden Cherry (Niwazakura) - The Chinese name for double-flowered varieties is "Kibai" (Joy Plum), and white petals are called "Gyokuchōka" (Jade Butterfly Flower)

"Wamyō Ruijushō" (Japanese Names Classified Collection) states:
　Red Cherry (Shukuō) - The "Honzō" (Materia Medica) says that cherry blossoms are also called Red Cherry, with the Japanese name "Hanaka," also called "Niwazakura" (Garden Cherry)

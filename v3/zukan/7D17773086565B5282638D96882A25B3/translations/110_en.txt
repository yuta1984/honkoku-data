**The Batan Bird (Cockatoo)**

This bird is a parrot from the land of Batan. There are two varieties: the large Batan and the small Batan. Its head resembles that of a Bohemian waxwing (renjaku), and when perched, it similarly resembles the waxwing. The undersides of its wings are a light reddish color (pale vermilion). Its call is extremely loud, and it is particularly skilled at mimicking sounds, much like a parrot. It is indeed a type of bird known for vocal imitation.

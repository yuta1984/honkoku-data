Recorded in Torishow Anshi

　　Kakesu (Jay; also known as Kuwashi-dori)

There is a separate variety called the Island Jay (Shima-kakesu), which originates from Nikkōzan. Many were captured in Edo during the first year of Kyōwa, the Year of the Rooster (1801).

On the 23rd day of the 10th month of the Bunsei Year of the Ox (1817), drawn from life based on a specimen sent by Hatako.

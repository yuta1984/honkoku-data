【Greater Bird-of-Paradise】
Wind Bird《Marginal note: Fūchō》

According to "Yamato Honzō": The ■鸘 (Kasakiri) is commonly called "Fūchō" (Wind Bird), and also "Kazakiri." It belongs to the swallow family and is also called "Amatsubame" (Swift); it is rarely seen. The ■鸘 (Kasakiri) and the Fūchō (Wind Bird) are separate creatures. The Fūchō also has short legs. It is often confused and discussed together with the Kazakiri (Swift). Details are given in the entry on ■鸘.

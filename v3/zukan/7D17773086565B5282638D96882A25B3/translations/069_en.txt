**Jutaichō (Paradise Flycatcher)**

As I have not yet seen a paradise flycatcher in person, I have made a reduced copy of an authentic painting (*shinsha*) by Shen Quan (Chin Sen), one scroll of which I possess, and added it to the Kinpu (bird compendium).

Painted by Nanpin Shen Quan

Copied at reduced scale on the eleventh day of the ninth month, in the year Hinoe-Saru (1836)

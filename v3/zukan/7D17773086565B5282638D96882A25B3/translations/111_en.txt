Ō-Murasakiinko (Large Purple Parakeet/Parrot)

Hi-inko (Scarlet Parakeet), Hatan-chō, and Ō-Murasakiinko (Large Purple Parakeet/Parrot)

On the eighth day of the fourth month of Kōka 2 (1845), Mr. Ozawa, a fellow enthusiast (kinyu), who was keeping these birds, made life drawings of them from the actual specimens. Three pages of these life drawings have been copied into this catalogue.

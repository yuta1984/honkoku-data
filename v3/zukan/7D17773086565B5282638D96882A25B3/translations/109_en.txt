## Hi-inko (Scarlet Parakeet/Parrot)

The Hi-inko (scarlet parakeet/parrot) is available in shops and was brought to Nagasaki from China (Chūka) throughout the year. They come in both large and small varieties, and all are fed sugar. All birds of the inko (parrot/parakeet) family have the habit of regurgitating the remnants of what they have eaten. There are several species of inko:

- Taika-inko (Large Chinese Parakeet)
- Ōmurasaki-inko (Large Purple Parakeet)
- Komurasaki-inko (Small Purple Parakeet)
- Goshiki-seikai-inko (Five-colored Blue-sea Parakeet)　*alternate name: Shichimō-inko (Seven-feather Parakeet)
- Zuguro-inko (Black-headed Parakeet)
- Shiro-inko (White Parakeet)
- Ao-inko (Blue/Green Parakeet)
- Onaga-ao-inko (Long-tailed Blue/Green Parakeet)

...and others.

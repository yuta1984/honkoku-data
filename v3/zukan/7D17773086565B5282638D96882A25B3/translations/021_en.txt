**Utou (善知鳥)**

A dried specimen of the Utou, with its skin and feathers preserved intact, held in the collection of Mr. Soga, was borrowed on the 20th day of the 6th month of the 5th year of Bunka (1808), having been rendered in accurate copy by Mr. Susumu (or "Shin"), from which this illustration was modeled.□ [Finished?]

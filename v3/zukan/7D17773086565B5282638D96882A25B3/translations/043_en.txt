**Dí Pheasant / Yamadori (Mountain Bird)**〔common name〕; also called *Sankei* (Mountain Fowl)
　　　Also known as: *Sanji* (Mountain Pheasant), *Dí-kei*　〔from the *Kinkei*〕

As stated in the *Shichikan Shokukyō* (Seven-Volume Food Classic):
　Mountain Fowl (*Sankei*)〔alternate name〕*Jiāoyù*; 〔Japanese name〕*Yamadori* (夜万土利)
　　Upon my examination, the Mountain Fowl (*Sankei*) and the *Junyu* (*Jùnyù*) are each of different species.
　　As seen in the commentary to the *Hanshu* (Book of Han), the *Jùnyù* is in fact the Golden Pheasant (*Kinkei*).

10th day of the Cherry Blossom Month (3rd month), Spring, year of Bingshén (1836)

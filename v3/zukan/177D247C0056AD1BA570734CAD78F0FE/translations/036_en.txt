[On Fukugyūka / Barberry]

From the *Bencao Gangmu* (本草綱目, Compendium of Materia Medica):
　　　Fukugyūka (伏牛花)　Also known as: Kakukoshika (隔虎刺花)
　　　　(Japanese name) Hebi-Noborazu ("Snakes Cannot Climb")
　　　　(Japanese name) Baraisu
　　　　(Japanese name) Inu-no-shiri-tsuki ("Pricks the Dog's Backside")
　　　　Berberus　※Latin (indicated as 羅甸, "Latin")

　　　　　　　　　　　Painted from life on the 28th day of the 3rd month,
　　　　　　　　　　　Year of the Water Snake (1833),
　　　　　　　　　　　by Yasaburō of Sokkyū, at a flower garden

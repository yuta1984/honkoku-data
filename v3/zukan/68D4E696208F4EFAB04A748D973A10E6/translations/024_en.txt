　　　　Pot cultivation
　The best time for planting is during the autumn equinox period. In years when autumn cold is strong,
　carefully consider and take them out after the equinox, divide the roots into individual clumps,
　and remove old roots. For planting soil, prepare well-draining soil in advance,
　lift and dry it, sift out poor soil with a sieve, and use mountain sand

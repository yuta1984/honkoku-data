According to the "Nihon Kōki" (Later Chronicle of Japan), Empress Shōtoku ordered Minister Kibi to issue an imperial decree stating that "wheat is the best of grains for relieving famine and helping those in need," and commanded all the people throughout the land to plant both barley and wheat.

According to "Yamato Honzō," recorded in the collected explanation of wild pea:
Beach pea (hama-endō)

The explanation states: The leaves resemble those of peas, and in the fourth month
purple flowers bloom. Similar to peas, it produces
fruit, but while similar, it differs [from regular peas], etc. The wild pea of "Kyūkō
Honzō" corresponds to this plant.

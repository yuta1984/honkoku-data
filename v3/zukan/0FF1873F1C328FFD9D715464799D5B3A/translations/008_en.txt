Beach pea (Hama-endō)

According to the explanation, the leaves resemble those of garden pea, and in the fourth month
it blooms with purple flowers resembling those of garden pea. The fruits it bears
are also similar yet different, etc. The wild pea in "Kyūkō
Honzō" corresponds to this plant.

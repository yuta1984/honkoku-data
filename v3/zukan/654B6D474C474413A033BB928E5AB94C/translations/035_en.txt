Rai-kin flower (Fusō-ka)

From 'Bencao Gangmu' (Compendium of Materia Medica):
　The silk tree is planted in household courtyards to prevent people from becoming angry.
　It is said that the silk tree removes anger, while day lily makes one forget worries.

On Large Sea Bream (Ōtai)
According to the "Nittō Gyofu" (Fish Records of Japan), large sea bream are found in China and Kyushu. Large specimens reach eight to nine shaku, or sometimes one jō in length. The taste is not good. Some say they are poisonous.

Medium Sea Bream, Mouth-viewing Sea Bream
A product of Kumano, approximately one shaku and nine sun in length.

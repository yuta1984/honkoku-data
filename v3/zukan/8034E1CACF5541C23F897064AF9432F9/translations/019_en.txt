【Menuke-dai (Rockfish)】
From Sendai. Menuke-dai resembles red sea bream but is much larger, with a color like that of red sea bream. About one shaku in length, with a mild taste. It has a large head and small tail. Similar to kasago (scorpionfish) from Odawara, with large eyes and mouth. The color is red without spots. When blown by wind, it immediately changes to white. It is a type of golden sea bream. The dorsal fin is in one place, with soft flesh like shad. The scales are like red pepper.

【Kinme-dai (Golden Eye Snapper)】
Red in color. Caught offshore. From Yamato. Also called Ebisu-dai.

Kinme has large eyes with red eye sockets. Those with long, forked tails are called "Kinme," and in Edo they are called "Kintarou."

【Kintarou】
Commercial name in Edo. Also called Kuruma-dai and Taruma-dai. From Ise Province seas.

【Kenhachi-dai】
A large variety of small sea bream. Slender and yellowish in color. Caught off the coast of Izu.

【Kinwazashi from Odawara】
Resembles sea bream but smaller, redder than sea bream. Eyes similar to gurnard. Head has a dignified shape like a marlin, also called "Tsuka" in some regions.

Picture of golden sea bream. This fish is also called a mermaid's child or three-treasure fish.

According to Tadashi from Ise, Kinme-haru is a small fish that is very beautiful, with a taste similar to itoyori (golden threadfin bream).

【Akasagi】
A member of the sea bream family. Has two long hair-like projections at the beginning and end of the fins. Long tail.

【Itokasago (Genhachi-dai)】
From Edo. Shape resembles blood sea bream but shorter with broader shoulders. Mouth is flat, and its form is quite elegant. Color resembles blood sea bream, vivid, and taste is also similar and delicious. Appears to be a type of blood sea bream. However, near the head of the dorsal fin, there is a very long thread-like projection that extends to the tail. This is what differs from regular sea bream.

【Akame-dai (Red-eye Sea Bream)】
More crimson than golden eye snapper, with a flattened shape. Red eyes, length over three sun. Large mouth with pointed scales. Found at Shiroko in Suruga and a place called Yaizu Port.

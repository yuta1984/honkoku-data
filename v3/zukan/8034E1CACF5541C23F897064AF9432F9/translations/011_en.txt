Itoyori (called Itouchi in Satsuma province)

Itoyori is about the size of a medium sea bream, with a small tail.
It has blue-yellow lines on its back, and the upper edge of the tail
extends long. Its shape and color are like sea bream, and its taste is
more delicious than sea bream.
Itoyori lives in the deep waters (80 fathoms) of the Iki Sea.
According to Kurimoto, it's called Itoyori-dai; fishermen call it Kyōshin.

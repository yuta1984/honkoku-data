【Geranium or a related species (Geraniaceae); flowers occur in both white and reddish-purple】
Tōgyūjibyo (Northern Ezo origin; known as Gennojōgo / Fūrosō)

Obtained from a flower dealer in Edo (Ebuka-kato)
　　From Sakuragawa Fūrō (an autumn-blooming specimen)

Pressed flower, sketched from life on the 22nd day of the 9th lunar month of the year Hinoto-Tori (1837), by Jinzanshi.

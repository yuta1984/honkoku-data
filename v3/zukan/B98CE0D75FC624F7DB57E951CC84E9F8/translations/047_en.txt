[Marginal note: *Aki-no-nogeshi* [Autumn Lettuce, *Lactuca indica*]. *Kiku-azami* bears purple flowers and more closely resembles a thistle, and is therefore a separate species.]

From *Tashiki-hen* [*Tashikiben*, a Japanese encyclopedia of plant names]:
　*Senninjōsō* *(marginal note: Kiku-azami [Chrysanthemum Thistle, *Cyrsium japonicum*] | Yamahito-no-tsue-kusa [Mountain Man's Staff Herb])*

On the 2nd day of the 8th lunar month of the Kinoe-Uma year [1834], a branch was cut from the garden and drawn true-to-life.

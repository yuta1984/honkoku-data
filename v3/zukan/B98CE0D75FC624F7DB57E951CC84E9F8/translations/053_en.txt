From "Jiuhuang Bencao" [Famine Relief Materia Medica]:
　　Entry for Qīngshězi　Kuma-yanagi (Berchemia racemosa)
　　　◦Tiě biān màn (Tetsuhenkazura)

In "Buyō Benryaku" [Military Practical Compendium], it is also noted that Kuma-yanagi (熊梁木) is suitable for making whips.

Sketched from life on the 14th day of the 7th lunar month of the year Hinoe-Saru (1836), using a branch cut from the garden.

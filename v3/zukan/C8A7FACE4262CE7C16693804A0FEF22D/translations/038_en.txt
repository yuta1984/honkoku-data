Kei-shō (Chicken mushroom)　　Nezumi-take (Mouse mushroom)


Two varieties of Nezumi-take

　Illustration recorded in
　"Shin'yō Kinpu." It has no cap and its shape
　resembles a mouse's
　foot, hence
　it received this name. Also,
　there are two varieties: those with round stems
　have a mild and delicious taste, being
　of superior quality. Those that have
　a sour taste will cause
　diarrhea if eaten.
　If bleached and dried
　before eating, they cause no harm
　and are considered inferior grade. People
　desire to gather
　these, it is said.

Sukumo-take (Compost mushroom)

　Illustration recorded in the "Kanka."
　According to the "Kinshi," its shape
　resembles the sasa-take but is somewhat smaller,
　and its color is grayish-white. It prefers to grow
　in places where dead leaves have accumulated
　in bamboo groves. When
　consumed, its poison causes people
　to laugh. It is also said to be
　a member of the laughing mushroom
　family.

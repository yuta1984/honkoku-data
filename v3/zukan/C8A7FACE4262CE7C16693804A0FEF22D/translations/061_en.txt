Sukumotake (Compost mushroom)

　Illustration recorded in the Kanka
　According to the Fungi History, its shape resembles the sasa
　mushroom but is somewhat smaller
　The color is grayish-white, and it prefers to grow
　in places where dead leaves have accumulated
　in bamboo groves. When
　eaten, its poison causes people to
　laugh. It is said to be
　a member of the laughing mushroom
　family

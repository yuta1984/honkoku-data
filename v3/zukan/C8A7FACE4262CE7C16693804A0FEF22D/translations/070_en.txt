Kitsune no efte (Fox's apron/covering)
　Kitsune no ekakifte

　Figure recorded in Kanko
　According to Kinshi, after summer
　prolonged rains, it grows in
　densely wooded places.
　It has a black head and red stem,
　shaped like a brush, soft
　and hollow inside. It dies immediately
　upon seeing sunlight. The base is slimy
　and has a foul odor.

Chikujoku (Bamboo fungi)

　　　Growing on the roots of decayed bamboo grass
　　　in the Aoyama area of Musashi Province,
　　　its shape resembles wood ear mushrooms or
　　　oyster mushrooms, and it is white in color


One variety

　　　Growing on thin bamboo in April and May during
　　　the long rainy season, appearing between the nodes.
　　　Its shape resembles wood ear mushrooms and is very
　　　soft; it falls off immediately when touched. It is
　　　yellow-red in color, and I have not heard of it being eaten

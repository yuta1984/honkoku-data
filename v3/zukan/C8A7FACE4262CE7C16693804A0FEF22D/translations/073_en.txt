Chikujoku (Bamboo fungus)

　　　Growing on the roots of old bamboo grass
　　　around Aoyama in Musashi Province, its shape
　　　resembles wood ear fungus (kikurage) or
　　　oyster mushrooms, and is white in color


One variety

　　Growing on small bamboo during the long rains
　　of April and May, appearing between the nodes.
　　Its shape resembles wood ear fungus and is very
　　soft; it falls off immediately when touched.
　　It is yellow-red in color and is not heard of being eaten

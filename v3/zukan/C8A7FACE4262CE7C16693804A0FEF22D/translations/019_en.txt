Ashitaka-shimeji

　Illustration recorded in Shobutsu Shasei. In Sanbutsu Shasei, it resembles shimeji but has a long stem, with a brown surface, and some have a pale reddish-brown color. The underside is white with vertical striped patterns. There is one variety with a small cap, pale yellow color and long stem that is slightly white. The texture is very soft. It is said that neither should be eaten.

Haritake (Needle mushroom)

　Illustration from the "Kinshi" (Mushroom History).
　According to the same book, it is also named
　for its similar shape.
　It grows in clusters in one location,
　extending five to six shaku, and comes in
　several colors including gray-white
　and light brown.
　Its flavor is similar to nezumitake (mouse mushroom)
　but somewhat inferior.

Dasai (Ship rudder mushroom) - Kajitake

　According to the "Kinshi" (Mushroom History), this is a fungus that grows on ship rudders. It is said that there is more than one variety. Mr. Tamura states that it often grows on ships that have been rotting for many years. Since this species has not yet been observed firsthand, no illustration is provided.

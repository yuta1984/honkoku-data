Kan-kin (Reed mushroom)
　Yoshitake

　Illustration recorded in Kanka.
　In Kinshi, it is described as a mushroom that
　grows on reed roots.
　Small in substance but delicious in taste, it
　grows abundantly in Ōu region,
　and is also said to be found in Takada, Echigo

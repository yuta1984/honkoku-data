Kan-kin (Reed mushroom)
　Yoshitake

　Illustration recorded in Kanko.
　According to Kinshi (Mushroom History), this is a mushroom that
　grows on reed roots.
　It is small in substance and delicious in taste, grows
　abundantly in Ou region, and is also said to
　be found in Takada, Echigo.

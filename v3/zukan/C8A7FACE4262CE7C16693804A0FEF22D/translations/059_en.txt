Ikuhitake　　　　　

　Illustration recorded in the Kanka.
　According to the Fungi History,
　it grows on rotten
　wood near water,
　resembles enokitake
　but the flesh is thin,
　both the surface and stem are pale
　reddish-brown color with white gills,
　bearing a slight [illegible character].
　Following the nature of poisonous trees,
　one must not
　err in selection
　when consuming this, it is said.

Nezumitake (Mouse mushroom)

　Illustration from the "Kanka".
　According to the "Kinshi" (Mushroom History),
　it is named for its shape resembling
　a mouse's leg. Small ones are about one sun
　in length, while large ones are three to four sun.
　They come in several colors: yellow-white,
　gray, red, black, light purple, and light brown.
　They are somewhat soft in texture and
　delicious in taste, and can be found everywhere.

Nezumitake (Mouse mushroom)

　Illustration from "Kanka." According to the "Kinshi"
　(Mushroom History), it is named so because its shape
　resembles a mouse's leg. Small ones are a little over
　one sun, large ones are three to four sun. There are
　several colors: yellow-white, gray,
　reddish-black, light purple, and light brown.
　It is somewhat soft and tastes
　good. It can be found everywhere.

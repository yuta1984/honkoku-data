Nezumitake (Mouse mushroom)

　Illustration from the "Kanka".
　According to the "Kinshi" (Mushroom History),
　it is named this way because its shape
　resembles a mouse's leg. Small ones are over one sun
　in size, large ones are three to four sun. There are
　several colors: yellow-white, also
　gray, red-black, light purple, light brown.
　It is somewhat soft in texture, delicious
　in taste, and grows everywhere,
　according to the text.

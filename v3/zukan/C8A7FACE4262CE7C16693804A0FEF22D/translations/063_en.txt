Waratake (Straw mushroom)
　Komokutake
　
　Illustration recorded in the Shinyo Fungi Catalog.
　It grows on decayed straw.
　Even if eaten, it causes
　no harm. The taste is said to be bland.

Makusotake (Dung mushroom)

　Illustration recorded in
　the Shinyo Fungi Catalog. It is the same
　species as waratake. It grows on
　horse dung. It is filthy and not eaten,
　it is said.

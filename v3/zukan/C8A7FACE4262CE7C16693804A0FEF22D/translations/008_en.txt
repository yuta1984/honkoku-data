Beni-take (Red mushroom)

　The illustration recorded in Kanko　The form in Kinshi
　resembles shimeji but is somewhat smaller, the surface is dyed
　red and has luster　The gills and stem are all
　pure white　Found in shaded and moist places everywhere
　Though it is said to be poisonous, it does not
　reach the point of harming people　Some say／if the outer skin is removed
　and then eaten, it is not poisonous, as Matsuoka Gen
　tatsu said, it is reported
　In Kōwa Shinpu it states that many near Edo
　are poisonous mushrooms　Probably they are momi-
　take, it says　One should choose carefully

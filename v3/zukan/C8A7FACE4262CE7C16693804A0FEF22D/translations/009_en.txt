Chichi-take (Milk mushroom)

　Illustration recorded in Kanko　In Kinshi it states
　it often grows in places where forest trees are thick
　Light yellow or light reddish-brown color　The gills are white
　with a slight yellow tinge　Resembles
　shiitake but is somewhat thinner　When damaged
　it produces a white thick juice　When soaked in water
　this stops immediately　Sweet and smooth with
　a rank odor　Used to make soup and served at meals
　Very good　Those who dry and store them
　say it is most excellent

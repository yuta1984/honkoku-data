Tengutake

　Illustration recorded in
　Shinyo Kinfu. In the same book,
　its size differs from other fungi.
　Because its shape is frightening,
　it was given this name.
　In ancient times, it was said to be harmful.
　Recently there are those who eat it,
　but the details are unclear,
　it is said.


Gosho-karakasa

　　　　　　　　Illustration recorded in Shobutsu Shasei. In Kinshi,
　　　　　　　　the cap is white with edges slightly tinged brown.
　　　　　　　　Diameter two to three sun, the long stem is white colored, reaching
　　　　　　　　several shaku in length. The texture is soft and easily damaged,
　　　　　　　　it is said.

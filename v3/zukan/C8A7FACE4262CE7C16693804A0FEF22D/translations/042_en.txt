Maitake (Dancing mushroom)

　Illustration from the "Shin'yō Kinfu" (Mushroom Compendium of Shin'yō).
　According to the same book, the cap differs from other fungi, and those with black caps and pure white stems are considered top grade. The taste is light and crisp, and should be cherished.

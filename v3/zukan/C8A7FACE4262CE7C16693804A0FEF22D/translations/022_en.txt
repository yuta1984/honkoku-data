Susutake (Soot bamboo mushroom)

　Illustration recorded
　in "Shobutsu Shasei" (Natural History Sketches). According to "Kinshi"
　(Mushroom Records), it grows
　in tree shade and in dung
　heaps. The stem is long and the cap spreads,
　reaching two to three sun in size.
　The surface is pale black with scale patterns,
　the underside is white and the texture
　is soft. It is said to be
　inedible.

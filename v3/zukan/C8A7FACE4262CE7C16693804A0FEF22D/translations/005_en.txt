Honzō Zufu Volume 58
　　　　　　Eastern Capital　Written by Iwasaki Tsunemasa
　　Vegetable Section, Mushroom Category

Kudzu Flower Vegetable (Kuzuhanasai)
　According to the 'Kinshi' (Mushroom Records), it grows in places where kudzu grows thickly and densely in the various mountains of Yamato. The texture is fleshy, and the color is red like rouge, resembling the red mushroom (benitake). It is said that this forms when the essence of kudzu is steamed. Mr. Tamura says that in the dialect of Nōshū (Noto Province), it is called "fushi no hanatake" or alternatively "shimokoshi." It is abundant in Nōshū but does not exist in Edo.

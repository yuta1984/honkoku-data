Honzō Zufu (Illustrated Manual of Materia Medica) Volume 58
　　　　　　Edo　Written by Iwasaki Tsunemasa
　　Vegetable Section: Mushroom Types
Kuzu-hana-na (Kudzu Flower Vegetable)
　According to the Kinshi (Mushroom Records), this grows in places where kudzu vines grow thickly and densely in the various mountains of Yamato. The texture is fleshy, and the color resembles rouge, similar to the beni-take (red mushroom). It is said to be formed from the essence of kudzu being steamed and transformed. Mr. Tamura says that in the Nōshū dialect it is called "fushi no hana-take," also known as "shimokoshi," and there are many different varieties in Noto. He says it does not exist in Edo.

　　　　The Shiui Honzō states:
　　　　　　　　　Nyoizui (also called) Ōshi　Wei is pronounced "i," zui is pronounced "nyo-sui-yū"

　　　　　　　　　　　　In the Chijinshō, izui is also called Hebi-yuri, etc.
　　　　　　　　　　　　Also Amadokoro, Naruko-yuri in various texts

The Yamato Honzō, in its collected commentary on peach trees, states:
　　According to the Kanjo (Han Shu):
　　　　Gold-and-Silver Peach — in the customs of this country, this is called the Edo Peach
　　　　　　　　　Also called the Genji-Heike Peach, and also the Sun-and-Moon Peach

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　First day of Koshō (3rd month), year of Kinoto-Tori
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Drawn from life from one cut branch

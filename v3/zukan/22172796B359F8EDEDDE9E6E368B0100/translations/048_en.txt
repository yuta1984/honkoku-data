Kairōkon　Kin-ebine (Golden Calanthe)
　　　　Haru-ebine (Spring Calanthe)
　　　　Kamakami-sō　From Sagami Province
　　　　Nankin-ebine (Nanking Calanthe)

There are several species of ebine orchids. Those that bloom in spring are called golden ebine orchids. The pale pink ones are called chakin (tea cloth) ebine orchids. There are also white-flowered, red-yellow, and pale-colored varieties. Karan (Cymbidium) is an ebine that blooms in autumn. There are also golden ebine with tea-brown flower bracts.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Kaei 1 (1848), March 5th

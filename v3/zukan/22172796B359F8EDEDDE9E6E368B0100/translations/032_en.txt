According to the "Yamato Honzō" (Japanese Materia Medica):
Wild radish (nozuroboku) - In the dialect of the western lands, called "ko-daikon" (small radish) or "Hadano daikon"
In Bukō (Edo) as well, it has long been mistakenly called "Hadana daikon"

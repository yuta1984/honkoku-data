Minister Tachibana no Moroe loved this plant and planted many in the residential land of Ide village in Yamashiro Province. The character "gidō" used in Lord Kintō's collection of recited poems is an error. Kantō is indeed yamabuki (butterbur).
　　The reading is "yamafufuki"

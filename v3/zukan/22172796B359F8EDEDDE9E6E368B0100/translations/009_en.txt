　　　Magnolia　Japanese name　According to current investigation, commonly
　　　　　　　　　called Mokuren

The Yamato Honzō states:
Jade Magnolia Flower

　　　The Yamato Honzō states:
　　　　　This is recorded in detail in the Enshi, Junsei Hasshō, Hanashi, and other works. There are two varieties of flowers, purple and white.
　　　　　In common parlance, the purple is called mokuren and the white is called white mokuren ○ The mokuren recorded in the Rōgakuan Hikki
　　　　　is incomparable ○ I say that the jade magnolia flower has the Japanese name ōyama renge.
　　　　　It is different from the mokuren that blooms when the leaves are full. The same book treats this as mokuren, but
　　　　　this is probably incorrect. The purple variety of mokuren is called seitei, but this is not recorded in various books.

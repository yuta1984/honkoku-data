　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　29th day of Kyāshō (2nd month), Year of Kinoto-Tori
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Breaking off a branch to draw a true illustration

In the Man'yōshū, Honchō-shiki, and Wamyōshō,
all read both tsubaki and sea pomegranate as "tsubaki."
Is this perhaps out of respect for ancient origins?

The single-petaled red ones are called mountain camellia. This is indeed
the original source.

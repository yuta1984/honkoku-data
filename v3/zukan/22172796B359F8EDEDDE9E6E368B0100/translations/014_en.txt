The Wakan Sansai Zue (Illustrated Japanese-Chinese Encyclopedia) states in its shrub section:
　　　Sea pomegranate (Tsubaki)
　　　　　Tsubaki - Japanese character
　　　　　Tsubaki originally belongs to the category of tall trees
　　　　　It is the Chu-chun tree, and differs greatly
　　　　　from the sea pomegranate

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　29th day of Kyōshō (2nd month), year of Kinoto-Tori
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Drawing a true illustration from a broken branch

　　　The Man'yōshū, Honchōshiki, and Wamyōshō all
　　　pronounce both tsubaki and sea pomegranate as "tsubaki"
　　　This usage has ancient origins

　　The red single-petaled variety is called mountain camellia. This is
　　the original source

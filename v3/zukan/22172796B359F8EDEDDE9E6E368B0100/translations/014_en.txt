　　Kaisekiryū (Camellia)

　　　different from the camellia

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　29th day of Kyāshō (2nd month), Year of Kinoto-Tori

The Manyōshū, Honchōshiki, and Wamyōshō all
use both "tsubaki" and "kaisekiryū" with the reading "tsubaki"
Their origins are ancient

The single-petaled red ones are called "yamatsubaki" (mountain camellia), and this is
the original source

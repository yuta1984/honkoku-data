The "Wakan Sansai Zue" (Illustrated Sino-Japanese Encyclopedia), Wetland Grass section, states:
　　　Thread-reeling grass　Ote-maki
　　　(Odamaki)　Both are common names
　　　　　　　　The proper name is unknown

　　　　　Its flowers have no surrounding (medari) angles, in the shape of a reel (waku)
　　　　　(I say) Because it refers to the tool for reeling thread, it is called
　　　　　thread-reeling grass

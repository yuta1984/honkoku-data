**Black-headed Parakeet (Zuguro Inko)**
Face, breast, belly, and tail are red; shoulders are blue; flight feathers and tail feathers are grey; head is black; wings are green.

**White Parakeet (Shiro Inko)**
Entire body is white; head is pale yellow.

**Hi Parakeet (Hi Inko)**
Red Parrot — cited from the *Sōsho* (宋書, History of the Liu Song Dynasty), under the entry "Chinchiku (沈鉤)"
- Beak: vermilion
- Back: green
- Iron-colored beak (a variant)

**Blue Parakeet (Ao Inko)**
Beak is vermilion; back is blue; breast is pale purple.

Shima-ikaru (Island Grosbeak) — Male

Always slightly smaller than the Kuwatori (Ikaru/Japanese Grosbeak). The eyes are pale red, the bill is yellow, and the feet are red. The head is black, with a small amount of white feathers on the nape. The back is deep reddish-brown. The wings are black with blue-green tips forming white markings. The belly is grey, the flanks are pale yellow, and the tail is black.

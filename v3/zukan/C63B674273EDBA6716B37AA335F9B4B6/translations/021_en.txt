Ren (Melia azedarach, Chinaberry Tree) 《Compendium of Materia Medica》
　　　Has mild toxicity. It grows abundantly in mountains and along roadsides. The leaves emerge in spring, with branches dividing from a single stem, bearing 5-7 leaves. When flowering, there are several calyxes in one cluster. Later it produces fruit, which turns yellow when ripe and contains seeds inside.

【Left Page】
Ukyūboku (Triadica sebifera, Chinese Tallow Tree) 《Compendium of Materia Medica》
　　　Has toxicity. It is planted from seeds transmitted from China. The leaves are flat and thin, light green in color, with no serrated edges. In summer, small yellow flowers appear at the tips of branches, forming drooping spikes. After flowering, it produces fruit that turns black when ripe and contains seeds inside.

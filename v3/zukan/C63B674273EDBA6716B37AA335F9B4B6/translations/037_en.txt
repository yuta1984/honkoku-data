Ginkgo (Ginkyō | Ginnan) 《Bencao Gangmu》
　　　Raw has no poison, but cooked has poison. The tree's form and characteristics are listed in the nature records. This fruit has male and female varieties.
　　　Also detailed in the nature records, eating it raw detoxifies alcohol poison, reduces phlegm, and kills
　　　parasites. Eating large quantities when cooked blocks qi. Children develop convulsions and suffer from
　　　malnutrition disorders. Should not be eaten together with eel.

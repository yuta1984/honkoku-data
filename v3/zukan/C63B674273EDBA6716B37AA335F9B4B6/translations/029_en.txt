Goosefoot (Rei｜Akaza) 《Bencao Gangmu》　
　　　It has mild toxicity. Seeds are sown in spring. The leaves initially bear reddish-purple powder, later becoming blue-green in color. When young,
　　　the leaf surfaces wilt mutually during the night. The branches divide and reach five to six shaku in height, and in autumn it opens fine small white flowers
　　　and forms small seeds. Also, one variety that bears blue-white powder is called Gray Goosefoot (Kwaichou｜Aoza) 《Bencao
　　　Gangmu》.

【Left Page】
Chard (Tensai｜Fudansou) 《Bencao Gangmu》
　　　It has mild toxicity. Seeds are sown in spring. It also grows from seeds sown at any time regardless of season. It also produces leaves from
　　　perennial roots. The leaf stems are triangular and soft. The leaves are green with luster. In summer it extends its stem and opens fine
　　　small white flowers forming spikes and produces fine seeds. It should not be
　　　eaten together with black pepper. It has great toxicity.

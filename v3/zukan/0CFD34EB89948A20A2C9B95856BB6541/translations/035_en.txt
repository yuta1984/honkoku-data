　　○　Grafting Method
　　　The timing is from around mid-February
　　　to around mid-May, which is not particularly
　　　difficult. Pull and cut the rootstock,
　　　削the cut surface so there are no saw marks,
　　　then pare the bark,
　　　ensuring there is no excess
　　　bark remaining.

[Left Page]
　　　Also, cutting the inner wood
　　　deeper than the bark is not good. Both the
　　　rootstock and scion
　　　should be treated similarly. When fitting and paring,
　　　the length should be about six to seven bu.
　　　Most importantly, use a small knife that has been well
　　　sharpened and honed. A blade that has mistakenly
　　　been used for salty things
　　　is very harmful. Using a moderately
　　　sharp blade for grafting
　　　produces good workmanship, but
　　　since you are cutting living wood,
　　　a blade as sharp as a famous sword

One variety: Chiri-tsubaki (Scattering Camellia)
　The flower shape resembles
　that of mountain camellia and is red in color. When it falls,
　the petals separate and drop

【Left page】
Sekiryu-cha (Pomegranate Tea Camellia) 《Collected Explanations》

　The flower form has
　large petals on the outside
　and dozens of fine petals
　in the center. It is red in color
　and resembles the double-flowered
　pomegranate

Doshin (Earth Ginseng) 《Note: Hiden Kakyō》　Yoshino Ginseng

Currently, it has been harvested to exhaustion in Yoshino, and it is said that it is now cultivated at the border with Kishū Province. The stems and leaves are the same as bamboo-jointed ginseng, but it has a taproot.

Fuji Astragalus (Fuji-ōgi)
Found in Mount Fuji in Suruga Province. The stem is vine-like,
and the root is white and hard with a deep taste. It produces
pale yellow flowers and forms pods. There is one variety that
produces purple flowers, and both the seedlings and roots are small.
Both types are woody astragalus (moku-ōgi) and are of inferior grade.

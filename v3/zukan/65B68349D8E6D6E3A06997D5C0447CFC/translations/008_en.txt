【Right page】
　Bean-roller (mame-mawashi)
　　　　　　Shuraku Kansue
Though meant to be kept secret, my chattering tongue—
this bean-roller, even hooking cranes—
will likely
　　reveal my reputation

　Woodpecker
　　　　　　Shinono Gyokuyū  
Will I wither away from love, gaining such a reputation?
The woodpecker's
pecking destroys
people's gossip

Alpinia oxyphylla (yakuchishi)　　Cardamomum minus (Dutch)

This is an illustration from the Dutch botanical work "Imban." It is a plant that has not been actually observed in either Japan or China. Its form resembles shukusha (Amomum), but the leaves are somewhat smaller, and the fruits are also smaller.

In my consideration, both shukusha and yakuchi, according to the illustrations and descriptions contained in "Imban," are tall trees. Moreover, when examining various later Western books, they are herbaceous plants, which matches what the Chinese scholars describe. However, since we cannot obtain the authentic

illustrations copied from the true figures, I temporarily include the old illustrations.

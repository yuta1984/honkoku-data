[Seal] Grass category

Kan says:
　Ryojo, Daigeki, Takushitsu, and Kansui all have similar forms but
　there are differences. Daigeki blooms yellow-purple flowers in spring. Kansui and Takushitsu
　are all similar, but Daigeki has no branching stems, while Kansui has branching stems.
　Takushitsu is also similar - Takushitsu has hair on its leaves, while Kansui is smooth.
　The grass is also not thick. Ryojo resembles Takushitsu, grows three to four feet tall, and slightly
　has hair on the stem. Each has similar form but is different, making them difficult to distinguish - these
　four types perhaps? Some call this Daigeki, but that is incorrect. Daigeki is a species
　category. They should be compared carefully. There are two types of Ryojo: spring Ryojo and
　autumn Ryojo.

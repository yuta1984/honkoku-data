【Shiode (Smilax riparia - Greenbrier)】

In the "Jiuhuang Bencao" (Famine Relief Herbal) it states:
　Niuweicai (Shiode)

There are two varieties, and there is also one with slender bamboo-like leaves. These are often found growing on cliffs in the mountains of Hiratsuka in Musashino Province (Musashi Province).

Some people call this plant "Hamakadzura" (Beach Vine).

【Seal】 Grass category

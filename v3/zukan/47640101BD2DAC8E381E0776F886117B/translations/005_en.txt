In the "Kyūkō Honzō" (Famine Relief Herbal):
　　Gyūbisai (Shiode - Smilax riparia)

There are two varieties, and there are also those with thin bamboo-like leaves. These are found occasionally.
It grows on cliffs in the mountains of Hiratsuka in Musashi Province.

Some people call this
　Hamakazura (beach vine).

【Seal】Grass category

Written on the 15th day of the rainy season in the month of Zuihin (5th month) of the year Hinoe-inu

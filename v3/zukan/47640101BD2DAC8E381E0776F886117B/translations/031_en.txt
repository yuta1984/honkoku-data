Plant shop says:
　Hitsuji-ran (Sheep orchid) 《Also called》 Hitsuji-sō (Sheep grass)
　　This flower blooms at the hour of the sheep (1-3 PM) and wilts at night, hence it got the name "sheep." Its roots resemble orchid, therefore
　　it is called sheep orchid. There is also the common name Nikkō kisuge. This is what the "Honzō" (Pharmacopoeia)
　　refers to as kentei flower. It is somewhat similar but different.

Green Frog - Also called Tree Frog
In winter and spring it is gray with brown spots. From summer to autumn it changes to green color. It calls for rain while crying on trees. Therefore it is called
Rain Frog (Amagaeru).
Also called Tree-climbing Frog (Kinobori-gaeru).

Also named Rain Demon 《from the same source》

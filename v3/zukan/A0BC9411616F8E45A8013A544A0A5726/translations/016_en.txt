Money crab　Zenigani　Also called small-pattern crab. It occasionally gets mixed in among various seafood catches. The hind legs are flat
like ladles. They resemble the fins of rays (kazame). There are sharp spines on the left and right sides of the carapace. When dried and stored
for years, the carapace becomes white with reddish-brown leopard-like patterns, which is beautiful. There are also several
species of this type.

Nanafushi (stick insect) is a highly venomous insect.
Also called Takenofushi, Nanafushi, or Kageto, it is a poisonous insect. Its front legs are long and it walks slowly, groping for things. It moves like a blind person who has lost their walking stick and gropes their way forward with their hands. Though it is a type of spider, it does not produce silk. There are three varieties: reddish-black, blue, and brown. The tail is long with seven segments, hence the name Nanafushi (seven segments).

Regarding an insect called Warekara, Mankōtei requested an identification. At first glance I found it strange and answered that this must be a type of Nanafushi that lives in water. It is probably venomous. 11th day of the 11th month, Kinoto-Ushi year.

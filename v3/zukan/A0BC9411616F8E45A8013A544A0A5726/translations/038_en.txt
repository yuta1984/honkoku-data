Mountain Frog - Red Frog - Called Akahiki in western provinces
Those that are extremely red as shown in the left figure are rare in the world.
In winter months, they enter warm places in earth burrows to endure the cold. Occasionally, when digging in warm places in the mountains and fields during severe cold periods, one may find those in winter hibernation
emerging from the soil. Sometimes one can obtain specimens with such vivid crimson and vermillion color. The taste is exceptionally delicious, and its medicinal effects are particularly
excellent. Those with markings descending from above the head and eyes resembling the character for "person" (人)
are the finest quality for making superior medicine.

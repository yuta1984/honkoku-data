【Upper Section】
Shoji-wasp (Paper screen wasp) - It clings to paper screens and beats its wings, making a buzzing sound. When the wings are removed and examined, it resembles an ant but is large and long. It does not sting people and is harmless. It has a constricted part at the waist. Though called a wasp, it is not actually a wasp but belongs to the fly family.

Horse fly - Different variety - In March, it has hair on its body that shines golden. It flies swiftly. Some have hair that shines white like silver. Its proboscis is long like a mosquito's. It attaches to cattle and horses to suck blood. It also attaches to humans.

【Lower Section】
Praying mantis - One variety - Captured in late June. Its four legs are long like a water strider's and have hair. The antennae can also be folded together with the front legs. At night, it comes drawn to the light of lanterns. It resembles a mosquito and also looks like a spider. Mantis egg case (sōhyōshō). During the Qingming festival, numerous mantis offspring emerge in a manner very similar to this creature.

The voice of the blue fly

【Left Page】
In March, it attaches to crabapple and pear blossoms to sip flower dew. When it folds its wings, they appear as one sheet. When captured and brought close to the ear, it makes sounds with its wings like a hichiriki (reed instrument).

【Right page, lower section】
Genseī (Spanish fly) - imported from barbarian lands - Cantharides
Spanish flies - flies said to be produced in Spain. Those exposed to scorching heat and intense sunlight have a color like blue-green indigo, resembling blue-green jade.

According to the "Chinkōfu-shi" (Gazetteer of Zhenjiang Prefecture): "Genseī is called blue maiden (seijōshi)."

【Left page】
A type of dragonfly that is small and yellow is named kori. Its common name is red-robed messenger. Akatonbo (red dragonfly).

One type that is blue-black has the Japanese name shio-karatonbo, and those with white tail tips are called shio-uri.

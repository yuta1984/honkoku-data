What is illustrated on the right is the Usagigashira (rabbit-head bat) that is found in the mountains of Utsunomiya in Shimotsuke Province. This is a type of
mountain bat. Both ears are particularly long, like those of a rabbit. A thin membrane grows separately on the inner side. This can be understood
by looking at this illustration. It is said that they are also found in Shirakawa in Mutsu Province. I have seen dried specimens in past years. They are completely absent
from Edo.

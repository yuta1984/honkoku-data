Okoze - This is a type of insect called yokobue (horizontal flute). It is pale blue-white in color with a soft body and has a somewhat transparent quality. Its shape resembles a cicada. Its size is like a sesame seed. As it grows, it develops white hairs on its tail, appearing as if carried on its back. This is drawn large based on microscopic observation and rendered to actual size.
The newly born ones do not develop white hairs.
One type of moth is extremely small and flies in clear weather around the time of Inoko in October. It has white hairs on its tail, and these white hairs fall off easily. In Owari Province it is called "Innoko," and in Awa Province it is called "Ten-no-oba."
When the tail develops white hairs, its shape resembles cimicifuga flowers.

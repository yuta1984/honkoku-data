Hakuchō (Swan)
　Koku (Swan)
　The taste is sweet and greatly warms the body. It is poisonous. When preserved in salt or roasted and eaten, it increases vitality.
　They are abundant in the Kantō and Ōshū regions. They are extremely fatty, and overconsumption causes
　eye diseases and head sores.

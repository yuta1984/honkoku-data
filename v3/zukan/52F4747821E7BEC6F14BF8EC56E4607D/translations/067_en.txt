Hototogisu (lesser cuckoo)
　Shikich-ō (子規鳥) 《Shuowen》　Shikiketsu (鶪鴂) 《Records of the Grand Historian》　Shiki (鶪帰) 《Gaotang Fu》　Shiki (子規) 《Xu Guang's Commentary on Records of the Grand Historian》
　The taste is sweet and has cooling properties, with no toxicity. It removes heat poison from smallpox and kills parasites.

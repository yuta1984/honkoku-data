Hototogisu (lesser cuckoo)
　Shikichō (child oriole) 《reference: Shuowen》　Shiki 《reference: Records of the Grand Historian》　Shiki (sister's return) 《reference: Gaotang Fu》　Shiki (child rule) 《reference: Xu Guang's commentary on Records of the Grand Historian》

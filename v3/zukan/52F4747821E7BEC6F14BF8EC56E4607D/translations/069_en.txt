Hashibuto-garasu (Large-billed Crow) - Also called Yama-garasu (Mountain Crow). All crows in Edo city are of this species.
　Crow (烏鴉)
　Taste: sour and astringent, nature: neutral, non-toxic. It has the effect of lowering qi and killing parasites. It is effective for the five exhaustions and seven injuries,
　hemoptysis, coughing, and childhood malnutrition disorders.

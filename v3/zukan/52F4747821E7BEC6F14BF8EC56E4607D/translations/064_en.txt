Kawasemi (kingfisher)
　Suichō (翠鳥) from "Erya"　Gyokō (魚狗) from "Bencao Shiyi"
　Its taste is sweet and salty, neutral in nature, with no toxicity. The meat is fishy and not suitable for eating.
　When charred and ground into powder for consumption, it has divine efficacy for fish bones or meat stuck in the throat. Taking the decoction
　is also good.

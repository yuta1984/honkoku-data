Udzura (quail)
　Quail (jun)
　The taste is sweet and of neutral nature with no toxicity. It is a delicacy among birds and greatly
　supplements the five organs. It is beneficial to eat. There are no taboos for sick people. However, it should not be eaten before the fourth month. It is good to eat after the fourth month. If eaten together with mushrooms, it will cause hemorrhoids.

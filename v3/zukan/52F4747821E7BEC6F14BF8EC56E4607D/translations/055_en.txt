Yamadori (copper pheasant)
　Mountain chicken (sankei)
　The taste is sweet and of warm nature with slight toxicity. The meat has excellent flavor and is not inferior to pheasant meat.
　Female meat is the best. The medicinal properties are the same as pheasant. People with ulcers or sores should not eat it.

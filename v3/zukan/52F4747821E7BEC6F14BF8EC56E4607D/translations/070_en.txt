Hashiboso-garasu (Carrion Crow) - Also called Hashiboso, also called Sato-garasu (Village Crow)
　Compassionate Crow (慈烏)
　Taste: sour and salty, nature: neutral, non-toxic. It supplements exhaustion, treats emaciation, and stops coughing.
　It is effective when roasted and eaten.

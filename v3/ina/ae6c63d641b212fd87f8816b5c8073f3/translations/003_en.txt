Document of Submission

The predecessor Katō Shinbei, in the 5th year of Bunsei, year of the Horse, had accumulated debts from your side, so through the mediation of Mr. Fujiya Kankichi and Mr. Kanaya Kichibei, he pledged his property as collateral and arranged for repayment in five annual installments. However, in the same year, the same person died, and since there was no successor, a temporary administrator took charge for a while. In the 5th year of Tenpō, year of the Horse, the property was destroyed by fire, but since your side could not afford the reconstruction, it was reportedly transferred to the town. Therefore, after consultation among the town officials, they graciously undertook the house construction, for which we are deeply grateful.

We have succeeded to the aforementioned Shinbei's position and have been serving in the same manner as our predecessor Shinbei.

Therefore, we petitioned Mr. Yamaya Itō Jizaemon and Mr. Ikegami Kichibei regarding the aforementioned debt, making repeated requests for assistance. Due to their special consideration for this long-standing matter, both gentlemen arranged to donate the amount of the borrowed money to Tennō Shrine and graciously returned Shinbei's sealed document, for which we are deeply grateful. Therefore, as proof of this matter, for perpetuity...

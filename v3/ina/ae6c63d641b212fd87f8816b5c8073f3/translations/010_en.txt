Regarding the house and property previously owned by Katō Shinbei: In the past, in the fifth year of Bunsei (1822), the said Shinbei had debts with Fujiya Tōzaemon and had placed his house and property as collateral. After Shinbei's death, the matter was suspended for a while, and moreover, in the fifth year of Tenpō (1834), it was destroyed by fire. Since Tōzaemon's side could not manage the reconstruction, the land was transferred to the town district.

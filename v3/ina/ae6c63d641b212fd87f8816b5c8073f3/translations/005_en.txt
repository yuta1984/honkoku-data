Regarding the house and residential lot that was owned by the late Kato Shinbei: In the past, in the 5th year of Bunsei, year of the Horse, the said person had debts to Fujiya Tōzaemon-dono, and had mortgaged his house and residential lot. After Shinbei's death, the property remained vacant for some time. Furthermore, in the 5th year of Tenpō, year of the Horse, it was destroyed by fire spreading from neighboring buildings. Since Fujiya Tōzaemon-dono could not afford to rebuild it, he surrendered the land to the town district. After consultation among the town officials, in the 2nd year of Kaei, year of the Rooster, a house was constructed with their assistance, and subsequently a Tennō shrine was established there.

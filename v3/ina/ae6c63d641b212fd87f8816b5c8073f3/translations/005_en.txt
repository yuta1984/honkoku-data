Regarding the house and property that the previous generation's Kato Shinbei possessed: In the past year of Bunsei 5 (1822), the said person had debts to Fujiya Toemon's establishment, and had mortgaged his house and property. However, Shinbei died, and afterwards the matter was suspended for some time. Furthermore, in Tenpo 5 (1834), the property was destroyed in a conflagration. Since Fujiya Toemon's establishment could not undertake the repairs, they submitted the land to the town district.

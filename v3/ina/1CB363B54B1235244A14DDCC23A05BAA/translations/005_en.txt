　　　　　A Written Pledge　　　
　　Regarding the domain forest of Takami Village, Nakayama Village, and Osokura Village in Nakazawa District
For the above-mentioned forest for which we petitioned, we have been graciously appointed as the contracted operators for the mountain代金 of 135 ryo, for which we are most grateful. We hereby submit 100 ryo at this time.
The remaining 35 ryo will be submitted when we enter the mountain in the coming Year of the Tiger.
Having affixed our contracted seal according to the separate sheet of regulations, we shall strictly observe all matters.
We shall also carefully examine and select reliable persons as woodcutters and day laborers before entering the mountain.
[Left Page]
　This we pledge.
One: We shall carefully place boundary markers for the domain mountain, and humbly accept that we shall absolutely not enter anywhere outside our contracted area. We shall strictly convey this point to the woodcutters and day laborers and ensure they exercise strict caution. Should any misconduct come to light from outside sources, we accept that any punishment may be imposed upon us following official investigation.
As stated above, we shall begin mountain operations from this year and submit our final report during the coming Year of the Tiger.

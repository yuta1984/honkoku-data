Upon arrest as described above, the prisoners shall be escorted to the headquarters within Hon-Seribuma village and handed over to the fourth squad, who shall then depart. Once the fourth squad has custody of the prisoners, they must pay attention to all matters such as loose ropes, patrol vigilantly, maintain security until the general arrest force returns, and depart as soon as all are assembled and the destination is determined.

From Shiojiri Pass, the second squad shall return and divide up to patrol the villages throughout the district. After two or three days have passed, they shall return upon receiving instructions.

Furthermore, when making arrests in the villages, rope binding shall be conducted together with village officials, and local laborers from the district must not be used for rope binding and similar tasks.

Guidelines for those participating in the general arrest operation:
1. Haori jackets and rain capes are unnecessary
2. Alcohol is prohibited
3. Casual conversation during travel is unnecessary except for discussions related to arrest duties
4. Prepare one spare rope each
5. Bringing equipment is naturally required
6. Bring and use a stretched bamboo hat
7. Each squad shall bring lanterns, candles, etc.
8. There must be no delay in departure time
9. For rain gear, straw raincoats shall be used at the destination
10. The headquarters shall be established at the residence of the village headman of Hon-Seribuma village

Be aware that in Hon-Seribuma village, green bamboo shall be cut and curtain hanging preparations made.

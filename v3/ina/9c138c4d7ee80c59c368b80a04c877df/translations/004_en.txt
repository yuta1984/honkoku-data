Regarding Ōkura and Gengōemon, detailed instructions were given in writing. One written document was handed to Gengōemon, and one written document was also given to Ōkura.

Same 11th year, year of the Horse (1726) - Oral statement
1. When I went to Edo this spring, I killed my uncle Matsumoto Jūrōemon's nephew by hand. After investigation, I was pardoned, and I was told that wherever I may reside from now on, there will be no restrictions, for which I am most grateful. This was announced in the presence of Inspector Ogasawara Heibē-sama and Town Magistrate Suwa Mino-no-kami-sama, so I am returning home.
The above
7th month, 8th day
Hongō Ōkura (seal)
To the Magistrate's Office

1. Hongō Ōkura reported that he returned on the night of the 7th day of the 7th month. When this was reported to both magistrate offices the following day, after investigation it was found that when he went to Edo he gave no notice of departure, and furthermore did not send a representative, so he was reprimanded and this was announced to him.

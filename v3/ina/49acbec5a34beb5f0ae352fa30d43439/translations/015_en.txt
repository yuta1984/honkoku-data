- Report to the supervisory office of disciples, and that office shall submit petitions to this ministry with accompanying letters

- Those who have received licenses and are practicing but cease operations due to incidents or death shall return their licenses

- Licenses and smallpox vaccinations shall be distributed only on the 3rd and 8th of each month

- Those who wish to receive smallpox vaccination shall apply to Toko (Eastern School) or licensed medical practitioners

- Licensed medical practitioners may charge appropriate fees when distributing smallpox vaccine at their residences

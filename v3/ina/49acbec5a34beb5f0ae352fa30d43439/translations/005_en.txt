　September 30th

1. Regarding opium tobacco, which has been strictly prohibited for some time,
　furthermore, regulations for the sale of opium have now been established,
　and strict prohibition has been declared for Chinese residents
　at each port. Moreover, even raw opium for medicinal use
　may not be handled freely, and handling regulations
　have been established as shown in the separate document. Therefore, local
　government officials must strictly control [opium] so that all people
　under their jurisdiction, down to the common folk, do not misunderstand [the regulations].
　　Kanoe-Uma, 8th month [August 1870]　　　Council of State

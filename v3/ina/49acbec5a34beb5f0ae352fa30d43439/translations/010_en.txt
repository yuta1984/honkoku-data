　　Kanoe-Uma, September　　　Ministry of Justice

Official notice of September 30th
Regarding sake brewing, since various crops have ripened abundantly this year, all licensed merchants are permitted to brew.
　　Kanoe-Uma, September　　　Council of State

Regarding the silver coins worth two bu in gold, after issuing notice last year (己year) that they could be exchanged at a rate of thirty ryō per hundred ryō, and further ordering in March of this year that they be submitted promptly, since there still appear to be unexchanged remnants and submissions continue without limit, the exchange period is set until December 15th, after which date no exchanges will be accepted.

Petition

Regarding the reconstruction of the portable shrine, the town elders came to Hokomochi village and said, "We intend to construct a portable shrine and will provide two guardians." At that time, we stated, "The three shrine priests should guard it, as they have done for generations since ancient times." When we insisted on this, the town elders said in various ways, "Although it may be burdensome for the shrine officials, please guard it." However, we firmly stated, "Since we have been farmers from ancient times, we cannot perform the duties of priests for Gongen-sama."

At that time, the shrine priests said, "If that is the case, please construct one portable shrine. If so, we will guard it wearing sashes. Please construct one shrine for us."

Regarding this matter, Uhyōe-dono, Ichiuemon-dono, and Chūzaemon-dono mediated, saying, "Although presumptuous, we will mediate. This concerns three shrines, but how about just one shrine? If naturally the government increases the shrine territory, then only the shrine priests should guard it. If not, half should be from the shrine town and half shrine priests. Furthermore, the matter of offering money was settled for the time being through mediation, but now the shrine priests are making various claims about the offering money being three portions.

Since this matter now concerns both the shrine town people and the priests, we respectfully request that the nine bags of rice granted by our lord be distributed to us as well, or to the priests of Ōshima, so that we may conduct the shrine rituals. The above.

March 27th　　Shrine town people
To the Deputy Official

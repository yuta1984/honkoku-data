Regarding the reconstruction of Gongen-sama's portable shrine in the year of Bosshi (1st year of Keian), there was disorder due to the absence of written agreements. Through the mediation of three persons - Chūzaemon, Ichiuemon, and Gonbei - it was decided that six shrine priests and six shrine town people would provide laborers in cooperation, and the matter of offering money was also settled for the time being. If the shrine territory should become heavier in responsibility, it was resolved that only the shrine priests would guard it.

However, there has been a dispute since last year, and this time it became an official legal case. Regarding this matter, through the mediation of Deputy Official Yoshida Kinuemon-sama and the town elders, it was decided that the shrine priests would collect all the offering money from the portable shrine, and in addition would pay 600 mon in compensation to the shrine town. Furthermore, regarding the matter of shrine carriers, as previously decided, the shrine town would provide six people, and this mediation was completed and resolved.

From now on, regardless of any grievances the shrine priests may have, we will provide six portable shrine carriers, and moreover, the entire shrine town will accompany the portable shrine procession. If the shrine territory becomes heavier in responsibility, we will not need to provide laborers, so the 600 mon compensation will also not need to be paid.

Having decided thus, we will not cause disorder with each other. A written pledge for future reference, therefore as stated.

Keian 4th year, Kanoto-u (Metal Rabbit)
April 3rd　　From Hokomochi town

Humbly submitting this verbal petition

Regarding the mountain forest and temple grounds that Kenpuku-ji has filed a lawsuit about this time - since this is a temple with ancient historical origins, we received vermillion seals from Lord Shingen and Lord Katsuyori. Since that time, even within the territory granted to Lord Hoshina Higo-no-kami, we have received official documents generation after generation without fail, and have been granted the aforementioned lands. Particularly as a family temple, in addition to the aforementioned lands, we were granted 100 koku of land in Kasagenmura village.

However, when Lord Higo-no-kami was transferred to another domain, Lord Torii Shuzen-no-sho received the grant. Under the direction of the senior retainers Toriyama Izumi-dono and Nagasaka Inaba-dono, we were unreasonably ordered to demolish Kenpuku-ji and make it into Minesan-ji, which was to be Lord Shuzen-no-sho's family temple.

The head priest and parishioners naturally presented documentary evidence of ancient historical claims, but were not granted a hearing. Therefore, the head priest, being at his wit's end, went down to Edo and filed a lawsuit. As a result, without fail as stated above, from the year of the Rat to the year of the Tiger, there was no interference, and we administered the mountain forests and temple grounds as before.

However, in the third year, the aforementioned Izumi-dono and Inaba-dono confiscated the mountain forests and temple grounds. Therefore, both the head priest and parishioners made various appeals saying "If things continue this way, the temple will be destroyed, so please grant us the lands as before," but this was not granted, and in the end the lands were not returned to us, causing us great distress.

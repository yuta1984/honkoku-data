1. Regarding the portable shrine (mikoshi) of the Three Shrines Great Gongen of Hokoji on this occasion: the three Shinto priests shall provide six mikoshi carriers. Among these, six shall be provided by the Hokoji uchiko (shrine supporters). Furthermore, regarding the matter of scattered coins (offerings), they shall be provided for the time being from among the aforementioned twelve people. Moreover, whenever the portable shrine is brought out, the matter of the twelve mikoshi carriers shall be arranged and provided by the three Shinto priests. For this purpose, we establish this agreement.

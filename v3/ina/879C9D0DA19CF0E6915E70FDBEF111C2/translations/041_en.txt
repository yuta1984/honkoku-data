10th Day
One item: Today, appearing at the official reception gate (御用口) of the castle to report on official business,
　　the following was communicated by Kumezaemon-dono:
　　During the previous month, a petition submitted by Ōmiya
　　regarding a contract for hemp (三人麻), hemp fiber (芋麻), and linen cloth (麻布),
　　along with the associated levy (運上) application, was returned (rejected/sent back).
　　It was indicated that in recent years, those associated (with such contracts) have been under review,
　　and that they should submit a formal expression of gratitude (礼) again.
　　Since they should submit a new petition after assessing the state of this year's hemp crop,
　　Ōmiya was summoned and so notified accordingly.

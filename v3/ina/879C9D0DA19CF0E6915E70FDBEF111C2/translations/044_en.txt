One item: A letter from Itosaemon-dono, as follows:

I write through my deputy (tedai). As you will be making your visit tomorrow until the welcoming banquet (nyūkyō), please dispatch two net-casters (ami-uchi) to the area above the Haru River crossing, past the sixth chō mark. However, should the weather over the fields be poor (i.e., inclement weather), your visit may be delayed, so please make inquiries accordingly. If you are not departing from the town, the items will be forwarded to you.
Please dispatch (the net-casters) in accordance with the above-stated time. Respectfully submitted.

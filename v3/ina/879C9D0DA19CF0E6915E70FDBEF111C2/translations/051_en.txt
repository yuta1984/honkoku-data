Same day
Item: A report was submitted regarding the embankment construction work on the Fujisawa River.

Item: Today, upon being summoned from the castle by Kumezaemon, I went to present myself. A message was conveyed through Mokuzaemon that if there were any relevant retained records, they would be reviewed on the evening of the 22nd, and that the precedent records should be brought to the residence of the inspector (ginmi-yaku). Accordingly, I went there, and upon being told to submit a written report after careful examination, I returned home with that understanding. I have thereupon submitted a written statement regarding the matter of gold/money.

Same day
Item: An instruction was received from Mozaemon. Regarding the delivery on the 23rd, I was directed to report that we have duly acknowledged [the order] to ensure delivery to the castle by the hour of nanatsu in the inner quarters (approximately 4 o'clock in the afternoon).

Same day

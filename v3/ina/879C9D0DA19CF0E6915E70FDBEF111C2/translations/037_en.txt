29th day
One item: Matoba village Kōtauemon, wishing to continue the dyeing business (kōya), submitted a petition to Lord Tabasami Zaemon; instructions were given to accept and process the petition. That matter has been conveyed. The above.
Same day
One item: Regarding the transport levy (unzōsen) on hemp fiber and hemp cloth, for both categories — how much in pack-horse fees and long coins (nagasen) per household — Lord Aizaemon instructed that this be looked into and a written report submitted. This has been communicated to the Kōchi-kata (the local district office).

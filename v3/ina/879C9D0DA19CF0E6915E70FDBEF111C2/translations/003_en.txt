New Year's Day, First Month
One, Clear weather. Those twelve years and older performed New Year's greetings.
At the hour of the Dragon (7-9 AM), we went to serve and pay respects. Due to
economy measures (gokennyaku) from this summer, certain duties were omitted, so fans were presented.
However, there were issues with the procedure, so the fan representatives were
reduced by half, and copper coins were given to each of the thirteen escort guards,
which was arranged by the palace page supervisors.
　　　　　　　Caretaker
Eleventh day
One, As customary, for the New Year's first duties, we went to Kyūemon's residence
along with colleagues and subordinate Kyūbei.
However, having received orders, from today we were assigned to
the council duties for calculating loan money.
Thirteenth day
One, A letter from Danemon-dono to Seiemon reads as follows:
"At this time, ducks have been spotted downstream at Kawanaga.
I believe nets should be sent out."
　　　　　　　　　　　　The above
　　First month, thirteenth day　Miura Danemon
　　To Satō Tarō-dono
Regarding the above, Tokuhei Isuke was summoned and agreed.
Seventeenth day
One, Today, Tsunahei Isuke presented several small cranes.
In the evening he returned, and of the requested thousand birds,
one kurohagi (black-backed bird) and four birds in total were prepared as ordered
to be presented to the officials for minor duties, and instructions were given
to present them to the repair officials through a monk.
Twentieth day
One, For the New Year's first duties, five colleagues went to the castle
and presented four account books for the Ox year loan calculations
for thorough examination.

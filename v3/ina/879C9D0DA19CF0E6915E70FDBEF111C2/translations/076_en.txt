25th Day

Item: Regarding today's official business — Kyūzaemon reported that, concerning this year's matsutake (mushroom) [procurement duty], Kyūzaemon-dono will be serving as fude-yado (the lodging/post for record-keeping duties). As stated above.

Item: Regarding a report by Kyūbee to the effect that townspeople upstream of the official weir (o-yana) had submitted a request [to the authorities] wishing to install their own fish weir — upon our inquiry [to the authorities regarding this], we were instructed that it should not be permitted (muyō). This was duly communicated to Kyūbee.

Item: Regarding the occasion on the 20th day when [officials] went out on official duty to inspect the fish weirs (yana-aratame) — as to the matter of our [party] lodging for one night wherever [we] happen to stop along the way to distant locations — [the question of] from what period this arrangement had been approved ■ — Jōzaemon-dono instructed [us] to ascertain [this] and report back. Accordingly, Kyūbee went to inquire at Kazuemon-dono's place, and it was learned [the matter came] from Shinbee-dono's ■emon. It was reported that for whatever reason, [the matter] had not been recorded in the register as prepared. This matter was reported and resolved.

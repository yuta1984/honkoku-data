I requested that the official seal for the combined stamping be granted henceforth, and was told by Lord Danemon that after due consideration, the combined stamping would be carried out.

1. It was ordered that all men without stipend (musoku) receive half a bale each of miso and soybeans, along with substitute rice, and Lord Shujirōemon was told to convey this in the same manner as previously communicated.

22nd day
1. The other day, Asahei Isuke returned from serving as bamboo grove official (yabu bugyō). As Lord Ono Sanshu stated, orders were promptly given to assign this to the御沢 (Osawa) office, and through the monk attendants, it was submitted to the御膳番 (meal guard) officers today to be forwarded to Lord Takerōemon for retention, as per established procedure. It was reported that Lord Zennojō and his companion were given sake, so they were immediately summoned, informed of the above matter, and then visited Lord Kyūzaemon, Lord Danemon, and Lord Dannojō to offer thanks.

23rd day
1. As Kyūbei was reported to be unwell, we sent word that we wished to wait and see for a day or two, and this was duly acknowledged.

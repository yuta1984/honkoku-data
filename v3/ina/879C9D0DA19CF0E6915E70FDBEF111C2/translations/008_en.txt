Second Month　　　Kōsaku
First day
One, Today is the first Horse day [Hatsuma festival], and according to previous custom
　After the hour of the Horse (around noon), we visited the shrine at our convenience
　and completed [the observance]
Second day
One, The two people who went out to Kawachō today
　returned, but because the water has dried up, 
　no birds were seen at all
Fourth day
One, Five to and eight shō of rice, being a stipend for two people
　This is for both months of February and March
　Kyūbei's stipend rice received, as usual
　No discrepancy from what is written above. Above
　　　　　　　　Akabane Kōsaku
Humbly submitting this written petition
One, I have been engaged in paper-making business
　until now, but having advanced in age, and moreover in recent years
　having become sickly, from this year forward
　I wish to cease this occupation. Regarding the tax obligation,
　I humbly request that you grant exemption
　　　　　　Petitioner
　　　　　　　Den'nosuke [seal]
If you would graciously hear the above
and grant as petitioned,
I would be most grateful.
Above
　Hōreki 8th year [1758]

Second Month　　　Kōsaku

First day
One, Today is Hatsu-Uma [First Day of the Horse]; as per previous precedent,
　After the ninth hour [around noon], participants attended the shrine visit
　at their own convenience. The matter has been concluded.

Second day
One, The two persons who went out to Kawachō [the river area]
　have returned. Due to the low water levels,
　they reported seeing no waterfowl whatsoever.

Fourth day
One, Rice: five to eight shō; this is for two persons' stipend
　The above covers the stipends for both the second and third months.
　The wording regarding Kyūbei's stipend rice follows the usual formula,
　and there are no discrepancies from the preceding entry. Above.
　　　　　　　　Akabane Kōsaku

　With all due respect, I humbly submit this written petition.
One, I have until now been engaged in the trade of papermaking,
　however, as I have grown old and furthermore have become
　infirm in recent years, I wish to cease
　the said trade from this year forward. I humbly petition
　that I be granted exemption from the trade tax (un'jō)
　associated with the above.
　　　　　　Petitioner:
　　　　　　　Dennojō [seal]

　If you would be so gracious as to hear this petition and grant it
　as requested, I would be most deeply grateful.
　Above.
　Hōreki 8th year [1758]

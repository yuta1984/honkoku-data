The above petition was brought in person by the group head on the fourth day. Upon inquiring into the circumstances, there were found to be no discrepancies, and the matter was accordingly reported during official business on the fifth day. Furthermore, the said written document was submitted for your inspection.

Eighth day
One, The manservant (chūgen) who had been dispatched to Kawachō returned today
　having caught one mallard duck. This was immediately
　reported to the Monthly Duty Officer (tsukiban), and following precedent,
　the duck was forwarded to those on repair/maintenance duty (tsukuroi-ban)
　through the intermediary of a page (bōzu).

Same day
One, Regarding the manservant dispatched to Kawachō, a query was made
　on the day of the Monthly Duty Officer as to whether he should now
　be recalled to the fields (畑; upland fields/base station). The response was
　that he should be recalled, as requested.

【Left Folio】

12th Day

One item: Regarding a petition for construction (築き申込み) submitted by Denshirō of Maedo-dōmura (or Saku-mochi-shimo-aka village), the matter was duly received and noted.

13th Day

One item: The firewood clearing work (maki-barai) at the lacquer field (urushi-batake) has been completed. From today, the fellow officer Kōsaku has been instructed to oversee the grand allocation (ō-wari).

　　　　　Memorandum (Oboegaki)

Rice: [Sufficient] for 9 persons. Each person to receive 2 gō, 5 shaku ■.

The above is issued because [the named persons] came out and performed duties in connection with the firewood clearing at the lacquer field. The rice was received and distributed as provisions for lunch. This is as stated above.

　Hōreki [8th year], 5th Month　　Ikegami Kyūbē
　　　To: Nezu Ryūemon-dono　　[Seal]
　　　To: Kumai Hidesaburō-dono

The foregoing is confirmed to be without error. That is all.
　　　　　　Kuzaemon　[Seal]

　　　　　　Memorandum (Oboegaki)

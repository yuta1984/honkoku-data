【Left Folio】

12th Day

One item: From Shimōakamae in Himochi Village, a man named Denshirō of the same village submitted a request regarding the installation of a fish weir (yana). The request was received and duly noted.

13th Day

One item: The firewood clearing (takigi-barai) at the lacquer field (urushi-bata) has been completed. From today onward, instructions have been given to arrange the allocation of persons (hito-wari) to be dispatched to the office of Kōsaku, who holds the same official role.

　　　　Memorandum (Oboegaki)

Rice: for 9 persons — at a rate of 2 gō 5 shaku per person

The above rice is hereby received as a midday meal, in recognition that [the recipients] came out for the full day and performed their duties in connection with the firewood clearing at the lacquer field. Accordingly, this is as stated.

　5th month, Year of the Tiger　　Ikegami Kyūbee　[seal]

　　To: Netsū Ryūemon-dono
　　To: Kumai Mokubee-dono

The foregoing is confirmed to be without error. Respectfully submitted.

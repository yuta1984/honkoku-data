【Right folio】

10th Day

Item: [Someone from] Ashizawa ■ wishes to set up a weir (簗打ち); a notification has been submitted by Tōemon of the village authority (gōson-kata).
Same [day]

Item: Teisuke of Tahara Village — weir installation.
　The above notifications have both been received.

11th Day

Item: Firewood — 48 [units], 2 ken [in length].
　The above pertains to the inspection and measurement duties at Katsuma Kawara.
Same [day]

Item: Today, at Benzai, Yagawara, and Shiba Kawara, an official inspection of the weirs was conducted. Three members of the official party, along with the two of us [the writer and a colleague], proceeded there with Kyūbei in attendance. [We were] ordered to make preparations for construction. Accordingly, Gonzaemon handled all directions and the matter was settled. The two of us [sharing] the same duties were instructed to consult with each other and appear together as arranged.

11th Day

Item: Being summoned by Jōzaemon, I attended, and was informed as follows: From Rokurōemon's side, there is a notification regarding the procurement of timber from the valley of Kajiki-iri, and its downstream conveyance. Furthermore, there is a notification from Taemon of Ichinose Village regarding the Yashimo River. [These matters] were processed. I, serving as the duty officer (yōban), reported that I had not received any such notifications of this kind whatsoever. However, it came to light that a notification had been submitted to colleagues of the same post the previous month, which I had not been made aware of — an inconsistency. [I was instructed] to submit [a document accordingly]. I therefore made inquiries with the internal office staff (uchi-kata), and [was informed that] during the current spring, [a person from] Kuiki and Iriya had come and negotiated, saying they wished to procure [timber from the valley].

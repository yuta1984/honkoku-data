1st day of the month
One item: Regarding Isuke, as his illness had somewhat subsided, he went out to the Yama■ River today, but reported that no fish were caught.

Same day
One item: Regarding the transport tax (unjo) on hemp fiber and hemp cloth, as there was an official instruction to submit a written report, word was sent to colleagues in the same role to inquire of the contractors and then submit a written statement. Accordingly, Kyūbei sent word today for Ōmiya Zenzō to come to this residence. Zenzō appeared promptly, and upon being asked, he submitted the above-mentioned written statement.

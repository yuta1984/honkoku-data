One item: Regarding the transport levy (unzōsen) on hemp cloth and hemp fiber,
　there was an instruction that a written report be submitted.
　As my colleague in the same office sent word that the matter should be
　inquired of the contracted parties (ukeoi-mono) and a written report submitted,
　I sent word via Kyū(bei) to the neighboring villages to come to the office today.
　As Haru(某) promptly came out repeatedly,
　I made inquiries, and thereupon the said written report was submitted.

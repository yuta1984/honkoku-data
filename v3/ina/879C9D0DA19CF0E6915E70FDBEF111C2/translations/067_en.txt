　7th Month, 5th Day　　Kamiya Kumezaemon
　To: Lord Satō Kyūzaemon

5th Day
Item: Regarding the request to report the weir (yana) matter at Kōmi in Kamishindenthe details have again been conveyed to Zen'nai, and he has been instructed to make payment at his own convenience as he sees fit.

6th Day
Item: The two net-rope workers have been received [into service] as of today.

6th Day
Item: Regarding the lacquer advance ■■ — for the interest payment, Kyūbei and two attendants (chūgen) each appeared [to attend to the matter].

7th Day
Item: For the Tanabata Festival greetings, [we] departed from Nakabuchi Village at or after the Hour of the Dragon (around 8 a.m.), and paid our respects to the Senior Councillors (karō), Elders (otoshiyori), and Officials (goyōnin). At the Karu Castle, Lord Jōzaemon conveyed instructions to the effect that: regarding the weir construction (o-tsuki) this time, as I [Jōzaemon] am absent, ■■ Lord Etsuzaemon ■■ has been directed to see that the weir business is brought to completion. We humbly acknowledged these instructions and so reported.

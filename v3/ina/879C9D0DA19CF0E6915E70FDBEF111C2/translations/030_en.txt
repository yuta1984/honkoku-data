March 17th

One　　Memorandum

　　Three to of rice　　For two persons' stipend ration

　　【Seal impression】

The above is hereby received as stipend rice (fuchi-mai) from the second month of the Year of the Tiger. Accordingly, this is as stated.
　　Third month, Year of the Ne (Rat)　　Ikeue Kyūbei　　[seal]
　　To: Nezu Ryūemon-dono
　　To: Kumai Mokubei-dono

The above is as written, with no discrepancy. End.
　　　　　　　Kyūzaemon

The above voucher (tegata) had not been sent out when the rice was received, and so the voucher for the current month has now been drawn up and dispatched.

19th day
One, Zenshichiemon of Kurosawa village — notification was conveyed that his petition to operate as a dyer (kōya) has been acknowledged and approved.

Same day
One, Kyūbei came to report that the storehouse for items held in custody at the kaisho (administrative office) had collapsed [■]. It was decided that repairs would be carried out on the 20th, an official business day.

　　14th day, 5th month　　Kyūzaemon

One item: The application regarding the fish weir (yana) on the river below ■-Kozaka at the Benzaiten River was received and duly noted by Shimabata Tōzō.

Same, 17th Day

One item: A letter received from Jōzaemon-dono, the contents of which are as follows:

　I write to you by letter. It is requested by the investigative office (gimmigata) that both net-fishermen (ami-uchi) come out every day. The purpose of this request is that there is a shortage of sea bream (tai) used during the repair ceremonies. However, I believe it would be difficult to dispatch both men on the same day every day, as there would be no time for preparation and rest. Therefore, I think it best to send [them] alternately, two persons at a time. As there are no pre-departure festivals (o-hatsuga mae no matsuri), please instruct them to come out [day and] night and apply themselves diligently to their duties. Respectfully submitted.

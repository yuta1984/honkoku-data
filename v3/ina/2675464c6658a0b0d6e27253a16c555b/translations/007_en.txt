First Generation Re-appointment
1. Kansei 11, 10th month, 1st day　　　Kido Jūzō
　　Appointed as district magistrate
　　Awarded a stipend of 20 koku

1. Kyōwa 2, 5th month, 5th day　　　　Yano Tōzaemon
　　　Appointed as district magistrate and awarded a stipend of 20 koku
　　　Bunka 1, 4th month, 5th day - relieved of duties

1. Bunka 1, 4th month, 5th day　　　　Asari Rikita
　　Appointed as district magistrate and awarded a stipend of 20 koku (later renamed Hikoemon, also known as Heitayū Yorimasa)
　　Bunka 7 - appointed to financial administration oversight
　　Bunka 9, 1st month, 15th day - appointed to goyōyaku
　　with concurrent district magistrate duties
　　Bunka 10, 11th month, 3rd day - appointed to elder councilor position

1. Bunka 3, 4th month, 24th day　　　Hoshino Jōbu
　　Appointed as district magistrate and awarded a stipend of 20 koku
　　Bunka 5, 7th month - relieved of duties

1. Bunka 3, 4th month, 24th day　　　Haruyama Sagoemon
　　Appointed as district magistrate and awarded a stipend of 20 koku
　　Bunka 7, 5th month - appointed to financial administration oversight
　　Bunka 9, 1st month, 15th day - awarded an increase of 10 koku
　　Bunpō 1, 8th month, 23rd day - due to illness,
　　as requested, relieved of duties and appointed as messenger officer

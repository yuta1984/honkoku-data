1. Kyōhō 9 (1724), 1st month, 15th day　　　Yamagata Gozaemon
   Appointed as district magistrate and granted a stipend of 20 koku
   Same 14th year (1729), 6th month, 22nd day, appointed as banner magistrate (ohata-bugyō)

1. Kyōhō 14 (1729), 6th month, 22nd day　　Abiko Heizaemon
   Appointed as district magistrate and granted a stipend of 20 koku
   Same 20th year (1735), 1st month, 22nd day, dismissed upon request

1. Kyōhō 16 (1731), 7th month, 11th day　　Andō Tarōbee
   Appointed as district magistrate
   Genbun 2 (1737), 12th month, 1st day
   Ordered to concurrently serve as special official (goyō-yaku) and district magistrate
   Enkyō 1 (1744), 5th month, 13th day, dismissed from concurrent service as district magistrate

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　First generation
1. Genbun 2 (1737), 7th month, 18th day　　　Miura Dōemon
   Appointed as district magistrate
   Same year, 9th month, 9th day [day possibly missing] granted stipend
   Enkyō 2 (1745), 9th month, due to serving alone (hitori-yaku)
   stipend increased to 30 koku
   Kan'en 2 (1749), 9th month, 22nd day, ordered to concurrently serve as special official and district magistrate
   Hōreki 12 (1762), 9th month, last day, died of illness

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　First generation
1. Hōreki 2 (1752), 10th month, 13th day　　　Sakamoto Un'shirō
   Appointed as district magistrate and granted a stipend of 20 koku
   Same 9th year (1759), 3rd month, 29th day, stipend increased to 30 koku
   Same 11th year (1761), 10th month, 19th day, ordered to concurrently serve as special official and district magistrate
   Meiwa 4 (1767), 4th month, 21st day, dismissed from both positions upon request and appointed to the rank of senior inspector (ōmetsuke-kaku)

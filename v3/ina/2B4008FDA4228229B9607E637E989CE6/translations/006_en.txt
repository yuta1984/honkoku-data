I am an incompetent person, but for several decades I have received your generous benevolence and have been employed most excellently by you. Furthermore, I have gradually received additional rice stipends and other benefits, for which I am extremely grateful and consider myself most fortunate. Given this, I wish to be of service and assistance to you in any way possible. I have been hoping to fulfill my duties faithfully when I fell ill with a cold and sickness. Despite receiving various medical treatments through your gracious influence, this appears to be a serious illness, and I fear my chances of survival are uncertain. Should I pass away, I humbly request that through your continued mercy, my son Iheiji will not fall into destitution.

I respectfully ask that you convey the above matter favorably to all those in authority.

First month of Bunka 14, Year of the Ox (1817)　Hayashi Kaheiji
To Lord Komatsu Sataemon
To Lord Iwasaki Genzo

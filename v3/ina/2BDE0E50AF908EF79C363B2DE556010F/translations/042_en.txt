1. The arrival at Imaichi station was reported to Nikkō mountain according to precedent.

　　　　19th day　Cloudy weather, rain from nine o'clock
1. This dawn, departed Imaichi station after eight-thirty.
1. Arrived at Hachiishi after seven-thirty at dawn.
1. With preparations complete, I sent messengers to notify the gate master, Nikkō magistrate, temples, etc. of my arrival, wore noshime hakama, and waited for visitors to come.
1. The lodging temple Shōson-in came to the reception room, served food, met in the small side room, requested assistance with various matters, and also met with the temple deputy for light greetings.
　　　However, Shōson-in stayed directly at the honjin, handled the reception of visitors, and after finishing and serving the meal, when departing, met again in the small side room for greetings.
　　　1. Although I sent notice of arrival to the mountain, in recent years the Nikkō magistrate and others have been coming from five o'clock, and also
　　　　for viewing the interior of the shrine and mausoleum, in recent years one can visit from nine o'clock at one's convenience, and there was discussion with Shōkō-in.

1. The shrine administrator Dairaku-in came and served sweets and soup, we dined together and met, and when he inquired after my well-being, I responded that I was in good health, and when he departed I escorted him to the next room.
　　　　However, having been appointed as deputy, my personal worship at the shrine was completed as planned, which is most grateful. Regarding this, I requested assistance during the worship ceremony and expressed my desire to conduct an interior inspection today.

1. The Shugaku-in Gon-sōjō, Ryūkō-in, and Iō-in representing the entire mountain came and served sweets and soup, we dined together and met, and when they inquired after my well-being, I responded that I was in good health. When they departed, I escorted Shugaku-in to the ceremonial entrance and Ryūkō-in and Iō-in to the next room.
　　　　However, to Ryūkō-in, regarding being appointed as deputy this time, my personal worship at the mausoleum was completed as planned, which is most grateful. Regarding this, I requested assistance both during the deputy ceremony and personal worship, expressed my desire to conduct an interior inspection today, and inquired about the timing for tomorrow morning.

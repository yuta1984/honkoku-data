【Two hanging tags】
【First tag】
　　Same year, third month
1. Regarding the matter of audience with the gate master, I dispatched someone to the estate manager and made application through the temple lodging Engaku-in.

【Second tag】
1. To those among the high-ranking retainers who conducted rehearsal procedures for us, I sent formal messengers after returning home as a courtesy.
　　　　Same year, fourth month
1. Similarly, with polite declining, I exchanged formal messengers with Lord Nakajo and Lord Yokose, sending fish offerings.

　　October 4th
- This morning, departed for Nikkō after six o'clock (wearing the bestowed haori jacket)
- At the time of departure, an attendant messenger came from the substitute Oki, but following precedent, I departed without meeting him, sending word of my departure
- During a brief rest at Senju post station, a messenger was sent from the same substitute, so I sent an appropriate reply, but also sent word through a messenger that I had arrived at this station and would depart immediately
- A messenger was sent to the same station from Lord Echizen, a disciple of Master Shuri, so I sent appropriate greetings in return
- Only calculated for the mountain ascent portion, did not meet with attendant messengers or hospitality personnel along the way
- Around half past five in the evening, arrived and lodged at Satte post station

　　　　October 5th
- At seven o'clock at dawn, departed from Satte
- Completed crossing the Tone River by ferry before half past six and sent notice to Oki. At the same location, hospitality boats and officials were provided by Lord Doi Ōi-no-kami
- Completed passage through Nakata checkpoint, and from that station to Koga station, Nogi station, and Tomonuma village, officials were provided by the same person
- At Kurihashi village, a messenger was sent from Lord Mizuno Hyūga-no-kami, and officials were also provided

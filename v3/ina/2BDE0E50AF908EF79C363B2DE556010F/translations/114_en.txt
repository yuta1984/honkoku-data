　　　　　4th day
1. Nothing unusual occurred
　　　　　5th day
1. Rose after six o'clock
1. Left residence at five o'clock. For the courtesy visits regarding this occasion, I visited Matsudaira Etchū-no-kami-dono, Matsudaira Izumi-no-kami-dono, and Honda Danjō-daihitsu-dono before going to the castle《wearing silk unlined garments with hemp formal wear》
　　　　However, with Etchū-no-kami-dono, since there was precedent, I stayed close and expressed my gratitude. With Izumi-no-kami-dono, since there was no precedent, I directly expressed my gratitude. With Danjō-daihitsu-dono, since it was close to castle attendance time, the retainer informed me that the meeting was declined, so I expressed my gratitude to the retainer
1. Since it was a duty day, I changed clothes at Izumi-no-kami-dono's residence and attended the castle in tsugi-kamishimo. As requested yesterday, Nakajō Yamashiro-no-kami appeared, so before the assembly, we practiced the ceremony for the formal audience regarding leave and return to Edo at the White Shoin
　　　　6th day
1. Nothing unusual occurred [character unclear]
　　　　7th day
1. After eight o'clock, a joint official letter arrived from Matsudaira Izumi-no-kami-dono, who was on duty. As follows:
　　　　　Tomorrow you will be granted leave to go to Nikko, so please
　　　　　attend the castle at half past six
　　　　　That is all

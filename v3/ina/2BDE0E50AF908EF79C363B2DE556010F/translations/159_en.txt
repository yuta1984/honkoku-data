Same day, 24th
1. Today, when the details of Lord Naitō Notono-kami's return audience were properly reported, a messenger came informing us that he received gracious words from his lord and was most grateful.
1. Went to Lord Naitō Notono-kami's residence after 8 o'clock as congratulations for the above matter, had a conversation, and returned home after 6 o'clock.

　　　Same day, 26th
1. Sent a messenger with one box of fish as congratulations for Lord Naitō Notono-kami's successful completion of his duties this time. From their side as well, one box of fish came as a return gift.

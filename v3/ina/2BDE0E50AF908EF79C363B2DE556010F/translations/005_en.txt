As follows:

　　　Nikko twentieth day
　Half
　Cut　Deputy　　Naito Yamato-no-kami
　　　　　　Substitute　
　　　　　　　　Kuchiki Oki-no-kami
　　　The above was so ordered
1. The above written order was conveyed verbally to the estate manager, and he took it back with him first.
1. Near the veranda of the Kiku-no-ma (Chrysanthemum Room), Lord Yokose Suruga and Lord Maeda Shinano-no-kami were present, so I myself

　spread the word and informed them. From them as well, on the 17th day,
　there was announcement that I had been appointed as deputy, and moreover
　regarding the rehearsal procedures for conversations in the audience chamber, though it was sudden, they offered greetings saying they would arrange among themselves to conduct practice for me.
　　　However, I also met with Lord Nakajo Kawachi-no-kami of the high-ranking retainers, so I made the same request of him.
1. Before long I went to the White Shoin, and both myself and Oki went similarly, practicing both the conversation procedures and the departure procedures. Immediately after, I withdrew together with Oki.
1. I also went to the Western Castle to pay respects, had an audience with the duty officer of ceremonial announcers, Lord Morikawa Kii-no-kami, in the Kari-no-ma (Wild Geese Room). Both myself and Oki appeared together, completed the procedures, and withdrew. For paying respects, I made rounds to the senior councilors and junior councilors of both castles,

　returning home at eight o'clock.
1. From Lord Abe Bitchu-no-kami, before half past eight, my estate manager received a summons, and the notification was as follows:

　　　Nikko　　　　　　　　Naito Yamato-no-kami
　　　Having been appointed as deputy,
　　　you are excused from
　　　Outer Sakurada Gate guard duty
1. Regarding the above, I immediately went to Lord Abe Bitchu-no-kami to pay respects.
1. Today, having been appointed as
　Nikko deputy, I informed my colleagues as usual.
1. Having been excused from guard post duty, same as above.
1. Today in the palace, I sent one copy of the written order given by Lord Bitchu-no-kami to Lord Oki-no-kami.
　　　　　Same month, 4th day
1. I sent my estate manager to the duty officer Lord Abe Bitchu-no-kami to request that I be allowed to appear for a formal meeting, and received acknowledgment.
　　　　　Same month, 5th day
1. Wearing court dress with half-hakama, I departed after half past seven and went to the duty officer Lord Abe Bitchu-no-kami.
1. The steward called me out, and I stated that I had come to pay respects having been appointed as Nikko deputy on the twentieth day. Before long, Lord Bitchu came out to the guest reception room, the steward opened the sliding doors, I came forward to the threshold of the guest area, announced my name, and came forward. I expressed my gratitude for being appointed as Nikko deputy on the twentieth day, and regarding this matter, I submitted a written inquiry. Being in an outer wrapper, I submitted two documents together. Lord Bitchu examined them, there was a brief acknowledgment, and I immediately withdrew.

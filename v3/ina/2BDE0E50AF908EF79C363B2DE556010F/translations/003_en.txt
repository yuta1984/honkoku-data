1. I departed my residence at the fifth hour and half, wearing formal silk jacket and hakama, and proceeded to the castle. Since I was summoned for official business, the Inspector sent notice through a monk attendant, which Tsugita Yozaemon acknowledged and reported back.
Kuchiki Oki-no-kami and Ooka Shuzen also received similar summons for official business and arrived at the castle shortly after, so the three of us conferred and proceeded to the Fuyo Room, where we agreed upon the proper procedures for receiving instructions.

1. Since the Senior Councilors had arrived at the castle, today's attendants - Inaba Tsushima-no-kami, Ishikawa Nakatsukasa, myself, Oki-no-kami, and Shuzen - all appeared in the assembly room. Shortly after, Inspector Okusa Shuzen approached and showed us a written notice. We took our seats behind the four-panel screens in the Kari Room - myself, Oki-no-kami, and Shuzen - read through the notice, and sent word that we had understood its contents.

　　Written Notice　　Fuyo Room
　　　　　　　　　　Naito Yamato-no-kami
　　　　　　　　　　Kuchiki Oki-no-kami
　　　　The above two shall appear together

1. During the rounds, behind the four-panel screens in the Kari Room, following the high-ranking retainers Yokose Suruga-dono and Maeda Shinano-dono who were summoned for official business, myself, Oki-no-kami, and Shuzen took our seats.
　　However, fans were placed behind the same location.

1. The attendants on duty inquired about His Lordship's condition, and after this was completed, the Senior Councilors took their seats in the Fuyo Room. The Grand Inspector, with proper courtesy, had the two high-ranking retainers Yokose Suruga-dono and Maeda Shinano-no-kami come forward together, and after their instructions were completed, the Grand Inspector, with proper courtesy, had myself and Oki-no-kami come forward together and take our seats as shown in the diagram.
The Senior Councilor on duty, Lord Abe Bitchu-no-kami, announced: "For the Nikko twentieth-day proxy representation, Yamato-no-kami is appointed as proxy representative, and Oki-no-kami as substitute." He then handed over an official document. I came forward to receive it, returned to my original lower seat, examined it, then passed it to Oki-no-kami. He examined it and returned it to me. After we both reviewed it, I placed it beside me and expressed our grateful acceptance, then held the document in my hand and returned it to Lord Bitchu-no-kami. The document read as follows:

　　　　Nikko twentieth day
　Half-sheet　Proxy representative　Naito Yamato-no-kami
　　　　　　　Substitute　　　　　Kuchiki Oki-no-kami
　　　　The above appointments are hereby made

1. The above official document was given to the estate manager to be reported, and he took it back first.

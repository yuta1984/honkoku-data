September 26th
Item: Regarding the matter of audience with the Gate Master, we sent the following message through the caretaker to the lodging temple Tozen-in:
　　　　Concerning the matter of Bunzen-no-kami this time
　　　　Due to the change of reign, he has been appointed
　　　　as deputy for the Nikko Mausoleum
　　　　We humbly wish to have an audience when the Gate Master is present, regarding this matter
　　　　We earnestly request your kind mediation

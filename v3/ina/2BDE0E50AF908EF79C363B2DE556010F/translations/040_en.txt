To Lord Ōkubo Naizen-no-shō
1. Lord Ōta Bitchū-no-kami did not come to the castle due to illness and was excluded from the list.

　　　　16th day　Clear weather
1. This morning after six o'clock, departed for Nikkō <<wearing the bestowed crested haori>>
1. At departure, I did not respond directly to messengers from close relatives due to purification matters, but only responded directly to the messenger from Lord Matsudaira Kii-no-kami who was serving as my replacement.
1. I sent word to the hosts at various places and those who would bow in lower positions that I would not exchange greetings due to purification.
1. Since I departed for Nikkō this morning, I sent messengers to the Senior Councilors, Junior Councilors, and Chamberlains, as well as to Lord Matsudaira Kii-no-kami who was my replacement, and sent letters to the Chief Inspectors, Inspectors, and trusted associates.
1. An escort came from Lord Matsudaira Kii-no-kami to Senju post station. From our side as well, immediately upon arriving at Senju post station, I sent a handwritten letter stating that we would depart from that station.
1. Rested for lunch at Koshigaya, arriving at nine o'clock.
1. Stayed overnight at Satte post station, arriving after seven-thirty.

　　　　17th day　Cloudy weather
1. Departed Satte post station after six o'clock.
1. At the Nakata purchasing office as well, I sent word declining and did not exchange greetings.
1. The lord of Koga castle, Doi Ōi-no-kami, sent a hospitality boat to the Bōgawa river and officials also came, and retainers were sent out in the castle town as well, but I sent word declining and did not exchange greetings.
1. Since today's crossing of the Bōgawa river was completed without delay, I sent a courier to Edo and notified Lord Matsudaira Kii-no-kami who was my replacement.
1. Rested for lunch at Mamada, arriving around nine o'clock.
1. During that rest, the Imperial Envoy Lord Ōgimachi Saisho-chūjō passed by.

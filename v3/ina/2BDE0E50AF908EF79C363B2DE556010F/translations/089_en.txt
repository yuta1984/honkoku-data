One: The two deputy administrators (medai) came and refreshments were served. Afterward, they showed the draft of tomorrow's formal report to the caretaker official, and after this was completed, they inquired about his well-being, but no response was given to this. Appropriate greetings were exchanged. Then, as the message from the senior councilors in Edo, they conveyed that "His Lordship is in increasingly good health" and instructed that this be communicated to Mino-no-kami.
When the two men reported that "the sacred mountain is secure," the administrative officer brought out a document box as it was, placed it on the lid, and handed it to me, so I instructed that it be communicated to Mino-no-kami and handed it over to the two men. Regarding the gratitude for being appointed as representative due to this succession, and after this, my own worship at the mausoleum was also arranged and completed. This too was most grateful. Later, I requested to view the interior of the mausoleum, and this was completed, then I entered the kitchen area.

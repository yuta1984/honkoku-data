[The order] was issued, so [I should] go to the castle at 5:30 AM. A formal letter from the senior councilors arrived, and I sent a reply acknowledging receipt of the notification.

Same day, the 10th
One item: Naitō Noto-no-kami sent a messenger today to inform that permission to depart for Nikkō had been granted, that he had received the lord's gracious instructions and gifts, for which he was grateful. I sent an appropriate reply expressing congratulations. However, he inquired whether the formal letter would be delivered on the 15th and departure should be made on the 16th. I sent replies acknowledging all matters. I sent a messenger from here to express congratulations.

Same day, the 14th
One item: I sent a messenger to Naitō Noto-no-kami to bid farewell as he departs the day after tomorrow.

Same day, the 15th
One item: Naitō Noto-no-kami sent word via messenger that he went to the castle today, received the formal letter for the Nikkō magistrate, and would depart tomorrow morning.

One item: The attendants, as it is the monthly period, may go to the castle without concern. After withdrawal, purification begins.

One item: After withdrawal, purification for all quarters. The retainers begin purification from this morning.

One item: From today's purification until it is lifted tomorrow morning, both official and private [matters are restricted].

　　　　Same day, 17th
- Although ordered to stand by for Nikko duty, today
  there was a procession for an official visit following precedent, so I participated and performed my duties.
　　　　Same day, 18th  
- A letter from Naito Notono-kami's caretaker informed us that yesterday, the 17th,
  he safely crossed the Fusagawa River by ferry.
　　　　Same day, 22nd
- A letter from Naito Notono-kami at Nikko reported that the ceremonial worship as imperial representative was
  performed on the 20th, and he departed immediately afterward.
  Since he would arrive soon, no reply letter was sent.
　
　　　Same day, 23rd
- A messenger came from Naito Notono-kami announcing that he had successfully completed his duties at Nikko and had just returned to Edo.
  We also sent a messenger from our side.
　　　Same day, 24th  
- Today, regarding Naito Notono-kami's return to Edo,
  when the results of his audience with the lord were first reported, we received word through a messenger that he was grateful to have received the lord's approval.

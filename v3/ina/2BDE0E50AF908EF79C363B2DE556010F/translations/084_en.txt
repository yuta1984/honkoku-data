- At dawn, 7 o'clock, departed from Ishibashi station

　matters, they did not appear
- After 4 o'clock, arrived at Kami-Tokujirō station for lunch break
- At Ōsawa station, two dōshin from the Nikkō magistrate's group were on duty
- At the same station, two palace guards were on duty
- At Imaichi station, two dōshin from the Nikkō magistrate's group were on duty
- Before 7 o'clock, arrived and lodged at the same station

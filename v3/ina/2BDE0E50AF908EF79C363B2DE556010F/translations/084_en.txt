- Completed crossing the Tone River by ferry before half past six and sent notice to Oki. At the same location, hospitality boats and officials were provided by Lord Doi Ōi-no-kami
- Completed passage through Nakata checkpoint, and from that station to Koga station, Nogi station, and Tomonuma village, officials were provided by the same person
- At Kurihashi village, a messenger was sent from Lord Mizuno Hyūga-no-kami, and officials were also provided
- Arrived at Mamada post station for lunch break after nine o'clock
- Officials were to be sent to the same station by Toda Kuninosuke, but there was a refusal due to inconvenient circumstances
- Officials were sent to Sendazuka village by Lord Yamato-no-kami
- Retainers were sent to Koganei station by Lord Hotta Bitchū-no-kami
- Arrived and lodged at Ishibashi station after six o'clock in the evening

　　　　October 6th
- Departed Ishibashi station at seven o'clock at dawn
- At Utsunomiya, officials were to be sent by Toda Nanosuke, but they did not come due to inconvenient circumstances
- Arrived at Kami-Tokujirō station for lunch break after four o'clock
- At Ōsawa station, two dōshin from the Nikkō magistrate's group were on duty
- At the same station, two palace guards were on duty
- At Imaichi station, two dōshin from the Nikkō magistrate's group were on duty
- Arrived and lodged at the same station before seven o'clock

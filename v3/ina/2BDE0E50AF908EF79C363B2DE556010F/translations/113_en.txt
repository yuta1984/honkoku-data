　　　　　Regarding the ceremonial worship I will perform as

　　　　　should I wear formal court dress (daimon) following precedent?
　　　　　After serving as representative

　　　　　I wish to personally worship at the shrine and mausoleum
　　　　　I humbly inquire about the above. That is all.
　　　　　　April 3rd　　　　　Tsuchiya Tajima-no-kami

　　　　　I will depart from here on the coming 16th, arrive at Nikko on the 19th,
　　　　　serve as representative on the 20th, and return to Edo on the 23rd.
　　　　　This is the established precedent.
　　　　　Should I understand that this time will also follow the same pattern? I humbly inquire.
　　　　　That is all.
　　　　　　　April 3rd　　　Tsuchiya Tajima-no-kami

1. At the Nakata barrier station as well, I sent word declining and did not exchange greetings.
1. The lord of Koga castle, Doi Ōi-no-kami, sent a hospitality boat to the Bōgawa river and officials also came, and retainers were sent out in the castle town as well, but I sent word declining and did not exchange greetings.
1. Since today's crossing of the Bōgawa river was completed without delay, I sent a letter by courier to Edo and notified Lord Matsudaira Kii-no-kami who was my replacement.
1. Rested for lunch at Mamada, arriving around nine o'clock.
1. During that rest, the Imperial Envoy Lord Ōgimachi Saisho-chūjō passed by.
1. From Lord Toda Noto-no-kami, advance clearers went out to Oyama post station.
1. To Oyama post station, a messenger came from Toriι Tanba-no-kami, lord of Mibu castle, to inquire about my well-being during the journey, but I did not exchange greetings due to purification.
1. Stayed overnight at Ishibashi post station, arriving after seven-thirty.

　　　　18th day　Rain, clear weather from after noon
1. Departed Ishibashi post station after seven-thirty.
1. Before departing Ishibashi post station, I was told that the deputy Lord Toda Bingo-no-kami, festival magistrate Lord Nagai Shinano-no-kami, and Niwa Shikibu-shōyū would be passing through one after another, but I did not encounter them on the route.
1. Rested for lunch at Kami-Tokujirō post station, arriving before nine o'clock.
1. From Lord Toda Noto-no-kami, lord of Utsunomiya castle, word came that he would send out retainers when I passed through the castle town, but as he was in mourning, he would refrain from meeting, and a messenger was sent during the lunch break.
1. Stayed overnight at Imaichi, arriving after seven o'clock.
1. The Nikkō palace guards and local magistrate's assistants were stationed at various places, but I did not exchange greetings.
1. I sent word to Mount Nikkō about my arrival at Imaichi post station, following previous precedent.

　　　19th day　Cloudy weather, rain from nine o'clock
1. This dawn, departed Imaichi post station after eight-thirty.
1. Arrived at Hachiishi after seven-thirty at dawn.
1. With preparations complete, I sent an announcement messenger to the temple head, Nikkō magistrate, temples, and others regarding my arrival, put on noshi-me half-hakama, and waited for visitors.

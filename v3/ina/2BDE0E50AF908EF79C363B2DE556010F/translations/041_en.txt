1. At the Nakata checkpoint as well, I sent word declining and did not exchange greetings.
1. The lord of Koga castle, Doi Ōi-no-kami, sent a hospitality boat to the Bōgawa river and officials also came, and retainers were sent out in the castle town as well, but I sent word declining and did not exchange greetings.
1. Since today's crossing of the Bōgawa river was completed without delay, I sent a letter by courier to Edo and notified Lord Matsudaira Kii-no-kami who was my replacement.
1. Rested for lunch at Mamada, arriving around nine o'clock.
1. During that rest, the Imperial Envoy Lord Ōgimachi Saisho-chūjō passed by.
1. Advance guards were sent out from Lord Toda Noto-no-kami to Oyama post station.
1. A messenger came to Oyama post station from the lord of Mibu castle, Torii Tamba-no-kami, to inquire about my well-being during the journey, but I did not exchange greetings due to purification.
1. Stayed overnight at Ishibashi station, arriving after seven-thirty.

　　　　18th day　Rain, clear weather from after noon
1. Departed Ishibashi station after seven-thirty.
1. Before departing Ishibashi station, it was reported that the deputy Toda Bingo-no-kami, festival magistrate Lord Nagai Shinano-no-kami, and Niwa Shikibu-shōyū would be passing through successively, but we did not encounter them on the road.
1. Rested for lunch at Kami-Tokujirou station, arriving before nine o'clock.
1. From the lord of Utsunomiya castle, Lord Toda Noto-no-kami, retainers were to be sent out when passing through the castle town, but he sent word that he would refrain from meeting due to being in mourning, and a messenger was sent during the lunch break.
1. Stayed overnight at Imaichi, arriving after seven o'clock.
1. The Nikkō palace guards and local yoriki were stationed at various places, but I did not exchange greetings.

1. The arrival at Imaichi station was reported to Nikkō mountain according to precedent.

　　　　19th day　Cloudy weather, rain from nine o'clock　
1. This dawn, departed Imaichi station after eight-thirty.
1. Arrived at Hachiishi after seven-thirty at dawn.
1. With preparations complete, I sent messengers to notify the gate master, Nikkō magistrate, temples, etc. of my arrival, wore noshime hakama, and waited for visitors to come.

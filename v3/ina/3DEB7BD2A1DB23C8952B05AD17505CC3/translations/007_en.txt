【This line in red ink】June 23rd
　Regarding this matter of enforcement measures, an official announcement was
　made to the public offices, and therefore it should also be
　communicated to the members of the inner palace
　By the lord's intention, at the main castle, Shirasu Kai-no-kami
　conveyed this message, and it should also be communicated to the inner palace of the western castle
　By the shogun's intention, Hiraoka Tsushima-no-kami
　communicated this to the inner palace of the western castle, and for the sake of understanding
　it was mentioned that this had been discussed, and the written record on the left was shown for review
　After examination, it is returned

　　Lacquer planting (pencil writing)
　　　　Written pledge respectfully submitted　　　　　[Seal] ("Iwasaki Collection")
　One item: Regarding Nakasokura Village's rope surveyor Matasaemon and Motosokura Village's Togenin Temple,
　　we have been ordered to plant lacquer trees in Taruno-sawa within Matsuba-dō,
　　which is common land between both villages. We have discussed that both parties will
　　clear the land proportionally, then plant lacquer trees and
　　submit them to the authorities. Having made this agreement, we will not
　　handle matters selfishly in the future, and will work diligently
　　in mutual cooperation. For future reference, thus as written.
　　　　　　　　　　　Motosokura Village

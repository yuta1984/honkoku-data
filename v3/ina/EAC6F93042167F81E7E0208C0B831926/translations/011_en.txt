Humbly submitting this written petition
One, regarding our situation: For years we have made our living by cutting and delivering firewood, receiving your generous favor,
　which we consider a most grateful blessing. Since the year before last (Mi year) until now, you have granted us higher prices,
　however, gradually the mountains have become depleted and we must work in distant locations,
　requiring excessive labor for cutting and delivery. Therefore, from this Child year through the Dragon
　year, for a period of five years, we petition that you
　purchase at the same prices as before.
　If our petition is granted as requested,
　we will exert ourselves in every way, cutting abundantly,
　and all workers will coordinate to cut and
　deliver to fulfill official needs without shortfall. We humbly request that with your compassion
　this be granted, which would be a most grateful blessing.

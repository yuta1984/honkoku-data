　　　　March　　Lord Hikoemon　　Makita
1st day
- There was notification that Tobei's wife gave birth at dawn and would observe blood defilement retreat.
- Since Genji of Ichinose Village and Tameuemon of Nakao Village have become elderly and find it difficult to serve as mountain inspectors, a petition for their dismissal was submitted with joint seals from village officials and an endorsement by Kurokawachi Zen'emon. The two copies of this petition addressed to the three mountain officials were submitted by Uemon and kept on file.
- Regarding sending Yosoji and Kashiwagi Genji tomorrow on the 2nd to inspect the official forest in Haruchika district, we ordered the village to make arrangements, and received notification that Yasuemon would accompany them.
2nd day
- We received notification that Uemon and Ryozo completed the delivery of well channel timber and tools to Omino Village, Yamadera Village, Nishimachi Village, and Koide Village and returned.

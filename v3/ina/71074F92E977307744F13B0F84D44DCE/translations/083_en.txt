【Right page】
　　If possible, greetings should be made to report on the matter of imperial forest logging, as instructed by the duty officer.
　　Accordingly, the two aforementioned persons were called and this matter was conveyed to them.
19th day
1. Since the two aforementioned persons would depart immediately tomorrow for purchasing temple and shrine timber, a request was made to send Yosoji along
　　as an escort. While this was acknowledged and conveyed, since the intent differed from the duty officer's instructions,
　　after consulting with colleagues, it was difficult to decide what should be done, so an inquiry was made.
　　The duty officer also considered the matter and stated that instructions should have been given yesterday.
20th day
1. The duty officer ordered that all matters concerning woodcutters this time should be discussed and arranged with Kato Hachizaemon.
　　If that is the case, regarding the matter of assigning Soji that was inquired about yesterday,
　　when asked what should be done after discussion, it was instructed to understand it as such. Therefore,
　　after discussing with Hachizaemon, arrangements were made through Yosoji for the woodcutters, and they were instructed to proceed at their discretion.

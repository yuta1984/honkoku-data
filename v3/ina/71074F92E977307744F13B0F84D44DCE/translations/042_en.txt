　　　The above concerns the proportional allocation for Kurukawa mountain warehouse replacement in the six villages of Haruchika district (Miyata). We humbly request approval to deliver as acknowledged and heard. The above.
　　　　　　June 20th　　　　　Hayami Tōbē

　　　The above was submitted as an inquiry to the Nishigahara duty officer.

　　then proceeded to Mizoguchi village and ordered the same procedures to be carried out there.

　　from the forest magistrate to the administrators.

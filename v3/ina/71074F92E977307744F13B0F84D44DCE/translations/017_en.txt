　　He conveyed to Ryōzō that the appointment had been made, and after returning home and donning his formal attire (kamishimo), he went out.

1. At half past eight o'clock, Okutani Genzaemon and scribe Nakayama Chūemon came out, completed their oath ceremony, and expressed their gratitude. Ryōzō also attended as the monthly duty officer.

1. Regarding Aemon's courtesy rounds, as previously arranged, he was instructed to make rounds together with the officials and both of us. While the oath tablets could be handled by just the two of us, since it was the same day, he was ordered to make rounds in both capacities, following the precedent set by Ryōzō.

1. After conveying the contents of the written instructions to both men, we went to the duty officer's office to report and express our gratitude. A separate report was prepared regarding the completion of the oath ceremony. The oath document will be reviewed on a subsequent official day.

23rd day
1. Sugizaemon and Aemon reported that they returned last evening. There were no problems during the inspection of Hagino-o.

[Left page]
1. They reported that there were impediments regarding the Nakamura Yoshihara forest crossing, so it was not carried out this time.

1. Gizaemon reported that he went out from midday to deliver tools for additional timber for the Kamiyamada river maintenance construction.

1. There was discussion about keeping three bamboo poles for quarterly rounds needed by Okano Kuraemon's group, and Sugiemon was instructed to have them cut and delivered from Toshigamisaka bamboo grove.

1. Aemon's oath document was submitted to the duty officer for review on that day and was promptly returned.

1. Regarding the timber request from Mineyama Temple, it was submitted to the relevant officials as follows. It was also reported that a similar written request was submitted to the district magistrate. Accordingly, consultation was made with the district magistrate today, and the document was also submitted to the duty officer for review. We were informed that further instructions would follow.

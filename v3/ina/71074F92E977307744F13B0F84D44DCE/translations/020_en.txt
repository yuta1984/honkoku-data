Regarding the boundary of Yamamuro Village forest lands, when we made an inspection the other day and inquired of the village officials and forest guards, none had certain knowledge, and they said they understood the ridge line to be the boundary. However, there were no boundary trees with official marks, so we conducted a thorough investigation and found boundary trees along the ridge with official marks. If this is the case, the boundary would be the reverse of what was previously thought. Nevertheless, we instructed them to conduct a thorough examination throughout the village before reporting. Afterward, village officials who came forward reported that indeed that location should be the boundary. Accordingly, we have informed the mountain officials to establish boundaries in sequence. This matter was also discussed with the county magistrates and reported to the duty officer today.

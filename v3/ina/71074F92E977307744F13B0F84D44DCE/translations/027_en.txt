　　We expressed gratitude for what was received today, conveyed our thanks to the duty officer, and paid our respects to the construction office.
1. The inspectors Shiga Haemon, Kobayashi Sugizaemon, Mizo Ryōzō, and Baba Yasemon returned home from Iri-no-tani today, and we acknowledged their mountain ascent reports.
1. We requested one candle, received it from the accounting office, and accepted it after inquiring about its use during the recent fire incident.

11th day
1. We instructed Kitahara Giemon to patrol the Himochi-yama and Okakoimi-yama (restricted mountain areas). Additionally, during today's mountain ascent, three sawara cypress trees were found to have been cut down at Taki-no-ue. Regarding this matter, we further instructed village officials to be more vigilant, and filed reports without separate written articles.
1. Due to discrepancies in the records of small timber quantities and storage locations, we ordered Kurokawa-uchi Zen'emon to conduct an investigation.

[Left page]
　　He reported that there were no discrepancies as stated in the written records, so we negotiated with the district magistrates regarding business at the town cargo brokers.

16th day
1. We acknowledged the report that Shiga Haemon and others who had ascended the mountain completed their western area inspection and returned. Regarding Baba Yasemon, Ryōzō also reported that he remained due to official firewood measurement duties.
1. Mountain officials Ryōzō and Yo-Sōji went out and reported on the Omokawa inspection as follows. Additionally, Kurokawa-uchi Zen'emon, mountain inspector Maruyama Kyūzaemon, and Hirase Kanzaemon attended. The headman of Ura village, representing the five villages, also attended.
　　　From the foot of Tekake-dake to Sangatsu-ishi along the Omokawa Nishi-tani route, the forest stands are extremely good, but the disputed trees are all small timber.

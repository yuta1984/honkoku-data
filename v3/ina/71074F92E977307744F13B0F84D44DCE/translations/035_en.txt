[Right page]
　　　　　　6th month　　Lord Sukedayū　　Makita

1st day
1. There was notice that Tōbei had returned from Haruchika village, and Uemon, Yasuemon, and Yosōji also reported their return. It was reported that Giemon and Ryōzō were directly assigned official duties from Miyata and dispatched to Nakazawa village.

2nd day
1. It was reported that Giemon and Ryōzō completed their official duties and returned.

3rd day
1. Regarding the ferry boat wooden equipment from Ōkubo village, it was reported that these were handed over to the two aforementioned persons, so this was immediately communicated to the duty officer as well.

[Left page]
1. There was a consultation with the construction magistrate office regarding the desire to receive 150 bamboo poles of 3-4 sun circumference. Since the Sotobaba hand bridge would be completed within 2-3 days, there was a consultation about wanting to transport them to that location. However, since it would be difficult to cut from other areas in the nearby government bamboo grove, it was agreed to make do with 100 poles first. This matter was naturally communicated to Sugizaemon.

1. The duty officer ordered that the contents of the official notice be conveyed as follows:
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Mizo Ryōzō
　　　　　　500 mon each　　　Baba Yasuemon  
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Mikoshiba Yūji
　　
　　　　These persons have many duties in their roles, but they worked especially hard and diligently as assistant roles in the firewood department, so reward money is hereby granted. They should continue to work with even greater diligence and attention.

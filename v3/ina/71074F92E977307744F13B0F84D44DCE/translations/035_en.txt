　　　Sixth month　　Nakatayū-sama　　Makita

1st day
1. There was notice that Tōbei had returned from Haruchika village. Regarding the matter of Sugizaemon, Yasuemon, and Yosōji, and furthermore the return notification heard: Emon and Shōzō were directly ordered on official business from Miyata and dispatched to Nakazawa village, as was reported.

2nd day  
1. It was reported that Isoemon and Inzō completed their official duties and returned.
1. From Ōkubo village ferry and wooden bridge, it was reported that the above two persons crossed over, so this was also reported to the duty officer.

[Left page]
1. Regarding timber of 3-4 sun circumference, there was discussion with the construction magistrate about wanting to obtain 150 pieces. Since within the next few days it will become the outer riding ground hand bridge, there were repeated discussions about bringing them to that location. However, since it would be quite difficult to cut [...] [...] [...] from the nearby government grove, it was discussed and decided to make do with 100 pieces first. This matter was also reported to Sugizaemon.

1. The duty officer ordered that the contents of the official notice be conveyed as follows:
　　　　　　　　　Mizō Ryōzō
　　500 mon each in copper coins　　Baba Yasuemon  
　　　　　　　　　Mikoba Yūji
　　
　　These persons, having many official duties, worked particularly hard in their postings as assistant roles in the firewood department. Therefore, reward money was granted. Furthermore, they should continue to serve diligently in their assignments.

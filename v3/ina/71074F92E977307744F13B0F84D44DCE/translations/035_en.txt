【Right Page】
　　　June　　Lord Nakatayū　　Bokuta

1st day
1. There was notice that Tōbei had returned from Haruchika village, and Sugi Zaemon, Yasuemon, and Yosōji reported his return. Regarding Uemon and Shōzō being directly assigned official duties from Miyata and dispatched to Nakazawa village, this was reported.

2nd day
1. It was reported that Iso Uemon and Inzō had completed their official duties and returned.
1. Regarding the ferry boat at Ōkubo village, it was reported that these two men had crossed via the wooden walkway, so this was also immediately reported to the duty officer.

【Left Page】
1. Regarding timber of 3-4 sun circumference, there was discussion with the construction magistrate about wanting to receive 150 pieces. Since the outer riding ground hand bridge would be completed within two or three days, there was repeated discussion about bringing the timber to that location. However, since it would be quite difficult to cut [...] [...] [...] from the nearby government bamboo grove, it was agreed to first make do with 100 pieces. This matter was also reported to Sugi Zaemon.

1. The duty officer ordered that the contents of the official document be announced as follows:
　　　　　　　　　Mizo Ryōzō
　　500 mon each　Baba Kō[An]emon  
　　　　　　　　　Mikoba Yūji
　　
　　Since these men have many official duties and have made special efforts working away from their posts as assistant firewood officers, reward money is hereby granted. Furthermore, they should continue to serve diligently with attention to their account books.

　　Regarding the mountain climbing at Miyata in Haruchika district, they wished to postpone it due to Makita's mourning period absence. When this inquiry was submitted, since there would be woodcutters entering the mountains from spring onwards, it was approved by the duty officer that even one person from the mountain staff and others should conduct the inspection. Accordingly, instructions for the mountain climbing were conveyed to the mountain staff and others.

The investigation was completed, and on the 27th day summons, after coordination with the district magistrate staff, this matter was conveyed to Sugiemon's office and also communicated to the subordinates.

27th day
1. At the castle, from the 4th to 9th hour, rice purchase payments were distributed, and immediately the portions for two subordinate officials were received and handed over to Ryōzō.

28th day
1. Firewood was distributed to the upper and lower retainers at Okugawa. For Natsukawa, it was postponed with the intention to deliver in winter, and this matter was also reported to the duty officer.

29th day
1. Yosōji reported that he returned last evening. The snow was still deep, making travel to Ise Valley difficult. He returned after assessing the rough conditions and reported the details.

Last day of month
1. Gizaemon reported that he returned from Kainuma Village last evening and that all fence binding was completed. He reported that eighty laborers were employed.

1. Uemon, Ryōzō, and Yasuemon reported that they departed for Kurogawa entry to handle remaining boundary surveys from this period. They are scheduled to depart early morning on the 1st of next month.

[Left page]
　　　　　4th month grandson　　　Uemon-dono　　　Tōbei
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　From the 6th day, monthly duty Makita

1. It was reported that through consultation with the district magistrates, twenty select bamboo poles, and through consultation with the construction magistrates, sixty bamboo poles of various sizes were delivered.

3rd day
1. It was reported that Uemon and Ryōzō returned. Regarding Yasuemon, he remained at Kurosawa entry and consulted with Yūji about plans to cut firewood up to the middle crossing point, which was also reported.

5th day
1. It was reported that Sugi Zaemon and Gizaemon departed for Hirasawa Village on official forest business.

6th day
1. There was notice that Tōbei was indisposed due to illness. Therefore, from today Shita will serve the monthly duty. After consultation, he will continue serving monthly duty for this month.

1. We report that Uemon and Shōzō are departing now for Hayama crossing for Nakamura Yoshihara.

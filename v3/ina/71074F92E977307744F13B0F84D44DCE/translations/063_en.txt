Day 3
1. Regarding the disposal of the non-possession mountain Kurobe, communication was made to Sugizaemon's office to issue the directive. Then
　　Kichizaemon of Fukuro-machi was summoned and given instructions, and the said person reported this matter.

　　to urgently log one sawara cypress tree.
Day 4
1. Communication was made to Sugizaemon that sawara cypress logging and Kurobe timber with official stamps should be
　　gradually delivered from the non-possession mountain for the construction office's delivery deadline. Then a report was received that
　　Kichizaemon of Fukuro-machi was brought along and departed from noon today.
1. When Kurokawachi Zenemon's monthly summary of small white wood was submitted and cross-checked, Yasuemon reported
　　that there were no discrepancies. The town record ledger was delivered to the district magistrates.
【Left page】
Day 5
1. Sugizaemon's group reported that about 500 sawara cypress trees were logged at the non-possession mountain. Moreover, 18 laborers
　　were assigned to the non-possession mountain village, as reported.
1. Regarding the sorting for Kurobe disposal, it was reported that this will be postponed for now. Later
　　delivery will be arranged, as communicated by Sugizaemon's office.
Day 7
1. As reported by Sugizaemon's office, Gihei of Hiranoya in this town urgently wishes to request
　　timber transport from Kuroishi Minami-sawa mountain within Okatsu valley. Therefore, instructions were given to
　　proceed depending on weather conditions, and also to proceed with logging Benzaiten temporary bridge timber at Mizoguchi village
　　as previously arranged.
1. Regarding the above mountain transport, instructions were given for Yuji to also proceed. Concerning the arrival of purchased boards and
　　logs, orders were given to proceed from the district once the one-percent calculation is completed.

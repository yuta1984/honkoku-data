1. From tomorrow, the 22nd, during the lord's residence at the castle, officials who attend daily service will confer together with Sanpachi Godō.

22nd day
1. A circular notice arrived stating that His Lordship received permission to leave for his domain on the 13th, departed Edo on the 21st, and if there are no delays, will arrive and take his seat on the 26th. The duty officer instructed that this should be communicated for everyone's information.

1. Another circular notice arrived stating that since it is before His Lordship's arrival, special care should be taken regarding fire prevention.
Both of the above matters were communicated to subordinates.

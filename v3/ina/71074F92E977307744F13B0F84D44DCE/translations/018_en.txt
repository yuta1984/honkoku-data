　　　　　　　To the Mountain Office
　　　　　　　　Forest Magistrate

24th day
1. Gizaemon reported that the delivery of wooden tools to Kamiyamada Village was completed.

1. We heard that Haruchika Mountain still has deep snow, and moreover, at this time of year, hail has made it particularly difficult to walk since some time ago. To confirm if this is indeed the case, I ordered Yosōji alone to go and observe the conditions.

25th day
1. There was notice that Tōbei had returned from Yūkurokawa.

1. Uemon and Ryōzō also reported the same regarding their return.

26th day
1. Gizaemon reported that he would go out today to tend to the bamboo grove in Kainuma Village and to construct the perimeter fence.

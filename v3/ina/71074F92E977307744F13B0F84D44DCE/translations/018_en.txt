　　　　　　　To the Mountain Department
　　　　　　　　Honorable Magistrate

24th day
1. Gizaemon reported that the delivery of timber tools to Kamiyamada Village has been completed.

1. Regarding Harunaka mountain's end lodging Gen, moreover, recently access to the lodging has become particularly difficult to traverse compared to before. Therefore, to see whether it would finally lean toward that direction and to observe the situation of going there, only Akuji was instructed and dispatched.

25th day
1. There was notice that Tōbei returned from Yuguro River entry.

1. Uemon and Ryōzō also reported the same and made their notification.

25th day
1. Gizaemon reported that he went out today for the maintenance of the bamboo grove at Kainuma Village and for repairs to the general fencing.

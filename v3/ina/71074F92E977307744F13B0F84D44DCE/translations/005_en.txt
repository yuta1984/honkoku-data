10th Day
- Tomorrow, the 11th, being the first year, there will be the Jinnai Kuruwa Inari festival. House retainers and unmarried foot soldiers with wives and children may freely visit from 5 o'clock in the morning until Saben. A circular notice was issued from the chief inspectors, and orders were immediately given to subordinates.

14th Day
　Regarding the forestry work in Kawashimo district during last summer of the Year of the Monkey, when villages sent rice stipend certificates totaling one koku via Yogorō Uemon, after receiving the district seal, they were sent to that same person. Six certificates in total that subordinates had placed in the villages were received.

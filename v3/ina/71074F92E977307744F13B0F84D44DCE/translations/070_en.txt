【Right page】
7th day
1. Kobayashi Sugizaemon and Mimitsu Ryozo returned yesterday evening from Fujisawa district. They reported that overall there were no changes in the official forests. However, both men reported that some chestnut trees were officially sold from the official forests in Nakajo village and [illegible] village.

8th day
1. Acknowledged the report that Kitahara Giemon returned from Mizoguchi village. He reported that although the wooden pipes are not yet completed, he has given instructions to the officials.

10th day
1. Regarding the payment for wood to be given to Mineyama Temple, a temporary receipt of 2 ryo and 2 bu was desired. After consultation among the chief supervisors, Hanaemon received and acknowledged this from the forest administration today.
1. 245 logs of miscellaneous wood for official construction use were purchased and delivered as previously arranged by one party. For this, payment of 6 kan 124 mon was instructed to be made to Hanaemon's office.

【Left page】
1. Instructions were given to Kitahara Giemon and Mimitsu Ryozo that subordinate officials should go to mark the boundaries of the official forests in Haruchika district and Kawashimo district.
1. A request came from the district magistrate's office for urgent receipt of another 30 pieces due to shortage of bamboo gratings for Benzaiten Bridge.
1. 10 pieces of foundation wood (lengths between 2 to 4 ken) were received and there was consultation with the construction office.
   Instructions for cutting both types of wood were given to Yuji of Hanaemon's office.

11th day
Yesterday's instructions regarding wooden tools for the district magistrate's office and construction office were carried out - they were cut and delivered from Obara village today. Yuji of Hanaemon's office reported this.

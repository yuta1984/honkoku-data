【Right page】
7th day
1. Kobayashi Sugizaemon and Mimizo Ryōzō returned yesterday evening from Fujisawa-gō. It was acknowledged that they reported no particular changes in the official forests overall. However, both men reported that some chestnut trees were sold from the official forests in Nakajō village and Furui village.

8th day
1. It was acknowledged that Kitahara Gisaemon reported his return from Mizoguchi village. However, he reported that although the conduit wood is not yet completed, he has given instructions to the officials.

10th day
1. Regarding the payment for wood to be granted to Mineyama-dera temple, Uemon received a temporary receipt for 2 ryō 2 bu after consultation with the chief supervisors, and reported the receipt from the audit office today.
1. 245 logs of trees and timber were purchased and delivered as requested for official construction use. For this, a payment of 6 kan 124 mon was to be made, and instructions were given to Uemon's office to receive it.

【Left page】
1. Instructions were given to Kitahara Gisaemon and Mimizo Ryōzō to send subordinate officials to establish boundaries of the official forests in Haruchika-gō and Kawashimo-gō.
1. The district magistrates requested urgent receipt of another 30 pieces due to shortage of bamboo grating for Benzaiten bridge.
1. 10 pieces of supporting wood, ranging from 2 to 4 ken in length, were received and discussed with the construction office.
   Instructions were given to Yūji from Uemon's office regarding the cutting of both types mentioned above.

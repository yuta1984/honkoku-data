【Right page】
　　We humbly received the lord's intentions and received bestowed items.
1. The count and sorting of small timber bundles along with the ledgers were submitted to the duty officer for review,
　　and after the review was completed, the ledgers and documents were returned in the evening.
26th day
1. Today there was official business, and at four o'clock everyone reported for duty and received direct
　　instructions at the書院 (administrative office). Regarding the five-year period from the year of the ox to the year of the tiger for construction materials, austerity measures were
　　announced. Subsequently, allocation ledgers for the designated period were
　　announced, and ledger documents were presented by the chief inspectors. After everyone reviewed and copied them,
　　both persons attended to convey the instructions to those under their supervision, with only the official department formally acknowledging receipt. Therefore,
　　both persons recorded in the duty officer's ledger that they had conveyed
　　the austerity measures announced to those under their supervision as an official report.
【Left page】
1. Kurokawachi Zen'emon and Miyashita Masuemon were summoned. I, along with both persons, Uemon and Giemon were in attendance, and
　　the monthly duty officer made the following announcement:
　　　　　　100 gold hiki*　　　　　　Kurokawachi Zen'emon
　　　　The above was given as a reward for maintaining the mountain office records.
　　　　　　100 gold hiki　　　　　　Miyashita Masuemon
　　The above was given as a reward for maintaining the firewood office records. Additionally,
　　rice stipends were given as usual, which was announced in a separate summons, with Uemon and subordinate officials in
　　attendance, announced by the monthly duty officer.
　For the above courtesy visits, we went as far as the duty officer's kitchen, and instructed that both persons
　from our side should make courtesy visits to the district magistrates as well.

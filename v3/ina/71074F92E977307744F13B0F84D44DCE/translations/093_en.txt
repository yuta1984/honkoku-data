1. Regarding the official sale of timber from Kurosawa within the Yokogawa official forest in Kami-Ina County, timber merchants from Edo submitted a petition.
　　This concerns the carpenter guild Daihashi group from our locality
　　who wish to conduct logging for official government use, and since they plan to procure with local timber,
　　they will purchase cypress in this Chikuma County. For this purpose, they wish to transport by cart to Utou Pass in Ono Village
　　and then send it down the Tenryu River. As this would also bring profit, they wish to submit an additional petition for
　　one location at Kurosawa within Yokogawa official forest. The petition from Kobayashi Sugizaemon's group was
　　submitted, and it was ordered that proper greetings should be made after consultation with colleagues.

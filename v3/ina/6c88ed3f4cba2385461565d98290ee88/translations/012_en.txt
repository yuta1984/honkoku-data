　　　　　　　　　　To Village Heads
According to this prefecture's proclamation No. 80 Otsu of this year,
the head and deputy village heads and below will be dismissed
as of June 30 of this month. In accordance with this,
please conduct the handover without delay according to the
attached "Guidelines for Administrative Handover."
We hereby notify you of this matter.
　　　　　　　　　　Acting for Nagano Prefectural Governor Narasaki Hirochika
May 28, Meiji 12 (1879)　　Nagano Prefecture Senior Secretary Matsuno Atsushi

Regarding the public election of village heads (tochō) as notified earlier this year by our prefecture's document Otsu No. 58, all said village heads shall be
treated equivalent to Extra Class First Grade and shall be given annual salaries
as specified below. We hereby proclaim this for your information.

　　　　　　　　　　Acting for Nagano Prefectural Governor Narasaki Hirochika
May 28, Meiji 12 (1879)　　Nagano Prefecture Senior Secretary Matsuno Atsushi

Households: 2,000 or more households in towns/villages　Annual salary: 120 yen
Same: 1,000 or more households, same　Same: 100 yen  
Same: 500 or more households, same　Same: 80 yen

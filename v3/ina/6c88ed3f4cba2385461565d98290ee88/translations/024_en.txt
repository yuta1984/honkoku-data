　　　　Notification

This time, in connection with the tour of Nichiren Sect Head Priest with the rank of Gon-Daikyōshō, Nii
Nissatsu, regarding the execution of a sermon on May 4th at our temple,
we have erected notice boards at various places for announcement purposes.
We hereby submit this notification regarding this matter.

　　　　　　　Nichiren Sect Shinmyōji Temple Head Priest

April 29th, Meiji 12 (1879)

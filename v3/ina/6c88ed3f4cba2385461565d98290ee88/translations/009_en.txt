　　　　　　　　　　　County Office
From the 11th of this month until September 10th, all government officials shall be
granted summer vacation as notified. Regarding this, during the said period,
the County Chief and secretaries may take vacation within fifteen days
according to the convenience of official duties. We hereby notify you of this matter.
　　However, although travel during vacation is permitted, the destination
　　must be reported. Furthermore, even those who do not travel must also
　　report their vacation period.

July 5, Meiji 12 (1879)　　Nagano Prefectural Governor Narasaki Hirochika

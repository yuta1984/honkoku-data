According to the promulgation of Meiji 11 No. 17, the large and small district system
will be abolished as of June 30 of this year, and the head and deputy village heads
as well as town/village clerks and representatives will be dismissed. From July 1
of the same year, village head offices will be established and newly appointed
village heads will handle administrative affairs. We hereby proclaim this matter.
　　　　　　　　　　Acting for Nagano Prefectural Governor Narasaki Hirochika
June 2, Meiji 12 (1879)　　Nagano Prefecture Senior Secretary Matsuno Atsushi

　　　Article 1
1. Among buildings located on vacant land, those with pillars, roofs, etc. but without proper enclosures shall be excluded from the survey. However, even toilets measuring merely six shaku square (approximately 1.8m × 1.8m), if they have proper enclosures, shall all be investigated and recorded.

　　　Article 2
1. For those who have, at their own discretion, converted originally vacant land into cleared land and changed cultivated fields into residential land in exchange, calculations shall be made based on the land value of those cultivated fields.

　　　Article 3
1. Buildings such as warehouses, storage houses, water mills, etc., constructed on cultivated land outside of residential areas for convenience shall also be handled according to Articles 1 and 2.

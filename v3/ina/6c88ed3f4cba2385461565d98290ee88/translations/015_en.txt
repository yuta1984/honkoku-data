【Right】
The responsible officials shall sign and conduct the handover of notification documents. If documents such as real estate and building mortgage/loan endorsement ledgers contain miscellaneous records that are difficult to categorize, extracts and copies shall be made noting the reasons, and the village head and deputy shall sign and conduct the handover. The original documents shall be submitted to the relevant district office, which shall issue a receipt.

　However, documents such as various official decrees that are difficult to divide shall all be submitted to the relevant district office.

　　　Article 7
In the case of the previous article, buildings and various equipment belonging to sub-district offices shall be subject to bidding, disposal and other procedures after consultation with town and village representatives and delegates, and the proceeds shall be redistributed within that sub-district.

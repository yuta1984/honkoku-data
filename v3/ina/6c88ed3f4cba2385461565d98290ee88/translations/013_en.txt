【Right】
Together with the explanatory document and inventory ledger, hand over to the newly appointed village head by July 1 of this year, and submit a copy of the receipt certificate to the relevant district office. The person receiving the handover shall also submit a copy of the certificate received from the transferor along with notification that the administrative transition has been completed to the relevant district office.
　However, due to the circumstances of civilian tax assessment, since there are insufficient days remaining for handling residual affairs, preparations for the handover procedures must be made in advance outside of regular duties, and the handover must be completed by the deadline specified in the main text. Even if there are items that cannot be completed by the specified deadline, no salaries or other expenses shall be paid or allocated.
　　　Article 1
【Left】
　Regarding matters under the jurisdiction of village heads and subordinate officials that remain incomplete by the time of dismissal, create an explanatory document detailing the procedures followed thus far and the prospects for future disposition, and attach it to the handover certificate.
　　　Article 2
　Clarify the classification of fully paid and unpaid various taxes and civilian expenses collected at sub-district offices and below, create an explanatory document regarding the order of entries in collection ledgers and handling procedures, and conduct the handover of consolidated cash.
　　　Article 3
　Conduct the handover with an inventory ledger attached for all documents including the following various ledgers, as well as all other documents and old records.

【Right Page】
1. Regarding lodging for the messenger:
   We have been instructed to make preparations,
   so we have ordered Senemon's household to handle this matter,
   which we hereby report.

1. On the day in question, after the inspection of the storehouse,
   we have been instructed to serve lunch
   during the rest period
   at Lord Okamura's residence.

1. When His Honor the Magistrate descends and
   arrives, he will immediately
   proceed downward, according to
   the official notice received.

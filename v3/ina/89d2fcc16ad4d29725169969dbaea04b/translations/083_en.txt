In recent years, there has been particularly heavy official traffic, and moreover, all goods have become exceptionally expensive. We had arranged to provide one horse for four ryō to four ryō and two bu, but we have gradually increased the fee, and this spring we arranged for five ryō. However, due to the extraordinary amount of official traffic, we have reported that we cannot manage the service, so we humbly submit the following difficulties:

1. Regarding the official post horse service of 25 people and 25 horses assigned by official notice:
   When His Lordship passes through, the additional personnel and horses 
   are petitioned for separately.

1. Regarding the porters for the 13 people and 13 horses beyond the official notice:
   Those purchased by the government are limited to continuing baggage transport for the retainers and other official cargo. When personnel and horses are all dispatched for this service, or when there are official notices from other domains requiring personnel and horses, we face difficulties.

1. Regarding the Kitahara Village crossing, which uses a temporary bridge: when floods occur or when the bridge falls, urgent personnel and horses cannot be arranged in time, requiring ferry boats

With all due respect, I humbly submit this oral petition:
Regarding myself, I received timber from land in Ota-machi
and was conducting construction work, but it was delayed, and previously
I received notice of withdrawal, which I humbly acknowledge with trepidation.
However, I had petitioned that the construction would be completed
by the 20th day of the 12th month and submitted a written pledge,
but there were difficulties with carpenters and woodcutters,
so although I repeatedly apologize for the inconvenience, I petition that it will be completed
during the 2nd month of the coming Snake year. If you would
graciously grant approval as I have petitioned
with your benevolence, it would be a most
grateful circumstance. The above.
　1st year of Meiji, Year of the Dragon
　　　　　12th month
　　　　　　　　Shirakiya
　　　　　　　Petitioner: Jinsa [seal]

Humbly submitted as a written petition

I am an inadequate person, but unexpectedly received the appointment of group leader (kumigashira), which I considered a grateful fortune and have been diligently serving. However, since last winter I have been ill, and it has gradually become a serious illness, making it difficult for me to continue serving in the role of group leader. Therefore, I humbly request to be granted release from this duty.

Regarding the above matter:　　　　　　Nakamachi
First month of Meiji 2, year of the Snake (1869)　Petitioner: Azuma Jū(rō)
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Group leader: Yaemon
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　District representative: Kanemon

To the five honorable clerks, joint signature
《Crossed out: To the honorable magistrate's office》

The above petition was submitted on the 10th day through Mr. Sakai as intermediary at the government office. It was immediately acknowledged, and after deliberation, it was ordered that the position be filled by lottery according to precedent. Notice was given to report on the 12th day, so group leader Yaemon was called and informed.

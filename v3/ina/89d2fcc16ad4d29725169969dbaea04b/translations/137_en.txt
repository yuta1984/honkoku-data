We humbly submit this oral petition.

Item: Twenty ryō in gold

We have long received your gracious benevolence, for which we are extremely grateful and feel most blessed. We humbly acknowledge that His Lordship has recently traveled to Kyoto and returned to the castle domain without incident, for which we respectfully express our joy. As proof of this sentiment, despite our trepidation and though the amount is modest, we wish to make a monetary contribution. If you would graciously permit this, we would all feel deeply grateful. The above concludes our petition.

Meiji 1st year, Year of the Dragon (1868), 11th month

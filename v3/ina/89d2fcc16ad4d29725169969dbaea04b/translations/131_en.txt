Ikegami Riemon
　　　　　　　　　　　　　Ikegami Honemon
　　　　　　　　　　　　　Kitahara Risaemon
　　　To Matsui Shuzo-sama
Submitted at the government office on November 7th

　　　Memorandum
　Item: One koku, seven to, eight sho of rice*
【Note】
"From August 1st
　to January 31st of the following year
　Total days including large and small months
　　　One hundred seventy-eight days"
　The above rice was granted as stipend rice for the horse attendant of this town,
　and we have certainly received it through the Dragon year tribute rice from Kawashimo village.
　This document serves as confirmation thereof.
　November, 1st year of Meiji, year of the Dragon (1868)　　Ikegami Riemon
　　　To Matsui Shuzo-sama
Item: On the 7th at the government office

The twelve ryō that Daiji received should be paid out by both families, and as it will be redistributed to both families anew, all matters should be handled with a fifty-fifty split. Furthermore, when Daiji's family name and position are inherited in the future, the successor shall pay the aforementioned sum to both families, and the sake brewing license shall be transferred accordingly. All other matters shall follow precedent, and understanding the ancestors' intentions, Iemon and Jirozaemon shall cooperate in perpetuity to maintain joint ownership. This arbitration being concluded, written documents are hereby issued to both parties as future evidence, and must not be violated.

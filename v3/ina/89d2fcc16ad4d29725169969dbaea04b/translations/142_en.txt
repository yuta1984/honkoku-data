Same position: Seiemon

Village head: Okiemon

Humbly submitting this petition:
One, I have received timber and other materials from elsewhere for lot number fourteen on the east side of Tamachi (frontage: seven and a half ken, depth: ten and a half ken), and was to build a house immediately, but due to two years of poor harvests, I have had to postpone construction repeatedly. Now I have received official instructions again, for which I am deeply apologetic. From now on, I will certainly complete the house construction by the twentieth day of the twelfth month of this year. I humbly request that you grant my petition with your compassion, which would be most gratifying.
The above

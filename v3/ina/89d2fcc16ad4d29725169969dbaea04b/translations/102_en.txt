Respectfully submitting this petition with humble deference

I am an incompetent person, but in the 6th year of Bunsei (1823), the year of the Sheep, I was unexpectedly appointed to a rear administrative position (ura-oyaku), which I consider a most grateful fortune. In the 9th year of Tenpō (1838), the year of the Dog, I was appointed to a front administrative position (omote-oyaku), which I consider a doubly grateful fortune, and I have been serving in this capacity. In the 1st year of Keio (1865), the year of the Ox, due to my years of faithful service, through special benevolence I was granted permission to wear a haori (formal jacket), and my son Kanetarō was unexpectedly appointed as an apprentice to a rear administrative position - truly an overwhelming blessing and an extremely grateful fortune.

Under such circumstances, I had intended to serve with utmost diligence to the extent of my abilities. However, since this spring I have been ill, and despite various treatments, I have now fallen seriously ill. As I cannot foresee recovery going forward, I humbly petition that through your benevolence, I may be pardoned from my official duties.

Keio 4 (1868), year of the Dragon, 6th month　　Petitioner
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Yaichi

Humbly submitting this oral petition

Regarding Yonezaburo of our group association, on the night of July 4th of this year, his wife and two daughters ran away from home, so we report this matter to you. As there are also rumors that Yonezaburo's conduct is improper, we cannot predict what kind of trouble he might cause wherever he goes. Therefore, we petition that this entire household be removed from the religious register.

Regarding the above matter　　　　　Aioicho

To the Magistrate's Office

When we submitted the above petition to Mr. Ikeda and Mr. Sakai at the government office on the 12th, we were told to submit it at the castle tomorrow. When we went to the Higashi-Ikegami castle on the 13th, Mr. Sakai informed us that it had been received.

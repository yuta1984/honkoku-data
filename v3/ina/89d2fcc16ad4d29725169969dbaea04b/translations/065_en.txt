With all due respect,
Although I am an inadequate person, I unexpectedly
received the appointment of group head (kumigashira), which I considered a grateful fortune, and have been serving
in this capacity. However, since the middle of the previous Year of the Rabbit, I have been ill, and gradually
my condition has worsened to a serious illness, making it difficult for me to continue serving in the role of group head.
Therefore, I humbly petition for your forgiveness and release from this position.
Regarding the above matter　　　Naka-machi
　Meiji era　　　　　　　　　East District No. 10
　　　　　　　　　　　　　Yaemon
　　　　　　　　　　　　　Kanemon

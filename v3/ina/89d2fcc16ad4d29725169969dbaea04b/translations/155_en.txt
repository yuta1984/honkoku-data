Most humbly, I submit this oral petition.
On this occasion,
His Lordship has returned to the castle
without any delay, which we find most joyous. Regarding this matter,
the persons whose names are listed on the separate sheet
have received Your Lordship's benevolent favor and, understanding this blessing, wish to
present humble gifts
to express their gratitude. We humbly request
that you serve as intermediary. If this matter receives your approval,
we would all consider it a most grateful blessing. The above
First year of Meiji, Year of the Earth Dragon, 11th month　Ikegami Kichibei

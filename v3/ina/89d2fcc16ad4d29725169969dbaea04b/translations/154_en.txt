Respectfully submitted,
I humbly request permission to construct a building on the land I received from Kinjiró of Óhara Village in Ōta-machi and to commence operating a medicinal bath (yakutō) business thereon.
Therefore, I submit this petition.
Regarding the above matter
　　　　　　　　　　　　　〽Aioi-machi
First year of Meiji, Year of the Earth Dragon, 11th month　Petitioner: Hyōmazaemon
　　　　　　　　　　　　Ōta-machi
　　　　　　　　　　　　Group head: Yoshiuemon
　　　　　　　　　　　　District representative: Matsujirō
　　　　　　　　　　　　Ditto: Chūzaemon
To the Magistrate's Office

On the 28th day of the 11th month, Matsujirō brought [the petition], on the following 29th day an endorsement was written at the meeting office, and on the 30th day it was submitted at the government office through the intermediary of Lord Yamashita Genzaemon. However, [the officials] stated that placing Hyōmazaemon, who belongs to Yosaburo's group, under Yoshiuemon's group would be irregular, so the petition was returned with instructions to revise it thoroughly, and therefore [we] have brought it [back].

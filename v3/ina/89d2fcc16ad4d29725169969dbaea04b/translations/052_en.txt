　　We humbly submit this oral petition with great trepidation
Currently, those selling sake by the measure in the town area are setting up large barrels and conducting extensive retail sales, treating their business the same as sake breweries. However, this makes it impossible for those who hold sake licenses to conduct their business, causing them great hardship. We humbly request that you mercifully order a halt to such practices. We brew sake using rice granted by the authorities, and regarding pricing, we submit requests and receive official rulings before handling prices accordingly.

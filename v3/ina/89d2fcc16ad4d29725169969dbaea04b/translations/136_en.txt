To the Honorable Magistrate's Office

Regarding the above petition: on the 5th day, Mr. Kitahara and both Ikegamis personally brought it to the government office. Mr. Sakai served as intermediary, reviewed the draft, and when we inquired about the official opinion, we were instructed to affix our seals and submit it. Accordingly, we have rewritten it as this formal document and respectfully submit it.

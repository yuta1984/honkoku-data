Certificate of Borrowed Funds with Annual Installment Repayment
Seventy ryo of gold
The above amount has been graciously loaned to us with deferred annual installment repayment due to financial difficulties in carrying out our post-horse duties, and we are most grateful for this arrangement.
Regarding repayment: seven ryo of gold per year, from the coming winter of the Tsuchinoto-Mi year to the winter of the Mizunoe-Tora year, to be repaid without fail by the 25th day of December each year.
This certificate of borrowed funds is executed for future reference. Accordingly written. Shimo-machi

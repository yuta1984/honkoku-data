With all respect, we humbly submit:
Regarding the construction on the land granted to us on the south side of Aioi-cho,
the project has been delayed year after year. When we attempted to proceed with construction,
although the timber was prepared, difficulties arose with carpenters and sawyers,
making it impossible to carry out the construction work.
We deeply apologize for this situation. We will without fail complete the construction
by the 28th day of the first month of next year, so we earnestly request
that you graciously grant us an extension of the deadline.
Respectfully submitted. Shimo-machi

Respectfully submitted as a petition through oral statement

Regarding the sake brewing license held by the farmer Yosōji of Kamikaise Village, Saku District, Shinshū, in the domain of Lord Matsudaira Iwami-no-kami, for twenty-six koku of rice for sake production: this license was transferred to Satarō of Habiro Village, Ina District, under the custody of Lord Matsudaira Tamba-no-kami. In the 5th year of Ansei (1858), the year of the Horse, I received the sake brewing rights from the aforementioned Satarō and have been conducting this business. Now that the reissuance of licenses has been ordered, I respectfully petition to increase production by twenty-four koku of rice, submit the required fee, and receive a license for a total of fifty koku of rice for sake production.

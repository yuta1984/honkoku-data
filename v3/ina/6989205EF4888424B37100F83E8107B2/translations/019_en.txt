Haruchika Township
Koide Village

Hira-no-bun: south is ridge area, north is main road boundary. Mountain guardian contractor: Yagorō, Kakunojō
1. Pine forest: 3 se 2 tan 20 bu (130 ken × 74 ken) Current guardians: Gizaemon, Buzaemon
   About one-third was cleared from Kyōwa 1 (Year of the Rooster) to the Year of the Boar. Currently about 80% of the lower slopes have small-standing pines
   Ridge has medium-standing pines, riverside trees suitable for tools, with some scattered hinoki cypress and sawara cypress

Shima
1. Bamboo grove: 20 bu (5 ken × 4 ken) Bamboo grove guardian contractor: Tōbei Current: Hyakuta
Due to a petition that this bamboo grove was causing shade problems for the rice fields, in Bunka 12 (Year of the Boar), 4th month, the bamboo grove was cleared and converted to new fields

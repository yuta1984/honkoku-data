We humbly submit this written petition
Item: Those charcoal burners who came forward in the previous Uma year (1870) requesting assistance for their livelihood,
submitted a petition stating they would continue to pay thirty ryō each as in previous years,
but upon returning to the village and consulting with the charcoal burners, the situation proved more difficult than expected, with the mountain forests extremely depleted.
Due to this truly difficult year, even paying ten ryō would be uncertain,
so through village officials we submitted a petition on the 21st day of the 9th month of Kōgo year (1870), and though presumptuous,
since the petition was not returned to us, we understood it to have been approved. While we feel extremely ashamed about this,

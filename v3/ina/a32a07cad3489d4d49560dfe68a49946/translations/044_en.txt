Regarding the tax on medicine sales, recently even assistants (apprentices) must pay the same two shu as has been the established rule from long ago. However, it has been heard that those who engage in medicine sales are placing assistants and sending them around to various places. No matter how many assistants they place, this is not questioned, but regarding the assistants paying their tax, it should be received properly according to the established procedure.

Recently, medicine sellers have been placing assistants in both locations and sending them around to customers. Some are conducting business without licenses, and they seem to think that assistants need not apply for licenses and can conduct business without licenses without any problem. Somehow this has become the state of affairs. However, this goes against the original regulations, and even assistants should pay taxes as individuals, so after investigating each case, there should be prohibitions. But due to many years of neglecting this matter, there are those who do not properly understand these regulations. Therefore, this time investigation and prohibition will not be pursued, but henceforth there should be no misunderstanding of these rules.

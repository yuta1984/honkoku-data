Kaei 6, Year of the Water Ox (1853)

Due to the passing of His Excellency Kii no Ichi-i on the 27th day of last month, construction work is to cease from that same day, and musical performances and entertainment are to be suspended for seven days as has been proclaimed. Until tomorrow the 3rd day, refrain from musical performances and entertainment, and take particular care with fire prevention.

The above matter shall be proclaimed throughout the town districts. The above.

2nd day of the 2nd month

As proclaimed in the above official notice, magistrates were promptly dispatched to deliver this to the town representatives (ch≈çdai) for proclamation.

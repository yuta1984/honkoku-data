　April 17th
　To Lord Tateiwa Yogoemon
　From the correspondence group

The above matter was delivered to the town representative of Shimonomachi for the same group leaders to review.

　On this day, we have heard that thieves have entered various places. Should such incidents occur, [officials] must work to apprehend [the criminals] and without fail report the matter, never letting it pass unnoticed.
　The above instructions should be announced to all locations.
　　April
　As the above official document has been issued from the government, it should be announced throughout the town. That is all.
　　April
Regarding the instructions issued on April 18th above, [we] have announced them without omission to each district.

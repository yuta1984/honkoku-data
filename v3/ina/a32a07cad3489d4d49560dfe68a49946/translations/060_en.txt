His lordship will make an early departure for Edo, and it has been announced that he will depart on the coming 28th day.
This matter should be proclaimed throughout the town.
The above
20th day of the first month
Regarding the manner of issuing the above written notice, it is ordered that it be proclaimed to the za-moto (town representatives).

Lord Oshin was ill, but his recovery was not possible, and he passed away on the 23rd day of last month. Due to this, since sufficient time has passed regarding construction work, there is no need for restraint, but musical instruments and music shall be prohibited until the 2nd day of next month, and special care should be taken regarding fire prevention.

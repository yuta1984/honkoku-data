One, Regarding food matters as well, in recent years there has been a gradual
　trend toward great extravagance, so one should live as frugally as possible,
　and even those who are well-off enough to afford unlimited luxury
　should consume simple food for twenty days.
　Also, when unavoidably entertaining relatives and close
　acquaintances during necessary occasions, first
　do not serve alcohol and food, and if unavoidably
　serving them, certainly use simple dishes
　and never serve anything beyond one soup and one vegetable dish.
　　However, even one soup and one vegetable should be prepared as simply as possible,
　　and it is acceptable to omit one bowl from the above.
　　Do not seek out wild animals or birds, and peasants should especially
　　prioritize frugality.
One, In recent years, many people have expanded their house construction
　focusing on appearance, elaborating even the interior,
　and putting on airs to create impressive facades.
　Many have forgotten their proper station
　and prideful, arrogant people have grown increasingly presumptuous.

At Edo, Lord Seiyama Genemon
has been appointed to the position of elder (toshiyori-yaku).
However, if there are others with the same name,
they should immediately change their names.
The above matter shall be announced throughout the town. End.
　Fourth month of the year of the Rat

The above was issued as an official directive on the fifteenth day of the fourth month, and it was announced to fellow officials that the town representative of Shimosono-machi should petition and deliver this as an inspector. It was also conveyed to the za-moto (guild heads) and eta (outcasts).

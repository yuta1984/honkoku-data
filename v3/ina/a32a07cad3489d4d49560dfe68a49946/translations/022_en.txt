As he has repented his past wrongdoings and personally petitioned to return to town, which was reported to the town officials, we hereby grant permission for his return to town. Therefore, we impose disciplinary service upon him once again. In his duties, he shall follow the law and serve faithfully in his official service.

The above matter shall be conveyed in the presence of all concerned parties.
The above.

Second day of the third month
To Lord Tatemyo Yogoemon
All clerks

This was delivered through the above written notice.

When the Young Lord made his formal visit to the castle this spring, he was graciously granted a court rank. In connection with this, his official title has become Lord Osumi-no-kami.

Nakamachi
1,000 mon coins　Ikegami Jinzo
You maintain a good disposition and handle not only your family business but all matters with sincerity, while also demonstrating filial piety toward your parents, as we have heard. Previously, coins were granted to you, and since then we have heard that you have been particularly attentive and diligent in your business. Therefore, coins are once again granted in recognition of your praiseworthy conduct. You shall deeply understand and repay this benevolent favor.

October 29th

Regarding town representatives, village headman deputies, and group leaders: When there are those who organize forbidden (gambling), they should add strict admonition, and when such admonition is not heeded, there should be proper handling procedures. However, for carelessly overlooking such matters, penalty money is imposed due to this misconduct.

As stated above, henceforth strictly obtaining the regulations and understanding their meaning, one should conduct oneself prudently according to one's status, strictly observe all legal prohibitions, and diligently pursue one's occupation. Should there be any further misconduct, severe punishment will be imposed, and this must be clearly understood. Furthermore, those who have been imposed disciplinary fines should receive proper guidance from the appropriate authorities and serve their public duties on the right path. Additionally, penalty money must be paid within three days.

The above matters should be announced to each person in the presence of witnesses. The above.

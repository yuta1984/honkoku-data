　　　Shinbi April 15th　　　Shinagawa Prefecture

Directive of May 26th
　　Regarding gold, silver, and copper mines within each domain and prefecture's jurisdiction, as well as
　　the annual quantities extracted, and additionally places where coal, lead, sulfur, and
　　medicinal stones are produced, investigations should be conducted and sample
　　items should be submitted along with reports. Although official proclamations have been
　　issued previously, some have still not submitted their reports,
　　which is inappropriate. Therefore, urgent investigations should be conducted and reports
　　should be submitted whether such resources exist or not.

One　The roadside trees along various roads and highways shall not be felled. Mixed wild trees may be felled without issue.
　　　　　Seedlings should be planted in cleared areas
　　　　　　However, for trees that obstruct traffic and harm fields,
　　　　　　Article Two shall apply

　　　　　　　Article Six
　　　One　In mountain forests that are water sources, whether valuable timber or wild trees,
　　　　　excessive felling shall not be permitted
　　　　　　However, standing dead trees, trees broken by wind and snow, and rotten wood are not subject to this restriction

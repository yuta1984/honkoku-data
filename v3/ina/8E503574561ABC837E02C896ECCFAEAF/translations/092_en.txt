9th day
1. There was over 1,400 ryō in temporary advance money from the previous firewood official, and there were irregularities at the accounting bureau, so after reporting this matter, 1,000 ryō was made into a formal receipt and the remaining amount was kept as a temporary receipt.

1. Submitted the following to the bureau chief:
　　　　　Record
　　　　50 ryō in gold
　　　　　Of which: 30 ryō in gold
　　　　　　　　　208 kan mon in money
　　　This is from the firewood cutter payments, which I humbly wish to receive temporarily.
　　　Kanoto-Hitsuji (1871), 11th month　　　　Iwasaki Minato
　　　　　　　To the Accounting Department
　　　The above was delivered to contractor Shirōe

1. Went to the site because official firewood had arrived at Benzaiten Kawahara.

11th day
1. There were irregularities in the forestry department, so after reporting this, Suzuki Kinjū received it.

12th day
1. When the daily wage money for transporting official firewood downstream was insufficient, the accounting bureau would not provide it, so the bureau chief made a temporary loan and it was given to Itō Shirōe.

1. Submitted a petition requesting the following proclamation be issued:
　　　Since the firewood distribution method throughout the prefecture has been reformed,

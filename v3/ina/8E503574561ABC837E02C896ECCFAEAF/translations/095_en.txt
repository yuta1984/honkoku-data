26th day
1. Today during the general attendance, the following was announced by Acting Senior Councilor Kono Etsu:
　　　Regarding the summons on the 20th, Senior Councilor Okano of the 10th district attended, whereupon it was announced that the various prefectures of Shinano Province would be abolished and Chikuma Prefecture and Nagano Prefecture would be established, and that the territories under previous jurisdiction should transfer their tax assessments, villages, etc. to the newly established prefectures from this year of the sheep.
　　　　However, officials of the abolished prefectures should, until further notice, handle affairs at their former offices under the direction of all councilors of the newly established prefectures, as announced.
　　　The above should be understood and kept in mind.
　　　　Kanoe-hitsuji [1871], 11th month

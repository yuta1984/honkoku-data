Shortly after, I went to the residence of Jinzō, the headman of Miyada post station, and soon after the lord's arrival, I went outside the inn. I met with the house administrator named Miura Kanzō, and regarding my service for the lord's passage, I submitted a letter requesting that appropriate duties be assigned to me.

One: When departing home, I submitted the following notification to the government office:
　　Iida Domain
　　For the passage of His Lordship the Governor to Miyada post station,
　　I am serving today. I hereby submit this notification.
　　　March 20th　　　Iwasaki Minato

21st day
One: This morning after six o'clock, for the lord's departure, I went outside the post station, where an envoy named Kubota Sōkitsu came and conveyed that His Lordship the Governor wished to express his appreciation for my service during this passage, and the following rewards were granted:
　　One: 100 mon in gold　　　Iwasaki Minato
　　One: 100 mon in gold　　　Inoue Ritsutarō
　　One: 50 mon in gold　　　for horse and porter allocation
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Itō Tsuneharu

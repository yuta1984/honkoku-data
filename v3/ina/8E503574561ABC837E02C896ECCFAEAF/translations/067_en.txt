This time
　　　having been ordered to be dismissed
　　　from our positions,
　　　we gratefully acknowledge your kind letter and
　　　the purport of your intentions, which truly penetrates our hearts and souls.
　　　We have no words to express our feelings
　　　and consider this a matter for deep regret. As we have
　　　for generations received
　　　your generous benevolence,
　　　with undivided loyalty
　　　we humbly wish to continue reporting to you in the future, though inadequately,
　　　and desire to repay your great kindness
　　　We humbly request that you treat us unchanged
　　　We earnestly beseech you to continue
　　　bestowing your kind commands upon us. Regarding this matter,
　　　we humbly request your favorable intercession
　　　The above
　　　　August
　　　　　　　　　　Junior Officials
　　　　　　　　　　Acting Junior Officials
　　　　　　　　　　Scribes
　　　　　　　　　　Bureau Chiefs
　　　　　　　　　　Civil and Military Officials
　　　　　　　　　　　　All together

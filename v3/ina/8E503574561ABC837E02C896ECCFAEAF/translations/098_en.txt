1. We submit the following official notice to the bureau chief:
　　Regarding the matter of firewood distribution to all people,
　　as reformed this spring, according to assessed value
　　without distinction between summer, winter, or rivers,
　　all shall be distributed this winter, and substitute rice
　　shall be collected at next year's market prices.
　　The delivery fees are as follows:
　　　1 ken of firewood - 21.5 monme of silver
　　　1 ken of sawara lumber - 25 monme of silver
　　　1 ken of larch - 31 monme of silver
　　For those desiring amounts beyond the standard allocation,
　　payment will be at appropriate prices, so please submit
　　estimates by the 10th.

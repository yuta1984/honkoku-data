7th month (large month)
1st day
One　I inspected the domain forests and groves of Ōsokura Village, Nakayama Village, and Nakasokura Village, completed inspections of the groves of Honjōkura Village and the domain forests of Kamitakami Village, Suganuma Village, and Nagamiyama Village, and stayed at the house of the village headman of Shimotakami Village.

2nd day
One　I inspected the new river crossing and flood control site at Shimotakami Village, inspected the domain forest of Hara Village and the grove of Ina Village, and stayed at the house of the village headman there.

3rd day
One　I inspected the grove of Kuribayashi Village and the domain forests and groves of Ōkubo Village, Shioda Village, and Kayama Village, and stayed at the house of the village headman there.

4th day
One　I inspected the domain forests and groves of Minami-Fukuchi Village, Kita-Fukuchi Village, and Kainuma Village, and stayed at the house of the village headman there.

5th day
One　Today I reported my departure as follows:

　　　this matter.

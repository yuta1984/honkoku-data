22nd day
One　For the Iijima Water God Festival on the upcoming 28th, I delivered
　　one ryō of festival money to Matazaburō of Kurokōchi Village, who serves as the annual representative.

27th day
One　Tomorrow, the 28th, I will go out for the Iijima Water God Festival in Nakao Village,
　　then make rounds to Nakatsu District to inspect various government forests,
　　and also inspect the Shingū River ferry crossing and flood control works.
　　I submitted the following to the office director:
　　　I will be on duty from Irinoya District to
　　　Nakazawa District starting before dawn tomorrow, the 28th, for official business.
　　　I hereby report this matter.
　　　　June 27th　　　　Iwasaki Minato
　　　　To the Domain Office

28th day
One　I took along junior clerk Matsuzawa Ritōta to inspect the government forests of Himochi Village and Mizoguchi Village,
　　then stopped by the Kurokōchi Valley gate area.
　　From around 8 o'clock, I went to the Iijima Water God shrine in Nakao Village, where
　　not only the contractors but also officials from the five timber villages
　　had gathered. The shrine priest was conducting services for Mishima Shrine from Ichise Village,
　　and the ritual offerings were provided by the village. In the evening,
　　I retired to stay at the house of village headman Matazaburō in Kurokōchi Village.

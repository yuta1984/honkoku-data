　　　　　　　Article Two
　　　One　Regarding the unavoidable felling of standing dead trees, uprooted trees, trees broken by wind and snow, and rotted decayed wood that
　　　　　obstruct traffic and harm fields and good trees,
　　　　　the tree species and dimensions shall be examined,
　　　　　the appropriateness of the price determined, and felling may be permitted

　　　　　　　Article Three
　　　One　For felling trees and bamboo for railways, shipbuilding, official building repairs,
　　　　　waterways, conduits, bridges, embankments, etc.,
　　　　　depending on circumstances, officials from the relevant department may be
　　　　　dispatched, but for matters handled by
　　　　　government offices, the responsible officials shall inspect
　　　　　and prohibit excessive felling

　　　　　　　Article Four
　　　One　Pine, cedar, cypress, hemlock, zelkova, oak, chestnut, camphor, beech,
　　　　　and shōshi trees are essential materials for the nation,
　　　　　therefore they should be carefully cultivated, and even in private forests
　　　　　deep care and loving nurturing should be applied

Sixth month (small)

4th day
- Went to attend the register for the start of the summer season
　　　I respectfully inquire during the summer season　　　　Iwasaki Minato
　　　How are you feeling?

- Went out to investigate the remaining timber at Shimo-gawara

- For those who desired the confiscated timber from both head priests of Hokoji,
　　an auction was held, and Shinshichi of Hokoji village won the highest bid,
　　so he was summoned with a village official in attendance and the timber was handed over. The price was five ryō.

7th day
- For the auction of unused timber from Itayama village, the highest bid was two ryō and two bu,
　　and I was instructed by the bureau chief to pay two kan mon to Kitahara Bunzō,
　　so I summoned him and conveyed this.

9th day
- Since he came today regarding the above matter, we discussed the payment and I
　　received the payment.

10th day
- Made the following payment to the treasury:
　　　　　　Record
　　　　Five ryō in gold
　　　This is paid to the treasury as the fee collected for the disposal
　　　of confiscated timber from both head priests of Hokoji.
　　　　Sixth month, 10th day　　　　Iwasaki Minato
　　The above was submitted to the bureau chief as equivalent to penalty money.

- Accounting for the festival money for Benzaiten and Iijima water deity

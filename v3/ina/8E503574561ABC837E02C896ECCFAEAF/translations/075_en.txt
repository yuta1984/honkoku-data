3rd day
One: Suzuki Kinjū departed for Mitsushima Village today.

7th day
One: Since the Industrial Development Bureau was abolished this time, and orders were given to sell off all the previously used equipment and tools, we called out the second-hand dealer Eirakuya Seizaburō to appraise the items, compiled them in a ledger, and submitted it to the Bureau Chief.

8th day
One: Went to the Industrial Development Bureau and sold off various tools and equipment.

10th day
One: For the handover of the said bureau, Maintenance Department's Furuta Rōzō, Inoue Takuhei《marginal note: clerk｜》and Takahashi Genshichi came, so we completed the handover according to the separate building fixtures inventory, and afterwards submitted the following note to the Bureau Chief:

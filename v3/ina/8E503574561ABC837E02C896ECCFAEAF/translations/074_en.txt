However, their statements were extremely contradictory, so we instructed them to submit written statements, and when they submitted documents the following 28th day, we discussed the matter further. Since it was difficult to reach an immediate decision, and given the current circumstances, there was no need for prolonged investigation at the assignment location. Having completed the site inspection and hearing of claims, we agreed that detailed questioning should be conducted at the Shiojiri office. Yesterday, the 29th, we departed from Seba-juku, and Assistant Official Iwasaki and all others have now withdrawn. Details will be reported in a separate ledger copy. We have agreed that further consultation will be necessary depending on how the investigation proceeds. This matter is hereby reported.

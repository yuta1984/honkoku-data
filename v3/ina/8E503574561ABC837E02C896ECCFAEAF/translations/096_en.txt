12th month, major
2nd day
1. The firewood was paid for as follows according to the price board:
　　　Firewood: 160 ken
　　　　Payment: 74 ryō 3 bu
　　　　　Cash: 1 kan 906 mon
　　The above was paid to Sugimoto Kanjū, Ōta ■, and Tsuruya ■■zō. The payment fee was received from Suzuki Kinjū and paid to the temporary receipt account.

3rd day
1. Went out regarding the arrival at Kamikawara firewood delivery site.

4th day
1. Submitted the following [note on right: "to the Bureau Chief"]:
　　　Regarding the matter of firewood distribution to all, as reformed this spring, all should be distributed this winter according to the assessed amount, payment in rice to be collected at market rates during next spring, distributed without distinction between summer and winter rivers, and for other desired directions, appropriate payments should be arranged, and we would like this to be abolished in the future. We respectfully inquire about this matter.
　　　　Kanoe-hitsuji [1871], 12th month　　　　Forestry Department

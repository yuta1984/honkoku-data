12th month (long month)

2nd day
1. The firewood was paid for according to the following rate board:
　　　Firewood: 160 ken
　　　　Payment: 74 ryō 3 bu
　　　　　Cash: 1 kan 906 mon
　　The above was paid to Sugimoto Kanjū, Ōta Rissai, and Tsuruya Sake Brewery. The payment was received from Suzuki Kinjū and paid to the temporary receipt account.

3rd day
1. Went out regarding the arrival at Kamikawara firewood delivery site.

4th day
1. Submitted the following [note on right: "to the director"]:
　　　Regarding the matter of firewood distribution to all, as reformed this spring, distribute all without remainder this winter according to assessment, collect payment in rice at market rates during next spring, distribute without distinction between summer and winter rivers. For other desired directions, we would like to discontinue the appropriate payment collection system in the future. We humbly inquire about this matter.
　　　　Kanoe-hitsuji [1871], 12th month　　　　Forestry Department

12th month (full month)
2nd day
1. The honorable firewood was paid for with the following price board:
　　　Firewood: 160 ken
　　　　Price: 74 ryō 3 bu
　　　　　Cash: 1 kan 906 mon
　　The above was paid to Sugimoto Kanjū, Ōta Rissai, and Tsuruya Sake Brewery, the payment was received from Suzuki Kinjū, and paid to the temporary receipt account.

3rd day
1. Went out regarding the arrival at Kamikawara firewood delivery site.

4th day
1. Submitted the following [note on right: "to the director"]:
　　　Regarding the matter of firewood distribution to all, as reformed this spring, all should be distributed this winter according to the assessed amount, payment in rice should be collected at market price during next spring, distribution should continue regardless of summer or winter river conditions, and for other requests, appropriate payments should be arranged, but we would like this system to be abolished in the future. We humbly inquire about this matter.
　　　　Kanoe-hitsuji [1871], 12th month　　　　Forestry Department

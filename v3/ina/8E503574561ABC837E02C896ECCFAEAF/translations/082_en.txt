October (small month)
1st day
One: Since I am returning to work from today, I went to see the bureau chief.

One: The investigation ledger for timber cutting fees for prefectural firewood transport during this summer's river work was completed, so it was submitted by a junior clerk. I showed it to the bureau chief and sent it around to the granary department.

One: There was a notification from the office head that there were no discrepancies in the magistrate's accounts from the previous rabbit year (1867). I expressed my relief on the spot and conveyed this to the bureau chief.

One: I issued the following directive to Irinoya Township:
　　　This time, regarding the downstream transport of official firewood from Mimpukawa, Oyokokawa, and Kumazawa rivers only, the villages should ensure there are no obstructions to irrigation channels and river maintenance work. This directive should be communicated down to all small farmers and villagers.

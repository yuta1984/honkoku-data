Regarding Princess Kazunomiya's journey to Edo, the expenses for ceremonial uniforms (mon-bei) and equipment (osase) are not insignificant. With the lord's consideration to avoid burdening the lower classes as much as possible, various directives were issued and matters were settled through arrangements with others. However, after the lord's attendance in Edo in Bunkyū 2 (Year of the Dog), when the government offices and such received imperial orders and were burned down, for seven years there were no proper entrance halls or lord's reception rooms. Although they managed with great difficulty, this was truly inconvenient, so they had no choice but to undertake construction of the entrance hall facilities.

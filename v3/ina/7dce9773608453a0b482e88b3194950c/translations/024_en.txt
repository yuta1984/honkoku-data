Due to the earthquake in the 10th month of Ansei 2 (Year of the Rabbit), fires broke out in various places, and the upper mansion in Edo was burned down all at once. At that time, everyone made donations out of goodwill, and with land borrowing funds and other emergency funds, construction was undertaken only for the living quarters and long houses. However, in the 8th month of the same 3rd year (Year of the Dragon), heavy wind and rain destroyed various parts of the upper and lower mansions, continuing for two years with enormous expenses. After that, in the 7th month of the same 6th year (Year of the Sheep), there were substantial expenses for retirement and succession of family headship.

On the 25th of last month,
the Shogun arrived at
Osaka Castle.
The following shall be announced. That is all.
June 6th
As stated above,
an official document has been issued from the bakufu government, so it shall be
announced throughout the town.
June 25th

Among the Choshu domain rebel forces, some have
deserted from their home domain and are said to be
hiding in various locations. Therefore, any suspicious-looking
persons should be immediately apprehended
or struck down upon sight, and this should be enforced by organized groups.

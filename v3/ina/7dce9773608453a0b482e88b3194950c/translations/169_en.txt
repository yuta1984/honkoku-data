Memorandum
Water should be sprinkled along the route of passage, and buckets should be placed out.
Also, tonight around the eighth hour, the magistrate 
will be coming down, so please keep this in mind.
So that the route of passage can be clearly seen,
each town should place out lanterns.
Item: For the towns that will attend as representatives,
they should rest at Momenya Zenbei's place,
so please keep this in mind.
August 18th

Keio 2nd year, Year of the Fire Tiger (1866)
February 3rd

Item: 8 ryo, 1 bu, remainder 324 mon
The above amount was received from Mr. Fujisawa Mataemon of Yokomachi as rotating funds for the ten towns. This was conducted by the town elders and is to be delivered to Hokomochimachi of the lower town.

Regarding the above, we shall distribute money proportionally to the members of the mutual financing association (mujin-ko). However, any shortfall will be allocated again on November 10th.

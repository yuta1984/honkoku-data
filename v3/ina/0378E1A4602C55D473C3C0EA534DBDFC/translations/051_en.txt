[From the sixth line]
1. Then the retainer guided [us] to the guest reception room, and all the osente sat in attendance on the veranda, placing their fans and swords behind them
1. Shimotsuke-dono came out to the guest reception room, and when the retainer bowed, [I] held the petition document in my right hand, made seasonal greetings at the threshold, and all the osente stood up and sat down about one ken (approximately 6 feet) away from him. [I] explained that we were petitioning for succession due to Itakura Iyo-no-kami's serious illness, approached close to him, took the outer wrapper of the petition document and presented it. The outer wrapper was placed to his right side, and [I] sat down again about one ken away. After he finished reviewing it and bowed, [I] made seasonal greetings, and all the osente withdrew and immediately returned
　　However, the documents that the accompanying osente submitted were received by the retainer at the service entrance. Also, when returning, [I] first conveyed greetings to the retainer that [the documents] had been received without delay
1. At the gate of Shimotsuke-dono's residence, Itakura Iyo's caretaker had come out, so [I] informed him that the petition document had been submitted without delay and returned home directly

Document for Transfer of House and Lot

Item: We own a separate residence in the same town, with a frontage of 2 ken 3 shaku 5 sun and a depth of 21 ken, as recorded in the house registry. Since the original owner has passed away, and due to Bansuke's marital relationship, we hereby transfer this property in perpetuity. There is no dispute regarding this matter. Concerning this house and lot, neither relatives nor any other parties have any objections whatsoever. Should anyone raise any disputes, we will resolve the matter promptly in the presence of witnesses.

Regarding service to the government, as half-duty has been performed up to now, please continue to serve in the same manner hereafter. This transfer document is executed for future reference.

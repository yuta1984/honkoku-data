Respectfully submitting this oral petition

Regarding Genzō, who belongs to the religious sect within the grounds of Saizenji Temple, along with his wife and children, I am a person of substance who has had connections for many years. Therefore, after consultation, I petition that from this year of the Tiger, they be added to the population register of Hokomotimachi from our religious sect.

Regarding the above matter, if you would graciously grant this petition as requested, it would be a grateful blessing. The above.

Bunsei 13, Year of the Metal Tiger, 2nd month　　Fujiya

When the above petition was submitted, there were problems on the side of Ōmiya Heizaemon, so we have decided to make individual petitions. December 12th

Respectfully submitting this oral petition

1. Though I am an incompetent person, I have been graciously appointed to the position of town representative and have been serving in this blessed capacity. However, in recent years business has become poor, and I find it difficult to fulfill my official duties. Therefore, I petition that you grant me release from my official position.
Regarding the above matter, if you would graciously grant my petition as requested, it would be a most grateful blessing. The above.

Bunsei 13, Year of Metal Tiger, 1st month　　Hokomotimachi
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Petitioner: Fujiemon
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Town representative: Yaohyakuemon

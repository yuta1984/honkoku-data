I humbly submit this oral petition
I have been engaged in the indigo dye trade until now, but business has been unsuccessful
and I believe it is difficult to continue this trade, therefore I wish to
cease the indigo dye business and submit this petition
Regarding the above matter, if you would kindly grant my petition through your compassion,
it would be most grateful. The above
　　　　　　　　　　　　　Hokomachi-machi Dye shop
　Bunka 15, Year of the Earth Tiger, 2nd month　　　Petitioner　Heikichi
　　　　　　　　　　　　　Town Representative Kichibei
　　　　　　　　　　　　　Same Jizaemon
　　Arakawa Sehei-sama
　　Nishina Uemonhachi-sama

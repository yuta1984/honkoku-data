Third month of Temmei 3, year of the Rabbit (1783)
　When ceremonial meals are bestowed during celebrations, the seating magistrates receive them in the Yanaginoma (Willow Room) partitioned by folding screens. During Noh performances by court nobles, they receive them in the Nakanoma (Middle Room).

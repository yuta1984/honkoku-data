Memorandum

1. Regarding Morimoto Sōbei, Kanamaru Matazaemon, and Hasegawa Rokubei:
   Three petitions have been submitted and received requesting permission to travel to Edo this spring, which were addressed to Maeda Aki-no-kami.
   Details will be reported by the Head of Finance.

1. Regarding Deputy Mannnen Den'bei: Due to illness affecting his speech, he has become unable to perform his official duties. A written report submitted to Aki-no-kami has been forwarded and received. This matter will also be reported by the Head of Finance.

The Mountain Dispute between Akaho and Miyata
Kyōhō 12th year
　　Two hundred twenty-three years ago
A copy of the judgment document
that received the shogunate's arbitration

Addendum
　At the residence of Mr. Ikegami Shinshichi in Akaho,
　there are excellent maps from this time,
　bearing the seals of Ōoka Echizen-no-kami and all
　the senior councilors (rōjū), which are
　preserved there.

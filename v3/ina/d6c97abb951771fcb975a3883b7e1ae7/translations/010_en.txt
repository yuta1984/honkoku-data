2nd month of the Boar Year　　　Town Representative

To the Town Officials

Regarding the above circumstances reported by the town representatives, we have consulted among ourselves repeatedly. As the district representatives state, the town quarters are increasingly declining, which we find deeply lamentable. We have worried greatly and attempted various measures, but as matters regarding official petitions are not simple affairs, we first wish to make informal inquiries up to your level. We submit the above document for your consideration. Though we realize you are busy with many official duties, we humbly ask that you read this carefully and, after wise deliberation on all matters, petition Genbei-sama for measures that will form the foundation for the prosperity of the town quarters. The above.

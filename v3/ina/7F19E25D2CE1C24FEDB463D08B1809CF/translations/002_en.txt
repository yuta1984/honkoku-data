Humbly submitting this written petition

I have been contracting to supply firewood for official use for the past two years, and I wish to apply again this year. Regarding the price of firewood, I have received word that it is considered too high, so I consulted with the woodcutters. Concerning the mountain cutting wages, since rice prices have risen from those of two years ago to last winter's high prices, the woodcutters are expressing hardship.

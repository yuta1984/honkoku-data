A written pledge submitted

Article 1: Regarding those who go to other provinces for stone-cutting work, the five-household group shall conduct strict examination, verify the population register, and arrange guarantors before allowing them to depart. Naturally, arrangements shall be made to ensure that annual rice taxes and various duties are paid without delay before departure.

Article 2: Regarding the prohibitions issued by the authorities, it goes without saying that we fully understand and comply with the instructions regularly given to us.

Article 3: Regarding the designated lodgings for these people during their travels, there is no discrepancy from what is recorded in the attached document. Should there be anyone who does not return home by year's end, the guarantors and the five-household group shall promptly report this matter without delay.

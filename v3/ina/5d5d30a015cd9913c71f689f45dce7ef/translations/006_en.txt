We pledge to devote ourselves wholeheartedly to prayers for peace throughout the realm. Accordingly, the aforementioned religious association will perpetually offer ten thousand purification rituals. We respectfully and humbly declare this.
　　　　　　　　　Both Head Priests of Hokomochi
Bunka 14, Year of the Ox　　　Ioka Iga ㊞
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Izawa Buzen ㊞

【Left page】
For the aforementioned perpetual initiation of the Daidai Okagura, one hundred mon of gold per unit shall be donated. The total association funds will be managed by town officials and chō-representatives who will oversee and circulate these funds, using the interest income to ensure the performances continue unfailingly for perpetuity. Since this is a perpetual arrangement, when officials change positions, the accounts shall be settled and transferred to their successors. As future proof, we affix our joint seals.

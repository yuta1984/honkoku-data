Regarding the magistrates of repairs, archery magistrate, firearms magistrate, and storehouse magistrate of this domain, there are official consultations concerning guard duty matters as well as discussions with retainers. Therefore, there are gifts to be given as well as items to be sent to lower-ranking officials. We have heard that gifts should be given gradually in any case. Since the aforementioned magistrates and their subordinates are particularly light-hearted individuals, there may occasionally be misunderstandings.

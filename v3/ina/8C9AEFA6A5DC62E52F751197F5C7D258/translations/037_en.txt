For the visit to the main citadel (honmaru), the guard captains (bantō-shū) departed first.
Upon seeing the guard captains depart, the additional guard (kaban), town magistrate, Sakai magistrate, and naval commander escorted them to the great hall's waiting area.
After that, they went through the reception hall again, bid farewell to the castle magistrate, and returned. The shift partners also all departed and exchanged courtesies at the entrance.

【〇】On the return, they went together with shift partners to the inspector's quarters, expressed gratitude for the shogun's will, and returned home.

【〇】On September 13th, Horikawachi-no-kami's retainer was summoned to the castle magistrate, and was informed through the retainer that on the 15th (the day after tomorrow), they should inspect the rear guard post firearms depot, Aoya-guchi drawbridge, multi-gate complex, multi-gate complex within Yamazato, and turret. Regarding this, Horikawachi-no-kami sent a response through a written statement from one person, while the remaining three units sent a joint written statement, with one messenger delivering the response.

【〇】On the same 15th day, when the castle magistrate was to inspect the multi-gate complex within Yamazato, the diamond turret, etc., wearing formal court dress with backing, around the time Mino-no-kami arrived at the Kabuki Gate, I waited at attention in front of the long spears before the great guard post. From below the great gate, our firearms officers led the way, followed by both building repair magistrates, while Mino-no-kami ascended the stone steps via the north passage of the great guard post, advanced a little and exchanged greetings, then also exchanged greetings with the accompanying regular guards, town magistrate, and inspector.
Mino-no-kami inspected the gate platform area from in front of the second gate, with our firearms officers leading the way, followed by the building repair magistrates leading, while I stood next to the regular guards and proceeded.
When Mino-no-kami passed through to the reception hall from in front of the third gate at the reception hall, I first descended the stone steps, and in the garden our retainer led the way, then I led and exchanged greetings below the veranda of the reception hall. Mino-no-kami passed through to the reception hall and subsequently took his seat in the reception hall. I also appropriately exchanged greetings with Mino-no-kami and others.

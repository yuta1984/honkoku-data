1. Same 9th year, year of the dog, 1st month, 15th day: appointed to treasury affairs
1. Same 12th year, year of the ox, 3rd month, 1st day: granted sequential rank in the lord's attendant guard, appointed to 
   concurrently serve as escort affairs and stable superintendent
1. Tenpō 2, year of the rabbit, 8th month, 16th day: for having devoted himself for several years to training disciples in Confucian studies, 
   when this matter reached His Lordship's attention, he was greatly pleased, 
   and therefore as a mark of praise, granted 200 hiki in gold
1. Same 6th year, year of the sheep, 10th month, 10th day: additional two hyō granted
1. Same 10th year, year of the boar, 12th month, 28th day: granted permission to establish a branch family, service rank and stipend 
   to remain as before
1. Kōka 4, year of the sheep, 8th month, 18th day: promoted in rank to chū-koshō, appointed to 
   inspector, continuing as escort affairs and stable superintendent as before for now, 
   additional two hyō and one person's stipend granted, total of twenty-two hyō and three person's stipend
1. Kaei 2, year of the rooster, 1st month, 28th day: due to numerous burdensome duties, 
   successfully relieved of stable superintendent position
1. Same year, 12th month, 1st day: when fire spread to the kitchen roof area, 
   he extinguished it and took care of the situation, therefore granted soup and sake
1. Same 6th year, mizunoto-ushi, 1st month, 15th day: additional two hyō granted, duties to continue as before

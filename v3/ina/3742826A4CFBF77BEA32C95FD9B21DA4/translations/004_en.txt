　　　　　　　　　Tanaka Ryūsen
During the reign of Lord Yorimoto
- On February 20th of Bunka 15, Year of the Tiger (1818), he succeeded to Tanaka Jin'an's family headship with a stipend of 18 hyō of rice for two persons, was appointed as formal foot soldier and chief physician, under the supervision of the magistrate

During the reign of Lord Yoriyasu  
- On May 5th of Bunpō 9, Year of the Dog (1826), he received an increase of two hyō as formal attendant, continuing as chief physician as before
- On May 13th of Tenpō 5, Year of the Horse (1834), he was granted an increase to a stipend of 20 hyō of rice for three persons
- On October 4th of the same year, he was ordered to exercise restraint for serving alcohol and food at a middle-rank gathering, and the restraint was lifted on the 24th of the same month
- On July 22nd of the same 11th year, Year of the Rat (1840), he was appointed as physician without land grant and granted an increase of two hyō (while stationed in Edo)
- On August 18th of the same year, he became second to Kobayashi Ichian
- On December 3rd of Kaei 3, Year of the Metal Dog (1850), he was ordered into house confinement in Edo for being stubborn regarding official travel matters and committing extreme falsification and disrespect. However, he was relieved of his Edo posting and the confinement was ordered upon his return
- On December 28th of the same year, the house confinement was lifted
- On February 8th of the same 4th year, Year of the Metal Pig (1851), he was ordered to retire as requested due to illness, and his son Jin'an was ordered to succeed to the family headship
　　　In service

　　　　　　　　　Tanaka Jin'an
During the reign of Lord Yoriyasu
- On February 8th of Kaei 4, Year of the Metal Pig (1851), due to his father Ryūsen's illness, he was ordered to retire as requested and succeeded to the family headship, receiving a stipend of 22 hyō of rice for three persons, granted the rank of formal attendant and chief physician, under the supervision of the magistrate

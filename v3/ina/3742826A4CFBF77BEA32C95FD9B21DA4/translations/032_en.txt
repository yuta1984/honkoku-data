Secretary (Goyūhitsu)
《Side note: Former master Mizuno Sōbee-sama, home province Mikawa, birth province Shinano, 37 years old》　Okutani Kōuemon
《Side note: Changed name to Genzaemon》

During the reign of Lord Genryūin
1. Kanpō 3, Year of the Water Boar, 6th month, 22nd day (1743): Newly appointed to the rank of middle page (chūkoshō), taken into service, granted a stipend of 35 hyō and provisions for 3 retainers, assigned to local residence duty

1. Hōreki 9, Year of the Earth Rabbit, 11th month, 4th day (1759): Appointed as supervisor of middle pages, concurrently serving as secretary

1. An'ei 4, Year of the Wood Sheep, 10th month, 8th day (1775): As requested, relieved of both positions, assigned to guard duty rotation

1. Same year, 12th month, 12th day: As requested, retired from active service
　　Service record

During the reign of Lord Genryūin
1. Hōreki 8, Year of the Tiger, 8th month, 9th day (1758): Called up as a foot soldier in the lord's retinue, granted a stipend of 16 hyō and provisions for 2 retainers

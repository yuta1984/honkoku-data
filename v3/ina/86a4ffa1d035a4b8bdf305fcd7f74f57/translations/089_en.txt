Respectfully submitting this verbal petition

I had previously lent a sake brewing license with a assessed rice yield of 15 koku to Kizaemon of Hirade Village. However, the authorities ordered it to be suspended (kyūkabu), and it remained inactive. Now, with the recent imperial restoration, active licenses (ritsukab) have been restored, and Kizaemon has petitioned to increase the rice assessment of this license to 100 koku. Since I have the documentation from when I originally lent the license, I negotiated for its return due to the suspension, but he would not agree. Therefore, I respectfully file this lawsuit and humbly petition for resolution.

After your investigation and gracious mediation, you ordered mediation talks, and an agreement was reached. Therefore, I respectfully petition that you return the petition document to me.

Regarding the above matter, if you would graciously grant my petition as requested, I would consider it a most grateful outcome. 

Meiji 3, Year of the Metal Horse, 10th month　　　　Nakamachi

To Mr. Kitahara Masutaro

Memorandum

One item: 1 koku, 7 to, 7 sho of rice　　From the 1st day of the 8th month of Tsuchinoto-Mi year
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Until the 29th day of the 1st month of Uma year
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　A total of 177 days, large and small combined

The above rice has been graciously granted as stipend rice for serving as uma-sashi (horse guide) of this town, distributed using the tax rice from Kawashimo village for the year of Tsuchinoto-Mi, which I respectfully acknowledge receipt of.
As proof thereof, I hereby certify as follows.

November, Meiji 2, Year Tsuchinoto-Mi (1869)　　Ikegami Shotaro

　　To Mr. Kitahara Masutaro

The above two certificates were submitted to the Civil Administration Bureau on November 8th.
To Mr. Inoue

go to the relief shelter, where they stayed receiving your benevolence. After they returned to town,
Zenrachi died this past June, and his wife, having been widowed, took their daughter and
went to her family home to Bunpei's household in Shimōōshima Village. However, since that person is also greatly
impoverished, he said he cannot care for the daughter. Originally,
Zenrachi came from Iwamura in Mino Province and was someone who had inherited an extinct family line,
so there are no relatives here at all. However,

as assistance. We took this last month and, with relatives and village officials present,
handed it over with earnest requests, and they agreed. However, recently
village officials have come repeatedly, telling us to take the daughter back to our district.

Respectfully submitting this oral petition

I wish to begin a tofu business, with new molds, and would like to conduct trade using the "well-frame" (井桁) mark as my seal. Therefore, I respectfully request that you grant me a business license.

Regarding the above matter　　　　　Yokomachi Hoteiya
November, Meiji 2, Year of the Snake (1869)　　Shichibee

Group head: Shohachi
Business license inspector: Jinemon
Same: Ikegami Kanemon
Town representative: Heijuro

To the Magistrate's Office

On November 22nd, approval was granted, with instructions to appear at the Industrial Development Bureau on the 25th bringing the seal impression. This was communicated to the town representative on the morning of the 23rd.

Memorandum
One portion: Premium white soybeans, 1.5 go per 100 mon
　　One block of tofu
　　　Price: 324 mon

We wish to sell at the above prices. The above.

November, Year of the Snake　　　　Tofu sellers association

The above was submitted on the morning of November 3rd and presented to Mr. Yamashita Yaemon the same day. On the evening of the 5th, we were ordered to sell at 272 mon starting from the 6th, and Yorozuya Kinji was called and informed.

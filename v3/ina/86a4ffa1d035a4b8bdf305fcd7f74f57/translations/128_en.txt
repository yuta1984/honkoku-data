Humbly submitting this oral petition

1. Regarding the matter of tofu prices: When we petitioned for a price reduction this past September, although it was difficult to sell at 268 mon per block, we saw signs that soybean prices would gradually decline, so we forced ourselves to continue selling until now. This time we have been ordered to sell at 218 mon per block, but the necessary materials are extremely expensive. Particularly at present, firewood and other items have risen exceptionally in price, and soybean prices have also increased since some time ago. Due to these mounting various expenses, we cannot continue business at the price ordered this time. We humbly beseech you to restore the previous price.

Furthermore, until soybeans, firewood, and other materials decrease in price, we petition that you approve the above matter. The above stated.

We submit the prices of materials needed for production:
- Premium white soybeans: 2 gō per 100 mon
- Per one form of tofu: oil extraction fee 100 mon  
- Same: nigari (coagulant) 72 mon
- Firewood for one form: 250 mon
The above are the current rates.

11th month of the Year of the Horse [1870] - Town Tofu Makers

The above was submitted on the 27th through Mr. Kondō's intermediation

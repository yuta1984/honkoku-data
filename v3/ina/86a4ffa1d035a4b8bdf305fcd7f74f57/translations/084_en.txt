Humbly submitted as a verbal petition

One item: Sake brewing base quota of 60 koku
　The above concerns a sake brewing license held by Waritōzaemon of Miyata-machi, which had become dormant.
　Recently, there have been discussions about permanently transferring it to us. Therefore,
　we humbly petition you. Of course, upon receiving approval, we earnestly request that
　one license be granted for the original quota of 60 koku.
Regarding the above matter, we humbly request your benevolent consideration as petitioned,
and if you would graciously approve this, we would consider it a most grateful blessing. The above stated.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Takasago-machi
　Meiji 3, Year of the Metal Horse, 10th month　　Petitioner: Keijirō
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Group Head: Taichi
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Town Representative: Jinzō
　　To the Magistrate's Office

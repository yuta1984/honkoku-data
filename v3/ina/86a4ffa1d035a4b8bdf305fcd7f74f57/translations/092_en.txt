Since it was purchased under Ginzō's ownership, it was bought in my own name for this purpose. Now that Ginzō has established a separate household, I would like to reside there under my own name, and I wish to provide a transfer document. However, the aforementioned Bunshirō has raised various objections and refuses to affix his seal to the property register, causing me great trouble. I humbly request that you summon Ginzō from Hokomotichō to investigate the circumstances of this purchase, and then explain the situation to Bunshirō so that he will seal my register and provide the ground rent that has been due. Therefore, I submit this petition.
Regarding the above matter, I humbly request your merciful consideration of this petition, and if you would kindly grant my request, I would be most grateful. The above.

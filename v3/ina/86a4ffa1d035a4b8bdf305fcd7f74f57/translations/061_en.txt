Humbly submitting this oral petition

Regarding Kanemon, who is a dependent of Hachigoro from Kitaogochi Village under the jurisdiction of Ina Prefecture, and his wife, who both received and inherited the succession of my relative Asaya Zenbei's house. As they were residing there but due to incompatibility, both parties discussed and decided on divorce, and we have privately discussed returning them to the aforementioned Hachigoro's household. Therefore, we humbly submit this petition.

Regarding the above matter          Shimo-machi
Meiji 3, Year of the Metal Horse, 6th month    Petitioner: Yuji
                                               Group head: Denuemon  
                                               Town representative: Jinuemon
To the Magistrate's Office

The above petition was submitted on the 22nd day through Mr. Nakayama's mediation at the government office, and on the same day we received notification from the same person that it was approved, which was conveyed to the town representative.

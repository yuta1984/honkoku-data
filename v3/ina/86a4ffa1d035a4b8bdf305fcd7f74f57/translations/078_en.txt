Respectfully submitted petition in writing

I wish to travel to Tokyo for business purposes. I respectfully request to be granted leave for twenty-five days for the round trip.

The above matter　　　　　　〽Shimizu-machi
Meiji 3, Kanoe-uma year, September　Town Representative: Tozo
　　　　　　　　　　　　　Group Head: Niemon
To the Magistrate's Office

This was submitted on the 12th through Mr. Nakayama's intermediation at the government office, and was immediately approved and communicated to Tozo. It was stated that departure would be at noon on the 14th.

Respectfully submitted petition in writing

Regarding my adopted son Taichi, I am embarrassed to petition about this troublesome matter, but eleven years ago in the Hitsuji year, his father Hikoemon rented a house with a deposit of fifteen ryo, and it was agreed that as collateral he would care for my mother. After five years passed, I adopted Taichi and arranged for him to meet my daughter Yone. However, Taichi sent Yone to his father Hikoemon's place, and being truly a lawless person, Yone had no choice but to leave home. Afterward, I was unaware of various matters, but in the Tora year I had Risuke inform him.

With great respect, I humbly submit this petition.
My relative Yamagataya Tarōbei has been granted a dwelling, and as there is also a common-law relationship involved, I would like to take in Ubei, the younger brother of Ōmiya Zenshirō, along with his wife and children, and have him inherit the house. Therefore, I respectfully submit this petition.
Regarding the above matter:　　　　Nakamachi
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Miyaya
March, 3rd year of Meiji (1870)　Petitioner: Iemon
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Group Head: Gihei
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Town Representative: Ichirōemon
To the Magistrate's Office

This was submitted on March 2nd, and approval was granted on the 5th, of which we have been notified.

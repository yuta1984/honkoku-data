Humbly submitted,

Regarding the judgment granted in the previous Year of the Dragon, for which we are most grateful, we have been unable to reach an amicable settlement despite various efforts, causing much trouble, and we deeply regret this. However, as we cannot feel at ease regarding the sake brewing license, we wish to obtain peace of mind. We humbly request that, in your mercy, you grant brewing licenses to both Iemon and Riheē.

First, regarding the sake brewing business and other financial accounts both internal and external related to the village headman's affairs at the branch family member Jirōzaemon's place: We have repeatedly visited him to discuss these accounts, but no settlement has been reached at all. Therefore, we recently requested Masuya Kichirōemon to negotiate with Jirōzaemon's side. However, they said that the accounts of Iemon's side and other detailed matters had been entrusted to Matsujirō, and that you should inquire with Matsujirō. When we again asked Kichirōemon to negotiate with Matsujirō, he refused, saying he knew nothing of such matters. Under these circumstances, we have no recourse for consultation. We humbly request that, in your mercy, you help Jirōzaemon understand as stated in the attached document.

Regarding the above matter,

Humbly submitted,
The blacksmith Masahachi from our group ran away from home during this spring, so we petitioned to have him removed from the kakuraku-cho (absconder registry). We were ordered to search for him and looked everywhere, but his whereabouts remain unknown to this day. Therefore, we humbly request that the go-suiyaku (collective responsibility) be pardoned.

Regarding the above matter　　　　　Shimo-machi
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Izumiya

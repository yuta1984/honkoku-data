Submitted mediation agreement
Regarding the land transfer from Kii-ya Kihei of Naka-machi to Fuji-ya Ginzo of Hokoji-machi:
This matter concerns a transfer that took place in the winter of the previous Boar year from blacksmith Masahachi of Shimo-machi to Toju, father of Kii-ya Kihei. At that time, there were inadequate considerations regarding Miyoshi-ya Bunshiro of Hokoji-machi, which resulted in an ongoing matter. This time, following a petition from the transferor Kihei, both parties were summoned for examination, and we were ordered to reach a mediation through your understanding. We have complied and reached an agreement whereby Ginzo paid ten ryo in gold as compensation to Bunshiro, Bunshiro transferred the building to Ginzo, and regarding the land, a new transfer from Kihei to Ginzo has been arranged through mediation and completed.

When she comes of age, we intend to have her inherit Zenrachi's household, but since she is
a person born with physical disabilities, we wonder how adoption arrangements and such might work. She is a pitiful
person, and we find this lamentable. Humbly, please exercise your wise consideration,
and by all means, with special compassion, please rescue her.
We earnestly petition you. Though it is presumptuous of us to make such a petition, we ourselves
are deeply ashamed, but we humbly
beseech your further mercy.

Regarding the above matter　　　Fukuromachi

Memorandum

One set of four government rifles (威筒)

Regarding the above matter, we respectfully requested to borrow these items, and we are deeply grateful that our request has been approved. We have certainly received the items that were handed over to us. We understand that we must handle them with great care, and we humbly acknowledge the instruction that we must not load them with ammunition during peacetime.
Therefore, we submit this receipt. The above.

October 24th, Meiji 2, Year of the Snake (1869)

In that case, should there be any official business to be conducted, it would also serve as a stopping place for traveling merchants, benefiting both towns' administrative offices. The number of artisans and merchants would increase, construction and repairs could be carried out, and naturally prosperity would continue permanently. However, even when members of our domain come to visit, we would establish and maintain regulations among our group after proper consultation, ensuring no problems arise. We humbly request your guidance regarding further administrative oversight.

We make this petition, earnestly hoping that you will hear about the prosperity of both towns and grant us your approval, which we petition for on behalf of everyone.

With this intent, we petition through your mercy as stated above. Should you graciously grant and implement this request, it would be most grateful and fortunate. The above.

Meiji 3, Year of the Metal Horse, November　　Right names
　　　　　　　　　　　　As written in the petition

This petition and memorandum were discussed with Ikegami-shi and Kasuga-shi of the bridge section, jointly prepared with fellow officials who gathered to discuss and compose the endorsement, sealed, and submitted on November 12th through Kondo-shi's intermediation at the government office. On the 18th of the same month, through Kondo-shi's intermediation, it was declared that the timing was not appropriate and the petition's content could not be accepted, that both parties were insufficient, and the petition and verbal statement were returned. As communicated by fellow officials, representatives of both districts were called and this was conveyed.
【Footnote】The petition and verbal statement were returned.

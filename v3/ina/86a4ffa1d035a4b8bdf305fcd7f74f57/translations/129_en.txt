Humbly submitting this oral petition
1. Laborers　　　　　　1,000 people
Regarding the above: This time we humbly perceive that the construction work on Benzaiten Bridge is no ordinary undertaking. When it is completed, we gratefully believe that the townspeople will prosper. Therefore, although it is a small contribution, we wish to provide assisting laborers, and if this could be accepted as a meritorious offering, we would consider it a most grateful fortune.
We petition regarding this matter. The above stated.

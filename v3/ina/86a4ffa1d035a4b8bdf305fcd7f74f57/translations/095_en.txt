To the Magistrate's Office

As petitioned above, we shall arrange for the official seal and act as intermediaries.
On November 3rd, word came from Mr. Kondo. There was an official notice that on the 5th, one should bring the seal and appear at the Eizen Bureau, and this was communicated to the town representatives.

Respectfully submitted as an oral petition
Item: Rice in 4-to measures　　　　400 bags
I have this time purchased promissory note rice from Masaya Kazo of Kamiho-shuku and will sell it to Tomiya Sobei of Narasaki in Koshu Province. Therefore, I respectfully petition that you allow passage through the grain export inspection office.
Regarding the above matter, may you mercifully grant as petitioned

Humbly submitted:
My adopted son Heibei is immature, so I wish to divorce him and have him return with his wife and children to Seihachi in Hokojimochi Village. Therefore, I respectfully submit this petition.

The above petition was submitted to Mr. Yazawa on the 19th day of the 2nd month, and approval was granted on the 22nd day and conveyed to the town representative.

Respectfully submitting this oral petition

I am an incompetent person, but unexpectedly received the appointment to the position of town representative (machi-dai), which I gratefully accepted and have been carrying out. However, due to illness, I find it difficult to perform my duties. Therefore, I respectfully request that I be relieved of this position.

Regarding the above matter　　　　Hokoji-machi
First month of Meiji 3, year of the Metal Horse　Petitioner: Jūzaburō
　　　　　　　　　　　Group leader: Eizō
　　　　　　　　　　　Same: Tōemon

【Footnote】"Saishichi was relieved, and a successor was appointed to fill the position"

All six petitions including the endorsements were submitted to the Civil Administration Bureau on the 22nd, and on the night of the 24th, regarding the approval letters for the four towns of Yoko-machi, Hon-machi, Naka-machi, and Hokoji-machi, the four town representatives were called and informed.

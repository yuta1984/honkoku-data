Respectfully submitted,
Regarding the rice field that Ota Rissai received from Kasuga Eiju-dono, which Kitahara Shozo is to buy back. In addition to that rice field, there is one plot of cultivated land that has been mortgaged to Usourceji-dono of Takada-shuku Daita since the year of the Dragon for a two-year term until this November, and I have co-signed for this. However, last year's interest payments have been delayed, leaving me with no excuse to offer to the other party. I have repeatedly requested Rissai to deliver the money provided by Shozo to Usourceji-dono, but he has completely ignored this request. Therefore, I can no longer take responsibility for having co-signed with Usourceji-dono. I humbly request your understanding so that this money may be repaid to Usourceji-dono.

The above matter　　　　　　　　Takasago-cho Petitioner
Meiji 3, year of the Metal Horse, November　　　　　Miyashita Heibei
　　To the Magistrate's Office
Regarding this petition: concerning the field retrieval matter, a petition was submitted by Kitahara Shozo to buy back from Eiju of Ohara village. Since Rissai was involved and there was a mediator leading to a settlement, after consulting with a colleague and hearing from mediator Tomishima Magohachi, believing it to be the previous endorsement (colleague's guarantee), it was submitted through Kondo-shi's intermediation. When told to provide an endorsement, the sealed document was submitted. November 4th.

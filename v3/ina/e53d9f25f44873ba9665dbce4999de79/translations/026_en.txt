　　　Article 4
After the initial issuance of land certificates, when ownership changes due to sale, transfer, exchange, or other matters such as pawning or foreclosure, the prescribed format previously communicated should be recorded on the back of the land certificate, and an application for certificate replacement should be submitted.
　　　Article 5
When the aforementioned replacement application is submitted, a new land certificate will be issued and the old

[Left page]
land certificate will be cancelled. During this process, the change of landowner should be recorded in detail in the village registry along with the date.
　　　Article 6
For mountains, forests, grasslands, and other types of land where land certificates have been issued for sales and transfers, the same procedures should be followed.
　　　Article 7
When wasteland is granted upon request, an application for a land certificate should be submitted in the same manner.

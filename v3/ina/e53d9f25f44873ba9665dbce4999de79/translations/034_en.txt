　Article 3
1. When mortgaging land certificates, the land may also be transferred to the creditor, and whether the landowner becomes a tenant farmer, or the creditor cultivates it himself, or has others work it as tenant farmers, shall be freely decided by mutual agreement between the parties.

　Article 4
1. In all cases of mortgage transactions, whether the land is transferred to the creditor or remains with the landowner is at the discretion of mutual agreement,

【Left Page】
but the land tax and various duties must be fulfilled by whoever controls and manages the land.
　However, when the creditor fulfills the land tax and various duties, notification must be given and documentary evidence must be submitted.

　Article 5
1. It is not permitted to mortgage only the land while keeping the land certificate with the landowner.

　Article 6
1. Land may be bought and sold regardless of whether the parties are nobility (kazoku), former samurai (shizoku), or commoners (heimin).

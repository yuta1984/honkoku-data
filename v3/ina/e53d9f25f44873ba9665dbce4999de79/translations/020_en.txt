　　Jinshin 10th month
Counterfeiting currency is strictly prohibited by the state, but in recent years there have been those who violate the law, and consequently counterfeit one-ryō notes (issued by the Daijōkan Bureau of Accounting) and two-bu notes (issued by the Ministry of Civil Affairs Commercial Bureau) are frequently circulating. We are conducting a strict search for these criminals, so district patrol guards should also pay close attention and [apprehend] those who use counterfeit bills

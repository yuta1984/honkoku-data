Regarding the current Monkey year (1872): five-tenths shall be paid within the year, two and a half tenths from the following January to February, and two and a half tenths from March to April to complete payment. While it is natural that payment should be completed promptly according to autumn harvest income, considering the benevolent intention that selling all rice and grain at once in a country accustomed to full payment would naturally affect market prices and cause hardship to the common people, this has been revised as stated in the separate document. We gratefully accept this, and shall inform everyone from small landowners to the lowest ranks without exception, ensuring payment is made on schedule without any

　　　9th month, 25th day of Mizunoe-Saru (September 25, 1872)
Transportation companies shall be established at post stations along the Nakasendo, Koshu Highway, both Hokurikudo highways, Ina Highway, and other secondary routes. Applications have been submitted to establish fair regulations for travel convenience regardless of whether travelers are samurai or commoners, public or private. The applications have been approved as requested, and operations will begin from the 1st day of the coming 9th month. Therefore, understanding this intent, travelers shall pay the company's standard fees when traveling.

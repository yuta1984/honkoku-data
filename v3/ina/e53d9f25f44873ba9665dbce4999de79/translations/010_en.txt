Regarding matters related to Shinto priests' funerals, since Proclamation No. 193 was issued previously, when Shinto priests submit requests concerning Shinto burial grounds, appropriate suitable land should be selected and reported.
　　However, if there is a desire to change to Shinto burial within temple grounds, after consultation, this should be communicated in advance to temples within the jurisdiction so that there will be no obstacles whatsoever.

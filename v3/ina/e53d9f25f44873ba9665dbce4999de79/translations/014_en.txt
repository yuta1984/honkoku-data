Regarding the prefectural system that was announced in the sixth month of Meiji 2 (1869), 
matters from before that time shall not be subject to judicial proceedings.
Article 1: For Shizuoka, Sendai, Aizu and other domains that were reestablished,
money and grain lending from before their reestablishment
shall not be subject to judicial proceedings.
Article 1: From now on, when people of all classes, high and low, mutually
agree on terms for lending money and silver, and when the term arrives
but repayment is not made, even if private discussions and repeated demands are made,

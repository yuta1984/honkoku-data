When such persons are spotted, they should be thoroughly questioned, and if they appear suspicious, they should be promptly arrested and turned in.
The above instructions should be strictly communicated from the head and deputy village heads to the patrol personnel.

The buying and selling of human beings, and subjecting them to harsh treatment at the master's discretion either for life or for a fixed period, goes against human morality and is abhorrent, which is why it has been prohibited since ancient times. However, various pretexts such as term service have been used

【Left page】
to make people serve as live-in servants, when in reality this amounts to the same practice as buying and selling, which is outrageous. Therefore, this shall be strictly prohibited from now on.
Apprentice service for learning the various trades of agriculture, industry, and commerce is acceptable, but the term shall not exceed seven full years.
　　However, extending the term through mutual agreement between both parties is acceptable.
Regular servants should serve for one year at a time.

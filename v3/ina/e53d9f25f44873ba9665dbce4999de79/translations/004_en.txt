The number of households in the Imperial nation is 7,058,960 households and more
It has been heard that recently there are those who conduct funerals by themselves, but this will not be permitted henceforth. Therefore, funerals must be requested through either Shinto priests or Buddhist monks
Those who have been using both common names and formal names should use only one name from now on　　Council of State

　　　　　　　　　　　From each village: three officials (village elders)
　　　　　　　　　　　From each district: head and deputy head

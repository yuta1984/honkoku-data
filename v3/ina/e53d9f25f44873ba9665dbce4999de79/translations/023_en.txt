Jinshi Year (1872), October 28th - Chikuma Prefectural Office
When children living with their fathers and elder brothers, or those living separately with separate assets, or when a father has already transferred the family headship to his child and retired to live separately with separate assets, borrow gold or silver individually, unless the family head of the main household has provided a guarantee seal on the certificate, creditors must not lend money using the main household's assets as collateral. If such persons fail to repay and litigation ensues,

[Left page]
in such cases, for those living together, judgment shall be rendered within the scope of their personal possessions, and for those living separately, judgment shall be limited to their own assets only. This is communicated for your knowledge.
Jinshi Year, September 18th - Grand Council of State
Araki Hakuomi, Assistant Administrator of this prefecture, was appointed to the seventh rank position at the Ministry of Justice on the 22nd of last month. This is communicated for your knowledge.

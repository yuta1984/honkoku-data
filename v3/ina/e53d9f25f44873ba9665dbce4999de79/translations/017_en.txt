Regarding land rent and shop rent, we have heard that there have been occasions when restrictions were imposed, beginning with Tokyo Prefecture. However, from now on, both parties shall be free to negotiate and make rental agreements through mutual consultation.

Regarding wages and employment fees for various servants, craftsmen, laborers, etc.,

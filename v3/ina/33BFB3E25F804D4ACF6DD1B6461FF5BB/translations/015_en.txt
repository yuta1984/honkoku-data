One: Regarding advance payments, sustenance rice, and other loans and borrowings for daily laborers working in forestry,
　　written contracts shall be made, and clear accounting
　　shall be conducted during later settlements. Even if there are defaults due to careless handling,
　　matters without evidence shall be resolved between the parties involved, and appeals shall not
　　be made to the authorities.

　The above articles must be strictly observed by all woodworkers.

　Anyone who violates these shall be subject to punishment.

　　　Ansei 5, Year of the Earth Horse, 11th month (November 1858)

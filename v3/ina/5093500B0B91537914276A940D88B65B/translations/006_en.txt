Memorandum

1. 4 kan 200 mon in copper coins - lodging fee in town for Yakuro and Chuemon for seven days in the fifth month

1. 6 kan 232 mon in copper coins - sake expenses for entertaining the aforementioned two men repeatedly during their stay

Total: 10 kan 432 mon in copper coins - converted to 6 kan 700 mon, equivalent to 1 ryo 2 bu in gold and 380 mon in copper coins

The above represents the itemized miscellaneous expenses incurred when these two men came to negotiate regarding the river transport vessel matter.

Man'en 1, Year of the Metal Monkey, 10th month - Inazawa Ichizo (seal)
To: Shimada Kojuro-sama
    Ohira Yaemon-sama

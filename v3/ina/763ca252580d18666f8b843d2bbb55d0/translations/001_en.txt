Document of Settlement Agreement Submitted

Regarding the lawsuit filed by Kyūzō of Niekawa-juku in Kiso: There was a loan made to Denji of Hiraide Village in your domain, so Kyūzō's representative Chūshichirō and accompanying official Kanazawa Ken'jūrō came forward to make a petition. The opposing parties - Denji, Hachiemon of the same village, and Sehachi - were summoned, and while the matter is currently under investigation, through special compassion, Ikegami Kichibei and Fujisawa Rokurōemon have been appointed as mediators.

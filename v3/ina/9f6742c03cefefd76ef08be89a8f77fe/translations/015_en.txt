- When appointed as district representative, accompanied by this office,
　one shall offer ceremonial greetings to
　the Senior Retainer and Lord Magistrate, whereupon town administration is assigned,
　and thereafter all district representatives shall
　make rounds personally with fellow officials and register attendance

- When assigned to preparation duties, this office
　shall accompany for ceremonial rounds, but currently
　district representatives accompany for attendance registration

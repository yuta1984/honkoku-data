- Town representative duties begin at six-and-a-half o'clock (around 1 PM) at the designated
　location

- We shall proceed downstream as usual
　to assist with operations following
　the magistrate, make our respectful inquiry, and then
　all together take rest at Honkoin Temple

- When the district magistrate is replaced and hands over duties,
　he appears in the main hall, and three of us
　are granted audience, followed by town servants and town
　representatives being summoned, wearing formal attire
　for the audience. This time,
　the town servants go together first,
　then we perform individual greetings, with junior officials making
　presentations and scribes handling acknowledgments

　When town representatives attend,
　we take the lead as during New Year's greetings,
　with presentations and acknowledgments handled by scribes
After this is completed, only the new magistrate
appears for the formal thanks

- For both His Lordship's departure and return to the castle, after
　the audience is completed, we perform courtesy rounds on that day
【Left Page】
　We report this duty, and after completing the six-mon courtesy rounds,
　we report as ordered during the past Tempo
　2nd year (Year of the Rabbit)
　when His Lordship returned to the castle. Again in Tempo
　6th year (Year of the Sheep), following the same
　procedure
　　　Courtesy rounds to:
　Chief Retainer, Elder
　Chamberlain, District Magistrate  
　Chief Inspector, Administrator

We accompany all of these visits, but
for the magistrate, we record in the register together
with the town representatives

- When we change names, we must submit a petition

During the reign of Lord Yoriyasu

1. On the 15th day of the 6th month of the same 8th year, tori (1837), appointed to personal attendant service

1. On the 2nd day of the 2nd month of the same 11th year, ne (1839), due to illness, dismissed from personal attendant service as requested and ordered to return to regular guard duty, with priority given to local garrison duty
1. On the 26th day of the same month of the same year, appointed to lower mansion administrative duties
1. On the 5th day of the 10th month of the same year, appointed to personal attendant service for the retired lord

Endō Kosen
Later called Zenji

During the reign of Lord Genryūin
- On the 15th day of the 1st month of An'ei 8, year of the Earth Boar (1779), he was promoted to the rank of kyūjin (stipend recipient) and ordered to move to Edo. His duties remained the same as before, and he was granted an additional 5 hyō, totaling 40 hyō.
- On the 23rd day of the 10th month of the same year, he was appointed to the pharmaceutical department.
　Due to the death of the retired lord, he was successfully released from both his duties as personal attendant and pharmaceutical officer, and was assigned to a position according to his rank in the guard rotation.

During the reign of Lord Honryūin
- On the 29th day of the 9th month of the same year
　He was appointed as attendant to Lady Shōun'in, Princess Isamu, and Princess Tsune, and moved to the upper residence.
- On the 5th day of the 7th month of Tenmei 3, year of the Water Rabbit (1783), he was granted the rank of messenger, with duties remaining the same as before.
　He was ordered to serve as attendant to Lord Okō.
- On the 8th day of the 4th month of Tenmei 6, year of the Fire Horse (1786)
　He was successfully released from his duties as attendant to Lady Shōun'in and the princesses, and was assigned to manage the domain's local affairs.
- On the 4th day of the 2nd month of the 7th year, Hinoto-Hitsuji (1787), he was appointed as financial magistrate.
- On the 23rd day of the 4th month of Kansei 1, year of the Earth Rooster (1789), upon his request, he was released from his duties and ordered to return to regular guard duty.

During the reign of Lord Yorimoto
- On the 28th day of the 12th month of the 5th year, Ox (1793), he was ordered into house confinement.
- On the 2nd day of the 12th month of the 9th year, Snake (1797), he died of illness.

　As his late father Zenji had requested on his deathbed, he was granted succession without change and assigned to a position according to his rank in the guard rotation.

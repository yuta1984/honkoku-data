【Location: Takato】Town Doctor
Suzuki Shun'an

During the reign of Lord Genryuin
- Kan'en 3 (1750), 5th month, 15th day: Having resided in the castle town for several years and devoted himself to medical practice, he was granted a stipend of 3-person rations.

- Horeki 5 (1755), 5th month, 9th day: Due to financial constraints of the domain, he petitioned to return his stipend. His request was heard, and he was granted a 1-person stipend for three years, continuing to serve in medical treatment for the lord's retainers without change.

- Same 9th year (1759), 2nd month, 28th day: He accompanied the treasurer (kurando) to Edo during the previous winter's official trip. Considering his service until this time to be commendable, he was granted 3 ryo as a reward.

- Same 11th year (1761), 4th month, 6th day: He accompanied the treasurer on an official trip to Edo in the spring and remained there for an extended period. When this came to his lordship's attention and was deemed commendable service, he was granted 5 ryo as a reward, announced while 【Location: Edo】.

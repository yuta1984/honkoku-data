- On May 13th of Bunsei 7, year of Saru (1824), he had his first formal audience with the lord
- On January 15th of Bunsei 9, year of Inu (1826), inheriting his deceased father Hasuke's position, he received 26 hyō and rations for 3 people, and was formally appointed to the middle page alternate duty
- On October 15th of Tenpō 6, year of Hitsuji (1835), he was appointed to serve at Lord Ryōnoshin's residence and received 1 koku of service rice
- On December 2nd of the same year, due to improper conduct, he was dismissed from residence duty, received 22 hyō and rations for 2 people, and was ordered house confinement while serving in the escort guard
- On January 22nd of Tenpō 7, year of Saru (1836), house confinement was lifted
- On February 18th of Tenpō 14, year of U (1843), due to consistently improper conduct, his stipend was confiscated and he was ordered to retire, with his younger brother Seinosuke being granted succession to their father Hasuke's family name. This was formally announced to him at {{Location: Edo}} palace

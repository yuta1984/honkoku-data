Yamazaki Zen'emon

During the reign of Lord Genryūin:
- February 19, Genbun 2 (1737): Granted a stipend of 3 ryō 2 bu and summoned as an ashigaru (foot soldier)
- October 1, Genbun 3 (1738): Following his deceased father Gen'zaemon, granted 23 hyō of rice and 2-person food allowance, appointed to the ceremonial guard rotation under the steward's supervision
- April 28, Kan'en 4 (1751): Appointed as temporary official for clothing affairs and tea utensil custodian
- December 15, Hōreki 9 (1759): Released from temporary duties as requested
- Appointed as gate guard for Lord Shōgeiin
- April 19, An'ei 2 (1773): Promoted to middle page with an increase of 3 hyō and 1-person food allowance
  Assigned to serve Lords Tetsunoshin and Isamu

During the reign of Lord Honryūin:
- February 28, An'ei 7 (1778): Appointed as inner palace gate guard, released from personal attendant duties
(Written in red ink: "Yamazaki")

- On the 3rd day of the 3rd month of Bunsei 11, year of the Rat (1828), he was granted his first audience with the lord.
- On the 15th day of the 1st month of Tenpō 2, year of the Rabbit (1831), he was appointed to temporary employment and assigned as assistant to the guard unit. Accordingly, he was granted a stipend of two-person rations as financial support.
- On the 3rd day of the intercalary 11th month of the 3rd year, Dragon (1832), he was appointed to temporary duty at the water works.
- On the 3rd day of the 12th month of the 10th year, Boar (1839), due to personnel adjustments, he was successfully released from temporary duty at the water works.
- On the 22nd day of the 5th month of the 14th year, Rabbit (1843), due to financial reforms in the domain administration, he was unavoidably released from temporary employment. Accordingly, he was granted a one-person stipend. However, he might still be assigned duties from time to time in the future.
- On the 22nd day of the 7th month of the same year, he died of illness.
　　Service record: 13 years

　　　　　　　　Third son
　　　　　　　　Endō Tamesaburō

During the reign of Lord Yoriyasu
- On the 10th day of the 8th month of Kōka 2, year of the Snake (1845), his father Kinji had matters of concern and was ordered to retire, but through special benevolence, he was granted succession with a stipend of 50 hyō and three-person rations without change, and was appointed to the ranking order of the guard unit's stipend class.

- On the 1st day of the intercalary 4th month of Kaei 2, year of the Rooster (1849), he died of illness.
　　Service record: 5 years

　　　　　　　　Emergency adoption
　　　　　　　　Actually the younger brother of Saji Ippei
　　　　　　　　Endō Toyozō

During the reign of Lord Yoriyasu
- On the 8th day of the 9th month of Kaei 2, year of the Snake (1849), in accordance with Endō Tamesaburō's deathbed wishes, he was appointed as adopted son and granted succession with a stipend of 50 hyō and three-person rations without change, and was appointed to the ranking order of the guard unit's stipend class.
- On the 8th day of the 10th month of the same year, he was appointed to enter the lord's reception hall guard duty.
- On the 5th day of the 11th month of the 4th year, Boar (1851), he was ordered to relocate his residence and was granted the former residence site of Ōkubo Matsuichi in Shimoda-machi.
- On the 15th day of the 12th month of the 6th year, Ox (1853), he was appointed to temporary duty at the water works.

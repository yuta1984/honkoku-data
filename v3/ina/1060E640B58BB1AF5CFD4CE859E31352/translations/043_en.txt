Service Record

Son
Kitahara Yasohachi
Later called Hachirotayu

During the reign of Lord Honryuin
- On the 28th day of the 10th month of Tenmei 7, year of the Fire Sheep (1787): Inherited the family headship with a twenty-person stipend without change, appointed to personal attendance duty
- On the 13th day of the 11th month of Tenmei 8, year of the Earth Monkey (1788): Appointed to hair-dressing service
- On the 5th day of the 12th month of Kansei 1, year of the Rooster (1789): Relieved from personal attendance duty and appointed to the rank equivalent to a stipend recipient

During the reign of Lord Yorimoto
- On the 1st day of the 4th month of Kansei 6, year of the Tiger (1794): Appointed to the hierarchical position according to stipend rank, appointed as gunnery instructor for the unit
- On the 8th day of the 10th month of Kansei 10, year of the Horse (1798): Appointed as chief supervisor
- On the 15th day of the 1st month of Bunka 2, year of the Ox (1805): Promoted to messenger rank, continuing as chief supervisor as before
- On the 2nd day of the 4th month of Bunka 9, year of the Monkey (1812): Due to deep considerations by the lord, his rank, official duties, and five-person stipend from his total stipend were confiscated, and he was assigned to guard duty according to stipend rank and ordered into house confinement
- On the 5th day of the 3rd month of Bunka 12, year of the Boar (1815): Appointed as gunnery instructor for the unit
- On the 15th day of the 1st month of Bunka 15, year of the Tiger (1818): Appointed to messenger rank

During the reign of Lord Yoriyasu
- On the 14th day of the 3rd month of Bunsei 5, year of the Horse (1822): Because Kitahara Kakuzaemon had embezzled a tremendous amount of domain money, he should have been sentenced to death, but through exceptional mercy was sentenced to severe banishment. Due to this, [Yasohachi] was ordered into house confinement
- On the 13th day of the 4th month of the same year: Released from house confinement
- On the 26th day of the 7th month of Bunsei 6, year of the Sheep (1823): Died of illness

Kitahara Shozaemon
First generation

During the reign of Lord Yorimoto
- On the 3rd day of the 3rd month of Kansei 12, year of the Monkey (1800): First audience with the lord
- Same year: Appointed to hired service, regular guard duty

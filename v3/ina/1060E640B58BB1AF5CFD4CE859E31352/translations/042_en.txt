Younger brother of Kakuzaemon
Kitahara Tazo

During the reign of Lord Genryuin
- On the 25th day of the 12th month of Horeki 13, year of the Water Sheep (1763): Granted three-person stipend as financial assistance, permitted to wear a sword, and assigned to treasury duties
- On the 11th day of the 12th month of Meiwa 8, year of the Metal Rabbit (1771): Promoted to the rank of attendant guard, with an increase of four-person stipend, making it seven-person stipend in total
- On the 3rd day of the 2nd month of An'ei 3, year of the Wood Horse (1774): Promoted to the rank of middle page, assigned to the remittance office, and permitted to carry a spear when going out on official business to other places

During the reign of Lord Honryuin
- On the 25th day of the 2nd month of the same 6th year, Ding-You (1777): Increase of three-person stipend
- On the 22nd day of the 2nd month of the same 7th year, Wu (1778): Appointed in the same manner as his elder brother Kakuzaemon, promoted to a rank of twenty-person stipend
- On the 28th day of the 10th month of Tenmei 7, year of the Fire Sheep (1787): Ordered to retire by the lord's discretion

"(In red ink) Kitahara"

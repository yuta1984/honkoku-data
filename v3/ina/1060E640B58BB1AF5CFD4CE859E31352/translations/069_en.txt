During the reign of Shinnyoin-sama:
1. On the 19th day of the 7th month of Kyōhō 17, year of the Rat (1732), he was summoned to serve as middle page and awarded 7 ryō and provisions for three people.
1. On the 22nd day of the 4th month of Kyōhō 19, year of the Tiger (1734), he was appointed as retainer and granted a stipend of 40 hyō and provisions for three people, and was ordered to relocate to Edo.
1. On the 8th day of the 6th month of the same year, he was appointed to guard duty.

During the reign of Genryūin-sama:
1. On the 4th day of the 6th month of Kyōhō 20, year of the Rabbit (1735), he was granted his adoptive father's hereditary stipend of 40 hyō and provisions for three people.
1. On the 21st day of the 10th month of Genbun 2, year of the Snake (1737), he was appointed to serve concurrently as warehouse magistrate and arms magistrate, and awarded an increase of 10 hyō.
1. On the 4th day of the 12th month of Genbun 5, year of the Monkey (1740), he died of illness.

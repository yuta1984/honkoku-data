Based on his dedication over several years, he was henceforth appointed exclusively to acupuncture treatment.

- Same 10th year (1839), 11th month, 10th day: During his service as表在番 【Location: Edo】, while receiving various assistance with medical practice and personal attendance, he neglected his studies and became distracted by miscellaneous affairs, treated official matters carelessly, and although he did not forget the official duties assigned to him here, similar issues occurred. Moreover, during his service he focused solely on clerical work for Nakamura Chūsho, frequently went out and neglected official duties on numerous occasions, and carelessly treated matters heard during meal service, showing imprudent behavior. Particularly, disregarding his station, he engaged in idle boastful talk and inappropriate social interactions. Given these various serious matters, if strict investigation were conducted, his position would become untenable. Therefore, this time, through special compassion, formal punishment was not imposed. Humbly, after consultation with our side, since the matter cannot be dismissed, internal disciplinary action was taken. He should quickly repent his past wrongs and devote himself to loyal service. Furthermore, Ito Gento and Fujita Sukesuke were instructed to provide guidance and advice, which he must follow without any violation, understanding that diligent service is of utmost importance.

- Same year, 12th month, 1st day: Due to certain considerations, 2 person-rations were withdrawn from his stipend, he was appointed as stipendless physician of attendant rank under house confinement, and placed under the supervision of senior officials henceforth.

- Same month, 29th day: Released from house confinement.

- Same 11th year (1840), 3rd month, 18th day: Regarding the recent internal disciplinary matter discussed with our side, although not much time has passed, due to signs of reform, strict confinement is no longer necessary. However, from now on, he should consider prudence as paramount and strive even more diligently in medical practice.

- Same 12th year (1841), 9th month, 9th day: Due to his diligent service and compassionate treatment of retainers' medical needs, he was appointed as stipendless physician, granted an increase of 1 person-ration, bringing his total to 7 person-rations.

- Same 13th year (1842), 9th month, 18th day: Appointed to 【Location: Edo】 garrison duty.

- Same year, 10th month, 8th day: Appointed to regular office duty.

- Kaei 2 (1849), 10th month, 24th day: Granted an increase of 1 person-ration.

- Same 3rd year (1850), 2nd month, 24th day: Died of illness while on 【Location: Edo】 garrison duty. His third son was designated as heir through special favor.

Palace duty for Lord Ryōnoshin continues as before
- On January 15th of Tenpō 5, year of Saru (1837)
　He was appointed to handle the detailed arrangements for items that Lord Ryōnoshin would use, taking responsibility for various minor duties
- On October 8th of Kaei 6, year of Mizunoto-Ushi (1853), he was granted the rank of gochū-koshō and received an increase of rations for 1 person, bringing his total to 22 hyō and rations for 3 people. His duties remain the same as before

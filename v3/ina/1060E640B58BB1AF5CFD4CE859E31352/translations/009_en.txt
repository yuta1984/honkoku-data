Reign of Lord Yoriyasu
- In Kaei 1, Year of the Monkey (1848), on the 23rd day of the 7th month, as requested by his elder brother Tansaburō on his deathbed, he was appointed as adopted son and granted the inherited stipend of 40 hyō and allowance for 3 persons without change, and appointed to guard duty according to stipend rank

- On the 3rd day of the 10th month of the same year, he was appointed to personal attendant service to the Retired Lord
- In the 2nd year, Year of the Rooster (1849), on the 28th day of the 4th month, when he submitted a petition requesting the lifting of his actual elder brother Shigakura's disownment, it was granted as requested
- In the 3rd year, Year of the Metal Dog (1850), on the 3rd day of the 5th month, his request to be sent to serve his adoptive family's aunt, the wife of Naitō Bunji, was granted as requested
- In the 4th year, Year of the Boar (1851), on the 13th day of the 4th month, when the lower residence's long house was destroyed by fire on the 3rd, he went to the Retired Lord's palace and worked diligently to clear and organize the implements. His dedication was deemed commendable, and he was rewarded with 100 mon in gold, soup, and sake
- In the 5th year, Year of the Water Rat (1852), on the 1st day of the 12th month, he was appointed to manage the Retired Lord's small implements
- In Ansei 2, Year of the Wood Rabbit (1855), on the 1st day of the 3rd month, he was appointed to manage the Retired Lord's personal wardrobe and clothing, with other duties remaining as before
- On the 28th day of the 6th month of the same year, he was promoted to messenger guard and appointed as temporary meal attendant and close attendant inspector to the Retired Lord, with other duties remaining as before
- On the 15th day of the 8th month of the same year, due to illness and as requested, his duties as messenger guard, temporary meal attendant and close attendant inspector to the Retired Lord, personal wardrobe manager, and concurrent construction and small implements manager were smoothly terminated, and he was ordered to return to regular rotation

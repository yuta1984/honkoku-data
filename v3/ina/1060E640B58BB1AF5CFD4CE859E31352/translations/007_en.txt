Reign of Lord Yoriyasu
- In Bunsei 3, Year of the Dragon (1820), on the 28th day of the 7th month, due to illness and as requested, he was relieved of his personal attendant duties and appointed as a temporary middle page attendant, henceforth under the supervision of the chief retainer
- In the 4th year, Year of the Snake (1821), on the 15th day of the 1st month, he was appointed to temporary guard duty
- In the 6th year, Year of the Sheep (1823), on the 1st day of the 9th month, he was appointed to personal attendant duties
- On the 10th day of the 10th month of the same year, he was appointed to manage small implements
- In the 7th year, Year of the Monkey (1824), on the 1st day of the 8th month, he inherited his late father Seiemon's position with a stipend of 70 koku and 3-person rice allowance without change, with duties remaining as before
- In the 9th year, Year of the Dog (1826), on the 17th day of the 6th month, he was appointed as record-keeper
- In the 11th year, Year of the Rat (1828), on the 8th day of the 10th month, due to illness and as requested, he was relieved of personal attendant duties and ordered to return to regular rotation
- In the 12th year, Year of the Ox (1829), on the 20th day of the 3rd month, he was appointed to serve concurrently as Storehouse Magistrate and Arms Magistrate, handling the affairs of the lower residence
- In Tenpō 6, Year of the Sheep (1835), on the 20th day of the 7th month, he was appointed as Construction Magistrate while continuing his concurrent role as Arms Magistrate
- In the 7th year, Year of the Monkey (1836), on the 20th day of the 4th month, he was appointed as Messenger-ranked Intermediary while continuing his concurrent roles as Construction and Arms Magistrate
- In the 9th year, Year of the Dog (1838), on the 23rd day of the 10th month, he was appointed as Chief Inspector for Mountain Affairs, continuing his concurrent roles as Construction and Arms Magistrate, and was ordered to relocate to the official residence below the watchtower
- In the 10th year, Year of the Boar (1839), on the 20th day of the 10th month, due to his son Shigakura's illness making service difficult, his temporary employment was terminated and he was allowed to surrender his rice allowance and retire as requested
- In the 11th year, Year of the Rat (1840), on the 15th day of the 1st month, due to numerous duties, his concurrent role as Arms Magistrate was smoothly terminated
- In the 13th year, Year of the Tiger (1842), on the 15th day of the 1st month, he was appointed to Ashigaru Commander rank and Finance Magistrate, with other concurrent roles remaining as before
- On the 25th day of the 8th month of the same year, due to misconduct, his stipend was confiscated and he was sentenced to house arrest
- In the 14th year, Year of the Rabbit (1843), on the 5th day of the 12th month, his house arrest was lifted
　　　　End of service

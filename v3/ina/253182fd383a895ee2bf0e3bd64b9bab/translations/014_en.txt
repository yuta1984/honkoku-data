【Right Page】
Regarding this matter, the petition states that no repayments have been made at all, but it also indicates that small amounts have indeed been repaid. During my late father's lifetime, though they were small sums of money, he paid the three creditors twice, and due to extreme financial hardship both internally and externally, he repeatedly requested extensions from these three people. With their understanding, the debt was extended during his lifetime. After Rokubei's death, there were no particular demands for payment, but last year there were expenses related to filing a lawsuit in Edo. Despite my financial difficulties, relatives gathered together to raise funds and paid two ryō to them. Also, when these people returned to their home province last winter, they presented a calculation statement demanding proportional payment, so I was compelled to raise one ryō and two bu to pay toward this amount.

【Left Page】
As there were repeated demands for payment, I pleaded for them to accept gradual payments since, as you well know, I am in financial distress, but they would not listen. This truly unavoidably led to this lawsuit, for which I deeply apologize. I humbly request that you show mercy and, understanding that I am in financial difficulty as you know, order them to accept gradual payments.

I am still young, and as it is difficult to care for my mother, I went to serve in Matsumoto castle town, while my mother survives day to day with various assistance from relatives. Our circumstances are of extremely impoverished status, but as I stated above, if they would accept gradual payments, I would like to make gradual repayments. I humbly request that you mercifully order this arrangement.

Memorandum

From the first day of the eighth month of the Year of the Dog to the last day of the fourth month of the Year of the Boar
Item: 1 koku, 7 to, 7 sho of rice　　　Total days (large and small months combined)
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　177 days

The above is stipend rice granted to the horse handlers of this town, which we have certainly received using the rice tax from Kawashimo village for the Year of the Dog. This document serves as proof thereof.

Bunsei 9, Year of the Fire Dog, 11th month　　Wholesale agent (tonya)
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Riemon
To Shimizu Yazaemon-sama

The above two certificates were submitted on the 7th day through the intermediation of Iguchi Sadaroku at the government office. We have requested that they be forwarded to the high official once the endorsement is completed.

We have petitioned Magistrate Shimizu-sama that the 20 bags of official duty rice be returned to Hokamochi village, and that allocation be made for the stipend rice for horse handlers. He has acknowledged our request.

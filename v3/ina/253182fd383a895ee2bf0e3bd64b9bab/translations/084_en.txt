To the Magistrate's Office

The above three permit applications were submitted at the government office on the 22nd of the same month through the intermediary of Lord Hata Buemon, following the standard format for final signatures and seals. On the first day of intercalary sixth month, we received the official seal and were given the permits. I immediately called Monjuro and handed them over to him.

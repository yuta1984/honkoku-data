Respectfully submitting this oral petition

I am in poor health and have no male heir, so regarding Denji, the son of Kajū from Kitafukuchi Village in Nakazawa District, with whom there is also a romantic relationship, I have discussed internally about adopting him as a son-in-law to continue the family line, and I humbly request permission to take him in.

If you would graciously grant this request as petitioned with your benevolence, I would consider it a most grateful blessing. The above.

Higashiya

The above petition was submitted with an addendum and seal at the government office through the intermediation of Mr. Isawa Iwaemon. On the 5th day of the 3rd month, approval was granted through Mr. Isawa's intermediation, and town representative Heibei was summoned to receive the notification.

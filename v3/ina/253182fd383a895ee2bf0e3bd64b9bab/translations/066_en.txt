The above petition was submitted with the usual signatures and seals at the end, on the 5th of the same month at the government office through the good offices of Mr. Izawa Iwaemon. [Left page]
Humbly submitting this oral petition

One: Kanezō from Kashio Village in Shimo-Ina District, under the jurisdiction of [correction: Habura Geki-sama crossed out, replaced with] Chimura Heizaemon-sama, has been residing in our locality for several years. As he has family connections and is a trustworthy person, we would like him to become a parishioner of Mankōji Temple, be added to the religious registry of Seri-chō, and permanently reside in our locality. Therefore, we humbly petition for this.

Regarding the above matter, if you would graciously grant our petition as requested through your benevolence, it would be most gratefully received. The above.

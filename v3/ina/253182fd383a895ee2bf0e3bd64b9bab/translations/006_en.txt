[Left page]
I humbly submit this written petition

I have been working as a plasterer (sakan) until now, but from this year forward, I would like to serve in the Public Works Department (ofushin-kata) for three days each year and fulfill my professional duties. Therefore, I humbly submit this petition.

Regarding the above matter, if you would graciously grant my request as petitioned, it would be a most grateful fortune. The above stated.

Bunsei 9, Year of the Fire Dog, 6th month　　　Seri-machi plasterer
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Petitioner: Shinjiro

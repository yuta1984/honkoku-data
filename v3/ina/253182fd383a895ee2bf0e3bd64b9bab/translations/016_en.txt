【Right Page】
　　　Respectfully submitting this petition in writing
　As I have no male heir, I have this time entered into negotiations 
　to adopt Kanzo, the younger brother of the middleman merchant Yamagataya Tarobei,
　in order to prepare for succession of my business.
　Therefore, I respectfully submit this petition.
Regarding the above matter, if you would graciously grant this petition
as requested, it would be most grateful and fortunate. The above.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Shimomachi
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Kisoya Ihei
　　Bunsei 9, Year of the Dog, 8th month　　　Petitioner: Widow
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Agent: Ichizaemon

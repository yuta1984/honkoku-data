The above petition was submitted in the third month, and on the fourth day of the sixth month, we received official approval, so we called Shinmachi town representative Denemon to convey the decision.

Humbly submitting this written petition:
One: I have been engaged in the indigo dyeing trade for many years, but from this year I wish to cease the indigo dyeing business. Therefore, I humbly submit this petition.

Regarding the above matter, if you would graciously grant my petition as requested, I would consider it a most grateful blessing. The above.

Wataya (Cotton shop)
Bunsei 10, Year of the Boar　　　　Tozaemon

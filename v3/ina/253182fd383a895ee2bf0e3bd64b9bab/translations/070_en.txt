The above petition was sealed and submitted on the same fifth day at the government office through the mediation of Mr. Izawa Iwauemon. On the same seventh day, an official directive was issued stating that the matter should be settled internally, and this instruction was conveyed to town representative Ichizaemon.

　　Humbly submitting this oral statement as a petition
　Although I am an incompetent person, I have been honored to receive appointments as town representative multiple times and have gratefully served for a long period. However, as I have advanced in age, I believe it has become difficult for me to fulfill the duties of this position. Therefore, I humbly request to be relieved of this duty.
Regarding the above matter, if you would graciously order as I have petitioned out of your compassion, it would be a most grateful blessing. The above stated.

　　　Humbly submitting this oral statement as a petition
　I have been working as a plasterer until now, but from this year
　I would like to serve in the construction department for three days per year and devote myself
　entirely to this official duty. Therefore, I humbly make this request.
Regarding this matter, if you would graciously grant my request as petitioned
through your benevolence, I would consider it a most grateful blessing. The above.
　　Bunsei 9, year of the Fire Dog, 6th month (June 1826)　　　Shimizu-machi plasterer
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Yoemon

Regarding this matter, if you would graciously grant my request as petitioned

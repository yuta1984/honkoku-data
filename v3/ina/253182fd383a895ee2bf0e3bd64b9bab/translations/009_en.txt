【Left Page】
　　　Respectfully submitting this oral statement as a petition
　I have been working as a plasterer until now, but from this year
　I would like to serve in the Public Works Department for about three days per year and
　specialize in my professional duties.
Regarding the above matter, if you would graciously grant my petition
as requested, it would be a most grateful blessing. The above.

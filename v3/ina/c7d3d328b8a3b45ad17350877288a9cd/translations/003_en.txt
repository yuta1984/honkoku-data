During his term of office, he ordered an investigation, and since there were official notice boards similar to those on the Koshu Highway, he directly ordered that the post stations along the Koshu Highway should follow the precedent of Kanazawa-juku, and graciously provided us with supplementary official notice boards with increased rates. This was entirely due to the benevolence of His Lordship, and we were gratefully relieved. However, in the previous Year of the Dog, the eleven post stations of the Nakasendo Kiso Road made an agreement, and because the relay cargo from Shiojiri, Shimo-no-Suwa, Kami-no-Suwa, and Kanazawa-juku to the Ina region had increased significantly, the eleven stations petitioned the Highway Magistrate's Office about their difficulties. Their petition was accepted, and we were informed that not a single pack horse load of cargo would be relayed to the Ina Highway.

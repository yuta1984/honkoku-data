Memorandum
One: Two chugen (middle-ranking retainers)
The above are serving for the official work of house/wood inspection,
therefore I humbly wish that they be granted
an official directive
　　5th month, 29th day　　Ōshimo Shichizaemon

29th day
One: Today went to Jūzō-sama's residence 
　on official business
One: Regarding the net work, this year we have been going out
　particularly frequently, so there were greetings, and until
　yesterday the 28th we completed three days of repairs.
　Regarding this, I requested that from today until
　the 1st of next month, three days of rest be granted.
　When I made this request, Jūzō-sama
　said that since there were difficult precedents in previous years,
　he would consult with Kyūzaemon's side, and then
　send a written directive according to the records.
　If done this way, no further notification would be needed
　and the matter could be settled.
　Accordingly, following the records from the 11th day of the 11th month of the Tiger year,
　I sent a written directive to Kyūzaemon-sama and Kyūbei.
　As above.

29th day
One: Regarding rest days during net work, when I spoke to
　Rokūemon and Jōzaemon-sama about this: if three days of
　rest are granted in the main quarters, it is established practice
　that morning and evening preparation rice is collected.
　Therefore, I requested that rest days be granted
　through the bunichi-kata (tax collection office) as before

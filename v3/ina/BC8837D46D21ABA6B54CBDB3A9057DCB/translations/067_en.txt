29th day
One: Regarding the matter of guard duty in the great hall during off-duty months, Genzaemon was told by Kyūshima that both men should consult and submit a report. As I have no knowledge of this matter, I conveyed that duties assigned should be performed. However, if timber comes downstream this spring, regarding the matter of going on guard duty, and the matter of Kyūbei alone accompanying them - for this too I requested guidance and conveyed this to Kyūemon-sama and Genzaemon-sama.

One: Regarding the rain cloaks that the two net-casting men have received and kept - two cloaks, nine signboards, and two pairs of leggings - when I asked Jūzō-sama what should be done with them, he said they would investigate later and that each should keep them in custody for now.

Same day
One: Two new signboards - these should be delivered. I conveyed this in various ways, and Kyūbei immediately took the report to Masanosin-sama. Tomorrow at the storehouse, Kyūbei will convey instructions to the two net-casting men.

One: Regarding the matter of Chinese net tags that I reported earlier, Jūzō-sama said that since various matters are complicated, they have not yet been properly handed over.

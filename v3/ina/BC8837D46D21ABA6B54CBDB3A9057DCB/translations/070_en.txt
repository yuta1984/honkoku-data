【Right Page】
Regarding the account keeper's monthly great hall duty assignment,
when the late hours arrive, one person from your own shift
should be assigned as subordinate and conduct inspections.
Of course, regarding the downstream area during great hall guard duty,
if it exists, as before, both persons together
should conduct inspections. Having received understanding on this matter,
Jūzō-sama issued these instructions. The above.
　May 29th　Okutani Gen'emon
　　To Ihara Shichizaemon-sama

June 1st
One: Regarding the deputy inspection gold certificates from April
　29th, thirty-nine documents were brought,
　among which one document lacked a seal,
　so it was returned. Regarding this, the number of certificates
　is thirty-eight documents. This time they were placed
　in the official box drawer, and naturally a representative copy was prepared and
　placed. Official duty acceptance exists.
　　June 1st　　　　Shichizaemon　
　　　To Kyūzaemon-sama

　lacking seven beads, received by Shichizaemon
　　　June　　　　Kyūzaemon

One: The net casting by both persons was completed

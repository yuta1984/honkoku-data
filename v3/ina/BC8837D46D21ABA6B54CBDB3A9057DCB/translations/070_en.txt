Regarding guard duty at the reception hall during off-duty months,
when [someone] is at the lower river, one monthly duty officer
should keep a subordinate and conduct inspections.
Of course, if [someone] goes to the lower river during reception hall guard intervals,
two people should conduct inspections as before.
Jūzō-sama gave orders to understand this matter. The above.

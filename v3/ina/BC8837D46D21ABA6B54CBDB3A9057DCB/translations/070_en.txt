Regarding the duty at the reception hall during off-duty months,
when going to the lower river, one monthly duty officer
should keep subordinate officials and conduct inspections.
Of course, when going to the lower river during reception hall guard duty,
as before, two people should
conduct inspections together. Regarding this matter, to ensure understanding,

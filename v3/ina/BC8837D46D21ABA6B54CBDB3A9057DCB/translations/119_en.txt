I respectfully write to you. Though it is the season of
autumn coolness, I trust that you are all in good health
and carrying out your duties well, which I find most gratifying. Regarding
Matsudaya Rokuroemon of this town, who last year cut timber
from Nakazawa mountain - pine lumber measured in square sun units -
he brought a certificate of the number of logs to submit to the government office
to my place. I immediately affixed my seal and
handed it to Rokuroemon. Please handle this matter appropriately.
I humbly and respectfully submit this.
　　　September 18th　　　　　　Sato Zaemon (seal)
　　　　To Iida Hanemon-sama
　　　　Akabane Jinnosuke-sama  
　　　　Kaneko Shirohachi-sama
　　　　　　　All gentlemen

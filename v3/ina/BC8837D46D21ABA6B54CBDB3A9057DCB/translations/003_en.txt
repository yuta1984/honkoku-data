23rd day
　Regarding the matter of Lord Kurando, although his illness has continued
　into this spring, he has not yet recovered. Since he holds
　important official duties, today he submitted a petition
　to the duty officer, which was immediately
　　heard. Upon hearing, [it was decided that] after his illness
　improves, he should resume his duties, and with
　kind　consideration, the petition was returned
　to him. Please convey this matter accordingly, Lord Jūzō
　has instructed. The above.

23rd day
　Regarding Kurando-dono's illness, though spring has arrived,
　he still has not recovered. As he holds important duties,
　today a petition was submitted to the monthly duty officer, which was immediately
　　heard. It was graciously decided that after his illness
　improves, he should resume his duties accordingly.
　The petition was graciously returned with kind words. Jūzō-dono
　instructed that this matter be communicated to all concerned parties.
　　　January 23rd
　The above is a circular notice from Tamura Yozaemon-dono

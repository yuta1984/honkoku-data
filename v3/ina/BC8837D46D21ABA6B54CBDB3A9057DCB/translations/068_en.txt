29th day
One: Regarding the ashigaru (foot soldiers), it was decreed to the group leaders of each unit that group-by-group investigations should be conducted. This is indeed the case. We have heard reports of ashigaru engaging in unlicensed net fishing, but when we reported that this might also have broader repercussions, Jūzō-sama said that reports should continue to be submitted in any case. We also discussed this matter with Kyūemon-sama on the same day.

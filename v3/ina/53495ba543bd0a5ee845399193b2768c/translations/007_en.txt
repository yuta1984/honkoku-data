　　　Fourth month of the year of the Boar

On the night of the 28th day of the fourth month of the previous year of the Dog, a peasant named Asaemon from Shimo-Oshima Village in Seta District, Joshu Province, took his wife Koyo and young son and traveled from that province to Kyoto, bypassing various checkpoints by crossing over mountains. The personal description of the said Asaemon is as follows:
One: Age - 45 years old in the current year of the Boar

【Left Page】
　　Appears to be of appropriate age
One: A peasant from Shimo-Oshima Village in Seta District, Joshu Province, born in that village. Medium height and build, long face, dark complexion, gentle disposition. Small eyes with a straight nose bridge, large ears and mouth, thick eyebrows. Uneven teeth but speaks clearly. Beard and shaved pate are thin, but facial hair is thick.
One: Clothing at that time: brown vertical-striped cotton wadded garment, middle garment of vertical and horizontal brown-striped cotton, silk undergarment, indigo broad-sleeved kosode with well-frame crest, white-based light blue amber-colored obi.

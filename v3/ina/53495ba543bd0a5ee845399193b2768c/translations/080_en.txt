　　10th month　　Already announced in the 11th month

When there are thieves and criminals in rural areas, village officials have instructed eta and hinin to capture them. Therefore, even when capturing vagrants, they should promptly report to village officials and seek instructions, and should not take matters into their own hands. However, there have been cases where peasants have been bound and interrogated under the pretext of theft or wrongdoing, which is improper conduct. Recently, regarding this improper conduct, the hinin Mosuke and two others from the village guard in a domain in Noto Province, as well as village officials, have each been given punishments. Such behavior must never occur again.
Regarding the above: in imperial domains, magistrate offices, and in private domains, lords and headmen must thoroughly communicate this without exception.
The above should be announced

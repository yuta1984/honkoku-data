Reports should be submitted as ordered. Since town officials and ward representatives must not establish town rankings based on their private intentions, they must understand all matters according to the memorandum as instructed. All unanimously complied, and monthly duty officer Oka Ichirōbee submitted the acceptance. Furthermore, after submitting the above acceptance, the magistrate issued the following through the secretarial staff: Regarding this commercial-related document matter, it is improper that town officials and ward representatives inappropriately claimed town status distinctions, wasted time, and neglected their clerical duties. The matter of the seven town representatives merely consulting among themselves and submitting resignation petitions is also misconduct. Hereafter, the ten towns must not engage in private consultations and should conduct all affairs as they have up to now.

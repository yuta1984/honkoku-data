1. Regarding cotton, hemp cloth, and hemp grass, although notices have been issued repeatedly, it is heard that there is urgency causing shortening and narrowing of width. Moreover, there may be smuggling occurring. It is generally heard that goods sent collectively from rural areas to towns have become scarce in recent years. This is due to negligence by various commercial inspectors. Henceforth, make every effort, and if there are any violations of regulations, they should be reported.

1. Regarding the nearby districts closest to the castle town, there are no other places to seek convenience outside the town areas, and since products can be easily received in large quantities,

naturally prosperity may come to the town areas. In the towns, not only products but also goods collected from rural areas should be controlled and handled exclusively. If money flows into the domain from other places, it will become the foundation for the gradual prosperity of commerce in the towns. Therefore, considering the future, even those in rural areas engaged in questionable livelihoods should be accepted broadly, provided there is no interference with legitimate business. Of course, if there is any improper handling, it will certainly be investigated.

The above intentions should be communicated without fail to officials of all districts and villages, as well as to those in towns and rural areas who handle commerce.

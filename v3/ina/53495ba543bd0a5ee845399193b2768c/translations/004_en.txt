This time, due to the financial difficulties of Itabashi-juku and thirteen other post stations along the Nakasendō road, an announcement is made that the following increases in charges for horses and porters may be collected:

Nakasendō Road
From the first month of the past Mi year to the third month of the coming Tora year, for ten years, a 15% increase in charges for horses and porters was ordered. Furthermore, from the first month of the coming I year to the twelfth month of the coming U year, an additional 30% increase is added, making a total increase of 45%.

Post stations: Itabashi-juku, Itahana-juku, Matsuida-juku, Sakamoto-juku, Karuizawa-juku, Kutsukake-juku, Oiwake-juku, Shionaida-juku, Yawata-juku, Mochizuki-juku, Ashida-juku, Ontake-juku, Kanō-juku, Sekigahara-juku

[Left Page]
The above surcharges are hereby announced, so please take note and collect them accordingly. Third month of Inu year

The above matter should be announced thoroughly without omission. That is all.

As written above, since a directive has been issued from the government, it should be announced throughout the town.

Thirteenth day of the second month of I year

This time, a princess was born at the Western Palace, and it has been announced that this princess shall be called "Hitoshi-tomo Himegimi-sama" using the character "儔 (hitoshi)." In connection with this, regarding the characters "向儔 (mukō-hitoshi)," both men and women should make appropriate changes.

　　　　　　September

Regarding the servant Shūsuke who murdered the widow "Moto," the proprietress of an inn at a rented house belonging to Shōnosuke in Honmachi, Shirakawa, Ōshū Province, on the night of September 9th of the previous year of the Boar, and fled after stealing money - his personal description:
- Age: appears older than thirty years - Birthplace: said to be from Ushū Province - Medium height and thin build, rather high nose, long face with reddish-black complexion and smallpox scars - Has a horizontal smallpox scar about five bu (approximately 1.5cm) below the nose - Both hair and shaved forehead (sakayaki) are rather thick, with some smallpox scars within the shaved area

[Left Page]
- Eyes are rather dark and protruding - One front upper tooth is crooked, but other teeth are well-aligned; speech is somewhat rapid with a slight stutter - Eyebrows are thin and dark - Ears are rather small
- Clothing at the time: wore a blue-plum navy checkered striped single garment and light yellow cotton undergarment, with a flower-colored silk obi sash

If anyone matching this description is found, detain them at that location. In government territories, report to the deputy governor (daikan); in private domains, report to the domain lord or local governor (jitō), and then report to the office of Mizuno Sakon-no-shōgen in Edo. Even if someone has only heard about this person, they must report it. Household retainers and servants must be thoroughly investigated. Those found to have concealed information shall be punished.

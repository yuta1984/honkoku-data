　　4th month of the Rat year
The head priest Miyazaki Sa of Suwa Myōjin shrine in Negabu village, Tama district, Musashi Province, has lost the official document (kuchitsū) for his temple lands bearing the vermillion seal. If anyone has seen or heard of someone who stole it, has knowledge of it, or found it along the road, they should report to the local magistrate in government lands, or to the territorial lord or magistrate in private domains, who will then report to Kawabe Bitchū-no-kami in Edo. Even if the person is a relative, their crime should be properly investigated and dealt with. If anyone conceals this information and it becomes known later through other sources, it will be considered a serious offense.

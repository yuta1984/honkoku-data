Kōno Uemon

During the reign of Lord Genryūin
- On the 20th day of the 8th month of Hōreki 12 (1762), year of the Monkey, he was employed under the supervision of the ōmetsuke of the lower residence. Until then, he had been serving under Kuzukami Gengorōemon.

- On the 24th day of the 11th month of the same year, he was appointed as shimetsuke of the upper residence and was granted an additional stipend of 1 koku 4 tō, making his total stipend 5 koku 6 tō plus provisions for two people.

- On the 7th day of the 12th month of Meiwa 6 (1769), year of the Earth Ox, his request to adopt Kōjirō, the younger brother of Mikami Shinsuke from Kawachi Kahē's group in the home domain, was approved.

- On the 20th day of the 2nd month of An'ei 3 (1774), year of the Wood Horse, he divorced his adopted son Banzō.

During the reign of Lord Zuiun'in
- On the 22nd day of the 9th month of the 5th year (An'ei 5, 1776), year of the Fire Monkey, he was appointed to serve in the kitchen of the lord's wife, also serving as head cook under the supervision of Arai Kiroku. Stipend: 5 koku 6 tō.

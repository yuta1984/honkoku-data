Kitahara Tajirōemon
Later renamed Nakabayashi

During the reign of Lord Honryūin
- Kansei 2 (1790), 3rd month, 23rd day: Appointed as scribe for the head supervisor department
During the reign of Lord Raiin
- Same 4th year (1792), 6th month, 1st day: Appointed as secretary to the chamberlain
- Same 6th year (1794), 2nd month, 15th day: Promoted to ranked ashigaru foot soldier, granted stipend of 18 hyō and provisions for 2 people, duties to continue as before
- Same 11th year (1799), 10th month, 18th day: Appointed to handle bat-通 gold transactions, scribe duties to continue as before
- Same 12th year (1800), 3rd month, 15th day: Appointed to concurrently serve in clothing department, scribe duties to continue as before
- Same year, 4th month, 1st day: Appointed to concurrently serve as head treasurer, other duties to continue as before
- Kyōwa 1 (1801), 11th month, 28th day: Granted ranked escort guard status, appointed as ashigaru squad leader and personal attendant leader, granted increase of 2 hyō
- Same 2nd year (1802), 3rd month, 15th day: Appointed as trainee for inspection department, catering department, and cooking department

Owada Jūzaemon
During the reign of Shinnyo-in-sama
1. On the first day of the fourth month of Kyōhō 10, year of the Wood Snake (1725), he was appointed to the position of daikan gokybōban-kaku and received an increase of 2 ryō 2 bu, making his total stipend 7 ryō and 3 person portions, with an additional 2 person portions as role allowance.

1. On the 26th day of the sixth month of the same 11th year, year of the Fire Horse (1726), he was appointed to the position of kakushiki gochūkoshō.

During the reign of Genryū-in-sama
1. On the 28th day of the twelfth month of Genbun 1, year of the Fire Dragon (1736), he received an additional allowance of 1 ryō, making his total 8 ryō.

1. On the 18th day of the eleventh month of the same 5th year, year of the Metal Monkey (1740), as requested, he was relieved of his daikan role and placed under goyōnin supervision.

1. On the 28th day of the fifth month of Kanpō 3, year of the Water Boar (1743), as requested, he retired and his son Riemon was appointed as head of the household.

During the reign of Lord Yoriyasu
1. 12th month, 25th day of the 8th year, year of the Rooster (1821) Due to reorganization, smoothly relieved of head of servants position,
   other duties continue as before
1. 1st month, 15th day of the 10th year, year of the Boar (1823)
   Appointed to Lord Shōun'in's catering and cooking departments, mountain affairs and cooking
   duties continue as before  
1. Intercalary 6th month, 13th day of the same year Due to numerous duties, smoothly
   relieved of concurrent cooking duties, monthly rotation for mountain affairs also excused,
   however, having absorbed the knowledge over several years, shall consult without reserve
   regarding guidance and instructions
1. 12th month, 23rd day of the same year Granted formal court dress with family crest and money
1. 5th month, 5th day of the 11th year, year of the Rat (1824) Granted rank of middle page, 1 person's provisions
   increased, duties continue as before
1. 10th month, 23rd day of Tenpō 3, year of the Dragon (1832)
   Successfully relieved of Lord Shōun'in's palace catering and cooking duties,
   2 hyō increase granted, mountain affairs continue as before, cooking department concurrent
   appointment ordered
1. 4th month, 28th day of Tenpō 7, year of the Monkey (1836) Lady's catering and key keeper duties,
   young lords' palace service concurrent appointment, upper residence kitchen duties
1. 5th month, 21st day of Tenpō 8, year of the Rooster (1837) Due to improper handling of official duties,
   all official positions revoked, 4 hyō and 1 person's provisions from stipend
   confiscated, appointed to ranked attendant guard, sequential seating, and personal service,
   ordered to remain under house arrest
1. 6th month, 28th day of the same year Died of illness

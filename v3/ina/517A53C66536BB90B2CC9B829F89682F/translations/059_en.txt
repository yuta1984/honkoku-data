Hirashima Sahei

- First year of Meiwa, Year of the Wood Monkey, intercalary 12th month, 5th day: Promoted to ashigaru (foot soldier) rank, granted 18 hyō and stipend for two retainers, assigned to御次番 (guard duty rotation)
- 7th year of the same era, Year of the Metal Tiger, 9th month, 29th day: Retired as requested

Hirashima Heiroku

- Same day: Following his father's petition for retirement, inherited the family headship without issue, appointed to 御供方 (lord's escort) ashigaru position
  Note: Before inheriting, he served in the Regular Edo Group

During the reign of Lord Honryūin
- 8th year of Tenmei, Year of the Earth Monkey, 7th month, 4th day: Promoted to ashigaru squad leader and御手廻頭 (personal guard leader), formal escort duty, granted additional 4 hyō stipend, under supervision of the magistrate

During the reign of Lord Yorimoto-kun
- 5th year of Kansei, Year of the Ox, 2nd month, 1st day: Due to his service record, placed under the supervision of the ashigaru commander

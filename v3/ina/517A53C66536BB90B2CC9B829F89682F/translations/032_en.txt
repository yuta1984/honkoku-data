- January 15th, 10th year of Kansei (1798): Promoted to the rank of formal ashigaru (foot soldier) in Osaka
  Granted a stipend of 16 hyō and support for two persons. Appointed as monk inspector.
- May 20th, 2nd year of Kyōwa (1802): Promoted to formal attendant guard
  Appointed to serve Lord Kamegoro's quarters, granted an increase of 2 hyō
- February 3rd, 4th year of Kyōwa (1804): Appointed to attendant guard in order of precedence, established as branch family
  Granted permission to establish separate household, ordered to relocate to Edo, granted increase of 4 hyō
  Duties to continue as before
- July 13th, 6th year of Bunka (1809): Concurrently appointed to investigation department and catering department
  Assigned to attend ladies of the rest quarters
- March 23rd, 10th year of Bunka (1813): Appointed to concurrently serve in cooking department and
  catering and cooking duties for Young Lord and Lord Tokunosuke
- May 8th, 1st year of Bunsei (1818): Appointed as additional clerk for the accounting office
- Intercalary April 3rd, 2nd year of Bunsei (1819): Son Tetsutaro
  ordered to be adopted into the Ito Kintayu family

During the reign of Lord Yoriyasu
- February 20th, 5th year of Bunsei (1822): Smoothly relieved from investigation department, catering department, cooking department, baggage
  department, and accounting office duties. Appointed to attendant service and waiting duties
  with rank maintained
- July 5th of the same year: Appointed head of attendants for accounting office duties
  with rank maintained
- June 28th, 7th year of Bunsei (1824): Appointed concurrently to Lady's catering department, gate guard, and cooking
  department duties
- March 29th, 8th year of Bunsei (1825): Relieved from Lady's catering department, gate guard, and cooking
  department duties. Appointed to Princess Akira's quarters catering department concurrently
  Also to oversee the management of ladies serving in Lord's new living quarters
- April 1st, 10th year of Bunsei (1827): Appointed concurrently to Lady's catering department, gate guard, and cooking
  department duties. Princess Akira's catering to continue as before
  as should be understood. Therefore granted an increase of 2 hyō
- June 23rd, 11th year of Bunsei (1828): Appointed as middle page, granted 2 hyō and support for one person
- April 25th, 6th year of Tenpō (1835): Due to current economy measures, the rest quarters
  gates temporarily closed. All business to be conducted through the inner palace gates
  therefore ordered to serve combined duties at rest quarters and inner catering
- October 10th, 8th year of Tenpō (1837): Promoted to middle page inspector rank
  Appointed as Lady's personal attendant, granted 7 hyō as official salary

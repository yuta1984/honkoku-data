【Right Page】

One: When those receiving stone-cutting rice stipends are granted increases within the first or second month, the increased rice portion shall be distributed at that time, the original amount shall be distributed throughout that year, and from the following year the increased rice shall be added to the base amount and distributed in three-season divisions.
　However, this was established when Akabane Shingorōemon and Kaneko Yasubei, lower officials of the construction department, were granted increased rice stipends in the first month of the Ox year.

One: When changing from money stipends to stone-cutting rice stipends, if it occurs in the first or second month, the rice amount shall be distributed in monthly portions without deductions, the money stipend shall be submitted in monthly portions, and the rice stipend from the third month to the second month of the following year shall also be distributed without deductions in the first year.

【Left Page】

When stone-cutting rice stipends are granted in the twelfth month, three months' worth from the twelfth month through the first and second months of the following year shall be distributed in monthly portions without deductions. The money stipend shall naturally be submitted in monthly portions, and rice stipends from the third month onward shall be distributed with five-shu deductions.
　However, this was established when Hara Jinsuke, the storehouse key keeper, was granted stone-cutting rice stipends in the first month of the Ox year.

One: When advancing from foot soldier squad leader to external service, if it occurs before the time when role money is distributed, no deductions are necessary and the full amount shall be distributed.

　However, when Yasuda Shinsuke and Endo Zenji were granted rice bag quotas on the fifteenth day of the second month of the Rat year, since quarterly tissue paper allowances had not been distributed, no deductions were necessary and they received full annual rice bag quotas. When Kitahara Koga was ordered to receive stone-cutting rice on the twenty-ninth day of the third month, since this was after the spring tissue paper allowance had been distributed, that portion was submitted as payment, and he received the full annual stone-cutting rice stipend. When Shinsuke and Zenji were granted rice bag quotas, this did not correspond to their initial summoning but was established as a new appointment, so deductions were waived for the first year.

When moving, after completing duties at the stationed post where one was appointed and returning, the additional stipend shall be paid at the current location, and upon proceeding to the relocation destination, payment shall be made at the new location from the day of arrival.

One: For those with regular attendant service stipends who remain at either location due to official duties or illness, regarding the payment of service stipends, no matter how many months it extends, until the previous month payment shall be made at the location where they are staying

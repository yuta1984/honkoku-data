One-third of the rice deduction amount shall be waived. If garrison duty is exempted between the first day of April and before the lord's departure, half of the annual rice deduction amount shall be waived.
　However, for those who have been ordered to serve regular attendant garrison duty since January of that year, if they are exempted from garrison duty before the lord's departure, the proportion of deduction waiver shall be the same as above.

【Left Page】

One: When a regular attendant retires or dies and family headship succession is granted to his son who continues in service, the waiver of rice deductions shall follow the same precedent according to the month when the succession petition was submitted.

One: Similarly, for those whose garrison duty ceases due to unsatisfactory performance, the presence or absence of deduction waivers and their proportions shall be determined through examination depending on the circumstances of each case.

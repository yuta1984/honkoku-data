If occurring after the spring period, it shall be distributed at the standard summer-end rate, and any shortfall shall be paid immediately as a lump sum. If adding the salary increase to the summer-end rate still results in a shortfall, only the year-end portion shall be paid at the standard rate, while the summer period portion shall be paid only for the shortfall amount. When the directive comes after the summer period, the year-end portion shall be paid at the standard rate, and any excess shall be paid at the time of promotion.

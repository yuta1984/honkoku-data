【Right Page】

1. In case a land certificate is lost due to flood, fire, or theft, two or more witnesses must be established, and an application for reissuance should be submitted with the joint seal of village officials.
1. After the initial issuance of land certificates, when ownership changes due to sale, transfer, succession, or other reasons such as foreclosure of mortgaged land, the details should be recorded on the back of the land certificate according to the template format, and an application for land certificate reissuance should be submitted.
1. For mountains, forests, wastelands, and other types of land, when land certificates are issued for sales and transfers, all should be handled in the same manner.

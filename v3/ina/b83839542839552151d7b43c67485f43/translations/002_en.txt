Deed for the Perpetual Transfer of Sacred Land

Regarding our owned sacred land measuring two ken and three shaku from north to south, bounded on the east by your living fence and on the west extending to the ditch at the bottom of the marsh, we hereby confirm without any discrepancy that we are transferring this land to you in perpetuity. As payment for this, we have just now certainly received five ryō of gold, which is entirely accurate. Given this arrangement, neither our relatives nor any other parties shall raise any objections regarding this property. Should anyone raise difficult matters, the guarantor signatories will personally appear wherever necessary to resolve the issue promptly, ensuring no trouble whatsoever for you. This perpetual transfer deed is executed for future reference as follows:

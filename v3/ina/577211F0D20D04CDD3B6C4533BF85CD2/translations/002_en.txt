Memorandum
1. Regarding the castle grounds, it goes without saying, but even extending to the residences of retainers, constant attention must be paid to fire prevention.
1. During times of strong wind, one must not go to other places without necessary business. If one has unavoidable business, consult with one's supervising authority, and for all others, report to the metsuke officials before departing. If strong winds arise or fires break out while one is elsewhere, return immediately.
1. When going elsewhere for personal business, consult with one's supervising authority, and for all others, report to the metsuke officials.

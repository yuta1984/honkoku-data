5. Bird hanging scrolls - Two scrolls
These hanging scrolls are on paper and painted by Kanō Yūsai Morinobu. Originally they were treasured by Itō Shirōe, a parishioner of our temple, but in January of Meiji 10 (1877), he donated them to our temple. While the exact date of their creation is not known in detail, one can tell at a glance that they are masterful works by an ancient renowned artist.

On the 21st day of the 10th month of the 1st year of Shōtoku, Year of the Metal Rabbit (1711), he was promoted from middle page to personal attendant.
On the 1st day of the 6th month of the 4th year of Kyōhō, Year of the Earth Boar (1719), he was appointed as a stipended retainer.
On the 15th day of the 1st month of the 9th year of Kyōhō, Year of the Wood Dragon (1724), he was appointed to temporary dining service duty.
On the 20th day of the 9th month of the 10th year, Year of the Wood Snake (1725), he was appointed to full dining service duty.
On the 7th day of the 6th month of the 17th year, Year of the Water Rat (1732), he was appointed to oversee the monks.
On the 1st day of the 11th month of the 19th year, Year of the Wood Tiger (1734), he was appointed to the rank of stable master, exempted from small treasury duties, and entrusted with sword bearers.
In public matters, he should be regarded as next in rank to the long-handled weapon magistrate.
However, the year and month when he was appointed to the small treasury office is unknown.
On the 13th day of the 4th month of the 20th year, Year of the Wood Rabbit (1735), by the will of the previous lord, he was appointed to the position of personal administrative officer, ranking next to the public administrative officer rank.

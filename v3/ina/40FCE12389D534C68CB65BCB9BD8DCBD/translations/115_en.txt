　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Fifth generation
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Komatsu Shimpei
1. On the 18th day of the 10th month of Bunka 8, Kanoto-Hitsuji (1811), he was appointed go-soku go-yatoi service.
1. On the 25th day of the 8th month of Bunka 13, Hinoe-Ne (1816), he was appointed temporary go-konando for the young lord's probationary service.
1. On the 3rd day of the 6th month of Bunsei 1, Tsuchinoe-Tora (1818), he was appointed regular go-konando for the young lord's probationary service.
1. On the 23rd day of the 11th month of Bunsei 2, Tsuchinoto-U (1819), he was appointed go-shiban-yaku with concurrent moto-shimeyaku.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Sixth generation
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Takeda Magokurō
1. On the 3rd day of the 9th month of Bunka 10, Mizunoto-Tori (1813), he was appointed go-soku service from go-kura-bugyō.
1. On the 20th day of the 10th month of Bunka 11, Kinoe-Inu (1814), he was granted go-shiban rank and appointed concurrent go-soku service to the young lord and go-konando go-meshimono-kata probationary service, transferred from service to Lord Kinnosuke.
1. On the 25th day of the 8th month of the same year, he died of illness.

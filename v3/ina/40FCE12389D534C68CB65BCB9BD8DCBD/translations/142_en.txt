Fifth Generation
Shioda Yasōemon
《Marginal note: later called Heihachi》

1. On the 28th day of the 9th month of Tenpō 10, Tsuchinoto-I (1839), he was appointed to go-soku service from go-bangata.
1. On the 25th day of the 1st month of the same 14th year, Mizunoto-U (1843), he was appointed to go-shiban.
1. On the 13th day of the 6th month of Kōka 2, Kinoto-Mi (1845), he was appointed to go-kinshū metsuke concurrent duties.
1. On the 13th day of the 4th month of Kaei 1, Tsuchinoe-Saru (1848), due to illness, as requested, he was exempted from ōmetsuke-kaku go-zenban go-kinshū metsuke go-konando and ordered to return to guard duty.

Sixth Generation
Namiki Jun'nosuke
《Marginal note: later called Daijirō》

1. On the 15th day of the 1st month of Tenpō 11, Kanoe-Ne (1840), he was appointed to go-dōsho-sama go-soku service from go-inkyo-sama go-soku service apprentice.
1. On the 25th day of the 6th month of the same 15th year, Kinoe-Tatsu (1844), he was appointed to go-inkyo-sama go-konando go-meshimono-kata concurrent duties.
1. On the 25th day of the 3rd month of Kōka 2, Kinoto-Mi (1845), he was appointed to ōmetsuke-kaku go-inkyo-sama go-zenban.

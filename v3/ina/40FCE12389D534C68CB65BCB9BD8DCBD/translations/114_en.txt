　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Fifth generation
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Kōno Shinsaburō
1. On the 20th day of the 3rd month of Bunka 7, Kanoe-Uma (1810), from go-bangata, due to the exceptional service his grandfather Uzaemon had performed in important official duties, he was appointed to service to the young lord.
1. On the 3rd day of the 9th month of Bunka 8, Kanoto-Hitsuji (1811), due to illness, as requested, he was dismissed from service to the young lord and appointed to return to regular duty.
1. On the 15th day of the 1st month of Bunka 13, Hinoe-Ne (1816), he was appointed go-soku service from go-bangata.
1. On the 25th day of the 6th month of the same year, he was appointed to service to the young lord. Especially since he had become accustomed to this duty, by special consideration he was advanced as an attendant.
1. On the 8th day of the 6th month of Bunsei 3, Kanoe-Tatsu (1820), he was granted go-shiban rank and appointed go-konando go-meshimono-kata.
1. On the 5th day of the 9th month of Bunsei 5, Mizunoe-Uma (1822), he was appointed temporary go-kinjū metsuke. 《Location: Osaka》 At the zaiban posting
1. On the 1st day of the 9th month of Bunsei 6, Mizunoto-Hitsuji (1823), he was granted ōmetsuke rank and appointed regular concurrent go-zenban go-kinjū metsuke, with concurrent go-konando go-meshimono-kata continuing as before.
1. On the 5th day of the 6th month of Bunsei 11, Tsuchinoe-Ne (1828), he was appointed ōmetsuke-yaku.

- Genbun 3, year of Tsuchinoe-Uma (1738), 9th month, 18th day: Appointed to 御側勤 (Go-soba-tsutome, personal attendant to the lord) from 御徒士頭 (Go-okachi-gashira, foot soldier commander). Continued to serve concurrently as 御徒士頭 as before. Ranked as second in 御近習目付 (Go-kinshū-metsuke, close attendant inspector) and senior in 御小納戸 (Go-konando, personal quarters attendant).

- Enkyō 3, year of Hinoe-Tora (1746), 1st month, 15th day: Promoted to 大目付格 (Ōmetsuke-kaku, inspector rank). Continued as 御徒士頭 as before, but relieved of 御側勤 duties.

　　　　　　　Successor to family name, third generation, actually fourth generation
　　　　　　　　Kanbe Yasohirō
　　　　　　　　　Later Sōsuke
- Genbun 4, year of Tsuchinoto-Hitsuji (1739), 1st month, 15th day: Appointed to 御側勤 from 御供方御中小性 (Go-tomogata Go-chū-koshō, middle-ranked page in the entourage).

- Enkyō 3, year of Hinoe-Tora (1746), 4th month, 24th day: Appointed as 御小納戸仮役 (Go-konando kariyaku, temporary personal quarters attendant).

- Same year, 5th month, 9th day: Elevated to 給人 (kyūnin, stipend recipient) status and appointed as 御小納戸本役 (Go-konando honyaku, regular personal quarters attendant).

- Kan'en 4, year of Kanoto-Hitsuji (1751), 5th month, 6th day: Appointed as 御近習目付. Other duties remained as before.

- Hōreki 4, year of Kinoe-Inu (1754), 11th month, 28th day: As he became a specialized official within the 御小納戸, relieved of 御召物方 (Go-meshimono-kata, clothing management) duties. Appointed to handle 御腰物 (go-koshimono, swords), 御器 (go-utsuwa, vessels), 御小道具 (go-kodōgu, small implements), and 御能装束 (go-nō-shōzoku, Noh performance costumes). When there were issues with 御膳番, appointed as 御試役 (go-shiyaku, probationary officer).

- Hōreki 7, year of Hinoto-Ushi (1757), 8th month, 7th day: Appointed as 大目付格御膳番 (Ōmetsuke-kaku Go-zenban, inspector-ranked meal attendant). 御腰物御武器頭 (Go-koshimono Go-buki-gashira, sword and weapons chief) remained as before.

- Hōreki 12, year of Mizunoe-Uma (1762), 2nd month, 25th day: Relieved of 御側勤 and appointed as 御取次 (Go-toritsugi, intermediary/liaison officer).

　　　　　　　Second generation
　　　　　　　　Kawachi Kanzō
　　　　　　　　　Later Kahē
- Kanpō 2, year of Mizunoe-Inu (1742), 6th month, 4th day: Appointed to 御側勤 from 御番方 (Go-bangata, guard duty).

- Kan'en 3, year of Kanoe-Uma (1750), 1st month, 15th day: Appointed as 御使番役 (Go-tsukaiban-yaku, messenger official).

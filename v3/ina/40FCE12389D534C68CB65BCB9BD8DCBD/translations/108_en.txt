　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Fifth generation
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Okabe Jūrozaemon
1. On the 15th day of the 1st month of Kansei 13, year of the Metal Rooster (1801), he was appointed go-soku assistant service from go-bangata.
1. On the 28th day of the 12th month of the same year, as requested, he was dismissed from go-soku go-soeban service and ordered to return to regular guard duty.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Second generation
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Miura Ichiichizaemon
1. On the 15th day of the 6th month of Kyōwa 1, year of the Metal Rooster (1801), from go-chū-koshō metsuke rank, he was promoted from service at the young lord's residence to kyūnin status and appointed to serve the young lord.

1. On the 13th day of the 3rd month of Bunka 4, year of the Fire Rabbit (1807), he was successfully dismissed from service to the young lord and appointed to a high rank in go-bangata.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Second generation
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Naitō Magonoshin
1. On the 22nd day of the 9th month of Kyōwa 3, year of the Water Pig (1803), he was appointed go-soku service from go-bangata.
1. On the 8th day of the 4th month of Bunka 9, year of the Water Monkey (1812), he was appointed go-konando go-meshimono-kata.
1. On the 2nd day of the 10th month of the 11th year, year of the Wood Dog (1814), he died of illness.

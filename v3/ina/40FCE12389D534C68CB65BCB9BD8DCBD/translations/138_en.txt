Fourth Generation
Yoshida Gakutarō

1. On the 15th day of the 1st month of Tenpō 6, Kinoto-Hitsuji (1835), he was appointed to go-soku service from go-bangata go-yatoizutome.
1. On the 15th day of the 8th month of the same year, he was granted his late father's hereditary position without change, and was appointed as go-fushin-bugyō and Bokuden-ryū koshimawari kenjutsu instructor for the New Year period.

Son of Third Generation Hachizaemon
Katō Rokusaburō
　　　　　　　Later: Taizō

1. On the 20th day of the 4th month of Tenpō 7, Hinoe-Saru (1836), he was appointed to go-soku service from go-tomogata go-chūkoshō go-yatoizutome.
1. On the 25th day of the 1st month of the same 14th year, Mizunoto-U (1843), he was granted go-tsukaiban rank and appointed go-kinshū-metsuke.
1. On the 1st day of the 3rd month of Kōka 2, Kinoto-Mi (1845), due to personnel arrangements, he was smoothly exempted from go-soku service and go-kinshū-metsuke duties and appointed go-toritsugi. However, he continued to concurrently serve as go-soeban as before.

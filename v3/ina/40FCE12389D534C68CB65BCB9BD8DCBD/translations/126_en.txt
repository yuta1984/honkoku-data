　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Fifth generation
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Okutsu Gengo
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　{{Marginal note: Later Sanhachi}}
1. On the 15th day of the 6th month of Bunsei 3, Kanoe-Tatsu (1820), he was appointed go-soku service from go-bangata.
1. On the 16th day of the 9th month of Bunsei 5, Mizunoe-Uma (1822), regarding the matter of Monzaemon of the same family name, by lord's consideration he was appointed go-yatoi retirement, and for family succession he was appointed go-bangata-daka rank.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Third generation
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Kitahara Yasohachi
1. On the 20th day of the 9th month of Bunsei 3, Kanoe-Tatsu (1820), from go-bangata assistant and go-tomogata service, he was appointed Ryōnoshin-sama go-soku go-yatoi service.
1. On the 15th day of the 6th month of Bunsei 8, Kinoto-Tori (1825), due to Ryōnoshin-sama's change in town residence arrangements, he was relieved of go-soku service and appointed go-bangata rank.

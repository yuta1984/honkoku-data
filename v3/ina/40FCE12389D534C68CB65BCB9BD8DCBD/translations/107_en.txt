Fourth generation
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Matsubara Gentabē
1. On the 28th day of the 8th month of Kansei 10, year of the Earth Horse (1798), he was appointed to go-soku go-yatoikin service.
1. On the 4th day of the 10th month of the 12th year, year of the Metal Monkey (1800), as requested, he was dismissed from go-soku service and appointed to go-kyūji-kata. His rank was to be understood as the lowest seat of go-chū-koshō metsuke-kaku.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Fifth generation
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Ichie Chōzō
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　《marginal note: later named Genpachi》
1. On the 1st day of the 10th month of Kansei 10, year of the Earth Horse (1798), he was appointed to go-soku go-yatoikin service.
1. On the 15th day of the 6th month of Bunka 5, year of the Earth Dragon (1808), he was appointed to concurrent positions of go-kinjū metsuke and go-konando go-meshimono-kata.
1. On the 1st day of the 3rd month of the 6th year, year of the Earth Snake (1809), he was appointed go-toritsugi and go-rusu-i soe-yaku with the rank of ō-metsuke-kaku.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Fifth generation by succession, actually sixth generation
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Kanbe Yasojūrō
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　《marginal note: later named Goroemon》
1. On the 23rd day of the 1st month of Kansei 12, year of the Metal Monkey (1800), he was appointed from go-bangata to go-soku service.
1. On the 23rd day of the 5th month of Bunka 1, year of the Wood Rat (1804), he was appointed go-kachi-gashira and was successfully dismissed from go-soku service.

Kaburagi Seihachirō

Kuzukami Gengo

Kaminami Sōshirō
1. On the 1st day of the 8th month of Kōka 2, Kinoto-Mi (1845), he was appointed from go-tomo-gata go-chū-koshō go-yatoi service to waka-tono-sama go-soku service.
1. On the 1st day of the intercalary 5th month of the same 3rd year, Hinoe-Uma (1846), due to personnel adjustments, he was appointed to go-omote go-soku service.

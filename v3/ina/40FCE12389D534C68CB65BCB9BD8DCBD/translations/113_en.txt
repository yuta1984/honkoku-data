Fourth generation
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Yoda Matasaburō
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　《Marginal note: Later Saburōbei》
1. On the 9th day of the 9th month of Bunka 6, year of the Earth Snake (1809), he was appointed to go-soku service from go-bangata.
1. On the 13th day of the 8th month of Bunka 12, year of the Wood Pig (1815), he was appointed go-kinjū metsuke.
1. On the 23rd day of the intercalary 8th month of Bunka 13, year of the Fire Rat (1816), he was appointed temporary assistant service concurrent with go-shiyaku go-konando go-meshimono-kata to Yoshinoshin-sama's side.
1. On the 23rd day of the 10th month of the same year, he was dismissed without incident from temporary concurrent service as go-shiyaku go-konando go-meshimono-kata to Yoshinoshin-sama.
1. On the 5th day of the 9th month of Bunka 14, year of the Fire Ox (1817), he was appointed temporary go-zenban.
1. On the 18th day of the 4th month of the same year, he was appointed to the regular position of go-zenban with ōmetsuke rank, continuing to serve concurrently as go-kinjū metsuke as before.
1. On the 15th day of the 3rd month of Bunka 15, year of the Earth Tiger (1818), he was appointed concurrent go-konando go-meshimono-kata.
1. On the 23rd day of the 3rd month of Bunsei 3, year of the Metal Dragon (1820), he died of illness.

　　　　　　　　　　　　Second return to service, second generation, actually fourth generation
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Kawamo Kohachirō
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　《Marginal note: Later Kobei》
1. On the 9th day of the 9th month of Bunka 6, year of the Earth Snake (1809), he was appointed to go-soku service from go-bangata.

1. On the 3rd day of the 7th month of Bunka 13, year of the Fire Rat (1816), he was granted ōmetsuke rank and appointed oshiai-yaku.
1. On the 25th day of the 9th month of Bunsei 2, year of the Earth Rabbit (1819), he was appointed from oshiai to service to the young lord concurrent with go-konando go-meshimono-kata.

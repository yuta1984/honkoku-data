Yokota Gosaburo
1. On the 1st day of the 8th month of Kōka 2, Kinoto-Mi (1845), he was appointed to waka-tono-sama go-soku go-yatoi service.

4th generation
Yamashita Inosuke
1. On the 1st day of the intercalary 5th month of Kōka 3, Hinoe-Uma (1846), he was appointed from go-tomo-gata go-chū-koshō to waka-tono-sama go-soku.
1. On the 8th day of the 4th month of Ka'ei 4, Tsuchinoto-I (1851), due to illness and according to his request, he was relieved from waka-tono-sama go-soku service and appointed to go-tomo-gata go-chū-koshō.

Sixth Generation
Ichie Genpachi
《Marginal note: later called Chōzō》

1. On the 28th day of the 2nd month of Tenpō 11, Kanoe-Ne (1840), from go-bangata, due to his years of diligent service in the important go-kariyaku duties of Shinsaburō-emon, he was appointed to go-soku service through special consideration.
1. On the 13th day of the 9th month of the same 13th year, Mizunoe-Tora (1842), due to personnel arrangements, he was appointed to go-inkyo-sama go-soku service.
1. On the 25th day of the 3rd month of Kōka 2, Kinoto-Mi (1845), he was appointed to go-shiban service and go-dōsho go-konando go-meshimono-kata concurrent duties.
1. On the 13th day of the 10th month of the same 3rd year, Hinoe-Uma (1846), he was granted ōmetsuke-kaku rank and appointed to go-dōsho-sama go-zenban. Go-konando go-meshimono-kata continues as concurrent duty as before.
1. On the 28th day of the 5th month of the same 4th year, Hinoto-Hitsuji (1847), he was appointed to go-dōsho-sama go-kinshū metsuke concurrent duties.

First generation
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Miyabe Zenbei
1. On the 15th day of the 1st month of Bunka 15, Tsuchinoe-Tora (1818), he was appointed go-soku service from go-bangata.
1. On the 17th day of the 2nd month of Bunsei 3, Kanoe-Tatsu (1820), since the retired lord received approval for his request this time, he was appointed go-tsuki, with his duties continuing as before.
1. On the 3rd day of the 11th month of Bunsei 6, Mizunoto-Hitsuji (1823), he was appointed go-kinshū-metsuke to the retired lord.
1. On the 15th day of the 8th month of Bunsei 9, Hinoe-Inu (1826), due to illness, as requested, he was relieved of go-soku service to the retired lord and appointed to return to go-ban duties.
1. On the 5th day of the 3rd month of Bunsei 10, Hinoto-I (1827), he was appointed go-soku service from go-bangata.
1. On the 5th day of the 6th month of Bunsei 11, Tsuchinoe-Ne (1828), he was appointed go-konando go-meshimono-kata.
1. On the 20th day of the 9th month of Tenpō 4, Mizunoto-Mi (1833), he was appointed go-shiban-rank go-kinshū-metsuke, with other duties continuing as before.
1. On the 25th day of the 12th month of Tenpō 6, Kinoto-Hitsuji (1835), due to illness, as requested, he was relieved of go-soku service and other official duties and appointed to return to go-ban duties. He was to serve at go-zaishō-zaiban first.

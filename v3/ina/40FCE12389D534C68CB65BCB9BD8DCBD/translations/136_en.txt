Sixth Generation
Tominaga Kihachirō
　　　　　　　Later: Hanpei

1. On the 17th day of the 3rd month of Tenpō 4, Mizunoto-Mi (1833), from go-bangata go-yatoizutome, he was granted succession to his late father's position without change and appointed go-soku service.

1. On the 15th day of the 6th month of the same 8th year, Hinoto-Tori (1837), he was appointed go-kinshū-metsuke.

1. On the 1st day of the 12th month of the same year, he was appointed oshiai regular duty and became senior go-kachi-gashira.

1. On the 16th day of the 8th month of Kaei 1, Tsuchinoe-Saru (1848), with ō-metsuke rank, he was transferred from service to the young lord to go-soku service probationary role for His Lordship, concurrently serving as go-konando go-meshimono-kata.

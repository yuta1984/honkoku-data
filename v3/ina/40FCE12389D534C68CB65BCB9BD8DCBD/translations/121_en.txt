1. On the 13th day of the intercalary 8th month of Bunka 13, Hinoe-Ne (1816), he was appointed go-soku go-yatoi service to Ryōnoshin-sama from go-bangata assistant go-tomogata.
1. On the 25th day of the 11th month of Bunsei 6, Mizunoto-Hitsuji (1823), he was to be dismissed from go-yatoi service due to personnel changes, but since he was familiar with Ryōnoshin-sama's circumstances, he was appointed provisional go-shiyaku go-konando go-meshimono-kata.
1. On the 15th day of the intercalary 8th month of Bunsei 7, Kinoe-Saru (1824), having completed his go-soku service to Ryōnoshin-sama without incident, he was dismissed and appointed go-soku service from go-bangata with one person's stipend for 15 days.
1. On the 25th day of the 10th month of Bunsei 8, Hinoto-Tori (1825), he was granted go-shiban rank and appointed as Ryōnoshin-sama's personal attendant.
1. On the 1st day of the 10th month of Kōka 2, Kinoto-Mi (1845), with mono-gashira rank, he was appointed go-soku go-konando to the lord from being Ryōnoshin-sama's attendant. He continued as Ryōnoshin-sama's attendant as before.
1. On the 15th day of the intercalary 5th month of Kōka 3, Hinoe-Uma (1846), due to illness, he was dismissed from both his service as Ryōnoshin-sama's attendant and his concurrent positions of go-soku service and go-konando to the lord, and ordered to return to regular duty.

Second generation
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Hoshino Shitomi
1. On the 28th day of the 9th month of Kansei 2, year of the Metal Dog (1790), he was appointed to go-soku go-yatoikin (temporary attendant service).
1. On the 3rd day of the 5th month of the 8th year, year of the Fire Dragon (1796), due to excellent performance in go-soku service, he was dismissed and appointed to go-bangata-kata takano-jun-seki.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Son of 4th generation Suketayū
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Ichie Genkichi
1. On the 6th day of the 3rd month of Kansei 4, year of the Water Rat (1792), he was appointed to go-soku go-yatoikin.

1. On the 12th day of the 8th month of the 9th year, year of the Fire Snake (1797), he died of illness while stationed in Osaka.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Fourth generation
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Abiko Shōzō

1. On the 6th day of the 3rd month of Kansei 4, year of the Water Rat (1792), he was appointed to go-soku go-yatoikin.
1. On the 15th day of the 1st month of the 13th year, year of the Metal Rooster (1801), he was appointed to concurrent positions of go-fushin bugyō and go-kura bugyō.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Fourth generation
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Okudaira Takigorō
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　《marginal note: later Yahē》
1. On the 15th day of the 1st month of Kansei 6, year of the Wood Tiger (1794), he was appointed to go-soku go-yatoikin.
1. On the 13th day of the 1st month of the 13th year, year of the Metal Rooster (1801), he was appointed to go-konando and go-meshimono-kata.
1. On the 29th day of the 5th month of Bunka 1, year of the Wood Rat (1804), he absconded while stationed in Edo, resulting in the extinction of his family line.

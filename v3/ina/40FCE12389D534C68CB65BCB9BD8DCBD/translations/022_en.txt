　　　　　　　Third Generation
　　　　　　　　Kaminami Den'goemon
　　　　　　　　　later called Risuke
One, Kyōhō 7th year, Year of the Water Tiger, 9th month, 19th day, was appointed to personal attendant service from guard duty
One, same 11th year, Year of the Fire Horse, 1st month, 7th day, was appointed as meal attendant and concurrently served as minor chamberlain
One, same 19th year, Year of the Wood Tiger, 11th month, 1st day, was appointed to the rank of unit commander, relieved of minor chamberlain duties, and entrusted with sword bearers
One, same 20th year, Year of the Wood Rabbit, 4th month, 13th day, served as personal attendant, and by the dying wish of the former lord, was appointed as musket unit commander

　　　　　　　Second Generation
　　　　　　　　Naitō Jibu-zaemon
　　　　　　　　　later called Ban
One, Kyōhō 7th year, Year of the Water Tiger, 9th month, 19th day, was appointed to personal attendant service from guard duty
One, same 12th year, Year of the Fire Sheep, 9th month, 11th day, was appointed as Arms Magistrate
One, Genbun 3rd year, Year of the Earth Horse, 9th month, 18th day, was appointed to personal attendant service from foot soldier commander, concurrently holding foot soldier commander, second to the personal attendant inspector, senior position in minor chamberlain role
One, Enkyō 2nd year, Year of the Wood Ox, 3rd month, 6th day, was appointed as personal attendant inspector, concurrently holding foot soldier commander, appointed to minor chamberlain role, and exempted from daily attendance
One, same 3rd year, Year of the Fire Tiger, 1st month, 15th day, was appointed as senior inspector rank meal attendant, concurrently holding personal attendant inspector, minor chamberlain, and foot soldier commander
One, same year, 2nd month, 27th day at night, his wife was wounded by some unknown person

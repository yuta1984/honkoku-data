Personal Attendant Service - specifically the unpaid personal attendants

First Generation
Ushioda Magotayu
In the 2nd year of Kanbun, Year of the Water Tiger (1662), he was summoned to service. Thereafter he served in personal attendance duties, and was also appointed to messenger and envoy duties. Later he was appointed head of gun bearers.
However, the exact dates and other details are unknown.

Second Generation
Saji Tomonosuke
In the 11th year of Kanbun, Year of the Metal Boar (1671), he was summoned as a page. Thereafter he served as personal attendant, meal service attendant, and wardrobe keeper.
On the 12th day of the 7th month of the 10th year of Genroku, Year of the Fire Ox (1697), he was appointed to the position of financial magistrate.

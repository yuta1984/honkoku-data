Fukaya Kyūzaburō
1. On the 10th day of the 9th month of Tenpō 15, Kinoe-Tatsu (1844), he was appointed to go-inkyo-sama go-soku go-yatoi service.
1. On the 3rd day of the 8th month of Ka'ei 1, Tsuchinoe-Saru (1848), he was appointed to go-dōsho-sama go-konatō go-meshimono-gata.

Second generation of the family line, actually 6th generation
Nakane Ken'ichirō
1. On the 3rd day of the 2nd month of Kōka 2, Kinoto-Mi (1845), he was appointed from go-tomo-gata go-chū-koshō to waka-tono-sama go-soku service.
1. On the 21st day of the 1st month of Ka'ei 2, Tsuchinoto-Tori (1849), he died of illness.

Iida Seitarō
1. On the 3rd day of the 2nd month of Kōka 2, Kinoto-Mi (1845), he was appointed to waka-tono-sama go-soku go-yatoi service.
1. On the 15th day of the 1st month of the same 4th year, Hinoto-Hitsuji (1847), due to personnel adjustments, he was appointed to go-omote go-soku service.

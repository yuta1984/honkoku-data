The year and month are unknown, but during the Genroku era he was summoned as a middle page, later appointed as personal attendant, and subsequently appointed to serve in Lady Tokubon'in's chambers, which he dutifully performed. However, due to estrangement between father and son, he was granted leave as requested and dismissed.

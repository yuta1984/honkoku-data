1. On the 15th day of the 10th month of Bunka 10, Mizunoto-Tori (1813), he was appointed go-soku service from go-bangata.
1. On the 28th day of the 8th month of Bunka 14, Hinoto-Ushi (1817), he was appointed go-konando go-meshimono-kata. {{Marginal note: At Edo residence}}
1. On the 1st day of the 9th month of Bunsei 2, Tsuchinoto-U (1819), he was granted ō-metsuke rank and appointed go-zenban. Go-konando go-meshimono-kata continued to be held concurrently as before. {{Marginal note: Same as above}}
1. On the 17th day of the 2nd month of Bunsei 3, Kanoe-Tatsu (1820), as the lord's retirement request was granted as requested, he was appointed go-tsuki. The duties remained the same as before. {{Marginal note: Same as above}}
1. On the 3rd day of the 12th month of Bunsei 6, Mizunoto-Hitsuji (1823), he was successfully dismissed from go-inkyosama go-zenban service and appointed temporary go-zenban to the lord. {{Marginal note: Same as above}}
1. On the 15th and 18th days of the 4th month of Bunsei 7, Kinoe-Saru (1824), he was appointed regular go-zenban with concurrent go-kin-bugyō.
1. On the 23rd day of the 11th month of the same year, he was appointed go-tsuki probationary go-konando go-meshimono-kata to Lord Ryōnoshin.

1. On the 18th day of the 6th month of Bunsei 8, Kinoto-Tori (1825), due to changes in Lord Ryōnoshin's residential arrangements, go-tsuki continued as before, but the title of probationary go-konando was abolished. Duties remained the same as before.

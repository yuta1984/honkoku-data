Son of Fifth Generation Hachirōemon
Kōno Toyonosuke

1. On the 15th day of the 1st month of Tenpō 10, Tsuchinoto-I (1839), he was appointed to go-soku service from go-tomogata go-chū-koshō go-yatoizutome.
1. On the 20th day of the 10th month of the same 15th year, Kanoe-Tatsu (1844), due to illness, as requested, he was exempted from go-soku service. Being still young, he should focus on long-term medical treatment, and furthermore, due to the circumstances of the times and established regulations, he was exempted from go-yatoizutome without incident and granted go-gōryoku of one person's stipend.

First, in the tenth month of Bunkyū 3 (1863), year of the boar, by special imperial consideration for the prosperity of the castle town, new townhouses were established, and land from Hokoji Village and Ohara Village was requisitioned as government land from the area facing Benzaiten Bridge. The landowners were paid compensation according to the acreage, and those who petitioned anew were granted land and

Regarding this matter, each person shall possess [their property] permanently, and shall not arbitrarily buy or sell houses and residential lots according to their own convenience. Should it become unavoidable to mortgage or permanently transfer [property], they must petition stating the reasons, and permission shall be granted after examination.

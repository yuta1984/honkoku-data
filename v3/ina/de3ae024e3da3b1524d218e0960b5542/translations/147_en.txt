[Left page]

Due to the passing away of Lord Hitotsubashi in the same manner
　His Excellency the Shogun from the past 20th day
　has observed the prescribed
　mourning period
　His Excellency Enpu observes the prescribed
　half-reduced
　mourning period
　Regarding this, construction work until the coming
　26th day, musical instruments until the 4th day of next month
　shall be suspended, and fire precautions, etc.
　should be ordered with particular care
　The above matters should be [announced] throughout the town

【Left Page】

This time, regarding the increase in porter and horse fees
due to the financial difficulties of Nagakubo post station
on the Nakasendō, the following shall be received
as instructed:

　From the 9th month of the previous Horse year to the 8th month of the current Boar year,
　for a period of five years, transportation fees
　were increased by a total of 45 percent.　　　　　Nakasendō
　Furthermore, from the 9th month of the current Boar year to the 　Nagakubo
　8th month of the coming Dragon year, for another five years,　　post station
　the 45 percent increase shall continue as before.

Regarding the above additional fees, this is hereby
communicated for your understanding.
　Boar year
　  10th month
As written above,
an official notice has been issued from the government,
so this shall be announced throughout the town.

　Boar year
　  11th month

As we have entered the season [of increased fire risk],
special attention should be paid to fire prevention, and particularly
ash dealers and similar merchants should be especially cautioned.

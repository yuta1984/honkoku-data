[Left page]

Yamada Ichirobei

Due to illness, acting on his behalf:
Kobayashi Kinuemon

As this person has become ill, he has petitioned for exemption from sword-bearing as relief. Due to unavoidable circumstances, this is acknowledged and filed. Since sword-bearing is exempted, henceforth he should conduct himself in town duties and all other matters in the same manner as commoners.

The above matter is to be communicated. The above.
February 25th
To Lord Takada Rokuuemon

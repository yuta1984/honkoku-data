Regarding plasterers (sakan) within the domain territory, since there have been no service fees or tribute payments (un-jō) until now, there have been cases where they petitioned the local office expressing their desire to work at construction sites as a form of gratitude (myōga). Upon investigation, while plasterers are generally not considered to be in the same category as the four main craftsmen (shishoku-nin), their petition seems reasonable. Therefore, from now on, each plasterer shall serve three days per year at the construction office. Accordingly, plasterers working within the domain territory as well as those coming from other places to practice the plastering trade shall be treated the same as the four main craftsmen and

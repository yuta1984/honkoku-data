【Right Page】

[He has] handled [duties] and other matters for several years with diligence,
therefore for future official convenience
this time [he is] appointed as town remittance trainee

【Left Page】

Accordingly, [he is] granted permission to wear swords. Following
the regular appointees, with careful attention to all matters
[he shall] endeavor in all official duties of the head office

　November 5th
　　　　　　　　Seri-machi
　　　　　　　　　Shinjirō
　200 mon coins each　Tamehei

Regarding the matter of the above persons, [when there was] a fire in a hut within the grounds of Kenpuku-ji temple, [they] immediately rushed over
and worked [to fight the fire], therefore the fire was extinguished, which was excellent.
Accordingly, coins are granted [to them].
The above matter shall be announced. That is all.
　 November 17th
　　From the scribes

The above fire [occurred on] the night of November 14th, Bunsei 12, Year of the Earth Ox,
around half past the 6th hour at dusk

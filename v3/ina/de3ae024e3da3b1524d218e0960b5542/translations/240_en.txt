　6th month, 14th day
One town representative was summoned and given the following instructions

For the sake of circulation in society
it has been ordered that one-shu denomination
coins be minted using high-quality nanryō silver, so right method
sixteen coins shall constitute the value of one ryō in gold
Moreover, for silver and copper coins as well as money exchange, two-shu coins
should be understood at the same rate, and these
should be used in combination with the existing
one-shu coins for circulation. Regarding the aforementioned nanryō one-
shu silver coins, they have been ordered
to circulate in the same manner as gold, so they should be put into circulation
without delay

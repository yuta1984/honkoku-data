　　　5th month
　Bunsei 12, year of the ox (1829), 5th month
The coming 27th day
The 50th memorial service for Lord Genryūin
As there will be a Buddhist memorial service and prayers at Mankōji Temple, it is ordered that besides regular business activities, all killing of living beings is strictly forbidden, and musical instruments and loud voices should be avoided, with particular careful attention paid to fire prevention.
　However, construction work need not be refrained from.

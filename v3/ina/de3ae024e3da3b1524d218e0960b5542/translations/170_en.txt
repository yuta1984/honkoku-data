Regarding the matter of sake brewing by those who do not engage in proper practices in later generations, we previously issued a notice in the 12th month of the year of the Rooster stating that there would be no need to pursue further deliberation on this matter. Since this is no longer necessary, sake brewing shall be conducted at one's own discretion as it was before the year of the Rooster.
This directive shall be announced without omission to all domains including government lands (goryō), private domains, and temple/shrine lands.

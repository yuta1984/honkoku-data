Even after the prohibition of old nishu-ban coins goes into effect, there may still be old nishu-ban coins remaining unexchanged in distant provinces and other places due to unavoidable circumstances. Therefore, the exchange period will be extended until the 12th month of the coming Year of the Ox as before, so those possessing old nishu-ban coins should present them for exchange without delay.
However, the same applies to gold and silver exchange offices.

1. Regarding the exchange of nishu-ban coins, in some provinces the distance to the nearest exchange office is considerable, and travel expenses for carrying them there would also be incurred.

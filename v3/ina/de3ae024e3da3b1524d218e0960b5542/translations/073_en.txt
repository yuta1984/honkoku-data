【Right Page】

Wishing to repay the benevolent favor, as an act of devotion, the seven
Districts together with the townspeople wish to unite and
Make a monetary offering, such being our earnest desire

【Left Page】

Due to these special circumstances,
Having received and acknowledged this, it was immediately submitted for
Your consideration today. We humbly believe all should be grateful.

October 4th

Regarding the fact that the rural districts and townspeople have
Made monetary offerings this time, this being deemed
An act of exceptional devotion, sake and delicacies
Have been graciously bestowed, which we shall gratefully
Receive.

October 4th

The above two written notices were
Proclaimed at the castle. Two copies each were distributed
To the various districts. Upon returning, they were shown to fellow officials, and handed over
To the magistrates of Gyoji and Shimizu districts.

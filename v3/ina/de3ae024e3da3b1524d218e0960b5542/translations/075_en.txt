　　　Village Notice　Hagura Geki
Regarding the matter of exchanging old gold and silver two-shu coins,
this time from the Finance Magistrate and the same Investigation Officer,
a copy of one village notice with joint seals is attached, and the main document
is sealed and circulated. Therefore, village officials shall
affix their seals to the receipt, and those who wish to inspect the main document
should open the seal, taking care that there be no
blackening or soiling, and furthermore reseal it, adding a
disclaimer, and with careful attention that nothing be omitted, with proper seal
markings, circulate it promptly, and from the final location
send it to our office in Iijima-machi, Shinshū

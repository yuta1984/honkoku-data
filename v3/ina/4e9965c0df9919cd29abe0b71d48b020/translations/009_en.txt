One: Since you graciously told us that we should by all means proceed to Iida,
　we requested leave and departed.
　One: We lodged for one night at Shioya Yaheiji's residence.
One: The matters we inquired about with the Asuke village officials are
　recorded in a separate document. Furthermore, according to what we heard,
　　we should prepare about six or seven desks. Inkstone boxes are
　　not required. At Koromo, we heard rumors that officials of about the rank of gokyu-ban
　　are assigned there, but
　　at Asuke, higher-ranking officials of the go-tsukai-ban
　　come to the honjin. Furthermore, when they depart,
　　young retainers carry spears and document boxes
　　as attendants, they appear in formal dress, present their credentials, and
　　perform greetings, which we witnessed.

One: We were told that they stop at all places
　where there are government offices (jin'ya) and such.
One: Regarding how they separate and make their rounds
　in Sanshu region as well, it is as recorded in the separate document.
One: Along all the routes they pass, road improvements have been made.
　Even mountain paths have been made clean
　by spreading sand.
One: At Asuke as well, though not without exception,
　there are many houses that
　have fallen into disrepair.

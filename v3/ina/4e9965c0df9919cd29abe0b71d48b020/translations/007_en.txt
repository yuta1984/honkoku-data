1. When I reported that it is about three ri from Hiraide to Oka-no-tani:

His lordship said:
　The surveying (bunma) from Oka-no-tani to Aizawa has already
　been completed previously. What about the place called Ono?

　　In response:
　　Regarding that matter, it is not on
　　the road to Shimo-Suwa. From Ono,
　　it is the road by which one would proceed
　　to Shiojiri post station.

1. Regarding the distance from Oka-no-tani to Shimo-Suwa:
　How is it?
　　It might be about two ri or so.
　　I reported that I do not know for certain.

1. Regarding the distance from Inabu village to Takato:
　How is it?
　　It is two ri.

Article: Regarding the official notice boards and prohibitions for the government forests and bamboo groves that have been decreed from long ago, it goes without saying that we understand the intent conveyed to us during this round of inspections and investigations. In recent years, these have been entrusted to village officials, but due to inadequate supervision, no instructions were given to forest guards, grove guards, or all the villagers, resulting in neglect. The saplings in the government forests have not grown properly, and likewise the bamboo groves have been left in the same condition, so that even good bamboo does not grow within the groves. We have no excuse for these matters of concern. From now on, so that the government forests and bamboo groves will flourish and not become wasteland, we will strictly instruct not only all the villagers but also men, women, and children to carefully protect them.

Article: When people from our village, not to mention those from within the domain, enter the government forests and cut down pine trees or young trees regardless of type, or come for cutting brush, pine debris, or gathering leaves, we shall immediately go there and apprehend not only their good hatchets and sickles but also capture the persons themselves and promptly report this to the authorities. If people from other domains come and commit such acts, we shall confiscate only their tools and that person...

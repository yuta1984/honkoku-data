Memorandum

Article: Within our village, there was one bridge on the public road that became damaged. I repaired it using timber and tools from my own resources. When this matter reached the authorities, after investigation, I was graciously granted six replacement trees. I am most grateful for this. I have cut them as originally done and received the approval seal as shown here. The above stated.

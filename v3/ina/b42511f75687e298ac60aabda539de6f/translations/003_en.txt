Pledge Document Respectfully Submitted

Regarding gambling and various betting games that have been previously prohibited by official decree, we should strictly observe all forbidden practices. However, there have been those who have acted inappropriately, resulting in official intervention, for which we are deeply ashamed. Hereafter, within our five-household groups, we shall strictly coordinate with each other and absolutely observe the prohibited practices. Should there be anyone who acts inappropriately, the neighboring households in the group shall earnestly advise and reform them. If they do not comply and any member of our group engages in improper conduct that comes to official attention, regardless of what official judgment we may receive, we shall offer no excuses whatsoever. Therefore, we all affix our seals together and respectfully submit this pledge without any falsehood. The above.

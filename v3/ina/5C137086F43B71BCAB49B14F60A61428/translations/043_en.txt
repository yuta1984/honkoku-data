Karasawa Masukuemon

During the reign of Lord Yoriyoshi
- Kansei 5 (1793), 3rd month: Appointed as chūgen kogashira (assistant head of servants)
- Kansei 6 (1794), 7th month, 23rd day: Ordered to join the osakte-gumi (advance guard unit), duties to remain the same as before
- Kansei 7 (1795), 6th month, 29th day: As substitute for adoptive father Ichiuemon, granted a stipend of 3 koku 5 to and support for 2 persons, duties to remain the same as before
- Kansei 8 (1796), 5th month, 2nd day: Relieved of chūgen kogashira position and assigned to osakte-gumi, with additional rice allowance of 3 to
- Kyōwa 1 (1801), 8th month, 1st day: Kitchen duty under gimmi-kata supervision
- Same year, 12th month, 24th day: Culinary department apprentice with additional rice allowance of 5 to
- Bunka 3 (1806), 1st month, 15th day: Assistant in culinary department with additional rice allowance of 1 koku
- Bunka 7 (1810), 3rd month, 18th day: Promoted to ashigaru rank and appointed to full culinary department position, granted stipend of 17 hyō and support for 2 persons
- Bunka 10 (1813), 3rd month, 23rd day: Appointed to concurrently serve in the culinary department of young lord Kannosuke-sama

(In red ink) Karasawa

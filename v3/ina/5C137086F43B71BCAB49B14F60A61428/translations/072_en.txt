Takahashi Rinzaemon

During the reign of Lord Yorimoto:
- Kansei 10 (1798): Appointed as group inspector (kumi-metsuke) with an additional rice stipend of three to
- Kyōwa 3 (1803), 9th month, 25th day: Due to illness, his son Seisaburō was appointed as his substitute guard upon request
- Bunka 2 (1805), 7th month, 28th day: Assigned to net-casting duties, granted one and a half person's rations during service
- Bunka 3 (1806), 5th month, 28th day: Appointed as assistant to the castle deputy group
- Bunka 10 (1813), 1st month, 15th day: Half ration increased

During the reign of Lord Yoriyasu:
- Bunpō 6 (1823), 11th month, 25th day: Due to administrative reorganization, dismissed from employment duties, permitted to pursue other work at his discretion
- Bunpō 10 (1827), 2nd month, 6th day: Appointed as regular employee of the castle deputy group, granted one person's rations
- Tenpō 2 (1831), 1st month, 15th day: Granted two koku of ishikiri rice as a lifetime physician
- Tenpō 10 (1839), 11th month, 24th day: Died of illness

End of Service Record

(In red ink) "Takahashi"

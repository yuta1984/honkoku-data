- On the 8th day of the 11th month of Bunsei 7 (1824), he was appointed as a subordinate official in the financial department and received an additional 5 to of rice
- On the 22nd day of the 6th month of Bunsei 9 (1826), since the court presenter had received orders, while he understood the official documents related to his duties, he was temporarily ordered to go to Edo as an assistant
- On the 15th day of the 1st month of Bunsei 10 (1827), he received an additional 8 to of rice, totaling 5 koku 3 to granted to him
- On the 23rd day of the 2nd month of Tenpō 2 (1831), he was appointed as an apprentice assistant subordinate official in the financial department, handling monetary payments. His subordinate duties remained the same as before
- On the 13th day of the 9th month of Tenpō 5 (1834), he was promoted to the rank of foot soldier, granted 16 hyō and a stipend for two persons as an assistant subordinate official in the financial department, under the supervision of the chief retainer
- On the 15th day of the 1st month of Kōka 5 (1848), according to his seniority among the foot soldiers, he received an increase of 1 hyō
- On a certain day in the 5th month of Ansei 2 (1855), due to old age and illness, as requested, he was relieved of his position as assistant subordinate official to the financial magistrate. Having served faithfully for several years, he was granted a monetary reward
- On the 21st day of the 9th month of the same year, he died of illness
　　　End of service record

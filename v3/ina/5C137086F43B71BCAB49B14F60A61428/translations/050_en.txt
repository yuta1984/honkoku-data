Ise Yaemon
(Original name: Zengoro)

1. Kansei 4, year of the Water Rat (1792), 4th month, 22nd day: With an additional rice stipend of 5 to, relocated to the lord's lower residence
   5 koku 5 to [total stipend], assigned to the front gate guard

1. Bunka 4, year of the Fire Rabbit (1807), 5th month, 3rd day: Appointed as assistant in the construction department of the lord's lower residence
   Roof craftsman duties to continue as before

1. Same 5th year, year of the Earth Dragon (1808), 9th month, 15th day: Ordered to specialize in roof craftsman duties
   When conflicts arise, assistant duties to continue as before

   Appointed as gatekeeper of the retired lord's rest quarters, henceforth to hold

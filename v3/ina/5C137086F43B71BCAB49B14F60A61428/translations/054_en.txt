Yamaura Yagohachi
During the reign of Lord Yoriyasu
1. Bunpō 6, Mizunoe-Hitsuji, 4th month, 28th day - Succeeded his father Gotayū's position with a stipend
   of 5 koku and rations for two people. Because Gotayū had served faithfully for several years,
   he was ordered to join the castle deputy group
1. Same 7th year, Kinoe-Saru, 4th month, 5th day - Appointed to serve as residential priest
1. Same year, 12th month, 21st day - Appointed to serve as inner residential priest, combined priest duties
1. Same 10th year, Hinoto-I, 10th month, 15th day - Appointed to serve as front priest
1. Same 13th year, Kanoe-Tora, 11th month, 15th day - Appointed as assistant inner priest, stationed away

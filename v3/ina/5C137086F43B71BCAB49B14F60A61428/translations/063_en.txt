Ushiyama Shinzō

Reign of Lord Yorimoto
1. Kansei 6 (1794), 9th month, 14th day: Appointed as replacement for father Shinzō
1. Bunka 9 (1812), 3rd month, 13th day: Appointed as inspector (metsuke), with additional three tō of rice

Reign of Lord Yoriyasu  
1. Bunsei 5 (1822), 4th month, 18th day: Appointed as squad leader under Okano Gihei,
   with six-year additional rice allowance
1. Tenpō 11 (1840), 3rd month, 28th day: Appointed as squad leader under Onodera Tōuemon
1. Kōka 3 (1846), 1st month, 15th day: Promoted in rank to foot soldier status,
   granted seventeen hyō and provisions for two people, appointed to guard duty rotation, under
   supervision of chief retainer
1. Kaei 5 (1852), 10th month, 5th day: Died of illness
　　　　 Seven years after promotion to service

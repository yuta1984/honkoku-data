From an auspicious day in January of Meiji 14 (1881), construction of silk reeling machinery began, and was completed in June. On the 29th day of the same month, an auspicious day with clear weather, the first silk reeling ceremony was conducted. On the 25th, all female workers were notified of the schedule in advance.

On June 28th, after 4 PM, Mr. Kitahara from Tonogaito came to attend the inaugural ceremony and stayed overnight.

On the 29th, from 8 AM, female workers gathered one after another.

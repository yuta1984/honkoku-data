1. 1st year of Kōka, Year of the Dragon (1844), last day of December: Due to shortage of personnel, appointed to employed service in guard and attendant duties, granted increased support for two retainers.

1. 2nd year of same era, Year of the Snake (1845), June 23rd: Appointed to lower residence stewardship.

1. Same year, August 8th: Following his deceased father Teizō's precedent, granted a stipend of 30 hyō and support for two retainers, appointed to guard duty with the rank of page.

1. 3rd year of same era, Year of the Horse (1846), intercalary 5th month, 1st day: Appointed to attend the young lord.

1. Same year, August 10th: Granted official duty allowance as customary.

1. 2nd year of Kaei, Year of the Snake (1849), March 1st: Appointed to upper residence stewardship, transferred to the upper residence on April 2nd.

1. 3rd year of same era, Year of the Dog (1850), April 26th: Due to the young lord's coming-of-age ceremony and sleeve-shortening ritual, appointed as attendant, notification from the chamberlain.

1. Same year, June 24th: His younger brother Teishirō appointed to temporary employed service as foot soldier, granted support for one and a half retainers, assigned to the secretarial office, and temporarily appointed as messenger. Under the supervision of the chamberlain.

1. Same year, September 15th: Due to the young lord's procession and the presence of guests, granted three bu in gold as preparation allowance.

1. 4th year of same era, Year of the Boar (1851), April 8th: Due to illness, relieved of attending the young lord as requested, appointed as guard page. Under chamberlain supervision.

1. 5th year of same era, Year of the Rat (1852), [month and day blank]: Appointed to lower residence stewardship.

1. 7th year of same era, Year of the Tiger (1854), April 18th: Absconded.

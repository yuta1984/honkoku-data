1. Same year, 11th month, 10th day: Request approved to send second son Yōzō as adopted son to Nakamura Hyōuemon's family

Reign of Lord Honryūin

1. Same 9th year, year of the rat (1780), 11th month, 9th day: Appointed middle page inspector, granted increase of 4 hyō

Reign of Lord Yoriyoshi

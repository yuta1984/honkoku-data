Morita Daisuke
- Kansei 1 (1789), 6th month, 20th day: He was appointed as magistrate clerk, permitted to serve wearing hakama, and granted a substitute allowance of 2 bu, under magistrate supervision [from Satō Seidayū's group].
- 2nd year (Kansei 2, 1790), 2nd month, 6th day: He was promoted to foot soldier with a stipend of eighteen hyō and two-person support. His duties as chief retainer/elder clerk and magistrate clerk continued as before.
- 3rd year (Kansei 3, 1791), 7th month, 5th day: His request regarding dependent status was approved in Osaka.
- Same year, 7th month:片岡団太, a cousin of Katakura village's Kyūzaemon, who was currently serving as a retainer to Lord Katō Kazue, was accepted as a dependent and his request to become an adopted son of Izawa Naouemon was approved.

Morita Genkō
    Kumetarō
    Sukeshirō
    Moroemon
- Kansei 4 (1792), 10th month, 5th day: He was granted relief support of three-person sustenance.
- 9th year (Kansei 9, 1797), 2nd month, 15th day: He was appointed as assistant clerk.
- 10th year (Kansei 10, 1798), 1st month, 15th day: He inherited his late father Daisuke's position with a stipend of eighteen hyō and two-person support, and was made a foot soldier.
- Kyōwa 2 (1802), 7th month, 13th day: As he wished to pursue medical studies, he was granted five years' leave, surrendering his stipend allocation for training purposes, as requested.
- Bunka 3 (1806), 8th month, 28th day: Having completed five years of medical training during his leave and now returning, he was restored to his former foot soldier position with a stipend of eighteen hyō and two-person support, and appointed to provide medical treatment within the domain.
- 4th year (Bunka 4, 1807), 4th month, 9th day: Due to certain implied circumstances, two hyō were deducted from his stipend, he was appointed to foot soldier rank rotating guard duty, his medical practice was terminated, and he was ordered to return to secular life and house confinement.
- Same year, 5th month, 30th day: House confinement was lifted.
- Same year, 6th month, 15th day: The family line was terminated due to his flight/desertion.

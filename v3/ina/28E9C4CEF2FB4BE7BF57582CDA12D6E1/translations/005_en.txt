Son of Kōuemon, initially named Sadenjii
Inoue Sakutadayū

1. Kyōhō 17, year of the rat (1732): As per the wishes left by deceased father Kōuemon, being of young age, granted relief stipend of rice for 3 people

During the reign of Gen'ryūin-sama
1. Same 20th year, year of the rabbit (1735), 5th month, 19th day: From tomorrow, he and Koie Katsu-roku are to take turns serving periods in Edo, attending to calls from monks and handling various affairs for the senior councilors and administrators, and learning official service. To report at 4 o'clock and remain until the senior councilors' departure

1. Genbun 2, year of the snake (1737), 1st month, 15th day: Promoted from child without rank to ranked go-ashigaru and granted residential stipend for 3 people

1. Same 4th year, year of the sheep (1739), 2nd month, 13th day: Request approved to send sister as adopted daughter to Mikami Jūgorōuemon's family, as she is niece to his wife

1. Same 4th year, 3rd month, 8th day: Having come of age, granted stipend of 18 hyō and rice for 2 people, appointed as go-tomo-gata go-ashigaru under command of ashigaru-gashira

1. Enkyō 1, year of the rat (1744), 3rd month, 28th day: Request approved to send sister as wife to Yaishi Gorōuemon, retainer of Matsudaira Yamato-no-kami

1. Kan'en 1, year of the dragon (1748), 11th month, 21st day: Request approved to send younger brother as adopted son to Higuchi Jindayū, retainer of Shimada Ichijūrō

1. Same 3rd year, year of the horse (1750), 8th month, 18th day: Appointed to kitchen duty, henceforth under administrator supervision. Given that deceased father Kōuemon had experience in such work, assigned to upper residence kitchen so as to be useful in the future《marginal note: until now was go-tomo-gata go-ashigaru》

1. Hōreki 4, year of the dog (1754), 4th month, 28th day: Request approved to marry sister of Mizoguchi Motoemon, retainer of Uchida Tonomo

1. Same year, 8th month, 3rd day: Due to incompatibility with wife, returned her to Mizoguchi Motoemon's family

1. Same year, 11th month, 5th day: Due to great retrenchment, kitchen staff reduced, so temporarily appointed to escort duty

1. Same 7th year, year of the ox (1757), 4th month, 8th day: Appointed to catering duty, concurrently serving kitchen duty

1. Same 8th year, year of the tiger (1758), 5th month, 15th day: Appointed to temporary equipment duty

1. Same year, 10th month, 18th day: Appointed to kitchen duty for Shōgeiin-sama and appointed to catering duty

1. Same 10th year, year of the dragon (1760), 11th month, 25th day: Request approved to marry as second wife the daughter of gōshi Ōhara Heishirō, residing in Umeda village, Adachi district, Musashi province, under jurisdiction of Ina Hanzaemon

1. Same 11th year, year of the snake (1761), 8th month, 24th day: Divorced wife due to incompatibility

1. Meiwa 1, year of the monkey (1764), 10th month, 3rd day: Fukushima Denjirō, retainer of Andō Tsushima-no-kami

1. Same 10th year, Year of the Horse (1798), June 28th: Absconded
1. Same 12th year, Year of the Rat (1800), intercalary April 22nd: Petition approved to become second wife under Matsuda Denshichi's care
1. Same year, May 17th: Temporarily assigned as assistant in the lady's kitchen department, concurrently appointed as lower castle gate guard
1. Same 13th year, Year of the Rooster (1801), January 15th: Document clerk status with three to of additional rice, apprentice in lady's kitchen department, lower castle gate guard as before, under inner palace supervision
1. 3rd year of Kyōwa, Year of the Boar (1803), January 20th: Ordered to serve exclusively as lower castle gate guard
1. 9th year of Bunka, Year of the Monkey (1812), October 15th: Petition approved to adopt Hachiōji, second son of town physician Fukunaga San'ei. Same year November 3rd, wife died
1. Same 10th year, Year of the Rooster (1813), March 23rd: Promoted to ashigaru (foot soldier) rank, appointed to serve □【Kane?】nosuke-sama's palace duties, granted seventeen hyō and support for two retainers under chief retainer's supervision
1. Same year, December 23rd: Granted one koku as official stipend
1. Same 11th year, Year of the Dog (1814), June 25th: Relieved of □【Kane?】nosuke-sama's palace duties, appointed as tea house keeper at the lower residence
1. 2nd year of Bunsei, Year of the Rabbit (1819), June 3rd: Died of illness

1. Same 10th year, Year of the Rooster (1813), March 23rd: Appointed as lower castle gate guard of the Eastern Palace, under inner palace supervision
1. Same 11th year, Year of the Dog (1814), February 8th: Appointed to temporary service as palace monk
1. Same 13th year, Year of the Rat (1816), July 5th: Appointed as assistant in the young lord's private quarters
1. Same 14th year, Year of the Ox (1817), June 8th: This time ordered to shave head (become a monk), therefore as celebration granted 100 hiki of gold, changed name to Ensai
1. 2nd year of Bunsei, Year of the Rabbit (1819), June 5th: Appointed to upper residence domestic affairs
1. Same year, July 23rd: Inherited deceased father Kichiemon's position with five koku and support for two retainers, appointed to full service in private quarters
1. Same 3rd year, Year of the Dragon (1820), February 17th: Appointed as monk supervisor at lower residence, inner service to continue as before

• Kan'en 10, 4th month, 14th day (April 14, 1757): Request completed to take as second wife the adopted daughter of Tanaka Gisuke's mother
• Kan'en 11, 8th month, 3rd day (August 3, 1758): Divorced his wife and returned her to the Tanaka Gisuke household
• Kan'en 12, 7th month, 28th day (July 28, 1759): His father died while in retirement
• Same year, 4th month, 9th day: Relieved of scribe duties and appointed to attendant service
• Meiwa 5, 5th month (May 1768): Request completed to marry the adopted daughter of Itō Isen
• Meiwa 6, from 1st day of 5th month, on the 10th day of the Rabbit: Proxy pilgrimage to Myōgi
• Meiwa 7, 4th month, 28th day (April 28, 1770): Granted formal attendant rank, increased by 4 hyō to a total stipend of 22 hyō with provisions for two people; work assignments to be communicated by the administrative office

During the reign of Honryūin-sama
• An'ei 6, 2nd month, 28th day (February 28, 1777): Appointed to tax collection office
• An'ei 9, 11th month, 9th day (November 9, 1780): Granted middle page rank, appointed to investigation office, increased by provisions for one person
• Tenmei 3, 7th month, 5th day (July 5, 1783): Continued appointment as Edo secretary in investigation office as before, increased by 4 hyō to total stipend of 26 hyō with provisions for three people《Marginal note: At this time he was in the domain》
• Tenmei 5, 8th month, 7th day (August 7, 1785): As prisoner Naozō was being sent to the domain, he was sent as escort and given 200 mon in gold
• Same year, 10th month, 23rd day: Appointed as middle page inspector, secretary investigation office to continue as before
• Tenmei 7, 2nd month, 29th day (February 29, 1787): Increased by 4 hyō to total stipend of 30 hyō with provisions for three people
• Same year, 3rd month, 22nd day: Appointed as head of the administrative office
• Same year, 12th month, 28th day: Ordered to conduct proxy pilgrimage to Myōgi on the day of the Rabbit in the coming New Year
• Tenmei 8, 11th month, 14th day (November 14, 1788): Appointed as year representative
• Tenmei 9, 1st month, 12th day (January 12, 1789): Had been serving in the scribe office until now, but henceforth to serve in the same manner as external appointments
• Same year, 2nd month, 4th day: Ordered to conduct proxy pilgrimage to Ise Province
• Kansei 1, 3rd month, 23rd day (March 23, 1789): Appointed as temporary relief distribution officer
• Same year, 5th month, 10th day: Due to multiple duties, smoothly relieved of scribe position
• Kansei 2, 5th month, 13th day (May 13, 1790): Absconded

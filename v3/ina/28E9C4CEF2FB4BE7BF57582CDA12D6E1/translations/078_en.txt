- On the 14th day of the 11th month of Kyōhō 9 (1724), Year of the Dragon, he was assigned to six months' garrison duty at the lord's domain.
- On the 4th day of the 12th month of the same year, he was appointed under the supervision of the foot soldier commander while at the lord's domain.
- On the 7th day of the 5th month of Kyōhō 17 (1732), Year of the Rat, he was appointed as foot soldier inspector with additional stipend of 4 ryō 2 bu and provisions for two people.

Reign of Lord Genryūin
- On the 12th day of the 8th month of Genbun 1 (1736), Year of the Dragon, he was appointed as chief of attendants with attendant rank, and granted an additional stipend raised to 5 ryō.
- On the 6th day of the 2nd month of Genbun 4 (1739), Year of the Sheep, his petition to take the sister of Igarashi Naoemon from the household of Lord Mizuno Iki-no-kami as wife was approved. He took her in marriage on the 11th day of the 3rd month.
- On the 15th day of the 7th month of Kanpō 3 (1743), Year of the Boar, his wife died.
- On the 7th day of the 10th month of the same year, his petition to take as second wife the sister of his first wife, sister of Igarashi Naoemon, a retainer of Lord Mizuno Iki-no-kami, was approved.
- On the 26th day of the 12th month of the same year, he received an increase of 3 hyō, making his total stipend 25 hyō with provisions for two people. At this time he held the position of chief attendant.
- On the 6th day of the 11th month of Enkyō 1 (1744), Year of the Rat, he died of illness.
　　　　　In service

　　　　　　　　　　　　Satō Saitarō
- On the 28th day of the 12th month of Enkyō 1 (1744), Year of the Rat, due to succeeding his father's position while still young, he was granted assistance of provisions for three people.
- On the 15th day of the 1st month of Enkyō 2 (1745), Year of the Ox, as announced according to Sanpei Tanzaburō's petition, Saiemon's widow was to be sent as second wife to the Nakane Tōemon household. Since Saitarō was young, [Nakane] took him in and cared for him.
- On the last day of the 9th month of Enkyō 4 (1747), Year of the Rabbit, he died of illness.
　　　　　Although his deceased father had been employed quite favorably, no succession was petitioned for, and only the customary notification was made. He was young and without service.

　　　　　　　　　　　　Satō Gondayū
Reign of Lord Shin'nyoin
- On the 15th day of the 7th month of Kyōhō 3 (1718), Year of the Dog, he was summoned with a stipend of 5 ryō and provisions for two people. On the same day he had an audience [with the lord].
- On the 11th day of the 7th month of Kyōhō 4 (1719), Year of the Boar, he was appointed as secretary to the chamberlain.
- On the 11th day of the 7th month of Kyōhō 7 (1722), Year of the Tiger, he was appointed to provisions management.

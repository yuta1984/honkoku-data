Memorandum

Norikimio, son of former Middle Counselor Ano, underwent his coming-of-age ceremony and was granted audience at court today. On the same day, his appointment to the position of Chamberlain with Senior Fifth Rank Upper Grade has been granted imperial permission. We inform you of this for your knowledge.

18th day of the 9th month
Former Middle Counselor Chigusa
Former Major Counselor Yanagihara

To Lord Naito, Governor of Yamato Province

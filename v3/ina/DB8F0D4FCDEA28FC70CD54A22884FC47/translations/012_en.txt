We humbly submit this written petition:
Regarding the famous place called Kitamatsuo, we had understood that our ancestors from four or five generations ago had inherited this land and conducted transactions such as transfers. However, on this occasion, when officials came to inspect the domain forests and made various inquiries before reaching an understanding, it was found that there exists a domain forest called Kitamatsuo, and we learned for the first time that our ancestors had contracted as mountain guards. We have no excuse for this and humbly apologize.
We respectfully acknowledge the directive that boundaries will be established after inspection. We do not know what misunderstanding our ancestors had when they conducted the transfer. Should this land now be reclaimed by the domain, it would be most unfortunate for the current landowners, but if this accords with the official policy, we understand it cannot be helped. We humbly request, with the utmost compassion, that consideration be given multiple times so that the current landowners may continue to make their living.

　　　　　　Petitioner: Hyōemon (seal)
　　　　　　Same: Heijū (seal)

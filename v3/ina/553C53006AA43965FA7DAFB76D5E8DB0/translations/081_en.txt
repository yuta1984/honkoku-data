Oya Jinpei

During the reign of Lord Honryu-in
- March 23rd, Kansei 2 (1790): Promoted to ashigaru (foot soldier), granted a stipend of 18 hyō and food allowance for two people, temporarily assigned to serve at the district magistrate's office

During the reign of Lord Yorimoto-kun  
- January 15th, Kansei 8 (1796): Assigned to the chief clerk's office handling payments and firewood distribution
- October 1st, Kansei 11 (1799): Promoted to formal attendant status with an increase of 2 hyō, appointed as deputy magistrate
- October 7th, Kyōwa 1 (1801): Relieved of duties as requested, assigned to secondary duty rotation
- August, Kyōwa 2 (1802): Died of illness. His adopted son Shikihachi was appointed to succeed his position
　　　　Service record

Adopted son
Oya Shikihachi
(Adopted son of Jinpei)

- October 20th, Kyōwa 2 (1802): Succeeded his late father Jinpei's position, granted stipend of 18 hyō and food allowance for two people, appointed as attendant corps foot soldier
- June 28th, Bunka 5 (1808): Appointed as bamboo grove inspector for the lord's forest lands

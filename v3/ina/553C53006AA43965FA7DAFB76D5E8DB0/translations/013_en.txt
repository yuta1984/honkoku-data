From the Gojōdai-gumi Metsuke (Castle Deputy Group Inspector):
Mikami Seibei
(Jūgorōemon)

During the reign of Lord Yorimoto:
- Kansei 8 (1796), 3rd month, 20th day: Appointed as shita-metsuke (junior inspector) and granted additional rice stipend of 3 to
- Same 12th year (1800), 12th month, 7th day: Appointed as clerk and inspector for various construction department procurement and disbursement matters
- Kyōwa 3 (1803), 1st month, 15th day: Granted additional rice stipend of 3 to
- Bunka 4 (1807), 6th month, 13th day: Promoted to ashigaru (foot soldier), granted stipend of 18 hyō and provisions for 2 persons, duties to continue as before, specifically supervising the construction department
- Same 9th year (1812), 1st month, 15th day: Granted additional stipend increase of 2 hyō
- Same 13th year (1816), 12th month, 20th day: Appointed to ōnadoto clothing department, successfully completed duties as construction department procurement and disbursement inspector and was relieved of those duties. Due to diligent service over many years despite numerous official responsibilities, awarded 200 hiki of gold as commendation

During the reign of Lord Yoriyasu:
- Bunsei 4 (1821), 11th month, 28th day: Due to advanced age and illness, relieved of both official positions as requested and appointed to go-jiban (next shift duty)

(In red ink) Mikami

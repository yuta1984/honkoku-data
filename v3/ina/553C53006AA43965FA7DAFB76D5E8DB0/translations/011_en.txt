Service record

Koike Okasaburō
《Side note: Changed name to Yuzaemon》

During the reign of Lord Yoriyasu
- On the 23rd day of the 8th month of Bunsei 2, year of the Rabbit (1819), he was appointed to temporary ashigaru service and granted support provisions for 1.5 people.
- On the 13th day of the 7th month of Bunsei 4, year of the Metal Snake (1821), he was appointed as escort ashigaru, received an increase of half provisions, and came under the command of the ashigaru head.
- On the 20th day of the 10th month of Bunsei 6, year of the Water Sheep (1823), he inherited his late father Hanzaemon's position with a stipend of 18 hyō and provisions for 2 people as escort ashigaru.
- On the 3rd day of the 8th month of Tenpō 4, year of the Water Snake (1833), he was appointed to ashigaru watchman duties.
- On the 15th day of the 1st month of Tenpō 5, year of the Wood Horse (1834), he received an increase of 2 hyō, bringing his stipend to 20 hyō and provisions for 2 people.
- On the 18th day of the 7th month of the same year, he was appointed as head of regular patrol duties for maintaining order among retainers. However, he should understand that his other duties remain the same and he should focus primarily on patrol duties.

- On the 20th day of the 9th month of Tenpō 7, year of the Fire Monkey (1836), during patrol on the night of the 17th, when he encountered a suspicious person, his response was immature and negligent, resulting in the confiscation of 2 hyō from his stipend, dismissal from ashigaru inspector duties, and an order of discretionary restraint.
- On the 4th day of the 10th month of the same year, the restraint was lifted and he was also dismissed from the head of regular patrol duties.
- On the 1st day of the 2nd month of Tenpō 9, year of the Dog (1838), he was appointed to special return duties and, due to exceptional service, was granted 1 bu of gold.
- On the 13th day of the intercalary 4th month of the same year, he was promoted to ranked escort guard with an increase of 2 hyō, appointed to attend Lord Ryōnoshin's residence, and granted 1 koku of rice as a retainer under the chamberlain's supervision.
- On the 27th day of the same month of the same year, regarding the recent fire at the main Edo residence, he went out with the official seal and showed various concerns, which reached his lord's attention and was considered meritorious, resulting in the grant of soup and sake.
- On the 3rd day of the 4th month of Tenpō 13, year of the Water Tiger (1842), the request to take care of Shin'ichirō, the third son of farmer Rokurōji from Nagaoka Village in Matsumoto domain territory, was approved.
- On the 25th day of the 1st month of Tenpō 14, year of the Water Rabbit (1843), he was relieved of duty attending Lord Ryōnoshin's residence and henceforth assigned to reserve guard duty.
- On the 18th day of the 12th month of Kōka 3, year of the Fire Horse (1846), his adoption request was approved as petitioned.

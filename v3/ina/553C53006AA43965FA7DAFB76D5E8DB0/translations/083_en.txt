- May 18th, Bunsei 12 (1829): Assigned to assist monk attendants while also serving as a trainee in service duties
- January 15th, Tenpō 2 (1831): Appointed to hired service for monk attendants with an increase of one person's food allowance
- January 15th, Tenpō 3 (1832): Appointed to hired service as attendant ashigaru with an increase of half food allowance, under the supervision of the ashigaru head
- June 18th, Tenpō 5 (1834): Appointed as assistant official in the products department
- September 13th, same year: Appointed as temporary supervisor of town administration, continuing products department assistant duties as before, under district magistrate supervision
- October 13th, same year: Appointed to inspector duties, continuing products department assistant duties as before, smoothly relieved of temporary town administration supervisor role, henceforth under chamberlain supervision
- December 24th, same year: Granted seven to of rice stipend for products department assistant role
- August 18th, Tenpō 6 (1835): Inspector role suspended temporarily due to special circumstances, smoothly relieved of duties
- October 29th, Tenpō 8 (1837): Due to improper handling of products department office matters, relieved of products department assistant duties and ordered into disciplinary confinement
- November 9th, same year: Released from disciplinary confinement and assigned to secondary duty rotation

- May 22nd, Tenpō 14 (1843): Due to financial reforms, unavoidably relieved of hired service and granted half food allowance. However, may still be assigned service duties from time to time
- October 1st, Kōka 2 (1845): As his father Jinbei was granted retirement as requested, succeeded as household head with stipend of 18 hyō and two persons' food allowance, appointed to ashigaru attendant service under ashigaru head supervision
- April 15th, Kōka 3 (1846): Appointed as ashigaru inspector
- December 1st, Kōka 4 (1847): Smoothly relieved of ashigaru inspector duties and assigned to secondary duty rotation, rank to remain as before

Kaneko Yahei

During the reign of Lord Genryuin
- On October 21st, Meiwa 2 (1765), promoted to the rank of foot soldier (ashigaru), granted a stipend of 18 hyō and provisions for 2 people, duties to continue as before, under the supervision of the Construction Department
- On March 25th, Meiwa 8 (1771), as requested, relieved of assistant duties, placed under the supervision of the chamberlain
- On February 23rd, Meiwa 9 (1772), retired as requested
Service record

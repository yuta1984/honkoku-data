Akabane Chūzaemon
Changed name to: Kōsaku
Also: Senzaemon

During the reign of Lord Genryūin
1. On the 1st day of the 12th month of Kanpō 1 (1741), promoted to ashigaru rank, granted a stipend of 16 hyō and provisions for 2 people, served as rural district clerk
1. On the 20th day of the 5th month of Enkyō 3 (1746), increase of 2 hyō
1. In the 4th year (1747), granted goshi-ban rank with an increase of 2 hyō, appointed to mountain district duties while concurrently serving as clerk
1. On the 19th day of the 12th month of Hōreki 5 (1755), appointed to tax collection duties
1. On the 22nd day of the 12th month of the 8th year (1758), by special consideration relieved of duties and assigned to reserve duty
1. On the 7th day of the 11th month of Meiwa 2 (1765), appointed as ashigaru inspector under the supervision of senior retainers

During the reign of Lord Honryūin
1. On the 12th day of the 8th month of An'ei 9 (1780), retired as requested

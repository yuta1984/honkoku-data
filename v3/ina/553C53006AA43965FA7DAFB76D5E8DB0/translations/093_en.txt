1. Bunka 7, year of the Horse (1810), 2nd month, 15th day: Died of illness (at age 87)
　　　　　End of service

　　　　　　　　　　　　　Kojima Danshichi
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　 (later Iemon)
During the reign of Lord Honryū-in
1. An'ei 7, year of the Earth Dog (1778), 3rd month, 15th day: Appointed to bōzu service, granted stipend of 1 nin-fuchi
1. Kansei 1, year of the Rooster (1789), 6th month, 20th day: Appointed to go-tomo-kata assistant duty, granted stipend of 3 nin-fuchi
During the reign of Lord Raiin
1. Same 10th year, year of the Horse (1798), 9th month, 28th day: Appointed to go-tomo-kata go-kachi, granted allocation of 16 hyō and 2 nin-fuchi
1. Kyōwa 1, year of the Rooster (1801), 5th month, 15th day: Appointed to concurrent positions of yama-kata and takidashi-kata under go-yōnin supervision
1. Bunka 2, year of the Ox (1805), 12th month, 23rd day: As requested by father Bunita, permitted to retire, granted family headship with stipend of 18 hyō and 2 nin-fuchi, continuing service
1. Same 4th year, year of the Dragon (1807), 2nd month, 28th day: Promoted to formal go-tomban rank with increase of 2 hyō

1. Same year, 9th month, 15th day: Appointed to Lord Shōun-in's palace duty
1. Same 13th year, year of the Rat (1816), 6th month, 23rd day: Due to Lord Ryōnoshin taking up temporary residence at the lower mansion, appointed to temporary go-makanai-kata duty at the same location
During the reign of Lord Yoriyasu
1. Bunsei 13, year of the Tiger (1830), 4th month, 10th day: Appointed to guard the south gate of the lower mansion, rank and stipend remain as before
1. Tenpō 2, year of the Rabbit (1831), 6th month, 1st day: Died of illness
　　　　　End of service

　　　　　　　　　　　　　Kojima Iemon
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　 (originally Danshichi)
1. Tenpō 2, year of the Metal Rabbit (1831), 7th month, 23rd day: Succeeded deceased father Iemon, granted stipend of 18 hyō and 2 nin-fuchi, appointed to formal go-kachi go-tomo-kata under go-kachi-gashira supervision
1. Same 7th year, year of the Fire Monkey (1836), 7th month, 20th day: Appointed to guard the south gate of the lower mansion under go-yōnin supervision
1. Same 10th year, year of the Earth Boar (1839), 8th month, 18th day: Due to certain confidential matters, as south gate guard

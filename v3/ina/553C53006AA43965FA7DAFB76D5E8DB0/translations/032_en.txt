《Marginal note: His father Kojūrō was an arrowhead smith who was employed from Matsumoto and served in the castle deputy group. Kinjirō also held the same position and became part of the castle deputy group during his turn of duty》
Shimada Jūjirō
《Marginal note: Changed name to Kojūrō》

During the reign of Lord Genryūin
1. Anei 2, year of Mizunoto-Mi (1773), 1st month, 26th day: Promoted to ashigaru rank, granted a stipend of 18 hyō and provisions for 2 people, appointed as secretary to the chief retainer
1. Same 4th year, Kinoto-Hitsuji (1775), 8th month, 25th day: Secretary position reduced, so temporarily served as deputy guard, ordered to also serve as secretary when there were many documents

During the reign of Lord Honryūin  
1. Same 8th year, Tsuchinoto-I (1779), 3rd month, 15th day: Appointed as secretary to the chief elder
1. Tenmei 7, Hinoto-Hitsuji (1787), 9th month, 21st day: Served concurrent duty as both secretaries
1. Same 8th year, Tsuchinoe-Saru (1788), 8th month, 11th day: Appointed as apprentice scribe and assigned to garrison duty

"(In red ink) Shimada"

From Lower Inspector
Kitajima Kyūzō
(Later Shin-Gorōemon)

Reign of Lord Gen'ryūin
- Meika 8, year of the Metal Rabbit (1771), 5th month, 5th day: Promoted to ashigaru rank, granted 18 hyō two-person stipend, appointed to kitchen management
- An'ei 2, year of the Water Snake (1773), 9th month, 29th day: Appointed to temporary position of catering supervisor during garrison duty
- Same 4th year, year of the Wood Sheep (1775), 12th month, 13th day: Promoted to attendant rank with four hyō salary increase, continuing dual responsibility for catering and kitchen management, relocated (to Edo)

Reign of Lord Hon'ryūin
- Same 7th year, year of the Earth Dog (1778), 8th month, 8th day: Took charge of inspection duties for kitchen accounting matters, appointed to rest quarters duties, continuing dual responsibility for catering and kitchen management
- Same 9th year, year of the Metal Rat (1780), 7th month, 28th day: Due to the recent passing of the retired lord, kitchen catering inspection duties
(Red ink) Kitajima

Lower official of the domain storehouse
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Son of Katsuemon
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Kaneko Masashichi
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Records of succession: Kahei, Katsuemon)

During the reign of Lord Genryuin
- Kanpō 1, year of the Metal Rooster (1741), 9th month, 26th day: Buddhist monk service, 4 koku 2 to,
  granted one person's stipend
- Enkyō 2, year of the Wood Ox (1745), intercalary 12th month, 26th day: increase of one person's stipend
- Same year 3, year of the Fire Tiger (1746), 3rd month, 29th day: appointed to garrison duty,
  granted standard stipend of 5 koku
- Hōreki 10, year of the Metal Dragon (1760), 5th month, 23rd day: appointed as substitute for
  father Katsuemon, duties to continue as before
- Meiwa 9, year of the Water Dragon (1772), 6th month, 9th day: granted tea ceremony rank equivalent to ashigaru,
  stipend of 17 hyō, and 300 hiki of gold for ceremonial robes

During the reign of Lord Honryuin
- An'ei 6, year of the Fire Rooster (1777), 1st month, 24th day: exempted from
  Buddhist monk duties, returned to lay status, appointed to guard rotation, granted ashigaru rank,
  and returned to domain residence

"(In red ink) Kaneko"

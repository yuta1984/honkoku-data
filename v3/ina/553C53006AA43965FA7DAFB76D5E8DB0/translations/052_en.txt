Adopted son
Actually the younger brother of Takada Rokuzaemon: Yoshihara Yuya

During the reign of Lord Yoriyasu

- Tenpō 13th year, Tora (1842), 4th month, 8th day: Due to his adoptive father Buhēji's old age and illness, granted retirement as requested, inherited family headship with stipend of 18 hyō and two-person allowance, appointed to foot soldier attendance corps rank

- Kōka 2nd year, Tori (1845), 11th month, 23rd day: Appointed as secretary to the senior councillors and administrators, under administrator supervision

- Kaei 2nd year, Tori (1849), 7th month, 20th day: Appointed concurrently to chief storehouse clothing department

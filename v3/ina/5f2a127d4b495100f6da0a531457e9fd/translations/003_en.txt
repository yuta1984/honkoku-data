From the 5th day of the 11th month, Takato domain-limited
paper currency (chouhei) was produced by the 15th day and
submission completed.
The money notes (zensatsu) lent out during the 7th month of this year
have an October deadline, so from today the 15th,
they should be submitted successively.
However, for amounts where the borrowed sum
is not available in full, submission with
gold notes (kinsatsu) is acceptable.

Item: Now that the aforementioned money notes are ready,
those who wish to exchange them
should apply to the town elder (machi-doshiyori) for exchange.
The above matter should be announced throughout the town...

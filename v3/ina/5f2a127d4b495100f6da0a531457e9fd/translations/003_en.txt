Paper currency (chohei) limited to Takato domain was produced from November 5th and submitted by the 15th, with submission completed.

Regarding the money bills (zensatsu) lent out during July of this year, which expire in October, they shall be progressively submitted starting from the 15th of this month. However, for amounts without outstanding loan balances, submission in gold bills (kinsatsu) is acceptable.

Item: Now that the aforementioned money bills have been produced, those who wish to exchange them should apply to the town elders (machi-doshiyori) for exchange. This matter shall be announced throughout the town

Respectfully submitting this petition in writing
Regarding the pawn shop license possessed by Riheiji of Momeniya in Nakamachi,
I would like to take custody of it for 10 years and conduct pawn shop business. Therefore, I submit this petition.

Regarding the above matter, if you would graciously grant this petition as requested, it would be most grateful. The above

Kaei 2, Year of the Rooster, 9th month　　Shimomachi
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Petitioner Tōbei
To the Magistrate's Office　　　　　　Town representative Chūzaemon

When this petition was submitted on the 25th at the government office through the intermediation of Mr. Nakayama Isoemon, [the response was]: "There is no precedent for conducting business through lending and borrowing. Decide between outright purchase ■ or conducting business under Riheiji's name, choose from these two options and submit your petition," and the petition was returned.

Humbly submitted petition

Regarding my son Yohei, in the second month past, I petitioned for him to serve as an apprentice at Kikyoya Genjiro's establishment in Kanda Mikawa-cho 4-chome, Edo, and received approval. However, afterward he was asked to serve as a village clerk (gōbu) in Nozasa Village, Fujisawa district. His wife was apparently informed of this, and he departed without consulting me. Therefore, I failed to submit the proper petition for the village clerk position, and I am deeply mortified by this oversight. I humbly request your mercy in withdrawing the apprentice petition and granting approval for the village clerk petition.

Regarding the above matter, I respectfully request your merciful consideration as petitioned. If you would graciously grant this request, I would be most grateful. The above.

Hokomochi-cho
Kanaya
4th month, 2nd year of Kaei, Year of the Rooster　Petitioner: Jinzaemon
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Town Representative: Jūzaburō
To the Magistrate's Office

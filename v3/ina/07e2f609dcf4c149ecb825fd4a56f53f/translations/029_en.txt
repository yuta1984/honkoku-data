Humbly submitting this oral statement as a petition
Regarding the succession of my relative Yamaya Sakuemon's family business, I had taken in Kichishichi, the second son of Sakai Seibei, as an adopted son-in-law to continue the family line, and he has been residing with us. However, due to recent discord, I wish to divorce him and send him back to his original family. Therefore, I humbly submit this petition.
If you would graciously grant this petition as requested, it would be most grateful. The above stated.

Kaei 2, Year of the Rooster, 9th month
Ikeno-ya, Petitioner: Tomouemon
Honmachi District Representative: Jinuemon
To the Magistrate's Office

These two petitions were submitted on the 10th day at the government office through the intermediation of Mr. Goto Shinuemon, and on the 26th of the same month, both petitions were approved through the intermediation of Mr. Nakayama Isoemon.

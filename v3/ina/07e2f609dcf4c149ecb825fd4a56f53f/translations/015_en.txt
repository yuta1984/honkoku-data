**Petition humbly submitted in writing**

Chūzō, a resident of Noguchi Village in Tera-gō [Tera district], under the jurisdiction of Lord Chimura Heizaemon, is my paternal uncle. As he has advanced in age and is experiencing financial hardship, I wish to take in both him and his wife into my household, add them to our religious parish register (*shūmon ninbetsu-chō*), and care for them with filial devotion. I hereby respectfully submit this petition.

If it should please you, in your great benevolence, to grant this petition as requested, I would be most deeply grateful. Respectfully submitted.

> Yokochō (Side Street district)
> Igeta-ya (shop name)
> Kaei 2nd year, 4th month (1849)
> Petitioner: Ihei
> District Representative (*chōdai*): Kichiemon

To the Honorable Magistrate's Office

**[Left folio]**

After endorsing the above with an official note (*okugaki*), it was submitted on the 7th day at the magistrate's office through the intermediary of Mr. Suzuki. On the following 8th day, Mr. Suzuki informed us that the petition had been approved, and this was duly conveyed to District Representative Kichiemon.

**Petition humbly submitted in writing**

Regarding my son Yohei: in the second month of this year, a petition was submitted requesting that he be sent to work as a sales apprentice (*uriko hōkō*) at Kikyō-ya Genjirō's establishment in Kanda-Mikawachō 4-chōme, Edo. However, as circumstances have arisen here at home that make this arrangement difficult, I wish to withdraw the said petition. I hereby respectfully submit this request.

If it should please you, in your great benevolence, to grant this petition as requested, I would be most deeply grateful. Respectfully submitted.

> Hokochicho (Halberd-carrier district)
> Kane-ya (shop name)
> Kaei 2nd year, 4th month (1849)
> Petitioner: Jinzaemon
> District Representative: Jūsaburō

To the Honorable Magistrate's Office

Regarding the above: on the 15th day, the petition was submitted at the magistrate's office through the intermediary of Mr. Gotō, and approval was granted immediately.

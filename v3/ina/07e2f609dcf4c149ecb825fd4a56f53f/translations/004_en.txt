【Right Page】
Humbly submitting a petition through this written statement:
Due to my financial difficulties, I am unable to make a living, so I would like to go to Shinano-ya Ikuzō's establishment in Mita 3-chōme, Edo, for a 10-year term of service starting from the third month of this year of the Rooster (Kaei 2). Therefore, I request that the religious census inspection be waived during this period.
Regarding the above matter, if you would graciously grant my petition as requested, I would be most grateful. The above.

Kaei 2, Year of the Rooster, 3rd month　　Shimozato-machi
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Enshū-ya
To the Magistrate's Office　　　　　　　Petitioner: Chūshichi
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Town Representative: Jinpachi

This petition was submitted on the 5th day through the intermediation of Lord Gotō Shinemon at the government office, and approval was granted on the 7th day and communicated to the town representative.

【Left Page】
Humbly submitting a petition through this written statement:
I would like to send my younger brother Kankyūji to the shop of Takeya Jūzaemon in Tsubakiyama-machi 3-chōme, Edo, for term service. Therefore, I request that the religious census inspection be waived for a period of 10 years.
Regarding the above matter, if you would graciously grant my petition as requested, I would be most grateful. The above.

Kaei 2, Year of the Rooster, 3rd month　　Hon-machi
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Ise-ya
To the Magistrate's Office　　　　　　　Petitioner: Rokubei
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Town Representative: Jinemon

This petition was submitted in the same manner on the 5th day at the government office, and approval was granted on the 7th day and communicated to the town representative.

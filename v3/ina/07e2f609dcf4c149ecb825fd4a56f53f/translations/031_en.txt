Humbly submitting a petition in writing

1. The pawnbroking license I possess has become inconvenient, making it impossible to conduct business. Therefore, I would like to entrust this license to Yoshidaya Tōbei in the lower town for a period of 10 years, and have him conduct business with it. I hereby petition for this arrangement.

Regarding the above matter, I humbly request your merciful consideration. If you would graciously grant my petition, it would be most grateful. Above stated.

Kaei 2, Year of the Earth Rooster　　　　Nakamachi
9th month　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Momenya (Cotton dealer)
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Riheiji
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Town representative
To the Magistrate's Office　　　　　　　Kanzō

The above petition was submitted on the 25th day through the intermediary of Mr. Nakayama Isoemon at the government office. However, since there is no precedent for lending arrangements, we were told to decide whether to conduct business through outright sale and purchase or under our own name,

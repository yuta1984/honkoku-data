Respectfully submitting this petition in writing

Item: The pawn shop license that I possess has become unprofitable, making it impossible to continue business. Therefore, I would like to entrust this license to Yoshidaya Tōbei in the lower town for a period of 10 years, and conduct business under his management. I hereby submit this petition.

Regarding the above matter, if you would graciously grant this petition as requested, it would be most grateful. The above

Kaei 2, Year of the Rooster　　　　Nakamachi
9th month　　　　　　　　　　　Momeniya (Cotton shop)
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Riheiji
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Town representative
To the Magistrate's Office　　　　　Kanzō

When this petition was submitted on the 25th at the government office through the intermediation of Mr. Nakayama Isoemon, [the response was]: "There is no precedent for lending or borrowing. In any case, decide whether it will be outright sale and purchase or business under your own name,

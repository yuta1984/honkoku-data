　　　Humbly submitting this oral statement as a petition
　A person named Hachiuemon from Yoshida Kajimachi in the castle town of Lord Matsudaira Izunokami came to my place and was conducting sickle business, but he has become ill and wishes to stop his business from this time forward. Therefore, we humbly submit this petition.

Regarding the above matter, if you would graciously grant our petition as requested with your benevolence, it would be most grateful. The above.

　　　Kaei 2, Year of the Rooster　　　　　Nakamachi

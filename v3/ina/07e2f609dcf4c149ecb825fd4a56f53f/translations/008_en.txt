【Right Page】
　　　　Respectfully submitting this petition in writing
　　Regarding my Shingo, who was sent as an adopted son to
　　Tomiya Genkichi's household in Hokomotomachi, but due to incompatibility,
　　we have consulted and agreed to divorce, and I wish to take him back
　　to my household. I humbly petition regarding this matter.
　Regarding the above matter, if you would graciously grant my petition
　as requested, it would be a most grateful outcome. Respectfully submitted. Shinmachi Hashiya
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Petitioner: Kohachi
　　　　　Kaei 2, Year of the Rooster, 3rd month　　Town Representative: Soshichi
　　　　To the Magistrate's Office
The above petition was submitted on the 7th day through the intermediary Shimizu Heisaku at the government office, and immediate approval was granted and conveyed to the town representative.

**A Humble Petition Submitted in Writing**

As we are traveling to Edo for a charity sumo tournament (*kanjin-zumo*), we humbly wish to hold a three-day outdoor performance at your honorable town upon completion of our engagement at Suwa. We therefore respectfully submit this petition.

We humbly pray that, in your great benevolence, you will grant our request as stated above, for which we would be most grateful. Respectfully submitted.

**A Humble Petition Submitted in Writing**

My husband Mosaku served as a water official (*omizuyaku*) and worked as a cooper (*okeya*). As he passed away this past sixth month, I humbly wish to surrender his water official's registration tag (*omizuyaku fuda*). I therefore respectfully submit this petition.

We humbly pray that, in your great benevolence, you will grant our request as stated above, for which we would be most grateful. Respectfully submitted.

16th day
One matter: Regarding the request to inspect the lime kiln site at Shibahirayama, Kinbei reported this matter. We shall send an inspector there in due course.

One matter: Takeya Goroshichi petitioned to borrow Tsuruya's subordinate shop. Since last year, Hyōemon from Shioda Village has had a petition to borrow it, so we instructed them to negotiate with that party.

One matter: The borrowers of the newly developed rice fields at Benzaiten are as follows:
　　　　　Shioya
　　　　　　Denbei  
　　　　　Tsuruya
　　　　　　Yonekura
The above two persons have been granted loans.

One matter: Timber and tools for the upper building at Benzaiten:
　One chestnut pillar
　One hemlock pillar  
　One set of rafters - twenty-two pieces
We wish to receive the above items from the construction office and request your decision on this matter.

26th day
- Lord Ogasawara Sagami-no-kami's younger sister was ill, but despite medical treatment, she passed away yesterday on the 25th. This refers to the wife of Lord Ogasawara Masanosuke.

27th day
- Lord Ikeda Yamashiro-no-kami's grandmother was ill at her domain residence and passed away on the 10th of this month.

28th day
　　　　Masterless samurai (rōnin)
　　　　　Ogino Rokubei
　　　　　Ogino Daijirō
These men are gun handlers who were granted an audience with Lord Endō Bizen-no-kami, and the same lord reported about them.

- Soon there will be an imperial viewing of sumo wrestling at Fukiage, and preparations are currently being made for this occasion.

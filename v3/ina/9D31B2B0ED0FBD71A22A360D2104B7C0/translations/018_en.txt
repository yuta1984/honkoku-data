One Hired Worker (yatoi tsutome): (No details provided)
One Child Attendant (kodomo tsutome): Those who serve as companions and personal attendants to the lord's children
One Inner Quarter Monk (oku bōzu): Handles affairs for the lord's wife and ladies-in-waiting in the inner quarters
One Scribe (monokaki): Including head scribes, assistant scribes, and rural scribes - they make clean copies of all important documents
One Tailor (shitatemono kata): Makes clothing for the lord, his wife, and other family members
One Messenger (tsukai kata): Those who serve as official messengers to various places
One Assistant Scribe (monokaki nami): Hold ceremonial rank but are not writing officials; they perform various minor duties
One District Magistrate Group Leader (gun-dai gumi kogashira): Head of associations who supervises the control of their respective groups
One Minor Official (koyakunin): Hold upper positions among foot soldiers, serving as petty officers and inspectors; some are exempt from stipend payments

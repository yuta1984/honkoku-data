One Advance Guard Group Leader (sente gumi kogashira): Head of one advance guard unit who assigns daily duties within the group, external missions, messengers to Edo, gate guard duties, emergency patrols, and other tasks. This person is called "wari-moto" (assignment coordinator).

One Advance Guard Group Inspector (sente gumi metsuke): Assists the group leader and supervises the members of the unit.

One Advance Guard Archery Unit (sente yumi gumi): Performs daily duties according to assignments from the group leader and inspectors, and receives commands from the unit commander (monogashira).

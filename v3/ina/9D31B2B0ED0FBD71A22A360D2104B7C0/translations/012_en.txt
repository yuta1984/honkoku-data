One Chief Financial Officer: Manages the castle lord's finances, converts rice tributes into payment money and sends it to Edo, and also distributes rice payment money to the domain in two installments in July and December. This is called "mononari" (tribute in kind).
　　　　　However, the rice payment exchange rate is determined by averaging the Edo rate, Takato rate, and Kiso rate.

One Head of Foot Soldiers: Serves at the left and right sides when the castle lord goes out, and commands the entire retinue. Therefore, also called "tomo-gashira" (retinue head). In battle, joins the horse guards to protect the lord.

One Accounting Magistrate: Oversees the inspection of accounts from various offices.

One Storehouse Magistrate: Manages the receipt and disbursement of rice tributes from within the domain, and also establishes ledgers to distribute food rice to the domain, making the remaining rice into purchased rice.

One Money Magistrate: Oversees the collection and safekeeping of money.

One Arms Magistrate: Manages the organization, storage, and disbursement of weapons.

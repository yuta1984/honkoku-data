Twenty mon approximately.
One Armaments Officer (bugu kata): Handles the organization and preservation of weapons and military equipment under the direction of the armaments magistrate.
One Secretary (yuhitsu): Records important documents, catalogs, official seals and edicts, territorial boundary markers, etc.
One Revenue Collection Officer (bunichi kata): Supervises the collection of commercial taxes and fees.
One Kitchen Officer (ryori kata): Prepares meals for the lord.

One Accounting Officer (kanjō kata): Investigates the accounts of various offices under the direction of the accounting magistrate.
One Servant Supervisor (chūgen gashira): Directs the duties of servants and attendants.
One Assistant Inspector (shimo metsuke): A subordinate of the chief inspector. Routinely investigates the attendance and moral conduct throughout the domain and secretly reports to the chief inspector. Therefore, the children of the domain fear the assistant inspector most, and when it is said that the assistant inspector is coming, even small children cease their mischief.

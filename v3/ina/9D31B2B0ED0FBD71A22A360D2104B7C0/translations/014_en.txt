One Physician
　　Court physicians, chief physicians and regular physicians all normally stand duty in the castle, specializing in medical treatment and hygiene matters, and must accompany the lord when he goes out.
　　Town physicians: Establish practices in town houses and provide medical care to the people.
　　Rural physicians: Establish practices in villages and provide medical care to the people.
　　Western physicians: Began practicing before the Meiji Restoration, gradually leading to the development of medical arts.
　　　In Takato, the Western physicians were Suda Tairei and Aoshima, residing in Nishi-Ina district villages

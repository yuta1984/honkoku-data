Two-person stipend given to the eldest son of those ranked down to stipended retainer (kyūnin).
One Kyūnin Rank: Same duties as working stipended retainers.
One Kyūnin Associate: Those who hold stipended retainer status and perform external duties.
One Ashless Attendant (musoku kinshū): Like side attendants, they handle the lord's affairs.
One Ashless Physician: Equivalent to court physicians.

One Page Inspector (chūkoshō metsuke): Those from foot soldiers up to pages are called "ashless." The page inspector evaluates the diligence and conduct of the ashless retainers, secretly reports this to the administrators, rewards those who should be rewarded, and punishes those who should be punished. They also handle all other intermediary duties for petitions and notifications.
One Page Inspector Rank: Does not participate in inspector duties, holding only honorary rank.
One Page: Like stipended mounted attendants, when there are affairs they guard the lord's immediate vicinity, and some regularly perform various duties.

"1. On the 13th day of the 10th month of Keio 1, year of the Wood Ox (1865), Uoya Genzaemon died of illness, and on the 28th day of the 3rd month of the same 2nd year, year of the Fire Tiger (1866), his adopted son [?]ta was granted succession with a stipend of 18 hyō and provisions for two people, with the rank of御徒士 (o-kachi, foot soldier), but due to interruption, it was decreed by Lord Seta Akakura that payments should be made according to the precedent established since Kansei 5, year of the Water Ox (1793)
However
　For mono-nari, large stipends, hyō amounts, and those without rank, the parent's stipend shall be paid on a daily basis until the day of death from illness, and from the day after death until the day before the adopted son takes over, due to the interruption, stipends shall be granted according to the number of people in the household both high and low, and from the day the adopted son takes over and receives investiture, the successor shall be granted the stipend amount announced for succession on a daily basis
　　However, for additional stipends, they shall be granted from the month the adopted son takes over"

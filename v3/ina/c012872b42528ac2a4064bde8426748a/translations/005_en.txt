【Left Page】

For those of ashigaru rank and below (無足以下), if summoned by the end of the sixth month, they shall receive their full stipend for that year. Those summoned from the first day of the seventh month onward shall receive half payment for that year. This applies regardless of whether there are government loans or not.
However, regarding attached stipends (附扶持), for those summoned from other domains, payment shall be calculated from the day of meeting with the senior councilors and elders. For those summoned from within the domain, payment shall be calculated from the day of notification.

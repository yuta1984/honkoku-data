Regarding the Written Pledge
In accordance with the proclamation recently issued,
the titles of village headman (shōya), village chief (nanushi), 
village elder (toshiyori), head village magistrate (ōshōya), etc. 
shall all be abolished and newly renamed as 
village headman (kochō) and deputy village headman (fuku-kochō).
Accordingly, all administrative duties that have previously been 
handled at the village office by village headmen, deputy village headmen,
village chiefs, group leaders, village elders, and peasant representatives,
as well as all matters relating to land and
people, shall be handled [by the new officials]

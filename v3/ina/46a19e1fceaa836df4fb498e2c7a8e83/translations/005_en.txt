23rd day
One: When village group head Gunji of Hokomochi village went to see the monthly duty officer,
　since the office was unattended, the same person went to Mr. Miyaji's place and
　spoke about the wind festival matter and expressed his thoughts. Mr. Miyaji
　said that since this was a matter that the town magistrates had consulted about,
　he should consult with the magistrate town. However, it was said that
　he had not visited the town magistrates.
24th day
One: After both magistrates inspected Benzaiten Bridge,
　village headman Kiemon and group head Gunji stopped by the inn
　and brought up the matter during a drinking session.
　The monthly duty officer said they should consult with the magistrate town.

Meiji 2nd Year, Year of the Earth Snake (1869)
October
5th Day
Monthly duty officer Ikegami Shotaro went to
the Civil Administration Bureau, where through the intermediation of Mr. Ikeda Kyuzo,
it was announced that for this occasion, the three men—
Kitahara Risaemon, Ikegami Kinzaemon, and Ikegami Shotaro—
have been appointed to the Shinano Province-wide Paper Currency Exchange Office.
It was conveyed that instructions regarding
operational procedures would be issued in due course.

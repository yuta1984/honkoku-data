10th Day
1. When the three men went to the Accounting Bureau, they were given the following items:
　　Guidelines for Exchange Offices
　In this domain in general, there are many large denomination bills but small bills and
　copper coins have run short, causing difficulties in transactions
　and hardship as has been reported. Therefore, this time
　after consultation with the domain government of this province, temporarily
　for the sake of facilitating transactions, paper money for use throughout Shinshū
　has been manufactured and will be issued from the 1st of October.
　Accordingly, you are appointed to operate exchange offices. In this regard, the proper bills
　to be exchanged for the paper money have been provided
　by this bureau, so whenever people come for exchange,
　exchange them without hindrance. However,
　at the rate of one mon per hundred mon,
　an exchange fee should be collected, as has been
　announced to the villages. When making exchanges,
　this fee should be received.

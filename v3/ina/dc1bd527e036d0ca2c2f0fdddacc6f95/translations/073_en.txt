【Right Page】
Respectfully submitting this oral petition
Regarding Kiyo, who is under the care of Sakunosuke from Mikkaichō Village in Minowa Domain under the jurisdiction of Lord Matsudaira Tamba-no-kami, as I have an informal relationship with her, I have privately discussed taking her under my care. Therefore, I humbly submit this petition.
Regarding the above matter, with your benevolence, if you would kindly grant my petition as requested,
I would consider it a most grateful blessing. The above.
　Kōka 2, Year of the Wood Snake, 2nd month　　Sakai Seibei
　　To the Magistrate's Office
Regarding the above, the monthly duty officer wrote the endorsement, and it was submitted on the 17th day through Mr. Shimizu's intermediation at the government office. On the 25th day, approval was granted and notification was given to the person concerned.

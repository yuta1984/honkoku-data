【Right Page】
　　Humbly submitting this oral petition
Regarding Kiyo, a dependent of Sakunosuke from Mikka-machi village in Minowa domain under the custody of Lord Matsudaira Tamba-no-kami,
since there is an informal engagement with her,
I have discussed taking her in as my dependent. Therefore, I humbly submit this petition.
Regarding the above matter, if you would mercifully grant
my petition as requested, I would be most grateful. The above.
　　Kōka 2nd year, 2nd month　　　　Sakai Seibei
　　　To the Magistrate's Office
Regarding the above matter, with only the monthly duty officer's endorsement, this was submitted on the 17th through Mr. Shimizu's intermediation at the government office, and approval was granted on the 25th, which I conveyed to the person concerned.

Respectfully submitting this oral petition
Regarding my daughter Tsuru, since she has a common-law relationship, I would like to send her to be taken care of by Fusazo in Kashio Village, which is under the jurisdiction of Chimimura Heiemon-sama. I have discussed this matter internally and therefore respectfully submit this petition.
Regarding the above matter, if you would graciously grant this petition as requested,
it would be most grateful and fortunate. The above.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Shimosato-cho Tsuboya
　Tenpō 15, Year of the Wood Dragon, 2nd month　　　Petitioner: Jinzo
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Town Representative: Takeshichi
　　To the Magistrate's Office
The above petition was submitted through Shimizu-shi's intermediation on the 27th day, whereupon Goto-shi immediately provided official approval, and this was conveyed to the town representative on the 28th day.

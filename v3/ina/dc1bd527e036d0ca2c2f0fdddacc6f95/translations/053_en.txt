【Right Page】
　　Humbly submitting this oral petition
I have been operating a dyer's business with five indigo vats until now, but this time I would like to
increase by three vats, making a total of eight vats for my work. Therefore, I submit this petition.
Regarding the above matter, if you would mercifully grant
what I have petitioned for,
it would be a most grateful blessing. Above stated.　　　　Shimizu-machi
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Karatsu-ya [possibly Kado-ya]
　　Tempo 15, Year of the Wood Dragon, 11th month　　　Petitioner　Mon　ji
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Town representative　Gihei
Upon examination, there is no discrepancy in the number of vats. Above stated.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Head of dyers' guild
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Jinpachi
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Same
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Ichizaemon
　　To the Magistrate's Office　　　　Same
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Miyashita Heibei
The above petition was circulated [as usual], endorsed with family names, and submitted on the 15th through Mr. Kitano's intermediation.
On the 20th, approval was announced by Mr. Kitano, and this was conveyed to the town representative.

【Left Page】
　　　Humbly submitting this oral petition
As I wish to engage in bowl-making business, regarding goods shipped from Komagatake (?),
I will receive inspection of pack-horse loads from the Local Products Office and, following standard practice,
pay profit taxes to conduct business. Therefore, I submit this petition.
Regarding the above matter, if you would mercifully grant
what I have petitioned for,
it would be a most grateful blessing. Above stated.
　　Tempo 15, Year of the Wood Dragon, 11th month　　　Naka-machi Matsuo-ya
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Shinemon
　　　To the Magistrate's Office　　　　Town representative　　Sōbei
The above petition was submitted on the 20th through Mr. Kitano's intermediation, and approval was granted
on the 15th of the first month of the following year, which was conveyed to town representative Sōbei.

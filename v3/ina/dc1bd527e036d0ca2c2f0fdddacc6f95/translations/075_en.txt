　　Humbly submitting this oral petition
Since I have no male heir, I have had internal discussions about having Tsuneji,
son of Kakuzou from Hara-katsuma village, who also has an intimate relationship
with my daughter Mise, marry into the family to continue the family name.
Therefore, I humbly submit this petition.
Regarding the above matter, if you would mercifully grant my petition as requested,
I would be most grateful. The above.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Hokoji-machi Kin-ya
　　Kōka 2nd year, 2nd month　　　　Petitioner: Jinzaemon
　　　To the Magistrate's Office　　　　Town Representative: Choubei
The above petition was submitted on the 25th through Mr. Takahashi's intermediation and was immediately approved, which was conveyed to the town representative.

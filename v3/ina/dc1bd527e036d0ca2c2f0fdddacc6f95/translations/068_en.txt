【Right Page】
Respectfully, I humbly submit this oral petition regarding the following matter:
I wish to take in my son Sōhachi as an apprentice (uchi-deshi) for a term of five years starting this year to have him learn the blacksmith trade. Therefore, I humbly submit this petition.
Regarding the above matter, if you would kindly grant permission as I have humbly requested through your benevolence, it would be a most grateful blessing. The above.

Hokomochi-machi
Kōka 2nd year, Kinoto-mi, 2nd month　　　Ginkgo-ya
Petitioner: Jūzaburō
To the Magistrate's Office　　　　　Town Representative: Chōbei

The above petition was submitted on the 7th day through the intermediary of Mr. Kitano.

Kōka 2, Year of the Wood Snake　　　Shimosato-machi
　　First month　　　　　　　　　Petitioner: Takeshichi
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Assistant: Jinpei
　To the Magistrate's Office

The above petition was submitted on the 17th day at the government office through the intermediation of Mr. Gotō,
and it was immediately approved, and Takeshichi was called and informed.

Humbly submitting this oral statement as a petition:
I am an inadequate person, but unexpectedly I was
appointed to the position of town representative,
which I consider a grateful fortune, and I have been serving in this capacity. However,
in recent years I have become frequently ill, and I believe it is difficult to continue serving in this role.
Therefore, I humbly petition that I be pardoned from the position of town representative

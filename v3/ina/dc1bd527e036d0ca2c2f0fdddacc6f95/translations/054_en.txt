　　Humbly submitting this oral petition
I have been operating a dyer's business with fifteen indigo vats until now, but this time I would like to
increase by six vats, making a total of twenty-one vats for my dyeing work. Therefore,
I submit this petition.
Regarding the above matter, if you would mercifully grant
what I have petitioned for,
it would be a most grateful blessing. Above stated.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Hon-machi
　　Tempo 15, Year of the Wood Dragon, 11th month　　　Kii-koku-ya
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Petitioner　　Chuemon
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Town representative　　Yozaemon
　There is no discrepancy in the number of vats. Above stated.　　Head of dyers' guild

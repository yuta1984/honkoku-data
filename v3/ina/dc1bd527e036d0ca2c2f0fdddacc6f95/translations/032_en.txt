Respectfully submitting this oral petition
Since I have no heir, at this time I have discussed adopting Uhei, the younger brother of Yashichi from Hokumochi Village, who also has a common-law relationship, as my heir to continue the family name, and therefore respectfully submit this petition.
Regarding the above matter, if you would graciously grant this petition as requested,
it would be most grateful and fortunate. The above.
　　　　　　　　　　　　Nakamachi Yamagataya
　Tenpō 15, Year of the Wood Dragon, 2nd month　　　　Tarobei
　　　　　　　　　　　　　Petitioner: Widow
　　To the Magistrate's Office　　　　　　Town Representative: Munebei
The above petition was submitted through Shimizu-shi's intermediation on the 27th day, whereupon it was immediately approved, and the official decision was communicated from Goto-shi and conveyed to the town representative on the 28th day.

【Right page】
Humbly submitting this oral petition

I wish to engage in cast net fishing as a side business alongside my regular trade.
Therefore, I humbly submit this petition.

Regarding the above matter, if you would graciously grant our petition through your benevolence and give your approval, we would consider it a most grateful blessing. The above.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Shimomachi
Kōka 2nd year, 7th month　　　　　Petitioner: Sōbei  
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Town representative: Tomoemon
To the Magistrate's Office

The above petition was submitted on the 17th through Mr. Shimizu's good offices. On the 22nd, official notice was given that for the license distribution on the coming 25th, one should report to the residence of Mr. Yamashita Tsunegorō of the taxation office, so this was conveyed to the town representative.

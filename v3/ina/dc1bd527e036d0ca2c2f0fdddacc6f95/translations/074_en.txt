【Right Page】
Respectfully submitting this oral petition
Regarding Mintaro, the son of Katsushichi of Kiikokuya in Seri-cho, who is currently 15 years old,
I wish to have him learn carpentry, so I would like to take him as an apprentice
under a seven-year contract. Therefore, I humbly submit this petition.
Regarding the above matter, if you would mercifully grant this petition as requested
and give your approval, it would be a most grateful blessing. The above
　　　　　　　　　　　　Shimo-machi Shimadaya
　Kōka 2, Year of the Snake, 2nd month　　Petitioner: Okiremon
　　To the Magistrate's Office　　　　　　Town Representative: Tōemon
The above petition was submitted on the 25th through Mr. Takahashi's mediation, and on the last day of the 4th month, there was an official notice to appear at the Public Works Office with a seal, which was conveyed to the town representative.

【Left Page】
Respectfully submitting this oral petition
As my business has become unsuccessful, I wish to go to serve
at Hashimotoya Hanzaburō's establishment in Koami-chō 2-chōme, Edo, under a five-year contract.
Therefore, I humbly request that during this period, I be exempted from religious certification.
Regarding the above matter, if you would mercifully grant this petition as requested
and give your approval, it would be a most grateful blessing. The above
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Hokomochi-chō Tomiya
　Kōka 2, Year of the Snake, 2nd month　　Petitioner: Genshichi
　　To the Magistrate's Office　　　　　　Town Representative: Chōbei
The above petition was submitted on the 25th through Mr. Takahashi's mediation and was immediately approved and conveyed to the town representative.

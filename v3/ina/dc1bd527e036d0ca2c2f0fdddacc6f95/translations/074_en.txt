【Right Page】
　　Humbly submitting this oral petition
Regarding Minjirou, son of Katsushichi of Kii-no-kuni-ya in Seri-machi, who is currently fifteen years old,
I wish to have him learn carpentry, so I would like to set a term of 《crossed out: eight｜》seven years
and take him as an apprentice under me. Therefore, I humbly submit this petition.
Regarding the above matter, if you would mercifully grant
my petition as requested, I would be most grateful. The above.
　　　　　　　　　　　　Shimo-machi Shimada-ya
　　Kōka 2nd year, 2nd month　　　　Petitioner: Okiuemon
　　To the Magistrate's Office　　　　Town Representative: Touemon
The above petition was submitted on the 25th through Mr. Takahashi's intermediation, and instructions were given to bring the seal on the last day of the 4th month and appear at the Construction Office, which was conveyed to the town representative.

【Left Page】
　　　Humbly submitting this oral petition
Since my business has become unsuccessful, I wish to go work
as an indentured servant for five years at Hashimoto-ya Hanzaburou's establishment in Koami-machi 2-chome, Edo.
Therefore, I petition that during the said term, I be exempted from religious inspection registration.

Regarding the above matter, if you would mercifully grant my petition as requested,
I would be most grateful. The above.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Hokoji-machi Tomi-ya
　Kōka 2nd year, 2nd month　　　　　　Petitioner: Genshichi
　　　To the Magistrate's Office　　　　　　Town Representative: Choubei
The above petition was submitted on the 25th through Mr. Takahashi's intermediation and was immediately approved, which was conveyed to the town representative.

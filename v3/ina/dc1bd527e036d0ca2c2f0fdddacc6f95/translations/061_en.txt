【Right page】
I was appointed to an official position, which I considered a grateful fortune, and
I have been serving in this capacity. However, I have become ill and
believe it is difficult to continue serving in this role. Therefore, I petition for pardoning from this official position.
Regarding the above matter, if you would graciously grant my petition as submitted,
it would be most grateful and fortunate.
The above.
　Kōka 2, Year of the Wood Snake　　Shin-machi
　　First month　　　　　　　　　Petitioner: Sōshichi
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Former official: Ichiji

【Left page】
　To the Magistrate's Office
The above petition was submitted on the 17th day at the government office through the intermediation of Mr. Gotō, and it was immediately
approved, and Sōshichi was called and informed.

Humbly submitting this oral statement as a petition:
I am an inadequate person, but I was
appointed to the position of head of dyers,
and have been serving gratefully. However, due to the term limit,
I petition that I be temporarily pardoned.
Regarding the above matter, if you would graciously grant my petition as submitted,
it would be most grateful and fortunate. The above.
　Kōka 2, Year of the Wood Snake, First month

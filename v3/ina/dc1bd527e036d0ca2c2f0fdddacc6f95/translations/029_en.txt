Humbly submitting this oral petition
Regarding one called Shigeju from Kinoshita village in Minowa domain, which is under the custody of Lord Matsudaira Tamba-no-kami
(Marginal note: Since he is a reliable person, he returned to the village last winter. Therefore)
I have guaranteed him and he has been residing here working as a carpenter, but I request that this guarantee arrangement be pardoned.
Regarding the above matter, if you would graciously grant my petition as requested,
I would consider it a most grateful fortune. The above.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　Sericho, Fujinoya
Tenpō 15, Year of the Wood Dragon, 2nd month　Petitioner: Yojizaemon
　　To the Magistrate's Office　　　　　　　Town Representative: Koemon
The above petition was submitted through Mr. Shimizu's intermediation on the 22nd day

Humbly submitting this oral petition
Regarding my relative Tomiya Tetsuji, I wish to send him to Kujiraya Izaemon's establishment in Nakamachi of Matsumoto castle town for a five-year period starting this year as an apprentice merchant serving as a shop assistant.
Therefore, I request that he be exempted from sectarian certification during this period.
Regarding the above matter, if you would graciously grant my petition as requested,
I would consider it a most grateful fortune. The above. Sericho
Tenpō 15, Year of the Wood Dragon, 2nd month　　　　Fujinoya
　　　　　　　　　　　　　Petitioner: Genemon
　　To the Magistrate's Office　　　　　　Town Representative: Koemon
The above petition was submitted through Mr. Shimizu's intermediation on the 20th day, and we were informed by letter from Mr. Goto that it was approved on the same day. This was immediately conveyed to the town representative.

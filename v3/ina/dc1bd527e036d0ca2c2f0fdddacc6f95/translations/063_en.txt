【Right page】
Humbly submitting this oral statement as a petition:
Regarding my daughter Shifu, since there is also an engagement arrangement, this summer I wish to send her to become the wife of Yasuyoshi, the younger brother of Kakisaburō of Yoshiokaya in Iida Tenma-machi first district in the castle town of Lord Hori Yamato-no-kami. I have conducted preliminary discussions regarding this matter. Therefore, I submit this petition.
Regarding the above matter, if you would graciously grant my petition as requested and give your permission, it would be a most grateful circumstance. The above.
　　Kōka 2, Year of the Wood Snake　　Naka-machi Ōmiya
　　　　　　Second month　　　　　　Petitioner: Heiemon
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Town representative: Sōbei
　　　To the Magistrate's Office
The above petition was submitted through the intermediation of Mr. Kitano, whereupon it was immediately approved, and notification was given by Mr. Takahashi and conveyed to the town representative.

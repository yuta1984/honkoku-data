【Right page】
　Humbly submitting this oral statement as a petition:
My daughter Shige has an arranged marriage, and this summer to Lord Hori Yamato-no-kami's
castle town Iida, Tenma-machi 1st district, to become the wife of Yasuyakichi, younger brother of Kakisaburo of Yoshiokaya. We have conducted private negotiations for this matter. Therefore, I petition you.
Regarding the above matter, if you would graciously grant my petition as requested,
it would be a most grateful blessing. The above.
　　Kōka 2, Year of the Wood Snake　　Nakamachi, Ōmiya
　　　　　　2nd month　　　Petitioner: Heiemon
　　　　　　　　　　　Town representative: Sōbei
　　　To the Magistrate's Office
The above petition was submitted through Mr. Kitano's intermediation, whereupon it was immediately approved, and notification was given by Mr. Takahashi and communicated to the town representative.

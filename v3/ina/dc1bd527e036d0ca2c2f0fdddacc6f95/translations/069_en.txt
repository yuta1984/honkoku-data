【Right Page】
Respectfully, I humbly submit this oral petition regarding the following matter:
The person called Yukitarō, son of Denshirō from Kashio village under the jurisdiction of Lord Chimura Heizaemon, is a reliable person, so in the 12th month of the previous Year of the Rooster, I submitted a petition and have had him as my apprentice learning carpentry. However, during the previous Year of the Dragon, he returned to his home village, so I wish to grant leave to my apprentice Yukitarō. Therefore, I humbly submit this petition.
Regarding the above matter, if you would kindly grant permission as I have humbly requested through your benevolence, it would be a most grateful blessing. The above.

Seri-machi, Fujino-ya
Kōka 2nd year, Kinoto-mi, 2nd month　　Petitioner: Yojizaemon
Town Representative: Kozaemon
To the Magistrate's Office

The above petition was submitted by Mr. Shimizu at the government office on the 10th day.

【Left Page】
Respectfully, I humbly submit this oral petition regarding the following matter:
I have been working as a cooper until now, but due to advancing age, I can no longer make barrels, so I humbly request that you grant exemption from the cooper's trade tax (un'jō).
Therefore, I humbly submit this petition.
Regarding the above matter, if you would kindly grant permission as I have humbly requested through your benevolence, it would be most gratefully received. The above.

Seri-machi
Kōka 2nd year, Kinoto-mi, 2nd month　　Fukakusa-ya
Petitioner: Kazaemon
To the Magistrate's Office　　Town Representative: Kozaemon

The above petition was also submitted in the same manner, and on the 15th day there was notice from Mr. Gotō that approval had been granted. This was communicated to the town representative during the general assembly (ōyose) on the 16th day.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　Nakamachi
　　Kōka 2, Kinoto-Mi year, 1st month [January 1845]　　Yoshidaya
　　　　　　　　　　　　　Petitioner　　Kanemon
　　　　　　　　　　　　　Town representative　　Sōbei
　　　To the Magistrate's Office
　The above petition was submitted on the 17th day at the government office through the intermediation of Mr. Gotō, and was received and held.

　　　Humbly submitting this oral petition
　　I am an incompetent person, but I have been honored to receive
　　the appointment as head of the dyers and have been serving gratefully.
　　As my term has reached its limit, I humbly request that temporary exemption
　　be granted.

　If you would graciously grant my petition as stated above through your benevolence,

　I would consider it a most grateful blessing. End.

　　Kōka 2, Kinoto-Mi year, 1st month [January 1845]　　Shimozato-chō
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Kyōya
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Petitioner　Jinpachi
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Town representative　Takeshichi
　　　To the Magistrate's Office

　The above petition was submitted on the 17th day at the government office through the intermediation of Mr. Gotō, and was received and held.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　On the 10th day of the 2nd month, a suspension order was issued.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　On the 15th of the same month, as the petition was returned,
　　Humbly submitting this oral petition　　　it was delivered to Jinpei on the 16th.

　I am an incompetent person, but I have been honored to receive
　the appointment as town representative, considering it a grateful blessing,
　and have been serving for a long time. However, I have become ill
　and find it difficult to perform my official duties, therefore
　I humbly request that exemption be granted.

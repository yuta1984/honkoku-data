【Right Page】
Respectfully submitting this oral petition
Regarding Senjuro, who is under my care, I wish to send him to Hibikimonya Tobei's establishment in Matsumoto castle town for a five-year term as an indentured servant, and therefore respectfully petition for a religious sect exemption certificate during his term of service.
Regarding the above matter, if you would graciously grant this petition as requested,
it would be most grateful and fortunate. The above.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Honmachi Nakaya
　Tenpō 15, Year of the Wood Dragon, 2nd month　　Petitioner: Seishichi
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Town Representative: Yozaemon
　　To the Magistrate's Office
The above petition was submitted through Shimizu-shi's intermediation on the 20th day, whereupon it was approved the same day, and this was communicated from Goto-shi and conveyed to the town representative.

【Left Page】
Respectfully submitting this oral petition
Regarding my younger sister Shiki, since she has a common-law relationship, I have discussed sending her to serve as a concubine to Kawasakiya Gohei of Iida Chikuhisa-cho First District in the castle town of Lord Hori Yamato-no-kami, and therefore respectfully submit this petition.
Regarding the above matter, if you would graciously grant this petition as requested,
it would be most grateful and fortunate. The above.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Nakamachi Yorozuya
　Tenpō 15, Year of the Wood Dragon, 2nd month　　Petitioner: Takebei
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Town Representative: Munebei
　　To the Magistrate's Office
The above petition was submitted through Shimizu-shi's intermediation on the 27th day, whereupon it was immediately approved, and the official decision was communicated from Goto-shi and conveyed to the town representative on the 28th day.

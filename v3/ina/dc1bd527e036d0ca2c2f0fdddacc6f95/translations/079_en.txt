【Right Page】
　　Humbly submitting this oral petition
I wish to engage in confectionery business from this year. Therefore,
I submit this petition.
Regarding the above matter, if you would graciously grant
my petition as requested, it would be a most grateful blessing. The above
　　　　　　　　　　　　　Shimizu-machi
　　Kōka 2, Kinoto-Mi year, 3rd month
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Kabuta-ya
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Petitioner: Heiemon
　To the Magistrate's Office　　　　District representative: Gihei

The above petition was submitted on the 17th at the government office through the intermediation of Lord Shimizu.
On the 22nd it was approved, and notification was given to visit Lord Yamashita Jūgorō's residence on the 25th to receive the permit.
The district representative Gihei was summoned and informed of this.

【Left Page】
　　Humbly submitting this oral petition
A person named Hachiemon from Yoshida Kajimachi in the castle town of Lord Matsudaira Izu-no-kami
wishes to come to my establishment and engage in sickle commerce.
Therefore, I submit this petition.
Regarding the above matter, with your mercy———　　　Naka-machi
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Petitioner: Junshichi
　Kōka 2, Kinoto-Mi year, 3rd month　　　　District representative: Sōbei
　To the Magistrate's Office

The above petition was submitted on the 17th at the government office through the intermediation of Lord Shimizu.
On the 2nd day of the 4th month, notification was given to bring a seal and visit the Construction Office on the 5th,
and this was conveyed to the district representative.

Respectfully submitting this oral statement as a petition
Item: One small weir　　　　　　　　　　　　　One location
Regarding the above matter, at the Ashizawa-jo Shimokawahara area, in the agricultural lands designated for irrigation channels and river maintenance where it would not cause stone obstruction, I humbly request permission to construct a small weir.
Regarding this matter, if you would mercifully grant permission as petitioned above, it would be most grateful and fortunate. The above stated.
　　Tenpo 15th year, Kinoe-Tatsu, 7th month　　　　Shimo-machi
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Petitioner: Gonshiro
　　To the Magistrate's Office　　　　　　　Town Representative: Tomouemon
The above petition was submitted on the 27th day through Mr. Ono's intermediation at the government office. Approved after hearing.

【Right Page】
　　Humbly submitting this oral petition
Regarding Kanetsugi of Ikedaya from Shimojōya-machi in Lord Hori Yamato-no-kami's castle town,
since there is an informal engagement, I wish to support and take in
his wife and children under my care and have them added to our religious registry.
Therefore, I humbly submit this petition.
Regarding the above matter, if you would mercifully grant
my petition as requested, I would be most grateful. The above.
　Kōka 2nd year, 2nd month　　　Honmachi Hikogamiya
　　　　　　　　　　　　Petitioner: Kohei
　　To the Magistrate's Office　　Town Representative: Yozaemon
The above petition was submitted on the 17th through Mr. Shimizu's intermediation, and approval was granted on the 25th, which I conveyed to the town representative.

【Left Page】
　　Humbly submitting this oral petition
I have been operating a dyer's business with three indigo vats until now, but this time
I wish to increase by two vats to operate a dyer's business with a total of five vats.
Therefore, I humbly submit this petition.
Regarding the above matter, if you would mercifully grant my petition as requested,
I would be most grateful. The above.　　　Shinmachi Yamaguchiya
　　Kōka 2nd year, 2nd month　　　　Petitioner: Katsuhachi

【Right Page】
　　Humbly submitting this oral petition
My son Kanzaburō has no male heir, so regarding Bunshichi,
the younger brother of Oriemon from Nagaoka Village in Minowa Domain
under Lord Matsudaira Tamba-no-kami's jurisdiction, since there is already
an informal engagement, I wish to have him marry my son Kanzaburō's daughter Matsu
as an adopted son-in-law. Therefore, I humbly submit this petition.
Regarding the above matter, if you would mercifully grant
my petition as requested, I would be most grateful. The above.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Shimo-machi
　　Kōka 2nd year, 2nd month　　　　Matsuuraya
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Petitioner: Seishichi
　　　To the Magistrate's Office　　Town Representative: Tōemon
The above petition was submitted on the 10th through Mr. Shimizu's intermediation, and approval was granted immediately, so I informed the town representative on the morning of the 11th.

【Left Page】
　　Humbly submitting this oral petition
My younger brother Kahei has no male heir, so regarding Gengo,
the son of Mr. Yajima Hanji who serves as group inspector under
Mr. Ushikoshi Ichizaemon, since there is already an informal engagement,
I wish to adopt him as a son. Therefore, I humbly submit this petition.
Regarding the above matter, if you would mercifully grant
my petition as requested, I would be most grateful. The above.
　　　　　　　　　　　　Nakagawa
　　　　　　　　　　　　　Kiya
　　Kōka 2nd year, 2nd month　　Petitioner: Shōbei
　　To the Magistrate's Office　　Town Representative: Sōbei
The above petition was submitted in the same manner, and on the last day of the 2nd month, a letter of approval came from Mr. Kitano, which I conveyed to the town representative on the 1st day of the 3rd month.

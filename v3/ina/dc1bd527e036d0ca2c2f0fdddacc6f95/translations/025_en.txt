【Right Page】
Humbly submitting this petition in writing
My relative Kahei of Imatsuyama shop in Yokocho has been temporarily severed from his family line and serving others.
At this time, there is an intimate relationship with Imaroku, younger brother of Seizaemon from Hayashi village, which is under the jurisdiction of Lord Waku Seieisuke.
I wish to adopt him to continue the family name.
Therefore, I humbly submit this petition.
Regarding the above matter, if you would graciously grant my petition as requested,
it would be most grateful. Above stated.　　　　　Honcho
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Shimaya
Tenpo 15, Year of the Wood Dragon, 2nd month　　Petitioner: Hikobei
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Town Representative: Mataemon
　　To the Magistrate's Office
The above petition was submitted on the 15th through Mr. Goto's intermediation, and was immediately approved,
so this was conveyed to the town representative.

【Right Page】
Respectfully submitting this oral petition
I have been working as a roofer under the patronage of Suzuki Hikobei with an official trade license, but now I wish to become independent. Therefore, I request that the said official trade license be rewritten.
Regarding the above matter, if you would graciously grant my request as petitioned, it would be most grateful and fortunate. The above.

Kōka 2, Year of the Snake, 4th month          Shimizu-machi
                                              Tachibana-ya
                                        Petitioner: Heiemon
To the Magistrate's Office                Town representative: Gihei

The above petition was submitted to Sakai-shi's office on the 27th day

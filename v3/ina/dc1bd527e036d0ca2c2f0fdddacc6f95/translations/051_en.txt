Total: 2 monme 9 bu 7 rin 5 mo, 131 mon (6 mon?)
　This matter: 458 mon
With the above ingredients, about 120 manju can be made.
In that case, the cost per piece comes to about 3 mon 7 bu, so we wish to sell them at 4 mon each.
　Dragon 11th month　　　　All confectionery sellers

Regarding the above matter, it was submitted on the 10th through the intermediation of Mr. Takahashi Isozaemon, and the official response on the 27th day of the 11th month was that henceforth they should make and sell both at 3 mon 5 bu and 4 mon, and furthermore to lower prices as much as possible and give firm instructions so there would be no misunderstandings. This official response from Mr. Shimizu was conveyed, so hereafter Izawaya Sozaemon and Kashiwaya Yoshiji were summoned and instructed according to the written order.

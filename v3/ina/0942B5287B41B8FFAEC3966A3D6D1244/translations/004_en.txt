　　　　Same day, 14th　●
Rain all day, returned home in the evening
　　　　Same day, 15th　◐ [Circle with partial black fill on right inside]
Fine weather. Arrived at the western location around 8 o'clock, then began inspection from the Koide farmland hundred-step cutting area, ending at Tsutsuji-hara (azalea field). Left the site before 8 PM, arrived at Nishi-machi at 9 o'clock, revised the field survey map. Departed around 2 AM, went directly to the lodging, Kawachi submitted documents and withdrew.
　　　　Same day, 16th ○　Slight wind in the afternoon
As there was business for the surveying team, went to the lodging where we were ordered to prepare another draft map, which Matsuzawa immediately created and submitted.

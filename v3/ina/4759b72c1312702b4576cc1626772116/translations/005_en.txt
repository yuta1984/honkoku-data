They met with the surface officials' craftsmen, and a messenger came from Yajiuemon's side saying that since they were going to request tree-cutting craftsmen for Yaichi, he should hurry and make an emergency trip. Although I thought this was arbitrary handling without consulting me, I went anyway. There, Yajiuemon, Taheiji, Hikouemon, and their sons had come out and apparently had Zensaku-sama give instructions. It appeared they had secretly asked him to have the branches cut down, telling him this beforehand. During the indiscriminate cutting, Yaichi said that cutting those branches would not be proper, so please leave them as they are. However, they said if these branches were not cut they would be in the way, and cut them anyway. Although he asked them not to cut those branches and leave them be, they pretended not to hear and cut them away. They left one branch extending to the south, and about two ken up where there were no dead trees, at the cutting boundary there were two thin eaves branches, and even though he asked that those branches be left as they were, it appeared they were already going to cut them.

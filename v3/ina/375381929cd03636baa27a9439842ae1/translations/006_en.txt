July 1st
　　Memorandum
- Mountain officials, village headmen, assistant officials, town messengers, official agents, and town representatives shall go out to meet [the lord] along the route when he returns to the castle

- Along the lord's route through the town, water shall be sprinkled, buckets placed in front of houses, cleaning ordered, and sand spread on the main thoroughfare

- Post stations for express messengers in the immediate domain shall pay attention to providing meals more than on ordinary days

[Left page]
　　However, boiled dishes and grilled dishes
- Regarding deputy magistrates' service duties, attendance on rest days shall be ordered

- Three daily-wage lodgings in Edo shall be designated
　　However, names must be submitted immediately

- Women and children in the town shall remain on raised floors to observe when the lord passes, while men shall remain on earthen floors, and orders shall be given to avoid any improper behavior

- When visiting Mankō-ji temple and going out to the fields, water sprinkling and buckets along the route

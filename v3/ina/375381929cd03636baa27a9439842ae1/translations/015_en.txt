When you arrive at Ashizawa, return and report immediately.

and report as instructed.

July 11th
One: Lord Katsugami Chōbei departed at 6:30.
　Five people went to Sagari-machi bridge approach to serve in his retinue.
　They saw him off in front of blacksmith Eizō's shop in Hokoji-machi,
　while town representatives, purveyors, and merchant supervisors
　waited at the square. From there, they went to the residence of village headman Gorobei of various towns.
【Left Page】
　He rested there. The five people inquired after his health, and following
　instructions for arrangements, rested at cotton merchant Chūbei's place.
　The town representatives rested at Fujiya Takehachi's place.

One: We confirmed that his lordship arrived at Mikka-machi
　and reported this to the magistrate's lodging.
One: We reported his departure to Mutsu-zuka.
One: We reported his arrival at
　Ashizawa village, at Shōbei's residence. We were instructed
　to also report this to the deputy magistrate.

We wished to make an inquiry and had traveled a great distance for this purpose. When we requested that the matter be handled favorably, they acknowledged our request and said they would certainly take care of us, and also that they would inform the officials at the liaison office about this matter. In the official capacity:

One matter was mediated. Through the same person's introduction:
　　　Ashuke Village Officials: Kyūzaemon
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Hikoshichi

We met with these two men and explained our business. They acknowledged it and when they reported to the Survey Officials, they were told that people had come from Takato, and they were immediately instructed to accompany them. Kyūzaemon promptly guided us to the main lodging (honjin), where we were immediately granted an audience with Lord Shimokawabe Seigoro. He confirmed that we had come from Takato and acknowledged our efforts. He then inquired about the distance of our journey, to which we replied that it was thirty-three ri. He also expressed appreciation for our coming from such a distant place and [arranged for us to rest] in the inner room and [provided accommodation] at the official residence.

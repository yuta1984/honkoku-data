1. Mile markers are installed at various points along the road.
   The posted notices differ in content, but this is
   not due to official government orders, rather
   the villages along the road have set them up
   　　　　　　　　　　　as they see fit

1. Regarding road construction work for the official passage

1. Toilets and other
   unsightly places along the route of passage
   should all be concealed;
   enclosures and buildings with boards are also
   sturdy in some places

1. For houses built in rows where rafters and such
   can be seen through, they should either be rebuilt
   or enclosed without board walls, and everything
   　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　should be
   properly tidied up

1. For all places where there are administrative offices (jinya)
   we will make inquiries when you make stops there.
   It is said that the Mikawa area will also be divided into two routes
   for the inspection tour

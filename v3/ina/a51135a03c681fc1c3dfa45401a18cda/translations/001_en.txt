Respectfully Submitted: A Petition Presented as an Oral Statement

1. Our castle town has been experiencing poor economic conditions in recent years, declining year by year. Particularly in the past two or three years, we have reached a critical point of stagnation, and all of us are experiencing extreme hardship in continuing our trades and businesses.

Under these circumstances, last November we received Your Lordship's compassionate written directive, for which we are deeply grateful and consider ourselves most fortunate.

Furthermore, you prohibited peddling and pawnbroking in the rural areas, and ordered that those engaged in peddling should come to conduct business in the castle town, issuing directives to this effect throughout the countryside. The recent written directives you have issued are extremely beneficial and we consider ourselves most fortunate, for which we are deeply grateful. We humbly wish to work diligently so that the castle town may prosper and our businesses may continue. Therefore, we respectfully submit this petition in the form of itemized articles.

<<Marginal note: Early morning announcement>> July 4th
Concerning the suspension of circulation of old gold and silver coins with regular script two-bu denomination, old two-shu silver coins, as well as Bunsei-era gold and silver coins with cursive script two-bu and two-shu silver coins - although it was announced in the previous Year of the Sheep that these should be exchanged by October of the current Year of the Monkey, since there are still many unexchanged coins remaining, regarding the matter of exchange procedures, they will continue to be handled as before until October of the coming Year of the Rooster.

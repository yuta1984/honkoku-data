Proclamation issued on the 26th day of the 7th month of Kaei 6, Year of the Water Ox (1853):
On the past 22nd day,
His Excellency the Shogun passed away, therefore construction work and music/festivals are suspended.
During this period, exercise strict restraint and pay particular attention to fire prevention and other matters.
The above directive shall be announced throughout the town.
　　7th month, 26th day

Kaei 7, Year of the Wood Tiger (1854)

[Left Page]

For the sake of circulation in society, it has been ordered that one-shu silver coins be minted using high-quality nanryo silver.
Sixteen of these one-shu silver coins shall be calculated as equivalent to one ryo of gold.
Naturally, both silver coins and copper coins should be exchanged at the same rate as one-bu silver, and should be used in transactions accordingly.
These nanryo one-shu silver coins have been ordered to circulate in the same manner as gold coins, so they should be put into circulation without delay. Naturally, matters concerning circulation and similar issues shall be deliberated upon.
The above directive shall be made known and announced to all provinces.
　　　12th month
Announce as stated above.

1. Regarding salt, tea, and fish, of the commission rates (kuchisen) that have been handled by wholesalers in previous years, a reduction of one-bu (25%) is hereby ordered. The prices of various goods should be thoroughly examined with trading partners to make them as affordable as possible, and this is entrusted to Tōji and Kanai-kichi. Furthermore, this should be handled in consultation with the brokers previously appointed.

1. Regarding goods sent directly from fish dealers in other places to those in the same trade in this locality, the same commission as goods consigned to Tōji and Kanai-kichi has been collected, but for this portion, the commission shall be reduced by half hereafter.

1. Regarding salt and tea as well, for portions delivered directly to the shops of those engaged in such trade, the commission shall similarly be reduced by half.

Shimoge-machi
　　　　　　　Fujiya
　　　　　　　　Chūzaemon
Regarding the above-mentioned person, as we have appointed him
as commercial license inspector this time,
concerning matters of his duties,
he shall receive instructions from the district official regarding all matters
and carry out his duties with careful attention to directions.
Regarding the above matter, each person shall witness
the announcement.
　December 15th
　　To Mr. Yamashita Junzō
　　To the clerks

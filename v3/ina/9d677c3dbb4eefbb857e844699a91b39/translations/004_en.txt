concerning what was ordered this summer
not to be neglected - regarding these matters,
it has been heard that there are some who
misunderstand the official intent.
Matters of official control and
reform should be strictly observed
exactly as initially ordered.
The above intent shall be announced without omission
in shogunal territories, private domains, and temple lands alike. That is all.

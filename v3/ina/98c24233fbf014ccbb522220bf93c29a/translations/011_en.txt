Humbly submitting this oral petition
Regarding Wazaemon, the younger brother of Jinbee from Okusa Village under the jurisdiction of Kishimoto Jūsuke-sama, who was also my common-law husband but has passed away, I wish to adopt him as my son and humbly request permission.
Regarding this matter, if you would mercifully grant permission as requested, it would be a most grateful blessing. The above.

Fukuromachi
Tenpō 8, year of the Fire Rooster, 2nd month          Takeharaya
                                            Petitioner: Miyauchi, widow
                                            Group head: Buntarō
                                            Town representative: Ikushichi
To the Magistrate's Office (Approved on the 25th day)

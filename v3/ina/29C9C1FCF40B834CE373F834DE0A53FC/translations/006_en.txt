My wife and children shall also come, and I ask that you take care of my wife and children as well. As for myself, after making this request, I shall depart for another province. Recently you have had countless troubles, but I humbly ask for your favor. Of course, regarding my wife and children, there are no restrictions whatsoever. The above.

　August 4th　　Miyoshi Manemon
　To Yamanaka Moriemon

　　　Actually the second son of Okabe Jūroemon
　　　　　Third generation　Miyoshi Yasutaka
　　　　　　　　Original name　Masaharu

1. Second year of Ansei, Kinoto-U year (1855): When the lord was in residence at the castle, he brought with him from Edo the swordsman Saitō Yakurō and the head of the same school, Katsura Kogorō (later Kido Takayoshi). With private instructions, arrangements were made for enrollment, and sword training was conducted at Saitō Yakurō's dojo in Edo Banchō.

1. First year of Bunkyū, Kanoto-Tori year (1861), ninth month: After obtaining permission through petition, he departed with Kawanishi Jirōzaemon's second son Junji for warrior training throughout the provinces.

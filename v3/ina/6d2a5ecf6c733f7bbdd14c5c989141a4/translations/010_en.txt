September 3rd

Due to the death of Former Right Minister Kazanin, musical instruments and festivities are prohibited for three days starting today. However, for those in distant areas, this shall apply from the day they receive notice.
   Note: Although this is during the period of prohibition of music and festivities, the above guidelines should be observed.

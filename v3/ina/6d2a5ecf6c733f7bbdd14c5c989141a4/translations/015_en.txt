Things to remember and understand:

First, those who possess fields and paddies should, of course, and even those with just small plots of vacant land, should be mindful to plant items that can become food.

First, regarding ceremonies for auspicious and inauspicious occasions, since it is heard that miscellaneous expenses are considerable, from now on one should be mindful to reduce these according to one's means, and make frugality the primary concern in all matters.

The above principles should be communicated without fail even to tenants and back-alley shops, making each town and household aware, and encouraging frugality. Town officials should make frequent rounds, and for those who do not understand, they should explain thoroughly. If one does not prepare from this time forward, each person will fall into destitution and extreme hardship, so this warning is given in advance.

【Main text】
　　Fujiwara no Ason Yoritada
　　　　The aforementioned person shall be appointed to Junior Fifth Rank Lower Grade
Having reported to the Ministry of Central Affairs his distinguished military service, and in his duties at the Imperial Guard,
his sincere devotion has been unwavering, serving the public interest day and night. It is appropriate to
bestow upon him this honorary rank to manifest imperial favor. In accordance with the above,
the responsible officials shall implement this decree.
　Hōreki 12th year, 12th month, 18th day (December 18, 1762)

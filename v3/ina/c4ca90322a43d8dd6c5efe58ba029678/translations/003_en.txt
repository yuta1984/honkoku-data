Kobayashi Hachiro, the representative of Master Osuitayu
On his way back from making rounds to temple patrons, he stopped by on March 18th.
Tayu Yoshishige states that during this autumn, following precedent,
he wishes to visit various patron families,
and at that time, he asks for your kind assistance.

Item: On the intercalary 7th month, 14th day, Kobayashi Hachiro came with a request.
As mentioned in spring, Tayu Yoshishige
went to Suwa district on the 23rd of last month
and will remain there during this month,
then proceed to Matsumoto to complete business there,
planning to arrive at our locality around mid-August.
Since we received your care in previous years,
he humbly requests lodging again this time.
In response, we explained that some time ago our town suffered a fire,
and we were affected by the spreading flames, our building
is quite different from previous years and unsightly,
so this time, if you have other arrangements in mind,
please feel free to make other provisions.

During the reign of Lord Yoriyasu
- November 25, Bunsei 6 (1823): Granted 3 koku and rations for 2 people, appointed as inner Buddhist priest to Lord Ryōnoshin
- December 21, Bunsei 10 (1827): As requested by his father Tsunaemon, succeeded to family headship upon father's retirement, granted ashigaru rank with stipend of 17 hyō and rations for 2 people, duties to continue as before
- January 15, Bunsei 12 (1829): Appointed to regular ashigaru position with 1 hyō increase, duties to continue as before
- September 13, Tenpō 4 (1833): Satisfactorily relieved of inner Buddhist priest duties at the residence, appointed to guard rotation duty
- July 18, Tenpō 5 (1834): Appointed to regular patrol duty within the domain for maintaining order
- Last day of October, same year: Died of illness
　　End of service

Tsuchiya Tadanosuke
During the reign of Lord Yoriyasu
- July 18, Tenpō 5 (1834): Appointed to assist Buddhist priests while learning service duties
- December 18, same year: Succeeded his late father Kanaemon, granted stipend of 18 hyō and rations for 2 people with ashigaru rank, appointed to escort duty, henceforth under the command of ashigaru commanders
- Last day of July, Tenpō 8 (1837): Died of illness
　　End of service

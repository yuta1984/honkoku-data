Ohira Ensai (younger brother of Ohira Saheta of the advanced guard unit, under the supervision of the Edo Senior Inspector)

One: Kanei 3, Kanoe-uma year (1750), 12th month, 29th day - transferred from Yano Tozaemon's Sadoedo unit, name changed from Ensuke (or Yaemon) to Ensuke

During the reign of Lord Genryuin (Lord Naito Yoriyoshi of Takato Domain)
One: Hōreki 4, Kinoe-inu year (1754), 6th month, 24th day - appointed as monk attendant in Edo, shaved head at own discretion and changed name to Ensai
One: Hōreki 12, Mizunoe-uma year (1762), intercalary 4th month, 29th day - monk attendant at the lower residence
One: Meiwa 9, Mizunoe-tatsu year (1772), 11th month, 20th day - additionally appointed to inner palace duties
One: An'ei 4, Kinoto-hitsuji year (1775), 10th month, 23rd day - granted ashigaru rank equivalent to 17 hyō and stipend for two retainers, given 300 hiki in gold for formal court dress
One: An'ei 6, Hinoto-tori year (1777), 1st month, 24th day - granted regular ashigaru status, ordered to return to secular life, duties to continue as before

During the reign of Lord Honryuin (Lord Naito Yorimochi of Takato Domain)  
One: An'ei 7, Tsuchinoe-inu year (1778), 3rd month, 15th day - appointed to mountain administration
One: Tenmei 4, Kinoe-tatsu year (1784), 7th month, last day - appointed to salt and miso distribution duties, successfully relieved of mountain administration duties
One: Tenmei 7, Hinoto-hitsuji year (1787), 7th month, 1st day - ordered to relocate to the domain seat

(In red ink) Ohira En

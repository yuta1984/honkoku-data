Tsuchiya Tsunaemon

During the reign of Lord Honryūin
- March 23, Kansei 2 (1790): Promoted to ashigaru (foot soldier), granted a stipend of 18 hyō and rations for 2 people, appointed to escort duty
- Same year: Appointed to guard duty rotation

During the reign of Lord Yorimoto
- August 14, Kansei 12 (1800): Appointed to attend the ladies-in-waiting quarters
- September 15, Bunka 3 (1806): Relieved of ashigaru inspector duties, reassigned to guard duty rotation

During the reign of Lord Yoriyasu
- December 28, Bunsei 10 (1827): As requested, retired and his son Kanaemon was granted succession of the family headship
- Last day of October, Tenpō 2 (1831): Died of illness
　　End of service

Tsuchiya Kanaemon

During the reign of Lord Yorimoto
- July 10, Bunka 2 (1805): Appointed to assist the Buddhist priests

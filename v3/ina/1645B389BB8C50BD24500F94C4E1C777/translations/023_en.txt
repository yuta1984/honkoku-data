- Same 6th year (1835), intercalary 7th month, 1st day: Appointed to provisions management
- Same 10th year (1839), October 23rd: Appointed to tax assessment duty, provisions management
　　satisfactorily completed and dismissed
- Same 14th year (1843), February 18th: Dismissed from tax assessment duty, appointed to rotation duty
- Same 15th year (1844), November 8th: Due to old age and illness, retirement granted as requested,
　　and family headship transferred to his son Sōbei

During the reign of Lord Yoriyasu

- Same 6th year (1823), November 25th: Due to administrative reorganization, temporary employment dismissed,
　　granted half provisions

　　ordered to work in the clerk's office to assist with writing duties
- Tenpō 2 (1831), January 15th: Appointed to temporary clerk assistant duty,
　　granted increase of provisions for one retainer, additional support of provisions for one and half retainers

- Same 6th year (1835), August 18th: Inspector duty, due to administrative policy temporarily
　　suspended by observation, dismissed from duty without incident

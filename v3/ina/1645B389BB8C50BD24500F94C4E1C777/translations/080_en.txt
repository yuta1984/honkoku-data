Furuta Shichirōbei

Reign of Lord Yorimoto
- 15th day, 2nd month, Bunka 7 (1810): Due to father Genzaemon's meritorious service in financial administration for several years, his son Yohachirō is newly appointed as an ashigaru foot soldier and granted a stipend of 18 hyō and provisions for two people. As he has been dedicated to studying armor and ancient practices, he should continue to work diligently and properly arrange the execution of duties before taking over the position.

- 8th day, 10th month, Bunka 14 (1817): Appointed to escort duty with an increase of 2 hyō and assigned to alternate duty.

- 15th day, 3rd month, Bunsei 2 (1819): Appointed to serve Lord Ryōnoshin in his quarters, thereby taking charge of preparing his morning and evening meals and other official duties.

Reign of Lord Yoriyasu
- 20th day, 2nd month, Bunsei 7 (1824): Successfully completed service to Lord Ryōnoshin in his quarters and was released from duty, then appointed to alternate duty.

- 26th day, 3rd month, Bunsei 8 (1825): Regarding misconduct by deceased father Genzaemon
"(Red ink notation) Furuta"

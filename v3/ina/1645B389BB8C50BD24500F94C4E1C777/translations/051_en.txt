Iwasaki Takuemon
During the reign of Lord Yorimochi
1. Kansei 3, Year of the Boar (1791), 5th month, 10th day: Appointed as head of the village group (gōgumi-gashira)
1. Same 8th year, Year of the Dragon (1796), 4th month, 1st day: Appointed as village clerk (gōkata-monogaki)
1. Kyōwa 3, Year of the Boar (1803), 11th month, 20th day: Promoted to foot soldier (o-kachi)
   Stipend of 18 hyō and provisions for 2 people, duties to continue as before
1. Bunka 12, Year of the Boar (1815), 3rd month, 5th day: Increase of 2 hyō

During the reign of Lord Yoriyasu
1. Bunsei 5, Year of the Horse (1822), 2nd month, 5th day: Due to illness, relieved of duties as requested, and awarded 200 hiki of gold as a reward
1. Same month, 6th day: Died of illness
   Service record (completed)

Iwasaki Takuemon
During the reign of Honryūin-sama
1. Kansei 2, Year of the Metal Dog (1790), 3rd month, 28th day: Appointed as regular village group employee and granted provisions for 1 person
1. Same 3rd year, Year of the Metal Boar (1791), 5th month, 23rd day: Granted an increase of provisions for 1 person

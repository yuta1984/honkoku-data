Initial name: Koike
Kinoshita Waemon
Later: Imaemon

During the reign of Lord Tanomo
• Kansei 4, year of the rat, 4th month, 22nd day (1792): Appointed to the position of scribe at the purchasing department and construction department under the chief administrator's office, with an additional rice stipend of 1 koku 5 to, assigned to Edo duties
• Same 6th year, year of the tiger, 2nd month, 28th day (1794): Additional rice stipend of 6 to, making the total 5 koku 6 to
• Same 9th year, year of the snake, 5th month, 11th day (1797): Promoted to the rank of ashigaru (foot soldier), granted 18 hyō and provisions for 2 people, duties remain as before
• Same 11th year, year of the sheep, 7th month, 20th day (1799): Successfully completed duties at chief administrator's office construction department, appointed to escort duties under the ashigaru commander
• Bunka 4, year of the rabbit, 10th month, 28th day (1807): Additionally appointed to mountain department and cooking duties, under the superintendent's supervision
• Same 10th year, year of the rooster, 6th month, 8th day (1813): Appointed to escort guard duty with an additional 2 hyō increase and head of servants, other duties remain as before
• Same 12th year, year of the boar, 1st month, 15th day (1815): Due to certain confidential matters, appointed as head of mountain department, other duties remain as before

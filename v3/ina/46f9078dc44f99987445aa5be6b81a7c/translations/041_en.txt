　　　Memorandum
1. Regarding apprentices kept by carpenters in the domain territory: Until now, they were not required to pay service fees regardless of their number, but differences should be made according to the number of people. Therefore, from now on, for apprentices kept, one person's service fee shall be paid for one apprentice, and for two or more apprentices, one person's service fee shall be paid for each person.
　　However, regarding carpenters keeping apprentices, the local officials shall conduct examinations, and future increases and decreases shall be reported to the master craftsmen from time to time.
1. Regarding apprentices kept by blacksmiths - how many people and from how many years ago they have been kept, as well as child apprentices who have gone to other places as caretakers - the local officials shall conduct examinations and report to the master craftsmen.

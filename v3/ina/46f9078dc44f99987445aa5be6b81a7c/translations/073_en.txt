Genbun 5, Year of the Metal Monkey
　The shrine of Haijisai-in [Hokoji Gongen] having suffered major damage, this time
　A petition for repair was submitted, and after inspection
　Funding was provided. However, the complete
　restoration of the main shrine and its subsidiary shrines requires more resources than available, so throughout the lord's domain

　So fundraising may proceed. However, according to people's intentions,
　Regardless of the amount donated, one must not forcefully

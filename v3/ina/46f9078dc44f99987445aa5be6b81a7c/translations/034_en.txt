Memorandum

1. Regarding the recoining and reforming of gold and silver currency due to reports that there is a shortage of gold and silver in circulation, causing inconvenience in trade.

1. Concerning the distribution of the newly recoined gold and silver: Keichō gold and new gold shall be exchanged at 100 ryō for 100 monme, Kanshi gold at 200 ryō for 200 ryō, and Keichō silver and new silver at 10 kan-me for 10 kan-me of medium-grade gold. Based on these exchange calculations, the gold and silver specified in the written records shall be accepted without discrimination, and distribution to feudal lords and money changing shall proceed smoothly. The same shall apply to tax payments in gold and silver.

1. For the recoined gold and silver issued by the gold and silver mints with additional premiums: when exchanging, according to the quantities involved, an additional premium of 65 monme shall be given per 100 monme of exchange gold, and an additional premium of 5 monme of silver shall be given per 10 kan-me of exchange silver.

1. Regarding the gold and silver to be exchanged: while the regulation states that exchanges should be conducted through merchants, daimyo houses and others may arrange exchanges through direct negotiations with merchants as convenient.

1. Concerning the gold and silver to be submitted for exchange: since the quantities are known, they should be submitted for exchange without hoarding. If anyone is found to be hoarding and not exchanging, they will be investigated and severely punished.
   Note: Silver not submitted for this exchange should be understood to be melted down and stored as before.

These provisions are to be understood in all provinces and localities.

Genbun 1st year, 5th month

Regarding this gold and silver exchange matter: exchanges will be conducted at the gold and silver mints from the coming [date], so this should be understood accordingly.

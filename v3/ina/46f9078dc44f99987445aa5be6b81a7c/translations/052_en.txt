Oral statement: Saga Imperial Villa

Hōrin-ji Temple in Kyoto Saga is an imperial prayer temple (chokugan-ji), therefore there was an official decree with joint seals from the Temples and Shrines Magistrate from the previous U-Ushi year to conduct fundraising tours throughout the country. This time, for the temple building repairs, we have received imperial command according to precedent. Based on this, the government has also issued orders throughout Japan with special consideration. Therefore, this time we will conduct fundraising in all villages and towns without exception, and village headmen and group leaders should provide lodging. Furthermore, communications to the temples and shrines should be received through the village headmen. Henceforth, fundraising matters should correspond to the temple's status, and we hope they will be completed without delay. Even fundraising up to individual villages will be paid directly, so please understand accordingly.

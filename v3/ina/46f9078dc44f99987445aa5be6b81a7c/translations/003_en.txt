Record of Official Proclamations from the 9th Year of Genroku, Year of the Rat, 8th Month

1. This time, aside from the designated locations in Hongō for refining gold and silver, all gold and silver refining is strictly prohibited. Should there be anyone refining in unauthorized locations, or anyone creating counterfeit gold and silver, they must be reported immediately. Even if they are accomplices, their crimes will be pardoned and rewards will definitely be granted. Arrangements will be made so that no retaliation can occur. If anyone conceals such information and it is later discovered by others, not only the person themselves but also all relatives and local residents will be subject to punishment.
　　9th Month, Year of the Rat

Memorandum
1. This time, two-shu coins have been made from new gold and are being distributed to the public. To facilitate free circulation, all provinces and localities should understand this purpose and use the two-shu coins without hindrance in commerce and official transactions. The two-shu coin should be valued at half of one bu.

1. Large gold coins (ōban), small gold coins (koban), and one-bu coins should of course continue to circulate as before.

1. As previously proclaimed, if there are those creating counterfeit gold and silver, they should be reported as informants. Even if they are accomplices, their crimes will be pardoned and rewards will definitely be granted. Arrangements will be made so that no retaliation can occur. In general, those who work with gold and silver should be vigilant at their locations, and if they see or hear even the slightest suspicious activity, they must report it immediately. If they conceal such information and it is discovered by others, not only the person themselves but also all relatives and local residents will be subject to punishment.
　　7th Month, Year of the Rat

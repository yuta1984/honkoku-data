　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Yasuda Shin-no-jō

Genbun 5, Year of Kinoe-Saru
Aoki Bunzō, under the jurisdiction of the Temples and Shrines Magistrate,
will tour around Kōshū and Shinshū provinces to investigate
books, writings, and the Four Classics at various locations.
He wishes to examine scholarly books and writings at shrines, temples,
towns, and villages. Regarding his requests to freely examine,
copy, and have materials provided, comply with Bunzō's instructions.
Furthermore, rural areas, towns, and temples and shrines should
provide lodging for Bunzō's party without delay.
Of course, regarding lodging fees, circulate notice that
no payment should be expected. For this purpose, the following. The above.

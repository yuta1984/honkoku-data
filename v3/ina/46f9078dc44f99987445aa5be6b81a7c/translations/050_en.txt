Memorandum
1. Regarding the dispatch of inspection officials to villages in the domain territories this time: cleaning of the routes through which the aforementioned officials will pass, as well as any road and bridge construction work, must not be carried out. Sending people for entertainment or escort is also unnecessary.

2. Concerning the aforementioned officials: when horses and personnel beyond the number recorded in the official seal document are needed, places with established rates for various fees shall follow those rates; places without established rates shall provide horses and personnel by collecting fees based on rates proportional to nearby post stations. Not even one person or horse without payment beyond what is recorded in the official seal document may be provided.

3. Along the routes where the inspection officials pass, peasants shall be instructed to carry out their agricultural work without any restraint whatsoever.

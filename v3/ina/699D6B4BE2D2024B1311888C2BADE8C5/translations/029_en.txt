　　　Endō
Tajima-no-kami Taira no Yoshitaka was a descendant of the Endō of the Watanabe faction in Settsu Province.
By the time of Hyōgo-no-kami Morimasa, his mother was a
daughter of Toki Mino-no-kami Mitsuhira, so through this connection
he moved to Mino Province. His descendants
eventually became retainers of the Toki clan. By the time of Endō Shinbei-no-jō Taneyoshi,
when Oda Kazusa-no-suke conquered this province, Taneyoshi
was the first to join his side. Taneyoshi's eldest son Shinbei-no-jō Taneyoshi
succeeded his father, and his second son Tarō Zaemon-no-jō Morikazu succeeded
the house of Azuma Shimotsuke-no-kami Taira no Tsuneyoshi.

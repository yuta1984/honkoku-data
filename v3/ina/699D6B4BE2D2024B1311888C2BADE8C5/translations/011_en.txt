In the year the Hojo were destroyed, Lord Tokugawa moved to the Kanto region, Kozuke Province became his domain, and Shinano Province was offered to the Kanpaku's household. In place of the land granted to the Sanada in that province (Shinano), there would be another disposition from Lord Tokugawa, so the original Numata territory was returned to the Sanada. Since the eldest son Genjiro Nobuyuki had ruled this place from the beginning, he moved back to Numata Castle and became a retainer of Lord Tokugawa.

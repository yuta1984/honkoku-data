　　　Wakebe Clan
Sakyō-no-suke Minamoto Masatoshi's ancestors for generations lived in Ise Province and served as retainers of the Nagano family, who were lords of Age and Aki districts in that province 《marginal note: Without seeing the genealogy, the details are unclear》.
In the spring of Eiroku 11 (1568), when Lord Oda intended to attack Ise Province, he first besieged Hosono Castle. Sakyō-no-suke Masatoshi, accompanied by Kawakita Takumi-no-suke, went to Nobunaga's camp and declared: "The Nagano house is descended from Satsuma-no-kami Sukenaga, the second son of Kudō Saemon-no-jō Sukekyō. At the beginning of the Genkō era, they were granted Age District and took the name Nagano from residing in the land of Nagano" 《marginal note: According to the Ise Gunki, in the beginning of Genkō》
《marginal note: Initially, Kudō Jirō Saemon-no-jō Takakage was granted this district, but according to another theory, Satsuma-no-kami Sukenaga had already ruled this district at that time. Which version is true is unknown》.

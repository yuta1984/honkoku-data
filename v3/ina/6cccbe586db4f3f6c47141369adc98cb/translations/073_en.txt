申-Hitsuji year, 12th month, 28th day, evening
1 item: 25 ryō of gold

The above is the allowance money that was granted.
At the meeting place, through the intermediation of Lord Izawa Gyōsuke,　　　
it was bestowed upon us, and Yozaemon with his single seal
affixed his seal mark to the official ledger and submitted it.
We intended to distribute the allocation immediately upon returning,
however, due to the circumstances of the season, at the request of the town representatives,
we made the allocation distribution on the 5th day of the 1st month of the Tori year.

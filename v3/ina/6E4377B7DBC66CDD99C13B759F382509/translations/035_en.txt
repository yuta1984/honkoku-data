　　　　Sōma
Nagato-no-kami Taira no Yoshitane was the son of Danjō-daihitsu Moritane. Emperor Kammu's
fifth imperial son, First Rank Minister of Ceremonial Prince Kazurahara's grandson Prince Takano was the first to
receive the surname Taira, was granted Kazusa Province and appointed Kazusa-no-daiju, departing from
royal status to become a subject. During the reign of Emperor Suzaku, his second son, Chinzei-fu-shōgun
Taira no Yoshimasa's son Sōma Kojirō Masakado rose from Sōma district in Shimōsa Province,
seized Hitachi Province, and eventually conquered and subdued
the various provinces of Bandō. He established his capital at Ishii village in Saruhashi district of Shimōsa Province, proclaimed himself

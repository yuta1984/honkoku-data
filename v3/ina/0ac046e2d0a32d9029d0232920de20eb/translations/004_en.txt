Inquiry Document Regarding the Construction of Stone Retaining Wall Around Takeda Shingen's Grave Monument

Regarding the case of village headman Ichibei and the village officials of Iwakubo Village in Yamanashi District, Kai Province, under my jurisdiction as deputy magistrate, a petition was submitted concerning the construction of a stone retaining wall around Takeda Shingen's grave monument within the village territory. After dispatching an official to inspect the site and conduct an investigation, it was confirmed that within the Shibadon area of the same village, there stands a grave monument inscribed "Tomb of Hōshō-in Dai-sōjō Shingen."

The aforementioned grave monument is 5 shaku 6 sun in height and is erected upon a three-tiered stone base.

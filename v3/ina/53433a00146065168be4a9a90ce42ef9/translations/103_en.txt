Respectfully, I humbly submit this written petition.
I have been serving as chōdai-yaku (town representative), but as considerable time has passed, I wish to retire from this position and humbly request your permission to do so. Above stated.

Takato Shimo-machi
Meiji 5 (Mizunoe-Saru year)     Resident of House No. 98
July 20th                      Chōdai (town representative)
                              Petitioner: Kurokōchi Jin'ichirō
                              Group Head: Fujisawa Sōshichirō

Chikuma Prefecture Takato Administrative
Office

[Marginal note]
On August 2nd, regarding the summons, Group Head Sōshichirō accompanied [the petitioner] and appeared [at the office], where they were informed that the chōdai role was granted exemption.

On July 19th, Kurokōchi Jin'ichirō brought [this document] and was told to submit the retirement petition the following day, the 20th, which was accordingly submitted.

I respectfully submit this written petition.
Regarding my personal matter: I have been appointed to serve as group head (kumigashira) and have been performing these duties, but years have passed, and there are many troublesome matters, plus business conditions are poor and I am in financial difficulty. Therefore, I humbly request that I be pardoned from this group head position. Above.

Shinmachi
Meiji 5 (Mizunoe-saru year), August　Petitioner: Ikegami Kyuji
Group Head: Komatsu Saiji  
Town Representative: Ikegami Iwamon
Takato
Administrative Office

The above was submitted on the 12th.

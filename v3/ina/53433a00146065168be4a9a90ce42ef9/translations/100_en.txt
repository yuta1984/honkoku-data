I humbly submit this written petition
As the weather this year has been favorable, we wish to hold
a children's lion dance performance for religious entertainment for one day only on July 3rd,
performed by traveling performers.
Therefore, I humbly submit this petition.
I humbly request that the above be approved. The above
Meiji 5, Year of the Water Monkey (1872), 6th month　　Hokoji-machi
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Petitioner: Saitō Gorō
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Group Head: Akabane Zenzō
Takato
　Administrative Office

As petitioned above, I hereby affix the official seal. The above

[Written in red ink]
"Limited to one day
Approved"

　　　　　　　　　Town Elder
　　　　　　　　　　Ikegami Kinmon
　　　　　　　　　Same position
　　　　　　　　　　Ikegami Shōtarō
　　　　　　　　　Same position, apprentice
　　　　　　　　　　Ikegami Hon'ei

On June 29th, Group Head Zenzō came, so the official seal was applied and handed to the town official,
who submitted it to the Administrative Office. Group Head
Zenzō came to report that approval was granted as shown above.

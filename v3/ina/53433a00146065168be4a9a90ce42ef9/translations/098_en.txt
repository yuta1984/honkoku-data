Meiji 5, Year of the Water Monkey (1872), June    Ikegami Kinmon
Takato
  Administrative Office

  I humbly submit this written petition
                                    Regarding myself
I have been appointed as an apprentice town elder and have received official rice stipend,
and have continued to serve since the transfer of authority. Under these circumstances,
I wish to work diligently, but due to illness, I humbly request
that I be relieved of this official position. The above.

Meiji 5, Year of the Water Monkey (1872), June    Ikegami Hon'ei

Takato
  Administrative Office
Three people came to present and submit the above petition.

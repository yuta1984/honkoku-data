Memorandum

One item: 8 to 8 shō of rice, from the 1st day of the 10th month to the last day of the 12th month
　　　　　　　　Total of 88 days counting large and small months

The above is stipend rice for the horse attendants of this town, distributed from the rice tax collection from Kawashimo village for the Year of the Sheep. We have certainly received it and record this as confirmation.

Meiji 4, Year of the Metal Sheep, 12th month　　Post Station
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Head Administrator
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Ikegami Shōtarō

Takatō Prefecture Office

[Written in red ink] Written at the town meeting place on the 7th day of the 12th month, submitted to the prefecture office on the following 8th day. Submitted through the intermediary of Mr. Satō Ruiji on the 9th day.

The bottom of the bay of (Ya) consists of marble, sand, and muddy soil. The Mediterranean Sea also has many aquatic animals as well as shellfish and fish. In the Mediterranean, water flows and pours out from underground so extensively that there are places where it is like river water flowing into the sea. The harbor of Mio-U is an example of this.

The Baltic Sea (Eastern Sea) has three straits that connect with the North Sea (Noord Sea). Although enormous amounts of water flow in through these straits, it does not increase in height, same as the Mediterranean Sea. When examined at depths of 5 fathoms, there are indeed outflow channels, and in deep places the force of water outflow is greatest.

The Indian Ocean does not have uniform coloring. Around the Maldive Islands, the sea color is black. In the vicinity of Cape Corrientes, it is red in color, similar to the Red Sea. The Banda Sea is white and gleaming in color. In the Persian Gulf around Barala, there appear to be freshwater springs. Also, this sea constantly has violent waves, like the Cape of Good Hope and the Sea of Japan.

Looking at the deposit amounts of these banks, deposits handled during the year were 205,697,064 yen, withdrawals were 200,784,135 yen, and the balance at the end of last year was 31,465,544 yen. Also, the loan amount at year-end was 12,609,172 yen. Last year's clearing house exchange volume was 100,601 bills totaling 34,772,599 yen, but dishonored bills numbered 63, totaling 17,383 yen.

Additionally, there is 1 mutual financing company head office and 1 branch office with 7,489 accounts. There are also 16 industrial cooperatives conducting credit business with 6,301 members, 1 public pawnshop, and 28 private pawnshops.

Furthermore, regarding postal savings in the city, last year there were 294,708 deposit accounts totaling 3,682,081 yen, with 12,465 new depositors. The number of withdrawal accounts during the year was 116,230, totaling 4,143,541 yen.

Next, the warehousing businesses within the city are four companies: Toyo Warehouse Co., Ltd. branch, Markei Tokai Warehouse Co., Ltd., Soushin Warehouse Partnership, and Kyoeki Transport Warehouse Co., Ltd., with 47 warehouse buildings, 71 bay doors, and a total floor area of 13,687 square meters. During Showa 9 (1934), incoming storage was 574,555 items worth 18,311,650 yen, and outgoing storage was 529,121 items worth 18,091,355 yen, with year-end inventory of 128,552 items worth 3,255,689 yen. 　　　　　　　　　7. Industrial Organizations
Within the city, there are the Toyohashi Chamber of Commerce and Industry, the Municipal Agricultural Association, the Commerce and Industry Association, and various other organizations and unions that strive to guide and encourage agriculture, industry, and commerce. They make continuous efforts in developing industrial facilities, mediating the expansion of sales channels for products, encouraging participation in exhibitions, competitive shows, and trade fairs, conducting research and studies, providing Q&A services, holding training sessions and lectures, and operating various other facilities related to industrial promotion.

This is because this region originally has no lodestone, and since it is generally clear except during the rainy season, they distinguish directions by terrain during the day and by stars at night. ○In the autumn of the twenty-seventh year, the rainy season arrived. Therefore we remained in the fort. ○Having gained Friday from two or three years before, my labor was greatly reduced. Together we cultivated fields and also obtained large quantities of dried grapes. ○Friday dug a small canal to draw in seawater and moor the boat. However, the bottom of the boat remained on land so it would not get wet. He also made a roof structure with tree branches above it to prevent rain, and as we reached around the eleventh and twelfth months, we waited for the time to set sail.

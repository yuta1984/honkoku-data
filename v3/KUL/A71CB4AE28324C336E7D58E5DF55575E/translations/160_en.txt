　　　"Sandwich Islands" Record 《Written note: Boshin 4th month, 10th day, Mr. Ta? and Mr. Tani both read and added to this record》
　The "Sandwich Islands" are said to have thirteen or fourteen islands.
　They were discovered during "Cook's" voyage. Their products are almost
　the same as "Tahiti" island, being both scarce and abundant. The climate is also quite
　similar. The natives are of the same race, but their physique is relatively short
and
　their skin color is even darker. The facial features show no uniform appearance across the entire island group. The lan-
　guage probably has the same origin as that of "Tahiti" and other South Pacific islands.
　Even frequent human conversation is gentle, relaxed, and unhurried. The natives devote their efforts to the
　manufacture of implements, and their sophistication surpasses that of "Tahiti."
　That is, in their precise woven mats ("mats"), clothing, vessels, weapons, fishing gear, etc.

In the Opium War, [Britain] sent expeditionary forces deep into the interior to conquer China. The British queen is a widow and is wise and talented. At that time the crown prince was young, being sixteen years of age, so he assumed the position of regent.

　　　Record of the United States of America 《Marginal note: In the sixth month of Boshin year, read and supplemented by Taniseи》

Now, when Westerners first settled people on the eastern shores of North America two hundred years ago, there was nothing to be seen except deep forests and great marshes. They cleared a corner of that vast wilderness of reed plains, built houses and dwellings to settle there, and made their living through hunting and such. Thereafter, mutual conflicts never ceased. Ah! From such humble beginnings, with people settling in this vast wilderness, to produce such a mighty great nation - is this not the height of wonder? Its territory encompasses land extending from Canada to the shores of Mexico, from the Atlantic Ocean to the Pacific Ocean. The area is 90,000 square ri. Great rivers flow through the territory

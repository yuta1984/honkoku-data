Geographical Description of Foreign Lands

○ According to the guest, "San'ina" consists of nine islands, two of which are uninhabited. The island king resides in "Sohō," they say. All of these accounts contradict Western explanations. For now, I shall compare these accounts with official maps and attach translated texts at the end of the volume for future reference.

○ "Ōihei" Island: The guest calls it "Owahe" or "Wahei." It is the largest among all the islands. There is a great mountain, and initially we anchored at its "Hedo" port.

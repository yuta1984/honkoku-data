　　　Miscellaneous Records
〇The language of "England" is favored and used by various foreign countries, and there is no nation that does not understand it. This is just like how the various provinces of our country use Edo dialect (as a common language).
〇In our house we keep a folding fan on which a map of "Russia" is painted. When we show this to guests, they read it and say:
　　"You-ha-a-i-wo-to-u-u-a, Sha-a-fu-i-, Na
　　A-bo-u-, Na-a-sho-n-ga-u, Gai-ka-u-"
　They say the meaning is difficult to understand.

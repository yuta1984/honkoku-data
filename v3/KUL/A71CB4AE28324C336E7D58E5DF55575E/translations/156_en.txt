Under the sovereignty of Britain, Holland, Scotland, and France first cleared the wilderness and settled people there, even building cities in the interior. However, those expelled by Western religions and the extremely poor and destitute continued to emigrate in succession.

In those coastal regions, the gentlemen who followed the law paid taxes and cultivated and tilled the land at will. The native peoples did not violently seize those fields and farms either. Most conducted thorough negotiations and engaged in peaceful buying and selling. Therefore, the population increase of this continent year after year is truly astonishing. Also, relying on the guidance of officials from England, Scotland, Georgia, Wisconsin, France, Spain, Holland and other places, the people of the states learned various skills such as agriculture, commerce, and navigation.

Initially under the rule of British governors 《marginal note: Government》, there were approximately thirteen states, all governed according to British legal systems and prohibitions. When the British disrupted commercial law and increased tax burdens, the people all became greatly resentful. In 1776 《marginal note: Anei 5, year of hinoe-saru》, they finally became an independent nation. The reason they achieved independence was that they borrowed the support of strong nations like France and greatly defeated the British forces. By 1782 《marginal note: Tenmei 2, year of mizunoe-tora》, Britain also gave up hope of keeping them as a dependency as before. From this time, the national power flourished vigorously.

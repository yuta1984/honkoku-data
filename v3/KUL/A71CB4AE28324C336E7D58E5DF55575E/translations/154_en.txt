〇Russia has discord with Holland. The cause is that Russia frequently entrusts Holland to request trade with Japan on their behalf, but Holland gives vague and evasive answers, so [Russia] is angered that the matter does not come to fruition.

〇When American ships approach Holland, they invariably open fire to drive them away. Therefore, when Dutch ships come to America, they immediately fire cannons to destroy them in retaliation. It is also said that Holland's actions are at the instigation of England. This too may be England's intention to monopolize trade profits for their country alone.

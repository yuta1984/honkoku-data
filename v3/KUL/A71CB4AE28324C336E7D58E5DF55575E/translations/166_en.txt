The length is six feet, thickness seven to eight inches or more. For roof tiles, the wealthy use copper plates, while the poor use wooden boards.

○For snow, "Russia" has the most. "Kamchatka" has the greatest amount, and while "Okhotsk" has severe cold, the snow is actually less. Even when accumulation is severe, it only reaches about six feet.

○In Russian customs, when guests arrive, both host and guest walk around indoors conversing together. This prevents numbness in the feet and contributes to health maintenance. Women do not take walks, so the bottom of their chairs is arc-shaped, like the bottom of ships in our country. Those seated constantly rock themselves and do not stop.

○When using leather pipes to draw water, such wooden buckets are added to the tip. Curved pipes are fitted into the holes below, with one end of the pipe joined to the leather tube. If concerned about shortness, additional curved pipes are added to connect the leather tubes. The joints are threaded with copper and iron. The whaling ships had over thirty of these.

○In "Sasuitchi" there are prostitute-like women. Though the island women are promiscuous by nature, the laws are strict, and if women commit adultery, they are punished with sentences like becoming government servants and are put to work. The same applies to men.

○For female beauty, "Malay" ranks first, "England" second. All English people, both men and women, are beautiful, but their character is considerably inferior. They revere red color for hair and pupils. The women are obedient.

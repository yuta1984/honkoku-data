In the meantime, as the imperial envoy Watanabe Geki withdrew from the Kokura-Wakamatsu area and proceeded to Nagasaki, all three domains decided to withdraw to their respective territories and station only the officials responsible for repelling Chinese ships.

Item 1: On May 19th, before the complete withdrawal from the Wakamatsu area, reports came from Jishima garrison commander Hagiwara Sadayū, Ōshima garrison commander Kojima Gozaemon, and Nishimura Yaroku stating that Chinese ships had been spotted intermittently at a distance of about ten ri offshore from Jishima and Ōshima since around the 6th and 7th of this month.

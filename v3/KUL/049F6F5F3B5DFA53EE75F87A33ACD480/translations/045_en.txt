1. The aforementioned Chinese ship became invisible from the 4th day, as reported from Jishima. From around the 10th day, it again drifted faintly toward the northwest direction, and afterward gradually approached and appeared to be conducting offshore trading. Some days it would go out to distant waters, and at night it appeared to approach and solicit offshore trading. While this was happening, from noon on the 21st day of the 4th month, the weather cleared and the sea became calm, so from evening they boarded ships and sent out reconnaissance vessels. All personnel dispersed and from noon boarded and ascended.

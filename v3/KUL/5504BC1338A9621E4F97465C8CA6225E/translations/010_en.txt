1. During my stay in this place, I went fishing on the beach with a person who seemed to be the master. Since there are no fishermen in this area, there are many fish, and we caught large fish. Indeed, I saw two or three fish that I had never seen before. Rather, the fish had soft flesh and did not seem to taste very good.

1. Around the first day of the eighth month of the Tiger year, due to wind and rain, the area between San José and San Diego where large trees grew thickly was completely washed away by flooding. Some houses in the village were also blown down and damaged, and crops in the fields such as corn and sugar cane were all blown down. It was not an unusual great storm, and they said such things had never been remembered in that land.

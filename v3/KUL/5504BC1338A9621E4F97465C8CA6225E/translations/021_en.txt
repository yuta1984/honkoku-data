"Left Page"
　　　　　　　Kii Province, Muro District, Kuchikumano
　　　　　　　Susami-ura, Einari-maru sailor
　　　　　　　　　　　　　Yaichi
The above person drifted to foreign countries in the year of the Tiger and was sent back by a Chinese ship in the year of the Snake. After examination, there was no proselytizing of the Christian faith and nothing suspicious was found, so he is being returned to his home province without issue. However, he is not to reside in other provinces and should engage in appropriate occupation in his home province as ordered. If this castaway should die, the Accounting Magistrates in Nagasaki should be notified. Also, regarding the clothing and other items given to him in foreign countries and China being allowed to be kept, this has been conveyed and understood. The above.

1. Satoran has a similar climate, but Satoran feels much less hot than San José, perhaps because the houses are built closely together.

1. While I was staying in this place, I saw fireworks launched around September. They were arranged in the shapes of human figures or flowers, and were quite magnificent.

1. There are six wholesale houses (tonya) in this place, and all cargo from ships arriving from various places is unloaded at these wholesale houses, from which it is then sold to various merchants. I do not know the details of these wholesale houses, but they are said to be branch stores from foreign countries. One or two British ships are always moored at the harbor entrance, and in addition, merchant ships from Spain, France, Portugal, Russia, etc. occasionally enter the port.

【Left page】
Among them, Russian ships frequently enter the port, and since this place has abundant rice, they take rice back with them. Regarding Dutch ships entering the port, this is unclear.

1. The townhouses have white walls, and goods for sale are written in horizontal script (Western letters), which serves as signage.

1. There are also two theaters, large and small. The small theater is located on land owned by the master where I was staying, so I frequently went to see performances. Both large and small are street theaters, and they take a five-day break each month. The theater setup has a stage at the front, with seats arranged in front, and the back area is built up in tiers. The musicians are lined up below in front of the stage, with flutes, drums, etc.

A ship from Batavia (Java) was crossing to Nagasaki when it encountered unfavorable winds in the open sea, was damaged, and drifted ashore. Accordingly, at Nagasaki, we consulted with Kusakabe Tamba-no-kami and Watanabe Geki, and it was decided to send guards along and transport [the ship and crew] to Nagasaki. Since this ship will pass through your domain, we are sending this urgent dispatch to inform you of this matter.
With all due respect, we humbly report.

Memorandum to be given to Miki Magoroku and Yoshida Sehei
Memorandum

1. Regarding the Batavia ship that drifted ashore at Kishi Bay this time and is being sent to Nagasaki: for security purposes, both of you along with physician Shiramizu Yōtaku, ship captain, walking inspector, attendants, etc. are to be assigned and dispatched. During the voyage, maintain vigilant watch without negligence, and ensure that proper decorum and tranquility are maintained throughout.

1. As per previous regulations, place two Chinese men as hostages on our ships, arrange provisions during the voyage, assign guards as usual, and ensure they are securely guarded with reliable crew members.
　Addendum: When the Chinese hostages fall ill, Shiramizu Yōtaku should provide medical treatment.

1. During the voyage, you must not provide grain, water, firewood, etc. when requested by the Chinese ship. However, if sea conditions are unfavorable, the voyage takes many days, and the Chinese appear to be in distress, only water and firewood may be provided in small amounts.

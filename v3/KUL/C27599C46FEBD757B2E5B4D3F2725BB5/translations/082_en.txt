Written document from Kusakabe Tamba-no-kami-dono
　　　　Oral statement
　Regarding the matter of transporting the Chinese ship that drifted ashore in the bay within your domain, as you have requested, the mast is damaged and navigation is also difficult. If you also wish to request transport to this location, we will transport it according to standard precedent. If any unusual circumstances arise thereafter, we will consult with Geki's office and act according to their instructions. That is all.
　　　　August 25th

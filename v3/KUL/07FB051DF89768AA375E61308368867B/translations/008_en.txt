How does the prosperity of Guandong and Shandong compare to Nanjing?　　Narusawa
I don't know which is superior.　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Wang
How many zhang long are the largest merchant ships of your country?　Narusawa
The largest ships are over twelve zhang.　　　　　　　　　　Wang
How many sailors do you employ?　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Narusawa
Over thirty men. 《Marginal note: I have heard that the number of sailors is unlimited》　Wang
For ships carrying about a thousand koku, how many sailors?　　Narusawa
Ten in winter, nine in summer.　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Wang
What kind of wood is the mast of your current ship made from?　Narusawa
Old cedar wood from Jiangnan.　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Wang

【Left Page】
What are the famous products of your region? Are the oranges of Jiangnan and the perch of Songjiang still called rare products today?　　　　　　　Narusawa
Oranges are from Dongting Mountain, and the fish of Songjiang are named for their excellent taste. The common product of Jiangnan people is cotton.　Wang
Is your county close to the sea and does it have the benefits of fish and salt?　Narusawa
Fish and salt come from Xijiang. There are few fish in Jiangnan.　Wang
In which region of your country are tigers and leopards most numerous?　Narusawa
Only in Guandong and in Sizhou, in steep mountains and deep valleys.　Wang

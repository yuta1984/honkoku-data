【Right Page】
From Tasuke in Hizen to Matsushima: 18 ri

19th day: Though the weather was good, the tide timing was poor, so waited until around 4 o'clock, then departed Tasuke Bay at that time. With favorable tailwinds, arrived at Matsushima around 7:30.

From Matsushima in Hizen to Nagasaki: 13 ri

20th day: Good weather. Departed Matsushima around 6 o'clock, arrived at Nagasaki around 8:30.
　Lord Matsudaira Hizen-no-kami provided 46 rowing boats and service vessels, plus 2 guard ships.

21st day: Due to some matters requiring attention by the local magistrates, the handover of the Chinese people was postponed today.

22nd day: Good weather. Regarding the handover of the Chinese ship and Chinese people, today around 4 o'clock, several of our people boarded the Chinese ship without exception, and with the presence of officials from their side, the handover was completed without incident.

【Left Page】
23rd day: Good weather. Made a tour of the Dutch quarters, Chinese quarters, and various Chinese temples.

24th day: Good weather. Carrying the remaining reward money to be given to those in charge of duties at this place, went to meet with the local magistrates.

25th day: Good weather. Though we were supposed to depart, due to many official duties, the officials who had gone ashore boarded the escort ship around 7 o'clock. As night had already fallen, remained anchored at the same place.

26th day: Though the weather was good, due to strong west winds, when the winds calmed down a little around 7 o'clock, departed from the same place. Traveled about 1 ri and arrived at a place called Kamisaki.

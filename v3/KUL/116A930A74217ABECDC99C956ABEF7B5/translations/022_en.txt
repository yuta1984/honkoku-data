　Being seen through by the Chinese ships would also be problematic. When the weather is calm at night,
　dispatch boats, and if the location of the Chinese ships is known, muskets should be fired at them.
　After all, loading with shot and firing near the ships would be effective.
One It is not necessarily the case that Chinese ships must be completely destroyed, so
　approaching too closely where the ship's condition might be discerned is
　inadvisable. Even without boarding strictly according to monthly duty schedules, it should be acceptable.
　Roughly estimating and firing should be appropriate.
One After firing muskets, if Chinese ships rush out and retreat, they should be pursued
　and fired upon to some extent. Long pursuits are unnecessary.

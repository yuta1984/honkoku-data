Amano Yozaemon, Tan Anzaemon, naval head Matsumoto Shuden, Matsumoto 《strikethrough: Jūemon｜Ichirōemon》, Miyamoto Dentayū, harbor magistrates Sawaki Tōemon and Ōshio Jihei, cannon role supervisors Hayashi Bunemon, Usuki Yaemon, and Wakamatsu Jintayū - detailed orders regarding the repelling of Chinese ships and preparations were issued to all these individuals. There are estimates for the number of ships both large and small, harbor boats, sailors, and harbor crew, as well as ship number calculations. First, the method of repelling with large cannons was ordered, and [the lord] wished to hear the procedures for firing in detail. After the preparations and ship arrangements were completed, Lord Shikibu personally wrote detailed instructions regarding firing methods and took charge of them. Furthermore, orders were issued to deploy about four or five boats per ship unit for large cannon firing, and ten cannon operators on five small fast boats were initially appointed.

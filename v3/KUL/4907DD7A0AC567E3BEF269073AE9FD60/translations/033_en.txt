【Right Page】
There are pearls, and I saw shells with pearls still inside them arranged in rows in a place like the tokonoma (alcove) of the lodging where I was staying.

First, I observed them printing books at this place. It appeared they were using horizontal script, combining cast type pieces made one character at a time to print.

First, throughout "America there are no shrines or temples, and there appear to be no Buddhist or Shinto practices.

First, I do not see many high mountains. All the mountains appear to have gently rounded shapes.

First, during my stay in "America, it rained only once in about two hundred days.

【Left Page】
When I inquired about this, I was told that it rains only about twice a year.

First, "America is an extremely warm country. April and September-October correspond to June-July in Japan, and the four months of May, June, July, and August are extremely hot - even walking on sand in leather shoes during the day makes the soles of one's feet hot. They use umbrellas made of silk stretched like parasols. Those who work outside do so only in the morning and evening, not going out during the day.

First, in that place, those who appear to be officials all wear swords, one each fastened at their waist.

First, all the women pierce copper through both earlobes, with pearls beneath the copper

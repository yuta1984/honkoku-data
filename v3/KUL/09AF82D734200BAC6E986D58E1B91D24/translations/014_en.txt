On the same 15th day, the three officials in charge of Chinese ship affairs from the three domains - Ōto Rokuzaemon, Watanabe Rōzaemon (military officer), and Nojima Kaname (senior retainer of Kokura) - agreed to depart from Edo together, and arrived in Osaka on the 27th of the same month. The three immediately consulted together and made inquiries to Osaka Town Magistrate Suzuki Hida-no-kami, whereupon Hida-no-kami

Item: Lord Rokuzaemon, as he was told on April 10th last by Lord Ukon-shōgen's household in Edo, immediately sent word about dispatching a pursuit ship to Fukuoka. On May 7th, in the sea off Bōshū Province, he encountered Lord Rokuzaemon's pursuit ship, boarded it, and headed for Fukuoka. On the 9th, when they had traveled five or six ri by main ship from Shimonoseki, the weather suddenly changed and became a great storm, and in a life-and-death situation they barely managed to reach the direction of Suō Province.

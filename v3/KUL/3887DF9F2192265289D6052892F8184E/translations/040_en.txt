　　　　　The Condition of Emiyo Island

Regarding the people: Men cut their hair and wear cotton leggings, and dress in a mixture of cotton, white fabric, and gauze. Women have long hair wound up on their heads and wear long garments. This island is in America, and everything is similar to Oahu (Hawaii).

Regarding food: Through ship provisions, they do not know about the local food.

Regarding house construction: They have thatched roofs with pillars set directly in the ground, the row of houses is poor, and no merchant houses were observed. This is a place where whaling ships from various countries take on firewood and water.

No castle fortifications or temple-like structures were observed.

Regarding the land: It is generally open with much flat land, and there are also mountains. There are coconut palms and other unfamiliar trees.

They arrived at the dependency "Kamchatka." They stayed at this place for about thirty days. A subordinate official of the local magistrate boarded the Russian ship from here, and departed from the same place around August 5th of the previous year. Heading south or southwest, around the 28th or 29th of the same month, they encountered a storm off Ryukyu, where high waves crashed into the ship causing damage, but they managed to endure it and arrived off Nagasaki on September 6th of the same year.

This is what they reported. However, since they did not venture out carelessly to other places during their stay in foreign countries, the castaways stated that they do not know the details of various matters. The above.

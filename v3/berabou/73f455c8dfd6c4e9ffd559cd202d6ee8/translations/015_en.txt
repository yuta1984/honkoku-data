"Chūgi Ōboshi Suikoden" (Loyal Great Stars Water Margin) by Santō-an Master, 5 volumes
This book uses the puppet theater play "Kanadehon Chūshingura" as its basis,
founded on the "Taiheiki" and following the style of "Suikoden," earnestly depicting
the virtues of loyal retainers, filial children, righteous men, and
chaste women. It is a book written in kana that is easy for children to read.

"Picture Book of the Twenty-Four Examples of Filial Piety," 3 volumes
The Twenty-Four Examples of Filial Piety commonly circulated in the world are hiragana reading books with many errors.

commentary, making it a primer for young students.

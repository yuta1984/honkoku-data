Since Tokimasa had long known that the son of the moxa shop was Yoritomo, he arranged the marriage ceremony with Masako and rejoiced. Now, Princess Masako's illness was quickly cured because Nagamochi Doi had applied three masu worth of moxa, so the moxa shop was granted the three masu (升) as their family crest. Additionally, they were allowed to take the character "平" (Taira) as a reward.

Furthermore, the fact that the three masu of moxa treatment was applied without boredom was because Yamanouchi-ya Sonbei had been eagerly reading popular books (kusazōshi), so Sonbei, having the same merit as Doi, was granted the three-scale crest received from Benzaiten of Enoshima as a reward, and was told to make it his permanent family crest.

For us unworthy ones to receive the scale-shaped crest and the character "平" is more than we deserve, and we are deeply grateful.

Artist and author: Koikawa Harumachi

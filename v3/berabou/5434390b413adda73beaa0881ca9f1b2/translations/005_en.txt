Preface to Flower Sumo Wrestling
People gaze upon and compose poetry about only the unblemished moon,
and cherish and celebrate flowers only when they are in full bloom.
This is the way of all people in the world.
Beautiful women also do not
exceed two in number. The only pleasure lies
in that one style,
that one style alone.
Rosei's four seasons,
even Eikura, who determined them to be before his eyes...

Grown weary of my usual long-winded droppings, I have composed this trivial scribbling so as to soften the hard turds with the slipperiness of loose ones, and to pass on to the world

the manner of relieving oneself—yet even those who call themselves "connoisseurs of the quarter" (tsūkyaku), without ever peering between my thighs, how could they ever know the breadth of my fundament? Ah, is not this dung a wonder to behold?

Written in the First Month of Tenmei 7, the Year of the Sheep (Hinoto-Hitsuji).

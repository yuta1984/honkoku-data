Now, Shirakakushi (White Concealer) practiced mountain asceticism with Single-sheet Print, and things settled down peacefully. However, since Red Book and Black Book's evil scheme this time was crude work and the book binding was poor, they cheerfully claimed there were warps in the ceiling. They stood up straight, punched holes with an awl, and had the ceiling repaired - it became like the busy year-end preparations.

Now, the origin of this disturbance was that conspiracy by Reading Books, Lecture Books, and Rhythm Books from Hachimonji became apparent, saying "Such old-fashioned things should be put together with Chinese chest pictures and used as backing paper. All the illustrated books should have scribbles drawn on their faces, have their binding eaten through by mice, and become backing paper for folding screens."

Blue books please people's eyes without distinction of social class, are clever in flattery, and as vessels possessing sophistication, they seek out contemporary trends and manage to survive. Having no oversight even as thin as a hair strand, they stand shoulder to shoulder with their companions in bean-throwing ceremonies, and with the patronage of ladies and children, they concentrate their efforts on devising new editions. Yet without becoming arrogant, they still hold monthly gatherings using recycled paper.

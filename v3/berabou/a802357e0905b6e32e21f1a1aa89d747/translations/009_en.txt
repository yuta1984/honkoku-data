The blue book, knowing the logic of the "sister-pillar hiding" technique and single-sheet illustrations, hopes to eventually unite them as one. He sets out to secretly discuss this with a sharebon (wit book) he's been close to for some time. On the way, he meets Ishizuri and they decide to go together to visit Kannon.

Among them, Tome appears to be from the northern provinces.

Artist: Kitao Masanobu

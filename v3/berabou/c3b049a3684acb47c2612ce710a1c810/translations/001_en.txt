**New Yoshiwara Saiken-awase, Volume I (Upper)**

[Formal Title: *Seirō Bijin Awase Sugatakagami* ("A Mirror of Beautiful Women of the Pleasure Quarters Compared")]

Illustrations: Katsukawa Shunshō and Kitao Shigemasa
Publisher: Edo Shorin (Yamazaki Kinbei and Tsutaya Jūzaburō)
Published: Anei 5 (1776)

※ This is a illustrated collection depicting the everyday appearances of courtesans in the Yoshiwara pleasure district. Though the complete work comprises three volumes, only the upper volume (Volume I) is held in this collection. The newly renamed courtesan Segawa makes an appearance (frame 4). It is said that this very book was the thick volume handed by Tsutaya Jūzaburō to Segawa upon her ransom and release from the quarter.

Preface to the Household Expense Ledger for All Things

Economy and miserliness are like narcissus and green onion. Though their forms are similar, miserliness has a detestable stench, while economy has a lovable flower that does not wither even in frost and snow.
To finally meet the time of "one yang returning and good fortune coming" is also due to the prudent management based on the virtue of the unwithering flower, and drawing eyes and nose on household goods and adding hands and feet to them represents the economical conclusion of this expense ledger. First, I record this at the beginning of the New Year.

Spring of Kansei 8, Year of the Dragon　Written by Jippensha Ikku

Now the evil balls that
escaped from within the jeweled box
had until now been
sealed by the kyogen monk
and huddled in the corner of the box,
but unexpectedly through Taronosuke's
delusion of worldly desires
they left the interior of the box,
and to bring
misfortune upon that
Taronosuke's person,
they gathered many
subordinate balls and held
various consultations—how frightening!

〽First, the eye-ball shall find
beautiful women
and corrupt the right-mindedness ball,
make the gold balls of the treasury
dance to our tune,
use the quality balls
on the abacus balls
to confuse
the bookkeeping,
put a noose
around the parent's neck,
make the mind like
a fart ball, string up
the gold balls of relatives,
and if we can even turn
the true heart ball into
a fake ball,
then it will be ours.
This is quite
an amusing plan,
all fluffy and light.

〽However,
　the wife's chaste

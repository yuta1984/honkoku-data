【Running title】Fourth Edition　　　One

Evil gems and good gems
are one name, yet also not one
name. For example,
good gems are like the
god of fortune, while evil gems are like
the god of poverty.
People who practice
compassion and good deeds toward others
have their descendants prosper,
lacking nothing,
eating their three meals
a day abundantly.
Though of humble
station, they have
plenty of
money.
The father
cherishes the mother,
the mother
respects and
honors
the father,
and their children
show filial
piety
to their parents.
This is that
household's
good gem.

In verse:
　〽With honesty held alongside sincerity
　　　The good gem of rice eaten thrice daily

【Illustration, on the face of the good gem】Lucky

Also, what is called an evil gem neglects
work for the sake of ease, drinks heavily,
thinking that even beggars
eat rice meals, so money
is just a
side matter.
The father
treats the mother
harshly,
the mother calls the father
a scoundrel,
and their child
shows unfilial behavior
to the parents,
finally
putting a rope
around the parent's
neck.
This is that household's
evil gem. If one
considers this matter,
one should know that
good gems and evil gems
exist within each person's own heart.

In verse:
　〽The quarrelsome couple's fight like soba noodles—
　　　Though struck and served, the evil gem won't take it

Tarosuke, separated from his wife's virtuous influence,
becomes increasingly dominated by evil impulses,
and now with nowhere to place his heart, wandering
the streets in confusion, just as he was about to
starve to death,
the kyōgen monk
suddenly appears and, while
bringing together his mother and
wife, instructs him.

〽Now, now, Tarosuke, why do you fear?
You never would have imagined this, would you? This is
what I call my vegetable kyōgen
performance. Because I saw through
that the resting place of your heart
was wrong, I made the rice-cooking
servant O-San appear as the courtesan
Akadama,
and even the teahouses and boat inns
were all my
accomplices. This is what's called
a heart substitute.
When you thought of her as the rice-cooking
O-San, there was no romance,
but when you thought of her as the courtesan
Akadama, you lost touch with reality.
All this happened because
your eyes of delusion
were clouded.
The money from selling your wife
was also from your mother's
purse. So without causing real
harm, I let you dream for fifty years.
This is the true teaching of reformed
heart-learning. Unfortunately, using the rope
of the five virtues of benevolence and righteousness,
I disciplined your
true heart, and
you're still the same
master of Urashimaya. Cherish
the eternal
treasure box of matrimony
　　　and
prosper with children
until the seventh
generation.
How auspicious—do you
understand
now?

〽All the evil
spirits that had
attached themselves

body

　disappearing
back into the original
treasure
box—how
　　　　　　mysterious

Thus, though the spirits of lust,
greed, and other
varieties may
change form,

all
evil spirits

good person—
how
　grateful

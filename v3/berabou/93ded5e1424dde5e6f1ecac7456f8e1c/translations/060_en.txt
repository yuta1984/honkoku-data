【Seventeen】Rules of Abstinence and Purification for Women

1. For the impurity of menstruation, abstain for seven days. From the tenth day onward, one may visit the gods.
For the impurity of childbirth, the father abstains for seven days, the mother for thirty-five days. From the eighth day onward, those who enter and exit the same place, sit together, or share the same fire should wait two nights and three days before visiting the gods.

1. For the impurity of miscarriage, up to three months is the same as menstruation. If it reaches four or five months, the father should abstain from religious ceremonies for five days, the mother for ten days.

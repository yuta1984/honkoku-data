1. Mole removal medicine: Mix ash and lime in equal parts, add
   a little glutinous rice, and let it sit for two to three days until it hardens.
   Dissolve this with purslane juice, apply to the mole, and cover
   with paper. It will come off within two to three days.

1. Removing warts: Depending on the size of the wart, wrap paper around
   a brush handle to about one inch in length, place it against the wart,
   and light the tip of the paper. This will burn down to the base of the wart.
   Doing this two to three times will cause the wart to wrinkle and fall off overnight.

1. Birthmark removal medicine: In June, gather goosefoot, char it black, and mix with lime
   in equal parts. Put in a jar, add water to cover, and add glutinous
   
   rice. In summer, dry in the sun; in winter, place by the stove. When the rice
   dissolves, scrape the birthmark with a bamboo spatula to draw blood,
   then apply the above medicine and cover with paper.

1. Medicine for rough skin:
   Mung beans (5 go), talc (1 ryo), white aconite (1 ryo), angelica (1 ryo)
   Sandalwood (1 ryo), nardostachys (1 ryo), borneol (2 monme)
   Powder these seven ingredients and apply to the body.

1. Medicine for chapped skin: Crush quince fruit and apply the juice. Also,
   applying vinegar water is good. Also, washing with Japanese pepper leaves is good.

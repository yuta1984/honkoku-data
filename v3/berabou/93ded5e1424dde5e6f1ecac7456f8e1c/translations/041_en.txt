1. On eating makuwa melon: Cut it lengthwise into four pieces, add a toothpick, and serve it on a plate. Take the toothpick, scrape out and discard the seeds from inside the melon, pierce it with the toothpick, and eat it.

1. On drinking tea: Take what has been placed on the tea stand with your right hand and drink, then take it with your left hand to set it down below. A visiting maid will take it and place it back on the stand. When the tea is hot, you may sip it, but you must never swing the tea bowl around.

㊄ The order of dishes and food for the wedding night feast, with illustrations

First the hand-appetizer (tegake), second the three-tiered stand (sanpō), third the presentation course (hikiwatashi), next the padded soup (watairi no suimono), next the pounded miso, next the rice cake soup (zōni - the rice cakes should be cut rectangular cakes), next the fin soup (hire no suimono - this is sea bream), next the kawai-o soup (this is fowl), next the formal feast tray arranged in seven-five-three style. All of these are shown in the diagram on the left. Between each of these courses on the right, both groom and bride drink three rounds of sake each. The food is only pretended to be eaten. It must not actually be consumed.

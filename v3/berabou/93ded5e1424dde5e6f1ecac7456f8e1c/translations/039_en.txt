1. On drinking hot water: You should put down your chopsticks and hold the bowl with your right hand to drink. It is good to leave a little hot water remaining. You must not eat pickled vegetables.

1. On eating rice with hot water poured over it: When eating vegetable hearts, you should put down your chopsticks and receive the rice with your left hand. The same applies when receiving hot water. If there are vegetables in the rice with hot water that would dirty your chopsticks, you must not touch them with your hands.

1. On eating rice porridge: You must never pour soup over it. Also, you must not stir it.

1. On eating mixed rice: Various chopped ingredients are placed at the front of the meal tray. First take your chopsticks, receive soup with your left hand, add the chopped ingredients, mix them together and eat. You must not leave any chopped ingredients.

1. On eating New Year's soup: Ladies should eat it as is without lifting anything out. If you wish to sip the broth, you should put down your chopsticks, lift the bowl, and sip.

1. On eating glutinous rice: It should be served in small portions on wooden plates. Even if chopsticks are provided, this is not something to be eaten with chopsticks. You should pick it up and eat it with your hands.

1. Rice cakes should be eaten by piercing them with a toothpick. You may also pick them up and eat them with your hands.

1. On eating steamed buns: Take it with your left hand, and pinch and break it with your thumb and index finger of your right hand to eat. If it comes with soup, you should eat it with chopsticks. However, the steamed bun should be divided into four pieces when served.

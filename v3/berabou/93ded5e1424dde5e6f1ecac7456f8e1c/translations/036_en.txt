The adding should be done so that the mouth of the chōshi (sake pot) and the mouth of the hisage (ladle) meet properly.

1. Regarding the act of adding: At any time, one should cross the threshold and add from inside. However, depending on the room, if it is too crowded, it is acceptable to do it across the threshold. In such cases, the hisage should be brought inside to add.

The way to hold the hisage: Hold the middle of the handle with the right hand, and similarly place the left hand under the right hand, overlapping them. Also, hold the rim and outside of the hisage with the thumb, index finger, and middle finger.

Regarding how to return the hisage: If the chōshi returns to the left side, the hisage should return to the right side. When the chōshi returns to the right, the hisage should return to the left.

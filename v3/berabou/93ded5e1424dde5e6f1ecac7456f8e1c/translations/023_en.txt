- Regarding obi (sashes): Silk obi one width wide with large scattered dye patterns, eight shaku in length, tied in front - this is the current fashionable style. According to one's natural waist size, thick or thin, the width of the obi should be appropriate for good appearance. For obi, the tea-colored satin and flower-colored satin that were popular in recent times are refined garments that suit both old and young without being conspicuous. The principle for women's attire is that it should be inconspicuous and modest.

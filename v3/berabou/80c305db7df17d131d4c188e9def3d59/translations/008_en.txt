Becoming husband and wife is due to karmic connections from past lives, not by our own will. Only through very deep connections can people become married couples. Therefore, the husband should show compassion to his wife, and the wife should cherish her husband, without quarreling, ill will, jealousy, or envy, and they should live together harmoniously.

However, people who do not know the true way start marital quarrels over trivial matters, beginning their usual clashing of pestle and mortar, and no longer showing smiling faces. There, inwardly they become angry, their rage does not subside, and they cannot remain calm. This is all because husband and wife are not in harmony. Marital quarrels are bad things even when depicted in pictures. One must be careful, one must be careful.

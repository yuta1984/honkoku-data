Acting wrongfully and trying to stop it is like floating clouds. Even if one becomes a millionaire through such unreasonable acts that go against heavenly principles, if they lack a heart of compassion and good deeds, make their servants thirsty, neglect social obligations, and are so greedy as to hammer even a one-mon coin, the image of a hungry ghost appears in the mirror of their heart. This is called a wealthy hungry ghost or a rich beggar.

On the other hand, even those who are poor and live day to day, if they harbor no particular greed, conduct their business based on honesty, maintain honest poverty and know contentment, the god of fortune is always reflected in the mirror of their heart. Therefore, among the wealthy there are poor people, and among the poor there are wealthy people. This is precisely the difference between knowing contentment and not knowing it. One should understand this.

However, moved by true compassion from ancient events and finding it mortifying to witness the prosperity of the Minamoto clan, he gouged out both his eyes before his lord, became blind, and withdrew to Miyazaki in Hyuga, taking the place name directly as his new name, Hyuga Kōtō.

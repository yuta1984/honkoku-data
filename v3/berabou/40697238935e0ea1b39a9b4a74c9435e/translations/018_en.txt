『About Kuramae and
Market Price Calculations』
Kuramae and Odawara-chō
are both districts where sophisticated people
live. However, not all ten
out of ten people are truly sophisticated.
There are three levels: sophisticated, half-sophisticated,
and unsophisticated.
Half-sophisticated people speak about
market prices, while unsophisticated people
like to talk about splitting costs.
This is what we call "Kuramae
market price splitting."
You should associate with people
according to their respective levels.

Also, there's the "opening square of large jūbako"—
a method of economy when giving gifts:

requiring little expense. The recipient

'On Cotton Trading'
[In illustration]
Stringing
　without stopping
　　Tamagawa village [up to here]
Cotton one tan, that is, two jō and six shaku
in length, when purchased at thirteen monme of silver,
how much per one shaku?
Answer: it comes to five bu each.
By calculation, two jō six shaku equals twenty-six.
Thirteen monme equals thirteen. Even dividing twenty-six by two gives thirteen, so one shaku equals five bu each. This can be known even through mental arithmetic.
Another question: cotton one tan measures
two jō seven shaku five sun by whale measure,
priced at nine monme seven bu. If you take
eight shaku three sun with a metal ruler,
what would the price be? Answer:
that should be seen through a draper's sales records.

○Second Level: Dividing in two - economizing by halving everything

Two-One: The Time for Economy - the heart of making two things into one for economy is precisely at this time

Self's Single Heart - from one's own heart, resolve with single-mindedness to economize without waiting for others' help

Private Heart's Double Heart - if private feelings arise and one becomes selfish, economy cannot stand; one must maintain it without duplicity

Single Life's Vital Point - if there are troublesome dependents, economy cannot be achieved; living alone is vital

Active Heart's Death by Drying - if one's heart becomes too broad and unrestrained, it will later lead to starvation and death; one should be cautious

○Third Level: Three means "scatter" - cutting and dispersing expenses
Childbirth's Difficulties First - starting with the expenses of childbirth, nothing costs more than children; if you have a wife, first send her quickly to her family home

Patience with Courtesans of the Northern Quarter - even if you send your wife away, going to the pleasure quarters is even worse; you should endure; courtesans of the Northern Quarter refers to the prostitutes of Yoshiwara

Offering Money as One Coin - if you have faith, offering one coin is the same as a twelve-mon donation

Greedy Heart as Dust - even if you maintain economy, if you harbor evil desires, it becomes as nothing

Loyalty and Faith as Vital - loyalty and faith mean having no private heart; this is the vital matter

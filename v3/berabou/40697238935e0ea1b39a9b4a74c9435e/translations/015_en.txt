○Ninth Level: Nine means suffering. Pleasure is the seed of suffering, suffering is the seed of pleasure, so in this section one enjoys pleasure while not forgetting suffering.
Rest One - High Status: When in comfort, it's best not to act cheaply. Maintain dignity so people don't look down on you.

Appearing Foolish to Experts: At such times, become thoroughly sophisticated, but when it comes to calculations, act as if you know nothing - this is clever pretense.

No Middle-Class Expenses: Make visiting courtesans your specialty and enjoy yourself freely without middle-class expenditures.

Moxibustion for Youthful Spirit: Don't forget health maintenance at this time; occasionally apply moxibustion and keep your spirit young while enjoying life.

Post-Rest Resolution: Even when enjoying such comfort, resolve not to squander your fortune or become arrogant.

Residential Desire for Comfort: Keep your residence clean and let your household also enjoy comfort.

Rest Day Theater District: On free days, go to the theater district and watch plays constantly. "Theater district" refers to all theaters.

Kyobashi-Nakabashi: For residence too, the center of Edo, around Kyobashi and Nakabashi is good. Not mentioning Nihonbashi is an oral tradition.

Restful Heart as Single Focus: Those who first endure hardship well have the single-minded goal of later putting their hearts at ease. This is the eight-calculation method.

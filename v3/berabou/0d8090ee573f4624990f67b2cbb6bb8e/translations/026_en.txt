《Nichō Province》《Eleven Districts》

The Chōji District of this province is a large district with many mountains. All of them are mining mountains where gold, silver, copper, and iron are abundantly produced. It is said that recently the output is not as much as before, but since there are many newly discovered mines now, it is very promising that in one or two years these mountains will flourish and treasures will overflow.

Geography of Tsukimoto-koku (Moon-Origin Country)

The Setsuyōshū dictionary states at the beginning: When Emperor Jinmu viewed the shape of Japan, it resembled the form of an akitsu-mushi (dragonfly), so he named Japan "Akitsu-shima" (Dragonfly Island).

At this time, in Tsukimoto-koku as well, at the beginning of the human emperors' reign, a ruler called Shōji Shin'emu-tennō had his wife examine the shape of Tsukimoto, and finding that it resembled the character for "uwaki" (upper garment), he was greatly dismayed. Following the sound of "Akitsu-shima," he named it "Akirensu" (Dismay Island).

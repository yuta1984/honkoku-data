▲Konoharusan (This Spring Mountain) - Though it has no adornment and is a mountain landscape with nothing remarkable, it is said that this district prospers because people come to view this mountain. It is said that "spring mountains are like laughter," referring to how budding trees make mountains "laugh," but this mountain seems to laugh in all four seasons and is beloved by people.

"Shōhi Chirikki" (Geographical Record of Courtesans) - Complete Volume

Reading: Shōhi-chirikki
Column title: Shōhi

This book appears in episode 15 of "Berabō" as one of the volumes displayed at Tsutaya's bookshop, and in episode 36, at the farewell party for Kizanji who was quitting writing gesaku literature, it was praised as "an unparalleled guide to Yoshiwara" and autographs were requested.

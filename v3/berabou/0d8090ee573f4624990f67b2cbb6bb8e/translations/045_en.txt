○Teppo Island is at the end of Fushimi tide ○Rashomon Island is at 
the end of Ni-cho province. It is an island that extends from Sumi-cho province to Shin-cho province. When the courtesan of Eguchi appeared as Fugen Bodhisattva, the boat she was riding transformed into this island. In the Noh song of Eguchi, it says "the boats became a hundred vessels." It is a country where oil is scarce, and they say one lantern is shared by two households. The people are all physically disabled and devour intermediaries. It is said they eat starting from the nose first. Even the people of the five provinces fear this and do not approach. The people of this island apply white powder like wall plaster. When you strike their backs, the white powder flakes off in chunks, revealing cheekbones like wooden lath bamboo. They smoke foul tobacco and gulp down demon-killer sake. It is said that the people of the five provinces can be peeled. No matter how much the people of this island scrub and polish themselves, they cannot be peeled. Comparing this to roof tiles, should we call them substitutes of the same kind?

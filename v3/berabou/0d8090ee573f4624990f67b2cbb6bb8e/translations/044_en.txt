○Suido-jiri. This is the end of Nakano-shio. The waves are rough and there is no passage
○Kaisho Island. Located in Daimon-nada. It guards the entrance and exit of one province
○Kuronosuke Island. Located to the south. It enshrines an Inari shrine
○There are many Cha Islands. Among these, there are said to be

○Merchant Islands. Starting from Emon-kai, there are many throughout the five provinces.
Sode-no-ume, Saiken, Tsurube-soba, Yamaya-tofu, Maki-senbei, Kan-
ro-bai, Monaka-no-tsuki, Annei-yu. All are famous products of this island
○Nishi-gashi Island. One island stretches from Edo-cho province to Ageya-machi.
There is one island from Ageya-machi to Kyomachi province. The people who live on this island
prefer nanryo silver and devour even the people of the five provinces. As for human
features, if the face is beautiful then the neck is thick, if the eyes are beautiful then
there is a nose that looks borrowed. If the nose bridge is
straight then the mouth is like a crocodile's. In any case, the bodily features do not come together

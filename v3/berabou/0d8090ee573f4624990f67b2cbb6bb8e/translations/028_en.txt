▲Hinatsuru-ga-sawa (Baby Crane Valley) - The scenery of this valley is extremely worth seeing. It looks like Zhuge Liang's Eight Trigrams formation. When one enters this valley, it is said that one loses direction and finds it difficult to return.

▲Meizan (Famous Mountain) - There are treacherous places on the path ascending from the foothills. When one ventures deep inside, the view of the flowers is beyond description. This is truly a place worth a thousand gold pieces for every moment.

▲Senzan (Thousand Mountains) - This is also a famous place for flowers. Where have the flowers of old been transplanted? Though they are now young trees, many people venture in to view them.

This was called Nansembusu Toyoashihara Great Japan Country. The name "Onokoro Island" is probably also derived from the sound of that glue. Afterward, this was divided into sixty-six provinces, and within each province, districts called "gun" were created - all matters well known to everyone.

At that time, there were also divine couple deities called Kosanamiki and Kosanami. These deities were the seventh generation of variant deities from Hentekodate-no-mikoto, and were the father and mother of the fifth generation of foreign deities. Since these deities were great wanderers, they became competitive and thought, "We too shall try to create a country."

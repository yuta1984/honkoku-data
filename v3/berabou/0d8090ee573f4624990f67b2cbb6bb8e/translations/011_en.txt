Because it is a country formed from solidified matter, it has a metallic smell, favors martial ways, and honors men. Yoshiwara, being formed from the drippings of kamaboko, has a fishy smell, has a fish market in its center, and especially honors women. The difference between this and Japan is as great as between the moon and a turtle, so they took the character for "moon" (tsuki) from the honorable moon and the character for "foundation" (hon) from the turtle to name it Getsu-hon-koku (Moon Foundation Country). This is further divided into five countries. These five countries are:

Because it is a land formed from congealed matter, it smells of iron, favors martial pursuits, and holds men in high esteem. Yoshiwara, being formed from the drippings of the kamaboko, smells of raw fish, and accordingly there is a fish market at its center. Above all, it reveres women. Since this differs from Japan as much as the moon differs from a soft-shelled turtle, they took the character "tsuki" (moon) from "o-tsuki-sama" (the honorable moon) and the character "hon" (origin/base) from "suppon" (soft-shelled turtle, written as 泥亀, lit. "mud turtle") and named it "Tsukimoto-koku" (the Land of Moon Origin). Furthermore, this is divided into five provinces. Those five provinces are:

△Kado-machi Province (currently 11 districts)　△Kyō-machi Province (currently 17 districts)
△Shin-machi Province (currently 10 districts)

《Title: Shinmachi Province》《Marginal note: Ten Districts》
This was originally a prosperous province with many famous places and historical sites. Recently, the scenery of the various bays has been roughened by waves and the landscape has deteriorated compared to the past, but lately the local people have renovated the facilities and replaced old trees with young ones, naturally improving the views. If we wait a few more years, it should return to the scenic beauty of old times.

《Title: Shinmachi Province》《Marginal note: Ten Districts》

This is a land of long-standing prosperity, with many famous places and historical sites. In recent years, the scenery of the various inlets was roughened by waves and had fallen short of its former beauty, but these days the local people have renovated their establishments, replacing old trees with young ones, naturally enhancing the views. If a few more years pass, it will once again become the scenery of old.

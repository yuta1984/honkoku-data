Preface

Unable to refuse the request from the bookseller, while wracking my meager wisdom, I see that a foolish clerk from the main shop has brought a Yoshiwara guide. Looking at it, I find it full of differences between before and after the Bon festival due to the great reform in the seventh month. Eight hundred courtesans from a hundred establishments known for their beauty that could topple nations have disappeared. The people over there, whether they be Shirōbei-dono or Kurōsuke-dono, have recently become obsessed with lies and bright crimson silk patterns with their superficial knowledge of this district. Making forced connections with "ka-ki-ku-ke-ko," the gentlemen who frequent this place so enthusiastically are hopeless fools.

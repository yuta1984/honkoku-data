Preface

Unable to refuse the request from the publisher, while wracking my meager wisdom, a shop clerk brought me a Yoshiwara saiken (guidebook to the pleasure quarters). Looking at it, I see it's full of changes from the great July reorganization, with differences between before and after the Bon festival. There are a hundred establishments with eight hundred courtesans of devastating beauty. The customers over there - whether they be Shirōbei-dono or Kurōsuke-dono - are frantically obsessed with crimson silk striped patterns, showing off with their superficial knowledge of this place and their lies and pretenses. Making forced connections with the "ka-ki-ku-ke-ko" syllables, the gentlemen who frequent this place so enthusiastically are in a hopeless state.

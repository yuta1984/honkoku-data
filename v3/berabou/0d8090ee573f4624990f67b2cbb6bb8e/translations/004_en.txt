Preface

Unable to decline the request from the book publisher, and while wracking my meager brain, I see the foolish clerk from the main shop bringing the Yoshiwara Saiken guide. Looking at it, I find it full of discrepancies from the July major inspection, differences between before and after the Bon festival. A hundred establishments ruined, eight hundred courtesans lost. The gentleman over there—is it Master Shirōbei or Master Kurōsuke? Being quite inexperienced in the ways of this quarter, getting absorbed in lies and crimson silk patterns. Making forced connections with the sounds "ka-ki-ku-ke-ko." The patrons who frequent this flourishing place, spouting nonsense they cannot even accomplish...

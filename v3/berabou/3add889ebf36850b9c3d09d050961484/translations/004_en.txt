　　General Rules
1. Although divided into various categories, only ten or twenty-three words are
　written with the brush and shown to two or three inexperienced disciples, so the language is crude.
1. Even in daily playful activities, there are many inadequate corners, but
　from now on when seeking to enter various paths,
　questions and memories should be written down without debate.
1. Speaking of reading methods and other understanding may be presumptuous,
　but for those beginning to enter this path, may it serve as a guide.
　I write this briefly. Furthermore, one should extensively study Etsumokushō,
　Gumon Kenchū, and other works. Written by disciple Konya Asate
　in his remaining years.

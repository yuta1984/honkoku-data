Like a strong arrow shot from a blowgun, just as yesterday evening's hair ornament is changed by the next morning, the speed of changing fashions is entirely the custom of the pleasure quarters. Therefore, I have once again thoroughly investigated this place, combining a twelve-mon brush's beginning with three-mon worth of inner thoughts to create one volume. Still, for those areas where my investigation was inadequate, I can only await the detailed knowledge of the learned scholars of this place.

【The 39th Berabo story. Since pre-publication censorship by guild members became required, it became difficult to write about Yoshiwara. So the author decided to publish this as a sealed book labeled "Moral Reader," assuming there would be no surprise inspections by the magistrate's office. This was one of three volumes sold with this scheme. This incident led to Kyoden being sentenced to fifty days in handcuffs】

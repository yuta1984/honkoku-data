Ono no Takamura Song Character Collection

Camellia, Hackberry, Catalpa, Holly, Paulownia
Spring is camellia, summer is hackberry, autumn is catalpa, winter is holly, paulownia is similarly "kiri" (cut)

Nutmeg yew, Oak, Pine, Cedar, Cypress
White is nutmeg yew, what is white is oak, lord/you is pine, what is long-lasting is cedar, to meet is cypress

Pomegranate, Peach, Plum, Apricot
Pomegranate is indeed zakuro, peach is peach, plum and apricot are sour and bitter to the mouth, sumomo and karamomo

《Title: Land of Simple Honesty》

Wasōbei soon descended, and the people of this country are foolish and honest like children from other lands. Hence it is named the Land of Simple Honesty (Guchoku-koku). The people of this country know neither appetite nor material desires, nor do they have hearts for fame or status. In exchange, they are foolish and do not understand things.

Now, this country differs from other lands in many ways. The stars of this country are holes from which rain falls, and rain falls from within the stars. Kamaboko (fish cake) swims as-is in the sea. Katsuobushi (dried bonito) are ox horns. Also, the heavens of this country are one with Tenjiku (India), so they call heaven "Tenjiku" and also call Tenjiku "Tenjiku." Moreover, whether they be Dutch people or Ryukyu people, they think all foreigners are Chinese people.

"Today is calm, so quite a lot of kamaboko have come."

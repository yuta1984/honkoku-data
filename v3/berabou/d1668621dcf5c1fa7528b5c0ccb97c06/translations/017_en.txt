Because everything in Dream Country is made of dreams, someone who is wealthy today becomes poor tomorrow, and those who are young now later become old. What you think is a son-in-law becomes a father-in-law, daughters instantly become grandmothers, and what appears to be a man seems like a woman - such things are impossible to determine. Therefore, the people of this country do not feel sorry when borrowing things from others, nor do they press for repayment when lending. They know nothing of the desires of wanting and coveting, have no sadness or joy, do not flatter the wealthy and noble, and do not despise the lowly. It is truly a country like a dream.

Wa Sōbei reflected deeply that Zhuangzi became a butterfly in a dream and said, "Am I dreaming of being a butterfly, or is the butterfly dreaming of being me? Am I myself, or is myself me?" Truly, humans are like this country - today's way of living is a dream, and dreams are indeed today's reality. If so, then our wandering from country to country must also be a dream. If it is a dream, awakening comes quickly. Why should we torment our life's dream with unrighteous pursuits of fame and profit? Upon reaching this country, he achieved great enlightenment.

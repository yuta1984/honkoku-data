Thus Wa Sōbei arrived in Dream Country and achieved great enlightenment, realizing that truly the seven emotions of humans - joy, anger, sadness, and pleasure - are all like dreaming within a dream. Thinking there were no more countries worth visiting and that he should quickly return to his homeland, he hurried through the sky on that crane when unexpectedly someone called "Hey! Hey!" from behind. Wondering who it could be, he looked back to see a hermit sage approaching on a wisp of cloud.

The hermit said: "You have achieved enlightenment believing there is no other way beyond this Dream Country and intend to return home, but in my immortal arts there are still deeper teachings. The desires of those who are human are wealth and longevity. However, as the ancients have said, 'Wealth lies in Heaven's hands, and life and death are matters of fate.' Without conforming to Heaven and going against fate, wealth and long life are difficult to obtain. Among the good things of this world, there exists a secret teaching that details how humans can instantly maintain wealth and longevity through their conduct and state of mind. I shall bestow this upon you. I am the Expedient Means Hermit." So saying, he vanished like smoke.

《Title: The Country of Dark Delusion》

Wasōbei arrived at yet another country. This country is called "Anmei-koku" (The Country of Dark Delusion), meaning a land of darkness and confusion.

In this country, one month in other lands equals one year here. One day becomes one month, and one hour becomes one day. Therefore, what is twelve hours in a single day in other countries becomes twelve days in this country.

In other countries, morning glory flowers are considered ephemeral things, but in this country morning glories are considered auspicious, and they even compose poems like "morning glories of eight thousand generations." For New Year's, they search for bamboo to use as substitutes for pine decorations. It is truly a fleeting country.

【From here begins the middle volume】

In other countries, people say "a lifetime of misfortune" or "autumn cicada" and use these as examples of transient things. But if you listen to what the exorcists of this Country of Dark Delusion say, misfortune lasts a thousand years, cicadas live ten thousand years, mayflies and lightning live eight thousand years, and Takao of Miura lived one hundred sixty years. What other countries consider ephemeral, they all consider auspicious.

《Title: Country of Not Looking Up》《Margin note: uhemisu | koku》
Wa Sōbei soon arrived at yet another country. The people of this country made it their foremost principle not to envy those of higher rank, more refined people, or more noble people than themselves. Therefore, old and young, men and women alike, always wore hats and did not look upward. This was a most reasonable country.

"Looking up, there are so many things beyond one's reach; wear a hat and live according to one's own heart"

This saying must refer to this country. As the worldly proverb goes, "looking up creates greed"—when one doesn't know contentment and looks upward, envious thoughts and desires arise, leading one astray into unrighteous delusions. There is nothing better than not looking up. Wa Sōbei was deeply moved by the character of this country.

"Dogs and such seem like they wouldn't cause much trouble even if they looked up, but even dogs, when they look up, see enviable beasts like bears and tigers. How much more so for humans!"

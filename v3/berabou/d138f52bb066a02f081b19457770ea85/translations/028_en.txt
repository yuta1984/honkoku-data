The aforementioned Kozaemon (Nagata Kozaemon) has performed his duties with integrity and has demonstrated notable achievements. As there is a suitable person to be appointed as Deputy Administrator, I respectfully request that the appointment be made as stated in the written document.

(Wa) The Kashima Shrine chief priest, Higashi Daizen, has on this occasion presented one falcon. As a reward, three ryō in gold shall be granted to him. This amount is to be paid from the revenue funds of the Deputy Administrator's territory, and shall be properly recorded in the accounts upon receipt of Daizen's acknowledgment form.
The above is noted per the instruction of Lord Ōkubo Sado-no-kami. The above is all.

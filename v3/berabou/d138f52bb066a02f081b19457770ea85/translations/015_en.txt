Ina Hanzaemon's Administrative Office
Copy of official document issued during the Hōei era regarding passage and river crossing at Tone River and Uchi River

Memorandum
1. Regarding passage along the Tone River and Uchi River, no one except local woodcutters, grass cutters, and farmers of that area shall cross to the other side of the river (territorial assignments are listed on a separate sheet). If travelers are carelessly allowed to cross, even if discovered later, the local residents will be punished. If violators are captured and turned in, rewards will be given according to the person involved, and they will definitely be bestowed.
If there are those who would provide boats or goods to facilitate crossing, they should be captured and detained

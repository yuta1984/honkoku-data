1st month, 27th day　Chikayama Rokuzaemon
To Takeuchi Kurōemon-sama　Anchō [monogram]
To Takeuchi Hachirōbei-sama
To Ishikawa Heijūrō-sama

Regarding Aoki Bunzō's book-related official duties
Record

Rice: 3 koku, 2 to, 5 shō
Silver: 819 monme, 5 bu, 5 rin

Breakdown:
1. Rice 3 koku, 2 to, 5 shō - stipend rice
   This is for Aoki Bunzō's book-related official mission from Kanazawa in Musashi Province to Sagami, Izu, Tōtōmi, and Mikawa Provinces. He departed on the 11th day of the 5th month of this year and returned on the 16th day of the 7th month. Excluding small [non-working] days, this amounts to 65 days (calculated at the rate of 5-person stipend, single portion)

1. Silver 21 monme - for brushes, ink, and candles
   Breakdown: 3 monme 5 bu for 7 pairs of brushes at 2 pairs per 1 monme. 3 monme for 2 ink sticks at 1 monme 5 bu per stick. 6 monme 5 bu for 25 bundles of half-paper at 2 monme 6 bu per 10 bundles. 6 monme for 3 bundles of Mino paper at [price] per bundle

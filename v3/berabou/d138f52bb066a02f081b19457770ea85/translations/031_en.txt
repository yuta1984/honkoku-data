　　　　　　　To Lord Itakura Suō-no-kami

Re　　　　Regarding the Tōmura Office
　One: Within the peasants of Matsudaira Kaga-no-kami's domain, we have established a position called "Tōmura,"
　　to handle matters concerning the status and other affairs of peasants in private domains. Within this Tōmura system,
　　they also handle the duties of peasants in imperial lands.
　　　　　　《Marginal note: Upon consideration, the phrase "appointed alongside Tōmura" found in the register of those noted for filial piety and exceptional conduct likely means to be treated on par with those holding Tōmura office》

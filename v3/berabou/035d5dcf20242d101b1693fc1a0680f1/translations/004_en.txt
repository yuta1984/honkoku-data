Human poverty and wealth all depend on luck. That luck resides in heaven. If it were stored in a warehouse together with treasures, everyone would want it, but if all the people in the world became fortunate and there were only many wealthy people, that would create inconveniences, so it is kept in heaven where people cannot easily obtain it. This is not just for fire prevention.

However, even though we call it luck, it cannot be held in one's hands, nor has anyone seen it. What we name as luck, as human fortune, is nothing other than gold and silver when it comes to immediate matters we call luck. Though this gold and silver comes from mountains, here there is a magic mallet that produces gold and silver refined into koban and small coins.

Since it is Daikokuten's secret treasure, worldly people worship Daikoku targeting this mallet and pray for fortune. His retainer white mice record the names of each person who prays sincerely and report to Daikoku, who grants fortune to each accordingly. This is precisely how luck arrives. It is best to simply pray sincerely to the god and wait for luck like waiting for a blocked toilet.

"Hokkoku Junrei Uta Hōben" (Northern Provinces Pilgrimage Song Guide)

[In the final episode of "Berabo," Tsutaju (Tsutaya Jūzaburō), who had collapsed from beriberi, tells his visiting companions that he wants to be remembered as someone who "made books until he died." This work was among the manuscripts that Tsutaju's wife and clerk were looking at while discussing how that statement wasn't just modesty but his true feelings.]

Author: Kyokutei Bakin
Illustrator: Kitao Shigemasa
Genre: Kusazōshi (popular illustrated fiction)
Publisher: Tsutaya
Kansei 9th year, 1797

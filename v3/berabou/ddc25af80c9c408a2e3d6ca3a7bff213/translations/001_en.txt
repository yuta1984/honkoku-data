"Hokkoku Junrei Uta Hōben" (Songs of Convenience for Northern Province Pilgrimage)

[About this work: In the final episode of "Berabo," Tsutaya Jūzaburō, who had collapsed from beriberi, tells his visiting friends that he wants to be remembered as someone who "made books until his death." His assistant and wife, while discussing that this was not mere embarrassment but his true feelings, discover this work among the manuscripts he left behind]

Author: Kyokutei Bakin
Illustrator: Kitao Shigemasa
Genre: Kusazōshi (illustrated popular fiction)
Publisher: Tsutaya Jūzaburō
Publication Year: Kansei 9 (1797)

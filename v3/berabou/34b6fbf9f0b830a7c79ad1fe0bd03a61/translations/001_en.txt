"Shintai Kaichō Ryaku Engi" (A Brief Account of the Body Exhibition) by Tsuta Karamaru

[This work appears as a book featured in the commentary of "Berabo" Episode 3, with an illustration of Tsutaya Jūzaburō by Shigemasa at the end of the volume. In the final episode, there is a scene where the author discusses the plan for this book with Hōseidō Kisanji. The editor told him "What needs to be fixed? That's as misplaced as a roofer's loincloth. You need to rewrite the whole thing from the beginning."]

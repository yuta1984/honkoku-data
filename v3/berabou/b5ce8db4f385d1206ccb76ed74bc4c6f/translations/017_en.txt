【Main text】
Now then, Dōi the moxa merchant received the three-measure crest and the character "Hei" from Hōjō Tokimasa, went down to Edo in Musashi Province, and opened a moxa shop in what is properly called Tōri-hatago-chō in the third district of Ōtemma-chō. He added the wood sorrel family crest within the three-measure crest, took the name Mimasu-ya Heiemon, and his business prospered more and more. He came to be revered as the originator of cut moxa and achieved an elegant status. After that, when Lord Yoritomo became the ruler of the realm, his business flourished even more fully and has prospered until the present day.

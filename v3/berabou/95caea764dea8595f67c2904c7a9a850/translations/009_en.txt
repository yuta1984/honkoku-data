To catch pigeons, tie one white bean firmly to the end of a string, coat that bean with something called "hazu," and when a pigeon eats that bean, it immediately gets diarrhea and quickly excretes it in its droppings. When another pigeon eats that bean and excretes it, gradually other pigeons eat it and defecate, eat it and defecate, creating exactly like a string of prayer beads, and you can catch them.

The number of pigeons depends entirely on the length of the string.

However, it's good to apply more "hazu" every tenth bird.

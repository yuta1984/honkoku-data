Though it was lamentable, since there was nothing to be done about it, he resolved to perform memorial services with the complete Buddhist scriptures so that his father might at least be freed from the sufferings of the animal realm in this world and the next. He made his way along the eastern road where birds sing, arriving penniless, and devised ways to make money that required no capital. Before long, he became unparalleled in the art of flatulence. Perhaps due to the virtue of his filial piety, he became famous throughout Edo as the fart-scatterer of Ryogoku Bridge. And more than that, blooming at Naniwa Harbor,

This flower-blooming man. Now blooming as spring farts, this flower's fragrance spreads throughout the flower capital, and he blooms again upon returning to Edo. Calling himself Sangoku Fukuhei, like the spring haze of Unemegahara, there is no one, whether standing children or crawling babies, who does not know him. Now the two men who had accompanied Sajibei on the Shikoku pilgrimage, witnessing such wonders before their very eyes and being moved by Fukuhei's devotion, joined forces to perform memorial services for Sajibei, taking up the bowl-beating of Saint Kuya and selling tea whisks.

Beginning with frivolous words like silent farts, in the middle grasping the principles of all things in the palm of one's hand, letting out the supreme secret techniques, and at the end, once again tightening the anus after a fart. If readers follow that stench, it will become a ladder ascending high, serving as an aid to flatulence.

　　Written by Koyadoro, a commoner of Kasai, aboard a night soil boat

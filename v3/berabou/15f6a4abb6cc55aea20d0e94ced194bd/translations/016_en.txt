Doctors engage in cowardly debates between the Ancient Method school and Later Generation school, but cannot cure or heal the diseases they should treat, causing mass death from epidemics. Those with the faces of haikai masters lick the drool of Bashō and Kikaku, and tea masters, though their character appears refined, lick the excrement of Rikyū and Sōtan. All other arts have declined, and without one's own ingenuity and talent, even what the ancients left unfinished cannot reach the level of the ancients' foundations - this is due to not applying one's heart

A group of unrefined troublemakers gathered together, and among these worthless, skeletal individuals working behind the scenes, someone created an instrument called "erekiteru" that produces fire from the human body and cures illnesses. This instrument was originally devised by Westerners using the principles of lightning. Though they initially applied their ingenuity to it, it was not completed in that person's lifetime, and it is said to have been accomplished only after three generations. Even among the Dutch, very few know of it, and naturally the people of Korea, China, and India (Tenjiku) know nothing of it even in their dreams. Since this is the first time such a thing has been created in Japan since the opening of the country, there are countless people who wish to see it, starting with the nobility.

One day, a Confucian scholar from a certain estate named Ishikura Shingozaemon came and observed it for quite a long time, then said: "To comprehend the three powers of heaven, earth, and humanity is called Confucianism.

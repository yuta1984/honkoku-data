Originally empty. Including both esoteric teachings and enlightenment, without this sun, earth would all remain as its original stone, and water would all remain as its original ice, so there would be no growth of plants and trees, and no way to nurture fish and turtles. This is no different from having actors but no producer - no theatrical performance could take place. When one understands such principles, whether it becomes excrement or sweat, whether flatulence or fire emerges, it is all the same body's microcosm, originally nothing to find strange. However, those ignorant of principle do not find fire from flint and steel strange because it is common, but regard fire from an elektel (electric generator) as if it were Iizuna magic, or think of it as the same as mechanical puppets, and many call for it as entertainment. Among them, from the master who has absorbed everything from astronomy and calendar calculations to sour and sweet, and people who have achieved understanding of principle, there is substance in their questions and vigor in their answers.

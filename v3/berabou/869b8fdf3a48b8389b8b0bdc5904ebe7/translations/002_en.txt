【Handwritten in ink】
Published in Tenmei 8 (1788)
During the An'ei and Tenmei periods, Russian ships began frequenting Ezo (Hokkaido), so the shogunate dispatched envoys to Ezo several times. This work appears to satirize the conduct of the mission sent in Tenmei 6 (1786). It is still unclear who exactly is being targeted.
　May, Meiji 26 (1893)　Recorded by Fuseitei Jomu

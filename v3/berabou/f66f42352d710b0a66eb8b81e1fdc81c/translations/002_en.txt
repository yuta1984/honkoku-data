Menashi Yōjinshō (Cautionary Notes for the Blind) - Preface

What is called "menashi" (eyeless/blind)? "Me" (eye) refers to the self (ware).
The "self" refers to selfish thoughts. When there are no selfish thoughts, one
forgets the body and only the light of one's true nature becomes clear,
which is what the words "no self" mean. If there is no self,
what evil could one possibly commit? That Ishikawa Goemon

Menashi Yōjinshō (Cautionary Notes for the Blind) - Preface

What state do we call "menashi" (without eyes)? "Me" (eyes) refers to the ego or self.
The self refers to selfish thoughts and private concerns. When there are no selfish thoughts, one forgets the body,
and only the light of one's true nature (jishō) becomes clear,
resulting in the expression "there is no self." If there is no self,
what evil could one possibly commit? Even that Ishikawa Goemon
fellow, it is said, achieved enlightenment when he realized there was nothing for
Enma's court to find fault with.

Right is right, wrong is wrong - leave them as they are. Life is life, death is
death, flowers are flowers, water is water, grass is grass, earth is earth.
Extract
　If people mistakenly have the consciousness that "right is right," then it is no longer
　right. When it is truly right, it is right because one forgets rightness and is unaware of it. Therefore,
　how is it that we have never heard of a sage having the consciousness of being a sage since ancient times?
　A filial child is a filial child precisely because they are unaware of performing filial acts.
　Flowers do not know they are flowers, water does not know it is water, and life, death, and grass are
　all the same.

What am I? What am I? One should
search from the crown of the head to the buttocks. What cannot be found
even when searching is the self.
Extract
　"From crown of head to buttocks" refers to the entire body. Though this body seems to exist,
　when one searches thoroughly and exhaustively, it is as if it does not exist. The
　reason is that we are constantly forgetting the body most of the time. When forgotten,
　it resembles empty space. That empty space well knows heat, cold, pain, and itching.
　That which knows is called "without eyes." This is
　the true self, also called the great self of no-self. This great self
　has nowhere to be grasped, so it is called the place that cannot be searched.

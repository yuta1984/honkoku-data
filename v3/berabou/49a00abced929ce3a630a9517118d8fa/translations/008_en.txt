First, those with the most straightforward temperament were chosen as leaders of this island, and the Buddhas taught them how to raise the human seeds. When someone died, their body would be buried in the field where they were born, and from there new baby sprouts would emerge - this was the mechanism that was established, so people gradually grow forth. Those who grow from the same field are called "land lineage," and they inherit the family estate.

Author: Ōji Fūsha, Artist: Kitao Masanobu

Yachō was deceived by a fox and became dissolute. She fled to Izumi-chō and became a courtesan, offering her body. Depending on her attitude, she could become charming and attractive.

Opening verse:
　First Horse Day—
　　the fox's
　　　freshly shaven head

Shinuemon also gratefully put his palms together, but Ikkyu laughed heartily and said, "Now then, Ninagawa, when will you use that bow and arrows of yours? These three deities seem suspicious. Shoot down these three deities!"

Shinuemon started with realization, nodded decisively, drew his five-man bow to full draw, released it with a shout, and when he looked at what fell with a thud, what had appeared to be the three deities of the welcoming ceremony was actually an old tanuki raccoon dog that had been shapeshifting.

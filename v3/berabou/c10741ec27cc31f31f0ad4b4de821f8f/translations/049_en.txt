【Catalog of Available Publications】

By Master Shikō (Yomo no Akara):
*Kyōshi So* (A Foundation for Comic Chinese Poetry)
Modeled after classical Chinese poetry primers (Tang and Chu-style), this book serves as a guide for beginners in composing kyōshi (comic Chinese-style poetry). It is also useful for impromptu composition at gatherings.

---

*Tsūgen Sōmagaki* (The Complete Fence of Edo Slang) — By Tōri Sanjin Kyōden
A work describing the human emotions of the Yoshiwara pleasure quarter, cleverly capturing the names of its five districts, and recording the sophisticated amusements found there — though (jokingly) a book with not a single truly stylish thing in it.

---

Selected by Master Shikō (Yomo no Akara):
*Kokon Kyōshi Sen* (Anthology of Comic Chinese Poetry, Ancient and Modern) — Recently Published
A collection of kyōshi composed by notable poets throughout Japanese history, both ancient and modern.

---

*Hyakunin Isshu Baka Kōshaku: Hatsukinu Shō* — By Kyōden Rōjin (the "Old Man" Kyōden)
Taking the one hundred waka poems of the Ogura Hyakunin Isshu (the famous anthology associated with the Ogura villa), this work draws references from Japanese and Chinese literature, applying them — with considerable creative stretching — to contemporary topics, with amusingly far-fetched annotations.

---

*Tōto Kyoku Hyakunin Isshu: Kyōka Shirogi* — Selected by Yadoya no Meshimori
A collection gathering poems from famous poets of the past as well as those of the present day, to which portrait illustrations have been added, printed in color.

---

*Kyōka Shō* (A Toast in Kyōka Poetry) — Selected by Shikatsube no Magao
This book gathers outstanding works from various poets of the time, allowing readers to appreciate the distinctive style of each school. In short, it could be called an anthology of kyōka masters' households, or alternatively read as a collection of self-praised poems.

---

Rare and novel new publications are being produced one after another. Please do seek them out and have a look.

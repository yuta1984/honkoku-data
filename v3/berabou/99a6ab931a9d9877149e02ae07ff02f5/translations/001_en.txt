【Saiken Hana no Minamoto (A Detailed Guide: The Source of Flowers)】
【Reading: Saiken Hana no Minamoto】
【Spine title: Saiken (Detailed Guide)】

This work is a saiken (a detailed guidebook to the pleasure quarters) produced by Tsutaya Jūzaburō in his capacity as a dedicated editor/contributor for the publisher Uroko-gataya.
Research & Editing: Tsutaya Jūzaburō
Illustrations: Ragetsutei Bunshō
Genre: Hyōbanki (celebrity/establishment review publication)
Publisher: Uroko-gataya (Urokogataya)
Published: An'ei 4 (1775)

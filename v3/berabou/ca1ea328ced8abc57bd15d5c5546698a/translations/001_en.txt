An'ei 9th Year (1780)

【Title: Geisha Yobukodori】
【Running title: Keishiya】
【Episode 17 of "Berabou," where Tsutaju recognizes this as Karamaru's (Utamaro's) artwork upon seeing the painting style that closely resembles Kitao Shigemasa】
【Author: Shosendo = Nishimuraya Yohachi / Based on the sharebon "Tanishi Kingyo"】
【Artist: Kitagawa Toyoaki / Toyoaki is generally considered to be the same person as Kitagawa Utamaro, but in "Berabou" they are different people, with Utamaro drawing under Toyoaki's name. This person later became Shimizu Enjū】
【Kusazōshi / What we would today call a comic adaptation of a sharebon (novel)】
【Publisher: Nishimuraya】

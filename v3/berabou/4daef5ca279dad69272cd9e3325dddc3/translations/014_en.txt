Now, Saito Yoshitatsu relied on his military strategist Takenaka Shigeharu, and never failed to achieve victory in battle. His power grew strong, and there was no one in the neighboring provinces who opposed him.

However, recently Shigeharu had spread word that he was ill and withdrew into seclusion. Yoshitatsu suspected that perhaps his reluctance to organize battles and his dislike of warfare indicated some disloyalty. In his strategic headquarters, he gathered his household retainers and held various war councils.

Among the Oda family's retainers was a man called Saeda Inukiyo. Due to certain circumstances, he had incurred Harunaga's displeasure. He disguised his appearance and went to serve the Takenaka family, hoping that somehow he could make Shigeharu side with the Oda faction, and through this achievement have his master's anger pardoned.

He entered into a romantic relationship with Takenaka's daughter named Chisato, and they had a child together. Hiding from both sets of parents, they raised the child and named him Kiyomatsu.

Miwa Goro Nagahide spoke to Lord Harunaga: "This banquet at Inaba-yama is surely Yoshitatsu's stratagem. You have never met Yoshitatsu in person, but previously I used your name when meeting with Ogaki Saburo. This works to our advantage. Now I shall again use the name Harunaga and go to Inaba-yama." With that, he summoned his retainer elders and went to Yoshitatsu's side.

Yoshitatsu and his son believed Miwa Goro to be the real Harunaga and offered him the poisoned sake they had prepared in advance. Since Dosan also drank the same sake, he did not realize it was poisoned.

When Miwa Goro tilted the cup slightly, the poison gradually took effect, numbing and tormenting his entire body. Dosan immediately used an antidote and recovered completely. Miwa Goro gnashed his teeth and fought desperately like a madman, but could not overcome the great numbers arrayed against him. Moreover, as the poison spread through his system, he finally met his bitter end.

The Saito faction greatly rejoiced, believing they had killed Harunaga.

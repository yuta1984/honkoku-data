Hisayoshi discovered Yoshimaru's sword when Goemon was subdued, and secretly helped Goemon's father Jiroemon, who had been taken alive, escape by hiding him in a wicker basket. In exchange for that sword, he exposed the true identity of the false imperial messenger. Thereupon, Miyoshi Chokei, leading the imperial messenger Shiro Kuninaga and his son, surrounded them to strike down Goemon. Goro thought nothing of it and cut through the large force, using the ninja arts he had learned to conceal his form, obscure his tracks, and vanish without a trace.

Through this disturbance, Shiro Kuninaga's complicity in Chokei's rebellion was revealed when Hisayoshi picked up a written pledge, exposing the rebellion piece by piece. Then the people who had long since infiltrated in disguise from the Oda house to protect the Ashikaga clamored to strike down the Miyoshi father and son. Shiro was killed, and Chokei cut through one direction and escaped.

Oda Kozuke-no-suke Taira no Harunaga assembled his forces and advanced to the Sunamata River in Mino Province, waiting for word from Nagahide. When he learned the details of Yoshitatsu's poisoning plot and that there had been such a loyal retainer, he became greatly enraged. Seeking to defeat Yoshitatsu in a single battle as a memorial service for Nagahide, he immediately attacked the fortress at the foot of Inaba-yama defended by Shinomiya Juro, with the general himself advancing at the forefront.

When Oda Kazusa-no-suke Taira no Harunaga approached again, saying he would "receive Yoshitatsu's hospitality," Yoshitatsu and Dosan, who met him at the fortress, spoke in loud voices. This caused an unexpected uproar within the castle. Realizing "the Harunaga from before was an impostor - so that's why he died," they panicked in confusion and were attacked relentlessly.

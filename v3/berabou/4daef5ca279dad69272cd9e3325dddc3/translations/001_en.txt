Kinoshita Kage Hazama Battle
[Kinoshita kage hazama gassen]
[Running title: Under the Tree]

[This appears to be a kibyōshi (yellow-cover book) adaptation of the jōruri puppet play "Kinoshita Kage Hazama Battle" that appeared in Berabo episode 44. Episode 44 was probably around Kansei 5 (1793), and this book seems to have been published seven or eight years later. See annotation 2 for a link to the jōruri text]

[Author and illustrator: Jippensha Ikku]
[Kusazōshi (popular illustrated fiction)]
[Publisher: Iwatoya]
[Kansei 12, 1800]

Saito Yoshitatsu planned somehow to eliminate Oda Harunaga and seize control of Mino Province. However, thinking that Harunaga might be a formidable enemy, he consulted with his father, the priest Dosan. They decided to send Yoshitatsu's younger sister to the Oda side to create kinship ties within the clan, make them lower their guard, and then strike them down. He sent Ogaki Saburo to the Oda side to make this proposal. The Oda side suspected this was Yoshitatsu's stratagem, but outwardly responded without suspicion and deliberately acted as if they had fallen for the plot. Due to Harunaga's deep wisdom, he eventually welcomed Dosan's daughter as his bride.

Yoshitatsu thought he had succeeded, and then invited Harunaga to Inaba-yama Castle for a banquet. Planning to poison and kill them all with poisoned sake, he summoned Oka Hokyo, made him swear a sacred oath, and ordered him to prepare the poisoned sake.

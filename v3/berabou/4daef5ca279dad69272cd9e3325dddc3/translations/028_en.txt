With Miyoshi Chokei's destruction and demise, the Shogun's Palace was restored from those of Mount Koya, and returned to the hands of Lord Yoshiteru. The Ashikaga realm returned to the great peace of Mount Tai. Lord Yoshiteru's strategies, using treasures as instruments of warfare, revealed the beginning of Kokei's rebellion from this point. The Palace attendants and their sword bearers once again emerged into the world, all due entirely to the efforts of this subordinate Hisayoshi. Even the Imperial Court was pleased. Through imperial messengers to Lord Yoshiteru, Toyoshi was also summoned and granted the title of Governor of Chikuzen. From this point, this subordinate changed his name to Hashiba Chikuzen-no-kami Hisayoshi, increasingly spreading his thunderous reputation across the four seas, achieving long-lasting prosperity and meritorious military accomplishments.

Goemon had already prepared his disguise as a false imperial messenger and departed for the villa in Shiga. Seeing this, Jirōemon thought to himself, "What a truly despicable villain!" and inwardly fuming, offered various advice and clung to Goemon's sleeve trying to stop him, but Goemon would not listen and shook him off before departing.

At that very moment, Kinoshita Tōkichi (later Toyotomi Hideyoshi) happened to be passing through this place and witnessed this scene. After Goemon had passed by, he questioned Jirōemon about the situation, but since there were circumstances that could not be fully disclosed, Jirōemon gave only vague answers. [Someone] put Jirōemon in a palanquin and took him away.

　　　Water Section
Rose water: Listed under the dew-water section in the Compendium of Materia Medica. Japanese name is Bara-no-tsuyu. In the Dutch language, it is called "rose water." The Dutch call all thorny plants "roses." "Rose" refers to the rose flower, and "water" means water.

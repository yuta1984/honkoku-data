　　　Water Section
Rose water　Listed under the dew-water entry in the Compendium of Materia Medica. Japanese name is bara no tsuyu. In the red-haired people's language it is called "rose water." The red-haired people call all thorny plants "bara." What they call "rose" water is water. This substance is water obtained by steaming rose flowers using a distillation apparatus.

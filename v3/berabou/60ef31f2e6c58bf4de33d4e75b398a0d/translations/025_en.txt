**[Gyūkichi / 牛吉]** "— what a hopeless fellow I am."

**[禿 (gebi)]** "Everyone says so, you know."

**[Gyūkichi]** "You're being cheeky without a care for how I feel. No matter how clever you try to be, a corpse on its way to the cremation ground still has to pass through the same road. **[Gyūkichi]** "What kind of bug, you say? A *namabi-no-mushi* [a 'raw' bug, i.e., an impertinent pest] — honestly, I can't believe my ears."

**[禿 (gebi)]** "What a nasty drunk you are. If you're going to get tipsy like that, go take a nap in the servants' room, would you."

The daytime world [illustration present] — a small booklet whose concept is utterly transformed from the glamorous nighttime scenery of the pleasure quarters. Thinking that this one should be a new weave entirely (a fresh approach), I simply titled it "The Reverse Side of Brocade," and nothing more.

Written on the day of watching the Ryukyuan envoys enter Edo

**[Kawaji / 川]** "Yukinoya, go and fetch the tooth-blackening (*ohaguro*). Then take the Kaidea-ya (*楓屋*) account book with you and get one board [of tooth-blackener] put on the tab. Bring back the *sukito-guro* [a fine black tooth-lacquer finish] while you're at it. And also bring back some scrap paper and tobacco. Yobune-san, get out the brush-pick (*fude-yōji*)."

*(She gives her orders. A "brush-pick" refers to the brush used for applying tooth-blackening.)*

General Outline of Kemari Rules and Regulations

1. Garden Construction
There are two types of gardens: wide and narrow. Regarding the formal measurements, the kake (suspension) should be taken as the foundation, and apart from building structures, there should be no further distinctions. Therefore, in recent times, while kemari courts are simply made according to the house and one's preference, when constructing a garden primarily [for kemari], it should fundamentally be square-shaped.

One: Regarding the height of the ball

A person's ball height is like the tall and short soldiers in archery - there are innate differences regardless of how much one practices or cultivates the skill. Generally, the ball height should be about one jō five shaku, so one should have a general understanding of this. One: When the ball hits a hanging branch and falls

When using a pole to retrieve it, one must not strike the ball down. One should insert the tip of the pole between the branches and gently hook it down. No matter how low the branch may be, one must not shake the tree. If the branch is within reach of one's hands, simply use the hands to brush it down.

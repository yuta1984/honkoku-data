【Left Page】
If it is a kake with 2 jō spacing between trees, the distance between the main pillar of the house eaves and the trees should be about 1 jō 3-4 shaku as a good measure. Closer than that is done out of necessity when the garden is narrow, and even if it is entirely an abbreviated arrangement, if trees must be planted, it is beyond reason—one should simply calculate and plant them at whatever distance possible.

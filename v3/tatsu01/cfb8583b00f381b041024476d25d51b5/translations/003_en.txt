The land of Yamato — the land of Shikishima — having been the site where Emperor Jinmu first established his capital, and having thereafter served as the seat of successive imperial reigns, perhaps for this very reason, the customs and manners of the land have followed that precedent, and the people have naturally cultivated a disposition that values achievement and renown, even now…

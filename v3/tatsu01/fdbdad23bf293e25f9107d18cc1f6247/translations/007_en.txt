When Shibata reported this to his lord, the Shogun (Yoritomo) heard it and said: "Does this Yamakita speak thus even while fearing for his life?
What a splendid warrior! Untie that man's bonds!" He had them untied, and as he was a fine man, his domain was
restored to him as before, which was truly remarkable. Yamakita no Jiro was greatly pleased and said: "Not only have you spared my life,
but graciously and wisely granted me my domain as before!" With tears
wetting his armor sleeves, he returned to his home province. Thus from the winter of Bunji 5 until the autumn of Tensho 18,
for about 400 years, he held his original domain and his house continued, but when Lord Toyotomi Hideyoshi conducted
the siege of the Hojo at Odawara in Sagami Province, Yamakita was seriously ill and could not go to battle, so he sent his chief retainer as deputy

to join the campaign. Soon after the battle ended, because Lord Yamakita had not participated in the battle, he was exiled to the western provinces.
His succession was entirely given to his chief retainer. Thus [the retainer] governed Senboku and Akita, and around Keicho 7
there was a transfer of domains to Mutsu. It is said that the one called Akita Jonosuke is a descendant of Lord Yamakita's chief retainer. Also,
Hitachibon Kaison still lives in the Senboku domain. However, due to his advanced age, he no longer
speaks of the old days. Also, in Kanen 7, Hitachibon died. He always kept a small box wrapped in bamboo bark,
never separating it from his body day or night, hanging it around his neck. After his death, when they opened it, there was
a document in Lord Yoshitsune's hand with his seal, addressed "To Hitachibon." Seeing this, everyone knew
he was indeed Hitachibon, and so forth. This Seietsu Monogatari tells that around the time of the battle of Koromogawa in Isawa District, Mutsu Province,
after all who followed and served Judge Yoshitsune were killed in battle, only Seietsu (Koshiro Kiyoetsu), Hitachibon, and two other close attendants—
these four alone survived. Seietsu lived on in this world until the summer of Kanen 7, remaining in Hiraizumi.

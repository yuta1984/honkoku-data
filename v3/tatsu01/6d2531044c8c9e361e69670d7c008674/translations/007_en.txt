The "Kuji Kongen" states:
What is now called the Kuzu performance,
in which they sing songs and play
the flute, refers to their coming
from Yoshino at the beginning
of the year (and so forth).

The "Gōke Shidai" states:
The Kuzu perform songs and flute music
outside the Shōmei Gate.

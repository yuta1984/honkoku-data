When they probed Toyoashihara with the shark kamaboko, the drippings from the kamaboko congealed and accumulated, becoming blockages. A person from Korea named Kōkeishi cut this open and made it into one village. This became a dependency of Japan, named Hokusembu-shū Shin-Yoshiwara Great Tsukimoto Country. Later the country was divided into five parts, and within the country, districts were divided.

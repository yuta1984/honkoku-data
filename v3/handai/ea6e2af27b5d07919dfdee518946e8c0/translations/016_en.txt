【Right page】
If one were to discuss human nature, women possess benevolence while men lack it. Women rescue their parents and siblings from poverty, maintain extremely harmonious relationships with their sisters, and work through countless hardships to enrich their master's fortune. However, men lack such compassion. Not only the masters and procurers, but even young men constantly harbor thoughts of threatening others, keeping torture instruments ready. Their sense of benevolence and righteousness is thin. Women also demonstrate extreme dedication to maintaining their honor through finger-cutting, hair-cutting, and written vows.

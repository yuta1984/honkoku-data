　　　Japanese National Climate and Customs
The climate and customs of this country have been described in various books since ancient times, so there is no need to speak of anything new now. However, if I were to describe the general outline: men are despised and women are revered, days are wasted and nights are utilized, men are forbidden from meeting with courtesans, women freely meet with men, the wedding cup ceremony begins with the man, and in seating arrangements women always take the place of honor. These are the virtues of a yin country, and

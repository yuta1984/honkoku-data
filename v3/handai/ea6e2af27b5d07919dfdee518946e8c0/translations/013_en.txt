The Climate and Customs of Tsukimoto Country
The climate and customs of this country have been described in various books since ancient times, so there is no need to say anything new about them now. However, to give an outline: men are despised and women are revered; the day is wasted and the night is utilized; men are forbidden from meeting with two women; women freely meet with men; the wedding cup begins with the man; and in seating arrangements, women are always given the upper seats.

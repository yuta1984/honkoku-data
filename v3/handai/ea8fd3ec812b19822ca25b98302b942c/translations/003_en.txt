First year of Kansei, Year of the Earth Rooster, Spring, Third Month, Eighth Day
Buddhist name: Shūsen Shinji, Futai rank
Secular name: Tatematsu Kaho Tōsaku

Lamenting Tō Mōzanjin　　　　　Nanpo Ōta Tan

In the past, I saw his aspirations toward the four directions, but now he has returned to the eternal night. Heaven spares not even one elder; literature naturally arises from the suffering of great talent. He laughed at and scorned the human world, drifting like moss upon stone. Looking back on thirty years of life, he savored flowers and moonlight in countless cups.

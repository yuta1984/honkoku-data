Here, the renowned pleasure quarters know nothing of the boundaries called dawn. The flowers know not the length of spring in the sacred kagura dance, the cuckoo knows not of light clothing, the autumn wind knows not the loneliness of evening, and even on snowy days they know not the cold. Such limitless pleasure is what should truly be called the flower of prosperity. Around this time, a wealthy patron came and playfully expressed his desire to "see the courtesans of this quarter engage in sumo wrestling." Someone called Rijaku (Village Sparrow) accepted this request and, likening the flowers of the four seasons to the forms of renowned courtesans, designated spring and summer as the East, autumn and winter as the West, and was about to begin the sumo match...

Preface to Hana Sumai (Flower Wrestling)
People gaze only upon the unblemished moon,
and treasure flowers only in their prime.
This is the common
aesthetic of worldly people. Even beautiful women
cannot surpass two in number. Yet pleasure
must surely depend on that single beauty.

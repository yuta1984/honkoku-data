Hana Sumo (Flower Wrestling) Intermission
Having waited expectantly and graciously
observed from early morning,
the promoter need not mention,
and all the spectators
are extremely delighted and grateful.
Now, the upcoming matches
bid farewell to all the honored guests,
and we humbly continue...

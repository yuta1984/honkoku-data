"Tales of the Great Earthquake for Future Generations"

【Upper section】
○In Settsu Province's Sanda, about seventy houses collapse, and in nine nearby villages, about seventy to eighty houses collapse, with many injured
○In eastern and western Kawachi, about 150-160 houses collapse, and temples and shrines suffer major damage
○In Sakai, fifty to sixty houses collapse, with countless severe damage to halls and shrines
○In Izumi Province's Sano, about two hundred houses collapse with severe damage
○Kyoto experiences less shaking than Osaka
○Kii Province and Ise Province experience much more severe shaking than Osaka, with enormous house collapses, and most people are camping outdoors, as reported in letters

【Lower section】
○In Tamba Province's Kameyama, about one hundred houses collapse
○In Sonobe of the same province, over two hundred houses collapse, with over two hundred deaths and countless injured
○In Koriyama, over twenty houses collapse, with about twelve to thirteen deaths and countless injured
○At Kasuga Shrine in Nanto (Nara), there is major collapse, with torii gates and metal lanterns collapsing and falling, more than half the townhouses collapse, with some injuries
○Kojima in Awa experiences major shaking with seventy percent of houses collapsing and catching fire, with flames not subsiding for a full day and night
○Takamatsu in Sanuki Province experiences the same with major fires

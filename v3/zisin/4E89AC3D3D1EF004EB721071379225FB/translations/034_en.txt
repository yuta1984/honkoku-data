Representative: Uemura Suruga-no-kami

Regarding the earthquake in the domain territory, the castle interior residential quarters and other areas suffered major damage, and both the samurai retainers and town quarters were entirely damaged. Moreover, due to land changes, flooding, and other issues within the domain, a request for a loan has reached His Lordship's attention, and thinking it must be a great hardship, a loan of 10,000 ryō in gold is hereby granted.
　April 28th
　　
　　　　　　　　　　　　Hori Nagato-no-kami
　　　　　　　　　　　　　Representative: Itami Saburōemon
Regarding the earthquake in the domain territory, the administrative compound residential quarters and other areas suffered major damage, and there were not few ruined and damaged places within the domain. The request for a loan has reached His Lordship's attention, and thinking it must be a great hardship, a loan of 500 ryō in gold is hereby granted.
　　April 28th

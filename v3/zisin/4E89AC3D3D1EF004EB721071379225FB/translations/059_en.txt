April 25th　　　　　　　　　Matsudaira Hōki-no-kami

1. Directive from Uesugi
　From a place called Tōrichō in Murayama Kamimura, Yonezawa, Ushū, within the domain of Danjō-daihitsu
　A fire broke out on the 15th at the hour of the Monkey, and though the castle town suffered from the spreading flames, the fire was extinguished during the hour of the Rat on the same night. Record of the fire damage:
1. 202 buildings　　Samurai residences　　1. 5 buildings　　Temples
1. 334 buildings　　Town residences　　1. Same　　Shugendō practitioners
1. 58 buildings　　Village headmen　　1. 190 buildings　Storehouses
1. 222 buildings　　Storage sheds　　1. 4 locations　Temples and shrines

The above is the situation. There was no particular damage to the third bailey within the castle grounds. There were no human casualties from the fire nor deaths of cattle or horses. I hereby report this matter. The above.
　　March 29th　　　Retainer of Uesugi Danjō-daihitsu
　　　　　　　　　　　　　Andō Tsunegorō

1. Directive from Sakai Saemon-no-jō
　In my domain of Tagawa District and Akumi District in Shōnai, Ushū, from the 5th of last month
　until the 6th, rain fell and snow melted, causing rivers to flood with water levels rising more than 9 shaku above normal, resulting in water flooding into rice fields and dry fields, and causing damage. Record of damaged locations:
1. Samurai residences flooded: 15 buildings

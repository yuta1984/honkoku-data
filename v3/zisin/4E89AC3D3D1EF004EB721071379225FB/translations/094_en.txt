In Takasaki, people from around Fujii and Toyooka organized a patrol by the leadership, so no disturbance occurred.

Two establishments were destroyed: Risuke the pawnbroker on Myōgi-machi and Senzō the sake dealer on Miyazaki-machi.

In Shimotsuke Province, two establishments were destroyed: Chūbei the grain dealer and an oil dealer on Tenmyō-machi in Sano. In the same province, at Itagi-machi under the jurisdiction of Daikan Numa Jūzaemon:
Kuramotsu, Kichibei, Heibei, Yuemon, Shirōemon, Jinemon, Yasutarō, Kiemon, Ryokusetsu, Sanetsu, Yaemon

The above twenty establishments were destroyed.

In the domain of Matsudaira Yamato-no-kami at Maebashi, eight establishments, plus one sake dealer at Komagata - a total of nine establishments were destroyed.

In Ashikaga territory, five locations were destroyed.

In Ōshū Sendai, two establishments of official purveyors (ōroku-nin) were destroyed.

In Yūki territory, disturbances occurred in late October. Details are not yet known.

In the twelfth month, twelve sake dealers were destroyed in Chichibu, Musashi Province.

On the second day of the twelfth month, in Sakamoto-juku in the domain of Itakura Ise-no-kami, two nakama-yado (transport inns) and two grain dealers were partially destroyed.

On the same day, at Kutsukake-juku on the Nakasendō under the jurisdiction of Daikan Endō Hyōzaemon in Saku District, Shinano Province, Tsutaya Motoemon was destroyed. From there, after nightfall, they moved out to Iwamura-ta-juku in the territory of Naitō Shima-no-kami and attacked Hotei-ya...

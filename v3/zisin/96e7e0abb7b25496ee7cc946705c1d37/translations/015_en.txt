**Written Report — Sakae-machi**

Item: Due to the earthquake, the roof of the entrance to the honorable bell-ringing estate (o-kane-tsuki yashiki) sustained damage. Other than this, there are no collapsed buildings, injured persons, or the like. Respectfully submitted.

Tora Year, 11th Month, 6th Day　　Town Headman: Katoya
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Kyōjirō (round seal)

To: Kunotomi Genjirō

**Written Report**

Item: The residence of the widow of Shichiuemon, a scabbard-maker (sayashi) of Niō-machi, has a frontage of three and a half ken. During last night's earthquake, the small roof along the front street as well as the projecting lattice windows both collapsed. Apart from this, there are no injured persons or the like. Respectfully submitted.

Tora Year, 11th Month, 6th Day　　Niō-machi Town Headman: Hyōguya
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Kyūuemon (square seal)

To: Kunotomi Genjirō

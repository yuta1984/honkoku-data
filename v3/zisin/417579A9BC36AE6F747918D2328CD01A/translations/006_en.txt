Houses that had been harmoniously and beautifully constructed and polished by various people,
hall buildings (houses large and small), Buddhist temples, and shrine grounds—some had their ridgepoles
broken, beams pulled out with roof tiles falling, rafters snapped, furniture
smashed and eaves tilted, or door sills and lintels warped
and twisted. When people tried to open the doors and sliding screens that were stuck shut,
they were jammed and wouldn't open. Distracted by this,
some lost consciousness, and others trying to flee found the ground tilted,
their legs stumbled and they fell down and rolled about.
Nearby, houses collapsed and fell down. Furniture,
horizontal beams, and lintels struck people's heads, knocking them down.
Lower walls broke people's hip bones, and from the second floors, tall

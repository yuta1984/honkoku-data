【Right page】
In any case, it was all in vain. And so, gathering up the torn corpse pieces here, they put them in a straw bag along with their tears, had someone carry it, and the two parents headed home - one can imagine the state of their hearts. Though one might say this was retribution for deeds from a previous life, if they had died lying sick in bed, at least the grief might have been somewhat bearable. But this was an unimaginably horrific end, so terrible that even the sleeves of strangers were wet with tears. I heard later that they were from the area near Goryō Shrine - one was the child of a koto maker, the other the child of a ball maker. They had consumed defiled fire and, without telling their parents, had come to worship at the sacred hot water ceremony at this shrine festival.

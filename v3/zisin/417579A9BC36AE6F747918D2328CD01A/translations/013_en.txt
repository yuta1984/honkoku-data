About a hundred day laborers all raised their voices at once, rubbing their hands together and pleading: "Namu Shakyamuni Buddha, destroying you in this way does not arise from our own hearts. We were hired by the day-labor officials and are breaking you apart by their orders. We have no sin in this—please forgive us." The magistrate officials said, "What is this? This is an earthquake! Day laborers, do not panic!" But the workers paid no attention, and those day laborers who had climbed onto the Buddha's shoulders and were perched on his hands

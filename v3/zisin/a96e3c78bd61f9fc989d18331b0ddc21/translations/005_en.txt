In any case, no matter how much I panicked, with a woman's strength I too would die together with them. Looking at my child in the water, I saw their face was pale and limp, so I thought to abandon my child's life that could not possibly be saved. But they were only two and four years old, such young children, so for a while my heart could not settle. With the preciousness of the children and my own predicament already seeming perilous, I thought of the old saying: "There may be bushes where one abandons a child, but there is no place where one abandons oneself." After much deliberation, I had no choice but to throw them into the water, and my children were lured away by the impermanence of death, flowing away. Thinking that someday they would reach the sea and become food for sharks and whales, my heart tightened with pain, but somehow I managed to save myself.

It cannot be helped, but in my household too, my husband and children were swept away, and with two children aged two and four, I cannot manage alone as a woman - such is my situation.

Record of the Great Flood in Ryōbi

Since heaven and earth opened, there have been events called miraculous - though I do not know the details, there was the Kanazu disturbance and the Shinano earthquake, these are stories of the world that I have never witnessed. What is miraculous here is the story of the great flood. The time was the third year of Kaei, the year of the dog (1850). Starting with Tsuruga port and Entsu, there was truly an extraordinary heavy rainfall, so water gushed forth from fields, mountains, and valleys, collapsing into rivers to become a great flood, and with a roar...

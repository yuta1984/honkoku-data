Due to the earthquake on the 2nd of this month and the resulting fires, there are many people in distress.
Regarding the matter of Reigan Temple in Fukagawa providing
one hundred mon of money each to the destitute people sleeping outdoors in the temple gate town
and to the landlords of the same town, we have investigated the approximate number of people
and report as follows:
At the same temple gate area:
- Number of destitute people: approximately 180 persons
- Number of landlords: 10 persons
As recorded above, charity was provided, with a total amount of approximately 47 ryō
and 2 bu.
This is an urgent investigation, so we report the approximate numbers of people and total amount of money.

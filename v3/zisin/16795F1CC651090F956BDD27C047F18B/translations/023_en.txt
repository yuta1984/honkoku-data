Sekiguchi
　　　Chōkō-ji Temple
　　　　Nichiyū
　　　　Age 40, Year of the Rabbit
The above-mentioned Nichiyū of Chōkō-ji Temple was born in Shimomachi Village, Kubiki District, Echigo Province, and became a monk at the age of nine in the same sect, becoming a disciple of Nichiei of Kuon-ji Temple on Mount Minobu. His daily conduct has been proper with no improper behavior. In the first month of the previous year of the Rooster, he took up residence as the head priest of the aforementioned Chōkō-ji Temple. Although this temple does not have many parishioners or much income from donations, he has carried out repairs to the main hall and other buildings at his own expense, and is well-regarded by his fellow priests and all parishioners. Particularly, regarding the earthquake on the night of the 2nd, to those among his impoverished parishioners who were crushed, suffered fire damage, or died instantly, he provided 50 mon of gold each as assistance, and furthermore did not charge funeral fees.

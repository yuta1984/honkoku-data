With utmost respect, we humbly submit this written petition:
Item: Kahei, the monthly magistrate of Shibaguchi 2-chōme, Hyōzō of the same 3-chōme, and Kamejirō of Gensuke-chō jointly submit the following: Due to the earthquake on the night of the 2nd day, our residences, storehouses, and other structures sustained considerable damage.

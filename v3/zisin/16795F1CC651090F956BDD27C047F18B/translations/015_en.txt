Banzui-in Temple
On the morning of the 3rd, they provided three rice balls with pickled plums
to each person who came to the temple gate, Yamazaki-chō, Okitte-chō, and other areas,
regardless of whether they were acquaintances or strangers, giving charity to all who came in order.
Although the exact number is unknown, it was provided to a great many people.

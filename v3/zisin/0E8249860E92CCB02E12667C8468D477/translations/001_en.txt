《Detailed Revised Edition》 Edo Great Earthquake Fire Locations

Even the blue ocean becomes shallow, and Mount Sumeru becomes light. Edo is a prosperous place of a great metropolis, where people from all provinces gather uninvited. For this reason, this is written to inform relatives and connections in distant provinces and other districts of urgent matters of life and death.

The time was the 2nd year of Ansei (1855)
October 2nd, night, at the fourth hour (around 10 PM)
Suddenly it began to shake
The sound resounded
in all directions, terrifying
beyond description, and needless to say,
old and young, men and women
were all in panic and confusion
Storehouses collapsed, houses were crushed
Fires broke out simultaneously in all directions
What can we call this pitiful sight
but unbearable to witness
Here we record the outline of

　　Honjo Area
Crossing Ryogoku Bridge heading south, the stone wall of Ichinohashi collapsed, and around the same Moto-cho area, there were many collapsed houses. The bell tower hall of Ekoin Temple collapsed. At the same location, Tsuchiya-sama's and Honda Kuranosuke-sama's residences were severely damaged. Also, from here eastward, all small residences were severely damaged without exception. Aioi-cho and Matsusaka-cho - this area had many houses with major collapse. Midori-cho 1st and 2nd districts burned. The 3rd district only collapsed but remained unburned. This fire stopped burning at the corner of the same 3rd district. The 4th and 5th districts burned, with the fire stopping at Hana-cho. The number of instant deaths up to this point was about 130 people, with about 140 injured. From there, Yanagihara-cho 5th district, Kayaba-cho 4th district, around Kameido Tenjin temporary shrine - all had many major collapses. About half a district burned near Itsutsumedoshi ferry landing. Across Kameido Tenjin Bridge, again more than half a district burned. Tenjin Shrine remained safe. From there, Yanagishima Myoken Shrine remained safe. From Tenjin Bridge to Hoon-ji Bridge, there were many small residences, and temples also suffered much severe damage and collapse. Townhouses near Hoon-ji Bridge burned slightly. Instant deaths during this period were about 120 people, with an unknown number of injured. Also, crossing Ryogoku Bridge and turning left, Todo Izumi-no-kami-sama's residence and Kamezawa-cho - this area had many small residences, and the Imperial Bamboo Storage remained safe. From there along Okawa Street, Tsugaru-sama's warehouse residence, Matsumae Izu-no-kami-sama's residence, Matsuura Iki-no-kami-sama's residence, and besides these, all the various daimyo secondary residences were severely damaged without exception. The Imperial Ship Office was also severely damaged. Mogami-sama's residence was also severely damaged. From there, Ishihara-cho, Arai-cho, around Benten-koji suffered major collapse and then burned.

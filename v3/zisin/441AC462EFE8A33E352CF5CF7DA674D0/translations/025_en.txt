On that day, offerings from various places were abundant, and the crowds of worshippers gathering were such that even elderly people over one hundred years of age said they had never seen or heard of such prosperity. That this occurred after such a great disaster truly shows this to be a sacred place of immense spiritual power that should be revered and respected.

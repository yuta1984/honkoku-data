1. On windy days, in all neighborhoods, except for official business, people are strictly forbidden from going out. They must guard only their fire sources, splash water on rooftops, eaves, and overhangs, and store water in nearby buckets and other containers.
   Note: For fire prevention on rooftops, ladders, water baskets, water guns, and other equipment should be prepared in advance.

1. Even on ordinary days, not only hearths but also second floors, storage areas, and all places that are out of sight must be constantly patrolled. At night, before sleeping, the household must be thoroughly inspected, and extinguished charcoal and other materials must be carefully checked.

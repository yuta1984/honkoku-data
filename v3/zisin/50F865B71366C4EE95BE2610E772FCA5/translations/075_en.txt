Even when personnel arrangements resulted in fewer people available, night watch duties continued to be performed. Therefore, for the additional allowances during night watch duties, the required expenses for the three bridges averaged 46 ryō, 3 bu, and 500 mon over six months from the tenth month to the third month. A request was submitted for funding to be provided. Upon investigation, it was confirmed that various miscellaneous expenses were indeed incurred. When consulted, Lord Tsushima-no-kami approved the additional allowances as requested, with the stipulation that to prevent any corruption in fire prevention duties, this matter should be reviewed annually each ninth month. Accordingly, yesterday all the bridge guards and responsible headmen were summoned to the Kujira-bune Saya guard post, where, in our presence, the approved arrangements were communicated to them. We hereby report on this matter. The above.

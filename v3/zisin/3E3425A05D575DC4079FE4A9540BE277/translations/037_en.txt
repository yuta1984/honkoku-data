We have been submitting reports continuously up to this point, but the disasters in the villages at the foot of Ōubasan and Mushikuradake mountains are unprecedented. As they are particularly severe, I am once again submitting this report. Detailed matters will be reported later. The above.
　　April 23rd, deadline from residence　　　　　　Sanada Shinano-no-kami

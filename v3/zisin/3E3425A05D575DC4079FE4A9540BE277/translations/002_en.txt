Copy of a report submitted to Lord Toda Yamashiro-no-kami, the duty officer

In my domain of Iiyama in Shinano Province, beginning around the hour of the Boar on the 24th day of last month, there was a great earthquake. Within the castle, residential quarters, turrets, gates, and surrounding walls were extensively damaged. The retainers' residences and the castle town were destroyed, and many houses in the villages of my domain collapsed. Fires also broke out as a result, causing destruction in various places. The local officials have reported that the ground continues to shake even now. Although I will report the details later, I first submit this notification. The above.
　　2nd day of the 4th month　　　　　Honda Bungo-no-kami

In my domain within Takai District of Shinano Province, from around the hour of the Boar on the 24th day (the day before yesterday), the earthquake was severe. There were several damaged locations in the administrative office and retainers' residences and barracks. Peasants' houses in the villages collapsed, and additionally, sand and mud gushed out from several hundred cracks in rice fields and other farmland, completely covering all the cultivated land.

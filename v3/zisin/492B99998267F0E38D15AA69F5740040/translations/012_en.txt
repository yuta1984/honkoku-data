【Right Page】
Being clumsy with writing and unable to adequately express the reasons even when able to speak them aloud, I spend hours moving my brush around the inkstone, but it is like reading an unfamiliar sutra. Still, if this does not relate to the floating world, then stories and oral traditions would also be uncertain. After deliberating slowly and making up my mind, writing with childish and crude sentences, having written only improper and rambling colloquial language up to this point, my child Kenzo came to my elbow and said, "The memorial service is grand and lively. Also, on the first tenth day there will be a ceremonial parade, and that procession..."

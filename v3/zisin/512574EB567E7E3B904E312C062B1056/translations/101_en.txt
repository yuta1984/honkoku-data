Flood damage in mountainous areas up to this point:
- Aniwa village: 1 house, Oyagi group completely swept away
- Nagai village: Yumi group and Funaba group completely swept away  
- Sasahira village: Suishu and eta completely swept away
- Sewaki village: Hongō completely swept away, same Imori group had 1 house remaining, all others completely swept away

The above is a report from Matsushiro domain territory. The number of collapsed houses, deaths, and losses in Matsumoto domain territory is unknown.

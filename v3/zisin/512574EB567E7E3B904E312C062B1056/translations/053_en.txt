After seeking and recording [the divination], I heard that continuous earthquakes for days are auspicious, and aftershocks continue for seven days.

Same 7th year
○ Wachō-kyō-ki: Meiō 7th year, 8th month, 25th day, at the hour of the dragon, there was a great earthquake. In all my life, there had never been such an event. All people were terrified. According to ancient texts, this was also extraordinary, and it was said that the water god moved.
9th month, 2nd day, kinoto-hitsuji: Until tonight {{7 days?}} the earthquakes continued without end.

Eishō 7th year
○ Shūgai-ki: Eishō 7th year, 8th month, 7th day, night, at the hour of the tiger, there was a great earthquake. All twenty-one shrines of Tennōji completely collapsed. Should this be called the time of Buddhism's destruction?

Tenshō 13th year
○ Bonshun-ki: Tenshō 13th year, 11th month, 29th day, at midnight there was a great earthquake that lasted for a long time. It continued like this until the next day. In the coastal areas and beaches of nearby provinces, all houses were overwhelmed by waves and there were many deaths. Afterwards, the trembling continued daily for twelve days. On the last day of the month there was also a great earthquake that thundered toward the east. This was also at midnight.
12th month, 1st day: great earthquake; 2nd day: great earthquake; from the 3rd day to the 10th day: earthquakes continued daily.

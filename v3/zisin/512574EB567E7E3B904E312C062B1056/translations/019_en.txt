【Red inscription】《"Era change due to consecutive years of disasters"》
The edict stated: "Since Chōryaku, there have been successive years of calamities and the realm has been unstable.
Therefore, virtue should be bestowed upon the realm and the era name should be changed. Also, those guilty of minor crimes should be
pardoned according to precedent."

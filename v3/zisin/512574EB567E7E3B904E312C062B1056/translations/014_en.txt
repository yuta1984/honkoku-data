Ōwa 4th year - became Kōhō 1st year (964 CE)
○Record of Emperor Murakami: Ōwa 4th year, 7th month, 10th day, [the Emperor] commanded Minister Nobumitsu to
convey to the Left Minister: "An edict for changing the era name should be prepared. The purport of this matter is that although I
lack virtue yet rule the realm, this year heavenly anomalies, earthquakes, and disasters have occurred in frequent succession.
Benevolent government should be implemented and the era name changed to dispel calamities, and a great amnesty throughout the realm should immediately be proclaimed" 《Note: middle portion omitted》.
Also, numerologists report that according to the theory of the Classic of Poetry, this year corresponds to a time of dynastic change《Note: middle portion omitted》. Now
only the essential matters should be briefly enumerated.

Tenroku 4th year - became Ten'en 1st year (973 CE)
○Nihon Kiryaku - Emperor En'yū
Ten'en 1st year, 8th month, 27th day, during the hour of the Dragon (7-9 AM): The earth shook violently.
12th month, 20th day: Today the era was changed to Ten'en due to heavenly anomalies and earthquakes.
There was an amnesty decree, taxes and corvée labor were remitted, and grain was bestowed upon officials.

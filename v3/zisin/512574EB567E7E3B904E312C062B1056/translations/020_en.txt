○Fusō Ryakki, Emperor Go-Sanjō
Enkyū 2nd year, 10th month, 20th day, at midnight there was an earthquake, and the
earthen walls of houses throughout Rakuchū collapsed in various places. The bell of Tōdaiji Temple shook and fell, and pagodas of temples in various provinces were also
damaged. On the 23rd day, there were earthquakes again from time to time.

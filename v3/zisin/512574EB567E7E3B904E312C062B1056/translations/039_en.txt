○ Hyakuren-shō, Emperor Shijō's reign, Bunryaku 1st year, September 16th, Mizunoe-ne
【Red inscription】"Divination text"
At the hour of the Tiger there was a great earthquake, and all people were alarmed and agitated. According to the divination text, there will be serious military conflicts and great mourning, etc.

○ Ichidai Yōki, Emperor Shijō's reign
【Red inscription】"Change of era name"
Bunryaku 1st year, Kinoe-uma, November 5th: The era name was changed. This was due to heavenly portents and earthquakes.

Bunryaku 2nd year became Katei 1st year
○ Hennenki: Katei 1st year, September 1st: The earth shook greatly.

○ Ichidai Yōki, Emperor Shijō's reign
【Red inscription】"Change of era name"
Katei 1st year, Kinoto-hitsuji, September 19th: The era name was changed. This was due to heavenly portents and earthquakes.

○ Hyakuren-shō, Emperor Shijō's reign, Katei 3rd year, June 1st, Kanoe-tatsu
At the hour of the Rabbit there was a great earthquake, and all people were alarmed and frightened. Since the Genryaku era, there has been no such event.

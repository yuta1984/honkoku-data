Hōen 3
○Hyakurenshō [Chronicle of a Hundred Refinements]　Emperor Sutoku
　Hōen 3, 7th month, 15th day: There was a great earthquake. Unparalleled in recent times. On the 16th day there were repeated tremors.

Kyūju 2
○Daiki [Record of the Regent's Office]　Kyūju 2, 8th month, 5th day kō【sic】: At the hour of the Dragon-Snake there was a great tremor, and after nightfall there were repeated tremors.

Chōkan 2
○Ichidai Yōki [Essential Records of One Reign]　Emperor Nijō
　Chōkan 2, 2nd month, 26th day: There was a great earthquake.

Nin'an 3
○Jinsha-ki [Record of Jinsha]　Nin'an 3, 12th month, 29th day《marginal note: Shrine fire｜Divine divination and court》
　《marginal note: nobles' imperial messenger appointment and departure｜》

Great earthquake on the 24th day of the 3rd month, 4th year of Kōka, year of the Metal Sheep (1847), at the 4th hour of night (around 10 PM). Summary of detailed investigation records of collapsed houses, fire damage, flood damage, and casualties from the four districts of Minochi, Takai, Hanishina, and Sarashina under Matsushiro domain administration.

Matsushiro Domain Administration:
- Dead: 2,806 persons  - Injured: 925 persons
- Completely collapsed houses: 3,615 buildings  - Half-collapsed: 2,493 buildings  
- Dead horses: 214 head

In addition to the above, temples, shrines, and community granaries/storehouses are unclear, so a follow-up investigation will be conducted and reported later.
Written report dated the 16th day of the 4th month

## Record of the Great Earthquake

This occurred in the year Bunsei 13 (the year of Kanoe-Tora, 1830), on the 2nd day of the 7th month, at the hour of Nanatsu in the afternoon (around 2 PM). In Kyoto, a great earthquake struck, beginning with a low rumbling tremor, then continuing into a full-scale major earthquake. For a time, it seemed as though every house would collapse; people unexpectedly threw themselves to the ground, prostrated themselves on tatami mats, clung to pillars, and used garden fences as walking sticks — all praying only for their own safety.

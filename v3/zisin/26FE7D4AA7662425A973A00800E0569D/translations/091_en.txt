The above are the words from when Getsudō went boating on the Sumida River to view the moon that autumn, which Teacher Mōsai showed me, and I have finished copying them. There were several other poems appended, but I stopped without copying them.

One item: During the Kansei era, sumo wrestling was held in the Fukiage Garden for the Shogun's viewing, with match rankings. At this time, Yoshida Tsuikaze, a retainer of Lord Hosokawa and from a family versed in sumo traditions, was said to have participated.

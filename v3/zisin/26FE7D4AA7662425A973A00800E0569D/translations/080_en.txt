One: Record of Seven Spears from various places
　　　Torii Shirōzaemon, Ōhara Sakon'emon, Yata Sakujūrō, Hachiya Hannojō, Ōkubo Shichirōemon,
　　　Ōkubo Jiemon, Takagi Kyūsuke
　　These were the Seven Spears of Ishigase in Owari Province during the battle with the Oda family in Eiroku 3
　　　Ōkubo Shinpachi, Ōkubo Jinshirō, Ōkubo Shichirōemon, Ōkubo Jiemon, Abe Shirōgorō,
　　　Sugiura Hachirōgorō, Sugiura Tōshirō
　　These were the Seven Spears of Kanie in Owari Province during the battle with Takikawa in Tenshō 12
　　　Nakayama Sukeroku (also known as Kageyu), Asakura Tōjūrō (later Chikugo-no-kami), Toda Hanpei, Ōta Zendayū,
　　　Tsuji Tarōsuke, Shizume Ichizaemon, Ono Jirōemon
　　These were the Seven Spears of Ueda in Shinano Province during the attack on Sanada in Keichō 5
　　　Tsuda Magosaburō, Oda Zōshunojō, Shimokata Yasaburō, Okada Sukeemon, Sassa Hayato,
　　　Sassa Magosuke, Nakano Matabei - these seven were retainers of Oda Nobuhide
　　These were the Seven Spears of Azukizaka in Mikawa Province during the battle between the Oda and Imagawa families in Tenbun 16
　　　Katō Magoroku (Samanosuke), Katō Toranosuke (Higo-no-kami), Fukushima Ichimatsu (Saemon-no-dayū),
　　　Kasuya Sukeemon (Naizen-no-shō), Wakizaka Jinnai (Nakatsukasa), Katagiri Sukesaku (Ichimasa), Hirano Gonpei (Tōtōmi-no-kami)
　　These were the Seven Spears of Shizugatake in Ōmi Province during the battle between Hideyoshi and the Shibata family in Tenshō 11. These seven were Hideyoshi's retainers

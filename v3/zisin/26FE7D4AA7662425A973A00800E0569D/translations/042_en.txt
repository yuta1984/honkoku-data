1. Genroku 5th year, Monkey year: Domain increase and Kasama castle grounds granted to Lord Honjō Inaba-no-kami, domain increase to Lord Yanagisawa Dewa-no-kami

○Same year: Decree issued to Lord Matsudaira Shimōsa-no-kami
The recent household turmoil resulting in many retainers departing was deemed misconduct due to consistently poor governance. Although severe punishment should be imposed, considering his noble lineage, his Shirakawa domain was confiscated, 50,000 koku was taken from his total of 150,000 koku, 100,000 koku was granted back, and house arrest was ordered. Retainers Okudaira Kin'ya and Kuroya Kazuma, who served as chief retainers and caused the household disturbance, were sentenced to death by beheading for misconduct. The upper residence was confiscated for the imperial procession route, and he was to move to the lower residence under house arrest. Kunai-shōyu was to receive the same treatment.

○Same year, 8th month: House arrest pardoned, granted Yamagata castle grounds in Dewa Province and ordered into confinement

○Same year: A legal dispute arose between the hijiri (holy men) and gyōnin (ascetics) factions of Mount Kōya, with both sides engaging in difficult litigation requiring extensive deliberation. Of the gyōnin faction monks who did not accept the official inquiry regarding this disturbance, 627 people were exiled to Satsuma's Gotō Islands and other islands, while 480 people including Renmyōin who did accept were pardoned and allowed to return to the mountain.

From Tenbun 12th year, Rabbit year to Genroku 6th year, Rooster year spans 151 years.

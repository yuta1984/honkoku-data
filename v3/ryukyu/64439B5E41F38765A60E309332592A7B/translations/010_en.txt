Izumo Province (Izumo no Kuni)

Geographic Layout of Districts in Izumo Province:
Western region: Kando District
Northern region: Izumo District, Tatenui District, Aika District, Shimane District
Central region: Matsue
Southern region: Iishi District, Ōhara District, Ou District
Eastern region: Nita District, Nōgi District
Central area: Hirose, Mori
Cardinal direction indicators (North, South, East, West)

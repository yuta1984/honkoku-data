A Brief Geography of Okinawa Prefecture
　　　　　　　　　　　　　Compiled by Okinawa Normal School
　　　　General Overview
Okinawa is an island nation scattered in the sea south of Satsuma in the Western Sea Circuit. It extends from 24 degrees 10 minutes to 27 degrees 50 minutes north latitude, and its longitude ranges from 11 degrees 10 minutes to 16 degrees 52 minutes west longitude. To the east it faces the Pacific Ocean, to the west it is separated by vast ocean waters from Fujian and Quanzhou of the Qing Empire, to the north it is connected by geological formations to the Oshima Islands of Kagoshima Prefecture. To the southwest it is close to Taiwan Island. There are over 75,570 households, with a population of over 363,830 people.

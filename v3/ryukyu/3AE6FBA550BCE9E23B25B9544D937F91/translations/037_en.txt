The products are grains, hakusai jōfu (fine white cloth), white fabric with kasuri patterns, cattle and horses, timber, fish and shellfish, etc. Hakusai jōfu is particularly famous.

Yonaguni Island is located about forty-eight ri to the southwest of Iriomote. That is to say, it is not far from Taiwan Island. Its circumference is about five ri. The island has few mountains and forests, but many plains that are also fertile. There are over 320 households with a population of over 1,780. Women actually outnumber men, and the customs are simple and sincere, said to be like one island, one family.

The products are rice, miscellaneous grains, cotton, rush mats, etc.

1. Reply letter from Ōgosho-sama
　　I have respectfully perused your letter.
　　That the three Gosho-sama are increasingly in good spirits
　　and residing in good health is most joyous indeed.
　　Regarding the
　　matter of the shogunal succession, as congratulations
　　to be offered, we now send as an envoy Gushikawa
　　Ōji (Prince) with tribute as listed in the catalog. When this matter was
　　reported, there was great
　　joy expressed. Furthermore, Matsudaira Satsuma-no-kami

Products
The climate of Chuzan is mostly warm and rarely cold. There is no ice, and frost and snow rarely fall. Vegetation is always green. Of local
products, those that are the same as China are merely marked by name. Different products have their forms described in detail. Flowers and
plants are recorded together with their blooming times. As follows.

For grains, all six grains are complete. Six types of rice. Three types of wheat. Legumes include green beans, red beans, black beans,
white beans, soybeans, and small beans. Soybeans are the same as China's yellow beans with hairy pods. They grow in the seventh and eighth months. Field

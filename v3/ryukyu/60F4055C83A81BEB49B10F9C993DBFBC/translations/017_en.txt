Round fans use bamboo as the frame. Cocoon paper is pasted on them. Some are blue and some are white. Gold is sprinkled to make paintings. There are ones with mud-gold five-flower designs, called jade round fans. Only the royal palace has these. Court ladies who receive them as gifts can then use them for the first time.

Craftsmen's Tools
Axes and chisels are all similar to those in China. However, saws are made using pure iron. Their shape is like that of a sword. A handle is attached at the bottom. Teeth are arranged at the mouth end to function as a tool.

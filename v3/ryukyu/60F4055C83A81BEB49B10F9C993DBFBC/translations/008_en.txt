Five Officials (Gokansho)

In the country, only three types of people shave their hair completely like monks. One type serves as medical officials, called "Gokansho" (Five Officials). Another type serves tea duties in the royal palace, called "Sosou" and also named "Ochanoyu" (Tea Ceremony). There are six of them. There are also six people who manage the gardens. All of them shave their hair completely and wear black six-ridged caps with soft crowns and wide brims. These are called "片帽" (katabou - single caps). Over their clothing they often wear short jackets. Compared to formal robes, these are about two to three shaku shorter. They are black in color. These two types of people are always on duty. Fearing that combing hair might delay urgent matters, they all follow this simplified approach.

**Jikogura**: Also called fire phoenix. People often plant this on house walls to ward off fire. The stem resembles the euphorbia (overlord whip grass). The leaves resemble fire-prevention grass. The flowers resemble yellow chrysanthemums. There are also red varieties, called fortune and prosperity wood.

Among fruits there are lotus root, sugar cane, watermelon, cucumber, papaya, tangerines *[marginal note: several varieties]*, fragrant oranges, kumquats, Buddha's hand citron, lychees, longan, grapes, cherries, Chinese bayberries, raspberries *[marginal note: shaped like Chinese bayberries]*, chestnuts, persimmons, walnuts, loquats, plums *[marginal note: small like longan]*. Among exotic products there are banana fruits and others.

**Banana Fruit**: What the banana plant produces. The fruit is called sweet dew. The flowers are purple-red in color. The size is like a gourd. One petal opens each day. The fruit formation is like a hand. Five or six fingers hang down in a row. After harvesting and waiting for some time, the flesh texture resembles the most tender lotus root. It can be ripened. It is like sweet potato but sweet.

**Ateni**: The leaves are long with thorns on the sides. Over time they form forests with connecting vines. They are strong and sharp, suitable for making hedges and walls. The leaves can be used to make mats. The roots can be used to make rope. Those that bloom are called male trees. The flowers are white like lotus. The petals converge at the tip, spreading left and right in layers, more than ten clusters growing straight up. The five-branched stamens are exposed like staffs. Several inches long. Fragrant and intense like orange blossoms. Female trees have no flowers but bear fruit. The size is like melons. The skin texture forms nail-like protrusions. All have six ridges. Edible. It is said to be a different variety of jackfruit. Guangdong also has them. They are called pineapple.

**Chinquapin**: Also called shii. The nuts are like acorns but smaller. They are found everywhere in the mountains. Also called椎子 (tsuishi).

Among bamboos there are bitter bamboo, cat bamboo, tiger-spotted bamboo, phoenix bamboo, pole bamboo, broom bamboo, black bamboo, large pole bamboo, arrow bamboo, and 竻 bamboo. Among exotic varieties there is Guanyin bamboo.

**Guanyin Bamboo**: Grows in clusters on the ground. Leaves about one shaku long. Width three to four sun. Purple colored.

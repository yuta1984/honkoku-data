1. Written instructions regarding tomorrow's route for the Ryukyuan delegation from Matsudaira Satsuma-no-kami's Shiba residence to the Ōte gate were delivered by Inoue Kawachi-no-kami to the Commissioners of Temples and Shrines, the Grand Inspectors, and the Town Commissioners.

Eleventh month, twenty-first day - Clear weather

1. The Ryukyuan delegation came to the castle so that their music could be heard by [the Shogun].

1. The two Ryukyuan envoys proceeded from Matsudaira Satsuma-no-kami's lower Shiba residence through the back gate area of Zōjō-ji temple, then out to the town, passing by Shibaguchi gate, along the moat embankment, past Saiwaibashi bridge and alongside Satsuma-no-kami's residence, then via Hibiya gate, Yorozu wharf street, Tatsunokuchi, in front of Inoue Kawachi-no-kami's residence, past the rest area, and finally entering the castle through the Ōte gate. The procession followed the same format as the previous day.

For the route security: from the Shiba residence to the Saiwaibashi residence, two groups of foot soldiers (ashigaru) - Niwa Gonbei's group and Ido Shinbei's group; from the Saiwaibashi residence to Ōte, two groups of foot soldiers - Kaneda Sōhachirō's group and Asakura Oribe's group (with Mamiya Yukie serving as assistant since Oribe was ill) provided security. The personnel along the route provided guard duty in front of their residences as on the previous day.

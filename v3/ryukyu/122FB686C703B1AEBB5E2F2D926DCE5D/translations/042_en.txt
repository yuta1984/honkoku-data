We humbly submit this reply to His Excellency the King of Chūzan

Having received your repeated correspondence, we have been informed of the succession matter in your esteemed domain. You have specially dispatched the envoy Prince Tomigusuku, presenting regional products and thereby expressing your gratitude. Your sincere intentions have been manifested from afar, and commendations are increasingly bestowed - this brings us the utmost joy.
The bestowed gifts, as listed in the separate record, are attached upon the envoy's return. All other matters are entirely [handled by] the Satsuma Chūjō

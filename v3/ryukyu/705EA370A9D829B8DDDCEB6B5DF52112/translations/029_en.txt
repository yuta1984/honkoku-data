Fourth Month: Flame trees turn red 《Note: Trees over ten zhang tall, flowers cluster like magnolia buds. Pomegranates, mallows, daylilies, coreopsis, iris, and balsam all bloom in this month》 Iron coin flowers bloom Sweet dew appears on banana plants 《Note: Banana plants first bear fruit in this month, called sweet dew》 Tiger lilies burst into flame Sugar cane fields ripen 《Note: Bayberries also ripen in this month》 Green melons appear Bamboo shoots emerge from groves Praying mantises are born 《Note: Dragonflies are also born in this month》 Cicadas sing Crows feed their young Earthworms emerge Mole crickets chirp Bird phoenixes arrive 《Note: Name of a small bird; there is also a bird called koharuru that comes in this month》

Fifth Month: Thorny peonies bloom Hibiscus flowers 《Note: Flowers no different from autumn mallows, woody plants three to four zhang tall》 Citrus blossoms 《Note: White as snow in color, fragrance carries ten li》 Summer chrysanthemums bloom 《Note: Jasmine, gardenias, and magnolias all bloom in this month》 Lotuses flower Amaranth flourishes Peaches bear fruit 《Note: Pomegranates also fruit in this month》 Water spinach grows Black-eyed peas appear Grass does not decay but becomes fireflies Hurricane mothers rage at times

Sixth Month: Hibiscus burns the sky 《Note: From the third month through winter, especially abundant in this month》 Hanging orchids are fragrant 《Note: Cymbidiums also flower in this month》 Balloon flowers bloom Senna opens All grain is brought to the threshing ground Green beans are harvested Deer enter water and become fish 《Note: Sharks leap onto shore and transform into deer. Deer fear heat, and when they lick water with their tongues, they also transform into sharks》

Seventh Month: Magnolias are fragrant again 《Note: Crabapples and hostas both bloom in this month》 Cotton is harvested Longan fruits are round Black birds arrive 《Note: Swallows first arrive in this month》 Cold cicadas sing Sword beans appear 《Note: Loofah also appears in this month》 Wild chrysanthemums flourish 《Note: Like China's qingrang, growing abundantly in the fields》 Hairy fish form battle arrays in water 《Note: Five days before and after the first of this month, and five days before and after the first of the eighth month》《Note: Hairy fish form ranks in battle formation; other months are not like this》

Eighth Month: Cotton roses bloom 《Note: Osmanthus also flowers in this month》 Bulbuls arrive 《Note: Bird's name; there is also a bird called hensha that comes in this month》 Red adzuki beans are harvested

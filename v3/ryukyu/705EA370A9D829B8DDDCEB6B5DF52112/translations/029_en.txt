Fourth Month: Pagoda trees turn red 《Note: Trees over ten zhang tall, flowers like magnolia clustered together. Pomegranate, day lily, golden coin flower, iris, and balsam all bloom in this month》 Iron coin flowers bloom Sweet dew appears on banana plants 《Note: Banana plants first bear fruit in this month, called sweet dew》 Tiger lilies burst into flame Sugar cane fields ripen 《Note: Bayberries also ripen in this month》 Cucumbers appear Bamboo shoots emerge from groves Praying mantises are born 《Note: Dragonflies are also born in this month》 Cicadas chirp Crows nurture their young Earthworms emerge Mole crickets chirp Phoenix birds arrive 《Note: Name of a small bird. Another bird called koharu also comes in this month》

Fifth Month: Thorny peonies bloom Right-receiving flowers 《Note: Flowers no different from autumn mallow, woody plants three to four zhang tall》 Citrus flowers 《Note: Color white as snow, fragrance heard ten li away》 Summer chrysanthemums bloom 《Note: Jasmine, gardenia, and magnolia all bloom in this month》 Lotus flowers bloom Amaranth flourishes Peaches bear fruit 《Note: Pomegranates also fruit in this month》 Vegetable shoots grow Cowpeas appear Grass does not decay but becomes fireflies Hurricane mothers occasionally rage

Sixth Month: Hibiscus burns the sky 《Note: From third month through winter, most abundant in this month》 Hanging orchids are fragrant 《Note: Cymbidium also flowers in this month》 Balloon flowers bloom Cassia opens All grain is brought to the threshing ground Green beans are harvested Deer enter water and become fish 《Note: Sand fish leap to shore and transform into deer; deer fear heat and lick water with their tongues, also transforming into sand fish》

Seventh Month: Magnolia is fragrant again 《Note: Crabapple and hosta both bloom in this month》 Cotton is harvested Longan fruits are round Black birds arrive 《Note: Swallows first come in this month》 Autumn cicadas chirp Sword beans appear 《Note: Loofah also appears in this month》 Wild chrysanthemums flourish 《Note: Like Chinese herbs, growing abundantly in the fields》 Hairy fish form ranks in water 《Note: Five days before and after the new moon of this month, five days before and after the new moon of eighth month》 《Note: Hairy fish form ranks and arrays, not so in other months》

Eighth Month: Cotton roses bloom 《Note: Osmanthus also flowers in this month》 Rong-rui birds arrive 《Note: Name of a bird. Another bird called hen-sha also comes in this month》 Red adzuki beans are harvested

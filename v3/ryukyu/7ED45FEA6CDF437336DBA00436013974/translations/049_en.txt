【Figure Descriptions】
Socks (Batsu)
　Made of cloth or leather, short socks reaching above the ankle. They have an opening along the outward center line that is crossed and tied. Near the toes, a separate hole is made to insert the toe into the sandals.

Sandals (Satsu)
　Sandals are woven from fine mat grass, with a cord in front that goes between the big toe, dragging the heel while walking. Both men and women wear them.

　　Ceremonial Attendants (Gijū)
Kokuwa: Two staff bearers, two sword bearers, two spear bearers, two flag bearers, totaling fifty people. Green silk umbrella. Carried by two bearers of board palanquin. 《Note: Exterior uses Japanese satin and copper nails, interior adds colored paintings. The palanquin style is the same as bamboo palanquins》

Hōshi Ōkyū (Legal Officer and Royal Uncle): Two red staff bearers, two red board bearers, two authentic Japanese sword bearers, two authentic Japanese spear bearers 《Note: Above the spears are large red woolen flags, about one shaku long and two sun wide》. Carried by two bearers of bamboo palanquin. Same below.

Shikin Taifū (Purple-Gold Minister): Two red staff bearers, two red board bearers, two imitation long Japanese spears, two authentic Japanese swords.

Seigi Chūgi Taifū (Senior and Middle Counselor Ministers): Two red staff bearers, two red board bearers, two imitation long Japanese spears 《Note: Made of wood, square above and round below》

【Right Page】
Question: What does Ryūkyū in the southwestern corner face?
　Answer: Taiwan

Question: What are the names of the major divisions of the entire country?
　Answer: Kinai and the Eight Circuits (Hachidō)

Question: How many provinces are there?
　Answer: Eighty-four provinces

Question: How many districts are there?
　Answer: Seven hundred and eighteen districts

Question: What is the population?
　Answer: Approximately 33,169,800 people

Question: What is the annual revenue?
　Answer: 31,676,400 koku and more

【Left Page】
Question: What is the prefectural office of Yamashiro Province called?
　Answer: Kyoto Prefecture

Question: What lies to the northwest of its four borders?
　Answer: Kawachi, Settsu, Tanba

Another question: What lies to the southeast?
　Answer: Ōmi, Iga

Question: What are the names of its districts?
　Answer: The eight districts of Otokuni, Kadono, Atago, Kii, Uji, Kuse, Tsuzuki, and Sagara.

Question: What are its famous mountains?
　Answer: Hiei, Atago, Kurama, Kasagi, Jūse (Eagle Peak)

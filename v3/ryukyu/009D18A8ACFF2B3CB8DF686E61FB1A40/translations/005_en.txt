Elementary School Geography of Japan with Maps and Q&A, Volume 1
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　By Dobashi Sō of Heian

Question: Where is our Great Japan located on the globe?
　Answer: In the eastern part of Asia.

Question: How is it divided?
　Answer: With the center being Honshū, it consists of four large islands and several thousand small islands.

Question: What does its southeast face?
　Answer: The Pacific Ocean.

Question: What does its northwest face?
　Answer: The China Sea. Across the Sea of Japan are Qing China, Korea, and Manchuria.

Question: What does Karafuto (Sakhalin) in its northern corner border?
　Answer: Russia.

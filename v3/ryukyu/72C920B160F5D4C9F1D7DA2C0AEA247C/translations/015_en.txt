　　Tea Bowls　《Tea Saucers　　Tea Whisks》
The tea bowls are yellow in color.《There are no white ones》 They are decorated with blue-green floral patterns. They are said to come from Tokara. Their quality is somewhat coarse.
Those without floral patterns but with ice crack patterns come from Oshima.
A small wooden lid is made for the top of the tea bowl. It is lacquered in vermillion or black. Below, a hollow saucer is made. The craftsmanship is quite skillful.
The tea bowls are quite large. When pouring tea, it is filled only two or three parts full. Small pieces of confection are stored in a spoon for use. This
follows the Chinese method of tea presentation. Regarding the method of brewing tea in the country, tea powder is mixed into fine powder, and a small
amount is placed in the bowl. Half a bowl of boiling water is added. Using a small bamboo whisk, it is stirred dozens of times. Raising foam to fill the surface of the tea bowl is considered
proper. This is how guests are entertained.

Fourth Month: Kapok trees turn red 《Note: Trees are over ten zhang tall. Flowers grow in clusters like magnolia. Pomegranate, hollyhock, daylily, coreopsis, pollia, and balsam all bloom in this month》
Iron coin flowers bloom　Sweet dew appears on banana plants 《Note: Banana plants first bear fruit in this month, called sweet dew》　Tiger lily spits flames
Sugar cane fields ripen 《Note: Bayberries also ripen in this month》　Green melons appear　Bamboo shoots emerge in forests　Mantises
are born 《Note: Dragonflies are also born in this month》　Cicadas chirp　Crows rear young and feel shame　Earthworms emerge　Mole crickets chirp
Bird-phoenix comes 《Note: Name of a small bird. There is also a bird called "ancient haha-ru" that also comes in this month》

Fifth Month: Thorny peonies bloom　Right-receiving flowers bloom 《Note: Flowers are no different from autumn hollyhocks. The tree trunk is three to four zhang tall》　Kumquat
flowers bloom 《Note: Color is white like snow, fragrance can be smelled ten li away》　Summer chrysanthemums bloom 《Note: Jasmine, gardenia, and magnolia all bloom in this month》　Lotus has flowers
Amaranth puts forth ears　Peaches bear fruit 《Note: Pomegranates also fruit in this month》　Water spinach grows　Cowpeas emerge　Grass
does not decay but becomes fireflies　Hurricane mothers rage at times

Sixth Month: Hibiscus burns the sky 《Note: Blooms from third month until winter. This month is most abundant》　Hanging orchids are fragrant 《Note: Cymbidium also flowers in this month》
Bellflower blooms　Kuai-ming opens　All grain is brought to the threshing floor　Mung beans are harvested　Deer enter
water and become fish 《Note: Sand fish leap onto shore and transform into deer. Deer fear heat and lick water with their tongues. They also transform into sand fish》

Seventh Month: Magnolia is fragrant again 《Note: Crabapple and hosta both bloom in this month》　Cotton is harvested　Longan fruits become round
Black birds come 《Note: Swallows first come in this month》　Cold cicadas chirp　Sword beans emerge 《Note: Luffa also emerges in this month》　Wild
chrysanthemums put forth ears 《Note: Like Chinese qing-rang, growing abundantly throughout the fields》　Hairy fish form formations in water 《Note: Five days before and after the new moon of this month, and five days before and after the new moon of the eighth month》
《Note: Hairy fish arrange themselves in ranks and form formations. Other months are not like this》

Eighth Month: Hibiscus flowers 《Note: Osmanthus also flowers in this month》　Rong-rui arrives 《Note: Name of a bird. There is also a bird called "hate-to-kill" that also comes in this
month》　Red azuki beans are harvested

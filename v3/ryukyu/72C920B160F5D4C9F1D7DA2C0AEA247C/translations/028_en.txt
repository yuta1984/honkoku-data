The length of bows is over seven shaku. When stood on the ground, they reach the height of house eaves. Compared to Chinese arrows, the arrows are shorter by about one grip's length. When shooting, one must stand the bow on the ground. When holding the target, it is not held at the center but rather at the narrow part below. Drawing the bowstring and releasing arrows, all use thumb rings (yugake) according to ancient custom. The range is not very far. An old record states: "Shooting beyond two hundred paces has never been seen."

Monthly Calendar

First Month: Peach blossoms bloom luxuriantly 《Note: Blue peach, crimson peach, and cherry peach all bloom in this month》 Vinca flowers begin to flourish 《Note: Blooms in four seasons. This month is most abundant》
New broad beans are eaten　Nightingale tongues have melody　Snakes emerge from holes　Lightning first appears　Thunder
makes its voice heard　Loquats ripen 《Note: No different from China. Shape is somewhat long like dates. Eaten fresh on New Year's Day, serving as the first of all fruits》

Second Month: Magnolia writes in the sky　Crabapple turns red　Kerria flowers bloom　Spring chrysanthemums are fragrant
《Note: Lilies and corn poppies both bloom in this month》　Kumquat fruits turn red 《Note: Kumquat, also called "ten-li fragrance." Flowers bloom in the sixth month. Fruits are red and clustered, filling the trees in this month》
Duckweed begins to grow　Field beans are harvested　Crickets chirp　Hibernating dragons emerge into the sea

Third Month: Orchids are fragrant　Carnation flowers bloom 《Note: Roses, wild roses, vines, and poppies all bloom in this month》　Perilla
grows　All beans are sown　Snake gourds appear in markets　Wheat reaches autumn　Rainbows first appear

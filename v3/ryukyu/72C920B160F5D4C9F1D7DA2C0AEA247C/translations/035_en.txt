**Kikoura (Portulaca)**: Also called "fire phoenix." Commonly planted on the walls of houses to ward off fire. The stem resembles hawang whip grass. The leaves are similar to fire-prevention grass. The flowers resemble yellow chrysanthemums. There are also red varieties called "fortune tree."

For fruits, there are lotus root sugar cane, watermelon, green melon, papaya, oranges (several varieties), fragrant orange, kumquat, Buddha's hand citron, lychee, longan, grapes, cherry peach, yangmei, raspberry (shaped like yangmei), chestnut, persimmon, walnut, loquat, and plum (small like longan). Special products include banana fruit and others.

**Banana Fruit**: The fruit produced by banana plants. The fruit is called "sweet dew." The flowers are purple-red in color, about the size of a gourd. One petal opens each day. The fruiting is hand-like, with five or six fingers hanging in parallel. When harvested and left for a long time, the texture resembles the most tender lotus root. It can be ripened. It is like sweet potato but sweet.

**Ateni (Pineapple)**: The leaves are long with thorns on the sides. Over time they form forests with connecting vines. They are hard and sharp, suitable for making hedges. The leaves can be used to make mats, and the roots can be used to make rope. Those that bloom are male plants. The flowers are white like lotus. The petals converge to a point, bursting and overlapping left and right in more than ten clusters growing straight up. The five-branched stamens are exposed like a staff. Several inches long. Fragrant and intense like orange blossoms. Female plants have no flowers but bear fruit. The size is like a melon. The skin has raised nail-like patterns, all six-ridged. Edible. It is said to be a variant of jackfruit. Guangdong also has this, called "phoenix pear."

**Chinquapin**: Also called "shii." The nuts are like acorns but smaller. Found everywhere in the mountains. Also called "椎子" (tsuishi).

For bamboo, there are bitter bamboo, cat bamboo, tiger-spotted bamboo, phoenix bamboo, pole bamboo, broom bamboo, black bamboo, large pole bamboo, arrow bamboo, and 竻 bamboo. Special products include Kannon bamboo.

**Kannon Bamboo**: Grows in clusters on the ground. Leaves about one shaku long. Width three to four sun. Purple in color.

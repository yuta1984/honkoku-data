Animals include cattle, horses, sheep, pigs, dogs, cats, deer, monkeys, and wild boars. There are no tigers, rabbits, or roe deer. 《marginal note: The Ming Yitong Zhi》《marginal note: mentions as local products》《marginal note: bears, brown bears, jackals, and wolves,》《marginal note: but upon current examination, none of these exist》

Domestic animals include chickens, ducks, and geese. Unusual domestic animals include Taihe chickens, which are particularly small compared to ordinary chickens, with short legs and long tails. The breed originates from the Seven Islands.

Birds include sparrows, crows, pigeons, herons, gulls, ducks, bulbuls, turtle doves, green pigeons《marginal note: come in the》《marginal note: twelfth month》, wild doves, quails, and wagtails《marginal note: commonly》《marginal note: called》《marginal note: divine》《marginal note: birds》, field birds, pheasants, wagtails, cuckoos, mandarin ducks, and swallows. Swallows come in July but do not nest in human dwellings. Hawks are blown in from outer islands by northeast winds in September. Geese occasionally appear but do not come regularly. Cranes may sometimes be seen, but this too is rare. Exotic birds include guharu and others.

【Guharu】Golden-yellow plumage with long beak and short tail. Sings in April.

【Mashi】Wing feathers are green with white eyebrows. Comes in September. Also【Ishikyuushi】resembles mashi.

【Wufeng】Also called Queen Mother Bird. Comes in April.

【Hensha】Plumage resembles hawks but somewhat smaller. Comes in August.

【Yongrui】Wings are grayish-brown with black head. Comes in August.

【Ishikyudoku】Plumage resembles sparrows. Comes in October and sings in spring.

【Modokuri】Green plumage. Comes in October.

Insects include rats, bats, and gecko lizards. Geckos can make sounds like sparrows, both in winter and summer. Lizards live in water ponds with red bellies and golden light on their backs. There are also small four-legged green snakes commonly seen.

Fourth Month: Tigu flowers bloom red 《marginal note: trees over ten zhang tall, flowers cluster together like magnolia buds; pomegranate, daylily, golden coin flower, iris, and balsam all bloom in this month》 Iron coin flowers bloom Sweet dew appears on banana plants 《marginal note: banana plants begin to fruit this month, called sweet dew》 Tiger lilies blaze forth Sugar cane fields ripen 《marginal note: bayberries also ripen this month》 Cucumbers appear Bamboo shoots emerge in groves Praying mantises are born 《marginal note: dragonflies are also born this month》 Cicadas chirp Crows feed their young Earthworms emerge Mole crickets chirp Phoenix birds arrive 《marginal note: name of a small bird; another bird called guharu also comes this month》

Fifth Month: Thorny peonies bloom Yunana flowers bloom 《marginal note: flowers identical to hollyhock, woody plant three to four zhang tall》 Orange jasmine flowers bloom 《marginal note: white as snow in color, fragrance carries ten li》 Summer chrysanthemums bloom 《marginal note: jasmine, gardenia, and magnolia all bloom this month》 Lotus flowers bloom Amaranth sends up stalks Peaches bear fruit 《marginal note: pomegranates also fruit this month》 Vegetable sprouts grow Cowpeas appear Grass transforms into fireflies without rotting Hurricane season brings fierce storms

Sixth Month: Hibiscus blazes across the sky 《marginal note: blooms from third month through winter, especially abundant this month》 Hanging orchids are fragrant 《marginal note: orchids also flower this month》 Balloon flowers bloom Cassia blooms All grain is brought to threshing floors Green beans are harvested Deer enter water and become fish 《marginal note: sand fish leap onto shore and transform into deer; deer fear heat and lick water with their tongues, transforming back into sand fish》

Seventh Month: Magnolias bloom fragrantly again 《marginal note: begonias and hostas both bloom this month》 Cotton is harvested Longan fruits ripen Swallows arrive 《marginal note: swallows first arrive in this month》 Autumn cicadas sing Sword beans appear 《marginal note: loofah also appears this month》 Wild chrysanthemums send up shoots 《marginal note: like Chinese qingnang, growing throughout the fields》 Hairy fish form formations in water 《marginal note: five days before and after the first day of this month and five days before and after the first day of eighth month》 《marginal note: hairy fish line up in formations, but not in other months》

Eighth Month: Cotton roses bloom 《marginal note: osmanthus also flowers this month》 Rongrui birds arrive 《marginal note: bird name; another bird called hensha also comes this month》 Red adzuki beans are harvested

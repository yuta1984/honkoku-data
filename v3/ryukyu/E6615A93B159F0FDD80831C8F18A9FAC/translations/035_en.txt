【Nago orchid】Leaves are short and thick, the same size as osmanthus leaves, barely finger-length. Blooms in the third and fourth months, no different from orchids. Eight to nine clusters bloom together on one stem, with a clear and superior fragrance that surpasses orchids. It grows among the rocks and cliffs of Mount Nago, requiring no soil or water, sometimes growing parasitically on tree branches or wrapped in palm bark and suspended. There is also 【wind orchid】with leaves relatively longer than orchids, fragrant like galanga or fennel. When planted in bamboo baskets and hung in the wind, it propagates extremely easily. The common people all value orchids and call them "Confucius flowers."

【Millet orchid】Also called shiran. Leaves are like phoenix tails and flowers like pearl orchids. 【Wild peony】Local name is 𦬯 flower. Leaves are no different from peonies. Flowers bloom in clusters in the second and third months, hanging abundantly like bells. White petals with purple hues, sandalwood-colored center, bowl-sized, extremely fragrant. Chewing the leaves serves as mouth freshener. Grows on Mount Taihei. 【Patterned day lily】Also called winter joy flower. Flowers are like day lilies but particularly small, with leaves having blue and white alternating patterns.

【Mountain perilla flower】Also called monkey mat flower. No flowers, no trunk, emerging from soil growing less than one shaku tall, leaves like banana but smaller, firm and thick with patterns.

【Thunder mountain flower】Local name is lucky eggplant. Leaves like iron-stem begonia, flowers like morning glory but somewhat smaller, crow-jade colored. Blooms from fourth and fifth months until eleventh month, bearing bean-like seeds. Pods are like pomegranate chambers containing dozens of seeds that can be planted.

It produces small yellow flowers and bears fruit like oranges that are edible. There is also another tree with the local name 【arabo】. All its leaves resemble fukugi, also growing in opposite pairs, with white flowers resembling plum blossoms, fruiting in the eleventh and twelfth months. Both are called gentleman trees. 《marginal note: The leaf patterns are paired like threads in weaving, the center becomes transparent when lit by sunlight, turning golden yellow. The legendary fighting-carved tree described as having orange-like leaves must be this very one》

【Iron tree】This is the phoenix-tail cycad, also called sea palm. The body has banana-like leaves that are firm and erect, growing in pairs, gracefully drooping like a phoenix tail. When lit by sunlight, there is a transparent line in the center with no shadow. It does not wither through the four seasons and is planted everywhere.

【Black wood】Leaves resemble osmanthus, growing straight up. The exterior appears no different from ordinary trees, but the heartwood is black in color. However, there are also those with white grain. There is also 【red wood】.

【Oil tree】Leaves resemble oranges, fruit is as large as oranges but inedible. Used for pressing oil. There is also 【fukuman tree】. Tree height is several feet, leaves resemble hibiscus but somewhat smaller, flowers cluster like small oranges in red color and are edible. There is another tree with leaves resembling holly, over ten feet tall, flowers cluster like jujube fruits, similar to Chinese privet berries, sweet and sour and edible. Can also be used to dye things blue-lotus color, called yamamai and also called nomago. This must be green essence.

【Kubatisu】Tree height over twenty to thirty feet, leaves large like persimmon leaves, flowers five-forked, fruit in eighth and ninth months resembles green olives but somewhat flattened, taste fragrant and sweet. Also found in Fujian, where it is called judo.

【Una】Tree height over thirty to forty feet, leaves resemble white paulownia, opens flowers like Chinese autumn mallow in summer season, with yellow petals and sandalwood heart.

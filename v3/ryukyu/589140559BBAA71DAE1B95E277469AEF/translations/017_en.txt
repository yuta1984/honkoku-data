【Figure Descriptions】
Clothing
　No belt
　Has collar
　Small notch
　Shape
　All clothing is loose and wide. There is no crossing collar in the back. The sleeves are two to three shaku in size. The length does not exceed the fingertips. The end of the right collar has a notched shape. There is no clothing belt. Most are made from banana fiber cloth and banana kudzu. They have beautiful patterns and colors. Men's and women's clothing are all the same. This is called "kin" (coverlet).

Belt
　Large belts are one jō four to five shaku in length. The width is six to seven sun. They are wound around the waist three to four times. Mixed flower brocade ground is considered most precious. Large flower brocade belts come next. Those with coiled dragons on yellow or red ground come after that. Lower-grade ones are all mixed-color flower belts.

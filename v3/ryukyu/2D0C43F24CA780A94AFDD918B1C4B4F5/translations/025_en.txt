On the second day of the twelfth month of Shōtoku 4 (1714), King Shō Kei of Chūzan had the congratulatory envoy Prince Yonagusuku and gratitude envoy Prince Kin present tribute goods. 《marginal note: Bunro Sō》

On the thirteenth day of the eleventh month of Kyōhō 3 (1718), King Shō Kei of Chūzan had the congratulatory envoy Prince Goeku and Nishihira Ōyakata present tribute goods. 《marginal note: Kyōhō Nikki》

On the fifteenth day of the twelfth month of Kan'en 1 (1748), King Shō Kei of Chūzan had the congratulatory envoy Prince Gushikawa and Yonahara Ōyakata present tribute goods. 《marginal note: Rekishi Yōryaku》

On the fifteenth day of the twelfth month of Hōreki 2 (1752), King Shō Boku of Chūzan had the gratitude envoy Prince Nakijin and others present tribute goods. 《marginal note: Rekishi Yōryaku》

In the eleventh month of Meiwa 1 (1764), King Shō Boku of Chūzan had the congratulatory envoy Prince Yomitanzan and others present tribute goods. 《marginal note: Sangoku Tsūran, Hayamizu Shiki》

On the second day of the twelfth month of Kansei 2 (1790), King Shō Boku of Chūzan had the congratulatory envoy Prince Ginowan and others present tribute goods. 《marginal note: Ryūkyū Dan》

On the sixth day of the twelfth month of Kansei 8 (1796), King Shō Sei of Chūzan had the gratitude envoy Prince Ōgimi and Yasumura Ōyakata present tribute goods. 《marginal note: Rinchi Shōroku》

On the twenty-third day of the eleventh month of Bunka 3 (1806), King Shō Kō of Chūzan had the gratitude envoy Prince Yomitanzan and Oroku Ōyakata present tribute goods.

In the eleventh month of Tenpō 3 (1832), King Shō Iku of Chūzan had the gratitude envoy Prince Tomigusuku and Takushi Ōyakata present tribute goods.

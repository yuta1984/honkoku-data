King Shō En────────────────────────
　　　　　　　　　　　　　[To King Shō Sen'i]

His childhood name was Chūtoku, and his given name was Kanemaru. He was born in the 15th year of Yongle (1415), the year of kinoto-hitsuji. From birth, he possessed natural dignity and the appearance of a dragon and phoenix. He also had a birthmark on the sole of his foot, golden in color. Before ascending to the throne, a man named Anri from Tomari village saw him once and said, "This person has the virtue to rule over hundreds of millions of people." He died on the 28th day of the 7th month of the 12th year of Chenghua (1476). He reigned for seven years and lived to be 67 years old.

King Shō Nei────────────────
　　　　　　　　　　　[To King Shō Hō]

His childhood name was Shitoku. He was born in the 43rd year of Jiajing (1564), the year of kinoe-ne. He ascended the throne in the 17th year of Wanli (1589) and died on the 19th day of the 9th month of the 48th year of the same era (1620). He reigned for 33 years and lived to be 57 years old. He paid tribute in the 15th year of Keichō (1610).

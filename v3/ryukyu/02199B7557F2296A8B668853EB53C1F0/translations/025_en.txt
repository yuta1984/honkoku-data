Appendix

Complete Map of the Ryukyu Kingdom

The Ryukyu Kingdom has three provinces: Chūzan is Nakagami Province, Sannan is Shimajiri Province, and Sanhoku is Kunigami Province. There are thirty-six administrative districts in total, called magiri. 《Note: Magiri is like a castle town, or refers to counties and prefectures》 The lord of each magiri is called aji. There are thirty-six affiliated islands, and Kikaigashima consists of twelve islands. These are collectively called the Five Islands and Seven Islands. The products of these islands include sweet potatoes (Ryukyu potatoes), ramie fiber (Ryukyu ramie), banana fiber, colorful fish, conch, tortoiseshell, sea cucumber, and stone fungus, totaling more than thirty varieties.

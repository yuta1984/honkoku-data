Socks (Tabi)
　Made of cloth or leather, the socks are short, extending above the ankle. They have an opening along the center line facing outward, which is crossed and tied. Near the toe area, a separate hole is made to accommodate the big toe for wearing with straw sandals.

Sandals (Zōri)
　Sandals are woven from fine mat grass, with a cord at the front that separates the big toe. One walks dragging the heels. Both men and women wear these.

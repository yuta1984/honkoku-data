【Right Page・Diagram and names in upper section, explanatory text in lower section】
Socks
　　Made of cloth or leather, the socks are short, reaching just above the ankle. The center line on the outside forms an opening that is crossed and tied. Near the toes, a separate hole is made for the big toe to fit into the grass sandals.

Sandals
　　Sandals are woven from fine mat grass, with a cord in front that separates the big toe. They drag the heel when walking. Both men and women wear them.

Clothing
　No belt
　Collar has
　small notch
　(cut)
　　All clothing is loose and wide, without rear overlapping panels (okumi). Sleeves are large, two to three feet long, not extending past the fingertips. The right collar end has a notched cut, and there are no clothing belts. Most are made from banana fiber cloth or banana hemp. They feature striped patterns with interwoven colors. Men's and women's clothing are all the same, called "kin" (coverlet).

【Left Page・Diagram and names in upper section, explanatory text in lower section】
Belt
　　Large belts are one jō four to five feet long and six to seven inches wide, wrapped around the waist three to four times. Those with mixed floral brocade ground are most precious, large floral brocade belts come next, and those with dragon patterns on yellow or red ground follow after that. Lower grade ones are all mixed-color floral belts.

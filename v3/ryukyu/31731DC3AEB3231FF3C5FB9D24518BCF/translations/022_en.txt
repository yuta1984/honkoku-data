　The Cai clan originally came from Jinjiang County, Quanzhou Prefecture, Fujian Province《marginal note: According to the Ming Veritable Records, when Magistrate Cai Jing presented tribute in the 5th year of Chenghua, he stated that his ancestor was from Nan'an County》. They are descendants of Xiang, the Song dynasty Duanming Scholar, spanning twelve generations with a total of eighty-one people.

The tree called jūrikō (ten-li fragrance) is also used for hedges. This tree is described in the products section. Common people's houses use bamboo spike fences. Rice granaries have floor heights of four to five shaku, with sixteen pillars installed under the floor, constructed so that people can walk through between them. All government storehouses are built in this manner. In villages, they come together to build one structure, store rice within it, and take turns keeping watch by dividing up the days.

○Women's Market
In this country, at a place called Tsujiyama along the seashore, there is a market twice daily, morning and evening. The merchants are all women without exception. The goods they trade are fish and shellfish, sweet potatoes, tofu, wooden vessels, porcelain dishes, pottery, wooden combs, straw sandals, and other coarse goods.

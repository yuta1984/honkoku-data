【Nago Orchid】Leaves short and thick, same size as osmanthus leaves, barely finger-like. Blooms in third and fourth months, no different from orchids, with eight to nine flowers clustering on one stem, fragrance clear and surpassing orchids. Found among rocks and crags of Mount Nago, requiring no soil or water, sometimes parasitic on tree forks, or wrapped in palm bark and suspended. There is also 【Wind Orchid】with leaves somewhat longer than orchids, fragrance like galangal and fennel. Using split bamboo as pots and hanging in the wind, it propagates extremely easily. Commoners all esteem orchids and call them "Confucius flowers."

【Millet Orchid】Also called Zhi orchid, leaves like phoenix tail, flowers like pearl orchid. There are also 【Pine Orchid】【Bamboo Orchid】【Stick Orchid】《Note: Shape like coral tree, green colored with no leaves, flowers emerge from branch forks, resembling orchids but smaller》.

【Wild Peony】Local name 𦬯 flower, leaves no different from peonies. Flowers bloom in second and third months forming clusters, hanging like bells. White petals with purple halo, sandalwood center, large as bowls and extremely fragrant. Its leaves are chewed as mouth freshener. Grows on Mount Daping. There are also 【Wild Begonia】【Immortal Rod】【Broom Peach】【Wild Orchid】《Note: namely China's qing rang》.

【Patterned Daylily Flower】Also called Happy Winter Flower, flowers like daylily but particularly small, leaves have blue and white alternating patterns.

【Mountain Su Flower】Also called Monkey Mat Flower, no flowers, no trunk, emerges from soil less than one shaku tall. Leaves like banana but small, firm and thick with patterns.

【Thunder Mountain Flower】Local name Ji eggplant, leaves like iron-stem begonia, flowers like morning glory but somewhat smaller in crow-green color. Blooms from fourth-fifth months until eleventh month, seeds form like beans, pods like pomegranate chambers containing dozens of seeds suitable for planting.

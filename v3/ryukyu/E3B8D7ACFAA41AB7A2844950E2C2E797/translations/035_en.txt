The leaves can be used to dye green color, it produces small yellow flowers and bears fruit like oranges that are edible. There is also another tree with the local name 【arabo】. All leaves resemble garcinia, also growing in pairs at nodes, with white flowers like plum blossoms, fruiting in the eleventh and twelfth months. Both are called "gentleman trees." 《Note: The leaf patterns are paired like threads in weaving, the center edges shine translucently in sunlight creating a golden-yellow color. The legendary fighting-carving tree with orange-like leaves is probably this one》

【Iron tree】This is the phoenix-tail cycad, also called sea palm. The trunk has cycad leaves that grow rigidly and straight in pairs, drooping like phoenix tails reflecting in sunlight, with one translucent line in the center casting no shadow, never withering in any season, planted everywhere.

【Black wood】Leaves are like osmanthus, growing straight up, externally no different from ordinary trees, but the heartwood is black. However, there are also those with white grain. There is also 【red wood】.

【Oil tree】Leaves resemble orange, fruit is as large as oranges but inedible, used for pressing oil. There is also 【fukuman tree】, several feet tall, leaves like hibiscus but smaller, flowers clustered red like small oranges and edible. There is also a tree with leaves like holly, over ten feet tall, flowers clustered like jujube, growing like Chinese privet, sweet and sour, edible, and can be used to dye things blue-violet. Called sanmai, also called nomasago. This should be the blue essence plant.

【Kubadis】Tree height over twenty to thirty feet, leaves large like persimmon leaves, flowers five-forked, fruit in eighth and ninth months like green olives but somewhat flattened, taste fragrant and sweet. Also found in Fujian, called inudo.

【Una】Tree height over thirty to forty feet, leaves like white paulownia, blooming in summer like Chinese autumn mallow with yellow petals and sandalwood center.

On the Five Officials (Wukan Zheng)

In the country, only three types of people all shave their hair like monks. One type is medical officials called "Five Officials" (Wukan Zheng). One type consists of those who serve tea in the royal palace, called "Sosou" and also known as "Honorable Tea Service," numbering six people. There are also six people who supervise garden irrigation, all of whom completely shave their hair and wear black six-ridged soft-crowned hats with wide brims, called "片帽" (katabōshi, one-sided hats). Over their clothing they mostly wear a short outer garment called "tankake," which is about two to three shaku shorter than regular robes and is black in color. These two types of people are all engaged in duties where they may be summoned at any time, and since taking time to comb their hair might interfere with official business, they all do this to save time, it is said.

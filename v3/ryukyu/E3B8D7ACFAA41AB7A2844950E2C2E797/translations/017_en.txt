Brazier
The water and fire brazier is made for lightweight portability, with a copper surface and tin lining. One holds fire and one holds water. A small wooden frame is made on the outside to hold them. Below are two tiers of black lacquered cosmetic boxes, three or four in number, which store tea utensils and are placed in tea carriers. The King of China assigns two scholars to manage these, and when guests go out for excursions, they carry them along.

Table (Book Stand)
The curved reclining table follows an ancient style, with the body curved like a fan shape, about one shaku high. A cushion is placed on top to lean against. The book stand is like a mirror stand with a small base, about half a shaku high, used while sitting on the floor.

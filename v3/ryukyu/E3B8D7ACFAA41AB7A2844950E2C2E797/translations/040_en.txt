Mosquitoes and flies all emerge in winter. Ants are the same as in China, but their abdomens shine like crystal, and when killed, they emit droplet-like water.

Scaled fish include sharks, carp, crucian carp, eels, loaches, shrimp, goldfish, and silver-thread fish. Unusual products include hair fish, needle fish, swallow fish, and others. Their green and red colors, green scales, and red patterns intermingle in five colors, some round and some long, too numerous to count. The local people call them by their colors and shapes, but none have proper names. When sea fish are cut into raw slices and viewed in dark places at night, they all appear bright and translucent with green firelight, colored like the luminescent wood of Rehe.

【Hair fish】Small and thin, appearing rotten from the outside but flavorful when chewed. Fujian people all value this as a delicacy. They appear in formation in the sea for five days before and after the first day of the seventh month, and five days before and after the first day of the eighth month. In other months they do not.

【Needle fish】Head shaped like a needle, also called halfbeak. 【Boot fish】Head long like a boot.

【Swallow fish】Like swallows with wings and able to fly. Ancient name is ray, commonly called flying fish.

Shelled creatures include turtles, soft-shelled turtles, and terrapins. Unusual ones include hawksbill turtles and others.

【Hawksbill turtle】Shell like turtle or terrapin, with head and tail somewhat pointed, head tinged with light red.

【Seahorse】Horse head with fish body, no scales, flesh like pork. Quite difficult to obtain; those who get them first present them to the king.

【Stone octopus】Round head with eight arms growing below, no legs. Local people all use this in cooking.

【Swimming crab】Meat is of finest quality, tastes like crab but larger, with gentle nature. 【Crabs】have different species of various sizes. There are small five-colored crabs with two claws, left large and right small. The small one is used for feeding, the large one for defense. Only the large claw is vermillion red, called "fire-illuminator." Small crabs that live in snail shells are called "parasites."

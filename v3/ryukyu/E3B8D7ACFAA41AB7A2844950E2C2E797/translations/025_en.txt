Women's Market {{Small text: Money, Women's Ornaments}}
According to old records about the marketplace, it was formerly located on flat ground in front of Tianfei Temple, east of the Chinese envoy's residence.
Later it moved to Horse Market Street, and now the market has relocated to a slope along the coast of Tsuji Mountain. The market convenes twice daily, morning and evening.
The market has no men - all commerce is conducted by women. The goods sold are fish, shrimp, sweet potatoes, tofu, wooden implements, porcelain dishes, pottery, wooden combs, straw sandals, and other humble items. Officials' households mostly do not patronize this market.

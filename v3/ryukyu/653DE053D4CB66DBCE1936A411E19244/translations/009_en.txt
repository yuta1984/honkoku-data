From Satsujiki Officials down to scribes, scribes also serve in these positions.

Kume Assistant Administration Officials: approximately six ranks

Purple Gold Grand Master: One person, Junior Second Rank. Adds the title of Assistant Legal Administrator, named Sōri Tōei.
The administration governs Kume village affairs as the most honored, presiding over tributary礼仪, correspondence exchanges, and various matters.

Senior Counselor Grand Master: Senior Third Rank. Adds Herald position, also named Mōshiguchi-za: Junior Third Rank
Middle Counselor Grand Master: Senior Fourth Rank. All have no fixed numbers.

Chief Secretary: Two persons, Senior Fifth Rank (or Junior Fourth Rank). They accompany the Purple Gold Grand Master and jointly manage various affairs.

Chief Interpreter: Junior Fourth Rank. Adds Ōtatri position. Vice Interpreter: Junior Fifth Rank. Vice Interpreter: Senior Seventh Rank. All have no fixed numbers. They specialize in tributary affairs. Those who remain at the Ryukyu Hall in Fuzhou, Fujian are called Remaining Interpreters.

All of the above positions are filled by talented scholars from Kume prefecture.

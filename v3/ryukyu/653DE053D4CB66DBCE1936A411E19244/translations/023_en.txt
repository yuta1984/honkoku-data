The Sai clan originally came from Jinjiang County, Quanzhou Prefecture, Fujian《Marginal note: According to the Ming Veritable Records, in the fifth year of Chenghua, when Chief Secretary Sai Kei came to pay tribute, he stated that his ancestor was from Nan'an County》. They are descendants of Xiang, an Academician of the Duanming Hall of the Song Dynasty, with twelve generations totaling eighty-one people.

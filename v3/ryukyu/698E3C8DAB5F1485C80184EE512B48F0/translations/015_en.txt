In the 9th month of Keian 2 (1649), King Shō Shitsu of Chūzan sent gratitude envoy Kushikawa Aji and others to present tribute goods. 《Ryukyu Jiryaku》 They also paid respects at the shrine of Mount Nikkō.

On September 20th of Jōō 2 (1653), King Shō Shitsu of Chūzan sent congratulatory envoy Kunigami Aji and others to present tribute goods. 《Razan Bunshū, Wakan Gōun, Kinsei Buke Hennen Ryaku》 They also paid respects at the shrine of Mount Nikkō.

On July 28th of Kanbun 11 (1671), King Shō Tei of Chūzan sent gratitude envoy Prince Kin and others to present tribute goods. 《Manten Nikki》 They also paid respects at the shrine of Mount Nikkō. 《Ryukyu Jiryaku, Rekidai Bikō》

On April 11th of Tenna 2 (1682), King Shō Tei of Chūzan sent congratulatory envoy Nago Aji Onna Oyakata and others to present tribute goods. 《Manten Nikki, Kanro Sō》

On November 18th of Hōei 7 (1710), King Shō Eki of Chūzan sent congratulatory envoy Prince Misato Tomori Oyakata, gratitude envoy Prince Tomigusuku Yosa Oyakata and others to present tribute goods. 《Ryukyu Heishi Kiji》 They also paid respects at the shrine of Mount Tōei. This was the first time that Chūzan envoys came to Mount Tōei without going to Mount Nikkō.

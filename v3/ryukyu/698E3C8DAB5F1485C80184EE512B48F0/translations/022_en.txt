Correction of Errors
Examination of the theory that the Sea Palace (Watatsumi no Miya) is the present-day Ryukyu Kingdom

According to one theory, "What historical records refer to as the Sea God's Palace is the present-day Ryukyu among the southern sea islands." Upon examination, the sea god Watatsumi originates from what was born of Izanagi no Mikoto. Watatsumi no Mikoto (Shōdō no Mikoto) is presumably a sea god, and Toyotamahiko was a chieftain of the sea islands. Therefore it is said that "the sea god was a person from the Miyazaki area of Hyūga Province who governed maritime affairs by the imperial edict of the heavenly gods." If this is so, then places like the various islands and Ryukyu were under the jurisdiction of the sea god Toyotamahiko at that time.

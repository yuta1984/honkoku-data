The climate of Chuzan is mostly warm with little cold, without ice, frost, or snow, which falls only rarely. Vegetation remains green throughout the year. For local products that are the same as those in China, only their names will be noted. For different products, their forms and characteristics will be described in detail, and for flowering plants, their blooming periods will also be recorded, as follows.

As for grains, all six types of grains are available. There are six varieties of rice, three types of wheat, and legumes including mung beans, red beans, black beans, white beans, soybeans, and small red beans.

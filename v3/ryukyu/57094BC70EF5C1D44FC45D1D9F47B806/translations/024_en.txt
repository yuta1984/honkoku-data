　May 29th, Meiji 8th year　Minister of State
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　To Ryukyu Domain

1. Throughout the domain, the Meiji era name shall be adopted, and all annual ceremonies and rituals shall be conducted in accordance with official proclamations.
1. Criminal law statutes shall be implemented as prescribed. Therefore, two or three persons in charge shall travel to Tokyo to investigate this matter.
1. To report on the current state of educational affairs, approximately ten young men shall be selected and sent to Tokyo.

You shall understand these conditions accordingly; this message is hereby conveyed.
　June 3rd, Meiji 8th year　Minister of State

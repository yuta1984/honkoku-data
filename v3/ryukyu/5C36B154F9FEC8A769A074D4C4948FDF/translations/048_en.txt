On the 9th day of the 12th month of Tiger year, Inken went to the residence of Satsuma-no-kami, where there was a go match between his disciple Aihara Kaken and Yarazato-shi. Five crafted items were sent to Yara Mōtsuke. Among these were items to be transmitted as teachings, so in order to perform this transmission, he went to Satsuma-no-kami's residence and after completing the bestowal, there was a go match between Yara Mōtsuke and Kaken.
　　　　　　　First player　　　Yarazato-shi

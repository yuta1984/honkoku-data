In the winter of Kanoe-Tora (1710), Yarazato-shi of the Ryukyu Kingdom was in Edo accompanying the princes. The younger brother of Satsuma-ko's Urin Yoshitaka, having received official orders from Satsuma-ko, arranged for Yarazato to play go. Yarazato placed three stones as a handicap and faced the national go master Hon'inbo. I was present nearby observing this and recorded the following.

Record of the Divine Response of Tianfei

Tianfei was a daughter of the Lin family from Meizhou Islet in Putian. 《Marginal note: Zhang Xueli's record says that Tianfei was a daughter of the Cai family from Houyu. This is incorrect.》 Her father's name was Yuan. 《Marginal note: His courtesy name was Weique. Her mother was of the Wang family. Another account says she was the sixth daughter of Lin Fu.》 At the beginning of the Song dynasty, he served as Capital Patrol Inspector. The goddess was born with divine spirit.

As a young girl, when looking into a well with other girls, a deity appeared holding a bronze talisman and bestowed it upon her. The other girls fled in terror. From this time on, she frequently manifested divine phenomena. She would常ly ride on a single mat to cross the sea. People all called her the Wise and Spiritual Maiden.

One day, while weaving, she suddenly leaned against the loom and sat in meditation. Her complexion changed dramatically. Her mother kicked her awake and questioned her. Upon awakening, she wept and said, "Father is safe, but elder brother has perished." After a while, news arrived. When father and elder brother were crossing the sea, their boat capsized. As if someone were supporting him, the father managed not to drown. The elder brother, due to the broken rudder, finally fell into the sea and died. In the fourth year of Yongxi, she ascended and was transformed at Meizhou Islet. 《Marginal note: Zhang Xueli's record says she threw herself into the sea to save her father and died. This is incorrect. Another account says the goddess was born on the twenty-third day of the third month in the first year of Jianlong, Gengshen.》

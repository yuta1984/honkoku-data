The imperial court has newly published geographical maps. The maps of tributary states such as Korea, Hami, and Lhasa are all included therein. Overseas
feudatory territories are customarily allowed to be appended in their proper sequence. If we were to continue with the previous absurd and false accounts without making corrections, how could we
demonstrate
the far-reaching influence of our sage dynasty's civilizing transformation, and the long-standing orientation of foreign lands toward the interior, so as to be appended to the Ministry of War records and be called most glorious?
Therefore, when taking up the brush to record, one must be especially cautious and scrupulous. In the sixtieth year of Kangxi, corresponding to the year xincho,
in the eighth month of autumn. Hanlin Academy Compiler, your subject Xu Baoguang respectfully writes this preface.

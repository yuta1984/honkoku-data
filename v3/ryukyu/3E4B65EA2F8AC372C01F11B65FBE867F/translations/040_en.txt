She was enfeoffed as "Princess of Spiritual Benevolence, Favorable Assistance, Auspicious Response, and Heroic Fierceness." In the second year of Baoyou, she rescued from drought. She was enfeoffed as "Princess of Favorable Assistance, Auspicious Response, Heroic Fierceness, and Collaborative Righteousness." In the third year, she was again enfeoffed as "Princess of Spiritual Benevolence, Favorable Assistance, Auspicious Response, and Compassionate Salvation." In the fourth year, she was enfeoffed as "Princess of Spiritual Benevolence, Collaborative Righteousness, Auspicious Response, and Compassionate Salvation." In this year, the Zhejiang embankment was completed. She was enfeoffed as "Princess of Spiritual Benevolence, Collaborative Righteousness, Auspicious Response, and Virtuous Celebration." In the fifth year, Instructor Wang Li petitioned the court, and the princess's father was enfeoffed as "Marquis of Accumulated Celebration" and her mother as "Lady of Manifest Celebration." Her elder sister and divine assistants all received imperial appointments. In the third year of Jingding, she reversed the wind to immobilize the sea pirates' boats and capture them. She was enfeoffed as "Princess of Spiritual Benevolence, Manifest Salvation, Auspicious Response, and Virtuous Celebration." 《Marginal note: Song enfeoffed her as Lady four times and made additional enfeoffments as Princess ten times, totaling fourteen enfeoffments.》 In the eighteenth year of Zhiyuan under Emperor Shizu of Yuan, due to divine protection received in maritime transport, she was enfeoffed as "Heavenly Princess of National Protection and Clear Manifestation." 《Marginal note: This was the beginning of enfeoffment as Heavenly Princess》 "Manifest Protection" was also advanced. In the third year of Dade under Emperor Chengzong, due to the spiritual efficacy in canal transport, she was enfeoffed as "Heavenly Princess of Assisting Saints, Protecting People, and Clear Manifestation." Emperor Renzong additionally enfeoffed her as "Heavenly Princess of National Protection, People Protection, Extensive Salvation, and Clear Manifestation." In the second year of Tianli under Emperor Wenzong, "Spiritual Response, Favorable Assistance, Blessed Benevolence, and Dignified Fierceness" was added to her title. 《Marginal note: A total of twenty characters》 The temple plaque read "Spiritual Compassion." 《Marginal note: Yuan promoted her to Heavenly Princess. A total of five additional enfeoffments.》 All were due to the dangers of maritime transport and the repeated manifestation of divine responses. Emperor Taizu of Ming enfeoffed her as "Holy Princess of Manifest Filial Piety, Pure Righteousness, Faithful Salvation, and Responsive感应." In the seventh year of Yongle under Emperor Chengzu, she was enfeoffed as "Heavenly Princess of National Protection, People Protection, Wonderful Spirituality, Manifest Response, Extensive Benevolence, and Universal Salvation." 《Marginal note: This title continues to be used to this day》 Thereafter, officials were dispatched to offer sacrifices annually as a regular practice. Emperor Zhuanglie enfeoffed her as "Heavenly Immortal Holy Mother, Azure Spirit of Universal Transformation, Jade Cloud Prime Lord." Subsequently, "Azure Virtue of Universal Transformation, Compassionate Response, Jade Cloud Prime Lord" was added. 《Marginal note: Ming made one enfeoffment as Holy Princess, one continued and changed to enfeoffment as Heavenly Princess, and two changed enfeoffments as Prime Lord, totaling four enfeoffments.》

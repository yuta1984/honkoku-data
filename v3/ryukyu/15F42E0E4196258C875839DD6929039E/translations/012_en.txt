When Shō Nei first had an audience with Taitoku-in-dono, there was a command: "Since the Ryukyu Kingdom has been the hereditary seat of the Chūzan Kings for generations, no person of another surname should be installed. Return to your home country and continue the ancestral rites." This was commanded, and Iehisa also received that command and was granted the tribute from Ryukyu. Iehisa returned to Satsuma taking Shō Nei with him, sent back all the Chūzan King and other captives to Ryukyu, and established laws by dispatching supervisory officials (kankoku) from Satsuma.

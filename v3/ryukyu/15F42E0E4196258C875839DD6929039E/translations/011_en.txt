An imperial envoy came and bestowed one thousand bales of white rice. On the twenty-eighth day, Shō Nei went up to the castle, had an audience with Taitoku-in-dono, presented one hundred rolls of donsu silk, one hundred rolls of banana fiber cloth, and two hundred rolls of taihei cloth, performed his prostrations and withdrew. He presented one hundred rolls of damask, one thousand pieces of silver, and a Nagamitsu sword,

and presented one sword, one horse, and one hundred kin of thread to Daiyū-in-dono. After completing his prostrations, on the eleventh day of the ninth month, a banquet was given to Iehisa and Shō Nei. Later, an imperial envoy was sent down to the Sakurada residence, a horse was bestowed upon Iehisa, and permission was given for their return home.

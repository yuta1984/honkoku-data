【The Matter of the Great Master's Ancestors】
Now then, the founding patriarch of the Shingon school in our land, the posthumously granted Grand Monk Supervisor, imperially named Kōbō Daishi, was born in a place called Byōbugaura in Tado District of Sanuki Province. His father was of the Saeki no Atai clan, and his mother was of the Ato clan—both were distinguished family lines.

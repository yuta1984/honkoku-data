【The Matter of Hachiman's Solemn Vow】

(Continuing from previous page) —how could one fail to look up to him in reverence and faith? After that, as the Daishi was passing alone through the central gate of Tōdaiji Temple, he noticed a solitary figure lingering near the gate. This person's appearance was clearly no ordinary human being. A radiant light shone about them, and their bearing was of an awe-inspiring, sacred quality. The Daishi found this most mysterious, and asked, "What manner of august personage might you be?"—

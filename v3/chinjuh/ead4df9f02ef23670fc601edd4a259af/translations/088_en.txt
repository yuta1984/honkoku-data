## The Biography of Kōbō Daishi, Volume Four

### How Grasses and Trees on the Mountain of Gasui in Chinzei All Flourished at the Same Time

Throughout Daidō 1 (806), [the Daishi] spent his days at Kanzeon-ji Temple in Tsukushi, and when the new spring arrived, he set out in haste for the capital. Now, back in Enryaku 23 (804), when the resolve to travel to Tang China had arisen, [the Daishi], while in Chinzei, had offered solemn prayers to the great deity of Usa and to all the various gods, beseeching them for safe passage across the sea. At that time, the deity of Gasui in Buzen Province delivered an oracle, saying: "It is truly admirable that the holy man, holding the Dharma dear and thinking little of his own life, is setting out along the distant sea route in order to seek the Law, and is crossing over to China. In that case——"

The person, with a most serene and pleasant demeanor, said: "I am Hachiman. You, the Venerable Monk, and I are as shadow and form—inseparable. Wherever I follow, you must never part from me; and wherever you dwell, I shall surely follow in your wake." With this solemn vow firmly made, it is said that they pledged together to protect the Dharma and to lead all sentient beings to salvation. Now, Hachiman Daibosatsu is the manifested trace-form (*ōjaku*) of Vairocana, the King of Enlightenment and Supreme Patriarch of the Shingon highest teaching.

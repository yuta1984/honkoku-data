【The Water Deity Seeks Blessings】

From there, he set out to the shores of Lake Ōmi (Lake Biwa), where the vast, brimming lake suddenly rose up with great white-crested waves, roaring and surging. As he watched for a while, there upon the surface of the water stood a single, remarkably beautiful court lady. When he asked, "What manner of being are you?" she replied, "I am the water deity of this lake. I have appeared before you, Reverend Master, to seek your blessings, so that I might attain peace and comfort in both this life and the next." Thereupon, he presented her with his sandals and thus saved her; the water deity, reverently receiving the footwear upon her head, descended into the depths of the lake.

"Sōgyō Shūji Kusen" (Selection of Cursive Script Characters and Phrases) - Calligraphy by Master Morikawa Chikusō, 2 volumes
　This book collects excellent fragments and couplets from ancient masters' masterpieces, gathering characters written by various renowned calligraphers since the Jin and Han dynasties, with translations provided for each. Starting from two characters up to fourteen characters, it is unparalleled in its convenience for use in plaques, paired scrolls, horizontal scrolls, folding screens, sliding doors, and one or two-line calligraphy. It is also excellent for selecting phrases for seals. Whether for moon eaves, flower mats, wind windows, or tea ceremony settings, this is something that literati and calligraphers should always keep at hand. It is truly a precious treasure for elegant pursuits and artistic meditation.

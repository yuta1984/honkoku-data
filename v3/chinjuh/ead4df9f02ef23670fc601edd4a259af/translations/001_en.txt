【Running title: Biography of Daishi】
【Originally published in Kanbun 2 (1662), reprinted in Tenmei 3 (1783)】
【Publishers: Kawachiya at Shinsaibashi, Osaka, and others】
【Buddhism, kana-zōshi (vernacular fiction)】

【This is a transcription made long ago, unrelated to "Minna de Honkoku" (Transcription by Everyone). The base text at that time was "The Biography of Kōbō Daishi" held by Nakamozu Library, Osaka Metropolitan University. The material we are looking at now has almost the same content, but is a different copy with slight variations in worn areas and such. Since it was created with the attitude that it would be fine as long as it could be enjoyed as a story, there are likely many mistakes including typos, misreadings, and missing ruby annotations. While some revisions have been made for this reposting, there is a high possibility that not all errors have been corrected. If you happen to find small mistakes while passing by, we welcome anyone who would casually fix even one or two characters. It's perfectly fine to make corrections and leave quickly like a ding-dong dash, so please help us out.】

【On Prayers for Going to Tang China at Usa Hachiman Shrine】
Revering the teaching master (Shakyamuni Buddha), now holding the Dharma waters of the Tōji school, the sacred image remains at the benevolent shrine in the western suburbs (Ninna-ji). When thinking of its original connection, tears of emotion well up spontaneously. Also, for protection on the sea voyage, he wrote one hundred scrolls of the Prajñāpāramitā Heart Sutra and dedicated them to Usa Hachiman Shrine.

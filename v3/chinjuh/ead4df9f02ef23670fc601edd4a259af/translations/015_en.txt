With heartfelt sincerity, he made his vow to all the Buddhas, and without hesitation threw his body into the deep valley where towering rocks jutted up precipitously. Heavenly beings descended from the sky and caught him in mid-air. To further demonstrate that his aspiration was unwavering, he leaped three times, but each time he was unharmed, as the heavenly beings lifted him up safely.

He remonstrated in this manner repeatedly and on many occasions, yet the master [Huiguo] paid him no heed whatsoever, and instead proceeded to transmit the secret teachings ever more fully to [Kūkai]. Then one night, Chinga had a dream in which the Four Heavenly Kings came flying down from the sky, and declared: "One who obstructs the transmission of the Dharma of the Buddhas and Bodhisattvas is a being of great sin!" They glared at him with furious eyes, and their voices resounded as if heaven and earth would crumble apart. Chinga broke out in a sweat across his entire body, and blood seeped from his nine orifices, whereupon he awoke from the dream.

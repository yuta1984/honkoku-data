Minamoto no Sanetomo, the third shogun of Kamakura and Right General, was of high court rank and served the imperial court with loyalty. He humbly taught righteousness to the four seas and made them submit, and the country was prosperous and reliable.

However, the Wada clan's ninety-three horsemen were falsely accused of rebellion due to slanderers, and punitive forces were dispatched from the shogunal palace.

Date no Kagenobu received orders to lead the attack force.

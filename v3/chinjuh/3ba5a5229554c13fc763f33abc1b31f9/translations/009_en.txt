Rokugatsu Village [Adachi Ward]
There is a temple called Hanshō-zan [Hanshō-zan] Enten-ji. Though it is not a particularly notable temple, Enten-ji in Rokugatsu Village is unusual and well-known among people. According to legend,

when Hachiman-tarō Yoshiie departed for Ōshū, for some reason, local warriors of this province gathered in great numbers and blocked the road of his southward journey. It was during the scorching heat of the sixth month, and his allies were exhausted with no spirit to fight. Yoshiie prayed to

Tsurugaoka Hachiman-gū in Kamakura, offering sacred gifts, and the sunlight became even more burning. He attacked with the sun always at his back, so the enemy faced the sun, their faces were illuminated and their eyes dazzled, thousands were struck down, they suffered a great defeat, and Yoshiie gained a great victory. Therefore, he established a Hachiman shrine at that place, named the village Rokugatsu Village, and named the temple administrator's temple Enten-ji. Whether this actually happened or not, in any case, this is a designation not yet heard of in other provinces. It should be called remarkable.

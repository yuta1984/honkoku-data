【Temple No. 54 - Enmei-ji】
Fudō Myōō
No. 54 - Enmei-ji - 1 ri
When I gaze upon the unclouded mirror's rim
It reflects all shadows without omission
Imabari domain prosperity

【Temple No. 55 - Bekku-zan Kongō-in Nankō-bō】
Daitsū Chishō Buddha
No. 55 - Bekku - 1 ri 2 chō
At this place, while gazing, the dream awakened
Even at Bekku, the same water and stone
To the left is Imabari castle town. Hiyoshi. Umagoe

【Temple No. 56 - Taisan-ji】
Jizō Bodhisattva
No. 56 - Taisan-ji - 18 chō
All people come worship and soon at Taisan-ji
Entrusting guidance for the next life
Sōja River

【Temple No. 57 - Eifuku-ji】
Amida Buddha
No. 57 - Hachiman - 20 chō

In the next life it is Amida Buddha who saves people
Mountain path, small slope. Hachiman

Yakushi Buddha
No. 59 - Kokubunji - 6 ri

【Temple No. 48 - Sairin-ji】
Eleven-faced Kannon
No. 48 - Sairin-ji - 25 chō
If you wish to seek and hear of Amida Buddha's world
Come worship at the temple in the western forest
At this place there is a statue of Kōbō Daishi, provisions are good

【Temple No. 49 - Jōdo-ji】
Shakyamuni Buddha
No. 49 - Jōdo-ji - 15 chō
Without abandoning this body of ten evils, just as it is
I come to worship at the Pure Land temple
Between here there is Hachiman Shrine, past that is Hata-dera

【Temple No. 50 - Hanta-ji】
Yakushi Buddha
No. 50 - Hanta-ji - 20 chō
Though all matters may be numerous and complex, do not neglect them
Pray with hope that illness and suffering may flow away
Geba town, Ishite village

【Temple No. 51 - Ishite-ji】
Yakushi Buddha
No. 51 - Ishite-ji - 2 ri
Do not regard the Western Paradise as distant - at Anyō's
Temple, come worship and receive the ten pleasures
Dōgo hot springs - to go to Matsuyama castle town, go left, provisions are good

【Temple No. 52 - Taisan-ji】
Eleven-faced Kannon
No. 52 - Taisan-ji - 18 chō
Climbing the great mountain, sweat pours forth, but
Thinking of the afterlife, there is no suffering at all
Wake

【Temple No. 53 - Enmyō-ji】
Amida Buddha
No. 53 - Enmyō-ji - 9 ri
At Enmyō-ji of Amida's light of welcome
The accompanying radiant shadow is the nightly moon
Awai tea hall available, Asanami, Kikuma, Ōi

Prayer of Repentance - To be recited three times
To be recited three times
All evil karma from past lives
All arises from beginningless greed, anger, and ignorance
Born from actions of body, speech, and mind
I now repent of them all

《Title: Shikoku Pilgrimage Road Guide》
Awa Province - From Muya ferry landing to Temple No. 1: 3 ri

【Temple No. 1 - Ryōzenji】
Shakyamuni Buddha
No. 1 - Ryōzenji Temple 《Note: From here to Gokurakuji Temple - 10 chō》
Having come before Shakyamuni of sacred Mount Ryōzen
All countless sins have vanished away
Itano District, Hinoki Village

【Temple No. 2 - Gokurakuji】
Amida Buddha
No. 2 - Gokurakuji Temple 《Note: To Konsenji Temple - 25 chō》
If you wish to go to Amida's Pure Land paradise
Make "Namu Amida Butsu" your constant refrain
Kawabata, Ōdera

【Temple No. 3 - Konsenji】
Shakyamuni Buddha
No. 3 - Konsenji Temple 《Note: To Dainichiji Temple - 1 ri》
Think only of the treasure pond of paradise
Where golden springs flow crystal clear
Fukita, Inubushi, Natō

【Temple No. 4】
Dainichi Buddha
No. 4 - Dainichiji Temple - 18 chō
When I gaze upon this moonlit white night
Only in the dark valley are sleeves dyed black
Yatake Village

【Temple No. 5 - Jizōji】
Jizō Bodhisattva
No. 5 - Jizōji Temple - 1 ri
Great Jizō Bodhisattva who guides the six realms
Please lead us on the path of this world and the next
Kanyake, Shichijō, Hikino

【Temple No. 6 - Anrakuji】
Yakushi Buddha
No. 6 - Anrakuji Temple - 10 chō
In this transient world, fighting over shame and honor is futile
Seek the protection of the Land of Peace
Takao Village

【Temple No. 7 - Jūrakuji】
Amida Buddha
No. 7 - Jūrakuji Temple - 1 ri
If you quickly escape the eight sufferings of human existence
Where you will arrive is the nine grades of ten pleasures
Tonari Village

【Temple No. 8 - Kumadaniji】
Thousand-armed Kannon
No. 8 - Kumadaniji Temple - 18 chō
Gathering firewood and drawing water at the valley temple
Those who practice austerities do so for the afterlife

【Temple No. 9 - Hōrinji】
Shakyamuni Buddha
No. 9 - Hōrinji Temple - 25 chō
The secret teachings of the great sage can overturn even transgressions
Know this as a connection to the turning of the Dharma wheel
Akizuki, Kirihata

【Temple No. 60 - Yokomine-ji】
Dainichi Buddha
No. 60 - Yokomine-ji - 3 ri
Building temples vertically and horizontally on peaks and mountainsides
Universally saving all people
To the right, return to Ōto. Kōon-ji

【Temple No. 61 - Kōon-ji】
Dainichi Buddha
No. 61 - Kōon-ji - 8 chō
Those who fear the next life, at Kōon-ji
Try to stop but cannot stop the white waterfall's water

【Temple No. 62 - Tenyō-zan Kannon-in Hōju-ji】
Eleven-faced Kanzeon
No. 62 - Ichinomiya - 7 chō
The jeweled well that appeared after the early summer rains
Is it a white jar, Ichinomiya River
Himi

【Temple No. 63 - Kichijō-ji】
Bishamonten
No. 63 - Kichijō-ji - 1 ri
Cast away the evil worldly desires within your body
And all pray hoping for good fortune
Nishi-izumi. Naraki. Tan

【Temple No. 64 - Maegami-ji】
Amida Buddha
No. 64 - Maegami-ji - 10 ri
In front is kami, behind is Buddha, the paradise's
Horizontal hammer that crushes myriad sins
Sunouchi. Ōmachi. Tsune. Yokoo

【Temple No. 65 - Sankaku-ji】
Eleven-faced Kanzeon
No. 65 - Sankaku-ji - 5 ri
How frightening, if you enter even into the three corners
Round your heart and chant to Amida

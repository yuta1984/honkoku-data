【Temple No. 76 - Konzō-ji】
Yakushi Buddha
No. 76 - Konzō-ji - 1 ri
When one truly opens to the Three Jewels of Buddha, Dharma, and Sangha
The wonders of Shingon ritual prayers are revealed
Katsura-hara

【Temple No. 77 - Dōryū-ji】
Yakushi Buddha
No. 77 - Dōryū-ji - 1.5 ri
Having completely entered the Buddha way with one's wishes fulfilled
How eager I am to see the moon of enlightenment
Marugame castle town. West of Doki River is Kugame, east is Takamatsu

【Temple No. 78 - Gōshō-ji = Dōjō-ji】
Amida Buddha
No. 78 - Dōjō-ji - 1.5 ri
Dancing and leaping while chanting nembutsu at Dōjō-ji
Keeping rhythm while striking the bell
Utazu town. Water of the Eighty-eight. Stone Buddha of Yakushi

【Temple No. 79 - Tennō-ji】
Eleven-faced Kannon
No. 79 - Sutoku Tennō-ji - 1.5 ri
One should seek eternal bliss in this fleeting world
Even emperors wander in exile
Aya River is a famous place. Kamo. Aisaka. Kokubu town

【Temple No. 80 - Kokubu-ji】
Thousand-armed Kannon
No. 80 - Kokubu-ji - 50 chō
Dividing provinces and crossing fields and mountains, to temples
You help those who come to worship
There is a slope called Kokubu-zaka with a valley stream. South Aomi

【Temple No. 81 - Shiramines-ji】
Thousand-armed Kannon
No. 81 - Shiramine-ji - 50 chō
In the frost-cold temple of white dew and pure silk
All voices chant the dharma together

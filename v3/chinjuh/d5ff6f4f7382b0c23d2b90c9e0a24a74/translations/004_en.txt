Record of the Four Shrines Pavilion, Volume One

　　　Azure Dragon

○Shibue Kyakujin Gongen: Located in Shibue Village, Katsushika District. Known as Seihōzan Kanshōji Temple. A branch temple of Sensōji in Asakusa. Two and a half ri from Edo.

○Tateishi Nanzōin: Located in Tateishi Village, Katsushika District. A Shingon temple called Gohōzan. About two chō away is a shrine to Kumano Gongen, whose sacred object is said to be a stone sword from the age of the gods. About three ri from Edo.

○Aoto Hōjiin: Located in Aoto Village, Katsushika District. A Shingon temple called Yakuōzan.

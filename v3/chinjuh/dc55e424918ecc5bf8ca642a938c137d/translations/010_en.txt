Since ancient times, [the crab] had thought to mock the monkey's form, but this was completely a misjudgment. The monkey had eloped and fled, and the crab felt increasingly frustrated. Though he searched here and there, since he inevitably walks sideways rather than straight like humans, he made no progress. Everyone's heart feels the same - thinking "Edo first," he finally arrived at the eel's place.

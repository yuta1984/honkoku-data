Brief Map of Bairyūzan Bokuboji Temple【Sumida Ward】

Two people say that near Iwatsuki there is the Sumida River, and there is also the tomb of Umewaka-maru. The Bokuboji Temple depicted in this map, which is undoubtedly the true site, is said to be a new land that was established based on the place name of Sumida Village during its restoration period, having relocated the historical traces of Umewaka from the vicinity of Iwatsuki. This is what we have heard as the true account.

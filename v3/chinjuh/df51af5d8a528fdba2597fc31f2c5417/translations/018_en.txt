In fact, there are descendants in Harima Province who have inherited the bow and arrows with which Honma Magoshiro shot down Kumano warriors in the battle of Mount Hiei. According to the account of someone who saw these, they were short-tipped round wood bows. Indeed, the catalpa bows carried in the Satsuma domain lord's procession are bows made in ancient times in the short-tipped style. People who do not know the form of ancient bows might think them half-bows, but since the Shimazu family has been the protector of the province continuously since Lord Yoritomo, ancient customs naturally remain, and following old precedents, they have ancient bows carried in their processions. The village headman of this place is called Sekiguchi [someone], and has resided in this place for several hundred years.

He must have casually and naturally preserved these ancient bows and arrows.

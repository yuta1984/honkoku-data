It is said to be a Kannon statue cast from natural gold that Murasaki Shikibu treasured. That night I lodged at the headman's house, and the master's oral tradition was as stated above - that they lived in wealth in the past, though this is not evident now. Large antique items such as sword boxes were visible, and on the spear rack there were bows and arrows that appeared extremely old, perhaps passed down for hundreds of years. When I asked to examine them, they were bows and arrows as shown in the diagram.

Shimo-Shinozaki Village [around present-day Shimo-Shinozaki-chō, Edogawa Ward]
Kinryūzan Muryōji Temple, Shingi Shingon sect, principal deity Amida Buddha, founder Kaiden Hōin, shogunal seal for three koku four to. Among the temple treasures is one old sword, nothing particularly remarkable. No other historical records are known.

Shimo-Kamada Village
Tenkawasan Meifukuji Temple, Jōdo sect, and this temple has a wooden statue of Saint Shinran {{marginalia: seated figure about three shaku tall}}. When the saint was traveling separately in Hitachi Province, this entire area suffered a great drought, and the people's suffering was extreme. They entreated the saint and invited him to this temple to pray for rain, whereupon rain immediately fell for several days and all the villages

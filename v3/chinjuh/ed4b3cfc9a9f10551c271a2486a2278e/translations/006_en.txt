In general, the virtuous deeds that Kūkai manifested throughout his lifetime are numerous examples found throughout the provinces. In Kawachi Province, there was a pond where water constantly filled and flowed. Even under the scorching summer sun, even when it did not rain, the water never dried up. However, for some unknown reason, the dragon king who lived in the pond left this pond and moved to another location. Because of this, the water in the pond completely dried up. The farmers who cultivated fields in the nearby area had relied on this pond's water. When summer came and they needed to irrigate their fields, there was no water at all.

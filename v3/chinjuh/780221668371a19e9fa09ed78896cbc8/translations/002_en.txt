The True Nature of Kōbō Daishi, Volume 3 (Lower)

Now, the monk Shubin lost face once again and was greatly enraged. Determined to subjugate Kūkai and pray for his death, he set up an altar at Sai-ji temple and performed the Gōzanze ritual.

When Kūkai heard of this, he immediately set up an altar at Tō-ji temple and performed the Gundari ritual.

Both sides raised black smoke as they engaged in a prayer battle against each other. Shubin was also a person of renowned spiritual accomplishments. Kūkai was even more virtuous...

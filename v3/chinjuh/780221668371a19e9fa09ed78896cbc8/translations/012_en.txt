Abbot Kangen stepped aside and called his disciple Shunyū. "How is it? Can you behold this sacred state?" he asked. The inner palace monk replied, "It feels as though I am enveloped in mist, and I cannot see." The abbot felt compassion and took the inner palace monk's hand, guiding it to touch the Great Master's knee, saying, "This is his sacred knee." Shunyū reverently touched it three times. The lingering fragrance remained on the inner palace monk's hand and would not fade. When he returned to Ishiyama, the sacred texts that he copied by hand and took up to read were imbued with that fragrance.

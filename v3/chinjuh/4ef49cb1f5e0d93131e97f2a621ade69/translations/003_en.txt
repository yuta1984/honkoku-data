The nine schools all possess both phenomenal and noumenal aspects, establishing gradual and sudden teachings to return to the source of one mind. Among these, particularly the esoteric Shingon vehicle represents the profound teachings expounded long ago by Dainichi Nyorai. Within the Iron Stupa of South India, Bodhisattva Nagarjuna received these from Vajrasattva. The secret teachings of both the Womb and Diamond Realms were first transmitted to the human world and spread to China through the Tripitaka Master Prajna. That these have now been transmitted to Japan, with the secret initiations and mudras of Shingon continuing to this day, can be traced back to its origin in the monk Kukai.

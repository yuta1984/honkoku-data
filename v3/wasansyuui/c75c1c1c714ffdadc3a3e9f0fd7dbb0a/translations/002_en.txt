　　　Hōen Hiken-shū, Volume 1 — Preface

From ancient times, various remarkable methods have been established by those knowledgeable in arithmetic and calculation. However, looking at the books that have been widely used in the world in recent times — the *Jinkōki*, the *Shikaku Mondō*, the *Enpō Shikan* (Four Volumes on Circular Methods), and the *Ketsugi-shū* — these works appear to be the texts that people have taken up and used for their study and practice. Yet none of these four collections contains anything deficient or crude; they are beyond the reach of the ordinary person. Although each contains various methods and approaches, the four collections each differ from one another in small ways, and if one ponders and reconsiders their methods and applies oneself diligently to practice, one can make their knowledge one's own.

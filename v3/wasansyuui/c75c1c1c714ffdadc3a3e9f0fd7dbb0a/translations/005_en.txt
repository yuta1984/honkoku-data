When distributing the above four types of purchased goods
　proportionally according to each merchant's contributed silver:

64 kanme, 800 monme of silver (Kyoto merchant's share):
　　　　　　　　Ginseng: 101 kin, 10 ryō
　　　　　　　　Aloes wood: 28 kin, 14 ryō
　　　　　　　　Silk thread: 3,402 kin
　　　　　　　　Rolled goods: 113 rolls, 1 jō, 5 shaku, 2 sun
　The allocation for the remaining two merchants need not be written out.
　The above method is made clear in the Jinkōki.

Dividing the four goods according to 64 kanme, 800 monme of silver:

　Among these, what is the unit price in silver
　for each of ginseng, aloes wood,
　silk thread, and rolled goods?

Ginseng: 101 kin, 10 ryō
　Silver for this: 16 kanme, 200 monme
　Unit price: 160 monme of silver per kin

Aloes wood: 28 kin, 14 ryō
　Silver for this: 16 kanme, 199 monme, 9 fun, 8 rin (and a remainder)
　Per kin: 571 monme, 4 fun, 2 rin, 8 mō, 4 shi

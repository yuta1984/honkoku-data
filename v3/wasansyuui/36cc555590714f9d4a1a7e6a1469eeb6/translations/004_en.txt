Gen surname　《Note: Person from the Kamakura period》


【Left Page】
・Mōri Shigenō《Note: Called himself Kan'bei》
　　　《Note: He served Lord Toyotomi Hideyoshi and was sent to the Ming court to study mathematics. When he returned to Japan, it was around the time of the fall of Osaka Castle, and he became a rōnin in Edo. He brought back the Senmyō calendar and wrote "Kijo Ranshō."》
・Yoshida Mitsuyoshi《Note: Called himself Shichihei》
　　　《Note: He studied under Mōri Shigenō and wrote works such as "Jinkō-ki," "Koryaku Benran," and "Wakan Gōun."》
・Imamura Tomokō《Note: Called himself Nihei》
　　　《Note: He studied under Mōri Shigenō and wrote "Ryūgai-roku."》
・Takahara Yoshitane《Note: Called himself Shōzaemon, later changed to Ichigen. He was a disciple of Mōri Shigenō.》

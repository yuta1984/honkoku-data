【Left Page】
　　　Planting Festival Procedures　《Marginal note: During the late April days of Mizunoe and Mizunoto》
First, at the Hour of the Snake, the morning and evening daily offerings are prepared and presented in the outer sanctuary《Marginal note: The shrine priest on duty performs this》
Next, when the appointed time arrives《Marginal note: Called at the Hour of the Sheep》each shrine priest《Marginal note: in court dress and cap》takes their seat at the earthen building《Marginal note: As usual》
Next, the inner sanctuary divine offerings are dispatched
Next, each shrine priest takes their seat before the deity《Marginal note: As usual》
Next, the ritual child takes their seat before the deity
Next, the ritualist opens the sacred doors《Marginal note: According to usual ceremony》
Next, the divine offerings are presented and advanced to the inner sanctuary《Marginal note: As usual, however the single portion offerings are handled by the ritualist's side》

【Right Page】
In this ceremony, from the southern steps, by the steward's direction, the shrine attendants set up in advance.
Particularly, the head priest takes the western seat, and the ritualist side takes the eastern seat.

Next, the mounted attendant of the horse racing comes and announces the return at the seat of the shrine officials.

Next, the chief priest and shrine officials below each rise from their seats and move from the bridge hall.

Next, they make pilgrimage rounds to Iwamoto, Sawada, Nara shrines, etc.

Next, the duty shrine official presents the evening divine meal offerings.

Next, each shrine official takes their seat in the office building, completes the divine offering banquet, and disperses.
However, before rising from their seats, the head steward, stewards, and subordinate shrine officials ask at the end of the seating area
about when the tree-planting ritual should be performed. On what day

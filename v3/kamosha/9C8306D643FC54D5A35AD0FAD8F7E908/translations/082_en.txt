Next, each auxiliary shrine priest proceeds to their respective shrine
Next, the five shrine ritualists recite the norito prayer
Next, the inner sanctuary divine offerings are removed and cleared away, with the shrine priests following《Marginal note: However, the Wakamiya ritualist should raise up ten round trays》
Next, the ritualist closes the sacred doors
Next, each shrine priest rises from their seat before the deity, performs ritual worship, and arrives at Sawada Shrine
Next, each of the five officials takes their seat before Sawada Shrine deity《Marginal note: Western precedence, facing north》
Next, each of the five officials presents sacred offerings　《Marginal note: As usual, however at the time of prostration after completing the norito prayer》
　《Marginal note: The shrine attendants present wooden rice grains《Marginal note: Now small stones are used as substitutes》to each of the five officials, however now only the five officials wrap those small stones in paper and present them》

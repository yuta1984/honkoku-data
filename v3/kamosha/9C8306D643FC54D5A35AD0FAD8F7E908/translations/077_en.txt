　　Procedures for Subsidiary Shrines
First, the head priest, ritualist, offering bearers, shrine parishioners and others proceed to a certain shrine and take their seats in the worship hall《Marginal note: Head priest sits on the left, ritualist on the right. However, among the offering bearers, those of higher rank sit on the left, those of lower rank on the right》
Next, the ritualist rises from his seat《Marginal note: Formal bowing while seated and shoe etiquette are omitted because he holds the sacred key. However, now he does not enter the worship hall but proceeds directly to the earthen platform》
At the earthen platform, he performs one bow and ascends the steps《Marginal note: Leading with the right foot, proceeding in the most continuous manner}} At the great platform,
one bow《Marginal note: Proceeding on knees, then one bow, then placing the ceremonial scepter, sacred keys, etc. on the right side, and also placing the sacred chain wrapper and sacred chain cover on the right side with additional}}
　　《Marginal note: The key is also placed on the same right side. However, none of these should be placed directly on the great platform}}

Sacred sakaki branch《over five feet long, with branches and leaves》The head priest and below, the five officials each tie sacred shide paper to the branches of the sacred sakaki. There are deep secrets involved.
Next, the sacred sakaki is made to stand facing the front of the sacred birthing place. The shrine attendants circle three times, and there are deep secrets regarding the sacred procession《The shrine officials and shrine halls are seated in rows. Proceeding northward, facing west. During the sacred procession, each prostrates》. The court musicians sound the rhythm of wooden clappers and offer secret songs. Next, the sacred sakaki is placed at the shrine grounds《extremely secret, its location is oral tradition》. Next, each shrine official proceeds to the shrine grounds, the superintendent emerges to greet them below the north gate, and each enters through the west gate. Next, the head priest and below shrine officials arrive at the seats before the sacred presence《the seats for the negi side and ritualist side as usual》. Next, the ritualist ascends and opens the sacred doors《as usual》. Next, the head priest and below shrine officials proceed《as usual》. Next, hollyhock and katsura are transferred forward《the procedure for transfer is like that for sacred offerings》. Next, the shrine officials withdraw in order and arrive at the prayer recitation building. The shrine officials of the subsidiary shrines each proceed to their respective shrines to offer hollyhock and katsura. Next, after completing the recitation of prayers, they rise and exit outside the middle gate. Next, each shrine official takes a seat under the north eaves of the sakura gate《arranged westward, facing north》. Next, the administrators and superintendent bring hollyhock and katsura and have each insert them in their hair《however, over the eight sets of hollyhock hung at the sacred birthing place, they are hung in additional layers》《The two officials of Ota tutelary deity do not take seats at the tower gate》. Next, the construction office supervisor completes the closing of the sacred doors of the middle gate, and each withdraws.

The procedure for the five officials withdrawing from before the sacred presence: After the prayers are completed, the chief ritualist proceeds to the earthen residence. During this time, the head priest, negi, and both deputy officials pass through the small courtyard and arrive at the ritualist side corridor. Next,《the chief ritualist》completes closing the sacred doors and withdraws from the Wakamiya worship hall direction as usual.
The two officials of Kifune complete prayers twice and withdraw.

Proceeding to that shrine: The negi takes his seat in the worship hall, the ritualist proceeds directly and performs one bow at the earthen residence, ascends the great platform for one bow, and opens the sacred doors.
The negi rises, and the ritualist and he stand side by side at the earthen residence for one bow, ascend the great platform for one bow, enter the inner sanctuary for one bow, and roll up the sacred curtains.

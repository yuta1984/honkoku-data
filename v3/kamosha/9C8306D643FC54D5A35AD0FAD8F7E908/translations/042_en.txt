Next, the head priest (kamunushi) and ritualist (hafuri) enter the inner sanctuary《as usual》
Next, the senior shrine attendants (sho-negi), assistant shrine attendants (gon-negi), and others wait on the left and right sides of the great platform or stairs《as usual》
From this point, five purification leaders (shojin-gashira) advance into the inner side of the Chinese-style gate and take their seats《on the long platform facing north with east as the upper position》
《However, the two senior members sit on the east side of the Chinese gate, and the three junior members sit on the west side. When the sacred doors are opened, they move from their seats and prostrate themselves》
Next, rice porridge bowls are offered《white camellia ○ This ritual follows the same procedure as the divine offering transfer ceremony, first for the negi group, then for the hafuri group. Each group places one bundle on small round seats and places them on round trays》
《These round trays are immediately removed from their legs and placed on the balustrade by the shrine priests below the stairs, as usual》

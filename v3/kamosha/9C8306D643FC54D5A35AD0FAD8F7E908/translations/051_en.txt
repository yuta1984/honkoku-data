Third Month
　Ritual Procedures for the Third Day　　《Court dress worn》
First, at the hour of the Rabbit, each shrine official arrives and gathers at the Tsuchiya building《In this ceremony, they first assemble at the attendants' quarters》
Next, the divine offerings are dispatched《As usual》
Next, each shrine official takes their seat before the deity《As usual》
Next, the ritualist opens the sacred doors《As usual》
Next, peach blossoms and magnolia flowers are offered《However, they are presented on round trays. For magnolia, large white flowers are used, and for those without flowers, only branches》
《According to the Chūhishō commentary, these are offered by the negi priest. The empty tray is taken by the Shingū negi, placed between the railings, and then he returns to sit at the base of the worship pillar. The empty tray offered by the ritualist side is handled similarly by the Kifune ritualist in his official capacity》

【Left Page】
Next, the inner sanctuary divine offerings are presented《As usual》
Next, each shrine official of the subsidiary shrines proceeds to their respective shrines
Next, the daily offerings for the outer sanctuary are prepared《The gon-negi and ritualists prepare these as usual》
Next, the five officials recite the ritual prayers《As usual》
Next, the divine offerings from both inner and outer sanctuaries are removed《Same as on New Year's Day》
Next, the ritualist closes the sacred doors《Same as on New Year's Day》
Next, the chief priest and others each withdraw from before the deity《As usual》
Next, the shrine officials make pilgrimages from Wakamiya to Nara Shrine
Next, they arrive at the office building, finish the divine offering banquet, and withdraw

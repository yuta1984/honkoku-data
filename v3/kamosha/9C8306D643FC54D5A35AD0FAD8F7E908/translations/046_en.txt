First, on the day before this, small pine and jeweled brooms are prepared as lantern lights and grass, and the treasurer brings them to the residences of the chief priest and shrine officials below. To immediately circulate the arrangements for that day, these should be wrapped beforehand. The diagram is as shown on the right. However, the negi's folding method only differs in left and right orientation, so it is not separately diagrammed. This should be understood by analogy. Now it is best to use something like hemp wrapping, with no distinction between left and right. However, for small shrines, wrap with rough paper, and the chief priest prepares it.

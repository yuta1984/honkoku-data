　《Marginal note: Next, the sacred chest of morning sacred meals is advanced. This is offered on the sacred tray stand of the head priest's side. If it cannot be placed on the sacred tray stand, kai leaves are spread and it is respectfully placed below the sacred tray stand. The outer sanctuary divine offerings are respectfully prepared》
Next, the ritualist performs one bow at the great platform, descends to the earthen platform, performs one bow and takes his seat in the worship hall
Next, the offering bearers and parishioners respectfully prepare the divine offerings in the courtyard　《Marginal note: In this procedure, when the ritualist descends to the earthen platform, the senior remains at the earthen platform. Next, taking the sacred table that is at the ritualist's earthen platform location, they descend from the earthen platform and place it in front of the sacred front ditch. They offer upon it, placing it properly in the sacred chest》　Performing one bow and taking seats in the worship hall
Next, the head priest and ritualist perform two bows, recite ritual prayers, clap hands, and perform two bows《Marginal note: The offering bearers and parishioners respond accordingly》

【Left Page】
Next, the offering bearers and parishioners rise from their seats and remove the courtyard divine offerings《Marginal note: This procedure follows the initial method》
Next, the ritualist rises from his seat, performs one bow at the earthen platform, ascends to the great platform, performs one bow and removes the outer sanctuary divine offerings　《Marginal note: In this procedure, first the morning sacred meals, next the ritualist's raised sacred tables, next the head priest's raised sacred tables, next the ritualist's sacred rice, next the head priest's sacred rice, next the ritualist's sacred eight-legged table, next the head priest's sacred eight-legged table are removed, then the sacred tray stands and others are hung on the high railing, one bow is performed, and they descend the steps》
Next, the head priest and ritualist perform one bow at the earthen platform, ascend to the great platform, perform one bow and enter the inner sanctuary《Marginal note: As in the beginning》
Next, the offering bearers and parishioners perform one bow at the earthen platform and wait at the great platform or earthen platform《Marginal note: As in the beginning》

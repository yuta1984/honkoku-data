【Left Page】
February
○ Last Day Earth Release Festival Procedures (In short months, the 29th day)　(In ancient times, there was sacred divination to determine the festival day)

First, today's morning and evening daily offering festivals are prepared and presented beforehand (however, the duty shrine official should perform this).

When the appointed time (hour of the monkey) arrives, each shrine official (wearing court dress) arrives at the earth house (first the attendants' quarters, then the earth house).

Next, the divine offerings are dispatched.

Next, each shrine official takes their seat before the deity.

Next, the hafuri opens the sacred doors.

Next, the shrine chief and shrine officials below each wait at the great platform of the inner sanctuary or to the left and right of the steps.

Next, the divine offerings are transferred forward from the hafuri group (however, the sacred chopsticks, sacred cups, etc. are next transferred forward from the negi group).

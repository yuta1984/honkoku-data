【Right Page】
This should be left open for the advance and retreat of the assistant officials (azukari) and others. The negi group, upon seeing the shrine officials of Tsuchiya advance to the Sakura Gate, each passes through the assistant officials' quarters and heads toward the western imperial supply house to gather together. Each shrine official advances to the transparent corridor, and before the clan deity negi takes his seat, they advance in order from the senior member and take their seats in the transfer hall. However, they first face the divine presence in front of the round cushion and perform one bow, then advance on their knees to take their seats. This ceremony is the same for the hafuri group as well.

Next, the assistant master (azukari-dayū) hands over the sacred key to the chief hafuri.
　During this time, the musicians take their seats in the western music hall.

Next, the chief hafuri rises from his seat, advances to the great platform, and opens the sacred doors.
　At this time, the shrine officials and all below shift from their seats and prostrate themselves, while the musicians play discordant sounds.

【Left Page】
Next, the hafuri descends and stands at the bottom of the steps.

Next, the chief priest rises from his seat and advances toward the bottom of the steps. The chief priest and hafuri stand together and perform one bow, ascend the steps, perform one bow at the great platform, and enter the inner sanctuary.

Next, the shrine officials rise from their seats in order, advance toward the bottom of the steps, perform one bow, and wait at the great platform or to the left and right of the steps. However, regarding the rotating service of the negi group, at this time, upon seeing the clan deity hafuri advance, the senior member rises from his seat, and when passing through the center of the temporary shrine, performs one bow while crossing, performs one bow facing the main shrine on the eastern sliding board of the temporary shrine, and temporarily takes his seat. The second senior member performs one bow at the center of the temporary shrine, performs one bow near the eastern worship pillar and takes his place there.

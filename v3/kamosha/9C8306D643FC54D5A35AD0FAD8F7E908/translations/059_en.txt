【Right Page】
Next, the deputy takes a torch and stands it in the small courtyard
Next, the blessing official ascends and opens the sacred door (as usual)
Next, the head priest and other shrine officials each come to attend (as usual)
Next, the sacred broom and sacred cloth are transferred, as in the divine offering ceremony. Especially from the shrine maiden and blessing officials
　However, the base is wrapped around the sacred broom, and cloth is wrapped around the sacred cloth, placing them on small round seats
Next, two legs of the sacred offering stand are removed (in this ceremony, first the blessing officials, then the shrine maiden officials. However, at this time, sakaki from the previous winter are placed and scattered. Especially to the shrine maiden officials)
Next, paper candles are offered from the shrine maiden officials, and the shrine maiden takes them and waits at the large platform to
　illuminate the inner sanctuary

【Left Page】
Next, the sakaki and other offerings presented during the winter purification are removed and transferred to the outer sanctuary
(This ceremony is mentioned above) The inner sanctuary is swept clean and purified. In this ceremony, the sacred beach platform, sacred
　curtains and the entire inner sanctuary are swept and wiped, and the sacred chopsticks, sacred cloths, etc. are transferred
　to the outer sanctuary (however, tatami mats in the inner sanctuary are handled with brooms)
Next, the shrine maidens and others sweep and clean the large platform outside the sacred door and the railings on the left and right of the stairs
　In this ceremony, the chief deputy shrine maiden sweeps and wipes thoroughly around the long beam, sacred doors, and komainu of the outer sanctuary, then the Kataoka shrine maiden and deputy blessing official clean the top of the stairs, the Kataoka Kibune shrine official
cleans the bottom of the stairs, then the Kibune blessing official, Shingu shrine maiden, blessing official, Ota shrine maiden, etc.
complete the cleaning in order and place them on the high railing. However, where these small round seats are stepped on is not permissible

【Right Page】
They perform purification with water at the side of the bridge, then when entering the second torii gate, they perform one bow at the lower hem.
Next, the top three officials take their seats from the western side of the earthen house, while the assistant priests (gon-hafuri) and below go around the eaves of the earthen house to the north and take their seats. However, they go around from the east and perform the shoe-removal bow and seating bow as usual.
Next, the administrative officer (shoshi) comes and reports that various officials' miscellaneous matters are in order, then withdraws.
Currently, the administrative officer makes this report at the attendants' quarters (jisho).
Next, the divine offerings (jingu) proceed (each tonne carries the sacred food containers). The shrine attendants (jinnin) follow before the divine offerings, and when passing through the earthen house, each shrine official prostrates themselves.
Next, the Master of Yin-Yang advances and stands at the rock area of Kataoka (the riverside south of Tama Bridge), performs one bow and purifies the divine offerings (the food containers of both the negi and hafuri groups are done consecutively in one go, with no need for separate bowing and purification).

【Left Page】
Next, the divine offerings of the negi group pass through Tama Bridge, enter the tower gate, middle gate, etc., and arrive at the western offering house. The divine offerings of the hafuri group pass through Kataoka Bridge, enter the eastern gate, and arrive at the eastern offering house.
Next, the outer sanctuary divine offerings proceed (carried by shrine attendants), and the food preparation officials offer the provisions, entering the offering house through the western gate.
Next, the shrine officials, starting from the highest ranking, each rise from their seats at the earthen house (shoe bow and seating bow as usual) and advance to the divine presence.
The Master of Yin-Yang purifies each shrine official's body at the same location (the shrine officials respond accordingly).
Next, the negi and hafuri divide left and right and advance, but their route is the same as for the divine offerings, with the negi group taking seats at the western corridor in front of the divine presence.

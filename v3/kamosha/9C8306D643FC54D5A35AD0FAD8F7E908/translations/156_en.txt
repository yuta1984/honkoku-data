　　　　　Kansei 5 (Year of the Water Ox) April 23rd - Festival Day
　　　　　Record of when Head Priest Yasuhisa-yo Yoshitoshi served

One, Sacred Hollyhock Divine Ceremony: The Konoe messenger arrives and rests in the music hall. After the sacred treasure procession, when the土屋 (tsuchiya) takes their seat, the shrine officials rise. The priest group crosses the Keydama Bridge, while the ritual officiants - the assistant head priest, Wakamiya shrine priests and other priests - take their positions as usual. Next, at the connecting hall, left and right divine offerings and outer sanctuary divine offerings are presented, and after the ritual prayers conclude, the assistant head priest and ritual officiants remove the outer sanctuary offerings and withdraw. Next, the chief priest rises, and the head ritual officiant rises. The chief priest advances first, performs one bow at the earthen platform, proceeds to the great platform and temporarily closes the sacred doors. However, the ritual officiant proceeds to the great platform, the chief priest performs a lateral bow at the main shrine, and the ritual officiants crouch on the sliding boards. Next, the head priest rises, and the priest group prostrates on the lower sliding boards. After temporarily closing the sacred doors, the head ritual officiant performs one bow at the earthen platform and withdraws. Next, the head priest performs a lateral bow at the main shrine toward the ritual officiants and withdraws, with each withdrawing in turn. The assistant head priest remains, performs one bow at the earthen platform and advances.

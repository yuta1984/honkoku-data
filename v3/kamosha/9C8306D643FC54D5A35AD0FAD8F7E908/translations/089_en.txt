【Left Page】
Next, shrine attendants bring mounted bows and mugwort arrows, each takes them and shoots to purify heaven, earth, and the four directions
　This is not done in recent times. However, in this ceremony, during the purification by the Yin-Yang Master, mounted bows and
　mugwort arrows are passed to the shrine attendants, head priest, and ritual priest. They immediately nock the arrows
　and wait for the purification to end. When the shrine attendants announce that the ritual is complete,
　the arrows are shot toward the purification site. This practice has now ceased.
Next, arrow swords, assistant priest reeds, and reed grass human effigies are brought and offered, with shrine priests and clan members taking
　them in order《Note: In this ceremony, the effigies are placed in the sacred container and brought before the seats, each person takes them and with those human figures》
　《Note: rubs and cleanses their body》

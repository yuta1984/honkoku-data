　○Procedures for the Otana-e (Shelf Festival) on the 14th Day
　　　One kago-shi (basket official) from a certain shrine brings meoto (paired offerings) in early morning. Immediately
　　　the shrine placard is replaced, and the sacred rope (shimenawa) is also renewed. Most importantly, they come to collect during the fish-reading time.

Next, after the direct communion (naorai) of divine offerings is finished, both officials withdraw to the shrine maiden's quarters and change their ritual garments
　In this ceremony, the divine offerings of the inner and outer sanctuaries are carried by the sword-bearer (katana-no-ne) while still in the sacred chest, and after both officials receive them, they are stored in the spice chest as before
Next, those who make daily visits offer divine food to the small shrines, and both officials perform the ceremony of presenting sacred offerings (hōhei) from the temporary shrine (gonden) to the inner shrine (okusha)
　The ceremony is performed as usual with both officials taking turns. When the ceremony is completed, each returns to Kamo
　In this ceremony, first the divine offerings to the small shrines are prepared by those who make daily visits. First the temporary shrine (offered on the offering table of the great platform as usual), next Shika shrine, next Kawao shrine (however, the divine offerings for Suzuka shrine are carried along at Kawao shrine), next Ushiichi shrine, next Yuikami, next Ichigi, next Hayashida shrine, next Hiyoshi shrine, next Suikuzura shrine, next the inner shrine (same as the temporary shrine, offered on the offering table), next Suzuichi shrine, etc.
　At the small shrines, offerings are made directly while still in the sacred chest, and those offered at the great platform are placed in spice chests, with one container carried by two bearers. After removal, they are stored in the spice chest as before. After the ceremony ends, both inner and outer divine offerings are transported to the chief priest's residence. 　etc. are divided in half between the two officials for direct communion, and the fifteen small shrine portions are divided into three parts among the two officials and those who make daily visits for direct communion. Thus each portion serves five shrines for direct communion. However, the two officials partake of the portions from the edge shrines: the temporary shrine, inner shrine, and main shrine. In ancient times, after each returned home, they would exchange portions at the chief priest's residence
　Next, both shrine officials visit the small shrines to present offerings, with one sword-bearer carrying the offerings (eight staffs) and accompanying them. First the temporary shrine, next Kawao shrine, next Kuroo general shrine (at both shrines, two offerings are grasped and bundled together for presentation), next Ushiichi shrine, next Ichigi shrine, next the inner shrine (at the inner shrine, at the beginning of shrine visitation, service is performed within the transparent fence; the second time is at the private shrine), next the private shrine (the ceremony at this shrine differs according to family tradition and has oral teachings; however, when a deputy official participates, Yuikami should be added; needless to say, the offering ceremony is the same as at the main shrine, presenting personally and then removing)
　After the ceremony is completed, they withdraw, and on the return journey, torches etc. are received from the guard station as usual, and this concludes

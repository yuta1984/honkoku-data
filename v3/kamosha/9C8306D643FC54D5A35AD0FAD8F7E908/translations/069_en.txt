【Right Page】
They sit facing each other alternately (chidori formation). Also, the second and third persons receive and pass the divine offerings at the center of the temporary shrine.

Next, the hafuri rolls up the sacred curtain.

Next, the sacred aoi (hollyhock) and katsura (Japanese Judas tree) offerings are transferred forward <<Marginal note: First the negi group, then the hafuri group. However, the round trays are immediately removed to the left and right>>

Next, the divine offerings of the negi group are transferred forward.
　At this time, the shrine attendants advance to the courtyard of the offering house, holding sakaki branches and calling out in loud voices to escort the divine offerings, and as the divine offerings pass before them, responding to their voices, the musicians play music.

Next, the divine offerings of the hafuri group are transferred forward <<Marginal note: This ceremony is the same as for the negi group>>
　However, regarding the rotating service of the negi group, immediately after the divine offerings of the negi group finish transferring forward, they return directly to their original seats. At this time, the senior member does not return to his seat but remains. When the hafuri group's divine offerings are being transferred forward, the round trays with legs being removed for the negi group are

【Left Page】
received while seated and stacked on the sacred table located beside the steps of the temporary shrine. Upon seeing the completion of the hafuri group's divine offering preparation, they rise and return to their original seats.

Next, the sacred sake cups are transferred forward from the negi group. In this ceremony, near the worship pillars of the temporary shrine, sake is poured into the sacred cups by both the negi and hafuri groups together, and passed directly from the officials to the clan deity negi. From there, they are transferred forward in order, just like the divine offerings.

Next, the shrine officials, starting from the lowest-ranking, advance to the bottom of the steps, perform one bow, and return to their original seats.

Next, the assistant master hands over the sacred key to a certain shrine hafuri.
　At this time, those of the hafuri group performing rotating service perform a seated bow and one bow, rise and complete the shoe bow, wait for both officials near Haji-no-o Shrine, and proceed together to a certain shrine.

Next, the chief priest, negi, and hafuri descend, complete one bow at the bottom of the steps, and arrive at the prayer house.

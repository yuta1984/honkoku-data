【Right Page】
　Procedures for Preparing and Presenting Divine Offerings to the Main Shrine
　　First from the Negi Priest's Side
Peach and magnolia《Two of each, totaling four branches, placed on round trays with small round mats》Sacred chopsticks
Sacred bowl with rice《Placed on small folding tables, then on round trays》Prostrate rabbit《With motohi placed on top, arranged in rabbit-shaped paper stands, placed on round trays, but set on wooden boards》
Prostrate rabbit《With motohi placed on top, arranged in drum-shaped paper stands, placed on wooden boards and round trays》Chinquapin leaves《Nothing placed on top, arranged in paper stands, placed on wooden boards and round trays》
Sacred carp《One fish placed in earthenware with kai matting, placed on round trays》Sacred bird　One bird, same as sacred carp
【Left Page】
Fresh offerings《Five sea bream, same as sacred carp. However, presented one at a time in five separate presentations》Accumulated removal fee《Fifty rays, placed in one sacred chest》
Vegetarian offerings《Ten varieties in ten packages, placed in one sacred chest》Horse offerings《One sea bream placed in a sacred chest》

　　　Next from the Ritualist's Side《Same as negi priest's divine offerings, but without sacred chopsticks》
Sacred sake《Served in two sacred cups, placed on two sacred tables. This is presented jointly by both the negi priest's side and ritualist's side》

　For the Temporary Hall, peach and magnolia are prepared and presented in the same manner as the main shrine《The bettō (administrator) prepares these》

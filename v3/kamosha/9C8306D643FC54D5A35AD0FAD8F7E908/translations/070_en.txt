【Left Page】
　<<Marginal note: arrive at the prayer house <<Marginal note: This ceremony is as usual>>. During this time, the Kataoka Shrine prayers and such are completed>>
　<<Marginal note: At this time, the yatone (shrine official) announces in a loud voice "earth festival," and upon hearing this voice>>
　<<Marginal note: the assistant master comes forward and at the dropping edge of the transparent corridor informs the chief priest that the Kataoka Shrine prayers have concluded>>

Next, the chief priest and the four other officials each perform two bows, recite prayers, clap twice, and bow twice <<Marginal note: The rotating service members follow suit>>

Next, the outer sanctuary divine offerings are removed. The music and such are as before.
　In this ceremony, the assistant negi and hafuri rise and advance to the bottom of the steps, bow once as before, ascend and remove the divine offerings and such. However, the rotating service members, upon seeing the assistant negi and hafuri rise and advance, likewise rise, cross at the center of the temporary shrine with one bow, advance as before with one bow, first remove the two vessels such as elevated offering stands, then place them on the elevated railing.

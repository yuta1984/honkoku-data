【Left Page】
　　Order of Proceedings for Branch Shrines
First, both officials and others proceed to a certain shrine and take their seats in the worship hall《Marginal note: This ceremony is like｜the rabbit staff ritual》
Next, the hafuri opens the sacred seat《Marginal note: This ceremony is as usual, but the tonegi takes the torch》
Next, both officials advance to the inner sanctuary《Marginal note: As usual, but in recent times it is abbreviated, and in shrines where the inner sanctuary is narrow｜the hafuri opens the sacred door, descends the steps, performs one bow and takes position》
　《Marginal note: Takes seat in the worship hall, next one negi advances to the inner sanctuary, arranges the sacred offerings and presents them｜upon completion retreats from the inner sanctuary, takes seat in the worship hall and reports to the hafuri, upon completion the hafuri rises and advances inside》
　《Marginal note: In the sanctuary removes the sacred offerings and closes the sacred door, and also for outer sanctuary offerings the negi arranges and presents while the hafuri｜removes them following this pattern, however in recent times shrines that do not follow this also use this ceremony》
Next, the inner sanctuary sacred offerings are presented《Marginal note: As usual》
Next, both officials retreat from the inner sanctuary, take seats in the worship hall and recite the ritual prayers《Marginal note: As usual, however》
Next, both officials advance to the inner sanctuary and remove the sacred offerings

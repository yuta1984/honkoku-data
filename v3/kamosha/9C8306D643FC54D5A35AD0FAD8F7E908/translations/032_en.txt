　　Order of Subsidiary Shrine Rituals
First, the two officials and others proceed to a certain shrine and take their seats in the worship hall《Marginal note: This ritual follows｜the u-tsue procedure》
Next, the hafuri opens the sacred seat《Marginal note: This ritual is as usual, but the tonne takes the torch》
Next, the two officials advance to the inner sanctuary《Marginal note: As usual, but in modern times it is abbreviated, and at shrines where the inner sanctuary is narrow｜the hafuri opens the sacred doors, descends to the base of the steps, performs one bow, and takes his seat》
　《Marginal note: Takes seat in the worship hall. Next, one negi advances to the inner sanctuary, arranges and presents the divine offerings,｜then withdraws from the inner sanctuary, takes his seat in the worship hall and recites prayers. When the hafuri finishes, he rises and advances inside》
　《Marginal note: In the inner sanctuary, scatters the divine offerings and closes the sacred doors. Also, the negi arranges and presents the outer sanctuary divine offerings, and the hafuri｜removes them. This is the practice. However, in modern times, shrines that do not follow this also use this ritual》
Next, the inner sanctuary divine offerings are presented《Marginal note: As usual》
Next, the two officials withdraw from the inner sanctuary, take their seats in the worship hall and recite prayers《Marginal note: As usual, however》
Next, the two officials advance to the inner sanctuary and remove the divine offerings

　　　　　　Purification Ritual on the Day of the Horse in the Fourth Month

On the previous day, the sacred birthing area (mi-ikisho) is surrounded with sakaki branches, and the yato negi-in leads the shrine attendants (jin-jin) to encircle it. The sacred lanterns of the main shrine's temporary hall and subsidiary shrines are renewed and properly arranged. The head priest and other shrine officials oversee this work, and the necessary paper supplies are delivered to the deputy administrator's servants. On the day itself, by the hour of the Snake (around 10 AM), two and a half sheets of sugihara paper and two sheets of regular paper must be brought to the shrine grounds.

After the hour of the Cock (after 6 PM) 《the threefold sacred visits are performed by the shrine attendants》, the shrine officials don court dress and gather at the attendants' quarters. The superintendent of the sacred vestments has the sacred garment chest carried and placed in the center. Next, the deputy administrator asks "May we raise the sacred broom?" and receives the reply "Yes." Then each shrine official rises and goes to the earthen building 《however, the two officials of the tutelary deity remain behind》. Next, the sacred garment chest is dispatched, and the two officials of the tutelary deity and the superintendent of sacred vestments follow it to place it in the Wakamiya worship hall. The two officials of the tutelary deity return to the earthen building. Next, the sacred broom advances, and then the shrine officials rise.

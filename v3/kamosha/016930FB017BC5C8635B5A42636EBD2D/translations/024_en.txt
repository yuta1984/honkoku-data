15th day, hinoto-u, clear with occasional snow. Year-end cleaning of the residence as in previous years. After nightfall, snow fell and accumulated about seven to eight sun.

18th day, kanoe-uma, cloudy, rain from evening. Tonight, regarding the presentation of sacred court music by the Bun clan, there was advance notice, and since Tsunesen and the dancers attended their duties, went to the shrine in the evening, and everything else was as usual.

19th day, kanoto-hitsuji, occasional light rain. Had direct attendance for shrine duty completed. ○Today the imperial deliberations at the palace concluded.

21st day, mizunoto-tori, clear. From the Uji-sōtei, regarding the preparation and presentation of sacred court music as in previous years, there was advance notice, and since the dancers attended their duties, went to the shrine in the evening, and the subsequent proceedings were as usual.

24th day, hinoe-ne, after morning meal, rice cake pounding at the residence as in previous years.

28th day, kanoe-tatsu, clear. Setsubun (seasonal division). At the hour of the Boar, everyone performed the age-taking celebration as in previous years.

Last day, mizunoe-uma, clear. For the monthly gathering of senior and junior members, attended at Kensei's request as representative of the sixth group.

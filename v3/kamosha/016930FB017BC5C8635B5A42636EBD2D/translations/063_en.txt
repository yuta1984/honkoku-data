12th day, Tsuchinoe-Uma, clear - First Horse Day of the month.

13th day, Tsuchinoto-Hitsuji, clear - The ninth kagura lecture meeting was held as usual at Okudaya in Sanbongi. Departed from the second half of the Snake hour, concluded in the evening, and returned home at the second half of the Rooster hour.

15th day, Kanoto-Tori, clear - Requested substitute service from Teisei for my father's duty shift.

16th day, Mizunoe-Inu - The Kanto Taishu (Shogun) Sakon-no-kami Ienari was today appointed as Daijō-daijin (Grand Minister of State). The presiding minister was Kazanin Dainagon Ieatsu. The Taishu's appointment as regent and chancellor is an unprecedented example.

18th day, Kinoe-Ne, rain - Due to the Kanto regent-chancellor appointment, Takatsukasa Udaishō Sukehiro and other court nobles of various ranks, both upper and lower court officials, departed today to visit Kanto.

19th day, Kinoto-Ushi, clear - The two imperial messengers, Hirohashi Tanesada and Kanroji Kuninaga, departed for Kanto. This is the usual annual departure of the instructing nobles to Kanto today.

20th day, Hinoe-Tora, clear - For the monthly accounting of the ninth kagura lecture meeting, assembled at the council chamber in the afternoon with a sake offering ○ Requested substitute service from Teisei for shrine duty.

22nd day, Tsuchinoe-Tatsu, clear - Sakaya Shōbei, a resident of Kodera village in the noble household's domain, visited for the first time regarding official silver transaction matters. Hosted a sake gathering at Sakai Jin's place and he departed in the evening.

24th day, Kanoe-Uma, clear - Due to the death of Hitotsubashi Yoshinao on the 20th of this month, Taishu Ienari donned the prescribed mourning attire (being his actual father), and Nishinomaru Naifu Ieyoshi wore half-mourning attire. This was communicated today from the attached samurai to the noble household ○ Similarly, a notice was circulated within the shrine community that musical performances are suspended until the 8th of next month.

26th day, Mizunoe-Saru, clear - This year's rice distribution for the Aoi Festival was distributed today and received as usual ○ For the noble household's one ryō lecture meeting, went to Sanbongi and Yoshidaya in the afternoon, returning home by the Rooster hour.

27th day, Mizunoto-Tori, clear - Regarding the Taishu's regent-chancellor appointment, it was announced from Kanto that the nobles who traveled down to Kanto should remain in Edo during the Taishu's leave period. This naturally applies to the imperial messengers as well.

28th day, Kinoe-Inu, clear - There was a rotation change for this month's shrine duty groups ○ From around the second half of the Rat hour until past the Ox hour, there were strong winds, thunder and lightning, and hail.

11th day
One: Regarding the shrine reconstruction being ordered, today the shrine official Kataoka Negi Yasuurara, who was on duty for daily offerings, attended service, and conducted fifty sessions of hemp purification rituals at today's monthly prayer ceremony. Particularly, the liaison officers at the shrine grounds informed those carrying jutte (police batons) of this matter.

　　　Kakui Oki-sama　　　　Okajima Goichiro
　　　Kakui Sanemon　　　Hiraoka Jugoro

18th day - Cloudy weather
One: Before the half-hour of the Tiger (around 3:30 AM), a written notice arrived from the Imperial Liaison Office staff stating that Magistrate Hinnonishi-ben-dono had resigned and Hamuro Sashō-ben-dono had been appointed to the position.
One: After breakfast, representatives Ikiuhisa and Suehide appeared. The matter was that early this morning, regarding the divine enshrinement ceremony following the announcement of the magistrate's replacement by the Imperial Liaison Office, they presented one hundred pieces of bronze coins as congratulatory gifts to Hamuro Sashō-ben-dono according to custom, and then appeared at both East and West Administrative Offices to report this matter.

21st day - Clear weather
One: Since Kitamura Teigoro reported illness and requested leave, Kuroyanagi Kuzo was appointed as his replacement, and went to pay respects regarding this appointment.

29th day - Clear weather
One: A written notice arrived from Magistrate Hamuro-ben-dono's staff to the head priest summoning the representatives.

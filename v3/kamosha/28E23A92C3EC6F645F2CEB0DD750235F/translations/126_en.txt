　　　Anei 6th year, 5th month (small month)
1st day (hinoe-tora)
6th day, cloudy (kanoto-hitsuji)

1. Regarding the upcoming 9th day when Kifune Shrine's roof beam raising ceremony will be held at the hour of the snake, the 11th day when the imperial messenger will visit, and the 15th day when the main shrine's temporary relocation will take place - attendance is optional. However, for the 15th day temporary relocation, ten people from the police force should participate in the vigil starting from the 9th day, and this notification was requested to be circulated to the junior officials from the meeting office.

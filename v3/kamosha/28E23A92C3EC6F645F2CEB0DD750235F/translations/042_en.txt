At the sacred record hall, ceremonies of divine offerings, communion feast, and ritual toasting (each person receiving three cups) are conducted by the head priest and below, shrine officials, and common people. However, regarding the divine offerings, they are passed through while remaining in the sacred boxes, and withdrawal occurs via the middle gate, tower gate, two torii, Nara bridge, etc.

The deputy head priest, construction site magistrate, and master craftsmen and below are seated in the administrative hall, where stipends are granted to the master carpenters and below (by the deputy and construction officials). Divine offerings, communion feast, ritual toasting, etc. are also conducted.

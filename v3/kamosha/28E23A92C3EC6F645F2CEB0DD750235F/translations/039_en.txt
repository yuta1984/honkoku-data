[The matter] was announced to the Right Middle Captain, and furthermore, all those who applied for court ranks this time received imperial appointments. Regarding the imperial appointment documents, they will be delivered at the Imperial Messenger's office at the hour of the Snake on the coming 5th day. Additionally, it was announced that the Kifune shrine wood construction commencement will be at the hour of the Horse on the coming 9th day. Moreover, regarding the imperial edict, it was announced that it will be delivered at the same time as the oral appointment document is handed over. Therefore, each of those who applied for court ranks appeared and expressed gratitude for the imperial appointments, offering bronze as customary.

Then they proceeded to the Imperial Messenger and collectively expressed gratitude for the notification of what was announced by the magistrate regarding the imperial appointments, offering bronze. What was announced was that both the oral appointment document and the imperial edict for the Kifune wood construction commencement will be delivered at the half-hour of the Dragon on the coming 5th day. Regarding receiving the imperial edict, when asked whether both officials of the Kifune position would attend, they replied that according to precedent, one of the two would appear, whereupon they were told that one person should appear.

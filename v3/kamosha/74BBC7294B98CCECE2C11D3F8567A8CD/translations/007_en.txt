The above horizontal folding

Having written the two vertical documents as described above in order and stacked them upon completion, next he again takes writing paper《most importantly, taking the middle paper as at first》. He folds it in half and writes the established regulatory articles. Having completed writing all three documents, he conducts verification again, then passes them to the chief deputy. After review, he has the head priest examine them, and so forth. After examination, he adds his written seal to both vertical documents and returns them to the deputy. Therefore, formal paper is added to each of the two vertical documents, and both are wrapped together with an outer covering. The folded paper is folded into quarters and placed alongside the two vertical documents on a three-legged tray《this tray is brought by the divine master》. The inkstone and other items are covered and put away.

Record of Successive Mokudai (Deputy) Service Duties

1. Regarding the annual offerings under the supervision of successive mokudai to their superiors: When the mokudai requested that these be provided as the mokudai's share from those performing service duties, it was agreed to provide them.
　　《Annual offerings include: office meals (first Tiger and Rabbit days of the 11th month), imperial banquets on the 4th and 11th of the 1st month, captain's farewell feast on the 16th of the 1st month, banquet meals on the 9th, 10th, and 11th of the 1st month, and ceremonies on the 10th of the 9th month and above - totaling nine occasions》

1. For the Kira-metsu-oni (demon expulsion) ceremony in early 12th month《date not fixed》, three people attend from the mokudai, with one of them being from the successive mokudai.

1. On New Year's Day morning and evening, when proceeding to the sake hall for congratulatory ceremonies during the imperial meal period, naturally the successive mokudai, head mokudai, shoshi《each wearing pure white robes and court dress》, minor mokudai《wearing standing court caps and pure white robes》, and mokudai associates gather in the western room of the sake hall《facing east with south as the head position》. The order is: first successive mokudai, then mokudai, then shoshi-taifu, then minor mokudai, along with mokudai associates《however, minor mokudai and associates sit in the next seats to the east, facing west》. After each takes their designated seat, there is a presentation ceremony.

1. Sacred ritual sequence: It begins at the hour of the Snake (9-11 AM). When the shrine priests go to the earthen building, they serve the sacred offerings. There is purification at the bridge hall, and when they serve before the sacred presence, the shrine priests also participate. The sequence of opening the sacred doors is as usual.
Sequence of presenting sacred offerings: First, the substitute for sacred chopsticks, next sacred rice, next rice cakes, next pine rice cakes, next grass rice cakes, next small carp, next birds. After that, various things are presented in sequence. When sacred vegetarian foods and sacred fruits are presented, sacred offerings go to the celebrant's side, and then sacred sake goes to the head priest's side. Peach blossoms are presented. When presented in this way, the shrine priests withdraw and go to the various shrines. The shrine administrator exits from the sacred interior. According to the congratulatory building.

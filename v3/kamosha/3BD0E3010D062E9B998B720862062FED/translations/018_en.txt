First, the first day of the monkey in this month is the tutelary deity festival's sacred ritual.
Ten young clan members participate as dancers. Their appearance involves wearing ritual purity garments. After the second
sacred offering ends, the sacred ritual is performed. First, the dancers and shrine priests
gather at the head priest's house. A small horse is led in front as the sacred horse. The ten
dancers go first (circling the courtyard) and the tutelary deity attendants participate. First, offerings are presented. Taking public
offerings and private offerings, they bow twice then bow twice again and give them to each spirit attendant.
After that there is a ceremonial drinking ritual. Next, the sacred horse is circled three times. Also
the dancers point to the chamber and stand. Next, they remove shoulder garments and stand. There are sounds of
flutes, reed pipes, and rhythm instruments. When things end, they withdraw. The horse handlers circle
around. After that, they go to the small shrine. The procedure is:

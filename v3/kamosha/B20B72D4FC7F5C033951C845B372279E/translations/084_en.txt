1. On the third day, after completing shrine visitation and returning home, according to the temple bell sequence, don court cap and purification robes (without sword), carry the ceremonial garment purification items, take along the water service maids to the administrative hall, and receive the ceremonial garments. Thoroughly examine all purification items, and if there are any damages, report to the magistrates. After completion, have the water service maids carry them back home
　　First, ascend from the cutting board on the south side of the administrative hall. Sit on the tatami mats with the negi priests' seats on the left and the hafuri priests' seats on the right. Ceremonial garment boxes are arranged in order from first to tenth, facing west (however, to the right of the first is seating in the main building. The boxes are located here). When twenty people are assembled, each opens the lid of their box, unrolls the four-fold purification streamers brought, purifies the inside of the box, and immediately places the purification items inside the box.

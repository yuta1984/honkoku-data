1. Within the first and second days: one cord for hanging the ceremonial cap (6 jō of Yoshino Sugihara paper), two sleeve ties for the ceremonial costume (8 jō of Yoshino Sugihara paper)
　　For the above: Twist thick paper cord, apply thin paste and squeeze to prepare in advance
　Additionally prepare one paper cord for binding scrolls (3 jō of Sugihara paper), twisted somewhat thick. This does not require paste application
　Two empty bags for sea bream (6 jō of half-size paper), approximately 70-80 strips of half-length half-size paper for binding iris roots
1. One book of Yoshino Sugihara paper (high quality), four folds of half-size paper (or five folds) should be procured in advance
　The above are needed for rolled offerings, torn offerings, paper cords, iris wrapping, etc. (However, if using the same paper for paper cords and wrapping paper, one book of Sugihara paper will be slightly insufficient)

[Left page]
1. On the evening of the first day, wrap bedding and pillow in a furoshiki and have the water service maid carry them to the vigil site (However, only have them carried when the first rider opens the vigil building)
1. From the second to fifth day, even after completing one's own purification at the bathhouse each morning, first wear only the undergarment of the purification robe and wait while observing others. There is an agreement that everyone should put on their outer garments after Kanazu completes his purification, and afterward put on the ceremonial cap and outer purification robe before departing. This is because Kanazu performs purification after nineteen people have finished.
1. Prepare one purification bamboo stick (about 4 sun long, needed when the yin-yang master visits on the third day)
1. Prepare four or five stealth bamboo sticks (However, if stealth bamboo is unavailable, split bamboo with joints may be prepared). Needed when the yin-yang master visits on the third day
1. On the morning of the second day, when going down for shrine worship, take sakaki branches at Mt. Ota and return (Used for sacred ropes at gates, purification rooms, tower chests, etc. However, if there are sakaki trees within the estate, this is not necessary)
1. Commission sacred rope making: Total of five strands - one for gate, one for purification room, one for water bucket
　Two for tower chests, one of which is for covering and binding (However, for covering and binding, there is no need to attach four streamers or sakaki leaves)

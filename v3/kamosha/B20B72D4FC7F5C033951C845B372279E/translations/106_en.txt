　　When daily shrine visits cannot cross due to flooding, perform distant worship at the riverbank (riverside)
　　　In Kanpō 3 {{year of the water boar}} due to flooding making crossing impossible, it was done thus {{this was the year of Yoshiuji's first appointment}}

1. Places for placing and distributing sacred staffs during the fourth day's pilgrimage to Shimogamo
　First, Chikono-gozen {{distribute staffs at the northeast corner going from the crossroads to Umegatsujii}}　　　　　　Next, Ninori Forest {{from the roadside going east after exiting Takegahana gate, distribute staffs to the left}}
　Next, Nogami (this place is at individual discretion) {{refers to the large tree on the left facing Imai along Takegahana road, distribute staffs from the road}}　　　　 　Next, Ryūboku Shrine {{staff distribution}} {{exit directly from within the forest to the narrow sacred ritual path to the south}}
　Next, Ishihiroi {{place staffs}} {{spread mats brought by attendants, divide left and right to place staffs on top. There are records mentioning Kifune for offerings at this location, but since Kifune receives offerings on the sixth day, this is questionable {{whether to Hiyoshi or Tanaka Shrine}} and should be inquired about}}
　Next, Mitsuka Shrine {{three small shrines facing Kawai, distribute staffs from west to east in order}}　　 　  　    Next, Kawai Shrine {{place staffs}} {{divide left and right to place staffs, and distribute staffs to all small shrines without exception}}
　Next, Guardian of Kannon Hall {{Hiyoshi Shrine and Sawada Shrine, two shrines located together. Distribute staffs from the left side of the road in the middle of the horse field}}　Next, Aioi Shrine {{distribute staffs to the small shrine on the left in the western area of the tower gate within the second torii}}
　Next, Izumoii Shrine {{located on the water to the east within Sakura gate, distribute staffs from around Tama bridge}}　　　 　Next, Nigon Shrine {{distribute staffs to two {{small shrines}} on the right within the middle gate}}
　Next, Eastern Main Shrine {{place staffs {{divided left and right}} from outside the side room}}　　　　　　　　  　Next, {{Eastern}} Ichigon Shrine {{distribute staffs to one {{small shrine}} facing east in front of the main shrine}}

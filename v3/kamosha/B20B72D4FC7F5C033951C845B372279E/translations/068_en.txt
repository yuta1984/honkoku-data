Regarding Recent Donations of Horse Racing Equipment

One: According to the Hideaki diary, in Bunroku 4th year, ceremonial hakama were donated by Tanaka Jūrō Zaemon, with Okamoto Kunai Shōyū Yasumochi serving as intermediary. The head priest at that time was Matsushita Motohisa.

One: According to the Kōken diary, on the 2nd day of the 5th month of Keichō 16th year (Year of the Metal Boar), there was an imperial donation from the Imperial Palace of ceremonial attire including left and right caps, cap cords, swords, and cicada-tail hakama - two complete sets in total.
They were worn for the first time in the first race《Margin note: Left - Yamashiro no Kami Yoshinaga, Right - Bingo no Kami Yasufusa》.

One: In Genna 9th year, two sacred spears were procured by Shimotsuke no Kami Kamo-uji Sen for necessary expenses. The order documents are held by the shrine officials.

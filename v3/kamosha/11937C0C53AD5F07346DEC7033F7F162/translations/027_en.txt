Suketomo: Jōkyū 3 (1221), 8th month, 23rd day. Hisatsugu: Appointed in Chōkan 2.

Yasumorimori: Bun'ei 6 (1269). Nōsuke: Nin'an 2 (1167), 4th month, 20th day, appointed as Assistant Ritualist from Ōta Ritualist. Jōan 1, 9th month, 24th day, appointed as Chief Ritualist.

Hisataka: Date unknown. Ujitsune: According to construction records, served as Chief Ritualist during Shrine Master Shigeyasu's tenure (appointed Jishō 1/9/28, died Kenkyū 2/1/12).

Shigemichi: Same as above. Tōsuke: Jishō 4 (1180), intercalary 2nd month, 6th day, appointed as Assistant Ritualist. Genryaku 1, 7th month, 5th day, transferred to Chief Ritualist.

Yasuhiro: Same as above. Mitsutada: Though not found in records, listed above Iemitsu and below Ujitsune in the provisional documents. Actually the fourth son of Ujitsune, became adopted son of Ujihiro.

Yasunari: Same as above. Iemitsu: According to construction records or old records, served as Chief Ritualist on Kenkyū 7/3/20. Served as Chief Ritualist during Shrine Master Sukeyasu's tenure (appointed Kenkyū 4/9/15, died Kennin 2/6/24) and during Shrine Master Yukihira's tenure (appointed Kennin 2/6/27, died Kenpō 2/9/2).

Yasukiyo: Same as above. According to old records, served as Assistant Ritualist on Kenkyū 7/3/20.

Yasutoshi: Same as above. Kōhei: Kenryaku 2/6/20, appointed as Chief Ritualist (formerly Assistant Ritualist). According to old records, served as Chief Ritualist on Jōkyū 1/6/30. According to genealogy, appointed as Assistant Ritualist on Kenpō 6/9/2.

Yasumasa: Same as above. Suketomo: According to old records, served on Jōkyū 1/6/30. Jōkyū 3/8/23, transferred to Chief Ritualist. According to construction records, served as ritualist for three generations of shrine masters: Shigemasa, Koreyoshi, and Sukehisa.

Iekage: Same as above. According to old records, served as Assistant Ritualist on Jōō 1 (1222)/5/5.

Yasutaka: Bunryaku 1 (1234)/11/7, served as Chief Ritualist.

Shigekazu: Same as above. According to construction records, served as Chief Ritualist during Shrine Master Hisatsugu's tenure (appointed Bunryaku 2/6/9, died Kenchō 6/6/12).

Suehisa: Same during Hisatsugu's tenure.

Shigetoshi: Same as above. Served as Chief Ritualist after Yasutaka's death.

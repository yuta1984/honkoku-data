According to the same records, served as Chief Ritualist during Shrine Master Nōtsugu's tenure (appointed Kenchō 6/6/13, died Kōchō 2/8/13).

Shigemoto: Same as above. According to the same records, served as Chief Ritualist during Nōtsugu's tenure.

Shigemichi: Same during Nōtsugu's tenure.

Nobutaka: Same as above. After Narikazu's death, served as Chief Ritualist. According to old records, served as Chief Ritualist on Shōka 2/3/13.

Shigefuji: Same as above. Yasumori: According to construction records, during Nōtsugu's tenure. According to old records, served as Chief Ritualist on Bun'ei 1/8/23. Same position in Bun'ei 10. After Shigemichi's death, served as Chief Ritualist.

Shigetomi: Same as above. According to construction records, served during Shrine Master Ujihisa's tenure (appointed Kōchō 2/8/14, abdicated to Chief Priest Yasunori on Kenji 2/10/2) and during Shrine Master Yasunori's tenure (appointed Kenji 2/10/2, died Kōan 2).

Shigeeki: Same as above. Hisataka: According to the same records, served during Yasunori's tenure after Yasumori's resignation.

Shigenao: Same as above. Yasumori (reappointment): According to the same records, served during Shrine Master Yasuie's tenure (appointed Kōan 2/7/6, dismissed in Kōan 9).

Shigenori: Bunmei 17 (1485). Yasuhiro: When reappointed by Shrine Master Ujihisa (Kōan 9). During Shrine Master Hisayo's tenure (appointed Kōan 9/12/21, abdicated Shōō 6/2/23) and during Shrine Master Tsunehisa's tenure (appointed Shōō 6/2/23, abdicated Tokuji 3/6/4).

Shigeaki: Entoku 3 (1491)/6/12. Served as Chief Ritualist, recorded until around Einin 4. According to the Record of Various Imperial Edicts, transferred to Assistant Ritualist on Kōan 9/6/15 (formerly Sawada Ritualist, then Junior Fifth Rank Lower).

Shigeken: Meiō 2 (1493)/3/27. Shigekazu: According to construction records, served as Chief Ritualist during Shrine Master Tsunehisa's tenure. According to the same records, Einin 4/3/26, Chief Ritualist Shigekazu, etc.

Shigetsune: Date unknown. Yasunari: According to the same records, served as Chief Ritualist during Tsunehisa's tenure (see above). Einin 4/3/26, Assistant Position Yasunari, etc. According to old records, served as Chief Ritualist in Kangen 2/1st month.

Shigechika: Same as above. According to construction records, Kagen 3/4/28, Chief Ritualist Yasunari, etc., age 30. During Shrine Master Tōhisa's tenure (appointed Tokuji 3/6/4, died Enkyō 2/1/24).

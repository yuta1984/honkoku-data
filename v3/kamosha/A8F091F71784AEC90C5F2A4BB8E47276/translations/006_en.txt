14th day, Day of the Wood Snake, clear weather. Oei goes to the Horikawa residence from the afternoon.

15th day, Day of the Fire Horse, clear weather. Regarding the rice tax from Mori-Kita Kawarada district, which was transferred from Shigeryū last August of the Dragon year, and the rice tax from Oyama-Sō-no-Mizo Hichitsubo district, which was received from Akihei this past New Year, a written notification to the current official Buken is as follows:

　　Memorandum
Mori-Kita Kawarada: One yield of 1 koku 5 to, from Yamamoto Chikuzen-no-suke Shigeryū
Oyama-Sō-no-Mizo Hichitsubo: Each 1 koku 4 to, from Horiuchi Gige Daibu Akihei

Second month of the Snake year, Kondō Hizen-no-kami
To the Arbitrators

○For the repair of the three guardian shrines of the family temple, and for the temporary enshrinement on the coming 21st, Tatsukichi Awaji-no-kami is summoned from Nanto and begins ritual seclusion from today. ○The Nirvana gambling proceeds as in previous years.

18th day, Day of the Earth Rooster, clear weather. As the New Year seasonal dishes are bestowed from the Satsuma residence, Chiyozō goes to work from after breakfast as usual. From this year, he receives the honor of the cuisine service throughout, and stays one night at Kobē's place. However, while this usually occurs on January 8th, it was delayed due to the lord's passage and the senior retainer's visit, etc. ○In the evening, Oei comes from Yoshida. As before, she enters through the side entrance, bringing Otomi and Ushinosuke to stay.

19th day, Day of the Earth Dog, clear weather. Chiyozō completes yesterday's formal thanks at the residence and returns home.

20th day, Day of the Metal Boar, rain. Otomi is sent to Yoshida Suzuka. Later, heavy rain and thunder.

21st day, Day of the Water Rat, rain. Oei returns to Horikawa lodging in the afternoon.

○For the temporary enshrinement of the guardian shrine at the hour of the Rooster, Assistant Officer Naka Iwami-no-kami comes and begins ritual seclusion from the 18th. At the appointed time, rough mats are spread south of the middle gate for the divine seating. In court dress and simple grass attire, slightly to the west and withdrawn, Chief Retainer Tōtōmi-no-kami sits in purification robes. To the north, the chief retainer's attendant sits in hunting dress. The primary officiant Tatsukichi Awaji-no-kami wears purification robes and purple hakama, and the assistant Naka Iwami-no-kami wears purification robes.

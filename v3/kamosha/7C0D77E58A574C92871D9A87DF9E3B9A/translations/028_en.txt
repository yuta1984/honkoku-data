On the 1st day, 300 hiki of gold from Tanida Kyūtarō, 3 pieces of silver coin from Itō Shōjūrō, 3 pieces of silver coin from Sakuma Jinpachi, 200 hiki of gold from the four men Yoshihama, Imai, Matsumura, and Kurahashi, and 100 hiki of gold from master carpenters Goichirō and Tsunoi Saburōbei arrived as lodging fees or courtesy payments. It was decided in council that these should be submitted to the silver magistrate.

1. Regarding an inquiry from Hokaku, an extract from the daily records during the monthly duty period was submitted.
   In council, since it states "the office building as usual" even during the third Kanpō era, it was decided that a reply should be given that precedent should be followed.

1. Regarding the keys to the temporary hall, since they would not be suitable for the Nagoshi purification ceremony, and if they might not be suitable for the formal shrine transfer ceremony, should the old key fittings be replaced and new ones made?
   In council, it was decided that they should be replaced on Tanabata (7th day of 7th month).
   With this concluded, everyone dispersed.

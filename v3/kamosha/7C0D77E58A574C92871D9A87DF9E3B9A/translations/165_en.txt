　14th day
One: After the hour of the Sheep, a letter arrived from the deputy of Controller Hinnonishi, the executive officer, to the head priest. The content was that the shrine liaison Kushige Former Grand Counselor had resigned, and Hirohashi Grand Counselor was just now appointed in his place. It was deemed most appropriate to inform the entire shrine community. Accordingly, representative Horei Suehide immediately appeared and presented one piece of silver as customary.
　At the hour of the Cock, the representatives returned to their quarters and reported that they had appeared before the executive officer to acknowledge receipt, then visited Hirohashi-dono to present one piece of silver, where they met with deputy Tsukiyama Sazen.

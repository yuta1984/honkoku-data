When we submitted the documents, we were asked about when the notification was submitted during the Kanpō era. When we replied that we believed it was around the 28th or 29th day of the 7th month of Kanpō 3, we were told to wait, and the official went inside. After a while he came out and said that we should still submit from our side, and that we should leave the matter aside and return, so we withdrew.

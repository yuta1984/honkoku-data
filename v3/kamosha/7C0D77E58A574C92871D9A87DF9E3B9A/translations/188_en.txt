　17th day
1. To the temple households, shrine attendants (tōne), divine servants (jinnin), and seven districts, announced that tomorrow's Noh performance viewing matter
1. To the responsible officials, sent word that they should come to view the Noh performance
　18th day - Rain, occasionally clearing
1. For the Noh performance, early morning offerings (nichiku) were presented to the main shrine and subsidiary shrines, with shrine priest representatives and shrine administration representatives Sueyoshi participating
1. To chief performer Kawakatsu Chūshichi, five shō of sake and one string of dried fish (twenty pieces) were given
1. For the Noh performance, from the hour of the Dragon (7-9 AM), various shrine officials - Suei Kanemochi, mediator Ujitetsu Kaneko, Mori Akira - each dressed in hemp formal wear (kamishimo), took their designated seats. The head priest, according to precedent, did not take a seat. Around the second half of the hour of the Rooster (6:30 PM), everything concluded without incident.

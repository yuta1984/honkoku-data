5th day (Hinoe-Tora) Light snow
One: Meeting, Kaneko of the meeting place disclosed
　One: On the 29th day of last winter, regarding the oil fee for donated lanterns, Hoketsu conveyed
　　that it was understood. By all means, please make it five shaku of oil per night as requested
　　Council: As this could cause other hindrances, publicly it should be one gō each, but in private
　　arrangements can be made however needed, so there should be consultation with the minor custodian
　12th day (Mizunoto-Tori) Clear sky
One: Regular meeting, contents disclosed by Kaneko of the meeting place
　One: When the oil fee matter was conveyed to Hoketsu, it was accepted, so regarding the document to be given to the head custodian
　　what should be done about the document
　　Council: Decided to give a document to the head custodian, the document to be recorded on the 13th

　One: Speaking from the seat, regarding the temple exhibition at Jinguji Shōjinji, Chūjirō also mentioned recently that
　　since official duties have begun, it would be acceptable to make a request. However, there are
　　rumors that it should be submitted to the public affairs office
　　Council: Since Kōkōin is to be invited to make the request, he was invited. Depending on circumstances, tomorrow there will be an appearance
　　so when asked whether a request should be made, he agreed, and since the petition should be written by the shrine community,
　　if there is an opportunity tomorrow, one person from the temple should also attend to make the request,
　　but due to loss and no public petition, it was postponed and decided to send the petition on the 18th

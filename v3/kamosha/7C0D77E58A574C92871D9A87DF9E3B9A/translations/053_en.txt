　　　　Eighth month (small month)
　First day (Kinoe-Uma/Wood Horse) Clear weather

1. For the eighth month first day formal court visits by both court nobles and military officials, Shigeue as deputy head priest and Shiaken as deputy administrator appeared as general representatives, following precedent.
   Also, in this sequence, regarding the names and times of yesterday's imperial messenger's visit, a written notification was submitted to the Western Office, with the following record:

　　　　　　Oral Statement of Notification
　　　Kami-Kamo Main Shrine formal reconstruction　　August 19th, hour of the rooster
　　　Same　　Kataoka Shrine formal reconstruction　　Same month 25th, hour of the rooster  
　　　Same　　Kifune Shrine formal reconstruction　　September 2nd, hour of the rooster
　　The ceremony for determining the above dates and times was carried out yesterday on the 30th, and on the same day at the hour of the monkey
　　an imperial messenger visited and announced the above dates and times. We hereby submit this notification. The above.

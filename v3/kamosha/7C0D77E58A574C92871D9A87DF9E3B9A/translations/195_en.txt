25th day
The kyōgen performance dedicated the day before yesterday having been completed, after morning meal, Suei, one of the representatives, went to the Eastern Government Office to deliver a report document. Its record:
　　　　Oral report submitted
　　The kyōgen performance we reported earlier was completed without incident yesterday, the 24th day. We hereby report this. The above.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Kami-Kamo Shrine Representative
　　　　Rooster year, 10th month, 25th day　　　　　　　　　　Yamamoto Kazusa-no-suke (seal)
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Umesuji Oki-no-kami (seal)
　　To the Magistrate's Office
　26th day　Clear weather
One: Today, the farmers held a charity sumo tournament dedicated at Baba-omote, so
　twenty sajiki (viewing stands) were set up by the sumo organizers in the shrine families' area west of the upper road of Seishin-ji Temple. Also, since the farmers were holding sumo, the shrine provided one to (about 18 liters) of sake

　and one set of woven goods (twenty dried foods items). The sumo concluded safely after half an hour.
One: Four raised platforms and yoshigakoi were set up south of the shrine families' sajiki above Seishin-ji Temple. This was
　prepared by the farmers in case officials from the government office might come to observe.

　27th day　Clear weather

　　　　Oral report submitted

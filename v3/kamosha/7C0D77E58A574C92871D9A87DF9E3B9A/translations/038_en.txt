Small folded documents were submitted, and all others receiving transfers also submitted their small folded documents.
A draft of the resignation letter was completed and given to the scribe, with the small folded documents given to the monthly duty officers.
The representatives were to appear in person, and after the small folded documents were completed, they were to be sent to deliver them to the intermediate lodging.

- Around the hour of the Snake, Ikyū Kanemochi appeared as representative, bringing the resignation letter of Yamato-no-kami Tsunekiyo.

Respectfully submitted
Shingū-negi Senior Fourth Rank Upper Tsunekiyo has reached old age and is particularly ill at this time,

27th day (Kanoto-Ushi)
One: Contents of the regular meeting disclosure by Mori Akira at the meeting hall
　One: The shoshi-dayū submitted the lower group for the population register
　　By council decision, they were assigned to the population register magistrates
　One: Kiyotari reported that regarding tomorrow's population register revision, the purification head persons should be summoned early for the long day,
　　and whether to instruct the minor custodians to keep the gates and Chinese-style gates closed as they are
　　By council decision, it was determined that this should be communicated to the head persons and minor custodians
　　　The above concluded and all dispersed
　28th day (Kanoe-Tora) Clear sky
One: Today for the population register ceremony, assembled at Shoshidai Yasuhiro's residence around the hour of the tiger
　As usual, there were offering ceremonies and morning meals, then proceeded to the population register building at the hour of the rabbit

　The middle gate and Chinese-style gate were closed, and the attending personnel were:
　　Population register magistrate: Ankyu Kanetame
　　Seated assembly: Fusun, Kiyotari, Fumiaki, Kiyotachi, Kaneken
　　Chief scribe: Kiyokage　Assistant scribe: Kaneko
　Concluded at the hour of the monkey, with evening meal at the shoshi residence
　Regarding the population register boards, when there was an inspection previously, the shrine community requested to the officials that
　the population register boards should be newly made for each major reconstruction as ordered, but currently
　such wood is difficult to obtain and there is no need for concern about poverty, so this time there was no new production
　Therefore, the boards newly made during the Kanpō reconstruction were planed and used

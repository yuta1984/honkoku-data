Pine ceremony officials, two persons, wearing suō robes, minor administrators and others. General superintendents: Morimori and Utoshi.
Those participating at will: Head Shrine Priest Naruhisa, Acting Shrine Priest Yasuhisa, Kibune Shrine Priest Ikuhisa, Sawada Celebrant Jūshun (later), Nara Shrine Priest Kenkei (each wearing purification robes and sashimuki), purification leaders Suetsuki, Yasukata, and Tsukeaki. Besides these, four or five non-official voluntary participants attended. The relocation ceremonies of the aforementioned four shrines began at the hour of the Rooster and were completed without delay after the hour of the Rat.

Tsunekata states: According to the divination document of the Yin-Yang Master, the order was Kajita Shrine, Yamamori Shrine, Shōjin-ji Temple Guardian Shrine, and Jingū-ji Temple Guardian Shrine, but due to the head priest's convenience, they were conducted in the order of first Yamamori Shrine, then Shōjin-ji Temple Guardian Shrine, then Kajita Shrine, then Jingū-ji Temple Guardian Shrine.

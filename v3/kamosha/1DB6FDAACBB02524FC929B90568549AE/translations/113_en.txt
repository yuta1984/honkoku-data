《Marginal note: Matter of the tent shelters for the head priest, left and right lower steps being established for the first time》【In red ink】
　One: For the head priest's seating and the tents for the left and right lower steps, in recent times there were no tent shelters,
　　and for both ceremonies attendants held umbrellas, but from this year onward, notification was made to the magistrate's office
　　and tent shelters were completed.

《Marginal note: Due to rain, both left and right riders receive rewards at the imperial residence》
　　Shoshi-dayū Kumachiyō-dayū Ujiyori's representative Hyōbu-shōyu Gyōhei. Heavy rain
　　fell, both left and right ascended from the end of the horse grounds like on New Year's Day
　　Receiving rewards under the western eaves of the imperial residence was the first in recent years
　　Yamamoto Minbu-jō Ujishige said in his opinion that there were precedents in the past
　　and thus it was done this way

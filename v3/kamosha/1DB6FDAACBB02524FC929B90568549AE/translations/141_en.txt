　　　　Left　Junior Fourth Rank Lower Suechin
　Kajika (lower officials)
　　　　Right　Junior Fifth Rank Upper Masayasu

On the 26th day of the 4th month of Hōreki 5th year, when all senior and junior officials assembled at the lecture hall, it was decided that the order of daikan (estate managers) should henceforth be determined according to court rank. 《The kajika should also be determined by court rank》【Written in red ink】
According to this decision, the kajika will also be arranged by court rank from this year forward.

Shuriryō Yasuaki was designated for the first ride, but having gone down to battle, he was in his home province. As an opinion of the clan members, Kammon-no-suke Naoaki (his elder brother) rode in his place. Due to heavy rain and flooding, the lower steps' seating area was located in the temporary pavilion.

《Marginal note: Due to heavy rain and flooding, the seating area for the lower steps riders was in the temporary pavilion》【Red ink】

The riders' area was also hung on the west eaves of the embankment. Similarly, the sixth race was also held at the west eaves. The right side was held at the right area. The riders' pilgrimage to Kifune being on the 9th day was unprecedented in recent years due to the flooding.

《Marginal note: Due to flooding, the Kifune pilgrimage of the 6th became the 9th》【Red ink】

《Marginal note: Red writing》
One text says: Rain fell on both the 1st and 5th days, and the Kifune pilgrimage of the 6th was postponed until the 9th. This year, Yoshiaki was appointed as Seii Taishōgun.

Bitchū-no-kami's front horse, Minbu-shōyu's inside horse - there was something to report. Even though the whips did not connect, they struck the embankment. Saying that young ones strike with sticks, Bitchū-no-kami brought his horse in again and struck the embankment twice - this was unprecedented in recent years.

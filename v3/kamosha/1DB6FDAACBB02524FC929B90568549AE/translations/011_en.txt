Same 23rd year
The Tsuchida manor horse was changed. He rode right in the first contest. On the first day, Shidori manor's Tajima-no-kami Ujinobu was not made to change horses, and because there were no mistakes, it was thus.

Same 24th year《Marginal note: On October 23rd, the era name was changed to Kōji 1》
Three horses were changed. Neither left nor right riders rode in the first race. However, they did ride in the later contests. On the first day, using three races, the horses were immediately withdrawn. These were Asukai-dono's horses, and it was a matter of observation.

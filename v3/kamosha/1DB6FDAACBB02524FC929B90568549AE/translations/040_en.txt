Jōō 3 (1654)
First race, left: Nishiichi-no-jō rode despite being ranked lower than Fuminami (Fukuhisa). How should this precedent be regarded? Although there was no precedent, since Sagami-no-kami rode on the first day based on deliberations about age and experience, Nishiichi-no-jō rode following that example. However, this may require discretionary consideration.

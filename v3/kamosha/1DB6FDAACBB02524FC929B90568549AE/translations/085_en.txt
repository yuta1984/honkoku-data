　1st Day　Izumo　《Marginal note: Horse provided by Matsushita family》
　　　　Takehara　《Marginal note: Horse provided by Sawada Priest Hiromasu》
　　　　Kifune Priest　《Marginal note: Horse provided by Shinmiya Priest Kiyoo》
　　　　Shinmiya Priest　《Marginal note: Horse provided by Sawada Priest Kanebayashi》

　　The Kifune priest position was vacant. Therefore, that horse was entrusted to 
　　Shinmiya Priest Kiyoo. The long-legged imperial horse belonged to the Matsushita family, 
　　but since last year it was dedicated to the entire shrine community, so it was entrusted 
　　to Wakamiya Priest. Accordingly, the horse for the Shinmiya priest position was 
　　entrusted to Sawada Priest.

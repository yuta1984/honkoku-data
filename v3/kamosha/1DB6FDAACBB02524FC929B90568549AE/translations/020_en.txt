Tenshō 4th year (1576)
The weather was excellent, and with the lord's military formation, the divine ceremony was hurried and began before the eighth hour.

《Marginal note: Red writing》
One text says: After passing the winning post, Sakon-dayū fell from his horse. However, although the whips were coordinated, on the grounds that the whips were not coordinated, they ran again, and there were two contests.

Tenshō 10th year (1582)

《Marginal note: Red writing》
One text says: This year on the 2nd day of the 6th month, Nobunaga was assassinated by Akechi Hyūga-no-kami. On the 13th of the same month, Akechi was killed by wild warriors.

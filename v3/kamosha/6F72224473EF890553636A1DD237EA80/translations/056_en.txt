【Imperial Proclamation (Senmyō) Issued During the Imperial Visit to Kamo Shrine on the 25th Day of the 11th Month of Kōnin 1 (1017 CE)】

By the words of the imperial proclamation (senmyō) of the Emperor, humbly, humbly presented before the awesome and revered Kamo Imperial Great Deity, the following is declared, and so forth:

Furthermore, as it had been prayed and petitioned the previous year that the entirety of Atago District be donated [to the shrine], the matter was taken up. However, within said district there exist places that have been transmitted through ten thousand generations as either the imperial capital or the sacred domains of the great deities. These are not lands that any single individual may freely dispose of. Therefore:

The southern boundary shall be set at the far end of the north main road of the imperial capital;
The eastern boundary shall extend to the district border;
The western boundary shall be set at the far end of the east main road of Ōmiya;
The northern boundary shall extend to the district border.
Within these limits, [the land] is hereby donated [to the shrine], and so forth.

　　11th month of Kōnin 1 (1017 CE)

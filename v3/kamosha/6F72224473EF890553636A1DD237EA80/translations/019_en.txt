**Same 4th Year (1618)**
　Shoshi: Matahachirō　　Deputy: Utano no Kami (Gagaku no Kami)
　Mokudai: Ōsumi no Kami　　Deputy: Utano no Kami (Gagaku no Kami)
　　(Note: In the 2nd month of the same 5th year, Shikibu no Tayū drew up the written record, and Utano no Kami (Gagaku no Kami) also served as deputy for him. That three persons alternately served [as deputies] is a rare and extraordinary occurrence.)

**Same 18th Year (1641)**
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　《Interlinear note: Of Daijōji》
　　Shoshi: Daikemonzujō　　Deputy: Nuinokamino Kami
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　《Interlinear note: The sata-nin also purchased and held [the position]》
　　Mokudai: Senkatsudayū　　Lodging: Uemon no Suke
　　　　　　　　　　　　Three-person purchase holding: Ōino no Kami
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　 Hōki no Kami

During this deputy's tenure, when Ōino no Kami read the keibun (ceremonial address) on this occasion, he omitted the reading of "Ono-gō" (Ono District) and withdrew. Nevertheless, it is recorded that neither the shrine priests (shashi) nor the ujibito (clan members) raised any objection or dispute. On the same 16th day, in the safu (duty roster) for the shōjingashira (head of ritual abstinence), at the time of returning the name-register documents in order, two sheets were not returned but left standing. Then, at the point of the reading, Nuinokamino Kami quietly whispered from the side, and it is said that [the sheets] were made to be returned.

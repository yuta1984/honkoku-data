**Keichō 13th Year (1608)**
　Mokudai (Deputy Shrine Administrator): Kōfuku Tayū　　Deputy: Ukon no Shōgen

**Same 13th Year**
　　(Interlinear note: Shōtai-bun / Yamashiro no Kami)
　Shoshi (Shrine Administrator): Hōki no Kami　　[serving] personally
　Mokudai: Etchū no Kami　　Deputy: Ukon no Shōgen

**Same 14th Year (1609)**
　Shoshi: Fujikoma　　Deputy: Utano no Kami (Gagaku no Kami)
　Mokudai: Yogorō　　Deputy: Samanosuke
　　(Interlinear note: The main/regular body belongs to Iwami no Kami's group)
　The seal of approval (upper seal) was affixed, but [this matter] was not recorded in the "Keihani" [a register or ledger].

**Same 15th Year (1610)**
　Shoshi: Izu no Kami　　Deputy: Samanosuke
　Mokudai: Hyōgo no Kami　　Deputy: Izumo no Kami
　　(Interlinear note: The main/regular body is Kurando no Jō)

**Same 16th Year (1611)**
　Shoshi: Kurando no Kami　　Deputy: Samanosuke
　　(Interlinear note: At this time, [he] did not forget his duties as御上役 (senior official role), and hired a Shōjingashira (head of ritual purification) to carry out the duties.)
　Mokudai: Bungo no Kami　　Deputy: Izumo no Kami

**Same 17th Year (1612)**
　　(Interlinear note: There were two kimoiri (intermediary overseers) — Uemon no Suke and Saimatsudayū)
　Shoshi: Tayū Shōgen　　Deputy: Utano no Kami (Gagaku no Kami)

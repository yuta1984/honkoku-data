The following day, the 9th - Chōyō (Double Ninth Festival) - End of the Five Seasonal Festivals

Early morning, the shrine visit announcements《shrine attendants serve in this role》are made three times, and each shrine official《wearing court dress with one attendant each》exits the gate《around the half of the hour of the Rabbit (around 6 AM). However, in ancient times this was a night ceremony. The ancient ritual procedures state: "The time was sunset to twilight《omitted》evening offerings, etc."》They assemble at the attendants' building《as usual. However, the head ritualist alone goes to the office building》

The rotating offerings staff《five people in purification robes with sashinuki (court trousers)》purification leader《same as above, purification robes》yin-yang master《wearing court cap and robes with one attendant》deputy master, superintendent master, administrative master《each wearing purification robes with sashinuki》court pages《two people. Clan children under fifteen years old, wearing purification robes with sashinuki. However, the sashinuki are provided by the administrators》sword bearers《two people, same as court pages. These four people are discussed among the deputies, particularly determining order by seating precedence, with court pages taking the highest position. In addition, two deputies participate for supervision》etc. assemble at the shrine grounds《however, administrative and superintendent officials assemble at the offering hall and attendants' building as customary》

Next, the administrative master《after the sword bearers and various officials have prepared and arranged the divine offerings》announces that the divine offerings should be established《the shrine priest responds as customary》Next, the shrine priest and below《the head ritualist remains at the office building》arrive at the earth building《bowing and saluting as customary》Next, divine offerings depart from the offering hall.

The procedure: first the advance guard《shrine attendants announce this》next the chrysanthemum cotton《the head ritualist, after ritual purification《the administrative master serves in this role》receives this from the superintendent in front of the office building, holds it reverently, carries it attached to the ritual tablet and proceeds first, later at the Wakamiya worship hall《or in the courtyard》hands it to the superintendent master as before》

Next the sacred sake vessel《sword bearer holds this》next the sacred food containers and below - shrine maiden division, ritualist division, auxiliary shrines, and outer sanctuary daily offering sacred bitter containers etc. depart as usual《particularly the administrative superintendent, food preparation, and Taguchi attendants follow as usual》When passing the earth building, each prostrates《shrine officials》Next, the yin-yang master purifies the divine offerings at the customary place《under the jewel bridge》《as usual》Upon completion, the divine offerings arrive at the east and west sacred offering shelves《the route and left-right distinctions as usual. However, the head ritualist carries the chrysanthemum cotton, crosses the shrine maiden division's bamboo bridge and jewel bridge to the superintendent master, enters the corridor, faces the sacred altar diagonally, makes one bow and takes seat as usual》

Next, the shrine priest and below rise from the earth building and proceed to the sacred altar《dividing left and right》At this time, the yin-yang master purifies bodies《as usual》and upon completion takes seat before the sacred altar《route and advancing-retreating bowing and saluting as usual》Next, rotating offerings staff《after shrine officials proceed to the transparent corridor》purification leader《before divine offerings departure》take customary seats.

Next, the deputy master presents the sacred key to the head ritualist《route and advancing-retreating as usual》Next, the head ritualist opens the sacred doors《bowing, saluting, and handling robes as usual》At this time, shrine officials and clansmen shift from their seats and prostrate.

Next, musicians perform music. Next, shrine priest ritualists enter the inner sanctuary《bowing, saluting, advancing and retreating as usual》Next, from the head shrine maiden to the clan deity ritualists, they rise in order and divide left and right, waiting on the upper and lower steps《bowing, saluting, and handling robes as usual》Upon completion, the sacred curtains are rolled up《the head ritualist serves in this role》

Next, the shrine maiden division announces the advance guard《shrine attendants announce this. Same as before for those below》Next, chrysanthemum cotton is presented《two chrysanthemum flowers with attached cotton placed on a small round mat and loaded on a round tray. The chrysanthemum flowers are prepared by the sword bearers, the cotton comes from the shrine priest. The above cotton attachment procedure is for appreciation》

First, the ritual abstainer takes his position at the designated place. Next, the key is handed over to the Hafuri in advance, then the Hafuri rises and proceeds to the divine presence to open the sacred doors《with musical accompaniment》《below as usual》. After completion, he descends to the earthen platform, at which time the chief priest rises and proceeds to the earthen platform, standing side by side for one bow before entering the inner sanctuary. Next, the senior associate Negi rises and proceeds to the earthen platform, standing side by side for one bow and waiting on the large platform. The other shrine officials and deputy administrators, as usual, stand side by side and after one bow, wait on the upper and lower steps《however, the two officials of Kifune do not participate in this shrine's sacred rituals but proceed directly to the wooden boat》.

Next, the Hafuri rolls up the sacred curtain. Next, first chopsticks are offered from the Negi side《placed on round plates with musical accompaniment, before the deity as usual, but empty plates are removed to the Negi side because there will be offering of sacred food from the Hafuri side》. Next, sacred food is offered from the Hafuri side《all announced in advance by divine servants》. First, the sacred bowl of rice《placed on flat wood, set on round plates》, next sacred carp《one throat, earthenware vessels spread three times on cypress leaves, loaded and placed on round plates, offered together with earthenware》, next birds《one pheasant, same as before》, next fresh items《five throats, one salted sea bream each offered five times, serving items same as before》, next stacked offerings《fifty rays placed in sacred chest》, next wrapped offerings《ten packages of sacred confections placed in sacred chest, without spoons》, next Koma offerings《one salted sea bream placed in sacred chest, all empty plates removed to the Negi side》.

Next, sacred sake is offered from the Negi side《as usual, but due to the special offerings there is no ceremony of combining them, only the Negi side sake server. Generally, during special offerings it is like this. Especially after removing empty plates to the Negi quarters, the Ōta Negi remains as is, posting at the base of the worship pillar in the space of the railings, then returns to his seat. Previously at this time, the transfer team members return to the Negi worship hall after the completion of Hafuri sacred food》.

Next, the Hafuri lowers the sacred curtain. At this time, the clan god Hafuri, deputy administrators, etc. make one bow in order and return to their original seats. Next, they come to the Hafuri worship hall in advance and hand over the key to the Hafuri of Kataoka and below. Next, each proceeds to their respective shrine, and the five officials take seats at the ritual prayer position. Next, they come to the seats in the covered corridor in advance and announce the completion of Kataoka ritual prayers《as usual》. Next, the five officials recite ritual prayers《as usual》. After completion, the chief priest Hafuri rises and enters the inner sanctuary as before《however, in the area of the western steps, the Hafuri advances first like the senior associate Negi. Especially after the shrine officials of Kataoka, Shingu, Wakamiya, etc. return to their seats in the covered corridor, worship hall, etc., then they rise》.

The other shrine officials wait on the upper and lower steps as before《however, the shrine officials below the Hafuri steps, after one bow, each line up below the Negi steps》. Next, the Hafuri rolls up the sacred curtain. Next, the sacred food is removed《with musical accompaniment as before》《all removed to the Negi side as usual, but previously at this time, sacred wiping cloths are brought in advance and placed on top of the offering tables, and the head of the transfer team advances to below the eastern steps of the authority hall and advances two or three round plates sufficiently》.

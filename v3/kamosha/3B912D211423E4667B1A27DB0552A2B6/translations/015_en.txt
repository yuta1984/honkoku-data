In this ceremony, they arrange their robe hems at the large platform. For the steps of the large platform, the negi holds with his right hand and the hafuri with his left hand, pushing the sacred curtain with their ritual batons to enter the inner sanctuary. In this ceremony at the seven shrines, because the space is narrow, first the negi and then the hafuri proceed to the inner sanctuary in two stages. At Honmiya and Kifune there is no such ceremony. Among these, at Nara, Sawada, Ujigami and others, the entrances to the sacred doors are exceptionally narrow, so only during New Year's Day, the 15th, the April festival, purification ceremonies, and special festivals do both officials stand together to enter the inner sanctuary and offer sacred provisions. For other sacred ceremonies, both officials consult together to prepare and advance, withdraw, and have alternated in service with the negi and hafuri.

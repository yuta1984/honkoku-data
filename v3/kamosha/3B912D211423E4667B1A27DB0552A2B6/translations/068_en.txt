《Hafuri's portion》One meal offering: the head priest holds a banquet　　《However, for the three occasions of the biannual clan deity festival and planting festival, six cups of elevated portions are reserved from the head priest's banquet and passed to the clan members' seats. Rice is cooked separately during daily offerings, with only this portion newly prepared and mixed with wakame seaweed. The elevated portions are divine remainders》

Procedures for Subsidiary Shrine Offering Service
《However, when presenting rice offerings to the main shrine, pass them to the senior official near the sliding board area, perform one bow, then immediately descend to the eastern side of the covered corridor and proceed through the middle gate. The matter of circulating footwear should be prepared in advance》

First proceed to Kataoka Shrine, one bow at the earthen platform, then one bow at the large platform, then arrange one leg of the Negi's offering table in the center, then offer divine provisions《place in the offering chest as is and arrange on the offering table》Morning offering portions《seven sets of chopsticks, seven rice portions, round rice, five earthenware containers with one slice of ray each, two earthenware sake cups with stands, one rice cake, one soup, one salt》

《One sweetfish》each placed in one offering chest, double bow with two-stage ritual prayers《recited at the earthen platform》clapping《as usual》then Shingu Shrine《one set of chopsticks, one rice portion, round rice, five portions with one slice of ray each, two earthenware sake cups with stands》then Wakamiya Shrine《same as Shingu》withdraw for morning offering banquet

The ceremony concludes as usual, the feast is completed at the feast hall, and everyone withdraws.

　　　Ceremony of Kifune Shrine

Both officials purify themselves, head attendants {{Note: each in purification robes, but head attendants wear collar garments and standing black caps, each with one attendant and mounted on horseback}} take their seats at the worship hall {{Note: as usual}}. The purification head attendants sit on the eastern side {{Note: arranged north to south, facing west}}. Next the ritual officiant rises and proceeds to the earth platform for one bow. Next the head shrine child advances with one sacred offering table {{Note: always kept at the worship hall and taken from there}}. The ritual officiant takes it and prepares it at the center of the earth platform. Next the sacred offerings for the shelf ceremony are advanced.

The ceremony proceeds as follows: first the lower serving of two portions {{Note: while stacked, the sword attendants manipulate and bring them out at the worship hall}} is advanced, and the ritual officiant offers them on the sacred table {{Note: while stacked, placed together on the western side}}. Next the same {{Note: while stacked, placed together on the eastern side}}. Next the upper serving of one portion is advanced, then offered on top of the four lower servings {{Note: naturally in the center}}. Next small folded containers and one vertical wooden piece are advanced. Then they are offered on the western side of the five right folded containers {{Note: the standing wood is placed on top; for the sacred offerings of the six folded containers from the upper serving and below, the items are the same as for the current subsidiary shrines, so they are omitted}}. Next the head shrine child returns to his seat, and the ritual officiant descends to the courtyard. At this time the senior priest advances to the small courtyard. At this time the sword attendants present sacred streamers to both officials {{Note: three poles each}}.

Next after both officials present the sacred streamers, they place the streamers standing at the stairs {{Note: left and right}}. Both officials return to their seats. Next the purification head attendants {{Note: five people}} advance to the courtyard {{Note: each in one line}}. After taking the streamers and presenting them, each places them standing at the left and right of the stairs and returns to their seat {{Note: naturally those who present streamers, from both officials down, perform eight bows}}. Next the ritual officiant advances to the earth platform and removes the sacred offerings.

The ceremony proceeds as follows: first the small folded containers and vertical wood, next the upper serving, next the two lower servings {{Note: eastern side}}, next the same {{Note: western side}}, next the one sacred offering table is removed.

When finished, each person withdraws to the shrine child quarters. The sacred offerings for the shelf ceremony of the small shrine are placed in the spice chest {{Note: the sacred offerings for the Kifune main shrine shelf ceremony and porridge offerings etc. are placed together, and two attendants on foot first transport them to the wooden boat}}. Two workers transport them to the wooden boat {{Note: naturally sword attendants accompany them}}. Those who make daily visits serve at each location. First Kajitori Shrine, next Umemiya, next Shiraishi Shrine {{Note: these two shrines have viewing intervals, their keys are kept at Taguchi residence, and should be examined beforehand}}, next Shirahige Shrine, next the temporary sanctuary, next Kawao Shrine, next Suzuka Shrine {{Note: Kawao Shrine is served on the large platform}}, next Ushi-ichi Shrine, next Yui-gami Shrine, next Kisaichi Shrine, next Hayashida Shrine, next Hiyoshi Shrine, next Suikuzu Shrine, next the inner main shrine, next Suzu-ichi Shrine. These are fifteen shrines in total. Each is served with offerings placed in small sacred chests.

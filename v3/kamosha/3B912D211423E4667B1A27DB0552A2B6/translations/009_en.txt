The musicians take their seats in the western music hall《Note: numbers are not fixed》. The purification leaders take their seats in the direct meal hall《Note: however, five people in one row facing north with eastern precedence》. People making voluntary visits gather in the direct meal hall.

Next, the deputy master presents the sacred key to the chief ritualist.

The ceremony involves taking the sacred key from the western offering place, passing through the authority hall to open the middle well, drop-edge, transparent corridor, etc., passing through the gable door of the transparent corridor, going around the outside of the prayer recitation building to reach the veranda, putting on shoes to ascend the Wakamiya worship hall, entering the eastern connecting hall and delivering the sacred key to the chief ritualist. Taking out a fan from within the robe and holding it, returning via the route of initial approach. Performing the shoe-removing bow, and when passing through the main authority hall, performing respectful bowing (or passing bow) and withdrawing to the western offering building《Note: However, the sacred keys for the main shrine authority hall and seven shrines are always kept at the deputy's residence and brought before divine ceremonies》.

Procedure for Soil Dissolution Festival on the last day of the second month《however, on small months, the 29th day》
First, on the current day, daily offerings at the main shrine and below are as usual, with the shrine official on duty performing the role《banquets etc. as usual, however within the chief priest's banquet, six elevated offerings are set aside, four of which are given to arrow sword bearers, and two are for the imperial banquet》. When the appointed time arrives《after three announcements, around the hour of Monkey》, shrine officials each《in court dress, with one attendant》 gather at the waiting hall《the ceremony is as usual, however deputy shrine administrators, yin-yang masters《each in court dress》, ritual abstainers《in white robes with loose hair》, and transfer team members《in purification robes and court caps》 gather at the shrine precincts before the appointed time》.

After preparations are complete, the administrative master《in purification robes》 comes to the lowest seat《within the threshold of the lower room》. He asks each assembled person "Can the sacred offerings be established?" and the chief priest answers that "they can be established"《at this time the administrative master turns toward the offering hall and reports this to the head priest》.

Next each rises and takes position at the earth house《the ceremony as usual》. Next the sacred offerings depart《however, this is for the Hafuri's special offerings. The procedure: first one divine servant carries sakaki branches and announces the advance, next one sake server with silk cover carried by sword bearer, next seven shrine offering chests, each with silk cover carried by sword bearers, next divine servant announces the advance《same as before》, next eight offering chests for main shrine Hafuri offerings, each with silk cover carried by sword bearers, but finally carried in stacks. The above sake server and seven shrine offerings proceed from Negi's bamboo bridge through Tama Bridge, tower gate, middle gate, etc., entering through the side door to reach the western offering hall. Hafuri offerings proceed from Negi's bamboo bridge through the worship hall and Shingu imperial gate, Chinese gate, etc. to reach the eastern offering hall. The head priest and administrators naturally accompany as usual》.

At this time the yin-yang master performs complete purification at the designated place. Next the chief priest and below rise and proceed to the divine presence《the ceremony as usual》. After the yin-yang master completes bodily purification at the designated place, each takes seats at the covered corridor, worship hall, etc.《however regarding deputy administrators, as usual after the clan god Negi takes his seat, he comes from the banquet hall to the covered corridor and sits next to the clan god Negi》, and transfer team clan members and musicians also take their seats.

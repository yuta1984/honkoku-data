7th day: Procedure for White Horse Presentation (On the night of the 6th, one worship visit is made. This is at the sacred tablet house seat)
At the first point of the hour of the tiger, shrine officials approach the shrine grounds (each wearing court dress) and take their seats before the deity (negi side at the western transparent corridor, hafuri side at the eastern crossing hall)
Next, the sacred doors are opened (as usual). Next, divine offerings of young herbs and sacred rice gruel are presented
　(However, the divine offerings of the outer sanctuary are for the morning meal. For the evening meal, those on daily rotation duty attend each time) The shrine officials' movements follow the New Year's Day ceremony, but all divine offerings of the inner sanctuary remain presented, with the head priest and hafuri waiting in the inner sanctuary, while all other shrine officials wait on the left and right sides of the great platform stairs, etc.
Next, the administrator calls the shrine attendants and commands: "Have the sacred horse brought forth"
Next, the shrine attendants go toward the sacred stable and convey this message. This continues up to seven and a half times

Naturally, even for servants who have scheduled time off, they shall be treated the same as the attending group members.
When there are those who have been absent three times, the annual event officials shall provide notice and request that one barrel and one dish be provided to the assembly. The quantity of one barrel shall be three shō, and one dish shall be tofu or dried goods as determined by the annual event officials. Hereafter, the annual event officials shall maintain attendance records for each assembly gathering, and accurate records shall be kept as instructed to the annual event officials.

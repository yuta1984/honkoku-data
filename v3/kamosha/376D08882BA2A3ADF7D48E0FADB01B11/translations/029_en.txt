4th Day, Kanoto-Inu: Clear. Miuno Chūsho visited, I offered him sake, and he departed in the evening. ○Today I submitted two copies of Tsunehiro's family registry to Nomura Hō-in Seisan's office. ○There was an evening shower.

7th Day, Mizunoto-Ushi: Clear. The Tanabata festival divine services, palace celebrations, etc. took place as usual.

8th Day, Kinoe-Tora: Cloudy. From early morning, mother, Tsuneyuki and others went to visit graves as usual. At half past the hour of the sheep there was thunder, followed by a brief evening shower that stopped.

14th Day, Mizunoe-Saru: Clear. The mid-year festival celebrations and礼 for the children took place as usual. ○This season's payment total was 1 kan 514 momme 7 bu 5 rin 9 mō of silver.

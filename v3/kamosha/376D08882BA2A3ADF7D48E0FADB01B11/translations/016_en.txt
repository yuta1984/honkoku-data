Kyōsen: In charge of two courtyard fire-braziers in the small courtyard inside the middle gate, two lantern houses, up to the Chinese gate.

○Before the hour of the Rat (around midnight), thunder rumbled and rain fell.

13th Day, Mizunoe-Saru: Cloudy. In the afternoon, heavy thunderstorms with rain fell, with several thunderclaps. Lightning struck trees to the east of the first torii, causing some damage to the wood.

15th Day, Kinoe-Inu: Clear. Today at the hour of the Cock (around 6 PM), the formal shrine relocation takes place. As I am assisting the head magistrate, word came from the shrine early in the morning that I should come around breakfast time. I immediately put on court hunting robes, went to worship together with Hitsukage, and after a brief rest, the first drum sounded. I immediately put on hunting robes, began service, and assembled at the bathhouse. However, from today I wore for the first time the light yellow divided skirts bestowed upon me. After all the head magistrates' hospitality staff assembled at the bathhouse, various matters were discussed among everyone. When the second drum sounded, the sword-bearing officials and their subordinates assembled. After the hour of the Horse (noon), His Excellency Middle Counselor Shimizudani as senior noble, Magistrate Lord Bōjō as controller, Deputy Minister Lord Nakain Sauma no Gon no Kami and others, the Senior Secretary, Official Duties Right Senior Assistant, Junior Secretary, Historian, Left Palm Official, attendants, controller attendants, and all other various officials arrived and entered their respective rest areas. When the third drum sounded, the shrine officials and below prepared their retinues. There is a separate record of the shrine procedures, so this is abbreviated. When the appointed time arrived, Kyōsen entered within the curtain partition of the small courtyard at his assigned temporary shrine, and when extinguishing the courtyard braziers and below, he received instruction from Acting Deputy Chief Priest Yasutora, and immediately had the courtyard braziers at the same location extinguished by the pine ritual official. Next, he reported to Deputy Senior Assistant Kyōdō that the sacred lights should be extinguished. This matter had been coordinated with him beforehand. Next, there was seating at the small office, and Construction Department hospitality staff Kikai was informed that the box lanterns should be extinguished. Next, the matter of extinguishing fires was reported to Controller Lord's hospitality staff Yasutake.

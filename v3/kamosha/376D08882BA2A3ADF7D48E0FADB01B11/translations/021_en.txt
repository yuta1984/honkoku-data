9th Day, Tsuchinoe-Inu: Clear. Storage house construction - today the second floor boarding began.

11th Day, Kanoe-Ne: Clear. It was the turn for the ten-day prayer ritual. Kyōki participated in shrine worship for the first time. ○Tonight at the hours of Cock and Dog (6-9 PM), the formal reconstruction ceremony of Wakamiya Nara Shrine took place.

13th Day, Mizunoe-Tora: Rain. At the hours of Cock and Dog (6-9 PM), the formal reconstruction ceremony of Sawada Shrine's tutelary deity shrine took place.

14th Day, Mizunoto-U: Clear. To the Seo Jibei household, five bundles of fans were sent privately as return gifts to celebrate the completion of Tedai-zō's succession to family headship.

15th Day, Kinoe-Tatsu: Clear. The foundation boards of the storehouse were laid today, and eaves sections one and two were ordered from carpenter Yoshichirō and installed today. ○In the past Tiger year, regarding the rice field owned by Gennosuke of Umegasuji town, in the area called Sakura Jūnin-ue, with a crop yield of eight to (measures) of rice, an agreement was made for five years with three ryō of gold and 320 momme of silver. However, by December of last year (Horse year), the gold and silver were not repaid, and it remained unchanged into this year. Therefore, through apologies and using Jinuemon of the same town as intermediary, negotiations continued. This time, they responded that they would permanently transfer the land, so the crop yield was set at six to, a total of 550 momme of silver was paid, documents were exchanged today, and the remaining two bu of gold was delivered. Additionally, beyond taking the interest from the Horse group month, about ten momme were given out of compassion.

18th Day, Hinoto-Hitsuji: Rain. For the Aoi Festival, in the evening I participated in shrine worship at Goshōsho for the first time and joined the ascending shrine circuit as in previous years.

19th Day, Tsuchinoe-Saru: Cloudy with occasional rain. In the afternoon, Kyōki and Kyōkan participated in shrine worship. ○Regarding Taga Bōnosuke, head of the retainers' group, word of his daily negligence in service reached His Lordship's ears, and by his judgment, he was ordered to refrain from duty starting today.
